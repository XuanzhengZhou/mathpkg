Apply 15.20 to obtain a sentence $\psi$ such that

$$\mathcal{P} \models Pr(\langle \neg' \Delta_{g}^{+} \psi \rangle) \leftrightarrow \psi.$$

Show that neither $\mathcal{P} \models \psi$ nor $\mathcal{P} \models \neg\psi$. This is another way of interpreting the Liar paradox different from that given following 15.20.
