The cardinal operation $m^{\partial}$ is a technical tool used in the Keisler-Shelah isomorphism theorem. It denotes the least cardinal $n$ such that $m^n > m$. Note that $\aleph_0 \leq m^{\partial} \leq m$, with $\aleph_0^{\partial} = \aleph_0$. Under GCH and for regular $m$, $m^{\partial} = m$.
