Define $\langle H_\alpha : \alpha < m \rangle$ and $\langle E_\alpha : \alpha < m \rangle$ by transfinite recursion. At each stage $\alpha$, if adding $\Gamma_\alpha$ to the current filter preserves $m$-consistency, extend accordingly; otherwise add the complement. The transfinite construction completes within $m$ steps by careful cardinal bookkeeping, and at most $m$ elements are removed from $F$ in total.
