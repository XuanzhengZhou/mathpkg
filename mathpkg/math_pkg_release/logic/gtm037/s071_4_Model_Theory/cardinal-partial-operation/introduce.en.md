Definition 26.32 introduces the cardinal operation $m^\partial$, the least cardinal $n$ for which $m^n > m$. This technical cardinal invariant plays a crucial role in the Keisler-Shelah isomorphism theorem, where it controls the size of sets of functions used in the ultraproduct construction. Under GCH, $m^\partial = m$ for regular $m$.
