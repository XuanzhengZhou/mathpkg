This lemma is the principle of explosion (ex falso quodlibet): from a statement $\varphi$ and its negation $\neg\varphi$, anything $\psi$ follows. In the object language this is expressed as $\varphi \to (\neg\varphi \to \psi)$. The proof uses the deduction theorem and the logical axioms, showing that a contradiction yields arbitrary conclusions in the formal system.
