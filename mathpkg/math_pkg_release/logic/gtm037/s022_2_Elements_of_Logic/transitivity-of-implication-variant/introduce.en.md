This lemma is a form of transitivity for implication: if $\varphi$ implies $\psi$ and $\psi$ implies $\chi$, then $\varphi$ implies $\chi$. It is proved using the deduction theorem applied three times. Together with similar lemmas, it forms the logical infrastructure for the completeness theorem of propositional logic, providing a syntactic derivation of the "syllogism" form within the Hilbert-style axiomatic system.
