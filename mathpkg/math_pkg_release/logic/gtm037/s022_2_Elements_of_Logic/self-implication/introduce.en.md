This lemma shows that every formula implies itself, i.e., $\varphi \to \varphi$ is a theorem of the sentential calculus. The proof is given in full detail to illustrate the mechanics of formal deduction in a Hilbert-style axiomatic system, using only axiom schemas A1 and A2 together with the rule of detachment (modus ponens).
