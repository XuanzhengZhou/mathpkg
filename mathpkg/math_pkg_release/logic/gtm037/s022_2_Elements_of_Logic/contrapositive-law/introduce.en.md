This lemma is the formal counterpart of the contrapositive inference rule: if $\varphi$ implies $\psi$, then the negation of $\psi$ implies the negation of $\varphi$. The proof uses the deduction theorem together with double-negation reasoning (which itself is developed through Lemma 8.18). It is an essential derived rule used in the construction toward the completeness theorem.
