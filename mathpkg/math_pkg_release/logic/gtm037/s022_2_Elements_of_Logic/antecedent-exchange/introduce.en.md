This lemma permits exchanging the order of two antecedents in a nested implication: if $\varphi$ implies that $\psi$ implies $\chi$, then $\psi$ implies that $\varphi$ implies $\chi$. The proof is an easy application of the deduction theorem, and the result is a useful derived rule in Hilbert-style propositional logic.
