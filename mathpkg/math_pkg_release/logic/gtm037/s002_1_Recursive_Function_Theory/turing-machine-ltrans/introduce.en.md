$T_{l\text{trans}}$ is a Turing submachine that performs a leftward translation of a block of consecutive $1$'s. In the case of interest, it transforms the configuration $a\ 0\ 1^{(b+1)}\ 0$ into $a\ 1^{(b+1)}\ 0\ 0$, effectively shifting the block of ones one position to the left while preserving the surrounding tape structure. This translation operation is a fundamental building block for more complex tape manipulations.
