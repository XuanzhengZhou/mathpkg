Proposition 28.26 shows that $m$-saturation is preserved when restricting a structure to the interpretation of a distinguished unary predicate, provided that predicate is closed under the operations and has size at least $\aleph_0$. The resulting substructure inherits the saturation property, which is useful for constructing saturated substructures within larger saturated models.
