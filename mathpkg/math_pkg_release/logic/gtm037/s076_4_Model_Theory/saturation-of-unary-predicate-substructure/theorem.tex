Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a language having a unary relation symbol $\mathbf{P}$, and suppose $\mathfrak{A}$ is an $m$-saturated $\mathcal{L}$-structure satisfying:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $|\mathbf{P}^{\mathfrak{A}}| \geq \aleph_0$;
\item[(ii)] for each operation symbol $\mathbf{O}$, $\mathbf{P}^{\mathfrak{A}}$ is closed under $\mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{A}}$.
\end{itemize}
Let $\mathfrak{B}$ be the $\mathcal{L}$-structure with $B = \mathbf{P}^{\mathfrak{A}}$, $\mathbf{R}^{\mathfrak{B}} = \mathbf{R}^{\mathfrak{A}} \cap {}^m \mathbf{P}^{\mathfrak{A}}$, and $\mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{B}} = \mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{A}} \upharpoonright {}^m \mathbf{P}^{\mathfrak{A}}$. Then $\mathfrak{B}$ is $m$-saturated, and $|B| \geq m$.
