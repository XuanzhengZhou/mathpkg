Definition 10.8 introduces auxiliary number-theoretic concatenation operations ($\text{Con}_m$, $\text{Con}'$, $\text{Con}''$) that enable arithmetic manipulation of G\"{o}del numbers of sequences of expressions. The collection $\text{Trm } \mathcal{L}$ of terms is defined as the smallest class of expressions containing all individual variables and closed under the formation rule: applying an $m$-ary operation symbol to $m$ terms yields a new term. This inductive closure definition is the standard syntactic specification of the well-formed terms of a first-order language.
