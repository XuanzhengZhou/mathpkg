Lemma 16.46 states that in the language $\mathcal{L}_9$ (the definitional expansion of $\mathcal{L}_8$ with the multiplication operation $\cdot$ defined by Definition 16.45), the sentence $v_0 \cdot 0 = 0$ is logically valid. This follows directly from the defining axiom in Definition 16.45 together with Lemma 16.41. This lemma verifies one of the key axioms of Robinson arithmetic Q in the language $\mathcal{L}_9$, namely that multiplication by zero yields zero.
