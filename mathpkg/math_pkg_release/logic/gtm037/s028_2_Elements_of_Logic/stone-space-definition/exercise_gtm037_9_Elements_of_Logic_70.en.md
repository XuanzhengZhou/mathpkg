A Stone space is a compact Hausdorff space in which the closed-open sets form a base. For any topological space $X$, the set of closed-open sets forms a field of sets; let $\mathfrak{G}X$ be the associated Boolean algebra. Show that for a Stone space $X$, $\mathfrak{G}X$ is isomorphic to the subalgebra of the direct power $^{X}2$ whose universe consists of all continuous functions from $X$ into $2$ (with the discrete topology). Furthermore, show that $F\mathfrak{G}X$ is homeomorphic to $X$.
