Show that the following functions are elementary:

(1) $f(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-2}, z) = \begin{cases} \max y \leq z((x_0, \ldots, x_{m-2}, y) \in R), \\ = 0 & \text{if there is no such } y, \end{cases}$ where $R$ is elementary.

(2) $g(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-2}, y) = \max \{f(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-2}, z) : z \leq y\}$, with $f$ elementary.

(3) $g(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-2}, y) = \min \{f(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-2}, z) : z \leq y\}$, with $f$ elementary.
