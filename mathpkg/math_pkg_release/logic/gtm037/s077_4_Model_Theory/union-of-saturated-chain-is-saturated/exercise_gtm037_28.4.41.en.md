If $\mathfrak{A}_\alpha$ is $m$-saturated for each $\alpha < n$, $\mathfrak{A}_\alpha \preceq \mathfrak{A}_\beta$ whenever $\alpha < \beta < n$, and $n$ is regular, then $\bigcup_{\alpha < n} \mathfrak{A}_\alpha$ is $m$-saturated.
