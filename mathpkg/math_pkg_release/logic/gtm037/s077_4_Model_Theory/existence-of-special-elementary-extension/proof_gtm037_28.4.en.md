If $m = n^+$ for some $n$, then the assumptions imply that $2^n = n^+$, so the desired result follows from Theorem 28.12 (existence of saturated elementary extensions). Now assume that $m$ is a limit cardinal. Define the desired sequence $\langle \mathfrak{B}_n : n < m \rangle$ by induction on $n$, leading up to the desired structure.
