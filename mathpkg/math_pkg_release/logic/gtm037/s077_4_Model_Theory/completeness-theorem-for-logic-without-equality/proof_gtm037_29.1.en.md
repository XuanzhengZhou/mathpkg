This follows immediately from Theorem 29.2. Given a consistent set $\Gamma$, one constructs a consistency family $S$ containing $\Gamma$ (the family of all consistent sets of sentences in the rich expansion). Theorem 29.2 then yields a model for $\Gamma$. The bound on the cardinality of the model follows from condition (C8).
