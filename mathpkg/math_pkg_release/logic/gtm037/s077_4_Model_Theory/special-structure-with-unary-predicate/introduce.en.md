This proposition is the analog of 28.26 for special structures. It shows that if a special structure contains a unary predicate $\mathbf{P}$ that is closed under all operations and has at least countably many elements, then the relativized reduct to $\mathbf{P}$ is also special. The proof is sketched in the text as being not quite as clear as the reduct case.
