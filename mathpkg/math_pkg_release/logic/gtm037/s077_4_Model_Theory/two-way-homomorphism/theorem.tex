Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a relational first-order language without equality, and let $\mathfrak{A}$ and $\mathfrak{B}$ be $\mathcal{L}$-structures. A two-way homomorphism from $\mathfrak{A}$ onto $\mathfrak{B}$ is a function $f$ mapping $A$ onto $B$ such that $f$ is a homomorphism from $\mathfrak{A}$ to $\mathfrak{B}$ and there exists a homomorphism $g$ from $\mathfrak{B}$ to $\mathfrak{A}$ with $g$ mapping $B$ onto $A$.
