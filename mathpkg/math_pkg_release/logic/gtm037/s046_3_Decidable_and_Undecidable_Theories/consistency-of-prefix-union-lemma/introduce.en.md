This lemma asserts that at every finite stage of the uniform extension construction, the original theory $\Gamma$ together with the first $n$ sentences selected by the binary recursive function $f(m,\cdot)$ remains consistent. It is proved by induction on $n$, with the base case $n=0$ following from the consistency of $\Gamma$ and $n=1$ from the definition of the construction. The induction step uses a contradiction argument: if consistency fails at stage $n$, then the $n-1$-th step would already have been inconsistent, contradicting the induction hypothesis.
