An $\omega$-projective class generalizes projective classes by allowing $\omega$-models in the expanded language. The class is the collection of reducts of $\omega$-models of some theory in an $\omega$-expansion. This notion connects weak second-order logic with infinitary and higher-order formalisms through the concept of standard natural numbers.
