This lemma establishes a basic property of strongly compact cardinals: they are strong limit cardinals (i.e., if $n < m$ then $2^n < m$). The proof constructs a set of sentences in $L_{mm}$ that has no model but every subset of size less than $m$ does have a model, using the failure of the strong limit property.
