Exercise 30.20 establishes that when weak second-order sentences in the normal form of 30.6 contain no existential quantifiers on individual variables, the class of models $\operatorname{Mod} \Gamma$ forms a universal (i.e., $\forall_1$-axiomatizable) class in ordinary first-order logic.
