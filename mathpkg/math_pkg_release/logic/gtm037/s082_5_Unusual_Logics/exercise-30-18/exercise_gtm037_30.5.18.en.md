Using 30.17, show that $(\omega^\omega \cdot \omega \cdot 2, <)$ and $(\omega^\omega \cdot (\omega + \omega^*), <)$ are weak second-order elementarily equivalent. Hence well-order is not definable in weak second-order logic.
