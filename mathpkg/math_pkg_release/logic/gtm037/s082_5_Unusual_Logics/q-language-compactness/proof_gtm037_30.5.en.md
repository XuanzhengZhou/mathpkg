Recalling from p. 323 our proof of the compactness theorem using ultraproducts, we obtain the result. Let $\Gamma$ be a countable set of $\mathcal{Q}$-sentences such that every finite subset has a model. Enumerate $\Gamma = \{\gamma_n : n \in \omega\}$ and for each $n$ let $\mathfrak{A}_n$ be a model of $\{\gamma_0, \ldots, \gamma_n\}$. Let $I = \omega$ and let $F$ be a nonprincipal ultrafilter on $\omega$. By Theorem 30.13, the ultraproduct $\prod_{n<\omega} \mathfrak{A}_n / F$ satisfies each $\gamma_k$ for $k \in \omega$, hence is a model of $\Gamma$.
