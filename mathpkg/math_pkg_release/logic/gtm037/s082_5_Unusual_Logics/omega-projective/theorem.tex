Let $K$ be a class of $\mathcal{L}$-structures. We say that $K$ is $\omega$-projective provided that there is an expansion $\mathcal{L}'$ of $\mathcal{L}$, an $\omega$-expansion $\mathcal{L}''$ of $\mathcal{L}'$, and a theory $\Theta$ in $\mathcal{L}''$, such that $K$ is the class of all $\mathcal{L}$-reducts of $\omega$-models of $\Theta$.
