A class $K$ of $\mathcal{L}$-structures is $\omega$-projective if it can be realized as the class of $\mathcal{L}$-reducts of $\omega$-models of some theory $\Theta$ in a suitably expanded language. This notion connects $\omega$-logic to projective classes and provides a semantic characterization of definability via $\omega$-models.
