A consistency family for $L_{\omega_1\omega}$ extends the first-order notion (Chapter 18) with conditions (C3') and (C4') to handle countable conjunctions and disjunctions. Condition (C3') requires that if a countable conjunction belongs to $\Gamma$, each conjunct can be added to $\Gamma$ while staying in $S$. Condition (C4') requires that if a countable disjunction belongs to $\Gamma$, at least one disjunct can be added. The definition of $\varphi^\rightarrow$ is also extended to infinitary formulas.
