Exercise 30.23 is a downward Löwenheim-Skolem-type result for $\mathcal{Q}$-languages under the $m$-interpretation: if a theory $\Gamma$ has a model of cardinality at least $m + |\operatorname{Fmla}_{\mathcal{L}}|$, then it has a model of cardinality exactly $m + |\operatorname{Fmla}_{\mathcal{L}}|$. This provides control over the size of models in $\mathcal{Q}$-languages.
