For any infinite cardinal m, the following are equivalent: (i) m is strongly compact; (ii) every m-complete, m-distributive Boolean algebra has an m-complete ultrafilter; (iii) in every m-complete field of sets, proper m-complete filters extend to m-complete ultrafilters; (iv) in the field of all subsets, proper m-complete filters extend to m-complete ultrafilters; (v) m-products of (m,infty)-compact Hausdorff spaces are (m,infty)-compact; (vi) arbitrary products of (m,infty)-compact Hausdorff spaces are (m,infty)-compact.
