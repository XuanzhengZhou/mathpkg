Corollary 18.10 is obtained directly from Theorem 18.9 by taking all admissible functions to be the identity. It states that every member of a consistency family on a rich expansion has a model whose cardinality does not exceed $|\text{Fmla}_\mathcal{L}|$, the number of formulas in the original language. This provides a uniform cardinality bound for Henkin-style model constructions without requiring the additional machinery of admissible functions.
