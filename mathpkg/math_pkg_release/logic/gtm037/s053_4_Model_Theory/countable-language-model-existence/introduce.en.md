Corollary 18.11 observes that when the language $\mathcal{L}$ has only countably many formulas (i.e., $|\text{Fmla}_\mathcal{L}| = \aleph_0$), the condition (C11) becomes superfluous in the proof of Theorem 18.9. In this case, the induction hypothesis (0) concerning cardinality bounds can be omitted from the transfinite construction, simplifying the Henkin-style argument for countable languages.
