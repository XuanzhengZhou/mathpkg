Lemma 2.37 establishes the basic lower bound $m \leq a(m, n)$ for the iterated exponential function. This simple inequality shows that the iterated exponential is at least linear in its first argument for all iteration depths, and is used as a building block for establishing the monotonicity properties and growth rate of $a(m, n)$.
