The associated first-order language $L^*$ has unary relation symbols $P_s$ for $s \in \mathcal{S}$. If $\mathbf{O}$ is an operation symbol of $L$ of rank $(s_0, \ldots, s_m)$, then as a symbol of $L^*$ the operation symbol $\mathbf{O}$ has rank $m$. The theory $\Gamma$ consists of sentences asserting that the $P_s$ are nonempty and partition the domain, and that operations and relations respect the sort partition. The structure $\mathfrak{A}^*$ is defined with universe $\bigcup_{s \in \mathcal{S}} A_s$, interpreting $P_s$ as $A_s$. The relativization $\varphi^*$ is defined by induction, relativizing quantifiers $\forall v_i^s$ to $P_s$. An induction on terms and formulas establishes the equivalence.
