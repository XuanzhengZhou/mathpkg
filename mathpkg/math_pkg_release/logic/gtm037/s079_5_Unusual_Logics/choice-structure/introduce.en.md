Definition 29.22 introduces the semantics of Hilbert's $\varepsilon$-operator. A choice structure pairs an ordinary structure with a choice function on its universe. The term $\varepsilon v_i \varphi$ is interpreted as a chosen witness satisfying $\varphi$, or the distinguished element $O$ if no such witness exists. This gives an ambiguous (non-deterministic) interpretation of the choice operator.
