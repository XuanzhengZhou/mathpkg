Corollary 29.30 establishes that the set of many-sorted logical validities is recursively enumerable. This follows from the reduction to ordinary first-order logic (Corollary 29.29) and the recursive enumerability of first-order validities, and justifies the possibility of complete proof systems for many-sorted logic.
