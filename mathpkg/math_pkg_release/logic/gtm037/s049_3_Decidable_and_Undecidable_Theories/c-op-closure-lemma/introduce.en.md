In the formal language $\mathcal{L}_2$, if a set $v_0$ satisfies the predicate $\mathbf{C}$, then the operation $\mathbf{Op}(v_0, v_1) = v_0 \cup \{v_1\}$ also produces a set satisfying $\mathbf{C}$. This lemma is the $\mathcal{L}_2$-analogue of Lemma 16.11 and establishes that the $\mathbf{C}$-property (related to complement formation) is preserved under the $\mathbf{Op}$ operation.
