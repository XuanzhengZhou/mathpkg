Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a relational language without equality, $\mathfrak{B}$ an $\mathcal{L}$-structure, $m$ a cardinal such that $|\text{Fmla}_{\mathcal{L}}| \leq m \leq |B|$, and $C$ a subset of $B$ such that $|C| \leq m$. Then there is an elementary substructure $\mathfrak{A}$ of $\mathfrak{B}$ such that $C \subseteq A$ and $|A| = m$.
