Theorem 29.15 shows that the downward Löwenheim–Skolem theorem carries over verbatim to logic without equality. For any relational language without equality and any structure, one can find an elementary substructure of any desired cardinality between the number of formulas and the size of the original structure, containing any prescribed small subset.
