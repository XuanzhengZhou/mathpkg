Proposition 10.43 establishes the recursiveness of the relation that a variable $\alpha$ occurs bound at the $k$th position of a formula $\varphi$, expressed through their Gödel numbers. A bound occurrence is characterized by the existence of some quantifier on $\alpha$ whose scope contains position $k$. The proof gives an explicit recursive characterization using the previously established relation $R$ (Proposition 10.42) together with straightforward conditions on Gödel number sequences.
