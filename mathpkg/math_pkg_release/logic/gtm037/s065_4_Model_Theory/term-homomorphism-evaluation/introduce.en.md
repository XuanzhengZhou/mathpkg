Proposition 24.16 establishes the universal property of the term algebra: any assignment of values to variables extends uniquely to a homomorphism from the term algebra to any structure. The evaluation of a term $\sigma$ under assignment $x$ equals the image of $\sigma$ under the homomorphism sending each variable $v_i$ to $x_i$. This is a foundational result in universal algebra.
