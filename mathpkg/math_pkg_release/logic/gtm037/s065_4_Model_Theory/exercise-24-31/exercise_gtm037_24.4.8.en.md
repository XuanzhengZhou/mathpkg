An algebra $\mathfrak{A}$ is a subdirect product of the system $\langle \mathfrak{B}_i : i \in I \rangle$ provided that $\mathfrak{A}$ is a subalgebra of $\prod_{i \in I} \mathfrak{B}_i$ and for each $i \in I$, the projection $\pi_i$ maps $\mathfrak{A}$ onto $\mathfrak{B}_i$.
