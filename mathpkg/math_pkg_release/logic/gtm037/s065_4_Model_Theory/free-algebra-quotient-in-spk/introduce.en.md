Lemma 24.20 shows that the quotient of the free term algebra by the congruence $\equiv_\Gamma$ (where $\Gamma$ consists of all equations true in $K$) belongs to the class $\mathbf{SPK}$ (substructures of products of members of $K$). This lemma is a crucial technical step in the proof of Birkhoff's HSP theorem.
