The proof follows from the definition of the operators. If $\mathfrak{A} \in \mathbf{UpSK}$, then $\mathfrak{A} \cong \prod_{i \in I} \mathfrak{B}_i / F$ where each $\mathfrak{B}_i \subseteq \mathfrak{C}_i$ for some $\mathfrak{C}_i \in K$. But then the natural embedding of $\prod \mathfrak{B}_i / F$ into $\prod \mathfrak{C}_i / F$ shows $\mathfrak{A} \in \mathbf{SUpK}$.
