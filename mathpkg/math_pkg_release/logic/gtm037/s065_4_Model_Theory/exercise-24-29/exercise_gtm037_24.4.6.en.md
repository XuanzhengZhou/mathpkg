In an algebraic language, a class $K$ is a variety if and only if $K = \mathbf{HSP}(\mathfrak{Fr}_\mathcal{L} / {\equiv_\Gamma})$, where $\Gamma = \{(\sigma, \tau) : \sigma = \tau \text{ holds in each member of } K\}$.
