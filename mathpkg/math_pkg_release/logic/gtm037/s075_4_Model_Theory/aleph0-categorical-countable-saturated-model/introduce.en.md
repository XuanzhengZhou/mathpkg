This corollary follows from Theorem 28.7 and Theorem 27.12. It states that any consistent $\aleph_0$-categorical theory with only infinite models in a countable language possesses a countable $\aleph_0$-saturated model. In fact, under these hypotheses, all countable models of $\Gamma$ are $\aleph_0$-saturated. Numerous examples of $\aleph_0$-categorical theories were given in Chapter 21.
