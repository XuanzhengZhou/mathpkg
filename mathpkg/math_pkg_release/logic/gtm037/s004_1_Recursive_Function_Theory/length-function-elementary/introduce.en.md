The length function $l(a)$ is elementary recursive. The proof expresses $l(a)$ using the bounded minimum operator: $l(a)$ is the least $i < a$ such that $p_i$ divides $a$ and no larger prime up to $a$ divides $a$. Since the divisibility relation, the prime function, and the bounded operators are all elementary, $l(a)$ is elementary.
