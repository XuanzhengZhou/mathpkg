$\mathfrak{A}$ is called an $m$-elementary extension of $\mathfrak{B}$, and $\mathfrak{B}$ an $m$-elementary substructure of $\mathfrak{A}$, where $m$ is a cardinal, if $\mathfrak{B} \subseteq \mathfrak{A}$ and for any formula $\varphi$ with at most $m$ quantifiers and any assignment of elements of $B$ to the free variables of $\varphi$, $\mathfrak{B} \models \varphi$ iff $\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi$.
