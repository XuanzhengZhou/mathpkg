Let $T$ be the theory in $\mathcal{L}_{\text{nos}}$ with the following axioms:

$$\forall v_0 \forall v_1 (s v_0 = s v_1 \rightarrow v_0 = v_1);$$
$$\forall v_0 \neg (s v_0 = 0);$$
$$[[\psi(0) \wedge \forall v_0 [\psi(v_0) \rightarrow \psi(s v_0)] \rightarrow \forall v_0 \psi(v_0)]];$$
$$\forall v_0 \forall v_1 (v_0 + v_1 = 0);$$
$$\forall v_0 \forall v_1 (v_0 \cdot v_1 = 0).$$

Show that not every recursive function is syntactically definable in $T$. Hint: eliminate quantifiers and show that not every recursive set is syntactically definable in $T$.
