Let $K$ be a class of $\mathcal{L}$-structures. Each of the following conditions implies the succeeding one. If $K$ is elementarily closed, then they are all equivalent.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $K$ is an elementary class;
\item[(ii)] $K$ is a projective class;
\item[(iii)] $\operatorname{Up} K = K$;
\item[(iv)] $K$ is compact.
\end{enumerate}
