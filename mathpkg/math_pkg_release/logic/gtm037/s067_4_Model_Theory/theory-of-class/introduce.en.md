The theory $\Theta_{\rho}K$ of a class $K$ of $\mathcal{L}$-structures is the set of all $\mathcal{L}$-sentences that hold in every member of $K$. For a single structure $\mathfrak{A}$, we write $\Theta_{\rho}\mathfrak{A}$ for its theory. This is one of the basic notions in first-order model theory, linking syntactic objects (sentences) with semantic objects (classes of structures).
