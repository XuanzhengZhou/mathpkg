Let $\mathcal{L}'$ be an expansion of $\mathcal{L}$, $\Gamma$ a set of sentences of $\mathcal{L}'$, and let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an $\mathcal{L}$-structure. Then the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $\mathfrak{A}$ can be embedded in $\mathfrak{B} \upharpoonright \mathcal{L}$ for some model $\mathfrak{B}$ of $\Gamma$.
\item[(2)] For every universal sentence $\varphi$ of $\mathcal{L}$, the condition $\Gamma \models \varphi$ implies that $\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi$.
\end{enumerate}
