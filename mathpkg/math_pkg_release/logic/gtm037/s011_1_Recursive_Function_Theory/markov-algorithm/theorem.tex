A Markov algorithm is a matrix $A$ of the form

$$
\begin{array}{cccc}
a_0 & b_0 & c_0 \\
a_1 & b_1 & c_1 \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\
a_m & b_m & c_m
\end{array}
$$

such that $a_0, \ldots, a_m, b_0, \ldots, b_m$ are words (finite sequences of $0$'s, $1$'s, and $2$'s) and $c_0, \ldots, c_m \in \{0, 1\}$.
