This lemma establishes that the class $A$ (used in the characterization of recursive functions) is closed under the minimalization operator when applied to special functions. A function $f$ is called special if certain technical conditions on its growth and definability are met. The proof proceeds by induction on the arity $m$ of $f$, reducing the general case to the binary case $m = 2$ via the pairing functions $\mathrm{Exc}$ and $L$.
