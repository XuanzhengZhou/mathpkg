This lemma, numbered (3) in the proof of Proposition 12.28, establishes a substitution-permutation identity in cylindric algebras. It states that certain compositions of cylindric substitution operations $s_{\kappa}^{\lambda}$ applied to $f_R$ are invariant under replacing a designated index $k_u$ with a fresh index $l$, provided the indices are chosen to be distinct from the relevant variables. This technical identity is needed to verify that the substitution axiom instances evaluate to the unit element under the auxiliary map $h$ constructed in the proof.
