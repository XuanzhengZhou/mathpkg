Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an effective syntactical $\mathcal{L}$-structure in $\mathcal{L}'$. Then the formation of $\sigma^{\mathfrak{A}}$ and $\varphi^{\mathfrak{A}}$ is effective. That is, the functions
$$g'^+ \circ f \circ g^{+-1} \upharpoonright g^{+*} \operatorname{Trm}_{\mathfrak{L}}$$
and
$$g'^+ \circ f \circ g^{+-1} \upharpoonright g^{+*} \operatorname{Fmla}_{\mathfrak{L}}$$
are partial recursive, where $f$ is the function assigning $\sigma^{\mathfrak{A}}$ to $\sigma$ and $\varphi^{\mathfrak{A}}$ to $\varphi$.
