Let $\langle \mathfrak{A}_i : i \in I \rangle$ be a system of $\mathcal{L}$-structures and let $F$ be an ultrafilter over $I$. Define the relation $\equiv_F$ on $\prod_{i \in I} A_i$ by
$$x \equiv_F y \quad \text{iff} \quad \{i \in I : x_i = y_i\} \in F.$$
Then $\equiv_F$ is a congruence relation on the product $\mathfrak{B} = \prod_{i \in I} \mathfrak{A}_i$; that is, $\equiv_F$ is an equivalence relation and for every operation symbol $\mathbf{O}$ of $\mathcal{L}$,
$$\text{if } x_t \equiv_F y_t \text{ for all } t < m, \text{ then } \mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{B}}(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-1}) \equiv_F \mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{B}}(y_0, \ldots, y_{m-1}).$$
