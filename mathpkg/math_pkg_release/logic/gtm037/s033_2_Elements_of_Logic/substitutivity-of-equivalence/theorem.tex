Let $\varphi, \psi, \chi$ be formulas and $\alpha \in {}^m\text{Rng } v$. Suppose that if $\beta$ occurs free in $\varphi$ or in $\psi$ but bound in $\chi$ then $\beta \in \{ \alpha_i : i < m \}$. Let $\theta$ be obtained from $\chi$ by replacing zero or more occurrences of $\varphi$ in $\chi$ by $\psi$. Then

$$\vdash \forall \alpha_0 \cdots \forall \alpha_{m-1} (\varphi \leftrightarrow \psi) \rightarrow (\chi \leftrightarrow \theta).$$
