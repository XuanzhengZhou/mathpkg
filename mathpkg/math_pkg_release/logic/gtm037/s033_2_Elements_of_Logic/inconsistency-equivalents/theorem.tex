For any $\Gamma \subseteq \text{Fmla}$ the following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\Gamma$ is inconsistent;
\item[(ii)] $\Gamma \vdash \varphi$ and $\Gamma \vdash \neg\varphi$ for some formula $\varphi$;
\item[(iii)] $\Gamma \vdash \varphi$ for every formula $\varphi$.
\end{enumerate}
