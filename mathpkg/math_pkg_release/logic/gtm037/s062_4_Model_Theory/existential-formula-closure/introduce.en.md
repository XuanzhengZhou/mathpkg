Proposition 21.22 is the existential dual of Proposition 21.21. It characterizes the set $\Delta$ of formulas obtained by closing atomic formulas and their negations under disjunction, conjunction, and existential quantification. Every formula in $\Delta$ is logically equivalent to an existential formula with the same free variables. The proof is analogous to that of Proposition 21.21, confirmed by the text's remark that it "is proved similarly."
