Let $\Delta$ be the smallest set of formulas containing all atomic formulas and their negations and closed under the operations $\vee$, $\wedge$, and $\forall v_i$ for each $i < \omega$. Then for each $\varphi \in \Delta$ there is a universal formula $\psi$ with $\operatorname{Fv}(\varphi) = \operatorname{Fv}(\psi)$ and $\models \varphi \leftrightarrow \psi$.
