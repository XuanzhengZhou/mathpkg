A basic formula in the language of Presburger arithmetic (with addition, order, and congruence relations) is an atomic formula of one of three forms: equality of terms, strict inequality of terms, or congruence modulo a positive integer. The third kind involves implicit quantifiers in the definition of $\equiv_m$, which is typical of quantifier elimination methods applied to theories beyond the simplest cases. These basic formulas serve as the building blocks for the quantifier elimination procedure in the theory $\Gamma_2$.
