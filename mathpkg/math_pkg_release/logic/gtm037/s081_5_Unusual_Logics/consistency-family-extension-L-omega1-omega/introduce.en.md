This lemma extends the consistency family machinery to $L_{\omega_1\omega}$. Conditions (C3') and (C4') handle countable conjunctions and disjunctions: if a countable conjunction is in a consistency set, each conjunct is added; if a countable disjunction is present, one disjunct is chosen. The closure under subsets preserves all the consistency conditions.
