Let $G$ be a frame for $\mathfrak{A}$, $\mathfrak{A}$ an $\mathcal{L}$-structure, $x$ an assignment over $A$ (i.e., $xP_i^\sigma \in G^\sigma$ for each type $\sigma \neq 0$, and $xv_i^0 \in A$ for each $i \in \omega$). Then

$$(\mathfrak{A}, G) \models \varphi[x] \quad \text{iff} \quad \mathfrak{A}_G^* \models \varphi^*[x].$$
