Let $\varphi$ and $\psi$ be sentences in an $L_{\omega_1\omega}$-language such that $\models \varphi \rightarrow \psi$. Then there is a sentence $\chi$ such that $\models \varphi \rightarrow \chi$, $\models \chi \rightarrow \psi$, and any nonlogical constant occurring in $\chi$ occurs in both $\varphi$ and $\psi$.
