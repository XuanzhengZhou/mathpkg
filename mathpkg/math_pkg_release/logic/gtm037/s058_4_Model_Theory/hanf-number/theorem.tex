If $R$ is a Hanf system, the \textbf{Hanf number} of $R$ is the least cardinal $m$ satisfying the condition of Theorem 19.28:

for all $\Gamma \in \mathrm{pr}_0^* R$, if there exist $\mathfrak{A}$, $n$ with $(\Gamma, \mathfrak{A}, n) \in R$ and $n \geq m$, then for every cardinal $p$ there is an $n \geq p$ and an $\mathfrak{A}$ such that $(\Gamma, \mathfrak{A}, n) \in R$.
