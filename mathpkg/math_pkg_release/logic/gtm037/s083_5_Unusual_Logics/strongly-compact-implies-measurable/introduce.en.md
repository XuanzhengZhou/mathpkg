Theorem 31.16 establishes that strongly compact cardinals are measurable, placing them above measurable cardinals in the large cardinal hierarchy. Combined with earlier results that measurable cardinals are weakly inaccessible (Ulam), this shows: strongly compact $\Rightarrow$ measurable $\Rightarrow$ weakly inaccessible, with none of the reverse implications provable in ZFC.
