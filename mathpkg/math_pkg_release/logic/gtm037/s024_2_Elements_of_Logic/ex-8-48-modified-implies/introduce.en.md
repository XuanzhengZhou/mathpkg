**Exercise 8.48** explores what happens when the definition of the implication connective is modified to use prefix notation without parentheses: $\varphi \rightarrow \psi = \varphi \langle c \rangle \psi$. The exercise asks to show that with this modified definition, parts (iii) and (iv) of Theorem 8.5 (the unique readability theorem for sentences) fail. This demonstrates that the parentheses in the infix notation are essential for ensuring unambiguous parsing of sentential formulas.
