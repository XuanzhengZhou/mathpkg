**Exercise 8.49** asks to show that the three Lukasiewicz axiom schemas can replace the standard axiom schemas (A1)-(A3) of the sentential calculus (from Section 8.7). The Lukasiewicz schemas are: (a) $(\psi \rightarrow \chi) \rightarrow [(\chi \rightarrow \theta) \rightarrow (\psi \rightarrow \theta)]$, (b) $(\neg\psi \rightarrow \psi) \rightarrow \psi$, and (c) $\psi \rightarrow (\neg\psi \rightarrow \chi)$. The exercise includes a hint providing 13 intermediate schemas to prove in succession, ultimately deriving the original axioms from the Lukasiewicz schemas.
