A Horn formula is a formula $\varphi$ of one of the following forms:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] An atomic formula.
\item[(ii)] $(\psi_0 \wedge \cdots \wedge \psi_{m-1}) \rightarrow \psi_m$, where each $\psi_j$ is an atomic formula.
\item[(iii)] $\neg(\psi_0 \wedge \cdots \wedge \psi_{m-1})$, where each $\psi_j$ is an atomic formula.
\end{enumerate}
