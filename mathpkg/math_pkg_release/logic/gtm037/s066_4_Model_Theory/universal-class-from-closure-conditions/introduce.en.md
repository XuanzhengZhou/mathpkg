Corollary 25.8 states that the class of reducts (to $\mathbf{U}$) of reducts (to $\mathcal{L}'$) of models of a theory $\Gamma$ that includes the $\mathcal{L}$-closure conditions for $\mathbf{U}$, closed under substructures, forms a universal class. This result follows from Propositions 25.2 and 25.3, which characterize universal classes via closure under substructures and ultraproducts. The corollary is applied to construct a specific universal class $K$ studied in algebraic logic and semigroup theory.
