This corollary follows from Propositions 14.3, 14.6, 14.8, 14.10, and Theorem 14.11. It establishes that the recursively enumerable relations are exactly those that can be weakly syntactically defined in theory $R$. Together with Corollary 14.12, this provides a complete logical characterization of both recursive functions and r.e. relations through the notions of syntactic and weak syntactic definability.
