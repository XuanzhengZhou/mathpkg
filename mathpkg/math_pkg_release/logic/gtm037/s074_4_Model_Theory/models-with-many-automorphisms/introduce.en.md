This corollary of the Ehrenfeucht--Mostowski theorem shows that any first-order theory with an infinite model possesses models of arbitrarily large cardinality that are maximally symmetric. Specifically, for any cardinal $\mathfrak{m}$ at least as large as the number of formulas in the language, there exists a model of $\Gamma$ of power $\mathfrak{m}$ whose automorphism group has cardinality $2^{\mathfrak{m}}$, the largest possible size. The result is proved by combining the indiscernibles provided by Theorem 27.17 with the construction of a suitable linear order admitting $2^{\mathfrak{m}}$ automorphisms.
