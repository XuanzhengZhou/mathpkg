\textbf{Theorem 27.17 (Ehrenfeucht--Mostowski).}
Let $\Gamma$ be a theory with an infinite model, and let $\mathfrak{A} = \langle A, \leq \rangle$ be a simple ordering structure. Then $\Gamma$ has a model $\mathfrak{B}$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\mathfrak{A}$ is homogeneous for $\mathfrak{B}$;
\item[(ii)] any automorphism of $\mathfrak{A}$ extends to an automorphism of $\mathfrak{B}$;
\item[(iii)] $|B| \leq |\mathrm{Fmla}_{\mathfrak{B}}| + |A|$.
\end{enumerate}
