Using ultraproducts and unions, and assuming the generalized continuum hypothesis, show that in any language $\mathcal{L}$, any infinite $\mathcal{L}$-structure $\mathfrak{A}$ has an elementary extension of each cardinality $>|\mathfrak{A}|$.
