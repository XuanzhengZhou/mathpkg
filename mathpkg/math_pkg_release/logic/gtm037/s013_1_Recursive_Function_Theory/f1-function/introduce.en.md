The function $f_1$ maps a natural number $x$ to the product $\prod_{i \leq x} p_i^2$, where $p_i$ denotes the $i$-th prime number. This auxiliary function is used in the construction of Gödel numberings for sequences of words in the theory of Markov algorithms.
