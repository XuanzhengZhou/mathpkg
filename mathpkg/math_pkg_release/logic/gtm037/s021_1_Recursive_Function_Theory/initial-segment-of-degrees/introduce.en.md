An initial segment of $(D, \leq)$ is a downward-closed subset: whenever a degree belongs to $E$, all lesser degrees also belong to $E$. Initial segments play a central role in the study of the global structure of $D$, since many partial orders can be embedded as initial segments of the Turing degrees.
