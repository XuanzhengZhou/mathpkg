The poset $(D, \leq)$ of Turing degrees is not $\omega$-complete: any strictly increasing sequence $\alpha_0 < \alpha_1 < \cdots$ fails to have a least upper bound. This is another manifestation of the structural complexity of the Turing degrees and contrasts with the behavior of many familiar lattices.
