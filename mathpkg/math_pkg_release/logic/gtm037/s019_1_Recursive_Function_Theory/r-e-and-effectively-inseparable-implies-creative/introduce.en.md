This theorem provides a powerful method for establishing that an r.e. set is creative: it suffices to find another r.e. set such that the pair is effectively inseparable. By symmetry, the result applies to both sets in the pair. This result is extensively used in Part III of the book to show that various sets of Godel numbers of theorems are creative.
