This axiom system for modal logic extends classical propositional logic (M1-M3) with three modal axioms. M4 states that necessity implies truth, M5 expresses transitivity of the accessibility relation ($\Box\varphi \to \Box\Box\varphi$), and M6 captures the K-axiom that necessity distributes over implication. Together with the rule of necessitation, these axioms characterize the system known as S5, one of the most commonly studied modal logics.
