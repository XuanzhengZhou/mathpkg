To derive Proposition 10.93 from Lemma 10.92 it is necessary to use 10.91. The argument follows by applying Lemma 10.92 with $\Delta = \Gamma$ and noting that for sentences the provability condition yields the unconditional derivability $\vdash \neg\varphi_0 \lor \dots \lor \neg\varphi_{m-1}$, since the syntactic consequence $\Gamma \vdash \dots$ reduces to pure provability when $\Gamma$ itself supplies the disjuncts and the logical axioms close the derivation.
