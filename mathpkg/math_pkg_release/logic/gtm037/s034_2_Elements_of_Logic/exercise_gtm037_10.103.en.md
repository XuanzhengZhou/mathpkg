For any $\Gamma \subseteq \text{Fmla}$, let $\Gamma\text{-Thm}^{m}$ be the intersection of all sets $\Delta \subseteq \text{Fmla}$ such that $\Gamma \cup \text{Axm}^{m} \subseteq \Delta$ and $\Delta$ is closed under detachment.

The axioms $\text{Axm}^{m}$ include:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(e)] $[[\varphi \rightarrow \forall \alpha \varphi]]$ if $\alpha$ does not occur free in $\varphi$;
\item[(f)] $[[\varphi]]$, $\varphi$ as in 10.23(4);
\item[(g)] $[[\varphi]]$, $\varphi$ as in 10.23(5).
\end{itemize}

For $\Gamma \subseteq \text{Sent}$, let $\Gamma\text{-Thm}^{vi}$ be the intersection of all sets $\Delta \subseteq \text{Sent}$ such that $\Gamma \cup \text{Axm}^{vi} \subseteq \Delta$ and $\Delta$ is closed under detachment. Show that $\Gamma\text{-Thm}^{vi} = \text{Sent} \cap \Gamma\text{-Thm}$.
