Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a first-order language in which $\mathbf{O} = 0$. An atomic formula $\mathbf{R}v_{i_0} \cdots v_{i_{m-1}}$ is \textit{standard} if $i_0 = 0, \ldots, i_{m-1} = m-1$; any atomic equality formula is standard. A formula is \textit{standard} if all of its atomic parts are standard. Let $\text{Fmla}^s$ be the collection of all standard formulas. Prove that for any formula $\varphi$ there is a standard formula $\psi$ with the same free variables as $\varphi$ such that $\vdash \varphi \leftrightarrow \psi$.
