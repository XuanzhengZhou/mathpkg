Every variety is closed under both the substructure operator $\mathbf{S}$ and the product operator $\mathbf{P}$. That is, any substructure of a member of the variety and any product of members of the variety again belong to the variety. This follows directly from the fact that identities are universal closures of equations, and both universal quantifiers and equations are preserved under substructures and products.
