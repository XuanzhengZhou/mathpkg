The class operators $\mathbf{H}$ (homomorphic images), $\mathbf{S}$ (substructures), and $\mathbf{P}$ (products) satisfy certain inclusion relations when composed. Specifically, taking a substructure of a homomorphic image factors through a homomorphic image of a substructure; a product of substructures embeds into a substructure of a product; and a product of homomorphic images is a homomorphic image of a product. These inclusions are generally strict.
