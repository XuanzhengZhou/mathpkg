This lemma shows that the occurrence relation $R_0$ is elementary (first-order definable in arithmetic). The proof uses the concatenation function $\operatorname{Cat}$ to express that $a$ occurs in $b$ if and only if there exist words $x$ and $y$ such that $b = \operatorname{Cat}(\operatorname{Cat}(x, a), y)$. Since all quantifiers are bounded (by $n$, the Gödel number of $b$), the defining formula falls within the class of elementary relations.
