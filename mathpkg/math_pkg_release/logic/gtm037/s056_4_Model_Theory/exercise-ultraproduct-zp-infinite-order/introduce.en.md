This exercise demonstrates a striking application of ultraproducts to group theory. The ultraproduct of the finite cyclic groups $\mathbb{Z}_p$ over all primes $p$ with respect to a nonprincipal ultrafilter yields a group in which every element has infinite order, despite each factor group being finite cyclic. This illustrates how ultraproducts can produce infinite structures from finite ones.
