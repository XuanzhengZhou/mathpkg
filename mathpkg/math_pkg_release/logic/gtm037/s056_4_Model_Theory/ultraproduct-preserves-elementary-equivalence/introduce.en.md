This proposition establishes that elementary equivalence is preserved under the ultraproduct construction. If we take two families of $L$-structures that are pairwise elementarily equivalent, then their ultraproducts with respect to the same ultrafilter remain elementarily equivalent. The proof is a direct application of the basic theorem on ultraproducts (Los's theorem).
