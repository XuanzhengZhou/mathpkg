This exercise shows another application of ultraproducts to algebra. The ultraproduct of all finite prime fields $\mathbb{F}_p$ (as $p$ ranges over all primes) with respect to a nonprincipal ultrafilter yields a field of characteristic zero. This demonstrates how ultraproducts can "average out" finite characteristics to produce characteristic zero structures.
