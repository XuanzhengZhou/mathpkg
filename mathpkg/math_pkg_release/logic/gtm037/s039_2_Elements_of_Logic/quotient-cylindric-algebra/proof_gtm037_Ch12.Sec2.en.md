The proof of Proposition 12.17 is not present in the extracted section text. The construction follows the standard Boolean algebra quotient: elements of $\mathfrak{A}/I$ are equivalence classes $[a]$ modulo the ideal $I$ with operations defined via representatives. For the cylindric operations specifically, one defines $c_\kappa' [a] = [c_\kappa a]$ and $d_{\kappa\lambda}' = [d_{\kappa\lambda}]$, justified in accordance with Definition 9.17. The verification that these operations are well-defined and satisfy the $\mathrm{CA}_\alpha$ axioms, and that the canonical map $I^*$ is a homomorphism, proceeds by routine checking using the ideal properties (closure under $+$, downward closure, and closure under $c_\kappa$).
