If $I$ is an ideal of a cylindric algebra $\mathfrak{A}$, then the quotient $\mathfrak{A}/I$ inherits the structure of a $\mathrm{CA}_\alpha$, with operations defined on equivalence classes. The canonical projection $I^*$ mapping each element to its equivalence class is a surjective homomorphism, paralleling the relationship between Boolean ideals and quotient Boolean algebras.
