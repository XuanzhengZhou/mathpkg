An ideal in a cylindric algebra $\mathrm{CA}_\alpha$ is a Boolean ideal (closed under $+$ and downward closed) that is additionally closed under all cylindrifications $c_\kappa$. Ideals play the role of congruence relations in algebraic logic, enabling the formation of quotient algebras that correspond to strengthening a logical theory by adding axioms.
