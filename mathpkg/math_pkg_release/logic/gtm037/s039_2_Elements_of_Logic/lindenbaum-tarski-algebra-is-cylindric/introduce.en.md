The Lindenbaum-Tarski construction lifts the Boolean algebra of sentences (modulo logical equivalence) to a full $\mathrm{CA}_\omega$ by interpreting the cylindrifications $c_\kappa$ as existential quantifiers $\exists v_\kappa$ and the diagonals $d_{\kappa\lambda}$ as equalities $v_\kappa = v_\lambda$. This algebra, denoted $\mathfrak{M}_\Gamma^{\mathcal{L}}$, captures the complete algebraic content of first-order logic and is the central object linking model theory with cylindric algebra theory.
