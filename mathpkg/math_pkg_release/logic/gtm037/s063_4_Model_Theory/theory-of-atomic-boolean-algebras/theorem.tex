$\Gamma_{\text{At}}$ is the extension of $\Gamma_{\text{BA}}$ with the additional axiom
$$\forall v_1\{\neg(v_1 = 0) \rightarrow \exists v_0[\varphi_{\text{At}}(v_0) \land v_0 \cdot v_1 = v_0]\}.$$
Thus $\mathfrak{A}$ is a model of $\Gamma_{\text{At}}$ iff $\mathfrak{A}$ is an atomic Boolean algebra.
