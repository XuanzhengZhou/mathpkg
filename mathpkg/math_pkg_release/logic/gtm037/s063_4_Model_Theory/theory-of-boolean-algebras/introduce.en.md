$\Gamma_{\text{BA}}$ is the first-order theory whose axioms are the formal sentences corresponding to the algebraic axioms of Boolean algebras (as given in section 9.3 of the text). A structure $\mathfrak{A}$ is a model of $\Gamma_{\text{BA}}$ if and only if $\mathfrak{A}$ is a Boolean algebra. This theory serves as the base theory for studying model-theoretic properties of Boolean algebras and their expansions.
