Let $\varphi$ and $\psi$ be sentences such that $\models \varphi \rightarrow \psi$. Then there is a sentence $\chi$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\models \varphi \rightarrow \chi$ and $\models \chi \rightarrow \psi$;
\item[(ii)] every nonlogical constant occurring in $\chi$ occurs in both $\varphi$ and $\psi$.
\end{enumerate}
