This exercise provides an alternative proof of the Deduction Theorem using the fact that $\text{Ded}(A)$ is closed under modus ponens. The key lemma $p \Rightarrow r \in \text{Ded}\{p \Rightarrow q, p \Rightarrow (q \Rightarrow r)\}$ is essentially the internalization of modus ponens within the deductive system, corresponding to axiom schema $\mathcal{A}_2$.
