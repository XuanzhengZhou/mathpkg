Given that $n = \{x \in \omega \mid x \subset n\}$ and that $n \in \omega$, show that $n^+ = \{x \in \omega \mid x \subset n^+\}$. Hence prove for all $n \in \omega$ that

(a) $n = \{x \in \omega \mid x \subset n\},$

(b) $n \notin n,$

(c) for all $x \in n, n \not\subset x.$

(Here $a \subset b$ means $a \subseteq b$ and $a \neq b$.)
