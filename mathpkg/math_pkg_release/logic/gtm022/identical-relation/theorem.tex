An \emph{identical relation} on $T$-algebras is a pair $(u, v)$ of elements of the free $T$-algebra $F$ on the countable set $X = \{x_1, x_2, \ldots\}$ of variables. A $T$-algebra $A$ \emph{satisfies} the identical relation $(u, v)$ if $f_u = f_v$ as functions $A^n \to A$, where $n$ is such that $u, v \in F(X_n)$.
