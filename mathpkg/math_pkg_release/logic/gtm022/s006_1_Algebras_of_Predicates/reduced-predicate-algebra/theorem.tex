Let $\approx$ be the congruence relation on $\tilde{P}(V, \mathcal{R})$ from Definition 1.4. The reduced predicate algebra is the quotient
$$P(V, \mathcal{R}) = \tilde{P}(V, \mathcal{R}) / {\approx},$$
with operations defined on congruence classes $[w]$ by:
$$(\forall x)[w] = [(\forall x)w],$$
$$[w_1] \Rightarrow [w_2] = [w_1 \Rightarrow w_2].$$
