Let $w \in \tilde{P}(V, \mathcal{R})$. The depth of quantification of $w$, denoted by $d(w)$, is defined inductively by:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $d(F) = 0$, $d(r(x_1, \ldots, x_n)) = 0$ for every free generator of $\tilde{P}$.
\item[(ii)] $d(w_1 \Rightarrow w_2) = \max(d(w_1), d(w_2))$.
\item[(iii)] $d((\forall x)w) = 1 + d(w)$ for $x \in V$.
\end{itemize}
