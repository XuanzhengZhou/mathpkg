\textbf{Definition 1.6.} $\text{Pred}_g(V, \mathcal{R})$ is the logic with algebra of propositions $P(V, \mathcal{R} \cup \{\mathcal{I}\})$, valuations those arising from proper interpretations, and with proof of $p$ from $A$ in $\text{Pred}_g(V, \mathcal{R})$ defined as a proof of $p$ from $I \cup A$ in $\text{Pred}(V, \mathcal{R} \cup \{\mathcal{I}\})$.
