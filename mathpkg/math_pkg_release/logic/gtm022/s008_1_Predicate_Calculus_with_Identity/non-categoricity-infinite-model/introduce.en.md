This corollary follows immediately from the Löwenheim-Skolem Theorem: if $\mathcal{T}$ has an infinite model, it has models of different infinite cardinalities, and models of different cardinalities cannot be isomorphic. Thus no first-order theory with infinite models can be categorical in the absolute sense of having a unique model up to isomorphism.
