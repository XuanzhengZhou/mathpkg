\textbf{Axioms of Identity.} Let $\mathcal{I} \in \mathcal{R}_2$ be a binary relation symbol. The set of axioms of identity is $I \subseteq P(V, \mathcal{R})$ consisting of:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item $(\forall x) \mathcal{I}(x, x)$
  \item $(\forall x_1) \cdots (\forall x_n) (\forall y) (\mathcal{I}(x_j, y) \Rightarrow (r(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \Rightarrow r(x_1, \ldots, x_{j-1}, y, x_{j+1}, \ldots, x_n)))$,
  for all $r \in \mathcal{R}_n$, all $n$, and all $j \leq n$.
\end{enumerate}
