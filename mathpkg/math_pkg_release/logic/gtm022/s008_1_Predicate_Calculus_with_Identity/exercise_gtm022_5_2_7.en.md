Let $\mathcal{T} = (\mathcal{R}, A, C)$ be a first-order theory and let $(\alpha, \beta): (P(V, \mathcal{R}), V) \rightarrow (P(V, \mathcal{R}), V)$ be a semi-homomorphism such that $\alpha(A) \subseteq \text{Ded}_g(A)$. If $\mathcal{T} \vdash p$, prove that $\mathcal{T} \vdash \alpha(p)$.
