Let $X, Y$ be any two sets, and let $\varphi: P(X) \rightarrow P(Y)$ be a homomorphism of the (free) proposition algebra on $X$ into the (free) proposition algebra on $Y$. Let $w = w(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ be any element of $P(X)$ and let $A$ be any subset of $P(X)$. Put $a_i = \varphi(x_i)$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item If $A \vdash w(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$, then $\varphi(A) \vdash w(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$.
\item If $A \models w(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$, then $\varphi(A) \models w(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$.
\end{enumerate}
