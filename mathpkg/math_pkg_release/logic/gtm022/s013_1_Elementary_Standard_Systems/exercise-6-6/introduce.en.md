Exercise 6.6 asks the reader to prove that every non-standard real number is infinitesimally close to some non-standard rational number. This establishes that $*\mathbb{Q}$ is dense in $*\mathbb{R}$ with respect to the infinitesimal equivalence relation $\cong$.
