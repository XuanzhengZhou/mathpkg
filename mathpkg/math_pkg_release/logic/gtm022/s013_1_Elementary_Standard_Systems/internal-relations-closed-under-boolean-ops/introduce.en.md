Internal relations on an enlargement $\mathbb{S}^\sigma$ are closed under the Boolean operations of intersection, union, and complement. This follows from the fact that the complete theory $\mathcal{T}(\mathcal{O}^1(\mathbb{S}))$ proves that $\mathcal{R}_n^{(1)}$ is closed under these operations, and this property transfers to the enlargement.
