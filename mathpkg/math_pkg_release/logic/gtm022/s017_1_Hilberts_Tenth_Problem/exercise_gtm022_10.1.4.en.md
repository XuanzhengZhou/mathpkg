(Cf.\ Lemma 6.5 of Chapter IX.) Define the pairing function $p: \mathbb{N}^2 \to \mathbb{N}$ by

$$p(x, y) = \sum_{r=0}^{x+y} r + y.$$

Prove that $p$ is bijective, and hence show that the functions $\ell, r: \mathbb{N} \to \mathbb{N}$ given by $p(\ell(z), r(z)) = z$ are well-defined. Show that $p$, $\ell$ and $r$ are recursive.
