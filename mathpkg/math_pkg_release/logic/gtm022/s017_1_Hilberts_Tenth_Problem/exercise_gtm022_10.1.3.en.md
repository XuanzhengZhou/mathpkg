Prove that the functions $+(x, y) = x + y$, $\times(x, y) = xy$ and $c_k(x) = k$ ($k \in \mathbb{N}$) are recursive, by using Theorem 1.2. Deduce that every polynomial $P: \mathbb{N}^n \to \mathbb{N}$ with coefficients in $\mathbb{N}$ is a recursive function.
