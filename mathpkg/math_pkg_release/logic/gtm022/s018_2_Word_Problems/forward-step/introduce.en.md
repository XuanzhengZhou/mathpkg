In the semigroup associated with a Turing machine, a forward step replaces an occurrence of the left-hand side of a relation by its right-hand side within a word. This substitution corresponds to simulating one forward move of the Turing machine on the encoded instantaneous description. Forward steps preserve semigroup equivalence and are used to build computation paths from an initial configuration to a terminal one.
