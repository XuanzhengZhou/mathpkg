This exercise asks the reader to find polynomial expressions over $\mathbb{Z}_2$ for the logical connectives. Since $\mathbb{Z}_2$ has two elements, logical operations correspond to Boolean functions, which can be expressed as polynomials: $\sim x = 1 + x$,  \lor y = x + y + xy$,  \land y = xy$.
