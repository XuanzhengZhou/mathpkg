This exercise asks whether the two-element Boolean algebra $\mathbb{Z}_2$ is a free proposition algebra. The answer is no: a free proposition algebra must have the universal mapping property for all hBalgebras, but $\mathbb{Z}_2$ satisfies additional identities (such as  \Rightarrow (y \Rightarrow x) = 1$) that do not hold in all proposition algebras.
