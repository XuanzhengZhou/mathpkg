The Peano axioms are formalized in first-order logic as the theory $\mathcal{P}$. The induction axiom is an axiom scheme parameterized by formulas $\pi$. Within ZF set theory, the natural numbers satisfy $\mathcal{P}$ and are determined up to isomorphism.
