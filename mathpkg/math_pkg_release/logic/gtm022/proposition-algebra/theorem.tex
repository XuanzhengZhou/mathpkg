Let $T = \{F, \Rightarrow\}$, where $F$ is a $0$-ary operation and $\Rightarrow$ is a binary operation. The \emph{proposition algebra} $P(X)$ is the free $T$-algebra on the set $X$ of \emph{propositional variables}. The operations $\sim$, $\vee$, $\wedge$, $\Leftrightarrow$ are defined as abbreviations:
$$\sim p = p \Rightarrow F,$$
$$p \vee q = (\sim p) \Rightarrow q,$$
$$p \wedge q = \sim(p \Rightarrow \sim q),$$
$$p \Leftrightarrow q = (p \Rightarrow q) \wedge (q \Rightarrow p).$$
