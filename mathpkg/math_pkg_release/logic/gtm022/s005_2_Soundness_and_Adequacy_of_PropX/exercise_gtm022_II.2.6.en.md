Show that $p \Rightarrow r \in \text{Ded}\{p \Rightarrow q, p \Rightarrow (q \Rightarrow r)\}$. Hence show that if $p \Rightarrow q, p \Rightarrow (q \Rightarrow r) \in \text{Ded}(A)$, then $p \Rightarrow r \in \text{Ded}(A)$, and so prove the Deduction Theorem without giving an explicit construction for a proof in $\text{Prop}(X)$.
