A $T$-subalgebra is a subset of a $T$-algebra that is closed under all operations of the type $T$. This closure property ensures the subset itself inherits the algebraic structure. The collection of $T$-subalgebras is closed under arbitrary intersections, guaranteeing the existence of a smallest subalgebra containing any given subset — the subalgebra generated by that subset.
