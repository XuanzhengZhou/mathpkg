This exercise asks the reader to construct two distinct $T$-algebras that share the same underlying set $A$ but differ in at least one of their operations. It reinforces the definition of equality of $T$-algebras (Definition 1.9), demonstrating that the algebraic structure is determined not just by the carrier set but by the specific interpretation of each operation symbol.
