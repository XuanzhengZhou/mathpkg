Let $\{B_i\}_{i \in I}$ be a family of $T$-subalgebras of $A$, and let $B = \bigcap_{i \in I} B_i$. Take any $n \in \mathbb{N}$, any $t \in T_n$, and any $b_1, \ldots, b_n \in B$. Then for each $i \in I$, we have $b_1, \ldots, b_n \in B_i$. Since each $B_i$ is a $T$-subalgebra, $t_A(b_1, \ldots, b_n) \in B_i$ for all $i \in I$. Hence $t_A(b_1, \ldots, b_n) \in \bigcap_{i \in I} B_i = B$. Therefore $B$ is a $T$-subalgebra of $A$.
