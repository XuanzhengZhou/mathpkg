The axioms of $\operatorname{Pred}(V, \mathcal{R})$ are all elements of $\mathcal{A} = \mathcal{A}_1 \cup \mathcal{A}_2 \cup \mathcal{A}_3 \cup \mathcal{A}_4 \cup \mathcal{A}_5$, where $\mathcal{A}_1, \mathcal{A}_2, \mathcal{A}_3$ are as in $\operatorname{Prop}(X)$ and
$$\mathcal{A}_4 = \{(\forall x)(p \Rightarrow q) \Rightarrow (p \Rightarrow ((\forall x)q)) \mid p, q \in P, x \notin \operatorname{var}(p)\},$$
$$\mathcal{A}_5 = \{(\forall x)p(x) \Rightarrow p(y) \mid p(x) \in P, y \in V\}.$$
