Proof. By induction on the depth of quantification of $p$, using the filter axioms. For atomic formulas, the result follows from the definition of the ultraproduct relations. For implication, the result follows from filter properties (ii) and (iii). For universal quantification, the key step uses the ultrafilter property (iv) and the induction hypothesis. $\square$
