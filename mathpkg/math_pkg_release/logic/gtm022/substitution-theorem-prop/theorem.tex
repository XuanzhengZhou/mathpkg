Let $X, Y$ be any two sets, and let $\varphi: P(X) \to P(Y)$ be a homomorphism of the (free) proposition algebra on $X$ into the (free) proposition algebra on $Y$. Let $w = w(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ be any element of $P(X)$ and let $A$ be any subset of $P(X)$. Put $a_i = \varphi(x_i)$. Then
$$\text{if } A \vdash w, \text{ then } \varphi(A) \vdash w(a_1, \ldots, a_n).$$
