For any set X and any type T, a free T-algebra on X exists and is unique up to isomorphism. The proof constructs the free algebra inductively as the union of layers $F_n$, where $F_0$ contains generators and 0-ary operations, and $F_n$ contains compound expressions of increasing complexity.
