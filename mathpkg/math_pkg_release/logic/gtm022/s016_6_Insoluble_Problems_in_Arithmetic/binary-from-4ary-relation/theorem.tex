Let $\rho$ be a 4-ary relation on the non-empty set $S$. Put $S' = \{K\} \cup S^2 \cup S^4$. For $x \in S$, define $\Delta(x) = (x, x) \in S'$. Let $r$ be the binary relation on $S'$ consisting of those pairs $(a, b)$ for which at least one of the following holds:

\text{(i)} $a = (x, y), b = (z, t)$, where $x, y, z, t \in S$ and $x = y = z$ or $y = z = t$,

\text{(ii)} $a = (x, y), b = (x, y, z, t)$ where $x, y, z, t \in S$ and $\rho(x, y, z, t)$ holds.
