Proof. The standard definition of uniform continuity is: $(\forall \varepsilon > 0)(\exists \delta > 0)(\forall x, y \in U)(|x - y| < \delta \Rightarrow |f(x) - f(y)| < \varepsilon)$. Applying transfer and the characterization of infinitesimals, this is equivalent to the stated non-standard condition. $\square$
