A free $T$-algebra on $X$ is the most general $T$-algebra generated by $X$. It satisfies the universal property that any map from $X$ to any $T$-algebra extends uniquely to a homomorphism. The elements of a free algebra can be thought of as formal expressions built from the generators using the operations of $T$.
