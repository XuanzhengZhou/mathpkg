Let $\mathcal{T}$ be a first-order theory and $(M_i, v_i, \psi_i)$ ($i \in I$) be models of $\mathcal{T}$. Let $\mathcal{F}$ be an ultrafilter on $I$. Then the ultraproduct $\prod_{i \in I} M_i / \mathcal{F}$ is a model of $\mathcal{T}$, and for any formula $p(x)$,
$$(a^{(1)}\mathcal{F}, \ldots, a^{(n)}\mathcal{F}) \text{ satisfies } p(x) \iff \{i \in I \mid (a^{(1)}_i, \ldots, a^{(n)}_i) \text{ satisfies } p(x)\} \in \mathcal{F}.$$
