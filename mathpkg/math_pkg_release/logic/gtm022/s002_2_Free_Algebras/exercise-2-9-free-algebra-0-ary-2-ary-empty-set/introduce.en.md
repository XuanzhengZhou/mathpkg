Exercise 2.9 from GTM022 §2. The similarity type $T$ contains one constant (0-ary operation) and one binary operation, and the generator set $X$ is empty. The exercise asks to show that the free $T$-algebra $F$ on $\emptyset$ is countable. This follows from the inductive construction: $F_0$ contains the single constant, each $F_n$ is finite, and $F = \bigcup F_n$ is a countable union of finite sets.
