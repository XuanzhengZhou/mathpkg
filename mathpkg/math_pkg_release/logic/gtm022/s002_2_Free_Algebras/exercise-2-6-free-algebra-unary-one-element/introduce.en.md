Exercise 2.6 from GTM022 §2. The similarity type $T$ contains exactly one unary operation symbol. Let $X = \{x\}$ be a singleton set. The exercise asks to determine the cardinality of the free $T$-algebra $F$ on $X$. The answer follows from the inductive construction in Theorem 2.2: $F$ will be countably infinite, consisting of elements $x, t(x), t(t(x)), \ldots$ where $t$ is the unary operation.
