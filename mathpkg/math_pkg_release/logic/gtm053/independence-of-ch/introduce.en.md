The independence of the Continuum Hypothesis from ZFC is one of the greatest achievements of 20th century mathematical logic. Godel showed that CH is consistent with ZFC by constructing the inner model L of constructible sets. Cohen proved that the negation of CH is also consistent by inventing the method of forcing, which constructs outer models where CH fails. Together, these results establish that CH is formally undecidable from the standard axioms of set theory.
