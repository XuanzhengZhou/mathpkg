The algebraic closedness axiom schema asserts that every non-constant monic polynomial with coefficients in the field has a root. It is stated as an infinite set of first-order sentences, one for each positive degree $n$. Together with the field axioms, this schema captures the property of being algebraically closed in the first-order language $L_1\text{Ar}$.
