The theory $\text{ACF}_p$, of an algebraically closed field of characteristic $p > 0$, is given by the following axioms in the language $L_1\text{Ar}$ with the binary operations $+, \cdot$ and constant symbols $0$ and $1$:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[I.] Axioms of fields.
\item[II.] The axioms of algebraic closedness: for each positive $n \in \mathbf{N}$,
$$\forall y_1, \ldots, y_n \; \exists x \; (x^n + y_1 x^{n-1} + \cdots + y_n = 0).$$
\item[III.] The axiom of characteristic $p$:
$$\underbrace{1 + \cdots + 1}_{p \text{ times}} = 0.$$
\end{enumerate}
