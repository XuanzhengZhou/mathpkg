This combinatorial lemma is the heart of the Kochen-Specker proof that there are no hidden variables in quantum mechanics. The constructed graph encodes the geometric constraints of spin measurements on a spin-1 particle. The impossibility of a consistent 0/1 assignment to all 117 vertices (satisfying the orthogonality constraints) demonstrates that quantum-mechanical predictions cannot be reproduced by any classical hidden-variable theory.
