An infinitesimal in a nonstandard model ${^*\mathbf{R}}$ is an element that is either a positive infinitesimal $\alpha$ (satisfying $0 < \alpha < 1/n$ for all $n \in \mathbb{N}_{>0}$) or its negative $-\alpha$. The set $\mu$ of all infinitesimals forms a maximal ideal in the ring $B$ of bounded elements of ${^*\mathbf{R}}$.
