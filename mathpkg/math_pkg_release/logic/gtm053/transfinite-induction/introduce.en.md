Transfinite induction extends the principle of mathematical induction beyond the natural numbers to all ordinals. The proof consists of three cases: base case ($\emptyset$), successor case ($\alpha$ implies $\alpha+1$), and limit case (if true for all smaller ordinals, then true at the limit). This principle is the primary tool for proving statements about all ordinals.
