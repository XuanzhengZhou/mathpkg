For an embedding $h : A \rightarrow B$ of $L$-structures, $A$ is called an elementary substructure of $B$ and $B$ an elementary extension of $A$, written $A \preceq B$ or $A \preceq_h B$, if for any $a_1, \ldots, a_n \in A$ and any $L$-formula $P$,
$$A \vDash P(a_1, \ldots, a_n) \quad \text{iff} \quad B \vDash P(h(a_1), \ldots, h(a_n)).$$
