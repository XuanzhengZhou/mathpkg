The primary mappings define how the non-logical symbols of a first-order language are interpreted in a given set $M$. Constants are mapped to elements of $M$, operation symbols to functions on $M$, and relation symbols to subsets of $M^r$. Together with the interpretation of variables (via the interpretation class $\overline{M}$), these mappings determine the truth values of all formulas in the language under the interpretation $\phi$.
