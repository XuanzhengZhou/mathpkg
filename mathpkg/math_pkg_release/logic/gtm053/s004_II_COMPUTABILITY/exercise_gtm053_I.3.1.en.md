Find the open parenthesis corresponding to the fifth closed parenthesis from the end in the formula expressing "$x_1 = \langle y_1, z_1 \rangle$" (the Kuratowski ordered pair definition):

$$\exists y_2 \exists z_2 \left("x_1 = \{y_2, z_2\}" \land "y_2 = \{y_1, y_1\}" \land "z_2 = \{y_1, z_1\}"\right).$$
