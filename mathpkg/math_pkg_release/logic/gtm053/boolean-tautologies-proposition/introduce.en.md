This proposition generalizes the notion of tautology from two-valued to Boolean-valued logic. A classical tautology evaluates to the top element 1 in any Boolean algebra, not just in {0,1}. This fact is essential for the Boolean-valued approach to forcing, where formulas deducible from the theory must have truth value 1 in the Boolean-valued model. The proof reduces to verifying the basis tautologies using the Boolean algebra axioms.
