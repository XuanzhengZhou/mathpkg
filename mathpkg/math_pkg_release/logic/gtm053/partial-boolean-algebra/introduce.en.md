A partial Boolean algebra axiomatizes the algebraic structure of quantum-mechanical observables. In quantum mechanics, not all observables are simultaneously measurable; the compatibility relation formalizes this restriction. The prototypical example is the set of closed subspaces of a Hilbert space, where compatibility corresponds to commutativity of the corresponding projection operators.
