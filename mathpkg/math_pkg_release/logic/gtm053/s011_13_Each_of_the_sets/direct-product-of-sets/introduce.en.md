The direct product $X \times Y$ is the set of all ordered pairs whose first component comes from $X$ and second from $Y$. For $n$ sets, the product is defined by iterated binary products. While the binary product is not strictly associative --- $(X \times Y) \times Z$ and $X \times (Y \times Z)$ are different sets --- there is a canonical bijection between them, so they are routinely identified in practice.
