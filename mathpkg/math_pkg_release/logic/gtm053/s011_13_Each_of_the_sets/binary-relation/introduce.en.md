A binary relation is defined set-theoretically as a collection of ordered pairs. Its domain $\operatorname{dom}(r)$ consists of all first components, and its range $\operatorname{rng}(r)$ consists of all second components. This definition unifies the notions of correspondences, graphs, and relations under the single primitive of set membership.
