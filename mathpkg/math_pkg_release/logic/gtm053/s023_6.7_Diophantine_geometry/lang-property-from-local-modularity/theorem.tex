Let $\Delta$ be a strongly minimal group definable in some expansion of a field $K$ (e.g., a differentially closed field or a difference field). Suppose that $\Delta$ satisfies condition (ii) of Section 6.4, i.e., $\Delta$ is locally projective. Then $\Delta^n$, and hence any subgroup $\Gamma \subseteq \Delta^n$, has the Lang property.
