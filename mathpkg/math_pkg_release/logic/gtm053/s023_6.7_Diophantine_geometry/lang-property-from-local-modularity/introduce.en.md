This corollary states that if a strongly minimal group $\Delta$ definable in an expansion of a field is locally projective (condition (ii) of the trichotomy), then the Cartesian power $\Delta^n$ possesses the Lang property. The result follows directly from the Hrushovski-Pillay coset decomposition theorem, since the definable subsets of $\Delta^n$ being finite unions of cosets of definable subgroups implies the defining condition of the Lang property.
