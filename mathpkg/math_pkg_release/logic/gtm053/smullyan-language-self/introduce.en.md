Smullyan's language SELF is a minimal formal language designed to study self-reference phenomena. Its extremely limited vocabulary (a display operator E, a relation symbol r, and quotation marks) is sufficient to generate self-referential statements. The expression E * E* is a name for itself, enabling liar-paradox-style constructions.
