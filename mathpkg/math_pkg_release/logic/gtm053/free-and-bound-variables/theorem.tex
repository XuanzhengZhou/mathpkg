\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Every occurrence of a variable in an atomic formula or term is free.
\item[(b)] Every occurrence of a variable in $\neg(P)$ or in $(P_1) * (P_2)$ (where $*$ is any connective) is free (resp. bound) if and only if the corresponding occurrence in $P, P_1$, or $P_2$ is free (resp. bound).
\item[(c)] Every occurrence of the variable $x$ in $\forall x(P)$ and $\exists x(P)$ is bound. The occurrences of other variables in $\forall x(P)$ and $\exists x(P)$ remain the same as in $P$.
\end{enumerate}
