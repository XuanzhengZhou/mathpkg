A subset of $F^n$ (for an algebraically closed field $F$) is called 	ext{constructible} if it is representable as a Boolean combination of zero-sets of polynomials. By Chevalley's theorem (a consequence of Tarski's quantifier elimination), the family of $L_1\mathrm{Ar}$-definable subsets of $F^n$ coincides with the family of constructible subsets.
