A collection $P$ in a category $B$ is a family of objects $P(m, n)$, $m, n \geq 0$ in $B$, together with group homomorphisms $S_m \times S_n^{\text{op}} \rightarrow \text{Aut}_B P(m, n)$. This gives a left action of the symmetric group $S_m$ and a commuting right action of $S_n$ on $P(m, n)$.

A morphism of collections $f : P \rightarrow Q$ is a family of morphisms $f_{m,n} : P(m, n) \rightarrow Q(m, n)$ commuting with the action of symmetric groups.
