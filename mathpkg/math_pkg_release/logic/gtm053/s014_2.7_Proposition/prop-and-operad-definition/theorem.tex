(a) A PROP in a category $\mathcal{B}$ is a collection in $\mathcal{B}$, endowed with horizontal and vertical compositions satisfying:
(i) Associativity of vertical products; units for them in $\mathcal{E}(m, m)$.
(ii) Compatibility of vertical products with actions of symmetry groups.
(iii) Associativity of horizontal products.
(iv) Compatibility of horizontal and vertical products.
(v) Compatibility of horizontal products with actions of symmetric groups.

(b) An operad in a category $\mathcal{B}$ is a collection whose only nontrivial terms are $\mathcal{P}(1, n)$, endowed with a right action of $\mathbf{S}_n$ and vertical products satisfying (i) and (ii).
