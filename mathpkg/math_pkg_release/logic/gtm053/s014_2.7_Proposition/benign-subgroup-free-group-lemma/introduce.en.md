This lemma provides a crucial reduction step in Higman's embedding theorem. It states that if $R$ is a benign subgroup of a finitely generated free group $F$, then the quotient $F/\overline{R}$ (where $\overline{R}$ is the normal closure of $R$) can be embedded in a finitely presented group. The proof constructs an explicit HNN-extension of a product group.
