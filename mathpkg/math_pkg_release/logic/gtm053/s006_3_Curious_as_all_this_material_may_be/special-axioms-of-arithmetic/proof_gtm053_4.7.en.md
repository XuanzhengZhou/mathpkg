The proof is trivial and left to the reader in the original text. The axioms of addition, multiplication, and distributivity are verified directly in the standard interpretation of $\mathrm{L}_1\mathrm{Ar}$ (the natural numbers $\mathbb{N}$ with the usual operations). The induction axioms are true because the standard interpretation satisfies the usual principle of mathematical induction; however, the "proof" that the induction axioms are true itself uses induction.
