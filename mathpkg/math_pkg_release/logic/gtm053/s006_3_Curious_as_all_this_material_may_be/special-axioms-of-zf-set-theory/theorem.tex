The following formulas are true in the standard interpretation of $\mathrm{L}_1\mathrm{Set}$ in the von Neumann universe $V$:

\textbf{(a) Axiom of the empty set:}
$$\forall x \; \neg (x \in \varnothing).$$

\textbf{(b) Axiom of extensionality:}
$$\forall z (z \in x \Leftrightarrow z \in y) \Leftrightarrow x = y.$$

\textbf{(c) Axiom of pairing:}
$$\forall u \forall w \exists x \forall z (z \in x \Leftrightarrow z = u \vee z = w).$$

\textbf{(d) Axiom of the union:}
$$\forall x \exists y \forall u (\exists z (u \in z \wedge z \in x) \Leftrightarrow u \in y).$$

\textbf{(e) Axiom of the power set:}
$$\forall x \exists y \forall z (z \subset x \Leftrightarrow z \in y),$$
where $z \subset x$ abbreviates $\forall u (u \in z \Rightarrow u \in x)$.

\textbf{(f) Axiom of regularity:}
$$\forall x (\neg x = \varnothing \Rightarrow \exists y (y \in x \wedge y \cap x = \varnothing)),$$
where $y \cap x = \varnothing$ abbreviates $\neg \exists z (z \in y \wedge z \in x)$.
