Let, for an ordinal $\kappa$,

$$A_0 \preceq A_1 \preceq \cdots \preceq A_\alpha \preceq \cdots \quad (\alpha < \kappa)$$

be a $\kappa$-sequence of $L$-structures forming a chain with respect to elementary embeddings. For limit ordinals $\delta \leq \kappa$, define $A_\delta$ as follows:

- the domain $A_\delta = \bigcup_{\alpha < \delta} A_\alpha$,
- predicates and functions are interpreted by taking the union of their interpretations in the $A_\alpha$.

Such a chain is called an elementary chain.
