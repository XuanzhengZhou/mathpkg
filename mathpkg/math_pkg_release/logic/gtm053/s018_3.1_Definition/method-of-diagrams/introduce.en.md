The Method of Diagrams reduces semantic relations between structures (embeddings and elementary embeddings) to syntactic satisfaction. An expansion of $B$ to $L_A$ satisfies $\text{Diag}(A)$ exactly when $A$ embeds into $B$, and satisfies $\text{CDiag}(A)$ exactly when $A$ is elementarily embeddable in $B$. This is a fundamental tool in model theory — it converts geometric/structural questions into questions about theories.
