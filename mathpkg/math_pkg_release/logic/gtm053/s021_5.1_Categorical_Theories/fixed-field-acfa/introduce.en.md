The fixed field of a difference field is the subfield of elements fixed by the automorphism $\sigma$. In the theory ACFA, it plays exactly the same structural role as the field of constants in DCF$_0$: it is a definable subfield of SU-rank 1 (the simple-theory analogue of Morley rank 1, i.e., strong minimality). The similarities between ACFA and DCF$_0$ are extensive, with the fixed field serving as the natural parameter space for analyzing definable sets via orthogonality and internality.
