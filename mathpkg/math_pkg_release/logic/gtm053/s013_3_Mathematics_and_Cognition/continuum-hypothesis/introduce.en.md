The Continuum Hypothesis (CH) states that the cardinality of the continuum is exactly $\aleph_1$, i.e., $2^{\aleph_0} = \aleph_1$. The Generalized Continuum Hypothesis (GCH) asserts that for every infinite cardinal $\kappa$, the power set cardinal $2^\kappa$ is the smallest cardinal strictly greater than $\kappa$.
