For every $m \geqslant 0$, there exist:
(a) A versal family of $m$-sets: an enumerable family $\{E_k\}$ of subsets of $(\mathbb{Z}^+)^m$ such that every enumerable $m$-set occurs as some $E_k$.
(b) A versal family of $m$-functions: an enumerable family $\{f_k\}$ of partial functions such that every partial recursive $m$-function occurs as some $f_k$.
