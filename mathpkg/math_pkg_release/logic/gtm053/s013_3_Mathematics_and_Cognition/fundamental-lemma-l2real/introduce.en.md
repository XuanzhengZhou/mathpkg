The Fundamental Lemma establishes four key properties of the Boolean-valued interpretation of L2Real: truth is preserved under deduction rules, logical axioms are true, the special axioms of real numbers (field axioms, order axioms, completeness) are true, and the Continuum Hypothesis is not true when the index set I has cardinality greater than omega_1.
