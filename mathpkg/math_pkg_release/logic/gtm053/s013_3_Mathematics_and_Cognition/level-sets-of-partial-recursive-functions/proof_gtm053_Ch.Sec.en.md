(a) $\subset$ (c): If $E = D(f)$, then $E$ is the 1-level of $1^{(1)} \circ f$.
(b) $=$ (c): The $m$-level of $f$ coincides with the 1-level of $(f - m)^2 + 1$.
(c) $\subset$ (a): If $E$ is the 1-level of $f$, set $g(x) = \min\{y \mid (f(x) - 1)^2 + y = 1\}$; then $E = D(g)$.
