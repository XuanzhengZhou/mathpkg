The axiom of extensionality
$$x = y \Leftrightarrow \forall z (z \in x \Leftrightarrow z \in y)$$
is "true" in $V^B$, i.e., $\|X = Y\| = \bigwedge_{Z \in V^B} (\|Z \in X\|' \vee \|Z \in Y\|) \wedge \bigwedge_{Z \in V^B} (\|Z \in Y\|' \vee \|Z \in X\|)$. Equivalently, $\|X = Y\| = 1$ if and only if for all $Z \in V^B$, $\|Z \in X\| = \|Z \in Y\|$.
