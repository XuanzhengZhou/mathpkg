This lemma establishes the fundamental computability properties of free groups -- the natural setting for recursion-theoretic combinatorial group theory. Reduced words form a decidable set, group operations are computable, and subgroups defined by enumerable generating sets are enumerable. These facts underpin the theory of recursive groups and the algorithmic study of group presentations.
