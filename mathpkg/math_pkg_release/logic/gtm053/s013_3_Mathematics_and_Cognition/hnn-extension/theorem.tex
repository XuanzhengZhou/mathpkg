Given two embeddings of groups $\alpha, \beta: A \to G$, the HNN-extension of $G$ relative to $A, \alpha, \beta$ is the group
$$K = \langle G \cup \{t\} \mid t^{-1} \alpha(a)t = \beta(a) \text{ for all } a \in A \rangle.$$
That is, $K$ is obtained from $G$ by adding a new generator $t$ (the "stable letter") and relations conjugating $\alpha(A)$ to $\beta(A)$.

Proposition: The natural maps $G \to K$ and $G *_A t^{-1}Gt \to K$ are embeddings.
