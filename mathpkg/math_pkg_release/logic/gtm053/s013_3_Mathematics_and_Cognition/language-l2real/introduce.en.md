L2Real is the second-order language of real numbers. It extends first-order logic by allowing quantification over functions $f: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$. The language includes variables for real numbers, function symbols, constants 0 and 1, operations $+$ and $\cdot$, and relations $=$ and $\leqslant$.
