The Correctness Lemma verifies that the function h-bar used to disprove CH in the Boolean-valued L2Real model satisfies the required technical conditions: measurability, the characterization of its zero set, and extensionality. These ensure h-bar belongs to the interpretation class and that its set of 'almost-everywhere zeros' has the correct intermediate cardinality.
