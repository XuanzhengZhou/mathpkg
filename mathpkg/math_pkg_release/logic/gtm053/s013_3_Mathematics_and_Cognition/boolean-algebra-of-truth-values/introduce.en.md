The Boolean algebra of truth values $B$ is the algebra of measurable sets in $[0,1]^I$ modulo sets of measure zero, equipped with complement, intersection, and union operations. It forms a complete Boolean algebra satisfying the countable chain condition, serving as the target for the Boolean-valued truth function in the simplified Cohen model.
