Every strongly minimal structure has an uncountably categorical theory — all models of the same uncountable cardinality are isomorphic. This is a cornerstone result in geometric stability theory: strong minimality provides a sufficient condition for uncountable categoricity, which in turn implies a well-behaved dimension theory via the associated combinatorial pregeometry. The result connects the local property of strong minimality to the global property of categoricity in power.
