This is the main theorem of the section, establishing the precise asymptotic behavior of the interval Green function. The normalized Green function converges to the kernel \(R(s,t) = \min(s,t) - st\), which is the Green function of \(-d^2/dx^2\) on \([0,1]\). This result unifies the discrete and continuous theories and enables the derivation of limit distributions for occupation times and exit times.
