For an aperiodic random walk with mean $\mu = 0$ and variance $0 < \sigma^2 < \infty$,
$$\lim_{N \to \infty} P_0\left[ \mathbf{N}_N(y) > N x \right] = e^{-\sigma^2 x}, \quad x > 0.$$
That is, the scaled occupation count converges in distribution to an exponential random variable with parameter $\sigma^2$.
