This decomposition formula expresses the interval Green function $g_N$ as the half-line Green function $g$ minus a correction term accounting for paths that exit the interval on the right before hitting the left half-line. The correction involves the right hitting probabilities $R_N(x,k)$ and the half-line Green function $g(N+k, y)$ evaluated at points beyond $N$. The proof is a direct application of the strong Markov property at the exit time $T_N$, and the formula is the key technical tool for deriving the asymptotic behavior of $g_N$ from the known asymptotics of $g$.
