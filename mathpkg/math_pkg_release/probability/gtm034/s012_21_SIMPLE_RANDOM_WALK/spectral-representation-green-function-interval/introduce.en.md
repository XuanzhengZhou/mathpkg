The spectral theorem for symmetric matrices expresses the Green function \(g_N = (I_N - Q_N)^{-1}\) and the hitting probabilities in terms of the eigenvalues and eigenvectors of \(Q_N\). Summation over the spectral decomposition yields explicit formulas for all quantities defined in D21.1.
