This identity relates the interval Green function \(g_N\) to the half-line Green function \(g\) and the exit probabilities \(R_N\). It decomposes the expected visits from \(x\) to \(y\) before leaving \([0,N]\) as the difference between the half-line expectation and the expected visits after exiting to the right.
