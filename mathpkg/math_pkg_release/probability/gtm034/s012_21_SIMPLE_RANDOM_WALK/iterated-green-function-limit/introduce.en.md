This proposition extends Theorem 23.T1 from the first power to arbitrary powers of the Green function matrix. It is the crucial technical step in the moment calculation for Theorem 23.T2: the $p$-th moment of $\mathbf{T}_N$ involves $g_N^p$, and this proposition provides the asymptotic formula needed. The proof proceeds by induction, writing $g_N^p$ as a $p$-fold matrix product and approximating it by Riemann sums of the iterated integrals of $R$. The uniformity of convergence is essential for ensuring that the limits can be interchanged with the summation over $y$ needed for the moment calculation.
