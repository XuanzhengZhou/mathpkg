The half-line Green function factors as a convolution of two sequences \(u\) and \(v\), which are related to the ladder height variables of fluctuation theory. This factorization is a direct consequence of the space-homogeneity of random walk (the transition function is a difference kernel) and is essential for obtaining sharp asymptotic estimates of the interval Green function.
