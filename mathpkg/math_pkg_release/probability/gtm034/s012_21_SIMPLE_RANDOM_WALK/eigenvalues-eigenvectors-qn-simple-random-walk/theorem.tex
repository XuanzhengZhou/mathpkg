For simple symmetric random walk on \([0,N]\), the eigenvalues of \(Q_N\) are
\[
\lambda_k = \cos\frac{k+1}{N+2}\pi, \quad k = 0,1,\dots,N,
\]
and the associated orthonormal eigenvectors are
\[
v_k(x) = \sqrt{\frac{2}{N+2}} \sin\frac{k+1}{N+2}\pi(x+1), \quad k,x = 0,1,\dots,N,
\]
so that \(Q_N v_k = \lambda_k v_k\) and \((v_k, v_m) = \delta(k,m)\).
