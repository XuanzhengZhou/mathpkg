Let $Q(x,y)$ be a transient kernel on $S$ such that $g(x,y) > 0$ for $x,y$ in $S$, and suppose that for some point $\xi$ in $S$

$$\lim_{|y| \to \infty} \frac{g(x,y)}{g(\xi,y)} = f(x) < \infty$$

exists for all $x$ in $S$. Suppose further that $f(x)$ is $Q$-regular.$^{10}$ Then the positive multiples of $f(x)$ are the only $Q$-regular function.
