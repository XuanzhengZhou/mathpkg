Here and in problem 3 the random walk is one dimensional and aperiodic, with a characteristic function $\phi(\theta)$ such that

$$0 < \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{1 - \phi(\theta)}{|\theta|^\alpha} = Q < \infty$$

for some $\alpha$, $1 \leq \alpha \leq 2$. Show that all such random walks are recurrent. Prove that

$$\lim_{|x| \to \infty} P_0[T_{-x} < T_x] = 1/2,$$

where $T_x$ is the time of the first visit to $x$.
