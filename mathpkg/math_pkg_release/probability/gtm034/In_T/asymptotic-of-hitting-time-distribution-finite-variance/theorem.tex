For one-dimensional aperiodic recurrent random walk with finite variance $\sigma^2 < \infty$, let $T = \min\{n \mid n \geq 1, x_n = 0\}$ and let $a(x)$ be the potential kernel. Then for every $x \neq 0$,

$$P_x[T > n] \sim a(x) P_0[T > n] \sim a(x) \sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi n}} \, \sigma, \quad \text{as } n \to \infty.$$
