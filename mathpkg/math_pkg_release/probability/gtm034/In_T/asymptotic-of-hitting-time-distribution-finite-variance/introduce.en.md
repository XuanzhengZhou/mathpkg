Proposition P4 strengthens T1 for the one-dimensional recurrent case with finite variance, giving the exact asymptotic constants. The result is obtained by combining the generating-function relation from P2 ($\lim_{t \to 1} \sqrt{1-t} \sum t^n P_x[T > n] = \sigma\sqrt{2}\,a(x)$) with Karamata's theorem, yielding the sharp asymptotic $P_x[T > n] \sim a(x)\sigma\sqrt{2/(\pi n)}$.
