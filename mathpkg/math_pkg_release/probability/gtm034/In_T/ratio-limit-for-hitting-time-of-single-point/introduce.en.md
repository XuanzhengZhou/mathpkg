This theorem describes the asymptotic ratio of the survival probabilities $P_x[T > n]/P_0[T > n]$ for the hitting time $T$ of the origin in an aperiodic random walk of arbitrary dimension. The limit is expressed in terms of the potential kernel via the sum of differences $P_k(0,0) - P_k(x,0)$. This result unifies the transient and recurrent cases, including the difficult one-dimensional recurrent case with infinite variance.
