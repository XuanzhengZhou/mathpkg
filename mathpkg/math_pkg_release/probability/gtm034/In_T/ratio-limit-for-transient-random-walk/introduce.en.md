Proposition P1 establishes the ratio limit for transient random walks. The proof uses only the methods of Section 1: the generating function identity relating $F_n(x,0)$ to $P_n(x,0)$ and $P_n(0,0)$, and passage to the limit $t \to 1$ via Abel's theorem. The limit is expressed in terms of the potential kernel $a(x) = G(0,0) - G(x,0)$.
