Proposition P8 establishes the key difference inequality $|q_n - \tilde{q}_{n+1}| \leq \epsilon_1 q_n$, relating the excursion-modified return probability at time $n$ to the shifted version at $n+1$. The proof decomposes $q_n$ and $\tilde{q}_{n+1}$ according to values of $S_k$ and the last time $k+j$ when $S_{k+j} = S_k$, then bounds differences $|c_{k,j+1} - c_{k,j}|$ of the conditional return probabilities.
