For aperiodic one-dimensional random walk with mean $\mu = 0$ and variance $\sigma^2 < \infty$,

(a) $$g(x,y) = \sum_{k=0}^{\min(x,y)} u(x-k)v(y-k), \quad x \geq 0, \quad y \geq 0,$$

and

(b) $$\lim_{x \to +\infty} u(x) = u > 0, \quad \lim_{x \to +\infty} v(x) = v > 0,$$

where

$$uv = \frac{2}{\sigma^2}.$$
