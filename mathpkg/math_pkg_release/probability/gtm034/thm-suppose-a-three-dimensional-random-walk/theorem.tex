Suppose a three-dimensional random walk satisfies conditions (a) through (c) in P1. Given an infinite set $A$, let $A_n$ denote the intersection of $A$ with the spherical shell of points $x$ such that $2^n \leq |x| < 2^{n+1}$, and let $C(A_n)$ be the capacity of $A_n$, defined in D25.3. Then $A$ is recurrent if and only if

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{C(A_n)}{2^n} = \infty.$$
