Suppose that $\mu = 0$ and $\sigma^2 < \infty$. Then

$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{E}[\mathbf{S}_n; \mathbf{S}_n \geq 0] = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{2\pi}},$$

$$\lim_{t \to 1} \sqrt{1 - t} \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{t^n}{n} \mathbf{E}[\mathbf{S}_n; \mathbf{S}_n \geq 0] = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{2}}.$$
