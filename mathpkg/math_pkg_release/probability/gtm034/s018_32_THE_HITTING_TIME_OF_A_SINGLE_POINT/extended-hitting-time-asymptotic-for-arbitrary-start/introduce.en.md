Proposition P4 combines the Abel limit from P2 with the asymptotic from P3 to extend the result to arbitrary starting points. Applying Karamata's theorem to the Abel sum $\sum t^n P_x[T > n]$, which P2 relates to $a(x) \sum t^n P_0[T > n]$, yields the complete asymptotic $P_x[T > n] \sim a(x) P_0[T > n]$ for the finite variance case.
