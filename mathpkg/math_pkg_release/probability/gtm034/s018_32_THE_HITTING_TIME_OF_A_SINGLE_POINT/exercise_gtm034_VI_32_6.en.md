For a one-dimensional aperiodic recurrent random walk, define
$$f(x) = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{P_x[T > n]}{P_0[T > n]}, \quad x \neq 0.$$
Show that either $f(x) \equiv 0$ for $x \neq 0$ or $f(x) > 0$ for all $x \neq 0$, and characterize those random walks which give $f(x) \equiv 0$.
