For aperiodic recurrent random walk there are two possibilities.

(1) The dimension $d = 2$, or $d = 1$ but $\sigma^2 = \infty$. In this case

$$\lim_{|x| \to \infty} [a(x + y) - a(x)] = 0, \quad y \in R.$$

(2) The dimension $d = 1$ and $\sigma^2 < \infty$, in which case

$$\lim_{x \to \pm \infty} [a(x + y) - a(x)] = \pm \frac{y}{\sigma^2}, \quad y \in R.$$
