$$g_N(x,y) = E_z \sum_{k=0}^{T_N-1} \delta(y,x_k) = E_z \sum_{k=0}^{T-1} \delta(y,x_k) - E_z \sum_{k=T_N}^{T-1} \delta(y,x_k)$$

$$= g(x,y) - E_z \left[ E_{x_T_N} \sum_{k=0}^{T-1} \delta(y,x_k) \right] = g(x,y) - E_z g(x_T_N,y)$$

$$= g(x,y) - \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} R_N(x,k)g(N+k,y).$$
