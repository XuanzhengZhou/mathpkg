The potential kernel \(a(x)\) of an aperiodic recurrent random walk is strictly positive for all nonzero \(x\), except for left- or right-continuous random walks with infinite variance, where it may vanish on a half-line. This positivity is essential for the potential-theoretic interpretation of \(a(x)\) as the expected difference in visits.
