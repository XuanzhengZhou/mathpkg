Definition 2-5 formalizes a denumerable stochastic process. The increasing Borel fields $\mathcal{B}_n$ represent information available up to time $n$, and the condition on conditional probabilities ensures that the present ($\mathcal{B}_n$) is not influenced by the future ($x_{n+1}$).
