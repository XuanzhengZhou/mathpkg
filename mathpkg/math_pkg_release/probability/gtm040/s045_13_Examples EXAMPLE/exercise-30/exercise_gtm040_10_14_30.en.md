Prove that for any transient chain,

$$\frac{{}_k L_{ij}}{H_{ij}} \leq \frac{{}_k L_{ij}}{N_{i0}} \cdot \frac{N_{00}}{P_{0j}^{(n)}}$$

for any $n$ with $P_{0j}^{(n)} > 0$, where ${}_k L_{ij}$ denotes the expected number of visits to $j$ before hitting the set $\{0,1,\ldots,k-1\}$.
