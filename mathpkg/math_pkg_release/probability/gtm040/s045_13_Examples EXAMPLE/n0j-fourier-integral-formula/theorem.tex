For the symmetric random walk in three dimensions, the potential kernel $N_{0j}$ admits the integral representation

$$N_{0j} = 3 \int_{-1/2}^{1/2} \int_{-1/2}^{1/2} \int_{-1/2}^{1/2} \frac{e^{-2\pi i(j \cdot x)} \, dx_1 \, dx_2 \, dx_3}{3 - \cos 2\pi x_1 - \cos 2\pi x_2 - \cos 2\pi x_3},$$

where $x = (x_1, x_2, x_3)$ and $j \cdot x = j_1 x_1 + j_2 x_2 + j_3 x_3$.
