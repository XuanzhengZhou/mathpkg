This theorem provides the leading-order asymptotic behavior of the potential kernel $N_{0j}$ for the three-dimensional symmetric random walk. It shows that $N_{0j}$ decays like $3/(2\pi|j|)$, which is the Green's function asymptotics for the continuous Laplacian in $\mathbb{R}^3$. The proof involves rewriting the Fourier integral representation, isolating the singular part of the integrand near the origin, and analyzing the Fourier transform of the resulting terms using distribution theory and estimates of homogeneous harmonic polynomials.
