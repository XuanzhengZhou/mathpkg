For the integer random walk with $p_{-1} = \frac{2}{9}$, $p_1 = \frac{3}{9}$, $p_2 = \frac{4}{9}$, construct extended chains representing the two minimal measures of Problem 10 (in the sense of Theorem 10-9). For each chain, verify that

$$\Pr[z_u \in C] = \int_C p(x) \, d\mu^v(x).$$
