Define $f$ by $f_j = \delta_{j0}$ (unit mass at state $0$). If an $\alpha > 0$ exists with $\alpha P = \alpha$ (a positive regular measure for the reversed chain), show that there is a point $x_0$ of the completed space $*S$ for which $J(x_0, \cdot)$ is a minimal entrance measure. Choose $E$ to be the set of states in some Cauchy sequence converging to $x_0$ and use the results of the preceding problems.
