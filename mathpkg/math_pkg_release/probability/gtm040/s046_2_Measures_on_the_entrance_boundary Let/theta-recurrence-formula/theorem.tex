If $E$ is any subset of $S$ containing $0$ and if $\nu$ satisfies conditions (1), (2), and (3) for an admissible row vector, then
$$\nu_E (I - P^E) = \theta^E_E - \delta_0,$$
where $P^E$ is the substochastic matrix restricted to $E$, $\nu_E$ is the restriction of $\nu$ to $E$, and $\theta^E_E$ is the restriction of $\theta^E$ to $E$.
