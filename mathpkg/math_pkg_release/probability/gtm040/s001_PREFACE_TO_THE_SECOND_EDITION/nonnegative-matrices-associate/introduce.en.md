This proposition establishes that non-negative matrices are associative under multiplication. The proof reduces the triple product to a double sum of non-negative terms and applies Lemma 1-3 on the interchange of limits for nondecreasing double sequences. This result is fundamental: it ensures that products of non-negative matrices (which include transition probability matrices of Markov chains) can be manipulated algebraically without worrying about order of operations.
