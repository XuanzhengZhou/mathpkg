This corollary states that finite matrices with finite entries always associate under multiplication. Since the index sets are finite and all entries are finite, the product of absolute values automatically has finite entries, so Corollary 1-5 applies. This recovers the familiar associativity of ordinary finite-dimensional matrix multiplication as a special case of the general theory.
