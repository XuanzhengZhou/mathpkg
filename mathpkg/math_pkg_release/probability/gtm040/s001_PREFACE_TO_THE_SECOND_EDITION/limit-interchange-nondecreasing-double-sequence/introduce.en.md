This lemma states that for a double sequence of real numbers which is monotone nondecreasing in both indices, the order of taking limits can be interchanged. Both iterated limits exist in the extended real numbers (possibly infinite), and they are equal. This result is a key technical tool used to prove that non-negative matrices are associative under multiplication.
