By Proposition 9-110,
$$g \geq B^E g - (\alpha f){}^E d.$$
By the hypothesis $g_E \geq g'_E$ and by Lemma 9-112, $\alpha f' \geq \alpha f$. Hence
$$g \geq B^E g' - (\alpha f'){}^E d = g'$$
by Proposition 9-109.
