By Proposition 9-109, $g = B^E g - (\alpha f){}^E d$. Since $\alpha f$ and ${}^E d$ are both non-negative, we have $g \leq B^E g$. The operator $B^E$ is sub-Markov with support in $E$, hence $g_i \leq \max_{k \in E} g_k$ for all $i$.
