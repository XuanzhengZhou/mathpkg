The $h$-process (Doob transform) modifies the transition probabilities of $P$ by weighting with a superregular function $h$: $P_{ij}^h = P_{ij} h_j / h_i$. When $h$ is harmonic, this conditions the chain to exit the state space at points where $h$ is concentrated.
