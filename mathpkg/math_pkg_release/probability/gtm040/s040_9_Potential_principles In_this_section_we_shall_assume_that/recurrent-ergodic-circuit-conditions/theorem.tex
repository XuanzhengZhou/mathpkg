Let $P$ be a chain which represents an electric circuit. Then $P$ is recurrent if and only if: when terminal $0$ is kept at a unit voltage and all terminals outside a finite set $F$ are grounded, as $F$ increases to $S$, the current at terminal $0$ tends to zero. Furthermore, $P$ is ergodic if and only if $\sum_{i,j} c_{ij} < \infty$.
