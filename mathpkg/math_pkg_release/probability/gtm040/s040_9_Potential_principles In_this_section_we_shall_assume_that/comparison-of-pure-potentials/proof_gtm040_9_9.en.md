By hypothesis and by Propositions 9-109 and 9-110,

$$0 \geq \lambda^E(g' - g) \geq (-\alpha f')k(E) + (\alpha f)k(E).$$

Thus $(\alpha f - \alpha f')k(E) \leq 0$, and since $k(E) > 0$, we obtain $\alpha f' \geq \alpha f$.
