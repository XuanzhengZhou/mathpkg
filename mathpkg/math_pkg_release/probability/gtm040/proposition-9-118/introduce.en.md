Definition 9-119: If $g = -Kf$ is a potential, the energy of $g$, denoted $I(g)$, is

Lemma 9-120: For any potential $g$ with support in a set $E \in \mathcal{L}$, $I(g) = \nu_E(I - P^E)g_E + (\alpha f)(\hat{\lambda}^E g)$. If $P = \hat{P}$, then

$$I(g) = \nu_E(I - P^E)g_E - k(E)(\alpha f)^2.$$
