If $(\mathbf{f}_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ is a submartingale with the property that $\mathbf{M}[|\mathbf{f}_n|] \leq K < \infty$ for all $n$, then

$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \mathbf{f}_n(\omega)$$

exists and is finite for almost all points $\omega$.
