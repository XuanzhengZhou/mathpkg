Proposition 8-75 establishes the exact equivalence between the probabilistic notion of a supermartingale (or martingale) and the potential-theoretic notion of a superregular (or regular) function on the space-time chain. The additional finiteness condition on $P^k h$ ensures that the expectations involved in the martingale definition remain finite, satisfying Definition 3-5.
