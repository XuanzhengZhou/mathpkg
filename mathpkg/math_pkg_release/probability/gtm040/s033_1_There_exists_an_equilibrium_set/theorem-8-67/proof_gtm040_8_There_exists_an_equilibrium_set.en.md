For each $m$, ${}_m z$ is bounded. If one of the conditions applies to $z$, then it applies to ${}_m z$ because $0 \leq {}_m z \leq z$. Hence by Lemma 8-64,
$${}_m h = N {}_m f.$$
By Lemma 8-66, ${}_m f$ increases to $f$, and by monotone convergence ${}_m h$ increases to $h$. Therefore $h = Nf$ by the Monotone Convergence Theorem.
