Lemma 9-3 establishes fundamental ratio limit theorems for occupation times $N_{ij}^{(n)}$ in a Markov chain admitting a positive superregular measure $\alpha$. The limits $\lim_n N_{ij}^{(n)} / N_{jj}^{(n)} = H_{ij}$ show that the relative frequency of visits to $j$ starting from $i$, compared to starting from $j$, converges to the hitting probability. This is a precursor to the Doeblin Ratio Limit Theorem.
