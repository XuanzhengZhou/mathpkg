Lemma 9-10 provides a uniform (in $n$) bound for the difference between the occupation time $N_{ki}^{(n)}$ and a weighted version $N_{k0}^{(n)} \alpha_i/\alpha_0$. The bound ${}_0 N_{ii}$ is finite and independent of both $n$ and $k$. This is a crucial technical estimate used in the proof of Proposition 9-12.
