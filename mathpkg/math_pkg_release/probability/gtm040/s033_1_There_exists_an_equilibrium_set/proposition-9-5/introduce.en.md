Proposition 9-5 reveals a fundamental difference between transient and recurrent potential theory: in a recurrent chain, every charge must have total charge zero. This is a necessary condition for the defining limit $\lim_n \sum_k \mu_k N_{kj}^{(n)}$ to be finite. The proof uses the Doeblin Ratio Limit Theorem and dominated convergence.
