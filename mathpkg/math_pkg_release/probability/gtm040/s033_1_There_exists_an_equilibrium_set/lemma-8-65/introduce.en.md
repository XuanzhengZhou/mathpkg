Lemma 8-65 establishes an essential monotonicity property for truncated random variables in potential theory. When $z$ dominates its shift $z^{(1)}$, the difference $z - z^{(1)}$ dominates the difference of their $m$-truncations. This inequality is the key to extending results from bounded random variables to unbounded ones via truncation and limit arguments.
