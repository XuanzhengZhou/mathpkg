Proposition 9-12 is the bridge between the divergent occupation time sequences $N_{ij}^{(n)}$ and the finite excluded-state matrices ${}_0 N_{ij}$. When the total charge is zero, the divergent parts cancel in the weighted linear combinations, leaving a convergent limit expressed in terms of ${}_0 N$. This is the key mechanism by which recurrent potential theory manages the divergence of $\sum P^n$.
