Proposition 8-68 gives a recursive formula for computing the $r$-th moments of the absorption time in an absorbing Markov chain. Starting from a transient state $i$, the absorption time $a(\omega)$ satisfies a shift relation $a^r(\omega) = (a+1)^r(\omega_1)$, which yields a binomial expansion. Applying the fundamental matrix $N$ then produces the recursive formula expressing $a^{(r)}$ in terms of lower moments $a^{(1)}, \ldots, a^{(r-1)}$.
