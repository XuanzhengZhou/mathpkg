Write
$$\frac{N_{ij}^{(n)}}{N_{i'j'}^{(n)}} = \left[ \frac{N_{i'j'}^{(n)}}{N_{j'j'}^{(n)}} \right]^{-1} \left[ \frac{N_{j'j'}^{(n)}}{N_{jj'}^{(n)}} \right]^{-1} \left[ \frac{N_{ij}^{(n)}}{N_{ij'}^{(n)}} \right],$$
and apply Lemma 9-3 to each factor. The limits of the three factors are, respectively, $H_{i'j'}$, the reciprocal of a Doeblin ratio, and a ratio involving $\alpha$ and hitting probabilities. Combining these yields the stated formula.
