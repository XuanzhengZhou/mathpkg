**Proof:** From Proposition 4-15 we have
$$N_{ij} = H_{ij}N_{jj} \leq N_{jj}.$$

Since $j$ is transient, by Proposition 4-20 we have $N_{jj} < \infty$. Therefore $N_{ij} \leq N_{jj} < \infty$ for all states $i$. The formula $N_{ij} = H_{ij} N_{jj}$ follows from Proposition 4-15.
