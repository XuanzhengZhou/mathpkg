The $R$-relation defines a partial ordering on the state space of a Markov chain. State $j$ is reachable from state $i$ if the hitting probability $H_{ij}$ is strictly positive. This relation is reflexive (since $H_{ii} = 1$) and transitive (by the inequality $H_{ik} \geq H_{ij}H_{jk}$). Together with the communication equivalence relation $\sim$, the $R$-relation provides the foundation for classifying Markov chain states into recurrence classes.
