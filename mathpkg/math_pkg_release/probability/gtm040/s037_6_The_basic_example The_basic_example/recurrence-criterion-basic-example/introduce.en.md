The recurrence behavior of the basic example is entirely determined by the limiting value $\beta_\infty$ of the $\beta$ sequence. The chain is recurrent precisely when $\prod_{k=1}^\infty p_k = 0$, which corresponds to the case where the probability of never returning to 0 is zero. This clean criterion mirrors the classical recurrence criterion for birth-death processes: divergence of the series $\sum (\beta_{i-1}/(p_i \beta_i))$ translates to the condition $\beta_n \to 0$.
