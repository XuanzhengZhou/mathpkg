Let $h$ be defined by

$$h_i = \int_S K(i, x) d\nu(x),$$

where $\nu$ is a measure with $\nu(\bar{S}) = 1$. Then the $h$-process is well defined, and for any $A$ in $\mathcal{G}$

$$\Pr^h[A] = \int_S \Pr^{K(\cdot, x)}[A] d\nu(x).$$
