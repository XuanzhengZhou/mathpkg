For every extended chain with transition matrix $P$,

(1) $\nu$ is a superregular measure for $P_S$.

(2) $\nu(I - P_S) = \mu$, where $\mu = \lim_E \mu^E$ as $E$ increases to $S$.

(3) $\nu^E$ and $\mu^E$ are the balayage potential and charge, respectively, for $\nu$ on $E$.
