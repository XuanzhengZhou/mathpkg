A martingale is a stochastic process $(f_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ where $f_n$ is adapted to the filtration $\mathcal{R}_n$ (constant on cells) and satisfies the martingale property $f_n = M[f_{n+1} \mid \mathcal{R}_n]$: the current value equals the conditional expectation of the next value. This formalizes the concept of a fair game.
