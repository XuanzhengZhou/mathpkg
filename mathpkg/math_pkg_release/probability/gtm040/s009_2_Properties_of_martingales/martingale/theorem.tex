Let $\{f_n\}$ be a sequence of denumerable-valued random variables, and suppose $\mathcal{R}_0 \subset \mathcal{R}_1 \subset \cdots$ is an increasing sequence of partitions of $\Omega$. The pair $(f_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ is called a \textbf{martingale} if three conditions are satisfied:
(1) $M[|f_n|]$ is finite for each $n$.
(2) $f_n$ is constant on cells of $\mathcal{R}_n$.
(3) $f_n = M[f_{n+1} \mid \mathcal{R}_n]$.
