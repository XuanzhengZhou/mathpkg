When the filtration is generated by the random variables themselves, the martingale condition simplifies to $M[f_{n+1} \mid f_0 \wedge \cdots \wedge f_n] = f_n$. In this case we refer to the martingale simply as $\{f_n\}$, omitting explicit mention of the partitions.
