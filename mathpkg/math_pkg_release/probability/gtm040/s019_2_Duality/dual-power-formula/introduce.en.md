This proposition gives explicit formulas for computing powers of the dual matrix and the dual potential kernel. The $n$-step transition probabilities of the dual chain satisfy $\hat{P}^n = D(P^n)^T D^{-1}$, and the mean visit counts satisfy the analogous transpose relation. Moreover, the dual chain preserves the type of the original: if $P$ is recurrent, so is $\hat{P}$; if $P$ is transient with only transient states, $\hat{P}$ is of the same type, and the fundamental matrix satisfies $\hat{N} = D N^T D^{-1}$.
