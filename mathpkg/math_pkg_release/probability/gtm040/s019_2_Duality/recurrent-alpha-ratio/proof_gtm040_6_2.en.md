Since $P$ is recurrent, so is $\hat{P}$ (by Proposition 6-12). For a recurrent chain, all states communicate and the hitting probability between any two states is 1. Thus $\hat{H}_{ji} = 1$ for all $i, j$. Applying Corollary 6-20 with $\hat{H}_{ji} = 1$ yields

$${}^i \bar{N}_{ij} = \frac{\alpha_j}{\alpha_i} \cdot 1 = \frac{\alpha_j}{\alpha_i}.$$
