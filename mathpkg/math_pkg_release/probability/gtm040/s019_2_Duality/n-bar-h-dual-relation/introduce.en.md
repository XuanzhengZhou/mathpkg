This corollary specializes Proposition 6-19 to the case where the set $E$ is a single state. It gives a direct relationship between the expected number of visits from $i$ to $j$ before returning to $i$ (${}^i \bar{N}_{ij}$) and the hitting probability $\hat{H}_{ji}$ in the dual chain, weighted by the ratio $\alpha_j / \alpha_i$ of the superregular measure.
