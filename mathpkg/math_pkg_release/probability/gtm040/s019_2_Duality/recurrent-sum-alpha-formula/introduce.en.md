For a recurrent chain, the weighted sum of expected visits from states in $E$ to $j$ (weighted by the regular measure $\alpha$) simplifies to just $\alpha_j$. This follows from Proposition 6-19 and the fact that for a recurrent dual chain, the hitting probability $\hat{h}_j^E = 1$. This identity is a key tool used in later computations involving mean first passage times and provides a normalization property of the measure $\alpha$.
