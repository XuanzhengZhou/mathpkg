For the transient basic example, the regular functions and signed measures of both the forward chain $P$ and its $\beta$-dual $\hat{P}$ are completely characterized. The forward chain admits only constant regular functions and no non-zero regular signed measures, while the reverse chain admits only multiples of $\beta$ as regular signed measures and no non-zero regular functions. This characterization directly determines the structure of non-negative superregular functions for both processes.
