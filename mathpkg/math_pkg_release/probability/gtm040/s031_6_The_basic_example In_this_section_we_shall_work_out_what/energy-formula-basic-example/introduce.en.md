The energy functional $\mathbf{I}(g) = (f, g) = \sum_i f_i g_i$ for a potential $g = Nf$ in the basic example admits a closed-form expression in terms of the charge $f$ and the $\beta$ sequence. Substituting the explicit potential representation formula (1) yields a compact quadratic form in $f$. Remarkably, the same energy value is obtained using the reverse chain representation (2), reflecting the fundamental duality between the forward and reverse processes.
