On almost every path,

$$z_v(\omega) = \lim_{n \uparrow v} z_n(\omega)$$

exists in $S^*$. On almost every path for which the limit exists, either $v < +\infty$ and $z_v \in S$ or else $v = +\infty$ and $z_v \in B_e$. Moreover, for any Borel set $C$ in $S^*$,

$$\Pr[z_v \in C] = \int_C q^v(y) d\mu(y).$$
