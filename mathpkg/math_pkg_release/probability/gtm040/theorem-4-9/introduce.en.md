Let $\{x_n\}$ be a Markov chain with starting distribution $\pi$, let $t$ be a random time, let $q$ be a statement relative to $\mathcal{F}_t$, let $r$ be a statement relative to $\mathcal{T}_t$, and suppose $\Pr_\pi[q \wedge x_t = i] > 0$. Then

$$\Pr_\pi[r \mid q \wedge (x_t = i)] = \Pr_\pi[r \mid x_t = i] = \Pr_i[r']$$

where $\omega \in R$ if and only if $\omega_t \in R'$.
