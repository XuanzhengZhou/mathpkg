**Proof:**

$$m z - m z^{(1)} = \begin{cases} z - z^{(1)} & \text{if } z \leq m \\ 0 & \text{if } z^{(1)} > m \\ m - z^{(1)} & \text{if } z > m \geq z^{(1)}. \end{cases}$$

Define vectors $m h$ and $m f$ by

$$m h_i = M_i[m z]$$

and

$$m f_i = M_i[m z - m z^{(1)}].$$
