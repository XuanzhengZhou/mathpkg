Proposition 4-2 follows from Proposition 2-8 and the Markov property. For a Markov chain, the transition probabilities simplify to a single matrix $P_{ij} = \Pr[x_{n+1} = j \mid x_n = i]$, and the process is fully specified by the starting distribution $\pi_i = \Pr[x_0 = i]$ and the transition matrix $P$.
