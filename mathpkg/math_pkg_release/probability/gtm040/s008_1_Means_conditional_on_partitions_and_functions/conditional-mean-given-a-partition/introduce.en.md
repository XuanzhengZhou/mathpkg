Definition 3-1 introduces the conditional mean $M[f \mid \mathcal{R}]$ of a random variable $f$ given a partition $\mathcal{R}$ of the probability space. On each cell $R_j$ of the partition, the conditional mean takes the constant value $M[f \mid r_j]$, the mean of $f$ restricted to that cell.
