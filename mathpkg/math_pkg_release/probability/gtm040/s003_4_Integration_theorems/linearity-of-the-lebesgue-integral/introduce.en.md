This proposition establishes the additivity (and hence linearity, combined with scalar multiplication) of the Lebesgue integral. The proof first handles non-negative functions by approximating with simple functions and applying the Monotone Convergence Theorem, then extends to signed functions by case analysis on the signs of $f$, $g$, and $h$.
