This corollary translates the term-by-term integration of non-negative series (Corollary 1-46) to the matrix setting. It justifies interchanging a row vector measure with an infinite sum of non-negative column vectors, a crucial operation in the analysis of denumerable Markov chains where potential operators are expressed as infinite series of transition matrices.
