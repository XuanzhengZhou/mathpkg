This proposition restates the Monotone Convergence Theorem in the language of denumerable matrices, where non-negative row vectors act as measures and column vectors as functions. The matrix product $\pi f$ is precisely the Lebesgue integral of $f$ with respect to the measure $\pi$. This perspective is central to the book's treatment of Markov chains, where states are indexed by a countable set.
