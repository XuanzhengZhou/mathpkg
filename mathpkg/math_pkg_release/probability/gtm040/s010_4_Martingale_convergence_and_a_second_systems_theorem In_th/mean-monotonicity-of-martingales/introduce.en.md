This lemma records how the unconditional means evolve over time for martingales, supermartingales, and submartingales. For a martingale the mean is constant, for a supermartingale it is nonincreasing, and for a submartingale it is nondecreasing. The result follows immediately from Lemma 3-6 by taking the conditioning set to be the whole space $\Omega$.
