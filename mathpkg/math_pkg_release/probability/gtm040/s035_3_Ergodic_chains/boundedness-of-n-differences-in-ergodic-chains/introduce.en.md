Lemma 9-48 establishes that the difference $N_{jj}^{(n)} - N_{ij}^{(n)}$ is bounded by the mean first passage time $M_{ij}$ and converges to $M_{ij}\alpha_j$. This is a key technical estimate for potential theory in ergodic chains.
