Summing the powers in Lemma 6-34, $N_{ij}^{(n)} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} F_{ij}^{(k)} N_{jj}^{(n-k)}$ (with $N_{jj}^{(m)} = 0$ for $m < 0$). Since $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} F_{ij}^{(k)} = H_{ij} = 1$, the difference $N_{jj}^{(n)} - N_{ij}^{(n)}$ is a convex combination of differences of the form $N_{jj}^{(n)} - N_{jj}^{(n-k)}$, each bounded by $M_{ij}$. The limit follows from the renewal theorem for ergodic chains.
