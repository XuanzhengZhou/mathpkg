Multiply the equation of Lemma 9-94 on the right by $l^E$ to obtain
\begin{align*}
B^E(K l^E) &= K l^E + {}^E N l^E - {}^E d(\alpha l^E) \\
&= K l^E - {}^E d.
\end{align*}
But $(K l^E)_E = K_E l_E^E$, which by Proposition 9-87 equals $k(E) 1$. Hence
$$K l^E = k(E) 1 + {}^E d.$$
The other result is dual.
