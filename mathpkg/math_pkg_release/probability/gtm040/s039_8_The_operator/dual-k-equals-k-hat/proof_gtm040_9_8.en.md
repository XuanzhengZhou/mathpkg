Using Lemma 9-81 and the duality relations $\hat{G} = \operatorname{dual} C$ and $\hat{C} = \operatorname{dual} G$, we compute
$$(\alpha_j / \alpha_i) \hat{K}_{ji} = (\alpha_j / \alpha_i) \left[ \hat{G}_{ji} + (\hat{C}_{j0} - \hat{G}_{j0}) \frac{\alpha_i}{\alpha_0} \right] = C_{ij} + (G_{0j} - C_{0j}) = K_{ij}.$$
Thus $\hat{K} = \operatorname{dual} K$.
