For any sets $A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_r$ which have at least one point in common,
\begin{align*}
{}^{A_1 \cap A_2 \cap \cdots \cap A_r} N_{ij}
&\geq \sum_s {}^{A_s} N_{ij} - \sum_{s \neq t} {}^{A_s \cup A_t} N_{ij} \\
&\quad + \sum_{s \neq t \neq u} {}^{A_s \cup A_t \cup A_u} N_{ij} - \cdots \\
&\quad + (-1)^{r+1} \, {}^{A_1 \cup A_2 \cup \cdots \cup A_r} N_{ij}.
\end{align*}
