Starting from the definition,
$$K_{ij} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left[ N_{00}^{(n)} \frac{\alpha_j}{\alpha_0} - N_{ij}^{(n)} \right].$$
Adding and subtracting $C_{ij}$ and using $C_{ij} = \lim_n (N_{jj}^{(n)} \alpha_i / \alpha_j - N_{ij}^{(n)})$, we obtain
$$K_{ij} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left[ N_{00}^{(n)} \frac{\alpha_j}{\alpha_0} - N_{ij}^{(n)} \right] = C_{ij} + (G_{0j} - C_{0j}),$$
and similarly
$$K_{ij} = G_{ij} + (C_{i0} - G_{i0}) \frac{\alpha_j}{\alpha_0}.$$
