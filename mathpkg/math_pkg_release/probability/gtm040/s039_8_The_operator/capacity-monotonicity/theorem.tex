The capacity function $k$ is monotone: if $A \subset B$, then $k(A) \leq k(B)$. Furthermore, $k$ satisfies the Choquet alternating inequalities: for sets $A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_r$,
$$k(A_1 \cap A_2 \cap \cdots \cap A_r) \geq \sum_s k(A_s) - \sum_{s \neq t} k(A_s \cup A_t) + \cdots + (-1)^{r+1} k(A_1 \cup A_2 \cup \cdots \cup A_r).$$
