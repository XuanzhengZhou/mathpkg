This lemma provides a formula for the difference between capacities of two finite sets $E$ and $F$, both contained in a larger set $L$. The identity expresses $k(E) - k(F)$ in terms of the equilibrium distribution $\lambda^L$ and the escape quantities $^E d$, $^F d$, as well as a dual version. This result is crucial for proving the monotonicity of capacity (Proposition 9-98).
