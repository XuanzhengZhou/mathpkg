Lemma 9-37 proves that a column of $B^E - 1\lambda^E$ is a potential with charge the corresponding column of $W^E$. By the definition of potential, this means $(B^E - 1\lambda^E) = -\lim_n N^{(n)} W^E$. Since $K = -\lim_n [N_{00}^{(n)} \alpha / \alpha_0 - N^{(n)}]$, the first result follows from Theorem 9-84. The second result follows by duality from Proposition 6-16.
