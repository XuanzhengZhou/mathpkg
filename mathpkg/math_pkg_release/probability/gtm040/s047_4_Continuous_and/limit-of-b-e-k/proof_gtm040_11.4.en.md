Denote the measure $\beta^\nu$ obtained from Proposition 11-2 for $\nu = {}_0N(x, \cdot)$ by $\beta^x$. If $\{E_k\}$ is an increasing sequence of finite sets with union $S$, then Proposition 11-5 asserts that $B^{E_k}(x, \cdot)$ converges weak-star to $\beta^x$. The result follows by applying the weak-star convergence to the continuous column vector $h$, which yields the pointwise limit identity
$$
\lim_k B^{E_k}(x, \cdot)h = \int_{*S} h(y)\,d\beta^x(y)
$$
for each $x \in *S$.
