Proposition 5-23 formalizes the ladder process as a bona fide Markov chain. Given a sums of independent random variables Markov chain $P$ on the integers, the process $P^+$ obtained by restricting observations to the positive step times $s_n$ satisfies the Markov property. Moreover, $P^+$ inherits spatial homogeneity: its transition probability from $i$ to $j$ depends only on the difference $j-i$, matching the transition probability from $0$ to $j-i$ in the original chain.
