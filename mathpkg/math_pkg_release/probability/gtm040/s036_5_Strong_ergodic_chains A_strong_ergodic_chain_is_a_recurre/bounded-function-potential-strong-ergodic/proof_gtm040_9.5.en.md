If $|f| \leq k 1$, then

$$G|f| \leq k G1 = k(\hat{M}^T D^{-1})1 = k\hat{M}^T \alpha^T = k(\alpha \hat{M})^T < \infty.$$

Hence $G|f|$ is finite-valued. If $\alpha f = 0$, then $f$ is a charge with potential $-Gf$ by Theorem 9-50.
