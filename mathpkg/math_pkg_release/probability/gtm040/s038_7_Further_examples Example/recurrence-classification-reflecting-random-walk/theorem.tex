For the reflecting $p$-$q$ random walk on the nonnegative integers:

\[
\begin{cases}
p > \frac{1}{2} & \Longrightarrow \text{the chain is transient}, \\[4pt]
p = \frac{1}{2} & \Longrightarrow \text{the chain is null recurrent}, \\[4pt]
p < \frac{1}{2} & \Longrightarrow \text{the chain is positive recurrent (all moments of the return time are finite)}.
\end{cases}
\]
