A bounded regular function on the state space of a Markov chain satisfies $Ph = h$. When the chain is absorbing (i.e., with probability 1 the process eventually enters an absorbing state and stops), this lemma states that the only bounded regular function is the zero function. This uniqueness result is foundational for establishing the uniqueness of solutions to the Dirichlet problem in denumerable Markov chains.
