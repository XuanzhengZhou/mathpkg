Let $\{h_\beta\}$ be a collection of superregular functions, so $P h_\beta \leq h_\beta$ for all $\beta$. Then for all $\beta$,
$$P(\inf_\beta h_\beta) \leq P h_\beta \leq h_\beta.$$
Since this holds for every $\beta$, we obtain
$$P(\inf_\beta h_\beta) \leq \inf_\beta h_\beta,$$
which means the pointwise infimum is superregular.
