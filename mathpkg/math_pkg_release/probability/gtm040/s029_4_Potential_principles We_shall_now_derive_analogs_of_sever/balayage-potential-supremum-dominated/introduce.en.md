This corollary provides a dual characterization of the balayage potential: it is the largest pure potential supported on $E$ that is dominated by $g$ on $E$. While Corollary 8-47 characterizes the balayage potential as an infimum from above, this corollary characterizes it as a supremum from below, providing a complete extremal description of the sweeping out operation.
