A regular measure is a non-negative row vector $\pi$ that is invariant under right multiplication by the transition matrix $P$, satisfying $\pi = \pi P$. This is the dual notion to $P$-regular functions (column vectors satisfying $f = Pf$). Regular measures correspond to stationary or invariant distributions of the Markov chain when normalized to sum to one, and play a fundamental role in the ergodic theory of Markov chains.
