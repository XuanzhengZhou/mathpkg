If $P$ is a null or noncyclic ergodic sums of independent random variables process with $P = P^T$, then $P$ is normal,
$$C_{ij} = G_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}\,{}^jN_{ii}, \quad \text{and} \quad {}^i\lambda_j = \frac{1}{2}.$$
In particular, the one-dimensional and two-dimensional symmetric random walks are normal.
