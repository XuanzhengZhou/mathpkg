If $E$ is a small set and $g$ is a bounded potential with support in $E$, then $g$ is regular at points of $\tilde{E}$ and $\lambda^E g = 0$. Conversely, if $E$ is small and ergodic and $g$ is a bounded function which is regular at points of $\tilde{E}$ and satisfies $\lambda^E g = 0$, then $g$ is a bounded potential with support in $E$.
