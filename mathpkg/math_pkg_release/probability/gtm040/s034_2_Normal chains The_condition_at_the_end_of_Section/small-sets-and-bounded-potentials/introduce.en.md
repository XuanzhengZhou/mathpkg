Proposition 9-42 establishes a precise correspondence between bounded potentials supported on a small set $E$ and bounded functions satisfying certain regularity conditions. For a small ergodic set $E$, a bounded function $g$ is a potential with support in $E$ if and only if it is regular off $E$ and $\lambda^E g = 0$. This characterization is fundamental for the Principle of Balayage and the study of equilibrium potentials in the recurrent setting.
