Corollary 9-39 gives a simple and elegant identity for normal chains: for any two distinct states $i$ and $j$, the long-run entrance probabilities satisfy ${}^i\lambda_j + {}^j\lambda_i = 1$. This follows from the fact that $\{i, j\}$ is a finite (hence small) set, and the $\lambda$ matrix for a two-element set has rows that are probability vectors summing to $1$.
