Lemma 9-37 provides a sufficient condition for the existence of $\lambda^E = \lim_n P^n B^E$ in a normal chain. If the weighted sum $\sum_{k \in E} {}^0N_{kk} P^E_{kj}$ is finite for all $j \in E$, then $\lambda^E$ exists. Furthermore, the columns of $B^E - 1 \lambda^E$ are potentials, establishing a crucial link between the hitting matrix and the potential theory of the chain.
