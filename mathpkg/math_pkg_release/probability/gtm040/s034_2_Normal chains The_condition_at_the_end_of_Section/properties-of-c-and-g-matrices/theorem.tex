All entries of $C$ and $G$ which exist are finite and non-negative. Moreover, $C_{ii} = G_{ii} = 0$ for every $i$, and
$$\hat{G}_{ij} = \frac{\alpha_j C_{ji}}{\alpha_i}.$$
Hence $\hat{G}$ is the dual of $C$ weighted by $\alpha$. Furthermore, if $G_{0j}$ and $C_{j0}$ exist, then ${}^0\nu_j$ and ${}^0\hat{\nu}_j$ exist.
