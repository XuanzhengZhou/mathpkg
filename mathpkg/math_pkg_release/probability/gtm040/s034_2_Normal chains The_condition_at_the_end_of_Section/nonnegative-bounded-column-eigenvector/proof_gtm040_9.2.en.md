Since $h$ is non-negative and bounded, each coordinate sequence $(P^n h)_i$ is non-negative and bounded. Moreover, $Ph \leq h$ implies $P^{n+1}h \leq P^n h$ for all $n$, so each coordinate sequence $(P^n h)_i$ is a non-increasing sequence of non-negative numbers. A non-increasing sequence bounded below converges, hence $\lim_n (P^n h)_i$ exists for each $i$. Therefore $\lim_n P^n h$ exists coordinate-wise.
