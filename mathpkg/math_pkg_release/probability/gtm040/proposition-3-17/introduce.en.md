Let $(\mathbf{f}_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ be a martingale, let $t$ be a random time, and let $\hat{\mathbf{f}}_n$ be the stopped process with

$$\hat{\mathbf{f}}_n(\omega) = \mathbf{f}_{\min(n, t(\omega))}(\omega).$$

Then $(\hat{\mathbf{f}}_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ is a martingale.
