Schwarz's inequality for the bilinear form $(g, \bar{g})$ holds whenever the potentials have finite support. This follows from properties (1), (2), and (3) of the bilinear form -- symmetry, homogeneity, and additivity -- together with the non-negativity of energy established earlier.
