Since $A \supset (A_1 \cup A_2 \cup \cdots \cup A_k)$, we have, by Lemma 1-20, $\mu(A) \geq \mu(A_1 \cup A_2 \cup \cdots \cup A_k)$, and by Lemma 1-26 the right side equals $\sum_{n=1}^k \mu(A_n)$ for each $k$. Hence $\mu(A) \geq \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \mu(A_n)$. The reverse inequality $\mu(A) \leq \sum_n \mu(A_n)$ follows from countable subadditivity (Lemma 1-21), yielding equality.
