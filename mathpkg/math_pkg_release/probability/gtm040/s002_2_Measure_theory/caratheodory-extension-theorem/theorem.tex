Let $\mathcal{F}$ be a field of sets in a space $X$ and let $\nu$ be a measure defined on $\mathcal{F}$. Suppose $X$ can be written as the denumerable union of sets in $\mathcal{F}$ of finite measure. If $\mathcal{G}$ is the smallest Borel field containing $\mathcal{F}$, then $\nu$ can be extended in one and only one way to a measure defined on all of $\mathcal{G}$ which agrees with $\nu$ on sets of $\mathcal{F}$.
