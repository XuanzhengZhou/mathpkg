The assertions follow from the observations that
$$\{x \mid \sup f_n(x) > c\} = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} \{x \mid f_n(x) > c\},$$
$$\{x \mid \inf f_n(x) < c\} = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} \{x \mid f_n(x) < c\},$$
$$\limsup_n f_n(x) = \inf_n \sup_{m \geq n} f_m(x),$$
and
$$\liminf_n f_n(x) = \sup_n \inf_{m \geq n} f_m(x).$$
