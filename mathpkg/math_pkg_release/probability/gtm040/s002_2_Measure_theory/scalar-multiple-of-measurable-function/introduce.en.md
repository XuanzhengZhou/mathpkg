This corollary follows immediately from Proposition 1-34 and Proposition 1-32: a constant function $g(x) = c$ is measurable, and the product of measurable functions is measurable, so $cf = g \cdot f$ is measurable. Combined with the closure under addition, this shows that the measurable functions form a vector space over $\mathbb{R}$.
