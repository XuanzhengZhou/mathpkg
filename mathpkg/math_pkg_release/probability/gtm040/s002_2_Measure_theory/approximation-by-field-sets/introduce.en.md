This lemma provides a crucial characterization: sets in the sigma-completion $\mathcal{G}^*$ that have finite outer measure are automatically in the completion $\mathcal{F}^*$, meaning they can be approximated arbitrarily well by sets in the original field $\mathcal{F}$. This characterization is key to proving that the outer measure is completely additive on $\mathcal{G}^*$.
