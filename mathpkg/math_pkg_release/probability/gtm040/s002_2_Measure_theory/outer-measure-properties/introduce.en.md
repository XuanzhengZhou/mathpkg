This lemma establishes the basic properties of the outer measure $\mu$ constructed in the proof of the Carathéodory Extension Theorem. It is non-negative, monotone, and agrees with the original measure $\nu$ on sets of the field $\mathcal{F}$. The proof shows that $\mu(C) \leq \nu(C)$ (since $\{C\}$ is a covering) and $\nu(C) \leq \mu(C)$ (by Proposition 1-18 applied to any covering).
