A normal chain is one where the limit quantities $^0\lambda_j$ (probabilities of reaching $j$ before 0 in the long run) exist for all states $j$. Normality ensures a rich class of charges and potentials, providing a foundation for potential theory. All noncyclic ergodic chains are normal.
