Proposition 9-21 gives an explicit formula for the potential $g$ of a charge $f$ in a normal chain: $g = (\,^0N - 1\,^0\nu)f$, where $\,^0\nu$ captures the long-run behavior. This generalizes the representation of potentials in transient chains.
