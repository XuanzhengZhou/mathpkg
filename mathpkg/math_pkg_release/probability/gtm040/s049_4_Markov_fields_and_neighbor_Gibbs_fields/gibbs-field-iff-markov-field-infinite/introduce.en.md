This theorem extends the finite-case equivalence between Markov fields and neighbor Gibbs fields to the countably infinite setting. The forward direction uses the fact that the local characteristic $\mu_a^{\Lambda}(\iota)$ depends only on the configuration restricted to $\bar{a}$, which implies the Markov property. The converse constructs a potential $V$ from an arbitrary Markov field, generalizing the finite-case procedure.
