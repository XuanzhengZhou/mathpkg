This technical lemma establishes the monotonicity of the sequences $\mu_n^+$ and $\mu_n^-$ obtained by taking the maximum and minimum of conditional probabilities $\mu^{\kappa^n}([\kappa^0, \ldots, \kappa^m])$ over all configurations $\kappa^n$ on $K(n)$. The sequence $\mu_n^-$ increases with $n$ because enlarging $K(n)$ restricts the configurations over which the minimum is taken. A similar argument shows $\mu_n^+$ decreases. These monotonicity properties guarantee that the limits $\mu^+$ and $\mu^-$ in Theorem 12-30 are well-defined.
