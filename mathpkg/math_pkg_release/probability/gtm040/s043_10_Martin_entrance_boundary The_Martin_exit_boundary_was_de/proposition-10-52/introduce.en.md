Proposition 10-52 is the dual counterpart of Proposition 10-51, establishing the almost-sure existence of the reverse-time limit $z_u$ of an extended Markov chain in the Martin entrance boundary $*S$. The proof proceeds by time-reversal: forming the reverse (dual) process and applying Proposition 10-51 to it, then identifying the dual exit boundary with the entrance boundary via the canonical homeomorphism. The starting distribution is expressed as an integral of $p(x)$ against the representing measure $\mu^\nu$.
