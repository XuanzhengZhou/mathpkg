On almost every path,
$$z_u(\omega) = \lim_{n \uparrow u} z_n(\omega)$$
exists in $*S$. On almost every path for which the limit exists, either $u > -\infty$ and $z_u \in S$ or else $u = -\infty$ and $z_u \in B^e$. Moreover, for any Borel set $C$ in $*S$,
$$\Pr[z_u \in C] = \int_C p(x) d\mu^\nu(x),$$
where $\mu^\nu$ is the unique measure on $S \cup B^e$ representing $\nu$.
