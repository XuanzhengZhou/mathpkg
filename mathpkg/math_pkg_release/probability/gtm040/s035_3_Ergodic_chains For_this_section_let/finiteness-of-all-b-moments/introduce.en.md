This proposition shows that the finiteness of the $r$-th moment of the return time for a single state implies the finiteness for all states. The proof proceeds by induction on $r$, using the relationship between $b^{(r)}$, $M^{(r)}$, and $c^{(r)}$ established in Propositions 9-65 and 9-67, together with the inequality $t_j \leq t_0 + t_{0,j}$ and Lemma 9-66. This result is essential for the well-definedness of ergodic degree.
