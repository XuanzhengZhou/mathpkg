Since $^{i}\!H_{kj} \leq \,^{i}\!N_{kj}$, it suffices to prove the result for $^{i}\!N_{kj}$. But $\sum_j \,^{i}\!N_{kj} = M_{ki} < \infty$, so that $\lim_j \,^{i}\!N_{kj} = 0$. The finiteness of $M_{ki}$ follows from Proposition 6-42 for noncyclic ergodic chains, and the sum identity is a standard property of taboo occupation times.
