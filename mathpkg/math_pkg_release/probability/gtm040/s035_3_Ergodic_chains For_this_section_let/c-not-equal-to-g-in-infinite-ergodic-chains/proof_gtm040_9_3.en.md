If $C = G$, then $(C_{ij} - G_{ij})/\alpha_j = 0$ for every $j$, contradicting Proposition 9-63 which shows this quantity tends to infinity. The finite example $P = \begin{pmatrix} 1 - a & a \\ a & 1 - a \end{pmatrix}$ with $0 < a < 1$ gives $\alpha = (1/2, 1/2)$, $M_{12} = M_{21} = 1/a$, and one can verify $C = G$ directly.
