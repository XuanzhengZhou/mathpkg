This lemma establishes that in a recurrent Markov chain, the taboo return probability $\bar{F}_{00}^{(n)}$ (the probability that the first return to state $0$ avoiding $0$ before that return occurs at exactly step $n$) is invariant under passage to the dual chain $\hat{P}$. This duality is a consequence of the algebraic symmetry between $P$ and $\hat{P}$ and is used to prove the equality of the dual moment vectors $c_0^{(r)} = \hat{c}_0^{(r)}$.
