This lemma establishes that for any function $h$ having a finite stationary mean $\alpha h$, the $n$-step transition operator $P^n$ applied to $h$ converges to the stationary limit $Ah$. The proof uses the dual chain representation and dominated convergence, and the result plays a key role in the characterization of potentials in ergodic chains.
