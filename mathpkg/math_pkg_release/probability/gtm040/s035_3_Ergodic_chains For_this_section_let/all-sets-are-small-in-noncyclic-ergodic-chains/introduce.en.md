This proposition establishes that in a noncyclic ergodic chain, every set is small, meaning the equilibrium measure $\lambda^E$ and the equilibrium potential $^{E}\!\nu$ exist for all subsets $E$. Explicit formulas are given: $\lambda^E = \alpha B^E$ (the stationary measure weighted by the balayage operator) and $^{E}\!\nu = \alpha\,^{E}\!N$ (the stationary measure weighted by the taboo potential). For the full state space, the equilibrium measure reduces to the stationary distribution $\alpha$.
