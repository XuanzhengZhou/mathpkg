This proposition states that in an infinite noncyclic ergodic chain, the equilibrium measure $^{i}\!\lambda_j$ of a single state $j$ relative to the taboo set $\{i\}$ tends to zero as $j$ goes to infinity. The proof uses the representation $^{i}\!\lambda_j = \sum_k \alpha_k \,^{i}\!H_{kj}$ from Proposition 9-55, together with dominated convergence and Lemma 9-59. This result is a key step in establishing the asymptotic behavior of mean first passage times.
