**Sums of independent random variables on the circle.** Mark $n$ ($n \geq 3$) points on a circle, labeled $i = 0, 1, \ldots, n-1$ clockwise. The process either moves one step clockwise with probability $\frac{2}{3}$, or it moves one step counterclockwise with probability $\frac{1}{3}$. Prove that the chain is ergodic. Is it cyclic?
