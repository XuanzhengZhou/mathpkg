**Sums of independent random variables on the circle (continued).** Mark $n$ ($n \geq 3$) points on a circle, labeled $i = 0, 1, \ldots, n-1$ clockwise. The process either moves one step clockwise with probability $\frac{2}{3}$, or it moves one step counterclockwise with probability $\frac{1}{3}$. Compute $M$ by means of Theorem 6-43. [It is sufficient to find the zeroth column of $M$.]
