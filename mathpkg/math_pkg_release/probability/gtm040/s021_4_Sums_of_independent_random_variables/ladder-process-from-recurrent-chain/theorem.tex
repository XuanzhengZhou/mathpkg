Let $P$ be a recurrent chain with outcome functions $x_n$. For a fixed state $s$, let $t_n(\omega)$ be the $(n+1)$st time on the path $\omega$ that state $s$ is reached. Then the random times $t_n$ for $n \geq 0$ are the outcome functions for a sums of independent random variables ladder process $P^*$ with state space the integers.
