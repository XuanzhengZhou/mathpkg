Proposition 12-6 provides three equivalent formulations of the Markov field property. Condition (1) is the definition: local characteristics depend only on closed neighborhoods. Condition (2) states that extending the conditioning set beyond the closed neighborhood does not change the local characteristic. Condition (3) is a symmetry condition: configurations that agree on neighbors yield identical local characteristics. These equivalences are fundamental tools for working with Markov fields and are used repeatedly in the proofs of the main theorems.
