Show that if $P$ is a normal null chain with $B = B_e$ and if $x$ is a point of the boundary with a neighborhood in $S^*$ containing no other limit points, then $\lim_n \Pr[x_n \in E]$ exists for all sufficiently small neighborhoods $E$ and is the same for all such sets $E$.
