Corollary 12-18 gives an explicit formula for the $(A, \bar{A})$-characteristic of a finite Markov field directly in terms of the canonical neighbor potential $V$. The formula shows that $\mu_A^{\bar{A}}(\iota)$ depends only on potentials $V_B$ for cliques $B$ that intersect $A$, and is invariant under changes to the configuration outside $\bar{A}$. This result generalizes Proposition 12-6 from single sites to arbitrary finite subsets and provides a practical way to compute conditional probabilities from potentials.
