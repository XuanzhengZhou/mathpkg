A homogeneous Markov field on the integers is the one-dimensional lattice analog of a Markov chain, but with index set $\mathbb{Z}$ (bi-infinite) rather than $\mathbb{N}$. The Markov property is symmetric: variables are conditionally independent of the distant past and future given a separating set.
