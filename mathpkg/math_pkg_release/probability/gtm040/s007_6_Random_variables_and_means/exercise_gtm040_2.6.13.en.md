Let $(\Omega, \mathcal{B}, \mu)$ be the unit interval with Lebesgue measure. If $x \in \Omega$, let $f(x) = x^2$. Let $p$ be the statement "$x \leq \frac{1}{2}$."
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Show that $f$ is a random variable.
\item[(b)] Find $M[f]$.
\item[(c)] Find $M[f \mid p]$ and $M[f \mid \sim p]$.
\item[(d)] Relate your answer in (b) to the solution of (c).
\end{enumerate}
