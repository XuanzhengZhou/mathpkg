In a randomly selected two-child family, let $f =$ the number of boys. Find
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $M[f]$.
\item[(b)] $M[f \mid \text{first child is a boy}]$.
\item[(c)] $M[f \mid \text{there is at least one boy}]$.
\end{enumerate}
