Let $\{x_n\}$ be a Markov chain with starting distribution $\pi$, let $q$ be a statement relative to $\mathcal{F}_n$, let $r$ be a statement relative to $\mathcal{T}_n$, and suppose $\Pr_\pi[q \land x_n = i] > 0$. Then

$$\Pr_\pi[r \mid q \land x_n = i] = \Pr_\pi[r \mid x_n = i] = \Pr_i[r']$$

where $\omega \in R$ if and only if $\omega_n \in R'$.
