Let $\{x_n\}$ be a Markov chain, let $t$ be a random time, and let $p$ be a statement satisfying

$$\Pr_{\pi}[p \land t = \infty] = 0,$$

and there exists a statement $p'$ such that if $t(\omega) < \infty$, then $\omega \in P$ if and only if $\omega_t \in P'$. Then

$$\Pr_{\pi}[p] = \sum_{k} \Pr_{\pi}[x_t = k] \Pr_k[p'].$$
