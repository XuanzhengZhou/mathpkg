Lemma 8-18 confirms that the fundamental matrix $N_E$ of the sub-stochastic matrix $P^E$ (the restriction of $P$ to $E$) is simply the $E \times E$ submatrix of the full fundamental matrix $N$. This is probabilistically obvious: restricting observation to states in $E$ does not change the expected number of visits to those states.
