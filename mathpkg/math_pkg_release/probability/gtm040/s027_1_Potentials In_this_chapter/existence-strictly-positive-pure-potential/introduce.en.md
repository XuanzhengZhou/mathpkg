Proposition 8-5 guarantees the existence of a strictly positive pure potential for any transient Markov chain with distinguished superregular measure $\alpha$. By choosing $f_j = 2^{-j}/\alpha_j$ and numbering the states, one obtains a non-negative charge $f$ whose potential $g = Nf$ satisfies $g_i > 0$ for every state $i$, since $g_i \geq N_{ii}f_i \geq f_i > 0$.
