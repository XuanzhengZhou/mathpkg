The fact that $g = B^E g$ is immediate from Lemma 8-17, since the pre-$E$ term $(I - B^E)Nf$ vanishes when the support of $f$ is in $E$. Hence $g_E$ determines $g$. Since $g = B^E g$, we have $f_E = (I - P^E)g_E$ by conclusion (3) of Proposition 8-16. Finally $g_E = N_E f_E$ by applying Theorem 8-3 to the reduced chain $P^E$ and using Lemma 8-18.
