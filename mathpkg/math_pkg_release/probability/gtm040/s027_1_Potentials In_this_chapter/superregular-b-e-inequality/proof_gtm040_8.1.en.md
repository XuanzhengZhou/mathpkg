First suppose that $h > 0$. Form the $h$-process; then $h^* = 1$. Since $(B^E)^* 1 \leq 1$ in the transformed chain, transforming back yields $B^E h \leq h$. The general case follows by considering $h + \varepsilon$ and letting $\varepsilon \to 0$.
