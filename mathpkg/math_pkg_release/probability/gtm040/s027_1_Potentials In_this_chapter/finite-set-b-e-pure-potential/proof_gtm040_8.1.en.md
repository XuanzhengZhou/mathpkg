$B^E h$ is a finite linear combination of columns of $B^E$ and is therefore by Proposition 8-20 a potential with support in the finite set $E$. Since $h$ is non-negative superregular, $B^E h$ is non-negative superregular by conclusion (3) of Proposition 8-16. Hence $B^E h$ is a pure potential (by the remark after Proposition 8-6).
