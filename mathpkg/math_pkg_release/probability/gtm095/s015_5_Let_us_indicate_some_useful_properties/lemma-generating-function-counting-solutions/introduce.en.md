This lemma converts a combinatorial counting problem into the algebraic problem of extracting a coefficient from a product of polynomials or power series. It is the workhorse behind solutions to classical problems such as Galileo's dice problem and the lucky tickets problem. When constraints are removed ($X_j$ unrestricted), the lemma recovers the stars-and-bars formula $N(r; n) = C_{n+r-1}^r$.
