# Exercise 5

Let $a$ and $b$ be two different positive numbers. Show that the number of nonnegative solutions of the system

$$aX_1 + bX_2 = n$$

is the coefficient of $x^n$ in the expansion of

$$\left(1 + x^a + x^{2a} + \cdots\right)\left(1 + x^b + x^{2b} + \cdots\right).$$
