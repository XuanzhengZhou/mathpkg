Every number $a \in [0, 1)$ has a unique binary expansion (containing infinitely many zeros):

$$a = \frac{a_1}{2} + \frac{a_2}{2^2} + \cdots, \quad a_i = 0, 1.$$

This establishes a one-to-one correspondence between the infinite binary sequences $\omega = (a_1, a_2, \ldots)$ and the points of the interval $[0, 1)$.
