# Exercise 1

In the notation of Lemma 2, set

$$P \wedge \tilde{P} = E_Q(z \wedge \tilde{z}),$$

where $z \wedge \tilde{z} = \min(z, \tilde{z})$. Show that

$$\|P - \tilde{P}\| = 2(1 - P \wedge \tilde{P}),$$

and consequently,

$$\mathcal{E}r(P, \tilde{P}) = 1 - \frac{1}{2}\|P - \tilde{P}\| = P \wedge \tilde{P}.$$
