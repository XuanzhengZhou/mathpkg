# Exercise 4

Establish formulas (11) and (12) relating the Hellinger integral and the Kakutani–Hellinger distance:

$$\rho^2(P, \tilde{P}) = 1 - H(P, \tilde{P}),$$

and for product measures,

$$H(P^n, \tilde{P}^n) = [H(P, \tilde{P})]^n.$$
