# Exercise 1

Let $A_1, A_2, \ldots$ be independent events with $P(A_n) < 1$ for all $n \geq 1$. Then $P\{A_n \text{ i.o.}\} = 1$ if and only if $P(\bigcup A_n) = 1$.
