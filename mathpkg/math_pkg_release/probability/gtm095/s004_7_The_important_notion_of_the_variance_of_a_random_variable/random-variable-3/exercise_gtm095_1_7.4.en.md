# Exercise 4

Show that $\inf_{-\infty < a < \infty} E(\xi - a)^2$ is attained for $a = E\xi$ and consequently

$$\inf_{-\infty < a < \infty} E(\xi - a)^2 = \operatorname{Var}\xi.$$
