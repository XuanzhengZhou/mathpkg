# Exercise 7

Let $\xi$ and $\eta$ be random variables with $\operatorname{Var}\xi > 0$, $\operatorname{Var}\eta > 0$, and let $\rho = \rho(\xi, \eta)$ be their correlation coefficient. Show that $|\rho| \leq 1$. If $|\rho| = 1$, there are constants $a$ and $b$ such that $\eta = a\xi + b$. Moreover, if $\rho = 1$, then $a > 0$, and if $\rho = -1$, then $a < 0$.
