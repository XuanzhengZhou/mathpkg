For a homogeneous Markov chain $\xi = (\xi_0, \ldots, \xi_n)$ with state space $X$ and transition matrix $\|p_{xy}\|$, the transition operator $T$ acting on functions $\varphi : X \to \mathbb{R}$ is defined by

$$T\varphi(x) = E[\varphi(\xi_1) \mid \xi_0 = x] = \sum_{y \in X} \varphi(y) p_{xy}.$$

The $n$-step transition operator is $T^n \varphi(x) = E[\varphi(\xi_n) \mid \xi_0 = x]$.
