# Exercise 8

Let $\{x_n\}$ be a sequence of real numbers and $A_n = (-\infty, x_n)$. Show that $x = \limsup x_n$ and $A = \limsup A_n$ are related in the following way:

$$(-\infty, x) \subseteq A \subseteq (-\infty, x].$$

In other words, $A$ is equal to either $(-\infty, x)$ or to $(-\infty, x]$.
