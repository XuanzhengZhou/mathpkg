# Exercise 2: Convergence Rate in the Bernoulli Scheme

Give a direct proof of the fact that in the Bernoulli scheme

$$\sup_{x} \bigl|F_{T_n}(x) - \Phi(x)\bigr|$$

is of order $1/\sqrt{n}$ as $n \to \infty$.
