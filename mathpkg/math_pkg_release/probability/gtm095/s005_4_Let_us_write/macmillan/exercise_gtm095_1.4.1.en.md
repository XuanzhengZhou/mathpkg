# Exercise 1

Let $\xi$ and $\eta$ be random variables with correlation coefficient $\rho$. Establish the following two-dimensional analog of Chebyshev's inequality:

$$P\{|\xi - E\xi| \geq \varepsilon \sqrt{\text{Var}\xi} \text{ or } |\eta - E\eta| \geq \varepsilon \sqrt{\text{Var}\eta}\} \leq \frac{1 + \sqrt{1 - \rho^2}}{\varepsilon^2}.$$

(Inequality (16) implies the validity of the law of large numbers in more general contexts than Bernoulli schemes.)
