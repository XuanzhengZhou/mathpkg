# Exercise 5

Let $\xi$ be a nonnegative random variable and $a > 0$. Find $\sup P\{\xi \geq a\}$ over all distributions such that:

(i) $E\xi = 20$;

(ii) $E\xi = 20$, $\text{Var}\xi = 25$;

(iii) $E\xi = 20$, $\text{Var}\xi = 25$ and $\xi$ is symmetric about its mean value.
