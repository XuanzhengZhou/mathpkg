# Exercise 19

(a) Let $\operatorname{Var}(X \mid Y) = \mathsf{E}\big[(X - \mathsf{E}(X \mid Y))^2 \mid Y\big]$. Show that

$$\operatorname{Var} X = \mathsf{E} \operatorname{Var}(X \mid Y) + \operatorname{Var} \mathsf{E}(X \mid Y).$$

(b) Show that

$$\operatorname{Cov}(X, Y) = \operatorname{Cov}(X, \mathsf{E}(Y \mid X)).$$
