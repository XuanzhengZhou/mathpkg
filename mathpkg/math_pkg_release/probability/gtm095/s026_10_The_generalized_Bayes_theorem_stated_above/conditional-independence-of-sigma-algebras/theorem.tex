Two $\sigma$-algebras $\mathcal{G}_1$ and $\mathcal{G}_2$ are said to be \text{conditionally independent} relative to a $\sigma$-algebra $\mathcal{G}_3$ if
$$\mathsf{P}(A_1 A_2 \mid \mathcal{G}_3) = \mathsf{P}(A_1 \mid \mathcal{G}_3)\,\mathsf{P}(A_2 \mid \mathcal{G}_3) \quad (\mathsf{P}\text{-a.s.})$$
for all $A_1 \in \mathcal{G}_1$, $A_2 \in \mathcal{G}_2$.
