# Exercise 3

Show that the rate of convergence in Poisson's theorem (with $p = \lambda / n$) is given by

$$\sup_k \left| P_n(k) - \frac{\lambda^k e^{-\lambda}}{k!} \right| \leq \frac{2\lambda^2}{n}.$$

(It is advisable to read Sect. 12, Chap. 3.)
