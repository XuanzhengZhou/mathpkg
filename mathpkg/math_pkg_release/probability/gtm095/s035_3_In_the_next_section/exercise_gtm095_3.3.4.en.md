# Exercise 4

Let $\xi_n$, $n \geq 1$, be random variables with characteristic functions $\varphi_{\xi_n}(t)$, $n \geq 1$. Show that $\xi_n \xrightarrow{d} 0$ if and only if $\varphi_{\xi_n}(t) \to 1$, $n \to \infty$, in some neighborhood of $t = 0$.
