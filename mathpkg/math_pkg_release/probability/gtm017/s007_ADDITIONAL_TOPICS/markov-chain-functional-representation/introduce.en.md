This theorem establishes that any finite-state stationary Markov chain with strictly positive transition probabilities can be expressed as a deterministic function of an i.i.d. sequence of uniform random variables. The construction proceeds by embedding the discrete state space into a continuous one via $Y_n = X_n - U_n$ and defining $\xi_n = F(Y_n \mid Y_{n-1})$, where $F$ is the conditional distribution function. The zero-one law from subsection (a) is used to ensure that the reconstruction from a finite window of $\xi$'s succeeds with probability one.
