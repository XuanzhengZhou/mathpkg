Let $A_1, A_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of independent events. Then $P(A_i \text{ i.o.})$ can assume only the values $0$ and $1$. Specifically,
$$P(A_i \text{ i.o.}) = \begin{cases}
0 & \text{if } \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} P(A_i) < \infty,\\[6pt]
1 & \text{if } \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} P(A_i) = \infty.
\end{cases}$$
