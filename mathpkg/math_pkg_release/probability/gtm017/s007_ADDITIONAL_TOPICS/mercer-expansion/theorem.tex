Let $\varphi_i(t)$ be orthonormal eigenfunctions of the covariance integral equation with corresponding eigenvalues $\lambda_i$. Then the kernel $r(t,\tau)$ admits the uniformly convergent expansion
$$r(t,\tau) = \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \frac{\varphi_i(t) \varphi_i(\tau)}{\lambda_i}.$$
The series $\sum_{i=1}^n \varphi_i^2(t)/\lambda_i$ converges to $r(t,t)$ uniformly in $t$.
