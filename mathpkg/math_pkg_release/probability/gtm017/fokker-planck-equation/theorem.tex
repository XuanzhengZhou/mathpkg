For a diffusion process with density $f$, the \textbf{Fokker-Planck equation} (or \textbf{forward equation}) is:
$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial t} = \frac{1}{2} \frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2}(a(t, x) f) - \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(b(t, x) f).$$

When $a(t, x) \equiv 1$:
$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial t} = \frac{1}{2} \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} - \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(b(t, x) f).$$
