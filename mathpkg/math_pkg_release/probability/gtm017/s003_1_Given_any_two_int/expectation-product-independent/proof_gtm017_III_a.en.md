By independence, the joint distribution function factors: $F(x_1, \ldots, x_k) = F_1(x_1) \cdots F_k(x_k)$. Then $\int \cdots \int \prod x_i \, dF(x_1, \ldots, x_k) = \int \cdots \int \prod x_i \, dF_1(x_1) \cdots dF_k(x_k) = \prod \int x_i \, dF_i(x_i) = \prod E[X_i]$. The iterated integration separates because the integrand is a product of functions each depending on a single variable.
