Let $X_1, X_2$ be two random variables with joint distribution function $F(x_1, x_2)$. Suppose that $F(x_1, x_2)$ is absolutely continuous, that is,
$$F(x_1, x_2) = \int_{-\infty}^{x_1} \int_{-\infty}^{x_2} f(y_1, y_2) \, dy_2 \, dy_1.$$
Let $F_1(x_1)$ be the marginal distribution function of $X_1$ and $F(x_2|x_1)$ the conditional distribution function of $X_2$ given $X_1$. Find the joint distribution of $F_1(X_1)$ and $F(X_2|X_1)$.
