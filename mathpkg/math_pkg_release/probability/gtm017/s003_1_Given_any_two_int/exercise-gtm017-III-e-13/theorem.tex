Let $f(u_1, \ldots, u_k)$ be a non-negative continuous function defined on the unit hypercube $0 \leq u_1, \ldots, u_k \leq 1$ and bounded above by one. Consider $(X_1^{(i)}, \ldots, X_k^{(i)})$, $i = 1, \ldots, n$, as independent, identically distributed points, each uniformly distributed over the $k$-dimensional hypercube. Find the mean and variance of the statistic
$$\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} f(X_1^{(i)}, \ldots, X_k^{(i)}).$$
