Let $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ be i.i.d. exponential with mean $\theta$. Let $Y_1 \leq \cdots \leq Y_n$ be the order statistics. Find the joint distribution of $Y_1, \ldots, Y_r$ for $1 \leq r \leq n$. Use the formula for the joint density of order statistics from i.i.d. samples.
