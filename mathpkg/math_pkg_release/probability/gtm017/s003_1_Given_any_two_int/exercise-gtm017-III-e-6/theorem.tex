Let $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ be $n$ independent random variables with common density function
$$f_\theta(x) = \begin{cases} \theta^{-1} \exp(-x/\theta) & x > 0 \ 0 & x \leq 0. \end{cases}$$
Let $Y_1, \ldots, Y_n, Y_1 \leq \cdots \leq Y_n$, be the magnitudes $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ relabeled in order of size. Find the joint distribution of $Y_1, \ldots, Y_r, 1 \leq r \leq n$.
