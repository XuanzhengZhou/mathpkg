When an irreducible non-negative matrix has $k$ eigenvalues of maximal absolute value, the matrix is imprimitive with period $k$. The eigenvalues lie at the vertices of a regular $k$-gon in the complex plane, and the matrix can be permuted to cyclic block form. For Markov chains, this corresponds to periodic states with period $k$.
