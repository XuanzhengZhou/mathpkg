Let $X_n$ be a strictly stationary process taking only values $0$ or $1$. Let the probability $P[X_{n_1}=i_1,\ldots,X_{n_k}=i_k] = \int_0^1 p^{\sum i_\alpha} (1-p)^{k-\sum i_\alpha} dF(p)$ where $F(p)$ has all mass on $[0,1]$. What are the invariant sets? When is it ergodic?
