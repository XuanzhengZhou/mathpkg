Consider the integers $1, 2, \ldots, n$ with the uniform distribution on them. Let the function $f$ map the integers $1, 2, \ldots, n$ onto themselves. Show that the process generated by $f$ is strictly stationary. Under what circumstances is it ergodic? Can it be mixing?
