Show that if $g(t,x)$ satisfies a Holder condition and $G(\tau,\xi) = \int_\tau^T dt \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} g(t,x) u(\tau,\xi;t,x) dx$, then $\partial G/\partial\tau + \frac{1}{2} \partial^2 G/\partial\xi^2 = -g(\tau,\xi)$.
