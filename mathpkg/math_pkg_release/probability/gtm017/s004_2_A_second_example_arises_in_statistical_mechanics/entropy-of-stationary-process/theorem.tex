Let $X_n(w)$ be a stationary process with finite alphabet $\{a_1, \ldots, a_s\}$. Let $\alpha_n$ be the field of $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ with entropy $H_n = H(\alpha_n)$. The \textbf{entropy of the process} (average entropy per symbol) is
$$H = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{H_n}{n},$$
which exists and is finite due to subadditivity $H_{n+m} \leq H_n + H_m$.
