Under continuously differentiable spectral density $f(\lambda)$:
(1) Asymptotically unbiased: $E I_N(\lambda) - f(\lambda) = O(\log N / N)$.
(2) Not consistent: $\lim_N \mathrm{Var}(I_N(\lambda)) = f^2(\lambda)$ for $\lambda \neq 0, \pm\pi$ ($2f^2$ at $0, \pm\pi$).
(3) $I_N(\lambda)$ and $I_N(\mu)$ are asymptotically uncorrelated for $\lambda \neq \mu$.
