Every weakly stationary process $X_t$ has the random spectral representation $X_t = \int_{-\infty}^\infty e^{it\lambda} dZ(\lambda)$ where $Z(\lambda)$ has orthogonal increments with $E dZ(\lambda) \overline{dZ(\mu)} = \delta_{\lambda,\mu} dF(\lambda)$. For discrete time: $X_n = \int_{-\pi}^\pi e^{in\lambda} dZ(\lambda)$.
