For, if $m < n$ and $S_0$ is the $\bar{P}$-null set of points $y$ for which $P(X_m = y; X_n \in S_n) > 0$, then $$P_s^n(x, R) = P^n(x, S_n) = \int P^m(x, dy) P(X_m = y; X_n \in S_n) \leq P^m(x, S_0) \leq P_s^m(x, R).$$
