The ergodic decomposition theorem for Markov chains: the state space decomposes into indecomposable invariant components (atoms of the invariant $\sigma$-field), and every invariant probability is a mixture of ergodic invariant probabilities supported on these components. Each ergodic component forms a stationary, indecomposable Markov chain.
