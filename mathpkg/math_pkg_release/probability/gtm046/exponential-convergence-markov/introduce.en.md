Under a Doeblin-type minorization condition (the $h$-step transition density is uniformly bounded below on a set of positive measure), the $n$-step transition probabilities converge exponentially fast to a limit independent of the starting point. This is a fundamental ergodic theorem for general state-space Markov chains.
