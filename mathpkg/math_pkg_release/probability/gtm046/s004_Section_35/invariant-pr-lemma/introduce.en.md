The Invariant Probability Lemma equates three formulations of the key probabilistic condition for the pointwise ergodic theorem: (i) uniform absolute continuity of $\overline{P^n}$, (ii) existence of a limiting probability $\overline{P}=\lim\overline{P^n}$, and (iii) existence of an invariant probability extension agreeing with $P$ on invariant events. This equivalence is fundamental to ergodic theory in $L^r$-spaces.
