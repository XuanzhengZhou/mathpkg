Each component chain in the ergodic decomposition is both stationary (the transition probability is invariant under the shift) and indecomposable (it cannot be further decomposed into non-trivial invariant sub-chains). This follows directly from the invariance $\bar{P}_t(S)=\int\bar{P}_t(dx)P(x,S)$ proved in the decomposition theorem.
