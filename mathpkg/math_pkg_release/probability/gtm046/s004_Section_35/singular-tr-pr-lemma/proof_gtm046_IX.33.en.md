For $m<n$ and $S_0$ the $\bar{P}$-null set where $P(X_m=y;X_n\in S_n)>0$,
$$P_s^n(x,R)=P^n(x,S_n)=\int P^m(x,dy)P(X_m=y;X_n\in S_n)\leq P^m(x,S_0)\leq P_s^m(x,R).$$
Thus $P_s^n(x,R)$ is nonincreasing and bounded below by $0$, hence convergent.
