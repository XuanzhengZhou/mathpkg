The Singular Transition Probability Lemma establishes monotonicity of $P_s^n(x,R)$: for any state $x$, the $n$-step singular transition probabilities form a nonincreasing sequence and therefore converge. The proof uses the Markov property with an intermediate $m$-step integration and the singular set where positive mass can transfer, yielding $P_s^n\leq P_s^m$ for $m<n$.
