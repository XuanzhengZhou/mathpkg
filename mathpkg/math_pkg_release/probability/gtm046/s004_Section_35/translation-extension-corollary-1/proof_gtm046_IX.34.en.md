Extend $T$ to simple functions by linearity: $T(\sum x_k I_{A_k})=\sum x_k I_{TA_k}$. For arbitrary measurable $\xi$, take simple $\xi^{(n)}\to\xi$. Then $T\xi^{(n)}$ are simple (hence measurable) and converge to $T\xi$ since $T$ commutes with $\sup$, hence with limits. Thus $T\xi$ is measurable.
