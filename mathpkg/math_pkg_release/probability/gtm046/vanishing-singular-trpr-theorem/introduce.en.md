When the singular part of the transition probability vanishes in the limit, the Ces\`{a}ro averages of the $n$-step transition probabilities converge to a limit $\bar{P}(x, S)$ that is absolutely continuous with respect to the invariant measure and satisfies the idempotent (projection) property. This is a fundamental ergodic decomposition result for general state-space Markov chains.
