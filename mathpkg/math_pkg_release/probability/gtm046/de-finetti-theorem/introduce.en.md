de Finetti's celebrated representation theorem: exchangeable random variables are conditionally independent and identically distributed given a suitable $\sigma$-field (the tail $\sigma$-field or the $\sigma$-field of symmetric events). The proof constructs the empirical distribution function $\xi_n(x)$ and shows its limit $\xi(x)$ serves as the common conditional distribution function.
