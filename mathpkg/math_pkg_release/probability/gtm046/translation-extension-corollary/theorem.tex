A translation $T$ on $I_{\mathcal{A}}$ (indicators) has a unique extension to a translation $T$ on $\mathfrak{M}$ (all measurable functions): a linear continuous transformation with $T1 = 1$ and $T I_{AB} = T I_A \cdot T I_B$. The translate of a simple function $\sum x_k I_{A_k}$ is $\sum x_k I_{TA_k}$, and the translate of any $\xi$ is the limit of the translates of any approximating sequence of simple functions.
