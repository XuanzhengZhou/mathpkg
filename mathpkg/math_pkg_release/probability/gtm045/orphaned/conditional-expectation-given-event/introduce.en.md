The conditional expectation $E_A X$ of a random variable $X$ given an event $A$ (of positive probability) is the expectation computed with respect to the conditional probability $P_A$. It represents the expected value of $X$ under the additional knowledge that $A$ has occurred. The conditional probability field $(\alpha, P_A)$ represents the original experiment augmented with the occurrence of $A$.
