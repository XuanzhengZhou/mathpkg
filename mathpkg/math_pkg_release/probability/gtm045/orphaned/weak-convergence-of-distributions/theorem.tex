$F_n$ \textit{converges weakly} to $F$ ($F_n \Rightarrow F$) if $F_n(x) \to F(x)$ at all continuity points $x \in C(F)$ of $F$. Equivalently, $\int g \, dF_n \to \int g \, dF$ for all bounded continuous functions $g$.

\textbf{Helly-Bray theorem:} Every sequence of d.f.'s contains a subsequence converging vaguely to a nondecreasing, left-continuous sub-d.f.
