The Andersen-Jessen product probability theorem extends Kolmogorov's consistency theorem to arbitrary (not necessarily countable) products of probability spaces. The key is proving $\sigma$-additivity of the finitely additive product probability on the field of cylinder sets, which then extends uniquely to the product $\sigma$-field.
