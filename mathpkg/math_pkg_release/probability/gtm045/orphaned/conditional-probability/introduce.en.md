Conditional probability $P_A B$ is the probability of event $B$ under the additional information that event $A$ has occurred. For fixed $A$ with $PA > 0$, the conditional probability $P_A$ defines a new probability measure on the same field of events. This concept underpins Bayesian inference, Markov chains, and martingale theory.
