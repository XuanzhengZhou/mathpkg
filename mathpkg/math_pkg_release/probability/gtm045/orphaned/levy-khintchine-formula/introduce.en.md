The Lévy-Khintchine formula provides a complete parametrization of all infinitely divisible characteristic functions via a drift parameter $\gamma$ and a Lévy measure $dG$. The three components — deterministic drift, Gaussian (Brownian) part, and jump (Poisson) part — correspond to the three building blocks of independent increment processes.
