Axiom II of the finite case specifies the properties of a probability function $P$. It must be normed (total mass 1), nonnegative, and finitely additive over disjoint events. From these axioms one deduces $P\emptyset = 0$ and monotonicity: $A \subset B$ implies $PA \leq PB$.
