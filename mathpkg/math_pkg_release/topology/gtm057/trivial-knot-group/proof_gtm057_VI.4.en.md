For the trivial knot (unknot) in regular position, choose a projection with a single overpass. The over presentation has $n=1$ generator and one relator. By Theorem (1.3), any one relator is a consequence of the others; with $n=1$, the single relator must be a consequence of the empty set, hence trivial. Thus the presentation reduces to $(x :)$, and $\pi(R^3 - K) \cong \mathbb{Z}$. The group of the trivial knot type is infinite cyclic.
