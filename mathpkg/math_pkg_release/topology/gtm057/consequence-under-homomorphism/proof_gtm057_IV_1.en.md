Let $K_G$ be the consequence of $\{g_i\}$ and $K_H$ the consequence of $\{\phi g_i\}$. Since $\phi K_G$ contains all $\phi g_i$ and $\phi$ is onto, $\phi K_G$ contains the consequence $K_H$. Conversely, $\phi^{-1} K_H$ is a normal subgroup of $G$ containing the generators $\{g_i\}$, hence containing $K_G$. So $\phi K_G = K_H$.
