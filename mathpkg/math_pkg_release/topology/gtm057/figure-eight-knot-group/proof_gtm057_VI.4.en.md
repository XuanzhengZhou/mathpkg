For the figure-eight knot in regular position (Figure 42), a Wirtinger presentation with generators $x, y, z, w$ gives relations: $x = z^{-1}wz$, $y = wxw^{-1}$, $z = x^{-1}yx$. Substituting $z = x^{-1}yx$ into the first two: $x = x^{-1}y^{-1}x w x^{-1}yx$, which gives $w = x^{-1}yxy^{-1}x$. Substituting into $y = wxw^{-1}$ and simplifying yields the presentation $|x, y : yx^{-1}yxy^{-1} = x^{-1}yxy^{-1}x|$.
