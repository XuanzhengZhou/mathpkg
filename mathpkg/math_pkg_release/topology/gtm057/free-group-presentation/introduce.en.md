A group presentation describes a group by generators and relations. The group is the quotient of a free group by the normal subgroup generated by the relators. A consequence of a set of relators is any product of conjugates of their powers. Presentations allow us to describe infinite groups finitely, and the knot groups studied in this book are all defined through presentations.
