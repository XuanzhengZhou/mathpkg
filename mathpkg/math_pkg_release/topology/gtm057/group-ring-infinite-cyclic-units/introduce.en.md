The only invertible elements in the Laurent polynomial ring $\mathbb{Z}[t, t^{-1}]$ are the monomials $\pm t^n$. This means knot polynomials are well-defined only up to multiplication by such units, leading to the normalization convention of making them ordinary polynomials with positive constant term.
