By the invariance theorem for elementary ideals, $f_{**}(E_k) = E_k'$. Since $f_{**}$ is a ring isomorphism, it preserves the property of being the smallest principal ideal containing $E_k$, hence $f_{**}(\Delta_k) = \Delta_k'$ up to units.
