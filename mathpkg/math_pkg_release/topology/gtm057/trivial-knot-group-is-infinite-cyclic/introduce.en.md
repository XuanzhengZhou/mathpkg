The trivial knot has knot group $\mathbb{Z}$, the infinite cyclic group. This can be seen from the Wirtinger presentation of the unknot, which has a single generator and no relators. More conceptually, the complement of the unknot is homotopy equivalent to a circle. All knot polynomials of the trivial knot are $\Delta_k = 1$ for $k \geq 1$.
