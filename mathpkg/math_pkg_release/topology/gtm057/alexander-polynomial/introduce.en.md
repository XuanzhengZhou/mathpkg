The Alexander polynomial is the first (and most important) knot polynomial $\Delta_1$. For the trivial knot it is 1, for the clover-leaf knot it is $t^2 - t + 1$, and for the figure-eight knot it is $t^2 - 3t + 1$. It is the primary polynomial invariant used to distinguish knot types.
