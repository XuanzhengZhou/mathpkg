Theorem 3.1 establishes the equivalence between the abstract definition of a free group (via the universal property) and the concrete construction $F[\mathcal{A}]$. The cardinality of the free basis equals the cardinality of $\mathcal{A}$, and this cardinal number is the rank of the free group. Free groups of different ranks are not isomorphic.
