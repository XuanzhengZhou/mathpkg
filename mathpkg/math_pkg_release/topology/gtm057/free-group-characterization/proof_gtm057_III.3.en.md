If $G$ is free with basis $E$, map $[\mathcal{A}] \to E$ bijectively. Since $[\mathcal{A}]$ is a free basis of $F[\mathcal{A}]$, this extends to an isomorphism $F[\mathcal{A}] \to G$. Conversely, $F[\mathcal{A}]$ has free basis $[\mathcal{A}] = \{[a] : a \in \mathcal{A}\}$.
