If $f: (\mathbf{x}:\mathbf{r}) \to (\mathbf{y}:\mathbf{s})$ is a presentation equivalence of knot groups, then to within units, the $k$th knot polynomial $\Delta_k$ of $(\mathbf{x}:\mathbf{r})$ is mapped by $f_{**}$ onto the $k$th knot polynomial $\Delta_k'$ of $(\mathbf{y}:\mathbf{s})$. Consequently, the knot polynomials are invariants of knot type.
