A $T_4$-space combines normality with the $T_1$ separation axiom, ensuring singletons are closed. In a $T_4$-space, Urysohn's lemma guarantees the existence of continuous functions separating disjoint closed sets. Every metric space is $T_4$, and $T_4$ implies $T_3$ (regular) and $T_2$ (Hausdorff).
