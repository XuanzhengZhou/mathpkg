For pseudo-metric spaces, separability and second countability are equivalent. While every second-countable space is separable in any topological space, the converse holds specifically for pseudo-metric spaces. The proof constructs a countable base from open spheres with rational radii centered at points of a countable dense subset. This equivalence is crucial for the Urysohn metrization theorem.
