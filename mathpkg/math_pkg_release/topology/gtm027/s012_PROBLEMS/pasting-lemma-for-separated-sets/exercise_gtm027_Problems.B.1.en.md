# Exercise B.1 — Pasting Lemma for Sets with Separated Complements

Let $A$ and $B$ be subsets of a topological space $X$ such that $X = A \cup B$, and $A \sim B$ and $B \sim A$ are separated. If $f$ is a function on $X$ which is continuous on $A$ and continuous on $B$, then $f$ is continuous on $X$.

(See 1.19.)
