The ordered pair $(x,y)$ is defined via the Kuratowski construction as the class whose members are the singleton $\{x\}$ and the unordered pair $\{x, y\}$. The crucial property (Theorem 55) is that $(x,y) = (u,v)$ iff $x = u$ and $y = v$, provided all are sets.
