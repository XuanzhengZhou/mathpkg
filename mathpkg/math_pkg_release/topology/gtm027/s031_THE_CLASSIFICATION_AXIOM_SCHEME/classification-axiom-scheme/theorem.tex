\textbf{Classification Axiom-Scheme.} For each formula $A$ with free variable $\alpha$ and variable $\beta$ not occurring in $A$, the following is an axiom:
$$\forall \beta \left( \beta \in \{\alpha : A\} \iff (\beta \text{ is a set} \land B) \right)$$
where $B$ is the formula obtained from $A$ by replacing each occurrence of $\alpha$ by $\beta$.
