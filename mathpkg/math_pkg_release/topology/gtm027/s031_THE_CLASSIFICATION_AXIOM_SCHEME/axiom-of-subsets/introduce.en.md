The Axiom of Subsets (also called Separation or Aussonderung) asserts that subclasses of sets are sets. This axiom guarantees that the power set of a set exists (as a subclass of the set provided by the axiom) and that intersections of sets exist. It is essential for ensuring that common set-theoretic constructions yield sets rather than proper classes.
