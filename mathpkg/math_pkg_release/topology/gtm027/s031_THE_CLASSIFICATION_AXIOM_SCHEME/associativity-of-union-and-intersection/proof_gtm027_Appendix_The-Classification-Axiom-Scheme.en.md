# Proof of Associativity of Union and Intersection

7 Theorem $*(x \cup y) \cup z = x \cup (y \cup z)$ and $(x \cap y) \cap z = x \cap (y \cap z)$.These theorems state that union and intersection are, in the usual sense, commutative and associative operations. The distributive laws follow.
