Intersection distributes over class difference from the right: intersecting $x$ with the difference of $y$ and $z$ equals the difference of $x \cap y$ and $z$. Note that the analogous statement with union, $x \cup (y \sim z) = (x \cup y) \sim z$, is not provable without additional axioms.
