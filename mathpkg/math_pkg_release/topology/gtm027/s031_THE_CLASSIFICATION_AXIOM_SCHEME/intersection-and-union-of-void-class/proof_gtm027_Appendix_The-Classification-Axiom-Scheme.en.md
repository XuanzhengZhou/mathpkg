# Proof of Intersection and Union of the Void Class

Proof $z \in \bigcap 0$ iff $z$ is a set and $z$ belongs to each member of 0. Since (theorem 16) there is no member of 0, $z \in \bigcap 0$ iff $z$ is a set, and by 19 and the axiom of extent $\bigcap 0 = \mathcal{U}$. The second assertion is also easy to prove.
