# Proof of Epsilon Well-Orders Ordinals

Proof If $x \in x$, then $x$ is a non-void set and is the only member of $\{x\}$. By the axiom of regularity there is $y$ in $\{x\}$ such that $y \cap \{x\} = 0$, and necessarily $y = x$. But then $y \in y \cap \{x\}$, which is a contradiction.
