# Proof of E-Last Member Characterization

Proof By theorem 123, $x + 1$ is the $E$-first member of $\{z : z \in R \text{ and } x < z\}$. Then $x + 1 \leq y$ because $y \in R$ and $x < y$. Since $x$ is the $E$-last member of $y$ and $x < x + 1$, it is false that $x + 1 < y$.
