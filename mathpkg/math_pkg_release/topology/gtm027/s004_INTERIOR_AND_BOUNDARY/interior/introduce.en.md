The interior of a subset $A$ of a topological space $X$, denoted $A^{0}$, is the collection of all interior points of $A$. It is an open set and in fact the largest open set contained in $A$. The interior operator is dual to the closure operator via complementation: the interior of $A$ is the complement of the closure of the complement of $A$.
