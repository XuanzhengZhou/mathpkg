Let $X$ be a regular topological space. The following statements are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $X$ is paracompact.
\item[(b)] Every open cover of $X$ has a locally finite refinement.
\item[(c)] Every open cover of $X$ has a closed locally finite refinement.
\item[(d)] Every open cover of $X$ is even.
\item[(e)] Every open cover of $X$ has an open $\sigma$-discrete refinement.
\item[(f)] Every open cover of $X$ has an open $\sigma$-locally finite refinement.
\end{enumerate}
