Theorem 7.18 generalizes the Ascoli theorem to $k$-spaces. The key difference from Theorem 7.17 is condition (c): instead of full equicontinuity, only equicontinuity on every compact subset is required. This is natural because on a $k$-space, continuity of a function is determined by its restrictions to compact sets. The result applies to locally compact Hausdorff spaces and first countable Hausdorff spaces (which are $k$-spaces by Theorem 7.13).
