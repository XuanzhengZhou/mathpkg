An immediate consequence of Theorem 17: every locally compact Hausdorff space is regular. The closed compact neighborhood base guaranteed by the theorem provides, for any point and closed set not containing it, a disjoint closed compact neighborhood that separates them. This establishes that local compactness together with the Hausdorff property is sufficient to guarantee the regularity separation axiom.
