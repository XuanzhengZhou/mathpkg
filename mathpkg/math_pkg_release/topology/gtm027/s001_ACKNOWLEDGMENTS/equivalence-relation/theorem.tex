An \text{equivalence relation} is a relation that is reflexive, symmetric, and transitive.
If $R$ is an equivalence relation with domain $X$, then the equivalence classes partition $X$:
$$
R = \bigcup \{A \times A : A \text{ is an } R\text{-equivalence class}\}.
$$
Consequently, two equivalence classes either coincide or are disjoint.
