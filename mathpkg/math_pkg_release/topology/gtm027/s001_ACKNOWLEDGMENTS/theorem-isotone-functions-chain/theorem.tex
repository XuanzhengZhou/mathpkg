\textbf{Theorem 11.} Let $f$ and $g$ be isotone functions on a chain $X$ to a chain $Y$, and suppose that $f$ and $g$ agree on a subset $X_0$ of $X$ which is dense in the sense that, if $x < y$ and $x$ and $y$ belong to $X$, then there is a member of $X_0$ between $x$ and $y$. Then $f$ and $g$ agree on all of $X$.
