\textbf{Theorem 10.} Let $X$ and $Y$ be chains, with $Y$ order-complete, let $X_0$ be a subset of $X$, and let $f$ be an order-preserving function on $X_0$ to $Y$. Then $f$ has an isotone extension whose domain is $X$ if and only if $f$ carries order-bounded sets into order-bounded sets. More precisely, the condition is that whenever $A$ is a subset of $X_0$ which is order-bounded in $X$, the image $f[A]$ is order-bounded in $Y$.
