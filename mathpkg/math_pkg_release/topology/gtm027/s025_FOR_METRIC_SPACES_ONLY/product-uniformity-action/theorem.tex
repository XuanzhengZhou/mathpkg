\begin{proposition}[Product Entourage Action on a Relation]
Let $(X, \mathfrak{u})$ and $(Y, \mathfrak{v})$ be uniform spaces, and let $R$ be a subset of $X \times Y$. For $U \in \mathfrak{u}$ and $V \in \mathfrak{v}$, the action of the product entourage $W(U, V)$ on $R$ is given by
\[
W(U, V)[R] = V \circ R \circ U^{-1} = \bigcup \{U[x] \times V[y] : (x, y) \in R\}.
\]
\end{proposition}
