Among all topologies on $X$ that induce the same relative topology as $\mathcal{J}$ on every compact subset, the $k$-extension $\mathcal{J}_k$ is the finest. This maximality property characterizes the $k$-extension as the optimal refinement of $\mathcal{J}$ that preserves the compactly-determined structure of the space.
