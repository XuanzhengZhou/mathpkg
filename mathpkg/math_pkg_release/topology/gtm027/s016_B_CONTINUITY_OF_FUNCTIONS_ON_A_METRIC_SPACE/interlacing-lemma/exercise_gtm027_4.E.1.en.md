# Exercise E.1 — Interlacing Lemma for Sequences in Ordinal Spaces

Let $\{x_n\}_{n \in \omega}$ and $\{y_n\}_{n \in \omega}$ be sequences in $\Omega_0$ (the space of countable ordinals) such that
$$x_n \leq y_n \leq x_{n+1} \qquad \text{for each } n \in \omega.$$

Prove that both sequences converge (in the order topology) and converge to the same point of $\Omega_0$. (Recall that $\Omega_0 = [0, \Omega)$ where $\Omega$ is the first uncountable ordinal.)
