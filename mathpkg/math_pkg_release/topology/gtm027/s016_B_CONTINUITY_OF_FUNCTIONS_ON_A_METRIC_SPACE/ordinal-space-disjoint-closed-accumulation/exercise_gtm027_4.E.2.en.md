# Exercise E.2 — Accumulation Point of Disjoint Closed Sets in $\Omega_0$

Let $A$ and $B$ be closed disjoint subsets of $\Omega_0 = [0, \Omega)$. Prove that $\Omega$ (the first uncountable ordinal) is not an accumulation point of both $A$ and $B$.

(Hint: If $\Omega$ is an accumulation point of both, construct interlacing sequences in $A$ and $B$ and derive a contradiction using the fact that a countable supremum of countable ordinals is countable.)
