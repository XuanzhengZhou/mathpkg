Let $X$ be the Sorgenfrey line (half-open interval space) and let $Y = X \times X$. Let $\mathfrak{U}$ be the product topology on $Y$ having as a base the family of all products $A \times B$, where $A$ and $B$ are members of the topology $\mathfrak{J}$ of the Sorgenfrey line. The space $(Y, \mathfrak{U})$ is called the Sorgenfrey plane.
