# Exercise U(b): Quotient by an invariant subgroup is a topological group

Let $G$ be a topological group and $H$ an invariant (normal) subgroup. Prove that $G/H$, with the quotient topology, is a topological group and that the canonical projection $\pi : G \to G/H$ is a continuous, open homomorphism.

*Note:* Part (a) already establishes that $\pi$ is open and continuous. Use this to verify the continuity of the group operations on $G/H$.
