# Exercise U(a): Projection onto homogeneous quotient space is open and continuous

Let $G$ be a topological group and $H$ a subgroup. Let $G/H$ be the family of left cosets with the quotient topology. Prove that the canonical projection $\pi : G \to G/H$ is open and continuous.

*Hint:* Show that the union of all left cosets which intersect an open set $U$ is $U \cdot H$, and apply the result of 3.10 (which states that a subset of $G/H$ is open iff its inverse image under $\pi$ is open).
