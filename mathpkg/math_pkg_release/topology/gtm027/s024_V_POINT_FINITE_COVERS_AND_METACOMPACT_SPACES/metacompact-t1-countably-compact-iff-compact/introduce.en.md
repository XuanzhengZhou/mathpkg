For metacompact T1-spaces, countable compactness implies compactness. The reverse implication holds generally: every compact space is countably compact. Thus in the class of metacompact T1-spaces, the two notions coincide. This result, due to Arens and Dugundji, illustrates how metacompactness bridges the gap between countable and full covering properties.
