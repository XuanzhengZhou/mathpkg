The Kuratowski Lemma asserts that every chain in a partially ordered set can be extended to a maximal chain. The proof applies the Maximal Principle to the family of all chains containing the given chain, noting that the union of a nest of chains is again a chain. This lemma is a convenient tool for applying Zorn's Lemma.
