The quotient group $G/H$ is formed when $H$ is a normal subgroup of $G$. Its elements are the cosets of $H$, and the group operation is defined by $(xH)(yH) = (xy)H$. This construction generalizes modular arithmetic and is fundamental to the isomorphism theorems of group theory. The natural projection $x \mapsto xH$ is a homomorphism from $G$ onto $G/H$.
