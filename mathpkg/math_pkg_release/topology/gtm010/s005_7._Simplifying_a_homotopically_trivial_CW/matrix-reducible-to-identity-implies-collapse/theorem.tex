Suppose that $(K, L)$ is a pair in simplified form which has matrix $A$ with respect to some set of characteristic maps. Suppose further that $A$ can be transformed to an identity matrix $I_q$ by operations of types (I)-(V), where (I), (II) and (III) are as in (8.3) and (IV) and (V) are the analogous column operations:
IV. $C_j \rightarrow \pm C_j \cdot \alpha$ ($\alpha \in \pi_1 \subset \mathbb{Z}\pi_1$)
V. $C_k \rightarrow C_k + C_i\rho$ ($\rho \in \mathbb{Z}\pi_1$)
Then $K \wedge L$ rel $L$.
