Given a pair of connected complexes $(P, P_0)$ and a point $x \in P_0$, $\pi_1 = \pi_1(P_0, x)$ acts on $\pi_n(P, P_0; x)$ by the condition that $[\alpha] \cdot [\varphi] = [\varphi']$, where $\varphi' : (I^n, I^{n-1}, J^{n-1}) \rightarrow (P, P_0, x)$ is homotopic to $\varphi$ by a homotopy dragging $\varphi(J^{n-1})$ along the loop $\alpha^{-1}$. This action makes $\pi_n(P, P_0; x)$ a $\mathbb{Z}\pi_1$-module via $(\sum n_j [\alpha_j])[\varphi] = \sum n_j ([\alpha_j] \cdot [\varphi])$.
