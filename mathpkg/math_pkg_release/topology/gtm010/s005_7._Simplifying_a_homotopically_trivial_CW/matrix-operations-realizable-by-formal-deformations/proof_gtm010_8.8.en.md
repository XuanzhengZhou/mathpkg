For I (multiply row by $\pm\alpha$): Change the characteristic map by composing with the covering transformation corresponding to $\alpha$. For II (add multiple of row): Attach an extra cell and use geometric constructions to modify the characteristic maps. For III (add corner identity): Use elementary expansions to add pairs of cells with identity boundary.
