$Wh$ is a covariant functor from the category of groups and group homomorphisms to the category of abelian groups and group homomorphisms given by
$$G \mapsto Wh(G)$$
$$(f: G \rightarrow G') \mapsto (f_*: Wh(G) \rightarrow Wh(G'))$$
where $f$ first induces the ring homomorphism $\mathbb{Z}(G) \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}(G')$ given by $\sum n_i g_i \mapsto \sum n_i f(g_i)$, and this in turn induces $f_*$ on Whitehead groups.
