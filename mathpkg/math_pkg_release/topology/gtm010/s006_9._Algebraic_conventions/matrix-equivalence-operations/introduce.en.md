These row and column operations define the elementary equivalence relation on matrices that generates the subgroup $E(R)$ of $GL(R)$. Operation $I_R$ allows adding a left multiple of one block of rows to another, while $II_R$ allows swapping two blocks of rows with a sign change. The column operations $I_C$ and $II_C$ are analogous. These elementary operations form the foundation of algebraic $K$-theory and are used to define the Whitehead group as $GL(R)/E(R)$.
