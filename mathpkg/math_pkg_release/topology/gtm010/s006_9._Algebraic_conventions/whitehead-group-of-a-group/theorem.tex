For any group $G$, the Whitehead group $Wh(G)$ is defined by
$$Wh(G) = K_T(\mathbb{Z}G) = GL(\mathbb{Z}G) / E_T(\mathbb{Z}G)$$
where $T = \{\pm g : g \in G\}$ is the group of trivial units of $\mathbb{Z}G$, and $E_T$ is the group generated by $E(\mathbb{Z}G)$ and all diagonal matrices with entries in $T$.
