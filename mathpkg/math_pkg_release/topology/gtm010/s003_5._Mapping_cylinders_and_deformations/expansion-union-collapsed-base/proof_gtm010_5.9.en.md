Let $g: K \to L$ be a cellular deformation corresponding to the collapse $K \searrow L$. Apply the relativity principle (5.3) to the expansion $K \nearrow P$. Since $K \wedge P$ rel $K$, the relativity principle yields $K \cup_g L \wedge P \cup_g L$ rel $L$. But $K \cup_g L \equiv L$ (since $g$ is a deformation retraction), so $L \wedge P \cup_g L$ rel $L$. Equivalently, $K \nearrow P \cup_g L$ rel $L$, which means $K \wedge P \cup_g L$ rel $L$.
