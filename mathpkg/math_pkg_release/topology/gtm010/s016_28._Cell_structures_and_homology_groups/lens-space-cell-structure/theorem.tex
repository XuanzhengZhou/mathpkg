The lens space $L = L(p; q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ has a natural CW structure with one cell in each dimension $0, 1, 2, \ldots, 2n-1$. The cellular chain complex of the universal cover $\tilde{L}$ (which is $S^{2n-1}$) is
$$0 \rightarrow C_{2n-1} \rightarrow C_{2n-2} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow C_1 \rightarrow C_0 \rightarrow 0$$
where each $C_k \cong \mathbb{Z}(\mathbb{Z}_p)$ and the boundary operators are multiplication by $(t^{q_j} - 1)$ in odd dimensions.
