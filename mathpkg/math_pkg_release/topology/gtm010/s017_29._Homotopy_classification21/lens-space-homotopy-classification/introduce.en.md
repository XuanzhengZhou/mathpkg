Lens spaces L(p; q_1,...,q_n) and L(p; q'_1,...,q'_n) are homotopy equivalent precisely when the n-tuples of parameters (q_1,...,q_n) and (q'_1,...,q'_n) differ by a signed square and a permutation modulo p. This classification reduces the geometric problem of homotopy equivalence to elementary number theory.
