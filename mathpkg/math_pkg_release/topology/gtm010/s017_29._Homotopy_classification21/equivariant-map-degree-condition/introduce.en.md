This theorem gives the precise arithmetic condition for the existence of an equivariant map between spheres with prescribed degree and group action behavior. The congruence condition relates the degree $d$, the exponent $a$ encoding the $\pi_1$-action, and the lens space parameters $(q_1, \dots, q_n)$ and $(q'_1, \dots, q'_n)$. The proof constructs explicit maps via joins of maps on the circle factors. This formula is the computational heart of the homotopy classification of lens spaces.
