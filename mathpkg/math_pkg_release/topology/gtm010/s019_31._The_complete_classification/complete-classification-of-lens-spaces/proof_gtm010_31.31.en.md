The proof of (1) uses the s-cobordism theorem and the fact that for n >= 3, the Whitehead group Wh(Z_p) can be computed (it is free abelian of known rank). The realization theorem shows every element is realized. The classification then follows from the surgery exact sequence. For n = 2, the 3-dimensional case, extra complications arise from the fact that the s-cobordism theorem requires dimension >= 5.
