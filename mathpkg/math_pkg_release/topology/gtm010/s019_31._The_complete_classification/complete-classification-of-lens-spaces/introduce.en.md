The complete classification of lens spaces up to homeomorphism, simple-homotopy equivalence, and homotopy equivalence provides the crowning achievement of the theory developed in this book. For dimensions >= 5, homeomorphism type, p.l. homeomorphism type, and simple-homotopy type all coincide for lens spaces. This demonstrates the power of Whitehead torsion as a classification tool.
