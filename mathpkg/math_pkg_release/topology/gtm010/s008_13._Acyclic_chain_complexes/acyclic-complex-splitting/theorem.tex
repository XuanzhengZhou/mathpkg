If $C$ is an acyclic $(R,G)$-complex with chain contraction $\delta$, then for each $i$,
$$C_i = B_i \oplus \delta B_{i-1}$$
where $B_i = d C_{i+1}$ and $\delta B_{i-1}$ is the image of the section $\delta_i: B_{i-1} \rightarrow C_i$. Moreover $d|_{\delta B_{i-1}}: \delta B_{i-1} \xrightarrow{\cong} B_{i-1}$ is an isomorphism with inverse $\delta|_{B_{i-1}}$.
