To confirm that the Whitehead group $\mathrm{Wh}(L)$ is indeed a group, one must exhibit additive inverses. For any class $[K, L]$, the inverse is given by the double mapping cylinder $2M_D$ of a cellular strong deformation retraction $D: K \to L$. This construction takes two copies of the mapping cylinder $M_D$, glued along $K$, and the resulting equivalence class $[2M_D, L]$ cancels $[K, L]$ under the group operation.
