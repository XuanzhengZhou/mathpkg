Let K be a CW complex such that K \searrow L and such that tau(K, L) = -tau_0. Such a complex exists by the first definition (Section 6) of Wh(L). Let f: K -> L be a homotopy inverse to the inclusion map i: L -> K. Then (22.3)-(22.5) yield tau(f) = -f_* tau(i) = -f_* i_* tau(K, L) = -(-tau_0) = tau_0.
