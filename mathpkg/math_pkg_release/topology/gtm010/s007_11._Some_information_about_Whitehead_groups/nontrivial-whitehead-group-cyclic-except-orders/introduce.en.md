For abelian groups containing an element of order q not equal to 1, 2, 3, 4, or 6, the Whitehead group is non-trivial. The proof constructs explicit non-trivial units in the integral group ring using cyclotomic polynomials. This shows that simple-homotopy theory is not vacuous for most fundamental groups.
