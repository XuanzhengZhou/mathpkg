Higman's theorem states that the Whitehead group of the infinite cyclic group Z is zero. The proof uses the fact that the Laurent polynomial ring Z[t, t^{-1}] has only trivial units (monomials +/- t^k), and any invertible matrix over this ring can be reduced to a 1x1 matrix of a trivial unit by elementary operations.
