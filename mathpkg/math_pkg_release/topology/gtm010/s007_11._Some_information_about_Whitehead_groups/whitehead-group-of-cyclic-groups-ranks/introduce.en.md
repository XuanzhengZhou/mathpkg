The Whitehead group of a finite cyclic group Z_q is a free abelian group whose rank depends on the number-theoretic properties of q. The rank formula rk Wh(Z_q) = [q/2] + 1 - d(q) connects algebraic K-theory to elementary number theory. Only for q = 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 is the group finite (zero).
