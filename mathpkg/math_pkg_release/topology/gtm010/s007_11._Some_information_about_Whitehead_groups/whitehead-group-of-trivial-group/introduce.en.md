The Whitehead group of the trivial group is zero: $Wh(\{1\}) = 0$. This was already established in the course of proving result (8.5) earlier in the text. It serves as the simplest base case for Whitehead group computations and confirms that the trivial group presents no obstruction in simple-homotopy theory.
