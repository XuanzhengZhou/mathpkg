For a CW complex $K$, define
$$Wh(K) = \left[ \bigcup_{x \in K} Wh(\pi_1(K, x)) \right] / \sim$$
where $a \sim b$ if $a \in Wh(\pi_1(K, x))$, $b \in Wh(\pi_1(K, y))$ and $f_{x,y}(a) = b$, where $f_{x,y}$ is induced by the change-of-basepoint isomorphism. Let $j_x: Wh(\pi_1(K, x)) \rightarrow Wh(K)$ be the natural bijection.
