The torsion of a homotopically trivial CW pair is the torsion of the cellular chain complex of its universal cover, viewed as a $Wh(\pi_1 L)$-complex. This definition synthesizes the geometry of Chapter II with the algebra of Chapter III, establishing the bridge between topological simple-homotopy theory and algebraic K-theory.
