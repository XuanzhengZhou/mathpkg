The product theorem relates the torsion of a product to the Euler characteristic of the other factor. For a pair, tau(K x P, K_0 x P) = chi(P) tau(K, K_0). For a product of maps, tau(f x g) = chi(L') i_* tau(f) + chi(L) j_* tau(g). The Euler characteristic appears as a natural coefficient in these formulas.
