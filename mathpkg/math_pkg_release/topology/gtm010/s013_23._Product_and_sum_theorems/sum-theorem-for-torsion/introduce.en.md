The sum theorem expresses the torsion of a map between unions in terms of the torsions of its restrictions to the pieces. This is the analog of the Mayer-Vietoris sequence for Whitehead torsion. It is a powerful computational tool that allows torsion to be computed by decomposing spaces into simpler pieces.
