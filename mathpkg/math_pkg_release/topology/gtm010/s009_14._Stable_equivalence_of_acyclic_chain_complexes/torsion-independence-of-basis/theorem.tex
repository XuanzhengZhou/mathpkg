If $c$ and $c'$ are two preferred bases for the acyclic $(R, G)$-complex $C$, and $\delta$ is a chain contraction, then
$$\tau(\langle d + \delta \rangle_{c'}) = \tau(\langle d + \delta \rangle_c) + \sum_i (-1)^i \tau\langle c'_i / c_i \rangle$$
where $\langle c'_i / c_i \rangle$ is the change-of-basis matrix in dimension $i$.
