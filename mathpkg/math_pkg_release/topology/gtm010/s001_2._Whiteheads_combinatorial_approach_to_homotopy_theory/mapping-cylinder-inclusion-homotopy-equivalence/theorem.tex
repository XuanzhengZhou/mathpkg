Suppose that $f: X \rightarrow Y$ is a map. Let $i: X \rightarrow M_f$ be the inclusion map. Then
(a) The following is a commutative diagram
$$\begin{array}{ccc}
X & \xrightarrow{i} & M_f \\
{\scriptstyle f}\downarrow & \swarrow{\scriptstyle p} & \\
Y & &
\end{array}$$
(b) $i$ is a homotopy equivalence iff $f$ is a homotopy equivalence.
