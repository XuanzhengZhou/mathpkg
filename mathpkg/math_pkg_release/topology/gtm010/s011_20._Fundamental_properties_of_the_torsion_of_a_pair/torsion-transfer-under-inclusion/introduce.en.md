When the base space is included in a larger complex, the torsion transfers via the induced map on Whitehead groups. This result is essential for tracking how torsion behaves under the various inclusions that arise when decomposing CW pairs. Combined with the sum formula (20.2), it provides the computational tools needed to relate the torsion of a pair to the torsion of its components.
