When every component of $K-L$ is simply connected, the universal cover lifts each component homeomorphically, and the preferred basis for the cellular chain complex can be chosen so that each cell is represented by a single basis element (not an entire orbit under the group action). The resulting $Wh(G)$-complex has the identity matrix as its boundary operator, yielding zero torsion. This property explains why torsion is fundamentally a $\pi_1$ phenomenon — it only becomes nontrivial when the fundamental group acts nontrivially on the cells of the universal cover.
