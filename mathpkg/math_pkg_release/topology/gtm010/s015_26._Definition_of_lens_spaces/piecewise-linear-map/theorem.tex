If $M_1$ and $M_2$ are p.l. manifolds of dimensions $m$ and $n$ respectively, then $f: M_1 \to M_2$ is a \text{p.l. map} if, for each $x \in M_1$, there is a coordinate chart $(U, h)$ about $x$ and a coordinate chart $(V, g)$ about $f(x)$ such that the map $g f h^{-1}: h[U \cap f^{-1}V] \to \mathbb{R}^m$ is piecewise linear.
