If $p > 2$, the lens space $L(p; q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ is p.l. homeomorphic to $S^{2n-1}/\bar{G}$ where $\bar{G}$ is the cyclic group generated by an orthogonal transformation of $S^{2n-1}$ given by rotating the $j$-th 2-plane through $2\pi q_j/p$ radians. The homeomorphism is covered by the radial projection $T: \Sigma^{2n-1} \rightarrow S^{2n-1}$.
