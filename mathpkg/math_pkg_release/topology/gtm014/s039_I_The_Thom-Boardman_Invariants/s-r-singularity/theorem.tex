For a mapping $f: X \rightarrow Y$, we say that $f$ has a singularity of type $S_r$ at $x$ in $X$ if $(df)_x$ drops rank by $r$; i.e., if
$$\text{rank}(df)_x = \min(\dim X, \dim Y) - r.$$
Denote by $S_r(f)$ the set of singularities of $f$ of type $S_r$. Then
$$S_r(f) = (j^1 f)^{-1}(S_r),$$
where $S_r \subset J^1(X, Y)$ is the submanifold of jets of corank $r$.
