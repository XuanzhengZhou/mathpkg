Let $G$ be a Lie group. A subset $H \subset G$ is a \textbf{Lie subgroup} if:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $H$ is a subgroup of $G$;
\item[(ii)] $H$ is an immersed submanifold of $G$; and
\item[(iii)] $H$ is a Lie group with the group operation from (i) and the manifold structure from (ii).
\end{enumerate}
