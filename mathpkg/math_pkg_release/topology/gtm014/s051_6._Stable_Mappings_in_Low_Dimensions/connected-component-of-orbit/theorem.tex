Let $G$ be a Lie group acting on a manifold $X$ and let $p \in X$. The connected component of the orbit $\mathcal{O}_p$ containing $p$ is $\mathring{G} \cdot p = \{\tau(g)(p) \mid g \in \mathring{G}\}$, where $\mathring{G}$ is the connected component of the identity in $G$ and $\tau$ denotes the action.
