Let $X$ be a smooth manifold and let $G$ be a Lie group.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] An \textbf{action} of $G$ on $X$ is a homomorphism $\tau: G \to \operatorname{Diff}(X)$ such that the mapping $G \times X \to X$ given by $(g, x) \mapsto \tau(g)(x)$ is smooth.
\item[(b)] For $p \in X$, the \textbf{orbit} through $p$ is $\mathcal{O}_p = \{\tau(g)(p) \mid g \in G\}$.
\item[(c)] The \textbf{isotropy subgroup} at $p$ is $H_p = \{g \in G \mid \tau(g)(p) = p\}$.
\end{enumerate}
$H_p$ is a closed subgroup of $G$ and thus a Lie subgroup of $G$.
