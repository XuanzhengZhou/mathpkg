In dimensions $\leq 4$, for a map germ to be stable it is necessary and sufficient that it exhibit only singularities on the list
$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\text{dim 1} & S_1 \text{ (folds)} \\
\text{dim 2} & S_1, S_{1_2} \text{ (folds, cusps)} \\
\text{dim 3} & S_1, S_{1_2}, S_{1_3} \\
\text{dim 4} & S_1, S_{1_2}, S_{1_3}, S_{1_4},\; \text{elliptic and hyperbolic umbilics } (S_{2,0})
\end{array}
$$
and that it exhibit these singularities transversely.
