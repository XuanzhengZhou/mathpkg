Let $E, F,$ and $G$ be vector bundles. Let $\rho : E \rightarrow F$ be a vector bundle morphism, and let $\tau : E \rightarrow G$ be a vector bundle isomorphism. Prove the chain rule formula for the intrinsic derivative:

$$D(\tau \cdot \rho)_x = \hat{\tau} \cdot (D\rho)_x$$

where $\hat{\tau} : \text{Hom}(\ker \rho, \text{coker } \rho) \rightarrow \text{Hom}(\ker \rho, \text{coker } \tau\rho)$ is the obvious map induced by $\tau$.
