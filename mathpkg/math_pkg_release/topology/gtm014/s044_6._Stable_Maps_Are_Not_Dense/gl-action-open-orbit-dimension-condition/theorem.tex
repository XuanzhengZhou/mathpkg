Let $V$ and $W$ be vector spaces of dimension $n$. Let $G$ be the group $GL(V) \times GL(W)$ acting on the space $\text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)$ by its standard representation. (The action is given as follows: let $C$ be in $\text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)$ and let $(A, B)$ be in $G$, then $(A, B) \cdot C(\bar{v}) = B \circ C \circ A^{-1}(\bar{v})$ for $\bar{v}$ in $V \circ V$. Note that $A(v_1 \otimes v_2) = Av_1 \otimes Av_2$ and $V \circ V$ is an invariant subspace of $V \otimes V$ under this representation.) Then $G$ has an open orbit only when $n < 3$.
