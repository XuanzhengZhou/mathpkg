Let $\sigma = j^k f(x)$ be in $A_W$. Choose coordinate neighborhoods $U$ and $V$ about $x$ and $f(x)$ such that $f(\bar{U}) \subset V$ and let $\rho$ be a function which is $1$ near $x$ and has support in $U$. Consider the set of maps $g$ defined by

$$g = \begin{cases} f + \rho h & \text{in } U \\ f & \text{in } X - U \end{cases}$$

where $h$ is a polynomial map of degree $\leq k$. If the coefficients of $h$ are sufficiently small this is well-defined (i.e., $g(\bar{U}) \subset V$) and is in $W$. The set $j^k g(y)$ for $y$ near $x$ and $h$ with coefficients small define an open neighborhood of $J^k(X, Y)$ in $A_W$.
