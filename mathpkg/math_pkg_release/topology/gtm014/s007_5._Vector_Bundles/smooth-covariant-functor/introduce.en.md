A smooth covariant functor is a covariant functor $T$ on the category of finite-dimensional real vector spaces whose action on Hom-sets is itself a smooth map between the corresponding smooth manifolds. Identifying $\operatorname{Hom}(V,W)$ with $\mathbf{R}^{m \cdot n}$ via a choice of bases gives Hom-sets a smooth structure; the smoothness of $T$ is independent of this choice and ensures that applying $T$ to vector bundle transition functions yields smooth transitions for the associated bundle.
