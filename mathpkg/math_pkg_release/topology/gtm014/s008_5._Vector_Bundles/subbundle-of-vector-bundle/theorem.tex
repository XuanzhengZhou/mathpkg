Let $E$ be a vector bundle over $X$ with projection $\pi$. A subset $F \subset E$ is a \textbf{subbundle} of $E$ if $F$ is a smooth submanifold of $E$ and the restriction $\pi|_F: F \to X$ is a vector bundle where, for each $x \in X$, the fiber $F_x$ has the vector space structure induced from $E_x$.
