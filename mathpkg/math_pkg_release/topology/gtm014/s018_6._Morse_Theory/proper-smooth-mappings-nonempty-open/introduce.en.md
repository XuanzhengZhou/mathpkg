Lemma 5.10 of GTM014 establishes that for any smooth manifold $X$, the space of smooth proper maps from $X$ into Euclidean space $\mathbb{R}^m$ is both nonempty and open in the $C^0$ Whitney topology. This result is a crucial technical step in proving the Whitney Embedding Theorem, as it guarantees the existence of proper immersions that can then be perturbed to embeddings.
