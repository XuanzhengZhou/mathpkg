Lemma 6.10 provides a first-order Taylor expansion with smooth remainder coefficients on a convex open set. Given a smooth function $f$ and a point $a$, the difference $f(x) - f(a)$ can be expressed as a linear combination of $(x_i - a_i)$ with smooth coefficient functions $g_i$, whose values at $a$ are the partial derivatives. The proof uses the fundamental theorem of calculus along line segments in the convex set.
