Let $U$ be an open convex nbhd of $0$ in $\mathbf{R}^n$. Let $f: U \to \mathbf{R}$ be a smooth function with a nondegenerate critical point at $0$. Assume that
$$\left(\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x_i \partial x_j}\right)(0) = \pm \delta_{ij}.$$
Then there exists a nbhd $V$ of $0$ contained in $U$ and smooth functions $h_i: V \to \mathbf{R}$ ($1 \leq i \leq n$) satisfying:

(a) $h_i(0) = 0$ and $\displaystyle\frac{\partial h_i}{\partial x_j}(0) = \pm \delta_{ij}$;

(b) $f(x) = f(0) + (\pm h_1^2(x) \pm \cdots \pm h_n^2(x))$ for all $x \in V$.
