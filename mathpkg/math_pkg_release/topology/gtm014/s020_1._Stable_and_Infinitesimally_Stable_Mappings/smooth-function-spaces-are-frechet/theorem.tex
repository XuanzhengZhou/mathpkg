Let $X$ be a compact finite-dimensional smooth manifold.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $C^\infty(X, \mathbf{R})$ is a Frechet space.
\item[(b)] If $E$ is a smooth vector bundle over $X$, then $C^\infty(E)$, the space of smooth sections of $E$, is a Frechet space.
\item[(c)] Let $f: X \to Y$ be a smooth map into a smooth manifold $Y$. Then $C_f^\infty(X, TY)$, the space of vector fields along $f$, is a Frechet space.
\end{enumerate}

In each case the topology is the Whitney $C^\infty$ topology.
