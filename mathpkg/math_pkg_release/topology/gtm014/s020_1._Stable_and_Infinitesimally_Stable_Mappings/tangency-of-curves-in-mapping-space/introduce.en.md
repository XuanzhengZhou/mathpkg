This lemma provides a pointwise criterion for tangency of curves in the Frechet manifold $C^\infty(X, Y)$. Two smooth families of mappings passing through the same map at $t=0$ are tangent if and only if, evaluating at each point of the domain, the resulting curves in the target manifold are tangent. This characterization is essential for identifying the tangent space $T_f C^\infty(X, Y)$ with $C_f^\infty(X, TY)$ and for computing the derivative of the action map.
