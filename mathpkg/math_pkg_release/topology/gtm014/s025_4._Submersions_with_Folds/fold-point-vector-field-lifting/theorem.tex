{\textbf{Lemma 4.7.}} Let $p$ be a fold point of a smooth map $f: X \rightarrow Y$ and let $\tau$ be a vector field along $f$ defined on some neighborhood $U$ of $p$ such that
$$\tau \big|_{S_1(f) \cap U} = 0.$$
Then there exists a vector field $\zeta$ defined on a neighborhood $V \subset U$ of $p$ such that
$$\tau = (df)(\zeta)$$
on $V$.
