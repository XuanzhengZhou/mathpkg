The following diagram commutes:
\[
\begin{CD}
C^{\infty}(TY)_{q} @>{(d\gamma_{1})}>> T_{\sigma}\mathcal{D}_{\sigma} \\
@V{\pi_{k}^{\infty}}VV @VVV \\
J^{k}(TY)_{q} @>{f^{*}}>> J^{k}(f^{*}TY)_{p}
\end{CD}
\]
where $(d\gamma_{1}): C^{\infty}(TY)_{q} \to T_{\sigma}\mathcal{D}_{\sigma}$ is the $k$-jet version of the infinitesimal action of $\operatorname{Diff}(Y)$ on $C^{\infty}(X,Y)$, $\pi_{k}^{\infty}: C^{\infty}(TY)_{q} \to J^{k}(TY)_{q}$ is the canonical $k$-jet projection, $f^{*}: J^{k}(TY)_{q} \to J^{k}(f^{*}TY)_{p}$ is the pullback by $f$, and the right vertical arrow is the natural restriction map induced by the identification of $T_{\sigma}\mathcal{D}_{\sigma}$ as a subspace of $T_{\sigma}J^{k}(X,Y)$.
