Let $F$ be a holomorphic function near $0 \in \mathbf{C} \times \mathbf{C}^n$ with $F(w, 0) = w^k g(w)$, $g(0) \neq 0$. For any holomorphic $G$ near $0$, there exist holomorphic $q$ and $r$ such that $G = qF + r$, where $r(w, z) = \sum_{i=0}^{k-1} r_i(z) w^i$. Moreover $q$ and $r$ are uniquely determined.
