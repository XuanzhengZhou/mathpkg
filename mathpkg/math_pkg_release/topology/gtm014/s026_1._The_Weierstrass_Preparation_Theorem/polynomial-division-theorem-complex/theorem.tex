Let $P_k(w, \lambda) = w^k + \sum_{i=0}^{k-1} \lambda_i w^i$. For any holomorphic $G(w, z)$ near $0 \in \mathbf{C} \times \mathbf{C}^n$, there exist holomorphic $q(w, z, \lambda)$ and $r_i(z, \lambda)$ ($0 \leq i \leq k-1$) such that
$$G(w, z) = q(w, z, \lambda) P_k(w, \lambda) + \sum_{i=0}^{k-1} r_i(z, \lambda) w^i$$
near $0 \in \mathbf{C} \times \mathbf{C}^n \times \mathbf{C}^k$.
