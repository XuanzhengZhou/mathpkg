Lemma 3.7 treats the special case of the Generalized Malgrange Preparation Theorem where the map is a submersion with fiber dimension one. Given a finitely generated $C_p^\infty(X)$-module $A$, if the quotient $A / \mathcal{M}_q(Y)A$ is finite dimensional, then $A$ is already finitely generated as a $C_q^\infty(Y)$-module. The proof uses a choice of generators whose images form a basis of the quotient, shows they generate $A$ as a $C_p^\infty(X)$-module via Corollary 3.5, and then applies a Nakayama-type decreasing chain argument to bound the dimension of the quotient.
