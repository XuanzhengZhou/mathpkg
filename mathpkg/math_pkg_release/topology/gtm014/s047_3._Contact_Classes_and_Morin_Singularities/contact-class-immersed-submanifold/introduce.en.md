Theorem 3.12 states that for any local ring $\mathcal{R}$, the corresponding contact class $S_{\mathcal{R}}$ is an immersed submanifold of the jet bundle $J^k(X, Y)$. This generalizes the Boardman result for $S_{r,s}$ singularities. The proof uses the natural action of the Lie group $G^k(\mathbf{R}^n)_0$ on the space of $k$-th order contact elements $H_{k,l}^n$ and the fact that $S_{\mathcal{R}} \cap J^k(X, Y)_{p,q}$ is a submanifold.
