Proposition 3.13 establishes that the Morin singularity class $S_{1_k}$ forms a codimension $k$ submanifold of the jet bundle $J^k(X, Y)$. This result is fundamental for the theory of Morin singularities, as it provides the dimension counts needed for transversality arguments and shows that these singularities occur in a controlled, geometrically meaningful way.
