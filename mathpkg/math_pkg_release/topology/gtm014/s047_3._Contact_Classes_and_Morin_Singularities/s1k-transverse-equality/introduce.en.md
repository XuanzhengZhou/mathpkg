Proposition 3.15 shows that when the jet extension of $f$ is transverse to all lower-order Morin singularity classes, the iteratively defined (Boardman-style) $S_{1_k}^k(f)$ coincides with the directly defined $S_{1_k}(f)$. This reconciliation between the Thom-Boardman iterative definition and the Morin direct definition ensures consistency of the two classification approaches.
