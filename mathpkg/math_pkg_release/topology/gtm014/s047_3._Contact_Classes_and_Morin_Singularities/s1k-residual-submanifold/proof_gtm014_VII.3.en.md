By Proposition 3.13, $S_{1_k}$ is a codimension $k$ submanifold of $J^k(X, Y)$. Applying the Thom Transversality Theorem, there exists a residual set of maps $f \in C^\infty(X, Y)$ such that $j^k f \pitchfork S_{1_k}$. For any such map $f$, the pre-image $S_{1_k}(f) = (j^k f)^{-1}(S_{1_k})$ is a submanifold of $X$ whose codimension equals the codimension of $S_{1_k}$ in $J^k(X, Y)$, namely $k$.
