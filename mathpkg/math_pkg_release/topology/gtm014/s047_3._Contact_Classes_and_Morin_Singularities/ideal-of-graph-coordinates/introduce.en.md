This lemma provides an explicit computation of the ideal $\mathcal{I}_p(A, B)$ in the crucial special case where $A$ is the zero-section $\mathbf{R}^k \times \{0\}$ and $B$ is the graph of a smooth map germ $b: \mathbf{R}^k \to \mathbf{R}^l$. The result shows that the pullback of the ideal of functions vanishing on the graph is precisely the ideal generated by the coordinate functions $b_1, \ldots, b_l$ of the map germ $b$. This computation is the essential bridge between the geometric contact theory and the algebraic theory of local rings of map germs.
