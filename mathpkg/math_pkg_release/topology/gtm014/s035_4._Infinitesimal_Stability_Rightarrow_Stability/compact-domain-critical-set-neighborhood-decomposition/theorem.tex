Let $X$ be compact, let $f \colon X \to Y$ be infinitesimally stable, and let
$F$ be a $k$-deformation of $f$. Then there exist vector fields $\zeta$
on $X \times \mathbf{R}^k$ and $\eta$ on $Y \times \mathbf{R}^k$ with
the $\mathbf{R}^k$-components of $\zeta$ and $\eta$ equal to zero on a
neighborhood $B$ of $\Sigma \times \{0\}$ in $X \times \mathbf{R}^k$ such that
$$\tau_F = (dF)(\zeta) + F^*\eta$$
on $B$.
