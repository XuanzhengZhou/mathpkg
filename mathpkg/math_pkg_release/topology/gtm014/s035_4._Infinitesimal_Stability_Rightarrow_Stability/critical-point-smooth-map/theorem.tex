Let $f \colon X \to Y$ be a smooth map between manifolds.
A point $p \in X$ is a \emph{critical point} of $f$ if the differential
$(df)_p \colon T_p X \to T_{f(p)} Y$ is not surjective.
For $q \in Y$, let
$$\Sigma_q = \{ p \in f^{-1}(q) \mid (df)_p \text{ is not surjective} \}$$
denote the set of critical points of $f$ in the fiber $f^{-1}(q)$.
The \emph{critical point set} of $f$ is
$$\Sigma = \bigcup_{q \in Y} \Sigma_q.$$
By Lemma~1.8, each $\Sigma_q$ is a finite set.
