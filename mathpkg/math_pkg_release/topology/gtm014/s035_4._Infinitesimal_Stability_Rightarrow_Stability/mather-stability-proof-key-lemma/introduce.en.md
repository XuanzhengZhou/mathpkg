The local-to-global lemma is crucial: it shows that stability (a global condition about neighborhoods in $C^\infty(X, Y)$) follows from pointwise conditions on finite-dimensionality of certain quotient modules. The proof uses a partition of unity argument combined with the compactness of $X$ (via properness) and the preparation theorem to patch local solutions.
