Let $X, Y$ be smooth manifolds, $W \subset Y$ a submanifold, and $f: X \to Y$ smooth. Let $p \in X$ and $f(p) \in W$. Suppose there is a neighborhood $U$ of $f(p)$ in $Y$ and a submersion $\phi: U \to \mathbf{R}^k$ (where $k = \operatorname{codim} W$) such that $W \cap U = \phi^{-1}(0)$. Then $f \pitchfork W$ at $p$ iff $\phi \circ f$ is a submersion at $p$.
