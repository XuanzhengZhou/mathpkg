This is a corollary to the proof of the Thom Transversality Theorem. Under the assumptions of the corollary, it is clear that for each $W_r$ in the decomposition of $W$, $\alpha(\overline{W}_r) \subset U$. Thus we can choose $U_r$ so that $U_r \subset U$. Then note that the perturbation $g_b = f$ off $U_r$, and thus off $U$.
