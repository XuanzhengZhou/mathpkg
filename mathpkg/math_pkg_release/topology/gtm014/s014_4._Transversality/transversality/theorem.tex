Let $X$ and $Y$ be smooth manifolds and $f: X \to Y$ be a smooth mapping. Let $W$ be a submanifold of $Y$ and $x$ a point in $X$. Then $f$ intersects $W$ transversally at $x$ (denoted by $f \pitchfork W$ at $x$) if either
$$
\text{(a) } f(x) \notin W,
$$
or
$$
\text{(b) } f(x) \in W \text{ and } T_{f(x)}Y = T_{f(x)}W + (df)_x(T_x X).
$$
If $A$ is a subset of $X$, then $f$ intersects $W$ transversally on $A$ (denoted by $f \pitchfork W$ on $A$) if $f \pitchfork W$ at $x$ for all $x \in A$. Finally, $f$ intersects $W$ transversally (denoted by $f \pitchfork W$) if $f \pitchfork W$ on $X$.
