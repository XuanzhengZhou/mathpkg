Exercise 2 from Chapter VII, Section 4. This exercise studies the parametric map $(x_1, x_2) \mapsto (x_1^2, x_2, x_1 x_2)$, which is a standard local model for the cross-cap (also known as the Whitney umbrella). The task is to describe its image, analyze the images of coordinate curves, and show that the map is injective except along the double line $x_2 = 0$, where it folds onto itself. This provides geometric intuition for cross-cap singularities.
