Let $f: X \to \mathbf{R}^{2n}$ be an immersion of the $n$-manifold $X$. Let $v$ be a regular value of the induced map

$$
df: TX \to \mathbf{R}^{2n}
$$

and let $\pi: \mathbf{R}^{2n} \to \mathbf{R}^{2n-1}$ be a surjective linear map with $v$ in its kernel. Show that $\pi \circ f$ has no singularities except cross-caps. (Compare with Chapter II, Section 1, Exercise 1.)
