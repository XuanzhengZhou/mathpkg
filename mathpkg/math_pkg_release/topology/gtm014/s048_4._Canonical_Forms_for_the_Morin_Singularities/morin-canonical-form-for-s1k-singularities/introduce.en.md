Theorem 4.1 provides a canonical coordinate form for equidimensional Morin singularities of type $S_{1_k}$. Under the jet transversality condition $j^k f \pitchfork S_{1_k}$, any such singularity at $x_0$ can be expressed in local coordinates as $f^*y_1 = \sum_{i=2}^k x_i x_1^{i-1} + x_1^{k+1}$ with $f^*y_i = x_i$ for $i \geq 2$. The result is due to B. Morin and generalizes the Whitney canonical forms for plane-to-plane map singularities in dimension 2.
