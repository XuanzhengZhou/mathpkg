Let $X$ be an $n$-dimensional manifold, $Y$ a $(2n-1)$-dimensional manifold, and $f: X \to Y$ a smooth map. A point $x_0 \in S_1(f)$ is called a \textit{cross-cap} if $j^1 f \pitchfork S_1$ at $x_0$.

For these dimensions, $\operatorname{codim} S_1 = n$, so cross-caps occur as isolated points of $X$.
