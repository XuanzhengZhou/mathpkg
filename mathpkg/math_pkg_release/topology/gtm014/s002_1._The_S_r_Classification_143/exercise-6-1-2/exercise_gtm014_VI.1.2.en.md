Show that the sequence

$$
0 \to T_z Z_x \xrightarrow{(d\rho)_z} T_y Y \to T_y Y / (d\rho)_z(T_z Z_x) \to 0
$$

is exact, and deduce the existence of a mapping

$$
T_x X \to T_y Y / (d\rho)_z(T_z Z_x)
$$

given by equation (1.6).
