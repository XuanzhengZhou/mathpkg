Let $V$ and $W$ be finite-dimensional real vector spaces, let $r$ be a non-negative integer, and let $s = \dim V - r$. Let $Q \to G(s, V)$ be the universal quotient bundle over the Grassmannian, and let $L^0(Q, W)$ be the open subset of $\operatorname{Hom}(Q, W)$ consisting of fiberwise injective maps. Let $M \subset \operatorname{Hom}(V, W)$ be the subset of elements of corank $> r$. Then the map
$$
\pi^*: L^0(Q, W) \to \operatorname{Hom}(V, W) \setminus M
$$
is a one-to-one proper immersion.
