For a smooth mapping $f: X \to Y$, denote by $S_r(f)$ the set of singularities of $f$ of type $S_r$, i.e.,
$$
S_r(f) = \{ x \in X \mid \operatorname{rank}(df)_x = \min(\dim X, \dim Y) - r \}.
$$
Equivalently, $S_r(f) = (j^1 f)^{-1}(S_r)$, where $S_r \subset J^1(X,Y)$ is the submanifold of 1-jets of corank $r$, and $j^1 f: X \to J^1(X,Y)$ is the 1-jet extension of $f$.
