$S_r(f)$ is the subset of the domain $X$ where the smooth map $f$ has an $S_r$ singularity — that is, where its differential drops rank by exactly $r$. This set is precisely the preimage of the $S_r$ submanifold in the 1-jet bundle under the 1-jet extension map $j^1 f$. The $S_r(f)$ sets partition the singular locus of $f$, providing the first level of the Thom-Boardman stratification.
