Proposition 2.7 of GTM014 Chapter III summarizes the discussion that establishes a relationship between infinitesimal stability and stability under $k$-deformations. The proposition states that for $k$ sufficiently large (e.g., $k = \dim B_{n,m}^\ell$), infinitesimal stability is equivalent to stability under $k$-deformations. This connects the infinitesimal criterion to the geometric notion of stability under deformations. The complete statement is partially lost due to OCR fragmentation in the source section.
