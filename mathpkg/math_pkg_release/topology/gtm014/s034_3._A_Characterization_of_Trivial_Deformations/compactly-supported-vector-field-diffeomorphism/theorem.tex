Let $\zeta$ be a compactly supported vector field on $X \times \mathbf{R}^k$ such that the $\mathbf{R}^k$-component of $\zeta$ is zero. Then there is a diffeomorphism $g: X \times \mathbf{R}^k \rightarrow X \times \mathbf{R}^k$ which is a deformation of $\text{id}_X$ satisfying

$$(*) \quad (dg)(g^{-1})^*\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial t_k}\right) = \zeta + \frac{\partial}{\partial t_k}.$$
