Lemma 1.2 states that the countable union of measure zero sets in $\mathbf{R}^n$ again has measure zero. This means the collection of measure zero sets forms a $\sigma$-ideal. This lemma is one of two results needed to show that the definition of measure zero on a manifold (Definition 1.1(2)) is independent of the choice of charts.
