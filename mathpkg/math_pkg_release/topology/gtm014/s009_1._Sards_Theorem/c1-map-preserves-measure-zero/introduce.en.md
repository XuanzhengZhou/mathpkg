Proposition 1.3 asserts that $C^1$ maps from an open subset of $\mathbf{R}^n$ to $\mathbf{R}^n$ preserve measure zero sets. The proof uses the fact that on a large open cube the derivative norm $|(df)_p|$ is bounded by some constant $K$, giving a Lipschitz estimate. Together with Lemma 1.2, this guarantees that the definition of measure zero on manifolds (via charts) is well-defined.
