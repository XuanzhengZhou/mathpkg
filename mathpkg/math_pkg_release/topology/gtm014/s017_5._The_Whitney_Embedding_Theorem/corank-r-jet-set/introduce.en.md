The set $S_r \subseteq J^1(X, Y)$ consists of all 1-jets whose corank equals $r$. These subsets form a stratification of the 1-jet bundle, with $S_0$ corresponding to jets of maximal rank. The $S_r$ are the first level of the Thom--Boardman stratification and play a central role in singularity theory. As shown in Theorem 5.4, each $S_r$ is a submanifold of $J^1(X, Y)$ of a specific codimension, making the stratification geometrically meaningful.
