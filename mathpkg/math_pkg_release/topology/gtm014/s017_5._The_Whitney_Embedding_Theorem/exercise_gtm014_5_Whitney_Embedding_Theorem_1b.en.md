Assume that $X$ is compact. Let $f: X \to Y$ be smooth with $f \mathrel{\perp\!\!\!\perp} W$. Show that there is an open neighborhood $N$ of $f$ in $C^\infty(X, Y)$ (in the $C^1$ topology) such that if $g \in N$, then $g \mathrel{\perp\!\!\!\perp} W$ and the number of points in $g^{-1}(W)$ is the same as the number of points in $f^{-1}(W)$.
