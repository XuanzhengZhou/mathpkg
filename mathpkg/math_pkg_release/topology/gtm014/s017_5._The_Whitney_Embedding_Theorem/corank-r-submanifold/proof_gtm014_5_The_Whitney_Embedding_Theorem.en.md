Let $U \subset X$ and $V \subset Y$ be coordinate charts. Then

$$J^1(X, Y)|_{U \times V} \cong U \times V \times \operatorname{Hom}(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m)$$

and under this isomorphism

$$S_r \cong U \times V \times L^r(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m),$$

where $L^r(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m)$ denotes the space of linear maps from $\mathbf{R}^n$ to $\mathbf{R}^m$ of corank $r$. Applying Proposition 5.3 (which identifies $L^r(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m)$ as a submanifold of $\operatorname{Hom}(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m)$ with the stated codimension), $S_r$ is then a submanifold with the claimed codimension and a subfiber-bundle with fiber $L^r(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m)$.
