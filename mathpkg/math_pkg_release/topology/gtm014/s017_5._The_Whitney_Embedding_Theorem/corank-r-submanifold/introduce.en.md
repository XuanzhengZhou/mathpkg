Theorem 5.4 establishes that each corank-$r$ stratum $S_r$ is a submanifold (in fact, a subfiber-bundle) of the 1-jet bundle $J^1(X, Y)$. The codimension of $S_r$ is given by the formula $(n - q + r)(m - q + r)$, where $n = \dim X$, $m = \dim Y$, and $q = \min(n, m)$. The fiber of the bundle is $L^r(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m)$, the space of linear maps of corank $r$. This result forms the foundation of the Thom--Boardman stratification of jet bundles and is essential for transversality arguments in singularity theory.
