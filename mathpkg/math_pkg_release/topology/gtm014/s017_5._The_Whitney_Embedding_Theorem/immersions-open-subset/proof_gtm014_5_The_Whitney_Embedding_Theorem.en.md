$S_0$ is an open subset of $J^1(X, Y)$, since its complement $\bigcup_{r \neq 0} S_r$ is closed. By Lemma 5.1, $M(S_0) = \operatorname{Im}(X, Y)$, where $M(S_0)$ denotes the set of smooth maps $f: X \to Y$ whose 1-jet section $j^1 f$ maps into $S_0$. Since the jet evaluation map is continuous in the Whitney $C^\infty$ topology, the preimage of an open set is open. Hence $\operatorname{Im}(X, Y)$ is open in $C^\infty(X, Y)$.
