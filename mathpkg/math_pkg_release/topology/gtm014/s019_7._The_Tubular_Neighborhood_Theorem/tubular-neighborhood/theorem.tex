Let $X$ be a submanifold of the smooth manifold $Y$. A \text{tubular neighborhood} of $X$ in $Y$ is an open subset $Z$ of $Y$ together with a submersion $\pi: Z \to X$ such that

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $Z \xrightarrow{\pi} X$ is a vector bundle, and
\item[(b)] $X \subset Z$ is the zero section of this vector bundle.
\end{enumerate}
