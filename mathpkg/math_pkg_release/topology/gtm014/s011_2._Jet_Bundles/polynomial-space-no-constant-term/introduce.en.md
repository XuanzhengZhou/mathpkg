$A_n^k$ is the finite-dimensional real vector space of polynomials in $n$ variables of degree at most $k$ whose constant term vanishes. The space $B_{n,m}^k = \bigoplus_{i=1}^m A_n^k$ parametrizes $m$-tuples of such polynomials and serves as the standard fiber for the local trivialization of the jet bundle $J^k(\mathbf{R}^n, \mathbf{R}^m)$. In local coordinates, a $k$-jet is represented by the Taylor polynomial of degree $k$ (without constant term), making $B_{n,m}^k$ the natural algebraic model for the jet fiber.
