Let $X$ and $Y$ be smooth manifolds, $p \in X$, and $f, g: X \to Y$ smooth maps with $f(p) = g(p) = q$. Let $U$ be a coordinate neighborhood of $p$ with coordinates $x_1, \ldots, x_n$, and let $f_i, g_i$ be the coordinate functions of $f$ and $g$ with respect to coordinates on $Y$. Then $f \sim_k g$ at $p$ if and only if

$$\frac{\partial^{|\alpha|} f_i}{\partial x^\alpha}(p) = \frac{\partial^{|\alpha|} g_i}{\partial x^\alpha}(p)$$

for every multi-index $\alpha$ with $|\alpha| \leq k$ and $1 \leq i \leq m$.
