Let $J^1(X, \mathbf{R})_{X,0}$ be the set of all 1-jets whose target is $0$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Show that $J^1(X, \mathbf{R})_{X,0}$ is a vector bundle over $X$ whose projection is the source mapping.
\item[(b)] Show that $J^1(X, \mathbf{R})_{X,0}$ is canonically isomorphic (as vector bundles) with $T^*X$.
\end{enumerate}
