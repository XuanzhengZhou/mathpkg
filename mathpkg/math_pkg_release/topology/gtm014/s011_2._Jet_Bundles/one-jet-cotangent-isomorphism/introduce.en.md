This exercise shows a fundamental identification: 1-jets of real-valued functions with target $0$ form a vector bundle over $X$ canonically isomorphic to the cotangent bundle $T^*X$. This is because a 1-jet of a function $f$ with $f(p)=0$ is determined by its differential $df_p \in T_p^*X$, and the zero condition ensures the constant term vanishes, making the space of such 1-jets naturally a vector space.
