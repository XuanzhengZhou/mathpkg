This lemma ensures that the $k$-th order contact relation $\sim_k$ is compatible with smooth composition: post-composition with any smooth map and pre-composition with a diffeomorphism both preserve the relation. This is the essential ingredient for defining the pushforward $h_*: J^k(X,Y) \to J^k(X,Z)$ and pullback $g^*: J^k(X,Y) \to J^k(Z,Y)$ operations on jet spaces, as the equivalence class of the composite depends only on the equivalence class of the original map.
