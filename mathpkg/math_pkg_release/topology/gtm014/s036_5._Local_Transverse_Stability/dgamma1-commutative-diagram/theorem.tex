The diagram
\[
\begin{CD}
C^\infty(TY)_q @>{(d\gamma_1)}>> T_\sigma \mathcal{D}_\sigma \\
@V{\pi_k^\infty}VV @A{\lambda}AA \\
J^k(TY)_q @>{f^*}>> J^k(f^*TY)_p
\end{CD}
\]
commutes where $\pi_k^\infty: C^\infty(TY)_q \to J^k(TY)_q$ is the obvious projection, $(d\gamma_1)$ is the map induced by the action of $\mathrm{Diff}(Y)$ on $J^k(X,Y)$, and $\lambda$ is the map from Proposition 5.8.
