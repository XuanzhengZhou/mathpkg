Consider the action of $\mathrm{Diff}(X) \times \mathrm{Diff}(Y)$ on $J^k(X, Y)$ given by
$$(g, h) \cdot \sigma = j^k h(q) \cdot \sigma \cdot j^k(g^{-1})(g(p))$$
where $\sigma \in J^k(X, Y)_{p,q}$. Let $\mathcal{D}_\sigma$ be the orbit of this action through the $k$-jet $\sigma$. Then $\mathcal{D}_\sigma$ is an immersed submanifold of $J^k(X, Y)$.
