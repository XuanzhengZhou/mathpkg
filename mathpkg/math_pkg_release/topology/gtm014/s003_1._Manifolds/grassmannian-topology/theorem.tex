Let $W$ be the set of all ordered $k$-tuples $P = (P_1,\ldots,P_k)$ of $k$ linearly independent vectors in $\mathbf{R}^n$. Then $W$ is an open subset of the $k$-fold direct sum
\[
\underbrace{\mathbf{R}^n \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathbf{R}^n}_{k \text{ times}}.
\]
Define an equivalence relation $\sim$ on $W$ by
\[
P \sim Q \quad \text{if} \quad \{P_1,\ldots,P_k\} \text{ and } \{Q_1,\ldots,Q_k\}
\]
span the same $k$-dimensional subspace of $\mathbf{R}^n$.

The Grassmannian $G_{k,n}$ of $k$-planes in $\mathbf{R}^n$ is identified with the quotient $W / {\sim}$ as sets, and is given the quotient (decomposition space) topology induced by this identification.
