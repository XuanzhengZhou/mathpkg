Let $f: X \to Y$ be a Boardman map, and let $I_1,\ldots,I_s$ be multi-indices (not necessarily distinct). Let $x_1,\ldots,x_s$ be distinct points of $X$ with $x_j \in S_{I_j}(f)$ and
$$f(x_1) = \cdots = f(x_s) = y.$$
Let $H_j$ be the tangent space to $S_{I_j}(f)$ at $x_j$. Then \textbf{Condition NC} requires that the subspaces
$$(df)_{x_1}H_1,\; \ldots,\; (df)_{x_s}H_s$$
are in general position in $T_y Y$.
