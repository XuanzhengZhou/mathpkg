The orbit map $\alpha_f: \operatorname{Diff}(X) \times \operatorname{Diff}(Y) \to C^\infty(X, Y)$ has derivative at the identity given by $(T_e \alpha_f)(\xi, \eta) = \eta \circ f - df \circ \xi = T_f(\xi, \eta)$. On a Frechet manifold, a map has open image near a point if its derivative is surjective (inverse function theorem for Frechet spaces). Thus surjectivity of $T_f$ implies the orbit is open, which by Lemma 1.2 is equivalent to stability.
