This lemma extends the Generalized Malgrange Preparation Theorem to the setting of direct sums of modules over rings of germs at finitely many distinct points. It states that finite generation over $C_q^\infty(Y)$ can be checked coordinatewise. This is the key technical tool that allows the passage from single-point local infinitesimal stability (Corollary 1.3) to simultaneous stability at multiple pre-image points (Proposition 1.5), and ultimately to the global criterion of Theorem 1.6.
