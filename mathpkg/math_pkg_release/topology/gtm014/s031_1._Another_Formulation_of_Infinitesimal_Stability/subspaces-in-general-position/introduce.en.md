This lemma is the bridge between the multijet condition $(\dagger)$ and the geometric fact that limits the number of critical points in a fiber. It states that when condition $(\dagger)$ holds at a finite set $S \subset f^{-1}(q)$, the images $H_i = (df)_{p_i}(T_{p_i}X)$ of the tangent maps at distinct pre-image points are in general position as subspaces of $T_qY$. This means their codimensions add under intersection, which is the key combinatorial fact used in Lemma 1.9 to bound $|S|$ by $\dim Y$.
