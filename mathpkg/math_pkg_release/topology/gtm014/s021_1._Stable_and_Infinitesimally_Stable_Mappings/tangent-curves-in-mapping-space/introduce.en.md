This lemma identifies the tangent space $T_f C^\infty(X, Y)$ at a smooth map $f$ in the Fréchet manifold of mappings. It states that two smooth curves passing through $f$ are tangent in the Fréchet manifold sense precisely when, evaluated pointwise at each $p \in X$, they give tangent curves in the target $Y$. This pointwise characterization makes the tangent space accessible for explicit computations, particularly for determining the differential of the action map $\gamma_f$ used in Mather's criterion.
