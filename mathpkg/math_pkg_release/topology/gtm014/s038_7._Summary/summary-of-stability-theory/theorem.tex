The main results of Mather's stability theory for a proper smooth mapping $f: X \to Y$:
(1) Stability $\Leftrightarrow$ infinitesimal stability.
(2) For $\dim X < \dim Y$: stability $\Leftrightarrow$ immersion with normal crossings.
(3) For $\dim X \geq \dim Y$: stability $\Leftrightarrow$ submersion with folds and $f|_{S_1(f)}$ is immersion with normal crossings.
(4) Infinitesimal stability $\Leftrightarrow$ local finite-dimensionality condition $\Leftrightarrow$ versality of constant deformation.
