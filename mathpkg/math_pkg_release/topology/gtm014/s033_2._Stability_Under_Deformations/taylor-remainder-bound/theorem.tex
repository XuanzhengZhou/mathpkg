Let $p$ be a point in a convex compact subset $K$ of $\mathbf{R}^n$ and let $g: \mathbf{R}^n \to \mathbf{R}$ be a smooth function. Write the Taylor expansion of $g$ about $p$ with remainder of order $r + 1$ as
\[
g(x) = \sum_{0 \leq |\alpha| \leq r} a_\alpha (x - p)^\alpha + \sum_{|\beta| = r + 1} g_\beta(x) (x - p)^\beta.
\]
If $\|g\|_s^K < \varepsilon$, then $\|g_\beta\|_{s-1}^K < \varepsilon$ for all $r < s$ and $|\beta| = r + 1$.
