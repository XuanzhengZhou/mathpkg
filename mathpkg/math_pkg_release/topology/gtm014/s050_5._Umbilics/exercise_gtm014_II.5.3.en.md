Show that for the map (5.24) the image of $S_1$ in the 3-dimensional plane $\operatorname{Im} w = 0$ has the appearance of the cusped surface depicted in Figure 9 (the elliptic umbilic). 

Hint: Show that $S_1$ is given by the equation $|z| = |w|/2$. Use the identity
$$\det \begin{pmatrix} \frac{\partial u_1}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial u_1}{\partial x_2} \\ \frac{\partial u_2}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial u_2}{\partial x_2} \end{pmatrix} = \left| \frac{\partial u}{\partial z} \right|^2 - \left| \frac{\partial u}{\partial \bar{z}} \right|^2$$
where $u = u_1 + i u_2$ and $z = x_1 + i x_2$. Now look at Figure 8(b).
