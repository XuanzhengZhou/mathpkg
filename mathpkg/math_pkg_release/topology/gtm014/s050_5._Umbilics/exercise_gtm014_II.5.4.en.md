Let $f: \mathbb{R}^4 \to \mathbb{R}^4$ be the mapping
$$(x_1, x_2, x_3, x_4) \mapsto (x_1^2 + x_3 x_2,\; x_2^2 + x_4 x_1,\; x_3,\; x_4).$$
(Compare with (5.11).) Sketch the images of some of the lines $x_1 = \text{const.}$, $x_3 = \text{const.}$, $x_4 = \text{const.}$, and of some of the lines $x_2 = \text{const.}$, $x_3 = \text{const.}$, $x_4 = \text{const.}$
