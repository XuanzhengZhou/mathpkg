Let $X$ and $Y$ be $n$-manifolds. Show that if $n < 6$, the set of maps $f: X \to Y$ which exhibit only Morin singularities and umbilics is residual. 

(Hint: Show that the phenomenon illustrated by Exercise 10 occurs generically only in dimensions $\geq 6$. Use part (b) of Exercise 7, and the same sort of trick as in the proof of Proposition 3.13.)
