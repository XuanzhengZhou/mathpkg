Two $C^k$-differentiable manifolds $X$ and $Y$ are said to be diffeomorphic if there exists a diffeomorphism between them. This defines the natural equivalence relation in the category of differentiable manifolds: diffeomorphic manifolds are indistinguishable from the viewpoint of differential topology.
