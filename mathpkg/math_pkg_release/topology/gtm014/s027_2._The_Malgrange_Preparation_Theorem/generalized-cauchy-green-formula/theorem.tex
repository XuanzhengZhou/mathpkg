Let $D$ be a bounded domain in $\mathbb{C}$ with smooth boundary $\gamma$, and let $F$ be a smooth complex-valued function defined on a neighborhood of the closure $\overline{D}$. Then for any $w \in D$,

$$F(w) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{F(z)}{z - w}\, dz + \frac{1}{2\pi i} \iint_D \frac{\frac{\partial F}{\partial \bar{z}}(z)}{z - w}\, dz \wedge d\bar{z}.$$

If $F$ is holomorphic in $D$, then $\partial F / \partial \bar{z} = 0$ and this formula reduces to the standard Cauchy Integral Formula.
