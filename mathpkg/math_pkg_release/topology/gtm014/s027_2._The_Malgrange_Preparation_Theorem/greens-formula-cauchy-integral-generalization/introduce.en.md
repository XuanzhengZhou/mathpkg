This formula generalizes the Cauchy Integral Formula to smooth (not necessarily holomorphic) functions via Green's Theorem. It expresses a smooth function $F$ as a contour integral plus a double integral involving $\partial F/\partial \bar{\eta}$. The double integral term accounts for the failure of holomorphicity. This is the key analytical tool for proving the smooth Polynomial Division Theorem, replacing the purely holomorphic Cauchy formula used in the complex case.
