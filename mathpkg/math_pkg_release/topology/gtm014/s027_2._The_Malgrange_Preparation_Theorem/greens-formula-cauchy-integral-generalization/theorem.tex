Let $D$ be a domain in $\mathbf{C}$ with smooth boundary $\gamma$, and let $F$ be smooth on $\bar{D}$. Then for $w \in D$,
$$F(w) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_\gamma \frac{F(\eta)}{\eta - w} d\eta - \frac{1}{2\pi i} \iint_D \frac{\partial F / \partial \bar{\eta}}{\eta - w} d\eta \wedge d\bar{\eta}.$$
If $F$ is holomorphic, $\partial F/\partial \bar{\eta} = 0$ and this reduces to the Cauchy Integral Formula.
