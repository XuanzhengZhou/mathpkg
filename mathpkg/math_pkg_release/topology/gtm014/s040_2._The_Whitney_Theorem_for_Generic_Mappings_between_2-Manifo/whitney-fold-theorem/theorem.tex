Let $f: X \to Y$ be a smooth map between 2-manifolds. Let $p \in S_1(f)$ and $q = f(p)$. Suppose that condition (a) holds:
$$T_p S_1(f) \oplus \operatorname{Ker}(df)_p = T_p X.$$
Then one can choose a system of coordinates $(x_1, x_2)$ centered at $p$ and $(y_1, y_2)$ centered at $q$ such that $f$ is the map
$$(x_1, x_2) \mapsto (x_1, x_2^2).$$
