The set of smooth maps from a compact manifold $X$ into a manifold $Y$ that are immersions with normal crossings forms a dense subset of $C^\infty(X, Y)$ in the Whitney $C^\infty$ topology. This proposition establishes that the normal crossings condition is generic among all smooth mappings, meaning any smooth map can be approximated arbitrarily well by one whose multi-jet extensions are transverse to the diagonals.
