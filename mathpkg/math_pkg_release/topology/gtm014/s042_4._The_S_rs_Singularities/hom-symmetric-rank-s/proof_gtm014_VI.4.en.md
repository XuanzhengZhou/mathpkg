The dimension of $\operatorname{Hom}(V \circ V, W)_s$ is computed as follows. Consider the diagram (4.5): the left-hand side $\operatorname{Hom}(Q \circ Q, W)$ has fiber dimension $(k-s)(k-s+1)\ell/2$ where $k = \dim V$, $\ell = \dim W$, and $Q$ is the quotient bundle of fiber dimension $k-s$. The base space $G(s, V)$ has dimension $s(k-s)$. Therefore, the total dimension of $\operatorname{Hom}(V \circ V, W)_s$ is $(k-s)(k-s+1)\ell/2 + s(k-s)$, and its codimension in $\operatorname{Hom}(V \circ V, W)$ (which has dimension $k(k+1)\ell/2$) is as asserted in Proposition 4.3.
