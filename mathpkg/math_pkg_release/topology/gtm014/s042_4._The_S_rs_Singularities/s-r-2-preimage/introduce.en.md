The set $S_r^{(2)}$ is the pre-image of the Thom-Boardman singularity set $S_r$ under the projection from the second jet bundle $J^2(X,Y)$ to the first jet bundle $J^1(X,Y)$. The intrinsic derivative (from the previous section) yields a surjective bundle map from $S_r^{(2)}$ to $\text{Hom}(K \circ K, L)$, where $K$ and $L$ are the kernel and cokernel of the first derivative. This construction is the starting point for defining the higher-order Thom-Boardman singularities $S_{r,s}$.
