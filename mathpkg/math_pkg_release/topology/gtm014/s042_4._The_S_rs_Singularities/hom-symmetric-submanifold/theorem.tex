Let $V$ and $W$ be vector spaces of dimensions $k$ and $l$ respectively. Then $\text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)_s$ is a submanifold of $\text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)$. Its dimension equals the dimension of $\text{Hom}(Q \circ Q, W)_0$, where $Q$ is a quotient of $V$ of dimension $k - s$. Explicitly,

$$\dim \text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)_s = \frac{(k-s)(k-s+1)l}{2} + s(k-s).$$

The codimension of $\text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)_s$ in $\text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)$ is given by formula (4.4).
