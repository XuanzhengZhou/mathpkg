Lemma 5.13 provides a smooth diagonalization result for parameter-dependent quadratic forms near a hyperbolic (indefinite) critical point. The key observation is that for a symmetric $2 \times 2$ matrix close to $\operatorname{diag}(1, -1)$, the eigenvalues are distinct and depend smoothly on parameters. The corresponding eigenvectors then provide a smoothly varying rotation matrix that diagonalizes the form.
