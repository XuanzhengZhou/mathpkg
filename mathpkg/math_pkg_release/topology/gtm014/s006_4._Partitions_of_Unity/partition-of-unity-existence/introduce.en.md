Every $C^k$ differentiable manifold admits a partition of unity subordinate to any given open covering. This foundational theorem reduces global constructions on manifolds to local problems in Euclidean space. When the covering is countable, the partition of unity can be indexed by the same set, with each function supported inside the corresponding open set.
