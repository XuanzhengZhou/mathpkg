For any open covering of a differentiable manifold, one can find a locally finite refinement whose members are chart domains mapping onto the ball of radius 3 in $\mathbf{R}^n$, with the further property that the preimages of the unit balls still cover the manifold. This corollary to Proposition 4.2 provides the geometric setup required for constructing smooth partitions of unity on manifolds, as the nested balls allow the insertion of bump functions.
