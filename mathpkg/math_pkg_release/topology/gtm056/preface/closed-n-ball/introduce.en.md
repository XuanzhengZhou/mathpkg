The closed $n$-dimensional ball $E^n$ is the set of all points in $\mathbf{R}^n$ whose Euclidean norm is at most 1. It is a compact, contractible space with boundary the $(n-1)$-sphere. The closed ball plays a fundamental role in algebraic topology as the basic building block for CW-complexes and as the domain for homotopies and deformation retracts.
