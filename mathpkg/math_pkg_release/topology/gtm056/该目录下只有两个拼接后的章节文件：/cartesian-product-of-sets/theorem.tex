The notation

$$\prod_{i \in I} S_i$$

denotes the \text{product} (or \text{cartesian product}) of the family of sets $\{S_i\}_{i \in I}$. An element $x$ of the cartesian product is a function that assigns to each index $i \in I$ an element $x_i \in S_i$. The element $x_i \in S_i$ is called the \text{coordinate} of the element $x$ corresponding to the index $i \in I$.
