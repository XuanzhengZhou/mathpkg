Let $\{S_i\}_{i \in I}$ be a family of sets indexed by a set $I$. The \textbf{cartesian product} (or \textbf{product}) of this family, denoted
\[
\prod_{i \in I} S_i,
\]
is the set whose elements are functions $x$ that assign to each index $i \in I$ an element $x_i \in S_i$. The element $x_i \in S_i$ is called the \textbf{coordinate} of $x$ corresponding to the index $i \in I$.
