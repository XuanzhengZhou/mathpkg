The following notation is fixed throughout the book:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathbf{Z} &= \text{set of all integers, positive and negative}.\\
\mathbf{Q} &= \text{set of all rational numbers}.\\
\mathbf{R} &= \text{set of all real numbers}.\\
\mathbf{C} &= \text{set of all complex numbers}.
\end{aligned}
$$
