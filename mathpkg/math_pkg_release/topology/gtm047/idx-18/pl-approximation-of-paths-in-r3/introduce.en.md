Theorem 6 justifies a fundamental technique in geometric topology: when computing the fundamental group of an open subset of $\mathbb{R}^3$, one may replace any continuous path by a piecewise linear (PL) path without changing its based homotopy class. This allows the use of combinatorial and simplicial methods in fundamental group calculations.
