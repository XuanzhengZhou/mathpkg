The main result of Section 13: any homeomorphism $f \colon M \to M'$ between two totally disconnected compact subsets of the plane can be extended to a self-homeomorphism of $\mathbb{R}^2$. The proof constructs an infinite sequence of nested frames around $M$ and $M'$, extending the homeomorphism step by step through successively finer approximations, and taking the union of these partial extensions to obtain the global homeomorphism $F \colon \mathbb{R}^2 \to \mathbb{R}^2$.
