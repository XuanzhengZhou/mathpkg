The equivalence relation $\cong$ on closed paths is based homotopy (also called homotopy relative to endpoints). Two loops $p$ and $q$ based at $P_0$ are equivalent if one can be continuously deformed into the other while keeping the base point $P_0$ fixed throughout the deformation. This is stronger than free homotopy because the vertical edges of the homotopy square must be constantly $P_0$.
