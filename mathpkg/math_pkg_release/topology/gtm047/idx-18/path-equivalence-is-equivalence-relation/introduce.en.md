Theorem 1 of Chapter 14 verifies that based homotopy $\cong$ is reflexive, symmetric, and transitive. Reflexivity uses the constant homotopy, symmetry follows by reversing the homotopy parameter, and transitivity is established by concatenating two homotopies and reparametrizing.
