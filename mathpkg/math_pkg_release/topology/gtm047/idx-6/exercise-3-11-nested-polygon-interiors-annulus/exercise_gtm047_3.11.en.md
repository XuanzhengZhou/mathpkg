Let $J_1$ and $J_2$ be polygons in $\mathbb{R}^2$, with interiors $I_1$ and $I_2$; and suppose that $\bar{I}_2 \subset I_1$. Prove that $\bar{I}_1 - I_2$ is homeomorphic to a closed plane region bounded by two concentric circles.
