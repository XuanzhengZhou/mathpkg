Prove that the standard unit 2-ball $\mathbf{B}^2$ is homeomorphic to a 2-simplex $\sigma^2$. This is a fundamental fact in planar topology: the convex shapes of a disk and a triangle are topologically indistinguishable. The construction can use radial projection or an explicit piecewise linear map.
