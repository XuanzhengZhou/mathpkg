Every component of $U$ is open, because every set $N(P, \varepsilon)$ is connected. Therefore every union of components of $U$ is open. Let $C_P$ be the component of $U$ that contains $P$. Then $U = C_P \cup (U - C_P)$, where $U - C_P$ is open and contains $Q$.
