Let $J$ be a polygon in $\mathbb{R}^2$, let $I$ be its interior, and let $U$ be an open set containing $\bar{I}$. Prove that there is a homeomorphism $f: \mathbb{R}^2 \leftrightarrow \mathbb{R}^2, \bar{I} \leftrightarrow \sigma^2$, such that $f|(\mathbb{R}^2 - U)$ is the identity.
