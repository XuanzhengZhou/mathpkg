Prove that given a polygon $J$ with interior $I$ and any open set $U$ containing $\bar{I}$, there is a homeomorphism of the plane sending the closed polygonal disk $\bar{I}$ onto a 2-simplex, while being the identity outside $U$. This is a strengthened version of Theorem 7 where additionally the whole closed region (not just the boundary) is mapped to the simplex.
