This is a technical lemma used in the proof that a topological 1-sphere separates the plane. Given a broken line $B$ crossing the 1-sphere $J = A_1 \cup A_2$ at a first intersection point on $A_1$, the lemma asserts that after the last intersection of $B$ with $A_1$, the broken line must eventually intersect $A_2$. Its proof uses the preceding separation theorem and constructs an arc that would otherwise connect $S$ and $Q$ while avoiding $A_2$.
