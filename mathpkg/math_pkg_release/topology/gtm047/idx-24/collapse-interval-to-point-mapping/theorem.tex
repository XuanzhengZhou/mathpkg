In $\mathbf{R}^3$, let $P_0 = (0, 0, 0)$, $P_1 = (0, 0, \frac{3}{2})$, $P_2 = (0, 0, 2)$, and $P_3 = (0, 1, 2)$; let $T$ be the 2-simplex $P_0P_2P_3$, and let $D^3$ be the solid of revolution of $T$ about the $z$-axis. Then there is a mapping $f: D^3 \rightarrow D^3$ such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item $f(P_0P_1) = P_0$,
\item $f|(D^3 - P_0P_1)$ is a homeomorphism $D^3 - P_0P_1 \leftrightarrow D^3 - \{P_0\}$.
\end{enumerate}
