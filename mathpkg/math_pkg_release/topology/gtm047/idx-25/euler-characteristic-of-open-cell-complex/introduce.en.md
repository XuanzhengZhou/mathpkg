The Euler characteristic for an open cell-complex $\mathcal{C}$ is defined by the same alternating sum $\chi(\mathcal{C}) = V - E + F$ used for simplicial complexes. This extension allows computing Euler characteristics of polyhedra using their natural cell decompositions rather than requiring a full triangulation.
