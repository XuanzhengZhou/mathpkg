Operation $\delta$ inserts a new loop edge into a face $C^2$, where the edge's frontier consists of a single vertex lying on the frontier of the face. This creates a $1$-cell that is attached to the face boundary at only one point. Together with $\alpha$, $\beta$, and $\gamma$, these four operations generate all subdivisions of open cell-complexes.
