Let $h$ be a homeomorphism $|K^1| \leftrightarrow M \subset \mathbf{R}^2$, where $K^1$ is a finite 1-complex, and let $\phi \gg 0$ on $|K^1|$. Then there is a PLH $f: |K^1| \rightarrow \mathbf{R}^2$, such that (1) $f$ is a $\phi$-approximation of $h$ and (2) for each vertex $v$ of $K^1$, $h(v) = f(v)$.
