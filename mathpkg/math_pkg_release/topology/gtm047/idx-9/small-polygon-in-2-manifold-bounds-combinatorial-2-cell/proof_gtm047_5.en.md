Let $f$ be a PLH: $|\text{St}\,v| \rightarrow \mathbf{R}^2$. Then $f(J)$ is a polygon in $\mathbf{R}^2$, and the interior of $f(J)$ in $\mathbf{R}^2$ lies in $f(|\text{St}\,v|)$. Let $I$ be the interior. Then $\bar{I}$ forms a combinatorial 2-cell in $\mathbf{R}^2$. Therefore $f^{-1}(\bar{I})$ forms a combinatorial 2-cell in $|K|$.
