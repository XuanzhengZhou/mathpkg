Let $J$ be a polygon in $\mathbf{R}^2$, let $I$ be its interior, and let $K$ be a subcomplex of a triangulation of $\mathbf{R}^2$ such that $|K| = \bar{I}$. Then there is a PL homeomorphism $f: \mathbf{R}^2 \leftrightarrow \mathbf{R}^2$ such that $f(\bar{I}) = \sigma^2$, where $\sigma^2$ is a 2-simplex.
