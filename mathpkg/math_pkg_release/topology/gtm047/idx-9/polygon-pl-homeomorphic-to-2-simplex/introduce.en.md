This result establishes that the closure of the interior of any polygon in the plane is PL homeomorphic to a standard 2-simplex. It uses the technique of deleting free 2-simplexes via PL homeomorphisms of the plane and relies on the subdivision invariance theorems (Theorems 5.1 and 5.2) to ensure the composite map is PL.
