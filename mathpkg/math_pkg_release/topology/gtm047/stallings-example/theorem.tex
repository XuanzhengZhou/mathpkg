**Theorem (Stallings).** The Loop theorem cannot be extended to arbitrary 2-manifolds in the boundary of a 3-manifold. There exists a solid torus $T$ and a torus $T^2$ in $\operatorname{Bd} T$ such that a loop in $T^2$ is contractible in $T$ but not in $T^2$, yet no 2-cell in $T$ bounds the loop. This occurs in certain lens spaces.
