Prove or disprove:

Let $K$ be a $2$-dimensional complex, and let $\phi$ be a strongly positive function on $|K|$. Then there is a mapping $\psi: |K| \to \mathbb{R}$ such that for each $P$, $0 < \psi(P) \leqslant \phi(P)$.
