Let $K$ be a combinatorial $2$-manifold with boundary (not necessarily finite), let $h$ be a homeomorphism $|K| \leftrightarrow M \subset \mathbb{R}^2$, and let $\phi$ be a strongly positive function $|K| \to \mathbb{R}$. Then there is a PLH $f: |K| \to \mathbb{R}^2$ such that $f$ is a $\phi$-approximation of $h$.
