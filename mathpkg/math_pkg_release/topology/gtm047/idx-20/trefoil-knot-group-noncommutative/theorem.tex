The group of the trefoil knot is not commutative:

$$\pi(\mathbf{R}^3 - K) \text{ is noncommutative}.$$

Explicitly, from the trefoil diagram one reads off generators $g_1, g_2, g_3$ and relations

$$r_1 = g_1 g_3^{-1} g_2^{-1} g_3 \cong e,$$
$$r_2 = g_2 g_1^{-1} g_3^{-1} g_1 \cong e,$$
$$r_3 = g_3 g_2^{-1} g_1^{-1} g_2 \cong e.$$

There exists a surjective homomorphism from the knot group onto the symmetric group $S_3$, and since $S_3$ is not commutative, the knot group is not commutative.
