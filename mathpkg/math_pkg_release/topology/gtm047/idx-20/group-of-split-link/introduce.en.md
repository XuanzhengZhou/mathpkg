When the components of a link bound disjoint polyhedral 2-cells, the link is essentially "split" and its group is a free group on $k$ generators (one per component). This generalizes the $k$-annulus result to three dimensions. The proof arranges the link diagram to obtain $k$ generators and no relations. This theorem provides a class of links whose fundamental groups are easily computable.
