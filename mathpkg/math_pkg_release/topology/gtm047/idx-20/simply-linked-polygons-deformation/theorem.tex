Let $J_1, J_2, J_3$ be plane polygons, simply linked in series. Let $D$ be the plane 2-cell bounded by $J_2$, and suppose that $D$ is simply punctured by $J_1$ and $J_3$. Let $p$ be a closed path in

$$U = D - (J_1 \cup J_2 \cup J_3).$$

If $p \cong e$ in $\mathbf{R}^3 - (J_1 \cup J_3)$, then $p \cong e$ in $U$.
