This theorem provides the fundamental presentation for the fundamental group of a link complement in $\mathbb{R}^3$. Given a link diagram, one can read off generators (one per arc) and relations (one per crossing). The theorem states that the group $\pi(\mathbb{R}^3 - L, P_0)$ is isomorphic to the quotient of the free group on the generators by the normal closure of the relations. This gives the Wirtinger presentation of the link group and justifies the method of computing knot groups from diagrams.
