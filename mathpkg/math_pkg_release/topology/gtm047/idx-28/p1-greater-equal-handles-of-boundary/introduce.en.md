For an orientable triangulated 3-manifold $K$ with boundary, the combinatorial invariant $p^1(K)$ (which is the rank of $H_1(N(L))$ modulo certain relations, roughly the first Betti number of a regular neighborhood representation) is bounded below by the handle number $h(\text{Bd }|K|)$ of the boundary surface. This theorem connects the combinatorial data of a 3-manifold triangulation to the topology of its boundary surface.
