Let $g: \tilde{M} \rightarrow M$ be a covering, $P_0 \in M$, $\tilde{P}_0 \in g^{-1}(P_0)$, and let $\pi_0 = g_0^*(\pi(\tilde{M}, \tilde{P}_0))$ be the group associated with $g$ and $P_0$. For $p \in \text{CP}(M, P_0)$, we have $\overline{p} \in \pi_0$ if and only if $p$ has a lifting $\tilde{p} \in \text{CP}(\tilde{M}, \tilde{P}_0)$.
