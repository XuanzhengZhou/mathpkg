For a $k$-fold covering $g: \tilde{M} \to M$, the number $k$ of sheets (the constant cardinality of each fiber $g^{-1}(P)$) equals the index $[\pi(M, P_0) : \pi_0]$ of the associated subgroup $\pi_0$ in the fundamental group of the base. This theorem establishes the fundamental algebraic-topological dictionary: finite-sheeted coverings correspond precisely to subgroups of finite index in the fundamental group.
