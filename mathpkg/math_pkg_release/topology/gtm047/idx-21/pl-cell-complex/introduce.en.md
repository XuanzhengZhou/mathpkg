A PL cell-complex is a cell-complex in the piecewise-linear category: the elements of the complex are polyhedra rather than merely topological cells. This structure allows the use of piecewise-linear homeomorphisms (PLH) in constructions and proofs, and provides a PL cell-decomposition of the underlying polyhedron $|K|$.
