**Theorem 2.** Let $M_1 = |K_1|$ and $M_2 = |K_2|$ be PL 3-manifolds, let $U$ be an open set in $M_1$, let $h: U \to M_2$ be a homeomorphism, and let $\phi$ be $\gg 0$ on $U$. Then there is a PLH $f$ on a neighborhood $N$ of the 2-skeleton of $K_1$, such that $f$ is a $\phi$-approximation of $h \mid N$, and $f(N)$ is a neighborhood of $h(|K_1^2|)$.
