**Theorem.** Let $[X, d]$ be a metric space. A set $U \subset X$ is open if it is the union of a collection of elements of $\mathcal{N}(d)$. The set of all open sets, denoted $\emptyset(\mathcal{N}(d))$, satisfies Conditions (O.1)-(O.4) of the definition of a topological space. Thus every metric space induces a topological space.
