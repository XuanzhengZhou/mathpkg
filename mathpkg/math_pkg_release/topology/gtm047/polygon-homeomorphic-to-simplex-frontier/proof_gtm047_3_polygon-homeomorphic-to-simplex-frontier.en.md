Let $I$ be the interior of $J$, and let $K$ be a triangulation of $\overline{I}$. Any free 2-simplex of $K$ can be removed by a homeomorphism $h: \mathbb{R}^2 \leftrightarrow \mathbb{R}^2$. By repeated applications, we reduce $\overline{I}$ to a single 2-simplex.
