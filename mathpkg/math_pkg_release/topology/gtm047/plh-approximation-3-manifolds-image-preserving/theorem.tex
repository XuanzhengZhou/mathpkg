**Theorem 1.** Let $M_1$ and $M_2$ be PL 3-manifolds, let $U$ be an open set in $M_1$, let $h$ be a homeomorphism $U \rightarrow M_2$, and let $\phi$ be a strongly positive function on $U$. Then there is a PLH $f: U \rightarrow M_2$ such that (1) $f$ is a $\phi$-approximation of $h$ and (2) $f(U) = h(U)$.
