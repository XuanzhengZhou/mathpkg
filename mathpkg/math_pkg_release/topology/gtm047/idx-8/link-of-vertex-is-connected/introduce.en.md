This lemma states that the link of any vertex $v$ in a triangulated 2-manifold (without boundary) is connected. If the link were disconnected, the vertex $v$ would separate its star $\operatorname{St} v$, which is impossible since the star is homeomorphic to a 2-cell. This connectivity property is essential for establishing that the star of every vertex is a combinatorial 2-cell.
