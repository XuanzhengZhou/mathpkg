Let $v$ be an interior point of $v_0 v_1$, and suppose that $v_0 v_1$ lies in no 2-simplex of $K$. Then $v$ has a neighborhood $U$ in $|K|$, lying in $\operatorname{Int} v_0 v_1$ and homeomorphic to the plane. This is impossible, because no point separates the plane.
