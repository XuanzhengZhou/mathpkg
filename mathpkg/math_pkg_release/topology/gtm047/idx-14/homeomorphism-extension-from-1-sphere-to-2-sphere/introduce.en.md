This theorem strengthens the Schönflies theorem for $2$-spheres by asserting that any homeomorphism between two $1$-spheres embedded in $S^2$ can be extended to a self-homeomorphism of the entire $2$-sphere. The proof uses Theorem 2 to decompose $S^2$ into pairs of $2$-cells bounded by $J$ and $J'$, then applies a known extension result (Theorem 5.6) to map each $2$-cell homeomorphically.
