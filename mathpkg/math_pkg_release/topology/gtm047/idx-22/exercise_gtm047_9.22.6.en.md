Let $C_1^3, C_2^3, \ldots$ be a sequence of polyhedral $3$-cells in $\mathbf{R}^3$, such that for each $i$, $C_{i+1}^3 \subset \operatorname{Int} C_i^3$; and let $U = \mathbf{R}^3 - \bigcap_{i=1}^{\infty} C_i^3$. Then $U$ is homeomorphic to the complement of a point in $\mathbf{R}^3$.
