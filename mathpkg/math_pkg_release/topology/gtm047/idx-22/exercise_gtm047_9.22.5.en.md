Let $S_1, S_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of polyhedral $2$-spheres in $\mathbf{R}^3$, such that for each $i$, $S_i$ lies in the bounded component of $\mathbf{R}^3 - S_{i+1}$. For each $i$, let $C_i^3$ be the $3$-cell in $\mathbf{R}^3$ such that $\operatorname{Bd} C_i^3 = S_i$; and let $U = \bigcup_{i=1}^{\infty} C_i^3$. Then $U$ and $\mathbf{R}^3$ are homeomorphic.
