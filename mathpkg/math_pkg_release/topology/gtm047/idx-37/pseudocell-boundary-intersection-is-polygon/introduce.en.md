This lemma simplifies the topology of the intersection between each pseudocell $E$ and the boundary of the polyhedral 3-manifold $X$. By splitting along annuli formed by multiple intersection polygons, the configuration is reduced to the case where each $E \cap \operatorname{Bd} X$ is a single polygon, which is technically convenient for the subsequent construction.
