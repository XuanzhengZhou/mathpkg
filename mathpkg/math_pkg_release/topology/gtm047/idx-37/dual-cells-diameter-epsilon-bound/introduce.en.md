This lemma ensures that the diameter of each dual cell $C_v$ and the associated sets $C_v''$ (images under the homeomorphism $h$) can be made arbitrarily small relative to the approximation tolerance $\varepsilon$. This is achieved by choosing a sufficiently small regular neighborhood $N$ of the polyhedron $K$, exploiting uniform continuity of $h$ on the compact set $K$.
