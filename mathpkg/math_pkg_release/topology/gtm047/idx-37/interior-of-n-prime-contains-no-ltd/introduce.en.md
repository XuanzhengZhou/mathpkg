This lemma asserts that under the conditions established by Lemmas 1 through 7, the interior of $N' = h(N)$ contains no locally tame disk (LTD). The proof uses general position arguments and splitting processes to eliminate all possible intersections between a hypothetical LTD and the pseudocells $E$, ultimately reaching a contradiction using Lemma 8.
