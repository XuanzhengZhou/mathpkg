(The statement of Lemma 13 is truncated in the source text. The proof proceeds by constructing a PL homeomorphism piecewise: each $A_v = \operatorname{Bd} C_v \cap \operatorname{Bd} N$ is a disk or disk with holes, and by Lemma 12 each corresponding $A_v'$ has the same topological type. Since $\operatorname{Bd} X$ and $\operatorname{Bd} N$ are homeomorphic (Lemma 11) and the decomposition into the $A_v$ and $A_v'$ pieces matches combinatorially, a PL homeomorphism carrying each $A_v$ onto $A_v'$ can be constructed. This homeomorphism on the boundary is then extended conewise to the interior of each dual 3-cell $C_v$, yielding the full PL homeomorphism $f: N \to X$ required for Theorem 1.)
