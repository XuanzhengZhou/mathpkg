This lemma proves that the complement of a polygon $J$ in the plane has at least two connected components, ruling out the possibility that the strip neighborhood is a M\"{o}bius band. The proof uses an index function modulo 2, defined by counting horizontal intersections to the left of a point, with coordinates placed in general position so that no horizontal line contains more than one vertex.
