This theorem completes the classification of compact connected 2-manifolds by their Euler characteristic. A compact connected 2-manifold with Euler characteristic 1 must be a projective plane. The proof follows from the representation of a compact 2-manifold as a 2-sphere with handles and cross-caps, together with the formula $\chi(M) = 2 - (2h + m)$ where $h$ is the number of handles and $m$ is the number of cross-caps (with $0 \leqslant m \leqslant 2$). Solving $2h + m = 1$ forces $m = 1$ and $h = 0$, yielding a projective plane.
