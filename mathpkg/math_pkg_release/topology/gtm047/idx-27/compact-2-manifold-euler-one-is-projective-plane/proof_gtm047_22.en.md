$M$ is a 2-sphere with $h$ handles and $m$ cross-caps, with $0 \leqslant m \leqslant 2$. By Theorem 5 we have $1 = 2 - (2h + m)$, so that $2h + m = 1$. Therefore $m = 1$ and $h = 0$. Therefore $M$ is a projective plane (Problem 21.1).
