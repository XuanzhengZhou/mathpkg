This is the orientable analog of Theorem 13. For an orientable triangulated 3-manifold $K$ with boundary, the doubling $K'$ used in the proof of Theorem 13 is orientable by Theorem 15, so the regular neighborhood representation $K \approx N(L)$ lives inside an orientable triangulated 3-manifold $K'$.
