This theorem describes the local structure of the boundary of a triangulated 3-manifold at a point $P$. Every sufficiently small 2-cell neighborhood of $P$ in the boundary arises as the intersection of a combinatorial 3-cell neighborhood with the boundary, where the 3-cell and the 2-cell face are obtained via combinatorial equivalence maps from a standard 3-simplex and its face.
