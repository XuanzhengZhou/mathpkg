This theorem verifies that the combinatorial definitions of boundary complex $\partial K$ and interior $I(K)$ for a triangulated 3-manifold with boundary coincide with the topological notions of boundary and interior. In particular, the geometric realization of the boundary complex equals the topological boundary of the manifold, and the set-theoretic difference defining $I(K)$ equals the topological interior.
