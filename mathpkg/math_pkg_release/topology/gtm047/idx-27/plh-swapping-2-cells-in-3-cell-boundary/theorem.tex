In a triangulated 3-manifold $K$, let $C^3$ be a polyhedral 3-cell, lying in a set $\operatorname{Int} |\operatorname{St} v|$. Let $D_1$ and $D_2$ be polyhedral 2-cells such that $D_1 \cup D_2 = \operatorname{Bd} C^3$ and $D_1 \cap D_2 = J = \operatorname{Bd} D_1 = \operatorname{Bd} D_2$. Let $N$ be a polyhedral closed neighborhood of $C^3 - J$. Then there is a PLH $h: |K| \leftrightarrow |K|$ such that (1) $h(D_1) = D_2$ and (2) $h(|K| - N)$ is the identity.
