We may assume that $N \subset \operatorname{Int} \operatorname{St} v$. Let $g: |\operatorname{St} v| \leftrightarrow \mathbf{R}^3$ be a PLH such that $g(C^3)$ is a standard 3-simplex, $g(D_1)$ and $g(D_2)$ are two faces of this simplex meeting along $g(J)$, and $g(N)$ is a polyhedral closed neighborhood of $g(C^3) - g(J)$. There is a PLH $f: \mathbf{R}^3 \leftrightarrow \mathbf{R}^3$ such that $f(g(D_1)) = g(D_2)$ and $f$ is the identity on $\mathbf{R}^3 - g(N)$. Then $h = g^{-1} f g$ is the required PLH on $|K|$.
