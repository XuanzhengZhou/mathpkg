Let $h: |\operatorname{St} v| \rightarrow \mathbf{R}^3$ be a PLH. By the PL Schonflies theorem (Theorem 17.12), $h(S)$ bounds a combinatorial 3-cell $D^3$ in $\mathbf{R}^3$, and it is easy to check that $D^3 \subset h(|\operatorname{St} v|)$. Let $C^3 = h^{-1}(D^3)$. Then $\operatorname{Bd} C^3 = S$.
