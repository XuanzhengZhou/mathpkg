This theorem is a local version of the Schonflies theorem in dimension 3. If a polyhedral 2-sphere $S$ lies within the star of some vertex $v$ in a triangulated 3-manifold $K$, then $S$ must be the boundary of a combinatorial 3-cell. The proof uses the fact that $\operatorname{St} v$ is PL homeomorphic to $\mathbf{R}^3$ (by Theorem 1), and then applies the PL Schonflies theorem in $\mathbf{R}^3$ to the image of $S$ under this homeomorphism.
