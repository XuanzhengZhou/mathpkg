**Theorem 2.** Let $K^1$ be the 1-skeleton of a combinatorial 2-manifold with boundary, let $h$ be a homeomorphism $|K^1| \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^2$, and let $\phi$ be $\gg 0$ on $|K^1|$. Then there is a PLH $f: |K^1| \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^2$, such that (1) $f$ is a $\phi$-approximation of $h$ and (2) for each vertex $v$ of $K^1$, $h(v) = f(v)$.
