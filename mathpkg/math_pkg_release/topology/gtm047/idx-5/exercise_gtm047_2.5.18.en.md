Let $K$ be a 1-dimensional complex, and let $v$ be a vertex of $K$. If $v$ lies in exactly $n$ edges of $K$, then $v$ is a vertex of order $n$ in $K$. If $|K'| = |K|$, and $v$ is a vertex of order $n$ in $K'$, then $v$ is a vertex of $K'$, and is of order $n$ in $K'$.
