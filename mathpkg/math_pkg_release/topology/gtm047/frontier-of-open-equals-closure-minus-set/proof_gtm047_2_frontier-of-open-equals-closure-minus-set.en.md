By definition, $\operatorname{Fr} U = \overline{U} \cap \overline{X - U}$. Therefore $\operatorname{Fr} U \subset \overline{U}$. Since $U$ is open, $U \cap \overline{X - U} = \varnothing$. Hence $\operatorname{Fr} U \subset \overline{U} - U$. Conversely, $\overline{U} - U \subset \overline{U} \cap (X - U) \subset \overline{U} \cap \overline{X - U} = \operatorname{Fr} U$.
