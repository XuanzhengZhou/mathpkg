Let $K$ be a complex, such that $|K|$ is an $n$-manifold. Then $K$ is called a triangulation of $|K|$, and is called a triangulated $n$-manifold. Show that if $K$ is a triangulated $n$-manifold, and $v \in K^{0}$, then $L(v)$ is connected.
