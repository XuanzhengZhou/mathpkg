**Theorem 12 (The PL Schönflies theorem).** Let $S$ be a polyhedral 2-sphere in $\mathbb{R}^3$, and let $W$ be a convex open set containing $S$. Then there is a PLH

$$f: \mathbb{R}^3 \leftrightarrow \mathbb{R}^3, \quad S \leftrightarrow \operatorname{Bd} \sigma^3,$$

where $\sigma^3$ is a 3-simplex, such that $f \mid (\mathbb{R}^3 - W)$ is the identity. Thus every polyhedral 2-sphere is simply imbedded.
