By repeated applications of Operation $\alpha$. Starting with the arbitrary arrangement of strips from Theorem 3, apply Operation $\alpha$ successively to each twisted strip $S_i$ ($i > k$), sliding its attachment arcs along $\operatorname{Bd}(D \cup S_i)$ so that they move into positions disjoint from each other and from all annular strip attachments. After finitely many such operations, all twisted strip intersections with $D$ lie in a single arc on $\operatorname{Bd} D$ containing no annular strip. Renumbering yields the conclusion. $\square$
