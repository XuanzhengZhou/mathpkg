By repeated applications of Operation $\alpha$, the twisted strips in the $2$-cell with strips decomposition can be repositioned so that all their attachments to $D$ lie within a single arc on $\operatorname{Bd} D$ that is disjoint from all annular strip attachments. This is a crucial simplification: it separates the orientable (annular) and non-orientable (twisted) contributions to the manifold, allowing them to be analyzed independently in the final classification.
