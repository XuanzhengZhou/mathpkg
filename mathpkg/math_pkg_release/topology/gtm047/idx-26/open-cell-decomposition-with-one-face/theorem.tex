Let $M$ be a compact connected $2$-manifold, and let $K$ be a triangulation of $M$. Then there exists an open cell-decomposition $\mathcal{C}$ of $M$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathcal{C}$ has exactly one $2$-face $C^2$, and
\item every edge of $\mathcal{C}$ is an edge of $K$.
\end{enumerate}
