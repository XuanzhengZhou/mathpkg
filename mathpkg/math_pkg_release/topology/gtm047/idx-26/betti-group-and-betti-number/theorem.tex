The first homology group $H_1(M) = H_1(M, \mathbf{Z})$ of a compact connected $2$-manifold $M$ decomposes as
$$H_1(M) \approx B^1 \oplus T^1,$$
where $B^1$ is the \emph{Betti group} (the free Abelian group generated by elements of infinite order) and $T^1$ is the \emph{torsion group} (generated by elements of finite order). The group $B^1$ is isomorphic to $\mathbf{Z}^{p}$ for some $p \geqslant 0$, and the \emph{$1$-dimensional Betti number} $p^1(M)$ is defined to be $p$.
