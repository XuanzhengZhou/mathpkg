Let $M$ be a $2$-sphere with $h$ handles and $m$ cross-caps ($0 \leqslant m \leqslant 2$). Then:
\begin{itemize}
\item For $m = 0$ (orientable case): $p^1(M) = 2h$, and $T^1 = 0$.
\item For $m = 1$ (non-orientable, one cross-cap): $p^1(M) = 2h$, and $T^1 \approx \mathbf{Z}_2$.
\item For $m = 2$ (non-orientable, two cross-caps): $p^1(M) = 2h + 1$, and $T^1 \approx \mathbf{Z}_2$.
\end{itemize}
