# Exercise 2.4.4

The Baire property for the strong topology on uniformly closed subsets of $C(X,Y)$ follows from Exercise 3 and the following. Let $Q$ be a space whose topology is defined by a family $\Delta$ of complete metrics. Suppose that $\Delta$ is a directed set under the partial ordering:

$$d_1 \leqslant d_2 \quad \text{if} \quad d_1(x,y) \leqslant d_2(x,y) \quad \text{for all } (x,y).$$

Then $Q$ has the Baire property.
