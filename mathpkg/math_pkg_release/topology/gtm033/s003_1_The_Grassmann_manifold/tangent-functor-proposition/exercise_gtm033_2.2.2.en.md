# Exercise 2

Let $M$ be a $C^r$ manifold, $r \geqslant 1$, and $A \subset M$ a connected subset. Suppose that there is a $C^r$ retraction $f: M \to A$, i.e. $f|A = \text{identity}$. Then $A$ is a $C^r$ submanifold. (The converse is proved in Chapter 4.)
