Let $0 \rightarrow \xi' \rightarrow \xi \rightarrow \xi'' \rightarrow 0$ be a short exact sequence of vector bundles over $B$. Given orientations $\omega', \omega''$ for $\xi', \xi''$ respectively, a family $\omega = \{\omega_x\}_{x \in B}$ of orientations of fibres of $\xi$ is obtained by setting $\omega_x = \omega_x' \oplus \omega_x''$. This $\omega$ is coherent and thus is an orientation of $\xi$. Any two of $\omega, \omega', \omega''$ determine the third.

\textbf{Lemma 4.1.} Two of $\xi, \xi', \xi''$ are orientable if and only if the third is.
