Theorem 4.5 states that in a simply connected manifold, a closed connected separating submanifold of codimension one inherits orientability from the ambient manifold. Combined with Lemma 4.4, this provides a powerful criterion for deducing orientability of hypersurfaces, generalizing the classical fact that a closed surface separating $\mathbb{R}^3$ must be orientable.
