\textbf{Theorem 6.2.} Let $M \subset V$ be a closed neat submanifold. Then $\partial V$ has a collar which restricts to a collar on $\partial M$ in $M$. That is, there exists a collar $f: \partial V \times [0, 1) \rightarrow V$ such that $f|(\partial M \times [0, 1))$ is a collar of $\partial M$ in $M$.
