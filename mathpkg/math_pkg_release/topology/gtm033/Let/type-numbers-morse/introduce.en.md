The type numbers of a Morse function count the number of critical points of each index. The vector $(v_0, \ldots, v_n)$ captures the distribution of critical points by their indices. The alternating sum of type numbers equals the Euler characteristic, while the individual type numbers are bounded below by the corresponding Betti numbers (the weak Morse inequalities).
