Given a short exact sequence of vector bundles, any two of the three bundles determine an orientation of the third via the direct-sum construction on fibres. Lemma 4.1 formalizes the consequence: orientability of any two bundles in the sequence implies orientability of the third, and vice versa. This is a fundamental compatibility condition that allows one to propagate orientability through exact sequences.
