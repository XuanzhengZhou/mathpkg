Theorem 1.3 is the isotopy extension theorem: an isotopy of a compact submanifold $V$ (staying either entirely in the interior or entirely on the boundary) can be extended to a compactly supported diffeotopy of the whole ambient manifold $M$. The proof uses tubular neighborhoods and partitions of unity to extend the velocity vector field from the isotopy track $\hat{F}(V \times I)$ to all of $M$.
