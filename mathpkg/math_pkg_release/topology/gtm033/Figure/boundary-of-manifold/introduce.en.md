The boundary of a $C^r$ manifold consists of all points that map to the boundary of a halfspace under some chart. This notion generalizes the naive idea of boundary from subsets of Euclidean space to abstract manifolds. The boundary $\partial M$ naturally inherits a $C^r$ manifold structure of dimension $n-1$ by restricting charts from $M$.
