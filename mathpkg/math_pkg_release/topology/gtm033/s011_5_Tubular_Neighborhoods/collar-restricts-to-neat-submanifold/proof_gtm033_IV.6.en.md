# Proof of Collar Restriction for Neat Submanifolds (Theorem 6.2)

**Theorem (6.2).** Let $M \subset V$ be a closed neat submanifold. Then $\partial V$ has a collar which restricts to a collar on $\partial M$ in $M$.

*Note.* The author states: "We leave as an exercise the proof of the following refinement of Theorem 6.1." Therefore, the proof is not provided in the text; it is intended as an exercise for the reader.

The proof would proceed by combining the collar construction of Theorem 6.1 with the neatness condition to ensure compatibility of the collars for $(V, \partial V)$ and $(M, \partial M)$.
