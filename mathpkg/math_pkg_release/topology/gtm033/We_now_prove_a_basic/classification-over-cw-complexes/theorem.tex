Let $K^k(X)$ denote the set of isomorphism classes of $k$-plane bundles over a paracompact space $X$. Let $[X, G_{s,k}]$ be the set of homotopy classes of maps from $X$ to the Grassmann manifold $G_{s,k}$. Define the natural map
$$\Theta_X: K^k(X) \to [X, G_{s,k}]$$
by $\Theta_X([\xi]) = [f_\xi]$, where $f_\xi: X \to G_{s,k}$ is a classifying map such that $\xi \cong f_\xi^* \gamma_{s,k}$. If $X$ has the homotopy type of a simplicial or CW complex of dimension less than $s - k$, then $\Theta_X$ is bijective.
