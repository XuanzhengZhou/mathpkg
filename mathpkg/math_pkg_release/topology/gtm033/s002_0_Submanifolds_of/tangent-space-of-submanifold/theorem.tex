Let $M \subset \mathbb{R}^{n+k}$ be an $n$-dimensional submanifold and $x \in M$. The set

$$M_x = \{x\} \times E_x$$

is called the \textit{tangent space} to $M$ at $x$, where $E_x$ is the $n$-dimensional subspace of $\mathbb{R}^{n+k}$ consisting of all vectors tangent to curves in $M$ passing through $x$. It inherits the natural vector space structure from $E_x$.

If $(\varphi, U)$ is a chart at $x$ with $\varphi(x) = a$, then $D\varphi_a^{-1}$ induces a vector space isomorphism between $\mathbb{R}^n$ and $M_x$.

The embedding $M_x \hookrightarrow \mathbb{R}^{n+k}$ given by $(x, y) \mapsto x + y$ identifies $M_x$ with an affine $n$-plane in $\mathbb{R}^{n+k}$ passing through $x$, tangent to $M$.
