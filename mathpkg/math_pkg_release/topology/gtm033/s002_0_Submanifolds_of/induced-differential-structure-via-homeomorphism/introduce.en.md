A differentiable structure can be transported along a homeomorphism: if $N$ is a manifold and $h: M \to N$ is a homeomorphism onto an open subset, then $M$ inherits a $C^r$ structure by pulling back charts from $N$ via $h$. This makes $h$ into a $C^r$ diffeomorphism onto its image.
