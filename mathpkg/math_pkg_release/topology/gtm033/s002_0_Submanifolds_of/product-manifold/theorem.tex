If $(M, \Phi)$ and $(N, \Psi)$ are $C^r$ manifolds, their Cartesian product is the manifold $(M \times N, \Theta)$, where $\Theta$ is the differential structure containing all charts of the form

$$(\varphi \times \psi, U \times V), \quad (\varphi, U) \in \Phi, \; (\psi, V) \in \Psi.$$

Here $\varphi \times \psi: U \times V \to \mathbb{R}^m \times \mathbb{R}^n$ is identified with a map into $\mathbb{R}^{m+n}$.
