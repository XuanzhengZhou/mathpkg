The tangent bundle $TM$ collects all tangent spaces of $M$ into a single geometric object, itself a subset of a Euclidean space. The fiber-wise tangent maps $Tf_x$ combine to give a global derivative map $Tf: TM \to TN$, which is the natural extension of differentiation to the manifold setting.
