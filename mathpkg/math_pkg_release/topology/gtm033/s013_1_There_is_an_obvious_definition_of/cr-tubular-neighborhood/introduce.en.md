A $C^r$ tubular neighborhood generalizes the smooth tubular neighborhood to the $C^r$ category, where $1 \leqslant r < \infty$. It consists of a $C^r$ vector bundle over a neat $C^r$ submanifold together with a $C^r$ embedding into the ambient manifold that identifies the zero section with the submanifold. This concept is the natural $C^r$ analogue of the smooth tubular neighborhood defined earlier in the chapter.
