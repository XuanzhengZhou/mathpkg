# Exercise 7

(a) The double of a $\partial$-manifold $M$ is the identification space obtained from $(M \times 0) \cup (M \times 1)$ by identifying $(x,0)$ and $(x,1)$ if $x \in \partial M$. The double is a $C^0$ manifold without boundary, of the same dimension, in which $M$ is embedded.
