# Exercise 11

(a) Regarding $S^1$ as the equator of $S^2$, we obtain $P^1$ as a submanifold of $P^2$. Show that $P^1$ is not a regular level surface of any $C^1$ map on $P^2$. [Hint: no neighborhood of $P^1$ in $P^2$ is separated by $P^1$.]
