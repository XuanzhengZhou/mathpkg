Define a $C^{r-1}$ map $\tau: T_1 M \to S^{q-1}$ as follows. Identify $TM$ with a subset of $M \times \mathbb{R}^q$; then $T_1 M \subset M \times S^{q-1}$. Define $\tau$ to be the restriction to $T_1 M$ of the projection $M \times S^{q-1} \to S^{q-1}$. Geometrically, $\tau$ is parallel translation of unit vectors based at points of $M$ to unit vectors based at $0$.
