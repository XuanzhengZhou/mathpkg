# Exercise 2

(a) Let $M, N, P$ be compact connected oriented $n$-manifolds without boundaries and let $M \xrightarrow{f} N \xrightarrow{g} P$ be continuous maps. Prove that $\deg(g \circ f) = (\deg g)(\deg f)$. The same formula holds for the mod 2 degree when $M, N, P$ are not oriented.

(b) Prove that the degree of a homeomorphism or a homotopy equivalence between compact connected oriented $n$-manifolds without boundary is $\pm 1$.
