# Exercise 1

A complex polynomial of degree $n$ defines a map of the Riemann sphere to itself of degree $n$. What is the degree of the map defined by a rational function $p(z)/q(z)$?

(Here the Riemann sphere $S^2$ is the compactification of the complex field $\mathbb{C}$ by $\infty$, and a rational function extends to a $C^\infty$ map $S^2 \to S^2$.)
