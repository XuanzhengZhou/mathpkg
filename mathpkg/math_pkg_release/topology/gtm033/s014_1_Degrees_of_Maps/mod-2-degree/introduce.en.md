The mod 2 degree is the $\mathbb{Z}_2$-valued analogue of the integer degree, defined without requiring orientations on the manifolds. It simply counts the parity of the number of preimages of a regular value. The mod 2 degree is a homotopy invariant for maps between unoriented (possibly nonorientable) compact manifolds.
