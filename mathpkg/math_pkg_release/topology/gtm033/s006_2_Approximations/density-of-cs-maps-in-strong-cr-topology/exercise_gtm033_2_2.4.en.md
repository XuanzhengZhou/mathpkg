# Exercise 4

There are relative versions of Theorems 2.6, 2.9, 2.12, and 2.13. Formulate and prove the relative version of each of these theorems (that is, versions where the maps are required to agree with a given map on a closed subset, or where the approximation takes place relative to a submanifold).
