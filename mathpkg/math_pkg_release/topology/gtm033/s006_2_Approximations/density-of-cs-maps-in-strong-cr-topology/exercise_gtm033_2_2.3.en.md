# Exercise 3

Let $p: M \to N$ be a $C^s$ map and $f: N \to M$ a $C^r$ section of $p$ (that is, $p \circ f = 1_N$).

(a) If $1 \leqslant r < s \leqslant \infty$ then $f$ can be $C^r$ approximated by $C^s$ sections. In fact if $g$ is sufficiently $C^r$ close to $f$ then $g(N)$ is the image of a section.

(b) If $0 = r < s \leqslant \infty$ and $p$ is submersive, then $f$ can be $C^r$ approximated by $C^s$ sections.
