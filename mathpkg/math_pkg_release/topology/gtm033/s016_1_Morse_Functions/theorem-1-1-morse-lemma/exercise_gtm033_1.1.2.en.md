# Exercise 2

Let $M \subset \mathbb{R}^{q+1}$ be a compact $C^2$ submanifold. For each $v \in S^q$ let $f_v: M \to \mathbb{R}$ be the map $f_v(x) = \langle v, x \rangle$. (This is essentially orthogonal projection into the line through $v$.) Then the set of $v \in S^q$ such that $f_v$ is a Morse function is open and dense.
