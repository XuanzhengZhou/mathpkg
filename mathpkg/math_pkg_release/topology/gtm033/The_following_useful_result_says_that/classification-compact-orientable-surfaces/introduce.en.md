This is the fundamental classification theorem for compact orientable surfaces: every such surface is diffeomorphic to a sphere with $p$ handles for a unique integer $p \geqslant 0$. The proof proceeds by induction on the number of saddles of an admissible Morse function, using Theorem 3.4 for the base case and a surgery argument at the topmost saddle for the inductive step.
