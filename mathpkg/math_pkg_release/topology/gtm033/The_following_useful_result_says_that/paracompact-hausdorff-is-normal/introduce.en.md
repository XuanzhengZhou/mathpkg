This theorem establishes the relationship between the covering axiom (paracompactness) and the separation axioms. Combined with the Hausdorff property, paracompactness implies normality, which in turn guarantees the existence of Urysohn functions separating disjoint closed sets. This is essential for constructing partitions of unity on manifolds.
