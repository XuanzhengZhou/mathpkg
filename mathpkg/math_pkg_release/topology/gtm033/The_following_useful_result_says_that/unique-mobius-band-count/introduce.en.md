The maximum number of pairwise disjoint Möbius bands that can be embedded in a nonorientable surface is a well-defined invariant. The proof uses the orientable double cover: preimages of disjoint Möbius bands are disjoint cylinders, and a genus bound on the double cover limits how many such cylinders can coexist.
