Let $p \geqslant 0$ be an integer. An orientable surface $M$ is of \text{genus} $p$ provided $M$ can be obtained from $S^2$ by successively attaching handles $p$ times. That is, there exist orientable surfaces $M_0, \ldots, M_p$ and orientable embeddings $f_i: S^0 \times D^2 \to M_{i-1}$ ($i = 1, \ldots, p$, if $p > 0$) such that

$$M_0 \approx S^2, \quad M_i \approx M_{i-1}[f_i], \quad M_p = M.$$
