# Exercise 9.1.8

Let $f: S^1 \to M$ be a loop in a surface $M$. Then $f$ preserves orientation (in the sense of Section 4.4) if and only if $\#_2(f, f) = 0$ (see Exercise 4, Section 5.2 for the mod $2$ intersection number $\#_2$).
