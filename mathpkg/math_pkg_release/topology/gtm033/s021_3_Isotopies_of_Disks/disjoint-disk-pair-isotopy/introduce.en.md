This theorem generalizes the single disk embedding isotopy result to pairs of disjoint disks. Given two pairs of disjoint disk embeddings in a connected manifold without boundary, an ambient isotopy can simultaneously carry each disk of the first pair to the corresponding disk of the second pair, provided the orientation conditions are satisfied. The result extends readily to any finite number of disjoint disks.
