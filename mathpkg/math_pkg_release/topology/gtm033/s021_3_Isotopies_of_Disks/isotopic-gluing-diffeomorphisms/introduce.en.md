If two gluing diffeomorphisms from the boundary of a manifold $Q$ to the boundary of $P$ are isotopic, then the resulting adjunction spaces $P \cup_f Q$ and $P \cup_g Q$ are diffeomorphic. The proof uses the fact that $g^{-1}f$ is isotopic to the identity on $\partial Q$, and this isotopy can be spread out over a collar to produce a diffeomorphism of $Q$ that mediates between the two gluings.
