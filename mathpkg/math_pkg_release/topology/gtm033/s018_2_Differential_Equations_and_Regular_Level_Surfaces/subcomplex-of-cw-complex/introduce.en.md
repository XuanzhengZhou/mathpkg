A subcomplex of a CW-complex $X$ is a closed subspace that is itself a union of cells of $X$. Subcomplexes form natural subspaces that inherit CW-complex structure. Together with the ambient complex they form a CW-pair, which is the natural setting for relative homotopy and homology computations.
