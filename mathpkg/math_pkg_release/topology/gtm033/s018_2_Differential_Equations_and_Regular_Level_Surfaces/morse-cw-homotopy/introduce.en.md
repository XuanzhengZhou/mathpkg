Theorem 4.1 is the culmination of Morse theory's application to CW-complexes: every compact manifold admits a CW-complex structure whose $k$-cells correspond bijectively to the critical points of index $k$ of an admissible Morse function. This provides a direct bridge between the analytic data of a Morse function and the combinatorial homotopy type of the manifold.
