# Exercise 5: Morse Functions on Surfaces

Let $S$ be a compact surface of genus $p$.

**(a)** Prove that every Morse function on $S$ has at least $2p + 2$ critical points.

**(b)** Construct a Morse function on $S$ that has exactly $2p + 2$ critical points.
