# Exercise 10: Homological Euler Characteristic with Boundary

Let $\partial_+ M$ be a union of components of the boundary of a compact $n$-manifold $M$; put $\partial_- M = \partial M - \partial_+ M$. Prove that

$$\chi'(M, \partial_+ M) = \chi'(M, \partial_- M).$$
