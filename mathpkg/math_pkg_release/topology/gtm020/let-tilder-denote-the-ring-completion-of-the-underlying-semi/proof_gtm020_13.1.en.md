The underlying additive group structure of $\tilde{R}$ is the group completion of the underlying semigroup structure of $R$. We consider $\lambda_t: R \rightarrow 1 + \tilde{R}[[t]]^+$, which is the multiplicative group of power series with 1 as a constant term. This defines to a group morphism $\tilde{\lambda}_t: \tilde{R} \rightarrow 1 + \tilde{R}[[t]]^+$ such that $\tilde{\lambda}_t\theta = \lambda_t$.
