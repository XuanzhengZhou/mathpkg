Let $\xi$ be a vector bundle over $B$ with total space $E(\xi)$. A \text{riemannian metric} (when $F = \mathbf{R}$) or \text{hermitian metric} (when $F = \mathbf{C}$) on $\xi$ is a continuous function
$$\beta: E(\xi \oplus \xi) \rightarrow F$$
such that on each fibre, $\beta$ restricts to an inner product in the sense of Definition 9.1.
