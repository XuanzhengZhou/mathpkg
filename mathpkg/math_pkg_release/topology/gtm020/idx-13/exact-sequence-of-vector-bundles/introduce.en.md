An exact sequence of vector bundles transplants the standard terminology of exact sequences from homological algebra into the category of vector bundles over a fixed base space $B$. The sequence is formed by vector bundles $\xi_i$ and $B$-morphisms $u_i$ of constant rank. Exactness at each interior position means the image of the incoming morphism equals the kernel of the outgoing morphism. This notion is fundamental for studying relationships between vector bundles, particularly in the context of short exact sequences and splitting.
