The $K$-ring of complex projective space $CP^n$ has a simple algebraic description: $K(CP^n) \cong \mathbb{Z}[v]/(v^{n-1})$ where $v = \xi_n(\mathbb{C}) - 1$ is the reduced canonical line bundle minus the trivial bundle. The ring structure is further elucidated by the Chern character $ch: K(CP^n) \to H^{\mathrm{ev}}(CP^n, \mathbb{Q})$, which provides a ring isomorphism between $K$-theory and even-dimensional rational cohomology.
