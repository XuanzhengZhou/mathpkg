The proof follows from the multiplicative property of the Adams operations $\psi^k$ with respect to external tensor products of vector bundles. Since the $\theta_k$ classes are defined via the action of $\psi^k$ on the generator $\Delta^+_{8m}$ in $KO(S(\xi))$, the formula $\theta_k(\xi \oplus \eta) = \theta_k(\xi) \cdot \theta_k(\eta)$ is derived from the corresponding property of $\psi^k$. The second part of the statement observes that the defining formulas for $\theta_k$ involve only $\psi^k$ applied to the classes $\alpha_j$ and their inverses, which are defined for any real oriented $2n$-dimensional vector bundle via the splitting principle and the Thom isomorphism.
