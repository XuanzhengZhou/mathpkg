The characteristic classes $\theta_k(\xi)$ satisfy a multiplicative property with respect to the Whitney sum of vector bundles: $\theta_k(\xi \oplus \eta) = \theta_k(\xi) \cdot \theta_k(\eta)$. This follows from the behavior of the Adams operations $\psi^k$ under direct sums. A key consequence is that these formulas, which involve only the Adams operations and their inverses, enable the definition of $\theta_k$ for any real oriented $2n$-dimensional vector bundle.
