A crucial property of the $\theta_k$ characteristic classes is their invariance under fibre homotopy equivalences of the associated sphere bundle $S(\xi)$. This means that if two vector bundles have fibre homotopy equivalent sphere bundles, their $\theta_k$ classes coincide. As a consequence, the vanishing of the $J$-homomorphism image $J(2n\zeta) = 0$ forces the relation $\frac{k^n-1}{2}(\zeta-1) = 0$ in $KO$-theory, providing a connection between the Adams conjecture and the $J$-homomorphism.
