Bott's theorem generalizes the classical Bott periodicity to the parametrized setting of $\operatorname{Spin}(8m+1)$ vector bundles. For such a bundle $\xi$, the $KO$-theory of its sphere bundle $S(\xi)$ is a free module of rank $2$ over the $KO$-theory of the base $B(\xi)$, generated by the unit $1$ and the class $\tilde{\alpha}^*_{\xi}(\Delta^+_{8m})$ coming from the lifted principal bundle. The involution exchanges the two generators, reflecting the symmetry of the positive and negative spin representations.
