The Atiyah--Hirzebruch spectral sequence provides a fundamental computational tool relating the generalized $K$-cohomology of a finite CW-complex $X$ to its ordinary singular cohomology. The $E_2$-page is given by $\tilde{H}^p(X, KO^q(*))$, and the spectral sequence converges to the associated graded of $KO^*(X)$. This is the $K$-theoretic analogue of the Serre spectral sequence for singular cohomology.
