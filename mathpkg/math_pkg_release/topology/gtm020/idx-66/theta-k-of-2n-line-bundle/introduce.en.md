Generalizing the $2$-plane bundle computation, the $\theta_k$ class of $2n\zeta$ (the Whitney sum of $2n$ copies of a line bundle $\zeta$) is computed. Using the relation $(\zeta-1)^2 = -2(\zeta-1)$, the binomial expansion simplifies to a linear expression in $\zeta-1$. The final result is $\theta_k(2n\zeta) = k^n + \frac{k^n-1}{2}(\zeta-1)$, which expresses $\theta_k$ purely in terms of the integer $k$ and the class $\zeta-1$.
