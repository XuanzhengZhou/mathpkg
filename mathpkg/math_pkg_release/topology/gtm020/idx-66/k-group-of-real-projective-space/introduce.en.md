The reduced $K$-group of real projective space $\tilde{K}(RP^n)$ is a finite cyclic group whose order is a power of $2$ depending on the dimension $n$. Specifically, $|\tilde{K}(RP^n)| = 2^{\lfloor n/2 \rfloor}$. The generator is obtained from the ring generator of $K(CP^n)$ via the natural map induced by the quotient $RP^{2n+1} \to CP^n$ and the collapse of the Atiyah-Hirzebruch spectral sequence.
