Recall that $E(\gamma_k^m)$ is the subspace of $(V, x) \in G_k(F^m) \times F^m$ with $x \in V$. Then the projection $q: E(\gamma_k^m) \rightarrow F^m$, given by the relation $q(V, x) = x$, is a Gauss map.
