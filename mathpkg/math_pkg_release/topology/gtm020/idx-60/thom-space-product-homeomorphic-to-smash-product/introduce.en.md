For real vector bundles $\xi$ and $\eta$ with compact base spaces, the Thom space of the product bundle $\xi \times \eta$ is naturally homeomorphic to the smash product $T(\xi) \wedge T(\eta)$ of the individual Thom spaces. This product formula is essential for computations involving Thom spaces and underlies the compatibility of the Thom isomorphism with external products. The result follows from the fact that $E(\xi \times \eta) = E(\xi) \times E(\eta)$, where the Thom space is the one-point compactification of the total space. A more general construction, valid without compactness, provides an explicit pair-homeomorphism.
