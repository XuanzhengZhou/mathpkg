If $\xi$ is a numerable principal $G$-bundle over $B$ and $f: B' \rightarrow B$ is any continuous map, then the pullback bundle $f^*(\xi)$ over $B'$ is also numerable. The proof uses the fact that the pullback of a trivial bundle along the inverse image of the trivializing open sets remains trivial, and the pullback of a locally finite partition of unity remains a locally finite partition of unity. This result is essential for establishing the functoriality of numerable bundles under pullback.
