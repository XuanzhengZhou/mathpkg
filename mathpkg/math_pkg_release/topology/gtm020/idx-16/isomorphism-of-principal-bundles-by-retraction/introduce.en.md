Under the notation established in (9.6) — where a retraction map $r$ and an associated construction are defined — the principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ over $B \times I$ is isomorphic to the pullback bundle $r^*(\xi)$ under the retraction. This result is a corollary of the construction in (9.6) and provides the key technical step for proving the homotopy classification theorem for numerable principal $G$-bundles in the following section.
