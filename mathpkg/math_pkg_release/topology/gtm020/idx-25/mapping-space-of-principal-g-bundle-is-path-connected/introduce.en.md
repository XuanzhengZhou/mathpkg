For a locally trivial principal $G$-bundle $P$ over a base space $B$, the space of equivariant maps $\text{Map}_G(P, E_G)$ into the universal bundle $E_G$ is always nonempty. This space is furthermore path connected. The path components of the associated mapping space $\text{Map}_P(B, B_G)$ correspond precisely to isomorphism classes of pullbacks of the universal bundle that are isomorphic to $P$.
