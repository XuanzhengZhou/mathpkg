The Kunneth formula describes the cohomology of a product space $X \times Y$ in terms of the cohomologies of the factors. For spaces with the homotopy type of CW-complexes, the formula states $H^n(X \times Y, \Pi) = \bigoplus_{0 \leq q \leq n} H^q(X, H^{n-q}(Y, \Pi))$. This decomposition follows directly from Thom's theorem on the mapping space into Eilenberg-MacLane spaces and the exponential law for mapping spaces. The formula is a consequence of the identification of cohomology with homotopy classes of maps into $K(\Pi, n)$.
