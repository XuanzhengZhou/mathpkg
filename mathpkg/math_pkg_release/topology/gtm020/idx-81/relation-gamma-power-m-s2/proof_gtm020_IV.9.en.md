Using the binomial expansion, we compute
$$\gamma^m = (1 + (\gamma - 1))^m = \sum_{k=0}^{m} \binom{m}{k} (\gamma - 1)^k.$$
Since $(\gamma - 1)^2 = 0$ in $K(S^2)$ by Corollary 9.2, all terms with $k \geq 2$ vanish. Hence
$$\gamma^m = 1 + m(\gamma - 1)$$
as claimed.
