This theorem describes the image of the $a$-morphism when restricted to the semiring of isomorphism classes of even-dimensional oriented real vector bundles. Over a $2$-divisible ring $R$, the possible values are precisely the even power series of the form $e^{at} + e^{-at}$ (or the zero series), parametrized by an element $a \in R$. This gives a complete classification of additive-type characteristic classes for oriented even-dimensional bundles.
