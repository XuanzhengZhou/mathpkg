This proposition establishes that the Chern character restricted to the $K$-theory of the $2$-sphere $S^2 = \mathbb{CP}^1$ is an isomorphism onto the integral cohomology ring. This is the base case for the inductive proof of Bott periodicity. The key observation is that $\mathrm{ch}(1) = 1$ and $\mathrm{ch}(\gamma - 1) = c_1(\gamma)$, which generates $H^2(S^2, \mathbf{Z})$.
