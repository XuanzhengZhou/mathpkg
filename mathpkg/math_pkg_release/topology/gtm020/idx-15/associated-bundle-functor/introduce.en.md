The assignment sending a principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ to its associated fibre bundle $\xi[F]$ extends to a functor. On morphisms, a principal bundle morphism $(u, f): \xi \to \xi'$ induces a fibre bundle morphism $(u_F, f): \xi[F] \to \xi'[F]$ by passing the $G$-equivariant map $u \times 1_F$ to the quotient. This expresses the fact that properties of a fibre bundle reflect the properties of the underlying principal bundle.
