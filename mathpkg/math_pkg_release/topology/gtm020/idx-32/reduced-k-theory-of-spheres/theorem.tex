The calculations of $\tilde{K}_F(S^i)$ for low dimensions are:

$$\begin{aligned}
\tilde{K}_R(S^0) &= \mathbb{Z}, & \tilde{K}_C(S^0) &= \mathbb{Z}, & \tilde{K}_H(S^0) &= \mathbb{Z}, \\
\tilde{K}_R(S^1) &= \mathbb{Z}_2, & \tilde{K}_C(S^1) &= 0, & \tilde{K}_H(S^1) &= 0, \\
\tilde{K}_R(S^2) &= \mathbb{Z}_2, & \tilde{K}_C(S^2) &= ? & \tilde{K}_H(S^2) &= ?
\end{aligned}$$

These values are obtained from the Bott periodicity isomorphism $\tilde{K}_F(S^i) \cong \pi_{i-1}(U_F)$ and the known homotopy groups of $U_F$.
