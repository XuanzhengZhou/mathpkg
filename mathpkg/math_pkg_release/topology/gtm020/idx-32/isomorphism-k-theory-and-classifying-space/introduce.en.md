The corepresentation of reduced $K$-theory extends naturally to the full $K$-theory cofunctor $K_F$ by incorporating the rank. Using the natural splitting $K_F(X) = \tilde{K}_F(X) \oplus \mathbb{Z}$, one obtains an isomorphism $\theta: [-, B_{(F)} \times \mathbb{Z}] \to K_F(-)$ on connected finite CW-complexes. The additional $\mathbb{Z}$ factor in the classifying space accounts for the rank (dimension) of vector bundles, which is lost when passing to reduced $K$-theory.
