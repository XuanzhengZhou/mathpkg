Let $g: E(\xi) \to F^\infty$ be a Gauss map. Define $\beta: E(\xi \oplus \xi) \to F$ by the relation $\beta(x, x') = (g(x)|g(x'))$. Then, since $g$ is continuous and a monomorphism on each fibre, $\beta$ is a riemannian or hermitian metric.

By (5.5) and (9.5), every bundle over a paracompact space has a metric.
