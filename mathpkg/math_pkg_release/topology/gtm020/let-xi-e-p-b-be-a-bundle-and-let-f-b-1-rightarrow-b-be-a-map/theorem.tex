5.4 Example. Let $\xi$ be a bundle over $B$, and let $A$ be a subspace of $B$ with inclusion map $j: A \rightarrow B$. Then $\xi|A$ and $j^*(\xi)$ are $A$-isomorphic. In effect, we define $u: \xi|A \rightarrow j^*(\xi)$ by $u(x) = (p(x), x)$, and this is clearly an $A$-isomorphism.
