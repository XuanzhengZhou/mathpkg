Let $\xi = (X, p, B)$ be a principal G-bundle, and let $W$ be a locally compact space. Then the relation $(x, w)s = (xs, w)$ defines a right G-space structure on $X \times W$. The translation map $\tau_1$ for $X \times W$ is defined by the relation $\tau_1((x, w), (x', w)) = \tau(x, x')$, where $\tau$ is the translation map for $X \mod G$ and $X \times W \rightarrow (X \times W) \mod G$ are identification maps, and the function $\phi: (X \times W) \mod G \rightarrow (X \mod G) \times W$, where $\phi((x, w)G) = (xG, w)$, is a homeomorphism.
