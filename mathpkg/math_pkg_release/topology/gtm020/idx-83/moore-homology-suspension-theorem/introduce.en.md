This theorem, pointed out to Husemoller by John Moore, identifies the homology Hopf algebra of the loop space of a suspension. For a path connected space $X$ with $R$-flat homology, the natural coalgebra morphism $H_*(\beta): H_*(X) \to H_*(\Omega S(X))$ factors through the tensor Hopf algebra $TH_*(X)$, and the resulting Hopf algebra morphism $TH_*(X) \to H_*(\Omega S(X))$ is an isomorphism. The proof combines Proposition 1.1 (which produces the coalgebra morphism factorization) with Proposition 1.2 (which upgrades a coalgebra morphism satisfying a certain condition to a Hopf algebra isomorphism).
