Using the notation of (5.1), prove that a map $g_0: B_G \rightarrow B_H$ satisfying $f_0 g_0 \simeq 1$ provides a canonical reduction of the structure group of every principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ over $B$. In other words, show that if there exists a homotopy left inverse to $f_0: B_H \rightarrow B_G$, then every principal $G$-bundle admits a canonical reduction to $H$.
