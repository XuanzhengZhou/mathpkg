This corollary of Theorem 1.1 identifies a principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ with the associated bundle $\xi[G]$ having fibre $G$ on which $G$ acts by left multiplication. The result follows by considering the trivial subgroup $H = \{e\}$ in Theorem 1.1, since $\xi \bmod \{e\} \cong \xi$ and $G \bmod \{e\} \cong G$. It shows that every principal bundle can be viewed as an associated bundle with fibre the structure group itself.
