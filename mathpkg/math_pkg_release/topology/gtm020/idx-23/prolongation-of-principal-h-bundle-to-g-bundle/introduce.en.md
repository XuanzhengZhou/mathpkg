This theorem guarantees that every principal $H$-bundle can be extended to a principal $G$-bundle when $H$ is a closed subgroup of $G$. The prolongation is constructed by taking the associated bundle $\eta[G]$ with fibre $G$, where $H$ acts on $G$ by left translation via the inclusion $H \hookrightarrow G$. The construction preserves key properties: if the original $H$-bundle is trivial, locally trivial, or numerable (in the sense of having a numerable trivializing cover), then the prolonged $G$-bundle shares that property.
