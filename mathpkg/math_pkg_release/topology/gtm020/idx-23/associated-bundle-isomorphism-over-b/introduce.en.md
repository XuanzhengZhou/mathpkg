This corollary sharpens the relationship between a principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ and a restriction $\eta$ to a closed subgroup $H$. It states that the associated bundle $\eta[G]$ obtained by extending the structure group from $H$ back to $G$ is isomorphic to $\xi$ as a bundle over $B$. Moreover, the natural map $g$ between the $G$-space total spaces is a $G$-space isomorphism, meaning it respects the full $G$-action on both sides. This shows that restricting and then prolonging recovers the original bundle up to canonical isomorphism.
