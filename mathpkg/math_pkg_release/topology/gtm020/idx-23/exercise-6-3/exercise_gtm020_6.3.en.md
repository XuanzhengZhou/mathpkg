Define restriction and prolongation of principal bundles for a (continuous) group morphism $h: H \rightarrow G$. In what sense do the results (2.2), (2.3), (3.1), (4.1), and (5.1) have analogues for this more general situation? Specifically, describe how the theorems on prolongation existence, cross-section criteria for restriction, associated bundle isomorphisms, local coordinate descriptions, and classifying space characterizations generalize when the closed subgroup inclusion $H \hookrightarrow G$ is replaced by an arbitrary continuous homomorphism.
