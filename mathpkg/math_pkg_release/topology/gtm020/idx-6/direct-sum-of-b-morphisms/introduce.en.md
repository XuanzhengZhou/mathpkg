The Whitney sum $\oplus$ is extended to a functor on the category $\text{Bun}_B$ of bundles over a fixed base $B$. Given two $B$-morphisms $u_1$ and $u_2$, their direct sum $u_1 \oplus u_2$ acts componentwise on the fibre product total space. The compatibility of the morphisms with the projections ensures that the result is again a $B$-morphism over the base $B$.
