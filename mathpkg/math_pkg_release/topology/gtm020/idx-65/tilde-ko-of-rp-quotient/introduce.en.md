This corollary gives a complete description of the reduced real $KO$-theory of the relative real projective space $RP^l/RP^k$ as a quotient of the representation ring $N_k$ of the Clifford algebra $C_k$ by the image $rN_l$. The structure depends on the congruence class of $k$ modulo $4$: when $k \equiv 3 \pmod{4}$ there is an extra infinite cyclic summand, while for all other cases the group is purely torsion. The exponent $e$ counts those Bott-periodic dimensions $q \in \{0,1,2,4\} \pmod{8}$ that fall in the interval $(k, l]$.
