Corollary 10.2 states that every element of the real K-theory group $KO(X)$ can be expressed as a formal difference $a_1 - a_2$ of two classes represented by oriented real vector bundles. This follows from Proposition 10.1 and allows one to reduce statements about general $KO(X)$-classes to statements about oriented bundles. The result is essential for extending properties of the Adams operations from the oriented subring to all of $KO(X)$, as done in the proof of Theorem 10.5.
