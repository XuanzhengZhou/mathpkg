Using the properties of Adams operations established in Theorem 10.5, we have $\psi^k(a_1) \cdots \psi^k(a_m) = k^m a_1 \cdots a_m$, which equals $k^m$ times the image of $\beta_{2m}$ under the external product. Since the map $\tilde{K}(S^{2m}) \rightarrow \tilde{K}(S^2 \times \cdots \times S^2)$ is injective in the relevant range, the generator $\beta_{2m}$ satisfies $\beta_{2m}^2 = 0$ and the Adams operation acts as $\psi^k(\beta_{2m}) = k^m \beta_{2m}$. The group is infinite cyclic, generated by $\beta_{2m}$.
