This proposition describes the homotopy type of the suspension of the loop space on the double suspension fibre $F_n$, after localizing at an odd prime $p$. The result is a wedge of $ST_n$ together with a collection of higher-dimensional spheres whose dimensions are determined by the homology of $S\Omega F_n$. The decomposition is analogous to the James splitting of $S\Omega S(X)$ and plays a fundamental role in Husemoller and Moore's analysis of the double suspension sequences for odd spheres.
