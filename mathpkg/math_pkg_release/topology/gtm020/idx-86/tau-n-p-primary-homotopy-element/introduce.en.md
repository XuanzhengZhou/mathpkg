The element $\tau_n$ is a $p$-primary homotopy class in $\pi_{2np-3}(S^{2n-1})_{(p)}$, defined as the image under the boundary map $\partial$ of a generator of the relative homotopy group $\pi_{2np-2}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}, S^{2n-1})_{(p)} \cong \mathbf{Z}/p$. It is nonzero exactly when $n > 1$, and its image in $\pi_{2np-3}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1})$ vanishes, implying that its double suspension is null homotopic. The mapping cone of $\tau_n$ defines the space $T_n$, which plays a central role in the James-type decomposition of $\Omega F_n$.
