The space $E_n$ is defined as the homotopy fibre of the canonical map from the odd sphere $S^{2n-1}$ into the double loop space $\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}$. It sits in a fibre sequence that connects the triple loop space of an odd sphere with $S^{2n-1}$. The space $E_n$ is used to relate two key fibre sequences in the study of the double suspension: one involving $\Omega F_n$ and $\Omega^2 S^{2np+1}$, and another involving $\Omega^2 S^{2np-1}$ and the sphere $S^{2n-1}$.
