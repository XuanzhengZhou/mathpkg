This proposition identifies the map $u_p \colon \Omega S^{2np-1} \to \Omega S^{2np-1}$ after localization at a prime $p$: it has degree $p$, meaning the induced map on $H_{2np-2}(-; \mathbf{Z}_{(p)})$ is multiplication by $p$. Moreover, the composition $\Delta \circ E^2$ equals $u_p$, where $E^2$ is the double suspension map and $\Delta$ is the boundary map appearing in the study of the fibre of $S^{2n-1} \to \Omega^2 S^{2n+1}$. This result is fundamental for understanding the $p$-primary homotopy of Stiefel varieties.
