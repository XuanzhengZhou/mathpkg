This theorem states that the property of being a weak fibration is local-to-global with respect to numerable coverings. If a bundle is a weak fibration over each member of a numerable covering of the base space, then it is a weak fibration globally. The proof, like those of Theorems A.6 and A.9, reduces to an application of the section extension property (Theorem A.4) via an appropriate mapping space construction.
