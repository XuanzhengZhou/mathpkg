The section extension property is one of the four key properties studied in Dold's theory. It states that any cross section defined on a subset $A$ of the base space, which can be extended to a halo of $A$, can in fact be extended to the entire base space. This property serves as a technical tool for proving that certain bundle properties hold globally if they hold locally over a numerable covering.
