This result is stated as a theorem. The statement should be compared with the relation between $R(G)$ and elements of $R(T)$ elementwise fixed by the action of the Weyl group. The proof relies on the spectral sequence of the fibration $G/T \to B_T \to B_G$ and the fact that $G/T$ has vanishing odd-dimensional cohomology, yielding that the edge homomorphism is injective with image equal to the Weyl-invariant subring.
