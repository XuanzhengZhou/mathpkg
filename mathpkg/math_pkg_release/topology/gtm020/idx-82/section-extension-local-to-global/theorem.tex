Let $p \colon E \to X$ be a bundle.
\begin{enumerate}
\item If $p \colon E \to X$ has the section extension property, its restriction to an open subset of $X$ has the section extension property.
\item If $p \colon E \to X$ has the section extension property over the subsets $U_i$ of a numerable covering $\{U_i\}$, then $p \colon E \to X$ has the section extension property.
\end{enumerate}
