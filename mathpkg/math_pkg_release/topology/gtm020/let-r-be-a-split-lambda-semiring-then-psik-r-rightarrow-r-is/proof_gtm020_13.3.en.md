In a $\lambda$-ring $R$, the $\psi^k$ are polynomial combinations of the $\lambda^i$. We consider here other operations which are combinations of the $\lambda^i$. These operations are useful in studying the representation rings of Spin($n$) and the immersion theory of manifolds.
