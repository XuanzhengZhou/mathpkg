A direct corollary of the Yoneda representation theorem: taking $T = [-, L]$ as the target cofunctor, the natural morphisms between the two representable cofunctors $[-, K]$ and $[-, L]$ are in bijective correspondence with morphisms from $K$ to $L$. This establishes that the Yoneda embedding is fully faithful, a fundamental fact in category theory.
