Apply Theorem 1.2 with $T = [-, L]$. Then $\beta: [[-, K], [-, L]] \to T(K) = [K, L]$ is a bijection, where $T(K) = [K, L]$ by definition of the cofunctor $[-, L]$. Hence $\beta: [[-, K], [-, L]] \to [K, L]$, defined by $\beta(\phi) = \phi(K)1_K$, is a bijection.
