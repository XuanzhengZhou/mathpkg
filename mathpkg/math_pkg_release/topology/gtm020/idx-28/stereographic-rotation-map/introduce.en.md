The map $\phi$ associates to each point $x$ on $S^n$ (except the south pole) the rotation that carries the north pole $e_n$ to $x$. This is a smooth map defined on the sphere minus one point, and together with a reflected version on the complementary punctured sphere, it provides the local trivializations needed to compute the characteristic map of the tangent bundle $\tau(S^n)$.
