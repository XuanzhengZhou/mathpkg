Let $\alpha: S^{n-1} \times S^{n-1} \to S^{n-1}$ be the map defined by $\alpha(x)y = y - 2(y|x)x$. Consider the map $S^{n-1} \times S^{n-1} \to S^{n-1}$ given by $(x, y) \mapsto \alpha(x)y$. Its bidegree (the pair of degrees of the partial maps $x \mapsto \alpha(x)y_0$ and $y \mapsto \alpha(x_0)y$) is
$$(+1 + (-1)^n, \; -1).$$
