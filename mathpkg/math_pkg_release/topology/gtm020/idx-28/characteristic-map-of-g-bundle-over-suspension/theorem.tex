A map $c_\xi: B \to G$ associated with a regular principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ over the suspension $SB$ as the transition function between the two trivializations over the upper and lower cones is called a \emph{characteristic map} of $\xi$. Specifically, let $SB$ be represented as the quotient of $B \times [-1, +1]$. For a regular bundle, there exist trivializations on $B(-\varepsilon, +1]$ and $B[-1, +\varepsilon)$, and their difference on the overlap $B(-\varepsilon, +\varepsilon)$ defines a map $B \to G$, the characteristic map.
