By viewing $\mathbf{C}^2 = \mathbf{H}$, prove that $SU(2) = Sp(1)$. Then prove that $\pi_i(U(2)) \cong \pi_i(SU(2)) \oplus \pi_i(S^1)$ for all $i \geq 1$, and use the homotopy exact sequences of the fibre bundles $U(n) \to S^{2n-1}$ and $Sp(n) \to S^{4n-1}$ to determine the low-dimensional homotopy groups of $U(n)$ and $Sp(n)$.
