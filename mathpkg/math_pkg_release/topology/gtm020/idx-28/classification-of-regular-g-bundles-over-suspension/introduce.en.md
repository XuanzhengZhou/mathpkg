For a path-connected group $G$, isomorphism classes of regular principal $G$-bundles over $SB$ correspond bijectively to homotopy classes of maps $B \to G$. The characteristic map $c_\xi$ encodes the transition between the two trivializations over the upper and lower cones. Changing the trivialization conjugates $c_\xi$ by a path, which preserves the homotopy class since $G$ is path-connected.
