Let $SB$ denote the (nonreduced) suspension of a space $B$, viewed as the quotient of $B \times [-1, +1]$ with $B \times \{-1\}$ identified to one point and $B \times \{+1\}$ identified to a second point. A bundle $\xi$ over $SB$ is called \emph{regular} provided, for some $\varepsilon > 0$, the restrictions $\xi|_{B(-\varepsilon, +1]}$ and $\xi|_{B[-1, +\varepsilon)}$ are trivial bundles, where $BJ$ denotes the image of $B \times J$ in $SB$ for $J \subset [-1, +1]$.
