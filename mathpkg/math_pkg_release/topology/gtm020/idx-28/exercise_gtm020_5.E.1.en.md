Verify that the formula in (9.1) has the desired properties: for $a, b \in S^{n-1}$ with $a \neq -b$, let $R(b, a)$ denote the rotation in $SO(n)$ that sends $a$ to $b$ by rotating in the $(a, b)$-plane through the smallest angle between $a$ and $b$, and fixes the orthogonal complement of the $(a, b)$-plane. Show that $R(b, a)$ is well-defined and satisfies $R(b, a)a = b$.
