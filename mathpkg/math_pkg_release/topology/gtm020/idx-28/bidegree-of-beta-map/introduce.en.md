The bidegree of the beta map is obtained from the bidegree of the alpha map by the relation $\beta(x)y = -\alpha(x)y$. Since multiplication by $-1$ on $S^{n-1}$ has degree $(-1)^n$, the bidegree of $\beta$ is the bidegree of $\alpha$ multiplied componentwise by $(1, (-1)^n)$. This computation is used in determining the homotopy class of the characteristic map of the tangent bundle of $S^n$.
