The characteristic map $c_n$ of the tangent bundle of $S^n$ is expressed concretely as the square of the rotation sending $e_{n-1}$ to $x$. By Proposition 9.8, this equals the composition of two reflections (or two $180^\circ$ rotations) through $x$ and $e_{n-1}$. This explicit formula enables the computation of the homotopy class of $c_n$ in $\pi_{n-1}(SO(n))$, which is fundamental for understanding parallelizability and vector fields on spheres.
