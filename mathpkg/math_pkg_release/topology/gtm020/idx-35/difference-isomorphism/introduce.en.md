Definition 4.1 from Relative K-Theory in Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. A difference isomorphism over a pair of spaces $(X,A)$ is a vector bundle morphism whose restriction to $A$ is an isomorphism. This concept is fundamental for constructing the relative $K$-theory groups $K(X,A)$ via the semigroups $L(X,A)$ of difference isomorphism classes.
