Definition 4.2 from Relative K-Theory. The set $S(X,A)$ is the semigroup of isomorphism classes of difference isomorphisms of $F$-vector bundles over a pair $(X,A)$, equipped with the Whitney sum operation as its semigroup structure. Pullback along maps of pairs induces compatible semigroup morphisms.
