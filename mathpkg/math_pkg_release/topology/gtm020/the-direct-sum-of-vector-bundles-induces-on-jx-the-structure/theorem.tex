5.3 Remarks. Let $X$ be a finite CW-complex. The group $J(X)$ is a subgroup of another group $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X)$. We outline its construction. Consider the set $F_n(X)$ of all fibre homotopy classes of fibrations with fibre $S^{n-1}$. The transition functions have values in the semigroup $H(n)$ of homotopy equivalences $S^{n-1} \rightarrow S^{n-1}$.
