Theorem 6.4 asserts that the natural transformation from $L$-theory to $K$-theory is compatible with the cup product structures on both theories. The diagram shows that applying the $L \to K$ map and then taking the $K$-cup product yields the same result as first taking the $L$-cup product and then applying the $L \to K$ map. This compatibility is fundamental for comparing cohomological operations in the two theories and ensures that the $L$-cup and $K$-cup products are consistent under the difference isomorphism construction.
