The following diagram is commutative.

$$L(X, A) \otimes L(Y, B) \xrightarrow{L\text{-cup}} L(X \times Y, (A \times Y) \cup (X \times B))$$

$$K(X, A) \otimes K(Y, B) \xrightarrow{K\text{-cup}} K(X \times Y, (A \times Y) \cup (X \times B))$$

Here the vertical arrows are the natural transformations $L \to K$ given by the difference isomorphism construction.
