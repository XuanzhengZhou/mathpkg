This theorem establishes that the natural projection from the Stiefel variety $V_k(F^n)$ of orthonormal $k$-frames to the Grassmannian $G_k(F^n)$ of $k$-dimensional subspaces is a locally trivial principal bundle with structure group $U_F(k)$. The proof uses the Gram-Schmidt map to construct local sections over open neighborhoods in the Grassmannian, providing an elementary and geometric argument that avoids deep properties of Lie groups.
