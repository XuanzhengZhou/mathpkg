The oriented Grassmannian $SG_k(F^n)$ parametrizes $k$-dimensional subspaces of $F^n$ equipped with an orientation, formalized as an equivalence class of orthonormal bases modulo the action of the special unitary group $SU_F(k)$. It is realized as a homogeneous space $U_F(n)/(SU_F(k) \times U_F(n-k))$. The forgetful map $SG_k(F^n) \to G_k(F^n)$ is a double cover for real vector spaces and a circle bundle for complex vector spaces.
