The bundle $(V_k(F^n), p, G_k(F^n))$ is a principal $U_F(k)$-bundle, and the bundle $(V_k(F^n), p, SG_k(F^n))$ is a principal $SU_F(k)$-bundle. Here $V_k(F^n)$ is the Stiefel variety of orthonormal $k$-frames in $F^n$, $G_k(F^n)$ is the Grassmann variety of $k$-dimensional subspaces, and $SG_k(F^n)$ is the oriented Grassmannian.
