Let $E$ be a smooth real vector bundle over a manifold $M$, and let $\Gamma(E)$ denote the space of smooth sections. A \textbf{pseudo-riemannian metric} $g$ on $E$ is an $A^0(M)$-bilinear map
$$
g: \Gamma(E) \times \Gamma(E) \to A^0(M)
$$
such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $g(s, s') = g(s', s)$ for all $s, s' \in \Gamma(E)$ (symmetry), and
\item there exists an isomorphism $\gamma: \Gamma(E) \to \Gamma(E^\vee)$ such that $\gamma(s)(s') = g(s, s')$ for all $s, s' \in \Gamma(E)$ (nondegeneracy, where $E^\vee$ is the dual bundle).
\end{enumerate}
