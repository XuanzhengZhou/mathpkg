Let $E$ be a smooth $n$-dimensional vector bundle with connection $\nabla$ over a smooth manifold $M$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item The characteristic Chern forms $c_q(E, \nabla)$ are closed forms, i.e., $d\,c_q(E, \nabla) = 0$.
\item For any invariant polynomial $\phi(x) \in \text{Inv}(n)$, the form $\phi(K)$ obtained by substituting the curvature $K$ of $\nabla$ is a closed form, i.e., $d\,\phi(K) = 0$.
\end{enumerate}
