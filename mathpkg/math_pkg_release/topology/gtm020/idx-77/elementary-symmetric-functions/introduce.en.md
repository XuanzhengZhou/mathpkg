The elementary symmetric functions $e_q(\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_n)$ are polynomials defined via the generating function $-t \frac{d}{dt} \log Q_x(t)$. In characteristic zero, these polynomials provide an alternative set of generators for the algebra of invariant polynomials, closely related to the Chern polynomials $c_q$ and the traces $\text{Tr}(X^q)$. They are the elementary symmetric functions in the eigenvalues when $X$ is diagonalizable.
