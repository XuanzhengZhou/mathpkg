Let $A$ be a $\mathbf{Z}_2$-graded algebra.

Let $M(A)$ denote the free abelian group with irreducible $\mathbf{Z}_2$-graded $A$-modules (modules with no proper nontrivial $\mathbf{Z}_2$-graded submodules) as free generators.

Let $N(A)$ denote the free abelian group with irreducible $A$-modules (modules with no proper nontrivial submodules, ignoring the grading) as free generators.
