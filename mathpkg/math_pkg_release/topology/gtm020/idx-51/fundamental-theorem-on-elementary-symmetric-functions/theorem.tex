Let $R$ be a commutative ring with $1$. The subring $R[\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_n]$ of $R[x_1, \ldots, x_n]$ generated by the elementary symmetric polynomials $\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_n$ contains all symmetric polynomials in $R[x_1, \ldots, x_n]$.

Moreover, every symmetric polynomial can be expressed uniquely as a polynomial in the elementary symmetric functions $\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_n$.
