From the fibre sequence $SO(n) \to O(n) \to \mathbf{Z}_2$, the homotopy exact sequence yields
$$0 = \pi_{i+1}(\mathbf{Z}_2) \to \pi_i(SO(n)) \to \pi_i(O(n)) \to \pi_i(\mathbf{Z}_2).$$
For $i \geq 1$, the term $\pi_i(\mathbf{Z}_2) = 0$ (since $\mathbf{Z}_2$ is discrete), and therefore the inclusion $SO(n) \hookrightarrow O(n)$ induces isomorphisms
$$\pi_i(SO(n)) \cong \pi_i(O(n))$$
for all $i \geq 1$.
