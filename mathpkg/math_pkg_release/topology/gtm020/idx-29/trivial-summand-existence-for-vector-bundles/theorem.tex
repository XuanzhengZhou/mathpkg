Let $X$ be an $n$-dimensional CW-complex and let $\xi^k$ be a $k$-dimensional $F$-vector bundle over $X$, where $F = \mathbf{R}, \mathbf{C},$ or $\mathbf{H}$. Let $c = \dim_{\mathbf{R}} F$. If $n \leq ck - 1$, then $\xi$ is isomorphic to $\eta \oplus \theta^1$ for some vector bundle $\eta$ of dimension $k-1$, where $\theta^1$ denotes the trivial line bundle.
