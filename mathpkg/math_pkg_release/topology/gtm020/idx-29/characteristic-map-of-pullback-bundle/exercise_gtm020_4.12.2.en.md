Let $f: B_1 \to B$ be a map, and let $\xi$ be a principal $G$-bundle over $S(B)$ (the suspension of $B$) with characteristic map $c_\xi: B \to G$. Prove that $c_\xi \circ f$ is a characteristic map of the pullback bundle $S(f)^*(\xi)$, where $S(f): S(B_1) \to S(B)$ is the suspension of $f$.
