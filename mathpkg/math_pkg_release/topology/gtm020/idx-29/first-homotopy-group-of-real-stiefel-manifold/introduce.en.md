This proposition determines the first non-vanishing homotopy group of the real Stiefel manifold $V_k(\mathbf{R}^n)$. The result distinguishes two cases: when $k=1$ (the sphere case, giving $\mathbf{Z}$) or when $n-k$ is even (also $\mathbf{Z}$), versus the case $k \geq 2$ with $n-k$ odd (giving $\mathbf{Z}_2$). The proof uses induction via the stability isomorphism from Proposition 11.1 together with direct computation for $k=1$.
