Let $M$ be an oriented $n$-manifold with orientation $\omega$. For each compact set $K \subset M$, let $\omega_K \in H_n(M, M - K)$ be the orientation class given by Theorem 4.4. For an open neighborhood $V$ of $K$, let $j: (V, V - K) \to (M, M - K)$ be the inclusion inducing the excision isomorphism $j_*: H_n(V, V - K) \xrightarrow{\cong} H_n(M, M - K)$. Define

$$\omega_K^V = j_*^{-1}(\omega_K) \in H_n(V, V - K).$$

Then the duality map

$$D_K^V: H^q(V) \to H_{n-q}(M, M - K)$$

is defined by

$$D_K^V(c) = j_*(c \cap \omega_K^V).$$
