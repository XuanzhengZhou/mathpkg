This corollary (11.5) follows from Theorem 11.4 and provides a condition on a complex $G$-module $M$ that is known to be either the complexification of a real representation or the restriction of a quaternionic one. The statement is truncated in the source, but in the context of the surrounding material it gives a further characterization involving the Adams operations or the $\psi^k$ operations on the representation ring.
