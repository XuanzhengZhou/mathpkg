Let $M$ and $N$ be $G$-modules over $F = \mathbf{R}$ or $\mathbf{C}$, where $G$ is a topological group.

(1) If $M$ and $N$ are isomorphic $G$-modules, then $\chi_M = \chi_N$.

(2) $\chi_{M \oplus N} = \chi_M + \chi_N$.

(3) $\chi_{M \otimes N} = \chi_M \cdot \chi_N$.

All properties follow from elementary properties of the trace $\operatorname{Tr}$.
