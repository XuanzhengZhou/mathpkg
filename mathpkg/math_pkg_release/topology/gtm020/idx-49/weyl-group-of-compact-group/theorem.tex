Let $T$ be a maximal torus of a compact group $G$. The normalizer of $T$ in $G$ is $N_T = \{s \in G : sTs^{-1} = T\}$, and $T$ is a normal subgroup of $N_T$. The Weyl group $W(G)$ of $G$ is the quotient group $W(G) = N_T / T$. The function $s \mapsto (t \mapsto sts^{-1})$ defines an action of $W(G)$ on $T$ by automorphisms.
