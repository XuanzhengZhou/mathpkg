The set of isomorphism classes of simple $G$-modules freely generates the abelian group $R_F(G)$. Equivalently, the map $[M] \mapsto \chi_M$ induces an isomorphism between $R_F(G)$ and the character ring $\operatorname{ch}_F G$, and the characters of non-isomorphic simple $G$-modules are linearly independent over $\mathbf{Z}$.
