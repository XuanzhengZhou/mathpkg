The Haar measure on a compact group $G$ is a normalized left-invariant positive linear functional on continuous functions. It is the fundamental tool for averaging constructions over compact groups, enabling the proof that every representation of a compact group admits an invariant hermitian inner product, which in turn implies complete reducibility. The extension to vector-valued functions allows averaging of matrix coefficients and other representation-theoretic data.
