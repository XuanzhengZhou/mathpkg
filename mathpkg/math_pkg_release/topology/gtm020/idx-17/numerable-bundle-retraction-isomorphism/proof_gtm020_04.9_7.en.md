This follows directly from Proposition 9.6, which establishes that the bundles $\xi$ and $r^*(\xi)$ are $(B \times I)$-isomorphic as principal $G$-bundles. The retraction $r: B \times I \to B \times \{1\}$ provides the homotopy equivalence, and the numerability condition ensures the applicability of the preceding results on local triviality and patching.
