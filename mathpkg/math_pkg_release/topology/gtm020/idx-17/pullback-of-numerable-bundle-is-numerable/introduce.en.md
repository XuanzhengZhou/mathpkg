Proposition 9.3 from Chapter 4 (General Fibre Bundles) of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. This proposition states that the pullback of a numerable bundle along any continuous map is again numerable. The proof constructs a numerable covering of $B'$ by pulling back the numerable covering of $B$ along $f$, and transfers the locally finite partition of unity by composition with $f$. This result is essential for establishing that the classification functor $k_G$ is well-defined on homotopy classes.
