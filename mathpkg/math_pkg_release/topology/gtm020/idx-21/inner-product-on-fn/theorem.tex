Let $F$ equal $\mathbf{R}$, $\mathbf{C}$, or $\mathbf{H}$, the quaternions of Hamilton. On the vector space $F^n$ we define the inner product
$$(x|y) = x_1 \bar{y}_1 + \cdots + x_n \bar{y}_n$$
and the norm
$$\|x\| = (x|x)^{1/2}$$
for $x = (x_1, \ldots, x_n)$, $y = (y_1, \ldots, y_n) \in F^n$, where $\bar{y}$ denotes conjugation in $F$ (for $x \in \mathbf{H}$, $x = x_0 + ix_1 + jx_2 + kx_3$, let $\bar{x} = x_0 - ix_1 - jx_2 - kx_3$).
