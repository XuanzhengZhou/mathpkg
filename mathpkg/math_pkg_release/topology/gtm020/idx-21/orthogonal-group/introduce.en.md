The orthogonal group $O(k)$ is the subgroup of the real general linear group $\mathbf{GL}(k, \mathbf{R})$ consisting of all $k \times k$ real matrices that preserve the standard Euclidean inner product on $\mathbf{R}^k$. Equivalently, $u \in O(k)$ if and only if $u^T u = I_k$. The group $O(k)$ is one of the three fundamental classical Lie groups, alongside $U(k)$ and $Sp(k)$.
