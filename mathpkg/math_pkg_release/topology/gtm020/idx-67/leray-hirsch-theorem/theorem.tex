Let $p: E \to B$ be a bundle which is of finite type (i.e., trivial over a finite covering). Let $E_{0}$ be an open subspace of $E$, and let $(F, F_{0})$ be an open pair of spaces such that for each $b \in B$ there is a homeomorphism
$$
j_{b}: (F, F_{0}) \xrightarrow{\cong} (p^{-1}(b), p^{-1}(b) \cap E_{0}) \subset (E, E_{0}).
$$
Let $a_{1}, \ldots, a_{r} \in H^{*}(E, E_{0})$ be homogeneous elements such that $\{j_{b}^{*}(a_{1}), \ldots, j_{b}^{*}(a_{r})\}$ is a $K$-basis of $H^{*}(F, F_{0})$ for each $b \in B$, where $K$ is a principal ring (e.g., $\mathbb{Z}$, $\mathbb{Z}_{p}$, or $\mathbb{Q}$). Then $H^{*}(E, E_{0})$ is a free $H^{*}(B)$-module with basis $a_{1}, \ldots, a_{r}$ under the action defined by
$$
p^{*}: H^{*}(B) \to H^{*}(E, E_{0}), \qquad (b, a_{i}) \mapsto p^{*}(b) \smile a_{i}.
$$
