This theorem establishes that any system of transition functions satisfying the cocycle condition comes from an actual principal bundle, and that such a bundle is unique up to isomorphism. The proof constructs the total space as a quotient of the disjoint union $\bigsqcup_i (V_i \times G)$ by the equivalence relation generated by the transition functions, with the projection defined as the natural map to $B$. This is the fundamental existence result underlying the local-to-global construction of fibre bundles.
