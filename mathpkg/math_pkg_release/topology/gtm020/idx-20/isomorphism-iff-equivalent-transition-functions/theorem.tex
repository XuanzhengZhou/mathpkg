Let $\eta$ and $\eta'$ be two fibre (or vector) bundles over $B$ with structure group $G$ and fibre $Y$. Let $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ be an atlas of $\eta$ with transition functions $\{g_{i,j}\}$, and let $\{(V_i, h_i')\}$ be an atlas of $\eta'$ with transition functions $\{g_{i,j}'\}$. Then $\eta$ and $\eta'$ are isomorphic over $B$ if and only if $\{g_{i,j}\}$ and $\{g_{i,j}'\}$ are equivalent systems of transition functions, i.e. there exist maps $r_i: V_i \to G$ such that

$$g_{i,j}'(b) = r_i(b)^{-1} g_{i,j}(b) r_j(b)$$

for each $b \in V_i \cap V_j$.
