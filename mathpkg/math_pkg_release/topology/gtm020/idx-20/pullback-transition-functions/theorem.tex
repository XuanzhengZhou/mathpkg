Let $\eta = \xi[Y]$ be a fibre bundle over $B$ with group $G$ and fibre $Y$, let $f: B_1 \to B$ be a map, and let $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ be an atlas for $\eta$ with transition functions $\{g_{i,j}\}$. Then the pullback bundle $f^*(\eta) = f^*(\xi)[Y]$ has an atlas

$$\{(f^{-1}(V_i), f^*(h_i))\}$$

with transition functions $\{g_{i,j} \circ f\}$, where the pullback chart morphism $f^*(h_i): \theta(f^{-1}(V_i)) \to f^*(\eta)|_{f^{-1}(V_i)}$ is given by

$$f^*(h_i)(b_1, y) = (b_1, h_i(f(b_1), y)).$$
