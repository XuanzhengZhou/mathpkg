This corollary extends Theorem 1.1 to associated fibre bundles. Given a left $G$-space $F$, the automorphisms of the associated trivial fibre bundle $\xi[F] = B \times F$ are obtained by quotienting the principal bundle automorphisms. The resulting automorphisms again take the form $h_g(b, y) = (b, g(b)y)$, with composition corresponding to pointwise multiplication of the maps $g, g': B \to G$.
