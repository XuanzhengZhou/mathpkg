For a vector bundle $\xi$, define a map $f: D(\xi) \rightarrow P(\xi \oplus \theta^1)$ by the relation that $f(x)$ is the line generated by $x + (1 - \|x\|^2)^{1/2}$ in the fibre $\xi_b \oplus \mathbf{R}$ for $x \in D(\xi)_b$. Prove that $f$ maps $S(\xi)$ into $P(\xi)$ and induces a homeomorphism $D(\xi)/S(\xi) \rightarrow P(\xi \oplus \theta^1)/P(\xi)$.
