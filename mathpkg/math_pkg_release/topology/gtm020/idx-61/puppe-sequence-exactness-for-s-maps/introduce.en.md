The Puppe sequence for a map $f: X \to Y$ induces long exact sequences in the $S$-category. For any test space $Z$, the contravariant sequence $\{X, Z\} \leftarrow \{Y, Z\} \leftarrow \{C_f, Z\} \leftarrow \{SX, Z\} \leftarrow \{SY, Z\}$ and the covariant sequence $\{Z, X\} \to \{Z, Y\} \to \{Z, C_f\} \to \{Z, SX\} \to \{Z, SY\}$ are both exact. This result is fundamental for computations in stable homotopy theory, as it extends the usual Puppe sequence exactness from ordinary homotopy classes to the stabilized $S$-maps.
