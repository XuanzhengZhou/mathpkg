This theorem establishes the injectivity part of the classification theorem for principal $G$-bundles: if two maps $f_0, f_1: X \to B_G$ into the classifying space pull back isomorphic $G$-bundles (i.e., $f_0^*(\omega_G) \cong f_1^*(\omega_G)$), then the maps themselves must be homotopic. Together with the surjectivity result, this implies a bijection between the set of homotopy classes $[X, B_G]$ and the set of isomorphism classes of principal $G$-bundles over $X$, establishing $B_G$ as the classifying space for $G$.
