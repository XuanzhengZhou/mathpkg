This proposition constructs two explicit homotopies of the universal $G$-bundle $\omega_G$ through $G$-bundle morphisms, each homotopy starting from the identity and ending at a map that sends the total space $E_G$ into designated odd (or even) parts, and similarly for the base space $B_G$. The key conclusion is that the universal bundle, its pullback under the odd-part map, and its pullback under the even-part map are all isomorphic. This structural decomposition is a technical tool used in the classification theory of principal $G$-bundles to verify that $\omega_G$ satisfies condition (2) of the classification setup (10.6).
