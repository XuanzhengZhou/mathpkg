For a space $X$ satisfying property $(S)$ -- that every vector bundle over $X$ has an inverse up to adding a trivial bundle -- the canonical map $\alpha$ from isomorphism classes of vector bundles to the $K$-theory ring $K_F(X)$ descends to a bijection on $s$-equivalence classes. This theorem is fundamental because it shows that $K_F(X)$ precisely classifies stable isomorphism classes of vector bundles, providing the geometric interpretation of $K$-theory as the Grothendieck group completion of the semiring of vector bundles.
