Let $\xi = (E, p, B)$ be an $n$-dimensional vector bundle with projective bundle $P\xi = (E', q, B)$. The induced bundle $q^*(\xi)$ over $E'$ has a canonical line bundle $\lambda_\xi$ as a subbundle. A point in the total space $E(\lambda_\xi)$ of $\lambda_\xi$ over a line $L \in E'$ is a pair $(L, x)$, where $q(L) = b = p(x)$, or equivalently $x \in L$. There is an exact sequence over $E'$:

$$0 \to \lambda_\xi \to q^*(\xi) \to \sigma_\xi \to 0$$

where $\sigma_\xi$ is the quotient of $q^*(\xi)$ by $\lambda_\xi$.
