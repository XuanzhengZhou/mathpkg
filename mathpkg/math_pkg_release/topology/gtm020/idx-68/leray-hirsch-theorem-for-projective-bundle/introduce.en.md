This theorem is the key cohomological result that makes the definition of Stiefel-Whitney and Chern classes possible. It asserts that the cohomology of the total space of the projective bundle $P\xi$ is a free module of rank $n$ over the cohomology ring of the base $B$, with basis given by the powers of the tautological class $a_\xi$. The injectivity of $q^*$ ensures that the characteristic classes $x_i(\xi)$ defined via the relation $a_\xi^n = -\sum x_i(\xi) a_\xi^{n-i}$ are uniquely determined elements of $H^*(B)$.
