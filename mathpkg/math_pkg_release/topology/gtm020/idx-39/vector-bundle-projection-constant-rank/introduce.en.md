This remark observes that the projection endomorphisms $p_0$ and $p_\infty$ arising from the analysis of a polynomial clutching map are bundle morphisms of constant rank. This is a general property of projections on vector bundles and can be seen by considering local cross sections that form a basis for the kernel and image at a point, which extend continuously to nearby fibres. The constancy of rank ensures that the subbundles $\zeta_+^0$, $\zeta_-^0$, $\zeta_+^\infty$, and $\zeta_-^\infty$ introduced in Notation 4.4 are well-defined vector bundles over $X$.
