Let $\zeta$ be a vector bundle over a space $X$, and let $p_0, p_\infty: \zeta \to \zeta$ be the bundle projections derived from a polynomial clutching map $p(x,z) = a(x)z + b(x)$ as in Proposition 4.2. Then $p_0$ and $p_\infty$ are of constant rank. That is, the dimensions of $\operatorname{im}(p_0)_x$ and $\ker(p_0)_x$ (respectively $\operatorname{im}(p_\infty)_x$ and $\ker(p_\infty)_x$) are independent of $x \in X$.
