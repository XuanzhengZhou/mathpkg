The second statement follows by observing that the dual of the composite isomorphism of (1.1) defined $\bar{H}^*(\Omega S(X)) \rightarrow H^*(\Omega S(X)) \otimes \bar{H}^*(X)$ is an isomorphism preserving the Steenrod algebra.
