By (1.3), $H_*(\Omega S(X)) = TH_*(X)$ over any field, where $T$ denotes the tensor algebra. Since $\bar{H}_*(\bigwedge_n X) = \bar{H}_*(X)^{\otimes n}$ over a field, we see that

$$H_*(v_n): S\bar{H}_*(X)^{\otimes n} = \bar{H}_*(S(\bigwedge_n X)) \rightarrow \bar{H}_*(S\Omega S(X))$$

maps isomorphically onto the suspension of the $n$th summand in $\bar{H}_*(\Omega S(X)) = \bigoplus_{n \geq 1} \bar{H}_*(X)^{\otimes n}$. Therefore $v$ induces an isomorphism on homology with coefficients in any field, and hence $v$ is a weak homotopy equivalence for path-connected $X$.
