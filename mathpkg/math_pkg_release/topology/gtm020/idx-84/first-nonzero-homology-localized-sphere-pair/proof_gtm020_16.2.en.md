We make the calculation in cohomology over $\mathbf{Z}_{(p)}$. The total space spectral sequence for the path space fibration over $\Omega S^{2n+1}$ has

$$E_2^{*,*} = S'(x, 2n) \otimes H^*(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1})$$

and $E_\infty^{*,*} = \mathbf{Z}_{(p)}$. Thus $H^{2n-1}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}) = \mathbf{Z}_{(p)}y$, $E_2^{*,*} = E_{2n}^{*,*}$, and $d_{2n}(y) = x$. Hence

$$d_{2n}(y\gamma_m(x)) = (m + 1)\gamma_{m+1}(x)$$

and $E_{2n+1}^{*,*} = 0$ for $(a, b) \neq (0, 0)$ and $a < 2np$ or $b < 2np - 1$. Since $d_{2n}(y\gamma_{p-1}(x)) = p\gamma_p(x)$, the differential kills the $p$-torsion, and the first nonzero homology group of the pair appears in degree $2np-2$ with value $\mathbf{Z}/p\mathbf{Z}$.
