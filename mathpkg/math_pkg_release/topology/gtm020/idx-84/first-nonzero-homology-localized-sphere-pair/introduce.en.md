This proposition identifies the first degree in which the relative homology of the localized pair $(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}, S^{2n-1})$ is nontrivial, and computes that homology group to be $\mathbf{Z}/p\mathbf{Z}$ in degree $2np-2$. This result is a key computational input for understanding the connectivity of the localized pair and for refining the double suspension theorem. It is derived from the mod-$p$ Hopf algebra structure of $H_*(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1})$ via the Serre spectral sequence of the path space fibration.
