The Thom isomorphism theorem is a fundamental result in the topology of vector bundles. It asserts the existence of a unique Thom class $U_\xi$ in the cohomology of the pair $(E, E_0)$ that restricts to a generator on each fibre, and establishes that multiplying by $U_\xi$ gives an isomorphism $H^i(B) \cong H^{i+n}(E, E_0)$. This isomorphism is the key to relating the cohomology of the base space to that of the Thom space.
