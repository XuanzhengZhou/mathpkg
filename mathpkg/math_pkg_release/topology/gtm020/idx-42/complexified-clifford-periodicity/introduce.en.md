This corollary provides the 2-fold periodicity for complexified Clifford algebras. Denoting by $C_k^c = C_k \otimes_{\mathbf{R}} \mathbf{C}$ the complexification of the real Clifford algebra $C_k$, it asserts that $C_{k+2}^c$ is isomorphic to the tensor product $C_k^c \otimes_{\mathbf{R}} \mathbf{C}(2)$ of the complexified Clifford algebra with the $2 \times 2$ complex matrix algebra. This follows by tensoring the isomorphism $C_{k+2} \cong C'_k \otimes C_2$ with $\mathbf{C}$.
