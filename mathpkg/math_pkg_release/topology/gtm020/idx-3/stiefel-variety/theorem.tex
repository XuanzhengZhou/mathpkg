The \textbf{Stiefel variety} of (orthonormal) $k$-frames in $\mathbf{R}^n$, denoted $V_k(\mathbf{R}^n)$, is the subspace of $(v_1, \ldots, v_k) \in (S^{n-1})^k$ such that $(v_i \mid v_j) = \delta_{i,j}$ for $1 \leq i, j \leq k$. Since $V_k(\mathbf{R}^n)$ is a closed subset of a compact space, it is a compact space.
