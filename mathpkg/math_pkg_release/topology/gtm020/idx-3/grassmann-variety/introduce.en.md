The Grassmann variety $G_k(\mathbf{R}^n)$ parametrizes all $k$-dimensional linear subspaces of $\mathbf{R}^n$. It is topologized as a quotient of the Stiefel variety $V_k(\mathbf{R}^n)$ via the map that sends an orthonormal $k$-frame to the subspace it spans. Grassmann varieties serve as classifying spaces for vector bundles.
