Every cross section $s$ of a product bundle $(B \times F, p, B)$ has the form $s(b) = (b, f(b))$, where $f: B \rightarrow F$ is a map uniquely defined by $s$. The function that assigns to each cross section $s$ of the product bundle $(B \times F, p, B)$ the map $\operatorname{pr}_2 \circ s: B \rightarrow F$ is a bijection from the set of all cross sections of $(B \times F, p, B)$ to the set of maps $B \rightarrow F$.
