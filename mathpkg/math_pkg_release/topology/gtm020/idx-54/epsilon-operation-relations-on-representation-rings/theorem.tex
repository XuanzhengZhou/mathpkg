Between $R(G)$ and $RO(G)$ the relations $\varepsilon_0 \varepsilon_U = 2$ and $\varepsilon_U \varepsilon_0 = 1 + \psi_{-1}$ hold, where $\psi_{-1}$ is the $\psi$ operation in the $\lambda$-ring $R(G)$ and refers to the conjugate representation of bundles. Between $R(G)$ and $RSp(G)$ the relations $\varepsilon_{Sp} \varepsilon_U = 2$ and $\varepsilon_U \varepsilon_{Sp} = 1 + \psi_{-1}$ hold, where $\psi_{-1} x$ denotes the conjugate class of $x$.
