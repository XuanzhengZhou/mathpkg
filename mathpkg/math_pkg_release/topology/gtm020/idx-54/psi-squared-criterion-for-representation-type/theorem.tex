Let $M$ be a simple complex $G$-module. Then $\psi^2(M) = M \otimes M - 2\lambda^2(M)$ contains the trivial representation $1$ with scalar multiple $+1$ if $M$ is the complexification of a real $G$-module, with scalar multiple $-1$ if $M$ is the restriction of a quaternionic $G$-module, and with scalar multiple $0$ if $M$ and its conjugate $M^*$ are nonisomorphic.
