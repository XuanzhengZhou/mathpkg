Define the bundle dimension of a space $B$, denoted $\dim_b B$, to be the inf of all $k$ such that the inclusion $G_m(\mathbf{R}^{2k}) \rightarrow G_m(\mathbf{R}^{\infty})$ induces a bijection $[B, G_m(\mathbf{R}^{2k})] \rightarrow [B, G_m(\mathbf{R}^{\infty})]$. Prove that $\dim_b B \leq \dim B$ for a CW-complex $B$.
