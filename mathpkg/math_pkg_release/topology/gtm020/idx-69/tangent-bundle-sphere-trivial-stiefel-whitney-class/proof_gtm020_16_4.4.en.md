Since $\tau(S^n) \oplus \theta^1$ and $\theta^{n+1}$ are isomorphic (by the fact that the normal bundle of $S^n \subset \mathbb{R}^{n+1}$ is trivial), the tangent bundle $\tau(S^n)$ is $s$-trivial. Therefore, by the stability of characteristic classes under $s$-equivalence and the fact that $w(\theta^{n+1}) = 1$, we obtain $w(\tau(S^n)) = 1$.
