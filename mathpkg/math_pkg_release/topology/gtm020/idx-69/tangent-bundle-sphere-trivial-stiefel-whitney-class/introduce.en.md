The tangent bundle of the $n$-sphere is stably trivial: adding a trivial line bundle yields the trivial bundle of rank $n+1$. Since stably equivalent bundles have equal Stiefel-Whitney classes and trivial bundles have total Stiefel-Whitney class $1$, the tangent bundle of the sphere has no non-trivial Stiefel-Whitney classes. This is one of the classical computations in characteristic class theory.
