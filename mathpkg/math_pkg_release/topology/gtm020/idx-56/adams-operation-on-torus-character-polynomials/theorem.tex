Let $T^r$ be a maximal torus of a compact group $G$, and consider $RG$ as a subring of $RT^r = \mathbf{Z}[\beta_1, \beta_1^{-1}, \ldots, \beta_r, \beta_r^{-1}]$. If an element $a \in RG$ is of the form $a = P(\beta_1, \beta_1^{-1}, \ldots, \beta_r, \beta_r^{-1})$, where $P$ is a polynomial, then $\psi^k(a) = P(\beta_1^k, \beta_1^{-k}, \ldots, \beta_r^k, \beta_r^{-k})$.
