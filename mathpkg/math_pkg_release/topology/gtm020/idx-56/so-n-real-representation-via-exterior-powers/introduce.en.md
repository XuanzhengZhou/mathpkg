The standard action of the special orthogonal group $SO(n)$ on $\mathbf{R}^n$ yields a real representation, and its exterior powers $\Lambda^i \mathbf{R}^n$ also carry natural real $SO(n)$-module structures. Under complexification, these real representations correspond to the standard complex representation $\rho_n$ of $SO(n)$ and its exterior powers $\lambda^i \rho_n$, providing fundamental examples in the real representation ring $RO(SO(n))$.
