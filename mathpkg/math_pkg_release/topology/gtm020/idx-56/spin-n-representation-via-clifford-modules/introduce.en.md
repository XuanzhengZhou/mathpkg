The spin group $\text{Spin}(n)$ sits inside the even Clifford algebra $C_n^0$, which is isomorphic to the full Clifford algebra $C_{n-1}$. Consequently, the simple modules over the Clifford algebra $C_{n-1}$ completely determine the simple $\text{Spin}(n)$-modules, providing a concrete algebraic description of all irreducible spin representations.
