An isomorphism $u \colon \tau(S^n) \oplus \nu(S^n) \rightarrow \theta^{n+1}$ is defined by the relation
$$u((b, x), (b, x')) = (b, x + x')$$
for $(b, x) \in E(\tau(S^n))$ and $(b, x') \in E(\nu(S^n))$. The inverse of $u$ is the $B$-morphism $v$ defined by the relation
$$v(b, x) = ((b, v_b(x)), (b, \pi_b(x) b)).$$
