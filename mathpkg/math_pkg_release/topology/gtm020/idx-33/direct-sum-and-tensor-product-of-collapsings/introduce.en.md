This proposition shows that the collapsing construction is compatible with the two fundamental operations on vector bundles: direct sum and tensor product. Collapsing a direct sum (or tensor product) of bundles yields the same result (up to isomorphism) as taking the direct sum (or tensor product) of the individual collapsings. This compatibility is essential for the collapsing construction to induce a well-defined ring structure on relative K-theory.
