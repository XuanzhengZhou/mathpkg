Define the bundle dimension of a space $B$, denoted $\dim_b B$, to be the infimum of all $k$ such that the inclusion $G_m(\mathbf{R}^{2k}) \rightarrow G_m(\mathbf{R}^{\infty})$ induces a bijection $[B, G_m(\mathbf{R}^{2k})] \rightarrow [B, G_m(\mathbf{R}^{\infty})]$.
