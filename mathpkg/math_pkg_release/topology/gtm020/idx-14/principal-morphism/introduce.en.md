Definition 3.1 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. A principal morphism is a bundle morphism between principal $G$-bundles whose total space map is a $G$-morphism. This defines the category $\operatorname{Bun}(G)$ of principal $G$-bundles and the fibered category $\operatorname{Bun}_B(G)$ over a fixed base $B$, with natural forgetful functors to the categories of bundles.
