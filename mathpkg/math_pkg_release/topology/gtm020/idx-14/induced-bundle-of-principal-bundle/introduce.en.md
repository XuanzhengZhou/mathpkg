Proposition 4.1 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. This result establishes that the property of being a principal $G$-bundle is stable under pullback (induced bundle construction). The induced bundle inherits a unique $G$-space structure making the canonical map $f_\xi$ a $G$-morphism, and if the original bundle is principal, so is the induced bundle.
