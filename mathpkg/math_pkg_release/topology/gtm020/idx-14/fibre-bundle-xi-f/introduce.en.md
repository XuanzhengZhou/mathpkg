Definition 5.1 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. This is the central construction of the chapter: given a principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ and a left $G$-space $F$, the associated fibre bundle $\xi[F]$ is formed by the mixed quotient $(X \times F)/G$ where $G$ acts diagonally. The resulting space has fibre $F$ and structure group $G$. This construction unifies vector bundles, sphere bundles, and all other bundles with a given structure group under a single framework.
