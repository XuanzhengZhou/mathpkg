Theorem 3.2 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. This fundamental result states that in the category $\operatorname{Bun}_B(G)$ of principal $G$-bundles over a fixed base $B$, every morphism is automatically an isomorphism. This is the analogue for principal bundles of the corresponding result for vector bundles (Theorem 3.2.5 in Chapter 3) and plays a crucial role in the homotopy classification of principal bundles.
