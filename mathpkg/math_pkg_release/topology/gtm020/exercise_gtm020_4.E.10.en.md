Let $\xi_i = (X_i, p_i, B_i)$ be a principal $G_i$-bundle, $i = 1, 2$. Then $\xi_1 \times \xi_2 = (X_1 \times X_2, p_1 \times p_2, B_1 \times B_2)$ has the structure of a principal $G_1 \times G_2$-bundle. If $\xi_i$ is a universal $G_i$-bundle, $i = 1, 2$, then $\xi_1 \times \xi_2$ is a universal $G_1 \times G_2$-bundle. Apply this theorem to prove the existence of a universal bundle with a CW-complex as classifying space for each finitely generated abelian group (finite direct sum of cyclic groups).
