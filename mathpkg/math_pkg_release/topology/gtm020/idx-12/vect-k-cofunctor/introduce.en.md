The cofunctor $\operatorname{Vect}_k$ assigns to each paracompact space $B$ the set of isomorphism classes of $k$-dimensional vector bundles over $B$. For a continuous map $f: B_1 \to B$, the induced map $\operatorname{Vect}_k(f)$ sends a bundle class $\{\xi\}$ to the class of the pullback bundle $\{f^*(\xi)\}$. This makes $\operatorname{Vect}_k$ a contravariant functor from the homotopy category of paracompact spaces to the category of sets. The central result of the homotopy classification of vector bundles is that $\operatorname{Vect}_k$ is corepresentable by the infinite Grassmannian $G_k(F^\infty)$.
