This proposition characterizes coreducibility of CW-complexes in terms of the bottom cell splitting off, dual to the reducibility characterization of Proposition 7.4. The full statement was truncated in the OCR source, but the surrounding discussion (Remark 7.6) indicates it states that a CW-complex $X$ is coreducible if and only if there exists a suitable map $g: X \to S^n$ splitting the bottom cell, analogous to the reducibility condition involving the top cell projection.
