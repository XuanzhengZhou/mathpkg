Let $\xi$ be a vector bundle over a connected finite CW-complex $X$. Then the following statements are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $J(\xi) = 0$ in $J(X)$
\item[(2)] The space $T(\xi)$ is $S$-coreducible.
\item[(3)] The spaces $T(\xi)$ and $T(0) = X^+$ have the same $S$-type.
\end{enumerate}
Recall that $X^+$ is the space $X$ plus an isolated discrete base point.
