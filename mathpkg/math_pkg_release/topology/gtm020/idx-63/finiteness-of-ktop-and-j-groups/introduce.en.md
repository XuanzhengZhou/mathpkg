This theorem states that for any finite CW-complex $X$, both the topological K-theory group $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X)$ and the image of the J-homomorphism $J(X)$ are finite groups. The result is proved by induction on the number of cells using the Puppe sequence, leveraging the half-exactness of $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}$ and the finiteness of $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(S^m)$ for each sphere $S^m$. Since $J(X)$ is a subgroup of $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X)$, its finiteness follows immediately.
