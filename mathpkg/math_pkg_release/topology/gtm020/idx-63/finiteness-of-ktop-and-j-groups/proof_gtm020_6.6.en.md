Since $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X)$ is a half-exact cofunctor, and since the $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(S^m)$ are finite groups, we prove by induction on the number of cells, using the Puppe sequence, that $\tilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X)$ is finite. Then $J(X)$ is finite because it is the subgroup of a finite group.
