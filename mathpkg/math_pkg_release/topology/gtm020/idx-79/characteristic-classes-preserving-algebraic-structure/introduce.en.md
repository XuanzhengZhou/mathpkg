When studying characteristic classes of vector bundles, it is useful to classify them by the algebraic structure they preserve. The notation $[\text{Vect}_F, H]_\mu$ denotes the set of characteristic classes from the category of vector bundles to a ring-valued cofunctor $H$ that are compatible with direct sum. The parameter $\mu$ specifies whether the class is additive $(\mu=(+,+))$, multiplicative $(\mu=(+,\times))$, or a semiring morphism $(\mu = r)$.
