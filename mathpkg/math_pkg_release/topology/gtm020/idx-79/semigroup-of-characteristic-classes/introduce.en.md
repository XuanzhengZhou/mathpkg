The natural morphism from the category of vector bundles to K-theory induces an injection at the level of characteristic classes, meaning that any characteristic class defined on K-theory uniquely extends to one on vector bundles. Moreover, the sets of additive and multiplicative characteristic classes carry natural semigroup structures via pointwise addition and multiplication respectively, reflecting the algebraic operations in the target cohomology ring $H$.
