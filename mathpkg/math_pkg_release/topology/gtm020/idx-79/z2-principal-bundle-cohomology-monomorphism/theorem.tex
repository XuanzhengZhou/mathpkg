Let $q: E \rightarrow B$ be a locally trivial principal $Z_2$-bundle, let $R$ be a 2-divisible ring, and let $\tau$ denote the involution of $H^*(E, R)$ induced by the action of $-1 \in Z_2$ on $E$. Then $q^*: H^*(B, R) \rightarrow H^*(E, R)$ is a monomorphism, and the image of $q^*$ is the fixed elements of $\tau$.
