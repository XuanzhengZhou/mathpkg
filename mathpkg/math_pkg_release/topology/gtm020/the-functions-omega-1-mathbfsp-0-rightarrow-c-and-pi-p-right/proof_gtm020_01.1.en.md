If $f: X \rightarrow Y$ is a map, then $\omega(f(x)) = \langle f(x), 1 \rangle = C(f)\omega(x)$ for each $x \in X$, and $f\pi(u) = fu(1) = \pi(P(f)u)$ for each $u \in P(X)$.
