For each $x \in F^n$ and $(v_1, \ldots, v_k)$, $(v'_1, \ldots, v'_k) \in V_k(F^n)$ with $(v_1, \ldots, v_k) a = (v'_1, \ldots, v'_k)$ for some $a = (a_{ij}) \in U_F(k)$, the following identity holds:

$$\sum_{1 \leq i \leq k} \langle x \mid v_i \rangle v_i = \sum_{1 \leq i \leq k} \langle x \mid v'_i \rangle v'_i$$

That is, the orthogonal projection of $x$ onto the subspace spanned by the frame is independent of the choice of orthonormal basis for that subspace.
