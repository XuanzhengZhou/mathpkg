The Stiefel manifold $V_k(F^n)$ of orthonormal $k$-frames in $F^n$ admits the structure of a principal fibre bundle over the Grassmannian $G_k(F^n)$ with projection $p$ sending each frame to its linear span. The structure group is $U_F(k)$, acting freely on the right by change of basis. An analogous statement holds for the oriented Grassmannian $SG_k(F^n)$ with structure group $SU_F(k)$. These are fundamental examples of principal bundles arising from homogeneous spaces of compact Lie groups.
