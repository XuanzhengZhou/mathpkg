The bundle $(V_k(F^n), p, G_k(F^n))$ is a principal $U_F(k)$-bundle, and the bundle $(V_k(F^n), p, SG_k(F^n))$ is a principal $SU_F(k)$-bundle, where

$$p: V_k(F^n) \rightarrow G_k(F^n)$$

maps a $k$-frame to the $k$-dimensional subspace it spans, and

$$p: V_k(F^n) \rightarrow SG_k(F^n)$$

maps a $k$-frame to the oriented $k$-dimensional subspace determined by the ordering of the frame.
