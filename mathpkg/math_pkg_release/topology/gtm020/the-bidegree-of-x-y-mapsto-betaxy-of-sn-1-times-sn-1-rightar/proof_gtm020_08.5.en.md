Since $\beta(x)y = -\alpha(x)y$, the degree of $x \mapsto -x$ which is $(-1)^n$ multiplied by the bidegree of $(x, y) \mapsto \alpha(x)y$ yields the bidegree of $\beta(x)y$.

The following theorem is very useful in making calculations of homotopy groups.
