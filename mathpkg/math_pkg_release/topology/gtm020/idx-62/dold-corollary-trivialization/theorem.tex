Let $p: E \rightarrow B$ be a locally trivial bundle over a finite CW-complex $B$, and let $u: E \rightarrow Y$ be a map such that the restriction $u|_{E_b}: E_b \rightarrow Y$ is a homotopy equivalence for all $b \in B$. Then $p: E \rightarrow B$ is fibre homotopically equivalent to the trivial bundle $B \times Y \rightarrow B$.
