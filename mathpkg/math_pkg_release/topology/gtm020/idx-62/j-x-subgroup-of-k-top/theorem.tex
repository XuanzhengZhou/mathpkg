Let $X$ be a finite CW-complex. The group $J(X)$ is a subgroup of $\widetilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X)$, which is constructed as follows: Let $F_n(X)$ be the set of fibre homotopy classes of fibrations with fibre $S^{n-1}$. The transition functions take values in the semigroup $H(n)$ of homotopy equivalences $S^{n-1} \to S^{n-1}$. Then $F_n(X) \cong [X, B_{H(n)}]$ where $B_{H(n)}$ is the classifying space for $H(n)$. Define $B_H = \varinjlim_n B_{H(n)}$, which admits a natural $H$-space structure. There is an $H$-space map $B_O \to B_H$ induced by the inclusion $O(n) \hookrightarrow H(n)$, and $\widetilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X) = [X, B_H]$.
