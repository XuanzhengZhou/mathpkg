Let $X$ be a space over which each real vector bundle is of finite type. The direct sum of vector bundles induces on $J(X)$ the structure of an abelian group, and the quotient function $\widetilde{KO}(X) \rightarrow J(X)$ is a group homomorphism, where $\widetilde{KO}(X)$ is viewed as the group of $s$-equivalence classes of vector bundles over $X$.
