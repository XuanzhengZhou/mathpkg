Let $\xi$ and $\eta$ be two vector bundles over $X$. The associated sphere bundles $S(\xi)$ and $S(\eta)$ are \textbf{stable fibre homotopically equivalent} if $S(\xi \oplus \theta^n)$ and $S(\eta \oplus \theta^m)$ have the same fibre homotopy type for some $n$ and $m$, where $\theta^n$ denotes the trivial $n$-dimensional vector bundle.
