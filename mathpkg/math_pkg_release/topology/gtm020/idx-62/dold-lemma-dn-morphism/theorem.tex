Let $Y$ and $Y'$ be two locally compact spaces. Let $f: D^n \times Y \rightarrow D^n \times Y'$ be a $D^n$-morphism of the bundles $D^n \times Y \rightarrow D^n$ and $D^n \times Y' \rightarrow D^n$ satisfying:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] The map $f|_{\{x\} \times Y}: \{x\} \times Y \rightarrow \{x\} \times Y'$ is a homotopy equivalence for each $x \in D^n$.
\item[(2)] There exists a map $g: D^n \times Y' \rightarrow D^n \times Y$ such that $g$ is also a $D^n$-morphism and the compositions $gf$ and $fg$ are fibre homotopic over $D^n$ to the respective identities.
\end{enumerate}
Then $f$ is a fibre homotopy equivalence.
