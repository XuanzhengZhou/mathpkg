The proof is a reference to Dold [1] and is outlined in the text. The key idea is that a $D^n$-morphism with fibre-wise homotopy equivalences can be used to construct a fibre homotopy equivalence by gluing local inverses over a cover of $D^n$. The complete argument appears in Dold's original work on fibre homotopy equivalence.
