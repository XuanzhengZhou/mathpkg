This corollary follows directly from Proposition 11.1. If a complex vector bundle is isomorphic to its conjugate, then its odd Chern classes $c_{2i+1}(\xi)$ satisfy $c_{2i+1}(\xi) = -c_{2i+1}(\xi)$, hence $2c_{2i+1}(\xi) = 0$. This gives a necessary condition for a complex vector bundle to be self-conjugate.
