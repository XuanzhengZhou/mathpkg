For a vector bundle $\xi$, define a map $f: D(\xi) \to P(\xi \oplus \theta^1)$ by the relation that $f(x)$ is the line generated by $x + (1 - |x|^2)^{1/2} \cdot 1$ in the fibre over $p(x)$. Prove that $f$ induces a homeomorphism $T(\xi) \to P(\xi \oplus \theta^1)$.
