A submanifold is a subspace of a manifold that locally looks like a linear subspace in the coordinate charts. For each point $x \in X$, there exists a chart of the ambient manifold $M$ in which $X$ corresponds to the intersection of a linear subspace $(0 \times \mathbb{R}^q)$ with the half-space model $H^n$. The restricted charts then give $X$ the structure of a $C^r$-manifold.
