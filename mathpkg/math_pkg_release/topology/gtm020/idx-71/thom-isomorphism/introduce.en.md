The Thom isomorphism is a fundamental result in the topology of vector bundles. It establishes an isomorphism between the cohomology of the base space $B$ of a vector bundle $\xi$ and the cohomology of the associated Thom space $(E, E_0)$, shifted by the dimension of the bundle. The isomorphism is given by pulling back a cohomology class from the base and taking the cup product with the Thom class $U_\xi$.
