The tangent bundle $\tau(M^n)$ is an $n$-dimensional vector bundle naturally associated to every $n$-dimensional $C^r$-manifold $M$. It is defined using the transition functions given by the derivatives of the coordinate changes between charts of the manifold's atlas. The chain rule ensures the cocycle condition for these transition functions. The tangent bundle is a functor from $C^r$-manifolds to vector bundles, and a vector field on $M$ is a cross section of $\tau(M)$.
