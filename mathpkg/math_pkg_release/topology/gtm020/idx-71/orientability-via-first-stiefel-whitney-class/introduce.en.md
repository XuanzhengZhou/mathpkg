This theorem characterizes orientability of real vector bundles in terms of the first Stiefel-Whitney class. A vector bundle is orientable precisely when $w_1(\xi) = 0$. The proof uses the fact that $\xi$ is orientable iff its determinant line bundle $\Lambda^n \xi$ is trivial, and that $w_1(\Lambda^n \xi) = w_1(\xi)$ by the Whitney sum formula.
