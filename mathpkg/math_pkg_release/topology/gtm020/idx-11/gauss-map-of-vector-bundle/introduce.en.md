A Gauss map of a vector bundle $\xi^k$ in $F^m$ is a map from the total space $E(\xi^k)$ to $F^m$ that restricts to a linear monomorphism on each fibre. The canonical example is the projection $q: E(\gamma_k^m) \to F^m$ defined by $q(V,x)=x$ on the tautological bundle. Every Gauss map can be constructed from the canonical Gauss map on the universal bundle $\gamma_k^m$ together with an appropriate vector bundle morphism.
