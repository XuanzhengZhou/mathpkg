There is a relation $\tilde{K}(S^{2n+1}) = 0$, and $\tilde{K}(S^{2n})$ is infinite cyclic with generator $\beta_{2n}$, where the ring structure is given by $\beta_{2n}^2 = 0$. Moreover, the natural map $S^2 \times \dots \times S^2 \to S^{2n}$ induces a monomorphism $\tilde{K}(S^{2n}) \to \tilde{K}(S^2 \times \dots \times S^2)$ and the image of $\beta_{2n}$ in $\tilde{K}(S^2 \times \dots \times S^2)$ equals a product $a_1 \cdots a_n$, where $a_i = \{ \zeta_i \} - 1$ such that $\zeta_i$ is a line bundle on $S^2 \times \dots \times S^2$.
