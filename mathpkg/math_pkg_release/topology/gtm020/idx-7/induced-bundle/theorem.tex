Let $\xi = (E, p, B)$ be a bundle, and let $f: B_1 \to B$ be a map. The \textbf{induced bundle} of $\xi$ under $f$, denoted $f^*(\xi)$, has as base space $B_1$, as total space
$$E_1 = \{(b_1, x) \in B_1 \times E \mid f(b_1) = p(x)\},$$
and as projection $p_1: E_1 \to B_1$ the map $(b_1, x) \mapsto b_1$.
