This corollary relates the restriction and pullback operations for a map of pairs $f: (B_1, A_1) \to (B, A)$. It states that restricting the induced bundle $f^*\xi$ to the subspace $A_1$ yields a bundle $A_1$-isomorphic to the pullback of the restricted bundle $\xi|A$ along the restricted map $g = f|_{A_1}$. In other words, restriction commutes with pullback in the natural way.
