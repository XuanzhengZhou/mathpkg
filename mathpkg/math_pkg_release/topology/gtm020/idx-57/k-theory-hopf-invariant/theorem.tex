Let $f: S^{2n-1} \rightarrow S^n$ be a map, and form the mapping sequence
$$S^{2n-1} \rightarrow S^n \rightarrow C_f \rightarrow S S^{2n-1} \xrightarrow{S_f} S S^n$$
with the exact sequence of $K$-groups
$$0 \leftarrow \tilde{K}(S^n) \xleftarrow{\phi} \tilde{K}(C_f) \xleftarrow{\psi} \tilde{K}(S^{2n}).$$
Let $b_f = b(f) = \psi(\beta_{2n}) \in \tilde{K}(C_f)$, and let $a_f = a(f)$ denote any element of $\tilde{K}(C_f)$ such that $\phi(a_f) = \beta_n$ for $n$ even and $a_f = 0$ for $n$ odd. The Hopf invariant of $f$ is the integer $h_f$ such that
$$a_f^2 = h_f b_f \quad \text{in} \quad \tilde{K}(C_f).$$
