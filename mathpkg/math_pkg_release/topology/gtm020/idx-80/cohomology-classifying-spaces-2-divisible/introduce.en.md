With 2-divisible coefficient rings $R$, the cohomology rings of the key classifying spaces simplify considerably. The cohomology of $CP^\infty$ is $R[c_1]$ (the Euler class of the canonical bundle), $G_2^+(R^\infty)$ (oriented 2-plane Grassmannian) has cohomology $R[e]$ generated by the Euler class, and $G_2(R^\infty)$ has cohomology $R[p_1]$ generated by the first Pontryagin class. The commutative diagram describes the relationships among these classes and the Chern classes of $G_2(C^\infty)$, reflecting the standard inclusions $SO(2) \hookrightarrow U(2)$ and $U(1) \times U(1) \hookrightarrow SO(2)$.
