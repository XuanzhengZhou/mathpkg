The injection $[K_F, H]_\mu \rightarrow [\text{Vect}_F, H]_\mu$ follows from the fact that every $K$-theory class comes from a vector bundle, and the natural morphism $\text{Vect}_F \rightarrow K_F$ is surjective on objects. The semigroup structures are verified directly: for $\mu = (+,+)$, the operation $(\phi + \psi)(\xi) = \phi(\xi) + \psi(\xi)$ is well-defined and associative because addition in the cohomology ring $H$ is associative. Similarly, for $\mu = (+,\times)$, the product $(\phi\psi)(\xi) = \phi(\xi)\psi(\xi)$ is associative because multiplication in $H$ is associative.
