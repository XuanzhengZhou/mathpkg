The natural morphism $\text{Vect}_F \rightarrow K_F$ defines an injection $[K_F, H]_\mu \rightarrow [\text{Vect}_F, H]_\mu$. In the two sets $[\text{Vect}_F, H]_{+,+}$ and $[\text{Vect}_F, H]_{+,\times}$ there is a natural semigroup structure, where $(\phi + \psi)(\xi) = \phi(\xi) + \psi(\xi)$ in the first case and $(\phi\psi)(\xi) = \phi(\xi)\psi(\xi)$ in the second case.
