The map $h_n$ from the $n$-fold product of $CP^\infty$ to the complex Grassmannian $G_n(C^\infty)$ is a splitting map for the universal $n$-dimensional vector bundle $\gamma_n$. This means that the pullback $h_n^*(\gamma_n)$ splits as a direct sum of line bundles, specifically the $n$-fold product of the canonical line bundle $\gamma$ over $CP^\infty$. The proposition furthermore implies that $h_n^*$ induces a monomorphism on cohomology, a crucial step in establishing the Chern class description of the cohomology ring of the Grassmannian.
