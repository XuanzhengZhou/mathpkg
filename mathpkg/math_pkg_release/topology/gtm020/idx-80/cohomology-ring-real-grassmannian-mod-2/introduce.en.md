The mod 2 cohomology ring of the infinite real Grassmannian $G_n(R^\infty)$ is a polynomial ring over $\mathbb{Z}_2$ freely generated by the universal Stiefel-Whitney classes $w_1, \dots, w_n$. Here $w_i = w_i(\gamma_n)$ is the $i$-th Stiefel-Whitney class of the universal $n$-dimensional real vector bundle $\gamma_n$. This identifies $G_n(R^\infty)$ as the classifying space $BO(n)$ and is the real analogue of the corresponding result for Chern classes and $BU(n)$.
