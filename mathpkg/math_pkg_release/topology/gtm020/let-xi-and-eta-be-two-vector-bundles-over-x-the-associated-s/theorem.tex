Clearly, stable fibre homotopy equivalence is an equivalence relation. We denote by $J(\xi)$ the stable fibre homotopy class determined by $\xi$ and by $J(X)$ the set of all stable fibre homotopy classes of vector bundles on $X$. If every bundle is of finite type on $X$, there is a natural quotient surjection

$$\tilde{KO}(X) \rightarrow J(X)$$

where $\tilde{KO}(X)$ is viewed as the group of $s$-equivalence classes of vector bundles over $X$, by 8(3.8).
