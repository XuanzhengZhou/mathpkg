Let $\xi$ be a vector bundle over $B$ with fibre $F^n$, where $F = \mathbf{C}$ or $\mathbf{H}$. The \textbf{conjugate bundle} $\overline{\xi}$ is the vector bundle with the same underlying total space and projection $\pi \colon E \to B$, but with the scalar multiplication on each fibre conjugated: for $\lambda \in F$ and $v \in \pi^{-1}(b)$, the new scalar multiplication is $\lambda \star v = \overline{\lambda} \cdot v$, where $\overline{\lambda}$ denotes conjugation in $F$ and $\cdot$ denotes the original scalar multiplication in $\xi$.
