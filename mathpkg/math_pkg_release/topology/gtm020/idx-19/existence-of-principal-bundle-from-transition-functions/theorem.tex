For a system of transition functions $\{g_{i,j}\}$ on a space $B$ relative to an open covering $\{V_i\}_{i \in I}$ with values in a topological group $G$, there exists a principal $G$-bundle $\xi = (X, p, B)$ over $B$ with an atlas $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ having $\{g_{i,j}\}$ as transition functions. Moreover, if $\eta$ is any other such principal $G$-bundle, then $\xi$ and $\eta$ are isomorphic over $B$.
