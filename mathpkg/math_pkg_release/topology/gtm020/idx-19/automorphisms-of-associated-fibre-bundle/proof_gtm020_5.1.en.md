By Theorem 1.1, fibre bundle automorphisms of $\xi[F]$ are quotients of $(b, s, y) \mapsto (b, g(b)s, y)$ under the diagonal $G$-action. Since $(b, g(b)s, y) \bmod G = (b, g(b)y)$, the fibre bundle automorphisms are of the form $(b, y) \mapsto (b, g(b)y) = h_g(b, y)$.

For composition: $h_{g'}h_g(b, y) = h_{g'}(b, g(b)y) = (b, g'(b)g(b)y) = h_{g'g}(b, y)$. And $h_g$ is the identity if and only if $g(b)y = y$ for all $y \in F$, which holds when $g(b) = 1$ for all $b \in B$.
