Let $\eta = \xi[Y]$ be a fibre bundle over $B$ with group $G$ and fibre $Y$, let $f: B_1 \rightarrow B$ be a map, and let $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ be an atlas for $\eta$ with transition functions $\{g_{i,j}\}$. Then $\{(f^{-1}(V_i), f^*(h_i))\}$ is an atlas for $f^*(\eta) = f^*(\xi)[Y]$ with transition functions $\{g_{i,j} \circ f\}$.
