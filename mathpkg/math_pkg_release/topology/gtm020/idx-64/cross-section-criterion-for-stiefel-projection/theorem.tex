The projection $q : V_{\rho(n)+1}(\mathbf{R}^n) \to S^{n-1}$ has a cross section if and only if there exists a map
\[
S^{n-1} \to \mathbf{R}P^{n-1}/\mathbf{R}P^{n-\rho(n)-2}
\]
whose composition with the projection $\mathbf{R}P^{n-1}/\mathbf{R}P^{n-\rho(n)-2} \to \mathbf{R}P^{n-1}/\mathbf{R}P^{n-2} = S^{n-1}$ is of degree $1$.
