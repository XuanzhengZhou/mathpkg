Let $F = \mathbf{F}_q$ be the finite field with $q$ elements, where $q = p^f$ for a prime $p$. Let $\chi: F^* \to \mathbf{C}^*$ be a multiplicative character of order $m$. Let $\tau(\chi)$ denote the Gauss sum
$$\tau(\chi) = \sum_{x \in F^*} \chi(x) \zeta^{\operatorname{Tr}_{F/\mathbf{F}_p}(x)}$$
where $\zeta$ is a primitive $p$-th root of unity. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\tau(\chi)^m$ lies in the cyclotomic field $\mathbf{Q}(\zeta_m)$.
\item $\tau(\chi)\,\zeta_m$ lies in $\mathbf{Q}(\zeta_{mp})$.
\end{enumerate}
