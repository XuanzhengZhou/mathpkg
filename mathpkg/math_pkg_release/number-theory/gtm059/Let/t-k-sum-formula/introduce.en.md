Lemma 1 in Chapter 1 of Lang's Cyclotomic Fields provides a formula for the function $t(k)$ in terms of a sum over powers of $q$. This lemma serves as a technical tool in the proof of Theorem 2.2, relating the character sum to the ideal factorization of Gauss sums.
