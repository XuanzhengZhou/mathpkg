A standard property of Bernoulli polynomials states that
$$\frac{1}{k}(B_k(X+1) - B_k(X)) = X^{k-1}.$$

Hence for any integer $m$ we see that $X^{k-1}$ satisfies the first assertion of the lemma. The second assertion follows easily by continuity. The lemma is also valid for $p = 2$.
