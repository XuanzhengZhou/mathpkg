The proof follows from the distribution relations satisfied by $g$. By considering the injective limit of group algebras over $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$, ordered by divisibility, with the injection from one level to a higher group given by adding the images of all group elements, one verifies the distribution property.
