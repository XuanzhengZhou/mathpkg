Let $B_k(X)$ be the $k$-th Bernoulli polynomial. Define:
$$\theta_i(N) = N^{i-1} \sum_{k=1}^{N} B_k\left(\frac{X}{N}\right) x_i^2,$$
or equivalently with $B_k(0)$:
$$\theta_i(N) = N^{i-1} \sum_{k=1}^{N} \left(B_k\left(\frac{X}{N}\right) - B_k(0)\right) x_i^2 = \theta_i - \frac{N^{i-1}}{N} B_k(G),$$
where $B_n = B_n(0)$ is the $n$-th Bernoulli number. We have $\deg \theta = 0$ and $\deg \theta' = 0$ for $k$ even.
