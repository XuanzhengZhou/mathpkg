This lemma provides a formula for the norm residue symbol $(x_n, x_n)$ when the element $x$ is highly divisible, i.e., when its $p$-adic valuation is sufficiently large relative to the ramification index. The result expresses the Hilbert symbol in terms of the bracket operation $[\cdot, \cdot]$ evaluated at $x_n$ and half its absolute value. This main lemma is a crucial technical step in the proof of explicit reciprocity laws, reducing the computation of local symbols to power series manipulations.
