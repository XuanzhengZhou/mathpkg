Under the hypotheses of Theorem 4.1 (condition IW with $s = 1$), we have an exact sequence

$$
1 \to G_n \to G \to \Gamma \to 1
$$

where $\Gamma = \operatorname{Gal}(K_\infty/K) \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$ and $G_n = \operatorname{Gal}(M_n/K_n)$ is finite. Moreover, $G$ acts on $G_n$ by conjugation, and the action of $\Gamma$ on $G_n$ factors through the restriction map, giving $G_n$ the structure of a $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[\Gamma]]$-module.
