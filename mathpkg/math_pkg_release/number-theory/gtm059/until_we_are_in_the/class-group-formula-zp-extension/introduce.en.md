Iwasawa's class group formula is the central result of the theory. It states that the $p$-primary part of the class number in layer $K_n$ of a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension grows asymptotically as $p^{\mu p^n + \lambda n + \nu}$ for $n$ sufficiently large. The invariants $\mu$, $\lambda$, and $\nu$ characterize the $\Lambda$-module structure of $X = \varprojlim A_n$. The celebrated "$\mu = 0$ conjecture" (later proved by Ferrero-Washington for cyclotomic extensions) asserts $\mu = 0$ for the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension.
