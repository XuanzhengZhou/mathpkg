Assume condition IW is satisfied. Let $A_n$ be the $p$-primary part of the ideal class group of $K_n$, and let $X = \varprojlim A_n$ be the projective limit under norm maps. Then $X$ is a finitely generated torsion $\Lambda$-module, and there exists a characteristic polynomial $f_X(T) \in \mathbb{Z}_p[T]$ (distinguished, determined up to a unit in $\Lambda$) such that for all sufficiently large $n$,

$$
\#A_n = p^{\mu p^n + \lambda n + \nu}
$$

where:
- $\mu = \mu(X) \geq 0$ is the $\mu$-invariant (the exponent of $p$ dividing the characteristic power series),
- $\lambda = \lambda(X) \geq 0$ is the $\lambda$-invariant (the degree of the distinguished polynomial $f_X$),
- $\nu = \nu(X)$ is a constant (depending on $n_0$).

Moreover, by Nakayama's lemma, $X$ is generated over $\Lambda$ by at most $s$ elements, where $s$ is the number of ramified primes in the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension.
