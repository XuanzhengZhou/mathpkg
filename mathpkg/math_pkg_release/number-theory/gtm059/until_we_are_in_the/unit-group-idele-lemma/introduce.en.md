This lemma describes how the global units $E$ of a number field $K$ interact with the unit idele group $U_p$ in the idele topology. It states that the intersection of $U_p$ with the closure of $K^\times$ in the idele group equals the closure of the global units. This technical result is needed for computing the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension via class field theory.
