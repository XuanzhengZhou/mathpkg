Theorem 6.2 provides a further factorization of the Dedekind determinant by separating the contribution of the trivial character. The factor $\sum_{a \in G} f(a)$ corresponding to the trivial character is extracted, leaving a product over nontrivial characters. This splitting is essential for expressing the class number of a cyclotomic field as a product of character sums, each corresponding to a nontrivial Dirichlet character.
