Cyclotomic units are explicit units constructed from roots of unity in a cyclotomic field $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)$. For $a$ coprime to the conductor $m$, the element $g_a = (\zeta^a - 1)/(\zeta - 1)$ is a unit, and the group generated by all such $g_a$ plays a role analogous to that of circular units. The subgroup of real cyclotomic units (those fixed under complex conjugation, up to sign) is of particular importance for the analytic class number formula.
