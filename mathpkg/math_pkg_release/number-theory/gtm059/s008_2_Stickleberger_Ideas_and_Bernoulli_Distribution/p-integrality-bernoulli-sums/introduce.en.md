This lemma establishes the $p$-integrality of certain sums involving Bernoulli numbers. It relies on the von Staudt-Clausen theorem (via Kummer's congruences) to control the $p$-adic denominators of the Bernoulli numbers $B_i$. The result is crucial for determining the intersection of the Stickelberger ideal with $\mathbb{Z}$.
