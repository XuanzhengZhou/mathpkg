The Stickelberger distribution associated with $k!$ (or its $k$-th derivative) is non-vanishing for all $k$. Moreover, on the space of odd distributions, the Stickelberger distribution attains the maximal possible rank.

Equivalently, for an odd character $\chi$, the Stickelberger sum $S_\chi$ does not vanish identically, and the resulting distribution spans the full space of odd distributions satisfying the distribution relation.
