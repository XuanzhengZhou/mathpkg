Let $\chi$ be a character of $G(N) = (\mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z})^\times$ with conductor $m$.

(i) If every prime dividing $N$ also divides $m$, then the Stickelberger sum $S_\chi$ satisfies a simple multiplicative relation.

(ii) Let $p$ be a prime dividing $N$ but not dividing $m$. Write $N = p^r M$ with $p \nmid M$. Then

$$
S_{\chi}(N) = (1 - \chi(p)) \, S_{\chi}(M).
$$

This is the fundamental distribution relation for the Stickelberger sum with respect to the character $\chi$.
