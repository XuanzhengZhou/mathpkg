This lemma describes how the operator $R(X)$ constructed from the Bernoulli polynomial fundamental identity acts on the polynomial ring $\mathbb{Q}[X]$. The integrality property of certain sums involving powers of $R(X)$ is a key technical tool for computing indices of Stickelberger ideals in the group ring of a cyclotomic field.
