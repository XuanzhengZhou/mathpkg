Generalized Bernoulli numbers $B_{k,\chi}$, introduced by Leopoldt, extend ordinary Bernoulli numbers by incorporating a Dirichlet character $\chi$. They are defined via a character sum over the Bernoulli polynomial evaluated at rational arguments. These numbers are central to the arithmetic of cyclotomic fields, appearing in Stickelberger's theorem and in formulas for the index of the Stickelberger ideal in the group ring.
