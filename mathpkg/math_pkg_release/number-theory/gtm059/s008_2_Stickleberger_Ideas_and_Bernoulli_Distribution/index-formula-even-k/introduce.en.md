This theorem gives a formula for the index $(R_{k,\chi} : R_{0,\chi})$ of the Stickelberger ideal in the group ring, expressed in terms of generalized Bernoulli numbers. It is the central computational result for even $k$, showing that the index is essentially $N^{k-1}$ times a product of the Bernoulli number $B_{k,\chi}$ divided by $k$.
