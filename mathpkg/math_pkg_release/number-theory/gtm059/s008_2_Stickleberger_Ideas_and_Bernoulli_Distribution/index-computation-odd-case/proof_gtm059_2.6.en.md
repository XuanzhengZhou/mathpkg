The group $2r$ is generated by elements of the form $(v_r, -v_r) = v_r(-v_r)$. An element $\xi \in L$ lies in $R[x]$, so the argument proceeds as in the even case. Computing the determinant of the relevant linear transformation gives $p^i$ where $i = n$, completing the proof.
