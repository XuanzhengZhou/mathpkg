Let $F = \mathbb{F}_q$ be a finite field and $\chi, \psi$ multiplicative characters of $F^*$. If $\chi\psi \neq 1$ (i.e., $\chi \neq \psi^{-1}$), then

$$J(\chi, \psi) = \frac{S(\chi) S(\psi)}{S(\chi\psi)}.$$

If $\chi\psi = 1$ but neither $\chi$ nor $\psi$ is trivial, then $J(\chi, \chi^{-1}) = -\chi(-1)$.
