Let $F = \mathbb{F}_q$ be a finite field, $\lambda$ a non-trivial additive character, and $\chi$ a multiplicative character of $F^*$ extended to $F$ with $\chi(0) = 0$ when $\chi \neq 1$. Let $T$ be the Fourier transform. Then for $y \in F$,

$$T\chi(y) = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if } y = 0 \text{ and } \chi \neq 1, \\ \chi(y^{-1}) S(\chi) & \text{if } y \neq 0. \end{cases}$$

Equivalently, $T\chi = S(\chi) \cdot \chi^{-1}$ on $F^*$.
