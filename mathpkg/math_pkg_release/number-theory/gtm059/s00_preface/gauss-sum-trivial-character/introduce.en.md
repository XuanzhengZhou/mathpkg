For the trivial multiplicative character $1$, the Gauss sum evaluates to $-1$: $S(1) = \sum_{x \in F^*} \lambda(x) = -1$. This follows from the orthogonality of the additive character over $F$, since $\sum_{x \in F} \lambda(x) = 0$ and $\lambda(0) = 1$, so the sum over $F^*$ equals $-1$.
