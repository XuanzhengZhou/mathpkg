This theorem describes the structure of the maximal $p$-abelian extension $Q_n$ of the $n$-th layer $K_n$ of a cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, under the assumption that only one prime of $K_n$ lies above $p$. Under this condition, $Q_n$ is entirely generated by adjoining $p$-power roots of units: $Q_n = Q(E_n^{(p^{n+1})})$. This simplifies the Kummer theory description considerably, as the contribution from the ideal class group is absorbed by the unit group in this setting.
