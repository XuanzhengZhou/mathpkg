This theorem describes the relationship between local and global units in the context of Kummer theory over $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions. The projective limits of unit groups are $\Lambda$-modules whose structure is closely tied to Galois groups via the Kummer pairing. The connection to Leopoldt's conjecture, which predicts that the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-rank of the global units modulo cyclotomic units equals the number of independent $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions, makes this a central result linking Iwasawa theory to classical questions about units.
