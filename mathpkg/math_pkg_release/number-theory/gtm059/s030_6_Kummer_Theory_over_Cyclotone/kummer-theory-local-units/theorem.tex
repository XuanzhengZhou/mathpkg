Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. Let $U_n$ be the group of local units at a prime above $p$ in $K_n$, and let $E_n$ be the global units. The projective limits $\varprojlim U_n$ and $\varprojlim E_n$ are $\Lambda$-modules, and the natural map

$$\varprojlim E_n \to \varprojlim U_n$$

has a kernel and cokernel that are controlled by Leopoldt's conjecture. The Kummer pairing induces a duality between the Galois groups $X = \operatorname{Gal}(M/K_{\infty})$ and the unit groups.
