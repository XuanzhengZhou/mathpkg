**Theorem 6.1.** *Let $x \in \mathfrak{d}(\pi_n)$ and suppose*

$$
\operatorname{ord}_i x \ge \mu[2] + 2 \quad \text{if $p$ is odd},
$$
$$
\operatorname{ord}_i x \ge x_{\min}[2] + 3 \quad \text{if $p = 2$}.
$$

*Then for any unit $u$ we have*

$$(x, u) = [x, \mu[2] + u].$$

*Here $[x, \cdot]$ denotes the explicit symbol defined via the logarithm and trace map of the Lubin-Tate formal group.*
