Theorem 5.2 provides a more precise formulation of the Explicit Reciprocity Law in the special case where the Frobenius power series $F(X)$ is a polynomial. This condition holds for the basic Lubin-Tate group and the formal multiplicative group. In this case, the symbol $[x, \pi_n]_n$ can be expressed via the derivative $F_X$ of the Frobenius series and the inverse power series $Y_n(X)$, yielding a technically simpler formula.
