**Theorem 7.1.** *Let $x \in \mathfrak{d}(\pi)$ and let*

$$n = (n_1, n_2, \dots, n_k) \quad\text{with}\quad n_k = N_{n_k} w_n.$$

*Assume that*

$$
k \ge |s| + 20 + 1 \quad \text{if $p$ is odd},
$$
$$
k \ge |n| + 20 + 2 \quad \text{if $p = 2$}.
$$

*Then $[x, w_n]$ lies in $\mathfrak{p}^{n+1}$ for $1 \le i \le k$ and we have*

$$(x, w_n) = [x_n, y_n]_B(x)$$

*for such $n$.*
