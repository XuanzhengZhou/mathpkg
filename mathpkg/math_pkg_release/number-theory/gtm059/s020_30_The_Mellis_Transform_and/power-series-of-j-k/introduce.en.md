Proposition 3.5 describes the power series $G_{k+1}$ associated with the operator $J_k$ when a nontrivial character $\chi$ on $\mathbf{Z}_p^2$ of conductor $N$ is given. The power series $G_{k+1}$ is expressed as $c_2(G_k(t))$, where $G_k(t)$ itself is built from sums involving $R(i)$ and the factor $(p-1)^2$. This forms part of the Mellin transform machinery used to construct the $p$-adic $L$-function in Lang's treatment of cyclotomic fields.
