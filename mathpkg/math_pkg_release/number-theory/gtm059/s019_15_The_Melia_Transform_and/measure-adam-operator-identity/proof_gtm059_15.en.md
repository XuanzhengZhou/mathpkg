Since $a^{*}\mu_X(0)$ is a measure on $\mathbb{Z}_p^{\times}$, there exists a power series $f$ such that $f[a^{*}\mu_X(0)] = a^{*}\mu_X(0) = \mu_X(0)$ and $Uf = f$.

Then $\mu_X(0) = \chi_0(X)$, whence by a previous lemma (Mean 6), we have $g = Df = Dh$. We let $h = Uf$ to conclude the proof.
