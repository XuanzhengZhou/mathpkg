This lemma establishes that every measure $\mu$ on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ admits a representation as a power series $h \in \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ with vanishing constant term. The integral representation $h(t) = \int \Omega^k(t) \, d\mu(t)$ expresses the power series coefficients in terms of the measure. This result is crucial for proving the analyticity properties of the $p$-adic $L$-function, as it allows one to interchange sums and integrals and to recognize the resulting expressions as power series expansions.
