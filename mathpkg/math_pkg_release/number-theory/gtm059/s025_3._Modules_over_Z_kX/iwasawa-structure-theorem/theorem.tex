Let $A = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$ be the Iwasawa algebra. Let $M$ be a finitely generated $A$-module. Then there exists a pseudo-isomorphism

$$
M \sim A^r \oplus \bigoplus_{i} A/(f_i^{n_i}) \oplus \bigoplus_{j} A/(p^{m_j})
$$

where each $f_i$ is a distinguished irreducible polynomial in $\mathbb{Z}_p[T]$, and $n_i$, $m_j$ are positive integers. The integers $r$, the polynomials $f_i$, and the exponents $n_i$, $m_j$ are uniquely determined up to order.
