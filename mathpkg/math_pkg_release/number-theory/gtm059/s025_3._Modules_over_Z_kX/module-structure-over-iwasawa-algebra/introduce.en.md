This structure theorem classifies finitely generated modules over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ up to pseudo-isomorphism. The proof uses admissible matrix operations (analogous to row and column operations in linear algebra) together with the Weierstrass preparation theorem to diagonalize the relation matrix. This theorem is the technical foundation for all of Iwasawa theory, providing the theoretical justification for the Iwasawa invariants $\mu$ and $\lambda$.
