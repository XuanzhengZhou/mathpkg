The unit group $U$ of $C_p$ admits a product decomposition
$$
U = U_0 \times U_1,
$$
where $U_0$ is the group of roots of unity in $C_p$ of order prime to $p$, and $U_1$ is the group of principal units
$$
U_1 = \{ u \in U : u \equiv 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}} \},
$$
with $\mathfrak{p}$ the maximal ideal of the ring of integers of $C_p$.
