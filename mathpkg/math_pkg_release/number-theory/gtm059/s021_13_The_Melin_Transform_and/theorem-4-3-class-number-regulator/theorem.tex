Let $m = p^n$ be a prime power and let $K = \mathbb{Q}(\mu_m)^+$ be the real subfield of the $m$-th cyclotomic field. Let $h^+$ be the class number of $K$ and let $R_p(K)$ be the $p$-adic regulator of $K$. Then
$$
h^+ = \frac{1}{2^{r}} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{\Delta_K}}{R_p(K)} \cdot \prod_{\chi \neq 1} L_p(1,\chi),
$$
where $r = \frac{\varphi(m)}{2} - 1$ is the rank of the unit group, $\Delta_K$ is the discriminant of $K$, and the product runs over all non-trivial even Dirichlet characters of conductor dividing $m$.

Equivalently, using the decomposition of the $p$-adic regulator:
$$
h^+ \cdot R_p(K) = \prod_{\chi \neq 1} \frac{1}{2} \cdot L_p(1,\chi).
$$
