Theorem 4.2 (also referred to in the text as the Bernoulli theorem for the regulator) expresses the $p$-adic regulator of the real cyclic field $\mathbb{Q}(\mu_p)^+$ as a product of values of the $p$-adic $L$-function $L_p(1,\chi)$ over all non-trivial characters. Equivalently, using the cyclotomic units, the regulator can be written as a determinant of $p$-adic logarithms. This factorization is a $p$-adic analogue of the classical formula relating the Dirichlet regulator to $L(1,\chi)$ and is a crucial step toward the $p$-adic class number formula.
