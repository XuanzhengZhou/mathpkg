Theorem 5.1 establishes a proportionality between the $p$-adic regulator of the full unit group $\mathcal{E}$ and the $p$-adic regulator of the cyclotomic units $\mathcal{C}$ in the real subfield $K$ of a cyclotomic field. The proportionality factor is precisely the unit index $(\mathcal{E}:\mathcal{C})$, which equals the class number $h^+$ of $K$ by a result from the preceding chapter. This theorem is crucial for expressing the $p$-adic class number formula in terms of cyclotomic units, which are computationally more accessible than general units.
