Let $\chi$ be a primitive Dirichlet character whose conductor $N$ is a power of $p$. Let $\tau(\chi)$ denote the Gauss sum of $\chi$, and let $L_p(1,\chi)$ denote the value of the $p$-adic $L$-function at $s=1$. Then
$$
L_p(1,\chi) = \frac{\tau(\chi)}{N} \sum_{a=1}^{N} \chi^{-1}(a) \log_p(1 - \zeta_N^a),
$$
where $\zeta_N$ is a primitive $N$-th root of unity and $\log_p$ is the $p$-adic logarithm extended to $C_p^\times$ with $\log_p p = 0$.
