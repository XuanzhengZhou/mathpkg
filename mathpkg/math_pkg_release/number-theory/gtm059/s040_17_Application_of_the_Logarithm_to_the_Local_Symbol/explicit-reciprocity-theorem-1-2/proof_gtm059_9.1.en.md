The proof occupies the remainder of the sections and proceeds by systematically developing the theory of the logarithmic derivative. The key steps are:

1. Establish the properties of the logarithmic derivative map $$D_1$$ on the Lubin-Tate formal group (DI 1).
2. Show that the symbol $$[x, a_n]$$ is well-defined under Condition (0) using the structural properties of the different ideal.
3. Relate the symbol to the trace pairing via the logarithm map, using the formula LS 7.
4. Apply the Lubin-Tate $$\psi$$-reciprocity (LS R) to connect the symbol to the norm residue symbol.
5. Conclude the equality $$\langle x, n \rangle = [x, N_n]_{A_f(n)}$$ by the compatibility of the formalisms.
