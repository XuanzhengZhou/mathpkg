Let $K$ be a finite extension of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ containing the $p$-th roots of unity. The local norm residue symbol (Hilbert symbol) $(\cdot, \cdot)_p: K^{\times} \times K^{\times} \to \mu_p$ can be expressed explicitly in terms of the $p$-adic logarithm:

$$(a, b)_p = \zeta_p^{\frac{1}{p} \operatorname{Tr}_{K/\mathbb{Q}_p}(\log a \cdot d\log b)}$$

where $d\log b$ is the logarithmic derivative and $\operatorname{Tr}$ is the trace map. This formula is the local analogue of the Coleman power series and provides an explicit reciprocity law.
