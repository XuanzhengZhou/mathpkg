For the explicit reciprocity symbol $$[x, a_n]$$ to be well-defined independently of auxiliary choices, certain divisibility or largeness conditions must be satisfied. Condition (0) specifies quantitative bounds on the indices $$n$$ and the field levels $$K_n$$, ensuring that the symbol takes values in $$K_n / \mathfrak{p}^{n+1}$$ and is independent of the power series representing the elements. These conditions are technical but necessary for the rigorous statement of the reciprocity law.
