As a concrete illustration of the explicit reciprocity law, Iwasawa's formula evaluates the norm residue symbol $$(x, \pi_0)$$ for the formal multiplicative group over $$\mathbb{Q}_p$$. When the logarithm series is $$\log(1+X)$$ and the primitive $$p^2$$-th root of unity $$\xi$$ is used, the symbol simplifies to $$\xi^{-1}$$. The formula expresses $$x$$ in terms of the trace of the logarithm and its logarithmic derivative, providing a completely explicit description of the local symbol in the cyclotomic case.
