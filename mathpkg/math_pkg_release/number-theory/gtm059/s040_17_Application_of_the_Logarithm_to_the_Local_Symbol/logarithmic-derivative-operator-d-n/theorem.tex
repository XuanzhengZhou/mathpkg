Let $$a \in K$$ and let $$\pi$$ be a uniformizer. Choose a power series $$g(X) \in \mathcal{O}[\![X]\!]$$ with unit constant term such that

$$a = g(\pi).$$

Let $$D = \frac{d}{dX}$$ denote the ordinary formal derivative, so that $$D(g) = g'(X)$$. Define

$$D_n(L) = g'(\pi).$$

The operator $$D_n$$ depends on the choice of the element $$(\pi_0, \pi_1, \dots) \in T_{\pi}(L)$$ and on the choice of the power series $$g$$.
