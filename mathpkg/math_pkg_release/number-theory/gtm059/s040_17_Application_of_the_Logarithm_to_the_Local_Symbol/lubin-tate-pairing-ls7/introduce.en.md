Formula LS 7 expresses the compatibility between the local symbol pairing and the trace pairing on the Lubin-Tate formal group. The local symbol defines a pairing between certain subgroups of the multiplicative group of the local field and the formal group, and LS 7 states that under the logarithm map, this pairing corresponds precisely to the trace pairing. This is a key step in establishing explicit reciprocity laws.
