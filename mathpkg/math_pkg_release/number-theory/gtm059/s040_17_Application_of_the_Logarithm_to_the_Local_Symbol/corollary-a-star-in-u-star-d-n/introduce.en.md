For the Lubin-Tate formal group $$A$$ associated to a prime element of a local field, the kernel of the canonical logarithm map $$u$$ satisfies a refined inclusion: the $$(k \times n)$$-torsion subgroup of the kernel is contained in $$[u^*]^k(d_n)$$, where $$d_n$$ is related to the different. This follows from the structure of the logarithm map on the formal group and the inclusion of the kernel in the maximal ideal.
