Theorem 3.1.3 presents the generalization of the classical Artin-Hasse reciprocity law by Coates and Wiles. For elements in the second cohomology group, the norm residue symbol $$(x, n_0)$$ equals the Lubin-Tate symbol $$[x, n_1]$$ multiplied by the absolute value of the unit parameter. This theorem unifies the classical explicit formulas of Artin-Hasse and Iwasawa within the Lubin-Tate framework and is valid for sufficiently large level $$n \geq 2n+1$$.
