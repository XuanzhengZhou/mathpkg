Since $$\log N_{n-1} \equiv z \pmod{\mathfrak{p}^2}$$ with $$n \equiv 0 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}^{n+1}}$$ and $$p \neq 2$$, we compute

$$-T_n(\log n) = \log N_{n-1} \equiv N_{n-1} - 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}^{n+1}}$$

using the power series expansion of the logarithm. When $$K$$ is unramified over $$\mathbb{Q}_p$$, we may take $$\pi = p$$, and the congruence simplifies accordingly. The result follows from the properties of the trace and the logarithm on the Lubin-Tate formal group.
