This corollary to the explicit reciprocity law relates the logarithm of the norm with a congruence involving the trace. Specifically, $$-T_n(\log n) \equiv N_{n-1} - 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}^{n+1}}$$, which provides a computational link between the logarithm on the Lubin-Tate formal group and the norm map on the field extension. When the field is unramified over $$\mathbb{Q}_p$$, one may take $$\pi = p$$.
