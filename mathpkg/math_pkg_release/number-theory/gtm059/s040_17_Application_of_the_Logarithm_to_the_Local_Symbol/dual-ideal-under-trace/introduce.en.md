Given an ideal $$\mathfrak{a}$$ in a finite extension $$K_i$$ of a local field $$K$$, its dual $$\mathfrak{a}^*$$ with respect to the trace pairing consists of all elements whose trace product with every element of $$\mathfrak{a}$$ lies in the ring of integers. This dual is naturally isomorphic to the module of $$\mathcal{O}$$-linear homomorphisms $$\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}}(\mathfrak{a}, \mathcal{O})$$, and is related to the inverse different by $$\mathfrak{a}^* = \mathfrak{a}^{-1} \mathfrak{B}_0^{-1}$$.
