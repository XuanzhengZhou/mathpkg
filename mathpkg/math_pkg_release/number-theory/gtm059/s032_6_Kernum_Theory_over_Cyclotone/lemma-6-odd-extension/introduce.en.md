Lemma 6 provides a criterion for when a compositum of cyclotomic extensions is an odd extension. Given the cyclotomic pairing $(C^{n+1}, K) = k_1 K_1$, the lemma asserts that the compositum $K_1 K_1$ is an odd extension, meaning its Galois group is stable under complex conjugation in the appropriate sense.
