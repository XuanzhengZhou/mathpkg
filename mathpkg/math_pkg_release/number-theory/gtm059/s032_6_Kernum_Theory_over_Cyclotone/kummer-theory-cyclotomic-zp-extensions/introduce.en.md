This section introduces Kummer theory in the setting of cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions, building on the foundational work of Iwasawa. The theory relates ideal class groups, unit groups, and Galois cohomology through projective limits taken over the finite layers of the cyclotomic tower. Key results include connections to the Vandiver conjecture, Kummer-Takagi exponents, and formal group methods derived from Lubin-Tate theory.
