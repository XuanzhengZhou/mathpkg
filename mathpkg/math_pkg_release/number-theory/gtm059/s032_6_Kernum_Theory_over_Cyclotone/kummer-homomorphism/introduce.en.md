The Kummer homomorphism $\mu_0$ is a fundamental construction in Iwasawa theory of local units. It maps units in the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension to $p$-adic integers via a logarithmic derivative operator $D = (1+X)d/dX$. For a unit $u$ with associated power series $f$, the value $\mu_0(u) = D^{-1}\{f'/f\}(0) \bmod p$ is well-defined and behaves as a homomorphism. This construction is later generalized via Lubin-Tate formal groups in the Coates-Wiles framework.
