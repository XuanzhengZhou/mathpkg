By Kummer theory at each finite level, there is a pairing between the Galois group and the $p^n$-th powers of units. Passing to the projective limit yields a pairing of $\Lambda$-modules. The maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension corresponds, via Kummer theory, to the group generated by all $p^n$-th roots of $S$-units where $S$ is the set of primes above $p$. The structure theorem follows by identifying the Kummer radical with the local units modulo cyclotomic units and applying Pontryagin duality in the category of compact $\Lambda$-modules.
