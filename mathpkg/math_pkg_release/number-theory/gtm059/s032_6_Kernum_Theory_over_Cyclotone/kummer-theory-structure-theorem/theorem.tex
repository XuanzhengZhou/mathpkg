Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension and let $\mathcal{U}_{\infty} = \varprojlim U_n$ be the projective limit of local units. The Kummer pairing induces a $\Lambda$-module isomorphism

$$\operatorname{Gal}(M/K_{\infty}) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(\mathcal{U}_{\infty}/\overline{\mathcal{E}}_{\infty}, \mu_{p^{\infty}})$$

where $\overline{\mathcal{E}}_{\infty}$ is the closure of the global cyclotomic units and $\mu_{p^{\infty}}$ is the group of all $p$-power roots of unity.
