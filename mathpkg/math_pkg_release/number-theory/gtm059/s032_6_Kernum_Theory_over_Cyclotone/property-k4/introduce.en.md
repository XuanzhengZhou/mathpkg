Property K.4 describes how Galois idempotents $e(\chi)$ act on the units $u_k = 1 - \zeta^k$ that topologically generate the local unit group $U_0$. The idempotent projection $e(\chi)(u_k)$ is non-zero precisely when $k$ matches the character mod $p$, and vanishes otherwise. This orthogonality property is fundamental for computing Kummer-Takagi exponents.
