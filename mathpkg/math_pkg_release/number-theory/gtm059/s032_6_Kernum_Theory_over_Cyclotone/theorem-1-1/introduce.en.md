Theorem 1.1 computes the explicit values of the Kummer-Takagi pairing $\varphi_0$ on the fundamental cyclotomic units $u_k = 1 - \zeta^k$. When the indices match ($k = j$), the value is $-k$ modulo $p$; when they differ, the value vanishes. This orthogonality result is the key to expressing any unit $\equiv 1 \bmod \pi$ as a product of the fundamental units $u_k$, with the exponents being the Kummer-Takagi exponents.
