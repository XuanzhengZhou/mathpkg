Theorem 4.3 describes the kernel of the map $C_1 \to C_2$ between ideal class groups in terms of Galois cohomology groups $H^i$ associated to the Vandiver quotient of $K_m$. Under the Vandiver conjecture, the cohomology $H^i(\text{Vandiver} \cdot K_m)$ vanishes, which forces the map $C_1 \to C_2$ to be injective. The proof uses a homomorphism from $K_1 \to C_2$ into the unit cohomology, constructed by sending an ideal representative to the cohomology class of a unit.
