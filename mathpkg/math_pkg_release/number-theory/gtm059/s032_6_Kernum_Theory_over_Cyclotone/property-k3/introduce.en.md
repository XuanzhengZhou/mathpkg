Property K.3 states that the Kummer homomorphism satisfies a chain rule when the variable is changed from $X$ to $T$. Since the differential operator $D = (1+X)d/dX$ is related to the logarithmic derivative, the chain rule property is essential for establishing the functorial behavior of the Kummer homomorphism under changes of formal group parameters.
