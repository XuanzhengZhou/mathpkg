*Proof.* Taking the logarithmic derivative $g \mapsto g'/g$ for any power series $g$ and applying the change of variables $Z = \lambda_{\mathcal{G}}(W)$, the equality of coefficients follows from Lemma 2 of §6 (in the next chapter), which establishes the basic properties of the Lubin-Tate logarithm.
