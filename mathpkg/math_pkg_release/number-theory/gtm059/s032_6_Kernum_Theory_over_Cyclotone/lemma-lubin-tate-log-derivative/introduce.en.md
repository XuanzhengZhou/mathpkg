This lemma is part of the Lubin-Tate formal group generalization of the Kummer homomorphism. It states that the coefficients obtained from the logarithmic derivative operator applied to power series expansions of Lubin-Tate formal groups agree up to order $q-2$ when changing from the local parameter $W$ to the additive parameter $Z$. This compatibility is essential for extending the Kummer homomorphism to arbitrary Lubin-Tate groups in the Coates-Wiles framework.
