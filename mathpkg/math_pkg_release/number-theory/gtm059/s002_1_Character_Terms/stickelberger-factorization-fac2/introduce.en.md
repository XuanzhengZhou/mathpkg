A refinement of FAC 1 describing the $n$-th power of the Stickelberger element $\theta(\mathfrak{n}) = \sigma_b$ acting on the Gauss sum. The factorization involves $\mathfrak{p}^{\mathfrak{n}-1} + \mathfrak{p}^{\mathfrak{n}-2}$ and shows how the group ring element interacts with the prime ideal factorization. This consequence is used to study the ideal class group and the annihilator properties of the Stickelberger ideal.
