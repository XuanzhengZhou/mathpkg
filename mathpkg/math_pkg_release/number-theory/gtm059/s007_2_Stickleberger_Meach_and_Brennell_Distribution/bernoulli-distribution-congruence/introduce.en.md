Property E.2 establishes a fundamental congruence relating $E_{k,c}^{(N)}(x)$ to $x^{k-1}E_{1,c}^{(N)}(x)$. This congruence is derived from the expansion of the Bernoulli polynomial $B_k(X) = X^k - \frac{k}{2}X^{k-1} + \text{lower terms}$ and links the higher-k distribution to the $k=1$ case. It is a key step in proving the $p$-adic integrality of the Stickelberger elements and underlies the "jacking up" argument used to prove Theorem 2.1.
