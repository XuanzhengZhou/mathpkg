Theorem 2.2 expresses the limit form of Property E2 in the $p$-adic projective limit. Taking $N = p^n$ and passing to the limit over the projective system $\mathbf{Z}(p^n) \to \mathbf{Z}_p$, the congruence becomes an exact equality. This relation shows that the higher $k$-th Bernoulli distribution is determined by the first one multiplied by $x^{k-1}$, providing a powerful structural result for $p$-adic integration theory.
