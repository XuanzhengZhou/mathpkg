For a cyclotomic field $K = \mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)$, the cyclotomic units form a subgroup of finite index in the full group of units. The unit index $Q_1$ measures this discrepancy. Theorem 4.1 of Lang's Cyclotomic Fields states that $Q_1 = 1$ precisely when the conductor $m$ is a prime power, and $Q_1 = 2$ otherwise. This result plays an important role in the analytic class number formula and in the study of the Iwasawa-Leopoldt conjecture.
