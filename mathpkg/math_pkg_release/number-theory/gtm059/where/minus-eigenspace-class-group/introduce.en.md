In the study of cyclotomic fields and more general CM-fields, the ideal class group decomposes into plus and minus eigenspaces under the action of complex conjugation $\tau$. The minus part $C_K^-$ is particularly important because the Stickelberger ideal and the analytic class number formula relate directly to its order. The structure of $C_K^-$ is governed by $p$-adic $L$-functions, Bernoulli numbers, and Iwasawa theory. The injectivity of the norm map $C_{K^+} \to C_K$ (Theorem 4.1) implies that the plus part can be studied via the maximal real subfield $K^+$.
