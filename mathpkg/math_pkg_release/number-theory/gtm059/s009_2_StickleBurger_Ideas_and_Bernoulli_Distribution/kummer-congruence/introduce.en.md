Kummer's congruence is a fundamental $p$-adic property of Bernoulli numbers. It states that the $p$-adically normalized Bernoulli numbers $B_k/k$ are $p$-adically continuous as functions of $k$ in the sense of $p$-adic topology on the indexing set. This congruence is the starting point for $p$-adic $L$-functions and Iwasawa theory. It follows naturally from the integral representation of Bernoulli numbers against the Bernoulli distribution.
