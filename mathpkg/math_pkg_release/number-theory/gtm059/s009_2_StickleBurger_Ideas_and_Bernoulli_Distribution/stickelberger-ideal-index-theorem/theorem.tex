Let $m = p^n$ be a prime power, and let $R = \mathbb{Z}[G]$ with $G = (\mathbb{Z}/m\mathbb{Z})^\times$.
Let $S$ be the Stickelberger ideal. Then

$$(R^* : S) = \prod_{\chi \neq 1} B_{1,\chi},$$

where the product is taken over all non-trivial even Dirichlet characters $\chi$ of
$(\mathbb{Z}/m\mathbb{Z})^\times$, and $B_{1,\chi}$ denotes the first generalized Bernoulli number
associated to $\chi$.
