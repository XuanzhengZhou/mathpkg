For the odd case (twisting by the sign character), let $R = \mathbb{Z}[G]$ and let
$S_k$ be the Stickelberger ideal. Then

$$(R : S_k) = N^{\varphi(N)/2} \prod_{\chi \text{ odd}} \frac{1}{k} B_{k,\chi},$$

where the product is taken over all odd Dirichlet characters $\chi$ of $G$. Here the
Stickelberger ideal is defined with respect to the twisted Bernoulli distribution
incorporating the sign character.
