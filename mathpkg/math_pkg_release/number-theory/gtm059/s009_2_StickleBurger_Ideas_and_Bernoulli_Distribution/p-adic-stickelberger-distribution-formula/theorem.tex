Let $c$ be a $p$-adic unit. Then for the Bernoulli distribution $E_k$ of order $k$,

$$E_{k,c}(x) = c^k E_{k,c}(x) \pmod{p^n \mathbb{Z}_p[c]},$$

where $E_{k,c}(x) = E_k(x) - c^k E_k(c^{-1}x)$ is the regularized distribution.
Equivalently, $E_{k,c}$ takes values in $\mathbb{Z}_p$ (up to a controlled denominator).
