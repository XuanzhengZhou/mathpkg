Let $N$ be a positive integer and $f: \mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{C}$ a function.
The \textbf{generalized Bernoulli polynomials} $B_{k,f}(X)$ (relative to $f$) are
defined by the generating series:

$$\sum_{a=1}^{N} \frac{f(a) t e^{t(a+X)/N}}{e^t - 1} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} B_{k,f}(X) \frac{t^k}{k!}.$$

In particular, the constant term $B_{k,f} = B_{k,f}(0)$ is the
\textbf{generalized Bernoulli number} associated to $f$.
