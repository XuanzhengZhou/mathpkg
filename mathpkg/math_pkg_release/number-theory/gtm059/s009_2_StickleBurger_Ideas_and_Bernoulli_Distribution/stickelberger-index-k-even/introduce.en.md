This theorem computes the index of the $k$-th Stickelberger ideal $S_k$ in the group ring $R = \mathbb{Z}[G]$ for even $k$. The index factors as a product of generalized Bernoulli numbers $B_{k,\chi}$ over all even characters, multiplied by a power of the conductor $N$. This result generalizes Theorem 1.1 (which was for $k=1$) to arbitrary even $k$, and is a central computational tool in the Iwasawa theory of cyclotomic fields.
