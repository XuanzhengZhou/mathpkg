This lemma constructs a projective system of elements $\zeta_c$ in the module of local units $U$. Beginning with a choice of $\lambda$ (depending on $k$), the construction produces elements that satisfy norm compatibility relations and form a coherent system. The Kummer-Takagi exponent is shown to be $0 \bmod p$, which by Nakayama's lemma implies that $U_c(\theta)$ is generated over $U_c$ by the resulting element.
