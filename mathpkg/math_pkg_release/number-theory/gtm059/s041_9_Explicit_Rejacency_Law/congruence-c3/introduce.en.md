Congruence C.3 establishes an integrality condition for elements $x$ in $d(A^3)$: the trace-different product satisfies $T_k^* \big( \frac{1}{2} T_k^* u_n \big) = n^{k+1} u_n$ whenever $m \geq 2$. Together with C.1, this provides the necessary control for Lemma 5.2 in the explicit reciprocity law. The proof follows the same verification strategy as C.2, establishing integrality of the relevant expressions.
