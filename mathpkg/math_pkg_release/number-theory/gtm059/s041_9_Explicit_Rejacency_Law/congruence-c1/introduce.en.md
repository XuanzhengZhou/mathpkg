Congruence C.1 is one of three integrality congruences needed for Lemma 5.2 in the explicit reciprocity law framework. For elements $x$ in the different $d(A_n)$ with sufficiently large $m$, the trace-different product $T_k^* \big( \frac{1}{2} T_k^* u_n \big)$ equals $n^{k+1} u_n$. Combined with C.3, this congruence provides the necessary integrality control for the local pairing.
