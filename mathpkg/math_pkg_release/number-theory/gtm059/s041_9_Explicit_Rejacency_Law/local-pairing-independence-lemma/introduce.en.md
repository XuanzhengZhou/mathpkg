Lemma 3.2 establishes that the value of the local pairing symbol $[x, x_n]$ is independent of the auxiliary parameter $m$ when $x_n$ is the component of an infinite norm-coherent vector and $m$ is sufficiently large. This allows one to define $[x]$ as this well-defined value. The lemma provides two cases: (i) when $x \neq x_0(x_n)$ with $m \geq 2n+1$, and (ii) when $x \neq x_0(4D^2(x_n))$ with $m \geq n$. The proof uses the trace-pairing identities and congruences C.1-C.3.
