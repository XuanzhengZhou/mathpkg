Coleman's explicit reciprocity law is one of the deepest results in Iwasawa theory. It provides an explicit formula relating $p$-adic $L$-functions to the Coleman power series of local units. This generalizes the classical explicit reciprocity laws of Artin-Hasse and Iwasawa-Shafarevich. The theorem is a key ingredient in the proof of the main conjecture of Iwasawa theory, as it allows the identification of the analytic $p$-adic $L$-function with the characteristic polynomial of the algebraic Iwasawa module.
