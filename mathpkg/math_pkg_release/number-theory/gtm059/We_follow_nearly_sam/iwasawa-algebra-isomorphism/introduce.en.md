This theorem establishes that the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda$, defined as a projective limit of truncated group rings, is isomorphic to the formal power series ring $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ in one variable over the $p$-adic integers. This identification depends on the choice of a topological generator $\gamma$ of $\Gamma$ and is fundamental for all subsequent work in Iwasawa theory, as it allows one to apply the structure theory of modules over power series rings to study arithmetic objects.
