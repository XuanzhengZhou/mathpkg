This lemma, labeled DL 3 in Lang's "Cyclotomic Fields," describes the action of the logarithmic derivative on units arising from the formal group of a Lubin-Tate formal group law. For a unit in the Galois extension $K_n/K$, the logarithmic derivative $\lambda_i$ transforms by a character $\omega$, yielding the formula $\lambda_i(u) = \omega(u) \lambda_i(u)^\alpha$. This relation is fundamental for computing the local pairing defining the explicit reciprocity law.
