Lemma DL 5 provides a congruence modulo $n$ relating the Coleman power series $h_K(s)$ with a trace-like operator $T_K(s)_n(s)$. The proof proceeds by assuming without loss of generality that $m = n + 1$, expanding $h_K(s)$ as a sum over the Galois group $\operatorname{Gal}(K)$, and applying DL 3 and DL 4 to reduce each term. When the Frobenius power series associated with the Lubin-Tate group is a polynomial (as for the basic Lubin-Tate group and the formal multiplicative group), a stronger congruence holds.
