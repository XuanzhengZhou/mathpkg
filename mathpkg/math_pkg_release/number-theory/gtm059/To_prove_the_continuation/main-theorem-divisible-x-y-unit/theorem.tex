Suppose that
$$\text{ord}_p(x) \ge |n_2| + 2$$
if $p$ is odd. Then for $x, y \in v_w$ with $\text{ord}_p(x), \text{ord}_p(y) \ge \text{ord}_p(y) \ge 2$, so that
$$\text{ord}_p(xy) \ge n + 3,$$
we have the interchangeability of addition in the formal group $A$ and addition in the ground field $G_a$:
$$x +_A [y] x = x +_{G_a} y \pmod{xy}, \qquad x -_A [y] x = x -_{G_a} y \pmod{xy}.$$

Consequently, addition on $A$ and addition on $G_a$ are interchangeable on the left of the symbol $(\text{ord}_x, y)$ under the condition $\text{ord}_p(x) \ge \text{ord}_p(y) \ge 2$.

In particular, we obtain the recursive relation:
$$\text{ord}_x(-1) = 1 + \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} (\text{ord}_x^{i+1})^{2-i+1}.$$
