Let $K$ be a number field and let $p$ be a fixed prime. Let $H$ be the $p$-Hilbert class field of $K$. Then there is a quasi-isomorphism (a homomorphism with finite kernel and cokernel) of $\mathbb{Z}_p$-modules:

$$G_M(K/H) \sim \overline{U_p E} \;/\; \overline{E},$$

where $U_p = \prod_{\mathfrak{p} \mid p} U_\mathfrak{p}$ is the product of local principal units at primes dividing $p$, $E$ is the group of global units, and bars denote closure in the idele topology.

Equivalently, writing $G_M(K)$ for $\operatorname{Gal}(M(K)/K)$, we have a quasi-isomorphism

$$G_M(K) \sim (U_p^\perp \cdot \overline{U_p E}) \;/\; \overline{U_p^\perp K^*}.$$

Furthermore, $G_M(K)$ contains an open subgroup of finite index isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}_p^{r_2+1}$, where $r_2$ is the number of complex places of $K$.
