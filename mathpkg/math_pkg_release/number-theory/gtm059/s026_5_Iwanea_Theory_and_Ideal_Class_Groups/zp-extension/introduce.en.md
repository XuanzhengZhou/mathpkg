A $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension is an infinite Galois extension of a number field whose Galois group is isomorphic to the additive group of $p$-adic integers $\mathbb{Z}_p$. It is the fundamental object of study in Iwasawa theory. Such an extension is built as a tower of cyclic extensions of $p$-power degree, reflecting the structure $\mathbb{Z}_p \cong \varprojlim \mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$.
