This theorem describes the algorithmic reduction of relation matrices over the Iwasawa algebra to diagonal form using admissible operations. These operations correspond to quasi-isomorphisms of the associated modules. The diagonal entries determine the structure of the module up to finite kernel and cokernel, providing the theoretical basis for the structure theorem of finitely generated $\Lambda$-modules.
