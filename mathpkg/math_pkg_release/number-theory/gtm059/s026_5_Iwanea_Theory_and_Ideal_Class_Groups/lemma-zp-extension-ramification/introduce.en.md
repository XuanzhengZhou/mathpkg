This lemma describes the ramification behavior in a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. Only finitely many primes can ramify in the infinite tower, and any ramified prime becomes totally ramified after a finite number of steps up the tower. This is a consequence of the fact that the ramification index must divide the degree $p^n$ and grows with $n$ until it stabilizes. The result is fundamental for the study of Iwasawa modules.
