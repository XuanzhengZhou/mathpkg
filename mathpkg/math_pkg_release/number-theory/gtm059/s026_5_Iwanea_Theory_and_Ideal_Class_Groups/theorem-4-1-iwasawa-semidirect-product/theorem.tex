Assume that $K_\infty / K$ is a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension satisfying condition IW with exactly $s = 1$ ramified prime. Let $M_n$ be the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension of $K_n$, and let $M_\infty = \bigcup M_n$. Set

$$G = \operatorname{Gal}(M_\infty / K), \quad G_n = \operatorname{Gal}(M_\infty / K_n).$$

Let $I$ be the inertia group of the unique ramified prime in $G$. Then:

(i) $G$ is the semidirect product $G = G_1 \rtimes I$, and the restriction homomorphism gives an isomorphism $I \xrightarrow{\sim} \operatorname{Gal}(K_\infty / K) \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$.

(ii) The commutator subgroup $[G, G]$ satisfies $[G, G] = G_1$, so $G_1$ is the commutator subgroup of $G$.

(iii) There is an exact sequence
$$1 \to G_1 \to G \to \mathbb{Z}_p \to 1$$
which splits via the inertia group $I$.
