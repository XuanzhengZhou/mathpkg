Theorem 1.4 establishes that the sup norm of a $p$-adic measure $\mu$ on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ equals the sup norm of its associated power series $f(X) = \int (1+X)^x \, d\mu(x)$. This is a foundational result confirming that the correspondence between measures and power series is an isometry with respect to the sup norm, which is crucial for the analytic theory of $p$-adic $L$-functions developed in later chapters.
