Any step function on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ that is constant on cosets of $p^n \mathbb{Z}_p$ can be expanded as a finite Fourier series in terms of the $p^n$-th roots of unity. The Fourier coefficients are given by the standard discrete Fourier transform formula. This expansion is a fundamental tool for computing integrals of step functions against $p$-adic measures, as the integral of a character $\zeta^x$ against a measure yields the value of the associated power series at $\zeta - 1$, via Theorem 1.2.
