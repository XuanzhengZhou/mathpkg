The Mean One formula describes how the operation of multiplying a measure by the function $(1+x)^x$ translates to an operation on the associated power series. Specifically, if $\mu$ has associated power series $f(X)$, then the modified measure $\nu$ (obtained by weighting with $(1+x)^x$) has associated power series $g(X) = f(X [+] x)$, where $X [+] x$ is the formal group addition. This is the second entry in the dictionary between operations on measures and operations on power series.
