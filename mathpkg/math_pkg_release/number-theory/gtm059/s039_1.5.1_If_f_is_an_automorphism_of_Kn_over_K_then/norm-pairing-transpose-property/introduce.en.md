This proposition states the transpose relation between the norm map and the Lubin-Tate endomorphism under the norm residue pairing. It is a fundamental structural property derived from the Lubin-Tate explicit reciprocity law. In the projective limit, this relation allows the definition of the symbol in the limit situation via $T(\mathcal{Z}_k)$, the group of norm-coherent sequences, which is the projective limit of the groups $K_n^\times$ under the norm mapping.
