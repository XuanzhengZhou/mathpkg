Let $K_n$ be the $n$-th layer of the Lubin-Tate tower over a local field $K$. Let $N_{k,m}$ denote the norm from $K_k$ to $K_m$ for $m \leq k$. Then under the norm residue pairing $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle_n$, the norm map $N_{k,m}$ is the transpose of the endomorphism $[\pi^{m+1}]$:

$$\langle N_{k,m}(a), b \rangle_m = \langle a, [\pi^{m+1}](b) \rangle_k$$

for $a \in K_k^\times$ and $b \in A_{k,m}$, where $A_{k,m}$ denotes the appropriate Lubin-Tate module.
