This lemma records algebraic vanishing relations in the context of Lubin-Tate formal groups over a $p$-adic field. It states that certain squares involving the unit $v_k$ and its translates vanish, with conditions depending on whether $p$ is odd and on the $\pi$-adic valuation of the variable $x$. These identities are used in subsequent computations with the formal group logarithm and endomorphism ring.
