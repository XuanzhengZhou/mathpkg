This lemma establishes the convergence of the inverse exponential map $e(X)$ (the formal inverse of the logarithm $\lambda$) on a specific $p$-adic disc. The convergence radius is determined by the condition $\operatorname{ord}_\pi z > 1/(q-1)$, where $q$ is the order of the residue field. The lemma shows that $\lambda$ and $e$ are mutual inverses on this disc and preserve the $\pi$-adic valuation, providing a full formal group isomorphism between $A(D)$ and the additive group on the disc.
