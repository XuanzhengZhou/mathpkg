Let $A$ be a Lubin-Tate formal group over the ring of integers $\mathfrak{o}$ of a local field $K$ of characteristic $0$, with Frobenius power series $[\pi](X)$. Define

$$\lambda(X) = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\pi^n} [\pi^n](X).$$

This limit exists (in the sense that for each $k$, the coefficient of $X^k$ converges $p$-adically as $n \to \infty$) and gives a formal isomorphism

$$\lambda: A \xrightarrow{\sim} G_a$$

from the Lubin-Tate formal group to the additive formal group $G_a$. That is, $\lambda(X +_A Y) = \lambda(X) + \lambda(Y)$.
