Lubin-Tate formal groups provide an explicit construction of local class field theory. They generalize the multiplicative formal group $\hat{\mathbb{G}}_m$ (which corresponds to the cyclotomic extension) to arbitrary local fields. The torsion points of a Lubin-Tate formal group generate totally ramified abelian extensions, and the associated Artin reciprocity map gives an isomorphism between the norm group and the Galois group. This theory is crucial for constructing the $p$-adic $L$-function and establishing explicit reciprocity laws.
