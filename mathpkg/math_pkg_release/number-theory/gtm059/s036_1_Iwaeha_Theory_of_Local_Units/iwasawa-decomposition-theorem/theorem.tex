For each even character $\chi \neq 1$ of the Galois group $\mathrm{Gal}(\mathbb{Q}_p(\zeta_{p^\infty})/\mathbb{Q}_p) \cong \mathbb{Z}_p^\times$, let $V(\chi)$ denote the $\chi$-component of the Iwasawa module $V = \varprojlim V_n$. Then
$$V(\chi) = \mathbb{Z}_p[\Gamma] \cdot u(\chi),$$
where $\Gamma \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$ is the Galois group of the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, $\mathbb{Z}_p[\Gamma] \cong \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$ is the Iwasawa algebra, and $u(\chi)$ is a certain element constructed from norm-compatible cyclotomic units. In other words, each even $\chi$-component $V(\chi)$ is a cyclic $\mathbb{Z}_p[\Gamma]$-module generated by a single element.

The associated power series $f(X)$ for the generating element $u$ satisfies
$$f(X) = \frac{X}{1 + F(X)} - 1$$
for some $F(X) \in \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$.
