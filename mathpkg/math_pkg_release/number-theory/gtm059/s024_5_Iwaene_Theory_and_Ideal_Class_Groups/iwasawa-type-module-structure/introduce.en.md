Theorem 1.3 extends the structure theorem for finitely generated torsion modules over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ to the class of modules of Iwasawa type. The essential content is that the asymptotic growth formula for the $p$-exponents $e_k = \operatorname{ord}_p(|V/(y^{p^k}-1)V|)$ established in Theorem 1.2 continues to hold, up to an additive constant $v_k$. This result is crucial for applying the structure theory to modules arising from ideal class groups in $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions, where the Iwasawa type condition is satisfied.
