The \text{Bernoulli polynomials} $B_k(X)$ are defined by the expansion
$$
\frac{t e^{tX}}{e^t - 1} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} B_k(X) \frac{t^k}{k!}.
$$
The Bernoulli numbers are the constant terms:
$$
B_k = B_k(0).
$$
The first few polynomials are
$$
B_0(X) = 1,\quad B_1(X) = X - \frac{1}{2},\quad B_2(X) = X^2 - X + \frac{1}{6}.
$$
