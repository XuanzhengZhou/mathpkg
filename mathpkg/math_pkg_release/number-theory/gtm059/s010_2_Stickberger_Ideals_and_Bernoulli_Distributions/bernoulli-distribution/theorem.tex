On the projective system $\{\mathbb{Z}/M\mathbb{Z}\}$ (ordered by divisibility), define for $x \in \mathbb{Z}/M\mathbb{Z}$:
$$
E_k^{(M)}(x) = M^{k-1} B_k\!\left(\left\langle \frac{x}{M} \right\rangle\right)
$$
where $B_k$ is the $k$-th Bernoulli polynomial and $\langle \cdot \rangle$ denotes the fractional part. Then the family $\{E_k^{(M)}\}$ forms an ordinary distribution on $\{\mathbb{Z}/M\mathbb{Z}\}$, called the \text{Bernoulli distribution} of degree $k$. It takes values in $\mathbb{Q}$.
