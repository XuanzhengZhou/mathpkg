This lemma establishes the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-rank and freeness properties of the $\chi$-eigenspaces $U_n(\chi)$ of the local principal units at the $n$-th level of the cyclotomic tower. For a nontrivial character $\chi \neq 1$, the eigenspace has rank exactly $p^n$ over $\mathbb{Z}_p$. Moreover, when $\chi$ is distinct from the Teichm\"uller character $\omega$, the eigenspace is a free $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module. These structural facts are essential for the subsequent analysis of the projective limit $U = \varprojlim U_n$ and for Iwasawa's celebrated theorem on the structure of $U(\chi)$ as a module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda$.
