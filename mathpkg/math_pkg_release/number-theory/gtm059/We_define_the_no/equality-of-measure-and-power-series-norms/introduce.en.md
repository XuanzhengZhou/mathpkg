Theorem 1.4 in Lang's treatment of operations on measures and power series establishes that the sup norm of a $p$-adic measure equals the sup norm of the associated point (the collection of values on cosets). This equality is fundamental because it allows one to freely pass between the measure-theoretic and power-series-theoretic viewpoints while preserving norm estimates, which is essential for the analytic continuation of the $p$-adic $L$-function.
