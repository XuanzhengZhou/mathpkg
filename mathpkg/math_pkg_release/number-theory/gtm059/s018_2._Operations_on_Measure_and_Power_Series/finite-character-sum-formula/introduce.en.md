This lemma provides an explicit formula for the measure $p_b$ (or $E_X$) associated to a character $b$ of finite order on $\mathbb{Z}_p$. When $b$ has conductor $N$ (a power of $p$) and we fix a primitive $N$th root of unity $\zeta$, the associated measure can be expressed via a finite Gauss-type sum $S_{X,b} = \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} b(X^n)$ divided by $N$. The lemma also gives a transformation rule for the associated power series $g_{X,f}$ when composed with a rational function $f$, generalizing the unitization operation from the previous lemma to characters of finite order.
