Let $K$ be a number field containing the $p$-th roots of unity and let $K_{\infty}/K$ be the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. Define:
\begin{itemize}
\item $M(K)$ = the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension of $K$ (unramified outside $p$).
\item $L(K)$ = the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension of $K$.
\end{itemize}
Then $M(K)/L(K)$ is controlled by the $p$-adic units via Kummer theory.
