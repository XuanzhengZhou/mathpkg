Let $K_n$ be the $n$-th layer of the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, let $Q_n$ be the maximal $p$-abelian extension of $K_n$, and let $D_n$ be the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension of $K_n$. Then the Galois groups $\operatorname{Gal}(Q_n/K_n)$ and $\operatorname{Gal}(D_n/K_n)$ are $\Lambda$-modules, where $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$ is the Iwasawa algebra.
