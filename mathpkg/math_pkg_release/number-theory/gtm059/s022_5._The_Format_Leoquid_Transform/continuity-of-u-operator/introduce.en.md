This lemma establishes that the projection operator $U$, defined by averaging over $p$-th roots of unity, is bounded with respect to the Leopold norm. The operator $U$ plays a central role in Iwasawa theory, as it allows one to decompose the Leopold space into eigenspaces under the action of the Galois group. The boundedness $\|U(f)\|_p \le \|f\|_p$ ensures that $U$ acts continuously on the Banach algebra $\mathcal{L}$.
