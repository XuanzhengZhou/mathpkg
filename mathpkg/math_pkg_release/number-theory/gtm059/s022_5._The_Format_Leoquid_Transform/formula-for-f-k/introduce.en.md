This theorem relates the Leopoldt transform $f^*(k)$ of a power series $f \in \mathcal{L}$ to the action of the projection operator $U$ at $X = 0$. The formula expresses $f^*(k)$ as $f(0)$ minus the average of $f$ evaluated at $\zeta - 1$ over all $p$-th roots of unity. This result is essential for connecting the Leopoldt transform with $p$-adic $L$-functions, as it shows that the transform of $f$ at integer arguments can be recovered from the action of the $U$-operator.
