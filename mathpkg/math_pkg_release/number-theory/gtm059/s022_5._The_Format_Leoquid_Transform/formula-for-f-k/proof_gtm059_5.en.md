The power series for $U(f)$ in terms of $X$ or $T$ have the same constant term. Hence

$$\Gamma(U(f)) = U(f)(0).$$

Taking $k = 0$, the theorem follows from Theorem 6.3 and the fact that $\zeta - 1$ lies in the domain of convergence of $f$ by Theorem 6.1.
