This theorem states that the Galois group $G$ of the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension over a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension $K_\infty$ is finitely generated as a module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[\![X]\!]$. The rank of $G/XG \cong G/G^{\gamma-1}$ is expressed in terms of the global degree and the Leopoldt defect. Under the Leopoldt conjecture, this rank simplifies to $r_2$, providing a structural link between the arithmetic of the base field and the module theory over $\Lambda$.
