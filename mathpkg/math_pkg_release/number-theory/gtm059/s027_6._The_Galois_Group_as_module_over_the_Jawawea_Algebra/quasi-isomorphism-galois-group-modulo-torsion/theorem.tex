Assume that $K_0$ is totally imaginary, and that each $K_n$ satisfies the Leopoldt conjecture, namely
$$r_p(K_n) = r_2(K_n) \quad \text{for all } n.$$
Then there is a quasi-isomorphism of $\Lambda$-modules
$$G/G_{\mathrm{tor}} \sim \Lambda^{r_2},$$
where $G_{\mathrm{tor}}$ is the $\Lambda$-torsion submodule of $G$.
