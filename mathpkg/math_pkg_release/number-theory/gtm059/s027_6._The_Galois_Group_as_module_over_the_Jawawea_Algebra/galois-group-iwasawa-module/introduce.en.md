The Galois group of the maximal unramified $p$-abelian extension of a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension carries a natural structure as a module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda$. Via class field theory, this module is closely related to the projective limit of the $p$-parts of the ideal class groups. Theorem 6.1 establishes that this Galois group $X$ is a finitely generated torsion $\Lambda$-module, which is the starting point for deeper structural results connecting Galois groups to $p$-adic $L$-functions.
