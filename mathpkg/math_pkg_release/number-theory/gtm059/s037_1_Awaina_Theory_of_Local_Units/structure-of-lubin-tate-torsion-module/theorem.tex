Let $K$ be a complete discretely valued field with ring of integers $\mathfrak{o}$, uniformizer $\pi$, and residue field of cardinality $q$. Let $F_f$ be a Lubin-Tate formal group and $A_n = \ker[\pi^n]_{F_f}$.

(i) $A_n$ is a free $1$-dimensional module over $\mathfrak{o}/\pi^n\mathfrak{o}$. In particular, $\operatorname{End}_{\mathfrak{o}}(A_n) \cong \mathfrak{o}/\pi^n\mathfrak{o}$ and $\operatorname{Aut}_{\mathfrak{o}}(A_n) \cong (\mathfrak{o}/\pi^n\mathfrak{o})^*$.

(ii) The field $K(A_n)$ is an abelian, totally ramified extension of $K$, and there is a natural isomorphism
$$
\chi: \operatorname{Gal}(K(A_n)/K) \xrightarrow{\cong} (\mathfrak{o}/\pi^n\mathfrak{o})^*.
$$

(iii) $[K(A_n) : K] = q^{n-1}(q-1)$.
