Theorem 2.1 is the main structure theorem for Lubin-Tate torsion points, establishing the local Kronecker-Weber theorem (or more precisely, the local class field theory for totally ramified extensions). Part (i) shows that $A_n$ has the same module structure as the group of $p^n$-th roots of unity in the multiplicative case. Part (ii) exhibits the Galois group of $K(A_n)/K$ as $(\mathfrak{o}/\pi^n)^*$, giving an explicit construction of the totally ramified abelian extensions. This is the formal group analog of adjoining roots of unity to obtain cyclotomic extensions.
