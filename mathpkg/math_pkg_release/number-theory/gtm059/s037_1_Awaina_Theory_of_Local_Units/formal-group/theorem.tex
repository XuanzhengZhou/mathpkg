Let $\mathfrak{o}$ be a commutative ring. A \textbf{formal group} over $\mathfrak{o}$ is a formal power series
$$
F(X,Y) \in \mathfrak{o}[[X,Y]]
$$
in two variables satisfying the following three conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $F(X,Y) = X + Y \pmod{\deg 2}$.
\item $F(F(X,Y), Z) = F(X, F(Y,Z))$ (associativity).
\item $F(X,Y) = F(Y,X)$ (commutativity).
\end{enumerate}
