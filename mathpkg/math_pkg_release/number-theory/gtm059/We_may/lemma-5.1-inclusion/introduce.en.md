Lemma 5.1 concerns the structure of the Lubin-Tate formal group and its associated modules. It states an inclusion relation between the set $A^*(u)_{k \times n}$ and the maximal ideal $m_n$, where $A^*(u)$ denotes elements arising from the Lubin-Tate group law. This lemma is recalled in the context of applying the logarithm to study the local symbol pairing on formal groups.
