The proof follows from the structure of the Lubin-Tate formal group. The set $A^*(u)_{k \times n}$ consists of elements arising from the formal group law applied to elements with certain divisibility properties. By the definition of the maximal ideal $m_n$, any such element lies in $m_n$ as a consequence of the convergence properties of the formal group logarithm.
