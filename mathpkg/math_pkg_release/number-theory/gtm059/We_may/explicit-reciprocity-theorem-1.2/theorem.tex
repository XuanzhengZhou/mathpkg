Let $\alpha \in A_f(\pi)$ and $x \in K_n$. Suppose that $N_n \alpha = N_{n,m} a_n \in A_f(\pi)$ for some $m$. Under the conditions (i) and (ii) of Condition (0), the symbol $[x, a]_n$ lies in $\mathfrak{p}^{n+1}$ and we have the equality

$$
\langle x, \alpha \rangle_n = \left[ x, N_n \alpha \right]_{A_f(\pi)}
$$

where $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle_n$ is the local norm residue symbol and $[\cdot, \cdot]_{A_f(\pi)}$ is the bracket symbol associated with the Lubin-Tate formal group $A_f(\pi)$.
