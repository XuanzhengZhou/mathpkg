The orthogonal complement with respect to the trace pairing generalizes the notion of the inverse different. For an ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ in $K_i$, the set $\mathfrak{a}^*$ consists of all elements whose trace products with elements of $\mathfrak{a}$ land in the ring of integers. This construction is essential for establishing the duality between the local symbol pairing and the trace pairing, and appears naturally in the proof of explicit reciprocity laws.
