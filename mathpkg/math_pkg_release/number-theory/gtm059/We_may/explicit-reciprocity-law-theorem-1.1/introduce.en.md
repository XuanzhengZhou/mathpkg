Theorem 1.1 is the fundamental explicit reciprocity law in Lang's treatment. It expresses the local norm residue symbol $\langle u_i, u_j \rangle_n$ directly in terms of the norm $N_1^{n+1}(u_i)$ modulo powers of the prime ideal. This theorem generalizes classical results of Artin-Hasse and was proved in full generality by Iwasawa. The proof uses the Lubin-Tate formal group formalism and the logarithm map to bridge between the multiplicative and additive structures.
