LS 7 is one of the key formulas in Lang's development of explicit reciprocity laws using the Lubin-Tate formalism. It expresses the local symbol pairing in terms of the duality induced by the trace pairing and the logarithm map. The formula establishes a connection between the local norm residue symbol and the additive trace pairing through the logarithm of the Lubin-Tate formal group.
