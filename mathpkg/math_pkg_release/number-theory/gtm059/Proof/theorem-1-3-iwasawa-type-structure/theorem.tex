Assume that $V$ is a compact $\Lambda$-module of Iwasawa type. Then the conclusions of Theorem 1.2 remain valid, except that in part (i) of Theorem 1.2 one has

$$e_k = b_k + w_k$$

where $w_k$ is a constant (independent of the layer index). More precisely, there exists an integer $n_0$ such that for all $n \geq n_0$,

$$p^{e_n} = p^{b n + c} \cdot (\text{constant factor})$$

where the constant factor depends only on the finite torsion submodule of $V$.
