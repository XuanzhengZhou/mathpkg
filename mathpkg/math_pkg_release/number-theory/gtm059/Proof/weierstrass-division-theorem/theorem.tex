Let $A$ be a complete local ring with maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$. Let

$$f(X) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_k X^k \in A[[X]]$$

be a power series such that $a_0, \dots, a_{n-1} \in \mathfrak{m}$ and $a_n$ is a unit in $A$ (that is, $f$ is $\mathfrak{m}$-regular of order $n$). Then for any power series $g(X) \in A[[X]]$, there exists a unique pair $(q, r)$ with $q \in A[[X]]$ and $r \in A[X]$ a polynomial of degree $< n$ such that

$$g(X) = q(X) f(X) + r(X).$$

Furthermore, $q$ and $r$ depend continuously on $g$ in the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology.
