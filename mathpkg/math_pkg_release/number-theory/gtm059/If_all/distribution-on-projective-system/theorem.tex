Let $\{X_n\}$ be a projective system of finite sets with connecting maps $\pi_{n,m}: X_n \to X_m$. A \textbf{distribution} on $\{X_n\}$ is a family of maps
$$\varphi_n : X_n \to V$$
where $V$ is an abelian group (or vector space), satisfying the \textbf{compatibility condition}:
$$\varphi_m(x) = \sum_{\substack{y \in X_n \\ \pi_{n,m}(y) = x}} \varphi_n(y)$$
for all $n \geq m$ and all $x \in X_m$. Equivalently, a distribution defines a functional $d\varphi$ on the space $S(X)$ of locally constant functions on the projective limit $X = \varprojlim X_n$.
