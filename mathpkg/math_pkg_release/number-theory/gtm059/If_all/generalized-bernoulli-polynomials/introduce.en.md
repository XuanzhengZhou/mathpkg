# Generalized Bernoulli Polynomials

Generalized Bernoulli polynomials $B_{k,f}(X)$ are built from the classical Bernoulli polynomials by averaging with respect to a function $f$ on $\mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z}$, typically a Dirichlet character. They are the polynomial analogues of the generalized Bernoulli numbers of Leopoldt, which appear in formulas for values of Dirichlet $L$-functions at negative integers. In the context of cyclotomic fields, generalized Bernoulli numbers are connected to the minus part of the ideal class group and to the Stickelberger ideal, providing the bridge between Bernoulli numbers and the arithmetic of cyclotomic fields.
