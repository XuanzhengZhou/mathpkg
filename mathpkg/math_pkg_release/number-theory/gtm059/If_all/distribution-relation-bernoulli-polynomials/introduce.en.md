# Distribution Relation for Bernoulli Polynomials

This fundamental identity, $B_k(X) = N^{k-1} \sum_{a=0}^{N-1} B_k(\frac{X+a}{N})$, is the key property that makes Bernoulli polynomials compatible with the structure of a distribution on a projective system. Multiplying both sides by $N^{k-1}$ yields the compatibility condition required for a distribution on the system $\{\frac{1}{M}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}\}$. This relation can be interpreted as saying that the assignment $x \mapsto M^{k-1} B_k(x)$ defines a distribution, which is the Bernoulli distribution central to the construction of $p$-adic $L$-functions.
