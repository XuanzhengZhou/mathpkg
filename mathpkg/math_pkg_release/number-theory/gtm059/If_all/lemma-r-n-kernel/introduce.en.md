# Lemma on the Kernel of $T$ and $R$

This lemma asserts that elements in the kernel of the trace map $T$ necessarily belong to the subgroup $R$ of the group ring. This inclusion is a key step in determining the index of the Stickelberger ideal, as it establishes one direction of the chain of containment relations among the subgroups that appear in the index computation diagram. The result relies on properties of characters on the group ring and their conductors.
