This lemma provides a $p$-adic congruence for factorials in terms of the base-$p$ digits of the argument. It is a key technical ingredient in the proof of Stickelberger's theorem, allowing one to simplify expressions involving factorials and the $p$-adic valuation. The general form reduces a factorial modulo $p$ to a product of factorials of the base-$p$ digits, times a sign factor determined by the $p$-adic valuation.
