Let $G = \operatorname{Gal}(\mathbf{Q}(\zeta_m)/\mathbf{Q}) \cong (\mathbf{Z}/m\mathbf{Z})^\times$ with $m > 1$ odd or divisible by $4$. Let $R = \mathbf{Z}[G]$ be the integral group ring. Let $e = \frac{1}{2}(1 - c)$ where $c \in G$ denotes complex conjugation. Then
$$R = (1-e)R \oplus eR$$
and the projection $x \mapsto (1-e)x$ maps $R$ onto the minus part $R^- = (1-e)R$.
