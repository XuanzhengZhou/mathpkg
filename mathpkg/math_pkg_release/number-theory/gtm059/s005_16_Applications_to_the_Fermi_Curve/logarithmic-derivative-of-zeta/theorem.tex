Taking the logarithmic derivative of the zeta function $Z(V,T)$ yields the identity
$$\frac{d}{dT} \log Z(V,T) = \sum_{\nu=1}^{\infty} N_\nu \, T^{\nu-1},$$
or equivalently,
$$T \frac{Z'(V,T)}{Z(V,T)} = \sum_{\nu=1}^{\infty} N_\nu \, T^{\nu}.$$
This expresses the generating function of point counts $N_\nu$ directly in terms of the logarithmic derivative of $Z(V,T)$.
