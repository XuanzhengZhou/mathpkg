This result determines the precise index of the Stickelberger ideal $S$ inside the minus part $R^-$ of the group ring. The index equals $w$, the number of roots of unity in $\mathbf{Q}(\zeta_m)$, which connects the arithmetic of the cyclotomic field to the structure of the Stickelberger ideal. The proof uses a homomorphism that extracts the first coefficient of a group ring element modulo $\mathbf{Z}$.
