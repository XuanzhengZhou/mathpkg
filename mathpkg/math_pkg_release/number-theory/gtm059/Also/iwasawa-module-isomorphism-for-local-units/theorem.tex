Let $U = \varprojlim_n U_n$ be the projective limit of the local principal unit groups, and let $\Lambda = \varprojlim_n \mathbb{Z}_p[\mathrm{Gal}(K_n/K)]$ be the Iwasawa algebra. Let $\chi$ be a non-trivial character of the Galois group $G$, and let $U(\chi)$ be the $\chi$-eigenspace of $U$. Then there is a $\Lambda$-module isomorphism
$$U(\chi) \cong \Lambda.$$
In other words, $U(\chi)$ is a free $\Lambda$-module of rank $1$.
