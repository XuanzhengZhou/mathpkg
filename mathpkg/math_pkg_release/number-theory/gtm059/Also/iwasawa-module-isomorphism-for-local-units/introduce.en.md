This theorem is the central structural result in the Iwasawa theory of local units. It states that the $\chi$-eigenspace $U(\chi)$ of the projective limit of local unit groups is isomorphic, as a module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda \cong \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$, to $\Lambda$ itself. Thus $U(\chi)$ is $\Lambda$-free of rank $1$. The proof occupies a substantial portion of the section and proceeds by constructing a canonical $\Lambda$-basis element for $U(\chi)$ using Galois cohomology and class field theory, analogous to the global case.
