This lemma describes the structure of the $\chi$-eigenspaces $U_n(\chi)$ of the local principal unit group $U_n$ under the action of the Galois group $G_n$. For a non-trivial character $\chi$, the eigenspace $U_n(\chi)$ is shown to be a free $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module whose rank equals $p^n$. This structural result is a key ingredient in proving that the projective limit $U = \varprojlim U_n$ has no $\mathbb{Z}_p$-torsion and is a free module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda$.
