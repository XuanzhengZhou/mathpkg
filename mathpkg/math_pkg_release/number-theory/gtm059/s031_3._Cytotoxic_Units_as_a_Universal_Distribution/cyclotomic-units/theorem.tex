Let $p$ be a prime number. The group of \emph{cyclotomic units} is the subgroup $A$ of the unit group of the cyclotomic field $\mathbb{Q}(\mu_{p^\infty})$ generated by the $p^n$-th roots of unity (all $n \geq 1$) and by the elements $\zeta - 1$, where $\zeta$ runs over all $p^n$-th roots of unity with $\zeta \neq 1$.
