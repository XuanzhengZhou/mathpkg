Let $p$ be a prime and let $\zeta$ denote a primitive $p^n$-th root of unity ($n \geq 1$). The cyclotomic units satisfy the following distribution relations:

\textbf{CU 1.} For $p \neq 2$, the norm from level $n$ to level $n-1$:
$$
N_{\mathbb{Q}(\mu_{p^n})/\mathbb{Q}(\mu_{p^{n-1}})}(1 - \zeta_{p^n}) = 1 - \zeta_{p^{n-1}}.
$$

\textbf{CU 2.} The product relation:
$$
\prod_{\substack{\zeta^{p}=1 \\ \zeta \neq 1}} (\zeta - 1) = p.
$$

More generally, for any $n \geq 1$:
$$
\prod_{\substack{\zeta^{p^n}=1 \\ \zeta \neq 1}} (\zeta - 1) = p^{p^{n-1}}.
$$
