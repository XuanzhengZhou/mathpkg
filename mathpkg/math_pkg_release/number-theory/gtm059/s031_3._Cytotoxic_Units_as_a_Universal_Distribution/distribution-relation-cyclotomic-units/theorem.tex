Let $V$ be the factor group of the group of cyclotomic units by the roots of unity. The association
$$
a \longmapsto g^n_p \in V
$$
(where $g^n_p$ denotes the cyclotomic $p$-units at level $n$) satisfies the universal distribution relation: for $a \neq 0$,
$$
\prod_{\substack{b \\ b^p = a}} f(b) = f(a)
$$
for the distribution $f$ defined on $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$ with values in $V$.
