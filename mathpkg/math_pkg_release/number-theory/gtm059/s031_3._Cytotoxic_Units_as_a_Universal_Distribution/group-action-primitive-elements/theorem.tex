Let $Z_k^* = \operatorname{Gal}(\mathbb{Q}(\mu_{p^k})/\mathbb{Q})$ be the Galois group of the $p^k$-th cyclotomic field. Then $Z_k^*$ acts simply transitively on the set of primitive elements in the cyclotomic unit group $V_k$, and there is a unique homomorphism
$$
Z_k^* \longrightarrow \operatorname{Aut}(V_k)
$$
which is an isomorphism onto its image.
