Let $G$ be the Galois group as a module over the Iwasawa algebra $\mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$, with structure decomposition $G = A^r \times G_{\text{tor}}$ given by the structure theorem. Then the invariants satisfy $t = r_B$, where $t$ is the number of torsion components and $r_B$ is the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-rank of the base field extension.
