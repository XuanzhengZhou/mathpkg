Since $X$ is a finitely generated torsion $\Lambda$-module by Theorem 6.1, the structure theorem for such modules applies directly. The proof proceeds by constructing the projective limit of the Artin reciprocity maps and verifying that the limit module satisfies the hypotheses of the structure theorem. The distinguished polynomials appearing in the decomposition correspond to the characteristic power series associated to the Iwasawa module.
