Applying the structure theorem for finitely generated $\Lambda$-modules to the Galois group $X$, one obtains a pseudo-isomorphism decomposition. The characteristic polynomial of $X$ is a fundamental invariant that encodes both the $\mu$ and $\lambda$ invariants. The main conjecture of Iwasawa theory (now a theorem of Mazur-Wiles) identifies this characteristic ideal with the ideal generated by the $p$-adic $L$-function, providing a deep link between Galois groups and special values of $L$-functions.
