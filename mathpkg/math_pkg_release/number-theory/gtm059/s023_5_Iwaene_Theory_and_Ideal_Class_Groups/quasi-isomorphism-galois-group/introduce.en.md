Theorem 5.1 provides a quasi-isomorphism description of the Galois group $G(K)$ of the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension. It expresses $G(K)$ as a quotient of local principal units by the closure of global units, and relates its $\mathbb{Z}_p$-rank to the Leopoldt conjecture. This theorem is crucial for setting up the Iwasawa module $X_{\infty} = \varprojlim G(K_n)$ in a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension.
