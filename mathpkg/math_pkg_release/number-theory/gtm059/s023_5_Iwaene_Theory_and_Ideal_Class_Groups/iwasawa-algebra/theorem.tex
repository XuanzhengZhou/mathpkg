Let $p$ be a prime and let $\Gamma \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$ be a topological group isomorphic to the additive group of $p$-adic integers. Let $\gamma$ be a topological generator of $\Gamma$. The Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[\Gamma]]$ is defined as the completed group ring

$$\Lambda = \varprojlim_n \mathbb{Z}_p[\Gamma/\Gamma^{p^n}].$$

Under the isomorphism sending $\gamma - 1$ to the variable $X$, we have $\Lambda \cong \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$, the ring of formal power series in one variable over $\mathbb{Z}_p$.
