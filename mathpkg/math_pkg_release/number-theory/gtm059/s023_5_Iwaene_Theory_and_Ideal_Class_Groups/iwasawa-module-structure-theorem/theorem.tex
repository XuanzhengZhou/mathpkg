Let $M$ be a finitely generated torsion $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$-module. There exists a $\Lambda$-module homomorphism with finite kernel and cokernel:

$$M \to \Lambda^r \oplus \bigoplus_{i=1}^{s} \Lambda/(p^{m_i}) \oplus \bigoplus_{j=1}^{t} \Lambda/(f_j(X)^{n_j})$$

where $r \geq 0$, each $f_j(X)$ is a distinguished irreducible polynomial, and $m_i, n_j$ are positive integers. The polynomial $\prod f_j(X)^{n_j}$ is called the characteristic polynomial of $M$.
