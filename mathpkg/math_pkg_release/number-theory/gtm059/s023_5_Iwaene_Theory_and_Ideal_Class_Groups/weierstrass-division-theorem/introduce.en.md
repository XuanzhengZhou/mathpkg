The Weierstrass Division Theorem is a fundamental result for power series over complete local rings. It states that division by a power series $f$ with a unit leading coefficient (after the first $n$ coefficients lie in the maximal ideal) yields a unique quotient and remainder of degree less than $n$. This theorem is the foundation for the Weierstrass Preparation Theorem and the structure theory of finitely generated modules over the Iwasawa algebra.
