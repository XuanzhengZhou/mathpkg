Let $A$ be a complete local ring with maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$. Let
$$f(X) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_k X^k \in A[[X]]$$
be a power series such that $a_0, \dots, a_{n-1} \in \mathfrak{m}$ and $a_n$ is a unit in $A$. Then for any $g \in A[[X]]$, there exist unique $q \in A[[X]]$ and unique $r \in A[X]$ with $\deg r < n$ such that
$$g = qf + r.$$
Moreover, if $g$ and $f$ have coefficients in $A$, then $q$ and $r$ also have coefficients in $A$.
