This theorem establishes the fundamental identification of the Iwasawa algebra with the power series ring $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$. The proof uses the fact that under the map sending $\gamma \mapsto 1+X$, the elements $h_n = (1+X)^{p^n} - 1$ form a system of distinguished polynomials with intersection zero in the maximal ideal $(p,X)$, giving an isomorphism between the projective limit and the power series ring. This identification is essential for all structure theorems about Iwasawa modules.
