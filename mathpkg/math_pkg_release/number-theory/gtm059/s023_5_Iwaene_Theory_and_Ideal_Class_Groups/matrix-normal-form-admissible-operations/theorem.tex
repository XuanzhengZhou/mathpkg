Let $R$ be a matrix of relations for a finitely generated $\Lambda$-module. Then $R$ can be transformed by a finite number of admissible operations (O1, O2, O3) into a diagonal matrix of the form
$$\begin{pmatrix}
f_1 & 0 & \dots & 0 & 0 \\
0 & f_2 & \dots & 0 & 0 \\
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\
0 & 0 & \dots & f_r & 0 \\
0 & 0 & \dots & 0 & 0
\end{pmatrix}$$
where each $f_i$ is either a power of $p$ or a distinguished polynomial, and $\deg_W(f_1) \le \deg_W(f_2) \le \dots \le \deg_W(f_r)$.
