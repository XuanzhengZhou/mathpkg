Theorem 4.2 relates the $p$-primary ideal class groups $C_n$ in the tower $K_n$ of a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, showing that they fit into a natural quotient structure. This is a key step in constructing the $\Lambda$-module $C_{\infty} = \varprojlim C_n$, whose structure as a $\Lambda$-module (via Theorem 3.1) yields the Iwasawa invariants $\mu$ and $\lambda$ for the growth of class numbers.
