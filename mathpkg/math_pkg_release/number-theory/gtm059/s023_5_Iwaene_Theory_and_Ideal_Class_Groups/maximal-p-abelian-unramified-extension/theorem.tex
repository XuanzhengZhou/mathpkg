Let $K$ be a number field and fix a prime $p$. Define:
\begin{itemize}
\item $M(K)$ = the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension of $K$.
\item $H(K)$ = the $p$-Hilbert class field of $K$ (maximal abelian unramified $p$-extension).
\end{itemize}
The Galois group $\operatorname{Gal}(M(K)/K)$ is isomorphic (via the Artin reciprocity map) to the quotient of the idele class group by the closure of the unit group in the $p$-adic topology.

We denote by $G(K) = \operatorname{Gal}(M(K)/K)$ the Galois group of the maximal abelian unramified $p$-extension.
