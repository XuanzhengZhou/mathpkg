The maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension $M(K)$ of a number field $K$ is the compositum of all abelian $p$-extensions of $K$ that are unramified at all primes. Its Galois group encodes the $p$-primary part of the ideal class group via class field theory and is a fundamental object in Iwasawa theory when studied over $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions.
