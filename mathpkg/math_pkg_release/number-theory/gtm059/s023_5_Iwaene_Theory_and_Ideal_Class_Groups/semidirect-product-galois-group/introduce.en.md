Theorem 4.1 describes the Galois group $G$ of the maximal abelian unramified $p$-extension of the tower $K_{\infty}$ as a semidirect product of the inertia group $I \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$ and the base layer Galois group $G_1$. This structural result is fundamental for Iwasawa's construction of the $\Lambda$-module structure on the projective limit of $p$-class groups.
