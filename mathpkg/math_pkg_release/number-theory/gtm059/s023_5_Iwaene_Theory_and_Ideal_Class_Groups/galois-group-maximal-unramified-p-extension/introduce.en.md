The Galois group $G(K)$ of the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension encodes all unramified abelian $p$-extensions of $K$. Via class field theory, it is expressed as a quotient of the idele class group. Its $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module structure (specifically its rank) is related to the Leopoldt conjecture. In Iwasawa theory, one studies the behavior of $G(K_n)$ in a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension $K_{\infty}/K$.
