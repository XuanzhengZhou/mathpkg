Prove that $2^{37} - 1$ is not prime. [In testing a given prime it is not necessary to find $d$ but only to determine whether the prime divides $2^{37} - 1$. To do this you need not find the remainders left by all the numbers $1, 2, 4, 8, 16, \ldots$ since it suffices to find the remainders left by $2, 2^2, 2^4, 2^8, 2^{16}, 2^{32}, 2^{32+4}, 2^{37}$.]
