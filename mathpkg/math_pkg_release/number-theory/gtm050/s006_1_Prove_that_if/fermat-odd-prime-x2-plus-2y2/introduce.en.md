Fermat stated and claimed a rigorous proof that an odd prime is of the form $x^2 + 2y^2$ if and only if it is of the form $8n+1$ or $8n+3$. The necessity (only if) direction is the simpler half; the sufficiency is much more difficult and is addressed in later exercises.
