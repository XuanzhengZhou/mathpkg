Using the multiplication formula for surds:

$$
\begin{aligned}
(p + q\sqrt{-3})(p - q\sqrt{-3})
&= p \cdot p + p \cdot (-q\sqrt{-3}) + q\sqrt{-3} \cdot p + q\sqrt{-3} \cdot (-q\sqrt{-3}) \\
&= p^2 - pq\sqrt{-3} + pq\sqrt{-3} - q^2(\sqrt{-3})^2 \\
&= p^2 - q^2(-3) \\
&= p^2 + 3q^2.
\end{aligned}
$$
