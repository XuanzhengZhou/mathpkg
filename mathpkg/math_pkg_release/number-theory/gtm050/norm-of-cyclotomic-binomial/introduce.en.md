The norm of a cyclotomic binomial $x + \alpha^j y$ is given by the simple rational expression $(x^\lambda + y^\lambda)/(x+y)$ when $j$ is odd. This formula arises from the factorization $x^\lambda + y^\lambda = \prod_{j=0}^{\lambda-1} (x + \alpha^j y)$ and the fact that $x+y = x + \alpha^0 y$. This norm formula is the starting point for Kummer's analysis of the Fermat equation in cyclotomic integers.
