The norm of a cyclotomic integer is the product of all its Galois conjugates. It is always an ordinary (rational) integer and is multiplicative. The norm is the key tool for relating factorization in cyclotomic integers to factorization in ordinary integers. For a binomial $x + \alpha^j y$, the norm is $(x^\lambda + y^\lambda)/(x + y)$.
