Let $\lambda$ denote a prime number and let $\alpha$ denote a complex root other than $1$ of the equation $\alpha^{\lambda} = 1$. A cyclotomic integer is a number of the form
$$a_0 + a_1\alpha + a_2\alpha^2 + \cdots + a_{\lambda-1}\alpha^{\lambda-1}$$
where $a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_{\lambda-1}$ are integers.
