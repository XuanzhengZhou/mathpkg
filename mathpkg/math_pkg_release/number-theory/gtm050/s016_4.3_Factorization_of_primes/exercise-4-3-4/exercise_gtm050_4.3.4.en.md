Find the norms of the following binomials.

(a) When $\lambda = 5$: $x + \alpha y = 1 + \alpha$, $2 + \alpha$, $2 - \alpha$, $3 + \alpha$, $3 - \alpha$, $3 + 2\alpha$, $3 - 2\alpha$, $4 + \alpha$, $5 + 2\alpha$, $5 - 4\alpha$, $7 + \alpha$.

(b) When $\lambda = 7$: $x + \alpha y = 2 \pm \alpha$, $3 \pm \alpha$, $3 \pm 2\alpha$, $4 \pm \alpha$, $4 \pm 3\alpha$, $5 + \alpha$, $5 + 2\alpha$, $5 + 3\alpha$, $5 + 4\alpha$.

[$N(x + \alpha y) = (x^\lambda + y^\lambda) / (x + y)$.]
