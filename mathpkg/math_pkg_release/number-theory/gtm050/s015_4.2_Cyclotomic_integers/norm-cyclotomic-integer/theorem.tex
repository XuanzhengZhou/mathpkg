Let $f(\alpha)$ be a cyclotomic integer and let $f(\alpha), f(\alpha^2), \ldots, f(\alpha^{\lambda-1})$ be its $\lambda-1$ conjugates. The \textbf{norm} of $f(\alpha)$ is defined as the product of all its conjugates:
$$Nf(\alpha) = f(\alpha) \cdot f(\alpha^2) \cdot f(\alpha^3) \cdots f(\alpha^{\lambda-1}).$$
