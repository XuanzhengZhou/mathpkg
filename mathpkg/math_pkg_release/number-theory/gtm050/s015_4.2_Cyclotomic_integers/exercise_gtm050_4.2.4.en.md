Show that when $\lambda = 5$ the norm of any cyclotomic integer is of the form $\frac{1}{4}(A^2 - 5B^2)$ where $A$ and $B$ are integers which are either both odd or both even. [Hint: In the product $f(\alpha)f(\alpha^2)f(\alpha^3)f(\alpha^4)$ multiply $f(\alpha)f(\alpha^4)$ first. The result is of the form $a + b\theta_0 + c\theta_1$ where $\theta_0 = \alpha + \alpha^4$, $\theta_1 = \alpha^2 + \alpha^3$. Then $f(\alpha^2)f(\alpha^3) = a + b\theta_1 + c\theta_0$ and the norm has the form $(b\theta_0 + c\theta_1)(b\theta_1 + c\theta_0)$.] Find an infinite number of units in this case.
