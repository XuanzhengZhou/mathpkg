For a quadratic field with discriminant $D$, the Dirichlet series formed from the norms of divisors factors as an Euler product:
$$\sum_A \frac{1}{N(A)^s} = \prod_P \left(1 - \frac{1}{N(P)^s}\right)^{-1}$$
where the sum is over all divisors $A$ and the product over all prime divisors $P$. This equals $\zeta(s) L(s, \chi_D)$ where $\chi_D(n) = \left(\frac{D}{n}\right)$ is the quadratic character modulo $|D|$.
