If $x^{4m} + y^{4m} = z^{4m}$ had a solution, then setting $X = x^m$, $Y = y^m$, $Z = z^m$ would give $X^4 + Y^4 = Z^4$, contradicting Fermat's Last Theorem for $n = 4$. Thus FLT for exponent $4$ immediately implies FLT for all exponents that are multiples of $4$.
