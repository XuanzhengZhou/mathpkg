Eisenstein polynomials are the hBadic building blocks for totally ramified extensions, analogous to how cyclotomic polynomials give unramified extensions. The polynomial ^e - p$ is an Eisenstein polynomial giving a totally ramified extension of degree $. The general structure of hBadic extensions is: first an unramified extension, then a totally ramified one.
