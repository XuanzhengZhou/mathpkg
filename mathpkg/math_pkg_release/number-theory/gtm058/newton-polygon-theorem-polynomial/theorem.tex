Let $f(X) = 1 + \sum_{i=1}^n a_i X^i \in 1 + X\Omega[X]$ and let its roots (in some extension) be $1/\alpha_1, \ldots, 1/\alpha_n$. If $\lambda_i = \operatorname{ord}_p \alpha_i$, and if the slopes of the Newton polygon of $f(X)$ are $m_1, m_2, \ldots$ (listed with multiplicities), then the $\lambda_i$ are exactly these slopes. In other words, the slopes of the Newton polygon of $f(X)$ are (counting multiplicity) the $p$-adic ordinals of the reciprocal roots of $f(X)$.
