The $p$-adic binomial series generalizes the binomial expansion $(1+X)^a$ to $p$-adic exponents. When $a \in \mathbb{Z}_p$, the coefficients are $p$-adic integers, so the series converges on the open unit disc. This series plays a key role in the construction of the Artin-Hasse exponential and in $p$-adic analysis more generally.
