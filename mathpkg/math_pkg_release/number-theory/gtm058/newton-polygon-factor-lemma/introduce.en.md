Lemma 6 describes how the Newton polygon changes when we multiply by a linear factor $(1-cX)$. The segment from $(0,0)$ to $(1,\operatorname{ord}_p c)$ is prepended, and the rest of the polygon is shifted. This lemma is a crucial building block for the proof of the Weierstrass Preparation Theorem.
