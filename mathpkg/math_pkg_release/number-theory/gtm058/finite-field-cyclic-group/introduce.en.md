The cyclic nature of $\mathbb{F}_q^\times$ is fundamental: it guarantees the existence of primitive $(q-1)$-st roots of unity in the algebraic closure of $\mathbb{F}_p$. This fact is used to construct unramified extensions of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ by adjoining roots of unity of order coprime to $p$.
