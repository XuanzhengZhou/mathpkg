Bernoulli polynomials generalize Bernoulli numbers; (0) = B_k$. They satisfy $\frac{d}{dx}B_k(x) = kB_{k-1}(x)$ and $\int_0^1 B_k(x)dx = 0$ for  \geq 1$. The Bernoulli polynomials are used to construct hBadic Bernoulli distributions, which play a key role in hBadic integration and the construction of the hBadic zeta function.
