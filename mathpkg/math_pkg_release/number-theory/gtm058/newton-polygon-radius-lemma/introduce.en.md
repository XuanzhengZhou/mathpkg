Lemma 5 provides a geometric interpretation of the radius of convergence: the supremum of the slopes of the Newton polygon equals $\log_p r$ where $r$ is the radius. When all slopes are bounded, the series converges inside a finite disc; when slopes grow without bound, the series converges everywhere; when slopes are bounded below, the radius is finite.
