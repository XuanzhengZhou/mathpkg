Ostrowski's Theorem classifies all nontrivial absolute values on $\mathbb{Q}$. The only completions of $\mathbb{Q}$ are $\mathbb{R}$ (for the usual absolute value) and $\mathbb{Q}_p$ (for the hBadic absolute values). This theorem is the starting point of valuation theory and hBadic analysis.
