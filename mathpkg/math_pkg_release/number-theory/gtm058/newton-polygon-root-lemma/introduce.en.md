Lemma 7 is the fundamental result linking Newton polygons to zeros of power series. It proves that the first slope predicts a zero whose norm corresponds to that slope. The proof approximates $f$ by its truncations, uses the polynomial case to find approximate roots, and then takes a limit. This lemma is the $p$-adic analogue of the principle that a power series has a zero inside its disc of convergence.
