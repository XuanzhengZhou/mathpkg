The residue field degree $f$ is the degree of the residue field extension over $\mathbb{F}_p$. Together with the ramification index $e$, the fundamental relation $n = ef$ holds. Unramified extensions have $f=n$, $e=1$; totally ramified extensions have $e=n$, $f=1$.
