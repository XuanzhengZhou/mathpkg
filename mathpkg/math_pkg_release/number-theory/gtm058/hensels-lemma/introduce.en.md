Hensel's Lemma is the hBadic analogue of Newton's method for finding roots. It allows one to lift a simple root modulo $ to an exact root in $\mathbb{Z}_p$. The proof constructs successive approximations $ satisfying (a_n) \equiv 0 \pmod{p^{n+1}}$, converging to the desired hBadic root. This lemma is fundamental for hBadic algebra and analysis.
