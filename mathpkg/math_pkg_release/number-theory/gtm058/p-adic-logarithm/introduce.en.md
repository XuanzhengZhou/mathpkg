The $p$-adic logarithm is defined by the usual Taylor series for $\log(1+x)$. It converges on the open unit disc $D(1^-)$ and satisfies the functional equation $\log_p(xy) = \log_p(x) + \log_p(y)$ on its domain. Together with the $p$-adic exponential, it gives a local isomorphism between the additive and multiplicative groups near 0 and 1 respectively.
