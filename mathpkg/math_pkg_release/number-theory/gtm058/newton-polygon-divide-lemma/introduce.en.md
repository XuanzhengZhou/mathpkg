Lemma 8 shows that dividing a power series by $(1-X/\alpha)$, where $\alpha$ is a root at the expected distance, produces another power series with the same (or better) convergence. This division is expressed as multiplication by the geometric series $\sum_{n=0}^\infty (X/\alpha)^n$, which converges precisely when $|X/\alpha|_p < 1$, i.e., on $D(p^\lambda)$. This lemma is used iteratively in the proof of the Weierstrass Preparation Theorem.
