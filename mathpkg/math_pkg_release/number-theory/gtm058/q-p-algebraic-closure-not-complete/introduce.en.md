Unlike $\mathbb{R}$, whose algebraic closure $\mathbb{C}$ is already complete, the algebraic closure $\bar{\mathbb{Q}}_p$ is not complete. A counterexample is constructed using a Cauchy sequence built from primitive roots of unity of increasing orders. This necessitates the construction of a larger complete field $\Omega$, the hBadic analogue of $\mathbb{C}$.
