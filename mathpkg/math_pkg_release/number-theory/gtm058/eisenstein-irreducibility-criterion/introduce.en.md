The Eisenstein irreducibility criterion gives a sufficient condition for a polynomial over $\mathbb{Z}_p$ to be irreducible over $\mathbb{Q}_p$. It is the hBadic analogue of the classical Eisenstein criterion over $\mathbb{Q}$, and plays a crucial role in the construction of totally ramified extensions of $\mathbb{Q}_p$.
