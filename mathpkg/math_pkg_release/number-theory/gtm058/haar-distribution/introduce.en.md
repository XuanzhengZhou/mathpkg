The Haar distribution is the hBadic analogue of Lebesgue measure. However, unlike classical measures where smaller sets have smaller measure, $|\mu_{\text{Haar}}(a + p^N\mathbb{Z}_p)|_p = p^N$ grows as the interval shrinks. This counterintuitive behavior is characteristic of hBadic distributions and explains why regularization is needed to obtain genuine hBadic measures.
