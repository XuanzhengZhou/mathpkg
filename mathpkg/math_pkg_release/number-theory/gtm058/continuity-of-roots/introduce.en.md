This proposition generalizes the idea that roots of a polynomial vary continuously with its coefficients to the hBadic setting. It is a crucial tool in the proof that $\Omega$ is algebraically closed, where one approximates polynomials over $\Omega$ by polynomials over $\bar{\mathbb{Q}}_p$ whose roots are known.
