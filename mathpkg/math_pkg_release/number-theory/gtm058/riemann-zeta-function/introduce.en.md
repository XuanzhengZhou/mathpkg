The Riemann zeta function is one of the most important functions in number theory. Its values at positive even integers are intimately related to Bernoulli numbers. Chapter II of this book shows that the values of $\zeta$ at negative odd integers can be hBadically interpolated by the Kubota-Leopoldt hBadic zeta function.
