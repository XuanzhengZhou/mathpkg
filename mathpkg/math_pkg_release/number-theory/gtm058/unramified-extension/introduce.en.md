Unramified extensions of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ are obtained by adjoining roots of unity of order coprime to $. They are in one-to-one correspondence with finite extensions of $\mathbb{F}_p$: the degree $ unramified extension corresponds to $\mathbb{F}_{p^f}$ as residue field. The ring of integers is denoted $\mathbb{Z}_p^{\text{unram}}$ and its elements can be represented by Teichmüller representatives.
