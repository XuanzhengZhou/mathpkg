By $n$-dimensional projective space over $F$, denoted $\mathbb{P}_F^n$, we mean the set of equivalence classes of points in $\mathbb{A}_F^{n+1} \setminus \{(0,\ldots,0)\}$ under the equivalence relation $(x_0,\ldots,x_n) \sim (x'_0,\ldots,x'_n) \Leftrightarrow \exists \lambda \in F^\times: x'_i = \lambda x_i$ for $i=0,\ldots,n$.
