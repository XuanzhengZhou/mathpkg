This lemma is the technical heart of the proof that the extended hBadic absolute value satisfies the ultrametric inequality. The proof uses the matrix representation of multiplication by $\gamma$ and bounds on binomial coefficients to show $|(I+A)^N|$ is bounded as  \to \infty$, hence $|1+\gamma|_p \leq 1$.
