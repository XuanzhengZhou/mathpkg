On the disc $D(p^{-1/(p-1)})$, the $p$-adic exponential and logarithm are mutually inverse, establishing a local isomorphism between the multiplicative group near 1 and the additive group near 0. This is the $p$-adic analogue of the classical exponential-logarithm relationship, but restricted to a smaller domain due to the limited convergence of $\exp_p$.
