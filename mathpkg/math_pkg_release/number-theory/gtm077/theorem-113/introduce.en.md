This is Theorem 113 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

The GCD of all ideals in $K$ which contain only numbers in $R_k(\theta)$ is $\mathfrak{F}$, where $\mathfrak{F}\mathfrak{D}_k = \Phi'(\theta)$.

This result is part of the foundational material in algebraic number theory, appearing in the section on Relative Norms of Numbe.
