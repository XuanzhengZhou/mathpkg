Let $\mathfrak{U}$ be a subgroup of an Abelian group $\mathfrak{G}$. The cosets $A\mathfrak{U}$ form a group $\mathfrak{R}$ under the composition
$$(A_1\mathfrak{U}) \cdot (A_2\mathfrak{U}) = (A_1 A_2)\mathfrak{U}.$$
This group is called the factor (quotient) group of $\mathfrak{G}$ by $\mathfrak{U}$, denoted $\mathfrak{R} = \mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{U}$. Its order equals the index of $\mathfrak{U}$ in $\mathfrak{G}$.
