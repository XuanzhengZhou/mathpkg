The translation $\tau - (h/k)$ moves $C(h, k)$ to the left a distance $h/k$, and thereby places its center at $i/(2k^2)$. Multiplication by $-ik^2$ expands the radius to $1/2$ and rotates the circle through $\pi/2$ radians in the negative direction. The expressions for $z_1(h, k)$ and $z_2(h, k)$ follow at once from the images of the tangency points under this composed transformation.
