Two reduced fractions $a/b$ and $c/d$ are said to be **similarly ordered** if $(c - a)(d - b) \geq 0$. Let $a_1/b_1 < a_2/b_2 < \cdots$ denote the Farey fractions in $F_n$.

(a) Prove that any two neighbors $a_i/b_i$ and $a_{i+1}/b_{i+1}$ are similarly ordered.

(b) Prove also that any two second neighbors $a_i/b_i$ and $a_{i+2}/b_{i+2}$ are similarly ordered.

*Note:* Erdos [8] has shown that there is an absolute constant $c > 0$ such that the $k$th neighbors $a_i/b_i$ and $a_{i+k}/b_{i+k}$ in $F_n$ are similarly ordered if $n > ck$.
