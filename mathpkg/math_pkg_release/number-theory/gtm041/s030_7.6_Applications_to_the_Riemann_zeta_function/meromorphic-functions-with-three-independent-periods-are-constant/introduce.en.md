By Theorem 7.13, any meromorphic function with three periods linearly independent over the integers must possess arbitrarily small nonzero periods. As shown in Chapter 1, a function with arbitrarily small nonzero periods is constant on every open connected subset in which it is analytic. Therefore, the only meromorphic functions admitting three independent periods are the constant functions. This provides a structural limitation on the period lattices of non-constant meromorphic functions.
