The modular group $\Gamma$ is generated by the two matrices
$$T = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \quad \text{and} \quad S = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}.$$
That is, every $A \in \Gamma$ can be expressed in the form
$$A = T^{n_1} S T^{n_2} S \cdots T^{n_k}$$
for some integers $n_1, n_2, \dots, n_k$.
