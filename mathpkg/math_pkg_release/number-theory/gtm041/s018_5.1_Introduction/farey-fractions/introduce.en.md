The Farey fractions $F_n$ of order $n$ are the reduced fractions in $[0,1]$ with denominator at most $n$, listed in increasing order. They play a fundamental role in the circle method by partitioning the unit circle into arcs near roots of unity. Consecutive Farey fractions satisfy the unimodular relation $bc - ad = 1$, which guarantees that their Ford circles are tangent.
