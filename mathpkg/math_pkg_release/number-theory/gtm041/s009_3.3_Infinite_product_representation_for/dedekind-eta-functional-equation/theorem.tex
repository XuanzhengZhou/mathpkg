If $\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \in \Gamma$, $c > 0$, and $\tau \in H$, then
$$\eta\!\left( \frac{a\tau + b}{c\tau + d} \right) = \varepsilon(a, b, c, d)\,\{-i(c\tau + d)\}^{1/2}\,\eta(\tau)$$
where
$$\varepsilon(a, b, c, d) = \exp\!\left\{ \pi i \left( \frac{a + d}{12c} + s(-d, c) \right) \right\}$$
and
$$s(h, k) = \sum_{r=1}^{k-1} \frac{r}{k} \left( \frac{hr}{k} - \left\lfloor \frac{hr}{k} \right\rfloor - \frac{1}{2} \right).$$
