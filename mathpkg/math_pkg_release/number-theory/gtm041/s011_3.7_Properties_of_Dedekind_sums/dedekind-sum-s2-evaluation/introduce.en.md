When $h=2$ and $k$ is odd, the Dedekind sum $s(2,k)$ also admits a closed-form evaluation. The formula involves the product $(k-1)(k-5)$ divided by $24k$, which shows that $s(2,k)$ can be expressed as a simple rational function of $k$ alone in this case.
