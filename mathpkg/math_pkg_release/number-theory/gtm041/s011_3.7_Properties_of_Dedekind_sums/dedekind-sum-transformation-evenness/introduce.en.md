Theorem 3.11 concerns the transformation of Dedekind sums under the action of the modular group $\Gamma$. Given a modular matrix with lower-left entry $c$, the defect $\delta$ measures how $s(a,c)$ differs from $s(a,c_1)$ and from the rational correction term. The theorem states that $12\delta$ is always an even integer, a crucial fact used in proving Dedekind's functional equation for $\eta(\tau)$.
