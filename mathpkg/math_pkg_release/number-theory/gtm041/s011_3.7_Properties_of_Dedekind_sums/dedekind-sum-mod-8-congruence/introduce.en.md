Theorem 3.9 provides an explicit congruence modulo 8 for the quantity $12ks(h,k)$ in terms of a sum involving floor functions. When $k$ is odd, the formula simplifies significantly. This congruence is a key ingredient in the proof of Dedekind's functional equation for $\eta(\tau)$ and in the study of transformation formulas for modular forms.
