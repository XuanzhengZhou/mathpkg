Assume $\sigma_0 > \sigma_a$, where $\sigma_a$ is the abscissa of absolute convergence of a Dirichlet series $f(s)$. Then the closure of $V_f(\sigma_0)$ contains $U_f(\sigma_0)$. That is, we have

$$V_f(\sigma_0) \subseteq U_f(\sigma_0) \subseteq \overline{V_f(\sigma_0)},$$

and hence $\overline{U_f(\sigma_0)} = \overline{V_f(\sigma_0)}$.
