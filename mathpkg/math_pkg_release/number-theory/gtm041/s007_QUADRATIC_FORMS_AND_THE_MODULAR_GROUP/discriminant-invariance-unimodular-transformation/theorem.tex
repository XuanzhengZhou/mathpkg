Let $Q(x, y) = ax^2 + bxy + cy^2$ be a quadratic form with discriminant $d = 4ac - b^2$. If $x$ and $y$ are subjected to a unimodular transformation
$$x = \alpha x' + \beta y', \quad y = \gamma x' + \delta y', \quad \text{where } \begin{pmatrix} \alpha & \beta \\ \gamma & \delta \end{pmatrix} \in \Gamma,$$
then $Q(x, y)$ is transformed to a quadratic form $Q_1(x', y')$ having the same discriminant $d$.
