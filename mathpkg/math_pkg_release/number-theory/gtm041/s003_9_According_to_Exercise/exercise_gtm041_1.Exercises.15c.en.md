Use Theorem 12.17 in [4] to prove the more general result

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n^{4k+1}}{1+e^{n\pi}} = \frac{2^{4k+1} - 1}{8k+4}B_{4k+2},$$

where $B_{4k+2}$ denotes the Bernoulli number of index $4k+2$.
