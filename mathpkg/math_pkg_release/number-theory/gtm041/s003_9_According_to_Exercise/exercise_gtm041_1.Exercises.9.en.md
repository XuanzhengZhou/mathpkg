According to Exercise 4, the function $\wp(2z)$ is a rational function of $\wp(z)$. Prove that, in fact,

$$\wp(2z) = \frac{\{\wp^2(z) + \frac{1}{4}g_2\}^2 + 2g_3\wp(z)}{4\wp^3(z) - g_2\wp(z) - g_3} = -2\wp(z) + \frac{1}{4}\left(\frac{\wp''(z)}{\wp'(z)}\right)^2.$$
