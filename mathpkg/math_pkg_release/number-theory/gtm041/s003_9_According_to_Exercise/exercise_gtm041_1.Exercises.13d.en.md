Apply the result of Exercise 12 to obtain the following formula, valid for $|x| < 1$:

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{\lambda(n)x^n}{1-x^n} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} x^{n^2},$$

where $\lambda(n)$ is Liouville's function.
