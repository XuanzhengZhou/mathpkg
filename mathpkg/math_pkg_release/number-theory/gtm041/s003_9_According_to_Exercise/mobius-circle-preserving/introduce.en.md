Möbius transformations map circles to circles (including straight lines as circles through infinity). This is proved by writing both circles and lines in the generalized form $Az\bar{z} + Bz + \bar{B}\bar{z} + C = 0$ with $A,C \in \mathbb{R}$, where $A \neq 0$ gives a circle and $A = 0$ gives a line. Substituting the Möbius transformation into this equation preserves the same algebraic form, establishing the result.
