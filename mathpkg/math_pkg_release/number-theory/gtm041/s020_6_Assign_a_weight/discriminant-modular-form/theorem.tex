The \textit{discriminant modular form} $\Delta(\tau)$ is a modular form of weight $12$ satisfying the transformation law
$$\Delta\left(\frac{a\tau + b}{c\tau + d}\right) = (c\tau + d)^{12} \Delta(\tau)$$
for every $\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}$ in the modular group $\Gamma$, with Fourier expansion
$$\Delta(\tau) = (2\pi)^{12} \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \tau(n) e^{2\pi i n \tau},$$
where $\tau(n)$ is Ramanujan's tau function.
