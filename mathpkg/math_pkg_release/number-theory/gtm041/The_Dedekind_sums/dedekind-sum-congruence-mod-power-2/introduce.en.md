Theorem 3.10 refines the congruence modulo $8$ to a congruence modulo an arbitrary power of $2$. When $k$ has a factor $2^{\lambda}$, the Theorem provides a congruence modulo $2^{\lambda+3}$ for $12hk\,s(h,k)$ when $h$ is odd. This sharp result is essential for analyzing the exact $2$-adic behavior of the Dedekind eta multiplier system and plays a role in the proof that the multiplier system quantity is even.
