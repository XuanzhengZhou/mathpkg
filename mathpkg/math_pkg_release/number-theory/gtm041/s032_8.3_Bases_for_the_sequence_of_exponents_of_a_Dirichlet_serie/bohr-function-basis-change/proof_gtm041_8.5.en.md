By Theorem 8.6 we have
$$\Lambda = R_B B = R_\Gamma \Gamma, \quad \text{where } R_B = R_\Gamma A.$$
Hence
$$F_B(Z) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a(n)e^{-(R_B Z)_n} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a(n)\exp\{-(R_\Gamma AZ)_n\} = F_\Gamma(AZ).$$
