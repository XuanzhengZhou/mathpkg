Theorem 4.6 describes how the Hecke operator $f_p$ transforms under the modular inversion $S\tau = -1/\tau$.
Since $f_p$ is already invariant under the translation $T\tau = \tau + 1$ (because $T \in \Gamma_0(p)$),
this theorem completes the description of the behavior of $f_p$ under both generators of the full modular group $\Gamma$.
The formula expresses $f_p(-1/\tau)$ as a linear combination of $f_p(\tau)$, $f(p\tau)$, and $f(\tau/p)$ with coefficients involving the prime $p$.
