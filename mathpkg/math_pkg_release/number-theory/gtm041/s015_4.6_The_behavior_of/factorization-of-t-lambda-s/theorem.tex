Let $T_\lambda\tau = (\tau + \lambda)/p$. Then for each $\lambda$ in the interval $1 \leq \lambda \leq p - 1$ there exists an integer $\mu$ in the same interval and a transformation $V$ in $\Gamma_0(p)$ such that
$$T_\lambda S = V T_\mu.$$
Moreover, as $\lambda$ runs through the numbers $1, 2, \ldots, p - 1$, so does $\mu$.
