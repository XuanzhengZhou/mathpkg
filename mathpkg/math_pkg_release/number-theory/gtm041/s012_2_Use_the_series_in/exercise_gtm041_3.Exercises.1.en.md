If $\tau \in H$ prove that

$$G_2(\tau) = 2\zeta(2) + \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} \sum_{m=-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{1}{(m+n\tau)^2}.$$

*Hint:* Start with Equation (12) of Chapter 1, replace $\tau$ by $n\tau$, where $n > 0$, and sum over all $n \geq 1$.
