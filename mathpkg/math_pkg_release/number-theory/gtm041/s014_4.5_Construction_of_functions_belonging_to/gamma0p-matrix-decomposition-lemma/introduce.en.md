Lemma 1 is the key technical step in proving that the averaging operator $f_p$ maps functions automorphic under $\Gamma$ to functions automorphic under $\Gamma_0(p)$. It establishes a coset decomposition identity for the translation operators $T_\lambda \tau = (\tau + \lambda)/p$ when conjugated by elements of $\Gamma_0(p)$. The matrix $W_\mu$ produced by the lemma belongs to $\Gamma_0(p^2)$, a subgroup contained in the full modular group $\Gamma$, so the automorphy of $f$ under $\Gamma$ can be applied.
