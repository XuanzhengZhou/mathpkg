Show that the Dirichlet series associated with the normalized modular form
$$f(\tau) = \frac{(2k-1)!}{(2\pi i)^{2k}} \zeta(2k) + \sum_{m=1}^{\infty} \sigma_{2k-1}(m) e^{2\pi i m\tau}$$
is $\varphi(s) = \zeta(s)\zeta(s+1-2k)$.
