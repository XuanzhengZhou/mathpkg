If $k \geq 12$, then $f \in M_{k,0}$ if and only if $f = \Delta h$, where $h \in M_{k-12}$. Therefore the linear transformation $T: M_{k-12} \rightarrow M_{k,0}$ defined by
$$
T(h) = \Delta h
$$
establishes an isomorphism between $M_{k,0}$ and $M_{k-12}$. Consequently, if $k \geq 12$ we have
$$
\dim M_{k,0} = \dim M_{k-12}.
$$
