Let $f$ be an entire modular form of weight $k$ which is not identically zero, and assume $f$ has $N$ zeros in the closure of the fundamental region $R_\Gamma$ of the modular group. Let $N(p)$ denote the order of the zero of $f$ at a point $p$. Then

$$N = \frac{k}{12} - \frac{1}{2} N(i) - \frac{1}{3} N(\rho) - N(i\infty),$$

where $\rho = e^{2\pi i/3}$ and the fundamental region $R_\Gamma$ has vertices at $\rho, i, \rho+1,$ and $i\infty$.
