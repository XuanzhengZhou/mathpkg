Let $S(0)$ denote the sum of the zeros of an elliptic function $f$ in a period parallelogram, and let $S(\infty)$ denote the sum of the poles in the same parallelogram. Prove that $S(0) - S(\infty)$ is a period of $f$. [Hint: Integrate $zf'(z)/f(z)$.]
