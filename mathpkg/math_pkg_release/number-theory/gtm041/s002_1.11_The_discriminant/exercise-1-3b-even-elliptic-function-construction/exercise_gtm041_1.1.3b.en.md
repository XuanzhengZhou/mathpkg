Let $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ and $b_1, \ldots, b_m$ be complex numbers such that none of the numbers $\wp(a_i) - \wp(b_j)$ is zero. Let

$$f(z) = \frac{\prod_{k=1}^{n} [\wp(z) - \wp(a_k)]}{\prod_{r=1}^{m} [\wp(z) - \wp(b_r)]}.$$

Prove that $f$ is an even elliptic function with zeros at $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ and poles at $b_1, \ldots, b_m$.
