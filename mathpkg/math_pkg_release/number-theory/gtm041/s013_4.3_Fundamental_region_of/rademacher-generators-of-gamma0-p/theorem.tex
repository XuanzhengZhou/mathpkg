For any prime $p > 3$, the congruence subgroup $\Gamma_0(p)$ has $2[p/12] + 3$ generators, and they may be selected from the following elements:
$$T, V_1, V_2, \ldots, V_{p-1},$$
where $T\tau = \tau + 1$, $S\tau = -1/\tau$, and
$$V_k = ST^kST^{-k'}S = \begin{pmatrix} k' & 1 \\ -(kk' + 1) & -k \end{pmatrix},$$
with $kk' \equiv -1 \pmod{p}$. The subgroup $\Gamma_0(2)$ has generators $T$ and $V_1$; the subgroup $\Gamma_0(3)$ has generators $T$ and $V_2$.
