A simultaneous eigenform is a modular form that is an eigenfunction for all Hecke operators $T_n$ simultaneously. This means the form is an eigenvector for the entire Hecke algebra. Many classical modular forms such as Eisenstein series $G_{2k}$ and the discriminant $\Delta$ are simultaneous eigenforms.
