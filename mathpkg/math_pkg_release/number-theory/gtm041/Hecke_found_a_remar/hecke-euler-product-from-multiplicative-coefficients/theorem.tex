If the coefficients $c(n)$ satisfy the multiplicative property
$$c(m)c(n) = \sum_{d|(m,n)} d^{2k-1}c\left(\frac{mn}{d^2}\right)$$
the Dirichlet series $\varphi(s) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} c(n)/n^s$ will have an Euler product representation of the form
$$\varphi(s) = \prod_p \frac{1}{1 - c(p)p^{-s} + p^{2k-1-2s}}$$
whenever the Dirichlet series converges absolutely.
