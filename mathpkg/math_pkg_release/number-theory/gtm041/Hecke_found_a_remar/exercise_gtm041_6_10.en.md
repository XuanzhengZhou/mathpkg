Derive each of the identities in Exercises 8, 9, and 10 by equating coefficients in appropriate identities involving modular forms.

$$\tau(n) = \frac{65}{756} \sigma_{11}(n) + \frac{691}{756} \sigma_5(n) - \frac{691}{3} \sum_{m=1}^{n-1} \sigma_5(m)\sigma_5(n-m).$$

Show that this identity implies Ramanujan's congruence
$$\tau(n) \equiv \sigma_{11}(n) \pmod{691}.$$
