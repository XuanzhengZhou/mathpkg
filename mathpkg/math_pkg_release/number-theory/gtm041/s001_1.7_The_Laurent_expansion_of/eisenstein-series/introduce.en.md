The Eisenstein series $G_n$ of order $n \geq 3$ is defined as the sum over all nonzero lattice points of $\omega^{-n}$. For odd $n$, the series vanishes identically because the lattice is symmetric under $\omega \mapsto -\omega$. These series converge absolutely for $n \geq 3$ and serve as the fundamental numerical invariants of the lattice. They appear as coefficients in the Laurent expansion of the Weierstrass $\wp$ function and in the differential equation it satisfies.
