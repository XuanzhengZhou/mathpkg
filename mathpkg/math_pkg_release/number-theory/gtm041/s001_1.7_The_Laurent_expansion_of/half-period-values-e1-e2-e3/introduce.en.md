The numbers $e_1, e_2, e_3$ are defined as the values of the Weierstrass $\wp$ function at the three non-zero half-periods of the lattice. Because $\wp'$ vanishes at these half-periods (as $\wp'$ is odd and periodic), the $e_i$ are the critical values of $\wp$. They turn out to be precisely the three roots of the cubic polynomial $4x^3 - g_2 x - g_3$, and they are always distinct.
