The coefficients $b(n)$ in the Laurent expansion $\wp(z) = z^{-2} + \sum b(n) z^{2n}$ satisfy recurrence relations obtained by differentiating the Weierstrass differential equation. This yields a second-order differential equation $\wp''(z) = 6\wp^2(z) - \frac{1}{2}g_2$, into which one substitutes the Laurent series and equates coefficients. The result is a recursive formula that expresses each $b(n)$ in terms of earlier coefficients, and equivalently gives recurrence relations for the Eisenstein series $G_{2m}$ themselves.
