Lemma 3 is the inductive step in the alternate proof of Dedekind's functional equation. Since the modular group $\Gamma$ is generated by $T$ and $S$, and every element can be expressed as a word $S T^{m_1} S \cdots S T^{m_k}$, it suffices to show that the validity of the functional equation for $A$ implies its validity for $A T^m$ and $A S$. Combined with the base case $A = S$ (Theorem 3.1), Lemmas 1-3 provide a complete proof of Dedekind's functional equation.
