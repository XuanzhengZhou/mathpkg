This definition introduces the number $e(k)$ of linearly independent simultaneous eigenforms (eigenforms for all Hecke operators $T_n$ simultaneously) in the space $M_{2k,0}$ of cusp forms of weight $2k$. The invariant $e(k)$ measures the size of the Hecke eigenbasis and trivially satisfies $e(k) \leq \kappa = \dim M_{2k,0}$. Determining $e(k)$ exactly — whether it equals $\kappa$ — is a central question concerning the spectral theory of Hecke operators on spaces of modular forms.
