The space $M_{2k,0}$ admits an orthonormal basis $\{f_1, \dots, f_\kappa\}$ where $\kappa = \dim M_{2k,0}$ and each $f_j$ is a simultaneous eigenform of all Hecke operators~$T_n$. Moreover, each $f_j$ can be scaled to a normalized simultaneous eigenform; the resulting basis is orthogonal. Because each $T_n$ is Hermitian with respect to the Petersson inner product, all eigenvalues $\lambda_j(n)$ satisfying $T_n f_j = \lambda_j(n) f_j$ are real.
