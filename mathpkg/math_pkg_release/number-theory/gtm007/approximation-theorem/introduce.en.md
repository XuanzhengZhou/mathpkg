The approximation theorem (weak approximation) states that $\mathbb{Q}$ is dense in the finite product $\mathbb{R} \times \prod_{i=1}^n \mathbb{Q}_{p_i}$. It allows simultaneous approximation at finitely many places, generalizing the Chinese remainder theorem. This density is essential for the Hasse principle.
