Let $K$ be a finite field with $q$ elements, and let $u$ be an integer $\geq 0$. Define
$$S(X^u) = \sum_{x \in K} x^u$$
(with the convention $x^0 = 1$ for all $x \in K$, including $x = 0$). Then:
$$S(X^u) = \begin{cases}
-1 & \text{if } u \geq 1 \text{ and } (q-1) \mid u,\\
0  & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}$$
