Over a finite field $K$ with $q$ elements, the sum of the $u$-th powers of all field elements admits a simple closed form. The sum vanishes unless $u \geq 1$ and $u$ is a multiple of $q-1$ (the order of the multiplicative group $K^*$), in which case it equals $-1$. This lemma is a key computational tool in the proof of the Chevalley-Warning theorem on solutions to polynomial equations over finite fields.
