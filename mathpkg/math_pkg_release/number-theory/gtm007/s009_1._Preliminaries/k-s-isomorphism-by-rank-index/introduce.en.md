Corollaries 1 and 2 to Theorem 1 of Chapter V give a complete description of $K(S)$. The rank and index invariants characterize stable isomorphism completely. In particular, $E$ and $E$ of different types can still be stably isomorphic, showing that type is not a stable invariant.
