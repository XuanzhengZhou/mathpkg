This is clear if $E = 0$. Otherwise, one of the two modules $E \oplus I_+$ or $E \oplus I_-$ is indefinite. Suppose that the first is. Since $E$ and $E'$ have the same signature, $E' \oplus I_+$ is also indefinite. By applying Theorem 4, we see that $E \oplus I_+$ and $E' \oplus I_+$ are isomorphic to $s I_+ \oplus t I_-$ and $s' I_+ \oplus t' I_-$, respectively. Since $E$ and $E'$ have the same signature, we have $s = s'$ and $t = t'$, hence the result.
