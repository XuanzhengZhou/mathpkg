For each prime $p$, the $p$-adic completion $V_p = E \otimes \mathbf{Q}_p$ is a quadratic space over $\mathbf{Q}_p$ with its $arepsilon$-invariant from Chapter IV. For odd primes, the invariant is always trivial due to the unimodularity of $E$. At $p = 2$, the invariant is given by an explicit formula involving the rank, discriminant, and $\sigma$-invariant.
