The proof is given in §3.5 of Chapter V. The key idea is to use the existence of isotropic vectors (Theorem 3) to split off hyperbolic planes $U$ one at a time, reducing the rank by 2 each time while preserving the type II condition. When only $\Gamma_8$ summands remain (which are positive definite), the decomposition is complete. The integers $p$ and $q$ are then forced by the rank and index.
