If $E \in S$ is an indefinite unimodular lattice of type II with $\tau(E) \geq 0$, then $E$ is isomorphic to
$$
E \simeq pU \oplus q\Gamma_8,
$$
where $p = \frac{1}{2}(r(E) - \tau(E))$ and $q = \frac{1}{8}\tau(E)$ are nonnegative integers, $U = \begin{pmatrix}0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0\end{pmatrix}$ is the hyperbolic plane, and $\Gamma_8$ is the $E_8$ lattice. When $\tau(E) \leq 0$, replace $E$ by $-E$ (negate the quadratic form) to obtain a corresponding decomposition.
