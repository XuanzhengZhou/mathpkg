Theorem 5 classifies indefinite type II (even) quadratic modules with non-negative index: they are isomorphic to $pU \oplus q\Gamma_8$, where $U$ is the hyperbolic plane and $\Gamma_8$ is the $E_8$ lattice. The integers $p$ and $q$ are determined by the rank and index. The proof is deferred to a later section ($n^\circ$ 3.5).
