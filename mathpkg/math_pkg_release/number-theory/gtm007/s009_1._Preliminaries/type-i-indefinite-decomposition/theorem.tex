Every indefinite unimodular lattice $E$ of type I is isomorphic to a direct sum of the unit forms $I_+ = (1)$ and $I_- = (-1)$:
$$
E \simeq \underbrace{I_+ \oplus \cdots \oplus I_+}_{s \text{ copies}} \oplus \underbrace{I_- \oplus \cdots \oplus I_-}_{t \text{ copies}},
$$
where $s = \frac{r+\tau}{2}$ and $t = \frac{r-\tau}{2}$.
