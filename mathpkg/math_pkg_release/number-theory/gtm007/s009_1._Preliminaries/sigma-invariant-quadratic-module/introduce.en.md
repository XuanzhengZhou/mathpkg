The $\sigma$-invariant is a congruence class modulo 8 attached to a unimodular lattice of type I. It measures the residue modulo 8 of the norm of an element with odd norm. This invariant plays a crucial role in the classification, as it relates to the signature via $\sigma(E) \equiv 	au(E) \pmod{8}$.
