The index $\tau(E) = r - s$ is the difference between the positive and negative dimensions of the signature of $V = E \otimes \mathbf{R}$. It controls whether $E$ is definite ($\tau(E) = \pm r(E)$) or indefinite, and satisfies the congruence $r(E) \equiv \tau(E) \pmod{2}$. Together with the rank, it determines the stable isomorphism class.
