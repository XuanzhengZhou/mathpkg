Theorem 4 (Chapter V, §2.2) is the main structure theorem for indefinite type I lattices: they are diagonalizable over $\mathbf{Z}$ with entries $\pm 1$. This is a remarkable rigidity result, showing that all such lattices are isomorphic to a standard form determined by the signature.
