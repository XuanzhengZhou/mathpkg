The lattice $\Gamma_n$ (for $n = 4k$) is constructed inside $\mathbf{Q}^n$ with the standard Euclidean quadratic form by adjoining the half-integer vector $e = (\frac{1}{2}, \ldots, \frac{1}{2})$ to $\mathbf{Z}^n$ and taking all vectors with integer coordinates summing to an even integer. For $n \equiv 0 \pmod{8}$, $\Gamma_n$ is an even unimodular (type II) lattice. The case $\Gamma_8$ is the celebrated $E_8$ root lattice.
