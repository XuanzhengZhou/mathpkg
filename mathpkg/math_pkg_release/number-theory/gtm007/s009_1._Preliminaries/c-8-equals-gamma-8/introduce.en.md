This is a classic result of Mordell: the only even, positive definite unimodular lattice of rank 8 is the $E_8$ lattice $\Gamma_8$. The proof uses the Minkowski-Siegel mass formula: the mass $M_8$ equals the reciprocal of the order of the automorphism group of $\Gamma_8$, showing that $\Gamma_8$ is the only lattice in its genus.
