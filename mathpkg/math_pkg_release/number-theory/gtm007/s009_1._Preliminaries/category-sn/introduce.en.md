The category $S_n$ consists of free abelian groups of rank $n$ equipped with a non-degenerate symmetric bilinear form taking values in $\mathbf{Z}$, whose Gram matrix has determinant $\pm 1$. These are integral quadratic modules that are "unimodular" (discriminant $\pm 1$). The condition is equivalent to the induced map $E \to \operatorname{Hom}(E, \mathbf{Z})$ being an isomorphism. The union over all ranks is denoted $S$.
