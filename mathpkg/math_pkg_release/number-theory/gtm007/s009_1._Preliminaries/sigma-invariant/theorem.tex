Consider the integer $u.u$. If we replace $u$ by $u+2x$, $u.u$ is replaced by

$$(u+2x).(u+2x) = u.u + 4(u.x + x.x) \equiv u.u \pmod{8}.$$

The image of $u.u$ in $\mathbf{Z}/8\mathbf{Z}$ is thus an invariant of $E$; we denote it by $\sigma(E)$. If $E$ is of type II, the form $\bar{x}.\bar{x}$ is zero (in other words, $x.y$ is alternating) and we can take $u = 0$, hence $\sigma(E) = 0$.
