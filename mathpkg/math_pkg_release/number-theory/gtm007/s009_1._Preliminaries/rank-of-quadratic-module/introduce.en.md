The rank $r(E)$ of an integral quadratic module $E \in S_n$ is simply the dimension $n$ of the underlying free abelian group. It is the simplest invariant of $E$ and appears in every formula involving other invariants such as the index $\tau(E)$, discriminant $d(E)$, and the sigma invariant $\sigma(E)$.
