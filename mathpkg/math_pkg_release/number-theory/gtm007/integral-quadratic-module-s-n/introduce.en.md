$S_n$ is the category of unimodular integral quadratic modules of rank $n$. These are free abelian groups equipped with a nondegenerate symmetric bilinear form whose Gram determinant is $\pm 1$. Objects of $\cup S_n$ are the integral analogues of nondegenerate quadratic spaces over $\mathbb{Q}$.
