$I_+$, $I_-$, and $U$ are the fundamental building blocks for integral quadratic modules. $I_+ \oplus I_-$ has matrix $\begin{pmatrix}1&0\\0&-1\end{pmatrix}$ and type I, while $U$ has the same rank and discriminant but type II. Every indefinite form decomposes into copies of these.
