\textbf{Proposition 2.} The Hilbert symbol satisfies:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $(a, b) = (b, a)$ (symmetry)
\item[(ii)] $(a, c^2) = 1$ for all $c \in k^*$
\item[(iii)] $(a, -a) = 1$ and $(a, 1-a) = 1$ if $a \neq 1$
\item[(iv)] $(a, b) = (a, -ab) = (a, (1-a)b)$ if $a \neq 1$.
\end{enumerate}
Moreover, $(aa', b) = (a, b)(a', b)$ (bilinearity).
