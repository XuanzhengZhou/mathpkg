The modular group $G = \mathbf{PSL}_2(\mathbb{Z})$ is the group of fractional linear transformations of the upper half-plane with integer coefficients and unit determinant, modulo sign. It is generated by $S(z) = -1/z$ and $T(z) = z+1$, satisfying $S^2 = (ST)^3 = 1$.
