The effect of $T(n)$ on $q$-expansions is completely explicit. The transformation mixes coefficients whose indices share common divisors with $n$. The simplicity of $\gamma(1) = c(n)$ is crucial: for normalized eigenforms, the eigenvalue $\lambda(n)$ equals $c(n)$.
