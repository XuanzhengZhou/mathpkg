Let $\varepsilon_n: \mathbb{Z}_p \rightarrow A_n = \mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$ be the map sending a $p$-adic integer $x$ to its $n$-th component $x_n$. The sequence
$$0 \longrightarrow \mathbb{Z}_p \xrightarrow{\;p^n\;} \mathbb{Z}_p \xrightarrow{\;\varepsilon_n\;} A_n \longrightarrow 0$$
is exact, where $p^n$ denotes multiplication by $p^n$.
