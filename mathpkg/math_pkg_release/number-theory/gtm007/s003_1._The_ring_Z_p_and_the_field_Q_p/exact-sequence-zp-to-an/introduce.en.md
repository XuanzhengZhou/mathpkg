This proposition establishes a fundamental exact sequence linking $\mathbb{Z}_p$ with its finite quotients $A_n = \mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$. The sequence $0 \to \mathbb{Z}_p \xrightarrow{p^n} \mathbb{Z}_p \xrightarrow{\varepsilon_n} A_n \to 0$ shows that multiplication by $p^n$ is injective on $\mathbb{Z}_p$, and that reduction modulo $p^n$ realizes $A_n$ as the quotient $\mathbb{Z}_p/p^n\mathbb{Z}_p$. This is the $p$-adic analogue of the exact sequence $0 \to \mathbb{Z} \xrightarrow{n} \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z} \to 0$ for ordinary integers.
