The ring of $p$-adic integers $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is a fundamental object in number theory, constructed as the projective limit of the finite rings $\mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$. This construction endows $\mathbb{Z}_p$ with a natural compact topology, making it a complete discrete valuation ring. The elements of $\mathbb{Z}_p$ can be thought of as "infinite precision" integers modulo powers of $p$, and the ring serves as the integer analogue in the $p$-adic world, just as $\mathbb{Z}$ sits inside $\mathbb{R}$.
