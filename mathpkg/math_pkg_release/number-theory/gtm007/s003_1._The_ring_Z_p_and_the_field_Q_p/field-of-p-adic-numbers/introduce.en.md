The field of $p$-adic numbers $\mathbb{Q}_p$ is the field of fractions of $\mathbb{Z}_p$, analogous to how $\mathbb{Q}$ is obtained from $\mathbb{Z}$. It carries a non-archimedean absolute value $|\cdot|_p$ defined via the $p$-adic valuation $v_p$, which measures the highest power of $p$ dividing an element. This valuation makes $\mathbb{Q}_p$ a complete metric space with rich topological and algebraic structure, central to modern number theory and arithmetic geometry.
