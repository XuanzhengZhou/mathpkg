Let $f \in \mathbb{Z}_p[X]$ be a polynomial with $p$-adic integer coefficients and let $f'$ be its formal derivative. Let $x \in \mathbb{Z}_p$ and $n, k \in \mathbb{Z}$ satisfy
$$0 \leq 2k < n, \quad f(x) \equiv 0 \pmod{p^n}, \quad v_p(f'(x)) = k.$$
Then there exists $y \in \mathbb{Z}_p$ such that
$$f(y) \equiv 0 \pmod{p^{n+1}}, \quad v_p(f'(y)) = k, \quad \text{and} \quad y \equiv x \pmod{p^{\,n-k}}.$$
