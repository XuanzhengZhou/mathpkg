Let $p$ be an odd prime, and let $S \subset F_p^*$ be a subset such that $F_p^*$ is the disjoint union of $S$ and $-S$. Take $S = \left\{1, \ldots, \frac{p-1}{2}\right\}$.

If $s \in S$ and $a \in F_p^*$, write $as$ in the form $as = e_s(a)s_a$ with $e_s(a) = \pm 1$ and $s_a \in S$.

Then:
$$\left(\frac{a}{p}\right) = \prod_{s \in S} e_s(a).$$
