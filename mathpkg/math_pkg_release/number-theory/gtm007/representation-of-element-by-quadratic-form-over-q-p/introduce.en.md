This corollary reduces the representation problem for a scalar $a$ to the problem of representing zero by the augmented form $f \div aZ^2$. The invariants of $f \div aZ^2$ are $d' = -ad$ and $\varepsilon' = (-a, d)\varepsilon$. Applying Theorem 6 to the augmented form yields the conditions stated.
