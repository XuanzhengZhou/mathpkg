The supplementary laws give explicit values of the Legendre symbol for $\pm 1$ and $2$. The first supplement states that $-1$ is a quadratic residue mod $p$ iff $p \equiv 1 \pmod{4}$. The second supplement states that $2$ is a quadratic residue mod $p$ iff $p \equiv \pm 1 \pmod{8}$. The proof of (iii) uses an 8th root of unity in an extension of $\mathbb{F}_p$.
