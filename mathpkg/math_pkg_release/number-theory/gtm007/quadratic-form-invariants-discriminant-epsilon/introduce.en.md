A quadratic form over $\mathbb{Q}_p$ has two fundamental invariants: the discriminant $d(f)$ and the Hasse-Witt invariant $\varepsilon(f)$. Together with the rank $n$ and (for $\mathbb{R}$) the signature, these completely determine the form up to equivalence.
