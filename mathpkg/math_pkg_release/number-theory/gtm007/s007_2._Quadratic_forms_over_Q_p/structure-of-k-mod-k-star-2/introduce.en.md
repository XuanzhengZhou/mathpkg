This lemma describes the structure of the square class group $k^*/k^{*2}$ over the $p$-adic field $\mathbf{Q}_p$ and the distribution of elements with respect to the Hilbert symbol $(x,a)$. The size of $k^*/k^{*2}$ is $4$ for odd $p$ and $8$ for $p=2$. For any nontrivial class $a \neq 1$, the preimage sets $H_a^\varepsilon = \{x : (x,a) = \varepsilon\}$ each contain exactly half of the elements. The intersection properties in part (c) are crucial for determining when a quadratic form represents zero.
