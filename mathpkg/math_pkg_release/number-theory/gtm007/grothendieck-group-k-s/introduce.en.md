$K(S)$ is the Grothendieck group of the semigroup of integral quadratic modules under orthogonal direct sum. It captures the stable isomorphism classes, where two modules become isomorphic after adding a common orthogonal summand. This is the algebraic $K$-theory analogue for quadratic forms.
