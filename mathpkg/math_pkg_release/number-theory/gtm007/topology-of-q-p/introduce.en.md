The $p$-adic metric is non-Archimedean (ultrametric). Every triangle is isosceles with the unequal side at most the equal ones. A sequence converges iff differences tend to zero; a series converges iff its general term tends to zero. These properties make $p$-adic analysis drastically different from real analysis.
