For odd characteristic, exactly half the nonzero elements of a finite field are squares. The quadratic character $x \mapsto x^{(q-1)/2}$ distinguishes squares (value $1$) from non-squares (value $-1$). In characteristic $2$, the Frobenius map $x \mapsto x^2$ is an automorphism, so every element is a square.
