The Dedekind eta function satisfies the modular transformation laws: $\eta(z+1) = e^{\pi i/12} \eta(z)$ and $\eta(-1/z) = \sqrt{z/i}\,\eta(z)$. Therefore $\eta^{24}$ transforms with weight 12 and trivial multiplier, making it a modular form of weight 12. Since it vanishes at $i\infty$ (the $q^{1/24}$ factor gives $q$ as leading term), it is a cusp form. Dimension considerations show $S_{12}$ is one-dimensional, so $\eta^{24}$ is proportional to $\Delta$. Matching leading coefficients yields the exact identity.
