The space $M_k$ has for basis the family of monomials
$$G_2^\alpha G_3^\beta,$$
with $\alpha, \beta$ integers $\geq 0$ and $2\alpha + 3\beta = k$.

Consequently, the graded algebra $M = \bigoplus_{k=0}^\infty M_k$ is isomorphic to the polynomial algebra $\mathbf{C}[G_2, G_3]$, where $G_2$ has weight $2$ (i.e. degree $2$ in the grading) and $G_3$ has weight $3$.
