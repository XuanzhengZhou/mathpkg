For the full modular group $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})$, the space $M_k$ of modular forms of even weight $k \geq 4$ admits a basis consisting of monomials $G_4^a G_6^b$ with $4a + 6b = k$, where $G_4$ and $G_6$ are the Eisenstein series of weights 4 and 6. The dimension of $M_k$ is:
$$
\dim M_k = \begin{cases}
\lfloor k/12 \rfloor + 1 & \text{if } k \not\equiv 2 \pmod{12}, \\
\lfloor k/12 \rfloor & \text{if } k \equiv 2 \pmod{12}.
\end{cases}
$$
