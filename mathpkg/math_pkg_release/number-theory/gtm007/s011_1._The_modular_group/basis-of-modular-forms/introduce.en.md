The dimension formula for spaces of modular forms of even weight for the full modular group is a classic application of the valence formula (Theorem 4). The space $M_k$ is spanned by monomials in the Eisenstein series $G_4$ and $G_6$, which together freely generate the graded algebra $M_* = \mathbf{C}[G_4, G_6]$.
