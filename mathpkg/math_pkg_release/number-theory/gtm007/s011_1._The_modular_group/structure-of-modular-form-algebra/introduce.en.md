This structure theorem is the culmination of the basic theory of modular forms for the full modular group. It shows that $G_4$ and $G_6$ are algebraically independent and generate the entire algebra. The proof uses the valence formula to count dimensions and shows that the map $\mathbf{C}[X,Y] 	o M_*$ sending $X \mapsto G_4$, $Y \mapsto G_6$ is an isomorphism.
