The map $\phi: \mathbf{C}[X,Y] \to M_*$ assigning $X \mapsto G_4$, $Y \mapsto G_6$ is a graded homomorphism (with $\deg X = 4$, $\deg Y = 6$). The dimension count from the valence formula shows that each graded piece $M_k$ has dimension exactly equal to the number of monomials $X^a Y^b$ with $4a+6b=k$, so $\phi$ is surjective on each graded piece. To prove injectivity: if $P(G_4, G_6) = 0$, factor out powers of $G_4$ and $G_6$ to reduce to a polynomial identity that contradicts the valence formula.
