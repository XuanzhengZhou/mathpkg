The graded algebra $M_* = \bigoplus_{k \geq 0} M_k$ of all modular forms for $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})$ is freely generated by the Eisenstein series $G_4$ and $G_6$:
$$
M_* = \mathbf{C}[G_4, G_6].
$$
That is, every modular form can be written uniquely as a polynomial in $G_4$ and $G_6$. In particular, the dimension of $M_k$ equals the number of monomials $G_4^a G_6^b$ of total weight $k$.
