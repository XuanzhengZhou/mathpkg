The group $G = \mathbf{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z}) / \{\pm 1\}$ is generated by the two elements
$$S = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} \quad \text{and} \quad T = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix},$$
which act on the upper half-plane by
$$Sz = -1/z, \qquad Tz = z + 1.$$
These satisfy the relations $S^2 = 1$ and $(ST)^3 = 1$.
