The fact that $\{G_4^a G_6^b\}$ spans $M_k$ follows from the valence formula: the number of linearly independent such monomials matches $\dim M_k$. Linear independence holds because $G_4^3$ and $G_6^2$ have distinct weights (12), so the monomials correspond to different $q$-expansions with distinct leading terms.
