The modular group $G = \mathbf{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z}) / \{\pm 1\}$ is the image of the integer special linear group in the projective special linear group $\mathbf{PSL}_2(\mathbf{R})$. It is a discrete subgroup of $\mathbf{PSL}_2(\mathbf{R})$ that acts on the upper half-plane $H$ via Möbius transformations $gz = (az+b)/(cz+d)$. The modular group is the central object in the theory of modular forms and is generated by the two elements $S: z \mapsto -1/z$ and $T: z \mapsto z+1$.
