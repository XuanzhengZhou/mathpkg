The Hecke bound is a fundamental estimate for Fourier coefficients of modular forms. The "trivial" bound $O(n^{k/2})$ follows from elementary estimates on the upper half-plane. The Ramanujan-Petersson conjecture (now Deligne's theorem) improves the exponent to $(k-1)/2$, which is sharp.
