For even $k \geq 2$, the Eisenstein series $G_k(z)$ has the Fourier expansion:
$$
G_k(z) = 2\zeta(k) + \frac{2(2\pi i)^k}{(k-1)!} \sum_{n=1}^\infty \sigma_{k-1}(n) e^{2\pi i n z},
$$
where $\sigma_{k-1}(n) = \sum_{d \mid n} d^{k-1}$. For $k=2$, the series converges conditionally and defines a modular form of weight 2 only after a suitable modification (the almost holomorphic modular form $\hat{E}_2$).
