The Fourier expansion of the Eisenstein series connects them to divisor functions. The coefficients $\sigma_{k-1}(n)$ are the sums of $(k-1)$-th powers of divisors of $n$. For $k=4$ and $k=6$, these give the generators $G_4$ and $G_6$ of the algebra of modular forms.
