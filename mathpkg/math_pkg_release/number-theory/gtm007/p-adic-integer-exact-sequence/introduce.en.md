This proposition identifies $\mathbb{Z}_p / p^n \mathbb{Z}_p$ with $\mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$, showing that the projective limit construction faithfully recovers the finite quotients. The kernel of reduction modulo $p^n$ is exactly $p^n \mathbb{Z}_p$.
