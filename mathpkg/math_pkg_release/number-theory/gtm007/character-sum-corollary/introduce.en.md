The dual orthogonality relation: the sum over all characters of $\chi(x)$ equals $|G|$ for $x = 1$ and $0$ otherwise. This is used to detect membership in arithmetic progressions: $\frac{1}{\phi(m)}\sum_\chi \chi(a^{-1})\chi(p)$ picks out primes $p \equiv a \pmod{m}$.
