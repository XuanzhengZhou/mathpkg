For a normalized Hecke eigenform $f = \sum c(n)q^n$ of weight $2k$, the Petersson conjecture states that the roots $\alpha_p, \alpha_p'$ of $1 - c(p)T + p^{2k-1}T^2 = (1-\alpha_p T)(1-\alpha_p' T)$ are complex conjugates. Equivalently, $|\alpha_p| = |\alpha_p'| = p^{k-1/2}$, i.e., $|c(p)| \leq 2p^{k-1/2}$. For $k=6$ (weight 12), this is the Ramanujan conjecture $|\tau(p)| \leq 2p^{11/2}$. Proved by Deligne in 1973.
