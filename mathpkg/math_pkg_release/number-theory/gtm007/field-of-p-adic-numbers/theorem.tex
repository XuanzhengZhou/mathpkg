The field of $p$-adic numbers $\mathbb{Q}_p$ is the field of fractions of the ring $\mathbb{Z}_p$. Equivalently, $\mathbb{Q}_p = \mathbb{Z}_p[p^{-1}]$. Every nonzero element $x \in \mathbb{Q}_p$ can be written uniquely as $x = p^n u$ where $n \in \mathbb{Z}$ and $u$ is a $p$-adic unit (an invertible element of $\mathbb{Z}_p$).
