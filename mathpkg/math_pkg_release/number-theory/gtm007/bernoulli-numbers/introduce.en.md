Bernoulli numbers appear throughout number theory: in the values $\zeta(2k) = \frac{2^{2k-1}}{(2k)!}B_k \pi^{2k}$, in the Eisenstein series normalization $E_k = 1 + \gamma_k \sum \sigma_{2k-1}(n)q^n$ with $\gamma_k = (-1)^k 4k/B_k$, and in the Minkowski-Siegel mass formula.
