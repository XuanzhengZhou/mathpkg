The characteristic of a field captures the behavior of repeated addition of the multiplicative identity. Every field has characteristic either zero or a prime $p$. In characteristic $p$, the Frobenius map $x \mapsto x^p$ is a field endomorphism, which plays a fundamental role in the theory of finite fields and $p$-adic numbers developed throughout this book.
