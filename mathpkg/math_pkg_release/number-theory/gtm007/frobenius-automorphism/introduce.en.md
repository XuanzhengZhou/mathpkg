The Frobenius automorphism is a fundamental map in fields of prime characteristic $p$. It sends each element to its $p$-th power. In characteristic $p$, the binomial theorem combined with $\binom{p}{k} \equiv 0 \pmod{p}$ for $0 < k < p$ implies that this map is a field homomorphism. Since it is clearly injective, it gives an isomorphism onto the subfield $K^p$ of $p$-th powers.
