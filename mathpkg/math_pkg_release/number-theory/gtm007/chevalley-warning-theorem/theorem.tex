\textbf{Theorem 3 (Chevalley-Warning).} Let $K$ be a finite field of characteristic $p$ with $q$ elements. Let $f_\alpha \in K[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$ be polynomials in $n$ variables such that $\sum_\alpha \deg f_\alpha < n$. Let $V$ be the set of common zeros of the $f_\alpha$ in $K^n$. Then
$$\operatorname{Card}(V) \equiv 0 \pmod{p}.$$
In particular, if the $f_\alpha$ have no constant term (so that $(0,\ldots,0) \in V$), they have a nontrivial common zero.
