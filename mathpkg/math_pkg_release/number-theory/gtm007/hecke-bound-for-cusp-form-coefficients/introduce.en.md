Hecke's bound $a_n = O(n^k)$ for cusp forms is proved using the invariance of $|f(z)|y^k$ under $G$ and its boundedness on the fundamental domain. This was improved by Deligne (Ramanujan-Petersson conjecture) to $a_n = O(n^{k-1/2+\varepsilon})$.
