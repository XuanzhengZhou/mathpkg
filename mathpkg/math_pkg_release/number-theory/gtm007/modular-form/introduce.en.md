A modular form is holomorphic on $H$ and at the cusp $\infty$. Modular forms of a given weight form a finite-dimensional vector space $M_k$. The first examples are the Eisenstein series $G_k$ (weight $2k \geq 4$) and the discriminant $\Delta$ (weight 12, cusp form).
