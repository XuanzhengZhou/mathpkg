A corollary of Theorem 7: the Fourier coefficients $c(n)$ of a normalized Hecke eigenform are multiplicative --- $c(mn)=c(m)c(n)$ for coprime $m,n$ --- and satisfy the prime-power recurrence $c(p)c(p^n) = c(p^{n+1}) + p^{2k-1}c(p^{n-1})$. These relations are a direct consequence of the corresponding identities for the Hecke operators $T(n)$ and the equality $c(n)=\lambda(n)$.
