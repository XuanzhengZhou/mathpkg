Let $\Gamma$ be a lattice satisfying the conditions of Theorem 8, so that $\theta_\Gamma$ is a modular form of weight $n/2$. Then $\theta_\Gamma$ can be written as
$$\theta_\Gamma = E_{n/4} + f_\Gamma,$$
where $E_{n/4}$ is the Eisenstein series of weight $n/2$ and $f_\Gamma$ is a cusp form. In particular, if the space of cusp forms of weight $n/2$ is zero (e.g., $n = 8$ giving weight $4$, or $n = 16$ giving weight $8$), then $\theta_\Gamma = E_{n/4}$ exactly.
