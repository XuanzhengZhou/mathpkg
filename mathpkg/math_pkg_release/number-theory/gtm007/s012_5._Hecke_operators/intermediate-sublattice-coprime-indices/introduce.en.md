This lemma provides the key arithmetic input for proving that Hecke operators for coprime indices compose multiplicatively: $T(m)T(n) = T(mn)$ when $(m,n)=1$. Given a sublattice $\Gamma''$ of index $mn$, there is a unique intermediate sublattice $\Gamma'$ with indices $n$ and $m$ respectively. The uniqueness follows from the decomposition of the finite abelian group $\Gamma/\Gamma''$ into its $m$-primary and $n$-primary parts.
