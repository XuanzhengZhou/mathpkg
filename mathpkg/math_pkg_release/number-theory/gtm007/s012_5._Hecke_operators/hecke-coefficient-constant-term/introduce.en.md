Corollary 1 of Proposition 12 gives the special values of the Hecke-transformed coefficients: the constant term scales by the divisor sum $\sigma_{2k-1}(n)$, and the first coefficient $\gamma(1)$ equals $c(n)$. This second equality is crucial: it shows that for an eigenform, the eigenvalue $\lambda(n)$ must equal $c(n)$ when $c(1)=1$.
