Let $k$ be an integer $\geq 2$. The Eisenstein series $G_k$ of weight $2k$ is an eigenfunction of all Hecke operators $T(n)$. The corresponding eigenvalue is $\sigma_{2k-1}(n)$:
$$T(n)G_k = \sigma_{2k-1}(n) G_k.$$
The normalized eigenfunction is
$$(-1)^k \frac{B_k}{4k} E_k = (-1)^k \frac{B_k}{4k} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \sigma_{2k-1}(n) q^n.$$
The associated Dirichlet series is $\zeta(s)\zeta(s-2k+1)$.
