A correspondence on a set $E$ is a $\mathbf{Z}$-linear operator $T$ on the free abelian group $X_E$ generated by $E$. It is specified by integer coefficients $n_y(x)$ giving the multiplicity of $y$ in $T(x)$. Correspondences act on numerical functions $F$ on $E$ via the transform $TF(x) = \sum n_y(x)F(y)$, providing the algebraic foundation for Hecke operators.
