Let $E$ be a set and let $X_E$ be the free abelian group generated by $E$. A correspondence on $E$ (with integer coefficients) is a homomorphism $T: X_E \to X_E$. The correspondence $T$ is given by its values on elements $x \in E$:
$$T(x) = \sum_{y \in E} n_y(x)y, \quad n_y(x) \in \mathbf{Z},$$
where $n_y(x) = 0$ for almost all $y$.

Let $F$ be a numerical valued function on $E$. By $\mathbf{Z}$-linearity $F$ extends to $X_E$. The transform of $F$ by $T$, denoted $TF$, is the restriction to $E$ of $F \circ T$:
$$TF(x) = F(T(x)) = \sum_{y \in E} n_y(x)F(y).$$
