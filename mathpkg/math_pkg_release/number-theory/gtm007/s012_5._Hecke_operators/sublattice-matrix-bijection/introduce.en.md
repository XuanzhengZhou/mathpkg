This lemma establishes an explicit bijection between upper-triangular integer matrices $\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ 0 & d \end{pmatrix}$ with $ad=n$, $a \ge 1$, $0 \le b < d$ and the set $\Gamma(n)$ of sublattices of index $n$ in a given lattice $\Gamma$. The bijection provides the combinatorial input needed to derive the explicit formula for the action of $T(n)$ on $q$-expansions of modular forms.
