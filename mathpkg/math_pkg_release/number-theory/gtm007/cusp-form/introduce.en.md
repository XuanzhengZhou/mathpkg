Cusp forms (Spitzenformen) vanish at the cusp $\infty$. They form a subspace $M_k^0$ of $M_k$ of codimension at most 1. The Petersson inner product makes $M_k^0$ a Hilbert space, and Hecke operators act as commuting normal operators on it.
