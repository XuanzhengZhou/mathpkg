The $j$-function is the fundamental modular invariant. It gives an isomorphism of the compactified modular curve $\widehat{H/G}$ onto the Riemann sphere $\mathbb{C} \cup \{\infty\}$. Every modular function is a rational function of $j$, and $j$ classifies elliptic curves up to isomorphism.
