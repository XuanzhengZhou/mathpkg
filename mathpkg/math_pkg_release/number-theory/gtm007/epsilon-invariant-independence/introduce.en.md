The invariant $\varepsilon(Q) = \pm 1$ is the Hasse-Witt invariant, defined as the product of Hilbert symbols of pairs of diagonal coefficients. The theorem proves this is independent of the choice of orthogonal basis, making it a genuine invariant of the quadratic form over $\mathbb{Q}_p$.
