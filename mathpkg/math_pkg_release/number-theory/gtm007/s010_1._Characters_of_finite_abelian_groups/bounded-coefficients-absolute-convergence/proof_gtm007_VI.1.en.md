Since $|a_n| \leq M$ for some constant $M$, we have
$$
\sum_{n=1}^\infty \left|\frac{a_n}{n^s}\right| \leq M \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^{\sigma}}
$$
where $\sigma = R(s)$. The series $\sum 1/n^{\sigma}$ converges for $\sigma > 1$, hence the original series converges absolutely in the half-plane $R(s) > 1$.
