Proposition 1 asserts that any character defined on a subgroup $H$ of a finite abelian group $G$ can be extended to a character on the whole group $G$. The proof proceeds by induction on the index $(G:H)$. This extension property is essential for constructing characters on quotient groups and underlies the exact sequence relating $\widehat{G}$, $\widehat{H}$, and $\widehat{G/H}$.
