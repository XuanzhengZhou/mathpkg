Let $G$ be a finite abelian group. For any character $\chi \in \hat{G}$:
$$
\sum_{x \in G} \chi(x) = \begin{cases} |G| & \text{if } \chi = 1, \\ 0 & \text{if } \chi \neq 1. \end{cases}
$$
Dually, for any element $x \in G$:
$$
\sum_{\chi \in \hat{G}} \chi(x) = \begin{cases} |G| & \text{if } x = 1, \\ 0 & \text{if } x \neq 1. \end{cases}
$$
