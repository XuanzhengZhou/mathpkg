The dual group $\hat{G}$ consists of all homomorphisms from $G$ to the multiplicative group of nonzero complex numbers. For a finite abelian group, $\hat{G}$ is isomorphic to $G$ (non-canonically in general) and has the same order. The dual of a cyclic group of order $n$ is cyclic of order $n$, consisting of the maps $s^a \mapsto \zeta_n^{a}$ for $n$-th roots of unity $\zeta_n$.
