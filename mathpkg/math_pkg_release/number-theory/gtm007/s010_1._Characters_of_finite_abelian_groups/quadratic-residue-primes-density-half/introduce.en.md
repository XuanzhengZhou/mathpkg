Proposition 14 (Chapter VI, §4.4): This application of Dirichlet's theorem uses the quadratic character $\chi_a$ from Proposition 5. The kernel of $\chi_a$ in $G(m)$ has index 2, so exactly half the residue classes (by density) satisfy the condition. This shows that the Legendre symbol is "random": it takes values $\pm 1$ with equal asymptotic frequency.
