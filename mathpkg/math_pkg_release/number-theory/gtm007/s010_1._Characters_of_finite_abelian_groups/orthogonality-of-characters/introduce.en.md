This corollary (to Proposition 4) gives the orthogonality relation for characters of a finite abelian group: the sum of all character values at an element $x$ equals the group order if $x = 1$ and zero otherwise. It follows from applying the bidual isomorphism (Proposition 3) to the dual group $\widehat{G}$. This is the key identity that underpins the Fourier analysis on finite abelian groups and the proof of Dirichlet's theorem.
