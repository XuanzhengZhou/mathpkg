For any $a \in (\mathbf{Z}/m\mathbf{Z})^*$,
$$
\sum_{\chi \in \hat{G}(m)} \chi(a) = \begin{cases} \phi(m) & \text{if } a \equiv 1 \pmod{m}, \\ 0 & \text{otherwise}. \end{cases}
$$
More generally, for any $a, b \in (\mathbf{Z}/m\mathbf{Z})^*$,
$$
\sum_{\chi} \chi(a^{-1}b) = \begin{cases} \phi(m) & \text{if } a \equiv b \pmod{m}, \\ 0 & \text{otherwise}. \end{cases}
$$
