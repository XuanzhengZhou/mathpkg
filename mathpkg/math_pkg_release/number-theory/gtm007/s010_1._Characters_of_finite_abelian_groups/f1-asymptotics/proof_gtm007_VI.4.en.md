The function $f_1(s)$ differs from $\sum_p 1/p^s$ by only the finitely many terms corresponding to primes $p \mid m$. Since $\sum_p 1/p^s \sim \log 1/(s-1)$ by Corollary 2 to Proposition 10, the same asymptotic holds for $f_1(s)$.
