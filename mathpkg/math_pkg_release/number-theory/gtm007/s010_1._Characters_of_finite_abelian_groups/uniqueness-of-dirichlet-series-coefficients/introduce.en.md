Corollary 4 establishes the uniqueness of Dirichlet series representations: if a Dirichlet series is identically zero in its half-plane of convergence, then all its coefficients must be zero. The proof extracts coefficients successively by multiplying by appropriate exponentials and taking limits. This ensures that Euler product identities for $L$-functions are rigorous.
