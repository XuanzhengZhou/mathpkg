Let $H$ be a subgroup of a finite abelian group $G$. Every character of $H$ extends to a character of $G$. Moreover, the restriction map $\rho: \hat{G} \to \hat{H}$ is surjective, and its kernel is isomorphic to $(\widehat{G/H})$, giving the exact sequence:
$$
\{1\} \to (\widehat{G/H}) \to \hat{G} \to \hat{H} \to \{1\}.
$$
