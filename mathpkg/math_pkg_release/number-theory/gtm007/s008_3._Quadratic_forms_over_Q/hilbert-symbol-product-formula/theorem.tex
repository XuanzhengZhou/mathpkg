Let $f$ be a nondegenerate quadratic form over $\mathbf{Q}$, and let $\varepsilon_v(f)$ be the Hasse–Witt invariant of $f$ at the place $v \in V$. Then

$$\prod_{v \in V} \varepsilon_v(f) = 1,$$

where the product is taken over all places $v$ of $\mathbf{Q}$ (all prime numbers together with the archimedean place $\infty$).
