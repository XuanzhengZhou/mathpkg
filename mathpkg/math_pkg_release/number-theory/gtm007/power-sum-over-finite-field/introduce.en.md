This lemma computes the sum of all $u$-th powers across a finite field $K$ with $q = p^f$ elements. The result depends on whether $u$ is a positive multiple of $q-1$. When $u \geq 1$ and $(q-1) \mid u$, every nonzero element satisfies $x^u = 1$, and the sum of $q-1$ copies of $1$ in characteristic $p$ equals $-1$. Otherwise, the cyclicity of $K^*$ provides an element $y$ with $y^u \neq 1$, allowing a scaling argument to force the sum to zero.
