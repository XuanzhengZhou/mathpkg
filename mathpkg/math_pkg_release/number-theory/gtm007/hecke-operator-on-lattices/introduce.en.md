Hecke operators are algebraic correspondences on the set of lattices. $T(n)$ maps a lattice to the formal sum of all its sublattices of index $n$. The number of such sublattices is $\sigma_1(n) = \sum_{d|n} d$. These operators act on modular forms and are fundamental to the theory of $L$-functions of modular forms.
