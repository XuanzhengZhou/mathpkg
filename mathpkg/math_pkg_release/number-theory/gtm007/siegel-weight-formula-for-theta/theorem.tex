\textbf{Siegel's formula.} Let $C_n$ be the set of isometry classes of even unimodular lattices of rank $n$, and let $g_\Gamma$ be the order of the automorphism group of $\Gamma$. Then
$$\sum_{\Gamma \in C_n} \frac{1}{g_\Gamma} \theta_\Gamma = M_n E_{n/4}, \quad \text{where } M_n = \sum_{\Gamma \in C_n} \frac{1}{g_\Gamma}.$$
In particular, the weighted mean of the cusp form error terms is zero.
