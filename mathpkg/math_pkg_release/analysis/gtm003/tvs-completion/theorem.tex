Every Hausdorff t.v.s. $L$ over $K$ can be embedded as a dense subspace into a complete Hausdorff t.v.s. $\tilde{L}$ over $K$, called the completion of $L$. The completion $(\tilde{L}, \tilde{\mathfrak{T}})$ is unique up to isomorphism. If $L$ is a Hausdorff t.v.s., the presence of a translation-invariant uniformity allows the completion to be constructed via equivalence classes of Cauchy filters.
