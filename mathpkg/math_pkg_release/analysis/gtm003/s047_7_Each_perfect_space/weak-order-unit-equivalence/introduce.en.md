Theorem 7.7 provides three equivalent characterizations of weak order units in order complete vector lattices of minimal type: (a) the algebraic definition via disjointness, (b) strict positivity on non-zero positive linear forms, and (c) the topological property of being a quasi-interior point of the positive cone under any locally convex vector lattice topology. The equivalence (a) ⇔ (c) is particularly important as it bridges the purely order-theoretic and topological viewpoints.
