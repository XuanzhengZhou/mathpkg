Theorem 8.7 extends Kakutani's representation to a broader class: every bornological, sequentially complete locally convex vector lattice is representable as the inductive limit of a family of $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X_\alpha)$ spaces. The proof reduces to the (AM)-space with unit case (8.5) by noting that a bornological l.c.v.l. carries the order topology $\mathfrak{T}_O$, which is the inductive limit of the (AM)-spaces with unit generated by the order intervals of a suitable directed family of positive elements.
