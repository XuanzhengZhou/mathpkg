Theorem 8.1 is the order-theoretic form of the Stone-Weierstrass theorem. It states that any vector sublattice of $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X)$ containing the constant function $e$ and separating points is dense in the uniform norm. The proof uses only the lattice operations (inf and sup), making it a purely order-theoretic argument that avoids multiplicative structure. This form highlights the special role of lattice operations in approximation theory for continuous functions.
