Let $[x, y]$ be a positive definite Hermitian form on a vector space $H$ over $\mathbb{C}$. Then $x \mapsto \sqrt{[x, x]}$ is a norm $\|\cdot\|$ on $H$, and $(H, \|\cdot\|)$ is called a pre-Hilbert space (or inner product space). The inner product satisfies Schwarz' inequality:
$$|[x, y]| \leq \|x\| \|y\|.$$
