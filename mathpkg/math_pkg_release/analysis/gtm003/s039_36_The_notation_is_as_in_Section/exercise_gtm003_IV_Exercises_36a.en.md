Let $E$ be a locally convex space such that each summable sequence in $E$ is absolutely summable. Show that each bounded subset of $l^1(N, E)$ is bounded as a subset of $l^1[N, E]$.

Hint: Consider a sequence $\{x'_n\}$ in $E'$ such that $\sum_n |\langle x_n, x'_n \rangle| < +\infty$ for each $x = (x_n) \in l^1(N, E)$, and show that the function $x \mapsto \sum_n |\langle x_n, x'_n \rangle|$ is bounded on every bounded subset of $l^1(N, E)$. See Pietsch [5], p. 54.
