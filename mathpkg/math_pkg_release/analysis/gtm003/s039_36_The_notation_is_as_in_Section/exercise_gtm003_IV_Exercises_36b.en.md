Assume that $l^1(N, E)$ is infrabarreled (which is the case, for example, if $E$ is metrizable) and that each summable sequence in $E$ is absolutely summable. Show that the canonical map $l^1[N, E] \to l^1(N, E)$ is a topological isomorphism, and deduce that $E$ is nuclear.

Hint: Use part (a).
