Deduce from (b). Since $E$ is normable, it is metrizable, and hence $l^1(N, E)$ is infrabarreled. By hypothesis, each summable sequence in $E$ is absolutely summable. Applying (b), the canonical map $l^1[N, E] \to l^1(N, E)$ is a topological isomorphism, which implies that $E$ is nuclear. But a normable nuclear space is necessarily finite dimensional (since nuclear normed spaces are finite dimensional). Therefore $E$ is finite dimensional.
