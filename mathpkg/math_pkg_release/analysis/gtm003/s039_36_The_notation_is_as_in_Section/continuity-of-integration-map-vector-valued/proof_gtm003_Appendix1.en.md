Under the hypothesis that the closed, convex, circled hull of each compact set in $E$ is compact, the closed convex circled hull of $f(\delta)$ is compact for any $f \in C_E(\delta)$. For a neighborhood $U$ of $0$ in $E$, let $V$ be the polar of the set of all $\int_\delta \langle f(\zeta), \cdot \rangle d\zeta$ for $f$ in a suitable $U$-neighborhood in $C_E(\delta)$. The continuity follows from the uniform estimate

$$\left\| \int_\delta f(\zeta) d\zeta \right\|_B \leq \sup_{\zeta \in \delta} \|f(\zeta)\|_B \cdot \text{length}(\delta)$$

for each continuous seminorm defining the topology of $E$.
