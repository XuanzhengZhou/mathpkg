For subsets of a vector space $L$, the properties of being circled or convex are invariant under the formation of arbitrary intersections. The **circled hull** of $A \subset L$ is the set $\{\lambda a : a \in A \text{ and } |\lambda| \leq 1\}$, the unique smallest circled set containing $A$.
