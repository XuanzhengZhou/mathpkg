This theorem characterizes the quotient space of a metrizable topological vector space by a closed subspace. It states that the quotient $L/M$ is again metrizable, and completeness passes to the quotient. Moreover, if the topology of $L$ is generated by a pseudo-norm $|\cdot|$, then the topology of the quotient is generated by the infimum pseudo-norm $|\hat{x}| = \inf_{x \in \hat{x}} |x|$. The requirement that $M$ be closed is necessary by a prior result on the $T_1$ separation axiom.
