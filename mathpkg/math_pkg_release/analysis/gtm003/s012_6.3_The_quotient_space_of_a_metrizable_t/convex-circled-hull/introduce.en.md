The convex circled hull $\Gamma A$, also called the absolutely convex hull, is the smallest subset of the vector space that is both convex and circled and contains $A$. It is obtained by first taking the circled hull, then the convex hull. The notation $\Gamma_\alpha A_\alpha$ extends this to unions of families of sets, which will be useful when discussing locally convex topologies generated by families of sets.
