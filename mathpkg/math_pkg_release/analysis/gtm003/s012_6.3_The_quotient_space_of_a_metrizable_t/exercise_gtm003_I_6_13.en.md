The metric $d(x, y) = \tan^{-1}|x - y|$ generates the unique topology on $\mathbb{R}$ under which $\mathbb{R}_0$ is a Hausdorff t.v.s., but the uniformity generated by $d$ (under which $\mathbb{R}$ is precompact) is distinct from the uniformity of the t.v.s. $\mathbb{R}_0$.
