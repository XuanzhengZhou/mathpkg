An exercise on the well-definedness of the trace for nuclear operators on Banach spaces with the approximation property. The trace is defined via a nuclear representation $\sum_n \lambda_n x_n' \otimes y_n$, and the exercise asks to prove that the sum $\sum_n \lambda_n \langle x_n', y_n \rangle$ is independent of the particular representation chosen. This is a key step toward Lidskii's trace theorem.
