Let $E$ be a bornological space, and let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a family of bounded subsets of $E$ such that the range of each null sequence in $E$ is contained in some $S \in \mathfrak{S}$. Show that if $F$ is a quasi-complete (respectively, complete) locally convex space, then $\mathcal{L}_{\mathfrak{S}}(E, F)$ is quasi-complete (respectively, complete). (Use (8.3) and Exercise 17, Chapter II.)
