This lemma, isolated from the proof of Ando's theorem on normal cones in Banach spaces, shows how a complete cone in a metrizable topological vector space gives rise to a complete metrizable t.v.s. on the subspace $E_1 = C - C$. The construction uses the difference sets $V_n = (U_n \cap C) - (U_n \cap C)$ of a standard neighborhood base to generate a topology under which $E_1$ is complete.
