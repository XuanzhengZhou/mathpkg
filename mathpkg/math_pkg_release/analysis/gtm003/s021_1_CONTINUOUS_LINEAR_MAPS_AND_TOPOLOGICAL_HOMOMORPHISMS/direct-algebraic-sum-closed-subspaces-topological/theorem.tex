Let $L$ be a complete, metrizable topological vector space. If $L$ is the direct algebraic sum of two closed subspaces $M$ and $N$, then the sum is topological:

$$L = M \oplus N,$$

i.e., the natural continuous linear mapping $(x_1, x_2) \mapsto x_1 + x_2$ from $M \times N$ onto $L$ is an isomorphism of topological vector spaces.
