The property of $E'_\beta$ expressed by (6.5) is evidently equivalent to the following: If $\{M_n\}$ is a sequence of equicontinuous subsets of $E''$ such that $M = \bigcup_n M_n$ is strongly bounded (i.e., $\beta(E'', E')$-bounded), then $M$ is equicontinuous. Since every finite subset of $E''$ is equicontinuous, the result follows.
