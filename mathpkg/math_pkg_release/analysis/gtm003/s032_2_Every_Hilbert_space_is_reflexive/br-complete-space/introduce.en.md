A $B_r$-complete space is a locally convex space $E$ with the property that every weakly dense subspace $Q$ of the dual $E'$ whose intersections with equicontinuous sets are relatively weakly closed must be the whole dual $E'$. This is a weaker condition than $B$-completeness (Ptak space). $B_r$-completeness plays an important role in the closed graph theorem (8.5) and in open mapping theorems.
