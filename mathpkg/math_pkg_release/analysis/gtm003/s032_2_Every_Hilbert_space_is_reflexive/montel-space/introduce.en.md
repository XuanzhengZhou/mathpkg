A Montel space (or M-space) is a reflexive locally convex space with the additional property that every closed, bounded subset is compact for the strong topology. The permanence properties of Montel spaces are virtually the same as those of reflexive spaces. Examples include the spaces enumerated in Chapter III, Section 8, which are non-normable reflexive spaces whose closed bounded sets are strongly compact.
