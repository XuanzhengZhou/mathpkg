An infrabarreled space is a locally convex space in which every barrel that absorbs all bounded sets is already a neighborhood of zero. This condition is intermediate between bornological and barreled: every bornological space is infrabarreled, and every barreled space is infrabarreled. Infrabarreled spaces are precisely those for which the natural topology on the bidual coincides with the strong topology, or equivalently those where the canonical imbedding into the strong bidual is a topological isomorphism.
