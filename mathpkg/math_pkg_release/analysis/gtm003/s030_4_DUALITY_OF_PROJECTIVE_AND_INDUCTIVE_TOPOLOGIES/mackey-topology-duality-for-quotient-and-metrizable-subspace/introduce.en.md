This corollary applies the duality theorem to Mackey topologies on locally convex spaces. The Mackey topology on a quotient space is precisely the quotient of the original Mackey topology, and metrizable restrictions of Mackey topologies coincide with the Mackey topology of the subspace. Consequently, the class of Mackey spaces is stable under formation of separated quotients and metrizable subspaces.
