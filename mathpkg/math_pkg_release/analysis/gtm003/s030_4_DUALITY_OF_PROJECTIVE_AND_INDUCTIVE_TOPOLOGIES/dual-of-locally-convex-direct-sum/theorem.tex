Let $\{E_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ be a family of locally convex spaces and let $E$ be their locally convex direct sum. Then $E'$ is algebraically isomorphic with $\prod_{\alpha \in A} E'_\alpha$, and the following topological identities hold:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\tau(E, E') = \bigoplus_{\alpha} \tau(E_\alpha, E'_\alpha)$.
\item $\tau(E', E) = \prod_{\alpha} \tau(E'_\alpha, E_\alpha)$.
\end{enumerate}
