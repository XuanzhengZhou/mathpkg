Let $E, F$ be locally convex spaces and denote by $\mathcal{L}_s(E, F)$ the space of continuous linear maps from $E$ into $F$ under the topology of simple convergence. Then the correspondence
$$\sum_i x_i \otimes y_i' \longmapsto f, \qquad f(u) = \sum_i \langle u x_i, y_i' \rangle \quad (u \in \mathcal{L}(E, F)),$$
is an algebraic isomorphism of the tensor product $E \otimes F'$ onto the topological dual $\mathcal{L}_s(E, F)'$ of $\mathcal{L}_s(E, F)$.
