If $E$ is a quasi-complete locally convex space, then every $\sigma(E', E)$-bounded subset of $E'$ is strongly bounded (i.e., bounded for $\beta(E', E)$). Equivalently, $\mathfrak{B} = \mathfrak{B}_\sigma$ for the families of strongly bounded and weakly bounded subsets of $E'$.

Consequently, every quasi-complete infrabarreled space is barreled.
