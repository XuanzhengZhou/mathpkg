Theorem 4.4 applies the product-direct sum duality to projective and inductive limits of locally convex spaces. The Mackey dual of a projective limit is the inductive limit of the Mackey duals. This result unifies the duality between projective and inductive topologies with the general categorical duality between limits and colimits, completing the program outlined at the beginning of Section 4.
