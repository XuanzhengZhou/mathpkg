Let $\{E_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ be a family of locally convex spaces and let $E = \prod_{\alpha \in A} E_\alpha$. Then the dual $E'$ of $E$ is algebraically isomorphic with $\bigoplus_{\alpha \in A} E'_\alpha$, and the following topological identities hold:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\sigma(E, E') = \prod_{\alpha} \sigma(E_\alpha, E'_\alpha)$.
\item $\tau(E, E') = \prod_{\alpha} \tau(E_\alpha, E'_\alpha)$.
\item $\tau(E', E) = \bigoplus_{\alpha} \tau(E'_\alpha, E_\alpha)$.
\end{enumerate}
Moreover, $\sigma(E', E) = \bigoplus_{\alpha} \sigma(E'_\alpha, E_\alpha)$ holds if and only if the index set $A$ is finite.
