A hypocontinuous bilinear map carries products of bounded sets to bounded sets. This property is essential for the theory of topological tensor products: hypocontinuity ensures that bilinear maps behave well with respect to bounded sets in each factor. The result follows from the definition of hypocontinuity and the characterization of bounded sets via absorption by neighborhoods.
