A family \(\mathfrak{S} \neq \{\varnothing\}\) of bounded subsets of a locally convex space \(E\) is called \textit{saturated} if:
\begin{enumerate}
\item It contains arbitrary subsets of each of its members.
\item It contains all scalar multiples of each of its members.
\item It contains the closed, convex, circled hull of the union of each finite subfamily.
\end{enumerate}
