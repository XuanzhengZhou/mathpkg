This theorem gives a clean characterization of metrizable uniform spaces: a uniform space is metrizable precisely when it is separated and its vicinity filter admits a countable base. The proof constructs a metric from a decreasing countable base of symmetric vicinities using a technique analogous to the Birkhoff-Kakutani metrization theorem for topological groups.
