This theorem establishes the existence of the coarsest (initial) uniformity on a set making a given family of maps into uniform spaces uniformly continuous. This is the uniform analogue of the initial (projective) topology. The construction is fundamental: the vicinity filter is generated by all inverse images under the product maps $f_\alpha \times f_\alpha$ of the vicinities in each $X_\alpha$.
