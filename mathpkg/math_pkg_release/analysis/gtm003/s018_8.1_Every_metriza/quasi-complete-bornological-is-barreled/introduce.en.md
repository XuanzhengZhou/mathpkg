A quasi-complete bornological space is necessarily barreled because it can be represented as the inductive limit of Banach spaces (by Theorem 8.4), and Banach spaces are barreled. Since inductive limits of barreled spaces are barreled, the result follows. This corollary provides an important relationship between bornological and barreled spaces: in the quasi-complete case, the bornological property implies the barreled property.
