A convenient way to describe the quotient topology on $L/M$ is through zero-neighborhood bases: the image under $\phi$ of any zero-neighborhood base in $L$ forms a zero-neighborhood base in the quotient. This follows because $\phi$ is linear, continuous, and open, so it carries a fundamental system of neighborhoods of the origin in $L$ to a fundamental system of neighborhoods of the origin in $L/M$.
