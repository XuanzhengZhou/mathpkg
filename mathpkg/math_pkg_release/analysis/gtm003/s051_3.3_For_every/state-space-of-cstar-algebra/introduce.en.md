The state space $S(A)$ of a $C^*$-algebra $A$ is the set of all states (positive linear forms of norm one). In the unital case, $S(A)$ is a $\sigma(A', A)$-closed subset of the unit ball of $A'$ and is therefore weak-* compact. The state space plays a central role in the GNS construction and the representation theory of $C^*$-algebras.
