A normal linear form on a $C^*$-algebra is a positive linear form that is order continuous. Normal states and normal functionals are precisely the elements of the predual of a $W^*$-algebra (Sakai's theorem). This concept bridges the order-theoretic and topological approaches to $W^*$-algebras: normal linear forms are those that are $\sigma(W, V)$-continuous.
