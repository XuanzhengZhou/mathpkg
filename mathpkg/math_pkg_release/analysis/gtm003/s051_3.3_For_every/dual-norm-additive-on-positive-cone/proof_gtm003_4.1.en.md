The simple proof can be left to the reader. The result follows directly from Proposition 4.1: for $\phi, \psi \in A'_+$, let $(u_\lambda)$ be an approximate unit of $A$. Then $\|\phi + \psi\| = \lim_\lambda (\phi + \psi)(u_\lambda) = \lim_\lambda \phi(u_\lambda) + \lim_\lambda \psi(u_\lambda) = \|\phi\| + \|\psi\|$.
