If the supremum of the intersection of the positive cone with the open unit ball exists in a $C^*$-algebra, then this supremum is necessarily a projection. This lemma is a crucial step in characterizing unital $C^*$-algebras: the existence of such a supremum is equivalent to the algebra being unital, and the supremum itself is the unit.
