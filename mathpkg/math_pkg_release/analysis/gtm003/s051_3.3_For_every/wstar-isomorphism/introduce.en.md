A $W^*$-isomorphism between two $W^*$-algebras is an algebraic *-isomorphism that is simultaneously a homeomorphism for the respective weak-* topologies. Equivalently, it is an isomorphism that admits a pre-adjoint -- an isometric isomorphism between the preduals. This defines the proper notion of isomorphism in the category of $W^*$-algebras, preserving both the algebraic and the topological structure.
