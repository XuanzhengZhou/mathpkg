This construction shows how any subset $M$ of the algebraic dual $E^*$ gives rise to a locally convex topology on $E$ via the semi-norms $x \mapsto |f(x)|$ for $f \in M$. The resulting space is a locally convex space if and only if $M$ separates points in $E$. This provides a flexible method for endowing vector spaces with locally convex topologies.
