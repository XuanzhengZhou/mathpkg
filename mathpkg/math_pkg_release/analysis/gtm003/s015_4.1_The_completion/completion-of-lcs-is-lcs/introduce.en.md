Theorem 4.1 states that the completion $\tilde{E}$ of a locally convex space $E$ inherits a natural locally convex topology: the continuous extensions of any generating family of semi-norms on $E$ generate the topology of $\tilde{E}$. This ensures that the category of locally convex spaces is closed under the operation of completion.
