An integral bilinear form is an element of the dual space of the completed topological tensor product $E 	ilde{\otimes} F$ when equipped with the topology of bi-equicontinuous convergence. These forms fill a subspace $\mathcal{J}(E, F)$ of the space of all continuous bilinear forms $\mathcal{B}(E, F)$. Integral bilinear forms admit an integral representation involving a measure on the product of equicontinuous subsets of the duals.
