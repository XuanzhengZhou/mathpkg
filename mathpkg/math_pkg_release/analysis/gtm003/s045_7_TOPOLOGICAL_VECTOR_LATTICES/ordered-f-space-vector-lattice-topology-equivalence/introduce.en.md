This corollary characterizes when the given topology of an ordered Fréchet space that is also a vector lattice coincides with its order topology: this happens precisely when the positive cone is normal. It serves as a substitute for the earlier Corollary 2 of (6.2) and relates the order-topological structure to the normality of the cone. The proof uses the duality between the space and its order dual together with the Mackey property of the space.
