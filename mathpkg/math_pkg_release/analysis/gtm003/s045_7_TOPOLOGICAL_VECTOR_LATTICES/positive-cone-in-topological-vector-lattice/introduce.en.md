Proposition 7.2 records the elementary but essential consequences of the definition of a topological vector lattice for its positive cone. In any topological vector lattice, the positive cone is automatically closed, normal, and a strict b-cone. These properties are fundamental for the duality theory of locally convex vector lattices developed in the subsequent sections.
