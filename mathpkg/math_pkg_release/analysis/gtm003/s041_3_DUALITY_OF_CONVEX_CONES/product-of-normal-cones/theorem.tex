Let $\{L_\alpha : \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ be a family of topological vector spaces, $C_\alpha$ a cone in $L_\alpha$, and $L = \prod_\alpha L_\alpha$ the product space. Then $C = \prod_\alpha C_\alpha$ is a normal cone in $L$ if and only if $C_\alpha$ is normal in $L_\alpha$ for all $\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$.
