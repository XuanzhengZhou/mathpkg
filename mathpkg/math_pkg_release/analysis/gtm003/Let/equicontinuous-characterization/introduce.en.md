A subset $H$ of continuous linear maps between t.v.s. is equicontinuous if and only if the intersection of inverse images of 0-neighborhoods is a 0-neighborhood, equivalently if the image of some 0-neighborhood under all maps in $H$ is contained in any given 0-neighborhood. Equicontinuous sets are bounded for every $\mathfrak{S}$-topology.
