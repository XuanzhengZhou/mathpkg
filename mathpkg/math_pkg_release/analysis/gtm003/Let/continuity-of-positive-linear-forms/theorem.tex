Let $E$ be an ordered t.v.s. with positive cone $C$. Each of the following conditions ensures continuity of every positive linear form on $E$: (i) $C$ has non-empty interior. (ii) $E$ is metrizable and complete, and $E = C - C$. (iii) $E$ is bornological, and $C$ is a semi-complete strict $\mathfrak{B}$-cone.
