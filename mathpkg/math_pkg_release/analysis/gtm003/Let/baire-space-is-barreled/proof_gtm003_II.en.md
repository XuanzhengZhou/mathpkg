Let $D$ be a barrel in $E$; since $D$ is radial and circled, $E = \bigcup_{n \in N} nD$. Since $E$ is a Baire space, there exists $n_0 \in N$ such that $n_0D$ (which is closed) has an interior point; hence $D$ has an interior point $y$. Since $D$ is circled, $-y \in D$; hence $0 = \frac{1}{2}y + \frac{1}{2}(-y)$ is interior to $D$ by (1.1) because $D$ is convex. Thus $D$ is a neighborhood of 0.
