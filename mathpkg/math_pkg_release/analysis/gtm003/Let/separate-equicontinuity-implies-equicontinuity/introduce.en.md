For bilinear maps on a product of metrizable spaces, separate equicontinuity implies joint equicontinuity when the domain $E$ is a Baire space or is barreled (and $G$ is locally convex). This fundamental result due to Bourbaki is a bilinear analogue of the Banach-Steinhaus theorem. A separately continuous bilinear map need not be jointly continuous in general.
