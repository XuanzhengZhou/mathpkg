Let $F$ and $F_1$ be vector spaces over $K$ and let $u$ be a linear map of $F$ into $F_1$. For each $y^* \in F_1^*$, the mapping $y^* \circ u: x \rightarrow \langle ux, y^* \rangle$ is a linear form $x^* \in F^*$, denoted by $u^*y^*$. Thus $\langle ux, y^* \rangle = \langle x, u^*y^* \rangle$ on $F \times F_1^*$. The map $y^* \rightarrow u^*y^*$, linear on $F_1^*$ into $F^*$, is called the algebraic adjoint $u^*$ of $u$.
