Rota's theorem: a positive endomorphism of $L^1(\mu)$ whose norm equals its spectral radius has a cyclic peripheral point spectrum. This follows because the integral functional $f \mapsto \int f d\mu$ is strictly positive and satisfies $u'(h) \leq r(u)h$ when $\|u\| = r(u)$.
