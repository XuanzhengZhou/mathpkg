The completed projective tensor product $L^1(\mu) \tilde{\otimes} E$ is naturally isomorphic to the space $L^1_E(\mu)$ of Bochner-integrable $E$-valued functions. When $E$ is a Banach space, this isomorphism is norm-preserving. This is a fundamental result linking tensor products with vector-valued integration.
