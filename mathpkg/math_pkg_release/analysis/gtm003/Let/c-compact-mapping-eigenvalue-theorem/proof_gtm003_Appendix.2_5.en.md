Let $U$ be the unit ball and $W$ the convex circled hull of $U \cap C$. The sets $\varepsilon W$ form a 0-neighborhood base for a normable topology $\mathfrak{T}$ on $E_0 = C - C$, which is finer than the induced topology. The $C$-compactness of $u$ and properties of $r_C$ allow applying fixed-point arguments on the cone to obtain an eigenvector in $C$ for $r_C$.
