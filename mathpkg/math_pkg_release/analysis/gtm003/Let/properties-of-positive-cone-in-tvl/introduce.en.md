In any topological vector lattice, the positive cone is automatically closed (so every TVL is an ordered t.v.s.), normal, and a strict $\mathfrak{B}$-cone (meaning $C$ is generating and there is a boundedness condition). This makes topological vector lattices particularly well-behaved ordered spaces where all positive linear forms are automatically continuous.
