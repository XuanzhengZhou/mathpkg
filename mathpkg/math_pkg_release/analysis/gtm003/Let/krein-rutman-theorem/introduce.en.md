The Krein-Rutman theorem is a generalization of the Perron-Frobenius theorem to ordered Banach spaces. If $r(u)$ is a pole of the resolvent, then $r(u) > 0$ is an eigenvalue with eigenvectors in the positive cone $C$ and its dual $C'$. This provides a positive eigendirection for the spectral radius.
