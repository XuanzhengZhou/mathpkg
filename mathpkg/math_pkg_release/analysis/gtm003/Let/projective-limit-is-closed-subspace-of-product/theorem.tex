Let $(E_\alpha)_{\alpha \in \mathbf{A}}$ be a projective system of locally convex spaces with linking maps $g_{\alpha \beta}: E_\beta \to E_\alpha$ for $\alpha \leq \beta$. Let $F = \prod_{\alpha \in \mathbf{A}} E_\alpha$ be the product space with projections $p_\alpha: F \to E_\alpha$. Define the projective limit
$$E = \{x = (x_\alpha) \in F : x_\alpha = g_{\alpha \beta}(x_\beta) \text{ for all } \alpha \leq \beta\}.$$
If each $E_\alpha$ is Hausdorff, then $E$ is a closed subspace of $F$.
