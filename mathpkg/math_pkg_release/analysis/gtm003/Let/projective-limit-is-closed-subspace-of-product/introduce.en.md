This proposition shows that a projective limit of Hausdorff locally convex spaces can be realized as a closed subspace of the product space. The key observation is that the compatibility conditions $x_\alpha = g_{\alpha \beta}(x_\beta)$ define closed subspaces $V_{\alpha \beta}$ of the product, and their intersection is therefore also closed. This structural result is fundamental for studying completeness, compactness, and other properties preserved by closed subspaces and products.
