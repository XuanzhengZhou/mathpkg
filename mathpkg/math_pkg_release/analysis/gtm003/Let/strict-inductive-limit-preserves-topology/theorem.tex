If $\{E_n(\mathfrak{T}_n): n \in N\}$ is an increasing sequence of l.c.s. such that the topology $\mathfrak{T}_{n+1}$ induces $\mathfrak{T}_n$ for all $n$ and if the vector space $E$ is the union of the subspaces $E_n$ $(n \in N)$, then the inductive topology on $E$ with respect to the canonical imbeddings $E_n \rightarrow E$ is separated and induces $\mathfrak{T}_n$ on each $E_n$.
