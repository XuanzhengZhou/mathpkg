In a locally convex vector lattice, the 0-neighborhoods can be chosen both convex and solid. The gauge of such a set is a lattice semi-norm: $|y| \leq |x|$ implies $p(y) \leq p(x)$. The topology is generated by a family of such semi-norms. The completion of a topological vector lattice is again a topological vector lattice.
