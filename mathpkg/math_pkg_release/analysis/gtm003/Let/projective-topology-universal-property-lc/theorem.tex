Let $E, F, G$ be locally convex spaces and provide $E \otimes F$ with the projective topology. Then the isomorphism $u \rightarrow u \circ \chi$ of (6.1) maps the space of continuous linear mappings $\mathcal{L}(E \otimes F, G)$ onto the space of continuous bilinear mappings $\mathcal{B}(E, F; G)$.
