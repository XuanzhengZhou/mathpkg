The projective limit of a projective system of locally convex spaces is a closed subspace of the product. Products, duals of inductive limits, and completions can all be viewed as projective limits. The section also notes that a projective limit of l.c.s. may be $\{0\}$, but with countability conditions surjectivity can be guaranteed.
