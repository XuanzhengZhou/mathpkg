In the completed projective tensor product $E \tilde{\otimes} F$ of metrizable locally convex spaces, every element admits a series representation $\sum \lambda_i x_i \otimes y_i$ where $x_i \to 0$, $y_i \to 0$, and $\sum |\lambda_i| < \infty$. This representation is crucial for applications to spaces of summable sequences and nuclear spaces.
