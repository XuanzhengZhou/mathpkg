The cone $\mathcal{H}$ of positive linear maps (those mapping $C_E$ into $C_F$) is closed in $\mathcal{L}_{\mathfrak{S}}(E, F)$. It is a proper cone exactly when $C_E$ is total in $E$. Preservation of the closedness property under $\mathfrak{S}$-topologies is important for spectral theory of positive operators.
