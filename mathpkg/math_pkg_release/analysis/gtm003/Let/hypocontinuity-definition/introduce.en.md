Hypocontinuity is an intermediate notion between separate continuity and joint continuity for bilinear maps. An $\mathfrak{S}$-hypocontinuous bilinear map is separately continuous and satisfies a uniform boundedness condition on sets from $\mathfrak{S}$. Separate continuity is the special case where $\mathfrak{S}$ and $\mathfrak{T}$ are the families of finite sets.
