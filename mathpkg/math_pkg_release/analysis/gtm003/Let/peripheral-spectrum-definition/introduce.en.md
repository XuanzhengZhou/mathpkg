For a positive endomorphism, the peripheral spectrum consists of spectral values on the circle $|\lambda| = r(u)$. The peripheral point spectrum consists of eigenvalues on this circle. The peripheral point spectrum may be cyclic (forming a group of roots of unity) under irreducibility conditions, generalizing the Perron-Frobenius theory.
