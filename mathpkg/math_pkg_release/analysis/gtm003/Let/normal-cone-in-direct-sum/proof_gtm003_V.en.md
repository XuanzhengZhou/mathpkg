Necessity is immediate since each $L_\alpha$ embeds as a subspace of $L$ with $C_\alpha = L_\alpha \cap C$. For sufficiency (over $R$), let $\mathfrak{V}_\alpha$ be 0-neighborhood bases satisfying (3.1)(d). The sets $V = \prod_\alpha V_\alpha$ form a 0-neighborhood base. $[V \cap C]$ is the convex hull of $\bigcup_\alpha [V_\alpha \cap C_\alpha]$, and since $[V_\alpha \cap C_\alpha] \subset V_\alpha$, we get $[V \cap C] \subset V$, establishing normality.
