Consider the family $\mathcal{T}$ of all locally convex topologies on $E \otimes F$ for which the canonical bilinear map on $E \times F$ into $E \otimes F$ is continuous. The upper bound $\mathfrak{T}_p$ of $\mathcal{T}$ is a locally convex topology, called the projective (tensor product) topology on $E \otimes F$. When $\mathfrak{U}, \mathfrak{V}$ are 0-neighborhood bases in $E, F$, the family of convex, circled hulls $\{\Gamma(U \otimes V): U \in \mathfrak{U}, V \in \mathfrak{V}\}$ is a neighborhood base of 0 for $\mathfrak{T}_p$.
