The adjoint $u'$ of a weakly continuous map is continuous for the $\mathfrak{S}$-topology on the dual if and only if $u$ maps $\mathfrak{S}$-sets into $\mathfrak{S}_1$-sets. This provides a clean characterization of continuity of adjoints in terms of the families of bounded sets defining the topologies.
