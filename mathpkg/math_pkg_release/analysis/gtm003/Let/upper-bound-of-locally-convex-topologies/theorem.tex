Let $\{\mathfrak{T}_\alpha: \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ be a family of locally convex topologies on a vector space $E$; their least upper bound $\mathfrak{T}$ (in the lattice of topologies on $E$) is a locally convex topology which is a projective topology; in fact, $\mathfrak{T}$ is the projective topology with respect to the family $\{(E, \mathfrak{T}_\alpha, e): \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$, where $e$ is the identity map of $E$.
