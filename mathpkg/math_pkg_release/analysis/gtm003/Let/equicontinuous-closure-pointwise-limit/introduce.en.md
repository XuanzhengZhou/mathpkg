The space of all linear maps $L(E, F)$ is closed in $F^E$ under pointwise convergence. Therefore, the pointwise closure of an equicontinuous set remains within $\mathcal{L}(E, F)$ and is again equicontinuous. When $F = K_0$ (the scalar field), this implies that the weak* closure of an equicontinuous subset of the dual is weak* compact (Alaoglu-Bourbaki theorem).
