Let $E_M$ be the linear hull of $M$; $E_M$ is a non-meager subspace of $E$, and hence a Baire space. Denoting by $\tilde{u}_n$ the restriction of $u_n$ to $E_M$, it follows from (4.6) that $\lim \tilde{u}_n(x) = \tilde{u}(x)$ for all $x \in E_M$, where $\tilde{u} \in \mathcal{L}(E_M, F)$. By the corollary of (4.2), the sequence $\{u_n\}$ is norm bounded in $\mathcal{L}(E, F)$ and hence equicontinuous; thus the unique continuous extension $u$ of $\tilde{u}$ to $E$, together with $\{u_n\}$, forms an equicontinuous set. By (4.5), $\lim u_n = u$ uniformly on every precompact subset of $E$.
