As a corollary of the general theorem for complete locally convex spaces, every Fréchet space (a complete metrizable locally convex space) is isomorphic to a projective limit of a countable sequence of Banach spaces. This follows from the fact that the topology of a Fréchet space is generated by a countable family of semi-norms.
