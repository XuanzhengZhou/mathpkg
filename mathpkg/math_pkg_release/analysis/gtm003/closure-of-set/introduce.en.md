The closure of a subset $A$ of a topological space is the smallest closed set containing $A$. It consists of all points that are adherent to $A$, meaning every neighborhood of the point intersects $A$. The closure operator is a hull operator: it is idempotent, monotone, and satisfies $A \subset \bar{A}$.
