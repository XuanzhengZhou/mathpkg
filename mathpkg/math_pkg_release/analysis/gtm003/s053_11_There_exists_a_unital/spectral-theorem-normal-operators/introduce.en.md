The Spectral Theorem for normal operators on a Hilbert space establishes an isometric $*$-isomorphism between the commutative $C^*$-algebra $C(\sigma(T))$ of continuous functions on the spectrum and the $C^*$-algebra generated by $T$ and the identity. This operational calculus $f \mapsto f(T)$ generalizes the familiar functional calculus for self-adjoint operators and is a cornerstone of operator theory on Hilbert spaces.
