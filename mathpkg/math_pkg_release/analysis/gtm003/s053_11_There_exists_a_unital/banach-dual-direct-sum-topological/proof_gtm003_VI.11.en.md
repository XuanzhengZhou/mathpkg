\textit{Proof sketch (Exercise).} Observe that each projection $\pi_i$ is norm continuous (cf.\ proof of III.2.1, Corollary~3), which implies that the unit ball of $N_i = \sum_{j \neq i} M_j$ is $\sigma(W, V)$-compact. Then apply (IV.6.4) and the corollary of (6.1) to conclude that each $\pi_i$ is $\sigma(W, V)$-continuous.
