\textit{Proof sketch (Exercise).} Consider appropriate operators $V_j$ on $\ell^2(\mathbb{N})$. One constructs $n$ isometries on the Hilbert space $\ell^2(\mathbb{N})$ whose range projections sum to the identity operator. The $C^*$-algebra generated by these operators then satisfies the required relations, and a standard argument using the universal property establishes uniqueness up to isomorphism. For the complete construction, see Cuntz~\cite{cuntz1977}.
