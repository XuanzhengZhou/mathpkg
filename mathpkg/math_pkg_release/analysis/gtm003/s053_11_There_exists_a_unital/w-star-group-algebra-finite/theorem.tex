Let $G$ be a group with neutral element $e$, and let $W(G)$ be the $W^*$-group algebra. Define $\delta_e(f) := f(e)$ for $f \in \ell^2(G)$, and set
$$
\tau(x) := [x\delta_e, \delta_e]
$$
(i.e., the inner product $(x\delta_e \mid \delta_e)$ on $\ell^2(G)$). Then $\tau$ is a faithful normal trace on $W(G)$, and consequently $W(G)$ is a finite von Neumann algebra.
