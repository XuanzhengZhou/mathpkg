Let $\langle F, G \rangle$ be a duality. The mapping $M \mapsto M^\circ$ is an anti-isomorphism of the lattice of all $\sigma(F, G)$-closed subspaces of $F$ onto the lattice of all $\sigma(G, F)$-closed subspaces of $G$, where the lattice operations are defined by
$$\inf(M_1, M_2) = M_1 \cap M_2, \quad \sup(M_1, M_2) = (M_1 + M_2)^-$$
and $(\cdot)^-$ denotes the closure in the respective weak topology. Specifically,
$$(\inf(M_1, M_2))^\circ = \sup(M_1^\circ, M_2^\circ), \qquad (\sup(M_1, M_2))^\circ = \inf(M_1^\circ, M_2^\circ).$$
