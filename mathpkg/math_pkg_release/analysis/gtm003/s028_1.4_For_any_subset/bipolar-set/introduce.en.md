The bipolar $M^{\circ\circ}$ of a subset $M$ in a dual pair $\langle F, G \rangle$ is obtained by applying the polar operation twice. By definition, $M^{\circ\circ} \subset F$ is the set of all vectors in $F$ whose pairing with every element of the polar $M^\circ$ is bounded by $1$. The bipolar theorem (1.5) characterizes $M^{\circ\circ}$ as the closed convex hull of $M \cup \{0\}$ in the weak topology.
