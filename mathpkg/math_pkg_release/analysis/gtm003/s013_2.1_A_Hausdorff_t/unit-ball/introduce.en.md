The closed unit ball of a normed space $(L, \|\cdot\|)$ is the set of all vectors whose norm does not exceed 1. It is a convex, circled, bounded neighborhood of 0, and it completely determines the norm via the Minkowski gauge functional. In Banach spaces, the closed unit ball plays a central role in compactness criteria and the geometry of the space.
