Theorem 6.2 provides a complete description of the order topology $\mathfrak{T}_0$ when $L$ is Archimedean and possesses an order unit $e$. In this case, $\mathfrak{T}_0$ is normable (generated by the gauge of $[-e, e]$), making it significantly easier to work with. The theorem also establishes the equivalence between topological completeness and order-theoretic completeness, and characterizes $\mathfrak{T}_0$ as the finest locally convex topology with a normal positive cone.
