Let $L$ be an Archimedean ordered vector space over $\mathbb{R}$ such that $L = C - C$, and let $H$ be a subset of $C$ such that the family $\{[-a, a] : a \in H\}$ covers $L$. Then $(L, \mathfrak{T}_0)$ is the locally convex inductive limit of the family $\{L_a : a \in H\}$ of its ordered subspaces, where each $L_a = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} n[-a, a]$. Moreover, if each $L_a$ is complete for its order topology $\mathfrak{T}_{0,a}$, then $(L, \mathfrak{T}_0)$ is barreled.
