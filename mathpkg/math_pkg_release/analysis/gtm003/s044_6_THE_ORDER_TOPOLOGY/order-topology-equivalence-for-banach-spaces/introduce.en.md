Corollary 2 of Theorem 6.2 characterizes when the given Banach space topology $\mathfrak{T}$ coincides with the order topology $\mathfrak{T}_0$: this happens precisely when the positive cone is normal. Examples include $\mathcal{C}(X)$ for compact $X$ and $L^\infty(\mu)$. More generally, this applies to every ordered Banach space whose positive cone is normal and has non-empty interior.
