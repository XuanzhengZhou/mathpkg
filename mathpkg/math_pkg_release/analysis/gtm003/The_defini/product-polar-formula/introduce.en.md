Theorem 4.2 provides a formula for computing polars of product-type sets in the duality between a product and a direct sum: the polar of a product set $S = \prod_\alpha S_\alpha$ is the absolute convex hull of the component-wise polars. This establishes that the $\mathfrak{S}$-topology on the direct sum, generated by product families of bounded sets, coincides with the locally convex direct sum of the individual $\mathfrak{S}_\alpha$-topologies.
