Corollary 4 of Theorem 4.3 identifies the dual of the space of continuous linear maps $\mathcal{L}_s(E, F)$ under the topology of simple convergence. The dual is algebraically isomorphic to the tensor product $E \otimes F'$ via the natural pairing $\sum x_i \otimes y_i' \mapsto (u \mapsto \sum \langle u x_i, y_i' \rangle)$. This result is fundamental for the theory of topological tensor products.
