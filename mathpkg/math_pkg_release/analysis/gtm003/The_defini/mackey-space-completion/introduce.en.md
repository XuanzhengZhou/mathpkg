The Mackey property is preserved under completion: if a locally convex space $E$ is a Mackey space, then its completion $\tilde{E}$ is also a Mackey space. This follows because any convex circled $\sigma(E', \tilde{E})$-compact subset is a fortiori $\sigma(E', E)$-compact, hence equicontinuous in $E$ and therefore in $\tilde{E}$.
