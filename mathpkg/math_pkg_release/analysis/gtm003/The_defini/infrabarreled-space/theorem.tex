A l.c.s. $E$ is called infrabarreled if every bound absorbing barrel in $E$ is a neighborhood of 0. Equivalently, $\mathfrak{E} = \mathfrak{B}$, where $\mathfrak{E}$ is the family of equicontinuous subsets and $\mathfrak{B}$ the family of strongly bounded subsets of $E'$. In particular, every bornological space and every barreled space is infrabarreled.
