Theorem 4.3 describes the dual of a product of locally convex spaces and establishes key topological identities: the dual of the product is algebraically the direct sum of the duals; the weak and Mackey topologies on the product are the products of the respective topologies; and the Mackey topology on the dual is the locally convex direct sum of the individual Mackey topologies. A notable exception: the weak topology on the dual is the direct sum of weak topologies only for finite families.
