This theorem (labeled 9.3 in Schaefer's text) gives an equivalent characterization of nuclearity for a locally convex space $E$ in terms of its dual $E'$. Instead of working directly with quotient spaces of $E$ as in the original definition (III, 7.2), the condition is phrased using the spaces $E'_A$ associated with equicontinuous subsets $A$ of the weak dual $E'_\sigma$. A locally convex space $E$ is nuclear precisely when every equicontinuous set $A$ in the dual is contained in a larger equicontinuous set $B$ such that the canonical linking map $E'_A \to E'_B$ is a nuclear operator, providing a powerful tool for verifying nuclearity in concrete spaces of analysis.
