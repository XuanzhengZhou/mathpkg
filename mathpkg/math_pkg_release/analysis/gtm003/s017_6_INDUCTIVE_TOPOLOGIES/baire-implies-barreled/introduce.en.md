This proposition establishes that every locally convex space satisfying the Baire category theorem is automatically barreled. Since Frechet spaces (and hence Banach spaces) are Baire spaces, this implies all Frechet and Banach spaces are barreled. The proof uses the radial and circled nature of barrels together with the Baire property to show that every barrel contains an interior point.
