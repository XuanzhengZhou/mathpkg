This proposition ensures that the strict inductive limit of an increasing sequence of locally convex spaces is well-behaved: the resulting inductive topology is Hausdorff (separated) and restricts to the original topology on each subspace. This property is fundamental for working with (LB)-spaces and (LF)-spaces, as it guarantees that each step retains its original topological structure.
