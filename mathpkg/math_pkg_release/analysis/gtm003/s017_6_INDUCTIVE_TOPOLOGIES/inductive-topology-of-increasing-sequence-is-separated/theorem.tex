If $\{E_n(\mathfrak{T}_n): n \in \mathbb{N}\}$ is an increasing sequence of locally convex spaces such that the topology $\mathfrak{T}_{n+1}$ induces $\mathfrak{T}_n$ on $E_n$ for all $n$, and if the vector space $E$ is the union of the subspaces $E_n$ ($n \in \mathbb{N}$), then the inductive topology $\mathfrak{T}$ on $E$ with respect to the canonical imbeddings $E_n \rightarrow E$ is separated and induces the topology $\mathfrak{T}_n$ on each $E_n$.
