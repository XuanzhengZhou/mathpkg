A barrel is a subset of a topological vector space that is radial (absorbing), convex, circled (balanced), and closed. Barrels form the basis of the theory of barreled spaces, a class of locally convex spaces that includes all Frechet and Banach spaces. In a barreled space, every barrel is a neighborhood of zero.
