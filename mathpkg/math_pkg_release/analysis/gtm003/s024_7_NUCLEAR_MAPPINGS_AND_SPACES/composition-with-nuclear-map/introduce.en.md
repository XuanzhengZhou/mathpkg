Corollary 2 of Proposition 7.1 shows that the class of nuclear maps forms a two-sided ideal in the category of locally convex spaces with continuous linear maps as morphisms. Pre-composing with a continuous map preserves the nuclear series representation through the equicontinuity of the pullback functionals. Post-composing uses the compactness of the nuclear map to ensure the bounded subset condition remains satisfied in the codomain.
