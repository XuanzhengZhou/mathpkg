By the sufficiency part of (7.1), if $u$ has the nuclear series representation
$$u(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \lambda_n f_n(x) y_n,$$
let $U = \{x \in E : |f_n(x)| \leq 1,\ n \in \mathbb{N}\}$. Then $U$ is a convex, circled $0$-neighborhood by equicontinuity of $\{f_n\}$. The image $u(U)$ is contained in the closed, convex, circled hull $C$ of the null sequence $\{y_n\}$ in $F_B$. Since $\{y_n\}$ is relatively compact in $F_B$ and $F_B$ is complete, $C$ is compact in $F_B$ (cf. (I, 5.2) and (II, 4.3)), hence compact in $F$ by continuity of the canonical imbedding $F_B \to F$.
