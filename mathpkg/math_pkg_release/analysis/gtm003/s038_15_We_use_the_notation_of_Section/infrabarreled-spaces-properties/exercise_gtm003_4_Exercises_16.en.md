(Infrabarreled Spaces). The product and l.c. direct sum of any family of infrabarreled spaces is infrabarreled (use Exercise 8). Every separated quotient of an infrabarreled space is infrabarreled (immediate verification), but a closed subspace is not necessarily infrabarreled (Exercise 20).
