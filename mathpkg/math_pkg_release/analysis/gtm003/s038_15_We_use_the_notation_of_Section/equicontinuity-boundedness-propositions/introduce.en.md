This exercise investigates the logical relationships between equicontinuity and various boundedness notions for a set $Q$ of continuous linear maps between locally convex spaces $E$ and $F$. Six propositions are considered, involving equicontinuity of $Q$, equicontinuity of the adjoint set $Q'$, and boundedness of $Q$ or $Q'$ with respect to different topologies (strong topology $\beta$, weak topology $\sigma$). The task is to establish the implications between these conditions.
