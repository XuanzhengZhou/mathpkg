The weak topology $\sigma(E, F)$ depends only on the linear span of $F$ in the algebraic dual $E^*$, not on the specific set $F$ itself. This allows one to always assume without loss of generality that the generating set $F$ is a linear subspace of $E^*$, which is convenient in duality theory and when considering the dual pair $(E, F)$.
