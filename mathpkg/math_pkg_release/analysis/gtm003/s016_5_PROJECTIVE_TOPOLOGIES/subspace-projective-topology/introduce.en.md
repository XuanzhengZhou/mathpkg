The subspace topology on a linear subspace $M$ of a locally convex space $E$ is an instance of a projective topology. Indeed, it is precisely the coarsest topology on $M$ making the canonical inclusion $M \hookrightarrow E$ continuous. This perspective unifies subspace topologies with products and weak topologies under the general projective construction.
