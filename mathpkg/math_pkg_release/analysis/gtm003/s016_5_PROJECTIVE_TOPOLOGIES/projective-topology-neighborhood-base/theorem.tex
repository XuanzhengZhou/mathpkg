Let $E$ carry the projective topology $\mathfrak{T}$ with respect to the family $\{(E_\alpha, \mathfrak{T}_\alpha, f_\alpha): \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$. For $x \in E$ and $x_\alpha = f_\alpha(x) \in E_\alpha$, a $\mathfrak{T}$-neighborhood base of $x$ is given by all intersections
$$\bigcap_{\alpha \in \mathbf{H}} f_\alpha^{-1}(U_\alpha),$$
where $U_\alpha$ is any neighborhood of $x_\alpha$ with respect to $\mathfrak{T}_\alpha$, and $\mathbf{H}$ is any finite subset of $\mathbf{A}$.
