A biorthogonal system in a dual pair where both families form bases is called doubly orthogonal. This means the coefficient functionals of one basis correspond exactly to the other family, and vice versa. Such systems are fundamental in the structure theory of locally convex spaces, providing simultaneous diagonalization of the duality pairing analogous to orthonormal bases in Hilbert spaces.
