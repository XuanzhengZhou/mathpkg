Let $E, F, G$ be topological vector spaces over $K$.

(a) [Not included in this section].

(b) For each $u \in E \otimes F$, the minimal number $k$ ($\geq 0$) of summands $x_i \otimes y_i$ such that $u = \sum_{i=1}^{k} x_i \otimes y_i$, is identical with the dimension $r$ of the range of $v: x^* \mapsto v(x^*) = \sum_{i=1}^{k} x^*(x_i)y_i$. In each representation of $u$ by $r$ summands, both sets $\{x_i\}$ and $\{y_i\}$ are linearly independent.

(c) The isomorphism of (a) is onto $L(E^*, F)$ if and only if at least one of the cardinals $d_1, d_2$ is finite.

(d) The dimension of $E \otimes F$ is $d_1 d_2$.
