Let $E_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$) and $G$ be vector spaces over $K$. A mapping $(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \mapsto f(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ of $\prod_{i=1}^{n} E_i$ into $G$ is called \textbf{multilinear} (or \textbf{$n$-linear}) if each of the partial maps $E_i \to G$, obtained by fixing the coordinates $x_j$ ($j \neq i$), is linear.
