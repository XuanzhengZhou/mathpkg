This theorem characterizes nuclearity for Kothe sequence (F)-spaces via a growth condition on the defining weights $s_m^{(n)}$. The space $E$ is nuclear precisely when the ratios $s_m^{(n)} / s_m^{(n+p)}$ form a summable sequence for each $n$ and some $p$. This criterion is fundamental for recognizing nuclearity in sequence spaces and provides a concrete bridge between the abstract tensor product definition of nuclearity and explicit sequence space computations.
