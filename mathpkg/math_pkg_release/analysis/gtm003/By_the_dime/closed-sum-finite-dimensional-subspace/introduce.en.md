This proposition asserts that the algebraic sum of a closed subspace and a finite-dimensional subspace in a topological vector space over a complete field is again closed. The proof uses the quotient map and the fact that finite-dimensional subspaces of a Hausdorff quotient are closed. This is a useful technical result for working with subspace operations in t.v.s.
