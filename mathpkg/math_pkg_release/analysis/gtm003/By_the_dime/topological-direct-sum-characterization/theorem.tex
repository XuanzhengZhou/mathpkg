Let $L$ be a t.v.s. and let $L$ be the algebraic direct sum of the subspaces $M$, $N$. Then $L$ is the topological direct sum of $M$ and $N$: $L = M \oplus N$, if and only if the mapping $v$ which assigns to each equivalence class mod $M$ its unique representative in $N$ is an isomorphism of the t.v.s. $L/M$ onto the t.v.s. $N$.
