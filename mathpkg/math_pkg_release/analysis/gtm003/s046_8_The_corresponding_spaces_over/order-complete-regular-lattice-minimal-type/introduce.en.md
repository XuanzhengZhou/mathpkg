Corollary 1 to Theorem (7.5) specializes the result to vector lattices of minimal type equipped with the order topology $\mathfrak{T}_0$. For an order complete vector lattice with regular order, being of minimal type is equivalent to the convergence of section filters of majorized directed sets (respectively, all order convergent filters) with respect to $\mathfrak{T}_0$. Furthermore, when $E$ is minimal, $\mathfrak{T}_0$ is characterized as the finest locally convex topology under which every order convergent filter converges.
