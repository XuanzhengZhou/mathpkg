For a semi-reflexive locally convex space $E$ whose strong dual $E'_\beta$ is nuclear, and any nuclear space $F$, the space $\mathcal{L}_b(E, F)$ of continuous linear maps equipped with the topology of bounded convergence is nuclear. The proof uses the identification of $\mathcal{L}_b(E, F)$ with a subspace of $\mathfrak{B}_e(E_\sigma, F'_\sigma)$ and the fact that nuclearity is inherited by subspaces.
