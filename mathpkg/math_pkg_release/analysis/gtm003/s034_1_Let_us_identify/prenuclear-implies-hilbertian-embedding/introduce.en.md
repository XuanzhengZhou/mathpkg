This theorem provides a geometric characterization of spaces on which every continuous seminorm is prenuclear. Such spaces admit a neighborhood base of 0 such that each associated Banach space (the completion of the quotient by the neighborhood) is isometrically isomorphic to a Hilbert subspace of an $L^2$-space over a suitable Radon measure. This embedding into Hilbert spaces is the crucial step in proving that prenuclearity of all seminorms characterizes nuclear spaces.
