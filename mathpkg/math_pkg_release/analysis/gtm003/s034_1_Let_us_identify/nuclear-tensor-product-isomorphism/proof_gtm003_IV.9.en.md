This follows from the first corollary (complete nuclear tensor product identification). The identity map of $E \otimes F$, which is continuous for the projective topology on the domain and the topology of bi-equicontinuous convergence on the range, is an isomorphism if $E$ is nuclear. Hence the canonical mapping of $E \tilde{\otimes} F$ into $E \tilde{\otimes} F$ (the completion in the bi-equicontinuous topology) is a topological isomorphism.
