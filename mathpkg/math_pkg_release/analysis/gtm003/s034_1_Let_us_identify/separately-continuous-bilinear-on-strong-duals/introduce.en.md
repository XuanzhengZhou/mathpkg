For a nuclear (F)-space $E$ and an (F)-space $F$, every bilinear form on the product of their strong duals $E'_\beta \times F'_\beta$ that is separately continuous is automatically jointly continuous. This is a remarkable automatic continuity result that reflects the strong completeness properties of the strong duals of nuclear spaces.
