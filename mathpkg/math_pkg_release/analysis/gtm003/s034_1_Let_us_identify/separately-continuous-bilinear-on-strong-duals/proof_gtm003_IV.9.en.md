This follows from Theorem 9.9. Every separately continuous bilinear form on $E'_\beta \times F'_\beta$ defines a continuous linear map from $E'_\beta$ into $F''$ (or equivalently from $F'_\beta$ into $E''$). Since $E$ is reflexive, $E'' = E$, and the identification in Theorem 9.9 shows that such a bilinear form corresponds to an element of the completed tensor product, which yields joint continuity.
