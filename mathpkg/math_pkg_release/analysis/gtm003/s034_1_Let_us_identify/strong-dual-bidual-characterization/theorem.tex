\text{Let } E \text{ be a nuclear (F)-space and } F \text{ an (F)-space (or } E, F \text{ (DF)-spaces with } E \text{ nuclear). Then:}
\text{(i) The strong dual of } E \tilde{\otimes} F \text{ can be identified with } \mathcal{B}_b(E, F); \text{ and}
\text{(ii) The strong bidual of } E \tilde{\otimes} F \text{ can be identified with } E \otimes F'', \text{ the completed projective tensor product of } E \text{ with the strong bidual of } F.
