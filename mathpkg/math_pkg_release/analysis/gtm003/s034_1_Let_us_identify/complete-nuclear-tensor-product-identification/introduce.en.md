For a complete nuclear space $E$ and any complete locally convex space $F$, the completed projective tensor product $E \tilde{\otimes} F$ admits two canonical identifications. First, it can be identified with the space $\mathfrak{B}_e(E'_\sigma, F'_\sigma)$ of separately continuous bilinear forms on the weak duals, equipped with the topology of bi-equicontinuous convergence. Second, it can be identified with the space $\mathcal{L}_e(E'_\tau, F)$ of continuous linear maps from the strong dual $E'_\tau$ into $F$, with the topology of equicontinuous convergence. This is an immediate consequence of Theorem 9.4.
