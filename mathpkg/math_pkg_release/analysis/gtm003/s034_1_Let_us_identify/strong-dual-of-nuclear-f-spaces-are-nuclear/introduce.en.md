As a consequence of Theorem 9.6, the strong duals of all nuclear (F)-spaces are themselves nuclear spaces. This applies in particular to the space $\mathcal{D}'_C$ of distributions with compact support, the space $\mathcal{S}'$ of tempered distributions, and the space $\mathcal{H}'$ of analytic functionals. These are the fundamental distribution spaces in analysis.
