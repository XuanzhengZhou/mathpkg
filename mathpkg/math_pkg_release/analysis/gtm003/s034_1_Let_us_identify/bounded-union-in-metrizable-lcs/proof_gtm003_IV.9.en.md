We can assume the topology of $E$ is generated by an increasing sequence $\{p_n\}$ of semi-norms, and that each $B_n$ contains a point $x_n$ such that $p_n(x_n) > 0$. Define $\mu_n$ by $\mu_n^{-1} = \sup\{p_n(x): x \in B_n\}$. For any semi-norm $p_k$ of the sequence, we obtain $p_k(x) \leq 1$ whenever $x \in \bigcup_{n \geq k} \mu_n B_n$; hence $\sup\{p_k(x): x \in B\} < +\infty$. Since this holds for all $k$, it follows that $B$ is bounded.
