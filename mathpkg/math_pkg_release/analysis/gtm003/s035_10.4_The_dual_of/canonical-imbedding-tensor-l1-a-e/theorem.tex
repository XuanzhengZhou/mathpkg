The canonical imbedding of $l^1(\mathbf{A}) \otimes E$ in $l^1(\mathbf{A}, E)$ is an isomorphism for the projective topology on $l^1(\mathbf{A}) \otimes E$, and the canonical imbedding of $l^1(\mathbf{A}) \otimes E$ in $l^1[\mathbf{A}, E]$ is an isomorphism for the topology of bi-equicontinuous convergence on $l^1(\mathbf{A}) \otimes E$. Moreover, the canonical image of $l^1(\mathbf{A}) \otimes E$ is dense in both $l^1(\mathbf{A}, E)$ and $l^1[\mathbf{A}, E]$.
