The dual of $l^1(\mathbf{A}, E)$ can be identified with the subspace of $(E')^\mathbf{A}$ each of whose elements constitutes a prenuclear family $\mathbf{x}' = \{x'_\alpha : \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ in $E'$, the canonical bilinear form being given by
$$\langle \mathbf{x}, \mathbf{x}' \rangle = \sum_\alpha \langle x_\alpha, x'_\alpha \rangle.$$
