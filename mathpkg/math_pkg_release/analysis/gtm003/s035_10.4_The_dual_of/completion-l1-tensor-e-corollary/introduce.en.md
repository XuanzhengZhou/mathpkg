This corollary to Theorem 10.6 describes the completed projective and bi-equicontinuous tensor products of $l^1(\mathbf{A})$ with a locally convex space $E$. The completed projective tensor product $l^1(\mathbf{A}) \mathbin{\tilde{\otimes}}_\pi E$ is canonically isomorphic to $l^1[\mathbf{A}, \tilde{E}]$ (summable families in the completion), while the completed tensor product under bi-equicontinuous convergence corresponds to $l^1(\mathbf{A}, \tilde{E})$ (absolutely summable families). Under these identifications, the natural map from the projective to the bi-equicontinuous completion becomes the canonical imbedding of $l^1[\mathbf{A}, \tilde{E}]$ into $l^1(\mathbf{A}, \tilde{E})$, which is injective. This result provides a concrete functional-analytic realization of abstract completed tensor products.
