Let $L$ be an ordered vector space which is the algebraic direct sum of the subspaces $M_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$). Then $L$ is said to be the \textbf{ordered direct sum} of the subspaces $M_i$ if the canonical algebraic isomorphism of $L$ onto $\prod_i M_i$ is an order isomorphism (for the canonical ordering of $\prod_i M_i$).
