In a regularly ordered vector lattice, extreme rays of the dual cone $C^*$ can be characterized by a condition on the kernel: a positive linear form $f$ generates an extreme ray iff its kernel $f^{-1}(0)$ is a solid subspace. Equivalently, $\inf(f(x^+), f(x^-)) = 0$ for all $x \in E$, which expresses that the positive and negative parts of each element cannot both have positive values under $f$.
