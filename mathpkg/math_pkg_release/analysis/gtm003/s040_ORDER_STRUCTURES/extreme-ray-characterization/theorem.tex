Let $E$ be a regularly ordered vector lattice and let $f \in C^*$ (where $C$ is the positive cone of $E$). Then the following are equivalent:
(a) $\inf(f(x^+), f(x^-)) = 0$ for each $x \in E$;
(b) $f^{-1}(0)$ is a solid subspace of $E$;
(c) $f$ generates an extreme ray of $C^*$;
(d) $f \geq 0$ and $f^{-1}(0)$ is solid.
