In a vector lattice, the disjointness relation between two elements propagates to the bands they generate. If $x$ and $y$ are disjoint, then every element in the band generated by $x$ is disjoint from every element in the band generated by $y$. This follows from the fact that $y \in \{x\}^{\perp}$ and $x \in \{y\}^{\perp}$.
