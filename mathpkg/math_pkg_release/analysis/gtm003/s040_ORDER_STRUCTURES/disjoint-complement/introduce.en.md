The disjoint complement $A^\perp$ of a subset $A$ in a vector lattice is the set of all elements disjoint from every element of $A$. This is the lattice-theoretic analogue of the orthogonal complement in Hilbert space theory, and it plays a central role in the theory of bands and projections in vector lattices.
