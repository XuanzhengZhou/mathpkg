This proposition collects the fundamental algebraic identities that hold in every vector lattice. It establishes the relationship between addition and the lattice operations (3), the canonical decomposition $x = x^+ - x^-$ into disjoint positive elements, scaling rules for positive/negative parts (4), the triangle inequality (5), a continuity estimate for the positive part map (6), and the Riesz decomposition property (D) which states that order intervals behave additively.
