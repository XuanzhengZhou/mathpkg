If $A$ is a subset of $L$, the \text{linear hull} of $A$ is the intersection $M$ of all subspaces of $L$ that contain $A$; $M$ is also said to be the \text{subspace of $L$ generated by $A$}. $M$ can also be characterized as the set of all linear combinations of elements of $A$ (including the sum over the empty subset of $A$). In particular, the linear hull of $\emptyset$ is $\{0\}$.
