A vector subspace is a non-empty subset of a vector space that is itself a vector space under the inherited operations of addition and scalar multiplication. The defining conditions are closure under addition and closure under scalar multiplication. The set of all subspaces of a given vector space is closed under arbitrary intersections, which is a key property used in defining the linear hull.
