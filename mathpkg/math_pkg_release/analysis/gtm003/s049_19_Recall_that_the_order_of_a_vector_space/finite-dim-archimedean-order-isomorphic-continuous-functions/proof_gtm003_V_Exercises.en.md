Use (8.5) from the text. In a finite-dimensional Archimedean ordered space, the positive cone is closed and generating. The extreme rays of the dual cone correspond to evaluation functionals at points of $X$. By choosing $n$ extreme rays, one obtains an isomorphism with $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbf{R}}(X)$ where $|X| = n$.
