Every spectral measure on a compact space $X$ extends uniquely to an algebraic homomorphism from the Banach algebra of bounded Baire functions into the range algebra. This extension maps the Boolean algebra of Baire sets onto a $\sigma$-complete Boolean algebra of idempotents (projections) in the algebra.
