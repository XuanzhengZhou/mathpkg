Every closed two-sided ideal $J$ of a $C^*$-algebra $A$ is self-adjoint ($J = J^*$), and the quotient $A/J$ is a $C^*$-algebra with the quotient norm and the natural involution $(x + J)^* = x^* + J$. Moreover, the quotient map $q: A \to A/J$ satisfies $\|q(x)\| = \inf_{u \in J_1} \|x(e - u)\|$, where $J_1$ is the set of positive elements of norm $\leq 1$ in $J$.
