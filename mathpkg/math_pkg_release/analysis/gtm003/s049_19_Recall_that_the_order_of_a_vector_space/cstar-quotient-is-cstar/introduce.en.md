The quotient of a C*-algebra by a closed two-sided ideal is again a C*-algebra. Moreover, every closed two-sided ideal is automatically self-adjoint, a property that does not hold for general Banach algebras. This theorem is essential for understanding the ideal structure of C*-algebras.
