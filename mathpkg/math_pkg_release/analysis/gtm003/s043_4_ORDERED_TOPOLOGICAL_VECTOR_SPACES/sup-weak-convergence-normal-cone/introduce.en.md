This corollary characterizes weak convergence of a directed set to its supremum in terms of positive continuous linear forms. When a directed set has a supremum, the condition that every positive continuous linear form attains its supremum on the set is equivalent to the weak convergence of the set. The uniform convergence on equicontinuous subsets of the dual provides a stronger quantitative statement.
