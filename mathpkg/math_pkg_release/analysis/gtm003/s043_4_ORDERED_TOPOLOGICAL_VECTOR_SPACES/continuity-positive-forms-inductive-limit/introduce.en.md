This corollary extends the automatic continuity result to inductive limits: any positive linear form on an ordered locally convex space that is the inductive limit of ordered Frechet spaces (each satisfying the cone-generation condition) is automatically continuous. The proof follows immediately from the bornological property of inductive limits combined with Theorem 5.5.
