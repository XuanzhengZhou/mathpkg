This is immediate in view of (II, 6.1). The inductive limit of bornological (F)-spaces is bornological, and the condition $E_\alpha = C_\alpha - C_\alpha$ transfers to the inductive limit, so condition (ii) or (iii) of Theorem 5.5 applies to each $E_\alpha$ and consequently to their inductive limit $E$.
