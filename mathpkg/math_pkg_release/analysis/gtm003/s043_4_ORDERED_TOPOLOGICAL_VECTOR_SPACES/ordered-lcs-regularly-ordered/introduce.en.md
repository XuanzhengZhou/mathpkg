This corollary shows that the (LTO) axiom, which requires the positive cone to be closed, automatically implies that the space is regularly ordered whenever the space is locally convex. The proof relies on the bipolar theorem and the duality between the space and its continuous dual, establishing a fundamental connection between topological closedness of the cone and order regularity.
