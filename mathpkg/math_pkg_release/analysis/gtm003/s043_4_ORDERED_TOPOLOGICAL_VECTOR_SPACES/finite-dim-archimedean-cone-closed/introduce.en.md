This lemma establishes that in any Archimedean ordered vector space, the restriction of the positive cone to any finite-dimensional subspace is closed. It serves as a technical tool in the proof of the characterization theorem for regularly ordered topological vector spaces. The result follows from the fact that a finite-dimensional Archimedean ordered vector space carries a unique Hausdorff vector topology in which all cones are closed.
