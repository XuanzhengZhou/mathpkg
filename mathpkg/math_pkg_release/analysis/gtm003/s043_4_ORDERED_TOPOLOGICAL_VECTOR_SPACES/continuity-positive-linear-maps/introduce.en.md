This proposition shows that under appropriate conditions, every positive linear map between ordered locally convex spaces is automatically continuous. The hypothesis requires the domain to be a Mackey space on which every positive linear form is continuous, and the codomain cone to be weakly normal. The proof uses the algebraic adjoint to reduce the continuity of the map to the continuity of positive linear forms on the domain.
