Let $L$ be a t.v.s. over $K$.

\begin{itemize}
  \item[(i)] The closure of a bounded (respectively, totally bounded) subset of $L$ is bounded (respectively, totally bounded).
  \item[(ii)] Every finite union of bounded (respectively, totally bounded) subsets of $L$ is bounded (respectively, totally bounded).
  \item[(iii)] The circled hull of a bounded set is bounded.
  \item[(iv)] If $K$ is locally precompact, then the circled hull of a totally bounded set is totally bounded.
\end{itemize}
