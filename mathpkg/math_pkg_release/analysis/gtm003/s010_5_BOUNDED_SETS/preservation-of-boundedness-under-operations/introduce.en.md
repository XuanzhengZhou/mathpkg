Corollary 1 to Theorem 5.1 summarizes the stability of boundedness and total boundedness under the three most fundamental operations: finite sums, finite unions, and affine dilatations (translations composed with scalar multiplication). These preservation properties justify treating bounded sets and totally bounded sets as natural structures (bornologies) on topological vector spaces.
