Let a t.v.s. $L$ be the algebraic direct sum of $n$ subspaces $M_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$). Then $L = M_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus M_n$ (topological direct sum) if and only if the associated projections $u_i$ are continuous ($i = 1, \ldots, n$). Since the identity map is continuous, the continuity of $n-1$ of these projections implies the continuity of the remaining one.
