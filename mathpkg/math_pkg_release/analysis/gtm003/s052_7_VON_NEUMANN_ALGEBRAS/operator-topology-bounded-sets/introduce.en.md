This result (Theorem 7.2) shows that the collections of bounded sets coincide for four natural topologies on $\mathcal{L}(H)$: the norm topology, the $\sigma$-weak topology, the strong operator topology, and the weak operator topology. The equalities follow from the Banach dual identification $\mathcal{L}(H) \cong (H \hat{\otimes} H)'$, the principle of uniform boundedness, and the fact that the strong and weak operator topologies share the same dual space.
