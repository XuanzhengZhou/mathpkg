A factor is a von Neumann algebra with trivial center (only scalar multiples of the identity). Factors are the building blocks of general von Neumann algebras: every von Neumann algebra on a separable Hilbert space admits a direct integral decomposition into factors. This reduces the classification of von Neumann algebras to the classification of factors, which fall into types I, II$_1$, II$_\infty$, and III.
