This lemma ensures that the properties of being abelian or finite are preserved under orthogonal sums of centrally orthogonal projections. The proof uses the fact that centrally orthogonal projections yield a direct sum decomposition $pWp = \bigoplus_\alpha p_\alpha W p_\alpha$, so commutativity (abelian) and finiteness lift from the summands to the sum. This result is used in constructing the maximal abelian and finite summands in the type decomposition theorem (8.7).
