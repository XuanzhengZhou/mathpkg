This corollary shows that the Banach bidual $A''$ of any $C^*$-algebra $A$ can be endowed with a $W^*$-algebra (von Neumann algebra) structure. Under this structure, the original $A$ embeds as a $C^*$-subalgebra of its bidual. The proof uses the universal representation of $A$, demonstrating that $A''$ is isometrically isomorphic to the strong closure of the universal representation, which is a von Neumann algebra by the bicommutant theorem.
