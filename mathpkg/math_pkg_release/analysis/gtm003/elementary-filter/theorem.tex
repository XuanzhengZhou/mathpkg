An elementary filter is the section filter of a sequence $\{x_n : n \in \mathbb{N}\}$ in $X$, where $\mathbb{N}$ is endowed with its usual order. The section filter of a directed family $\{x_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ is defined analogously, with sections $S_\alpha = \{x_\beta : \beta \geq \alpha\}$.
