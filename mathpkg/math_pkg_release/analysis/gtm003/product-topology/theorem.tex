Let $\{X_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ be a family of topological spaces. The product topology on $X = \prod_\alpha X_\alpha$ is the coarsest topology for which all canonical projections $\pi_\alpha: X \to X_\alpha$ are continuous. A base is given by sets $\prod_\alpha G_\alpha$ where $G_\alpha$ is open in $X_\alpha$ and $G_\alpha = X_\alpha$ for all but finitely many $\alpha$.
