The converse direction of Theorem 1.2: any filter base satisfying the three conditions (a) $U+U \subset V$, (b) radial and circled, (c) scaling property, generates a unique TVS topology. This is the practical method for defining TVS topologies, e.g., the $K[t]$ topology using $\sum |\alpha_n|^r \leq \varepsilon$.
