The space $E = C([0,1])$ endowed with the topology of pointwise convergence is not metrizable. The proof proceeds by contradiction: assuming a countable local base $\{U_{T_i, \varepsilon_i}\}$ exists, one chooses a point $s \in [0,1]$ not in the countable union of the finite sets $T_i$, and observes that no $U_{T_i, \varepsilon_i}$ can be contained in $U_{\{s\}, 1}$.
