The topology of pointwise convergence on $E = C([0,1])$ is weaker than the topology on $E$ defined by the norm $\|x\| = \sup \{ |x(s)| : s \in S \}$. Consequently, the identity mapping from $E$ with the norm topology onto $E$ with the topology of pointwise convergence is a continuous image of a metrizable linear topological space which is not metrizable.
