This corollary follows from Theorem 22.1 together with Proposition 19.5. It states that every locally convex pseudo-metrizable space automatically carries a bound topology, and consequently also the Mackey topology. The Mackey topology is the strongest locally convex topology compatible with the given dual pairing, so this result places a significant structural constraint on the topology of such spaces.
