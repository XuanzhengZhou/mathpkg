Let $E$ be a separable pseudo-metrizable linear topological space of dimension greater than one. Then $E$ contains a countable dense set $A$ no three points of which lie on any straight line. The complement of $A$ is radial at each of its points, but has void interior. (Let $\{x_n\}$ be dense, and put $S_n = \{x: x \in E, d(x,x_n) < 1/n\}$. Let $y_1 = x_1$; choose $y_2$ in $S_2 \setminus \{x_1\}$; choose $y_3$ in $S_3$ but not on the line determined by $y_1$ and $y_2$; and so on. Let $A = \{y_n: n = 1, 2, \ldots\}$.)
