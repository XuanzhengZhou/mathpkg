This exercise establishes a fundamental structure theorem: every locally convex Hausdorff space can be embedded as a topological vector subspace of a product of normed spaces. The embedding is constructed by taking the product over all continuous pseudo-norms, where each factor is the normed quotient $E/p^{-1}(0)$. This result shows that normed spaces are the basic building blocks of all locally convex spaces.
