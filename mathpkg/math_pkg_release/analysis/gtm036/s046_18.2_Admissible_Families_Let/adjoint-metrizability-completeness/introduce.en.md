This theorem provides a complete characterization of metrizability and completeness for the strong dual of a locally convex space. Part (i) links metrizability of the strong dual to the existence of a countable fundamental system of bounded sets. Part (ii) identifies the strong closure of the adjoint as the space of linear functionals continuous on each bounded subset. Part (iii) characterizes completeness of the strong dual in terms of whether bounded-continuous linear functionals are automatically continuous on the whole space.
