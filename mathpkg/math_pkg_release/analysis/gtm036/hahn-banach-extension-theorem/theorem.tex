Let $E$ be a real linear space, let $F$ be a subspace, let $p$ be a subadditive non-negatively homogeneous functional on $E$, and let $f$ be a linear functional on $F$ such that $f(x) \leq p(x)$ for all $x$ in $F$. Then there is a linear functional $f^{\sim}$ on $E$, an extension of $f$, such that $f^{\sim}(x) \leq p(x)$ for all $x$ in $E$.
