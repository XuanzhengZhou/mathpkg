The quotient mapping $Q: E \to E/F$ sends each element $x \in E$ to its coset $x + F$. This canonical linear surjection is the universal map associated with the quotient space construction. It is straightforward to verify that $Q$ is linear and onto $E/F$, making it a fundamental tool for studying linear mappings via factorization through quotients.
