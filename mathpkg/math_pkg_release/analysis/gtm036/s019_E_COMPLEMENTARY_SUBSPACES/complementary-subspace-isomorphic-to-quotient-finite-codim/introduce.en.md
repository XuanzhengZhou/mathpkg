In a Hausdorff linear topological space, if $F$ is a closed subspace of finite co-dimension, then every algebraic complement of $F$ is topologically isomorphic to the quotient space $E/F$. This result establishes that all complements of such a subspace share the same topological vector space structure as the quotient.
