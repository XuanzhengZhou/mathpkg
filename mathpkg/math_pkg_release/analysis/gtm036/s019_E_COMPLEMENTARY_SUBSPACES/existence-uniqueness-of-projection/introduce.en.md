For any closed convex subset $F$ of a Hilbert space $H$ and any point $x \in H$, there exists a unique point in $F$ that minimizes the distance to $x$. Existence is proved using the parallelogram law to show that any minimizing sequence is Cauchy, and uniqueness follows from the strict convexity of the Hilbert space norm.
