This Cauchy criterion characterizes when an arbitrary indexed family of vectors in a Hilbert space is summable (i.e., the net of finite partial sums converges). The condition is that the partial sums over subsets disjoint from a sufficiently large finite set have arbitrarily small norm. This generalizes the Cauchy criterion for series convergence to unordered sums.
