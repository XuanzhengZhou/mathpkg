If $E$ is a vector lattice and $f$ is a linear functional on $E$, then the following three conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] the functional $f$ is positive and, if $x$ and $y$ are disjoint positive elements of $E$, then $f(x) \wedge f(y) = 0$;
\item[(ii)] the functional $f$ is a lattice homomorphism; and
\item[(iii)] the functional $f$ is positive and, if $g$ is a linear functional such that $0 \leq g \leq f$, then $g$ is a scalar multiple of $f$.
\end{enumerate}
