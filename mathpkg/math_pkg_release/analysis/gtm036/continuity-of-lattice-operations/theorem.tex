Let $E$ be a vector lattice and let $\mathcal{T}$ be a locally convex topology for $E$ such that the set of all continuous pseudo-norms admits a base consisting of lattice pseudo-norms. Then the lattice operations on $E$ are continuous relative to the topology $\mathcal{T}$. In particular the lattice operations are continuous relative to the Mackey topology $m(E,E^{\star})$.
