Let $V$ be a closed, convex and circled neighborhood of 0 in $H$. Then the set $W = \bigcap \{f^{-1}[V] : f \in F\}$ is a closed, convex and circled set which absorbs each point of $A$ (theorem 12.1(iv)). In view of corollary 10.3, the set $W$ absorbs $A$; hence $F$ is uniformly bounded on $A$ (theorem 12.1(iii)).
