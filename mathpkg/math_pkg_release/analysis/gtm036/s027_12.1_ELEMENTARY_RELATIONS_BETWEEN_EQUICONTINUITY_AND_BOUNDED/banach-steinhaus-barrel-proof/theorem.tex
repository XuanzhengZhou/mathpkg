(Proposition 12.1) Let $F$ be a family of continuous linear functions, each from a linear topological space $E$ to a linear topological space $G$, and suppose $F$ is pointwise bounded on a set of second category in $E$. For each neighborhood $V$ of $0$ in $G$, the set $\bigcap\{f^{-1}[V] : f \in F\}$ is a barrel in $E$, hence a neighborhood of $0$ in $E$, and consequently $F$ is equicontinuous.
