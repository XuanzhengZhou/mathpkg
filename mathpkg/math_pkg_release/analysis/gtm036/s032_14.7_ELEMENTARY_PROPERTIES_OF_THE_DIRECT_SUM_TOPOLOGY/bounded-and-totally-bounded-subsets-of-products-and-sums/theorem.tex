Let $\{E_t : t \in A\}$ be a family of locally convex Hausdorff spaces.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] The family of all products of totally bounded (respectively, bounded) subsets of the factors is a co-base for the family of totally bounded (respectively, bounded) subsets of the product $\prod \{E_t : t \in A\}$ of linear topological spaces.

Equivalently, a subset of a product is totally bounded (respectively, bounded) if and only if its projection into each coordinate space is totally bounded (respectively, bounded).

\item[(ii)] The family of all convex extensions of finite unions of images under injection of totally bounded (respectively, bounded) subsets of the factors is a co-base for the family of totally bounded (respectively, bounded) subsets of the direct sum $\sum \{E_t : t \in A\}$ of locally convex Hausdorff spaces.
\end{enumerate}
