Every normed lattice can be completed to a Banach lattice by taking its closure in the bidual. This follows from the fact that the evaluation map is a lattice isomorphism (Theorem 24.4) together with the continuity of the lattice operations in the bidual (23.14). The resulting Banach lattice $E^-$ contains the original normed lattice $E$ as a dense sublattice, providing a canonical completion of $E$ that respects both the norm and the order structure.
