The spectrum of an $M$ space with unit is the space of all normalized real lattice homomorphisms equipped with the weak* topology. It plays the role of the underlying compact Hausdorff space in the Gelfand-type representation theorem for $M$ spaces (Theorem 24.6). The spectrum is a weak* compact subset of the dual unit sphere, and its points correspond bijectively to evaluation functionals on the representing space $C(X)$.
