Let $E$ be a normed space of type $M$ which has a unit $u$. The **spectrum** $X$ of $E$ is defined to be the set of all real lattice homomorphisms of $E$ which are of norm one, equipped with the weak* topology. Equivalently, $X$ is the set of all homomorphisms $h \in E^*$ such that $h(u) = 1$, with the weak* topology.

The spectrum $X$ is a weak* closed subset of the unit sphere of $E^*$. Each $h \in X$ is positive, and $\|h\| = h(u) = 1$.
