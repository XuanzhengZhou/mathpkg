This theorem shows that any Archimedean vector lattice whose positive cone has a radial point can be canonically endowed with an $M$ space structure. The norm is the Minkowski functional of the order interval $[-u, u]$, i.e., $\|x\| = \inf\{t > 0 : |x| \leq t u\}$. This provides a fundamental link between the purely algebraic notion of an Archimedean vector lattice with an order unit and the topological notion of an $M$ space, and is a key ingredient in the structure theory of vector lattices.
