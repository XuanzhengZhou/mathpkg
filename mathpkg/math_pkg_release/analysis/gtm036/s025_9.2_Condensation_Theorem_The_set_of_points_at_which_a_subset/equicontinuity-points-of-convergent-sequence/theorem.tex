Let $X$ be a topological space and let $Y$ be a pseudo-metric space. Let $\{f_n\}$ be a sequence of continuous functions on $X$ to $Y$ which converges pointwise to a function $f$. Then the set of points of equicontinuity of the sequence $\{f_n\}$ is a residual subset of $X$ (its complement is of the first category). In particular, $f$ is continuous at the points of a residual subset of $X$.
