Let $A$ be a convex set in a real linear space $E$ and suppose that $p$ is a convex functional on $A$ (that is, $p\!\left(\sum \{a_i x_i : 1 \leq i \leq n\}\right) \leq \sum \{a_i p(x_i) : 1 \leq i \leq n\}$ whenever $x_i \in A$, $a_i \geq 0$, and $\sum \{a_i : 1 \leq i \leq n\} = 1$). Let $F$ be a subspace of $E$ and $f$ a linear functional on $F$ such that $f \leq p$ on $A \cap F$. Then, if $A \subset F - C$ where $C$ is the conical extension of $A$, there exists a linear functional $\bar{f}$ on $E$ such that $\bar{f}$ is an extension of $f$ and $\bar{f} \leq p$ on $A$.
