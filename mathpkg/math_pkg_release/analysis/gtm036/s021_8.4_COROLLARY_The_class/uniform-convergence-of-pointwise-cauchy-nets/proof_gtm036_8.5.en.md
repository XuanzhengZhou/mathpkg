Let $U$ be a closed neighborhood of $0$ in $E$. If $\alpha$ is such that

$$f_{\alpha}(t) - f_{\beta}(t) \in U$$

for all $t$ in $A$ and all $\beta \geq \alpha$, then

$$f_{\alpha}(t) - f(t) \in U$$

for all $t$ in $A$, because $U$ is closed. Hence $\{f_{\gamma}, \gamma \in \Gamma\}$ converges to $f$ uniformly on $A$.
