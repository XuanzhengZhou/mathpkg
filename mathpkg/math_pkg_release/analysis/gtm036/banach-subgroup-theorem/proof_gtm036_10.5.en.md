Let $N = (A - A)^i$; by the difference theorem, $N$ is a neighborhood of $0$. Since $G \supset G - G \supset N$ and $0 \in N$, we have $G \supset G - G \supset G - N \supset G$, and hence $G = G - N$. Since $N$ is open, so is $-N$; it follows that $G - N$ is open and that $G^- \subset G - N$. Hence $G = G^i = G^-$. From the connectedness of $E$ it follows that $G = E$.
