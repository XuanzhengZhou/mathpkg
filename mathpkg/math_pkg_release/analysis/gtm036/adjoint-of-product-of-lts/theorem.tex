A linear functional $\phi$ on a product $igtimes \{E_t: t \in A\}$ of linear topological spaces is continuous relative to the product topology if and only if $\phi$ can be represented in the form $\phi(x) = \sum \{g_t(x_t): t \in A\}$ where $g$ is a member of the direct sum $\sum \{E_t^{*}: t \in A\}$ of the adjoints.
