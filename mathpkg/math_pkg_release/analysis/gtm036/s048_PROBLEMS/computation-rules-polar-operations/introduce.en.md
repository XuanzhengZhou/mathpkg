This result establishes the basic computation rules for two fundamental operations on families of convex circled sets: the "absorbed by" operation ($~$) and the "absorbs" operation ($^~$). These operations are central to the duality between bounded sets and neighborhoods of zero in locally convex spaces. They form a Galois connection and provide the foundation for the theory of bound spaces developed in Section 19.
