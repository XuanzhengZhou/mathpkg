In the context of a dual pairing $(E,F)$, this result connects the weak topology $w(E,F)$ with any admissible topology $\mathcal{T}$ on $E$. It states that convex subsets which are compact in the weak topology are necessarily bounded in every admissible topology. This provides a useful bridge between weak compactness and boundedness in the general theory of locally convex spaces and dual pairs.
