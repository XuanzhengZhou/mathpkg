Let $E$ be a linear topological space with topology $\mathcal{T}$, such that

$(P)$: the only vector topologies which have local bases consisting of $\mathcal{T}$-closed sets are those weaker than $\mathcal{T}$ (that is, $\mathcal{T}$ is maximal among vector topologies with $\mathcal{T}$-closed local bases).

Under this condition, the Banach-Steinhaus theorem (12.2) can be extended. This, together with 7C, yields an alternative proof of 18.3.
