This counterexample demonstrates that in general, the weak* closed convex extension of a weak* compact set need not be weak* compact. The construction uses the space of finitely supported sequences with the $\ell^1$-norm. It also shows that in this space, the weak* topology coincides with the Mackey topology, and the dual equipped with the Mackey topology fails to be sequentially complete.
