On any normed space, the norm function is weakly lower semicontinuous. This means that for any weakly convergent net, the norm of the limit does not exceed the limit inferior of the norms. This property plays a fundamental role in the study of weak topologies on Banach spaces and is closely related to the fact that closed convex sets are weakly closed.
