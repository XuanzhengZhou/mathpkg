This theorem gives an elegant characterization of barrelled locally convex spaces in terms of their relationship with other locally convex topologies. A space is barrelled precisely when no strictly stronger locally convex topology can have a local base consisting of sets that are closed in the original topology. This is closely related to property (P) used in the extension of the Banach-Steinhaus theorem.
