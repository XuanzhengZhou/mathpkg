The universal property of the product topology: a function $f: Z \to \prod_{t \in A} X_t$ is continuous precisely when every coordinate function $P_t \circ f: Z \to X_t$ is continuous. This characterizes the product topology as the coarsest topology with this property, and it is the categorical product in the category of topological spaces.
