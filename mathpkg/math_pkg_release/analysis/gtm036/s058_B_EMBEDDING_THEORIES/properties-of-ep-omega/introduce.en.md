This proposition summarizes key structural properties of the projective limit space $E^p(\Omega) = \varprojlim_{\omega \in \Omega} l^p(\omega)$. It is Hausdorff precisely when the weight family $\Omega$ separates points of $A$. For all $1 \leq p \leq \infty$, the space is complete in its projective limit topology. The algebraic dual $E^p(\Omega)^+$ is described as the union of the duals $l^p(\omega)^+$ over all weights $\omega \in \Omega$, with the canonical pointwise pairing.
