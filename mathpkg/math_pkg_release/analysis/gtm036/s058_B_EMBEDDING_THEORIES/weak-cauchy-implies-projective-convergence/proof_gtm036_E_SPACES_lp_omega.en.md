The proof uses the fact that $E^p(\Omega)$ is the projective limit of the $l^p(\omega)$ spaces. A sequence Cauchy in the weak topology of $E^p(\Omega)$ projects to a sequence Cauchy in the weak topology of each $l^p(\omega)$. Since $l^p(\omega)$ spaces are weakly sequentially complete for $1 < p < \infty$, the projections converge weakly. By the structure of the projective limit topology, this implies convergence in $E^p(\Omega)$.
