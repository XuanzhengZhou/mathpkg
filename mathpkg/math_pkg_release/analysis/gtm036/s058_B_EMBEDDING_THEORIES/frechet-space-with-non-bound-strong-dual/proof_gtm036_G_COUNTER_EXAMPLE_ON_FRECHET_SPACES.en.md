The construction produces a Fréchet space $E$ such that the strong dual $E^*$ fails to be bound. The equivalence with non-evaluable and non-barrelled follows from Theorem 22.15, which states that for the strong dual of a pseudo-metrizable space, the properties of being bound, evaluable, and barrelled are equivalent. The explicit construction is detailed in the text.
