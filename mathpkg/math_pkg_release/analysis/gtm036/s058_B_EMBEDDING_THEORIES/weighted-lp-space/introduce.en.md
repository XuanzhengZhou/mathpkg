The weighted $l^p$ space $l^p(\omega)$ is the $L^p$ space with respect to the purely atomic measure $\mu_\omega$ defined by a non-negative weight function $\omega$ on an index set $A$. The norm is given by the weighted sum $\left(\sum |x(t)|^p \omega(t)\right)^{1/p}$. Functions are identified if they differ only on points where $\omega$ vanishes. The dual of $l^p(\omega)$ for $1 \leq p < \infty$ is the weighted $l^q$ space with the conjugate exponent $q$, generalizing the classical duality $l^p \cong (l^q)^*$.
