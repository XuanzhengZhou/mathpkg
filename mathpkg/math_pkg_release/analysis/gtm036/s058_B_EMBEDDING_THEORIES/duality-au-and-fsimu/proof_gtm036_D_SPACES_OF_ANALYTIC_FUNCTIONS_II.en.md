Since both are generalized $LF$ spaces, it is sufficient, by 22C(e), to show that the algebraic isomorphism of $F(\sim U)$ onto $A(U)^*$ is continuous. This can be achieved by proving that, for each open set $V$ containing $\sim U$, the mapping of $A(V)$ into $A(U)^*$ is continuous. The natural pairing is given by integration along a contour separating the singularities, and the continuity follows from standard estimates for the Cauchy integral formula.
