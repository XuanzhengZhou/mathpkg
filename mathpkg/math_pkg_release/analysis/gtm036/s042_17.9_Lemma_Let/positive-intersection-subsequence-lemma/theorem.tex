Let $m$ be a non-negative real valued additive function defined on an algebra $\mathcal{A}$, and let $\{A_k\}$ be a sequence of members of $\mathcal{A}$ such that, for some positive number $e$, $m(A_k) > e$ for all $k$. Then there exists a subsequence $\{A_{k_i}\}$ of $\{A_k\}$ such that $m\left(\bigcap \{A_{k_i} : i = 1, \cdots, n\}\right)$ is positive for all $n$.
