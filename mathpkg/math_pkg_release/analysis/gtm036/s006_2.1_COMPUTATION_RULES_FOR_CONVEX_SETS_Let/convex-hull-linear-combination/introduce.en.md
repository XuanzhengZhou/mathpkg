The convex hull operator commutes with affine combinations of sets: the convex hull of $aA + bB$ equals the same combination of the individual convex hulls. This property shows that taking convex hulls is compatible with the linear structure of the ambient space. It is one of the fundamental computation rules for convex sets established in Section 2.1.
