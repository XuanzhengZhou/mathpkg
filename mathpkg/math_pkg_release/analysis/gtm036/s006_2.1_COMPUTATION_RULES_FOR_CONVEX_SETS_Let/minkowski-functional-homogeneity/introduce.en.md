A simple computation shows that the Minkowski functional $p$ is positively homogeneous: $p(ax) = a p(x)$ for all non-negative scalars $a$. This is one of the two key properties (along with subadditivity for convex $U$) that make the Minkowski functional a seminorm when $U$ is convex and circled. The homogeneity follows directly from the definition via an infimum.
