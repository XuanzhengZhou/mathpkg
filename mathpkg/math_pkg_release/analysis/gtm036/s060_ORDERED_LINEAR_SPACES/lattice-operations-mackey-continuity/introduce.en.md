The lattice operations (join and meet) are continuous with respect to the Mackey topology $m(E, E^\star)$ determined by the order dual. This is a crucial compatibility result: it means the vector lattice structure and the natural topological structure derived from the order dual are in harmony. When the Mackey topology is the relevant topology, one can freely pass limits through lattice operations.
