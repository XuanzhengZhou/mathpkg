A cone in a real linear space is a convex subset closed under multiplication by non-negative scalars. Cones provide the geometric foundation for vector orderings: every cone naturally determines a partial ordering, and conversely, the positive cone of an ordering fully determines the ordering. This correspondence between cones and vector orderings is the starting point for the theory of ordered linear spaces.
