Let $E$ and $F$ be paired linear spaces, let $H$ be a subspace of $E$, and let $H$ and $F/H^\circ$ and $E/H$ and $H^\circ$ have the induced pairings. Then

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] The topology $w(E,F)$ relativized to $H$ is identical with the topology $w(H, F/H^\circ)$.

\item[(ii)] The quotient topology for $E/H$ which is derived from $w(E,F)$ is identical with $w(E/H, H^\circ)$.
\end{enumerate}
