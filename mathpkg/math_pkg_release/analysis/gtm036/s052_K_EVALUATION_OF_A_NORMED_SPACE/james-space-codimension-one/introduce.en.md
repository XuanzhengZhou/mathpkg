This proposition provides the explicit description of the dual of James' space. Each functional in the dual admits a series expansion in terms of the coordinate functionals, with the constant sequence playing the role of a distinguished basis vector. The key conclusion is that the canonical embedding of the space into its dual image has codimension one, meaning the image is a hyperplane in the dual — as close to being reflexive as possible without actually being reflexive.
