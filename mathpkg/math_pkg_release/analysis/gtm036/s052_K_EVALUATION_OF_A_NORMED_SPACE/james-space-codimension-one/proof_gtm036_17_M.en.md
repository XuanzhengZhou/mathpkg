Show that $\zeta_n \to \zeta_0'$, say, and take $\zeta_n' = \zeta_n - \zeta_0'$. The convergence $\zeta_n \to \zeta_0'$ follows from the norm convergence of the series representation in the dual. With this identification, one verifies that the canonical image of $E$ in $E^*$ has codimension one.
