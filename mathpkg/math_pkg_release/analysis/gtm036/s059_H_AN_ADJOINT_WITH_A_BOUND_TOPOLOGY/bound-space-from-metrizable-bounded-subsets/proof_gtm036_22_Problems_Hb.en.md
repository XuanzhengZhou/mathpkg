The proof relies on Theorem 22.11 and part (a) of this problem. By Theorem 22.11, the bound extension topology $s^b$ on $E^*$ has a local base consisting of polars of strongly bounded subsets of $E$. Under the hypothesis that every strongly bounded subset of $E^*$ is strongly metrizable, one can apply part (a) to show that the strong topology and the bound extension topology coincide on $E^*$, which implies that $E^*$ is a bound space.
