This theorem establishes that the weak* topology $s(E^*, E)$ and its bound extension $s^b$ agree on strongly separable subsets of the adjoint of a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space. Consequently, sequential convergence is equivalent in both topologies, which is useful for analyzing the structure of the adjoint space.
