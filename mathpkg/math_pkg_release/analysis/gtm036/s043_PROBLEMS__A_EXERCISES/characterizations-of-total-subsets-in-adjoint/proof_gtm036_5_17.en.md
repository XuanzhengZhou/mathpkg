This is stated as Problem B(b) in the text; the proof is left to the reader. The characterization follows from the definition of the adjoint and the Hausdorff property. If $E$ is Hausdorff, then $E^*$ separates points of $E$, and a subset $A \subseteq E^*$ is total precisely when it also separates points. The equivalence is then a direct consequence of the Hahn-Banach separation theorem.
