Let $E$ and $F$ be linear spaces, with algebraic duals $E'$ and $F'$. Let $B(E', F')$ denote the space of all bilinear forms on $E' \times F'$. Let $k \colon E \times F \to B(E', F')$ be the canonical mapping defined by
$$k(x, y) = x \otimes y,$$
where $(x \otimes y)(x', y') = \langle x, x' \rangle \langle y, y' \rangle$. The tensor product $E \otimes F$ of $E$ and $F$ is the linear subspace of $B(E', F')$ spanned by $k(E, F) = \{x \otimes y : x \in E,\; y \in F\}$.
