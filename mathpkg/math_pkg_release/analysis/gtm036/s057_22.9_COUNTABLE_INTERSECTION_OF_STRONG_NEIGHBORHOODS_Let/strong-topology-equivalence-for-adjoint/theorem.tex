If $E$ is a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space, then the following three statements concerning the strong topology for $E^*$ are equivalent.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $E^*$ with the strong topology is evaluable.
\item[(ii)] The strong topology for $E^*$ is bound.
\item[(iii)] $E^*$ with the strong topology is barrelled.
\end{enumerate}
