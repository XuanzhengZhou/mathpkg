This theorem shows that for the adjoint $E^*$ of a pseudo-metrizable locally convex space, any convex circled set that absorbs all strongly bounded sets contains a strongly closed convex circled subset with the same absorbing property. The proof constructs an increasing sequence of weak* compact convex circled sets whose union is contained in $C$, and then shows that half the strong closure of this union remains inside the union itself, establishing the desired strongly closed subset.
