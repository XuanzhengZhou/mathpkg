If a family $M$ of functions from $S$ into $E$ is equicontinuous and $M$ is given the topology of pointwise convergence on $S$, then the evaluation map $(f,s) \mapsto f(s)$ is jointly continuous as a map from $M \times S$ (with the product topology) into $E$. This is a fundamental result linking equicontinuity to continuity of the evaluation mapping.
