The weak closure of a subset $A$ of $E$ is a subset of the $\mathcal{T}$-closure of the convex extension of $A$. If $E$ is metrizable, each point of the weak closure of $A$ is the $\mathcal{T}$-limit of a sequence $\{x_n\}$; each $x_n$ is of the form $\sum \{t_i y_i : i = 1, \dots, n\}$, where the $t_i$ are positive real numbers such that $\sum \{t_i : i = 1, \dots, n\} = 1$, and $y_i \in A$.
