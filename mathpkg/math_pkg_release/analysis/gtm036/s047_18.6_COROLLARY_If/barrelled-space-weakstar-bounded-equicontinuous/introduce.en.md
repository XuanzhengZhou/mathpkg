This theorem provides an equivalent characterization of barrelled spaces in terms of the adjoint space: a locally convex space is barrelled precisely when every weak* bounded subset of its adjoint is equicontinuous. This follows by translating the barrel neighborhood condition into a condition on polars and using the duality between $(E, \mathcal{T})$ and $(E, w(E, E^*))$.
