The space $F^\circ$ with the relativized topology $w(E^*,E)$ is topologically isomorphic to the space $(E/F)^*$ with the weak* topology. If $A$ is a weak*-compact convex circled subset of $F^\circ$, the polar of $A$ in $E/F$ is equal to the image $Q[A_0]$ of the polar $A_0$ of $A$ in $E$. Because $E$ is a Mackey space, $A_0$ is a neighborhood of $0$ in $E$; hence $Q[A_0]$ is a neighborhood of $0$ in $E/F$ since $Q$ is open. Therefore $E/F$ is a Mackey space.
