The strong topology for the adjoint of a direct sum $\sum\{E_t : t \in A\}$ of linear topological spaces has as a local base the family $\mathcal{A}$ where each member contains a member of the form $\times \{B_t^\circ : t \in A\}$, with $B_t$ a bounded subset of $E_t$ for each $t$, and $B_t = \{0\}$ (hence $B_t^\circ = E_t$) except for finitely many $t$. In brief, $\mathcal{A}$ is a local base for the product of the strong topologies.
