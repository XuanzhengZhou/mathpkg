A Mackey space is a locally convex space whose topology coincides with the Mackey topology $m(E,E^*)$. Equivalently, every weak* compact convex circled subset of the adjoint is equicontinuous. Mackey spaces are precisely those locally convex spaces that carry the strongest locally convex topology compatible with the given dual pairing.
