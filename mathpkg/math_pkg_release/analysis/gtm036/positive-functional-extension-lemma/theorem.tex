Let $E$ be an ordered linear topological space, let $f$ be a positive linear functional on $E$, and let $A$ be a subset of the positive cone of $E$ such that: (i) $A$ is directed upward; (ii) if $x \in A$ then $tx \in A$ for $t \geq 0$; (iii) $A$ is radial at some point. Then $f$ can be extended to a positive linear functional on the entire space.
