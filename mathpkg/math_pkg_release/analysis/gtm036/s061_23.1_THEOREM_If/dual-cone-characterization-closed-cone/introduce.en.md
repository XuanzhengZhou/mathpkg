This theorem provides a dual characterization of closed cones in locally convex spaces. If a cone $C$ is closed in the strongest locally convex topology on $E$, then the dual cone $C^*$ separates points outside $C$ from $C$ itself. Specifically, for any $x \notin C$, there exists a positive functional $f \in C^*$ strictly separating $x$ from $C$. Consequently, membership in a closed cone can be tested by checking non-negativity against every functional in the dual cone.
