The adjoint of the Banach space $C_0(X)$, with the uniform norm, is the space of all finite regular Borel signed or complex measures on $X$, with the total variation as norm. That is,
$$(C_0(X))^* \cong M(X),$$
where $M(X)$ is the space of finite regular Borel measures with norm $\|\mu\| = |\mu|(X)$, the isomorphism being given by $\mu \mapsto \left(f \mapsto \int_X f \, d\mu\right)$.
