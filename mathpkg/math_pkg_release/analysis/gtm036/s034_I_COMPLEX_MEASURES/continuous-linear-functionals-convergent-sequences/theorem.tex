The continuous linear functionals on the space $c$ of all convergent sequences are of the form
$$\{x_n : n = 1, 2, \cdots\} \mapsto \sum_{n=1}^\infty x_n y_n + \left( \lim_{n \to \infty} x_n \right) \cdot y_0,$$
with $\{y_n : n = 0, 1, 2, \cdots\} \in l^1$. More generally, the continuous linear functionals on $c(Y)$ for the one-point compactification $Y$ of a discrete space $X$ are of the form
$$f \mapsto \sum_{x \in X} f(x) g(x)$$
with $g \in l^1(Y)$.
