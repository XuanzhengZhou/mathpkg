% Log Capacity Subharmonic Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $(A, X, \Omega, p)$ be a maximum modulus algebra over $\Omega$. Fix $F \in A$. Then $\lambda \mapsto \log Z_F(\lambda)$ is subharmonic on $\Omega$, where $Z_F(\lambda) = \max\{|F(x)| : x \in p^{-1}(\lambda)\}$.
