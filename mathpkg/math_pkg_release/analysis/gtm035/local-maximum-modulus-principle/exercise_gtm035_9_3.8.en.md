# Exercise 9.8

Let $A$ be a Banach algebra and fix $p \in \check{S}(A)$. Assume that $p$ is an isolated point of $\check{S}(A)$, viewed in the topology induced on $\check{S}(A)$ by $\mathcal{M}$. Show that $p$ is then an isolated point of $\mathcal{M}$.
