% Bishop Disk Theorem High Dimension
% From GTM035, Section s018
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\Sigma^k$ be a $k$-dimensional real submanifold of $\mathbb{C}^n$ of class $C^e$ near $0$, with a complex tangent at $0$. Under appropriate genericity conditions, there exists an analytic disk whose boundary lies in $\Sigma^k$.
