# Exercise 20.3

Let $Y$ be a compact set in $\mathbb{C}^2$ lying over the unit circle $\Gamma$ with convex fibers $Y_\lambda$ for each $\lambda \in \Gamma$. Let $(\lambda_0, w_0) \in \hat{Y}$ with $\lambda_0 \in \Gamma$.

Show that $(\lambda_0, w_0) \in Y$.

(This exercise completes the proof of Theorem 20.2.)
