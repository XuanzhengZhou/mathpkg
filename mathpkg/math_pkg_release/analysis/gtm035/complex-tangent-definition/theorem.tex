% Complex Tangent Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

A complex tangent to a real submanifold $\Sigma$ at $x$ is a complex line, i.e., a complex-linear subspace of $\mathbb{C}^n$ of complex dimension 1, contained in the real tangent space $T_x(\Sigma)$.
