% Joint Spectrum Polynomially Convex
% From GTM035, Section s008
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Assume that $x_1, \ldots, x_n$ generate $\mathfrak{A}$. Then $\sigma(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ is a polynomially convex subset of $\mathbb{C}^n$.
