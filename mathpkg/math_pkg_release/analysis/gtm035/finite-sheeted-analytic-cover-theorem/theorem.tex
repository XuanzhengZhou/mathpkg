% Finite Sheeted Analytic Cover Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s011
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $(A, X, \Omega, f)$ be a maximum modulus algebra. Assume that $\#f^{-1}(\lambda) \leq n$ for all $\lambda \in \Omega$. Then $X$ is an $n$-sheeted analytic cover of $\Omega$.
