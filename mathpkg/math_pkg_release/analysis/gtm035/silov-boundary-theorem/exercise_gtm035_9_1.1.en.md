# Exercise 9.1

Let $\Omega$ be a bounded plane region whose boundary consists of finitely many simple closed curves. Show that $\check{S}(A(\Omega)) = \partial\Omega$, the topological boundary of $\Omega$.

Here $A(\Omega)$ denotes the algebra of continuous functions on $\overline{\Omega}$ that are holomorphic in $\Omega$.
