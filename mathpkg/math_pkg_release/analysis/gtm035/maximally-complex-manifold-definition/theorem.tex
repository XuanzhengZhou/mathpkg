% Maximally Complex Manifold Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

$M$ (of real dimension $2p - 1$) is maximally complex if, for all $z \in M$, the real tangent space $T_z(M)$ contains a complex subspace of complex dimension $p - 1$.
