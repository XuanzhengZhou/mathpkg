# Exercise 11.1

Let $X$ be the product of the open unit disk and the closed unit interval, i.e.,

$$X = \{(\lambda, t) : \lambda \in \mathbb{C}, \; |\lambda| < 1, \; 0 \leq t \leq 1\}.$$

Let $A$ be the algebra of functions continuous on $X$, such that, for all $t$ with $0 \leq t \leq 1$,

$$\lambda \mapsto f(\lambda, t)$$

is analytic on $\{|\lambda| < 1\}$. Put $p(\lambda, t) = \lambda$ and $\Omega = \{|\lambda| < 1\}$. Show that $(A, X, \Omega, p)$ is a maximum modulus algebra on $X$.
