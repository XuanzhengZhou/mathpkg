% Plurisubharmonic Exhaustion For Polynomially Convex
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $K$ be polynomially convex in $\mathbb{C}^n$ with $K \subseteq U$, $U$ open and bounded. Then there exists a smooth strictly plurisubharmonic function $\rho : \mathbb{C}^n \to \mathbb{R}$ and $R > 0$ such that $K \subseteq \{\rho < 0\} \subseteq \{\rho \leq R\} \subseteq U$.
