% Full Subalgebra Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

A subalgebra $\mathcal{L}$ of $C(X)$ is full if for every $f \in \mathcal{L}^{-1}$, the cohomology class $\eta(f) \in H^1(X, \mathbb{Z})$ vanishes whenever $f$ can be written locally as an exponential of elements of $\mathcal{L}$.
