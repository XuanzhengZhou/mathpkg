This lemma justifies differentiating under the integral sign when the integration limits are fixed. For a $C^1$ function on a rectangle, one may exchange differentiation with respect to one variable and integration with respect to the other. This result is used in the proof of Theorem 7.1 (equality of mixed partials) and is a special case of the Leibniz integral rule.
