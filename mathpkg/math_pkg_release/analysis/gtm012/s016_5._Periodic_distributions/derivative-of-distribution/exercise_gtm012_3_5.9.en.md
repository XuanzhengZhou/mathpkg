# Exercise 9

Suppose that $v \in \mathcal{C}$ is differentiable at each point of $\mathbb{R}$ and $Dv = w$ is in $\mathcal{C}$. Show that $DF_v = F_w$.

That is, the distributional derivative of the function distribution $F_v$ agrees with the function distribution of the pointwise derivative.
