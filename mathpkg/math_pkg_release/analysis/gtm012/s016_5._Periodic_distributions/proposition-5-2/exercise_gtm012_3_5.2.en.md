# Exercise 2

Verify formulas (8), (9), and (10):

(8) $(F + G)^\sim = F^\sim + G^\sim$, for $F, G \in \mathcal{P}'$.

(9) $(T_t F)^\sim = T_{-t}(F^\sim)$, for $F \in \mathcal{P}'$.

(10) $(DF)^\sim = D(F^\sim)$, for $F \in \mathcal{P}'$.

Recall that $F^\sim$ is defined by $F^\sim(u) = F(\tilde{u})$, where $\tilde{u}(x) = u(-x)$, and $T_t F$ is defined by $T_t F(u) = F(T_{-t}u)$.
