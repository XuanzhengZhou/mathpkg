# Exercise 1

Find the Fourier coefficients of the following integrable functions on $[0, 2\pi]$:

(a) $u(x) = 0$ for $x \in [0, \pi]$, $u(x) = 1$ for $x \in (\pi, 2\pi]$.

(b) $u(x) = 0$ for $x \in [0, \pi]$, $u(x) = x - \pi$ for $x \in (\pi, 2\pi]$.

(c) $u(x) = |x - \pi|$.

(d) $u(x) = (x - \pi)^2$.

(e) $u(x) = x$.

(f) $u(x) = |\cos x|$.
