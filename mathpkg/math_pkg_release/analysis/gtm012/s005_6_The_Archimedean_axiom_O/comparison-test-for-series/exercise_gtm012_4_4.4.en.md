# Exercise 4 (2^k-Test)

Suppose $a_1 \geq a_2 \geq \cdots \geq a_n \geq 0$ for all $n$. Then $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n < \infty$ if and only if $\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} 2^k a_{2^k} < \infty$.

(Hint: use the methods used to show divergence of $\sum 1/n$ and convergence of $\sum 1/n^2$.)
