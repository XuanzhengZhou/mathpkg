The Weierstrass trigonometric approximation theorem states that trigonometric polynomials are dense in the continuous periodic functions with respect to uniform convergence. The proof constructs an approximate identity consisting of trigonometric polynomials $\varphi_n(x) = c_n(1 + \cos x)^n$.
