# Exercise 8

Prove L'Hopital's rule: if \(f\) and \(g\) are as in Exercise 7 and if

\[\lim_{x \to a} f'(x)[g'(x)]^{-1}\]

exists and \(f(a) = g(a) = 0\), then

\[\lim_{x \to a} f(x)[g(x)]^{-1}\]

exists and the two limits are equal.
