If $u \in \mathcal{P}$ and $v \in \mathcal{C}$, then the Riemann sum functions
$$w_n(x) = \frac{1}{2\pi n} \sum_{m=1}^{n} u\left(x - \frac{2\pi m}{n}\right) v\left(\frac{2\pi m}{n}\right)$$
converge to $w = u * v$ in the sense of $\mathcal{P}$ as $n \to \infty$:
$$w_n \to u * v \quad (\mathcal{P}).$$
In particular, $D^k w_n \to D^k(u * v)$ uniformly as $n \to \infty$, for each $k$.
