# Exercise 8

Use the Maximum Modulus Theorem to prove Corollary 4.4. (Hint: let $w_0 = f(z_0)$ and let $C$ be a small circle around $z_0$ such that $f(z) \neq w_0$ if $z \in C$. Choose $\varepsilon > 0$ so that $|f(z) - w_0| \geq 2\varepsilon$ if $z \in C$. If $|w - w_0| < \varepsilon$, can $(f(z) - w)^{-1}$ be holomorphic inside $C$?)
