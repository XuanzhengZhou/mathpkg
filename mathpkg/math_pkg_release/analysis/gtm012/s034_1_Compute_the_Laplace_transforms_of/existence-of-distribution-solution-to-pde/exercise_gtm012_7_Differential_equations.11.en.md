# Exercise 11

Use Exercise 9 to interpret the problem

$$p(D)u(t) = f(t), \quad t > 0,$$
$$D^k u(0+) = b_k, \quad 0 \leq k < n$$

as a problem of finding a distribution (when the function $f$ defines a distribution in $\mathcal{L}'$). Discuss the solution of the problem.
