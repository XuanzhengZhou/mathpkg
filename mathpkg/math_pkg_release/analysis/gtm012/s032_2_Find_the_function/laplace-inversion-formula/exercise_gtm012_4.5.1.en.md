# Exercise 1

Let $f(t) = 0$ for $t < 0$ and $f(t) = t e^{c t}$ for $t > 0$, where $c \in \mathbb{C}$ is a constant. Compute $Lf$ and verify that

$$f(t) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{C} e^{zt} \, Lf(z) \, dz,$$

where $C$ is an appropriate vertical line $\{z \mid \operatorname{Re} z = b\}$ with $b > \operatorname{Re} c$.
