This is one of the first consequences deduced from the field axioms: $0 \cdot x = (0 + 0) \cdot x = 0 \cdot x + 0 \cdot x$, so subtracting $0 \cdot x$ from both sides yields $0 \cdot x = 0$. Despite its simplicity, this fact is essential and is used repeatedly throughout analysis.
