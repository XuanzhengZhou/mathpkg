The real numbers $\mathbb{R}$ satisfy the field axioms: addition is associative and commutative with identity $0$ and inverses; multiplication is associative and commutative with identity $1$ and inverses for nonzero elements; and multiplication distributes over addition. These algebraic properties alone do not characterize $\mathbb{R}$ (e.g., $\mathbb{F}_2$ also satisfies them).
