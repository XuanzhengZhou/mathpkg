# Exercise 1

Prove the converse of Theorem 6.2: if

$$f(z) = \sum_{0}^{\infty} a_n(z - z_0)^n, \quad |z - z_0| < R,$$

and we let

$$u(x, y) = \operatorname{Re} f(x + iy), \quad |x + iy - z_0| < R,$$

then $u$ is harmonic.
