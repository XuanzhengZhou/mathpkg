# Exercise 4

Suppose $u$ is continuous on the closed disc $x^2 + y^2 \leq R$ and harmonic in the open disc $x^2 + y^2 < R$. Give a formula for $u(x, y)$ (or $u(r, \theta)$) for $x^2 + y^2 < R$ in terms of the values of $u$ for $x^2 + y^2 = R$. Give formulas for the derivatives $\partial u/\partial r$ and $\partial u/\partial \theta$ also.
