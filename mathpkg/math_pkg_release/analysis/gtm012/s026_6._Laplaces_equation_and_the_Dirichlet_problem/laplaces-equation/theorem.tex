Laplace's equation in two variables is the partial differential equation

$$\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} = 0.$$

In polar coordinates $(r, \theta)$ with $x = r\cos\theta$, $y = r\sin\theta$, the equation takes the form

$$r^2 \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial r^2} + r \frac{\partial u}{\partial r} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \theta^2} = 0.$$
