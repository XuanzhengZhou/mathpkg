If a point $x$ of $X$ is not in $C$, then there exist two disjoint open sets $U_x$ and $V_x$ such that $x \in U_x$ and $C \subset V_x$. Let $\{x_n\}$ be a countable dense set in $X - C$; then $C = \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} V_{x_n}$ is a $G_{\delta}$.
