The Completion Theorem shows that any measure can be extended to a complete measure by adjoining all subsets of null sets to the $\sigma$-ring. The completed $\sigma$-ring consists of sets differing from a measurable set by a subset of a null set. Lebesgue measure is the completion of Borel measure.
