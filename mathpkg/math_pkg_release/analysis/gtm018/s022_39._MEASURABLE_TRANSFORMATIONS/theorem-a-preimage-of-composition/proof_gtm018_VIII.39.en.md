The following statements are mutually equivalent:
(a) $x_0 \in \{x: (gT)(x) \in M\}$,
(b) $g(T(x_0)) \in M$,
(c) if $y_0 = T(x_0)$, then $g(y_0) \in M$, and
(d) $T(x_0) \in \{y: g(y) \in M\}$.

The equivalence of (a) and (d) is exactly the assertion of the theorem.
