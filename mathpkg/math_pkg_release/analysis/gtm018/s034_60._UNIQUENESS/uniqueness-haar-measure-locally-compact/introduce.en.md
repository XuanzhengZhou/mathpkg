Theorem C is the classical uniqueness theorem for Haar measure on locally compact topological groups. It states that any two regular Haar measures on such a group are proportional by a positive finite constant. The proof reduces the problem to the measurable group case (Theorem B) by restricting to the class of Baire sets, and then uses regularity to extend the proportionality to all Borel sets.
