Theorem E (continuity from above): for a decreasing sequence with at least one set of finite measure, the measure of the limit equals the limit of the measures. The finiteness condition is essential (consider $E_n = [n, \infty)$ with Lebesgue measure). The proof reduces to continuity from below by taking complements relative to a set of finite measure.
