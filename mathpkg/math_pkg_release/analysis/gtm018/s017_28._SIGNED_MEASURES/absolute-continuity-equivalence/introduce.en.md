The definition of absolute continuity $\nu \ll \mu$ for signed measures (in terms of total variations) is equivalent to both variations $\nu^+$ and $\nu^-$ being individually absolutely continuous with respect to $\mu$, and also equivalent to $|\nu| \ll |\mu|$. This shows the apparent asymmetry in the definition is only superficial.
