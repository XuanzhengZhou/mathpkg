The proof of the assertion concerning increasing sequences is the same as for measures (replacing $\{E_n\}$ by the disjoint sequence $\{E_i - E_{i-1}\}$ of differences, cf. 9.D); the same is true for decreasing sequences (reduction to the preceding case by complementation, cf. 9.E), except that Theorem A (28.A) has to be used to ensure the finiteness of the subtrahends that occur.
