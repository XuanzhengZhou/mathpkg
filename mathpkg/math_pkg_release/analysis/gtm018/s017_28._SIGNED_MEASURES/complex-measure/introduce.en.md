A complex measure generalizes signed measures by allowing complex values: $\mu(E) = \mu_1(E) + i\mu_2(E)$ where $\mu_1$ and $\mu_2$ are signed measures. This is the natural complex-valued analog of the real-valued signed measure, and analogous questions (like the validity of Theorems A, B, C) apply.
