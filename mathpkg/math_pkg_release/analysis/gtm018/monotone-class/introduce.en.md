A monotone class is closed under limits of monotone (increasing or decreasing) sequences of sets. The monotone class generated by a ring equals its generated $\sigma$-ring (Theorem B in Halmos), providing a powerful technique for proving that a property holds for all sets in $\mathbf{S}(\mathbf{R})$ by verifying it on the ring and showing it is preserved under monotone limits.
