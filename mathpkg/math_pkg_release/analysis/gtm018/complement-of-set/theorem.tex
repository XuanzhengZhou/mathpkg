The complement of a subset $E$ of $X$ is the set of all those points of $X$ which do not belong to $E$; it will be denoted by $E'$. The operation of forming complements satisfies:
$$E \cap E' = 0, \quad E \cup E' = X,$$
$$0' = X, \quad (E')' = E, \quad X' = 0,$$
and if $E \subset F$, then $E' \supset F'$.
