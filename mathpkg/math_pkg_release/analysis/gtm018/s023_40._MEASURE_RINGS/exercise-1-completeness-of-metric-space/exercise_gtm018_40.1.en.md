The metric space $\mathbf{s}$ of a measure space $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ is complete.

*Hint:* If $\{E_n\}$ is a fundamental sequence in $\mathbf{s}$, and if $\chi_n$ is the characteristic function of $E_n$, $n = 1, 2, \cdots$, then $\{\chi_n\}$ is fundamental in measure and therefore 22.E may be applied.
