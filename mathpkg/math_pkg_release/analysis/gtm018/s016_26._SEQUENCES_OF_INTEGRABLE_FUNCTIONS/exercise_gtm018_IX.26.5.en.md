Let $X$ be the closed unit interval with Lebesgue measure and let $\{E_n\}$ be a decreasing sequence of open intervals such that $\mu(E_n) = \frac{1}{n}, n = 1, 2, \dots$. The sequence $\{n \chi_{E_n}\}$ may be used to show that the boundedness condition cannot be omitted from Theorem D.
