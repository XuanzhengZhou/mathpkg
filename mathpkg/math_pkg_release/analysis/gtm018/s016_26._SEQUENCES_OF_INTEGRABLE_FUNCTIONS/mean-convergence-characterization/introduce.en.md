This theorem gives a necessary and sufficient condition for a sequence of integrable functions to converge in mean to an integrable function. Mean convergence is characterized by convergence in measure together with two uniformity conditions on the indefinite integrals of $|f_n|$: uniform absolute continuity and equicontinuity from above at $0$. This result is a precursor to the Vitali convergence theorem and provides a powerful tool for verifying $L^1$ convergence.
