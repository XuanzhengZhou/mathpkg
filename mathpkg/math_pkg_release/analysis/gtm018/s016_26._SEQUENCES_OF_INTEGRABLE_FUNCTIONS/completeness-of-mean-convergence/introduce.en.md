This theorem establishes the completeness of the space of integrable functions with respect to the metric $\rho(f,g) = \int |f - g| \, d\mu$. Every mean fundamental (i.e., Cauchy in $L^1$) sequence of integrable functions converges in mean to some integrable function. Consequently, the integral of the limit equals the limit of the integrals.
