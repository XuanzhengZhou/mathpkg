A section of a set in a product space $X \times Y$ is obtained by fixing one coordinate and collecting all points whose second coordinate pairs with the fixed one to belong to the set. The $X$-section $E_x$ is a subset of $Y$, and the $Y$-section $E^y$ is a subset of $X$. Sections are fundamental tools for analyzing product measure and for stating Fubini's theorem, where one integrates over sections.
