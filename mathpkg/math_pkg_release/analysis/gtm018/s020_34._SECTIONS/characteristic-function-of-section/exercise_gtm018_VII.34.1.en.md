If $\chi$ is the characteristic function of a subset $E$ of $X \times Y$, show that $\chi_x$ and $\chi^y$ are the characteristic functions of $E_x$ and $E^y$ respectively. If, in particular, $\chi$ is the characteristic function of a rectangle $A \times B$, show that
$$\chi(x,y) = \chi_A(x) \chi_B(y).$$
Conclude that every section of a simple function is a simple function.
