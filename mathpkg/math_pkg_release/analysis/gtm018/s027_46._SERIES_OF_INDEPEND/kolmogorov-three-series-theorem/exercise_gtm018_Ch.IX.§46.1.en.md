The following result, which is implicitly contained in the earlier discussion of the relation between mean convergence and convergence in measure, is known as the equivalence of mean convergence and convergence in measure for sequences of independent functions. If $\{f_n\}$ is a sequence of independent functions, then $\{f_n\}$ converges in measure to $0$ if and only if it converges to $0$ in the mean of order two, i.e., $\int f_n^2 \, d\mu \to 0$.
