For independent sets, the Borel-Cantelli lemma has a converse: the limsup of a sequence of independent sets has measure zero if and only if the sum of their measures is finite. This is a sharpening of the classical Borel-Cantelli lemma (where the "if" direction holds generally but the "only if" direction requires independence). The proof applies Theorem D to the characteristic functions of the sets.
