For a non-negative integrable function, larger sets yield larger indefinite integrals. This monotonicity follows from $\chi_E f \leq \chi_F f$ when $E \subset F$ and the monotonicity of the integral (Theorem C). It shows that the indefinite integral of a non-negative function is a measure.
