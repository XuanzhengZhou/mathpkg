Theorem A describes how to compute sections of the images of a set $E \subseteq X \times X$ under the shearing transformation $S$ and the reflected transformation $T$. The $x$-section of $S(E)$ is the left translate by $x$ of the $x$-section of $E$, while the $y$-section of $T(E)$ is the left translate by $y$ of the $y$-section of $E$. These identities are fundamental tools for manipulating sets in the product space of a measurable group.
