The $\sigma$-ring generated by the class of all bounded open Baire sets, or, equivalently, the $\sigma$-ring generated by $\mathbf{U}_0$, coincides with $\mathbf{S}_0$. (Hint: if $C$ is compact, $U$ is open, and $C \subset U$, then there exists a bounded open Baire set $U_0$ such that $C \subset U_0 \subset U$.)
