If $\lambda$ and $\mu$ are totally $\sigma$-finite measures on a $\sigma$-algebra $\mathbf{S}$ such that $\mu \ll \lambda$, and if $\nu$ is a totally $\sigma$-finite signed measure on $\mathbf{S}$ such that $\nu \ll \mu$, then
$$\frac{d\nu}{d\lambda} = \frac{d\nu}{d\mu} \frac{d\mu}{d\lambda} \quad [\lambda].$$
