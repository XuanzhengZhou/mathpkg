We write
$$\nu(E) = \int_E f \, d\mu$$
for every measurable set $E$, and apply Theorem A (the chain rule). It follows that
$$\nu(E) = \int_E f \frac{d\mu}{d\lambda} \, d\lambda$$
for every measurable set $E$; the desired result follows by putting $E = X$.
