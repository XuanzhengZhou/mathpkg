The Radon-Nikodym theorem remains true even if $\mu$ is only a signed measure.
(Hint: let $X = A \cup B$ be a Hahn decomposition with respect to $\mu$ and apply the Radon-Nikodym theorem separately to $\nu$ and $\mu^+$ in $A$ and to $\nu$ and $\mu^-$ in $B$.)
