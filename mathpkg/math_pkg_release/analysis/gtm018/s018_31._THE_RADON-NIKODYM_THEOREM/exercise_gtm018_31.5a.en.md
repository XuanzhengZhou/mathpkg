Let $\mu$ be a totally $\sigma$-finite signed measure. Since both $\mu^+$ and $\mu^-$ are absolutely continuous with respect to both $\mu$ and $|\mu|$, we have
$$\mu^+(E) = \int_E f_+ \, d\mu = \int_E g_+ \, d|\mu| \quad \text{and} \quad \mu^-(E) = \int_E f_- \, d\mu = \int_E g_- \, d|\mu|.$$
Find expressions for $f_+$, $f_-$, $g_+$, and $g_-$.
