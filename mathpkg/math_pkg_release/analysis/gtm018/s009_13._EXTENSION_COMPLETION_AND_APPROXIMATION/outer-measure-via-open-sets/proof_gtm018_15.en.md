From the definition of $\mu^*$, there exist $[a_n,b_n) \in \mathbf{P}$ with $E \subset \bigcup [a_n,b_n)$ and $\sum (b_n-a_n) \leq \mu^*(E) + \epsilon/2$. Enlarging each interval to $(a_n - \epsilon/2^{n+1}, b_n)$ gives an open set $U$ with $E \subset U$ and $\mu(U) \leq \mu^*(E) + \epsilon$. The result follows from the arbitrariness of $\epsilon$.
