Applying Theorem G to $A \cap E$ and $A' \cap E$ yields $\mu_*(E) \leq \mu_*(A \cap E) + \mu^*(A' \cap E) \leq \mu^*(E)$. Since $E \in \bar{\mathbf{S}}$, Theorem F gives $\mu_*(E) = \mu^*(E) = \bar{\mu}(E)$.
