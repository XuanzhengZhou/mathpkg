For $S$: using Fubini's theorem, $(\mu \times \mu)(S(E)) = \int \mu((S(E))^y) d\mu(y) = \int \mu(yE_y) d\mu(y) = \int \mu(E_y) d\mu(y) = (\mu \times \mu)(E)$, where $E_y = \{x: (x,y) \in E\}$.
