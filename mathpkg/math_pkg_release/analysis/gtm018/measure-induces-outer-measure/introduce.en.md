Theorem A constructs the outer measure induced by a measure: $\mu^*(E)$ is the infimum of $\sum \mu(E_n)$ over all countable covers of $E$ by sets in the ring. This is the first step in Carathéodory's extension procedure, generalizing the construction of Lebesgue outer measure from interval length.
