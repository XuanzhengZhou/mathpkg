Theorem C shows that every set $N$ in the Weil neighborhood base class $\mathbf{N}$ is measurable and has positive measure, and that finiteness of $\mu(E^{-1})$ implies finiteness of $\mu(N)$. This is essential for the measure-theoretic properties of the Weil topology.
