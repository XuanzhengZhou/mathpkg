Theorem G shows that for any Baire measure $\mu$ on a locally compact topological group $X$, the stabilizer subgroup $Y = \{y : \mu(yE) = \mu(E) 	ext{ for all Baire sets } E\}$ is a closed subgroup of $X$. This result is fundamental for the uniqueness theory of Haar measure.
