Theorem G strengthens Theorem E by combining it with Theorem F: every Baire set $E$ in a locally compact group $X$ is a union of cosets of a compact invariant subgroup $Y$ such that the quotient $X/Y$ is separable. This powerful structure theorem is the culmination of the regularity theory.
