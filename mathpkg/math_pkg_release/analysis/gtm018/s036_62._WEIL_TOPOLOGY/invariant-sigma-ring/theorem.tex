An \emph{invariant $\sigma$-ring} is a $\sigma$-ring $\mathbf{T}$ of Baire sets such that if $E \in \mathbf{T}$ and $x \in X$, then $xE \in \mathbf{T}$. The invariant $\sigma$-ring \emph{generated} by a class $\mathbf{E}$ of Baire sets is the intersection of all invariant $\sigma$-rings containing $\mathbf{E}$.
