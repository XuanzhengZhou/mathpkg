This exercise strengthens the inner regularity result from Exercise (3d) for the special case of a Polish space (separable, complete metric space). In this setting, closed sets of finite measure can be further approximated by compact subsets. This result is crucial in proving that Borel measures on Polish spaces are Radon measures, i.e., inner regular with respect to compact sets.
