This exercise asks the reader to extend all the theorems and definitions of Section 13 (Theorems A, D, E and the definition of absolute continuity) from finite positive measures to finite signed measures. The key insight is that monotonicity of the distribution function must be replaced by bounded variation, which can be decomposed as the difference of two monotone functions via the Jordan decomposition.
