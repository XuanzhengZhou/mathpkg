Define for every compact set $C$, $\lambda(C) = \inf \{\mu_0(U_0): C \subset U_0 \in \mathbf{U}_0\}$. Then $\lambda$ is a regular content by Theorem C, and the Borel measure induced by $\lambda$ is regular and extends $\mu_0$. Uniqueness follows from the fact that two regular Borel measures agreeing on Baire sets agree everywhere.
