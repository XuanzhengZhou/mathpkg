The existence of Haar measure is a cornerstone result: every locally compact group admits a non-trivial, left-invariant, regular Borel measure. The construction uses the group structure to define a translation-invariant content on compact sets and then applies the Carathéodory extension. Haar measure enables harmonic analysis on locally compact groups, generalizing Fourier analysis on the circle and real line.
