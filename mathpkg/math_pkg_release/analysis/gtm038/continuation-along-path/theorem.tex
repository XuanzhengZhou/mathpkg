Let $(G_1, \pi_1), (G_2, \pi_2)$ be domains over $\mathbb{C}^n$, $\psi_\lambda: [0,1] \to G_\lambda$ paths with $\pi_1 \circ \psi_1 = \pi_2 \circ \psi_2$. Let $f_\lambda$ be holomorphic on $G_\lambda$. If the germs of $f_1$ and $f_2$ agree at the starting points $(f_1)_{\psi_1(0)} = (f_2)_{\psi_2(0)}$, then they agree at the endpoints: $(f_1)_{\psi_1(1)} = (f_2)_{\psi_2(1)}$.
