**Proof.** The ring $\mathbb{C}\{z_1,\ldots,z_n\}$ of convergent power series is a Hensel ring. This follows from the Weierstrass preparation theorem and Hensel's Lemma. Given a polynomial (X) = X^k + a_1 X^{k-1} + \cdots + a_k$ with coefficients in $\mathbb{C}\{z\}$ such that modulo the maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$ it factors as  \equiv \bar{Q} \cdot \bar{R} \pmod{\mathfrak{m}}$ with coprime $\bar{Q}, \bar{R}$, one lifts this factorization. By the Weierstrass preparation theorem, one reduces to the case where $ is a Weierstrass polynomial, and the factorization lifts via an iterative Newton-type argument. $\square$
