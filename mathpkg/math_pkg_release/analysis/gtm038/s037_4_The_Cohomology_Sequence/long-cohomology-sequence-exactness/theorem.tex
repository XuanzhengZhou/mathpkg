Let $O \to \mathcal{S}^* \xrightarrow{\varphi} \mathcal{S} \xrightarrow{\psi} \mathcal{S}^{**} \to O$ be an exact sequence of sheaves of $R$-modules over a complex manifold $X$. Then the following long cohomology sequence is also exact:

$$0 \to \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}^*) \xrightarrow{\varphi_*} \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}) \xrightarrow{\psi_*} \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}^{**}) \xrightarrow{\partial} H^1(X, \mathcal{S}^*) \xrightarrow{\bar{\varphi}} \cdots$$

$$\cdots \xrightarrow{\bar{\psi}} H^{i-1}(X, \mathcal{S}^{**}) \xrightarrow{\partial} H^i(X, \mathcal{S}^*) \xrightarrow{\bar{\varphi}} H^i(X, \mathcal{S}) \xrightarrow{\bar{\psi}} H^i(X, \mathcal{S}^{**}) \xrightarrow{\partial} \cdots$$
