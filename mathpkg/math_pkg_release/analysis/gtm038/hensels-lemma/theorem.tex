$H_n$, the ring of convergent power series in $n$ variables, is a henselian ring. Moreover, Hensel's lemma holds: if $\omega(u, z) \in H_n^0[u]$ has decomposition $\omega(u, 0) = \prod_{\lambda=1}^{\ell} (u - c_\lambda)^{s_\lambda}$ with distinct $c_\lambda$, then there exist uniquely determined polynomials $\omega_\lambda \in H_n^0[u]$ with $\deg(\omega_\lambda) = s_\lambda$ such that $\omega = \prod \omega_\lambda$.
