Let
$$\mathcal{O} \rightarrow \mathcal{S} \rightarrow \mathcal{S}_0 \xrightarrow{\varphi_0} \mathcal{S}_1 \xrightarrow{\varphi_1} \mathcal{S}_2 \xrightarrow{\varphi_2} \cdots$$
be an exact sequence of sheaves of abelian groups over a topological space $X$, where all $\mathcal{S}_{\lambda}$ are flabby. Define $\mathcal{S}_{-1} := \mathcal{S}$ and
$$\mathcal{B}_{\lambda} := \operatorname{Im}(\varphi_{\lambda} : \mathcal{S}_{\lambda-1} \rightarrow \mathcal{S}_{\lambda}), \quad \lambda = 0, 1, 2, \ldots$$
Then every $\mathcal{B}_{\lambda}$ is a flabby sheaf.
