This theorem is a fundamental structural result in sheaf cohomology: in a flabby resolution, the image sheaves appearing at each step remain flabby. The proof proceeds by induction and relies on the flabbiness of the original sheaves $\mathcal{S}_{\lambda}$ and on the surjectivity property of flabby sheaves. This result is essential for proving that flabby sheaves are acyclic with respect to the global sections functor.
