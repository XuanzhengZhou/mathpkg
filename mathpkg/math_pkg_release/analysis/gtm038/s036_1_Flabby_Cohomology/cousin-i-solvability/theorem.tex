Let $(U_t, f_t)_{t \in I}$ be a Cousin I distribution on $X$, $\mathfrak{H}$ the structure sheaf of $X$, $\mathfrak{U} := (U_t)_{t \in I}$. Then

1. $\gamma(t_0, t_1) := (f_{t_0} - f_{t_1})|U_{t_0t_1}$ defines an element $\gamma \in Z^1(\mathfrak{U}, \mathfrak{H})$.
2. $(U_t, f_t)_{t \in I}$ is solvable if and only if $\gamma$ lies in $B^1(\mathfrak{U}, \mathfrak{H})$.
