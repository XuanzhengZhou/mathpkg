The coboundary operator $\delta^{\ell}: C^{\ell}(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S}) \rightarrow C^{\ell+1}(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S})$ is defined by
$$(\delta^{\ell}\xi)(\iota_0, \dots, \iota_{\ell+1}) := \sum_{\lambda=0}^{\ell+1} (-1)^{\lambda+1} \xi(\iota_0, \dots, \widehat{\iota_{\lambda}}, \dots, \iota_{\ell+1}) | U_{\iota_0 \dots \iota_{\ell+1}}.$$

The Čech complex $C^{\bullet}(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S})$ is the sequence
$$C^0(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S}) \xrightarrow{\delta} C^1(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S}) \xrightarrow{\delta} C^2(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S}) \rightarrow \cdots.$$

The augmentation map $\varepsilon: \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}) \rightarrow C^0(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S})$ is defined by $(\varepsilon s)(\iota) := s|U_{\iota}.$
