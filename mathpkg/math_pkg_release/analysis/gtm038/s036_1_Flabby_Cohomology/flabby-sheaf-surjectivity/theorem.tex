Let
$$\mathcal{O} \rightarrow \mathcal{S}' \xrightarrow{\varphi} \mathcal{S} \xrightarrow{\psi} \mathcal{S}'' \rightarrow \mathcal{O}$$
be an exact sequence of sheaves of abelian groups over a topological space $X$. If $\mathcal{S}'$ is flabby, then the induced map on global sections
$$\psi_* : \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}) \rightarrow \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}'')$$
is surjective.
