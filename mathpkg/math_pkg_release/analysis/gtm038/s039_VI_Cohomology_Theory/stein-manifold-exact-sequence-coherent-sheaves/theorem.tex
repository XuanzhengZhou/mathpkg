Let $X$ be a Stein manifold, and let

$$0 \to \mathcal{S}^* \to \mathcal{S} \to \mathcal{S}^{**} \to 0$$

be an exact sequence of coherent analytic sheaves over $X$. Then

$$0 \to \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}^*) \to \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}) \to \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}^{**}) \to 0$$

is exact, and

$$\Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}^{**}) \simeq \frac{\Gamma(X, \mathcal{S})}{\Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}^{*})}.$$
