Theorem 5.4 establishes that the property of being a Stein manifold is preserved under finite intersections of open submanifolds. If $U_1$ and $U_2$ are open Stein submanifolds of a complex manifold $X$, then their intersection $U = U_1 \cap U_2$ is also Stein. The proof proceeds by verifying the two defining properties of Stein manifolds: holomorphic separability (using the fact that holomorphic functions on $U_1$ restrict to $U$) and holomorphic convexity (using that compact subsets of $U$ remain compact in $U_1$ and $U_2$).
