Let $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be a region. A presheaf consists of: for every open set $W \subset B$ a set $M_W$, and for every pair $(V, W)$ of open sets with $V \subset W$ a restriction homomorphism $r^W_V: M_W \to M_V$ satisfying $r^U_U = \operatorname{id}$ and $r^W_U = r^V_U \circ r^W_V$ for $U \subset V \subset W$.
