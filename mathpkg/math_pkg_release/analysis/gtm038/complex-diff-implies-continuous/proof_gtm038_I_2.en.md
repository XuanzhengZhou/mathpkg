We have (z) = f(z_0) + \sum_{v=1}^{n} (z_v - z_v^{(0)}) \Delta_v(z)$; the right side of this equation is clearly continuous at $ since each $\Delta_v$ is continuous at $ and each term $ tends to 0 as  \to z_0$.
