Let $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be a region, $f$ holomorphic in $B$, $\varepsilon > 0$, and $K \subset B_\varepsilon$ compact. Then for every $\delta$ with $0 < \delta < \varepsilon$ there exists an $M > 0$ such that for all derivatives $\frac{\partial^{|v|} f}{\partial z^v}$ one has $\sup_K |\frac{\partial^{|v|} f}{\partial z^v}| \leqslant M \cdot |v|! \cdot \delta^{-|v|}$.
