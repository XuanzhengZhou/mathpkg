Theorem 6.4 establishes the factorization property of pseudopolynomials in the Weierstrass preparation context. Given a domain $G \subset \mathbb{C}^n$, let $A = A(G)$ be the ring of holomorphic functions and $Q = Q(A)$ the field of meromorphic functions. If the product of two monic polynomials with meromorphic coefficients lies in $A^0[u]$ (monic polynomials with holomorphic coefficients), then each factor must already belong to $A^0[u]$. This result is fundamental for the uniqueness of prime decompositions of pseudopolynomials.
