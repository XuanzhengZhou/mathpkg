Definition 6.4 introduces a formal differentiation operator $D$ on the polynomial ring $A[u]$, where $A = A(G)$ is the ring of holomorphic functions on a domain $G \subset \mathbb{C}^n$. For a polynomial $\sum A_v(\zeta) u^v$ with holomorphic coefficients $A_v(\zeta)$, the operator $D$ acts as formal differentiation with respect to the indeterminate $u$, applying the power rule $D(u^v) = v \cdot u^{v-1}$ while leaving the coefficient functions $A_v(\zeta)$ unchanged. This is the algebraic analogue of partial differentiation with respect to $u$.
