Define complex scalar multiplication on $T_{x_0}$ by
$$i \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x_v} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y_v} \quad \text{and} \quad i \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial y_v} = -\frac{\partial}{\partial x_v} \quad \text{for} \quad v = 1, \ldots, n.$$

Then $T_{x_0}$ is an $n$-dimensional complex vector space with basis $\{\partial/\partial x_1, \ldots, \partial/\partial x_n\}$, and this complex structure is compatible with the given real structure on $T_{x_0}$.
