This theorem generalizes the classical Cauchy integral formula by adding an area integral term involving $g_{\bar{z}}$. It expresses the value of a continuously differentiable function $g$ at a point $w$ as the sum of the boundary contour integral of $g$ and the area integral of its $\bar{z}$-derivative over the region. This is also known as the Cauchy-Green formula or the Pompeiu formula, and it serves as the foundation for solving the inhomogeneous Cauchy-Riemann equation $\bar{\partial}u = f$.
