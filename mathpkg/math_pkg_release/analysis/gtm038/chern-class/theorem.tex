Let $h \in H^1(X, \mathcal{O}^*)$ be the cohomology class of a Cousin II distribution. The Chern class $c(h)$ is $\partial(h) \in H^2(X, \mathbb{Z})$ where $\partial$ is the connecting homomorphism from the exponential sequence $0 \to \mathbb{Z} \to \mathcal{O} \xrightarrow{\exp(2\pi i \cdot)} \mathcal{O}^* \to 0$.
