Definition 2.6 formalizes the Levi condition for a domain $G \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ with $C^2$ boundary. At a boundary point $\mathfrak{z}_0$, one considers a local defining function $\varphi$ satisfying the conditions of Def. 2.4. The Levi condition requires the Levi form $L_\varphi$ to be positive semidefinite on the holomorphic tangent space. This condition characterizes pseudoconvexity (Theorem 2.5).
