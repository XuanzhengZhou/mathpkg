[The full statement of Theorem 2.2 is only partially preserved in this section. The surviving fragment reads:] If $Q \subset G$ is a connected subset, then $F|V$ is holomorphic, $Q \cap V$ is open and non-empty, and $F|Q \cap V = f|Q \cap V$. Therefore $G$ is not a domain of holomorphy. This completes the proof by contradiction.
