Definition 3.3 introduces the holomorphically convex hull $\hat{K}_B$, the complex-analytic analogue of the geometrically convex hull. For a subset $K$ of a region $B$, $\hat{K}_B$ consists of all points $z \in B$ where $|f(z)| \leq \sup|f(K)|$ for every holomorphic $f$ on $B$. Holomorphic functions replace linear functionals as test functions.
