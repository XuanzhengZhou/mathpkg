Let $K_v \subset \mathbb{C}$ be compact sets for $v = 1, \ldots, n$, and let $U_v \subset \mathbb{C}$ be open neighborhoods of $K_v$. Let $\varphi$ be a $d''$-closed differential form of type $(0, q)$ with $q \geq 1$ and arbitrarily often differentiable coefficients on $U_1 \times \cdots \times U_n$.

Then there exist open sets $U'_v$ with $K_v \subset U'_v \subset U_v$ and a differential form $\psi$ of type $(0, q-1)$ on $U'_1 \times \cdots \times U'_n$ such that

$$d''\psi = \varphi.$$
