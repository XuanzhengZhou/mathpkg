A complex manifold is a complex ringed space $(X, \mathcal{H})$ such that $X$ is Hausdorff and for every point $x \in X$ there is an open neighborhood $U$ such that $(U, \mathcal{H}|U)$ is isomorphic to $(G, \mathcal{O}_G)$ where $G \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ is a domain and $\mathcal{O}_G$ is its structure sheaf.
