An isomorphism of complex ringed spaces $\varphi = (\tilde{\varphi}, \varphi_*)$ induces a $\mathbb{C}$-algebra isomorphism on sections over every open set. Specifically, for every open $V \subset X_2$, the map $\tilde{\varphi}_V$ gives an isomorphism $\Gamma(V, \mathcal{H}_2) \cong \Gamma(\tilde{\varphi}^{-1}(V), \mathcal{H}_1)$, showing that the algebraic structure of sections is preserved under isomorphisms of ringed spaces.
