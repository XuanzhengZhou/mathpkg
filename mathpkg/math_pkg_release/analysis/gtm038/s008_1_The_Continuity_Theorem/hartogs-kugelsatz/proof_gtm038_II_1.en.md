The proof of the Kugelsatz is substantially more difficult than that of the preceding Theorem 1.3. An important tool in its proof is the Bochner-Martinelli integral formula, which is a generalization of the Cauchy integral formula to a domain with piecewise smooth boundary. The proof is not given in this section.
