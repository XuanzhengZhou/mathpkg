Let $X$ be an $n$-dimensional complex manifold. For the diagonal $D \subset X \times X$, consider the holomorphic functions $z_1 - w_1, \ldots, z_n - w_n$ defined on $X \times X$ in terms of local coordinates $(z_1, \ldots, z_n, w_1, \ldots, w_n)$. Then at every point $(x, x) \in D$,
$$\operatorname{rk}_{(x, x)}(z_1 - w_1, \ldots, z_n - w_n) = n.$$
Consequently, the diagonal $D$ is a regular analytic subset of $X \times X$ of codimension $n$.
