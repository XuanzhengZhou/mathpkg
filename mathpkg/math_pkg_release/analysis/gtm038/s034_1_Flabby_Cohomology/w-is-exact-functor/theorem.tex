The functor $W$ is exact: if
$$0 \to \mathcal{S}_1 \xrightarrow{\psi} \mathcal{S} \xrightarrow{\varphi} \mathcal{S}_2 \to 0$$
is an exact sequence of $R$-module sheaves, then the induced sequence
$$0 \to W(\mathcal{S}_1) \xrightarrow{W\psi} W(\mathcal{S}) \xrightarrow{W\varphi} W(\mathcal{S}_2) \to 0$$
is also exact.
