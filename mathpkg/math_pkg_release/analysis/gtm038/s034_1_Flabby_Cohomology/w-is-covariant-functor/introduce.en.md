The construction $W$ that assigns to each $R$-module sheaf $\mathcal{S}$ the flabby sheaf $W(\mathcal{S})$ is not just an object assignment but a covariant functor. This means that a sheaf homomorphism $\varphi: \mathcal{S}_1 \to \mathcal{S}_2$ naturally induces a sheaf homomorphism $W\varphi: W(\mathcal{S}_1) \to W(\mathcal{S}_2)$, and this assignment respects composition and identities. The functoriality is essential for extending $W$ to a resolution functor $\mathfrak{W}$ that maps exact sequences to complexes.
