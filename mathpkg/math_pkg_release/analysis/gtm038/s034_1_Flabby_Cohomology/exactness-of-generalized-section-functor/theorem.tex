$(\hat{\Gamma}_U : \mathcal{S} \mapsto \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}),\; \varphi \mapsto \varphi_*)$ is an exact covariant functor from the category of $R$-module sheaves over $X$ to the category of $R$-modules. In particular:
\begin{enumerate}
\item If $\mathcal{S}$ is an $R$-module sheaf, then $\hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S})$ is an $R$-module.
\item If $\varphi : \mathcal{S}_1 \to \mathcal{S}_2$ is a homomorphism of $R$-module sheaves, then $\varphi_* : \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}_1) \to \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}_2)$ is an $R$-module homomorphism.
\item If $0 \to \mathcal{S}_1 \to \mathcal{S} \to \mathcal{S}_2 \to 0$ is exact, then $0 \to \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}_1) \to \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}) \to \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}_2) \to 0$ is exact.
\end{enumerate}
