Let $\mathcal{S}$ be a sheaf of $R$-modules over a complex manifold $X$ and $U \subset X$ open. The set of \textbf{generalized sections} is
$$
\hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}) := \{ s : U \to \mathcal{S} \mid \pi \circ s = \text{id}_U \},
$$
where $\pi : \mathcal{S} \to X$ is the sheaf projection. These functions are not necessarily continuous. The $R$-module of continuous sections $\Gamma(U, \mathcal{S})$ is an $R$-submodule of $\hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S})$.
