If $\varphi: \mathcal{S}_1 \rightarrow \mathcal{S}_2$ is an analytic sheaf morphism and $\mathfrak{q}: \mathcal{S}_1 \rightarrow \mathcal{S}_1/\operatorname{Ker}\varphi$ and $\iota: \operatorname{Im}\varphi \hookrightarrow \mathcal{S}_2$ are the canonical mappings, then $\varphi$ admits the canonical decomposition:
$$
\varphi = \iota \circ \bar{\varphi} \circ \mathfrak{q}: \mathcal{S}_1 \twoheadrightarrow \mathcal{S}_1/\operatorname{Ker}\varphi \simeq \operatorname{Im}\varphi \hookrightarrow \mathcal{S}_2.
$$
