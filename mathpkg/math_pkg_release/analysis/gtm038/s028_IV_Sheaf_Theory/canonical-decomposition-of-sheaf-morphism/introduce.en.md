The **canonical decomposition** of an analytic sheaf morphism $\varphi: \mathcal{S}_1 \to \mathcal{S}_2$ factors $\varphi$ into three steps: a quotient epimorphism $\mathfrak{q}$ onto $\mathcal{S}_1/\operatorname{Ker}\varphi$, an isomorphism $\bar{\varphi}$ onto the image (by Theorem 3.2), and an inclusion monomorphism $\iota$ into $\mathcal{S}_2$.

This decomposition is the sheaf-theoretic analog of the standard factorization of a module homomorphism through its image, and is a direct consequence of the first isomorphism theorem for sheaves (Theorem 3.2).
