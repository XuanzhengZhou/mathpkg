This proposition states that the composition of two analytic sheaf morphisms is again an analytic sheaf morphism.

This is a basic categorical property: analytic sheaves over a fixed base $B$ together with analytic sheaf morphisms form a category. The composition $\psi \circ \varphi$ is defined stalk-wise by $(\psi \circ \varphi)_{\mathfrak{z}} = \psi_{\mathfrak{z}} \circ \varphi_{\mathfrak{z}}$, and continuity follows from the continuity of $\varphi$ and $\psi$.
