Let
$$
\mathcal{S}' \xrightarrow{j_1} \mathcal{S}'' \xrightarrow{j_2} \mathcal{S} \xrightarrow{p_1} \mathcal{S}^* \xrightarrow{p_2} \mathcal{S}^{**}
$$
be an exact sequence of analytic sheaves over $B$. If $\mathcal{S}', \mathcal{S}'', \mathcal{S}^*, \mathcal{S}^{**}$ are coherent, then $\mathcal{S}$ is also coherent.
