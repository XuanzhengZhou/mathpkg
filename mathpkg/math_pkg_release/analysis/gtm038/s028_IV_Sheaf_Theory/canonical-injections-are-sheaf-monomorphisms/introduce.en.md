This proposition states that for a finite direct sum of analytic sheaves, each canonical injection $j_i: \mathcal{S}_i \hookrightarrow \mathcal{S}_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathcal{S}_\ell$ (which embeds the $i$-th summand by putting zeros in all other components) is a sheaf monomorphism.

Together with the dual statement about canonical projections (Proposition 5), this establishes the structural properties of the direct sum in the category of analytic sheaves over $B$.
