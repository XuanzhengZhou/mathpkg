At each stalk $\mathfrak{z} \in B$, the induced map $(j_i)_{\mathfrak{z}}: (\mathcal{S}_i)_{\mathfrak{z}} \to (\mathcal{S}_1)_{\mathfrak{z}} \oplus \cdots \oplus (\mathcal{S}_\ell)_{\mathfrak{z}}$ sends $\sigma_i$ to $(0, \ldots, 0, \sigma_i, 0, \ldots, 0)$, which is injective. Since injectivity at every stalk characterizes sheaf monomorphisms, each $j_i$ is a sheaf monomorphism.
