At each stalk $\mathfrak{z} \in B$, the stalk of the direct sum is $(\mathcal{S}_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathcal{S}_\ell)_{\mathfrak{z}} = (\mathcal{S}_1)_{\mathfrak{z}} \oplus \cdots \oplus (\mathcal{S}_\ell)_{\mathfrak{z}}$. The induced map $(p_i)_{\mathfrak{z}}: (\mathcal{S}_1)_{\mathfrak{z}} \oplus \cdots \oplus (\mathcal{S}_\ell)_{\mathfrak{z}} \to (\mathcal{S}_i)_{\mathfrak{z}}$ is the canonical projection of $\mathcal{O}_{\mathfrak{z}}$-modules, which is surjective. Since surjectivity at every stalk characterizes sheaf epimorphisms, each $p_i$ is a sheaf epimorphism.
