By Oka's Coherence Theorem, the structure sheaf $\mathcal{O}$ is coherent. Theorem 4.9 then implies that for any coherent ideal sheaf $\mathcal{I} \subset \mathcal{O}$, the quotient $\mathcal{O}/\mathcal{I}$ is coherent as a sheaf of $\mathbb{C}$-algebras.
