**Proof.** We have $f(\mathfrak{z}) = f(\mathfrak{z}_0) + \sum_{v=1}^{n} (z_v - z_v^{(0)}) \Delta_v(\mathfrak{z})$; the right side of this equation is clearly continuous at $\mathfrak{z}_0$. $\square$
