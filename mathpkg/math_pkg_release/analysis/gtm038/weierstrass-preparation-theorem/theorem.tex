Let $g \in H_n$ be regular of order $s$ in $z_1$. Then there exists a unit $e \in H_n$ and a normalized polynomial $\omega \in H_{n-1}[z_1]$ of degree $s$ such that $g = e \cdot \omega$, where $\omega(z_1, z_2, \ldots, z_n) = z_1^s + a_1(z_2, \ldots, z_n)z_1^{s-1} + \cdots + a_s(z_2, \ldots, z_n)$ with $a_j(0) = 0$.
