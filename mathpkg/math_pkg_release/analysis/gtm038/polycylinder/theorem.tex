Let $r \in V_+ := \{r = (r_1, \ldots, r_n) \in \mathbb{R}^n: r_k > 0\}, \mathfrak{z}_0 \in \mathbb{C}^n$. Then $P_r(\mathfrak{z}_0) := \{z \in \mathbb{C}^n: |z_k - z_k^{(0)}| < r_k$ for $1 \leq k \leq n\}$ is called the polycylinder about $\mathfrak{z}_0$ with (poly-)radius $r$. $T = T(P) := \{z \in \mathbb{C}^n: |z_k - z_k^{(0)}| = r_k\}$ is called the distinguished boundary of $P$ (see Fig. 1).
