Choose $x \in A$ with $\sup f(A) \leqslant f(x) + 1$ and apply the conical support point lemma with $\varepsilon = 1$ and $\gamma = \delta$. We obtain $\psi \in X^*$ such that $\sup \psi(A) = \psi(x_0)$ for some $x_0 \in A$, and $\|\phi - \psi\| \leqslant \delta < 1 = \|\phi\|$, so $\psi \neq \theta$ and is a support functional.
