Necessity is trivial. For sufficiency, if $N = \ker(T)$ is closed, the quotient $X/N$ is a finite-dimensional Hausdorff LTS. By 9E, any linear map on $X/N$ is continuous. Then $T = \hat{T} \circ Q_N$ is continuous.
