The subset $F \subset A$ where $f$ attains its infimum is non-empty, closed and convex. By 8B, $F$ has an extreme point. Since $F$ is $A$-extremal, this extreme point belongs to $\operatorname{ext}(A)$. The converse uses concavity: $f(\sum t_i e_i) \geqslant \sum t_i f(e_i)$.
