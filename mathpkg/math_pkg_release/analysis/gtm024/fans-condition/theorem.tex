Let $A$ be balanced convex absorbing in $X$, $\phi_1,\ldots,\phi_n\in X'$, $c_1,\ldots,c_n\in\mathbb{R}$. Necessary and sufficient that $\forall\delta>0$ $\exists x_\delta\in(1+\delta)A$ with $\phi_i(x_\delta)=c_i$ is $|\sum\alpha_i c_i|\leqslant\sup\{|\sum\alpha_i\phi_i(x)|:x\in A\}$ for all $\alpha_i\in\mathbb{R}$.
