a) Clear. b) Given $x,y$, choose $t > \rho_A(x)+\rho_A(y)$. There exist $\alpha > \rho_A(x)$, $\beta > \rho_A(y)$ with $t = \alpha+\beta$. Since $x/\alpha, y/\beta \in A$ and $A$ is convex, $(x+y)/t$ is in $A$, so $\rho_A(x+y) \leqslant t$. c) $\rho_A(\lambda x) = \inf\{t>0: \lambda x \in tA\} = \inf\{t>0: x \in (t/|\lambda|)A\} = |\lambda|\rho_A(x)$.
