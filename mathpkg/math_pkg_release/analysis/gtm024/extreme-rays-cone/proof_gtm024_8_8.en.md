Choose a strictly positive linear functional $\phi$ on $C \setminus \{\theta\}$ and form the base $K = [\phi; 1] \cap C$. $K$ is compact. Each extreme point of $K$ generates an extreme ray of $C$, and these extreme rays generate $C$ via convex combinations.
