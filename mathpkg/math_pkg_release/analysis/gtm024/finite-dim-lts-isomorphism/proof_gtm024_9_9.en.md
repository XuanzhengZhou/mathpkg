$T$ is an algebraic isomorphism and continuous by induction on the LTS axioms. To show it is open, let $B$ be the unit 2-ball in $\mathbb{F}^n$. Its boundary $S$ is compact, so $T(S)$ is compact and $\theta \notin T(S)$. Thus $X \setminus T(S)$ contains a balanced $\theta$-neighborhood $U$, and $U \subset T(B)$.
