Let $V$ be a balanced $\theta$-neighborhood with $V \subset U$, so $U^\circ \subset V^\circ$. $V^\circ$ is a closed subset of the product $\prod_{x \in X}[-\rho_V(x), \rho_V(x)]$ which is compact by Tychonov's theorem. The weak* topology on $V^\circ$ coincides with the product topology, hence $V^\circ$ is compact.
