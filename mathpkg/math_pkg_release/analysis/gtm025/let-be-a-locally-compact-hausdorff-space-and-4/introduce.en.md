(20.47) Theorem. Let $X$ be a locally compact Hausdorff space and let $L \in \mathfrak{C}_0(X)^*$. Then there exists a complex measure $\mu \in M(X)$ is a theorem from Chapter 20 (Integration Theory Continued) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25).
