Let \(A, B\) be well-ordered sets with order types \(\alpha, \beta\). Consider the family \(\mathfrak{F}\) of all order isomorphisms between initial segments. By the Hausdorff Maximality Principle, there is a maximal such isomorphism \(h\). Either \(\operatorname{dom} h = A\) or \(\operatorname{rng} h = B\), giving the comparability.
