(19.16) Theorem. Let $\nu$ be a complex measure on $(X, \mathcal{A})$ and let $\nu = \sum_{k=1}^{4} \alpha_k \nu_k$ be its Jordan decomposition. A com is a theorem from Chapter 19 (Complex Analysis) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of complex-analysis.
