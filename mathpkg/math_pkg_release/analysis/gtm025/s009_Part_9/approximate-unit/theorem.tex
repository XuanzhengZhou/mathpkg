A sequence $(u_n)_{n=1}^{\infty} \subset \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ is called an approximate unit [or positive kernel] if:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $u_n \geq 0$ for all $n$;
\item[(ii)] $\|u_n\|_1 = 1$ for all $n$;
\item[(iii)] for each neighborhood $V$ of $0$, $\lim_{n \to \infty} \int_{V'} u_n(t) dt = 0$.
\end{enumerate}
