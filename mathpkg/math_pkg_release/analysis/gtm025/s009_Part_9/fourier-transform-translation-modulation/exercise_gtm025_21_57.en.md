(a) For $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ and $a \in R$, let $f_a(x) = f(x + a)$. Prove that $\widehat{f_a}(y) = \hat{f}(y) \exp(iay)$ for all $y \in R$.

(b) Prove that if $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$, $a \in R$, and $g(x) = \exp(-iax)f(x)$, then $\widehat{g}(y) = \hat{f}(y + a)$ for all $y \in R$.
