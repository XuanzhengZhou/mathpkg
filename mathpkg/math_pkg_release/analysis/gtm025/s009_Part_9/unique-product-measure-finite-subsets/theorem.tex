Let $\Omega = \{\gamma_1,\ldots,\gamma_m\} \subset \Gamma$ be finite and nonvoid. There is a unique measure $\mu_\Omega$ on $\mathcal{M}_\Omega$ such that
$$\mu_\Omega(A_{\gamma_1} \times \cdots \times A_{\gamma_m}) = \prod_{j=1}^m \mu_{\gamma_j}(A_{\gamma_j})$$
for all $A_{\gamma_j} \in \mathcal{M}_{\gamma_j}$.
