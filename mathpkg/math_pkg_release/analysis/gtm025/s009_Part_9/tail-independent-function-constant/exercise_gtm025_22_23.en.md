Let $f$ be an $\mathcal{M}$-measurable complex-valued function such that
$$f(u_1,\ldots,u_n,t_{n+1},t_{n+2},\ldots) = f(v_1,\ldots,v_n,t_{n+1},t_{n+2},\ldots)$$
for all positive integers $n$ and all choices of $u_1,\ldots,u_n$ and $v_1,\ldots,v_n$. Prove that $f$ is a constant $\mu$-a.e.

[Hint. Use the argument of (22.21), the Zero-One Law.]
