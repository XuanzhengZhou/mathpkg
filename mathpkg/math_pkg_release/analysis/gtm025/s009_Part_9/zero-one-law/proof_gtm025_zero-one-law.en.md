Let $f = \xi_U$ and form $f_n$ as in (22.16.i). By the tail-invariance hypothesis on $U$, $f_n$ is independent of the first $n$ coordinates, hence $\mathcal{M}_{\Omega_n'}$-measurable. In fact $f_n$ is constant (either $0$ or $1$). By Jessen's pointwise theorem (22.17), $\lim f_n = f$ a.e. Since each $f_n$ is constant, $f$ is constant a.e. Therefore $\mu(U) \in \{0,1\}$.
