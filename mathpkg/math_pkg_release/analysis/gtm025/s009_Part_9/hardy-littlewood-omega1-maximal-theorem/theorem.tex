For $f \in \Omega_1(R)$, $0 < k < 1$, $E \in \mathcal{M}_\lambda$:
$$\int_E f^{\Lambda(j)} d\lambda \leq \frac{\lambda(E)}{k} + \frac{1}{1-k} \int_R f(x) \log^+ f(x) dx \quad (j=r,l),$$
and for $0 < p < 1$:
$$\int_E (f^{\Lambda(j)})^p d\lambda \leq \frac{1}{(1-k)^p} \frac{\lambda(E)^{1-p}}{1-p} \left(\int_R f d\lambda\right)^p.$$
