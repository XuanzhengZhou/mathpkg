(6.72) Theorem. Let $X, Y$, and $f$ be as in (6.68). Suppose that $X$ is compact and that $f$ is continuous on $X$. Then $f(X)$ is a compact subspace is a theorem from Chapter 6 (Topology) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of topology.
