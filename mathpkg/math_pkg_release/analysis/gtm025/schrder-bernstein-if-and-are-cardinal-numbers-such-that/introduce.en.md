(4.7) Theorem [Schröder-Bernstein]. If $u$ and $v$ are cardinal numbers such that $u \leq v$ and $v \leq u$, then $u = v$. is a theorem from Chapter 4 (Unknown Chapter) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of analysis.
