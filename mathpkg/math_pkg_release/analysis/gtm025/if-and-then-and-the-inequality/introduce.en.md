(13.17) Theorem. If $\mu(X) < \infty$ and $0 < p < q < \infty$, then $\mathfrak{L}_q \subset \mathfrak{L}_p$, and the inequality is a theorem from Chapter 13 (Spaces of Integrable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
