(6.59) Theorem. Let $U$ be a nonvoid open subset of $R$. Then there exists one and only one pairwise disjoint family $I$ of open intervals of $R$ such is a theorem from Chapter 6 (Topology) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of topology.
