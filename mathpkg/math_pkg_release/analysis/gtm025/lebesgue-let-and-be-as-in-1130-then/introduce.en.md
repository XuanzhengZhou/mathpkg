(11.31) Theorem [LEBESGUE]. Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu), f,$ and $(f_n)$ be as in (11.30). Then $f_n \rightarrow f$ in measure. is a theorem from Chapter 11 (Measurable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
