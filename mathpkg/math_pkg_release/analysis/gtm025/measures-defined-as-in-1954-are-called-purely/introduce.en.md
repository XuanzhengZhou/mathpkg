(19.55) Definition. Measures $\iota$ defined as in (19.54) are called purely discontinuous. A measure $\iota$ such that $\iota(\{x\}) = 0$ for all $x is a definition from Chapter 19 (Complex Analysis) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of complex-analysis.
