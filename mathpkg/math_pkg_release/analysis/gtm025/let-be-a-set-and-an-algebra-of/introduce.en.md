(10.3) Definition. Let $X$ be a set and $\mathcal{A}$ an algebra of subsets of $X$. A set function $\mu$ defined only on $\mathcal{A}$ is called a fin is a definition from Chapter 10 (Measures) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
