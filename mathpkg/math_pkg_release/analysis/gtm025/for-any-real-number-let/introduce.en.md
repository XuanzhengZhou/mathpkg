(7.25) Theorem. For any real number $\alpha$, let $\binom{\alpha}{n} = \frac{\alpha(\alpha - 1)(\alpha - 2) \cdots (\alpha - n + 1)}{n!}$ is a theorem from Chapter 7 (Spaces of Continuous Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of function-spaces.
