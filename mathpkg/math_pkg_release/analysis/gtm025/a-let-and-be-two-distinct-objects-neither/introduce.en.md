(6.1) Definition. (a) Let $\infty$ and $-\infty$ be two distinct objects, neither of which is a real number$^1$. The set $R^{\#} = R \cup \{-\infty\} is a definition from Chapter 6 (Topology) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of topology.
