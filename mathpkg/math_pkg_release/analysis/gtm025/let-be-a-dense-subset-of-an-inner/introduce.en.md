(16.21) Lemma. Let $S$ be a dense subset of an inner product space $H$. If $x \perp S$, then $x = 0$. is a lemma from Chapter 16 (Integration on Locally Compact Spaces) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
