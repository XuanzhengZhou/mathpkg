(16.3) Theorem. Let $\|x\| = \langle x, x \rangle^{\frac{1}{2}}$. With this definition of norm, an inner product space $H$ is a normed linear space. is a theorem from Chapter 16 (Integration on Locally Compact Spaces) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
