The fact that $T$ is a norm-preserving mapping from $\mathfrak{L}_{\infty}$ into $\mathfrak{L}_1^*$ is (20.16). It follows from (20.19) that $T$ is onto $\mathfrak{L}_1^*$. It is trivial that $T$ is linear. Since $T$ is both linear and norm-preserving, it is one-to-one. $\square$
