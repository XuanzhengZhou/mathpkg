Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a decomposable measure space (19.25). Then the mapping $T$ defined by

$$T(g) = L_{\bar{g}}$$

[see (20.16)] is a norm-preserving linear mapping of $\mathfrak{L}_{\infty}$ onto the conjugate space $\mathfrak{L}_1^*$. Thus, as Banach spaces, $\mathfrak{L}_{\infty}$ and $\mathfrak{L}_1^*$ are isomorphic.
