The sequence of negative powers of two converges to zero in any Archimedean ordered field. This follows from the inequality $2^p > p$ (by binomial expansion) and the fact that $(\frac{1}{p})$ is null, which is a consequence of the Archimedean property. This result is frequently used in completeness arguments for ordered fields.
