The completion operation is idempotent up to isomorphism: completing an already complete field produces an isomorphic copy. Moreover, every Cauchy sequence in a complete field is the sum of a constant sequence and a null sequence. This characterizes completeness in terms of the Cauchy-null decomposition and shows that the completion construction stabilizes after one iteration.
