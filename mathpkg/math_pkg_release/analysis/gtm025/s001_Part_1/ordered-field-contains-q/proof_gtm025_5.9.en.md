Since $1 \in P$, (5.7.iii) implies that $n1 \in P$ for all $n \in N$. This implies that $F$ has characteristic $0$, and so the isomorphism $\tau$ of (5.6.e) can be constructed. It is easy to check that $\tau$ preserves order.
