Every element of the completion $\bar{F}$ can be approximated arbitrarily closely by constant sequences (elements of $F$). This density property means that $F$ embeds as a dense subfield of its completion, analogous to how $Q$ is dense in $R$. The proof uses the Cauchy property of representative sequences and the definition of the ordering on $\bar{F}$.
