The Cauchy completion construction forms a field by taking the quotient of the ring of Cauchy sequences by the ideal of null sequences. This is the algebraic analogue of the Cantor construction of the real numbers. The resulting field $\mathfrak{C}/\mathfrak{N}$ is denoted $\bar{F}$, and elements of $F$ embed as constant sequences. This construction is fundamental for completing any ordered field into one where every Cauchy sequence converges.
