(11.2) Definition. Let $f$ be an extended real-valued function defined on $X$. Suppose that $f^{-1}([a, \infty]) \in \mathcal{A}$ for every $a \in R$, is a definition from Chapter 11 (Measurable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
