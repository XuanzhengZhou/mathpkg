This lemma constructs a nonnegative linear functional I from an arbitrary bounded linear functional L on C_0(X). By taking the supremum of |L(g)| over g dominated by f, one obtains an additive and homogeneous functional on nonnegative functions. This I is a critical intermediate step in proving the Riesz representation theorem, allowing one to apply the representation theorem for nonnegative functionals.
