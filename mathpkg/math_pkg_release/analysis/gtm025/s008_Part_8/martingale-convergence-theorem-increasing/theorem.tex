Let $X$, $\mathcal{M}$, $\mu$, $\eta$, $(\mathcal{M}_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ be as in (20.55). Suppose that

(i) $\mathcal{M}_1 \subset \mathcal{M}_2 \subset \mathcal{M}_3 \subset \cdots,$

and write $\mathcal{M}_\omega$ for the smallest $\sigma$-algebra containing $\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} \mathcal{M}_n$. Let $\mu_\omega$ and $\eta_\omega$ be $\mu$ and $\eta$, respectively, restricted to $\mathcal{M}_\omega$. Then both of the functions

$$f = \liminf_{n \to \infty} f_n \quad \text{and} \quad \bar{f} = \limsup_{n \to \infty} f_n$$

are derivatives of $\eta_\omega$ with respect to $\mu_\omega$.
