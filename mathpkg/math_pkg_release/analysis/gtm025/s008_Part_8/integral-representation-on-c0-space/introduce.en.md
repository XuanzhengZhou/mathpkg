This theorem shows that every complex regular Borel measure mu defines a bounded linear functional L_mu on C_0(X) via integration. Moreover, the operator norm of L_mu coincides with the total variation of mu. This establishes an isometric embedding of M(X) into the dual space of C_0(X), which will later be shown to be surjective via the Riesz representation theorem.
