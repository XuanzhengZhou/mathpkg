This theorem is the surjectivity part of the Riesz representation theorem for C_0(X). It states that every bounded linear functional L on C_0(X) can be represented as integration against a complex regular Borel measure mu. The proof uses the nonnegative functional I constructed from L, applies F. Riesz's representation theorem for nonnegative functionals to obtain a positive measure iota, then uses the Radon-Nikodym theorem to extract mu.
