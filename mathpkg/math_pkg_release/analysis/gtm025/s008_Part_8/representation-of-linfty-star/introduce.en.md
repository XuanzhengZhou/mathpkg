This theorem completes the description of the dual space of L_infinity for arbitrary measure spaces. It shows that every bounded linear functional on L_infinity can be represented as integration against a finitely additive measure tau in F(X, A, mu). Together with (20.33), this establishes an isometric isomorphism between F(X, A, mu) and the dual of L_infinity.
