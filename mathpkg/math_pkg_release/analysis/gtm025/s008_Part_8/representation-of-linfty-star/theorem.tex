Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be an arbitrary measure space. Then the mapping $T$ defined by
$$T(\tau) = L_{\tau}$$
[where $L_{\tau}(g) = \int_X g d\tau$] is a norm-preserving linear mapping of $F(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ onto $\mathfrak{L}_{\infty}(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)^*$. Thus, as Banach spaces, $F(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ and $\mathfrak{L}_{\infty}^*$ are isomorphic.
