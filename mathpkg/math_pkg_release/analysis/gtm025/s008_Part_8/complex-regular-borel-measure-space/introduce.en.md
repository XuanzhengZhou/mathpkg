This definition introduces the space M(X) of complex regular Borel measures on a locally compact Hausdorff space X. A complex measure mu on the Borel sigma-algebra is regular if each Borel set can be approximated from inside by compact sets and from outside by open sets with respect to the total variation of mu. The space M(X) serves as the predual of C_0(X) via the Riesz representation theorem.
