This theorem describes how absolute continuity and mutual singularity behave under the formation of product measures. If both factor measures are absolutely continuous, the product is absolutely continuous with a Radon-Nikodym derivative equal to the product of the factor derivatives. If either factor is singular, the product is singular. The theorem also provides an explicit formula for the Lebesgue decomposition of the product.
