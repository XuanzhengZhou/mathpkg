This theorem extends (21.4) from sets to functions: if f is product-measurable, then each X-section and Y-section is measurable in the appropriate factor space. This property is essential for the application of Tonelli's and Fubini's theorems, as it ensures the inner integrals in iterated integration are well-defined.
