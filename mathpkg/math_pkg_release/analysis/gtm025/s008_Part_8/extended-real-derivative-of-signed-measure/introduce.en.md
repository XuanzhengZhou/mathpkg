This definition generalizes the Radon-Nikodym derivative to allow infinite values. For a signed measure eta with Lebesgue decomposition eta_a + eta_s relative to mu, the derivative f equals the ordinary Radon-Nikodym derivative on the mu-continuous part, and takes the values plus or minus infinity on the singular part according to the Hahn decomposition. This extended concept is particularly useful for martingale limit theorems.
