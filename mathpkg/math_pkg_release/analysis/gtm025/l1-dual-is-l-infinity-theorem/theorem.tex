Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a decomposable measure space and $L$ a bounded linear functional on $\mathfrak{L}_1(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$. Then there exists $g \in \mathfrak{L}_{\infty}(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ such that $L(f) = \int_X f g d\mu$ for all $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1$ and $\|L\| = \|g\|_{\infty}$.
