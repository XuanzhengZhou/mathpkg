Let $\delta > 0$ satisfy the conditions of Definition (18.10) for $\varepsilon = 1$. Let $n$ be any integer such that $n > \frac{b-a}{\delta}$, and subdivide $[a, b]$ by points $a = x_0 < x_1 < \cdots < x_n = b$ such that $x_k - x_{k-1} = \frac{b-a}{n} < \delta$ for $k = 1, 2, \ldots, n$. From our choice of $\delta$ it follows that $V_{x_{k-1}}^{x_k} f \leq 1$ for all $k$. Thus $V_a^b f = \sum_{k=1}^{n} V_{x_{k-1}}^{x_k} f \leq n$.
