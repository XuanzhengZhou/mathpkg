(18.12) Theorem. Any complex-valued absolutely continuous function $f$ defined on $[a, b]$ has finite variation on $[a, b]$. is a theorem from Chapter 18 (Differentiation) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of differentiation.
