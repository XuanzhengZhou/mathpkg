**Proof.** The Caratheodory condition is symmetric in $A$ and $A'$:
$$\mu(T) = \mu(T \cap A) + \mu(T \cap A') = \mu(T \cap A') + \mu(T \cap (A')'),$$
for all $T \subset X$. Hence $A'$ satisfies the measurability condition. $\square$
