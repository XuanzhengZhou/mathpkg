Let $X$ be a metric space. For $\varepsilon > 0$, let $\mathcal{O}_\varepsilon = \{U \subset X : \operatorname{diam} U \leq \varepsilon\} \cup \{\emptyset\}$. Define $\mu_{p,\varepsilon}(E) = \inf\{\sum (\operatorname{diam} U_n)^p : U_n \in \mathcal{O}_\varepsilon, E \subset \bigcup U_n\}$. Prove that $\mu_{p,\varepsilon}(E)$ is nondecreasing as $\varepsilon$ decreases. Define $\mu_p(E) = \lim_{\varepsilon \to 0} \mu_{p,\varepsilon}(E)$ (Hausdorff $p$-dimensional outer measure).
