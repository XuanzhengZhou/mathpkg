An outer measure $\mu$ on $\mathcal{P}(X)$ is regular if for each $E \subset X$ there is a $\mu$-measurable $A$ with $E \subset A$ and $\mu(A) = \mu(E)$. Prove that any outer measure obtained from a measure on an algebra as in (10.36) is regular.
