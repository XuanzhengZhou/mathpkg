**Proof.** For sup: $\{\sup_n f_n > a\} = \bigcup_{n} \{f_n > a\} \in \mathcal{A}$. For inf: $\{\inf_n f_n < a\} = \bigcup_{n} \{f_n < a\} \in \mathcal{A}$. For limsup: $\limsup f_n = \inf_m \sup_{n \geq m} f_n$, measurable as composition of measurable operations. Similarly for liminf. $\square$
