Let $X$ be locally compact Hausdorff and $\iota$ as in Section 9. For $A \subset X$ with $\iota(A) < \infty$, the following are equivalent:
(i) $A$ is $\iota$-measurable;
(ii) $\iota(U) = \iota(U \cap A) + \iota(U \cap A')$ for all open $U$;
(iii) $A \cap U$ is $\iota$-measurable for all open $U$;
(iv) $U \cap A$ is a countable union of $\iota$-measurable sets for all open $U$.
