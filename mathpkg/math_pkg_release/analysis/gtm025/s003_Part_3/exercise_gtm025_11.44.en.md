Let $X$ be locally compact Hausdorff and $\iota$ as in Section 9. Suppose $f$ is a complex-valued $\mathcal{M}_\iota$-measurable function on $X$ such that $\{x : f(x) \neq 0\}$ is $\sigma$-finite. Prove there exists a Borel measurable function $g$ on $X$ with $|g| \leq |f|$ and $\iota(\{x : f(x) \neq g(x)\}) = 0$.
