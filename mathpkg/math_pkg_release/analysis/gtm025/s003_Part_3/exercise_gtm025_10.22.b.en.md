Let $X$ be locally compact Hausdorff, $\{x_n\}$ a countable subset of $X$, and $(\alpha_n)$ a sequence of positive numbers with $\sum \alpha_n < \infty$. Let $I(\varphi) = \sum \alpha_n \varphi(x_n)$ for $\varphi \in \mathfrak{C}_{00}(X)$. Prove $I$ is a nonnegative linear functional; the corresponding measure $\iota$ is $\sum \alpha_n \varepsilon_{x_n}$; and $M_i = \mathcal{P}(X)$.
