Let $\mu$ be a regular outer measure, $E \subset X$, and $\mathcal{A}$ the smallest algebra containing $\{E\} \cup \mathcal{M}_\mu$. Prove that if $\mu$ is finitely additive on $\mathcal{A}$, then $E \in \mathcal{M}_\mu$.
