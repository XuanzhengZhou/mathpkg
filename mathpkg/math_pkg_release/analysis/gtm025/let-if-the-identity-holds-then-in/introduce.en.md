(16.34) Theorem. Let $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1([-\pi, \pi])$. If the identity $f = 0$ holds, then $f = 0$ in $\mathfrak{L}_1$. is a theorem from Chapter 16 (Integration on Locally Compact Spaces) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
