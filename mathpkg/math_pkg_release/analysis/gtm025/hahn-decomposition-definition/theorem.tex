Let $\nu$ be a signed measure on $(X, \mathcal{A})$. A \textbf{Hahn decomposition} is a partition $(P, P')$ of $X$ such that $\nu(E) \geq 0$ for $E \subset P$ and $\nu(E) \leq 0$ for $E \subset P'$. The measures $\nu^+, \nu^-, |\nu|$ are defined by $\nu^+(E) = \nu(E \cap P)$, $\nu^-(E) = -\nu(E \cap P')$, $|\nu| = \nu^+ + \nu^-$.
