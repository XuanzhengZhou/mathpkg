(12.13) Theorem. Let $f$ be a nonnegative $\mathcal{A}$-measurable function on $X$. Then $L(f) = 0$ if and only if $f = 0$ $\mu$-a.e. is a theorem from Chapter 12 (The Lebesgue Integral) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
