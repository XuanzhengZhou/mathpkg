Let $\{x_k\}$ be a countable subset of $\mathbb{R}$ and $\varphi(x_k) > 0$ with $\sum_{|x_k|\leq n} \varphi(x_k) < \infty$ for all $n$. Then $\iota(A) = \sum_{k=1}^\infty \varphi(x_k) \xi_A(x_k)$ is a regular Borel measure, obtained from the nonnegative linear functional $I(f) = \sum \varphi(x_k) f(x_k)$ on $\mathfrak{C}_{00}(\mathbb{R})$.
