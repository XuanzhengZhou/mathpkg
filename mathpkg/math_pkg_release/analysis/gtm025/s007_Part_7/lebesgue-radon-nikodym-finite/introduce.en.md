The Lebesgue-Radon-Nikodym theorem is a cornerstone of measure theory. For finite measures with $\nu \ll \mu$, it guarantees a density $f_0 = d\nu/d\mu$ such that integration against $\nu$ is integration against $f_0 d\mu$. This generalizes the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.
