Links the classical notion of absolute continuity for functions to the measure-theoretic notion. A nondecreasing function is absolutely continuous in the classical sense exactly when its associated Lebesgue-Stieltjes measure is absolutely continuous with respect to Lebesgue measure. This is the key to connecting the Radon-Nikodym theorem with the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.
