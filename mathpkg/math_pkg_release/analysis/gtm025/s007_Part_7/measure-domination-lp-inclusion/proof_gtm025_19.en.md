Take a sequence $(\sigma_n)$ of nonnegative simple functions increasing to $|f|^p$. Since $\int \sigma_n d\nu \le \int \sigma_n d\mu$, the monotone convergence theorem yields $\int |f|^p d\nu = \lim \int \sigma_n d\nu \le \lim \int \sigma_n d\mu = \int |f|^p d\mu < \infty$.
