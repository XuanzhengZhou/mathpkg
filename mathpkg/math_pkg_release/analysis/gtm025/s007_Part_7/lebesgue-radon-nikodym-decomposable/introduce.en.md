The most general Radon-Nikodym theorem. For each set $F$ in the decomposition, the $\sigma$-finite version is applied after using Lemma 19.26 to separate the extreme-value part. On sets where $\nu$ takes only $0$ or $\infty$, the density is $\infty$.
