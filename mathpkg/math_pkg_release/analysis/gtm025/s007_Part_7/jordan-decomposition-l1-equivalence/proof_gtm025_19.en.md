For a nonnegative simple function $f = \sum \beta_j \xi_{E_j}$, we have
$\int f d|\nu| = \sum \beta_j |\nu|(E_j) \leq \sum \beta_j \sum \nu_k(E_j) = \sum \int f d\nu_k$,
and $\int |f| d\nu_k \leq \int |f| d|\nu|$ for all $k$. The general case follows by approximation.
