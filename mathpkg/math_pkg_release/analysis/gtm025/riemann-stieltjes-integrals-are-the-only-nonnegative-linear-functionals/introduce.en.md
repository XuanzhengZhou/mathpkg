(19.50) Theorem. Riemann-Stieltjes integrals $S_\alpha$ are the only nonnegative linear functionals on $\mathfrak{C}_{00}(R)$. is a theorem from Chapter 19 (Complex Analysis) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of complex-analysis.
