Assume $f_n$ does not converge weakly to $f$. Choose $g \in \mathfrak{L}_{p'}$ with $\limsup |\int (f - f_n)g| = \alpha > 0$. Extract $n_k$ with $|\int (f - f_{n_k})g| \to \alpha$. By (11.26), extract a further subsequence converging a.e. to $f$. By (13.44), this would converge weakly, contradiction.
