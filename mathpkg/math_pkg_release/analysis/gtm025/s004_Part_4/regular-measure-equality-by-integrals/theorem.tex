Let $X$ be a locally compact Hausdorff space and let $\mu$ and $\nu$ be regular measures defined on $\sigma$-algebras $\mathcal{M}_\mu$ and $\mathcal{M}_\nu$ respectively. Suppose that $\int_X f d\mu = \int_X f d\nu$ for all $f \in \mathfrak{C}_{00}^+(X)$. Then $\mu(E) = \nu(E)$ for all $E \in \mathcal{M}_\mu \cap \mathcal{M}_\nu$.
