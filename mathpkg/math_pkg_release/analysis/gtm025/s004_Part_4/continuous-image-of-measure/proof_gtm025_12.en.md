The mapping $f \mapsto \int_X f \circ \varphi d\mu$ is a nonnegative linear functional on $\mathfrak{C}_{00}(Y)$. By the Riesz representation theorem (12.36), it corresponds to a unique regular measure $\nu$. The equality $\nu(B) = \mu(\varphi^{-1}(B))$ for Borel sets is established via approximation from above by open sets and from below by compact sets.
