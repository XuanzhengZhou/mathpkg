For $f \geq 0$, the translation and inversion invariance of the Riemann integral gives $\bar{S}(f_t) = \bar{S}(f^\star) = \bar{S}(f)$. Now apply (12.35) and refer to (12.2) and (12.26) for conditions under which the integrals are defined.
