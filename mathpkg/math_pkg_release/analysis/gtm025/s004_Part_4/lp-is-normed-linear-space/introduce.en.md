After identifying functions that are equal almost everywhere, $\mathfrak{L}_p$ becomes a normed linear space. The norm is $\|f\|_p = (\int |f|^p d\mu)^{1/p}$, and all norm axioms are satisfied, with Minkowski's inequality providing the triangle inequality.
