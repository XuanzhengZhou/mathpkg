Given $\varepsilon > 0$, choose $\varphi \in \mathfrak{C}_{00}(R)$ with $\|\varphi - f\|_p < \varepsilon/3$ using (13.21). Since $\varphi$ is uniformly continuous and compactly supported, for sufficiently small $h$, $\|\varphi_h - \varphi\|_p < \varepsilon/3$. Then $\|f_h - f\|_p \leq \|f_h - \varphi_h\|_p + \|\varphi_h - \varphi\|_p + \|\varphi - f\|_p < \varepsilon$.
