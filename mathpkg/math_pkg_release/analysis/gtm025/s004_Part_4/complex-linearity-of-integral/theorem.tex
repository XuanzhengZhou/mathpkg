Let $f, g \in \mathfrak{L}_1$ and $\alpha, \beta \in K$. Then $\alpha f + \beta g \in \mathfrak{L}_1$ and $\int_X (\alpha f + \beta g) d\mu = \alpha \int_X f d\mu + \beta \int_X g d\mu$, i.e., $\mathfrak{L}_1$ is a complex linear space and $\int_X \cdots d\mu$ is a linear functional on $\mathfrak{L}_1$.
