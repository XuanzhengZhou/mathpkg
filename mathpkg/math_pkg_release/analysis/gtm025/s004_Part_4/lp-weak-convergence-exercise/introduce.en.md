Notation is as in (13.41). Suppose that $\|f_n\|_p \to \|f\|_p$. Prove the following.

(a) If $f_n \to f$ $\mu$-a.e., then $\|f - f_n\|_p \to 0$.

(b) If $f_n \to f$ in measure, then $\|f - f_n\|_p \to 0$. [Recall that $1 \le p < \infty$.]
