The operator norm of a bounded linear transformation can be computed as the supremum of $\|T(x)\|$ over the closed unit ball, the unit sphere, or as the supremum of the ratio $\|T(x)\|/\|x\|$ over all nonzero vectors. All these formulas are equivalent and follow from the definition and linearity of $T$.
