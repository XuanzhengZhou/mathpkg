(10.27) Lemma. For all $x \in R$ and $A \subset R$, the equalities $\lambda(x + A) = \lambda(A) = \lambda(-A)$ obtain. is a lemma from Chapter 10 (Measures) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
