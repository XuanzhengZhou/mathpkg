(13.4) Theorem [Hölder's inequality for $p > 1$]. Let $f \in \mathfrak{L}_p$ and $g \in \mathfrak{L}_p'$, where $p > 1$. Then $fg \in \mathfrak{L}_1$, is a theorem from Chapter 13 (Spaces of Integrable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
