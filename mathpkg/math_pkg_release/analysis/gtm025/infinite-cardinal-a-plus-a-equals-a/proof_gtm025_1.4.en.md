Let \(A\) be a set with \(\overline{A} = a\). Consider the family \(\mathfrak{F}\) of all one-to-one functions \(f\) with domain a subset of \(A\) and range a subset of \(A \times \{0,1\}\). Using the Hausdorff Maximality Principle, obtain a maximal such function \(g\). Show that \(\operatorname{dom} g\) has cardinality \(a\), giving the result.
