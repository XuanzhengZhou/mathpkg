[HOLDER's inequality for $0 < p < 1$]. Let $0 < p < 1$ and let $f$ and $g$ be functions in $\mathfrak{L}_p^+$ and $\mathfrak{L}_p^+$, respectively. Then we have

(i) $$\int f g d\mu \geq \left( \int f^p d\mu \right)^{\frac{1}{p}} \left( \int g^{p'} d\mu \right)^{\frac{1}{p'}}$$

unless $\int g^{p'} d\mu = 0$ [note that $p' < 0$].
