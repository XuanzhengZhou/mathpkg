Since \(1 \in P\), we have \(n \cdot 1 \in P\) for all \(n \in N\), so \(F\) has characteristic \(0\). Construct the isomorphism \(\tau((m \cdot 1)(n \cdot 1)^{-1}) = \frac{m}{n}\) and verify it preserves order.
