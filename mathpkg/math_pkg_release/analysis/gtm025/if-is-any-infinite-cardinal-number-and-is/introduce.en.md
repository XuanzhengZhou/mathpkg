(4.30) Corollary. If $a$ is any infinite cardinal number and $b$ is any cardinal number such that $\mathfrak{b} \leq a$, then $a + b = a$. is a corollary from Chapter 4 (Unknown Chapter) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of analysis.
