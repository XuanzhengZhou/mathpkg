(10.7) Theorem. Every subset $A$ of $X$ such that $\mu(A) = 0$ is $\mu$-measurable, and $\mu(T) = \mu(T \cap A')$ for all $T \subset X$. is a theorem from Chapter 10 (Measures) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
