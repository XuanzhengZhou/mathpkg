(16.19) Definition. An orthonormal set $E$ in an inner product space $H$ is said to be complete if the only vector orthogonal to all elements of $E$ i is a definition from Chapter 16 (Integration on Locally Compact Spaces) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
