(5.27) Lemma. For $\alpha > \bar{0}, \alpha \in \bar{F}$, there exists $e \in F$ such that $\bar{0} < \bar{e} < \alpha$. If $F$ is Archimedean ordered is a lemma from Chapter 5 (Set Theory and Algebra) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of set-theory.
