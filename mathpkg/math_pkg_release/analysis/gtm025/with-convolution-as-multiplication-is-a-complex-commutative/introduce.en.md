(21.34) Theorem. With convolution as multiplication, $\mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ is a complex commutative Banach algebra. is a theorem from Chapter 21 (Hilbert Spaces and Spectral Theory) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
