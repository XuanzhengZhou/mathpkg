Let $E$ be a separable Banach space, $(x_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ a countable dense subset of $E$, and $B = \{f \in E^* : \|f\| \leq 1\}$. For $f, g \in B$, define
$$\varrho(f, g) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2^n} \frac{|f(x_n) - g(x_n)|}{1 + |f(x_n) - g(x_n)|}.$$
Prove that (a) $\varrho$ is a metric for $B$, and (b) with the metric $\varrho$, $B$ is a compact metric space.
