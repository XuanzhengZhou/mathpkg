Let $A$ and $B$ be normed linear spaces and $T: A \to B$ bounded linear. For $g \in B^*$, define $T^*(g) = g \circ T$. Prove:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $T^*$ is a bounded linear transformation from $B^*$ into $A^*$;
\item[(b)] $\|T^*\| = \|T\|$;
\item[(c)] $T^*$ is one-to-one iff $T(A)$ is dense in $B$;
\item[(d)] $T$ is one-to-one iff $T^*(B^*)$ separates points of $A$.
\end{itemize}
