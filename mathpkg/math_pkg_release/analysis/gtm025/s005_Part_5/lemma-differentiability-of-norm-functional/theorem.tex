Let $E$ be a complex normed linear space, and let $L$ be a nonzero bounded linear functional on $E$ for which there exists $g \in E$ with $\|g\| = 1$ and $L(g) = \|L\|$. For $f \in E$, define $\psi_f(t) = \|g + tf\|$ on $\mathbb{R}$. If $\psi_f$ and $\psi_{-if}$ are differentiable at $t = 0$, then
$$\frac{1}{\|L\|} L(f) = \psi_f'(0) + i \psi_{-if}'(0).$$
