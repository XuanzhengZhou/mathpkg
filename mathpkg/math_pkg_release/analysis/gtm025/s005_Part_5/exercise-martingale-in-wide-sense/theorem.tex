Let $H$ be an inner product space, $\{x_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty} \subset H$, and $S_n = \operatorname{span}\{x_1,\ldots,x_n\}$. Suppose $x_n$ is the element of $S_n$ nearest to $x_{n+1}$ for each $n$ (a martingale in the wide sense). Write $y_1 = x_1$ and $y_n = x_n - x_{n-1}$ for $n > 1$. Prove: (a) $y_n$ are pairwise orthogonal and $x_n = y_1 + \cdots + y_n$; (b) $\|x_1\| \leq \|x_2\| \leq \cdots$.
