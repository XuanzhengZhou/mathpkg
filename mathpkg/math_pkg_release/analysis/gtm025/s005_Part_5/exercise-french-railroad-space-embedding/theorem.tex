Let $H = \ell_2(\mathbb{R})$ and for each $t \in \mathbb{R}$ let $u_t(s) = \delta_{t,s}$. Define $X = \{f \in H : f = \alpha u_t \text{ for some } t \in \mathbb{R}, 0 \leq \alpha \leq 1\}$. Prove there exists a bijection $\varphi$ of $X$ onto the French railroad space such that $\varphi$ and $\varphi^{-1}$ are uniformly continuous.
