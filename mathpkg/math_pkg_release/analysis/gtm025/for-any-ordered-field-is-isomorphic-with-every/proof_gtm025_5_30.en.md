For a Cauchy sequence $(\alpha_p)$ of elements of $\overline{F}$, let $\beta = \lim_{p \to \infty} \alpha_p$ (5.29). Then $(\alpha_p - \beta(p))$ is a null sequence, and the theorem follows.
