(10.10) Theorem.** If $A$ and $B$ are $\mu$-measurable, then $A \cap B'$ is $\mu$-measurable. is a theorem from Chapter 10 (Measures) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
