(15.10) Lemma.** Let $E$ be a complex normed linear space, and let $L$ be a nonzero bounded linear functional on $E$ for which there exists $g \in E$ is a lemma from Chapter 15 (Lp Spaces) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
