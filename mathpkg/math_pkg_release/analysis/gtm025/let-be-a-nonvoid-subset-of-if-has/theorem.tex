Let $A$ be a nonvoid subset of $R^\#$. If $A$ has no upper [lower] bound in $R$, we say that the supremum [infimum] of $A$ is $\infty [-\infty]$ and write $\sup A = \infty [\inf A = -\infty]$.

(6.58) Remark. In view of (5.33) and (6.57), every nonvoid subset of $R$ has both a supremum and an infimum in $R^\#$.
