This corollary identifies $vX$ as the maximal subspace of the Stone-Čech compactification $\beta X$ in which $X$ remains $C$-embedded. In other words, any subspace $T$ with $X \subset T \subset \beta X$ in which $X$ is $C$-embedded must satisfy $T \subset vX$. This property characterizes $vX$ uniquely among the subspaces of $\beta X$.
