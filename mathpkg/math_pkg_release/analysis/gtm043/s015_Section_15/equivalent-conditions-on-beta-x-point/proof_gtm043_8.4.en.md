If $M^p$ is real, then by Theorem 7.6, each of the conditions (2), (3), and (4) is satisfied.

Conversely, if $M^p$ is hyper-real, there exists a positive function $g \in C(X)$ such that $M^p(g)$ is infinitely large. Then $g$ violates (2) (and (3)), while $g^{-1}$ violates (3) and (4).
