When the original map $\tau$ is a homeomorphism (embedding), the closed extension $\tau_0$ maps the remainder $X_0 - X$ onto $Y - \tau[X]$. This implies that every compactification $Y$ of $X$ is a quotient of $\beta X$ obtained by identifying points in $\beta X - X$ without deleting any. Thus $\beta X$ is the maximal compactification, and all others arise from it by forming quotients of the remainder.
