Let $\tau$ be a quotient mapping from $X$ onto $Y$. The quotient space of $X$ relative to the quotient mapping $\tau$ is denoted by $Y_{\tau}$. The quotient topology is characterized as follows: a subset $F$ of $Y$ is closed in $Y_{\tau}$ if and only if $\tau^{\leftarrow}[F]$ is closed in $X$. Dually, the corresponding condition holds for open sets.
