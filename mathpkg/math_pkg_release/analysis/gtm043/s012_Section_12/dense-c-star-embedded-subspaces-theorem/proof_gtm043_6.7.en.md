The proof is based upon condition (2) of Theorem 6.4: $X$ is $C^*$-embedded in $T$.

**(2) implies (7).** $X$ is dense and $C^*$-embedded in $\beta T$. Therefore $\beta X = \beta T$.

**(7) implies (6).** $X \subset T \subset \beta T = \beta X$.

**(6) implies (2).** $X$ is $C^*$-embedded in $\beta X$, and hence in $T$.
