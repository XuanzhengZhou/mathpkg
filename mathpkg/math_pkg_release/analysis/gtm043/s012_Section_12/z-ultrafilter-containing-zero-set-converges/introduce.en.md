If a point $p$ belongs to the closure of a zero-set $Z$ in $T$, then we can find a $z$-ultrafilter on $X$ that contains $Z$ and has $p$ as its limit. The proof uses the trace on $X$ of the $z$-filter of zero-set-neighborhoods of $p$, which together with $Z$ generates a $z$-filter with the finite intersection property, extendable to a $z$-ultrafilter. This lemma is a key technical tool in the construction of $\beta X$ and the proof of the Compactification Theorem (6.5).
