Let $X$ be a completely regular space. A $C$-embedded countable set $S$ is completely separated from every disjoint closed set. [Theorem 1.18.] (This is false if $S$ is uncountable (5.13) or if $S$ is only $C^*$-embedded (4M), even if $S$ is closed (8.20 and 6P).)
