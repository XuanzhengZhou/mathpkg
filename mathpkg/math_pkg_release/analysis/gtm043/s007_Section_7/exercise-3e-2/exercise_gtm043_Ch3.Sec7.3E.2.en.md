Let $X$ be a nonnormal, Hausdorff space. $X$ has a subspace $S$ with the following property: any two completely separated sets in $S$ have disjoint closures in $X$, yet $S$ is not $C^*$-embedded in $X$. Compare Urysohn's extension theorem.
