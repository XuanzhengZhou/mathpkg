Let $X$ be a completely regular space. If $X$ is pseudocompact, then $X$ is countably compact if and only if every countable, closed discrete subset $D$ satisfies the conditions of 4. (The interesting condition here is (2), which does not mention continuous functions. We remark that a countably compact space need not be normal; see 8L.)
