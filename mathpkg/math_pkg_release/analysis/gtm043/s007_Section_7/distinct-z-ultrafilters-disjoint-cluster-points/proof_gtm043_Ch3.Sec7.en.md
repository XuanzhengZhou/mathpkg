Suppose two distinct $z$-ultrafilters $\mathcal{F}_1$ and $\mathcal{F}_2$ have a common cluster point $p$. Then by 3.18(a), $\mathcal{F}_1 \subset A_p$ and $\mathcal{F}_2 \subset A_p$. Since $A_p$ is a $z$-ultrafilter, maximality implies $\mathcal{F}_1 = A_p = \mathcal{F}_2$, contradicting that they are distinct. Therefore distinct $z$-ultrafilters cannot share a cluster point.
