Let $\mathcal{F}$ be a $z$-filter on a completely regular space $X$, and let $p$ be a cluster point of $\mathcal{F}$. $\mathcal{F}$ converges to $p$ if and only if $\mathcal{F}$ is contained in a unique $z$-ultrafilter. [Modify the proof of Theorem 3.17.]
