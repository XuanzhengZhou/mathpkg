This corollary establishes that for each point $p$ in a completely regular space, there is exactly one $z$-ultrafilter that converges to $p$, namely $A_p$, the family of all zero-sets containing $p$. This is an immediate consequence of the characterization of cluster points via $A_p$ (3.18(a)), together with the fact that convergence of a $z$-ultrafilter is equivalent to having that point as a cluster point.
