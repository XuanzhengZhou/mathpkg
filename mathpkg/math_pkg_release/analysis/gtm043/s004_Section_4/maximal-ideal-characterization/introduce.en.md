Theorem 2.6 provides a simple characterization of maximal ideals in $C(X)$ and $z$-ultrafilters: a function belongs to a maximal ideal precisely when its zero-set meets every zero-set of the ideal. Dually, a zero-set belongs to a $z$-ultrafilter exactly when it meets every member of the ultrafilter. This property is characteristic of maximality.
