$Z(f^n) = Z(f)$ for every $n \in \mathbf{N}$. Hence if $I$ is any $z$-ideal, then $f^n \in I$ implies $f \in I$. But this property characterizes $I$ as the intersection of all the prime ideals containing it (0.18).
