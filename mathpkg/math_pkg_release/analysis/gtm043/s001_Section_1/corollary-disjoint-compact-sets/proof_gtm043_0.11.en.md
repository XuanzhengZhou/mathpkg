In a Hausdorff space, a compact set and a point outside it have disjoint neighborhoods. Thus, given disjoint compact sets $K_1$ and $K_2$, one set and each point of the other have disjoint neighborhoods. Applying Lemma 0.10 (the lemma on disjoint neighborhoods of a set and a compact set) now yields the result.
