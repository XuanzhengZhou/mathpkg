Let $X$ be a completely regular space. Prove: If $f \in C(X)$ and $f$ is unbounded, then $d = \psi_f \wedge 1$, which is a bounded pseudometric, does not belong to $\mathcal{C}^*$. [$X$ has an infinite $d$-discrete subset.]
