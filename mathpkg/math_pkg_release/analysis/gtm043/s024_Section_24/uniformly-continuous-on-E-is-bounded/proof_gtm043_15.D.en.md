If $d(x, y) \leq \delta$ implies $|f(x) - f(y)| \leq 1$, then for all $x$,
$$|f(x)| \leq |f(0)| + 1 + 1/\delta.$$
Hence every uniformly continuous function on $E$ is bounded. Consequently, in spite of property 3, every pseudometric belonging to the structure on $E$ is bounded.
