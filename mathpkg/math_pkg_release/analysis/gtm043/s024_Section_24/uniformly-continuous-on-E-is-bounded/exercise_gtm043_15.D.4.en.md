Prove: Every uniformly continuous function on $E$ is bounded. [If $d(x, y) \leq \delta$ implies $|f(x) - f(y)| \leq 1$, then $|f(x)| \leq |f(0)| + 1 + 1/\delta$ for all $x$.] Hence, in spite of 3, every pseudometric belonging to the structure on $E$ is bounded.
