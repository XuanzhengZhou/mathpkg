By hypothesis, $X - U_k$ is a zero-set $Z_k$. Since $\mathcal{U}$ is a cover, $\bigcap_k Z_k = \emptyset$. Therefore $\bigcap_k \operatorname{cl} Z_k = \emptyset$, by (IV) of Theorem 6.5, whence $\mathcal{U}^\beta$ covers $\beta X$. Since
$$\bigcup_k \operatorname{cl} Z_k = \operatorname{cl} \bigcup_k Z_k,$$
over any common (finite) index set, the orders of $\mathcal{U}$ and $\mathcal{U}^\beta$ are the same.
