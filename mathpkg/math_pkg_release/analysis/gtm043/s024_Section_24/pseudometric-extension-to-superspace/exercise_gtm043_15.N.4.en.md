Prove: If $[X; \mathcal{D}]$ is dense in a Hausdorff uniform space $[T; \mathcal{U}]$, then every pseudometric in $\mathcal{D}$ has an extension to one in $\mathcal{U}$. [Use 15N.1. Alternatively, use 15N.3 and the fact that $\gamma T = \gamma X$.]
