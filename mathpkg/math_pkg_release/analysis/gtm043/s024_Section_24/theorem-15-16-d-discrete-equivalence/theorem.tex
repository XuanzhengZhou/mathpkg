Let $[X; \mathcal{D}]$ be a Hausdorff uniform space. The following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] Every real $z$-ultrafilter on $X$ is Cauchy.
\item[(2)] The cardinal of every $d$-discrete set in $X$ is nonmeasurable.
\item[(3)] $X$ is a nonmeasurable union of sets of arbitrarily small $d$-diameter.
\item[(4)] $vX \subset cX$.
\end{enumerate}
