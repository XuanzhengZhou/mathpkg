This result establishes that the $F$-space property is preserved under $C^*$-embeddings and homomorphic images of the function ring. Consequently, every cozero-set in an $F$-space is itself an $F$-space. These hereditary properties are essential for understanding the structure of $F$-spaces and their subspaces, and follow directly from the characterization that all finitely generated ideals are principal.
