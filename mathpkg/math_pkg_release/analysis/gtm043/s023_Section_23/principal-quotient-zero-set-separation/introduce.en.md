This lemma provides a key link between algebraic conditions in the quotient ring $C/I$ and topological separation properties of zero-sets. When the ideal generated by the images of $f$ and $|f|$ in $C/I$ is principal, one can find a zero-set $Z$ of the ideal $I$ on which the positive and negative parts of $f$ are completely separated. This result serves as a crucial technical tool for subsequent characterizations of prime $z$-ideals and $F$-spaces.
