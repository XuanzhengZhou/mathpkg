Let $\Sigma = \mathbf{N} \cup \{\sigma\}$, where $\sigma \in \beta\mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$. The natural isomorphism of $C(\Sigma)$ into $C(\mathbf{N})$ carries $O_{\sigma}$ (in $C(\Sigma)$) onto $M^{\sigma}$ (in $C(\mathbf{N})$).
