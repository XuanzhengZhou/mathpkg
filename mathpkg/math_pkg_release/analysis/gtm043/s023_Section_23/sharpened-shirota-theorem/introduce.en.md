Theorem 15.21 sharpens Shirota's result by providing equivalent characterizations of completeness under the nonmeasurable cardinal hypothesis. The theorem shows that in this setting, completeness of the uniform structure, realcompactness of the underlying topological space, and the property that every real $z$-ultrafilter is a Cauchy $z$-filter are all equivalent.
