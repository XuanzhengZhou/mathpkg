This theorem provides five equivalent characterizations of absolutely convex ideals in a lattice-ordered ring. The most significant equivalence is between absolute convexity and the condition that the canonical projection $A \to A/I$ be a lattice homomorphism, meaning that it preserves the join, meet, and absolute value operations. Condition (3) states that $I$ is a sublattice of $A$, while condition (2) expresses closure under absolute value.
