For suitable choice of $\sigma \in \beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$, the subspace $\Sigma = \mathbf{N} \cup \{\sigma\}$ of $\beta \mathbf{N}$ is the same as the space $\Sigma$ of 4M. Evidently, $\{\sigma\}$ is a zero-set in $\Sigma$.
