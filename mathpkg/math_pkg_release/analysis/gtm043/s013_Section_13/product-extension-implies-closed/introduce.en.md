If a topological space $T$ contains a product space $X = \prod_{\alpha} X_{\alpha}$, and each projection map $\pi_{\alpha}: X \to X_{\alpha}$ admits a continuous extension $\pi_{\alpha}^{1}: T \to X_{\alpha}$ to the whole space $T$, then $X$ must be a closed subset of $T$. The proof constructs a continuous map from $T$ to $X$ whose restriction to $X$ is the identity, which forces $X$ to be closed.
