Every nonempty, proper zero-set in $\beta \mathbf{R}^+ - \mathbf{R}^+$ is disconnected. [For $f \in C^*(\mathbf{R}^+)$, if $f^\beta$ assumes both the values $0$ and $1$ on $\beta \mathbf{R}^+ - \mathbf{R}^+$, then the set $\{x \in \mathbf{R}^+ : f(x) \leq \tfrac{1}{2}\}$ is a union of two disjoint closed sets whose closures in $\beta \mathbf{R}^+$ both meet $Z(f^\beta) - \mathbf{R}^+$.]
