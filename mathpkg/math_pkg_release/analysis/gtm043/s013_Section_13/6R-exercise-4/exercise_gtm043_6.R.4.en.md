Every nonempty open-and-closed set in $\beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$ is of the form $A'$. The sets $A'$ form a base for the closed sets in $\beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$, and a base for the open sets in $\beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$.
