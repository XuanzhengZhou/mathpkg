Let $\sigma$ denote the identity mapping of $X$ into $T$. A cluster point or limit of $\mathcal{F}$ in $T$ is precisely a cluster point or limit, respectively, of $\sigma \# \mathcal{F}$. Thus, to "fix" $\mathcal{F}$ is to ensure that $\sigma \# \mathcal{F}$ be a fixed $z$-filter on $T$.
