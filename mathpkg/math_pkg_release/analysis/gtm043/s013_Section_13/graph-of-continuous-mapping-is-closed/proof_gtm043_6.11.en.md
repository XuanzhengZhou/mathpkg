Each of the mappings $(a, b) \mapsto a$ and $(a, b) \mapsto \varphi a$ is continuous. By 3.10(c), the mapping $(a, b) \mapsto (a, \varphi a)$ onto the graph $X$ of $\varphi$ is continuous. Obviously, its restriction to $X$ is the identity.
