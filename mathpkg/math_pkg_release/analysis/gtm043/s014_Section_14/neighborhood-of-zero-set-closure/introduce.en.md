This theorem gives a purely algebraic condition characterizing when the closure of one zero-set in $\beta X$ is a neighborhood of the closure of another. The condition is expressed in terms of the existence of a third function $h$ whose cozero-set separates the two zero-sets. This result connects the topological notion of neighborhood in the Stone-Čech compactification with the algebraic structure of the ring $C(X)$.
