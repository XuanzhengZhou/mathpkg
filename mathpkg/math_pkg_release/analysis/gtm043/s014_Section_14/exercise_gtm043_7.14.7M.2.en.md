The closure of any subset $\mathfrak{T}$ of $\mathfrak{S}$ is the set
$$\{M \in \mathfrak{S} : M \supset \bigcap \mathfrak{T}\}.$$
Hence $\mathfrak{T}$ is dense if and only if $\bigcap \mathfrak{T} = \bigcap \mathfrak{S}$.
