The family $C_K(X)$ of all functions in $C(X)$ with compact support is characterized by
$$C_K(X) = \{f \in C^*(X) : Z_{\beta X}(f^\beta) \text{ is a neighborhood of } \beta X \setminus X\}.$$
Equivalently,
$$C_K(X) = \bigcap_{p \in \beta X \setminus X} O^p.$$
Consequently, $C_K$ is the intersection of all the free ideals in $C(X)$, and also the intersection of all the free ideals in $C^*(X)$.
