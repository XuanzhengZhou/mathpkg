This proposition states that for a prime ideal $P$ contained in $M^p$, the sign of a function's residue in $C/P$ respects the sign of the function on zero-sets from the $z$-filter $Z[O^p]$. If a function is nonnegative on such a zero-set, its image in the ordered residue field $C/P$ is nonnegative. The proof uses the identity $f - |f| \in O^p$.
