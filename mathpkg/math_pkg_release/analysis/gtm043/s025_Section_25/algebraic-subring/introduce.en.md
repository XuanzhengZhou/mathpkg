An algebraic subring of $C^*(X)$ is a subring closed under the extraction of square roots of its members and containing all constant functions. This closure property makes algebraic subrings particularly well-behaved under the metric topology: the uniform closure of an algebraic subring is an analytic subring. The smallest algebraic subring is $A_0$, consisting of all functions with finite range.
