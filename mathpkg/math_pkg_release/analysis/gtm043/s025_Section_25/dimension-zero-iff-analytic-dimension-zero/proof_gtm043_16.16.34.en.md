If $X$ is compact and totally disconnected, then by the Stone-Weierstrass theorem, $C(X)$ has no proper analytic subring. Conversely, if $X$ contains a connected subset $S$ of more than one point, the set of functions constant on $S$ is a proper analytic subring.
