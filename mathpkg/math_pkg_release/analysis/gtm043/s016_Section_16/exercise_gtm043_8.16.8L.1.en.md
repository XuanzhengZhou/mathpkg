Let $$E$$ be a subset of $$\Omega$$, disjoint from both the top edge and the right edge. If $$E$$ meets every neighborhood of $$w$$, then $$\operatorname{cl}_\Omega E$$ meets the diagonal in every neighborhood of $$w$$. [Apply the interlacing technique as in 5.12(b).]
