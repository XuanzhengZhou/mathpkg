The following conditions on a space $$Y$$ are equivalent.

(1) For each space $$X$$, if there exists a continuous mapping $$\tau: X \to Y$$ such that $$\tau^{\leftarrow}(y)$$ is compact for each $$y \in Y$$, then $$X$$ is realcompact.

(2) Every space of which $$Y$$ is a one-one, continuous image is realcompact.

(3) Every subspace of $$Y$$ is realcompact.

(4) For each point $$y$$, $$Y - \{y\}$$ is realcompact.
