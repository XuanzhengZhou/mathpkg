Recall that $X$ contains a $C$-embedded copy of $\mathbf{N}$ if and only if it is not pseudocompact (Corollary 1.21). If $\mathbf{N}$ is $C$-embedded in $X$ and $U$ is any neighborhood of $\mathbf{N}$, then $\operatorname{cl}_{\beta X}(X - U)$ is disjoint from $\operatorname{cl}_{\beta X}\mathbf{N}$.
