The ideal $C_K(\mathbf{N})$ of finitely supported functions on $\mathbf{N}$ is the intersection of all free ideals in $C(\mathbf{N})$ and in $C^*(\mathbf{N})$. That is, if $\mathcal{F}$ denotes the collection of all free ideals in $C(\mathbf{N})$ (respectively $C^*(\mathbf{N})$), then
$$C_K(\mathbf{N}) = \bigcap_{I \in \mathcal{F}} I.$$
