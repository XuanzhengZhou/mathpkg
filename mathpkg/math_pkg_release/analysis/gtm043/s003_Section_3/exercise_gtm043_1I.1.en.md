Let $t$ be a (ring) homomorphism from $C(Y)$ or $C^*(Y)$ into $C(X)$. Then $tr = r \cdot t1$ for each $r \in \mathbf{R}$. [For each $x \in X$, the mapping $r \mapsto (tr)(x)$ is a homomorphism from $\mathbf{R}$ into $\mathbf{R}$, and hence is either the zero homomorphism or the identity (0.22). Also, $(t1)(x) = 0$ or $1$.]
