The following are equivalent:
(1) For every $f \in C$, there exists a unit $u$ of $C$ such that $f = u|f|$.
(2) For every $g \in C^*$, there exists a unit $v$ of $C^*$ such that $g = v|g|$.

[(1) implies (2): $g = u|g|$ for some unit $u$ of $C$; consider $\operatorname{pos} u$ and $\operatorname{neg} u$.]
