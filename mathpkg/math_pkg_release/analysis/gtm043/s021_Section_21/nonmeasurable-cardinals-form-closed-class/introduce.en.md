This theorem establishes that the class of nonmeasurable cardinals is closed under all cardinal arithmetic operations. Combined with $\aleph_0$ being nonmeasurable, this shows that $\aleph_1, \aleph_2, \dots, \aleph_\omega$ and many more are nonmeasurable. The proof shows $2^{\mathfrak{m}}$ is nonmeasurable by analyzing measures on the power set.
