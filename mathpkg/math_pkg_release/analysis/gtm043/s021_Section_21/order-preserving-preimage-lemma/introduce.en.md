This lemma shows that any countable family in the quotient by an absolutely convex ideal lifts to order-preserving preimages. This applies to prime ideals in $C(X)$ (always absolutely convex by Theorem 5.5). The proof inductively adjusts preimages using lattice operations while preserving residue classes.
