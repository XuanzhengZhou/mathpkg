This theorem establishes that any hyper-real residue class field has transcendence degree over $\mathbb{R}$ of at least $\mathfrak{c}$, hence cardinality at least $\mathfrak{c}$. The proof constructs $\mathfrak{c}$ algebraically independent elements using an infinitely large element raised to $\mathbb{Q}$-linearly independent real exponents.
