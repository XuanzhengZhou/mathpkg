This theorem establishes the key order-theoretic property of hyper-real residue class fields: they are $\eta_1$-sets, hence $\eta_1$-fields (since they are real-closed by Theorem 13.4). The proof uses the interpolation lemma with $P = M$ and handles the strict inequality case using the fact that ordered fields have no consecutive elements.
