title: "Nonmeasurable sums of spaces"
---
If $X$ is the topological sum of spaces $X_\alpha$, where $\alpha$ ranges over an index set $A$ of nonmeasurable cardinal, then $vX$ is the topological sum of the spaces $vX_\alpha$.

[Let $\varphi$ denote the obvious mapping of $X$ onto $A$. Then $\varphi^\circ : vX \to A$ satisfies $\varphi^\circ \leftarrow (\alpha) = \text{cl}_{vX} X_\alpha = vX_\alpha$.]
