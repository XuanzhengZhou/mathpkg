Since $\sigma$ maps $X$ homeomorphically onto $\sigma[X]$, there is an induced isomorphism of $C(\sigma[X])$ onto $C(X)$. Composing this with the restriction map from $C(\operatorname{cl} \sigma[X])$ to $C(\sigma[X])$ (which is an isomorphism because $\sigma[X]$ is dense and $C$-embedded in $\operatorname{cl} \sigma[X]$), one obtains the isomorphism $f \mapsto \pi_f|_{\operatorname{cl} \sigma[X]}$.
