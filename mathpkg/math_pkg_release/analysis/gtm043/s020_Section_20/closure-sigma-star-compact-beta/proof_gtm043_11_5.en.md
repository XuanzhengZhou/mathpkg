By Tychonoff's theorem (4.14), the subspace

$$\prod_{f \in C^*(X)} \operatorname{cl}_{\mathbf{R}} f[X]$$

of $P_*$ is compact; and it evidently contains $\sigma_*[X]$. Therefore $\operatorname{cl} \sigma_*[X]$ is a compactification in which $\sigma_*[X]$ is $C^*$-embedded. Since $\sigma_*[X]$ is homeomorphic with $X$, $\beta(\sigma_*[X])$ is a model for $\beta X$.
