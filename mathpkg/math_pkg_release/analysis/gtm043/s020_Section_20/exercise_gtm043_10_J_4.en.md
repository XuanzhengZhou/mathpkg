Let $\mathcal{U}$ be the ultrafilter on $X$ that converges to a point $q$ in $\beta D$ (where $D$ is the discrete space with the points of $X$). Then $\mathcal{U}$ converges to $p$ in $\beta X$ if and only if $\delta q = p$, and if and only if $\Delta\mathcal{U} = A^p$.
