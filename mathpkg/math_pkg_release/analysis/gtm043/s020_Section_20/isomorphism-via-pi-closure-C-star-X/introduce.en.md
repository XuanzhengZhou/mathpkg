The bounded-function analogue of 11.4(a). The isomorphism identifies each $f \in C^*(X)$ with the restriction of the corresponding projection $\pi_{*f}$ to the closure of $\sigma_*[X]$. This is the $C^*$ counterpart to the $C$-embedding isomorphism.
