Let $S \subset H$. In order that the functions $\pi_f|S$ be distinct for distinct $f \in C(X)$, and constitute all of $C(S)$, it is necessary and sufficient that $S$ be dense and $C$-embedded in $H$. The analogue holds for $H_*$.
