Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a free ultrafilter on $\mathbf{N}$. There exist unique $p, q \in \beta \mathbf{R}^+$, satisfying

$$p \in \bigcap_{U \in \mathcal{U}} \operatorname{cl}_{\beta \mathbf{R}^+} \bigcup_{n \in U} [r_n, t_n], \qquad q \in \bigcap_{U \in \mathcal{U}} \operatorname{cl}_{\beta \mathbf{R}^+} \bigcup_{n \in U} [t_n, r_{n+1}],$$

where $(r_n)$ and $(t_n)$ are as in 1.
