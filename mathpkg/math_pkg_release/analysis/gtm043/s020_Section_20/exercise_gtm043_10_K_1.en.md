A necessary and sufficient condition that $A^p$ be contained in every ultrafilter on $X$ that converges to $p$ is that $M^p = O^p$. [See 10J.4. Necessity. If $Z \in A^p - Z[O^p]$, then $p \in \operatorname{cl}_{\beta X}(X - Z)$.] Thus, this holds for a point $p$ of $X$ if and only if $p$ is a $P$-point (4L); and it holds for every point $p$ of $\beta X$ if and only if $X$ is a $P$-space.
