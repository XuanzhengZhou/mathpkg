A subbase for the closed sets is the dual of the more familiar notion of a subbase for open sets. A family of closed sets is a subbase if closing it under finite unions yields a base for the closed sets. In the context of weak topologies induced by families of functions, subbases for closed sets arise naturally as preimages of closed rays in $\mathbf{R}$.
