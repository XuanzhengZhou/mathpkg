The fixed maximal ideals in \(C(X)\) are precisely the sets

\[
M_p = \{ f \in C(X) : f(p) = 0 \} \quad (p \in X).
\]

The ideals \(M_p\) are distinct for distinct \(p\). For each \(p\), \(C(X)/M_p\) is isomorphic with the real field \(\mathbf{R}\); in fact, the mapping \(M_p(f) \mapsto f(p)\) is the unique isomorphism of \(C(X)/M_p\) onto \(\mathbf{R}\).
