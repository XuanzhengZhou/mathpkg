The Stone topology on the set \(\mathfrak{M}(X)\) of all maximal ideals of \(C(X)\) is a hull-kernel-type topology where basic closed sets are those maximal ideals containing a given function \(f \in C(X)\). For compact \(X\), the map \(p \mapsto M_p\) is a homeomorphism between \(X\) and \(\mathfrak{M}(X)\), showing that the space \(X\) can be recovered purely algebraically from the ring \(C(X)\).
