The support of \(f\) is, by definition, the closure of \(X - Z(f)\). Let \(C_K(X)\) denote the family of all functions in \(C\) having compact support.

If \(X\) is compact, then \(C_K = C\); otherwise, \(C_K\) is both an ideal in \(C\) and an ideal in \(C^*\).
