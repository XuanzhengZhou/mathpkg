The fixed maximal ideals in \(C^*(X)\) are precisely the sets

\[
M^*_p = \{ f \in C^*(X) : f(p) = 0 \} \quad (p \in X).
\]

The ideals \(M^*_p\) are distinct for distinct \(p\). For each \(p\), \(C^*(X)/M^*_p\) is isomorphic with the real field \(\mathbf{R}\); in fact, the mapping \(M^*_p(f) \mapsto f(p)\) is the unique isomorphism of \(C^*(X)/M^*_p\) onto \(\mathbf{R}\).
