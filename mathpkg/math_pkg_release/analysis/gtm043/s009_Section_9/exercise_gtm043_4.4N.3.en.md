Define \(\overline{I} = \bigcap \{M_p : M_p \supset I\}\). Then \(\overline{I}\) is a closed ideal in the uniform norm topology on \(C(X)\) [2M.4], and consists of all \(g\) for which \(Z(g) \supset \bigcap Z[I]\).

Furthermore, \(\bigcap Z[\overline{I}] = \bigcap Z[I]\).
