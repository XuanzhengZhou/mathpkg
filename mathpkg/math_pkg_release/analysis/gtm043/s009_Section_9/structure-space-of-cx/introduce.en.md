The structure space of \(C(X)\) is the set \(\mathfrak{M}(X)\) of all maximal ideals equipped with the Stone (hull-kernel) topology. It generalizes the notion of the maximal ideal space of a commutative ring. For compact \(X\), the structure space recovers \(X\) up to homeomorphism, demonstrating that the topology of \(X\) is encoded algebraically in the ideal structure of \(C(X)\).
