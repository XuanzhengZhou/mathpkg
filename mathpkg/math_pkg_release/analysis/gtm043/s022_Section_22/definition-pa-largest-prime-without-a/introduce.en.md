In Section 14.3, the authors introduce the canonical notation $P_a$ and $P^a$ for elements of the prime ideal chain in $C/P$. For each positive $a$, $P_a$ is the largest prime ideal not containing $a$, while $P^a$ is its immediate successor (which must contain $a$). This provides a complete characterization: every pair of successive prime ideals is of the form $(P_a, P^a)$ for some positive $a$. This correspondence is fundamental for all subsequent results about the chain structure.
