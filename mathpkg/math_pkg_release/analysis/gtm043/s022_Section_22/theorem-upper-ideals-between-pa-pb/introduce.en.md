When a maximal ideal contains nonmaximal prime ideals, it contains at least $2^{\aleph_1}$ of them. The upper ideals between consecutive prime pairs form an $\eta_1$-set, and by general theory, any $\eta_1$-set has cardinal at least $2^{\aleph_1}$.
