The proof of Theorem 13.13 can be modified, like that of Theorem 13.9 on similarity of $\eta_1$-sets, to show that a real-closed $\eta_1$-field contains a copy of any real-closed field of cardinal $\leq \aleph_1$. By the Artin-Schreier theorem, every ordered field $E$ can be embedded in its real-closure $\mathcal{R}E$, with $|E| = |\mathcal{R}E|$. Hence the embedding of $\mathcal{R}E$ into the $\eta_1$-field restricts to an embedding of $E$ itself. The embedding of $\mathbf{R}$ is addressed in Problem 13M.
