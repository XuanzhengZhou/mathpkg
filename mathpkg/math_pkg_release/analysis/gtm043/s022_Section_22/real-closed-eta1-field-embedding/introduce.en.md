Theorem 13.15 establishes a powerful embedding property of real-closed $\eta_1$-fields: they are universal for all real-closed fields (and, more generally, ordered fields) of cardinality at most $\aleph_1$. This follows from the Artin-Schreier theorem and the similarity properties of $\eta_1$-sets. The result generalizes the idea that an $\eta_1$-set contains a copy of every ordered set of cardinal $\leq \aleph_1$, extended here to the field structure.
