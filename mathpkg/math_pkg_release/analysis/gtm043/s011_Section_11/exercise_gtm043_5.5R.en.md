The norm $\|f\| = \sup_{x \in X} |f(x)|$ on $C^*(X)$ satisfies

$$\|f\| = \sup_M |M(f)|,$$

where $M$ ranges over all the maximal ideals in $C^*$. [$|f| \leq r$ implies $|M(f)| \leq r$. Alternatively, use 2C.2.] Compare 1J.6.
