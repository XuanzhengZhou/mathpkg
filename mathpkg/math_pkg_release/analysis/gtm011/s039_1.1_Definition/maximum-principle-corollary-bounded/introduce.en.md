This corollary applies the Second Maximum Principle to bounded regions: if a continuous function satisfies the MVP and vanishes on the boundary of a bounded region, it must vanish identically in the interior. This is a useful uniqueness result that follows from applying Theorem 1.7 twice — once to show $w \leq 0$ and once to show $w \geq 0$.
