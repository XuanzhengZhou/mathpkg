Harmonic conjugates are pairs of real-valued functions that arise as the real and imaginary parts of an analytic function. If $f = u + iv$ is analytic on a region $G$, then $u$ and $v$ are harmonic conjugates of each other. Every harmonic function on a simply connected region possesses a harmonic conjugate, which means it can be realized as the real part of some analytic function on that region.
