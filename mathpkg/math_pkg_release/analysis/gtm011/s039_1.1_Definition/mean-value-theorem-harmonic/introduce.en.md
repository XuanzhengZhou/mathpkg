The Mean Value Theorem for harmonic functions states that the value of a harmonic function at the center of any closed disk equals the average of its values over the boundary circle. This is the direct analogue of Cauchy's Integral Formula for analytic functions. The result follows by expressing the harmonic function locally as the real part of an analytic function and applying the Cauchy Integral Formula.
