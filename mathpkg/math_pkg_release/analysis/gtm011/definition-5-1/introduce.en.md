5.1 Definition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $G$ be a region in the plane and let $a \in G$. A Green’s Function of $G$ with singularity at $a$ is a function $g_a: G - \{a\} \to \mathbb{R}$ with the properties:

(a) $g_a$ is harmonic in $G - \{a\}$;
