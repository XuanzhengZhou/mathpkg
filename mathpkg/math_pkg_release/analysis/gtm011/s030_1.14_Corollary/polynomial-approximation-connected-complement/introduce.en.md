Corollary 1.15 is a special case of Corollary 1.14 obtained by taking $E = \{\infty\}$. It states that if $G$ is an open subset of the complex plane whose complement in the extended plane $\mathbb{C}_{\infty}$ is connected, then every analytic function on $G$ can be approximated uniformly on compact sets by polynomials. This result applies, for example, to simply connected domains and provides a converse to the fact that uniform limits of analytic functions are analytic.
