Corollary 5.9 states that the sheaf of germs $\mathcal{S}(G)$ over a region $G$ is locally arcwise connected, and its connected components are open and arcwise connected. This structural result is fundamental for understanding the topology of the sheaf space and for studying analytic continuation along curves.
