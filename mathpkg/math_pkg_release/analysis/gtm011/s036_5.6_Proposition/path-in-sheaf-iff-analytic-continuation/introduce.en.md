Theorem 5.10 establishes the fundamental equivalence between paths in the sheaf of germs $\mathcal{S}(G)$ and analytic continuation along curves in $G$. A point $(a, [f]_a)$ can be connected to $(b, [g]_b)$ by a continuous path in the sheaf space if and only if $[g]_b$ is obtained from $[f]_a$ by analytic continuation along some path $\gamma$ in the base region $G$.
