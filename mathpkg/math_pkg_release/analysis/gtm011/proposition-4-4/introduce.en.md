4.4 Proposition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
A set $K \subset X$ is compact iff every collection $\mathcal{F}$ of closed subsets of $K$ with the f.i.p. has $\bigcap \{F: F \in \mathcal{F}\} \neq \square$....
