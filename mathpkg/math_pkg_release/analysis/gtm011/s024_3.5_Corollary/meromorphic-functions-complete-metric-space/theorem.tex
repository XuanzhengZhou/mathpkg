Let $G$ be an open subset of the complex plane, let $M(G)$ denote the set of meromorphic functions on $G$, and let $C(G, \mathbb{C}_\infty)$ denote the continuous functions from $G$ to the extended complex plane $\mathbb{C}_\infty$ equipped with the chordal metric $d$. Then
$$M(G) \cup \{\infty\} \text{ is a complete metric space.}$$
