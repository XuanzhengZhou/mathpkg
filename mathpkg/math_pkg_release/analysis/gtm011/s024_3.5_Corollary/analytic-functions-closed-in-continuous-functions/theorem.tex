Let $G$ be an open subset of the complex plane, let $H(G)$ denote the set of analytic functions on $G$, and let $C(G, \mathbb{C}_\infty)$ denote the space of continuous functions from $G$ to the extended complex plane equipped with the chordal metric. Then
$$H(G) \cup \{\infty\} \text{ is closed in } C(G, \mathbb{C}_\infty).$$
