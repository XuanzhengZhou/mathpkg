Let $\Gamma_1$ and $\Gamma_2$ be two circles in $\mathbb{C}_{\infty}$ and let $T$ be a Möbius transformation such that $T(\Gamma_1) = \Gamma_2$. Let $(z_1, z_2, z_3)$ be an orientation for $\Gamma_1$. Then $T$ takes the right side and the left side of $\Gamma_1$ onto the right side and left side of $\Gamma_2$ with respect to the orientation $(Tz_1, Tz_2, Tz_3)$.
