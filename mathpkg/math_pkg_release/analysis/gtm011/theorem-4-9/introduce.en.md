4.9 Theorem from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $G$ be a region in $\mathbb{C}$ and suppose that $a \in \partial_\infty G$ such that the component of $\mathbb{C}_\infty - G$ which contains a does not reduce to a point. Then $G$ has a barrier at $a$....
