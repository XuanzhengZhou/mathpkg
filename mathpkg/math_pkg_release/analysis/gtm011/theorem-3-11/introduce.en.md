3.11 Theorem from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $G$ be a region and $f: \partial_\infty G \to \mathbb{R}$ a continuous function; then $u(z) = \sup \{ \varphi(z): \varphi \in \mathcal{P}(f, G) \}$ defines a harmonic function $u$ on $G$....
