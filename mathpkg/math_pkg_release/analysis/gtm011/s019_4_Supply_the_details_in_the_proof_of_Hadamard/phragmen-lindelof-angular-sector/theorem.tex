Let $a \geq \frac{1}{2}$ and put
\[
G = \left\{ z : |\arg z| < \frac{\pi}{2a} \right\}.
\]
Suppose $f$ is analytic on $G$ and there is a constant $M$ such that
\[
\limsup_{z \to w} |f(z)| \leq M \quad \text{for all } w \in \partial G.
\]
If there exist positive constants $P$ and $b$ with $0 < b < a$ such that
\[
|f(z)| \leq P \exp(|z|^b)
\]
for all $z \in G$, then $|f(z)| \leq M$ for all $z \in G$.
