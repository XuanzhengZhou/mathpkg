Let $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ be analytic and suppose $M$ is a constant such that $\limsup |f(z_n)| \leq M$ for each sequence $\{z_n\}$ in $G$ which converges to a point in $\partial_\infty G$. Show that $|f(z)| \leq M$. (See Exercise 1.8.)
