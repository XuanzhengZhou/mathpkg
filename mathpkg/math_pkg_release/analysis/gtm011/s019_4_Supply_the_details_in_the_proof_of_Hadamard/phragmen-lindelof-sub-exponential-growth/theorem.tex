Let $a \geq \frac{1}{2}$,
\[
G = \left\{ z : |\arg z| < \frac{\pi}{2a} \right\},
\]
and suppose that for every $w \in \partial G$, $\limsup_{z \to w} |f(z)| \leq M$. Moreover, assume that for every $\delta > 0$ there is a constant $P$ (which may depend on $\delta$) such that
\[
|f(z)| \leq P \exp(\delta |z|^a)
\]
for $z \in G$ and $|z|$ sufficiently large. Then $|f(z)| \leq M$ for all $z \in G$.
