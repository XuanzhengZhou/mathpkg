Find the radius of convergence for each of the following power series:
(a) $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a^n z^n$, $a \in \mathbb{C}$;
(b) $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a^{n^2} z^n$, $a \in \mathbb{C}$;
(c) $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} k^n z^n$, $k$ an integer $\neq 0$;
(d) $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_n z^n$ where $a_{2n} = a^n$ and $a_{2n+1} = b^n$ with $a, b \in \mathbb{C}$.
