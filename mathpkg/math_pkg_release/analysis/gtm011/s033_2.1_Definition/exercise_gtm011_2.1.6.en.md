Let $D_0 = B(1; 1)$ and let $f_0$ be the restriction to $D_0$ of the principal branch of the logarithm. For an integer $n$ let $\gamma(t) = \exp(2\pi i n t)$, $0 \leq t \leq 1$. Find a continuation $\{(f_t, D_t): 0 \leq t \leq 1\}$ along $\gamma$ of $(f_0, D_0)$ and show that $[f_1]_1 = [f_n]_1$ where $f_n(z) = \log z + 2\pi i n$.
