Suppose $\gamma: [0, 1] \to \mathbb{C}$ is a closed path with $\gamma(0) = \gamma(1) = a$ and let $\{(f_t, D_t): 0 \leq t \leq 1\}$ be an analytic continuation along $\gamma$ such that $[f_1]_a = [f_0]_a$ and $f_0 \neq 0$. What can be said about $(f_0, D_0)$?
