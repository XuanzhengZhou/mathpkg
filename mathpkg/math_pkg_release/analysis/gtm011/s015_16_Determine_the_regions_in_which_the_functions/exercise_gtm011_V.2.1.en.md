Calculate the following integrals:

(a) $\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^2 \, dx}{x^4 + x^2 + 1}$

(b) $\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{\cos x - 1}{x^2} \, dx$

(c) $\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\pi} \frac{\cos 2\theta \, d\theta}{1 - 2a \cos \theta + a^2}$ where $a^2 < 1$

(d) $\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\pi} \frac{d\theta}{(a + \cos \theta)^2}$ where $a > 1$
