7.17 Proposition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $G = \{z: \text{Re } z > 0\}$ and

$$f_n(z) = \int_{1/n}^{n} e^{-t} t^{z-1} dt$$

for $n \geq 1$ and $z$ in $G$, then each $f_n$ is analytic on $G$ and the sequence is convergent in $H(G)$....
