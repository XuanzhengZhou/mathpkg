Let $f$ be a branch of log on $G$ and $g(z) = \exp(z)$. Then $g(f(z)) = z$ and $g$ is analytic with $g(w) = \exp(w) \neq 0$ for all $w$. By the Inverse Function Theorem (Proposition 2.20), $f$ is analytic and $f(z) = [g(f(z))]^{-1} = [\exp(f(z))]^{-1} = z^{-1}.$
