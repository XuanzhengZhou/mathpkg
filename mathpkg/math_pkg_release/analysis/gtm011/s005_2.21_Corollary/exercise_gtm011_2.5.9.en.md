Suppose that $z_n, z \in G = \mathbb{C} \setminus \{z : z \leq 0\}$ and $z_n = r_n e^{i\theta_n}$, $z = r e^{i\theta}$ where $-\pi < \theta, \theta_n < \pi$. Prove that if $z_n \to z$ then $\theta_n \to \theta$ and $r_n \to r$.
