Show that if the series

\[
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[ \frac{b_n}{z - a_n} + \frac{b_n}{a_n} \sum_{j=0}^{k_n-1} \left( \frac{z}{a_n} \right)^j \right] \tag{3.5}
\]

converges in \(M(\mathbb{C})\) to a meromorphic function \(f\) then

\[
f(z) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[ \frac{b_n}{z-a_n} + \frac{b_n}{a_n} \left\{ 1 + \left( \frac{z}{a_n} \right) + \ldots + \left( \frac{z}{a_n} \right)^{k_n-1} \right\} \right].
\]
