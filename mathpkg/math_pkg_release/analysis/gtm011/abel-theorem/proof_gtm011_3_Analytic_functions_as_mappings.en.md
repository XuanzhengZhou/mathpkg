Given any two paths $\gamma_1$ and $\gamma_2$ through $z_0$, $f$ maps these paths onto two paths through $\omega_0 = f(z_0)$ and, when $f'(z_0) \neq 0$, the angles between the curves are preserved both in magnitude and direction.
