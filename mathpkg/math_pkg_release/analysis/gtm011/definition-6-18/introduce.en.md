6.18 Definition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $G$ is an open set then $\gamma$ is homologous to zero, in symbols $\gamma \approx 0$, if $n(\gamma; w) = 0$ for all $w$ in $\mathbb{C} - G$.
