Let $f$ be analytic in a region containing $\bar{B}(0; r)$ and let $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ be the zeros of $f$ in $B(0; r)$ repeated according to multiplicity. If $|z| < r$ and $z \neq a_k$ for $1 \leq k \leq n$, then
$$
\log |f(z)| = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \log \left| \frac{r(z-a_k)}{r^2 - \bar{a}_k z} \right| + \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} \frac{r^2 - |z|^2}{|re^{i\theta} - z|^2} \log |f(re^{i\theta})| \, d\theta.
$$
