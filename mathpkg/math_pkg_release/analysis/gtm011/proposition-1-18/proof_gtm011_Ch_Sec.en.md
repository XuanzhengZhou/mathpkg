Proof. The proof that $d$ is a metric is left to the reader. Suppose $d(\xi^k, \xi) \rightarrow 0$; since

$$\frac{d_n(x_n^k, x_n)}{1 + d_n(x_n^k, x_n)} \leq 2^n d(\xi^k, \xi)$$

we have that

$$\lim_{k \rightarrow \infty} \frac{d_n(x_n^k, x_n)}{1 + d_n(x_n^k, x_n)} = 0.$$

This gives that $x_n^k \rightarrow x_n$ for each $n \geq 1$. The proof of the converse is left to the reader.
