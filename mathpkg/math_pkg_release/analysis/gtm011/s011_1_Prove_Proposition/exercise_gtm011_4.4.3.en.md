Let $p(z)$ be a polynomial of degree $n$ and let $R > 0$ be sufficiently large so that $p$ never vanishes in $\{z : |z| \geq R\}$. If $\gamma(t) = Re^{it}$, $0 \leq t \leq 2\pi$, show that
$$
\int_{\gamma} \frac{p'(z)}{p(z)} \, dz = 2\pi i n.
$$
