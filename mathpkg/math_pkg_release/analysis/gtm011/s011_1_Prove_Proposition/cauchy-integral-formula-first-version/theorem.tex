Let $G$ be an open subset of the plane and $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ an analytic function. If $\gamma$ is a closed rectifiable curve in $G$ such that $n(\gamma; w) = 0$ for all $w \in \mathbb{C} - G$, then for $a \in G - \{\gamma\}$,
$$
n(\gamma; a) f(a) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{f(z)}{z - a} \, dz.
$$
