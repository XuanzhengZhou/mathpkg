This corollary specializes the derivative form of Cauchy's Integral Formula to the case of a single closed rectifiable curve $\gamma$ whose winding number vanishes outside $G$. It gives a direct formula for the $k$-th derivative $f^{(k)}(a)$ weighted by the winding number $n(\gamma; a)$. The result follows immediately from the more general theorem by setting $m = 1$ and $\gamma_1 = \gamma$.
