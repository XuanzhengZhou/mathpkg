Fix $w = re^{i\theta} \neq 0$ and let $\gamma$ be a closed rectifiable curve. Use the factorization
$$
\frac{1}{(w-z)^m} - \frac{1}{(w-a)^m} = \left( \frac{1}{w-z} - \frac{1}{w-a} \right) \sum_{k=1}^{m} \frac{1}{(w-z)^{m-k}} \frac{1}{(w-a)^{k-1}}
$$
to investigate the difference of Cauchy-type integrals.
