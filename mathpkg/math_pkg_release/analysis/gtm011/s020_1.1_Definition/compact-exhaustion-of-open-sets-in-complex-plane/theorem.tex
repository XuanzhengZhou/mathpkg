If $G$ is open in $\mathbb{C}$, then there exists a sequence $\{K_n\}$ of compact subsets of $G$ such that:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $K_n \subset \operatorname{int} K_{n+1}$ for all $n$;
\item[(b)] $G = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} K_n = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} \operatorname{int} K_n$;
\item[(c)] every component of $\mathbb{C}_{\infty} - K_n$ contains a component of $\mathbb{C}_{\infty} - G$.
\end{enumerate}
