Let $G = \left\{ z : -\frac{\pi}{2} < \operatorname{Re} z < \frac{\pi}{2} \right\}$ and define $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ by $f(z) = \sin z$. Give a discussion for $f$ similar to the discussion of $\sqrt{z}$ at the end of this section. In particular, analyze the Riemann surface of local inverses for $\sin z$ on this strip.
