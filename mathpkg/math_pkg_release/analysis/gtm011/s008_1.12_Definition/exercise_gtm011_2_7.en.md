Use the results of this section to evaluate the following integrals:

(a) $\int_{\gamma} \frac{e^{iz}}{z^2} dz$ where $\gamma(t) = e^{it}$, $0 \leq t \leq 2\pi$;

(b) $\int_{\gamma} \frac{dz}{z - \frac{1}{2}}$ where $\gamma(t) = \frac{1}{2} + e^{it}$, $0 \leq t \leq 2\pi$.
