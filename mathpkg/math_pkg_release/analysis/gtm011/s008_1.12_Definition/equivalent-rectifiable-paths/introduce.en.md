Two rectifiable paths $\sigma$ and $\gamma$ are equivalent if they differ by a continuous, strictly increasing, surjective reparametrization $\varphi$ of the parameter interval. This equivalence relation is designed so that equivalent paths have the same trace and yield identical line integrals for any continuous function on that trace. The notion allows one to treat curves as equivalence classes of paths, abstracting away from the particular parametrization.
