This lemma states that if a sequence of continuous functions converges uniformly on the trace of a rectifiable curve, then the line integral of the limit equals the limit of the line integrals. The proof follows directly from the estimate in Proposition 1.17(b): the absolute difference of the integrals is bounded by $V(\gamma)$ times the supremum of $|F_n - F|$ on the trace. This result is essential for integrating power series term-by-term along contours.
