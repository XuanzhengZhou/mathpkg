6.1 Definition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $X$ be a topological space; a coordinate patch on $X$ is a pair $(U, \varphi)$ where $U$ is an open subset of $X$ and $\varphi$ is a homeomorphism of $U$ onto an open subset of the plane. If $a \in U$ then the coordinate patch $(U, \varphi)$ is said to contain $a$....
