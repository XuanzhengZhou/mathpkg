2.8 Theorem from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $f$ be analytic in $B(a; R)$; then $f(z) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_n(z-a)^n$ for $|z-a| < R$ where $a_n = \frac{1}{n!} f^{(n)}(a)$ and this series has radius of convergence $\geq R$....
