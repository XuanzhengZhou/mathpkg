3.6 Corollary from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
$H(G) \cup \{\infty\}$ is closed in $C(G, \mathbb{C}_\infty)$.

To discuss normality in $M(G)$ one must introduce the quantity

$$\frac{2|f'(z)|}{1 + |f(z)|^2},$$

for each meromorphic function $f$. However if $z$ is a pole of $f$ then the above expression is meaningless since $f'(z)$ has no meaning...
