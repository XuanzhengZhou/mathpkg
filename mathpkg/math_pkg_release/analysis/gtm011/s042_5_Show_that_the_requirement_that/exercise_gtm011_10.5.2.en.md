Let $g_a$ be the Green's Function on a region $G$ with singularity at $z = a$. Prove that if $\psi$ is a positive superharmonic function on $G - \{a\}$ with $\liminf_{z \to a} [\psi(z) + \log|z - a|] > -\infty$, then $g_a(z) \leq \psi(z)$ for $z \neq a$.
