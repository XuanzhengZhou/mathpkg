Let $G$ be a region in the plane and let $a \in G$. A \textbf{Green's Function} of $G$ with singularity at $a$ is a function $g_a: G - \{a\} \to \mathbb{R}$ with the properties:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $g_a$ is harmonic in $G - \{a\}$;
\item[(b)] $G(z) = g_a(z) + \log |z-a|$ is harmonic in a disk about $a$;
\item[(c)] $\displaystyle\lim_{z \to w} g_a(z) = 0$ for each $w$ in $\partial_\infty G$.
\end{enumerate}
