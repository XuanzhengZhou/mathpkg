Let $G$ be a region and $a$ a point in $\partial_\infty G$ such that there is a harmonic function $u: G \to \mathbb{R}$ with $\lim_{z \to a} u(z) = 0$ and $\liminf_{z \to w} u(z) > 0$ for all $w$ in $\partial_\infty G$, $w \neq a$. Show that there is a barrier for $G$ at $a$.
