1.4 Proposition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $G$ is a region with $a$ in $G$ and if $f$ is analytic on $G - \{a\}$ with a pole at $z = a$ then there is a positive integer $m$ and an analytic function $g: G \to \mathbb{C}$ such that

$$f(z) = \frac{g(z)}{(z-a)^{m}}.$$...
