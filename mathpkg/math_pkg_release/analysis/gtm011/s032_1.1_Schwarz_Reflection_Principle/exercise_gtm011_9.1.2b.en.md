Let $G$ and $G^*$ be as defined in Exercise 2(a). If $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ is analytic, define
$$f^{\#}(z) = \overline{f(1/\bar{z})}$$
for $z$ in $G^*$. Show that $f^{\#}$ is analytic on $G^*$.
