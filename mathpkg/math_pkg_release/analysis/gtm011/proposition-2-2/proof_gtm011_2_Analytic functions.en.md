Proof. In fact,

$$\lim_{z \to a} |f(z) - f(a)| = \left[ \lim_{z \to a} \frac{|f(z) - f(a)|}{|z - a|} \right] \cdot \left[ \lim_{z \to a} |z - a| \right]$$

$$= |f'(a)| \cdot 0 = 0.$$
