A Mobius transformation is a linear fractional transformation $S(z) = \frac{az+b}{cz+d}$ whose coefficients satisfy the non-degeneracy condition $ad - bc \neq 0$. This condition ensures that $S$ is non-constant and invertible, making it a biholomorphic automorphism of the extended complex plane $\mathbb{C}_\infty$. The set of all Mobius transformations forms a group under composition, isomorphic to the projective general linear group $\mathrm{PGL}(2, \mathbb{C})$.
