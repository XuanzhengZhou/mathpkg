The collection of all Mobius transformations forms a group under the operation of composition. This follows because the composition of two linear fractional transformations is again a linear fractional transformation, and each Mobius transformation possesses an explicit inverse that is also a Mobius transformation. The group is isomorphic to the projective general linear group $\mathrm{PGL}(2, \mathbb{C})$.
