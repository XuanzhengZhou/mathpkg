\textbf{Runge's Theorem 1.7.} Let $K$ be a compact subset of $\mathbb{C}$ and let $E$ be a subset of $\mathbb{C}_\infty - K$ that meets each component of $\mathbb{C}_\infty - K$. If $f$ is analytic in an open set containing $K$ and $\varepsilon > 0$, then there exists a rational function $R(z)$ whose only poles lie in $E$ and such that
$$|f(z) - R(z)| < \varepsilon$$
for all $z$ in $K$.
