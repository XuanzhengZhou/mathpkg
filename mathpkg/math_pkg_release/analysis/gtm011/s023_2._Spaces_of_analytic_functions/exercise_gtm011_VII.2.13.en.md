(a) Show that if $f$ is analytic on an open set containing the disk $\overline{B}(a; R)$, then

$$|f(a)|^2 \leq \frac{1}{\pi R^2} \int_0^{2\pi} \int_0^R |f(a + re^{i\theta})|^2 \, r \, dr \, d\theta.$$

(b) Let $G$ be a region and let $M$ be a fixed positive constant. Let $\mathcal{F}$ be the family of all functions $f \in H(G)$ such that

$$\iint_G |f(z)|^2 \, dx \, dy \leq M.$$

Show that $\mathcal{F}$ is normal.
