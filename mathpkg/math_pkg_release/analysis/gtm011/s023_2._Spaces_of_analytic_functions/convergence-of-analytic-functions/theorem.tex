Let $G$ be an open subset of the complex plane and let $H(G)$ denote the collection of analytic functions on $G$. If $\{f_n\}$ is a sequence in $H(G)$ and $f$ belongs to $C(G, \mathbb{C})$ such that $f_n \to f$ in $C(G, \mathbb{C})$ (i.e., uniformly on compact subsets of $G$), then $f$ is analytic and

$$f_n^{(k)} \to f^{(k)}$$

for each integer $k \geq 1$, where the convergence is also uniform on compact subsets of $G$.
