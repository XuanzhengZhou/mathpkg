Let $f$ be analytic on $\bar{B}(0; R)$ with $|f(z)| \leq M$ for $|z| \leq R$ and $|f(0)| = a > 0$. Show that the number of zeros of $f$ in $\bar{B}(0; \frac{1}{3}R)$ is less than or equal to $\frac{1}{\log 2} \log\left(\frac{M}{a}\right)$.

Hint: If $z_1, \ldots, z_n$ are the zeros of $f$ in $\bar{B}(0; \frac{1}{3}R)$, consider the function

$$g(z) = f(z) \left[ \prod_{k=1}^{n} \left(1 - \frac{z}{z_k}\right)^{-1} \right]$$

and apply the Maximum Modulus Theorem.
