Let $f$ be an entire function and let $\{a_n\}$ be the non-zero zeros of $f$ repeated according to multiplicity; suppose $f$ has a zero at $z = 0$ of order $m \geq 0$ (where $m = 0$ means $f(0) \neq 0$). Then there is an entire function $g$ and a sequence of integers $\{p_n\}$ such that

$$f(z) = z^m e^{g(z)} \prod_{n=1}^{\infty} E_{p_n}\left(\frac{z}{a_n}\right).$$
