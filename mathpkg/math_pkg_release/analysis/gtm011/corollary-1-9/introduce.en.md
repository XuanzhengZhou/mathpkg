1.9 Corollary from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $G$ be a bounded region and suppose that $w: G^- \to \mathbb{R}$ is a continuous function that satisfies the MVP on $G$. If $w(z) = 0$ for all $z$ in $\partial G$ then $w(z) = 0$ for all $z$ in $G$....
