Let $(X, \mathcal{T})$ and $(\Omega, \mathcal{S})$ be topological spaces and let $Y \subset X$. Show that if $f: X \to \Omega$ is a continuous function then the restriction of $f$ to $Y$ is a continuous function of $(Y, \mathcal{T}_Y)$ into $(\Omega, \mathcal{S})$.
