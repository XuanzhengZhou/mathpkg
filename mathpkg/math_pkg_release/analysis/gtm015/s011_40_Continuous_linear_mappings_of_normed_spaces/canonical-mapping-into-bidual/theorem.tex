\text{Let } E \text{ be a normed space over } \mathbb{K} \text{ and let } E'' = (E')' \text{ be its bidual. The mapping}

$$x \mapsto x'', \quad \text{where} \quad x''(f) = f(x) \quad (f \in E'),$$

\text{from } E \text{ into } E'' \text{ is called the canonical mapping (or canonical embedding) of } E \text{ into its bidual } E''.
