This theorem establishes the fundamental one-to-one correspondence between nondegenerate $*$-representations of the group algebra $L^1(G)$ and continuous unitary representations of the locally compact group $G$. It reduces the study of unitary representations to the study of $*$-representations of a Banach $*$-algebra, opening the door to $C^*$-algebraic methods.
