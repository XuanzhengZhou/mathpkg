# Proof of Bijection between star-representations and unitary representations

Proof. If $\varphi: L^1(G) \to \mathcal{L}(H)$ is a nondegenerate $*$-representation and if $U_{(\cdot)}$ is the continuous unitary representation of $G$ derived from $\varphi$ via (69.20), then it results from (4) of (69.20) that $\varphi$ is the $*$-representation of $L^1(G)$ derived from $U_{(\cdot)}$ via (69.21).

Conversely, if $t \mapsto U_t$ is a continuous unitary representation of $G$ and if $\varphi$ is the $*$-representation of $L^1(G)$ derived from $U_{(\cdot)}$ via (69.21), then it results from (4) of (69.21) that $U_{(\cdot)}$ is the unitary representation of $G$ derived from $\varphi$ via (69.20).

Thus the correspondences

$$\varphi \mapsto U_{(\cdot)}, \qquad U_{(\cdot)} \mapsto \varphi$$

of Theorems (69.20) and (69.21) are mutually inverse bijections between the set of all nondegenerate $*$-representations $\varphi$ of $L^1(G)$ and the set of all continuous unitary representations $t \mapsto U_t$ of $G$.
