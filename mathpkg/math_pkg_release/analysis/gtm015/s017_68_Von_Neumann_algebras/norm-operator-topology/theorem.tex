\begin{definition}
The \text{norm} (or ``uniform'') topology on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ is the topology derived from the norm
$$T \mapsto \|T\| = \sup \{ \|Tx\| : x \in H, \|x\| \leq 1 \}.$$
This is the topology of uniform convergence on the closed unit ball of $H$. It is denoted by $\tau_n$.
\end{definition}
