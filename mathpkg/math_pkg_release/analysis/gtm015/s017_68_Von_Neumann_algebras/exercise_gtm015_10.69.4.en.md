# Exercise 4

(69.30) Let $G$ be a locally compact group, let $H$ be a Hilbert space, and suppose $\varphi: L^1(G) \to \mathcal{L}(H)$ is a continuous algebra homomorphism such that (i) $\|\varphi\| \leq 1$, and (ii) $H$ is the closed linear span of the vectors $\varphi(u)x$ $(u \in L^1(G), x \in H)$. Then $\varphi$ is a $*$-representation of $L^1(G)$.
