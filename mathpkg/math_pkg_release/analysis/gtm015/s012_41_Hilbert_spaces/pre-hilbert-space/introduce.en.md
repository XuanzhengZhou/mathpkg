A pre-Hilbert space is a normed space whose norm satisfies the parallelogram law. Equivalently, it is a normed space whose completion is a Hilbert space. The parallelogram law is preserved under completion, so the distinction between a pre-Hilbert space and a Hilbert space is precisely the distinction between a normed space and a Banach space: the former may be incomplete, while the latter is required to be complete.
