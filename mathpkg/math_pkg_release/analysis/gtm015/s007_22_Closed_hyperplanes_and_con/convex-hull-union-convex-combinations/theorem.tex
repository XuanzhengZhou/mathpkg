Let $(A_i)_{i \in I}$ be a family of convex subsets of $E$ and let $S = \bigcup_{i \in I} A_i$. Then $\operatorname{conv} S$ coincides with the set of all convex combinations $x = \sum_{i \in I} \lambda_i x_i$, where $\lambda_i = 0$ for all but finitely many $i$, $\lambda_i \geq 0$ for all $i$, $\sum_{i \in I} \lambda_i = 1$, and $x_i \in A_i$ whenever $\lambda_i > 0$.
