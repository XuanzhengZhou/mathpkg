Let $(E_\iota)_{\iota \in I}$ be a family of vector spaces over $\mathbb{K}$, let $E = \prod_{\iota \in I} E_\iota$ be the product vector space, and let $\pi_\iota: E \rightarrow E_\iota$ be the coordinate projections. A subset $A$ of $E$ is convex if and only if each coordinate projection $\pi_\iota(A)$ is convex.
