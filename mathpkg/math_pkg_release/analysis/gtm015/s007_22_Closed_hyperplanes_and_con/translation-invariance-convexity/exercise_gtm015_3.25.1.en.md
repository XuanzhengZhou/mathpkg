# Exercise 25.27

Let $E$ be a vector space over $\mathbb{K}$. A subset $A$ of $E$ is said to be \emph{circled} if $\lambda A \subset A$ whenever $|\lambda| = 1$. Prove: A convex subset of $E$ is circled iff it is balanced.
