Let $E$ be a real TVS, let $A$ and $B$ be nonempty convex subsets of $E$ such that $A \cap B = \varnothing$, and suppose that $A$ is open. Then (1) there exists a closed hyperplane $H$ separating $A$ and $B$; (2) $A$ lies strictly to one side of $H$; (3) if $B$ is also open, then $H$ separates $A$ and $B$ strictly.
