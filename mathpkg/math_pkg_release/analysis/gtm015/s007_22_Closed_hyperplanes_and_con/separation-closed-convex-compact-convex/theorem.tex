Let $E$ be a locally convex TVS over $\mathbb{R}$, let $A$ and $B$ be nonempty convex sets with $A \cap B = \varnothing$, and suppose that $A$ is closed and $B$ is compact. Then there exist a continuous linear form $g$ on $E$ and a real number $\alpha$ such that $g < \alpha$ on $A$ and $g > \alpha$ on $B$.
