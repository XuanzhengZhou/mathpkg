# Exercise 28.11

Let $E$ be a vector space over $\mathbb{R}$, and suppose that $p$ is a real-valued function on $E$ satisfying conditions (i), (ii) of (28.6). Given any $a \in E$, there exists a linear form $f$ on $E$ such that $f(a) = p(a)$ and $f(x) \le p(x)$ for all $x \in E$.
