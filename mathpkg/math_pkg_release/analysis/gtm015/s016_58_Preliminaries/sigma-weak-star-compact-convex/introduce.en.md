The set of states on a unital $C^*$-algebra forms a weak$^*$-compact convex set in the dual space. By the Krein-Milman theorem, it is the closed convex hull of its extremal points (pure states). This compact convex structure is fundamental to the representation theory of $C^*$-algebras.
