# Proof of State space is weak* compact convex

Proof. Let $\Sigma$ be the set of all normalized states on $A$. Each $f \in \Sigma$ satisfies $\|f\| = f(1) = 1$, so $\Sigma$ is contained in the closed unit ball of the dual space $A'$. The weak$^*$ topology on this ball is compact by the Alaoglu-Bourbaki theorem (44.12). It remains to verify that $\Sigma$ is weak$^*$ closed; this follows from the characterizations in (61.9). Convexity of $\Sigma$ is evident from the definition of a state.
