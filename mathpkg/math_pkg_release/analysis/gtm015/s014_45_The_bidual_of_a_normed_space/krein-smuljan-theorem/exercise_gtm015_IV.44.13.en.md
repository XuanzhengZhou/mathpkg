# Exercise 44.13

If $M$ is a linear subspace of the normed space $E$ and if $y_0$ is a vector in $E$ such that

$$\inf \{ \|y_0 + z\| : z \in M \} = d > 0,$$

then there exists $f \in E'$ such that $f(y_0) = 1$, $f = 0$ on $M$, and $\|f\| = 1/d$.
