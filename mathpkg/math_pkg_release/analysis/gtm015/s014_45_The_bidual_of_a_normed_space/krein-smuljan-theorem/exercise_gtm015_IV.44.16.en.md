# Exercise 44.16 (Krein-Smuljan)

Let $E$ be a Banach space and let $K$ be a convex subset of $E'$. The following conditions on $K$ are equivalent:
(a) $K$ is weak* closed;
(b) for every $r > 0$, the set $K \cap B_r$ is weak* closed, where

$$B_r = \{ f \in E' : \|f\| \leq r \}.$$
