# Proof of Non-surjectivity and right topological divisors of zero

**Proof.** (a) $\Leftrightarrow$ (c): By (57.16), $T$ is not surjective iff $T'$ is not bounded below. By (57.4), $T'$ is not bounded below iff $T'$ is a left TDZ in $\mathcal{L}(E')$. By (57.8), $T'$ is a left TDZ in $\mathcal{L}(E')$ iff $T$ is a right TDZ in $\mathcal{L}(E)$.
