For any element $x$ in an associative algebra $A$, there is a unique algebra homomorphism from the polynomial algebra $\mathbb{C}[t]$ into $A$ sending $t$ to $x$. This defines $p(x)$ for every polynomial $p$, establishing the polynomial functional calculus. The image is the smallest subalgebra containing $x$ and the identity.
