# Proof of Spectral radius in closed subalgebras

**Proof.** By (55.1), $r_A(x) = \lim_{n \to \infty} \|x^n\|^{1/n}$ and $r_B(x) = \lim_{n \to \infty} \|x^n\|^{1/n}$. Both limits use the same norm $\|\cdot\|$ (the norm of $A$, which restricts to the norm of $B$). Hence $r_A(x) = r_B(x)$.
