# Exercise 55.3

(55.3) (i) In (55.1) the hypothesis $\|1\| = 1$ is inessential: the spectral radius formula holds even if $\|1\| > 1$.

(ii) The formula $\lim \|x^n\|^{1/n} = \inf \|x^n\|^{1/n}$ is valid in any normed algebra.
