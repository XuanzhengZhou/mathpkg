# Exercise 51.14

(51.14) Let $A$ be a Banach algebra with unity element 1, $\|1\| = 1$, and let $B$ be a closed subalgebra of $A$ such that $1 \in B$.

(i) If $x \in B$ then $\sigma_A(x) \subset \sigma_B(x)$. The inclusion may be proper.

(ii) If $x \in B$ then $r_A(x) = r_B(x)$.
