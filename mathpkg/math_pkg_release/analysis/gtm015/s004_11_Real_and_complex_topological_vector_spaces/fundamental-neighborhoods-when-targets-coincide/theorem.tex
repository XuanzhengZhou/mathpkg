If $E$ is a vector space over $\mathbb{K}$, $G$ is a TVS over $\mathbb{K}$, and $f_\iota: E \to G$ ($\iota \in I$) is a family of linear mappings, then the sets
$$\bigcap_{\iota \in J} f_\iota^{-1}(V),$$
where $J$ is a finite subset of $I$ and $V$ is a neighborhood of $\theta$ in $G$, form a fundamental system of neighborhoods of $\theta$ in $E$ for the initial TVS topology.
