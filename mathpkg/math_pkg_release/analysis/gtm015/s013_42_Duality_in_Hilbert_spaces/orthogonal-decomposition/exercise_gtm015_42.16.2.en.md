# Exercise 42.16

The proof of the theorem on the minimizing vector (42.3) is independent of the notion of inner product, and the notion of orthogonality can also be formulated directly in terms of the norm (42.15). Give an inner-product-free proof of the theorem on orthogonal projection (42.7).
