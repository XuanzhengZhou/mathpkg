# Exercise 10.10 (V. L. Klee)

Let $G$ be a metrizable, bi-uniform topological group, and suppose $G$ admits a complete compatible metric. Then $G$ is a complete topological group (therefore $G$ admits a bi-invariant complete compatible metric).
