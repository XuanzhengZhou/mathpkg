Theorem 4.4 refines the characterization of sets with the property of Baire by showing they can be expressed in terms of the Borel hierarchy: as a $G_\delta$ set (countable intersection of open sets) plus a first-category set, or dually as an $F_\sigma$ set minus a first-category set. This parallels the structure of measurable sets, though the roles of $G_\delta$ and $F_\sigma$ are reversed compared to the measure-theoretic case.
