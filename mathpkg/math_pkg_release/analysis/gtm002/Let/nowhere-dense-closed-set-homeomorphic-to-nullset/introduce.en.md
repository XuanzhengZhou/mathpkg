A simple but elegant observation: any nowhere dense closed subset of $[0,1]$ can be transformed into a Lebesgue nullset by a homeomorphism of the interval onto itself. The explicit construction defines $h(x)$ as the normalized measure of $[0,x] \cap G$ where $G$ is the complement of $F$. This stretches the dense open complement $G$ to full measure, compressing the nowhere dense closed set $F$ into a nullset.
