Let $H$ denote the set of all automorphisms of $I = [0, 1]$ (homeomorphisms of $I$ onto itself) that leave the endpoints fixed, metrized by the distance function

$$\varrho(g, h) = \max |g(x) - h(x)|.$$

$(H, \varrho)$ is a subspace of the space $C = C[0, 1]$ of continuous functions on $I$. $H$ is a $G_\delta$ subset of $C$, and therefore topologically complete.
