The space of measurable sets modulo nullsets, equipped with the symmetric difference metric, is a complete metric space. The proof constructs, for any Cauchy sequence of sets, a limit set $E$ consisting of points belonging to all but finitely many of the approximating sets. The completeness of this space is analogous to the completeness of $L^1$ and reflects the completeness properties of Lebesgue measure.
