Although $H$ is not complete under the uniform metric (a sequence of piecewise linear homeomorphisms approximating a non-injective map can be Cauchy without converging in $H$), it is a $G_\delta$ subset of the complete space $C[0,1]$. By Alexandroff's Theorem 12.1, $H$ is therefore topologically complete — it admits an equivalent complete metric. This topological completeness is the key property used in the category argument for Theorem 13.1.
