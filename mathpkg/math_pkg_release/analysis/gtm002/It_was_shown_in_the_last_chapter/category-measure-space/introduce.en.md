A category measure space is a topological space equipped with a finite measure defined on the $\sigma$-algebra of sets having the property of Baire, with the defining property that a set has measure zero precisely when it is of first category. In such spaces, the extended principle of duality between measure and category becomes a tautology. The concept unifies the measure-theoretic and Baire-category viewpoints by making the nullsets and first-category sets coincide exactly.
