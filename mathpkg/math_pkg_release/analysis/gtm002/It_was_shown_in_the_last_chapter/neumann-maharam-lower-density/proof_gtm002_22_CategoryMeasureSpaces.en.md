The general proof of Theorem 22.4 is not given in the text; the reader is referred to von Neumann and Maharam [19] and an alternative proof by A. and C. Ionescu Tulcea [15].

For the special case of the Lebesgue measure space, the Lebesgue density theorem (Theorem 3.21) already defines such a mapping: $\phi(A)$ is the set of points at which $A$ has density $1$.
