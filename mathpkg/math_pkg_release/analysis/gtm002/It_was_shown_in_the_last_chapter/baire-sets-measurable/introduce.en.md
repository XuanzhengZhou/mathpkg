This theorem establishes the fundamental correspondence that makes the density topology a category measure space: the $\sigma$-algebra $S$ of measurable sets coincides precisely with the class of sets having the property of Baire in the density topology. Thus the measure $\mu$ becomes a category measure, and the problem of turning a measure space into a category measure space reduces to finding a lower density.
