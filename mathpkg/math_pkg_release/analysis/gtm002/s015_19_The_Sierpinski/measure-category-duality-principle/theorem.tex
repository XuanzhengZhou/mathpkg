Let $P$ be any proposition involving solely the notion of nullset and notions of pure set theory (for example, cardinality, disjointness, or any property invariant under arbitrary one-to-one transformation). Let $P^*$ be the proposition obtained from $P$ by replacing ``nullset'' by ``set of first category'' throughout. Then, assuming the continuum hypothesis, each of the propositions $P$ and $P^*$ implies the other.
