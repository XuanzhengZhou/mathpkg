Theorem 15.2 of Oxtoby's text extends the Kuratowski-Ulam theorem to sets having the Baire property. If a plane set $E$ has the property of Baire (i.e., it can be expressed as the symmetric difference of an open set and a first-category set), then for all $x$ outside a first-category exceptional set, the $x$-section $E_x$ also has the property of Baire. The proof is a direct application of Theorem 15.1 combined with the fact that every section of an open set is open.
