Let $E = G \triangle P$, where $G$ is open and $P$ is of first category. Then $E_x = G_x \triangle P_x$, for all $x$. Every section of an open set is open, hence $E_x$ has the property of Baire whenever $P_x$ is of first category. By Theorem 15.1, this is the case for all $x$ except a set of first category. $\square$
