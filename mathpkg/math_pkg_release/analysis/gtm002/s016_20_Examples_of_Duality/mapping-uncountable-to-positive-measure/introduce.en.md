Proposition 20.2* is the measure-theoretic dual of Proposition 20.2 under the Sierpiński duality principle. It asserts the existence of a one-to-one mapping from the real line onto a subset of itself such that the image of any uncountable set has positive outer measure. The proof follows by duality: replace "second category" with "positive outer measure" and use the set $N$ from Proposition 20.1*, which is the measure-theoretic counterpart of a Lusin set.
