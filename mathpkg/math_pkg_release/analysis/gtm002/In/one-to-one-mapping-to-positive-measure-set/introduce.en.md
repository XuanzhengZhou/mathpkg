Proposition 20.2* is the measure-theoretic dual of Proposition 20.2. It asserts the existence of a one-to-one mapping from the real line onto a subset of itself such that the image of every uncountable set has positive outer measure. This follows from Proposition 20.1* by taking a one-to-one mapping onto the set $N$ obtained there. The proposition depends on the continuum hypothesis through the duality principle.
