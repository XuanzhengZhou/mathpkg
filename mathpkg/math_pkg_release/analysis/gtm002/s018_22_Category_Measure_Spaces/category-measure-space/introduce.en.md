A category measure space is a topological space equipped with a finite measure on the Baire-property $\sigma$-algebra such that nullsets coincide exactly with sets of first category. In this setting the extended principle of duality between measure and category becomes a tautology: the measure-theoretic and topological notions of "smallness" are identical. This concept unifies the parallel threads of measure theory and Baire category theory developed throughout the book.
