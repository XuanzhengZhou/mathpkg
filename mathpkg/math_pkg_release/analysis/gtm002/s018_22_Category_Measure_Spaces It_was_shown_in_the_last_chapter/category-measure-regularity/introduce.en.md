Theorem 22.1 establishes that every category measure on a regular Baire space is regular: open sets are inner regular (approximable from within by closed sets) and closed sets are outer regular (approximable from without by open sets). This is analogous to the regularity of Radon measures but holds for the purely topological notion of category measure, where measure zero coincides with first category.
