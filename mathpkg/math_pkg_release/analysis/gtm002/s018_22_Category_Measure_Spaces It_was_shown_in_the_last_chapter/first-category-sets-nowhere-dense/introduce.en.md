Theorem 22.2 strengthens the relationship between measure and category: in a regular Baire space equipped with a category measure, every set of first category is nowhere dense. This contrasts with the usual situation where first category sets may be dense (e.g., the rationals in $\mathbb{R}$). The result follows from the regularity property (Theorem 22.1) and the fact that a category measure assigns measure zero exactly to sets of first category.
