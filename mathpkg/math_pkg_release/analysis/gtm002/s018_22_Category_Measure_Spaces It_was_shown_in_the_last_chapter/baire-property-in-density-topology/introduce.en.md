Theorem 22.7 establishes that in the density topology, the $\sigma$-algebra of sets with the property of Baire coincides with the original $\sigma$-algebra of measurable sets. This is the final link that makes the density topology a category measure space: the domain of the measure is precisely the sets with the Baire property, and by Theorem 22.6, the nullsets are exactly the nowhere dense (hence first category) sets.
