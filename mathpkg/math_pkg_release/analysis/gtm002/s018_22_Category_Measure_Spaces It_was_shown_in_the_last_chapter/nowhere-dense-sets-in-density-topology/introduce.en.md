Theorem 22.6 characterizes the nowhere dense sets in the density topology: they are precisely the $\mu$-nullsets. Moreover, every nowhere dense set is closed in this topology. This is the key result that makes the density topology into a category measure space -- it establishes that the ideal of nowhere dense sets coincides with the ideal of nullsets, so $\mu(E) = 0$ if and only if $E$ is of first category.
