Let $G = G^{-'-'}$ and $H = H^{-'-'}$ be regular open. Since $G \cap H$ is open, it follows that
$$G \cap H \subset (G \cap H)^{-'-'} \subset G^{-'-'} = G.$$
Similarly,
$$G \cap H \subset (G \cap H)^{-'-'} \subset H^{-'-'} = H.$$
Therefore $G \cap H = (G \cap H)^{-'-'}$, so $G \cap H$ is regular open.
