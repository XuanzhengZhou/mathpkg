A wandering set under a mapping $T$ is one whose preimages under all forward iterates of $T$ are pairwise disjoint. The existence or non-existence of wandering sets of positive measure (or non-meager category) characterizes whether a dynamical system exhibits recurrent behavior. Wandering sets are the fundamental obstruction to recurrence.
