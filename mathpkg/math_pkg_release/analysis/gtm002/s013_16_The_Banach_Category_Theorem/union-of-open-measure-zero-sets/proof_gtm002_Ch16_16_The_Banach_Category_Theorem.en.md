Let $\{G_{\alpha} : \alpha \in A\}$ be the family of open sets of measure zero. Let $\mathcal{B}_0$ be a maximal family of disjoint non-empty open sets that are contained in some member of $\mathcal{G}$. Then $\mu(\bigcup \mathcal{G}) = \mu(\bigcup \mathcal{B}_0) = 0$.
