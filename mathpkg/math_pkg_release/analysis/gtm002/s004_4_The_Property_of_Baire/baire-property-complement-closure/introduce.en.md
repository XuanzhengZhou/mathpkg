Theorem 4.2 states that the class of sets having the property of Baire is closed under complementation. This follows directly from the closed-set characterization of Theorem 4.1: if $A = G \triangle P$ with $G$ open and $P$ of first category, then $A' = G' \triangle P$, and since $G'$ is closed, Theorem 4.1 implies $A'$ has the property of Baire. Together with Theorem 4.3, this establishes that the Baire-property sets form a $\sigma$-algebra.
