In a metric space, any family of closed sets that are sufficiently separated from one another (in the sense that distinct members have positive distance) has a union that is an $F_\sigma$ set. This lemma provides a key structural fact used to extend the Banach Category Theorem to the measure-theoretic setting, where one needs to show that certain unions of closed sets are Borel measurable.
