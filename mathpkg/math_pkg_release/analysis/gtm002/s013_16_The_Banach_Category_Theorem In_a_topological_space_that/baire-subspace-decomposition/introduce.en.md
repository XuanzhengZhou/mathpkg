Every topological space decomposes into a Baire subspace (open or closed) together with a meager remainder of first category. This follows directly from the Banach Category Theorem by taking the largest open Baire subspace and noting that its complement is of first category. The decomposition provides a structural bridge between Baire category considerations and arbitrary topological spaces.
