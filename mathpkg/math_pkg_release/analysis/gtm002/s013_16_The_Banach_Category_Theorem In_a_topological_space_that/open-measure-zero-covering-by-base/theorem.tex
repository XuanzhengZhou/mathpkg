In a metric space $X$ with a Borel measure $\mu$, if $\mathcal{G}$ is a family of open sets of measure zero and $\mathcal{B}_0$ is a base for $X$, then the union of all members of $\mathcal{B}_0$ that are contained in some member of $\mathcal{G}$ has the same measure as $\bigcup \mathcal{G}$. In particular, if the family $\mathcal{G}$ has cardinality of measure zero, then $\mu(\bigcup \mathcal{G}) = \mu(\bigcup \mathcal{B}_0) = 0$.
