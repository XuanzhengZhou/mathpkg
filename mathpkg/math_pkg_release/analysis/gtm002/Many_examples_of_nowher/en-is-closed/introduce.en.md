This lemma establishes that each $E_n$ is a closed subset of the Banach space $C = C[0,1]$ under the uniform metric. The proof proceeds by taking a sequence $\{f_k\} \subset E_n$ converging uniformly to $f$, with associated points $x_k$ satisfying the bounded difference quotient condition. Passing to a convergent subsequence $x_k \to x$, one verifies that the limiting function $f$ also satisfies the defining inequality at $x$, using the triangle inequality to control the difference quotients.
