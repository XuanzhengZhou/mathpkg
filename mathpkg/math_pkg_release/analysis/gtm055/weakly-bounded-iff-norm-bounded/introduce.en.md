Proposition 15.7 states that weak boundedness and norm boundedness are equivalent for subsets of a normed space. The proof uses the natural embedding $j: \mathcal{E} \to \mathcal{E}^{**}$ and the Uniform Boundedness Theorem: a weakly bounded set is pointwise bounded on $\mathcal{E}^*$, hence norm bounded in $\mathcal{E}^{**}$, and $j$ being an isometry transfers this back to $\mathcal{E}$.
