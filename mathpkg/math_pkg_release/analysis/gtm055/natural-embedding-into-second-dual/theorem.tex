The linear isometry $j$ of Proposition 15.6 is known as the natural embedding of the normed space $\mathcal{E}$ in the second dual $\mathcal{E}^{**}$ of $\mathcal{E}$. For each $x \in \mathcal{E}$, the functional $F_x$ on $\mathcal{E}^*$ is defined by
$$F_x(f) = f(x), \quad f \in \mathcal{E}^*,$$
and $j(x) = F_x$ defines a linear isometry $j: \mathcal{E} \to \mathcal{E}^{**}$.
