The topological closure $\mathcal{L}^-$ of a linear manifold $\mathcal{L}$ in a normed space $\mathcal{E}$ is again a linear manifold, and is therefore a subspace of $\mathcal{E}$. Hence the subspace spanned by a subset $M$ of $\mathcal{E}$ coincides with the closure of the linear submanifold generated (algebraically) by $M$. In particular, the supremum $\bigvee_{\gamma} M_{\gamma}$ of an indexed family $\{M_{\gamma}\}$ of subspaces of $\mathcal{E}$ is the closure of the sum $\sum_{\gamma} M_{\gamma}$ (as defined in Chapter 2).
