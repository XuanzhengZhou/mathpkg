By Proposition 16.2(i), $^a(M^a)$ is the smallest closed linear manifold containing $M$. Since $M^a$ depends only on the closed linear span of $M$, we have $(^a(M^a))^a = M^a$. The dual statement follows symmetrically from Proposition 16.2(ii).
