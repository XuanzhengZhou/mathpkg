Let $\mathcal{E}$ be an infinite dimensional normed space, and let $C$ be a closed bounded convex subset of $\mathcal{E}$. Show that $C$ is the weak closure of the boundary $\partial C$ (taken in the norm topology). Thus, for example, the closed unit ball $\mathcal{E}_1$ is the weak closure of the unit sphere $\{x \in \mathcal{E}: \|x\| = 1\}$.

\textit{Hint:} Use Problem H.
