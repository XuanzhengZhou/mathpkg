Corollary 16.10 states that the adjoint of an invertible operator is also invertible, and its inverse is the adjoint of the inverse. This follows directly from the functorial property $(S \circ T)^* = T^* \circ S^*$ applied to $T \circ T^{-1} = I$ and $T^{-1} \circ T = I$, showing that the adjoint operation respects the group of invertible operators.
