\textbf{Problem V.} Let $U$ be a nonempty open subset of $\mathbb{R}^n$. For each index $i = 1, \ldots, n$ and for each distribution $T$ on $U$ we define the \textbf{distributional derivative} $D_i T$ by setting
$$(D_i T)(\varphi) = -T\left(\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial x_i}\right), \quad \varphi \in \mathcal{D}(U),$$
where $\mathcal{D}(U)$ denotes the space of test functions on $U$.
