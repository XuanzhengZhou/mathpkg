Conclude that if $\mathcal{E}$ is an infinite dimensional normed space, then the weak topology on $\mathcal{E}$ fails to satisfy the first axiom of countability, and hence is not metrizable. Show further that if $\mathcal{E}$ is complete, then the weak$^*$ topology on $\mathcal{E}^*$ also fails to satisfy the first axiom of countability.

\textit{Hint:} Recall Problem 11Q.
