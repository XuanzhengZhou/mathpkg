The identity $(^0(M^0))^0 = M^0$ follows from the Bipolar Theorem (Proposition 16.14) applied to the absolutely convex set $A = M^0$ (which is already weakly closed as a polar), giving $^0((M^0)^0) = M^0$, and then taking polars of both sides.

The statement about absolutely convex sets is exactly the content of Proposition 16.14.
