Proposition 16.2 shows that the double annihilator $^a(M^a)$ recovers the closed linear span of $M$ in $\mathcal{E}$, while the double preannihilator $(^a M)^a$ recovers the weak$^*$ closed linear span of $M$ in $\mathcal{E}^*$. These are fundamental duality results: they establish that annihilator operations provide a bijection between closed linear manifolds of $\mathcal{E}$ and weak$^*$ closed linear manifolds of $\mathcal{E}^*$.
