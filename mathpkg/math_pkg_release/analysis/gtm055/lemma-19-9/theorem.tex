Let $(X, A)$ be a biorthogonal system for a Banach space $\mathcal{E}$ as in the foregoing definition. Then the expansion operators $E_N$ of the system $(X, A)$ have the property that $E_M E_N = E_N E_M = E_M \wedge N$ for every pair of nonnegative integers $M$ and $N$. In particular, each $E_N$ is idempotent.
