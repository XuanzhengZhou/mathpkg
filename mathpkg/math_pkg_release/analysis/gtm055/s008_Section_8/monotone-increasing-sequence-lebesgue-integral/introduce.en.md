Proposition 7.2 establishes the monotone convergence property for the Lebesgue integral. For a monotone increasing sequence $\{f_n\}$ of nonnegative integrable functions with pointwise limit $f$, the limit function $f$ is integrable precisely when the sequence of integrals $\{L(f_n)\}$ is bounded above. In that case, the integral of the limit equals the limit of the integrals.
