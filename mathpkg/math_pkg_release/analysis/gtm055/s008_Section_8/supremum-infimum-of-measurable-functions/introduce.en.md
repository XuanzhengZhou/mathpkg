Proposition 6.3 generalizes the basic fact that measurability is preserved under pointwise lattice operations. Given a sequence $\{f_n\}$ of measurable extended real-valued functions, the pointwise supremum $\sup_n f_n$ and infimum $\inf_n f_n$ are both measurable. Consequently, the limit superior $\limsup_n f_n = \inf_m \sup_{n \ge m} f_n$ and limit inferior $\liminf_n f_n = \sup_m \inf_{n \ge m} f_n$ are also measurable.
