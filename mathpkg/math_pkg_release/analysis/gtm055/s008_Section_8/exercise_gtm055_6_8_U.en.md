Use Problem M and the Stone-Weierstrass theorem (see Example 18D) to show that if $U$ is an open subset of the complex plane, then there exists a sequence $\{p_n(\lambda, \bar{\lambda})\}$ of complex polynomials in $\lambda$ and $\bar{\lambda}$ that converges pointwise to $\chi_U$. Then follow the argument sketched in Problem T to prove the complex version of Theorem 6.5.
