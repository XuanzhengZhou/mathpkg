Proposition 6.2 addresses the measurability of pointwise limits of measurable functions. Given a sequence of measurable complex-valued functions $\{f_n\}$ on a measurable space $X$, the set $E$ of points $x$ where $\{f_n(x)\}$ converges is itself measurable. Moreover, the pointwise limit function $f(x) = \lim_n f_n(x)$ defined on $E$ is measurable with respect to the subspace measurable structure on $E$.
