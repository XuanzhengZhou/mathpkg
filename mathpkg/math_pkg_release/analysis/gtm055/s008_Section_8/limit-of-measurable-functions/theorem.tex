Let $X$ be a measurable space, and let $\{f_n\}$ be a sequence of measurable complex-valued functions on $X$. Denote by $E$ the set of all those points $x$ at which the numerical sequence $\{f_n(x)\}$ is convergent. Then $E$ is a measurable set, and the function $f$ defined on $E$ by $f(x) = \lim_n f_n(x)$ is measurable on the subspace $E$.
