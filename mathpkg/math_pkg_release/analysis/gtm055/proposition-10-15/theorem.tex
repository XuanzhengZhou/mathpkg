A signed Borel measure $\mu$ on a locally compact Hausdorff space $X$ is regular if and only if its positive and negative variations $\mu_+$ and $\mu_-$ are both regular. A complex Borel measure $\xi$ on $X$ is regular if and only if $\text{Re} \xi$ and $\text{Im} \xi$ are regular.
