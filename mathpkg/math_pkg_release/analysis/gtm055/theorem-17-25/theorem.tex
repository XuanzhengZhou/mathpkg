Let $U$ be an open subset of $\mathbb{C}$, and let $\mathcal{F}_U$ denote the unital algebra of all locally analytic complex-valued functions defined on $U$. If $\mathcal{A}$ is a unital Banach algebra and $x$ is an element of $\mathcal{A}$ such that $\sigma_{\mathcal{A}}(x) \subset U$, then $f \rightarrow f(x)$ is an algebra homomorphism of $\mathcal{F}_U$ into $\mathcal{A}$ with the property that $\rho(1) = 1_{\mathcal{A}}$.
