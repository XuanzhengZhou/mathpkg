For any normed space $\mathcal{E}$ the weak* topology on $\mathcal{E}^*$ turns $\mathcal{E}^*$ into a separated, locally convex, topological linear space. A net $\{f_\lambda\}$ in $\mathcal{E}^*$ converges to a limit $f$ in the weak* topology if and only if $\lim_\lambda f_\lambda(x) = f(x)$ in $\mathbb{C}$ for every vector $x$ in $\mathcal{E}$.
