One of the most important consequences of simple connectivity: every analytic function on a simply connected domain has a global primitive. Path integrals between two points are independent of the choice of path, so $F(\lambda) = \int_{\lambda_0}^{\lambda} f(\zeta) d\zeta$ (taken along any rectifiable arc) is well-defined and satisfies $F' = f$.
