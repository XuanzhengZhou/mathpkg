Let $U$ and $V$ be open subsets of $\mathbb{C}$ and let $\alpha$ and $\beta$ be rectifiable arcs lying in $U$ and $V$, respectively. Let $W_{\alpha}$ and $W_{\beta}$ denote the ranges of $\alpha$ and $\beta$, and let $g(\zeta, \lambda)$ be a continuous complex-valued function on $W_{\alpha} \times W_{\beta}$. Then
$$f_1(\zeta) = \int_{\beta} g(\zeta, \lambda) \, d\lambda$$
is continuous on $W_{\alpha}$, and
$$f_2(\lambda) = \int_{\alpha} g(\zeta, \lambda) \, d\zeta$$
is continuous on $W_{\beta}$.
