Let $f$ be a continuous real-valued function defined on a closed interval $[a, b]$, let $U$ be an open subset of $[a, b]$, and let $m$ be a real number. Then the set $R_m(f; U) [L_m(f; U)]$ is open, and if $(c_k, d_k)$ is any one of its components, then

$$\frac{f(d_k) - f(c_k)}{d_k - c_k} \geq m \quad \left[ \frac{f(d_k) - f(c_k)}{d_k - c_k} \leq m \right].$$
