Proposition 17.13 establishes that integrable simple functions are dense in the vector-valued Lebesgue spaces $\mathcal{L}_p(X; \mathcal{E})$ for $1 \leq p < +\infty$. The proof constructs approximations using a countable dense subset of $\mathcal{E}$ and a countable collection of measurable sets generating the measure ring. This density result is fundamental for the theory of the Bochner integral.
