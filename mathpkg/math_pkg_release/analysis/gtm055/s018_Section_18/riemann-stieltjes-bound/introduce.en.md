Proposition 17.18 provides a fundamental norm estimate for the Riemann-Stieltjes integral of continuous Banach space valued mappings with respect to integrators of bounded variation. The norm of the integral is bounded by the integral of the norm function with respect to the total variation measure $dL$, which in turn is bounded by the maximum norm times the total variation.
