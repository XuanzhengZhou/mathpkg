The line integral (or contour integral) of a Banach-valued continuous function along a rectifiable curve is defined via Riemann--Stieltjes sums. When the Banach space is the complex numbers, this reduces to the classical complex line integral. The definition extends the functional calculus for Banach algebras by providing a means to integrate operator-valued functions along contours.
