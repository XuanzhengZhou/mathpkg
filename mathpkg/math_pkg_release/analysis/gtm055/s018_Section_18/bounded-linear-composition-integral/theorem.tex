Let $\mathcal{E}$ and $\mathcal{F}$ be Banach spaces and let $T$ be an element of $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{E}, \mathcal{F})$. If $\Phi$ belongs to $\mathcal{L}_1(X; \mathcal{E})$, then $T \circ \Phi$ belongs to $\mathcal{L}_1(X; \mathcal{F})$, and

$$T\left( \int_X \Phi \, d\mu \right) = \int_X (T \circ \Phi) \, d\mu.$$
