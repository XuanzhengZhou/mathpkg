Theorem 17.7 establishes the equivalence between separability of a Lebesgue space $\mathcal{L}_p(X)$ and separability of the underlying measure space. In particular, if the measure space $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ is separable (meaning the associated metric space $\dot{\mathbf{S}}_\mathcal{F}$ is separable), then all $\mathcal{L}_p(X)$ for $1 \leq p < +\infty$ are separable Banach spaces.
