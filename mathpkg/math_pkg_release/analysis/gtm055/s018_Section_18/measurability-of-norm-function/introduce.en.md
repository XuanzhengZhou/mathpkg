Lemma 17.8 ensures that the pointwise norm of a measurable vector-valued function is measurable. The proof uses the fact that the set $\{x \in X : \|\Phi(x)\| \leq r\}$ equals $\{x \in X : \Phi(x) \in \mathcal{E}_r\}$, where the closed balls $\mathcal{E}_r$ are Borel sets in the normed space $\mathcal{E}$. This lemma is essential for defining the $\mathcal{L}_p$ spaces of vector-valued functions.
