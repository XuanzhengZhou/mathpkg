Theorem 17.6 extends the Riesz representation theorem to the case $p = 1$ under the additional hypothesis that the measure space is $\sigma$-finite. It establishes that the dual of $\mathcal{L}_1(X)$ is isometrically isomorphic to $\mathcal{L}_\infty(X)$ via the natural pairing $\varphi_g(f) = \int_X f g d\mu$. The $\sigma$-finiteness condition is needed to patch together locally defined representing functions.
