Theorem 17.5 generalizes the Riesz representation theorem to $\mathcal{L}_p$ spaces for $1 < p < +\infty$. It establishes an isometric isomorphism between the dual space $\mathcal{L}_p(X)^*$ and $\mathcal{L}_q(X)$ where $q$ is the Holder conjugate of $p$. The proof constructs a complex measure via $\nu(E) = \varphi(\chi_E)$, applies the Radon-Nikodym theorem to obtain a density $g$, and verifies that $g \in \mathcal{L}_q(X)$ with the appropriate norm equality.
