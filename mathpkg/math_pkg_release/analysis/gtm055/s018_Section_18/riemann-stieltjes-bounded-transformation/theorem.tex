Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a Banach space, let $[a, b]$ be a real interval, and let $\alpha$ be a complex-valued function of bounded variation on $[a, b]$. Then the mapping

$$\Phi \mapsto \int_a^b \Phi(t) d\alpha(t)$$

is a bounded linear transformation of $\mathcal{C}([a, b]; \mathcal{E})$ into $\mathcal{E}$ with norm no greater than the total variation of $\alpha$ over $[a, b]$.
