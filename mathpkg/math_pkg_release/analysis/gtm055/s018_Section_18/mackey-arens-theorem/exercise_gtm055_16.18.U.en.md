Verify that every Frechet space is barreled. (Hint: If $B$ is a barrel in a Frechet space $\mathcal{E}$, then $\{nB\}_{n=1}^{\infty}$ is a countable closed covering of the complete metric space $\mathcal{E}$, and the Baire category theorem applies.)
