The oriented envelope integral equality relates the Cauchy integral of a Banach-valued locally analytic function to integration against a complex measure supported on a compact set. It uses the Fubini theorem for vector-valued (or complex measure) integration to interchange the order of integration, a crucial step in proving Runge-type approximation theorems for operator-valued functions.
