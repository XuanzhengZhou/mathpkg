Let $U$ be an open subset of $\mathbb{C}$, and let $K$ be a compact subset of $U$. Then there exists a finite set $\Delta_1, \ldots, \Delta_p$ of Jordan domains such that the corresponding Jordan regions $R_i = \Delta_i^-$ are pairwise disjoint, and such that
$$K \subset \Delta_1 \cup \cdots \cup \Delta_p$$
and $R_i \subset U$, $i = 1, \ldots, p$.
