If $f$ is a continuous mapping of a closed interval $[a, b]$ into a topological space $Y$, then $f$ is called an \textbf{arc} in $Y$, and $[a, b]$ is the \textbf{parameter interval} of the arc $f$. If $f(a) = y_0$ and $f(b) = y_1$, then $f$ is called an arc from $y_0$ to $y_1$, and $y_0$ and $y_1$ are the endpoints of the arc.
