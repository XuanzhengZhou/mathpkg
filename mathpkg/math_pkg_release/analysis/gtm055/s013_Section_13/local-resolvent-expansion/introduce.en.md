Proposition 12.12 establishes that the resolvent $R_x(\lambda)$ of an element $x$ in a unital Banach algebra admits a local power series expansion around any point $\lambda_0$ in the resolvent set. Specifically, for $|\lambda - \lambda_0| < 1/\|R_x(\lambda_0)\|$, the resolvent expands as $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (\lambda_0 - \lambda)^n R_x(\lambda_0)^{n+1}$. This implies that the resolvent set is open, and together with Proposition 12.11 shows that the spectrum is a closed and bounded (hence compact) subset of the complex plane.
