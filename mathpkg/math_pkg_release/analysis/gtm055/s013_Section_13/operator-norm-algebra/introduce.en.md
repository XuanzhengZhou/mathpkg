Corollary 12.4 asserts that for any nontrivial normed space $\mathcal{E}$, the space $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{E})$ of bounded operators on $\mathcal{E}$ forms a normed algebra. The operator norm is submultiplicative ($\|ST\| \leq \|S\|\|T\|$) and the identity operator has norm one. Together with Theorem 12.2 (completeness of $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{E},\mathcal{F})$ when $\mathcal{F}$ is complete), this shows that $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{E})$ is a unital Banach algebra whenever $\mathcal{E}$ is a Banach space.
