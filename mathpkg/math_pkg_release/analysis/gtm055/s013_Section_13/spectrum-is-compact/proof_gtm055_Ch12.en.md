Proposition 12.12 shows that the complement of $\sigma_{\mathcal{A}}(x)$ is open (every point in the resolvent set has an open disc around it also contained in the resolvent set). Proposition 12.11 shows that $\sigma_{\mathcal{A}}(x)$ is bounded, being a subset of $\{\lambda \in \mathbb{C}: |\lambda| \leq \|x\|\}$. Therefore $\sigma_{\mathcal{A}}(x)$ is closed and bounded, hence compact.
