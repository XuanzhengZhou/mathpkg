Corollary 12.13 follows immediately from Propositions 12.11 and 12.12: the spectrum is bounded (contained in the closed disc of radius $\|x\|$) by Proposition 12.11, and its complement (the resolvent set) is open by Proposition 12.12. Hence the spectrum is a closed and bounded subset of $\mathbb{C}$, i.e., compact. This is a fundamental fact in Banach algebra theory: the spectrum of any element is always a nonempty compact set.
