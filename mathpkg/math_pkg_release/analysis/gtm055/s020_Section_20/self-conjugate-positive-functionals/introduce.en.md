Problem 18.L characterizes important classes of bounded linear functionals on $\mathcal{C}(X)$ in terms of properties of their representing measures via the Riesz representation theorem. Self-conjugate functionals correspond to real-valued measures, positive functionals to nonnegative measures, and multiplicative functionals to Dirac point masses.
