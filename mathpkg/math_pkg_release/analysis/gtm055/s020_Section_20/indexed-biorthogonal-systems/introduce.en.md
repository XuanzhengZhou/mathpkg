Indexed biorthogonal systems generalize biorthogonal sequences to arbitrary index sets. When the vector family is fundamental and the functional family is total, one obtains a Markushevich basis. Unlike Schauder bases, Markushevich bases exist in every separable Banach space, making them a universal tool for studying Banach space structure.
