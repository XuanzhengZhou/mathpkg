If $\zeta$ is a complex Borel measure on the interval $[a, b]$, then
$$f \mapsto \int_a^b f \, d\zeta$$
is a bounded linear functional on the space $C^{(1)}$, and must therefore be representable in terms of some complex Borel measure $\xi$ and a complex number $\alpha$ as
$$\varphi(f) = \int_a^b f \, d\xi + \alpha f(t_0)$$
where $\xi$ is a complex Borel measure on $[a, b]$, $t_0$ denotes a fixed point of $[a, b]$, and $\alpha$ is a complex number. This representation is unique once $t_0$ has been chosen.
