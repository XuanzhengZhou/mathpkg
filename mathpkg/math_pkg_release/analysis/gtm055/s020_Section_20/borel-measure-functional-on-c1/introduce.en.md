Problem 18.K asks to find the most general bounded linear functional on the space $C^{(k)}$ of $k$ times continuously differentiable functions. For $C^{(1)}$, every bounded linear functional can be represented as an integral against a Borel measure plus a point evaluation term. The hint uses the differentiation operator $D$ and the Volterra integration operator $V$, noting that $D$ is a left inverse of $V$.
