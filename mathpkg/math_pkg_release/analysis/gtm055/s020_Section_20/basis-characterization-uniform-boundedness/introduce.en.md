Proposition 19.10 gives a fundamental characterization: a biorthogonal system $(X, A)$ forms a Schauder basis for the closed subspace spanned by $X$ if and only if the expansion operators $E_N$ are uniformly bounded. This connects the analytic notion of basis convergence to the functional-analytic property of uniform boundedness, a consequence of the Banach-Steinhaus theorem.
