Let $\{x_\gamma\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ be a family of vectors in a Banach space $\mathcal{E}$. The family is absolutely summable if $\sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma} \|x_\gamma\| < +\infty$. The collection of all absolutely summable families indexed by $\Gamma$ is isometrically isomorphic to $\ell_1(\Gamma; \mathcal{E})$, the space of $\mathcal{E}$-valued $\ell_1$ functions on $\Gamma$. Every absolutely summable family in a Banach space is summable (unconditionally convergent).
