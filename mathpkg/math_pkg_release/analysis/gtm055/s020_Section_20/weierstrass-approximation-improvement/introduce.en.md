Problem 18.P improves upon the classical Weierstrass approximation theorem by showing that polynomials are dense not just in $\mathcal{C}([a,b])$ with the uniform norm, but also in the space $\mathcal{C}^{(k)}([a,b])$ of $k$ times continuously differentiable functions with a stronger norm. Historically, Weierstrass himself proved this stronger version.
