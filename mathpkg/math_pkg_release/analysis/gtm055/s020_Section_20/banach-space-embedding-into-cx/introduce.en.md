Problem 18.H establishes that $\mathcal{C}(X)$ is "universal" for Banach spaces: every Banach space can be viewed as a subspace of some $\mathcal{C}(X)$. Furthermore, $X$ can be chosen metrizable exactly when $\mathcal{E}$ is separable. The proof uses the map $j(x)|_{(\mathcal{E}^*)_1}$ sending each $x \in \mathcal{E}$ to its evaluation functional restricted to the closed unit ball of the dual space.
