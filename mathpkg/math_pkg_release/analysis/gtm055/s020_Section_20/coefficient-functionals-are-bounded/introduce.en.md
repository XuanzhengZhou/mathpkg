Theorem 19.8 is a fundamental result in Banach space theory: the coefficient functionals of a Schauder basis are automatically continuous (bounded). This remarkable fact follows from the completeness of the coefficient sequence space (Proposition 19.7) and the open mapping theorem. It implies that bases provide a powerful structural link between a Banach space and sequence spaces.
