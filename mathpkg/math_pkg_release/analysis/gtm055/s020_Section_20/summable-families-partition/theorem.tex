Let $\{x_\gamma\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ be a summable family in a Banach space $\mathcal{E}$, and let $\{\Gamma_\alpha\}_{\alpha \in A}$ be a partition of the index set $\Gamma$. Then for each $\alpha$, the subfamily $\{x_\gamma\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma_\alpha}$ is summable, and the family of partial sums $\{s_\alpha = \sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma_\alpha} x_\gamma\}_{\alpha \in A}$ is summable with sum equal to the original total sum:
$$\sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma} x_\gamma = \sum_{\alpha \in A} \left( \sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma_\alpha} x_\gamma \right).$$
