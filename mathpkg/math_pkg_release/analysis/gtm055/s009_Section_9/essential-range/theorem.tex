Let $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ be a measure space and let $f$ be a complex-valued function that is defined and measurable $[\mu]$ on $X$. The \textbf{essential range} of $f$ with respect to $\mu$ is the set of all those complex numbers $\lambda$ with the property that the set
$$\{x \in X : |f(x) - \lambda| < \varepsilon\}$$
has positive measure for every $\varepsilon > 0$.
