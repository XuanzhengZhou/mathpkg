In any topological linear space $\mathcal{E}$ the following conditions are equivalent:

(i) The topology on $\mathcal{E}$ is induced by a countably determined separating family of pseudonorms,
(ii) The topology on $\mathcal{E}$ is induced by a countable separating family of pseudonorms,
(iii) $\mathcal{E}$ is locally convex and quasinormable,
(iv) $\mathcal{E}$ is locally convex and metrizable.
