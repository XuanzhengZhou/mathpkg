Let $(X, \mathbf{S})$ be a measurable space, let $\mathcal{E}$ be a normed space, and let $\Phi$ be a measurable $\mathcal{E}$-valued mapping on $X$. Then the function

$$N_{\Phi}(x) = \| \Phi(x) \|, \quad x \in X,$$

is also measurable on $(X, \mathbf{S})$.

Proof. The set $\{x \in X : \| \Phi(x) \| \leq r\}$ can be written as $\{x \in X : \Phi(x) \in \mathcal{E}_r\}$, $r > 0$, and the closed balls $\mathcal{E}_r$ are Borel sets in $\mathcal{E}$. (Recall Problem 6C.)
