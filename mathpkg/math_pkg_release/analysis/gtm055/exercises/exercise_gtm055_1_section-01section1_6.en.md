F. Let $\{A_\gamma\}$ and $\{B_\gamma\}$ be two similarly indexed families of subsets of a set $X$. Verify that both

$$\bigcup_{\gamma} A_\gamma \setminus \bigcup_{\gamma} B_\gamma$$ and $$\bigcap_{\gamma} A_\gamma \setminus \bigcap_{\gamma} B_\gamma$$

are subsets of $\bigcup_{\gamma} (A_\gamma \setminus B_\gamma)$. Verify, likewise, that

$$\bigcup_{\gamma} A_\gamma \nabla \bigcup_{\gamma} B_\gamma$$ and $$\bigcap_{\gamma} A_\gamma \nabla \bigcap_{\gamma} B_\gamma$$

are subsets of $\bigcup_{\gamma} (A_\gamma \nabla B_\gamma)$.
