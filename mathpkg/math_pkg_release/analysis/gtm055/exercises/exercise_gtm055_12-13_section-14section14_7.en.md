G. Let $M$ be a subset of a normed space $\mathcal{E}$, let $M$ be the subspace of $\mathcal{E}$ spanned by $M$, and let $\varphi$ be a mapping of $M$ into a normed space $\mathcal{F}$. Show that there exists a bounded linear transformation $T: \mathcal{M} \rightarrow \mathcal{F}$ that extends $\varphi$ if and only if there exists a positive constant $K$ such that

$$\left\| \sum_{i=1}^{n} \lambda_i \varphi(x_i) \right\| \leq K \left\| \sum_{i=1}^{n} \lambda_i x_i \right\|$$

for every linear combination $\lambda_1 x_1 + \cdots + \lambda_n x_n$ of vectors in $M$ (and every positive integer $n$).
