M. If $\Gamma$ is an arbitrary set, then the collection $\mathcal{D}$ of all finite subsets of $\Gamma$ is a directed set under the inclusion ordering. Let $\{\lambda_\gamma\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ be a family of complex numbers indexed by $\Gamma$, and for each $D$ in $\mathcal{D}$ define $\sigma_D = \sum_{\gamma \in D} \lambda_\gamma$.
