Let $D = D_R(\alpha)$ be a disc in $\mathbb{C}$ ($R > 0$), let $f$ be a function defined and analytic on $D$, and let $\gamma$ be a closed rectifiable arc in $D$. Then
$$w_{\gamma}(\lambda) f(\lambda) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{f(\zeta)}{\zeta - \lambda} \, d\zeta$$
for every $\lambda$ in $D$ that does not lie on the range of $\gamma$.
