Let $\mathcal{F}$ and $\mathcal{G}$ be quasinormed spaces, and suppose given a one-to-one continuous linear transformation $S$ of $\mathcal{F}$ into $\mathcal{G}$. Show that if $T$ is a linear transformation of an $F$-space $\mathcal{E}$ into $\mathcal{F}$ such that $ST$ is continuous, then $T$ is continuous. (Hint: Apply Corollary 13.5.)
