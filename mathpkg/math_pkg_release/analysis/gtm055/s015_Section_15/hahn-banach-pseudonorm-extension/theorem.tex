Let $\sigma$ be a pseudonorm on a linear space $\mathcal{E}$, let $\mathcal{M}$ be a linear submanifold of $\mathcal{E}$, and let $f$ be a linear functional defined on $\mathcal{M}$ that is bounded with respect to $\sigma$. Then there exists a linear functional $f_0$ defined on $\mathcal{E}$ that extends $f$, is bounded with respect to $\sigma$, and satisfies $\|f_0\|_\sigma = \|f\|_\sigma$.
