Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a linear space and let $\{\sigma_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}$ be a separating sequence of pseudonorms on $\mathcal{E}$. Then $\mathcal{E}$ is a Frechet space in the topology induced by $\{\sigma_n\}$ if and only if each sequence $\{x_p\}_{p=1}^{\infty}$ in $\mathcal{E}$ that is Cauchy with respect to every $\sigma_n$ is also convergent with respect to every $\sigma_n$ (to a limit independent of $n$).
