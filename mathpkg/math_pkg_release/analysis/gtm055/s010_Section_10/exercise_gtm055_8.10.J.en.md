Let $\mathbf{B}$ denote the $\sigma$-ring of Borel subsets of $\mathbb{R}$, and suppose given a measure $\nu$ on $(\mathbb{R}, \mathbf{B})$ which is finite on the bounded sets in $\mathbf{B}$. Let

$$f(t) = \begin{cases} \nu((0, t]), & t \geq 0, \\ -\nu((t, 0]), & t < 0. \end{cases}$$

Show that $f$ is a monotone increasing right-continuous function on $\mathbb{R}$ and that $\nu$ is the Stieltjes--Borel measure associated with $f$.
