Exercise H establishes the integral form of the Minkowski inequality, which interchanges an $L_p$ norm with an integral. The result states that the $L_p$ norm of an integrated function is bounded by the integral of its $L_p$ norms in the other variable. The proof reduces to the case of nonnegative simple functions of product form $\sum t_{ij} \chi_{E_i \times F_j}$, making it a consequence of the ordinary Minkowski inequality.
