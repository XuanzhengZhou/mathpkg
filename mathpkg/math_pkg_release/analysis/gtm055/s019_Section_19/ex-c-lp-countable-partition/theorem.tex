Let $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ be a measure space and let $p$ be an extended real number such that $1 \leq p \leq +\infty$. For a finite measurable partition $\{E_1, \ldots, E_m\}$ of $X$, $L_p(X)$ is naturally isometrically isomorphic to the direct sum
$$L_p(E_1) \oplus_p \cdots \oplus_p L_p(E_m).$$
State and prove the analogous result for a countable partition of $X$.
