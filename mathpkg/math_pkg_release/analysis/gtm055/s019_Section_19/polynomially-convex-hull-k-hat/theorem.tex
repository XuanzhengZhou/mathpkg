If $K$ is a compact subset of $\mathbb{C}$ and if $U_{\infty}$ denotes the unbounded component of $\mathbb{C} \setminus K$, then $\hat{K}$ denotes the compact set $\mathbb{C} \setminus U_{\infty}$, so that $\hat{K}$ consists of the union of $K$ with all of the various holes in $K$. The boundary of $\hat{K}$ (which is the same as the boundary of $U_{\infty}$) is called the outer boundary of $K$.
