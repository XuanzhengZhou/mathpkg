Proposition 18.11 establishes the basic relationship between two important subalgebras of $C(K)$ for compact $K \subset \mathbb{C}$: $\mathcal{P}(K)$, the uniform limits of polynomials on $K$, and $\mathcal{A}(K)$, the functions that are locally analytic on $K^\circ$ and continuous on $K$. Both are closed subalgebras, and every polynomial limit is analytic, giving $\mathcal{P}(K) \subset \mathcal{A}(K)$.
