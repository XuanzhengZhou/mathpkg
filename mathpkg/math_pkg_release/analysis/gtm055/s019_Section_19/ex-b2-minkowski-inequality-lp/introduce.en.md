Exercise B(ii) asks the reader to establish the triangle inequality for the $L_p$ pseudonorm, also known as the Minkowski inequality. The proof follows from the Hölder inequality (Exercise B(i)), which establishes that $\|fg\|_1 \leq \|f\|_p \|g\|_q$ for Hölder conjugates $p$ and $q$.
