The authors fix notation for the standard number systems: positive integers $\mathbb{N}$, nonnegative integers $\mathbb{N}_0$, all integers $\mathbb{Z}$, real numbers $\mathbb{R}$, and complex numbers $\mathbb{C}$. The empty set is denoted $\varnothing$, and the set-theoretic difference $X \setminus Y$ and symmetric difference $X \nabla Y$ are defined for arbitrary sets $X$ and $Y$. For a mapping $f: X \to Y$, the image $f(A)$ of a subset $A \subset X$ and the preimage $f^{-1}(B)$ of a subset $B \subset Y$ are defined in the usual way.
