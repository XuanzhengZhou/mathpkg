For a noetherian separated scheme, the Čech cohomology groups computed with respect to an open affine covering coincide with the derived functor cohomology groups. This result provides a practical computational method: instead of using injective resolutions, one can compute cohomology via the Čech complex associated to any affine open cover. The proof proceeds by embedding the quasi-coherent sheaf into a flasque quasi-coherent sheaf and using the long exact sequence of Čech cohomology, together with the vanishing of higher Čech cohomology for flasque sheaves.
