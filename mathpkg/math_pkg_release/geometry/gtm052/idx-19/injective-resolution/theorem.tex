An \text{injective resolution} of an object $A$ of an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$ is a complex $I^\bullet$, defined in degrees $i \geqslant 0$, together with a morphism $\varepsilon: A \to I^0$, such that each $I^i$ is an injective object of $\mathfrak{A}$, and the sequence
$$0 \to A \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} I^0 \to I^1 \to \cdots$$
is exact.
