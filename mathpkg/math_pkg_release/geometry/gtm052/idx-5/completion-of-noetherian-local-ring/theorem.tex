Let $A$ be a noetherian local ring with maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$, and let $\hat{A}$ be its $\mathfrak{m}$-adic completion.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\hat{A}$ is a local ring, with maximal ideal $\hat{\mathfrak{m}} = \mathfrak{m}\hat{A}$, and there is a natural injective homomorphism $A \to \hat{A}$.
\item[(b)] If $M$ is a finitely generated $A$-module, its completion $\hat{M}$ with respect to its $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology is isomorphic to $M \otimes_A \hat{A}$.
\item[(c)] $\dim A = \dim \hat{A}$.
\item[(d)] $A$ is regular if and only if $\hat{A}$ is regular.
\end{enumerate}
