Let $X$ be a projective variety of dimension $r$ in $\mathbf{P}^n$, with $n \geqslant r + 2$. Show that for suitable choice of $P \notin X$, and a linear $\mathbf{P}^{n-1} \subseteq \mathbf{P}^n$, the projection from $P$ to $\mathbf{P}^{n-1}$ (Ex. 3.14) induces a birational morphism of $X$ onto its image $X' \subseteq \mathbf{P}^{n-1}$.
