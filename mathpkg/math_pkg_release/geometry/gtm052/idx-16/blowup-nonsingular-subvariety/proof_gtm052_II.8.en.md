See Hartshorne [1, Ch. II, §8] for the complete proof. The statement (b) identifies the exceptional divisor $Y'$ with $\mathbf{P}(\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2)$ via the construction of the blowing-up as $\operatorname{Proj} \bigoplus_{d \geqslant 0} \mathcal{I}^d$.
