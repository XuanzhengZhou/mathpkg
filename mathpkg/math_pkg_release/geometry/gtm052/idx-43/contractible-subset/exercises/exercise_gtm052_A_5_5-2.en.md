Let $Y \cong \mathbf{P}^1$ be a curve on a surface $X$, with $Y^2 = -1$. Show that $Y$ is contractible to a nonsingular point (i.e., there exists a birational morphism $f: X \rightarrow X_0$ to a nonsingular projective surface $X_0$ such that $f(Y)$ is a point and $f|_{X - Y}$ is an isomorphism to $X_0 - f(Y)$).
