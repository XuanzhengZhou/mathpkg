Let $X$ be a surface with function field $K$. Show that every valuation ring $R$ of $K/k$ is one of the three kinds described in (II, Ex. 4.12). Hint: In case (3), let $f \in R$. Use (Ex. 5.1) to show that for all $i \gg 0$, $f \in \mathcal{O}_{X_i}$, so in fact $f \in R_0$.
