Let $\mathfrak{X}$ be a noetherian formal scheme, let $\mathfrak{J}$ be an ideal of definition, and for each $n$, let $Y_n$ be the scheme $(\mathfrak{X}, \mathcal{O}_{\mathfrak{X}} / \mathfrak{J}^n)$. Assume that the inverse system of groups $(\Gamma(Y_n, \mathcal{O}_{Y_n}))$ satisfies the Mittag-Leffler condition. Then show that the natural map

$$\Gamma(\mathfrak{X}, \mathcal{O}_{\mathfrak{X}}) \to \varprojlim \Gamma(Y_n, \mathcal{O}_{Y_n})$$

is surjective.
