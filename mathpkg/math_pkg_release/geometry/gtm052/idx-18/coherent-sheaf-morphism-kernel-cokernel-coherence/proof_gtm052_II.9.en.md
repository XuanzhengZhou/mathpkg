These questions are all local, in which case they reduce to (9.4). By Theorem 9.7, locally the coherent sheaves correspond to finitely generated modules over a complete noetherian ring, and the operations of kernel, cokernel, and image correspond to the same operations on modules. Since the category of finitely generated modules over a noetherian ring is abelian, the result follows.
