This is Proposition 1.1 from Chapter I, Section §1 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. It shows that the collection of algebraic sets in $\mathbf{A}^n$ satisfies the axioms for the closed sets of a topology. This topology is known as the Zariski topology.
