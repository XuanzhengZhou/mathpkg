This is a definition from Chapter I, Section §1 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. An algebraic set is the zero locus of a collection of polynomials in affine $n$-space over an algebraically closed field $k$. These are the fundamental closed sets of the Zariski topology on $\mathbf{A}^n$.
