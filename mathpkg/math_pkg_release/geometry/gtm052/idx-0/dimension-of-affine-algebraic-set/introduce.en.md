This is Proposition 1.7 from Chapter I, Section §1 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. It establishes the fundamental identification between the geometric dimension of an affine algebraic set (the maximal length of a chain of irreducible closed subsets) and the algebraic Krull dimension of its coordinate ring. This allows us to apply dimension theory of noetherian rings to algebraic geometry.
