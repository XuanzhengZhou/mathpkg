This is Theorem 1.8A from Chapter I, Section §1 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. It provides the fundamental link between the Krull dimension of a finitely generated domain over a field and the transcendence degree of its fraction field. Part (b) states that for such algebras, the dimension is catenary, meaning every maximal chain of prime ideals has the same length.
