A presheaf of abelian groups on a topological space $X$ assigns an abelian group to each open subset and restriction homomorphisms between them, respecting composition and identities. Categorically, it is a contravariant functor from the poset category of open subsets of $X$ (with inclusions as morphisms) to the category of abelian groups. Presheaves provide the algebraic foundation for tracking local data, and sheaves are presheaves satisfying additional gluing and locality conditions.
