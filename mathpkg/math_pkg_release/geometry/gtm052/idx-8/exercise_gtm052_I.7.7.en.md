Let $Y$ be a variety of dimension $r$ and degree $d > 1$ in $\mathbf{P}^n$. Let $P \in Y$ be a nonsingular point. Define $X$ to be the closure of the union of all lines $PQ$, where $Q \in Y, Q \neq P$.

(a) Show that $X$ is a variety of dimension $r + 1$.

(b) Show that $\deg X < d$. [Hint: Use induction on $\dim Y$.]
