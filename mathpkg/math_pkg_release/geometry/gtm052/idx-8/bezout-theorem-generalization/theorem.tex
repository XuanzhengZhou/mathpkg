Let $Y$ be a variety of dimension $\geqslant 1$ in $\mathbf{P}^n$, and let $H$ be a hypersurface not containing $Y$. Let $Z_1, \ldots, Z_s$ be the irreducible components of $Y \cap H$. Then

$$\sum_{j=1}^{s} i(Y,H; Z_j) \cdot \deg Z_j = (\deg Y)(\deg H).$$

Here $i(Y,H; Z_j) = \mu_{\mathfrak{p}_j}(S/(I_Y + I_H))$, where $\mathfrak{p}_j$ is the homogeneous prime ideal of $Z_j$, and $\mu$ is the multiplicity (length) of the module $S/(I_Y + I_H)$ at the minimal prime $\mathfrak{p}_j$.
