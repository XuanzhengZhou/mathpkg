Let $Y \subseteq \mathbf{A}^n$ be an affine variety with affine coordinate ring $A(Y)$. Then:

(a) $\mathcal{O}(Y) \cong A(Y)$;

(b) for each point $P \in Y$, let $\mathfrak{m}_P \subseteq A(Y)$ be the ideal of functions vanishing at $P$. Then $P \mapsto \mathfrak{m}_P$ gives a 1-1 correspondence between the points of $Y$ and the maximal ideals of $A(Y)$;

(c) for each $P$, $\mathcal{O}_P \cong A(Y)_{\mathfrak{m}_P}$, and $\dim \mathcal{O}_P = \dim Y$;

(d) $K(Y)$ is isomorphic to the quotient field of $A(Y)$, and hence $K(Y)$ is a finitely generated extension field of $k$, of transcendence degree $= \dim Y$.
