Proposition 3.3 shows that the standard open covering $\{U_i\}_{i=0}^n$ of projective space $\mathbf{P}^n$ consists of affine varieties, each isomorphic to $\mathbf{A}^n$. This is fundamental because it allows local computations on projective varieties to be carried out in affine charts. The isomorphism is given by dehomogenization with respect to the $i$-th coordinate.
