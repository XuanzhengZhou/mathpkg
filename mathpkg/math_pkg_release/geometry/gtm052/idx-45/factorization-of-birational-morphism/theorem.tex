Let $f: X' \to X$ be a birational morphism of nonsingular projective surfaces, and let
$$n(f) = \#\{\,C' \subseteq X' \mid C' \text{ is an irreducible curve and } f(C') \text{ is a point}\,\}.$$
Then $f$ factors into $n(f)$ monoidal transformations. In particular, if $n(f) = 0$, then $f$ is an isomorphism.
