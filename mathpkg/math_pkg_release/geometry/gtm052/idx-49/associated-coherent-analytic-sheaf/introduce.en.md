Given a coherent sheaf $\mathcal{F}$ on a scheme $X$ of finite type over $\mathbf{C}$, the associated coherent analytic sheaf $\mathcal{F}_h$ is constructed by locally representing $\mathcal{F}$ as the cokernel of a morphism of free sheaves, interpreting the defining matrix entries as holomorphic functions, and taking the analytic cokernel. This construction is compatible with the functor $X \mapsto X_h$ and is an essential part of the GAGA correspondence.
