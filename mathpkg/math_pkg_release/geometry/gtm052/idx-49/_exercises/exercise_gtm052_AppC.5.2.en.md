Let $X = \mathbf{P}_k^n$, where $k = \mathbf{F}_q$, and show from the definition of the zeta function that

$$Z(\mathbf{P}_n,t) = \frac{1}{(1-t)(1-qt) \cdots (1-q^n t)}$$

Verify the Weil conjectures for $\mathbf{P}_n$.
