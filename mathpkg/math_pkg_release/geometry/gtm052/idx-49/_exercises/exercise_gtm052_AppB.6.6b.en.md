Show that if $X$ and $Y$ are projective schemes over $\mathbf{C}$, and $\mathfrak{f}: X_h \rightarrow Y_h$ is a morphism of analytic spaces, then there exists a (unique) morphism $f: X \rightarrow Y$ with $f_h = \mathfrak{f}$.

[Hint: First reduce to the case $Y = \mathbf{P}^n$. Then consider the invertible analytic sheaf $\mathfrak{L} = \mathfrak{f}^* \mathcal{O}(1)$ on $X_h$, use (2.1), and the techniques of (II, §7).]
