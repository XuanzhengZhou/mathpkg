Let $X$ be a nonsingular projective variety of dimension $n$, and let $\Delta \subseteq X \times X$ be the diagonal. Show that $c_n(\mathcal{T}_X) = \Delta^2$ in $A^n(X)$, under the natural isomorphism of $X$ with $\Delta$.
