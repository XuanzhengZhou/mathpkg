Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a locally free sheaf of rank 2 on $\mathbf{P}^3$, with Chern classes $c_1, c_2$. Since $A(\mathbf{P}^3) = \mathbf{Z}[h]/(h^4)$, one can write $c_1 = ah$, $c_2 = bh^2$. Determine $a$ and $b$ and the dimensions of $H^i(\mathbf{P}^3, \mathcal{E})$ in terms of $a$ and $b$.
