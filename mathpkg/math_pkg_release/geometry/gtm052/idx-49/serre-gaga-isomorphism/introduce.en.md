Serre's GAGA theorem (Géométrie Algébrique et Géométrie Analytique) establishes that for projective schemes over $\mathbf{C}$, the functor $X \mapsto X_h$ induces an equivalence of categories of coherent sheaves and an isomorphism on all cohomology groups. This provides a complete dictionary between the algebraic and analytic categories in the projective case, allowing algebraic and analytic methods to be freely interchanged for projective varieties.
