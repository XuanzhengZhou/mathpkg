The second Weil conjecture is a functional equation relating $Z(t)$ and $Z(1/q^n t)$, analogous to the functional equation of the Riemann zeta function. The invariant $E$ is the self-intersection of the diagonal, which equals the topological Euler characteristic. This follows from Poincaré duality in $l$-adic cohomology.
