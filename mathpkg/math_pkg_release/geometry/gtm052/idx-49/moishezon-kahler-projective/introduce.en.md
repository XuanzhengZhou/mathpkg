Moishezon's theorem states that among compact complex manifolds, a Moishezon manifold (one with as many algebraically independent meromorphic functions as its dimension) that also admits a Kähler metric is necessarily projective algebraic. This provides a clean relationship between the Kähler, Moishezon, projective algebraic, and abstract algebraic categories of complex manifolds.
