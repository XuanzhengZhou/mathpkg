The proof follows from Serre's Theorem 2.1 (GAGA). Given a compact analytic subspace $\mathfrak{X} \subseteq \mathbf{P}_{\mathbf{C}}^n$, one considers the ideal sheaf of $\mathfrak{X}$ as a coherent analytic sheaf. By GAGA, this corresponds to a coherent algebraic sheaf on $\mathbf{P}^n$, which defines the subscheme $X$ with $X_h = \mathfrak{X}$.
