This elementary but crucial lemma in linear algebra relates the exponential generating function of traces of powers of an endomorphism to the determinant of $1 - arphi t$. It is the key step in converting the Lefschetz trace formula for the Frobenius endomorphism into the expression of the zeta function as an alternating product of characteristic polynomials.
