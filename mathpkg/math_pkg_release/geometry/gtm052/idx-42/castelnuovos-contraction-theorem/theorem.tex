Let $Y$ be a curve on a surface $X$ with $Y \cong \mathbf{P}^1$ and $Y^2 = -1$. Then there exists a morphism $f: X \to X_0$ to a nonsingular projective surface $X_0$, and a point $P \in X_0$, such that $X$ is isomorphic via $f$ to the monoidal transformation of $X_0$ with center $P$, and $Y$ is the exceptional curve.
