The notation $g_d^r$ is a classical shorthand used in the study of algebraic curves, particularly in the Italian school of algebraic geometry. It denotes a linear system of dimension $r$ and degree $d$ on a curve. For instance, a $g_2^1$ is a linear system of dimension $1$ and degree $2$, which characterizes hyperelliptic curves.
