According to (3.1), we must show that for each $P \in X$, $\dim|K - P| = \dim|K| - 1$.

Now $\dim|K| = \dim H^0(X, \omega_X) - 1 = g - 1$. On the other hand, since $X$ is not rational (as $g \geqslant 2$), for any point $P$, $\dim|P| = 0$, so by Riemann–Roch we find that $\dim|K - P| = g - 2$, as required.
