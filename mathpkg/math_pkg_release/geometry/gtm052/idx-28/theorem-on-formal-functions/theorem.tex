Let $f: X \to Y$ be a projective morphism of noetherian schemes, let $\mathcal{F}$ be a coherent sheaf on $X$, and let $y \in Y$. For each $n \ge 1$, set
$$X_n = X \times_Y \operatorname{Spec} \mathcal{O}_y / \mathfrak{m}_y^n, \qquad \mathcal{F}_n = v^* \mathcal{F},$$
where $v: X_n \to X$ is the natural map. Then for each $i \ge 0$, the natural map
$$(R^i f_* \mathcal{F})_y^{\wedge} \;\longrightarrow\; \varprojlim_n H^i(X_n, \mathcal{F}_n)$$
is an isomorphism, where $(R^i f_* \mathcal{F})_y^{\wedge}$ denotes the $\mathfrak{m}_y$-adic completion of the stalk.
