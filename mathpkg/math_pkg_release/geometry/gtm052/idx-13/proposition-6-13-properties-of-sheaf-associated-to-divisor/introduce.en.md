Proposition 6.13 establishes the fundamental properties of the sheaf $\mathcal{L}(D)$ associated to a Cartier divisor. It is always an invertible sheaf, the construction is compatible with the group structure (tensor product), and linear equivalence of divisors corresponds exactly to isomorphism of the associated invertible sheaves.
