Let $X$ be a nonsingular curve over $k$. The following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $X$ is complete;
\item[(ii)] every discrete valuation ring of $K/k$ has a unique center on $X$;
\item[(iii)] $X \cong t(C_K)$, the abstract nonsingular complete curve associated to the function field $K = K(X)$.
\end{enumerate}
