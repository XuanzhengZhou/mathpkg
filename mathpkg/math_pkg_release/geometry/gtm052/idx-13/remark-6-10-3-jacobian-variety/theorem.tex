For any complete nonsingular curve $X$, the group $\text{Cl}^0 X$ is isomorphic to the group of closed points of an abelian variety called the Jacobian variety of $X$. The dimension of the Jacobian variety is the genus of the curve. The whole divisor class group $\text{Cl} X$ is an extension of $\mathbb{Z}$ by the group of closed points of the Jacobian variety.
