Proposition 7.2 gives a practical criterion for when a morphism to projective space is a closed immersion. The condition is that the preimage of each standard affine open $D_+(x_i)$ is affine and the corresponding ring map is surjective. This is the global analogue of the fact that closed subschemes of affine schemes correspond to surjective ring maps.
