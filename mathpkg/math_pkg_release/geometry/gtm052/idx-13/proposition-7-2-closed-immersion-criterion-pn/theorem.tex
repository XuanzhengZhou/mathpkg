Let $\varphi: X \to \mathbf{P}_A^n$ be an $A$-morphism, with $X_i = \varphi^{-1}(U_i)$ where $U_i = D_+(x_i)$. Then $\varphi$ is a closed immersion if and only if:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] each $X_i$ is affine, and
\item[(2)] the corresponding ring maps $A[y_0,\ldots,\hat{y_i},\ldots,y_n]_{(x_i)} \to \Gamma(X_i, \mathcal{O}_{X_i})$ are surjective.
\end{enumerate}
