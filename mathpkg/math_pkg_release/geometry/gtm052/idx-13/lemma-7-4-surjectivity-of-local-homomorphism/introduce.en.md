Lemma 7.4 is a technical result in commutative algebra used in the proof of Proposition 7.3. It gives sufficient conditions for a local homomorphism of noetherian local rings to be surjective: isomorphism on residue fields, surjectivity on the Zariski tangent space, and finiteness of $B$ as an $A$-module. The proof uses Nakayama's lemma twice.
