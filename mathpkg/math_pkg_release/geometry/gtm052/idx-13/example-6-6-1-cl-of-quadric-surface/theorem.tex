Let $Q$ be the nonsingular quadric surface $xy = zw$ in $\mathbf{P}_k^3$. Then
$$\text{Cl} Q \cong \mathbb{Z} \oplus \mathbb{Z}.$$
$Q$ is isomorphic to $\mathbf{P}_k^1 \times_k \mathbf{P}_k^1$. If $p_1$ and $p_2$ are the projections of $Q$ onto the two factors, then the two families of lines correspond to $pt \times \mathbf{P}^1$ and $\mathbf{P}^1 \times pt$, which are of type $(1,0)$ and $(0,1)$ in $\text{Cl} Q = \mathbb{Z} \oplus \mathbb{Z}$.
