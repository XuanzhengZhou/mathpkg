A Cartier divisor generalizes the notion of a Weil divisor to arbitrary schemes (not necessarily integral or regular in codimension one). It is defined as a global section of the quotient sheaf $\mathcal{K}^*/\mathcal{O}^*$, which locally amounts to specifying a rational function up to units. On locally factorial schemes, Cartier divisors coincide with Weil divisors.
