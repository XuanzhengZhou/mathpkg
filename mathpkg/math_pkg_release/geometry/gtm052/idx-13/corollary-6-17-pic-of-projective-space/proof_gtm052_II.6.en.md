By (6.4), $\text{Cl} \mathbf{P}_k^n \cong \mathbb{Z}$, generated by the class of a hyperplane $H$. By (6.16), $\text{Cl} \mathbf{P}_k^n \cong \text{Pic} \mathbf{P}_k^n$. The generator of $\text{Cl} \mathbf{P}_k^n$ corresponds to $\mathcal{O}(1)$ in $\text{Pic} \mathbf{P}_k^n$, so every invertible sheaf is isomorphic to $\mathcal{O}(l)$ for some $l \in \mathbb{Z}$, and $\text{Pic} \mathbf{P}_k^n \cong \mathbb{Z}$.
