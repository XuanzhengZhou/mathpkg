Remark 7.4.4 points ahead to Chapter III, where Serre's cohomological criterion for ampleness is developed. An invertible sheaf $\mathcal{L}$ is ample if and only if for every coherent sheaf $\mathcal{F}$, the higher cohomology groups $H^i(X, \mathcal{F} \otimes \mathcal{L}^n)$ vanish for all $i > 0$ and sufficiently large $n$.
