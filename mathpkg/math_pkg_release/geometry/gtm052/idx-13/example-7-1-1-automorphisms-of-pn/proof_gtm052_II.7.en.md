Let $\varphi$ be a $k$-automorphism of $\mathbf{P}_k^n$. Since $\text{Pic} \mathbf{P}_k^n \cong \mathbb{Z}$ is generated by $\mathcal{O}(1)$ (6.17), we must have $\varphi^*(\mathcal{O}(1)) \cong \mathcal{O}(1)$. The global sections $x_0, \ldots, x_n$ of $\mathcal{O}(1)$ pull back to global sections $s_i = \varphi^*(x_i)$ of $\mathcal{O}(1)$, which are linear forms in $x_0, \ldots, x_n$. The $(n+1) \times (n+1)$ matrix expressing the $s_i$ in terms of the $x_j$ must be invertible, giving an element of $\text{PGL}(n,k)$.

The faithfulness follows by considering the images of the points $(1,0,\ldots,0), (0,1,0,\ldots,0), \ldots, (0,\ldots,0,1),$ and $(1,1,\ldots,1)$.
