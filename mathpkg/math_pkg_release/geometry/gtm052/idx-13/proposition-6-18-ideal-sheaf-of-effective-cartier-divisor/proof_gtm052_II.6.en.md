$\mathcal{L}(-D)$ is the subsheaf of $\mathcal{K}$ generated locally by $f_i$. Since $D$ is effective, the $f_i$ are actually in $\mathcal{O}_X$, so this is a subsheaf of $\mathcal{O}_X$, which is precisely the ideal sheaf $\mathcal{I}_Y$ of $Y$.
