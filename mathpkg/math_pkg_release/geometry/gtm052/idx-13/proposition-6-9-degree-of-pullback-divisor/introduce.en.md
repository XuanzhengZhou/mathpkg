Proposition 6.9 establishes the multiplicative behavior of the degree of a divisor under pullback by a finite morphism of nonsingular curves. The degree of the pullback $f^*D$ equals the degree of the morphism $f$ times the degree of $D$. This is a key tool for studying ramification and the Riemann-Hurwitz formula.
