Let $f: X \to Y$ be a projective morphism of noetherian schemes, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a coherent sheaf on $X$, flat over $Y$. Let $y \in Y$ be a point. For each $i \geq 0$, let

$$\varphi^i(y): R^i f_*(\mathcal{F}) \otimes k(y) \to H^i(X_y, \mathcal{F}_y)$$

be the natural base change map.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Assume that $\varphi^i(y)$ is surjective. Then $\varphi^i(y)$ is an isomorphism, and $\varphi^i(y')$ is an isomorphism for all $y'$ in a suitable neighborhood of $y$.

\item[(b)] Assume that $\varphi^i(y)$ is surjective. Then the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\varphi^{i-1}(y)$ is also surjective;
\item[(ii)] $R^i f_*(\mathcal{F})$ is locally free in a neighborhood of $y$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}
