Exercise 4.4 from Chapter V, Section 4 of Hartshorne constructs a proof of the associativity of the geometric group law on a smooth plane cubic curve. Part (a) establishes a key lemma about collinearity of certain intersection points derived from two intersecting lines. Part (b) uses this lemma to prove that the group operation defined via an inflection point $P_0$ is associative, confirming that the set of regular points of $C$ forms a group.
