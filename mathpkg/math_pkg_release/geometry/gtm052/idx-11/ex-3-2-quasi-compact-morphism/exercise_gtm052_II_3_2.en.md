A morphism $f: X \to Y$ of schemes is quasi-compact if there is a cover of $Y$ by open affines $V_i$ such that $f^{-1}(V_i)$ is quasi-compact for each $i$. Show that $f$ is quasi-compact if and only if for every open affine subset $V \subseteq Y$, $f^{-1}(V)$ is quasi-compact.
