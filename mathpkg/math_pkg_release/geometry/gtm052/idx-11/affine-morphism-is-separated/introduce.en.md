Proposition 4.1 of Hartshorne states that every morphism between affine schemes is separated. Since $X = \operatorname{Spec} A$, $Y = \operatorname{Spec} B$, the fiber product $X \times_Y X$ is also affine, isomorphic to $\operatorname{Spec} (A \otimes_B A)$. The diagonal morphism corresponds to the multiplication map $A \otimes_B A \to A$, which is surjective, hence a closed immersion.
