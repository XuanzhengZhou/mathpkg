Every Moishezon manifold which is Kähler is projective algebraic. Summing up, we have the following implications among properties of compact complex manifolds, and there are examples to show that no further implications are possible:

$$\text{Hodge} \iff \text{projective algebraic} \Rightarrow \text{abstract algebraic}$$

$$\text{Kähler} \Rightarrow \text{Moishezon}$$
