This is a corollary of Serre's GAGA theorem (2.1). Given a compact analytic subspace $\mathfrak{X} \subseteq \mathbf{P}_h^n$, one considers its ideal sheaf $\mathcal{I}_{\mathfrak{X}}$ as a coherent analytic sheaf. By Theorem 2.1, there exists a coherent algebraic sheaf $\mathcal{I}$ on $\mathbf{P}^n$ such that $\mathcal{I}_h \cong \mathcal{I}_{\mathfrak{X}}$. This $\mathcal{I}$ is an ideal sheaf defining a subscheme $X \subseteq \mathbf{P}^n$, and by construction $X_h = \mathfrak{X}$.
