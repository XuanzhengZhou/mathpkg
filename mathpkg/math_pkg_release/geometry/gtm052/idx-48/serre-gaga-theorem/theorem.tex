Let $X$ be a projective scheme over $\mathbf{C}$. Then the functor $h$ induces an equivalence of categories from the category of coherent sheaves on $X$ to the category of coherent analytic sheaves on $X_h$. Furthermore, for every coherent sheaf $\mathcal{F}$ on $X$, the natural maps

$$\alpha_i: H^i(X, \mathcal{F}) \rightarrow H^i(X_h, \mathcal{F}_h)$$

are isomorphisms, for all $i$.
