Ruled surfaces are classified by whether the normalized locally free sheaf $\mathcal{E}$ is decomposable or indecomposable. If $\mathcal{E} \cong \mathcal{O}_C \oplus \mathcal{L}$ splits, then $e = -\deg \mathcal{L} \geq 0$, and every nonnegative integer occurs. In the indecomposable case, the invariant $e$ is constrained to the interval $[-2g, 2g-2]$. This bound arises from the fact that any indecomposable $\mathcal{E}$ corresponds to a nontrivial extension class.
