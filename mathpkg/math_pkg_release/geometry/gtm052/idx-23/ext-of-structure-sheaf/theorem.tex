Let $(X,\mathcal{O}_X)$ be a ringed space. For any $\mathcal{G} \in \operatorname{Mod}(X)$ and all $i \geqslant 0$, we have:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $\mathcal{E}xt^0(\mathcal{O}_X,\mathcal{G}) = \mathcal{G}$;
\item[(b)] $\mathcal{E}xt^i(\mathcal{O}_X,\mathcal{G}) = 0$ for $i > 0$;
\item[(c)] $\operatorname{Ext}^i(\mathcal{O}_X,\mathcal{G}) \cong H^i(X,\mathcal{G})$.
\end{itemize}
