This proposition establishes how Ext groups and Ext sheaves interact with tensor products by locally free sheaves. When the first argument is tensored with a locally free sheaf $\mathcal{L}$ of finite rank, the Ext can be "moved" to the second argument by tensoring with the dual $\mathcal{L}^\vee$. For Ext sheaves, the result further simplifies to tensoring the Ext sheaf itself with $\mathcal{L}^\vee$. This is a fundamental computational tool for Ext in algebraic geometry.
