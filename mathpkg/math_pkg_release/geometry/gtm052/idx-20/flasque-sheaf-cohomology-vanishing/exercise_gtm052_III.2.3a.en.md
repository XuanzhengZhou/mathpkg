**Cohomology with Supports (Grothendieck [7]).** Let $X$ be a topological space, let $Y$ be a closed subset, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a sheaf of abelian groups. Let $\Gamma_Y(X, \mathcal{F})$ denote the group of sections of $\mathcal{F}$ with support in $Y$ (II, Ex. 1.20).

Show that $\Gamma_Y(X, \cdot)$ is a left exact functor from $\mathfrak{Ab}(X)$ to $\mathfrak{Ab}$.
