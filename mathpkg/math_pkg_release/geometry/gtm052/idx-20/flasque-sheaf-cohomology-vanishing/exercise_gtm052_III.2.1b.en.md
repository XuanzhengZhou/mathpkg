More generally, let $Y \subseteq X = \mathbf{A}_k^n$ be the union of $n + 1$ hyperplanes in suitably general position, and let $U = X - Y$. Show that $H^n(X, \mathbf{Z}_U) \neq 0$. Thus the result of (2.7) is the best possible.
