**Proof.** By Theorem 6.9, $C_K$ is isomorphic to a nonsingular projective curve, hence in particular it is quasi-projective. Since every abstract nonsingular curve is of the form $C_K$ for some $K$, the first statement follows. For the second statement, if $Y$ is a nonsingular quasi-projective curve with function field $K$, then $Y$ embeds as an open subset of $C_K$ by Theorem 6.7, and $C_K$ is projective by Theorem 6.9.
