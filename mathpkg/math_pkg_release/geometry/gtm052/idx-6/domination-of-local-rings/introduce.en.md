Domination is a partial order on local rings contained in a common field. A local ring $B$ dominates $A$ if $A$ is a subring of $B$ and the maximal ideal of $B$ restricts to the maximal ideal of $A$. This notion is crucial for characterizing valuation rings: a local ring is a valuation ring if and only if it is maximal with respect to domination.
