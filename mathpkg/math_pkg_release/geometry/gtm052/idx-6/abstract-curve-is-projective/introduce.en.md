Theorem 6.9 is the main result of Section 6: the abstract nonsingular curve $C_K$, constructed purely from the function field $K/k$ as the set of all DVRs, is itself isomorphic to a nonsingular projective curve. This solves problem (b) from the introduction --- finding a nonsingular projective model in each birational equivalence class --- and shows that the abstract and geometric viewpoints are equivalent.
