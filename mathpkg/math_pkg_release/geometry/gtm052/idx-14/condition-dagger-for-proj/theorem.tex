Let $X$ be a noetherian scheme and let $\mathcal{S}$ be a sheaf of graded $\mathcal{O}_X$-algebras satisfying $(\dagger)$. Then $\mathcal{S} \cong \bigoplus_{d \geqslant 0} \mathcal{S}_d$, where $\mathcal{S}_d$ is the homogeneous part of degree $d$, $\mathcal{S}_0 = \mathcal{O}_X$, $\mathcal{S}_1$ is a coherent $\mathcal{O}_X$-module, and $\mathcal{S}$ is locally generated by $\mathcal{S}_1$ as an $\mathcal{O}_X$-algebra.
