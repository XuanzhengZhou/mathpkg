Let $\mathcal{S}$ be a sheaf of graded algebras on a scheme $X$ satisfying $(\dagger)$. Let $\mathcal{L}$ be an invertible sheaf on $X$, and define a new sheaf of graded algebras $\mathcal{S}' = \mathcal{S} * \mathcal{L}$ by $\mathcal{S}'_d = \mathcal{S}_d \otimes \mathcal{L}^d$ for each $d \geqslant 0$. Then $\mathcal{S}'$ also satisfies $(\dagger)$, and there is a natural isomorphism $\varphi: P' = \operatorname{Proj} \mathcal{S}' \xrightarrow{\cong} P = \operatorname{Proj} \mathcal{S}$, commuting with the projections $\pi$ and $\pi'$ to $X$, and having the property that
$$\mathcal{O}_{P'}(1) \cong \varphi^*\mathcal{O}_P(1) \otimes \pi'^*\mathcal{L}.$$
