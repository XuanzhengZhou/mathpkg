The Fermat curve $X: x^3 + y^3 = z^3$ gives a nonsingular curve in characteristic $p$ for every $p \neq 3$. Determine the set $\mathfrak{P} = \{p \neq 3 \mid X_{(p)} \text{ has Hasse invariant } 0\}$, and observe (modulo Dirichlet's theorem) that it is a set of primes of density $\frac{1}{2}$.
