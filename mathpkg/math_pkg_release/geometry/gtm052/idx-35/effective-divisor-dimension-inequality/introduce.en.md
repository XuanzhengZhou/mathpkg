Lemma 5.5 from Chapter IV, Section 5 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry establishes a basic inequality for the dimensions of linear systems on a curve: the dimension of the sum of two complete linear systems is at least the sum of their individual dimensions. Though simple in statement, this lemma is a crucial technical tool, providing the additive estimate needed in the proof of Clifford's theorem and the classification of curves via their canonical embeddings.
