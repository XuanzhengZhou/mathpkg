Let $f: X \rightarrow Y$ be a morphism of schemes.

(a) If $\mathcal{G}$ is a quasi-coherent sheaf of $\mathcal{O}_Y$-modules, then $f^*\mathcal{G}$ is a quasi-coherent sheaf of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules.

(b) If $X$ and $Y$ are noetherian, and if $\mathcal{G}$ is coherent, then $f^*\mathcal{G}$ is coherent.

(c) Assume that either $X$ is noetherian, or $f$ is quasi-compact and separated. Then if $\mathcal{F}$ is a quasi-coherent sheaf of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules, $f_*\mathcal{F}$ is a quasi-coherent sheaf of $\mathcal{O}_Y$-modules.
