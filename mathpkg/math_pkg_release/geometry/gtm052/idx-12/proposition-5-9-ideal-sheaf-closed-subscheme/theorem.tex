Let $X$ be a scheme. For any closed subscheme $Y$ of $X$, the corresponding ideal sheaf $\mathcal{I}_Y$ is a quasi-coherent sheaf of ideals on $X$. If $X$ is noetherian, it is coherent. Conversely, any quasi-coherent sheaf of ideals on $X$ is the ideal sheaf of a uniquely determined closed subscheme of $X$.
