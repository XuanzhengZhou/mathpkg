Let $X = \operatorname{Spec} A$ be an affine scheme, let $f \in A$, let $D(f) \subseteq X$ be the corresponding open set, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a quasi-coherent sheaf on $X$.

(a) If $s \in \Gamma(X, \mathcal{F})$ is a global section of $\mathcal{F}$ whose restriction to $D(f)$ is $0$, then for some $n > 0$, $f^n s = 0$.

(b) Given a section $t \in \mathcal{F}(D(f))$ of $\mathcal{F}$ over the open set $D(f)$, then for some $n > 0$, $f^n t$ extends to a global section of $\mathcal{F}$ over $X$.
