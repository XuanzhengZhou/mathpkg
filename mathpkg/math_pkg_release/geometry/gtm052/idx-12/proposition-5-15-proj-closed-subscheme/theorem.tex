(a) Every closed subscheme of $\mathbf{P}_A^r$ is of the form $\operatorname{Proj} S/I$ for some homogeneous ideal $I \subseteq S = A[x_0, \ldots, x_r]$.

(b) A scheme $Y$ over $\operatorname{Spec} A$ is projective if and only if it is isomorphic to $\operatorname{Proj} S$ for some graded ring $S$, where $S_0 = A$, and $S$ is finitely generated by $S_1$ as an $S_0$-algebra.
