Proposition 5.12 establishes the key properties of the Serre twisting sheaf on $X = \operatorname{Proj} S$. When $S$ is generated by $S_1$ as an $S_0$-algebra, the sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X(n)$ is invertible (locally free of rank 1). Moreover, the twisting operation $\mathcal{F} \mapsto \mathcal{F}(n)$ is compatible with the tilde construction for graded modules: $	ilde{M}(n) \cong (M(n))^{\sim}$. As a corollary, the tensor product of twisting sheaves satisfies $\mathcal{O}_X(n) \otimes \mathcal{O}_X(m) \cong \mathcal{O}_X(n+m)$, giving the twisting operation the structure of a $\mathbf{Z}$-action on the category of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules.
