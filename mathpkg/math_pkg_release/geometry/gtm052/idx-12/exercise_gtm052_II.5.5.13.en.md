Let $S$ be a graded ring, generated by $S_1$ as an $S_0$-algebra. For any integer $d > 0$, let $S^{(d)}$ be the graded ring $\bigoplus_{n \geqslant 0} S_n^{(d)}$ where $S_n^{(d)} = S_{nd}$. Let $X = \operatorname{Proj} S$. Show that $\operatorname{Proj} S^{(d)} \cong X$, and that the sheaf $\mathcal{O}(1)$ on $\operatorname{Proj} S^{(d)}$ corresponds via this isomorphism to $\mathcal{O}_X(d)$.

This construction is related to the $d$-uple embedding (I, Ex. 2.12) in the following way. If $x_0, \ldots, x_r$ is a set of generators for $S_1$, corresponding to an embedding $X \hookrightarrow \mathbf{P}_A^r$, then the set of monomials of degree $d$ in the $x_i$ gives an embedding of $X$ in a larger projective space.
