The plurigenera $P_n(X)$ are fundamental numerical invariants of a projective nonsingular variety, defined as the dimensions of the spaces of global sections of powers of the canonical sheaf $\omega_X^{\otimes n}$. They generalize the geometric genus $p_g = P_1$ and play a central role in the classification of algebraic varieties, particularly through Kodaira dimension. A key property is that the plurigenera are birational invariants: birationally equivalent nonsingular projective varieties have the same $P_n$ for all $n$.
