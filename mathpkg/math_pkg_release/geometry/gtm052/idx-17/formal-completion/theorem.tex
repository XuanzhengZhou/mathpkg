Let $X$ be a noetherian scheme and let $Y \subseteq X$ be a closed subscheme defined by a sheaf of ideals $\mathcal{I} \subseteq \mathcal{O}_X$. The \text{formal completion of $X$ along $Y$}, denoted $(\hat{X}, \mathcal{O}_{\hat{X}})$, is the ringed space whose underlying topological space is $Y$ and whose structure sheaf is
$$\mathcal{O}_{\hat{X}} = \varprojlim_{n} \mathcal{O}_X / \mathcal{I}^n,$$
where each $\mathcal{O}_X / \mathcal{I}^n$ is regarded as a sheaf of rings on $Y$, forming an inverse system via the natural quotient maps.
