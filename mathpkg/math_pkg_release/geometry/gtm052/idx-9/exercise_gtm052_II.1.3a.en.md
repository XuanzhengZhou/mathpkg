Let $\varphi: \mathcal{F} \to \mathcal{G}$ be a morphism of sheaves on $X$. Show that $\varphi$ is surjective if and only if the following condition holds: for every open set $U \subseteq X$, and for every $s \in \mathcal{G}(U)$, there is a covering $\{U_i\}$ of $U$, and there are elements $t_i \in \mathcal{F}(U_i)$, such that $\varphi(t_i) = s|_{U_i}$ for all $i$.
