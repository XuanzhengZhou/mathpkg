Let $S$ be a graded ring, $S_+ = \bigoplus_{d > 0} S_d$, and let $\text{Proj } S$ be the set of all homogeneous prime ideals $\mathfrak{p}$ of $S$ that do not contain all of $S_+$. For a homogeneous ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ of $S$, define $V(\mathfrak{a}) = \{\mathfrak{p} \in \text{Proj } S \mid \mathfrak{p} \supseteq \mathfrak{a}\}$.

(a) If $\mathfrak{a}$ and $\mathfrak{b}$ are homogeneous ideals in $S$, then $V(\mathfrak{ab}) = V(\mathfrak{a}) \cup V(\mathfrak{b})$.

(b) If $\{\mathfrak{a}_i\}$ is any family of homogeneous ideals of $S$, then $V(\sum \mathfrak{a}_i) = \bigcap V(\mathfrak{a}_i)$.
