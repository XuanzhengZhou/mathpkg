Proposition 2.5 constructs the structure sheaf on $\text{Proj } S$ and proves that projective spectra are schemes. Part (a) gives the locally ringed space structure, analogous to $\text{Spec } A$. Part (b) provides affine open coverings by $D_+(f) \cong \text{Spec } S_{(f)}$ where $S_{(f)}$ is the degree-zero part of $S_f$, establishing that $\text{Proj } S$ is locally affine. Part (c) concludes that $\text{Proj } S$ is a scheme. This construction generalizes projective varieties from Chapter I: for $S = k[x_0,\ldots,x_n]$, $\text{Proj } S = \mathbb{P}^n_k$.
