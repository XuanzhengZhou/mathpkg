Let $Y$ be a hypersurface in $X = \mathbf{P}_k^N$ with $N \geqslant 4$, where $k$ is an algebraically closed field. Let $\hat{X}$ be the formal completion of $X$ along $Y$ (II, \S 9). Prove that the natural map $\operatorname{Pic} \hat{X} \to \operatorname{Pic} Y$ is an isomorphism.

*Hint:* Use (II, Ex. 9.6), and then study the maps $\operatorname{Pic} X_{n+1} \to \operatorname{Pic} X_n$ for each $n$ using (Ex. 4.6) and (Ex. 5.5).
