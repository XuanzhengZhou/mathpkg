Let $f: X \to Y$ be a morphism of schemes, let $y \in Y$, and let $\mathcal{O}_y$ be the local ring of $Y$ at $y$ with maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}_y$. For each $n \geqslant 1$, define
$$
X_n = X \times_Y \operatorname{Spec} \mathcal{O}_y / \mathfrak{m}_y^n.
$$
For $n = 1$, $X_1 = X_y$ is the fibre of $f$ over $y$. For $n > 1$, $X_n$ is a scheme with nilpotent elements having the same underlying topological space as $X_y$. It is called the $n$-\text{th thickened fibre} of $X$ over $y$.

Let $\mathcal{F}$ be a coherent sheaf on $X$. Define
$$
\mathcal{F}_n = v^*\mathcal{F},
$$
where $v: X_n \to X$ is the natural morphism.
