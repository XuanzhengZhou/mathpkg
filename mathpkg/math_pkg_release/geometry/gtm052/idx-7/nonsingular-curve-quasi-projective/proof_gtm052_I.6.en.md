This follows immediately from Theorem 6.9. For the first statement, the abstract nonsingular curve $C_K$ is projective by Theorem 6.9, hence quasi-projective. For the second statement, a nonsingular quasi-projective curve is birationally equivalent to $C_K$, and by (6.8) the birational map extends to an open immersion.
