Lemma 12.3 is a purely homological algebra result that provides a replacement for a bounded-above complex $C^\bullet$ of $A$-modules (with finitely generated cohomology) by a complex $L^\bullet$ of finitely generated free $A$-modules with isomorphic cohomology. When additionally each $C^i$ is flat, the isomorphism extends after tensoring with any $A$-module $M$. This lemma is the crucial algebraic input for Proposition 12.1, allowing one to replace the Cech complex of a sheaf with a complex of free modules while preserving cohomological information.
