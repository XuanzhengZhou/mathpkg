The blowing-up of $\mathbf{A}^n$ at the origin $O$ is a fundamental birational construction. The blow-up $X \subseteq \mathbf{A}^n \times \mathbf{P}^{n-1}$ is defined by the equations $x_i y_j = x_j y_i$, which encode that the point $(y_1, \ldots, y_n) \in \mathbf{P}^{n-1}$ lies on the line through $O$ in the direction of $(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$. Away from the origin, $\varphi: X \to \mathbf{A}^n$ is an isomorphism, while the fiber over $O$ is a full $\mathbf{P}^{n-1}$ representing all tangent directions at $O$. This construction is the local model for blowing up arbitrary subvarieties of varieties.
