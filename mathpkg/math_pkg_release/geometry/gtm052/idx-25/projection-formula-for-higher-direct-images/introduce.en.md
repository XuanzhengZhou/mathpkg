The projection formula for higher direct images states that tensor product with a locally free sheaf of finite rank pulled back from the base commutes with $R^i f_*$. More precisely, given a morphism $f: X \to Y$ of ringed spaces, a sheaf $\mathcal{F}$ on $X$, and a locally free sheaf $\mathcal{E}$ on $Y$ of finite rank, there is a natural isomorphism $R^i f_*(\mathcal{F} \otimes f^*\mathcal{E}) \cong R^i f_*(\mathcal{F}) \otimes \mathcal{E}$. This is presented as an exercise in Hartshorne.
