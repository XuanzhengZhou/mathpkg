Let $f: X \to Y$ be a morphism of ringed spaces, let $\mathcal{F}$ be an $\mathcal{O}_X$-module, and let $\mathcal{E}$ be a locally free $\mathcal{O}_Y$-module of finite rank. Prove the projection formula (cf. (II, Ex. 5.1))
$$R^i f_*(\mathcal{F} \otimes f^*\mathcal{E}) \cong R^i f_*(\mathcal{F}) \otimes \mathcal{E}.$$
