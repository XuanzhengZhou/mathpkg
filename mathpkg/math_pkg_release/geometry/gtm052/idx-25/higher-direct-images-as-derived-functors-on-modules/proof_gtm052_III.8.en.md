To calculate the derived functors of $f_*$ on $\mathfrak{Mod}(X)$, we use resolutions by injective objects of $\mathfrak{Mod}(X)$. Any injective of $\mathfrak{Mod}(X)$ is flasque by (2.4), hence acyclic for $f_*$ on $\mathfrak{Ab}(X)$ by (8.3), so they can be used to calculate $R^i f_*$.
