Localization with respect to any multiplicative system yields a flat algebra. The localization $S^{-1}A$ is flat over $A$ because the localization map $M \to S^{-1}M$ is an isomorphism after tensoring with $S^{-1}A$, and more concretely because $S^{-1}A \otimes_A M \cong S^{-1}M$ is exact in $M$. This flatness is fundamental to the construction of schemes and the study of quasi-coherent sheaves.
