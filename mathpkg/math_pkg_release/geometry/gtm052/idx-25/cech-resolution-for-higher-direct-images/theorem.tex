Let $f: X \to Y$ be a morphism of separated noetherian schemes. Let $\mathcal{F}$ be a quasi-coherent sheaf on $X$, let $\mathfrak{U} = (U_i)$ be an open affine cover of $X$, and let $\mathcal{C}'(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{F})$ be the Čech resolution of $\mathcal{F}$. Then for each $p \geqslant 0$,
$$R^p f_*(\mathcal{F}) \cong h^p(f_*\mathcal{C}'(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{F})).$$
