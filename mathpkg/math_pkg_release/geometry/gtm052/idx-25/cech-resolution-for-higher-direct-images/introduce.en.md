For a separated noetherian scheme $X$ with quasi-coherent sheaf $\mathcal{F}$, the higher direct images $R^p f_*(\mathcal{F})$ can be computed via the Čech resolution with respect to any open affine cover. The resolution complex $\mathcal{C}'(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{F})$ pushed forward by $f_*$ yields a complex whose cohomology sheaves are exactly $R^p f_*(\mathcal{F})$. This provides a practical computational tool by reducing to Čech cohomology on affine covers.
