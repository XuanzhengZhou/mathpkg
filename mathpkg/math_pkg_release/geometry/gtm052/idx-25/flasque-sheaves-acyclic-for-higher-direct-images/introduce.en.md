Flasque sheaves are acyclic for the higher direct image functors $R^i f_*$ for all $i > 0$. This follows because the restriction of a flasque sheaf to any open subset remains flasque, and flasque sheaves have vanishing higher cohomology on any open set. Consequently, flasque resolutions can be used to compute the derived functors of $f_*$.
