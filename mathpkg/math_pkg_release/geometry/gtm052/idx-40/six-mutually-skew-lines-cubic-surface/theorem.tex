Let $X$ be a nonsingular cubic surface, and let $E'_1, \ldots, E'_6$ be any subset of six mutually skew lines chosen from among the 27 lines on $X$. Then $E'_1, \ldots, E'_6$ can be blown down to points $P'_1, \ldots, P'_6$ in $\mathbf{P}^2$, giving an isomorphism of $X$ with $\mathbf{P}^2$ blown up at $P'_1, \ldots, P'_6$.
\end{proof}
