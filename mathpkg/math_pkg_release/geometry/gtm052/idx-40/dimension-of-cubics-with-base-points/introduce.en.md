This corollary gives the dimensions of linear systems of plane cubics through $r$ points in general position. For $r \leqslant 7$, the system has no unassigned base points by Proposition 4.3, so each prescribed point drops the dimension by exactly one from 9. For $r = 8$, the dimension is 1, with only finitely many base points (there are only finitely many ways to pass three lines or one line and one irreducible conic through 8 points in general position).
