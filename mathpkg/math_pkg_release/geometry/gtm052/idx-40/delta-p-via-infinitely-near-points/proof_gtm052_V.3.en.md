Working with the infinitesimal neighborhood of one point at a time, each blow-up of a point of multiplicity $r$ reduces the arithmetic genus by $\frac{1}{2}r(r-1)$ by (3.7). Summing these contributions over all infinitely near singular points $Q$ lying over $P$ (including $P$ itself) yields the formula

$$\delta_P = \sum_{Q} \frac{1}{2} r_Q(r_Q - 1).$$
