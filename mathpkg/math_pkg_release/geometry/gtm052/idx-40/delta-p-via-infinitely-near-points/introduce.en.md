This result gives a local formula for the delta-invariant $\delta_P$ of a curve singularity, expressed as a sum over all infinitely near singular points lying above $P$. The formula follows from working with the infinitesimal neighborhood of one point at a time, applying the genus drop formula $\frac{1}{2}r(r-1)$ at each blow-up. The configuration of infinitely near points and their multiplicities completely determines $\delta_P$.
