This theorem establishes that the blow-up of $\mathbf{P}^2$ at six points in general position is isomorphic to a nonsingular cubic surface in $\mathbf{P}^3$. The embedding is given by the linear system of plane cubics through the six points, and the canonical sheaf is the negative of the hyperplane bundle. This gives a concrete construction of cubic surfaces and shows that their anticanonical embedding realizes them as cubic hypersurfaces in $\mathbf{P}^3$.
