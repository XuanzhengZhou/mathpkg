This exercise establishes that the sheaf Ext functor preserves coherence and quasi-coherence properties on a noetherian scheme. Specifically, if both sheaves are coherent then $\mathcal{E}xt^i(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G})$ is coherent for all $i$, and if $\mathcal{F}$ is coherent and $\mathcal{G}$ quasi-coherent then the Ext sheaf is quasi-coherent. This is essential for working with Ext sheaves in algebraic geometry.
