The projective dimension $\operatorname{pd}(M)$ of a module $M$ is the minimum length of a projective resolution of $M$, or $+\infty$ if no finite projective resolution exists. A projective resolution is an exact sequence ending in $M$ with all intermediate terms projective. This invariant measures how far $M$ is from being projective and is fundamental in homological algebra.
