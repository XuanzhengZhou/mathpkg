For a closed subscheme $X$ of projective space $P = \mathbf{P}_k^N$ of codimension $r$, the sheaf $\omega_X = \mathcal{E}xt^r_P(\mathcal{O}_X, \omega_P)$ serves as a dualizing sheaf. This lemma establishes a functorial isomorphism $\operatorname{Hom}_X(\mathcal{F}, \omega_X) \cong \operatorname{Ext}^r_P(\mathcal{F}, \omega_P)$ for any $\mathcal{O}_X$-module $\mathcal{F}$, which is a crucial step in the proof of Serre duality for projective schemes.
