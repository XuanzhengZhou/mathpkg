Let $f: X \to Y$ be a finite morphism of noetherian schemes. Duality for a Finite Flat Morphism.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] For any quasi-coherent $\mathcal{O}_Y$-module $\mathcal{G}$, $\mathcal{H}om_Y(f_*\mathcal{O}_X, \mathcal{G})$ is a quasi-coherent $f_*\mathcal{O}_X$-module, hence corresponds to a quasi-coherent $\mathcal{O}_X$-module, which we call $f^!\mathcal{G}$ (II, Ex. 5.17e).

\item[(b)] Show that for any coherent $\mathcal{F}$ on $X$ and any quasi-coherent $\mathcal{G}$ on $Y$, there is a natural isomorphism

$$f_*\mathcal{H}om_X(\mathcal{F}, f^!\mathcal{G}) \xrightarrow{\sim} \mathcal{H}om_Y(f_*\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}).$$
\end{enumerate}
