This exercise constructs the cohomology class $\eta(Y) \in H^p(X, \Omega_X^p)$ of a closed subvariety $Y$ of pure codimension $p$ in a projective nonsingular variety $X$, using Serre duality and trace maps. It verifies that for points $\eta(P)$ is the canonical generator, for projective space $\eta(Y) = (\deg Y) \cdot 1$, and for divisors $\eta(Y)$ equals the Chern class $c(\mathcal{L}(Y))$ via the $d\log$ map.
