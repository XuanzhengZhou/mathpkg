Let $X$ be a nonsingular projective variety of dimension $n$. For any $p = 0, 1, \ldots, n$, let $\Omega^p = \bigwedge^p \Omega_X$ be the sheaf of differential $p$-forms. Then for each $p, q = 0, 1, \ldots, n$, we have a natural isomorphism

$$H^q(X, \Omega^p) \cong H^{n-q}(X, \Omega^{n-p})^\vee.$$
