Let $\mathcal{F}$ and $\mathcal{G}$ be coherent sheaves on a projective scheme $X$ over a noetherian ring $A$, with $\mathcal{O}_X(1)$ a very ample invertible sheaf. Then for each $i \geq 0$, there exists an integer $n_0 > 0$ such that for every $n \geq n_0$,

$$\operatorname{Ext}^i(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}(n)) \cong \Gamma(X, \mathcal{E}xt^i(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}(n))).$$
