For a projective scheme $X$ over a noetherian ring, the global Ext functor $\operatorname{Ext}^i(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}(n))$ can be computed via the sheaf Ext. Specifically, for sufficiently large twists $n$, the natural map from global Ext to global sections of the Ext sheaf becomes an isomorphism. This result links the homological algebra of coherent sheaves on $X$ with their local Ext sheaves.
