A closed immersion $f: Y \to X$ is a morphism of schemes that is a homeomorphism onto a closed subset of $X$ and whose structure sheaf map is surjective. This is the scheme-theoretic generalization of a closed subvariety. The surjectivity condition ensures that the structure sheaf of $Y$ is a quotient of that of $X$, corresponding to an ideal sheaf.
