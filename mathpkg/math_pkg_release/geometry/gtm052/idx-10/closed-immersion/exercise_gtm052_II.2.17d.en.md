Let $f: Y \to X$ be a morphism of schemes, and suppose that $f$ induces a homeomorphism of $\operatorname{sp}(Y)$ onto a closed subset of $\operatorname{sp}(X)$. Prove the converse to (c), namely, if $f: Y \to X$ is a homeomorphism onto a closed subset, and the sheaf map $f': \mathcal{O}_X \to f_*\mathcal{O}_Y$ is surjective, then the corresponding ring homomorphism $\varphi$ is surjective. [Hint: Consider $X' = \operatorname{Spec}(A / \ker \varphi)$ and use (b) and (c).]
