A morphism $f: X \to Y$ of schemes is locally of finite type if there exists a covering of $Y$ by open affine subsets $V_i = \operatorname{Spec} B_i$, such that for each $i$, $f^{-1}(V_i)$ can be covered by open affine subsets $U_{ij} = \operatorname{Spec} A_{ij}$, where each $A_{ij}$ is a finitely generated $B_i$-algebra.
