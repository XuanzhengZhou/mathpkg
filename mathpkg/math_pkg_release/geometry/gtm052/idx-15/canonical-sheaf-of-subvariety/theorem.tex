Let $Y$ be a nonsingular subvariety of codimension $r$ in a nonsingular variety $X$ over $k$. Then

$$\omega_Y \cong \omega_X \otimes \bigwedge^r \mathcal{N}_{Y/X},$$

where $\omega_X = \bigwedge^{\dim X} \Omega_{X/k}$ is the canonical sheaf, $\mathcal{N}_{Y/X} = \mathcal{H}om_{\mathcal{O}_Y}(\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2, \mathcal{O}_Y)$ is the normal sheaf, and $\bigwedge^r$ denotes the $r$-th exterior power.

Equivalently, using $\bigwedge^r \mathcal{N}_{Y/X} \cong (\bigwedge^r \mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2)^{-1}$, we have

$$\omega_Y \cong \omega_X \otimes (\bigwedge^r \mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2)^{-1}.$$
