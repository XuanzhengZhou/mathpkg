This theorem characterizes nonsingular closed subschemes of a nonsingular variety using the sheaf of differentials. A subvariety $Y \subset X$ is nonsingular precisely when $\Omega_{Y/k}$ is locally free and the conormal sequence is exact on the left. In this case the ideal sheaf $\mathcal{I}$ is locally generated by $\operatorname{codim}(Y,X)$ elements, and the locally free sheaf $\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2$ (the conormal sheaf) has rank equal to the codimension. This provides the foundation for the theory of normal bundles and canonical sheaves on subvarieties.
