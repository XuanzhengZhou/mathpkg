The exact sequence of sheaves of differentials for a composition of morphisms $X \xrightarrow{f} Y \xrightarrow{g} Z$ globalizes the first exact sequence of Kähler differentials (Prop. 8.3A). It yields a right-exact sequence $f^* \Omega_{Y/Z} \to \Omega_{X/Z} \to \Omega_{X/Y} \to 0$ of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules, connecting the relative differentials of the three schemes. This is a fundamental computational tool for studying differentials on schemes.
