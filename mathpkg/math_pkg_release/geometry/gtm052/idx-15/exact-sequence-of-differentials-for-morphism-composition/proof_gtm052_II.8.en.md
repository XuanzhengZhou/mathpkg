This follows directly from Proposition 8.3A (the first exact sequence for rings) by taking affine open covers. On each affine open subset, the sequence of sheaves restricts to the sheafification of the first exact sequence of Kähler differentials for the corresponding ring homomorphisms. Since exactness of sheaves of modules is a local property, the global sequence follows.
