Let $S = A[x_0, \ldots, x_n]$ and consider the graded $S$-module $M$ defined by the exact sequence

$$0 \to M \to S(-1)^{n+1} \xrightarrow{\psi} S \to 0,$$

where $\psi(e_i) = x_i$ for the standard basis $e_0, \ldots, e_n$ of $S(-1)^{n+1}$. We show that $\tilde{M} \cong \Omega_{X/Y}$.

On the standard affine open set $U_i = D_+(x_i) \cong \operatorname{Spec} A[x_0/x_i, \ldots, x_n/x_i]$, the sheaf $\Omega_{X/Y}|_{U_i}$ is a free $\mathcal{O}_{U_i}$-module generated by $d(x_0/x_i), \ldots, d(x_n/x_i)$. Define an isomorphism $\varphi_i: \Omega_{X/Y}|_{U_i} \to \tilde{M}|_{U_i}$ by

$$\varphi_i(d(x_j/x_i)) = (1/x_i^2)(x_i e_j - x_j e_i).$$

On the intersection $U_i \cap U_j$, the relation

$$d\left(\frac{x_k}{x_i}\right) - \frac{x_k}{x_j} d\left(\frac{x_j}{x_i}\right) = \frac{x_j}{x_i} d\left(\frac{x_k}{x_j}\right)$$

holds in $\Omega|_{U_i \cap U_j}$. Applying $\varphi_i$ and $\varphi_j$ to the respective sides yields the same expression $(1/x_i x_j)(x_j e_k - x_k e_j)$, so the isomorphisms glue to give a global isomorphism $\varphi: \Omega_{X/Y} \cong \tilde{M}$.
