Base change for relative differentials establishes that the formation of Kähler differentials commutes with extension of the base ring. If $A'$ is an $A$-algebra and $B' = B \otimes_A A'$, then $\Omega_{B'/A'} \cong \Omega_{B/A} \otimes_B B'$. An analogous formula holds for localization: if $S \subset B$ is a multiplicative set, then $\Omega_{S^{-1}B/A} \cong S^{-1}\Omega_{B/A}$.
