Let $f: X \to Y$ be a morphism of schemes. Let $\Delta: X \to X \times_Y X$ be the diagonal morphism. Then $\Delta$ gives an isomorphism of $X$ onto its image $\Delta(X)$, which is a locally closed subscheme of $X \times_Y X$, i.e., a closed subscheme of an open subset $W$ of $X \times_Y X$. Let $\mathcal{I}$ be the sheaf of ideals of $\Delta(X)$ in $W$. Then the **sheaf of relative differentials** of $X$ over $Y$ is

$$\Omega_{X/Y} = \Delta^*(\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2).$$

It is a quasi-coherent $\mathcal{O}_X$-module. If $Y$ is noetherian and $f$ is of finite type, then $\Omega_{X/Y}$ is coherent.
