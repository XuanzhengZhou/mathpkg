This lemma shows that a torsion-free module of finite type over a local integral domain is free if its rank, measured by the dimension of the fiber $M \otimes_A k$, is constant. The proof uses Nakayama's lemma to lift $r$ generators and then a dimension argument with the quotient field $K$ to conclude the module is isomorphic to $A^r$. It is a crucial technical step for relating the local freeness of the sheaf of differentials to the regularity of local rings.
