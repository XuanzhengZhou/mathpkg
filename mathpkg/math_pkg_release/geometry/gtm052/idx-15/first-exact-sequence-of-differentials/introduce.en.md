The first exact sequence of differentials relates the modules of relative differentials for a composition of ring homomorphisms $A \to B \to C$. It yields a right-exact sequence $\Omega_{B/A} \otimes_B C \to \Omega_{C/A} \to \Omega_{C/B} \to 0$ of $C$-modules, where the first map is induced by functoriality and the second is the natural projection. This sequence is the algebraic foundation for the corresponding exact sequence of sheaves of differentials on schemes.
