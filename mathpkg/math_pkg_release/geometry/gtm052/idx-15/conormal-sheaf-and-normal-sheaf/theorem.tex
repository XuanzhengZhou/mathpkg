Let $Y$ be a nonsingular subvariety of a nonsingular variety $X$ over $k$. The locally free sheaf $\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2$ of (8.17) is called the **conormal sheaf** of $Y$ in $X$. Its dual

$$\mathcal{N}_{Y/X} = \mathcal{H}om_{\mathcal{O}_Y}(\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2, \mathcal{O}_Y)$$

is called the **normal sheaf** of $Y$ in $X$. It is locally free of rank $r = \operatorname{codim}(Y, X)$. Dualizing the exact sequence of (8.17) yields

$$0 \to \mathcal{T}_Y \to \mathcal{T}_X \otimes \mathcal{O}_Y \to \mathcal{N}_{Y/X} \to 0,$$

where $\mathcal{T} = \mathcal{H}om(\Omega, \mathcal{O})$ denotes the tangent sheaf.
