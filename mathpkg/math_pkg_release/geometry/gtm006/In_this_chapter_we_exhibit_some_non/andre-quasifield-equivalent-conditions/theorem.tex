Let $F_\phi$ be an André quasifield. The following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $F_\phi$ is a division ring;
\item $F_\phi$ is a field;
\item $F_\phi$ is isomorphic to $F$;
\item $k^\phi = \varepsilon$ for all $k \in N$, where $\varepsilon$ is the identity of $\Gamma$.
\end{enumerate}
