Lemma 9.8 provides an explicit formula for the associator in the constructed division ring $D$. The associator measures the failure of associativity. The formula reveals that the obstruction to associativity is controlled by whether $a$ is fixed by $\theta$ and whether $b$ is fixed by $\theta$. This lemma is the key technical tool for analyzing when $D$ is associative, leading to Lemma 9.9.
