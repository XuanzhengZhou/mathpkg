**Exercise 9.12.** Show that the mapping $\theta$ defined on the quaternions by $(x + iy + jz + kt)^\theta = x - iy - jz - kt$ is an anti-automorphism, and that $(x + iy + jz + kt)^{1+\theta}$ is a real number and, hence, is in the centre of $Q$.
