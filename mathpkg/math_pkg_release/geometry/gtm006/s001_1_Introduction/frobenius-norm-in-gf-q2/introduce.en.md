This result describes the norm map associated to the Frobenius automorphism x ↦ x^q on the quadratic extension GF(q^2) over GF(q). The automorphism has order 2, and its norms (elements of the form x·x^q = x^{q+1}) are exactly the elements of the base field GF(q).
