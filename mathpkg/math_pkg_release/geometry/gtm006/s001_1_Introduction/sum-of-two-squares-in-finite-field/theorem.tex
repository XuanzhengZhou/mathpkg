Every element of $GF(q)$ is a sum of two squares.

More precisely, let $K = GF(q)$ where $q = p^n$ for some prime $p$ and positive integer $n$.
\begin{itemize}
\item[(iii)] If $p=2$ then every element of $GF(q)$ is a square.
\item[(iv)] Every element of $GF(q)$ is a sum of two squares.
\end{itemize}
