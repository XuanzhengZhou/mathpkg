A semiregular permutation group has the property that non-identity elements have no fixed points. This is a strong condition on the action: all orbits have size equal to the group order. Semiregularity is a key ingredient in the definition of Frobenius groups and plays an important role in the study of regular collineation groups (Singer groups) of finite projective planes.
