The Frobenius automorphism $x \mapsto x^q$ of $\mathrm{GF}(q^2)$ has order 2 and its fixed field is $\mathrm{GF}(q)$. The norm map $N(x) = x^{q+1}$ maps $\mathrm{GF}(q^2)^*$ onto $\mathrm{GF}(q)^*$. This result is essential for constructing unitary (hermitian) polarities in projective planes over finite fields.
