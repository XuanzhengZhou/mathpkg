Result 1.4 collects the fundamental properties of finite (Galois) fields. Part (ii) asserts the cyclicity of the multiplicative group, which is essential for studying primitive elements and powers. Parts (iii)-(iv) describe the quadratic structure: squares form an index-2 subgroup in odd characteristic, and remarkably every element is a sum of two squares regardless of the field. These properties are used throughout the book for constructing and analyzing finite projective planes.
