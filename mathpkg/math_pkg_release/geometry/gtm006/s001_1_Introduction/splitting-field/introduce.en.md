A splitting field of a polynomial $f(x)$ over $K$ is the smallest field extension of $K$ in which $f(x)$ splits completely into linear factors. Splitting fields are central to Galois theory and provide the construction of finite fields $GF(p^n)$ as splitting fields of $x^{p^n} - x$ over $GF(p)$.
