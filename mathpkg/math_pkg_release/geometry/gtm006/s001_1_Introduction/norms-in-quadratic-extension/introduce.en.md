Result 1.7 characterizes norms in the quadratic extension of finite fields. The Frobenius automorphism $x \mapsto x^q$ generates the Galois group of $GF(q^2)/GF(q)$, and its norms are precisely the $(q+1)$-st powers of elements of $K^*$, which constitute exactly the subfield $GF(q)$. This result is essential for constructing unitary polarities and unitals in finite projective planes.
