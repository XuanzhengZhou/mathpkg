Let $A_1, A_2, \dots, A_k$ be the points of $\mathcal{H}$ -- the centres of non-trivial homologies in $\Gamma$ with axis $\ell$. Put $h_i = |\Gamma_{(A_i,\ell)}|$ for $i = 1, \dots, k$, so the number of non-identity homologies with axis $\ell$ is $\sum_{i=1}^k (h_i - 1)$.

The proof involves counting arguments and the action of $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$ on $\mathcal{H}$. Since $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$ must leave $\mathcal{H}$ invariant and $|\mathcal{H}| = k = |\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}|$ (after careful counting), $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$ is transitive on the points of $\mathcal{H}$.
