We recall that $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$ permutes the lines of $\mathcal{P}^\ell$ through any point $A$ on $\ell$. Part (i) follows since any elation fixing a line $m \neq \ell$ must have centre at $\ell m$. For parts (ii) and (iii), the orbit-stabilizer theorem applied to the action of $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$ on the $n$ lines through a point of $\ell$ (other than $\ell$ itself) yields the bounds.
