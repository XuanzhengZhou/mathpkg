By Theorem 4.25, $\mathcal{H}$ is an orbit under $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$. Since $A$ and $B$ are both in $\mathcal{H}$, there exists an elation in $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$ mapping $A$ to $B$. This elation lies in the group generated by $\alpha$ and $\beta$, as the orbit action is realized through the group structure.
