Let $\alpha$ be a $(V,\ell)$-perspectivity of a projective plane $\mathcal{P}$ and let $\mathcal{P}_0$ be a subplane of $\mathcal{P}$ such that $V \in \mathcal{P}_0$ and $\ell \in \mathcal{P}_0$. If, for some point $A$ of $\mathcal{P}_0$, $A^\alpha \in \mathcal{P}_0$ and $A^\alpha \neq A$, show that $\mathcal{P}_0^\alpha = \mathcal{P}_0$, i.e. that $\alpha$ leaves $\mathcal{P}_0$ invariant.
