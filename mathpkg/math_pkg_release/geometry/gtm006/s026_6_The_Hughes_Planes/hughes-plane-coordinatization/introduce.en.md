Lemma 9.16 establishes the explicit correspondence between the coordinates $(u,v)$, $(m)$, and $(\infty)$ used in the planar ternary ring coordinatization of Chapter V and the homogeneous coordinates $\langle (x_1, x_2, x_3) \rangle$ used to define the Hughes plane. The proof uses the choice of reference points $(\infty) = (0,0,1)$, $(0) = (1,0,0)$, $(0,0) = (0,1,0)$, and $(1) = (1,0,-1)$, together with the line equation $x + yt + z = 0$ for lines through $(1,0,-1)$, to identify affine point coordinates with their homogeneous representations.
