Lemma 9.16 provides the explicit coordinatization formulas for the Hughes plane $\mathcal{H}$. After fixing the standard reference points $(\infty) = (0,0,1)$, $(0) = (1,0,0)$, $(0,0) = (0,1,0)$, and $(1) = (1,0,-1)$, the lemma states that an affine point $(u,v)$ corresponds to the homogeneous triple $(u,1,v)$, the slope point $(m)$ on the line at infinity corresponds to $(1,0,-m)$, and the point at infinity on the $y$-axis remains as $(0,0,1)$. These formulas are essential for computing the ternary ring operations of $\mathcal{H}$.
