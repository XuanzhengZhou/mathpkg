Lemma 10.3 examines the restriction of the derived affine plane structure to a single retained line $k$ (one that meets $\ell_{\infty}$ in $\mathcal{S}'$). The induced incidence structure $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}'}(k)$ has points equal to the $n^2$ points of $k$, and its "lines" are the intersections of $k$ with the affine Baer subplanes belonging to $\mathcal{S}$. Each such intersection contains exactly $n$ points, and through any two points of $k$ there is a unique such intersection, yielding an affine plane of order $n$.
