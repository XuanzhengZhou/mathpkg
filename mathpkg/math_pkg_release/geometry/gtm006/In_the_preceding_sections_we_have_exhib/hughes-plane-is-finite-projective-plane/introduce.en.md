Theorem 9.14 of GTM006 establishes that the incidence structure $\mathcal{H}$ constructed from a nearfield $N$ of order $q^2$ satisfies the axioms of a finite projective plane. The proof proceeds by counting the number of points ($q^4+q^2+1$), verifying each line contains exactly $q^2+1$ points, and confirming that the number of lines is also $q^4+q^2+1$. The theorem then reduces to showing that any two distinct lines intersect in a unique point, completing the verification of the projective plane axioms.
