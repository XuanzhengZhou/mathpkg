Let $\mathcal{P} = \mathcal{P}_2(K)$ be a projective plane over a field $K$. Any two non-empty unitals in $\mathcal{P}$ are equivalent under the action of the collineation group $PGL(V)$. Consequently, the unitary group (the subgroup of $PGL(V)$ fixing a unital) is independent of the particular non-empty unital chosen.
