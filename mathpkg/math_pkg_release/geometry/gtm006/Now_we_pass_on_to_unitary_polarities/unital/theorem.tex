Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a projective plane over a field $K$ with an involutory automorphism $\phi$, and let $\beta$ be a unitary polarity on $\mathcal{P}$. The incidence structure consisting of the set of absolute points of $\beta$ together with the set of non-absolute lines of $\beta$ is called a \text{unital}, denoted $\mathcal{U}$.
