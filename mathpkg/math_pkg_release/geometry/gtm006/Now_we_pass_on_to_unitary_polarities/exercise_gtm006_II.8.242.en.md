Using the terminology of Exercise 2.39, show that the discriminant of a line $\langle v \rangle + \langle w \rangle$ with respect to a unital can only vary by a non-zero norm factor (i.e., an element of the form $k^{1+\phi}$ for some $k \in K^*$) for different choices of the points $\langle v \rangle$ and $\langle w \rangle$ on the line.
