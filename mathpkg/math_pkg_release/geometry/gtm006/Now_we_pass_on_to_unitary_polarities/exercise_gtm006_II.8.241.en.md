Let $K = GF(q^2)$. Show that:

(a) The number of points in a unital $\mathcal{U}$ is $q^3 + 1$.

(b) The number of lines of $\mathcal{U}$ is $q^2(q^2 - q + 1)$.

(c) Every point of $\mathcal{U}$ is on exactly $q^2$ lines of $\mathcal{U}$.
