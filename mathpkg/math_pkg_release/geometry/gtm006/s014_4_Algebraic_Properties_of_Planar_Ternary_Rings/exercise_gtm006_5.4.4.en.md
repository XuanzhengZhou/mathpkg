Let $R$ be the set of rationals and let $T$ be given by $T(a, b, c) = ab + c$. Show that $(R, T)$ is a planar ternary ring. If $R_0$ is the subset of integers, show that $(R_0, T)$ is a ternary subring which is not planar.
