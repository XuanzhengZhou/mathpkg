Let $\mathcal{S}$ be a derivation set for an affine plane $\mathcal{A}$, and let $\mathcal{A}_1 = \mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}}(\mathcal{A})$ be the derived plane. The **inherited collineation group** of $\mathcal{A}_1$ is the collineation group induced by all collineations of $\mathcal{A}$ that leave $\mathcal{S}$ invariant.
