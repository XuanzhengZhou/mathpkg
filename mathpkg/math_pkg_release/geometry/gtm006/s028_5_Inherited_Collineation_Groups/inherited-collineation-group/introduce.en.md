The inherited collineation group of a derived affine plane $\mathcal{A}_1 = \mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}}(\mathcal{A})$ is the subgroup of the full collineation group of $\mathcal{A}_1$ consisting of all collineations that are induced by collineations of the original plane $\mathcal{A}$ which leave the derivation set $\mathcal{S}$ invariant. In general, the inherited group is only a subgroup of the full collineation group, but for important examples such as finite Hall planes of order greater than $9$, the inherited group coincides with the full collineation group.
