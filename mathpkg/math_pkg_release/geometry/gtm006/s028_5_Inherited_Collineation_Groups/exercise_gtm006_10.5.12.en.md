If $W_1, W_2, \dots, W_s$ is a partial spread and if $U_1, U_2, \dots, U_i$ is a replacement for $W_1, W_2, \dots, W_t$ (with $t \leq s$), show that $U_1, U_2, \dots, U_i, W_{t+1}, \dots, W_s$ is a partial spread.
