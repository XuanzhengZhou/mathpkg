This corollary to Theorem 10.18 asserts that for any finite Hall plane of order $q^2 > 9$, the full collineation group coincides with the inherited collineation group -- that is, the subgroup of $\operatorname{Aut}(\mathcal{A}_H(q^2))$ that preserves the derivation set $\mathcal{S} = \{ m \mid m = \infty \text{ or } m \in \operatorname{GF}(q^2) \}$. Consequently, every collineation of such a Hall plane arises from a collineation of the underlying desarguesian plane that stabilizes the derivation set.
