This corollary bounds the order of any collineation of a finite projective plane of order $n$ by $n^2 + n + 1$, which is the total number of points (and lines) in the plane. The bound is best possible, as the desarguesian plane $\mathcal{P}_2(q^n)$ admits a collineation (a Singer cycle) of order $q^{2n} + q^n + 1$.
