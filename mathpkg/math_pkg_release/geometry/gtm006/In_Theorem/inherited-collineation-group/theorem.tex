Let $\mathcal{S}$ be a derivation set for an affine plane $\mathcal{A}$ and let $\mathcal{A}_1 = \mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{g}}(\mathcal{A})$ be the derived plane. The \textit{inherited collineation group} of $\mathcal{A}_1$ is the collineation group induced by all collineations of $\mathcal{A}$ that leave $\mathcal{S}$ invariant.
