The inherited collineation group of a derived affine plane $\mathcal{A}_1$ consists of all collineations of $\mathcal{A}_1$ that are induced by collineations of the original plane $\mathcal{A}$ preserving the derivation set $\mathcal{S}$. This group is generally a proper subgroup of the full collineation group of $\mathcal{A}_1$, but for certain important examples such as finite Hall planes of order greater than 9, it coincides with the full collineation group. Determining the inherited group provides crucial information about the structure of the derived plane.
