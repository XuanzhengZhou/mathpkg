This follows directly from Theorem 10.18 together with the orbit structure of special points. The inherited collineation group of $\mathcal{A}_H(q^2)$ is the subgroup of the full collineation group of the desarguesian plane that leaves the derivation set $\mathcal{S}$ invariant. Theorem 10.18 shows that the full collineation group of the Hall plane has the same orbit structure on special points as this inherited subgroup, and a counting argument using the size of the orbits forces equality of the two groups when $q^2 > 9$.
