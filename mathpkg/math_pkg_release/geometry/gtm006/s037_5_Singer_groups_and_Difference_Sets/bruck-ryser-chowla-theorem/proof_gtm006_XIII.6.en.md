*Proof.* The proof of the Bruck-Ryser-Chowla theorem is not included in the main text; it is referenced in the appendix/supplementary material. The original proof uses number-theoretic methods involving the Hasse-Minkowski theory of quadratic forms and the Hilbert symbol. See:

1. Bruck, R.H. and Ryser, H.J.: The non-existence of certain finite projective planes. *Canad. J. Math.* 1, 88-93 (1949).
2. Chowla, S. and Ryser, H.J.: Combinatorial problems. *Canad. J. Math.* 2, 93-99 (1950).
