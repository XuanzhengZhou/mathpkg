Lemma 13.12 is the fundamental combinatorial property of difference sets arising from projective planes. It says $\mathcal{D}$ is a $(v,k,\lambda)$-difference set with $\lambda = 1$: every non-identity group element has exactly one representation as a difference (or quotient) of two elements from $\mathcal{D}$. This property is the basis for constructing projective planes from abelian groups.
