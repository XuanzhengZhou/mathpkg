A faithful orbit is an orbit of a permutation group on which the group acts faithfully. For a collineation group of a finite projective plane, Theorem 13.1 guarantees the existence of at least one faithful orbit -- specifically, every orbit of maximal length is faithful. For abelian groups, an orbit is faithful if and only if its length equals the order of the group.
