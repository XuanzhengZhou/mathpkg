Let $\mathcal{P}^{l_{\infty}}$ be a finite affine plane with a collineation group $\Pi$. Show that $\Pi$ is transitive on the affine lines of $\mathcal{P}^{l_{\infty}}$ if and only if $\Pi$ is transitive on the points of $\mathcal{P}^{l_{\infty}}$.
