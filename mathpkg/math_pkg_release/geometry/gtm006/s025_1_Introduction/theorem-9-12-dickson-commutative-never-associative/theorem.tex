Let $D$ be the Dickson commutative division ring constructed from $F = GF(p^n)$ ($p$ an odd prime, $n > 1$) with non-square $a \in F$ and automorphism $\theta$. Then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $D$ is a division ring (every non-zero element has a multiplicative inverse),
\item[(ii)] $D$ is commutative,
\item[(iii)] $D$ is never associative.
\end{enumerate}
