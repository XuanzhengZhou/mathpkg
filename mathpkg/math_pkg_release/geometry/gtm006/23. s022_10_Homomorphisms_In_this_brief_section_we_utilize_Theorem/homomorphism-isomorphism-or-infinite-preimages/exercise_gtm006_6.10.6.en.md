Let $(R_1, T_1)$ be the planar ternary ring of rational numbers, and let $(R_2, T_2)$ be the planar ternary ring $\mathrm{GF}(p)$, where $p$ is a prime. Let $\mathcal{P}_1$ and $\mathcal{P}_2$ be the projective planes coordinatized by $(R_1, T_1)$ and $(R_2, T_2)$ respectively. Analyze the possible homomorphisms from $\mathcal{P}_1$ onto $\mathcal{P}_2$ and determine the structure of the sets $Z$, $J$, and $\overline{R}_1$ in this case.
