*Proof.* It is straightforward to verify that $\lambda_a$ is a perspectivity with axis $W$ and centre $E_3$. The group of such mappings $\{\lambda_a\}$ is transitive on the points of any line through $E_3$ (excluding $E_3$ and the intersection with $W$). If there were any additional perspectivities with this property, the same counting argument as in Lemma 2.54 would lead to a contradiction. The composition law $\lambda_a \lambda_b = \lambda_{ab}$ follows directly from the definition. $\square$
