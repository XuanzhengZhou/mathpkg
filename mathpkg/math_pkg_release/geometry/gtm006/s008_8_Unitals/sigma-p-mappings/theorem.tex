Let $P = E_3 = \langle e_3 \rangle$. Then the stabilizer $\Sigma_P$ of $P$ in $\Sigma$ is the group of all mappings $\lambda_a$ defined for $a \neq 0$ in $K$ by:
$$\lambda_a: \langle x_1 e_1 + x_2 e_2 + x_3 e_3 \rangle \mapsto \langle x_1 e_1 + x_2 e_2 + x_3 a e_3 \rangle.$$
Moreover, $\lambda_a \lambda_b = \lambda_{ab}$, so this group is isomorphic to the multiplicative group $K^*$.
