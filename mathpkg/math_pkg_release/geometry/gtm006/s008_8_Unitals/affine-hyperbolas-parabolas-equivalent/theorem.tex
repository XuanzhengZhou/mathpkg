Under the action of the affine collineation group $\text{Aut}\,\mathcal{A}$, all affine hyperbolas are equivalent, and all affine parabolas are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] Any two affine hyperbolas (conics meeting the line at infinity in two points) are related by an element of $\text{Aut}\,\mathcal{A}$.
\item[(ii)] Any two affine parabolas (conics meeting the line at infinity in exactly one point) are related by an element of $\text{Aut}\,\mathcal{A}$.
\end{enumerate}
