Lemma 2.67 completes the characterization of regular abelian collineation groups: if $\Gamma$ acts regularly on points, then it also acts regularly on lines. Combined with Lemmas 2.65 and 2.66, this establishes a strong duality in the action of abelian Singer groups on projective planes.
