The norm-one subgroup $N$ consists of all non-zero elements of $K$ whose product with their image under an involutory automorphism $\phi$ equals $1$. This subgroup plays a fundamental role in the study of unitary polarities in projective planes. Over a finite field $\text{GF}(q^2)$, $N$ is a cyclic group of order $q+1$, which corresponds to the kernel of the norm map $K^* \to F^*$.
