Let $K = \text{GF}(q^2)$. Show that the number of points in a unital $\mathcal{U}$ is $q^3 + 1$, the number of lines meeting $\mathcal{U}$ is $q^2(q^2 - q + 1)$, and every point of $\mathcal{U}$ lies on $q^2$ lines that meet $\mathcal{U}$.
