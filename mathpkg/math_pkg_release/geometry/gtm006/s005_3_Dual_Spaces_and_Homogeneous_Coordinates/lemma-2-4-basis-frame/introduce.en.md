Given any basis of the underlying vector space $V$, one can construct a frame for $\mathcal{P}(V)$ by taking the lines generated by the basis vectors together with the line generated by their sum. Conversely (Lemma 2.5), every frame arises in this way from some basis, unique up to a common scalar multiple.
