Let $V$ be a left vector space over a skewfield $K$ and let $N = \{\gamma \in \Gamma L(V) \mid \langle v^\gamma \rangle = \langle v \rangle \text{ for all } v \in V\}$. Then:
$$P\Gamma L(V) \cong \Gamma L(V)/N, \quad PGL(V) \cong GL(V)/(N \cap GL(V))$$
and, if $K$ is a field, $PSL(V) \cong SL(V)/(N \cap SL(V))$.
