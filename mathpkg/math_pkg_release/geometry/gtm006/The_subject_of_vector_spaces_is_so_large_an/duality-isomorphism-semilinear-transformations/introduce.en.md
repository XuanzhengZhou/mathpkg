Result 1.26 establishes the fundamental duality between a left vector space $V$ and its dual right vector space $V'$. The mapping $\alpha \mapsto \alpha'$ sending a semi-linear transformation to its contragredient (inverse transpose composed with the automorphism) is an isomorphism of the semi-linear groups that is involutive ($\alpha'' = \alpha$) and respects the evaluation pairing. This duality is central to the representation of collineations in projective geometry.
