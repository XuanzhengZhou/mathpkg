For a finite division ring $D$, the autotopism group $\Delta$ has a normal subgroup $\Delta_1$ consisting of autotopisms that act trivially on all semi-nuclei. The quotient $\Delta/\Delta_1$ is soluble, a key structural result that is used to establish the solubility of the full autotopism group under certain conditions.
