Show that if $\mathcal{P}_1$ and $\mathcal{P}_2$ are isomorphic division ring planes with respective autotopism triangles, then by a suitable choice of collineation one can obtain an isomorphism $\alpha$ from $\mathcal{P}_1$ onto $\mathcal{P}_2$ with $(\infty)^\alpha = (\infty)'$, $(0)^\alpha = (0)'$, and $(0,0)^\alpha = (0,0)'$.
