Every autotopism of a division ring plane gives rise to a triple $(P, Q, R)$ of non-singular additive mappings satisfying $(xP)(yQ) = (xy)R$. The set of all such triples forms a group under component-wise composition. This group structure is fundamental to the algebraic study of autotopisms.
