An element of order $2$ in $\Delta_2$ (the subgroup of $\Delta_1$ consisting of autotopisms where $P, Q, R$ are all linear transformations) either induces a Baer involution of $\mathcal{P}(D)$ or is one of the mappings
$$(A, A, I), \quad (A, 1, A), \quad (I, A, A)$$
where $A = R_{-1} = L_{-1}$ (multiplication by $-1$).
