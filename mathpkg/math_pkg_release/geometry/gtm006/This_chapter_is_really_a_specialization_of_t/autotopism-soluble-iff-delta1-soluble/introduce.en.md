Since the quotient $\Delta/\Delta_1$ is soluble (by Lemma 8.14), the solubility of the full autotopism group $\Delta$ reduces to the solubility of the subgroup $\Delta_1$ of autotopisms that act trivially on the semi-nuclei. This follows from the standard group-theoretic fact that an extension of a soluble group by a soluble group is soluble.
