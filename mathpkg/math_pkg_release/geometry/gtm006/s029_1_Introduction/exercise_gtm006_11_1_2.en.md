Show that the following confined configuration with eight points and eight lines is a sub-configuration of $A_2(3)$:

Points: the symbols $0, 1, \dots, 7$.

Lines: the point sets $\{0, 1, 2\}$, $\{5, 6, 7\}$, $\{0, 6, 3\}$, $\{5, 1, 4\}$, $\{1, 7, 3\}$, $\{6, 2, 4\}$, $\{0, 7, 4\}$, $\{5, 2, 3\}$.
