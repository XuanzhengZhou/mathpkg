Theorem 11.3 establishes that the configuration generated by $\mathcal{C}$ inside a closed configuration $\mathcal{P}$ is itself closed and can be described constructively as the union of all full $n$-step extensions of $\mathcal{C}$ within $\mathcal{P}$. This bridges the abstract definition of $\langle \mathcal{C} angle_{\mathcal{P}}$ as an intersection of closed configurations with a concrete iterative construction, and justifies the term "closure" for the generated configuration.
