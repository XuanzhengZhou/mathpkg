A free one-step extension of a configuration $\mathcal{C}$ is a one-step extension $\mathcal{C}_1$ in which every newly added element (point or line not already in $\mathcal{C}$) is incident with exactly two elements of $\mathcal{C}_1$. This is the minimal way to extend a configuration while introducing new elements, and is fundamental to the construction of free closures.
