The component mappings of an autotopism $(P,Q,R)$ are semi-linear transformations when $D$ is viewed as a vector space over its semi-nuclei. This result connects the abstract theory of autotopisms to the familiar linear algebra of semi-linear transformations, providing a powerful tool for analyzing autotopism groups.
