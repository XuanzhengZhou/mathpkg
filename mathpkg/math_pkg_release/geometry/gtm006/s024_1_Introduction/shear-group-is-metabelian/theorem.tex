Let $\mathcal{P}(D)$ be a division ring plane and let $\Pi = \operatorname{Aut}\mathcal{P}(D)$. If

$$\Sigma = \Pi_{([\infty], [0])} \cdot \Pi_{([\infty], [\infty])}$$

then
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\Sigma$ is a group,
\item[(ii)] $\Pi_{([\infty], [0])} \trianglelefteq \Sigma$ and $\Pi_{([\infty], [0])} \cap \Pi_{([\infty], [\infty])} = 1$, so that $\Sigma$ is metabelian.
\end{enumerate}
