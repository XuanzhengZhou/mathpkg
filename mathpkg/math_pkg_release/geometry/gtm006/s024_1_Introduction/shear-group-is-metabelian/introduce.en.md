In a division ring plane, the product of the shear group fixing $[0]$ and the shear group fixing $[\infty]$ forms a subgroup $\Sigma$ of the full collineation group. This subgroup is metabelian, meaning its commutator subgroup is abelian, providing important structural information about the group of shears.
