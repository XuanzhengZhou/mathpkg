This theorem gives sufficient conditions under which the autotopism group of a finite division ring plane is soluble (i.e., has a subnormal series with abelian factor groups). Combined with earlier results, it implies that the full collineation group of such planes is also soluble, confirming that finite non-desarguesian division ring planes tend to have soluble collineation groups.
