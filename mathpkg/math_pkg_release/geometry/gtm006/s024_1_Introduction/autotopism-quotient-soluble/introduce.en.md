For a finite division ring $D$, the quotient of the autotopism group by the subgroup $\Delta_1$ (those autotopisms acting trivially on the semi-nuclei) is abelian, hence soluble. This reduces the solubility question for the full autotopism group to that of $\Delta_1$, which consists of autotopisms whose action on the semi-nuclei is trivial.
