This lemma classifies the involutions (elements of order 2) in a certain subgroup $\Delta_2$ of the autotopism group. Every involution is either a Baer involution (fixing a Baer subplane pointwise) or a homology induced by multiplication by $-1$ in two coordinates. This gives precise information about the possible involutory homologies in the autotopism group of a finite division ring plane.
