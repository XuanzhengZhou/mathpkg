A one-to-one mapping $\beta$ of the projective line $\mathcal{P}(V)$ over a field $K$ onto itself is an element of $P\Gamma L(V)$ if and only if there is an automorphism $\phi$ of the underlying field $K$ such that $(P_1^\beta, P_2^\beta; P_3^\beta, P_4^\beta) = (P_1, P_2; P_3, P_4)^\phi$ for all appropriate choices of the points $P_i$.
