This corollary provides an intrinsic geometric characterization of the collineation group of the projective line: a bijection belongs to $\operatorname{P\Gamma L}(V)$ precisely when it preserves the cross-ratio up to a field automorphism. For $\operatorname{PGL}(V)$, the automorphism $\phi$ is the identity, so the cross-ratio is preserved exactly.
