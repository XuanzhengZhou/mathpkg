A perspectivity is a collineation that fixes every point on some line (the axis) and every line through some point (the centre). Perspectivities are the building blocks of collineation groups: elations (centre on axis) and homologies (centre not on axis) are the two fundamental types. The little projective group is generated by elations with incident centre and axis.
