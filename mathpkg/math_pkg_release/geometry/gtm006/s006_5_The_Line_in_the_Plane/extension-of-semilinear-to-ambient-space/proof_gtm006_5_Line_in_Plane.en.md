For any $e \notin W_1$ and $f \notin W_2$, any vector of $V$ has a unique representation $w + xe$ for $w \in W_1$, $x \in K$. Define $\alpha^*$ by $(w + xe)^{\alpha^*} = w^\alpha + x^\phi f$ where $\phi$ is the automorphism associated to $\alpha$. This is easily verified to be a non-singular semi-linear transformation.
