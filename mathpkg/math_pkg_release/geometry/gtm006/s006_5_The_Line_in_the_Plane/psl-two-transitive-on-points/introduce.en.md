This theorem strengthens the known transitivity properties: while $\operatorname{P\Gamma L}(V)$ is three-transitive on the projective line, $\operatorname{PSL}(V)$ is shown to be two-transitive on the points of a projective plane over a field. The proof uses the perspectivity lemma and the fact that the constructed linear transformation has determinant one.
