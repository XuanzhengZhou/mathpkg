Let $A_1, A_2, A_3$ be three distinct points on a projective line $W$ in a projective plane, and let $A_4$ be the point on $W$ constructed as the intersection of $W$ with the line joining the two diagonal points of a complete quadrilateral having $A_1, A_2, A_3$ as three of its vertices. Then $(A_1, A_2; A_3, A_4) = -1$, i.e. $A_4$ is the harmonic conjugate of $A_3$ with respect to $A_1$ and $A_2$.
