The mapping $\beta$ sends the frame $P_1, P_2, P_3$ to the frame $P_1^\beta, P_2^\beta, P_3^\beta$. If $X$ is an arbitrary point of $\mathcal{P}(V)$ with parametric coordinate $r$ in the $P_i$ frame, then by definition of the cross-ratio and the action of semi-linear transformations, $X^\beta$ must have parametric coordinate $r^\phi$ in the $P_i^\beta$ frame. The equality of cross-ratios follows directly from the fact that semi-linear transformations apply $\phi$ to coordinates.
