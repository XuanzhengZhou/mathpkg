The cross-ratio is not an absolute invariant of $\operatorname{P\Gamma L}(V)$; rather, it transforms via the companion automorphism $\phi$ of the semi-linear transformation. Elements of $\operatorname{PGL}(V)$ (where $\phi$ is the identity) preserve the cross-ratio exactly. This theorem is fundamental because it shows how the cross-ratio characterizes the action of the collineation group on the projective line.
