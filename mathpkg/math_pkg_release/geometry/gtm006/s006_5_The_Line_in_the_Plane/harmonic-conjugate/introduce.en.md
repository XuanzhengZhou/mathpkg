The harmonic conjugate is the most important special cross-ratio. The value $-1$ has special geometric significance: it is the only cross-ratio value (other than $0, 1, \infty$) that is invariant under all permutations of the symmetric group $S_4/K_4$ acting on the four points. Harmonic conjugates can be constructed via a complete quadrilateral (Theorem 2.24) and are characterized by the existence of an involutory perspectivity (Theorem 2.25).
