Let $\beta: \mathcal{P}(V) \to \mathcal{P}(W)$ be induced by a non-singular semi-linear transformation with associated automorphism $\phi$. Then for points $P_1, P_2, P_3, P_4$:
$$(P_1^\beta, P_2^\beta; P_3^\beta, P_4^\beta) = (P_1, P_2; P_3, P_4)^\phi.$$
In particular, cross-ratio is preserved by linear transformations ($\phi = 1$).
