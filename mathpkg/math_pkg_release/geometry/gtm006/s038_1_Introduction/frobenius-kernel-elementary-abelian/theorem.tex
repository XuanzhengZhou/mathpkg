Let $\Pi$ be a doubly transitive permutation group on a set of $n+1$ elements ($n$ odd). Suppose the stabilizer $\Pi_a$ contains a subgroup $\Gamma_a$ of even order which is a Frobenius group on the remaining $n$ points, and whose kernel $\Sigma_a$ is normal in $\Pi_a$. Then $\Sigma_a$ is elementary abelian and $n$ is a prime power.
