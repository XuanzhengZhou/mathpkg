Lemma 14.13 classifies the possible structure of an affine plane whose collineation group satisfies the involutory homology conditions of Lemma 14.12. Through a detailed case analysis, the plane is shown to be either a translation plane, the dual of a translation plane, or of type $\mathcal{H}(1)$. In the proof of Wagner's Theorem, the other two possibilities are eliminated, leaving only the translation plane case — which is then used to deduce that the original plane must also be a translation plane.
