Exercise 14.2 asks the reader to verify that the Hall plane of order 9 is an exception: its full collineation group is transitive on affine lines. By Wagner's Theorem, this plane must be a translation plane (which Hall planes are), but the line-transitivity of the full group is a special feature of order 9. This is the smallest non-desarguesian Hall plane.
