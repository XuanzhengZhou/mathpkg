Lemma 7.4 describes three fundamental properties of elation groups in a translation plane. Part (i) states that the group of elations with a given axis $x_2$ and arbitrary centers on the line at infinity decomposes as the union of elation groups with fixed center and axis. Part (ii) asserts that elation groups with distinct centers on the line at infinity intersect trivially. Part (iii) identifies the $(x_1, x_2)$-elation group as the intersection of two such groups with distinct centers. These properties are foundational for the algebraic analysis of translation planes via their automorphism groups.
