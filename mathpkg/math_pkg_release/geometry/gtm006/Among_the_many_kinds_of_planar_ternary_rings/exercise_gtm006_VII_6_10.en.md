Let $F = GF(q^2)$, $q$ an odd prime power. Define a new multiplication $\circ$ on $F$ by $x \circ y = xy$ if $x$ is a square in $F$, and $x \circ y = xy^q$ if $x$ is a non-square. Show that $F$ with its original addition and new multiplication $\circ$ is a quasifield but not a field.
