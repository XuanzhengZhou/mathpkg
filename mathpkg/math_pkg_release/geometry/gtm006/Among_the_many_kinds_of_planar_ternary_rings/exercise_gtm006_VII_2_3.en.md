(a) Prove that axioms (1), (2), (3), (5) in the definition of a quasifield imply (4).

(b) If $F$ is any field, define a new multiplication by $x \cdot y = xy$ if $x \neq 0$ and $0 \cdot y = y$. Show that $(F, +, \cdot)$ satisfies axioms (1), (2), (3) for a quasifield, but does not satisfy (4).
