Prove that a "quasifield" as defined algebraically (axioms (1)-(5)) is a planar ternary ring and is a "quasifield" by the definition in Chapter VI (i.e., a PTR obtained by coordinatizing a projective plane which has $[\infty]$ as a translation line).
