The special linear group is the kernel of the determinant homomorphism $\det : GL(V) \to K^*$. For an $n$-dimensional vector space over a field $K$, $SL(V) \cong SL(n, K)$. $SL(V)$ is important in the study of classical groups and their geometries, and appears in the classification of collineation groups of Desarguesian projective planes.
