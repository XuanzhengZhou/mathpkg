Result 1.24 specializes Result 1.23 to the case of fields: when $K$ is commutative, any semi-linear transformation preserving all lines through the origin must actually be linear (its associated automorphism is the identity) and must be a scalar multiple of the identity. This is because the automorphism is forced to commute with all inner automorphisms, which in a field are all trivial. This result is used in proving the Fundamental Theorem of Projective Geometry.
