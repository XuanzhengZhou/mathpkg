$\Gamma L(V)$ is the full group of collineations of the projective space $\mathcal{P}(V)$ induced by semi-linear transformations. It contains the general linear group $GL(V)$ as a normal subgroup, and the quotient $\Gamma L(V)/GL(V)$ is isomorphic to the automorphism group of the underlying skewfield. This group structure (Result 1.21) is essential for classifying collineations of Desarguesian projective planes.
