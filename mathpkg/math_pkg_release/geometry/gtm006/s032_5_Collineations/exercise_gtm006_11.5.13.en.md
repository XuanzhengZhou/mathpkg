Let $\mathcal{A}$ be an o.f. (open-free) configuration and $\mathcal{P} = \mathcal{F}(\mathcal{A})$ its free closure; let $\theta$ be a polarity of $\mathcal{P}$. Show that the number of absolute elements of $\theta$ that are not in $\mathcal{K}(\mathcal{A})$ is finite.
