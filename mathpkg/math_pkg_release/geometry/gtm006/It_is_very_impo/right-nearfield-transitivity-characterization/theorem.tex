A projective plane is coordinatized by a right nearfield if and only if it admits $(V,V)$-transitivity for a point $V$ not incident with a line $k$, together with an additional condition on a further point $W$ on $k$. In this case $V = (\infty)$ and $k = [\infty]$, with $W$ lying on $k = [\infty]$.
