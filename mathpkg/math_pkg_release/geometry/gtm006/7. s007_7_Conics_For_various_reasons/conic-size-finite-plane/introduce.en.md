Corollary 2 to Lemma 2.34 determines the exact cardinality of a conic in a finite projective plane of order $q$: every conic is either empty or contains exactly $q+1$ points. This follows immediately from Corollary 1 (nonempty conics are in bijection with a projective line) and the fact that a projective line in $\mathcal{P}_2(q)$ has $q+1$ points.
