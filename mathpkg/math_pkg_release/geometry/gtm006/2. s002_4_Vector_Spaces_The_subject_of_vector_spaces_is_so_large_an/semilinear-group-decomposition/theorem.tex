Let $V$ be a finite dimensional left or right vector space over the skewfield $K$. Let $\Gamma L(V)$ be the group of all invertible semi-linear transformations of $V$, and let $GL(V)$ be the group of all invertible linear transformations of $V$. Then

$$\Gamma L(V) = A \cdot GL(V),$$

where $GL(V)$ is normal in $\Gamma L(V)$, $A \cap GL(V) = \{1\}$, and $A \cong \operatorname{Aut}(K)$.
