Let $\mathcal{P} = \mathcal{P}_2(K)$ for any field $K$ and let $l$ be the line $z = 0$. Show that $\mathcal{P}^l \cong \mathcal{A}_2(K)$, where $\mathcal{A}_2(K)$ is the affine plane of order $|K|$ constructed from the affine space $K^2$.
