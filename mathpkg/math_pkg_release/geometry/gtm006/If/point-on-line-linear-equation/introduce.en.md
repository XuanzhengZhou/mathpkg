Lemma 2.7 provides the geometric interpretation of the skewfield operations in a coordinatized projective plane. Using a frame, a point $\langle x e_1 + y e_2 + e_3 \rangle$ lies on the line joining $\langle m e_2 + e_1 \rangle$ to $\langle b e_2 + e_3 \rangle$ if and only if $y = xm + b$. This is the projective analogue of the familiar equation of a line in affine coordinates $y = mx + b$, giving a geometric construction for multiplication and addition in the coordinatizing skewfield.
