Let $F_1, F_2, \dots, F_{n+2}$ be any frame in $\mathcal{P}(V)$. Then there exists a basis $e_1, e_2, \dots, e_{n+1}$ for $V$ such that $F_i = \langle e_i \rangle$ for $i = 1, 2, \dots, n+1$ and $F_{n+2} = \langle e_1 + e_2 + \cdots + e_{n+1} \rangle$. This basis is unique up to multiplication by a common non-zero scalar.
