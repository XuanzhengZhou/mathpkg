This corollary to the Fundamental Theorem of Projective Geometry (Theorem 2.8) characterizes the automorphism group of a projective plane. The group of all automorphisms of $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto itself is induced by the group of all non-singular semi-linear transformations of $V$, i.e., $\operatorname{Aut} \mathcal{P}(V) = P\Gamma L(V)$. This is the case for $g$-dimension two and generalizes to higher dimensions.
