Result 14.3 is a fundamental fact in permutation group theory: any non-trivial normal subgroup of a primitive permutation group acts transitively on the set on which the group acts. This is one of the standard characterization results for primitive permutation groups. In the context of Wagner's Theorem, this result is combined with Results 14.1 and 14.2 to prove Result 14.4, which establishes that under certain conditions the kernel of a Frobenius subgroup is elementary abelian and the degree is a prime power.
