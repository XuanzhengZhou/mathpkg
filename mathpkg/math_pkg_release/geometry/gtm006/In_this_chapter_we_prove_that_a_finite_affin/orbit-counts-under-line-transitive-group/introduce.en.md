This corollary to Theorem 14.6 computes the orders of various stabilizers in a collineation group $\Pi$ that acts transitively on the affine lines of a finite affine plane of order $n$. Setting $k = |\Pi_{C,l}|$ as the order of a flag stabilizer, the corollary gives formulas for $|\Pi|$, the stabilizer of a special point $|\Pi_D|$, the stabilizer of an affine line $|\Pi_l|$, the stabilizer of an affine point $|\Pi_C|$, and the stabilizer of an incident point-special-point pair $|\Pi_{C,D}|$. These orbit-counting formulas are derived from flag-transitivity (a consequence of Theorem 14.6) and are used extensively throughout the proof of Wagner's Theorem to relate group orders to the plane order $n$.
