Result 14.1 is a permutation group theorem about doubly transitive groups acting on a set of $n+1$ elements with $n$ odd. It states that if each point stabilizer $\Pi_a$ contains a Frobenius subgroup of even order on the complement, then that point stabilizer acts primitively on the complement. This result, proved by Wagner, is a crucial building block in the proof of Wagner's Theorem on finite affine line-transitive planes. The proof is given in the appendix of Chapter XIV.
