Repeat Exercises 7.10 and 7.11 with $F = \operatorname{GF}(q^2)$ and multiplication given by
$$x \circ y = xy \text{ if $x$ is a square, and } x \circ y = xy^q \text{ if $x$ is a non-square}$$
for any prime power $q$, $q \neq 2^r$.
