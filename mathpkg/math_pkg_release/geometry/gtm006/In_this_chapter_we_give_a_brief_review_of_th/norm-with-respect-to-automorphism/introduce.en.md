The norm with respect to an automorphism $\alpha$ of a skewfield $K$ is defined as the product of all Galois conjugates of an element under the cyclic group generated by $\alpha$. Norms are always fixed by $\alpha$ and play a key role in understanding unitary polarities in projective planes, particularly for quadratic extensions of finite fields.
