A projective plane is Moufang if and only if every coordinating planar ternary ring is an alternative (or IP) division ring, if and only if the plane is $(V,k)$-transitive for all incident pairs $V$ and $k$. Equivalently, the plane is $(k,k)$-transitive for all $k$, or $(V,V)$-transitive for all $V$, or $(k,k)$-transitive for two distinct $k$, or $(V,V)$-transitive for two distinct $V$.
