Let $\mathcal{A}$ be an affine plane coordinatized by a right quasifield $R$ of dimension $2$ over its kernel $F$. Suppose there exist elements $a, b, c \in R$ with $a \notin F$ such that the set $\mathcal{B}(a, b, c)$ of points $(\alpha a + b, \beta a + c)$ for $\alpha, \beta \in F$ forms a Baer subplane of $\mathcal{A}$. Then $\mathcal{A}$ is derivable.
