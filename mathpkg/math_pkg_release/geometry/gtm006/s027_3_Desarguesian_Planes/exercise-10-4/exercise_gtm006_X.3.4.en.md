Generalize the proof of Theorem 10.12 to show: if the elements of $F^*$ are partitioned into two sets $\{t_1, \dots, t_r\}$ and $\{t_{r+1}, \dots, t_{q-1}\}$, then the multiply derived plane on $\mathcal{S}_{t_1}, \dots, \mathcal{S}_{t_r}$ is isomorphic to the multiply derived plane on $\mathcal{S}_{t_{r+1}}, \dots, \mathcal{S}_{t_{q-1}}$.
