Exercise 10.1 asks the reader to prove that if $\alpha$ is a translation of an affine plane $\mathcal{A}$, then $\alpha$ induces a translation of the derived plane $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{F}}(\mathcal{A})$. The problem notes that the special case where the centre of $\alpha$ belongs to $\mathcal{G}'$ was already established in the proof of the preceding theorem.
