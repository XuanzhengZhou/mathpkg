Let $\mathcal{S}$ and $\mathcal{T}$ be derivation sets for an affine plane $\mathcal{A}$, with $\mathcal{S} \cap \mathcal{T} = \emptyset$. Show that $\mathcal{T}$ is a derivation set for $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}}(\mathcal{A})$, and that $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{T}} \mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}}(\mathcal{A}) = \mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}} \mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{T}}(\mathcal{A})$.
