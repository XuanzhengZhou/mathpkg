Lemma 2.34 establishes a fundamental bound for the intersection of a line with a conic in a projective plane: any line meets a conic in at most two points. The proof uses the symmetric bilinear form associated with the conic's matrix $A$ and reduces the intersection condition to a quadratic equation in the homogeneous parameters of points on the line. This bound is the projective analogue of the familiar fact that a line intersects a conic in the Euclidean plane in at most two points.
