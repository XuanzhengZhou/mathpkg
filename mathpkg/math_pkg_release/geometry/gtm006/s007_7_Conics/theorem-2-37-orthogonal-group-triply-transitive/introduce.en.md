The orthogonal group of a non-empty conic acts as $PGL(2, K)$ on the conic points via fractional linear transformations of the parametric coordinate. This establishes a fundamental isomorphism between the symmetry group of a conic and the collineation group of a projective line, and shows the group is triply transitive.
