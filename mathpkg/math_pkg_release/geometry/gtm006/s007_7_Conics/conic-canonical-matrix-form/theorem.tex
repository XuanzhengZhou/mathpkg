Let $\mathcal{C}$ be a non-empty conic in a projective plane $\mathcal{P}(V)$ over a field of characteristic $\neq 2$. Then a basis for $V$ can be chosen so that the symmetric matrix $A$ for $\mathcal{C}$ has the form

$$A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 \\ -1 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix},$$

and the points of $\mathcal{C}$ admit the parametric representation $\langle (y^2, y, 1) \rangle$ together with $\langle (1, 0, 0) \rangle$ (the point at infinity).
