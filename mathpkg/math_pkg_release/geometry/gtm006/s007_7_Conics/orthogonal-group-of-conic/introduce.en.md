The orthogonal group of a conic $\mathcal{C}$ is the group of all linear collineations that preserve $\mathcal{C}$. Equivalently, it consists of collineations commuting with the associated polarity. This group is isomorphic to $PGL(2, K)$ and is triply transitive on the points of the conic.
