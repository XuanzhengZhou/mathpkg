Verify that

$$
\{1\} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & a & b \\
1 & 0 & b & a \\
a & b & 0 & 1 \\
b & a & 1 & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\quad
\{a\} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & a & b \\
a & b & 0 & 1 \\
b & a & 1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & b & a
\end{pmatrix}
\quad
\{b\} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & a & b \\
b & a & 1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & b & a \\
a & b & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

is a set of 3 mutually orthogonal Latin squares of order 4, i.e., a complete set of MOLS.
