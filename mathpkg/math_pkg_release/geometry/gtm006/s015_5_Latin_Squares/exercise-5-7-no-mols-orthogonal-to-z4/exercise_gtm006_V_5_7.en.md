Show that there is no Latin square orthogonal to

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & 2 & 3 \\
1 & 2 & 3 & 0 \\
2 & 3 & 0 & 1 \\
3 & 0 & 1 & 2
\end{pmatrix}.
$$

Hint: try to construct one starting with

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & 2 & 3 \\
2
\end{pmatrix}.
$$
