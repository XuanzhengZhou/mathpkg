Let $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ be non-degenerate configurations such that $\mathcal{B}$ is contained in a closed configuration $\mathcal{P}$, and suppose $\beta$ is a homomorphism of $\mathcal{A}$ onto $\mathcal{B}$. Then $\beta$ extends to a homomorphism $\bar{\beta}$ of $\mathcal{F}(\mathcal{A})$ onto $\langle \mathcal{B} \rangle$.
