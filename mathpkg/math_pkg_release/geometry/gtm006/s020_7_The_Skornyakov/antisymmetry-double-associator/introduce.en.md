The double associator $[[a,b],a,c]$ is antisymmetric in the arguments $b$ and $c$. This follows from expanding $[[a, b+c], a, b+c] = 0$ (which holds by Lemma (9)) using linearity and canceling the diagonal terms. This identity is used in Step (A) of the main proof to transfer membership between the auxiliary subgroups $A(a,b)$ and $A(a,ba)$.
