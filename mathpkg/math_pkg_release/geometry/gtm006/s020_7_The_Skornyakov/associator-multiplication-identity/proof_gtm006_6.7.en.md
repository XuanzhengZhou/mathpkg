Put $q = [a, b]$. Then
$$0 = h(x, q, a, b) = [xq, a, b] + [x, q, q] - x[q, a, b] - [x, a, b] q.$$

By the right alternative law (RA), $[x, q, q] = 0$. By (9), $[q, a, b] = 0$. Therefore
$$[xq, a, b] = [x, a, b] q,$$
which is exactly $[x[a, b], a, b] = [x, a, b][a, b]$. $\square$
