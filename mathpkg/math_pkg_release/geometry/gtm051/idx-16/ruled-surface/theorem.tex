A surface $f$ in $\mathbb{R}^3$ locally parametrized as
$$f(s, t) = s X(t) + c(t),$$
where $X(t)$ is a vector field along a curve $c(t)$ on $f$, is called a \text{ruled surface}. The curves $t = \text{constant}$ are lines in $\mathbb{R}^3$ (generators) and the curve $s = \text{constant}$ is a directrix.
