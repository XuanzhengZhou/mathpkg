Geodesic coordinate properties describe the behavior of the first fundamental form coefficients and Christoffel symbols in a geodesic coordinate system based on a unit-speed geodesic. When the basis curve is a unit-speed geodesic, the metric simplifies to $g_{22}(0, u^2) = 1$, $g_{22,1}(0, u^2) = 0$, and all Christoffel symbols vanish along the basis curve. This lemma provides the foundational computational simplifications used to analyze the local intrinsic geometry of surfaces.
