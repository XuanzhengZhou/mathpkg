Let $f(s, t)$ be a ruled surface in $(s, t)$-parameters.

\textbf{(i)} On a ruled surface, generators are asymptotic curves. Consequently, $K \leq 0$.

\textbf{(ii)} A ruled surface $f$ is developable if and only if in $(s, t)$-parameters $f_{st}$ is a linear combination of $f_s$ and $f_t$, if and only if $K = 0$ on $f$.
