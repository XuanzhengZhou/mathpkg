(i) In $(s, t)$-parameters, $f_{ss} = 0$. Therefore
$$h_{11} = II(f_s, f_s) = -n_s \cdot f_s = n \cdot f_{ss} = 0,$$
and so
$$K = -\frac{h_{12}^2}{\det(g_{ij})} \leq 0.$$

(ii) In $(s, t)$-parameters, we have shown in (i) that $n_s \cdot f_s = 0$. Therefore if $f(s, t)$ is a ruled surface,
$$n_s = 0 \;\Leftrightarrow\; n_s \cdot f_t = n_s \cdot f_s = 0 \;\Leftrightarrow\; n_s \cdot f_t = 0 \;\Leftrightarrow\; n_s \cdot f_{st} = 0 \;\Leftrightarrow\; h_{12} = 0 \;\Leftrightarrow\; K = -\frac{h_{12}^2}{\det(g_{ij})} = 0.$$
