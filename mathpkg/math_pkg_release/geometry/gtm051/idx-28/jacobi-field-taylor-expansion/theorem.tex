Let $Y(t) = y(t)e_2(t)$ be a Jacobi field along a geodesic $c(t)$ with $y(0) = 0$, $\dot{y}(0) = 1$, where $e_2(t)$ is a unit parallel orthonormal vector field along $c(t)$. Then the Taylor series expansion of $y(t)$ at $t = 0$ is

$$y(t) = t - K \circ c(0) \cdot \frac{t^3}{6} + \dots$$

where $K \circ c(0)$ denotes the Gauss curvature of the surface evaluated at the point $c(0)$.
