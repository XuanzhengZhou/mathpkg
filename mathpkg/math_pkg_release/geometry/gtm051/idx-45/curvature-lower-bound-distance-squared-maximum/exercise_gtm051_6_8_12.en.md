Let $c(t)$ be a unit-speed curve in $\mathbb{R}^n$ with the property that $|c(t)|^2$ has a local maximum at $t_0$. Let $p_0 = c(t_0)$ and $\rho^2 = |p_0|^2$. Show that
$$\kappa(t_0) \geq \frac{1}{\rho},$$
where $\kappa(t_0) = |\ddot{c}(t_0)|$ is the curvature of $c$ at $t_0$.
