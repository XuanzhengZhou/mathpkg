Let $M$ be a manifold with a Riemannian metric $g$. Consider $p \in M$ and the cut locus $C(p) \subset M$. Let $\tilde{g}$ be another Riemannian metric which is close to $g$ in some natural sense, and let $\tilde{C}(p)$ be the cut-locus of $p$ in the $\tilde{g}$ metric. Then for almost all $p \in M$ and a large generic class of metrics on $M$, there exists a homeomorphism $\phi: M \to M$ such that the restriction $\phi|_{C(p)}: C(p) \to \tilde{C}(p)$ is also a homeomorphism.
