Let $\mathrm{SL}(2, \mathbb{R})$ denote the special linear group in dimension $2$, defined as the group of all real $(2 \times 2)$-matrices with determinant equal to $1$:
$$
\mathrm{SL}(2, \mathbb{R}) = \left\{ \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \in M_2(\mathbb{R}) \;\middle|\; ad - bc = 1 \right\}.
$$
