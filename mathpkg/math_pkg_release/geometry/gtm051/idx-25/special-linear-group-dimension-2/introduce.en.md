The special linear group $\mathrm{SL}(2, \mathbb{R})$ is the group of $2 \times 2$ real matrices with determinant $1$. It is a three-dimensional Lie group that plays a fundamental role in two-dimensional hyperbolic geometry, as it serves as the isometry group of the Poincar\'e upper half-plane model. Its study connects linear algebra, group theory, and Riemannian geometry.
