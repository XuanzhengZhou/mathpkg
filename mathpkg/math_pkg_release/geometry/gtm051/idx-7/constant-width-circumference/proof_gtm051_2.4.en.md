The statement is Barbier's theorem, presented as an exercise (2.4.2) in the text. Using the support function $h(\theta)$ of the convex curve parametrized by the angle $\theta$ of the outward normal, the constant width condition implies $h(\theta) + h(\theta + \pi) = d$. The circumference is given by $L = \int_0^{2\pi} h(\theta)\,d\theta$, from which $L = \pi d$ follows.
