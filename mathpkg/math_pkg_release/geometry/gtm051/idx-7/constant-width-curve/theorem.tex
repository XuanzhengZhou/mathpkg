Let $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^2$ be a closed, strictly convex curve. For every point $c(t)$, there exists a unique point $c(t')$ such that $e_1(t) = -e_1(t')$, where $e_1(t)$ is the unit tangent vector. The curve $c$ is said to have \emph{constant width} if $d(c(t), c(t')) = d$, where $d > 0$ is a constant independent of $t$.
