The total curvature of a space curve is the integral of its curvature with respect to arc length. For plane curves, this is related to the rotation number; for space curves, it is a non-negative quantity that generalizes the total turning of the tangent vector. Fenchel's theorem gives a fundamental lower bound: $K(c) \geq 2\pi$ for any closed space curve.
