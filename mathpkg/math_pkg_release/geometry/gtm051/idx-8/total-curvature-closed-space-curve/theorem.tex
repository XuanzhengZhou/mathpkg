Let $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^n$, $n \geq 3$, be a closed curve with curvature function $\kappa(t)$ (where $\kappa(t) > 0$ for the curves considered, i.e., curves with linearly independent derivatives up to order $n-1$). The total curvature $K(c)$ of $c$ is defined as
$$K(c) = \int_0^L |\kappa(t)|\,dt = \int_0^L \kappa(t)\,dt,$$
where $L$ is the arc length of $c$.
