For a parametrized surface $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^3$, the curvature tensor satisfies

$$
R_{ijkl} = h_{ij} h_{kl} - h_{ik} h_{jl}
$$

for all $1 \leq i, j, k, l \leq 2$. Consequently, the curvature tensor possesses the symmetries

$$
R_{ijkl} = -R_{jikl} = -R_{ijlk} = R_{klij},
$$

and is totally determined by the single independent component

$$
R_{1212} = -R_{2112} = R_{2121} = -R_{1221} = \det(h_{ij}).
$$

All other components $R_{ijkl}$ are equal to zero.
