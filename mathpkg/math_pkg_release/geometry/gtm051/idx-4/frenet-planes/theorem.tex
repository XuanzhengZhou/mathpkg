At a point $c(t_0)$ of a space curve $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^3$ with Frenet frame $e_1(t_0), e_2(t_0), e_3(t_0)$, the three mutually orthogonal planes are:

\begin{itemize}
  \item $(e_1, e_2)$-plane = \textbf{osculating plane} at $c(t_0)$,
  \item $(e_2, e_3)$-plane = \textbf{normal plane} at $c(t_0)$,
  \item $(e_3, e_1)$-plane = \textbf{rectifying plane} at $c(t_0)$.
\end{itemize}
