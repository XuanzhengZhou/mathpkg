Suppose $f$ is a surface and $X$ is a tangent vector at $u_0$ with $|X| = 1$ and $\kappa(X) \neq 0$. If $c = f \circ u$ is a curve on $f$ with $\dot{c}(t_0) = \pm |\dot{c}(t_0)| X$, then the osculating circle of $c$ at $t_0$ is the intersection of the osculating plane of $c$ at $t_0$ with the sphere of radius $1/|\kappa(X)|$ centered at $f(u_0) + n(u_0)/\kappa(X)$.
