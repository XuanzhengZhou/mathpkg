Let $f$ be a surface and $c = f \circ u: I \to \mathbb{R}^3$ a curve on $f$. A tangential vector field $X(t)$ along $c$ is parallel if its covariant derivative vanishes: $\nabla X/dt := \nabla_{\dot{c}} X = 0$. In coordinates:
$$\frac{d a^k}{dt} + \sum_{i,j} \Gamma_{ij}^k \dot{u}^i a^j = 0, \quad k = 1,2,$$
where $X(t) = \sum a^k(t) f_{u^k}(u(t))$.
