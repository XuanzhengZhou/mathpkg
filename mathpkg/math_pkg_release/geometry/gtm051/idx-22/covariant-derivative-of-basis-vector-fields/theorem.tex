The covariant derivative of the coordinate basis vector fields $e_i(u)$ on a Riemannian surface $(U, g)$ is defined by

$$\frac{\nabla e_i(u)}{\partial u^j} := \sum_k \Gamma_{ij}^k \circ u \; e_k(u), \quad 1 \leq i, j, k \leq 2,$$

where $\Gamma_{ij}^k$ are the Christoffel symbols of the Riemannian metric.
