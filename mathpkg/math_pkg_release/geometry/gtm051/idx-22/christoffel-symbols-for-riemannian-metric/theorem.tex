The Christoffel symbols $\Gamma_{ij}^k$ of a Riemannian metric $(U, g)$ are defined by

$$\Gamma_{ij}^k := \frac{1}{2} \sum_{l} g^{kl} \left( g_{il,j} + g_{jl,i} - g_{ij,l} \right),$$

where $g^{kl}$ denotes the entries of the inverse matrix of $(g_{ik})$, and $g_{ij,k} := \frac{\partial g_{ij}}{\partial u^k}$.
