Let $X = \sum_k \xi^k(u) e_k(u)$ be a vector field on a Riemannian surface $M$, expressed in local coordinates $(U, g)$. The covariant differential $\nabla X: T_u \mathbb{R}^2 \rightarrow T_u \mathbb{R}^2$ is the linear transformation corresponding to the matrix with $(k, j)$-entry

$$(\nabla X(u))_{j}^{k} = \frac{\partial \xi^k(u)}{\partial u^j} + \sum_i \xi^i(u) \, \Gamma_{ij}^k \circ u,$$

and the divergence is

$$\operatorname{div} X = \operatorname{trace}(\nabla X).$$
