This lemma gives an explicit formula for the geodesic curvature $\kappa_g$ of a curve on an oriented Riemannian surface, expressed in geodesic (polar) coordinates. It decomposes $\kappa_g$ into the rate of change of the angle $\theta(t)$ between the curve's tangent vector and the coordinate basis vector $E_1$, plus a term involving the covariant derivative of the orthonormal frame. In geodesic coordinates the Christoffel symbols simplify, yielding a compact expression involving only the derivative of the metric coefficient $g_{22}$.
