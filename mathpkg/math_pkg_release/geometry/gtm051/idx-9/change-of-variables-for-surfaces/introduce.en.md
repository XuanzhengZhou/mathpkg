A change of variables for a surface is a diffeomorphism between two open subsets of $\mathbb{R}^2$ that reparameterizes the surface. This gives a way to relate different parameterizations of the same geometric object. An orientation-preserving change of variables has positive Jacobian determinant, preserving the sense of orientation on the surface.
