Let $F: U \to \mathbb{R}^m$ be a differentiable map on an open set $U \subset \mathbb{R}^n$. For $x_0 \in U$, the differential $dF_{x_0}: \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}^m$ is the unique linear map $L$ satisfying
$$|F(x) - F(x_0) - L(x - x_0)| = o(|x - x_0|).$$
The differential is also denoted by $dF$ or $dF_{x_0}$. In coordinates, it is represented by the Jacobian matrix $(\partial F^i/\partial x^j)|_{x_0}$.
