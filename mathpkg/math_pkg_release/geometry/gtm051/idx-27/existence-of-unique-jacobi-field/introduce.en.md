This proposition states the well-posedness of the Jacobi equation as an initial value problem. Since the Jacobi equation reduces to the scalar ODE $\ddot{y}(t) + K(c(t)) y(t) = 0$ when expressed in the Frenet frame, specifying the initial position $y(0)=a_0$ and initial velocity $\dot{y}(0)=a_1$ determines a unique Jacobi field along the geodesic. This follows directly from the standard existence and uniqueness theorem for ordinary differential equations.
