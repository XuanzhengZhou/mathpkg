Let $c, \tilde{c}: I \to \mathbb{R}^n$ be two curves satisfying condition (1.2.2), and let $B$ be a congruence (an isometry of $\mathbb{R}^n$) with orthogonal component $R \in O(n)$ such that $\tilde{c} = B \circ c$. Let $\{e_i(t)\}$ and $\{\tilde{e}_i(t)\}$ be the distinguished Frenet frames of $c$ and $\tilde{c}$ respectively, with connection forms $\omega_{ij}(t) = \dot{e}_i(t) \cdot e_j(t)$ and $\tilde{\omega}_{ij}(t) = \dot{\tilde{e}}_i(t) \cdot \tilde{e}_j(t)$. Then

$$\tilde{\omega}_{ij}(t) = \omega_{ij}(t)$$

for all $i, j$ and all $t \in I$.
