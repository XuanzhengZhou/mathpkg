Let $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^n$ be a curve satisfying condition (1.2.2) and let $\kappa_i$, $i = 1, \dots, n-1$, be the curvature functions of its distinguished Frenet frame as in Definition 1.3.3. Then

$$\kappa_i(t) > 0 \quad \text{for all } i < n - 1 \text{ and all } t \in I.$$

The highest curvature $\kappa_{n-1}$ may change sign.
