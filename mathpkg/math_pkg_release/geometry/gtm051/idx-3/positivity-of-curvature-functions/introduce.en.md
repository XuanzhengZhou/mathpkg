For the distinguished Frenet frame, all curvature functions except possibly the last one are strictly positive. This positivity follows from the Gram-Schmidt construction of the distinguished Frenet frame, which ensures that the derivatives $\dot{e}_i$ have positive projections onto $e_{i+1}$. Only the highest curvature $\kappa_{n-1}$ is allowed to change sign.
