This proposition characterizes Weingarten surfaces in terms of the partial derivatives of their principal curvatures with respect to principal curvature coordinates. On any $W$-surface, the Jacobian determinant $\kappa_{1,1}\kappa_{2,2} - \kappa_{1,2}\kappa_{2,1}$ vanishes identically, which implies that the principal curvatures are functionally dependent. This condition provides an alternative, differential characterization that is equivalent to the existence of a functional relation $\varphi(\kappa_1, \kappa_2) = 0$.
