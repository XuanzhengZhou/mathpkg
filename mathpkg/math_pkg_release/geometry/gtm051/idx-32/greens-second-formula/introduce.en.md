Green's second formula follows immediately from Green's first formula by exchanging $\psi_1$ and $\psi_2$ and subtracting. It expresses the fact that the Laplace–Beltrami operator is formally self-adjoint: the integral of $\psi_1 \Delta\psi_2 - \psi_2 \Delta\psi_1$ over $P$ is given by a boundary integral involving normal derivatives. This antisymmetrized form is fundamental in potential theory and the theory of harmonic functions.
