Suppose the change of variables $\phi: V \to U$ is given, in terms of coordinates, by $u^i = u^i(v^1, v^2)$, $i = 1, 2$. Then the fundamental matrix $(\tilde{g}_{ij})$ of $\tilde{f} = f \circ \phi$ is related to the fundamental matrix $(g_{ij})$ of $f$ by

$$\tilde{g}_{ij}(v) = \sum_{k, l} \frac{\partial u^k}{\partial v^i} \frac{\partial u^l}{\partial v^j} \, g_{kl}(\phi(v)).$$
