The tangent bundle $TM$ of a differentiable surface $M$ is the disjoint union of all tangent spaces $T_p M$ for $p \in M$, equipped with the structure of a four-dimensional differentiable manifold. Its differentiable structure is defined via the atlas of $M$: for each chart $(u_{\alpha}, M_{\alpha})$, the map $Tu_{\alpha}$ identifies $TM_{\alpha} = \pi^{-1}(M_{\alpha})$ with $U_{\alpha} \times \mathbb{R}^2$. The projection $\pi: TM \to M$ is differentiable and sends each tangent vector to its base point.
