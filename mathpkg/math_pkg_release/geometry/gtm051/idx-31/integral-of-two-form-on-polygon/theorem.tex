Let $\Omega$ be a 2-form on a surface $M$ and $P: F \to M$ a polygon. The **integral** of $\Omega$ over $P$ is
$$\iint_P \Omega := \iint_F \Omega\!\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial t^1}, \frac{\partial P}{\partial t^2}\right) dt^1 dt^2.$$
This integral is invariant under orientation-preserving changes of variables of the polygon.
