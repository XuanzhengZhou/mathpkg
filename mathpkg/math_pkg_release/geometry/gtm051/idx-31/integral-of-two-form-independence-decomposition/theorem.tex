Let $M$ be a compact oriented surface. Suppose $\Pi = \{P_\rho\}$ and $\Pi' = \{P'_\rho\}$ are two polygonal decompositions of $M$. If $\Omega$ is a 2-form on $M$, then
$$\sum_\rho \iint_{P_\rho} \Omega = \sum_{\rho'} \iint_{P'_{\rho'}} \Omega.$$
Hence $\iint_M \Omega$ is well-defined independent of the choice of polygonal decomposition.
