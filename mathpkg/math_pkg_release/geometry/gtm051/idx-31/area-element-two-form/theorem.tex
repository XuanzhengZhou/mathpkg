Let $M$ be an oriented Riemannian surface. The **area element** is the 2-form $dM$ defined by
$$dM(X, Y) := \sqrt{g(X, X) g(Y, Y) - g(X, Y)^2}$$
whenever $\{X, Y\}$ is a positively oriented basis of $T_pM$. In local coordinates, $dM = \sqrt{\det g} \, du^1 \wedge du^2 = \sqrt{g} \, du^1 \wedge du^2$.
