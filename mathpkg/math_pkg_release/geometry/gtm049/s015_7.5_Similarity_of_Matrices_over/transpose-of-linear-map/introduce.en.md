The transpose $f^t$ of a linear mapping $f \colon V \to V$ is the induced mapping on the dual space $V^*$, defined by precomposition: applying $f^t$ to a linear functional $\varphi$ yields the functional $\varphi \circ f$. The defining equation $v(\varphi f^t) = (vf)\varphi$ expresses the duality pairing. This construction plays a key role in establishing the isomorphism between the $F[X]$-module structures of $V_f$ and $(V^*)_{f^t}$.
