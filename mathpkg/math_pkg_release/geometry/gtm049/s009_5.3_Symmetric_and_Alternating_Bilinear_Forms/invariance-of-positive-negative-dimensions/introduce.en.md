Proposition 8 is Sylvester's Law of Inertia: the dimensions $p = \dim P$ and $q = \dim Q$ of the positive-definite and negative-definite summands in an orthogonal decomposition of a real symmetric space are independent of the chosen decomposition. The proof shows that $p$ is the maximum dimension of any positive-definite subspace, making it an intrinsic geometric invariant.
