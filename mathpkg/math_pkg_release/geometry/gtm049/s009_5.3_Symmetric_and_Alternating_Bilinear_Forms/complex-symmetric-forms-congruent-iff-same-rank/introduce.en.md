The corollary to Proposition 7 gives a complete classification of symmetric bilinear forms over $\mathbb{C}$: congruence is determined entirely by rank. This is analogous to the alternating case (Corollary 3) and follows directly from the canonical form $\operatorname{diag}(I_r, 0)$.
