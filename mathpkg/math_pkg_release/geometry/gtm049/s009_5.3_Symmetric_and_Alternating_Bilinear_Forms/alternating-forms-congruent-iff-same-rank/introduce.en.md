Corollary 3 provides a complete classification of alternating bilinear forms up to congruence: two such forms are congruent precisely when they have the same rank. This follows from Corollary 2, since the standard symplectic matrix $A_{n,s}$ is completely determined by the dimension $n$ and the number $s$ of hyperbolic blocks (where $2s = \operatorname{rank}$).
