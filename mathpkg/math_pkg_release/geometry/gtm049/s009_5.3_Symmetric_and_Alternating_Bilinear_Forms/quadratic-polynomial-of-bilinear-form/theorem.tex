If $(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$ is an ordered basis of $V$, $\sigma(a_i, a_j) = s_{ij}$ and $a = \sum x_i a_i$, then

$$q(a) = \sigma(a, a) = \sum s_{ij} x_i x_j.$$

The homogeneous polynomial

$$f(X_1, \ldots, X_n) = \sum s_{ij} X_i X_j$$

is called the \textbf{quadratic polynomial} of $\sigma$ with respect to the ordered basis $(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$.
