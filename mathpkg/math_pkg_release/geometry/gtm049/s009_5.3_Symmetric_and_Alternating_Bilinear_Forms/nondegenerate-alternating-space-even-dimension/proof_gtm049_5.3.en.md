**PROOF.** By Theorem 2, a non-degenerate alternating space $V$ decomposes as an orthogonal direct sum of 2-dimensional subspaces $L_i$, each of which admits a basis with matrix $\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$. Each $L_i$ is non-degenerate, and their number $s$ satisfies $\dim V = 2s$, which is even.
