Theorem 3 establishes that any symmetric bilinear form which is not alternating admits an orthogonal decomposition into $r$ non-degenerate one-dimensional subspaces plus the radical $V^\perp$. The number $r$ equals the rank of $\sigma$. Over fields of characteristic $\neq 2$, an alternating symmetric form is necessarily zero, so the non-alternating condition is only relevant in characteristic 2.
