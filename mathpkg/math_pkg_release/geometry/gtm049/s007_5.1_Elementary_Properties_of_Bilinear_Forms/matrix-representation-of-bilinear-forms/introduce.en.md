Proposition 1 formalizes the correspondence between bilinear forms and their matrix representations. Fixing an ordered basis gives two canonical linear isomorphisms: one into the space of $n \times n$ matrices, another into the space of bilinear polynomials. Both isomorphisms are linear, meaning the vector space structure of $\mathcal{B}(V)$ transfers faithfully to matrices and polynomials.
