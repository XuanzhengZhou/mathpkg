This exercise explores the relationship between the non-degeneracy conditions on a bilinear form and the properties of the associated linear maps $\varrho$ and $\tilde{\sigma}$ from $V$ to $V^*$. In the finite-dimensional case, the two conditions become equivalent and characterize the non-degeneracy of the form.
