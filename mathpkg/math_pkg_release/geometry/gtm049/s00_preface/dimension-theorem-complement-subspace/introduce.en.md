For any subspace $K$ of a finite dimensional vector space $V$, there exists a complementary subspace $M$ such that $V$ is the direct sum of $M$ and $K$. The subspace $M$ is obtained by extending a basis of $K$ to a basis of $V$ and taking the span of the newly added basis vectors.
