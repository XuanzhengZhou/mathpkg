Let $F$ be a finite field and let $F^*$ be its multiplicative group, viewed as a $\mathbb{Z}$-module. If $m$ is the positive generator of the smallest invariant factor of $F^*$, show that $x^m = 1$ for all $x \in F^*$ and that there exists an element $x_0 \in F^*$ whose annihilator is $\mathbb{Z}m$.
