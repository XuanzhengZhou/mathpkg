Let $$c$$ be a common eigenvector of $$f$$ and $$g$$ and let $$M$$ be a subspace for which $$V = M \oplus [c]$$. If $$mf$$ is expressed as $$mf' + xc$$ where $$m, mf' \in M$$ and $$x \in F$$, it is easy to check that $$f'$$ is a linear mapping of $$M$$ into $$M$$ and $$(fg)' = f'g'$$. Now use induction on dim $$V$$.
