Let $R$ be a principal ideal domain and let $M$ be a finitely generated $R$-module.

(a) In the proof of the uniqueness of invariant factors, suppose $[d] = [m_j] + [m_k]$ and $d = um_j + vm_k$. Show that by a suitable change of basis, $m_j b_j + m_k b_k = d b_k'$ and conclude that $m_k$ divides $m_j$.

(b) If $d$ is a greatest common divisor of $m_k$ and $n_k$ with $d = um_k + vn_k$, show from the defining relations that $m_k$ divides $n_k$.
