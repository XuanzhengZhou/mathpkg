This exercise introduces the terminology of ranges and pencils in projective geometry. A range of points on a line dualizes to a pencil of lines through a point (in the plane) or a pencil of planes through a line (in 3-space). These are all one-dimensional projective spaces and illustrate how duality identifies geometrically distinct but structurally identical configurations.
