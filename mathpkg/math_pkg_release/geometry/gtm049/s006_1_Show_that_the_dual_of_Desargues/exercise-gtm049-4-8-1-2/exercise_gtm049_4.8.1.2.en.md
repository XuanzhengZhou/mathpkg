Assume that $n \geq 2$ and let $U_n$ be the point $A_n U \cap A_n'$. Show that $(A_0, \ldots, A_{n-1}, U_n)$ is a frame of reference for the subgeometry $\mathcal{P}(A_n')$ and that the dual frame of reference is

$$(A_0' \cap A_n', \ldots, A_{n-1}' \cap A_n', U' \cap A_n').$$
