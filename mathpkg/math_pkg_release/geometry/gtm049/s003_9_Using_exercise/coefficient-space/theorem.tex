For a system of linear equations
$$\sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} X_j = b_i \quad (i = 1, \ldots, m)$$
or homogeneous equations
$$\sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} X_j = 0 \quad (i = 1, \ldots, m),$$
the **coefficient space** is the subspace of $F^n$ spanned by the rows $(a_{i1}, \ldots, a_{in})$, $i = 1, \ldots, m$. The number $r$ appearing in the statements of Theorems 3 and 4 is the dimension of the coefficient space.
