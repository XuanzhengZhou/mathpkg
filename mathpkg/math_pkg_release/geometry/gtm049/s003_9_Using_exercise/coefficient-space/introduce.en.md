The coefficient space of a system of linear equations is the subspace of $F^n$ spanned by the rows of the coefficient matrix. Its dimension $r$ — the row rank — is the key invariant appearing in the dimension formulas for solution spaces: the solution space of the homogeneous system has dimension $n - r$, while the solution set of the inhomogeneous system is either empty or an affine subspace of dimension $n - r$.
