Theorem 5 is the fundamental classification of affine isomorphisms. In dimension one, an affine isomorphism determines only a bijection of the ground fields. In dimension two or higher, it determines both a field isomorphism and a semi-linear isomorphism of the underlying vector spaces. This theorem generalizes the earlier result for affinities (where the fields coincide) to the case of possibly different ground fields.
