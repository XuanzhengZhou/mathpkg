This exercise establishes the projective-geometric characterization of affine dilatations and translations. A collineation leaving the line at infinity invariant restricts to a dilatation exactly when it is a central collineation with the line at infinity as axis. Furthermore, the restriction is a translation precisely when the center also lies on the line at infinity, making it an elation.
