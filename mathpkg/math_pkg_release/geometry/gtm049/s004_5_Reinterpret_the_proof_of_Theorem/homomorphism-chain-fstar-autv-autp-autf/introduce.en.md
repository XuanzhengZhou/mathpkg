Proposition 5 describes the chain of homomorphisms connecting the multiplicative group of the field $F^*$, the general linear group $\operatorname{Aut} V$, the automorphism group of the projective space $\operatorname{Aut} \mathcal{P}(V)$, and the automorphism group of the field $\operatorname{Aut} F$. It shows that scalar multiples of the identity form the kernel of the map from linear automorphisms to projective automorphisms, and that the projective group is the kernel of the map to field automorphisms.
