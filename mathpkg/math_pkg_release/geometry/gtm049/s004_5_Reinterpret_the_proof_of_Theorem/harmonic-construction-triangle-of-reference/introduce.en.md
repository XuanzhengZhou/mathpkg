This exercise requests a coordinate-based reinterpretation of the Harmonic Construction theorem. Using triangle-of-reference coordinates, the configuration yields the specific coordinate rows $(-1,1,1)$, $(1,-1,1)$, $(1,1,-1)$ for the vertices $A$, $B$, $C$, providing an algebraic verification of the classical geometric construction.
