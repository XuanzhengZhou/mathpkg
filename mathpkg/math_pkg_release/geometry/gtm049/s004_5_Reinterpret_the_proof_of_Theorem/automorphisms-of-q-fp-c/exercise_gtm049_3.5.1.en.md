Prove that the identity mapping is the only automorphism of each of the fields $\mathbb{Q}$ and $\mathbb{F}_p$ for any prime $p$. Show further that there are only two automorphisms of $\mathbb{C}$ that fix all the real numbers: the identity mapping and the conjugation mapping $x + iy \to x - iy$.
