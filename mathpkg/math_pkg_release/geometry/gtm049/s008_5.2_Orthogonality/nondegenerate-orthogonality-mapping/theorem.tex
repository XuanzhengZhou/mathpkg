Let $\sigma$ be a non-degenerate bilinear form on a vector space $V$. Then the mappings $\bot(\sigma), \top(\sigma)$ are one-one mappings of the set of all subspaces of $V$ onto itself and have the following properties:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $\dim M^{\perp} = \dim V - \dim M = \dim M^{\top}$;
\item $M^{\perp \top} = M = M^{\top \perp}$;
\item $M \subset N$ if, and only if, $M^{\perp} \supset N^{\perp}$; $M \subset N$ if, and only if, $M^{\top} \supset N^{\top}$;
\item $(M + N)^{\perp} = M^{\perp} \cap N^{\perp}$, \quad $(M + N)^{\top} = M^{\top} \cap N^{\top}$;
\item $(M \cap N)^{\perp} = M^{\perp} + N^{\perp}$, \quad $(M \cap N)^{\top} = M^{\top} + N^{\top}$.
\end{enumerate}
