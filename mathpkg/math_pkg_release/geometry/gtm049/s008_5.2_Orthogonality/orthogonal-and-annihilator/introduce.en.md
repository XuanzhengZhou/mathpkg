Lemma 1 establishes a crucial link between the orthogonal complement operators $\perp(\sigma)$, $\top(\sigma)$ and the annihilator mapping $\circ$ from §4.6. The right orthogonal complement $M^{\perp}(\sigma)$ equals the annihilator $(M\tilde{\sigma})^{\circ}$ of the image of $M$ under the right correspondence map $\tilde{\sigma} \colon V \to V^{*}$, and similarly $M^{\top}(\sigma) = (M\tilde{\sigma})^{\circ}$. This connection yields a clean alternative proof of the dimension formula in Proposition 4.
