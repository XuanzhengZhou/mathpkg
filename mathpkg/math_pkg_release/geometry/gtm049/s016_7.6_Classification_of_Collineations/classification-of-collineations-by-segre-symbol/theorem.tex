Let $f, g$ be linear mappings of $V$ into $V$ whose minimum polynomials factorize in $F[X]$ into linear factors. Then $\mathcal{P}(f)$ and $\mathcal{P}(g)$ have the same Segre symbol if and only if there exists a collineation of $\mathcal{P}(V)$ mapping the invariant configuration of $\mathcal{P}(f)$ onto the invariant configuration of $\mathcal{P}(g)$.
