Let $f$ be a linear mapping of $V$ into $V$ and suppose that the elementary divisors of $f$ are $(X - x_i)^{n_{ij}}$, $i = 1, \ldots, t$; $j = 1, \ldots, k_i$, where
$$n_{i1} \geq n_{i2} \geq \cdots \geq n_{ik_i}.$$
To each eigenvalue $x_i$ there corresponds the $k_i$-tuple $(n_{i1}, \ldots, n_{ik_i})$ and we define the set of all these,
$$\{(n_{11}, \ldots, n_{1k_1}), \ldots, (n_{t1}, \ldots, n_{tk_t})\},$$
to be the \textit{Segre symbol} of $f$. The Segre symbol is defined only for linear mappings whose minimum polynomials factorize in $F[X]$ into linear factors.
