The Segre symbol is a combinatorial invariant assigned to a linear endomorphism whose minimum polynomial splits into linear factors over the base field. It records the sizes of the elementary divisor blocks associated to each eigenvalue, arranged as nonincreasing tuples, thereby encoding the Jordan block structure of the mapping. This symbol provides a complete classification of collineations of projective spaces up to the action of the projective general linear group on invariant configurations.
