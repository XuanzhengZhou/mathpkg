If a subspace $M$ is non-degenerate (the restriction of $\sigma$ to $M$ has trivial radical), then $M$ intersects its orthogonal complements only in the zero vector. This lemma is crucial for the direct sum decomposition $V = M \oplus M^{\perp}$ when $M$ is non-degenerate (Proposition 5), ensuring the sum is indeed direct.
