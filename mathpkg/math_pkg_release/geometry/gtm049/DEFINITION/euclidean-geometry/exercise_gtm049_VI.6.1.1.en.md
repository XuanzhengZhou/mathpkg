Define a mapping $d$ of the ordered pairs of vectors of the real vector space $V$ by setting $d(a, a) = 0$ for all $a$ and $d(a, b) = 1$ whenever $a \neq b$. Which of the six distance axioms D.1-D.6 does $d$ satisfy? Which does it fail?
