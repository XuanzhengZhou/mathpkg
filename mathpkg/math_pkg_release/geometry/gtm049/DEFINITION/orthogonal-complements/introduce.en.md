The right orthogonal complement $M^{\perp}$ consists of all vectors that are orthogonal to every vector in $M$ when placed in the second argument. The left orthogonal complement $M^{\top}$ consists of all vectors orthogonal to every vector in $M$ when placed in the first argument. Both are subspaces, and the maps $\perp$ and $\top$ are inclusion-reversing operations on the lattice of subspaces.
