This proposition gives the fundamental dimension formula for orthogonal complements. In the non-degenerate case (where $V^{\top} = V^{\perp} = 0$), it reduces to $\dim M + \dim M^{\perp} = \dim V$, analogous to the dimension formula for annihilators in the dual space. The proof uses either a basis argument or the link between orthogonal complements and annihilators via the map $\varrho: V \to V^*$.
