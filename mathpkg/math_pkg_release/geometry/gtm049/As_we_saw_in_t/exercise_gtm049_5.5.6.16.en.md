If a non-degenerate quadric $Q$ in a projective geometry of dimension 3 contains one generator $L$, show that every plane through $L$ cuts $Q$ in $L$ and another generator. Show that a coordinate system can be chosen with respect to which $Q$ has equation $X_0X_3 - X_1X_2 = 0$ and $L$ has equations $X_0 = X_1 = 0$.
