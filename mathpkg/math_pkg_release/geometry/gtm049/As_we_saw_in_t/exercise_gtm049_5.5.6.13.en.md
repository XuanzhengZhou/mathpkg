Let $Q(\sigma)$ be a non-degenerate conic containing three distinct points $A_0, A_2, E$ and let $A_1$ be the pole of $A_0A_2$, i.e., the point $(A_0A_2)^{\perp(\sigma)}$. Show that if $A_0A_1A_2$ is taken as triangle of reference and $E$ as unit point then the equation of $Q$ is $X_0X_2 - X_1^2 = 0$.
