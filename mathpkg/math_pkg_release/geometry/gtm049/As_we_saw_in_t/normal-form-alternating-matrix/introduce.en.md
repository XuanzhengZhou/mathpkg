Corollary 2 provides the normal form for alternating bilinear forms under congruence: any alternating matrix of rank $2s$ is congruent to a block-diagonal matrix consisting of $s$ copies of the standard hyperbolic block $egin{pmatrix}0 & 1 \ -1 & 0\end{pmatrix}$ and zeros. This completely solves the congruence classification problem for alternating matrices over any field.
