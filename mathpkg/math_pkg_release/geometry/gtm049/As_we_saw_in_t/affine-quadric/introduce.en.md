An affine quadric is defined by an equation of degree at most 2 in an affine space. Unlike projective quadrics, which are defined by homogeneous quadratic equations, affine quadrics can include linear and constant terms. The image of an affine quadric under a translation is again an affine quadric, and more generally, the image under any affine isomorphism is an affine quadric.
