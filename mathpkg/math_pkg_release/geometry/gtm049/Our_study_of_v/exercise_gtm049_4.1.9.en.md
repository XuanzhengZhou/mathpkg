Let $f$ be a linear mapping of $V$ into $V$ satisfying $f^2 = f$. Show that if $M$ is the image of $f$ and $K$ is the kernel of $f$, then $V = M \oplus K$. Hence show that $f$ is the projection of $V$ onto $M$ with kernel $K$.
