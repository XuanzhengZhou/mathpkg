The identity matrix $I_n$ is the $n \times n$ matrix with ones on the main diagonal and zeros elsewhere. It represents the identity linear transformation on an $n$-dimensional vector space with respect to any single ordered basis. As the multiplicative identity in the ring of square matrices, it satisfies $AI = A$ and $IB = B$ for all conformable matrices $A, B$.
