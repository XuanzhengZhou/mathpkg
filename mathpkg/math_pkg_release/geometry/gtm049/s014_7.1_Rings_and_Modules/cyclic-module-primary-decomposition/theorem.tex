Let $M$ be a cyclic $F[X]$-module whose annihilator $\mathfrak{a}(M)$ is generated by the non-zero polynomial $p$. If $p_1, \ldots, p_n$ are the distinct monic irreducible factors of $p$, then
$$M = [a_1] \oplus \cdots \oplus [a_n],$$
where $\mathfrak{a}(a_i)$ is generated by a power of $p_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$).
