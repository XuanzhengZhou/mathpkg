Let $M$ be a finitely generated module over $F[X]$. Then $M$ can be expressed as a direct sum
$$M = [a_1] \oplus \cdots \oplus [a_r] \oplus [a_{r+1}] \oplus \cdots \oplus [a_k]$$
where $\mathfrak{a}(a_1) = \cdots = \mathfrak{a}(a_r) = 0$ and $\mathfrak{a}(a_{r+1}) \subset \cdots \subset \mathfrak{a}(a_k)$ are non-zero proper ideals of $F[X]$. The integers $r$ and $k$ and the ideals $\mathfrak{a}(a_i)$ are uniquely determined by $M$.
