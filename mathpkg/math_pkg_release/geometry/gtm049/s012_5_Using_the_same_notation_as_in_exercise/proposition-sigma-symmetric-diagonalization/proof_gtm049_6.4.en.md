By Lemma 2 we can find an eigenvector $a_1$ of $f$. Since $\sigma$ is positive definite we may choose $a_1$ so that $\sigma(a_1, a_1) = 1$. The subspace $[a_1]$ is non-degenerate with respect to $\sigma$ and therefore

$$V = [a_1] \oplus [a_1]^{\perp(\sigma)}$$

by Proposition 5 of Chapter V (p. 96). Our proposition now follows by Lemma 3 and induction on the dimension of $V$.
