This lemma extracts the equality condition of the Schwarz inequality from Exercise 7. When the triangle inequality holds with equality, the vectors $u = b-c$ and $v = a-b$ satisfy $\sigma(u,v) = \|u\|\|v\|$, forcing $u$ and $v$ to be linearly dependent. This proves collinearity of $a, b, c$ and is a key step in showing that distance-preserving maps preserve lines.
