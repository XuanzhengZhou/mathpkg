Rank 0 is always possible. Let $$V = M \oplus V^\perp$$ where $$\dim M = \operatorname{rank} \sigma = r \geq 1$$ and $$Q(\sigma_M)$$ is empty. Choose coordinates so that $$Q$$ has equation $$a_0X_0^2 + \cdots + a_{r-1}X_{r-1}^2 = 0$$ ($$a_0 \cdots a_{r-1} \neq 0$$).

(i) As long as $$a_0, \ldots, a_{r-1}$$ are all positive or all negative, $$Q(\sigma_M)$$ is empty. Thus any $$r \geq 1$$ is possible over $$\mathbf{R}$$.

(ii) Over $$\mathbf{C}$$, if $$r > 1$$ then $$Q(\sigma_M)$$ is not empty. Hence $$r = 1$$.

(iii) Over $$\mathbf{F}_3$$, if $$r > 2$$ then $$Q(\sigma_M)$$ is not empty. If $$r = 2$$ and $$a_0 = a_1$$, then $$Q(\sigma_M)$$ is empty. Hence $$r$$ is $$1$$ or $$2$$.
