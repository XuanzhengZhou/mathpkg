If $Q'$ is the affine conic in $A$ determined by a non-degenerate projective conic $Q$ in $\mathcal{P}(\mathbf{R}^3)$, then $Q'$ is called an ellipse, a parabola or a hyperbola according as the intersection of $Q$ with the line at infinity is empty, consists of one point or consists of two points. Show that an affine conic with equation
$$a_{11}X_1^2 + 2a_{12}X_1X_2 + a_{22}X_2^2 + 2a_{01}X_1 + 2a_{02}X_2 + a_{00} = 0,$$
where the $3 \times 3$ matrix $(a_{ij})$ is invertible, is an ellipse, parabola or hyperbola according as $a_{11}a_{22} - a_{12}^2$ is positive, zero or negative, respectively.
