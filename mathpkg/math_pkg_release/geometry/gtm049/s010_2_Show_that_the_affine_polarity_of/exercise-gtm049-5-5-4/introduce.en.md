This exercise asks for the possible ranks of quadrics that are contained in a hyperplane, over the real and complex fields. The rank is determined by the dimension of $V^{\perp(\sigma)}$, and the answer depends on whether the ground field admits anisotropic forms of various dimensions (e.g., over $\mathbf{R}$, the only ranks possible are those for which a positive or negative definite form exists).
