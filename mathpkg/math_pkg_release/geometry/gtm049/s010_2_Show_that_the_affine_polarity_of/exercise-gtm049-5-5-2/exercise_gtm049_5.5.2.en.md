Show that the affine polarity of $\mathcal{A}(\mathbf{R}^2)$ (see exercise 5 of §5.5) which sends the point $(x_1, x_2) \neq (0, 0)$ into the line $x_1X_1 + x_2X_2 + 1 = 0$ has empty conic. (This polarity is the product of a reflexion in the origin followed by the polarity with conic $X_1^2 + X_2^2 = 1$.)
