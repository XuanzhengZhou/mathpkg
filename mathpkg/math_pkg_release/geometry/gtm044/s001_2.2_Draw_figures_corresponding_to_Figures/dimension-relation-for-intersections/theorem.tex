Let $V(p_i)$ and $V(p_j)$ be algebraic sets. Then
$$\operatorname{cod}(V(p_i) \cap V(p_j)) \leqslant \operatorname{cod} V(p_i) + \operatorname{cod} V(p_j).$$
In particular, for curves in $\mathbb{P}^2(\mathbb{C})$ this yields
$$\dim(V(p_i) \cap V(p_j)) \geqslant 0,$$
hence $V(p_i) \cap V(p_j) \neq \emptyset$ (since $\dim \emptyset = -1$).
