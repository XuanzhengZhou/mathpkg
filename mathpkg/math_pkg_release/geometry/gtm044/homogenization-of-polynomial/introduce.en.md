Homogenization converts an affine polynomial into a homogeneous polynomial by introducing a new variable to make every term have the same total degree. This operation is fundamental for passing from affine varieties to their projective completions. The homogenization of $p$ at $X_{n+1}$ is denoted $H_{X_{n+1}}(p)$ and satisfies $D_{X_{n+1}}(H_{X_{n+1}}(p)) = p$, where $D$ is the dehomogenization operator.
