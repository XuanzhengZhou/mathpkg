Statement (3.12) establishes that the extension map from the lattice $\mathcal{J}(R)$ of closed ideals to $\mathcal{J}(R_{\mathfrak{p}})$ is a lattice homomorphism. The intersection-preserving property follows from the corresponding fact for $\mathcal{I}(R)$ (equation 13), while the sum-preserving property requires the radical: $(\sqrt{\mathfrak{a}+\mathfrak{b}})^e = \sqrt{\mathfrak{a}^e+\mathfrak{b}^e}$. This uses the general inclusion $(\sqrt{\mathfrak{c}})^e \subset \sqrt{\mathfrak{c}^e}$ valid in any ring extension.
