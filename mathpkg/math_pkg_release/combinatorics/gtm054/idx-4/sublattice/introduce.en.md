A sublattice is a subset of a lattice that is itself a lattice under the induced order, with the additional requirement that the meet and join of any two elements in the subset (computed in the ambient lattice) also belong to the subset. This closure condition ensures that the sublattice inherits the lattice operations faithfully from the ambient structure.
