Let $\mathcal{A}$ be a subspace of $(\mathcal{P}(U), +)$. The collection $M(\mathcal{A})$ of minimal, nonempty subsets belonging to $\mathcal{A}$ is called the collection of \textit{elementary sets} in $\mathcal{A}$. The collection $M(\mathcal{A})$ is incommensurable, and if $\mathcal{A}$ is a subspace of $\mathcal{P}(U)$, then $M(\mathcal{A})$ spans $\mathcal{A}$.
