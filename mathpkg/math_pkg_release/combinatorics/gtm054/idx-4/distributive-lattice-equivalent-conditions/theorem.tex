A lattice $(U, \leq)$ satisfies the following equivalent conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $x \wedge (y \vee z) = (x \wedge y) \vee (x \wedge z)$ for all $x, y, z \in U$;
\item[(b)] $(x \vee y) \wedge z = (x \wedge z) \vee (y \wedge z)$ for all $x, y, z \in U$;
\item[(c)] $(x \vee y) \wedge (x \vee z) = x \vee (y \wedge z)$ for all $x, y, z \in U$.
\end{enumerate}
