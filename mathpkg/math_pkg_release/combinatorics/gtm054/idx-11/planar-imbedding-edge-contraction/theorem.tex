Let $\Gamma$ be a planar multigraph and let $Z_1, \ldots, Z_k$ be a planar imbedding of $\Gamma$. If $e \in \operatorname{Fnd}(\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma))$ and $e \in Z_1 \cap Z_2$, then $Z_1 + Z_2, Z_3, \ldots, Z_k$ is a planar imbedding of $\Gamma_{(e)}$, the multigraph obtained by contracting edge $e$.
