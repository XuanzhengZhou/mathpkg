Lemma E14 provides the fundamental algebraic bridge between a multigraph and its contractions: the cocycle space of the contraction is precisely the restriction of the original cocycle space to the edge set $E_1$, while the cycle space of the contraction is the projection of the original cycle space. This lemma is essential for proving that planarity (defined combinatorially via the cycle space) is preserved under contraction operations.
