Let $\Gamma = (V, f, E)$ be a biconnected multigraph. Given any planar imbedding for $\Gamma$, for each vertex $x$ of $\Gamma$ there exists a cyclic ordering $e_0, \ldots, e_{\rho(x)-1}$ of the elements of $f^*(x)$ such that $e_i$ and $e_{i+1}$ lie in a common cycle of the imbedding for $i = 0, \ldots, \rho(x) - 1$ (indices taken modulo $\rho(x)$).
