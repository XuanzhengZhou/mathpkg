Show that if $a$ is a polynomial of degree $m$ with $a_0 \neq 0$, if $b$ is a polynomial of degree $n$, and if $m \leq n$, then $b/a = d + c/a$ where $d$ is a polynomial of degree $n - m$ and $c \in \mathbb{M}_m$.

If $z \in \mathbb{C}$ and $n > 0$, then $1 - zx^n$ is a unit by Proposition A5. It is particularly useful to know the form of its inverse.
