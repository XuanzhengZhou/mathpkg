(P. Erdős and T. Gallai [e.4]). Prove for any graph

$$\nu_1 \leq \begin{cases} 
\left( \frac{2\alpha_1}{2} \right) & \text{if } \nu_0 = 2\alpha_1; \\
\left( \frac{2\alpha_1 + 1}{2} \right) & \text{if } 2\alpha_1 < \nu_0 \leq \frac{5\alpha_1 + 2}{2}; \\
\left( \frac{\alpha_1}{2} \right) + \alpha_1(\nu_0 - \alpha_1) & \text{if } (5\alpha_1 + 2)/2 < \nu_0.
\end{cases}$$

For further information on 1-factors, see Lovász [l.5].
