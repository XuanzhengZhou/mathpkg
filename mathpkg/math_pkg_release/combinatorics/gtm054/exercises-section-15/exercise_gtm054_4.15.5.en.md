For $Y \subseteq X$ and $L$ a subspace of $\mathbb{Q}^X$, prove that the minimal functions of $L \cap \mathbb{Q}^Y$ are precisely the minimal functions of $L$ with support contained in $Y$.

The next proposition, which is parallel to Proposition IIIC1, is the central result of this section.
