If $Z_1, \ldots, Z_m$ is a planar imbedding of a connected multigraph $\Gamma$ without isthmus, then the multigraph $\Theta$ whose vertex cocycles are $Z_1, \ldots, Z_m$ is called the multigraph orthogonal to $\Gamma$ with respect to (the planar imbedding) $Z_1, \ldots, Z_n$. One observes that $\Theta$ is already furnished with an imbedding $C_1, \ldots, C_n$ (which are the vertex cocycles of $\Gamma$).
