Given a planar imbedding of a connected multigraph without isthmuses, the orthogonal multigraph is constructed by taking the planar imbedding cycles as its vertex cocycles. This construction realizes Whitney's combinatorial dual: the orthogonal multigraph is itself endowed with a planar imbedding given by the vertex cocycles of the original multigraph. The relationship is symmetric -- the regions of one correspond to the vertices of the other.
