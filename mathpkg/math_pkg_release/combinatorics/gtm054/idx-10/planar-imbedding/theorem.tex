A sequence $Z_1, \ldots, Z_k$ of cycles of the multigraph $\Gamma$ is called a planar imbedding of $\Gamma$ if:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\{Z_1, \ldots, Z_k\}$ spans $\mathcal{X}(\Gamma)$, and
\item if $e \in \mathrm{Fnd}(\mathcal{X}(\Gamma))$, then $e \in Z_i$ for precisely two indices $i \in \{1, \ldots, k\}$.
\end{enumerate}
