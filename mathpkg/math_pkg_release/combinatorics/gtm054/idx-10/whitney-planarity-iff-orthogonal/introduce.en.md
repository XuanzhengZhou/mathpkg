Whitney's characterization of planar multigraphs states that a multigraph without isthmuses is planar if and only if there exists an orthogonal multigraph. This theorem bridges the combinatorial definition of planarity via planar imbeddings with the algebraic notion of orthogonal multigraphs. It is an immediate consequence of the definition of planar imbedding and Proposition D3, which constructs a multigraph from a spanning set of cocycles with each edge in exactly two members.
