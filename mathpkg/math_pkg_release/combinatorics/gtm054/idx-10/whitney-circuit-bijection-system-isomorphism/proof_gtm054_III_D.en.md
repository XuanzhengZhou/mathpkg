Define $g = hp$, and observe that the lower rectangle in Figure D11 commutes. Thus $\Gamma' = (U, g, E)$ and $\Theta$ are system-isomorphic. By this and the hypothesis, $p[\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma')] = \mathcal{Z}(\Theta) = p[\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)]$. Since $p$ is a bijection, $\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma') = \mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)$. Hence $\mathcal{Z}^\perp(\Gamma') = \mathcal{Z}^\perp(\Gamma)$, and the result follows from Proposition D8.
