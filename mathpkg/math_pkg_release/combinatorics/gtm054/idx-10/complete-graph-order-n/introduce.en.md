The complete graph $K_n$ of order $n$ is the graph on $n$ vertices where every pair of distinct vertices is joined by exactly one edge. It is the graph $(V, \mathcal{P}_2(V))$ where $\mathcal{P}_2(V)$ denotes the set of all 2-element subsets of $V$. The cycle space of $K_n$ has dimension $\binom{n-1}{2}$ and is spanned by the cycles corresponding to all 3-element subsets of $V$.
