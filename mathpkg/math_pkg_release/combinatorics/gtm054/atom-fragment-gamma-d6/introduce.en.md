D6 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $A$ is an atom and $W$ is a fragment of $\Gamma$, then exactly one of the following holds: $A \subseteq W; A \subseteq \overline{W};$ or $A \subseteq N(W)$.
