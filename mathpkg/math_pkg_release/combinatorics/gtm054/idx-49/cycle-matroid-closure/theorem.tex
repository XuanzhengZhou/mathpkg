Let $\Lambda = (E, \mathcal{M}(\mathcal{X}(\Gamma)))$ be the cycle matroid of $\Gamma = (V, f, E)$. Then:

(1) $r(F)$ is the number of edges in a spanning forest of $\Gamma_F$, and $r(F) = \nu_0(\Gamma_F) - \nu_{-1}(\Gamma_F)$.

(2) The closure is given by

$$c(F) = F \cup \{e \in E \setminus F : e \in Z \subseteq F \cup \{e\} \text{ for some } Z \in \mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)\}.$$

In other words, if $e \in c(F)$ then the vertices incident with $e$ are joined by a path all of whose edges are in $F$. If $W_1, \ldots, W_k$ are the vertex sets of the components of $\Gamma_F$, then $c(F)$ is the set of edges of $\bigoplus_{i=1}^{k} \Gamma_{W_i}$.
