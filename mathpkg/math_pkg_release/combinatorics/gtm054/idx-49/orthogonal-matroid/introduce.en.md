The orthogonal matroid (or dual matroid) $\Lambda^{\perp}$ of a matroid $\Lambda$ on an $n$-element set is defined by taking the complements of the bases of $\Lambda$ as the bases of $\Lambda^{\perp}$. Its independent sets are the subsets of these dual bases, and the dependent sets (the matroid $\mathcal{M}^{\perp}$) consist of all subsets that are not independent. The orthogonal matroid has rank $n - r$, where $r$ is the rank of $\Lambda$.
