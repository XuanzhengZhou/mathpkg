The closure operator of a matroid generalizes the notion of linear span from vector spaces. Given a matroid with rank function $r$, an element $x$ belongs to the closure of a set $U$ precisely when adding $x$ to $U$ does not increase the rank. This operator satisfies the exchange property and enables the characterization of matroids in terms of closed sets.
