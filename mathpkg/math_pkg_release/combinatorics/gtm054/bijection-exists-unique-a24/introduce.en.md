A24 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

$f$ is a bijection if and only if there exists $b \in X^Y$ such that $bf = 1_X$ and $fb = 1_Y$. In this case $b = f^{-1}$, and so $b$ is unique.
