A11 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Given any bipartite graph $B$, there exists a $\hat{\rho}(B)$-valent bipartite graph $A = \{W_1, W_2\}, \mathcal{F}$ such that $V_i \subseteq W_i$ for $i = 1, 2$ and $B = A_{V_1 \cup V_2}$.
