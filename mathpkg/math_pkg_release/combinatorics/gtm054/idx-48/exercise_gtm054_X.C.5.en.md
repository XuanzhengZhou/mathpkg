If $(V, r)$ is a rank structure, prove that

**(a)** If $U_1 \subseteq U_2$, $x \in V \setminus U_2$, and $r(U_1 \cup \{x\}) = r(U_1)$, then $r(U_2 \cup \{x\}) = r(U_2)$.

**(b)** $r(U_1 \cup U_2) + r(U_1 \cap U_2) \leq r(U_1) + r(U_2)$ for all $U_1, U_2 \in \mathcal{P}(V)$.
