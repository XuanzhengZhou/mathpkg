Let $\Gamma$ be connected, let $S \subseteq V$ be a separating set of $\Gamma$, let $W_1, \ldots, W_k$ be the vertex sets of the components of $\Gamma_{(S)}$, and let $\Theta_i = \Gamma_{W_i + S}$. Suppose that for each $i = 1, \ldots, k$, there exists a $\chi_0(\Theta_i)$-coloring of $\Theta_i$ which is injective when restricted to $S$. Then

$$\chi_0(\Gamma) = \max\{\chi_0(\Theta_i): i = 1, \ldots, k\}.$$
