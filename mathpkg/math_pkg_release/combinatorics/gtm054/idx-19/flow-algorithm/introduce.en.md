The Flow Algorithm is a constructive procedure for finding a maximum flow through a distinguished edge in an integral network. Starting from any feasible flow (such as the zero flow), the algorithm iteratively searches for unsaturated elementary circuits and augments the flow along them. Termination is guaranteed by the fact that each augmentation increases the flow on the distinguished edge by at least one unit, and the flow is bounded above by the capacity function.
