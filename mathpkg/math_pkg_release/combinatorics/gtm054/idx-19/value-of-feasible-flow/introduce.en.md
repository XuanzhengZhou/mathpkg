The value $v(f)$ of a feasible flow $f$ in a classical network is defined as the net flow entering the sink $y_0$, or equivalently, the total flow into $y_0$ minus the total flow out of $y_0$. Exercise E1 asks the reader to verify this formula, which formalizes the definition of the flow value in the source-sink formulation of network flows.
