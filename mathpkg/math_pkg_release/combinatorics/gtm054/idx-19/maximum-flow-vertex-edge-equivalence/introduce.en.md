This result states that the maximum flow through a distinguished vertex $x_0$ in a vertex-capacitated directed graph equals the maximum flow through the corresponding edge $e_0 = (x_0^2, x_0^1)$ in the associated edge-capacitated network constructed via F3. The equality follows from the fact that $h(x_0) = \bar{h}(e_0)$ holds for the bijection $h \mapsto \bar{h}$ defined in F3. This allows the Max-Flow–Min-Cut Theorem (Vertex Form, F2) to be deduced directly from the edge-capacitated version (C6).
