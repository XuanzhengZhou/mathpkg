$$\sum_{T \in \mathcal{P}(V)} (-1)^{|S+T|}[S, T][T, W] = \sum_{T \in \mathcal{P}(V)} (-1)^{|S+T|}[S + T, S + W][S, W]$$
$$= \sum_{R \in \mathcal{P}(V)} (-1)^{|R|}[R, S + W][S, W]$$
$$= \left[ \sum_{R \in \mathcal{P}(S+W)} (-1)^{|R|} \right][S, W]$$
$$= 0^{|S+W|}[S, W] \quad \text{by C22}$$
$$= 0^{|S+W|}.$$

The next result is the inverse of E4; it allows us to recover $s$ when $\bar{s}$ is given.
