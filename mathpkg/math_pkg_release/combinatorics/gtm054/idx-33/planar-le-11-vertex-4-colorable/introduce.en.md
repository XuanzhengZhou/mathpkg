This corollary establishes that any planar graph with at most 11 vertices is 4-colorable. The proof constructs a vertex ordering where each vertex has degree at most 4 in the remaining subgraph, then inductively applies Proposition B5. This result provides a concrete finite bound below which the Four-Color Conjecture is automatically satisfied.
