Let $\Gamma$ be planar with $\nu_0(\Gamma) \geq 4$. A necessary and sufficient condition for $\chi_0(\Gamma) \leq 4$ is that there exist three pairwise-disjoint subsets $\mathcal{E}_1, \mathcal{E}_2, \mathcal{E}_3 \subseteq \mathcal{E}$ such that $\mathcal{E} = \mathcal{E}_1 \cup \mathcal{E}_2 \cup \mathcal{E}_3$, and if $Z$ is any region of a planar imbedding of $\Gamma$, then $|Z \cap \mathcal{E}_1| \equiv |Z \cap \mathcal{E}_2| \equiv |Z \cap \mathcal{E}_3| \pmod{2}$.
