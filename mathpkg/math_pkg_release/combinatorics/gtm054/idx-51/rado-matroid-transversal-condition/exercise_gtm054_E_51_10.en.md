Let $\Lambda_1, \ldots, \Lambda_n$ be systems with the same vertex set $V$. Let $\mathcal{T}_i$ be the collection of transversals of $\Lambda_i$ $(i = 1, \ldots, n)$. Under the assumption that $(V, \bigcap_{i=1}^{n-1} \mathcal{T}_i)$ is an independence system, state and prove a necessary and sufficient condition for the existence of an LCR $(\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_n)$ for the systems $\Lambda_1, \ldots, \Lambda_n$.
