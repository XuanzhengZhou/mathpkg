**Proof.** This is immediate from Proposition A5. By the proposition, the value of $\bar{s}$ on $\mathcal{P}_t(V)$ for smaller $t$ is determined by the value on larger $t$, so the design automatically satisfies the regularity conditions for all intermediate parameters. The dual argument applies to the block-side parameters. $\square$
