Corollary A8 follows immediately from Proposition A5 and describes the design-type of one-sided designs. If a design is a $(t; a)$-design with either $t = 1$ or $a = 1$, then its full design-type consists of all parameters $(1, \ldots, t'; 1, \ldots, a')$ for some $t' \geq t$ and $a' \geq a$, reflecting the hierarchical nature of design parameters.
