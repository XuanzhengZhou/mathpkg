Exercise D19 asks the reader to characterize crucial graphs $\Gamma$ with $\omega(\Gamma) = 2$ or $\alpha_0(\Gamma) = 2$. In the first case, $\Gamma$ must be an odd circuit of length at least 5; in the second case, the complement $\Gamma'$ is an odd circuit of length at least 5. This exercise connects crucial graphs to the classical odd circuit obstructions in perfect graph theory.
