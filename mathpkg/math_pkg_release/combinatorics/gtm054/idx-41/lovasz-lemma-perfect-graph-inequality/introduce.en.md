Lemma D13, due to Lovász, is a critical technical result used in the proof of the Berge Conjecture (Theorem D11). It establishes that a certain inequality relating the covering number $\nu_0$, the independence number $\alpha_0$, and the clique number $\omega$ is preserved under the Lovász graph construction, provided all proper induced subgraphs are perfect.
