Exercise A10 asks the reader to derive the explicit formula $\lambda(i) = \frac{\binom{k}{i}}{\binom{v}{i}} b$ for a $t$-design. This formula expresses the number of blocks containing an $i$-subset as a ratio of binomial coefficients times the total number of blocks $b$. Combined with Exercise A9's recurrence, it provides a complete description of the $\lambda$ function.
