This proposition asserts that in a bipartite graph, the family of critical subsets of $V_1$ (those subsets achieving the maximum deficiency $\delta_1$) is closed under the operations of union and intersection. It follows from the deficiency subadditivity inequality $\delta(U_1) + \delta(U_2) \leq \delta(U_1 \cup U_2) + \delta(U_1 \cap U_2)$ together with the maximality of $\delta_1$, which forces equality throughout. This property is fundamental for understanding the structure of maximum matchings in bipartite graphs.
