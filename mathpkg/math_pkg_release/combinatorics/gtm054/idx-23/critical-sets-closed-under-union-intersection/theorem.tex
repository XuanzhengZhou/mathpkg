Let $B = (V_1, V_2, \mathcal{E})$ be a bipartite graph and let $\delta_1 = \max\{\delta(U) : U \subseteq V_1\}$ where $\delta(U) = |U| - |N(U)|$. If $U_1, U_2 \subseteq V_1$ are critical subsets (i.e., $\delta(U_1) = \delta(U_2) = \delta_1$), then $U_1 \cup U_2$ and $U_1 \cap U_2$ are also critical subsets.
