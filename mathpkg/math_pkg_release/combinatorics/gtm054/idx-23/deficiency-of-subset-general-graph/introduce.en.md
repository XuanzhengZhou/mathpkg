This definition extends the concept of deficiency from bipartite graphs to arbitrary graphs. Instead of counting all vertices in $U$, one counts the number of components of the subgraph induced by $U$ that have an odd number of vertices. When $U$ is an independent set, every vertex forms its own component (of odd size 1), so $\|U\| = |U|$ and the new definition reduces to the bipartite one.
