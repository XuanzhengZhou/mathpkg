This corollary provides a fundamental identity relating three key parameters of a bipartite graph: the vertex covering number $\nu_0$, the edge independence number $\alpha_1$, and the graph deficiency $\delta$. It follows directly from combining Proposition B1 ($\delta = \delta_1 + \delta_2$) with Corollary A5. This identity motivates extensive work in the theory of 1-factors, as Theorem B14 shows it holds for all graphs, not just bipartite ones.
