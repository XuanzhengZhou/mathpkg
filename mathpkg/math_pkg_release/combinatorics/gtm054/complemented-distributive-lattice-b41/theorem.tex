Let $(U, \leq)$ be a complemented distributive lattice, and let $A$ be the set of atoms of $(U, \leq)$. Let $f: \mathcal{P}(A) \rightarrow U$ be given by

$$f(B) = \bigoplus_{a \in B} a \quad \text{for each } B \in \mathcal{P}(A),$$

where the join of an empty collection is understood to be 0. Then $f$ is a bijection.
