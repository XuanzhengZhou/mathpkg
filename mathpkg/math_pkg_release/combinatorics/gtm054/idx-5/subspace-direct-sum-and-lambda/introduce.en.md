Proposition C6 establishes that the $\Lambda$ operator respects direct sum decompositions. Part (a) shows that a direct sum of subspaces maps to the direct sum of their associated systems. Part (b) provides the converse: a decomposition of $\Lambda(\mathcal{A})$ induces a corresponding decomposition of the subspace $\mathcal{A}$ itself via restriction to the vertex sets of the component systems.
