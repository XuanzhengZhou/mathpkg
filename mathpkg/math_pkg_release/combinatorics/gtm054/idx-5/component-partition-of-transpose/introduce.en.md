Exercises C17 and C18 explore the relationship between the component partition of a system $\Lambda$ and its transpose $\Lambda^*$. Since connectedness is preserved under transpose (C15), the components of $\Lambda$ and $\Lambda^*$ are closely related; these exercises make this relationship precise by examining how component partitions behave under the transpose operation.
