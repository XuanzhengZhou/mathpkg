Exercise C13 concerns the equicut space $\mathcal{E}(U)$ (the subspace of $\mathcal{P}(U)$ consisting of subsets of even cardinality). It asks to prove that $\mathcal{E}(U)$ is always connected and, when $|U| \geq 3$, it is uniquely characterized as the only connected subspace of $\mathcal{P}(U)$ having dimension $|U| - 1$.
