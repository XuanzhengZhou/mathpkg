Proposition C14 states that the transpose operation on hypergraphs (systems) commutes with the direct sum. If $\Lambda_1, \ldots, \Lambda_k$ are hypergraphs whose direct sum is well-defined, then the transpose of the direct sum equals the direct sum of the transposes. This follows from the block-diagonal structure of incidence matrices for direct sums.
