Proposition C22 provides a path-theoretic characterization of connected subspaces. Part (a) states that a subspace $\mathcal{A}$ is connected exactly when any two elements of its foundation lie together in some elementary set. Part (b) shows that the relation of "sharing an elementary set" is an equivalence relation whose equivalence classes give the component partition. This connects the algebraic decomposition theory with a graph-like connectivity notion.
