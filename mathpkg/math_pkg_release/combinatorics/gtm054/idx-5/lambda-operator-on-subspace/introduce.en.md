The map $\Lambda$ assigns to each subspace $\mathcal{A}$ of $\mathcal{P}(V)$ a system (hypergraph) whose vertex set is $\operatorname{Fnd}(\mathcal{A})$ and whose blocks are the elementary sets $\mathcal{M}(\mathcal{A})$. This operator establishes a fundamental bridge between the subspace structure and the system structure, allowing properties of subspaces to be studied via their associated systems.
