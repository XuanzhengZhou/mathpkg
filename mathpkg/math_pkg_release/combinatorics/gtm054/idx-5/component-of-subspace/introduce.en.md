A component of a subspace $\mathcal{A}$ is a nontrivial connected direct summand. Components are the minimal indecomposable pieces into which $\mathcal{A}$ can be uniquely decomposed. The component partition describes how the foundation set $\operatorname{Fnd}(\mathcal{A})$ is partitioned by the foundations of the components, providing a decomposition analogous to connected components in graph theory.
