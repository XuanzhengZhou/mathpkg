Lemma C11 establishes a duality between a subspace $\mathcal{A}$ and its orthogonal complement $\mathcal{A}^{\perp}$ with respect to connectedness. When the ground set $U$ has at least two elements, $\mathcal{A}$ is connected with full foundation if and only if the same holds for $\mathcal{A}^{\perp}$. This symmetry is fundamental to the duality theory of subspaces and systems.
