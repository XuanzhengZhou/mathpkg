The capacity of a cut through a directed edge $e_0$ measures the total capacity of all edges that cross the cut in the forward direction. It is defined using the auxiliary function $g_U$, which indicates the orientation of each edge relative to the vertex set $U$ that determines the cut. The capacity $k(C; e_0)$ is the sum of capacities $k(e)$ over all edges $e$ for which $g_U(e) = 1$, i.e., edges directed from $U$ to its complement.
