This proposition provides the theoretical foundation for the flow augmentation algorithm in integral networks. It states that if a feasible flow is not yet maximal through a given edge, one can find an elementary circuit whose characteristic function adds one unit of flow through that edge while preserving feasibility. By repeatedly applying this augmentation, the algorithm iteratively increases the flow until the maximum is reached.
