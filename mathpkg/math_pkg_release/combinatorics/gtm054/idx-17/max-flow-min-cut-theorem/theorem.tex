Let $(V, k)$ be a network and let $e_0 = (y_0, x_0) \in W$. Then there exists a maximum flow $h$ through $e_0$ such that

$$h(e_0) = \min \bigl( \{k(e_0)\} \cup \{k(C; e_0) : C \text{ is a cut through } e_0\} \bigr).$$

In particular, the maximum flow value through $e_0$ equals the minimum cut capacity among all cuts through $e_0$.
