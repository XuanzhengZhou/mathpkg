Proposition F6 establishes two key structural properties of Tutte subspaces. Part (a) relates Tutte subspaces to matroid duality: if $\mathcal{T}$ is a Tutte subspace for $\Lambda$, then its orthogonal complement $\mathcal{T}^\perp$ under the standard inner product on $\mathbb{F}^V$ is a Tutte subspace for the dual matroid $\Lambda^\perp$. Part (b) asserts that matroid representability over a field is closed under taking minors: every minor of a representable matroid remains representable over the same field. These properties are fundamental to the excluded-minor characterization of matroid representability classes, such as binary and regular matroids.
