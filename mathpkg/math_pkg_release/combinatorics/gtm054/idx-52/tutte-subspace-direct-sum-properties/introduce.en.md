Exercise F15 establishes the relationship between Tutte subspaces and the direct sum decomposition of matroids. Part (a) shows that restricting a Tutte subspace of $\Lambda$ to a subset $U \subseteq V$ yields a Tutte subspace for the submatroid $\Lambda_{[U]}$. For a direct summand $V_i$, the restricted Tutte subspace equals $\mathcal{T} \cap \mathbb{F}^{V_i}$. Part (b) shows the converse: given Tutte subspaces for each direct summand, their direct sum forms a Tutte subspace for the whole matroid. Part (c) extends the notion of unimodularity to direct sums: the direct sum of subspaces of $\mathbb{Q}^{V_i}$ is unimodular precisely when each summand is unimodular. These results are fundamental to the decomposition theory of representable matroids.
