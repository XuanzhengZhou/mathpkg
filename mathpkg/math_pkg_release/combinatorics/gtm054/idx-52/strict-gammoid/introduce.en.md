A strict gammoid is a matroid defined on the vertex set of a directed graph, relative to a designated subset $Z$ of target vertices. An independent set in this matroid is any subset $A$ of vertices for which there exists a collection of $|A|$ pairwise vertex-disjoint directed paths, each starting at a distinct vertex of $A$ and ending in $Z$. The strict gammoid captures the combinatorial structure of vertex-disjoint linking systems within directed graphs. Any submatroid of a strict gammoid (induced by restriction to a subset of vertices) is called a gammoid.
