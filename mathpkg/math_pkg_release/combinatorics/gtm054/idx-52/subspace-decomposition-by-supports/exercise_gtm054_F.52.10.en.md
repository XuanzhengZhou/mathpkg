Let $\mathcal{S}$ be a subspace of $\mathbb{F}^V$ and let $h \in \mathcal{S}$. Prove that $h = \sum_{i=1}^{m} h_i$ where for $i = 1, \ldots, m$, $h_i \in \mathcal{S}$ and:

(a) $\sigma(h_i) \in M(\{\sigma(g): g \in \mathcal{S}\}) = M(\sigma[\mathcal{S}])$;

(b) $\sigma(h_i) \subseteq \sigma(h)$.

Here $\sigma(g) = \{x \in V : g(x) \neq 0\}$ denotes the support of $g$, and $M(\sigma[\mathcal{S}])$ denotes the family of minimal nonempty members of $\sigma[\mathcal{S}] = \{\sigma(g) : g \in \mathcal{S}\}$.
