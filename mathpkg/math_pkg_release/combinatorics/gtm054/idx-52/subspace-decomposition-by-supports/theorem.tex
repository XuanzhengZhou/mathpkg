Let $\mathcal{S}$ be a subspace of $\mathbb{F}^V$ and let $h \in \mathcal{S}$. Prove that
$$h = \sum_{i=1}^{m} h_i$$
where for $i = 1, \ldots, m$, $h_i \in \mathcal{S}$ and:
\begin{itemize}
  \item[(a)] $\sigma(h_i) \in M(\{\sigma(g): g \in \mathcal{S}\}) = M(\sigma[\mathcal{S}])$;
  \item[(b)] $\sigma(h_i) \subseteq \sigma(h)$.
\end{itemize}
Here $\sigma(g) = \{x \in V : g(x) \neq 0\}$ denotes the support, and $M(\sigma[\mathcal{S}])$ denotes the set of minimal nonempty elements of the family of supports of functions in $\mathcal{S}$.
