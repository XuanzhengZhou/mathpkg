D18 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

The Möbius function $\mu$ for the lattice $(\mathbb{P}(X), \leq)$ is

$$\mu(\mathcal{Q}, \mathcal{R}) = \prod_{R \in \mathcal{R}} (-1)^{|\mathcal{Q}(R)|-1}(|\mathcal{Q}(R)|-1)!.$$
