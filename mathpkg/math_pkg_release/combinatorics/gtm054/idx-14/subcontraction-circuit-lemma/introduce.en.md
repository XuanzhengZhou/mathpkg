This lemma, labeled G8 by Graver and Watkins, provides a sufficient condition for a nonplanar graph to be isomorphic to $K_5$ or $K_{3,3}$ in the context of subcontractions. It is a key intermediate step in the proof of the Kuratowski-type theorem (G11 Proposition), which characterizes minimal nonplanar graphs under the subcontraction relation. The lemma handles the case where deleting the two vertices incident with some edge leaves an elementary circuit, and the proof proceeds by case analysis on the valences of the remaining vertices.
