D8 (theorem) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

(P. J. Heawood [h.7], 1890). If $S$ is an orientable surface with $\eta(S) \geq 1$, then

$$\chi_0(S) \leq \left[ \frac{7 + \sqrt{1 + 48\eta(S)}}{2} \right].$$
