Prove that for any subspace $L \subseteq \mathbb{Q}^X$ and any $Y \subseteq X$,

$$(L \cap \mathbb{Q}^Y)^{\perp_Y} = \pi_Y[L^{\perp}]$$

and

$$(\pi_Y[L])^{\perp_Y} = L^{\perp} \cap \mathbb{Q}^Y,$$

where $\perp_Y$ indicates the orthogonal complement in $\mathbb{Q}^Y$.
