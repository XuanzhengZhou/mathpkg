Example A16 presents the canonical unimodular subspace: all functions $f \in \mathbb{Q}^X$ whose values sum to zero. In this subspace, elementary functions correspond to pairs of points $\{x, y\}$ with values $\pm 1$ at one point and $\mp 1$ at the other, and zero elsewhere. Its orthogonal complement is the subspace of constant functions.
