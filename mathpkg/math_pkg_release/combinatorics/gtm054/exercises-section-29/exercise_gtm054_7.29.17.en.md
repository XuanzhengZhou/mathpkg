VI Separation and Connectivity in Multigraphs

Let $\Theta = (U, g, F)$ be a submultigraph of $\Gamma$. Let $A(\Theta)$ denote the set of attachment vertices of $\Theta$. (The extension to multigraphs of this term defined in §C is obvious: an attachment vertex of $\Theta$ is a vertex incident with an edge in $F$ and an edge in $E + F$.) $\Gamma$ is $k$-separated if there exists a proper nonempty submultigraph $\Theta = (U, g, F)$ of $\Gamma$ such that

(a) $k \leq \nu_1(\Theta) \leq \nu_1(\Gamma) - k$;
(b) $|A(\Theta)| = k$.
