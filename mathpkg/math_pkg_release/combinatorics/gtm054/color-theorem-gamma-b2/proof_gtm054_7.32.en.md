Let $\Gamma$ be planar, and let $\Theta$ be a $\chi_0(\Gamma)$-critical subgraph of $\Gamma$. By IIIE15, $\Theta$ is planar, and so by IIIF12, $\rho(\Theta) \leq 5$. The result follows by A13. $\square$
