This corollary (C37) follows directly from the fact that the Kronecker product of Hadamard matrices is again a Hadamard matrix. Starting from the $2 \times 2$ Hadamard matrix $\begin{pmatrix}1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1\end{pmatrix}$, one obtains $2^n \times 2^n$ Hadamard matrices by iterated Kronecker products. Consequently, corresponding Hadamard designs with $(4 \cdot 2^n) - 1$ vertices exist for all $n \geq 1$.
