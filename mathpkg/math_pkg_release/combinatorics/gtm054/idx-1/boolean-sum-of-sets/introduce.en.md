The Boolean sum (also called symmetric difference) of two subsets of a universe $U$ is the set of elements belonging to exactly one of the two sets. It serves as the primary set operation in the text, with properties A1-A9 formalizing its algebraic behavior. Notably, $S + U$ gives the complement of $S$ in $U$, eliminating the need for a separate complement notation.
