The Boolean sum satisfies commutativity (A1), associativity (A2), and interacts with union and intersection through distributivity and inclusion relations (A6-A9). Property A3 shows that the empty set is the additive identity, while A4 shows every set is its own inverse. These properties make $\mathcal{P}(U)$ a Boolean ring under symmetric difference and intersection.
