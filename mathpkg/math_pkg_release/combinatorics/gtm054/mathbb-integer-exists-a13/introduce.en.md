A13 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $a \in \mathbb{C}^N$ and let $n \geq 2$ be an integer. There exists $b \in \mathbb{C}^N$ such that $a = b^n$ if and only if the least $j$ for which $a_j \neq 0$ is an integral multiple of $n$.
