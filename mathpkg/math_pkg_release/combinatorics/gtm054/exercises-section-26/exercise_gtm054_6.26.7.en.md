Apply Corollary F6 to complete bipartite graphs and thereby obtain "half" of Proposition IC13, namely that

$$|\text{inj}(Y^x)| \geq \frac{|Y|!}{(|Y| - |X|)!} \quad \text{if} \quad |X| \leq |Y|.$$

The $i$th row-sum of an $r \times s$ matrix $A = [a_{ij}]$ over an integral domain is $\rho_i = \sum_{j=1}^{s} a_{ij}$, and the $j$th column-sum is $\sigma_j = \sum_{i=1}^{r} a_{ij}$. We say that $A$ has constant row-sums (respectively, has constant column-sums) if $\rho_i = \rho_j$ (respectively, $\sigma_i = \sigma_j$) for all $i, j$.
