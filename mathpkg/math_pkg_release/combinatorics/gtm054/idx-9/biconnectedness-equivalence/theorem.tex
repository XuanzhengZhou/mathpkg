For a multigraph $\Gamma$ with $|E| \geq 2$, the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\Gamma$ is biconnected.
\item[(b)] $\mathcal{Z}^\perp(\Gamma)$ is connected.
\item[(c)] $\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)$ is connected.
\item[(d)] Every two edges of $\Gamma$ lie in a common elementary circuit.
\end{enumerate}
