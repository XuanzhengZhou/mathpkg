This theorem provides several equivalent characterizations of biconnectedness for multigraphs with at least two edges. It establishes a deep connection between the topological notion of biconnectedness, the algebraic structure of the cycle and cocycle spaces, and the combinatorial condition that any two edges lie in a common elementary circuit. This result is the central theorem of Section IIIB and underpins the entire theory of lobes and graphic spaces.
