This lemma states that any lobe (a cell of the component partition of the cycle space) containing more than one edge is biconnected. Since $Q$ is a cell of the component partition of $\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)$, every two edges in $Q$ belong to a common elementary cycle, and by a previous result they belong to a common elementary circuit. The lemma then follows from the equivalence theorem B3.
