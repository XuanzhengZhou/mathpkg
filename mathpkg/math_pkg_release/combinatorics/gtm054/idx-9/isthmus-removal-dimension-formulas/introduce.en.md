This lemma quantifies precisely how the dimensions of the cycle space, cocycle space, and the rank change when a single edge is removed from a multigraph. The behavior bifurcates based on whether the edge is an isthmus: removing a non-isthmus decreases the cycle space dimension by 1 while preserving the cocycle space dimension; removing an isthmus preserves the cycle space dimension but decreases the cocycle space dimension by 1 and increases the number of components by 1.
