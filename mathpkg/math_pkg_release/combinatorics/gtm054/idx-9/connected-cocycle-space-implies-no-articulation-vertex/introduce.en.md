This lemma establishes a fundamental connection between the connectedness of the cocycle space $\mathcal{L}^\perp(\Gamma)$ and the absence of articulation vertices. If the cocycle space is connected (cannot be decomposed as a direct sum of proper subspaces), then the multigraph has no articulation vertex — no single vertex whose removal disconnects the graph. This result is the first step in establishing the equivalence of various biconnectedness conditions.
