Let $D_{t,k}(V)$ denote the collection of all isomorphism classes of $t$-designs with vertex set $V$ and blocksize $k$. (Recall that each isomorphism class is uniquely determined by its selection.) Define the sum of two isomorphism classes to be the isomorphism class of the sum of representative designs. Show that $D_{t,k}(V)$ together with this sum forms a commutative semigroup with a zero element.
