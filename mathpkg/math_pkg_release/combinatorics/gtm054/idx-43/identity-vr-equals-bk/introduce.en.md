The identity $vr = bk$ is a fundamental counting relation for $t$-designs, obtained from Axiom A9 with $i = 0$. It relates the four basic parameters: number of vertices $v$, number of blocks $b$, replication number $r$, and blocksize $k$. This identity is essential for establishing necessary conditions for the existence of designs.
