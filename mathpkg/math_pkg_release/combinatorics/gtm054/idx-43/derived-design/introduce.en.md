The derived design of a symmetric block design $\Lambda$ is obtained by taking the blocks that intersect a designated block $e_0$ and restricting them to the vertices in $e_0$. Together with the residual design, this construction provides a decomposition of a symmetric block design. As with residual designs, different choices of the distinguished block may lead to nonisomorphic derived designs.
