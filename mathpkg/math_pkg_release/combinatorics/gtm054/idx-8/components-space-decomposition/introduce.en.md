Proposition A4 states that the space $Y(\Gamma)$ of a multigraph $\Gamma$ decomposes as a direct sum of the vertex-edge spaces $E(V_i)$ of its individual components $\Gamma_i$. This structural result reflects the fact that the algebraic data of a multigraph factors cleanly across its connected components.
