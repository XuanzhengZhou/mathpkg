[The proof of Proposition A4 is not given in full detail in this section; it follows from the definition of the space $Y(\Gamma)$ and the fact that the vertex sets $V_i$ of distinct components are disjoint, so the corresponding vector spaces $E(V_i)$ are in direct sum.] The statement is that $Y(\Gamma) = \bigoplus_{i=1}^{k} E(V_i)$ where $\Gamma_i = (V_i, f_i, E_i)$ are the components of $\Gamma$.
