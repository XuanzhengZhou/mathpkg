A circuit in a multigraph is a nontrivial path
$$x_0, e_1, x_1, \ldots, e_k, x_k = x_0$$
where all of the edges $e_1, \ldots, e_k$ are distinct. The circuit is called elementary if the vertices $x_0, x_1, \ldots, x_{k-1}$ are all distinct. The length of a circuit is the number of edges $k$ in the path.
