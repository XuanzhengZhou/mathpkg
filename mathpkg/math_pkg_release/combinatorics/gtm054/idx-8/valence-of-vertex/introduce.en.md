The valence $\rho(x)$ of a vertex $x$ in a multigraph $\Gamma$ is the number of edges incident with $x$, counted with multiplicity. It is defined via the selection function $s$ as $\bar{s}(\{x\})$. The valence is the multigraph analogue of the degree of a vertex in a simple graph, and it plays a central role in many fundamental results such as the Handshaking Lemma and Euler's Theorem.
