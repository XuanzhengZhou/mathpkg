Theorem A13 provides a fundamental characterization of bipartite multigraphs: a multigraph is bipartite if and only if every circuit has even length. The algebraic proof reveals that this result is dual to Euler's Theorem (A12) via the cycle space formalism. The equivalence is established through the orthogonal complement of the cycle space: every circuit has even length if and only if the cycle space is even, which is equivalent to the edge set being in the orthogonal complement of the cycle space.
