B14 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Any nonempty subcollection of $(\mathcal{P}(U), \subseteq)$ which is closed under $\cap$ (respectively, $\cup$) contains a minimum (respectively, maximum) subset.
