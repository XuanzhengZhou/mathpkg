Let $\mathcal{Q}_0, \mathcal{Q}_1 \in \mathbb{P}(U)$ be given with $\mathcal{Q}_0 \leq \mathcal{Q}_1$. Determine when there exists $S \subseteq \mathcal{P}(U)$ such that $\mathcal{Q}_0$ is the fine partition of $S$ and $\mathcal{Q}_1$ is the coarse partition of $S$. What is the answer if it is imposed further that $S$ be incommensurable?
