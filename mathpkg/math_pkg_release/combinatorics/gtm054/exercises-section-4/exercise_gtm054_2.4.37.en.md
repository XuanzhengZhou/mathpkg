Let $(U, \leq)$ be a distributive lattice. Let $a, b \in U$ and let $a \leq b$. Let $W = \{x \in U : a \leq x \leq b\}$. Show that
(a) $(W, \leq)$ is a distributive lattice.
(b) For each $x \in W$ there exists at most one element $y \in U$ such that $x \wedge y = a$ and $x \vee y = b$.
(c) If $(U, \leq)$ is complemented, then $(W, \leq)$ is complemented.

If $(U, \leq)$ is a partially-ordered set and if $(W, \leq)$ is a lattice where $W \subseteq U$, then $(W, \leq)$ is a sublattice of $(U, \leq)$.
