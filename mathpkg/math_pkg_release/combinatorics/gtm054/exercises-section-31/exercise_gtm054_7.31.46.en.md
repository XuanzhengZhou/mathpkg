(J. Petersen-B. Descartes). Let $h: \mathcal{E} \rightarrow \{1, 2, 3\}$ be a Tait coloring of the trivalent graph $\Gamma$, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be any minimal nonempty cocycle of $\Gamma$. Prove that the three integers $|\mathcal{F} \cap h^{-1}[i]|$ for $i = 1, 2, 3$ are pairwise congruent modulo 2.
