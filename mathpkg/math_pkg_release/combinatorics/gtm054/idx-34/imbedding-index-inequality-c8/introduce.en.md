Inequality C8 provides a fundamental lower bound relating the imbedding index $\iota$ of a graph to its girth $\gamma$, number of edges $\nu_1$, and number of vertices $\nu_0$. It is derived from the relationship $\iota \geq \nu_1 - \nu_0 + 2 - m$ (from C2) together with the inequality $m\gamma \leq 2\nu_1$ that follows from the definitions of cycle cover and girth. This inequality is a key step toward bounding the average valence $\rho$ in terms of $\iota$ and $\gamma$.
