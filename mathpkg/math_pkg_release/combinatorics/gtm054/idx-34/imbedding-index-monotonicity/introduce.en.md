This proposition establishes that the imbedding index $\iota(\Gamma)$ is monotone non-increasing under edge deletion: removing an edge cannot increase the imbedding index. If the deleted edge is an isthmus (bridge), the imbedding index remains unchanged. This property is crucial for inductive proofs involving the imbedding index, and it relies on the relationship between the dimension of the cycle space and the minimum size of a cycle cover.
