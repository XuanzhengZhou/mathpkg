The matching matroid of a graph $\Gamma = (V, \mathcal{E})$ is the matroid whose bases are the largest matched sets of $\Gamma$. This construction connects the theory of matchings in graphs to the general theory of matroids, allowing one to apply matroid-theoretic tools such as duality, connectivity, and the greedy algorithm to matching problems.
