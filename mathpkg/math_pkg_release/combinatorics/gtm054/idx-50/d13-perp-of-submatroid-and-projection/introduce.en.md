This proposition describes how the perpendicular (dual) operation interacts with the two fundamental derived matroid constructions: projection $\Lambda_{[U]}$ and restriction $\Lambda_U$. Part (a) states that taking the perpendicular commutes with projection onto $U$. Part (b) states that taking the dual of the restriction equals the projection of the dual. Together these identities provide essential tools for manipulating matroid minors and duality in connectivity arguments.
