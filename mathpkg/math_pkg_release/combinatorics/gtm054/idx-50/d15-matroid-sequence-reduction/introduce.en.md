This corollary states that any finite sequence of matroid restrictions and projections applied successively can be reduced to a single projection followed by a single restriction, or equivalently a single restriction followed by a single projection. This is obtained by successive applications of the commutation relations between restrictions and projections established in Proposition D13. It provides the foundational justification for the definition of a matroid minor.
