A matching of $U_1$ into $U_2$ is a matching $\mathcal{E}'$ in a bipartite graph $B = (\{V_1, V_2\}, \mathcal{E})$ such that every edge in $\mathcal{E}'$ connects a vertex in $U_1 \subseteq V_1$ to a vertex in $U_2 \subseteq V_2$. This concept refines the general notion of a matching by specifying which subsets of the vertex partition are involved, and is used in formulating the Main Matching Theorem and its corollaries.
