If $s$ is the selection of $\Lambda$, then with $i$ and $j$ being distinct indices,

$$s^*(\emptyset) = n_0 \quad \overline{s^*}(\emptyset) = |V|$$
$$s^*(\{e_i\}) = n_i \quad \overline{s^*}(\{e_i\}) = |S_i|$$
$$s^*(\{e_i, e_j\}) = n_{ij} \quad \overline{s^*}(\{e_i, e_j\}) = |S_i \cap S_j|$$
$$s^*(E) = \overline{s^*}(E) = n_{123} = |S_1 \cap S_2 \cap S_3|.$$
