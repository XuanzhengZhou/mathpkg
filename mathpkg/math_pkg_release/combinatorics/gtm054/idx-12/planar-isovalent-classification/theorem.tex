Let $\Gamma$ be a planar isovalent multigraph without isthmuses or isolated vertices, and let $\Gamma$ have an $m$-covalent imbedding for some $m$. Then the parameters $\nu_0, \nu_1, \nu_2, \rho, \rho^{\perp}$ of $\Gamma$ must conform to one of the seven types in the following table, where $k$ is any integer greater than $1$:

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
Type & $\nu_0$ & $\nu_1$ & $\nu_2$ & $\rho$ & $\rho^{\perp}$ \\
\hline
I   & 2  & $k$  & $k$  & $k$ & 2 \\
II  & $k$  & $k$  & 2  & 2 & $k$ \\
III & 4  & 6  & 4  & 3 & 3 \\
IV  & 6  & 12 & 8  & 4 & 3 \\
V   & 8  & 12 & 6  & 3 & 4 \\
VI  & 12 & 30 & 20 & 5 & 3 \\
VII & 20 & 30 & 12 & 3 & 5 \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
Types III--VII correspond to the graphs of the five Platonic solids: tetrahedron, octahedron, cube, icosahedron, and dodecahedron respectively.
