Let $I$ be the set of isthmuses of the planar multigraph $\Gamma$. If $\Gamma_{(I)} \perp \Theta$ where $\Theta = (U, g, E + I)$, then
$$\nu_2(\Gamma) = \nu_0(\Theta).$$
Moreover, if $Z_1, \ldots, Z_{\nu_2(\Gamma)}$ is a planar imbedding of $\Gamma$ and $Z_i = g^*(u_i)$ for $u_i \in U$, then the covalence $\rho^{\perp}(Z_i) = |Z_i|$ equals the valence of $u_i$ in $\Theta$.
