This corollary expresses Euler's Formula purely in terms of the average valence $\rho$, average covalence $\rho^{\perp}$, and the number of edges $\nu_1$. It follows by substituting $\nu_0 = 2\nu_1/\rho$ and $\nu_2 = 2\nu_1/\rho^{\perp}$ into the Euler Formula and dividing by $2\nu_1$. This symmetric form reveals the deep duality between vertices and regions in a planar multigraph.
