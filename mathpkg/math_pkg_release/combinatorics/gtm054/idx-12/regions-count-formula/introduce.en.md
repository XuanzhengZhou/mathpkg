This proposition gives a formula for the number of regions in any simple planar imbedding of a multigraph in terms of its Betti numbers. If $Z_1, \ldots, Z_k$ is a simple planar imbedding of $\Gamma$, then $k$ equals the dimension of the cycle space plus one. This shows that the number of regions $\nu_2(\Gamma)$ is an invariant of the multigraph itself, independent of the choice of planar imbedding.
