Let $\Lambda = (V, f, E)$ be a $(t;)$-design. Prove:

Let $W$ and $F$ be any disjoint sets such that $|W| > t$ and $|F| > a$. Then there exists a function $f: F \rightarrow \mathcal{P}(W)$ such that $(W, f, F)$ is a degenerate $(t; a)$-design.
