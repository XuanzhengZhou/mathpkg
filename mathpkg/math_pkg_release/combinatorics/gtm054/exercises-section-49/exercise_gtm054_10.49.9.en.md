For $i = 1, 2$, let $\Lambda_i = (V, f_i, E_i)$ where $|E_1| = |E_2| = m$, and let $r_i$ denote the rank function of the transversal matroid of $\Lambda_i$. Prove that a necessary and sufficient condition for the existence of an LCR for the systems $\Lambda_1$ and $\Lambda_2$ is that $r_1(S) + r_2(V + S) \geq m$ for all $S \subseteq V$.
