Let $\Gamma$ be a graph and let $\Theta$ be a subcontraction of $\Gamma.$

(a) Show that the cycle matroid (cocycle matroid) of $\Theta$ is a minor of the cycle matroid (cocycle matroid) of $\Gamma.$

(b) Show that every minor of the cocycle matroid of $\Gamma$ is the cocycle matroid of a subcontraction of $\Gamma.$

(c) Find necessary and sufficient conditions under which a minor of the cycle matroid of $\Gamma$ is the cycle matroid of a subcontraction of $\Gamma.$
