If $\Lambda_1, \ldots, \Lambda_n$ are matroids whose vertex sets are pairwise-disjoint, prove that

$$\bigoplus_{i=1}^{n} \Lambda_i^\perp = \left( \bigoplus_{i=1}^{n} \Lambda_i \right)^\perp.$$

The next exercise shows why one cannot substitute the words “independence system,” “basis system,” or “spanning system” for “matroid” in D18 or D19.
