Consider the graph $\Theta_{13} = (\mathbb{Z}_{13}, \mathcal{F})$ where
$$\mathcal{F} = \{\{x, y\}: x - y = 1 \text{ or } 5\}.$$
Show that $\Theta_{13}$ is a $(3, 5)$-graph on $13$ vertices, and conclude that $n(3, 5) \geq 14$.
