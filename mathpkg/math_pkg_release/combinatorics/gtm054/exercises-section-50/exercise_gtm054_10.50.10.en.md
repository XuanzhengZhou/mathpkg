Let $S$ be a subspace of $\mathbb{F}^V$ and let $h \in S$. Prove that $h = \sum_{i=1}^{m} h_i$ where for $i = 1, \ldots, m$, $h_i \in S$ and:

(a) $\sigma(h_i) \in M(\{\sigma(g): g \in S\}) = M(\sigma[S])$;
(b) $\sigma(h_i) \subseteq \sigma(h)$.

Realizability of a matroid over $Q$ is almost a sufficient condition for regularity, as we now see.
