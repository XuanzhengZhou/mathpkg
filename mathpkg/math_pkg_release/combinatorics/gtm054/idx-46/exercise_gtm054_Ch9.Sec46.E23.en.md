Let $\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{E})$ be a BIB-design with $\lambda = 1$. Form a $2$-class association scheme on $V$ by declaring two distinct elements to be first associates if they are incident with a common block and second associates otherwise. Obtain the parameters of both the association scheme and the design.
