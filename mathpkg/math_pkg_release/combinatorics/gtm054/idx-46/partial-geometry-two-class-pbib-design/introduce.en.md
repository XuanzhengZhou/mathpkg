This proposition establishes the deep connection between partial geometries and partially balanced incomplete block designs (PBIB-designs). Every partial geometry naturally gives rise to a two-class PBIB-design by treating points as treatments and declaring two points to be first associates if they are collinear. The intersection numbers $n_1, n_2$ and $p_{11}^1, p_{11}^2$ are expressed directly in terms of the partial geometry parameters $(r, k, t)$.
