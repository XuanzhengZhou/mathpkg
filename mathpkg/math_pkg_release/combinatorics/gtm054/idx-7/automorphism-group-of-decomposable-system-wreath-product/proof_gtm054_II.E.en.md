The proof follows from Proposition E9, which states that any automorphism permutes the components of $\Lambda$ and restricts to system-isomorphisms between corresponding components. Grouping components by system-isomorphism class, the automorphisms within each class $j$ can independently permute the $m_{hj}$ components (vertex or edge nonempty, depending on $h$) while simultaneously applying automorphisms within each component. This yields the wreath product $\Pi(m_{hj}) \text{ wreath } G_h(\Lambda_{1j})$. Taking the direct product over all isomorphism classes $j = 1, \ldots, r$ gives the full structure.
