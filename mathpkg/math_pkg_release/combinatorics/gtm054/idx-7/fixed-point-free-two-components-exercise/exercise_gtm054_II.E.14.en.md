Let $\Lambda$ be fixed-point free and let $\Lambda_1$ and $\Lambda_2$ be distinct components with nonempty vertex sets. Show that if $G_0(\Lambda)$ is nontrivial, then:

(a) $\Lambda_1$ and $\Lambda_2$ are isomorphic;

(b) $|G_0(\Lambda)| = 2$;

(c) if $\Lambda$ is a set system with $|V| \geq 3$, then $|G_1(\Lambda)| = 2$.
