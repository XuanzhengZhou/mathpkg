This corollary states that degeneracy of a $(t;a)$-design is symmetric with respect to duality: if a design is degenerate due to a positive surplus $\bar{s}(V) > 0$ on the vertex side, it is necessarily also degenerate with $s^*(E) > 0$ on the block side, and vice versa. More precisely, the surplus $\bar{s}(V)$ equals the count parameter $\lambda(t)$ and the dual surplus $s^*(E)$ equals $\lambda^*(a)$. This follows from the definition of degeneracy and duality principles established earlier in Chapter IX.
