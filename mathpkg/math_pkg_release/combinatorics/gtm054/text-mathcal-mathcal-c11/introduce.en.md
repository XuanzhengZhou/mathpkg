C11 (lemma) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $|U| \geq 2$. $\text{Fnd}(\mathcal{A}) = U$ and $\mathcal{A}$ is connected if and only if $\text{Fnd}(\mathcal{A}^{\perp}) = U$ and $\mathcal{A}^{\perp}$ is connected.
