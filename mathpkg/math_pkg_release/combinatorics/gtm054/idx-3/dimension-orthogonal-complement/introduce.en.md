This theorem states that for any subspace $\mathcal{A}$ of the power set vector space $\mathcal{P}(U)$, the dimensions of $\mathcal{A}$ and its orthogonal complement sum to the cardinality of the ground set $U$. This is the combinatorial analog of the familiar linear algebra identity $\dim(W) + \dim(W^{\perp}) = \dim(V)$ for a subspace $W$ of a finite-dimensional inner product space $V$.
