This corollary follows from Proposition A1 and the dimension formula for vector spaces over $\mathbb{K}$. Since $\mathcal{E}(U)$ is a subspace of $\mathcal{P}(U)$ of dimension $|U|-1$, its cardinality equals $2^{|U|-1}$. Moreover, the complement $\mathcal{P}(U) \setminus \mathcal{E}(U)$ (the odd-cardinality subsets) has the same cardinality, showing that exactly half of all subsets of a nonempty finite set have even cardinality.
