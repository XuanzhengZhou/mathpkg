For any subspaces $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ of $\mathcal{P}(U)$,
$$\dim(\mathcal{A} \vee \mathcal{B}) + \dim(\mathcal{A} \cap \mathcal{B}) = \dim(\mathcal{A}) + \dim(\mathcal{B}).$$

In particular, when the foundations are disjoint,
$$\dim(\mathcal{A} \oplus \mathcal{B}) = \dim(\mathcal{A}) + \dim(\mathcal{B}).$$
