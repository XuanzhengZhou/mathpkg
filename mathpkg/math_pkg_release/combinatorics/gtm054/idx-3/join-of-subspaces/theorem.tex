If $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ are subspaces of $\mathcal{P}(U)$, their join (also referred to as "sum") is given by
$$\mathcal{A} \vee \mathcal{B} = \{S + T: S \in \mathcal{A}; T \in \mathcal{B}\},$$
which is also a subspace of $\mathcal{P}(U)$. The operation $\vee$ is commutative and associative on the collection of subspaces of $\mathcal{P}(U)$.
