Exercise B1 asks the reader to verify that the strict order relation $<$ derived from a partial order $\leq$ inherits antisymmetry and transitivity. While $<$ is irreflexive by definition (since $x < y$ requires $x \neq y$), the antisymmetry condition becomes vacuously true for a strict order.
