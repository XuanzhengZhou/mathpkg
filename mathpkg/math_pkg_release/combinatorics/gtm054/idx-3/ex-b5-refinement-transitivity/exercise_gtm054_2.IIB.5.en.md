Suppose $\mathcal{R}, \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{T} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(U)$ and that $\mathcal{S}$ covers $U$. Show that if $\mathcal{R}$ refines $\mathcal{S}$ and $\mathcal{S}$ refines $\mathcal{T}$, then $\mathcal{R}$ refines $\mathcal{T}$.
