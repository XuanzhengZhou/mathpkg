Suppose that $\Lambda$ is nondegenerate. From Theorem B6a and its transpose, we infer that $b = v$. If $ta > 4$, we apply B6b or its transpose to infer that $\Lambda$ is trivial.

Up to this point no effort has been made to identify those design-types which are possible. Using Proposition A5 and Corollary B8 we can completely identify the design-types of all two-sided designs.
