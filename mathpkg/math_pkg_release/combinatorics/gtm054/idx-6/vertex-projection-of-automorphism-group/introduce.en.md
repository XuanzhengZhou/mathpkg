$G_0(\Lambda)$ is the projection of the automorphism group $G(\Lambda)$ onto the vertex permutation component. It consists of all permutations $q$ of the vertex set $V$ for which there exists some edge permutation $p$ such that the pair $(p, q)$ is an automorphism of $\Lambda$. In other words, $G_0(\Lambda)$ captures how automorphisms act on the vertices, disregarding the specific edge permutation.
