This dimension formula states that the dimensions of the cycle space $\mathcal{Z}(\Lambda)$ and the bond space $\mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)$ sum to the size of the edge set $E$. It follows because the bond space is the image of the linear map $\vec{f}: \mathcal{P}(E) \to \mathcal{P}(V)$ and the cycle space is its kernel, and by the rank-nullity theorem, the dimensions of the kernel and image sum to the dimension of the domain, which is $|E|$.
