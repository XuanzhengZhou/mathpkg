Let $\Omega = (W, g, F)$ be a subsystem of a system $\Lambda$. Prove:
(a) $\mathcal{Z}(\Omega) = \mathcal{P}(F) \cap \mathcal{Z}(\Lambda)$.
(b) $\mathcal{Z}^{\perp}(\Omega) = \pi_F[\mathcal{Z}^{\perp}(\Lambda)]$. (Hint: see A15.)
(c) $\dim(\mathcal{Z}(\Omega)) \leq \dim(\mathcal{Z}(\Lambda))$ and $\dim(\mathcal{Z}^{\perp}(\Omega)) \leq \dim(\mathcal{Z}^{\perp}(\Lambda))$.
