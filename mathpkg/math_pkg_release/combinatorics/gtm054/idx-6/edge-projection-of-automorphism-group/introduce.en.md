$G_1(\Lambda)$ is the projection of the automorphism group $G(\Lambda)$ onto the edge permutation component. It consists of all permutations $p$ of the edge set $E$ for which there exists some vertex permutation $q$ such that the pair $(p, q)$ is an automorphism of $\Lambda$. Together with $G_0(\Lambda)$, these projections decompose the action of the automorphism group into its edge and vertex components.
