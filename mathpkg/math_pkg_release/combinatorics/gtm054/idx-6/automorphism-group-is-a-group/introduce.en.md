Under componentwise composition of permutations, the set $G(\Lambda)$ of all automorphisms of a set system $\Lambda$ forms a group. The group axioms are verified immediately: the identity is $(1_E, 1_V)$, the inverse of $(p, q)$ is $(p^{-1}, q^{-1})$, and associativity follows from associativity of permutation composition. Thus $G(\Lambda)$ is a permutation group encoding all symmetries of the incidence structure of $\Lambda$.
