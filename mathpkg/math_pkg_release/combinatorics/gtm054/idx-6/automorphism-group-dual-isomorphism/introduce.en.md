A set system $\Lambda$ and its dual system $\Lambda^*$ have isomorphic automorphism groups. This is a natural consequence of the symmetry between the roles of vertices and edges in the dual construction: an automorphism $(p, q)$ of $\Lambda$, where $p$ permutes edges and $q$ permutes vertices, corresponds to an automorphism $(q, p)$ of $\Lambda^*$. The isomorphism is one of abstract group structure, not necessarily of permutation group structure.
