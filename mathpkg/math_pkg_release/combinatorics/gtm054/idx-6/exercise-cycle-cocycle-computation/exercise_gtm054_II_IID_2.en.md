Let $R = \{a, b, c, d, e, f\}$. Let
$$S = \{a, b\},\quad T = \{a, c\},\quad U = \{b, d\},\quad V = \{c, d\},$$
$$W = \{b, c\},\quad X = \{d, e\},\quad Y = \{e, f\},\quad Z = \{d, f\}.$$
Let $\mathcal{E} = \{S, T, U, V, W, X, Y, Z\}$, and let $\Gamma$ be the graph $(R, \mathcal{E})$. Determine $\mathcal{Y}(\Gamma)$, $\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)$, $\mathcal{Y}^\perp(\Gamma)$, and $\mathcal{Z}^\perp(\Gamma)$. Compare these findings with your results in Example C9.
