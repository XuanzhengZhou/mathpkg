The \textbf{genus} $\eta(\Gamma)$ of the graph $\Gamma$ is the genus of the orientable surface $S$ of least genus such that $\Gamma$ can be topologically imbedded in $S$. An imbedding of $\Gamma$ in an orientable surface of genus $\eta(\Gamma)$ is called a \textbf{minimal imbedding}.

For any subgraph $\Theta$ of $\Gamma$, clearly $\eta(\Theta) \leq \eta(\Gamma)$.
