Taking complements and applying the result VIIA5 with $i=0$ gives
$$\nu_0(\Gamma) \leq \omega(\Gamma)\,\theta(\Gamma).$$

The argument uses the fact that a vertex cover in $\Gamma$ corresponds to a complete-set cover in the complement, together with the parameters $\omega$ and $\theta$ that bound these respective sizes.
