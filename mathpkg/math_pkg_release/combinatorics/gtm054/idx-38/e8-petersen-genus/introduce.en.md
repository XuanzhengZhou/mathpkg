This exercise asks to determine the genus of the Petersen graph — the minimum genus of an orientable surface into which the Petersen graph can be 2-cell imbedded. The Petersen graph is a well-known 3-regular graph on 10 vertices with girth 5, and determining its genus is a classic problem in topological graph theory.
