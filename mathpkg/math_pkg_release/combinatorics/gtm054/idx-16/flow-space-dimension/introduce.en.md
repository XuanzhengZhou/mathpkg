Proposition B5 establishes the dimensions of the flow space $F$ and its orthogonal complement $F^\perp$ for the multigraph $\Gamma = (V, f, W)$ where $W$ is the complete set of ordered pairs. The dimension of $F$ equals $(|V| - 1)^2$, while the dimension of $F^\perp$ equals $|V| - 1$. These formulas are fundamental for understanding the structure of flows and tensions in network theory.
