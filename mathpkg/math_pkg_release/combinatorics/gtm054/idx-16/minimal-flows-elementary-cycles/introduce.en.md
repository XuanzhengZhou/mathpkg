Corollary B9 establishes a fundamental bijective correspondence: the set $\mathcal{M}(F)$ of minimal (non-zero) flows in the flow space $F$ coincides exactly with the set of elementary cycles of the multigraph $\Gamma$. This is an immediate consequence of Proposition B8 and result IIIA9, completing the characterization of the cycle space of $\Gamma$ in terms of the flow space $F$.
