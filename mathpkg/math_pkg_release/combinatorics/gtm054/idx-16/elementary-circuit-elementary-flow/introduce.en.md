Proposition B8 establishes that every elementary circuit $D$ in $\Gamma$ yields an elementary flow $h_D$ in the flow space $F(V)$. The proof shows that $h_D$ is minimal among non-zero flows supported on its cycle, and since $h_D[W] \subseteq \{0, 1, -1\}$, every such function with minimal support is elementary. This refines Lemma B7 by showing that $h_D$ is not merely a flow but a minimal (elementary) one.
