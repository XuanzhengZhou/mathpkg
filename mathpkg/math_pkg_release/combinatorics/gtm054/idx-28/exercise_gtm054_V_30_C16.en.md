Let $\Gamma$ be connected and not complete.

(a) Show that $\Gamma$ is $m$-connected if and only if every subgraph $\Theta$ of $\Gamma$ having at least one interior vertex and one exterior vertex has at least $m$ attachment vertices.

(b) Prove that (a) holds when "subgraph" has been replaced by "vertex-induced subgraph."
