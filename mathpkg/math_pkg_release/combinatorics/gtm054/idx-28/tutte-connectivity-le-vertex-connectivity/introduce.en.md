For any multigraph where some pair of vertices are not incident, the Tutte connectivity is at most the vertex connectivity. This holds because a submultigraph witnessing the vertex connectivity also satisfies the edge-count conditions required in the definition of $k$-separated, since each interior and exterior vertex contributes edges to the submultigraph.
