For any multigraph not among the small forbidden cases, the Tutte connectivity cannot exceed the girth. This lemma connects two fundamental graph invariants: the girth measures the size of the smallest cycle, while the Tutte connectivity measures the edge-centric separation threshold. The proof handles low-valence cases using the known bound $\tau(\Gamma) \leq \kappa(\Gamma)$ and high-valence cases by considering a smallest cycle directly.
