Write $\kappa = m\alpha + b$ with $m \geq 1$ and $0 \leq b < \alpha$. From D11, $\rho(x) \leq \kappa + \alpha - 1$. Substituting the expression for $\kappa$ and simplifying yields the stated bound. The construction in the text (a $\rho$-valent graph with $\rho = 3n-1$, $\kappa = 2n$, atoms of size $n$) shows the ratio $\frac{3}{2} - \rho/\kappa = 1/(2n)$ can be made arbitrarily small, demonstrating sharpness.
