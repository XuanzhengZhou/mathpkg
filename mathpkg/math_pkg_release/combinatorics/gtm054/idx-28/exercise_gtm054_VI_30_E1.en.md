Assume that $\Theta = (U, f, F)$ satisfies condition (a) in the definition of $k$-separated: $k \leq \nu_1(\Theta) \leq \nu_1(\Gamma) - k$. Show that

(a) Both $\Gamma_F$ and $\Gamma_{E+F}$ satisfy (a) above;

(b) $A(\Gamma_F) = A(\Gamma_{E+F}) = A(\Theta)$.
