B5 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $\Gamma$ be planar and let $x_0$ be a vertex of $\Gamma$ such that $\rho(x_0) \leq 4$. If $\chi_0(\Gamma_{x_0}) \leq 4$, then $\chi_0(\Gamma) \leq 4$.
