Prove:
(a) The component partition of $\mathcal{A}$ is the coarse partition of $\mathcal{M}(\mathcal{A})$.
(b) If $\{x\} \in \mathcal{A}$ for some $x \in \text{Fnd}(\mathcal{A})$, then $\mathcal{P}(\{x\})$ is a component of $\mathcal{A}$.
(c) If $x \in U + \text{Fnd}(\mathcal{A})$, then $\mathcal{P}(\{x\})$ is a component of $\mathcal{A}^{\perp}$.
