$$
\text{Let }\hat{\Lambda}\text{ be the projective plane of order }2.
$$
$$
\text{Define the set system }\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{M})\text{ where }V\text{ is the set of }7\text{ points of }\hat{\Lambda}.
$$
$$
\mathcal{M}\text{ consists of the complements of the }7\text{ lines of }\hat{\Lambda}\text{ (each a }4\text{-subset of }V).
$$
$$
\text{Then }\Lambda\text{ is a matroid, called the }\textit{Fano matroid of rank }4.
$$
$$
\mathcal{I}(\Lambda) = \{J \subseteq V : J\text{ contains no complement of a line}\}
= \{J \subseteq V : \text{every }4\text{-subset of }J\text{ contains a line}\}
$$
$$
\text{together with all }k\text{-subsets for }k < 4.
$$
$$
\mathcal{B}(\Lambda) = \{B \subseteq V : |B| = 4\text{ and }B\text{ contains a line}\}.
$$
$$
r(\Lambda) = 4.
$$
$$
\mathcal{S}(\Lambda) = \mathcal{B}(\Lambda) \cup \{S \subseteq V : |S| > 4\}.
$$
