The Fano matroid of rank 4 is a concrete matroid on 7 elements derived from the projective plane of order 2 (the Fano plane). The cycles are the complements of the seven lines of the Fano plane, each being a 4-subset. The independent sets are those subsets containing no complement of a line, the bases are the 4-subsets that contain a line, and the rank is 4. This matroid is the orthogonal dual of the standard (rank-3) Fano matroid and serves as an important example in matroid theory.
