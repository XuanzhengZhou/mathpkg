B38 (lemma) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $(U, \leq)$ be a lattice and let $W \subseteq U$. If $x \wedge y, x \vee y \in W$ for all $x, y \in W$, then $(W, \leq)$ is a sublattice of $(U, \leq)$.
