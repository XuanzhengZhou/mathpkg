Prove that for any $\{0, 1\}$-matrix $M$ the following three statements are equivalent.

(a) The nondiagonal entries on $MM^*$ are 0 or 1.
(b) The nondiagonal entries of $M^*M$ are 0 or 1.
(c) $M$ has no $2 \times 2$ submatrix of the form

$$\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}.$$
