Lemma E21 states that in a biconnected multigraph, any planar imbedding induces a natural cyclic ordering of the edges incident to each vertex. Consecutive edges in this ordering share a common region cycle of the planar imbedding. This lemma captures the local combinatorial structure of a planar embedding around each vertex and is a key ingredient in the proof that cycles that cross at a vertex must share another vertex.
