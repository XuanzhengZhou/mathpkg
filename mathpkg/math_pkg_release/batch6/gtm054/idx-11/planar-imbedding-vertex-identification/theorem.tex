Let $\Gamma$ be a planar multigraph and let $Z_1, \ldots, Z_k$ be a planar imbedding of $\Gamma$. If $\Gamma'$ is obtained from $\Gamma$ by identifying two vertices incident with the edge $e$, and if $F = \operatorname{Fnd}(\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)) \setminus f^{-1}[f(e)]$, then the projections $Z_1 \cap F, \ldots, Z_k \cap F$ yield a planar imbedding of $\Gamma'$ via Lemma E14.
