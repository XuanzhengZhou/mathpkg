This lemma, which follows immediately from Lemma E14, completes the proof that planarity is preserved under the elementary contraction operations: if two vertices sharing a common edge are identified, the projection of the cycle space (and consequently of the planar imbedding regions) onto the reduced edge set of the contracted graph gives a planar imbedding. Together with the edge contraction lemma, this shows that all contractions of planar multigraphs remain planar.
