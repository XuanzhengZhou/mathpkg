This exercise (labeled E12) establishes two fundamental properties of subcontractions: first, every cycle of the subcontraction can be extended to a cycle of the original multigraph; second, isthmus edges retain their isthmus property when passing down to a subcontraction. These results are foundational for understanding how cycle spaces and connectivity behave under graph minor operations in the context of planarity.
