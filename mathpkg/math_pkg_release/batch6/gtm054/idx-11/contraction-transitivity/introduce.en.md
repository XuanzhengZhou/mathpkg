Exercise E13 asks to prove that the contraction relation is transitive and that every contraction can be built by iterating three elementary operations: deleting an isthmus edge, contracting an arbitrary edge, and identifying two vertices that share a common edge. This provides a constructive understanding of how general contractions are generated from local operations, which is crucial for the algebraic analysis of planarity via the cycle space.
