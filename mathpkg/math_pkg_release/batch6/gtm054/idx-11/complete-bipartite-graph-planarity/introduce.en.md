Exercise E19 asks to verify that the complete bipartite graphs $K_{m,n}$ are planar whenever one of the two part sizes is at most 2, and that all complete graphs $K_n$ with $n \leq 4$ are planar. These are the "easy" cases of Kuratowski's theorem; the harder converse — that $K_{3,3}$ and $K_5$ are non-planar — is established in the next section (F9), showing these bounds are sharp.
