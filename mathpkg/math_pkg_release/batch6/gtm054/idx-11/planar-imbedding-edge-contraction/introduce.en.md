This lemma establishes that planarity (in the combinatorial sense of possessing a planar imbedding) is preserved under edge contraction for non-isthmus edges. Given a planar imbedding $Z_1, \ldots, Z_k$ of $\Gamma$ and an edge $e$ belonging to the fundamental edge set of the cycle space that appears in exactly two regions $Z_1$ and $Z_2$, contracting $e$ merges those two regions: $Z_1 + Z_2, Z_3, \ldots, Z_k$ becomes a planar imbedding of the contracted multigraph $\Gamma_{(e)}$. This is a key technical step in proving the equivalence of combinatorial and topological planarity.
