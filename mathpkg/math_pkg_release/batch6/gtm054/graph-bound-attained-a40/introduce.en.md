A40 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

For any graph, $\hat{\rho} \leq \chi_1 \leq \hat{\rho} + 1.$

Each bound in A40 can be attained; it suffices to consider even or odd circuits, respectively.
