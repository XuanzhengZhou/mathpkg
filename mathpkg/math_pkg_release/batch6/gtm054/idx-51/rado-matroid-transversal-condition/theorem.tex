Let $\Lambda = (V, f, E)$ be a system and let $\Theta = (V, \mathcal{M})$ be a matroid with rank function $r$. A necessary and sufficient condition for there to exist an LDR $\lambda$ of $\Lambda$ such that $\lambda[E] \in \mathcal{I}(\Theta)$ is that
$$r\!\left(\bigcup_{e \in F} f(e)\right) \geq |F| \quad \text{for all } F \in \mathcal{P}(E).$$
