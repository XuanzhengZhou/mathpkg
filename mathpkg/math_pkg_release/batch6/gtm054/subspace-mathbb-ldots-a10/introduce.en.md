A10 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $L$ is a subspace of $\mathbb{Q}^x$ and if $\{h_1, \ldots, h_m\}$ is an $\mathcal{M}$-decomposition of $h \in L$, then

$$|h(x)| = \sum_{i=1}^{m} |h_i(x)|, \quad \text{for all } x \in X.$$
