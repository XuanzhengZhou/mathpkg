A32 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

(R. L. Brooks, [b.18]). Let $m \geq 4$ be an integer. If no component of $\Gamma$ is the complete graph $K_m$, and if $\rho(x) \leq m - 1$ for all $x \in V$, then $\chi_0(\Gamma) \leq m - 1$.
