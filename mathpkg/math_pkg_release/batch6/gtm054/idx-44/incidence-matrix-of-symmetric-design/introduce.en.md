This proposition (labeled C31 in the text) characterizes the incidence matrix $M$ of a symmetric $(v,k,\lambda)$-block design. Setting $q = k - \lambda$, the product $MM^*$ equals $qI + (q-1)J$, where $J$ is the all-ones matrix. This equation is the starting point for constructing Hadamard matrices from symmetric designs via the transformation $H_1 = 2M - J$.
