Starting from the incidence matrix equation $MM^* = qI + (q-1)J$ (C31), one defines $H_1 = 2M - J$. Through algebraic manipulation, a new normalization equation $H_1 H_1^* = 4qI - J$ is obtained (C32). This equation governs when the $(4q-1) \times (4q-1)$ matrix $H_1$ (with entries $\pm 1$ and line sums $-1$) can be extended by a bordering row and column of $1$'s to produce a $4q \times 4q$ Hadamard matrix.
