Let $M$ be the incidence matrix of a symmetric $(v,k,\lambda)$-block design with $q = k - \lambda$, and let $H_1 = 2M - J$ where $J$ denotes the matrix whose every entry is $1$. Then $H_1$ is a $(4q - 1) \times (4q - 1)$ matrix whose entries are $\pm 1$ and whose line sums are $-1$, satisfying

$$H_1 H_1^* = 4qI - J,$$

where $I$ is the identity matrix.
