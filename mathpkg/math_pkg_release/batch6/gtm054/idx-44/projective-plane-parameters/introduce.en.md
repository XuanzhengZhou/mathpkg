From the definition of a finite projective plane as a symmetric block design with $\lambda = 1$, Proposition C14b yields $v = k^2 - k + 1$. Substituting $n = k - 1$ (the order) gives the compact formulas $b = v = n^2 + n + 1$ and $r = k = n + 1$. Thus a projective plane of order $n$ has equally many points and lines, with each line containing $n+1$ points and each point lying on $n+1$ lines.
