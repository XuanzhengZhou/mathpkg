Let $V$ be the $3$-dimensional vector space over the finite field $\mathbb{F}$ where $|\mathbb{F}| = n$. Let $\mathcal{V}$ be the set of $1$-dimensional subspaces of $V$ and let $\mathcal{E}$ be the set of $2$-dimensional subspaces of $V$. Let $f: \mathcal{E} \to \mathcal{P}(\mathcal{V})$ be defined for each $e \in \mathcal{E}$ by

$$f(e) = \{x \in \mathcal{V} : x \text{ is a subspace of } e\}.$$

Prove that $(\mathcal{V}, f, \mathcal{E})$ is a finite projective plane of order $n$.
