Let $\Lambda$ be a block design satisfying conditions (a), (b), and (c) below. Then $\Lambda$ is a finite projective plane.

\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] Any two distinct points determine a unique line.
\item[(b)] Any two distinct lines intersect in a unique point.
\item[(c)] There exists a set of four points, no three of which are collinear.
\end{itemize}

Conversely, every finite projective plane satisfies conditions (a), (b), and (c).
