Hadamard designs are a special class of symmetric block designs intimately connected with Hadamard matrices. Starting from the incidence matrix characterization $MM^* = qI + (q-1)J$, one can construct a Hadamard matrix by bordering the transformed submatrix $H_1$. Conversely, any Hadamard matrix yields a Hadamard design whose incidence matrix satisfies the requisite relations.
