$\mathcal{M}(F)$ is the set of elementary cycles of $\Gamma$.

The close relationship between flows and cycles of $\Gamma$ delineated by B7, B8, and B9 suggests that $F^\perp$ may be related to the cocycle space of $\Gamma$. This is indeed the case.

For each vertex $x \in V$, define $g_x \in \mathbb{Q}^W$ by

$$g_x(u, v) = \begin{cases} 
1 & \text{if } u = x; \\
-1 & \text{if } v = x; \\
0 & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}$$

Clearly $\sigma(g_x) = f^*(x)$.
