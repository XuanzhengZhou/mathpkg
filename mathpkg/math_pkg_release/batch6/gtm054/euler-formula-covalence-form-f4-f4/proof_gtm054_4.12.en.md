Suppose now that $I$ is the set of isthmuses of $\Gamma$ and that $Z_1, \ldots, Z_{v_2(\Gamma)}$ is a planar imbedding of $\Gamma$. The covalence of the region $Z_i$ is the integer $\rho^{\perp}(Z_i) = |Z_i|$. Note that if $\Gamma_{(I)} \perp \Theta$ where $\Theta = (U, g, E + I)$ and $Z_i = g^*(u_i)$ for $u_i \in U (i=1, \ldots, v_2(\Gamma_{(I)}))$, then the covalence of $g^*(u_i)$ is precisely the valence of $u_i$.
