A13 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $m$ be a positive integer. If $\check{\rho}(\Theta) \leq m - 1$ for some $\chi_0(\Gamma)$-critical subgraph $\Theta$ of $\Gamma$, then $\chi_0(\Gamma) \leq m$.
