This proposition is the Möbius inversion formula on the Boolean lattice of subsets of $V$, expressing the selection $s$ in terms of the cumulative selection $\bar{s}$. It shows that the transformation $s \mapsto \bar{s}$ is invertible, with the inverse given by the same formula but with alternating signs $(-1)^{|S+T|}$. This is the combinatorial foundation of the Principle of Inclusion–Exclusion.
