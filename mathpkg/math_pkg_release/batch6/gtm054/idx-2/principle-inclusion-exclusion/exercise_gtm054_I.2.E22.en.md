Verify that if $(V, \mathcal{E})$ is a set system, then
$$\bigcup_{E \in \mathcal{E}} E = \sum_{t=1}^{|V|} (-1)^{t+1} \sum_{\substack{\mathcal{A} \subseteq \mathcal{E} \\ |\mathcal{A}| = t}} \left|\bigcap_{E \in \mathcal{A}} E\right|.$$
