This lemma states that the sum of $(-1)^{|S+T|}$ over all sets $T$ satisfying $S \subseteq T \subseteq W$ equals $1$ if $S = W$ and $0$ otherwise. It is the combinatorial heart of the Möbius inversion on the Boolean lattice, reflecting the fact that the Möbius function of the subset poset is $\mu(S, T) = (-1)^{|T|-|S|}$. The result quantifies the orthogonality of the inclusion matrix and its signed inverse.
