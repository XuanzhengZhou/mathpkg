Let $M$ be the incidence matrix for the system $\Lambda = (V, f, E)$ corresponding to the indexing $V = \{x_1, \ldots, x_v\}$ and $E = \{e_1, \ldots, e_b\}$. If $M^*M = [m_{ij}]$, show that $m_{ij} = |f(e_i) \cap f(e_j)|$ for all $i, j \in \{1, \ldots, b\}$. Interpret the entries of $MM^*$.
