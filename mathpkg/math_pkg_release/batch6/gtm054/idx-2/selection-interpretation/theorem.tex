Let $s$ be the selection of the system $(V, f, E)$. Let $S \in \mathcal{P}(V)$ and $A \in \mathcal{P}(E)$. Then:

\begin{align*}
\text{(a)} \quad & s(S) = |\{e \in E : f(e) = S\}|; \\
\text{(b)} \quad & \bar{s}(S) = |\{e \in E : f(e) \supseteq S\}|; \\
\text{(c)} \quad & s^*(A) = \left|\left(\bigcap_{e \in A} f(e)\right) \cap \left(\bigcap_{e \in E \setminus A} (V \setminus f(e))\right)\right|; \\
\text{(d)} \quad & \bar{s}^*(A) = \left|\bigcap_{e \in A} f(e)\right|.
\end{align*}
