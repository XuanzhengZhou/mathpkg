For two systems $\Lambda = (V, f, E)$ and $\Omega = (W, g, F)$ with selections $s$ and $t$ respectively, the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\Lambda$ and $\Omega$ are system-isomorphic.
\item[(b)] There exists a bijection $q: V \to W$ such that $q$ is a selection-isomorphism from $s$ to $t$.
\end{enumerate}
