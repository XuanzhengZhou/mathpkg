This proposition establishes that two systems are isomorphic precisely when their vertex sets can be matched by a bijection that respects the selection structure. Combined with results about function-isomorphism, this means system isomorphism can be detected at the level of the multiset of block images, up to renaming of vertices. This characterization allows systems with vertex set $V$ to be identified with elements of $\mathbb{S}(\mathcal{P}(V))$.
