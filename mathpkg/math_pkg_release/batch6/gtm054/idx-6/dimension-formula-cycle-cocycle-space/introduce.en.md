This formula follows from property A6, which states that for any subspace $\mathcal{A}$ of $\mathcal{P}(E)$, the dimensions of $\mathcal{A}$ and its orthogonal complement $\mathcal{A}^\perp$ sum to $|E|$ (the dimension of the ambient space). Since $\mathcal{Z}^\perp(\Lambda)$ is the orthogonal complement of $\mathcal{Z}(\Lambda)$, applying A6 with $\mathcal{A} = \mathcal{Z}(\Lambda)$ yields the result.
