An automorphism of a set system $\Lambda = (V, f, E)$ is a system-isomorphism from $\Lambda$ to itself. Equivalently, it is a pair $(p, q)$ where $p$ is a permutation of the edge set $E$ and $q$ is a permutation of the vertex set $V$, such that the incidence structure is preserved: $q[f(e)] = f(p(e))$ for all edges $e \in E$. The automorphisms of $\Lambda$ capture the symmetries of the incidence structure.
