The automorphism group $G(\Lambda)$ of a set system $\Lambda = (V, f, E)$ consists of all pairs of permutations $(p, q)$ -- with $p$ permuting edges and $q$ permuting vertices -- that preserve the incidence structure. The group operation is componentwise composition of permutations. It is immediate from the definition that $G(\Lambda)$ forms a group, and the automorphism group of the dual system $\Lambda^*$ is isomorphic to $G(\Lambda)$.
