Let $\Lambda = (V, f, E)$ be a set system with bond space $\mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)$ and cycle space $\mathcal{Z}(\Lambda)$. The orthogonal complement
$$\mathcal{Y}^\perp(\Lambda)$$
of $\mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)$ in $\mathcal{P}(E)$ is called the **cospace** of $\Lambda$, and the orthogonal complement
$$\mathcal{Z}^\perp(\Lambda)$$
of $\mathcal{Z}(\Lambda)$ in $\mathcal{P}(E)$ is called the **cocycle space** of $\Lambda$.
