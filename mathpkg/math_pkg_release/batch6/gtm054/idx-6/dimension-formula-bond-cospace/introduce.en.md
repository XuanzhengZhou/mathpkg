This formula follows from property A6 applied to the bond space $\mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)$. Since $\mathcal{Y}^\perp(\Lambda)$ is the orthogonal complement of $\mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)$ in $\mathcal{P}(E)$, property A6 directly yields $\dim(\mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)) + \dim(\mathcal{Y}^\perp(\Lambda)) = |E|$. Together with D3 and D4, this completes the dimension-theoretic picture of the four fundamental spaces associated with a set system.
