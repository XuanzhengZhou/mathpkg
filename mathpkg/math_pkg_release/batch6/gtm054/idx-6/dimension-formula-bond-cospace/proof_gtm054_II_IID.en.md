By property A6, for any subspace $\mathcal{A}$ of $\mathcal{P}(E)$, the dimensions of $\mathcal{A}$ and its orthogonal complement $\mathcal{A}^\perp$ satisfy
$$\dim(\mathcal{A}) + \dim(\mathcal{A}^\perp) = |E|.$$
Taking $\mathcal{A} = \mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)$, the bond space, we have $\mathcal{A}^\perp = \mathcal{Y}^\perp(\Lambda)$, the cospace. Substituting yields
$$\dim(\mathcal{Y}(\Lambda)) + \dim(\mathcal{Y}^\perp(\Lambda)) = |E|.$$
