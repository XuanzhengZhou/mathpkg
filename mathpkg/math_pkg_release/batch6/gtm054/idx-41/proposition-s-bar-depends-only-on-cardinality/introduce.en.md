Proposition A5 shows that for any design with constant blocksize $k$, the average selection function $\bar{s}(S)$ depends only on the cardinality of $S$, not on the particular subset. This is a fundamental structural result that implies being a $(t; 1)$-design automatically makes a system an $(i; 1)$-design for all $i \leq t$. The explicit formula in terms of binomial coefficients and $\lambda(t)$ is a direct consequence of Lemma A4.
