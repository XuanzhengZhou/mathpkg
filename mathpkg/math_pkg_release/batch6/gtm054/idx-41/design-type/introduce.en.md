The design-type of a design $\Lambda$ is the maximal collection of parameters $(t_1, \ldots, t_p; a_1, \ldots, a_q)$ for which $\Lambda$ satisfies the regularity conditions. While any subset of the parameters automatically yields a valid design (A1 implies A2), the design-type captures the converse: if $\Lambda$ satisfies the conditions for a subset of parameters, then those parameters must have been included in the original set. This ensures the design-type is the minimal description from which all other design properties follow.
