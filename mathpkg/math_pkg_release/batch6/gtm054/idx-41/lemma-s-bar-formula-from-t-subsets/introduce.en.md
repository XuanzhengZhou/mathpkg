Lemma A4 provides a key formula expressing the value of the average selection function $\bar{s}$ on a subset $S$ of vertices in terms of its values on $t$-subsets of $V$. The formula uses the incidence indicator $[S, T]$ and a binomial coefficient normalization. This lemma is fundamental because it shows that for a $(; 1)$-design, the value of $\bar{s}$ on subsets of size less than $t$ is determined by the values on $t$-subsets.
