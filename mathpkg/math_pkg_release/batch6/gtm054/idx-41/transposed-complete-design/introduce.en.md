A transposed-complete design is the dual notion of a complete design: its dual system $\Lambda^*$ is isomorphic to the set system of all $k$-subsets of the block set $E$, for some $k < b$. Together, complete and transposed-complete designs represent the maximally symmetric cases in design theory.
