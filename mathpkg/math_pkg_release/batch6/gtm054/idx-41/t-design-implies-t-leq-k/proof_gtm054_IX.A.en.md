**Proof.** Since $\lambda(t) > 0$ by definition of a $t$-design, each $t$-subset of $V$ is contained in at least one block. Since the blocksize of $\Lambda$ is $k$, a $t$-subset can be contained in a block only if $t \leq k$. $\square$
