Statement A6 records the elementary but fundamental observation that for a $t$-design, the parameter $t$ cannot exceed the blocksize $k$. This follows because $\lambda(t) > 0$ by definition (each $t$-subset must be contained in some block), which forces $t \leq k$ since a $t$-subset cannot be contained in a block of size smaller than $t$.
