D8 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $A$ is an atom and $U$ is a union of atoms of $\Gamma$, then exactly one of the following holds: $A \subseteq U, A \subseteq \overline{U},$ or $A \subseteq N(U)$.
