Compute each of the parameters $\alpha_0, \alpha_1, \beta_{00}, \beta_{01}, \beta_{10}, \beta_{11}$ for:
(a) the complete graph $K_n$;
(b) the $n$-circuit $\Delta_n$, $n \geq 3$;
(c) the graph obtained by removing an elementary $n$-cycle from $K_n$, $n \geq 3$.
