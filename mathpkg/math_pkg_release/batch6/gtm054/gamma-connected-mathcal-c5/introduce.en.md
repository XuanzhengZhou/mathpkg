C5 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $\Gamma$ be $m$-connected for some $m \geq 1$. Let $x \in V$ and $E \in \mathcal{E}$. Then the subgraphs $\Gamma_{(x)}$ and $\Gamma_{(E)}$ are each $(m - 1)$-connected.
