Let $\Lambda$ be a nondegenerate design. Prove that $\Lambda$ satisfies conditions (a) and (b) but not (c) of Proposition D3 if and only if $\Lambda$ is either $(V, \mathcal{P}_{v-1}(V))$ for $v = 3$ or $\Lambda$ is the symmetric $\lambda$-linked design described in Exercise A16.

We now turn to the question of existence of finite projective planes. The following exercise gives a construction for a projective plane of order $n$ whenever there exists a finite field of order $n$; i.e., whenever $n = p^m$ where $p$ is prime and $m$ is a positive integer.
