The sum of two $t$-designs with the same blocksize and disjoint block sets is again a $t$-design. Moreover, the design parameters are additive: the design parameter of the sum is the sum of the design parameters. This proposition justifies the notation $\Lambda_1 + \Lambda_2$ and underpins the arithmetic of designs.
