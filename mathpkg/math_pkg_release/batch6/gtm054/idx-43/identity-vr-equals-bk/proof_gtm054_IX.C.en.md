From Axiom A9 with $i = 0$, we obtain the identity $vr = bk$ for all $t$-designs. This follows by counting incidences in two ways: each of the $v$ vertices appears in $r$ blocks (giving $vr$), and each of the $b$ blocks contains $k$ vertices (giving $bk$).
