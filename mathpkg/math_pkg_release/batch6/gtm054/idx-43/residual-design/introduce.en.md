The residual design of a symmetric block design $\Lambda$ is obtained by removing a designated block $e_0$ and all vertices in its image, then restricting the remaining blocks to the remaining vertices. Together with the derived design, these constructions provide important methods for generating new designs from existing symmetric block designs. Different choices of the distinguished block may yield nonisomorphic results.
