For a symmetric block design, three important parameter relations hold: the blocksize equals the replication number ($k = r$), there is a quadratic relation $(v-1)\lambda = k(k-1)$, and the dual design parameter at level 2 equals the original $\lambda$. This explains why symmetric block designs are also called $(v, k, \lambda)$-configurations.
