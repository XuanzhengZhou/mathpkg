(C. Berge [b.5]). Let $\Gamma = (V, \mathcal{E})$ be $h$-valent and suppose that whenever $\varnothing \subset S \subset V$, then the number of edges incident with exactly one element of $S$ is at least $h - 1$. Show that $\Gamma$ either admits a 1-factor or is almost factorable.
