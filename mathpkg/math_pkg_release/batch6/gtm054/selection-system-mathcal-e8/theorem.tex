Let $s$ be the selection of the system $(V, f, E)$. Let $S \in \mathcal{P}(V)$, and $A \in \mathcal{P}(E)$. Then:

(a) $s(S) = |\{e \in E: f(e) = S\}|$;
(b) $\bar{s}(S) = |\{e \in E: f(e) \supseteq S\}|$;
(c) $s^*(A) = |(\bigcap_{e \in A} f(e)) \cap (\bigcap_{e \in E + A} (V + f(e)))|$;
(d) $\bar{s}^*(A) = |\bigcap_{e \in A} f(e)|$.
