Proposition C6 establishes fundamental linear relationships among four key graph parameters: the vertex-covering number of edges $\beta_{10}$, the edge independence number $\alpha_1$, the deficiency $\delta$, and the vertex independence number $\alpha_0$. Part (a) gives an exact identity involving the number of isolated vertices $k$, while part (b) provides lower and upper bounds for the vertex independence number in terms of the deficiency and the vertex-covering number of edges.
