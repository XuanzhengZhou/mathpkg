For any graph $\Gamma$ with $k$ isolated vertices,
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\beta_{10} + k = \alpha_1 + \delta$;
\item[(b)] $\delta \leq \alpha_0 \leq \beta_{10} + k$.
\end{enumerate}

Here $\beta_{10}$ is the vertex-covering number of edges (the minimum size of a set of edges that covers all vertices having degree at least $1$), $\alpha_1$ is the edge independence number, $\alpha_0$ is the vertex independence number, and $\delta$ is the deficiency of $\Gamma$.
