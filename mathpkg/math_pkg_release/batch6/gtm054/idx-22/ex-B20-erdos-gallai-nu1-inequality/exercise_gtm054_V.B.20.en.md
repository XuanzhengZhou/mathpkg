(P. Erdős and T. Gallai [e.4]) Prove for any graph

$$\nu_1 \leq \begin{cases}
\binom{2\alpha_1}{2} & \text{if } \nu_0 = 2\alpha_1; \\[6pt]
\binom{2\alpha_1 + 1}{2} & \text{if } 2\alpha_1 < \nu_0 \leq \dfrac{5\alpha_1 + 2}{2}; \\[6pt]
\binom{\alpha_1}{2} + \alpha_1(\nu_0 - \alpha_1) & \text{if } \dfrac{5\alpha_1 + 2}{2} < \nu_0.
\end{cases}$$

where $\nu_1$ is the number of edges and $\alpha_1$ is the edge independence number.
