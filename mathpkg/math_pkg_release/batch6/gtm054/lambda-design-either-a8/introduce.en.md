A8 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $\Lambda$ is a $(t; a)$-design where either $t = 1$ or $a = 1$, then $\Lambda$ has design-type $(1, \ldots, t'; 1, \ldots, a')$ for some $t' \geq t$ and some $a' \geq a$.
