Let $L$ be a subspace of $\mathbb{Q}^X$ and let $h \in L \cap \mathbb{Z}^X$. Prove that

$$h = \sum_{i=1}^{m} g_i$$

where $g_i$ is an elementary function of $L$, $\sigma(g_i) \subseteq \sigma(h)$ for $i = 1, \ldots, m$, and $g_i(x)h(x) \geq 0$ for all $x \in X$ and $i = 1, \ldots, m$.
