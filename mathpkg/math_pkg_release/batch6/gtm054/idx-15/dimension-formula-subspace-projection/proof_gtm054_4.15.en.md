Since $L \cap \mathbb{Q}^{X \setminus Y}$ is the kernel of the restriction $\pi_Y|_L$, the formula follows immediately from the rank-nullity theorem applied to the linear map $\pi_Y|_L: L \to \mathbb{Q}^Y$.
