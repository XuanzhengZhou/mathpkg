This formula is the analogue of the rank-nullity theorem for the projection $\pi_Y$ restricted to a subspace $L \subseteq \mathbb{Q}^X$. The dimension of $L$ equals the sum of the dimension of the kernel (functions vanishing on $Y$) and the dimension of the image under $\pi_Y$.
