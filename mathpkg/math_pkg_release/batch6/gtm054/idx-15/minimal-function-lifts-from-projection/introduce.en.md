Corollary A11 shows that minimal functions are well-behaved under projection: any minimal function of the projected subspace $\pi_Y[L]$ can be lifted to a minimal function of the original subspace $L$. The proof uses the $\mathcal{M}$-decomposition theorem and the scalar multiple property (A2).
