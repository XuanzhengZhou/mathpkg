This proposition establishes that matroid duality is compatible with the operations of restriction and contraction. Specifically, the orthogonal dual of a contraction equals the contraction of the orthogonal dual, and the orthogonal dual of a restriction equals the contraction of the orthogonal dual. These identities are fundamental for working with minors and duals simultaneously in matroid theory.
