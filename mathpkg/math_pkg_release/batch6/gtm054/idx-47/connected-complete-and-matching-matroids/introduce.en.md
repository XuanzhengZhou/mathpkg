This exercise investigates connectivity for two important classes of matroids. Part (a) asks for conditions under which a complete matroid is connected, while part (b) asks when the matching matroid of a multigraph (whose independent sets are the matched sets) is connected. Both problems connect matroid connectivity to properties of the underlying combinatorial structures that generate these matroids.
