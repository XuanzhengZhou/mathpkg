This exercise connects matroid minors to graph-theoretic subcontractions. It shows that taking the cycle matroid or cocycle matroid of a graph subcontraction yields a minor of the corresponding matroid of the original graph. Conversely, every minor of a cocycle matroid comes from some subcontraction, while for cycle matroids additional conditions are needed -- illustrating a fundamental asymmetry between cycle and cocycle matroids of graphs.
