This exercise collects seven fundamental identities describing how restriction $\Lambda_U$ and contraction $\Lambda_{[U]}$ interact with the basic structure of a matroid. The identities characterize independence systems, spanning systems, rank functions, and basis systems of the restricted and contracted matroids in terms of the corresponding structures of the original matroid $\Lambda$. Part (g) gives a composition property: independent sets from the contraction and from the complement restriction combine to form an independent set in the original matroid.
