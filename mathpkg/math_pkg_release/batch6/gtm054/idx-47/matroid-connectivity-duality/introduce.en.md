This proposition states that the Tutte connectivity $\tau(\Lambda)$ of a matroid is invariant under taking the orthogonal dual. That is, a matroid and its dual have exactly the same connectivity. The proof uses the rank formula for the dual matroid (D4e) to show that the condition $r(U) + r(V \setminus U) - r(V) = m$ for $m$-separation is symmetric under duality.
