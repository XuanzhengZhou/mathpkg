For a connected nonplanar graph $\Gamma$ with a given cycle cover $Z_1, \ldots, Z_m$, this corollary bounds the chromatic number $\chi_0(\Gamma)$ in terms of the combinatorial invariant $\nu_2(Z_1, \ldots, Z_m) - \nu_1 + \nu_0$. This result is obtained from IIIA15b and C6. Notably, this formula differs from Heawood's formula only insofar as the term $\nu_2(Z_1, \ldots, Z_m) - \nu_1 + \nu_0$ replaces the Euler characteristic.
