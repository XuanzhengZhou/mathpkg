D13 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{M})$ be a matroid and let $U \in \mathcal{P}(V)$. Then

(a) $(\Lambda_{[U]})^\perp = (\Lambda^\perp)_{[U]}$;

(b) $(\Lambda_U)^\perp = (\Lambda^\perp)_{[U]}$.
