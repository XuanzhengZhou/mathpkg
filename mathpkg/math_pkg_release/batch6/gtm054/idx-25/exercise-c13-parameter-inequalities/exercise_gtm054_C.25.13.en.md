From C6 and C9 we deduce that for $j \neq k$, we have $\alpha_i \leq \beta_{j,k}$ in three of the four possible cases. From C12, we infer $\beta_{j,j} \leq \alpha_i$ in three of four possible cases. Determine whether the remaining inequalities, namely $\alpha_0 \leq \beta_{01}$ and $\beta_{11} \leq \alpha_0$, are true or false, and show that in any graph without isolated vertices, we have the weaker result $\beta_{11} \leq \beta_{01}$.
