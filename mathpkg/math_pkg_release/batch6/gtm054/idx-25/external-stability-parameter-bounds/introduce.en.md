Proposition C12 gives three bounds relating the external stability numbers $\beta_{00}$ and $\beta_{11}$ to the independence numbers $\alpha_0$ and $\alpha_1$. Part (a) states that the vertex external stability number is at most the vertex independence number. Part (b) gives the analogous bound for edge parameters. Part (c) strengthens $\beta_{00} \leq \alpha_0$ to $\beta_{00} \leq \alpha_1$ when the graph has no isolated vertices, using the Main Matching Theorem and properties of bipartite graphs.
