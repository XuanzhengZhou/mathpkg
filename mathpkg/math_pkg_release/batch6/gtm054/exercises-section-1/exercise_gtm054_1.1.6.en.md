Prove that if $\mathcal{Q} \in \mathbb{P}_m(U)$ and $\mathcal{R} \in \mathbb{P}_n(U)$, then $\mathcal{QR} \in \mathbb{P}_p(U)$ for some $p \leq mn$. Show, moreover, that this multiplication is commutative and associative and admits an identity in $\mathbb{P}(U)$.

The next result delineates the fundamental relationship between partitions and equivalence relations.
