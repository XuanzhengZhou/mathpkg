This lemma asserts that every Platonic graph admits an orthogonal partner that is itself connected and Platonic. The orthogonal graph $\Theta$ is constructed from the vertex cocycles of an isocovalent imbedding of $\Gamma$, and the orthogonality relation $\perp$ is symmetric in its essential properties. The statement is a key structural result used to propagate properties such as biconnectivity between orthogonal Platonic graphs.
