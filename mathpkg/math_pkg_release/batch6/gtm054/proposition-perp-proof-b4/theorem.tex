B5 Proposition. (a) $\dim(F) = (|V| - 1)^2$; (b) $\dim(F^\perp) = |V| - 1$.

$$\dim(\mathbb{Q}^W) = \dim(\ker \partial) + \dim(\partial[\mathbb{Q}^W])$$

it follows that

$$\dim(F) = |W| - \dim(\partial[\mathbb{Q}^W]) = |V|^2 - |V| - \dim(\partial[\mathbb{Q}^W])$$
