Let $\mathcal{A} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(E)$ be a subspace and let $\{F_1, F_2\} \in \mathbb{P}_2(E)$. Then

$$\dim(\mathcal{A}) - \dim(\mathcal{A} \cap \mathcal{P}(F_1)) - \dim(\mathcal{A} \cap \mathcal{P}(F_2))$$
$$= |F_i| - \dim(\mathcal{A} \cap \mathcal{P}(F_i)) - \dim(\mathcal{A}^\perp \cap \mathcal{P}(F_i))$$

for $i = 1, 2$.
