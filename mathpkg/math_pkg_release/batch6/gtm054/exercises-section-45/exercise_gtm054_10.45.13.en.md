Let $A_0 \in \mathcal{A}$ be a set obtained by application of the greedy algorithm. Let $A_1 \in \mathcal{A}$ and suppose that $|A_0 + A_1| \leq 2$. Then $w(A_1) \leq w(A_0)$.

The next result generalizes a theorem of D. Gale [g.1].
