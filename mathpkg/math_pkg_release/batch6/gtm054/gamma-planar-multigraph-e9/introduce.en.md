E9 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $\Gamma$ be a planar multigraph. Every planar imbedding of $\Gamma$ is both simple and elementary if and only if $\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)$ is connected.
