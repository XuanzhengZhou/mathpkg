A necessary and sufficient condition for B to admit a matching of $V_i$ is

$$U \subseteq V_i \Rightarrow |U| \leq |N(U)| \quad (i = 1, 2).$$

Proof: Sufficiency. The condition is clearly equivalent to the assertion that $\delta(U) \leq 0$ for all $U \subseteq V_i$, i.e., that $\delta_i(B) = 0$. Now apply the theorem.

Necessity. Suppose that B admits a matching of $V_i$ and let $U \subseteq V_i$. By the theorem,

$$0 = \delta_i(B) \geq \delta(U) = |U| - |N(U)|,$$

whence the condition follows.
