Let $r$ be the rank function of the geometry $\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{M})$ and let $h$ be the lattice-isomorphism in the above proof. If $r'$ is the rank function of the lattice $L$ (same proof), show that $r(U) = r'(h(U))$ for all closed sets $U$ of $\Lambda$.

A lattice $L = (S, \leq)$ is said to be relatively complemented if $L_{[x,y]}$ is complemented for all $x, y \in S, x \leq y$.
