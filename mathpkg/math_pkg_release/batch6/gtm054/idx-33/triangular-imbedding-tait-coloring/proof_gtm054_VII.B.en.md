Since by definition, $\{Z_1, \ldots, Z_m\}$ is the set of vertex cocycles of $\Theta$, we have that $\Theta$ is trivalent. The sets $\mathcal{E}_1, \mathcal{E}_2, \mathcal{E}_3$ of Proposition B14 correspond to the color classes of a Tait coloring of $\Theta$ (cf. Exercise A46), whence the result follows. $\square$
