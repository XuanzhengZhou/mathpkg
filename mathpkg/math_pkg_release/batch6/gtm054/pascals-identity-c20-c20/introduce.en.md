C20 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

$MJ = kJ.$

Hence by C17, C18, and C20,

$$MM^* = MM^*MM^{-1}$$
$$= M[(k - \lambda^*)I + \lambda^*J]M^{-1}$$
$$= (k - \lambda^*)I + \lambda^*J$
$$= M^*M.$$
