Prove that the vertex group and the edge group of a finite graph are group-isomorphic if and only if the graph has at most one isolated vertex and no component has exactly one edge. Show that this statement is false for infinite graphs. (The result is due to Sabidussi [s.2] and Harary and Palmer [h.6].)
