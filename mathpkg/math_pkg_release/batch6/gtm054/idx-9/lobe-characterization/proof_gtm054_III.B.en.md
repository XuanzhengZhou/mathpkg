Let $\Gamma'$ be a lobe of $\Gamma$ which is not induced by an isolated vertex, i.e., $\Gamma' = \Gamma_Q$ for some cell $Q$ of the component partition of $\mathcal{Z}^\perp(\Gamma)$. If $|Q| = 1$ then by B6, $\Gamma'$ is induced by an isthmus. If $|Q| > 1$, then by B8, $\Gamma'$ is biconnected, and by B7, $\Gamma'$ is a maximal biconnected submultigraph of $\Gamma$.

Conversely, if $\Gamma'$ is induced by an isthmus, then it is a lobe by B6. If $\Gamma'$ is a maximal biconnected submultigraph of $\Gamma$, then it is a lobe by B7 and B8.
