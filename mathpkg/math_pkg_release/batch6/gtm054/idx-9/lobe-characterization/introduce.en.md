This proposition provides a complete structural characterization of lobes in a multigraph. A lobe is either: (1) a trivial component consisting of an isolated vertex, (2) an isthmus (bridge) considered as a degenerate two-connected component, or (3) a maximal biconnected submultigraph. This decomposition of a multigraph into lobes — its "biconnected components" — is fundamental to understanding multigraph structure.
