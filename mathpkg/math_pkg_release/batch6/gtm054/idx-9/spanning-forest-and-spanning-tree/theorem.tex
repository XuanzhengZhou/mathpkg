We call a subgraph of $\Gamma$ which is a forest or tree a \textbf{subforest} or \textbf{subtree}, respectively. If $\Gamma$ is a subforest (subtree) of the multigraph $\Gamma$ such that $\nu_0(\Gamma) = \nu_0(\Gamma)$ and $\nu_{-1}(\Gamma) = \nu_{-1}(\Gamma)$, we call $\Gamma$ a \textbf{spanning forest} (\textbf{spanning tree}).
