This proposition is the dual of C11, giving six equivalent characterizations of subsets $F$ that determine spanning coforests. The edge set of a spanning coforest is called a dendroid (Tutte's terminology). The conditions mirror those for spanning forests: dimension constraints, extremal subset properties (maximal containing no cocycle, minimal meeting every cycle), and basis characterizations of both the cycle and cocycle spaces.
