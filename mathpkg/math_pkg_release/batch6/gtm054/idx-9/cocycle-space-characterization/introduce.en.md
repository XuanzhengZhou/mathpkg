This proposition answers the fundamental question: which subspaces of the power set $\mathcal{P}(E)$ arise as cocycle spaces of multigraphs? The answer is remarkably simple: a subspace is a cocycle space exactly when it is spanned by sets $C_i$ with the property that each edge belongs to exactly two of the $C_i$. These $C_i$ correspond to the vertex cocycles $f^*(x)$ of the vertices. Moreover, any cocycle space can be realized by a connected multigraph.
