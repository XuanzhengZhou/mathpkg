Prove that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\Gamma$ is geodetic if and only if every lobe of $\Gamma$ is geodetic.
\item[(b)] If $\Gamma$ is geodetic and biconnected, then $\dim(\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)) \neq 2$.
\end{enumerate}
