This proposition gives multiple equivalent characterizations of forests. A multigraph is a forest exactly when every edge is an isthmus, which is also equivalent to the cocycle space having full dimension $
u_1$. Adding either connectivity or the edge-vertex relation $
u_1 = 
u_0 - 1$ to any of these yields a tree — a connected forest, characterized by having exactly one fewer edge than vertices.
