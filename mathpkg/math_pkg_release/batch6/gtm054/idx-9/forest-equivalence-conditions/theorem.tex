For a multigraph $\Gamma$, conditions (a), (b), and (c) below are equivalent. If two of the conditions hold including at least one from among (d) and (e), then all five hold. In particular, if $\Gamma$ is a tree, then all five hold.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\Gamma$ is a forest.
\item[(b)] Every edge is an isthmus.
\item[(c)] $\dim(\mathcal{L}^\perp) = \nu_1$.
\item[(d)] $\Gamma$ is connected.
\item[(e)] $\nu_1 = \nu_0 - 1$.
\end{enumerate}
