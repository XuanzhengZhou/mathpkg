This exercise establishes a dichotomy for vertices adjacent to isthmuses (bridges) in a multigraph. If a vertex is incident with an isthmus, it must be either an articulation vertex — whose removal disconnects the multigraph — or a pendant vertex of degree one. This result links the local structure around bridges to the global connectivity properties of the multigraph.
