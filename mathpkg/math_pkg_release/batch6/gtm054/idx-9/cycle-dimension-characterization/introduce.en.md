This exercise establishes a bound on the dimension of the cycle space: for any multigraph with at least one edge, the dimension of $\mathcal{L}$ is strictly less than the number of edges. It then asks to characterize the extremal case where the dimension equals $
u_1 - 1$, which corresponds to multigraphs that are forests plus one additional edge creating a single cycle.
