B6 (theorem) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $\Lambda$ be a $(; a)$-design no vertex of which is incident with every block.

(a) If $a \geq 2$, then $v \geq b$.

(b) If $a \geq 3$, then $v = b$ if and only if $\Lambda$ is a trivial design.
