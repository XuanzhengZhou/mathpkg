C19 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

$r(U + \{x_1, x_2\}) - r(U + \{x_2\}) = 1.$

By successive substitutions of C18, C19, then C17,

$$r(U) = r(U + \{x_2\}) - 1$$
$$= r(U + \{x_1, x_2\}) - 2$$
$$= r(U + \{x_1\}) - 2,$$

contrary to C2. ...
