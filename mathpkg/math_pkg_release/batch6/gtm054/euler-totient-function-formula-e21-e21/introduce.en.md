E21 (theorem) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $n \in \mathbb{N}$, and let $V$ be the set of prime divisors of $n$. Then

$$\varphi(n) = n \prod_{p \in V} \left(1 - \frac{1}{p}\right).$$
