E11 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

A subcontraction of a multigraph $\Gamma$ is a submultigraph of a contraction of $\Gamma$. For example, every submultigraph of $\Gamma$ is also a subcontraction of $\Gamma$.
