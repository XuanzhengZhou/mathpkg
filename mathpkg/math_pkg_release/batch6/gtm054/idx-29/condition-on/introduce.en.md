Condition $O_n$ is a strong linkedness property of multigraphs. Given any $2n$ distinct vertices paired as $n$ source-sink pairs $(a_i, z_i)$, there must exist $n$ openly-disjoint paths where the $i$-th path connects $a_i$ specifically to $z_i$. This is substantially stronger than Proposition C9, which only guarantees the correct multiplicities without specifying which source connects to which sink. The property $O_n$ is monotone decreasing in $n$, and its relationship to connectivity is captured by Theorem C10.
