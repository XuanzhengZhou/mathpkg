If $v$ is a vertex of minimum degree $\check{\rho}(\Gamma)$, then the set of neighbors of $v$ separates $v$ from the rest of the graph. Hence $\kappa(\Gamma) \leq \deg(v) = \check{\rho}(\Gamma)$.
