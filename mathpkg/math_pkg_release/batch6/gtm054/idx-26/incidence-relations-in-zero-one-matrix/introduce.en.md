In a $\{0,1\}$-matrix, incidence relations generalize the geometric notion of incidence found in graphs and combinatorial designs. The definition is motivated by the incidence relations in multigraphs and the incidence matrix of a set system. Five distinct incidence relations are specified: row-column, row-row, column-column, position-line, and position-position, each characterized by the entries of the matrix.
