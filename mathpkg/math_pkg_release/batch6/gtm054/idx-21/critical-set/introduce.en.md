A critical set in a bipartite graph $B$ is a subset $U \subseteq V_i$ achieving the maximum possible deficiency, i.e., $\delta(U) = \delta_i(B)$. Critical sets are the subsets that maximally violate Hall's condition and play a central role in the proof of the Main Matching Theorem (König's theorem on the size of a maximum matching).
