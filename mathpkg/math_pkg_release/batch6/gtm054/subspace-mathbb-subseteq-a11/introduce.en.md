A11 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $L$ be a subspace of $\mathbb{Q}^x$, let $Y \subseteq X$, and let $g$ be a minimal function of $\pi_Y[L]$. Then $g = \pi_Y(h)$ for some minimal function $h$ of $L$.
