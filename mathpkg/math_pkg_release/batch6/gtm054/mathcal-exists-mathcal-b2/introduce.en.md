B2 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $A_1, A_2 \in \mathcal{A}$ and if $|A_1| < |A_2|$, then there exists $x \in A_2 + (A_1 \cap A_2)$ such that $A_1 + \{x\} \in \mathcal{A}$.
