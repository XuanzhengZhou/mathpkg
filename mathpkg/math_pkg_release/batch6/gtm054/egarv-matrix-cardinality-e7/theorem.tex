(Egarváry [e.3]). In a {0, 1}-matrix, the cardinality of a largest set of positions occupied by 1's no two of which are incident equals the cardinality of a smallest set of lines such that every position occupied by a 1 is incident with a line in the set.

Occasionally we may require a matrix over an arbitrary integral domain, in which case the symbol 1 in the above theorem can be replaced by the words "nonzero element."
