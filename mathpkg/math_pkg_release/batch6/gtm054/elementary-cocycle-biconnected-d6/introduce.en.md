D6 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $C$ is an elementary cocycle of the biconnected multigraph $\Gamma$, and if $\pi_{E+C}[\mathcal{Z}^{\perp}(\Gamma)]$ is connected, then $C$ is a vertex cocycle of $\Gamma$.
