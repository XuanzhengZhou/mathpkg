B11 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $\mathcal{Q} \in \mathbb{P}(\mathbb{N} + \{0\})$ and $|\mathcal{Q}| < \infty$, then there exists $Q \in \mathcal{Q}$ and $x, y \in Q$ such that $x + y \in Q$.
