A7 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If in B one has $\mathcal{E} \neq \emptyset$ and $\rho(x_1) \geq \rho(x_2)$ for all $x_i \in V_i$, then there exists a matching of $V_1$.
