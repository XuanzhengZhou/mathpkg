Suppose $A \not\subseteq N(W)$. Then $A \cap P \neq \varnothing$, where $P = W$ or $P = \overline{W}$. By D2, $A \cap P$ is a fragment, but by definition of an atom, $|A| = \alpha(\Gamma)$ is minimal, hence $A = A \cap P$, whence $A \subseteq P$.
