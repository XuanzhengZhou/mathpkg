By E4, we may assume that some two vertices are not incident. By Exercise C16, there exists a submultigraph $\Theta$ of $\Gamma$ with an interior vertex $a$, an exterior vertex $z$, and $|A(\Theta)| = \kappa(\Gamma)$. By C4, $\kappa(\Gamma) \leq \rho(a) \leq \nu_1(\Theta)$, and $\kappa(\Gamma) \leq \rho(z) \leq \nu_1(\Gamma) - \nu_1(\Theta)$. So $\kappa(\Gamma) \leq \nu_1(\Theta) \leq \nu_1(\Gamma) - \kappa(\Gamma)$. Hence $\tau(\Gamma) \leq |A(\Theta)| = \kappa(\Gamma)$.
