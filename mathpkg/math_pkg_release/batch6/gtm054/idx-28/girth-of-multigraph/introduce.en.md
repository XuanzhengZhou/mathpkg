The girth of a multigraph is the number of edges in a smallest nonempty cycle. For a forest (acyclic multigraph), the girth is defined to be infinity. The girth is at least 2 for any multigraph, and is at least 3 precisely when the multigraph has no parallel edges, i.e., is a simple graph.
