The girth of $\Gamma$ is the cardinality of a smallest nonempty cycle of $\Gamma$ and is denoted by $\gamma(\Gamma)$. We adopt the convention that $\gamma(\Gamma) = \infty$ if $\Gamma$ is a forest. Clearly $\gamma(\Gamma) \geq 2$ for any multigraph $\Gamma$, and $\gamma(\Gamma) \geq 3$ if and only if $\Gamma$ is a graph.
