C12 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

In any graph,

(a) $\beta_{00} \leq \alpha_0$;
(b) $\beta_{11} \leq \alpha_1$;
(c) $\beta_{00} \leq \alpha_1$ if there are no isolated vertices.
