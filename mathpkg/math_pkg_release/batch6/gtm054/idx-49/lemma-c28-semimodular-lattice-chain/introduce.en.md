Lemma C28 establishes two fundamental properties of semimodular lattices. Part (a) states that the Jordan-Dedekind chain condition holds for intervals: all maximal chains between two comparable elements have the same length. Part (b) asserts that the join of an atom with an incomparable element is always a successor (cover) of that element. These properties are essential for defining a rank function on semimodular lattices.
