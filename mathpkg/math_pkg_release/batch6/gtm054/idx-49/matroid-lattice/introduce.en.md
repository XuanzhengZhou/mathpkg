A matroid lattice is a semimodular lattice satisfying the additional condition that every element is the join of a set of atoms (minimal non-zero elements). This property characterizes the lattices that arise as the closed sets of a geometry. Every Boolean lattice is a matroid lattice, and matroid lattices are relatively complemented.
