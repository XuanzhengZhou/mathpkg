Lemma C29 characterizes when one closed set is a successor (cover) of another in the lattice of closed sets of a closure structure. A closed set $U_2$ covers $U_1$ precisely when $U_2$ can be obtained by closing $U_1$ together with a single additional element from $U_2 \setminus U_1$. This lemma is used in proving that the lattice of closed sets of a geometry is a semimodular matroid lattice.
