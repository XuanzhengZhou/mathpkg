A geometry is a matroid in which every element (that is not a loop) and every pair of distinct non-loop elements forms a closed set. The closed sets of a geometry correspond exactly to the "geometric objects" such as points, lines, and the entire space. Both Fano matroids are geometries. Geometries correspond in a nice way to a class of lattices, specifically matroid lattices.
