A minimum flow through a designated edge $e_0$ is a feasible flow that minimizes the net flow across that edge. This notion is the dual of a maximum flow and is motivated by the lower bound provided by Proposition G2. When the lower capacity function $k_1$ itself satisfies the flow conservation constraints, it serves as a minimum flow through every edge simultaneously.
