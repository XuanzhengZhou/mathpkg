Exercise G6 establishes the complete characterization of when a doubly-capacitated network admits a feasible flow. The necessary condition from Theorem G5 is also sufficient. The proof constructs an auxiliary network with two new vertices $x_0$ and $y_0$, defines new capacity functions $k_1'$ and $k_2'$, and reduces the problem to verifying that the max-flow min-cut theorem applies to the augmented network.
