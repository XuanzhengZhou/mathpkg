For a cut $C$ through $e_0$ in a doubly-capacitated network $(V, k_1, k_2)$, define the \text{upper capacity} and the \text{lower capacity} of $C$ through $e_0$ respectively as

$$k_2(C; e_0) = \sum_{g_U(e)=1} k_2(e) - \sum_{\substack{e \neq e_0 \\ g_U(e)=-1}} k_1(e),$$

$$k_1(C; e_0) = \sum_{g_U(e)=1} k_1(e) - \sum_{\substack{e \neq e_0 \\ g_U(e)=-1}} k_2(e).$$
