This proposition describes the structure of the automorphism groups $G_0(\Lambda)$ and $G_1(\Lambda)$ when the system is decomposed into components. The groups are direct products of wreath products: for each system-isomorphism class $j$, a symmetric group $\Pi(m_{hj})$ acts by permuting components isomorphic to a representative $\Lambda_{1j}$, and this is wreathed with the automorphism group of that representative component. This generalizes the familiar wreath product decomposition of automorphism groups of disjoint unions of graphs.
