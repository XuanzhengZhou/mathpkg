This exercise asks the reader to prove that the classical Ford-Fulkerson formulation of the Max-Flow–Min-Cut Theorem (E2, stated in terms of source-sink networks) is equivalent to the algebraic formulation (C6) developed in the earlier sections of this chapter, for the case of integral networks. The equivalence bridges the gap between the graph-theoretic and algebraic viewpoints of network flow theory presented in the book.
