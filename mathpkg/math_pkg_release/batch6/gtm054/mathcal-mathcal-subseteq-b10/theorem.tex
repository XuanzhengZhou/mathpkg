Let $\mathcal{I}, \mathcal{B} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(V)$. Then the following two statements are equivalent:

(a) $(V, \mathcal{I})$ is an independence system and $\mathcal{B}$ is the collection of largest sets in $\mathcal{I}$;

(b) $(V, \mathcal{B})$ is a basis system and $\mathcal{I} = \bigcup_{B \in \mathcal{B}} \mathcal{P}(B)$.
