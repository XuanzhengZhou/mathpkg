Lemma C1 states that any set in a subspace $\mathcal{A}$ of the power set (under symmetric difference) can be decomposed as a disjoint union of elementary sets — the minimal nonempty members of $\mathcal{A}$. The proof proceeds by induction on the cardinality of the set, repeatedly extracting an elementary subset. This decomposition lemma is a key structural result, analogous to the representation of vectors in a vector space as linear combinations of basis elements.
