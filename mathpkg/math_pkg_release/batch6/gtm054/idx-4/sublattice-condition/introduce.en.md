Lemma B38 provides a simple sufficient condition for a subset of a lattice to be a sublattice: closure under the meet and join operations. When the subset is closed under these binary operations, the meets and joins computed within the subset coincide with those computed in the ambient lattice. The proof follows from the definition of meet and join as maximum lower bound and minimum upper bound, respectively.
