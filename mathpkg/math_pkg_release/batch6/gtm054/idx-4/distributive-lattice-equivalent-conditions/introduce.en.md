Proposition B32 establishes that three formulations of the distributive law are mutually equivalent in any lattice. Condition (a) is the standard left-distributive law of meet over join, (b) is the left-distributive law of join over meet, and (c) is a symmetric self-dual condition. The proof proceeds via a cyclic chain of implications (a) $\Rightarrow$ (b) $\Rightarrow$ (c) $\Rightarrow$ (a), each using only the lattice axioms and the absorption laws.
