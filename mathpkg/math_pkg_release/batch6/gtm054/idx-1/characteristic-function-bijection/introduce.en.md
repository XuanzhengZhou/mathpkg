The correspondence $S \mapsto c_S$ between subsets of $U$ and functions from $U$ to the two-element field $\mathbb{K}$ is a bijection. This establishes that $\mathcal{P}(U)$ and $\mathbb{K}^U$ are equinumerous and provides the algebraic perspective on subsets as binary vectors.
