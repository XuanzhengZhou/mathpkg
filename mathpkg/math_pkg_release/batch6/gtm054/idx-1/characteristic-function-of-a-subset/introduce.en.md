The characteristic function $c_S$ maps elements of $U$ to the field $\mathbb{K} = \{0,1\}$ of order 2, indicating membership in $S$. The symbol $\mathbb{K}$ (rather than $\mathbb{F}_2$) is used throughout the text, and the field structure makes the set of all characteristic functions into a vector space over $\mathbb{K}$.
