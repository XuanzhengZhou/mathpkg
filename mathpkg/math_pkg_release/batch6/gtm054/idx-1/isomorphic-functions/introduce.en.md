Two functions are isomorphic if they are "the same up to relabeling" of both domain and codomain elements. One can also consider partial relabelings: a partition-isomorphism relabels only the domain while preserving the partition structure; a selection-isomorphism relabels only the codomain while preserving multiplicities. These notions provide a categorical framework for combinatorial enumeration.
