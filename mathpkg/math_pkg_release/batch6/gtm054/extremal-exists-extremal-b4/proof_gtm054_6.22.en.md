If $U$ is extremal, let $W = V + N(U)$. Clearly $U \subseteq W$, and every component of $\Gamma_U$ is a component of $\Gamma_W$. Hence $\|U\| \leq \|W\|$ while $N(U) = N(W)$. Thus $\delta(W) \geq \delta(U)$, and so $W$ is extremal.
