Let $\mathcal{I} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(V)$ and let $r$ be a selection of $\mathcal{P}(V)$. Then the following two statements are equivalent:

(a) $(V, \mathcal{I})$ is an independence system and

$$r(U) = \max\{|J| : J \in \mathcal{I}; J \subseteq U\}, \quad U \in \mathcal{P}(V);$$

(b) $(V, r)$ is a rank structure and

$$\mathcal{I} = \{J \in \mathcal{P}(V) : r(J) = |J|\}.$$
