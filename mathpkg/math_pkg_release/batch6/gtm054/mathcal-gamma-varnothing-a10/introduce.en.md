A10 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $Z \in \mathcal{L}(\Gamma) + \{\varnothing\}$ and if the submultigraph $\Gamma_z$ is connected, then $\Gamma_z$ is a circuit.
