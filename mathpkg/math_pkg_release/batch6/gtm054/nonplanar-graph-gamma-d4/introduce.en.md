D4 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

For any nonplanar graph $\Gamma$,

$$\chi_0(\Gamma) \leq \left[ \frac{7 + \sqrt{49 - 24\varepsilon(\Gamma)}}{2} \right] \leq \left[ \frac{7 + \sqrt{1 + 48\eta(\Gamma)}}{2} \right].$$
