G11 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $\Gamma = (V, \mathcal{E})$ is a nonplanar graph such that every proper subcontraction is planar, then $\Gamma$ is $K_5$ or $K_{3,3}$.
