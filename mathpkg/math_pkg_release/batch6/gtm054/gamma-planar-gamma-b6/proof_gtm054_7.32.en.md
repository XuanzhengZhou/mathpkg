By IIIF12 and IIIF13, $\rho(\Theta) \leq 4$ for any subgraph $\Theta$ of $\Gamma$. Let $S_0 = V$. Inductively, for $i \geq 0$, there exists a vertex $x_i \in S_i = S_{i-1} + \{x_{i-1}\}$ whose valence in $\Gamma_{S_i}$ is at most 4. Clearly $\chi_0(\Gamma_{S_k}) \leq 4$ for some $k$. By B5, if $\chi_0(\Gamma_{S_i}) \leq 4$, then $\chi_0(\Gamma_{S_{i-1}}) \leq 4$ and the result follows by induction.
