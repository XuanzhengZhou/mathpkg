By the definition of $\chi_0(S)$, one may select a graph $\Gamma$ which is topologically imbeddable in $S$ and such that $\chi_0(\Gamma) = \chi_0(S)$. Clearly $\eta(\Gamma) \leq \eta(S)$. The result now follows from D4.
