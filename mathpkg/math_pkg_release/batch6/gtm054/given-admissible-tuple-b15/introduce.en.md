B15 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Given the admissible 4-tuple $(A_1, A_2, x, y)$, there exists $A_3 \in \mathcal{A}$ such that $x \in A_3$ and $A_3 \subseteq (A_1 \cup A_2) + \{y\}$. (See Figure B16.)
