E6 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Let $s \in \mathbb{S}(\mathcal{P}(V))$. Then
$$s(S) = \sum_{T \in \mathcal{P}(V)} (-1)^{|S+T|}[S, T]\bar{s}(T), \text{ for all } S \in \mathcal{P}(V).$$
