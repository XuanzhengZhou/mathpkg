C3 (theorem) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

For all $q_1, q_2 \geq 1$, $n(q_1, q_2)$ exists. For all $q_1, q_2 \geq 2$,

$$n(q_1, q_2) \leq n(q_1 - 1, q_2) + n(q_1, q_2 - 1).$$
