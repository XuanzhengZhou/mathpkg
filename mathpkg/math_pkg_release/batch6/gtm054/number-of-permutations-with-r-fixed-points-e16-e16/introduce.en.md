E16 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

(Whitney [w.9]). If $\Gamma \perp \Theta$ and each are without isolated vertices, then $\Gamma$ is 3-connected if and only if $\Theta$ is 3-connected.
