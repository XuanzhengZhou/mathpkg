Let $\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{M})$ be a matroid which is representable over $\mathbb{F}$.

(a) If $\mathcal{T} \subseteq \mathbb{F}^V$ is a Tutte subspace for $\Lambda$, then $\mathcal{T}^\perp$ is a Tutte subspace for the dual matroid $\Lambda^\perp$.

(b) Every minor of $\Lambda$ is representable over $\mathbb{F}$.
