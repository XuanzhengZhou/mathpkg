If $\mathcal{T}$ is a Tutte subspace of the matroid $\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{M})$ and $w: V \to \mathbb{F}^V / \mathcal{T}$ is the corresponding Whitney function, then
$$r(\Lambda) = \dim(\mathbb{F}^V / \mathcal{T}) = |V| - \dim(\mathcal{T}).$$
Equivalently,
$$r(\Lambda) + \dim(\mathcal{T}) = |V|.$$
