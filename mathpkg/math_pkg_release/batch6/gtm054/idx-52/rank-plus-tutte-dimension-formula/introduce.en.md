Formula F5 gives the fundamental relationship between the rank of a representable matroid and the dimension of its Tutte subspace. If $\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{M})$ has a Tutte subspace $\mathcal{T} \subseteq \mathbb{F}^V$, then the rank of $\Lambda$ equals the codimension of $\mathcal{T}$ in $\mathbb{F}^V$, i.e., $r(\Lambda) = |V| - \dim(\mathcal{T})$. Equivalently, the rank plus the Tutte subspace dimension equals the size of the ground set: $r(\Lambda) + \dim(\mathcal{T}) = |V|$. This reflects the fact that the Whitney function $w: V \to \mathbb{F}^V/\mathcal{T}$ takes values in a vector space of dimension $r(\Lambda)$, and the cycle space (Tutte subspace) captures the dependencies among the ground set elements.
