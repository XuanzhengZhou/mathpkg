If $\Lambda$ is the set system $(V, \mathcal{P}_k(V))$, determine the selection $s$ of $\Lambda$ and show that for all $S \in \mathcal{P}(V)$,

$$\bar{s}(S) = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if } |S| > k; \\ \binom{|V| - |S|}{k - |S|} & \text{if } |S| \leq k. \end{cases}$$
