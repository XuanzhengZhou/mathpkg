Let $\mathcal{A}$ be a subspace of $\mathcal{P}(V)$.

(a) If $\mathcal{A} = \bigoplus_{i=1}^{k} \mathcal{B}_i$, then $\Lambda(\mathcal{A}) = \bigoplus_{i=1}^{k} \Lambda(\mathcal{B}_i)$.

(b) If $\Lambda(\mathcal{A}) = \bigoplus_{i=1}^{k} \Lambda_i$ where $\Lambda_i = (V_i, \mathcal{E}_i)$, then $\Lambda_i = \Lambda(\mathcal{A} \cap \mathcal{P}(V_i))$ for $i = 1, \ldots, k$. Furthermore,
$$\mathcal{A} = \bigoplus_{i=1}^{k} (\mathcal{A} \cap \mathcal{P}(V_i)).$$
