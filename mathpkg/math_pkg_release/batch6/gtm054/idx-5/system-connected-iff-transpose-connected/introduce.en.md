Corollary C15 follows directly from Proposition C14 and the fact that a system is trivial if and only if its transpose is trivial. It establishes that connectedness is a property invariant under the transpose operation: a system $\Lambda$ is connected exactly when its transpose $\Lambda^*$ is connected. This reflects the symmetry between vertices and blocks in the bipartite representation.
