If $\mathcal{A}$ is a subspace of $\mathcal{P}(V)$, we define
$$\Lambda(\mathcal{A}) = (\operatorname{Fnd}(\mathcal{A}), \mathcal{M}(\mathcal{A})),$$
where $\operatorname{Fnd}(\mathcal{A})$ is the foundation of $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{M}(\mathcal{A})$ is the collection of minimal nonempty subsets in $\mathcal{A}$ (the elementary sets).
