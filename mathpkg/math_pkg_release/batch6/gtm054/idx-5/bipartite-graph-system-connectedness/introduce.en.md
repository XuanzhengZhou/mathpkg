Proposition C23 establishes that the connectedness of a system $\Lambda$ is equivalent to the connectedness of its bipartite graph $\Gamma$. Since paths in $\Lambda$ correspond to paths in $\Gamma$, the component structure of a system is exactly reflected in the component structure of its bipartite graph representation.
