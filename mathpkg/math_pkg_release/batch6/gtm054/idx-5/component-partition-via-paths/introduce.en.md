Proposition C20 provides a concrete characterization of the component partition of a system: the components are precisely the equivalence classes of the path-connectivity relation $\sim$ restricted to the vertex set $V$. This connects the algebraic notion of components (as minimal direct summands) with the graph-theoretic notion of connectivity via paths in the bipartite graph.
