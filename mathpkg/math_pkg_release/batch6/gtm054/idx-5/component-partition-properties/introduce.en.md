Exercise C10 asks the reader to prove three properties relating the component partition of a subspace $\mathcal{A}$ to its elementary sets $\mathcal{M}(\mathcal{A})$. Part (a) characterizes the component partition as the coarsest partition refining the elementary sets. Parts (b) and (c) describe when singleton-generated subspaces become components, both in $\mathcal{A}$ and its orthogonal complement $\mathcal{A}^{\perp}$.
