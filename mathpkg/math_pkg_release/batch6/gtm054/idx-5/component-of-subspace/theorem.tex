A \text{component} of $\mathcal{A}$ is a nontrivial direct summand of $\mathcal{A}$ that is connected (i.e., it has no proper nontrivial direct summand). The \text{component partition} of $\mathcal{A}$ is the partition of $\operatorname{Fnd}(\mathcal{A})$ whose cells are the foundations of the components of $\mathcal{A}$.
