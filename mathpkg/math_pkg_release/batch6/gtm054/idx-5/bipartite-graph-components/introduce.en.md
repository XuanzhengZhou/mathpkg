Exercise C24 asks to prove that the component decomposition of a system and its bipartite graph correspond exactly: the subsystems $\Lambda_1, \ldots, \Lambda_n$ are components of $\Lambda_0$ if and only if their bipartite graphs $\Gamma_1, \ldots, \Gamma_n$ are components of $\Gamma_0$. This reinforces the correspondence established in Proposition C23.
