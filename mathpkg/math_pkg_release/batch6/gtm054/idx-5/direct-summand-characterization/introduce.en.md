Exercise C16 asks to prove that a direct summand $\Omega$ of a system $\Lambda$ is completely determined by either its vertex set $W$ or its block set $F$ (provided both are nonempty). Specifically, $\Omega$ equals both the subsystem induced by $W$ ($\Lambda_W$) and the subsystem induced by $F$ ($\Lambda_F$).
