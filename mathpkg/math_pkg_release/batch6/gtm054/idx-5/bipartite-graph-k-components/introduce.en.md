Exercise C26 concerns the enumeration of systems (hypergraphs) that have a given bipartite graph $\Gamma$. If $\Gamma$ has $k$ connected components, then there are precisely $2^k$ systems whose bipartite graph is $\Gamma$. This is because each component of $\Gamma$ is bipartite with respect to a unique partition (C25), and the choice of which part is designated as vertices vs. blocks gives two possibilities per component.
