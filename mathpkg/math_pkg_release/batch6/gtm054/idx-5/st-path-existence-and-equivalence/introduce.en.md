Exercise C19 establishes basic properties of paths in systems. Part (a) shows that any $st$-path can be reduced to an elementary $st$-path (one with no repeated elements) by removing cycles. Part (b) proves that the relation of being connected by a path is an equivalence relation on the set of vertices and blocks, which partitions $V \cup E$ into connected components.
