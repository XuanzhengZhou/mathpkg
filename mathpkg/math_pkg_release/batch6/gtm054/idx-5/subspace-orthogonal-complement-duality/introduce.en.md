This result states that the orthogonal complement operation distributes over direct sums. If a subspace $\mathcal{A}$ decomposes as a direct sum of subspaces $\mathcal{B}_1$ and $\mathcal{B}_2$, then its orthogonal complement decomposes correspondingly as the direct sum of the orthogonal complements of $\mathcal{B}_1$ and $\mathcal{B}_2$ (taken within their respective foundation sets). This is a key structural property used in the proof of the connectedness duality lemma C11.
