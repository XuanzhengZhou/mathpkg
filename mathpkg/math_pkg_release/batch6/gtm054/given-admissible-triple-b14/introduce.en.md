B14 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Given the admissible triple $(A_1, A_2, y)$, there exists $A_3 \in \mathcal{A}$ such that $A_3 \neq \emptyset$ and $A_3 \subseteq (A_1 \cup A_2) + \{y\}$.
