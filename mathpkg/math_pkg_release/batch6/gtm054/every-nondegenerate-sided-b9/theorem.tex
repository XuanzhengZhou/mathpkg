Every nondegenerate 2-sided design has one of the following design-types:

(a) $(1, \ldots, t; 1)$, $t \geq 1$, $(nontrivial t$-design)
(b) $(1; 1, \ldots, a)$, $a \geq 1$, $(transpose of (a))$
(c) $(1, 2; 1, 2)$, $(nontrivial symmetric block design)$
(d) $(2; 2)$, $(symmetric \lambda$-linked design)
(e) $(1, \ldots, v - 1; 1, \ldots, v - 1)$, $(trivial design)$.
