(a) A necessary and sufficient condition for a subspace $A$ to be connected is that given $x_1, x_2 \in \text{Fnd}(A)$, there exists $M \in \mathcal{M}(A)$ such that $\{x_1, x_2\} \subseteq M$.

(b) The relation $\sim$ on $\text{Fnd}(A)$ given by

$$x \sim y \Leftrightarrow \{x, y\} \subseteq A \text{ for some } A \in \mathcal{M}(A)$$

is an equivalence relation. The equivalence classes are precisely the cells of the component partition.
