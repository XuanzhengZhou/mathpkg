Suppose that $\Lambda$ is nondegenerate. From Theorem B6(a) and its transpose (applied to the dual design), we infer that $b = v$. If $ta > 4$, we apply B6(b) or its transpose to infer that $\Lambda$ is trivial. Thus, if $\Lambda$ is not degenerate, it must be trivial.
