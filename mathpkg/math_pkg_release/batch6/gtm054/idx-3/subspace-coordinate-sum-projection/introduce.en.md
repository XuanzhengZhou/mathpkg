Proposition A12 provides a structural characterization of coordinate sum decompositions of subspaces of $\mathcal{P}(U)$ in terms of the projection map $\pi_B$. Part (a) shows that if a subspace decomposes as a coordinate sum $\mathcal{B} \oplus \mathcal{C}$, then projecting onto the foundation of $\mathcal{B}$ recovers $\mathcal{B}$ itself. Part (b) gives a converse: if projection onto a set $B$ preserves the subspace structure in a suitable sense, then the subspace decomposes as a coordinate sum over $B$ and its complement. This result is fundamental for the decomposition theory of subspaces in algebraic combinatorics.
