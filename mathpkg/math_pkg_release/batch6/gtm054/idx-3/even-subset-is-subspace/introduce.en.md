This proposition establishes that the collection $\mathcal{E}(U)$ of subsets with even cardinality is a subspace of the vector space $\mathcal{P}(U)$ whenever $U$ is nonempty. In that case, the dimension of $\mathcal{E}(U)$ is exactly $|U| - 1$, making it a hyperplane (codimension 1 subspace) within $\mathcal{P}(U)$. The degenerate case $U = \varnothing$ yields $\mathcal{E}(U) = \mathcal{P}(U)$ since the only subset is $\varnothing$, which has even cardinality zero.
