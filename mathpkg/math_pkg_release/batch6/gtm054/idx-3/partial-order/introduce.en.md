A partial order is a binary relation on a set that is reflexive, transitive, and antisymmetric. This fundamental concept generalizes the familiar ordering of numbers and the subset relation. The associated strict order $<$ is defined by requiring $x \neq y$ in addition to $x \leq y$. Partial orders appear naturally throughout combinatorics, notably in the subset inclusion order on $\mathcal{P}(U)$ and the refinement order on partitions.
