Exercise B5 establishes the transitivity of the refinement relation restricted to coverings of $U$. Combined with reflexivity (B3) and antisymmetry (B4), this exercise completes the verification that refinement is a partial order on the set of partitions $\mathbb{P}(U)$, leading to Proposition B6. The condition that $\mathcal{S}$ covers $U$ ensures that each element belongs to some member of $\mathcal{S}$, which is needed for the transitivity argument.
