A chain in a partially-ordered set $(U, \leq)$ is a subset that is totally ordered by the induced order. An $m$-chain is a chain of size $m$. Chains are the fundamental building blocks for studying the structure of posets; the maximum size of a chain and the decomposition of a poset into chains are central topics in order theory and combinatorics. Every singleton subset is trivially a 1-chain.
