D12 (theorem) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

(R. C. Bose [b.10], 1939). Let $n \geq 2$ be an integer. There exists a finite projective plane of order $n$ if and only if $\mathbb{L}_n$ admits a complete orthogonal set.
