Let $\Lambda^{(i)} = (V_i, \mathcal{M}_i)$ for $i = 0, \ldots, n$ be a sequence of matroids such that for each $i = 1, \ldots, n$, $V_i \subseteq V_{i-1}$ and either $\Lambda^{(i)} = \Lambda^{(i-1)}_{V_i}$ or $\Lambda^{(i)} = \Lambda^{(i-1)}_{[V_i]}$. Then there exist $T, U \in \mathcal{P}(V_0)$ containing $V_n$ such that
$$\Lambda^{(n)} = (\Lambda^{(0)}_{[T]})_{V_n} = (\Lambda^{(0)}_{[U]})_{V_n}.$$
