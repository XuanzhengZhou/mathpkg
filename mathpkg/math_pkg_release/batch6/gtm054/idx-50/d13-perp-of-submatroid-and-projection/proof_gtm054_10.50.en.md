(a) We show that the two matroids have the same cycle collection. Let $\mathcal{E}$ denote the collection of cycles of $\Lambda$. The cycles of $(\Lambda_{[U]})^\perp$ are the minimal sets meeting every basis of $\Lambda_{[U]}$. Using D12f, the bases of $\Lambda_{[U]}$ are of the form $B \cap U$ for $B \in \mathcal{B}(\Lambda)$. By D4c, $(\Lambda^\perp)_{[U]}$ has bases of the form $B' \cap U$ for $B' \in \mathcal{B}(\Lambda^\perp)$. Since $\mathcal{B}(\Lambda^\perp) = \{V \setminus B : B \in \mathcal{B}(\Lambda)\}$, the collections coincide.

(b) Apply part (a) to $\Lambda^\perp$ and use $(\Lambda^\perp)^\perp = \Lambda$:
$$(\Lambda_U)^\perp = ((\Lambda^\perp)_{[U]})^\perp = (\Lambda^\perp)^\perp_{[U]} = \Lambda_{[U]}.$$
By taking perpendiculars on both sides we obtain the stated equality.
