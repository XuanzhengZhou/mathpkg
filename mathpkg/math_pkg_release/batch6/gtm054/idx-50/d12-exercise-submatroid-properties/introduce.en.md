This exercise establishes the complete algebraic relationships between a matroid $\Lambda$, its restriction $\Lambda_U$ to a vertex subset $U$, and its projection $\Lambda_{[U]}$ onto $U$. It connects these derived matroids with the four fundamental equivalent formulations: independence systems, spanning systems, basis systems, and rank functions. Part (g) gives a crucial independence-preserving composition property for the restriction and projection.
