This proposition establishes that the connectivity invariant $\tau$ is self-dual: a matroid and its perpendicular (dual) matroid have exactly the same connectivity. The proof follows from the rank formula for the perpendicular matroid (Proposition D4e), which shows that the rank deficiency across any cut is identical for $\Lambda$ and $\Lambda^\perp$.
