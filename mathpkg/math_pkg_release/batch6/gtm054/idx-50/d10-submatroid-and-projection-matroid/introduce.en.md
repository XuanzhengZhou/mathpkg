For a matroid $\Lambda = (V, \mathcal{M})$ and a subset $U$ of its vertex set, two natural derived set systems are defined: the restriction $\Lambda_U = (U, \mathcal{M}(\pi_U[\mathcal{E}]))$ and the projection $\Lambda_{[U]}$. This proposition states that both constructions preserve the matroid property: the restriction and the projection of a matroid are again matroids. This result is fundamental for the theory of matroid minors and connectivity.
