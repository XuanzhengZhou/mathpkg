This exercise establishes the precise relationship between the graph-theoretic operation of subcontraction and the matroid-theoretic operation of taking minors. Part (a) shows that the cycle and cocycle matroid constructions are functorial with respect to subcontractions. Part (b) gives a converse for cocycle matroids. Part (c) asks for the more subtle necessary and sufficient conditions needed for cycle matroids.
