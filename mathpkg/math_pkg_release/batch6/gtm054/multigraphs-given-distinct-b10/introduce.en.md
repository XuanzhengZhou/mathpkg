B10 (axiom) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

III Multigraphs

(f) Given distinct $x, y, z \in V$, there exists an elementary xy-path containing $z$.
(g) Given distinct $x, y, z \in V$, there exists an elementary xy-path avoiding $z$.
