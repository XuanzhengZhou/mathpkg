C21 (corollary) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

(Ryser, ibid). Let $\Lambda$ be a $(; 1, 2)$-design for which $b = v$ and $\lambda^*(2) < k < v$. Then $\Lambda$ is a symmetric block design.
