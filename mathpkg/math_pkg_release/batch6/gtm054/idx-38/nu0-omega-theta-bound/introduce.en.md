Proposition A6 gives an upper bound on the number of vertices $\nu_0(\Gamma)$ of a graph in terms of its clique number $\omega(\Gamma)$ and clique covering number $\theta(\Gamma)$: $\nu_0(\Gamma) \leq \omega(\Gamma)\theta(\Gamma)$. This follows by taking complements in the previously established result VIIA5 applied to the vertex covering number.
