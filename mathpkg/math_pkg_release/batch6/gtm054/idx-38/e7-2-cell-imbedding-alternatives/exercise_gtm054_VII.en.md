\textbf{Exercise E7.}
Let $\Gamma$ be a graph and let $a$ be a vertex of $\Gamma$. Let $\pi_a$ be a cyclic permutation of the edges incident with $a$, where $(\rho(a) - 1)! = 6$ possible choices exist for $\pi_a$ if $\pi_a$ is to be cyclic.

(a) Keeping $\pi_b, \ldots, \pi_g$ as in example E4, find the other five 2-cell imbeddings of $\Gamma$. Determine whether they are all toroidal.

(b) Give an upper bound for the number of distinct 2-cell imbeddings in orientable surfaces for an arbitrary graph.
