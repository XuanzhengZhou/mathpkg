\textbf{Exercise E10.}
Prove:

(a) If $K_n$ admits a triangular imbedding on some surface, then $n \equiv 0, 3, 4,$ or $7 \pmod{12}$.

(b) Under the assumption that $n \equiv 0, 3, 4,$ or $7 \pmod{12}$, $K_n$ has a triangular imbedding if and only if
$$\eta(K_n) = \left\{ \frac{(n - 3)(n - 4)}{12} \right\},$$
where $\eta(K_n)$ denotes the genus of $K_n$ and $\{\cdot\}$ denotes the ceiling function.

(c) Use the sequence $(1, 11, 14, 13, 15, 3, 8, 9, 7, 4, 17, 10, 18, 5, 16, 12, 2, 6)$ to prove that $\eta(K_{19}) = 20$.

(d) Prove that $\eta(K_{12}) = 6$.
