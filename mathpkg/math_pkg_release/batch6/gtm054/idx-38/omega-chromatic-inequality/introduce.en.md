Proposition A4 states the elementary bound $\omega(\Gamma) \leq \chi_0(\Gamma)$: the size of the largest complete subgraph cannot exceed the minimum number of colors needed for a proper vertex coloring. In any proper coloring, all vertices in a clique must receive distinct colors, so the number of colors must be at least the clique size.
