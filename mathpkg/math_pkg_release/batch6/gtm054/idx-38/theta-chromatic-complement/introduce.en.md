Theorem A3 establishes a second fundamental duality via graph complement: the clique covering number $\theta(\Gamma)$ equals the vertex chromatic number $\chi_0(\Gamma')$ of the complement. A surjection $h: V \to \{1,\ldots,m\}$ whose fibers are complete sets in $\Gamma$ corresponds exactly to a proper vertex coloring of the complement $\Gamma'$, since independent sets in $\Gamma'$ are precisely the complete sets in $\Gamma$.
