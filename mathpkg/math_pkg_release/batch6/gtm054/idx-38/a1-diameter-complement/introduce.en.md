This exercise asks to prove a fundamental relationship between a graph and its complement: for any graph $\Gamma$, at least one of $\Gamma$ or its complement $\Gamma'$ has diameter at most 3 (where a disconnected graph is considered to have infinite diameter). This result highlights the tight structural interdependence between a graph and its complement.
