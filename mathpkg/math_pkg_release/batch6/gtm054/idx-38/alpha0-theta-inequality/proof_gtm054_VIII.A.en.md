By A2, $\alpha_0(\Gamma) = \omega(\Gamma')$. By A3, $\theta(\Gamma) = \chi_0(\Gamma')$. Applying the inequality A4 ($\omega \leq \chi_0$) to the complement graph $\Gamma'$ yields
$$\omega(\Gamma') \leq \chi_0(\Gamma').$$
Substituting the equalities from A2 and A3 gives
$$\alpha_0(\Gamma) \leq \theta(\Gamma).$$
