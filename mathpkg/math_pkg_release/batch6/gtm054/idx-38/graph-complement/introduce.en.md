The complement $\Gamma'$ of a graph $\Gamma = (V, \mathcal{E})$ has the same vertex set $V$, but two distinct vertices are adjacent in $\Gamma'$ precisely when they are not adjacent in $\Gamma$. Thus $\Gamma' = (V, \mathcal{P}_2(V) \setminus \mathcal{E})$, where $\mathcal{P}_2(V)$ denotes the set of all 2-element subsets of $V$. The complement operation is an involution: $(\Gamma')' = \Gamma$, and for any induced subgraph, $(\Gamma')_S = (\Gamma_S)'$.
