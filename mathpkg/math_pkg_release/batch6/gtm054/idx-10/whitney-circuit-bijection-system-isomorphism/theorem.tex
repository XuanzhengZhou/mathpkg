Let $\Gamma = (V, f, E)$ be triconnected, and let $\Theta = (U, h, F)$ be a multigraph with no isolated vertices. Let there be a bijection $p: E \rightarrow F$ such that $Z$ is the edge set of a circuit in $\Gamma$ if and only if $p[Z]$ is the edge set of a circuit in $\Theta$. Then $\Gamma$ and $\Theta$ are system-isomorphic.
