Whitney's fundamental theorem states that for a triconnected multigraph, the combinatorial structure of its circuits alone determines the multigraph up to system-isomorphism. If there exists a bijection on edge sets that preserves the circuit property, then the two multigraphs are system-isomorphic. This result highlights the deep relationship between algebraic cycle structure and combinatorial graph structure.
