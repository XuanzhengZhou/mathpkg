Let $\Gamma$ be connected and let $C$ be an elementary cocycle. By C7, $\Gamma_{(C)}$ has two components. If $C$ is not a vertex cocycle, both of these components contain edges, and so $\mathcal{X}^{\perp}(\Gamma_{(C)})$ is not connected. The contrapositive yields the desired result.
