This corollary reformulates the vertex cocycle criterion in terms of the projection map $\pi_{E+C}$ acting on the cocycle space. By the identity $\pi_{E+C}[\mathcal{Z}^{\perp}(\Gamma)] = \mathcal{Z}^{\perp}(\Gamma_{(C)})$, the connectedness condition on the projected cocycle space implies the connectedness condition in Proposition D5, yielding the same conclusion.
