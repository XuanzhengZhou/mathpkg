F5 (proposition) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $\Lambda$ is a partial geometry with parameters $b, v, r, k,$ and $t,$ then

(a) $bk = vr$;
(b) $t \leq r$;
(c) $t \leq k$;
(d) $(v - k)t = k(k - 1)(r - 1)$;
(e) $(b - r)t = r(k - 1)(r - 1)$.
