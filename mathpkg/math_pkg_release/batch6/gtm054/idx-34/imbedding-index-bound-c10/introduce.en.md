Inequality C10 provides an upper bound on the average valence $\rho$ of a graph in terms of its imbedding index $\iota$, girth $\gamma$ (through $G(\gamma) = 2\gamma/(\gamma-2)$), and vertex count $\nu_0$. It is derived from C8 and C9 by eliminating the edge count $\nu_1$. This bound is essential for the case $\iota > 2$ in the main theorem of this section, and together with the simpler cases $\iota \leq 2$, leads to a complete classification of possible average valences for graphs with a given imbedding index.
