By C2 (with the argument $\Gamma$ suppressed), $\iota \geq \nu_1 - \nu_0 + 2 - m$, where $m$ is the size of a cycle cover. From the definitions of cycle cover and girth, each cycle in a minimal cycle cover has length at least $\gamma$, so $m\gamma \leq 2\nu_1$, since each edge belongs to at most two cycles. Eliminating $m$ from these two inequalities yields:

$$\iota \geq \nu_1 - \nu_0 + 2 - \frac{2\nu_1}{\gamma},$$

which rearranges to

$$\iota - 2 \geq \frac{\gamma - 2}{\gamma} \nu_1 - \nu_0.$$

This is exactly C8.
