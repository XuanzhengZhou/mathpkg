Consider a planar imbedding of $\Gamma$ which does not include $Z$ as a region. By Exercise IIIE24, the set $S$ of vertices incident with the edges in $Z$ form a separating set of $\Gamma$. Since $\Gamma_S$ is complete, the restriction to $S$ of any coloring of a subgraph of $\Gamma$ containing $S$ is an injection. By Proposition A15 and the definition of $G$, $\chi_0(\Gamma) \leq 4$, contrary to assumption.
