Let $\mathcal{S} \subseteq \mathbb{P}(U)$ and let $\leq$ denote refinement on $\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{P}(U))$. Then $\{ \mathcal{Q} \in \mathbb{P}(U) : \mathcal{S} \leq \mathcal{Q} \}$ has a minimum element and $\{ \mathcal{Q} \in \mathbb{P}(U) : \mathcal{Q} \leq \mathcal{S} \}$ has a maximum element.
