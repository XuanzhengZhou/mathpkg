Since $Q$ is a cell of the component partition of $\mathcal{Z}(\Gamma)$, we have by IIIC22a, that every two edges in $Q$ belong to a common elementary cycle. By A9, every two edges in $Q$ belong to a common elementary circuit. Finally by B3, $\Gamma_Q$ is biconnected.
