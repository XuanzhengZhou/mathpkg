Lemma B7 states that the flow function $h_D$ associated with any circuit $D$ in the multigraph $\Gamma = (V, f, W)$ belongs to the flow space $F(V)$. This result is the crucial link connecting the combinatorial structure of circuits (oriented cycles) to the algebraic framework of flows, showing that every circuit naturally gives rise to a flow.
