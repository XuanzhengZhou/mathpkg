Lemma B11 describes the support relation for a non-trivial linear combination $g = \sum_{x \in U} \eta_x g_x$ of vertex cocycle basis functions. The union of the coboundaries $f^*(x)$ for all vertices $x \in U$ is contained in the support $\sigma(g)$. This result is useful for analyzing the structure of the cocycle space $F^\perp$ and its relation to cuts in the multigraph $\Gamma$.
