This follows directly from Proposition B8 and IIIA9. By B8, every elementary circuit $D$ in $\Gamma$ gives an elementary flow $h_D$, and conversely, the support of any elementary flow is an elementary cycle. By IIIA9, the support of an elementary flow is an elementary cycle of $\Gamma$. Hence the set $\mathcal{M}(F)$ of all supports of minimal flows equals the set of all elementary cycles of $\Gamma$.
