The **circuit flow function** $h_D$ encodes the orientation of a cyclically-ordered circuit $D$ in a multigraph $\Gamma = (V, f, W)$. It assigns $+1$ to edges traversed in the forward direction, $-1$ to edges traversed backward, and $0$ to edges not in the circuit. This function $\mathbb{Q}^W$ provides the fundamental connection between circuits and elements of the flow space $F(V)$.
