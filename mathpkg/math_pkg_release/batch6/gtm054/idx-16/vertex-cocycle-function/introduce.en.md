The **vertex cocycle basis function** $g_x \in \mathbb{Q}^W$ assigns $+1$ to all edges emanating from a given vertex $x$, $-1$ to all edges terminating at $x$, and $0$ otherwise. Its support $\sigma(g_x)$ equals the coboundary $f^*(x)$ of $x$. These functions form the building blocks of the orthogonal complement $F^\perp$ of the flow space, establishing the relationship between $F^\perp$ and the cocycle space of the multigraph $\Gamma$.
