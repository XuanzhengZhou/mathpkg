(A. C. Green). Construct a graph Γ such that for each {a, z₁, z₂, z₃} ∈ 𝒐₄(V), there is an openly-disjoint 6-family of a{z₁, z₂, z₃}-paths, two of which are az-paths for each i = 1, 2, 3, but Γ is not 6-connected.

If Γ is connected, we define a distance function d: V × V → ℕ by letting d(x, y) denote the least number of edges in an xy-path.
