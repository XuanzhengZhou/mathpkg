Prove that the vertex group and the edge group of a finite graph are group-isomorphic if and only if the graph has at most one isolated vertex and no component has exactly one edge. [Hint: use IIE5.] Show that this statement is false for infinite graphs. (The result is due to Sabidussi [s.2] and Harary and Palmer [h.6]. The suggested method of proof appears in [w.5].)
