Show that any spanning forest of $\Gamma$ can be obtained from any other spanning forest by a finite number of applications of the Exchange Property.

If $\Gamma_{(F)}$ is a spanning forest of $\Gamma$, then $\Gamma_{(E+F)}$ is a spanning coforest. (The edge set of a spanning coforest is called a “dendroid” by Tutte [t.6].) The following result is dual to C11 and should be proved as an exercise.
