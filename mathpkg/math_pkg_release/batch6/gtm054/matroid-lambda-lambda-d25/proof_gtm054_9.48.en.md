Let $r^\perp$ denote the rank function of $\Lambda^\perp$, and let $U \in \mathcal{P}(V)$. Then by D4e,

$$r^\perp(U) + r^\perp(V + U) - r^\perp(V)$$
$$= (|U| + r(V + U) - r(V))$$
$$+ (|V + U| + r(U) - r(V)) - (|V| + r(\varnothing) - r(V))$$
$$= r(U) + r(V + U) - r(V),$$

and the result

Recall that if $\mathcal{B}$ is the collection of largest matched sets of a graph $\Gamma = (V, \mathcal{E})$, then $(V, \mathcal{B})$ is a basis system. The matroid $\Theta$ such that $\mathcal{B}(\Theta) = \mathcal{B}$ is called the matching matroid of $\Gamma$.
