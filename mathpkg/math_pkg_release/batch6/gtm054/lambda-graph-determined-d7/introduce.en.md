D7 (lemma) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

If $\Lambda$ is the Lovász graph determined by $(\Gamma, W, g)$ and if $T \subseteq W$, then $\Lambda_T$ is the Lovász graph determined by both $(\Gamma, T, g_{|T})$ and $(\Gamma_{g_{|T}}), T, g_{|T})...
