Let $V$ consist of the 10 points and let $\mathcal{L}$ consist of the 9 lines shown in Figure F24. Let $\mathbb{F}$ be an arbitrary field.

(a) Show that $(V, \mathcal{P}_3(V) + \mathcal{L})$ is a basis system.
(b) Show that if $V \subseteq \mathbb{F}^3$ and if each set $L \in \mathcal{L}$ were contained in a line in $\mathbb{F}^3$, then $\{a'', b'', c''\}$ would be contained in a line in $\mathbb{F}^3$.
(c) Let $\Lambda$ be the matroid such that $\mathcal{B}(\Lambda) = \mathcal{P}_3(V) + \mathcal{L}$. Show that there exists no Whitney function over $\mathbb{F}$ for $\Lambda$.
(d) In the case that $\mathbb{F} = \mathbb{K}$, describe the minors of $\Lambda$ which are isomorphic to $\Theta_2$.
