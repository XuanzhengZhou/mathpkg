E2 (theorem) from Combinatorics with Emphasis on the Theory of Graphs.

Max-Flow–Min-Cut Theorem: Classical Form (L. R. Ford, Jr. and D. R. Fulkerson [f.3]). In any network $((V, D), k, x_0, y_0)$, $\max\{v(f): f$ is a feasible flow\} = \min\{k(U): U$ is a cut$\}$.
