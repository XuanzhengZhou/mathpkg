Substituting $\nu_0 = 2\nu_1/\rho$ (from A1, the definition of average valence) and $\nu_2 = 2\nu_1/\rho^{\perp}$ (from F3, the definition of average covalence) into the Euler Formula $\nu_2 - \nu_1 + \nu_0 = 2$ yields:
$$\frac{2\nu_1}{\rho^{\perp}} - \nu_1 + \frac{2\nu_1}{\rho} = 2.$$
Dividing by $2\nu_1$ (since $\nu_1 > 0$) gives:
$$\frac{1}{\rho^{\perp}} - \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{\rho} = \frac{1}{\nu_1},$$
which rearranges to
$$\frac{1}{\rho} + \frac{1}{\rho^{\perp}} = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{\nu_1}.$$
