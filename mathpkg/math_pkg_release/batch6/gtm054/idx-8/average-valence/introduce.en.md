The average valence $\rho(\Gamma)$ of a multigraph is defined as twice the number of edges divided by the number of vertices. This quantity, motivated by the Handshaking Lemma $\nu_1(\Gamma) = \frac{1}{2} \sum \rho(x)$, gives the mean number of edge-incidences per vertex. It is a basic density parameter for multigraphs.
