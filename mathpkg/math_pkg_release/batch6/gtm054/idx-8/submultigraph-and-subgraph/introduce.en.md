Given a system $\Lambda$, a subsystem $\Gamma$ that is itself a multigraph is called a submultigraph of $\Lambda$; if it is a graph, it is called a subgraph. These notions allow us to identify paths and circuits as submultigraphs of the original multigraph, and to reason about containment of one path or circuit within another.
