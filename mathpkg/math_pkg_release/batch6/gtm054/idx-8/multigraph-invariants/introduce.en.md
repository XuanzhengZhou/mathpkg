For a multigraph $\Gamma = (V, f, E)$, three fundamental numerical invariants are introduced: $\nu_{-1}(\Gamma)$ counts the number of connected components, $\nu_0(\Gamma)$ counts the number of vertices, and $\nu_1(\Gamma)$ counts the number of edges. These invariants serve as basic parameters throughout the combinatorial and algebraic study of multigraphs.
