$\Delta_k$ denotes the graph consisting of a single elementary circuit (or cycle) of length $k$. This is the standard $k$-cycle graph. Every circuit of length $k$ in a multigraph contains $\Delta_n$ as a subgraph for some $n \leq k$ (Exercise A5).
