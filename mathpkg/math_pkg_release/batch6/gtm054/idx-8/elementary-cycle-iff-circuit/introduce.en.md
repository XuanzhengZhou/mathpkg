Proposition A9 establishes the fundamental correspondence between elementary cycles in the algebraic cycle space $\mathcal{L}(\Gamma)$ and circuits in the multigraph. An elementary cycle (a set of edges with every vertex having even valence in the submultigraph) corresponds exactly to a circuit. The converse holds as a "partial converse": the edge set of a circuit is always an elementary cycle, but a general cycle may be the disjoint union of multiple circuits.
