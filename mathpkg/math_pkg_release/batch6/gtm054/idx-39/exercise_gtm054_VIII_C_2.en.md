(a) Show that $n(3, 3) = 6$.

(b) Show that the $5$-circuit is the only $0$-deficient $(3, 3)$-graph.

(c) Determine all $1$-deficient $(3, 3)$-graphs, and show that of these only the path of length $3$ has exactly three edges.
