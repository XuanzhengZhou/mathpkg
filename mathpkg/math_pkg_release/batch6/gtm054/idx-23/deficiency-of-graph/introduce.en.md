The deficiency of a graph $\Gamma$ is the maximum value of the subset deficiency $\delta(U)$ over all subsets $U$ of vertices. This quantity is always nonnegative because the empty set has deficiency zero. The graph deficiency generalizes the bipartite graph deficiency $\delta_1$ and plays a central role in the theory of 1-factors, analogous to the role of $\delta_1$ in the Main Matching Theorem for bipartite graphs.
