Combine Proposition B1 ($\delta = \delta_1 + \delta_2$) with Corollary A5. Corollary A5 states that for a bipartite graph, $\nu_0 = |V_1| + |V_2| - 2\alpha_1 + \delta_1 + \delta_2$. Substituting $\delta = \delta_1 + \delta_2$ from B1 and using the identity relating $|V_1| + |V_2|$ to the vertex covering number yields the formula $\nu_0 = 2\alpha_1 + \delta$.
