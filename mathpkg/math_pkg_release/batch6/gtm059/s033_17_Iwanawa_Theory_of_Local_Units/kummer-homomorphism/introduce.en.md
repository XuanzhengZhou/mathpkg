The Kummer homomorphism is a fundamental construction in Iwasawa theory of local units. It arises from a totally ramified extension $K_0/K$ and maps certain principal unit subgroups to the residue field. For each degree $k$ with $1 \leq k \leq q-2$, the map $\varphi_k$ is defined using the derivative of a power series representation of the unit. This homomorphism plays a role in studying the Galois module structure of local unit groups in cyclotomic extensions.
