Theorem 13.1 from Lang's *Cyclotomic Fields* gives a structural description of the principal unit modules $U^{(n)}$ in the Iwasawa theory of local units. It describes how these modules decompose with respect to the action of the Galois group and the Kummer homomorphism. The theorem provides the foundation for the Kummer-Takagi pairing and the subsequent analysis of local unit groups in cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions. Note: the OCR-recovered text for this theorem is heavily truncated; only the quantifier prefix has been recovered.
