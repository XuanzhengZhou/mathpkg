Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension and let $U_n$ be the principal local units at a prime above $p$ in the $n$-th layer. Then the projective limit $\mathcal{U} = \varprojlim U_n$ is a finitely generated $\Lambda$-module whose structure is governed by the $p$-adic logarithm map. If $K_{\infty}$ is the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, $\mathcal{U}$ is $\Lambda$-free of rank $[K:\mathbb{Q}_p]$.
