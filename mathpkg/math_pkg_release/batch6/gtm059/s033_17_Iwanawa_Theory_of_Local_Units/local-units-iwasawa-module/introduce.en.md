The projective limit of local units in a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension forms a $\Lambda$-module whose structure is well understood via the $p$-adic logarithm. For the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, this module is free over $\Lambda$ of rank equal to the local degree. The study of local units is essential because the global units embed into the local units, and the quotient controls the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension via Kummer theory.
