For an odd prime $p$, $R_{ij}$ denotes the least positive integer congruent to $ij$ modulo $p$. This notation is used in the construction of the Maillet determinant, which appears in the complex analytic class number formula for cyclotomic fields. The $(p-1)/2 \times (p-1)/2$ matrix $(R_{ij})$ and its variants $(R_{ij}^+)$, $(R_{ij}^-)$ — where indices run from $1$ to $(p-1)/2$ — encode residue data that connects to the relative class number $h^-$ of the $p$-th cyclotomic field.
