The notation $R_{ij}^+$ and $R_{ij}^-$ extends the least positive residue notation to signed residues modulo $p$. While $R_{ij}^+$ simply equals $R_{ij}$ (the least positive residue of $ij$ mod $p$), the $R_{ij}^-$ variant tracks the least positive residue of $-ij$, which plays a dual role. These two families of integers form the entries of two key matrices whose determinants are central to the Maillet determinant appearing in the complex analytic class number formula for $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_p)$.
