Let $A = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$. Given a matrix $R$ over $A$ with entries $a_{ij}$, define

$$
\deg^0(R) = \min_{i \ge j} \deg^0(a_{ij})
$$

where $\deg^0(a_{ij})$ denotes the Weierstrass degree of the element $a_{ij}$, ranging over all admissible transformations of $R$ which leave neither the component $i$ nor the component $j$ unchanged. For matrices in normal form, $\deg^0(R)$ equals the minimum degree of the distinguished polynomial entries appearing on the diagonal.
