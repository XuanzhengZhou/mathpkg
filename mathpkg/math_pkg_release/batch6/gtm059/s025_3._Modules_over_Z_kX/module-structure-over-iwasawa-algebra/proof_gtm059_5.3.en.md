Given generators $u_1, \ldots, u_m$ for $M$, one forms the relation matrix $R$. Admissible operations include: (O.1) replacing a row by itself minus a multiple of another row; (O.2) embedding $M$ in a larger module with finite cokernel by adjoining a new generator; (O.3) replacing by a surjection with finite kernel. Through successive application, the matrix can be brought to diagonal form with entries that are either $0$, powers of $p$, or powers of distinguished polynomials. This yields the pseudo-isomorphism decomposition.
