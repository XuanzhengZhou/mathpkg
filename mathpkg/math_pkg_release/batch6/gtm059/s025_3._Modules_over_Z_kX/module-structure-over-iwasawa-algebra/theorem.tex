Let $M$ be a finitely generated $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$-module. There exists a pseudo-isomorphism (homomorphism with finite kernel and cokernel)

$$M \sim \Lambda^r \oplus \bigoplus_i \Lambda/(p^{m_i}) \oplus \bigoplus_j \Lambda/(f_j^{n_j})$$

where $r$ is the $\Lambda$-rank, each $f_j$ is a distinguished irreducible polynomial, and $m_i, n_j$ are positive integers. The ideal generated by $\prod p^{m_i} \prod f_j^{n_j}$ is the characteristic ideal of the torsion submodule of $M$.
