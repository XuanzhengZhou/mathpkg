Let $F = \mathbb{F}_q$ be the finite field with $q$ elements, and let $E$ be a finite extension of $F$. Let
$$\operatorname{Tr}_{E/F}, \operatorname{N}_{E/F}$$
be the trace and norm from $E$ to $F$. Let $\chi$ be a multiplicative character of $F^*$ and $\lambda$ a non-trivial additive character of $F$. Define
$$\chi_E = \chi \circ \operatorname{N}_{E/F}, \qquad \lambda_E = \lambda \circ \operatorname{Tr}_{E/F}.$$
Then
$$-S_E(\chi_E, \lambda_E) = \bigl(-S_F(\chi, \lambda)\bigr)^{[E:F]},$$
where $S_F(\chi, \lambda) = -\sum_{x \in F^*} \chi(x)\lambda(x)$ denotes the Gauss sum over $F$.
