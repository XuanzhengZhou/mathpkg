In the study of local class field theory, the trace pairing provides a fundamental duality between a finite extension $$K_i$$ of a local field $$K$$ and itself. The pairing $$(x,y) \mapsto T_i(xy)$$ is $$K$$-bilinear and non-degenerate, making $$K_i$$ a self-dual vector space over $$K$$. This pairing is essential for defining the dual of an ideal and for establishing the orthogonality relations needed in the theory of the local symbol.
