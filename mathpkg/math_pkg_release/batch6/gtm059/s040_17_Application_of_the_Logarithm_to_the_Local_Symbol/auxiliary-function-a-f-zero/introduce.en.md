The auxiliary function $$A_f(0)$$ is defined as the reciprocal of $$\pi^2$$ times the logarithmic derivative. Together with the operator $$D_n$$, it forms the building block for the explicit reciprocity symbol $$[x, a_n]$$. Its definition involves a choice of uniformizer, but the eventual symbol will be independent of this choice under the well-definedness conditions.
