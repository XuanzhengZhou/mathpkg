Let $$d \in A_f(n)$$ and $$k \in K_n$$. Suppose that $$N_n = N_n$$ and $$a_n \in A_f(n)$$ for some norm condition. Under conditions (i) and (ii) ensuring well-definedness, the symbol $$[x, a_n]$$ takes values in $$\mathfrak{p}^{n+1}$$ and

$$\langle x, n \rangle = \left[ x, N_n \right]_{A_f(n)}.$$

Moreover, this holds for arbitrarily large $$n$$, and the value is the same for any $$m \geq 2n + 1$$.
