Statement DI 1 asserts that the logarithmic derivative map $$D_1$$ is a homomorphism from the multiplicative group $$K_1^{\times}$$ to the module of differentials. The image of the unit subgroup is shown to lie in a specific submodule $$\mu_1^n \varpi_1$$. This homomorphism property is fundamental for establishing the compatibility of the explicit reciprocity symbol with the group structure and for proving that the symbol is well-defined modulo the different.
