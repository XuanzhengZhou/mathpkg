In the context of explicit reciprocity laws for local fields, the subgroup $$N_1^*(\mathfrak{u})$$ consists of elements of the maximal abelian extension whose norm to the base field $$K$$ lies in the subgroup generated by $$\pi$$. The vector group $$T(K_2)$$ collects compatible sequences of elements whose images under the norm map form a coherent system. These definitions are essential for formulating the reciprocity law in the Lubin-Tate setting.
