Formula LS R expresses the action of the Lubin-Tate $$\psi$$-map in terms of the representation of the Galois group. This reciprocity formula relates the local symbol to the trace pairing through the Lubin-Tate logarithm, providing a crucial computational tool for evaluating local symbols in cyclotomic extensions. The formula uses the identification $$[ab] = [a][b]$$ and properties of the tangent space.
