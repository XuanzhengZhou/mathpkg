The explicit formula for the local norm residue symbol in terms of the $p$-adic logarithm is a cornerstone of explicit reciprocity laws. It provides a computationally accessible description of the Hilbert symbol, which in turn controls the Kummer pairing and the structure of Galois groups. In the context of Iwasawa theory, this formula is used to relate the $p$-adic $L$-function to the characteristic polynomial of the Iwasawa module, proving special cases of the main conjecture.
