Let $$K_i$$ be a finite extension of the local field $$K$$ with ring of integers $$\mathcal{O}$$ and trace pairing $$(x,y) \mapsto T_i(xy)$$. For an ideal $$\mathfrak{a}$$ in $$K_i$$, its dual is

$$\mathfrak{a}^* = \{ y \in K_i : T_i(xy) \in \mathcal{O} \text{ for all } x \in \mathfrak{a} \}.$$

If $$\mathfrak{B}_0 = \mathfrak{D}_{K_i/K}$$ denotes the different, then

$$\mathfrak{a}^* = \mathfrak{a}^{-1} \mathfrak{B}_0^{-1}.$$

Moreover, $$\mathfrak{a}^* \cong \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}}(\mathfrak{a}, \mathcal{O})$$ as $$\mathcal{O}$$-modules.
