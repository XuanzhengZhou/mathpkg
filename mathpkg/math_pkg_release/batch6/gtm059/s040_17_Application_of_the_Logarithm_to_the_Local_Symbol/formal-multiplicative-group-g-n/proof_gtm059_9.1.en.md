This is a special case of Corollary 1, obtained by specializing to the formal multiplicative group $$\mathbb{G}_m$$. When $$A = \mathbb{G}_m$$, the Lubin-Tate logarithm is the ordinary $$p$$-adic logarithm $$\log(1+X)$$. The power series description of elements follows by applying the corollary's congruence formula and expressing elements of the formal group as power series in the given parameters.
