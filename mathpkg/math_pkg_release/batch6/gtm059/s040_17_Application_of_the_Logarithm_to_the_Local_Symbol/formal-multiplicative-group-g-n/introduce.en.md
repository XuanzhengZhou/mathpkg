As a special case of the corollaries to the reciprocity law, when the Lubin-Tate formal group is the formal multiplicative group $$\mathbb{G}_m$$, its elements admit an explicit power series description in terms of a primitive root of unity and a parameter $$m \equiv 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}}$$. This description follows from the logarithm congruence formula and provides an explicit parametrization of the formal group arising in cyclotomic theory.
