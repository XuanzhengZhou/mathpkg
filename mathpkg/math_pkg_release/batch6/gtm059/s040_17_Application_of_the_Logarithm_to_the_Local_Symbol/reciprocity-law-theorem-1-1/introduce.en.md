Theorem 1.1 states the fundamental explicit reciprocity law relating the norm residue symbol to the Lubin-Tate formal group logarithm. Under the condition that $$u \equiv 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}}$$ and $$s$$ lies in the ring of integers, the symbol $$\langle \mathfrak{u}_i, \mathfrak{u}_j \rangle$$ is expressed in terms of the norm map. This generalizes classical reciprocity laws of Artin-Hasse type and is proved using the formalism of the norm residue symbol and Lubin-Tate theory.
