The proof uses the Kummer pairing described in the previous theorem. By passing to the projective limit, the pairing between Galois groups and unit groups becomes a perfect duality of $\Lambda$-modules. The structure of the local unit projective limits is understood via the logarithm map and the $p$-adic exponential, while the global units are studied via the regulator. The cokernel of the global-to-local map is measured by the $p$-adic regulator, whose non-vanishing is exactly Leopoldt's conjecture.
