Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. Let $G = \varprojlim G_n$ where $G_n = \text{Gal}(L_n/K_n)$ is the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian extension of $K_n$, and let $A = \varinjlim A_n$ where $A_n$ is the $p$-primary subgroup of the ideal class group of $K_n$.

The Kummer pairing
$$G \times A \longrightarrow \mu_{p^{\infty}}$$
gives a compact-discrete duality between the projective limit $G$ and the direct limit $A$.

In particular, for $g \in G$ and $a \in A$,
$$\langle g^{p^n}, a \rangle = \langle g, p^n a \rangle.$$
