# Projective System of Sets

A projective (or inverse) system of finite sets is the basic structure on which distributions in the sense of ordinary and Bernoulli distributions are defined. The connecting maps allow one to pass from a finer level $X_n$ to a coarser level $X_m$. The projective limit $X = \varprojlim X_n$ is a compact totally disconnected space under the natural product topology. In the context of cyclotomic fields, the relevant projective systems are $\{\frac{1}{M}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}\}$ and $\{\mathbb{Z}/M\mathbb{Z}\}$ as $M$ ranges over positive integers ordered by divisibility.
