# Bounded Distribution (Bernoulli Distribution)

A bounded distribution, also called a Bernoulli distribution in this context, is a distribution whose values are uniformly bounded in the non-Archimedean norm. The boundedness condition is essential because it guarantees that the integral with respect to the distribution can be extended from locally constant functions to all continuous functions on the projective limit $X$. The distributions arising from Bernoulli polynomials are bounded, making the integration theory applicable to them and connecting them to $p$-adic measures and $p$-adic $L$-functions.
