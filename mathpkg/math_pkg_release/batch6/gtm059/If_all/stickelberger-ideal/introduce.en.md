# Stickelberger Ideal

The Stickelberger ideal is a fundamental algebraic object in the study of cyclotomic fields. It arises from the intersection of an ideal generated by Stickelberger elements with the integral group ring of the Galois group. Understanding the index of this ideal within the group ring is a central problem that leads to deep connections with $L$-functions, class numbers, and Iwasawa theory. The Stickelberger ideal encodes crucial arithmetic information about the cyclotomic field and its subfields.
