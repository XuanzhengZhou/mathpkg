Let $m = p^n$ be a prime power, and let $R$ be the group ring. The \textbf{Stickelberger ideal} $\mathcal{P}$ is defined as
$$\mathcal{P} = I \cap \mathbb{Z}[G]$$
where $I$ is the ideal generated by the Stickelberger elements, and $\mathbb{Z}[G]$ is the integral group ring of the Galois group $G$ of the cyclotomic field.
