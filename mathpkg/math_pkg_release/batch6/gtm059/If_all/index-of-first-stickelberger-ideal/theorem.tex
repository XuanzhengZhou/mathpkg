Let $m = p^n$ be a prime power, let $G$ be the Galois group of the $m$-th cyclotomic field, and let $\mathcal{P}$ be the Stickelberger ideal in $\mathbb{Z}[G]$. Then the index of $\mathcal{P}$ in the group ring $R = \mathbb{Z}[G]$ is given by
$$\bigl(R^* : \mathcal{P}\bigr) = \prod_{i=1}^{n} R_{i,i}$$
where $R_{i,i}$ are certain determinants derived from the Stickelberger elements.
