# Integral with Respect to a Distribution

Every distribution on a projective system defines a linear functional --- the integral --- on the space of locally constant functions on the projective limit. When the distribution is bounded, the integral extends by continuity to all continuous functions. This integration theory generalizes the Riemann-Stieltjes integral and provides the framework for expressing generalized Bernoulli numbers as integrals: $B_{k,f} = \int f \, d\varphi$ where $\varphi$ is the Bernoulli distribution. This connection is crucial for the construction of $p$-adic $L$-functions.
