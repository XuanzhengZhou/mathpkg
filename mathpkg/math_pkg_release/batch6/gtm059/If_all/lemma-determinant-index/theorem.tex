Let $r$ and $r'$ be elements of the group ring $\mathbb{Z}[G]$, and let $m = p^n$ be a prime power. Then the product of the indices satisfies
$$(r^* r) \cdot (r' r) = m^n \prod_{i=1}^{n} R_{i,i}$$
where $R_{i,i}$ are the determinants appearing in the computation of the Stickelberger ideal index. The sign of the right-hand side is determined by the absolute value of the determinant, and in Chapter 3 it is shown that this sign equals $(-1)^{n-1}$ times the order of the minus part of the ideal class group.
