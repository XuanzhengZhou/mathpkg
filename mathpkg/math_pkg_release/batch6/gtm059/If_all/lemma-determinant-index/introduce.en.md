# Lemma on the Determinant and Index Product

This lemma provides an algebraic formula expressing the product of certain index factors in terms of determinants $R_{i,i}$. The determinant of the map $r^* m^n$ acting on $R$ decomposes into a product corresponding to the various eigenspaces under the action of the Galois group, yielding the product $\prod R_{i,i}$. The absolute value of this determinant gives the index, while the sign is later identified with the parity of the order of a certain ideal class group.
