This theorem establishes an equality between certain invariants arising from the structure theorem for finitely generated modules over the Iwasawa algebra. By combining the decomposition $G = A^r \times G_{\text{tor}}$ with Theorem 5.2 on the Galois group of a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, one deduces that the torsion-component count $t$ coincides with the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-rank $r_B$ of the base field extension. This is a sample of Iwasawa's results on the module structure of Galois groups in cyclotomic towers.
