Let $X = \operatorname{Gal}(L_{\infty}/K_{\infty})$ be the Galois group of the maximal unramified $p$-abelian extension over the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension $K_{\infty}/K$. Then $X$ is pseudo-isomorphic to a $\Lambda$-module of the form

$$\bigoplus_{i} \Lambda/(p^{m_i}) \oplus \bigoplus_{j} \Lambda/(f_j^{n_j})$$

where each $f_j$ is a distinguished polynomial. The characteristic ideal of $X$ is generated by $\prod p^{m_i} \prod f_j^{n_j}$.
