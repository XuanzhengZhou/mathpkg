In the study of Kummer theory over the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension of a number field, certain Galois extensions are distinguished by the property that their Galois group carries a bilinear structure. This definition isolates the class of extensions whose Galois groups are projective limits of finite abelian $p$-groups, arising from bilinear pairings between the Galois group and units in the base field. Such extensions play a key role in describing the maximal Kummer extension as a quotient of a free module over the Iwasawa algebra.
