This technical lemma describes the intersection of the closure of the global unit group with the subgroup of ideles that are units away from $p$. It is a key computational tool for relating the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension to the $p$-adic completion of the unit group. The result underlies the idelic description of Iwasawa modules.
