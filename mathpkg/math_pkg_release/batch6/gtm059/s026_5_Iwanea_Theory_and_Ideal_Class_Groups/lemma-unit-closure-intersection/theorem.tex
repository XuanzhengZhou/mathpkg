Let $K$ be a number field, and let $p$ be a fixed prime. For each prime $\mathfrak{p}$ of $K$ dividing $p$, let $U_\mathfrak{p}$ be the group of local principal units at $\mathfrak{p}$. Let $U_p = \prod_{\mathfrak{p} \mid p} U_\mathfrak{p}$ and let $U_p^\perp = \prod_{\mathfrak{q} \nmid p} U_\mathfrak{q}$ where $U_\mathfrak{q}$ are the local units at primes not dividing $p$. Let $E$ be the group of global units of $K$, embedded diagonally into the ideles. Then

$$\overline{U_p E} \cap U_p^\perp K^* = \overline{U_p^\perp K^*} \cap \overline{U_p E},$$

where the closure is taken in the idele topology. Equivalently, in the quotient by $K^*$,

$$\overline{U_p E} \cap \overline{U_p^\perp K^*} \;/\; \overline{K^*} = \{1\}.$$
