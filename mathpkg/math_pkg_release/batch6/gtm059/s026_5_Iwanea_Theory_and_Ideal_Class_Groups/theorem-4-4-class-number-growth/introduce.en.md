Theorem 4.4 is Iwasawa's celebrated asymptotic formula for the growth of the $p$-part of the class number in a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. It states that $v_p(h_n) = \mu p^n + \lambda n + \nu$ for large $n$. The invariants $\mu$, $\lambda$, $\nu$ are fundamental in Iwasawa theory and can be computed from the structure of the Iwasawa module as a $\Lambda$-module. The $\mu$-invariant is particularly significant: $\mu = 0$ is equivalent to the Iwasawa module being finitely generated over $\mathbb{Z}_p$.
