Theorem 5.1 gives an explicit idelic description of the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension of a number field. It states that $G_M(K)$ is quasi-isomorphic to the $p$-adic completion of the global unit group modulo the closure of units. This quasi-isomorphism is fundamental for relating the Iwasawa module $C = \varprojlim C_n$ to $p$-adic $L$-functions via the Main Conjecture of Iwasawa theory.
