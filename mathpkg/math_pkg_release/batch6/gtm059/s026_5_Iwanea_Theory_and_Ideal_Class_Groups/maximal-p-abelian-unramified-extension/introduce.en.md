The maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension $M(K)$ is the compositum of all abelian extensions of $K$ of $p$-power degree that are everywhere unramified. Its Galois group $G_M(K)$ is the $p$-completion of the idele class group of $K$ and is a fundamental object in Iwasawa theory. The degree $[M(K) : K]$ is equal to the $p$-part of the class number of $K$ times a product of local unit indices.
