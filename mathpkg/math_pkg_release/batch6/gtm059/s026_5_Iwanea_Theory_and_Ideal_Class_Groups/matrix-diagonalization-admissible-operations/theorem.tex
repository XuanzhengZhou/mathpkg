Let $R$ be a matrix of relations for a finitely generated $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$-module. By a finite sequence of admissible operations (row and column operations, embeddings into modules with finite cokernel, and surjections with finite kernel), $R$ can be transformed into a diagonal matrix whose diagonal entries are either $0$, powers of $p$, or powers of distinguished polynomials.
