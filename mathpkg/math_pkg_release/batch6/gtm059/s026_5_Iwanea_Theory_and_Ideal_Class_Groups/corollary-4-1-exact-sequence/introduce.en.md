This corollary extracts the exact sequence description from Theorem 4.1. It states that when a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension has exactly one ramified prime, the Galois group $G$ of the maximal unramified $p$-extension is an extension of $\mathbb{Z}_p$ by the pro-$p$ group $G_1$, and this extension splits. The descending filtration $G \supset G_1 \supset G_2 \supset \cdots$ reflects the tower structure of the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension.
