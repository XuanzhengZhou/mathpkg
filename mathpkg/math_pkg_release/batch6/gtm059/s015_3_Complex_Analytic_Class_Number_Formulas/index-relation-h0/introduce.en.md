Theorem 5.3 expresses the class number $h_0$ of the maximal real subfield as the index $(E : C)$ of the cyclotomic unit group $C$ in the full unit group $E$. This index relation is a key step in the complex analytic derivation of the class number formula, as it connects the purely algebraic notion of units to the arithmetic invariant $h_0$. The proof uses norms of cyclotomic units from the full cyclotomic field down to the real subfield.
