This corollary of Theorem 4.4 asserts that the quotient $q_{G_2}$ — derived from the exact sequence of class groups — is an integer. It also identifies $K$ as the order of the group $G_2$, linking the algebraic structure of the Galois group to the arithmetic invariants of the cyclotomic field.
