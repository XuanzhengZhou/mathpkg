This proposition states that the class number $h_0$ of the maximal real subfield divides the class number $h_1$ of the full cyclotomic field. The result follows from class field theory: since $K^*$ is totally ramified over $F$ at the prime $p$, the Hilbert class field $H_0$ of $F$ satisfies $H_0 \cap K^* = F$, which forces the divisibility relation.
