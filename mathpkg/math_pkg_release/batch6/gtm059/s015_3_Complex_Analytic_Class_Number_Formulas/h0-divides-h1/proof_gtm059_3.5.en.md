Let $H_0$ be the Hilbert class field of the maximal real subfield $F$. Then $H_0 \cap K^* = F$, where $K^*$ is the full cyclotomic field. Consequently,
$$[H_0 K^* : K^*] = [H_0 : F].$$
Since $H_0 K^*$ is an unramified extension of $K^*$, it follows from class field theory that $h_0$ divides $h_1$.
