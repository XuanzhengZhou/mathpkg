Theorem 4.4 establishes an exact sequence relating the class groups $C_1$, $C_2$, and $C_3$ associated with the cyclotomic field $K = \mathbb{Q}_{G_2}$. The exactness of the sequence $1 \to C_1 \to C_2 \to C_3 \to 1$ encodes fundamental structural relations among these groups. This result plays a key role in the complex analytic derivation of class number formulas for cyclotomic fields.
