We consider the norm map followed by the injection,
$$C_2 \rightarrow C_3 \rightarrow \tfrac{1}{2} C_1.$$
The kernel of this composite map is $C_2$ by definition. The theorem follows since the order of the group $G_2$ forces exactness at each term of the sequence.
