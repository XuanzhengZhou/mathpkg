We consider $N = N^e$. We can write an element of $I$ in the form
$$\sum_{i=1}^{k-1} \binom{k}{i} \binom{k}{i-1} p^{i-1} = p \text{ integral}.$$
The first term is $i = l$, proving the stated relations.
