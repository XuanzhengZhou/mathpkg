Theorem 2.2 in Lang's Cyclotomic Fields states a factorization of the Gauss sum ideal in terms of powers of the prime ideal. The theorem expresses the Gauss sum attached to a character as an explicit ideal power, connecting the analytic Gauss sums to the algebraic ideal structure in cyclotomic fields.
