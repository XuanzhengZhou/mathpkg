The group $2r : 1$ is generated by elements of the form
$$(v_r, -v_r) = v_r(-v_r).$$

An element $\xi \in L$ lies in $R[x]$, so $\xi$ is not an argument as in the case given in the lemma. The Stickelberger elements should really be indexed by the groups to which they correspond.
