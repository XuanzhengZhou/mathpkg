Write $\zeta = \sum_{i=0}^{n} a_i B_k(X)$, with integral coefficients $a_i$. Then
$$S^n = N^n - \sum_{i=0}^{n} a_i B_k(X) \frac{1}{k} \left[ R_X \right]_{i=0}^{n-1} r_i^n;$$
and therefore
$$\frac{N^n}{k} \sum_{i=0}^{n} a_i B_k(X) \left[ R_X \right]_{i=0}^{n-1} \text{ is integral}.$$

But an elementary formula for Bernoulli polynomials, obtained directly from the definition, gives for an integer $b$:
$$\frac{N^{n-1}}{k} B_k\left(\frac{b}{k}\right) = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \cdots$$

This establishes the claimed equalities.
