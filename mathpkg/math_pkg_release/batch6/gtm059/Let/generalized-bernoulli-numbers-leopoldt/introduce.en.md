The generalized Bernoulli numbers $B_{k,\chi}$ (Leopoldt) are defined by summing the $k$-th Bernoulli polynomial evaluated at fractional parts, weighted by a Dirichlet character $\chi$. They play a central role in the $p$-adic $L$-function and Stickelberger theory.
