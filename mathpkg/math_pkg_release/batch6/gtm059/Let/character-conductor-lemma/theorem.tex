Let $g$ be a character of $G(N)$ with conductor $m$.

(i) If every prime dividing $N$ also divides $m$, then $S_{up} S_{up} u_p M$ is determined directly.

(ii) Let $p$ be a prime dividing $N$ but not dividing $m$. Write $N = p^e M$ with $p \nmid M$. Then
$$S_{up} S_{up} u_p M = (1 - g) p^e S_{up} S_{up} u_p M.$$
