This theorem establishes the $p$-adic integrality properties of the regularized Bernoulli distribution $E_{k,c}$. The regularization process, which subtracts a twist by $c^k$, removes the denominators that appear in the raw Bernoulli distribution. This integrality result is essential for constructing the Stickelberger ideal as an ideal in $\mathbb{Z}_p[G]$ rather than merely in $\mathbb{Q}_p[G]$, enabling the connection to ideal class groups.
