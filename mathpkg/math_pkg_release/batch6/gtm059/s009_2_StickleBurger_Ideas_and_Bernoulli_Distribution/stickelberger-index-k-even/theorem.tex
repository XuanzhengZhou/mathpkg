Let $k$ be an even positive integer and let $S_k$ be the $k$-th Stickelberger ideal
(generated by elements involving $B_k$). Then the index of $S_k$ in the group ring
$R = \mathbb{Z}[G]$ is given by:

$$(R : S_k) = N^{\varphi(N)/2} \prod_{\chi \text{ even}} \frac{1}{k} B_{k,\chi},$$

where the product is taken over all even Dirichlet characters $\chi$ of $G$, and
$B_{k,\chi}$ are the Leopoldt generalized Bernoulli numbers. Here $\varphi$ is
Euler's totient function.
