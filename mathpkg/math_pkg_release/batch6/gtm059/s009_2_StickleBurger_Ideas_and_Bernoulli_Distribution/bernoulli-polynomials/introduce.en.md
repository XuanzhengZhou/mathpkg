Bernoulli polynomials generalize Bernoulli numbers to polynomial functions. They satisfy the fundamental distribution relation $B_k(X) = N^{k-1} \sum_{a=0}^{N-1} B_k(\frac{X+a}{N})$, which is the key property that allows the construction of Bernoulli distributions on projective systems $\frac{1}{N}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}$. Their leading term structure and integrality properties are essential in the study of Stickelberger ideals.
