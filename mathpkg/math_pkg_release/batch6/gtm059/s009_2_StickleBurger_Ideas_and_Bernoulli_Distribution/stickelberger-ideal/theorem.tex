Let $G = \mathrm{Gal}(\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_N)/\mathbb{Q}) \cong (\mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z})^\times$.
For $a \in (\mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z})^\times$, let $\sigma_a \in G$ be the automorphism
$\zeta_N \mapsto \zeta_N^a$. The \textbf{Stickelberger element} is

$$\theta = \sum_{\substack{a=1 \\ (a,N)=1}}^N \left\langle \frac{a}{N} \right\rangle \sigma_a^{-1} \in \mathbb{Q}[G],$$

where $\langle x \rangle$ denotes the fractional part of $x \in \mathbb{Q}$.
The \textbf{Stickelberger ideal} $I$ is defined as

$$I = \mathbb{Z}[G] \cap \theta \mathbb{Z}[G],$$

i.e., the set of elements $\alpha \in \mathbb{Z}[G]$ such that $\alpha \theta \in \mathbb{Z}[G]$.
Equivalently, $I$ is the ideal of $\mathbb{Z}[G]$ generated by the elements
$\theta \sigma_a - \sigma_a \theta$ for $a \in (\mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z})^\times$.
