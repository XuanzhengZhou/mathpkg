For a positive integer $k$, the \textbf{Bernoulli distribution of order $k$} on the
projective system $\{\mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z}\}$ is defined by:

$$E_k^{(N)}(x) = N^{k-1} B_k\!\left(\left\langle \frac{x}{N} \right\rangle\right), \quad x \in \mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z},$$

where $\langle \cdot \rangle$ denotes the fractional part and $B_k$ is the $k$-th
Bernoulli polynomial. This defines a distribution with values in $\mathbb{Q}$, and
after suitable normalization, yields a $p$-adic distribution.
