This theorem is the counterpart of Theorem 5.5 for the odd case. When the Bernoulli distribution is twisted by the sign character, the relevant Stickelberger ideal involves odd characters, and its index is expressed as a product of generalized Bernoulli numbers $B_{k,\chi}$ for odd $\chi$. Together with the even case, this covers all characters of the Galois group, providing a complete description of the Stickelberger filtration.
