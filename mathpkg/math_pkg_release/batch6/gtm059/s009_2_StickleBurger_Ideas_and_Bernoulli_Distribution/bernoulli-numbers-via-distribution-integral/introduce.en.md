This theorem expresses classical Bernoulli numbers $B_k$ as integrals of the power function $x^{k-1}$ against the regularized Bernoulli distribution. The formula provides a bridge between the classical arithmetic of Bernoulli numbers and the $p$-adic distribution theory. By choosing $c$ to be a primitive root modulo $p$, one can derive the classical congruences of Kummer and von Staudt from the $p$-adic integral representation.
