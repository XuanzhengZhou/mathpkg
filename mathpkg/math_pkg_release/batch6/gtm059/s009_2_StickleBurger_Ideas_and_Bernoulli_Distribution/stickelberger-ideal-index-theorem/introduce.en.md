This theorem computes the index of the Stickelberger ideal $S$ in the group ring $R = \mathbb{Z}[G]$, for the case where the conductor $m$ is a prime power. The index is expressed as a product of first generalized Bernoulli numbers $B_{1,\chi}$ over all non-trivial even characters $\chi$. This result connects the Stickelberger ideal to the arithmetic of cyclotomic fields via Bernoulli numbers, and is crucial for understanding the structure of the ideal class group.
