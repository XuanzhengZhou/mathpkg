This fundamental distribution relation for Bernoulli polynomials is the crucial property that makes them into a distribution on the projective system $\{\frac{1}{N}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}\}$. The relation follows from manipulating the generating series and shows that the association $x \mapsto N^{k-1} B_k(\langle x \rangle)$ for $x \in \frac{1}{N}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}$ defines a distribution. This construction is the prototype for all Bernoulli-type distributions used in the theory of Stickelberger ideals.
