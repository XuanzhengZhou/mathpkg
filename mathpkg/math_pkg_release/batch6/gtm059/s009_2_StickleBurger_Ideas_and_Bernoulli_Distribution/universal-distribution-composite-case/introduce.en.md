This theorem extends the universal distribution construction from prime level to composite level $N$. The primitive elements are now those that are primitive for some prime power component in the partial fraction decomposition. The universal distribution provides the maximal rank for the space of distributions on the projective system of level $N$, generalizing the prime case and providing the theoretical framework for understanding the rank of Bernoulli-type distributions.
