The proof reduces to the prime power case using the Chinese Remainder Theorem decomposition. For each prime power component $p_i^{e_i}$, Theorem 9.1 provides the universal distribution. The distribution relation for composite $N$ decomposes according to the partial fraction decomposition, and the relations among the components are given by the compatibility conditions at overlapping levels.

A key lemma in the proof analyzes the sum $S_{N/p}(g)$ for a character with conductor dividing $N/p$ but not $N$, showing that the distribution relation factors appropriately. The primitivity condition ensures that the generators at different prime power components are independent modulo the distribution relations.

The proof concludes by verifying that the constructed group with the given generators and relations satisfies the universal property for distributions on the full projective system of level $N$.
