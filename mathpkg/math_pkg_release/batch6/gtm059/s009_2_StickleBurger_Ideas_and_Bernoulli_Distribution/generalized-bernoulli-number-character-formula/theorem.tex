Let $\psi$ be a character of finite order on $\mathbb{Z}_p^\times$ (a Dirichlet character
of $p$-power conductor). Then for any integer $k \geq 1$,

$$\frac{1}{k} B_{k,\psi} = \frac{1}{1 - \psi(c) c^k} \int_{\mathbb{Z}_p^\times} \psi(x) x^{k-1} \, dE_{1,c}(x),$$

where $c$ is an integer prime to $p$ with $\psi(c) c^k \neq 1$, and
$dE_{1,c}(x) = dE_1(x) - c^k dE_1(c^{-1}x)$ is the regularized (imprimitive)
Bernoulli distribution.
