This theorem expresses generalized Bernoulli numbers $B_{k,\psi}$ associated to a $p$-adic character $\psi$ as an integral of $\psi(x) x^{k-1}$ against the regularized Bernoulli distribution. The factor $1/(1 - \psi(c)c^k)$ removes the Euler factor at $p$ from the imprimitive distribution to recover the primitive Bernoulli number. This formula is the basis for $p$-adic interpolation of $L$-functions and for understanding the action of the Stickelberger element on cyclotomic units.
