The integral Stickelberger ideal refines the Stickelberger ideal to an integral setting, defined by congruence conditions on the coefficients of group ring elements. Together with the augmentation ideal and the ideal generated by $\sigma_c - c$, these ideals form a lattice of $\mathbb{Z}$-submodules of the group ring $R = \mathbb{Z}[G]$. Understanding the relations and indices among these ideals is crucial for computing the index of the Stickelberger ideal.
