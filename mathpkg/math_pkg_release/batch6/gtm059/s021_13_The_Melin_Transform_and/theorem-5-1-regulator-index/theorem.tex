Let $K$ be the real subfield of $\mathbb{Q}(\mu_n)$, let $\mathcal{E}$ be the group of units of $K$, and let $\mathcal{C}$ be the group of real cyclotomic units of $K$. Denote by $R_p(\mathcal{E})$ the $p$-adic regulator of a basis of $\mathcal{E}$ modulo roots of unity, and by $R_p(\mathcal{C})$ the $p$-adic regulator of a basis of $\mathcal{C}$ modulo $\pm 1$. Then
$$
R_p(\mathcal{E}) = (\mathcal{E} : \mathcal{C}) \cdot R_p(\mathcal{C}),
$$
where $(\mathcal{E} : \mathcal{C})$ is the index of the cyclotomic units in the full unit group.

Moreover, from Theorem 5.1 of the preceding chapter,
$$
h^+ = (\mathcal{E} : \mathcal{C}),
$$
where $h^+$ is the class number of the real subfield $K$.
