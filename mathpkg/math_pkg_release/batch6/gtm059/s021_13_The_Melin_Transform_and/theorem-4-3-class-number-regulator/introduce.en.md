Theorem 4.3 is the $p$-adic class number-regulator formula, a central result in Iwasawa theory. It relates the class number $h^+$ of the real cyclotomic subfield $\mathbb{Q}(\mu_{p^n})^+$ to the $p$-adic regulator $R_p(K)$ and a product of $p$-adic $L$-function values $L_p(1,\chi)$. The formula is the $p$-adic analogue of the classical analytic class number formula of Dirichlet, with the ordinary logarithm replaced by the $p$-adic logarithm and the classical $L$-function replaced by the $p$-adic $L$-function. The factor $\frac{1}{2}$ in the product arises from the structure of the Galois group.
