For the real cyclic field $K = \mathbb{Q}(\mu_p)^+$, the $p$-adic regulator can be expressed as a product over the non-trivial characters of the Galois group:
$$
R_p(K) = \prod_{\chi \neq 1} L_p(1, \chi),
$$
where the product is taken over all non-trivial Dirichlet characters $\chi$ of $\operatorname{Gal}(K/\mathbb{Q})$ and $L_p(1,\chi)$ is the value of the $p$-adic $L$-function at $s=1$.

Alternatively, if $\eta_1, \dots, \eta_r$ generate the cyclotomic units modulo $\pm 1$, then
$$
R_p(\eta_1, \dots, \eta_r) = \det\!\bigl( \log_p \eta_i^{\sigma_j} \bigr) = \prod_{\chi \neq 1} \sum_{a=1}^{N} \chi^{-1}(a) \log_p(1 - \zeta_N^a),
$$
where $\sigma_j$ range over the embeddings of $K$ into $C_p$.
