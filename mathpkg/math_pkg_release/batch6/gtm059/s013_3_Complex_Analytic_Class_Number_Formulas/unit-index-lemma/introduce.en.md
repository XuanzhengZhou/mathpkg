This lemma provides an explicit formula for the unit index $Q = (E : W \cdot E^+)$ in terms of the class numbers and regulators of $K$ and its maximal real subfield $K^+$. The proof follows directly from comparing the analytic class number formula for $K$ (imaginary case) with that of $K^+$ (real case), noting that the odd characters contribute to the relative factor $h^- = h/h^+$. The lemma is essential for relating the class number of a cyclotomic field to that of its real subfield.
