Let $\Gamma$ be a topological group isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}_p$, the additive group of $p$-adic integers. Let $\gamma$ be a fixed topological generator of $\Gamma$. For each integer $n \geq 1$, define the polynomial

$$h_n = h_n(X) = (1 + X)^{p^n} - 1 \in \mathbb{Z}_p[X].$$

The \textbf{Iwasawa algebra} $\Lambda$ is the projective limit

$$\Lambda = \varprojlim_n \mathbb{Z}_p[\Gamma/\Gamma^{p^n}] = \varprojlim_n \mathbb{Z}_p[X]/(h_n),$$

where the transition maps are the natural projections. Given the choice of topological generator $\gamma$, the Iwasawa algebra is canonically identified with the power series ring $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ via Theorem~1.1, and one writes $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$.
