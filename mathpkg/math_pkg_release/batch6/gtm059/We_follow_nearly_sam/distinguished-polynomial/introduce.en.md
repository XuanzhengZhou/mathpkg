A distinguished polynomial over $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is a monic polynomial whose non-leading coefficients are all divisible by $p$. Distinguished polynomials are the polynomial analogues of power series units in $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$, and they play a central role in the Weierstrass Preparation Theorem. In Iwasawa theory, the elementary torsion modules $\Lambda/(f)$ for distinguished irreducible $f$ form one of the basic building blocks in the structure theorem for finitely generated $\Lambda$-modules.
