Let $\gamma$ be a fixed topological generator of $\Gamma \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$. For each $n \geq 1$, set $h_n = (1 + X)^{p^n} - 1 \in \mathbb{Z}_p[X]$. The natural homomorphism

$$e: \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]] \to \varprojlim_n \mathbb{Z}_p[X]/(h_n)$$

induced by the projections $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]] \to \mathbb{Z}_p[X]/(h_n)$ is an isomorphism of $\mathbb{Z}_p$-algebras.
