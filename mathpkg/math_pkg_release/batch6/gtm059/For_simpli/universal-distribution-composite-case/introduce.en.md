Theorem 9.2 generalizes the structure theorem for universal distributions from prime level to arbitrary composite level $N$. The key technical innovation is the use of partial fraction decomposition to parametrize elements of $\frac{1}{N}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}$. The free generators of $U(N)$ correspond to those elements whose partial fraction components are either prime to the corresponding prime power or zero. The proof proceeds by induction on the number of prime factors, reducing the composite case to the prime case via the distribution relation.
