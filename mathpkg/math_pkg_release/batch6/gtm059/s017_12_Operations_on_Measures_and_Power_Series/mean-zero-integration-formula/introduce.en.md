The Mean Zero formula states that the total mass of a $p$-adic measure $\mu$ on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ equals the value at $X = 0$ of the associated power series $f(X) = \int (1+X)^x \, d\mu(x)$. This is a special case of Theorem 1.2 with $z = 0$ and serves as the first in a list of correspondences between operations on measures and operations on power series.
