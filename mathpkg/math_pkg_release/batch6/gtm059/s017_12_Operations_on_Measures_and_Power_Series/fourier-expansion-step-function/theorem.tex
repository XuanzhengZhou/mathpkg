Let $\varphi$ be a step function on $\mathbb{Z}_p$, constant on cosets of $p^n \mathbb{Z}_p$. Then $\varphi$ admits a Fourier expansion
$$
\varphi(x) = \sum_{\zeta} \widehat{\varphi}(\zeta) \, \zeta^x,
$$
where $\zeta$ runs over the $p^n$-th roots of unity and
$$
\widehat{\varphi}(\zeta) = \frac{1}{p^n} \sum_{x \bmod p^n} \varphi(x) \, \zeta^{-x}
$$
are the Fourier coefficients.
