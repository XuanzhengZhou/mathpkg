A sup norm is defined on the space of $p$-adic measures on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ and on the space of associated power series. For a measure $\mu$, the norm $\|\mu\|$ is the supremum of $|\mu(x)|$ over all $x \in \mathbb{Z}_p$. For the associated power series $f(X) = \int (1+X)^x \, d\mu(x)$, the norm $\|f\|$ is defined analogously as the supremum of $|f(x)|$. These norms are fundamental for establishing the correspondence between measures and power series in Iwasawa theory.
