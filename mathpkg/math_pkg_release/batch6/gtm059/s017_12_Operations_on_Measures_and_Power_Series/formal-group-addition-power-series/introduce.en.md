The formal group addition on power series is defined by $X [+] Y = (1+X)(1+Y) - 1 = X + Y + XY$. This operation arises naturally from the multiplicative formal group and is used to express how operations on measures translate to operations on their associated power series. In the context of $p$-adic measures, the operator $X [+] x$ (with $x$ a constant) describes the effect of translation on the underlying measure.
