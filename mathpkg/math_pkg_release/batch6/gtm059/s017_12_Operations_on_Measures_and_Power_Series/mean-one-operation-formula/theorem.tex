Let $\mu$ be a measure on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ and let $f(X) = \int (1+X)^x \, d\mu(x)$ be the associated power series. Define a new measure $\nu$ by
$$
\int \varphi(x) \, d\nu(x) = \int \varphi(x) (1+x)^x \, d\mu(x)
$$
for locally constant functions $\varphi$. Then the power series associated to $\nu$ is
$$
g(X) = f(X [+] x) = f((1+X)(1+x) - 1),
$$
where $X [+] x = (1+X)(1+x) - 1$ is the formal group addition.
