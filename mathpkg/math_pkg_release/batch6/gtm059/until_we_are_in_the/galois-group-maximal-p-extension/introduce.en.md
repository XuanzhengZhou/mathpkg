This theorem computes the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension $M(K)$ over the $p$-Hilbert class field $H$ via class field theory. The result is that $\operatorname{Gal}(M/H)$ is quasi-isomorphic to the quotient of the $p$-adic completion of global units by the closure of $E$. The $\mathbb{Z}_p$-rank of this group is at least $r_2 + 1$, and the Leopoldt conjecture (which is true for abelian extensions of $\mathbb{Q}$) asserts equality.
