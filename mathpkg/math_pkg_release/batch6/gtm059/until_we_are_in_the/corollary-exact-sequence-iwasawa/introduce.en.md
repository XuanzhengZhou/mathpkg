This corollary extracts the exact sequence structure from Theorem 4.1, showing that $G = \operatorname{Gal}(M_\infty/K_\infty)$ fits into a short exact sequence with the finite Galois group $G_n$ of the $p$-Hilbert class field as kernel and $\Gamma \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$ as quotient. This sequence is the starting point for studying the $\Lambda$-module structure of the projective limit of class groups.
