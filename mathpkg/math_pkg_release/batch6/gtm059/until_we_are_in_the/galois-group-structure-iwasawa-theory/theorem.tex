Assume that condition IW is satisfied with $s = 1$ (a single totally ramified prime). Let $M_n$ be the maximal abelian $p$-extension of $K_n$ unramified outside the ramified prime, and let $L_n$ be the $p$-Hilbert class field of $K_n$. Let

$$
G = \operatorname{Gal}(M_\infty / K_\infty), \quad G_n = \operatorname{Gal}(M_n / K_n)
$$

where $M_\infty = \bigcup_n M_n$. Let $I$ be the inertia group of the ramified prime in $G$. Then:

(i) $G = G_n \cdot I$ is a semidirect product, and the restriction map $G \to G_n$ gives an isomorphism of $I$ onto $\operatorname{Gal}(M_n/K_n)$ for sufficiently large $n$.

(ii) The commutator subgroup $[G, G]$ is contained in $G_n$.

(iii) $G_n$ is the maximal abelian $p$-quotient of $G$.
