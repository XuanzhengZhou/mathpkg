This theorem describes the structure of the Galois group $G = \operatorname{Gal}(M_\infty/K_\infty)$ of the maximal abelian $p$-extension unramified outside the ramified prime in a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. Under Iwasawa's condition (IW) with a single ramified prime, $G$ decomposes as a semidirect product of the Galois group of the $p$-Hilbert class field and the inertia group $I$. This structural result is fundamental for understanding how class groups grow in $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions.
