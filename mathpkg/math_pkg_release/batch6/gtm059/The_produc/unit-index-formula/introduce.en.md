This lemma relates the unit index $(E : \mu_K \cdot E^+)$ of a CM field $K$ to its arithmetic invariants: the class numbers $h$, $h^+$ and regulators $R$, $R^+$ of $K$ and its maximal real subfield $K^+$. The formula arises naturally in the analytic proof of the class number formula, where the quotient of zeta functions $\zeta_K(s)/\zeta_{K^+}(s)$ leads to a comparison of regulators. The unit index measures how many independent units in $K$ are not already accounted for by the roots of unity and the units of the real subfield.
