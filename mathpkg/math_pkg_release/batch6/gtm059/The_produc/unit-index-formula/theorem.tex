Let $K$ be a CM field with maximal real subfield $K^+$. Let $E$ be the group of units in $K$, let $E^+$ be the group of units in $K^+$, and let $\mu_K$ be the group of roots of unity in $K$. Let $h$ and $h^+$ denote the class numbers of $K$ and $K^+$ respectively, and let $R$ and $R^+$ denote their regulators. Let $N = [K : \mathbb{Q}]$. Then the unit index is given by
$$(E : \mu_K \cdot E^+) = \frac{2^{r_1 + r_2} h R^+}{h^+ R}$$
where $r_1$ and $r_2$ are the numbers of real and complex places of $K$ respectively, and $r_1 + 2r_2 = N$.
