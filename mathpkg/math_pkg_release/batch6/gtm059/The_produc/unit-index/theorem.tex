Let $K$ be a CM field with maximal real subfield $K^+$. Let $E$ be the group of units in $K$, let $E^+$ be the group of units in $K^+$, and let $\mu_K$ be the group of roots of unity in $K$. The **unit index** $Q$ (also denoted $Q_0$) of $K$ is defined as the index
$$Q = Q_0 = (E : \mu_K \cdot E^+)$$
where $\mu_K \cdot E^+$ denotes the subgroup of $E$ generated by the roots of unity and the units of the maximal real subfield. The unit index measures the extent to which the full unit group $E$ exceeds the subgroup generated by the "obvious" units coming from roots of unity and the real subfield.
