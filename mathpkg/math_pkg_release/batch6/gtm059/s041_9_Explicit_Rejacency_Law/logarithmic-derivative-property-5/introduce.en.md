This proposition (designated DL 5 in the text) establishes a congruence between the logarithmic derivative function $h_K$ and the image of the trace map $T_K$ applied to a formal power series. When $m = n$ and $n = K$, we have $h_K(s) \equiv T_K(s)_n(s) \pmod{n}$. The proof reduces to the case where $m = n + 1$, combining DL 3 and DL 4 with expansions in the Galois group.
