The proof for C.3 is the same as the proof for C.2. It suffices to verify integrality of the relevant power series expressions involving the trace map $T_k^*$ and the uniformizer $u_n$. By expanding in terms of the different $d(A^3)$ and using the bound $m \geq 2$, the congruence follows from the integrality estimates established in the preceding lemmas.
