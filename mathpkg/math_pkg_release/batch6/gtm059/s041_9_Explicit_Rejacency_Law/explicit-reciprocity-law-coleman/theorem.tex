Let $K_{\infty}/\mathbb{Q}_p$ be the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. For a norm-compatible system of local units, the Coleman power series provides an explicit isomorphism between the projective limit of local units and a subring of $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$. The explicit reciprocity law states that the value of the $p$-adic $L$-function at a character can be expressed as the trace of the logarithm of the Coleman power series evaluated at the character.
