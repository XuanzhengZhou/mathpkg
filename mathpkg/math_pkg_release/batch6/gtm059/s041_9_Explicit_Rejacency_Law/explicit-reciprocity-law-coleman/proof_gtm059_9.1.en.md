The proof constructs the Coleman power series for a norm-compatible sequence of local units using the theory of the Artin-Hasse exponential and the $p$-adic logarithm on Lubin-Tate formal groups. The key step is showing that the Coleman map $\mathcal{N}: \varprojlim U_n \to \Lambda$ is an isomorphism onto the subring of power series satisfying a certain Frobenius compatibility condition. The explicit reciprocity formula then follows by evaluating the trace pairing on the Coleman power series against characters of the Galois group.
