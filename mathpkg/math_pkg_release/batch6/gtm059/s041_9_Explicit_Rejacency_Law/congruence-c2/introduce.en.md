Congruence C.2 establishes an integrality condition for elements $x$ in $d(A^2)$: the trace-different product equals $n^{k+1} u_n$ whenever $m \geq 2$. The verification reduces to showing that $x^{2k}(T/2)$ is integral, or equivalently that $-2 \leq \frac{1}{x-2} + \frac{2}{x+2} \geq 0$, which holds. The proof follows the same structure as C.3.
