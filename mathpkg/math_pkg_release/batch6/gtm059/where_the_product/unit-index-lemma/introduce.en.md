This lemma establishes the relationship between the unit index $Q$, the class numbers $h$ and $h^+$, and the regulators $R$ and $R^+$ of a CM field and its maximal real subfield. The proof proceeds by comparing the regulator determinants of $K$ and $K^+$: the regulator of $K$ is related to that of $K^+$ via the logarithmic embedding and the index of the unit groups, with the factor $2^{N/2}$ arising from the $N/2$ complex places of $K$. This result is fundamental for deriving the factorization $h = h^+ \cdot h^-$ and analyzing the relative class number.
