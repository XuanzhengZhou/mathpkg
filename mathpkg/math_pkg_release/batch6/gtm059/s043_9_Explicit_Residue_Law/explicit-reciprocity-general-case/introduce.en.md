Theorem 7.1 is the final form of the Explicit Reciprocity Law, establishing the formula $(x, w_n) = [x_n, y_n]_B(x)$ in the general case. The theorem reduces the problem to the case where $x$ is a unit (neither a power of the uniformizer nor the uniformizer itself). The proof combines the results of the preceding sections: the main lemma for highly divisible elements, the refined formula via the Frobenius power series, and the unit case treated in Theorem 6.1 and Theorem 6.2.
