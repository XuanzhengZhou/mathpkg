Theorem 6.1 extends the explicit reciprocity law to the case where the second argument is a unit (rather than a uniformizer). The units are generated by elements $\mu_i$, and the formula expresses the norm residue symbol $(x, u)$ in terms of the explicit symbol $[x, \mu[2] + u]$. The result holds under an order condition on $x$ that depends on the parity of $p$.
