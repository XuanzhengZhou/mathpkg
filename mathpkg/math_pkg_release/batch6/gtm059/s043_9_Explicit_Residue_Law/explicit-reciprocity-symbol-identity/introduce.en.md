Theorem 6.2 gives a refined formula for the norm residue symbol when both arguments are elements of the valuation ring $v_w$. The key equality $(x, -y) = (x^2, -y) + [x^2, -y]$ exploits the alternating property of the symbol and the interchangeability of addition on the formal group $A$ with addition on the multiplicative group $\mathbb{G}_m$ under sufficient order conditions. This result is a stepping stone toward the proof of the general explicit reciprocity law.
