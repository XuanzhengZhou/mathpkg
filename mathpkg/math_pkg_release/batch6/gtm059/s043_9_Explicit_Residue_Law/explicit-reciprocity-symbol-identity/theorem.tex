**Theorem 6.2.** *Suppose*

$$\operatorname{ord}_i a_i \ge |n_2| + 2$$

*if $p$ is odd. Then for $x, y \in v_w$ satisfying $\operatorname{ord}_x, \operatorname{ord}_y \ge 2$ (so that $\operatorname{ord}_x, \operatorname{ord}_y \ge n + 3$), we have*

$$(x, -y) = (x^2, -y) + [x^2, -y]$$

*where addition on the formal group $A$ and addition on the multiplicative group $\mathbb{G}_m$ are interchangeable on the left of the symbol under the stated order conditions.*
