The Explicit Reciprocity Law of Theorem 5.1 gives a formula for the local norm residue symbol $(x, \pi_n)$ in terms of an explicit power series $[x, \pi_n]_n(x)$ involving the logarithmic derivative and the trace map. This is the central result of local explicit class field theory, connecting the abstract norm residue symbol to concrete analytic data. The formula holds under an order condition on $x$, namely $\operatorname{ord}_K x \ge 2n + 1$ when $x$ lies in the field generated by $\pi_n$, or $\operatorname{ord}_K x \ge n$ when $x$ lies in a larger field $K(\pi_{2n^2 + n})$.
