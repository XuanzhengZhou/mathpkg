**Theorem 5.1.** *Let $\pi_n$ be a uniformizer for the Lubin-Tate formal group. We have the equality*

$$(x, \pi_n) = [x, \pi_n]_n(x)$$

*under either of the following conditions:*

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{(i)}&\quad x \in K(\pi_n) \quad\text{and}\quad m \ge 2n + 1,\\[4pt]
\text{(ii)}&\quad x \in K(\pi_{2n^2 + n}) \quad\text{and}\quad m \ge n.
\end{aligned}
$$

*Here $(x, \pi_n)$ denotes the local norm residue symbol and $[x, \pi_n]_n(x)$ is defined via the logarithm and trace map of the formal group.*
