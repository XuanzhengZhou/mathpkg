Let $n = \prod p_i^{e_i}$ be the prime power factorization. Then
$$\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z} \cong \prod_i \mathbb{Z}/p_i^{e_i}\mathbb{Z}$$
and correspondingly any character $\chi$ on $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}$ decomposes as a product
$$\chi = \prod_i \chi_i$$
where each $\chi_i$ is a character on $\mathbb{Z}/p_i^{e_i}\mathbb{Z}$.
