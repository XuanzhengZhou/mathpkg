Theorem 3.1.3 gives Iwasawa's generalization of the classical Artin-Hasse explicit reciprocity law. It expresses the norm residue symbol $(x, \alpha)_n$ in terms of the bracket symbol $[x, \alpha]_n$ multiplied by $|u_n|$. In the special case over $\mathbb{Q}_p$, the formula reduces to Iwasawa's classical formula involving the logarithm and the logarithmic derivative, which itself generalizes formulas of Artin-Hasse and Coates-Wiles. The proof occupies the remainder of the chapter.
