Let $u \in \mathfrak{o}$ with $u \equiv 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}}$. Then

$$
N_1^*(u) \equiv 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}^{n+1}}
$$

and the local symbol satisfies

$$
\langle u_i, u_j \rangle_n = \left[ \frac{1}{p^2} \left( N_1^{n+1}(u_i) - 1 \right) \right]_u
$$

where $N_1^*$ denotes the norm from $K_1$ to $K$ and $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle_n$ is the local norm residue symbol at level $n$.
