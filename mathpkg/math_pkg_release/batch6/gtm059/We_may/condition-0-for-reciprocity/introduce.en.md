Condition (0) is a technical hypothesis needed to ensure that the local symbol $[x, a]_n$ is well-defined independently of the choice of level $n$. The condition involves divisibility requirements on the elements $a_n$ in the Lubin-Tate tower and ensures sufficient compatibility under norm maps. These conditions are always satisfied in the situations of interest for explicit reciprocity laws.
