For the local symbol $[x, a]_n$ to be well-defined independently of $n$, the following conditions are required:

\textbf{Condition (0).} $n \geq 2$ and $m \geq 1$, and there exists an integer

$$
k = \lfloor n/2 \rfloor - 2m + 1
$$

such that $a_m = N_{n,m} a_n \in K_m$ with $m \geq 2$.

\textbf{Condition (0')} $m \geq n$ and $n \geq 2$.
