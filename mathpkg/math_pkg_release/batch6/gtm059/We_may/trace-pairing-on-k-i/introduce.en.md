The trace pairing on $K_i$ is a fundamental tool in local class field theory. Using the trace map $T_i : K_i \to K$, one obtains a non-degenerate $K$-bilinear pairing $K_i \times K_i \to K$ defined by $(x, y) \mapsto T_i(xy)$. This pairing allows the identification of dual modules and plays a crucial role in the study of local symbols and explicit reciprocity laws.
