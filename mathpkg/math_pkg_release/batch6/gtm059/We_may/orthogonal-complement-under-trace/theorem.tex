Let $\mathfrak{a}$ be an ideal in $K_i$. The orthogonal complement $\mathfrak{a}^*$ with respect to the trace pairing is defined by

$$
\mathfrak{a}^* = \{ y \in K_i \mid T_i(xy) \in \mathfrak{o} \text{ for all } x \in \mathfrak{a} \}
$$

where $\mathfrak{o}$ is the ring of integers of $K$. Equivalently,

$$
\mathfrak{a}^* = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathfrak{o}}(\mathfrak{a}, \mathfrak{o})
$$

under the identification given by the trace pairing. If $\mathfrak{D}_{K_i/K}$ is the different, then

$$
\mathfrak{a}^* = \mathfrak{a}^{-1} \mathfrak{D}_{K_i/K}^{-1}.
$$
