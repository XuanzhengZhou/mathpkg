Let $\mathbb{G}_m$ be the formal multiplicative group. Let $\zeta$ be a primitive $p^{n+1}$-th root of unity, and let $u \equiv 1 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}}$. Then

$$
\mathbb{G}_m(\mathfrak{p}^n) = \left\{ c \;\middle|\; c = \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} a_i b_i u^i \right\}
$$

where the coefficients $a_i, b_i$ are determined by the Lubin-Tate group law. This is a special case of the main corollary to the explicit reciprocity law.
