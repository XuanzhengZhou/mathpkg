This corollary specializes the explicit reciprocity law to the case of the formal multiplicative group $\mathbb{G}_m$. It provides a concrete description of the points of $\mathbb{G}_m$ in terms of roots of unity and power series expansions. The result is closely related to the classical formulas of Artin-Hasse and Iwasawa for the norm residue symbol in cyclotomic extensions.
