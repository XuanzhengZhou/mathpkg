Theorem 1.2 establishes that the local norm residue symbol $\langle x, \alpha \rangle_n$ can be computed via the bracket symbol $[x, N_n \alpha]_{A_f(\pi)}$ associated with the Lubin-Tate formal group. The result requires the technical Condition (0) to ensure well-definedness. An important special case occurs when $\alpha$ can be chosen so that the equality holds for arbitrarily large $n$, with the value being independent of the choice of $m \geq 2n+1$.
