This corollary to Lemma 5.1 strengthens the inclusion statement by relating $A^*(u)_{k \times n}$ to a power of the different $d_n$ multiplied by the unit group $[u^*]^k$. It provides a finer containment result that is useful when applying the logarithm to study the local symbol pairing.
