Theorem 4 establishes a Kummer duality pairing between the Galois tensor conjugate and the ideal class group. Using the eigenspace decomposition for complex conjugation, the theorem first asserts that $G_d(D/G_d)$ is dual to $C_c$. Secondly, it shows that $D^n \subseteq G_d$ and that $G_d$ is an odd extension of $D^n$, again via eigenspace analysis.
