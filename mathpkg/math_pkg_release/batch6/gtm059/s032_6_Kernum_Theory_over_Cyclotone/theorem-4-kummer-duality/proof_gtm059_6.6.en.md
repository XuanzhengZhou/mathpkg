*Proof.* By Theorems 2.2, 2.3 and Lemma 1 of §2 we know that $D = G_d$ and that

$$G_d(D / G_d) \text{ is dual to } C_c.$$

Taking eigenspaces for complex conjugation yields the first assertion.

As to the second, we know from Theorem 4.2 that

$$D^n \subseteq G_d$$

and $G_d$ is an odd extension of $D^n$. Again considering the eigenspaces yields the second assertion.
