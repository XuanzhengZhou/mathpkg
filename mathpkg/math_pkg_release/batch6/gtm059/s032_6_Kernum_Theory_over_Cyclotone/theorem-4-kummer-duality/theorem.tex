\textbf{Theorem 4.} The Galois tensor conjugate and the Kummer duality give rise to a compact pairing. Specifically:

(1) $D = G_d$ is dual to $C_c$, and the eigenspaces for complex conjugation give the first assertion.

(2) $D^n \subseteq G_d$ and $G_d$ is an odd extension of $D^n$. Again considering the eigenspaces yields the second assertion.
