Theorem 1.2 provides the explicit formula relating the Kummer-Takagi exponents $t_k$ of a local unit to the Kummer pairing $\varphi_0$. Specifically, $t_k \equiv -\frac{1}{2}\varphi_0(u_k) \bmod p$. Together with Theorem 1.1, this gives a complete computational method: the Kummer-Takagi exponents can be read off from the values of the Kummer pairing on the fundamental cyclotomic units.
