This theorem is one of the central structural results of Iwasawa theory. It identifies the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension with the Pontryagin dual of the quotient of local units by cyclotomic units. This establishes a precise relationship between the arithmetic of units (measured by the $p$-adic regulator and Leopoldt's conjecture) and the Galois theory of the cyclotomic tower. The structure of this $\Lambda$-module is governed by the $p$-adic $L$-function via the main conjecture.
