Property K.2 describes how the associated power series transforms under $p$-adic translation within the Iwasawa-theoretic framework. Given a power series $f$ associated with a unit $u$, the translated power series satisfies a specific functional equation involving $p$-adic powers, which is fundamental for computing Kummer-Takagi exponents.
