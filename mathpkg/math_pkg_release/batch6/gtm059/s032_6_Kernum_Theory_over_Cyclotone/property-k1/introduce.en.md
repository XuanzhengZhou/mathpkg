Property K.1 describes the multiplicative behavior of the association between units and power series in the Iwasawa theory of local units. If two power series $f$ and $f'$ are associated with units $u$ and $u_0$ respectively, then their product $ff'$ is associated with the product $uu_0$. Furthermore, exponentiation by $p$-adic integers is compatible with this association.
