\textbf{K.4.} Let $\chi$ be a character of $G_0 = \operatorname{Gal}(K_0/\mathbb{Q}_p)$ and let
$$e(\chi) = \frac{1}{p-1} \sum_{a=1}^{p-1} \chi(a)^{-1} \sigma_a$$
be the corresponding idempotent in the group algebra $\mathbb{Z}_p[G_0]$.

(1) If $k \equiv a \bmod p$, then $e(\chi)(u_k) = e(\chi)(u)$.

(2) If $k \not\equiv a \bmod p$, then $e(\chi)(u_k) = 0$.
