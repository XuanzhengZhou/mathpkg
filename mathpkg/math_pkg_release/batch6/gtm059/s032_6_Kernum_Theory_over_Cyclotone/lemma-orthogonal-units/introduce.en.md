This lemma provides an explicit product formula for the orthogonalized unit $v_0$ obtained by applying Galois idempotents $e(\chi_a)$ to the cyclotomic units $1-\zeta^a$. The congruence modulo $p^{n-1}$ shows that $v_0$ can be expressed as a simple algebraic combination of roots of unity, which is crucial for computing the Kummer-Takagi exponents in terms of character values.
