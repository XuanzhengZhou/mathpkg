*Proof.* We have

$$v_0 = \prod_{a=1}^{p-1} \left(1 - \zeta^{a}\right)^{e(\chi_a)} \equiv \prod_{a=1}^{p-1} \left(1 + \zeta^{a}\right)^{e(\chi_a)} \pmod{p^{n-1}}.$$

Expanding the product using the idempotent relations and the fact that $\sum e(\chi_a) = 1$ yields the stated congruence.
