This lemma establishes two key arithmetic properties of the coefficients $y_k(k)$. Part (a) gives an explicit alternating sum formula involving binomial coefficients. Part (b) asserts that $k!$ divides $y_k(k)$, which is crucial for proving the $p$-adic integrality of the Leopoldt transform applied to power series with integral coefficients.
