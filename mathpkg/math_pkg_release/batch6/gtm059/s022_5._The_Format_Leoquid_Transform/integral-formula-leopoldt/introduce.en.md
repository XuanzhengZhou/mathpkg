This theorem provides an integral representation of the $p$-adic Leopoldt transform, expressing $\Gamma_s(f)$ as an integral over $\mathbb{Z}_p^*$ of $f^*(x)$ against the character $\omega^s$. It shows that the Leopoldt transform can be viewed as an extension of the classical Gauss sum / Mellin transform to the $p$-adic setting. The result was derived from a conversation with Ribet and is fundamental to the construction of $p$-adic $L$-functions.
