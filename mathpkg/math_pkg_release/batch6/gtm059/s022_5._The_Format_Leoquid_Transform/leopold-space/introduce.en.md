The Leopold space $\mathcal{L}_p$ consists of all power series with coefficients in a finite extension $K$ of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ whose coefficients tend to zero $p$-adically. This is the natural domain on which the $p$-adic Leopoldt transform acts. The space is a Banach space (and in fact a Banach algebra) under the Leopold norm, providing the analytic framework for the $p$-adic interpolation of special values of $L$-functions.
