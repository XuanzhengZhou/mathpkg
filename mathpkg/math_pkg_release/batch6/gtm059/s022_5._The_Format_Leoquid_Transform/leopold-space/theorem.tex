Let $p$ be an odd prime. Let $K$ be a finite extension of $\mathbb{Q}_p$. Let $\mathbb{C}_p$ be a completion of the algebraic closure of $K$. Denote the $p$-adic absolute value by $|\cdot|$, normalized so that $|p| = 1/p$.

The Leopold space $\mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_p$ is the space of power series

$$f(X) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_n X^n, \quad a_n \in K$$

such that

$$\lim_{n \to \infty} |a_n| = 0.$$
