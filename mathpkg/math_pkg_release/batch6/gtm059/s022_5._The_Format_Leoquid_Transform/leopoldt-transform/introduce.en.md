The Leopoldt transform maps a power series to a function on the integers. Given a power series $f(X) = \sum a_n X^n$ with coefficients in a field $K$ of characteristic 0, its Leopoldt transform $f^*$ is defined on integers $k$ by evaluating the formal expression $\sum a_n k^n / n!$. This transform plays a fundamental role in the study of $p$-adic $L$-functions, as it provides the bridge between power series expansions and $p$-adic interpolation of special values.
