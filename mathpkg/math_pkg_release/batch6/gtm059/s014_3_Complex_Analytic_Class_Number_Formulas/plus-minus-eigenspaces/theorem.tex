Let $K$ be a CM field (a totally imaginary quadratic extension of a totally real field). Let $C_K$ denote the ideal class group of $K$. Let $\tau$ denote complex conjugation. The \emph{plus and minus eigenspaces} of $C_K$ are defined as

$$
C_K^+ = \{ c \in C_K : c^{\tau - 1} = 1 \}, \qquad
C_K^- = \{ c \in C_K : c^{\tau + 1} = 1 \}.
$$

Equivalently, $C_K^+$ is the subgroup of $C_K$ fixed by complex conjugation, and $C_K^-$ is the subgroup on which complex conjugation acts as $-1$.
