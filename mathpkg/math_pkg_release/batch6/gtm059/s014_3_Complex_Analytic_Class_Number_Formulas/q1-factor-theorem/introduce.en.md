This theorem determines the index $Q_1 = [E : C]$, where $E$ is the full unit group and $C$ is the group of cyclotomic units in the cyclotomic field $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)$. The value depends only on whether $m$ is a prime power: $Q_1 = 1$ in the prime power case, and $Q_1 = 2$ otherwise. This factor appears in the analytic class number formula as part of the expression for $h^+$, the plus part of the class number.
