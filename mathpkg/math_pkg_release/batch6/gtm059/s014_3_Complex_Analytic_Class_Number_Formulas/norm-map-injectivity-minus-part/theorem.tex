Let $K$ be an imaginary abelian extension of $\mathbb{Q}$ and let $K^+$ be its maximal real subfield. Let $C_K$ be the ideal class group of $K$ and let $C_{K^+}$ be the ideal class group of $K^+$. Then the norm map

$$
N_{K/K^+}: C_K \longrightarrow C_{K^+}
$$

restricted to the minus eigenspace $C_K^- = \{ c \in C_K : c^{1+\tau} = 1 \}$, where $\tau$ is complex conjugation, is injective. That is,

$$
\ker( N_{K/K^+} |_{C_K^-} ) = \{1\}.
$$
