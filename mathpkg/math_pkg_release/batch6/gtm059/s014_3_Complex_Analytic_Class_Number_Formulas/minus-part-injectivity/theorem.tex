Let $K = \mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)$ be the $m$-th cyclotomic field and let $K^+$ be its maximal real subfield. Let $C_K$ denote the ideal class group of $K$ and let $C_K^- = \{c \in C_K : c^{1+\tau} = 1\}$ be the minus eigenspace under complex conjugation $\tau$. Then the natural inclusion map

$$
C_K^- \hookrightarrow C_K
$$

is injective, so $C_K^-$ can be identified with a subgroup of $C_K$. Consequently the minus part of the class number $h^- = |C_K^-|$ is an integer dividing $h_K = |C_K|$.
