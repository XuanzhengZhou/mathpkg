Let $K$ be an abelian extension of a number field $F$. Let $H$ be the Hilbert class field of $F$, i.e., the maximal unramified abelian extension of $F$. For any ideal class $c \in C_F$, the restriction of the Artin symbol satisfies

$$
(c, K/F) \big|_H = (c, H/F).
$$

Moreover, the Artin symbol induces a natural isomorphism of Galois groups

$$
\operatorname{Gal}(K/F) \cong \operatorname{Gal}(H/F)
$$

when $K \cap H = F$. In general, for any ideal class $c \in C_K$, the norm map $N_{K/F}$ and the restriction of the Artin symbol are compatible:

$$
(c, K/F) \big|_H = (N_{K/F}(c), H/F).
$$
