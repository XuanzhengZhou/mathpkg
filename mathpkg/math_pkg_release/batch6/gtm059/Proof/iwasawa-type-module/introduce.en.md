A module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$ is said to be of Iwasawa type if it admits a finite set of generators whose $\mathbb{Z}_p$-span under the action of $(T-1)^j$ is of finite index. This condition ensures that the module is finitely generated over $\Lambda$ with finite torsion submodule. Modules of Iwasawa type are the natural objects for which the structure theorems of Iwasawa theory (characteristic power series, $\lambda$- and $\mu$-invariants) apply.
