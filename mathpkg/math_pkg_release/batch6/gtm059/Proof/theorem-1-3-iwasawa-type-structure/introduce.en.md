Theorem 1.3 extends the structure theorem (Theorem 1.2) for finitely generated $\Lambda$-modules to the slightly broader class of modules of Iwasawa type. The key difference is that the exponent $e_k$ of $p$ dividing the order of the $k$-th layer includes an additive constant term $w_k$, reflecting the finite torsion submodule. This result is essential for applying Iwasawa theory to ideal class groups, where the full Iwasawa module may not be strictly finitely generated but is always of Iwasawa type.
