It suffices to apply Lemma 3 and to compute the Fourier transform of $p$. This is trivial, and we have
$$\sum_{n=0}^{N-1} b(X^n) = \left[ \frac{0}{S_{X,b}} \right] \frac{1}{N}.$$

The formula for $g_{X,f}(T)$ follows by substituting $T$ for the argument and using the linearity of the unitization operator with respect to the finite character sum.
