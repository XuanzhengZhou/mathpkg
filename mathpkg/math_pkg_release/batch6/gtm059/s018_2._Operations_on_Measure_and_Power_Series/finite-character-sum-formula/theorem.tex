Let $b$ be a character on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ of finite order with conductor $N$ equal to a power of $p$. Let $\zeta$ be a primitive $N$th root of unity, and define
$$S_{X, b} = \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} b(X^n).$$
Then the associated measure $E_X$ satisfies
$$E_X = p_b,$$
and for the associated power series $g$, if $f$ is a rational function, we have
$$g_{X, f}(T) = \frac{S_{X, b}}{N} \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} g(f(X^n) + T).$$
