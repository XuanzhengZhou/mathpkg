This lemma states that when the unitization operator $U$ is applied to the characteristic function $g$ of $\mathbb{Z}_p$, the resulting measure $V_0$ coincides with the normalized Haar measure $\mu_0$ on $\mathbb{Z}_p$. The proof proceeds by computing the Fourier expansion of $g$, which simplifies to the constant function $1$, and then applying the general relation between a measure and its unitization. This result is fundamental for connecting Iwasawa's unitization formalism to the standard p-adic integration theory.
