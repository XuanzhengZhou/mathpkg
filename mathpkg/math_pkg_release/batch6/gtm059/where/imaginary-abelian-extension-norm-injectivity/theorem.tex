Let $K$ be an imaginary abelian extension of $\mathbb{Q}$, and let $K^+$ be its maximal real subfield. Then the norm map

$$
N_{K/K^+}: C_K \longrightarrow C_{K^+}
$$

on the ideal class group is injective. Equivalently, the natural map

$$
C_K^- \longrightarrow C_K
$$

from the $(-1)$-eigenspace of the ideal class group to the full class group is injective.
