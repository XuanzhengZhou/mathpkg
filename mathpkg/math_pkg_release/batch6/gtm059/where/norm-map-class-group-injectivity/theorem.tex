Let $K = \mathbb{Q}(\zeta_{p})$ where $p$ is a prime, and let $K^+ = \mathbb{Q}(\zeta_p + \zeta_p^{-1})$ be its maximal real subfield. Then the natural map

$$
C_K^- \longrightarrow C_K
$$

from the $(-1)$-eigenspace of the ideal class group to the full class group is injective. Equivalently, the kernel of the norm map

$$
N_{K/K^+}: C_K \longrightarrow C_{K^+}
$$

intersects $C_K^-$ trivially.
