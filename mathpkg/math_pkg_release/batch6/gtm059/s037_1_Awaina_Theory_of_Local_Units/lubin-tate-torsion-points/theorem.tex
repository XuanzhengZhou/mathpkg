Let $\mathfrak{o}$ be a complete discrete valuation ring with quotient field $K$, maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$, and uniformizer $\pi$. Let $F_f$ be a Lubin-Tate formal group associated with a Frobenius power series $f \in \mathcal{F}_\pi$. For each integer $n \ge 1$, define
$$
A_n = \{ x \in \overline{\mathfrak{m}} : [\pi^n]_{F_f}(x) = 0 \},
$$
where $\overline{\mathfrak{m}}$ denotes the maximal ideal of the ring of integers of an algebraic closure of $K$. That is, $A_n$ is the kernel of the endomorphism $[\pi^n]_{F_f}$ acting on $\overline{\mathfrak{m}}$.
