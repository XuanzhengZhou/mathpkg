Let $F_f$ be the Lubin-Tate formal group associated with the Frobenius power series $f \in \mathcal{F}_\pi$. For each $a \in \mathfrak{o}$, there exists a unique power series $[a]_f(X) \in \mathfrak{o}[[X]]$ such that
$$
[a]_f(X) = aX \pmod{\deg 2}, \qquad f([a]_f(X)) = [a]_f(f(X)).
$$
The association $a \mapsto [a]_f$ is an \textbf{injective ring homomorphism}
$$
\mathfrak{o} \hookrightarrow \operatorname{End}(F_f),
$$
satisfying $[\pi]_f = f$, and for all $a, b \in \mathfrak{o}$:
$$
[a + b]_f(X) = F_f([a]_f(X), [b]_f(X)), \qquad [ab]_f(X) = [a]_f([b]_f(X)).
$$
