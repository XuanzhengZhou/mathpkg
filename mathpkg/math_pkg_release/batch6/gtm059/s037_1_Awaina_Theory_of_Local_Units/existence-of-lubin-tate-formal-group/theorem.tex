Let $\mathfrak{o}$ be a complete discrete valuation ring, and let $f$ be a Frobenius power series in $\mathcal{F}_\pi$. There exists a \textbf{unique} formal group $F_f(X,Y)$ defined over $\mathfrak{o}$ such that $f$ is an endomorphism of $F_f$, i.e.,
$$
f(F_f(X,Y)) = F_f(f(X), f(Y)).
$$
The formal group $F_f$ is called the \textbf{Lubin-Tate formal group} associated with $f$.
