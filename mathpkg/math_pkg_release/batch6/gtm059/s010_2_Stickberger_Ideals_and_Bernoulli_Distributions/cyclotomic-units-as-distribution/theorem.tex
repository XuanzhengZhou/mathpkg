The \text{cyclotomic units} in a cyclotomic field $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_N)$ form a distribution on the projective system $\{\mathbb{Z}/M\mathbb{Z}\}$. For each level $N$, the cyclotomic units are elements of the form
$$
\prod_{a} (1 - \zeta_N^a)^{c_a}
$$
with integer exponents $c_a$ satisfying certain parity conditions. The norm compatibility relations between different levels correspond precisely to the distribution relations, making the cyclotomic units an example of an ordinary distribution with values in the multiplicative group of the cyclotomic field modulo roots of unity.
