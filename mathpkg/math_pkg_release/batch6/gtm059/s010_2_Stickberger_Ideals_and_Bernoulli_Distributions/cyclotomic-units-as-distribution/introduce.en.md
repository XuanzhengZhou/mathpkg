Cyclotomic units are fundamental arithmetic objects in cyclotomic fields. They form a subgroup of the unit group of $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_N)$ and satisfy norm compatibility relations that can be interpreted as distribution relations. This viewpoint, developed by Kubert and Lang, unifies the study of cyclotomic units with the theory of distributions and Stickelberger ideals. The index of the cyclotomic units in the full unit group is related to the class number of the cyclotomic field.
