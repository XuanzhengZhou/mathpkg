Theorem 1.1 computes the index of the Stickelberger ideal $\mathcal{S} = R \cap R_1$ inside the full group ring $R^*$ when the conductor $m = p^n$ is a prime power. The index is expressed as a product of determinants $R_{i,i}$ arising from the decomposition of the group ring. In Chapter 3, this index is related to the order of the minus part of the ideal class group of the cyclotomic field.
