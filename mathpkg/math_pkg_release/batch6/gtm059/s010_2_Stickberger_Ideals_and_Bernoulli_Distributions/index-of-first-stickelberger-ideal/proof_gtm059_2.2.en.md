The proof proceeds by analyzing the sequence of groups and subgroups in a diagram indicating inclusion relations among various submodules of the group ring. The indices are computed by evaluating determinants of certain linear transformations. The sign is determined by the absolute value of the determinant. Multiplication by certain elements yields the factorization, and the result follows by standard elementary linear algebra applied to the group ring decomposition.
