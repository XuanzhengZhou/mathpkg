The Stickelberger ideal is a fundamental construction in the theory of cyclotomic fields. It is defined as the intersection $R \cap R_1$ of two distinguished submodules of the integral group ring $\mathbb{Z}[G]$, where $G$ is the Galois group of the cyclotomic extension. The index of this ideal in the full group ring encodes deep arithmetic information, including class numbers of cyclotomic fields.
