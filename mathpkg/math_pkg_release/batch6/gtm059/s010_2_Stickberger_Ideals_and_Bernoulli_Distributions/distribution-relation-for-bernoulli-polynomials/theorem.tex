For any positive integer $N$, the Bernoulli polynomials satisfy the distribution relation
$$
B_k(NX) = N^{k-1} \sum_{a=0}^{N-1} B_k\!\left(X + \frac{a}{N}\right).
$$
Equivalently, for $y \in \mathbb{R}/\mathbb{Z}$,
$$
N^{k-1} \sum_{a=0}^{N-1} B_k\!\left(\left\langle y + \frac{a}{N} \right\rangle\right) = B_k(\langle N y \rangle).
$$
On the projective system $\{\frac{1}{M}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}\}$, the association
$$
x \mapsto M^{k-1} B_k(\langle x \rangle) \quad \text{for } x \in \frac{1}{M}\mathbb{Z}/\mathbb{Z}
$$
defines a distribution.
