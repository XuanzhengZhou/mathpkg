This theorem computes the free part of the Iwasawa module $G = \operatorname{Gal}(Q/K_\infty)$ arising from the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension. When the base field $K_0$ is totally imaginary and the Leopoldt conjecture holds at every finite layer $K_n$, the quotient of $G$ by its $\Lambda$-torsion submodule is quasi-isomorphic to a free $\Lambda$-module of rank $r_2$. Combined with Theorem 6.1, this gives a fairly complete structural description of $G$ as an Iwasawa module.
