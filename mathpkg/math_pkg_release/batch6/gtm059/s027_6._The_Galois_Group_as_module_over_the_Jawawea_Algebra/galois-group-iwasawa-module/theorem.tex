Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension and let $L_{\infty}$ be the maximal unramified $p$-abelian extension of $K_{\infty}$. Let $X = \operatorname{Gal}(L_{\infty}/K_{\infty})$. Then $X$ is a finitely generated torsion $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[\operatorname{Gal}(K_{\infty}/K)]]$-module, where the action of $\Lambda$ on $X$ is given by lifting and conjugating automorphisms.
