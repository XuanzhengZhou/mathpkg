This lemma establishes that the projective limit $U = \varprojlim U_n$ of local unit groups is torsion-free as a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module. The absence of $\mathbb{Z}_p$-torsion is a crucial structural property: if $U$ contained $p$-power torsion, then taking successive preimages under the norm maps would produce arbitrarily large roots of unity in the finite layers $U_n$, which is impossible by the structure of the unit groups. This fact is essential for proving that $U$ is a free module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda$.
