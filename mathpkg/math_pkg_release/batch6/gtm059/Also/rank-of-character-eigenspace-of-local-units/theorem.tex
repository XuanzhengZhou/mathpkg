Let $G_n = \mathrm{Gal}(K_n/K)$ operate on $K_n$ and on the unit group $U_n$. Let $\Gamma = \Gamma_n$ and let $\gamma$ be a topological generator of $\Gamma$. Let $\Gamma^* = \Gamma \times G_n$. For each character $\chi$ of $G_n$ with $\chi \neq 1$, let $U_n(\chi)$ denote the $\chi$-eigenspace of $U_n$. Then:

(1) $\mathrm{rank}_{\mathbb{Z}_p}(U_n(\chi)) = p^n$.

(2) $U_n(\chi)$ is a free $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module.
