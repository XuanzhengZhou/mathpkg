The projective limit of local unit groups $U = \varprojlim U_n$ is the fundamental Iwasawa module in the local theory. It is constructed from the principal units $U_n$ at each finite layer $K_n$ of the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, with the norm maps providing the transition morphisms. The resulting object $U$ carries a natural structure of a compact $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module and a module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda \cong \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$, making it the central object of study in the Iwasawa theory of local units.
