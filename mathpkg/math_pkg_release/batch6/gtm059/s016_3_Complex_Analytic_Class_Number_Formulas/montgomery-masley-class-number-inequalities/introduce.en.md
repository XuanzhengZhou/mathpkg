Montgomery and Masley established two-sided bounds for the class number $h_p^+$ of the maximal real subfield of $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_p)$, valid for sufficiently large primes $p$. Their result shows that the growth rate of $h_p^+$ is approximately $p^{(p+1)/4}$, confirming that the Carlitz--Hadamard upper bound captures the correct order of magnitude. The proof refines the determinant method using more sophisticated estimates of the relevant group determinant.
