This lemma extends the interchangeability result of Theorem 6.2 to the special Lubin-Tate group $B$ whose associated Frobenius power series has the form $F(X) = X + L w_i w_j B$. The group law on $B$ satisfies $F(X, Y) = X + Y$ modulo terms of degree at least $n$, which implies that $x +_B y = p(x) + y$ and $p^n \in [x | B_n)$. As a result, addition on $B_n$ and addition on $G_n$ are interchangeable on the left of the symbol $(x, w_n)$ under the stated conditions. The proof is identical in structure to that of Theorem 6.2, and the lemma serves as the final technical step before the conclusion of the main theorem.
