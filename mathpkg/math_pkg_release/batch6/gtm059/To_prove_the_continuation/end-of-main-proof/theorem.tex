Let $x \in d(x)$ and let
$$n = (n_1, n_2, \dots, n_k) \quad \text{with} \quad n_k = N_{n_k} w_n.$$
Assume that
$$k \ge |s| + 21 \quad \text{if } p \text{ is odd},$$
$$k \ge |n| + 21 + |x + 1| = f \quad \text{if } p = 2.$$
Then $[x, w_n]$ lies in $i^{n+1}$ for $1 \le i \le k$ and we have
$$(x, w_n) = [x_n, y_n]_B(x)$$
for such $n$.
