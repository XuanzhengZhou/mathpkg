Let $x \in \kappa(n_i)$ and let $n = (n_1, \ldots, n_k)$. Then we have the equality
$$(x, n_i) = [x, n_i]_{\kappa}(x)$$
under either of the following conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $x \in \kappa(n_i)$ and $m \ge 2n + 1$;
\item[(ii)] $x \in \kappa(2n^2 + n_i)$ and $m \ge n$.
\end{enumerate}
