Assume that the Lubin-Tate power series associated with the formal group $A$ has the form
$$F(X) = X^n + \cdots + X^k,$$
i.e., is a polynomial of degree $n$ with leading coefficient $1$. Define more precisely
$$[x_n] = \frac{1}{n^2} F_X(x) \left( \frac{1}{Y_n(X)} \right).$$
Then for $x \in d(X)$ we have
$$(x - x_n) = [x - x_n]_a.$$
