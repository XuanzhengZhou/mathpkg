This lemma provides an identity relating the weighted sum of Bernoulli-type terms $B(i)$ to $T/N$ and to the difference $g_{l,l}(G_l(T)) - G_l(T)$. The identity is proved by taking the logarithmic derivative of the polynomial expression $T^* - (1 + \prod_{i=1}^{N} (T - i^*))$ and then multiplying by $S_{l,l}$ and $Q(N)$. It serves as an auxiliary computation in the construction of the $p$-adic $L$-function via the Mellin transform.
