Let $\{b_n\}_{n \geq 1}$ be a sequence of complex numbers and let $\{a_n\}_{n \geq 1}$ be a decreasing sequence of positive real numbers with $\lim_{n \to \infty} a_n = 0$. If the partial sums

$$B_n = \sum_{k=1}^{n} b_k$$

are bounded, i.e., there exists a constant $C > 0$ such that $|B_n| \leq C$ for all $n$, then the series

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n b_n$$

converges.
