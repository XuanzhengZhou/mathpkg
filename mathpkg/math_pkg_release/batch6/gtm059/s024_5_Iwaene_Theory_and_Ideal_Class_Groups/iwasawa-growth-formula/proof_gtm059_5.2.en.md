Let $X = \varprojlim A_n$ be the projective limit of the $p$-parts of the class groups, which is a finitely generated torsion $\Lambda$-module. Applying the structure theorem for $\Lambda$-modules, $X$ is quasi-isomorphic to a direct sum of cyclic modules $\Lambda/(p^{m_i})$ and $\Lambda/(f_j^{n_j})$. For each cyclic module $\Lambda/(p^m)$, the $n$-th layer contributes $m p^n$ to $e_n$ (giving the $\mu$ term). For each $\Lambda/(f^m)$ where $f$ is a distinguished polynomial of degree $d$, the contribution is $m d n$ (giving the $\lambda$ term). The constant $\nu$ accounts for the finite quasi-isomorphism correction.
