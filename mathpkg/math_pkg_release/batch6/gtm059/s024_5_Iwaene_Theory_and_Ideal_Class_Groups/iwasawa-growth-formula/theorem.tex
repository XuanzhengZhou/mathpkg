Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension and let $A_n$ be the $p$-Sylow subgroup of the ideal class group of the $n$-th layer $K_n$. Let $p^{e_n} = |A_n|$. Then there exist integers $\mu \geq 0$, $\lambda \geq 0$, and $\nu$ such that for all sufficiently large $n$,

$$e_n = \mu p^n + \lambda n + \nu.$$

The invariants $\mu = \mu(K_{\infty}/K)$, $\lambda = \lambda(K_{\infty}/K)$, and $\nu = \nu(K_{\infty}/K)$ are called the Iwasawa invariants.
