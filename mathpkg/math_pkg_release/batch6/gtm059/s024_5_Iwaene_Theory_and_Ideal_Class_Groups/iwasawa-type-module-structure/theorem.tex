Assume that $V$ is a $\Lambda$-module of Iwasawa type. Then the conclusions of Theorem 1.2 remain valid, except that in case (i) of Theorem 1.2 the formula must be written as

$$e_k = b_k + v_k$$

with some constant $v_k$, where $e_k$ is the exponent of the $p$-primary part of $V/(y^{p^k}-1)V$ and $b_k$ is the corresponding exponent in the structure decomposition.
