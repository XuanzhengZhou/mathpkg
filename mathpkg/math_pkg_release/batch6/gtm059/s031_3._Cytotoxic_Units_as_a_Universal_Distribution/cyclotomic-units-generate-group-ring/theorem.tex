Let $p$ be a prime and let $a, b$ be integers with $c$ a primitive root modulo $p$. Let $E_n$ be the cyclotomic unit group at level $n$. Then $E_n$ generates the integral group ring $\mathbb{Z}[E_n]$ as a cyclic $\mathbb{Z}[G]$-module, where $G = \operatorname{Gal}(\mathbb{Q}(\mu_{p^n})/\mathbb{Q})$. More precisely, the element
$$
v_n = \frac{v^a - 1}{v - 1} \in \mathbb{Z}[E_n]
$$
generates the augmentation ideal of the group ring.
