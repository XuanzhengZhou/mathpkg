Cyclotomic units are special units in cyclotomic fields constructed from roots of unity. Together with the cyclotomic numbers, they form a universal distribution on the projective system of layers in the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. The Kummer pairing relates this distribution to the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian extension, providing a powerful tool for understanding the structure of Iwasawa modules arising from unit groups. This theory is fundamental for the proof of the main conjecture of Iwasawa theory.
