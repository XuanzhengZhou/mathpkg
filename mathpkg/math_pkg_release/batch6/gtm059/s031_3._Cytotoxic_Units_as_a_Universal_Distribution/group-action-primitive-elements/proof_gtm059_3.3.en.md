The homomorphism $Z_k^* \to \operatorname{Aut}(V_k)$ is induced by the Galois action on cyclotomic units. Surjectivity is obvious from the definition of the action. For injectivity, suppose an element of $Z_k^*$ acts trivially on $V_k$. Then it fixes all cyclotomic units, hence fixes all elements $\zeta - 1$ for primitive $p^k$-th roots of unity $\zeta$. Since the cyclotomic field is generated by these elements together with roots of unity, the automorphism must be the identity. Therefore the ranks of $Z_k^*$ and the automorphism group are equal, giving the isomorphism.
