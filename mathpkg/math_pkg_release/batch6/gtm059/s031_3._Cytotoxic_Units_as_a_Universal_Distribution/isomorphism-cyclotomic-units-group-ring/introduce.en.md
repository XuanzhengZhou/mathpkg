Theorem 3.5 provides a structural isomorphism between a distinguished subgroup $V_2$ of the cyclotomic unit group (mod roots of unity) and the integral group ring $\mathbb{Z}[E_n]$. The subgroup $V_2$ consists of elements of the form $(v^a - 1)/(v - 1)$ factored through the distribution. This isomorphism shows that the cyclotomic unit group, after factoring by roots of unity and restricting to the "positive" subgroup, is isomorphic to the augmentation ideal of the group ring, which is a free $\mathbb{Z}$-module of rank $[E_n : 1]$.
