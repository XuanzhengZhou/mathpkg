Let $k$ be a generator of the cyclic group of order $p-1$ (if $p > 2$) or $k = 5$ (if $p = 2$), viewed as a primitive root modulo $p$. Let
$$
t_n = \text{image of } \frac{v^a - 1}{v - 1} \text{ in } V_n
$$
and let $V_2$ be the subgroup of $V_1$ represented by elements of the form $(v^a - 1)/(v - 1)$ (not just positive). Then there is an isomorphism
$$
V_2 \cong \mathbb{Z}[E_n]
$$
of $\mathbb{Z}[G]$-modules, and consequently $V_2^1$ is free of rank $1$ in $\mathbb{Z}[E_n]^1$.
