The theorem follows directly from cyclotomic unit relation CU 2. For $a \neq 0$, the distribution condition $\prod_{b: b^p = a} f(b) = f(a)$ is precisely the product relation $\prod (\zeta - 1) = p$ reformulated in the language of distributions on $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$. The factor group $V$ is taken modulo roots of unity to eliminate the ambiguity from the choice of primitive root.
