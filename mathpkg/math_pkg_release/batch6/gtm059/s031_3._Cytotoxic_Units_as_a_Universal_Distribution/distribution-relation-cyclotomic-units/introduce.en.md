Theorem 3.1 of Lang's Chapter 3 establishes that cyclotomic units, modulo roots of unity, form a distribution on $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$ satisfying the universal distribution relation. Given an element $a \in \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$, the product over all $b$ with $pb = a$ of the cyclotomic unit $f(b)$ recovers $f(a)$. This is a direct consequence of the cyclotomic unit relations CU 2.
