The logarithmic derivative of the zeta function recovers the sequence of point counts $N_\nu$ over all finite extensions of the base field $\mathbf{F}_q$. This relationship is the key to extracting arithmetic information from the zeta function. For the Fermat curve, the explicit factorization of the zeta function in terms of Jacobi sums then yields a concrete description of the point-counting generating function.
