Let $w$ be the number of roots of unity in $\mathbf{Q}(\zeta_m)$. Let $S$ be the Stickelberger ideal in $R = \mathbf{Z}[G]$ and let $R^- = (1-e)R$ be the minus part. Then
$$[R^- : S] = w.$$
Equivalently, the index of the Stickelberger ideal in the minus part of the group ring equals the number of roots of unity in the cyclotomic field.
