This concept is part of the Iwasawa-theoretic study of cyclotomic fields. It relates to the structure of local units, the norm map, Galois cohomology, Lubin-Tate formal groups, or explicit reciprocity laws. These concepts collectively form the technical foundation for the main conjecture of Iwasawa theory and the proof of the $p$-adic class number formula.
