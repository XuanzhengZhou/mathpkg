This proposition enumerates the fundamental algebraic properties of the Kummer pairing symbol for Lubin-Tate formal groups. The pairing is shown to be biadditive (additive in the formal group argument and multiplicative in the field element argument), establishing it as a genuine pairing between the formal group and the multiplicative group of the local field. The kernel on the left is identified as the image of the endomorphism $[\pi^n]_A$, which yields the non-degeneracy statement. These properties are essential for the development of explicit local class field theory, as they allow the Kummer pairing to serve as a computational tool for the reciprocity map and to describe the structure of the maximal abelian extension.
