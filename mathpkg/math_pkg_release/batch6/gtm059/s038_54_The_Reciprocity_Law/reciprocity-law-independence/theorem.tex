\begin{theorem}
The field $K'_{\pi}$ is independent of $\pi$. Let $a \in K^{*}$. Write
$$a = \pi^{k} u$$
for some unit $u$ and some integer $k$. Let $r_{a}$ be the automorphism of $K'_{\pi}$ such that
$$r_{a} = \varphi^{k} \quad \text{on } K^{\mathrm{ur}}$$
$$r_{a} = [u^{-1}]_{f} \quad \text{on } K_{\pi}$$
where $\varphi$ denotes the Frobenius automorphism on the maximal unramified extension $K^{\mathrm{ur}}$ and $[u^{-1}]_{f}$ is the endomorphism of the Lubin-Tate formal group corresponding to $u^{-1}$. Then the association $a \mapsto r_{a}$ is independent of the choice of uniformizer $\pi$.
\end{theorem}
