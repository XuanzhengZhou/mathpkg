*Proof.* The result follows from the preceding lemmas. By Lemma 4, a finitely generated $A$-module whose reduction modulo $T$ is free over $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is quasi-isomorphic to $A$. The exact sequence
$$0 \to U(x) \to A \to B \to 0$$
together with Lemma 5 shows that $B = 0$ for the given choice of $i$. Lemma 1 provides the explicit construction of a generating element via the projective system $\zeta_c$, and the Nakayama lemma argument shows that $U_c(\theta)$ is generated by this element over $U_c$. Combining these facts yields that $U_i^c$ is generated by the constructed element over the full Iwasawa algebra $A$.
