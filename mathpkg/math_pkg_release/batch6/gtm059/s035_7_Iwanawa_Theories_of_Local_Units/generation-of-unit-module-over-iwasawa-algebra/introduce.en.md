This theorem is a central structural result in the Iwasawa theory of local units. It states that for each $p$-adic character $x$, the corresponding component $U_i^c$ of the local unit module is cyclic as a module over the Iwasawa algebra $A = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$. The generator is constructed explicitly as $U_i^c(1+x)$, and the proof relies on the lemmas establishing the quasi-isomorphism properties and the vanishing of certain torsion submodules.
