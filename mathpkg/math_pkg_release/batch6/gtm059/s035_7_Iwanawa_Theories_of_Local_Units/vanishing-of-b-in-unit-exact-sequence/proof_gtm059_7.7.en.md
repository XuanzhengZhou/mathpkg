*Proof.* From the exact sequence
$$0 \to U(x) \to A \to B \to 0$$
we obtain the cohomology sequence. Consider the map
$$U(x) \to U(x^0) \to U(x^0) \to U(x^0).$$
By taking an element in $B^*$ and analyzing the induced maps, we obtain an injection
$$0 \to B^* \to U(x^0) \cup U(x^0)$$
by Theorem 2.2.

Since $U(x)$ has no torsion (by Lemma 10), it follows that $B^* = 0$ for all $x$. As $B$ is finite, this implies $B = 0$, completing the proof of Theorem 2.1.
