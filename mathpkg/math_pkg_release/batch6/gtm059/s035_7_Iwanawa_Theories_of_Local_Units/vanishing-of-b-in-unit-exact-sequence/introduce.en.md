In the study of local units in Iwasawa theory, one encounters a short exact sequence $0 \to U(x) \to A \to B \to 0$. This lemma asserts that the quotient $B$ vanishes for all characters $x$ different from the trivial character and a distinguished character $x_0$. The vanishing of $B$ is a crucial step in identifying $U(x)$ with the full Iwasawa algebra $A$, thereby establishing the module structure of local units.
