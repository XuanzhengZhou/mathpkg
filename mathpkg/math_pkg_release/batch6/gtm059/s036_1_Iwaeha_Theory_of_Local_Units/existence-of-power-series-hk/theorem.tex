Given a congruence class $a \pmod{p-1}$, there exists a power series $h_k \in \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ such that for all $k$ with $k \equiv a \pmod{p-1}$,
$$1 - p^{k+1} y_k = h_k(\gamma) - (1 - p) \varphi_0^{(k)}.$$
If $a$ is even or $a \equiv 0 \pmod{p-1}$, then one can choose $\lambda$ so that $\varphi_0^{(2^k)}$ is a unit, and consequently $h_k$ is a unit in $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$.
