For each integer $k \ge 1$, the Coleman-Iwasawa homomorphism $\delta_k$ on the group of principal local units $U$ is defined by
$$\delta_k(u) = \frac{1}{\pi^k} \log f_u(X),$$
where $f_u$ is the Coleman power series associated to the norm-compatible system determined by $u$, and $\log$ denotes the Iwasawa logarithm on the formal group over $\mathbb{Z}_p$.
