Let $U_n$ be the group of principal local units in the $n$-th layer of the $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. Define
$$V_n = \overline{E_n} \quad \text{(closure of $E_n$ in $U_n$)},$$
where $E_n$ is the group of cyclotomic units. Let
$$V = \varprojlim_n V_n$$
be the projective limit with respect to the norm maps. Then $V_n$ is an $\mathbb{A}(\overline{U}_n)$-module, and $V$ is an $\mathbb{A}$-module over the Iwasawa algebra $\mathbb{A} = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$.
