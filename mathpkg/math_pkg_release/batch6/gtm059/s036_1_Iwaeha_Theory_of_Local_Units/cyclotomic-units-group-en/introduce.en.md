The group of cyclotomic units $E_n$ is a subgroup of the local principal units $U_n$ at the $n$-th level of the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. It is generated by roots of unity and the quotients $(1 - \zeta_n^a)/(1 - \zeta_n)$, which are algebraic integers cyclotomic in nature. These units form a norm-compatible system and play a central role in Iwasawa theory. The closure of $E_n$ in $U_n$, denoted $V_n$, and its projective limit $V = \varprojlim V_n$, provide the algebraic framework for studying the relationship between cyclotomic units and the full unit group in $p$-adic settings. The index $[U_n : V_n]$ is related to deep arithmetic invariants such as $p$-adic $L$-functions.
