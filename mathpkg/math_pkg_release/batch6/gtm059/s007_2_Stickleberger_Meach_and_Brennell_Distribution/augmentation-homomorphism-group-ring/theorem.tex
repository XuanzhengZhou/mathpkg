Let $k$ be an integer $\geq 2$ and let $N = p^n$ be a prime power with $p \geq 3$. Set

$$G = \mathbf{Z}(N)^* \quad \text{if } k \text{ is odd},$$
$$G = \mathbf{Z}(N)^*/\pm 1 \quad \text{if } k \text{ is even},$$

and let $R = R_G = \mathbf{Z}[G]$, $R_p = \mathbf{Z}_p[G]$.

The augmentation homomorphism $\deg: R \to \mathbf{Z}$ is defined by

$$\deg\left( \sum_{a \in G} n_a \sigma_a \right) = \sum_{a \in G} n_a.$$
