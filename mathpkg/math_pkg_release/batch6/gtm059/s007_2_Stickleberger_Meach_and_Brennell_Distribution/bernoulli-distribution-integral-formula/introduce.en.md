Property E.1 expresses the integralized Bernoulli distribution $E_{1,c}^{(N)}$ for $k=1$ as an explicit linear combination of fractional part functions. The formula involves $\langle x/N \rangle$, the smallest non-negative real representative of $x/N$ modulo $\mathbb{Z}$, together with the transformed version under multiplication by $c^{-1}$. This explicit form is the foundation for proving integrality properties of the higher-k distributions.
