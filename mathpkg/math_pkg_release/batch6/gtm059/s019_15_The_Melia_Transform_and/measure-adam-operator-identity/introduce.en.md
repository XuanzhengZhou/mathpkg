This proposition states an identity relating the adjoint operator $a^{*}$ applied to a measure on $\mathbb{Z}_p^{\times}$ with the action of the $D$ operator on power series. If $g = U\mu$ is a measure and $Dh = g$ for a power series $h$, then $a^{*}\mu_X(0) = \mu_X(0)$. The result is used in the study of the Mellin transform to relate measures and their associated power series under integral transforms.
