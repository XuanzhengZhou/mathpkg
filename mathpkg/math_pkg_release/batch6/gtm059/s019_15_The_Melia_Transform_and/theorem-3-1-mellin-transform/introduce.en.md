Theorem 3.1 establishes that if $g$ belongs to the space $\Omega(X)$ and satisfies the relation $U_X = g$, and if $h$ is a power series whose image under the operator $D$ equals $g$, then $h$ also belongs to $\Omega(X)$. Moreover, the Mellin transform $\Gamma_h$ of the power series $h$ coincides with the functional $M_{\mu_0}$ associated to the measure $\mu_0$. This result is fundamental for connecting power series representations with measure-theoretic formulations of $p$-adic $L$-functions.
