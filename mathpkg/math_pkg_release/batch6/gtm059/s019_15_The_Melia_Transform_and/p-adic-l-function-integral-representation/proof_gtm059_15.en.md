The first assertion follows from the explicit values given at integer positions of the form $1$, $4$, or $8$ as described. For these, we have:

$$M_L(z_k)(k) = \int_0^{z_k} \left( y^2 - (y)^2 (y)^2 - dz_k(y) \right)$$

$$= \int_0^{z_k} \left( y^2 - (y)^2 (y)^2 - dz_k(y) \right)$$

$$= \left( 1 - y z_k - (y)^2 (y)^2 \right) \frac{1}{2} \int_0^{z_k} (y)(y) dz_k + y.$$

This proves the theorem.
