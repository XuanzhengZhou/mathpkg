This lemma describes the relationship between two variants $J$ and $I$ of the Stickelberger ideal in the integral group ring $\mathbb{Z}[G]$ of a cyclotomic Galois group. The decomposition $J = I + \mathbb{Z}$ and the power relation $(J+I)^e = p^{e-1}J^e$ are essential for computing indices of Stickelberger ideals, which in turn control class groups of cyclotomic fields.
