This lemma describes how the Stickelberger sum $S_\chi(N)$ transforms when the level $N$ changes by a prime factor. When a new prime $p$ is introduced that does not divide the conductor $m$, the sum picks up a factor of $(1 - \chi(p))$. This is the Euler-factor-like behavior of the Stickelberger distribution.
