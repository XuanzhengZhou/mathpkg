For odd $k$, the Stickelberger ideal in the twisted case satisfies

$$
(R : R \cap R') = N\, p^{n+1} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{1}{i} B_{n,i},
$$

where $R$ is the Stickelberger ring, $R'$ is the twisted variant, and $B_{n,i}$ are the corresponding generalized Bernoulli numbers. More precisely,

$$
(R : R) = \left(\frac{r : R}{r : R}\right) B_{n,i},
$$

and

$$
(r : R : x : R) = \prod_{i=1}^{n} B_{n,i} (R : r : R).
$$
