The fundamental identity of Bernoulli polynomials relates the finite difference $B_k(X+1) - B_k(X)$ to the monomial $X^{k-1}$. This identity is the defining characteristic of Bernoulli polynomials and is essential for studying Stickelberger ideals and their relationship with Bernoulli numbers in cyclotomic fields.
