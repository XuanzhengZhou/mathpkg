This theorem shows that the Stickelberger distribution is naturally a projective limit over group algebras of cyclotomic Galois groups. This projective limit perspective connects the Stickelberger distribution to Iwasawa modules and is essential for understanding its relationship with the class group of the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension.
