The Stickelberger distribution is a fundamental object in Iwasawa theory and the arithmetic of cyclotomic fields. Following Kubert-Lang, it is defined as a distribution on $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$ satisfying a specific distribution relation. This distribution encodes the Stickelberger elements at all levels simultaneously and is the key to understanding the universal properties of Stickelberger ideals.
