Let $k$ be an even positive integer and let $\chi$ be an even Dirichlet character with conductor $f$. Then the index of the Stickelberger ideal satisfies

$$
(R_{k,\chi} : R_{0,\chi}) = N^{k-1} \prod_{p \mid N} \frac{1}{k} B_{k,\chi},
$$

where $R_{k,\chi}$ denotes the Stickelberger ring component of weight $k$ corresponding to $\chi$, $R_{0,\chi}$ is the component of weight $0$, and $N$ is the level. The index is computed using the generalized Bernoulli numbers $B_{k,\chi}$ of Leopoldt.
