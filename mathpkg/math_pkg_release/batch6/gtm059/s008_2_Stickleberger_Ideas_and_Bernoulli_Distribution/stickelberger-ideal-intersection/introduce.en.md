This theorem characterizes the intersection of the Stickelberger ideal $R_k$ (indexed by the weight $k$) with the full Stickelberger ring $R$. The result shows that the intersection equals $R^{(\infty)}$, the set of elements that are locally in the Stickelberger ring at every prime. This structural result is fundamental for comparing Stickelberger ideals at different levels.
