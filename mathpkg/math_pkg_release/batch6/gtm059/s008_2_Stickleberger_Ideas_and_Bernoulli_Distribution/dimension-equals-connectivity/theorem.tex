Let $\chi$ be a character of $G(N) = (\mathbb{Z}/N\mathbb{Z})^\times$ with conductor $m = m(\chi)$. Define $S_Z(N) = S_Z(\chi) + S_Z(a)$ where $z_\chi$ is the character on $G(N)$ determined by $\chi$. Let $\mathcal{G}_Z(N)$ be the set of characters such that $S_Z(\chi) = 0$.

Then the dimension of the space $A$ spanned by the elements $u(\chi)$ (with $\chi \in \mathcal{G}_Z(N)$) equals the connectivity of the graph $\mathcal{G}_Z(N)$.
