This concept defines the index $(A : B)$ for two lattices $A$ and $B$ in a finite-dimensional $\mathbb{Q}$-vector space. The index is well-defined and independent of the ambient lattice $C$ chosen. It generalizes the usual group index and satisfies natural multiplicative properties, making it a fundamental tool for comparing Stickelberger ideals at different levels in the group ring $\mathbb{Z}[G]$.
