Theorem 1.2 (also known as the Iwasawa structure theorem for $\Lambda$-modules) gives the asymptotic formula $s_n(V) = \mu p^n + \lambda n + \nu$ for the $p$-adic valuation of the order of the finite modules $V_n = V/(\gamma^{p^n}-1)V$. The invariants $\mu, \lambda, \nu$ are fundamental in Iwasawa theory: $\mu$ is the $\mu$-invariant, $\lambda$ the $\lambda$-invariant, and $\nu$ is a constant. Applied to the $p$-primary part of the ideal class group in a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension, this yields the famous Iwasawa class number formula.
