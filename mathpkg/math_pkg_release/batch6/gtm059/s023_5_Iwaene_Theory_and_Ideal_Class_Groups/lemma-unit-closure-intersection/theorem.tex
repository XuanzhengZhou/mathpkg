Let $K$ be a number field. For each prime $\mathfrak{p}$ above $p$, let $U_{\mathfrak{p}}^{(1)}$ be the group of principal local units. Let $E$ be the group of global units of $K$, embedded diagonally into the product of local unit groups. Then
$$\overline{E} \cap \prod_{\mathfrak{p} \mid p} U_{\mathfrak{p}}^{(1)} = \overline{E} \cap \prod_{\mathfrak{p}} U_{\mathfrak{p}},$$
where the bar denotes closure in the $p$-adic topology. That is, the closure of the global units in the product of local principal units equals their closure in the product of all local units.
