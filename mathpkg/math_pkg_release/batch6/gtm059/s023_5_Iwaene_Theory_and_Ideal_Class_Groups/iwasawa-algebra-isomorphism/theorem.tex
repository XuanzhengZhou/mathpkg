Let $p$ be a prime, let $\Gamma \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$, and let $\gamma$ be a topological generator of $\Gamma$. Set $X = \gamma - 1$. Then the natural homomorphism

$$\varphi: \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]] \to \Lambda = \varprojlim_n \mathbb{Z}_p[\Gamma/\Gamma^{p^n}]$$

is an isomorphism of topological $\mathbb{Z}_p$-algebras.
