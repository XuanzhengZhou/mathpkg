Let $A$ be a complete local ring with maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$, and let $f \in A[[X]]$ be a power series not all of whose coefficients lie in $\mathfrak{m}$. The integer $n$ appearing in Theorems 2.1 and 2.2 (the smallest index such that $a_n$ is a unit) is called the \textbf{Weierstrass degree} of $f$, denoted $\deg_W(f)$.
