The Weierstrass degree of a non-zero power series $f$ over a complete local ring is the smallest index $n$ such that the coefficient $a_n$ is a unit. It measures the "order of vanishing" modulo the maximal ideal and determines the degree of the distinguished polynomial in the Weierstrass Preparation Theorem. This concept is essential for the structure theory of $\Lambda$-modules.
