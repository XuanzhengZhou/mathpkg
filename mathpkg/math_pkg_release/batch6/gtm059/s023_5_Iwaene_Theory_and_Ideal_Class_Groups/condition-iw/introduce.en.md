Condition IW (the Iwasawa condition) is a hypothesis on a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension requiring that only finitely many primes ramify and that every ramified prime is totally ramified. This condition is often satisfied in practice (e.g., the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension satisfies it) and greatly simplifies the study of Galois modules and ideal class groups in the tower.
