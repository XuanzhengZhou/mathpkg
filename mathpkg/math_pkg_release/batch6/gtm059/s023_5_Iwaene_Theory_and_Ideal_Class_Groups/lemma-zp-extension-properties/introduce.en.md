This lemma collects basic facts about ramification in $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions. It shows that condition IW (finitely many totally ramified primes) is automatically satisfied by the ramification behavior, and that any non-trivial $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension must ramify at at least one prime. These properties are fundamental for the study of ideal class groups in $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions.
