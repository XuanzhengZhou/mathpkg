Let $M$ be a compact (or equivalently, finitely generated) $\Lambda$-module, where $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$. Let $\mathfrak{m} = (p, X)$ be the maximal ideal of $\Lambda$. If $M/\mathfrak{m}M$ is finite (respectively, zero), then $M$ is finitely generated (respectively, zero) as a $\Lambda$-module.
