The Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[\Gamma]]$ is the completed group ring of the $p$-adic group $\Gamma \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$. It is isomorphic to the power series ring $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ via the choice of a topological generator. This algebra is the fundamental ring over which Iwasawa modules are studied. In Iwasawa theory, class groups, unit groups, and Galois groups of $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions are naturally $\Lambda$-modules, and their structure as $\Lambda$-modules encodes deep arithmetic information.
