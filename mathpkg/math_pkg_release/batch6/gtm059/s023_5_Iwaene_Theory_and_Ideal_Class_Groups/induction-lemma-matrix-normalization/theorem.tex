Suppose that a relation matrix $R$ over $\Lambda$ is in $(r-1)$-normal form (i.e., its first $r-1$ rows are diagonalized with distinguished polynomials on the diagonal), and that $R$ has at least $r$ rows. Then $R$ can be transformed by admissible operations (O1, O2, O3) into an $r$-normal form, preserving the first $r-1$ diagonal entries.
