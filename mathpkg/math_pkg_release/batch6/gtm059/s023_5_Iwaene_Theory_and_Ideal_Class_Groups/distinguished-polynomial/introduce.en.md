A distinguished polynomial over a local ring is a monic polynomial whose non-leading coefficients lie in the maximal ideal. In the Iwasawa algebra $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$, these are polynomials like $X^n + p c_{n-1} X^{n-1} + \dots + p c_0$. Distinguished polynomials play a fundamental role in the structure theory of finitely generated $\Lambda$-modules, appearing as the irreducible factors in the quasi-isomorphism decomposition (analogous to elementary divisors).
