The Weierstrass preparation theorem is a fundamental result in the theory of formal power series over complete local rings. It generalizes the classical Weierstrass preparation theorem from complex analysis. In Iwasawa theory, it is applied to the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ to prove the structure theorem for finitely generated $\Lambda$-modules. The theorem allows any power series to be factored as a distinguished polynomial times a unit, providing an analog of the division algorithm essential for the classification of $\Lambda$-modules.
