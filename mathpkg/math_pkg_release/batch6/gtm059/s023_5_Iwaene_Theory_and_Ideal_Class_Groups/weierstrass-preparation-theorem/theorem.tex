Let $A$ be a complete local ring with maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$. Let $f(X) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_k X^k \in A[[X]]$ be a power series such that not all coefficients lie in $\mathfrak{m}$. Let $n$ be the smallest integer such that $a_n \notin \mathfrak{m}$. Then $f$ can be uniquely written as

$$f(X) = (X^n + b_{n-1}X^{n-1} + \cdots + b_0) \cdot u(X)$$

where $b_i \in \mathfrak{m}$ for all $i$ and $u(X) \in A[[X]]^\times$ is a unit. The polynomial factor is called a distinguished polynomial of Weierstrass degree $n$.
