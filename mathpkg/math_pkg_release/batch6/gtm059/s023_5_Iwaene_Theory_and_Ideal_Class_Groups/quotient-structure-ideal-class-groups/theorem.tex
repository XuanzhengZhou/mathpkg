Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension satisfying condition IW. Let $C_n$ be the $p$-primary part of the ideal class group of $K_n$. Then there exists a subgroup $U_n \subset C_n$ such that the norm map induces an exact sequence relating $C_n$ and $C_{n-1}$, and under certain conditions,
$$C_n \cong C_1 / U_n$$
as modules over the Iwasawa algebra, where $U_n$ is determined by the action of the Galois group.
