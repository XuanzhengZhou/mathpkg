Assume condition IW is satisfied with a single ramified prime. Let $K_{\infty}/K$ be a $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension with Galois group $\Gamma \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$. Let $M_n$ be the maximal abelian unramified $p$-extension of $K_n$ (the $p$-Hilbert class field), and let $M_{\infty} = \bigcup_n M_n$. Let
$$G = \operatorname{Gal}(M_{\infty}/K), \quad G_n = \operatorname{Gal}(M_{\infty}/K_n).$$
Let $I$ be the inertia group of the ramified prime in $G$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $G$ is a semidirect product: $G = G_1 \rtimes I$, and the restriction of $I$ to $K_{\infty}$ gives an isomorphism $I \cong \Gamma$.
\item[(ii)] The commutator subgroup $[G, G] = G_1$.
\end{enumerate}
