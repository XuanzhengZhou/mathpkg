For each $n$, standard Kummer theory provides the pairing between $\operatorname{Gal}(K_n(A_n^{1/p^n})/K_n)$ and $A_n$ where $A_n$ is the $p^n$-torsion of $K_n^{\times}$ modulo $p^n$-th powers. The compatibility of the pairings for different $n$ follows from the norm-compatibility of the Kummer extensions. The involution arises because the cyclotomic character gives a natural isomorphism between the Tate module and $\mathbb{Z}_p(1)$, and the action of $\Gamma$ on roots of unity introduces the involution $\iota$.
