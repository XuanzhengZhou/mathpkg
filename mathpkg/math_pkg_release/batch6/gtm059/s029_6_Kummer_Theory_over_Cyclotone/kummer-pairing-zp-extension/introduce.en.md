The Kummer pairing provides the fundamental link between Galois groups and multiplicative groups in the context of $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions. At each finite level $n$, it pairs the Galois group of the maximal $p$-abelian extension with the $p^n$-torsion of the multiplicative group modulo $p^n$-th powers. Passing to the projective limit, one obtains a perfect pairing of compact $\Lambda$-modules. The involution on $\Lambda$ that arises encodes the action of complex conjugation in the cyclotomic setting.
