In the study of cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions, two maximal $p$-abelian extensions are of central importance: the maximal $p$-abelian unramified extension $L(K)$ (whose Galois group is related to the $p$-class group) and the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension $M(K)$ (unramified outside $p$, whose Galois group involves units). Kummer theory over the cyclotomic tower provides the framework for analyzing these extensions and their Iwasawa modules.
