This theorem establishes the fundamental module structure of the Galois groups arising from maximal $p$-abelian extensions over the layers of a cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. The Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$ acts naturally on these Galois groups via the Galois action of the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension. Both $\operatorname{Gal}(Q_n/K_n)$ and $\operatorname{Gal}(D_n/K_n)$ are shown to be $\Lambda$-modules, which is the starting point for Iwasawa's structure theory of these groups.
