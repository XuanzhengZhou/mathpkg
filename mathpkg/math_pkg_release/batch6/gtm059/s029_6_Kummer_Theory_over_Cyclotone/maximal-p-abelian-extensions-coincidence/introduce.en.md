In the Kummer theory over cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions, one studies various maximal $p$-abelian extensions of the layers $K_n$. This lemma identifies two such extensions: the maximal $p$-abelian extension $Q_n$ and the maximal $p$-abelian $p$-ramified extension $D_n$. The proof uses Kummer theory to show that both are composites of cyclic extensions and that the ideal class constraints force their equality.
