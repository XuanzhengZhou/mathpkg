Let $u_i$ be elements for $i = 1, \dots, q^{q-1}$ such that with these indices the $u_i$ generate $d_i(\mathfrak{n})^* \pmod{\mathfrak{m}^{q-1}}$ over $\mathfrak{n}$.

Then the elements $a_i$ with $i \in I$ generate $A(\mathfrak{a}_i)$ modulo $\mathfrak{m}^{q-1}[A(\mathfrak{a}_i)]$ over $I$. Moreover, they are linearly independent modulo $\mathfrak{m}^{q-1}$.
