This theorem establishes that certain elements $u_i$ generate the formal $A$-module $A(\mathfrak{a}_i)$ modulo a power of the maximal ideal, and that these generators are linearly independent in the same quotient. The result is a structural generation theorem for Lubin-Tate formal modules, providing a basis-like description of the module in terms of elements with controlled $\pi$-adic valuations. The proof proceeds by induction and case analysis on the valuation of coefficients.
