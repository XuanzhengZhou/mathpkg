Let $K$ be a finite extension of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ and let $F$ be a Lubin-Tate formal group over $\mathcal{O}_K$ relative to a uniformizer $\pi$. Let $K_{\pi} = \bigcup_n K(F[\pi^n])$ be the field generated by the $\pi^n$-torsion points of $F$. Then $K_{\pi}/K$ is a totally ramified abelian extension, and the Artin map induces an isomorphism

$$\mathcal{O}_K^{\times} \xrightarrow{\sim} \operatorname{Gal}(K_{\pi}/K).$$
