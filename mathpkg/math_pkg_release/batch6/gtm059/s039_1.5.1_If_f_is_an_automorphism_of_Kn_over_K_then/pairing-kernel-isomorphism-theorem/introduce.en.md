This theorem describes the kernel of the norm residue pairing in the Lubin-Tate setting when the $p$-th roots of unity are not contained in $K(A_1)$. Using the order computation of $K^\times / K^{\times p^n}$ from local algebraic number theory, the theorem shows that the left and right kernels of the pairing coincide, establishing a duality isomorphism. This is a key step in the Lubin-Tate explicit construction of local class field theory.
