This lemma shows that the quotient modules $A_n(\mathfrak{a})[\pi_i]^n[\mathfrak{a}]^n$ are independent of the choice of the prime element $\pi_i$ in $K_n$, provided $\mathfrak{a} \neq \mathfrak{p}$. It establishes that near the origin, the operator $[\mathfrak{a}]$ acts with a regularity that makes these quotient structures canonical. This result is a technical tool used in analyzing the structure of the formal group module.
