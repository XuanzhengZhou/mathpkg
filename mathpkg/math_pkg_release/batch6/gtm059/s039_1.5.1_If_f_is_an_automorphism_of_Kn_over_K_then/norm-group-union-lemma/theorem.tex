Let $K$ be a local field and let $K_n$ be the Lubin-Tate tower over $K$. Let $N_k$ denote the norm from $K_k$ to $K$, and let $v$ be a unit in $K$. Define

$$N_k^*(v^*) = \{\text{group of elements of } v^* \text{ whose norm } N_k \text{ is a power of } v\}.$$

Then

$$N_k^*(v^*) = \bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty} N_k(v^*).$$
