This lemma characterizes the subgroup of elements in a local field $K$ that are universal norms from the Lubin-Tate tower. Specifically, it identifies the set of elements each of whose norms down to $K$ from successive layers $K_k$ is a power of a fixed unit $v$. The statement equates this set with the union of the norm images $N_k(v^*)$ over all layers $k$, providing a structural description crucial for the norm-coherent sequences used in Lubin-Tate theory.
