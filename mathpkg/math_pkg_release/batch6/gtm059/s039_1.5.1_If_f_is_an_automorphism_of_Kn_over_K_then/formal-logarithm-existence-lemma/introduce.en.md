This lemma establishes the existence of the formal logarithm for a Lubin-Tate formal group. The logarithm $\lambda(X)$ is defined as the limit of $\pi^{-n}[\pi^n](X)$ and provides a formal isomorphism from the Lubin-Tate group to the additive formal group. The limit is understood coefficient-wise: for each degree $k$, the coefficient $c_k^{(n)}$ of $X^k$ in $\pi^{-n}[\pi^n](X)$ converges $p$-adically to a limit $c_k$, and the resulting power series $\lambda(X) = \sum c_k X^k$ is a formal group isomorphism.
