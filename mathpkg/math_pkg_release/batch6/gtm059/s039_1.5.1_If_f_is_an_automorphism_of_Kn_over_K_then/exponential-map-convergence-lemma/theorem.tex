Let $e(X)$ be the power series (with coefficients in $K$) which is the inverse of the formal logarithm $\lambda(X)$. Let $D$ be the disc in the maximal ideal consisting of those elements $z$ such that

$$\operatorname{ord}_\pi z > \frac{1}{q-1}.$$

Then $e(X)$ converges on this disc, and induces the inverse isomorphism to $\lambda$ on the groups:

$$A(D) \xrightarrow{\lambda} G_a(D), \qquad G_a(D) \xrightarrow{e} A(D).$$

For $z$ in this disc we have

$$\operatorname{ord}_\pi z = \operatorname{ord}_\pi \lambda(z) = \operatorname{ord}_\pi e(z).$$
