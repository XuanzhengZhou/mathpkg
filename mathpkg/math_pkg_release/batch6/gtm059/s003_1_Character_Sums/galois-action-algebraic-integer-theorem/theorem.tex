Let $\alpha$ be an algebraic integer in $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)$ and let $u \equiv 1 \pmod{m}$ be an integer. Let $w(\alpha, u)$ be the associated algebraic number constructed from character sums. Then $w(\alpha, u) = 1$.

Equivalently, for the action of the Galois group of $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)/\mathbb{Q}$ on the algebraic integers, the elements congruent to $1$ modulo $m$ act trivially on the associated character sum values.
