A technical summation formula used in the proof of the Gauss sum factorization theorem. The lemma expresses $t(k)$ as $(p-1)$ times the sum of $k/q^{j-1}$ over $j=0,\dots,n-1$, where $p$ is a prime and $q$ is a power related to the cyclotomic field. The formula is proved by noting that both sides are $(q-1)$-periodic in the relevant parameter and checking the congruence modulo $q-1$, reducing the verification to a finite computation.
