Let $\mathfrak{P}$ be the prime ideal in $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)$ lying above $p$. Let $\mathfrak{a}$ as before be the Teichmüller character. Let $\chi = \chi_{\mathfrak{a}}$.

The Gauss sum $S(\chi\chi^{*-1})$ has the factorization

$$(S(\chi\chi^{*-1})) = \mathfrak{P}^{p(\chi^*) + q(\chi^*) - p(\chi^*)^{\,*}} = \mathfrak{p}^{p(\chi^*)^*}.$$
