Let $\omega$ be the Teichmüller character. The Stickelberger element of level $n$ is defined as

$$\theta(n) = \sum_{\substack{a=0 \\ (a,m)=1}}^{m-1} \left\langle \frac{a}{m} \right\rangle \sigma_a^{-1}$$

where $\langle x \rangle$ denotes the fractional part of $x$, and $\sigma_a \in \operatorname{Gal}(\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_m)/\mathbb{Q})$ is the automorphism sending $\zeta_m \mapsto \zeta_m^a$.
