A technical lemma describing the structure of character eigenspaces in the group ring $\mathbb{Z}[G]$ when $m=p$ is prime. The $\chi$-eigenspace $t_{\chi}$ is defined as the ideal generated by $\sigma_a - \chi(a)$. The lemma determines its value depending on whether $\chi$ is a low power of the Teichmüller character (case i) or a non-trivial character distinct from the Teichmüller character (case ii). This classification is used in subsequent sections to analyze the Stickelberger ideal and Bernoulli distributions.
