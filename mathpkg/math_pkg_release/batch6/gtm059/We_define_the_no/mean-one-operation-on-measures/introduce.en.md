The Mean 1 operation is one of the operations on $p$-adic measures whose effect on the associated power series is given by composing with the formal group addition law $X [+] Y = (1+X)(1+Y) - 1$. For a fixed element $x$, the measure $\psi$ whose power series is $g(X) = f(X [+] x)$ is denoted as the Mean 1 operation. These operations, together with Mean 0 and others, form the framework for translating between operations on measures and operations on power series, which is central to the construction of the $p$-adic $L$-function in Lang's cyclotomic theory.
