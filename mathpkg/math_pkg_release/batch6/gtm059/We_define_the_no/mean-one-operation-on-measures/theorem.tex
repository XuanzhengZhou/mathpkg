Define the formal group addition

$$X [+] Y = X + Y + XY = (1 + X)(1 + Y) - 1.$$

Let $\psi$ be the measure whose associated power series is $g$, and let $f$ be the power series associated with a measure $\mu$. The Mean 1 operation is defined by

$$\psi = \psi_0 \quad \text{where} \quad g(X) = f(X [+] x)$$

for a fixed $x \in \mathbb{Z}_p$.
