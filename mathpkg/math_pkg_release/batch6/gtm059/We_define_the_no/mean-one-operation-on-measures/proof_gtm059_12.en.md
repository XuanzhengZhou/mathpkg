For $w \in \mathbb{Z}_p$, we have

$$\int \psi_w \, d\psi_0(u) = \int (1 + w)^u d\psi_0(u) = \int (1 + w + u + wu) \, d\psi_0(u)$$

which follows from expanding the formal group addition $(1+w)(1+u) - 1 = w + u + wu$.

The property is then clear from the definitions: the measure $\psi$ corresponding to the power series $g(X) = f(X [+] x)$ satisfies the stated integral identity by the relationship between measures and power series established in Theorem 1.2.
