Let $u$ be a step function on $\mathbb{Z}_p$, constant on the cosets of $p^N \mathbb{Z}_p$. Write the Fourier expansion

$$\psi(u) = \sum_{j=1}^{p^N} \phi_j(u) (1 + X)^j - 1.$$

Equivalently, the Fourier coefficients are given by

$$\phi_j(u) = \frac{1}{p^N} \sum_{k=1}^{p^N} \psi_k(u) \zeta^{-jk}$$

where $\zeta$ is a primitive $p^N$-th root of unity.
