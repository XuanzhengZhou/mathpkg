The Mean 0 operation is a fundamental integration formula in Lang's theory of $p$-adic measures. It states that integrating the constant function $1$ against the measure $\mu$ yields the value of the associated power series $f$ evaluated at $0$. This is a special case of Theorem 1.2 (the integration formula with $z = 0$) and serves as a baseline operation in the correspondence between operations on measures and operations on power series.
