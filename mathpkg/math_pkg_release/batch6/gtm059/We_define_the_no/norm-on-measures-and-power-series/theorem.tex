Let $f$ be a $p$-adic measure on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ with associated power series, and let $\mu$ be the associated measure. Define the sup norms:

$$|f| = \sup_{x \in \mathbb{Z}_p} |\mu(x)|, \qquad |x| = \sup_i |x_i|$$

where $x_i$ are the values of the measure on the cosets, and $|x|$ denotes the sup norm as defined previously.
