Let $U_n$ denote the group of local principal units in the cyclotomic field $K_n = \mathbb{Q}_p(\zeta_{p^n})$, i.e., units congruent to $1$ modulo the maximal ideal. The groups $U_n$ form a projective system under the norm maps $N_{m,n}: U_m \to U_n$ for $m \ge n$. The projective limit
$$U = \varprojlim_{n} U_n = \left\{ u = (u_n)_{n \ge 1} \mid u_n \in U_n,\ N_{m,n}(u_m) = u_n \text{ for } m \ge n \right\}$$
is a compact $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module and also carries the structure of a module over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[\Gamma]] \cong \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$, where $\Gamma = \operatorname{Gal}(K_\infty/K_1) \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$.
