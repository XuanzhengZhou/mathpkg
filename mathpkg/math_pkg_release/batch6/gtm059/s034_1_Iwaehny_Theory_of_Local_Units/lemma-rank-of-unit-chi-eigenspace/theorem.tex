Let $\chi$ be a character of $G_n = \operatorname{Gal}(K_n/K_1)$ with $\chi \neq 1$. Let $U_n(\chi)$ denote the $\chi$-eigenspace of the local principal units $U_n$ in $K_n = \mathbb{Q}_p(\zeta_{p^n})$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\operatorname{rank}_{\mathbb{Z}_p} U_n(\chi) = p^n$.
\item If $\chi \neq \omega$ (the Teichm\"uller character), then $U_n(\chi)$ is a free $\mathbb{Z}_p$-module, and $U_n(\chi) \subseteq \mathbb{Z}_p^{p^n}$.
\end{enumerate}
