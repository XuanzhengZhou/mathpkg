This is one of the cornerstone results of Iwasawa theory for local fields: the $\chi$-eigenspace $U(\chi)$ of the projective limit of local principal unit groups is a free module of rank $1$ over the Iwasawa algebra $\Lambda \cong \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$. The theorem provides a complete structural description of the local unit tower at the level of projective limits. Its proof occupies a substantial portion of this chapter and relies on a series of lemmas establishing torsion-freeness properties, rank calculations for the finite-level eigenspaces, and the construction of an explicit $\Lambda$-basis element. The result is the local analogue of the corresponding global statement for ideal class groups in the cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extension and is intimately connected with the existence and properties of $p$-adic $L$-functions.
