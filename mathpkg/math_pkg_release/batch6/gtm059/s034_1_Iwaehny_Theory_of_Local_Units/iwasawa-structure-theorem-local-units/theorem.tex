Let $\chi$ be a nontrivial character of $\Delta = \operatorname{Gal}(K_1/\mathbb{Q}_p)$ and let $U(\chi) = \varprojlim_n U_n(\chi)$ be the $\chi$-eigenspace of the projective limit of local principal units. Let $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}_p[[T]]$ be the Iwasawa algebra, isomorphic to the completed group ring $\mathbb{Z}_p[[\Gamma]]$ where $\Gamma = \operatorname{Gal}(K_\infty/K_1) \cong \mathbb{Z}_p$.

Then $U(\chi)$ is a free $\Lambda$-module of rank $1$:
$$U(\chi) \cong \Lambda.$$

In other words, the projective limit of the $\chi$-eigenspaces of the local principal units is a free module of dimension $1$ over the Iwasawa algebra.
