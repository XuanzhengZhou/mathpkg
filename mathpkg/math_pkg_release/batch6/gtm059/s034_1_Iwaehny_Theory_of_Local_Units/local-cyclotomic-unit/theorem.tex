Let $u = (u_1, \dots, u_n)$ be a family of integers (or elements of a local field) satisfying
$$\prod_{i=1}^{n} u_i \equiv 1 \pmod{p} \quad \text{and} \quad \sum_{i=1}^{n} u_i = 0.$$
Define
$$u = u(u_1, \dots, u_n) = \prod_{i=1}^{n} (1 - u_i).$$
Then $u$ is called a \text{local cyclotomic unit}, and satisfies $u \equiv 1 \pmod{p}$.
