Let $f: A \rightarrow B$ be a local homomorphism of local noetherian rings, such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $A/\mathfrak{m}_A \rightarrow B/\mathfrak{m}_B$ is an isomorphism,
\item[(2)] $\mathfrak{m}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{m}_B/\mathfrak{m}_B^2$ is surjective, and
\item[(3)] $B$ is a finitely generated $A$-module.
\end{enumerate}
Then $f$ is surjective.
