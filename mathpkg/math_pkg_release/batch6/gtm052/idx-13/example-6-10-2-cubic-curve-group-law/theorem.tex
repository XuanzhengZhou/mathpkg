Let $X$ be the nonsingular cubic curve $y^2z = x^3 - xz^2$ in $\mathbf{P}_k^2$, with $\text{char } k \neq 2$. Let $\text{Cl}^0 X$ be the kernel of the degree map $\text{Cl} X \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}$. Then there is a natural 1-1 correspondence between the set of closed points of $X$ and the elements of the group $\text{Cl}^0 X$, making $X$ into a group variety.
