Proposition 7.5 establishes that ampleness is stable under tensor powers: $\mathcal{L}$ is ample if and only if some (equivalently, any) positive tensor power of $\mathcal{L}$ is ample. This is a foundational result used throughout the theory of ample sheaves, for instance to show that some power of an ample sheaf is very ample.
