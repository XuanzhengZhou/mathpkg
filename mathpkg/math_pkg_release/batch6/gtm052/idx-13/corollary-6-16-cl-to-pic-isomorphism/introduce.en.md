Corollary 6.16 combines Propositions 6.11 and 6.15 to give the complete chain of isomorphisms: on a sufficiently nice scheme (noetherian, integral, separated, locally factorial), Weil divisors equal Cartier divisors, and Cartier divisors modulo principal divisors equal the Picard group. Thus $\text{Cl} X \cong \text{Pic} X$, unifying the three fundamental notions.
