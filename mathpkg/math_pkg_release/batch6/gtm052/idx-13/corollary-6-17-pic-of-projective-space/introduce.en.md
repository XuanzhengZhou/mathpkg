Corollary 6.17 is one of the most important computations in algebraic geometry: the Picard group of projective space $\mathbf{P}^n_k$ is infinite cyclic, generated by the twisting sheaf $\mathcal{O}(1)$. Combined with Corollary 6.16, this follows from the earlier computation $\text{Cl} \mathbf{P}^n \cong \mathbb{Z}$ (Proposition 6.4).
