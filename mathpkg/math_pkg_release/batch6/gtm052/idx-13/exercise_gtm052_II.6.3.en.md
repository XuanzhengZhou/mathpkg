**Projective Cone.** Let $V \subseteq \mathbf{P}^n$ be a projective variety. The cone over $V$ is the affine variety $\bar{X} \subseteq \mathbf{A}^{n+1}$ obtained by taking the equations of $V$ as equations in the affine coordinates $x_0, \ldots, x_n$. Let $P = (0,\ldots,0)$ be the vertex of the cone, and let $X = \bar{X} - P$.

(a) Show that $\text{Cl} \bar{X} \cong \text{Cl} X$.

(b) We have $V \subseteq \bar{X}$ as the hyperplane section at infinity. Show that the class of $V$ in $\text{Cl} \bar{X}$ is equal to $\pi^*$ (class of $V.H$) where $H$ is any hyperplane of $\mathbf{P}^n$ not containing $V$. Thus conclude using (6.5) that there is an exact sequence
$$0 \rightarrow \mathbb{Z} \rightarrow \text{Cl} V \rightarrow \text{Cl} X \rightarrow 0,$$
where the first arrow sends $1 \mapsto V.H$, and the second is $\pi^*$ followed by restriction to $X-P$ and inclusion in $X$.

(c) Let $S(V)$ be the homogeneous coordinate ring of $V$ (which is also the affine coordinate ring of $X$). Show that $S(V)$ is a unique factorization domain if and only if (1) $V$ is projectively normal (Ex. 5.14), and (2) $\text{Cl} V \cong \mathbb{Z}$ and is generated by the class of $V.H$.

(d) Let $\mathcal{O}_P$ be the local ring of $P$ on $X$. Show that the natural restriction map induces an isomorphism $\text{Cl} X \to \text{Cl}(\text{Spec} \mathcal{O}_P)$.
