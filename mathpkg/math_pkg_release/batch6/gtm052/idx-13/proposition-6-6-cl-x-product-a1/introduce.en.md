Proposition 6.6 shows that taking the product with the affine line $\mathbf{A}^1$ does not change the divisor class group. The isomorphism $\text{Cl} X \cong \text{Cl}(X \times \mathbf{A}^1)$ is a fundamental stability result in the study of Weil divisors on algebraic varieties.
