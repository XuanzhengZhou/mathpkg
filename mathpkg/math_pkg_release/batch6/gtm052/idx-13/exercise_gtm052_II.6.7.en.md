Let $X$ be the nodal cubic curve $y^2z = x^3 + x^2z$ in $\mathbf{P}^2$. Imitate (6.11.4) and show that the group of Cartier divisors of degree 0, $\text{CaCl}^0 X$, is naturally isomorphic to the multiplicative group $\mathbf{G}_m$.
