Example 7.1.1 classifies all automorphisms of projective space $\mathbf{P}^n_k$: they are exactly those induced by linear transformations on the homogeneous coordinates, i.e., elements of $\text{PGL}(n,k)$. This generalizes the well-known result for $\mathbf{P}^1$ and is proved using the fact that $\text{Pic} \mathbf{P}^n \cong \mathbb{Z}$ is generated by $\mathcal{O}(1)$.
