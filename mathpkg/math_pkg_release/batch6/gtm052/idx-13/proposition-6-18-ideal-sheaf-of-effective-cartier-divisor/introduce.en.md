Proposition 6.18 relates the sheaf $\mathcal{L}(D)$ for an effective Cartier divisor to the ideal sheaf of the corresponding closed subscheme. Since $D$ is effective, $\mathcal{L}(-D)$ is a subsheaf of $\mathcal{O}_X$, and it is precisely the ideal sheaf defining the subscheme $Y$ associated to $D$.
