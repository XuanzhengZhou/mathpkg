Remark 6.11.2 provides an alternative characterization of Cartier divisors on normal schemes: they are exactly the Weil divisors that are locally principal. This bridges the gap even when the scheme is not locally factorial, and shows that the obstruction to a Weil divisor being Cartier is precisely the failure of local principality.
