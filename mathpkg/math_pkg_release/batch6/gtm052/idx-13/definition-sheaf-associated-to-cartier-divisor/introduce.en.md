The definition constructs an invertible sheaf $\mathcal{L}(D)$ from a Cartier divisor $D$ by taking the $\mathcal{O}_X$-submodule of the sheaf of total quotient rings generated locally by $f_i^{-1}$. This is the fundamental link between Cartier divisors and invertible sheaves, and is the basis for the correspondence between $\text{CaCl} X$ and $\text{Pic} X$.
