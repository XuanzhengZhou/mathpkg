Let $D$ be a Cartier divisor on a scheme $X$, represented by $\{(U_i, f_i)\}$. The sheaf $\mathcal{L}(D)$ associated to $D$ is the sub-$\mathcal{O}_X$-module of the sheaf of total quotient rings $\mathcal{K}$ generated by $f_i^{-1}$ on $U_i$. This is well-defined since $f_i/f_j$ is invertible on $U_i \cap U_j$, so $f_i^{-1}$ and $f_j^{-1}$ generate the same $\mathcal{O}_X$-module.
