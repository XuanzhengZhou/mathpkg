Let $X$ be the cuspidal cubic curve $y^2z = x^3$ in $\mathbf{P}_k^2$ ($\text{char } k \neq 2$), and let $Z$ be the singular point. Then the group $\text{CaCl}^0 X$ of Cartier divisors of degree 0 is in 1-1 correspondence with the set of closed points of $X - Z$, making $X - Z$ into a group variety isomorphic to $\mathbf{G}_a = \text{Spec } k[t]$.
