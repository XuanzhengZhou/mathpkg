If $\varphi$ is a closed immersion, then $X_i = X \cap U_i$ is a closed subscheme of $U_i$, hence affine, and the corresponding ring map is surjective by (5.10). Conversely, if (1) and (2) hold, then each $X_i$ is a closed subscheme of $U_i$. Since $X_i = \varphi^{-1}(U_i)$ and the $X_i$ cover $X$, it follows that $X$ is a closed subscheme of $\mathbf{P}_A^n$.
