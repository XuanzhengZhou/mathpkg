(a) Show that an irreducible curve $Y$ of degree $d > 1$ in $\mathbf{P}^2$ cannot have a point of multiplicity $\geqslant d$ (Ex. 5.3).

(b) If $Y$ is an irreducible curve of degree $d > 1$ having a point of multiplicity $d - 1$, then $Y$ is a rational curve (Ex. 6.1).
