If $Y \subseteq \mathbf{P}^n$ is an algebraic set of dimension $r$, we define the Hilbert polynomial of $Y$ to be the Hilbert polynomial $P_Y$ of its homogeneous coordinate ring $S(Y)$. It is a polynomial of degree $r$. We define the degree of $Y$, denoted $\deg Y$, to be $r!$ times the leading coefficient of $P_Y$.
