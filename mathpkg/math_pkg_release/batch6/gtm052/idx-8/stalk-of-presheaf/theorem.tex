If $\mathcal{F}$ is a presheaf on $X$, and if $P$ is a point of $X$, we define the \text{stalk} $\mathcal{F}_P$ of $\mathcal{F}$ at $P$ to be the direct limit

$$\mathcal{F}_P = \varinjlim_{U \ni P} \mathcal{F}(U)$$

over the directed system of all open neighborhoods $U$ of $P$, with respect to the restriction maps. An element of $\mathcal{F}_P$ is called a \text{germ} of a section of $\mathcal{F}$ at $P$.
