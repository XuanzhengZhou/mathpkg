Let $Y^r \subseteq \mathbf{P}^n$ be a variety of degree 2. Show that $Y$ is contained in a linear subspace $L$ of dimension $r + 1$ in $\mathbf{P}^n$. Thus $Y$ is isomorphic to a quadric hypersurface in $\mathbf{P}^{r+1}$ (Ex. 5.12).
