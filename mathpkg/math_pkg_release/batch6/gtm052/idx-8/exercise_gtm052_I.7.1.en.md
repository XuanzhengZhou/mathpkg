(a) Find the degree of the $d$-uple embedding of $\mathbf{P}^n$ in $\mathbf{P}^N$ (Ex. 2.12). [Answer: $d^n$]

(b) Find the degree of the Segre embedding of $\mathbf{P}^r \times \mathbf{P}^s$ in $\mathbf{P}^N$ (Ex. 2.14). [Answer: $\binom{r+s}{r}$]
