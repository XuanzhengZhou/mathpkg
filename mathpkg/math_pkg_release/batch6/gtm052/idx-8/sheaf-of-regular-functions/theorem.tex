Let $X$ be a variety over a field $k$. For each open set $U \subseteq X$, let $\mathcal{O}_X(U)$ be the ring of regular functions from $U$ to $k$, and for each $V \subseteq U$, let $\rho_{UV} : \mathcal{O}_X(U) \to \mathcal{O}_X(V)$ be the restriction map. Then $\mathcal{O}_X$ is a sheaf of rings on $X$, called the \text{sheaf of regular functions} on $X$. The sheaf conditions hold because a function which is $0$ locally is $0$, and a function which is regular locally is regular globally.
