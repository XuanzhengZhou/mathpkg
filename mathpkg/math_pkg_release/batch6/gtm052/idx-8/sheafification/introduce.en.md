Sheafification is the universal construction that turns any presheaf into a sheaf. The associated sheaf $\mathcal{F}^+$ is constructed by taking functions from $U$ to the union of stalks that are locally induced by sections of $\mathcal{F}$. This construction satisfies the universal property that any morphism from $\mathcal{F}$ to a sheaf factors uniquely through $\theta: \mathcal{F} \to \mathcal{F}^+$. Sheafification is the left adjoint to the forgetful functor from sheaves to presheaves, making it a fundamental tool in sheaf theory.
