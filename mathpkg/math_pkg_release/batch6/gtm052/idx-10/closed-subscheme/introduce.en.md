A closed subscheme of a scheme $X$ is defined as an equivalence class of closed immersions into $X$, where two closed immersions are equivalent if they differ by an isomorphism commuting with the immersions. This reconciles the fact that a given closed subset may support several distinct scheme structures (differing by nilpotents).
