Reduced Schemes. A scheme $(X, \mathcal{O}_X)$ is reduced if for every open set $U \subseteq X$, the ring $\mathcal{O}_X(U)$ has no nilpotent elements. Show that this is equivalent to the condition that the local rings $\mathcal{O}_P$ for all $P \in X$ have no nilpotent elements.
