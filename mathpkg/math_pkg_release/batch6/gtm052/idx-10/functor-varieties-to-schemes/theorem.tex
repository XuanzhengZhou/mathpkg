Let $k$ be an algebraically closed field. There is a natural fully faithful functor $t: \mathfrak{Var}(k) \rightarrow \mathfrak{Sch}(k)$ from the category of varieties over $k$ to schemes over $k$. For any variety $V$, its topological space is homeomorphic to the set of closed points of $\text{sp}(t(V))$, and its sheaf of regular functions is obtained by restricting the structure sheaf of $t(V)$ via this homeomorphism.
