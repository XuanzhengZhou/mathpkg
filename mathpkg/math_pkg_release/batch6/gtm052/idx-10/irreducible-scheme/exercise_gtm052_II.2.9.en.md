If $X$ is a topological space, and $Z$ an irreducible closed subset of $X$, a generic point for $Z$ is a point $\zeta$ such that $Z = \overline{\{\zeta\}}$. If $X$ is a scheme, show that every (nonempty) irreducible closed subset has a unique generic point.
