Let $(X, \mathcal{O}_X)$ be a scheme, and let $U \subseteq X$ be any open subset. Show that $(U, \mathcal{O}_X|_{U})$ is a scheme. We call this the induced scheme structure on the open set $U$, and we refer to $(U, \mathcal{O}_X|_{U})$ as an open subscheme of $X$.
