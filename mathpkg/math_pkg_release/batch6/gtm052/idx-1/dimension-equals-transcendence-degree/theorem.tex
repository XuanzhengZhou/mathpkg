Let $k$ be a field, and let $B$ be an integral domain which is a finitely generated $k$-algebra. Then:

(a) the dimension of $B$ (Krull dimension) is equal to the transcendence degree of the quotient field $K(B)$ of $B$ over $k$;

(b) For any prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ in $B$, we have
$$\operatorname{height} \mathfrak{p} + \dim B/\mathfrak{p} = \dim B.$$
