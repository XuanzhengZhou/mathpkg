Let $Y$ be a nonsingular projective curve with function field $K$. For each point $P \in Y$, the local ring $\mathcal{O}_P$ is a discrete valuation ring of $K/k$, by (5.1) and (6.2A). Let $U \subseteq C_K$ be the set of these local rings, and let
$$\varphi: Y \to U, \quad \varphi(P) = \mathcal{O}_P.$$
Then $U$ is an open subset of $C_K$, and $\varphi$ is an isomorphism of the abstract nonsingular curve $Y$ with the open subset $U$ of $C_K$.
