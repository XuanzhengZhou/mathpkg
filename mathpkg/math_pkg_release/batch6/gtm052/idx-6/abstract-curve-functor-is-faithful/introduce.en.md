Theorem 6.7 shows that any nonsingular projective curve $Y$ with function field $K$ embeds as an open subset of the abstract curve $C_K$ via the map sending each point to its local ring. Since distinct points have distinct local rings (Lemma 6.4), this map is injective. The theorem further establishes that the image is open and the map is an isomorphism onto its image, making the functor from curves to $C_K$ faithful.
