The ring of regular functions on an open subset $U$ of the abstract curve $C_K$ is the intersection of all DVRs corresponding to points in $U$. This definition makes $\mathcal{O}$ a sheaf of $k$-valued functions on $C_K$. Crucially, two regular functions that agree as functions (i.e., have the same residue at every point) are equal, so $\mathcal{O}(U)$ embeds faithfully into the ring of $k$-valued functions on $U$.
