An abstract nonsingular curve $C_K$ is the set of all discrete valuation rings of a function field $K/k$ of transcendence degree one. It is endowed with the cofinite topology (finite sets are closed) and a sheaf of regular functions defined by intersecting the DVRs over open sets. This construction is the culmination of Section 6: it yields a curve-like object purely from the function field, without needing an embedding in projective space.
