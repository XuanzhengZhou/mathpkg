Theorem 6.8, together with Theorem 6.7, establishes that the functor from nonsingular projective curves to their function fields is fully faithful. This means that the category of nonsingular projective curves (with dominant morphisms) is equivalent to a full subcategory of the category of function fields. The proof uses the valuation criterion and the abstract curve $C_K$ to extend morphisms.
