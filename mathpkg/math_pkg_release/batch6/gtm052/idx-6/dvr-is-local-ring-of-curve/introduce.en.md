Corollary 6.6 shows that every discrete valuation ring of a function field $K/k$ comes from geometry: it is isomorphic to the local ring $\mathcal{O}_P$ of a point $P$ on some nonsingular affine curve with function field $K$. Conversely, by Theorem 6.2A, every such local ring is a DVR. This establishes the key correspondence between algebraic DVRs and geometric points.
