Corollary 6.11 is the resolution of singularities for curves: any curve $Y$ (possibly singular, possibly not projective) is birationally equivalent to a nonsingular projective curve, namely $C_{K(Y)}$. This solves problem (b) from the introduction --- within each birational equivalence class, there exists a nonsingular projective model.
