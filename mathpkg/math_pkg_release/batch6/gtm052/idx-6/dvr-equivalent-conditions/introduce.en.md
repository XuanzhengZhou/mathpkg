Theorem 6.2A provides six equivalent characterizations of discrete valuation rings among noetherian local domains of dimension one. In particular, it shows that such a ring is a DVR exactly when it is integrally closed, or equivalently when its maximal ideal is principal. This theorem bridges the gap between the geometric notion of a regular point (dimension one regular local ring) and the algebraic notion of a DVR.
