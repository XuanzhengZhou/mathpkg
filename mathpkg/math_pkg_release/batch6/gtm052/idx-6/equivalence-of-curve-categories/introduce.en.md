Corollary 6.12 states the fundamental equivalence of three perspectives on algebraic curves: the geometric category of nonsingular projective curves, the birational category of all curves, and the algebraic category of function fields. The functor $K \mapsto C_K$ from function fields to nonsingular projective curves provides the inverse to the function field functor, establishing a complete equivalence of categories.
