Assume $p \neq 2$, and let $X$ be the elliptic curve given by the Legendre equation $y^2 = x(x-1)(x-\lambda)$, with $\lambda \neq 0, 1$, over a perfect field $k$ of characteristic $p > 0$. Then the Hasse invariant of $X$ is $0$ if and only if $h_p(\lambda) = 0$, where

$$h_p(\lambda) = \sum_{i=0}^{k} \binom{k}{i}^2 \lambda^i, \qquad k = \frac{p-1}{2}.$$

The polynomial $h_p$ is called the Deuring polynomial.
