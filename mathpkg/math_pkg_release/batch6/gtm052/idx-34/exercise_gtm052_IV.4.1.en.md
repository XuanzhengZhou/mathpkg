Let $X$ be an elliptic curve over $k$, with $\operatorname{char} k \neq 2$, let $P \in X$ be a point, and let $R$ be the graded ring $R = \bigoplus_{n \geq 0} H^0(X, \mathcal{O}_X(nP))$. Show that for suitable choice of $t, x, y$,

$$R \cong k[t, x, y] / (y^2 - x(x - t^2)(x - \lambda t^2))$$

as a graded ring, where $k[t, x, y]$ is graded by setting $\deg t = 1$, $\deg x = 2$, $\deg y = 3$.
