This follows from Proposition 8.4A (the second exact sequence for rings) by taking affine open covers. On each affine open subset $\operatorname{Spec} B \subset X$ with $Z \cap \operatorname{Spec} B = \operatorname{Spec} B/I$, the sheafified sequence corresponds to the exact sequence $I/I^2 \to \Omega_{B/A} \otimes_B B/I \to \Omega_{(B/I)/A} \to 0$. The global exactness follows since exactness of sheaves is local.
