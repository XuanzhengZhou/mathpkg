The conormal exact sequence relates the differentials of a closed subscheme $Z \subset X$ to those of the ambient scheme. The conormal sheaf $\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2$ maps via $\delta$ to the restriction $\Omega_{X/Y} \otimes \mathcal{O}_Z$, with cokernel $\Omega_{Z/Y}$. This sequence is the geometric analogue of the second exact sequence of Kähler differentials (Prop. 8.4A) and is essential for studying nonsingular subvarieties and their normal bundles.
