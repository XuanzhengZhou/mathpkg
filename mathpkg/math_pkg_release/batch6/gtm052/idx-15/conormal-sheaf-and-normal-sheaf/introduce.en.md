For a nonsingular subvariety $Y \subset X$, the conormal sheaf $\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2$ and its dual, the normal sheaf $\mathcal{N}_{Y/X}$, are locally free sheaves on $Y$ of rank equal to the codimension. The normal sheaf encodes the "normal directions" to $Y$ in $X$, and the dualized exact sequence $0 \to \mathcal{T}_Y \to \mathcal{T}_X \otimes \mathcal{O}_Y \to \mathcal{N}_{Y/X} \to 0$ expresses the geometric fact that normal vectors are tangent vectors of the ambient space modulo tangent vectors of the subspace.
