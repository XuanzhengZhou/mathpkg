Let $A$ be a ring, let $Y = \operatorname{Spec} A$, and let $X = \mathbf{P}_A^n$. Then there is an exact sequence of sheaves on $X$:

$$0 \to \Omega_{X/Y} \to \mathcal{O}_X(-1)^{n+1} \to \mathcal{O}_X \to 0,$$

where $\mathcal{O}_X(-1)^{n+1}$ denotes a direct sum of $n+1$ copies of the twisting sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X(-1)$.
