The Euler sequence is a fundamental exact sequence of sheaves on projective space $\mathbf{P}_A^n$ relating the sheaf of differentials $\Omega_{\mathbf{P}^n/A}$ to the twisting sheaves $\mathcal{O}(-1)$. It takes the form $0 \to \Omega \to \mathcal{O}(-1)^{n+1} \to \mathcal{O} \to 0$, where the middle term is a direct sum of $n+1$ copies of $\mathcal{O}(-1)$. This sequence is the computational backbone for all calculations involving differentials on projective varieties, analogous to the Euler sequence in complex geometry.
