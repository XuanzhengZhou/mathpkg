Let $S$ be a graded ring and let $X = \operatorname{Proj} S$. Assume that $S$ is generated by $S_1$ as an $S_0$-algebra.

(a) The sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X(n)$ is an invertible sheaf on $X$.

(b) For any graded $S$-module $M$, $\tilde{M}(n) \cong (M(n))^{\sim}$. In particular, $\mathcal{O}_X(n) \otimes \mathcal{O}_X(m) \cong \mathcal{O}_X(n + m)$.
