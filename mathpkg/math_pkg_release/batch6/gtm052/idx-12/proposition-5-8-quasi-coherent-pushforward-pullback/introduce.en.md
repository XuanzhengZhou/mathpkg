Proposition 5.8 establishes the behavior of quasi-coherent and coherent sheaves under pullback ($f^*$) and pushforward ($f_*$) along a morphism $f: X 	o Y$ of schemes. Part (a) shows that the pullback of a quasi-coherent sheaf remains quasi-coherent, and part (b) adds that coherence is also preserved under pullback in the noetherian case. Part (c) states that the direct image of a quasi-coherent sheaf is quasi-coherent provided either the source scheme is noetherian or the morphism is quasi-compact and separated. These results are essential for the functorial manipulation of sheaves in algebraic geometry.
