Let $S$ be a graded ring, let $X = \operatorname{Proj} S$, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a sheaf of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules. We define the graded $S$-module associated to $\mathcal{F}$, as a group, to be
$$\Gamma_*(\mathcal{F}) = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbf{Z}} \Gamma(X, \mathcal{F}(n)).$$
We give it a structure of graded $S$-module as follows. If $s \in S_d$, then $s$ determines in a natural way a global section $s \in \Gamma(X, \mathcal{O}_X(d))$. Then for any $t \in \Gamma(X, \mathcal{F}(n))$ we define the product $s \cdot t$ in $\Gamma(X, \mathcal{F}(n + d))$ by taking the tensor product $s \otimes t$ and using the natural map $\mathcal{F}(n) \otimes \mathcal{O}_X(d) \cong \mathcal{F}(n + d)$.
