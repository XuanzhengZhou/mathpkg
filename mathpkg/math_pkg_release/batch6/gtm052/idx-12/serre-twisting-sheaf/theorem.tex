Let $S$ be a graded ring, and let $X = \operatorname{Proj} S$. For any $n \in \mathbf{Z}$, we define the sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X(n)$ to be $S(n)^{\sim}$. We call $\mathcal{O}_X(1)$ the twisting sheaf of Serre. For any sheaf of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules $\mathcal{F}$, we denote by $\mathcal{F}(n)$ the twisted sheaf $\mathcal{F} \otimes_{\mathcal{O}_X} \mathcal{O}_X(n)$.
