Example 5.16.3 shows that on $X = \operatorname{Proj} S$ with $S$ generated by $S_1$ over $S_0$, the twisting sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X(1)$ is generated by the global sections coming from the degree 1 elements $S_1$. This is the fundamental fact underlying the construction of embeddings of projective schemes into projective space: the generators of $S_1$ provide the "homogeneous coordinates" for the embedding.
