Proposition 5.13 computes the graded module associated to the structure sheaf on projective $r$-space $\mathbf{P}^r_A = \operatorname{Proj} A[x_0, \ldots, x_r]$. The result $\Gamma_*(\mathcal{O}_X) \cong S$ shows that the functor $\Gamma_*$ recovers the homogeneous coordinate ring from the structure sheaf. This is a fundamental computation that generalizes the classical fact that the global regular functions on projective space are constants (corresponding to the degree zero part $S_0 = A$), and more generally that sections of $\mathcal{O}_X(n)$ correspond to homogeneous polynomials of degree $n$.
