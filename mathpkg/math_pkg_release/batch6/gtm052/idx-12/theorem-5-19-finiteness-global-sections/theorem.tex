Let $k$ be a field, let $A$ be a finitely generated $k$-algebra, let $X$ be a projective scheme over $A$, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a coherent $\mathcal{O}_X$-module. Then $\Gamma(X, \mathcal{F})$ is a finitely generated $A$-module. In particular, if $A = k$, $\Gamma(X, \mathcal{F})$ is a finite-dimensional $k$-vector space.
