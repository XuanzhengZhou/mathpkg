Corollary 9.9 states that the category of coherent sheaves on a noetherian formal scheme is closed under taking kernels, cokernels, and images of morphisms. In other words, if $\mathfrak{F} \to \mathfrak{G}$ is any morphism between coherent sheaves on a noetherian formal scheme, then $\ker(\mathfrak{F} \to \mathfrak{G})$, $\operatorname{coker}(\mathfrak{F} \to \mathfrak{G})$, and $\operatorname{im}(\mathfrak{F} \to \mathfrak{G})$ are all coherent sheaves. This follows from Theorem 9.7 together with the local nature of the question.
