Use (Ex. 9.3) to prove that if

$$0 \to \mathfrak{F}' \to \mathfrak{F} \to \mathfrak{F}'' \to 0$$

is an exact sequence of $\mathcal{O}_{\mathfrak{X}}$-modules on a noetherian formal scheme $\mathfrak{X}$, and if $\mathfrak{F}'$, $\mathfrak{F}''$ are coherent, then $\mathfrak{F}$ is coherent also.
