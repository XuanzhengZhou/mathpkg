Let $A$ be a noetherian ring, and let $X$ be a proper scheme over $\operatorname{Spec} A$. Let $L$ be an invertible sheaf on $X$. Then the following conditions are equivalent:

(i) $L$ is ample;

(ii) For each coherent sheaf $F$ on $X$, there is an integer $n_0$, depending on $F$, such that for each $i > 0$ and each $n \geq n_0$, $H^i(X, F \otimes L^n) = 0$.
