Let $X$ be a curve of degree $d$ and genus $g$ in $\mathbf{P}^3$, which is not contained in any plane. Then $d \geqslant 3$, and

$$g \leqslant \begin{cases} \frac{1}{4} d^2 - d + 1 & \text{if } d \text{ is even} \\ \frac{1}{4} (d^2 - 1) - d + 1 & \text{if } d \text{ is odd.} \end{cases}$$

Furthermore, the equality is attained for every $d \geqslant 3$, and any curve for which equality holds lies on a quadric surface.
