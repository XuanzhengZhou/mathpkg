Let $X$ be an elliptic curve over a field $k$ of characteristic $p > 0$, and let $R = \operatorname{End}(X, P_0)$ be its ring of endomorphisms.

(a) Let $X_p$ be the curve over $k$ defined by changing the $k$-structure of $X$ (2.4.1). Show that the Frobenius morphism $F: X \to X_p$ is a purely inseparable isogeny of degree $p$.

(b) Show that there is a dual morphism $V: X_p \to X$ (the Verschiebung) such that $V \circ F = p_X$ and $F \circ V = p_{X_p}$.

(c) Show that the endomorphism ring $R$ is a free $\mathbf{Z}$-module of rank at most $4$, and that it has no zero divisors.
