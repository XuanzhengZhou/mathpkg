Lemma 5.5 gives a subadditivity property for the dimensions of linear systems on a curve: the sum of the dimensions of two complete linear systems $|D|$ and $|E|$ is bounded above by the dimension of $|D+E|$. The proof uses the finite-to-one addition map $\varphi: |D| \times |E| \to |D+E|$, which arises from the bilinear multiplication map on global sections of the corresponding line bundles.
