Let $X$ be the curve $y^2 = x^3 - 7x + 10$. This curve has at least 26 points with integer coordinates. Find them (use a calculator), and verify that they are all contained in the subgroup (maybe equal to all of $X(\mathbf{Q})$?) generated by $P = (1,2)$ and $Q = (2,2)$.
