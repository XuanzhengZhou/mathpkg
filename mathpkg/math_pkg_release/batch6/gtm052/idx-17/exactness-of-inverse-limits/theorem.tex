Let
$$0 \to (A_n) \xrightarrow{f} (B_n) \xrightarrow{g} (C_n) \to 0$$
be a short exact sequence of inverse systems of abelian groups. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] If $(B_n)$ satisfies the Mittag--Leffler condition (ML), then $(C_n)$ also satisfies (ML).
\item[(b)] If $(A_n)$ satisfies the Mittag--Leffler condition (ML), then the induced sequence of inverse limits
$$0 \to \varprojlim A_n \to \varprojlim B_n \to \varprojlim C_n \to 0$$
is exact.
\end{enumerate}
