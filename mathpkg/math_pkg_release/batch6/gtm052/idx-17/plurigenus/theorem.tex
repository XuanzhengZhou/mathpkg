Let $X$ be a projective nonsingular variety over a field $k$. For any integer $n > 0$, the \text{$n$th plurigenus} of $X$ is defined as
$$P_n(X) = \dim_k \Gamma(X, \omega_X^{\otimes n}),$$
where $\omega_X$ is the canonical sheaf on $X$. In particular, $P_1 = p_g$ is the geometric genus of $X$. The plurigenera $P_n$ are birational invariants of $X$.
