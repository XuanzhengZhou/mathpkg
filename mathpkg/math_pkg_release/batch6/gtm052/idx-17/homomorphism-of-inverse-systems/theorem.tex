A \text{homomorphism} $f: (A_n) \to (B_n)$ of inverse systems of abelian groups is a collection of homomorphisms $f_n: A_n \to B_n$ for each $n$, compatible with the transition maps of the inverse systems: for each $n' \geq n$, the diagram
$$
\begin{CD}
A_{n'} @>{f_{n'}}>> B_{n'} \\
@V{\varphi_{n'n}^A}VV @VV{\varphi_{n'n}^B}V \\
A_n @>>{f_n}> B_n
\end{CD}
$$
commutes. This definition extends to inverse systems in any category.
