The Chow group $A^r(X)$ is the group of algebraic cycles of codimension $r$ on $X$ modulo rational equivalence. It generalizes the divisor class group to higher codimensions. The Chow groups are the fundamental objects of intersection theory, serving as the domain for intersection products and pullback/pushforward operations.
