Axiom A5 of intersection theory states that the intersection product $Y \cdot Z$ can be computed by pulling back the exterior product $Y \times Z$ along the diagonal morphism $\Delta: X \to X \times X$. This "reduction to the diagonal" is a crucial technical tool that reduces the computation of intersections of arbitrary cycles to the case where one cycle is the diagonal.
