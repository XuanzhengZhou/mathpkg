Let $X = \mathbf{P}_k^1$ be the projective line over an algebraically closed field $k$. Show that the exact sequence $0 \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{H} \rightarrow \mathcal{K} \rightarrow 0$ of (II, Ex. 1.21d) is a flasque resolution of $\mathcal{C}$. Conclude from (II, Ex. 1.21e) that $H^i(X, \mathcal{C}) = 0$ for all $i > 0$.
