If $\mathcal{J}^\bullet$ is a flasque resolution of $\mathcal{F}$ on $Y$, then $j_*\mathcal{J}^\bullet$ is a flasque resolution of $j_*\mathcal{F}$ on $X$, since $j_*$ is exact on flasque sheaves for a closed immersion. For each $i$, we have

$$\Gamma(Y, \mathcal{J}^i) = \Gamma(X, j_*\mathcal{J}^i)$$

by the definition of the direct image sheaf. Taking cohomology of the global sections complexes on both sides yields the same cohomology groups $H^i(Y, \mathcal{F}) \cong H^i(X, j_*\mathcal{F})$.
