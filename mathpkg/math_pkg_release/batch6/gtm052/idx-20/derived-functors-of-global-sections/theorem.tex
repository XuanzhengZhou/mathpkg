Let $(X, \mathcal{O}_X)$ be a ringed space. Then the derived functors of the functor $\Gamma(X, \cdot)$ from $\mathfrak{Mod}(X)$ to $\mathfrak{Ab}$ coincide with the derived functors of $\Gamma(X, \cdot)$ from $\mathfrak{Ab}(X)$ to $\mathfrak{Ab}$. In other words, for any sheaf of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules $\mathcal{F}$, its cohomology groups $H^i(X, \mathcal{F})$ are the same whether computed in the category of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules or in the category of abelian sheaves.
