Let $X$ be a closed subscheme of $P = \mathbf{P}_k^N$ of codimension $r$, let $\omega_P$ be the canonical sheaf on $P$, and let $\omega_X = \mathcal{E}xt^r_P(\mathcal{O}_X, \omega_P)$. Then for any $\mathcal{O}_X$-module $\mathcal{F}$, there is a functorial isomorphism

$$\operatorname{Hom}_X(\mathcal{F}, \omega_X) \cong \operatorname{Ext}^r_P(\mathcal{F}, \omega_P).$$
