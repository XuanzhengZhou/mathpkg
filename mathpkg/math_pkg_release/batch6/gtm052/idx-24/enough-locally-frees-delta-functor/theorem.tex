Let $X$ be a noetherian scheme, and suppose that every coherent sheaf on $X$ is a quotient of a locally free sheaf. In this case we say $\operatorname{Coh}(X)$ has \textbf{enough locally frees}. Then for any $\mathcal{G} \in \operatorname{Mod}(X)$, show that the $\delta$-functor $(\mathcal{E}xt^i(\cdot, \mathcal{G}))$, from $\operatorname{Coh}(X)$ to $\operatorname{Mod}(X)$, is a contravariant universal $\delta$-functor.

[Hint: Show $\mathcal{E}xt^i(\cdot, \mathcal{G})$ is coeffaceable for $i > 0$.]
