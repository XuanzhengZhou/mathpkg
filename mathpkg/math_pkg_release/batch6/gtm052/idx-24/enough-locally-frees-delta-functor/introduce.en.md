This exercise generalizes the notion of "enough projectives" from module categories to the category of coherent sheaves. When every coherent sheaf on a noetherian scheme $X$ is a quotient of a locally free sheaf, we say $\operatorname{Coh}(X)$ has enough locally frees. The exercise shows that under this condition, the Ext sheaf functors $(\mathcal{E}xt^i(\cdot, \mathcal{G}))$ form a contravariant universal $\delta$-functor.
