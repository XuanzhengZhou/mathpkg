This exercise establishes the fundamental connection between extensions of sheaves and the Ext functor. Given two sheaves $\mathcal{F}'$ and $\mathcal{F}''$ on a ringed space, an extension of $\mathcal{F}''$ by $\mathcal{F}'$ is a short exact sequence. The exercise asks to show that equivalence classes of extensions correspond naturally to elements of $\operatorname{Ext}^1(\mathcal{F}'', \mathcal{F}')$, generalizing the classical correspondence between group extensions and Ext.
