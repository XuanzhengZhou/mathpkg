Let $X$ be a proper scheme over $k$. Then a dualizing sheaf for $X$, if it exists, is unique. More precisely, if $\omega^\circ$ is one, with its trace map $t$, and if $\omega', t'$ is another, then there is a unique isomorphism $\varphi: \omega^\circ \to \omega'$ such that $t = t' \circ H^n(\varphi)$.
