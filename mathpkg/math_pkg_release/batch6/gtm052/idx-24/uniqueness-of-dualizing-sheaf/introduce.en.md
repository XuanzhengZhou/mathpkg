If a dualizing sheaf exists for a proper scheme $X$ over $k$, it is unique up to a unique isomorphism compatible with the trace maps. Specifically, given two dualizing sheaves $\omega^\circ$ and $\omega'$ with trace maps $t$ and $t'$, there is a unique isomorphism $\varphi: \omega^\circ \to \omega'$ such that the induced map on cohomology identifies the two trace morphisms.
