This exercise develops duality for a finite morphism $f: X \to Y$ of noetherian schemes. It constructs a functor $f^!$ from quasi-coherent $\mathcal{O}_Y$-modules to quasi-coherent $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules, defined by $f^!\mathcal{G} = \widetilde{\mathcal{H}om_Y(f_*\mathcal{O}_X, \mathcal{G})}$, and establishes a natural adjunction isomorphism $f_*\mathcal{H}om_X(\mathcal{F}, f^!\mathcal{G}) \cong \mathcal{H}om_Y(f_*\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G})$. This is a precursor to Grothendieck duality.
