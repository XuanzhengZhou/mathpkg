Let $X$ be a nonsingular projective curve over an algebraically closed field $k$, with function field $K$. For any rational differential form $\tau \in \Omega_K$,

$$\sum_{P \in X} \operatorname{res}_P \tau = 0$$

where $\operatorname{res}_P \tau$ denotes the residue of $\tau$ at the closed point $P \in X$.
