This proposition gives a homological characterization of projective modules and their projective dimension using the Ext functor. A module $M$ is projective precisely when $\operatorname{Ext}^1(M, N) = 0$ for all modules $N$, and its projective dimension $\operatorname{pd}(M)$ is bounded by $n$ exactly when all higher Ext groups vanish. This connects the algebraic notion of projectivity with the derived functor Ext.
