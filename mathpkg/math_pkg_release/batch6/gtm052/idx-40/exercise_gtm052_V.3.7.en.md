For each of the following singularities at $(0,0)$ in the plane, give an embedded resolution, compute $\delta_p$, and decide which ones are equivalent.

(a) $x^3 + y^5 = 0$.

(b) $x^3 + x^4 + y^5 = 0$.

(c) $x^3 + y^4 + y^5 = 0$.

(d) $x^3 + y^5 + y^6 = 0$.

(e) $x^3 + xy^3 + y^5 = 0$.
