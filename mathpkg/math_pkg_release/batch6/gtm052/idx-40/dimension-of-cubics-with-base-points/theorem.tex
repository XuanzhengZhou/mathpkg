Let $P_1, \ldots, P_r$ be points in $\mathbf{P}^2$ with no 4 collinear and no 7 on a conic, and let $\mathfrak{d}$ be the linear system of all plane cubics through $P_1, \ldots, P_r$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] if $r \leqslant 7$, then $\dim \mathfrak{d} = 9 - r$;
\item[(b)] if $r = 8$, then $\dim \mathfrak{d} = 1$ (with at most finitely many base points).
\end{enumerate}
