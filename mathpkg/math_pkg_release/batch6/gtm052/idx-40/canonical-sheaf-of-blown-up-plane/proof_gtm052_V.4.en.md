By (4.3) and (4.0.1), $|D|$ is very ample, so it gives an embedding of $X'$ into a projective space. The dimension of the linear system is $\dim |D| = 9 - 6 = 3$ by (4.4)(a), so the embedding is into $\mathbf{P}^3$. The degree is $D^2 = (3l - \sum e_i)^2 = 9l^2 + \sum e_i^2 = 9 + 6(-1) = 3$, since $l^2 = 1$, $e_i^2 = -1$, and $l.e_i = 0$, $e_i.e_j = 0$ ($i \neq j$). Thus $X'$ is a cubic surface in $\mathbf{P}^3$.

The canonical divisor on $X'$ is $K_{X'} = \pi^* K_{\mathbf{P}^2} + \sum E_i = -3l + \sum e_i = -D$. Therefore $\omega_{X'} \cong \mathcal{O}_{X'}(-D)$, and in the embedding $\omega_{X'} \cong \mathcal{O}_{X'}(-1)$.
