This proposition shows the flexibility of the representation of a cubic surface as a blow-up of $\mathbf{P}^2$: any set of six mutually skew lines among the 27 lines can serve as the exceptional curves of a blow-up representation. This reflects the high degree of symmetry of the 27-line configuration and is key to understanding the automorphism group of the cubic surface and the Weyl group $E_6$ acting on the Picard lattice.
