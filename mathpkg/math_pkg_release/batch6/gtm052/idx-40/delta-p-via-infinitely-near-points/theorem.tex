Let $C$ be a curve on a surface $X$ with a singular point $P$. The integer $\delta_P$ defined in (IV, Ex. 1.8) can be computed as

$$\delta_P = \sum_{Q} \frac{1}{2} r_Q(r_Q - 1),$$

where the sum is taken over all infinitely near singular points $Q$ lying over $P$, including $P$ itself, and $r_Q$ is the multiplicity at $Q$.
