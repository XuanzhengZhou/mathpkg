This corollary computes the dimension of the linear system of conics passing through $r$ points in general position. Since the complete linear system of conics has dimension 5 and each prescribed base point (which is not already an unassigned base point) drops the dimension by one, the dimension is $5 - r$ for $r \leqslant 5$. When $r = 5$, the system has dimension 0, yielding a unique irreducible conic through the five points.
