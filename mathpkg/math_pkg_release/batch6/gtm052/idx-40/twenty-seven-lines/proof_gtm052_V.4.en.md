The lines on $X$ are irreducible curves $C$ with $C^2 < 0$. Write $C \sim al - \sum b_i e_i$ as in (4.8.1). Since $C$ is a line in $\mathbf{P}^3$, we have $C.h = 1$, which gives $3a - \sum b_i = 1$. The adjunction formula gives $C^2 + C.K = -2$, and using $C.K = -C.h = -1$, we get $C^2 = -1$, so $a^2 - \sum b_i^2 = -1$.

Substituting $\sum b_i = 3a - 1$ and $\sum b_i^2 = a^2 + 1$, we obtain
$$3a^2 - 6a - 5 \leqslant 0.$$

Solving the quadratic equation, this implies $a \leqslant 1 + (2/3)\sqrt{6} < 3$. Therefore $a = 1$ or $2$.

If $a = 1$, then $b_i = b_j = 1$ for some $i,j$, the rest 0. This gives $F_{ij}$ (15 lines). If $a = 2$, then all $b_i = 1$ except for one $b_j = 0$. This gives $G_j$ (6 lines). Together with $E_i$ (6 lines), this yields $6 + 15 + 6 = 27$ lines.
