Let $\{\mathcal{F}_i\}$ be a direct system of sheaves on a noetherian topological space $X$. Show that the presheaf $U \mapsto \varinjlim \mathcal{F}_i(U)$ is already a sheaf. In particular, $\Gamma(X, \varinjlim \mathcal{F}_i) = \varinjlim \Gamma(X, \mathcal{F}_i)$.
