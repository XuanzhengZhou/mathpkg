Lemma 2.1 establishes the fundamental properties of the sets $V(\mathfrak{a})$ of prime ideals containing a given ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ in a ring $A$. These sets form the closed sets of the Zariski topology on $\text{Spec } A$. Part (a) shows that $V$ converts ideal multiplication to union; part (b) shows that $V$ converts ideal sums to intersection; and part (c) gives a condition for containment of closed sets in terms of radicals.
