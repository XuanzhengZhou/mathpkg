The following conditions are equivalent, for a divisor $D$ on the cubic surface $X$:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $D$ is very ample;
\item[(ii)] $D$ is ample;
\item[(iii)] $D^2 > 0$, and for every line $L \subseteq X$, $D.L > 0$;
\item[(iv)] for every line $L \subseteq X$, $D.L > 0$.
\end{enumerate}
