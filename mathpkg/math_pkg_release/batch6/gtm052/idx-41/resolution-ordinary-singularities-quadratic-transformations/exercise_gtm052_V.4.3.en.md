Let $C$ be an irreducible curve in $\mathbf{P}^2$. Then there exists a finite sequence of quadratic transformations, centered at suitable triples of points, so that the strict transform $C'$ of $C$ has only ordinary singularities, i.e., multiple points with all distinct tangent directions (I, Ex. 5.14). Use (3.8).
