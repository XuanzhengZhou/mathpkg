In a noetherian topological space $X$, every nonempty closed subset $Y$ can be expressed as a finite union $Y = Y_1 \cup \ldots \cup Y_r$ of irreducible closed subsets $Y_i$. If we require that $Y_i \nsubseteq Y_j$ for $i \neq j$, then the $Y_i$ are uniquely determined. They are called the \textbf{irreducible components} of $Y$.
