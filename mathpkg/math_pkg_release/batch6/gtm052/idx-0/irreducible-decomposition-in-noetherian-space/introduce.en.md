This is Proposition 1.5 from Chapter I, Section §1 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. It asserts that in any noetherian topological space (in particular, any algebraic set with the Zariski topology), every closed subset decomposes uniquely into a finite union of irreducible closed subsets, called its irreducible components. This is the topological analog of primary decomposition in noetherian rings.
