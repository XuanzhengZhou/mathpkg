This is a definition from Chapter I, Section §1 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. The ideal $I(Y)$ of a subset $Y$ of affine space is the set of all polynomials that vanish on every point of $Y$. Together with the zero-set operation $Z$, it establishes a Galois correspondence between algebraic subsets of $\mathbf{A}^n$ and ideals of the polynomial ring $k[x_1,\ldots,x_n]$.
