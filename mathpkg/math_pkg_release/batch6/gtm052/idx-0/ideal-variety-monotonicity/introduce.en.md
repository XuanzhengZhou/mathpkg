This is Proposition 1.2 from Chapter I, Section §1 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. It summarizes the fundamental properties of the maps $Z$ (zero-set) and $I$ (ideal of a set), establishing a Galois connection between ideals in $k[x_1,\ldots,x_n]$ and algebraic subsets of affine space. Part (d) is equivalent to Hilbert's Nullstellensatz.
