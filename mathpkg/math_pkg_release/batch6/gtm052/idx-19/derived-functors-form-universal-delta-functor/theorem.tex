Assume that the abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$ has enough injectives. Then for any left exact functor $F: \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$, the derived functors $(R^i F)_{i \geqslant 0}$ form a universal $\delta$-functor with $R^0 F \cong F$.

Conversely, if $T = (T^i)_{i \geqslant 0}$ is any universal $\delta$-functor with $T^0 \cong F$, then $T^i \cong R^i F$ for all $i \geqslant 0$.
