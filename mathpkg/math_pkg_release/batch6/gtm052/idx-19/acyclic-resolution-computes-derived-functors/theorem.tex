Let $F: \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ be a left exact covariant functor between abelian categories, with $\mathfrak{A}$ having enough injectives. Suppose there is an exact sequence
$$0 \to A \to J^0 \to J^1 \to \cdots$$
where each $J^i$ is acyclic for $F$, for $i \geqslant 0$. (Such a sequence is called an \text{$F$-acyclic resolution} of $A$.) Then for each $i \geqslant 0$ there is a natural isomorphism
$$R^i F(A) \cong h^i(F(J^\bullet)).$$
