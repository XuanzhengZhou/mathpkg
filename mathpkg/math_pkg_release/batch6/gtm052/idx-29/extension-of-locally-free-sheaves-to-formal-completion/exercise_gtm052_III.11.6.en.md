Again let $Y$ be a hypersurface in $X = \mathbf{P}_k^N$, this time with $N \geqslant 2$, and let $\hat{X}$ be the formal completion of $X$ along $Y$.

**(a)** If $\mathcal{F}$ is a locally free sheaf on $X$, show that the natural map
$$H^0(X, \mathcal{F}) \to H^0(\hat{X}, \hat{\mathcal{F}})$$
is an isomorphism.

**(b)** Show that the following conditions are equivalent:

(i) for each locally free sheaf $\mathfrak{F}$ on $\hat{X}$, there exists a coherent sheaf $\mathcal{F}$ on $X$ such that $\mathfrak{F} \cong \hat{\mathcal{F}}$;

(ii) for each locally free sheaf $\mathfrak{F}$ on $\hat{X}$, there exists an integer $n$ such that $\mathfrak{F}(n)$ is generated by global sections.

*Hint:* For (a), use the Theorem on Formal Functions. For (b), use (II, 9.9) and (II, 5.17).
