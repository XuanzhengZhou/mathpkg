**Principle of Connectedness.** Let $\{X_t\}$ be a flat family of closed subschemes of $\mathbf{P}_k^n$ parametrized by an irreducible curve $T$ of finite type over an algebraically closed field $k$. Suppose there is a nonempty open set $U \subseteq T$, such that for all closed points $t \in U$, $X_t$ is connected. Then prove that $X_t$ is connected for all $t \in T$.
