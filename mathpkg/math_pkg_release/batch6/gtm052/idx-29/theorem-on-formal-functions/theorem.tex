Let $f: X \to Y$ be a projective morphism of noetherian schemes, let $\mathcal{F}$ be a coherent sheaf on $X$, and let $y \in Y$. Define
$$
X_n = X \times_Y \operatorname{Spec} \mathcal{O}_y / \mathfrak{m}_y^n, \qquad \mathcal{F}_n = v^*\mathcal{F},
$$
where $v: X_n \to X$ is the natural morphism. Then for each $i \geqslant 0$, the natural map
$$
R^i f_*(\mathcal{F})_y^{\widehat{\ }} \longrightarrow \varprojlim_{n} H^i(X_n, \mathcal{F}_n)
$$
is an isomorphism of $\widehat{\mathcal{O}}_y$-modules, where $\widehat{\ }$ denotes completion with respect to the maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}_y$.
