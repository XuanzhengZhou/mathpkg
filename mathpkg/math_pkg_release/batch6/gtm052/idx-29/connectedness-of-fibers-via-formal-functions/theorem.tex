Let $f: X \to Y$ be a projective morphism of noetherian schemes, and let $y \in Y$. Then $f^{-1}(y)$ is connected if and only if the natural map $\mathcal{O}_{Y,y} \to (f_*\mathcal{O}_X)_y^{\widehat{\ }}$ is an isomorphism, where $\widehat{\ }$ denotes completion with respect to the maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}_y$. In particular, if $f_*\mathcal{O}_X \cong \mathcal{O}_Y$, then $f^{-1}(y)$ is connected for all $y \in Y$.
