The proof is due to Moishezon [2]. If $\mathfrak{X}$ is a Moishezon manifold (algebraic dimension = $\dim \mathfrak{X}$) that is also Kähler, then one can show that $\mathfrak{X}$ carries a Hodge metric. The Kähler condition provides a positive $(1,1)$-form $\omega$, and the Moishezon condition implies that after possibly modifying $\omega$ within its cohomology class, it becomes a rational class, hence (after scaling) an integral class. Then Kodaira's embedding theorem (4.1) implies $\mathfrak{X}$ is projective algebraic.
