This elementary but crucial linear algebra lemma relates the trace of powers of an endomorphism to its characteristic polynomial. It transforms the exponential generating function of traces into the reciprocal of the determinant $\det(1 - \varphi t)$. This identity is the key algebraic step in the cohomological interpretation of the Weil zeta function: combined with the Lefschetz trace formula, it factors the zeta function into alternating products of characteristic polynomials of Frobenius acting on $l$-adic cohomology.
