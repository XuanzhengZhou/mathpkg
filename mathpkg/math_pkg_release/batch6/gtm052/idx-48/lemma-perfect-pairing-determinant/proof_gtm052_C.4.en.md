The proof is a computation in linear algebra. Since the pairing is perfect, we can identify $W$ with the dual space $V^*$ and $\psi$ with the adjoint of $\varphi$ with respect to the pairing, scaled by $\lambda$. One then computes the characteristic polynomial of $\psi$ in terms of that of $\varphi$ using standard determinant identities. Specifically, one has $\psi = \lambda \cdot (\varphi^{-1})^T$ in appropriate bases, from which the relation between the determinants follows.
