This linear algebra lemma describes how the characteristic polynomials of two endomorphisms $\varphi$ and $\psi$ are related when they satisfy a compatibility condition with respect to a perfect pairing. It is the algebraic heart of the proof that the functional equation of the Weil zeta function follows from Poincaré duality in $l$-adic cohomology. In the geometric context, $V = H^i$, $W = H^{2n-i}$, the pairing is given by cup-product, $\varphi = f^*$, $\psi = f^*$ on the dual space, and $\lambda = q^n$.
