The comparison theorem for $l$-adic cohomology establishes that for smooth proper schemes over $\mathbf{C}$, the $l$-adic cohomology groups (after tensoring with $\mathbf{C}$) are isomorphic to the ordinary singular cohomology groups of the associated complex manifold. This theorem justifies the interpretation of $l$-adic Betti numbers as actual topological Betti numbers in characteristic zero, and is crucial for the final part (1.4) of the Weil conjectures connecting characteristic $p$ invariants to topological invariants in characteristic zero.
