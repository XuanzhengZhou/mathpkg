The exponential sequence is a fundamental short exact sequence of sheaves on a complex analytic space that links topology (via the constant sheaf $\mathbf{Z}$), analysis (via the structure sheaf $\mathcal{O}$), and geometry (via the sheaf of units $\mathcal{O}^*$). Its associated long exact cohomology sequence relates integral cohomology, coherent cohomology, and the Picard group. This yields deep results such as the finite generation of the Néron–Severi group and the structure of the Picard variety as a complex torus.
