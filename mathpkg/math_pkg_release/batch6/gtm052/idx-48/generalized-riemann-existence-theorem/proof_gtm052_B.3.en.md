The proof was originally given by Grauert and Remmert [1]. Grothendieck [SGA 1, XII] later gave an elegant proof using Hironaka's resolution of singularities. The idea is to reduce to the case where the morphism is étale by resolving the branch locus, then apply the classical Riemann existence theorem and descent arguments to algebraize the covering.
