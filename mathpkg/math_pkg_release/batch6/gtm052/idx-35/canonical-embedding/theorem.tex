Let $X$ be a nonhyperelliptic curve of genus $g \geqslant 2$ over an algebraically closed field $k$. The canonical linear system $|K|$ is base-point-free and determines a morphism $\varphi_K: X \to \mathbf{P}^{g-1}$, which is a closed embedding. This embedding is called the \text{canonical embedding} of $X$.
