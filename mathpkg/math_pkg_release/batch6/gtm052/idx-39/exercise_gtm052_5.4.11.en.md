**The Weyl Groups.** Given any diagram consisting of points and line segments joining some of them, we define an abstract group, given by generators and relations, as follows: each point represents a generator $x_i$. The relations are $x_i^2 = 1$ for each $i$; $(x_i x_j)^2 = 1$ if $i$ and $j$ are not joined by a line segment, and $(x_i x_j)^3 = 1$ if $i$ and $j$ are joined by a line segment.
