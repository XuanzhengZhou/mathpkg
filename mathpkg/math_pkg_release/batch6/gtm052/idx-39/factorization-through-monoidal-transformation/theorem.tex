Let $f: X' \rightarrow X$ be a birational morphism of nonsingular projective surfaces. Let $P$ be a fundamental point of $f^{-1}$. Then $f$ factors through the monoidal transformation $\pi: \tilde{X} \rightarrow X$ with center $P$, i.e., there exists a morphism $f_1: X' \rightarrow \tilde{X}$ such that $f = \pi \circ f_1$.
