Let $T$ be a connected noetherian scheme, and let $X \subseteq \mathbf{P}_T^n$ be a closed subscheme which is flat over $T$. For any $t \in T$, let $X_t$ be the fibre, considered as a closed subscheme of $\mathbf{P}_{k(t)}^n$. Then the dimension of $X_t$, the degree of $X_t$, and the arithmetic genus of $X_t$ are all independent of $t$.
