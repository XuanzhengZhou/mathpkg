Let $X$ be a curve of genus $g$. Show that there is a finite morphism $f: X \to \mathbf{P}^1$ of degree $\leq g + 1$.

(Recall that the degree of a finite morphism of curves $f: X \to Y$ is defined as the degree of the field extension $[K(X) : K(Y)]$ (II, \S 6).)
