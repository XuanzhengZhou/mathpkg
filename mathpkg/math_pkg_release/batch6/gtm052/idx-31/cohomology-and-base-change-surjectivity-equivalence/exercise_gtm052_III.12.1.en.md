Let $Y$ be a scheme of finite type over an algebraically closed field $k$. Show that the function

$$\varphi(y) = \dim_k (\mathfrak{m}_y / \mathfrak{m}_y^2)$$

is upper semicontinuous on the set of closed points of $Y$.
