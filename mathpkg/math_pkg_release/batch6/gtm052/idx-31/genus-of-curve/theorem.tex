The \textbf{genus} $g$ of a curve $X$ over an algebraically closed field $k$ is the common value

$$g = p_a(X) = p_g(X) = \dim_k H^1(X, \mathcal{O}_X),$$

where $p_a(X)$ is the arithmetic genus, $p_g(X)$ is the geometric genus, and the equality is established in Proposition 1.1. The genus is always non-negative: $g \geq 0$. Conversely, for any $g \geq 0$, there exist curves of genus $g$.
