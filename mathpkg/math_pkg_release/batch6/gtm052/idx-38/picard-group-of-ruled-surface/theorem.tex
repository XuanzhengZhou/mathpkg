Let $\pi: X \rightarrow C$ be a ruled surface, and let $C_0$ be the image of a section. Then:

$$C_0.f = 1, \qquad f^2 = 0,$$

where $f$ is a fibre. Moreover,

$$\operatorname{Pic} X \cong \mathbf{Z} \oplus \pi^*\operatorname{Pic} C,$$

and

$$\operatorname{Num} X \cong \mathbf{Z} \oplus \mathbf{Z},$$

generated by $C_0$ and $f$.
