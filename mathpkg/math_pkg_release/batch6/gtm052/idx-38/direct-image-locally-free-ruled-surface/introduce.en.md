This lemma computes the direct image of a line bundle $\mathcal{L}(D)$ on a ruled surface $X \to C$. When the intersection number $D.f$ with a fibre is nonnegative, the sheaf $\pi_*\mathcal{L}(D)$ is locally free of rank $D.f + 1$ on the base curve $C$. The special case $D = 0$ recovers $\pi_*\mathcal{O}_X \cong \mathcal{O}_C$, which is fundamental for the theory of ruled surfaces.
