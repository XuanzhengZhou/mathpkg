The canonical divisor $K$ of a ruled surface $X = \mathbf{P}(\mathcal{E})$ over a curve $C$ of genus $g$ is given explicitly by $K \sim -2C_0 + (2g - 2 - e)f$, where $C_0$ is the distinguished section and $e$ is the invariant of the normalized bundle. The formula is derived using the adjunction formula applied to both a fibre $f \cong \mathbf{P}^1$ and the section $C_0$.
