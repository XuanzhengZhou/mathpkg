Any section $D$ of a ruled surface corresponds to a surjection $\mathcal{E} \to \mathcal{L}$ from the normalized bundle to an invertible sheaf. Its linear equivalence class is determined by the divisor $\mathfrak{d}$ corresponding to $\mathcal{L}$ and the invariant $\mathfrak{e}$: we have $D \sim C_0 + (\mathfrak{d} - \mathfrak{e})f$. As a special case, the self-intersection of the distinguished section is $C_0^2 = e$, the negative of the degree of the normalized bundle.
