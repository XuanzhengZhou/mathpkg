The higher direct image functor $R^i f_*$ commutes with restriction to open subsets of the target. For any open $V \subseteq Y$, the $i$-th higher direct image of a sheaf $\mathcal{F}$ restricted to $V$ coincides with the higher direct image of $\mathcal{F}|_{f^{-1}(V)}$ under the restricted morphism $f': f^{-1}(V) \to V$. This compatibility with restrictions makes higher direct images convenient for local computations on the base scheme.
