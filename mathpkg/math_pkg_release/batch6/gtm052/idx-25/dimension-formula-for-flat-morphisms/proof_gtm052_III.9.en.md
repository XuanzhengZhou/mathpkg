First make a base change $Y' \to Y$ where $Y' = \operatorname{Spec} \mathcal{O}_{y,Y}$, and consider the new morphism $f': X' \to Y'$. This reduces to the case where $Y$ is the spectrum of a local ring. Then use properties of flat local homomorphisms and the fact that for a flat extension of local rings, the dimension adds.
