See Matsumura [2, Ch. 2, §3] or Bourbaki [1, Ch. I]. The proof uses the long exact sequence of Tor: for any $A$-module $N$, applying $-\otimes_A N$ to the exact sequence gives a long exact sequence of Tor groups. The vanishing of Tor determines flatness.
