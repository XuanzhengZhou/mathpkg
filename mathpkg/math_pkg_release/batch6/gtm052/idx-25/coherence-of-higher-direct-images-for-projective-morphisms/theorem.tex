Let $f: X \to Y$ be a projective morphism of noetherian schemes, let $\mathcal{O}_X(1)$ be a very ample invertible sheaf on $X$ over $Y$, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a coherent sheaf on $X$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] for all $n \gg 0$, the natural map $f^* f_*(\mathcal{F}(n)) \to \mathcal{F}(n)$ is surjective;
\item[(b)] for all $i \geqslant 0$, $R^i f_*(\mathcal{F})$ is a coherent sheaf on $Y$.
\end{enumerate}
