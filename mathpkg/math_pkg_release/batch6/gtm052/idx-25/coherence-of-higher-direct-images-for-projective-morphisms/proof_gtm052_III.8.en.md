The proof proceeds by reducing to the case $Y$ is affine and $X = \mathbb{P}^r_Y$, then using the computation of cohomology of projective space (III, §5) together with descending induction on $i$. For part (a), one shows that for $n$ sufficiently large, the sheaf $\mathcal{F}(n)$ is generated by global sections relative to $Y$.
