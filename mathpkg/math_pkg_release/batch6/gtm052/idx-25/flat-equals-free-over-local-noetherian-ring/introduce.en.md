Over a local noetherian ring, flatness coincides with freeness for finitely generated modules. This is a crucial characterization that simplifies working with flat modules in local algebraic geometry. The forward direction (flat implies free) uses Nakayama's lemma and the fact that Tor vanishes; the converse follows since free modules are always flat.
