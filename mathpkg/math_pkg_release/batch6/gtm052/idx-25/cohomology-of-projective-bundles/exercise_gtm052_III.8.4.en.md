Let $Y$ be a noetherian scheme, and let $\mathcal{E}$ be a locally free $\mathcal{O}_Y$-module of rank $n + 1$, $n \geqslant 1$. Let $X = \mathbf{P}(\mathcal{E})$ (II, §7), with the invertible sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X(1)$ and the projection morphism $\pi: X \to Y$.

(a) Then $\pi_*(\mathcal{O}(l)) \cong S^l(\mathcal{E})$ for $l \geqslant 0$, $\pi_*(\mathcal{O}(l)) = 0$ for $l < 0$ (II, 7.11); $R^i \pi_*(\mathcal{O}(l)) = 0$ for $0 < i < n$ and all $l \in \mathbf{Z}$; and $R^n \pi_*(\mathcal{O}(l)) = 0$ for $l > -n - 1$.

(b) Show there is a natural exact sequence
$$0 \to \Omega_{X/Y} \to (\pi^*\mathcal{E})(-1) \to \mathcal{O} \to 0,$$
cf. (II, 8.13), and conclude that the relative canonical sheaf $\omega_{X/Y} = \wedge^n \Omega_{X/Y}$ is isomorphic to $(\pi^* \wedge^{n+1} \mathcal{E})(-n - 1)$. Show furthermore that there is a natural isomorphism $R^n \pi_*(\omega_{X/Y}) \cong \mathcal{O}_Y$.
