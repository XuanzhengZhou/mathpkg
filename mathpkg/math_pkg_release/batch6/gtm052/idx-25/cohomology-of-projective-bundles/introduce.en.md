This exercise computes the higher direct images of the line bundles $\mathcal{O}(l)$ under the projection $\pi: \mathbf{P}(\mathcal{E}) \to Y$ of a projective bundle. The direct image $\pi_* \mathcal{O}(l)$ recovers the symmetric powers $S^l(\mathcal{E})$ for $l \geq 0$, while the relative Euler sequence describes the sheaf of relative differentials $\Omega_{X/Y}$ and identifies the relative canonical sheaf $\omega_{X/Y}$.
