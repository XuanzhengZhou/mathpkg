The $\mathfrak{a}$-adic completion $\hat{A}$ of a noetherian ring $A$ is flat over $A$. This is a deep result that relies on the Artin-Rees lemma. Flatness of completion is essential in algebraic geometry for studying formal neighborhoods, deformation theory, and passing between a local ring and its completion without losing homological information.
