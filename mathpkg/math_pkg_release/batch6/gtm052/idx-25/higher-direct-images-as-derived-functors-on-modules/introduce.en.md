The higher direct image functors $R^i f_*$ on the category $\mathfrak{Mod}(X)$ of $\mathcal{O}_X$-modules coincide with the right derived functors of the direct image functor $f_*: \mathfrak{Mod}(X) \to \mathfrak{Mod}(Y)$. This unifies the sheaf-cohomological definition of $R^i f_*$ (via $\mathcal{H}^i(X, \cdot)$ on $\mathfrak{Ab}(X)$) with the general machinery of derived functors in abelian categories.
