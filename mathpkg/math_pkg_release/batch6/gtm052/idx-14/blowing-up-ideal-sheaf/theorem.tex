Let $X$ be a noetherian scheme and let $\mathcal{I}$ be a coherent sheaf of ideals on $X$. Consider the sheaf of graded algebras $\mathcal{S} = \bigoplus_{d \geqslant 0} \mathcal{I}^d$, where $\mathcal{I}^d$ is the $d$-th power of the ideal $\mathcal{I}$, and we set $\mathcal{I}^0 = \mathcal{O}_X$. The pair $(X, \mathcal{S})$ satisfies $(\dagger)$, and the **blowing-up** of $X$ with respect to $\mathcal{I}$ is defined as
$$\tilde{X} = \operatorname{Proj} \mathcal{S}.$$
If $Y$ is the closed subscheme of $X$ corresponding to $\mathcal{I}$, then $\tilde{X}$ is also called the blowing-up of $X$ along $Y$, or with center $Y$.
