Let $(X, \mathcal{O}_X)$ be a locally ringed space, and let $f: \mathcal{L} \to \mathcal{M}$ be a surjective map of invertible sheaves on $X$. Show that $f$ is an isomorphism.

*Hint:* Reduce to a question of modules over a local ring by looking at the stalks.
