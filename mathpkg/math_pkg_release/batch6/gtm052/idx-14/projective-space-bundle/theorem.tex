Let $X$ be a noetherian scheme and let $\mathcal{E}$ be a locally free coherent sheaf on $X$. Let $\mathcal{S} = S(\mathcal{E})$ be the symmetric algebra of $\mathcal{E}$, so $\mathcal{S} = \bigoplus_{d \geqslant 0} S^d(\mathcal{E})$. Then $\mathcal{S}$ is a sheaf of graded $\mathcal{O}_X$-algebras satisfying $(\dagger)$. The **projective space bundle** associated to $\mathcal{E}$ is defined as
$$\mathbf{P}(\mathcal{E}) = \operatorname{Proj} \mathcal{S},$$
together with its projection morphism $\pi: \mathbf{P}(\mathcal{E}) \to X$ and its natural invertible sheaf $\mathcal{O}(1)$.
