Corollary 7.15 shows that blowing up is functorial: given a morphism of schemes, one can blow up compatibly on both the source and target. This allows the blowup construction to be applied in relative situations. The corollary follows directly from the universal property of Proposition 7.14 and is essential for studying how subschemes behave under blowing up.
