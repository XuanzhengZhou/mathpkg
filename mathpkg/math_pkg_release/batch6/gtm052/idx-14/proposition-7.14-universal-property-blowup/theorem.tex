Let $X$ be a noetherian scheme, let $\mathcal{I}$ be a coherent sheaf of ideals, and let $\pi: \tilde{X} \to X$ be the blowing-up with respect to $\mathcal{I}$. Then $\tilde{X}$ has the following universal property: for any morphism $f: Z \to X$ such that $f^{-1}\mathcal{I} \cdot \mathcal{O}_Z$ is an invertible sheaf of ideals on $Z$, there exists a unique morphism $g: Z \to \tilde{X}$ factoring $f$ through $\pi$. Moreover, under this correspondence, $g^*\mathcal{O}_{\tilde{X}}(1) \cong f^{-1}\mathcal{I} \cdot \mathcal{O}_Z$.
