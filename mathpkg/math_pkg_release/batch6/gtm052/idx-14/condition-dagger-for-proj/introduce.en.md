Condition (dagger) specifies the technical hypotheses required on a scheme and a sheaf of graded algebras before one can form the relative Proj construction. The conditions ensure that the sheaf is quasi-coherent, that its degree-zero part is the structure sheaf, that its degree-one part is coherent, and that it is locally generated by degree-one elements. These hypotheses guarantee that the relative Proj is well-behaved and enjoys the expected properties.
