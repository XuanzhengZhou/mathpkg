This lemma establishes that the linear system of plane conics passing through four points in general position (no three collinear), possibly with one point infinitely near another, has no unintended base points. This result is fundamental for counting dimensions of linear systems on blown-up models of $\mathbf{P}^2$, forming the basis for the study of cubic surfaces via blowing up six points.
