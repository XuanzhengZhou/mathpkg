Let $C$ be a reduced curve on a surface, and let $P \in C$ be a singular point. The integer $\delta_P$ of (IV, Ex. 1.8) can be computed as
$$\delta_P = \sum_Q \frac{1}{2} r_Q(r_Q - 1),$$
where the sum is taken over all infinitely near singular points $Q$ lying over $P$, including $P$ itself, and $r_Q$ denotes the multiplicity of $C$ (or its strict transform) at $Q$.
