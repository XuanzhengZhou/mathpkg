This formula relates the geometric genus $g(\tilde{C})$ of the normalization of an irreducible curve $C$ on a surface to its arithmetic genus $p_a(C)$ and the multiplicities of its singular points. By repeatedly blowing up singular points until the curve becomes nonsingular, the arithmetic genus drops by $\tfrac{1}{2}r(r-1)$ at each blowup of a point of multiplicity $r$, ultimately reaching the geometric genus. The sum includes infinitely near singular points.
