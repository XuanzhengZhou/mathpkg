Theorem 5.4A summarizes the fundamental properties of the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic completion of a noetherian local ring. The completion $\hat{A}$ is again a local ring and the natural map $A \to \hat{A}$ is injective and faithfully flat. For finitely generated modules, completion coincides with tensoring with $\hat{A}$. Crucially, completion preserves both Krull dimension and regularity, making it a powerful tool for studying local properties of varieties.
