Let $X$ and $Y$ be two varieties. Suppose there are points $P \in X$ and $Q \in Y$ such that the local rings $\mathcal{O}_{P,X}$ and $\mathcal{O}_{Q,Y}$ are isomorphic as $k$-algebras. Then show that there are open sets $P \in U \subseteq X$ and $Q \in V \subseteq Y$ and an isomorphism of $U$ to $V$ which sends $P$ to $Q$.
