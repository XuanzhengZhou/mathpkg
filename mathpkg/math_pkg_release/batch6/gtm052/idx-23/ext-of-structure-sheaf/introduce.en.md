This proposition computes Ext sheaves and Ext groups when the first argument is the structure sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X$. Since $\mathcal{H}om(\mathcal{O}_X,\cdot)$ is the identity functor, its derived functors vanish in positive degree, giving (a) and (b). Part (c) identifies the global Ext groups $\operatorname{Ext}^i(\mathcal{O}_X,\mathcal{G})$ with sheaf cohomology $H^i(X,\mathcal{G})$, as both are derived functors of the global sections functor $\Gamma(X,\cdot)$.
