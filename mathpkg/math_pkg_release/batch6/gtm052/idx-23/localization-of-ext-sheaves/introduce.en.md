This proposition states that the Ext sheaf construction is local on the ringed space $X$. The restriction of the Ext sheaf $\mathcal{E}xt^i_X(\mathcal{F},\mathcal{G})$ to an open subset $U$ is naturally isomorphic to the Ext sheaf of the restricted sheaves on $U$. This locality property is essential for computing Ext sheaves on affine open covers and for reducing global Ext computations to local ones.
