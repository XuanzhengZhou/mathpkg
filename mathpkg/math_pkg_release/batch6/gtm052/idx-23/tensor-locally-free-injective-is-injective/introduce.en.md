This lemma shows that tensoring an injective sheaf with a locally free sheaf of finite rank preserves injectivity. The key observation is that $\operatorname{Hom}(-, \mathcal{L} \otimes \mathcal{I}) \cong \operatorname{Hom}(- \otimes \mathcal{L}^\vee, \mathcal{I})$, where $\mathcal{L}^\vee$ is the dual of $\mathcal{L}$. Since tensoring with $\mathcal{L}^\vee$ is exact (locally free implies flat) and $\mathcal{I}$ is injective, the composite functor is exact, proving injectivity of $\mathcal{L} \otimes \mathcal{I}$.
