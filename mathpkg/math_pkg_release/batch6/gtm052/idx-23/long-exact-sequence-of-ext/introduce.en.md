A short exact sequence of sheaves $0 \to \mathcal{F}' \to \mathcal{F} \to \mathcal{F}'' \to 0$ induces a long exact sequence of Ext groups (and similarly for Ext sheaves) by applying the functor $\operatorname{Hom}(-,\mathcal{G})$ and taking its derived functors. This is the fundamental tool for computing Ext groups from short exact sequences, analogous to the long exact cohomology sequence but with the short exact sequence in the first argument rather than the second.
