If $0 \rightarrow \mathcal{F}' \rightarrow \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{F}'' \rightarrow 0$ is a short exact sequence in $\operatorname{Mod}(X)$, then for any $\mathcal{G} \in \operatorname{Mod}(X)$ there is a long exact sequence
$$
0 \rightarrow \operatorname{Hom}(\mathcal{F}'',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \operatorname{Hom}(\mathcal{F},\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \operatorname{Hom}(\mathcal{F}',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \operatorname{Ext}^1(\mathcal{F}'',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \operatorname{Ext}^1(\mathcal{F},\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \operatorname{Ext}^1(\mathcal{F}',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \operatorname{Ext}^2(\mathcal{F}'',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \cdots
$$
and similarly for the sheaf $\mathcal{E}xt$:
$$
0 \rightarrow \mathcal{H}om(\mathcal{F}'',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \mathcal{H}om(\mathcal{F},\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \mathcal{H}om(\mathcal{F}',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \mathcal{E}xt^1(\mathcal{F}'',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \mathcal{E}xt^1(\mathcal{F},\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \mathcal{E}xt^1(\mathcal{F}',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \mathcal{E}xt^2(\mathcal{F}'',\mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \cdots
$$
