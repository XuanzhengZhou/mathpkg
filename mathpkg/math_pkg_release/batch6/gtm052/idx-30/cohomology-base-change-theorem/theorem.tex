Let $f: X \to Y$ be a projective morphism of noetherian schemes, and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a coherent sheaf on $X$, flat over $Y$. Suppose for some $i \geq 0$ that the function $h^i(y, \mathcal{F})$ is constant on $Y$. Then $R^i f_*(\mathcal{F})$ is a locally free sheaf on $Y$, and for every $y \in Y$, the natural map

$$R^i f_*(\mathcal{F}) \otimes k(y) \to H^i(X_y, \mathcal{F}_y)$$

is an isomorphism. Furthermore, the same conclusions hold for $i-1$ if the function $h^{i-1}(y, \mathcal{F})$ is also constant.
