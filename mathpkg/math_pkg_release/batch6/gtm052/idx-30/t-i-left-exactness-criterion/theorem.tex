Let $A$ be a noetherian ring, $f: X \to Y = \operatorname{Spec} A$ a projective morphism, $\mathcal{F}$ a coherent sheaf on $X$ flat over $Y$, and let $T^i(M) = H^i(X, \mathcal{F} \otimes_A M)$ for each $A$-module $M$. Let $L^\bullet$ be the complex of finitely generated free $A$-modules given by Lemma 12.3 applied to the Cech complex $C^\bullet$, and let $W^i = \operatorname{coker}(d^{i-1}: L^{i-1} \to L^i)$. Then the following conditions are equivalent:

(i) $T^i$ is left exact;
(ii) $W^i$ is a flat $A$-module;
(iii) $T^i(M) = \operatorname{Hom}_A(Q, M)$ for some finitely generated $A$-module $Q$ and for all $A$-modules $M$.
