This linear algebra lemma is the algebraic core behind the functional equation of the zeta function. It describes how the characteristic polynomials of adjoint endomorphisms with respect to a perfect pairing are related, and when applied to Poincaré duality in $l$-adic cohomology (where the Frobenius and its adjoint satisfy the pairing condition with $\lambda = q^n$), it yields the functional equation $Z(1/q^n t) = \pm q^{nE/2} t^E Z(t)$.
