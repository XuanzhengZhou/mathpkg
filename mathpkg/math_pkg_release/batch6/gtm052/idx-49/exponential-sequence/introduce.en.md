The exponential sequence is a fundamental short exact sequence of sheaves on any reduced complex analytic space. It relates the constant sheaf $\mathbf{Z}$, the structure sheaf $\mathcal{O}_{\mathfrak{X}}$, and the sheaf of units $\mathcal{O}_{\mathfrak{X}}^*$ via the exponential map $f(x) = e^{2\pi i x}$. Its long exact cohomology sequence provides deep connections between topology, algebraic geometry, and complex analysis.
