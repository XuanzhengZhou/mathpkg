Summing up, we have the following implications among properties of compact complex manifolds, and there are examples to show that no further implications are possible.

$$	ext{Hodge} \iff 	ext{projective algebraic} \Rightarrow 	ext{abstract algebraic}$$

$$	ext{Kähler} \Rightarrow 	ext{Moishezon}$$

The proof that Moishezon + Kähler implies projective algebraic uses Hodge theory and the Kodaira embedding theorem.
