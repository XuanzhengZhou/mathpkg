Using the Lefschetz trace formula in $l$-adic cohomology and Lemma 4.1, the zeta function of a smooth projective variety over $\mathbf{F}_q$ is expressed as an alternating product of characteristic polynomials of the Frobenius endomorphism acting on $l$-adic cohomology. This formula immediately implies the rationality of the zeta function (Weil Conjecture 1.1) and gives a cohomological interpretation of the other conjectures.
