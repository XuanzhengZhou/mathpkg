Let $X$ be a nonsingular projective 3-fold, with Chern classes $c_1, c_2, c_3$. Show that

$$1 - p_a = \frac{1}{24} c_1 c_2,$$

and for any divisor $D$,

$$\chi(\mathcal{L}(D)) = \frac{1}{12} D.(D - K).(2D - K) + \frac{1}{12} D.c_2 + 1 - p_a.$$
