Let $X$ be a scheme of finite type over an algebraically closed field $k$ of characteristic $p \geqslant 0$. Let $l$ be a prime number $l \neq p$. Let $\mathbf{Z}_l = \varprojlim \mathbf{Z}/l^r\mathbf{Z}$ be the ring of $l$-adic integers, and $\mathbf{Q}_l$ its quotient field. We consider the etale topology of $X$, and then using etale cohomology, we define the $l$-adic cohomology of $X$ by

$$H^i(X, \mathbf{Q}_l) = \left( \varprojlim H^i_{\text{et}}(X, \mathbf{Z}/l^r\mathbf{Z}) \right) \otimes_{\mathbf{Z}_l} \mathbf{Q}_l.$$
