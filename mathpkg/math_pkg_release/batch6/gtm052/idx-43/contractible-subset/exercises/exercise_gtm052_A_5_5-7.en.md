Let $Y$ be an irreducible curve on a surface $X$, and suppose there is a morphism $f: X \to X_0$ to a projective variety $X_0$ of dimension $2$, such that $f(Y)$ is a point $P$ and $f^{-1}(P) = Y$. Then show that $Y^2 < 0$. Hint: Let $|H|$ be a very ample (Cartier) divisor class on $X_0$, let $H_0 \in |H|$ be a divisor containing $P$, and let $H_1 \in |H|$ be a divisor not containing $P$. Then consider $f^*H_0$, $f^*H_1$ and $\tilde{H}_0 = f^*(H_0 - P)^-$.
