Let $X$ be a variety and let $Y \subseteq X$ be a closed subset. We say that $Y$ is contractible if there exists a birational morphism $f: X \rightarrow X_0$ such that $f(Y)$ is a single point $P$, and the restriction $f|_{X \setminus Y}: X \setminus Y \rightarrow X_0 \setminus \{P\}$ is an isomorphism.
