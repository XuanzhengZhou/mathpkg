Theorem 5.8 from Appendix A, Section 5 of Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry states that every surface admits a birational morphism to a relatively minimal model. The proof, due to Matsumura, proceeds by repeatedly contracting exceptional curves of the first kind via Castelnuovo's contraction theorem (5.7). The process must terminate after finitely many steps, which is proved by considering the intersection form on the Neron-Severi group.
