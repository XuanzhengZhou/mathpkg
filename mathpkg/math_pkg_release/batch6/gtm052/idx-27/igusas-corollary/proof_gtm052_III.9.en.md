Any two closed points of $T$ lie in the image of a morphism $g: T' \rightarrow T$, where $T'$ is a nonsingular curve, or can be connected by a finite number of such curves, so by base extension, we reduce to the case where $T$ is a nonsingular curve. Then the result follows from (9.10) and (9.11).
