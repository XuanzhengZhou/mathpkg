Igusa's Corollary states that in an algebraic family of normal varieties parametrized by a variety $T$, the Hilbert polynomial and arithmetic genus are invariant across all members of the family. The proof reduces to the case where $T$ is a nonsingular curve by connecting any two closed points through morphisms from nonsingular curves, then applying base extension and the flatness results for algebraic families of normal varieties.
