Let $X$ be a nonsingular projective variety of dimension $n$, and let $\mathcal{E}$ be a locally free sheaf of rank $r$ on $X$. Let $\operatorname{ch}(\mathcal{E})$ denote the Chern character of $\mathcal{E}$, and let $\operatorname{td}(\mathcal{T}_X)$ denote the Todd class of the tangent sheaf $\mathcal{T}_X$ of $X$. Then

$$\chi(\mathcal{E}) = \deg\bigl(\operatorname{ch}(\mathcal{E}) \cdot \operatorname{td}(\mathcal{T}_X)\bigr)_n,$$

where $(\cdot)_n$ denotes the component in degree $n$ of the Chow ring $A(X) \otimes \mathbb{Q}$, and $\deg$ denotes the degree map.
