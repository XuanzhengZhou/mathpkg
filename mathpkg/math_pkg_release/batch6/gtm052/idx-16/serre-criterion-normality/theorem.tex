Let $A$ be a noetherian ring. Then $A$ is normal if and only if it satisfies the following two conditions:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] For every prime ideal $\mathfrak{p} \subseteq A$ of $\operatorname{height} \mathfrak{p} \leqslant 1$, the localization $A_\mathfrak{p}$ is regular (hence a field or a discrete valuation ring).
\item[(2)] For every prime ideal $\mathfrak{p} \subseteq A$ of $\operatorname{height} \mathfrak{p} \geqslant 2$, we have $\operatorname{depth} A_\mathfrak{p} \geqslant 2$.
\end{enumerate}
