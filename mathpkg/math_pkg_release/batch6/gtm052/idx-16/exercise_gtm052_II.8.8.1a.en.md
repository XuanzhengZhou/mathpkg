Here we will strengthen the results of the text to include information about the sheaf of differentials at a not necessarily closed point of a scheme $X$.

Generalize (8.7) as follows. Let $B$ be a local ring containing a field $k$, and assume that the residue field $k(B) = B/\mathfrak{m}$ of $B$ is a separably generated extension of $k$. Then the exact sequence of (8.4A),

$$0 \to \mathfrak{m}/\mathfrak{m}^2 \to \Omega_{B/k} \otimes k(B) \to \Omega_{k(B)/k} \to 0$$

is exact on the left also. [Hint: In copying the proof of (8.7), first pass to $B/\mathfrak{m}^2$, which is a complete local ring, and then use (8.25A) to choose a field of representatives for $B/\mathfrak{m}^2$.]
