Theorem 3.2 is the fundamental structure theorem for affine varieties. It establishes that the ring of global regular functions $\mathcal{O}(Y)$ recovers the affine coordinate ring $A(Y)$, that points correspond bijectively to maximal ideals, and that local rings at points are localizations of the coordinate ring. The function field $K(Y)$ is identified as the quotient field, with transcendence degree equal to the dimension of $Y$.
