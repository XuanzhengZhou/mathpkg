Let $Y \subseteq \mathbf{P}^n$ be a projective variety with homogeneous coordinate ring $S(Y)$. Then:

(a) $\mathcal{O}(Y) = k$;

(b) for each point $P \in Y$, let $\mathfrak{m}_P \subseteq S(Y)$ be the ideal generated by the set of homogeneous $f \in S(Y)$ such that $f(P) = 0$. Then $\mathcal{O}_P \cong S(Y)_{(\mathfrak{m}_P)}$;

(c) $K(Y) \cong S(Y)_{((0))}$.
