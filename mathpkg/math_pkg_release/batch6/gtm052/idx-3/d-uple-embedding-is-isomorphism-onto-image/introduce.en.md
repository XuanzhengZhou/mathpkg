Exercise 3.4 asks to verify that the $d$-uple (Veronese) embedding of $\mathbf{P}^n$ is an isomorphism onto its image. The Veronese embedding maps $\mathbf{P}^n$ into a higher-dimensional projective space via all monomials of degree $d$, and the image is a projective variety isomorphic to the original $\mathbf{P}^n$.
