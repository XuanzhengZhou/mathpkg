Exercise 3.3(a) establishes that a morphism of varieties naturally induces a homomorphism between the corresponding local rings. Given $P \in X$, a germ of a regular function near $\varphi(P)$ pulls back to a germ of a regular function near $P$, giving a well-defined local homomorphism $\varphi_P^*$.
