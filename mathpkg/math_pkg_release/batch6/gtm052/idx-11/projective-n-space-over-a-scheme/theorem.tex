If $Y$ is any scheme, we define projective $n$-space over $Y$, denoted $\mathbf{P}_Y^n$, to be $\mathbf{P}_{\mathbb{Z}}^n \times_{\operatorname{Spec} \mathbb{Z}} Y$. Equivalently, for any affine open $\operatorname{Spec} A \subseteq Y$, the preimage of this open in $\mathbf{P}_Y^n$ is isomorphic to $\mathbf{P}_A^n = \operatorname{Proj} A[x_0, \ldots, x_n]$.
