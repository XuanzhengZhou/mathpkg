The adjunction isomorphism $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}_X}(f^*\mathcal{G}, \mathcal{F}) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}_Y}(\mathcal{G}, f_*\mathcal{F})$ expresses that $f^*$ is left adjoint to $f_*$ in the category of sheaves of modules. This fundamental relationship (analogous to Exercise 1.18 for sheaves of abelian groups) is essential throughout algebraic geometry, from base change theorems to the construction of derived functors.
