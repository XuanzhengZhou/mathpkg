Let $X = \operatorname{Spec} A$, $Y = \operatorname{Spec} B$. Then $A$ is a $B$-algebra, and $X \times_Y X$ is also affine, given by $\operatorname{Spec} (A \otimes_B A)$. The diagonal morphism $\Delta: X \to X \times_Y X$ corresponds to the ring homomorphism $A \otimes_B A \to A$ defined by $a \otimes a' \mapsto aa'$, which is surjective. Therefore $\Delta$ is a closed immersion, so $f$ is separated.
