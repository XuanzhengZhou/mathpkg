Let $X$ be an integral scheme. Show that the local ring $\mathcal{O}_\xi$ of the generic point $\xi$ of $X$ is a field. It is called the function field of $X$ and is denoted by $K(X)$. Show also that if $U = \operatorname{Spec} A$ is any open affine subset of $X$, then $K(X)$ is isomorphic to the quotient field of $A$.
