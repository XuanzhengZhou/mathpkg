Let $f: X \to Y$ be a morphism of ringed spaces, and let $\mathcal{G}$ be an $\mathcal{O}_Y$-module. We define the inverse image $f^*\mathcal{G}$ to be the tensor product

$$f^*\mathcal{G} = f^{-1}\mathcal{G} \otimes_{f^{-1}\mathcal{O}_Y} \mathcal{O}_X.$$

Thus $f^*\mathcal{G}$ is an $\mathcal{O}_X$-module, called the inverse image of $\mathcal{G}$ by the morphism $f$.
