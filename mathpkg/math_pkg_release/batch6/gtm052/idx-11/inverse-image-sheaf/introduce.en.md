The inverse image sheaf $f^*\mathcal{G}$ (also called the pullback) is the tensor product $f^{-1}\mathcal{G} \otimes_{f^{-1}\mathcal{O}_Y} \mathcal{O}_X$. It converts an $\mathcal{O}_Y$-module into an $\mathcal{O}_X$-module along a morphism $f: X \to Y$ of ringed spaces. Together with the direct image $f_*$, it forms an adjoint pair: $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}_X}(f^*\mathcal{G}, \mathcal{F}) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}_Y}(\mathcal{G}, f_*\mathcal{F})$.
