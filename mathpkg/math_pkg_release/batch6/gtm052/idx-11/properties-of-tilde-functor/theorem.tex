The functor $M \mapsto \widetilde{M}$ from the category of $A$-modules to the category of $\mathcal{O}_{\operatorname{Spec} A}$-modules has the following properties:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item It is exact, fully faithful, and commutes with direct sums and tensor products.
  \item For any $B$-module $N$, if $f: \operatorname{Spec} B \to \operatorname{Spec} A$ is induced by a ring homomorphism $A \to B$, then $f_*(\widetilde{N}) \cong ({}_{A}N)^{\sim}$, where ${}_{A}N$ means $N$ considered as an $A$-module.
  \item For any $A$-module $M$, $f^*(\widetilde{M}) \cong (M \otimes_A B)^{\sim}$.
\end{enumerate}
