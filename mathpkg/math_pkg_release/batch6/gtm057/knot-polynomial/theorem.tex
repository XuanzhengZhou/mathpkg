For a finite presentation $(\mathbf{x} : \mathbf{r})$ of a knot group, the \textit{$k$th knot polynomial} $\Delta_k$ is the g.c.d. of all $(n-k) \times (n-k)$ determinants of the Alexander matrix, where $\Delta_k = 0$ if $n-k > m$ and $\Delta_k = 1$ if $n-k \leq 0$. The polynomial is defined up to multiplication by units $\pm t^n$ in the ring $JH$ of L-polynomials.
