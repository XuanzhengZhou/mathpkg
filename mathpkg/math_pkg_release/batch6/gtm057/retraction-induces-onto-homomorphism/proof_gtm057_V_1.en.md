Let $i: Y \to X$ be the inclusion. Then $\rho \circ i = \text{id}_Y$, so $\rho_* \circ i_* = (\rho \circ i)_* = \text{id}_{\pi(Y,\rho p)}$, which forces $\rho_*$ to be onto.
