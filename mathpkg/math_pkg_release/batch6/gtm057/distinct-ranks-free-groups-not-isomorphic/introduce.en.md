Theorem 4.2 establishes that the rank of a free group is a complete isomorphism invariant. The proof uses the abelianization $F/F^2$: for finite rank $m$, $|F_m/F_m^2| = 2^m$, so distinct ranks give non-isomorphic abelianizations. This justifies calling the rank a well-defined invariant of free groups.
