Since $E_k \subset E_{k+1}$, we have $(\Delta_{k+1}) \supset E_{k+1} \supset E_k$. Since $(\Delta_k)$ is the smallest principal ideal containing $E_k$, we get $(\Delta_{k+1}) \supset (\Delta_k)$, so $\Delta_{k+1} \mid \Delta_k$.
