Let $\{h_s\}$ be a continuous family of $p_0$-based loops exhibiting $a \simeq b$. Extend to a continuous function $h$ on a rectangle $E$, then lift uniquely to $\bar{h}: E \to \mathbb{R}$ with $\bar{h}(0,0) = 0$. The function $s \mapsto \bar{h}(s, \|h_s\|)$ is continuous and integer-valued (since $\phi\bar{h}(s, \|h_s\|) = p_0$), hence constant. Thus $\bar{h}(0, \|a\|) = \bar{h}(1, \|b\|)$, giving equal winding numbers.
