Let $(\Delta_k)$ and $(\Delta_{k+1})$ denote the principal ideals generated by $\Delta_k$ and $\Delta_{k+1}$. Since $(\Delta_{k+1}) \supset E_{k+1} \supset E_k$, and $(\Delta_k)$ is the smallest principal ideal containing $E_k$, we have $(\Delta_{k+1}) \supset (\Delta_k)$. Thus $\Delta_k = a\Delta_{k+1}$, i.e., $\Delta_{k+1} \mid \Delta_k$.
