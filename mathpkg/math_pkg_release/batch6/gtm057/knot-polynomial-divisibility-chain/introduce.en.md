Theorem 3.3 states that the knot polynomials form a divisibility chain: each polynomial divides the previous one. This follows from the ascending chain property of elementary ideals and the fact that $\Delta_k$ generates the smallest principal ideal containing $E_k$. The same proof shows the first elementary ideal is principal, generated by the Alexander polynomial $\Delta_1$.
