The trefoil knot group is one of the most important examples in knot theory. Its Wirtinger presentation with three generators simplifies to two generators $x,y$ with the single relation $xyx = yxy$. Setting $a = xy$ and $b = xyx$ yields the alternative presentation $a^2 = b^3$, which is the group of the torus knot $K_{2,3}$. This nonabelian group is the simplest nontrivial knot group.
