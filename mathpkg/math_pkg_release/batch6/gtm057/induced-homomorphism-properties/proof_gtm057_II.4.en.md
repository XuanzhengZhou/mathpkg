The proofs follow from (4.1), (4.2), and the associativity of function composition. For (i), the image of a constant path under $f$ is constant. For (ii), $f_*([a][b]) = f_*([a \cdot b]) = [f(a \cdot b)] = [fa \cdot fb] = [fa][fb] = f_*[a] f_*[b]$. For (iii), if $f(x) = x$, then $fa = a$. For (iv), $(gf)_*[a] = [(gf)a] = [g(fa)] = g_*[fa] = g_* f_* [a]$.
