The \textit{Wirtinger presentation} of a knot group is the simplified over presentation
$$(x_1, \ldots, x_n : v_1^\sharp, \ldots, v_n^\sharp)_\phi,$$
where $v_i$ is a loop in $\mathbb{R}^2 - \mathcal{P}B$ around the projected underpass $\mathcal{P}B_i$. It is obtained from the over presentation by $n$ Tietze I operations.
