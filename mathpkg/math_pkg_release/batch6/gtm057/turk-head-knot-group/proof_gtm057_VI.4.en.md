For the three-lead four-bight Turk's head knot (Figure 43), a Wirtinger presentation with generators $x_1, x_2, x_3, x_4$ yields relations of the form $x_i = [x_{i+3}, x_{i+2}^{-1}] x_{i+1} [x_{i+2}^{-1}, x_{i+3}]$ for $i = 1,\dots,4$ (indices mod 4), where $[a,b] = aba^{-1}b^{-1}$ is the commutator. Any one relation is a consequence of the other three by Theorem (1.3), so the presentation uses three relators. This is the group presented in Chapter VI, Example (4.4).
