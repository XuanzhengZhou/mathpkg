If $(\mathbf{x} : \mathbf{r})$ and $(\mathbf{y} : \mathbf{s})$ are finite presentations of knot groups and $f: (\mathbf{x} : \mathbf{r}) \to (\mathbf{y} : \mathbf{s})$ is a presentation equivalence, then to within units, $\Delta_k$ of $(\mathbf{x} : \mathbf{r})$ is mapped by $f_{**}$ onto $\Delta_k'$ of $(\mathbf{y} : \mathbf{s})$.
