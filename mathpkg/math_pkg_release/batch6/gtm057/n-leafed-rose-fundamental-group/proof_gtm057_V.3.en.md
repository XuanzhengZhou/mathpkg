By induction on $n$. $C_{(1)}$ is a circle with infinite cyclic fundamental group. For $C_{(n+1)} = C_{(n)} \cup X_{n+1}$ with $\{p\} = C_{(n)} \cap X_{n+1}$, enlarge to open sets by taking a neighborhood $N$ of $p$. Then $C_{(n)}$, $X_{n+1}$, and $\{p\}$ are deformation retracts of $C_{(n)} \cup N$, $X_{n+1} \cup N$, and $N$ respectively. Applying (3.3) yields the result.
