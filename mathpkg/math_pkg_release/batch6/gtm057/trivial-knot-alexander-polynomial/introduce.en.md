The trivial knot has Alexander polynomial $\Delta(t) = 1$ (and all higher knot polynomials are also 1). Since the Alexander polynomial is a knot invariant, any knot with a nontrivial Alexander polynomial (such as the trefoil with $\Delta(t) = t^2 - t + 1$) must be nontrivially knotted.
