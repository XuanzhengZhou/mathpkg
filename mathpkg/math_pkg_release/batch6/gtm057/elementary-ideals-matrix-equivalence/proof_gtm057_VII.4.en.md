Operations (i)-(iv) correspond to standard row/column operations that preserve determinants up to sign. Operation (v) adjoins a new row and column with a 1 in the corner; expanding by minors shows $E_k(A) = E_k(A')$ for this operation as well. The details verify each operation preserves the ideal generated by the appropriate-sized minors.
