Let $E$ be any set of generators of a given group $G$, and let $F[\mathcal{A}]$ be a free group on an alphabet $\mathcal{A}$ with $|\mathcal{A}| \geq |E|$. Let $\lambda: [\mathcal{A}] \to E$ be any surjection. Since $[\mathcal{A}]$ is a free basis of $F[\mathcal{A}]$, $\lambda$ extends uniquely to a homomorphism $F[\mathcal{A}] \to G$ which is onto because $E$ generates $G$.
