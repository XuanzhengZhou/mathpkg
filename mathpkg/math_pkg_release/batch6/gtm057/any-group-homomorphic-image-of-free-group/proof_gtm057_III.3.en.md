Let $E$ be any set of generators of a given group $G$, and let $F[\mathcal{A}]$ be a free group on an alphabet $\mathcal{A}$ whose cardinality equals or exceeds that of $E$. Let $\lambda: [\mathcal{A}] \to E$ be a surjection. Since $[\mathcal{A}]$ is a free basis, $\lambda$ extends to a homomorphism of $F[\mathcal{A}]$ onto $G$.
