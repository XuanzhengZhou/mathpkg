For a polygonal knot $K$ in regular position with overpasses $A_1, \ldots, A_n$, the \textit{over presentation} of $\pi(\mathbb{R}^3 - K, p_0)$ is
$$(x_1, \ldots, x_n : r_1, \ldots, r_n)_\phi,$$
where $r_i = (c_i \cdot v_i \cdot c_i^{-1})^\sharp$ records the crossings around the $i$th underpass, and $\phi x_j = [^*a_j]$ are based loops above the knot corresponding to paths under the $j$th overpass.
