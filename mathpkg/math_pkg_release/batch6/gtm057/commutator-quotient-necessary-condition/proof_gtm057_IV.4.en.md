If $\phi: G_1 \to G_2$ is an isomorphism, then by the word subgroup theorem, $\phi([G_1,G_1]) = [G_2,G_2]$. Hence $\phi$ induces an isomorphism $G_1/[G_1,G_1] \cong G_2/[G_2,G_2]$.
