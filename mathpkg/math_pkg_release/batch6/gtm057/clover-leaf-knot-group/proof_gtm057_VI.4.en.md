Reading around the three underpasses of the clover-leaf knot (trefoil) in regular position yields the Wirtinger relators $v_1^\sharp = x^{-1}yzy^{-1}$, $v_2^\sharp = y^{-1}zxz^{-1}$, $v_3^\sharp = z^{-1}xyx^{-1}$. By Theorem (1.3), any one relator is a consequence of the others, so we may drop $v_3^\sharp$. Substituting the relation $z = xyx^{-1}$ into the other two yields $x = yxyx^{-1}y^{-1}$ and $y = xyxy^{-1}x^{-1}$, which are both equivalent to $xyx = yxy$. Thus $\pi(R^3 - K) = |x, y : xyx = yxy|$.
