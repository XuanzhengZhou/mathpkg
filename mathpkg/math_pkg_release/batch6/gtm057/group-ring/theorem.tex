The \textit{group ring} $JG$ of a multiplicative group $G$ over the integers $J$ consists of formal finite sums $\sum n_i g_i$ with $n_i \in \mathbb{Z}, g_i \in G$. Addition is componentwise; multiplication extends the group operation linearly:
$$(\sum_i n_i g_i)(\sum_j m_j h_j) = \sum_{i,j} n_i m_j (g_i h_j).$$
