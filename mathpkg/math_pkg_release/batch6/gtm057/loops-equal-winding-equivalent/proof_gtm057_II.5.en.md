Let $a$ and $b$ be $p_0$-based loops with equal winding numbers, with lifts $\bar{a}$ and $\bar{b}$ in $\mathbb{R}$. Since all paths in $\mathbb{R}$ with the same endpoints are equivalent, there exists a fixed-endpoint family $\{\bar{h}_s\}$ with $\bar{h}_0 = \bar{a}$ and $\bar{h}_1 = \bar{b}$. The images $h_s = \phi \bar{h}_s$ form a fixed-endpoint family showing $a \simeq b$.
