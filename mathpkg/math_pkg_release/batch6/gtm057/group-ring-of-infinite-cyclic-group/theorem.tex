Let $H$ be an infinite cyclic group with generator $t$. The group ring $JH$ consists of all finite sums $a = \sum_{-\infty}^{\infty} a_n t^n$ (Laurent polynomials). Define $\mu(a) = \min\{n : a_n \neq 0\}$ for $a \neq 0$, $\mu(0) = \infty$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $JH$ is an integral domain.
\item $\mu(ab) = \mu(a) + \mu(b)$.
\item The only units of $JH$ are $\pm t^n$, $n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
\item $JH$ is a g.c.d. domain (and a unique factorization domain).
\end{enumerate}
