The inclusion $i: Y \to X$ satisfies $\rho \circ i = \mathrm{id}_Y$, hence $\rho_* \circ i_* = \mathrm{id}_{\pi(Y)}$. Thus $\rho_*$ has a right inverse, implying it is surjective (onto).
