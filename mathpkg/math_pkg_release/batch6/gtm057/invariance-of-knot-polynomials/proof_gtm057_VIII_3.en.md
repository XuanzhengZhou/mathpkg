The result follows from the Invariance Theorem for elementary ideals (4.5, Chapter VII) and property (3.2) that the $k$th knot polynomial generates the smallest principal ideal containing $E_k$. Since $f_{**}$ is a ring isomorphism, it maps the smallest principal ideal containing $E_k$ to the smallest principal ideal containing $E_k'$, so $\Delta_k$ maps to $\Delta_k'$ up to units.
