The trefoil knot has Alexander polynomial $\Delta(t) = t^2 - t + 1$. Since this is different from the trivial knot's polynomial $\Delta(t) = 1$, this provides an algebraic proof that the trefoil is nontrivially knotted (distinct from the existence proof using $S_3$ representations). The polynomial is normalized to have positive constant term and no negative powers.
