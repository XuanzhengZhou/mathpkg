The mapping $f(x) \mapsto f(x) - f(1)$ is a derivative in $JF$ (it satisfies the axioms). The mapping $f(x) \mapsto \sum_j \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_j}(x_j-1)$ is also a derivative, and both agree on the generators $x_i$ (giving $x_i - 1$). By uniqueness of derivatives with specified values on a free basis (cf. (2.4)), they are equal.
