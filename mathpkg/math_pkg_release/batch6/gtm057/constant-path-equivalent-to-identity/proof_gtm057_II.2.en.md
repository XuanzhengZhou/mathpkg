Let $k$ be a constant path defined by $k(t) = p$, $0 \leq t \leq \|k\|$. The collection $\{h_s\}$ defined by $h_s(t) = p$, $0 \leq t \leq s\|k\|$ is a fixed-endpoint family with $h_1 = k$ and $h_0 = e$, where $e$ is the identity path at $p$.
