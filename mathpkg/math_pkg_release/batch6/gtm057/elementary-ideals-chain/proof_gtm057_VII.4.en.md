Any determinant of an $(n-k) \times (n-k)$ submatrix can be expanded as a combination of cofactors of elements of any row or column, each of which is a determinant of an $(n-k-1) \times (n-k-1)$ submatrix. Thus each generator of $E_k$ lies in $E_{k+1}$.
