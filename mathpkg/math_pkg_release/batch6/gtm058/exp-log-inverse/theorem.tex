The functions $\log_p$ and $\exp_p$ give mutually inverse isomorphisms between the multiplicative group of the open disc of radius $p^{-1/(p-1)}$ about 1 and the additive group of the open disc of radius $p^{-1/(p-1)}$ about 0. This means $\exp_p(\log_p(1+x)) = 1+x$ and $\log_p(\exp_p(x)) = x$ for $x \in D(p^{-1/(p-1)})$.
