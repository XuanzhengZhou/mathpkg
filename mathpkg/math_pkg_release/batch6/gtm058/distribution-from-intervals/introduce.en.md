This proposition provides a practical way to construct hBadic distributions: it suffices to define them on intervals (sets of the form  + p^N\mathbb{Z}_p$) and check the additivity condition across one level of refinement. Every compact-open set can be written as a finite disjoint union of intervals, and the distribution value is then the sum of the interval values.
