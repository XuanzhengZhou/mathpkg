This theorem establishes that the hBadic absolute value extends uniquely to every finite extension of $\mathbb{Q}_p$. The key difficulty is proving the ultrametric inequality $|\alpha+\beta|_p \leq \max(|\alpha|_p, |\beta|_p)$, which is reduced to proving $|1+\gamma|_p \leq 1$ for $|\gamma|_p \leq 1$.
