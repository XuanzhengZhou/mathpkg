The Newton polygon is a combinatorial-geometric tool that encodes the $p$-adic ordinals of the coefficients of a polynomial. Its slopes reveal the $p$-adic ordinals of the reciprocal roots. This makes it an indispensable tool for studying the zeros of polynomials and power series over $p$-adic fields.
