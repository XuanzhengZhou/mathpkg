Let $f(X) = 1 + \sum_{i=1}^n a_i X^i \in 1 + X\Omega[X]$. Consider the points $(0,0), (1,\operatorname{ord}_p a_1), \ldots, (i,\operatorname{ord}_p a_i), \ldots, (n,\operatorname{ord}_p a_n)$ (omitting points with $a_i = 0$). The \text{Newton polygon} of $f(X)$ is the convex hull of these points, specifically the lower convex envelope when these points are viewed from below.
