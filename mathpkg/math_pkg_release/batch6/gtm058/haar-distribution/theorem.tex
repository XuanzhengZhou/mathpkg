The Haar distribution $\mu_{\text{Haar}}$ on $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is defined by
3130479\mu_{\text{Haar}}(a + (p^N)) = \frac{1}{p^N}.3130479
It is the unique distribution (up to a constant multiple) which is translation invariant: $\mu_{\text{Haar}}(a + U) = \mu_{\text{Haar}}(U)$ for all  \in \mathbb{Z}_p$.
