(Lemma 6) Suppose $\lambda_1$ is the first slope of the Newton polygon of $f(X) = 1 + \sum a_i X^i$. Let $c \in \Omega$ with $\operatorname{ord}_p c = \lambda \leq \lambda_1$, and suppose $f(X)$ converges on $D(p^\lambda)$. Then $g(X) = (1-cX)f(X)$ has Newton polygon obtained by joining $(0,0)$ to $(1,\lambda)$ and then translating the Newton polygon of $f(X)$ by 1 to the right and $\lambda$ upward.
