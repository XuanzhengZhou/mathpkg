These theorems guarantee that the hBadic absolute value extends uniquely to any finite extension of $\mathbb{Q}_p$. This is a critical fact: unlike the real case where $\mathbb{C}$ has a unique norm extending the absolute value on $\mathbb{R}$, the p-adic analogue enjoys even stronger uniqueness — any algebraic extension has precisely one extension of the norm.
