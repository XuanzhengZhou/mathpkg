(Lemma 7) Let $f(X) = 1 + \sum_{i=1}^\infty a_i X^i$ have Newton polygon with first slope $\lambda_1$. Suppose $f(X)$ converges on $D(p^{\lambda_1})$ and the line through $(0,0)$ with slope $\lambda_1$ passes through a point $(i,\operatorname{ord}_p a_i)$. Then there exists $x$ with $\operatorname{ord}_p x = -\lambda_1$ and $f(x) = 0$.
