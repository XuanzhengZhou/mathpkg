A norm on a vector space over a non-Archimedean field must itself be non-Archimedean to be compatible with the field norm. Such norms are used to extend the hBadic absolute value from $\mathbb{Q}_p$ to finite extensions. Remarkably, all norms on a finite-dimensional vector space over a locally compact non-Archimedean field are equivalent.
