The integrality of the coefficients follows from a Galois-theoretic argument: the $\mathbb{F}_{q^s}$-points of $H_f$ can be grouped into orbits under the action of $\operatorname{Gal}(\mathbb{F}_{q^s}/\mathbb{F}_q)$, each of size equal to the degree of the smallest field of definition of the point.
