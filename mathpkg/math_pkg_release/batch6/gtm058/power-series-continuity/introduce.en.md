A convergent $p$-adic power series defines a continuous function on its disc of convergence. The proof uses the non-Archimedean property to bound $|f(x)-f(x')|_p$ uniformly, since $|x^n - x'^n|_p \leq |x-x'|_p \max(|x|_p, |x'|_p)^{n-1}$.
