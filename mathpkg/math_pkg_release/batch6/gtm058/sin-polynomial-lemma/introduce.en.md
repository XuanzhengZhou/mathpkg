This lemma expresses $\sin(nx)$ and $\cos(nx)$ as polynomials in $\sin x$ (with an extra $\cos x$ factor for cosine). It is a key step in the derivation of the infinite product expansion for $\sin x$, which leads to Euler's formula for $\zeta(2k)$.
