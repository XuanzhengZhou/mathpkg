Let $K/\mathbb{Q}_p$ be a finite extension. The \text{ramification index} $e = e(K/\mathbb{Q}_p)$ is the index of $\mathbb{Z}$ (the value group of $\mathbb{Q}_p$) in $\operatorname{ord}_p(K^\times)$. Equivalently, $e$ is the denominator of $\operatorname{ord}_p \pi$ for any uniformizer $\pi$ of $K$.
