The ramification index measures how much the prime $p$ ramifies in the extension. An extension is unramified if $e=1$, and totally ramified if $e=[K:\mathbb{Q}_p]$. The fundamental relation is $n=ef$, where $n$ is the degree and $f$ is the residue field degree.
