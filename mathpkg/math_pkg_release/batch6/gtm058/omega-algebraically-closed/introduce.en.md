Despite being an enormous extension (uncountable transcendence degree over $\bar{\mathbb{Q}}_p$), $\Omega$ is algebraically closed. The proof uses the continuity of roots to lift a polynomial over $\Omega$ to polynomials over $\bar{\mathbb{Q}}_p$, finds approximate roots, and takes limits. Thus $\Omega$ is both complete and algebraically closed, like $\mathbb{C}$.
