$\Omega$ is the hBadic analogue of the complex numbers: it is the smallest field that is both algebraically closed and complete with respect to the hBadic absolute value. Its residue field is $\bar{\mathbb{F}}_p$, and its value group is $\mathbb{Q}$ (all rational numbers occur as hBadic ordinals). $\Omega$ is the natural setting for hBadic analysis and power series.
