The Artin-Hasse exponential $E_p(X)$ is defined by removing the $p$-power factors from the infinite product expansion of $\exp(X)$: $$E_p(X) = \prod_{\substack{n=1 \\ p \nmid n}}^\infty (1 - X^n)^{-\mu(n)/n} \in \mathbb{Q}[[X]].$$ Its coefficients lie in $\mathbb{Z}_p$, so $E_p(X) \in 1 + X\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$, and it converges on the open unit disc $D(1^-)$.
