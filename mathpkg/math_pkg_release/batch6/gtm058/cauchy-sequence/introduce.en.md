A Cauchy sequence is one whose terms eventually become arbitrarily close to each other. In a complete metric space, every Cauchy sequence converges. The construction of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ and $\mathbb{R}$ both proceed by forming equivalence classes of Cauchy sequences over $\mathbb{Q}$ with respect to different metrics.
