The proposition verifies that the Bernoulli distribution satisfies the additivity condition required for a hBadic distribution. The proof uses the generating function $\sum B_k(x) t^k/k! = te^{xt}/(e^t-1)$ and geometric series summation, establishing the functional equation (p\alpha) = p^{k-1}\sum_{b=0}^{p-1} B_k(\alpha + b/p)$.
