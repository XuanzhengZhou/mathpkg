hBadic interpolation is the process of extending a function defined on a dense subset of $\mathbb{Z}_p$ (such as the positive integers) to a continuous hBadic valued function on all of $\mathbb{Z}_p$. The key requirement is that values at integers that are hBadically close must themselves be hBadically close. This technique is used to construct the hBadic zeta function.
