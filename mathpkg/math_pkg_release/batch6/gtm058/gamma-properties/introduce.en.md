The $p$-adic gamma function satisfies a functional equation, a reflection (complement) formula, and a multiplication formula analogous to those of the classical gamma function, but adapted to the $p$-adic context. These properties make $\Gamma_p$ a powerful tool for studying $p$-adic special values and for constructing $p$-adic $L$-functions.
