The maximal unramified extension $\mathbb{Q}_p^{\text{unram}}$ is the union of all finite unramified extensions of $\mathbb{Q}_p$. Its ring of integers $\mathbb{Z}_p^{\text{unram}}$ has residue field $\bar{\mathbb{F}}_p$, the algebraic closure of $\mathbb{F}_p$. Every element of $\bar{\mathbb{F}}_p$ has a unique Teichmüller representative in $\mathbb{Z}_p^{\text{unram}}$ which is a root of unity.
