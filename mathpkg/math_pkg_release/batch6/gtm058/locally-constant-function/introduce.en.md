In $\mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$, locally constant functions are just constant functions on connected components. But on totally disconnected spaces like $\mathbb{Z}_p$, there are many nontrivial locally constant functions. They play the role of step functions in hBadic integration theory, being finite linear combinations of characteristic functions of compact-open sets.
