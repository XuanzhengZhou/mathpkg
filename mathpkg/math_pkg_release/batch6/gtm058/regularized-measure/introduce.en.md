The regularized measure $\mu_{k,\alpha}$ is obtained from the Bernoulli distribution by subtracting a suitably scaled version. For =1$, $\mu_{1,\alpha}$ is a genuine measure (bounded by 1), and it plays a fundamental role in hBadic integration analogous to $ in real analysis. Higher $ regularized measures are related to $\mu_{1,\alpha}$ via a key congruence.
