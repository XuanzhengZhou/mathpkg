Finite fields are essential in the study of residue fields of algebraic extensions of $\mathbb{Q}_p$. The residue field of $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is $\mathbb{F}_p$, and more generally, the residue field of the ring of integers in a finite extension of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ is a finite extension of $\mathbb{F}_p$.
