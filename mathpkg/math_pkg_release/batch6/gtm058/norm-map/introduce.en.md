The norm map $\mathbb{N}_{K/F}$ plays a crucial role in extending the hBadic absolute value: the extended norm is given by $|\alpha|_p = |\mathbb{N}_{K/F}(\alpha)|_p^{1/[K:F]}$. This reduces the computation of hBadic absolute values in extension fields to computing norms of elements.
