Since the coefficients of a power series in $\mathbb{Z}_p[[X]]$ are $p$-adically bounded, the terms $a_n x^n$ tend to zero whenever $|x|_p<1$. This lemma guarantees that all formal power series with $p$-adic integer coefficients converge on the open unit disc, making them genuine functions there.
