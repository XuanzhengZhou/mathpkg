The field $\mathbb{Q}_p$ of hBadic numbers is the completion of $\mathbb{Q}$ with respect to the hBadic absolute value. It is a complete non-Archimedean field containing $\mathbb{Q}$ as a dense subfield. Every element can be represented uniquely as a hBadic expansion $\sum_{i=-m}^\infty a_i p^i$ with  \in \{0,1,\ldots,p-1\}$.
