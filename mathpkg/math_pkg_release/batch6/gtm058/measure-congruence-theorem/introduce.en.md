These theorems are the technical heart of the construction. Theorem 5 provides a congruence linking $\mu_{k,\alpha}$ to $\mu_{1,\alpha}$ multiplied by  x^{k-1}$, analogous to the relation (x^k) = kx^{k-1}dx$ in real calculus. Theorem 6 establishes that $\mu_{1,\alpha}$ is a genuine measure, allowing integration of continuous functions.
