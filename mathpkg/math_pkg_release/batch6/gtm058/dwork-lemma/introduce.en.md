Dwork's lemma provides a practical criterion for checking whether a formal power series has $p$-adic integer coefficients. It is the key tool for proving that the Artin-Hasse exponential $E_p(X)$ has coefficients in $\mathbb{Z}_p$. The lemma relates $F(X^p)$ and $F(X)^p$: their quotient is congruent to 1 modulo $p$ precisely when $F$ has integral coefficients.
