A locally analytic function is the $p$-adic analogue of a complex analytic function: it admits a convergent power series expansion around every point. The Iwasawa logarithm is locally analytic on $\Omega\setminus\{0\}$, while the $p$-adic exponential is locally analytic on its disc of convergence. This notion is central to $p$-adic function theory.
