The notation
$$
\prod_{i \in I} S_i
$$
denotes the \textbf{product} (or \textbf{cartesian product}) of the family of sets $S_i$, $i \in I$. An element $x$ of the cartesian product is a function that assigns to each index $i \in I$ an element $x_i \in S_i$. The element $x_i \in S_i$ is also called the \textbf{coordinate} of the element $x$ corresponding to the index $i \in I$.
