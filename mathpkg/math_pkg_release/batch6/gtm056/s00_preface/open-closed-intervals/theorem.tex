If $a$ and $b$ are real numbers such that $a < b$, then the following standard notation is used for the open and closed intervals with $a$ and $b$ as end points:
$$
\begin{aligned}
(a, b) &= \{\, x \in \mathbf{R} : a < x < b \,\},\\
[a, b] &= \{\, x \in \mathbf{R} : a \leq x \leq b \,\},\\
(a, b] &= \{\, x \in \mathbf{R} : a < x \leq b \,\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
