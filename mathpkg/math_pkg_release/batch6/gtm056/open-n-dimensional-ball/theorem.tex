The \textbf{open $n$-dimensional disc} (or \textbf{open $n$-dimensional ball}) is the subset of $\mathbf{R}^n$ defined by
\[
\mathring{D}^n = \{x \in \mathbf{R}^n : \|x\| < 1\},
\]
topologized as a subspace of $\mathbf{R}^n$ with the standard Euclidean topology. The same name is also applied to any topological space homeomorphic to $\mathring{D}^n$.
