The open $n$-dimensional ball $\operatorname{Int} E^n$ is the set of all points in $\mathbf{R}^n$ whose Euclidean norm is strictly less than 1. It is homeomorphic to $\mathbf{R}^n$ itself and serves as the standard model for an $n$-dimensional manifold without boundary. The open ball is the interior of the closed ball and is a contractible, non-compact space.
