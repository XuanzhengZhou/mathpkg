The \textbf{open $n$-dimensional ball} (or open $n$-dimensional disc) is the subset of $\mathbf{R}^n$ defined by
\[
\operatorname{Int} E^n = \{ x \in \mathbf{R}^n : |x| < 1 \},
\]
topologized as a subspace of $\mathbf{R}^n$. The same name is sometimes applied to any topological space homeomorphic to $\operatorname{Int} E^n$.
