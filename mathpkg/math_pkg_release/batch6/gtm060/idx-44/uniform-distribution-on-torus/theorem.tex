Corollary 2. If the frequencies are independent, then every trajectory is uniformly distributed on the torus $T^n$: for any (Jordan) measurable region $D \subset T^n$,
$$\lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{\tau_D(T)}{T} = \frac{\operatorname{mes} D}{(2\pi)^n},$$
where $\tau_D(T)$ is the time the interval $0 \leq t \leq T$ of the trajectory spends inside $D$.
