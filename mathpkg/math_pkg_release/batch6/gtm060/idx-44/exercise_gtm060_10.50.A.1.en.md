Show that the manifold $M_f$ has a neighborhood diffeomorphic to the direct product of the $n$-dimensional torus $T^n$ and the disc $D^n$ in $n$-dimensional euclidean space.

Hint. Take the functions $F_i$ and the angles $\varphi_i$ constructed above as coordinates. In view of the linear independence of the $dF_i$, the functions $F_i$ and $\varphi_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$) give a diffeomorphism of a neighborhood of $M_f$ onto the direct product $T^n \times D^n$.
