Lemma 1. For $f(\varphi) = e^{i(k, \varphi)}$ with $k \in \mathbb{Z}^n$, the time average along $\varphi(t) = \varphi_0 + \omega t$ with independent frequencies $\omega_i$ exists and coincides with the space average:
$$f^*(\varphi_0) = \bar{f} = \begin{cases} 1, & k = 0 \\ 0, & k \neq 0 \end{cases}.$$
