The action functional $S[q] = \int L(q, \dot{q}, t) dt$ is the time integral of the Lagrangian along a path in configuration space. According to Hamilton's principle of least action, the actual physical trajectory between two configurations is the one that makes the action stationary (usually minimal). This variational formulation of mechanics is profoundly elegant and generalizes directly to field theories and quantum mechanics (via the path integral).
