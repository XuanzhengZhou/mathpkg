Find the differential equation for the family of all straight lines in the plane in polar coordinates.

*Hint.* Express the length functional in polar coordinates as $\Phi_{\text{pol}} = \int \sqrt{\dot{r}^2 + r^2 \dot{\varphi}^2}\, dt$ and write the Euler–Lagrange equations for both $r$ and $\varphi$.
