The integral functional \(\Phi(\gamma) = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} L(x(t), \dot{x}(t), t)\, dt\) is differentiable, and its differential is given by \(F(h) = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} [\frac{\partial L}{\partial x} - \frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{x}}] h\, dt + (\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{x}} h)|_{t_0}^{t_1}\). This formula is obtained by expanding the integrand and integrating by parts.
