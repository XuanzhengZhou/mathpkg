Let $(q_1, \ldots, q_{3n})$ be any coordinates in the configuration space of a system of $n$ mass points. Then the evolution of $\mathbf{q}$ with time is subject to the Euler–Lagrange equations

$$\frac{d}{dt} \left( \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{\mathbf{q}}} \right) - \frac{\partial L}{\partial \mathbf{q}} = 0, \quad \text{where } L = T - U.$$
