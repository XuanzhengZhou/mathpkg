Generalized momenta are defined as \(p_i = \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}_i}\), the partial derivative of the lagrangian with respect to the generalized velocity \(\dot{q}_i\). For a simple mechanical system, \(p_i = m_i \dot{q}_i\) coincides with the ordinary momentum, but in general it may depend on both positions and velocities.
