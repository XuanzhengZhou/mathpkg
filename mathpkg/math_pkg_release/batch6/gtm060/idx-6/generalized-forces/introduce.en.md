Generalized forces are defined as \(\frac{\partial L}{\partial q_i}\), the partial derivative of the lagrangian with respect to the generalized coordinate \(q_i\). For a potential system with  = T - U$, this equals \(-\partial U/\partial q_i\), the negative gradient of the potential energy in generalized coordinates.
