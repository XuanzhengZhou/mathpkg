**Proof.** This follows directly from the preceding theorem. If the transformations are canonical, then by the theorem the infinitesimal generator satisfies Hamilton's equations, and hence the one-parameter group evolves by Hamilton's equations. Conversely, if the one-parameter group satisfies Hamilton's equations, then the infinitesimal generator is a hamiltonian vector field, and the flow of a hamiltonian vector field consists of canonical transformations (since the Lie derivative of the symplectic form along a hamiltonian vector field vanishes).
