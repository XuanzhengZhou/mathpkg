Using conservation of angular momentum, the 2D motion in a central field reduces to a 1D problem in the radial coordinate with effective potential $V(r) = U(r) + M^2/(2r^2)$. The centrifugal term $M^2/(2r^2)$ acts as a repulsive barrier preventing the particle from reaching $r = 0$ when $M \neq 0$. This reduction enables complete solution by quadratures.
