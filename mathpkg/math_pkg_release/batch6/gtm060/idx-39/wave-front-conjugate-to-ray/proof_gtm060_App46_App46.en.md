source_section: "Huygens' principle"

Look at points $q_\tau$ of the ray $q_0 q_t$, $0 \leq \tau \leq t$. Take $\varepsilon$ very small. Then the front $\Phi_{q_{t-\varepsilon}}(\varepsilon)$ differs by quantities of order $O(\varepsilon^2)$ from the indicatrix at the point $q_t$, contracted by $\varepsilon$. By Huygens' principle, this front $\Phi_{q_{t-\varepsilon}}(\varepsilon)$ is tangent to the front $\Phi_{q_0}(t)$ at the point $q_t$. Passing to the limit as $\varepsilon \to 0$, we obtain the theorem.
