The evolution of the local coordinates $q = (q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ of a point $\gamma(t)$ under motion in a Lagrangian system on a manifold satisfies the Lagrange equations

$$\frac{d}{dt} \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}} = \frac{\partial L}{\partial q},$$

where $L(q, \dot{q})$ is the expression for the Lagrangian function $L: TM \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ in the coordinates $q$ and $\dot{q}$ on $TM$.
