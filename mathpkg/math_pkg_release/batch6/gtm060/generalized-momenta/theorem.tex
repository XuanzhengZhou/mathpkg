The generalized momentum conjugate to the coordinate $q_i$ is
$$p_i = \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}_i}.$$

Lagrange's equations then take the form $\dot{p}_i = \partial L/\partial q_i$. If a coordinate $q_i$ is cyclic ($\partial L/\partial q_i = 0$), then the corresponding generalized momentum is conserved: $p_i = \text{const}$.
