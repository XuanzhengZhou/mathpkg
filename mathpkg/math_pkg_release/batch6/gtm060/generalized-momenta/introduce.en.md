Generalized momenta are the partial derivatives of the Lagrangian with respect to the generalized velocities. For a standard kinetic energy $T = \frac{1}{2}m\dot{q}^2$, the generalized momentum $p = m\dot{q}$ is the ordinary linear momentum. For angular coordinates, the generalized momentum is the angular momentum. The conservation of generalized momenta associated with cyclic coordinates is the Lagrangian version of Noether's theorem.
