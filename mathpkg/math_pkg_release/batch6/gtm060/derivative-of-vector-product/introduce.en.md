The derivative of the vector (cross) product satisfies the Leibniz rule: $d/dt[\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}] = [\dot{\mathbf{a}}, \mathbf{b}] + [\mathbf{a}, \dot{\mathbf{b}}]$. This follows directly from the bilinearity of the cross product and the definition of the derivative. The lemma is essential for proving conservation of angular momentum.
