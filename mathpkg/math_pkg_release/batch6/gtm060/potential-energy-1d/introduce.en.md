For a one-degree-of-freedom system $\ddot{x} = f(x)$, a potential energy can always be defined as $U(x) = -\int_{x_0}^x f(\xi)d\xi$. The force is recovered as $f = -dU/dx$, and adding a constant to $U$ does not affect the equations of motion. In higher dimensions, not every force field admits such a potential.
