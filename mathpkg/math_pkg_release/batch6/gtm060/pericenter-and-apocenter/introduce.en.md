For bounded motion in a central field, the radial coordinate oscillates between a minimum $r_{\min}$ (pericenter) and maximum $r_{\max}$ (apocenter). These are the turning points where $\dot{r} = 0$ and are determined by the condition $V(r) = E$. The orbit is symmetric about each apsidal ray.
