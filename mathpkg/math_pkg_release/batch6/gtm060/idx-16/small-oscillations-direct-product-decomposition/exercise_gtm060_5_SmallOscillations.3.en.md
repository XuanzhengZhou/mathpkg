Show that the eigenvalues $\lambda_i$ of the form $B$ with respect to $A$ satisfy the characteristic equation

$$\det|B - \lambda A| = 0,$$

all the roots of which are real (the matrices $A$ and $B$ are symmetric, and $A > 0$).
