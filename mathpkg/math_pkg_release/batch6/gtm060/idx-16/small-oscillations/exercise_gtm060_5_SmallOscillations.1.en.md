Find the period of small oscillations of a bead of mass 1 on a wire $y = U(x)$ in a gravitational field with $g = 1$, near an equilibrium position $x = x_0$.

Hint: The kinetic energy is $T = \frac{1}{2}[1 + (\partial U/\partial x)^2]\dot{x}^2$ and the potential energy is $U = mgy = U(x)$. Let $x_0$ be a stable equilibrium position where $(\partial U/\partial x)|_{x_0} = 0$.
