Is the field in the plane minus the origin given by $F_1 = x_2/(x_1^2 + x_2^2)$, $F_2 = -x_1/(x_1^2 + x_2^2)$ conservative?
Show that a field is conservative if and only if its work along any closed contour is equal to zero.
