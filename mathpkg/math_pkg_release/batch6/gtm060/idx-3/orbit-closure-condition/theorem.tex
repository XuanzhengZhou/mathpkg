The angle between successive pericenters and apocenters is given by the integral
$$\Phi = \int_{r_{\min}}^{r_{\max}} \frac{M/r^2 \, dr}{\sqrt{2(E - V(r))}}.$$

The angle between two successive pericenters is $2\Phi$. The orbit is closed if and only if the angle $\Phi$ is commensurable with $2\pi$, i.e., if $\Phi = 2\pi(m/n)$ where $m$ and $n$ are integers.

If $\Phi$ is not commensurable with $2\pi$, then the orbit is everywhere dense in the annulus $r_{\min} \leq r \leq r_{\max}$.
