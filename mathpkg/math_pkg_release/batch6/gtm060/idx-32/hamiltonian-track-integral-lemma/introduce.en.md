This lemma is the key computational tool for proving that Hamiltonian phase flows preserve the symplectic structure. It relates the $\tau$-derivative of the integral of $\omega^2$ over the track $J\gamma$ of a $1$-chain $\gamma$ to the integral of $dH$ over the transported chain $g^\tau\gamma$. The proof uses the definition of the Hamiltonian vector field: for $\xi$ tangent to the chain and $\eta$ the Hamiltonian field velocity, $\omega^2(\xi, \eta) = dH(\xi)$.
