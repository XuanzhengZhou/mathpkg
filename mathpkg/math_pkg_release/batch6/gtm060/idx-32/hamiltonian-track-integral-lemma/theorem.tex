Let $\gamma$ be a $1$-chain in the symplectic manifold $(M^{2n}, \omega^2)$. Let $g^t$ be a Hamiltonian phase flow on $M$ with Hamiltonian function $H$, and let $J\gamma$ be the track of $\gamma$ under the homotopy $g^t$, $0 \leq t \leq \tau$. Then
$$
\frac{d}{d\tau} \int_{J\gamma} \omega^2 = \int_{g^\tau\gamma} dH.
$$
