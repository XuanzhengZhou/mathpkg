The orbital parameter $p = M^2/k$ is a geometric invariant of the Kepler orbit, appearing in the focal equation $r = p/(1 + e \cos \varphi)$. It measures the distance from the focus to the orbit at $\varphi = \pi/2$ (i.e., at right angles to the major axis). The parameter is determined purely by the angular momentum $M$ and the gravitational strength $k$.
