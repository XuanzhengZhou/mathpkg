The focal equation represents conic sections (ellipses, parabolas, and hyperbolas) in a unified polar form with the origin at one focus. The parameter $p$ controls the size, while the eccentricity $e$ determines the shape, distinguishing between bounded elliptic orbits ($e < 1$) and unbounded parabolic or hyperbolic trajectories.
