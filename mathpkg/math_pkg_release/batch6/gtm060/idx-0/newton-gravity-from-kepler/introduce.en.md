Given Kepler's empirical observation that planetary orbits are ellipses with the Sun at a focus, and assuming the motion occurs in a central force field, one can deduce that the potential must be $U(r) = -k/r$, i.e., Newton's universal law of gravitation. This deduction connects the empirical laws of planetary motion to the inverse-square nature of gravitational force.
