Let $U(r) = -kr^{-\beta}$ where $0 < \beta < 2$. Find

$$\Phi_0 = \lim_{E \to -0} \Phi,$$

where $\Phi$ is the angular period.

*Answer:* $\Phi_0 = \int_0^1 \frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^\beta - x^2}} = \frac{\pi}{2 - \beta}$.

Note that $\Phi_0$ does not depend on the angular momentum $M$.
