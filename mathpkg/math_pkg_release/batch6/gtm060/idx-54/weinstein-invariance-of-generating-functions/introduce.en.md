Weinstein's theorem resolves the apparent non-invariance of Poincaré's generating function under changes of canonical coordinates. Near a fixed point with no eigenvalue equal to $-1$, the germ of the generating function is an invariant of the symplectic diffeomorphism up to a smooth change of coordinates and an additive constant. This result provides a coordinate-free foundation for the generating function approach to fixed point theory in symplectic geometry.
