The travel time formula expresses the time needed to go from position $x_1$ to $x_2$ as an integral depending on the potential energy $U(x)$ and total energy $E$.
It follows directly from solving $\dot{x} = \sqrt{2(E - U(x))}$ for $dt = dx / \sqrt{2(E - U(x))}$ and integrating.
This formula reduces the solution of the one-degree-of-freedom system to quadrature (evaluating an integral).
