The energy level hypersurface $\pi_{E_0}$ is the set of all phase points having total energy $E_0$.
It is a three-dimensional submanifold of the four-dimensional phase space, invariant under the phase flow.
The phase flow "flows along" these hypersurfaces: every phase curve with energy $E_0$ lies entirely within $\pi_{E_0}$.
For a spherical pendulum, $\pi_{E_0}$ is a three-dimensional sphere, and the phase curves are its great circles.
