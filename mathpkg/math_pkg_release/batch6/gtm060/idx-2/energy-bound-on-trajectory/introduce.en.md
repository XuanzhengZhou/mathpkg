This corollary of energy conservation states that the motion is confined to the region $\{x: U(x) \leq E\}$ in the configuration plane.
Since kinetic energy $T = E - U(x)$ is nonnegative, the particle cannot enter regions where $U(x) > E$.
This provides a geometric "potential well" that bounds the possible positions of the system.
