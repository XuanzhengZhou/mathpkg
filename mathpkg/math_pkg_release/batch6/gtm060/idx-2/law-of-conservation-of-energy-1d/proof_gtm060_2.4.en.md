$$\frac{d}{dt}(T + U) = \dot{x}\ddot{x} + \frac{dU}{dx}\dot{x} = \dot{x}(\ddot{x} - f(x)) = 0,$$
since $\ddot{x} = f(x)$ by the equation of motion, and $\frac{dU}{dx} = -f(x)$ by definition of the potential energy.
