Show that the connected components of the invariant two-dimensional manifold $V_c$ (Section 28B) in the six-dimensional space $T\mathrm{SO}(3)$ are tori, and that one can choose coordinates $\varphi_1$ and $\varphi_2$ mod $2\pi$ on them so that $\dot{\varphi}_1 = \omega_1(c)$ and $\dot{\varphi}_2 = \omega_2(c)$.

*Hint.* Take the phase of the periodic variation of $M$ as $\varphi_1$.
