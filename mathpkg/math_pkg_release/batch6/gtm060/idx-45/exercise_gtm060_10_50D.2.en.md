Let $\omega_k$ be independent, $a_k > 0$. Calculate

$$\lim_{t \to \infty} \frac{1}{t} \arg \sum_{k=1}^{3} a_k e^{i\omega_k t}.$$

**Answer.** $(\omega_1 \alpha_1 + \omega_2 \alpha_2 + \omega_3 \alpha_3)/\pi$, where $\alpha_1, \alpha_2,$ and $\alpha_3$ are the angles of the triangle with sides $a_k$.
