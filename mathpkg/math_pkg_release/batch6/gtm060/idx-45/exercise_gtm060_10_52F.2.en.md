The length of a pendulum is slowly doubled ($l = l_0(1 + \varepsilon t)$, $0 \leq t \leq 1/\varepsilon$). How does the amplitude $q_{\max}$ of the oscillations vary?

**Solution.** $I = \frac{1}{2} l^{3/2} g^{1/2} q_{\max}^2$; therefore,
$$q_{\max}(t) = q_{\max}(0) \left( \frac{l(0)}{l(t)} \right)^{3/4}.$$
