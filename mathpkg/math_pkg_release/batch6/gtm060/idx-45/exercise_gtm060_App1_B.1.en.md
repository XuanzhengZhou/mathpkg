Find the curvature forms on the plane, on a sphere of radius $R$, and on the Lobachevsky plane.

**Answer.** $\Omega = 0$, $\Omega = R^{-2} dS$, $\Omega = -dS$, where the 2-form $dS$ is the area element on the oriented surface.
