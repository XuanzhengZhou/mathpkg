Show that the closure of a trajectory $\{\varphi(t) = \varphi_0 + \omega t\}$ (on $T^n$) is a torus of dimension $n - r$ if there are $r$ independent relations among the frequencies $\omega$; in this case the motion on $T^{n-r}$ is conditionally periodic with $n - r$ independent frequencies.
