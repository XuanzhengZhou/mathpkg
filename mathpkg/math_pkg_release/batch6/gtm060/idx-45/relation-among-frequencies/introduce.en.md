A resonance or rational relation among the frequencies $\omega_1, \ldots, \omega_n$ of a conditionally periodic motion is encoded by an integer vector $k$ whose dot product with the frequency vector vanishes. Such relations determine the dimension of the closure of trajectories on the $n$-torus: each independent relation reduces the dimension of the invariant torus filled by the motion by one.
