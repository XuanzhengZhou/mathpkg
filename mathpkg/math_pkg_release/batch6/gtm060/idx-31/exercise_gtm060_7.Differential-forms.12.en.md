Prove Poincaré's lemma for 1-forms: every closed 1-form $\omega^1$ on a vector space is the differential of a 0-form (i.e., a function).

*Hint.* Consider the function $\varphi(x_1) = \int_{x_0}^{x_1} \omega^1$ defined by integrating the 1-form along a path from a fixed point $x_0$ to $x_1$. Show that $d\varphi = \omega^1$.
