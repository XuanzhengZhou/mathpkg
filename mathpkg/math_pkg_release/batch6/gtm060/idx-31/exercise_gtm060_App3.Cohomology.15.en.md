Find the first Betti number of the torus $T^2 = S^1 \times S^1$.

*Answer:* $\beta_1(T^2) = 2$. This corresponds to the two independent non-bounding 1-cycles on the torus: the meridian and the parallel. Equivalently, the closed 1-forms $d\theta_1$ and $d\theta_2$ (the pullbacks of the angle forms from each $S^1$ factor) are linearly independent and not exact.
