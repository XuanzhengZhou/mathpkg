The $k$-th \textit{homology group} of a manifold $M$ is the quotient group:
$$H_k(M) = \frac{\{\text{cycles}\}}{\{\text{boundaries}\}} = \frac{\ker \partial_k}{\operatorname{im} \partial_{k+1}},$$
where cycles are chains $c_k$ with $\partial c_k = 0$, and boundaries are chains of the form $\partial c_{k+1}$ (cycles homologous to zero). An element of this group is a class of cycles homologous to one another.
