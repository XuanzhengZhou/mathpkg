The cone operators on chains and on differential forms satisfy the homotopy identities:
For chains: $$\partial \circ p + p \circ \partial = \text{id}.$$
For differential forms: $$d \circ p + p \circ d = \text{id}.$$
As a consequence, if a form $\omega^k$ is closed ($d\omega^k = 0$), then $d(p\omega^k) = \omega^k$, showing every closed form on a vector space is exact.
