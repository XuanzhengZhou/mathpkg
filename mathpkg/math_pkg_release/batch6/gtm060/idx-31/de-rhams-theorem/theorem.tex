The rank of the $k$-th homology group of a smooth manifold $M$ equals the $k$-th Betti number:
$$\operatorname{rank} H_k(M) = \beta_k(M) = \dim H^k(M, \mathbb{R}).$$
Moreover, integration of differential forms over chains induces a nondegenerate bilinear pairing
$$H^k(M, \mathbb{R}) \times H_k(M) \to \mathbb{R}$$
given by $([\omega], [c]) \mapsto \int_c \omega$, which provides an isomorphism $H^k(M, \mathbb{R}) \cong \operatorname{Hom}(H_k(M), \mathbb{R})$.
