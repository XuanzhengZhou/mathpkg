For any chain $c$ on a vector space, the \textit{cone over $c$ with vertex $0$} is the chain $pc$ obtained by joining each point of $c$ to the origin $0$ along straight line segments. The cone operator $p$ satisfies the homotopy relation:
$$\partial \circ p + p \circ \partial = \text{id}.$$
Consequently, if $c$ is a cycle ($\partial c = 0$), then $\partial(pc) = c$.
