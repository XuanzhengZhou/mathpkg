The proof is not included in this section. The text refers the reader to:

\begin{itemize}
\item V. I. Arnold, \textit{Modes and quasi-modes}, Functional Analysis and Its Applications, 6:2 (1972), 94--101.
\item V. N. Karpushkin, \textit{The asymptotic behavior of the eigenvalues of symmetric manifolds and the ``most probable'' representations of finite groups}, Moscow Univ. Math. Bull. 29 (1974), no. 2, 136--139.
\end{itemize}

The result follows from transversality arguments in the space of symmetric quadratic forms (ellipsoids). The codimension of the submanifold of ellipsoids with a given eigenvalue multiplicity pattern is computed using the dimension of the orbit of the symmetry group acting on the space of quadratic forms.
