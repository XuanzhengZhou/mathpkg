Let $\{g^t\}$ be the (local) phase flow of the characteristic vector field $\xi$ on a hypersurface $E^{2n}$ in a contact manifold $M^{2n+1}$. If $I$ is an integral manifold of the contact field and $\eta$ is a vector tangent to $I$, then $g^t I$ is an integral manifold of the contact field for all small $t$. Moreover, the manifold formed by the union

$$\bigcup_{t \text{ small}} g^t I$$

is a Legendre manifold.
