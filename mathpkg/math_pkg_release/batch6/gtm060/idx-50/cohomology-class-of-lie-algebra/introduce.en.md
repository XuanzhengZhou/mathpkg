The cohomology class of a Lie algebra is an equivalence class of $2$-cocycles modulo coboundaries. These classes form the second Lie algebra cohomology group $H^2(\mathfrak{g}; \mathbb{R})$. In mechanics, the cohomology class is the invariant associated to a Poisson action of a Lie group with single-valued Hamiltonians, classifying the obstruction to lifting the action to a genuine Hamiltonian action (a moment map).
