A bilinear skew-symmetric function $C(a,b)$ on a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ is called a \textit{two-dimensional cocycle} of the Lie algebra if it satisfies the condition

$$C([a,b], c) + C([b,c], a) + C([c,a], b) = 0$$

for all $a, b, c \in \mathfrak{g}$. This condition follows from the Jacobi identity in the Lie algebra.
