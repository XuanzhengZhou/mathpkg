This theorem gives an explicit formula for the Riemannian metric on complex projective space induced by the hermitian structure of the ambient space $\mathbb{C}^{n+1}$. The square of the length of a tangent vector is expressed in terms of the hermitian scalar product, generalizing the Fubini-Study metric.
