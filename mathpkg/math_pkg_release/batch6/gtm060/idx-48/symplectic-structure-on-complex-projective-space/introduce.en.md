A symplectic structure on complex projective space $\mathbb{C}P^n$ is obtained by taking the imaginary part of the hermitian form (3) with coefficient $-1/\pi$. This gives a closed, nondegenerate 2-form that makes $\mathbb{C}P^n$ into a K\"ahler manifold and serves as the foundation for constructing symplectic structures on all smooth complex algebraic varieties.
