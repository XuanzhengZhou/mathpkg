The \textit{symplectification} of a contact manifold is the set of all contact forms on the contact manifold, provided with the structure of a symplectic manifold. The set of all contact forms has a natural structure of a smooth manifold of even dimension $N+1$, realized as a principal $\mathbb{R}^\times$-bundle over the contact manifold, where the fiber over a point consists of all contact forms at that point (obtained from each other by multiplication by a nonzero real number).
