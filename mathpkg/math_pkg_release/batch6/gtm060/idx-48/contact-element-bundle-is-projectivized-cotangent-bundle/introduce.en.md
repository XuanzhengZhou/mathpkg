The bundle of contact elements over an $n$-dimensional manifold is the projectivization of the cotangent bundle. This means a contact element can be identified with a nonzero cotangent vector up to scalar multiplication, establishing the fundamental link between contact geometry on the projectivized cotangent bundle and symplectic geometry on the cotangent bundle itself.
