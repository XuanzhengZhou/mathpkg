The proof of this theorem is contained in the first article listed in the introduction to Appendix 2. The calculation involves expressing elements of the Lie algebra of the group $S_0 \text{Diff} T^2$ as real functions on the torus with average value zero (stream functions), choosing a Fourier basis $\{e_k\}$ indexed by wave vectors $k \in \Gamma \setminus \{0\}$ where $\Gamma$ is the dual lattice, and computing the induced geometric quantities on this basis using the right-invariant metric arising from the $L^2$ inner product on velocity fields.
