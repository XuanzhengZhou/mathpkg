Show that the symplectic structure $\Omega$ on the affine chart $(w_k = z_k z_0^{-1})$ of the projective space $\mathbb{C}P^n$ is given by

$$\Omega = \frac{i}{2\pi} d' d'' \ln \sum_{k=0}^{n} |w_k|^2,$$

where $d'$ and $d''$ are the Dolbeault operators: $d' = \sum \frac{\partial}{\partial w_k} dw_k$, $d'' = \sum \frac{\partial}{\partial \bar{w}_k} d\bar{w}_k$.
