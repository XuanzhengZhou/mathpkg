The construction of the Riemannian metric on $\mathbb{C}P^n$ also endows each tangent space with a hermitian structure. By carrying over the hermitian scalar product from the hermitian-orthogonal complement $H$ via the isomorphism $p_*$, one obtains a natural hermitian metric on the complex manifold $\mathbb{C}P^n$ whose real part is the Riemannian metric and whose imaginary part is the symplectic form.
