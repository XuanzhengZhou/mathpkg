The map $p_*: H \to T(\mathbb{C}P^n)_{pz}$ maps the hermitian-orthogonal complement $H$ to the direction of $z$ isomorphically onto the tangent space. Since the scalar square of the hermitian structure carried over from $H$ coincides with formula (2) for the Riemannian metric, the hermitian scalar product formula (3) follows without further calculation by replacing the real inner product with the hermitian one, noting the normalization by $\langle z, z \rangle$.
