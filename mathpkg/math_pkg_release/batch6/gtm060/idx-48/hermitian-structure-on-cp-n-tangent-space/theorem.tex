The hermitian scalar product in the tangent space to $\mathbb{C}P^n$ at $pz$ is given by

$$\langle \zeta_1, \zeta_2 \rangle = \frac{\langle \xi_1, \xi_2 \rangle \langle z, z \rangle - \langle \xi_1, z \rangle \langle z, \xi_2 \rangle}{\langle z, z \rangle^2}$$

for any vectors $\xi_1, \xi_2$ in $T\mathbb{C}_z^{n+1}$ satisfying $p_*\xi_k = \zeta_k \in T(\mathbb{C}P^n)_{pz}$. The point $z$ does not necessarily lie on the unit sphere.
