The canonical $1$-form $\alpha$ on the symplectified space of a contact manifold is the differential $1$-form whose value on a tangent vector $\xi$ equals the value of the contact form $p$ (the point in the symplectification) applied to the projection of $\xi$. This generalizes the canonical $1$-form $p\,dq$ on cotangent bundles.
