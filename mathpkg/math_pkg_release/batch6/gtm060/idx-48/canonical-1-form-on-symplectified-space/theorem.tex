The \textit{canonical $1$-form} $\alpha$ on the symplectified space of a contact manifold is the differential $1$-form whose value on any vector $\xi$ tangent to the symplectified space at a point $p$ is equal to the value on the projection of $\xi$ onto the tangent plane to the contact manifold of the $1$-form on that tangent plane which is the point $p$:

$$\alpha(\xi) = p(\pi_* \xi),$$

where $\pi$ is the projection of the symplectified space onto the contact manifold.
