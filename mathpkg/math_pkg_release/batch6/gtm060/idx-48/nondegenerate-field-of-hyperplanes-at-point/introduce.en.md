A field of hyperplanes is nondegenerate at a point if the $2$-form $d\omega|_{\omega=0}$ restricted to the hyperplane has maximal possible rank. This means every nonzero vector in the hyperplane pairs nontrivially with some other vector under the $2$-form, ensuring the field is maximally far from being integrable at that point.
