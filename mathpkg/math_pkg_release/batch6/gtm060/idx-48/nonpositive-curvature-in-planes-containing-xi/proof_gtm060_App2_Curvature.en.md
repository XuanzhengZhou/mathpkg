The proof follows by direct computation from the explicit formulas of Theorem 14. Substituting the curvature tensor formula $R_{k,l,m,n}$ into the definition of the sectional curvature operator yields a sum-of-squares expression that is manifestly non-positive. The vanishing condition follows from analyzing when each term in the sum is forced to zero, which occurs precisely when $[\xi, \eta] = 0$, i.e., when the two flows are parallel.
