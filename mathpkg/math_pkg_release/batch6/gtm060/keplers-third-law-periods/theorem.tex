\textbf{Kepler's Third Law.} The squares of the revolution periods of two planets on different elliptical orbits have the same ratio as the cubes of their major semi-axes:
$$\frac{T_1^2}{T_2^2} = \frac{a_1^3}{a_2^3}.$$

Equivalently, $T = 2\pi a^{3/2} k^{-1/2}$ where $k$ is the gravitational constant and $a$ is the major semi-axis.
