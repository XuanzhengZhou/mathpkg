**Corollary** (from Lax's Theorem). The operators $L(t) = -\frac{d^2}{dx^2} + u(x,t)$ constructed from a solution $u(x,t)$ of the KdV equation are unitarily equivalent for all $t$. In particular, each of the eigenvalues $\lambda$ of the Sturm-Liouville problem

$$Lf = \lambda f$$

with zero boundary conditions at infinity is a **first integral** of the Korteweg–de Vries equation.
