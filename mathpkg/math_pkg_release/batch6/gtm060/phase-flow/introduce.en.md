The phase flow $g^t$ is a one-parameter group of diffeomorphisms of the phase plane, where $g^t M$ is the state at time $t$ of the system that started at state $M$ at time 0. The group property $g^{t+s} = g^t \circ g^s$ reflects the determinism of Newtonian mechanics. The phase flow gives a complete, global description of all motions of the system.
