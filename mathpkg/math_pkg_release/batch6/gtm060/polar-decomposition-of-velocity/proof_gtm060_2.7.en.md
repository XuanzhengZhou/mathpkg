The vectors $e_r$ and $e_\varphi$ rotate with angular velocity $\dot{\varphi}$, so $\dot{e}_r = \dot{\varphi} e_\varphi$ and $\dot{e}_\varphi = -\dot{\varphi} e_r$. Differentiating $r = r e_r$:
$$\dot{r} = \dot{r} e_r + r \dot{e}_r = \dot{r} e_r + r \dot{\varphi} e_\varphi.$$
