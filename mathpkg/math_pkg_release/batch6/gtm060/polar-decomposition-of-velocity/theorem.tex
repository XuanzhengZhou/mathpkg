\textbf{Lemma.} In polar coordinates, the velocity vector decomposes as
$$\dot{r} = \dot{r} e_r + r \dot{\varphi} e_\varphi,$$
where $e_r$ and $e_\varphi$ are unit vectors in the radial and angular directions. The basis vectors rotate with angular velocity $\dot{\varphi}$:
$$\dot{e}_r = \dot{\varphi} e_\varphi, \quad \dot{e}_\varphi = -\dot{\varphi} e_r.$$
