Once reduced to one dimension, the central force problem can be integrated by quadratures. The time dependence $r(t)$ is obtained from the energy integral, and the orbit equation $\varphi(r)$ follows by eliminating time using $d\varphi/dr = \dot{\varphi}/\dot{r}$. These quadratures provide the complete solution in principle, though the integrals may be expressible only in terms of special functions for most potentials.
