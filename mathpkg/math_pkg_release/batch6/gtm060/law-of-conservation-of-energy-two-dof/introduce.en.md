For conservative two-degree-of-freedom systems, the total energy $E = \frac{1}{2}|\dot{x}|^2 + U(x)$ is conserved. The energy level sets are three-dimensional hypersurfaces in the four-dimensional phase space, invariant under the phase flow. The corollary that motion is confined to the potential well $U(x) \leq E$ is crucial for understanding boundedness of orbits.
