Since kinetic energy $T = E - U(\mathbf{x})$ is nonnegative, the motion is confined to the region $\{ \mathbf{x} : U(\mathbf{x}) \leq E \}$. The particle cannot escape the potential well determined by its initial energy. This geometric constraint provides a powerful qualitative tool for understanding the possible trajectories.
