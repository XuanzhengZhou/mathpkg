The tangent space at the identity element of a Lie group inherits a natural Lie algebra structure. This Lie algebra encodes the infinitesimal structure of the group near the identity. The Lie bracket on this tangent space can be defined either via one-parameter subgroups or via the Poisson bracket of right-invariant vector fields on the group.
