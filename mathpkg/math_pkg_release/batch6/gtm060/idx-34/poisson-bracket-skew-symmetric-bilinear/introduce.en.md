This corollary establishes that the Poisson bracket satisfies the axioms of a Lie bracket: skew-symmetry and bilinearity. Together with the Jacobi identity (which follows from the closedness of the symplectic form), this makes the space of smooth functions on a symplectic manifold into an infinite-dimensional Lie algebra. The linearity in each argument follows directly from the differential geometric definition.
