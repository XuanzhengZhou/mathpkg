The Legendre transformation is an involution on the class of smooth convex functions: applying it twice returns the original function. This property is fundamental to convex analysis and establishes Legendre duality as a symmetric relationship between a convex function and its convex conjugate. The involutivity can be understood geometrically by interpreting the Legendre transform in terms of tangent lines to the graph of the function.
