The phase flow is the one-parameter group of diffeomorphisms $g^t: (\mathbf{p}(0), \mathbf{q}(0)) \mapsto (\mathbf{p}(t), \mathbf{q}(t))$ of phase space onto itself, where $(\mathbf{p}(t), \mathbf{q}(t))$ is the solution of Hamilton's equations with initial condition $(\mathbf{p}(0), \mathbf{q}(0))$ at $t = 0$.
