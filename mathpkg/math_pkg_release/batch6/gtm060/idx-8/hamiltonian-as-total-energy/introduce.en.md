For mechanical systems where the Lagrangian has the standard form $L = T - U$ with kinetic energy $T$ being a quadratic form in the generalized velocities, the Hamiltonian $H$ obtained via Legendre transformation equals the total mechanical energy $H = T + U$. This fundamental identification links the abstract Hamiltonian formalism to the physical notion of energy and underlies the law of conservation of energy in Hamiltonian mechanics.
