Suppose a mechanical system has Lagrangian $L = T - U$, where $U = U(\mathbf{q})$ and the kinetic energy $T$ is a quadratic form with respect to $\dot{\mathbf{q}}$:

$$T = \frac{1}{2} \sum a_{ij} \dot{q}_i \dot{q}_j, \quad a_{ij} = a_{ij}(\mathbf{q}, t).$$

Then the Hamiltonian $H$ is the total energy:

$$H = T + U.$$
