Draw a graph of the multiple-valued "functions" $S(q)$ and $p(q)$ for $t = t_3$ (see Figure 201 in the text). The point of self-intersection of the graph of $S$ corresponds on the graph of $p$ to the Maxwell line: the shaded areas are equal. The graph of $S(q, t)$ has a singularity called a *swallowtail* at the point $(0, t_2)$.
