For a conservative 2-DOF system, the total energy $E = \frac{1}{2}|\dot{\mathbf{x}}|^2 + U(\mathbf{x})$ is a first integral. The proof uses the chain rule and the equation of motion: $dE/dt = (\dot{\mathbf{x}}, \ddot{\mathbf{x}} + \partial U/\partial \mathbf{x}) = 0$. This conservation restricts trajectories to a 3-dimensional energy hypersurface in the 4-dimensional phase space.
