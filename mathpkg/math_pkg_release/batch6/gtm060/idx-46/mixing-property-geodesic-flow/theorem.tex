The phase flow $g^t$ on $T_1M$ has the mixing property: if $A$ and $B$ are two measurable regions in $T_1M$, then

$$\lim_{t \to \infty} \operatorname{mes}[(g^t A) \cap B] = \operatorname{mes} A \cdot \operatorname{mes} B,$$

where $\operatorname{mes}$ denotes the normalized volume (total measure of $T_1M$ equals $1$).
