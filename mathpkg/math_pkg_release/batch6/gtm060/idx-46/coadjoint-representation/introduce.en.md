The coadjoint representation is the dual of the adjoint action. It acts on the dual space $\mathfrak{g}^*$ of the Lie algebra. The coadjoint orbits (orbits of this action) carry a natural symplectic structure (the Kirillov form) and play a fundamental role in the orbit method in representation theory and in the geometry of mechanical systems on Lie groups.
