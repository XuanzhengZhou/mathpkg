Let $\xi, \eta$ be a pair of orthogonal vectors of length 1 in the tangent space $TM_x$ to a riemannian manifold $M$. The curvature $K$ of $M$ in the two-dimensional direction determined by $\xi, \eta$ is expressed in terms of the curvature tensor $\Omega$ by

$$K = \langle \Omega(\xi, \eta)\xi, \eta \rangle,$$

where the brackets $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle$ denote the scalar product giving the riemannian metric.
