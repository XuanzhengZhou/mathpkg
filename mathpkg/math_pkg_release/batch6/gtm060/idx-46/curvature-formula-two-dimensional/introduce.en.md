This theorem expresses sectional curvature -- the Gaussian curvature of the surface swept out by geodesics in a given 2-plane -- directly in terms of the curvature tensor. For an orthonormal pair $\xi, \eta$ spanning the 2-plane $L$, the formula $K = \langle \Omega(\xi,\eta)\xi, \eta \rangle$ recovers the curvature in that direction. This is the fundamental link between the algebraic curvature tensor and the geometric notion of sectional curvature.
