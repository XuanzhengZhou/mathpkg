Consider a two-dimensional subspace $L$ in the tangent space $TM_x$ to a riemannian manifold $M$. Take geodesics emanating from $x$ in all directions in $L$; they form a smooth surface near $x$. This surface lies in $M$ and has an induced riemannian metric. The curvature of $M$ in the direction of the 2-plane $L$ at $x$ is the riemannian (Gaussian) curvature at $x$ of this surface.
