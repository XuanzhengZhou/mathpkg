Let $x(t)$ be a point moving along a geodesic in the manifold $M$ with velocity $v(t) \in TM_{x(t)}$. If the initial condition depends smoothly on a parameter $\alpha$, consider the motion $x(t, \alpha)$ with $x(t, 0) = x(t)$. The \textbf{vector field of geodesic variation} is the derivative with respect to $\alpha$ evaluated at $\alpha = 0$:

$$\xi(t) = \frac{d}{d\alpha} \bigg|_{\alpha=0} x(t, \alpha) \in TM_{x(t)}.$$
