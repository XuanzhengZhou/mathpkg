The proof follows from Noether's theorem: the kinetic energy (Hamiltonian) is right-invariant, so the momentum map for the right $G$-action is conserved. Concretely, $M_s = R_g^* M$ satisfies $dM_s/dt = 0$ because the equations of motion are geodesic equations of a left-invariant metric. The right-invariance of the metric (or more precisely, the invariance of the Lagrangian under right translations) yields this conservation law by the same argument as in the classical rigid body case ($G = SO(3)$).
