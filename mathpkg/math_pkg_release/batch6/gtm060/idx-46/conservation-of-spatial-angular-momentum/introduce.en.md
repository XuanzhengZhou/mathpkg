This theorem generalizes the conservation of angular momentum for an ordinary rigid body (no external torques) to any Lie group with left-invariant metric. The spatial angular momentum $M_s$, defined via right translation of the momentum covector to $\mathfrak{g}^*$, is a constant of motion. This follows from the right-invariance of the Hamiltonian (kinetic energy) under the right action of $G$.
