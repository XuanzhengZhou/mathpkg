Compute $\Phi$ if $A$ is a rotation of the plane through a small angle $\varphi$.

Answer.

$$A = \begin{pmatrix} \cos \varphi & \sin \varphi \\ -\sin \varphi & \cos \varphi \end{pmatrix}, \quad \Phi = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & \varphi \\ -\varphi & 0 \end{pmatrix}.$$
