A left-invariant Riemannian metric on a Lie group is one for which left multiplication acts by isometries. Such metrics are in one-to-one correspondence with inner products on the Lie algebra. They generalize the kinetic energy metric of a rigid body (which is left-invariant on SO(3)) and are essential for studying geodesic flows on Lie groups and their applications to hydrodynamics.
