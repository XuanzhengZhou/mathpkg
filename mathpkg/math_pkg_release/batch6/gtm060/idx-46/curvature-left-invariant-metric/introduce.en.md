This formula gives the sectional curvature of a Lie group with left-invariant metric entirely in terms of the Lie bracket $[\cdot,\cdot]$ and the bilinear operation $B$ (which encodes the metric). Since left translation is an isometry, curvature is determined at the identity. Applied to the diffeomorphism group $SDiff(D)$, this yields curvature formulas relevant to hydrodynamic stability.
