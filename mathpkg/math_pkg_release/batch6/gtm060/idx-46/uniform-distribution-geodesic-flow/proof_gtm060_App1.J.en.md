The proof follows from the ergodicity of the geodesic flow on compact manifolds of negative curvature. This was established by E. Hopf and extended by D. V. Anosov and Ya. G. Sinai. The exponential instability (Anosov property) implies the flow is ergodic with respect to the Liouville measure, and the Birkhoff ergodic theorem then yields the uniform distribution property.
