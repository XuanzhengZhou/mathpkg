The proof is outside the scope of this appendix. Arnold refers the reader to the work of D. V. Anosov (1967) and Ya. G. Sinai (1960s). The result follows from the theory of Anosov (U-systems) flows: the exponential instability of geodesics (derived from the Jacobi equation with negative curvature) implies the geodesic flow is an Anosov flow, which has the property that the union of dense trajectories has full measure.
