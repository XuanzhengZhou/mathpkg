On a compact manifold of negative curvature, the geodesic flow is topologically transitive in a very strong sense: almost every trajectory visits every region of the phase space $T_1M$ arbitrarily closely. This density property is a consequence of the exponential instability of geodesics and is a hallmark of chaotic (Anosov) dynamical systems.
