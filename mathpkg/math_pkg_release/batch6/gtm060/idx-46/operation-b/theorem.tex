Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a Lie algebra with inner product $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle$ given by a symmetric operator $A: \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}^*$. The bilinear operation $B: \mathfrak{g} \times \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}$ is defined implicitly by the relation

$$\langle [a, b], c \rangle = \langle B(c, a), b \rangle \quad \text{for all } a, b, c \in \mathfrak{g}.$$

Equivalently, $B$ is determined by the condition that the covariant derivative at the identity satisfies $(\nabla_\xi \eta)_e = B(\xi, \eta)$ for a left-invariant metric.
