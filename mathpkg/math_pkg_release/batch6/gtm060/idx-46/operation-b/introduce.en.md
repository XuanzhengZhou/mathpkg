The operation $B$ encodes the Levi-Civita connection of a left-invariant metric at the identity. It is the algebraic object through which covariant derivatives, geodesic equations (Euler equations), and curvature are expressed purely in Lie-algebraic terms. For the rigid body ($G = SO(3)$), $B$ is related to the vector cross product.
