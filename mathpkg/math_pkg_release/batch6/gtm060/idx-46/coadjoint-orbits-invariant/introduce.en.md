This theorem shows that Euler's equation on $\mathfrak{g}^*$ is tangent to coadjoint orbits. Each coadjoint orbit is an invariant submanifold, and the Euler flow restricted to an orbit is a Hamiltonian system with respect to the Kirillov symplectic form. This geometrizes the conservation laws: the Casimir functions (functions constant on coadjoint orbits) are first integrals.
