Stationary rotations generalize the steady rotations of an ordinary rigid body (rotations about principal axes). On a Lie group with left-invariant metric, they correspond to one-parameter subgroups -- the group-theoretic analogue of "spinning about a fixed axis." They are critical points of the kinetic energy restricted to coadjoint orbits.
