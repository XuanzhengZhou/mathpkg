The formula $Ad_g[\xi,\eta] = [Ad_g\xi, Ad_g\eta]$ follows from the fact that the differential of a group homomorphism (inner automorphism $A_g$) preserves the Lie bracket. For $Ad_{gh} = Ad_g Ad_h$, note that $A_{gh} = A_g \circ A_h$, and taking differentials at the identity gives $Ad_{gh} = (A_g \circ A_h)_{*e} = (A_g)_{*e} \circ (A_h)_{*e} = Ad_g Ad_h$.
