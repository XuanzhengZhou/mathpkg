On every coadjoint orbit $V \subset \mathfrak{g}^*$, Euler's equation

$$\frac{dM_c}{dt} = \{\omega_c, M_c\}$$

is a Hamiltonian system with respect to the Kirillov symplectic form. The Hamiltonian function is the kinetic energy restricted to the orbit:

$$H: V \to \mathbb{R}, \quad H(M_c) = \frac{1}{2}(M_c, A^{-1}M_c).$$
