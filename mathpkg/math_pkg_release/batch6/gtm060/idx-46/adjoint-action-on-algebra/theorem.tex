The map $Ad$ is differentiable. Its derivative at the identity of the group is a linear map from $\mathfrak{g}$ to $\operatorname{End}(\mathfrak{g})$, denoted by $ad$:

$$ad = Ad_{*e} : \mathfrak{g} \to \operatorname{End}(\mathfrak{g}), \quad ad_\xi = \frac{d}{dt}\bigg|_{t=0} Ad_{e^{t\xi}},$$

where $e^{t\xi}$ is the one-parameter subgroup with tangent vector $\xi$.
