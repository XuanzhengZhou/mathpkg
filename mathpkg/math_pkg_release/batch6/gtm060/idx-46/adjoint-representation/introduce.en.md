The adjoint representation $Ad: G \to GL(\mathfrak{g})$ encodes how the group acts on its own Lie algebra by conjugation. It is a fundamental tool in Lie theory, linking the group structure to linear algebra. Its derivative at the identity yields the adjoint action $ad$ of the Lie algebra on itself, given by the Lie bracket.
