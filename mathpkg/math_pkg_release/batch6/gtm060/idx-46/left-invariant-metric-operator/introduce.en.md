The operator $A_g$ is the left-translated version of the inertia operator $A$, providing the Riemannian metric at every point of the group. The commutative diagram $A_g = L_{g^{-1}}^* \circ A \circ L_{g^{-1}}$ expresses the left-invariance: the metric at any point is obtained by pulling back the inner product on the Lie algebra.
