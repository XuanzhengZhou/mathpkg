Given an inertia operator $A: \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}^*$, extend it to a symmetric operator $A_g: TG_g \to T^*G_g$ at every point $g \in G$ by left translation:

$$A_g \xi = L_{g^{-1}}^* A L_{g^{-1}} \xi.$$

The scalar product at $g$ is

$$\langle \xi, \eta \rangle_g = (A_g \xi, \eta) = (A_g \eta, \xi).$$
