Starting from Euler's equation for momentum $dM_c/dt = \{\omega_c, M_c\}$ and the relation $M_c = A\omega_c$, we compute $\dot{\omega}_c = A^{-1} \dot{M}_c = A^{-1}\{\omega_c, A\omega_c\}$. By definition of the operation $B$, this equals $B(\omega_c, \omega_c)$. The result follows from translating the equation from $\mathfrak{g}^*$ to $\mathfrak{g}$ via the inverse inertia operator $A^{-1}$.
