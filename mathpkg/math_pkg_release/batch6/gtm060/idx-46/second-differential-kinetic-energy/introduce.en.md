This formula computes the Hessian of the kinetic energy at a stationary rotation, which determines stability. The sign-definiteness of $d^2 H$ (restricted to the orbit) indicates whether the stationary rotation is stable (positive-definite) or unstable (indefinite). For $SO(3)$, this yields Euler's theorem: rotations about the largest and smallest principal axes are stable, while rotation about the middle axis is unstable.
