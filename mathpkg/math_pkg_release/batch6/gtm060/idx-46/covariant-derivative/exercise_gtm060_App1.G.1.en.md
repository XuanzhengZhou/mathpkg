Prove the following properties of covariant differentiation:

1. $\nabla_\xi v$ is a bilinear function of $\xi$ and $v$.
2. $\nabla_\xi (fv) = (L_\xi f)v + f(x)\nabla_\xi v$, where $f$ is a smooth function and $L_\xi f$ is the derivative of $f$ in the direction of the vector $\xi$ in $TM_x$.
3. $L_\xi \langle v, w \rangle = \langle \nabla_\xi v, w(x) \rangle + \langle v(x), \nabla_\xi w \rangle$.
4. $\nabla_{v(x)} w - \nabla_{w(x)} v = [w, v](x)$ (where $L_{[w, v]} = L_v L_w - L_w L_v$).
