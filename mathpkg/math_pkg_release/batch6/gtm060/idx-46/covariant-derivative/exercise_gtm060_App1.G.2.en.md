Show that the curvature tensor can be expressed in terms of covariant differentiation in the following way:

$$\Omega(\xi_0, \eta_0)\zeta_0 = -\nabla_\xi \nabla_\eta \zeta + \nabla_\eta \nabla_\xi \zeta + \nabla_{[\eta, \zeta]} \zeta,$$

where $\xi, \eta, \zeta$ are any vector fields whose values at the point under consideration are $\xi_0, \eta_0$, and $\zeta_0$.
