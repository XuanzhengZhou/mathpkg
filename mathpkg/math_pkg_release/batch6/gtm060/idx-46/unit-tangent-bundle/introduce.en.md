The unit tangent bundle $T_1M$ of a Riemannian manifold $M$ is the space of all unit-length tangent vectors. By conservation of energy, geodesic flow with unit speed is confined to $T_1M$, making it the natural $(2n-1)$-dimensional phase space for studying geodesics. Dynamical properties like ergodicity and mixing are studied on this invariant manifold.
