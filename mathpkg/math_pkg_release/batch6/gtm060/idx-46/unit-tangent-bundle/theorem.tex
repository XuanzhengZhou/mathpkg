Let $M$ be a riemannian manifold of dimension $n$. The \textbf{unit tangent bundle} $T_1M \subset TM$ is the fiber bundle consisting of the unit spheres in the tangent spaces to $M$ at every point:

$$T_1M = \{ v \in TM : \|v\| = 1 \}.$$

A point of $T_1M$ is represented as a vector of length $1$ at a point of $M$. Each energy level manifold of the geodesic flow has dimension $2n - 1$. Fixing the energy at $\frac{1}{2}$ (unit speed), the level manifold is precisely $T_1M$.
