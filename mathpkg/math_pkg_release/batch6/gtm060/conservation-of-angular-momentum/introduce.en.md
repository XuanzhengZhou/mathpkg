For motion in any central field, the angular momentum $\mathbf{M} = \mathbf{r} \times \dot{\mathbf{r}}$ is conserved. The proof relies on the fact that central forces produce acceleration collinear with position, so $\dot{\mathbf{M}} = \mathbf{r} \times \ddot{\mathbf{r}} = 0$. This conservation law is a consequence of rotational symmetry via Noether's theorem.
