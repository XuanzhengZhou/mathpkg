$$(\sum m_i)\dot{r} = \sum m_i \dot{r}_i = P.$$
Therefore, $(\sum m_i)\ddot{r} = dP/dt = \sum_i F_i$ by the momentum theorem. The corollary follows since $\sum_i F_i = 0$ for a closed system.
