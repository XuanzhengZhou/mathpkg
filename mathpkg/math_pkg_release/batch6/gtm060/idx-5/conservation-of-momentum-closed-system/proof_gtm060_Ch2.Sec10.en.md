**Proof.** From the theorem on the rate of change of momentum, $d\mathbf{P}/dt = \sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i$. For a closed system, there are no external forces, so $\mathbf{F}'_i = 0$ for all $i$. Hence $d\mathbf{P}/dt = 0$ and $\mathbf{P} = \text{const}$.
