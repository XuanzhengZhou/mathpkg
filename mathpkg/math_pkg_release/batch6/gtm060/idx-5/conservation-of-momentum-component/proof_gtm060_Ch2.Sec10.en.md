**Proof.** From $d\mathbf{P}/dt = \sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i$, the component along a direction $\mathbf{e}$ satisfies $dP_{\mathbf{e}}/dt = (\sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i, \mathbf{e})$. If $\sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i \perp \mathbf{e}$, then $dP_{\mathbf{e}}/dt = 0$, so $P_{\mathbf{e}} = \text{const}$.
