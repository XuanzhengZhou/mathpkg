For a system consisting entirely of two points $i$ and $j$ interacting via a central force of magnitude $f_{ij}(\rho)$ depending only on the distance $\rho = |\mathbf{r}_i - \mathbf{r}_j|$, the potential energy of the interaction is given by
$$U_{ij}(\mathbf{r}) = \int_{r_0}^{r} f_{ij}(\rho) \, d\rho,$$
where $r = |\mathbf{r}_i - \mathbf{r}_j|$. The force satisfies
$$-\frac{\partial U_{ij}(\mathbf{r}_i - \mathbf{r}_j)}{\partial \mathbf{r}_i} = f_{ij} \mathbf{e}_{ij},$$
where $\mathbf{e}_{ij}$ is the unit vector from the $j$-th point to the $i$-th point.
