The \textit{angular momentum of a system} of $n$ material points relative to a point $0$ is the sum of the angular momenta of all points in the system:
$$\mathbf{M} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} [\mathbf{r}_i, m_i \dot{\mathbf{r}}_i],$$
where $\mathbf{r}_i$ is the radius vector of the $i$-th point relative to $0$.
