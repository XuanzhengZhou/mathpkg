**Proof.** From $d\mathbf{M}/dt = \sum_i [\mathbf{r}_i, \mathbf{F}'_i]$, if the system is closed then $\mathbf{F}'_i = 0$ for all $i$. Hence $d\mathbf{M}/dt = 0$ and $\mathbf{M} = \text{const}$.
