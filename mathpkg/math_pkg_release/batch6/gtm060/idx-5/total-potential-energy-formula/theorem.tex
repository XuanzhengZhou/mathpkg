For a system with pairwise central interactions and conservative external forces, the total potential energy is the sum of all pairwise interaction potentials plus the external potentials:
$$U(\mathbf{r}) = \sum_{i > j} U_{ij}(|\mathbf{r}_i - \mathbf{r}_j|) + \sum_{i} U'_i(\mathbf{r}_i).$$

The total mechanical energy
$$E = T + U = \sum_i \frac{\dot{\mathbf{r}}_i^2}{2} + \sum_{i > j} U_{ij} + \sum_i U'_i$$
is then conserved.
