**Proof.** By definition of the center of mass, $(\sum m_i) \mathbf{r} = \sum m_i \mathbf{r}_i$, so $(\sum m_i) \dot{\mathbf{r}} = \mathbf{P}$. Differentiating again:
$$\left(\sum m_i\right) \ddot{\mathbf{r}} = \frac{d\mathbf{P}}{dt} = \sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i,$$
where the last equality follows from the theorem on the rate of change of momentum.
