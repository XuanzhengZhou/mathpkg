For a rotation of a rigid body fixed at a point $O$, with angular velocity $\Omega = \Omega \mathbf{e}$ ($\Omega = |\Omega|$) around the $\mathbf{e}$ axis, the kinetic energy is equal to

$$T = \frac{1}{2} I_e \Omega^2, \quad \text{where } I_e = \sum_i m_i r_i^2$$

and $r_i$ is the distance of the $i$-th point to the $\mathbf{e}$ axis.
