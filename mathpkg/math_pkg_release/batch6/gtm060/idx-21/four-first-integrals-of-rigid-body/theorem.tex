In the problem of the motion of a rigid body around a stationary point $O$, in the absence of outside forces, there are four first integrals:

$$M_x, \quad M_y, \quad M_z, \quad E = T.$$

Here $M_x, M_y, M_z$ are the three components of the angular momentum vector, and $E = T$ is the total energy (equal to the kinetic energy in the absence of potential forces).
