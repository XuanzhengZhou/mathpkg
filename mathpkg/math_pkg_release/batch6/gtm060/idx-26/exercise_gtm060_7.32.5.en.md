Show that the mapping
$$(\omega_1, \omega_2) \rightarrow \omega_1 \wedge \omega_2$$
is bilinear and skew symmetric:
$$\omega_1 \wedge \omega_2 = -\omega_2 \wedge \omega_1,$$
$$(\lambda' \omega'_1 + \lambda'' \omega''_1) \wedge \omega_2 = \lambda' \omega'_1 \wedge \omega_2 + \lambda'' \omega''_1 \wedge \omega_2.$$
