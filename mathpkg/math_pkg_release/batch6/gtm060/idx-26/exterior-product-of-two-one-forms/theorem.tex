Let $\omega_1$ and $\omega_2$ be two $1$-forms on $\mathbb{R}^n$. Their exterior product $\omega_1 \wedge \omega_2$ is the $2$-form defined on pairs of vectors $(\xi_1, \xi_2)$ by the determinant:
$$(\omega_1 \wedge \omega_2)(\xi_1, \xi_2) = \begin{vmatrix} \omega_1(\xi_1) & \omega_2(\xi_1) \\ \omega_1(\xi_2) & \omega_2(\xi_2) \end{vmatrix} = \omega_1(\xi_1)\omega_2(\xi_2) - \omega_1(\xi_2)\omega_2(\xi_1).$$
