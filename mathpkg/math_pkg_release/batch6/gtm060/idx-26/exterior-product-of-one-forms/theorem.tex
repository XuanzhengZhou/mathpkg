Let $\omega_1, \ldots, \omega_k$ be $k$ 1-forms on $\mathbb{R}^n$. Their exterior product $\omega_1 \wedge \cdots \wedge \omega_k$ is the $k$-form defined by the determinant:
$$(\omega_1 \wedge \cdots \wedge \omega_k)(\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_k) = \begin{vmatrix} \omega_1(\xi_1) & \cdots & \omega_k(\xi_1) \\ \vdots & & \vdots \\ \omega_1(\xi_k) & \cdots & \omega_k(\xi_k) \end{vmatrix}.$$
