The Galilean coordinate space $R \times R^3$ is the standard model of Galilean space-time, where $R$ is the time axis and $R^3$ is Euclidean space. Its Galilean transformations are generated by three types: uniform motions (Galilean boosts), translations, and rotations, yielding a 10-dimensional group.
