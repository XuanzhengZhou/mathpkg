This KAM variant concerns the existence of invariant tori in a neighborhood of an equilibrium position of an autonomous Hamiltonian system. After reduction to Birkhoff normal form (which exists when low-order resonances are absent), the coefficients $\omega_{kl}$ of the quadratic part of $H_0(\tau)$ play the role of the Hessian in the standard nondegeneracy condition. The isoenergetic variant of the condition also appears. For two degrees of freedom, the isoenergetic nondegeneracy condition implies full Liapunov stability, a result that cannot be obtained from linearization alone.
