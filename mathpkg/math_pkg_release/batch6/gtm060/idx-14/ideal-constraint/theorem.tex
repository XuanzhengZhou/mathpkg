A holonomic constraint is called \textit{ideal} if the work of the constraint forces on any virtual variation is zero:

$$\sum_i (\mathbf{R}_i, \xi_i) = 0, \quad \forall \{\xi_i\} \in TM_{\mathbf{x}}.$$

Equivalently, the constraint force at each configuration is orthogonal to the configuration manifold in the kinetic energy metric.
