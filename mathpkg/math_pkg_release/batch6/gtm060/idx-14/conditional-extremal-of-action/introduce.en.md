A conditional extremal of the action is a curve on a constrained configuration manifold that makes the action functional stationary with respect to variations that remain tangent to the constraint surface. Unlike unconstrained extremals, conditional extremals account for the geometric restriction imposed by holonomic constraints. In local coordinates on the constraint manifold, the condition reduces to the standard Euler-Lagrange equations, providing a bridge between the variational approach and the direct Newtonian treatment with constraint forces.
