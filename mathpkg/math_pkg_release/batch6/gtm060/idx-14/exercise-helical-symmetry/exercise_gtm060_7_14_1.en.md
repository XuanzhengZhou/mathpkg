Suppose that a particle moves in the field of the uniform helical line

$$x = \cos \varphi, \quad y = \sin \varphi, \quad z = c\varphi.$$

Find the law of conservation corresponding to this helical symmetry.

\textbf{Answer.} In any system which admits helical motions leaving this helical line fixed, the quantity $I = c P_3 + M_3$ is conserved, where $P_3$ is the third component of linear momentum and $M_3$ is the third component of angular momentum.
