For a point mass $\mathbf{x}$ of mass $m$ constrained to lie on a smooth surface $M$ in $\mathbb{R}^3$ with potential energy $U(\mathbf{x})$, the \textit{constraint force} $\mathbf{R}$ is defined by

$$\mathbf{R} = m \ddot{\mathbf{x}} + \frac{\partial U}{\partial \mathbf{x}}.$$

For a system of $n$ point masses $\mathbf{x}_i$ with masses $m_i$, the constraint forces are

$$\mathbf{R}_i = m_i \ddot{\mathbf{x}}_i + \frac{\partial U}{\partial \mathbf{x}_i}.$$
