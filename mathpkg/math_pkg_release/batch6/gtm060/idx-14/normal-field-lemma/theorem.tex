Let $\mathbf{f} : [t_0, t_1] \to \mathbb{R}^N$ be a continuous vector field. If, for every continuous tangent vector field $\xi$, tangent to $M$ along a curve $\mathbf{x}$ (i.e., $\xi(t) \in TM_{\mathbf{x}(t)}$, with $\xi(t_0) = \xi(t_1) = 0$), we have

$$\int_{t_0}^{t_1} \mathbf{f}(t) \cdot \xi(t) \, dt = 0,$$

then the field $\mathbf{f}(t)$ is perpendicular to $M$ at every point $\mathbf{x}(t)$, i.e., $(\mathbf{f}(t), \mathbf{h}) = 0$ for every vector $\mathbf{h} \in TM_{\mathbf{x}(t)}$.
