The angular momentum of a material point of unit mass relative to the point $0$ is the vector product

$$\mathbf{M} = [\mathbf{r}, \dot{\mathbf{r}}].$$

The vector $\mathbf{M}$ is perpendicular to the plane of the vectors $\mathbf{r}$ and $\dot{\mathbf{r}}$. In general, the moment of a vector $\mathbf{a}$ "applied at the point $\mathbf{r}$" relative to the point $0$ is $[\mathbf{r}, \mathbf{a}]$.
