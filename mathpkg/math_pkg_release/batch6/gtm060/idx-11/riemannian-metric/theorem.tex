Let $U$ be a chart of an atlas for $M$ with coordinates $q_1, \ldots, q_n$. Then a Riemannian metric is given by the formula

$$ds^2 = \sum_{i,j=1}^{n} a_{ij}(q)\, dq_i\, dq_j, \qquad a_{ij} = a_{ji},$$

where $dq_i$ are the coordinates of a tangent vector.

The functions $a_{ij}(q)$ are assumed to be differentiable as many times as necessary.
