In local coordinates $(q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ on a chart of a Riemannian manifold, the Riemannian metric is expressed by the symmetric quadratic differential form $ds^2 = \sum a_{ij}(q)\, dq_i dq_j$, where $a_{ij} = a_{ji}$ are smooth functions of the coordinates. The squared length element $ds^2$ applied to a tangent vector with components $\xi_i$ yields $\sum a_{ij}(q) \xi_i \xi_j$. The functions $a_{ij}(q)$ constitute the components of the metric tensor.
