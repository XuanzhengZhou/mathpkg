Let $V$ be a smooth manifold and $G$ a Lie group acting on $V$ as a group of diffeomorphisms. Let $M = T^*V$ be the cotangent bundle of $V$ with the usual symplectic structure $\omega = d\alpha$. For each element $a$ of the Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$, define the hamiltonian function of the corresponding one-parameter group $g^t$ by

$$H_a(x) = \alpha\left( \frac{d}{dt} \bigg|_{t=0} g^t x \right), \quad x \in T^*V.$$

Then this action of $G$ on $T^*V$ is a Poisson action.
