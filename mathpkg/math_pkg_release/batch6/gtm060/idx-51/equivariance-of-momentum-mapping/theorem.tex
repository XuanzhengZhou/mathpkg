Under the momentum mapping $P$, a Poisson action of a connected Lie group $G$ on a symplectic manifold $M$ is taken to the co-adjoint action of $G$ on the dual space $\mathfrak{g}^*$ of its Lie algebra. That is, the following diagram commutes:

$$\begin{array}{ccc}
M & \xrightarrow{g} & M \\
\downarrow P & & \downarrow P \\
\mathfrak{g}^* & \xrightarrow{\operatorname{Ad}_{g^{-1}}^*} & \mathfrak{g}^*
\end{array}$$

where $g \in G$ acts on $M$ by the given Poisson action, and on $\mathfrak{g}^*$ by the co-adjoint representation.
