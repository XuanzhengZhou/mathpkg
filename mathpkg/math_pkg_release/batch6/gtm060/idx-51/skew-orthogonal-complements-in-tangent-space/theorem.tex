Let $M$ be a symplectic manifold with a Poisson action of a Lie group $G$, and let $P: M \to \mathfrak{g}^*$ be the momentum mapping. At a point $x \in M$, consider:

$$T(M_p), \quad \text{the tangent space to the level manifold } M_p = P^{-1}(p),$$
$$T(Gx), \quad \text{the tangent space to the orbit of the group } G.$$

Then these two subspaces are skew-orthogonal complements to one another in the tangent space $T_x M$:

$$T(M_p) = \left( T(Gx) \right)^{\perp}, \quad T(Gx) = \left( T(M_p) \right)^{\perp}.$$
