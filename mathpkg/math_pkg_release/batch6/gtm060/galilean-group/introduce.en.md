The Galilean group consists of all affine transformations of Galilean space-time that preserve time intervals and the Euclidean distance between simultaneous events. Its elements — Galilean transformations — include uniform motions (boosts), spatial rotations, and space-time translations. The Galilean group encodes the relativity principle of Newtonian mechanics.
