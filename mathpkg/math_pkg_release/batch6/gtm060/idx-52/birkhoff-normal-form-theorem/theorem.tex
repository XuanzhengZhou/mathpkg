Assume that the characteristic frequencies $\omega_l$ do not satisfy any resonance relation of order $s$ or smaller. Then there exists a canonical coordinate system in a neighborhood of the equilibrium position such that the Hamiltonian is reduced to a Birkhoff normal form of degree $s$ up to terms of order $s+1$:
$$H(p,q) = H_s(P,Q) + R, \quad R = O(|P| + |Q|)^{s+1}.$$
