Calculate the value of the forms $\omega_1 = dx_2 \wedge dx_3$, $\omega_2 = x_1 dx_3 \wedge dx_2$, and $\omega_3 = dx_3 \wedge dr^2$ ($r^2 = x_1^2 + x_2^2 + x_3^2$), on the pair of vectors $\xi = (1, 1, 1)$, $\eta = (1, 2, 3)$ at the point $x = (2, 0, 0)$.

Answer. $\omega_1 = 1$, $\omega_2 = -2$, $\omega_3 = -8$.
