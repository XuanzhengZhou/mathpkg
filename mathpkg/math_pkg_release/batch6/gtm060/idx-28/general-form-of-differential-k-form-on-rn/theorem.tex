Every differential $k$-form on the space $\mathbb{R}^n$ with a given coordinate system $x_1, \ldots, x_n$ can be written uniquely in the form
$$\omega^k = \sum_{i_1 < \cdots < i_k} a_{i_1, \ldots, i_k}(x)\, dx_{i_1} \wedge \cdots \wedge dx_{i_k},$$
where the $a_{i_1, \ldots, i_k}(x)$ are smooth functions on $\mathbb{R}^n$.
