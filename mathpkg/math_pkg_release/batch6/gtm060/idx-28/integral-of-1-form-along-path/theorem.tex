Let $\omega^1$ be a 1-form on a manifold $M$ and let
$$\gamma: [0 \leq t \leq 1] \to M$$
be a smooth map (the "path of integration"). The integral of the form $\omega^1$ on the path $\gamma$ is defined as a limit of Riemann sums:
$$\int_{\gamma} \omega^1 = \lim_{\Delta \to 0} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \omega^1(\xi_i).$$
The tangent vectors $\xi_i$ are constructed by dividing the interval $0 \leq t \leq 1$ into parts $\Delta_i: t_i \leq t \leq t_{i+1}$; the interval $\Delta_i$ is regarded as a tangent vector to the $t$-axis.
