A differential $k$-form at a point $x$ of a manifold $M$ generalizes the notion of a linear functional on tangent vectors to a multilinear, skew-symmetric function of $k$ tangent vectors. It assigns a real number to each ordered $k$-tuple of tangent vectors at $x$, changing sign under odd permutations of the arguments. This construction is the fiberwise foundation for the global theory of differential forms on manifolds.
