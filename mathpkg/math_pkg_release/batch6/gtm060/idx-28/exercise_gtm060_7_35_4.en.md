Show that the map $f^*$ preserves operations on forms:
$$f^*(\lambda_1\omega_1 + \lambda_2\omega_2) = \lambda_1 f^*(\omega_1) + \lambda_2 f^*(\omega_2),$$
$$f^*(\omega_1 \wedge \omega_2) = (f^*\omega_1) \wedge (f^*\omega_2).$$
