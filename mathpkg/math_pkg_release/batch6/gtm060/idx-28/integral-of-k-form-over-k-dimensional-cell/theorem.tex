Let $\omega$ be a differential $k$-form on an $n$-dimensional manifold $M$. Let $\sigma = (D, f, \text{Or})$ be a $k$-dimensional cell. The integral of the $k$-form $\omega$ over the $k$-dimensional cell $\sigma$ is defined as the integral of the pullback form over the polyhedron $D$:
$$\int_{\sigma} \omega = \int_D f^* \omega.$$
