Let $\dot{\mathbf{x}} = \mathbf{f}(\mathbf{x})$, $\mathbf{x} = (x_1, \ldots, x_n)$, be a system of ordinary differential equations with flow $g^t$. Let $D(0)$ be a region in $\mathbf{x}$-space with volume $v(0)$, and let $D(t) = g^t D(0)$ with $v(t) = \text{volume of } D(t)$. Then

$$\left. \frac{dv}{dt} \right|_{t=0} = \int_{D(0)} \text{div } \mathbf{f} \, d\mathbf{x}, \qquad (d\mathbf{x} = dx_1 \cdots dx_n).$$
