Let $\dot{\mathbf{x}} = \mathbf{f}(\mathbf{x})$, $\mathbf{x} = (x_1, \ldots, x_n)$, be a system of ordinary differential equations whose solutions extend to the whole time axis. Let $\{g^t\}$ be the corresponding group of transformations:

$$g^t(\mathbf{x}) = \mathbf{x} + \mathbf{f}(\mathbf{x})t + O(t^2), \quad (t \to 0).$$

Let $D(0)$ be a region in $\mathbf{x}$-space with volume $v(0)$, and let $D(t) = g^t D(0)$ with $v(t) = \text{volume of } D(t)$.

If $\text{div } \mathbf{f} \equiv 0$, then $g^t$ preserves volume: $v(t) = v(0)$ for all $t$.
