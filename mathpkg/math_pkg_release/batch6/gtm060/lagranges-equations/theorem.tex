\textbf{Lagrange's Equations.} For a mechanical system with lagrangian $L(q, \dot{q}, t)$, the equations of motion are
$$\frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}} - \frac{\partial L}{\partial q} = 0.$$

For a newtonian potential system $L = T - U$ with $T = \frac{1}{2}\sum a_{ij}(q,t)\dot{q}_i\dot{q}_j$, this gives
$$\frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial T}{\partial \dot{q}} - \frac{\partial T}{\partial q} = -\frac{\partial U}{\partial q}.$$
