Using energy conservation $E = \frac{1}{2}\dot{x}^2 + U(x)$, the velocity is expressed as $\dot{x} = \sqrt{2(E-U(x))}$, and separating variables yields an integral formula for the travel time between two positions. This quadrature reduces the solution of the equation of motion to a single integration.
