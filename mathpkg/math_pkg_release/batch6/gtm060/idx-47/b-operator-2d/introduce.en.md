The bilinear operation $B$ on the Lie algebra of divergence-free vector fields takes a particularly elegant form in two dimensions. Expressed in terms of stream functions, $B(c, a)$ is given by the negative of the product of the Laplacian of the second stream function with the gradient of the first, modulo a gradient correction term. This formula reflects the deep interplay between the Lie algebra structure of incompressible vector fields and the Poisson bracket formalism on two-dimensional symplectic manifolds.
