This proposition establishes a deep connection between the Lie algebra structure of divergence-free vector fields and the Hamiltonian formalism in two dimensions. The commutator of two velocity fields corresponds to the Poisson bracket of their stream functions. This relation reveals that the Lie algebra of incompressible vector fields in two dimensions is isomorphic to the Lie algebra of Hamiltonian functions under the Poisson bracket, providing a crucial link between hydrodynamics and symplectic geometry.
