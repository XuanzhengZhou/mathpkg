Suppose that the vector fields $A$ and $B$ are given by their components $A_i, B_i$ in coordinates $x_i$. Find the components of the Poisson bracket.

*Solution given in the text:*
$$[A, B]_j = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \left( B_i \frac{\partial A_j}{\partial x_i} - A_i \frac{\partial B_j}{\partial x_i} \right).$$
