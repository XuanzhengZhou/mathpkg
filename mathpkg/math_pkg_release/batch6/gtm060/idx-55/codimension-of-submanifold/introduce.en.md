The codimension of a submanifold captures how many independent constraints are needed to specify it within its ambient manifold. In the context of ellipsoids of revolution, the fact that they have codimension at least 2 means that a single equation cannot characterize them -- at least two independent conditions are required to force an ellipsoid to have coincident axes.
