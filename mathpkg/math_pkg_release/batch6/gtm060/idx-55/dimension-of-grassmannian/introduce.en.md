The Grassmannian $\text{Gr}(k,n)$ of $k$-dimensional subspaces in an $n$-dimensional vector space is a smooth manifold of dimension $k(n-k)$. This can be seen by representing a generic $k$-plane as the graph of a linear map from a $k$-dimensional space to an $(n-k)$-dimensional space, which is given by a $k \times (n-k)$ matrix with $k(n-k)$ independent entries.
