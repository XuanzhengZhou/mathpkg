For a system where the force between points $i$ and $j$ is central and depends only on distance, $F_{ij} = f_{ij}(|r_i - r_j|) e_{ij}$ with $f_{ij} = f_{ji}$, the potential energy of the interaction is
$$U_{ij}(r) = \int_{r_0}^{r} f_{ij}(\rho) d\rho.$$

The force on point $i$ from the interaction potential is then
$$-\frac{\partial U_{ij}(r_i - r_j)}{\partial r_i} = f_{ij} e_{ij}.$$

The total interaction potential energy is $U(r) = \sum_{i>j} U_{ij}(|r_i - r_j|)$.
