Mass is defined operationally through the ratio of accelerations produced by the same force. If the same spring produces accelerations $\ddot{x}_1$ and $\ddot{x}_2$ on two bodies, their mass ratio is $m_2/m_1 = \ddot{x}_1/\ddot{x}_2$. This definition is independent of the spring's extension and of the particular spring used, reflecting an intrinsic property of bodies. The definition of force as $F = m\ddot{x}$ then follows naturally.
