$$\frac{dT}{dt} = \sum_i m_i(\dot{r}_i, \ddot{r}_i) = \sum_i (\dot{r}_i, F_i).$$
Integrating from $t_0$ to $t_1$:
$$T(t_1) - T(t_0) = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} \sum_i (\dot{r}_i, F_i) dt = \int_{r(t_0)}^{r(t_1)} (F, dr).$$
