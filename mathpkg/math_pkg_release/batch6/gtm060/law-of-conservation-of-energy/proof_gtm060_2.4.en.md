$$\frac{d}{dt}(T + U) = \dot{x}\ddot{x} + \frac{dU}{dx}\dot{x} = \dot{x}(\ddot{x} - f(x)) = 0,$$
since $f(x) = -dU/dx$ and $\ddot{x} = f(x)$ by the equation of motion. Therefore $E(x(t), \dot{x}(t))$ is constant.
