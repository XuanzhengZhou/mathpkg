Every central field is automatically conservative, with a potential energy that depends only on the radial distance. The proof uses the fact that in a central field $F = \Phi(r) e_r$, and the line integral reduces to a one-dimensional integral $\int \Phi(r) dr$, which is manifestly path-independent. This theorem is the foundation for reducing central force problems to one-dimensional effective potential problems.
