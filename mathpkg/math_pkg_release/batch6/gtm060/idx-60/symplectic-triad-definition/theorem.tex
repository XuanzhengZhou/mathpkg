A \textbf{symplectic triad} $(H, L, \ell)$ consists of a smooth hypersurface $H$ in a symplectic manifold and a lagrangian submanifold $L$ which is tangent to $H$ to first order along a hypersurface $\ell$ of $L$. The \textbf{lagrangian variety generated by the triad} is the image of $L$ in the manifold of characteristics of the hypersurface $H$.
