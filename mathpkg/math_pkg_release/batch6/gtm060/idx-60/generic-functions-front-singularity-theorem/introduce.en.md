This 1976 theorem is dual to Lyashko's result: all generic holomorphic functions vanishing at the most singular point of a simple front singularity are locally equivalent under diffeomorphisms preserving the front. At a swallowtail, the normal form is the coordinate function $a$. This equivariant Morse lemma is fundamental for studying metamorphoses of moving wave fronts by reducing the time function to normal form.
