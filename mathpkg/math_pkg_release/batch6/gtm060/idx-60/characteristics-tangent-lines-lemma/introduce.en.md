Lemma A identifies the characteristics of the manifold of lines tangent to a hypersurface. Using the identification of cotangent vectors with tangent vectors via the euclidean structure, unit vectors to a hypersurface form a codimension-3 submanifold in phase space whose characteristics define the geodesic flow. Projecting to the space of lines maps characteristics to characteristics.
