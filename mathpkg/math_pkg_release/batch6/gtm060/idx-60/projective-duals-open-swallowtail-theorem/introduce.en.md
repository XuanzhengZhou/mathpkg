Shcherbak's theorem (1982) connects projective duality with the open swallowtail singularity. For a generic one-parameter family of space curves, when one curve develops a point of double flatness of type $(1,2,5)$, the projective dual surfaces form an open swallowtail in space-time. This provides another geometric realization of this fundamental singularity.
