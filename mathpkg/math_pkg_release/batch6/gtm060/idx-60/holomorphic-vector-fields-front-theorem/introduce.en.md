Lyashko's theorem (1979) provides a normal form for holomorphic vector fields in a neighborhood of a simple front singularity. All such vector fields that are transverse to the front are equivalent under holomorphic diffeomorphisms preserving the front. The normal form at a swallowtail is the field $\partial/\partial c$ in the polynomial representation.
