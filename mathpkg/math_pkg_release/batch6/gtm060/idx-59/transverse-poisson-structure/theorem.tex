On a minimal transverse manifold to a symplectic leaf at a nongeneric point of a Poisson manifold, there arises a (unique up to diffeomorphism) local Poisson structure — the **transverse Poisson structure** (cf. A. Weinstein, The local structure of Poisson manifolds, J. Diff. Geom.).

The linearized transverse Poisson structure at a distinguished point is given by

$$\{x_i, x_j\} = \sum_k c_{i,j}^k x_k + \text{higher order terms},$$

where $c_{i,j}^k$ are the structure constants of a finite-dimensional Lie algebra.
