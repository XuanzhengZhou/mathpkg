The **degree of non-quasi-homogeneity** of a germ $f$ is the dimension of the quotient space

$$\frac{(f, \partial f / \partial x_1, \ldots, \partial f / \partial x_n)}{(\partial f / \partial x_1, \ldots, \partial f / \partial x_n)},$$

i.e., the dimension of the factor space of the ideal generated by $f$ and its partial derivatives modulo the Jacobian ideal of $f$.
