For $\ell \leq 6$, in generic $\ell$-parameter families of forms $dx \wedge dy \wedge dz / f(x, y, z)$, one finds only forms which in the neighborhood of each point are locally equivalent to one of the following 24 types:

$$\frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{1}, \quad \frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x},$$

$$\frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^2 + y^2 \pm z^2}, \quad \frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^3 + y^2 \pm z^2},$$

$$\frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^4 \pm y^2 \pm z^2}, \quad \frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^5 + y^2 \pm z^2},$$

$$\frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^2 y \pm y^3 + z^2}, \quad \frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^6 \pm y^2 \pm z^2},$$

$$\frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^2 y + y^4 \pm z^2}, \quad \frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^7 + y^2 \pm z^2},$$

$$\frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^2 y \pm y^5 + z^2}, \quad \frac{dx \wedge dy \wedge dz}{x^3 + y^4 \pm z^2}.$$
