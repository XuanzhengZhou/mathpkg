The proof follows directly from the definitions. If $$X$$ and $$Y$$ generate local groups of contact diffeomorphisms, then their commutator generates the commutator of these groups, which again consists of contact diffeomorphisms. Hence the Lie bracket $$[X, Y]$$ is a contact vector field.
