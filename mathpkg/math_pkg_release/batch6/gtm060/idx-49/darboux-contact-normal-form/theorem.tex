Every differential 1-form defining a nondegenerate field of hyperplanes on a manifold of dimension $$2n + 1$$ can be written in some local coordinate system in the normal form

$$\omega = x\,dy + dz,$$

where $$x = (x_1, \ldots, x_n)$$, $$y = (y_1, \ldots, y_n)$$ and $$z$$ are the local coordinates.
