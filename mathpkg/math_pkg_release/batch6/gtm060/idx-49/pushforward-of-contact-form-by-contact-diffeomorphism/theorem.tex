The image of a contact form $$p$$ with point of contact $$x$$ under the action of a contact diffeomorphism $$f$$ of the contact manifold to itself is the form

$$f!\,p = \left(f^*_{f(x)}\right)^{-1} p.$$

In simple terms, we carry the form $$p$$ from the tangent space at the point $$x$$ to the tangent space at $$f(x)$$ using the diffeomorphism $$f$$ (whose derivative at $$x$$ determines an isomorphism between these two tangent spaces). The form $$f!\,p$$ is a contact form since the diffeomorphism $$f$$ is a contact diffeomorphism.
