The proof is clear from the preceding theorems: the symplectification map sends contact vector fields to Hamiltonian vector fields with homogeneous Hamiltonians of degree 1, and this map preserves the Lie bracket since the symplectification of a commutator of flows is the commutator of the symplectified flows.
