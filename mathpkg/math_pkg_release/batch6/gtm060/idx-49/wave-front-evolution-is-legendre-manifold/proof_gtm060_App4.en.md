The geodesic flow consists of contact diffeomorphisms (by the theorem on geodesic flow of contact elements). A contact diffeomorphism carries Legendre manifolds to Legendre manifolds. The family of elements tangent to the initial wave front is a Legendre manifold. Therefore its image under the geodesic flow after time $$t$$ is also a Legendre manifold. It should be noted that this new Legendre manifold may not be the family of all elements tangent to some smooth manifold, since a wave front may develop singularities.
