Legendre submanifolds are the contact analog of Lagrangian submanifolds in symplectic geometry. Any contactomorphism (contact-structure-preserving diffeomorphism) maps Legendre submanifolds to Legendre submanifolds, preserving their fundamental property of being isotropic and of maximal dimension.
