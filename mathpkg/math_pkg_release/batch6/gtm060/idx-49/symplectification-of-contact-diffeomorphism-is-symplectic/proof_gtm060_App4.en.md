The assertion of the theorem follows from the fact that the canonical 1-form, the symplectic 2-form, and the action of the group of real numbers are all determined by the contact structure itself (for their construction we did not use coordinates or any other non-invariant tools), and the diffeomorphism $$f$$ preserves the contact structure. It follows from this that $$f!$$ preserves all that which was invariantly constructed using the contact structure, in particular the 1-form $$\alpha$$, its derivative $$d\alpha$$, and the action of the group.
