The energy level hypersurface $\pi_{E_0}$ is an invariant manifold of the phase flow: every trajectory starting on it remains on it for all time. The phase velocity vector is everywhere tangent to $\pi_{E_0}$, meaning the dynamics are constrained to this 3-dimensional surface. This dimensional reduction is a crucial consequence of having a first integral.
