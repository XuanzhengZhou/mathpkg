The trajectory of a particle moving in a central field with potential $U = -k/r$ is given by the focal equation of a conic section:

$$r = \frac{p}{1 + e \cos \varphi},$$

where the parameter $p$ and eccentricity $e$ are defined by

$$p = \frac{M^2}{k}, \quad e = \sqrt{1 + \frac{2EM^2}{k^2}}.$$

For $E < 0$, we have $e < 1$ and the orbit is an ellipse.
