Let $U(r) = -kr^{-\beta}$, where $0 < \beta < 2$. Find

$$\Phi_0 = \lim_{E \to -0} \Phi.$$

**Answer.** 

$$\Phi_0 = \int_0^1 \frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^\beta - x^2}} = \frac{\pi}{2 - \beta}.$$

Note that $\Phi_0$ does not depend on $M$.
