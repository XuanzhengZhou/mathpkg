The parameter $p$ of an ellipse (more generally, of a conic section) is a geometric constant that appears in the focal equation $r = p/(1 + e \cos \varphi)$. In the context of central field motion with an inverse-square force $U = -k/r$, the parameter is expressed in terms of the conserved angular momentum $M$ and the gravitational constant $k$ as $p = M^2/k$. The parameter has dimensions of length and determines the scale of the orbit.
