Find the shape of the trajectories of the small oscillations of a point mass on the plane, sitting inside an equilateral triangle and connected by identical springs to the vertices (Figure 89).

*Solution.* Under rotation by $120^\circ$ the system is mapped onto itself. Consequently, all directions are characteristic, and both characteristic frequencies are the same: $U = \frac{1}{2}\omega^2(x^2 + y^2)$. Therefore, the trajectories are ellipses (cf. Figure 20).
