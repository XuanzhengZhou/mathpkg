This geometric theorem describes the relationship between the semi-axes of an ellipsoid and those of its central cross-section by a hyperplane. The semi-axes of the cross-section interlace with those of the original ellipsoid. It is equivalent to Theorem 3 on the separation of characteristic frequencies under a linear constraint and is proved using the extremal characterization of semi-axes (Theorem 5).
