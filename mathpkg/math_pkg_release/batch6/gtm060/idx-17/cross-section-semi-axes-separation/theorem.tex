Consider the cross-section of the $n$-dimensional ellipsoid $E = \{q : (Bq, q) = 1\}$ with semi-axes $a_1 \geq a_2 \geq \cdots \geq a_n$ by a hyperplane $\mathbb{R}^{n-1}$ through its center. Then the semi-axes of this $(n-1)$-dimensional ellipsoid---the cross-section $E'$---separate the semi-axes of the ellipsoid $E$:
$$a_1 \geq a'_1 \geq a_2 \geq a'_2 \geq \cdots \geq a_{n-1} \geq a'_{n-1} \geq a_n.$$
