Let $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^3$ be a surface. The Gauss curvature and the mean curvature of $f$ are the following two functions on $U$:

$$K(u) := \kappa_1(u) \cdot \kappa_2(u), \quad H(u) := \frac{1}{2}(\kappa_1(u) + \kappa_2(u)),$$

where $\kappa_1(u)$ and $\kappa_2(u)$ are the principal curvatures at $u \in U$.
