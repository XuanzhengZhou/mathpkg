Let $\phi: V \to U$ be a change of variables given by $u^i = u^i(v^1, v^2)$. Then the fundamental matrix $(\tilde{g}_{ij})$ of $\tilde{f} = f \circ \phi$ is related to $(g_{ij})$ of $f$ by
$$\tilde{g}_{ij}(v) = \sum_{k,l} \frac{\partial u^k}{\partial v^i} \frac{\partial u^l}{\partial v^j} g_{kl}(\phi(v)).$$
