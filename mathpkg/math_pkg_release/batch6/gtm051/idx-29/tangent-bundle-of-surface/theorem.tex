The \textbf{tangent bundle} $TM$ of a differentiable surface $M$ is the four-dimensional differentiable manifold defined in (5.5.5), obtained by endowing the set
$$TM := \bigcup_{p \in M} T_p M$$
with the differentiable structure induced by the charts $(Tu_{\alpha}, TM_{\alpha})_{\alpha \in A}$, where for each chart $(u_{\alpha}, M_{\alpha})$ of $M$,
$$TM_{\alpha} := \pi^{-1}(M_{\alpha}), \qquad Tu_{\alpha}: TM_{\alpha} \to TU_{\alpha} = U_{\alpha} \times \mathbb{R}^2$$
sends a tangent vector with representative $X_{\alpha} \in T_{u_{\alpha}(p)}\mathbb{R}^2$ to $(u_{\alpha}(p), X_{\alpha})$.
