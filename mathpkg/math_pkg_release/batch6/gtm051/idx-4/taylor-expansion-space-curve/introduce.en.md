The local behavior of a space curve near a point is completely determined, up to third order, by its curvature, torsion, and the derivative of curvature at that point. By substituting the Frenet equations $\dot{e}_i(t) = |\dot{c}(t)| \sum_j \omega_{ij} e_j(t)$ into the Taylor series of the position vector $c(t)$, one obtains explicit expansions whose coefficients involve only the geometric invariants $\kappa$, $\tau$, and $\dot{\kappa}$.
