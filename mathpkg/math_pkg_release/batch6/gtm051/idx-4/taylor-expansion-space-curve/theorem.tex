Let $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^3$ be a space curve parametrized by arc length, with Frenet frame $e_1(t), e_2(t), e_3(t)$, curvature $\kappa(t)$, and torsion $\tau(t)$. Then the Taylor expansion of $c(t)$ at $t_0 = 0$ up to third order is determined by the values $\kappa(0)$, $\tau(0)$, and $\dot{\kappa}(0)$ through substitution of the Frenet equations into the Taylor series of $c$.
