Show that the curvature and torsion of a space curve are in general given by the formulae
$$\kappa(t) = \frac{|\dot{c}(t) \times \ddot{c}(t)|}{|\dot{c}(t)|^3},$$
$$\tau(t) = \frac{\det(\dot{c}(t), \ddot{c}(t), \dddot{c}(t))}{|\dot{c}(t) \times \ddot{c}(t)|^2},$$
where $x \times y$ is the cross-product in $\mathbb{R}^3$.
