Let $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^3$ be a regular space curve with distinguished Frenet frame $e_1(t), e_2(t), e_3(t)$. The \textbf{curvature} $\kappa(t)$ and \textbf{torsion} $\tau(t)$ of $c$ are defined by
$$\kappa(t) := \frac{\dot{e}_1(t) \cdot e_2(t)}{|\dot{c}(t)|} > 0,$$
$$\tau(t) := \frac{\dot{e}_2(t) \cdot e_3(t)}{|\dot{c}(t)|}.$$
The Frenet equations in matrix form are
$$\dot{e}(t) = |\dot{c}(t)| \begin{pmatrix} 0 & \kappa(t) & 0 \\ -\kappa(t) & 0 & \tau(t) \\ 0 & -\tau(t) & 0 \end{pmatrix} e(t).$$
