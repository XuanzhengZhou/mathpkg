The Frenet connection forms $\omega_{ij}$ are invariant under Euclidean congruences. If two curves are related by a rigid motion (rotation followed by translation), their connection forms coincide. This reflects the fact that the Frenet frame and its associated differential invariants are intrinsic to the geometry of the curve, not to its position in space.
