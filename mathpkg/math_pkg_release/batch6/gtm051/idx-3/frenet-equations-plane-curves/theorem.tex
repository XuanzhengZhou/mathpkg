For a regular plane curve $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^2$ with distinguished Frenet frame $\{e_1(t), e_2(t)\}$ and connection form $\omega_{12}(t) = \dot{e}_1(t) \cdot e_2(t)$, the Frenet equations of (1.3.1) specialize to

$$\dot{c}(t) = |\dot{c}(t)| \, e_1(t),$$
$$\dot{e}_1(t) = \omega_{12}(t) \, e_2(t),$$
$$\dot{e}_2(t) = -\omega_{12}(t) \, e_1(t).$$

Equivalently, letting $\kappa(t) = \omega_{12}(t)/|\dot{c}(t)|$ denote the curvature, we have

$$\dot{e}_1(t) = \kappa(t) |\dot{c}(t)| \, e_2(t), \qquad \dot{e}_2(t) = -\kappa(t) |\dot{c}(t)| \, e_1(t).$$
