Under reparametrization of a curve, the Frenet connection forms scale in a controlled way. When normalized by the speed of the curve, the ratio $\omega_{ij}/|\dot{c}|$ is invariant under reparametrization. This property justifies defining the curvature functions as normalized connection forms, ensuring that curvature is a geometric invariant independent of parametrization.
