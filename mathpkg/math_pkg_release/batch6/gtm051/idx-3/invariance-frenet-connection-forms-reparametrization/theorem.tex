Let $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^n$ be a curve satisfying condition (1.2.2), and let $\tilde{c}(s) = c(\phi(s))$ be a reparametrization by a diffeomorphism $\phi: J \to I$. Let $\omega_{ij}$ and $\tilde{\omega}_{ij}$ be the connection forms of the distinguished Frenet frames of $c$ and $\tilde{c}$ respectively. Then

$$\frac{\tilde{\omega}_{ij}(s)}{|\tilde{c}'(s)|} = \frac{\omega_{ij}(\phi(s))}{|\dot{c}(\phi(s))|}$$

for all $i, j$ and all $s \in J$, where $'$ denotes differentiation with respect to $s$ and $\dot{}$ denotes differentiation with respect to $t$.
