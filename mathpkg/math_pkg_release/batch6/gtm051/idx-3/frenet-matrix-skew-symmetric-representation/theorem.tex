For the distinguished Frenet frame of a curve $c: I \to \mathbb{R}^n$ satisfying condition (1.2.2), the matrix of connection forms $\omega = (\omega_{ij})$ is skew-symmetric and tridiagonal, given by

$$\omega = |\dot{c}| \begin{pmatrix}
0 & \kappa_1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
-\kappa_1 & 0 & \kappa_2 & \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & -\kappa_2 & \ddots & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & \cdots & -\kappa_{n-2} & 0 & \kappa_{n-1} \\
0 & \cdots & 0 & -\kappa_{n-1} & 0
\end{pmatrix}$$

where $\kappa_i$ are the curvature functions defined in Definition 1.3.3.
