A piecewise smooth curve is a continuous curve that is differentiable on each subinterval of a finite partition, with finitely many corner points where differentiability may fail. This generalization of smooth curves is essential for defining the rotation number of closed plane curves, which must accommodate curves with corners. The terminology distinguishes regular, closed, and simple closed piecewise smooth curves.
