The **curvature tensor** of a parametrized surface $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^3$ is the collection of functions

$$
R_{ijkl} = \sum_m g_{lm} R_{ijk}^m, \qquad 1 \leq i, j, k, l \leq 2,
$$

where

$$
R_{ijk}^m := \Gamma_{ij,k}^m - \Gamma_{ik,j}^m + \sum_l \left( \Gamma_{ij}^l \Gamma_{lk}^m - \Gamma_{ik}^l \Gamma_{lj}^m \right), \qquad 1 \leq i, j, k, m \leq 2,
$$

with $\Gamma_{ij}^k$ the Christoffel symbols of the second kind, $(g_{ij})$ the first fundamental form, and $\Gamma_{ij,k}^m = \partial \Gamma_{ij}^m / \partial u^k$.
