Prove: The lines of curvature on a surface of second order (3.7.3) determine a Liouville line element. For example, the line element on the ellipsoid is

$$ds^2 = \operatorname{grad} \psi(v) \, dv^2 + \operatorname{grad} \psi(w) \, dw^2$$

(cf. (3.9.5)).
