This lemma applies the divergence product rule to the specific vector field $X = \operatorname{grad}\psi_2$, yielding an identity that involves the Laplace–Beltrami operator $\Delta\psi_2$ and the Riemannian inner product of gradients. Combined with Gauss's theorem, it directly yields Green's first formula, making it a fundamental computational identity in Riemannian surface theory.
