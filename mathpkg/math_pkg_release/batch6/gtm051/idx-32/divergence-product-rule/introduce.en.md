This lemma states the product rule for the divergence operator on a Riemannian surface. For a differentiable function $\psi$ and a vector field $X$, the divergence of $\psi X$ decomposes as $\psi \operatorname{div} X$ plus the pairing of the differential $d\psi$ with $X$. This formula is a key step in deriving Green's integral formulae from Gauss's divergence theorem.
