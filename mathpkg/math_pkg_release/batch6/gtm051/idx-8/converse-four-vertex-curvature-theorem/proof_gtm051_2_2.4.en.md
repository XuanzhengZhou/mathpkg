The proof constructs the curve explicitly via its tangent angle function $\theta(t) = \int_0^t \kappa(s) ds$. The condition on the total curvature ($\int \kappa = 2\pi$) and the qualitative properties of $\kappa$ ensure that the resulting curve closes smoothly and is simple and convex.
