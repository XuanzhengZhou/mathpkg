Prove: The circumference of a closed, strictly convex curve of constant width $d$ is equal to $\pi d$.

*Hint:* Use the parametrization by the tangent direction angle. For a curve of constant width, the sum of the support functions $h(\theta) + h(\theta + \pi) = d$, where $h(\theta)$ is the support function. The circumference is $L = \int_0^{2\pi} h(\theta) d\theta$, from which the result follows.
