This exercise establishes a geometric inequality relating the curvature of a unit-speed curve to its distance from the origin. At a point where the squared distance $|c(t)|^2$ attains a local maximum, the curvature $\kappa(t_0)$ is bounded below by $1/\rho$, where $\rho$ is the distance from the origin to the curve at that point. This provides a quantitative link between the bending of a curve and its position relative to the origin.
