The proof of the Cohn-Vossen theorem is not included in the source text. The text presents this as a result of a detailed study of complete open surfaces initiated by Cohn-Vossen, without reproducing the proof. The paraboloid of revolution in $\mathbb{R}^3$ is pointed to as the prototypical example on which all the stated properties can be verified directly.
