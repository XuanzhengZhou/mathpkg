Arc length parameterization is a canonical choice of parametrization where the curve is traversed at constant unit speed. Under this parametrization, the arc length of a curve segment equals the difference of its parameter values, and geometric quantities such as curvature take particularly simple forms. Every regular curve can be reparametrized by arc length, making this the preferred parametrization for theoretical analysis in the differential geometry of curves.
