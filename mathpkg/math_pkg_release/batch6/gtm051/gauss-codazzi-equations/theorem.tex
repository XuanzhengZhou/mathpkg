For a surface $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^3$, the coefficients of the first and second fundamental forms satisfy the following compatibility conditions.

**Gauss equation:**
$$K = \frac{R_{1212}}{\det(g_{ij})}, \quad R_{1212} = -\frac{1}{2}(g_{11,22} + g_{22,11} - 2g_{12,12}) + \sum g^{kl}(\Gamma_{11,k}\Gamma_{22,l} - \Gamma_{12,k}\Gamma_{12,l}).$$

**Codazzi-Mainardi equations:**
$$h_{11,2} - h_{12,1} = \sum h_{1k}\Gamma_{21}^k - \sum h_{2k}\Gamma_{11}^k$$
$$h_{12,2} - h_{22,1} = \sum h_{1k}\Gamma_{22}^k - \sum h_{2k}\Gamma_{12}^k.$$
