This converse theorem characterizes exactly which nonnegative periodic functions can arise as curvature functions of closed convex plane curves. Together with the strengthened four-vertex theorem, it establishes that the necessary conditions on the oscillatory behavior of $\kappa$ are also sufficient for realizing the function as the curvature of some closed convex curve.
