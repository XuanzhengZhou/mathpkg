\begin{proof}
We have $d\phi \tilde{e}_i = \sum_k (\partial u^k / \partial v^i) e_k$, where $(\tilde{e}_i)$ (respectively $(e_k)$) is the canonical basis of $T_v \mathbb{R}^2$ (respectively $T_{\phi(v)} \mathbb{R}^2$). The corollary follows by applying the formula

$$\tilde{I}_v(\tilde{e}_i, \tilde{e}_j) = I_{\phi(v)}(d\phi \tilde{e}_i, d\phi \tilde{e}_j)$$

from Proposition (3.2.5) ii).
\end{proof}
