The tangent bundle $\mathbb{T}_M$ of a surface $M$ is the disjoint union of all tangent spaces at all points of $M$, equipped with a natural projection $\pi$ sending each tangent vector to its base point. It carries a natural differentiable structure that makes it a smooth vector bundle of rank 2 over $M$. This global object unifies all tangent vectors into a single geometric space, and its differentiable structure is independent of coordinate choices.
