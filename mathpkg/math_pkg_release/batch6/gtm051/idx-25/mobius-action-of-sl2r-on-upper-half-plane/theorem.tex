Identify $(u,v) \in H_r^2 = \{ (u,v) \in \mathbb{R}^2 \mid v > 0 \}$ with the complex variable $z = u + iv$, $v > 0$. For $g = \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \in \mathrm{SL}(2, \mathbb{R})$ and $z \in H_r^2$, define the action by the M\"obius (fractional linear) transformation
$$
g z = \frac{a z + b}{c z + d}.
$$
The map $(g, z) \mapsto g z$ defines an action of $\mathrm{SL}(2, \mathbb{R})$ on $H_r^2$.
