The **tractrix** is the plane curve $(h(u), k(u))$ in the $(x, z)$-plane defined for $u \geq 0$ by
$$h(u) = e^{-u}, \quad k(u) = \int_0^u \sqrt{1 - e^{-2t}} \, dt.$$

It is characterized by the property that the distance, measured along the tangent line from the point $(h(u), k(u))$ to the $z$-axis, is always equal to $1$.
