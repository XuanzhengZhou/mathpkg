Let $M$ be a Riemannian manifold with metric $g$. For each $p \in M$, the map
$$Lg_p: X \in T_pM \mapsto g_p(X, \cdot) \in T_p^*M$$
is a linear isomorphism. Thus a vector field $X$ defines a 1-form and conversely: if $X = \sum_i \xi^i(u) e_i(u)$ in local coordinates, the corresponding 1-form is
$$\omega = \sum_{i,j} g_{ij}(u) \xi^i(u) \, du^j.$$
