A 1-form on a manifold is a smoothly varying linear functional on tangent vectors. At each point $p$, it belongs to the cotangent space $T_p^*M$. In local coordinates $(u^1, u^2)$, a 1-form is represented as $\omega = a_1 du^1 + a_2 du^2$, where the coordinate 1-forms $du^i$ are the differentials of the coordinate functions. Under a change of coordinates, the components transform via the Jacobian: $a_l = \sum_j b_j \frac{\partial u_\beta^j}{\partial u_\alpha^l}$.
