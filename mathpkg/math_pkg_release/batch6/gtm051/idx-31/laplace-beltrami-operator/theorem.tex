Let $F(M)$ denote the algebra of differentiable functions on a Riemannian manifold $M$. The **Laplace-Beltrami operator** is the mapping $\Delta: F(M) \to F(M)$ defined by
$$\Delta\psi := \operatorname{div} \operatorname{grad} \psi.$$
In local coordinates on a surface:
$$\Delta\psi = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\det g}} \sum_{i,j} \frac{\partial}{\partial u^i}\left(\sqrt{\det g} \, g^{ij} \frac{\partial \psi}{\partial u^j}\right).$$
