Let $M$ be an oriented surface with a Riemannian metric and let $X$ be a vector field on $M$. Then for every polygon $P: F \to M$,
$$\iint_P (\operatorname{div} X) \, dM = \int_{\partial P} i_X dM.$$
In local coordinates where $dM = \sqrt{g} \, du^1 \wedge du^2$ and $X = \sum \xi^i e_i$:
$$\operatorname{div} X = \frac{1}{\sqrt{g}} \sum_i \frac{\partial}{\partial u^i}(\sqrt{g} \, \xi^i), \quad i_X dM = -\xi^2 \sqrt{g} \, du^1 + \xi^1 \sqrt{g} \, du^2.$$
