Use Gauss's theorem (5.6.9) to show Green's first identity:
$$\iint_P \psi_1 \Delta\psi_2 + g(\operatorname{grad} \psi_1, \operatorname{grad} \psi_2) \, dM = \int_{\partial P} \psi_1 \frac{\partial\psi_2}{\partial n} \, ds,$$
where $\partial\psi_2/\partial n = d\psi_2(n)$ and $n$ is the outward pointing normal to $\partial P$.
