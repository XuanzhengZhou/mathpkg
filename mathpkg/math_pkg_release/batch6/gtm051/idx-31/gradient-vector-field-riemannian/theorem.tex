Let $M$ be a Riemannian manifold and $\psi: M \to \mathbb{R}$ a differentiable function. The **gradient** of $\psi$ is the vector field
$$\operatorname{grad} \psi(p) := Lg_p^{-1}(d\psi_p),$$
where $Lg_p: T_pM \to T_p^*M$ is the metric isomorphism $X \mapsto g_p(X, \cdot)$. Equivalently, $\operatorname{grad} \psi$ is the unique vector field satisfying
$$g(\operatorname{grad} \psi, X) = d\psi(X)$$
for all vector fields $X$ on $M$.
