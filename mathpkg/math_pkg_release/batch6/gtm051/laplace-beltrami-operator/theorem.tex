Let $F(M)$ denote the algebra of differentiable functions on a Riemannian manifold $M$. The Laplace-Beltrami operator is the mapping $\Delta: F(M) \to F(M)$ defined in local coordinates by
$$\Delta f = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\det g}} \sum_{i,j} \frac{\partial}{\partial u^i} \left( \sqrt{\det g}\, g^{ij} \frac{\partial f}{\partial u^j} \right).$$
