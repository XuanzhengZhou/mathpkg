For a regular curve $c(t) = f \circ u(t)$ on a surface $f$, the following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\kappa_g(t) = 0$.

\item[(ii)] If $s \mapsto t(s)$ is a change of variable on $c$ such that $\tilde{c}(s) = c \circ t(s)$ is a unit-speed curve, then $\tilde{c}(s)$ is a geodesic.
\end{enumerate}
