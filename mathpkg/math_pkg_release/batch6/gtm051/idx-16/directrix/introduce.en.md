On a ruled surface $f(s, t) = s X(t) + c(t)$, the curves obtained by fixing $s$ and varying $t$ are called directrices. These curves trace the base path along which the generators sweep out the surface. The directrix plays a role analogous to the spine of the surface, while the generators are the straight lines emanating from it.
