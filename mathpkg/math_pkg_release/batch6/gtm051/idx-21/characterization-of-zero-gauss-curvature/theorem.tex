Let $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^3$ be a surface. The following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $K(u) \equiv 0$.
\item[(ii)] There exist local coordinates in which $g_{ik} = \delta_{ik}$.
\item[(iii)] Parallel translation is independent of path.
\item[(iv)] The surface is locally isometric to the Euclidean plane.
\end{enumerate}
