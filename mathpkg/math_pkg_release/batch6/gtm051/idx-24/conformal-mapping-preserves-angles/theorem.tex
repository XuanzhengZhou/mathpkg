Let $(U, g)$ and $(\tilde{U}, \tilde{g})$ be two surfaces with Riemannian metrics. If a mapping $\phi: U \rightarrow \tilde{U}$ is conformal, then $\phi$ preserves angles. That is, for any two tangent vectors $X, Y \in T_u U$, the angle between $X$ and $Y$ measured with respect to $g$ equals the angle between $d\phi(X)$ and $d\phi(Y)$ measured with respect to $\tilde{g}$.
