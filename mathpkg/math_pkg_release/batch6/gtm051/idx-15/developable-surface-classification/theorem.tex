Let $f(s, t) = s X(t) + c(t)$ be a developable surface written locally in $(s, t)$-parameters with $f_s \cdot f_t = 0$ and $n_s \cdot f_t = n \cdot f_{st} = 0$. Then $X(t) \cdot \dot{c}(t) = 0$ and $\dot{X}(t) = r(t) \dot{c}(t)$ for some differentiable function $r(t)$. Locally, the surface is one of the following types:

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Cylinder:} $r(t) = 0$ in a neighborhood, so $X(t)$ is constant.
\item \textbf{Cone:} $r(t) = \text{constant} \neq 0$ in a neighborhood.
\item \textbf{Tangential developable:} $r(t) \neq 0$ and $\dot{r}(t) \neq 0$ in a neighborhood.
\end{enumerate}
