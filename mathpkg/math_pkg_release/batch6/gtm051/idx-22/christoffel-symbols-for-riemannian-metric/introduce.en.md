The Christoffel symbols $\Gamma_{ij}^k$ are the connection coefficients of the Levi-Civita connection associated to a Riemannian metric. They are expressed in terms of the first partial derivatives of the metric components $g_{ij}$ and the inverse metric $g^{kl}$. In two dimensions, these symbols encode the local geometry of the surface, determining how basis vector fields change under parallel transport and governing the geodesic equation.
