Let $U$ be an open subset of $\mathbb{R}^2$. A Riemannian metric on $U$ is a differentiable map

$$g: U \rightarrow S(2),$$

where $S(2)$ denotes the set of all real symmetric, positive definite $2 \times 2$ matrices $(g_{ik})$, considered as an open subset of the three-dimensional space of all $2 \times 2$ symmetric matrices.
