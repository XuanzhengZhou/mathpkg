Let $(U, g)$ and $(V, \tilde{g})$ be two open subsets of $\mathbb{R}^2$ with Riemannian metrics. They are equivalent (isometric) if there exists a diffeomorphism $\phi: V \rightarrow U$ such that

$$g_{\phi(v)}(d\phi X, d\phi Y) = \tilde{g}_v(X, Y) \quad \text{for all } X, Y \in T_v \mathbb{R}^2 \text{ and all } v \in V.$$
