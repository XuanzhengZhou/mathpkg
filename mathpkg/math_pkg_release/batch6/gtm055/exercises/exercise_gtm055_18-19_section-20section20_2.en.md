B. Show that if $[a, b]$ is a closed interval of real numbers, then $\mathcal{C}([a, b])^*$ may not only be identified with the subspace $\mathcal{R}$ of $\mathcal{V}([a, b])$ consisting of those functions that are right-continuous on $(a, b)$ and vanish at $a$, as in Example $A$, but may also be identified with a subspace of $\mathcal{V}([a, b])$ consisting of left-continuous functions.
