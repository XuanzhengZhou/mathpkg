(iii) Suppose the topology on $\mathcal{E}$ is induced by some indexed family $\{v_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in A}$ of deminorms and that $\mathcal{F}$ is likewise topologized by a second indexed family $\{v_{\beta}\}_{\beta \in B}$ of deminorms. Show that $T$ is continuous if and only if, given a positive number $\varepsilon$ and an index $\beta$, there exist a positive number $\delta$ and indices $\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_n$ such that $v_{\alpha i}(x) < \delta, i = 1, \ldots, n$, implies $v_{\beta}(Tx) < \varepsilon$.

(iv) Suppose $\mathcal{E}$ and $\mathcal{F}$ are topologized by saturated indexed families of pseudo-norms $\{\sigma_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in A}$ and $\{\sigma_{\beta}\}_{\beta \in B}$, respectively. Show that $T$ is continuous if and only if for every index $\beta$ there exist a positive number $M$ and an index $\alpha$ such that
