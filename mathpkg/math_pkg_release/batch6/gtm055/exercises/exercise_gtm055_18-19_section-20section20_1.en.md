A. Let $\xi$ and $\zeta$ be regular complex Borel measures on a locally compact Hausdorff space $X$. Verify that

$$||\xi|(E) - |\zeta|(E)| \leq \|\xi - \zeta\|$$

for every Borel set $E$ in $X$.
