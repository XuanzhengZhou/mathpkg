(For one-to-one mappings this is equivalent to the assertion that the inverse mapping is also continuous.) The reader is already familiar with one theorem of this type, viz., the theorem asserting that a continuous and one-to-one mapping of a compact topological space onto a Hausdorff space is a homeomorphism (Prop. 3.3).
