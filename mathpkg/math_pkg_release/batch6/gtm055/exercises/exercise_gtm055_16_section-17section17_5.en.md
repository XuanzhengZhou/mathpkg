E. Let $\mathcal{E}$ and $\mathcal{F}$ be Banach spaces, and let $T$ be a bounded linear transformation of $\mathcal{E}$ into $\mathcal{F}$. Show that if $\Phi$ is an analytic $\mathcal{E}$-valued mapping on a domain $\Delta$ in $\mathbb{C}$, then $T \circ \Phi$ is an analytic $\mathcal{F}$-valued mapping on $\Delta$, and verify that $(T \circ \Phi)' = T \circ \Phi'$.
