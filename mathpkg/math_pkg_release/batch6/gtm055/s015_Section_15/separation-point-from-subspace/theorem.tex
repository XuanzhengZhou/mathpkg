Let $\mathcal{M}$ be a closed linear submanifold of a normed space $\mathcal{E}$, and let $x_0 \in \mathcal{E} \setminus \mathcal{M}$. If $d = d(x_0, \mathcal{M})$ denotes the distance from $x_0$ to $\mathcal{M}$, then there exists $f \in \mathcal{E}^*$ such that $f(\mathcal{M}) = (0)$, $f(x_0) = 1$, and $\|f\| = 1/d$.
