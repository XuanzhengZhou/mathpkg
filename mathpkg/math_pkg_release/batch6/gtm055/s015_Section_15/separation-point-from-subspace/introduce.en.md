Lemma 14.10 is a geometric consequence of the Hahn-Banach theorem. Given a closed subspace $\mathcal{M}$ and a point $x_0$ outside it, there exists a bounded linear functional vanishing on $\mathcal{M}$, taking the value $1$ at $x_0$, and whose norm is precisely the reciprocal of the distance from $x_0$ to $\mathcal{M}$. This is fundamental for the duality theory of normed spaces.
