The proof follows the same pattern as Lemma 14.10. Let $M_0$ be the linear submanifold generated by $\mathcal{M}$ and $x_0$, and define $g$ on $M_0$ by $g(y + \lambda x_0) = \lambda$. The distance from the origin to the coset $\mathcal{M} + x_0$ with respect to the pseudometric induced by $\sigma$ is $d = d_\sigma(x_0, \mathcal{M})$. As before, one shows that $\|g\|_\sigma = 1/d$. The result then follows from Proposition 14.4 (the Hahn-Banach theorem for pseudonorms), which extends $g$ to a linear functional $f$ on all of $\mathcal{E}$ with the same $\sigma$-norm.
