In any topological linear space $\mathcal{E}$ the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] The topology is induced by a countably determined separating family of pseudonorms,
\item[(ii)] The topology is induced by a countable separating family of pseudonorms,
\item[(iii)] $\mathcal{E}$ is locally convex and quasinormable,
\item[(iv)] $\mathcal{E}$ is locally convex and metrizable.
\end{enumerate}
