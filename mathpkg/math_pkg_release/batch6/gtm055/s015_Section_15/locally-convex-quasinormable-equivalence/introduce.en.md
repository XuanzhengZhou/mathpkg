Proposition 14.8 characterizes those locally convex spaces whose topology can be described by a countable family of pseudonorms. Being locally convex and metrizable is equivalent to being locally convex and quasinormable, and both are equivalent to having a countable separating family of pseudonorms. Such spaces, when also complete, are precisely the Frechet spaces.
