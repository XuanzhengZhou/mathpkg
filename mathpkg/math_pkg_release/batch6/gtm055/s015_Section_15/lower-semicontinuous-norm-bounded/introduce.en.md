Proposition 13.7 is an elegant application of the Baire category theorem to linear transformations. It states that if the norm of the image of a linear transformation is lower semicontinuous on a Banach space, then the transformation is automatically bounded. The proof uses Baire category by expressing the domain as a countable union of closed sets $M_n = \{x : \|Tx\| \leq n\}$.
