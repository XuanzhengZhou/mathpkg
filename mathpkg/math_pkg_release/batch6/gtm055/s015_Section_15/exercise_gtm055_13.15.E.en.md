Let $\mathcal{E}$ and $\mathcal{F}$ be $F$-spaces, and let $S$ and $T$ be continuous linear transformations of $\mathcal{E}$ into $\mathcal{F}$ and of $\mathcal{F}$ into $\mathcal{E}$, respectively, such that $TS = 1_{\mathcal{E}}$ (so that $T$ is a left inverse of $S$ and $S$ is a right inverse of $T$). Show that the range of $S$ and the null space of $T$ are necessarily closed complementary submanifolds of $\mathcal{F}$. (Hint: $ST$ is idempotent.)
