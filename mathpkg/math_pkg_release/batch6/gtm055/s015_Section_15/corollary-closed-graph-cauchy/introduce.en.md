Corollary 13.5 provides an equivalent reformulation of the closed graph theorem that is often easier to verify in practice. Instead of checking that the graph is closed, one only needs to verify that whenever a sequence converges to zero in the domain and its image sequence is Cauchy, the image sequence must converge to zero. This criterion is particularly useful when working with unbounded operators or differential operators.
