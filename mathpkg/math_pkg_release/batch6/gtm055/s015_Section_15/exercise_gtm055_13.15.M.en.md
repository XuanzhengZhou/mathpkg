Suppose that a linear space $\mathcal{E}$ is complete with respect to each of two quasinorms $|\cdot|_1$ and $|\cdot|_2$, and suppose also that there exists some separated linear topology on $\mathcal{E}$ that is refined by both of the topologies induced by $|\cdot|_1$ and $|\cdot|_2$. Show that $|\cdot|_1$ and $|\cdot|_2$ must be equivalent. (Hint: Use Example C.)
