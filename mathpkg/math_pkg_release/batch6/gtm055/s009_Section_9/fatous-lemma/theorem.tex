Let $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ be a measure space, and let $\{f_n\}$ be a sequence of functions defined and nonnegative a.e. $[\mu]$ on $X$. Suppose that there exists a real number $M$ such that $\int_X f_n d\mu \leq M$ for every $n$. Then $\liminf_n f_n$ is integrable $[\mu]$ and
$$\int_X (\liminf_n f_n) d\mu \leq \liminf_n \int_X f_n d\mu.$$
