This follows directly from the Dominated Convergence Theorem (Theorem 7.12). Since $\mu(X) < +\infty$, the constant function $g(x) = M$ is integrable $[\mu]$, because $\int_X M d\mu = M \mu(X) < +\infty$. The hypothesis $|f_n| \leq M$ a.e. provides the dominating function. The conclusion then follows immediately from Theorem 7.12.
