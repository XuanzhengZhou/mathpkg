The Lebesgue differentiation theorem states that for an integrable function on $\mathbb{R}^n$, the averages over shrinking balls converge to the function value at almost every point. It generalizes the fundamental theorem of calculus and is proved using the Hardy--Littlewood maximal function and covering lemmas.
