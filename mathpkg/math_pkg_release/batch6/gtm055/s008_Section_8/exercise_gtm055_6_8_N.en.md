If $X$ is a metric space there is a transfinite construction of the $\sigma$-ring of Borel sets in $X$ that goes as follows. Set $\mathcal{B}^{(0)}$ equal to the collection of all open sets in $X$. For a countable ordinal $\alpha$, define $\mathcal{B}^{(\alpha)}$ by transfinite recursion analogous to that described in Problem G. Show that the Borel $\sigma$-ring $\mathbf{B}$ equals $\bigcup_{\xi < \Omega} \mathcal{B}^{(\xi)}$.
