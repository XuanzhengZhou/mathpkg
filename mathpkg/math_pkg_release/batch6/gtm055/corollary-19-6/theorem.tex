Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a Banach space and let $\{x_{\gamma}\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ be an indexed family of vectors in $\mathcal{E}$. A sufficient condition for the family $\{x_{\gamma}\}$ to be summable is for it to be absolutely summable, i.e., for the family of norms $\{\|x_{\gamma}\|\}$ to be a summable family of real numbers.
