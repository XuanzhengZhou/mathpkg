Proposition 15.10 summarizes the weak* topology as an application of Proposition 15.8: $\mathcal{E}^*$ with the weak* topology is a separated locally convex space. Convergence in this topology is precisely pointwise convergence on $\mathcal{E}$. This topology is fundamental for the Banach-Alaoglu theorem and the study of dual spaces.
