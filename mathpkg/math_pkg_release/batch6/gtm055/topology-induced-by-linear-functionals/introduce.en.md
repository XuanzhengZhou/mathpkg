Proposition 15.8 generalizes the weak topology construction: for any linear space $\mathcal{E}$ and any linear manifold $\mathcal{M}$ of linear functionals, the coarsest topology making all $f \in \mathcal{M}$ continuous is locally convex. The topology is induced by the family of pseudonorms $\sigma_f(x) = |f(x)|$ for $f \in \mathcal{M}$. This framework unifies the weak and weak* topologies.
