Alaoglu's theorem is a cornerstone of functional analysis: the closed unit ball of the dual space $\mathcal{E}^*$ is compact in the weak* topology. The proof embeds $(\mathcal{E}^*)_1$ into a product of compact discs via evaluation maps and uses Tychonoff's theorem. This result is essential for the study of reflexivity, the Krein-Milman theorem, and many applications in operator theory.
