Lemma 12.8 is a fundamental result in operator theory: on a Banach space, any operator sufficiently close to the identity (within distance 1) is invertible. The inverse is given by the Neumann series $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (1 - T)^n$. This lemma implies that the set of invertible operators in $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{E})$ is open, a fact that has far-reaching consequences for the study of spectra and perturbation theory.
