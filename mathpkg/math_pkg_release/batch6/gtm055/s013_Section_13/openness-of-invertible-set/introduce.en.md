Proposition 12.9 generalizes Lemma 12.8: not only is the set of invertible operators open, but the sets of left-invertible and right-invertible operators are also open in $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{E},\mathcal{F})$ when $\mathcal{E}$ and $\mathcal{F}$ are Banach spaces. Moreover, the inversion map $T \mapsto T^{-1}$ is continuous on the open set of invertible operators. These facts are fundamental to perturbation theory and spectral analysis.
