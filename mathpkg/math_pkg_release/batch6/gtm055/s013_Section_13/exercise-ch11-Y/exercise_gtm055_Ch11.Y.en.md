Show that the following properties are equivalent for an arbitrary separated topological linear space $\mathcal{E}$:

(i) $\mathcal{E}$ is quasinormable,
(ii) $\mathcal{E}$ is metrizable as a topological space,
(iii) $\mathcal{E}$ satisfies the first axiom of countability.
