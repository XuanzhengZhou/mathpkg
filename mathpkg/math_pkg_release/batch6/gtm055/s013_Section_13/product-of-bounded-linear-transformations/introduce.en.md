Theorem 12.3 states that the composition (product) of two bounded linear transformations is again bounded, and its operator norm satisfies the submultiplicativity inequality $\|ST\| \leq \|S\|\|T\|$. This property is fundamental for the theory of operator algebras, as it shows that the space of bounded operators on a normed space forms a normed algebra in which multiplication is jointly continuous.
