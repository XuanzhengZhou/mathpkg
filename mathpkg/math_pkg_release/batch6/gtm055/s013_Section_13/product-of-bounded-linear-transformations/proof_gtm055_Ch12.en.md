We have $\| STx \| \leq \| S \| \| Tx \| \leq \| S \| \| T \| \| x \|$ for every vector $x$ in $\mathcal{E}$. This inequality shows that $ST$ is bounded and that $\| ST \| \leq \| S \| \| T \|$.
