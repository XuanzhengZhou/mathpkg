If $(X, S, \mu)$ is a $\sigma$-finite measure space, then $\mathcal{L}_1(X, S, \mu)^*$ is isometrically isomorphic to $\mathcal{L}_\infty(X, S, \mu)$ under the correspondence $\varphi_g \rightarrow g$, where

$$\varphi_g(f) = \int_X f g d\mu, \quad f \in \mathcal{L}_1(X), \quad g \in \mathcal{L}_\infty(X).$$
