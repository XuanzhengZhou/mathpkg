Lemma 15.1 establishes that each continuous linear functional $f \in \mathcal{E}^*$ defines a pseudonorm $\sigma_f(x) = |f(x)|$. The continuity of $f$ with respect to any linear topology $\mathcal{T}$ is equivalent to the continuity of $\sigma_f$. Consequently, the weak topology on $\mathcal{E}$ coincides with the topology induced by the family $\{\sigma_f\}_{f \in \mathcal{E}^*}$.
