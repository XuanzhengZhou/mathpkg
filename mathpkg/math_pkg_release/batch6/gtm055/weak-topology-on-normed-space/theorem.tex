If $\mathcal{E}$ is a normed space, then the coarsest topology on $\mathcal{E}$ making all of the linear functionals in $\mathcal{E}^*$ continuous is the weak topology on $\mathcal{E}$. Equivalently, the weak topology on $\mathcal{E}$ is the topology inversely induced on $\mathcal{E}$ by the family of linear functionals in $\mathcal{E}^*$.
