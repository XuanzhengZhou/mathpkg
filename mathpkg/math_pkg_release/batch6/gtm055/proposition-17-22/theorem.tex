Let $U$ be an open subset of $\mathbb{C}$, let $K$ be a compact subset of $U$, and suppose given two oriented envelopes $\gamma_1$ and $\gamma_2$ of $K$ in $U$. Then

$$\int_{\gamma_1} \Phi(\zeta) d\zeta = \int_{\gamma_2} \Phi(\zeta) d\zeta$$

for an arbitrary locally analytic Banach space valued mapping $\Phi$ defined on $U \setminus K$.
