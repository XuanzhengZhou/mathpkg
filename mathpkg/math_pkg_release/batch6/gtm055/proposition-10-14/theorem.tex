Let $X$ be a locally compact Hausdorff space. If $\mu$ is a signed Borel measure on $X$, then $\mu_+$, $\mu_-$ and $|\mu|$ are Borel measures on $X$. If $\xi$ is a complex Borel measure on $X$, then $\text{Re} \xi$ and $\text{Im} \xi$ are signed Borel measures on $X$, and $|\xi|$ is a finite Borel measure.
