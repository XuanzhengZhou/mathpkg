Let $(X, S, \mu)$ be a measure space, and suppose that $v$ is a measure on $(X, S)$ with the property that $\mu(E) = 0$ implies $v(E) = 0$ for every set $E$ in $S$. Then we say that $v$ is absolutely continuous with respect to $\mu$, or absolutely continuous $[\mu]$, and we write $v \ll \mu$.
