Proof. According to Proposition 11.18, if $x_0$ is any vector in $\mathcal{E}$, then $\mathcal{V}$ is a neighborhood base at the origin 0 if and only if $x_0 + \mathcal{V} = \{x_0 + V : V \in \mathcal{V}\}$ is a neighborhood base at $x_0$.
