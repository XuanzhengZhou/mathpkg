The quotient space $\mathcal{E} / \mathcal{M}$ of a vector space by a linear submanifold is the vector space of cosets. It provides a way to "mod out" a subspace, collapsing all vectors differing by an element of $\mathcal{M}$. The natural projection is a linear surjection, and every linear transformation vanishing on $\mathcal{M}$ factors uniquely through this projection. Quotient spaces are essential in the isomorphism theorems and in constructing dual spaces.
