The Hamel dimension (algebraic dimension) of a vector space is the cardinality of any Hamel basis. All Hamel bases of a given space have the same cardinality. Finite dimensional spaces are those with finite Hamel dimension; infinite dimensional spaces are central objects of study in functional analysis. Unlike the analytic dimension (which counts a Schauder basis), the Hamel dimension considers only finite linear combinations.
