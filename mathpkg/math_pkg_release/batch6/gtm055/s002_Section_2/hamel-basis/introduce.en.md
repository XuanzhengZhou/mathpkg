A Hamel basis (algebraic basis) is a maximal linearly independent subset of a vector space such that every vector is uniquely expressible as a finite linear combination of basis elements. Unlike Schauder bases, Hamel bases allow only finite linear combinations and exist for every vector space (via the Axiom of Choice). The cardinality of a Hamel basis is the Hamel dimension of the space.
