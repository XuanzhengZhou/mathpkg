Let $\gamma$ be a closed rectifiable arc in $\mathbb{C}$ and let $W$ denote the range of $\gamma$. Then the function
$$w_{\gamma}(\lambda) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{d\zeta}{\zeta - \lambda}$$
is constant and integer-valued on each component of the complement $\mathbb{C} \setminus W$. In particular, $w_{\gamma}$ vanishes identically on the unbounded component of $\mathbb{C} \setminus W$. The integer $w_{\gamma}(\lambda)$ is called the index or winding number of $\gamma$ at (or about) $\lambda$.
