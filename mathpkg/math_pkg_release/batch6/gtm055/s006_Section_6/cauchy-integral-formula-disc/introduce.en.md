The Cauchy integral formula expresses the value of an analytic function at a point via a contour integral weighted by the winding number. It shows that the values of an analytic function on a disc are completely determined by its values on any closed curve inside the disc. Together with Taylor's theorem and Cauchy estimates, it forms the foundation of complex analysis.
