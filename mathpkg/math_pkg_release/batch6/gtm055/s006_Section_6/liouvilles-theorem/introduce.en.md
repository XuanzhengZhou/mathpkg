Liouville's Theorem states that every bounded entire function must be constant. The proof uses Cauchy estimates: $|\alpha_n| \leq M_r / r^n$ for every $r > 0$. If $f$ is bounded by $M$, then $M_r \leq M$ for all $r$, and letting $r \to \infty$ forces $\alpha_n = 0$ for all $n \geq 1$. This yields a simple proof of the Fundamental Theorem of Algebra.
