The Laurent expansion generalizes Taylor series to annular domains, allowing both positive and negative powers. Every analytic function on an annulus admits a unique Laurent series. The negatively indexed coefficients constitute the principal part and are central to the classification of isolated singularities. When the inner radius is zero, the Laurent expansion describes the behavior near an isolated singularity.
