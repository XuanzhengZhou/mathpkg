Corollary 16.11 shows that isometric isomorphisms are preserved under the adjoint operation: if $T: \mathcal{E} \to \mathcal{F}$ is an isometric isomorphism, then $T^*: \mathcal{F}^* \to \mathcal{E}^*$ is also an isometric isomorphism. The proof uses the fact that an isometric isomorphism satisfies $\|T\| = \|T^{-1}\| = 1$, together with Proposition 16.9 ($\|T^*\| = \|T\|$) and Corollary 16.10.
