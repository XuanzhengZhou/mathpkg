Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a Banach space with the property that the dual $\mathcal{E}^*$ is separable. Show that a subset of $\mathcal{E}$ is weakly compact if and only if it is weakly sequentially compact.

\textit{Hint:} If $K$ is a subset of $\mathcal{E}$ that is either weakly compact or weakly sequentially compact, then $K$ is (weakly) bounded, and the weak topology on $K$ is therefore metrizable.
