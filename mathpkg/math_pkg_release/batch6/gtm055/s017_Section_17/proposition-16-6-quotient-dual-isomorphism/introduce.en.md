Proposition 16.6 establishes that the dual of a quotient space $\mathcal{E}/\mathcal{M}$ is canonically isomorphic to the annihilator $\mathcal{M}^a$ of the subspace $\mathcal{M}$. The isomorphism $\alpha$ is given by precomposition with the quotient map: $\hat{f} \mapsto \hat{f} \circ \pi$. In the Banach space setting, this isomorphism is isometric, reflecting the fact that the quotient norm and the dual norm are perfectly compatible.
