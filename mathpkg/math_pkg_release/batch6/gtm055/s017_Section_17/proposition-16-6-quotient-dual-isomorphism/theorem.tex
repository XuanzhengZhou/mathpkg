Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a separated locally convex space and let $\mathcal{M}$ be a closed linear submanifold of $\mathcal{E}$. Let $\pi$ denote the natural projection of $\mathcal{E}$ onto $\mathcal{E}/\mathcal{M}$. Define $\alpha: (\mathcal{E}/\mathcal{M})^* \to \mathcal{M}^a$ by
$$\alpha(\hat{f}) = \hat{f} \circ \pi, \quad \hat{f} \in (\mathcal{E}/\mathcal{M})^*.$$

Then $\alpha$ maps $(\mathcal{E}/\mathcal{M})^*$ onto $\mathcal{M}^a$ and is a linear isomorphism. If $\mathcal{E}$ is a Banach space, then $\alpha$ is an isometric isomorphism.
