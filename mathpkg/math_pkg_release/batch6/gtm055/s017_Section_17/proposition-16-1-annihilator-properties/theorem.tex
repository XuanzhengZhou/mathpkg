Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a locally convex topological linear space.

(i) If $M$ and $N$ are subsets of $\mathcal{E}$ such that $M \subset N$, then $M^a \supset N^a$.

(ii) If $\{ M_\gamma \}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ is a nonempty indexed family of subsets of $\mathcal{E}$, then
$$\left( \bigcup_\gamma M_\gamma \right)^a = \bigcap_\gamma M_\gamma^a.$$

(iii) Dually, if $M$ and $N$ are subsets of $\mathcal{E}^*$ such that $M \subset N$, then $^a M \supset \,^a N$, and the analogous union-intersection formula holds for preannihilators.
