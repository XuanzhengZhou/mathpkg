Let $\langle \mathcal{E}, \mathcal{F} \rangle$ be a dual pair.

(i) If $M$ and $N$ are subsets of $\mathcal{E}$ such that $M \subset N$, then $M^0 \supset N^0$.

(ii) If $\{M_\gamma\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ is a nonempty indexed family of subsets of $\mathcal{E}$, then
$$\left( \bigcup_{\gamma} M_\gamma \right)^0 = \bigcap_{\gamma} M_\gamma^0.$$

Analogous statements hold for prepolars.
