Proposition 16.7 shows that the dual of a subspace $\mathcal{M}^*$ is canonically isomorphic to the quotient $\mathcal{E}^* / \mathcal{M}^a$ of the dual of the ambient space by the annihilator of the subspace. The isomorphism is given by restriction: a coset $f + \mathcal{M}^a$ maps to the restriction $f|_{\mathcal{M}}$, which is well-defined since any two representatives differ by a functional vanishing on $\mathcal{M}$. In Banach spaces, the Hahn–Banach extension theorem guarantees this isomorphism is isometric.
