Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a separated locally convex space and let $\mathcal{M}$ be a closed linear submanifold of $\mathcal{E}$. Define $\hat{\beta}: \mathcal{E}^* / \mathcal{M}^a \to \mathcal{M}^*$ by
$$\hat{\beta}(f + \mathcal{M}^a) = f|_{\mathcal{M}}, \quad f \in \mathcal{E}^*.$$

Then $\hat{\beta}$ is a linear isomorphism of $\mathcal{E}^* / \mathcal{M}^a$ onto $\mathcal{M}^*$. If $\mathcal{E}$ is a Banach space, then $\hat{\beta}$ is an isometric isomorphism.
