Let $\langle \mathcal{E}, \mathcal{F} \rangle$ be a dual pair and let $A$ be an absolutely convex subset of $\mathcal{E}$. Then the bipolar $^0(A^0)$ coincides with the closure of $A$ in the weak topology on $\mathcal{E}$ induced by $\mathcal{F}$. In particular, if $A$ is weakly closed, then $^0(A^0) = A$.
