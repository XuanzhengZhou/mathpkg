Proof. If $|\lambda - \lambda_0| < 1/d$, then

$$\| (\lambda_0 - \lambda) R_x(\lambda_0) \| = r < 1$$

and

$$\| (\lambda_0 - \lambda)^n R_x(\lambda_0)^n \| \leq r^n$$

for every $n$. Hence the series (9) is absolutely convergent and therefore convergent. Moreover, multiplying this series (on either side) term by term by $\lambda - x = (\lambda_0 - x) - (\lambda_0 - \lambda)$ gives rise to the difference

$$\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} ((\lambda_0 - \lambda) R_x(\lambda_0))^n - \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} ((\lambda_0 - \lambda) R_x(\lambda_0))^{n+1} = 1_A.$$
