Let $\varphi_0$ be a positive linear functional on $\mathcal{C}(X)$, where $X$ is a compact Hausdorff space, and suppose in addition that $\varphi_0(1) = 1$. For each open set $U$ in $X$ let

$$\rho(U) = \sup\{\varphi_0(f): 0 \leq f \leq \chi_U, f \in \mathcal{C}(X)\}.$$

Then $\rho$ is a countably subadditive gauge (Prob. 8D) on the collection $\mathcal{T}$ of all open sets in $X$. In particular, $\rho$ is monotone increasing on $\mathcal{T}$.
