Theorem 17.4, the Riesz-Fischer theorem, establishes that all Lebesgue spaces $\mathcal{L}_p(X)$ for $1 \leq p \leq +\infty$ are complete normed spaces, hence Banach spaces. The proof uses the fact that absolute convergence in norm implies almost everywhere convergence, together with an application of Lemma 17.3 and the monotone convergence theorem.
