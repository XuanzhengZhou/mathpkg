Proposition 17.2 establishes the $p = +\infty$ counterpart of Proposition 17.1. The collection of all essentially bounded measurable functions on a measure space forms a linear space, and the essential supremum defines a pseudonorm on this space. Together with the $\mathcal{L}_p$ spaces for $1 \leq p < +\infty$, this completes the family of Lebesgue spaces.
