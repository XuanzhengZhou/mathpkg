Proposition 17.1 generalizes the classical $\ell_p$ sequence spaces to the setting of arbitrary measure spaces using Lebesgue integration. For any measure space $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ and positive $p$, the collection $\mathcal{L}_p(X)$ of measurable functions with $|f|^p$ integrable forms a linear space under pointwise operations. When $p \geq 1$, the expression $\|f\|_p = (\int_X |f|^p d\mu)^{1/p}$ defines a pseudonorm, with the triangle inequality following from the Minkowski inequality.
