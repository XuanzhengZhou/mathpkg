Proposition 17.19 views the Riemann-Stieltjes integral as a bounded linear operator from the space of continuous $\mathcal{E}$-valued functions on $[a, b]$ into $\mathcal{E}$, with operator norm bounded by the total variation of the integrator $\alpha$. This perspective is essential for the functional calculus developed later.
