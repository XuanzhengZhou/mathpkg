The set $\{x \in X : \| \Phi(x) \| \leq r\}$ can be written as $\{x \in X : \Phi(x) \in \mathcal{E}_r\}$, $r > 0$, and the closed balls $\mathcal{E}_r$ are Borel sets in $\mathcal{E}$.
