Proposition 17.15 establishes that the Bochner integral commutes with bounded linear transformations. The proof verifies the identity first for integrable simple mappings and then extends by continuity, using the density of simple functions in $\mathcal{L}_1(X; \mathcal{E})$ and the dominated convergence theorem.
