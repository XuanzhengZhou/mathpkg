Let $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ be a measure space, and let $\mathcal{E}$ be a separable Banach space. Then there exists a unique bounded linear transformation $L$ of $\mathcal{L}_1(X; \mathcal{E})$ into $\mathcal{E}$ satisfying the condition

$$L(\chi_E v) = \mu(E) v$$

for every measurable set $E$ of finite measure and every vector $v$ in $\mathcal{E}$.
