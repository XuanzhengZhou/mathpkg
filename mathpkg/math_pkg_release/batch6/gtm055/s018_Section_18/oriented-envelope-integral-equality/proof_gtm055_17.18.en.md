The winding number of $\gamma_1 - \gamma_2$ on $\mathbb{C} \setminus U$ is zero since $w_{\gamma_1} = w_{\gamma_2} = 0$ there. The winding number of $\gamma_1 - \gamma_2$ on $K$ is likewise $w_{\gamma_1} - w_{\gamma_2} = 0$. Hence $\gamma_1 - \gamma_2 \sim 0$ in $U \setminus K$, so

$$\int_{\gamma_1 - \gamma_2} \Phi(\zeta) d\zeta = 0$$

by Theorem 17.21.
