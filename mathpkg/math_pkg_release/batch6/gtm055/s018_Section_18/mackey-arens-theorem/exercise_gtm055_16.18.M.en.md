Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a normed space and let $j_{\mathcal{E}}$ and $j_{\mathcal{E}^*}$ denote, respectively, the natural embeddings of $\mathcal{E}$ in $\mathcal{E}^{**}$ and $\mathcal{E}^*$ in $\mathcal{E}^{***}$. Verify that $(j_{\mathcal{E}})^*$ is a left inverse of $j_{\mathcal{E}^*}$, and use this fact to show that $(j_{\mathcal{E}}(\mathcal{E}))^a$ and $j_{\mathcal{E}^*}(\mathcal{E}^*)$ are complementary subspaces in $\mathcal{E}^{***}$.
