Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a reflexive Banach space and let $T$ be a bounded linear transformation of $\mathcal{E}$ into a normed space $\mathcal{F}$. Show that $T$ carries an arbitrary closed, bounded, convex subset of $\mathcal{E}$ onto a closed, bounded, convex subset of $\mathcal{F}$. (Hint: Recall that a closed convex set is also weakly closed. Use the Šmul'yan criterion.)
