Exercise C generalizes the finite partition decomposition of $L_p(X)$ to the case of a countable measurable partition. The finite case states that $L_p(X)$ is isometrically isomorphic to the $\ell_p$-direct sum of the $L_p$ spaces on each part. The countable analogue extends this to an $\ell_p$-direct sum of countably many spaces.
