Let $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ be a measure space, let $p$ be an extended real number such that $1 \leq p \leq +\infty$, and let $\{f_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}$ and $f$ be functions in $\mathcal{L}_p(X)$.

$L_p(X)$ is naturally isometrically isomorphic to the direct sum
$$L_p(E_1) \oplus_p \cdots \oplus_p L_p(E_m)$$
(see Problem 11T). State and prove the analogous result for a countable partition of $X$.
