Let $X$ be a compact Hausdorff space. Then for each bounded linear functional $\varphi$ on the real space $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X)$ of continuous real-valued functions on $X$ there is a unique signed measure $\mu$ in $\mathcal{M}_0(X)$ such that

$$\varphi(f) = \varphi_\mu(f) = \int_X f \, d\mu, \quad f \in \mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X).$$

Moreover, the correspondence $\mu \rightarrow \varphi_\mu$ is an isometric isomorphism of the real Banach space $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X)^*$ onto the real Banach space $\mathcal{M}_{\mathbb{R},0}(X)$ of all regular signed Borel measures on $X$.
