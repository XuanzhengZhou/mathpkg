Use the Hölder inequality to establish the triangle inequality for the pseudonorm $\|\cdot\|_p$ on $\mathcal{L}_p(X)$. In other words, verify that

$$\| f + g \|_p \leq \| f \|_p + \| g \|_p, \quad f, g \in \mathcal{L}_p(X).$$

(This inequality is also known as the Minkowski inequality.)
