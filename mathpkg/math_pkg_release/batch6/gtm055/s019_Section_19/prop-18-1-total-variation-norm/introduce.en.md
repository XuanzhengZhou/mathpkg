Proposition 18.1 establishes that the total variation $\|\xi\| = |\xi|(X)$ defines a norm on the space $\mathcal{M}(X)$ of complex Borel measures on a locally compact Hausdorff space $X$. The key nontrivial part is completeness: a Cauchy sequence in total variation norm converges setwise to a finitely additive set function, and semicontinuity of the limit follows from the Cauchy condition. Thus $\mathcal{M}(X)$ is a Banach space under this norm.
