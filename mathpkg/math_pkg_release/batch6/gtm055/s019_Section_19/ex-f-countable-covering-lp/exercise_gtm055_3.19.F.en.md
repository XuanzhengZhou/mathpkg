Show that if $\mathcal{C}$ is any countable covering of a measure space $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ by measurable sets, then $\bigcup_{E \in \mathcal{C}} L_E$ spans $L_p(X)$ as a Banach space for all $1 \leq p < +\infty$. Show also that this result is not true in general for $p = +\infty$.
