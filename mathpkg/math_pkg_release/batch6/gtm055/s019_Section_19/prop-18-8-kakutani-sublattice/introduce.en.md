Proposition 18.8 (Kakutani) is a key technical result used in proving the Stone-Weierstrass Theorem. It states that every closed subalgebra of $C_{\mathbb{R}}(X)$ is a sublattice -- meaning it is closed under the lattice operations of pointwise maximum and minimum. The proof reduces to showing that $|f|$ belongs to the subalgebra whenever $f$ does, using polynomial approximations to $\sqrt{t}$ on $[0,1]$ derived from the Taylor series of $\sqrt{1-\lambda}$.
