Let $u$ be a real harmonic function on a simply connected domain $\Delta'$ in $\mathbb{R}^2$. Then there exists an analytic function $f$ on the corresponding complex domain $\Delta$ having $u$ for its real part. Consequently, $u$ possesses a harmonic conjugate $v$ on $\Delta$ (unique up to an additive constant) such that $f = u + iv$ is analytic.
