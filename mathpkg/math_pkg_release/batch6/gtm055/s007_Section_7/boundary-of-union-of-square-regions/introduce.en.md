For any finite union $L$ of square regions from a grid $\mathcal{G}_m$, the topological boundary $\partial L$ can be expressed as a formal sum of closed polygonal arcs whose winding number is identically one on $L^\circ$ and zero outside $L$. The proof uses standard parametrizations of each $\partial Q_i$, sums them, and cancels oppositely directed shared edges to obtain exactly $\partial L$.
