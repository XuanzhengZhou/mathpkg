Let $\{Q_1, \ldots, Q_n\}$ be an arbitrary finite, nonempty collection of square regions of a grid $\mathcal{G}_m$, and set $L = Q_1 \cup \cdots \cup Q_n$. The boundary $\partial L$ can be parametrized as a formal sum $\pi = \pi_1 + \cdots + \pi_m$ of closed polygonal arcs in such a way that the winding number of $\pi$ is one at each point of $L^\circ$ and zero on the complement of $L$.
