Let $U$ be an open set in $\mathbb{C}$, let $f$ be a locally analytic function on $U$, and let $\gamma$ be a formal sum of closed rectifiable arcs in $U$ with the property that if $\lambda \notin U$ then $w_{\gamma}(\lambda) = 0$ (denoted $\gamma \sim 0$ in $U$). Then for all $\lambda \in U$ not on the range of $\gamma$,
$$w_{\gamma}(\lambda) f(\lambda) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{f(\zeta)}{\zeta - \lambda} \, d\zeta.$$
Moreover, $\int_{\gamma} f(\zeta) \, d\zeta = 0$ for every locally analytic $f$ on $U$.
