If $c$ is an infinite cardinal number, then $\aleph_0 c = c$. Consequently, if $c$ and $d$ are cardinal numbers and at least one of them is infinite, then
$$
c + d = c \vee d,
$$
the larger of the two. More generally, the sum of any finite collection of cardinal numbers, at least one of which is infinite, equals the largest cardinal number in the collection.
