Zermelo's theorem is equivalent to the Axiom of Choice. The authors of GTM055 state that "the axiom of choice is used without apology or explanation" and do not reproduce the standard proof (which proceeds via Zorn's Lemma or transfinite induction). The well-ordering principle is taken as a background set-theoretic fact.
