See Problem T for a sketch of the proof. The proof uses the fact that a space is compact if and only if every family of closed sets with the finite intersection property has nonempty intersection, together with Zorn's Lemma (which is equivalent to the Axiom of Choice) to extend finite intersection families to maximal ones.
