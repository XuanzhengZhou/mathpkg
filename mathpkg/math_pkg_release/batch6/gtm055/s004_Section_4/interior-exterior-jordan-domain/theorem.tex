If $J$ is the range of a Jordan loop $\gamma$, then the bounded component of $\mathbb{C} \setminus J$ is called the \textbf{interior domain} of $J$, and is denoted by $\text{Int}(\gamma)$ or $\text{Int}(J)$. The unbounded component of $\mathbb{C} \setminus J$ is the \textbf{exterior domain} of $J$ and is denoted by $\text{Ext}(\gamma)$ or $\text{Ext}(J)$.
