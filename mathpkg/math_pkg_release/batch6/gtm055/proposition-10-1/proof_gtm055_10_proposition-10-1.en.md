Proof. It suffices to show that every closed set $F$ in $X$ belongs to $\mathbf{S}(\mathcal{K})$. If $X = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} K_n$, where each $K_n$ is compact, then $F = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} (F \cap K_n)$, and each set $F \cap K_n$ is compact.
