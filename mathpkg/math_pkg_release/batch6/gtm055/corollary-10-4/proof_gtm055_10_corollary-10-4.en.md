Proof. It is an immediate consequence of Problem A that the $\sigma$-ring generated by the collection of topologically bounded open sets coincides with $S(\mathcal{H})$. If $X$ is $\sigma$-compact, this $\sigma$-ring coincides with the $\sigma$-ring of all Borel subsets of $X$ (Prop. 10.1).
