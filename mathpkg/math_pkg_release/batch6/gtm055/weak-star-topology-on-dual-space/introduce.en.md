The weak* topology on $\mathcal{E}^*$ is the topology of pointwise convergence on $\mathcal{E}$: a net $f_\lambda$ converges to $f$ if and only if $f_\lambda(x) \to f(x)$ for every $x \in \mathcal{E}$. It is induced by the evaluation functionals $j(x) \in \mathcal{E}^{**}$ for $x \in \mathcal{E}$, making it the coarsest topology for which all these evaluation maps are continuous.
