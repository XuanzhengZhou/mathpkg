The natural embedding $j: \mathcal{E} \to \mathcal{E}^{**}$ sends each vector $x$ to the evaluation functional $F_x(f) = f(x)$ on $\mathcal{E}^*$. Proposition 15.6 shows that $j$ is a linear isometry. This embedding plays a central role in duality theory, characterizing weak boundedness (Proposition 15.7) and relating to reflexivity.
