Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a locally convex topological linear space and let $M$ be a closed linear subspace of $\mathcal{E}$. Then $M$ is the intersection of the kernels of all those linear functionals in $\mathcal{E}^*$ that vanish on $M$. In particular, if $\mathcal{E}$ is separated, then $\mathcal{E}^*$ is separating on $\mathcal{E}$.
