Problem 18.Q provides a complete structural classification of closed self-conjugate subalgebras of $\mathcal{C}(X)$ for compact Hausdorff $X$. Every such subalgebra consists precisely of those continuous functions that are constant on the equivalence classes of a suitably defined equivalence relation on $X$. This generalizes the Stone-Weierstrass perspective on subalgebras.
