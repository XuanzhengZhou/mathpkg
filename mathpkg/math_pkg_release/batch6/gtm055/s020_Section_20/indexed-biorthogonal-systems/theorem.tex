An indexed biorthogonal system in a dual pair $\langle \mathcal{E}, \mathcal{F} \rangle$ is a family $\{(x_\gamma, f_\gamma)\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ with $x_\gamma \in \mathcal{E}$ and $f_\gamma \in \mathcal{F}$ satisfying $f_\alpha(x_\beta) = \delta_{\alpha\beta}$. The system is an indexed Markushevich basis if $\{x_\gamma\}$ is fundamental in the weak topology induced by $\mathcal{F}$ and $\{f_\gamma\}$ is total in the weak* topology induced by $\mathcal{E}$. Every separable Banach space admits a countable Markushevich basis.
