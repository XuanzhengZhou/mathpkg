Weak summability is a condition on a family of vectors requiring absolute summability of all scalar images under continuous linear functionals. While strictly weaker than norm summability in general (related to the presence of $c_0$ subspaces), it is a crucial concept in the Orlicz--Pettis theorem and the theory of unconditionally convergent series in Banach spaces.
