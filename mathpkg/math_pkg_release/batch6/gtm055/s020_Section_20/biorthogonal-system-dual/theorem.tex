Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a Banach space. A biorthogonal system in $\mathcal{E}$ is a family of pairs $\{(x_\gamma, f_\gamma)\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ with $x_\gamma \in \mathcal{E}$ and $f_\gamma \in \mathcal{E}^*$ such that $f_\alpha(x_\beta) = \delta_{\alpha\beta}$ (the Kronecker delta) for all $\alpha, \beta \in \Gamma$. The system is fundamental if the linear span of $\{x_\gamma\}$ is dense in $\mathcal{E}$, and total if the linear span of $\{f_\gamma\}$ is weak* dense in $\mathcal{E}^*$.
