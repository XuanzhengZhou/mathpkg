A biorthogonal system generalizes the notion of a basis and its dual coefficient functionals. It consists of paired families of vectors and functionals satisfying $f_\alpha(x_\beta) = \delta_{\alpha\beta}$. Such systems arise naturally in the study of Schauder bases, Markushevich bases, and the duality theory of Banach spaces.
