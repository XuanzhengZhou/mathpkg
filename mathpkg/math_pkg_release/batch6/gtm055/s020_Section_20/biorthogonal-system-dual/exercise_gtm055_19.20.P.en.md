If $X = \{x_n\}_{n=0}^{\infty}$ and $A = \{\alpha_n\}_{n=0}^{\infty}$ form a biorthogonal system for a Banach space $\mathcal{E}$, then $(A, j(X))$ is a biorthogonal system for the dual space $\mathcal{E}^*$ (where $j$ denotes the canonical embedding of $\mathcal{E}$ into $\mathcal{E}^{**}$).
