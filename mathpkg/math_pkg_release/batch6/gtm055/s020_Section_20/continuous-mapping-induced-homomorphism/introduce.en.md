Problem 18.G studies the pullback map $\Phi_F(f) = f \circ F$ induced by a continuous mapping $F: X \to Y$ between compact Hausdorff spaces. The map is always a contraction between the Banach algebras of continuous functions, is an isometry when $F$ is surjective, and is surjective when $F$ is injective (via the Tietze extension theorem). When $F$ is a homeomorphism, $\Phi_F$ is a unital Banach algebra isomorphism.
