For any finite set of indices $D \subset \Gamma$ we have
$$\|s_D\| \leq \sum_{\gamma \in D} \|x_{\gamma}\|.$$

If the family of norms is summable, then by the Cauchy criterion for real numbers, for any $\varepsilon > 0$ there exists a finite set $D_{\varepsilon}$ such that $\sum_{\gamma \in D} \|x_{\gamma}\| < \varepsilon$ for all finite $D$ disjoint from $D_{\varepsilon}$. The inequality above then gives $\|s_D\| < \varepsilon$ for such $D$, and the result follows from Proposition 19.4.
