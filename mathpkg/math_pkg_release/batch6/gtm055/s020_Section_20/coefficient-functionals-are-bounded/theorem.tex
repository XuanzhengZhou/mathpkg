Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a (separable) Banach space, let $\{x_n\}_{n=0}^{\infty}$ be a basis for $\mathcal{E}$, and for each nonnegative integer $n$ and vector $x$ in $\mathcal{E}$ let $\alpha_n(x)$ denote the $n$th coefficient of $x$ with respect to the basis $\{x_n\}$, so that
$$x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \alpha_n(x) x_n, \quad x \in \mathcal{E}.$$

Then $\alpha_n$ is a bounded linear functional on $\mathcal{E}$.
