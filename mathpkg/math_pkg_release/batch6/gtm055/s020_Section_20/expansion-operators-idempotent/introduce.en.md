Lemma 19.9 establishes that the expansion operators $E_N x = \sum_{n=0}^{N} \alpha_n(x) x_n$ of a biorthogonal system are commuting idempotents, with composition giving the operator indexed by the minimum of the indices. This algebraic property is fundamental to the theory of bases and biorthogonal systems.
