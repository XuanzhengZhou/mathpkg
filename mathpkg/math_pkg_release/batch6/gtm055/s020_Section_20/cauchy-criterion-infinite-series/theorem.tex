If $\{x_n\}_{n=0}^{\infty}$ is a sequence of vectors in a Banach space $\mathcal{E}$, then the infinite series $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} x_n$ is convergent in $\mathcal{E}$ if and only if, for each given positive number $\varepsilon$, there exists a positive integer $K$ such that $\left\|\sum_{n=K}^{K+p} x_n\right\| < \varepsilon$ for every positive integer $p$.
