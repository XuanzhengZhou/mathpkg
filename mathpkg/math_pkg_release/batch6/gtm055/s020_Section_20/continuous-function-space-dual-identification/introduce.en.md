Problem 18.B asks to show that the dual space $\mathcal{C}([a,b])^*$ can be identified not only with right-continuous functions of bounded variation (as in Example A) but also with a subspace of left-continuous functions. This highlights the non-uniqueness of the representing function in the Riesz representation for continuous functions on an interval.
