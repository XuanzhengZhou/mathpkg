Show that, for any Banach space $\mathcal{E}$ whatever, there exists a compact Hausdorff space $X = X_{\mathcal{E}}$ and an isometric isomorphism of $\mathcal{E}$ onto a subspace of $\mathcal{C}(X)$. Show also that $X$ can be taken to be metrizable if and only if $\mathcal{E}$ is separable. (Hint: For each $x$ in $\mathcal{E}$ consider $j(x)|_{(\mathcal{E}^*)_1}$.)
