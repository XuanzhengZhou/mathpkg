Proposition 19.7 shows that for any linearly independent sequence $\{x_n\}$ in a Banach space $\mathcal{E}$, the space $\mathcal{L}$ of coefficient sequences producing convergent series can be equipped with the sup-of-partial-sums norm, making $\mathcal{L}$ a Banach space. This structure is crucial for the proof that coefficient functionals are continuous (Theorem 19.8).
