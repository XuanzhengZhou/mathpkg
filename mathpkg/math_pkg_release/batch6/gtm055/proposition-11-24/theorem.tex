If $\{\mathcal{E}_\gamma\}_{\gamma \in \Gamma}$ is an arbitrary indexed family of topological vector spaces, then the product topology (Ex. 3N) is a linear topology on the full algebraic direct sum of the given family. (The full algebraic direct sum of the indexed family $\{\mathcal{E}_\gamma\}$ equipped with the product topology is usually called the product of the family.)
