The proposition **Ord($A$) $\wedge$ Tr($B$) $\rightarrow [B \subset A \leftrightarrow B \in A]$** (Proposition 7.7.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
