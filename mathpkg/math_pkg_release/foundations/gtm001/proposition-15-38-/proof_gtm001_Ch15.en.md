(1) If $\mathcal{P}(F''\aleph_\alpha) \subseteq F''\aleph_{\alpha+1}$ then from Proposition 15.37

$$\overline{2^{\aleph_\alpha}} = \overline{\mathcal{P}(\aleph_\alpha)} = \overline{\mathcal{P}(F''\aleph_\alpha)} \subseteq \overline{F''\aleph_{\alpha+1}} = \aleph_{\alpha+1}.$$

Since by Cantor’s Theorem $\overline{2^{\aleph_\alpha}} > \aleph_\alpha$ we have

$$\overline{2^{\aleph_\alpha}} = \aleph_{\alpha+1}.$$

(2) The proof is left to the reader.
