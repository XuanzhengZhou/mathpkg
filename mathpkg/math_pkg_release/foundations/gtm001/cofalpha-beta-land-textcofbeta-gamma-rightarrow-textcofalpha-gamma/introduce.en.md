The proposition **cof($\alpha, \beta$) $\land \text{cof}(\beta, \gamma) \rightarrow \text{cof}(\alpha, \gamma)$** (Proposition 11.5.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
