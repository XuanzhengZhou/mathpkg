The lemma **If $\pi$: On $\rightarrow$ On is a strong $M$-map, then there exists a $\Sigma_1$ elementary embedding $h$: $L \rightarrow L$ such that $\pi = h \upharpoonright$ On** (Lemma 17.31.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
