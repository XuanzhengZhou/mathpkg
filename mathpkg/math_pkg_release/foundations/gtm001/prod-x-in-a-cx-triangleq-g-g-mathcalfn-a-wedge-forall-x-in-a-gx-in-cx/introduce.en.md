The definition **$\prod_{x \in a} c'x \triangleq \{g | g \mathcal{F}n a \wedge (\forall x \in a) [g'x \in c'x]\}.$** (Definition 11.24.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
