From the Axiom of Regularity $E$ is a well-founded relation on $V$. From Proposition 9.12 if

$$A = \{x | \text{Wf}(x)\}$$

then $a \subseteq A$ implies $a \in A$. Then by $\in$-induction (Proposition 5.22) $A = V$. $\square$

Remark. From Proposition 9.13 we see that in the presence of the Axiom of Regularity the function $R_1$ determines a class of sets $\{R_1^\prime \alpha | \alpha \in \text{On}\}$ whose union is the entire universe. Furthermore, from Proposition 9.10, these sets are nested, i.e., $\alpha < \beta \rightarrow R_1^\prime \alpha \subset R_1^\prime \beta$.
