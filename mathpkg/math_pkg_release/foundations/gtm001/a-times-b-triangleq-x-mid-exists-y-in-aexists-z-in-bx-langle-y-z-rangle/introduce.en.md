The definition **$A \times B \triangleq \{x \mid (\exists y \in A)(\exists z \in B)[x = \langle y, z \rangle]\}$** (Definition 6.1.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 6 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
