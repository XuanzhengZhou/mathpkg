If $M$ is transitive, almost universal, and closed under the eight fundamental operations and $a \in M$, then:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(1)] $\cup(a)$ is $M$-constructible.
\item[(2)] $\mathcal{P}(a) \cap M$ is $M$-constructible.
\item[(3)] If $\varphi(c, d)$ satisfies the functionality condition and its parameters are in $M$, then the replacement image is $M$-constructible.
\end{itemize}
