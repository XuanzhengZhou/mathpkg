Defines the eight fundamental operations $\mathcal{F}_1$ through $\mathcal{F}_8$ introduced by Gödel for constructing the constructible universe $. These operations generate all constructible sets via transfinite recursion. Closure under these eight operations, together with transitivity and the almost universal property, is sufficient for a class to be a standard transitive model of ZF.
