$\alpha + \beta$ is order isomorphic to $(\{0\} \times \alpha) \cup (\{1\} \times \beta)$ where the order on the latter set is Le, i.e., $(\exists f)[f \text{ Isom}_{E, \text{Le}}(\alpha + \beta, (\{0\} \times \alpha) \cup (\{1\} \times \beta))]$.
