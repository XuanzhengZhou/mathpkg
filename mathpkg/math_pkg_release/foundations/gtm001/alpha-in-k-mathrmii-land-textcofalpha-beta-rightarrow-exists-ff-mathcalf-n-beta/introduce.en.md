The proposition **$\alpha \in K_{\mathrm{II}} \land \text{cof}(\alpha, \beta) \rightarrow (\exists f)[f \mathcal{F}_n \beta \land \alpha = \cup(f“\beta).]$** (Proposition 11.3.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
