Let $\Pi_1$ and $\Pi_2$ be two $\kappa$-direct limit systems. Then their sum $\Pi$ with index set $I = I_1 \cup I_2$ (assuming $I_1 \cap I_2 = 0$) is a $\kappa$-direct limit system. For $i \in I_1$, $j \in I_2$, the ordering is defined by:

$$i < j \iff \xi_i \leq \eta_j \wedge \alpha_i \leq \beta_j \wedge \rho_{i\infty}^* P_i \subseteq \mathcal{W}(\theta_{j\infty}) \wedge \sup\{\rho_{i\infty}(v) + 1 \mid v < \xi_i\} \in \mathcal{W}(\theta_{j\infty}).$$

The connecting map is $\pi_{ij} = \theta_{j\infty}^{-1} \circ \rho_{i\infty}$, which is a medium $M$-map.
