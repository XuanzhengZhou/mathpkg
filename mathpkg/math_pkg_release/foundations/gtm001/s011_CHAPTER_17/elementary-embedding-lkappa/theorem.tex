There exists an elementary embedding $h: L_{\bar{\kappa}} \rightarrow L_{\kappa}$ such that

(1) $X \subseteq \mathcal{W}(h)$ and $\bar{X} = \overline{\mathcal{W}(h)}$;

(2) if $\bar{\Pi} = \langle \langle \bar{\delta}_i, \bar{\alpha}_i, \bar{P}_i \rangle, \bar{\pi}_{ij} \rangle_{i,j \in I}$ is a well-founded $\bar{\kappa}$-direct limit system, then $h(\bar{\Pi})$ is a well-founded $\kappa$-direct limit system.
