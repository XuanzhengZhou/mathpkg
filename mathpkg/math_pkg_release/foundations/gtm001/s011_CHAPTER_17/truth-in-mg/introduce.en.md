This definition establishes the truth predicate for the generic extension $M[G]$ with respect to limited sentences of the forcing language. A limited sentence $\varphi$ is true in $M[G]$ precisely when its denotation $D^G(\varphi)$ holds. This internal definition of truth is what allows the forcing relation to be formalized and the fundamental theorems of forcing to be proved.
