Let $\kappa$ be a regular uncountable cardinal and let $\lambda$ be an ordinal such that $\kappa < \lambda$. For each set $X$ with $X \subseteq \lambda$ and $\overline{X} < \kappa$, there exists a subalgebra $Y$ of $M^{\lambda}$ such that $X \subseteq Y$, $\overline{Y} < \kappa$, and $Y \cap \kappa \in \kappa$.
