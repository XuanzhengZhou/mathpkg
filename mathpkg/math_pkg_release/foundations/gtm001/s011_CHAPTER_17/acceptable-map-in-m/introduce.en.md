This lemma shows that acceptable maps are absolute for transitive models elementarily equivalent to some $L_\kappa$. The proof uses the fact that the fundamental operations $F_0, J, C_i, T, K$ are absolute with respect to $M$, and constructs $\pi$ via a sequence of approximations defined using these operations within $M$.
