Property $I$ asserts that every medium $M$-map defined on a regular cardinal is the identity. This is a strong structural property of the Silver machine, asserting that the machine operations cannot collapse or move regular cardinals. The main theorem of this section (Theorem 17.21) shows that $I$ implies property $S$, a statement about cardinal arithmetic in $L$.
