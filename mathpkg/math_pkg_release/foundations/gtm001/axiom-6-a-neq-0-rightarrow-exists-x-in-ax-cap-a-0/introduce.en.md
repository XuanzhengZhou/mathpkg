The theorem **(Axiom 6'). $A \neq 0 \rightarrow (\exists x \in A)[x \cap A = 0]$** (Theorem 9.20) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
