** (By induction). If $(\forall \gamma < \alpha)[f' \gamma = g' \gamma]$ then $f \uparrow \alpha = g \uparrow \alpha$ and hence $f' \alpha = G'(f \uparrow \alpha) = G'(g \uparrow \alpha) = g' \alpha.$
