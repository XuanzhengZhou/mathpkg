The lemma **Let $Z$ be an elementary submodel of $L_\kappa$ such that $X \subseteq Z$ and $\overline{Z} = \overline{X}$. Then there exists a $Z'$, an elementary submodel of $L_\kappa$ such that** (Lemma 17.27.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
