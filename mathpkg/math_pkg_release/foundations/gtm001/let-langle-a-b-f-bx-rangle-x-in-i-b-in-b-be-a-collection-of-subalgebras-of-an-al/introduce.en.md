The proposition **Let $\{ \langle A_b, F_{bx} \rangle_{x \in I} | b \in B \}$ be a collection of subalgebras of an algebra $\mathfrak{A}$. Then** (Proposition 16.5.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
