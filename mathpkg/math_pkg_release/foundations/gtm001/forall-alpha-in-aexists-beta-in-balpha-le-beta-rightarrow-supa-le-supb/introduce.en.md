The proposition **$(\forall \alpha \in A)(\exists \beta \in B)[\alpha \le \beta] \rightarrow \sup(A) \le \sup(B).$** (Proposition 8.6.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 8 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
