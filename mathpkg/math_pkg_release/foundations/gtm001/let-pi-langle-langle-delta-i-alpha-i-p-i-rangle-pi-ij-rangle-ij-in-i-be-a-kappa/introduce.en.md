The definition **Let $\Pi = \langle \langle \delta_i, \alpha_i, P_i \rangle, \pi_{ij} \rangle_{i,j \in I}$ be a $\kappa$-direct limit system, and $Z \subseteq L_\kappa$** (Definition 17.24.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
