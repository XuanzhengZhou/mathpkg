The lemma **If $9 \subseteq b \subseteq$ On, if $b$ is closed w.r.t. $C$ and $J$, and if $x \in F''b$, then** (Lemma 2.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
