The proposition **If $F \mathcal{F}_n$ On $\wedge (\forall \alpha)[F' \alpha \in [A - F' \alpha]]$, then** (Proposition 7.48.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
