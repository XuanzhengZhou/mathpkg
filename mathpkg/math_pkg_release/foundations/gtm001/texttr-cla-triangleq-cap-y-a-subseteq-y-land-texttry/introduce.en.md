The definition **$\text{Tr Cl}(a) \triangleq \cap \{y | a \subseteq y \land \text{Tr}(y)\}.$** (Definition 9.2.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
