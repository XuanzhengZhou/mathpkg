The proposition **If $\pi: \xi \rightarrow \eta$ is a strong $\mathfrak{A}$-map and $\delta \leq \eta$ is an ordinal such that $\mathcal{W}(\pi) \subseteq \delta$, then $\pi: \xi \rightarrow \delta$ is a strong $\mathfrak{A}$-map** (Proposition 16.14.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
