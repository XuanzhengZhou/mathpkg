** For any $\alpha \in \xi^\omega$,

$$F_i^\xi(\alpha) \simeq \beta \rightarrow F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha)) \simeq \pi(\beta) \wedge \pi(\beta) < \delta$$
$$\rightarrow F_i^\delta(\pi(\alpha)) \simeq \pi(\beta)$$
$$F_i^\xi(\alpha) \text{ undefined} \rightarrow F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha)) \text{ undefined}$$
$$\rightarrow F_i^\delta(\pi(\alpha)) \text{ undefined.}$$
