The definition **The ordered triple $\langle \delta, \alpha, P \rangle$ is an acceptable triple iff** (Definition 17.8.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
