The definition **$J' \langle \alpha, \beta, m \rangle \triangleq 9 \cdot J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle + m, m < 9$** (Definition 15.2.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
