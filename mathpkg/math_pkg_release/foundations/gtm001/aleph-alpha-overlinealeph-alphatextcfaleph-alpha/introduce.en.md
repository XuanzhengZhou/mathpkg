The theorem **$\aleph_\alpha < \overline{\aleph_\alpha^{\text{cf}(\aleph_\alpha)}}$** (Theorem 11.28.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
