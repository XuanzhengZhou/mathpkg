The proposition **If $A$ is nonempty and transitive then $A$ is a standard transitive model of the Axiom of Powers iff** (Proposition 13.32.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
