If $a \subseteq b$, then since $b \simeq \bar{b}$ it follows that $(\exists x)[x \subseteq \bar{b} \wedge a \simeq x]$. By Proposition 10.12 there exists an ordinal $\beta \leq \bar{b}$ such that $\beta \simeq x$. We then have

$$\bar{a} = \bar{x} = \bar{\beta} \leq \beta \leq \bar{b}.$$
