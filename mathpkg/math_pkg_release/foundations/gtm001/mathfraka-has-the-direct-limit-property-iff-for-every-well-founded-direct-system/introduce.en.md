The definition **$\mathfrak{A}$ has the direct limit property iff for every well-founded direct system $\Pi = \langle \eta_i, \pi_{ij} \rangle_{i \leq j}$ of strong $\mathfrak{A}$-maps (medium $\mathfrak{A}$-maps), each $\pi_{i\infty} : \eta_i \rightarrow \eta_\infty$** (Definition 16.29.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
