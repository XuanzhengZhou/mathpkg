The definition **The mapping $\pi: \langle \delta, \alpha, P \rangle \rightarrow \langle \delta', \alpha', P' \rangle$ is an acceptable map iff** (Definition 17.9.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
