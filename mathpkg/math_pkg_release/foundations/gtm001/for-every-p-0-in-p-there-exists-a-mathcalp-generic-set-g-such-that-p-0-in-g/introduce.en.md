The lemma **For every $p_0 \in P$, there exists a $\mathcal{P}$-generic set $G$ such that $p_0 \in G$** (Lemma 1.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
