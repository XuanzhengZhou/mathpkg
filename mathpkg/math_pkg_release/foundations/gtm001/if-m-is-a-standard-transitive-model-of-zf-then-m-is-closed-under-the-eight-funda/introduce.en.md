The proposition **If $M$ is a standard transitive model of ZF then $M$ is closed under the eight fundamental operations** (Proposition 14.3.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 14 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
