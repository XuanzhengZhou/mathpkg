For any base $\alpha > 1$ and any positive ordinal $\beta$, there exists a unique ordinal $\delta$ such that $\alpha^\delta \le \beta < \alpha^{\delta+1}$. This $\delta$ is called the logarithm of $\beta$ base $\alpha$. This proposition is essential for the Cantor Normal Form representation of ordinals.
