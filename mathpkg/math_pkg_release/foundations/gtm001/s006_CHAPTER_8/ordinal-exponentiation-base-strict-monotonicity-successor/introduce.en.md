If $\alpha < \beta$ and the exponent $\gamma$ is a successor ordinal (i.e., $\gamma \in K_{\mathrm{I}}$ and $\gamma \neq 0$), then the strict inequality is preserved: $\alpha^\gamma < \beta^\gamma$. For limit ordinal exponents, the inequality may collapse to equality.
