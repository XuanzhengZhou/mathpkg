The unordered pair $\{a, b\}$ is the class of all $x$ such that $x = a$ or $x = b$. This construction captures the intuitive notion of a two-element collection without regard to order. When $a = b$, the pair $\{a, a\}$ is called the singleton $\{a\}$. The Axiom of Pairing (Axiom 2) postulates that every unordered pair is a set.
