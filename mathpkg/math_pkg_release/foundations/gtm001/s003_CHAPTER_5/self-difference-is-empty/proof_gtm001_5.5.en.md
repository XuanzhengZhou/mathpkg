By Definition 5.12,

$$a - a = \{x \in a \mid x \notin a\}.$$

No $x$ can satisfy both $x \in a$ and $x \notin a$, so

$$a - a = \{x \mid x \neq x\} = 0.$$

The last equality holds by Definition 5.14.
