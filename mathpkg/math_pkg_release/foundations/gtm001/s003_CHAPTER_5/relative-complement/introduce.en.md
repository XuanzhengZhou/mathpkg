The relative complement $A - B$, also called the complement of $B$ relative to $A$, is the class of all $x$ such that $x \in A$ and $x \notin B$. The symbol $A - B$ is read as "$A$ minus $B$." When $A$ is a set, $A - B$ is also a set by Proposition 5.13.
