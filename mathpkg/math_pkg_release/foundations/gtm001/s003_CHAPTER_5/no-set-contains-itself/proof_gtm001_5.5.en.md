This is the special case $n = 1$ of Proposition 5.18, which asserts that there are no finite membership cycles $\neg [a_1 \in a_2 \in \cdots \in a_n \in a_1]$. Taking $n = 1$, we obtain $\neg [a_1 \in a_1]$, i.e., $a \notin a$.
