By *Theorem 10.3

$(\exists \beta)(\exists h)[h: \beta \xrightarrow{1-1} R_1^\alpha].$

Since $(\forall x \in a)(\exists y \in R_1^\alpha) \varphi(x, y)$ and since $h$ is onto,

$(\forall x \in a)(\exists \gamma \in \beta) \varphi(x, h^\gamma).$

There is then a smallest such $\gamma$. Let $f$ be a function defined on $a$ by

$$f'x = h' \mu_\gamma(\varphi(x, h^\gamma)), \quad x \in a$$

then

$$(\forall x \in a) \varphi(x, f'x).$$
