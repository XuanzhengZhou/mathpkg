**Proof.** Since $(\forall \alpha \in M)(\exists x \in M)[x = F' \alpha]^M$ and $x = F' \alpha$ Abs $M$, we have $(\forall \alpha)(\exists x \in M)[x = F' \alpha]$ because $\mathrm{On} \subseteq M$. Therefore $L \subseteq M$.
