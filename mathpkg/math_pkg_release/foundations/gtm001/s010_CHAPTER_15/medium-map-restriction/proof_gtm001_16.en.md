**Proof.** Assume $\pi$ strong. Let $\delta = \sup \{\pi(\alpha) + 1 \mid \alpha < \gamma\}$. For $\alpha \in \gamma^\omega$: $F_i^\gamma(\alpha) \simeq \beta \rightarrow F_i^\xi(\alpha) \simeq \beta \land \beta < \gamma \rightarrow F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha)) \simeq \pi(\beta)$ with $\pi(\beta) < \delta$.
