The ordinal pairing function $J_0$ well-orders $\mathrm{On}^2$. This proposition establishes two fundamental bounds: $J'_0\langle\alpha,\beta\rangle \geq \max(\alpha,\beta)$, and $J'_0$ preserves the property of being below $\aleph_\gamma$.
