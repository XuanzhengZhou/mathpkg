**Proof.** (1) As $F$ is a function, $\overline{F'' \aleph_\alpha} \leq \aleph_\alpha$. Also $F' J' \langle 0, \beta, 0 \rangle = F'' J' \langle 0, \beta, 0 \rangle$, and the map $\beta \mapsto F'J'\langle 0, \beta, 0 \rangle$ is injective on $\aleph_\alpha$, giving $\aleph_\alpha \leq \overline{F'' \aleph_\alpha}$.
