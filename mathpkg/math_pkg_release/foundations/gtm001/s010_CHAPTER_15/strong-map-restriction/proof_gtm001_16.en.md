**Proof.** For $\alpha \in \xi^\omega$: $F_i^\xi(\alpha) \simeq \beta \rightarrow F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha)) \simeq \pi(\beta)$ with $\pi(\beta) < \delta$, so $F_i^\delta(\pi(\alpha)) \simeq \pi(\beta)$. If $F_i^\xi(\alpha)$ undefined, $F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha))$ and $F_i^\delta(\pi(\alpha))$ are also undefined.
