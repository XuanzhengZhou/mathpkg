The definition **$\text{Le} \triangleq \{ \langle \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle, \langle \gamma, \delta \rangle \rangle | \alpha < \gamma \vee [\alpha = \gamma \wedge \beta < \delta] \}$** (Definition 7.55.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
