The definition **A structure $\langle A, F_x \rangle_{x \in I}$ is an algebra iff $A$ and $I$ are classes and for each $x$ in $I, F_x$ is a partial map from $A^\omega$ to $A$. The class $A$ is the universe of the algebra** (Definition 16.3.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
