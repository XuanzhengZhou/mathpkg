(1) $\varphi$ Abs $A \overset{\Delta}{\leftrightarrow} [\varphi^A \leftrightarrow \varphi]$ if $\varphi$ is closed.

(2) $\varphi$ Abs $A \xleftrightarrow{a_1} \in A \wedge \cdots \wedge a_n \in A \rightarrow [\varphi^A \leftrightarrow \varphi]$ where $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ is a complete list of all of the free variables in $\varphi$.
