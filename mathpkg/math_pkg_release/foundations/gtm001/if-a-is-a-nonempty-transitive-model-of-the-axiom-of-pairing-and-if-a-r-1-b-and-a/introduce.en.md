The proposition **If $A$ is a nonempty transitive model of the Axiom of Pairing and if $a R_1 b$ and $a R_2 b$ are each absolute with respect to $A$ then $f$ Isom$_{R_1, R_2}(a, b)$ Abs $A$** (Proposition 13.30.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
