The corollary **$(\exists! f)[f \mathcal{F}_n \alpha \wedge (\forall \beta < \alpha)[f' \beta = G'(f \upharpoonright \beta)]]$** (Corollary 7.42.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
