Let $\beta = cf(\alpha)$. If $\gamma \simeq \beta$ and $\gamma < \beta$, then Corollary 11.8 gives $(\exists \eta \leq \gamma)[\text{cof}(\beta, \eta)]$, so $\beta = cf(\alpha) \leq \eta \leq \gamma$, contradiction. Hence $\gamma \geq \beta$ and $\beta$ is a cardinal.
