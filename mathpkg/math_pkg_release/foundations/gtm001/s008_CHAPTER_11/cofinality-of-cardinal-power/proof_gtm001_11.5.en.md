By contradiction. If $cf(\overline{\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}}}) = \aleph_{\gamma} \leq \aleph_{\beta}$, then Theorem 11.28 gives $\overline{\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}}} < (\overline{\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}}})^{\aleph_{\gamma}} = \overline{\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta} \times \aleph_{\gamma}}} = \overline{\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}}}$, contradiction.
