Case 1 ($\aleph_{\beta} < cf(\aleph_{\alpha})$): Theorem 11.23 gives the result.\n\nCase 2 ($cf(\aleph_{\alpha}) \leq \aleph_{\beta} \leq \aleph_{\alpha}$): By Theorem 11.28, $\aleph_{\alpha} < \aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}}$. By GCH and Theorem 11.21, $\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}} \leq (2^{\aleph_{\alpha}})^{\aleph_{\alpha}} = 2^{\aleph_{\alpha}} = \aleph_{\alpha+1}$, so $\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}} = \aleph_{\alpha+1}$.\n\nCase 3 ($\aleph_{\alpha} \leq \aleph_{\beta}$): By GCH, $\aleph_{\beta} \leq \aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}} \leq (2^{\aleph_{\beta}})^{\aleph_{\beta}} = 2^{\aleph_{\beta}} = \aleph_{\beta+1}$. Since $\aleph_{\alpha}^{\aleph_{\beta}}$ is a cardinal, equality to $\aleph_{\beta+1}$ follows.
