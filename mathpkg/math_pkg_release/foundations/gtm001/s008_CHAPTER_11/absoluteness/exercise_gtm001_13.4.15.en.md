(18) $\text{Orf}(f)$.

(19) $\text{Smo}(f)$.

(20) $a \simeq b$.

(21) $\text{Fin}(a)$.

(22) $\text{Inf}(a)$.

(23) $\text{cof}(\alpha, \beta)$.

(24) $\text{Reg}(\alpha)$.

(25) $\text{Inacc}_w(\aleph_\alpha)$.

(26) $\text{Inacc}(\aleph_\alpha)$.

(27) $\text{Cl}(r, a)$.

(28) $\text{Cl}_2(r, a)$.

(29) $\text{St}(a)$.
