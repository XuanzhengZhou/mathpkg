The proposition **$\overline{a} \geq \omega \rightarrow \overline{a \times a} = \overline{a}.$** (Proposition 10.40.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
