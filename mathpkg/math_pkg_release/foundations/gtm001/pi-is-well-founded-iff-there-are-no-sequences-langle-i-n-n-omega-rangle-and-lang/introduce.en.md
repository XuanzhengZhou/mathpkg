The lemma **$\Pi$ is well founded iff there are no sequences $\langle i_n | n < \omega \rangle$ and $\langle \sigma_n | n < \omega \rangle$ such that $i_0 \leq i_1 \leq \cdots \leq i_n \leq \cdots$ and $\pi_{i_n i_{n+1}}(\sigma_n) > \sigma_{n+1}$** (Lemma 16.28.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
