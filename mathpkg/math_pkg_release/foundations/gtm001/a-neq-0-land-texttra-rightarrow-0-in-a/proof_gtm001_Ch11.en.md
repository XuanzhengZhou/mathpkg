** By the Axiom of Regularity

$$A \neq 0 \land \text{Tr}(A) \rightarrow (\exists x)[x \in A \land x \cap A = 0]$$

$$\rightarrow (\exists x)[x \in A \land x = 0]$$

$$\rightarrow 0 \in A.$$
