For any formula $\varphi(x)$ and any $u \in V^{(B)}$ (or $V^{(\tilde{B})}$):

$$\llbracket (\exists x \in u)\,\varphi(x) \rrbracket = \sum_{x \in \mathcal{D}(u)} (u(x) \cdot \llbracket \varphi(x) \rrbracket),$$

$$\llbracket (\forall x \in u)\,\varphi(x) \rrbracket = \prod_{x \in \mathcal{D}(u)} (u(x) \Rightarrow \llbracket \varphi(x) \rrbracket).$$

Here $\mathcal{D}(u)$ denotes the domain of $u$, and $\sum$, $\prod$ denote Boolean supremum and infimum respectively.
