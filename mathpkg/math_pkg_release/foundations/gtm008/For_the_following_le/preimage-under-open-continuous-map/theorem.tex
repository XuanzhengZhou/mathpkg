Let $X, Y$ be topological spaces and let $f: X \rightarrow Y$ be an open continuous map onto $Y$. Then for $B \subseteq Y$ we have:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $(f^{-1})``(B^{-}) = ((f^{-1})``B)^{-}$.
\item $(f^{-1})``(B^{0}) = ((f^{-1})``B)^{0}$.
\end{enumerate}
Here $(f^{-1})``B$ denotes the preimage of $B$ under $f$.
