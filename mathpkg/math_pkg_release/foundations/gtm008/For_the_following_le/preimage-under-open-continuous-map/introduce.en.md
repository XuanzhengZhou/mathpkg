For an open continuous surjection $f: X \to Y$, the preimage operation commutes with both topological closure and interior. That is, the preimage of the closure of a set equals the closure of the preimage, and similarly for interior. This property is used extensively in the study of complete monomorphisms between complete Boolean algebras, where the associated maps between the Stone spaces (partial order structures) are required to be open, continuous, and onto.
