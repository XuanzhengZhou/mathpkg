Both constructions $V^{(B)}$ and $V^{(\tilde{B})}$ of the Boolean-valued universe over a class-sized Boolean algebra satisfy the Axiom of Regularity. This is because the Boolean-valued membership relation $\in$ is well-founded by construction: each element of $V^{(B)}$ is built from elements of strictly lower rank in the cumulative hierarchy, mirroring the well-foundedness of the real $\in$ relation in the ground model.
