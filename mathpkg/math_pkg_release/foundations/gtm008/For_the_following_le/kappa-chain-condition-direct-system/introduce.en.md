Corollary 22.31 provides sufficient conditions for a direct system of complete Boolean algebras to produce a limit algebra satisfying the $\kappa$-chain condition. When each $B_\alpha$ has cardinality less than the regular cardinal $\kappa$, the union $B$ already satisfies the $\kappa$-chain condition and is its own completion.
