$\tilde{B}$ satisfies the uniform convergence law (UCL) iff for every set $I$ and family $\{b_{\alpha i} \mid \alpha \in On\} \subseteq \tilde{B}$ ($i \in I$):
1. $(\forall \alpha)(\forall \beta)[\alpha < \beta \rightarrow b_{\alpha i} \geq b_{\beta i}]$,
2. $\prod_{\alpha \in On} b_{\alpha i} = 0$,
imply $\prod_{\alpha \in On} \sum_{i \in I} b_{\alpha i} = 0$.
