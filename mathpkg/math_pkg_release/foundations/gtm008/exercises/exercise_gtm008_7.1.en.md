If $M$ is a standard transitive model of $ZF$, $On \subseteq M$, and $a \subseteq M$ then $M[a]$ is the smallest standard transitive model $N$ of $ZF$ in the language $\mathcal{L}_0(\{M(), C_a\})$ such that (i) $M \subseteq N$ and (ii) $a \in N$.
