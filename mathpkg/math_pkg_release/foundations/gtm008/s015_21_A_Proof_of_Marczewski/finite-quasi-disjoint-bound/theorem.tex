Let $\alpha, \gamma$ be cardinals with $\gamma \geq \aleph_0$. Let $\{A_t \mid t \in T\}$ and $\{B_t \mid t \in T\}$ be families of sets such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item $(\forall t \in T)[\bar{A}_t \leq \alpha \;\wedge\; \bar{B}_t \leq \gamma]$,
\item $(\forall t \in T)[A_t \cap B_t = 0]$,
\item $(\forall t, t' \in T)[t \neq t' \rightarrow A_t \cap B_{t'} \neq 0]$.
\end{enumerate}
Then $\bar{T} \leq \gamma^\alpha$.
