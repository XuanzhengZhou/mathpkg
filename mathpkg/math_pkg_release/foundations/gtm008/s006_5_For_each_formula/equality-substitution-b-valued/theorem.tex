For all $t_1, t_2, t_3 \in T_{\alpha}$,

1. $\llbracket t_1 = t_2 \rrbracket \llbracket t_2 \in t_3 \rrbracket \leq \llbracket t_1 \in t_3 \rrbracket$.
2. $\llbracket t_1 = t_2 \rrbracket \llbracket t_3 \in t_1 \rrbracket \leq \llbracket t_3 \in t_2 \rrbracket$.
3. $\llbracket t_1 = t_2 \rrbracket \llbracket t_3 = t_1 \rrbracket \leq \llbracket t_3 = t_2 \rrbracket$.
4. $\llbracket t_1 = t_2 \rrbracket \llbracket t_2 = t_3 \rrbracket \leq \llbracket t_1 = t_3 \rrbracket$.
