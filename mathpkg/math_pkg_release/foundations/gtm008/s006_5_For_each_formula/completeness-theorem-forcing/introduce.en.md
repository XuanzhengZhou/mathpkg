Theorem 10.6 is the completeness theorem for forcing, establishing the fundamental equivalence between the syntactic forcing relation $p \Vdash \varphi$ and truth in all generic extensions $M[G']$ containing $p$. This theorem bridges the combinatorial definition of forcing with semantic truth in generic models, and is central to the technique of independence proofs in set theory.
