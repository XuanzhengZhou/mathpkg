Theorem 9.25 verifies that $M$ satisfies the Axiom Schema of Replacement with Boolean truth value $1$. The proof uses Corollary 9.15 to uniformly bound the existential quantifier in the replacement formula over all $x \in M_{\alpha}$, and Theorem 9.23 to collect the witnesses into a single set $b$.
