Theorem 9.20 is a key technical lemma for proving the Powerset Axiom. It states that any $B$-valued subset of $a \in M_{\alpha+1}$ definable by a formula $\varphi$ is already represented by an element defined over $M_{\alpha}$. This provides the necessary control over the complexity of subsets in the cumulative $B$-valued hierarchy.
