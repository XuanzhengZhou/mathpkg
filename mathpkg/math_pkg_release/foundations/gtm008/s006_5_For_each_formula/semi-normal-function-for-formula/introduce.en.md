Theorem 9.16 strengthens Corollary 9.15 by producing a semi-normal (continuous and increasing) function $F$ that uniformly bounds the existential quantifier for a given formula $\varphi$ with parameters. The function $F(\alpha)$ is defined as the least $\gamma \geq \alpha$ such that the existential supremum over $M_{\gamma}$ equals the full supremum for all parameter tuples from $M_{\alpha}$.
