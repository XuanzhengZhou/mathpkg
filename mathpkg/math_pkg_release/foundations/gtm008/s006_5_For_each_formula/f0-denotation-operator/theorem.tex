Given an $M$-complete homomorphism $h: |B| \to |2|$, define

$$
F_0 \triangleq \{k \in K \mid h(f_0(k)) = 1\}.
$$

For constant terms $t, t_1, t_2$, the denotation operator $D$ is defined by:

1. $D(t_1 \in t_2) \triangleq D(t_1) \in D(t_2)$.
2. $D(V(t)) \triangleq D(t) \in M$.
3. $D(F(t)) \triangleq D(t) \in F_0$.

Let $M[F_0] \triangleq M[h] \triangleq \{D(t) \mid t \in T\}$, where $T = \{T_{\alpha}^M \mid \alpha \in M\}$.
