Theorem 9.33 establishes that every level $T_{\alpha}$ of the ramified $B$-valued hierarchy satisfies the Axiom of Extensionality. The proof is immediate from Definition 9.27(6), which defines the Boolean truth value of equality $\llbracket t_1 = t_2 \rrbracket$ precisely as the product over all $t \in T_{\alpha}$ of $\llbracket t \in t_1 \leftrightarrow t \in t_2 \rrbracket$. This definition ensures that the extensionality axiom holds with value $1$ at every stage of the construction.
