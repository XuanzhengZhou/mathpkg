Definition 10.2 introduces the fundamental forcing relation $p \Vdash \varphi$ ("$p$ forces $\varphi$"), which is at the heart of Cohen's method of forcing. The definition identifies forcing with membership in the Boolean truth value $\llbracket \varphi \rrbracket$, where $\llbracket \varphi \rrbracket$ is computed in the $B$-valued model constructed from the partial order $P$. This elegantly connects the syntactic forcing relation to the semantic Boolean-valued interpretation.
