By Definition 10.2, $p \Vdash \varphi$ is defined as $p \in \llbracket \varphi \rrbracket$. Since $\llbracket \varphi \rrbracket$ is a regular open set in the Boolean algebra $B$ associated with $P$, and the membership relation is definable in $\mathcal{L}_0$ (using the parameters $\llbracket \varphi \rrbracket$, $b$, and $\mathbf{P}$), the forcing relation is definable as claimed. The uniformity for limited formulas follows from the uniform definability of $\llbracket \varphi \rrbracket$ for limited $\varphi$.
