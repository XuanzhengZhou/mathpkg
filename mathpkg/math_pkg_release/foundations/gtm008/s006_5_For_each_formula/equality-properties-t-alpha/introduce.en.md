Theorem 9.28 verifies the two fundamental properties of the equality predicate in the ramified $B$-valued structure $\mathbf{T}_{\alpha}$: reflexivity ($\llbracket t_1 = t_1 \rrbracket = 1$) and symmetry. These are immediate consequences of Definition 9.27, which defines the Boolean truth value of equality in terms of the product over all elements of the membership equivalence. This theorem confirms that $\mathbf{T}_{\alpha}$ interprets equality as a well-behaved $B$-valued equivalence relation.
