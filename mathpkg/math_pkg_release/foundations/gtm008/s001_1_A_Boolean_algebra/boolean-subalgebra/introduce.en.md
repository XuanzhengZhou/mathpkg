A Boolean subalgebra $B'$ of a Boolean algebra $B$ is a Boolean algebra whose universe is a subset of $|B|$, with the operations $(+, \cdot, -)$ being the restrictions of those of $B$, and sharing the same distinguished elements $0$ and $1$. This is the standard algebraic notion of a substructure applied to Boolean algebras, and forms the basis for studying embeddings and intersections of Boolean algebras.
