Theorem 13.9 verifies that each restricted $B$-valued structure $V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$ satisfies the Axiom of Extensionality with Boolean truth value $1$. This is a consequence of the way equality was defined in Definition 13.3 — the definition of $[\![u = v]\!]$ directly encodes the extensionality condition as a product of Boolean implications, ensuring that if $u$ and $v$ have the same elements (in the Boolean sense), they are equal.
