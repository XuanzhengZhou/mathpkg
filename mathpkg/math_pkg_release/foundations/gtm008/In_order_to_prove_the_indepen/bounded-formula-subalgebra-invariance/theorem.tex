Let $B$ be a complete subalgebra of the complete Boolean algebra $B'$ and $\varphi(u_1, \ldots, u_n)$ be a formula in which every quantifier is bounded (i.e., of the form $\exists x \in y$ or $\forall x \in y$). Then for $u_1, \ldots, u_n \in V^{(B)}$,
$$[\![\varphi(u_1, \ldots, u_n)]\!]^{(B)} = [\![\varphi(u_1, \ldots, u_n)]\!]^{(B')}.$$
