Theorem 13.18 extends the atomic formula preservation result (Theorem 13.15) to all bounded formulas (formulas where every quantifier is restricted to the form $\exists x \in y$ or $\forall x \in y$). This absoluteness property is crucial for comparing Boolean-valued models built from different complete Boolean algebras, and is the Boolean-valued analogue of the fact that $\Delta_0$-formulas are absolute between transitive models of set theory.
