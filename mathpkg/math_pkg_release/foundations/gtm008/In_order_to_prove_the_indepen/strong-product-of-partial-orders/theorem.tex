Let $P_i = \langle P_i, \leq \rangle$, $i \in I$, be a family of partial order structures with $I$ a set. Then
$$P = \prod_{i \in I} P_i$$
(the strong product of the $P_i$'s) is defined to be the partial order structure $\langle P, \leq \rangle$ where
$$P \triangleq \prod_{i \in I} P_i$$
and
$$p_1 \leq p_2 \xrightarrow{\Delta} (\forall i \in I)[p_1(i) \leq p_2(i)] \text{ for } p_1, p_2 \in P.$$
