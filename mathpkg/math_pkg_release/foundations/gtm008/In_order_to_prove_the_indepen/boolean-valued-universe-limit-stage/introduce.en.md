Theorem 13.2 states that the Boolean-valued hierarchy $V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$ is continuous at limit ordinals: the $\alpha$-th level for a limit ordinal $\alpha$ is simply the union of all previous levels. This mirrors the behavior of the standard cumulative hierarchy $V_\alpha$ and ensures the recursion is well-defined.
