Theorem 13.12 is the central result of Boolean-valued set theory: the Boolean-valued universe $V^{(B)}$ satisfies all axioms of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with Boolean truth value $1$. The proof follows from showing that the sequence of structures $\langle V_{\alpha}^{(B)} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$ satisfies the conditions of the ramified language framework established in Section 9, from which Theorem 9.26 yields the result. This provides the foundation for independence proofs via Boolean-valued models.
