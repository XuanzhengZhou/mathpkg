The product of a family of partial order structures, each having a greatest element, is defined by taking the restricted direct product (all but finitely many coordinates equal the greatest element) with the pointwise order. The restricted product is needed to ensure compatibility with the ultrafilter construction: the ultrafilter space of the product is homeomorphic to the topological product of the individual ultrafilter spaces.
