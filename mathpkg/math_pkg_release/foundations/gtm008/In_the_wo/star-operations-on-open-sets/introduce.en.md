The star operations $^*$ and $^\Delta$ provide a Galois-like correspondence between open sets of a partial order structure $P$ and open sets of its associated ultrafilter space $\mathcal{F}$. The operation $G_1^*$ collects all ultrafilters whose basic neighborhoods are contained in $G_1$, while $G_2^\Delta$ collects all elements of $P$ whose basic open sets are contained in $G_2$. These operations are key to establishing the connection between regular open algebras of partial orders and topological spaces.
