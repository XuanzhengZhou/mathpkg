1. $[\text{rank}(u) < \alpha] \wedge [\text{rank}(u') < \alpha] \wedge [\text{rank}(v) \leq \alpha] \rightarrow [[u = u']] \cdot [[u \in v]] \leq [[u' \in v]]$.

2. $[\text{rank}(u) < \alpha] \wedge [\text{rank}(v) \leq \alpha] \wedge [\text{rank}(v') \leq \alpha] \rightarrow [[u \in v]] \cdot [[v = v']] \leq [[u \in v']]$.

3. $[\text{rank}(u) \leq \alpha] \wedge [\text{rank}(v) \leq \alpha] \wedge [\text{rank}(w) \leq \alpha] \rightarrow [[u = v]] \cdot [[v = w]] \leq [[u = w]]$.
