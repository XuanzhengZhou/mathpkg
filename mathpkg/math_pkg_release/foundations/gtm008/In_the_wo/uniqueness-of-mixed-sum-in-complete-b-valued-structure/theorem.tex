Let $\mathbf{A}$ be a complete $B$-valued structure. If $\{a_i \mid i \in I\} \subseteq A$ and $\{b_i \mid i \in I\} \subseteq B$ is a partition of unity, then the mixed sum $\sum_{i \in I} b_i a_i$ exists and is unique up to equivalence (Boolean value $1$ of equality). That is,

$$\|a = a'\| = 1 \text{ whenever } a \text{ and } a' \text{ satisfy } (\forall i \in I)[b_i \leq \|a = a_i\|].$$
