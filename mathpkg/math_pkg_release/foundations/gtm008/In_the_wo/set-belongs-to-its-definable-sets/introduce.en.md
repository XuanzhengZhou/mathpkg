Every element of $x$ is a definable subset of $x$ (using the formula $v = a$ with parameter $a$), so $x \subseteq \operatorname{Def}(x)$. This ensures that the constructible hierarchy is cumulative: $L_\alpha \subseteq L_{\alpha+1}$ for all $\alpha$. The containment $x \subseteq \operatorname{Def}(x)$ combined with $\operatorname{Def}(x) \subseteq \mathcal{P}(x)$ places the definable power set properly between the set and its full power set.
