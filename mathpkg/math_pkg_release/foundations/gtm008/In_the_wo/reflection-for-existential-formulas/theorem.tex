For any existential formula $(\exists x)\varphi(x, x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ and any ordinal $\alpha$, there exists an ordinal $\beta > \alpha$ such that

$$(\forall x_1, \ldots, x_n \in M_\alpha)[(\exists x)\varphi^M(x, x_1, \ldots, x_n) \leftrightarrow (\exists x \in M_\beta)\varphi^M(x, x_1, \ldots, x_n)].$$
