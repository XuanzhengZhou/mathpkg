Let $\mathcal{P} = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ be the partial order structure associated with the Boolean algebra $B$. Then $\langle \mathcal{F}, \mathcal{T} \rangle$ is a Hausdorff space, where $\mathcal{F}$ is the set of all ultrafilters for $\mathcal{P}$ and $\mathcal{T}$ is the topology generated by the sets $N(p) = \{F \in \mathcal{F} \mid p \in F\}$ for $p \in P$.
