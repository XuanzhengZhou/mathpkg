This corollary to the reflection principle states that for any formula $\varphi$, there are arbitrarily large ordinals $\beta$ where truth of $\varphi$ in the full model $\mathbf{M}$ coincides with truth in the substructure $\mathbf{M}_\beta$ for all parameters from $M_\beta$. This "partial absoluteness" is the key tool for proving that $\mathbf{M}$ satisfies the axioms of ZF, as the axioms are finitely many formulas for which such reflecting ordinals exist.
