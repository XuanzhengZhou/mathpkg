A class is definable in the language of set theory $\mathcal{L}_0$ if it can be characterized as the extension of a formula with one free variable. Parameters from a given set $c$ may be used in the defining formula, giving the notion of definability from $c$. This is a generalization of Gödel's notion of definability used in the construction of the constructible universe $L$, extended here to relative constructibility.
