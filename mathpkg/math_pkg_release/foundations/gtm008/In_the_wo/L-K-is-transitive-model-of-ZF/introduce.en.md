The class $L(K)$ of sets constructible relative to $K$ is a transitive model of all ZF axioms. This is the relative version of Godel's fundamental result that $L$ is a model of ZF. The proof proceeds by verifying each ZF axiom holds in $L(K)$, using the definability of the constructible hierarchy and the fact that the construction is absolute for transitive models.
