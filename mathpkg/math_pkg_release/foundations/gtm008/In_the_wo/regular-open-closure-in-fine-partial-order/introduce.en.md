In the fine partial order, closure and interior operations are defined algebraically without reference to a topology. The regular open sets (those satisfying $G = G^{-0}$) form a complete Boolean algebra. This construction is fundamental to the forcing technique: the Boolean algebra of regular open sets of a partial order structure (forcing poset) is the complete Boolean algebra used for Boolean-valued models and generic extensions.
