This is the culmination of the results on star operations: the complete Boolean algebra of regular open sets of a partial order structure $P$ is isomorphic to the complete Boolean algebra of regular open sets of its ultrafilter topological space. This theorem justifies the equivalence between the Boolean-algebraic approach to forcing (via complete Boolean algebras) and the topological approach (via regular open sets of forcing posets).
