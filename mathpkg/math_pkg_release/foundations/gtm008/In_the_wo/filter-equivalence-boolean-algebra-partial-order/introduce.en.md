This theorem establishes that the notions of proper filter in a Boolean algebra and filter in the associated partial order structure coincide. The proof uses the fact that the meet operation in the Boolean algebra serves as a common lower bound for strong compatibility, while the upward hereditary condition carries over directly from the Boolean algebra to the partial order. This equivalence provides the crucial link needed for defining topological spaces of ultrafilters.
