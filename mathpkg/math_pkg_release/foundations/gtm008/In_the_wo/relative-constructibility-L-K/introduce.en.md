Relative constructibility $L(K)$ generalizes Godel's constructible universe $L$ by starting with a class $K$ as an additional predicate. The hierarchy builds up from the transitive closure of $K$ intersected with $V_\omega$, then iteratively applies the definable power set operation $\operatorname{Def}$. When $K = \emptyset$, this reduces to the usual $L$. This construction is fundamental to the study of relative consistency results in set theory.
