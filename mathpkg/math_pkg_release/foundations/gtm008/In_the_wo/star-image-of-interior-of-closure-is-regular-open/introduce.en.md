The star operations preserve the property of being regular open. This ensures that the restriction of the star maps to the regular open algebras is well-defined. Combined with the identity property $G^{*\Delta} = G$, this establishes an isomorphism between the complete Boolean algebras of regular open sets of $P$ and $\mathcal{F}$, which is the central result of the section connecting partial order structures to topological spaces.
