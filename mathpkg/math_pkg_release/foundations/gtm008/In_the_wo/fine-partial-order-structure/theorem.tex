Let $\langle X, T \rangle$ be a topological space. The fine partial order structure $\langle P, \leq \rangle$ associated with $X$ is defined by:

$$P \triangleq T - \{0\},$$

and for $U, V \in P$,

$$U \leq V \xrightarrow{\Delta} U \subseteq V.$$

The associated space of ultrafilters $\langle \mathcal{F}, \mathcal{T} \rangle$ is the topological space where $\mathcal{F}$ is the set of all ultrafilters for $\langle P, \leq \rangle$ and $\mathcal{T}$ is generated by $\{N(U) \mid U \in P\}$ with $N(U) \triangleq \{F \in \mathcal{F} \mid U \in F\}$.
