If $\varphi(a_0, \ldots, a_n)$ is a formula of $\mathcal{L}$, then
$$(\forall \alpha)(\exists \beta \geq \alpha)(\forall a_1, \ldots, a_n \in M_\alpha)$$
$$[\mathbf{M} \models (\exists x) \varphi(x, a_1, \ldots, a_n) \leftrightarrow (\exists a \in M_\beta)[\mathbf{M} \models \varphi(a, a_1, \ldots, a_n)]].$$
