If $M$ is a transitive class and $\varphi(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ is a bounded formula (all quantifiers are of the form $\exists x \in y$ or $\forall x \in y$), then $\varphi$ is absolute for $M$:

$$(\forall x_1, \ldots, x_n \in M)[\varphi^M(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \leftrightarrow \varphi(x_1, \ldots, x_n)].$$
