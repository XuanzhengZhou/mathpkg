This corollary internalizes the cardinal preservation result of Corollary 17.5. When the complete Boolean algebra $B$ satisfies the countable chain condition, the statement that "cardinals are absolute" holds with Boolean value $1$ in $V^{(B)}$. That is, $V^{(B)}$ believes that an ordinal is a cardinal if and only if its canonical embedding is a cardinal.

**Note:** The original statement was partially truncated in the OCR source; this reconstruction is derived from the preceding remark that "cardinals are absolute if $B$ satisfies the c.c.c."
