This lemma (Lemma 8 of Section 14) generalizes the previous lemmas to an arbitrary definable operation $\mathcal{F}_8$ on sets. It states that the Boolean-valued application of $\mathcal{F}_8$ to check sets equals the check of the ground-model application, with Boolean value 1. This completes the series of lemmas that collectively demonstrate that the canonical check embedding is a Boolean-valued elementary embedding, preserving all definable set-theoretic operations.
