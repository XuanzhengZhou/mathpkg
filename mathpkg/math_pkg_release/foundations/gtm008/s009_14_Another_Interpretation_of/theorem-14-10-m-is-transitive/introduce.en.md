This theorem establishes that $M$ is transitive in $V^{(B)}$ with Boolean value 1. This is the Boolean-valued counterpart of the fact that the ground model $V$ is a transitive class. The proof uses Theorem 13.13.2 and reduces to showing $u(x) \cdot [M(u)] \leq [M(x)]$ for all $x \in \mathcal{D}(u)$.
