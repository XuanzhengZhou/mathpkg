The structures $V_{\alpha}^{(B)} = \langle V_{\alpha}^{(B)}, \overline{=}, \overline{\in}, \overline{M}_{\alpha} \rangle$ satisfy the conditions of Section 9 (p. 87-88). By Theorem 9.26, the direct limit structure $\mathbf{V} = \langle V^{(B)}, \overline{=}, \overline{\in}, \overline{M} \rangle$ is a $B$-valued model of ZF.
