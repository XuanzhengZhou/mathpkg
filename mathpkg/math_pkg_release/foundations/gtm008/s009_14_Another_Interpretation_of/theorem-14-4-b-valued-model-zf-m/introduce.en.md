This theorem extends the fundamental result that $V^{(B)}$ is a Boolean-valued model of ZF. With the new predicate $M$, the extended structure $\mathbf{V}$ remains a $B$-valued model of ZF. The proof follows from Theorem 9.26 and Theorem 14.1 which verifies the Axioms of Equality for the extended structure.
