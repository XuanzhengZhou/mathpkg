By induction on rank. For $u \in V_{\alpha+1}^{(B)}$, $[\check{k} \in u] = \sum_{x \in \mathcal{D}(u)} u(x) \cdot [\check{k} = x]$. Since $\mathcal{D}(u) \subseteq V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$ and by induction $[\check{k} = x] = 0$ when $\alpha \leq \operatorname{rank}(k)$ for $x \in V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$, the sum vanishes. For part 2, $[M(\check{k})]_{\alpha} = \sum_{k' \in R(\alpha)} [\check{k} = \check{k}']$ includes $[\check{k} = \check{k}] = 1$ since $\operatorname{rank}(k) \leq \alpha$, giving value 1.
