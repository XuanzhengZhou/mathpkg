$$[F \subseteq \check{B}] = \prod_{b \in B} \left(F(\check{b}) \Rightarrow [\check{b} \in \check{B}]\right) = 1.$$

Since $[\check{b} \in \check{B}] = 1$ for all $b \in B$, each implication has Boolean value 1, so the product equals 1.
