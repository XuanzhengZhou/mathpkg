This corollary of Theorem 13.18 and Theorem 13.17 establishes that bounded formulas (also called $\Delta_0$-formulas) are absolute between the standard universe $V$ and the Boolean-valued universe $V^{(B)}$. A bounded formula holds in $V$ if and only if its Boolean truth value under the canonical embedding is $1$. This absoluteness property is fundamental for the metamathematical analysis of forcing and Boolean-valued models.
