Let $\langle M, \mathbf{P} \rangle$ be a setting for forcing where $M$ is a standard transitive model of $ZF + AC$. If $G$ is $\mathbf{P}$-generic over $M$ and
$$(\forall S \subseteq \mathbf{P})[\bar{\bar{S}} \leq \omega \land \text{Comp}(S) \rightarrow (\exists p \in \mathbf{P})(\forall q \in S)[p \leq q]]$$
holds in $M$ (i.e., every countable compatible subset of $\mathbf{P}$ has a lower bound), then every $\omega$-sequence of ordinals in $M[G]$ is already in $M$.
