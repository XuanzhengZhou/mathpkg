This theorem establishes that the forcing relation is invariant under permutations of the countable index set $\omega$, provided both the forcing condition and the formula are transformed accordingly. It is the central technical result used to prove the independence of the Axiom of Choice, as it allows one to exploit symmetry to show that certain sets (such as a choice function on the generic sets $F_i$) cannot be forced to exist.
