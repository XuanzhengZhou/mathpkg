For every formula $\varphi$,
$$(\forall t \in T) \left( \forall p \in \prod_{i \in \omega}^{w} P_i \right) (\exists m) (\forall \pi) [\pi \in \mathbf{G}_m \rightarrow \pi(t) = t \land \pi(\varphi) = \varphi \land \pi(p) = p],$$
where $\mathbf{G}_m$ is the subgroup of permutations of $\omega$ fixing all $i \leq m$.
