This is the central result of Boolean-valued set theory: the full Boolean-valued universe $V^{(B)}$ satisfies every axiom of ZF with Boolean truth value $1$. Since the sequence $\langle V_\alpha^{(B)} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$ satisfies all conditions of Section 9's framework, the result follows directly from Theorem 9.26. This theorem provides the foundation for using Boolean-valued models to prove independence results in set theory.
