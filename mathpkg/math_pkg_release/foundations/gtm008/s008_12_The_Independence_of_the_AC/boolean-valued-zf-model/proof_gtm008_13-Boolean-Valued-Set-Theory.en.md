Since the conditions of Section 9 are satisfied by the sequence $\langle V_\alpha^{(B)} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$, Theorem 9.26 directly implies that $V^{(B)}$ is a $B$-valued model of $ZF$. The conditions verified are: (1) $V_\alpha^{(B)}$ is an increasing sequence; (2) continuity at limit ordinals (Theorem 13.2); (3) extensionality (Theorem 13.9); (4) the separation axiom schema; and (5) the collection axiom schema. Conditions 4 and 5 are verified by arguments similar to those used in Section 9.
