Deduce the following Boolean implications from the identity $\llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = \llbracket \sup(u) \Rightarrow b \rrbracket$, where $u$ is extensional:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\llbracket b \geq \sup(u) \rrbracket = 1 \to \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = 1$,
\item $\llbracket b < \sup(u) \rrbracket = 1 \to \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket < 1$,
\item $\llbracket u = 0 \rrbracket = \neg \sup(u)$,
\item $\llbracket b \cdot u = 0 \rrbracket = \neg b + \llbracket u = 0 \rrbracket$.
\end{enumerate}
