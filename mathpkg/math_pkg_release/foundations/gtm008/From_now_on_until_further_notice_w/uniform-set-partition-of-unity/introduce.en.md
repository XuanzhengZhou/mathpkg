This property characterizes how uniform (extensional with complete domain) Boolean-valued sets interact with Boolean mixtures. When elements $x_i$ of the domain are mixed with a partition of unity $\{b_i\}$, the value $u$ at the mixture equals the Boolean mixture of the individual values. This linearity property is essential for constructing elements witnessing suprema and for the factorization theorem for Boolean-valued sets.
