Let $u, v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ be definite and $\varphi: \mathcal{D}(u) \rightarrow \mathcal{D}(v)$ be an extensional function. Then

$$(\exists f \in V^{(\mathbf{B})})[\llbracket f: u \rightarrow v \rrbracket = 1 \wedge (\forall x \in \mathcal{D}(u))[\llbracket f(x) = \varphi(x) \rrbracket = 1]].$$
