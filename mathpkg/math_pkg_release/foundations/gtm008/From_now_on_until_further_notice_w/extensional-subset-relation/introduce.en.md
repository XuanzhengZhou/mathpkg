These companion theorems handle the relationship between extensional sets under the subset relation. Theorem 16.22 shows that multiplying an extensional $v$ by the truth value of $v \subseteq u$ produces a set $v'$ that is fully contained in $u$ (with value $1$). Theorem 16.23 provides the crucial technical lemma that any extensional subset $v$ of $u$ can be replaced by an equivalent extensional set $v'$ whose domain equals $\mathcal{D}(u)$. This domain unification is essential for the Boolean-valued treatment of power sets.
