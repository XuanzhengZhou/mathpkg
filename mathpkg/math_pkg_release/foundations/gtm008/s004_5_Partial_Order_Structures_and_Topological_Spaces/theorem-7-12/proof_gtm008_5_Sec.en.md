From the Axiom Schema of Replacement it follows that

$$(\exists \alpha)\left[ \alpha = \bigcup_{x \in a} \mu_\beta(x \in M_\beta) \right]$$

then $a \subseteq M_\alpha$.

Remark. In order to prove the Axiom of Separation in $M$ we first prove a kind of reflection principle in $M$. This proof requires several preliminary results.
