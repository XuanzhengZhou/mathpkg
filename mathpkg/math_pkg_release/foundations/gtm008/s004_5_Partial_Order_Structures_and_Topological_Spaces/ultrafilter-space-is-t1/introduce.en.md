Theorem 5.7 establishes that for any partial order structure $P$, the associated topological space $\langle \mathcal{F}, \mathcal{T} \rangle$ of ultrafilters is always a $T_1$-space. This means that distinct ultrafilters can be separated by neighborhoods, a basic topological property that holds without any additional assumptions on the partial order.
