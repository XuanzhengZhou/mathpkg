Theorem 7.25 asserts that $L_K$, the class of sets constructible relative to a class $K$, is a standard transitive model of ZF that contains all ordinals. The proof follows the same pattern as for Godel's $L$: the sequence $\langle A_\alpha \rangle$ satisfies the three structural conditions (monotone substructures, set-sized stages, continuity), and the reflection principle plus the fixed-point theorem imply that the union satisfies all ZF axioms.
