Theorem 7.26 states that $L_K$ is itself definable in the language with the added predicate symbol $K(\cdot)$. This means there is a single formula $\psi(x)$ that expresses "$x$ is in $L_K$". This definability is important for meta-mathematical considerations and for the subsequent applications of relative constructibility to independence proofs, as it allows one to work with $L_K$ as a definable inner model within ZF.
