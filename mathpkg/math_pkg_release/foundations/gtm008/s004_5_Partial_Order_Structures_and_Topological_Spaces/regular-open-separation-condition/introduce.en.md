Theorem 5.15 provides a criterion for when one open set is contained in the closure of another: if every regular open set disjoint from $G_1$ is also disjoint from $G_2$, then $G_2$ is contained in the closure of $G_1$. This result is used in proving that the star operations map open sets to regular open sets.
