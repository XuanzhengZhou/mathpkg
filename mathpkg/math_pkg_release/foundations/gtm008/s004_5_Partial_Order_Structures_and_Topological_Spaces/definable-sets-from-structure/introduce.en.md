$Df(\mathbf{A})$ is the collection of all subsets of $A$ that are definable (with parameters from $A$) in the structure $\mathbf{A}$. This is the key operation used to build Godel's constructible hierarchy: each new stage $L_{\alpha+1}$ consists exactly of the sets definable from the structure $\langle L_\alpha, \in \rangle$. The notation emphasizes that definability is always relative to a given structure.
