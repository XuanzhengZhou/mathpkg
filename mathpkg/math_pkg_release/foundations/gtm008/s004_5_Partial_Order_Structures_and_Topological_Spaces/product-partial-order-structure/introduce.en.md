The product of a family of partial order structures $\{P_i\}_{i \in I}$, each possessing a greatest element $1_i$, is defined as the restricted product (elements equal to the greatest element in all but finitely many coordinates) with the pointwise order. This construction generalizes the product of Boolean algebras and is used to relate the ultrafilter space of a product to the product of the individual ultrafilter spaces.
