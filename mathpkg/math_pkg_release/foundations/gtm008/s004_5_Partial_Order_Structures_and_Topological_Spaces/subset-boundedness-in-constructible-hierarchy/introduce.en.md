Theorem 7.12 states that any subset $a$ of the union $M = \bigcup_{\alpha \in On} M_\alpha$ of a cumulative hierarchy is contained in some stage $M_\alpha$. This follows from the Axiom Schema of Replacement: each element $x \in a$ first appears at some rank $\mu_\beta(x \in M_\beta)$, and the supremum of these ranks is bounded by some ordinal $\alpha$. This theorem is crucial for establishing that the limit $M$ satisfies the axioms of ZF.
