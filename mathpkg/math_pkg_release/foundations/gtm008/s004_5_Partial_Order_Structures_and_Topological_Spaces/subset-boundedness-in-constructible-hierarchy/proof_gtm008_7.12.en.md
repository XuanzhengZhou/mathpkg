From the Axiom Schema of Replacement, it follows that
$$\alpha = \bigcup_{x \in a} \mu_\beta(x \in M_\beta)$$
is an ordinal (since it is a union of ordinals indexed by the set $a$). Then by definition, for each $x \in a$, $x \in M_{\mu_\beta(x \in M_\beta)} \subseteq M_\alpha$. Hence $a \subseteq M_\alpha$.
