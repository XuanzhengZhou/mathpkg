Under the given hypothesis if

$$S' = S \cup \{q \in P \mid \neg \text{Comp}(p, q)\}$$

then $S' \in M$ and $S'$ is dense, hence $G \cap S' \neq 0$. But any two elements of $G$ are compatible, hence $G \cap S \neq 0$.
