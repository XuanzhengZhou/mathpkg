A filter for a partial order structure $P = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ is a nonempty subset $F \subseteq P$ that is both strongly compatible (any two elements have a common lower bound in $F$) and upward hereditary (if $x \in F$ and $x \leq y$, then $y \in F$). This generalizes the notion of a filter from Boolean algebras to arbitrary partial orders, playing a key role in the construction of topological spaces from order structures.
