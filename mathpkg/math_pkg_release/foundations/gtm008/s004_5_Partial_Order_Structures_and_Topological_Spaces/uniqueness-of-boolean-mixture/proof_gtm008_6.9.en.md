For each $i \in I$,
$$b_i \leq [\![a = a_i]\!] \cdot [\![a' = a_i]\!] \leq [\![a = a']\!],$$
using the transitivity axiom for equality (Definition 6.5, axiom 3). Summing over all $i \in I$:
$$1 = \sum_{i \in I} b_i \leq [\![a = a']\!] \leq 1.$$
Hence $[\![a = a']\!] = 1$.
