Theorem 7.21 establishes that the limit structure $\mathbf{M}$ built from a cumulative hierarchy satisfying the three conditions (monotone chain of substructures, sets at each stage, continuity at limits) satisfies the Axiom Schema of Separation. Using the reflection corollary (7.20), any subset $C \subseteq M$ definable by a formula is bounded below some $M_\alpha$, and thus $C = C \cap M_\alpha \in M$. Together with other axioms, this shows $\mathbf{M}$ is a model of ZF.
