This theorem defines the order topology on a partial order structure: the collection of downward closures $\{[x] \mid x \in P\}$ forms a base for a topology. The theorem characterizes interior, closure, regular-open interior, complement-interior, and density purely in terms of the order relation. This topology is the key bridge between Boolean algebras (with their natural order) and topological spaces.
