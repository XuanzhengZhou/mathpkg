**Proof.** If $a \in A$ then by Definition 1.9, $a \leq b$ and hence $ca \leq cb$. If for each $a \in A$, $ca \leq d$, then since $a = (-c + c)a = -ca + ca \leq -c + d$, it follows from Definition 1.9 that $b \leq -c + d$. Hence $cb \leq d$, and again from Definition 1.9, $\sum_{a \in A} ca = cb$.
