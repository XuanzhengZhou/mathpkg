If $\langle B, +, \cdot, ^-, 0, 1 \rangle$ is a Boolean algebra with natural order $\leq$, if $A \subseteq B$ and $b \in B$ then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $b = \sum_{a \in A} a \xleftrightarrow{\Delta} (\forall a \in A)[a \leq b] \land (\forall b' \in B)[(\forall a \in A)[a \leq b'] \rightarrow b \leq b']$.
\item $b = \prod_{a \in A} a \xleftrightarrow{\Delta} (\forall a \in A)[b \leq a] \land (\forall b' \in B)[(\forall a \in A)[b' \leq a] \rightarrow b' \leq b]$.
\end{enumerate}
