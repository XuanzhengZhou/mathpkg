The notation $[x]$ denotes the set of all elements of $P$ that are less than or equal to $x$ (the downward closure or principal ideal generated by $x$). These sets form a base for the order topology on $P$ (Theorem 1.33) and are essential in the construction linking partial orders to topological spaces and Boolean algebras.
