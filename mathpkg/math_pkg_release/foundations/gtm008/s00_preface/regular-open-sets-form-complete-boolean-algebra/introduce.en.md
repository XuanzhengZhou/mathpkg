This is a central result connecting topology and Boolean algebra: the collection of regular open subsets of any topological space, with suitably defined operations, forms a complete Boolean algebra. Addition is defined as the regular-open interior of the union, multiplication as intersection, and complementation as the interior of the set-theoretic complement. This theorem establishes the topological half of the duality between partial order structures and complete Boolean algebras.
