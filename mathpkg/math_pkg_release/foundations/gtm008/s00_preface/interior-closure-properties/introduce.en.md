This theorem establishes the fundamental properties of the interior and closure operators in any topological space. The interior is contained in the set which is contained in its closure (1). Both operators are idempotent (2) and monotone (3). The fourth property relates complementation with interior and closure: the closure of the complement equals the complement of the interior, and vice versa.
