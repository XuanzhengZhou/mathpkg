If $A \neq 0$ and $\forall a \in A$, $\langle X_a, T_a \rangle$ is a topological space, then

$$T' = \left\{ B \subseteq \prod_{a \in A} X_a \mid (\exists n \in \omega) (\exists \sigma \in A^n) (\forall i < n) \left[ [p_{\sigma(i)}(B) \in T_{\sigma(i)}] \right] \land (\forall a \in A) [a \neq \sigma(i) \rightarrow p_a(B) = X_a] \right\}$$

is a base for a topology on $\prod_{a \in A} X_a$.
