This lemma establishes that the map $\tilde{a}$ is injective on $P$-generic filters: two distinct generic filters produce distinct subsets of $\omega$. Combined with Lemmas 1 and 2, this shows $\tilde{a}$ is a bijection between the $P$-generic sets over $M$ and certain subsets of $\omega$. The proof follows immediately from the identities established in the previous lemmas.
