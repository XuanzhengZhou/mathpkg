This theorem describes the behavior of generic extensions under automorphisms of the forcing poset. Given an automorphism $\pi \in \operatorname{Aut}(P)$ in the ground model $M$, applying $\pi$ to a generic filter $G$ yields another generic filter, and the corresponding Boolean ultrahomomorphism transforms by composition with $\tilde{\pi}^{-1}$. Crucially, all resulting generic extensions coincide: $M[G] = M[\pi''G]$, showing that the automorphism does not change the forcing extension.
