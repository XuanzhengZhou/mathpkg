This corollary generalizes Theorem 11.8 from ccc to $\lambda$-cc: if $P$ has the $\lambda$-chain condition (every antichain has size $< \lambda$) and $\lambda$ is regular in $M$, then all cardinals $\geq \lambda$ are preserved. The proof uses the regularity of $\lambda$ to bound the size of unions of sets witnessing cardinal collapse, extending the ccc preservation argument.
