Let $\langle M, P \rangle$ be a setting for forcing and let $G$ be $P$-generic over $M$. Suppose $M$ satisfies

$$(\forall S \subseteq P)\left[(\forall p_1, p_2 \in S)[p_1 \neq p_2 \rightarrow \neg \operatorname{Comp}(p_1, p_2)] \rightarrow \overline{S} < \lambda\right]$$

and $\lambda$ is a regular cardinal in $M$. Then the sets of cardinals $\geq \lambda$ in $M$ and $M[G]$ respectively are the same.
