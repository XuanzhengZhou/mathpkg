For every $s \subseteq \omega$, we have $[\check{\check{s}} \subseteq \check{\omega}] = 1$, and consequently $[\check{\check{s}} \in \mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})] = 1$. Therefore

$$[\mathcal{P}(\omega)^{\vee} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})] = 1.$$
