Let $B$ be a complete Boolean algebra and let $\omega_{\alpha}$ be a cardinal with $\mathrm{cf}(\omega_{\alpha}) > \omega$ in the ground model. Then $B$ satisfies the $(\omega, \omega_{\alpha})$-weak distributive law if and only if

$$\llbracket \mathrm{cf}((\omega_{\alpha})^{\vee}) > \check{\omega} \rrbracket = 1$$

in the Boolean-valued universe $V^{(B)}$.
