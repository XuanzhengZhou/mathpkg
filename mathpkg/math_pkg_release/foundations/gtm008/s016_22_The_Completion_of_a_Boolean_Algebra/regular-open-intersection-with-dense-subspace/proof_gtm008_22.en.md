We need only show that $(A \cap S)^{-S 0 S} \subseteq A^{-0} \cap S$, for by the proof of Theorem 22.2 we know that the reverse inclusion holds. Let

$$p \in (A \cap S)^{-S 0 S},$$

then

$$(\exists N(p))[N(p) \cap S \subseteq (A \cap S)^{-} \cap S]$$

by Theorem 22.1,

$$N(p) = N(p) \cap S^{-} \subseteq (N(p) \cap S)^{-} \subseteq (A \cap S)^{-} \subseteq A^{-}.$$

Thus

$$p \in A^{-0} \cap S.$$
