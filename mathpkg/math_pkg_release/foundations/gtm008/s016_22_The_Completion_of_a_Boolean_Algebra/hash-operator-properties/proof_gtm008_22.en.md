1--4 follow from the definition of $\#$. (Note that $B_0$ and $B_1$ are complete and so is $i$.)

5. $i(b_0) \cdot b_1 \leq i(b_0 \cdot \#(b_1))$ by 1. Hence
   $$\#(i(b_0) \cdot b_1) \leq b_0 \cdot \#(b_1).$$
   Conversely, $b_0 \cdot \#(b_1) \leq \#(i(b_0) \cdot b_1)$ follows from the definition.

6. Follows from 5.
