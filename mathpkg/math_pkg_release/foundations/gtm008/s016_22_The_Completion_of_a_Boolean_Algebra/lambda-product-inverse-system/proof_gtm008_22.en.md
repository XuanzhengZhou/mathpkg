The o.c.o. inverse system property follows from the fact that each projection is open, continuous, and onto. For the limit identification when $\mathrm{cf}(\kappa) \geq \lambda$, one verifies that the natural map $X \to \varprojlim X^{\alpha}$ is bijective, open, and continuous under the $\lambda$-product topology.
