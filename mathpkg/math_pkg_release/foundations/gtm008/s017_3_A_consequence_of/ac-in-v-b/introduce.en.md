Theorem 23.23 proves that the Axiom of Choice (AC) always has Boolean value $1$ in any Boolean-valued universe $V^{(B)}$. This is a remarkable result: even without assuming AC in the meta-theory in its full generality, the Boolean-valued model always satisfies AC internally. The proof uses a direct construction in the general case, taking the Axiom of Choice in a form involving a choice function and showing its Boolean value is $1$.
