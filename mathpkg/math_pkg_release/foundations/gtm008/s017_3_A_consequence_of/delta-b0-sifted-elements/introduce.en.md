Definition 23.28 introduces $\Delta(B_0)$, the set of all elements $b$ in a Boolean algebra $B$ whose $\#$-value (with respect to a complete subalgebra $B_0$) equals $1$. Here $\#(b)$ is the projection of $b$ onto the complete subalgebra $B_0$, defined as the infimum of all $b_0 \in B_0$ with $b \leq b_0$. Elements of $\Delta(B_0)$ play a key role in the theory of sieve laws for Boolean-valued models.
