Suppose that $\tilde{B}$ is the completion of $B$ and $\tilde{B}$ satisfies the UCL. Then $V^{(B)}$ is a $\tilde{B}$-valued elementary substructure of $V^{(\tilde{B})}$, i.e., for each formula $\varphi$ of the language of $V^{(B)}$ and every $u_1, \ldots, u_n \in V^{(B)}$,
$$[\varphi(u_1, \ldots, u_n)]^{V^{(B)}} = [\varphi(u_1, \ldots, u_n)]^{V^{(\tilde{B})}}.$$
