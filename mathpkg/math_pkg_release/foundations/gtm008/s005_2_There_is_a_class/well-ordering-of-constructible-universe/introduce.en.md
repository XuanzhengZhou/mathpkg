The constructible universe $L_{K_0}$ admits a definable well-ordering constructed from the ordinal rank function $Od(a)$ and well-orderings $<_\alpha$ of each level $A_\alpha$. This well-ordering is definable within $L_{K_0}$ itself and restricts to well-order each element of $L_{K_0}$.
