Unlimited formulas extend limited formulas by allowing unranked quantification over all terms. The ordinal measure $\text{Ord}(\varphi)$ provides a well-ordering for induction proofs, combining the grade $\text{Ord}^1$, a secondary measure $\text{Ord}^2$, and the length $\text{Ord}^3$.
