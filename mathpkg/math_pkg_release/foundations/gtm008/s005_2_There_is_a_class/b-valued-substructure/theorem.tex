$M$ is a $B$-valued substructure of $M'$ iff
\begin{enumerate}
\item $M \subseteq M'$,
\item For each $n$-ary predicate symbol $R$ of $L$, including $=$ and $\in$,
  $$(\forall a_1, \ldots, a_n \in M) [\llbracket R(a_1, \ldots, a_n) \rrbracket_M = \llbracket R(a_1, \ldots, a_n) \rrbracket_{M'}]$$
\item $c^M = c^{M'}$ for each individual constant $c$ of $L$.
\end{enumerate}
