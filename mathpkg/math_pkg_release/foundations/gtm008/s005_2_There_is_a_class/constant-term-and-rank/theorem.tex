A constant term is either an individual constant or an abstraction term. We define the rank $\rho$ of a constant term:
$$\rho(k) \triangleq \text{rank}(k) \quad k \in K.$$
$$\rho(\hat{x}^\alpha \varphi(x^\alpha)) \triangleq \alpha.$$
$$T_\alpha \triangleq \{t \mid t \text{ is a constant term and } \rho(t) < \alpha\}.$$
$$T \triangleq \bigcup_{\alpha \in \text{On}} T_\alpha.$$
