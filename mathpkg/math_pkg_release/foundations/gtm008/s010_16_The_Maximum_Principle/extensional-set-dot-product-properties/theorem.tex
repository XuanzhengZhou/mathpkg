Let $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ be extensional and $b \in B$. Then

1. $\llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = \llbracket \sup(u) \Rightarrow b \rrbracket$.

As consequences:

$$\llbracket b \geq \sup(u) \rrbracket \rightarrow \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = 1,$$
$$\llbracket b < \sup(u) \rrbracket \rightarrow \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket < 1,$$
$$\llbracket u = 0 \rrbracket = \neg \sup(u),$$
$$\llbracket b \cdot u \subseteq u \rrbracket = 1.$$
