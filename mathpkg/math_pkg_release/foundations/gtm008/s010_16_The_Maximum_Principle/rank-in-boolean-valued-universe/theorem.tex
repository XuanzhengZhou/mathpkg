For $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$:

1. $\llbracket u \in R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = \sum_{v \in V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})}} \llbracket u = v \rrbracket = \llbracket u \in V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})} \times \{1\} \rrbracket$.

2. If $\mathcal{D}(u) \subseteq V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})}$, then $\llbracket u \subseteq R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = 1$.

3. $\llbracket u \subseteq R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = \sum_{t \in A_{\alpha}} \llbracket u = t \rrbracket$, where $A_{\alpha} = \{t \in V^{(\mathbf{B})} \mid \mathcal{D}(t) = \mathcal{D}(u) \land \llbracket t \subseteq R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = 1\}$.
