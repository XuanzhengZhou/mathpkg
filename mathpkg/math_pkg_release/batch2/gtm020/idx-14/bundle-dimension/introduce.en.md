Exercise 3 from Chapter 3, Section 9 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. This exercise defines the bundle dimension $\dim_b B$ of a topological space $B$ as the threshold dimension beyond which all vector bundles of a given rank over $B$ are classified by maps into a finite Grassmannian rather than the infinite one. It refines the homotopy classification theorem by measuring how large a classifying space is actually needed.
