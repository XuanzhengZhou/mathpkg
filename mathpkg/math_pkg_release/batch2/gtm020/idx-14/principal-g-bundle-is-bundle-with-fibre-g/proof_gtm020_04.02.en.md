For $x \in p^{-1}(b)$ we define a bijective map $u: G \rightarrow p^{-1}(b)$ by the relation $u(s) = xs$. The inverse function of $u$ is $x' \mapsto \tau(x, x')$, which is continuous, and $u$ is a homeomorphism.
