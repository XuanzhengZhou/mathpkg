Proposition 2.6 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. This result establishes that every principal $G$-bundle is locally trivial with fibre $G$, since the translation function provides a natural homeomorphism between each fibre $p^{-1}(b)$ and the group $G$ via $s \mapsto xs$.
