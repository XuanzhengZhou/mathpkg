Definition 2.1 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. A free $G$-space is a $G$-space where no non-identity group element fixes any point. For free $G$-spaces, the translation function $\tau: X^* \to G$ is defined by the condition $x\tau(x, x') = x'$, satisfying cocycle-like properties: $\tau(x,x)=1$, multiplicativity $\tau(x,x')\tau(x',x'')=\tau(x,x'')$, and antisymmetry $\tau(x',x)=\tau(x,x')^{-1}$.
