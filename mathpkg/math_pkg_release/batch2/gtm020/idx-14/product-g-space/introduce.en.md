Example 2.3 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. The product $G$-space $B \times G$ is the trivial example of a principal $G$-space, where $G$ acts by right translation on the second factor. This construction is fundamental: the associated bundle to the product $G$-space is the trivial bundle $B \times F$ for any left $G$-space $F$.
