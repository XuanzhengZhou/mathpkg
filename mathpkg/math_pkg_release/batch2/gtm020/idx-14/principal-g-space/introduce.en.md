Definition 2.2 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. A principal $G$-space is a free $G$-space whose translation function is continuous. A principal $G$-bundle is a $G$-bundle whose total space is a principal $G$-space. This is the central definition of the chapter: principal bundles serve as the organizing structure for all fibre bundles with structure group $G$.
