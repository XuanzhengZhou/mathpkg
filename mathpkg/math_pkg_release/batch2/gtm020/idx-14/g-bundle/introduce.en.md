Definition 1.6 from Chapter 4 of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. A $G$-bundle is a bundle whose total space admits a $G$-space structure such that the bundle is isomorphic to the natural projection $\alpha(X): X \to X \bmod G$, with the base space homeomorphic to the orbit space $X \bmod G$. This definition connects the abstract notion of group actions to the concrete structure of bundles.
