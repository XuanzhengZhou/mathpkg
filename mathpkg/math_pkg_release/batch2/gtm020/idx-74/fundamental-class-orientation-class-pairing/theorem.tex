Let $M^n$ be a closed connected manifold with tangent bundle $\tau$, fundamental class $U_M \in H^n(M \times M)$, and orientation class $\omega_M \in H_n(M)$ with dual $\bar{\omega}_M \in H^n(M)$. Then the following relation holds:
$$\langle U_M, 1 \times \omega_M \rangle = 1,$$
where $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle$ denotes the natural substitution pairing $H^n(M \times M) \otimes H_n(M \times M) \to R$.
