This proposition states that the fundamental class $U_M \in H^n(M \times M)$ pairs with the cross product $1 \times \omega_M$ to give the value $1$ in the coefficient ring $R$. Here $\omega_M \in H_n(M)$ is the orientation class of the closed connected manifold $M^n$, and $\bar{\omega}_M \in H^n(M)$ its dual. The result follows from the relation $\langle \bar{\omega}_M, \omega_M \rangle = 1$ and the compatibility of the Thom class $U$ with the exponential map, linking the local orientation at a point to the global fundamental class $U_M$.
