This notation introduces the set $[\text{Vect}_F, H]_\mu$ of characteristic classes from the category of vector bundles $\text{Vect}_F$ to a ring-valued cofunctor $H$ that preserve certain algebraic operations. The subscript $\mu$ specifies the preserved structure: $(+,+)$ for additive classes (direct sum maps to sum), $(+,\times)$ for multiplicative classes (direct sum maps to product), and $r$ for semiring morphisms. This formalism unifies the study of Chern classes, Stiefel-Whitney classes, and other characteristic classes through their algebraic behavior.
