For a principal $\mathbb{Z}_2$-bundle $E \to B$ and a coefficient ring $R$ in which 2 is invertible, the induced map in cohomology $q^*$ is injective and its image consists precisely of the cohomology classes on $E$ invariant under the involution $\tau$ induced by the $\mathbb{Z}_2$-action. This result is fundamental for studying real characteristic classes with 2-divisible coefficients, as it allows one to recover the cohomology of the base from the invariant part of the cohomology of the total space.
