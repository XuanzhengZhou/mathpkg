The canonical map from the category of vector bundles to $K$-theory induces an injective map on the sets of characteristic classes preserving a given algebraic structure. Thus any characteristic class defined on $K_F$ yields one on $\text{Vect}_F$ by restriction. Moreover, the sets $[\text{Vect}_F, H]_{+,+}$ and $[\text{Vect}_F, H]_{+,\times}$ carry natural semigroup structures via pointwise addition and multiplication respectively, making them algebraic objects in their own right.
