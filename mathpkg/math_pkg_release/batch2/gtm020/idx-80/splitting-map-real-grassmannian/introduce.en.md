This is the real analogue of the complex splitting map proposition, working modulo 2. The map $h_n$ from the $n$-fold product of real projective spaces $RP^\infty$ to the real Grassmannian $G_n(R^\infty)$ classifies the product of canonical line bundles and serves as a splitting map for the universal real $n$-plane bundle $\gamma_n$ with respect to $\mathbb{Z}_2$ cohomology. This result underpins the description of the mod 2 cohomology ring of $BO(n)$ in terms of Stiefel-Whitney classes.
