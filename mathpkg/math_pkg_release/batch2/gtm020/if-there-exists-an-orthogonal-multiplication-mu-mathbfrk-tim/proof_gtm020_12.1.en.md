2.3 Remarks. The existence of vector fields is closely related to the existence of orthogonal multiplications. Observe that $v_i(-x) = -v_i(x)$ in Theorem (2.2), and therefore $v_1(x), \ldots, v_{k-1}(x)$ define vector fields on $RP^{n-1}$ which are orthonormal. Scalar multiplication $\mathbf{C} \times \mathbf{C}^n \rightarrow \mathbf{C}^n$ defines an orthogonal multiplication $\mathbf{R}^2 \times \mathbf{R}^{2n} \rightarrow \mathbf{R}^{2n}$.
