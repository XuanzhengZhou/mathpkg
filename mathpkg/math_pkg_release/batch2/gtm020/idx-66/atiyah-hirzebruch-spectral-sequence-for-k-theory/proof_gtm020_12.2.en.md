The proof of the Atiyah-Hirzebruch spectral sequence is not included in this section of the text. The statement is given without proof; full details can be found in Atiyah and Hirzebruch [2]. The spectral sequence is constructed by filtering $X$ by its CW-skeleta and applying the generalized cohomology theory $KO^*$, yielding an exact couple whose associated spectral sequence has the stated $E_2$-term via the identification of the cohomology of the skeleta filtration with ordinary singular cohomology.
