To simplify notation, the class $\tilde{\alpha}^*_{\xi}(\Delta^+_{8m})$ in $KO(S(\xi))$ is abbreviated as $\Delta^+_{8m}$. With this notation, the action of the Adams operation $\psi^k$ on the generator $\Delta^+_{8m}$ defines the characteristic classes $\theta_k(\xi)$ via the formula $\psi^k \Delta^+_{8m} = \theta_k(\xi) \Delta^+_{8m} + b$, where $b$ belongs to the submodule generated by the unit $1$. Computing these $\theta_k(\xi)$ classes is the central goal of Chapter 13.
