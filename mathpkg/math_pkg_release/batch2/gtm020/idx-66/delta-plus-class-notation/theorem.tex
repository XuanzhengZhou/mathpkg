We denote $\tilde{\alpha}^*_{\xi}(\Delta^+_{8m})$ simply by $\Delta^+_{8m}$ in $KO(S(\xi))$. From (13.2), the Adams operation $\psi^k$ acts by

$$
\psi^k \Delta^+_{8m} = \theta_k(\xi) \Delta^+_{8m} + b
$$

where $b$ lies in the submodule generated by $1$. The classes $\theta_k(\xi)$ are the characteristic classes of interest.
