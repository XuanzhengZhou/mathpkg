Let $\zeta$ be a line bundle with $(\zeta - 1)^2 = -2(\zeta - 1)$. Then for $k$ odd with $r = (k-1)/2$:

$$
\theta_k(2n\zeta) = [(2r + 1) + r(\zeta - 1)]^n
$$

$$
= (2r + 1)^n + a(\zeta - 1)
$$

Substituting $\zeta - 1 = -2$ yields the relation $1^n = (2r + 1)^n - 2a$, so $2a = (2r+1)^n - 1$. With $2r + 1 = k$, we obtain:

$$
\theta_k(2n\zeta) = k^n + \frac{k^n - 1}{2}(\zeta - 1)
$$
