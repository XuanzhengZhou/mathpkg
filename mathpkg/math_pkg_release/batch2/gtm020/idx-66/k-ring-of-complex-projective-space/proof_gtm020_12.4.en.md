The proof uses the multiplicative structure of $K(CP^n)$ via the ring morphism $ch: K(CP^n) \to H^{\mathrm{ev}}(CP^n, \mathbb{Q})$. The Atiyah-Hirzebruch spectral sequence for $\tilde{K}(CP^n)$ collapses, and one shows that the additive structure is $\mathbb{Z}[v]/(v^{n-1})$. The relation $v^{n-1} = 0$ follows from the fact that $\xi_n(\mathbb{C})$ has rank $1$ and applying the dimension of the underlying vector bundle. A full treatment is given in Chapter 20 of the book.
