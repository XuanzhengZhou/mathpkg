The proof proceeds via the following steps. There is a natural map $q: RP^{2n+1} \to CP^n$ and $q^*(\xi_n(\mathbb{C}))$ is the complexification of $\xi_{2n+1}(\mathbb{R})$, where $\xi_k(F)$ denotes the canonical line bundle over $FP^k$. The Atiyah-Hirzebruch spectral sequence for $\tilde{K}(RP^{2n+1})$ collapses. Using the exact sequence

$$
\mathbb{Z} \leftarrow \tilde{K}(RP^{2n}) \leftarrow \tilde{K}(RP^{2n+1}) \leftarrow 0
$$

and the collapse of the spectral sequence, one computes $\tilde{K}(RP^n)$ as stated. The generator $w$ is the image of $v$, the generator of $K(CP^n)$, under the induced map.
