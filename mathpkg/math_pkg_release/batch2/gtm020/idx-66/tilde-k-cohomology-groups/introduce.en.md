The generalized $K$-cohomology groups $\tilde{K}^{-p}(X)$ and $\widetilde{KO}^{-p}(X)$ are defined for $p \geq 0$ by taking the reduced $K$-theory of the $p$-fold suspension $S^p X$. These cofunctors form a generalized cohomology theory on finite CW-complexes satisfying all Eilenberg--Steenrod axioms except the dimension axiom. By the Bott periodicity theorems, the definition extends to all integers $p$, yielding the full $\mathbb{Z}$-graded generalized cohomology theories $K^*$ and $KO^*$.
