As a first explicit computation, the $\theta_k$ class of the $2$-plane bundle $2\zeta$ (the Whitney sum of a line bundle $\zeta$ with itself) is evaluated. Under the assumption $\zeta^2 = 1$ (i.e., $\zeta$ is an order-$2$ line bundle), the Adams operation expansion simplifies to $\theta_k(2\zeta) = (2r+1) + r(\zeta-1)$ where $r = (k-1)/2$. This computation is the prototype for determining $\theta_k$ on higher multiples of line bundles.
