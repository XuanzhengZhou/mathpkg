Let $\xi$ be a $\operatorname{Spin}(8m+1)$ real vector bundle over $B(\xi)$. Let $\alpha^*_{\xi}$ denote the induced bundle of the associated principal $\operatorname{Spin}(8m+1)$-bundle $\alpha_{\xi}$ on the sphere bundle $S(\xi)$. Then $\alpha^*_{\xi}$ is a $\operatorname{Spin}(8m)$ principal bundle, and $KO(S(\xi))$ is a free $KO(B(\xi))$-module with two generators $1$ and $\tilde{\alpha}^*_{\xi}(\Delta^+_{8m})$. Moreover, the $KO(B(\xi))$-module $KO(S(\xi))$ has an involution with

$$
\tilde{\alpha}^*_{\xi}(\Delta^+_{8m}) \mapsto \tilde{\alpha}^*_{\xi}(\Delta^-_{8m}).
$$
