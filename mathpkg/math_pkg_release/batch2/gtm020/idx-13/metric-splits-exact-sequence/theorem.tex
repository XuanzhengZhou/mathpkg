Let
$$0 \rightarrow \xi \xrightarrow{u} \eta \xrightarrow{v} \zeta \rightarrow 0$$
be a short exact sequence of vector bundles over $B$, where $u$ is a $B$-monomorphism, $\operatorname{im} u = \ker v$, and $v$ is a $B$-epimorphism. Let $\beta$ be a riemannian or hermitian metric on $\eta$. Then there exists a $B$-morphism $w: \xi \oplus \zeta \rightarrow \eta$ splitting the exact sequence, i.e., making the following diagram commute:
\[
\begin{CD}
\xi @>i>> \xi \oplus \zeta @>j>> \zeta \\
@| @VwVV @| \\
\xi @>u>> \eta @>v>> \zeta
\end{CD}
\]
where $i$ is the inclusion into the first factor and $j$ is the projection onto the second factor.
