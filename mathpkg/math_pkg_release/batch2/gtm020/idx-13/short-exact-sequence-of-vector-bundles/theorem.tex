A short exact sequence of vector bundles is an exact sequence of the form
$$0 \rightarrow \xi \xrightarrow{u} \eta \xrightarrow{v} \zeta \rightarrow 0.$$
In this case, $\xi$ and $\ker v$ are isomorphic, and $\zeta$ and $\operatorname{coker} v$ are isomorphic. The bundle $0$ is $(B, 1, B)$, the trivial zero-dimensional bundle over $B$.
