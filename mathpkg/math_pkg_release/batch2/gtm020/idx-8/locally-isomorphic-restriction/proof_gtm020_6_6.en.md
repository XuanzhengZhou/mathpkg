By Corollary (5.8), we have $$f^*(\xi|U) \cong f^*(\xi)|f^{-1}(U)$$ for each open set $$U \subset B$$. If $$\xi|U$$ and $$\eta|U$$ are $$U$$-isomorphic, then $$f^*(\xi)|f^{-1}(U)$$ and $$f^*(\eta)|f^{-1}(U)$$ are $$f^{-1}(U)$$-isomorphic.
