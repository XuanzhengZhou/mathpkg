We iterate (5.7) and use
$$
\mathbf{H}(2) \otimes \mathbf{H}(2) = \mathbf{H} \otimes \mathbf{H} \otimes \mathbf{R}(2) \otimes \mathbf{R}(2) \cong \mathbf{R}(4) \otimes \mathbf{R}(4) \cong \mathbf{R}(16).
$$
Applying the periodicity relation (5.7) repeatedly for eight steps yields the isomorphism $C_{k+8} \cong C_k \otimes \mathbf{R}(16)$, and similarly for $C'_{k+8}$.
