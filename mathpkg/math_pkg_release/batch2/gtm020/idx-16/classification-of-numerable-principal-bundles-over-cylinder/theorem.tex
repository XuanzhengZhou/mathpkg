Let $\xi$ be a numerable principal $G$-bundle over $B \times I$. Then the bundles $\xi$, $(\xi|(B \times 1)) \times I$, and $(\xi|(B \times 0)) \times I$ are $G$-isomorphic. If $\varepsilon_i: B \rightarrow B \times I$ is the map $\varepsilon_i(b) = (b, i)$ for $i = 0, 1$, then $\varepsilon_0^*(\xi)$ and $\varepsilon_1^*(\xi)$ are $B$-isomorphic bundles.
