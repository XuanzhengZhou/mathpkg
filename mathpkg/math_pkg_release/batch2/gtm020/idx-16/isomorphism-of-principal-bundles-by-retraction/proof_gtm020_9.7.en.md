This follows directly from the construction given in (9.6). The retraction map $r$ and the associated construction produce an isomorphism between the principal $G$-bundles $\xi$ and $r^*(\xi)$ over $B \times I$.

Specifically, the construction in (9.6) provides a locally finite partition of unity $\{u_k\}$ on $B$ and a homeomorphism $\phi: (X \times W) \bmod G \to (X \bmod G) \times W$ for $W = [0, 1] = I$, which together yield the required bundle isomorphism.
