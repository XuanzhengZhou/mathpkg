This corollary extends the relation $(\gamma - 1)^2 = 0$ in $K(S^2)$ to arbitrary powers. Using the binomial expansion and the fact that higher powers of $\gamma - 1$ vanish, one obtains $\gamma^m = (1 + (\gamma - 1))^m = 1 + m(\gamma - 1)$ for any integer $m$. This formula is fundamental for computations in the $K$-theory of the $2$-sphere.
