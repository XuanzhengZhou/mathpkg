This theorem follows immediately from Theorems (6.5) and (1.2), which establish the bijection between characteristic classes of real vector bundles with values in a $2$-divisible ring and power series in the appropriate coefficient ring. The unoriented case reduces to polynomials in Pontrjagin classes, while the oriented case additionally involves the Euler class for even-dimensional bundles.
