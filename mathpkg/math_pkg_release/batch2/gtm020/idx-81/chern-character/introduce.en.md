The Chern character is the additive characteristic class of complex vector bundles with rational coefficients that corresponds to the exponential series $e^t$ under the bijection $a$. It is a ring homomorphism from the semiring of vector bundles to even-dimensional rational cohomology. The Chern character is stable and can be viewed as a morphism $\mathrm{ch} \colon K(-) \to H^{\mathrm{ev}}(-, \mathbf{Q})$ on $K$-theory.
