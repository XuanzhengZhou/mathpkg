Under the bijection
$$a \colon [\mathrm{Vect}(-), H^{\mathrm{ev}}(-, \mathbf{Q})]_{\text{ring}} \xrightarrow{\cong} \{0, e^{at}\} \subset \mathbf{Q}[[t]]_{+},$$
the Chern character $\mathrm{ch} \colon \mathrm{Vect}(-) \to H^{\mathrm{ev}}(-, \mathbf{Q})$ is defined to be the morphism corresponding to $e^t$.
