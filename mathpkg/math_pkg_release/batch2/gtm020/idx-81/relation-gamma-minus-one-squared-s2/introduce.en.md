This corollary states that in the $K$-theory of the $2$-sphere, the element $\gamma - 1$ squares to zero, where $\gamma$ is the canonical line bundle. This follows immediately from the fact that the Chern character is a ring isomorphism on $S^2$ and $c_1(\gamma)^2 = 0$ in the cohomology of $S^2$.
