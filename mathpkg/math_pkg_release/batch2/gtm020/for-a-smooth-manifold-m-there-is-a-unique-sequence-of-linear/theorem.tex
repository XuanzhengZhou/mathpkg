(1) $d(\omega \omega') = (d\omega)\omega' + (-1)^q\omega(d\omega')$ where $\omega \in A^q(M), \omega \in A^{q'}(M)$,

(2) the composite $dd = 0$, and

(3) $d: A^0(M) \rightarrow A^1(M)$ is given by $df(\xi) = \xi(f)$ for any $\xi \in \text{Vect}(M)$, the Lie algebra of vector fields on $M$.

The proof comes from the defining formula and the local version of condition (3), namely, that $df = \sum_{1 \leq i \leq n} \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_i}$.
