Let $H$ be a closed subgroup of $G$, and let $\xi = (X, p, B)$ be a principal $G$-bundle. Then a restriction of $\xi$ to a principal $H$-bundle exists if and only if the associated bundle $\xi[G \bmod H]$ admits a cross section. The restrictions are in bijective correspondence with cross sections of $\xi[G \bmod H]$.
