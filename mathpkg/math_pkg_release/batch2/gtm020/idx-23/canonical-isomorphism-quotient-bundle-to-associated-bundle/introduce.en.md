Given a principal $G$-bundle $\xi = (X, p, B)$ and a closed subgroup $H \subset G$, the quotient of the total space $X$ by the $H$-action yields a new bundle $\xi \bmod H$ over $B$. This theorem establishes a canonical $B$-isomorphism between this quotient bundle and the associated bundle $\xi[G \bmod H]$ whose fibre is the homogeneous space $G/H$ of right cosets. The isomorphism is constructed by mapping each $H$-orbit $xH$ in $X \bmod H$ to the $G$-orbit of $(x, eH)$ in the associated bundle, providing a fundamental link between quotient constructions and associated bundles.
