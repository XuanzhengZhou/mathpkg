This theorem corresponds to (2.8), and corresponding to (2.10) is the next remark.

9.8 Remark. For a subcomplex $A$ of $X$ there is a coboundary morphism $U(S(A)) \rightarrow U(X/A)$ such that the following sequence is exact.

$$U(A) \leftarrow U(X) \leftarrow U(X/A) \leftarrow U(S(A)) \leftarrow U(S(X))$$

We leave it to the reader to state and verify (3.2) to (3.4) for half-exact cofunctors.
