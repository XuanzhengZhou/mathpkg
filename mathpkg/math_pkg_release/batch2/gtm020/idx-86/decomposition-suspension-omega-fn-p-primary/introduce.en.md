This proposition states that after localization at a prime $p$, the suspension of the loop space $\Omega F_n$ decomposes as a wedge sum $S\Omega F_n \simeq ST_n \vee \bigvee_i S^i$, where $T_n$ is the mapping cone of $\tau_n$ and the remaining spheres are all in dimensions greater than $2np-2$. The homology of $S\Omega F_n$ determines the dimensions of the spheres in the decomposition: they appear in the form $(2np-2)i+1$ for $i \geq 2$ and $(2np-2)i+(2n-1)+1$ for $i \geq 1$.
