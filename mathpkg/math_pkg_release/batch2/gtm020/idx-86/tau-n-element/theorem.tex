Let $\tau_n \in \pi_{2np-3}(S^{2n-1})_{(p)}$ denote the image under $\partial$ of a generator of
$$\pi_{2np-2}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}, S^{2n-1})_{(p)} = \mathbb{Z}/p.$$
This element is nonzero exactly when $n > 1$, and since the image of $\tau_n$ in $\pi_{2np-3}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1})$ is zero, the double suspension $S^2\tau_n$ is null homotopic.
