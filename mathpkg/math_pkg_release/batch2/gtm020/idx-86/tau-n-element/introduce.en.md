The element $\tau_n$ lives in the $p$-localized homotopy group $\pi_{2np-3}(S^{2n-1})_{(p)}$ and arises as the boundary image of a generator of the relative homotopy group $\pi_{2np-2}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}, S^{2n-1})_{(p)} \cong \mathbb{Z}/p$. It is a key object in the Husemoller--Moore analysis of the double suspension sequences for odd spheres. When $n > 1$, the element is nontrivial yet its double suspension vanishes, making it a crucial source of torsion phenomena in the homotopy theory of spheres.
