This proposition characterizes the endomorphism $u_p$ of the loop space $\Omega S^{2np-1}$ localized at an odd prime $p$: it induces multiplication by $p$ in homology and factors as the composition of the double suspension $E^2$ with the boundary map $\Delta$ from the relevant fibre sequence. The result is crucial for understanding the homological algebra of the double suspension, as it links the boundary map in the fibre sequence of $\Omega F_n$ with the degree $p$ map on a looped sphere.
