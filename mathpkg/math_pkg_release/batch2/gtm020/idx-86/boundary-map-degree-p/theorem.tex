The map $u_p \colon \Omega S^{2np-1} \to \Omega S^{2np-1}$ localized at an odd prime $p$ is of degree $p$ (i.e., induces multiplication by $p$ on $H_{2np-2}(-; \mathbb{Z}_{(p)})$), and the composition satisfies
$$\Delta \circ E^2 = u_p,$$
where $\Delta \colon \Omega^3 S^{2np+1} \to \Omega S^{2np-1}$ is the boundary map in the fibre sequence of (7.1) and $E^2 \colon \Omega S^{2np-1} \to \Omega^3 S^{2np+1}$ is the double suspension map.
