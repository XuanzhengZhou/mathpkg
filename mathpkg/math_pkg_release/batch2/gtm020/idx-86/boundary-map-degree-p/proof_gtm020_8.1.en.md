The second statement $\Delta \circ E^2 = u_p$ is immediate from the commutative diagram of fibre sequences given in the text.

For the first statement, recall from (2.4) that
$$H_{2np-2}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}; \mathbb{Z}_{(p)}) = \mathbb{Z}/p.$$
Consider the exact sequence of homology groups over $\mathbb{Z}_{(p)}$:
$$\mathbb{Z}_{(p)} = H_{2np-2}(\Omega^3 S^{2np+1}) \to \mathbb{Z}_{(p)} = H_{2np-2}(\Omega F_n) \to H_{2np-2}(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}) = \mathbb{Z}/p.$$

From this exact sequence we see that the map
$$H_{2np-2}(\Omega^3 S^{2np+1}) = \mathbb{Z}_{(p)} \to \mathbb{Z}_{(p)} = H_{2np-2}(\Omega F_n)$$
is multiplication by $p$ up to a unit in $\mathbb{Z}_{(p)}$. Since the double suspension $E^2$ and the map $g_n$ (adjoint to the projection $g_n' \colon S\Omega F_n \to S^{2np-1}$) induce isomorphisms on $H_{2np-2}$ over $\mathbb{Z}_{(p)}$, it follows that $H_{2np-2}(u_p)$ is multiplication by $p$. Hence $u_p$ is of degree $p$ in the $p$-local homotopy category.
