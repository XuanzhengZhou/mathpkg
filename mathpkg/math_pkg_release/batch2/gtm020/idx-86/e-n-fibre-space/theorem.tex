Let $E_n$ denote the homotopy fibre of the map $S^{2n-1} \to \Omega^2 S^{2n+1}$, yielding the fibre sequence
$$\Omega^3 S^{2n+1} \longrightarrow E_n \longrightarrow S^{2n-1} \longrightarrow \Omega^2 S^{2n+1}.$$
The space $E_n$ fits into a commutative diagram of fibre sequences together with the sequences
$$\Omega^3 S^{2np+1} \to \Omega F_n \to \Omega^2 S^{2n+1} \to \Omega^2 S^{2np+1}$$
and
$$\Omega^2 S^{2np-1} \to S^{2n-1} \to \Omega F_n \to S^{2np-1},$$
where the map $\Omega^2 S^{2np-1} \to E_n$ exists because the composite $\Omega^2 S^{2np-1} \to S^{2n-1} \to \Omega^2 S^{2n+1}$ is null homotopic.
