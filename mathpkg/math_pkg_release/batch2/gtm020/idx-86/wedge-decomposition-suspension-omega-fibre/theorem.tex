Localized at an odd prime $p$, the space $S\Omega F_n$ has the homotopy type of a wedge
$$S\Omega F_n \simeq ST_n \vee \bigvee_{i} S^{d_i},$$
where each sphere $S^{d_i}$ appears with dimension $d_i > 2np-2$ and all spaces are localized at $p$. The dimensions of the spheres in this wedge are of the form $(2np-2)i + 1$ for $i \ge 2$ and $(2np-2)i + (2n-1) + 1$ for $i \ge 1$.
