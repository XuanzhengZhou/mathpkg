The space $T_n$ is the mapping cone of the element $\tau_n \in \pi_{2np-3}(S^{2n-1})_{(p)}$, a construction that attaches a $(2np-2)$-cell to the sphere $S^{2n-1}$ via the attaching map $\tau_n$. Its double suspension splits as a wedge $S^{2n+1} \vee S^{2np-1}$, which reflects the fact that $\tau_n$ becomes null-homotopic after two suspensions. The space $T_n$ serves as a crucial building block in the wedge decomposition of $S\Omega F_n$ localized at an odd prime $p$.
