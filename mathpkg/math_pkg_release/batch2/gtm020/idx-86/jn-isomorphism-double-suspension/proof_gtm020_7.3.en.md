Since $\partial \colon \pi_{2np-2}(T_n, S^{2n-1})_{(p)} \to \pi_{2np-3}(S^{2n-1})_{(p)}$ is a monomorphism, and the groups involved in the Hurewicz homomorphisms are cyclic of order $p$, the horizontal maps $w$ and $v$ in the diagram are monomorphisms of cyclic groups of order $p$ and hence are isomorphisms. Therefore, the entire diagram consists of isomorphisms. In particular, the map $j_{n*} \colon \pi_{2np-2}(T_n, S^{2n-1})_{(p)} \to \pi_{2np-2}(\Omega F_n, S^{2n-1})_{(p)}$ is an isomorphism.
