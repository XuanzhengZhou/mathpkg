The space $T_n$ is the mapping cone of the $p$-primary homotopy element $\tau_n \in \pi_{2np-3}(S^{2n-1})_{(p)}$, defined as $T_n = S^{2n-1} \cup_{\tau_n} e^{2np-2}$. It serves as a building block in the James-type decomposition of $\Omega F_n$ after localization at $p$. Its double suspension splits as $S^2 T_n = S^{2n+1} \vee S^{2np-1}$, reflecting the null homotopy of the double suspension of $\tau_n$.
