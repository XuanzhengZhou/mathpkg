The Chern classes are characteristic classes for complex vector bundles, taking values in integral cohomology. They are uniquely determined by the axiomatic properties (C$_0$) through (C$_3$), which encode naturality, the Whitney sum formula, and a normalization condition on the canonical line bundle over complex projective space. Together with Stiefel-Whitney classes for real vector bundles, Chern classes form the foundation of the theory of characteristic classes in algebraic topology.
