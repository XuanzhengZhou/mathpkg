Let $\xi$ and $\eta$ be vector bundles over $B$ which are either both real or both complex. If $\xi$ and $\eta$ are $s$-equivalent (stably equivalent), i.e., there exist trivial bundles $\theta^n$ and $\theta^m$ such that
$$\xi \oplus \theta^n \cong \eta \oplus \theta^m,$$
then in the real case
$$w(\xi) = w(\eta)$$
and in the complex case
$$c(\xi) = c(\eta).$$
Consequently, characteristic classes cannot distinguish between $s$-equivalent bundles.
