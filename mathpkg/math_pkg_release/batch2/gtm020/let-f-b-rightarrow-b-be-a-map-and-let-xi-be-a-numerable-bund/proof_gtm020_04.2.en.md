Suppose that $\xi|U_i$ is trivial, where $\{U_i\}_{i \in S}$ is an open covering of $B$ and $\{u_i\}_{i \in S}$ is a locally finite partition of unity with the closure of $u_i^{-1}(0, 1) \subset U_i$ for each $i \in S$. Then $f^*(\xi)|f^{-1}(U_i)$ is trivial, and $\{u_i f_i\}_{i \in S}$ is a locally finite partition of unity with the closure of $(u_i f_i)^{-1}(0, 1) \subset f^{-1}(U_i)$ for each $i \in S$.

The results and the arguments proceed as in Sec. 4 of Chap. 3. Where the proof in this more general situation is completely parallel to that given in Chap. 3, we omit the argument and leave it as an exercise for the reader.
