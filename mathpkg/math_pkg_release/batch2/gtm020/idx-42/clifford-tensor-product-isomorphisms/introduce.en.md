This proposition establishes isomorphisms between tensor products of low-dimensional real Clifford algebras and certain algebras over the real numbers. For $k=1,2$, it identifies $C \otimes C_k$ with $C \otimes C'_k$. The third case constructs an explicit algebra isomorphism $w$ from the tensor product of quaternion algebras $\mathbf{H} \otimes \mathbf{H}$ to the full matrix algebra $\mathbf{R}(4)$, defined by the action $w(x_1 \otimes x_2)x = x_1 x \bar{x}_2$. The fact that $w$ is surjective follows from computations on standard basis elements of $\mathbf{H}$.
