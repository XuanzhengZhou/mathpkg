Let $C_k$ denote the real Clifford algebra on $k$ generators, and let $C_k^c = C_k \otimes_{\mathbf{R}} \mathbf{C}$ be its complexification. Denote by $N_k$ and $M_k$ the Grothendieck groups of irreducible $Z_2$-graded $C_k$-modules, and by $a_k$ the associated rank invariant. Then the following periodicity relations hold:
$$N_{k+8} \cong N_k, \quad M_{k+8} \cong M_k, \quad a_{k+8} = 16 a_k,$$
$$N_{k+2}^c \cong N_k^c, \quad M_{k+8}^c \cong M_k^c, \quad a_{k+2}^c = 2 a_k^c.$$
