This proposition establishes an isomorphism between the Clifford algebra of the orthogonal direct sum of a quadratic space with a 1-dimensional negative-definite form and the graded tensor product of the respective Clifford algebras. As a corollary, the Clifford algebra $C_{k-1}$ on $k-1$ generators is isomorphic to the even subalgebra $C_k^0$ of the Clifford algebra on $k$ generators. An explicit vector space isomorphism $\phi(x_0 + x_1) = x_0 + e_k x_1$ is given, which respects the multiplicative structure of the $Z_2$-graded algebra.
