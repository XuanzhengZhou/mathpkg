This corollary states the 8-fold Bott periodicity for real Clifford algebras. It asserts that the Clifford algebra $C_{k+8}$ on $k+8$ generators is isomorphic to the tensor product of the Clifford algebra $C_k$ on $k$ generators with the full matrix algebra $\mathbf{R}(16)$ of $16 \times 16$ real matrices. The same periodicity holds for the primed Clifford algebras $C'_k$. This result follows by iterating the isomorphism $\mathbf{H}(2) \otimes \mathbf{H}(2) \cong \mathbf{R}(16)$.
