For the principal $U_F(n)$-bundle $V_n(F^{2n}) \rightarrow G_n(F^{2n})$, the homotopy exact sequence yields an isomorphism

$$\partial: \pi_i(G_n(F^{2n})) \xrightarrow{\cong} \pi_{i-1}(U_F(n))$$

for $i < (n+1)c-2$. By the stability of $\pi_{i-1}(U_F(n))$ for $i-1 < c(n+1)-3$, the inclusion $U_F(n) \rightarrow U_F$ induces an isomorphism $\pi_{i-1}(U_F(n)) \xrightarrow{\cong} \pi_{i-1}(U_F)$. Composing with the corepresentation isomorphism $\theta: \tilde{K}_F(S^i) \xrightarrow{\cong} \pi_i(B_{(F)})$ yields the Bott periodicity isomorphism

$$\tilde{K}_F(S^i) \xrightarrow{\cong} \pi_i(B_{(F)}) \xrightarrow{\cong} \pi_i(G_n(F^{2n})) \xrightarrow{\cong} \pi_{i-1}(U_F(n)) \xrightarrow{\cong} \pi_{i-1}(U_F).$$
