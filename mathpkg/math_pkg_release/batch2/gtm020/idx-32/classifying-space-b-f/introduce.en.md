The classifying space $B_{(F)}$ is the infinite Grassmannian obtained as the union $\bigcup_{n \geq 1} G_n(F^{2n})$ equipped with the inductive (colimit) topology. This space serves as a corepresenting object for reduced topological $K$-theory, meaning that homotopy classes of maps into $B_{(F)}$ classify stable equivalence classes of vector bundles. The notation varies by field: $B_O$ for real bundles, $B_U$ for complex bundles, and $B_{Sp}$ for quaternionic bundles.
