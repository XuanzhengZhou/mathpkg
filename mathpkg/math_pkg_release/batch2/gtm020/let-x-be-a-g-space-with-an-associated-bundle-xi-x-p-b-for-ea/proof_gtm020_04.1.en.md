5. Definition of Fibre Bundles

Let $\xi = (X, p, B)$ be a principal $G$-bundle, and let $F$ be a left $G$-space. The relation $(x, y)s = (xs, s^{-1}y)$ defines a right $G$-space structure on $X \times F$. Let $X_F$ denote the quotient space $(X \times F) \bmod G$, and let $p_F: X_F \rightarrow B$ be the factorization of the composition of $X \times F \rightarrow X \rightarrow B$ by the projection $X \times F \rightarrow X_F$. Explicitly, we have $p_F((x, y)G) = p(x)$ for $(x, y) \in X \times F$.
