This proposition establishes the relationship between stable fibre homotopy equivalence of sphere bundles and the behaviour of their associated Thom spaces. Given two vector bundles $\xi$ and $\eta$ over the same base space $X$, if their sphere bundles $S(\xi)$ and $S(\eta)$ are fibre homotopy equivalent, then their Thom spaces $T(\xi)$ and $T(\eta)$ are homotopy equivalent. Moreover, equality of their $J$-classes implies that the Thom spaces are isomorphic in the stable homotopy category ($S$-category).
