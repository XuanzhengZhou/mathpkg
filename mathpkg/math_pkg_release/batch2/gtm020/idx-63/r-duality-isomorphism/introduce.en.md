Given an $r$-duality map $u: X \wedge X' \to S^r$, the induced map $\delta^W(u)_Z$ on stable homotopy sets is an isomorphism whenever $W$ and $Z$ are finite CW-complexes. This result is proved by induction on the number of cells of $W$ and $Z$ using the Puppe sequence, establishing that $S$-duality provides a natural isomorphism between appropriate sets of stable homotopy classes.
