This is the fundamental classification theorem for principal $G$-bundles over CW-complexes. Given a CW-complex $B$ and a locally trivial principal $G$-bundle $\omega = (X_0, p_0, B_0)$ whose total space $X_0$ has vanishing homotopy groups up to the dimension of $B$, the natural map $\phi_\omega(B)$ from homotopy classes of maps $[B, B_0]$ to isomorphism classes of principal $G$-bundles $k_G(B)$ is a bijection. This establishes $B_0$ as a classifying space for $G$-bundles over spaces of dimension at most $\dim B$, with the bijection given by pulling back the universal bundle $\omega$.
