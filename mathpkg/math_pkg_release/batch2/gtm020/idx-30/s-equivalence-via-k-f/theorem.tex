Let $X$ be a space with the following property $(S)$: For each vector bundle $\xi$ over $X$ there exists a vector bundle $\eta$ over $X$ such that $\xi \oplus \eta$ is isomorphic to a trivial bundle. Then the natural map $\alpha: \operatorname{Vect}_F(X) \to K_F(X)$ induces a bijection between the set of $s$-equivalence classes of vector bundles over $X$ and $K_F(X)$.
