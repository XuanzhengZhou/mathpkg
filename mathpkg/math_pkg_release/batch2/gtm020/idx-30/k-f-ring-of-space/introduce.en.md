The $K_F(X)$ ring (or group when $F = \mathbf{H}$, the quaternions) of a topological space $X$ is defined as the ring completion of the isomorphism classes of $F$-vector bundles over $X$. This construction generalizes the Grothendieck group construction from abelian semigroups to commutative semirings. The functorial behavior is inherited from $\operatorname{Vect}_F$, which is a cofunctor from spaces to semirings.
