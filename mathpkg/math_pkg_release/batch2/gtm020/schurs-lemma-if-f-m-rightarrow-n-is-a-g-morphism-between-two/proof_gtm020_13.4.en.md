The first statement follows from (4.9). For the second statement, let $\lambda$ be an eigenvalue of $f$, and let ker $(f - \lambda) = L$ be a $G$-submodule of $M$. Since $L \neq 0$, we have $L = M$ and $f(x) = \lambda x$. This proves the theorem.
