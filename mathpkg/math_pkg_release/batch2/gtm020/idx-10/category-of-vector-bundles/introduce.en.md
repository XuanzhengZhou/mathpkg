The category of vector bundles $\mathbf{VB}$ organizes vector bundles and their morphisms into a categorical framework. Important subcategories include $\mathbf{VB}_k$ (bundles of fixed fibre dimension $k$), $\mathbf{VB}_B$ (bundles over a common base $B$ with $B$-morphisms), and $\mathbf{VB}_B^k = \mathbf{VB}_B \cap \mathbf{VB}_k$. Within $\mathbf{VB}_B$, an isomorphism is defined as an invertible $B$-morphism, giving the natural notion of equivalence for vector bundles over the same base.
