This corollary provides the product form of homotopy invariance for vector bundles. It states that any vector bundle over $B \times I$ is isomorphic to the product of its restriction to $B \times \{1\}$ with the interval $I$. The proof follows from the observation that the pullback $r^*(\xi|_{B \times 1})$ coincides with $(\xi|_{B \times 1}) \times I$, where both bundles have total space consisting of triples $(b, t, x)$ with $(b, 1) = p(x)$.
