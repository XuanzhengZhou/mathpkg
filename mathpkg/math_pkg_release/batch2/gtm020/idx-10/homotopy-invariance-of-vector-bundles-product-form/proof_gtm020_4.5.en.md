It suffices to observe that $r^*(\xi|_{B \times 1}) = (\xi|_{B \times 1}) \times I$. In both cases the total space of the bundle is the subspace of $(b, t, x) \in B \times I \times E(\xi|_{B \times 1})$ such that $(b, 1) = p(x)$, and the projection is the map $(b, t, x) \mapsto (b, t)$. The result then follows from Corollary 4.4.
