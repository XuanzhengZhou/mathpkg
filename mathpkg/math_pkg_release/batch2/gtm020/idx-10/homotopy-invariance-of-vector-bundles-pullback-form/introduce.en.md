This corollary expresses the homotopy invariance of vector bundles in pullback form. Given a vector bundle $\xi$ over $B \times I$, it is isomorphic to the pullback of its restriction at $t = 1$ along the retraction $r(b, t) = (b, 1)$. This is a direct consequence of the main homotopy theorem (4.3) and provides the key stepping stone to the product form of homotopy invariance.
