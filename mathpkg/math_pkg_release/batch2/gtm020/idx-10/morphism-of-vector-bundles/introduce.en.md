A morphism of vector bundles generalizes the notion of a bundle map to the vector bundle setting. It consists of a pair of continuous maps $(u, f)$ between the total spaces and base spaces that respect the projection maps, together with the requirement that $u$ be linear on each fibre. This definition provides the correct notion of structure-preserving map between vector bundles, enabling the formation of categories of vector bundles over fixed or varying base spaces.
