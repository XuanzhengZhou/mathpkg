This lemma is a crucial technical step toward proving the homotopy invariance of vector bundles. It states that for any vector bundle over a product $B \times I$, the base $B$ admits an open covering where the restriction of the bundle to each $U_i \times I$ is trivial. The proof uses the local triviality of $\xi$ and the compactness of the interval $I=[0,1]$ to produce a finite chain of subintervals over which the bundle trivializes near each point of $B$.
