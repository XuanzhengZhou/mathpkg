The space of $F$-linear maps between two $G$-modules carries a natural $G$-module structure via the conjugation action $sf = s_N f s_M^{-1}$. Under this action, the $G$-morphisms $\operatorname{Hom}_G(M, N)$ are precisely the $G$-invariant elements. This construction also endows the dual module $M^+$ with a $G$-module structure, where the scalar field $F$ is given the trivial $G$-action.
