The first statement follows from Theorem 1.3 by duality. The second statement follows by observing that the dual of the composite isomorphism of Proposition 1.1 defines $\bar{H}^*(\Omega S(X)) \to H^*(\Omega S(X)) \otimes \bar{H}^*(X)$, which is an isomorphism preserving the Steenrod algebra action. This Steenrod compatibility is carried through in the dual to Proposition 1.2.
