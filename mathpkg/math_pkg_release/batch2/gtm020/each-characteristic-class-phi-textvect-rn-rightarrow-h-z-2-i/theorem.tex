6. 2-Divisible Real Characteristic Classes in Dimension $n$

The theory of real characteristic classes divides naturally in two parts: the theory for rings $R$ with $2R = 0$ (see Sec. 5) and the theory for rings $R$ with $1/2 \in R$, that is, 2-divisible rings which we consider in this and the following section.
