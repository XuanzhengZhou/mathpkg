For a locally trivial principal $Z_2$-bundle $q: E \to B$ and a coefficient ring $R$ in which 2 is invertible (a 2-divisible ring), the induced map in cohomology $q^*$ is injective. Moreover, the image of $q^*$ consists exactly of those cohomology classes on $E$ that are fixed by the involution $\tau$ induced by the $Z_2$-action. This result provides the key to passing from cohomology of the base to cohomology of the total space when studying real characteristic classes with 2-divisible coefficients.
