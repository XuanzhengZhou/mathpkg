There is a fundamental correspondence between additive (resp. multiplicative) characteristic classes of complex vector bundles and formal power series. For each characteristic class $\phi$, one defines a formal power series $a(\phi)(t) \in R[[t]]$ by evaluating $\phi$ on the canonical line bundle over $CP^n$. Conversely, using the splitting principle, any formal power series determines a characteristic class. This isomorphism respects the algebraic structure: $a(\phi+\psi)=a(\phi)+a(\psi)$ and $m(\phi\psi)=m(\phi)m(\psi)$.
