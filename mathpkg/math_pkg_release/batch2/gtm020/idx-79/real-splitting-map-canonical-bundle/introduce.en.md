The real analogue of the complex splitting principle uses real projective spaces $RP^\infty$ and the canonical real line bundle $\gamma$. The classifying map $h_n$ for the $n$-fold product of $\gamma$ provides a splitting map for the universal $n$-plane bundle $\gamma_n$ over the real Grassmannian $G_n(R^\infty)$. The proof is parallel to the complex case (Proposition 3.1), with the key difference that it works with respect to $Z_2$ cohomology, reflecting the fact that Stiefel-Whitney classes are $Z_2$-valued.
