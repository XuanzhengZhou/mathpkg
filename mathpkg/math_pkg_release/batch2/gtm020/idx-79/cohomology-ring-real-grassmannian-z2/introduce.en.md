The $Z_2$ cohomology ring of the infinite real Grassmannian $G_n(R^\infty) = BO(n)$ is a polynomial ring over $Z_2$ freely generated by the Stiefel-Whitney classes $w_1, \ldots, w_n$. This is the real counterpart to the computation of the complex Grassmannian cohomology with Chern classes as generators. The algebraic independence of the Stiefel-Whitney classes justifies their role as the universal characteristic classes for real vector bundles with $Z_2$ coefficients.
