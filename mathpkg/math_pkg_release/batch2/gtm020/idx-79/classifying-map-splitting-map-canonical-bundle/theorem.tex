Let $h_n: CP^\infty \times \dots \times CP^\infty \rightarrow G_n(C^\infty)$ be the classifying map for the bundle $\gamma \times \dots \times \gamma$, where $\gamma$ is the canonical line bundle over $CP^\infty$. Then $h_n$ is a splitting map for the canonical bundle $\gamma_n$ over $G_n(C^\infty)$.
