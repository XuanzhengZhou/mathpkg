For a principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ and any map $f: B_1 \to B$, the pullback of the associated bundle $f^*(\xi[F])$ is canonically isomorphic to the associated bundle of the pullback $f^*(\xi)[F]$. This isomorphism is compatible with the natural morphisms back to the original bundles, demonstrating that the associated bundle construction commutes with pullbacks.
