A fibre bundle morphism between associated bundles $\xi[F]$ and $\xi'[F]$ is induced by a morphism $(u, f)$ of the underlying principal $G$-bundles $\xi$ and $\xi'$ by passing the $G$-morphism $u \times 1_F$ to the quotient. When the base spaces coincide and $f$ is the identity, one speaks of a fibre bundle morphism over $B$. This definition establishes the category of fibre bundles with fibre $F$ and structure group $G$ as the image of the associated bundle functor acting on the category of principal $G$-bundles.
