In the analysis of polynomial clutching maps for vector bundles over $X$, one obtains two canonical bundle endomorphisms $p_0$ and $p_\infty$ via contour integrals of the clutching function $p(x,z) = a(x)z + b(x)$. This proposition establishes the fundamental commutation relation $p p_0 = p_\infty p$ and verifies that both $p_0$ and $p_\infty$ are idempotent projection operators on the bundle $\zeta$. These projections are the key structural elements used to decompose the bundle constructed from a clutching map.
