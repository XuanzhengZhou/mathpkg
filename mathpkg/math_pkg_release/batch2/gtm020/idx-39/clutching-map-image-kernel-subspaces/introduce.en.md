This notation introduces the canonical decomposition of the vector bundle $\zeta$ induced by the projections $p_0$ and $p_\infty$ obtained from a polynomial clutching map. The image subbundles $\zeta_+^0$ and $\zeta_+^\infty$ and the kernel subbundles $\zeta_-^0$ and $\zeta_-^\infty$ are well-defined because $p_0$ and $p_\infty$ have constant rank. Moreover, the commutation relation $p p_0 = p_\infty p$ ensures that the clutching map $p$ restricts to bundle morphisms $p_+$ between the image subbundles and $p_-$ between the kernel subbundles.
