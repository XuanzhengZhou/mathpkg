Let $p$ be a polynomial clutching map for a vector bundle $\zeta$ of degree $\leq n$. The operator $L^n$ applied to $\zeta$ is defined by
$$
L^n(\zeta) = (n+1)\zeta,
$$
the $(n+1)$-fold Whitney sum of $\zeta$ with itself. The linear clutching map $L^n(p)$ induced on $L^n(\zeta)$ yields a decomposition
$$
L^n(\zeta) = L^n(\zeta, p)_+ \oplus L^n(\zeta, p)_-.
$$
Moreover, there is an isomorphism of vector bundles over $X \times S^2$:
$$
[L^n(\zeta), L^n(p)] \cong [L^n(\zeta, p)_+, z] \oplus [L^n(\zeta, p)_-, 1].
$$
