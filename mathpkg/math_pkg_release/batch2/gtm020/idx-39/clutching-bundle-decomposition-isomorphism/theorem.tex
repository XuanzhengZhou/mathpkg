Let $\zeta$ be a vector bundle over a space $X$, and let $p(x,z) = a(x)z + b(x)$ be a polynomial clutching map for $\zeta$. Let $p_+^t$ and $p_-^t$ be the linear homotopies defined in (4.5), which are isomorphisms onto their images for all $t \in [0,1]$. Then there is an isomorphism of vector bundles over $X \times S^2$:
$$
[\zeta, p] \cong [\zeta_+^0, z] \oplus [\zeta_-^0, 1].
$$
Here $[\zeta, p]$ denotes the bundle over $X \times S^2$ obtained by clutching $\zeta$ with $p$, $[\zeta_+^0, z]$ is the bundle obtained by clutching $\zeta_+^0$ with the identity clutching map $z$, and $[\zeta_-^0, 1]$ is the bundle obtained by clutching $\zeta_-^0$ with the constant clutching map $1$.
