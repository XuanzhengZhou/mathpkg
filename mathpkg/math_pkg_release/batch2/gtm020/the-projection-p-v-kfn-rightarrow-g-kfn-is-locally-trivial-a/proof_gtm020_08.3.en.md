4. Stability of the Homotopy Groups of the Classical Groups

Let $F$ denote $\mathbf{R}$, $\mathbf{C}$, or $\mathbf{H}$, and let $c$ be a corresponding integer, where $c$ equals the dimension of the real vector space $F$ over $\mathbf{R}$. We make use of the following fibre maps $p$.

$$U_F(n) \rightarrow V_{n+1}(F^{n+1}) = U_F(n+1) \xrightarrow{p} V_1(F^{n+1}) = S^{(n+1)c-1}$$

$$SU_F(n) \rightarrow V_n(F^{n+1}) = SU_F(n+1) \xrightarrow{p} V_1(F^{n+1}) = S^{(n+1)c-1}$$
