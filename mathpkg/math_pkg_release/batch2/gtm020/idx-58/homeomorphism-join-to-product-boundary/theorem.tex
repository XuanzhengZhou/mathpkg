A homeomorphism
$$u: S^{n-1} \ast S^{m-1} \rightarrow (B^n \times S^{m-1}) \cup (S^{n-1} \times B^m) = \partial(B^n \times B^m)$$
is given by
$$u\langle x,t,y\rangle = (2 \min(t,1/2)\,x,\; 2 \min(1-t,1/2)\,y),$$
and a homeomorphism
$$v: S^{n-1} \ast S^{m-1} \rightarrow S^{n+m-1}$$
is given by
$$v\langle x,t,y\rangle = (\sin(\pi t/2)\,x,\; \cos(\pi t/2)\,y).$$
