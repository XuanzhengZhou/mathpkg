A $\text{Spin}(n)$-module $M$ decomposes into a direct sum of submodules $M_1 \oplus M_2$ where $-1$ acts as the identity on $M_1$ and $M_1$ is an $SO(n)$-module and where $-1$ acts as multiplication by $-1$ on $M_2$ and $M_2$ is a $C_{n-1}$-module with $\text{Spin}(n)$ coming from the inclusion $\text{Spin}(n) \subset C_n^0$ and the isomorphism $C_n^0 \cong C_{n-1}$.
