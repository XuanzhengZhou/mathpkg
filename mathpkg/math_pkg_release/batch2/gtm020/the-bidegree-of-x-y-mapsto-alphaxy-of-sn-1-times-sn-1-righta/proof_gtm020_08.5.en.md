By (10.1) $x \mapsto \alpha(x) \alpha(e_{n-1})y$ has degree $1 + (-1)^n$ and is independent of $\alpha(e_{n-1})y \in S^{n-1}$. Moreover, the map $y \mapsto \alpha(x)y$ is a reflection through a hyperplane. It has degree $-1$.
