This follows from Proposition 5.7 (transitivity of induced bundles) applied to the inclusion $j: A_1 \hookrightarrow B_1$ and the map $g = f|_{A_1}: A_1 \to A$. Specifically, $(f^*\xi)|A_1 = j^*(f^*(\xi)) \cong (f \circ j)^*(\xi) = (j_A \circ g)^*(\xi) = g^*(j_A^*(\xi)) = g^*(\xi|A)$, where $j_A: A \hookrightarrow B$ is the inclusion.
