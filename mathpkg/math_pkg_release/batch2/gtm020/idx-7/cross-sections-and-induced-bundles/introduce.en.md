This proposition describes the relationship between cross sections and induced bundles. Given a cross section $s$ of a bundle $\xi$ over $B$, one can construct a cross section $\sigma$ of the pullback bundle $f^*(\xi)$ over $B_1$ by composing $s$ with the map $f$. Conversely, if $f$ is an identification (quotient) map and a cross section of $f^*(\xi)$ is constant on the fibres of $f$, it descends to a cross section of $\xi$ over $B$.
