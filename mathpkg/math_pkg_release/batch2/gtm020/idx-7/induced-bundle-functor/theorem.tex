For each map $f: B_1 \to B$, the family of functions $f^*: \mathrm{Bun}_B \to \mathrm{Bun}_{B_1}$ defines a functor. Moreover, for a $B$-morphism $u: \xi \to \eta$, the induced bundle morphism $f^*(u): f^*(\xi) \to f^*(\eta)$ makes the following diagram commute:

$$\begin{CD}
E(f^*(\eta)) @>{f_\eta}>> E(\eta) \\
@V{f^*(u)}VV @VV{u}V \\
E(f^*(\xi)) @>>{f_\xi}> E(\xi) \\
@VVV @VVV \\
B_1 @>>{f}> B
\end{CD}$$
