In Theorem (8.2), observe that if $u(t)$ is a path from 1 to $s$ then $u(t) c_\xi u(t)^{-1}$ is a homotopy from $c_\xi$ to $sc_\xi s^{-1}$. This remark and Theorem (8.2) demonstrate the corollary.
