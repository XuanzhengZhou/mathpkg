A direct construction yields inverses for the restrictions of $q_1$ and $q_2$. The induced maps are continuous bijections which are bicontinuous except possibly at the base point. Observe that $q_1(V)$ is open in $S(\xi \oplus \theta^1)$ and $q_2(V)$ in $P(\xi \oplus \theta^1)$, where $V$ is an open neighborhood of $S(\xi)$ in $D(\xi)$.
