The Whitney sum of the tangent bundle $\tau(S^n)$ and the normal bundle $\nu(S^n)$ of the $n$-sphere is isomorphic to the trivial $(n+1)$-plane bundle $\theta^{n+1}$. This isomorphism is constructed explicitly: the forward map $u$ simply adds the tangent and normal components as vectors in the ambient Euclidean space, while the inverse $v$ decomposes an ambient vector into its tangential and normal parts using the normal map $v_b$ and projection $\pi_b$.
