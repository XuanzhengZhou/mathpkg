Let $\lambda$ denote the canonical line bundle on $\mathbf{R}P^n$. Over $\mathbf{R}P^n$, there is an isomorphism $u \colon (n+1)\lambda \rightarrow \tau(\mathbf{R}P^n) \oplus \theta^1$ with inverse $v$ defined by the following relations:
$$u(\pm b, (a_0 b, \ldots, a_n b)) = (\pm(b, v_b(a_0, \ldots, a_n)), (\pm b, \pi_b(a_0, \ldots, a_n)))$$
$$v(\pm b, (x, \pm b, k)) = (\pm b, p_0(x + k b) b, \ldots, p_n(x + k b) b)$$
where $p_i \colon \mathbf{R}^{n+1} \rightarrow \mathbf{R}$ denotes the projection onto the $i$-th coordinate. These maps are well defined and are inverses of each other from the relations $a = v_b(a) + \pi_b(a) b$ and $-a = -v_b(a) + \pi_b(-a)(-b)$.
