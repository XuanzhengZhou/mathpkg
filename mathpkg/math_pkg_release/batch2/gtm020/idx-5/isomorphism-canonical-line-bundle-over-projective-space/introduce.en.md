Over the real projective space $\mathbf{R}P^n$, the $(n+1)$-fold Whitney sum of the canonical line bundle $\lambda$ is isomorphic to the Whitney sum of the tangent bundle $\tau(\mathbf{R}P^n)$ and the trivial line bundle $\theta^1$. The isomorphism $u$ and its inverse $v$ are constructed explicitly using the normal map $v_b$ and projection $\pi_b$, together with the coordinate projections $p_i$ of $\mathbf{R}^{n+1}$. This is a fundamental relation linking the geometry of $\mathbf{R}P^n$ to its tautological line bundle.
