(1) $n$ is odd.
(2) $x \mapsto -x$ is of degree 1.
(3) $x \mapsto -x$ is homotopic to the identity.
(4) $S^n$ has a unit tangent vector field.

By negation, the following statements are also equivalent properties of $S^n$.

(1) $n$ is even.
(2) $x \mapsto -x$ is of degree $-1$.
(3) $x \mapsto -x$ is not homotopic to the identity.
(4) $S^n$ has no unit tangent vector field
