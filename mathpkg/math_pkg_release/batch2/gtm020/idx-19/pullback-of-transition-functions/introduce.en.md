This proposition describes the functorial behavior of transition functions under pullback. Given a fibre bundle $\eta$ over $B$ with atlas $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ and transition functions $\{g_{i,j}\}$, the pullback bundle $f^*(\eta)$ over $B_1$ inherits an atlas over the open covering $\{f^{-1}(V_i)\}$ whose transition functions are simply the compositions $g_{i,j} \circ f: f^{-1}(V_i) \cap f^{-1}(V_j) \rightarrow G$. The pullback chart $f^*(h_i)$ is defined by $(b_1, y) \mapsto (b_1, h_i(f(b_1), y))$, and the transition function relation follows directly from the original cocycle condition.
