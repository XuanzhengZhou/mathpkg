A system of transition functions on a space $B$ relative to an open covering $\{V_i\}$ with $i \in I$ of $B$ is a family of maps $g_{i,j}: V_i \cap V_j \rightarrow G$ for each $i, j \in I$ such that property (T1) is satisfied: for each $b \in V_i \cap V_j \cap V_k$, the relation $g_{i,k}(b) = g_{i,j}(b)g_{j,k}(b)$ holds. It follows that $g_{i,i}(b) = 1$ (the identity in $G$) and $g_{i,j}(b) = g_{j,i}(b)^{-1}$ on $V_i \cap V_j$.
