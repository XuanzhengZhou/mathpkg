Let $\xi = (B \times G, p, B)$ be the product principal $G$-bundle. Then the $B$-automorphisms $\xi \rightarrow \xi$ over $B$ are in bijective correspondence with maps $B \rightarrow G$. More precisely, the $B$-automorphisms of $\xi$ have the form $h_g(b, s) = (b, g(b)s)$, where $g: B \rightarrow G$ is a map.
