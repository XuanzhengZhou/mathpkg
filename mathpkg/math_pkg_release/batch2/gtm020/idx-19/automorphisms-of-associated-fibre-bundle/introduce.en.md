This corollary extends the characterization of automorphisms from principal $G$-bundles to their associated fibre bundles with fibre $F$. When $F$ is a left $G$-space, the automorphisms of the trivial associated bundle $(B \times F, p, B)$ are also parametrized by maps $g: B \rightarrow G$, acting through the $G$-action on the fibre: $(b, y) \mapsto (b, g(b)y)$. The composition of automorphisms corresponds to pointwise multiplication of the parameter maps, and the identity automorphism corresponds exactly to the constant map to the identity element of $G$.
