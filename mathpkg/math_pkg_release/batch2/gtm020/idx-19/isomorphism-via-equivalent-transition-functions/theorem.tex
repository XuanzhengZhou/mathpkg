Let $\eta$ and $\eta'$ be two fibre bundles (or vector bundles) over $B$ with atlases $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ and $\{(V_i, h'_i)\}$ having transition functions $\{g_{i,j}\}$ and $\{g'_{i,j}\}$, respectively. Then $\eta$ and $\eta'$ are isomorphic over $B$ if and only if $\{g_{i,j}\}$ and $\{g'_{i,j}\}$ are equivalent systems of transition functions, i.e., there exist maps $r_i: V_i \rightarrow G$ such that $g'_{i,j}(b) = r_i(b)^{-1} g_{i,j}(b) r_j(b)$ for each $b \in V_i \cap V_j$.
