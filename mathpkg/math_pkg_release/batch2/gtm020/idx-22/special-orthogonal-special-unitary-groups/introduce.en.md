The special orthogonal group $SO(k)$ and the special unitary group $SU(k)$ are the subgroups of $O(k)$ and $U(k)$ consisting of matrices with determinant $+1$. These closed subgroups are the identity components of the full orthogonal and unitary groups and are themselves compact Lie groups. They appear in the classification of oriented vector bundles and play a central role in the reduction theory of structure groups for real and complex vector bundles with a metric.
