Let $\xi$ be a vector bundle over $B$ with fibre $F^n$, where $F = \mathbf{R}$, $\mathbf{C}$, or $\mathbf{H}$, and let $\beta$ be a metric on $\xi$. Then there exists an atlas $\{(V_i, h_i^*)\}$ of $\xi$ such that for each $b \in V_i \cap V_j$, the transition functions $g_{i,j}(b)$ take values in the orthogonal group $O(n)$ (if $F = \mathbf{R}$), the unitary group $U(n)$ (if $F = \mathbf{C}$), or the symplectic group $Sp(n)$ (if $F = \mathbf{H}$).
