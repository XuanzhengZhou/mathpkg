5.3 Remarks. We have $G_n(R^\infty) = BO(n)$ and $RP^\infty \times \dots \times RP^\infty = B(Z_2)^n$. The inclusion of diagonal matrices $(Z_2)^n$ into $O(n)$ induces the splitting map $B(Z_2)^n \rightarrow BO(n)$ or $RP^\infty \times \dots \times RP^\infty \rightarrow G_n(R^\infty)$.
