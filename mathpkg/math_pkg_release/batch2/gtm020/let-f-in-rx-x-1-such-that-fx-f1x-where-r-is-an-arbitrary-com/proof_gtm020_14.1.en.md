We have $f(x) = \sum_m a_m x^m$ with $a_m = a_{-m}$ since $f(x) = f(1/x)$. Therefore, $f$ has the form $\sum_{m \geq 0} a_m(x^m + x^{-m}) = \sum_{m \geq 0} a_m j_m(x + x^{-1})$ by (5.1). This proves the proposition.
