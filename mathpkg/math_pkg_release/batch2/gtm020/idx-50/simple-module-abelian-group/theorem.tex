Let $G$ be an abelian group, and let $M$ be a simple $G$-module over $\mathbb{C}$. Then $M$ is one-dimensional over $\mathbb{C}$, and $s_M$ is multiplication by $\lambda_s$, where $s \mapsto \lambda_s$ is a group morphism $G \rightarrow \mathbb{C}^\times$, with $\mathbb{C}^\times$ the multiplicative group of non-zero elements in $\mathbb{C}$.
