The isomorphism $\Lambda^{n+1}(V \oplus L) \cong \Lambda^n(V) \otimes L$ holds for vector spaces $V$ of dimension $n$ and a one-dimensional vector space $L$, via a functorial isomorphism. By the result of Section 5(6.4), which states that functorial isomorphisms of vector spaces induce isomorphisms of the associated vector bundles, the same isomorphism holds for vector bundles $\xi$ and $\zeta$ over $X$.
