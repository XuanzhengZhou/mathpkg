Proposition 10.1 provides a vector bundle isomorphism relating the top exterior power of the direct sum of an $n$-dimensional vector bundle $\xi$ with a line bundle $\zeta$. Specifically, $\Lambda^{n+1}(\xi \oplus \zeta) \cong \Lambda^n(\xi) \otimes \zeta$. This identity is a functorial isomorphism that holds at the level of vector spaces, and by the results of Section 5(6.4) it lifts to an isomorphism of vector bundles over the base space $X$. This lemma is used in the study of Adams operations to relate the exterior powers involved in the definition of $\psi^k$.
