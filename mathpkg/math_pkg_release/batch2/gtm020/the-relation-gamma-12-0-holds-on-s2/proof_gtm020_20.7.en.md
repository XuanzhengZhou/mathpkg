Since $ch$ is a ring homomorphism, $ch(\gamma - 1)^2 = ch(\gamma - 1) ch(\gamma - 1) = c_1(\gamma)^2 = 0$. Since $ch$ is an isomorphism, $(\gamma - 1)^2 = 0$.
