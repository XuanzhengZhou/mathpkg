Let $M^n$ be a manifold. If $M^n$ can be immersed in $\mathbf{R}^{n+k}$, then $\tilde{w}_i(M) = 0$ for $i > k$. If $M^n$ can be embedded in $\mathbf{R}^{n+k}$, then $\tilde{w}_i(M) = 0$ for $i \geq k$, and if, in addition, $M^n$ is embedded in $\mathbf{R}^{n+k}$ with an orientable normal bundle $\nu$, then $e(\nu) = 0$ (besides $w_i(\nu) = 0$ for $i \geq k$).
