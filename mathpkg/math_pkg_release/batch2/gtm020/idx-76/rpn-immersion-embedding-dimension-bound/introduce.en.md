This corollary extends the immersion and embedding conditions for real projective spaces to arbitrary dimensions by decomposing $n = 2^r + q$ with $0 \leq q < 2^r$. It provides explicit dimension bounds: $\mathbf{RP}^n$ cannot be immersed into $\mathbf{R}^{2^{r+1}-2}$ and cannot be embedded into $\mathbf{R}^{2^{r+1}-1}$. The proof applies Theorem 10.3 to the inclusion $\mathbf{RP}^{2^r} \subset \mathbf{RP}^n$, using the fact that the dual Stiefel-Whitney classes of the larger projective space restrict nontrivially from the smaller one.
