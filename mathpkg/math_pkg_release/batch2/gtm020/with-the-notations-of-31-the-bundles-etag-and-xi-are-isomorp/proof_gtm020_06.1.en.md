5. Classifying Spaces and the Reduction of Structure Group

Let $H$ be a closed subgroup of $G$, let $\omega_H = (Y_0, q_0, B_H)$ be a universal bundle for $H$, and let $\omega_G = (X_0, p_0, B_G)$ be a universal bundle for $G$. By Corollary (3.2), $\omega_H[G]$ is a numerable principal $G$-bundle over $B_H$. By the classification theorem 4(12.2), there is a principal $G$-bundle morphism $(h_0, f_0): \omega_H[G] \rightarrow \omega_G$, where $f_0^*(\omega_G)$ and $\omega_H[G]$ are isomorphic over $B_H$.
