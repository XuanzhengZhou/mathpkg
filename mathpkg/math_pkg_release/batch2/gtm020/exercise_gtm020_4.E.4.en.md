Let $U$ and $V$ be two open subsets of a space $B$ such that $B = U \cup V$ and $U \cap V = W_1 \cup \cdots \cup W_k$, the connected components of $U \cap V$. Determine, up to isomorphism, all principal $G$-bundles $\xi$ over $B$ for $G$, a discrete group such that $\xi|U$ and $\xi|V$ are trivial.
