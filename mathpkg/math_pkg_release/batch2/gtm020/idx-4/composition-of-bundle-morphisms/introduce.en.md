The composition of two bundle morphisms is defined componentwise: $(u', f') \circ (u, f) = (u' \circ u, f' \circ f)$. The commutativity condition $p'' \circ (u'u) = (f'f) \circ p$ follows directly from the commutativity of the two individual morphisms: $p'' u' u = f' p' u = f' f p$. This composition is associative and, together with the identity morphisms $(1_E, 1_B)$, makes the collection of all bundles and bundle morphisms into a category.
