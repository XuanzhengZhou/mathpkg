The canonical line bundle $\gamma_1^n$ is the special case $k = 1$ of the canonical $k$-dimensional vector bundle over the Grassmannian manifold. Its base space is the real projective space $\mathbb{R}P^{n-1} = G_1(\mathbb{R}^n)$, the Grassmannian of $1$-dimensional subspaces of $\mathbb{R}^n$. The total space consists of pairs $(V, x)$ where $V$ is a line through the origin in $\mathbb{R}^n$ and $x \in V$, with projection $(V, x) \mapsto V$. This bundle plays a fundamental role in the classification theory of vector bundles.
