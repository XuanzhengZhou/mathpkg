Let $E$ be a smooth vector bundle over a smooth manifold $M$, and let $\nabla_0$ and $\nabla_1$ be two connections on $E$. A \textbf{homotopy} from $\nabla_0$ to $\nabla_1$ is a connection $\nabla$ on the pullback bundle $E \times [0,1]$ over $M \times [0,1]$ such that $\nabla$ restricts to $\nabla_i$ on $E \times \{i\}$ over $M \times \{i\}$ for $i = 0, 1$.
