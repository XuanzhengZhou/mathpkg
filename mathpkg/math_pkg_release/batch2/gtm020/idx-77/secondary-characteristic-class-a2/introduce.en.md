The class $a_2(E) = c_1(E)^2 - 2c_2(E)$ is a secondary characteristic class arising naturally from the Newton identity relating traces and Chern polynomials: $\text{Tr}(X^2) = c_1(X)^2 - 2c_2(X)$. In the Chern-Weil framework, $a_2(E)$ corresponds to the form $\text{Tr}(K^2)$ evaluated on the curvature. This class appears in the study of flat bundles over 3-manifolds, where it relates to the Chern-Simons invariant.
