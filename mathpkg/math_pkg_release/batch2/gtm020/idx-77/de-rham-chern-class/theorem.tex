Let $E$ be a smooth vector bundle over a smooth manifold $M$, and let $\nabla$ be any connection on $E$. The \textbf{de Rham Chern class} $c_q(E)_{DR} \in H^{2q}_{DR}(M, \underline{\mathbb{C}})$ is the de Rham cohomology class represented by the characteristic Chern form:
$$
c_q(E)_{DR} = [c_q(E, \nabla)] \in H^{2q}_{DR}(M, \underline{\mathbb{C}}).
$$
There is a coefficient morphism $H^{2q}(M, \underline{\mathbb{Z}}) \to H^{2q}_{DR}(M, \underline{\mathbb{C}})$ in cohomology, and under this morphism, the topologically defined Chern class $c_q(E) \in H^{2q}(M, \underline{\mathbb{Z}})$ is mapped to $c_q(E)_{DR}$.
