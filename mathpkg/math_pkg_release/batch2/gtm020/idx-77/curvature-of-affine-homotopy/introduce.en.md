This proposition gives the explicit formula for the curvature of the affine homotopy connection $\nabla_t = (1-t)\nabla_0 + t\nabla_1$. The term $(\theta_0 - \theta_1)dt$ in the curvature is critical: when applying the homotopy formula $dQ + Qd = j_1 - j_0$ to a closed characteristic form $\phi(K)$, this $dt$ term produces the transgression form, which explicitly demonstrates that $\phi(K_0)$ and $\phi(K_1)$ are cohomologous. The formula also specializes in the case $\nabla_0 = 0$ to yield the curvature used in defining Chern-Simons forms.
