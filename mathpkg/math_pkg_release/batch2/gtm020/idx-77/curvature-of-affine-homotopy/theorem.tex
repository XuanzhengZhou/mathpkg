Let $\nabla_0$ and $\nabla_1$ be two connections on a smooth vector bundle $E$ over $M$, with local connection forms $\theta_0$ and $\theta_1$ and curvature forms $K_0$ and $K_1$. Let $\nabla_t = (1-t)\nabla_0 + t\nabla_1$ be the affine homotopy. Then the curvature form $K_t$ of $\nabla_t$ is given by:
$$
K_t = (1-t)K_0 + tK_1 + t(1-t)(\theta_0 - \theta_1)^2 + (\theta_0 - \theta_1)dt.
$$
