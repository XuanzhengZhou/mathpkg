Let $\nabla_0$ and $\nabla_1$ be two connections on a smooth vector bundle $E$ over a smooth manifold $M$. The \textbf{affine homotopy} from $\nabla_0$ to $\nabla_1$ is the connection $\nabla_t$ on $E \times [0,1]$ over $M \times [0,1]$ defined by
$$
\nabla_t = (1-t)\nabla_0 + t\nabla_1, \quad t \in [0,1].
$$
This is the standard construction of a homotopy between any two connections.
