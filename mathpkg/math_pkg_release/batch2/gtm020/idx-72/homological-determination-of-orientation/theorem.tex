Let $f: U \to V$ be a diffeomorphism between two open connected subsets of $\mathbf{R}^n$. Then for $x \in U$ there are the relations
$$f_*(\alpha_x) = O(f)\,\alpha_{f(x)}$$
and
$$f^*(\beta_{f(x)}) = O(f)\,\beta_x,$$
where $\alpha_x \in H_n(U, U - x)$ is a canonical generator and $\beta_x \in H^n(U, U - x)$ is its dual.
