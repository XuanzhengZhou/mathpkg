Let $k$ be a field of characteristic $p > 2$. As a Hopf algebra over $k$,

$$H_*(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}, k) = \bigotimes_{0 \leq i} E(u_i, 2np^i - 1) \otimes \bigotimes_{1 \leq j} S(v_j, 2np^j - 2)$$

where $E(u_i, d)$ denotes an exterior algebra on a generator of degree $d$ and $S(v_j, e)$ denotes a polynomial algebra on a generator of degree $e$.
