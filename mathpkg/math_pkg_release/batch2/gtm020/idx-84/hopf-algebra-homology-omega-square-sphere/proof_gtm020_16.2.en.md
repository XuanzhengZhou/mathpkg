As a Hopf algebra $H^*(\Omega S^{2n+1}, k) = S'(w, 2n)$ by (1.4) and as an algebra, for $w_j = \gamma_{p^j}(w)$ we have

$$H^*(\Omega S^{2n+1}, k) = \bigotimes_{0 \leq i} S'(w_i, 2np^i).$$

The Eilenberg-Moore spectral sequence (or equivalently the bar construction spectral sequence) applied to the path-space fibration over $\Omega S^{2n+1}$ yields the homology $H_*(\Omega^2 S^{2n+1}, k)$ as the cotensor product. The structure of the Hopf algebra follows by dualizing the cohomology Hopf algebra and identifying the primitive and indecomposable elements: the exterior generators $u_i$ in degree $2np^i - 1$ correspond to the suspension of the divided power generators, and the polynomial generators $v_j$ in degree $2np^j - 2$ correspond to the transpotence elements arising from the Bockstein spectral sequence.
