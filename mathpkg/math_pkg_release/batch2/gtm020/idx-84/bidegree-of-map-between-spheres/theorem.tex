The **bidegree** of a map $g: S^{n-1} \times S^{n-1} \rightarrow S^{n-1}$ is the pair $(d_1, d_2)$ of integers defined by the condition that:

\begin{itemize}
\item the map $x \mapsto g(x, y)$ has degree $d_1$ for each fixed $y \in S^{n-1}$,
\item the map $y \mapsto g(x, y)$ has degree $d_2$ for each fixed $x \in S^{n-1}$.
\end{itemize}
