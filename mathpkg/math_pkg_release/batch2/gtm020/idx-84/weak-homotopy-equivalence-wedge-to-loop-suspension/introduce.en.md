This theorem generalizes the classical James splitting. It states that for any path-connected space $X$, the suspension of the loop space $S\Omega S(X)$ is weakly homotopy equivalent to the wedge sum of suspensions of iterated smash products $\bigwedge_n X$. This decomposition is fundamental for understanding the homotopy type of loop spaces and is used throughout the study of homotopy groups of spheres, particularly in the construction of the James-Hopf maps and the analysis of the $EHP$ sequence.
