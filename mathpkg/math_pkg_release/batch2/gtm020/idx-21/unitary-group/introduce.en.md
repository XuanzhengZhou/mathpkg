The unitary group $U(k)$ is the subgroup of the complex general linear group $\mathbf{GL}(k, \mathbf{C})$ consisting of all $k \times k$ complex matrices that preserve the standard Hermitian inner product on $\mathbf{C}^k$. Equivalently, $u \in U(k)$ if and only if $u^* u = I_k$, where $u^*$ denotes the conjugate transpose. The unitary group plays a central role in the theory of complex vector bundles.
