The symplectic group $Sp(k)$ is the subgroup of the quaternionic general linear group $\mathbf{GL}(k, \mathbf{H})$ consisting of all $k \times k$ quaternionic matrices that preserve the standard inner product on $\mathbf{H}^k$. Here $\mathbf{H}$ denotes Hamilton's quaternions. Note that $Sp(k)$ should not be confused with the real symplectic group $Sp(2n, \mathbf{R})$; it is a compact Lie group, also denoted $USp(2k)$ in some conventions.
