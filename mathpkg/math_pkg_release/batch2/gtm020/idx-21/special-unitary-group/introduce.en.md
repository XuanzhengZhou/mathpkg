The special unitary group $SU(k)$ is the subgroup of $U(k)$ consisting of unitary matrices with determinant $1$. It is the connected compact Lie group underlying many important fibre bundles and plays a fundamental role in the study of complex vector bundles with a Hermitian metric. The group $SU(k)$ is simply connected and has dimension $k^2 - 1$.
