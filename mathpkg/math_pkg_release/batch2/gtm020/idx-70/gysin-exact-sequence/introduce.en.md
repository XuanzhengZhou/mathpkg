The Gysin exact sequence is a long exact sequence that relates the cohomology of the base space $B$ to the cohomology of the total space of nonzero vectors $E_0$ of a real vector bundle $\xi^n$. The map $H^i(B) \to H^{i+n}(B)$ in the sequence is multiplication by the Euler class $e(\xi)$, providing a fundamental link between the Euler class and the cohomology of sphere bundles.
