For an $n$-dimensional real vector bundle $\xi^n$ there is the following exact sequence of cohomology groups where the coefficients are in $Z_2$ in general or $Z$ for oriented bundles.

$$\cdots \rightarrow H^i(B) \xrightarrow{\text{mult. by } e(\xi)} H^{i+n}(B) \xrightarrow{p^*} H^{i+n}(E_0) \xrightarrow{\psi} H^{i+1}(B) \rightarrow \cdots$$
