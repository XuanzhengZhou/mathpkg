This theorem states that the Euler class is multiplicative with respect to Whitney sums of vector bundles. That is, for two real vector bundles $\xi$ and $\eta$, the Euler class of their direct sum equals the cup product of their individual Euler classes. This is the Whitney sum formula for the Euler class, which is a fundamental property shared by all characteristic classes.
