Let $\xi$ and $\xi'$ be two vector bundles over $X$ with trivializations $t$ and $t'$ over $A$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $(\xi/t) \oplus (\xi'/t')$ and $(\xi \oplus \xi')/(t \oplus t')$ are isomorphic over $X/A$.
  \item $(\xi/t) \otimes (\xi'/t')$ and $(\xi \otimes \xi')/(t \otimes t')$ are isomorphic over $X/A$.
\end{enumerate}
