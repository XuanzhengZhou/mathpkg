This proposition establishes the functorial nature of the collapsing construction. Given a morphism between two bundles equipped with compatible trivializations over subsets, there is a uniquely induced morphism between the corresponding collapsed bundles over the quotient spaces. When the original morphism is an isomorphism and respects the subspaces, the induced morphism is also an isomorphism. This functoriality is essential for defining the relative K-theory groups as a cofunctor on CW-pairs.
