Let $t$ be a trivialization over $A$ of a vector bundle $\xi$ over $X$. The \textbf{collapsing} of $\xi$ with respect to the trivialization $t$ is a triple $(\xi/t, u, r)$, where:
\begin{itemize}
  \item $\xi/t$ is a vector bundle over $X/A$,
  \item $(u, p_A): \xi \rightarrow \xi/t$ is a vector bundle morphism, and
  \item $r: (\xi/t)_* \rightarrow F^n$ is an isomorphism.
\end{itemize}
It is assumed that the restriction of $u$ to $\xi_x \rightarrow (\xi/t)_{p_A(x)}$ is a linear isomorphism and that $r: (\xi/t)_* \rightarrow F^n$ is an isomorphism such that $t$ equals $ru$ restricted to $E(\xi|A)$.
