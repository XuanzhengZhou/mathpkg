This proposition establishes homotopy invariance for the collapsing construction with respect to the choice of trivialization. If two trivializations of a bundle over a subset $A$ are connected by a continuous one-parameter family (a homotopy), then the resulting collapsed bundles are isomorphic. This ensures that the collapsing construction is well-defined at the level of homotopy classes of trivializations, a crucial property for the definition of relative K-theory as a homotopy functor.
