The symplectic group $Sp(n)$ acts naturally on the quaternionic vector space $\mathbf{H}^n$, defining an element in the quaternionic representation ring $RSp(Sp(n))$. Restricting scalars from $\mathbf{H}$ to $\mathbf{C}$ yields a complex representation of $Sp(n)$, and the exterior powers of this complex representation generate the entire complex representation ring $RSp(n)$ as a ring.
