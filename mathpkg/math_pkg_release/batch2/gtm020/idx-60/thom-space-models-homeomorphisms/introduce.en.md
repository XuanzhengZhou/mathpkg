This proposition establishes that certain natural restriction maps between the disc bundle, sphere bundle, and projective bundle associated to a real vector bundle are homeomorphisms. These identifications induce two alternative models for the Thom space: as the quotient of the sphere bundle of $\xi \oplus \theta^1$ by the base space $B(\xi)$, and as the projective bundle $P(\xi \oplus \theta^1)$. These models are fundamental because they show the Thom space is independent of the choice of Riemannian metric used in its definition.
