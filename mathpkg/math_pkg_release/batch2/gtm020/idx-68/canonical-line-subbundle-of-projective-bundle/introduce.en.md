The canonical line bundle $\lambda_\xi$ over the projective bundle $P\xi$ is the tautological subbundle of the pullback $q^*(\xi)$. Its fibre over a line $L$ consists precisely of the points on that line, making it the universal subbundle that encodes the incidence relation between lines and vectors. The quotient $\sigma_\xi = q^*(\xi)/\lambda_\xi$ completes the short exact sequence, and together these bundles are essential for defining the tautological cohomology class $a_\xi$.
