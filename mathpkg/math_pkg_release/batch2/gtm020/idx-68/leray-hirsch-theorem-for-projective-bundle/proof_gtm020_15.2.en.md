We need only check the hypothesis of Theorem (1.1). For this, we note that $j_b^*(f^*(\gamma_1))$ is the conjugate of the canonical line bundle over $\mathbb{F}P^{n-1}$. Therefore, up to an automorphism of $\mathbb{F}P^{n-1}$, the map $f j_b$ is homotopic to the inclusion $\mathbb{F}P^{n-1} \to \mathbb{F}P^\infty$.

This means that $j_b^*(1), \ldots, j_b^*(a_{\xi}^{n-1})$ is a $K_c$-base of $H^*(\mathbb{F}P^{n-1}, K_c)$. The theorem follows from (1.1).
