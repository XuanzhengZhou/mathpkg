Let $\xi$ be an $n$-dimensional vector bundle, let $P\xi$ be the associated projective bundle, and let $\lambda_\xi$ be the canonical line bundle over $P\xi$. There exists a map $f: E(P\xi) \to \mathbb{F}P^\infty$ classifying $\lambda_\xi$ such that $f^*(\gamma_1) \cong \lambda_\xi$, where $\gamma_1$ is the universal line bundle over $\mathbb{F}P^\infty$.

Let $z \in H^c(\mathbb{F}P^\infty, K_c)$ be the generator. The class $a_{\xi}$ is defined as

$$a_{\xi} = f^*(z) \in H^c(E(P\xi), K_c).$$

Since $f$ is unique up to homotopy, the class $a_{\xi}$ is well defined.
