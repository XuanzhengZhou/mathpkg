The tautological class $a_\xi \in H^c(E(P\xi), K_c)$ is the pullback of the universal cohomology generator $z \in H^c(\mathbb{F}P^\infty, K_c)$ under the classifying map of the canonical line bundle $\lambda_\xi$. It plays a central role in the Leray-Hirsch theorem for projective bundles, providing the distinguished set of generators $1, a_\xi, \ldots, a_\xi^{n-1}$ that make the cohomology of the total space $E(P\xi)$ a free module over the cohomology of the base $B$.
