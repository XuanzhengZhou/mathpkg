For $A = B = \emptyset$, the vertical maps $L(X, \emptyset) \to K(X, \emptyset)$ and $L(Y, \emptyset) \to K(Y, \emptyset)$ are isomorphisms, and the diagram commutes by the naturality of the cup product constructions. The general case follows by reduction to this case using the natural isomorphisms $L(X/A, *) \to L(X, A)$ and $K(X/A, *) \to K(X, A)$.
