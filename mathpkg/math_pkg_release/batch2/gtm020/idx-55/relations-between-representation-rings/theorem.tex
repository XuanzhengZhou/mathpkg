Between $R(G)$ and $RO(G)$ the relations
$$\varepsilon_O \varepsilon_U = 2 \quad \text{and} \quad \varepsilon_U \varepsilon_O = 1 + \psi_{-1}$$
hold, where $\psi_{-1}$ is the $\psi$ operation in the $\lambda$-ring $R(G)$ and refers to the conjugate representation of bundles.
Between $R(G)$ and $RSp(G)$ the relations
$$\varepsilon_{Sp} \varepsilon_U = 2 \quad \text{and} \quad \varepsilon_U \varepsilon_{Sp} = 1 + \psi_{-1}$$
hold, where $\psi_{-1}x$ denotes the conjugate class of $x$.
