The fibre product (or Whitney sum) $\xi_1 \oplus \xi_2$ of two bundles over a common base space $B$ is a new bundle whose total space is the pullback of the two projection maps. Concretely, the total space $E_1 \oplus E_2$ consists of pairs $(x, x')$ lying over the same basepoint. The fibre over any $b \in B$ is naturally the Cartesian product of the individual fibres, which explains the terminology "fibre product."
