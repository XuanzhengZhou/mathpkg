The underlying additive group structure of $\tilde{R}$ is the group completion of the underlying semigroup structure of $R$. We consider $\lambda_t: R \to 1 + \tilde{R}[[t]]^+$, which is the multiplicative group of power series with $1$ as a constant term. This defines a group morphism $\tilde{\lambda}_t: \tilde{R} \to 1 + \tilde{R}[[t]]^+$ such that $\tilde{\lambda}_t \theta = \lambda_t$.
