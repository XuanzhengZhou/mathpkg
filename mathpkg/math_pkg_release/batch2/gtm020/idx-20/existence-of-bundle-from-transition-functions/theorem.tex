Given a space $B$, an open covering $\{V_i\}_{i \in I}$ of $B$, and a system of transition functions $\{g_{i,j}: V_i \cap V_j \to G\}$ on $B$ satisfying the cocycle condition (T1), there exists a principal $G$-bundle $\xi = (X, p, B)$ and an atlas $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ of $\xi$ such that $\{g_{i,j}\}$ are the transition functions of this atlas. Moreover, $\xi$ is unique up to isomorphism over $B$. The associated fibre bundle $\xi[Y]$ with fibre $Y$ also exists and has $\{g_{i,j}\}$ as its transition functions.
