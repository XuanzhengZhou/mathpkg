A \textbf{system of transition functions} on a space $B$ relative to an open covering $\{V_i\}_{i \in I}$ of $B$ is a family of maps

$$g_{i,j}: V_i \cap V_j \to G$$

for each $i, j \in I$ such that the following cocycle condition holds:

$$\text{(T1)} \quad g_{i,k}(b) = g_{i,j}(b) g_{j,k}(b) \quad \text{for each } b \in V_i \cap V_j \cap V_k.$$

From (T1) it follows that for each $b \in V_i$, $g_{i,i}(b) = 1_G$, and for each $b \in V_i \cap V_j$, $g_{i,j}(b) = g_{j,i}(b)^{-1}$.
