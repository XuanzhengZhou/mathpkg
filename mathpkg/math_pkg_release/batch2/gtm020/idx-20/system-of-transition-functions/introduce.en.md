This definition formalizes the notion of a system of transition functions, which is the key data for describing a fibre bundle locally. Given an open covering $\{V_i\}$ of the base space $B$ and a structure group $G$, a system of transition functions is a family of maps $g_{i,j}: V_i \cap V_j \to G$ satisfying the cocycle condition $g_{i,k} = g_{i,j} g_{j,k}$. Such systems arise from atlases of charts of a fibre bundle and conversely can be used to construct bundles.
