By 4(4.2) and 4(6.3) the morphism $f^*(h_i): \theta(f^{-1}(V_i)) \to f^*(\eta)|_{f^{-1}(V_i)}$ is an isomorphism given by

$$f^*(h_i)(b_1, y) = (b_1, h_i(f(b_1), y)).$$

If $h_i(b, g_{i,j}(b)y) = h_j(b, y)$, we have

$$f^*(h_i)(b_1, g_{i,j}(f(b_1))y) = f^*(h_j)(b_1, y),$$

and $\{g_{i,j} \circ f\}$ is the set of transition functions for $f^*(\eta)$.
