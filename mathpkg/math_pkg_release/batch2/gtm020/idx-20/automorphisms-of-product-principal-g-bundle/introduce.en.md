This theorem characterizes the $B$-automorphisms of the product principal $G$-bundle. It states that every $B$-automorphism of the trivial principal bundle $\xi = B \times G$ is given by pointwise left multiplication by a map $g: B \to G$, establishing a bijective correspondence between automorphism groups and mapping spaces. The result is a fundamental building block for the local coordinate description of fibre bundles via transition functions.
