The proof is achieved by reducing the statement to an application of Theorem A.4 (section extension local-to-global) via a mapping space construction. One forms the space of paths or homotopies and uses the local fibration property on each $U_i$ together with the numerable covering to construct a global lifting, extending stepwise using the section extension property.
