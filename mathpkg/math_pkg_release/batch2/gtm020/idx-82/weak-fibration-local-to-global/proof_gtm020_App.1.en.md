The proof is achieved by reducing the statement to an application of Theorem A.4 (section extension local-to-global) via a mapping space construction. One constructs an appropriate bundle of paths or homotopies, translates the weak fibration property into a section extension problem for this associated bundle, and then applies Theorem A.4 to piece together the local weak fibration data over the numerable covering.
