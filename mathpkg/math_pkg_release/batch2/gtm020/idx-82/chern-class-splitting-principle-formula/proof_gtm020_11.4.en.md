By the naturality of the Chern class and Chern character under pullback, and using the fact that $M$ decomposes as a direct sum of one-dimensional $T^r$-modules $M_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus M_m$, the formulas follow from the Whitney sum formula and the exponential property of the Chern character. The splitting map $q_T$ reduces the computation to the case of line bundles associated to the $M_j$.
