This theorem gives the splitting principle formulas for the total Chern class and the Chern character of an associated vector bundle $\alpha[M]$. Pulling back via the splitting map $q_T \colon B(\alpha_T) \to B(\alpha)$, the total Chern class factors as a product of terms $(1 + \tau(M_j))$, and the Chern character becomes a sum of exponentials $e^{\tau(M_j)}$, where each $M_j$ is a one-dimensional $T^r$-module in the decomposition of $M$.
