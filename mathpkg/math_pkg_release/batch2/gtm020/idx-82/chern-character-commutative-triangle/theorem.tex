Let $G$ be a compact, connected Lie group and let $\alpha$ be the universal $G$-bundle. There is a commutative triangle

\[
\begin{CD}
R(G) @>{\alpha}>> H^*(B_G, \mathbf{Q}) \\
@V{\operatorname{ch}}VV @| \\
H^*(B_T, \mathbf{Q})
\end{CD}
\]

where $\operatorname{ch}$ is the Chern character, and $\operatorname{ch}^*$ has the property that $\operatorname{ch}^* M = e^{-\tau(M)}$ for $M$, a complex $T$-module.
