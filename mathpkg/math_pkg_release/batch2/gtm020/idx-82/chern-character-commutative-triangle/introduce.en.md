This theorem establishes a commutative diagram relating the representation ring $R(G)$ of a compact connected Lie group $G$ to the rational cohomology of its classifying space $B_G$. The construction uses the Chern character and the universal $G$-bundle to define a morphism from $R(G)$ to $H^*(B_G, \mathbf{Q})$. The pullback $\operatorname{ch}^*$ sends a complex $T$-module $M$ to $e^{-\tau(M)}$, linking the representation theory of $T$ to the cohomology of $B_T$.
