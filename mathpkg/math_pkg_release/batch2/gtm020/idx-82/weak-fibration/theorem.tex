A bundle $p \colon E \to X$ is a \textit{weak fibration} provided for each homotopy $h_t \colon W \to X$ and map $f \colon W \to E$ with $p f = h_0$, there is a homotopy $f_t \colon W \to E$ such that $p f_t = h_t$. Moreover, it is assumed that $f_0$ and $f$ are homotopic with a homotopy $k_t \colon W \to E$ such that $p k_t = f_0 = f$ for all $t \in [0,1]$ and $k_0 = f_0$, $k_1 = f$.
