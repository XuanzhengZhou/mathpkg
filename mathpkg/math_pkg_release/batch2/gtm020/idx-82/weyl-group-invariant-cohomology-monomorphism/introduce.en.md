This theorem describes the relationship between the cohomology of the classifying space $B_G$ of a compact connected Lie group $G$ and the cohomology of the classifying space $B_T$ of its maximal torus $T$. The pullback along the quotient map is injective, and its image is precisely the subring of $H^*(B_T, \mathbf{Q})$ consisting of elements invariant under the action of the Weyl group. This result mirrors the classical relation between the representation ring $R(G)$ and the Weyl-invariant elements of $R(T)$.
