For the universal $G$-bundle $\alpha$, the morphism
\[
q^*_T \colon H^*(B_G, \mathbf{Q}) \to H^*(B_T, \mathbf{Q})
\]
is a monomorphism, and the image of $q^*_T$ consists of the subgroup of $H^*(B_T, \mathbf{Q})$ of elements left elementwise fixed by the action of the Weyl group on $H^*(B_T, \mathbf{Q})$.
