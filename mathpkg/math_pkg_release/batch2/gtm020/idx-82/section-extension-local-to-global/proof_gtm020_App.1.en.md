For the first statement, if $p \colon E \to X$ has the section extension property and $U \subset X$ is open, a cross section over $A \subset U$ that extends to a halo of $A$ in $U$ also extends to a halo of $A$ in $X$, and thus to a cross section over $X$, which restricts to a cross section over $U$.

For the second statement, one uses the partition of unity subordinate to the numerable covering $\{U_i\}$ to inductively extend the cross section. The key technique is pasting together local extensions using the halo functions provided by the partition of unity, extending step by step over the sets of the covering.
