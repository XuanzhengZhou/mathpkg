The proof is achieved by reducing the statement to an application of Theorem A.4 by a mapping space construction. One constructs the space of fibre-preserving maps and uses the local fibre homotopy inverses to build a global fibre homotopy inverse, extending stepwise over the sets of the numerable covering using the section extension property.
