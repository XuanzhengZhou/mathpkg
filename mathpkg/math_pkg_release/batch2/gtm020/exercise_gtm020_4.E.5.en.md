Let $\xi$ be a $k$-dimensional vector bundle with atlas $\{(V_i, h_i)\}$ and transition functions $\{g_{i,j}\}$, where $g_{i,j}(b) \in O(k)$ for each $b \in V_i \cap V_j$. Prove that $\xi$ has a metric $\beta$ and, moreover, that the metric $\beta$ is unique under the requirement that each $h_i$ is metric preserving.
