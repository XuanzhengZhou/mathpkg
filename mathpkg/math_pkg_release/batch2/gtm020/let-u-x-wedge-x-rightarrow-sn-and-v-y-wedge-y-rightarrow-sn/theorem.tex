$$D_n(u, v) = u_{Y'}^{-1} v^X: \{X, Y\} \rightarrow \{Y', X'\}$$

We have the following commutative diagram:

$$\begin{array}{ccc}
\{X, Y\} & \rightarrow & \{Y', X'\} \\
\downarrow v^X & \rightarrow & \downarrow u_{Y'}$$
\end{array}$$

If $w: Z \wedge Z' \rightarrow S^n$ is a third $n$-duality map, we have

$$D_n(u, w) \{g\} \{f\} = (D_n(u, v) \{f\})(D_n(v, w) \{g\})$$

and $D_n(u, u) \{1\} = \{1\}$.
