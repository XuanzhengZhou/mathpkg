A bundle is the fundamental underlying structure for vector bundles and fibre bundles. It is simply a continuous map $p: E \to B$ viewed as an object. Intuitively, a bundle is a union of fibres $p^{-1}(b)$ parametrized by the base space $B$ and glued together by the topology of the total space $E$.
