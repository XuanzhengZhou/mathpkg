The Stiefel variety parametrizes all orthonormal $k$-frames in $\mathbf{R}^n$. It is a compact space because it is a closed subset of $(S^{n-1})^k$. With each $k$-frame $(v_1, \ldots, v_k)$ one associates the $k$-dimensional subspace $\langle v_1, \ldots, v_k \rangle$, yielding a natural map to the Grassmann variety.
