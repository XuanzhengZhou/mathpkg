Let $\mathcal{P}$ denote the category of paracompact spaces and homotopy classes of maps. Let $\mathbf{ens}$ denote the category of sets and functions. Let $\operatorname{Vect}_k(B)$ denote the set of $B$-isomorphism classes of $k$-dimensional vector bundles over $B$. For a $k$-dimensional bundle $\xi$, denote by $\{\xi\}$ the class in $\operatorname{Vect}_k(B)$. For a map $f: B_1 \to B$, the induced bundle construction defines a function
$$
\operatorname{Vect}_k(f): \operatorname{Vect}_k(B) \to \operatorname{Vect}_k(B_1)
$$
by $\operatorname{Vect}_k(f)\{\xi\} = \{f^*(\xi)\}$. Then $\operatorname{Vect}_k$ is a cofunctor from $\mathcal{P}$ to $\mathbf{ens}$.
