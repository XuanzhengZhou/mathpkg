Define the bundle dimension of a space $B$, denoted $\dim_b B$, to be the infimum of all $k$ such that the inclusion $G_m(\mathbf{R}^{2k}) \to G_m(\mathbf{R}^\infty)$ induces a bijection
$$
[B, G_m(\mathbf{R}^{2k})] \to [B, G_m(\mathbf{R}^\infty)]
$$
for all $m \ge 1$. Relate $\dim_b B$ to other notions of dimension for $B$.
