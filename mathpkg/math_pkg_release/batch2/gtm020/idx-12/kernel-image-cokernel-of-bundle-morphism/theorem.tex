Let $u: \xi \to \eta$ be a morphism of vector bundles over $B$. Define three constructions:

\begin{itemize}
\item $\ker u$ — a subbundle of $\xi$ whose total space is the subspace of $E(\xi)$ consisting of all $x \in E(\xi)$ with $u(x) = 0$ in $E(\eta)$;

\item $\operatorname{im} u$ — a subbundle of $\eta$ whose total space is the subspace $u(E(\xi)) \subset E(\eta)$;

\item $\operatorname{coker} u$ — a quotient bundle of $\eta$ whose total space is obtained from $E(\eta)$ by the equivalence relation identifying elements with the same image under the projection from $\eta$ to the quotient.
\end{itemize}
