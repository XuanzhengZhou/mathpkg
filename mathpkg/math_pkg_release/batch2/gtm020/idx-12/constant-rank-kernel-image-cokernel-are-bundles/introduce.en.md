For a morphism $u: \xi^n \to \eta^m$ of vector bundles whose fibre-wise rank is constant (equal to $k$), the set-theoretic kernel, image, and cokernel are themselves vector bundles over the base $B$. The kernel is a subbundle of $\xi$ of dimension $n-k$, the image is a subbundle of $\eta$ of dimension $k$, and the cokernel is a quotient bundle of $\eta$ of dimension $m-k$. This theorem is fundamental for constructing exact sequences of vector bundles and for studying morphisms via their kernels and cokernels.
