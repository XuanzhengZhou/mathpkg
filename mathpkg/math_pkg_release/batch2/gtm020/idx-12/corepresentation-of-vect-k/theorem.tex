For each paracompact space $B$, define
$$
\phi_B: [B, G_k(F^\infty)] \to \operatorname{Vect}_k(B)
$$
by $\phi_B([g]) = \{g^*(\gamma_k)\}$, where $\gamma_k$ is the canonical $k$-dimensional bundle over $G_k(F^\infty)$. Then the family $\{\phi_B\}$ defines a natural isomorphism of cofunctors
$$
\phi: [-, G_k(F^\infty)] \xrightarrow{\cong} \operatorname{Vect}_k.
$$
In particular, each $\phi_B$ is a bijection: it is surjective by the existence of Gauss maps (5.5) and (5.6), and injective by the third homotopy classification theorem (6.2).
