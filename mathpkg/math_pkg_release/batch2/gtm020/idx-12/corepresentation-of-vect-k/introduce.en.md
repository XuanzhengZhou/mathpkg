The family of maps $\phi_B: [B, G_k(F^\infty)] \to \operatorname{Vect}_k(B)$ sending a homotopy class $[g]$ to the isomorphism class of the pullback $\{g^*(\gamma_k)\}$ defines a natural isomorphism of cofunctors from the homotopy category of paracompact spaces to sets. This means that the infinite Grassmannian $G_k(F^\infty)$ corepresents the cofunctor $\operatorname{Vect}_k$, reducing the classification of $k$-dimensional vector bundles over $B$ to the computation of homotopy classes of maps $[B, G_k(F^\infty)]$. The surjectivity follows from the Gauss map construction, and injectivity from the third homotopy classification theorem.
