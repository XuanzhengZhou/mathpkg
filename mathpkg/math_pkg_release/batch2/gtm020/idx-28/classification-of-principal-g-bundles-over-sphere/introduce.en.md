Principal $G$-bundles over $S^n$ for a path-connected group $G$ are classified by $\pi_{n-1}(G)$. This follows from the classification of regular bundles over suspensions and the identification $S^n \cong S(S^{n-1})$. The characteristic map $c_\xi: S^{n-1} \to G$ encodes the clutching construction, and its homotopy class is a complete invariant of the bundle.
