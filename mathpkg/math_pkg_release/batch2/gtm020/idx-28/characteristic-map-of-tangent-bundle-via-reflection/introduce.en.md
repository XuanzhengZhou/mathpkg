The characteristic map of the tangent bundle of $S^n$ has the property that antipodal points on the equator $S^{n-1}$ map to the same element of $SO(n)$. This symmetry follows from the identity $R(-x, e_{n-1})^2 = R(x, e_{n-1})^2$, which holds because the rotation $R$ depends only on the unoriented axis and twice the angle.
