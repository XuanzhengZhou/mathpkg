Let $f: B_1 \to B$ be a map, and let $\xi$ be a principal $G$-bundle over $S(B)$ with characteristic map $c_\xi: B \to G$. Prove that $c_\xi \circ f$ is a characteristic map of the pullback bundle $S(f)^*(\xi)$ over $S(B_1)$.
