The characteristic map of a regular bundle over a suspension encodes how the bundle is assembled from two trivial pieces (the upper and lower cones) by specifying the transition function along the ``equator'' $B$. For vector bundles, the structure group is $U_F(k)$ or $O(k)$, and the characteristic map takes values in the appropriate group. This is the bundle-theoretic analogue of the clutching construction.
