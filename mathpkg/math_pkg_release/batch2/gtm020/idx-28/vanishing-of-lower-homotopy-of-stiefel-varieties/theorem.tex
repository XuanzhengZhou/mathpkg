Let $V_k(F^{k+m})$ denote the Stiefel variety of $k$-frames in $F^{k+m}$, where $F = \mathbb{R}, \mathbb{C}, \text{ or } \mathbb{H}$ and $c = \dim_{\mathbb{R}} F$. Then

$$\pi_i(V_k(F^{k+m})) = 0 \quad \text{for all } i \leq (m+1)c - 2.$$

Equivalently, the Stiefel variety $V_k(F^{k+m})$ is $((m+1)c - 2)$-connected.
