Let $\xi[F] = (E_F, p_F, S^n)$ be a fibre bundle with structure group $G$ and fibre $F$. Let $c_\xi: S^{n-1} \to G$ be the characteristic map of $\xi$, and for $y_0 \in F$, let $c_F: S^{n-1} \to F$ be the map defined by $c_F(x) = c_\xi(x) y_0$. Then in the homotopy exact sequence of the fibre bundle
$$\cdots \to \pi_n(S^n) \xrightarrow{\partial} \pi_{n-1}(F) \xrightarrow{\alpha} \pi_{n-1}(E_F) \to \cdots$$
the image $\operatorname{im} \partial = \ker \alpha$ is generated by the homotopy class $[c_F] \in \pi_{n-1}(F)$.
