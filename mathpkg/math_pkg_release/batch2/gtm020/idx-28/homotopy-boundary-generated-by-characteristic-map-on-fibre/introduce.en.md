For a fibre bundle $\xi[F]$ over $S^n$ with characteristic map $c_\xi: S^{n-1} \to G$, the image of the boundary homomorphism $\partial: \pi_n(S^n) \to \pi_{n-1}(F)$ in the homotopy exact sequence is generated by the class $[c_F]$, where $c_F(x) = c_\xi(x) y_0$ is the evaluation of the characteristic map on a basepoint $y_0 \in F$. This theorem connects the algebraic topology of the classifying map to the geometric structure of the bundle, and is fundamental for computing homotopy groups of classical groups.
