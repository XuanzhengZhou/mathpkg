The notation $R(b, a)$ specifies the unique rotation in $SO(n)$ that maps the unit vector $a$ to the unit vector $b$ while preserving the orthogonal complement of the two-dimensional plane they span. It is the shortest rotation in the sense of geodesic distance on $SO(n)$, and it is well-defined whenever $a \neq -b$. This rotation is used to construct the characteristic map of the tangent bundle of $S^n$.
