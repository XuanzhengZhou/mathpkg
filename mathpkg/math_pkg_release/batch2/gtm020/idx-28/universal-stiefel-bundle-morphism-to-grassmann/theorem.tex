The bundle morphism $f: \alpha_k^n[F^k] \to \gamma_{k,n}$ defined by

$$f((v_1, \ldots, v_k, y) \bmod U_F(k)) = (\langle v_1, \ldots, v_k \rangle, \; y_1 v_1 + \cdots + y_k v_k)$$

is a well-defined bundle morphism from the associated vector bundle of the universal principal $U_F(k)$-bundle $\alpha_k^n = (V_k(F^n), p, G_k(F^n))$ to the tautological $k$-plane bundle $\gamma_{k,n}$ over the Grassmannian $G_k(F^n)$.
