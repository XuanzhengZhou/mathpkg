Let $\omega_0 = (E_0, p_0, B_0)$ be a universal $G$-bundle in dimensions $\leq n$. Let $c$ denote the characteristic map for $f^*(\omega_0)$, where $f: S^n \to B_0$ is a map classifying a principal $G$-bundle over $S^n$. If $\partial: \pi_n(B_0) \to \pi_{n-1}(G)$ is the boundary homomorphism in the homotopy exact sequence of the universal bundle, prove that $\partial[f] = [c]$.
