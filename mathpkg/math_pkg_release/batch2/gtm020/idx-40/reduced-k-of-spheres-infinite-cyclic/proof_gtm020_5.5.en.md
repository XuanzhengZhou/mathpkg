Since the external cup product induces an isomorphism

$$K(S^2) \otimes K(X) \to K(S^2 \times X)$$

by the Bott periodicity theorem, the sequence of isomorphisms

$$\tilde{K}(S^{2n}) \cong \tilde{K}(S^2)^{\otimes n} \cong \mathbb{Z}$$

follows by induction. The vanishing $\tilde{K}(S^{2n+1}) = 0$ follows from the suspension isomorphism and the periodicity relation.
