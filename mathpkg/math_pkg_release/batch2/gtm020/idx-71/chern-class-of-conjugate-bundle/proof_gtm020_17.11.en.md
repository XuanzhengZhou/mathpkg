The proposition is true for line bundles. Let $f: B_1 \to B$ be a splitting map, where $f^*(\xi) = \lambda_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus \lambda_n$. Then

$$c(f^*(\xi^*)) = c(\lambda_1^*) \cdots c(\lambda_n^*) = (1 - c_1(\lambda_1)) \cdots (1 - c_1(\lambda_n)) = \sum_{0 \leq i} (-1)^i c_i(f^*(\xi))$$

Since $f^*$ is a monomorphism in cohomology, the result follows.
