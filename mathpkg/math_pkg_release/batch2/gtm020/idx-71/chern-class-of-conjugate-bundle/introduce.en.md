This proposition gives the relationship between the Chern classes of a complex vector bundle $\xi$ and its conjugate bundle $\xi^*$. It states that the $i$-th Chern class of the conjugate bundle equals $(-1)^i$ times the $i$-th Chern class of the original bundle. The proof uses the splitting principle, reducing to the case of line bundles where the result is immediate.
