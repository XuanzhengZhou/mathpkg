An open submanifold is an open subset of a manifold that inherits a natural $C^r$-manifold structure by restricting the charts of the ambient manifold. The charts on the open submanifold $N$ are obtained by intersecting the domains of charts on $M$ with $N$ and restricting the coordinate maps. This construction shows that open subsets of manifolds are again manifolds.
