Let $\xi$ be the canonical (real) line bundle on $\mathbb{RP}^{2n-1}$ and $\eta$ the canonical (complex) line bundle on $\mathbb{CP}^{n-1}$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] The bundle $q^*(\eta)$ is isomorphic to $\varepsilon_U(\xi)$, where $q: \mathbb{RP}^{2n-1} \to \mathbb{CP}^{n-1}$ is the standard quotient map.
\item[(2)] The class $w_2(\varepsilon_0(\eta))$ is the mod $2$ reduction of $c_1(\eta)$.
\item[(3)] $c_1(\eta) = e(\varepsilon_0(\eta))$, where $e$ denotes the Euler class.
\end{enumerate}
