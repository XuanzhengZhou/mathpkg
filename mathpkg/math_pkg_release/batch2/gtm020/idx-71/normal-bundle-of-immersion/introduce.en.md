The normal bundle of an immersion $f: M \to N$ is the quotient of the pullback of the tangent bundle of $N$ by the image of the tangent bundle of $M$. It measures how the manifold $M$ sits inside $N$ in the normal directions. The normal bundle always exists as a vector bundle over $M$, and its dimension is $\dim N - \dim M$.
