Let $\xi$ be a vector bundle. If $\xi$ is oriented, then $\mathbb{Z}$-coefficients are used in the cohomology groups; otherwise $\mathbb{Z}_2$ is used. There is an isomorphism
$$\phi(a) = p^*(a) \smile U_\xi$$
of $H^i(B) \to H^{i+n}(E, E_0)$, or equivalently $H^*(B) \to H^*(E, E_0)$, where $U_\xi$ is the Thom class of $\xi$.
