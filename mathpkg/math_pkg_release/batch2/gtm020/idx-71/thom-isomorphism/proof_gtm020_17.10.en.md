The Thom isomorphism is established in Theorem (7.3) of the text. The proof uses the properties of the Thom class $U_\xi$ and the Leray-Hirsch theorem for the pair $(E, E_0)$. For an oriented vector bundle, the Thom class lies in $H^n(E, E_0; \mathbb{Z})$ and the cup product with $p^*(a)$ for $a \in H^i(B)$ gives the isomorphism $H^i(B) \cong H^{i+n}(E, E_0)$. For non-oriented bundles, $\mathbb{Z}_2$ coefficients are used.
