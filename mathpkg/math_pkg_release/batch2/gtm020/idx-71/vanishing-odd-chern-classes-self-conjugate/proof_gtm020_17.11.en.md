We have $c_{2i+1}(\xi) = -c_{2i+1}(\xi^*) = -c_{2i+1}(\xi)$ by Proposition 11.1 and the isomorphism $\xi \cong \xi^*$. Therefore $2c_{2i+1}(\xi) = 0$.
