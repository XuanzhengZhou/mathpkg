A hermitian form on an $F$-module generalizes the classical notion of a hermitian inner product on a complex vector space. When the module carries a $G$-action and the form is $G$-invariant, it provides the essential tool for proving that $G$-modules over compact groups are completely reducible (semisimple). The existence of a $G$-invariant hermitian form is established via averaging over the Haar measure of a compact group.
