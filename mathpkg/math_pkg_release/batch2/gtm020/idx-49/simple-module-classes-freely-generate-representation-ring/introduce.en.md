This fundamental structural result for the representation theory of compact groups states that the representation ring is a free abelian group on the set of isomorphism classes of irreducible representations. The proof uses the orthogonality relations of characters established via Haar measure integration. Combined with the semisimplicity theorem, this yields a complete description: every representation is uniquely a direct sum of irreducibles, and $R_F(G) \cong \mathbf{Z}[\operatorname{Irr}(G)]$ as a free abelian group.
