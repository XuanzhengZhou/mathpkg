The representation ring $R_F(G)$ of a topological group $G$ is the Grothendieck ring completion of the semiring $\mathbf{M}_F(G)$ of isomorphism classes of finite-dimensional $G$-modules over $F = \mathbf{R}$ or $\mathbf{C}$. Elements are formal differences $[M] - [N]$ of $G$-module classes. The ring inherits a $\lambda$-ring structure from exterior powers, making it a fundamental algebraic invariant in representation theory and a key tool for computing characteristic classes of fibre bundles.
