The representation ring $R_F(G)$ of a topological group $G$ is the ring associated with the semiring $\mathbf{M}_F(G)$ of isomorphism classes of $G$-modules over $F$ (where $F = \mathbf{R}$ or $\mathbf{C}$). The elements of $R_F(G)$ are of the form $[M] - [N]$, where $M$ and $N$ are $G$-modules, and there is a natural semiring morphism $\mathbf{M}_F(G) \rightarrow R_F(G)$. Moreover, $R_F(G)$ admits a natural $\lambda$-ring structure induced by the exterior powers $\lambda^i[M] = [\Lambda^i M]$.
