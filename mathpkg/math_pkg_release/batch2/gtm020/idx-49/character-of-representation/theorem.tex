The character of a representation $M$ (a $G$-module), denoted $\chi_M$, is the continuous function $\chi_M: G \to F$ given by $s \mapsto \operatorname{Tr}(s_M)$, where $s_M: M \to M$ is the linear transformation representing $s \in G$. The ring $\operatorname{ch}_F G$ is the subring of $C_F(G)$ generated by the characters $\chi_M$ of all (finite-dimensional) representations $M$ of $G$.
