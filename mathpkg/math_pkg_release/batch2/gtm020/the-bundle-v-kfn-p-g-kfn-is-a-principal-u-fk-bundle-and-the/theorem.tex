A map $SG_k(F^n) \rightarrow G_k(F^n)$ results by “forgetting” orientation. Over each point of $G_k(\mathbf{R}^n)$ are precisely two points representing the two orientations of a subspace, and over each point of $G_k(\mathbf{C}^n)$ is a circle.
