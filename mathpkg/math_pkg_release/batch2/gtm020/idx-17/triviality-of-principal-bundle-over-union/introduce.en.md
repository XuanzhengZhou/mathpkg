Lemma 9.4 from Chapter 4 (General Fibre Bundles) of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. This lemma provides a gluing criterion for the triviality of a principal $G$-bundle over a base space that decomposes as a union of two subsets of the form $A \times [a, c]$ and $A \times [c, b]$. If the bundle is trivial on each piece, then it is globally trivial. This result is a key technical step in proving that homotopy invariance holds for numerable bundles, as it allows one to extend trivializations step by step across the interval factor.
