Corollary 9.7 from Chapter 4 (General Fibre Bundles) of Husemoller's Fibre Bundles. With the notations of Proposition 9.6, this corollary states that a numerable principal $G$-bundle $\xi$ over $B \times I$ is isomorphic to the pullback $r^*(\xi)$, where $r(b, t) = (b, 1)$ is the retraction map. This result is a key intermediate step toward the homotopy invariance theorem for numerable bundles, showing that the bundle over the cylinder is determined by its restriction to one end.
