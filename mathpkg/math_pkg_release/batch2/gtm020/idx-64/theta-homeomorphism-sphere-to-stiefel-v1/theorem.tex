The map $\theta : S^{n-1} = \mathbf{R}P^{n-1}/\mathbf{R}P^{n-2} \to V_1(\mathbf{R}^n) = S^{n-1}$ defined by
\[
\theta(\{x, -x\}) = \theta(\{x, -x\})e_n
\]
where $\theta$ is the map in the commutative diagram
\[
\begin{CD}
\mathbf{R}P^{n-1} @>\theta>> O(n) \\
@VVV @VVV \\
\mathbf{R}P^{n-1}/\mathbf{R}P^{n-2} @>\theta>> O(n)/O(n-1) = V_1(\mathbf{R}^n) = S^{n-1}
\end{CD}
\]
is a homeomorphism.
