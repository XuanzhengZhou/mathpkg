For the Stiefel manifold $V_1(\mathbf{R}^n) = S^{n-1}$, there is a natural identification $\mathbf{R}P^{n-1}/\mathbf{R}P^{n-2} \cong S^{n-1}$. This proposition states that the map $\theta$ induced by the action of $O(n)$ on the unit sphere, when restricted to the quotient of consecutive real projective spaces, yields a homeomorphism onto $V_1(\mathbf{R}^n)$. This identification is fundamental in the study of vector fields on spheres, as it connects the geometry of Stiefel manifolds with quotients of projective spaces.
