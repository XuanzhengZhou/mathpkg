Remark 4.7 from Relative K-Theory. The zero element of the semigroup $L(X,A)$ is represented by any isomorphism of vector bundles defined over the whole space $X$. In particular, the class $[1: \xi \to \xi]$ of the identity isomorphism on any vector bundle $\xi$ is the zero element.
