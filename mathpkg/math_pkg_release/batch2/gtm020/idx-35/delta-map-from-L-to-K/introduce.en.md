This proposition constructs the fundamental homomorphism $\Delta$ from the semigroup $L(X,A)$ of difference isomorphism classes to the relative $K$-theory group $K(X,A)$. A difference isomorphism $\alpha: \xi_0 \to \xi_1$ is mapped to the formal difference of the class of $\xi_0$ (relative to $\alpha$) and the class of $\xi_1$. The definition requires stabilizing by a trivial bundle to ensure well-definedness.
