This proposition shows that in the semigroup $L(X,A)$, the inverse of a difference isomorphism class can be represented by the orthogonal complement (adjoint) map with respect to a fibrewise Riemannian metric. This provides a geometric interpretation of the group structure in $K(X,A)$ via the Riemannian metric on vector bundles.
