Proposition 4.6 from Relative K-Theory. The assignment $(X,A) \mapsto L(X,A)$ together with the induced pullback morphisms $f^*$ forms a cofunctor from the category of pairs of spaces to the category of commutative semigroups. This is immediate from the definition of $L(X,A)$ as a quotient of $S(X,A)$.
