This is immediate from the construction. The equivalence relation on $S(X,A)$ defining $L(X,A)$ is compatible with the semigroup structure, and the induced morphisms $f^*: S(X,A) \to S(Y,B)$ preserve this equivalence relation, yielding well-defined quotient semigroup morphisms $f^*: L(X,A) \to L(Y,B)$. The cofunctoriality follows directly from the functoriality of pullback on $S(X,A)$.
