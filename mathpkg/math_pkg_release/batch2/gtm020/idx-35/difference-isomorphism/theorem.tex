Let $(X, A)$ be a pair of spaces. A \text{difference isomorphism} (over $(X, A)$) is a vector bundle morphism $\alpha: \xi_0 \rightarrow \xi_1$ such that the restriction $\alpha: \xi_0|A \rightarrow \xi_1|A$ is an isomorphism. Two difference isomorphisms $\alpha: \xi_0 \rightarrow \xi_1$ and $\beta: \eta_0 \rightarrow \eta_1$ are isomorphic provided there exist isomorphisms $u_i: \xi_i \rightarrow \eta_i$ (over $X$) for $i = 0, 1$ such that the following diagram commutes:
$$\begin{array}{ccc}
\xi_0|A & \xrightarrow{\alpha} & \xi_1|A \\
u_0 \downarrow & & \downarrow u_1 \\
\eta_0|A & \xrightarrow{\beta} & \eta_1|A
\end{array}$$
