Let $\alpha: U \rightarrow U'$ and $\beta: V \rightarrow V'$ be two linear transformations such that one is an isomorphism. The following sequence is exact:
$$0 \rightarrow U \otimes V \xrightarrow{(\alpha \otimes 1, 1 \otimes \beta)} (U' \otimes V) \oplus (U \otimes V') \xrightarrow{1 \otimes \beta - \alpha \otimes 1} U' \otimes V' \rightarrow 0$$
