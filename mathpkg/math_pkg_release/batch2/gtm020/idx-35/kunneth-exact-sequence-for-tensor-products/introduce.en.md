Lemma 6.1 from Relative K-Theory. This is a special case of the Künneth formula for vector spaces, providing an exact sequence involving tensor products of linear transformations. When one of the maps $\alpha$ or $\beta$ is an isomorphism, this four-term exact sequence is used in the construction of products in $L(X,A)$ and the verification of the $L$-cup product.
