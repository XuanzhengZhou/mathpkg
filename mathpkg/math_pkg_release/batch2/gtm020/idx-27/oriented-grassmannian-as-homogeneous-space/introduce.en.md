The oriented Grassmannian $SG_k(F^n)$ consists of $k$-dimensional subspaces of $F^n$ equipped with an orientation, represented by an equivalence class of orthonormal bases modulo the action of $SU_F(k)$. The group $U_F(n)$ acts transitively on these oriented subspaces, yielding a homogeneous space description $\psi_k^n: U_F(n)/(SU_F(k) \times U_F(n-k)) \cong SG_k(F^n)$. The forgetful map to the unoriented Grassmannian is a double cover for real subspaces and a circle bundle for complex subspaces.
