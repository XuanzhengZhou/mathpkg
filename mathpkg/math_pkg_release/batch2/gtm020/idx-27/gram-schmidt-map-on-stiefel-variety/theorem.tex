Let $\tilde{V}_k(F^n)$ be the subspace of $(F^n)^k$ consisting of linearly independent $k$-tuples in $F^n$. The Gram-Schmidt map $GS: \tilde{V}_k(F^n) \to V_k(F^n)$ is given by

$$GS(y_1, \ldots, y_k) = (v_1, \ldots, v_k)$$

where $v_1$ equals $y_1$ divided by $\|y_1\|$ and for $j > 1$,

$$v_j = \frac{y_j - \sum_{i < j} \langle y_j \mid v_i \rangle v_i}{\left\| y_j - \sum_{i < j} \langle y_j \mid v_i \rangle v_i \right\|}$$

This map is continuous and surjective, producing an orthonormal $k$-frame from any linearly independent $k$-tuple.
