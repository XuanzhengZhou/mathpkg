This result computes the fundamental groups of the classical Lie groups. The symplectic group $Sp(n)$ and $Sp(1) \cong SU(2) \cong S^3$ are simply connected. For the orthogonal groups with $n \geq 3$, the fundamental group is $\mathbf{Z}_2$, established by showing $SO(3) \cong \mathbf{R}P^3$ and then using stability to extend to higher $n$. The identification $SO(3) \cong \mathbf{R}P^3$ arises from the action of $SO(3)$ on the unit sphere: each rotation fixes an axis and rotates by an angle, parametrizing $SO(3)$ as a ball of radius $\pi$ with antipodal boundary identifications.
