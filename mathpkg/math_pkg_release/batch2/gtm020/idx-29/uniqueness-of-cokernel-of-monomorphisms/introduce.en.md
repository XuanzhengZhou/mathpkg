This proposition provides the uniqueness counterpart to the existence result of Proposition 1.1. When the base space dimension is small enough relative to the fibre dimension, any two ways of embedding a trivial line bundle into a given vector bundle yield isomorphic quotient bundles. Together with Proposition 1.1, this establishes that the decomposition of high-dimensional bundles into a lower-dimensional core plus trivial summands is essentially unique.
