This result states that the second homotopy groups of all unitary, special unitary, and symplectic groups vanish identically. The proof uses the stability isomorphisms of Proposition 11.1 to reduce to base cases: $U(1) \cong S^1$ has $\pi_2(S^1) = 0$, and $Sp(1) \cong SU(2) \cong S^3$ has $\pi_2(S^3) = 0$. The result extends to all $n$ via the homotopy exact sequences of the appropriate Stiefel fibrations.
