This result summarizes the connectedness properties of the classical Lie groups. Only the full orthogonal group $O(n)$ is disconnected, having two components (one orientation-preserving, one orientation-reversing). All other classical groups -- $SO(n)$, $U(n)$, $SU(n)$, and $Sp(n)$ -- are path-connected. These calculations follow from the stability properties of Stiefel varieties established in Section 11.
