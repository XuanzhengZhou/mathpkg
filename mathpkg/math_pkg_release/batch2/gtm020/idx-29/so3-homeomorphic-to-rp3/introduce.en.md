This lemma establishes a concrete geometric homeomorphism between $SO(3)$ and $\mathbf{R}P^3$. Every non-identity rotation in $SO(3)$ has a unique axis and angle, allowing one to parametrize the group as the 3-dimensional ball of radius $\pi$ with antipodal identifications on the boundary. This identification is crucial for computing homotopy groups since $\mathbf{R}P^3$ has universal cover $S^3$.
