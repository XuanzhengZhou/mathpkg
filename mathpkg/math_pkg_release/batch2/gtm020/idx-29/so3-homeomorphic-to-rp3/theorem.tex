The special orthogonal group $SO(3)$ is homeomorphic to the real projective 3-space $\mathbf{R}P^3$. The homeomorphism is given by associating to each $u \in SO(3)$ the point $\theta_u x_u$ in the ball $B(0, \pi) \subset \mathbf{R}^3$, where $x_u \in S^2$ is a unit vector fixed by $u$ (the axis of rotation) and $\theta_u$ is the rotation angle in the plane orthogonal to $x_u$, with antipodal points of $\partial B(0, \pi)$ identified.
