For quaternionic Stiefel manifolds, the first non-vanishing homotopy group occurs in dimension $4(n-k)+3$ and is infinite cyclic. The argument parallels the complex case, using $c=4$ in Proposition 11.1. Since $V_1(\mathbf{H}^n) = S^{4n-1}$, the base case gives $\pi_{4n-1}(S^{4n-1}) = \mathbf{Z}$, and stability extends this to all higher $k$.
