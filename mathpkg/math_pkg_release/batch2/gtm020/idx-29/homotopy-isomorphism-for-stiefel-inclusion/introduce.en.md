The inclusion map $j$ embeds the Stiefel manifold $V_k(F^n)$ into $V_{k+1}(F^{n+1})$ by appending the standard basis vector $e_{n+1}$ to each $k$-frame. This map is identified as the fibre inclusion of a natural projection fibration. The homotopy exact sequence of this fibration then implies that $j_*$ induces an isomorphism on homotopy groups $\pi_i$ for $i$ up to $c(n+1)-3$, yielding a powerful stability result for the homotopy of Stiefel varieties.
