This corollary follows from the homeomorphism $\mathbf{R}P^3 \cong SO(3)$ established in Proposition 12.7. The universal covering map $f: S^3 \to \mathbf{R}P^3$ has degree 2, and since $\pi_3(S^3) = \mathbf{Z}$, the induced map on $\pi_3$ factors through the double cover, yielding $\pi_3(SO(3)) = \mathbf{Z}$ generated by the quotient of the identity map on $S^3$.
