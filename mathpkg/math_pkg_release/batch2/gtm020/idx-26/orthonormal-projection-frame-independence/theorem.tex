For each $x \in F^n$ and $(v_1, \ldots, v_k)$, $(v'_1, \ldots, v'_k)$ two orthonormal $k$-frames in $V_k(F^n)$ related by the action of $U_F(k)$, i.e. $(v'_1, \ldots, v'_k) = (v_1, \ldots, v_k)a$ for some $a = (a_{i,j}) \in U_F(k)$, the following relation holds:

$$\sum_{1 \leq i \leq k} (x|v_i)v_i = \sum_{1 \leq i \leq k} (x|v'_i)v'_i$$

In other words, the orthogonal projection of $x$ onto the subspace spanned by the frame is independent of the choice of orthonormal basis within the same $U_F(k)$-orbit.
