**Proof.** This theorem follows from the fact that homogeneous spaces of compact groups define principal bundles. The Stiefel variety $V_k(F^n)$ is homeomorphic to the homogeneous space $U_F(n)/U_F(n-k)$, and the Grassmannian $G_k(F^n)$ is homeomorphic to $U_F(n)/(U_F(k) \times U_F(n-k))$. With these identifications, the projection $p: V_k(F^n) \to G_k(F^n)$ corresponds to the natural quotient map

$$U_F(n)/U_F(n-k) \to U_F(n)/(U_F(k) \times U_F(n-k))$$

which is a principal $U_F(k)$-bundle. Similarly, using the homeomorphisms described in Remark 2.3, the projection to the oriented Grassmannian $SG_k(F^n) \cong U_F(n)/(SU_F(k) \times U_F(n-k))$ is a principal $SU_F(k)$-bundle.
