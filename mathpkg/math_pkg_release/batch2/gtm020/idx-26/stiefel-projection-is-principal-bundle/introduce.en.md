The natural projection from the Stiefel variety $V_k(F^n)$ of orthonormal $k$-frames onto the Grassmannian $G_k(F^n)$ defines a principal $U_F(k)$-bundle. This follows from the general fact that homogeneous spaces of compact groups give rise to principal bundles. Similarly, the projection to the oriented Grassmannian $SG_k(F^n)$ yields a principal $SU_F(k)$-bundle, where the orientation is encoded by the special unitary group action.
