The projection $p: V_k(F^n) \to G_k(F^n)$ is locally trivial, and $(V_k(F^n), p, G_k(F^n))$ is a locally trivial principal bundle with fibre $V_k(F^k) = U_F(k)$. Here $V_k(F^n)$ is the Stiefel variety of orthonormal $k$-frames in $F^n$, and $G_k(F^n)$ is the Grassmann variety of $k$-dimensional subspaces.
