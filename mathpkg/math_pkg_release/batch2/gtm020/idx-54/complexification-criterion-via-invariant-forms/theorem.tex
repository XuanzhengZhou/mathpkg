Let $M$ be a complex $G$-module with a $G$-invariant hermitian form $\eta$. Then $M$ is the complexification of a real $G$-module if and only if $M$ admits a $G$-invariant nondegenerate symmetric form $\beta$, and $M$ is the restriction of a quaternionic $G$-module if and only if $M$ admits a $G$-invariant nondegenerate alternating form.
