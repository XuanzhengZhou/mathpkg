Let $f(t) = 1 + y_1 t + \cdots + y_n t^n$ in $1 + R[[t]]^+$. Then
$$
\sum_{k \geq 0} s_k^n(y_1, \ldots, y_n)(-t)^k = -t\,\frac{(d/dt)f(t)}{f(t)},
$$
where $s_k^n(y_1, \ldots, y_n)$ denotes the $k$-th power sum evaluated on the roots, i.e., $s_k^n(\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_n) = x_1^k + \cdots + x_n^k$, with $\sigma_i$ being the $i$-th elementary symmetric function in $x_1, \ldots, x_n$.
