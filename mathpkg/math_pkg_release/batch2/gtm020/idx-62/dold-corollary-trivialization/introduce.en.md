Dold's Corollary states that a locally trivial bundle over a finite CW-complex is fibre homotopically equivalent to the trivial product bundle, provided there exists a map $u: E \to Y$ whose restriction to each fibre is a homotopy equivalence. The result follows from applying Dold's Theorem (4.3) to the map $(p, u): E \to B \times Y$.
