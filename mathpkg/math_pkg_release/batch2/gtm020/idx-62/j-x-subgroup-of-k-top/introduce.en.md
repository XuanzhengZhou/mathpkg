For a finite CW-complex $X$, the group $J(X)$ of stable fibre homotopy classes is a subgroup of the topological $K$-group $\widetilde{K}_{\text{Top}}(X)$. The latter is defined via classifying spaces $B_{H(n)}$ for the semigroups $H(n)$ of homotopy self-equivalences of $S^{n-1}$, with $B_H$ as their inductive limit. The $H$-space map $B_O \to B_H$ induces the $J$-homomorphism in $K$-theory.
