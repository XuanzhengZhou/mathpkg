The set $J(X)$ of stable fibre homotopy classes of vector bundles over a space $X$ (of finite type) acquires an abelian group structure via the direct sum (Whitney sum) operation. The natural quotient map from the reduced real $K$-theory group $\widetilde{KO}(X)$ to $J(X)$ is a group homomorphism, reflecting the fact that stable fibre homotopy equivalence is coarser than $s$-equivalence.
