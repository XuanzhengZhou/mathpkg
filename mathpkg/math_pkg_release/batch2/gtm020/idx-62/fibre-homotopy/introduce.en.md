Fibre homotopy is a homotopy relation between bundle morphisms that respects the projection maps, requiring $p' f_t = p$ for all times $t$. This defines fibre homotopic maps, fibre homotopy equivalences, and the notion of two bundles having the same fibre homotopy type. The category of bundles modulo fibre homotopy equivalence has fibre homotopy equivalences as its isomorphisms.
