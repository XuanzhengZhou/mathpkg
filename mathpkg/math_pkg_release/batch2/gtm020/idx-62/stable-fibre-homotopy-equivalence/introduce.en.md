Two vector bundles are stably fibre homotopically equivalent if their associated sphere bundles become fibre homotopy equivalent after adding trivial vector bundles (i.e., stabilizing). This defines an equivalence relation on vector bundles. The set of stable fibre homotopy classes is denoted $J(X)$, and the class of a bundle $\xi$ is written $J(\xi)$.
