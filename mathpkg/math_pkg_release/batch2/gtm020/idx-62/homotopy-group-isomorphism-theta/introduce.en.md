The isomorphism $\theta$ identifies the $k$-th homotopy group of the degree-1 component $M_n^1$ of the basepoint-preserving mapping space $\operatorname{Map}_*(S^{n-1}, S^{n-1})$ with the $(n+k-1)$-st homotopy group of the sphere $S^{n-1}$, valid in the range $1 \leq k \leq n-3$. This follows from the homeomorphism $\operatorname{Map}_*(S^{n-1}, S^{n-1}) \cong \Omega^{n-1}(S^{n-1})$ via the loop space functor.
