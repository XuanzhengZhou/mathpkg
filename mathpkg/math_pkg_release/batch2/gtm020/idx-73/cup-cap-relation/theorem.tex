Let $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle: H^*(M, A) \otimes H_*(M, A) \to \mathbf{Z}$ (or $\mathbf{Z}_2$) be the canonical pairing induced by the substitution pairing $C^*(M, A) \otimes C_*(M, A) \to \mathbf{Z}$ (or $\mathbf{Z}_2$). Let $u \in H_n(V, V - K)$, let $c_1 \in H^k(V)$, and let $c_2 \in H^{n-k}(V)$. Then

$$\langle c_1 \cup c_2, u \rangle = \langle c_1, c_2 \cap u \rangle.$$
