If the transition probability function depends on $\tau, t$ only through the time difference, i.e.,
$$P(\tau, x; t, A) = P(t - \tau, x, A),$$
the process has a \textbf{stationary transition mechanism}.

In discrete time, higher order transition probabilities are given recursively by:
$$P(t+1, x, A) = \int_{\Omega_X} P(t, x, dy) P(1, y, A) = \int_{\Omega_X} P(1, x, dy) P(t, y, A).$$
