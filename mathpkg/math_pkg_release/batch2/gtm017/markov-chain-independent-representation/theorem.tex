Let $X_n$, $n = 0, \pm 1, \ldots$, be a finite state stationary Markov chain with all transition probabilities positive. Then $X_n$ can be represented as a one-sided function of independent uniformly distributed (on $[0,1]$) random variables $\xi_n$ and its shifts:
$$X_n = f(\xi_n, \xi_{n-1}, \ldots).$$

A necessary and sufficient condition for a denumerable state stationary Markov chain to have such a representation is that the chain be ergodic and have no periodic states.
