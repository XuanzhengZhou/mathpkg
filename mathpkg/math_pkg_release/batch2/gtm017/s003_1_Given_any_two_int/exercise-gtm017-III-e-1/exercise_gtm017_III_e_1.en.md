Let $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ be $n$ independent, identically distributed random variables with an exponential distribution $F(x) = 1 - e^{-x}$ for $x \geq 0$ and 0 otherwise. Find the distribution function of the sum $X_1 + \cdots + X_n$. The sum of $n$ i.i.d. exponential random variables follows a Gamma (Erlang) distribution with shape $n$ and rate 1.
