For a bounded continuous function $f$ on the unit hypercube, and $n$ i.i.d. uniform points on the hypercube, find the mean and variance of $\frac{1}{n} \sum f(X^{(i)})$. The mean is $\int f$ (the integral over the hypercube), and the variance is $\frac{1}{n}\operatorname{Var}(f(X)) = \frac{1}{n}(\int f^2 - (\int f)^2)$.
