Let $X_1, X_2$ have an absolutely continuous joint distribution with density $f(x_1, x_2)$. Let $F_1$ be the marginal CDF of $X_1$ and $F(x_2|x_1)$ the conditional CDF of $X_2$ given $X_1$. Find the joint distribution of $F_1(X_1)$ and $F(X_2|X_1)$. Both transforms produce independent uniform $[0,1]$ random variables.
