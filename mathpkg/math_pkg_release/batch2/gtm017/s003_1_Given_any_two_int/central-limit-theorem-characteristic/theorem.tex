Let $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ be $n$ independent, identically distributed random variables with mean $0$ and variance $1$. Then $\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\sum_{j=1}^{n} X_j$ is asymptotically normally distributed with mean $0$ and variance $1$ as $n \to \infty$. Its characteristic function converges to $e^{-t^2/2}$.
