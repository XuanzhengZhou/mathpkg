Define the product measure via Caratheodory's extension theorem on the field of finite disjoint unions of measurable rectangles. For indicator functions $f = I_{A_1 \times A_2}$, the result holds by definition. Extend by linearity to simple functions. For non-negative $f$, approximate by increasing simple functions and apply monotone convergence to each iterated integral and the product integral. For general integrable $f$, decompose into positive and negative parts.
