Conditional expectation generalizes the notion of averaging over a sub-sigma-field. It is defined via the Radon-Nikodym theorem as the $\mathfrak{B}$-measurable function whose integral over any $\mathfrak{B}$-set matches that of $Y$. This definition is fundamental to martingale theory and filtering, where it represents the best prediction given available information.
