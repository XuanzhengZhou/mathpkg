When a continuous-time process $X_t$ is sampled at $t = k\Delta$, the spectral distribution $F_\Delta$ of $X_{k\Delta}$ is $dF_\Delta(\lambda) = \sum_{j} dF(\lambda + 2j\pi/\Delta)$ for $|\lambda| < \pi/\Delta$. The full spectrum cannot be recovered from discrete samples unless $X_t$ is band-limited.
