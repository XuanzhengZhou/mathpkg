Let $X_n$ be weakly stationary (discrete time) with mean $m$. Then $\lim_n \frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=1}^n X_k = \Delta Z(0) + m$ in mean square, where $\Delta Z(0)$ is the jump of the spectral process at $\lambda = 0$. The limiting time average equals the space average $m$ iff $\Delta Z(0) = 0$ (equivalently $F$ is continuous at $0$).
