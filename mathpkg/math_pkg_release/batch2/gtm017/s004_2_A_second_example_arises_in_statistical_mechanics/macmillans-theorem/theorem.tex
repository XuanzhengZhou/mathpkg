Let $X_n(w)$ be an ergodic stationary process with finite alphabet. Then
$$-\frac{1}{n} \log p_n(X_0(w), \ldots, X_{n-1}(w)) = f_n(w)$$
converges to the entropy $H$ in mean as $n \to \infty$. Equivalently, with probability approaching one, a typical message of length $n$ has probability approximately $e^{-nH}$, and there are roughly $e^{nH}$ such sequences.
