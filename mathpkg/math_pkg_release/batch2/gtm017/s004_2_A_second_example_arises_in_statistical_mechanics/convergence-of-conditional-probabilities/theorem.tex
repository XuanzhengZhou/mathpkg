Let $\mathcal{C}_n$ be increasing sub-$\sigma$-fields with $\mathcal{C}$ generated by $\bigcup_n \mathcal{C}_n$. For any event $A$,
$$P(A \mid \mathcal{C}_n) \to P(A \mid \mathcal{C})$$
almost everywhere as $n \to \infty$. More generally, for bounded measurable $f$,
$$E(f(w) \mid \mathcal{C}_n) \to E(f(w) \mid \mathcal{C})$$ almost everywhere.
