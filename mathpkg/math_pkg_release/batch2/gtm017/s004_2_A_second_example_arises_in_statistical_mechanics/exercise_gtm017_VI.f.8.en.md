Show that the backward diffusion equation can be reduced to $\partial G/\partial\tau + \frac{1}{2} \partial^2 G/\partial\eta^2 + c(\tau,\eta) \partial G/\partial\eta = 0$ via $\eta = \int_0^\xi dy / \sqrt{a(\tau, y)}$.
