The OU transition density satisfies the forward equation $\partial p/\partial t = \beta \partial^2 p/\partial y^2 + \beta \partial(y p)/\partial y$ and backward equation $-\partial p/\partial \tau = \beta \partial^2 p/\partial x^2 - \beta x \partial p/\partial x$. The joint $(B(t), U(t))$ process satisfies a singular two-dimensional forward equation $\partial p/\partial t = \beta \partial^2 p/\partial x^2 + \beta \partial(x p)/\partial x - \partial(x p)/\partial y$.
