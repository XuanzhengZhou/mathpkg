Let $X_t(w)$, $-\infty < t < \infty$, be complex-valued with $E|X_t|^2 < \infty$ and $E X_t \equiv 0$. $X_t$ is \textbf{weakly stationary} (wide-sense stationary) if its covariance $E X_t \overline{X}_\tau = r(t-\tau)$ depends only on the time difference and is continuous. For discrete time, $E X_k \overline{X}_j = r_{k-j}$.
