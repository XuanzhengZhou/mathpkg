Consider the unit square $0 \leq x, y \leq 1$ with uniform measure on it. Let $T(x,y) = (2x, \frac{1}{2}y)$ if $0 \leq x < \frac{1}{2}$ and $T(x,y) = (2x, \frac{1}{2}(y + 1))$ if $\frac{1}{2} \leq x < 1$. Examine the measure-preserving properties of $T$.
