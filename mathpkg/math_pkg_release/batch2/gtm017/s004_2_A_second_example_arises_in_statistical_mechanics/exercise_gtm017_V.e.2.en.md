Let $X$ be a random variable uniformly distributed on $[0,1]$. Consider the process $X_n = X + n\alpha \bmod 1$ where $\alpha$ is an irrational number. Show that the process $X_n$, $n = 0, \pm 1, \ldots$ is strictly stationary. Is it ergodic?
