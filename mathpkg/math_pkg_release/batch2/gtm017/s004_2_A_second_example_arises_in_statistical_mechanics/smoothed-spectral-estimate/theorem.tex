A \textbf{smoothed spectral estimate} is $f_N(\lambda) = \int_{-\pi}^\pi w_N(\lambda - \mu) I_N(\mu) d\mu$ where $w_N \geq 0$, $\int w_N = 1$, $w_N(\mu) \to 0$ for $\mu \neq 0$. Under smoothness of $f$, $f_N$ is asymptotically unbiased with variance $\sigma^2(f_N(\lambda)) \cong \frac{2\pi}{N} f^2(\lambda) \int w_N^2(\alpha) d\alpha$ (for $\lambda \neq 0, \pm\pi$), yielding consistency.
