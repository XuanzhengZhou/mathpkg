Let $V(x,t) = \Phi(x/\sqrt{t}) + \varepsilon t$ with $\varepsilon > 0$ fixed. Given any $\delta > 0$, there is an $n$ (depending on $\delta, \varepsilon$) sufficiently large so that
$$V\left(x, t + \frac{1}{n}\right) > \int V(x - \xi, t) \, dF_n(\xi)$$
in the whole half-plane $t > \delta$, where $F_n(x) = F(\sqrt{n}x)$.
