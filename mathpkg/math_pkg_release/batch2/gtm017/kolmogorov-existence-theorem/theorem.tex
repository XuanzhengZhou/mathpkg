\textbf{Kolmogorov Existence Theorem:} Given a consistent family of finite-dimensional joint distribution functions, there exists a probability space $(\Omega, \mathcal{F}, P)$ and a stochastic process $\{X_t, t \in T\}$ on it having those distribution functions.

The sigma-field $\mathcal{F}$ is generated by sets of the form
$$\{w \mid w(t_1) \leq x_1, \ldots, w(t_k) \leq x_k; t_1, \ldots, t_k \in T\}.$$
