The Karhunen-Loeve coefficients $Z_i$ form an orthonormal system of random variables. This follows from the definition $Z_i = \sqrt{\lambda_i} \int X_t \varphi_i(t) dt$ together with the eigenvalue equation and orthonormality of the eigenfunctions. The orthonormality ensures the KL expansion provides an orthogonal decomposition of the process variance.
