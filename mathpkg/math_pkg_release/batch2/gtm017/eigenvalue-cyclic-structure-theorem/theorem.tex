Let $A$ be an irreducible non-negative matrix with $k$ roots of the characteristic equation of maximal absolute value. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item All $k$ maximal roots are simple and of the form $\lambda e^{2\pi i j/k}$, $j = 0, 1, \ldots, k-1$.
\item The set of all eigenvalues is invariant under rotation by $2\pi/k$ but not through any smaller angle.
\item By appropriate relabeling, $A$ can be put in the cyclic block form
$$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & A_{12} & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
0 & 0 & A_{23} & \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & A_{k-1,k} \\
A_{k1} & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0
\end{bmatrix}.$$
\end{enumerate}
