Let $F_n$ be a sequence of distribution functions with corresponding characteristic functions $\varphi_n$. If $F_n(x) \to F(x)$ at all continuity points of $F$, where $F$ is a distribution function, then $\varphi_n(t) \to \varphi(t)$ for all $t$, where $\varphi$ is the characteristic function of $F$.

Conversely, if $\varphi_n(t) \to \varphi(t)$ for all $t$ and $\varphi$ is continuous at $0$, then $\varphi$ is a characteristic function and $F_n(x) \to F(x)$ at all continuity points of the corresponding distribution function $F$.
