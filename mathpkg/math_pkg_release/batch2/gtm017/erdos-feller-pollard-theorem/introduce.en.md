The Erdős-Feller-Pollard theorem is a renewal theorem establishing the limit of a sequence satisfying a renewal equation. Applied to Markov chains, it shows that $n$-step return probabilities converge to the reciprocal of the mean recurrence time. The proof uses a clever blocking argument to handle the aperiodic case.
