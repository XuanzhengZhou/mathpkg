MacMillan's theorem (the Shannon-MacMillan-Breiman theorem) is the asymptotic equipartition property for stationary ergodic processes. The probability of a typical $n$-length sequence decays exponentially as $e^{-nH}$, where $H$ is the entropy rate. This generalizes the law of large numbers to the information-theoretic setting and is fundamental to coding theory.
