\textbf{Corollary:} Let $(Y_n)$ be a strictly stationary process with $E(Y_n \mid \mathcal{B}_{-\infty}) = 0$, where $\mathcal{B}_{-\infty} = \bigcap_{k} \mathcal{B}_{-k}$. Assume
$$\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \{E(E(Y_k \mid \mathcal{B}_0)^2)\}^{1/2} < \infty$$
and
$$E Y_0^2 + 2 \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} E(Y_k Y_0) = \sigma^2 > 0.$$
Then $\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}\sigma} \sum_{j=1}^{n} Y_j$ is asymptotically normal with mean zero and variance one.
