This criterion characterizes ergodicity in terms of the asymptotic independence of shifted events: the time-averaged probability of $T^{-j}A \cap B$ converges to the product $P(A)P(B)$. This is equivalent to the mixing property in the mean, and provides a checkable condition for when time averages equal space averages.
