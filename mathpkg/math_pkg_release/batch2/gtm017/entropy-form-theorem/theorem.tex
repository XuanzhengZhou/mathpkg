The entropy $H(p_1, \ldots, p_n)$ of an experiment with $n$ elementary outcomes having probabilities $p_i > 0$, $\sum p_i = 1$, satisfying the axioms of additivity, maximality for equiprobable outcomes, and continuity, has the unique form
$$H(p_1, \ldots, p_n) = -\lambda \sum_{k=1}^{n} p_k \log p_k$$
where $\lambda > 0$ is a positive constant.
