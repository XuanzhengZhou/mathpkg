For an ideal $I$ of a ring $R$, the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $I = J(R)$;
\item[(b)] $I$ is left quasi-regular and $J(R/I) = 0$;
\item[(c)] $I$ is left quasi-regular and $J(R) \subseteq I$;
\item[(d)] ${}_R I$ is superfluous in ${}_R R$ and $J(R/I) = 0$;
\item[(e)] ${}_R I$ is superfluous in ${}_R R$ and $J(R) \subseteq I$.
\end{enumerate}
