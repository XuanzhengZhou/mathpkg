Proposition 15.15 gives eight equivalent characterizations of a local ring in terms of its Jacobson radical. A ring $R$ is local precisely when $J(R)$ is the unique maximal left ideal, $R/J(R)$ is a division ring, or the non-invertible elements form the radical. The equivalence (a) $\Leftrightarrow$ (h) -- that every element or its complement by $1$ is invertible -- is a particularly useful criterion.
