Levitzki's Theorem (15.22) generalizes a consequence of (15.10) and (15.19) from left artinian to left noetherian rings: in a left noetherian ring, every nil one-sided ideal is actually nilpotent. Together with Hopkins' Theorem, this forms the Hopkins-Levitzki theory connecting chain conditions with nilpotence of the radical.
