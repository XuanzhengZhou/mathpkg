If $x \notin I$, then by (15.3) and (3.8) there exists a maximal left ideal $M$ of $R$ with $I \subseteq M$ and $x \notin M$. Hence $x \notin J(R)$. Taking the contrapositive, $J(R) \subseteq I$.
