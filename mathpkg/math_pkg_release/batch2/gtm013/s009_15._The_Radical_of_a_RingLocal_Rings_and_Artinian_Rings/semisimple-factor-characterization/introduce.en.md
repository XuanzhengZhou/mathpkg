Proposition 15.17 characterizes rings $R$ for which $R/J(R)$ is semisimple. This is equivalent to $R/J(R)$ being left artinian, to products of simple (or semisimple) modules being semisimple, and to the identity $\operatorname{Soc} M = r_M(J(R))$ for every left $R$-module $M$. Such rings play a fundamental role in the structure theory of noncommutative rings.
