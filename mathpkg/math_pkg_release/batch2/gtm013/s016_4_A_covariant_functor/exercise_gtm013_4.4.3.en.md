Let $C$ and $D$ be categories. Prove that a covariant functor $F: C \rightarrow D$ is an equivalence iff it is both faithful and full, and each $D \in D$ is isomorphic to some $F(C)$ with $C \in C$. [Note: $F$ is full in case $F(\mathrm{mor}_C(C, C')) = \mathrm{mor}_D(F(C), F(C'))$.]
