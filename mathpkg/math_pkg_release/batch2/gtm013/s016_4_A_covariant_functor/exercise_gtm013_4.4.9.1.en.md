Let $R$ be a finite dimensional algebra over a field $S$. Then $R$ is called a Frobenius algebra if ${}_S R_R$ is a Frobenius bimodule. Prove that if $R$ is a Frobenius algebra over $S$, then $R$ is both left and right self injective.
