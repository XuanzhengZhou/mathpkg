Under a Morita duality defined by a bimodule ${}_R U_S$, the dual functor $M \mapsto M^* = \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, U)$ establishes a precise dictionary between properties of a $U$-reflexive module $M$ and its dual $M^*$. This exercise collects ten fundamental correspondences: finite generation vs finite cogeneration, exactness, simplicity, composition length, socle and radical duality, injectivity vs projectivity, envelopes vs covers, and the generator/cogenerator dichotomy.
