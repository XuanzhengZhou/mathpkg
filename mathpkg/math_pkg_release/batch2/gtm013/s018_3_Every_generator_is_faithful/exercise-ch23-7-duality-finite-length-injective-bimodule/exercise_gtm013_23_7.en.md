Let $R$ and $S$ be rings and let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule with both ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ injective. Let ${}_R \mathcal{FLM}$ and $\mathcal{FLM}_S$ be the full subcategories of ${}_R \mathcal{M}$ and $\mathcal{M}_S$ of modules of finite length. Prove that $H' = \operatorname{Hom}_R(-, U)$ and $H'' = \operatorname{Hom}_S(-, U)$ define a duality $(H', H'')$ between ${}_R \mathcal{FLM}$ and $\mathcal{FLM}_S$.
