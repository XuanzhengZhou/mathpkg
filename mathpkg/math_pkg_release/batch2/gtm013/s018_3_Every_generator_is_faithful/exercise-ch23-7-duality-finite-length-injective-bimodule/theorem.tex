Let $R$ and $S$ be rings and let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule with both ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ injective. Let ${}_R \mathbf{FLM}$ and $\mathbf{FLM}_S$ be the full subcategories of ${}_R \mathbf{M}$ and $\mathbf{M}_S$ of modules of finite length. Then

$$H' = \operatorname{Hom}_R(-, U) \quad \text{and} \quad H'' = \operatorname{Hom}_S(-, U)$$

define a duality $(H', H'')$ between ${}_R \mathbf{FLM}$ and $\mathbf{FLM}_S$.
