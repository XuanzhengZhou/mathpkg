When a bimodule ${}_R U_S$ is injective on both sides, the Hom functors into $U$ define a duality between the categories of left $R$-modules and right $S$-modules of finite length. This provides a natural setting for dualities in the context of artinian rings, where every module of finite length is reflexive with respect to an appropriate injective bimodule.
