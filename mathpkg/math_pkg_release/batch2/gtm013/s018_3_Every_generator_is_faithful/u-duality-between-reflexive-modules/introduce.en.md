Given a bimodule ${}_R U_S$, the Hom functors $H' = \operatorname{Hom}_R(-, U)$ and $H'' = \operatorname{Hom}_S(-, U)$ are contravariant functors between the categories of left $R$-modules and right $S$-modules. When restricted to the full subcategories of $U$-reflexive modules, these functors become inverse to each other, yielding a duality. This proposition confirms that the abstract framework of dualities introduced in Section 20 is not vacuous — dualities between module categories do exist and are naturally induced by any non-zero bimodule.
