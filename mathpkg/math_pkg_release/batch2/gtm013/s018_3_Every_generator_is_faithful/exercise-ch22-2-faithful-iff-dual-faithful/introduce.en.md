This exercise generalizes Exercise (17.4.1) to the setting of prime rings. It shows that for a finitely generated projective module over a prime ring, faithfulness of the module and its $R$-dual are equivalent properties. The result connects the module-theoretic notion of faithfulness with the dual module in the context of prime rings.
