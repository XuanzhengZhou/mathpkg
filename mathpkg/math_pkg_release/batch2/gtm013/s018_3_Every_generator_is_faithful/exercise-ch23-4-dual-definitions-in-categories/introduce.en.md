In category theory, many fundamental concepts come in dual pairs. This exercise formalizes the duality between: projective and injective objects, superfluous epimorphisms and essential monomorphisms, projective covers and injective envelopes, and generators and cogenerators. Each pair is interchanged when passing to the opposite category or under a contravariant duality.
