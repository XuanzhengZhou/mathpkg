Let $P_R$ be a finitely generated projective right $R$-module, let $P^*$ be its $R$-dual $\operatorname{Hom}_R(P, R)$. The ideal $T = \operatorname{Tr}_R(P)$ is the trace of $P$. Prove that:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(1)] $T^2 = T$; $PT = P$; and $T = \operatorname{Tr}_R(P^*)$.
    \item[(2)] The natural maps $P \otimes_R T \to P_R$ and $T \otimes_R P^* \to P^*$ are isomorphisms.
    \item[(3)] If $e \in R$ is an idempotent and $P \cong eR$, then $T = ReR$.
\end{enumerate}
