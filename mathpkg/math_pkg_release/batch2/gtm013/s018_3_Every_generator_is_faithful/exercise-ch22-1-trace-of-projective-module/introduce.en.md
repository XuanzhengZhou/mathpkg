This exercise explores fundamental properties of the trace ideal $T = \operatorname{Tr}_R(P)$ of a finitely generated projective module $P_R$. Part (1) shows that $T$ is idempotent, preserves $P$ under multiplication, and equals the trace of the dual. Part (2) establishes natural isomorphisms using the dual basis. Part (3) computes the trace explicitly when $P$ is a cyclic projective module $eR$.
