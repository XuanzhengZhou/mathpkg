Let $C$ be a category. For each $A \in C$ and each morphism $f$ in $C$, let $\operatorname{op}(A) = A$ and $\operatorname{op}(f) = f$. Prove that:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(1)] $C^{\operatorname{op}}$ is a category and $C^{\operatorname{opop}} = C$.
    \item[(2)] $\operatorname{op}: C \to C^{\operatorname{op}}$ is a contravariant functor.
    \item[(3)] $(\operatorname{op}, \operatorname{op})$ is a duality between $C$ and $C^{\operatorname{op}}$.
\end{enumerate}
