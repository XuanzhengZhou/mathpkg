This exercise constructs a Morita context from the endomorphism rings of two modules over a common base ring $K$. The bimodules are given by Hom-sets between the modules, and the pairing maps are composition. Part (2) characterizes when this Morita context is strict in terms of similarity of the original modules. Part (3) deduces that similar modules have Morita equivalent endomorphism rings, connecting the abstract notion of similarity with Morita theory.
