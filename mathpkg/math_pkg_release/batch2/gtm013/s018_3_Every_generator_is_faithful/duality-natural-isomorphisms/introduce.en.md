This section establishes the formal framework for studying dualities between full subcategories of module categories. A duality between ${}_R \mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{D}_S$ consists of a pair of contravariant functors that are mutually inverse up to natural isomorphism. The natural isomorphisms $\eta$ and $\zeta$ encode the requirement that applying both functors in succession returns an object isomorphic to the original, with the isomorphisms being natural in the categorical sense. This setup mirrors the definition of equivalences of categories (Section 21) but with contravariant functors replacing covariant ones.
