This exercise analyzes the structure of a Morita context when the pairing map $\theta$ is epic. It shows that epic $\theta$ forces the context to be strict (an isomorphism), and that both bimodules then become finitely generated projective on both sides. Moreover, the maps $\hat{\phi}$ and $\bar{\phi}$ become isomorphisms, and the ring homomorphisms from $S$ to the endomorphism rings are also isomorphisms, establishing a Morita equivalence between $R$ and $S$.
