This exercise shows that von Neumann regularity and left self-injectivity are Morita invariant properties for finitely generated projective modules. If the base ring has these properties, then the endomorphism ring of any finitely generated projective module also has them. The proof uses Morita equivalence to reduce to the free case, then applies (5.9) and Exercise (22.14). The special case $eRe$ for $e^2 = e \in R$ follows because $eRe \cong \operatorname{End}(eR)$.
