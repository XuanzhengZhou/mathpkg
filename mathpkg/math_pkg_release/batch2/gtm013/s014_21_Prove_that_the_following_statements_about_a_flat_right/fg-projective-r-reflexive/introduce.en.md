Every finitely generated projective module $P$ over a ring $R$ is $R$-reflexive (with respect to the bimodule ${}_R R_R$), meaning the evaluation map $P \to P^{**}$ is an isomorphism. Moreover, its $R$-dual $P^* = \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, R)$ is also finitely generated and projective. This generalizes the familiar fact from linear algebra that a finite-dimensional vector space is isomorphic to its double dual.
