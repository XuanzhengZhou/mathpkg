Let $F$ and $G$ be functors (of the same variance) between two categories $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{D}$. We say that $F$ and $G$ are \text{isomorphic} and write

$$F \cong G$$

in case there is a natural isomorphism $\eta: F \to G$. This concept induces an equivalence relation on the class of functors from $\mathcal{C}$ to $\mathcal{D}$.
