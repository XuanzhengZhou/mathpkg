For every triple of modules $({}_R M, N_S, {}_R U_S)$ there is an isomorphism

$$\eta: \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, \operatorname{Hom}_S(N, U)) \to \operatorname{Hom}_S(N, \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, U)),$$

defined via

$$[\eta(\gamma)](n): m \mapsto [\gamma(m)](n),$$

that is natural in each of the three variables $M$, $N$, and $U$.
