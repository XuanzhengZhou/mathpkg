Let $(F_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ be an indexed class of additive functors $F_\alpha: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ all of the same variance. Define

$$\bigoplus_A F_\alpha : M \mapsto \bigoplus_A F_\alpha(M), \quad \bigoplus_A F_\alpha : f \mapsto \bigoplus_A F_\alpha(f)$$
$$\prod_A F_\alpha : M \mapsto \prod_A F_\alpha(M), \quad \prod_A F_\alpha : f \mapsto \prod_A F_\alpha(f).$$

Prove that these are additive functors and that $\eta: \bigoplus_A F_\alpha \to \prod_A F_\alpha$ is a natural transformation where $\eta_M: \bigoplus_A F_\alpha(M) \to \prod_A F_\alpha(M)$ is the inclusion map.
