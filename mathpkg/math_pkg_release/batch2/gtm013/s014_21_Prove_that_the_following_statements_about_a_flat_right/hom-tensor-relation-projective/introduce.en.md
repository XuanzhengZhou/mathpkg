This proposition provides a natural map $\eta: \operatorname{Hom}_S(P, U) \otimes_T N \to \operatorname{Hom}_S(P, U \otimes_T N)$ defined by $\eta(\gamma \otimes n)(p) = \gamma(p) \otimes n$. For a general module $P$, this is only a homomorphism, but when $P$ is finitely generated and projective, it becomes an isomorphism. This result is fundamental for understanding how Hom and tensor interact for nice modules.
