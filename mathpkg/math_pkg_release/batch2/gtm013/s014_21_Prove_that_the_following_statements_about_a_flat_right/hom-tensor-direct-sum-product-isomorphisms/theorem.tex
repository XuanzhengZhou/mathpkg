Let $(U_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ be an indexed set of left $R$-modules. Then, as functors from ${}_R\mathcal{M}$ to ${}_{\mathbb{Z}}\mathcal{M}$,

(1) $\operatorname{Hom}_R(\bigoplus_A U_\alpha, -) \cong \prod_A \operatorname{Hom}_R(U_\alpha, -)$;

(2) $\operatorname{Hom}_R(-, \prod_A U_\alpha) \cong \prod_A \operatorname{Hom}_R(-, U_\alpha)$;

(3) $(- \otimes_R (\bigoplus_A U_\alpha)) \cong \bigoplus_A (- \otimes_R U_\alpha)$.
