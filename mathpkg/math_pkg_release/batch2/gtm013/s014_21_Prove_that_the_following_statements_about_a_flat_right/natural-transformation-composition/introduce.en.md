This exercise establishes the basic compositional properties of natural transformations. The "vertical" composite of two natural transformations $\eta: F \to F'$ and $\mu: F' \to F''$ is again a natural transformation (or natural isomorphism) defined componentwise. Moreover, the inverse of a natural isomorphism is itself a natural isomorphism. These facts make natural isomorphisms an equivalence relation on functors.
