This lemma establishes that an additive functor between module categories, when applied to bimodule objects equipped with their canonical bimodule structure, preserves bimodule homomorphisms. Moreover, natural transformations between such functors yield bimodule homomorphisms componentwise. This justifies restricting functors to full subcategories of bimodules and ensures that natural isomorphisms between functors remain valid after such restriction.
