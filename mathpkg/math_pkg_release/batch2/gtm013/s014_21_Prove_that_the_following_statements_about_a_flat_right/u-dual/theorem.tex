Let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule. The $U$-dual functors are

$$(-)^* = \operatorname{Hom}_R(-, {}_R U_S): {}_R\mathcal{M} \to \mathcal{M}_S$$

and

$$(-)^* = \operatorname{Hom}(-, {}_R U_S): \mathcal{M}_S \to {}_R\mathcal{M}.$$

For $M_1 \xrightarrow{f} M_2$ in ${}_R\mathcal{M}$ we have $M_2^* \xrightarrow{f^*} M_1^*$ in $\mathcal{M}_S$ and $M_1^{**} \xrightarrow{f^{**}} M_2^{**}$ in ${}_R\mathcal{M}$. The module $M^*$ is the $U$-dual of $M$ and $f^*$ is the $U$-dual of $f$. Also $M^{**}$ and $f^{**}$ are the double dual of $M$ and $f$, respectively.
