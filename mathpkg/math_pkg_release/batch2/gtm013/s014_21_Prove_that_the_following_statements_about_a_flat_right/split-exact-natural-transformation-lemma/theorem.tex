Let $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{D}$ be full subcategories of the categories of left or right modules over rings $R$ and $S$. Let $F$ and $G$ be additive functors from $\mathcal{C}$ to $\mathcal{D}$ and let $\eta: F \to G$ be a natural transformation. If

$$0 \to M' \xrightarrow{\iota} M \xrightarrow{\pi} M'' \to 0$$

is split exact in $\mathcal{C}$, then $\eta_M$ is monic (epic) if and only if both $\eta_{M'}$ and $\eta_{M''}$ are monic (epic).
