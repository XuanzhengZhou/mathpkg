This lemma describes how natural transformations between additive functors behave on split exact sequences. If $0 \to M' \to M \to M'' \to 0$ is split exact, then the component $\eta_M$ of the natural transformation is monic (respectively, epic) precisely when both $\eta_{M'}$ and $\eta_{M''}$ are monic (respectively, epic). This is a powerful tool for proving properties of functorial maps by reducing to direct summands.
