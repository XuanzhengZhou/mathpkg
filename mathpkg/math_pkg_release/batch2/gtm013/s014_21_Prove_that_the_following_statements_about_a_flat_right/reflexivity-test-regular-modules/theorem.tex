Let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule and let

$$\lambda: R \to \operatorname{End}(U_S)$$

be left multiplication. Then $\sigma_{{}_R R}$ is injective or surjective if and only if $\lambda$ is injective or surjective, respectively. In particular, ${}_R R$ is $U$-torsionless ($U$-reflexive) if and only if $\lambda$ is injective (an isomorphism).
