This proposition gives a practical test for the reflexivity (and torsionlessness) of the regular modules ${}_R R$ and $S_S$ with respect to a bimodule ${}_R U_S$. The evaluation map $\sigma_{{}_R R}$ is injective (resp. surjective) precisely when the left multiplication map $\lambda: R \to \operatorname{End}(U_S)$ is injective (resp. surjective). This connects the algebraic properties of the ring with the dual module structure.
