Let $\mathcal{C}$ be a full subcategory of ${}_R\mathcal{M}$ and let $F: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{C}$ be isomorphic to the identity functor $F \cong 1_{\mathcal{C}}$ via $\eta: 1_{\mathcal{C}} \to F$. Then for all $M, N \in \mathcal{C}$, the restriction

$$F: \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N) \to \operatorname{Hom}_R(F(M), F(N))$$

of $F$ is an isomorphism whose inverse is given by

$$g \mapsto \eta_N^{-1} \circ g \circ \eta_M$$

for each $g \in \operatorname{Hom}_R(F(M), F(N))$.
