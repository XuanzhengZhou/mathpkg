The trace radical $\operatorname{Trad} M$ of a left $R$-module $M$ is defined as the annihilator in $M$ of the direct sum of all simple right $R$-modules. This exercise establishes three key properties: $\operatorname{Trad}_{{}_R R}$ equals the Jacobson radical $J(R)$, for commutative rings $\operatorname{Trad} M$ coincides with the ordinary radical $\operatorname{Rad} M$, and this equality also holds whenever $R/J(R)$ is semisimple.
