For every triple of modules $({}_R M, {}_S W_R, {}_S N)$ there is an isomorphism

$$\phi: \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, \operatorname{Hom}_S(W, N)) \to \operatorname{Hom}_S((W \otimes_R M), N),$$

defined via

$$\phi(\gamma)(w \otimes m) = [\gamma(m)](w),$$

that is natural in each of the three variables $M$, $N$, and $W$.
