This proposition establishes the fundamental Hom-tensor adjunction isomorphism. Given modules $({}_R M, {}_S W_R, {}_S N)$, there is a natural isomorphism $\phi: \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, \operatorname{Hom}_S(W, N)) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_S((W \otimes_R M), N)$ defined by $\phi(\gamma)(w \otimes m) = [\gamma(m)](w)$. Importantly, this isomorphism is natural in all three variables simultaneously, making it one of the most versatile tools in module theory.
