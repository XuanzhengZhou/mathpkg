Let $x_1, \ldots, x_n \in P_R$ and $f_1, \ldots, f_n \in P^* = \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, R)$. Prove that $(x_1, \ldots, x_n), (f_1, \ldots, f_n)$ is a dual basis for $P_R$ if and only if $(f_1, \ldots, f_n), (\sigma_P(x_1), \ldots, \sigma_P(x_n))$ is a dual basis for $P^*$.
