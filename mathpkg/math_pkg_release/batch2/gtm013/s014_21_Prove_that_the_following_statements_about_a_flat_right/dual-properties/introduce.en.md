This proposition establishes three fundamental properties of the $U$-dual. First, the composite $\sigma_{M^*} \circ \sigma_M^*$ is the identity on $M^*$, which implies $\sigma_M^*$ is a split monomorphism. Second, every dual module $M^*$ is automatically $U$-torsionless. Third, reflexivity is inherited by the dual: if $M$ is $U$-reflexive, then so is $M^*$.
