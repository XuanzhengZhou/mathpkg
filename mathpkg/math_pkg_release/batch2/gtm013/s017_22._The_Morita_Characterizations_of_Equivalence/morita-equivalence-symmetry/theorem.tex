For two rings $R$ and $S$, the following are equivalent:

$$R \approx S \quad \text{if and only if} \quad R^{\text{op}} \approx S^{\text{op}}.$$

In particular, if there exists an additive equivalence $F: {}_R\mathbf{M} \to {}_S\mathbf{M}$, then there also exists an additive equivalence $F^{\text{op}}: \mathbf{M}_R \to \mathbf{M}_S$ between the categories of right modules. Thus Morita equivalence is a symmetric relation and left equivalence coincides with right equivalence.
