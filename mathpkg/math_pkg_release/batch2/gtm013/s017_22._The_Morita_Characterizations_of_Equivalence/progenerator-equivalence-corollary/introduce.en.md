Corollary 22.5 provides the explicit construction of a Morita equivalence from a progenerator $P_R$. The equivalence is given by the tensor product with $P$ in one direction and with the dual module $P^{\circledast} = \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, R)$ in the reverse direction. This establishes a concrete equivalence between the categories of left $R$-modules and left $\operatorname{End}(P_R)$-modules, with the tensor and Hom functors being naturally isomorphic.
