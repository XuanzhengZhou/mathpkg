For two rings $R$ and $S$ the following assertions are equivalent:

\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $R \approx S$ (i.e., $R$ and $S$ are Morita equivalent);
\item[(b)] There is a progenerator $P_R$ with $S \cong \operatorname{End}(P_R)$;
\item[(c)] There is a progenerator ${}_R Q$ with $S \cong \operatorname{End}({}_R Q)$.
\end{itemize}
