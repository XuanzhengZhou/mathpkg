Lemma 19.8 establishes the functoriality of the tensor product bifunctor: the tensor product of composition equals the composition of tensor products. This identity, together with $1_M \otimes 1_N = 1_{M \otimes_R N}$, shows that $M \mapsto M \otimes_R N$ (for fixed $N$) and $N \mapsto M \otimes_R N$ (for fixed $M$) are additive functors. The proof is straightforward from the definition on generators $m \otimes n$.
