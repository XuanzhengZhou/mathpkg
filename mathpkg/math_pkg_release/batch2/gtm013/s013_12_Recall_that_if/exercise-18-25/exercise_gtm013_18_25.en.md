A module is faithful iff it cogenerates every projective module. A module is co-faithful in case it generates every injective module. Prove that:
(1) $M$ is co-faithful iff $M$ finitely cogenerates the regular module ${}_R R$.
(2) Every faithful left $R$-module is co-faithful iff $R$ is finitely cogenerated.
(3) Every co-faithful quasi-injective module is injective.
