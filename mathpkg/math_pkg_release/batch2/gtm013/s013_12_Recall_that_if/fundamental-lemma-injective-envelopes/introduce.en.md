The Fundamental Lemma for Injective Envelopes is the dual of the Fundamental Lemma for Projective Covers (17.17). It states that any injective module $Q$ containing a copy of $M$ must contain a copy of the injective envelope $E(M)$ as a direct summand. Moreover, injective envelopes are unique up to isomorphism. This lemma is the cornerstone for developing the theory of injective hulls and their properties.
