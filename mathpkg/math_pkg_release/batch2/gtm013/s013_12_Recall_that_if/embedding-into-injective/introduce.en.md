This fundamental result asserts that the category ${}_R\mathbf{M}$ has enough injectives: every module is a submodule of an injective module. The proof proceeds by first embedding $M$ into $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(R_R, Q)^A$ where $Q = \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$ is the standard divisible cogenerator of $\mathbb{Z}\mathbf{M}$, then using Lemma 18.5. This is the dual statement to "every module is an epimorph of a projective (free) module."
