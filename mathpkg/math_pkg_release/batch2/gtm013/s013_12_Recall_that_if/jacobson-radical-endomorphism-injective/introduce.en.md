Corollary 18.21 computes the Jacobson radical of the endomorphism ring of an injective module with essential socle. The radical equals the right annihilator of the socle, and the quotient $S/J(S)$ is isomorphic to the endomorphism ring of the socle. This is dual to Corollary 17.12 for projective modules, and it plays a crucial role in the structure theory of injective modules, particularly in the decomposition theory of injectives over artinian rings.
