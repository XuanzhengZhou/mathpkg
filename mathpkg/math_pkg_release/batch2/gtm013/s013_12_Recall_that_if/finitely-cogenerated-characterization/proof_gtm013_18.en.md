We need only prove sufficiency (necessity is (10.2)). By (18.16), $M$ is cogenerated by injective envelopes of simple modules. Thus by hypothesis there exists a finite set of simple modules $T_1, \ldots, T_n$ such that $M$ is isomorphic to a submodule of $E(T_1) \oplus \cdots \oplus E(T_n) = E(T_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus T_n)$. By (10.7), this module (whose socle is $T_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus T_n$) is finitely cogenerated; hence $M$ is finitely cogenerated.
