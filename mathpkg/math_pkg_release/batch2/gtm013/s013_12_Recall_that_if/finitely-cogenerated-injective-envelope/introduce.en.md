This proposition characterizes finitely cogenerated modules in terms of their injective envelopes: $M$ is finitely cogenerated precisely when $E(M)$ is a finite direct sum of injective envelopes of simple modules. This provides a structural description of finitely cogenerated modules and their injective hulls, linking the finiteness condition on cogeneration to the decomposition of the injective envelope into indecomposable summands.
