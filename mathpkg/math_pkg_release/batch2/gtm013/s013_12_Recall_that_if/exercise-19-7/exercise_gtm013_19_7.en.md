For each left $R$-module $M$ let $M^*$ be the right $R$-module $\operatorname{Hom}_R({}_R M, {}_R R_R)$. Prove that:
(1) If ${}_R P$ is finitely generated projective, then $P \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} P^*$ is finitely generated projective over $R \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} R^{op}$.
(2) If ${}_R G$ is a generator, then $G \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} G^*$ is an $R \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} R^{op}$-generator.
