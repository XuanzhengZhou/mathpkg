Proposition 19.4 lists the fundamental arithmetic identities that govern computations in the tensor product. These bilinearity relations follow directly from the construction of $M \otimes_R N$ as $F/K$: the generators $(m_1+m_2,n)-(m_1,n)-(m_2,n)$, $(m,n_1+n_2)-(m,n_1)-(m,n_2)$, and $(mr,n)-(m,rn)$ become zero in the quotient. Note that while the pure tensors $m \otimes n$ span $M \otimes_R N$, they do not exhaust it in general, and representations as finite sums are not unique.
