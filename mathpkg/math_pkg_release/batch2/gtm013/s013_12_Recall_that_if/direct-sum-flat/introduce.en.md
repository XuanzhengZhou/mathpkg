Proposition 19.15 shows that flatness is preserved by arbitrary direct sums: a direct sum is flat iff each summand is flat. The proof uses the duality between flatness and injectivity (19.14): $(\bigoplus V_\alpha)^* \cong \prod V_\alpha^*$, and a product is injective iff each factor is injective (18.2). This result contrasts with injective modules, where products are injective but direct sums may not be, and with projective modules, where direct sums are projective but products may not be.
