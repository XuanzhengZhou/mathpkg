Proposition 18.12 collects the fundamental properties of the injective envelope. Part (1) says injective modules are precisely those equal to their own injective envelope. Part (2) states that essential extensions share the same injective envelope. Part (3) says the injective envelope appears as a direct summand of any injective module containing $M$. Part (4) shows that the injective envelope commutes with finite direct sums (and arbitrary direct sums when the sum of envelopes is injective).
