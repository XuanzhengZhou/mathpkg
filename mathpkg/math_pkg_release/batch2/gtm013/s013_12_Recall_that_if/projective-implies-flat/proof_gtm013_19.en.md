Since projective modules are isomorphic to direct summands of free modules (17.2), and flatness is preserved by direct sums (19.15) and direct summands, it suffices to show that $R_R$ is flat. If $f: M \to M$ is a monomorphism in ${}_R\mathbf{M}$, then by (19.6) the diagram
$$0 \to M \xrightarrow{f} M$$
$$R \otimes_R M \xrightarrow{R \otimes_R f} R \otimes_R M$$
commutes with vertical isomorphisms $\mu$ and $\mu$. Thus $R \otimes_R f$ is monic, so $R_R$ is flat.
