This proposition establishes that tensor products are unique up to a unique isomorphism compatible with the structure maps. The proof is a standard categorical argument using the universal property: the unique maps induced by applying the universal property in both directions are mutually inverse isomorphisms. This uniqueness justifies the notation $M \otimes_R N$ and allows us to speak of "the" tensor product.
