Let $F = \bigoplus_{M \times N} \mathbb{Z}(m, n)$ be the free abelian group on $M \times N$, and let $K$ be the subgroup generated by all elements of the form
\begin{align*}
(m_1 + m_2, n) &- (m_1, n) - (m_2, n),\\
(m, n_1 + n_2) &- (m, n_1) - (m, n_2),\\
(mr, n) &- (m, rn).
\end{align*}
Then with $T = F/K$ and $\tau(m, n) = (m, n) + K$, the pair $(T, \tau)$ is a tensor product of $(M_R, {}_R N)$.
