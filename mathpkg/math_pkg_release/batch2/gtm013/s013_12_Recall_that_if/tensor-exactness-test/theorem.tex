Let $f: M \to M$ and $g: M \to M$ be $R$-homomorphisms in ${}_R\mathbf{M}$ and let $U$ be a right $R$-module. Then
$$U \otimes_R M \xrightarrow{U \otimes_R f} U \otimes_R M \xrightarrow{U \otimes_R g} U \otimes_R M$$
is exact if and only if
$$\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, U^*) \xrightarrow{\operatorname{Hom}_R(g, U^*)} \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, U^*) \xrightarrow{\operatorname{Hom}_R(f, U^*)} \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, U^*)$$
is exact, where $U^* = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(U, C)$ for a fixed injective cogenerator $C$ in $\mathbb{Z}\mathbf{M}$.
