A ring $R$ is (left) hereditary in case each of its left ideals is projective. Prove that:
(1) For a ring $R$ the following are equivalent: (a) $R$ is left hereditary; (b) Every factor module of an injective left $R$-module is injective; (c) Every submodule of a projective left $R$-module is projective.
(2) Let $R$ be left artinian with $J = J(R)$ and let $e_1, \ldots, e_n$ be a complete set of pairwise orthogonal primitive idempotents. Prove that the following are equivalent: (a) $R$ is left hereditary; (b) Every maximal left ideal is projective; (c) $Je_i$ is projective ($i=1,\ldots,n$); (d) ${}_R J$ is projective.
