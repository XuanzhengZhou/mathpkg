A right $R$-module $V$ is flat if and only if for every (finitely generated) left ideal $I \leq {}_R R$, the $\mathbb{Z}$-homomorphism
$$V \otimes_R I \to V \otimes_R R$$
induced by the inclusion $I \hookrightarrow R$ is monic; equivalently, the natural map $V \otimes_R I \to VI$ is an isomorphism.
