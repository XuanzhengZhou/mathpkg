For a left $R$-module $E$, the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $E$ is injective;
\item[(b)] $E$ is injective relative to ${}_R R$;
\item[(c)] For every left ideal $I \leq {}_R R$ and every $R$-homomorphism $h: I \to E$, there exists an $x \in E$ such that $h$ is right multiplication by $x$:
$$h(a) = ax \quad (a \in I).$$
\end{enumerate}
