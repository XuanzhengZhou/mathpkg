The tensor functors are right exact. In particular, if
$$0 \to M \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} M \to 0$$
is exact in ${}_R\mathbf{M}$, then for every bimodule ${}_S U_R$,
$$U \otimes_R M \xrightarrow{U \otimes_R f} U \otimes_R M \xrightarrow{U \otimes_R g} U \otimes_R M \to 0$$
is exact in ${}_S\mathbf{M}$.
