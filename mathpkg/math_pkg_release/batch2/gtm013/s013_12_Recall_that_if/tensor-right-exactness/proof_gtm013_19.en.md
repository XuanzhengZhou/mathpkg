The right exactness follows from the left exactness of the Hom functor together with the exactness test (19.12). For any right $R$-module $V$, the sequence $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, V^*) \to \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, V^*) \to \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, V^*)$ is exact by left exactness of $\operatorname{Hom}_R(-, V^*)$. By the exactness test (19.12), this implies exactness of $V \otimes_R M \to V \otimes_R M \to V \otimes_R M \to 0$, establishing right exactness.
