Lemma 19.9 shows that the tensor product distributes over arbitrary direct sums: $(\bigoplus_\alpha M_\alpha) \otimes_R (\bigoplus_\beta N_\beta) \cong \bigoplus_{\alpha,\beta} (M_\alpha \otimes_R N_\beta)$. This is the key structural property that makes tensor functors preserve direct sums (Theorem 19.10). The proof uses the canonical injections and projections of the direct sum decompositions, checking that the maps $(i_\alpha \otimes j_\beta)$ and $(p_\alpha \otimes q_\beta)$ satisfy the direct sum identities.
