Let ${}_R M$, $N_S$, and ${}_R U_S$ be modules, and let $\mu: M \times N \to U$ be $(R, S)$-bilinear. Prove that if for each $M' \leq M$ and each $N' \leq N$

$$l_M r_N(M') = M' \quad \text{and} \quad r_N l_M(N') = N'$$

then for each indexed set $(M_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ of submodules of $M$,

$$r_N\left(\bigcap_A M_\alpha\right) = \sum_A r_N(M_\alpha)$$

and for each indexed set $(N_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ of submodules of $N$,

$$l_M\left(\bigcap_A N_\alpha\right) = \sum_A l_M(N_\alpha).$$
