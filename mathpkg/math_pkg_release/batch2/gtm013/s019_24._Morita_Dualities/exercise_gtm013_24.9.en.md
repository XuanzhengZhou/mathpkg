(1) Let $R$ be a ring with radical $J = J(R)$ and let ${}_R T$ be simple. Prove that if $R/J$ is semisimple and $J^2 = 0$, then $E(T)/T \cong \operatorname{Hom}_R(J, T)$. [Hint: Exercise (20.15).]

(2) P. M. Cohn [61] has shown that there is a division ring $D$ and a division subring $C$ of $D$ such that $D_C$ is finite dimensional and ${}_C D$ is not. Let $R$ be the ring of all matrices

$$r = \begin{bmatrix} d_1 & d_2 \\ 0 & c \end{bmatrix}$$

with $d_1, d_2 \in D$ and $c \in C$. Prove that $R$ is both left and right artinian but that ${}_R \mathbf{FM}$ and $\mathbf{FM}_R$ are not dual.
