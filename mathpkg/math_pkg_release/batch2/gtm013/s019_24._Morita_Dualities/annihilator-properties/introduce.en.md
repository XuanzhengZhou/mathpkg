Proposition 24.3 establishes the basic Galois connection properties of the annihilator maps $l_M$ and $r_N$ induced by an $(R,S)$-bilinear pairing $\mu: M 	imes N 	o U$. These properties mirror those of classical annihilators in ring theory: the maps are order-reversing, form a Galois connection, and satisfy the closure property $r_N l_M r_N = r_N$. The results hold symmetrically for submodules of $N$.
