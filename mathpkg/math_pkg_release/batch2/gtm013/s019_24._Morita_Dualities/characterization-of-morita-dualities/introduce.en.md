Theorem 24.1 provides the fundamental characterization of Morita dualities. It establishes that a bimodule ${}_R U_S$ defines a Morita duality precisely when $U$ is a balanced bimodule and both ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are injective cogenerators. The equivalence of conditions (a)-(c) is the primary tool for verifying whether a given bimodule yields a Morita duality.
