Let $R$ and $S$ be rings. Then for a bimodule ${}_R U_S$ the following statements are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] ${}_R U_S$ defines a Morita duality;
\item[(b)] Every factor module of ${}_R R$, $S_S$, ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ is $U$-reflexive;
\item[(c)] ${}_R U_S$ is a balanced bimodule such that ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are injective cogenerators.
\end{enumerate}
