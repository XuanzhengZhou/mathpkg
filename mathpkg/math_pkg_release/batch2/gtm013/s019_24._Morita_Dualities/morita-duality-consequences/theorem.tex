Let $R$ and $S$ be rings. If there exists a bimodule ${}_R U_S$ that defines a Morita duality, then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] The lattices of two-sided ideals of $R$ and $S$ are isomorphic;
\item[(2)] The centers of $R$ and $S$ are isomorphic;
\item[(3)] Every finitely generated or finitely cogenerated left $R$- (right $S$-) module is reflexive;
\item[(4)] A left $R$- (right $S$-) module is finitely generated projective if and only if its $U$-dual is finitely cogenerated injective.
\end{enumerate}
