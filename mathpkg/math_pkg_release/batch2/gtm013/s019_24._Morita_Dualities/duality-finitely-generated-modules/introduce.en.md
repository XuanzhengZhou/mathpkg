Theorem 24.8 establishes the fundamental connection between Morita dualities and artinian rings. A duality between categories of finitely generated modules exists precisely when a Morita duality exists and the rings are artinian. Combined with Osofsky's Lemma (24.7), this shows that the artinian condition is both necessary and sufficient for a Morita duality to restrict to a duality between finitely generated modules.
