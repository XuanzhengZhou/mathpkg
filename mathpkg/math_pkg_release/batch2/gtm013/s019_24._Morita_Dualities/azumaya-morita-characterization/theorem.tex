Let $R$ and $S$ be rings and let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule. Then

$$\operatorname{Hom}_R(-, U) \quad \text{and} \quad \operatorname{Hom}_S(-, U)$$

is a duality between the categories ${}_R \mathbf{FM}$ and $\mathbf{FM}_S$ if and only if ${}_R R$ and $S_S$ are artinian, ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are finitely generated injective cogenerators, and ${}_R U_S$ is balanced.
