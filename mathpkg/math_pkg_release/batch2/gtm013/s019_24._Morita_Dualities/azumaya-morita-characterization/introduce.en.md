The Azumaya-Morita Corollary gives a clean characterization of when a bimodule induces a duality between categories of finitely generated modules. The conditions are: the rings must be artinian, the bimodule must be finitely generated and an injective cogenerator on both sides, and the bimodule must be balanced. This result is the culmination of the section's theory, synthesizing the earlier characterization theorems.
