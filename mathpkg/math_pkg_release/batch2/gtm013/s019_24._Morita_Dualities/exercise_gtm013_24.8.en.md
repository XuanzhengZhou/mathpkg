Prove that if ${}_R U_S$ defines a Morita duality, then:

(1) $R/J(R)$ and $S/J(S)$ are semisimple. [Hint: The sum of all minimal submodules in $U_S$ is the sum of a finite number of them.]

(2) $\operatorname{Soc}({}_R U) = \operatorname{Soc}(U_S)$. [Hint: By (1) the intersection of the maximal left ideals in $R$ equals the intersection of the maximal two-sided ideals in $R$.]
