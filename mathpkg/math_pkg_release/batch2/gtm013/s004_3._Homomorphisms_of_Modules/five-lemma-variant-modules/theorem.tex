Suppose that the following diagram of $R$-modules and homomorphisms
$$\begin{CD}
A @>f>> B @>g>> C \\
@V\alpha VV @V\beta VV @V\gamma VV \\
A' @>f'>> B' @>g'>> C'
\end{CD}$$
is commutative and has exact rows. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(1)] If $\alpha, \gamma,$ and $f'$ are monic, then $\beta$ is monic;
  \item[(2)] If $\alpha, \gamma,$ and $g$ are epic, then $\beta$ is epic;
  \item[(3)] If $\beta$ is monic, and if $\alpha$ and $g$ are epic, then $\gamma$ is monic;
  \item[(4)] If $\beta$ is epic, and if $f'$ and $\gamma$ are monic, then $\alpha$ is epic.
\end{enumerate}
