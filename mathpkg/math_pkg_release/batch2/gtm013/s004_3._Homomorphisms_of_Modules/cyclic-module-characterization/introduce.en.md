Corollary 3.9 gives a complete classification of cyclic modules: up to isomorphism, every cyclic left $R$-module is a quotient of the left regular module ${}_R R$ by a left ideal. This is a direct consequence of the right multiplication homomorphism and the First Isomorphism Theorem.
