Let $R$ be a ring and let $M' \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} M''$ be a sequence of $R$-module homomorphisms. Prove that the sequence is exact if and only if both $gf = 0$ and for every $R$-module $N$ the induced sequence
$$\text{Hom}_R(M'', N) \to \text{Hom}_R(M, N) \to \text{Hom}_R(M', N)$$
is exact (as a sequence of abelian groups).
