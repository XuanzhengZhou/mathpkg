Corollary 3.8 establishes that an epimorphism $f: M \to N$ with kernel $K$ induces a one-to-one correspondence between submodules of $M$ containing $K$ and all submodules of $N$. This lattice isomorphism is a fundamental tool for relating the submodule structure of a module and its quotients.
