Every module homomorphism has four canonical submodules and quotient modules associated with it. The kernel measures the failure of injectivity, the image captures the set of values attained, the coimage is the quotient by the kernel, and the cokernel is the quotient of the codomain by the image. These constructions are fundamental to the homomorphism theorems of module theory.
