Let $f: M \to N$ be a homomorphism of left $R$-modules. The \textbf{kernel} and \textbf{image} of $f$ are
$$\text{Ker}\, f = \{x \in M \mid f(x) = 0\}, \qquad \text{Im}\, f = \{f(x) \mid x \in M\}.$$
These are submodules of $M$ and $N$, respectively. The \textbf{coimage} and \textbf{cokernel} of $f$ are
$$\text{Coim}\, f = M / \text{Ker}\, f, \qquad \text{Coker}\, f = N / \text{Im}\, f.$$
