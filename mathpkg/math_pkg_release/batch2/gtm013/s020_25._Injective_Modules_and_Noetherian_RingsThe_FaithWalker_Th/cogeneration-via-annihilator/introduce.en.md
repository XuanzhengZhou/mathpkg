This lemma characterizes when a module $M$ cogenerates the quotient module $R/I$ for a left ideal $I$. The condition is that $I$ must be exactly the left annihilator of some subset of $M$, i.e., $I = \ell_R(X)$ for some $X \subseteq M$. This connection between cogeneration and annihilator ideals is a key technical tool used in the study of minimal cogenerators and the characterization of noetherian rings via injective modules.
