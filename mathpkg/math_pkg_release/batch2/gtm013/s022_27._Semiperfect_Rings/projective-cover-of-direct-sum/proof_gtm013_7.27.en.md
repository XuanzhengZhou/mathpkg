Let $q_i: Q_i \rightarrow M_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$) be projective covers. Then it follows inductively from (16.11) and (5.20) that

$$\bigoplus_{i=1}^{n} q_i: Q_1 \oplus \ldots \oplus Q_n \rightarrow M_1 \oplus \ldots \oplus M_n$$

is a projective cover. Thus, letting $q_i = (p \mid P_i)$ we see that the condition is sufficient. The necessity follows from the last statement of (17.17).
