This exercise verifies key properties of basic idempotents in semiperfect rings. The natural map $R e \to R e/J e$ is the projective cover of the simple module $R e/J e$, and the corner ring $e R e$ is precisely the basic ring of $R$. These facts are fundamental to the Morita theory of semiperfect rings developed in Section 27.
