This corollary establishes that semiperfectness is a Morita invariant property. It follows from the categorical characterization (27.6.c) — the existence of projective covers for simple modules — which is preserved under any equivalence of module categories. More concretely, the endomorphism ring of any finitely generated projective module over a semiperfect ring is itself semiperfect, since it can be realized as a corner ring $e S e$ for a Morita equivalent ring $S$.
