For a ring $R$, the following statements are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $R$ is semiperfect;
\item[(b)] $R$ has a complete orthogonal set $e_1, \ldots, e_n$ of idempotents with each $e_i R e_i$ a local ring;
\item[(c)] Every simple left $R$-module has a projective cover;
\item[(d)] Every finitely generated left $R$-module has a projective cover.
\end{itemize}
