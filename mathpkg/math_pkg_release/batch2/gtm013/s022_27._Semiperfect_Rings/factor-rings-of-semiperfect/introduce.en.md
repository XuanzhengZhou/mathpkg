This corollary states that the class of semiperfect rings is closed under taking factor rings. The proof is straightforward from characterization (27.6.b): the existence of a complete orthogonal set of idempotents $e_1, \ldots, e_n$ with each $e_i R e_i$ local is preserved under surjective ring homomorphisms. This property distinguishes semiperfect rings from more restrictive classes such as semisimple rings.
