Prove that the following are equivalent:

(a) $R$ is semiperfect with local basic ring;

(b) $R$ is semiperfect and has (to within isomorphism) only one simple left module;

(c) $R$ is Morita equivalent to a local ring;

(d) $R$ is isomorphic to $\mathbb{M}_n(S)$ for some local ring $S$.
