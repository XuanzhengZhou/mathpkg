Prove that the following are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $R$ is semiperfect with local basic ring;
\item[(b)] $R$ is semiperfect and has (to within isomorphism) only one simple left module;
\item[(c)] $R$ is Morita equivalent to a local ring;
\item[(d)] $R$ is isomorphic to $\mathbb{M}_n(S)$ for some local ring $S$.
\end{itemize}
