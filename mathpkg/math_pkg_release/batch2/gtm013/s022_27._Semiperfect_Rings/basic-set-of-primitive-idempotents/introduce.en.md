Proposition 27.10 characterizes basic sets of primitive idempotents in a semiperfect ring. It establishes a precise correspondence between (a) basic sets of idempotents, (b) isomorphism classes of indecomposable projective left modules, (c) isomorphism classes of simple left modules, and (d) the Wedderburn-Artin block decomposition of $R/J(R)$. The result confirms that in a semiperfect ring, the indecomposable projectives correspond bijectively to the simple modules, just as in a semisimple ring, with the idempotent lifting property providing the bridge.
