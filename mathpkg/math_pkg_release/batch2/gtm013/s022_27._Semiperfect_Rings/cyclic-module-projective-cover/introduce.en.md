This lemma gives a precise structural characterization of cyclic modules that admit projective covers: they are exactly quotients of principal left ideals generated by an idempotent, modulo a subideal of the Jacobson radical. This result is instrumental in linking the lifting of idempotents to the existence of projective covers, forming a bridge between the idempotent structure and the module-theoretic properties of semiperfect rings.
