This exercise asks to show that the idempotent lifting property is inherited by corner rings. Given an ideal $I \subseteq J(R)$ modulo which idempotents lift, one must prove that in the corner ring $f R f$, idempotents lift modulo the restricted ideal $f I f$. The hint suggests assuming $g = f g f$ with $g - g^2 \in f I f$ and constructing an idempotent lift.
