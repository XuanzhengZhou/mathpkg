Let $I$ be an ideal in a ring $R$ and let $g + I$ be an idempotent element of $R/I$. We say that this idempotent can be lifted (to $e$) modulo $I$ in case there is an idempotent $e \in R$ such that $g + I = e + I$. We say that idempotents lift modulo $I$ in case every idempotent in $R/I$ can be lifted to an idempotent in $R$.
