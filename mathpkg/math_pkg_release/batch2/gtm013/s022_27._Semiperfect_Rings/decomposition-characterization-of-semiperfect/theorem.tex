The following statements about a ring $R$ are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $R$ is semiperfect;
\item[(b)] Every projective (left) $R$-module has an indecomposable decomposition that complements maximal direct summands;
\item[(c)] Every finitely generated projective (left) $R$-module has a decomposition that complements direct summands;
\item[(d)] The free (left) $R$-module $R^{(2)}$ has a decomposition that complements direct summands.
\end{itemize}
