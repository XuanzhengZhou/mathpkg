Let $R$ be a commutative integral domain with field of fractions $Q$, and let $M$ be a divisible and torsion-free $R$-module via $\lambda: R \rightarrow \operatorname{End}^l(M)$. Then there exists a unique ring homomorphism $\theta: Q \rightarrow \operatorname{End}^l(M)$ such that $\theta|_R = \lambda$. In particular, $\theta$ induces a $Q$-vector space structure on $M$.
