**Exercise 16 (The Prüfer $p$-group).**

Let $p \in \mathbb{P}$. Consider the abelian group $\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}$. (See Exercise (2.8).)

1. Prove that $\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}$ is divisible (see Exercise (3.15)). Moreover, show that if $n \in \mathbb{Z}$ is not divisible by $p$, then $x \mapsto nx$ defines an isomorphism $\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty} \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}$.
2. Prove that an abelian group $M$ is isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}$ iff $M$ is spanned by a countable set $g_1, g_2, \ldots$ of non zero elements satisfying $pg_1 = 0$ and $pg_{n+1} = g_n$ ($n = 1, 2, \ldots$).
