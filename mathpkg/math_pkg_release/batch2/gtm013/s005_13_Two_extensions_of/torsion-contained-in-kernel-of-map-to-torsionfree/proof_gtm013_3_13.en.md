*Proof.* This is Exercise 14(2) in the source text. Let $x \in T(M)$, so there exists $0 \neq r \in R$ with $rx = 0$. Then $r f(x) = f(rx) = f(0) = 0$. Since $N$ is torsion-free, this implies $f(x) = 0$, so $x \in \operatorname{Ker} f$.
