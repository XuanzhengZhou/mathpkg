An extension of a module $K$ by a module $N$ is defined as a short exact sequence $0 \to K \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} N \to 0$. The module $M$ sits between $K$ (the kernel) and $N$ (the cokernel), with $f$ injective, $g$ surjective, and $\operatorname{Im} f = \operatorname{Ker} g$. This fundamental construction captures the idea of building $M$ from $K$ and $N$.
