Two extensions
$$0 \rightarrow K \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} N \rightarrow 0$$
and
$$0 \rightarrow K \xrightarrow{f'} M' \xrightarrow{g'} N \rightarrow 0$$
are equivalent if there exists a homomorphism $h: M \rightarrow M'$ such that the diagram

\[
\begin{CD}
0 @>>> K @>f>> M @>g>> N @>>> 0 \\
@. @| @VhVV @| @. \\
0 @>>> K @>>f'> M' @>>g'> N @>>> 0
\end{CD}
\]

commutes.
