A module $M$ over a commutative integral domain is torsion-free if no nonzero element is annihilated by any nonzero scalar. Free modules, vector spaces, and subgroups of vector spaces over fields of fractions are all torsion-free. The quotient $M/T(M)$ is always torsion-free.
