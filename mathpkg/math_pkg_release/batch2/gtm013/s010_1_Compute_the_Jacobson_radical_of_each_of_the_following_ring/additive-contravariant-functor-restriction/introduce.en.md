For an additive contravariant functor $T$, the induced map $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M,N) \to \operatorname{Hom}_S(T(N), T(M))$ is an abelian group homomorphism. Note the reversal of $M$ and $N$ in the target, reflecting the contravariance of $T$. This follows from the additivity condition $T(f+g) = T(f) + T(g)$ and ensures that contravariant additive functors also respect the pre-additive structure.
