Let $\mathbf{C}$ be a full subcategory of $R$-modules and let $\mathbf{D}$ be a full subcategory of $S$-modules. A functor $T: \mathbf{C} \to \mathbf{D}$ is \text{additive} in case for each pair $M, N$ of modules in $\mathbf{C}$ and each pair $f, g: M \to N$ of morphisms in $\mathbf{C}$,

$$T(f + g) = T(f) + T(g).$$
