For $f, g \in \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N)$, the additivity of $T$ gives $T(f + g) = T(f) + T(g)$. Since $T$ is covariant, $T(f)$ and $T(g)$ are morphisms in $\operatorname{Hom}_S(T(M), T(N))$, and the equality states precisely that $T|_{M,N}$ preserves the group operation, i.e., is an abelian group homomorphism.
