Let $(M_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ be an indexed class of left $R$-modules and let $I$ be a right ideal of $R$.

(1) Prove that $I(\prod_{A} M_\alpha) \subseteq \prod_{A}(IM_\alpha)$ and that equality holds whenever $I$ is finitely generated.

(2) Show that if $I$ is not finitely generated, then equality need not hold in part (1). [Hint: Try $R = \mathbb{Z}^{\mathbb{N}}$ and $I = \mathbb{Z}^{(\mathbb{N})}$.]
