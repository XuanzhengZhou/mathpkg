Let $_R M_S$ be a bimodule. The left and right actions induce ring homomorphisms

$$\lambda: R \rightarrow \operatorname{End}(M_S) \quad \text{and} \quad \rho: S \rightarrow \operatorname{End}(_R M)$$

such that for $r \in R$, $x \in M$ and $s \in S$

$$\lambda(r): x \mapsto rx \quad \text{and} \quad \rho(s): x \mapsto xs.$$

The module $_R M$ (respectively, $M_S$) is \textit{faithful} iff $\lambda$ (respectively, $\rho$) is injective.

If both $\lambda$ and $\rho$ are surjective, then $_R M_S$ is a \textit{balanced bimodule}.

If $\lambda$ and $\rho$ are isomorphisms, then $_R M_S$ is a \textit{faithfully balanced bimodule}.
