Let $e \in R, f \in S$ be non-zero idempotents and let $_R M_S$ be a bimodule. Then $_{eRe} eM_S$ and $_R Mf_{fSf}$ are bimodules, and

$$\rho: eM \rightarrow \operatorname{Hom}_R(Re, M) \quad \text{and} \quad \lambda: Mf \rightarrow \operatorname{Hom}_S(fS, M)$$

defined via

$$\rho(em)(re) = rem \quad \text{and} \quad \lambda(mf)(fr) = mfr$$

are bimodule isomorphisms.
