Suppose that $_S M$ is a non-zero left $S$-module and that $\phi: R \rightarrow S$ is a ring homomorphism. Then considering $_R M$ with the $R$-structure induced by $\phi$,

$$\operatorname{End}(_S M) \leq \operatorname{End}(_R M) = \operatorname{End}(_{\phi(R)} M) \leq \operatorname{End}_{\mathbb{Z}}(M).$$
