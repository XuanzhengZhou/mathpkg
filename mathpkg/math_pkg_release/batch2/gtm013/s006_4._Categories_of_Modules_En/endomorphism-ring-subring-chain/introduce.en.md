Statement 4.8 is the endomorphism ring analogue of the Hom set subgroup chain (4.2). Given an $S$-module $M$ and a ring homomorphism $\phi: R \to S$, the $S$-module structure pulls back to an $R$-module structure. Every $S$-endomorphism of $M$ remains an $R$-endomorphism, yielding the subring chain $\operatorname{End}(_S M) \leq \operatorname{End}(_R M)$. The intermediate equality reflects that only the image $\phi(R)$ matters, and the final inclusion notes that every module endomorphism is in particular an endomorphism of the underlying abelian group.
