Statement 4.9 is the endomorphism ring analogue of (4.3). When an ideal $I$ annihilates a module $M$, the $R$-module structure factors through $R/I$, and every $R$-endomorphism of $M$ is automatically an $R/I$-endomorphism and vice versa. Thus the endomorphism rings with respect to $R$ and $R/I$ are identical. This is used extensively in the study of modules over quotient rings.
