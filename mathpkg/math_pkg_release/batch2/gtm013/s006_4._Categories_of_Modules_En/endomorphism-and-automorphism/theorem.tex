An $R$-homomorphism of an $R$-module $M$ to itself is called an $R$-\textit{endomorphism} of $M$. The set of all $R$-endomorphisms is

$$\operatorname{End}(_R M) = \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, M).$$

An $R$-isomorphism from $M$ to itself is an $R$-\textit{automorphism} of $M$.

If $M \neq 0$, then $\operatorname{End}(_R M)$ is a subring of the ring of endomorphisms of the abelian group $M$, with addition defined pointwise and multiplication given by composition of maps.
