For a bimodule $_R M_S$, the left and right actions induce canonical ring homomorphisms

$$\lambda: R \rightarrow \operatorname{End}(M_S) \quad \text{and} \quad \rho: S \rightarrow \operatorname{End}(_R M)$$

defined for $r \in R$, $x \in M$, $s \in S$ by

$$\lambda(r): x \mapsto rx \quad \text{and} \quad \rho(s): x \mapsto xs.$$

The left $R$-module $_R M$ is faithful iff $\lambda$ is injective; the right $S$-module $M_S$ is faithful iff $\rho$ is injective.
