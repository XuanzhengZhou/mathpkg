Let $M$ and $N$ be abelian groups and let $R, S,$ and $T$ be rings.

(1) $_R M_S$, $_R N_T$ induces a left $S$-right $T$-bimodule structure on $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N)$ via
$$(sf)(x) = f(xs) \quad \text{and} \quad (ft)(x) = f(x)t;$$

(2) $_S M_R$, $_T N_R$ induces a left $T$-right $S$-bimodule structure on $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N)$ via
$$(tf)(x) = tf(x) \quad \text{and} \quad (fs)(x) = f(sx).$$
