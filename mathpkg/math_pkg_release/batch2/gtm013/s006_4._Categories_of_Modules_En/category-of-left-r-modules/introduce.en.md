The category of left $R$-modules, denoted $_R\mathbf{M}$, is a concrete category whose objects are all left $R$-modules and whose morphisms are $R$-module homomorphisms with the usual composition. This categorical viewpoint allows one to apply general category-theoretic notions (functors, natural transformations, limits, adjunctions) to module theory. The text also mentions the categories of right $R$-modules $\mathbf{M}_R$ and the full subcategories of finitely generated left/right $R$-modules.
