Given a ring $R$, the category of left $R$-modules is the system

$$_R\mathbf{M} = (_R\mathbf{M}, \operatorname{Hom}_R, \circ)$$

where $_R\mathbf{M}$ is the class of all left $R$-modules, $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N)$ is the set of $R$-homomorphisms from $M$ to $N$, and $\circ$ denotes the usual composition of functions.
