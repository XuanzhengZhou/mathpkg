Every abelian group $M$ admits a unique $(\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z})$-bimodule structure $_{\mathbb{Z}} M_{\mathbb{Z}}$. Prove that if $M$ is a finitely generated abelian group, then $_{\mathbb{Z}} M_{\mathbb{Z}}$ is balanced iff $M$ is cyclic.
