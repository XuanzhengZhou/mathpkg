Let $I$ be the ideal of the polynomial ring $\mathbb{Q}[X, Y]$ generated by $\{X^2, XY, Y^2\}$ and let $R = \mathbb{Q}[X, Y] / I$. By Proposition 4.11 the regular bimodule $_R R_R$ is faithfully balanced. Prove that $_R R_R$ has submodules and factor modules that are not balanced.
