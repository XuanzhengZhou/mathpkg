Let $\mathcal{F}$ be the full subcategory of $_\mathbb{Z}\mathbf{M}$ whose object class consists of all torsionfree groups (see Exercise (3.14)) and let $\mathcal{D}$ be the full subcategory of $_\mathbb{Z}\mathbf{M}$ whose object class consists of all divisible groups (see Exercise (3.15)). Prove that $_R V_\mathcal{D}$ is balanced.

[Hint: Let $\sigma \in \operatorname{End}(_R V)$, let $x \in V$, and suppose $x$ and $x\sigma$ are $\mathcal{D}$-linearly independent. Then there is an $r \in R$ with $rx = 0$ and $r(x\sigma) = x$.]
