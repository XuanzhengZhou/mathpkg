Let $_R M$ be a non-zero module. Prove that

(1) As sets of functions, $\operatorname{Cen}(\operatorname{BiEnd}(_R M)) = \operatorname{Cen}(\operatorname{End}(_R M))$.

(2) If $R$ is commutative, then $_R M$ is balanced iff every element in $\operatorname{Cen}(\operatorname{End}(_R M))$ is multiplication by an element of $R$.
