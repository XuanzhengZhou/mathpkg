Let $p \in \mathbb{P}$ be a positive prime. Compute each of the following abelian groups:

$$\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Q}, \mathbb{Z}), \quad \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}, \mathbb{Q}), \quad \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}, \mathbb{Z}).$$

[Hint: Exercises (3.14), (3.15), and (3.16).]
