Let $M$ be a left $R$-module and let $\lambda$ be the canonical ring homomorphism $R \to \operatorname{BiEnd}({}_R M)$.
(1) Prove that the following assertions are equivalent: (a) $\lambda(R)$ is dense in $\operatorname{BiEnd}({}_R M)$; (b) For each $n > 0$, each $R$-submodule of $M^{(n)}$ is a $\operatorname{BiEnd}({}_R M)$ submodule of $M^{(n)}$; (c) For each $n > 0$, each $R$-submodule of $M^{(n)}$ is a $\operatorname{BiEnd}({}_R M^{(n)})$ submodule of $M^{(n)}$.
(2) Prove that if ${}_R M$ is a cogenerator, then $\lambda(R)$ is dense in $\operatorname{BiEnd}({}_R M)$. [Hint: Part (1) and Exercise (8.5).]
