In view of (13.8) it will clearly suffice to prove the equivalence of the "left"-hand version of the conditions.

(a) $\Rightarrow$ (b). By (8.8), $R$ is a left generator.

(b) $\Rightarrow$ (d). Every module is an epimorphic image of a direct sum of copies of any generator. Now apply (9.4).

(d) $\Rightarrow$ (c) $\Rightarrow$ (a). This is by Theorem (9.6). $\square$
