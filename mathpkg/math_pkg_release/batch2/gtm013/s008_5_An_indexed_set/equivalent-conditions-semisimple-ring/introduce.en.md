This proposition gives four equivalent characterizations of semisimple rings: (a) $R$ itself is semisimple as a left module over itself; (b) $R$ possesses a semisimple generator; (c) every short exact sequence of left $R$-modules splits (i.e., the category is semisimple); (d) every left $R$-module is semisimple. The equivalence holds symmetrically for right modules.
