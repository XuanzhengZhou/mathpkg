Let $D$ be a division ring, let $M_D$ be a right $D$-vector space, and let $R$ be a dense subring of $\operatorname{End}(M_D)$. Prove that if $x_1, x_2, x_3, \ldots$ are $D$-linearly independent in $M$, then
$$\ell_R(x_1) > \ell_R(x_1, x_2) > \ell_R(x_1, x_2, x_3) > \cdots.$$
