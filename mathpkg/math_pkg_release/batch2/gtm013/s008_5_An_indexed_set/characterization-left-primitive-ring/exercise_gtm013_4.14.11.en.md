(1) Let $R$ be a prime ring. Prove that if $\operatorname{Soc}_R R \neq 0$, then $R$ is primitive, $\operatorname{Soc}_R R$ is homogeneous, and $\operatorname{Soc}_R R \trianglelefteq R_R$.
(2) Prove that if $R$ is prime and $\operatorname{Soc}_R R$ is non-zero and of finite length, then $R$ is simple artinian.
(3) Prove that if $R$ is prime and $\operatorname{Soc}_R R$ is a simple left ideal, then $R$ is a left primitive ring.
