Let $D$ be a division ring and let $M_D$ be a right $D$-vector space with basis $(x_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$. Let $R$ be a subring of $\operatorname{End}(M_D)$. Prove that $R$ is dense in $\operatorname{End}(M_D)$ iff for each finite subset $F \subseteq A$ and every set $(m_\gamma)_{\gamma \in F}$ of elements of $M$, there is an $r \in R$ with $r(x_\gamma) = m_\gamma$ for all $\gamma \in F$.
