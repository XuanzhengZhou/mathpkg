This proposition collects equivalent characterizations of rings having a simple (left or right) generator. Such rings are precisely the simple left (equivalently right) artinian rings, equivalently those simple rings whose regular module is semisimple. The symmetry between left and right conditions is a notable feature: having a simple left generator implies having a simple right generator and vice versa.
