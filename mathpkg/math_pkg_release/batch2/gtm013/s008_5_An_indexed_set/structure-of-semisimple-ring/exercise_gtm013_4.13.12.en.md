Let $R$ be a finite dimensional algebra over an algebraically closed field $K$. Prove that if $R$ is a simple ring, then $R \cong \mathbb{M}_n(K)$. [Hint: First $R$ is artinian (Exercise (10.7)). If $R \cong \mathbb{M}_l(D)$ where $D$ is a division ring, then $\operatorname{Cen} D = \operatorname{Cen} R = K$ (see Exercises (4.4) and (4.5)). So $D$ is finite dimensional over $K$. Thus $K = D$.]
