Proposition 16.12 describes how the projectivity domain $\mathcal{P}_R^{-1}(U)$ behaves with respect to exact sequences and direct sums. If $U$ is $M$-projective, then $U$ is also projective relative to any submodule $M'$ and quotient $M''$ in an exact sequence with middle term $M$. Furthermore, projectivity relative to each $M_\alpha$ implies projectivity relative to their direct sum.
