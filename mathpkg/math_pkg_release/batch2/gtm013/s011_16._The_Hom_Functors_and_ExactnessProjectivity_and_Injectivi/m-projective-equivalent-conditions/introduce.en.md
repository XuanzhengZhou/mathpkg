Proposition 16.7 gives three equivalent characterizations of $M$-projectivity. Condition (a) is the diagrammatic lifting definition. Condition (b) characterizes $M$-projectivity in terms of exactness of the covariant Hom functor $Hom_R(U, -)$ on short exact sequences with middle term $M$. Condition (c) states that every homomorphism to a quotient $M/K$ lifts through the natural epimorphism $M \to M/K$.
