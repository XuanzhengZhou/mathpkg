(1) Let $F: C \to D$ be an exact covariant functor. Prove that if $M' \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} M''$ is exact in $C$, then

$$F(M') \xrightarrow{F(f)} F(M) \xrightarrow{F(g)} F(M'')$$

is exact in ${}_S\mathcal{M}$. [Hint: See Exercise (3.10).]

(2) $F$ is left exact if and only if for each exact sequence $0 \to M' \to M \to M''$ in $C$ the induced sequence $0 \to F(M') \to F(M) \to F(M'')$ is exact in ${}_S\mathcal{M}$.

(3) State and prove the "right exact" version of part (2).
