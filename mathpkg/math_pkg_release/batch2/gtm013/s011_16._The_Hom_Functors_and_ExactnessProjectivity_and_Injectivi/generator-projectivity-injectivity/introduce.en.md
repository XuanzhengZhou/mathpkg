Corollary 16.14 shows that for a generator $G$, relative injectivity (resp. projectivity) with respect to $G$ implies absolute injectivity (resp. projectivity, assuming finite generation). This is because every module is an epimorphic image of a direct sum of copies of $G$, and Propositions 16.12 and 16.13 transfer the relative properties through epimorphisms and direct sums.
