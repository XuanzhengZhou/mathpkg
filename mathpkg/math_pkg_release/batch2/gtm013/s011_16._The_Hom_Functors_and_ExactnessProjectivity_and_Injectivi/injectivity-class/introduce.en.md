The injectivity class $\mathcal{I}_R(M)$ is the collection of all modules that are $M$-injective. Together with the projectivity class $\mathcal{P}_R(M)$, these classes organize modules according to their behavior with respect to the Hom functors. Proposition 16.10 shows that $\mathcal{P}_R(M)$ is closed under direct sums and $\mathcal{I}_R(M)$ is closed under direct products.
