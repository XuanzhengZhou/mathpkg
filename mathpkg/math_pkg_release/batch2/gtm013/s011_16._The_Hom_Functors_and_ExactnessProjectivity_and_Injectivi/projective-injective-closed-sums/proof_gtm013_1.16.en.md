This follows immediately from Proposition 16.10. A module $U$ is projective if and only if $U \in \mathcal{P}_R(M)$ for all modules $M$, and injective if and only if $U \in \mathcal{I}_R(M)$ for all modules $M$. Since Proposition 16.10 shows these classes are closed under direct sums (for projectivity) and direct products (for injectivity), the corollary follows.
