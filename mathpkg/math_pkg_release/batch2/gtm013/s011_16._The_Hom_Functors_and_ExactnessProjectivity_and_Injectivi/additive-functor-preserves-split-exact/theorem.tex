Let $C$ and $D$ be full subcategories of the categories of left (or right) modules over rings $R$ and $S$. Let $F: C \to D$ be an additive covariant functor and $G: C \to D$ be an additive contravariant functor. If

$$0 \to K \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} N \to 0$$

is split exact in $C$, then both

$$0 \to F(K) \xrightarrow{F(f)} F(M) \xrightarrow{F(g)} F(N) \to 0$$

and

$$0 \to G(N) \xrightarrow{G(g)} G(M) \xrightarrow{G(f)} G(K) \to 0$$

are split exact in $D$. In particular, if $g: M \to N$ is an isomorphism, then both $F(g)$ and $G(g)$ are isomorphisms.
