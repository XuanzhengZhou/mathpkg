Proposition 16.3 shows that additive functors preserve finite direct sums. For a covariant additive functor $F$, the image of a direct sum decomposition $M = \bigoplus M_i$ with its canonical injections and projections is again a direct sum decomposition. A contravariant additive functor $G$ also preserves direct sums, but swaps the roles of injections and projections.
