Let $U$ be a left $R$-module.

(1) If $0 \to M' \xrightarrow{h} M \to M'' \to 0$ is exact in ${}_R\mathcal{M}$ and $U$ is $M$-injective, then $U$ is injective relative to both $M'$ and $M''$.

(2) If $U$ is injective relative to each of the $R$-modules $M_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in A$), then $U$ is $\bigoplus_A M_\alpha$-injective.
