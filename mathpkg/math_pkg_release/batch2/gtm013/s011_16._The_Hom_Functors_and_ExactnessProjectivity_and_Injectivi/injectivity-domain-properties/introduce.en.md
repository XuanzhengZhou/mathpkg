Proposition 16.13 is the dual of Proposition 16.12 for injectivity domains. If $U$ is $M$-injective, then $U$ is also injective relative to any submodule $M'$ and quotient $M''$ appearing in an exact sequence with middle term $M$. Moreover, injectivity relative to each $M_\alpha$ implies injectivity relative to their direct sum.
