Proposition 16.4 shows that the covariant Hom functor $Hom_R(U, -)$ preserves direct products, while the contravariant Hom functor $Hom_R(-, U)$ converts direct sums into direct products. This is a stronger property than merely preserving finite direct sums (as in 16.3); it holds for arbitrary indexed families.
