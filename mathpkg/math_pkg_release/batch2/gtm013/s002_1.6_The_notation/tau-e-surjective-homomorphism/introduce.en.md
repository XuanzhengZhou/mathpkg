When $e$ is a central idempotent, the map $\tau_e(x) = exe$ is a surjective ring homomorphism from $R$ onto the corner ring $eRe$. The kernel is $(1-e)R$, giving an isomorphism $R/(1-e)R \cong eRe$. This is the ring-theoretic analogue of projecting onto a direct factor.
