A ring anti-homomorphism reverses the order of multiplication: $\phi(ab) = \phi(b)\phi(a)$. This is precisely a ring homomorphism from the opposite ring $R^{\text{op}}$ to $S$. Anti-homomorphisms arise naturally when considering transposition of matrices or the involution in quaternion algebras.
