The residue map $r_n: \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}_n$ sending each integer to its least nonnegative remainder modulo $n$ is a surjective ring homomorphism. Its kernel is the principal ideal $n\mathbb{Z}$, yielding the isomorphism $\mathbb{Z}_n \cong \mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}$.
