The identity matrix $I_{\Gamma}$ for $\Gamma \times \Gamma$ matrices over a ring $R$ has Kronecker delta entries: 1 on the diagonal and 0 elsewhere. It is simultaneously row finite and column finite, making it a well-defined multiplicative identity in both $\mathbb{CFM}_{\Gamma}(R)$ and $\mathbb{RFM}_{\Gamma}(R)$.
