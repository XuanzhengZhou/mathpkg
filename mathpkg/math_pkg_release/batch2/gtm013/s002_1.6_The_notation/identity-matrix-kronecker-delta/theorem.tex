Let $I_{\Gamma}$ be the $\Gamma \times \Gamma$-square matrix over $R$

$$I_{\Gamma} = \llbracket \delta_{\alpha\beta} \rrbracket$$

where $\delta_{\alpha\beta}$ denotes the Kronecker delta over $R$: $\delta_{\alpha\beta} = 1$ if $\alpha = \beta$ and $0$ otherwise. Then $I_{\Gamma}$ is both row finite and column finite.
