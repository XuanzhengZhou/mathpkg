Let $R = \prod_A R_\alpha$ be the product of the rings $(R_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$. The canonical injections $\iota_\alpha: R_\alpha \rightarrow R \; (\alpha \in A)$ defined coordinatewise by

$$\pi_\beta \iota_\alpha = \delta_{\alpha \beta} 1_{R_\alpha} \quad (\beta \in A)$$

preserve both operations and are injections, but if $A$ has at least two elements, then the $\iota_\alpha$ are not ring homomorphisms.
