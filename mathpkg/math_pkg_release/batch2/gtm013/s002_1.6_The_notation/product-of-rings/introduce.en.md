The cartesian product of an indexed family of rings $(R_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ inherits a ring structure via coordinatewise addition and multiplication. The additive identity is the tuple of zeros and the multiplicative identity is the tuple of ones from each factor ring. The resulting ring is denoted $\prod_A R_\alpha$.
