A natural transformation provides a way to "translate" between two functors while respecting the categorical structure. It consists of a family of morphisms (one for each object) making the naturality squares commute. Natural transformations are the 2-morphisms in the 2-category of categories and formalize the intuitive notion of a "natural" construction.
