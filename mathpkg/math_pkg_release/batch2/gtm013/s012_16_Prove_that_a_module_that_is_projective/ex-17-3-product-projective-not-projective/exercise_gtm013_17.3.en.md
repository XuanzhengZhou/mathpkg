Prove that the abelian group $\mathbb{Z}^{\mathbb{N}}$ is not projective; hence products of projectives need not be projective. [Hint: Let $K$ consist of those $x \in \mathbb{Z}^{\mathbb{N}}$ such that each power of $2$ divides all but finitely many $\pi_n(x)$. Show that if $\mathbb{Z}^{\mathbb{N}}$ is free, then $K$ is free of uncountable rank. Now consider $K/2K$.]
