**Proof.** (a) $\Rightarrow$ (b). Suppose $f: M \\rightarrow P$ is an epimorphism. If $P$ is projective, then there is a homomorphism $g$ such that $fg = 1_P$, so the epimorphism $f$ splits.

(b) $\Rightarrow$ (c). This follows from the fact that every module is an epimorph of a free module.

(c) $\Rightarrow$ (a). Every free module is projective. Apply the fact that direct sums of projective modules are projective.
