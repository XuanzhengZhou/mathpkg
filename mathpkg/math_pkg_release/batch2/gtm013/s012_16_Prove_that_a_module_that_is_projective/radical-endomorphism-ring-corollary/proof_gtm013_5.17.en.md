**Proof.** This follows directly from (17.11) and the natural identifications:

$$J(\operatorname{End}({}_R P)) = \{a \in \operatorname{End}({}_R P) : \operatorname{Im} a \ll P\} = \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, JP).$$

The second isomorphism is then obtained by applying Schur's Lemma and (17.10).
