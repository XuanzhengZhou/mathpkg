The Jacobson radical respects matrix ring formation and passage to corner rings: the radical of the full matrix ring is the matrix ring over the radical, and the radical of the corner ring $eRe$ is $eJe$. These formulas are fundamental in the structure theory of rings and are derived from the projective module perspective via endomorphism rings.
