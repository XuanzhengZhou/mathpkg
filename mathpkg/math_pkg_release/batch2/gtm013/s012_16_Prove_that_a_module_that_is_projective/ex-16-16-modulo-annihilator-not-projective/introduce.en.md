The property of being projective modulo the annihilator is strictly weaker than actual projectivity — there exist modules that are projective over the quotient ring $R/\ell_R(U)$ but not projective as $R$-modules. This exercise highlights the distinction between relative projectivity (modulo an ideal) and absolute projectivity.
