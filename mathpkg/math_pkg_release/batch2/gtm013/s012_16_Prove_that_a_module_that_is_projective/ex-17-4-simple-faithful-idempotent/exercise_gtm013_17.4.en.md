Let $e \in R$ be idempotent. Prove that:

(1) $Re$ is simple and faithful iff $eR$ is simple and faithful. [Hint: By (17.20), if $J(R) = 0$, then $Re$ is simple iff $eR$ is. If $Re$ is simple and faithful and $0 \neq r \in R$, then there exist $s, t \in R$ with $srte = e$.]

(2) If $Re$ is simple and faithful, then $\operatorname{Soc}({}_R R) = ReR = \operatorname{Soc}(R_R)$.
