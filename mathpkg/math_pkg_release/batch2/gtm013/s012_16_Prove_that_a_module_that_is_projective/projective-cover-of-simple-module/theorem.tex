Let $R$ be a ring with radical $J = J(R)$. Then the following statements about a projective left $R$-module $P$ are equivalent:

(a) $P$ is the projective cover of a simple left $R$-module;

(b) $JP$ is a superfluous maximal submodule of $P$;

(c) $\operatorname{End}({}_R P)$ is a local ring.

Moreover, if these conditions hold, then $P \cong Re$ for some idempotent $e \in R$.
