The following statements about a left $R$-module $G$ are equivalent:

(a) $G$ is a generator;

(b) For every homomorphism $f$ in ${}_R\\mathbf{M}$, if $\\operatorname{Hom}_R(G, f) = 0$, then $f = 0$;

(c) For every $f: {}_R M \\to {}_R N$ in ${}_R\\mathbf{M}$, if $f_*: \\operatorname{Hom}_R(G, M) \\to \\operatorname{Hom}_R(G, N)$ is epic, then $f$ is epic;

(d) A sequence

$$M' \\xrightarrow{f} M \\xrightarrow{g} M''$$

is exact in ${}_R\\mathbf{M}$ if the sequence

$$\\operatorname{Hom}_R(G, M') \\xrightarrow{f_*} \\operatorname{Hom}_R(G, M) \\xrightarrow{g_*} \\operatorname{Hom}_R(G, M'')$$

is exact.
