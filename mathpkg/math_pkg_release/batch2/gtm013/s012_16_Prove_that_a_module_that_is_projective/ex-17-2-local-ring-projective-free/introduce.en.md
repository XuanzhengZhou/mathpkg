Over a local ring, the distinction between projective and free modules disappears for finitely generated modules: every finitely generated projective module is actually free. This is a fundamental result in ring theory, analogous to the fact that over a field every module is free, and it follows from the characterization of projective modules as direct summands of free modules combined with the property that idempotents lift modulo the radical in local rings.
