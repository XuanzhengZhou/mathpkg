Let $F: {}_R \mathbf{M} \to {}_S \mathbf{M}$ be a category equivalence. Then for each $M, M'$ in ${}_R \mathbf{M}$ the restriction of $F$ to $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, M')$ is an abelian group isomorphism

$$F: \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, M') \to \operatorname{Hom}_S(F(M), F(M'))$$

such that $F(f)$ is an epimorphism (monomorphism) in ${}_S \mathbf{M}$ if and only if $f$ is an epimorphism (monomorphism) in ${}_R \mathbf{M}$. Moreover, if $M \neq 0$, then the restriction

$$F: \operatorname{End}({}_R M) \to \operatorname{End}({}_S F(M))$$

is a ring isomorphism.
