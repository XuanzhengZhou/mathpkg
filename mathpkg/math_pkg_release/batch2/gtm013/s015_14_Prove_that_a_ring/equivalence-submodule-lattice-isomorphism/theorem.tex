Let $F: {}_R \mathbf{M} \to {}_S \mathbf{M}$ be a category equivalence and let $M \in {}_R \mathbf{M}$. Then the map

$$\Lambda_M: K \mapsto F(K) \leq F(M)$$

is a lattice isomorphism from the lattice of submodules of $M$ onto the lattice of submodules of $F(M)$. Its inverse is given by

$$\Gamma_M(N) = \operatorname{Im} \phi(i_{N \leq F(M)})$$

where $\phi$ is the adjoint isomorphism of the Fundamental Lemma (21.3) and $i_{N \leq F(M)}$ is the inclusion. Both $\Lambda_M$ and $\Gamma_M$ are order-preserving.
