This result shows that a category equivalence induces a lattice isomorphism between the submodule lattices of corresponding modules. The map $\Lambda_M$ sends a submodule to its image under $F$, while the inverse $\Gamma_M$ constructs the preimage using the adjoint isomorphism from the Fundamental Lemma. This is the deepest structural fact about equivalences: the entire submodule structure is faithfully preserved, which immediately implies preservation of composition length, simplicity, semisimplicity, and all chain conditions.
