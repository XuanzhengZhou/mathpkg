This comprehensive proposition catalogs the categorical constructions preserved by a module category equivalence. Every fundamental construction — products, coproducts, generators, cogenerators, faithful modules, essential monomorphisms, superfluous epimorphisms, injective envelopes, and projective covers — is preserved. This means the categorical structure of ${}_R \mathbf{M}$ is completely mirrored in ${}_S \mathbf{M}$, establishing that Morita equivalent rings are categorically indistinguishable at the level of their module categories.
