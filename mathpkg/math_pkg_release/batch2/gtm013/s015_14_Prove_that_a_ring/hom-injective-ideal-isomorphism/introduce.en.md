This exercise establishes an isomorphism between the Hom-module from an ideal $I$ (viewed as a right $R$-module) into an injective module $E$, and the quotient of $E$ by the rejection of $I$. When the ring $R$ is itself injective as a module, this formula simplifies the computation of $\operatorname{Hom}_R(I_R, E)$. The result is a special case of a more general adjunction between Hom and tensor functors.
