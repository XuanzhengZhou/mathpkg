Let $F: {}_R \mathbf{M} \to {}_S \mathbf{M}$ be a category equivalence. Then a sequence

$$0 \to M' \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} M'' \to 0$$

is (split) exact in ${}_R \mathbf{M}$ if and only if

$$0 \to F(M') \xrightarrow{F(f)} F(M) \xrightarrow{F(g)} F(M'') \to 0$$

is (split) exact in ${}_S \mathbf{M}$.
