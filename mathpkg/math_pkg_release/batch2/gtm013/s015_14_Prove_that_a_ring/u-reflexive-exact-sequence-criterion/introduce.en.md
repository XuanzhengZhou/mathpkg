This result provides a criterion for determining when the middle term of a short exact sequence is $U$-reflexive, assuming the bimodule $U$ is injective on both sides and the quotient term is $U$-torsionless. The condition that $N$ is $U$-torsionless ensures the dual sequence remains exact. The proof uses the Five Lemma and properties of the double-dual functor to compare the canonical evaluation maps.
