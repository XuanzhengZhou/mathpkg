Let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule and let

$$0 \to {}_R K \to {}_R M \to {}_R N \to 0$$

be an exact sequence of left $R$-modules. Prove that if ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are injective and $N$ is $U$-torsionless, then $M$ is $U$-reflexive if and only if $K$ and $N$ are $U$-reflexive.
