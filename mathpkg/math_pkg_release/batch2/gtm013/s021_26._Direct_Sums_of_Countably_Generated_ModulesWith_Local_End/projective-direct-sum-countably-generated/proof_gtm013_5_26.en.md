Since $P$ is projective, $P$ is a direct summand of a free module $F \cong R^{(X)}$ for some set $X$. The free module $R^{(X)} = \oplus_X R_R$ is a direct sum of copies of $R_R$, and each copy $R_R$ is countably generated (indeed, singly generated by $1_R$). Letting $c = \aleph_0$ in Theorem (26.1), we conclude that the direct summand $P$ of $F$ is itself a direct sum of countably generated modules.
