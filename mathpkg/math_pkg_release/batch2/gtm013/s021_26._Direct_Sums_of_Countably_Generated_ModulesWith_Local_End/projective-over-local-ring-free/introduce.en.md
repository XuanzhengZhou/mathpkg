Corollary 26.7, originally proved by Kaplansky, states that every projective module over a local ring is free. This result follows from Theorem 26.5 (on the decomposition of countably generated modules with local endomorphism rings) together with the observation that $R \cong \operatorname{End}({}_R R)$ via (4.11). Over a local ring, the regular module has a local endomorphism ring, so any projective module (being a direct summand of a free module) inherits a decomposition into modules with local endomorphism rings. The result is a cornerstone of the theory of semiperfect rings developed in the following section.
