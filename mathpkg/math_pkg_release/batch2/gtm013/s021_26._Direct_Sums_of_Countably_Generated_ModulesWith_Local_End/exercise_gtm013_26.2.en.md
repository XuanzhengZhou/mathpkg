Let $R$ be a von Neumann regular ring. Prove that:

(1) Every finitely generated submodule of a projective $R$-module is a direct summand.

[Hint: Let $K$ be a finitely generated submodule of $R^{(n)}$. Let $F = \operatorname{Hom}_R(R^{(n)}, -)$. Then $F : {}_R \mathbf{M} \to \operatorname{End}(R^{(n)})\mathbf{M}$ is a category equivalence and $0 \to F(K) \to F(R^{(n)})$ splits.]

(2) If ${}_R P$ is projective then there exist idempotents $(e_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ in $R$ such that $P \cong \oplus_A R e_\alpha$.
