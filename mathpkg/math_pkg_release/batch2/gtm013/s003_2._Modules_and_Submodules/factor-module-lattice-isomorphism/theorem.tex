Let $M$ be a left $R$-module and let $K$ be a submodule of $M$. Then the lattice of submodules of the factor module $M/K$ is lattice isomorphic to the sublattice of submodules of $M$ that contain $K$, via the inverse order-preserving bijections

$$n_K: H \mapsto H/K = \{x + K \mid x \in H\},$$

$$n_K^+: T \mapsto \{x \in M \mid x + K \in T\}.$$
