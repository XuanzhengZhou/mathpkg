Proposition 2.8 asserts that every non-zero finitely spanned module possesses a maximal proper submodule. The proof uses the Maximal Principle (equivalent to Zorn's Lemma) applied to the poset of proper submodules missing a fixed element from a minimal spanning set. This result guarantees the existence of maximal submodules in finitely generated modules over arbitrary rings.
