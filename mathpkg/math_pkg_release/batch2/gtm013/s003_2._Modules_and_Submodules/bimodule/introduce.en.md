A bimodule is an abelian group $M$ that carries both a left $R$-module structure and a right $S$-module structure, with the compatibility condition $r(xs) = (rx)s$ for all $r \in R$, $s \in S$, $x \in M$. Bimodules arise naturally in the study of endomorphism rings of modules and provide a unified framework for studying module structures relative to two rings simultaneously.
