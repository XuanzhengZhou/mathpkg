Let $M$ be a left $R$-module. If $X \subseteq M$ and $A \subseteq R$, then any element of $M$ of the form

$$a_1 x_1 + \cdots + a_n x_n = \sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i x_i$$

with $x_1, \ldots, x_n \in X$ and $a_1, \ldots, a_n \in A$ is a \textbf{linear combination} of $X$ with coefficients in $A$, or simply an \textbf{$A$-linear combination} of $X$. The set of all such $A$-linear combinations of $X$ is denoted by $AX$.
