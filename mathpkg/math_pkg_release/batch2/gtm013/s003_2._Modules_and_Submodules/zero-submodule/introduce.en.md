The zero submodule $\{0\}$ of a module $M$ is the trivial submodule consisting only of the additive identity. It is the unique smallest submodule of any module and serves as the least element in the submodule lattice. By convention, $R\emptyset = 0$, meaning that the only $R$-linear combination of the empty set is the zero element.
