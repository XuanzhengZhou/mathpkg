Let $M$ be a left $R$-module and let $K$ be a submodule of $M$. The \textbf{factor module} (or \textbf{quotient module}) $M/K$ is the abelian factor group equipped with the induced $R$-module structure. The lattice of submodules of the factor module $M/K$ is lattice isomorphic to the sublattice of submodules of $M$ that contain $K$, via the correspondence maps

$$n_K: H \mapsto H/K = \{x + K \mid x \in H\},$$

$$n_K^+: T \mapsto \{x \in M \mid x + K \in T\}.$$
