The factor module $M/K$ is the quotient of a module $M$ by a submodule $K$, inheriting an $R$-module structure from $M$. The fundamental lattice isomorphism theorem for modules states that the submodules of $M/K$ correspond bijectively to the submodules of $M$ that contain $K$, preserving the lattice order. This generalizes the correspondence theorem for groups to the module setting.
