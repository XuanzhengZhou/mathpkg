Let $\phi: R \rightarrow S$ be a ring homomorphism and let $M$ be an abelian group that is simultaneously a left $R$-module and a left $S$-module such that for all $r \in R$ and $x \in M$,

$$r x = \phi(r) x.$$

Then, as lattices,

$$\mathcal{S}(_S M) \leq \mathcal{S}(_R M) = \mathcal{S}(_{\phi(R)} M) \leq \mathcal{S}(_{\mathbb{Z}} M).$$
