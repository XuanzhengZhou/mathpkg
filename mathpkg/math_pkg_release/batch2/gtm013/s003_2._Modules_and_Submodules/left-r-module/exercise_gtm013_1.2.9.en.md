Let $M$ be a non-zero module, let $N$ be a proper submodule, and let $x \in M \setminus N$. Prove that:

(1) $M$ has a submodule $K$ maximal with respect to $N \leq K$ and $x \notin K$.

(2) If $M = R x + N$, then $M$ has a maximal submodule $K$ with $N \leq K$ and $x \notin K$.
