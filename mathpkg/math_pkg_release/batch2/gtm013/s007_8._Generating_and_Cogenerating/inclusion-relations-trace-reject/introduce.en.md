Lemma 8.19 establishes monotonicity properties for trace and reject with respect to inclusion of module classes. If every module in $\mathcal{V}$ is generated by $\mathcal{U}$, then the trace of $\mathcal{V}$ is contained in the trace of $\mathcal{U}$. Dually, if $\mathcal{V}$ is contained in $\mathrm{Cog}(\mathcal{U})$, then the reject of $\mathcal{U}$ is contained in the reject of $\mathcal{V}$. These results are natural consequences of the transitivity property (Corollary 8.6).
