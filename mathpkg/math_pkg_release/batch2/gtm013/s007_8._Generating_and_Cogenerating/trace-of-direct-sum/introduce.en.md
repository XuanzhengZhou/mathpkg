This proposition (8.17/8.18) establishes that the trace behaves additively with respect to direct sums: the trace of a class $\mathcal{U}$ in a direct sum of modules is exactly the direct sum of the traces in each summand. This is proved using the natural injections and projections, together with Proposition 8.16.
