The reject $\mathrm{Rej}_M(\mathcal{U})$ of a class $\mathcal{U}$ of modules in $ is the intersection of all kernels of homomorphisms from $ into modules in $\mathcal{U}$. It is the unique smallest submodule $ of $ such that /K$ is cogenerated by $\mathcal{U}$. The reject is the dual notion of the trace, measuring which elements of $ are annihilated by all homomorphisms into $\mathcal{U}$.
