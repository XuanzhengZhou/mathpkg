Proposition 8.12 provides the definitive characterization of trace and reject. Part (1) states that $\mathrm{Tr}_M(\mathcal{U})$ is the unique largest submodule of $ generated by $\mathcal{U}$, meaning every submodule generated by $\mathcal{U}$ is contained in the trace. Part (2) states that $\mathrm{Rej}_M(\mathcal{U})$ is the unique smallest submodule $ such that the factor module /K$ is cogenerated by $\mathcal{U}$.
