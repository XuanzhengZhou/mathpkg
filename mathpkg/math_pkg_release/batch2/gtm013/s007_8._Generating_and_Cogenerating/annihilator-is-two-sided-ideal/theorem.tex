For each class $\mathcal{U}$ of left $R$-modules, the reject

$$\mathrm{Rej}_R(\mathcal{U}) = l_R(\mathcal{U})$$

is a two-sided ideal, where $l_R(\mathcal{U})$ is the annihilator of $\mathcal{U}$, i.e., the intersection of all left ideals $I$ of $R$ such that $R/I$ embeds in some element of $\mathcal{U}$.
