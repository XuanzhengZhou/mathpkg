Proposition 8.10 characterizes generation of a module $ by a single module $ in terms of the hom-set $\mathrm{Hom}_R(U, M)$: $ generates $ if and only if there exists a subset $ of homomorphisms from $ to $ whose images span $. When $ finitely generates $, the set $ can be taken to be finite. This bridges the categorical definition with the element-based notion of spanning.
