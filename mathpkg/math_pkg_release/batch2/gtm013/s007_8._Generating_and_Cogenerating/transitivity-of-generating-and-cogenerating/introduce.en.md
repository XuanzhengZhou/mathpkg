Corollary 8.6 establishes the transitivity of generation and cogeneration. If every module in $\mathcal{V}$ is generated by $\mathcal{U}$, then any module generated by $\mathcal{V}$ is also generated by $\mathcal{U}$. The dual statement holds for cogeneration. This means that the operators $\mathrm{Gen}$ and $\mathrm{Cog}$ behave like closure operators on classes of modules.
