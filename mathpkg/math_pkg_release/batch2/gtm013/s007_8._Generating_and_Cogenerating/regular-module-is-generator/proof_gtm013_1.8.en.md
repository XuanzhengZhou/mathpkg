Every module $_R M$ has a spanning set (for example, $M$ itself spans $M$). By Theorem 8.1, there is an epimorphism $R^{(X)} \to M \to 0$ for some set $X$. Since $R^{(X)}$ is a direct sum of copies of $R$, this means $R$ generates $M$. As $M$ was arbitrary, $R$ is a generator in $_R\mathbf{M}$.
