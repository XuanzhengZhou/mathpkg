Suppose first that \(T\) is a diagonalizable toral \(k\)-subbiring of \(H(K/k)\), so that \(T = T(G) = \{\bar{t} \mid t \in (kG)^*\}\) for some splitting \(G\) of \(K/k\).

Then \(T_\pi = \{\bar{t} \mid t \in (\pi G)^*\}\) where

\[
(\pi G)^* = \{t \in (kG)^* \mid t(\pi G) \subset \pi\}.
\]

It follows from the properties of \(T(G)\) that \(T_\pi\) is a \(\pi\)-subcoring/\(\pi\)-subbiring of \(H(K)\), and the equalities \(H(K/K^T) = K\langle T \rangle = K\langle T_\pi \rangle\) and \(H(K/K^T) = KT = KT_\pi\) hold by the correspondence between toral structures and fixed fields.
