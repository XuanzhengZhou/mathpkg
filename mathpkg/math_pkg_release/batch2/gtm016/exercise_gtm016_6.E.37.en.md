Let $K/k$ be a finite dimensional radical extension of characteristic $p > 0$ and let $D \in \operatorname{Der}_k K$. Show that for some $e$, the $k$-span $\langle D^{p^e} \rangle$ of $D^{p^e}, D^{p^{e+1}}, \ldots$ is a toral $k$-subcoalgebra of $H(K/k)$.
