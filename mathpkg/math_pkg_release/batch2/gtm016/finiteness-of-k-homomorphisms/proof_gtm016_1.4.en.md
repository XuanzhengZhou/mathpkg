Let $s_1,\ldots,s_n$ be a basis for $K/k$. For $\sigma \in \operatorname{Hom}_k(K, k_{\text{Alg}})$, $\sigma(s_i)$ is a root of the minimum polynomial $f_{s_i}(X)$. There are only finitely many choices for each $\sigma(s_i)$, and $\sigma$ is determined by these values.
