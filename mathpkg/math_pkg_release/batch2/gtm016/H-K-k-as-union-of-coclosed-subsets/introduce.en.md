In the biring approach to Galois theory (Chapter 5.3), $H(K)$ is defined as the union of all coclosed subsets of $\operatorname{End} K$, and $H(K/k)$ is the union of all coclosed subsets of $\operatorname{End}_k K$. Note that $H(K) = H(K/\pi)$ where $\pi$ is the prime field. These birings generalize the Galois group and form the foundation of the biring correspondence theory.
