Let $K = k(s)$. The minimum polynomial $f_s(X) = \prod (X - s_i)$ has distinct roots. Each $\sigma \in \operatorname{Aut}_k K$ sends $s$ to some $s_i$, determining $\sigma$. There are at most $n$ such automorphisms. The Frobenius iterates $1, \tau, \ldots, \tau^{n-1}$ give $n$ distinct automorphisms, so they form the full group. The fixed field is $\operatorname{Roots}_K(X^q - X) = k$.
