We have $x(abc) = x(a(bc)) = \sum_i x(a) x_{(1)i}(b) x_{(2)i}(c)$ and $x(a) = x(1a) = \sum_i x(1)x_i(a) = \sum_i \varepsilon(x_{(1)i})x_{(2)i}(a)$ for $a,b,c \in K$. The other two assertions are similarly established using $x(abc) = x((ab)c)$ and $x(a) = x(a1)$.
