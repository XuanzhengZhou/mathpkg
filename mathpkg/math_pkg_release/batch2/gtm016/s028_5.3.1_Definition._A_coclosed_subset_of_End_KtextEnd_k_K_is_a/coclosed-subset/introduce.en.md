A coclosed subset of $\End_k K$ is a subset $H$ such that for each $x \in H$, the action of $x$ on products factors as $x(ab) = \sum_i x(a)x_i(b)$. This condition encodes a comultiplication structure, making $H(K/k)$ into a $K$-bialgebra.
