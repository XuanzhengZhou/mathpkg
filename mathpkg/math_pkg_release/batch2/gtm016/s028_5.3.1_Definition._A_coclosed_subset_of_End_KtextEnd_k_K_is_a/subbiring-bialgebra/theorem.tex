For any coclosed subset $H$ of $H(K/k)$, the restrictions $\Delta_H$ and $\varepsilon_H$ satisfy properties (1), (2), (3), and (5) of Proposition 5.3.8 with $K$ replaced by $K_H$. If $H$ is a $K$-subbiring of $H(K/k)$, then property (4) of Proposition 5.3.8 is also satisfied, and $(H, \Delta_H, \varepsilon_H)$ is a $K_H/k$-bialgebra in the sense of B.1.
