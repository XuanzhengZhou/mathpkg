The bialgebra structure on $H(K/k)$ restricts to any coclosed subset $H$: $\Delta_H,\varepsilon_H$ satisfy coalgebra axioms (1)-(3) and compatibility (5). If $H$ is a $K$-subbiring, then multiplicativity of $\varepsilon$ (property 4) also holds, making $(H,\Delta_H,\varepsilon_H)$ a full $K_H/k$-bialgebra.
