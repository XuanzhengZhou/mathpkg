This proposition establishes that $\Delta$ (the coproduct) and $\varepsilon$ (the counit) endow $H(K/k)$ with the structure of a $K/k$-bialgebra. Together with 5.3.7, this provides the full bialgebra axiomatics for the biring. These properties are fundamental to the biring approach to Galois theory developed in Chapter 5.
