If $n=1$, this follows from 1.2.2. For $n>1$, let $s \in S$. By induction, $k(s)(S \setminus \{s\}):k(s) < \infty$ and $k(s):k < \infty$. By transitivity of dimension, $k(S):k < \infty$.
