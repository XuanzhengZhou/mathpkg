Let $k' \supset k$ and let $K = k'(x_1) \cdots k'(x_n)$ be an internal tensor product of $k'$-algebras, where $x_i \in K$, $e_i \geq 0$, and $x_i^{e_i} \in k$ for $1 \leq i \leq n$. Then

$$
G = \{ kx_1^{f_1} \cdots x_n^{f_n} \mid 0 \leq f_i \leq e_i - 1 \text{ for } 1 \leq i \leq n \}
$$

is a splitting for $K/k$ with basis $g_1 = k'x_1, \ldots, g_n = k'x_n$ and identity $K^G = k'$.
