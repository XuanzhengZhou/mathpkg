Let $A, B$ be subfields of $K$ containing $k$. If $P$ is injective and $A/k, B/k$ are $P$-extensions, then $AB/k$ is a $P$-extension. In particular, if $A/k, B/k$ are Abelian (resp. solvable), then $AB/k$ is Abelian (resp. solvable).
