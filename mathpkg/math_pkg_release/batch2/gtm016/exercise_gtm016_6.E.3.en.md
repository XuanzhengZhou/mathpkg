Let $K/k$ be a finite dimensional radical extension. Show that for any separable algebraic extension $L$ of $k$, there is a bijective $k$-linear mapping from $L \otimes_k \operatorname{End}_k K$ to $\operatorname{End}_{L \otimes_k k} L \otimes_k K$ mapping $x \otimes T$ to $(x\,\mathrm{id}_L) \otimes T$ for $x \in L, T \in \operatorname{End}_k K$.
