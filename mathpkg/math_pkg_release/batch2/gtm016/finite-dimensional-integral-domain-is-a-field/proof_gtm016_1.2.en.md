Let $a \neq 0$ in $A$. Left translation $a_L: b \mapsto ab$ is $k$-linear and injective since $A$ is an integral domain. By finite dimensionality it is surjective, so $a_L(x) = 1$ has a solution, giving $ax = 1$.
