The Biring Correspondence Theorem establishes that for a finite dimensional extension $K/k$, the biring $H(K/k)$ coincides with the full endomorphism ring $\operatorname{End}_k K$. This is a key structural result in the biring Galois theory, showing that the biring recovers the endomorphism ring for finite extensions. The theorem is part of a larger correspondence between subfields of $K$ and $K$-subbirings of $\operatorname{End} K$.
