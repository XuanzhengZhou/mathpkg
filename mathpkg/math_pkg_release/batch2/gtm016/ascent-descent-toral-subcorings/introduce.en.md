This theorem enables the passage from a toral $k$-subcoring $T$ of $H(K/k)$ to the diagonalizable toral $k$-subcoring $\bar{T}$ of $H(\bar{K}/k)$ (ascent), study $\bar{T}$, and then transfer conclusions back to $T = \bar{T}^G$ (descent). It is a fundamental tool in the study of radical extensions via birings, connecting the structure of $K/k$ to properties of the Galois group action on the algebraic closure.
