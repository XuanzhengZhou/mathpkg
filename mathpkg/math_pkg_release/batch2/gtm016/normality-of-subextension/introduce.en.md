Let $K$ be a normal algebraic extension of $k$ containing $k'$. Then the following are equivalent: (1) $k'/k$ is normal; (2) $k'$ is stable under $\operatorname{Aut}_k K$; (3) $\operatorname{Aut}_k k' = \operatorname{Aut}_k K|_{k'}$.
