$\mathbf{S}$ is the set of all $K$-subbirings $H$ of $\End K$ that are finite-dimensional as $K$-vector spaces. This set forms the "algebraic side" of the Biring Correspondence Theorem, which pairs each $H \in \mathbf{S}$ with its fixed subfield $K_H$ and each $k \in \mathbf{F}$ with $H(K/k) = \End_k K$.
