$k_{\text{Alg}}^G = \{s \in k_{\text{Alg}} \mid f_s(X) \text{ has only one root in } k_{\text{Alg}}\}$, where $f_s(X)$ denotes the minimum polynomial of $s$ over $k$ for $s \in k_{\text{Alg}}$ and $G = \operatorname{Aut}_k k_{\text{Alg}}$.
