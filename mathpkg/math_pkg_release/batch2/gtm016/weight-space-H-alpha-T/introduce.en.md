This defines the weight space decomposition of a subspace $H$ with respect to a toral subbiring $T$. The weight $\alpha$ is a linear functional on $T$, and $H_\alpha(T)$ consists of elements $x$ that transform under the adjoint action of $t \in T$ via the scalar $\alpha(t)$. This generalizes the root space decomposition from Lie theory to the biring setting and is used in Chapter 6 to study the structure of radical extensions.
