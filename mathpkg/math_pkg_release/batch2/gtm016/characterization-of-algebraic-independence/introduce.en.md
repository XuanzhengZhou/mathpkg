A subset $S$ of $K$ is algebraically independent over $k$ if and only if for any distinct elements $s_1, \ldots, s_n$ of $S$ and any nonzero polynomial $f(X_1, \ldots, X_n) \in k[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$, $f(s_1, \ldots, s_n) \neq 0$.
