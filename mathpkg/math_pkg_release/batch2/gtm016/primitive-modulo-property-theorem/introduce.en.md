Let $K/k$ be a finite dimensional radical extension and let $S, T$ be toral $k$-subcorings of $H(K/k)$ with $S \subset T$ and every element of $T^0$ primitive modulo $S$. Then $T \cap \langle S \rangle = \{t \in T \mid t(a) = \varepsilon(t)a \text{ for all } a \in K^S\}$.
