We let $(K:k)_{\text{sep}} = k_{\text{sep}}:k$ and $(K:k)_{\text{rad}} = K:k_{\text{sep}}$. The integer $(K:k)_{\text{sep}}$ (resp. $(K:k)_{\text{rad}}$) is called the separability degree (resp. radical degree) of $K$ over $k$. Note that $K:k = (K:k)_{\text{sep}}(K:k)_{\text{rad}}$.
