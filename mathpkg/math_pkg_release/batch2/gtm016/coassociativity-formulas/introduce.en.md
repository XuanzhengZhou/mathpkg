This proposition gives explicit formulas for the iterated action of a higher derivation on a triple product $abc$ and for recovering $x(a)$ from the comultiplication components via the counit $\varepsilon$. These identities express the coassociativity of $\Delta$ and the counit property of $\varepsilon$, fundamental to the bialgebra structure of $H(K/k)$.
