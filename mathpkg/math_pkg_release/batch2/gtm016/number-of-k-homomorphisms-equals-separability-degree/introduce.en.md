Let $K:k < \infty$. Then the number of elements of $\operatorname{Hom}_k(K, k_{\text{Alg}})$ is $(K:k)_{\text{sep}}$. If $G$ is a subgroup of $\operatorname{Aut}_kK$, then $G:1 = K:K^G$. Two subgroups $G, H$ of $\operatorname{Aut}_kK$ are equal iff their fixed fields $K^G, K^H$ are equal.
