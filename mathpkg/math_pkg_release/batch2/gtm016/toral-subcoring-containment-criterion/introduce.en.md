This theorem characterizes the intersection of a toral subcoring $T$ with the subbiring generated by a smaller subcoring $S$ in terms of the action on the fixed field $K^S$. Elements of $T$ that act as scalar multiplication by $\varepsilon(t)$ on $K^S$ are precisely those in the intersection.
