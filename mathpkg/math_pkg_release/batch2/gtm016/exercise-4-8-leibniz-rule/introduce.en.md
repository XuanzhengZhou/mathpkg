Exercise E.4.8 establishes key algebraic properties of derivations. Part (a) generalizes Leibniz's rule to $n$-th powers using binomial coefficients. Part (b) extends this to shifted derivations $D - aI$. Part (c) shows that in characteristic $p$, the $p$-th power of a derivation is again a derivation, a crucial fact for the Jacobson Differential Correspondence Theorem. Part (d) defines the generalized eigenspace $K_a(D)$ and asks to prove it is a subring.
