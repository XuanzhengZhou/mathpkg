Let $K = k(x)$ where $x \in K-k$ and $x^p \in k$. Let $s$ and $t$ be the derivations of $K/k$ such that

$$s(x^i) = ix^i$$
$$t((x+1)^i) = i(x+1)^i$$

for all $i$. Let $S$ be the $k$-span of $I, s, \ldots, s^{p-1}$ and let $T$ be the $k$-span of $I, t, \ldots, t^{p-1}$. Show that $S$ and $T$ are two distinct cosplit $k$-forms of the $K/k$-bialgebra $H(K/k)$.
