Let $f^*(X) = \sum_0^m a_i(x)X^i$ and $g^*(X) = \sum_0^n b_j(x)X^j$. Let $c(x)$ be irreducible in $k[x]$. Let $a_i$ and $b_j$ be the first coefficients not divisible by $c(x)$. The $(i+j)$th coefficient of $f^*g^*$ is $a_i b_j + \sum a_{i-r}b_{j+r} + \sum a_{i+s}b_{j-s}$ which is not divisible by $c(x)$.
