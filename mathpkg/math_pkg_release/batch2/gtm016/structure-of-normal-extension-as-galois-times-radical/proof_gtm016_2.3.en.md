If $K/k$ is normal, then $K = k_{\text{sep}}k_{\text{rad}}$ by 2.3.9. And $k_{\text{sep}}$ is stable under $\operatorname{Aut}_k K$, so $k_{\text{sep}}/k$ is normal by 2.3.3, hence Galois by 2.3.12. Conversely, if $K = k_{\text{sep}}k_{\text{rad}}$ and $k_{\text{sep}}/k$ is Galois, then both factors are stable under $\operatorname{Aut}_k k_{\text{Alg}}$, so $K/k$ is normal.
