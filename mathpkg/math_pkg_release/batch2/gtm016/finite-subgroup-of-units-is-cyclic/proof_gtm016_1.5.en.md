Let $m$ be the exponent of $A$. Then $A \subset \operatorname{Roots}_L(X^m - 1)$, so $A:1 \leq m$. But $A$ has a cyclic subgroup of order $m$ by 0.2.4. Thus $A$ is cyclic of order $m$.
