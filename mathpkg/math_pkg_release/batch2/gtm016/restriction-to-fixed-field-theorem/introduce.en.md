Let $K/k$ be a finite dimensional radical extension and let $T$ be a toral $k$-subbiring of $H(K/k)$. Then $K^T$ is stable under $H(K/k)^T$ and the restriction mapping $x \mapsto x|_{K^T}$ from $H(K/k)^T$ to $H(K^T/k)$ is a surjective homomorphism of $k$-algebras and $K^T$-coalgebras.
