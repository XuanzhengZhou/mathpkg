We let $k_{\text{Sep}} = \{s \in k_{\text{Alg}} \mid s \text{ is separable over } k\}$ and $k_{\text{Rad}} = \{s \in k_{\text{Alg}} \mid s \text{ is radical over } k\}$. These are called the separable closure and radical closure of $k$.
