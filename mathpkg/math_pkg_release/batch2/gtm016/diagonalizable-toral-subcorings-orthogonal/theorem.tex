Let $K/k$ be a finite dimensional radical extension and let $S$ and $T$ be diagonalizable toral $k$-subcorings of $H(K/k)$ with $S \subset T$. Then for $\alpha, \beta \in \mathbf{R}^S$, the set $\mathbf{R}^S - \varepsilon_T = \{\alpha - \varepsilon_T \mid \alpha \in \mathbf{R}^S\}$ is a $\pi$-subspace of $T^*_\pi$, and
$$(\mathbf{R}^S - \varepsilon_T)^{\perp} = \{t \in T_\pi \mid t \in H(K/K^S)\} = T_\pi \cap \langle S \rangle.$$
Consequently,
$$\mathbf{R}^S = \{\alpha \in T^*_\pi \mid (\alpha - \varepsilon_T)(t) = 0 \text{ for all } t \in T_\pi \cap \langle S \rangle\}.$$
