When $S \subset T$ are diagonalizable toral $k$-subcorings of $H(K/k)$ with $K/k$ a finite dimensional radical extension, Theorem 6.3.8 describes the character group $\mathbf{R}^S$ as an orthogonal subspace of the dual $T^*_\pi$. The key computation identifies the orthogonal complement $(\mathbf{R}^S - \varepsilon_T)^\perp$ with the intersection $T_\pi \cap \langle S \rangle$, yielding a duality between the characters of the smaller subcoring $S$ and the elements of $T_\pi$ that lie in the subcoring generated by $S$.
