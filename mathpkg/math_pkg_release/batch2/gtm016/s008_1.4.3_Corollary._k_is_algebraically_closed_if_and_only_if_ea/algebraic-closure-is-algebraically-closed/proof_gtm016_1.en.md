Let $K'$ be an algebraic extension of $k_{\text{Alg}}$. Then $K'/k$ is algebraic by transitivity of algebraic extensions. Thus $K' = k_{\text{Alg}}$, so $k_{\text{Alg}}$ has no proper algebraic extensions and is therefore algebraically closed.
