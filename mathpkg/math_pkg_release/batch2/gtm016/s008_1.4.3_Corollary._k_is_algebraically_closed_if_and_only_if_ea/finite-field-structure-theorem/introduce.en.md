Finite fields are completely classified: for each prime power $p^m$, there exists a unique field of order $p^m$ (up to isomorphism), namely the splitting field of $X^{p^m} - X$ over $\mathbb{Z}_p$. The multiplicative group is cyclic, and the Galois group is cyclic generated by Frobenius.
