Fatou's lemma states that for non-negative integrable functions, the integral of the pointwise liminf is bounded by the liminf of the integrals. The proof uses $g_n(x) = \inf_{i \geq n} f_i(x)$, which increases to $f$, and the monotone convergence theorem. This inequality is essential for proving the dominated convergence theorem and many other limit theorems.
