Since $Z^0 \neq 0$, there is an element $z_0 \in Z^0$. For any $z \in Z$, $zz_0^{-1} \in Z$ and $zz_0^{-1}Z = Z\$. Thus $z = (zz_0^{-1})z_0 \in Z^0\$, proving $Z \subset Z^0$, i.e., $Z$ is open. Every coset is a translate of an open set, hence open.
