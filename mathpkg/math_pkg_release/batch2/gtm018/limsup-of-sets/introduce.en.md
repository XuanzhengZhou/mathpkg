The superior limit $\limsup_n E_n$ consists of points that belong to $E_n$ for infinitely many indices $n$. It can be expressed as $\bigcap_{n=1}^\infty \bigcup_{m=n}^\infty E_m$, and is fundamental for studying convergence of sequences of measurable sets in measure theory.
