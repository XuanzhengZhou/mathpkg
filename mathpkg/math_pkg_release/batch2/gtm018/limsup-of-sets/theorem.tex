If $\{E_n\}$ is a sequence of subsets of $X$, the set $E^*$ of all those points of $X$ which belong to $E_n$ for infinitely many values of $n$ is called the superior limit of the sequence and is denoted by
$$E^* = \limsup_n E_n.$$
Equivalently,
$$\limsup_n E_n = \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} \bigcup_{m=n}^{\infty} E_m.$$
