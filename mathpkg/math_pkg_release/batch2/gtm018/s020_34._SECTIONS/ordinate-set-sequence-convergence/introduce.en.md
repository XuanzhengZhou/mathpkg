Exercise (5c) of Section 34 relates ordinate sets to limits of sequences of functions. For an increasing sequence $f_n \uparrow f$ of non negative functions, the lower ordinate sets $V_*(f_n)$ form an increasing sequence whose union is $V_*(f)$. For a decreasing sequence $f_n \downarrow f$, the upper ordinate sets $V^*(f_n)$ form a decreasing sequence whose intersection is $V^*(f)$. These continuity properties are essential for extending results from simple to measurable functions.
