Theorem B of Section 34 extends Theorem A from sets to functions: every section of a measurable function on a product space is itself measurable. The proof uses Theorem A together with the identity $f_x^{-1}(M) = (f^{-1}(M))_x$ to relate the inverse image under a section to a section of the inverse image under the original function. This result is essential for the formulation of Fubini's theorem.
