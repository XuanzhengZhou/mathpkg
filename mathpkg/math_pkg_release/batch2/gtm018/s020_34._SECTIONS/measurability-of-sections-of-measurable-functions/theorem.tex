If $f$ is a measurable function on the product space $X \times Y$, then every section of $f$ is a measurable function. That is, for every $x \in X$, the $X$-section $f_x(y) = f(x,y)$ is a measurable function on $Y$, and for every $y \in Y$, the $Y$-section $f^y(x) = f(x,y)$ is a measurable function on $X$.
