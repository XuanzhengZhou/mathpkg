Exercise (5b) of Section 34 establishes the monotonicity of ordinate sets: if two non negative functions satisfy $f \leq g$ pointwise, then both the lower ordinate set $V^*(f)$ and the upper ordinate set $V_*(f)$ are contained in the corresponding ordinate sets of $g$. This property is used in the alternative treatment of measurable functions via ordinate sets.
