Let $D$ be a compact neighborhood of $e$. Define inductively $D_0 = D$ and $D_{n+1} = D_n^{-1}D_n \cup C_{n+1}$ where $\{C_n\}$ are compact sets covering $E$. Then $Z = \bigcup_{n=0}^{\infty} D_n$ is a $\sigma$-compact full subgroup containing all $C_n$ and hence $E$.
