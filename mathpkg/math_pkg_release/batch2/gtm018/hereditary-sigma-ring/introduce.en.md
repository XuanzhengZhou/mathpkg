The hereditary $\sigma$-ring $\mathbf{H}(\mathbf{E})$ generated by a class $\mathbf{E}$ consists of all sets that can be covered by a countable union of sets from $\mathbf{E}$. When $\mathbf{E}$ is already closed under countable unions, $\mathbf{H}(\mathbf{E})$ simplifies to all subsets of sets in $\mathbf{E}$. This is the natural domain for outer measures.
