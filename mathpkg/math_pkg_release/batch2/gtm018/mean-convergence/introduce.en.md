Mean convergence is convergence in the $L^1$ seminorm: $\int |f_n - f| d\mu \to 0$. This is the natural notion of convergence for integrable functions. It implies convergence in measure (Theorem A), and together with uniform absolute continuity and equicontinuity it characterizes mean convergence (Theorem C of \S26).
