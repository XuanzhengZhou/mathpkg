A linear functional on $\mathcal{L}$ is a real valued function $\Lambda$ of the functions in $\mathcal{L}$ such that

$$\Lambda(\alpha f + \beta g) = \alpha \Lambda(f) + \beta \Lambda(g)$$

for every pair, $f$ and $g$, of functions in $\mathcal{L}$ and every pair, $\alpha$ and $\beta$, of real numbers.
