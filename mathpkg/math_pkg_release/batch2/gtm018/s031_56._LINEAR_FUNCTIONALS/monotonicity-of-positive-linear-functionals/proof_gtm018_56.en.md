If $f \geq g$, then $f - g \in \mathcal{L}_+$. Since $\Lambda$ is positive, $\Lambda(f - g) \geq 0$. By linearity, $\Lambda(f - g) = \Lambda(f) - \Lambda(g)$, so $\Lambda(f) \geq \Lambda(g)$.
