Let $X$ be the one-point compactification, by $\infty$, of the countable discrete space of positive integers. A function $f$ in $\mathcal{L}$ is, in this case, a convergent sequence $\{f(n)\}$ of real numbers with $f(\infty) = \lim_n f(n)$. Show that the most general positive linear functional $\Lambda$ on $\mathcal{L}$ is defined by

$$\Lambda(f) = \sum_{1 \leq n \leq \infty} f(n) \Lambda_n,$$

where $\sum_n \Lambda_n$ is a convergent series of positive numbers.
