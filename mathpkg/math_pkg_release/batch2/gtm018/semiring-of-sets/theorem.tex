A semiring is a non empty class $\mathbf{P}$ of sets such that
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] if $E \in \mathbf{P}$ and $F \in \mathbf{P}$, then $E \cap F \in \mathbf{P}$, and
\item[(b)] if $E \in \mathbf{P}$ and $F \in \mathbf{P}$ and $E \subset F$, then there is a finite class $\{C_0, C_1, \cdots, C_n\}$ of sets in $\mathbf{P}$ such that $E = C_0 \subset C_1 \subset \cdots \subset C_n = F$ and $D_i = C_i - C_{i-1} \in \mathbf{P}$ for $i = 1, \cdots, n$.
\end{itemize}
