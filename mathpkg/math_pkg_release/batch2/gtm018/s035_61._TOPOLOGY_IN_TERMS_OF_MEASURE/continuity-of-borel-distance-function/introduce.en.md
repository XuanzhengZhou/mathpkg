In a locally compact topological group with a regular Haar measure, the function $f(x) = \rho(xE, E) = \mu(xE \Delta E)$ is continuous for any Borel set $E$ of finite measure. This theorem establishes the first bridge between the measure-theoretic and topological structures, showing that the distance induced by the symmetric difference metric varies continuously under left translation.
