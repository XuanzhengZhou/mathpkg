If $U$ is any neighborhood of $e$ in a locally compact topological group $X$, then there exist a Baire set $E$ of positive, finite measure and a positive number $\epsilon$ such that

$$\{x: \rho(xE,E) < \epsilon\} \subset U,$$

where $\mu$ is a regular Haar measure on $X$ and $\rho(E,F) = \mu(E \Delta F)$.
