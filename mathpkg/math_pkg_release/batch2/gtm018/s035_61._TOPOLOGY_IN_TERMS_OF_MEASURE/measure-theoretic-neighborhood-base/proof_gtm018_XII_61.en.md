Let $V$ be a neighborhood of $e$ such that $VV^{-1} \subset U$ and let $E$ be a Baire set of positive, finite measure such that $E \subset V$. If $\epsilon$ is such that $0 < \epsilon < 2\mu(E)$, then

$$\{x: \rho(xE,E) < \epsilon\} \subset \{x: xE \cap E \neq 0\} = EE^{-1} \subset VV^{-1} \subset U.$$
