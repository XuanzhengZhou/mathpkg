Every neighborhood of the identity contains a set of the form $\{x: \rho(xE,E) < \epsilon\}$ for some Baire set $E$ of finite positive measure. Together with Theorem A, this shows that the collection of all such measure-theoretic sets forms a neighborhood base at the identity, thereby characterizing the topology of a locally compact group entirely in terms of its Haar measure.
