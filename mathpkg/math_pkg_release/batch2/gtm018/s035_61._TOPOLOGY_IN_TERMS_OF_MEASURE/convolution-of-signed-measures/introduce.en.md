For a locally compact topological group with a Haar measure, the convolution of two finite signed measures $\lambda$ and $\nu$ on the Baire sets is defined via integration of the characteristic function $\chi_E(xy)$ over the product space $N_\lambda \times N_\nu$. When both measures are indefinite integrals of integrable functions, their convolution corresponds to the indefinite integral of the standard convolution of those functions.
