Theorem E is the first extension theorem: the length function on semiclosed intervals extends uniquely to a finite measure on the ring generated by those intervals. The extension is defined by decomposing any set in $\mathbf{R}$ as a finite disjoint union of intervals and summing their lengths; well-definedness follows from the additivity of $\mu$ on $\mathbf{P}$.
