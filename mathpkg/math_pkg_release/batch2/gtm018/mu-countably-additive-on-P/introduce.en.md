Theorem D establishes that the length function on semiclosed intervals is countably additive. The proof combines Theorem A (finite subadditivity yields one inequality $\sum_{i=1}^\infty \mu(E_i) \leq \mu(E)$) with Theorem C (countable subadditivity yields the reverse inequality), establishing equality.
