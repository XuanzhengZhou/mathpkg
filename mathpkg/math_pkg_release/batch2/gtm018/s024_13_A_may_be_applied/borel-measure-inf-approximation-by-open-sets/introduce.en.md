This exercise establishes that every finite Borel measure on a metric space is outer regular: the measure of any Borel set can be approximated arbitrarily closely from above by the measure of an open superset. The proof uses the construction of a metric outer measure from an infimum over open sets, then applies the uniqueness result from Exercise (3b) to conclude equality.
