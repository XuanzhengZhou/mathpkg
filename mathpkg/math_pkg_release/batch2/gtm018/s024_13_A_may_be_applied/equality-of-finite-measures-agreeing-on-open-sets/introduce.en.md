This exercise establishes a uniqueness result for finite Borel measures on a metric space: two such measures that assign the same value to every open set must be identical on all Borel sets. This follows from a general principle that Borel measures are determined by their values on open sets, and can be proved using the results of Sections 5, 8, and 13 of this chapter.
