If two finite measures $\mu$ and $\nu$ are defined on the class of Borel subsets of a metric space $X$ and agree on the class $\mathbf{U}$ of open subsets of $X$, then they agree for all Borel sets.

(Hint: if $E \in \mathbf{L}$, $F \in \mathbf{L}$, and $E \subset F$, then $\mu(F - E) = \nu(F - E)$. Apply 5.2, 8.5, and 13.A.)
