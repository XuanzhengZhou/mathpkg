If $\nu$ is a finite measure on the class $\mathbf{S}$ of Borel sets on the real line, and $\mu$ denotes Lebesgue measure on $\mathbf{S}$, then a necessary and sufficient condition that the distribution function $f_\nu$ be absolutely continuous is that $\nu$ be absolutely continuous with respect to $\mu$, i.e., $\nu \ll \mu$.
