This exercise establishes inner regularity of Borel measures on metric spaces: any Borel set of $\sigma$-finite measure can be approximated from below by closed subsets of finite measure. The proof reduces to the finite measure case and then applies the outer regularity result from Exercise (3c) to the complement. This property is fundamental in the study of regular measures.
