For a mean fundamental sequence, the indefinite integrals are uniformly absolutely continuous. This means there is a $\delta>0$ (depending only on $\epsilon$) such that $|\int_E f_n d\mu| < \epsilon$ whenever $\mu(E) < \delta$, uniformly in $n$. This uniform control is crucial for proving the completeness of $L^1$ and limit theorems.
