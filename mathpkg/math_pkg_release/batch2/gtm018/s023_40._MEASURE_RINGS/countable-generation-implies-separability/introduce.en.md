This theorem provides a practical criterion for verifying separability: if a $\sigma$-finite measure ring is generated by a countable collection of elements, then its associated metric space is separable. The proof reduces the generating class to a countable ring and uses approximation results to show that the elements of finite measure generated by this ring form a countable dense subset of $\mathbf{s}(\mu)$.
