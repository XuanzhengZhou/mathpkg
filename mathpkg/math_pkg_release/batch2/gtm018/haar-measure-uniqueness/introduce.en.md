Haar measure is unique up to a positive scalar multiple. The proof uses the mutual absolute continuity of any two left-invariant measures and the translation-invariance of the Radon-Nikodym derivative. This uniqueness gives a canonical integration theory on each locally compact group, with the constant fixed by convenience (e.g., total mass 1 for compact groups).
