If $C = \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} U_n$ where each $U_n$ is a bounded open set, then for each $n$ there exists a function $g_n$ in $\mathfrak{F}$ such that $g_n(x) = 1$ for $x$ in $C$ and $g_n(x) = 0$ for $x$ in $X - U_n$. Take $f_n = g_1 \cap \cdots \cap g_n$.
