Define $\lambda(C) = \inf \{\Lambda(f): C \subset f \in \mathcal{L}_+\}$ for every compact set $C$. Then $\lambda$ is a regular content. Let $\mu$ be the regular Borel measure induced by $\lambda$. One proves $\int f d\mu \leq \Lambda(f)$ for all $f \in \mathcal{L}_+$, and then equality holds by applying the inequality to $-f$ for general $f \in \mathcal{L}$.
