Theorem A establishes that every compact Baire set $C$ can be pointwise approximated from above by a decreasing sequence of non-negative continuous functions with compact support. Specifically, there exist functions $f_n \in \mathcal{L}_+$ such that $f_n \downarrow \chi_C$ pointwise. This result is the key bridge between the topological class $\mathcal{L}_+$ and the measure-theoretic class of compact Baire sets, and it enables the transfer of measure-theoretic statements into the language of continuous functions.
