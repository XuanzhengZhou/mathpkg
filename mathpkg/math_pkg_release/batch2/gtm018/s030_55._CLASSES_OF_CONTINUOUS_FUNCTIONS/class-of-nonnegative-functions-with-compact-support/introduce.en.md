The class $\mathcal{L}_+(X)$ is the set of all non-negative functions belonging to $\mathcal{L}(X)$, the space of continuous functions with compact support on a locally compact Hausdorff space $X$. These functions are essential for approximating characteristic functions of compact Baire sets and for constructing positive linear functionals. Many approximation theorems in measure theory are phrased in terms of $\mathcal{L}_+$ functions.
