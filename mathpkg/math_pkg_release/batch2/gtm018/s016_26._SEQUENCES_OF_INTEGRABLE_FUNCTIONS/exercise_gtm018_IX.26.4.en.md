Let $(X, \mathbf{S}, \mu)$ be the space of positive integers (cf. 22.4).

**(4a)** Write

$$f_n(k) = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{n} & \text{if } 1 \leq k \leq n, \\[4pt] 0 & \text{if } k > n. \end{cases}$$

Show that the sequence $\{f_n\}$ converges uniformly to $0$, but that it does not converge in the mean.
