This definition extends continuity from above at $0$ to a uniform condition for an entire sequence of set functions. A sequence $\{\nu_n\}$ is equicontinuous from above at $0$ if, for any decreasing sequence of sets converging to the empty set, the values $|\nu_n(E_m)|$ can be made uniformly small in $n$ by taking $m$ sufficiently large. This uniform property is a key hypothesis in the characterization of mean convergence of integrable functions (Theorem C).
