This theorem establishes that integrable simple functions are dense in the space of integrable functions with respect to the mean convergence topology. Given any integrable function $f$ and a mean fundamental sequence of integrable simple functions $\{f_n\}$ converging to $f$ in measure, the sequence also converges to $f$ in mean. As a direct corollary, every integrable function can be approximated arbitrarily well in the $L^1$ norm by an integrable simple function.
