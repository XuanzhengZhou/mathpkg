section: "§26. Sequences of Integrable Functions"
exercise_number: 6
---

If $\{f_n\}$ is a sequence of integrable functions which converges in the mean to the integrable function $f$, and if $g$ is an essentially bounded measurable function, then $\{f_n g\}$ mean converges to $f g$.
