The necessity of both conditions is trivial. To prove sufficiency, it is enough to prove that every bounded set in $\hat{\mathbf{S}}$ is regular, since every set in $\hat{\mathbf{S}}$ is the union of an increasing sequence of bounded sets. Using the ring generated by proper differences of compact sets and the monotone class theorem, the result follows elegantly.
