**Proof.** Let $E = \{x : f(x) > 0\}$. By hypothesis $\int_E f d\mu = 0$, so by Theorem D, $\mu(E) = 0$. Applying the same reasoning to $-f$ shows that $\{x : f(x) < 0\}$ also has measure zero. Hence $f = 0$ a.e.
