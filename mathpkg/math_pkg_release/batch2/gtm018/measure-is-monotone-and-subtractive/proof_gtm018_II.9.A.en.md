If $E \in \mathbf{R}$, $F \in \mathbf{R}$, and $E \subset F$, then $F - E \in \mathbf{R}$ and $\mu(F) = \mu(E) + \mu(F - E)$. The fact that $\mu$ is monotone follows now from the fact that it is non negative; the fact that it is subtractive follows from the fact that $\mu(E)$, if it is finite, may be subtracted from both sides of the last written equation.
