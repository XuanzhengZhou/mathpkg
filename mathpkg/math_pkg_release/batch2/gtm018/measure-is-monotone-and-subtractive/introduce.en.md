Theorem A establishes two fundamental properties of measures: monotonicity ($E \subset F$ implies $\mu(E) \leq \mu(F)$) and subtractivity ($\mu(F-E) = \mu(F) - \mu(E)$ when $\mu(E) < \infty$). Both follow directly from additivity and non-negativity.
