A topological group is a group endowed with a topology such that the group operations are continuous. Equivalently, the shearing transformation $S(x,y) = (x,xy)$ on the product space $X \times X$ is a homeomorphism. This latter characterization motivates the measure-theoretic analog of a topological group, known as a measurable group.
