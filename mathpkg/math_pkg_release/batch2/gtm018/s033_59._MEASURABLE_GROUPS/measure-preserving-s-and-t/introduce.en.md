Theorem B establishes that the shearing transformation $S$ and its reflected analog $T$ are measure-preserving transformations of the product measure space $(X \times X, \mathbf{S} \times \mathbf{S}, \mu \times \nu)$. This property is crucial for applying Fubini-type arguments and for establishing the fundamental formulas relating measures of translated and inverted sets in a measurable group.
