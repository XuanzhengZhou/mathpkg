Mean convergence is equivalent to convergence in measure plus two uniformity conditions on the indefinite integrals of $|f_n|$: uniform absolute continuity and equicontinuity from above at zero. This theorem, due to Vitali, provides the definitive characterization of $L^1$ convergence and is the key to proving the Lebesgue dominated convergence theorem.
