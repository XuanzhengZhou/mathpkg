Given a measure $\mu$ on a $\sigma$-ring $\mathbf{S}$, one forms its completion by adjoining all subsets of null sets. The completed class $\bar{\mathbf{S}}$ consists of sets $E \Delta N$ with $E \in \mathbf{S}$ and $N$ contained in a $\mu$-null set. The completed measure $\bar{\mu}(E \Delta N) = \mu(E)$ is a complete measure on $\bar{\mathbf{S}}$.
