Since $F_1$ is a measurable kernel of $E$ and $(F_1 \cup F_2) - F_1 \subset E - F_1$, we have $\mu((F_1 \cup F_2) - F_1) = 0$. Similarly $\mu((F_1 \cup F_2) - F_2) = 0$. Hence $\mu(F_1 \Delta F_2) = 0$.
