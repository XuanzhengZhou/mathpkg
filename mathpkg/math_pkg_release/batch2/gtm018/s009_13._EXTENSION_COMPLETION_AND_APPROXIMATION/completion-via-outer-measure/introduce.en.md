Theorem C establishes the precise connection between two approaches to extending a $\sigma$-finite measure $\mu$: the outer measure construction (Carathéodory) and the completion procedure. The completion of the canonical extension coincides with the restriction of $\mu^*$ to the $\mu^*$-measurable sets.
