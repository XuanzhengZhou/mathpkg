If $F$ is a measurable kernel of $A \cap E$, then $\mu_*(A \cap E) = \mu(F) = \sum \bar{\mu}(F \cap E_n) \leq \sum \mu_*(A \cap E_n)$. The reverse inequality follows from Theorem D.
