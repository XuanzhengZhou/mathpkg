If $\phi$ is Borel measurable with $\phi(0)=0$ and $f$ is measurable, then the composition $\tilde{f} = \phi \circ f$ is measurable. The condition $\phi(0)=0$ ensures proper handling of the null set $N(f)$. This implies that $|f|^\alpha$, positive integer powers, and constant multiples of measurable functions are measurable.
