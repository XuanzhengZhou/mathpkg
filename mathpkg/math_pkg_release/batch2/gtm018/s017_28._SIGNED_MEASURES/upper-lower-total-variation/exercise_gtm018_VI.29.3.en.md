If $\mu$ is a signed measure and if $E$ is a measurable set, then
$$\mu^{+}(E) = \sup \left\{ \mu(F): E \supset F \in \mathbf{S} \right\} \quad \text{and} \quad \mu^{-}(E) = -\inf \left\{ \mu(F): E \supset F \in \mathbf{S} \right\}.$$
