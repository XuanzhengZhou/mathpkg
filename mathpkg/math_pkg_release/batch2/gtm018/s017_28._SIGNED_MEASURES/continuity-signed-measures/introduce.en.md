Signed measures are continuous along monotone sequences of measurable sets under the same finiteness condition required for ordinary measures. For increasing sequences, replace $\{E_n\}$ with the disjoint sequence $\{E_i - E_{i-1}\}$ of differences. For decreasing sequences, reduce to the increasing case by complementation, using Theorem 28.A to ensure finiteness of subtracted terms.
