If $\mu$ is a finite signed measure and if $\{E_n\}$ is a sequence of measurable sets such that $\lim_{n} E_n$ exists, (i.e. such that $\limsup_{n} E_n = \liminf_{n} E_n$), then
$$\mu(\lim_{n} E_n) = \lim_{n} \mu(E_n).$$
