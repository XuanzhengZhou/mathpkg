The inferior limit $\liminf_n E_n$ consists of points that belong to $E_n$ for all but finitely many indices $n$. It is expressed as $\bigcup_{n=1}^\infty \bigcap_{m=n}^\infty E_m$. One always has $\liminf_n E_n \subset \limsup_n E_n$.
