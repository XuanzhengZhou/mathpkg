For a mean fundamental sequence, the indefinite integrals converge pointwise on measurable sets to a limit set function $\nu$, which is countably additive. This limit is the indefinite integral of the $L^1$ limit function, and this theorem provides the mechanism for extending the domain of the integral from simple to general integrable functions.
