Continuity from above at zero captures the property that a set function vanishes on decreasing sequences converging to the empty set. Equicontinuity requires this behavior uniformly across a sequence of set functions. These concepts are essential for the characterization of mean convergence (Theorem C) and connect measure-theoretic continuity with $L^1$ convergence.
