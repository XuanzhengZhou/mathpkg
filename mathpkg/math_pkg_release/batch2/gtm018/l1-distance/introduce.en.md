The $L^1$ distance $\rho(f,g) = \int |f-g| d\mu$ is a seminorm making integrable functions into a seminormed space. It satisfies all metric axioms except definiteness: $\rho(f,g)=0$ only implies $f=g$ almost everywhere, not everywhere. Identifying functions equal a.e. yields the complete metric space $L^1$, the completion of integrable simple functions.
