Theorem B states that if an invariant $\sigma$-ring $\mathbf{T}$ is generated by a countable class of Baire sets of finite measure, then the metric space of finite-measure sets in $\mathbf{T}$ under the symmetric difference metric is separable. This is a key technical result used in proving the regularity of Haar measure.
