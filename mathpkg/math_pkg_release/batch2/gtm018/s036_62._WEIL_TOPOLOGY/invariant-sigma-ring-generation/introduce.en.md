Theorem A provides a concrete description of the invariant $\sigma$-ring generated by a class $\mathbf{E}$: it equals the ordinary $\sigma$-ring generated by all translates of sets in $\mathbf{E}$. This characterization simplifies the analysis of invariant $\sigma$-rings by reducing them to standard $\sigma$-ring generation.
