Theorem A of the Weil topology construction states that every measurable set $F$ of positive measure contains a measurable subset $G$ of positive, finite measure whose product $GG^{-1}$ is contained in any prescribed neighborhood $N$ from the class $\mathbf{N}$. This technical result is crucial for establishing that the class $\mathbf{N}$ forms a suitable neighborhood base for a group topology on a separated measurable group.
