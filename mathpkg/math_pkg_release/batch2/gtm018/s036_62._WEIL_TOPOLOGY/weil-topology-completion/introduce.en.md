Theorem H shows that the Weil topology completion $\hat{X}$ of a separated measurable group $X$ is a locally compact group containing $X$ densely. Moreover, the class of subsets $\hat{E}$ of $\hat{X}$ with $\hat{E} \cap X \in \mathbf{S}$ (the original $\sigma$-ring) forms a $\sigma$-ring containing all Baire sets of $\hat{X}$, allowing the extension of the measure structure.
