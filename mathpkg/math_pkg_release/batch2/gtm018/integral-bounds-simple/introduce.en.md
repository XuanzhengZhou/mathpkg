If a simple function is bounded between $\alpha$ and $\beta$ on a set $E$, its integral over $E$ is bounded between $\alpha\mu(E)$ and $\beta\mu(E)$. This is the simplest mean value estimate for integrals, a direct consequence of monotonicity when $\mu(E) < \infty$.
