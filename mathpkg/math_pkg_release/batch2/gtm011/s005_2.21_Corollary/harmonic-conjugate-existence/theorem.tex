Let $G$ be either the whole complex plane $\mathbb{C}$ or some open disk $B(0; R)$ with $0 < R \leq \infty$. If $u : G \to \mathbb{R}$ is a harmonic function, then $u$ has a harmonic conjugate on $G$. That is, there exists a harmonic function $v : G \to \mathbb{R}$ such that $f = u + iv$ is analytic on $G$.
