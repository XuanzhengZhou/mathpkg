7.12 Theorem from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $\gamma$ and $\sigma$ be two closed curves in $\mathbb{C} - \{0\}$ such that $\sigma(0) = \gamma(0) = 1$; then $\gamma \sim \sigma$ in $\mathbb{C} - \{0\}$ iff $n(\gamma; 0) = n(\sigma; 0)$....
