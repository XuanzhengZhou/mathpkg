Let $B_{\pm} = \overline{B}(\pm 1; \frac{1}{2})$, $G = B(0; 3) - (B_{+} \cup B_{-})$. Let $\gamma_1, \gamma_2, \gamma_3$ be curves whose traces are $|z - 1| = 1$, $|z + 1| = 1$, and $|z| = 2$, respectively. Give $\gamma_1, \gamma_2$, and $\gamma_3$ orientations such that $n(\gamma_1; w) + n(\gamma_2; w) + n(\gamma_3; w) = 0$ for all $w$ in $\mathbb{C} - G$.
