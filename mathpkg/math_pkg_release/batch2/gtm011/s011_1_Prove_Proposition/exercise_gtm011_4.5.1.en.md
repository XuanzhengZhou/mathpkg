Suppose $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ is analytic and define $\varphi: G \times G \to \mathbb{C}$ by $\varphi(z, w) = [f(z) - f(w)](z - w)^{-1}$ if $z \neq w$ and $\varphi(z, z) = f'(z)$. Prove that $\varphi$ is continuous and for each fixed $w$, $z \mapsto \varphi(z, w)$ is analytic.
