For $z$ in $G_-$ define $g(z) = \overline{f(\bar{z})}$ and for $z$ in $G_+ \cup G_0$ let $g(z) = f(z)$. It is easy to see that $g: G \to \mathbb{C}$ is continuous; it must be shown that $g$ is analytic.
