Let $G$ be a simply connected region and let $f: G \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be an analytic function such that $f(z) \neq 0$ for any $z$ in $G$. Then there is an analytic function $g: G \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ such that $f(z) = \exp g(z)$. If $z_0 \in G$ and $e^{w_0} = f(z_0)$, we may choose $g$ such that $g(z_0) = w_0$.
