Let $P: \mathbb{C} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be defined by $P(z) = \operatorname{Re} z$; show that $P$ is an open map but is not a closed map. (Hint: Consider the set $F = \{z: \operatorname{Im} z = (\operatorname{Re} z)^{-1} \text{ and } \operatorname{Re} z \neq 0\}$.)
