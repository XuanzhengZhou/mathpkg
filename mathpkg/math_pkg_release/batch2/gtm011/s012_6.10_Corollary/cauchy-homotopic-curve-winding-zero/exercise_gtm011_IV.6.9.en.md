Let $G$ be a region and let $\gamma_0$ and $\gamma_1$ be two closed smooth curves in $G$. Suppose $\gamma_0 \sim \gamma_1$ and $\Gamma$ satisfies (6.2). Also suppose that $\gamma_t(s) = \Gamma(s, t)$ is smooth for each $t$. If $w \in \mathbb{C} - G$ define $h(t) = n(\gamma_t; w)$ and show that $h$ is a continuous function on $[0, 1]$.
