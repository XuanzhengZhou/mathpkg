Two curves $\gamma_0$ and $\gamma_1$ from a point $a$ to a point $b$ in an open set $G$ are FEP homotopic (fixed endpoint homotopic) if there is a continuous map $\Gamma: I^2 \rightarrow G$ such that

$$\Gamma(s, 0) = \gamma_0(s), \quad \Gamma(s, 1) = \gamma_1(s)$$
$$\Gamma(0, t) = a, \quad \Gamma(1, t) = b$$

for $0 \leq s, t \leq 1$.
