Let $G$ be a region and let $f$ be an analytic function on $G$ with zeros $a_1, \ldots, a_m$ (repeated according to multiplicity). If $\gamma$ is a closed rectifiable curve in $G$ which does not pass through any point $a_k$ and if $\gamma \approx 0$ in $G$, then

$$\frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} \, dz = \sum_{k=1}^{m} n(\gamma; a_k).$$
