Let $f$ be an entire function, $M(r) = \sup \{|f(re^{i\theta})| : 0 \leq \theta \leq 2\pi\}$, and let $n(r)$ be the number of zeros of $f$ in $B(0; r)$ counted according to multiplicity. Suppose that $f(0) = 1$ and show that

$$
n(r) \log 2 \leq \log M(2r).
$$
