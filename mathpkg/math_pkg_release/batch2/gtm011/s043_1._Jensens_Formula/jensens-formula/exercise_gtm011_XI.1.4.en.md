**(a)** Using the notation of Exercise 2, prove that

$$
\int_{0}^{r} \frac{n(t)}{t} \, dt = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \log \left( \frac{r}{|a_k|} \right)
$$

where $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ are the zeros of $f$ in $B(0; r)$.

**(b)** Let $f$ be meromorphic without a pole at $z = 0$ and let $n(r)$ be the number of zeros of $f$ in $B(0; r)$ minus the number of poles (each counted according to multiplicity). Evaluate

$$
\int_{0}^{r} \frac{n(t)}{t} \, dt.
$$
