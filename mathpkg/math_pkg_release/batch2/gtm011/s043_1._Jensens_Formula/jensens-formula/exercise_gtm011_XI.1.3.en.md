In Jensen's Formula do not suppose that $f$ is analytic in a region containing $\bar{B}(0; r)$ but only that $f$ is meromorphic with no pole at $z = 0$. Evaluate

$$
\frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} \log |f(re^{i\theta})| \, d\theta.
$$
