Assume that $f$ is not the identically zero function and let $K$ be a compact subset of $G$ such that $f(z) \neq 0$ for all $z \in K$. Show that

$$\frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{f'_n(z)}{f_n(z)}$$

and the convergence is uniform over $K$.
