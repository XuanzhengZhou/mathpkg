Let $G$ be a region and $f: \partial_\infty G \to \mathbb{R}$ a continuous function. The \textbf{Perron family} (or class of admissible subharmonic functions) associated with $f$ is

$$\mathcal{P}(f, G) = \{\, \varphi: G \to \mathbb{R} \text{ subharmonic} : \limsup_{z \to a} \varphi(z) \leq f(a) \text{ for all } a \in \partial_\infty G \,\}.$$
