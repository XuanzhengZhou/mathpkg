4.13 Definition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $Y$ is a subset of a topological space $(X, \mathcal{T})$ then $\mathcal{T}_Y$ is called the relative topology on $Y$. A subset $W$ of $Y$ is relatively open in $Y$ if $W \in \mathcal{T}_Y$; $W$ is relatively closed in $Y$ if $Y - W \in \mathcal{T}_Y$.
