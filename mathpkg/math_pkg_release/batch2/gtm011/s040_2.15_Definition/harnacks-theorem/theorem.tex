Let $G$ be a region.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] The metric space $\text{Har}(G)$ is complete.
\item[(b)] If $\{u_n\}$ is a sequence in $\text{Har}(G)$ such that $u_1 \leq u_2 \leq \ldots$ then either $u_n(z) \to \infty$ uniformly on compact subsets of $G$ or $\{u_n\}$ converges in $\text{Har}(G)$ to a harmonic function.
\end{enumerate}
