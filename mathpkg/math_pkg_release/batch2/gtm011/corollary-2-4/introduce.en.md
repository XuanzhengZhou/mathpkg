2.4 Corollary from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $f_n: G \to \mathbb{C}$ is analytic and $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} f_n(z)$ converges uniformly on compact sets to $f(z)$ then

$$f^{(k)}(z) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} f_n^{(k)}(z).$$
