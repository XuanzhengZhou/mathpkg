4.1 Proposition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $\gamma : [0, 1] \to \mathbb{C}$ is a closed rectifiable curve and $a \notin \{\gamma\}$ then

$$\frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{dz}{z-a}$$

is an integer....
