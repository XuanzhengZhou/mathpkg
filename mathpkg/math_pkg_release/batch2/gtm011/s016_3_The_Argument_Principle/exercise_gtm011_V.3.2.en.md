Suppose $f$ is analytic on $\overline{B}(0; 1)$ and satisfies $|f(z)| < 1$ for $|z| = 1$. Find the number of solutions (counting multiplicities) of the equation $f(z) = z^n$ where $n$ is an integer larger than or equal to $1$.
