Let $\lambda > 1$ and show that the equation $\lambda - z - e^{-z} = 0$ has exactly one solution in the half plane $\{z: \operatorname{Re} z > 0\}$. Show that this solution must be real. What happens to the solution as $\lambda \to 1$?
