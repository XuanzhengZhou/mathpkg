2.5 Theorem from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $f: D \rightarrow D$ be a one-one analytic map of $D$ onto itself and suppose $f(a) = 0$. Then there is a complex number $c$ with $|c| = 1$ such that $f = c\varphi_a$....
