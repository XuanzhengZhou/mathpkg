4.2 Definition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $G$ be a region and let $a \in \partial_\infty G$. A barrier for $G$ at $a$ is a family $\{\psi_r: r > 0\}$ of functions such that:

(a) $\psi_r$ is defined and superharmonic on $G(a; r)$ with $0 \leq \psi_r(z) \leq 1$;
