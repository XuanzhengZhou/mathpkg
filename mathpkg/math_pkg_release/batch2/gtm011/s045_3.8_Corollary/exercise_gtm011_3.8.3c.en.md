Find all entire functions $f$ of finite order such that $f(\log n) = n$.

(Hint: Consider the interpolation problem. The sequence $\{\log n\}$ has a specific growth rate; compare with the order of $f$ and apply the uniqueness results from parts (a) and (b).)
