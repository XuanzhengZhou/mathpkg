Let $f$ be analytic in a region $G$ and suppose that $f$ is not identically zero. Let $G_0 = G - \{z: f(z) = 0\}$ and define $h: G_0 \to \mathbb{R}$ by $h(z) = \log |f(z)|$. Show that

$$\frac{\partial h}{\partial x} - i \frac{\partial h}{\partial y} = \frac{f'}{f}$$

on $G_0$.
