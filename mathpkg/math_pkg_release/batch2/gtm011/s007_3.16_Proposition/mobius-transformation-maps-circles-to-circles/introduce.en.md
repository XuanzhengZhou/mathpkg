This proposition establishes that Möbius transformations map circles to circles in the extended complex plane $\mathbb{C}_\infty$. Moreover, one can prescribe the images of any three distinct points on the source circle, and this prescription uniquely determines the Möbius transformation. This property is fundamental to the conformal mapping capabilities of Möbius transformations and underlies the subsequent discussion of symmetry and orientation.
