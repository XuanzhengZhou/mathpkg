Let $Sz = \frac{az + b}{cz + d}$ and $Tz = \frac{\alpha z + \beta}{\gamma z + \delta}$; show that $S = T$ if and only if there is a nonzero complex number $\lambda$ such that $\alpha = \lambda a$, $\beta = \lambda b$, $\gamma = \lambda c$, $\delta = \lambda d$.
