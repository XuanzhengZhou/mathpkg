This theorem establishes the existence of the Riemann-Stieltjes integral $\int_a^b f \, d\gamma$ for a continuous integrand $f$ and an integrator $\gamma$ of bounded variation. The integral is defined as the limit of Riemann-Stieltjes sums $\sum f(\tau_k)[\gamma(t_k) - \gamma(t_{k-1})]$ as the mesh of the partition tends to zero. This is the foundational construction for defining contour integrals along paths in the complex plane.
