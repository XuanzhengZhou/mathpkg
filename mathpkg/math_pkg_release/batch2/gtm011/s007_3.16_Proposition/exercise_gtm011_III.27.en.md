If $G$ is a group and $N$ is a subgroup, then $N$ is said to be a normal subgroup of $G$ if $S^{-1}TS \in N$ whenever $T \in N$ and $S \in G$. $G$ is a simple group if the only normal subgroups of $G$ are $\{I\}$ ($I =$ the identity of $G$) and $G$ itself. Prove that the group $\mathcal{M}$ of Möbius transformations is a simple group.
