If $\gamma$ is a closed rectifiable curve in $\mathbb{C}$ then for $a \notin \{\gamma\}$

$$n(\gamma; a) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma}(z - a)^{-1} dz$$

is called the \text{index} of $\gamma$ with respect to the point $a$. It is also sometimes called the \text{winding number} of $\gamma$ around $a$.
