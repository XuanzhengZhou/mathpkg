For a non-empty subset $A \subseteq \mathbb{R}$:
\begin{itemize}
\item The \textbf{supremum} (or least upper bound) of $A$, denoted $\sup A$, is the smallest real number $s$ such that $a \leq s$ for all $a \in A$.
\item The \textbf{infimum} (or greatest lower bound) of $A$, denoted $\inf A$, is the largest real number $t$ such that $t \leq a$ for all $a \in A$.
\end{itemize}
