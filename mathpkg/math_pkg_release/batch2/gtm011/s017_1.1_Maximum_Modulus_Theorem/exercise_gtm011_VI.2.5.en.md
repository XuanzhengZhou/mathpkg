Let $f$ be analytic in $D = \{z : |z| < 1\}$ and suppose that $|f(z)| \leq M$ for all $z$ in $D$.

(a) If $f(z_k) = 0$ for $1 \leq k \leq n$, show that

$$|f(z)| \leq M \prod_{k=1}^{n} \frac{|z - z_k|}{|1 - \bar{z}_k z|}$$

for $|z| < 1$.

(b) If $f(z_k) = 0$ for $1 \leq k \leq n$, each zero being repeated according to its multiplicity, show that

$$|f(0)| \leq M \prod_{k=1}^{n} |z_k|.$$
