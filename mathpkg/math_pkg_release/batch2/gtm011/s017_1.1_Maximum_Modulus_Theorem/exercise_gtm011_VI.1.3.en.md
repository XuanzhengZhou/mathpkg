(a) Let $f$ be entire and non-constant. For any positive real number $c$ show that the closure of $\{z : |f(z)| < c\}$ is the set $\{z : |f(z)| \leq c\}$.

(b) Let $p$ be a non-constant polynomial and show that each component of $\{z : |p(z)| < c\}$ contains a zero of $p$. (Hint: Use Exercise 2.)

(c) If $p$ is a non-constant polynomial and $c > 0$, show that $\{z : |p(z)| = c\}$ is the union of a finite number of closed paths. Discuss the behavior of these paths as $c \to \infty$.
