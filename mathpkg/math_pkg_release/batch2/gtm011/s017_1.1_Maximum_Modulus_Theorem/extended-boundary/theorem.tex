If $G \subset \mathbb{C}$, let $\partial_{\infty} G$ denote the boundary of $G$ in $\mathbb{C}_{\infty}$ and call it the extended boundary of $G$. Then

$$\partial_{\infty} G = \begin{cases}
\partial G & \text{if } G \text{ is bounded}, \\
\partial G \cup \{\infty\} & \text{if } G \text{ is unbounded}.
\end{cases}$$
