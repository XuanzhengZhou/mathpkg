Find the domain of analyticity of

$$f(z) = \frac{1}{2i} \log \left( \frac{1 + iz}{1 - iz} \right);$$

also, show that $\tan f(z) = z$ (i.e., $f$ is a branch of $\arctan z$). Show that

$$f(z) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} (-1)^k \frac{z^{2k+1}}{2k+1}$$

for $|z| < 1$.
