Corollary 2.13 extends Cauchy's Integral Formula to derivatives of arbitrary order. It shows that the $n$-th derivative of an analytic function at a point can be expressed as a contour integral involving $f(w)/(w-a)^{n+1}$. This formula follows by differentiating the Cauchy Integral Formula under the integral sign and matching coefficients with the Taylor series expansion. It is a cornerstone result used throughout complex analysis, particularly for obtaining estimates like Cauchy's Estimate.
