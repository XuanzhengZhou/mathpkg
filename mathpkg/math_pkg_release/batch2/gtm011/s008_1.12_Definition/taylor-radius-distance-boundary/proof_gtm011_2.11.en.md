Since $R = d(a, \partial G)$, the open ball $B(a; R)$ is contained in $G$. Hence $f$ is analytic on $B(a; R)$. The result now follows directly from Theorem 2.8, which guarantees a power series expansion converging for $|z - a| < R$ with radius of convergence at least $R$.
