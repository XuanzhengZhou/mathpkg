3.5 Definition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
A mapping of the form $S(z) = \frac{az+b}{cz+d}$ is called a linear fractional transformation. If $a, b, c$, and $d$ also satisfy $ad-bc \neq 0$ then $S(z)$ is called a Möbius transformation.
