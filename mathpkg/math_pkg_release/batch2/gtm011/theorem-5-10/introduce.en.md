5.10 Theorem from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
There is a path in $\mathcal{S}(G)$ from $(a, [f]_a)$ to $(b, [g]_b)$ iff there is a path $\gamma$ in $G$ from $a$ to $b$ such that $[g]_b$ is the analytic continuation of $[f]_a$ along $\gamma$....
