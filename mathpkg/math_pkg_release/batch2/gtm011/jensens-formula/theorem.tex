Let $f$ be analytic in an open set containing $\bar{B}(0; r)$ and suppose that $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ are the zeros of $f$ in $B(0; r)$ repeated according to multiplicity. If $f(0) \neq 0$ then

$$\log |f(0)| = -\sum_{k=1}^{n} \log \left( \frac{r}{|a_k|} \right) + \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} \log |f(re^{i\theta})| d\theta.$$
