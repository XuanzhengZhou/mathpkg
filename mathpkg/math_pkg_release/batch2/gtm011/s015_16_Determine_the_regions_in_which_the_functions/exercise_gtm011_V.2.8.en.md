Let $\gamma$ be the polygonal path defined in Exercise 6 and evaluate $\displaystyle \int_{\gamma} \pi(z^2 - a^2)^{-1} \cot \pi z \, dz$ for $a \neq n$ integer. Show that $\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} \int_{\gamma} \pi(z^2 - a^2)^{-1} \cot \pi z \, dz = 0$, and consequently

$$\pi \cot \pi a = \frac{1}{a} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{2a}{a^2 - n^2}$$

for $a$ not an integer.
