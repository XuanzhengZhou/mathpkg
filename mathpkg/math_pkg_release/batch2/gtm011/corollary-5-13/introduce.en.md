5.13 Corollary from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $K \subset X$ is compact and $f: X \to \mathbb{C}$ is continuous then there are points $x_0$ and $y_0$ in $K$ with

$$|f(x_0)| = \sup \{|f(x)|: x \in K\} \text{ and } |f(y_0)| = \inf \{|f(x)|: x \in K\}.$$...
