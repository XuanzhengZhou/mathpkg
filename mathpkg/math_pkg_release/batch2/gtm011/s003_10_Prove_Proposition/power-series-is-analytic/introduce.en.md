A power series defines an analytic (indeed, infinitely differentiable) function within its disk of convergence, and the derivative can be obtained by termwise differentiation. Moreover, the coefficients are uniquely determined by the Taylor formula $a_n = f^{(n)}(a)/n!$. This shows that power series are analytic functions.
