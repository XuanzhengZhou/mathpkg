The Hadamard formula gives the radius of convergence of a power series as $R = 1 / \limsup |a_n|^{1/n}$. Inside the disk of convergence the series converges absolutely and uniformly on compact subsets. Outside, it diverges. This generalizes the geometric series and is the fundamental result governing power series in complex analysis.
