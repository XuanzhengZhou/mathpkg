Let $f: (X, d) \to (\Omega, \rho)$ be a function. The following are equivalent statements:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(a)] $f$ is continuous;
    \item[(b)] If $\Delta$ is open in $\Omega$ then $f^{-1}(\Delta)$ is open in $X$;
    \item[(c)] If $\Gamma$ is closed in $\Omega$ then $f^{-1}(\Gamma)$ is closed in $X$.
\end{enumerate}
