Let $p = (p_1, \ldots, p_n)$ and $q = (q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ be points in $\mathbb{R}^n$ with $p_k < q_k$ for each $k$. Let $R = [p_1, q_1] \times \ldots \times [p_n, q_n]$ and show that
$$\operatorname{diam} R = d(p, q) = \left[ \sum_{k=1}^{n} (q_k - p_k)^2 \right]^{\frac{1}{2}}.$$
