2.8 Proposition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
(a) If $A \subset X$ is connected and $A \subset B \subset A^-$, then $B$ is connected.
(b) If $C$ is a component of $X$ then $C$ is closed.
The proof is left as an exercise....
