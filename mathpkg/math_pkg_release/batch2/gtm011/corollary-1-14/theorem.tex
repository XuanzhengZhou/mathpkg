Let $G$ be an open subset of the plane and let $E$ be a subset of $\mathbb{C}_{\infty} - G$ such that $E$ meets every component of $\mathbb{C}_{\infty} - G$. Let $R(G, E)$ be the set of rational functions with poles in $E$ and consider $R(G, E)$ as a subspace of $H(G)$. If $f \in H(G)$ then there is a sequence $\{R_n\}$ in $R(G, E)$ such that $f = \lim R_n$. That is, $R(G, E)$ is dense in $H(G)$.
