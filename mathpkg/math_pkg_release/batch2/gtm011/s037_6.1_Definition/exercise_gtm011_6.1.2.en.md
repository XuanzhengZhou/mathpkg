Which of the following are analytic manifolds? What is its analytic structure if it is a manifold?

(a) A cone in $\mathbb{R}^3$.

(b) $\{(x_1, x_2, x_3) \in \mathbb{R}^3 : x_1^2 + x_2^2 + x_3^2 = 1$ or $x_1^2 + x_2^2 > 1$ and $x_3 = 0\}$.
