Let $f: \mathbb{C}_{\infty} \to \mathbb{C}_{\infty}$ be an analytic function.

(c) If $f$ is one-one, show that either $f(z) = az + b$ (some $a, b$ in $\mathbb{C}$) or $f(z) = \frac{a}{z - c} + b$ (some $a, b, c$ in $\mathbb{C}$).
