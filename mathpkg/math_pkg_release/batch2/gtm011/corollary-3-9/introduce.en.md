3.9 Corollary from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $f$ and $G$ are as in Theorem 3.7 and $f$ is not constant then

$$|f(z)| < \sup \{ |f(w)| : w \in \partial G \}$$

for all $z$ in $G$.
