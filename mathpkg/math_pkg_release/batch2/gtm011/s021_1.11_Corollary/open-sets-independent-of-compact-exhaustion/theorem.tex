The collection of open sets in $(C(G, \Omega), \rho)$ is independent of the choice of the sets $\{K_n\}$. That is, if $G = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} K_n'$ where each $K_n'$ is compact and $K_n' \subset \operatorname{int} K_{n+1}'$, and if $\mu$ is the metric defined by the sets $\{K_n'\}$, then a set is open in $(C(G, \Omega), \mu)$ iff it is open in $(C(G, \Omega), \rho)$.
