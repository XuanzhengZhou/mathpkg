If $(X, \mathcal{T})$ is a topological space and $Y \subset X$, define

$$\mathcal{T}_Y = \{U \cap Y : U \in \mathcal{T}\}.$$

Then $\mathcal{T}_Y$ is a topology on $Y$, called the \textbf{relative topology} on $Y$. A subset $W$ of $Y$ is \textbf{relatively open} in $Y$ if $W \in \mathcal{T}_Y$; $W$ is \textbf{relatively closed} in $Y$ if $Y \setminus W \in \mathcal{T}_Y$.
