Let $G$ be a region in the plane that does not contain zero and let $G^*$ be the set of all points $z$ such that there is a point $w$ in $G$ where $z$ and $w$ are symmetric with respect to the circle $|\xi| = 1$. (See Definition III.3.17.)

Show that
$$G^* = \{z : (1/\bar{z}) \in G\}.$$
