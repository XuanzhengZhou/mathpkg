Let $(X, \rho)$ be a covering space of $\Omega$ and let $\gamma$ be a path in $\Omega$. A path $\tilde{\gamma}$ in $X$ is called a lifting of $\gamma$ if $\rho \circ \tilde{\gamma} = \gamma$.
