Let $K$ be a compact subset of the open set $G$ and suppose that any bounded component $D$ of $G - K$ has $D^- \cap \partial G \neq \square$. Then every component of $\mathbb{C}_{\infty} - K$ meets $\mathbb{C}_{\infty} - G$.

*Note: This exercise is a lemma useful in proving Exercise 4. The statement is truncated in the source text.*
