Let $K$ be a compact subset of the complex plane. The \textit{polynomially convex hull} of $K$, denoted by $\hat{K}$, is defined to be the set of all points $w$ such that for every polynomial $p$
$$|p(w)| \leq \max \{|p(z)| : z \in K\}.$$
That is, if the right hand side of this inequality is denoted by $\|p\|_K$, then
$$\hat{K} = \{w : |p(w)| \leq \|p\|_K \text{ for all polynomials } p\}.$$
