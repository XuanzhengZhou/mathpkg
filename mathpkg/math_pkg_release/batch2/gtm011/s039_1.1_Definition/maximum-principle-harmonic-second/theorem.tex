Let $G$ be a region and let $u$ and $v$ be two bounded continuous real valued functions on $G$ that have the MVP. If for each point $a$ in the extended boundary $\partial_\infty G$,
$$\limsup_{z \to a} u(z) \leq \liminf_{z \to a} v(z),$$
then either $u(z) < v(z)$ for all $z$ in $G$ or $u \equiv v$.
