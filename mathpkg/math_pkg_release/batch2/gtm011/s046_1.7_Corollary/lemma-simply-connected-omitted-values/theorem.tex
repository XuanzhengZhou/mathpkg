Let $G$ be a simply connected region and suppose that $f$ is an analytic function on $G$ that does not assume the values $0$ or $1$. Then there exist analytic functions $\ell$, $F$, $H$, $g$ on $G$ such that

$$e^{\ell} = f, \quad F = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \ell, \quad H = \sqrt{F} - \sqrt{F-1}, \quad e^g = H,$$

and

$$f = -\exp[\pi i \cosh(2g)].$$
