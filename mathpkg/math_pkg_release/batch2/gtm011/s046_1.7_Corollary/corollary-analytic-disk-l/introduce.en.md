Corollary 1.11 refines Corollary 1.7 by replacing the explicit constant $\frac{1}{72}$ with Bloch's constant $L$, yielding a sharper bound. The conclusion that $f(B(0; R))$ contains a disk of radius $R|f'(0)|L$ follows from Proposition 1.10 and the definition of Bloch's constant as the infimum of all possible such radii. This is the best possible uniform bound of its type.
