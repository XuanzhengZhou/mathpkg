The space $H(G)$ of analytic functions on an open set $G$, equipped with the topology of uniform convergence on compact subsets, is a complete metric space. This follows directly from Theorem 2.1 — the limit of a uniformly convergent sequence of analytic functions is analytic — together with the completeness of $C(G, \mathbb{C})$.
