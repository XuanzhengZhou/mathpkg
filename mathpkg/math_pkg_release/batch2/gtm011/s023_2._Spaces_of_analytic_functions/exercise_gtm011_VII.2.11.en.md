Suppose that $\{f_n\}$ is a sequence in $H(G)$, $f$ is a non-constant analytic function, and $f_n \to f$ in $H(G)$. Let $a \in G$ and $\alpha = f(a)$. Show that there is a sequence $\{a_n\}$ in $G$ such that:
(i) $a = \lim a_n$;
(ii) $f_n(a_n) = \alpha$ for sufficiently large $n$.
