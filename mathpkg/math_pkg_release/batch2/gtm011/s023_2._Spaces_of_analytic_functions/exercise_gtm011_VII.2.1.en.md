Let $f, f_1, f_2, \ldots$ be elements of $H(G)$ and show that $f_n \to f$ in $H(G)$ (i.e., uniformly on compact subsets of $G$) if and only if for each closed rectifiable curve $\gamma$ in $G$, $f_n(z) \to f(z)$ uniformly for $z$ in $\{\gamma\}$ (the trace of $\gamma$).
