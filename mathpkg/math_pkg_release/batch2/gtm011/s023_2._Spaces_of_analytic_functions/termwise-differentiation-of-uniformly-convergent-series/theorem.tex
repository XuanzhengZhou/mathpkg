Let $G \subset \mathbb{C}$ be open and let $f_n: G \to \mathbb{C}$ be analytic for each $n \geq 1$. If $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} f_n(z)$ converges uniformly on compact subsets of $G$ to a function $f(z)$, then $f$ is analytic and for each $k \geq 1$,

$$f^{(k)}(z) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} f_n^{(k)}(z),$$

where the series of derivatives also converges uniformly on compact subsets of $G$.
