Let $G$ be a region and let $M(G)$ denote the set of all meromorphic functions on $G$, considered as a subset of $C(G, \mathbb{C}_\infty)$ with the chordal metric. Let $\{f_n\}$ be a sequence in $M(G)$ and suppose $f_n \to f$ in $C(G, \mathbb{C}_\infty)$. Then either $f$ is meromorphic or $f \equiv \infty$. Moreover, if each $f_n$ is analytic, then either $f$ is analytic or $f \equiv \infty$.
