5.12 Corollary from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $X \to \mathbb{R}$ is continuous and $K \subset X$ is compact then there are points $x_0$ and $y_0$ in $K$ with $f(x_0) = \sup \{f(x): x \in K\}$ and $f(y_0) = \inf \{f(x): x \in K\}$....
