2.13 Corollary from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ is analytic and $\bar{B}(a; r) \subset G$ then

$$f^{(n)}(a) = \frac{n!}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{f(w)}{(w-a)^{n+1}} dw$$

where $\gamma(t) = a + re^{it}$, $0 \leq t \leq 2\pi$.
