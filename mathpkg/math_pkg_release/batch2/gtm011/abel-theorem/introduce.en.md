3.4 Theorem (Conformal Mapping - Angle Preservation) from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
If $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ is analytic then $f$ is conformal (angle-preserving) at each point $z_0$ in $G$ where $f'(z_0) \neq 0$....
