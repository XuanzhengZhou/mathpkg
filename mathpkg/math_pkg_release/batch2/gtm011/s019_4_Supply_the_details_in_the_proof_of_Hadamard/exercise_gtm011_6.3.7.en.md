Let $f$ be analytic in $\operatorname{ann}(0; R_1, R_2)$ and not identically zero; define
\[
I_2(r) = \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} |f(re^{i\theta})|^2 \, d\theta.
\]
Show that $\log I_2(r)$ is a convex function of $\log r$, for $R_1 < r < R_2$.
