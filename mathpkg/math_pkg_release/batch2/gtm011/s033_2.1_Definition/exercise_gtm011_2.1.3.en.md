Let $f$ be an entire function, $D_0 = B(0; 1)$, and let $\gamma$ be a path from $0$ to $b$. Show that if $\{(f_t, D_t): 0 \leq t \leq 1\}$ is a continuation of $(f, D_0)$ along $\gamma$ then $f_1(z) = f(z)$ for all $z$ in $D_1$. (This exercise is rather easy; it is actually an exercise in the use of the terminology.)
