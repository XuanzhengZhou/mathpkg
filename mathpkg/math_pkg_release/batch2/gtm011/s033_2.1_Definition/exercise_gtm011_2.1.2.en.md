Let $\sigma: [0, 1] \to \mathbb{C}$ be a path from $1$ to $-1$ given by $\sigma(t) = \exp(\pi i t)$, and let $D_0 = B(1; 1)$. Let $f_0$ be a function element on $D_0$.

(a) What is the largest $\delta > 0$ such that $D_0 \cap B(\sigma(t); \delta) \neq \emptyset$ for all $t$?

(b) Find an analytic continuation $\{(g_t, B_t): 0 \leq t \leq 1\}$ of $(f_0, D_0)$ along $\sigma$ and show that $[g_1]_1 = [g_0]_1$.
