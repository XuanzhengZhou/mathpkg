2.6 Proposition from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
Let $f: G \to \mathbb{C}$ be analytic and suppose $\bar{B}(a; r) \subset G(r > 0)$. If $\gamma(t) = a + re^{it}$, $0 \leq t \leq 2\pi$, then

$$f(z) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{f(w)}{w - z} dw$$

for $|z - a| < r$....
