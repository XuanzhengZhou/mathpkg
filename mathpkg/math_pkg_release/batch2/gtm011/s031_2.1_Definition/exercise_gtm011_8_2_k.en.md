If \(\mathcal{I}\) is a proper free ideal show that for any finite subset \(\mathcal{S}\) of \(\mathcal{I}\), \(\mathcal{Z}(\mathcal{S}) \neq \varnothing\). Use this to show that \(\mathcal{I}\) can contain no polynomials.
