Show that if there is an integer \(k\) such that the series

\[
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{b_n}{a_n^{k+1}}
\]

converges absolutely, then the series

\[
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[ \frac{b_n}{z - a_n} + \frac{b_n}{a_n} \sum_{j=0}^{k_n-1} \left( \frac{z}{a_n} \right)^j \right]
\]

converges absolutely if \(k_n = k\) for all \(n\).
