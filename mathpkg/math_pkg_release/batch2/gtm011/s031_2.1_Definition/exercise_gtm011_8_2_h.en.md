Let \(\mathcal{I}\) be a fixed ideal and prove that there is an \(f\) in \(H(G)\) with \(\mathcal{Z}(f) = \mathcal{Z}(\mathcal{I})\) and \(\mathcal{I} \subset (f)\). Also show that \(\mathcal{I} = (f)\) if \(\mathcal{I}\) is finitely generated.
