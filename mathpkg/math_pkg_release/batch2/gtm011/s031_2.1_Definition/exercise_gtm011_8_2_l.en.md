Let \(\mathcal{I}\) be a proper free ideal; then \(\mathcal{I}\) is a maximal ideal if and only if whenever \(g \in H(G)\) and \(\mathcal{Z}(g) \cap \mathcal{Z}(f) \neq \varnothing\) for all \(f \in \mathcal{I}\), then \(g \in \mathcal{I}\).
