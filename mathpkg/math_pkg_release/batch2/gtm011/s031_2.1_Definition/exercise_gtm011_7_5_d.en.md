Suppose that \(z \in \{a_1, a_2, \ldots\}\); show that \(z\) is a removable singularity of the function \(f\) defined in part (c). Furthermore, show that \(f(z) = 0\) and that the multiplicity of this zero equals the number of times that \(z\) appears in the sequence \(\{a_1, a_2, \ldots\}\).
