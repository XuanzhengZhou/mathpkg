Let \(\{a_n\}\) be a sequence of distinct points in \(G\) with no limit point in \(G\). Let \(\mathcal{I} = \{f \in H(G) : f(a_n) = 0 \text{ for all but a finite number of the } a_n\}\). Show that \(\mathcal{I}\) is a proper free ideal in \(H(G)\).
