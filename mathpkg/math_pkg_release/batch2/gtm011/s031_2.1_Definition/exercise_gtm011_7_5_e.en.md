Show that

\[
f(z) = \prod_{n=1}^{\infty} \left( 1 - \frac{z}{a_n} \right) \exp\left[ \frac{z}{a_n} + \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{z}{a_n} \right)^2 + \ldots + \frac{1}{k_n} \left( \frac{z}{a_n} \right)^{k_n} \right].
\]
