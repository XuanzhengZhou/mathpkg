\textbf{Lemma 1.10.} Let $K$ be a compact subset of $\mathbb{C}$ and let $E$ be a subset of $\mathbb{C}_\infty - K$ that meets each component of $\mathbb{C}_\infty - K$. Define $B(E)$ as the closure in $C(K, \mathbb{C})$ of the set of rational functions whose poles lie in $E$. If $a \in \mathbb{C} - K$, then $(z - a)^{-1} \in B(E)$.
