Prove that $\frac{\zeta(z-1)}{\zeta(z)} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{\varphi(n)}{n^z}$ for $\operatorname{Re} z > 1$, where $\varphi(n)$ is Euler's totient function, the number of integers less than $n$ and relatively prime to $n$.
