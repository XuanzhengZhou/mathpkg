Lemma 1.5 is a technical approximation result that bridges Cauchy integrals and rational functions. It states that the Cauchy-type integral $\int_\gamma f(w)/(w-z) dw$ can be uniformly approximated on a compact set $K$ (disjoint from the curve $\gamma$) by a rational function whose poles all lie on the trace of $\gamma$. The proof constructs this rational function as a Riemann sum approximation of the integral, exploiting the uniform continuity of the integrand on the compact product set to control the error.
