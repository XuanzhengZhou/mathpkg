\textbf{Theorem 8.14.} The Riemann zeta function can be defined to be meromorphic in the complex plane with only a simple pole at $z = 1$ and $\operatorname{Res}(\zeta; 1) = 1$. For $z \neq 1$, $\zeta$ satisfies Riemann's functional equation:
$$\zeta(z) = 2^z \pi^{z-1} \sin\left(\frac{\pi z}{2}\right) \Gamma(1-z) \, \zeta(1-z).$$
