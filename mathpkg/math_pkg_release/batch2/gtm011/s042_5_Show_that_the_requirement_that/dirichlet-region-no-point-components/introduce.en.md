This corollary gives a purely geometric sufficient condition for a region to be a Dirichlet Region: every component of the complement in the extended complex plane must contain more than one point. Under this hypothesis, Theorem 4.9 guarantees a barrier at every boundary point, and Theorem 4.3 then yields that the Perron solution attains all prescribed boundary values.
