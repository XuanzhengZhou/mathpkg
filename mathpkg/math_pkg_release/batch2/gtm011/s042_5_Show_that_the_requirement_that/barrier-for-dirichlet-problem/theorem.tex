Let $G$ be a region and let $a \in \partial_\infty G$. A \textbf{barrier} for $G$ at $a$ is a family $\{\psi_r: r > 0\}$ of functions such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\psi_r$ is defined and superharmonic on $G(a; r)$ with $0 \leq \psi_r(z) \leq 1$;
\item[(b)] $\displaystyle\lim_{z \to a} \psi_r(z) = 0$;
\item[(c)] $\displaystyle\lim_{z \to w} \psi_r(z) = 1$ for $w$ in $G \cap \partial B(a; r)$.
\end{enumerate}
where $G(a; r) = G \cap B(a; r)$ for all $r > 0$.
