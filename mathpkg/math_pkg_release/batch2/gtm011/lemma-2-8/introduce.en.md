2.8 Lemma from Conway's "Functions of One Complex Variable I."
A set $\mathcal{F}$ in $H(G)$ is locally bounded iff for each compact set $K \subset G$ there is a constant $M$ such that

$$|f(z)| \leq M$$

for all $f$ in $\mathcal{F}$ and $z$ in $K$.

The proof is left to the reader.
