For every sequence of integers $r_1 \ge r_2 \ge \cdots \ge r_k \ge 0$ one can define a fiber subbundle $S_{r_1,\ldots,r_k}$ of $J^k(X, Y)$ (with respect to the projection $J^k(X, Y) \to X \times Y$) such that a smooth map $f: X \to Y$ whose $k$-jet extension $j^k f$ is transversal to all $S_{r_1,\ldots,r_k}$ yields a well-defined descending chain of submanifolds
$$S_{r_1}(f) \supset S_{r_1,r_2}(f) \supset \cdots \supset S_{r_1,\ldots,r_k}(f)$$
in $X$, where each $S_{r_1,\ldots,r_i}(f) = (j^i f)^{-1}(S_{r_1,\ldots,r_i})$.
