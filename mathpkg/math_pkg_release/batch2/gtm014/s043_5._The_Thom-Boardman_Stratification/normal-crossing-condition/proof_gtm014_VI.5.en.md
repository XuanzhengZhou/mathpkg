Condition NC (Normal Crossing) is a necessary condition for stability of Boardman maps. For distinct points $x_j \in S_{I_j}(f)$ with common image $y$, the images of the tangent spaces $(df)_{x_j}(T_{x_j} S_{I_j}(f))$ must be in general position in $T_y Y$. This condition implies in particular that $f$ restricted to each Boardman stratum with terminal index $0$ is an immersion with normal crossings, and that the images of different strata intersect transversely. The condition is a precise formulation of the requirement that self-intersections of the singular set behave generically.
