The ring $C_p^\infty(X)$ of germs of smooth functions at $p$ is a local ring: its unique maximal ideal $\mathcal{M}_p(X)$ consists of germs of functions vanishing at $p$. The evaluation map $[f]_p \mapsto f(p)$ identifies the residue field with $\mathbf{R}$. This algebraic structure is essential for the Generalized Malgrange Preparation Theorem.
