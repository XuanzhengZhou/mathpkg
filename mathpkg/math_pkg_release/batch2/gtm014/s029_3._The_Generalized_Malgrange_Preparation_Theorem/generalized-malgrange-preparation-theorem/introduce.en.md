The Generalized Malgrange Preparation Theorem states that a finitely generated $C_p^\infty(X)$-module $A$ is also finitely generated over $C_q^\infty(Y)$ (via pullback along $\phi$) if and only if the quotient $A/\mathcal{M}_q(Y)A$ is finite-dimensional. The forward direction is automatic; the converse uses Nakayama's Lemma and is proved by decomposing $\phi$ into an immersion followed by a sequence of submersions, then applying the submersion and immersion cases separately.
