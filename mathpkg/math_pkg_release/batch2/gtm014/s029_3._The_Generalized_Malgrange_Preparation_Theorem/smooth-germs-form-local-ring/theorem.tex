$C_p^\infty(X)$ is a local ring if the ring operations are given by $[f]_p + [g]_p = [f + g]_p$ and $[f]_p \cdot [g]_p = [fg]_p$ where $f + g$ and $fg$ are assumed to be defined on $\operatorname{dom} f \cap \operatorname{dom} g$ which is a neighborhood of $p$. Let

$$M_p(X) = \{[f]_p \in C_p^\infty(X) \mid f(p) = 0\}.$$

Then $M_p(X)$ is the unique maximal ideal.
