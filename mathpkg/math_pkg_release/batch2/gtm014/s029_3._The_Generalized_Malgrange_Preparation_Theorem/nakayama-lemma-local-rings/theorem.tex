Let $\mathcal{R}$ be a commutative local ring with maximal ideal $\mathcal{M}$, and let $A$ be a finitely generated $\mathcal{R}$-module. If $A = \mathcal{M}A$, then $A = 0$. Equivalently, if $e_1, \ldots, e_n \in A$ have images generating $A/\mathcal{M}A$ as an $\mathcal{R}/\mathcal{M}$-vector space, then $e_1, \ldots, e_n$ generate $A$ as an $\mathcal{R}$-module.
