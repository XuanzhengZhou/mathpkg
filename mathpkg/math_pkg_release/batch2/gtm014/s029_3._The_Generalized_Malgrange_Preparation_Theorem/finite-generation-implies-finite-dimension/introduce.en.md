If $A$ is a finitely generated module over the local ring $C_p^\infty(X)$ of smooth germs at $p$, then reducing modulo the maximal ideal $\mathcal{M}_p(X)$ yields a finite dimensional real vector space $A / \mathcal{M}_p(X)A$. This follows directly because the residue field is $\mathbf{R} \cong C_p^\infty(X) / \mathcal{M}_p(X)$, and the images of any finite generating set span the quotient as an $\mathbf{R}$-vector space. This corollary gives the easy (forward) direction of the Generalized Malgrange Preparation Theorem.
