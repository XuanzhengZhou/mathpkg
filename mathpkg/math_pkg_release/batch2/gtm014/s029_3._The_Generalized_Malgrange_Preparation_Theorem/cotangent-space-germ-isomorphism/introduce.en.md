The cotangent space $T_p^*X$ is algebraically identified with $\mathcal{M}_p/\mathcal{M}_p^2$. The map sends a germ $[f]_p$ with $f(p)=0$ to its differential $df_p$, and the kernel is precisely $\mathcal{M}_p^2$ (functions vanishing to second order). This shows that the Zariski tangent space $(\mathcal{M}_p/\mathcal{M}_p^2)^*$ coincides with the usual tangent space.
