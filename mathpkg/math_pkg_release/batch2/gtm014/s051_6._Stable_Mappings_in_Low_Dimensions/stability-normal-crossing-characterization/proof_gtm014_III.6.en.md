This theorem is deduced from a slightly more general result, Theorem 6.4. The necessity of Condition NC follows from the fact that for a stable map $f$, the images of distinct singularity strata must intersect transversely and $f$ restricted to each stratum must be an immersion with normal crossings, as shown in VI, Proposition 5.2. The sufficiency is established by Theorem 6.4, which shows that for maps exhibiting only certain Boardman-type singularities transversely, condition NC implies infinitesimal stability and hence stability.
