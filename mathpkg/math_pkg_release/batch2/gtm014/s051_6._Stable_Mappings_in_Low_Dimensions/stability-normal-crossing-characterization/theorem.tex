Let $\dim X = \dim Y \leq 4$ and let $f: X \to Y$ be a map which exhibits only the singularities on the list
$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\text{dim 1} & S_1 \\
\text{dim 2} & S_1, S_{1_2} \\
\text{dim 3} & S_1, S_{1_2}, S_{1_3} \\
\text{dim 4} & S_1, S_{1_2}, S_{1_3}, S_{1_4},\; \text{elliptic and hyperbolic umbilics } (S_{2,0})
\end{array}
$$
and exhibits these singularities transversely. Then a necessary and sufficient condition for $f$ to be stable is that it satisfy the normal crossing condition (Condition NC).
