We may identify $T_e GL(n, \mathbf{R})$ with $M(n, \mathbf{R})$, the vector space of $n \times n$ real matrices. Using this identification, show that
$$
\exp A = \operatorname{Exp} A
$$
where $A$ is in $M(n, \mathbf{R})$ and
$$
\operatorname{Exp} A = \sum_{i=0}^{\infty} \frac{A^i}{i!}.
$$
