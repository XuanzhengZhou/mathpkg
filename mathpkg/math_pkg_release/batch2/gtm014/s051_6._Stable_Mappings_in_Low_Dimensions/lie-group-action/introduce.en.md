Definition A.12 formalizes the notion of a smooth Lie group action on a manifold. An action is a group homomorphism from $G$ into the diffeomorphism group such that the evaluation map is smooth. For each point $p$, the orbit $\mathcal{O}_p$ is the set of all images under the action, and the isotropy subgroup $H_p$ consists of elements fixing $p$. The isotropy subgroup is always closed, hence a Lie subgroup, and the orbit is diffeomorphic to $G/H_p$.
