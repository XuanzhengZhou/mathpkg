Definition A.5 defines a Lie subgroup as a subgroup that is simultaneously an immersed submanifold and a Lie group in its own right. Importantly, Lie subgroups are not required to be embedded submanifolds; for instance, a line of irrational slope in the torus $T^2 = \mathbf{R}^2/\mathbf{Z}^2$ is a Lie subgroup that is dense, hence not a closed submanifold. Closed subgroups, however, are always embedded Lie subgroups by Theorem A.7.
