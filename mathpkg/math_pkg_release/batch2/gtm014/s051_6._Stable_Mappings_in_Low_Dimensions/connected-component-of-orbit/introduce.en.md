Lemma A.14 identifies the connected component of an orbit through $p$ as the set of points reachable by elements of the identity component $\mathring{G}$ of the Lie group. Since $\mathring{G}$ is connected, its image under the orbit map is connected. Moreover, for any other connected component $G'$ of $G$, the set $G' \cdot p$ is either disjoint from or equal to $\mathring{G} \cdot p$, making $\mathring{G} \cdot p$ both open and closed in the orbit.
