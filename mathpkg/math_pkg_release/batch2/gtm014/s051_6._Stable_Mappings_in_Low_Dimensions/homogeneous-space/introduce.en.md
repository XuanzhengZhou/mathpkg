Definition A.11 introduces homogeneous spaces as quotients $G/H$ of a Lie group $G$ by a closed subgroup $H$. Theorem A.10 ensures that such quotients carry a natural smooth manifold structure making the projection $\pi: G \to G/H$ a smooth submersion. Classic examples include the Grassmann manifold $G_{k,n} = GL(n, \mathbf{R})/H$ where $H$ is the subgroup preserving a fixed $k$-plane.
