Corollary A.6 provides a local factorization of a Lie group near the identity: for any decomposition $T_e G = V \oplus W$ of the Lie algebra, the map $(v, w) \mapsto \exp v \cdot \exp w$ is a local diffeomorphism. This follows immediately from Theorem A.5, which establishes that the exponential map is a local diffeomorphism with derivative equal to the identity at $0$.
