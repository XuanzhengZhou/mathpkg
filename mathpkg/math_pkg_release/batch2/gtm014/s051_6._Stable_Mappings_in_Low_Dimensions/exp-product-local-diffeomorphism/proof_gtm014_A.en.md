The map $\gamma$ is smooth as the product of smooth maps. Moreover, $(d\gamma)_0|_V = (d\exp)_0|_V = \operatorname{id}_V$ by Theorem A.5, and similarly $(d\gamma)_0|_W = (d\exp)_0|_W = \operatorname{id}_W$. Therefore $(d\gamma)_0$ is invertible (its matrix with respect to the decomposition $V \oplus W$ is the identity). By the inverse function theorem, $\gamma$ is a diffeomorphism on a neighborhood of $0$.
