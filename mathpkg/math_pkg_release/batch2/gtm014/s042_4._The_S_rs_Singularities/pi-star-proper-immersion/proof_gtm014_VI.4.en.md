The proof of this proposition is almost identical with the proof of Proposition 1.2 (Chapter III, §1). For the details, see the Exercises at the end of §1 (Chapter III). The key idea is that $\text{Hom}(Q \circ Q, W)_0$ parametrizes those symmetric homomorphisms that factor through a fixed quotient $Q$ of $V$, and the properness follows from the compactness of the relevant Grassmannian and the fact that $M_s$ is a closed subset of $\text{Hom}(V \circ V, W)$.
