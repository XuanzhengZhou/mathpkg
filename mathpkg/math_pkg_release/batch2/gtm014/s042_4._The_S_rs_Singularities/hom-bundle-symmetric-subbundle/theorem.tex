Let $E \to X$ and $F \to X$ be vector bundles. Define $\text{Hom}(E \circ E, F)_s$ to be the fiber bundle whose fiber at $p \in X$ is $\text{Hom}(E_p \circ E_p, F_p)_s$. Then by Proposition 4.3, $\text{Hom}(E \circ E, F)_s$ is a fiber subbundle of $\text{Hom}(E \circ E, F)$, and its codimension is given by formula (4.4).
