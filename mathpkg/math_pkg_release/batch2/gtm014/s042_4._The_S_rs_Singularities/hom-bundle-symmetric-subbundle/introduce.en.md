This result extends Proposition 4.3 from vector spaces to vector bundles. Given two vector bundles $E \to X$ and $F \to X$, the space $\text{Hom}(E \circ E, F)_s$ is defined fiberwise using the symmetric tensor product of the fibers. The proposition states that this is a fiber subbundle of the full homomorphism bundle $\text{Hom}(E \circ E, F)$, with the same codimension formula as in the vector space case. This globalizes the $S_{r,s}$ construction: the universal $S_{r,s}$ singularities are obtained from this bundle stratification.
