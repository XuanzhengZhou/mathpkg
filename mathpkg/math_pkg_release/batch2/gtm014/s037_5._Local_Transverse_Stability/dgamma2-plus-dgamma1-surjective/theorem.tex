The linear map
\[
(d\gamma_{2}) \oplus (d\gamma_{1}) : C^{\infty}(TX)_{p} \oplus C^{\infty}(TY)_{q} \to T_{\sigma}\mathcal{D}_{\sigma}
\]
is onto. Here $(d\gamma_{1}): C^{\infty}(TY)_{q} \to T_{\sigma}\mathcal{D}_{\sigma}$ is the $k$-jet version of the infinitesimal action of $\operatorname{Diff}(Y)$, and $(d\gamma_{2}): C^{\infty}(TX)_{p} \to T_{\sigma}\mathcal{D}_{\sigma}$ is the $k$-jet version of the infinitesimal action of $\operatorname{Diff}(X)$.
