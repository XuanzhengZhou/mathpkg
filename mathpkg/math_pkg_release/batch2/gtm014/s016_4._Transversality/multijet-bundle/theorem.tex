Let $X$ and $Y$ be smooth manifolds. Let $\alpha: J^k(X, Y) \to X$ be the source map and define $\alpha^s: J^k(X, Y)^s \to X^s$ in the obvious fashion. Then
$$J_s^k(X, Y) = (\alpha^s)^{-1}(X^{(s)})$$
is the $s$-fold $k$-jet bundle. Since $X^{(s)}$ is an open subset of $X^s$, $J_s^k(X, Y)$ is an open subset of $J^k(X, Y)^s$ and is thus a smooth manifold. A multijet bundle is some $s$-fold $k$-jet bundle.
