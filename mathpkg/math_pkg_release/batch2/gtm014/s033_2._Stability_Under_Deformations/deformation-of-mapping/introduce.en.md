A deformation of a mapping $f$ is a parameterized family $f_u$ depending smoothly on parameters $u \in \mathbf{R}^s$ with $f_0 = f$. Deformations are the natural setting for studying how mappings change under perturbation. The concept is central to the theory of unfoldings and versality in singularity theory.
