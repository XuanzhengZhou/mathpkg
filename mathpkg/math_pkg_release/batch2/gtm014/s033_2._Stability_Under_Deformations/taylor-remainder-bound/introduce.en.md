This lemma provides a bound on the remainder coefficients $g_\beta$ in a Taylor expansion about a point $p$ in a convex compact set $K$. If the $C^s$-norm of $g$ on $K$ is bounded by $\varepsilon$, then each remainder coefficient $g_\beta$ (for $|\beta| = r+1$) has $C^{s-1}$-norm also bounded by $\varepsilon$. This estimate is a technical tool used in the proof that infinitesimal stability together with a generalized homotopic stability criterion implies the openness condition for stable mappings.
