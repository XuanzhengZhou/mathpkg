A deformation $F$ of $f$ is versal if any other deformation of $f$ can be induced from $F$ by a smooth map of parameter spaces, up to equivalence. That is, for any deformation $G(x, v)$ of $f$, there exists a smooth map $\phi: \mathbf{R}^t \to \mathbf{R}^s$ such that $\phi^*F$ is equivalent to $G$.
