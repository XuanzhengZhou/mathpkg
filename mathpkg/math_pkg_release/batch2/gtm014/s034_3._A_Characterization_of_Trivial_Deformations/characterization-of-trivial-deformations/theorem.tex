A deformation $F(x, u) = (f_u(x), u)$ of $f$ is trivial iff there exist smooth vector fields $\xi(x, u)$ on $X$ and $\eta(y, u)$ on $Y$ (depending on $u$) such that
$$\frac{\partial f_u}{\partial u_i} = df_u(\xi_i) + \eta_i \circ f_u$$
for each parameter direction $i = 1, \ldots, s$, where $\xi_i = \partial \xi/\partial u_i$, $\eta_i = \partial \eta/\partial u_i$.
