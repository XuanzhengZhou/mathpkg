A deformation is trivial precisely when the infinitesimal deformation $\partial f_u/\partial u_i$ lies in the image of $T_{f_u}$ for each parameter direction. This is a version of the implicit function theorem on Frechet manifolds: the orbit map is a submersion along trivial deformations. This characterization is the key bridge between the geometry of deformations and the algebra of infinitesimal stability.
