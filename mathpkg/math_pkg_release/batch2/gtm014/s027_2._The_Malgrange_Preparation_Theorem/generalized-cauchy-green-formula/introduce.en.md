This formula generalizes the Cauchy Integral Formula from holomorphic functions to arbitrary smooth functions on a domain in $\mathbb{C}$. When $F$ is holomorphic ($\partial F/\partial \bar{z} = 0$), the area integral vanishes and one recovers the classical Cauchy formula. When $F$ is merely smooth, the additional double integral over the domain compensates for the failure of holomorphicity. The formula is derived directly from Green's Theorem and provides the crucial tool for extending complex-analytic division proofs to the smooth category.
