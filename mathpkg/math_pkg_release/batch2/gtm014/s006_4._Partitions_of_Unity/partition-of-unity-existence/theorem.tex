Let $X$ be a $C^k$ differentiable manifold and let $\{U_\alpha\}_{\alpha \in I}$ be an open covering of $X$. Then there exists a partition of unity $\{\rho_\beta\}_{\beta \in J}$ on $X$ subordinate to the covering $\{U_\alpha\}_{\alpha \in I}$. Moreover, if $I$ is countable, then we may let $J = I$ and assume that $\mathrm{supp}\,\rho_\alpha \subset U_\alpha$ for all $\alpha \in I$.
