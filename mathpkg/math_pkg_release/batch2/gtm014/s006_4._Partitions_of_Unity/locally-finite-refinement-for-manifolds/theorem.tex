Let $X$ be a differentiable manifold and let $\{U_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in I}$ be an open covering of $X$. Then there exists a locally finite refinement $\{V_{\beta}\}_{\beta \in J}$ of $\{U_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in I}$ such that

\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(a)] for every $\beta \in J$, there is a chart $\phi_{\beta}: V_{\beta} \rightarrow B_3$ which is onto, and
    \item[(b)] the sets $V_{\beta}^1 = \phi_{\beta}^{-1}(B_1)$ form an open covering of $X$,
\end{enumerate}

where $B_r = \{x \in \mathbf{R}^n \mid |x| < r\}$.
