The open cube $C(a,b)$ in $\mathbf{R}^n$ is the $n$-dimensional generalization of an open interval. It is defined by componentwise strict inequalities $a_i < t_i < b_i$ for $i = 1, \ldots, n$. This geometric object serves as the fundamental building block for defining Lebesgue measure and measure zero sets in Euclidean space.
