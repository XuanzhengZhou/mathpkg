Lemma 1.8 provides a combinatorial bound: for any minimal covering of a closed interval $[a, b]$ by closed subintervals, the sum of the lengths of the covering intervals is less than $2(b-a)$. The proof orders the intervals by left endpoints, uses the non-overlap of alternate intervals (a consequence of minimality), and bounds the total length by twice the interval length. This lemma is an auxiliary result used in establishing Theorem 1.7.
