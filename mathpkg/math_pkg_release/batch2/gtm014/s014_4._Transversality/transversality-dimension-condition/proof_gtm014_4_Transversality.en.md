Suppose $f(x) \in W$. Then

$$\dim\bigl(T_{f(x)}W + (df)_x(T_x X)\bigr) \leq \dim T_{f(x)}W + \dim T_x X$$
$$= \dim W + \dim X < \dim Y = \dim T_{f(x)}Y.$$

So it is impossible for $T_{f(x)}W + (df)_x(T_x X) = T_{f(x)}Y$. Hence if $f \pitchfork W$ at $x$, $f(x) \notin W$.
