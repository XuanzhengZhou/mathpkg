Let $X$ and $Y$ be smooth manifolds and $W$ a submanifold of $J^k(X, Y)$ such that $\alpha(W)$ is contained in an open subset $U$ of $X$ (where $\alpha$ is the source map). Let $f: X \to Y$ be a smooth mapping and $V$ an open neighborhood of $f$ in $C^\infty(X, Y)$. Then there exists a smooth mapping $g$ in $V$ such that $j^k g \pitchfork W$ and $g = f$ off $U$.
