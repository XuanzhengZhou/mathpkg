This corollary extends the Thom Transversality Theorem to multiple submanifolds simultaneously. For a countable collection of submanifolds $W_i$ in jet bundles (possibly of different orders $k_i$), the set of maps simultaneously transversal to all $W_i$ remains dense in $C^\infty(X, Y)$. When there are only finitely many $W_i$ and each is closed, the set is also open. The proof follows immediately from Theorem 4.9 and the fact that $C^\infty(X, Y)$ is a Baire space.
