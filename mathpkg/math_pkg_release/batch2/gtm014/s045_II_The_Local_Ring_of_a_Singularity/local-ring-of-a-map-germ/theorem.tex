Let $f: (X, x) \to (Y, y)$ be a map germ between smooth manifolds. Let $C_x^\infty = C_x^\infty(X)$ denote the ring of germs of smooth functions at $x$, with maximal ideal $M_x = M_x(X)$ consisting of germs vanishing at $x$. Let $f^*: C_y^\infty(Y) \to C_x^\infty(X)$ be the pullback homomorphism induced by $f$. The {\em local ring} of the map germ $f$ is the quotient ring

$$
\mathcal{R}_f = \frac{C_x^\infty(X)}{f^*(M_y(Y)) \cdot C_x^\infty(X)},
$$

where $f^*(M_y(Y)) \cdot C_x^\infty(X)$ denotes the ideal in $C_x^\infty(X)$ generated by the pullback $f^*(M_y(Y))$ of the maximal ideal $M_y(Y)$ of function germs at $y$ vanishing at $y$.
