Let $E$ and $X$ be smooth manifolds and $\pi: E \rightarrow X$ a submersion. Let $E_U = \pi^{-1}(U)$ for any subset $U$ of $X$. Then $E$ is a \textbf{family of vector spaces over $X$ of dimension $k$} if for every $p$ in $X$, $E_p$ is a real vector space of dimension $k$ whose operations (addition and scalar multiplication) are compatible with the topology on $E_p$ induced from $E$. Let $k$ be denoted by $\dim_X E$.
