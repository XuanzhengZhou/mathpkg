Proposition 5.6 asserts that every smooth vector bundle over a smooth manifold admits a fiberwise metric — a smoothly varying positive definite symmetric bilinear form on each fiber. The proof proceeds in three stages: first showing product bundles admit metrics, then trivial bundles via isomorphism, and finally arbitrary bundles by gluing local metrics using a partition of unity.
