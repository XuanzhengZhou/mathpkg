A Riemannian metric on a smooth manifold $X$ is a metric on the tangent bundle $TX$, i.e., a smoothly varying choice of positive definite symmetric bilinear form on each tangent space $T_p X$. A manifold equipped with a Riemannian metric is called a Riemannian manifold, and the metric enables the measurement of lengths of curves, angles between vectors, and the definition of geodesics.
