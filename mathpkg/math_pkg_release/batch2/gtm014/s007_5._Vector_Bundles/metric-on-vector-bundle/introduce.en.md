A metric on a vector bundle $E$ is a smooth section of the bundle of symmetric bilinear forms $S^2(E^*)$ that assigns a positive definite symmetric bilinear form to each fiber. This provides a smoothly varying inner product structure on the fibers, generalizing the notion of a Riemannian metric from the tangent bundle to an arbitrary vector bundle.
