A section of a family of vector spaces $\pi: E \to X$ is a smooth right-inverse of the projection: a smooth map $s: X \to E$ satisfying $\pi \circ s = \operatorname{id}_X$. Sections are the natural generalizations of vector fields and differential forms; the space of all smooth sections is denoted $C^\infty(E)$.
