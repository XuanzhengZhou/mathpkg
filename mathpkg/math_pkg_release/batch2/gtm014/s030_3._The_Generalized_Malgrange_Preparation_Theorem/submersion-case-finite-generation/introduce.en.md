This lemma establishes the Generalized Malgrange Preparation Theorem for the special case of a submersion of relative dimension 1. The proof uses local coordinates where $\pi(x_1, \ldots, x_n) = (x_2, \ldots, x_n)$, lifts a basis of $V$ to $A$, applies Nakayama's Lemma to show it generates $A$, and then shows finite generation over $C_q^\infty(Y)$ using the generalized Division Theorem.
