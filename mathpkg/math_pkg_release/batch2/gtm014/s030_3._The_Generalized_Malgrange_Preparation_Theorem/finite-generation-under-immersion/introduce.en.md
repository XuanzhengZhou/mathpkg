Lemma 3.8 treats the immersion case of the Generalized Malgrange Preparation Theorem. When $\phi: X \to Y$ is an immersion, the pullback map $\phi^*: C_q^\infty(Y) \to C_p^\infty(X)$ is surjective because locally $\phi(U)$ is a submanifold of $Y$ and every smooth function on a submanifold extends to the ambient space. The surjectivity of $\phi^*$ means that any finitely generated $C_p^\infty(X)$-module $A$ automatically becomes a finitely generated $C_q^\infty(Y)$-module via restriction of scalars.
