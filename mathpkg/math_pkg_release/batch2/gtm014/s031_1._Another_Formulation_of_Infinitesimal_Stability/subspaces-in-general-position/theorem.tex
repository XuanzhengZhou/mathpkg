\begin{lemma}
Let $f: X \to Y$ satisfy condition $(\dagger)$, let $q \in Y$, and let $S = \{p_1,\ldots,p_k\} \subset f^{-1}(q)$.
Define the subspaces
\[
H_i = (df)_{p_i}(T_{p_i}X) \subseteq T_qY, \qquad i = 1,\ldots,k.
\]
Then for any $\tau \in C^\infty(f^*TY)$ there exist $\zeta \in C^\infty(TX)$ defined near $S$ and $\eta \in C^\infty(TY)_q$ such that
\[
\tau = (df)(\zeta) + f^*\eta \quad \text{on a neighborhood of } S.
\]
In particular, the subspaces $H_1,\ldots,H_k$ are in general position in $T_qY$ in the sense that
\[
\operatorname{codim}(H_1 \cap \cdots \cap H_k) = \sum_{i=1}^{k} \operatorname{codim} H_i.
\]
\end{lemma}
