% Lemma 1.9: Bound on Number of Critical Points in a Fiber
% From GTM014, Chapter V, §1
%
% STATEMENT ONLY — no proof text.

A point $p \in X$ is called a \textbf{critical point} of $f: X \to Y$ if
$(df)_p: T_pX \to T_{f(p)}Y$ is not surjective.

\begin{lemma}
Let $f: X \to Y$ satisfy condition $(\dagger)$, let $m = \dim Y$, and let $q \in Y$.
Then the number of critical points in $f^{-1}(q)$ is at most $m$.
\end{lemma}
