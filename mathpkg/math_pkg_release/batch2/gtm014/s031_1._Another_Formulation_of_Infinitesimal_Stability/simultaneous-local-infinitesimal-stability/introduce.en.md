This proposition generalizes Corollary 1.3 from a single point to a finite set $S$ of pre-image points of $q \in Y$. Simultaneous local infinitesimal stability means that the same target vector field germ $\eta \in J^m(TY)_q$ can be chosen to solve the stability equations at all $p_i \in S$ at once. The proof substitutes Lemma 1.4 (the direct-sum Malgrange Preparation Theorem) for the ordinary Malgrange Preparation Theorem used in the single-point case, with $M_f^S = \bigoplus_{i=1}^k M_f^{p_i}$ replacing $M_f^p$.
