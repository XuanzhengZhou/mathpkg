This corollary translates the finite-order solvability criterion of Theorem 1.2 into a purely algebraic condition on $m$-jets: local infinitesimal stability at $p$ holds exactly when the $m$-jet of every vector field along $f$ lies in the sum of the images of the $m$-jets of vector fields on $X$ (pushed forward by $df$) and the $m$-jets of vector fields on $Y$ (pulled back by $f$). This jet-space formulation is the local bridge between infinitesimal stability and transversality conditions on jet bundles.
