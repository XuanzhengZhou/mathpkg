$f$ is infinitesimally stable iff $C^\infty_f(X, TY)$ is a finitely generated $C^\infty(Y)$-module (via $f^*$) and the natural map $C^\infty(X, TX) \to C^\infty_f(X, TY)$ has finitely generated cokernel. Equivalently, $C^\infty_f(X, TY) / (df)(C^\infty(X, TX))$ is a finitely generated $C^\infty(Y)$-module.
