This proposition establishes the commutativity of a fundamental diagram linking the geometry of the $\mathrm{Diff}(Y)$-action on jet space with the algebra of jet bundles. The map $(d\gamma_1): C^\infty(TY)_q \to T_\sigma \mathcal{D}_\sigma$ is the infinitesimal version of the action of $\mathrm{Diff}(Y)$ on the jet orbit $\mathcal{D}_\sigma$: given a vector field $\eta$ on $Y$, one flows by its one-parameter group and differentiates the resulting path in the orbit. The diagram shows that applying $(d\gamma_1)$ then lifting via $\lambda^{-1}$ (on the image) is the same as taking the $k$-jet of $\eta$ and pulling back by $f$. The proof is a direct computation using $F_t = \phi_t \circ f$ as the deformation.
