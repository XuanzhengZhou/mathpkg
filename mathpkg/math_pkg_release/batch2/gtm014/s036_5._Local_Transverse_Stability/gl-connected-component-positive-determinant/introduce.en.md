This lemma characterizes the identity component of the general linear group $\mathrm{GL}(n,\mathbb{R})$ as precisely those invertible matrices with positive determinant. The result is left as an exercise in the text. It is used in the proof of Proposition 5.5 to connect a linear map with positive determinant to the identity matrix via a continuous path in $\mathrm{GL}(n,\mathbb{R})$, enabling the stepwise construction of global diffeomorphisms from local ones.
