Let $f: X \to Y$ be smooth with $p$ in $X$ and $\sigma = j^k f(p)$. Let $\omega$ be in $J^k(f^*TY)_p$ and let $\tau: X \to TY$ be a vector field along $f$ representing $\omega$. Let $F$ be a deformation of $f$ satisfying $dF_t/dt|_{t=0} = \tau$. Consider the path $t \mapsto j^k F_t(p)$ in $J^k(X, Y)$ based at $\sigma$ and define $\lambda(\omega)$ to be the tangent vector to this path at $t = 0$. Then
$$\lambda: J^k(f^*TY)_p \to T_\sigma J^k(X, Y)$$
is a well-defined linear injection.
