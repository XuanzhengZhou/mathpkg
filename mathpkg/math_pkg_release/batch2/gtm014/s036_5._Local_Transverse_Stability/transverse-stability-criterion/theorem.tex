\text{The main results of Mather's stability theory for a proper smooth mapping $f: X \to Y$ are:}
\begin{enumerate}
\item \text{Stability $\Leftrightarrow$ infinitesimal stability (Mather's Theorem).}
\item \text{For $\dim X < \dim Y$: stability $\Leftrightarrow$ $f$ is an immersion with normal crossings.}
\item \text{For $\dim X \geq \dim Y$: stability $\Leftrightarrow$ $f$ is a submersion with folds and $f|_{S_1(f)}$ is an immersion with normal crossings.}
\item \text{Infinitesimal stability $\Leftrightarrow$ local finite-dimensionality condition $\Leftrightarrow$ versality of constant deformation.}
\end{enumerate}
