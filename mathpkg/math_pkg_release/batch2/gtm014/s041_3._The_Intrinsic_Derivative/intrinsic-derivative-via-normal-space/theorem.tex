Let $\rho : E \to F$ be a vector bundle map. View $\rho$ as a map $\rho : X \to \operatorname{Hom}(E, F)$. Let $\sigma = \rho_x$, assume $\sigma$ is of corank $r$ (i.e., $\sigma \in L^r(E, F)$), and let $N_\sigma$ be the normal space to $L^r(E, F)$ in $\operatorname{Hom}(E, F)$. Then the composite of the maps
$$
T_x X \xrightarrow{(d\rho)_x} T_\sigma \operatorname{Hom}(E, F) \longrightarrow N_\sigma \cong \operatorname{Hom}(K_\sigma, L_\sigma)
$$
is identical with the intrinsic derivative $(D\rho)_x$.
