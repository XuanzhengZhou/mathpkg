Show that the map (3.9) when restricted to $K_x \otimes K_x$ is in fact a symmetric map; i.e.,
$$
D(df)_x(k_1, k_2) = D(df)_x(k_2, k_1).
$$
Thus $D(df)_x$ induces a mapping
$$
(3.10)\qquad \delta^2 f_x : K_x \circ K_x \to L_x
$$
where $K_x \circ K_x$ denotes the symmetric product of $K_x$ with itself.
