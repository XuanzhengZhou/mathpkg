Let $f$ be a real valued function and let $x$ be a critical point of $f$. Then $K_x = T_x X$ and $L_x = \mathbf{R}$. Show that
$$
\delta^2 f_x : T_x X \circ T_x X \to \mathbf{R}
$$
is just the Hessian of $f$ at $x$. (See Chapter II, Definition 6.5.)
