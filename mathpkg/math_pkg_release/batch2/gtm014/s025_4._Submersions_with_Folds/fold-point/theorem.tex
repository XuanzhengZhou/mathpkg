Let $f: X \to Y$ satisfy $j^1 f \pitchfork S_1$ where $S_1$ is the submanifold of $J^1(X, Y)$ of jets of corank 1. Then $x \in S_1(f) = (j^1 f)^{-1}(S_1)$ is a fold point if
$$T_x S_1(f) + \operatorname{Ker}(df)_x = T_x X.$$
Note that $\dim \operatorname{Ker}(df)_x = k+1$ where $k = \dim X - \dim Y$, so $T_x S_1(f)$ and $\operatorname{Ker}(df)_x$ have complementary dimensions.
