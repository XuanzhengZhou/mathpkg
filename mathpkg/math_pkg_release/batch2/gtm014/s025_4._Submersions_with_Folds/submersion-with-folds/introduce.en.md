A submersion with folds is a smooth mapping whose singular set consists entirely of fold points. These are the generic stable singularities when $\dim X \geq \dim Y$, analogous to how immersions with normal crossings are generic when $\dim X < \dim Y$. The fold is the first in the hierarchy of stable singularities classified by Whitney and Thom.
