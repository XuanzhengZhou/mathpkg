Let $f: X \to Y$ be a smooth map between $n$-dimensional manifolds. If $j^k f \pitchfork S_{1_k}$ and $x_0 \in S_{1_k}(f)$, then there exist coordinates $x_1, \ldots, x_n$ centered at $x_0$ and coordinates $y_1, \ldots, y_n$ centered at $f(x_0)$ such that $f$ has the form

$$
\begin{align*}
f^* y_1 &= x_2 x_1 + x_3 x_1^2 + \cdots + x_k x_1^{k-1} + x_1^{k+1} \\
f^* y_2 &= x_2 \\
&\;\;\vdots \\
f^* y_n &= x_n.
\end{align*}
$$
