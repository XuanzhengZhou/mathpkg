Describe the image of the map $(x_1, x_2) \mapsto (x_1^2, x_2, x_1 x_2)$. What are the images of the curves $x_1 = \text{const}$ and $x_2 = \text{const}$? Show that this map is $1$-$1$ except along the ``double line'' $x_2 = 0$. (See Figure 6: The Cross Cap.)
