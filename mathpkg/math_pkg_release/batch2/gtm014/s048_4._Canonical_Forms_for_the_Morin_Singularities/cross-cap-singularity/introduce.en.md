Definition 4.5 introduces the cross-cap singularity for maps from an $n$-manifold to a $(2n-1)$-manifold. A point $x_0$ in the Boardman singularity set $S_1(f)$ is a cross-cap if the 1-jet $j^1 f$ is transverse to $S_1$ at $x_0$. In these dimensions the codimension of $S_1$ equals $n$, so cross-caps are isolated. The term originates from Whitney's work on singularities of smooth maps.
