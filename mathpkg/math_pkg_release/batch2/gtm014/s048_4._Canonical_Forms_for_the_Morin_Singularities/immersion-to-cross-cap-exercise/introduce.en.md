Exercise 1 from Chapter VII, Section 4. Given an immersion $f: X \to \mathbf{R}^{2n}$ of an $n$-manifold and a regular value $v$ of the derivative map $df$, this exercise asks to show that composing with a surjective linear projection $\pi$ whose kernel contains $v$ produces a map $\pi \circ f$ whose only singularities are cross-caps. This relates to the discussion in Chapter II, Section 1, Exercise 1.
