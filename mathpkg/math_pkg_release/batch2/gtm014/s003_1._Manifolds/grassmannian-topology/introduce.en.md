The Grassmannian $G_{k,n}$ is the set of all $k$-dimensional linear subspaces of $\mathbf{R}^n$. To give it a topology, one first considers the Stiefel-like space $W$ of ordered $k$-tuples of linearly independent vectors, which is an open subset of a product of $k$ copies of $\mathbf{R}^n$.

Two $k$-tuples are equivalent if they span the same subspace. The Grassmannian is then the quotient $W / {\sim}$ with the decomposition space topology, making it a topological space on which one can subsequently define an analytic structure.
