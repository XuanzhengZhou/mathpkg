Let $U$ be an open convex subset of $\mathbb{R}^n$, $a \in U$, and $f: U \to \mathbb{R}$ smooth. Then there exist smooth functions $g_1, \ldots, g_n: U \to \mathbb{R}$ such that for every $x \in U$,
$$f(x) = f(a) + \sum_{i=1}^{n} g_i(x)(x_i - a_i).$$
Moreover, $g_i(a) = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_i}(a)$.
