Let $f: X \to \mathbb{R}$ be a smooth function on a smooth manifold $X$ with a critical point at $p \in X$ (so $df_p = 0$). The **intrinsic second derivative** (or **Hessian**) of $f$ at $p$ is the symmetric bilinear form
$$(d^2 f)_p: T_p X \times T_p X \to \mathbb{R}$$
