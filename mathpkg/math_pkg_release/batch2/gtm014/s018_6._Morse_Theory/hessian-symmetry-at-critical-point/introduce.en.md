Lemma (A) establishes that the intrinsic second derivative $(d^2 f)_p$ at a critical point is always a symmetric bilinear form. This symmetry is an invariant property that does not depend on the choice of coordinates. The result follows from the equality of mixed partial derivatives in local coordinates. It is used in the proof of Proposition 6.6 to show that the Hessian on a manifold is symmetric.
