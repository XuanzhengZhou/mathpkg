Compute using local coordinates: in a chart around $0$, the symmetry of $(d^2 f)_0$ follows from the equality of mixed partial derivatives $\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x_i \partial x_j}(0) = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x_j \partial x_i}(0)$, which holds by smoothness of $f$.
