Let $U$ be an open convex subset of $\mathbb{R}^n$ containing $a$, and let $f: U \to \mathbb{R}$ be smooth with $g_i(a) = 0$ for the functions $g_i$ from Lemma 6.10. Then there exist smooth functions $g_{ij}: U \to \mathbb{R}$ ($1 \leq i, j \leq n$) with $g_{ij} = g_{ji}$ such that for all $x \in U$,
$$f(x) = f(a) + \sum_{i,j=1}^{n} g_{ij}(x)(x_i - a_i)(x_j - a_j).$$
Moreover, $g_{ij}(a) = \frac{1}{2} \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x_i \partial x_j}(a)$.
