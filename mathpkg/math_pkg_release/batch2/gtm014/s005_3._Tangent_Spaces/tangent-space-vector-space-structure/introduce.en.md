This proposition endows the tangent space $T_p X$ with the structure of a real vector space of dimension $n$, isomorphic to $\mathbf{R}^n$. The vector space operations are defined by transporting the standard operations on $\mathbf{R}^n$ via any chart $\phi$: for $\alpha, \beta \in T_p X$, set $\alpha + \beta = \lambda_\phi^p((\lambda_\phi^p)^{-1}(\alpha) + (\lambda_\phi^p)^{-1}(\beta))$ and similarly for scalar multiplication. The uniqueness and consistency across all charts are guaranteed by the fact that the transition map $(\lambda_\psi^p)^{-1} \circ \lambda_\phi^p = d(\psi \circ \phi^{-1})_{\phi(p)}$ is a linear isomorphism of $\mathbf{R}^n$.
