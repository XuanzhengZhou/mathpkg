Let $X, Y$ be differentiable manifolds and let $f \colon X \to Y$ be a smooth map. For $p \in X$, the \emph{differential of $f$ at $p$} is the linear map
$$
df_p \colon T_p X \to T_{f(p)} Y
$$
defined by $df_p([c]) = [f \circ c]$ for any curve $c$ based at $p$, where $[c]$ denotes the tangency equivalence class in $T_p X$.
