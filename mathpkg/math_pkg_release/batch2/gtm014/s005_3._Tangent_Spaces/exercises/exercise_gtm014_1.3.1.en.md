Let $f \colon \mathbf{R}^n \to \mathbf{R}^2$ be defined by
$$
(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \mapsto \bigl( x_1^2 + \cdots + x_n^2,\; x_1^2 - (x_2^2 + \cdots + x_n^2) \bigr).
$$

(a) For which $x \in \mathbf{R}^n$ is $f$ a submersion at $x$?

(b) Let $f_1$ and $f_2$ be the coordinate functions of $f$. For which $r, s \in \mathbf{R}$ is $f_1^{-1}(r) \cap f_2^{-1}(s)$ a smooth submanifold of $\mathbf{R}^n$?
