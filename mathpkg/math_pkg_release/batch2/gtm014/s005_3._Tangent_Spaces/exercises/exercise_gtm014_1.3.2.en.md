Let $M_n$ be the set of $n \times n$ real matrices. Let $M_n^k$ be the set of matrices in $M_n$ of rank $k$. Prove that $M_n^k$ is a submanifold of $M_n$ and compute its dimension.

*Hint:* Let $S = \begin{pmatrix} A & B \\ C & D \end{pmatrix}$ be in $M_n$, where $A \in M_k^k$. Show that $S \in M_n^k$ if and only if $D - C A^{-1} B = 0$.
