Let $X$ be a differentiable $n$-manifold, let $p \in X$, and let $\phi$ be a chart on $X$ with $p \in \operatorname{dom} \phi$. Define $\lambda_\phi^p \colon \mathbf{R}^n \to T_p X$ by
$$
\lambda_\phi^p(v) = [c_v],
$$
where $c_v(t) = \phi^{-1}(\phi(p) + tv)$ and $[c_v]$ denotes the tangency equivalence class of $c_v$. Then $\lambda_\phi^p$ is a bijection.
