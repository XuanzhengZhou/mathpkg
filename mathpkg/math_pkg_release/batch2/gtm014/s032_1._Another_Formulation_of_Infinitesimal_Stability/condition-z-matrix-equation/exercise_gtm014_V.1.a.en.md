**Exercise (a).** Show that condition $(Z)$ holds (to order $m$) at $0$ iff equations $(**)$ hold (to order $m$) at $0$.

*Hint:* For $(**) \Rightarrow$ condition $(Z)$. Let $\tau^l = (0, \ldots, \phi, \ldots, 0)$ where $\phi$ is in the $l$th position for $1 \leq l \leq m$. Using $(**)$ obtain $\tau^l = (df)(\xi^l) + \eta^l \circ f$. Let $H = \{\xi_i^l\}$ and $K = \{\eta_i^l\}$ where $\xi^l = (\xi_1^l, \ldots, \xi_n^l)$ and $\eta^l = (\eta_1^l, \ldots, \eta_m^l)$. For condition $(Z) \Rightarrow$ $(**)$, let $\tau = (\tau_1, \ldots, \tau_m)$ and solve $\tau_l I_m = (df)H + K \circ f$. Let $\xi_i = \sum_{j=1}^m h_{ij} \tau_j$ and $\eta_i = \sum_{j=1}^m k_{ij} \tau_j$ where $H = (h_{ij})$ and $K = (k_{ij})$. Then let $\xi = (\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_n)$ and $\eta = (\eta_1, \ldots, \eta_m)$.
