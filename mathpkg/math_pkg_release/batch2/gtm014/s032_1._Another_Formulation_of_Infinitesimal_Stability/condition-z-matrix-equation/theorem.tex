Let $f: \mathbf{R}^n \to \mathbf{R}^m$ be a smooth map germ at $0$ with $f(0) = 0$. Condition $(Z)$ holds (to order $m$) at $0$ if for every germ $\phi \in C_0^\infty(\mathbf{R}^n)$ there exists an $n \times m$ matrix $H$ of germs in $C_0^\infty(\mathbf{R}^n)$ and an $m \times m$ matrix $K$ of germs in $C_0^\infty(\mathbf{R}^m)$ such that

$$\phi I_m = (df)H + K \circ f,$$

where $I_m$ is the $m \times m$ identity matrix.
