A proper smooth mapping $f: X \to Y$ is stable iff for every $p \in X$ with $q = f(p)$, the $C_q^\infty(Y)$-module
$$\frac{C_f^\infty(X, TY)_p}{(df)(C^\infty(X, TX)_p) + f^*\mathcal{M}_q(Y) \cdot C_f^\infty(X, TY)_p}$$
is a finite-dimensional real vector space. This condition can be checked locally at each point.
