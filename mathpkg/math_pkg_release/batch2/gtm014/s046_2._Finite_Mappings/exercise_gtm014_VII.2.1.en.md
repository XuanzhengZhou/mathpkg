Consider the Whitney map given by
$$f: \mathbf{R}^2 \to \mathbf{R}^2, \quad (x_1, x_2) \mapsto (x_1, x_1x_2 + \tfrac{1}{3}x_2^3).$$
Given $a \in \mathbf{R}^2$, what values can the sum
$$\sum_{p \in f^{-1}(a)} \dim_{\mathbf{R}} \mathcal{R}_f(p)$$
take on? Here $\mathcal{R}_f(p)$ denotes the local ring of $f$ at $p$.
