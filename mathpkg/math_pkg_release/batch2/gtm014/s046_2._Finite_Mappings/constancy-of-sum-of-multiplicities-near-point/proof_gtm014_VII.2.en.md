**Proof.** The proof of Proposition 2.4 is not included in the main text. The authors refer the reader to the literature:

> See [38].

The reference [38] corresponds to: B. Malgrange, *Ideals of Differentiable Functions*, Oxford University Press, 1966.

The result can also be derived from the complex-analytic version, where the local ring dimension equals the local topological degree. In the smooth ($C^\infty$) category, the proof relies on the Malgrange Preparation Theorem and the fact that the sum of dimensions over pre-images is invariant under small deformations, which follows from the coherence properties of the sheaf of smooth functions modulo the pullback ideal.
