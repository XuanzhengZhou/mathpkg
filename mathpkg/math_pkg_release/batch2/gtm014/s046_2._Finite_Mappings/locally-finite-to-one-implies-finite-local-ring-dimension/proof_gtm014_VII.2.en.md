**Proof.** The proof is not included in the text. The authors cite:

> See [14].

Reference [14] is: R. C. Gunning and H. Rossi, *Analytic Functions of Several Complex Variables*, Prentice-Hall, 1965.

The result follows from the fact that in the complex analytic category, a locally finite-to-one holomorphic map between manifolds of the same dimension has a local ring isomorphic to a finite-dimensional $\mathbf{C}$-algebra (via the Weierstrass Preparation Theorem and the fact that the ring of germs of holomorphic functions is Noetherian). The dimension of the local ring then equals the local topological degree of the map.
