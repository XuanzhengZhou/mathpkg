Consider the analogous problem for the complex Whitney map
$$f: \mathbf{C}^2 \to \mathbf{C}^2, \quad (x_1, x_2) \mapsto (x_1, x_1x_2 + \tfrac{1}{3}x_2^3).$$
Determine the possible values of the sum
$$\sum_{p \in f^{-1}(a)} \dim_{\mathbf{C}} \mathcal{R}_f(p)$$
for $a \in \mathbf{C}^2$, where $\mathcal{R}_f(p) = \mathcal{O}_{\mathbf{C}^2,p} / f^*(\mathcal{M}_{\mathbf{C}^2,f(p)})$ denotes the analytic local ring.
