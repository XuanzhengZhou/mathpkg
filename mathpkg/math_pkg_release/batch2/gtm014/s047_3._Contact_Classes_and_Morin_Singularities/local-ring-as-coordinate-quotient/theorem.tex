Let $f: (\mathbf{R}^n, 0) \to (\mathbf{R}^m, 0)$ be a smooth map germ with coordinate functions $f_1, \ldots, f_m$. Identify $\mathbf{R}^n$ with $\mathbf{R}^n \times \{0\}$ in $\mathbf{R}^n \times \mathbf{R}^m$. Then
$$
\mathcal{R}_f(0) = C_0^\infty(\mathbf{R}^n) / (f_1, \ldots, f_m),
$$
where $(f_1, \ldots, f_m)$ denotes the ideal generated by the coordinate functions of $f$ in $C_0^\infty(\mathbf{R}^n)$.
