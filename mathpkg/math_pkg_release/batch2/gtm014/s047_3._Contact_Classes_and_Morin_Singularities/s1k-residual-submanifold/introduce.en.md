Corollary 3.14 follows immediately from Proposition 3.13 and the Thom Transversality Theorem: since $S_{1_k}$ is a codimension $k$ submanifold of the jet bundle, for a residual set of maps $f$ the jet extension $j^k f$ is transverse to $S_{1_k}$, making $S_{1_k}(f) = (j^k f)^{-1}(S_{1_k})$ a codimension $k$ submanifold of $X$.
