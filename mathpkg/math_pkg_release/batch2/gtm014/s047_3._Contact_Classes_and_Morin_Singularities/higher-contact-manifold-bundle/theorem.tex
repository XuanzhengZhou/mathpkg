Let $H_{k,l}^n$ denote the space of $k$th-order contact elements of $l$-dimensional submanifold germs in $\mathbf{R}^n$ at $0$. Then $H_{k,l}^n$ is a smooth manifold. Moreover, the map $\rho: H_{k,l}^n \to G_{l,n}$ given by $\rho(A) = T_0 A$ (the tangent space at $0$) makes $H_{k,l}^n$ into a fiber bundle over the Grassmann manifold $G_{l,n}$ whose typical fiber is the fiber $F$ of the bundle
$$
J^k(\mathbf{R}^l, \mathbf{R}^{n-l})_{0,0} \to J^1(\mathbf{R}^l, \mathbf{R}^{n-l})_{0,0}.
$$
