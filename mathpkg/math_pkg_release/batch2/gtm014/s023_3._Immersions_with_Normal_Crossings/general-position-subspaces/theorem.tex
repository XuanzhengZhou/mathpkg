Let $V$ be a vector space and let $H_1, \ldots, H_r$ be subspaces of $V$. Then $H_1, \ldots, H_r$ are in general position if for every sequence of integers $i_1, \ldots, i_s$ with $1 \leq i_1 < \cdots < i_s \leq r$,
$$\operatorname{codim}(H_{i_1} \cap \cdots \cap H_{i_s}) = \operatorname{codim}(H_{i_1}) + \cdots + \operatorname{codim}(H_{i_s}).$$
In the case $r = 2$, $H_1$ and $H_2$ are in general position iff $H_1 + H_2 = V$.
