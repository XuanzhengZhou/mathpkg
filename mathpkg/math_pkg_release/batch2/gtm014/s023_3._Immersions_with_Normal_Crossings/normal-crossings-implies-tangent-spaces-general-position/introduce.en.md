For an immersion with normal crossings $f: X \to Y$, the tangent spaces at preimage points of any $q \in Y$ are in general position as subspaces of $T_qY$. Specifically, if $f^{-1}(q) = \{p_1, \ldots, p_r\}$, then the subspaces $H_i = df(T_{p_i}X)$ satisfy the codimension-additivity condition for all intersections. This lemma provides the crucial link between the geometric condition of normal crossings (transversality of multi-jets to diagonals) and the algebraic condition of general position for the tangent spaces, enabling dimension-counting arguments at multiple points.
