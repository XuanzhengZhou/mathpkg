Let $H_1, \ldots, H_r$ be subspaces of $V$ in general position and let $D = H_1 \cap \cdots \cap H_r$. Let $D_i = \bigcap_{j \neq i} H_j$ and choose a complementary subspace $F_i$ to $D$ in $D_i$ ($1 \leq i \leq r$). Then:
(a) $V = D \oplus F_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus F_r$,
(b) $\dim F_i = \operatorname{codim} H_i$ for each $i$.
