When subspaces $H_1, \ldots, H_r$ are in general position, the ambient space $V$ decomposes as a direct sum $V = D \oplus F_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus F_r$, where $D = \bigcap H_i$ and each $F_i$ is a complement of $D$ in $\bigcap_{j \neq i} H_j$. Moreover, $\dim F_i = \operatorname{codim} H_i$. This structural lemma is a key tool for analyzing the local geometry of mappings with normal crossings.
