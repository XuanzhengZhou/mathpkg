Let $V$ and $W$ be finite-dimensional real vector spaces, let $s = \dim V - r$, and let $Q \to G(s, V)$ be the universal quotient bundle over the Grassmannian of $s$-dimensional subspaces of $V$. For each $p \in G(s, V)$ (corresponding to an $s$-dimensional subspace $K \subset V$), the projection $\pi: V \to Q_p = V/K$ induces a transpose map
$$
\pi^*: \operatorname{Hom}(Q_p, W) \to \operatorname{Hom}(V, W).
$$

The image of $\pi^*$ is precisely the set of elements in $\operatorname{Hom}(V, W)$ of corank $\geq r$. Moreover, $\pi^*$ maps $L^0(Q_p, W)$ (the injective linear maps from $Q_p$ to $W$) bijectively onto the elements in $\operatorname{Hom}(V, W)$ of corank exactly $r$.
