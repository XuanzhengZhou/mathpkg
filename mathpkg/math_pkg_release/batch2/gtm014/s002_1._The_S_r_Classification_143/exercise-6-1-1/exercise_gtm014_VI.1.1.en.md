Prove Proposition 1.4: the map $\pi^*: L^0(Q, W) \to \operatorname{Hom}(V, W) \setminus M$ is a one-to-one proper immersion, where $Q \to G(s, V)$ is the universal quotient bundle, $s = \dim V - r$, and $M$ is the subset of $\operatorname{Hom}(V, W)$ consisting of elements of corank $> r$.
