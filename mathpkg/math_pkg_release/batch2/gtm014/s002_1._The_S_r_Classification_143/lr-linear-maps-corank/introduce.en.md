$L^r(V, W)$ is the set of linear maps between vector spaces $V$ and $W$ that have corank exactly $r$. To show that the $S_r$ submanifolds in jet space are genuine submanifolds, one equips $L^r(V, W)$ with a manifold structure. The construction uses the Grassmannian of $\dim V - r$ dimensional subspaces (kernels) and the universal quotient bundle: a linear map of corank $r$ is identified with a pair consisting of its kernel $K \in G(s, V)$ and an injective linear map from the quotient $V/K$ to $W$. This gives $L^r(V, W)$ the structure of a fiber bundle over $G(s, V)$ with fiber $\operatorname{GL}(V/K, W)$.
