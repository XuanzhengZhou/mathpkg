This statement asserts that for a residual set of smooth maps, each $S_r(f)$ is a submanifold of the domain $X$ whose codimension equals that of the corresponding $S_r$ submanifold in the 1-jet bundle. The formula $\operatorname{codim} S_r(f) = r^2 + er$ (with $e = |\dim X - \dim Y|$) follows directly from the Thom Transversality Theorem applied to the 1-jet extension $j^1 f$ and the known fact that $S_r$ is a submanifold of $J^1(X,Y)$. This is the foundational genericity result for the Thom-Boardman stratification.
