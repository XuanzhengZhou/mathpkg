Let $E \to X$ and $F \to Y$ be finite-rank real vector bundles. Define $L^r(E, F)$ to be the fiber bundle over $X \times Y$ whose fiber at $(x, y) \in X \times Y$ is
$$
L^r(E, F)_{(x,y)} = L^r(E_x, F_y),
$$
the set of linear maps from the fiber $E_x$ to the fiber $F_y$ that have corank $r$.

Equivalently, $L^r(E, F)$ is the associated fiber bundle obtained from the vector bundle $\operatorname{Hom}(E, F) \to X \times Y$ by restricting to the submanifold of corank-$r$ maps in each fiber, using the manifold structure constructed for $L^r(V, W)$.
