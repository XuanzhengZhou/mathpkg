Lemma 5.1 gives a clean jet-theoretic characterization of immersions: a smooth map $f: X \to Y$ is an immersion precisely when the image of its 1-jet section $j^1 f(X)$ does not intersect any of the corank-$r$ strata $S_r$ for $r \neq 0$. Equivalently, $j^1 f(X)$ is contained entirely in $S_0$, the set of 1-jets of maximal rank. This lemma bridges the geometric notion of immersion with the algebraic stratification of the 1-jet bundle by corank.
