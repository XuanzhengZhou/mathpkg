Lemma 5.5 states that the set of immersions $\operatorname{Im}(X, Y)$ is an open subset of $C^\infty(X, Y)$ in the Whitney $C^\infty$ topology. The proof is immediate from the fact that $S_0$ is an open subset of $J^1(X, Y)$ (since its complement $\bigcup_{r \neq 0} S_r$ is a closed stratified subset) and the characterization $M(S_0) = \operatorname{Im}(X, Y)$ from Lemma 5.1, where $M(S_0)$ denotes those maps whose 1-jet lies in $S_0$. The openness of the immersion condition is a fundamental fact used throughout differential topology.
