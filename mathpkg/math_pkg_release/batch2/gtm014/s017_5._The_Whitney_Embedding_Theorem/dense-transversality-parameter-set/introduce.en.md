Lemma 4.15 provides a density result for the set of parameters $b \in B$ for which the $k$-jet of a smooth family of maps $G_b: X \to Y$ remains transverse to a given submanifold $W$ of the jet bundle $J_s^k(X, Y)$. The result assumes that the associated map $H$ on the $s$-fold product $X^{(s)}$ is transverse to $W$ on the closure of an open subset $W' \subseteq W$. This lemma generalizes the Thom Transversality Theorem to parametrized families and serves as a key technical tool for establishing that transversality conditions are generic in parameter spaces.
