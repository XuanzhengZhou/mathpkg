The corank of a 1-jet $\sigma \in J^1(X, Y)$ measures the deficit from maximal rank of the differential of any representative smooth map $f$ at the source point. It is defined as $\min(\dim X, \dim Y)$ minus the rank of the derivative $(df)_p$. The corank is a fundamental invariant in singularity theory, as it classifies 1-jets by their degeneracy: corank $0$ corresponds to immersions (when $\dim X \leq \dim Y$) or submersions (when $\dim X \geq \dim Y$).
