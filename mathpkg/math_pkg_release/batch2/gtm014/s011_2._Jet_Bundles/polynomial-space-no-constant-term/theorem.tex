Let $A_n^k$ be the vector space of polynomials in $n$ variables of degree $\leq k$ which have their constant term equal to zero. That is,

$$A_n^k = \left\{ \sum_{1 \leq |\alpha| \leq k} a_\alpha x^\alpha : a_\alpha \in \mathbf{R} \right\}$$

where $\alpha = (\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_n)$ is a multi-index, $|\alpha| = \alpha_1 + \cdots + \alpha_n$, and $x^\alpha = x_1^{\alpha_1} \cdots x_n^{\alpha_n}$.

Let $B_{n,m}^k = \bigoplus_{i=1}^{m} A_n^k$ be the direct sum of $m$ copies of $A_n^k$.
