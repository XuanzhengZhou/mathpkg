Let $Y$ and $Y'$ be smooth manifolds with $X \subset Y$ a submanifold. Let $f: Y \to Y'$ be a smooth map such that $f|_X$ is an embedding and $df_x: T_x Y \to T_{f(x)} Y'$ is an isomorphism for every $x \in X$. Then there exists an open neighborhood $U$ of $X$ in $Y$ such that $f|_U: U \to Y'$ is a diffeomorphism onto an open subset of $Y'$.
