This is really a corollary to the proof of the Thom Transversality Theorem. Under the assumptions of the corollary it is clear that for each $W_{r}$, $\alpha(\overline{W_{r}}) \subset U$. Thus we can choose $U_{r}$ so that $U_{r} \subset U$. Then note that the perturbation $g_{b} = f$ off $U_{r}$, and thus off $U$.
