Corollary 4.10 generalizes the Thom Transversality Theorem to any (possibly infinite) collection of submanifolds $W_i$ in jet bundles of varying orders $k_i$. The set of smooth maps simultaneously transverse to all $W_i$ is dense in $C^{\infty}(X, Y)$. When only finitely many closed $W_i$ are involved, this set is also open, providing both genericity and stability. The proof follows from Theorem 4.9 and the fact that $C^{\infty}(X, Y)$ is a Baire space.
