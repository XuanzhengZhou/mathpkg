Assume that the submanifolds $Y_1, \ldots, Y_r$ of $Y$ are in general position at a point $q$. Then there exists a neighborhood $W$ of $q$ in $Y$, a chart $\phi: W \to \mathbb{R}^n$, and subspaces $H_1, \ldots, H_r$ such that
$$Y_i \cap W = \phi^{-1}(H_i) \qquad (1 \leq i \leq r)$$
where $n = \dim Y$.
