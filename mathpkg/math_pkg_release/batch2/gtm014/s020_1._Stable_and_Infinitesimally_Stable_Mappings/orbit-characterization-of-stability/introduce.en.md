This lemma reformulates the geometric notion of stability in terms of group actions. The group of diffeomorphisms of the domain and codomain acts naturally on the space of smooth mappings, and the orbit of a mapping consists precisely of all mappings equivalent to it. Stability then corresponds to the orbit being open, which connects the geometric definition to the infinitesimal criterion developed later via differentiation of the action map.
