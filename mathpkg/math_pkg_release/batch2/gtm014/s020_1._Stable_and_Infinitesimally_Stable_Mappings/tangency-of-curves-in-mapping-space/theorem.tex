Let $X$ and $Y$ be smooth manifolds with $X$ compact. Let $t \mapsto f_t$ and $t \mapsto g_t$ be smooth curves in $C^\infty(X, Y)$ with $f_0 = g_0$.

Then $f_t$ and $g_t$ are tangent at $t = 0$ if and only if for each $p \in X$, the curves $t \mapsto f_t(p)$ and $t \mapsto g_t(p)$ in $Y$ are tangent at $t = 0$.
