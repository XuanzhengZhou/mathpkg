This proposition constructs a smooth extension $\tilde{G}$ of $G$ to complex variables such that $\partial \tilde{G}/\partial \bar{z}$ vanishes to infinite order on the zero set of $P_k$. This ensures the double integral in the generalized Cauchy formula converges and is smooth, completing the proof of the smooth Polynomial Division Theorem. The construction uses induction on $k$ and the initial value problem lemma.
