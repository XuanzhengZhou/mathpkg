This lemma constructs a smooth function on $\mathbf{R}^n$ that simultaneously extends two smooth functions $g$ and $h$ defined on transverse linear subspaces $V$ and $W$, provided they agree to infinite order on the intersection $V \cap W$. The construction uses the Borel theorem technique with bump functions and rapidly increasing scaling factors to blend the Taylor expansions of $g$ along $V$ into a smooth function on the whole space. It can be reduced to the case $h \equiv 0$ by considering $g - h$.
