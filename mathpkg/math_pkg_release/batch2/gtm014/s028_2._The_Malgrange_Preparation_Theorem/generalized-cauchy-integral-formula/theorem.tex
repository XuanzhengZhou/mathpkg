Let $D \subset \mathbf{C}$ be a domain with smooth boundary $\gamma$, and let $F$ be a smooth (not necessarily holomorphic) function defined on a neighborhood of $\overline{D}$. Then for any $w \in D$,
$$F(w) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{F(z)}{z - w} \, dz - \frac{1}{2\pi i} \iint_D \frac{\partial F / \partial \bar{z}}{z - w} \, dz \wedge d\bar{z}.$$
If $F$ is holomorphic in $D$, then $\partial F / \partial \bar{z} = 0$ and this formula reduces to the standard Cauchy Integral Formula.
