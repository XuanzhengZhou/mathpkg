Let $f: X \rightarrow Y$ be smooth and let $X$ be compact. Then the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $f$ is stable;
\item[(b)] $f$ is transverse stable;
\item[(c)] $f$ is infinitesimally stable;
\item[(d)] $f$ is homotopically stable;
\item[(e)] $f$ is stable under $k$-parameter families of deformations.
\end{enumerate}
