This summary synthesizes the main results of Chapters III-V: Mather's theorem establishing equivalence of stability and infinitesimal stability, the geometric characterization of stable mappings in terms of folds and normal crossings, and the algebraic criterion via finite generation. Together, these results provide a complete theory of stable mappings between smooth manifolds.
