Let $X$ and $Y$ be $n$-dimensional manifolds, $n \geq 4$. Let $f: X \to Y$ be a smooth map exhibiting either an elliptic or hyperbolic umbilic at $x_0 \in X$. Suppose $j^1 f \pitchfork S_2$ at $x_0$. Then there exist coordinates $(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ centered at $x_0$ and $(y_1, \ldots, y_n)$ centered at $f(x_0)$ such that $f$ has the canonical form:
\begin{align*}
f^* y_1 &= x_1^2 \pm x_2^2 + \sum_{i=3}^n a_i x_i \\
f^* y_2 &= x_1 x_2 + \sum_{i=3}^n b_i x_i \\
f^* y_i &= x_i \quad \text{for } i = 3, \ldots, n,
\end{align*}
where the $+$ sign corresponds to the elliptic case and the $-$ sign to the hyperbolic case, and $a_i, b_i$ are constants.
