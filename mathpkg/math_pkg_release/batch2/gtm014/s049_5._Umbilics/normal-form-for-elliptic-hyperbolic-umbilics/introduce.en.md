Theorem 5.10 establishes canonical coordinate forms for generic elliptic and hyperbolic umbilics in dimensions $n \geq 4$. The proof uses the Malgrange Preparation Theorem to express quadratic terms in the coordinate functions and relies on smooth diagonalization lemmas for parameter-dependent quadratic forms. Two generic umbilics of the same type (elliptic or hyperbolic) are equivalent as map germs.
