Let $\mathcal{R}$ be a local ring over $\mathbf{R}$ with $\dim_{\mathbf{R}} \mathcal{R} = 4$. Then $\mathcal{R}$ is isomorphic to one of the following rings:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathbf{R}[t]/(t^4)$
\item $\mathbf{R}[x, y]/(x^2 - y^2, xy)$
\item $\mathbf{R}[x, y]/(x^2, y^2)$
\item $\mathbf{R}[x, y]/(x^2, xy)$
\item $\mathbf{R}[x, y, z]/(x^2, y^2, z^2, xy, xz, yz)$.
\end{enumerate}
