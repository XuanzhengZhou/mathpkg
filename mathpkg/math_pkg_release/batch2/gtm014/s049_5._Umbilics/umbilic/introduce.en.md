An umbilic is a singularity of type $S_2$ (in the Thom-Boardman classification) that occurs with multiplicity 4. Umbilics are the simplest $S_2$ singularities and are the only $S_2$ singularities that can occur stably in dimensions less than 6. They come in three types -- hyperbolic, elliptic, and parabolic -- classified according to the isomorphism type of their local ring.
