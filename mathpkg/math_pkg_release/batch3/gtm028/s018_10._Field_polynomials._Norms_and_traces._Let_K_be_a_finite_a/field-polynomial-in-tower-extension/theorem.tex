Let $k \subset K \subset \Delta$ be successive finite algebraic extensions. Let $f(X)$ be the field polynomial of $x \in K$ over $k$ when $x$ is regarded as an element of $K$, and let $F(X)$ be the field polynomial of $x$ over $k$ when $x$ is regarded as an element of $\Delta$. Then
$$F(X) = [f(X)]^m,$$
where $m = [\Delta : K]$.
