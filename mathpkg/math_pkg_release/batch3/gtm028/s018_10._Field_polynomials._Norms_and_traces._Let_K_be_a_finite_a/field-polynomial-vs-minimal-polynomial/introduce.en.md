Theorem 20 establishes a fundamental relationship between the field polynomial and the minimal polynomial of an element. The field polynomial is always a power of the minimal polynomial, with the exponent being $[K : k(x)]$, the degree of the extension relative to the simple subextension generated by $x$. Equality holds precisely when $x$ itself generates the full extension $K/k$.
