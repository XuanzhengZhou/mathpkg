Let $K/k$ be a finite algebraic extension of degree $n$. Let $\{\omega_1, \dots, \omega_n\}$ be a basis of $K/k$, and let $\tau_1, \dots, \tau_n$ be the $n$ distinct $k$-isomorphisms of $K$ into a normal closure $K^\star$. Set $\omega_i^{(\alpha)} = \omega_i^{\tau_\alpha}$. Then
$$d(\omega_1, \dots, \omega_n) = \big[\det(\omega_i^{(\alpha)})_{i,\alpha=1}^{n}\big]^2.$$
