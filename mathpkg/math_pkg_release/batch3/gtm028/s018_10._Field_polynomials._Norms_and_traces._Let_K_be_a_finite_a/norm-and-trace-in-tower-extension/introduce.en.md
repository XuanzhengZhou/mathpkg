When an element $x$ belongs to an intermediate field $K$ of a tower $k \subset K \subset \Delta$, its norm and trace relative to the full extension $\Delta/k$ are expressible as simple powers and multiples of those relative to $K/k$. The norm is raised to the $m$-th power and the trace is multiplied by $m$, where $m = [\Delta:K]$.
