The field polynomial of an element $x$ in a finite extension $K/k$ is the characteristic polynomial of the $k$-linear map given by multiplication by $x$. It is a monic polynomial of degree $n = [K:k]$ with coefficients in $k$, having $x$ as a root. Crucially, the field polynomial is independent of the choice of basis used to compute the matrix representation, making it an invariant of the element $x$ relative to the extension $K/k$.
