These formulas generalize the conjugate expressions for norm and trace from the simple extension case $K = k(x)$ to arbitrary finite extensions. Using all $n$ $k$-isomorphisms of $K$ into a normal closure (counting with the multiplicities coming from extensions of isomorphisms), the field polynomial factors as $\prod (X - x_i)$, and the norm and trace are the product and sum of all $n$ values $x_i$.
