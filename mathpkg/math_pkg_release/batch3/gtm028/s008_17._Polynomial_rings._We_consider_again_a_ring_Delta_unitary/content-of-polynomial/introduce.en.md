The content $c(f)$ of a polynomial over a UFD is the greatest common divisor of its coefficients, well-defined up to multiplication by a unit. Every polynomial factors uniquely as its content times a primitive polynomial: $f = c(f) \cdot f_1$. The content function satisfies the multiplicative property $c(fg) = c(f) c(g)$ (up to units), which is equivalent to Gauss's Lemma.
