**Proof.** Apply the preceding theorem with $T_0$ being the identity automorphism of $R$. Then $\bar{R} = R$, and the theorem yields a unique $R$-homomorphism $T : S' \to \bar{S}$ with $xT = y$, which is an $R$-isomorphism since the identity is an isomorphism and $y$ is transcendental over $R$.
