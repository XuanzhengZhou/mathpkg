**Proof.** The first part of the corollary follows from the fact that under the assumptions made we must have $R[x] = R[xT]$, so by the preceding theorem $xT$ must be of the form $a_0 + a_1 x$ with $a_1$ a unit. The second part follows directly from the preceding theorem and from the corollary to Theorem 7 (which gives the uniqueness of the extension of the identity on $R$ sending $x$ to $x'$).
