This corollary classifies all $R$-automorphisms of a polynomial ring $R[x]$ over an integral domain $R$: they correspond precisely to affine linear changes of variable $x \mapsto a_0 + a_1 x$ with $a_1 \in R^\times$. In particular, the automorphism group $\operatorname{Aut}_R(R[x])$ is isomorphic to the affine group $R \rtimes R^\times$.
