A root of a polynomial $f(X)$ over a ring $R$ is an element $a \in R$ that makes the polynomial vanish upon substitution. Combined with the Factor Theorem, this means $a$ is a root if and only if the linear factor $X - a$ divides $f(X)$ in $R[X]$.
