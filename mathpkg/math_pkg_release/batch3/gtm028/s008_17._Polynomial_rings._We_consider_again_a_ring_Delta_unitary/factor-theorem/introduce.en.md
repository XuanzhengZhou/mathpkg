The Factor Theorem states that an element $a \in R$ is a root of a polynomial $f(x)$ precisely when the linear polynomial $x - a$ divides $f(x)$. It follows immediately from the division algorithm: dividing $f(x)$ by $x - a$ (which has leading coefficient $1$, always regular) gives $f(x) = q(x)(x - a) + b$ with constant remainder $b = f(a)$.
