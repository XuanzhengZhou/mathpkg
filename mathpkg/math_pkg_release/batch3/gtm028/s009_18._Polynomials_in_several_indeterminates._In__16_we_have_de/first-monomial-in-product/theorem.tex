Let $f$ and $g$ be non-zero polynomials with homogeneous decompositions
$$f = f_0 + f_1 + \cdots + f_p, \quad g = g_0 + g_1 + \cdots + g_q,$$
where $f_p \neq 0$ and $g_q \neq 0$. With respect to the lexicographic ordering of monomials, let
$$aX_1^{\alpha_1} X_2^{\alpha_2} \cdots X_n^{\alpha_n} \quad (a \neq 0)$$
be the first monomial which actually occurs in $f_p$, and let
$$bX_1^{\beta_1} X_2^{\beta_2} \cdots X_n^{\beta_n} \quad (b \neq 0)$$
be the first monomial which actually occurs in $g_q$. Then the monomial
$$abX_1^{\alpha_1+\beta_1} X_2^{\alpha_2+\beta_2} \cdots X_n^{\alpha_n+\beta_n}$$
is the first monomial in the product $f_p g_q$, and since $ab \neq 0$, it follows that $f_p g_q \neq 0$.
