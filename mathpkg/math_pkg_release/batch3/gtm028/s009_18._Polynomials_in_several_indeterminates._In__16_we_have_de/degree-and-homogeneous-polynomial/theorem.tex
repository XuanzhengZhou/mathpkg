By the **degree** of a monomial $a_{(i)} X_1^{i_1} X_2^{i_2} \cdots X_n^{i_n}$ we mean the sum of the exponents $i_1 + i_2 + \cdots + i_n$.

By the **degree** $\partial f$ of any non-zero polynomial $f$ we mean the maximum of the degrees of the monomials of which $f$ is the sum.

If all the monomials in this sum have the same degree, then $f$ is said to be **homogeneous** or to be a **form**.
