Gauss's classical result that $R[X]$ is a UFD whenever $R$ is a UFD extends by induction to multivariate polynomial rings: if $R$ is a UFD, then $R[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ is also a UFD for any finite number $n$ of indeterminates. This follows from the inductive structure $R[X_1,\dots,X_n] \cong (R[X_1,\dots,X_{n-1}])[X_n]$ together with the base case (Theorem 10 of §17).
