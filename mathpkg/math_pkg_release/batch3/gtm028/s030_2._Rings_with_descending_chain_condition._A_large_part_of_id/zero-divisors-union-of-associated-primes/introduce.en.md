This corollary gives a structural characterization of zero-divisors in a Noetherian ring: an element is a zero-divisor if and only if it belongs to some associated prime ideal of the zero ideal. This follows from applying Corollary 2 of Theorem 11 to the ideal $\mathfrak{a} = (0)$. The result connects the algebraic concept of zero-divisors to the geometric notion of associated primes.
