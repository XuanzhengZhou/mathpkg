Lemma 3 states that the property of being closed (with respect to a fixed ideal $\mathfrak{m}$) is preserved under arbitrary intersections. This is a basic topological fact: the intersection of closed sets is closed. It is used in the characterization of the closure of an arbitrary ideal in terms of its primary decomposition.
