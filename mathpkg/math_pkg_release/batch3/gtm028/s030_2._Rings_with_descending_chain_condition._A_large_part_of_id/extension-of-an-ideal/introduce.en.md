Given a ring homomorphism $f: R \to S$, the extension of an ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ of $R$ is the ideal generated in $S$ by the image $f(\mathfrak{a})$. It is the smallest ideal of $S$ containing $f(\mathfrak{a})$. Extension is one of the two fundamental operations (along with contraction) that relate the ideal theory of $R$ and $S$.
