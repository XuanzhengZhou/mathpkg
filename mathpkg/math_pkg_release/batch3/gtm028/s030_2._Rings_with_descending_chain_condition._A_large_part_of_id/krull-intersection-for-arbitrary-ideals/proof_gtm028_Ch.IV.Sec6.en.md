Apply Theorem 12 to the residue class ring $\overline{R} = R/\mathfrak{a}$. The condition $\bigcap_n (\mathfrak{a} + \mathfrak{m}^n) = \mathfrak{a}$ in $R$ translates to $\bigcap_n \overline{\mathfrak{m}}^n = (0)$ in $\overline{R}$. The associated primes of $(0)$ in $\overline{R}$ correspond to the associated primes of $\mathfrak{a}$ in $R$. The condition from Theorem 12 states that no element of $1 - \overline{\mathfrak{m}}$ is a zero-divisor in $\overline{R}$, which translates to $\mathfrak{m} + \mathfrak{p}_i \neq R$ for every associated prime $\mathfrak{p}_i$ of $\mathfrak{a}$ in $R$.
