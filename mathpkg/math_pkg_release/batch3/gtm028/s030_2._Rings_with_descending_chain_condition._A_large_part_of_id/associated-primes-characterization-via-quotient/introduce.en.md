Theorem 6 provides an intrinsic characterization of the associated prime ideals of an ideal $\mathfrak{a}$, independent of the choice of primary decomposition. The associated primes are exactly the radicals of quotient ideals $\mathfrak{a} : (c)$ that happen to be primary. This theorem is fundamental because it shows that the set of associated prime ideals is uniquely determined by $\mathfrak{a}$.
