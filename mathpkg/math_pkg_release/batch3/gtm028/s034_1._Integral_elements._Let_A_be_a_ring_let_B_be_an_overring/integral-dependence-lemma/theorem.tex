Let $A$ be a ring, $\mathfrak{q}$ an ideal in $A$, $M$ a finite $A$-module contained in an overring $R$ of $A$, and $x$ an element of $R$ such that

\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(1')] $xM \subset M\mathfrak{q}$;
  \item[(2')] zero is the only element $y$ of $A[x]$ such that $yz = 0$ for all $z$ in $M$.
\end{enumerate}

Then $x$ satisfies an equation of integral dependence of the form

$$x^n + q_{n-1}x^{n-1} + \cdots + q_1 x + q_0 = 0,$$

where all the $q_i$ ($0 \leq i \leq n-1$) belong to $\mathfrak{q}$.
