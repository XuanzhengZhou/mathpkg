Let $\mathfrak{q}$ be a primary ideal of $R$ disjoint from $M$, and let $\mathfrak{p}$ be its (prime) radical. Then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\mathfrak{p}$ is disjoint from $M$, $\mathfrak{p}$ and $\mathfrak{q}$ are contracted ideals, and both contain the kernel $\mathfrak{n}$ of the canonical homomorphism $h: R \to R_M$.
\item[(b)] $\mathfrak{q}^e$ is primary in $R_M$, and $\mathfrak{p}^e$ is its associated prime.
\end{enumerate}
