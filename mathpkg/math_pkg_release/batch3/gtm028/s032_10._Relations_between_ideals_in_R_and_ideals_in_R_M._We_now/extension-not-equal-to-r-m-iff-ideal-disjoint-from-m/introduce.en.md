Corollary 2 to Theorem 15 gives a simple criterion: the extension $\mathfrak{a}^e$ of an ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ is a proper ideal in the quotient ring $R_M$ precisely when $\mathfrak{a}$ is disjoint from the multiplicative system $M$. Equivalently, $\mathfrak{a}^e = R_M$ if and only if $\mathfrak{a} \cap M \neq \emptyset$. This follows from Theorem 15(a) by noting that $\mathfrak{a}^e = R_M$ is equivalent to $1 \in \mathfrak{a}^{ec}$.
