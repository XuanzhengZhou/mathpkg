Corollary 1 to Theorem 15 states that the noetherian property is preserved under localization. If $R$ is noetherian, then $R_M$ is also noetherian for any multiplicative system $M$. This follows either from the $1$-$1$ correspondence in Theorem 15(d) together with the maximum condition on ideals, or from Theorem 15(c) and the finite basis property of noetherian rings.
