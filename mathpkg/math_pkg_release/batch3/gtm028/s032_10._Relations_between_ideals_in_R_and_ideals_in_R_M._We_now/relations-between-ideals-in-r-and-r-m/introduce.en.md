Theorem 15 establishes the fundamental relationships between ideals in a ring $R$ and ideals in its quotient ring $R_M$ with respect to a multiplicative system $M$. Part (a) characterizes the extension-contraction $\mathfrak{a}^{ec}$ as those elements killed by some $m \in M$ modulo $\mathfrak{a}$. Part (b) gives the criterion for $\mathfrak{a}^e$ to be the whole ring $R_M$. Part (c) shows every ideal in $R_M$ is an extended ideal, and part (d) establishes a $1$-$1$ correspondence between ideals of $R_M$ and contracted ideals of $R$, preserving all ideal operations.
