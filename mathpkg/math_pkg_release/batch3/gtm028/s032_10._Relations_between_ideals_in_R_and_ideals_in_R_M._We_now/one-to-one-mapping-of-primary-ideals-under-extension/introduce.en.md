Corollary 2 to Theorem 16 refines the correspondence: for a fixed contracted prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$, extension gives a bijection between $\mathfrak{p}$-primary ideals of $R$ and $\mathfrak{p}^e$-primary ideals of $R_M$. Moreover, this bijection preserves the ideal quotient and intersection operations, except for the trivial case where $\mathfrak{q}_1:\mathfrak{q}_2 = R$ in which the operation is also preserved after extension since $R^e = R_M$.
