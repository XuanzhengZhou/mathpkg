Given a contracted prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ of $R$, the mapping $\mathfrak{q} \to \mathfrak{q}^e$ is a $1$-$1$ mapping of the set of all ideals in $R$ which are primary for $\mathfrak{p}$ onto the set of all ideals in $R_M$ which are primary for $\mathfrak{p}^e$, and this mapping is an isomorphism for the operations $:$ (ideal quotient) and $\cap$ (intersection).
