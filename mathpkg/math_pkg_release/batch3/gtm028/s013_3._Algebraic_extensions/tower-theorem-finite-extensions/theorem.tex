Let $k \subset K \subset L$ be fields. If $\{\omega_1, \ldots, \omega_n\}$ is a basis of $K$ over $k$ and $\{\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_m\}$ is a basis of $L$ over $K$, then the $mn$ products
$$\omega_i \xi_j \qquad (i = 1, 2, \ldots, n;\; j = 1, 2, \ldots, m)$$
form a basis of $L$ over $k$. Consequently, $L$ is a finite extension of $k$.
