Let $R$ be a ring, $D$ a derivation of $R$, and $A$ the polynomial ring $R[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$. For every polynomial
$$f(X_1, \ldots, X_n) = \sum a_{q_1, \ldots, q_n} X_1^{q_1} \cdots X_n^{q_n}$$
define
$$f^D(X_1, \ldots, X_n) = \sum D(a_{q_1, \ldots, q_n}) X_1^{q_1} \cdots X_n^{q_n}.$$
Then the mapping $f \mapsto f^D$ is a derivation of $A$ extending $D$.
