Corollary 4' generalizes Corollary 4 to arbitrary purely inseparable extensions satisfying $F^p \subset K$. The condition $D(x^p) = 0$ for each generator $x \in S$ ensures that the necessary compatibility conditions from Theorem 39 are met. Zorn's lemma is used to handle the case of infinitely many generators by considering the set of all partial extensions.
