Let $K$ be a field and $F = K(S)$ a purely transcendental extension of $K$, where $S$ is a set of generators algebraically independent over $K$. Let $x \mapsto u_x$ be a mapping of $S$ into a field $L$ containing $F$. If $D$ is any derivation of $K$ with values in $L$, then there exists one and only one derivation $D'$ of $F$ extending $D$ such that $D'(x) = u_x$ for all $x \in S$.
