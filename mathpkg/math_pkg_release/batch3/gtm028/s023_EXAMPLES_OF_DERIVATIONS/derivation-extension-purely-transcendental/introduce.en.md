Extending Corollary 1 to an arbitrary set of algebraically independent generators, a derivation $D$ of $K$ extends uniquely to a purely transcendental extension $F = K(S)$ with independently prescribed values on each generator. The proof uses Zorn's lemma to handle the case of infinitely many generators, reducing to the finitely generated case where Theorem 39 applies trivially due to the absence of algebraic relations.
