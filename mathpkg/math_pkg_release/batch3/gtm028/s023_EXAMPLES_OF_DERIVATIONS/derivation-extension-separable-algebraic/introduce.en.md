For a separable algebraic extension $F/K$, every derivation of $K$ extends uniquely to $F$. The proof is a straightforward application of Zorn's lemma, similar to the purely transcendental case but simpler, using the fact that separable algebraic extensions are generated by simple separable algebraic elements for which the extension is determined by the minimal polynomial.
