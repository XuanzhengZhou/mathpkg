In characteristic $p \neq 0$, every derivation $D$ of a field $L$ is trivial on the subfield $L^p = \{x^p \mid x \in L\}$. In particular, a perfect field of characteristic $p \neq 0$ admits no non-trivial derivations. More generally, $L^p$ is exactly the set of all elements $x \in L$ annihilated by every derivation of $L$.
