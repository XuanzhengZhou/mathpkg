Let $K$ be a field and $L$ an extension field. The set of all derivations of $K$ with values in $L$ forms a vector space over $L$, denoted by $\mathcal{D}_K$ or $\mathcal{D}_K(L)$, with addition $(D + D')(x) = D(x) + D'(x)$ and scalar multiplication $(aD)(x) = a D(x)$ for $a \in L$. If $K'$ is a subfield of $K$, the derivations of $K$ that are trivial on $K'$ form a vector subspace denoted by $\mathcal{D}_{K/K'}$.
