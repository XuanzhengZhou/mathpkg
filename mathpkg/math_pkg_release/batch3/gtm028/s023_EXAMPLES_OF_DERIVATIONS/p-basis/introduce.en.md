A $p$-basis of $F$ is a $p$-independent set that also generates $F$ as a field extension of $F^p$. It serves as a "basis" for the purely inseparable extension $F/F^p$ in much the same way a transcendence basis serves for field extensions in characteristic $0$. The cardinality of a $p$-basis, the $p$-degree or Ershov invariant, equals the dimension of the derivation space $\mathcal{D}_{F/F^p}$.
