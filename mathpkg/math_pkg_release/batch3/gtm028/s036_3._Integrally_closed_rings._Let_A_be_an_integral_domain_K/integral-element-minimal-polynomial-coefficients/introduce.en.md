For an integral domain A with quotient field K, any element x integral over A is algebraic over K. The minimal polynomial of x over K has coefficients that are integral over A. Consequently, when A is integrally closed, the minimal polynomial already lies in A[X]. This theorem bridges integral dependence and field theory, showing that integral elements satisfy monic equations whose coefficients belong to the integral closure.
