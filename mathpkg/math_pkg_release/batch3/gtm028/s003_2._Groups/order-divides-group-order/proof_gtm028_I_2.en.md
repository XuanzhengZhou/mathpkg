Let $G$ be a finite group and $a \in G$. Consider the cyclic subgroup $H = \langle a \rangle = \{a^n \mid n \geq 0\}$ generated by $a$. Since $G$ is finite, $H$ is also finite; therefore $a$ has finite order $m = |H|$. By Lagrange's theorem, the order $m$ of the subgroup $H$ divides the order $|G|$ of $G$. Thus the order of $a$ divides the order of $G$.
