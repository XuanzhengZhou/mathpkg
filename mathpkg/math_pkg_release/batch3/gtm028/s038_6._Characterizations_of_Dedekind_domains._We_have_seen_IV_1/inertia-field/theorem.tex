The fixed field $K_T$ of the inertia group $G_T$ is called the \textit{inertia field} of $\mathfrak{P}$. We have the tower of fields
$$K \subset K_Z \subset K_T \subset L,$$
with $[K_Z:K] = g$, $[K_T:K_Z] = f_0$ (reduced relative degree), and $[L:K_T] = e p^s$ (ramification index times inseparable degree).
