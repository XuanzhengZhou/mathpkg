Let $R$ be a Dedekind domain with quotient field $K$, $L$ a finite normal extension of $K$ with Galois group $G$, and $R'$ the integral closure of $R$ in $L$. For a prime ideal $\mathfrak{P}$ of $R'$ lying over a prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ of $R$, the \textit{decomposition group} $G_Z$ of $\mathfrak{P}$ is the subgroup of $G$ consisting of all automorphisms $s \in G$ such that $s(\mathfrak{P}) = \mathfrak{P}$.
