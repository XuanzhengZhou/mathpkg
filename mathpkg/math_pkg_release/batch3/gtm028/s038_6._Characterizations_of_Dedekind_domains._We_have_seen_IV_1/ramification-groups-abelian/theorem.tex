Let $R$ be a Dedekind domain, $L$ a finite normal extension of its quotient field $K$, and $R'$ the integral closure of $R$ in $L$. Let $\mathfrak{P}$ be a prime ideal of $R'$ lying over a prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ of $R$. Then the higher ramification groups $G_{V_n}$ of $\mathfrak{P}$ satisfy: for $n \geq 1$, the quotient group $G_{V_n} / G_{V_{n+1}}$ is isomorphic to a subgroup of the additive group of $R'/\mathfrak{P}$, and is therefore abelian. In the case that the residue field extension $R'/\mathfrak{P}$ over $R/\mathfrak{p}$ is separable, $G_{V_n} / G_{V_{n+1}}$ is an elementary abelian $p$-group where $p = \operatorname{char}(R/\mathfrak{p})$.
