The higher ramification groups $G_{V_n}$ form a descending filtration of the decomposition group of a prime ideal in a Galois extension of Dedekind domains. A fundamental structural result is that the successive quotients $G_{V_n}/G_{V_{n+1}}$ for $n \geq 1$ are abelian groups, embedding into the additive group of the residue field. This underpins the solvability properties of ramification groups and is essential for the study of wild ramification.
