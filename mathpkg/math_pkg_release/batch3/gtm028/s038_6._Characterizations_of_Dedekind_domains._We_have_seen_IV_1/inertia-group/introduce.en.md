The inertia group $G_T$ is the subgroup of the decomposition group consisting of automorphisms that act trivially on the residue field $R'/\mathfrak{P}$. It measures the ramification phenomenon: the order of $G_T$ equals the ramification index $e$ (in the separable case). The fixed field $K_T$ of $G_T$ is called the inertia field, and the extension $L/K_T$ is totally ramified at $\mathfrak{P}$.
