Theorem 23 describes the behavior of the prime ideal $\mathfrak{P}$ in the decomposition field $K_Z$. Over $K_Z$, the prime $\mathfrak{P}$ is the unique prime lying over $\mathfrak{P}_Z$, and it already exhibits the full ramification index $e$ and relative degree $f$. This means the splitting into $g$ distinct primes occurs entirely in the extension $K_Z/K$, while ramification and residue field extension occur in $L/K_Z$.
