This proposition shows that the distributivity of ideal addition and intersection in Dedekind domains follows directly from Theorem 11. Using the prime exponent functions $n_{\mathfrak{p}}$, ideal operations translate to $\min$ and $\max$ on integers, and these integer operations mutually distribute. This provides a clean verification that Dedekind domains satisfy the Chinese Remainder Theorem property.
