This corollary of the Jordan-Hölder theorem states that the multiset of composition factors of $M$ is the disjoint union of the composition factors of any submodule $N$ and those of the quotient $M/N$. This provides a concrete interpretation of the additivity of length and is essential for inductive arguments that decompose a module's structure via its submodules and quotients.
