Let composition series for $N$ and $M - N$ be given, from which a composition series for $M$ is obtained as in the proof of the additivity theorem. The differences for $M$ are $N_{j-1} - N_j$ (from $N$) and $L_{i-1} - L_i$ (from $M - N$, via the correspondence $L_{i-1} - L_i \cong (L_{i-1} - N) - (L_i - N)$). The corollary follows from the definition of the combined series.
