If an $R$-module $M$ has a composition series, then any two composition series are equivalent. That is, if
$$M = M_0 > M_1 > \cdots > M_r = (0)$$
and
$$M = N_0 > N_1 > \cdots > N_r = (0)$$
are two composition series, then the composition differences $M_{i-1} - M_i$ and $N_{j-1} - N_j$ can be paired so that paired differences are $R$-isomorphic. Thus the composition differences of $M$ are uniquely determined up to $R$-isomorphism.
