Theorem 17 provides the fundamental characterization of Noetherian modules: the ascending chain condition is equivalent to every submodule being finitely generated. This is one of the most important equivalences in commutative algebra, as it connects the chain-theoretic formulation of the Noetherian property with the practical finiteness condition that every submodule admits a finite generating set.
