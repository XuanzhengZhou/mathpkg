This corollary shows that chain conditions are preserved under finite sums: if a module is the sum of finitely many submodules each satisfying the a.c.c. (or d.c.c.), then the whole module satisfies the same chain condition. Combined with Theorem 16, this provides powerful tools for proving that modules constructed from Noetherian or Artinian pieces are themselves Noetherian or Artinian.
