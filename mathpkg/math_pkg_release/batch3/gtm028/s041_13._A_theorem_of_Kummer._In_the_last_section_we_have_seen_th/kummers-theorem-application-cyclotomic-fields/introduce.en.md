For cyclotomic fields obtained by adjoining a primitive p-th root of unity, Kummer's Theorem gives a complete description of prime ideal decomposition. The prime p is the unique ramified prime. For any other prime q, the number of distinct prime ideals lying over q and their residue field degrees are determined by the multiplicative order of q modulo p. This provides a beautiful connection between prime decomposition and elementary number theory.
