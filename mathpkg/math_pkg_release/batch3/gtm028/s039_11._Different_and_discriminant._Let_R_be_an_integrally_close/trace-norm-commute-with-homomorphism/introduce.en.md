This lemma describes the interaction between the trace/norm maps and reduction modulo a prime ideal. It states that the $\mathfrak{p}$-residue of the trace (resp. norm) decomposes as a weighted sum (resp. product) of the traces (resp. norms) taken in the residue field extensions $k_i/k$, with weights equal to the ramification indices $e_i$. This result is essential for computing differential exponents, since it relates $T(zR') \subset R$ to conditions in the residue fields.
