Let $R$ be a Dedekind domain, $K$ its quotient field. Let $K \subset K' \subset K''$ be finite algebraic separable extensions, and $R', R''$ the integral closures of $R$ in $K', K''$ respectively. Then

$$\mathfrak{D}_{R''/R} = \mathfrak{D}_{R''/R'} \cdot \mathfrak{D}_{R'/R} R'',$$

$$\mathfrak{d}_{R''/R} = \mathfrak{d}_{R'/R}^{[K'':K']} \cdot N_{K'/K}(\mathfrak{d}_{R''/R'}).$$
