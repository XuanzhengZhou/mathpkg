Let $R$ be a Dedekind domain, and $R'$ its integral closure in a finite algebraic separable extension $K'$ of the quotient field $K$ of $R$. Let $\mathfrak{p}$ be a proper prime ideal in $R$, $M$ the complement of $\mathfrak{p}$ in $R$, $\mathfrak{P}$ a prime ideal in $R'$ lying over $\mathfrak{p}$, and $m(\mathfrak{P})$ its differential exponent.

Then the differential exponent of $\mathfrak{P}R'_M$ over $R_M$ is $m(\mathfrak{P})$.
