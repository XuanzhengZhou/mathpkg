\text{Let } K \subset K' \subset K'' \text{ be finite algebraic separable extensions, } R \text{ an integrally closed ring with quotient field } K, \text{ and } R', R'' \text{ the integral closures of } R \text{ in } K' \text{ and } K'' \text{ respectively. Then:}

$$\mathfrak{D}_{R''/R} = \mathfrak{D}_{R''/R'} \cdot N_{K''/K'}(\mathfrak{D}_{R'/R}),$$

$$\mathfrak{d}_{R''/R} = N_{K'/K}(\mathfrak{d}_{R''/R'}) \cdot (\mathfrak{d}_{R'/R})^{[K'':K']},$$

\text{where } N \text{ denotes the norm of fractionary ideals (in the sense of §10).}
