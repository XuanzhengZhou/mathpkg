This corollary states that the length of a primary ideal $\mathfrak{q}$ is invariant under localization at any multiplicative system $M$ disjoint from $\mathfrak{q}$. The proof applies Theorem 16 of §10 concerning the behavior of primary ideals under extension and contraction with respect to quotient rings.
