Corollary 1 to Theorem 28 gives a precise algebraic condition characterizing when two ideals $\mathfrak{b} \subsetneq \mathfrak{a}$ are adjacent in the lattice of ideals (i.e., no ideal lies strictly between them). The condition involves a maximal ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ satisfying $\mathfrak{p}\mathfrak{a} \subset \mathfrak{b}$ and an element $x$ generating $\mathfrak{a}$ modulo $\mathfrak{b}$.
