Let $K$ and $K'$ be two extensions of a field $k$, and let $x$ and $x'$ be elements of $K$ and $K'$ respectively which are algebraic over $k$. If $x$ and $x'$ have the same minimal polynomial $f(X)$ in $k[X]$, then there exists a $k$-isomorphism of $k(x)$ onto $k(x')$ which carries $x$ into $x'$, and conversely.
