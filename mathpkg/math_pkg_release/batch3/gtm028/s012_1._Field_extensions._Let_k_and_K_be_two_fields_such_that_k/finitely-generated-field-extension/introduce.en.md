A field extension $K/k$ is finitely generated if $K$ can be expressed as $k(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ for some finite set of elements $x_1, \ldots, x_n \in K$. In other words, $K$ is the smallest subfield containing $k$ and these finitely many elements. When $n = 1$, the extension is called a simple extension.
