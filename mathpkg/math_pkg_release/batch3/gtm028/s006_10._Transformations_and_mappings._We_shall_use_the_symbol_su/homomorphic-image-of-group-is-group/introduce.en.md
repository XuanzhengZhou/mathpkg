Lemma 1 confirms that the surjective homomorphic image of a group is again a group: the associative law holds, the image of the identity is the identity of the image, and each element has an inverse given by the image of its inverse. Moreover, commutativity is preserved under homomorphisms. This lemma is essential for establishing that quotient groups and other homomorphic constructions remain within the category of groups.
