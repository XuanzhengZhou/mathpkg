Corollary 3 is the fundamental existence theorem: every field extension $K/k$ possesses at least one transcendence basis. It follows from Corollary 2 by taking $S = K$, since $K$ is trivially an algebraic extension of $k(K) = K$. This result, together with Theorem 24 (uniqueness of cardinality), justifies the definition of transcendence degree.
