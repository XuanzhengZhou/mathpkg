Theorem 24 establishes that in any dependence relation satisfying $(S_1)$-$(S_5)$, all bases have the same cardinality. For field extensions, this means all transcendence bases of $K/k$ have the same cardinal number, justifying the definition of transcendence degree. The proof for infinite bases uses Zorn's Lemma to associate to each element a minimal finite supporting subset of a given basis, then compares cardinalities of the bases via these finite subsets.
