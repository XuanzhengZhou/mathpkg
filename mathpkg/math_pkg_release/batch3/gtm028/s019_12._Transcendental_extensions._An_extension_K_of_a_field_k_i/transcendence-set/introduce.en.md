A transcendence set (or algebraically independent set) in a field extension $K/k$ is a subset $L$ of $K$ such that every finite subset of $L$ is algebraically independent over $k$. This means that for any finite collection of elements from $L$, there is no nonzero polynomial with coefficients in $k$ that vanishes on them. A transcendence set that is maximal with respect to inclusion is called a transcendence basis of $K/k$.
