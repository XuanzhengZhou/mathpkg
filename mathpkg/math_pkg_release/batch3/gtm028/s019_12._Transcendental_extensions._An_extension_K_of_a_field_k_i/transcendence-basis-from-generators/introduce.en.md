Corollary 2 is the special case of Corollary 1 with $L = \emptyset$. It asserts that any subset $S$ of $K$ that generates $K$ as an algebraic extension of $k(S)$ (i.e., every element of $K$ is algebraic over $k(S)$) contains a transcendence basis of $K/k$. In particular, taking $S = K$ yields Corollary 3.
