We apply Corollary 1 to the case in which $L$ is the empty set. The empty set is trivially a transcendence set, and $S$ is a generating set by hypothesis. Hence there exists $S' \subset S$ such that $L \cup S' = S'$ is a transcendence basis of $K/k$.
