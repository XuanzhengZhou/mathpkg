The indeterminate $X$ is defined as the sequence $(0, 1, 0, 0, \cdots)$ in the polynomial ring constructed from sequences. Using the ring operations, every polynomial can then be expressed uniquely in the familiar form $f = a_0 + a_1 X + a_2 X^2 + \cdots + a_n X^n$. The ring is denoted by $R[X]$, reconciling the formal sequence construction with the standard notation for polynomials.
