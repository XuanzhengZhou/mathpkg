In an integral domain, mutual divisibility implies associateness. If $a \mid b$ and $b \mid a$, then $a = bc$ and $b = ac'$ for some $c, c' \in R$, whence $a = ac'c$, so $c'c = 1$ and $c, c'$ are units. Thus the concept of associates admits an equivalent divisibility-based characterization in integral domains.
