Let $k \subset k' \subset K$ be a tower of fields, let $k(x)$ be a finitely generated extension of $k$, and let $\{z\}$ be a transcendence basis of $k(x)/k$. For any field $k'$ between $k$ and $K$, let $L(k')$ denote the maximal separable extension of $k'(z)$ in $k'(x)$. Let $\{\xi_1, \xi_2, \dots, \xi_m\}$ be a basis of $k(x)$ over $L(k)$ (so that $m = [k(x):k(z)]_i$). If $[K(x):K(z)]_i = [k(x):k(z)]_i$, then the $\xi_i$ are linearly independent over $L(K)$.
