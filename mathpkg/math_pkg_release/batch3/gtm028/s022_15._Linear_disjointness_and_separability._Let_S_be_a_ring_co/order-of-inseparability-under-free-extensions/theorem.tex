Let $k(x)$ be a finitely generated extension of $k$ and let $K$ be an extension field of $k$ such that $K$ and $k(x)$ are free over $k$. Then for any transcendence basis $\{z\}$ of $k(x)/k$ we have:
$$\frac{[K(x):K(z)]_i}{[k(x):k(z)]_i} = \frac{[K(x):K]_i}{[k(x):k]_i}.$$

In particular, if $K$ is a finite algebraic extension of $k$, then the common value of both sides equals $\frac{[K:k]_i}{[K(x):k(x)]_i}$.
