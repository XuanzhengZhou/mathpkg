In a finite normal extension, any $k$-isomorphism from an intermediate field into the extension can be lifted to a $k$-automorphism of the whole extension. This follows from the uniqueness theorem on splitting fields (Theorem 12) by viewing $K$ as a splitting field over $L$ and over its isomorphic image.
