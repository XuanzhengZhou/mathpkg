Let $L$ be a finite algebraic extension of $k$ and let $K$ be an extension of $L$ which is normal over $k$. Let $n_0$ be the separable factor of the degree $[L:k]$, i.e., $n_0 = [L:k]_s$. Then there exist exactly $n_0$ distinct $k$-isomorphisms of $L$ into $K$. Moreover, every $k$-isomorphism of $L$ into $K$ is the restriction of some $k$-automorphism of $K$.
