Let $k$ be a field of characteristic $p \neq 0$, and let $E$ be the subring consisting of the integral multiples $ne \ (n \ge 0)$ of the identity $e$ of $k$. Then $E$ is a field consisting of the $p$ distinct elements
$$
0, e, 2e, \dots, (p-1)e.
$$
Consequently, the prime subfield of $k$ is isomorphic to the finite field $\mathbb{F}_p$ of $p$ elements.
