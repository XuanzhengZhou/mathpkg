A system of generators for a vector space $V$ is a subset $X \subseteq V$ whose span equals the whole space: $s(X) = V$. Equivalently, every element of $V$ can be expressed as a linear combination of finitely many elements of $X$. A minimal system of generators is one from which no element can be removed without losing the generating property. Minimal generating sets are precisely the bases of $V$.
