Theorem 20 provides three equivalent perspectives on what it means for a subset $X$ to be a basis of a vector space $V$: (a) $X$ is a minimal system of generators -- it generates $V$ but no proper subset does; (b) $X$ is a maximal free subset -- it is linearly independent and no larger subset containing it remains free; (c) $X$ is a basis -- every element of $V$ has a unique linear representation in terms of $X$. The proof uses the axiomatic properties $(S_1)$-$(S_4)$ of the span operation in a cyclic implication.
