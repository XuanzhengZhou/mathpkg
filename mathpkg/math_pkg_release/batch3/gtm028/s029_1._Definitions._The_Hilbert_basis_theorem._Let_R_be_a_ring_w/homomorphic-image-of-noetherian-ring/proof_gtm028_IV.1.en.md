Let $\varphi: R \to R'$ be a surjective ring homomorphism with $R$ noetherian. Every ideal $\mathfrak{A}'$ of $R'$ is the image under $\varphi$ of the ideal $\mathfrak{A} = \varphi^{-1}(\mathfrak{A}')$ of $R$. Since $R$ is noetherian, $\mathfrak{A}$ has a finite basis $\{a_1, \dots, a_n\}$. The image in $R'$ of this finite set, namely $\{\varphi(a_1), \dots, \varphi(a_n)\}$, is then a finite set of generators of $\mathfrak{A}'$. Thus $R'$ satisfies the finite basis condition and is therefore noetherian.
