Let $k$ be a field and let $x$ be an algebraic element over $k$ with minimal polynomial $f(X) \in k[X]$. Then $x$ is separable over $k$ if and only if $x$ is a simple root of $f(X)$ in any extension field of $k$ containing $x$. More generally, if $g(X) \in k[X]$ is any polynomial with $g(x) = 0$, then $g'(x) = 0$ whenever $x$ is inseparable over $k$.
