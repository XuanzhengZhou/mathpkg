Let $k$ be a field and let $k[X]$ be the polynomial ring in the indeterminate $X$ over $k$. If
$$f(X) = a_0 X^n + a_1 X^{n-1} + \cdots + a_n, \quad a_i \in k,\ a_0 \neq 0,$$
is any polynomial in $k[X]$ of degree $n$, the derivative $f'(X)$ of $f(X)$ is defined by
$$f'(X) = n a_0 X^{n-1} + (n-1) a_1 X^{n-2} + \cdots + a_{n-1}.$$
The derivative $f'(X)$ is again a polynomial in $k[X]$.
