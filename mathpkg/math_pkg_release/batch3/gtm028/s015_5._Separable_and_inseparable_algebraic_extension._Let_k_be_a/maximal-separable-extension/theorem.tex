Let $K$ be an arbitrary extension of a field $k$, and let $\bar{k}$ be the algebraic closure of $k$ in $K$. Let $k_0$ be the set of all elements of $K$ which are separable algebraic over $k$. Then $k \subset k_0 \subset \bar{k}$, and $k_0$ is a subfield of $K$ (Theorem 10). The field $k_0$ is called the **maximal separable extension** of $k$ in $K$.
