Let $K$ be an extension of a field $k$ and let $\bar{k}$ be the algebraic closure of $k$ in $K$. Let $k_0$ be the maximal separable extension of $k$ in $K$. Then every element $x \in \bar{k}$ is purely inseparable over $k_0$. Consequently, the algebraic extension $k \to \bar{k}$ can be decomposed as a separable extension $k \to k_0$ followed by a purely inseparable extension $k_0 \to \bar{k}$.
