Let $K$ be an extension of a field $k$. The field $k$ is said to be **quasi-algebraically closed** in $K$ if $k = k_0$, where $k_0$ is the maximal separable extension of $k$ in $K$. Equivalently, $k$ is quasi-algebraically closed in $K$ if every element of $K$ that is separable algebraic over $k$ already lies in $k$.
