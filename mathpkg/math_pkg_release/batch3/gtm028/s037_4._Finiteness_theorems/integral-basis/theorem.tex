Let $A$ be an integrally closed domain with quotient field $K$, and let $F$ be a finite separable algebraic extension of $K$. A basis $\{y_1, \cdots, y_n\}$ of $F$ over $K$ is called an **integral basis** of $F$ (with respect to $A$) if each $y_i$ is integral over $A$ and $A' = \sum_i A y_i$, where $A'$ is the integral closure of $A$ in $F$.
