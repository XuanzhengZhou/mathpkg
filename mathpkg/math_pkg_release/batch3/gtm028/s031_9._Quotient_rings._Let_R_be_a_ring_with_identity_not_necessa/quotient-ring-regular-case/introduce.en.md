When $M$ is a regular multiplicative system in a ring $R$, the quotient ring $R_M$ is defined as the set of all formal fractions $a/m$ with $a \in R$ and $m \in M$, subject to the usual equivalence relation $a/m \sim b/n$ if there exists $t \in M$ such that $t(an - bm) = 0$. Since all elements of $M$ are regular, $R_M$ is a subring of the total quotient ring $F$ of $R$.
