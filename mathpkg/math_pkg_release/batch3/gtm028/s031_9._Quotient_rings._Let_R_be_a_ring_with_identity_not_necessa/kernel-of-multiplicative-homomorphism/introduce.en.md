This lemma characterizes a necessary condition on the kernel of any homomorphism that maps a multiplicative system $M$ into units. If an element $x$ is annihilated by some element of $M$, then $x$ must be sent to zero under any such homomorphism. This observation is fundamental to the construction of the generalized quotient ring $R_M$, as it identifies the minimal ideal that must be factored out.
