The total quotient ring $F$ of a ring $R$ is the localization of $R$ at the multiplicative system of all regular elements of $R$. It is the maximal ring of fractions of $R$ in which every non-zero-divisor becomes a unit. Every element of $F$ can be expressed as a fraction $a/b$ with numerator $a \in R$ and denominator $b$ a regular element of $R$.
