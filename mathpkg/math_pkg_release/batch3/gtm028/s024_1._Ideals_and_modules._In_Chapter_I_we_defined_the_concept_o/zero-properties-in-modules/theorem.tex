Let $M$ be an $R$-module. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $a0 = 0$ for all $a \in R$, where $0$ denotes the zero element of $M$.
\item $0x = 0$ for all $x \in M$, where $0$ denotes the zero element of $R$ (and the right side is the zero of $M$).
\item $-(ax) = (-a)x = a(-x)$ for any $a \in R$ and $x \in M$.
\end{enumerate}
