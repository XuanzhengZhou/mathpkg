By axiom (1) of the definition of an $R$-module, $a(x + y) = ax + ay$ for all $x, y \in M$. This means $T_a(x + y) = T_a(x) + T_a(y)$, so $T_a$ is a homomorphism of the additive group of $M$ into itself, i.e., an endomorphism of $M$ as a group.
