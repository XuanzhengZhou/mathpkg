Let $R$ be a ring and let $M$ be a multiplicative system in $R$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item If $R$ has an identity and $M$ is the set of all units of $R$, then $R_M = R$.
\item If $M$ is the set of all regular elements of $R$, then $R_M = F$, where $F$ is the total quotient ring of $R$.
\end{enumerate}
