Let $(\mathcal{A}, U) \in \text{Struct}(\mathbf{Set})$ be a category of sets with structure. Then $U: \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathbf{Set}$ is algebraic if and only if the following three conditions are satisfied:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $U$ has a left adjoint;
\item $\mathcal{A}$ has separators;
\item $U$ creates quotients of congruences.
\end{enumerate}
