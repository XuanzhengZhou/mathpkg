Show that the intersection (infimum) of subobjects represented by monos $f_1: S_1 \rightarrow K$, $f_2: S_2 \rightarrow K$ coincides with the subobject represented by $j: P \rightarrow K$ in the pullback (in the sense that if the pullback exists they are equal). Show that if $f, g: K \rightarrow L$ and if $K \times K$, $\ker\mathrm{pair}(f)$ and $\ker\mathrm{pair}(g)$ exist, then the separator of $f$ and $g$ is just the intersection of $\ker\mathrm{pair}(f)$ and $\ker\mathrm{pair}(g)$.
