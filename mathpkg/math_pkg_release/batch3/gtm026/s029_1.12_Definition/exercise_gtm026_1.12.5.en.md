Show that the intersection (infimum) of subobjects represented by monos $f_1: S_1 \rightarrow K$, $f_2: S_2 \rightarrow K$ coincides with the subobject represented by $j: P \rightarrow K$ in the pullback

$$\begin{CD}
P @>>> S_1\\
@VVV @VVf_1V\\
S_2 @>>f_2> K
\end{CD}$$

(in the sense that if the pullback exists they are equal). Show that if $f, g: K \rightarrow L$ and if $K \times K$, $\ker\text{pair}(f)$ and $\ker\text{pair}(g)$ exist, then the separator of $f$ and $g$ is just the intersection of $\ker\text{pair}(f)$ and $\ker\text{pair}(g)$.
