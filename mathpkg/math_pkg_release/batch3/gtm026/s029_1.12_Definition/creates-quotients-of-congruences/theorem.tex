Let $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ be a category of sets with structure. Then $U$ creates quotients of congruences if whenever we are given an object $A$ of $\mathcal{A}$ and a congruence $R$ on $A$, we may conclude that the canonical projection $\theta: A \longrightarrow AU/R$ has a unique lift $\bar{\theta}: A \longrightarrow A/R$; and then, moreover, $\bar{\theta}$ is co-optimal in $\mathcal{A}$.
