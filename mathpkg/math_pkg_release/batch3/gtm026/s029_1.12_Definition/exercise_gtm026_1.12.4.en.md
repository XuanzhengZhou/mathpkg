Let $\mathcal{K}^-$ be the category whose objects are morphisms $f: X \to Y$ in $\mathcal{K}$ and whose morphisms are commutative squares:

$$\begin{CD}
X @>\varphi>> X'\\
@VfVV @VV\bar{f}V\\
Y @>\psi>> Y'
\end{CD}$$

(Here, $(\varphi, \psi)$ is a morphism from $f$ to $\bar{f}$). Given $(f, g, h)$ with $fh = gh$, show that $(f, g, h)$ extends to a contractible coequalizer if and only if $(f, h): g \to h$ is a split epimorphism in $\mathcal{K}^-$.
