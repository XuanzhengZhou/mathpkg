A pair of maps $f, g: A \longrightarrow B$ is reflexive if there exists $d: B \longrightarrow A$ with $d.f = \mathrm{id}_B = d.g$. In the context of 1.29, show that it is enough to postulate that every reflexive pair of $\mathcal{A}$-morphisms has a coequalizer.
