Let $(\mathcal{P}, V) \in \text{Struct}(\mathcal{L})$ be the pullback of $(\mathcal{A}, U) \in \text{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ along the full replete subcategory $\mathcal{L}$ and let $W$ be the common value of $Vi$ and $jU$. Assume further that $\mathcal{P}$ is reflective in $\mathcal{A}$ and that there exists a functor $s: \mathcal{K} \longrightarrow \mathcal{L}$ with $i.s = \text{id}_{\mathcal{L}}$. Then if $U$ is algebraic, $W$ is algebraic.
