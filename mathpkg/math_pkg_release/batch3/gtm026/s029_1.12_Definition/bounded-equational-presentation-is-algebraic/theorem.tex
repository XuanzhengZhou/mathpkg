Let $(\Omega, E)$ be an equational presentation as in 1.5.34 and assume that $(\Omega, E)$ is bounded, i.e., there exists a cardinal $n_0$ such that for all $n \geqslant n_0$, $\Omega_n$ is empty. Then the underlying set functor $U: (\Omega, E)\text{-alg} \longrightarrow \mathbf{Set}$ is algebraic.
