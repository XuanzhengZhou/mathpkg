Assume given a commutative diagram of functors
\[
\begin{array}{ccc}
\mathcal{A} & \xrightarrow{U} & \mathcal{B} \\
\downarrow{W} & & \downarrow{V} \\
\mathcal{K} & = & \mathcal{K}
\end{array}
\]
and assume that $V$ is algebraic and that $\mathcal{A}$ has coequalizers. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $U$ has a left adjoint if and only if $W$ does.
\item If $W$ is algebraic, so is $U$.
\end{enumerate}
