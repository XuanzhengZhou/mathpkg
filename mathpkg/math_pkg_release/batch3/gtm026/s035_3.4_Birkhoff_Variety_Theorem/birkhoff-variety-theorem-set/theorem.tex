Let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathbf{Set}$ and let $\mathcal{A}$ be a full subcategory of $\mathbf{Set}^{\mathbf{T}}$ with inclusion functor $V : \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathbf{Set}^{\mathbf{T}}$. Then the following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $V$ is closed under the formation of products, subalgebras, and quotient algebras in the sense of 1.4.22.
\item $V$ is an abstract Birkhoff subcategory as in 3.1.
\end{enumerate}
