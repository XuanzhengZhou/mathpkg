Proposition 4.19 demonstrates that the category of Hausdorff spaces provides a natural example of a regular category. All three image factorization systems introduced in 4.8 (dense/closed embedding, etc.) satisfy the regular category axioms. The key nontrivial verification is $E$-co-well-poweredness for the case $E = \text{maps with dense image}$, which follows from a cardinality argument using ultrafilter limits: since each point of a Hausdorff space $X$ is uniquely determined as the limit of an ultrafilter on any dense subset $A$, the cardinality of $X$ is bounded by $2^{2^{|A|}}$.
