Proposition 4.14 states that in any image factorization system $(\mathbb{E}, \mathbb{M})$, every split monomorphism (a morphism with a left inverse) belongs to the $\mathbb{M}$ class, and dually every split epimorphism (a morphism with a right inverse) belongs to the $\mathbb{E}$ class. This is proved using the diagonal fill-in property (4.11) and is a fundamental structural fact about factorization systems.
