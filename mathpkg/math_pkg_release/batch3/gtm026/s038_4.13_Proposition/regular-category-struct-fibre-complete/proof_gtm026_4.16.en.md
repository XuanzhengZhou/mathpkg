This proposition is easily proved by the reader. The proof consists of verifying that $(E_1, M_1)$ and $(E_2, M_2)$ satisfy the axioms of an image factorization system and that the resulting categories are $\mathbb{E}$-co-well-powered, using the fibre-completeness of $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ and the regular category structure of $(\mathcal{K}, E, M)$.
