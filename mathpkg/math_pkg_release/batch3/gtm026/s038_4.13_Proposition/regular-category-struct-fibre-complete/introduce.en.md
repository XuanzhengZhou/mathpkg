Proposition 4.16 shows how to lift a regular category structure from a base category $\mathcal{K}$ to a fibre-complete $\mathrm{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ category. Two different image factorization systems on $\mathcal{A}$ are defined: $(E_1, M_1)$ uses co-optimal $\mathcal{A}$-morphisms whose underlying $\mathcal{K}$-morphisms are in $E$, while $(E_2, M_2)$ uses optimal $\mathcal{A}$-morphisms whose underlying $\mathcal{K}$-morphisms are in $M$. Both yield regular category structures on $\mathcal{A}$. The proof is stated to be easily verified by the reader.
