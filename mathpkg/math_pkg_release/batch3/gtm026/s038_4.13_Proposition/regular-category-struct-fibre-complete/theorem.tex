Let $(\mathcal{K}, E, M)$ be a regular category and let $(\mathcal{A}, U) \in \mathrm{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ be fibre-complete. Define

$$E_1 = \{e \in \mathcal{A}: e \text{ is co-optimal and } eU \in E\}$$
$$M_1 = \{m \in \mathcal{A}: mU \in M\}$$
$$E_2 = \{e \in \mathcal{A}: eU \in E\}$$
$$M_2 = \{m \in \mathcal{A}: m \text{ is optimal and } mU \in M\}$$

Then $(\mathcal{A}, E_1, M_1)$ and $(\mathcal{A}, E_2, M_2)$ are regular categories.
