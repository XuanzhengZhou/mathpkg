Let $f: A \longrightarrow B$ and $g: B \longrightarrow C$ in $\mathcal{H}$, and let $(\mathbb{E}, \mathbb{M})$ be an image factorization system on $\mathcal{H}$.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item If $f \circ g \in \mathbb{M}$, then $f \in \mathbb{M}$.
  \item If $f \circ g \in \mathbb{E}$, then $g \in \mathbb{E}$.
\end{enumerate}
