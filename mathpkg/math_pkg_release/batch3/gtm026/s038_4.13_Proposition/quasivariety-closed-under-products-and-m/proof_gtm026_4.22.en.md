This follows at once from 2.1.22, which states that under the hypothesis that every equalizer is in $\mathbb{M}$, a full subcategory $\mathcal{B}$ closed under products and $\mathbb{M}$-subobjects is automatically replete and reflective with reflections in $\mathbb{E}$. Together with the Quasivariety Theorem (4.21), this yields the stated equivalence.
