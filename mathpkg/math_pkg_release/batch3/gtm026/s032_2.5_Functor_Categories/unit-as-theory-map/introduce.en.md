Proposition 2.12 observes a pleasing consistency: the unit $\eta$ of an algebraic theory $T$ can itself be viewed as a theory map from the trivial identity theory to $T$. Under the correspondence of Proposition 2.9, this theory map induces exactly the forgetful functor from the category of $T$-algebras back to the base category. This ties together the algebraic structure of the theory with the universal property of its algebras.
