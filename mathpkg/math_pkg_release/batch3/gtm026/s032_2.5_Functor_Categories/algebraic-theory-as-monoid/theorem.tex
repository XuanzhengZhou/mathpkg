A monoid of the strict monoidal category $(\mathcal{K}^\mathcal{K}, \mathrm{comp}, \mathrm{id}_\mathcal{K})$ is the same thing as an algebraic theory $(T, \eta, \mu)$ in $\mathcal{K}$ in monoid form. Consequently, the algebraic theories in a category $\mathcal{K}$ themselves form a category: the category of monoids in $(\mathcal{K}^\mathcal{K}, \mathrm{comp}, \mathrm{id}_\mathcal{K})$.
