A strict monoidal category is a monoidal category where the associativity and unit constraints are identities, meaning the tensor product is associative "on the nose" rather than only up to natural isomorphism. The endofunctor category with composition as the tensor product is a pleasant example: it is strictly associative and has the identity functor as a strict unit, making it a strict monoidal category.
