Proposition 2.9 establishes that a theory map $\lambda: T \to T'$ induces functors between the corresponding categories of algebras. This is a fundamental structural result: morphisms between theories translate to functors between their categories of models. The correspondence is a key ingredient in the proof that the category of theories (up to opposites) embeds into the category of algebraic functors.
