Exercise 3 introduces symmetric monoidal categories, which are monoidal categories equipped with a natural symmetry isomorphism $A \otimes B \cong B \otimes A$ satisfying coherence conditions. The exercise asks the reader to verify examples including the cartesian monoidal structure on categories with finite products, and to identify their monoids.
