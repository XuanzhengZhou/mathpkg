Let $\mathcal{T}$ be a topology of open sets on $X$. An ultrafilter $\mathcal{U}$ on $X$ \text{converges} to $x$ with respect to $\mathcal{T}$, written $\mathcal{U} \longrightarrow x$ (or just $\mathcal{U} \longrightarrow x$), if $\mathcal{U} \supset \mathfrak{N}_x$, where $\mathfrak{N}_x = \{N \subset X : x \in N^0\}$ is the set of $\mathcal{T}$-neighborhoods of $x$.
