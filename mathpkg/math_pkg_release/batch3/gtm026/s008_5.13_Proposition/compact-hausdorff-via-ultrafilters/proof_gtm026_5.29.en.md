This theorem can be found in many standard texts, or can be taken as definition.

The proof follows from the ultrafilter characterizations established earlier in the section (the equivalent conditions 1-5 for ultrafilters) together with the definitions of compactness (every open cover has a finite subcover) and the Hausdorff property (distinct points have disjoint neighborhoods). The equivalence with ultrafilter convergence is then established via the correspondence between open covers and families of closed sets with the finite intersection property.
