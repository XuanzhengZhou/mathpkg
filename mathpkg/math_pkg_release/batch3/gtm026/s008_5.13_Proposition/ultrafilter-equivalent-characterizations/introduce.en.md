An ultrafilter is a maximal filter: one that cannot be properly extended. This lemma provides five equivalent formulations of this maximality. Condition (1) is the standard definition (every set or its complement belongs to the ultrafilter), while condition (4) states maximality directly in terms of the finite intersection property. These equivalences are fundamental for the ultrafilter-based characterizations of compactness and the Hausdorff property.
