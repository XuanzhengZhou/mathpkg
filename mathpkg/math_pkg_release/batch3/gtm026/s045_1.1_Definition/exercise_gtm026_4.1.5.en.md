Let $M$ be a monoid and consider the set $M\beta$ of ultrafilters on the set $M$. For $\mathcal{U}, \mathcal{V}$ in $M\beta$ define

$$\mathcal{U} \cdot \mathcal{V} = \{A \subset M : \exists V \text{ in } \mathcal{V}\; \forall v \text{ in } V\; \exists U \text{ in } \mathcal{U}\; \text{with } vU \subset A\}.$$

Show that this defines a monoid structure on $M\beta$. [This is the Stone-Cech compactification of the discrete monoid $M$.]
