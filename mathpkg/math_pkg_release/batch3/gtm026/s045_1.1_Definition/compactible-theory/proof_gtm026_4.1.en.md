*Proof.* For any $T$-algebra $(X, \xi)$ and $p$ in $E = E(X, \xi)$, the left multiplication $L_p: E \rightarrow E$ is a $T$-homomorphism. Now use the corresponding functor $V: \mathbf{Set}^T \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}^{\beta}$ induced by the theory map. The $\beta$-structure on $E$ provides the required compact Hausdorff topology. $\square$
