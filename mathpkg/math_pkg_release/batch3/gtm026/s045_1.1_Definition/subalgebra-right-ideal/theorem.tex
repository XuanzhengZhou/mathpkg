Let $(X, \xi)$ be a $T$-algebra and set $E = E(X, \xi)$. Let $I$ be a subset of $E$. The following three statements are true:

1. If $I$ is a $T$-subalgebra of $E$ then $I$ is a right ideal of $E$.
2. For $p$ in $E$, the $T$-subalgebra $\langle p \rangle$ generated by $p$ is the set $pE = \{pq : q \in E\}$.
3. $I$ is a minimal subalgebra of $E$ if and only if $I$ is a minimal right ideal of $E$.
