This theorem identifies the canonical $T$-homomorphism $\lambda$ from the free $T$-algebra on one generator $(1T, 1\mu)$ to the enveloping monoid $E(X,\xi)$ as a reflection arrow. Concretely, $\lambda$ is the universal arrow from $(1T, 1\mu)$ into the Birkhoff subcategory $\mathrm{SMP}(X,\xi)$ generated by $(X,\xi)$. This means that any $T$-homomorphism from $(1T, 1\mu)$ into an algebra in $\mathrm{SMP}(X,\xi)$ factors uniquely through $\lambda$, making the enveloping monoid the "closest" algebra in $\mathrm{SMP}(X,\xi)$ to the free algebra on one generator.
