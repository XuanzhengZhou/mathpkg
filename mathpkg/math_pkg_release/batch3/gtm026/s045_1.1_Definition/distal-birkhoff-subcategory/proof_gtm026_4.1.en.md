*Proof.* That "distal" is closed under subalgebras and quotient algebras is immediate from conditions (4) and (5) of 1.21. Now let $(X, \xi)$ be the product of the family $(X_i, \xi_i)$ of distal algebras. The diagram

$$\xi^g = \prod_i \xi_i^g$$

shows that the unary operation $\xi^g$ on the product is the product of the unary operations on the factors. Since each $\xi_i^g$ is injective (by condition (2) of 1.21), their product is injective. Hence $(X, \xi)$ satisfies condition (2) and is distal. $\square$
