A \emph{limit} of a diagram $(\Delta, D)$ in a category $\mathcal{K}$ is a lower bound $(L, \psi)$ with the universal property that whenever $(L', \psi')$ is another lower bound, there exists a unique $\mathcal{K}$-morphism $f: L' \longrightarrow L$ such that

$$f \circ \psi_i = \psi'_i$$

for all nodes $i \in N(\Delta)$.
