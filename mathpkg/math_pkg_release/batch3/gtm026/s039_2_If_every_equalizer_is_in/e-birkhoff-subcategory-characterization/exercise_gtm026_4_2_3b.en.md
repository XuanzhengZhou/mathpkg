section: "2. If every equalizer is in M"
exercise_number: "3(b)"

In contradistinction to exercise 2(a) of section 3, show that if there is at least one morphism between every pair of $\mathcal{K}$-objects and if $\mathcal{K}$ has no cogenerator (e.g. the category of groups, 2.1.64) then no small set of objects generates $\mathcal{K}$ as a quasivariety.
