Theorem 4.25 provides an operator-theoretic description of the $\mathsf{E}$-Birkhoff subcategory generated by a class $\mathcal{A}$ of $\mathbf{T}$-algebras. In a regular category where every equalizer belongs to $\mathsf{M}$ and the theory functor $\mathbf{T}$ preserves $\mathsf{E}$, the closure under products $\mathsf{P}$, $\mathsf{M}^{\mathbf{T}}$-subobjects, and $\mathsf{U}^{\mathbf{T}}$-split epimorphisms $\mathsf{S}^{\mathbf{T}}$ yields exactly the $\mathsf{E}$-Birkhoff hull of $\mathcal{A}$. This generalizes the classical HSP theorem for varieties to the setting of regular factorization systems.
