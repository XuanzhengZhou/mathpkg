Extending slightly the definition of equalizer, a collective equalizer of a set $C$ of morphisms from $A_1$ to $A_2$ is a map $i: E \longrightarrow A_1$ universal with the property that $ix = iy$ whenever $x, y \in C$; that is $ix = iy$ for all $x, y \in C$ and whenever $i'x = i'y$ for all $x, y \in C$ then there exists unique $h$ with $h.i = i'$.
