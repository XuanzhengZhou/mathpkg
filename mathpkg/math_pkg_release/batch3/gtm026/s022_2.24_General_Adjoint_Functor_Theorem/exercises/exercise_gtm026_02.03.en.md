Show that partially ordered sets is a full reflective subcategory of the category of reflexive and transitively ordered sets and order-preserving maps. [Hint: divide out by antisymmetry.] Let $X$ be a set and let $Y$ be the collection of all families of subsets of $X$ with reflexive and transitive ordering $A \leqslant B$ if the topology generated by $A$ (i.e., with $A$ as subbase) is contained in the topology generated by $B$; show that the reflection of $(Y, \leqslant)$ may be identified with the set of topologies on $X$.
