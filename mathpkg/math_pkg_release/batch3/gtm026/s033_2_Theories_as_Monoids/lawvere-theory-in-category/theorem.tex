\text{A} \emph{Lawvere theory} \text{in} $\mathcal{K}$ \text{is a category} $\mathcal{T}$ \text{with the same objects as} $\mathcal{K}$ \text{together with a functor}
$$
R: \mathcal{K}^{\text{op}} \longrightarrow \mathcal{T}
$$
\text{such that} $R$ \text{is the identity function on objects and such that} $R$ \text{has a left adjoint}.
