In the category of sets, the abstract notion of monosubobject coincides with the concrete notion of subset. Mapping an isomorphism class of monomorphisms $[i]$ to the image set of $i$ gives a bijection onto the power set of $X$. This confirms that the categorical definition of monosubobject correctly generalizes the set-theoretic concept of subset.
