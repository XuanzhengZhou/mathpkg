If $\mathcal{K}$ has pullbacks, every morphism $f: X \rightarrow Y$ induces the "inverse image map" $[i]f^{-1} = [j]$ where $j$ is a pullback of $i$ along $f$. The pullback of a monomorphism is again a monomorphism, so $j$ is indeed mono. The construction is independent of the choice of pullback, and $[j] = [j']$ if $[i] = [i']$.
