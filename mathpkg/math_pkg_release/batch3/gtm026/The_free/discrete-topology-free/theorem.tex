Let $U$ be the forgetful functor from the category of topological spaces and continuous maps to the category of sets and functions. For each set $n$, let $F_n$ be the set $n$ provided with its discrete topology. Let $\eta: n \longrightarrow F_nU$ be the identity function of $n$. Then $(F_n, \eta)$ is free over $n$ with respect to $U$.
