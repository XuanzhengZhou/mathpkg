Let $M$ be a compactible monoid. Show that all minimal right ideals of $M$ are closed (in any compact Hausdorff topology rendering the left multiplications continuous) and that if $I, J$ are two minimal right ideals of $M$ then there exists $p$ in $J$ such that $L_p$ restricts to an $M$-set isomorphism of $I$ onto $J$.
