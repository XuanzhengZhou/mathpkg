We first prove necessity. Let $E = E(1T, 1\mu)$ and let $I$ be a minimal subalgebra of $E$. Since $I$ satisfies the first two properties of 1.10, $I$ is the universal minimal T-algebra. By 1.9, $I$ is a minimal right ideal. For all $p$ in $M$, $L_p:E \longrightarrow E$ is a T-endomorphism and hence its restriction $L_p:I \longrightarrow I$ is an isomorphism. In particular, $L_p:I \longrightarrow I$ is injective for $p$ in $I$, and $I$ is a coalescent ideal of $E$.
