Verify that the axioms of a monoidal category hold for the motivating example $(\mathcal{K}, \times, 1, a, b, c)$, where $\mathcal{K}$ is a category with finite products, $\times$ is the binary product functor, $1$ is a terminal object, $a$ is the associativity isomorphism for products, and $b, c$ are the projection natural equivalences.
