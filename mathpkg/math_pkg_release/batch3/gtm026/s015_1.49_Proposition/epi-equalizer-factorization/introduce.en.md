An epi-equalizer factorization is the categorical dual of the coequalizer-mono factorization. It expresses a morphism $f$ as $f = p \cdot i$ where $p$ is an epimorphism and $i$ is an equalizer. While the coequalizer-mono factorization models the "image factorization" (surjection followed by injection), the epi-equalizer factorization models the "coimage factorization" (epimorphism followed by equalizer). In many categories, one or both of these factorization systems may exist.
