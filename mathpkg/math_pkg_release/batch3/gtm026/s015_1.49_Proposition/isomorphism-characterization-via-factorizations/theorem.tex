Let $\mathcal{K}$ be a category and let $f: A \longrightarrow B$ be a morphism in $\mathcal{K}$. The following three conditions on $f$ are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $f$ is an isomorphism;
\item[(2)] $f$ is a coequalizer and $f$ is a monomorphism;
\item[(3)] $f$ is an equalizer and $f$ is an epimorphism.
\end{enumerate}
