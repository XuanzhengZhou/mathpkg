The Uncoupling Lemma (1.11) guarantees that every $p \in A\Omega$ of word length greater than $1$ admits a unique decomposition $p = p_1 \cdots p_n \omega$ with $n > 0$, $\omega \in \Omega_n$, and $p_i \in A\Omega$. For $a \in A$, the word length is $1$ and the base clause (1.15) applies. For words of length greater than $1$, the recursive clause (1.16) applies using the unique decomposition. By induction on word length, $\psi$ is uniquely determined on all of $A\Omega$.
