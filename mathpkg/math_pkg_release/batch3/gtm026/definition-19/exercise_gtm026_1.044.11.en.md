Let $i:\mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathcal{B}$ be a subcategory for which there exists $U:\mathcal{B} \longrightarrow \mathcal{A}$ with $i.U = \text{id}_{\mathcal{A}}$. Let $\Delta$ be a diagram scheme. Prove that if $\mathcal{B}$ has weak colimits of type $\Delta$ then so does $\mathcal{A}$.
