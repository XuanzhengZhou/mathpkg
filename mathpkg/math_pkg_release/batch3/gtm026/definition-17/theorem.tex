1.13 Characterization Theorem for Algebraic Structure on Sets. Let $(\mathcal{A}, U) \in \text{Struct}(\text{Set})$ be a category of sets with structure. Then $U: \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \text{Set}$ is algebraic if and only if the following three conditions are satisfied:

1. $U$ has a left adjoint;
2. $\mathcal{A}$ has separators (1.11); and
3. $U$ creates quotients of congruences (1.12).
