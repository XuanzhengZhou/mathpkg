If $f_i: (L, s) \longrightarrow (L_i, t_i)$ is optimal in $\mathcal{C}$ with $L$ and all $L_i$ in $\mathcal{L}$, then this family is a fortiori optimal in $\mathcal{P}$. This is all we need check by 3.31.
