Let $\mathcal{C}$ be in $\text{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ and let $\mathcal{L}$ be a full replete subcategory of $\mathcal{K}$. Then if $\mathcal{C}$ is fibre complete over $\mathcal{K}$, its pullback $\mathcal{P}$ along $\mathcal{L}$ is again fibre complete over $\mathcal{L}$.

$$\mathcal{P} = \mathcal{C} \times_{\mathcal{K}} \mathcal{L}$$
