$H: \mathcal{C} \longrightarrow \mathcal{D}$ in $\text{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ is \textbf{taut} if $H$ preserves optimal families, that is, $f_i: (K, s)H \longrightarrow (L_i, t_i)H$ is optimal in $\mathcal{D}$ whenever $f_i: (K, s) \longrightarrow (L_i, t_i)$ is optimal in $\mathcal{C}$.

Dually, $H$ is \textbf{cotaut} if $H$ preserves co-optimal families.
