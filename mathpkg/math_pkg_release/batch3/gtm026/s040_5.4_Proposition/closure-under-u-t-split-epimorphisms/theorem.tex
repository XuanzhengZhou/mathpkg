Let $\mathcal{C}$ be a full replete subcategory of $\mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$ as in Theorem 5.6. Let $(X, s, \xi) \in \mathcal{C}$ and let $f: (X, s, \xi) \longrightarrow (Y, t, \theta) \in \mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$ and $d: (Y, t) \longrightarrow (X, s) \in \mathcal{A}$ satisfy $d \circ f = \mathrm{id}_Y$. Then for any semantic operation $\alpha: (U^{\bar{T}})^n \longrightarrow U^{\bar{T}}$, the map $(Y, \theta)\alpha = d^n \circ (X, \xi)\alpha \circ f: (Y^n, t^n) \longrightarrow (Y, t)$ is admissible. Hence $\mathcal{C}$ is closed under $U^{\bar{T}}$-split epimorphisms.
