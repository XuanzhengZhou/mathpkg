Lemma 5.9 is the second auxiliary result in the proof of Theorem 5.6. It shows that the subcategory $\mathcal{C}$ is closed under $U^{\bar{T}}$-split epimorphisms: if $(X, s, \xi)$ is in $\mathcal{C}$ and $f$ is a $T$-homomorphism with a section $d$ in $\mathcal{A}$, then the target $(Y, t, \theta)$ also lies in $\mathcal{C}$. The proof uses the fact that $(Y, \theta)\alpha$ factors as $d^n \circ (X, \xi)\alpha \circ f$, which is admissible as a composite of admissible maps.
