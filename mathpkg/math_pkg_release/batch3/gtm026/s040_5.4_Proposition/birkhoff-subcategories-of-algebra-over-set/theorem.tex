Let $T$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathbf{Set}$, let $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ be a concrete category over $\mathbf{Set}$ such that $(\mathcal{A}, E, M)$ is a regular category with $E =$ admissible surjections and $M =$ optimal injections. Let $\bar{T}$ be the canonical lift of $T$ to $\mathcal{A}$. If $\mathcal{C}$ is a full replete subcategory of $\mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$ closed under products, optimal injective $T$-homomorphisms into, and $T$-homomorphisms which are split epimorphisms in $\mathcal{A}$ out of, then $\mathcal{C}$ is an $E$-Birkhoff subcategory of $\mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$ and is algebraic over $\mathcal{A}$.
