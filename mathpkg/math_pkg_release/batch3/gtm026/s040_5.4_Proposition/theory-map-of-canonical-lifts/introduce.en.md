Proposition 5.4 establishes that a theory map $\lambda: T_1 \to T_2$ between algebraic theories in a base category $\mathcal{K}$ lifts functorially to a theory map between their canonical lifts $\bar{T}_1$ and $\bar{T}_2$ in $\mathcal{A}$. The proof reduces to showing that the induced map $K\lambda$ is admissible in $\mathcal{A}$ for every object $(K, s)$ in $\mathcal{A}$. This result ensures that the canonical lift construction is functorial with respect to theory maps.
