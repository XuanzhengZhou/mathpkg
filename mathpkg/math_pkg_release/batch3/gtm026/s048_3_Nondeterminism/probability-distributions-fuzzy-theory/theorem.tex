A subtheory of the matrices theory (1.3.6) in $\mathbf{Set}$ given by:

$$QT = \{ (\lambda_b : b \in B) \mid \lambda_b \geqslant 0,\; \sum_{b \in B} \lambda_b = 1 \}$$

That is, $QT$ is the set of probability distributions on $Q$. Fuzzy morphisms are row-stochastic matrices.

\text{Every convex subset of a real vector space is an algebra over this theory.}
