This proposition gives two fundamental characterizations of $T$-subalgebras in terms of operations. Part (1) states that the subalgebra generated by a subset $A$ equals the image under the inclusion map of the set of all $A$-tuple-valued $T$-operations. Part (2) gives a criterion: $A$ is itself a $T$-subalgebra precisely when the result of applying any operation to elements of $A$ remains in $A$ — the familiar closure-under-operations characterization.
