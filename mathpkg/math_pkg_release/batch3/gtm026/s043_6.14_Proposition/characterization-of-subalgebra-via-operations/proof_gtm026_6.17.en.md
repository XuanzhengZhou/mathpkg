(1) Using 1.4.31, the subalgebra generated by $A$ is the image of the inclusion map composed with the algebraic structure, which yields $\langle A \rangle = i \mathcal{O}_A(X, \xi)$.

(2) If $A$ is a subalgebra by virtue of $\xi_0 \colon AT \to A$, the desired factorization is $\psi = (A, \xi_0)\tilde{\omega}$. Conversely, suppose $\psi$ exists. Evaluating at $\operatorname{id}_A$,
\[
\langle i, (X, \xi)\tilde{\omega} \rangle = \langle \operatorname{id}_A, \psi \rangle \in A
\]
so that, by (1), $\langle A \rangle = i \mathcal{O}_A(X, \xi) \subseteq A$. Thus $A$ is a subalgebra.
