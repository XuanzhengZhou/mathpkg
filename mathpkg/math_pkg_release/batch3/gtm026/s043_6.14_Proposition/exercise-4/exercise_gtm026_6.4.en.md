Let $R$ be a ring. Show that $R$-modules forms a commutative theory if and only if $R$ is a commutative ring. Let $R[x]$ denote the ring of one-variable polynomials over $R$. Show that $R[x]$-modules are the algebras over the theory of $R$-modules tensored with the theory corresponding to a single unary operation.
