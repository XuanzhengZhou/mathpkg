A representative subcategory $\mathcal{A}$ of $\mathcal{B}$ is a full subcategory that contains at least one object from every isomorphism class of $\mathcal{B}$. Every object of $\mathcal{B}$ is isomorphic to some object in the image of the inclusion. Together with fullness and repleteness, the representative property characterizes when an inclusion functor is actually an isomorphism of categories.
