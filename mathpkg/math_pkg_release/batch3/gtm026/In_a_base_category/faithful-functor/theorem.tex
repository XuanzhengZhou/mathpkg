Let $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ be categories and let $U: \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{B}$ be a functor. Then $U$ is \textbf{faithful} if for each pair $(A, A')$ of objects in $\mathcal{A}$, the assignment

$$f: A \to A' \quad \longmapsto \quad fU: AU \to A'U$$

is injective. Equivalently, for all morphisms $f, g: A \to A'$ in $\mathcal{A}$, if $fU = gU$, then $f = g$.
