Let $\mathcal{K}$ be a fixed base category. The full subcategory $\text{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ of $\text{Con}(\mathcal{K})$ is defined to consist of all concrete categories $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ over $\mathcal{K}$ such that $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ is a category of $\mathcal{K}$-objects with structure (i.e., $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ is isomorphic in $\text{Con}(\mathcal{K})$ to a literal category of $\mathcal{K}$-objects with structure).

By definition, $\text{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ is a full replete subcategory of $\text{Con}(\mathcal{K})$.
