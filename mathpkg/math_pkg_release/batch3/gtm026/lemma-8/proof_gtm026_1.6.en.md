To fully complete the discussion preceding the lemma, we note that the S-T subalgebra of $(X, \xi, \theta)$ generated by the subset $A_0$ of X is $\langle \langle A_0 \rangle_{S} \rangle_T$ which has cardinal at most that of $A_0ST$.

Since the bialgebra condition of 6.1 involves operations, it is natural, before attempting to apply 6.13, that we detour to explore the relationships between the "subalgebra generated by" operator $\langle \rangle_T$ and T-operations. The reader should recall the notations and results of 1.5.5. We will write just $\langle \rangle$ instead of $\langle \rangle_T$ when only one algebraic theory is in the picture.
