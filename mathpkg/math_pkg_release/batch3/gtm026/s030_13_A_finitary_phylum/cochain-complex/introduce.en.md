A cochain complex is a sequence of objects and morphisms in an abelian category where the composition of any two consecutive morphisms is zero. This fundamental structure underlies all of homological algebra and cohomology theory. The condition $d^n d^{n+1} = 0$ ensures that every coboundary is a cocycle, making the cohomology groups (quotients of cocycles by coboundaries) well-defined.
