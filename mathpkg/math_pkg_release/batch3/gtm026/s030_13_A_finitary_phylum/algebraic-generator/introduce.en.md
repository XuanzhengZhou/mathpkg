An algebraic generator is a $T$-algebra whose representable functor is weakly algebraic. This means the functor $\operatorname{Hom}((X, \xi), -)$ from $T$-algebras to sets preserves and reflects certain limits and colimits in a way that makes it behave like an algebraic (monadic) functor. The concept is due to Lawvere and is important for understanding the structure of categories of algebras.
