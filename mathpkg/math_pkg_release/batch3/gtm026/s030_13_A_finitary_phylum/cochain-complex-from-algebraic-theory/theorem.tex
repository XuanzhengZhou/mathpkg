Let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathcal{K}$ and let $F: \mathcal{K}^{\text{op}} \rightarrow \mathcal{A}$ be an arbitrary functor into an abelian category $\mathcal{A}$. For each $\mathbf{T}$-algebra $(X, \xi)$, the diagram

$$0 \xrightarrow{d^0} (XT, X\mu)F \xrightarrow{d^1} \cdots \xrightarrow{d^n} (XT^{n+1}, XT^n \mu)F \xrightarrow{d^{n+1}} (XT^{n+2}, XT^{n+1} \mu)F \rightarrow \cdots$$

with coboundary maps given by the alternating sum formula

$$d^{n+1} = \xi T^{n+1} F + \sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^{k+1} (XT^k \mu T^{n-k}) F$$

defines a functor from $(\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}})^{\text{op}}$ to the category of cochain complexes in $\mathcal{A}$.
