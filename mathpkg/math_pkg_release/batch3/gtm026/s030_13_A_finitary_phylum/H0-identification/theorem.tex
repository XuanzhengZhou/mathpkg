Let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathcal{K}$ and let $(Y, \theta; +, i, 0)$ be an abelian group in $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$. For any $\mathbf{T}$-algebra $(X, \xi)$,

$$H^0(X, \xi) \cong \mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}((X, \xi), (Y, \theta))$$

as abelian groups, where the right-hand side is the set of $\mathbf{T}$-algebra homomorphisms from $(X, \xi)$ to $(Y, \theta)$ equipped with pointwise addition.
