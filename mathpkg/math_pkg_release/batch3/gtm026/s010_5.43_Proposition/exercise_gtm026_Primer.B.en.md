Prove Cantor's theorem: for any set $A$, $\text{card}(2^A) > \text{card}(A)$ (where $2^A$ means the set of all subsets of $A$). [Proof outline: no $f: A \longrightarrow 2^A$ can be surjective since (cf. Russell's Paradox) the set $\{a \in A : a \notin f(a)\}$ is not in the image of $f$.]
