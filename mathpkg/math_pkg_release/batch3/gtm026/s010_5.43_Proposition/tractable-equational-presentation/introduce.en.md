An equational presentation $(\Omega, E)$ is tractable precisely when its forgetful functor $U_E$ to $\mathbf{Set}$ is tractable at every cardinal. Tractability is a necessary condition for an equational presentation to correspond to an algebraic theory $\mathbf{Set}^T$, and remarkably it is also sufficient (see Theorem 5.45).
