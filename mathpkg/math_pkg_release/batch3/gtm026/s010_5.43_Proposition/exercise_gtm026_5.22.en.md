Let $T$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathbf{Set}$, let $\mathcal{A}$ be an arbitrary class of sets and let $\xi: XT \longrightarrow X$ be a function such that certain conditions hold. Show that the resulting structure yields a well-defined algebraic theory in the category $\mathcal{K}$ of compactly generated spaces, and that $T$-algebras have to do with "continuous selections."
