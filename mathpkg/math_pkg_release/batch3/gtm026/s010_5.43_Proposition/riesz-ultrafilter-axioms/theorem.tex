Riesz's five axioms for a collection of subsets $U$ of a set to be an ``ideale Verdichtungsstelle'' (ultrafilter):
\begin{enumerate}
\item Every superset of an element of $U$ is again in $U$;
\item If $A$ and $B$ are disjoint and $A \cup B \in U$, then $A \in U$ or $B \in U$;
\item The intersection of two elements of $U$ is again in $U$;
\item $U$ is maximal with respect to properties (1)--(3);
\item The intersection of all elements of $U$ is empty.
\end{enumerate}
