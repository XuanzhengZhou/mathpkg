Riesz's axioms provide an early characterization of ultrafilters as "ideal condensation points." Axioms (1)-(4) are equivalent to the modern definition of an ultrafilter (a maximal filter), while axiom (5) distinguishes nonprincipal ultrafilters (free ultrafilters). The text notes that for modern ultrafilter definitions, axiom (4) is actually implied by the other axioms (see Exercise 9).
