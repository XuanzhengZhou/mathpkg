There is a \textbf{universe} $U$ modelling the ``set of all sets.'' A \textbf{small set} is an element of $U$. A subset of $U$ which is not an element of $U$ is a \textbf{large set}. 

A cartesian product of a family of small sets indexed by a small set is a small set. The cartesian product of all small sets is a large set.
