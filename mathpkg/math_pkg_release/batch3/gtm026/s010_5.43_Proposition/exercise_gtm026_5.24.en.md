Let $\alpha$ be a regular cardinal, let $T$ have rank $\leqslant \alpha$, and let $(X, \xi)$ be a $T$-algebra possessing an inclusion-minimal set $A$ of generators with $\text{card}(A) = \alpha$. Show that every set generating $(X, \xi)$ has cardinal $\geqslant \alpha$ (and hence that any two minimal sets of generators have the same cardinal). [Hint: if $\langle B \rangle = X$ then for $b$ in $B$ choose $S_b \subset A$ with $\text{card}(S_b) < \alpha$ and $b \in \langle S_b \rangle$; as $\bigcup S_b = A$, $\text{card}(B) \geqslant \alpha$.]
