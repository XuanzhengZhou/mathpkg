A left-to-right picture of the first few ordinals is

$$0 \quad 1 \quad 2 \quad \cdots \quad n \quad \cdots \quad \omega \quad s(\omega) \quad s^2(\omega) \quad \cdots$$

Extend the picture to the right as far as you can. Then prove that the set of all countable ordinals is uncountable. [Hint: consider the least uncountable ordinal.]
