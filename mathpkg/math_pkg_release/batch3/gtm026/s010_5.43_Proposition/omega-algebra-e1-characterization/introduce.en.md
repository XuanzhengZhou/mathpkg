An $\Omega$-algebra $(X, \delta)$ satisfies the equation set $E_1$ precisely when the action on $XT$ is representable by a unique function $\xi$ evaluating formal composites. This proposition establishes the equivalence between structural satisfaction of $E_1$ and the existence of the evaluation map, which is central to the correspondence between equational presentations and algebraic theories.
