Let $A$ be a collection of subsets of $X$ and define $A^\bullet = \{U \in X\beta : \text{for all } U \in \mathcal{U} \text{ there exists } A \in A \text{ with } A \subset U\}$. Show that $\{A^\bullet : A \subset 2^X\}$ is the set of all closed subsets of the topological space $(X\beta, X\mu)$.
