Trivially true from the definition of $\Omega$-homomorphism (2.3): for any operation symbol $\omega \in \Omega_n$ and any $x_1, \ldots, x_n \in X$,

$$
\text{id}_X((x_1, \ldots, x_n)\delta_\omega) = (x_1, \ldots, x_n)\delta_\omega = (\text{id}_X(x_1), \ldots, \text{id}_X(x_n))\delta_\omega
$$

so $\text{id}_X$ commutes with all $\Omega$-operations.
