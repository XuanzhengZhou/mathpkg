Lemma 2.22 is a technical lemma used within the proof of Theorem 2.17, specifically for the direction (2.20) implies (2.18). It ensures that the $(\Omega', E')$-structure $\delta'$ defined on $X$ via equation (2.21) is well-defined, i.e., the value $(x_1, \ldots, x_n)\delta'_{\omega'}$ does not depend on the particular choice of the auxiliary function $r: V \to X$ satisfying $v_i r = x_i$. The proof uses the identity (2.23) together with algebraic manipulation in the clone $\text{Set}(\Omega, E)$.
