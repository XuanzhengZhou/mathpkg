\text{An algebraic theory (of sets) is any construction} $T = (T, \eta, \circ)$ \text{such that}
$\circ$ \text{is associative,} $\eta$ \text{is a two-sided unit for} $\circ$, \text{and}
$$
(A \xrightarrow{f} B \xrightarrow{B\eta} BT) \circ (B \xrightarrow{\beta} CT) = A \xrightarrow{f} B \xrightarrow{\beta} CT.
$$
