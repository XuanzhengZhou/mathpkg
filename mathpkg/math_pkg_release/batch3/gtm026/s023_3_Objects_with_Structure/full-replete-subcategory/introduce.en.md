A subcategory is replete when it is closed under isomorphism of objects: any object isomorphic to one already in the subcategory must belong to the subcategory as well. Combined with fullness, this gives the notion of a full replete subcategory. The text characterizes: $U$ is an isomorphism of categories if and only if $U$ is a full, representative, replete subcategory.
