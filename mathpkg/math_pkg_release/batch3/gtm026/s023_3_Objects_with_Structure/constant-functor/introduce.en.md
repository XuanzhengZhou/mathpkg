For any object $B$ of a category $\mathcal{B}$, the constant functor $U_B: \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{B}$ maps every object of $\mathcal{A}$ to $B$ and every morphism to $\operatorname{id}_B$. The text notes the observation that no constant functor from the category of groups to the category of sets is faithful.
