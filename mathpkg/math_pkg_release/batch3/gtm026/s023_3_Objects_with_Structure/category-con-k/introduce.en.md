$\operatorname{Con}(\mathcal{K})$ is the category whose objects are concrete categories over $\mathcal{K}$ and whose morphisms are homomorphisms of concrete categories — functors that commute with the forgetful functors to $\mathcal{K}$. The text notes that $\operatorname{Con}(\mathcal{K})$ may fail to be locally small even if $\mathcal{K}$ is. The full subcategory of $\operatorname{Con}(\mathcal{K})$ consisting of categories of sets with structure is denoted $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$.
