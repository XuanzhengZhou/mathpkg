A full subcategory of $\mathcal{B}$ is one in which all morphisms of $\mathcal{B}$ between objects of the subcategory are retained. The text defines this alongside related notions: a representative subcategory (every object of $\mathcal{B}$ is isomorphic to an object in the subcategory) and a full replete subcategory (closed under isomorphism).
