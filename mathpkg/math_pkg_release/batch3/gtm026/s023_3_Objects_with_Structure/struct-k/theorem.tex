The full subcategory of $\operatorname{Con}(\mathcal{K})$ consisting of all categories of sets with structure will be denoted as $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$. By definition, $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ is a full replete subcategory of $\operatorname{Con}(\mathcal{K})$. This reflects our confidence that the invariants in a given category of structured objects will be implicit in the forgetful functor, a principle which has been with us since 1.2.1.
