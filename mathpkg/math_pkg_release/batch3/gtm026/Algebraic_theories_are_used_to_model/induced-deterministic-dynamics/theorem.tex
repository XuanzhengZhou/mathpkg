Each multiple branching dynamics $\delta: Q \times X \longrightarrow QT$ induces the ordinary (deterministic) dynamics $\delta^\bullet: QT \times X \longrightarrow QT$ by

$$\delta^\bullet = QT \times X \xrightarrow{Q\lambda} (Q \times X)T \xrightarrow{\delta^\#} QT$$

where $\delta^\#$ is the unique union-preserving extension of $\delta$.
