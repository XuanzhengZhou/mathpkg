Let $M$ be a monoid and let $T_M$ be the corresponding theory in $\text{Set}$ of 1.3. For any theory $S$ in $\text{Set}$, $S \otimes T_M$ exists by 3.6.21.

(a) Show that $A\lambda: AS \times M \longrightarrow (A \times M)S$ as in 3.7 is a distributive law of $T_M$ over $S$ (as in exercise 6) and that $\text{Set}^{\lambda} = \text{Set}^{\Sigma \otimes T_M}$.

(b) Let $T = (2^{(-\times M)}, \eta, \circ)$ be the "relations with credibility" theory obtained by setting $S$ to be the "subsets" theory. Verify that

$$Q \xrightarrow{Q\eta} 2^{Q \times M}$$

$$q \mapsto \{(q, e)\}$$

and that, given $\alpha: A \longrightarrow 2^{B \times M}$ and $\beta: B \longrightarrow 2^{C \times M},$

$$(\alpha \circ \beta)_a = \{(c, mm') : \exists b, m, m' \text{ with } (b, m) \in \alpha_a, (c, m') \in \beta_b\}$$
