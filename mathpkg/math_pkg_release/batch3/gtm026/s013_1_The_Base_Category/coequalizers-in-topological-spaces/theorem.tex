The category $\mathbf{Top}$ of topological spaces and continuous functions has coequalizers. Given continuous $f, g: A \to B$, construct the coequalizer at the level of $\mathbf{Set}$ as in 1.30 (quotient $Q = B/R$ where $R$ is the intersection of all equivalence relations containing $\{(af, ag) : a \in A\}$). Assign $Q$ the quotient topology (the largest topology making the canonical projection $q: B \to Q$ continuous). Then $q = \text{coeq}(f, g)$ in $\mathbf{Top}$.
