Let $(\Omega, E)$ be a finitary equational presentation and let $\mathbf{T}$ be its algebraic theory as constructed in 4.1. Then the category $\mathbf{Set}^{\mathbf{T}}$ of $\mathbf{T}$-algebras and the category of $(\Omega, E)$-algebras are isomorphic as categories of sets with structure. That is, for each set $X$, there is a natural bijection between $\mathbf{T}$-algebra structures $\xi: XT \longrightarrow X$ on $X$ and $(\Omega, E)$-algebra structures $\delta$ on $X$, and this bijection respects homomorphisms.
