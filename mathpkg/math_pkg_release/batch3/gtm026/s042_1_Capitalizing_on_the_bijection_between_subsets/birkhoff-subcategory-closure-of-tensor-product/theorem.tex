Let $S$ and $T$ be algebraic theories in $\mathbf{Set}$. Then $\mathbf{Set}^S \otimes T$, which is a full replete subcategory of $\mathbf{Set}^S \times \mathbf{Set}^T$, is a Birkhoff subcategory in the sense that $\mathbf{Set}^S \otimes T$ is closed under products, subalgebras, and quotients. In particular,
$$
U^S \otimes T: \mathbf{Set}^S \otimes T \to \mathbf{Set}
$$
is a Beck functor.
