The duality principle for image factorization systems states that passing to the opposite category interchanges the roles of the epi-class $E$ and the mono-class $M$. If $(E, M)$ forms an image factorization system in a category $\mathcal{H}$, then $(M, E)$ forms an image factorization system in the opposite category $\mathcal{H}^{\mathrm{op}}$. This symmetry is immediate from the axioms, since reversing all arrows exchanges epimorphisms with monomorphisms while preserving the factorization structure.
