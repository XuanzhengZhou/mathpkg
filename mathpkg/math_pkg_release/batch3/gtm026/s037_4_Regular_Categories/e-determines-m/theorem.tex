Let $\mathcal{K}$ be a category with an image factorization system $(E, M)$. Let $t: C \longrightarrow D$ have the property that for every commutative square of the form
$$
\begin{array}{ccc}
A & \xrightarrow{e} & B \\
{\scriptstyle f}\downarrow & & \downarrow{\scriptstyle g} \\
C & \xrightarrow{t} & D
\end{array}
$$
with $e \in E$, there exists $h: B \longrightarrow C$ with $e \circ h = f$. Then $t \in M$.
