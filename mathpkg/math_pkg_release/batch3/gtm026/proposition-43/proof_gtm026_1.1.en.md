For any $T$-algebra $(X, \xi)$ and $p$ in $E = E(X, \xi)$, $L_p: E \rightarrow E$ is a $T$-homomorphism. Now use the corresponding $V: \mathbf{Set}^T \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}^{\beta}$.
