Let $\operatorname{Cat}/\mathcal{K}$ be the category whose objects are pairs $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ with $U: \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{K}$ an arbitrary functor and whose morphisms are functors over $\mathcal{K}$ just as in 3.3. Show that every such $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ has a reflection in $\operatorname{Con}(\mathcal{K})$, and hence in $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$. [Hint: keep the same objects and divide out by the obvious equivalence relation on morphisms.]
