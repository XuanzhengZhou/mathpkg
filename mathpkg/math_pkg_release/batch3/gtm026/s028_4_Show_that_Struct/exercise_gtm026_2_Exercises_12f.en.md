Let $\mathcal{C}$ in $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathbf{Set})$ be the subcategory of $\mathbf{Set}$ having all sets as objects but with $f: X \to Y$ admissible if and only if $X$ and $Y$ have the same cardinality. Show that $\mathcal{C}$ cannot be represented as a full replete subcategory of any fibre-complete category which is small over $\mathcal{K}$.
