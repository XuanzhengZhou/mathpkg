Let $U: \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathcal{K}$ be a functor which has a left adjoint. Then the following three conditions on $U$ are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $U$ is algebraic;
\item $U$ creates coequalizers of $U$-absolute pairs;
\item $U$ creates coequalizers of $U$-contractible pairs.
\end{enumerate}
