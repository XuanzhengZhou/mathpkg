Let $f, g: A \to B$ and $h: B \to C$ be given in a category $\mathcal{K}$, with $h = \operatorname{coeq}(f, g)$. Then the following two conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[1.] There exist $d: B \to A$ and $d': C \to B$ such that $(f, g, h; d, d')$ is a contractible coequalizer.
\item[2.] There exists $d: B \to A$ such that
$$d \cdot f = \mathrm{id}_B \quad \text{and} \quad f \cdot d \cdot g = g \cdot d \cdot g.$$
\end{enumerate}
