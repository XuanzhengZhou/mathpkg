Let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathcal{K}$ and suppose given a commutative square

$$\xymatrix{
LT \ar[r]^{hT} \ar[d]_{\gamma} & KT \ar[d]^{\xi} \\
L \ar[r]_{h} & K
}$$

with $(L, \gamma)$ a $\mathbf{T}$-algebra and with $h$, $hT$, and $hTT$ epi in $\mathcal{K}$. Then $(K, \xi)$ is a $\mathbf{T}$-algebra and $h: (L, \gamma) \to (K, \xi)$ is co-optimal in $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$.
