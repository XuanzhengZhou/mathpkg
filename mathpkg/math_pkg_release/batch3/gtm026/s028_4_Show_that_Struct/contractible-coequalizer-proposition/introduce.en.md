This proposition establishes that every contractible coequalizer diagram is automatically an absolute coequalizer. The proof is straightforward: applying any functor $H$ preserves the contraction data, and the split epimorphism property of $h$ guarantees uniqueness of the induced map, making $hH$ a coequalizer of $(fH, gH)$ for every functor $H$.
