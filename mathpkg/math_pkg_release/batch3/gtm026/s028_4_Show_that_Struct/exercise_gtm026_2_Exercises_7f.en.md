Let $XT$ be the set of filters on $X$ with functorial action $\langle \mathcal{F}, fT \rangle = \{B \subset Y: Bf^{-1} \in \mathcal{F}\}$ (cf. exercise 4 of section 1.5). A convergence structure [Fischer '74] is a $T$-model $(X, \xi)$ subject to the three axioms: (i) $(\operatorname{prin}(x), x) \in \xi$; (ii) if $(\mathcal{F}, x) \in \xi$ and $\mathcal{G} \supset \mathcal{F}$ then $(\mathcal{G}, x) \in \xi$; (iii) if $(\mathcal{F}, x) \in \xi$ and $(\mathcal{G}, x) \in \xi$ then $(\mathcal{F} \cap \mathcal{G}, x) \in \xi$. Show that the category of convergence structures is fibre complete with taut reflective embedding in $T\text{-mod}$; show that this embedding is not cotaut.
