Let $\mathcal{C}$ in $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{H})$ be fibre complete and let $S$ be any object of $\mathcal{C}$. Let $\mathcal{P}$ be the full replete subcategory of all $C$ for which the family of all admissible maps $C \to S$ is optimal. Prove that $\mathcal{P}$ is fibre complete with taut embedding in $\mathcal{C}$ and reflective in such a way that the reflector is again over $\mathcal{H}$. Observe that $S$ is a Sierpinski object in $\mathcal{P}$.
