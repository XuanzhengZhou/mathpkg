In this exercise we develop a "fibre-completion" for arbitrary $\mathcal{C}$ in $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$. For $K$ in $\mathcal{K}$ consider all $(L, t, a)$ with $t$ in $\mathcal{C}(L)$, $a: L \to K$ in $\mathcal{K}$. Define the fibre-completion $H: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ by specifying objects and admissible maps appropriately.
