1.19 Distal Transformation Groups. A compact transformation group $X$ with phase group $G$ is distal if whenever $x \neq y$ in $X$ there exists a neighborhood $\alpha$ of the diagonal in $X \times X$ such that $(xg, yg) \notin \alpha$ for all $g$ in $G$.
