4.19 Hausdorff Spaces. All three image factorization systems on $\mathcal{K} = \text{Hausdorff}$ spaces as in 4.8 render $\mathcal{K}$ a regular category. The following hint is useful to prove $E$ co-well-powered for $E = \text{maps with dense image}: if X$ is Hausdorff and $A$ is a dense subset of $X$ then each element of $X$ is the limit of an ultrafilter on $A$ and such ultrafilters converge uniquely; therefore the cardinality of $X$ is at most the cardinality of the set of all ultrafilters on $A$.
