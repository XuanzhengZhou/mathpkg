The argument that fibre-completeness implies $U$ constructs limits follows from the definition: given any diagram in $\mathcal{C}$, one forms the limit in $\mathcal{K}$, and fibre-completeness provides the optimal lift of the limiting cone, which yields a limit in $\mathcal{C}$.
