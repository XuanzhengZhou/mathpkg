Let $(\Delta, D)$ be a diagram in $\mathcal{K}$. A \textit{quasi-colimit} of $D$ is an $N(\Delta)$-indexed family $(\tau_i: D_i \longrightarrow C)$ of $\mathcal{K}$-morphisms with the property that for each upper bound $(A, \Gamma)$ of $D$ there exists a (not necessarily unique) morphism $t: C \longrightarrow A$ in $\mathcal{K}$ such that $\tau_i t = \Gamma_i$ for all $i \in N(\Delta)$.
