Let $\mathcal{K}$ have small colimits and let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathcal{K}$ such that $T$ preserves coequalizers of reflexive pairs. Prove that $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$ has small colimits.
