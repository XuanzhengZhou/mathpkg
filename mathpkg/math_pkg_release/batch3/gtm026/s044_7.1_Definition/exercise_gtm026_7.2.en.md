Prove that if $U^{\mathbf{T}}: \mathbf{Set}^{\mathbf{T}} \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}$ preserves coproducts, then $\mathbf{T}$ is the algebraic theory corresponding to the monoid $1T$.

*Hint:* Since $T$ preserves coproducts and every set is the coproduct of its elements, $T$ is naturally equivalent to $1T \times -$.
