Let $(\mathcal{K}, E, M)$ be a category with a proper factorization system and let $U: \mathcal{C} \longrightarrow \mathcal{K}$ be a functor with a left adjoint such that $\mathcal{C}$ has small limits. Assume that $\mathcal{K}$ is $E$-co-well-powered and that $\mathcal{K}$ has quasi-colimits. Then $\mathcal{C}$ has small colimits.
