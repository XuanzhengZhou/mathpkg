Let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in a category $\mathcal{K}$ and let $\Delta$ be a diagram scheme. Prove that if $\mathbf{T}$ preserves colimits of type $\Delta$ then $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$ has colimits of type $\Delta$.
