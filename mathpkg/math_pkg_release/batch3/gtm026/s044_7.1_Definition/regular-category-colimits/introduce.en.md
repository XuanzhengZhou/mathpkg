This corollary states that any regular category $\mathcal{K}$ possessing small weak coproducts automatically has all small colimits. The result follows by applying Corollary 7.9 to the identity algebraic theory on $\mathcal{K}$, or equivalently by observing that a regular category with weak coproducts satisfies the conditions for colimit existence. This provides a clean sufficient condition for cocompleteness in the context of regular categories equipped with a factorization system.
