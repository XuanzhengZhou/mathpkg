This corollary specializes Corollary 7.9 to the category of sets: for any algebraic theory $\mathbf{T}$ in $\mathbf{Set}$, the category $\mathbf{Set}^{\mathbf{T}}$ of $\mathbf{T}$-algebras has all small colimits. Since $\mathbf{Set}$ is a regular category with small weak coproducts (indeed, all small coproducts) and every functor on $\mathbf{Set}$ preserves epimorphisms (the $E$ in the regular factorization), the hypotheses of Corollary 7.9 are automatically satisfied. This result covers groups, rings, modules, and all other varieties of universal algebra.
