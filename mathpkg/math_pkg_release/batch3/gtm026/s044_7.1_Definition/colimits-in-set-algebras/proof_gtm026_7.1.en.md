*Proof.* This follows immediately from Corollary 7.9. $\mathbf{Set}$ is a regular category (with the epi-mono factorization system), and every functor on $\mathbf{Set}$ preserves epimorphisms, so in particular the underlying endofunctor $T$ of the algebraic theory preserves $E$. $\mathbf{Set}$ has all small coproducts, hence certainly small weak coproducts. Thus all hypotheses of Corollary 7.9 are satisfied, and $\mathbf{Set}^{\mathbf{T}}$ has small colimits. $\square$
