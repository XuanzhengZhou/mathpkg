If $A$ is finite then $AT'$ is finite. (Hint: show that $AT'$ has a universal property in $\mathrm{Var}(\mathcal{X})$ and proceed as in 2.7.) This generalizes [Birkhoff '35, Theorem 11]. $\mathcal{X}$ must be a "small set" in order that $P$ be definable; see the "primer on set theory" at the end of this chapter.
