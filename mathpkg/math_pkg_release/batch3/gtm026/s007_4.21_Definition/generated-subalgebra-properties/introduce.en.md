This theorem collects the fundamental properties of the generated $T$-subalgebra construction. It establishes that $\langle \cdot \rangle$ is a closure operator on the subsets of a $T$-algebra: it is extensive ($A \subset \langle A \rangle$), monotone ($A \subset B \implies \langle A \rangle \subset \langle B \rangle$), and idempotent ($\langle \langle A \rangle \rangle = \langle A \rangle$). Moreover, the fixed points of this closure operator are precisely the $T$-subalgebras, and $\langle A \rangle$ is the smallest $T$-subalgebra containing $A$. These properties are used in the next section for further results about $T$-subalgebras.
