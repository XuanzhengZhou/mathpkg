Let $(X_i, \xi_i)$ be a family of $T$-algebras. Then the product set $X = \prod X_i$ admits a unique $T$-algebra structure $\xi: XT \longrightarrow X$ such that for all $i \in I$ the projection $\mathrm{pr}_i: (X, \xi) \longrightarrow (X_i, \xi_i)$ is a $T$-homomorphism, and this structure coincides with the cartesian product algebra structure.
