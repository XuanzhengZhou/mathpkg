Let $T$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathbf{Set}$, let $X$ be a set, and let $\omega \in XT$ be a syntactic operation in $X$ with corresponding semantic operation $\tilde{\omega}: U^X \longrightarrow U$ as in Theorem 5.5. Then $\omega$ and $\tilde{\omega}$ have the same arity:

$$\mathrm{ar}(\omega) = \mathrm{ar}(\tilde{\omega})$$
