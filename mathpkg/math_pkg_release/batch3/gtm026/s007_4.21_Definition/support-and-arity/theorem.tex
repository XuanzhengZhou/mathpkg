Let $\alpha: U^X \longrightarrow U$ be a semantic operation. A subset $S \subset X$ is a support of $\alpha$ if $S$ is a support of $(Y, \theta)\alpha: Y^X \longrightarrow Y$ for every $T$-algebra $(Y, \theta)$, meaning that $\alpha$ depends only on the coordinates in $S$: there exists a factorization

$$\xymatrix{
Y^X \ar[rr]^{(Y,\theta)\alpha} \ar[rd]_{\text{restriction to }S} & & Y \\
& Y^S \ar[ru] &
}$$

The arity of $\alpha$ is defined by:
$$\mathrm{ar}(\alpha) = \min\{ n : n \text{ is a cardinal and there exists a support of } \alpha \text{ of cardinal } n \}$$
