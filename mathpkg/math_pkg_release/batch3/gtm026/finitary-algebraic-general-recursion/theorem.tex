Let $\Omega$ be an operator domain and let $A$ be a set. To define a function $\psi$ on $A\Omega$ (the set of all $\Omega$-terms in $A$) it suffices to specify:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1.15)] $a\psi$ for all $a \in A$.
\item[(1.16)] $(p_1 \cdots p_n \omega)\psi$ in terms of $p_i\psi$ and $\omega$.
\end{enumerate}
