Let $g: A\Omega \longrightarrow A\Omega$ be a bijective $\Omega$-homomorphism. Prove that $g^{-1}$ is also an $\Omega$-homomorphism. Prove that $g$ maps $A$ bijectively onto $A$ and that $g = f\Omega$ for $f: A \longrightarrow A$, $a f = a g$. [Hint: use 1.11.]
