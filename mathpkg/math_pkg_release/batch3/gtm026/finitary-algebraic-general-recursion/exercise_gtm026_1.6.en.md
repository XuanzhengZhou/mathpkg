Let $S$ be the set of all $\Omega$-homomorphisms from $(X, \delta)$ to $(X', \delta')$. Then $S$ is a subset of the topological space $(X')^X$, the Tychonoff cartesian power of copies of $X'$ with the discrete topology. Prove that $S$ is closed.
