Given $p \in A\Omega$, the set $\text{var}(p)$ of variables occurring in $p$ is defined in the obvious way by algebraic recursion:

$$\text{var}(a) = \{a\}$$
$$\text{var}(p_1 \cdots p_n\omega) = \text{var}(p_1) \cup \cdots \cup \text{var}(p_n)$$

Given an $(\Omega, E)$-algebra $(X, \delta)$, prove that if $r, s: A \longrightarrow X$ agree on $\text{var}(p)$ then $[p]r^\# = [p]s^\#$. Verify that this description of $\text{var}(p)$ agrees with the one given in 2.10$^{-}$.
