Assume $X$ is an output process, so that $U: \operatorname{Dyn}(X) \to \mathcal{K}$ has a right adjoint. The \textbf{cofree dynamics} over an object $A \in \mathcal{K}$ with respect to $U$ is denoted
$$(AX_{\sharp}, AL; A\Lambda).$$
Here $AX_{\sharp} \in \mathcal{K}$ is the object, $AL: AX_{\sharp} X \to AX_{\sharp}$ is the dynamics structure map, and $A\Lambda: AX_{\sharp} \to A$ is the counit of the adjunction.
