An $X$-dynamorphism is simply the name given to a morphism in the category $\operatorname{Dyn}(X)$ of $X$-dynamics. It is an $\mathcal{H}$-morphism between the underlying objects that commutes with the dynamics transition maps $\delta$ and $\delta'$. This notion captures the natural concept of a homomorphism between state transition systems.
