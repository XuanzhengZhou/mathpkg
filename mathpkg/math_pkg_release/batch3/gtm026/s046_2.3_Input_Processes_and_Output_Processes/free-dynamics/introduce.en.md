When $X$ is an input process (the forgetful functor $U$ has a left adjoint), the free $X$-dynamics over any object $A$ is denoted $(AX^{\sharp}, A\mu_0; A\eta)$. This triple consists of the object $AX^{\sharp}$ (called the object of inputs over $A$), a dynamics morphism $A\mu_0: AX^{\sharp} X \to AX^{\sharp}$ encoding the transition structure, and the unit $A\eta: A \to AX^{\sharp}$ of the adjunction. In the classical Set case $X = - \times X_0$, we have $AX^{\sharp} = A \times X_0^*$, the Cartesian product of $A$ with the free monoid on $X_0$.
