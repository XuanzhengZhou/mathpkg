Assume $X$ is an input process, so that $U: \operatorname{Dyn}(X) \to \mathcal{K}$ has a left adjoint. The \textbf{free dynamics} over an object $A \in \mathcal{K}$ with respect to $U$ is denoted
$$(AX^{\sharp}, A\mu_0; A\eta).$$
Here $AX^{\sharp} \in \mathcal{K}$ is the object of inputs over $A$, $A\mu_0: AX^{\sharp} X \to AX^{\sharp}$ is the dynamics structure map, and $A\eta: A \to AX^{\sharp}$ is the unit of the adjunction.
