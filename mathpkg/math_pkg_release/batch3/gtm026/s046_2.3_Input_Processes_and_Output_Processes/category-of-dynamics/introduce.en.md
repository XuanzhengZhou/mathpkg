For a process $X$ (an endofunctor), the category $\operatorname{Dyn}(X)$ collects all pairs $(Q, \delta)$ consisting of an object $Q$ of $\mathcal{H}$ together with a structure map $\delta: QX \to Q$ called the dynamics. A morphism $f: (Q, \delta) \to (Q', \delta')$ in this category is an $\mathcal{H}$-morphism $f: Q \to Q'$ that respects the dynamics in the sense that $\delta' \circ fX = f \circ \delta$. The forgetful functor $U: \operatorname{Dyn}(X) \to \mathcal{H}$ sending $(Q, \delta)$ to $Q$ belongs to the class $\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{H})$ of structured categories over $\mathcal{H}$.
