For any process $X: \mathcal{H} \to \mathcal{H}$, the \textbf{category $\operatorname{Dyn}(X)$ of $X$-dynamics} has as objects all pairs $(Q, \delta)$ where $\delta: QX \to Q$ and as morphisms all $f: (Q, \delta) \to (Q', \delta')$ such that the following diagram commutes:

$$\begin{array}{ccc}
QX & \xrightarrow{fX} & Q'X \\
{\scriptstyle\delta}\downarrow & & \downarrow{\scriptstyle\delta'} \\
Q & \xrightarrow{f} & Q'
\end{array}$$

Equivalently, $\delta' \circ fX = f \circ \delta$.
