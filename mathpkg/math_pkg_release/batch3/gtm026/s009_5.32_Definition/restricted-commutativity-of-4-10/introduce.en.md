This remark establishes that for any function $\xi: XT \to X$, the commutativity condition of diagram 4.10 holds when restricted to the interpretation of true constants. The proof uses the fact that both paths from $(\emptyset \to XT)T$ through $X\mu$ and $\xi T$ coincide, being the unique $T$-homomorphism between the free algebras. This restricted commutativity is sufficient for establishing that $M_\xi$ preserves composition under certain conditions.
