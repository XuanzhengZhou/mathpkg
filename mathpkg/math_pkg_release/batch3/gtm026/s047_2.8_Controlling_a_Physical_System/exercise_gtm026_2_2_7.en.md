Let $f:X^* \longrightarrow Y$ be a function. Using the proof of 2.18, prove that the minimal realization of $f$ (with respect to $-\times X: \text{Set} \longrightarrow \text{Set}$, $I = 1$) is constructed as the subdynamics $Q_f = \{L_w f : w \in X^*\} \subset Y^{X^*}$ with initial state $f$ and output $\text{pr}_{\Lambda}$, where $L_w: X^* \longrightarrow X^*$ is the map $w' \longmapsto ww'$. Derive $M_f$ for $f$ as in 2.2 using this approach.
