Let $X = \{a\}$, $Y = \{0, 1\}$, $f = \chi_L: X^* \longrightarrow Y$ where $L = \{a^m : m \text{ is prime}\} \subset X^*$. Prove that $Q_f$ is infinite (and hence that all realizations of $f$ have infinitely many states).
