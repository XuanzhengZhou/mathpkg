Let $(K, \psi)$ be a colimit of $D$ qua diagram in $\mathcal{K}$ and let $\eta: K \longrightarrow L$ be a reflection of $K$ in $\mathcal{L}$. If $(L', \Gamma)$ is a lower bound of $D$ with $L' \in \mathcal{L}$

there exists unique $f: K \longrightarrow L'$ with $\psi_i: f = \Gamma_i$ and, as $L' \in \mathcal{L}$, there exists unique $g: L \longrightarrow L'$ with $\eta.g = f$.

The following corollaries of 7.7 and 7.8 show that most of the categories mentioned in this book have small colimits.
