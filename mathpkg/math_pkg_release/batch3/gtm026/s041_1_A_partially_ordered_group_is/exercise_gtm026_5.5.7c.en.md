Let $L$ be the two element lattice $\{0, 1\}$ with $0 < 1$. Determine the structure of the free $L$-fuzzy Boolean algebra over id: $L \longrightarrow L$. [Hint: it has sixteen elements, coinciding as a Boolean algebra with the free complete atomic Boolean algebra generated by a two-element set.]
