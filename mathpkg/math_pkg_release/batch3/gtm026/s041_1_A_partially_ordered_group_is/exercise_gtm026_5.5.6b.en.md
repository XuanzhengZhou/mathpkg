Let QLS ("quasinormed linear spaces") be the full subcategory of $\mathcal{C}^{\bar{T}}$ of all $(X, d, A, \xi)$ whose "norm" $\|x\| = d(x, 0)$ satisfies $\|\lambda x\| \leqslant |\lambda| \|x\|$ and $\|x + y\| \leqslant \|x\| + \|y\|$ and which are such that $A = \{0\}$. Show that QLS is a Birkhoff subcategory of $\mathcal{C}^{\bar{T}}$, and hence that $U_1$ is algebraic.
