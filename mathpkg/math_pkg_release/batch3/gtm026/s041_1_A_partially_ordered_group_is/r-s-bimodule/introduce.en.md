An R-S bimodule is an abelian group that is simultaneously a left module over a ring R and a right module over a ring S, with the crucial compatibility condition that the left and right actions commute: r(as) = (ra)s. Manes observes that left R-modules and right S-modules each form algebraic categories over sets, and that an R-S bimodule is exactly an R-S bialgebra in the sense of Section 6.1, connecting module theory to the general theory of bialgebras.
