A theory $\mathbf{T}$ in $\mathcal{A}$ is \textit{enriched} if for all $A, B, C$ in $\mathcal{A}$ the map

$$[A, BT] \otimes [B, CT] \xrightarrow{\circ} [A, CT], \quad \alpha, \beta \longmapsto \alpha \circ \beta$$

is admissible in $\mathcal{A}$.

If $\mathbf{T}$ is enriched, show that the map $[-, X] : \mathcal{A}^{\text{op}} \longrightarrow \mathcal{A}$ has $[-, X] : \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathcal{A}^{\text{op}}$ as a left adjoint. Show that the induced algebraic theory $T_{[X]}$ in $\mathcal{A}$ is enriched.
