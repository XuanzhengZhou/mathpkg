Let MET be the full subcategory of $\mathcal{L}$ of metric spaces with base point and let CMP be the full subcategory of MET of complete metric spaces with base point. Verify that the pullbacks along MET and CMP are the usual categories, NLS and BAN of normed linear spaces and Banach spaces (with contractive linear maps). Use 1.36 and 1.38 to prove that $U_3$, $U_4$, and $U$ are algebraic.
