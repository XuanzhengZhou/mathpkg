Proposition 3.23 generalizes the minimal realization theorem for $X$-automata (Theorem 2.18) to the setting of implicit $\lambda$-automata. Given an image factorization system $(E, M)$ in $\mathcal{K}$ with $X^{@}T$ preserving $E$, every response $f: IX^{@} \to Y$ admits a realization whose reachability map lies in $E$ and whose observability map lies in $M$. Moreover, any two such realizations are isomorphic as $\lambda$-algebras, yielding a well-defined minimal realization up to isomorphism. This result provides the theoretical foundation for constructing state-minimal fuzzy automata in the algebraic theories framework.
