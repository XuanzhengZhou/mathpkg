An optimal lift provides a structure on an object $K$ that is "optimal" with respect to a family of morphisms $f_i: K \to K_i$ into already-structured objects. Informally, it is the coarsest structure on $K$ making all the $f_i$ admissible. The defining universal property states that any morphism $g$ out of $K$ whose compositions with the $f_i$ are admissible must itself be admissible relative to the optimal structure.
