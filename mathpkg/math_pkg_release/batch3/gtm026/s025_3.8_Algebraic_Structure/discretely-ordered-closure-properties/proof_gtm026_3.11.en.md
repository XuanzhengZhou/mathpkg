For products: the fibre of a product category over $K$ is the product of the individual fibres. Since a product of discrete preordered sets is discrete, the product category is discretely ordered. For a full replete subcategory $D$ of a discretely ordered category $C$: the fibre $D(K)$ is a sub-preorder of the discrete preorder $C(K)$, hence is itself discrete. Thus $D$ is discretely ordered.
