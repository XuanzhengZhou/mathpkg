Let $\mathcal{C} \in \text{Struct}(\text{Set})$ be topological spaces and continuous maps. Any family $f_i: K \to (L_i, \mathcal{T}_i)$ has an optimal lift $\mathcal{S} \in \mathcal{C}(K)$, where a subbase for $\mathcal{S}$ is all sets of the form $U f_i^{-1}$ with $U \in \mathcal{T}_i$. The empty optimal lift in $\mathcal{C}(K)$ is the indiscrete topology, while the empty co-optimal lift is the discrete topology.
