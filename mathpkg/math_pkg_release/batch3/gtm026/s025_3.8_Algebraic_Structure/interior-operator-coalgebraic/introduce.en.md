An interior operator is the coalgebraic dual of a closure operator in the partially ordered setting. Just as a closure operator is an algebraic theory (monad) satisfying extensivity and idempotence, an interior operator is an algebraic cotheory (comonad) satisfying contractivity ($x^{\circ} \leqslant x$) and idempotence ($x^{\circ\circ} = x^{\circ}$). This duality is a special case of the general notion of coalgebraic structure.
