Let the category $K$ be a partially ordered class. An algebraic theory $T = (T, \eta, \mu)$ in $K$ is an order-preserving endomorphism satisfying $x \leqslant xT$ and $xTT = xT$. Such a $T$ is precisely a closure operator on $K$. The category of algebras $K^T$ is the full subcategory of closed elements: all $x$ such that $x^{-} \leqslant x$ (writing $x^{-}$ for $xT$).
