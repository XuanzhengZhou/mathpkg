By definition, $A$ is coalgebraic over $K$ iff $A^{\text{op}}$ is algebraic over $K^{\text{op}}$. Since $K^{\text{op}}$ is also a complete poset (infima in $K^{\text{op}}$ correspond to suprema in $K$), the previous characterization applies: $A^{\text{op}}$ is algebraic over $K^{\text{op}}$ iff $A^{\text{op}}$ is closed under infima in $K^{\text{op}}$, which is equivalent to $A$ being closed under suprema in $K$.
