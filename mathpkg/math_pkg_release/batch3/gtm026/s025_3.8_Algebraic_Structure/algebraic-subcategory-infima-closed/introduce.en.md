In a complete partially ordered set, a full subcategory is algebraic over the base poset precisely when it is closed under arbitrary infima. The correspondence is constructive: given an infimum-closed subcategory $A$, the associated closure operator sends $x$ to the infimum of all elements of $A$ above $x$. This provides a concrete order-theoretic criterion for algebraicity.
