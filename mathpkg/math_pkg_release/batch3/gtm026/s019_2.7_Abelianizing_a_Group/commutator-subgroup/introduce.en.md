The commutator subgroup $[G, G]$ is the smallest normal subgroup of $G$ such that the quotient $G/[G, G]$ is abelian. It is generated by all commutators $ab(ba)^{-1}$ with $a, b \in G$. The commutator subgroup is a characteristic subgroup of $G$ and is central to the abelianization construction.
