Let $M = (Q, \delta: QX \to QT, I, \tau, Y, \theta, \beta)$ be a $\lambda$-automaton. Then
$$M^* = (QT, \delta^*, Qm, I, \tau, Y, \beta^*)$$
as defined in 3.9 is an implicit $\lambda$-automaton and the response of $M$ (as defined in 3.9, i.e., as in 2.3) is the response of $M^*$ (as defined in 3.19).
