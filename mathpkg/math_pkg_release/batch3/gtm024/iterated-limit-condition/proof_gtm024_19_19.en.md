If $A$ is weakly compact, let $x_0$ be a weak cluster point of $\{x_n\}$ and $\phi_0$ a weak* cluster point of $\{\phi_m\}$. Then both iterated limits equal $\phi_0(x_0)$. The converse uses a diagonal argument: if the iterated limit condition fails, one can construct a sequence with no weak cluster point.
