If ψ∈span{φᵢ}, clearly ∩ker(φᵢ)⊂ker(ψ). Conversely, define S:X→ℝⁿ by S(x)=(φ₁(x),…,φₙ(x)). If ∩ker(φᵢ)⊂ker(ψ), then ker(S)⊂ker(ψ), so ψ factors through S by the factorization theorem.
