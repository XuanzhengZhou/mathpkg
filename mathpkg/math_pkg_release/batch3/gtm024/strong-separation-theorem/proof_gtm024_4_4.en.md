If such $V$ exists, then $A+V$ and $B$ are disjoint convex sets with $\operatorname{cor}(A+V) \neq \emptyset$, so the basic separation theorem applies, giving strong separation. Conversely, if $A$ and $B$ are strongly separated by $[\phi; \alpha]$ with margin $\varepsilon$, take $V = \{x: |\phi(x)| < \varepsilon\}$ which is convex and absorbing.
