Observe that if $h \in \operatorname{Conv}(X)$ with $h(\theta) = 0$, then for $0 < s \leqslant t$, $h(sx) \leqslant \frac{s}{t}h(tx) + \frac{t-s}{t}h(\theta)$, so $h(sx)/s \leqslant h(tx)/t$. Apply to $h(y) = g(x_0 + y) - g(x_0)$.
