Let $A$ be a convex subset of an LTS $X$. Then $\bar{A}$ is convex. If $A$ is also solid: a) $\operatorname{int}(A) = \operatorname{cor}(A)$. b) $\bar{A} = \overline{\operatorname{int}(A)}$ and $\operatorname{int}(A) = \operatorname{int}(\bar{A})$. c) $\operatorname{lin}(A) = \bar{A}$. d) The algebraic boundary of $A$ is its topological boundary.
