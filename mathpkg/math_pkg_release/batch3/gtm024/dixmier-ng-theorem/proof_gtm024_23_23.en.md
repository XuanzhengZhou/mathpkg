Let $V = \{\phi \in X^* : \phi \text{ is } \tau\text{-continuous}\}$. Then $J_V: X \to V^*$ is an isomorphism. $U(X)$ $\tau$-compact implies $J_V(X)$ is weak*-closed in $V^*$. Since $V$ is total, $J_V(X)$ is weak*-dense, so by the Banach-Dieudonné theorem, $J_V$ is surjective.
