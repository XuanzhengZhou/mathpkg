It suffices to show $B \subset C_B + \operatorname{co}(\operatorname{ext}(B))$ for a closed line-free convex $B$. Proceed by induction on $\dim(B)$. Any point not in the convex hull of extreme points can be expressed using a supporting hyperplane, reducing the dimension.
