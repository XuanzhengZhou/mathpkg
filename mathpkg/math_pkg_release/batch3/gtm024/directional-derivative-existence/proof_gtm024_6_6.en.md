Existence follows from the monotonicity lemma and boundedness below of the difference quotient. Subadditivity: $g(x_0 + t(x+y)) \leqslant \frac{1}{2}g(x_0+2tx) + \frac{1}{2}g(x_0+2ty)$. Dividing by $t$ and letting $t \downarrow 0$ gives $g'(x_0; x+y) \leqslant g'(x_0; x) + g'(x_0; y)$.
