Let $M$ be the subspace of $\operatorname{Lip}([0,1], \mathbb{R})$ of functions vanishing at $0$. The map $f \mapsto f'$ gives a congruence $M \cong L^\infty$. By Pelczyński, $M \sim m$. Then $\operatorname{Lip}([0,1]) \cong \mathbb{R} \oplus M \sim \mathbb{R} \oplus m \sim m \sim L^\infty$.
