The closed convex hull $\overline{\operatorname{co}}(A)$ of $A \subset X$ is the smallest closed convex set in $X$ that contains $A$: $\overline{\operatorname{co}}(A) = \bigcap\{K \subset X : A \subset K, K \text{ closed and convex}\}$. It equals $\overline{\operatorname{co}(A)}$, the closure of the ordinary convex hull.
