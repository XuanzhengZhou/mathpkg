An instantaneous description captures the complete configuration of a Turing machine at a given moment during its operation. It records the current internal state of the machine, the symbols printed on the tape, and the position of the tape head (indicated by the state symbol's position within the string). This representation allows the step-by-step evolution of a Turing machine computation to be formally analyzed as transformations of finite strings.
