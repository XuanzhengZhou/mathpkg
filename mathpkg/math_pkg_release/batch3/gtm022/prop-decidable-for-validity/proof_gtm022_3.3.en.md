For $w \in P(X)$, let $w = w(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ be expressed in terms of finitely many variables. Let $\bar{w} \in L(X_n)$ be the associated truth function. Calculate $\bar{w}(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$ for each $(a_1, \ldots, a_n) \in \mathbb{Z}_2^n$. $w$ is valid iff all $2^n$ values are $1$.
