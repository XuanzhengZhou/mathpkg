An \emph{interpretation} of $P(V, \mathcal{R})$ consists of a non-empty set $U$, a function $\varphi: V \to U$, a function $\psi: \mathcal{R} \to \bigcup_n \operatorname{Pow}(U^n)$ such that $\psi(r) \subseteq U^{\operatorname{ar}(r)}$, and a valuation $v: P(V, \mathcal{R}) \to \mathbb{Z}_2$ satisfying natural conditions that make $v$ a homomorphism respecting the quantifiers.
