The language of $S$ is the subset $\mathcal{L}(S) = \{p \in P(V, R) \mid \operatorname{var}(p) \subseteq S\}$ of $P(V, R)$. Interpreting each element of $S$ and $R$ as itself assigns a truth value to every element of $\mathcal{L}(S)$. The theory $\mathcal{T}(S) = (R, A, S)$ where $A = \{p \in \mathcal{L}(S) \mid p \text{ is true in } S\}$ is complete and has $S$ as a model.
