A valuation is a homomorphism from the proposition algebra $P(X)$ into the two-element Boolean algebra $\mathbb{Z}_2$. It assigns truth values (1 for true, 0 for false) to propositions in a way consistent with the logical operations. A valuation is uniquely determined by its values on the generators $X$.
