One direction (diophantine $\Rightarrow$ recursive) is straightforward: checking membership involves an unbounded search. The converse uses the algebraic characterization of recursive functions: the initial functions are diophantine, and the class of diophantine functions is closed under composition, primitive recursion (using the sequence number function to encode intermediate values), and minimalisation.
