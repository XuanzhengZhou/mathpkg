Proof. If $A \models p$, then $A \vdash p$ by Adequacy. Any proof uses only finitely many assumptions, so $A_0 \vdash p$ for some finite $A_0 \subseteq A$, and $A_0 \models p$ by Soundness. The equivalent version follows by considering $p = F$. $\square$
