Lemma 4.5 establishes a key combinatorial property of $\alpha$-complete ultrafilters: when the index set $I$ is partitioned into at most $\alpha$ disjoint pieces, the ultrafilter must contain one of the pieces. This is a natural generalization of the fact that an ultrafilter cannot be partitioned into finitely many sets none of which belong to it. The lemma plays a crucial role in the characterization of when an ultrapower collapses to the original structure (Theorem 4.7).
