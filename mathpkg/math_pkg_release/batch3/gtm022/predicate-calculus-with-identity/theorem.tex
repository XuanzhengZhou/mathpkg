$\operatorname{Pred}_g(V, \mathcal{R})$ is the logic with algebra $P(V, \mathcal{R} \cup \{\mathcal{I}\})$, valuations from proper interpretations, and proof of $p$ from $A$ defined as a proof of $p$ from $I \cup A$ in $\operatorname{Pred}(V, \mathcal{R} \cup \{\mathcal{I}\})$, where $I$ contains $(\forall x)\mathcal{I}(x,x)$ and all substitutivity axioms.
