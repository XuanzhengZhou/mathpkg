Let $A$ be a $T$-algebra. A subset $B \subseteq A$ is called a \textbf{$T$-subalgebra} of $A$ if $B$ forms a $T$-algebra with operations the restrictions to $B$ of those on $A$; i.e., if for all $n \in \mathbb{N}$, for all $t \in T_n$, and for all $b_1, \ldots, b_n \in B$, we have $t_A(b_1, \ldots, b_n) \in B$.
