Let $\tilde{P}(V, \mathcal{R})$ be the free algebra on the set $\{(r, x_1, \ldots, x_n) \mid r \in \mathcal{R}, x_i \in V, n = \operatorname{ar}(r)\}$ of free generators, of type $\{F, \Rightarrow, (\forall x) \mid x \in V\}$, where $F$ is a 0-ary operation, $\Rightarrow$ is binary, and each $(\forall x)$ is unary. $\tilde{P}(V, \mathcal{R})$ is called the full first-order algebra on $(V, \mathcal{R})$.
