This exercise asks the reader to verify that the algebraic definitions of the logical connectives in terms of $ and $\Rightarrow$ produce the standard truth tables. For example, (\sim p) = v(p \Rightarrow F) = 1 + v(p)(1 + v(F)) = 1 + v(p)$, so $\sim p$ is true exactly when $ is false.
