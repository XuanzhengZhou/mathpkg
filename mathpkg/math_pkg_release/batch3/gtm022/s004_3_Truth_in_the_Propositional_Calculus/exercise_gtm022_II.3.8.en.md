Show that $p \Rightarrow (q \Rightarrow p)$, $(p \Rightarrow (q \Rightarrow r)) \Rightarrow ((p \Rightarrow q) \Rightarrow (p \Rightarrow r))$ and $\sim\sim p \Rightarrow p$ are tautologies, for all $p, q, r \in P(X)$.
