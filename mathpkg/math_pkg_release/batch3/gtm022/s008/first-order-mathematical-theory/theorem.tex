A first-order mathematical theory $\mathcal{T} = (\mathbb{R}, A, C)$ with $\mathcal{I} \in \mathbb{R}$, axioms $A \subseteq P(V, \mathbb{R})$, constants $C$ where $\text{var}(A)=C$, $V-C$ infinite. The language $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{T}) = \{p \in P(V, \mathbb{R}) \mid \text{var}(p) \subseteq C\}$. A theorem is $p \in \mathcal{L}(\mathcal{T})$ with $A \vdash p$.
