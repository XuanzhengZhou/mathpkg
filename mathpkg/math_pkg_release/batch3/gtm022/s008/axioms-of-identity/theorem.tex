The axioms of identity $I$ are: $(\forall x) \mathcal{I}(x, x)$ (reflexivity) and for all $r \in \mathcal{R}_n$, all $j \leq n$: $(\forall x_1)\cdots(\forall x_n)(\forall y)(\mathcal{I}(x_j, y) \Rightarrow (r(x_1,\ldots,x_n) \Rightarrow r(x_1,\ldots,x_{j-1},y,x_{j+1},\ldots,x_n)))$ (Leibniz's law).
