Let $\mathcal{T}$ be a first-order theory and $\{(M_i, v_i, \psi_i)\}_{i \in I}$ a family of models of $\mathcal{T}$. Let $\mathcal{F}$ be an ultrafilter on $I$. Define the ultraproduct $M = \prod_{i \in I} M_i / \mathcal{F}$ with componentwise interpretation of constants and relations modified by $\mathcal{F}$. Then for any $p \in P(V, \mathcal{R})$ and $a\mathcal{F} \in M$, $p$ is true in $M$ at $a\mathcal{F}$ if and only if $\{i \in I \mid p \text{ is true in } M_i \text{ at } a_i\} \in \mathcal{F}$.
