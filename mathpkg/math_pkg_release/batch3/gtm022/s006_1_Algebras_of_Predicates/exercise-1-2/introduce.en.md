This exercise asks the reader to verify the properties of the variable occurrence function $V(w)$ on the free predicate algebra $\tilde{P}(V, \mathcal{R})$. The function collects all variables (both free and bound) appearing in a formula. The reader must show that the stated inductive clauses correctly describe $V$, and that these clauses suffice as a definition of $V$.
