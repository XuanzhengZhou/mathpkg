Given a non-empty set $U$, a map $\varphi : V \to U$, and a map $\psi$ assigning to each $r \in \mathcal{R}$ a relation on $U$ of the appropriate arity, prove that there is one and only one function $v : P(V, \mathcal{R}) \to \mathbb{Z}_2$ satisfying conditions (a), (b), and $(c_i)$ for all $i$.
