This exercise verifies that the definition of free variables $\text{var}(w)$ for $w \in P(V, \mathcal{R})$ via a representative in $\tilde{P}(V, \mathcal{R})$ is independent of the choice of representative. The reader must prove that the syntactic congruence $\approx$ preserves the set of free variables, so that $\text{var}(w)$ is well-defined on the quotient algebra.
