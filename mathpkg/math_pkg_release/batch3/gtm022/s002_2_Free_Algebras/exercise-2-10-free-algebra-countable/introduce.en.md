Exercise 2.10 from GTM022 §2. Under the assumptions that the similarity type $T$ is finite or countable, contains at least one constant (0-ary operation) and at least one operation of positive arity, and the generator set $X$ is finite or countable, the exercise asks to prove that the free $T$-algebra $F$ on $X$ is countable. This generalizes Exercises 2.6-2.9. The key observation is that each layer $F_n$ in the inductive construction is finite or countable, and $F = \bigcup_{n \in \mathbb{N}} F_n$ is a countable union of countable sets.
