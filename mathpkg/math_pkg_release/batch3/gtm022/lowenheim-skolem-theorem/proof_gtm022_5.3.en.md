Choose $V_0 \supseteq C$ with $|V_0 \setminus C| = \aleph$. Then $|P(V_0, \mathcal{R})| = \aleph$. Add axioms $\sim \mathcal{I}(x, y)$ for all distinct $x, y \in V_0$ to force the model to have at least $\aleph$ elements. The resulting theory is consistent (by Compactness, since any finite subset involves only finitely many variables). A model is obtained via the Satisfiability Theorem with universe of cardinality $\aleph$.
