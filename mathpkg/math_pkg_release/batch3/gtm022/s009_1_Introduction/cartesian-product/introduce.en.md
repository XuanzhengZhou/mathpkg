The Cartesian product $a \times b$ of two sets $a$ and $b$ is the set of all ordered pairs $(x, y)$ with $x \in a$ and $y \in b$. Its existence in ZF follows from the Axiom of the Power Set (ZF5) together with the Axiom of Union (ZF4) and the Axiom Schema of Subsets (ZF2). Using the Kuratowski definition $(x, y) = \{\{x\}, \{x, y\}\}$, one can verify that every ordered pair formed from elements of $a$ and $b$ belongs to a suitable power set construction, from which the Cartesian product can then be isolated.
