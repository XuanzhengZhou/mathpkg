By induction on the complexity of formulas. For atomic formulas, it follows from the definition of the ultraproduct structure (operations and relations are defined componentwise via the ultrafilter). For propositional connectives, the filter properties (ii) and (iii) handle $\wedge$ and $\vee$, while (iv) handles negation. For the universal quantifier, both directions require careful argument using condition (iv) and the fact that elements of the ultraproduct are equivalence classes of functions.
