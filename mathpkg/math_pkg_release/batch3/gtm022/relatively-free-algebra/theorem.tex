A $T$-algebra $R$ in the variety $V$ is the \emph{(relatively) free algebra} of $V$ on the set $X$ of \emph{(relatively) free generators} (where a function $\sigma: X \to R$ is given, usually as an inclusion) if, for every $T$-algebra $A$ in $V$ and every function $\tau: X \to A$, there exists a unique homomorphism $\varphi: R \to A$ such that $\varphi\sigma = \tau$.
