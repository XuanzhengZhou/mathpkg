Let $X_n = \{x_1, \ldots, x_n\}$. The \emph{Lindenbaum algebra} $L(X_n)$ is the set of congruence classes of elements of $P(X_n)$ with respect to the equivalence relation $u \sim v$ if $u \models v$ and $v \models u$ (i.e., $u$ and $v$ are semantically equivalent). Elements of $L(X_n)$ are identified with their associated truth functions $\mathbb{Z}_2^n \to \mathbb{Z}_2$.
