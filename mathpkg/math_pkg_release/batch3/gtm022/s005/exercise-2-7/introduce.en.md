This exercise asks the reader to prove the double negation introduction law in Prop$(X)$. The strategy is to first derive a contradiction from $\{p, \sim p\}$ using modus ponens, then apply the Deduction Theorem twice: once to obtain $\sim p \Rightarrow F$ (which is $\sim\sim p$), and again to obtain $p \Rightarrow \sim\sim p$.
