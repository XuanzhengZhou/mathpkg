Show that the following are theorems of $\text{Prop}(X)$:
(a) $p \Rightarrow p \lor q$,
(b) $q \Rightarrow p \lor q$,
(c) $(p \lor q) \Rightarrow (q \lor p)$,
(d) $p \land q \Rightarrow p$,
(e) $p \land q \Rightarrow q$,
(f) $(p \land q) \Rightarrow (q \land p)$.
