An ultraproduct $\prod_{i \in I} M_i / \mathcal{F}$ in which $M_i = M$ for all $i \in I$ is called an ultrapower of $M$ and denoted by $M^I / \mathcal{F}$. There is a natural diagonal embedding $\theta: M \to M^I / \mathcal{F}$ given by $\theta(m) = f_m\mathcal{F}$, where $f_m(i) = m$ for all $i \in I$.
