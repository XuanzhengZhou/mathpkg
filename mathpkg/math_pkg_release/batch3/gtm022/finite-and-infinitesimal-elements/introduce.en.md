In a non-standard extension of the reals, elements are classified as finite (bounded by some standard real), infinite (unbounded), or infinitesimal (smaller than every positive standard real). The relation $a \cong b$ (infinitesimally close) is an equivalence relation, and each finite element is infinitesimally close to exactly one standard real number.
