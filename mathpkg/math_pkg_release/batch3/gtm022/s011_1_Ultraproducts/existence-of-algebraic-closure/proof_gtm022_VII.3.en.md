Let $T$ be elementary field theory augmented by the addition of the elements of $F$ to the set of constants, and of all the relations $a_1 + a_2 = a_3$, $b_1 b_2 = b_3$ holding in $F$ to the set of axioms. The models of $T$ are the extension fields of $F$. Put $R = F[x]$, the ring of polynomials over $F$. For each $r \in R$, let $F_r$ be a splitting field of $r$. Put

$$J_r = \{ s \in R \mid r \text{ splits over } F_s \}.$$

Since $r_1 r_2 \cdots r_n \in J_{r_1} \cap J_{r_2} \cap \cdots \cap J_{r_n}$, the set $J = \{ J_r \mid r \in R \}$ has the finite intersection property. By Lemmas 2.4 and 2.5, there exists an ultrafilter $\mathcal{F}$ on $R$ containing $J$. Put $F^* = \prod_{r \in R} F_r / \mathcal{F}$. Then $F^*$ is a model of $T$ and so is an extension field of $F$.
