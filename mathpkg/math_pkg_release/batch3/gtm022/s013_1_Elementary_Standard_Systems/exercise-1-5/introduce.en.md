Exercise 1.5 asks the reader to prove that the first-order theory with relation symbols $\mathcal{R}$ and axioms $A$ indeed has the elementary standard system $\mathbb{S}$ as its set of constants. This verifies a foundational assumption underlying the construction of the complete theory $\mathcal{T}(\mathbb{S})$.
