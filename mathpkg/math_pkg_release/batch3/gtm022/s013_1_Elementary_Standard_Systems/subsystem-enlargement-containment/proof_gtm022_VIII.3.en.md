Put $\mathbb{S}^\sigma = \mathbb{S} \cup \{c_\rho \mid \rho \in \sigma\}$ with the relations restricted to those of the enlargement $\mathbb{S}_1^{\sigma_1}$. Since $\sigma \leqslant \sigma_1$, for each $\rho \in \sigma$, the corresponding concurrent relation $\rho_1$ on $\mathbb{S}_1$ has its constant $c_{\rho_1}$ already present in $\mathbb{S}_1^{\sigma_1}$. Identifying $c_\rho$ with $c_{\rho_1}$ gives the required embedding, and the axioms $A_\sigma$ are satisfied because they are instances of the corresponding axioms in $A_{\sigma_1}$.
