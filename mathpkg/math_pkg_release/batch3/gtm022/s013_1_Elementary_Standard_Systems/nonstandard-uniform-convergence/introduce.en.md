Uniform convergence of a sequence of functions $s_n$ to $r$ on $U$ is characterized in non-standard analysis by the condition that for every point $x$ in the non-standard extension $*U$ and every infinite index $n$, $*s_n(x)$ is infinitesimally close to $*r(x)$. The crucial difference from pointwise convergence is that $x$ ranges over all of $*U$, not just $U$.
