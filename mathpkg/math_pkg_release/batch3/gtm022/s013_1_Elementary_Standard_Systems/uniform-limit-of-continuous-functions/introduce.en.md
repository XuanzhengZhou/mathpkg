A classical theorem of analysis states that the uniform limit of continuous functions is continuous. In the non-standard framework, the proof becomes transparent: for $x$ infinitesimally close to $a$ and $n$ infinite, $*r(x) \cong *s_n(x) \cong *s_n(a) \cong *r(a)$ by combining the uniform convergence characterization with the continuity characterization of each $s_n$. The middle congruence uses Corollary 6.11 for each $s_n$.
