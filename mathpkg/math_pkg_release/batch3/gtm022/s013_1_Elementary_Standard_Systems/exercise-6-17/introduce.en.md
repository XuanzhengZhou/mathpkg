Exercise 6.17 asks the reader to prove Theorem 6.16 (the non-standard characterization of uniform convergence) by adapting the argument used for Theorem 6.13 (the non-standard characterization of uniform continuity). Both proofs rely on transfer principles and the concurrent relation construction to pass between standard epsilon-delta statements and their non-standard monad formulations.
