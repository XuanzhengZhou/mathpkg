An internal first-order $n$-ary relation on $\mathbb{S}^\sigma$ is an element of $(\mathcal{R}_n^{(1)})^\sigma$. A relation on $\mathbb{S}^\sigma$ which is not internal is called external.

Higher-order internal $n$-ary relations may be defined similarly, by using the set $\mathcal{R}_n^{(k)}$ of $n$-ary relations on $\mathcal{O}^{k-1}(\mathbb{S})$.
