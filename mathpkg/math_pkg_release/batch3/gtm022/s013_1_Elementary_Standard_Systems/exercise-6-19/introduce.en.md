Exercise 6.19 asks the reader to prove, using non-standard methods, that the limit of the product of two functions equals the product of their limits. In the non-standard framework, this follows from the fact that if $*f(x) \in \mu(r)$ and $*g(x) \in \mu(s)$, then $*f(x)*g(x) \in \mu(rs)$, using the algebraic properties of finite and infinitesimal numbers established in Theorem 6.8.
