Let $\Pi \subseteq P(\mathcal{T})$. $\mathcal{T}$ admits $\Pi$-reduction of quantifiers if there is a process assigning to each $p \in P(\mathcal{T})$ a $q \in P(\mathcal{T})$ such that:
(i) $q$ is a propositional combination of elements of $\Pi$,
(ii) $\operatorname{var}(q) \subseteq \operatorname{var}(p)$,
(iii) $\mathcal{T} \vdash p \Leftrightarrow q$.
