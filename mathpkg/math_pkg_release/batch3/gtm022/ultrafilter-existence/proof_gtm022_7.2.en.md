The set of filters on $I$ containing $\mathcal{F}$ is partially ordered by inclusion. Every chain has an upper bound (the union). By Zorn's Lemma, there exists a maximal element, which must be an ultrafilter (if it did not satisfy condition (iv), it could be properly extended).
