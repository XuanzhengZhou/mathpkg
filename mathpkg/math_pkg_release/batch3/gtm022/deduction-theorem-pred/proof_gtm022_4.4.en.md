The proof follows the same inductive construction as for the propositional case, with the additional check for Generalisation. When the proof uses Generalisation to derive $(\forall x)r$ from $r$, where $x$ is not free in the assumptions (including $p$ by the hypothesis restriction), the corresponding step in the constructed proof uses $\mathcal{A}_4$ to distribute $p \Rightarrow$ over $(\forall x)$.
