By the Soundness Theorem (Theorem 2.1), if $\vdash F$ were provable, then $F$ would be a tautology. But for any valuation $v: P(X) \to \mathbb{Z}_2$, we have $v(F) = 0$ (since $F$ is the false proposition). Hence $F$ is not a tautology, and therefore $F$ is not a theorem of $\text{Prop}(X)$.
