A semi-homomorphism $(\alpha, \beta): (P_1, V_1) \to (P_2, V_2)$ consists of a function $\alpha: P_1 \to P_2$ and a function $\beta: V_1 \to V_2$ such that $\alpha$ preserves $F$ and $\Rightarrow$, $\alpha((\forall x)p) = (\forall \beta(x))\alpha(p)$, and $x \notin \operatorname{var}(p)$ implies $\beta(x) \notin \operatorname{var}(\alpha(p))$.
