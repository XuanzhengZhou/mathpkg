This exercise explores the relatively free algebras in the variety of commutative $R$-algebras (commutative rings containing $R$ as a subring). The relatively free algebra on $n$ generators in this variety is the polynomial ring $R[x_1, \ldots, x_n]$, since any assignment of the generators to elements of an $R$-algebra extends uniquely to a polynomial evaluation homomorphism.
