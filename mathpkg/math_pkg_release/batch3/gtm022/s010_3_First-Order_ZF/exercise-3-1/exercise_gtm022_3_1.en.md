Some later axioms of $\mathcal{S}$ will have the form $(\forall)(\exists a)(\forall x)(p(x) \Rightarrow x \in a)$ for certain elements $p(x) \in P(V, \mathcal{R})$. Show that
$$
\{(\forall)(\exists a)(\forall x)(p(x) \Rightarrow x \in a)\} \vdash_{\mathcal{S}} (\forall)(\exists a)(\forall x)(p(x) \Leftrightarrow x \in a).
$$
