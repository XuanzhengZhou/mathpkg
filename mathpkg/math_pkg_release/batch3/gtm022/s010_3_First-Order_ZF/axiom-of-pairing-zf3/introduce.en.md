The Axiom of Pairing (ZF3) asserts that for any two sets $a$ and $b$, there exists a set $c$ that contains both $a$ and $b$ as elements. Combined with the Axiom Schema of Separation, this yields the existence of the unordered pair $\{a, b\}$.
