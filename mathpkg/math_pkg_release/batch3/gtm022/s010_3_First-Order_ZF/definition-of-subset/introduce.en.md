The subset relation $a \subseteq b$ is introduced as an abbreviation in the formal language $\mathcal{S}$ of first-order ZF set theory. It abbreviates the formula $(\forall x)(x \in a \Rightarrow x \in b)$, meaning every element of $a$ is also an element of $b$.
