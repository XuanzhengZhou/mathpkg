The axioms of the predicate calculus extend those of the propositional calculus with two axiom schemas for quantifiers: $\mathcal{A}_4$ handles pulling quantifiers past non-free variables, and $\mathcal{A}_5$ is universal instantiation. The rules of inference are Modus Ponens and Generalisation.
