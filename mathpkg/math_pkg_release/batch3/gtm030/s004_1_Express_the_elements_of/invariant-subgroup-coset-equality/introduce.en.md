For an invariant (normal) subgroup, the distinction between left and right cosets disappears: $\mathfrak{H}y = y\mathfrak{H}$ for every $y$. This property is both necessary and sufficient for normality. It ensures that the product of cosets is well-defined, enabling the construction of the factor group $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$.
