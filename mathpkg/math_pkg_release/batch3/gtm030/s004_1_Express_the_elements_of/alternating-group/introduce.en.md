The alternating group $A_n$ is the subgroup of the symmetric group $S_n$ consisting of all even permutations. An even permutation is one that can be expressed as a product of an even number of transpositions. The alternating group $A_n$ has index 2 in $S_n$ for $n \geq 2$, and its order is $n!/2$.
