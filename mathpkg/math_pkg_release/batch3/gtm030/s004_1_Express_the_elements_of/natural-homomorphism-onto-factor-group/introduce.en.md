The natural projection $\pi: \mathfrak{G} \to \mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$ defined by $\pi(x) = x\mathfrak{H}$ is a surjective homomorphism. This is a special case of the first isomorphism theorem and shows that every factor group is a homomorphic image of the original group. Conversely, every homomorphic image of $\mathfrak{G}$ is isomorphic to a factor group of $\mathfrak{G}$.
