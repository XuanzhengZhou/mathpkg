This theorem verifies that the factor group construction is valid. When $\mathfrak{H}$ is invariant, the product of two cosets $x\mathfrak{H}$ and $y\mathfrak{H}$ is again a coset, namely $xy\mathfrak{H}$. The group axioms are satisfied: associativity follows from associativity of subset multiplication, the subgroup $\mathfrak{H}$ itself serves as the identity coset, and each coset $x\mathfrak{H}$ has inverse $x^{-1}\mathfrak{H}$.
