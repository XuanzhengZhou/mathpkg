This exercise asks the reader to work out the explicit structure of the symmetric group $S_4$. Each of the 24 permutations should be written in two forms: as a product of disjoint cycles and as a product of transpositions. The alternating group $A_4$ consists of the 12 even permutations (those expressible as a product of an even number of transpositions).
