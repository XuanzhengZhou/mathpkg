We verify the three axioms. (1) Reflexive: $a\alpha = a\alpha$, so $a \sim a$. (2) Symmetric: If $a \sim b$, then $a\alpha = b\alpha$, so $b\alpha = a\alpha$, hence $b \sim a$. (3) Transitive: If $a \sim b$ and $b \sim c$, then $a\alpha = b\alpha = c\alpha$, so $a \sim c$. Thus $\sim$ is an equivalence relation.
