A lattice homomorphism is a map between two lattices that preserves the algebraic operations of join ($\cup$) and meet ($\cap$). This is the natural notion of structure-preserving map for the algebraic definition of a lattice (via axioms L1-L4). Homomorphisms provide the morphisms in the category of lattices, and an isomorphism is a bijective homomorphism.
