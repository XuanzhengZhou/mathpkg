Lemma 2 states that the greatest common divisor operation on a commutative semi-group satisfying Condition C is associative up to associates. Both $(a, (b, c))$ and $((a, b), c)$ serve as greatest common divisors of the three elements $a, b, c$. This property is essential for manipulating expressions involving multiple gcd operations and underpins the algebraic structure developed in Jacobson's subsequent lemmas that connect gcd with multiplication.
