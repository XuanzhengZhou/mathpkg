From the proof of Lemma 1, both $(a, (b, c))$ and $((a, b), c)$ are greatest common divisors of the three elements $a, b,$ and $c$. Since any two greatest common divisors of the same set of elements are associates, it follows that $(a, (b, c)) \sim ((a, b), c)$.
