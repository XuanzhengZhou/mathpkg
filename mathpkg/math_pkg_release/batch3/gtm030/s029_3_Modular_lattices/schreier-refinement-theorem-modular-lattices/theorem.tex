Any two finite descending chains connecting the elements $a, b$ ($a \geq b$) of a modular lattice have equivalent refinements. That is, if

$$a = a_1 \geq a_2 \geq \cdots \geq a_{s+1} = b$$

and

$$a = b_1 \geq b_2 \geq \cdots \geq b_{t+1} = b$$

are two finite descending chains, then there exist refinements of both chains such that the intervals of the two refinements can be paired off into projective pairs.
