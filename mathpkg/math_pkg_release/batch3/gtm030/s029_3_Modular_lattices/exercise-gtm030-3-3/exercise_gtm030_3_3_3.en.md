Prove that, if $\mathfrak{G}$ is a group that is generated by two elements $a$ and $b$ such that

$$a^{p^v} = 1, \quad b^{p^r} = 1, \quad b^{-1}ab = a^n$$

where $n^{p^r} \equiv 1 \pmod{p^m}$, then the lattice of invariant subgroups of $\mathfrak{G}$ is a chain.
