If $a$ and $b$ are any two elements of a modular lattice, then the intervals

$$I[a \cup b, a] = \{ x \mid a \cup b \geq x \geq a \}$$

and

$$I[b, a \cap b] = \{ y \mid b \geq y \geq a \cap b \}$$

are isomorphic. Explicitly, the maps

$$x \mapsto x \cap b, \quad y \mapsto y \cup a$$

are mutually inverse order-isomorphisms between these two intervals.
