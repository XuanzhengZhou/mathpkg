Theorem 4 is the lattice-theoretic analogue of the second isomorphism theorem for groups. In any modular lattice, the interval between $a \cup b$ and $a$ is isomorphic to the interval between $b$ and $a \cap b$, via the maps $x \mapsto x \cap b$ and $y \mapsto y \cup a$. This result provides the foundation for the theory of projective intervals and underlies the Schreier and Jordan-Hölder refinement theorems for modular lattices.
