The lattice of invariant subgroups of any group is modular.

Specifically, if $\mathfrak{H}_1, \mathfrak{H}_2, \mathfrak{H}_3$ are invariant subgroups of a group $\mathfrak{G}$ with $\mathfrak{H}_1 \geq \mathfrak{H}_2$ (i.e., $\mathfrak{H}_1 \supseteq \mathfrak{H}_2$), then

$$\mathfrak{H}_1 \cap (\mathfrak{H}_2 \cup \mathfrak{H}_3) = \mathfrak{H}_2 \cup (\mathfrak{H}_1 \cap \mathfrak{H}_3).$$
