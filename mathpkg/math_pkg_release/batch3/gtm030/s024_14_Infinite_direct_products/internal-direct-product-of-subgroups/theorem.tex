Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be an $M$-group and let $\{\mathfrak{G}_{\alpha}' \mid \alpha \in J\}$ be a collection of invariant $M$-subgroups of $\mathfrak{G}$ satisfying:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathfrak{G}$ is generated by the $\mathfrak{G}_{\alpha}'$,
\item For each $\alpha \in J$, $\mathfrak{G}_{\alpha}' \cap \prod_{\beta \neq \alpha} \mathfrak{G}_{\beta}' = 1$.
\end{enumerate}

Then $\mathfrak{G}$ is isomorphic to the direct product $\prod_{\alpha \in J} \mathfrak{G}_{\alpha}'$. In this case we say $\mathfrak{G}$ is the direct product of its subgroups and write $\mathfrak{G} = \prod_{\alpha \in J} \mathfrak{G}_{\alpha}'$.
