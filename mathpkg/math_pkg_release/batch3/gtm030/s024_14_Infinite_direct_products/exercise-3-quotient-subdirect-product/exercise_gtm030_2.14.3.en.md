Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be an $M$-group and let $\{\mathfrak{R}_{\alpha}\}$ be a collection of invariant $M$-subgroups of $\mathfrak{G}$ such that $\bigcap_{\alpha} \mathfrak{R}_{\alpha} = 1$. Show that $\mathfrak{G}$ is isomorphic to a subdirect product of the groups $\mathfrak{G}_{\alpha} = \mathfrak{G} / \mathfrak{R}_{\alpha}$.
