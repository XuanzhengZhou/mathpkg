Show that, if the group $\mathfrak{G}$ considered in Exercise 1 is the additive group of a ring, then the $\mathfrak{G}_p$ are ideals. Hence the ring $\mathfrak{G}$ is the direct sum $\sum_p \oplus \mathfrak{G}_p$ and $\mathfrak{G}_p \mathfrak{G}_q = 0$ if $p \neq q$.
