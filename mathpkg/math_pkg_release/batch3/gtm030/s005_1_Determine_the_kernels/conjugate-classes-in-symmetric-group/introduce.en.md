In the symmetric group $S_n$, the conjugacy class of a permutation is completely determined by its cycle type. The cycle type is given by the lengths of the cycles in the disjoint cycle decomposition, which form a partition of $n$. Thus there is a natural bijection between conjugacy classes of $S_n$ and integer partitions of $n$, and the number of conjugacy classes equals the partition function $p(n)$.
