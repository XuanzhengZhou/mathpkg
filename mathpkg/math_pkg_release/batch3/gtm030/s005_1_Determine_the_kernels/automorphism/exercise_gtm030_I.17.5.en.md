The transformation group generated by the group of automorphisms and the group of right multiplications is called the holomorph $\mathfrak{H}$ of the group $\mathfrak{G}$. Show that

(1) $\mathfrak{H}$ contains all the left multiplications,

(2) any element of $\mathfrak{H}$ can be written in one and only one way as a product $\alpha a_r$ of an automorphism $\alpha$ and a right multiplication $a_r$,

(3) if $\mathfrak{G}$ is finite, then the order of $\mathfrak{H}$ is the product of the order of $\mathfrak{G}$ by the order of $\mathfrak{A}$.
