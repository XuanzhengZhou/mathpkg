This theorem identifies the structure of the inner automorphism group of any group. The map $a \mapsto C_a$ is a homomorphism from the group to its automorphism group, and its kernel is exactly the center of the group. By the fundamental theorem of homomorphisms, the inner automorphism group is isomorphic to the quotient of the group by its center.
