If there exists a maximum positive integer $m > 0$ for the orders of the elements of the additive group $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$ of a ring $\mathfrak{A}$, then the number $m$ is called the \text{characteristic} of $\mathfrak{A}$. If no such maximum exists, the characteristic of $\mathfrak{A}$ is said to be $0$.
