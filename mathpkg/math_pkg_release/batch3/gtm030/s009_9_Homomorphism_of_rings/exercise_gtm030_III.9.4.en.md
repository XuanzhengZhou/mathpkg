Prove that, if $a \mapsto \bar{a}$ is a homomorphism of $\mathfrak{R}$ into $\bar{\mathfrak{R}}$, then the mapping
$$(a_{ij}) \mapsto (\bar{a}_{ij})$$
is a homomorphism of the matrix ring $\mathfrak{R}_n$ into $\bar{\mathfrak{R}}_n$.
