The set $\Re_n$ of all $n \times n$ matrices with entries from a ring $\Re$ inherits a ring structure from $\Re$. Matrix addition is defined entrywise, and matrix multiplication follows the usual row-by-column rule. The ring axioms for $\Re_n$—associativity of addition and multiplication, commutativity of addition, and the distributive laws—follow from the corresponding properties in $\Re$.
