Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an integral domain and let $\mathfrak{B}$ be the ring extension constructed in the text (with identity). Show that the totality $\mathfrak{Z}$ of elements $z \in \mathfrak{B}$ such that $za = 0$ for all $a \in \mathfrak{A}$ is an ideal of $\mathfrak{B}$, and that $\mathfrak{B}/\mathfrak{Z}$ is an integral domain with an identity.
