The field of rational numbers $\mathbb{Q}$ (denoted $R_0$) is precisely the field of fractions of the ring of integers $\mathbb{Z}$. Through the general field of fractions construction, a rational number $a/b$ corresponds to the equivalence class of the pair $(a, b)$ with $a, b \in \mathbb{Z}$ and $b \neq 0$, under the relation $(a, b) \sim (c, d) \iff ad = bc$. This is the prototypical example that motivates the general construction for arbitrary commutative integral domains.
