Let $\mathfrak{F}$ be a field and let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a subring $\neq 0$ of $\mathfrak{F}$. Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be the subfield of $\mathfrak{F}$ generated by $\mathfrak{A}$ (i.e., the intersection of all subfields of $\mathfrak{F}$ containing $\mathfrak{A}$). If $\mathfrak{F} = \mathfrak{G}$, then we say that $\mathfrak{F}$ is a \text{minimal field containing} $\mathfrak{A}$. In this case, every element of $\mathfrak{F}$ can be expressed in the form $ab^{-1}$ with $a, b \in \mathfrak{A}$ and $b \neq 0$.
