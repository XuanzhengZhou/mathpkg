Let $\mathfrak{F}$ be a field and let $\mathfrak{A} \neq 0$ be a subring of $\mathfrak{F}$. Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be the subfield of $\mathfrak{F}$ generated by $\mathfrak{A}$. Then

$$\mathfrak{G} = \{ab^{-1} \mid a, b \in \mathfrak{A}, b \neq 0\}.$$

In particular, if $\mathfrak{F} = \mathfrak{G}$, then $\mathfrak{F}$ is a minimal field containing $\mathfrak{A}$ and every element of $\mathfrak{F}$ has the form $ab^{-1}$ with $a, b \in \mathfrak{A}, b \neq 0$.
