Let $B$ be a ring with identity and let $A$ be a subring containing $1$. Suppose $u_1, u_2, \cdots, u_r$ are elements of $B$ that commute with each other and with every element of $A$. Then

$$A[u_1, u_2, \cdots, u_r] = A[u_1][u_2] \cdots [u_r],$$

where $A[u_1][u_2] \cdots [u_r] = ((\cdots(A[u_1])[u_2])\cdots)[u_r]$ denotes iterated adjunction.
