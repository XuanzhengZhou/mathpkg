Let $B$ be a ring with an identity and let $A$ be any subring of $B$ containing $1$. Suppose that $u_1, u_2, \dots, u_r$ are elements of $B$ that commute with each other and that commute with every $a \in A$. Then $A[u_1, u_2, \dots, u_r]$ denotes the subring of $B$ generated by $A$ and by the elements $u_1, u_2, \dots, u_r$.
