Show that a ring $\mathfrak{A}[x_1, x_2, \dots, x_r]$ where the $x_i$ are algebraically independent over $\mathfrak{A}$ can also be obtained as a semigroup ring over $\mathfrak{A}$ of the semigroup $S$ of $r$-tuples $(i_1, i_2, \dots, i_r)$ of non-negative integers $i_j$ where the composition is

$$
(i_1, i_2, \dots, i_r)(j_1, j_2, \dots, j_r) = (i_1 + j_1, i_2 + j_2, \dots, i_r + j_r).
$$
