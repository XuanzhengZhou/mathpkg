If $\mathfrak{J}$ is a left ideal of $\mathfrak{A}$, let $\mathfrak{J}\mathfrak{M}$ denote the set of finite sums $\sum b_i x_i$ where $b_i \in \mathfrak{J}$ and $x_i \in \mathfrak{M}$. Show that $\mathfrak{J}\mathfrak{M}$ is a submodule of $\mathfrak{M}$.
