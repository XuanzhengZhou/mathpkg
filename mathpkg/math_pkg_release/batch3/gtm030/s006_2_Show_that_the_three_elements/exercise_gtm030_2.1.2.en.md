Show that the three elements $0, 1, 2$ constitute a ring if addition and multiplication are defined by the following tables:

$$
\begin{array}{c|ccc}
+ & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
\hline
0 & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
1 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\
2 & 2 & 0 & 1
\end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{c|ccc}
\cdot & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
\hline
0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
2 & 0 & 2 & 1
\end{array}
$$
