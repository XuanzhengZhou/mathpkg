Let $u$ be an element of a ring with an identity that has a right inverse. Prove that the following conditions on $u$ are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $u$ has more than one right inverse,
\item[(2)] $u$ is not a unit,
\item[(3)] $u$ is a left zero-divisor.
\end{enumerate}
