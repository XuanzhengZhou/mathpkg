Let $M$ be any non-vacuous subset of a group $\mathfrak{G}$ and let $\{\mathfrak{H}\}$ be the collection of subgroups of $\mathfrak{G}$ that contain the set $M$. The subgroup generated by $M$, denoted $[M]$, is defined as
$$[M] = \bigcap_{\{\mathfrak{H}\}} \mathfrak{H}.$$
It is the unique smallest subgroup of $\mathfrak{G}$ containing $M$.
