Prove that a semigroup having the following properties is a group:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(a)] $\mathfrak{G}$ has a right identity $1_r$.
  \item[(b)] Every element $a$ of $\mathfrak{G}$ has a right inverse relative to $1_r$.
\end{enumerate}
