The associative law in $\mathfrak{G}$ gives the rule $a_l b_r = b_r a_l$ for all $a, b$ in $\mathfrak{G}$, since
$$x a_l b_r = (ax)b \quad \text{and} \quad x b_r a_l = a(xb).$$
Hence any transformation belonging to the set $\mathfrak{G}_r$ commutes with any transformation belonging to $\mathfrak{G}_l$.
