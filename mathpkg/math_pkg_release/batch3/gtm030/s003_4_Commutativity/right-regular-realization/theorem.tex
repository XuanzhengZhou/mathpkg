The totality $\mathfrak{G}_r = \{a_r \mid a \in \mathfrak{G}\}$ is a transformation group in $\mathfrak{G}$. The mapping $a \to a_r$ is an isomorphism of $\mathfrak{G}$ onto $\mathfrak{G}_r$. This is called the \text{(right) regular realization} of $\mathfrak{G}$ as a transformation group.

In particular, every group is isomorphic to a transformation group.
