Every group $\mathfrak{G}$ is isomorphic to a transformation group. Specifically, the mapping $a \mapsto a_r$, where $a_r$ is the right multiplication $x a_r = xa$, is an isomorphism of $\mathfrak{G}$ onto the group $\mathfrak{G}_r = \{a_r \mid a \in \mathfrak{G}\}$ of transformations of the set $\mathfrak{G}$.
