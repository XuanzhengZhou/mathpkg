Let $\gamma$ and $\gamma'$ be disjoint cycles. If $i$ is not moved by $\gamma$, then $i\gamma' \gamma = i\gamma'$. If $i$ is moved by $\gamma$, then $i$ is not moved by $\gamma'$ (by disjointness), and also $i\gamma$ is not moved by $\gamma'$. Hence $i\gamma\gamma' = i\gamma = i\gamma'\gamma$. Thus in all cases, $i\gamma\gamma' = i\gamma'\gamma$ for every $i$, proving $\gamma\gamma' = \gamma'\gamma$.
