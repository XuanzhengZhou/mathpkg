The correspondence $\mathfrak{W} \to s$ (where $s$ is the smallest positive integer with $a^s \in \mathfrak{W}$) is 1-1 from the set of subgroups $\mathfrak{W} \neq 1$ of a finite cyclic group of order $r$ onto the set of positive divisors of $r$ that are less than $r$. Including the trivial subgroup, there are exactly $d(r)$ subgroups, where $d(r)$ denotes the number of positive divisors of $r$.
