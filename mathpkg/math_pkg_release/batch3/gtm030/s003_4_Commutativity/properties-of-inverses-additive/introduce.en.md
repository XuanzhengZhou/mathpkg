In the additive notation for a group, the inverse of $a$ is denoted $-a$. The basic properties of additive inverses include that the inverse of the inverse is the original element, and that the inverse of a sum is the sum of the inverses in reverse order. The notation $a - b$ is introduced as shorthand for $a + (-b)$.
