The symmetric group $S_n$ of degree $n$ is the group of all permutations of a finite set of $n$ elements. It is one of the most important finite groups, with order $n!$. Every finite group can be embedded in some symmetric group (Cayley's theorem). The symmetric group serves as a fundamental example throughout group theory and has deep connections to combinatorics, representation theory, and Galois theory.
