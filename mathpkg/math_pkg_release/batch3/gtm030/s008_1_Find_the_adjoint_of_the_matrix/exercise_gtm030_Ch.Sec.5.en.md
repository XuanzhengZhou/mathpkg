Define the trace $T(a)$ of $a = \alpha_0 + \alpha_1 i + \alpha_2 j + \alpha_3 k$ to be the number $2\alpha_0$ and the norm $N(a) = \Delta = \alpha_0^2 + \alpha_1^2 + \alpha_2^2 + \alpha_3^2$. Verify that $a$ satisfies the quadratic equation $x^2 - T(a)x + N(a) = 0$.
