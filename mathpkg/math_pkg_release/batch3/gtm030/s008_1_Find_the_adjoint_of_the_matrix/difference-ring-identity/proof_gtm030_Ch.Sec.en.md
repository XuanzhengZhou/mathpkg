If $\mathfrak{A}$ has an identity $1$, then for the coset $\bar{1} = 1 + \mathfrak{B}$:
$$(a + \mathfrak{B})\bar{1} = a \cdot 1 + \mathfrak{B} = a + \mathfrak{B}, \qquad \bar{1}(a + \mathfrak{B}) = 1 \cdot a + \mathfrak{B} = a + \mathfrak{B}.$$
Thus $\bar{1}$ is an identity in $\mathfrak{A}/\mathfrak{B}$.
