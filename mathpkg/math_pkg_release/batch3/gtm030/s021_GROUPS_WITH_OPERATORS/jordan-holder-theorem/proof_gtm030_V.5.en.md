By Schreier's theorem, the two composition series have equivalent refinements. On the other hand, it is clear from the definition of a composition series that a refinement of such a series has the same factors $\neq 1$ as the given series -- any attempt to properly refine would merely insert factors equal to $1$ between terms that are already maximal.

Now in the 1-1 correspondence between the factors of the refinements, the factors $= 1$ are paired. Hence the factors $\neq 1$ are also paired. Since these are precisely the composition factors of the two given composition series, we conclude that the two composition series are equivalent.
