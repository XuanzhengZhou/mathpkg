Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be an $M$-group. A \textbf{normal series} for $\mathfrak{G}$ is a sequence of $M$-subgroups

$$\mathfrak{G} = \mathfrak{G}_1 \supseteq \mathfrak{G}_2 \supseteq \cdots \supseteq \mathfrak{G}_{s+1} = 1$$

such that each $\mathfrak{G}_{i+1}$ is invariant in $\mathfrak{G}_i$. The factor groups

$$\mathfrak{G}_1/\mathfrak{G}_2,\; \mathfrak{G}_2/\mathfrak{G}_3,\; \cdots,\; \mathfrak{G}_s/\mathfrak{G}_{s+1} = \mathfrak{G}_s$$

are the \textbf{factors} of the normal series.
