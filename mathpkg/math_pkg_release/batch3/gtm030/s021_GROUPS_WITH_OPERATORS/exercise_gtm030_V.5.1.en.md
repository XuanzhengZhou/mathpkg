section: "5. Normal series and composition series"
exercise_number: 1
---

Show that, if

$$\mathfrak{G} = \mathfrak{G}_1 \supseteq \mathfrak{G}_2 \supseteq \cdots \supseteq \mathfrak{G}_{s+1} = 1$$

is a normal series for the $M$-group $\mathfrak{G}$ and $\mathfrak{H}$ is any $M$-subgroup of $\mathfrak{G}$, then

$$\mathfrak{H} = (\mathfrak{H} \cap \mathfrak{G}_1) \supseteq (\mathfrak{H} \cap \mathfrak{G}_2) \supseteq \cdots \supseteq (\mathfrak{H} \cap \mathfrak{G}_{s+1}) = 1$$

is a normal series for $\mathfrak{H}$.
