Any factor group of an $M$-group $\mathfrak{G}$ relative to an invariant $M$-subgroup is a homomorphic image of $\mathfrak{G}$. Conversely, if $\mathfrak{G}'$ is an $M$-group which is a homomorphic image of the $M$-group $\mathfrak{G}$, then $\mathfrak{G}'$ is isomorphic to a factor group of $\mathfrak{G}$ relative to an invariant $M$-subgroup.
