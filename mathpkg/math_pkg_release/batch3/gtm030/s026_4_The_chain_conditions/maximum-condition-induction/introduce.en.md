This lemma gives an induction principle for submodules of a module satisfying the maximum condition. If a property $P$ holds for a submodule whenever it holds for all strictly larger submodules, then $P$ holds for every submodule. This is analogous to the second principle of induction for natural numbers and follows directly by considering the collection of submodules for which $P$ is false.
