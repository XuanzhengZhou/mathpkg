The maximum condition for modules states that every nonempty collection of submodules contains a maximal element with respect to inclusion. This condition is equivalent to the ascending chain condition. A module satisfying the maximum condition is Noetherian. The maximum condition implies a useful induction principle for submodules, analogous to the second principle of induction for natural numbers.
