The minimum condition for modules states that every nonempty collection of submodules contains a minimal element with respect to inclusion. This condition is equivalent to the descending chain condition (using the axiom of choice). The minimum condition provides an alternative, set-theoretic formulation of the Artinian property that is often more convenient in proofs involving the existence of minimal submodules with certain properties.
