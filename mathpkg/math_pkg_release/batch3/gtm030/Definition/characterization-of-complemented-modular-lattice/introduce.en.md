This theorem provides a complete characterization of complemented modular lattices satisfying the ascending and descending chain conditions. Such lattices are precisely those whose top element can be expressed as the join of finitely many independent atoms (points). The forward direction uses the chain conditions to build a maximal independent set of points, while the converse direction constructs complements algorithmically from a generating set of points.
