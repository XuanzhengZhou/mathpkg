The following two types of abstract systems are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Boolean algebra $(B, \cup, \cap, ')$.
\item Boolean ring with identity $(\mathfrak{B}, +, \cdot)$.
\end{enumerate}

The correspondence is given by:
\begin{align*}
a + b &= (a \cap b') \cup (a' \cap b) \quad \text{(symmetric difference)}, \\
a \cdot b &= a \cap b, \\
a \cup b &= a + b - ab, \\
a \cap b &= ab, \\
a' &= 1 - a.
\end{align*}
