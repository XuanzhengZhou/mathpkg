Let $\eta$ be a homomorphism of a ring $\mathfrak{A}$ into itself. Show that the elements of $\mathfrak{A}$ that are fixed relative to $\eta$ (i.e., $a\eta = a$) form a subring of $\mathfrak{A}$. If $\mathfrak{A}$ is a division ring and $\mathfrak{A}\eta \neq 0$, then the set of fixed elements constitutes a division subring.
