A relative complement generalizes the notion of complement to an interval $[0, a]$ in a lattice. Given elements $b \leq a$, a relative complement of $b$ with respect to $a$ is an element $b_1$ also below $a$ that serves as a complement of $b$ within the sublattice $[0, a]$ (i.e., treating $a$ as the top element). In modular complemented lattices, relative complements always exist whenever $b \leq a$.
