Show that, if $L$ satisfies the chain conditions, then the elements $a_1, a_2, \dots, a_n$ are independent if and only if
$$l(a_1 \cup a_2 \cup \dots \cup a_n) = l(a_1) + l(a_2) + \dots + l(a_n),$$
where $l(x)$ denotes the length of the element $x$ in the lattice.
