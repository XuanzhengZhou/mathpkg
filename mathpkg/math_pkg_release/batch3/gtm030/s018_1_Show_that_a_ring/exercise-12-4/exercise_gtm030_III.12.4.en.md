Show that any polynomial in $\mathfrak{F}[x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_r]$ can be written in the form

$$\sum_{i=1}^{r} g_i(x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_r)(x_i^q - x_i) + g_0(x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_r)$$

where $g_0$ is of degree $< q$ in each $x_i$.
