Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a subring of a ring $\mathfrak{A}$ and let $\mathfrak{G}$ be the ring of all elements of $\mathfrak{A}$ that are integral over $\mathfrak{g}$. Then $\mathfrak{G}$ is integrally closed in $\mathfrak{A}$; that is, every element of $\mathfrak{A}$ that is integral over $\mathfrak{G}$ already belongs to $\mathfrak{G}$.
