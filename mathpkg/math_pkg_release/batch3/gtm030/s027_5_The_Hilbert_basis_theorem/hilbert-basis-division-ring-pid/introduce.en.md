This corollary specializes the Hilbert Basis Theorem to two important classes of rings. A division ring trivially satisfies the ascending chain condition (its only left ideals are $0$ and the whole ring). A principal ideal domain satisfies the ascending chain condition because every ideal is generated by a single element. In either case, the polynomial ring in finitely many variables $\mathfrak{A}[x_1, \dots, x_r]$ is also left (and right) Noetherian.
