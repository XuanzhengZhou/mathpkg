Gauss's Lemma states that the product of primitive polynomials remains primitive. This result is the cornerstone for proving that if a domain is a UFD, then so is its polynomial ring. It connects irreducibility in $\mathfrak{A}[x]$ with irreducibility in the polynomial ring over the field of fractions.
