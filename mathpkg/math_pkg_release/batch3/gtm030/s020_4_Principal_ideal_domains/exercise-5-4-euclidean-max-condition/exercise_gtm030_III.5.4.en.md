Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a Euclidean domain whose Euclidean function $\delta$ satisfies the condition $\delta(a + b) \leq \max(\delta(a), \delta(b))$ for all $a, b \in \mathfrak{A}$. Show that $\mathfrak{A}$ is either a field or a polynomial domain $\mathfrak{F}[x]$ over a field $\mathfrak{F}$.
