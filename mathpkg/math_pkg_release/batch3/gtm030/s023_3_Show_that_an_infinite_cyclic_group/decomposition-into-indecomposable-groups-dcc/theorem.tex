Any $M$-group $\mathfrak{G} \neq 1$ that satisfies the descending chain condition for invariant $M$-subgroups can be expressed as a direct product of a finite number of indecomposable groups $\neq 1$:

$$\mathfrak{G} = \mathfrak{G}_1 \times \mathfrak{G}_2 \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{G}_n$$

where each $\mathfrak{G}_i$ is indecomposable.
