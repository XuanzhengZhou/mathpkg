If $\mathfrak{G}$ is a finite cyclic group of order $n = p_1^{e_1} p_2^{e_2} \cdots p_s^{e_s}$ where the $p_i$ are distinct primes, then $\mathfrak{G}$ is a direct product of cyclic groups of orders $p_i^{e_i}$:

$$\mathfrak{G} \cong C_{p_1^{e_1}} \times C_{p_2^{e_2}} \times \cdots \times C_{p_s^{e_s}}.$$
