Let $\mathfrak{G}_1, \mathfrak{G}_2, \cdots, \mathfrak{G}_n$ be $M$-groups. The \text{direct product} $\mathfrak{G} = \mathfrak{G}_1 \times \mathfrak{G}_2 \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{G}_n$ is the product set with componentwise composition

$$(a_1, \cdots, a_n)(b_1, \cdots, b_n) = (a_1b_1, \cdots, a_nb_n)$$

and operator action $(a_1, \cdots, a_n)m = (a_1m, \cdots, a_nm)$. When written additively, it is called the \text{direct sum} $\mathfrak{G}_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{G}_n$.
