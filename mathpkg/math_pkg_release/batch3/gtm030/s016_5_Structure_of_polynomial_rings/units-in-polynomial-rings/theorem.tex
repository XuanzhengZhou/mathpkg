If $\mathfrak{A}$ is an integral domain, then the only units in $\mathfrak{A}[x]$ are the elements of $\mathfrak{A}$ that are units in $\mathfrak{A}$. In particular, if $I$ is the ring of integers, the only units in $I[x]$ are $\pm 1$. If $\mathfrak{J}$ is a field, the units of $\mathfrak{J}[x]$ are the non-zero constant polynomials (i.e., the non-zero elements of $\mathfrak{J}$).
