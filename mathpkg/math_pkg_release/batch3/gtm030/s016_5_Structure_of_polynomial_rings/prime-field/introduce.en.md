Every field contains a unique smallest subfield called its prime field, defined as the intersection of all subfields. The structure of the prime field determines the characteristic of the field. The prime field is either isomorphic to the rational numbers $\mathbb{Q}$ (characteristic $0$) or to the finite field $\mathbb{Z}/(p)$ for a prime $p$ (characteristic $p$).
