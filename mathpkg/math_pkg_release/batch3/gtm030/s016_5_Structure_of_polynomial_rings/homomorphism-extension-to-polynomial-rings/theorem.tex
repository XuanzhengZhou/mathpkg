Let $\mathfrak{A}_1[x]$ be a ring of polynomials in a transcendental element $x$ and let $\mathfrak{A}_2[u]$ be a ring of polynomials in an arbitrary $u$. Suppose that $\sigma$ is a homomorphism of $\mathfrak{A}_1$ onto $\mathfrak{A}_2$. Then $\sigma$ can be extended in one and only one way to a homomorphism $\Sigma$ of $\mathfrak{A}_1[x]$ onto $\mathfrak{A}_2[u]$ mapping $x$ into $u$.
