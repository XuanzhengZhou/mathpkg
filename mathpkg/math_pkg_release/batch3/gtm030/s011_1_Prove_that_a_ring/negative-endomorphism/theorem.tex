For an endomorphism $\eta$ of a commutative group $\mathfrak{G}$, the \textbf{negative endomorphism} $-\eta$ is defined by

$$a(-\eta) = -(a\eta)$$

for all $a \in \mathfrak{G}$. The mapping $-\eta$ is an endomorphism of $\mathfrak{G}$ and satisfies $\eta + (-\eta) = 0$, where $0$ is the zero endomorphism.
