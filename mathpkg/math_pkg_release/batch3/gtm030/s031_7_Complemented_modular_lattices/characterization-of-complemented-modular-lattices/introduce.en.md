Theorem 10 provides a structural characterization of complemented modular lattices with chain conditions. In the forward direction, using the ascending chain condition and the existence of relative complements, one can construct a maximal independent set of points whose join is $1$. Conversely, if $1$ is a l.u.b. of finitely many points in a modular lattice with $0$ and $1$, then every element has a complement (constructed by an independence argument) and both chain conditions are satisfied. This theorem reveals the intrinsic finiteness structure underlying complemented modular lattices with chain conditions.
