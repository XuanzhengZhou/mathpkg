A relative complement is a generalization of the notion of complement to an arbitrary interval $[0, a]$ in a lattice. Given elements $b \leq a$, a relative complement $b_1$ of $b$ in $a$ satisfies $b \cup b_1 = a$ and $b \cap b_1 = 0$, with the additional condition $b_1 \leq a$. Thus, the element $a$ plays the role of the unit in the sublattice $[0, a]$. The existence of relative complements is closely tied to the property of modularity combined with complementedness.
