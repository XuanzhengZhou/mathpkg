The ring of left multiplications $\mathfrak{A}_l$ is the image of the anti-homomorphism $a \mapsto a_l$. Even though the map reverses multiplication, its image is still a subring of $\mathfrak{E}$. For rings with identity, the kernel of this anti-homomorphism is trivial, making $a \mapsto a_l$ an anti-isomorphism between $\mathfrak{A}$ and $\mathfrak{A}_l$.
