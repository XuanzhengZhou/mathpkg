The left multiplication $a_l$ in a ring $\mathfrak{A}$ is the map sending $x$ to $ax$. Unlike right multiplications which form a homomorphism, left multiplications form an anti-homomorphism into the endomorphism ring of the additive group, reflecting the asymmetry between left and right multiplication in a non-commutative ring.
