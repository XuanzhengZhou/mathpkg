The ring of right multiplications $\mathfrak{A}_r$ is the image of the ring under the homomorphism $a \mapsto a_r$ into the endomorphism ring of the additive group. As a homomorphic image, it is always a subring of the endomorphism ring. This construction embeds the multiplicative structure of $\mathfrak{A}$ into the endomorphism ring of its additive group.
