Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be any ring and let $\mathfrak{E}$ be the ring of endomorphisms of the additive group $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$. The correspondence $a \mapsto a_r$ from $\mathfrak{A}$ to $\mathfrak{E}$ satisfies, for all $x \in \mathfrak{A}$,

$$x(a + b)_r = x(a + b) = xa + xb = xa_r + xb_r = x(a_r + b_r)$$

and

$$x(ab)_r = x(ab) = (xa)b = (xa_r)b_r = x(a_r b_r).$$

Hence

$$(a + b)_r = a_r + b_r, \qquad (ab)_r = a_r b_r.$$

Thus $a \mapsto a_r$ is a homomorphism of the ring $\mathfrak{A}$ into the endomorphism ring $\mathfrak{E}$.
