Let $\mathfrak{A}_i$, $i = 1, 2$, be a subring $\neq 0$ of the field $\mathfrak{F}_i$ and suppose that $\mathfrak{F}_i$ is the smallest subfield of $\mathfrak{F}_i$ containing $\mathfrak{A}_i$. Then if $\sigma$ is an isomorphism of $\mathfrak{A}_1$ onto $\mathfrak{A}_2$, $\sigma$ can be extended in one and only one way to an isomorphism of $\mathfrak{F}_1$ onto $\mathfrak{F}_2$.
