When $\mathfrak{G}$ is a commutative $M$-group, every $M$-subgroup is automatically invariant and every subgroup is a term of a normal series. The descending chain condition therefore simplifies to the statement that every descending sequence of $M$-subgroups must stabilize. No invariance conditions need be imposed on the terms of the chain.
