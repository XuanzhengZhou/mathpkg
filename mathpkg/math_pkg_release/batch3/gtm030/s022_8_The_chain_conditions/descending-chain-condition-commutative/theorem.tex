Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be a commutative $M$-group. If
\[
\mathfrak{G}_1 \supseteq \mathfrak{G}_2 \supseteq \mathfrak{G}_3 \supseteq \cdots
\]
is a descending sequence of $M$-subgroups of $\mathfrak{G}$, then there exists a positive integer $N$ such that
\[
\mathfrak{G}_N = \mathfrak{G}_{N+1} = \cdots.
\]
