Let $\mathfrak{Q}$ be an arbitrary subring of a field $K$ and let $\mathfrak{p}$ be a given prime ideal in $\mathfrak{Q}$, different from $\mathfrak{Q}$. Let $\mathfrak{Q}$ be the quotient ring of $\mathfrak{Q}$ with respect to $\mathfrak{p}$. If $N$ denotes the set of all valuation rings $R$ in $K$ which belong to places $\mathfrak{P}$ of $K$ having center $\mathfrak{p}$ in $\mathfrak{Q}$, then

$$\bigcap_{R \in N} R = \text{integral closure of } \mathfrak{Q} \text{ in } K.$$
