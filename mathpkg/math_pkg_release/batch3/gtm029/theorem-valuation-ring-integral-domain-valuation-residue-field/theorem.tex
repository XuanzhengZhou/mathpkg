The quotients $X_i/X_n$, $i=1, 2, \cdots, n-1$, belong to the valuation ring of the $\mathfrak{x}$-adic valuation $\mathbf{o}$. If $t_i$ denotes the residue of $X_i/X_n$ in the valuation $\mathbf{o}$, then $t_1, t_2, \cdots, t_{n-1}$ are algebraically independent over $A$, and the residue field of $\mathbf{o}$ is $k(t_1, t_2, \cdots, t_{n-1})$, where $k$ is the quotient field of $A$ ($A$, an integral domain).
