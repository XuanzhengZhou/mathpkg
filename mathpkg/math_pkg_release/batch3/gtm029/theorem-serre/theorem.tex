(Hilbert-Serre). Let $A$ be a ring satisfying the descending chain condition (d.c.c.), $M$ a finitely generated graded module over $R = A[X_1, \cdots, X_n]$ and $M = \sum M_q$ the direct sum decomposition of $M$. Then $M_q$, considered as an $A$-module, has a finite length $\varphi_M(q)$. For sufficiently large $q$, the function $\varphi_M(q)$ is a polynomial in $q$ whose degree is at most $n - 1$.
