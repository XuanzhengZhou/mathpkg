The completion of a noetherian ring (with respect to any ideal for which it is Hausdorff) is again noetherian. This follows because $G(A)$ is noetherian and $G(\hat{A}) \cong G(A)$, so $G(\hat{A})$ is noetherian, and Corollary 3 to Theorem 7 applied to $\hat{A}$ yields the result.
