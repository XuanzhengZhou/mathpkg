Since $A$ is noetherian and Hausdorff, $G(A)$ is noetherian (proved in $\S$1). By Corollary 1 to Theorem 6 ($\S$2), $G(\hat{A}) \cong G(A)$, so $G(\hat{A})$ is noetherian. Applying Corollary 3 to Theorem 7 with $E = \hat{A}$ (which is complete and Hausdorff in its $\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}$-topology) yields that $\hat{A}$ is noetherian.
