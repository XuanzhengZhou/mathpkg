Since the $\bar{\mathfrak{m}}_i$ generate $G(A)$ over $A/\mathfrak{m}$, the natural map $(A/\mathfrak{m})[x_1,\ldots,x_q] \to G(A)$ sending $x_i \mapsto \bar{\mathfrak{m}}_i$ is a surjective homomorphism of graded rings. Its kernel $\mathfrak{I}$ is a homogeneous ideal, giving the isomorphism $G(A) \cong (A/\mathfrak{m})[x_1,\ldots,x_q]/\mathfrak{I}$. If $A/\mathfrak{m}$ is noetherian and $\mathfrak{m}$ is finitely generated, then the polynomial ring $(A/\mathfrak{m})[x_1,\ldots,x_q]$ is noetherian (Hilbert basis theorem), hence its quotient $G(A)$ is noetherian.
