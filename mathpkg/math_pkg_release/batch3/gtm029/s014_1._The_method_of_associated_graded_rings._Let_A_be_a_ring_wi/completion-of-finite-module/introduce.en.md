Theorem 5 establishes that for a finite module over a noetherian ring, the completion $\hat{E}$ is naturally isomorphic to the tensor product $E \otimes_A \hat{A}$, and is a finite $\hat{A}$-module. The original generators of $E$ over $A$ also generate $\hat{E}$ over $\hat{A}$, showing that completion preserves the finiteness property.
