Let $A$ be a noetherian ring, $E$ a finite $A$-module, $F$ a submodule of $E$, and $\mathfrak{m}$ an ideal in $A$. There exists an integer $k$, depending only on $A$, $E$, $F$ and $\mathfrak{m}$, such that

$$\mathfrak{m}^n E \cap F = \mathfrak{m}^{n-k}(\mathfrak{m}^k E \cap F) \quad \text{for every } n \geq k.$$
