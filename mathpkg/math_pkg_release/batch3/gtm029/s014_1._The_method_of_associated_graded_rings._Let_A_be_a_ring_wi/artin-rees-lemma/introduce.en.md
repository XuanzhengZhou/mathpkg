The Artin-Rees Lemma is a foundational result in commutative algebra stating that for a noetherian ring, the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic filtration on a finite module restricts to a filtration on any submodule that is equivalent to the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic filtration of the submodule. It implies that the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology on a submodule coincides with the induced topology (Theorem 4).
