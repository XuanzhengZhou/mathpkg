The associated graded module $G(E)$ of an $A$-module $E$ with respect to an ideal $\mathfrak{m}$ is the direct sum of the successive quotients $\mathfrak{m}^n E / \mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E$. This construction provides a graded module over the associated graded ring $G(A)$, capturing the leading-order behavior of elements of $E$ under the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic filtration.
