Let $A$ be a ring with element $1$, $\mathfrak{m}$ an ideal in $A$ ($\mathfrak{m} \neq A$) and $E$ an $A$-module. The modules $\mathfrak{m}^n E$ form a descending sequence of submodules of $E$. We consider the direct sum

$$G(E) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \mathfrak{m}^n E / \mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E.$$

This is a graded abelian group, the elements of $\mathfrak{m}^n E / \mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E$ being considered as homogeneous elements of degree $n$.
