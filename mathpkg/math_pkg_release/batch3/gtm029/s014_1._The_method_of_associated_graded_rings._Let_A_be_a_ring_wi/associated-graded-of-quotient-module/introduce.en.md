The associated graded module of a quotient $E/F$ is canonically isomorphic to the quotient of $G(E)$ by the leading submodule of $F$. This fundamental result (Theorem 1) establishes the close relationship between submodules and their leading submodules, enabling the use of graded techniques to study filtered objects.
