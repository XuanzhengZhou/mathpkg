Let $E$ admit a finite basis $\{e_1, \cdots, e_r\}$ over $A$. Then $\mathfrak{m}^n E$ is generated by the products $b_\lambda e_j$ where the $b_\lambda$ are the monomials of degree $n$ in $\mathfrak{m}_1, \cdots, \mathfrak{m}_q$. Therefore $G(E)$, considered as a $G(A)$-module, is generated by the residue classes $\bar{e}_1, \cdots, \bar{e}_r$ modulo $\mathfrak{m}E$, and is therefore a finite $G(A)$-module.
