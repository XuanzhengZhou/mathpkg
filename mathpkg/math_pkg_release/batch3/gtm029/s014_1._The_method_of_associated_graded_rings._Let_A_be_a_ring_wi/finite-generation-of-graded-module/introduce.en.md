If an $A$-module $E$ has a finite basis, then its associated graded module $G(E)$ is finitely generated as a module over the associated graded ring $G(A)$. The generators are the residue classes of the basis elements modulo $\mathfrak{m}E$, and this finiteness property is crucial for transferring properties from the graded setting back to the original module.
