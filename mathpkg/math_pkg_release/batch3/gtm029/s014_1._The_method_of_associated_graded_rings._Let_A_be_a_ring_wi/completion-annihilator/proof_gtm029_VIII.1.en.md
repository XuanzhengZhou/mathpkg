Apply Corollary 3 to Theorem 11 with $F = E$, $F' = G$, and $f$ being the multiplication map $x \mapsto ax$ from $E$ to $E$. The exactness of completion for this map yields $\hat{A}(G :_E a) = \hat{A}G :_{\hat{E}} a$.
