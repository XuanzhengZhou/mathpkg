Theorem 4 (a consequence of the Artin-Rees Lemma) states that for a noetherian ring and a finite module, the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology on a submodule coincides with the induced topology from the ambient module. This result is fundamental for the theory of completions, ensuring that the completion functor is exact on finite modules over noetherian rings.
