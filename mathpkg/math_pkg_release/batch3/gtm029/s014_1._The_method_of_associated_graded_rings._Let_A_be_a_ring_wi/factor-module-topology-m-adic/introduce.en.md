The factor module $E/F$ carries a natural quotient topology making the canonical projection both open and continuous. The neighborhoods of zero in $E/F$ are the images of the neighborhoods of zero in $E$, which in the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic case are the submodules $\mathfrak{m}^n(E/F) = (\mathfrak{m}^n E + F)/F$.
