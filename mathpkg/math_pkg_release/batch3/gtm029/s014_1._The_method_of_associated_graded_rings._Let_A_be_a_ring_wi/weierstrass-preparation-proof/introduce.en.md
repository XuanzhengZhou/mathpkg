The Weierstrass preparation theorem admits a second proof using the method of associated graded rings, specifically Theorem 7 (generation by initial forms). The proof applies Theorem 7, Corollary 2, to show that $\{1, X_n, \ldots, X_n^{s-1}\}$ forms a basis for $R$ over the subring $K[[X_1,\ldots,X_{n-1},F]]$. This elegant application demonstrates the power of the associated graded ring method.
