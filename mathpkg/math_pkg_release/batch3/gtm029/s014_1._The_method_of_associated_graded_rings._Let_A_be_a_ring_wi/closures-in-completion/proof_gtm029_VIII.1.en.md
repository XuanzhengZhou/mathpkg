Since $\mathfrak{m}^s \mathfrak{m}^n E = \mathfrak{m}^{n+s}E \cap \mathfrak{m}^n E$ for every $s$, the $\mathfrak{m}$-topology of $\mathfrak{m}^n E$ is induced by the $\mathfrak{m}$-topology of $E$. Thus the closure of $\mathfrak{m}^n E$ in $\hat{E}$ may be identified with the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic completion of $\mathfrak{m}^n E$. Since $\mathfrak{m}^n E$ is a finite $A$-module, Theorem 5 shows this completion is $\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}^n E = (\hat{A}\mathfrak{m})^n E$. The remaining statements follow from standard topological facts about metric completions.
