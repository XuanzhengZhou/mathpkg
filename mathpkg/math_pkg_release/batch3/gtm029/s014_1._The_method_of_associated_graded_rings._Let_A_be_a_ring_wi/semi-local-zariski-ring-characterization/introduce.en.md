A Zariski ring $A$ is semi-local if and only if $A/\mathfrak{m}$ is Artinian (satisfies the descending chain condition), and $A$ is local if and only if $A/\mathfrak{m}$ is Artinian with a unique prime ideal. This characterizes the structure of the residue ring in terms of chain conditions on the quotient.
