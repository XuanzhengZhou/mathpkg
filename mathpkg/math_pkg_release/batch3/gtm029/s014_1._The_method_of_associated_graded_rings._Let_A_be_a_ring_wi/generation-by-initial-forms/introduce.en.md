Theorem 7 establishes a fundamental bridge between a submodule and its leading submodule: if the initial forms of a finite set of elements generate the leading submodule in the associated graded module, then those elements themselves generate the original submodule, provided the ring and module are complete and Hausdorff. This is the key tool for proving finiteness and noetherianity results via associated graded objects.
