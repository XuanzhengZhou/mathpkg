The order function satisfies fundamental properties analogous to a non-archimedean valuation: the order of a sum is at least the minimum of the orders of the summands (ultrametric inequality), and the order of a product is at least the sum of the orders. These properties underlie the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology and the distance function used for completions.
