Let $A$ be a Zariski ring with respect to an ideal $\mathfrak{m}$, and let

$$0 \to F \xrightarrow{f} E \xrightarrow{g} G \to 0$$

be an exact sequence of finite $A$-modules. Then the completed sequence

$$0 \to \hat{F} \xrightarrow{\hat{f}} \hat{E} \xrightarrow{\hat{g}} \hat{G} \to 0$$

is exact, where completions are taken with respect to the $\mathfrak{m}$-topologies.
