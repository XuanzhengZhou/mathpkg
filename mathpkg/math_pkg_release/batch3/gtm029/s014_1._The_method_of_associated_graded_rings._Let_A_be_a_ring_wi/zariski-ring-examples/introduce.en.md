Key examples of Zariski rings include: noetherian local rings (with respect to the maximal ideal), noetherian semi-local rings (with respect to the intersection of maximal ideals), complete Hausdorff noetherian rings, and quotient rings of Zariski rings. These examples cover the most important cases in commutative algebra.
