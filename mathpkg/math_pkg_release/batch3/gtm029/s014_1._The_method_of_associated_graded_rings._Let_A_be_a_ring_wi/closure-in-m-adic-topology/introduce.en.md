In the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology, the closure of a subset $S$ of a module $E$ is the intersection of the sets $S + \mathfrak{m}^n E$ over all $n$. This explicit description of the closure operation is a consequence of the fact that the $\mathfrak{m}^n E$ form a basis of neighborhoods of zero.
