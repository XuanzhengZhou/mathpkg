The leading submodule of a submodule $F$ of $E$ is the homogeneous submodule of $G(E)$ generated by the initial forms (lowest-degree nonzero homogeneous components) of elements of $F$. It plays a role analogous to the leading terms in a polynomial ideal and is essential for applying graded ring techniques to filtrations.
