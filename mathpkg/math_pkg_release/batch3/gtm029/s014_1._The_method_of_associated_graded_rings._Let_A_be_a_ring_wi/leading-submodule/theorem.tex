Let $F$ be a submodule of $E$. The homogeneous submodule of $G(E)$ generated by the initial forms of the elements of $F$ is

$$\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(\mathfrak{m}^n E) \cap (\mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E + F)}{\mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E}.$$

This submodule is called the leading submodule of $F$ in $G(E)$. It is the homogeneous submodule of $G(E)$ consisting of the initial forms of elements of $F$.
