Completion does not change the associated graded objects: the associated graded ring (resp. module) of a ring (resp. module) is canonically isomorphic to the associated graded of its completion. This is because the quotients $\mathfrak{m}^n/\mathfrak{m}^{n+1}$ carry the discrete topology and are unchanged by completion.
