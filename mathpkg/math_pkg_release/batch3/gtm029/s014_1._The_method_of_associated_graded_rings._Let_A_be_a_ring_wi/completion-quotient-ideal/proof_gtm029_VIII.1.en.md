Consider the multiplication map $f: F \to E/F'$ given by the composition $F \hookrightarrow E \twoheadrightarrow E/F'$. Applying the exactness of completion (Theorem 11) to the exact sequence $0 \to F/F' \to E/F' \to E/F \to 0$ and using $\hat{A}(F/F') = \hat{A}F/\hat{A}F'$ yields $\hat{A}F/\hat{A}F' = \hat{A}E/\hat{A}F' : \hat{A}E/\hat{A}F$. Simplifying gives the result.
