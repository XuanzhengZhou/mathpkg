By Corollary 4 (or 5) to Theorem 11, $(0) :_{\hat{A}} c = \hat{A}((0) :_A c)$. If $c$ is not a zero divisor in $A$, then $(0) :_A c = (0)$, so $(0) :_{\hat{A}} c = \hat{A}(0) = (0)$. Thus $c$ is not a zero divisor in $\hat{A}$.
