If the fiber $E/\mathfrak{m}E$ is finitely generated over $A/\mathfrak{m}$ and $\mathfrak{m}$ is finitely generated, then $E$ itself is finitely generated over $A$. This corollary follows because the initial forms of generators of $E/\mathfrak{m}E$ generate $G(E)$ over $G(A)$.
