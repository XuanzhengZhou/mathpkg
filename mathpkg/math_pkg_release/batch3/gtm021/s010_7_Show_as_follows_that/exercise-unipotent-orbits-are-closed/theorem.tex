Let G be a unipotent algebraic group acting on an affine variety X. Prove that all G-orbits are closed in X. In particular, all conjugacy classes of G are closed. [Hint: Consider the action of G on K[X] by translations. If Y is a nonclosed orbit, find f in K[X] vanishing on Y-bar minus Y but not on Y. The G-translates of f span a finite dimensional G-stable space of functions vanishing on Y-bar minus Y. Apply Theorem 17.5.]
