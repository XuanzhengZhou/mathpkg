A connected algebraic group  \neq e$ is reductive if its unipotent radical (G)$ is trivial. This class includes $\text{GL}(n, \mathbb{K})$, all tori, and all semisimple groups. The quotient /R_u(G)$ of any connected algebraic group is reductive.
