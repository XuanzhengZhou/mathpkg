From conjugacy of Borel subgroups, Z(G)^circ subset Z(B) subset Z(G) (corollary of 21.4A). If x in Z(G), Theorem 22.2 shows x lies in some Borel, hence in all Borel subgroups by conjugacy. Therefore x in Z(B) for each B.
