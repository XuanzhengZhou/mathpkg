Define W = {x in N_G(H) | m(s,x)=0 and c(Ad x|h, T) = c(Ad s|h, T)}. W is a closed subset of N_G(H), stable under H-conjugation. The first condition requires each x in W satisfy the minimal polynomial of s, which has no multiple roots; thus every element of W is semisimple. Since s in W and W is H-stable, Cl_H(s) is contained in W. But W is closed and Cl_H(s) is constructible; since Cl_H(s) is dense in its closure and W is closed, Cl_H(s) = W intersect H, which is closed in H.
