Let $A, B$ be closed subgroups of the algebraic group $G$, and let $C$ be the closure of the group $(A, B)$ generated by commutators. Then $\mathfrak{c}$ contains all elements
$$\mathbf{y} - \operatorname{Ad} x(\mathbf{y}), \quad \mathbf{x} - \operatorname{Ad} y(\mathbf{x}), \quad [\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{y}]$$
for $x \in A$, $\mathbf{x} \in \mathfrak{a}$, $y \in B$, $\mathbf{y} \in \mathfrak{b}$.
