If $\mathrm{char}\ \mathbb{K} = 0$, $B$ a Borel subgroup of $G$, then $\mathfrak{b}$ is a maximal solvable subalgebra of $\mathfrak{g}$, $\mathfrak{n}_\mathfrak{g}(\mathfrak{b}) = \mathfrak{b}$, and $N_G(\mathfrak{b}) = B$.
