Let $G$ be a reductive group of semisimple rank 1, $B$ a Borel subgroup containing a maximal torus $T$, $U = B_u$, and let $\sigma$ be the nontrivial element of the Weyl group. Then each element of $G$ can be written uniquely as $u b$ or $u \sigma t u'$, where $u, u' \in U$, $b \in B$, and $t \in T$.
