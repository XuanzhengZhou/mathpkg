Let $x^{-1}Px = Q$. Then $B$ and $x^{-1}Bx$ are two Borel subgroups of the connected group $Q$, so there exists $y \in Q$ such that $B = y^{-1}x^{-1}Bxy$. Therefore $xy \in N_G(B) = B$, forcing $x \in Q$ and $P = Q$.
