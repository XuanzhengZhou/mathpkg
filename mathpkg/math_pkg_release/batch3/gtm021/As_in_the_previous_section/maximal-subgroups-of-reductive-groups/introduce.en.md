This theorem classifies maximal closed subgroups of a reductive group: they are either reductive (modulo connected components) or parabolic. It also shows that every unipotent subgroup is contained in a Borel subgroup, so the maximal unipotent subgroups are exactly the unipotent radicals of the Borel subgroups.
