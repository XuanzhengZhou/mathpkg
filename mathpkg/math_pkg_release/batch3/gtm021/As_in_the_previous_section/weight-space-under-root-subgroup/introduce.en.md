This proposition describes how root subgroups act on weight spaces: applying a root subgroup $U_\alpha$ to a weight vector of weight $\chi$ produces vectors whose weights are $\chi$ plus nonnegative integer multiples of $\alpha$. This is the algebraic group analogue of the raising operator in Lie algebra representation theory.
