The Levi decomposition expresses a parabolic subgroup as the semidirect product of a reductive Levi factor and its unipotent radical. This generalizes the decomposition of an upper triangular block matrix group into its diagonal (Levi) and strictly upper triangular (unipotent radical) parts. Levi factors are unique up to conjugation by the unipotent radical.
