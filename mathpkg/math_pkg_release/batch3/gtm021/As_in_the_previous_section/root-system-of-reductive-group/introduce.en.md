This corollary collects the essential properties of the root system of a reductive group: the root system is symmetric ($\Phi = -\Phi$), root spaces are 1-dimensional, singular tori correspond to root kernels, the groups $Z_\alpha$ have semisimple rank 1 and generate $G$, the center is the intersection of root kernels, and the root lattice rank equals the semisimple rank.
