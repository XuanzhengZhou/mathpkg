Let $U$ be the unipotent radical of a Borel subgroup $B$ of a reductive group $G$, with maximal torus $T \subset B$. Let $H$ be a closed, $T$-stable subgroup of $U$. Then $H$ is connected and is directly spanned by those $U_\alpha$ for which $\mathfrak{g}_\alpha$ lies in $\mathfrak{h}$ (taken in any order).
