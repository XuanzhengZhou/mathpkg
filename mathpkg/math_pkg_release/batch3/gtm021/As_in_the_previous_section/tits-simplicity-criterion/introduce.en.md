This theorem provides a powerful simplicity criterion for groups with a Tits system. If the Weyl group is irreducible, $G$ is generated by conjugates of a solvable normal subgroup of $B$, and $G$ is perfect, then $G/Z$ is simple. This applies in particular to simple algebraic groups.
