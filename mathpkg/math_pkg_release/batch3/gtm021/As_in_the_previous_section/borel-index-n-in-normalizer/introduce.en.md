This lemma states that a Borel subgroup $B$ of a connected algebraic group $G$ coincides with the identity component of its own normalizer. Equivalently, the normalizer $N_G(B)$ has the same identity component as $B$, so the quotient $N_G(B)/B$ is finite. It is a key step toward proving that $B$ equals its full normalizer.
