This structure theorem provides a complete description of semisimple algebraic groups. They are almost direct products of almost simple groups (the connected normal subgroups that are minimal). Every closed connected normal subgroup is a product of some of these minimal factors. The group is perfect (equal to its derived group) and generated by its unipotent root subgroups.
