Let $G$ be a semisimple algebraic group.
(a) $G$ has finitely many minimal closed connected normal subgroups $G_1, \ldots, G_n$, which are almost simple.
(b) $G = G_1 \cdots G_n$, the product being almost direct.
(c) Every closed connected normal subgroup of $G$ is the product of certain $G_i$.
(d) $(G, G) = G$.
(e) $G$ is generated by its unipotent subgroups $U_\alpha$.
