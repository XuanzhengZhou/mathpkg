This theorem describes the structure of parabolic subgroups in a reductive group. Each parabolic subgroup is conjugate to a unique standard parabolic $P_I$, and its Lie algebra consists of the Cartan subalgebra, all positive root spaces, and those negative root spaces that are linear combinations of simple roots in $I$.
