Let $G$ be a reductive group, $T$ a maximal torus, $B$ a Borel subgroup containing $T$, and $\Delta$ the corresponding base of $\Phi$.
(a) Each parabolic subgroup of $G$ is conjugate to one and only one subgroup $P_I = B W_I B$, where $I \subset \Delta$.
(b) The roots of $P_I$ relative to $T$ are those in $\Phi^+$ along with those roots in $\Phi^{-}$ which are $\mathbb{Z}$-linear combinations of $I$.
