This theorem states that a reductive group is generated by the centralizers $Z_\alpha$ of the singular tori corresponding to simple roots. Equivalently, $G$ is generated by the maximal torus together with the root subgroups for simple roots and their negatives. This is a crucial reduction step: the whole group is built from rank-1 pieces.
