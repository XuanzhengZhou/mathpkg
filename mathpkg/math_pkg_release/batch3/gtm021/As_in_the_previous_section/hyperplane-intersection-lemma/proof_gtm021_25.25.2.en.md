If $X \cap \mathbf{P}(W)$ were empty, then $X$ would be a complete closed subset of the affine variety $\mathbf{P}(V) - \mathbf{P}(W)$, forcing $\dim X = 0$ (21.1)(c). Now the remaining assertion follows from Theorem 3.3, combined with Corollary 3.2.
