The big cell is the open subset $\Omega = U^{-}B$ of a reductive group, isomorphic to the product of the opposite unipotent radical and the Borel subgroup. It corresponds to the dense Schubert cell in the flag variety and plays a crucial role in relating global properties of $G$ to its local structure.
