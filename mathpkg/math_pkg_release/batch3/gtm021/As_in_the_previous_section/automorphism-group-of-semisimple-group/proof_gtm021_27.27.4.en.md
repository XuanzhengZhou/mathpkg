The proof involves analyzing the action of an automorphism on the Bruhat decomposition and showing that, after composing with an inner automorphism, the automorphism stabilizes $T$ and $B$. The induced action on the character group preserves the root system, giving a homomorphism to the automorphism group of the based root datum. The kernel consists of inner automorphisms by elements of $T$ that act trivially on roots, which is finite.
