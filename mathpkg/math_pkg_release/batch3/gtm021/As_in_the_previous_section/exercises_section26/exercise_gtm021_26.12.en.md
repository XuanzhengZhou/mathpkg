Prove that the Weyl group $W$ permutes the root spaces $\mathfrak{g}_\alpha$ (via $\operatorname{Ad} N$): $\operatorname{Ad} n(\mathfrak{g}_\alpha) = \mathfrak{g}_{\sigma(\alpha)}$ for $n \in N$ representing $\sigma \in W$.
