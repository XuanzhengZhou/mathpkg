Let $W$ be the Weyl group of a Tits system. Let $\sigma \in W$ have reduced expression $\rho_{i(1)} \cdots \rho_{i(t)}$ ($\rho_i \in S$). Suppose that $\ell(\rho_{i(0)}\sigma) \leqslant \ell(\sigma)$. Then there exists $s$, $1 \leqslant s \leqslant t$, such that $\rho_{i(0)}\rho_{i(1)} \cdots \rho_{i(s-1)} = \rho_{i(1)} \cdots \rho_{i(s)}$.
