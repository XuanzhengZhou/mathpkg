This proposition establishes the fundamental correspondence between regular 1-parameter subgroups and Borel subgroups containing a fixed maximal torus. The partition of $Y(T)_{\text{reg}}$ into Weyl chambers mirrors the decomposition of $\mathfrak{B}^T$ into Borel subgroups, and the Weyl group action is compatible with this correspondence.
