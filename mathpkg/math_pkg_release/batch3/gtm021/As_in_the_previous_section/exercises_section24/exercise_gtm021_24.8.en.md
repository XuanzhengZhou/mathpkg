Let $T$ be a torus in $G$, $W = W(G, T)$, $X = X(T)$, $Y = Y(T)$. If $\sigma \in W$ has coset representative $n \in N_G(T)$, show that the following formulas yield an action of $W$ on $X$: $(\sigma\chi)(t) = \chi(n^{-1}tn)$ for $\chi \in X$, and dually on $Y$: $(\sigma\lambda)(a) = n\lambda(a)n^{-1}$ for $\lambda \in Y$.
