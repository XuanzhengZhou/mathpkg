Let $\varphi: G \rightarrow \operatorname{GL}(V)$ be a rational representation, $T$ a maximal torus of $G$. A weight of $\varphi$ (relative to $T$) is a character $\lambda \in X(T)$ such that the weight space
$$V_\lambda = \{v \in V \mid t \cdot v = \lambda(t)v \text{ for all } t \in T\}$$
is nonzero.
