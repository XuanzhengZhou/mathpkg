Let $G$ be an algebraic group with Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$.

(a) $d\mu_{(e,e)}(x, y) = x + y$ for $x, y \in \mathfrak{g}$.
(b) $d\iota_e(x) = -x$.
(c) For fixed $x \in G$, the differential at $e$ of the commutator map $\gamma_x(y) = yxy^{-1}x^{-1}$ is $1 - \operatorname{Ad} x$, where $\operatorname{Ad} x = d(\operatorname{Int} x)$.
