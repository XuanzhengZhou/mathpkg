Let $\psi: T \rightarrow H$ be a homomorphism of abstract groups, and let $h_\alpha \in H$ ($\alpha \in \Delta$). Then $\psi$ can be extended to a homomorphism $N \rightarrow H$, sending $n_\alpha$ to $h_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \Delta$), if and only if
$$(h_\alpha h_\beta)^{m(\alpha, \beta)} = \psi(t_{\alpha\beta})$$
for all $\alpha, \beta \in \Delta$.
