$N = N_G(H)$ is closed in $G$ (Corollary 8.2), so its Lie algebra $\mathfrak{n}$ is defined. Since $H$ is a normal subgroup of $N$, Corollary A applied to the pair $(N, H)$ yields $[\mathfrak{n}, \mathfrak{h}] \subset \mathfrak{h}$. This means precisely that $\mathfrak{n} \subset \mathfrak{n}_{\mathfrak{g}}(\mathfrak{h})$.
