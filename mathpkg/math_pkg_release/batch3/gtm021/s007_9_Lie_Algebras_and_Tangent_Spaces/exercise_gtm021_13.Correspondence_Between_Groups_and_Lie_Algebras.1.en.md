Let $G$ be a connected algebraic group (char $0$), and let $H$ be a closed connected subgroup. Prove that $\mathcal{L}(N_G(H)) = \mathfrak{n}_{\mathfrak{g}}(\mathfrak{h})$ (cf. Corollary 10.4B) and that $\mathcal{L}(C_G(H)) = \mathfrak{c}_{\mathfrak{g}}(\mathfrak{h})$ (cf. Exercise 10.5).
