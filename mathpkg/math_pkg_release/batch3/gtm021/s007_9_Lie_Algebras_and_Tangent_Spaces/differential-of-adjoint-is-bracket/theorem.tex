The differential of the adjoint representation $\operatorname{Ad}: G \to \operatorname{GL}(\mathfrak{g})$ is the adjoint representation of the Lie algebra:
$$d(\operatorname{Ad})_e = \operatorname{ad}: \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{gl}(\mathfrak{g}),$$
where $\operatorname{ad} X (Y) = [X, Y]$ for all $X, Y \in \mathfrak{g}$.
