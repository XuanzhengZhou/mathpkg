The map $\operatorname{Ad}: G \to \operatorname{Aut} \mathfrak{g}$ satisfies $(\operatorname{Ad} x)(\operatorname{Ad} y) = \operatorname{Ad}(xy)$ for all $x, y \in G$, and $\operatorname{Ad} e = \operatorname{id}_{\mathfrak{g}}$. Thus $\operatorname{Ad}$ is a homomorphism of abstract groups from $G$ to the automorphism group of its Lie algebra.
