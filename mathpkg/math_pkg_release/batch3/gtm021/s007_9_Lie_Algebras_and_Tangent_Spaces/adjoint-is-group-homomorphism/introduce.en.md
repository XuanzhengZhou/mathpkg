This proposition verifies that the adjoint map $\operatorname{Ad}$ is indeed a group homomorphism. The invertibility follows from $(\operatorname{Ad} x)(\operatorname{Ad} x^{-1}) = d(\operatorname{Int} x \circ \operatorname{Int} x^{-1}) = d(\operatorname{Int} e) = 1_{\mathfrak{g}}$, and multiplicativity from the functoriality of the differential.
