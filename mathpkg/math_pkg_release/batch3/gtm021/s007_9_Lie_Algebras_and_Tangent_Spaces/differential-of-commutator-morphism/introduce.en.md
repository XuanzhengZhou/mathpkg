The commutator morphism $\gamma_x$ measures the failure of $x$ to commute with other group elements. Its differential at the identity is $1 - \operatorname{Ad} x$, expressing at the infinitesimal level how conjugation by $x$ deviates from the identity. This formula is used extensively in the study of commutator subgroups and centralizers.
