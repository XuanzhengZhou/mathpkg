An algebraic subalgebra is one that comes from an actual algebraic subgroup. In characteristic 0, by Theorem 13.1, the map from closed connected subgroups to algebraic subalgebras is a bijection. However, not every Lie subalgebra of $\mathfrak{g}$ is algebraic. Determining which subalgebras are algebraic is a difficult problem, even for $G = \operatorname{GL}(n,K)$.
