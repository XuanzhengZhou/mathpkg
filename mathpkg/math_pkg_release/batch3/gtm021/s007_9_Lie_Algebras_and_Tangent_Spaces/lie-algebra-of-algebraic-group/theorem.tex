Let $G$ be an algebraic group over $K$ with coordinate ring $A = K[G]$. The Lie algebra of $G$ is
$$\mathcal{L}(G) = \{\delta \in \operatorname{Der} A \mid \delta \circ \lambda_x = \lambda_x \circ \delta \text{ for all } x \in G\},$$
the space of left invariant derivations of $A$, where $(\lambda_x f)(y) = f(x^{-1}y)$ is left translation.
