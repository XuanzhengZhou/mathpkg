Let $\operatorname{char} K = 2$, $G = \mathrm{SL}(2, K)$, $B = T(2, K) \cap G$. Verify that $N_G(B) = B$, whereas $\mathfrak{n}_{\mathfrak{g}}(\mathfrak{b}) = \mathfrak{g}$ (cf. Corollary B of Theorem 10.4).
