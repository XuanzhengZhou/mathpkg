This theorem provides the fundamental identification between the Lie algebra $\mathcal{L}(G)$ (defined via left invariant derivations) and the tangent space $\mathfrak{g} = \mathcal{T}(G)_e$ at the identity of $G$. The isomorphism $\theta$ evaluates a derivation at the identity. The bracket on $\mathfrak{g}$ is transported from $\mathcal{L}(G)$ via $\theta$, yielding a formula in terms of right convolution. The theorem also establishes functoriality: $d\varphi$ preserves brackets. As a consequence, $\dim \mathcal{L}(G) = \dim G$.
