For an algebraic group $G$ viewed as a functor on $K$-algebras, the tangent bundle $\mathcal{T}(G)$ is defined by $\mathcal{T}(G) = G(K[\delta])$, where $K[\delta]$ is the algebra of dual numbers ($\delta^2 = 0$). Elements of $\mathcal{T}(G)$ can be written as $\varepsilon_x + \delta x$ where $x \in G$ and $x \in \mathcal{T}(G)_x$, corresponding to the homomorphism $f \mapsto f(x) + \delta \cdot x(f)$ from $K[G]$ to $K[\delta]$.
