In characteristic $0$, the exponential map (or more precisely, the Lie correspondence) provides a bijection between connected subgroups of $G$ and Lie subalgebras of $\mathfrak{g}$. Under this correspondence, normal subgroups correspond to ideals, and the restriction to characteristic $0$ ensures the connectedness properties needed. For positive characteristic, additional care is needed as the correspondence is more subtle.
