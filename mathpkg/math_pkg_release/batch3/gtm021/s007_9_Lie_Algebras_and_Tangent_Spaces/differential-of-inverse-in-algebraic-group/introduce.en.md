The differential of the inverse map at the identity sends each tangent vector $X$ to its negative $-X$. This follows from differentiating the composite $G \to G \times G \to G$ given by $x \mapsto (x, x^{-1}) \mapsto x x^{-1} = e$, which is the constant map, so its differential is zero. Combined with $d\mu_{(e,e)}(X,Y) = X+Y$, this forces $d\iota_e(X) = -X$.
