Let $G$ be a connected algebraic group over a field $K$ of characteristic $0$. The correspondence $H \mapsto \mathfrak{h} = \mathcal{L}(H)$ is injective and inclusion-preserving between the collection of closed connected subgroups $H$ of $G$ and the collection of their Lie algebras, regarded as subalgebras of $\mathfrak{g} = \mathcal{L}(G)$.
