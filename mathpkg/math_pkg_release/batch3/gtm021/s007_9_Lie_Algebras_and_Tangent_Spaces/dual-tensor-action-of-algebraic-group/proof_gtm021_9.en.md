The Lie algebra action is obtained by differentiating the group action. For $V^*$: differentiating $g \cdot f = f \circ g^{-1}$ at $g = e$ gives $x \cdot f = -f \circ x$. For $V \otimes W$: the Leibniz rule for the tensor product follows from differentiating the bilinear group action.
