The forward direction is the proposition above. For the converse, if $\mathfrak{h}$ is an ideal, then the connected subgroup $H$ whose Lie algebra is $\mathfrak{h}$ is normalized by $G$ (since $\operatorname{Ad} x(\mathfrak{h}) \subset \mathfrak{h}$ for all $x$ implies $xHx^{-1} \subset H$ for $G$ connected).
