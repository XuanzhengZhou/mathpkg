The Lie algebra of the general linear group $\mathrm{GL}(n, K)$ is the general linear Lie algebra:
$$\mathcal{L}(\mathrm{GL}(n, K)) \cong \mathfrak{gl}(n, K),$$
the space of all $n \times n$ matrices with the commutator bracket $[X, Y] = XY - YX$. The tangent space at the identity has canonical basis consisting of the partial differentiation operators $\partial/\partial T_{ij}$ evaluated at $e$, corresponding to the matrix units $e_{ij}$.
