Since $\mathrm{GL}(n,K)$ is an open subset of affine $n^2$-space (defined by the non-vanishing of the determinant), its tangent space at the identity has dimension $n^2$. The canonical basis of this tangent space is given by the partial derivative operators $\partial/\partial T_{ij}|_e$, which correspond to the matrix units $e_{ij}$ in $\mathfrak{gl}(n,K)$. The Lie bracket computed via the convolution formula of Theorem 9.1 yields the usual matrix commutator $[X,Y] = XY - YX$.
