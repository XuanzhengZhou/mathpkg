Let $V$ be a vector space over $K$. For a subspace $W \subset V$, define
$$G_W = \{x \in \operatorname{GL}(V) \mid x(W) = W\}, \quad \mathfrak{g}_W = \{X \in \mathfrak{gl}(V) \mid X(W) \subset W\}.$$
For $v \in V$, define
$$G_v = \{x \in \operatorname{GL}(V) \mid x(v) = v\}, \quad \mathfrak{g}_v = \{X \in \mathfrak{gl}(V) \mid X(v) = 0\}.$$
Then (in any characteristic):
$$\mathcal{L}(G_W) = \mathfrak{g}_W, \qquad \mathcal{L}(G_v) = \mathfrak{g}_v.$$
