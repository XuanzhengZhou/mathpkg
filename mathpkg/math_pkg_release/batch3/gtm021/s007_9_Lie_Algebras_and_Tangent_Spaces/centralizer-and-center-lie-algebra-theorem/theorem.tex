Assume $\operatorname{char} K = 0$. Let $G$ be a connected algebraic group.
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] If $x \in G$, then $\mathcal{L}(C_G(x)) = \mathfrak{c}_{\mathfrak{g}}(x) = \{X \in \mathfrak{g} \mid \operatorname{Ad} x(X) = X\}$.
\item[(b)] $\operatorname{Ker} \operatorname{Ad} = Z(G)$, and its Lie algebra is $\operatorname{Ker} \operatorname{ad} = \mathfrak{z}(\mathfrak{g}) = \{X \in \mathfrak{g} \mid [X, \mathfrak{g}] = 0\}$.
\end{itemize}
