Let $G$ be a closed subgroup of $\mathrm{GL}(n, K)$, and let $J$ be the ideal of all polynomials in $K[T_{ij}]$ vanishing on $G$. Prove that
$$G = \{x \in \mathrm{GL}(n, K) \mid \rho_x J = J\},$$
and that $\mathfrak{g}$ consists of all $X \in \mathfrak{gl}(n, K)$ such that $J * X \subset J$.
