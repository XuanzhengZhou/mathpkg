$U(n,K)$ is the subgroup of $\mathrm{GL}(n,K)$ consisting of upper triangular matrices with $1$'s on the diagonal. It is the closed subset of $M(n,K)$ defined by the vanishing of the linear polynomials $T_{ii} - 1$ and $T_{ij}$ for $i > j$. By the Jacobian criterion, its tangent space at $e$ (translated to $0$) consists of all matrices with zeros on and below the diagonal, denoted $\mathfrak{n}(n,K)$.
