$D(n,K)$ is the subgroup of diagonal matrices in $\mathrm{GL}(n,K)$. As a principal open subset of the affine $n$-space of diagonal entries, its Lie algebra $\mathfrak{d}(n,K)$ consists of all diagonal matrices. This is an $n$-dimensional abelian Lie algebra, reflecting the commutativity of the group $D(n,K)$.
