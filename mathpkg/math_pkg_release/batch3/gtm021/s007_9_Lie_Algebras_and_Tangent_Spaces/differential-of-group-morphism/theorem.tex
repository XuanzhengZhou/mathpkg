Let $\varphi: G \to G'$ be a morphism of algebraic groups. The differential of $\varphi$ is the linear map
$$d\varphi = d\varphi_e: \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}'$$
obtained by restricting the differential of $\varphi$ as a morphism of varieties to the tangent space at the identity, where $\mathfrak{g} = \mathcal{T}(G)_e$ and $\mathfrak{g}' = \mathcal{T}(G')_e$.
