This proposition describes the Lie algebra of the commutator subgroup $(A,B)$. Using $d(\gamma_x)_e = 1 - \operatorname{Ad} x$, one sees that the image of the differential of $\gamma_x|_B$ is contained in $\mathfrak{c}$. By further differentiation, the Lie bracket $[X,Y]$ is also contained in $\mathfrak{c}$.
