This lemma characterizes the Lie algebra $\mathfrak{h}$ of a closed subgroup $H \subset G$ in terms of the vanishing ideal $I$ of $H$. A tangent vector $x \in \mathfrak{g}$ belongs to $\mathfrak{h}$ if and only if the right convolution of every function in $I$ with $x$ remains in $I$. This provides a practical tool for computing Lie subalgebras corresponding to closed subgroups defined by polynomial equations.
