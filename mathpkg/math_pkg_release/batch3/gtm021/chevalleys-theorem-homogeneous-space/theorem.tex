Let $G$ be an algebraic group, $H$ a closed subgroup. Then there exists a rational representation $\varphi: G \rightarrow \operatorname{GL}(V)$ and a one-dimensional subspace $L$ of $V$ such that:
$$H = \{x \in G \mid \varphi(x) L = L\},$$
$$\mathfrak{h} = \{\mathbf{x} \in \mathfrak{g} \mid d\varphi(\mathbf{x}) L \subset L\}.$$
