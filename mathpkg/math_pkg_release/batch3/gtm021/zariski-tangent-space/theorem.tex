The tangent space $\mathcal{T}(X)_x$ to a variety $X$ at a point $x$ is the dual space of $m_x/m_x^2$, where $m_x$ is the maximal ideal of the local ring $\mathcal{O}_x$.

Equivalently, $\mathcal{T}(X)_x$ is the space of $K$-linear point derivations $\delta: \mathcal{O}_x \rightarrow K$ satisfying
$$\delta(fg) = f(x)\delta(g) + \delta(f)g(x).$$
