The uniqueness theorem guarantees that the linear operator differential equation $\dot{\varphi}(t) = \psi(t) \circ \varphi(t)$ has at most one solution satisfying a given initial condition. The proof uses an integral inequality similar to Gronwall's lemma, showing that the norm of any difference of two solutions must be identically zero.
