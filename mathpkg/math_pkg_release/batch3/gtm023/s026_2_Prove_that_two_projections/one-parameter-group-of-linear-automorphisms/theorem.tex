A differentiable family of linear automorphisms $\varphi(t)$ ($-\infty < t < \infty$) is called a 1-parameter group if
$$\varphi(t + \tau) = \varphi(t) \circ \varphi(\tau).$$
This implies $\varphi(0) = \iota$ and $\varphi(t) \circ \varphi(-t) = \iota$. Moreover, the group $\varphi(t)$ is commutative.
