To every skew mapping $\psi$ there exists an orthonormal basis $a_v (v = 1 \dots n)$ in which the matrix of $\psi$ has the form

$$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & \kappa_1 \\
-\kappa_1 & 0 \\
& & \ddots \\
& & & 0 & \kappa_p \\
& & & -\kappa_p & 0 \\
& & & & & 0 \\
& & & & & & \ddots \\
& & & & & & & 0
\end{bmatrix}$$

where $\kappa_i > 0$ for $i = 1, \dots, p$, and the remaining diagonal entries are zero. The rank of $\psi$ is $2p$, which is always even.
