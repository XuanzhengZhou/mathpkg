Proposition I establishes that the map from unit quaternions $p \in S^3$ to proper rotations of 3-space given by $x \mapsto pxp^{-1}$ is a surjective homomorphism with kernel $\{\pm 1\}$. Thus $SO(3) \cong S^3/\{\pm 1\}$. The rotation angle determined by $p = e \cos \omega + a \sin \omega$ (with $a$ a unit vector orthogonal to $e$) is $\Theta = 2\omega$, and the axis is generated by $a$.
