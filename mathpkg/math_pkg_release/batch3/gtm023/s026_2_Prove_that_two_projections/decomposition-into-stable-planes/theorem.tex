For every rotation $\varphi$ there exists an orthogonal decomposition of $E$ into stable subspaces of dimension 1 and 2. Denote by $E_1$ and $E_2$ the eigenspaces corresponding to the eigenvalues $\lambda = +1$ and $\lambda = -1$ respectively. Then $E_1$ is orthogonal to $E_2$. The orthogonal complement of $E_1 \oplus E_2$ decomposes into mutually orthogonal stable planes, each carrying a proper rotation.
