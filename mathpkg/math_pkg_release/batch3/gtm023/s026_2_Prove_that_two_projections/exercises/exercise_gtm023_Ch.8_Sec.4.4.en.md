Applying the result of problem 3 to the mappings
$$\varphi x = (a_1 \times a_2) \times x$$
and
$$\psi x = a_2(a_1, x) - a_1(a_2, x)$$
prove the formula
$$(a_1 \times a_2) \times a_3 = a_2(a_1, a_3) - a_1(a_2, a_3).$$
