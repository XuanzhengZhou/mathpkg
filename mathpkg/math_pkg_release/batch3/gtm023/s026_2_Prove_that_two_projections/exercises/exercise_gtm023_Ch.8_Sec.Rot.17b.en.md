Denote by $\Theta$ the common rotation angle of $\varphi$ and $\psi$ in $F$ and by $\Theta^\perp_\varphi$, $\Theta^\perp_\psi$ the rotation angles in $F^\perp$. Show that $\Theta^\perp_\varphi = \Theta$ and $\Theta^\perp_\psi = -\Theta$.
