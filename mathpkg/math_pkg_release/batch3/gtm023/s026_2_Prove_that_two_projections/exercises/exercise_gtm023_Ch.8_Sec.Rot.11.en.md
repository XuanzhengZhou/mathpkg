Let $\varphi$ be a proper rotation of the 3-space not having the eigenvalue $-1$. Prove that the skew transformations $\chi = (\varphi - \iota) \circ (\varphi + \iota)^{-1}$ and $\psi = \frac{1}{2}(\varphi - \tilde{\varphi})$ are connected by $\chi = \frac{1}{1 + \cos \theta} \psi$ where $\theta$ is the rotation-angle of $\varphi$.
