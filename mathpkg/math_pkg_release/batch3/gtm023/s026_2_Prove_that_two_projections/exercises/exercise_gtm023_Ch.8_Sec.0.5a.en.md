Given a selfadjoint mapping $\varphi$ of $E$, consider the distinct eigenvalues $\lambda_i$ and the corresponding eigenspaces $E_i$ ($i = 1 \dots r$). If $\pi_i$ denotes the orthogonal projection $E \rightarrow E_i$, prove that
$$\pi_i \circ \pi_j = 0 \quad (i \neq j).$$
