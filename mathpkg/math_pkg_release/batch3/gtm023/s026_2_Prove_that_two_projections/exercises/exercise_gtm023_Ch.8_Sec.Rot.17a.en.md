Let $p \neq \pm e$ be a unit vector and set $a = p + e$, $b = p - e$. Define $\varphi(x) = axa^{-1}$, $\psi(x) = bxb^{-1}$. Show that planes $F$ and $F^\perp$ are stable under $\varphi$ and $\psi$, and that $\varphi = \psi$ in $F$.
