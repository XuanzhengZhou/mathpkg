The reflection $\varrho$ of $E$ at a subspace $E_1$ is defined by
$$\varrho x = p - h$$
where $x = p + h$ ($p \in E_1$, $h \in E_1^\perp$). Show that the reflection $\varrho$ and the projection $\pi: E \rightarrow E_1$ are related by
$$\varrho = 2\pi - \iota.$$
