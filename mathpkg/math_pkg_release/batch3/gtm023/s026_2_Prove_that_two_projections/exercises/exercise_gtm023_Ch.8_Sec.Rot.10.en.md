Let $E$ be an inner product space of dimension $n > 2$. Consider a proper rotation $\tau$ which commutes with all proper rotations. Prove that $\tau = \varepsilon \iota$ where $\varepsilon = 1$ if $n$ is odd and $\varepsilon = \pm 1$ if $n$ is even.
