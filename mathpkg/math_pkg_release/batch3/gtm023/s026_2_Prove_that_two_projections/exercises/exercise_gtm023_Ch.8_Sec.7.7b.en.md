Let $\theta$ be a derivation in $A$ and define $\varphi_t$ by $\dot{\varphi}_t = \theta \varphi_t$, $\varphi_0 = \iota$. Prove that every $\varphi_t$ is an automorphism of $A$ and commutes with $\theta$.
