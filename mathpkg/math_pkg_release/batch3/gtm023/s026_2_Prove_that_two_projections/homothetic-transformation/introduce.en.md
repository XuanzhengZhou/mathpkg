A homothetic transformation on an inner product space is a composition of a proper rotation and a non-negative scaling: $\lambda \varphi$ with $\lambda \geq 0$ and $\det \varphi = +1$. Homothetic transformations preserve angles and ratios of distances, generalizing similarity transformations in Euclidean geometry.
