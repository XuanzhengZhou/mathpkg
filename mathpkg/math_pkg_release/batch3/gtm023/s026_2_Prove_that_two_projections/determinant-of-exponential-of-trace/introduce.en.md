The determinant of the solution $\varphi(t)$ to the operator differential equation $\dot{\varphi}(t) = \psi(t) \circ \varphi(t)$ is given by the exponential of the integral of the trace: $\det \varphi(t) = \exp(\int_{t_0}^{t} \operatorname{tr} \psi(t) \, dt)$. This generalizes the scalar formula $\dot{y} = a y \implies y(t) = y_0 \exp(\int a \, dt)$ and shows in particular that $\varphi(t)$ remains invertible for all times.
