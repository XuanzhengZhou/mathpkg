Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ be a linear mapping between inner product spaces and let $\tilde{\varphi}: F \rightarrow E$ be its adjoint mapping. Then the adjoint of $\tilde{\varphi}$ is again $\varphi$:

$$\tilde{\tilde{\varphi}} = \varphi.$$

Thus the adjoint operation is an involution on the space of linear mappings between inner product spaces.
