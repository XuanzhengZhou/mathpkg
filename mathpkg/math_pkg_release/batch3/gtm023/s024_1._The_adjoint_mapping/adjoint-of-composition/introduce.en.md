The adjoint operation is contravariant with respect to composition: the adjoint of a composition is the composition of the adjoints in reverse order. This is analogous to the transpose of a product of matrices: $(AB)^T = B^T A^T$. This property makes the adjoint a contravariant functor on the category of inner product spaces.
