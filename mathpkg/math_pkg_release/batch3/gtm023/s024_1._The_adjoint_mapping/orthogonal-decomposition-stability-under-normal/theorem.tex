Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow E$ be a linear transformation and let
$$E = E_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus E_r$$
be an orthogonal decomposition such that each subspace $E_i$ is stable under $\varphi$. Denote by $\varphi_i$ the restriction of $\varphi$ to $E_i$. Then $\varphi$ is normal if and only if each $E_i$ is also stable under the adjoint $\tilde{\varphi}$ and each restriction $\varphi_i$ is normal.
