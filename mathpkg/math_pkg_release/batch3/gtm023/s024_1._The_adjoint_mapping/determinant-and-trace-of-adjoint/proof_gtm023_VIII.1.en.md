The matrices of $\tilde{\varphi}$ and $\varphi$ relative to an orthonormal basis are transposes of each other. Since the determinant of a matrix equals the determinant of its transpose, and the trace of a matrix equals the trace of its transpose, we have

$$\det \tilde{\varphi} = \det \varphi, \quad \operatorname{tr} \tilde{\varphi} = \operatorname{tr} \varphi.$$

Alternatively, this follows from the general formula for the adjoint matrix under arbitrary bases, which shows that the characteristic polynomial and hence the determinant and trace are preserved.
