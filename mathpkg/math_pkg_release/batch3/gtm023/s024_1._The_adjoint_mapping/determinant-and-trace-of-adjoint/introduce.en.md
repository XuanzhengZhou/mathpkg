The determinant and trace are invariant under the adjoint operation. This follows immediately from the fact that, relative to an orthonormal basis, the matrix of the adjoint is the transpose of the original matrix, and both the determinant and trace are invariant under transposition. These invariants play an important role in the spectral theory of linear transformations on inner product spaces.
