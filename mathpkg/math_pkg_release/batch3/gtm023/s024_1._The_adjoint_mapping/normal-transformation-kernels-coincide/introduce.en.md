A direct consequence of the norm equality $|\varphi x| = |\tilde{\varphi} x|$ for normal transformations is that a vector is annihilated by $\varphi$ if and only if it is annihilated by $\tilde{\varphi}$. In other words, the kernel (nullspace) is invariant under taking the adjoint. This simple fact has important consequences for the spectral decomposition of normal transformations.
