a) Show that the only projection operators in $C(\varphi)$ are $\iota$ and $0$ if and only if $E$ is irreducible with respect to $\varphi$.

b) Show that the set of projection operators in $C(\varphi)$ is a subset of $C^2(\varphi)$ if and only if $E$ is cyclic with respect to $\varphi$.
