Theorem I characterizes when a decomposition $E = \bigoplus F_j$ into stable subspaces corresponds to grouping generalized eigenspaces. Three equivalent conditions are given: (i) each $F_j$ is a sum of generalized eigenspaces $E_i$; (ii) the projection operators are polynomials in $\varphi$; (iii) every stable subspace $U$ satisfies $U = \sum_j U \cap F_j$. This theorem is fundamental for understanding the structure of the commutant.
