a) Prove that two commuting diagonalizable transformations are simultaneously diagonalizable; i.e., there exists a basis of $E$ with respect to which both matrices are diagonal.

b) Use a) to prove that if $\varphi$ and $\psi$ are commuting semisimple transformations of a complex space, then $\varphi + \psi$ and $\varphi \psi$ are again semisimple.
