Let $a_v$ ($v = 0, \ldots, n-1$) be a basis of $E$ such that $\varphi(a_v) = a_{v+1}$ for $v = 0, \ldots, n-2$. Then the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$ is given by
$$\mu = t^n - \sum_{v=0}^{n-1} \alpha_v t^v,$$
where the $\alpha_v$ are determined by
$$\varphi(a_{n-1}) = \sum_{v=0}^{n-1} \alpha_v a_v.$$
