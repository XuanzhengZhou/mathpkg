When the minimum polynomial is a pure power of an irreducible polynomial, the notions of cyclicity and irreducibility coincide for stable subspaces. This follows directly from Theorem I: any cyclic stable subspace of an irreducible space $E$ (which must satisfy $\mu = f^k$) is itself irreducible.
