A cyclic subspace of $E$ is a stable subspace that is cyclic for the induced transformation. Every vector $a \in E$ generates a canonical cyclic subspace $E_a = \operatorname{Im} \sigma_a$, which is the image of the polynomial evaluation map. These subspaces are the building blocks for decomposing $E$ and play a central role in the structure theory of linear transformations.
