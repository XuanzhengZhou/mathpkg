Suppose $\varphi$ is a linear transformation of a complex space $E$. Let $E = \sum_i E_i$ be the decomposition of $E$ into generalized eigenspaces. Prove that the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$ restricted to $E_i$ is $(t - \lambda_i)^{k_i}$.
