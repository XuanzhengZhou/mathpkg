Proposition V guarantees the existence of at least one cyclic subspace $E_a$ whose dimension attains the degree of the minimum polynomial. This follows from Proposition I, which provides a vector $a$ with $\ker \sigma_a = I_\mu$, making $\sigma_a$ induce an isomorphism $\Gamma[t]/I_\mu \cong E_a$, so $\dim E_a = \deg \mu$.
