Corollary III states that $\varphi: E \to E$ and $\varphi^*: E^* \to E^*$ are conjugate. Since $E$ and $E^*$ have the same dimension and $\varphi$ and $\varphi^*$ have the same invariant factors (e.g., the same minimum polynomial and ranks), they satisfy the conjugacy criterion.
