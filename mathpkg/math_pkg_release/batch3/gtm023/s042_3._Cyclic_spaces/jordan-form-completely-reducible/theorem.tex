Suppose that $E$ is an irreducible space and that the minimum polynomial is completely reducible ($p=1$); i.e.,
$$\mu = (t - \lambda)^k.$$
Then the Jordan canonical matrix of $\varphi$ is given by
$$\begin{bmatrix}
\lambda & 1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
0 & \lambda & 1 & \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & \cdots & 0 & \lambda & 1 \\
0 & \cdots & 0 & 0 & \lambda
\end{bmatrix}.$$
