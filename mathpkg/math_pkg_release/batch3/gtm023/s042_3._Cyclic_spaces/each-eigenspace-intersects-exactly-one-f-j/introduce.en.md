Lemma I is the key technical step in proving Theorem I (13.19). Under condition (iii), if a generalized eigenspace $E_i$ intersected nontrivially with two different $F_{j_1}$ and $F_{j_2}$, one could construct a contradiction using cyclic subspaces generated by vectors from both intersections and the fact that the minimum polynomial is a product of powers of distinct irreducible polynomials.
