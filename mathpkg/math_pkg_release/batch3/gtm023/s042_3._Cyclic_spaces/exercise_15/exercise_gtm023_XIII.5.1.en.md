Let
$$\mu = f_1^{k_1} \ldots f_r^{k_r}$$
be the decomposition of the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$, and let $E_i$ be the generalized eigenspaces. If $f_i$ has degree $p_i$, denote by $N_{ij}$ the number of irreducible subspaces of $E_i$ of dimension $p_i j$ ($1 \leq j \leq k_i$). Set
$$l_i = \sum_{j=1}^{k_i} j N_{ij}.$$
Show that the characteristic polynomial of $\varphi$ is given by
$$\chi = f_1^{l_1} \ldots f_r^{l_r}.$$
