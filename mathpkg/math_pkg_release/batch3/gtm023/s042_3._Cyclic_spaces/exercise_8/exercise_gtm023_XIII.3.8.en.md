Suppose that $E$ is irreducible with respect to $\varphi$. Let $f^k$ ($f$ irreducible) be the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$.

a) Prove that the $k$ subspaces $K(f), \ldots, K(f^{k-1})$ are the only non-trivial stable subspaces of $E$.

b) Conclude that
$$\text{Im } f(\varphi)^\kappa = K(f^{k-\kappa}) \quad 0 \leq \kappa \leq k.$$
