Theorem IV is a fundamental structural result: pairwise commuting semisimple transformations are simultaneously semisimple. The proof proceeds by induction on the number of transformations, using the generalized eigenspace decomposition of the first transformation and the fact that the remaining transformations (commuting with the first) restrict to semisimple transformations on each eigenspace.
