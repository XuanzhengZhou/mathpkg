In a cyclic space $E$ generated by $a$, the iterates $a, \varphi(a), \dots, \varphi^{m-1}(a)$ constitute a basis, where $m = \deg \mu = \dim E$. Every vector $x \in E$ can be expressed as $x = f(\varphi)a$ for some polynomial $f$, and the linear independence of the iterates follows from the fact that the degree of the minimum polynomial is exactly $m$.
