Suppose $\varphi$ is nilpotent and let $\varphi^*: E^* \leftarrow E^*$ be the dual mapping. Assume that $E$ is cyclic with respect to $\varphi$ and that $\varphi$ is of degree $k$. Let $a$ be a generator of $E$. Prove that $E^*$ is cyclic with respect to $\varphi^*$ and that $\varphi^*$ is of degree $k$. Let $a^* \in E^*$ be any vector. Show that
$$\langle a^*, \varphi^{k-1} a \rangle \neq 0$$
if and only if $a^*$ is a generator of $E^*$.
