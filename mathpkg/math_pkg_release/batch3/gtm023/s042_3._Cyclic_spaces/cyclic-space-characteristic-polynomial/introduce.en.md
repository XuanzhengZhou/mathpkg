Lemma II establishes that for a cyclic space, the characteristic polynomial is essentially the minimum polynomial (up to sign). The proof computes $\chi(\lambda)$ using a basis of iterates of a generator $a$ for $\varphi - \lambda \iota$, showing $\chi(\lambda) = (-1)^m \mu(\lambda)$ for all $\lambda$. Since this holds for all scalars, the polynomials coincide.
