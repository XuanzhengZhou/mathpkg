Let $\varphi$ be a linear transformation of $E$ and assume that $E = \sum_j F_j$ is a decomposition of $E$ as a direct sum of stable subspaces.

If each $F_j$ is a sum of generalized eigenspaces, prove that each $F_j$ is stable under every $\psi \in C(\varphi)$.

Conversely, assume that each $F_j$ is stable under every $\psi \in C(\varphi)$ and prove that each $F_j$ is a sum of generalized eigenspaces of $\varphi$.
