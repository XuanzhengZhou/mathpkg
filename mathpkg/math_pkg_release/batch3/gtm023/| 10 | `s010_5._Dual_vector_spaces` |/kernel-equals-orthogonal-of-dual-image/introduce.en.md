For dual linear mappings $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ and $\varphi^*: E^* \leftarrow F^*$, the kernel of the original mapping equals the orthogonal complement of the image of the dual mapping. Together with the dual formula $\ker \varphi^* = (\operatorname{Im} \varphi)^\perp$, this establishes a symmetric relationship between kernels and images under duality.
