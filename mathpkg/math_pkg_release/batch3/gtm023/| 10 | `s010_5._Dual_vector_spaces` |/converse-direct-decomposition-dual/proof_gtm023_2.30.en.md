We have

$$(E_1 + E_1^*)^{\perp} = E_1^\perp \cap (E_1^*)^{\perp} = E_1^\perp \cap E_1^* = 0,$$

whence

$$E = 0^\perp = (E_1 + E_1^*)^{\perp\perp} = E_1 + E_1^*.$$

On the other hand, since $E_1$ and $E_1^*$ are dual, it follows that $E_1 \cap E_1^* = 0$, which together with the above proves $E = E_1 \oplus (E_1^*)^\perp$. The formula $L(E) = E_1^* \oplus E_1^\perp$ follows from Proposition V and the decomposition of $E$.
