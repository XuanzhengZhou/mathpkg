For a linear mapping $\varphi$ between vector spaces, the image of its dual map $\varphi^*: L(E) \leftarrow L(F)$ is precisely the orthogonal complement of the kernel of $\varphi$. This result characterizes which linear functions on $E$ arise as pullbacks of linear functions on $F$, namely those that vanish on $\ker \varphi$.
