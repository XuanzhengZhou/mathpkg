Suppose $E, E^*$ is a pair of dual spaces such that every linear function $f: E \rightarrow \Gamma$ induces a dual mapping $f^*: E^* \leftarrow \Gamma$. Show that the natural injection $E^* \rightarrow L(E)$ is surjective.
