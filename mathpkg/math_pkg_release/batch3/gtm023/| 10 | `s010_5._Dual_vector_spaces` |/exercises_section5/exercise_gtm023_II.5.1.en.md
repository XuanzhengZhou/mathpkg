Given two pairs of dual spaces $E^*, E$ and $F^*, F$, prove that the spaces $E^* \oplus F^*$ and $E \oplus F$ are dual with respect to the bilinear function

$$\langle (x^*, y^*), (x, y) \rangle = \langle x^*, x \rangle + \langle y^*, y \rangle.$$
