Let $E$ be a finite dimensional vector space and let $E_1^*$ and $E_2^*$ be any two vector spaces dual to $E$. Then there exists a unique linear isomorphism $\varphi: E_1^* \xrightarrow{\cong} E_2^*$ such that

$$\langle \varphi x^*, x \rangle = \langle x^*, x \rangle, \quad x^* \in E_1^*, \quad x \in E.$$
