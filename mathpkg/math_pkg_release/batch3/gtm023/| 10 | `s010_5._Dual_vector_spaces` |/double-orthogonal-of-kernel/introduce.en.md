The double orthogonal complement of the kernel of a linear mapping $\varphi$ coincides with the kernel itself. This follows from the interplay between Proposition III and formula (2.48), and is a special case of the general fact that $E_1^{\perp\perp} = E_1$ for any subspace $E_1$ of $E$.
