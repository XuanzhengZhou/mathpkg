Suppose $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$ is a decomposition of $E$ as a direct sum of subspaces. Then

$$L(E) = E_1^\perp \oplus E_2^\perp,$$

and the pairs $E_1^\perp, E_2$ and $E_2^\perp, E_1$ are dual with respect to the induced scalar products. Moreover, the induced injections

$$E_1^\perp \rightarrow L(E_2), \quad E_2^\perp \rightarrow L(E_1)$$

are surjective, and hence

$$L(E) = L(E_1) \oplus L(E_2).$$

Finally, $(E_1^\perp)^{\perp\perp} = E_1^\perp$ and $(E_2^\perp)^{\perp\perp} = E_2^\perp$.
