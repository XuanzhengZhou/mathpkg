Let $E, F, G$ be three vector spaces. Given two linear mappings $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ and $\psi: F \rightarrow G$, prove that

$$r(\psi \circ \varphi) \leq r(\varphi) \quad \text{and} \quad r(\psi \circ \varphi) \leq r(\psi).$$
