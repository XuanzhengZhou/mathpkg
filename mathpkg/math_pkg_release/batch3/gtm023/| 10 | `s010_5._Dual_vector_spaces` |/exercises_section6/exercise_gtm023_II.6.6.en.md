Let $E$ be a vector space of dimension $n$ and consider a system of $n$ linear transformations $\sigma_i: E \rightarrow E$, $\sigma_i \neq 0$, such that

$$\sigma_i \circ \sigma_j = \sigma_i \delta_{ij} \quad (i, j = 1, \ldots, n).$$

a) Show that every $\sigma_i$ has rank 1.

b) If $\sigma_i'$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$) is a second system with the same property, prove that there exists a linear automorphism $\tau$ of $E$ such that

$$\sigma_i' = \tau^{-1} \circ \sigma_i \circ \tau.$$
