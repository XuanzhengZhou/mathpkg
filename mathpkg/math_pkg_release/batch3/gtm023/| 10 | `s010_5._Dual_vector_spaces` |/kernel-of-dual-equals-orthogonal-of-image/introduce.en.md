For a pair of dual linear mappings $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ and $\varphi^*: E^* \leftarrow F^*$, the kernel of the dual mapping $\varphi^*$ is exactly the orthogonal complement of the image of the original mapping $\varphi$. This fundamental relation connects the algebraic structure of kernels and images with the duality pairing between a space and its dual.
