Let $E$ and $F$ be finite dimensional vector spaces and let $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ and $\varphi^*: E^* \leftarrow F^*$ be dual mappings. Then

$$r(\varphi^*) = r(\varphi),$$

where $r$ denotes the rank. Moreover,

$$\operatorname{Im} \varphi = (\ker \varphi^*)^\perp \quad \text{and} \quad \operatorname{Im} \varphi^* = (\ker \varphi)^\perp.$$
