The factor group (or quotient group) of a commutative group $G$ by a subgroup $H$ is the set of cosets $G/H$ with a group structure induced by $G$. The canonical projection $\pi: G \to G/H$ is a surjective homomorphism. This construction is the group-theoretic precursor to factor spaces in linear algebra.
