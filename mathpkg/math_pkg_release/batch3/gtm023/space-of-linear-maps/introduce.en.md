The set of all linear mappings between two vector spaces is itself a vector space under pointwise addition and scalar multiplication. $L(E; F)$ is the internal Hom in the category of vector spaces. For finite-dimensional $E$ and $F$, $\dim L(E; F) = (\dim E)(\dim F)$. $L(E) = L(E; \Gamma)$ is the dual space of $E$ (as a set, but not yet identified with the abstract dual space).
