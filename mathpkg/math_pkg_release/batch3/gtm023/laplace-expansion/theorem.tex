**Proposition IV:** Let $\Delta$ be a determinant function in $E$ ($\dim E = n$). Then the following identity holds:
$$\sum_{j=1}^{n} (-1)^{j-1} \Delta(x, x_1, \ldots, \hat{x}_j, \ldots, x_n) \cdot x_j = \Delta(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \cdot x$$
for all $x_v \in E$ and $x \in E$, where $\hat{x}_j$ denotes omission of $x_j$.
