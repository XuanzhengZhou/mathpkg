The polynomial algebra $\Gamma[t]$ is the free commutative associative algebra on one generator over $\Gamma$. Polynomials are formal expressions $\sum \alpha_v t^v$ with finitely many non-zero coefficients. The degree formula $\deg(fg) = \deg f + \deg g$ shows $\Gamma[t]$ is an integral domain. Polynomial algebras are fundamental for the theory of linear transformations via evaluation homomorphisms.
