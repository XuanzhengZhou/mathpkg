The sum of two subspaces $E_1$ and $E_2$ of a vector space $E$ is the smallest subspace containing both $E_1$ and $E_2$. It consists of all vectors that can be expressed as the sum of a vector from $E_1$ and a vector from $E_2$. The decomposition of a vector in $E_1 + E_2$ is generally not unique.
