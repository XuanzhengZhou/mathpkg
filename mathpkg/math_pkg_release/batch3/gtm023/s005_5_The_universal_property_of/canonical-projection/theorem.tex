Let $E_1$ be a subspace of a vector space $E$. The \textbf{canonical projection}
$$\pi: E \to E/E_1$$
is the linear mapping defined by
$$\pi x = \bar{x}, \quad x \in E$$
where $\bar{x}$ denotes the equivalence class of $x$ modulo $E_1$. The map $\pi$ is surjective, linear, and satisfies $\ker \pi = E_1$.
