Let $\varphi: E \to F$ be a linear mapping. The \textbf{image space} of $\varphi$, denoted by $\operatorname{Im} \varphi$, is the set of vectors $y \in F$ of the form $y = \varphi x$ for some $x \in E$. $\operatorname{Im} \varphi$ is a subspace of $F$. The map $\varphi$ is surjective if and only if $\operatorname{Im} \varphi = F$.
