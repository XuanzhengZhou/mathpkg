Let $E_\alpha \subset E$, $\alpha \in A$, be an arbitrary family of subspaces of a vector space $E$. The \textbf{sum} $\sum_\alpha E_\alpha$ is defined as the set of all vectors which can be written as finite sums
$$x = \sum_\alpha x_\alpha, \quad x_\alpha \in E_\alpha$$
and is a subspace of $E$. The intersection $\bigcap_\alpha E_\alpha$ is also a subspace of $E$.
