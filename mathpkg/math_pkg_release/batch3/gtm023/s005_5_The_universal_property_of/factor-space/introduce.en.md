The factor space (or quotient space) $E/E_1$ is constructed by identifying vectors that differ by an element of the subspace $E_1$. The linear operations on $E/E_1$ are defined so that the canonical projection $\pi$ becomes a linear map. This construction is fundamental in linear algebra, analogous to quotient groups in group theory.
