Let $y_\mu$ ($\mu=1,\ldots,m$) be a basis of $F$. Write
$$\varphi x = \sum_\mu \eta^\mu(x) y_\mu$$
where the $\eta^\mu$ are linear functions in $E$. By $T_2$, each $\eta^\mu$ is continuous. By $T_1$, the linear combination $\sum_\mu \eta^\mu(x) y_\mu$ is continuous in $x$. Hence $\varphi$ is continuous.
