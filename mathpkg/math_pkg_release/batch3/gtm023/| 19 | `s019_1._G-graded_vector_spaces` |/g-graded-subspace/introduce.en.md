A $G$-graded subspace of a $G$-graded vector space $E$ is a linear subspace $F$ whose intersection with each homogeneous component $E_k$ spans $F$. The defining condition $F = \sum_k F \cap E_k$ ensures that the grading of $E$ restricts naturally to $F$, with each vector in $F \cap E_k$ retaining its degree $k$. This is the natural notion of substructure in the category of graded vector spaces.
