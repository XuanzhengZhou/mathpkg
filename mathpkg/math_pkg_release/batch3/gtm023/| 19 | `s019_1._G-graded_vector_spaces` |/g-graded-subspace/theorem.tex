Let $E$ be a $G$-graded space, $E = \sum_{k \in G} E_k$, and consider a subspace $F \subset E$ such that
$$F = \sum_{k \in G} F \cap E_k.$$
Then a $G$-gradation is induced in $F$ by assigning the degree $k$ to the vectors of $F \cap E_k$. $F$ together with its induced gradation is called a \textit{$G$-graded subspace} of $E$.
