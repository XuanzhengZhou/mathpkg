Let $E$ and $F$ be two $G$-graded spaces and let $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ be a linear map. The map $\varphi$ is called \textit{homogeneous} if there exists a fixed element $k \in G$ such that
$$\varphi E_j \subset F_{j+k} \quad j \in G.$$
$k$ is called the \textit{degree} of the homogeneous mapping $\varphi$.
