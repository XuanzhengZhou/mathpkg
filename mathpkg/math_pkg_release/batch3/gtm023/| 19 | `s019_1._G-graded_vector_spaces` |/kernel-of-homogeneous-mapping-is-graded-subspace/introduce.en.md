The kernel of a homogeneous linear mapping between $G$-graded vector spaces inherits the $G$-gradation from the domain, making it a $G$-graded subspace. This follows from the commutation relation $\sigma_{k+j} \circ \varphi = \varphi \circ \varrho_j$ between the projection operators $\varrho_j$ on $E$ and $\sigma_j$ on $F$, which characterises the homogeneity condition.
