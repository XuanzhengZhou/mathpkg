The Poincaré series is additive with respect to direct sums. Since $(E \oplus F)_k = E_k \oplus F_k$, we have $\dim (E \oplus F)_k = \dim E_k + \dim F_k$, and the generating series sums coefficient-wise. This additivity property makes the Poincaré series a useful invariant for decomposing graded spaces.
