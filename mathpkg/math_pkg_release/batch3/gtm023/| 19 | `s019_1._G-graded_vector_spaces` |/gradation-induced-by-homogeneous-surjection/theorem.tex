Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ be a homogeneous linear mapping of $G$-graded vector spaces which is onto. Define $F_j = \varphi E_j$ for each $j \in G$. Then
$$F = \sum_{j \in G} F_j$$
is a direct decomposition, and it defines a $G$-gradation in $F$ with respect to which $\varphi$ is homogeneous of degree zero. Moreover, this $G$-gradation is the unique one on $F$ satisfying that $\varphi$ is homogeneous of degree zero.
