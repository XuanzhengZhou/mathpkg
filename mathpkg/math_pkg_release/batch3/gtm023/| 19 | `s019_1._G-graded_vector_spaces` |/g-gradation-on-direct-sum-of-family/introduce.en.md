Given a family of $G$-graded vector spaces $\{E^\lambda\}_{\lambda \in I}$, their direct sum $E = \bigoplus_{\lambda} E^\lambda$ inherits a natural $G$-gradation: the homogeneous component of degree $k$ in $E$ is the direct sum of all degree-$k$ components from each $E^\lambda$. This construction shows that the category of $G$-graded vector spaces is closed under direct sums.
