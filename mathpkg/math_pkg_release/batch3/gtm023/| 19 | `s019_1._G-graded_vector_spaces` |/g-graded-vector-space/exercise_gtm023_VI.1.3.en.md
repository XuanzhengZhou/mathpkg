Let $E, F$ be $G$-graded vector spaces and suppose that $E_1 \subset E$, $F_1 \subset F$ are $G$-graded subspaces. Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ be a linear mapping homogeneous of degree $k$. Assume that $\varphi$ can be restricted to $E_1, F_1$ to obtain a linear mapping $\varphi_1: E_1 \rightarrow F_1$ and an induced mapping
$$\bar{\varphi}: E/E_1 \rightarrow F/F_1.$$
Prove that $\varphi_1$ and $\bar{\varphi}$ are homogeneous of degree $k$.
