Let $E$ and $F$ be two $G$-graded vector spaces and $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ be a homogeneous linear mapping of degree $k$. Assume further that a homomorphism $\omega: G \rightarrow H$ is given. Prove that $\varphi$ is homogeneous of degree $\omega(k)$ with respect to the induced $H$-gradation.
