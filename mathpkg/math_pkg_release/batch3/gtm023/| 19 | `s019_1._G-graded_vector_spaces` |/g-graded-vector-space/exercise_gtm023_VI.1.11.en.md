Suppose $E, E^*$ is a pair of almost finite dual $G$-graded vector spaces. Let $\{x_\alpha\}$ be a basis of $E$ consisting of the set union of bases for the homogeneous subspaces of $E$. Prove that a dual basis $\{x^{*\alpha}\}$ in $E^*$ exists.
