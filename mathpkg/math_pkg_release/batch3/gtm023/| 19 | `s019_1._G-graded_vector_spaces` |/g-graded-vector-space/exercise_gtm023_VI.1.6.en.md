Let $E$ and $E^*$ be a pair of dual $G$-graded vector spaces. Prove that there exists a homogeneous linear isomorphism of degree zero
$$\varphi: E_1^* \rightarrow E_2^*$$
such that
$$\langle \varphi y^*, x \rangle = \langle y^*, x \rangle \quad y^* \in E_1^*, \quad x \in E.$$
