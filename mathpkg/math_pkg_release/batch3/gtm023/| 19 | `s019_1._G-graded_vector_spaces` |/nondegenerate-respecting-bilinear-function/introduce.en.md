For a bilinear function that respects $G$-gradations, non-degeneracy can be checked degree by degree: the global pairing is non-degenerate precisely when each restriction $\varphi_k: E_k \times F_k \rightarrow \Gamma$ is non-degenerate. In the special case of a scalar product (symmetric non-degenerate bilinear form) that respects the gradation, the homogeneous components $E_k$ and $F_k$ are dual vector spaces, and consequently $\dim E_k = \dim F_k$ for all $k \in G$.
