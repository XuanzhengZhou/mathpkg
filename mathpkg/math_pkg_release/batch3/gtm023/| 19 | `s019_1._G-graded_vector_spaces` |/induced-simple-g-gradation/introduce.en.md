From a $G^p$-gradation (a multi-grading by $p$-tuples), one obtains a simple $G$-gradation by summing the components of each $p$-tuple via the addition homomorphism $\tau(k_1,\ldots,k_p) = k_1+\cdots+k_p$. The degree-$j$ homogeneous component of the simple gradation collects all multi-homogeneous vectors whose total degree is $j$. This is the standard way to pass from a bigrading (e.g., in a double complex) to a single grading (the total complex).
