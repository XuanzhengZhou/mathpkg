Let $\Phi$ be a symmetric bilinear function in $E$ and let $E = E^+ \oplus E^- \oplus E_0$ be its decomposition into maximal positive definite, negative definite, and null subspaces. The index of $\Phi$ is defined as

$$\operatorname{index}(\Phi) = \dim E^+.$$

This number is uniquely determined by $\Phi$ (independent of the choice of decomposition).
