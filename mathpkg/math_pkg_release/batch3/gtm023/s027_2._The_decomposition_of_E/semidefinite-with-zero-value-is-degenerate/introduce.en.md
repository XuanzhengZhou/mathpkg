This lemma shows that for a positive semidefinite bilinear function, any nonzero vector on which the quadratic form vanishes must lie in the nullspace. The proof relies on the Schwarz inequality: if $\Phi(x_0) = 0$, then $|\Phi(x_0, y)|^2 \leq \Phi(x_0)\Phi(y) = 0$, forcing $\Phi(x_0, y) = 0$ for all $y$. Consequently, such a bilinear function is necessarily degenerate.
