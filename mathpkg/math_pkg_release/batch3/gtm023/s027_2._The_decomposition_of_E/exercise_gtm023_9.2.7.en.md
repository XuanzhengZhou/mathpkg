Consider a (not necessarily symmetric) bilinear function $\Phi$ such that $\Phi(x, x) > 0$ for all vectors $x \neq 0$. Construct a basis of $E$ in which the matrix of $\Phi$ has the block diagonal form consisting of blocks

$$\begin{bmatrix}
1 & \kappa_1 \\
-\kappa_1 & 1
\end{bmatrix}, \quad \ldots, \quad \begin{bmatrix}
1 & \kappa_p \\
-\kappa_p & 1
\end{bmatrix}$$

and possibly additional $1 \times 1$ blocks equal to $[1]$.
