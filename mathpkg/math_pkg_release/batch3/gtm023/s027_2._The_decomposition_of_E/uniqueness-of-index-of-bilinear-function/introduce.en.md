This proposition establishes the well-definedness of the index: regardless of how one chooses the maximal positive definite subspace in the decomposition, its dimension is always the same. The proof uses a dimension-counting argument: if two different maximal positive definite subspaces had different dimensions, their intersection properties would violate the total dimension of $E$.
