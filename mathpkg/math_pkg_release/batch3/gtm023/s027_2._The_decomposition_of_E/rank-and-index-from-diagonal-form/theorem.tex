Let $\Phi(x) = \sum_{v,\mu} \alpha_{v\mu} \xi^v \xi^\mu$ be a symmetric bilinear form in $n$ variables, with $\Phi \neq 0$. By successive application of completing the square and the substitution $\xi^i = \bar{\xi}^i + \bar{\xi}^j$, $\xi^j = \bar{\xi}^i - \bar{\xi}^j$ when all diagonal entries vanish, one can reduce $\Phi$ to diagonal form

$$\Phi(x) = \sum_{v=1}^n \lambda_v (\zeta^v)^2$$

with, after rearrangement,
$$\lambda_v > 0 \quad (v = 1, \ldots, s),$$
$$\lambda_v < 0 \quad (v = s+1, \ldots, r),$$
$$\lambda_v = 0 \quad (v = r+1, \ldots, n).$$

Then $r$ is the rank and $s$ is the index of $\Phi$.
