This proposition provides a constructive algorithm, Lagrange's method of completing the square, to compute the rank and index of a symmetric bilinear form directly from its quadratic form expression. The rank $r$ is the number of nonzero coefficients in any diagonalization, and the index $s$ is the number of positive coefficients. The procedure handles the case where all diagonal entries are zero by first applying a transformation that creates nonzero diagonal entries.
