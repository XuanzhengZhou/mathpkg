From the previous proposition, the rank of $\Phi$ equals the rank of its matrix $(\alpha_{v\mu})$. The matrix is an $n \times n$ matrix. Its rank equals $n$ if and only if its determinant is nonzero. But $\Phi$ has rank $n$ if and only if $\dim E_0 = 0$, which is precisely the condition that $\Phi$ is non-degenerate. Therefore $\Phi$ is non-degenerate if and only if $\det(\alpha_{v\mu}) \neq 0$.
