This proposition establishes the fundamental relationship between a symmetric bilinear function $\Phi$ and its associated linear map $\varphi: E \to E^*$. The nullspace of $\Phi$ is exactly the kernel of $\varphi$, and the matrix of $\Phi$ with respect to any basis coincides with the matrix of $\varphi$. This yields the important fact that the rank of $\Phi$ equals the rank of its matrix.
