This lemma establishes that the functional $F$ obtained from the path-independent integral $J$ is homogeneous of degree zero: $F(\lambda x) = F(x)$. This property is essential because it allows the minimization argument on the unit sphere to extend to all non-zero vectors. The proof uses the straight-line path between $x$ and $\lambda x$, along which the integrand vanishes identically.
