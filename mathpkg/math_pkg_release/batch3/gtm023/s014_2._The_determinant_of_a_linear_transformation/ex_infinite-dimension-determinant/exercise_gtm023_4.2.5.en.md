Let $E$ be a vector space with a countable basis and assume that a function $F$ is given in $L(E; E)$ which satisfies the conditions of problem 4a). Prove that

$$F(\varphi) = 1 \quad \text{for all } \varphi \in L(E; E).$$

Hint: Construct an injective mapping $\varphi$ and a surjective mapping $\psi$ such that

$$\psi \circ \varphi = 0.$$
