If $\varphi = \lambda \iota$ where $\iota$ is the identity map, then

$$\Delta_\varphi(x_1, \ldots, x_n) = \Delta(\lambda x_1, \ldots, \lambda x_n) = \lambda^n \Delta(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$$

and hence

$$\det(\lambda \iota) = \lambda^n.$$

In particular, $\det \iota = 1$ and the determinant of the zero-map is zero.
