Let $A = (\alpha_v^\mu)$ be an $n \times m$-matrix. For every system of indices

$$1 \leq i_1 < i_2 < \cdots < i_k \leq n \quad \text{and} \quad 1 \leq j_1 < j_2 < \cdots < j_k \leq m$$

denote by $A_{i_1, \ldots, i_k}^{j_1, \ldots, j_k}$ the submatrix of $A$, consisting of the rows $i_1, \ldots, i_k$ and the columns $j_1, \ldots, j_k$. The determinant of $A_{i_1, \ldots, i_k}^{j_1, \ldots, j_k}$ is called a minor of order $k$ of the matrix $A$.
