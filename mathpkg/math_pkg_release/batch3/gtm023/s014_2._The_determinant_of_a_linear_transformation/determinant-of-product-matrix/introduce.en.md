Just as the determinant of a composition of linear transformations is the product of their determinants, the determinant of a product of matrices satisfies $\det(AB) = \det A \, \det B$. This follows by interpreting $A$ and $B$ as matrices representing linear transformations $\varphi$ and $\psi$, and applying the multiplicativity result for transformations.
