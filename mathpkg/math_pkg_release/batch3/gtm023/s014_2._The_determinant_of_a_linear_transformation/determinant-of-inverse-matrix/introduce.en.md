For an invertible matrix $A$, the determinant of the inverse is the reciprocal of the determinant: $\det(A^{-1}) = (\det A)^{-1}$. This follows directly from $\det(AA^{-1}) = \det I = 1$ and the multiplicativity $\det(AB) = \det A \det B$.
