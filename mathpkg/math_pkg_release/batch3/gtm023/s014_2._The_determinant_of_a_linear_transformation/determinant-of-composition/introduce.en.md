The determinant is multiplicative with respect to composition: applying two linear transformations in succession multiplies their determinants. This follows directly from applying the definition twice, first with $\varphi$ and then with $\psi$, to a non-trivial determinant function. As an important corollary, the determinant of the inverse of an automorphism is the reciprocal of the original determinant.
