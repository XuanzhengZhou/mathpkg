For a block diagonal matrix $A = \operatorname{diag}(A_1, A_2)$, the determinant factorizes as $\det A = \det A_1 \cdot \det A_2$. This result follows from the corresponding property for linear transformations on a direct sum decomposition $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$, where the induced transformations have determinants $\det \varphi_1$ and $\det \varphi_2$.
