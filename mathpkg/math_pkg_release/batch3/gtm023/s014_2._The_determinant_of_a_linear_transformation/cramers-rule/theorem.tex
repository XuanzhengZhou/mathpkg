Consider the system of $n$ linear equations in $n$ unknowns

$$\sum_{k=1}^{n} \alpha_k^j \xi^k = \eta^j \quad (j = 1, \ldots, n)$$

whose coefficient matrix $A = (\alpha_k^j)$ has non-zero determinant. Then the unique solution is given by

$$\xi^i = \frac{1}{\det A} \sum_{j} \operatorname{cof}(\alpha_i^j) \, \eta^j,$$

where $\operatorname{cof}(\alpha_i^j)$ denotes the cofactor of $\alpha_i^j$. Equivalently,

$$\xi^i = \frac{1}{\det A} \sum_{j} (-1)^{i+j} (\det S_i^j) \, \eta^j.$$
