Let $\varphi$ be a linear transformation of the $n$-dimensional vector space $E$ and let $\varphi^*$ be the dual transformation on $E^*$. Then

$$\det \varphi^* = \det \varphi.$$

Consequently, for any $n \times n$-matrix $A$, we have

$$\det A^* = \det A,$$

where $A^*$ denotes the transpose of $A$.
