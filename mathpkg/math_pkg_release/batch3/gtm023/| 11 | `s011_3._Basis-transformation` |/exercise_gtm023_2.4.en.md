Denote by $A_{ij}$ the $n \times n$ matrix having the entry $1$ at the place $(i,j)$ and zero elsewhere.

1. Verify the formula

$$A_{ij} \cdot A_{jk} = A_{ik}.$$

2. Prove that the matrices $A_{ij}$ ($i, j = 1 \ldots n$) form a basis of the space $M^{n \times n}$.
