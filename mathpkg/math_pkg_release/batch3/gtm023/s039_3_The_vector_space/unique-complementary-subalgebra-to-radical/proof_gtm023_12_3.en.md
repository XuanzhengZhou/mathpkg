**Proof of 5):** Let $B$ be any subalgebra of $\Gamma(\bar{t})$ which complements the radical. Then dividing out by the radical we obtain an algebra isomorphism $A \xrightarrow{\cong} B$. Thus, by statement 4), the elements of $B$ are semisimple. Hence, again by statement 4), $B \subset A$. It follows that $B = A$.
