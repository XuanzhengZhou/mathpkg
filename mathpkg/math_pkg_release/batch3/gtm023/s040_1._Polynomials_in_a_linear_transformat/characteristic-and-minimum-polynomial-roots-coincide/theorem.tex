Let $\varphi: E \to E$ be a linear transformation on a finite-dimensional vector space over a field of characteristic $0$. Let $\mu$ be the minimum polynomial and $\chi$ the characteristic polynomial of $\varphi$. Then:

(i) The distinct roots of $\chi$ coincide with the distinct roots of $\mu$ (i.e., with the eigenvalues of $\varphi$).

(ii) $\mu \mid \chi$ (the minimum polynomial divides the characteristic polynomial).
