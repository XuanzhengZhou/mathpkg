It is a classical fact that the characteristic polynomial and the minimum polynomial have the same irreducible factors. The eigenvalues appear as roots of both polynomials. The stronger divisibility $\mu \mid \chi$ (Cayley–Hamilton) is stated here and proved in sec. 13.20. Together, these results mean both polynomials carry the same spectral information.
