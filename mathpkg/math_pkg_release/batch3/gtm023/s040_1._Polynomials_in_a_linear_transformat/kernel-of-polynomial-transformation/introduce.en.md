For a fixed linear transformation $\varphi \colon E \to E$, every polynomial $f \in \Gamma[t]$ induces a linear transformation $f(\varphi)$. The kernel $K(f) = \ker f(\varphi)$ collects all vectors annihilated by $f(\varphi)$. These kernels are always $\varphi$-invariant subspaces and form the central objects of study for the structure theory of a single linear transformation.
