This result shows that every monic divisor $f$ of the minimum polynomial is realized as the minimum polynomial of the restriction of $\varphi$ to $K(f)$. It establishes a bijective correspondence between monic divisors of $\mu$ and the $\varphi$-invariant subspaces of the form $K(f)$, which are precisely the summands in the primary decomposition.
