Let $\varphi: E \to E$ be a linear transformation on a finite-dimensional vector space over a field of characteristic $0$, with minimum polynomial $\mu$. Let $f \in \Gamma[t]$ be a non-zero monic polynomial dividing $\mu$, and let $\varphi_f$ denote the restriction of $\varphi$ to the invariant subspace $K(f)$. Then the minimum polynomial $\mu_f$ of $\varphi_f$ is

$$\mu_f = f.$$
