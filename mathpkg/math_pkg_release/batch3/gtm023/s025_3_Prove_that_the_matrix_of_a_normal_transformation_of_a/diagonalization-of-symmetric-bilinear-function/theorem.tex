For every symmetric bilinear function $\Phi$ on an $n$-dimensional inner product space $E$, there exists an orthonormal basis $e_v$ ($v = 1, \ldots, n$) of $E$ in which the matrix of $\Phi$ has diagonal form:

$$\Phi(e_v, e_\mu) = \lambda_v \delta_{v\mu}$$

where $\lambda_v$ are real numbers (the eigenvalues of $\Phi$).
