In the orthonormal basis of eigenvectors $e_v$ ($v = 1, \ldots, n$) of a selfadjoint transformation $\varphi$, the matrix of $\varphi$ has diagonal form:

$$\varphi e_v = \lambda_v e_v \quad (v = 1, \ldots, n)$$

where $\lambda_v$ are real numbers. Equivalently, the matrix of $\varphi$ relative to this basis is $\operatorname{diag}(\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_n)$.
