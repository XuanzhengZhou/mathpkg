A selfadjoint transformation $\varphi$ of an $n$-dimensional inner product space $E$ has $n$ eigenvectors $e_v$ ($v = 1, \ldots, n$) which are mutually orthogonal:

$$(e_v, e_\mu) = \delta_{v\mu}.$$

The eigenvectors $e_v$ form an orthonormal basis of $E$. In this basis the mapping $\varphi$ has the diagonal form

$$\varphi e_v = \lambda_v e_v$$

where $\lambda_v$ denotes the real eigenvalue corresponding to $e_v$.
