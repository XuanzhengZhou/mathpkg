For a selfadjoint transformation $\varphi$, the dimension of each eigenspace $E(\lambda_v)$ equals the multiplicity of $\lambda_v$ as a root of the characteristic polynomial:

$$\dim E(\lambda_v) = \operatorname{mult}_{\lambda_v}(\det(\varphi - \lambda \iota)).$$

That is, the geometric multiplicity of every eigenvalue equals its algebraic multiplicity.
