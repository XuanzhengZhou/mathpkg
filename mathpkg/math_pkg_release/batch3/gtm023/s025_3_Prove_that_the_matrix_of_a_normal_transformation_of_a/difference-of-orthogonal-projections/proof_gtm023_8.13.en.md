Consider the mapping $\varphi = \iota - (\pi_1 - \pi_2) = (\iota - \pi_1) + \pi_2$. Now $\iota - \pi_1$ is the orthogonal projection onto $E_1^\perp$. By the sum theorem (8.12), $\varphi$ is a projection if and only if $E_1^\perp \perp E_2$, i.e., $E_2 \subset E_1$. If this condition holds, $\varphi$ is the projection onto $E_1^\perp \oplus E_2$.

Then $\pi_1 - \pi_2 = \iota - \varphi$ is the projection onto
$$(E_1^\perp \oplus E_2)^\perp = E_1 \cap E_2^\perp,$$
which is the orthogonal complement of $E_2$ relative to $E_1$.
