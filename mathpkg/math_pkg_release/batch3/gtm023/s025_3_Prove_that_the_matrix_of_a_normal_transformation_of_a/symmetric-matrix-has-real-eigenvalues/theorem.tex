A symmetric $n \times n$ real matrix $A = (\alpha_v^\mu)$ has $n$ real eigenvalues (counted with multiplicity). Its characteristic polynomial factors over $\mathbb{R}$ as

$$\det(A - \lambda I) = (\lambda_1 - \lambda)^{k_1} (\lambda_2 - \lambda)^{k_2} \cdots (\lambda_r - \lambda)^{k_r}$$

where $\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_r \in \mathbb{R}$ are the distinct eigenvalues.
