Let $\varphi$ be any linear transformation of an inner product space $E$. Prove that $\varphi \tilde{\varphi}$ is a positive self-adjoint mapping. Prove that

$$(x, \varphi \tilde{\varphi} x) > 0 \quad (x \neq 0)$$

if and only if $\varphi$ is regular.
