Let $\varphi$ be a linear transformation of a real $n$-dimensional linear space $E$. Show that an inner product can be introduced in $E$ such that $\varphi$ becomes a selfadjoint mapping if and only if $\varphi$ has $n$ linearly independent eigenvectors.
