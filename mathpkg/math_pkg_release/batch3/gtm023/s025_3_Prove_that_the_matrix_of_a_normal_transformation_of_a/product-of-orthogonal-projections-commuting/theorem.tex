Let $\pi_1: E \rightarrow E_1$ and $\pi_2: E \rightarrow E_2$ be orthogonal projections. The product $\pi_2 \circ \pi_1$ is an orthogonal projection if and only if the projections commute:

$$\pi_2 \circ \pi_1 = \pi_1 \circ \pi_2.$$

In this case, $\operatorname{Im}(\pi_2 \circ \pi_1) = E_1 \cap E_2$.
