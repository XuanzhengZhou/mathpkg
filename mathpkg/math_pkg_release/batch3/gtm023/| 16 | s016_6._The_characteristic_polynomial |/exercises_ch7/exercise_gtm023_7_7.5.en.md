Let $f$ be a linear function in the space $L(E; E)$. Show that $f$ can be written as

$$f(\varphi) = \operatorname{tr}(\varphi \circ \alpha)$$

where $\alpha$ is a fixed linear transformation in $E$. Prove that $\alpha$ is uniquely determined by $f$.
