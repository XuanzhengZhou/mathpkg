Let $A: L(E; E) \to L(E; E)$ be a linear transformation such that

$$A(\varphi \circ \psi) = A(\varphi) \circ A(\psi), \quad \varphi, \psi \in L(E; E)$$

and

$$A(\iota) = \iota.$$

Prove that $\operatorname{tr} A(\varphi) = \operatorname{tr} \varphi$.
