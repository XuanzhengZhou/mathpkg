Consider two $n \times n$-matrices $A$ and $B$. Prove the relation

$$\operatorname{tr}(AB) = \operatorname{tr}(BA)$$

a) by direct computation.

b) using the relation $\operatorname{tr} \varphi = \operatorname{tr} M(\varphi)$.
