Assume that $f$ is a linear function in the space $L(E; E)$ such that

$$f(\psi \circ \varphi) = f(\varphi \circ \psi).$$

Prove that

$$f(\varphi) = \lambda \cdot \operatorname{tr} \varphi$$

where $\lambda$ is a scalar.
