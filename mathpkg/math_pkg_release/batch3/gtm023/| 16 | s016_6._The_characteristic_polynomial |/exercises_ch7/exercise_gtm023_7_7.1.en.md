Show that the characteristic polynomial of a linear transformation $\varphi$ of a 2-dimensional linear space can be written as

$$f(\lambda) = \lambda^2 - \lambda \operatorname{tr} \varphi + \det \varphi.$$

Verify that every such $\varphi$ satisfies its characteristic equation,

$$\varphi^2 - \varphi \cdot \operatorname{tr} \varphi + \iota \cdot \det \varphi = 0.$$
