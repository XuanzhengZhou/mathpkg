If $\varphi$ and $\psi$ are two linear transformations of a 2-dimensional linear space, prove the relation

$$\psi \circ \varphi + \varphi \circ \psi = \varphi \operatorname{tr} \psi + \psi \operatorname{tr} \varphi + \iota \left( \operatorname{tr}(\psi \circ \varphi) - \operatorname{tr} \varphi \operatorname{tr} \psi \right).$$
