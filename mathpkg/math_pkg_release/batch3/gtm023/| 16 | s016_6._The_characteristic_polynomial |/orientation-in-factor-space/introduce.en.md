When both a vector space $E$ and a subspace $F$ are oriented, a natural orientation is induced on the quotient space $E/F$. This is done by fixing the arguments of a determinant function on $E$ to a positive basis of $F$ and considering the resulting function on the remaining variables modulo $F$. The construction is well-defined and independent of the auxiliary choices.
