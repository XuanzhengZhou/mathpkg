When a linear transformation $\varphi$ is invertible, the characteristic polynomials of $\varphi$ and $\varphi^{-1}$ are related by a simple algebraic transformation. The polynomial of the inverse is obtained by reversing the order of coefficients (up to a multiplicative factor involving the determinant). This relationship reveals the duality between a transformation and its inverse at the spectral level.
