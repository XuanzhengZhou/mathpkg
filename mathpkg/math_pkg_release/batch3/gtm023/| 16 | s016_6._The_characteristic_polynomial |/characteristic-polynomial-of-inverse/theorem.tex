Let $\varphi: E \to E$ be a regular linear transformation and let $f(\lambda) = \det(\varphi - \lambda \iota)$ be its characteristic polynomial. Let $F(\lambda) = \det(\varphi^{-1} - \lambda \iota)$. Then

$$\det(\varphi^{-1} - \lambda \iota) = (-\lambda)^n \det \varphi^{-1} \cdot \det(\varphi - \lambda^{-1} \iota),$$

and consequently

$$F(\lambda) = (-\lambda)^n \det \varphi^{-1} \, f(\lambda^{-1}).$$

Writing $F(\lambda) = \sum_{v=0}^{n} \beta_v \lambda^{n-v}$, the coefficients satisfy

$$\beta_v = (-1)^n \det \varphi^{-1} \, \alpha_{n-v} \quad (v = 0, \ldots, n).$$
