By formula (4.27) from sec. 4.10, the determinant of a linear transformation equals the determinant of its dual: $\det \varphi^* = \det \varphi$. Applying this to $\varphi - \lambda \iota$, we have
$$\det(\varphi^* - \lambda \iota) = \det((\varphi - \lambda \iota)^*) = \det(\varphi - \lambda \iota).$$
Thus the characteristic polynomials coincide.
