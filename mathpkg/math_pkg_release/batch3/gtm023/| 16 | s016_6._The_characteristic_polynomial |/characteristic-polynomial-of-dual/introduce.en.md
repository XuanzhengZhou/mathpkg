The dual transformation $\varphi^*$ acting on the dual space $E^*$ shares the same characteristic polynomial as the original transformation $\varphi$. This follows from the fact that the determinant of a linear transformation equals the determinant of its dual. Consequently, $\varphi$ and $\varphi^*$ have identical eigenvalues with the same algebraic multiplicities.
