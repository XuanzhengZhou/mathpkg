Consider a direct decomposition $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$. Given linear transformations $\varphi_i: E_i \to E_i$ ($i = 1, 2$), consider the linear transformation $\varphi = \varphi_1 \oplus \varphi_2: E \to E$. Prove that the characteristic polynomial of $\varphi$ is the product of the characteristic polynomials of $\varphi_1$ and of $\varphi_2$.
