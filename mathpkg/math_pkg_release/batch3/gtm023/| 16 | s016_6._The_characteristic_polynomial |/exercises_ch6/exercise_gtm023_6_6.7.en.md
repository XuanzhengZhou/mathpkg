A linear transformation $\varphi$ of $E$ is called nilpotent if $\varphi^k = 0$ for some $k$. Prove that $\varphi$ is nilpotent if and only if the characteristic polynomial has the form

$$\chi(\lambda) = (-\lambda)^n.$$

Hint: Use problem 6.
