Prove that the characteristic polynomial of a projection $\pi: E \to E_1$ (see Chapter II, sec. 2.19) is given by

$$f(\lambda) = (-1)^{n-p} \lambda^{n-p}(1 - \lambda)^p$$

where $n = \dim E$ and $p = \dim E_1$.
