Suppose that $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$ where $E_1$ and $E_2$ are stable subspaces under $\varphi$. Then the characteristic polynomial of $\varphi$ is the product of the characteristic polynomials of the induced transformations $\varphi_1: E_1 \to E_1$ and $\varphi_2: E_2 \to E_2$:

$$\det(\varphi - \lambda \iota) = \det(\varphi_1 - \lambda \iota_1) \cdot \det(\varphi_2 - \lambda \iota_2).$$
