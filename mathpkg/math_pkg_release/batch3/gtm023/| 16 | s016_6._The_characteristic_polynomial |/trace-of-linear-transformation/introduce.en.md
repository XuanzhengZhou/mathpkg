The trace of a linear transformation is a scalar invariant obtained by summing the contributions of the transformation on each argument of a determinant function. It can be thought of as the sum of diagonal entries of any matrix representing the transformation. Unlike the determinant, the trace is a linear functional on the space of linear transformations, making it useful for studying additive properties of operators.
