The characteristic polynomial of a linear transformation $\varphi$ on an $n$-dimensional vector space is the degree-$n$ polynomial $f(\lambda) = \det(\varphi - \lambda \iota)$. By expanding the determinant using a determinant function $\Delta$, the coefficients $\alpha_p$ are expressed as sums of terms involving the action of $\varphi$ on basis vectors. The characteristic polynomial encodes the spectral information of $\varphi$, as its roots are precisely the eigenvalues.
