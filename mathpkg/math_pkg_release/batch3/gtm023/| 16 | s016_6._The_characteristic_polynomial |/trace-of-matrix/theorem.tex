Let $e_v\;(v = 1, \ldots, n)$ be a basis of $E$ and $A = (\alpha_v^\mu) = M(\varphi)$ be the matrix of $\varphi$ relative to this basis. Then

$$\operatorname{tr} \varphi = \sum_i \alpha_i^i.$$

For any $n \times n$-matrix $A = (\alpha_v^\mu)$, the sum of its diagonal entries is called the trace of $A$:

$$\operatorname{tr} A = \sum_i \alpha_i^i.$$

Consequently,
$$\operatorname{tr} \varphi = \operatorname{tr} M(\varphi).$$
