The fore-cone $T^+$ and past-cone $T^-$ are convex subsets of the pseudo-Euclidean space, meaning that the straight line segment connecting any two time-like vectors in the same cone lies entirely within that cone. This convexity is fundamental to the causal structure of Minkowski space in special relativity.
