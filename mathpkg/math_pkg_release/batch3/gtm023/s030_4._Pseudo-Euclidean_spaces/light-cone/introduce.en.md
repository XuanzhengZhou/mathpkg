In a pseudo-Euclidean space, the light-cone is the set of all non-zero vectors whose self-inner product vanishes. These light-vectors are characterized by being orthogonal to themselves. The classification into space-like, time-like, and light-vectors partitions the non-zero elements of the space by the sign of their quadratic norm.
