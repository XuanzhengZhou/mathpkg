Minkowski space is the mathematical model of spacetime in special relativity: a 4-dimensional pseudo-Euclidean space with signature $(+,-,-,-)$, corresponding to index 3. The inner product has one positive direction (time) and three negative directions (space), making it the ambient space for Lorentz transformations.
