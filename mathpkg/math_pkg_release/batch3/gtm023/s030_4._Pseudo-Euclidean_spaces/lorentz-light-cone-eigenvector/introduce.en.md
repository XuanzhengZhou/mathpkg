A fundamental result in the theory of Lorentz transformations states that every proper orthochroneous Lorentz transformation of Minkowski space has at least one eigenvector lying on the light-cone. The proof uses the selfadjoint part $\psi = (\varphi + \varphi^{-1})/2$ and the spectral theory developed for pseudo-Euclidean spaces, proceeding by contradiction to construct such an eigenvector.
