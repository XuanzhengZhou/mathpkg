In a pseudo-Euclidean space, the spectral theorem for selfadjoint linear transformations requires an additional hypothesis: the quadratic form $(x, \varphi x)$ must never vanish on light-vectors. Under this condition, there exists an orthonormal basis of eigenvectors, generalizing the well-known result from Euclidean spaces. The eigenvalues are related to the signs $\varepsilon_v$ of the orthonormal basis vectors.
