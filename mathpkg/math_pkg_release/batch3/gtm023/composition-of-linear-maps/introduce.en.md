The composition of two linear mappings $\varphi: E \to F$ and $\psi: F \to G$ is defined by x = \psi(\varphi x)$. The composition of linear mappings is again linear. Repeated composition yields powers $\varphi^k$, with $\varphi^0 = \iota$.
