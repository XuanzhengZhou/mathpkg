Let $I \neq 0$ be an ideal in $\Gamma$. If $x \in I$ is nonzero, then $1 = x^{-1} x \in I$. It follows that $\Gamma = \Gamma \cdot 1 \subset I$, so $\Gamma = I$. Since $\Gamma^2 \neq 0$, $\Gamma$ is simple.
