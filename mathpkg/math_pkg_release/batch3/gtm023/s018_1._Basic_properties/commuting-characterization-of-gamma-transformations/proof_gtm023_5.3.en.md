Since $\iota \in \Gamma$, the identification of $\Gamma$ with scalar multiplications $\varepsilon_{\alpha}$ holds. The condition that $\varphi$ commutes with all elements of $\Gamma$ is exactly the condition $\varphi \circ \varepsilon_{\alpha} = \varepsilon_{\alpha} \circ \varphi$ for all $\alpha \in \Gamma$. The result then follows directly from the preceding proposition.
