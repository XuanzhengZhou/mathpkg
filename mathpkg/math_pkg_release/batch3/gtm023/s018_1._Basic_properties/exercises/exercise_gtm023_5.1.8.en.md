Let $L$ be an algebra in which the product of two elements $x, y$ is denoted by $[x, y]$. Assume that

$$
[x, y] + [y, x] = 0 \quad \text{(skew symmetry)}
$$
$$
[[x, y], z] + [[y, z], x] + [[z, x], y] = 0 \quad \text{(Jacobi identity)}
$$

Then $L$ is called a Lie algebra. Let $\operatorname{Ad}(a)$ be the multiplication operator in the Lie algebra $L$. Prove that $\operatorname{Ad}(a)$ is a derivation.
