Show that the algebra $S$ of Problem 3 has ideals which are not principal. Let $(a, b) \subset [0,1]$ be any open interval, and let $f$ be a step function such that $f(t) = 0$ if and only if $a < t < b$. Prove that the ideal generated by $f$ is precisely the subset of functions $g$ such that $g(t) = 0$ for $a < t < b$.
