Let $E$ be a finite dimensional vector space and consider the mapping $\theta_\alpha: A(E; E) \to A(E; E)$ defined by

$$
\theta_\alpha(\varphi) = \alpha \varphi - \varphi \alpha.
$$

Prove that $\theta_\alpha$ is a derivation. Conversely, prove that every derivation in $A(E; E)$ is of this form.

Hint: Use Problem 14.
