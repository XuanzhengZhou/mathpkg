Let $A$ be an arbitrary algebra and consider the set $C(A)$ of elements $a \in A$ that commute with every element in $A$. Show that $C(A)$ is a subspace of $A$. If $A$ is associative, prove that $C(A)$ is a subalgebra of $A$. $C(A)$ is called the centre of $A$.
