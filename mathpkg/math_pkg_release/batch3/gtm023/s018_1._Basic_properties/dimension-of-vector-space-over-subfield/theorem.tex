Let $E$ be a vector space over a field $\Gamma$ and let $\Delta$ be a subfield of $\Gamma$. If $\Gamma$ is finite-dimensional over $\Delta$ and $E$ is finite-dimensional over $\Gamma$, then $E$ is finite-dimensional over $\Delta$ and
$$
\dim_{\Delta} E = \dim_{\Gamma} E \cdot \dim_{\Delta} \Gamma.
$$
