Let $e_1, \ldots, e_n$ be a basis of $E$ over $\Gamma$ and let $E_i$ be the $\Gamma$-subspace generated by $e_i$. Then there is a $\Gamma$-isomorphism $\varphi: \Gamma \to E_i$, which is also a $\Delta$-isomorphism. Hence $\dim_{\Delta} E_i = \dim_{\Delta} \Gamma$ for each $i$. Since $E$ is the $\Delta$-direct sum of the $E_i$,

$$
\dim_{\Delta} E = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \dim_{\Delta} E_i = n \cdot \dim_{\Delta} \Gamma = \dim_{\Gamma} E \cdot \dim_{\Delta} \Gamma.
$$
