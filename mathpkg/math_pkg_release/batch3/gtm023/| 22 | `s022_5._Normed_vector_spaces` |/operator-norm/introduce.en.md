The operator norm of a bounded linear transformation is defined as the supremum of $\|\varphi x\|$ over the unit sphere $\|x\| = 1$. This yields the optimal constant $M$ in the boundedness inequality $\|\varphi x\| \leq M\|x\|$, making the operator norm the sharpest possible bound for the transformation.
