The trace of a linear transformation is the sum of its diagonal matrix entries (in any basis) and equals the sum of eigenvalues (counted with multiplicity). It is a linear functional satisfying the cyclic property $\operatorname{tr}(\psi\varphi) = \operatorname{tr}(\varphi\psi)$. The trace appears as a coefficient of the characteristic polynomial and is fundamental in the Cayley-Hamilton theorem.
