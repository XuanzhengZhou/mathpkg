The trace of a linear transformation $\varphi$ of an $n$-dimensional vector space $E$ is the scalar $\operatorname{tr} \varphi$ defined as the negative of the coefficient of $\lambda^{n-1}$ in the characteristic polynomial:
$$\det(\varphi - \lambda\iota) = (-\lambda)^n + \operatorname{tr} \varphi \cdot (-\lambda)^{n-1} + \cdots + \det \varphi.$$

The trace satisfies:
(i) $\operatorname{tr}(\varphi + \psi) = \operatorname{tr} \varphi + \operatorname{tr} \psi$
(ii) $\operatorname{tr}(\lambda \varphi) = \lambda \operatorname{tr} \varphi$
(iii) $\operatorname{tr}(\psi \circ \varphi) = \operatorname{tr}(\varphi \circ \psi)$
