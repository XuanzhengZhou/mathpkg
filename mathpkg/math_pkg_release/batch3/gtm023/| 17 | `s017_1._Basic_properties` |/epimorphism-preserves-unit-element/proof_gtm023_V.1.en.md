If $y \in B$ is arbitrary, there exists an element $x \in A$ such that $y = \varphi x$. This gives

$$y \cdot \varphi e_A = \varphi x \cdot \varphi e_A = \varphi(x e_A) = \varphi x = y$$

and similarly $\varphi e_A \cdot y = y$. Hence $\varphi e_A$ is the unit element of $B$.
