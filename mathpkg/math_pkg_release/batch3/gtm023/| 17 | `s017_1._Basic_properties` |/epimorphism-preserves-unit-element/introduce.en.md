If an algebra $A$ has a unit element $e_A$ and $\varphi: A \to B$ is a surjective homomorphism (epimorphism), then the image $\varphi e_A$ is the unit element of $B$. This follows because every element of $B$ is of the form $\varphi x$ for some $x \in A$, and $\varphi$ preserves multiplication.
