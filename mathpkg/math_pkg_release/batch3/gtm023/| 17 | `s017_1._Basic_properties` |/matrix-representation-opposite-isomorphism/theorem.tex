Let $M^{n \times n}$ be the vector space of $n \times n$ matrices over $\Gamma$. The map $M$ determines a linear isomorphism of $A(E; E)$ onto $M^{n \times n}$. Moreover,

$$M(\psi \circ \varphi) = M(\varphi)M(\psi).$$

This relation shows that $M$ is an isomorphism of the algebra $A(E; E)$ onto the opposite algebra $(M^{n \times n})^{\text{opp}}$.
