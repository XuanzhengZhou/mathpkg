The linear closure (or span) of a subset $S \subset E$ is the smallest subspace containing $S$. It consists of all finite linear combinations of elements of $S$. The linear closure is the intersection of all subspaces containing $S$. If $S$ is linearly independent, it forms a basis of its linear closure.
