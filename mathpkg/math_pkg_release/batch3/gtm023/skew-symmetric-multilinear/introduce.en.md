A skew symmetric (or alternating) multilinear map changes sign under transposition of any two arguments. Equivalently, it vanishes whenever two arguments are equal, or whenever the arguments are linearly dependent. The antisymmetrization operator $\sum \varepsilon_\sigma \cdot \sigma$ projects any multilinear map onto the subspace of skew symmetric ones. Determinant functions are skew symmetric $n$-linear functions on $n$-dimensional spaces.
