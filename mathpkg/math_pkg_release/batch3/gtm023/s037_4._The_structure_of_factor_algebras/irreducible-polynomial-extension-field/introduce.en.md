If $f$ is an irreducible polynomial over the field $\Gamma$, then the factor algebra $\Gamma(\bar{t}) = \Gamma[t]/I_f$ is itself a field. This follows from the fact that the canonical map $\varphi: \Gamma \to \Gamma(\bar{t})$ sending $\alpha \mapsto \bar{\alpha}$ is a monomorphism, embedding $\Gamma$ as a subfield. Consequently, $\Gamma(\bar{t})$ is an extension field of $\Gamma$.
