A determinant function is a skew symmetric $n$-linear function on an $n$-dimensional space. Non-zero determinant functions exist and the space of all determinant functions is 1-dimensional — any two differ by a scalar factor. Proposition III shows that every skew symmetric $n$-linear map factors through a fixed determinant function. The determinant of a linear transformation will be defined via determinant functions.
