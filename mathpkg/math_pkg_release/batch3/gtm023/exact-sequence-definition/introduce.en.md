Exact sequences are a powerful language for describing relationships between linear mappings. Exactness at $E$ means the image of the incoming map equals the kernel of the outgoing map. Short exact sequences capture the relationship between a subspace, the ambient space, and the factor space. The concept generalizes to abelian categories and is fundamental in homological algebra.
