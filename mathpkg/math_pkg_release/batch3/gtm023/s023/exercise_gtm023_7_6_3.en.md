Let $p$ be a fixed quaternion and consider the linear transformation $\varphi$ given by $\varphi x = px$. Show that the characteristic polynomial of $\varphi$ reads
$$f(t) = \left(t^2 - 2(p, e) t + |p|^2\right)^2.$$

Conclude that $\varphi$ has no real eigenvalues unless $p$ is a multiple of $e$.
