Let $\Phi: E^p \to F$ be a $p$-linear map over a field $\Gamma$ of characteristic $0$. Then the following properties are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\Phi$ is skew symmetric.
\item[(ii)] $\Phi(x_1, \ldots, x_p) = 0$ whenever $x_i = x_j$ for some $i \neq j$.
\item[(iii)] $\Phi(x_1, \ldots, x_p) = 0$ whenever the vectors $x_1, \ldots, x_p$ are linearly dependent.
\end{enumerate}
