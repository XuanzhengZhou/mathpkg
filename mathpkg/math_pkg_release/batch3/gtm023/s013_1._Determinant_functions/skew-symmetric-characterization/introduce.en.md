This proposition provides two practical characterizations of skew symmetry for $p$-linear maps over fields of characteristic zero. Condition (ii) states that the map vanishes when two arguments coincide, and condition (iii) states that it vanishes on any linearly dependent $p$-tuple. These equivalent formulations are essential tools in determinant theory; for instance, condition (iii) immediately implies that any skew symmetric $p$-linear map on an $n$-dimensional space is identically zero when $p > n$.
