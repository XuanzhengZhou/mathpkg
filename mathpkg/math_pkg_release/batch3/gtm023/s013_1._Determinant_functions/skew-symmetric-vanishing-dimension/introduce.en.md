This corollary follows immediately from condition (iii) of the characterization of skew symmetric maps: when $p > n = \dim E$, any $p$ vectors are linearly dependent, so the skew symmetric map must vanish. This explains why nontrivial determinant functions exist only when the number of arguments equals the dimension of the space.
