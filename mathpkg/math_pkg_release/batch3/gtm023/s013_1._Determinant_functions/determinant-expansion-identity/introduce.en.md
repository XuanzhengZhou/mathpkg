This identity is a coordinate-free formulation of Laplace expansion along a column. It expresses the product of a determinant with a vector as a linear combination of the argument vectors, where the coefficients are signed determinants obtained by replacing one argument with $x$. This identity is fundamental for proving properties of the adjugate matrix and Cramer's rule.
