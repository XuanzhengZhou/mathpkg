An even permutation is a permutation whose sign equals $+1$. Even permutations form the alternating group $A_p$, which is a normal subgroup of index $2$ in the symmetric group $S_p$ (for $p \geq 2$). The evenness of a permutation determines whether it can be expressed as a product of an even number of transpositions.
