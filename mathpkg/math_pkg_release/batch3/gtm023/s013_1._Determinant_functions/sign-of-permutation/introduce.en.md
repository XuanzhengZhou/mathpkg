The sign of a permutation is a fundamental invariant in the symmetric group. It is defined by considering the action of a permutation on the Vandermonde-like product $\prod_{i<j}(x_i-x_j)$, which changes by at most a factor of $\pm 1$. This sign function $\varepsilon_\sigma$ distinguishes even and odd permutations and is the crucial ingredient that makes determinant functions alternating.
