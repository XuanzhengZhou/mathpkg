Which of the following mappings of $\Gamma^4$ into itself are linear transformations?

a) $(\xi^1, \xi^2, \xi^3, \xi^4) \rightarrow (\xi^1 \xi^2, \xi^2 - \xi^1, \xi^3, \xi^4)$

b) $(\xi^1, \xi^2, \xi^3, \xi^4) \rightarrow (\lambda \xi^2, \xi^2 - \xi^1, \xi^3, \xi^4)$, where $\lambda \in \Gamma$ is a fixed scalar

c) $(\xi^1, \xi^2, \xi^3, \xi^4) \rightarrow (0, \xi^3, \xi^2, \xi^1 + \xi^2 + \xi^3 + \xi^4)$
