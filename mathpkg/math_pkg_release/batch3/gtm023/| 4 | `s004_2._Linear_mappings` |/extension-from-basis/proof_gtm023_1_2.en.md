*Uniqueness.* Since a basis is in particular a system of generators, uniqueness follows immediately from Proposition I.

*Existence.* Consider a linear relation among the basis vectors:
$$
\sum_{\alpha} \lambda^{\alpha} x_{\alpha} = 0.
$$
Since the $x_{\alpha}$ form a basis, they are linearly independent, so $\lambda^{\alpha} = 0$ for all $\alpha$. Consequently,
$$
\sum_{\alpha} \lambda^{\alpha} \varphi_0 x_{\alpha} = \sum_{\alpha} 0 \cdot \varphi_0 x_{\alpha} = 0.
$$
Thus the condition of Proposition I is satisfied, and the existence of the extension $\varphi$ follows.
