A basis of a vector space is a linearly independent system of generators. Every vector has a unique representation as a linear combination of basis vectors. The coefficients in this representation are called components. The standard basis of $\Gamma^n$ is $e_i = (0,\ldots,1,\ldots,0)$ with $1$ in the $i$-th position.
