A **field** is a set $\Gamma$ with two binary operations, addition and multiplication, such that:
1. $\Gamma$ is a commutative group under addition.
2. $\Gamma \setminus \{0\}$ is a commutative group under multiplication.
3. Addition and multiplication satisfy the distributive law:
$$(\alpha + \beta)\gamma = \alpha\gamma + \beta\gamma, \quad \alpha, \beta, \gamma \in \Gamma.$$
