The factor group $G/H$ of a commutative group $G$ by a subgroup $H$ is the set of cosets equipped with a well-defined addition induced from $G$. This construction is the prototype for quotient spaces in linear algebra: the quotient of a vector space by a subspace is defined by the same equivalence relation on the underlying additive group.
