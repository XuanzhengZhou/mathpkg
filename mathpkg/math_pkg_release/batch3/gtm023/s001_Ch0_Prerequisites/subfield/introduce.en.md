A subfield is a subset of a field closed under addition, multiplication, and inverses. The real numbers form a subfield of the complex numbers. When the coefficient field of a vector space is restricted to a subfield, one obtains a vector space over the subfield of double the dimension (in the case $\mathbb{C}$ over $\mathbb{R}$).
