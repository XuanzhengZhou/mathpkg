(i) Every finitely generated non-trivial vector space has a finite basis.

(ii) Let $S = (x_1, \ldots, x_m)$ be a finite system of generators and $R = (x_1, \ldots, x_r)$ ($r \leq m$) be a linearly independent subset. Then there exists a basis of $E$ of the form $(x_1, \ldots, x_r, y_1, \ldots, y_s)$ where $y_j \in S$.
