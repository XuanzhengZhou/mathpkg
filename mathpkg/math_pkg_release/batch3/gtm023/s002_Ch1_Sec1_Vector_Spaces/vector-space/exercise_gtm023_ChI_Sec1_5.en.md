Let $E$ be a real linear space. Consider the set $E \times E$ of ordered pairs $(x, y)$ with $x \in E$, $y \in E$. Show that $E \times E$ becomes a complex vector space under the operations:
$$(x_1, y_1) + (x_2, y_2) = (x_1 + x_2, y_1 + y_2)$$
and
$$(\alpha + i\beta)(x, y) = (\alpha x - \beta y, \alpha y + \beta x), \quad (\alpha, \beta \text{ real numbers}).$$
