A short exact sequence /usr/bin/zsh \to E_1 \to E \to E_2 \to 0$ splits (i.e.,  \cong E_1 \oplus E_2$) if and only if there exists a linear map $\chi: E_2 \to E$ such that $\psi \circ \chi = \iota_{E_2}$, or equivalently a map $\omega: E \to E_1$ with $\omega \circ \varphi = \iota_{E_1}$.
