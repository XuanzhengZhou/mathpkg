A short exact sequence
$$0 \longrightarrow E_1 \xrightarrow{\varphi} E \xrightarrow{\psi} E_2 \longrightarrow 0$$
is said to \textbf{split} if there exists a linear mapping $\chi: E_2 \to E$ such that $\psi \circ \chi = \iota_{E_2}$ (equivalently, a linear mapping $\omega: E \to E_1$ such that $\omega \circ \varphi = \iota_{E_1}$). In this case,
$$E \cong E_1 \oplus E_2.$$
