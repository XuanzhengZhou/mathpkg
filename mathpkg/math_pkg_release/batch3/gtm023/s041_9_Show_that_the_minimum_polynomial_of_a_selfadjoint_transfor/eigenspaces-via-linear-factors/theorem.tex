Suppose $\lambda$ is an eigenvalue for $\varphi$ and let
$$\mu = f_1^{k_1} \cdots f_r^{k_r}$$
be the decomposition of the minimum polynomial into irreducible factors. Then $t - \lambda \mid \mu$, and so for some $i$ ($1 \leq i \leq r$)
$$f_i = t - \lambda.$$
Hence the eigenspaces of $\varphi$ are precisely the spaces
$$K(f_i)$$
where the $f_i$ are those polynomials in the decomposition which are linear.
