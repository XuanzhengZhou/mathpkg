Let $E^*$ be dual to $E$ and $\varphi^* : E^* \leftarrow E^*$ be dual to $\varphi$. If $E^* = \sum_i E_i^*$ is the decomposition of $E^*$ into generalized eigenspaces of $\varphi^*$, prove that

$$\pi_i^* = g_i(\varphi^*)$$

where the $\pi_i^*$ are the corresponding projection operators and the $g_i$ are the polynomials from Problem 7.
