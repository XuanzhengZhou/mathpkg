Consider the Fitting decomposition $E = F_0 \oplus F_1$ of a linear transformation $\varphi$.

a) Show that $F_0 = \bigcup_{j \geq 1} \ker \varphi^j$.

b) Show that $E = F_0 \oplus F_1$.

c) Prove that $F_0$ and $F_1$ are stable under $\varphi$ and that the restrictions $\varphi_0 : F_0 \rightarrow F_0$ and $\varphi_1 : F_1 \rightarrow F_1$ are respectively nilpotent and regular.

d) Prove that c) characterizes the decomposition $E = F_0 \oplus F_1$ and conclude that $F_0$ and $F_1$ are respectively the Fitting null and the Fitting $1$-component of $E$.
