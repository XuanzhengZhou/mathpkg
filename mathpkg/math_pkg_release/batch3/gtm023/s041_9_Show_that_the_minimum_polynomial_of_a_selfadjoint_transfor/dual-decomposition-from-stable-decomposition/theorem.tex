Let $E = F_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus F_r$ be a direct decomposition of $E$ into subspaces stable under $\varphi$. Then $E^*$ admits the dual decomposition
$$E^* = F_1^* \oplus \cdots \oplus F_r^*, \quad F_i^* = \left( \sum_{j \neq i} F_j \right)^\perp$$
into subspaces stable under $\varphi^*$. Each pair $F_i, F_i^*$ is dual, and the restrictions $\varphi_i = \varphi|_{F_i}$ and $\varphi_i^* = \varphi^*|_{F_i^*}$ are dual mappings.
