Consider the linear transformations of problem 7, §1. For each transformation:

a) Construct the decomposition of $\mathbb{R}^4$ into the generalized eigenspaces.

b) Determine the eigenspaces.

c) Calculate explicitly polynomials $g_i$ such that the $g_i(\varphi)$ are the projection operators in $E$ corresponding to the generalized eigenspaces. Verify by explicit consideration of the vectors $g_i(\varphi)x$ that the $g_i(\varphi)$ are in fact the projection operators.

d) Determine the Fitting decomposition of $E$.
