A linear transformation and its dual share the same minimum polynomial. This follows from the general identity $f(\varphi^*) = [f(\varphi)]^*$ which holds for any polynomial $f$. Since the dual of the zero transformation is the zero transformation, the annihilating polynomials for $\varphi$ and $\varphi^*$ are identical, and thus their minimum polynomials coincide.
