Let $E = E_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus E_r$ be the decomposition of $E$ into generalized eigenspaces corresponding to the factorization $\mu = f_1^{k_1} \cdots f_r^{k_r}$ of the minimum polynomial, and let $\pi_i$ be the projection operators associated with this decomposition. Define
$$g_i = f_1^{k_1} \cdots \widehat{f_i^{k_i}} \cdots f_r^{k_r}.$$
Then the $g_i$ are relatively prime, so there exist polynomials $h_i$ such that $\sum_i g_i h_i = 1$. The projection operators are given by
$$\pi_i = h_i(\varphi) g_i(\varphi) \quad i = 1, \ldots, r.$$
In particular, $\pi_i \in \Gamma(\varphi)$; i.e., each projection operator is a polynomial in $\varphi$.
