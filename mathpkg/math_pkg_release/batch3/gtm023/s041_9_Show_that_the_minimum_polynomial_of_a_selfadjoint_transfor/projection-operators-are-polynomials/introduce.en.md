When a linear transformation has a minimum polynomial that factors into powers of irreducible polynomials, the vector space admits a primary decomposition into generalized eigenspaces. A fundamental result is that the projection operators onto these generalized eigenspaces are themselves polynomial expressions in the original linear transformation. This means the decomposition is completely determined by $\varphi$ and its minimum polynomial, with no arbitrary choices involved.
