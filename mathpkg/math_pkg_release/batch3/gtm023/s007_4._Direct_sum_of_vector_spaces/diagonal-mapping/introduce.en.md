The diagonal mapping $\Delta: E \to E \oplus E$ sends each vector $x$ to the pair $(x, x)$. It embeds $E$ diagonally into the direct sum of two copies of itself. The diagonal mapping can be expressed as the sum of the two canonical injections, and composing it with either canonical projection recovers the identity on $E$.
