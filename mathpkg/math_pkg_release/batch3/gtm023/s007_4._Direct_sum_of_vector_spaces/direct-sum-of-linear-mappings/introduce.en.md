Given linear mappings $\varphi_1$ and $\varphi_2$, their direct sum $\varphi_1 \oplus \varphi_2$ acts componentwise on the direct sum of the domain spaces. This construction is functorial and compatible with the direct sum structure, making it a fundamental operation in linear algebra.
