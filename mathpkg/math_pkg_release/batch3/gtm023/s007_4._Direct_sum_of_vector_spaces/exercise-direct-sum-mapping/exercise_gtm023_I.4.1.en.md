Assume a decomposition
$$E = E_1 + E_2.$$
Consider the external direct sum $E_1 \oplus E_2$ and define a linear mapping $\varphi: E_1 \oplus E_2 \to E$ by
$$\varphi(x_1, x_2) = x_1 + x_2.$$

Show that $\varphi$ is surjective. Prove that $\varphi$ is injective if and only if $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$ (i.e., the sum is direct).
