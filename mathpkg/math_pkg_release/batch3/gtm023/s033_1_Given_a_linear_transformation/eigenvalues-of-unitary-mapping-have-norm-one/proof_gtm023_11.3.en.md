Let $\varphi e = \lambda e$ with $e \neq 0$. Since a unitary mapping preserves the norm, we have

$$|\varphi e| = |e|.$$

On the other hand,

$$|\varphi e| = |\lambda e| = |\lambda| \, |e|.$$

Equating the two expressions yields

$$|\lambda| \, |e| = |e|,$$

and since $e \neq 0$, we obtain $|\lambda| = 1$.
