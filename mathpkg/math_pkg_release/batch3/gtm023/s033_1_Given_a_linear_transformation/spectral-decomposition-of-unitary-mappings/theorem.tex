Let $\varphi$ be a unitary mapping on an $n$-dimensional unitary space $E$. Then $\varphi$ is normal, and there exists an orthonormal basis $e_\nu$ ($\nu = 1, \ldots, n$) of $E$ such that

$$\varphi e_\nu = \lambda_\nu e_\nu \quad (\nu = 1, \ldots, n),$$

where the $\lambda_\nu$ are complex numbers satisfying $|\lambda_\nu| = 1$.
