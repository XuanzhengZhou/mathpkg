For a linear transformation $\varphi$ of an $n$-dimensional space $E$, the characteristic polynomial is
$$\det(\varphi - \lambda\iota) = \sum_{p=0}^{n} \alpha_p \lambda^{n-p}$$
where $\alpha_0 = 1$ and the coefficients $\alpha_p$ are scalars determined by $\varphi$.

The determinant of $\varphi$ is $\det \varphi = \alpha_n$, and the trace of $\varphi$ is $\operatorname{tr} \varphi = -\alpha_1$.

More explicitly,
$$\alpha_p = (-1)^p \sum_{v_1 < \cdots < v_p} \det(\varphi_{v_i}^{v_j})$$
where $\det(\varphi_{v_i}^{v_j})$ is the principal minor of order $p$ of the matrix of $\varphi$.
