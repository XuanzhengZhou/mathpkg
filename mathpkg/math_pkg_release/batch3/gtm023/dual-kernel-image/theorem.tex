Let $\varphi: E \to F$ and $\varphi^*: E^* \leftarrow F^*$ be a pair of dual mappings. Then
$$\ker \varphi^* = (\operatorname{Im} \varphi)^\perp$$
and
$$\ker \varphi = (\operatorname{Im} \varphi^*)^\perp.$$

If $\varphi$ is surjective, then $\varphi^*$ is injective. For $L(E)$, the converse also holds: if $\varphi$ is injective, then $\varphi^*: L(E) \leftarrow L(F)$ is surjective, and
$$\operatorname{Im} \varphi^* = (\ker \varphi)^\perp.$$
