Select orthonormal bases in $F$ and $F^\perp$. These two bases form an orthonormal basis of $E$ in which the matrix of $\varphi$ has the form

$$\begin{bmatrix}
\cosh \theta & \sinh \theta & 0 & 0 \\
\sinh \theta & \cosh \theta & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & \varepsilon & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 0 & \varepsilon
\end{bmatrix}, \quad \theta \neq 0, \quad \varepsilon = \pm 1.$$
