The characteristic polynomial $\chi_1$ of the induced mapping $\varphi_1: E_1 \to E_1$, computed from the matrix form of the mapping, is

$$\chi_1 = (t^2 - 2t \cos \omega + 1)(1 - t).$$

At the same time, since the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$ is $(t-1)^k$, the characteristic polynomial of $\varphi_1$ must be

$$\chi_1 = (1 - t)^3.$$

Comparing these two expressions yields $\omega = 0$. Consequently, the mapping equations reduce to

$$\varphi e_1 = e_1 + \alpha_1 e, \quad \varphi e_2 = e_2 + \alpha_2 e, \quad \varphi e = e.$$
