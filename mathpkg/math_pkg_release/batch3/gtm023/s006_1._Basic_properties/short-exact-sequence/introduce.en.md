A short exact sequence is a five-term exact sequence beginning and ending with the zero vector space. It encodes the information that $F$ embeds as a subspace of $E$ via $\varphi$, and $G$ is isomorphic to the quotient $E / \operatorname{Im} \varphi$. The canonical example is $0 \to E_1 \xrightarrow{i} E \xrightarrow{\pi} E/E_1 \to 0$ for any subspace $E_1 \subset E$.
