Let $\varphi: E \to E$ be a linear transformation of the vector space $E$. A subspace $E_1 \subset E$ is called \text{stable} (or \text{invariant}) under $\varphi$ if
$$
\varphi(x) \in E_1 \quad \text{for all} \quad x \in E_1.
$$
The restriction $\varphi_1 = \varphi|_{E_1}: E_1 \to E_1$ is then a linear transformation of $E_1$, and $\varphi$ induces a linear transformation
$$
\bar{\varphi}: E/E_1 \to E/E_1
$$
on the quotient space.
