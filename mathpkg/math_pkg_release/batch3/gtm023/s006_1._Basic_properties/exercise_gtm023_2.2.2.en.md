Suppose
$$\varphi_1, \psi_1, \chi_1: E \to F \quad \text{and} \quad \varphi_2, \psi_2, \chi_2: F \to G$$
are linear mappings. Assume that $\varphi_1, \varphi_2$ are injective, $\psi_1, \psi_2$ are surjective, and $\chi_1, \chi_2$ are bijective. Prove that:
a) $\varphi_2 \circ \varphi_1$ is injective.
b) $\psi_2 \circ \psi_1$ is surjective.
c) $\chi_2 \circ \chi_1$ is bijective.
