Let $\varphi: E \to F$ be a linear mapping and let $\pi: E \to E/\ker \varphi$ be the canonical projection. Then $\varphi$ induces an injective linear mapping
$$
\bar{\varphi}: E/\ker \varphi \to F
$$
such that $\bar{\varphi} \circ \pi = \varphi$. Consequently,
$$
E/\ker \varphi \xrightarrow{\cong} \operatorname{Im} \varphi.
$$
