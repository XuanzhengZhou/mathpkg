Let $\varphi: E \to F$ and $\psi: E \to G$ be linear mappings such that
$$
\ker \varphi \subset \ker \psi.
$$
Then $\psi$ can be factored over $\varphi$; that is, there exists a linear mapping $\chi: F \to G$ such that the following diagram commutes:
$$
\begin{CD}
E @>{\varphi}>> F \\
@V{\psi}VV @AA{\chi}A \\
G
\end{CD}
$$
Equivalently, $\chi \circ \varphi = \psi$.
