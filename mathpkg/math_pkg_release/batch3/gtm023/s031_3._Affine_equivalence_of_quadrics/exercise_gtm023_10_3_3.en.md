Denote by $N_1$ and $N_2$ the total number of affine classes with center and without center, respectively, in an $n$-dimensional affine space. Prove that
$$N_1 = \frac{n(n+1)}{2},$$
$$N_2 = \begin{cases} k^2 + k - 1 & \text{if } n = 2k, \\ k^2 + 2k & \text{if } n = 2k + 1. \end{cases}$$
