Let $x_1$ and $x_2$ be two points of the quadric
$$Q: \Phi(x) = 1.$$
Assume that an isomorphism $\tau: T_{x_1} \to T_{x_2}$ between the tangent spaces is given with the property that $\Phi(y) = \Phi(\tau y)$ for all $y \in T_{x_1}$. Show that there exists an affine automorphism of $Q$ which maps $x_1$ into $x_2$.
