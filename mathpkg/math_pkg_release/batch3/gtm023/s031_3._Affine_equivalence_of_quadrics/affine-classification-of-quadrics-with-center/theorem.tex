Consider two quadrics with center
$$Q_1: \Phi_1(x - c_1) = 1, \qquad Q_2: \Phi_2(x - c_2) = 1$$
in an affine space $A$. The two quadrics $Q_1$ and $Q_2$ are affine equivalent if and only if the symmetric bilinear functions $\Phi_1$ and $\Phi_2$ have the same rank and the same index:
$$r_1 = r_2 \quad \text{and} \quad s_1 = s_2.$$
