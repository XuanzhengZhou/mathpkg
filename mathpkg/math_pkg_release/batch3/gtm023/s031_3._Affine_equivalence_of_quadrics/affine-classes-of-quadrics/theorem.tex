The normal forms
$$\sum_{v=1}^{s} \xi^v \xi^v - \sum_{v=s+1}^{r} \xi^v \xi^v = 1 \quad (1 \leq s \leq r)$$
and
$$\sum_{v=1}^{s} \xi^v \xi^v - \sum_{v=s+1}^{r} \xi^v \xi^v + 2\xi = 0 \quad (r \leq 2s)$$
form a complete system of representatives of the affine equivalence classes of quadrics in an $n$-dimensional affine space. The total numbers $N_1(r)$ and $N_2(r)$ of affine classes with center and without center, respectively, of a given rank $r$ are
$$N_1(r) = r, \qquad N_2(r) = \begin{cases} \frac{r+1}{2} & \text{if } r \text{ is odd}, \\ \frac{r+2}{2} & \text{if } r \text{ is even}, \end{cases} \quad (1 \leq r \leq n-1).$$
