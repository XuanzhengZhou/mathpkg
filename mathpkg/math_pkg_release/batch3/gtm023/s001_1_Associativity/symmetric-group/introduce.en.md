The symmetric group $S_n$ is the group of all permutations of an $n$-element set, with composition of permutations as the group operation. It is a fundamental example of a finite group and plays a central role in group theory, combinatorics, and the theory of group representations. Every finite group is isomorphic to a subgroup of some symmetric group (Cayley's theorem).
