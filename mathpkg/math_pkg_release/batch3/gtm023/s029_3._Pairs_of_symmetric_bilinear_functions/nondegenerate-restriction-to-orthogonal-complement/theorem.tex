Let $E$ be an $n$-dimensional real vector space, $\Psi$ a non-degenerate symmetric bilinear function on $E$, and let $e_1 \in E$ be a vector with $\Psi(e_1, e_1) \neq 0$. Define the subspace
$$E_1 = \{ z \in E : \Psi(e_1, z) = 0 \}.$$
Then $\dim E_1 = n - 1$ and the restriction of $\Psi$ to $E_1$ is again non-degenerate.
