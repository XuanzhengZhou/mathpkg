This corollary expresses the block-diagonal structure of a projection operator with respect to the direct sum decomposition $E = \ker \varphi \oplus \operatorname{Im} \varphi$. The projection acts as the identity on its image subspace and as the zero transformation on its kernel. This representation makes explicit the fact that a projection operator is completely determined by the choice of which subspace it projects onto (its image) and which complementary subspace it annihilates (its kernel).
