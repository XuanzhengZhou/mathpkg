For any projection operator $\varphi$ on a vector space $E$, the space decomposes as the direct sum of the kernel and the image of $\varphi$:

$$
E = \ker \varphi \oplus \operatorname{Im} \varphi.
$$

Moreover, $\varphi$ acts as the identity on $\operatorname{Im} \varphi$ and as the zero map on $\ker \varphi$:

$$
\varphi = \iota_{\operatorname{Im} \varphi} \oplus 0_{\ker \varphi}.
$$
