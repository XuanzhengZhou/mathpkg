Let $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$ and $F = F_1 \oplus F_2$ be decompositions of $E$ and $F$ as direct sums of subspaces. Show that the external direct sum, $G$, of $E$ and $F$ can be written as $G = G_1 \oplus G_2$ where $G_1$ and $G_2$ are subspaces of $G$ and $G_i$ is the external direct sum of $E_i$ and $F_i$ ($i = 1, 2$).
