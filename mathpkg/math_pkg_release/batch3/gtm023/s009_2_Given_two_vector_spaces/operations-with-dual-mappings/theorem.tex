Assume that $E^*$, $E$ and $F^*$, $F$ are two pairs of dual vector spaces. Assume further that $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ and $\psi: E \rightarrow F$ are linear mappings and that there exist dual mappings $\varphi^*: E^* \leftarrow F^*$ and $\psi^*: E^* \leftarrow F^*$. Then there are mappings dual to $\varphi + \psi$ and $\lambda \varphi$, and these dual mappings are given by
$$(\varphi + \psi)^* = \varphi^* + \psi^*$$
and
$$(\lambda \varphi)^* = \lambda \varphi^*.$$
