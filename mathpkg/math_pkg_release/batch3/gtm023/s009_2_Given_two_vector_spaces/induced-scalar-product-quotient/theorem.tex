Suppose that $E^*$, $E$ are a pair of dual spaces and that $F$ is a subspace of $E$. Then a scalar product is induced in the pair $E^* / F^\perp$, $F$ by
$$\langle \bar{x}^*, y \rangle = \langle x^*, y \rangle, \quad \bar{x}^* \in E^* / F^\perp, \; y \in F,$$
where $x^*$ is a representative of the class $\bar{x}^*$.
