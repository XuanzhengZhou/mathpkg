Consider the dual pairs $E^*$, $E$ and $E^*/E_1^\perp$, $E_1$ where $E_1$ is a subspace of $E$. Let $\pi$ be the canonical projection of $E^*$ onto $E^*/E_1^\perp$,
$$\pi: E^*/E_1^\perp \leftarrow E^*.$$
Let $i: E_1 \rightarrow E$ be the canonical injection. Then $i$ is dual to $\pi$, i.e.,
$$\pi = i^*.$$
