Given an arbitrary bilinear function $\Phi$, let $N_E$ and $N_F$ be its nullspaces and let
$$\pi_E: E \rightarrow E/N_E, \quad \pi_F: F \rightarrow F/N_F$$
be the canonical projections. Then $\Phi$ induces a non-degenerate bilinear function $\bar{\Phi}$ in $E/N_E \times F/N_F$ such that
$$\bar{\Phi}(\pi_E x, \pi_F y) = \Phi(x, y).$$
