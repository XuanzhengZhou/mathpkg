Given two vector spaces $E$ and $F$, consider subspaces $E_1 \subset E$, $F_1 \subset F$ and the canonical projections

$$\pi_E: E \rightarrow E/E_1, \quad \pi_F: F \rightarrow F/F_1.$$

Define a mapping

$$\varphi: E \oplus F \rightarrow E/E_1 \oplus F/F_1$$

by

$$\varphi(x, y) = (\pi_E x, \pi_F y).$$

Show that $\varphi$ induces a linear isomorphism

$$\bar{\varphi}: (E \oplus F)/(E_1 \oplus F_1) \rightarrow E/E_1 \oplus F/F_1.$$
