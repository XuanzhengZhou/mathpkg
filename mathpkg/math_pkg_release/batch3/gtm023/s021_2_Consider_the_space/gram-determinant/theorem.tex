Let \(E\) be an inner product space and \(x_1, \ldots, x_p \in E\). The **Gram determinant** \(G(x_1, \ldots, x_p)\) is defined by

$$G(x_1, \ldots, x_p) = \det \begin{pmatrix}
(x_1, x_1) & \cdots & (x_1, x_p) \\
\vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
(x_p, x_1) & \cdots & (x_p, x_p)
\end{pmatrix}.$$

Then \(G(x_1, \ldots, x_p) \geq 0\), and equality holds if and only if the vectors \(x_1, \ldots, x_p\) are linearly dependent.
