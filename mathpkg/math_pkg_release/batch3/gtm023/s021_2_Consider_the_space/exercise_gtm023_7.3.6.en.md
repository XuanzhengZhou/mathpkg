Consider an oriented inner product space of dimension 2. Given two positive orthonormal bases \((e_1, e_2)\) and \((\bar{e}_1, \bar{e}_2)\), prove that

$$\bar{e}_1 = e_1 \cos \omega - e_2 \sin \omega,$$
$$\bar{e}_2 = e_1 \sin \omega + e_2 \cos \omega,$$

where \(\omega\) is the oriented angle between \(e_1\) and \(\bar{e}_1\).
