Let \(C\) be the space of all continuous functions in the interval \(0 \leq t \leq 1\) with the inner product defined as in sec. 7.2. If \(C^1\) denotes the subspace of all continuously differentiable functions, show that \((C^1)^{\perp} = 0\).
