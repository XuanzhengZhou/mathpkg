Let \(e\) be a given unit vector of an \(n\)-dimensional inner product space \(E\) and \(E_1\) be the orthogonal complement of \(e\). Show that the distance of a vector \(x \in E\) from the subspace \(E_1\) is given by

$$d = |(x, e)|.$$
