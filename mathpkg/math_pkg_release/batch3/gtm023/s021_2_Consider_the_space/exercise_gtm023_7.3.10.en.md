Let \(e_i\) (\(i = 1, 2, 3\)) be unit vectors in an oriented plane and denote by \(\Theta_{ij}\) the oriented angle determined by \(e_i\) and \(e_j\) (\(i < j\)). Prove the formulae

$$\cos \Theta_{13} = \cos \Theta_{12} \cos \Theta_{23} - \sin \Theta_{12} \sin \Theta_{23},$$
$$\sin \Theta_{13} = \sin \Theta_{12} \cos \Theta_{23} + \cos \Theta_{12} \sin \Theta_{23}.$$
