Consider the space \(S\) of all infinite sequences \(x = (\xi_1, \xi_2, \ldots)\) such that

$$\sum_{\nu} \xi_{\nu}^2 < \infty.$$

Show that \(\sum_{\nu} \xi_{\nu} \eta_{\nu}\) converges and that the bilinear function \((x, y) = \sum_{\nu} \xi_{\nu} \eta_{\nu}\) is an inner product.
