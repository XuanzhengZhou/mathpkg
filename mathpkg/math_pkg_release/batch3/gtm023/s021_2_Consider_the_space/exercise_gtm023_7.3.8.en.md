Given an orthonormal basis \(e_{\nu}\) (\(\nu = 1, 2, 3\)) in the 3-space, define linear transformations \(\varphi_{\nu}\) by

$$\varphi_{\nu} x = x \times e_{\nu} \quad (\nu = 1, 2, 3).$$

Prove that

$$\sum_{\nu} \varphi_{\nu}^2 = -2i.$$
