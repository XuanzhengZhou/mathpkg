The subspace $K(f) = \ker f(\varphi)$ consists of vectors annihilated by the polynomial $f$ evaluated at $\varphi$. These subspaces are $\varphi$-stable and form a lattice: $g \mid f$ implies $K(g) \subset K(f)$. The intersection formula $K(\gcd(f,g)) = K(f) \cap K(g)$ is key to the primary decomposition theorem.
