The uniqueness follows from Proposition I. For existence, note that the condition of Proposition I is satisfied because any relation $\sum_\alpha \lambda^\alpha x_\alpha = 0$ among basis vectors has all $\lambda^\alpha = 0$ by linear independence, so $\sum_\alpha \lambda^\alpha \varphi_0 x_\alpha = \sum_\alpha 0 \cdot \varphi_0 x_\alpha = 0$.
