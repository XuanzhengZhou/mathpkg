Prove the following exchange theorem of Steinitz: Let $(x_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ be a basis of $E$ and $a_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, p$) be a system of linearly independent vectors. Then it is possible to exchange certain $p$ of the vectors $x_\alpha$ by the vectors $a_i$ such that the new system is again a basis of $E$.
