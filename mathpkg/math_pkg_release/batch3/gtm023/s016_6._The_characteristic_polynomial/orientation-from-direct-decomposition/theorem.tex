Consider a direct decomposition $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$ and assume orientations are defined in $E_1$ and $E_2$. Let $a_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, p$) and $b_j$ ($j = 1, \ldots, q$) be positive bases of $E_1$ and $E_2$. Then choose the orientation of $E$ such that the basis $a_1, \ldots, a_p, b_1, \ldots, b_q$ is positive. This orientation depends only on the orientations of $E_1$ and $E_2$.
