Let a Lorentz-transformation $\varphi$ be defined by the matrix

$$\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{1}{2} & \frac{2}{3} & -1 & -\frac{5}{6} \\[4pt]
\frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{9} & \frac{4}{3} & \frac{10}{9} \\[4pt]
1 & -\frac{4}{3} & 1 & \frac{5}{3} \\[4pt]
\frac{5}{6} & -\frac{10}{9} & \frac{5}{3} & \frac{43}{18}
\end{bmatrix}$$

Construct a decomposition of $\varphi$ into plane rotations.
