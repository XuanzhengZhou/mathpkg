Consider a 3-dimensional pseudo-Euclidean space $E$ with the index 2. Let $l_i$ ($i = 1, 2, 3$) be three light-vectors such that

$$(l_i, l_j) = 1 \quad i \neq j.$$

Define a linear transformation $\varphi$ by the equations

$$\varphi l_1 = l_1$$
$$\varphi l_2 = \alpha (\alpha - 1) l_1 + \alpha l_2 + (1 - \alpha) l_3 \quad \alpha \neq 1$$
$$\varphi l_3 = (\alpha - 2) (\alpha - 1) l_1 + (\alpha - 1) l_2 + (2 - \alpha) l_3.$$

Prove that $\varphi$ is a rotation and that $E$ is irreducible with respect to $\varphi$.
