Let $\alpha$ be the linear transformation of a complex plane defined by the matrix

$$\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2i \\
-i & 3
\end{pmatrix}.$$

Find the real $4 \times 4$ matrix which corresponds to the Lorentz-transformation $T_{\alpha}$ with respect to the basis $\iota, \sigma_1, \sigma_2, \sigma_3$ (cf. sec. 11.16).
