Every Lorentz transformation $T_{\alpha}: S \to S$ obtained from a linear transformation $\alpha$ of $C$ with $\det \alpha = 1$ is proper and orthochroneous. That is,

$$\det T_{\alpha} = 1,$$

and $T_{\alpha}$ preserves the fore-cone and past-cone (the causal structure) of the indefinite inner product space $S$.
