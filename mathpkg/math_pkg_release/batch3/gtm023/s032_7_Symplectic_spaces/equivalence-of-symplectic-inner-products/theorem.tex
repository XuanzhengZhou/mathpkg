If $\Phi$ and $\Psi$ are symplectic inner products in $E$, then there is a linear automorphism relating them whose matrix relative to a symplectic basis has the block form
$$\begin{pmatrix} A & B \\ C & D \end{pmatrix}$$
where $A, B, C, D$ are square $(n \times n)$-matrices satisfying
$$B^* = B, \quad C^* = C, \quad D = -A^*.$$
Consequently, the dimensions of the spaces of symplectic selfadjoint and skew symplectic transformations are given by
$$N_1 = n(2n - 1) \quad N_2 = n(2n + 1).$$
