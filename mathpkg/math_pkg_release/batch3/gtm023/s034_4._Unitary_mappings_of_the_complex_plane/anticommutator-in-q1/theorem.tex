For $\varphi, \psi \in Q_1$ (the traceless subalgebra of $Q$), the symmetrized product satisfies
$$
\frac{1}{2}(\varphi \circ \psi + \psi \circ \varphi) = -(\varphi, \psi) \cdot \iota.
$$
In particular, $\varphi$ and $\psi$ anticommute if and only if they are orthogonal with respect to the inner product on $Q$.
