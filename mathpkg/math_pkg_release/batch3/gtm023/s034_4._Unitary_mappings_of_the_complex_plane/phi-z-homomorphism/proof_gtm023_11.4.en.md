Both $\varphi_{z_1 z_2}$ and $\varphi_{z_2} \circ \varphi_{z_1}$ belong to $Q$ and map the fixed unit vector $a$ to the same vector:
$$
(\varphi_{z_2} \circ \varphi_{z_1}) a = \varphi_{z_2} (\varphi_{z_1} a) = \varphi_{z_2} z_1 = z_1 z_2 = \varphi_{z_1 z_2} a.
$$
Since an element of $Q$ is uniquely determined by its action on $a$ (the mapping $z \mapsto \varphi_z$ is a bijection), the two transformations must be equal. Hence $\varphi_{z_1 z_2} = \varphi_{z_2} \circ \varphi_{z_1}$.
