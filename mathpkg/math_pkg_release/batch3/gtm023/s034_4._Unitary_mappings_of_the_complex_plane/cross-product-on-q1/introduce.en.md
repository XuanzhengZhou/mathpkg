On the traceless subalgebra $Q_1 = \{\varphi \in Q \mid \operatorname{tr} \varphi = 0\}$, the commutator of any two elements remains in $Q_1$, allowing the definition of a bilinear cross product $\varphi \times \psi = \frac{1}{2}(\varphi \circ \psi - \psi \circ \varphi)$. This operation mimics the cross product on $\mathbb{R}^3$ and equips $Q_1$ with the structure of the pure imaginary quaternions, satisfying orthogonality $(\varphi \times \psi, \varphi) = (\varphi \times \psi, \psi) = 0$.
