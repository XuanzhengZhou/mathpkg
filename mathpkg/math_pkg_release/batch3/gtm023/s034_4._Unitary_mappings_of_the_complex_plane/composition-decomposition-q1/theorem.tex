For $\varphi, \psi \in Q_1$, the composition $\varphi \circ \psi$ decomposes as
$$
\varphi \circ \psi = -(\varphi, \psi) \iota + \varphi \times \psi,
$$
where $(\varphi, \psi)$ is the inner product and $\varphi \times \psi = \frac{1}{2}(\varphi \circ \psi - \psi \circ \varphi)$ is the cross product on $Q_1$. This is the quaternionic multiplication formula: the product of two pure quaternions equals minus their dot product plus their cross product.
