In the traceless subalgebra $Q_1 = \{\varphi \in Q \mid \operatorname{tr} \varphi = 0\}$, the condition $\tilde{\varphi} = -\varphi$ together with the fundamental identity (11.34) gives $\varphi^2 = -(\varphi, \varphi) \iota$. This means each traceless element squares to a negative real multiple of the identity, exactly as the pure imaginary quaternions do. The norm $|\varphi| = \sqrt{(\varphi, \varphi)}$ thus measures the "length" of the corresponding pure quaternion.
