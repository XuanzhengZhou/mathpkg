Consider the canonical projection $\pi: E \rightarrow E/E_1$. Then $\ker \pi = E_1$. By Corollary II (which states $(\ker \varphi)^{\perp\perp} = \ker \varphi$ for $\varphi$ into $L(E)$), we have $E_1^{\perp\perp} = (\ker \pi)^{\perp\perp} = \ker \pi = E_1$.
