This lemma gives a three-term recurrence for the determinant of a tridiagonal matrix with $x_i$ on the diagonal, $1$ on the superdiagonal, and $-1$ on the subdiagonal. The recurrence $\Delta_n = x_n \Delta_{n-1} + \Delta_{n-2}$ follows from the Laplace expansion along the last row. Such recurrences are fundamental in the study of orthogonal polynomials (where the $x_i$ are related to moments), continued fractions, and the spectral theory of Jacobi matrices.
