Direct sum decompositions of $E$ induce direct sum decompositions of $L(E)$ via annihilators. Proposition V shows $L(E_1 \oplus E_2) \cong L(E_1) \oplus L(E_2)$. Proposition VI provides a converse: a dual pair $E_1, E_1^*$ with the double annihilator property determines complementary summands. These results establish a correspondence between direct decompositions and duality.
