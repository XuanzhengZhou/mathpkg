The scalar term of a polynomial is its constant term $\alpha_0$. The map sending each polynomial to its scalar term is a surjective algebra homomorphism from $\Gamma[t]$ onto $\Gamma$. This homomorphism corresponds to evaluation at $t = 0$ and its kernel consists of all polynomials with zero constant term.
