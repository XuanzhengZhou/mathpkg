When the GCD $f$ is factored out of two polynomials as $f_i = f h_i$, the cofactors $h_1$ and $h_2$ are relatively prime. The LCM is then $f h_1 h_2$, giving the product formula $f_1 f_2 = \gcd(f_1, f_2) \cdot \operatorname{lcm}(f_1, f_2)$. This mirrors the integer identity and is fundamental to polynomial arithmetic.
