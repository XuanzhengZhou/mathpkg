The least common multiple (LCM) of monic polynomials is the unique monic polynomial whose principal ideal equals the intersection of the ideals generated by the polynomials. The LCM is a common multiple, and every common multiple is a multiple of the LCM. The LCM of several polynomials is defined analogously via intersection of ideals.
