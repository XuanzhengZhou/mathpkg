The least common multiple $f \wedge g$ of monic polynomials $f$ and $g$ is defined by
$$I_{f \wedge g} = I_f \cap I_g.$$
$f \wedge g$ is a common multiple of $f$ and $g$, and every common multiple of $f$ and $g$ is a multiple of $f \wedge g$. For monic polynomials $f_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, r$), the least common multiple is defined by
$$I_{f_1 \wedge \cdots \wedge f_r} = I_{f_1} \cap \cdots \cap I_{f_r}.$$
