The polynomial algebra $\Gamma[t]$ is a unique factorization domain: every monic polynomial factors uniquely into powers of distinct irreducible monic polynomials. This is the polynomial analogue of the fundamental theorem of arithmetic. The proof uses induction on degree for existence and a key lemma about irreducible divisors for uniqueness.
