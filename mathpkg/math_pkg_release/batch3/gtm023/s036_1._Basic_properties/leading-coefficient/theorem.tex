Let $f = \sum_{v=0}^{n} \alpha_v t^v$ with $\alpha_n \neq 0$ be a non-zero polynomial. Then $\alpha_n$ is called the leading coefficient of $f$. The leading coefficient of $fg$ ($f \neq 0, g \neq 0$) is the product of the leading coefficients of $f$ and $g$. This implies that $fg \neq 0$ whenever $f \neq 0$ and $g \neq 0$.
