The evaluation homomorphism $\Phi: \Gamma[t] \to \Gamma(a)$ factors uniquely through the quotient by the minimum polynomial's ideal, giving an isomorphism $\Gamma[t]/I_{\mu} \cong \Gamma(a)$. This identifies the subalgebra generated by $a$ with a quotient of the polynomial algebra. The structure of $\Gamma(a)$ is thus completely determined by the minimum polynomial $\mu$.
