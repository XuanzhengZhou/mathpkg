The evaluation homomorphism $\Phi: \Gamma[t] \to A$ factors through its kernel to give an injective homomorphism $\bar{\Phi}: \Gamma[t]/\ker\Phi \to A$. When $A$ is generated by the unit $e$ and the element $a$, this map becomes an isomorphism, identifying $A$ with a quotient of the polynomial algebra. This is a fundamental structure theorem for singly-generated commutative algebras.
