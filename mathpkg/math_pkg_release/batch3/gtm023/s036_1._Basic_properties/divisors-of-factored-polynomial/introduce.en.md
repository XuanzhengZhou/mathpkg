A monic polynomial $g$ divides $f = \prod f_i^{k_i}$ (the prime factorization) if and only if $g$ is of the form $\prod f_i^{j_i}$ with $0 \leq j_i \leq k_i$. This characterizes all divisors of a polynomial in terms of its prime factorization, generalizing the analogous statement for integers.
