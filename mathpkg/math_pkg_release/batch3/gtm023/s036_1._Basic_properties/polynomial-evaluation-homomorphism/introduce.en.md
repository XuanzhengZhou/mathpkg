The polynomial evaluation homomorphism is the unique algebra homomorphism from $\Gamma[t]$ to any associative algebra $A$ sending $1$ to the unit $e$ and $t$ to a chosen element $a$. This universal property characterizes $\Gamma[t]$ as the free commutative algebra on one generator. The image $\Gamma(a)$ is the subalgebra generated by $e$ and $a$, consisting of all polynomial expressions in $a$.
