The degree of a sum of two polynomials is bounded above by the maximum of their degrees, with equality unless the leading terms cancel. The degree of a product equals the sum of the degrees, reflecting the fact that the leading coefficient of the product is the product of the leading coefficients. These formulas make the degree function a valuation on $\Gamma[t]$.
