A field \(F\) is said to be \textbf{ordered} if there is a subset \(P \subset F\) such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] \(P \cap (-P) = \varnothing\);
\item[(ii)] \(P \cup \{0\} \cup (-P) = F\);
\item[(iii)] \(a, b \in P\) imply \(a + b \in P\) and \(ab \in P\).
\end{enumerate}
Elements of \(P\) are called \textbf{positive}.
