Let $X$ be a locally compact Hausdorff space and let $L \in \mathfrak{C}_0(X)^*$ (a bounded linear functional on continuous functions vanishing at infinity). Then there exists a unique complex regular Borel measure $\mu \in M(X)$ such that $L(f) = \int_X f d\mu$ for all $f \in \mathfrak{C}_0(X)$ and $\|L\| = |\mu|(X)$.
