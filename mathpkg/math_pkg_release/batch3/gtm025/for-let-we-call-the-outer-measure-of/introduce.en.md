(9.19) Definition. For $A \subset X$ let $\iota(A) = \bar{I}(\xi_A)$. We call $\iota(A)$ the [outer] measure of $A$. The function $\iota$, defined on is a definition from Chapter 9 (Extending Functionals) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
