Let $A$ and $B$ be normed linear spaces and let $T$ be a bounded linear transformation from $A$ into $B$. Then

$$\| T \| = \sup \left\{ \frac{\| T(x) \|}{\| x \|} : x \in A, x \neq 0 \right\}$$

$$= \sup \left\{ \| T(x) \| : x \in A, \| x \| = 1 \right\}$$

$$= \sup \left\{ \| T(x) \| : x \in A, \| x \| \leq 1 \right\},$$

and

$$\| T(x) \| \leq \| T \| \cdot \| x \|$$ for all $x \in A$.
