(13.47) Theorem. Notation is as in (13.41). Suppose that $p = 1$, that $f_n \to f \mu$-a.e., and that $\|f_n\|_1 \to \|f\|_1$. Then is a theorem from Chapter 13 (Spaces of Integrable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
