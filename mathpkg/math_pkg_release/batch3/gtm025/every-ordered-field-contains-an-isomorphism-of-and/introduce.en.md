(5.9) Theorem. Every ordered field $F$ contains an isomorphism of $Q$, and the isomorphism can be taken as order-preserving. is a theorem from Chapter 5 (Set Theory and Algebra) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of set-theory.
