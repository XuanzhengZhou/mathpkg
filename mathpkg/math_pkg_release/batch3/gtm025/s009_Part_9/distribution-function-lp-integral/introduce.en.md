This corollary specializes the layer cake representation to $\varphi(t) = t^p$, giving the $L^p$ integral via the distribution function. For $p=1$, this can serve as an alternative definition of the Lebesgue integral. This formula is essential for proving Hardy-Littlewood maximal inequalities and for interpolation theory.
