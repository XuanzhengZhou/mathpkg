This lemma provides refined measure estimates for the Hardy-Littlewood maximal function by introducing a parameter $k \in (0,1)$. The maximal sets are controlled by integrals restricted to $\{f > kt\}$ rather than to the maximal sets themselves. These estimates are crucial for proving the $\Omega_1$ maximal theorem (21.80).
