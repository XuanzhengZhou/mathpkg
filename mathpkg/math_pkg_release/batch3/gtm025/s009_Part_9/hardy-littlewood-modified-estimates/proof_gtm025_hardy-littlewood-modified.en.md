Define $g(x) = f(x)$ if $f(x) > kt$, $0$ otherwise. Then $f^{\Lambda(r)}(x) \leq g^{\Lambda(r)}(x) + kt$, so $M_t^{(r)} \subset N_{(1-k)t}$ where $N_u = \{g^{\Lambda(r)} > u\}$. Applying (21.75.i) to $g$ and noting $g$ vanishes on $\{f \leq kt\}$ gives the result.
