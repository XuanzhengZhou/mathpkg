(a) Let $\varphi \in \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$. Suppose $\varphi$ is twice differentiable on $R$, $\varphi', \varphi'' \in \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$, and $\varphi, \varphi'$ are absolutely continuous on $R$. Prove there exists $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ such that $\hat{f} = \varphi$. [Use (21.59.b).]

(b) Compute $\int_R [\frac{\sin(ay)}{y}]^3 dy$. [Use (21.53.ii), (a), and (21.55).]

(c) Evaluate $\int_R [\frac{\sin(ay)}{y}]^n dy$ for $a \in R$ and $n \in \{5,6,7,\ldots\}$.
