With convolution as multiplication, $\mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ is a complex commutative Banach algebra. For all $f,g,h \in \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ and $\alpha \in K$,
$$f * (g * h) = (f * g) * h, \quad f * (g + h) = (f * g) + (f * h), \quad \alpha (f * g) = (\alpha f) * g = f * (\alpha g),$$
and $\|f * g\|_1 \leq \|f\|_1 \|g\|_1$.
