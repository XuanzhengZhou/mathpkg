The reader should verify associativity, distributivity, and scalar homogeneity of convolution. By (21.32), $\|f * g\|_1 \leq \|f\|_1 \|g\|_1$, which together with the algebraic identities establishes that $\mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ is a complex commutative Banach algebra.
