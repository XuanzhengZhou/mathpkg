This theorem extends classical integration-by-parts to Lebesgue-Stieltjes integrals with respect to arbitrary nondecreasing functions. It handles discontinuities using right/left limits and reduces to the absolutely continuous case (18.19) when both functions are absolutely continuous. The formula extends to functions of finite variation via Jordan decomposition.
