Let $\alpha,\beta$ be real-valued nondecreasing functions on $R$ with Lebesgue-Stieltjes measures $\lambda_\alpha,\lambda_\beta$. For $a<b$:
$$\int_{[a,b]} \beta(x+) d\lambda_\alpha(x) + \int_{[a,b]} \alpha(x-) d\lambda_\beta(x) = \alpha(b+)\beta(b+) - \alpha(a-)\beta(a-),$$
and the symmetric form with averages $(\beta(x+)+\beta(x-))/2$, $(\alpha(x+)+\alpha(x-))/2$.
