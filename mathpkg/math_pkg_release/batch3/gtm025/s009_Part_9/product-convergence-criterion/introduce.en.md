This elementary lemma characterizes when an infinite product $\prod(1-\alpha_k)$ with factors in $[0,1]$ vanishes: precisely when $\sum \alpha_k$ diverges. The proof uses the exponential inequality $1-x \leq e^{-x}$ for divergence and logarithmic estimates for convergence. This criterion is essential for the Borel-Cantelli lemma in product spaces and appears frequently in probability theory.
