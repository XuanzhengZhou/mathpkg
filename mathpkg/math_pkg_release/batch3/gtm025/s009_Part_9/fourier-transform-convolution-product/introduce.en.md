This theorem states that the Fourier transform converts convolution in $\mathfrak{L}_1(R)$ into pointwise multiplication. This makes the Fourier transform a homomorphism from the convolution algebra to the pointwise multiplication algebra of continuous functions vanishing at infinity. This property is essential for solving differential equations and all of harmonic analysis.
