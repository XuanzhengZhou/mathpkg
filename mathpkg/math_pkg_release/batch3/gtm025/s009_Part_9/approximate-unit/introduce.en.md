An approximate unit (positive kernel) is a sequence of nonnegative $L^1$ functions with unit norm whose mass concentrates near the origin. It serves as a substitute for an identity element in the Banach algebra $\mathfrak{L}_1(R)$. Approximate units are fundamental for proving approximation and reconstruction results in harmonic analysis.
