Use (21.72) and (21.79): $\int_E f^{\Lambda(j)} d\lambda = \int_0^\infty \lambda(M_t^{(j)} \cap E) dt$. Split at $1/k$, bound the first part by $\lambda(E)/k$, and apply (21.79) plus Fubini to the second. The inner integral yields $\log f(x)$ when $f(x) > 1$, giving the $\log^+$ term. For $0 < p < 1$, a similar computation with careful parameter optimization gives the stated bound.
