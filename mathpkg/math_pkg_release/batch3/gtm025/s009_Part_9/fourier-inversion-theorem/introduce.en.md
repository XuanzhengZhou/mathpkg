The Fourier Inversion Theorem shows how to reconstruct a function $f$ from its Fourier transform $\hat{f}$: apply the Fourier transform again with a sign change in the exponential. When both $f$ and $\hat{f}$ are integrable, the formula holds at every Lebesgue point. This is the continuous analogue of the Fourier series reconstruction formula.
