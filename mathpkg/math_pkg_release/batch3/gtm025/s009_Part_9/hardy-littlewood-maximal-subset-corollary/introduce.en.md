This corollary restricts the Hardy-Littlewood maximal inequality to subsets outside which $f$ vanishes. The constants are raised to the $p$-th power because the inequality is written for the $p$-th power of the integrals rather than for the norms themselves. This formulation is convenient when $f$ has compact support.
