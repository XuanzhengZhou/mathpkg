First, verify the result for cylinder functions $f = \xi_{A_\Omega \times T_{\Omega'}}$. Then show the set $\mathfrak{J}$ of functions satisfying the conclusions is a closed linear subspace of $\mathfrak{L}_p$. Since cylinder functions span a dense subspace, $\mathfrak{J} = \mathfrak{L}_p$. The limit is identified as $\int_T f d\mu$ by exhausting the directed set of finite subsets.
