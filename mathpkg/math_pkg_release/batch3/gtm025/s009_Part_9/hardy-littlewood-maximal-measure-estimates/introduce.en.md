This lemma provides the fundamental measure estimates for the Hardy-Littlewood maximal function. The measure of the maximal set is controlled by the average of $f$ over that set times $1/t$. These estimates form the technical core for proving the $L^p$ boundedness of the maximal operator via the layer cake representation and Holder's inequality.
