This lemma relates the Fourier transform of the reflected-conjugate $\tilde{f}(x) = \overline{f(-x)}$ to the complex conjugate of $\hat{f}$. This symmetry property is essential for extending the Fourier transform to $\mathfrak{L}_2(R)$ and for proving Plancherel's theorem.
