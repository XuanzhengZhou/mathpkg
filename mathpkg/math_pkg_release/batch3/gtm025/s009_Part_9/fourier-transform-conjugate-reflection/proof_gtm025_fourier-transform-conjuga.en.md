$\widehat{\tilde{f}}(y) = (2\pi)^{-\frac{1}{2}} \int_R \overline{f(-x)}\exp(-iyx)dx = (2\pi)^{-\frac{1}{2}} \int_R \overline{f(t)\exp(-iyt)}dt = \overline{\hat{f}(y)}$, using the substitution $t = -x$.
