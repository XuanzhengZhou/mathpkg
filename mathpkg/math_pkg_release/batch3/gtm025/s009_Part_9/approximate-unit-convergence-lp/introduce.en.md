This theorem justifies the term "approximate unit": convolving any $\mathfrak{L}_p(R)$ function ($1 \leq p < \infty$) with an approximate unit converges back to the original function in $L^p$ norm. The approximate unit acts as an approximate identity for convolution on $\mathfrak{L}_p$ spaces.
