Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be any measure space and let $\tau \in F(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$. Define $L_{\tau}$ on $\mathfrak{L}_{\infty}(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ by the rule

(i) $L_{\tau}(g) = \int_X g d\tau$.

Then $L_{\tau}$ is a bounded linear functional on $\mathfrak{L}_{\infty}$ and

(ii) $\|L_{\tau}\| = \|\tau\|$.
