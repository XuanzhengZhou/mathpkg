(1.10) Definition. For sets $A$ and $B$, the symmetric difference of $A$ and $B$ is the set $\{x : x \in A$ or $x \in B$ and $x \notin A \cap B\}$, an is a definition from Chapter 1 (Unknown Chapter) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of analysis.
