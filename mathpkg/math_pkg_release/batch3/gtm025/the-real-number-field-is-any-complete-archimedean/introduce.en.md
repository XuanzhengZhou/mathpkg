(5.35) Definition. The real number field is any complete Archimedean ordered field; e.g., $\bar{Q}$. We will always denote this field by $R$. is a definition from Chapter 5 (Set Theory and Algebra) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of set-theory.
