(7.1) Definition. Let $X$ be any nonvoid set [no topology as yet], and consider the set $K^X$ of all complex-valued functions defined on $X$. For $f, is a definition from Chapter 7 (Spaces of Continuous Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of function-spaces.
