(22.21) Theorem: The Zero-One Law. Let $U$ be a set in $M$ such that for all $t \in T$, $t$ is in $U$ if and only if all points $u$ ultimately equal t is a theorem from Chapter 22 (Further Topics) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
