(5.12) Theorem. If $F$ is an ordered field, if $a, b, c, d \in F$, and $a < b$ and $c \leq d$, then $a + c < b + d$. is a theorem from Chapter 5 (Set Theory and Algebra) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of set-theory.
