Let \(\mathfrak{F}\) be the family of all one-to-one functions \(f\) with \(\operatorname{dom} f \subset U\) and \(\operatorname{rng} f \subset V\). This is of finite character. By Tukey's lemma, \(\mathfrak{F}\) has a maximal member \(h\). Either \(\operatorname{dom} h = U\) (giving \(u \leq v\)) or \(\operatorname{rng} h = V\) (giving \(v \leq u\) via \(h^{-1}\)).
