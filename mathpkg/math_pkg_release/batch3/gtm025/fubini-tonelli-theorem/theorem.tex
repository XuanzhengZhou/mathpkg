Let $(X, \mathcal{M}, \mu)$ and $(Y, \mathcal{N}, \nu)$ be $\sigma$-finite measure spaces. For a nonnegative $\mathcal{M} \times \mathcal{N}$-measurable function $f$ on $X \times Y$: $\int_{X \times Y} f d(\mu \times \nu) = \int_X \int_Y f(x,y) d\nu(y) d\mu(x) = \int_Y \int_X f(x,y) d\mu(x) d\nu(y)$.
