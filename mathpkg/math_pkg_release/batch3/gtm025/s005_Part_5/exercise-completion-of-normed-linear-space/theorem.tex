Let $A$ be a normed linear space.
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] Prove there exists a Banach space $B$ and a norm-preserving linear $T: A \to B$ such that $T(A)$ is dense in $B$.
\item[(b)] If $B_1, B_2$ are any two such Banach spaces, there exists a norm-preserving linear transformation from $B_1$ onto $B_2$.
\end{itemize}
