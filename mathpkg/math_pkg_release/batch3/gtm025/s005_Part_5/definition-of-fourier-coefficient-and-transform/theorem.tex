For $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1([-\pi, \pi], \mathcal{M}_{\lambda}, \lambda)$, let
$$\hat{f}(n) = \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(t) \exp(-int) \, dt \quad (n \in \mathbb{Z}).$$
The number $\hat{f}(n)$ is called the $n$-th \textbf{Fourier coefficient} of $f$. The function $\hat{f}$ on $\mathbb{Z}$ is called the \textbf{Fourier transform} of $f$.
