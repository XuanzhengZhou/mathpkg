For each $x \in E$, let $\hat{x}$ be the function defined on $E^*$ by
$$\hat{x}(f) = f(x) \quad \text{for all} \quad f \in E^*.$$
Then the mapping $\pi: x \mapsto \hat{x}$ is a norm-preserving linear transformation from $E$ into $E^{**}$, i.e.,
$$\|\pi(x)\| = \|x\| \quad \text{for all} \quad x \in E.$$
