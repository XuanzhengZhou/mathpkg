Let $(X, \mathfrak{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space, $\mathfrak{F}$ a dense subalgebra of $\mathfrak{L}_\infty$, and $\mathfrak{M}$ a closed subspace of $\mathfrak{L}_2$ invariant under multiplication by $\mathfrak{F}$. Let $P$ be the projection of $\mathfrak{L}_2$ onto $\mathfrak{M}$. Prove that $P(\varphi) = P(1) \cdot \varphi$ for all $\varphi \in \mathfrak{L}_2$ and $P(1) = \xi_E$ for some $E \in \mathfrak{A}$.
