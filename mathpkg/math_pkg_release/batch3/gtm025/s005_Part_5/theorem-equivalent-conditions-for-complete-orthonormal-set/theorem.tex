Let $E$ be an orthonormal set in a Hilbert space $H$. TFAE: (i) $E$ is complete; (ii) $x = \sum_{z \in E} \langle x, z \rangle z$ for all $x$; (iii) $\|x\|^2 = \sum_{z \in E} |\langle x, z \rangle|^2$ for all $x$; (iv) $\langle x, y \rangle = \sum_{z \in E} \langle x, z \rangle \overline{\langle y, z \rangle}$ for all $x,y$; (v) $\overline{\operatorname{span}(E)} = H$.
