(19.19) Definition. Let $(X, \mathcal{A})$ be a measurable space and let $\mu$ and $v$ be signed or complex measures on $(X, \mathcal{A})$. We say tha is a definition from Chapter 19 (Complex Analysis) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of complex-analysis.
