A decomposable measure space can be partitioned into sets of finite measure where measurability and the measure are determined locally on each piece. This concept generalizes $\sigma$-finiteness and is the right setting for the most general Radon-Nikodym theorem and the duality $\mathfrak{L}_1^* = \mathfrak{L}_\infty$.
