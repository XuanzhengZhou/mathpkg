Applies the general Radon-Nikodym theorem to measures on locally compact Hausdorff spaces. By 19.31, $(X, \mathcal{M}_i, \iota)$ is decomposable, so 19.27 applies directly. This provides the Radon-Nikodym derivative for a wide class of measures in analysis.
