(i) If $|\mu|(E) = 0$, then $(\alpha\nu)(E) = \alpha\nu(E) = 0$ and $(\nu+\sigma)(E) = \nu(E)+\sigma(E) = 0$. (ii) Choose $A, B$ with $|\mu|(A) = |\mu|(B) = 0$ and $|\nu|(A') = |\sigma|(B') = 0$. Let $C = A \cup B$. Then $|\mu|(C) = 0$ and $|\nu+\sigma|(C') \leq |\nu|(A') + |\sigma|(B') = 0$.
