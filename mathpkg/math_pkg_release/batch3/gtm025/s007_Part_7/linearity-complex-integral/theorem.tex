Let $\nu$ be a complex measure on $(X, \mathcal{A})$. If $f, g \in \mathfrak{L}_1(X, \mathcal{A}, |\nu|)$ and $\alpha \in K$, then
(i) $\int_X (f + g) \, d\nu = \int_X f \, d\nu + \int_X g \, d\nu$,
(ii) $\int_X \alpha f \, d\nu = \alpha \int_X f \, d\nu$.
Thus the integral is a linear functional on $\mathfrak{L}_1(X, \mathcal{A}, |\nu|)$.
