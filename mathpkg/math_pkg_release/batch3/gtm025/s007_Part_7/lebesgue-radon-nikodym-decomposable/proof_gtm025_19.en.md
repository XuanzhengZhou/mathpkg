For each $F \in \mathcal{F}$, apply 19.26 to the restrictions to get $D_F$ (where $\nu$ is $\sigma$-finite) and $E_F$ (where $\nu$ takes extreme values). On $D_F$, use 19.24 to get a density $f_0^{(F)}$; on $E_F$, set $f_0 = \infty$. Piece together via decomposability.
