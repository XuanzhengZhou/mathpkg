Every nonnegative linear functional on the space of continuous functions with compact support on $\mathbb{R}$ is given by a Riemann-Stieltjes integral. This is the concrete version of the Riesz representation theorem for $\mathbb{R}$, unifying Riemann-Stieltjes integration, Lebesgue-Stieltjes measures, and nonnegative linear functionals.
