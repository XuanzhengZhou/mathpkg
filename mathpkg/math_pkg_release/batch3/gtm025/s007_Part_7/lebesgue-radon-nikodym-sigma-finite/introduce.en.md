Extends the Radon-Nikodym theorem from finite to $\sigma$-finite measures. The space is decomposed into countably many sets of finite measure, the finite version is applied on each piece, and the densities are pieced together. Uniqueness of $d\nu/d\mu$ holds up to $\mu$-a.e. equality.
