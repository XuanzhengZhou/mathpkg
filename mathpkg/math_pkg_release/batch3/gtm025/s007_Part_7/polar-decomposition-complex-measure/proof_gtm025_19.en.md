Since $\nu \ll |\nu|$, apply 19.36 with $\mu = |\nu|$ to obtain $f_0$ satisfying (ii) and (iii). From (iii), $\int_A |f_0| d|\nu| \leq |\nu|(A)$ for all $A$, which together with $\nu(A) = \int_A f_0 d|\nu|$ forces $|f_0| = 1$ $|\nu|$-a.e. The inequality follows from $|\int f f_0 d|\nu|| \leq \int |f| \cdot 1 d|\nu|$.
