This is the crucial technical step for proving the Lebesgue-Radon-Nikodym theorem. Using the Riesz representation theorem on L2, it constructs a function $g$ mediating between the two measures: integrating $f g$ against $\mu$ equals integrating $f(1-g)$ against $\nu$.
