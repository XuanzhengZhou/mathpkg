Consider all families of compact pairwise disjoint sets with positive measure satisfying: if $U$ open meets $F$, then $\iota(U \cap F) > 0$. Order by inclusion, apply Zorn to get a maximal family $\mathcal{F}_0$. Maximality forces the complement $D$ to be locally $\iota$-null (otherwise one could adjoin a compact subset of $D$ with positive measure, contradicting maximality).
