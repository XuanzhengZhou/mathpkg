Let $X$ be a locally compact Hausdorff space and $(X, \mathcal{M}_i, \iota)$ a measure space as in \S\S 9-10. There exists $\mathcal{F}_0$ of compact pairwise disjoint sets of finite positive measure such that: $D = X \cap (\bigcup \mathcal{F}_0)'$ is locally $\iota$-null; if $E$ has finite measure it meets only countably many $F \in \mathcal{F}_0$; and local measurability on each $F$ implies global measurability.
