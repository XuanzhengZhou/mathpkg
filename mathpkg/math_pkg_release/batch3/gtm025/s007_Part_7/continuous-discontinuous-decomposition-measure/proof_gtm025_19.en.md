Define $\iota_d = \sum_k \iota(\{x_k\}) \varepsilon_{x_k}$ where $\{x_k\}$ are the atoms of $\iota$. Then $\iota_d \leq \iota$. Define $I_c(f) = \int f d\iota - \int f d\iota_d$, which is a nonnegative linear functional. Let $\iota_c$ be the measure from $I_c$. Then $\iota = \iota_c + \iota_d$ and $\iota_c(\{x\}) = 0$ for all $x$. Uniqueness is clear.
