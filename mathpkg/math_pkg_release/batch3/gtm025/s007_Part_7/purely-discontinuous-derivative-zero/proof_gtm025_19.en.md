For $t>0$, $\alpha(t) = \iota([0,t]) = \sum \iota(\{x_k\}) \sigma_{x_k}(t)$ where $\sigma_u = \xi_{]u,\infty[}$. By (17.18), each $\sigma_{x_k}$ has derivative $0$ a.e. The sum inherits this property.
