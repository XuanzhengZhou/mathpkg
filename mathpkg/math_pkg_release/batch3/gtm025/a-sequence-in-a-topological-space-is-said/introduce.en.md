(6.24) Definition. A sequence $(x_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ in a topological space $X$ is said to converge to an element $x \in X$, or to have limit $x$, if is a definition from Chapter 6 (Topology) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of topology.
