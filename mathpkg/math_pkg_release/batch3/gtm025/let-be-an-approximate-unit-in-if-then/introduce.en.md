(21.37) Theorem. Let $(u_n)$ be an approximate unit in $\mathfrak{L}_1(R)$. If $1 \leq p < \infty$, then $\lim_{n \to \infty} \|f * u_n - f\|_p = 0$ f is a theorem from Chapter 21 (Hilbert Spaces and Spectral Theory) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
