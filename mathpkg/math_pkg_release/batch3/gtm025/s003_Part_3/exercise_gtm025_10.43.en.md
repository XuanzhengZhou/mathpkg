Let $T \in \mathcal{M}_\lambda$ with $\lambda(T) > 0$. Prove that $T - T$ contains an interval $[-\alpha, \alpha]$ for some $\alpha > 0$ (Steinhaus theorem). [Hint: The function $x \mapsto \lambda((x + T) \cap T)$ is continuous at $0$ and positive there.]
