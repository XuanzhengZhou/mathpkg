For $X$ and $\mathcal{E} \subset \mathcal{P}(X)$, $\mathcal{S}(\mathcal{E})$ is the intersection of all $\sigma$-algebras containing $\mathcal{E}$. For a topological space $X$, $\mathcal{B}(X) = \mathcal{S}(\{U \subset X : U \text{ is open}\})$ is the Borel $\sigma$-algebra; its elements are Borel sets.
