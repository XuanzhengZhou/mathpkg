Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space with $0 < \mu(X) < \infty$ and $\mu$ assuming only finitely many distinct positive values. Prove that $X$ decomposes as $E_1 \cup \cdots \cup E_n \cup F$ where the $E_k$ are atoms and $\mu(F) = 0$.
