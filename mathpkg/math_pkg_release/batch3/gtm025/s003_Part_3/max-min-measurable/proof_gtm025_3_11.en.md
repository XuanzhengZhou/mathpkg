**Proof.** Extend the finite list to a sequence by setting $f_n = f_m$ for all $n > m$. Then $\max\{f_1, \ldots, f_m\} = \sup_n f_n$ and $\min\{f_1, \ldots, f_m\} = \inf_n f_n$, both measurable by (11.12). $\square$
