**Proof.** Enumerate $\mathbb{Q} = \{r_n\}$. Then
$$\{f > g\} = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} (\{f > r_n\} \cap \{g < r_n\}) \in \mathcal{A}.$$
Also $\{f \geq g\} = X \setminus \{g > f\} \in \mathcal{A}$, and $\{f = g\} = \{f \geq g\} \cap \{g \geq f\} \in \mathcal{A}$. $\square$
