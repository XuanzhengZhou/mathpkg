**Proof.** It suffices to show that for $E \subset A \cap B'$ and $F \subset (A \cap B')'$, we have $\mu(E \cup F) = \mu(E) + \mu(F)$. Since $F = (F \cap A') \cup (F \cap B)$, apply measurability of $A$ and $B$ repeatedly to decompose $\mu(E \cup F)$ and obtain the desired additivity. $\square$
