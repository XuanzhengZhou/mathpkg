**Proof.** Given $\delta, \varepsilon > 0$, use (11.30) to find $J \in \mathcal{A}$ and $n_0$ such that $\mu(J') < \varepsilon$ and $|f - f_n| < \delta$ on $J$ for $n \geq n_0$. Then for $n \geq n_0$, $\{|f - f_n| \geq \delta\} \subset J'$, so the measure is less than $\varepsilon$. $\square$
