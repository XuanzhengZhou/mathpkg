Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space with $\mu(X) < \infty$, and let $f, f_n$ be $\mathcal{A}$-measurable, finite-valued $\mu$-a.e., with $f_n \to f$ $\mu$-a.e. Then $f_n \to f$ in measure: for all $\delta, \varepsilon > 0$, there exists $n_0 \in \mathbb{N}$ such that
$$\mu(\{x \in X : |f(x) - f_n(x)| \geq \delta\}) < \varepsilon$$
for all $n \geq n_0$.
