**Proof.** For any open $U$ with $\iota(U) < \infty$, $\iota(A \cap U) = 0$. By (10.7), $A \cap U$ is $\iota$-measurable for all such $U$, which is condition (iii) of (10.31). Hence $A$ is $\iota$-measurable. $\square$
