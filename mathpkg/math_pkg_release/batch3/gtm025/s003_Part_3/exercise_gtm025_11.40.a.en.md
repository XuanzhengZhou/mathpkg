Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space, $\mathcal{S} \subset \mathcal{P}(X)$ such that $P \in \mathcal{S} \cap \mathcal{A} \Rightarrow \mu(P) = 0$ and $\mathcal{S}$ is closed under countable unions. Let $\mathcal{A}^* = \{A^* \subset X : A^* \triangle A \in \mathcal{S} \text{ for some } A \in \mathcal{A}\}$ and $\mu^*(A^*) = \mu(A)$. Prove that $(X, \mathcal{A}^*, \mu^*)$ is a complete measure space extending $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$.
