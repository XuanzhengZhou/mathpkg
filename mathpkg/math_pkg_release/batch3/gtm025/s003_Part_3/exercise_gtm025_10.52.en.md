Let $\alpha$ be a real-valued nondecreasing function on $\mathbb{R}$ and $\lambda_\alpha$ the Lebesgue-Stieltjes measure induced by the Riemann-Stieltjes integral. Prove that $\lambda_\alpha(\{x\}) = 0$ if and only if $\alpha$ is continuous at $x$.
