Let $\alpha$ be continuous and $\lambda_\alpha$ the corresponding Lebesgue-Stieltjes measure. For each $\varepsilon > 0$, there exists a nowhere dense perfect set $A \subset [0, 1]$ such that $\lambda_\alpha(A) > \lambda_\alpha([0, 1]) - \varepsilon$.
