**Proof.** (i) $\Rightarrow$ (ii): Trivial since $D \subset \mathbb{R}$.
(ii) $\Rightarrow$ (iii): $f^{-1}([a, \infty]) = \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} f^{-1}(]a - 1/n, \infty])$. Choose $a_n \in D$ with $a_n \to a$ from below.
(iii) $\Rightarrow$ (iv): $f^{-1}([-\infty, a[) = X \setminus f^{-1}([a, \infty])$.
(iv) $\Rightarrow$ (v): $f^{-1}([-\infty, a]) = \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} f^{-1}([-\infty, a + 1/n[)$.
(v) $\Rightarrow$ (i): $f^{-1}(]a, \infty]) = X \setminus f^{-1}([-\infty, a])$. $\square$
