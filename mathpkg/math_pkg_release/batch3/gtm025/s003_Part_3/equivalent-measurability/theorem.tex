Let $D \subset \mathbb{R}$ be dense. For extended real-valued $f$, the following are equivalent:
(i) $f$ is $\mathcal{A}$-measurable;
(ii) $f^{-1}(]a, \infty]) \in \mathcal{A}$ for all $a \in D$;
(iii) $f^{-1}([a, \infty]) \in \mathcal{A}$ for all $a \in D$;
(iv) $f^{-1}([-\infty, a[) \in \mathcal{A}$ for all $a \in D$;
(v) $f^{-1}([-\infty, a]) \in \mathcal{A}$ for all $a \in D$.
