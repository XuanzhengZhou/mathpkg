Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space and $(A_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ a sequence in $\mathcal{A}$ with $\mu(A_1) < \infty$ and $A_1 \supset A_2 \supset \cdots$. Then
$$\mu\left(\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} A_n\right) = \lim_{n \to \infty} \mu(A_n).$$
In particular, if the intersection is empty, the limit is $0$.
