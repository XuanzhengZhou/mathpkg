**Proof.** Apply (11.14) to $\operatorname{Re} f_n$ and $\operatorname{Im} f_n$, which converge pointwise to $\operatorname{Re} f$ and $\operatorname{Im} f$ respectively. Both are $\mathcal{A}$-measurable by (11.14), hence $f$ is measurable by (11.15). $\square$
