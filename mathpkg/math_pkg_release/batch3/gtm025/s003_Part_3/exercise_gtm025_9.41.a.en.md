Let $X = \mathbb{R}_d \times \mathbb{R}$ where the first factor has the discrete topology and the second has the usual topology. For $x, a, b \in \mathbb{R}$ with $a < b$, let $U(x, a, b) = \{(x, y) \in X : a < y < b\} = \{x\} \times ]a, b[$. Show that $\{U(x, a, b) : x, a, b \in \mathbb{R}, a < b\}$ is a base for the product topology on $X$.
