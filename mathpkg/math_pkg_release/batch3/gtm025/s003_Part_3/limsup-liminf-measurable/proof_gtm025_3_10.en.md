**Proof.** By definition, $\liminf \mu(A_k) \leq \mu(B) \leq \limsup \mu(A_k)$. The hypothesis that $\liminf A_k = \limsup A_k = B$ together with $\mu(\bigcup A_k) < \infty$ forces these inequalities to be equalities, so $\lim \mu(A_k)$ exists and equals $\mu(B)$. $\square$
