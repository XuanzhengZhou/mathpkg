(6.61) Definition. Let $X$ be a topological space and let $A \subset X$. A point $a \in A$ is called an isolated point is a definition from Chapter 6 (Topology) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of topology.
