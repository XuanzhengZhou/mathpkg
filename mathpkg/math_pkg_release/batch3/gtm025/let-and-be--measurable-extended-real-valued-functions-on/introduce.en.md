(12.14) Theorem. Let $f$ and $g$ be $\mathcal{A}$-measurable, extended real-valued functions on $X$ such that $f = g \mu$-a.e. and $L(f)$ is defined. is a theorem from Chapter 12 (The Lebesgue Integral) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
