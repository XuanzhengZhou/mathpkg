Let $V$ be any open cover of $f(X)$. Then $\{f^{-1}(V) : V \in V\}$ is an open cover of $X$, so there exist $V_1, \ldots, V_n \in V$ such that $X = \bigcup_{k=1}^{n} f^{-1}(V_k) = f^{-1}\bigcup_{k=1}^{n} V_k$. $\square$
