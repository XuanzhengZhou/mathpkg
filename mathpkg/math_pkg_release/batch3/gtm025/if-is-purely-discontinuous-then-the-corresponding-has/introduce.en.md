(19.56) Theorem. If $\iota$ is purely discontinuous, then the corresponding $\alpha$ has derivative 0 a.e.$^2$ is a theorem from Chapter 19 (Complex Analysis) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of complex-analysis.
