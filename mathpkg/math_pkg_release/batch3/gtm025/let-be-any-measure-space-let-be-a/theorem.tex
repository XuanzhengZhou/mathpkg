The reader will recall from (15.14.b) that, for certain measure spaces, if $p > 1$ and $g$ is a measurable function such that $fg \in \mathfrak{L}_1$ for all $f \in \mathfrak{L}_p$, then $g \in \mathfrak{L}_{p'}$.
