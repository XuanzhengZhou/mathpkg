(10.8) Theorem. If $A$ is $\mu$-measurable, then $A'$ is also. is a theorem from Chapter 10 (Measures) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
