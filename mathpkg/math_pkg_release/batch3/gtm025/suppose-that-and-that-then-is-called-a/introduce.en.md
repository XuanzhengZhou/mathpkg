(18.6) Definition. Suppose that $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1([a, b])$ and that $x \in ]a, b[$. Then $x$ is called a Lebesgue point for $f$ if (18.5.i) holds. is a definition from Chapter 18 (Differentiation) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of differentiation.
