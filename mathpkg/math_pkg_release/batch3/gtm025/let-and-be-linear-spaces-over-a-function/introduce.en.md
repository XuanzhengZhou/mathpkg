(14.1) Definition. Let $A$ and $B$ be linear spaces over $F$. A function $T$ from $A$ into $B$ is called a linear transformation [or linear operator] is a definition from Chapter 14 (Abstract Banach Spaces) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
