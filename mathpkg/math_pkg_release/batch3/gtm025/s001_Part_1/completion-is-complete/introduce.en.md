The completion $\bar{F}$ of an ordered field $F$ is itself complete: every Cauchy sequence in $\bar{F}$ converges to an element of $\bar{F}$. This is the central theorem justifying the Cauchy construction. The proof reduces to showing that any Cauchy sequence in $\bar{F}$ has a subsequence converging to an element represented by a Cauchy sequence from $F$, using the density of $F$ in $\bar{F}$.
