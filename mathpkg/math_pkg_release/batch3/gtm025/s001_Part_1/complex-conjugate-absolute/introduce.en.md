The complex conjugate and absolute value satisfy fundamental algebraic identities. The conjugate distributes over addition and multiplication, and the absolute value is invariant under conjugation. Most importantly, $|z|^2 = z\bar{z}$ connects the algebraic and metric structures of the complex numbers, enabling computations like $1/z = \bar{z}/|z|^2$ for $z \neq 0$.
