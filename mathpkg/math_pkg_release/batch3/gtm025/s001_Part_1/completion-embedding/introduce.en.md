This lemma establishes two important properties of the completion $\bar{F}$. First, there are no infinitesimals relative to $F$: between any positive element of $\bar{F}$ and zero there is a positive constant element from $F$. Second, the Archimedean property is preserved under completion, which is essential for constructing the real numbers as the completion of the rationals.
