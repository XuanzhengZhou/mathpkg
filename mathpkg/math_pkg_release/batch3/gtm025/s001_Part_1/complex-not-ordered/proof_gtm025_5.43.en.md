Assuming the existence in $K$ of a subset $P$ as in (5.7), we have $i \in P$ or $-i \in P$. If $i \in P$, then $i^2 = -1 \in P$, which contradicts (5.8). If $-i \in P$, then $(-i)^2 = -1 \in P$, again a contradiction. Hence no such $P$ exists.
