Every ordered field contains a subfield isomorphic to the rational numbers $Q$, with the isomorphism preserving the order structure. This means $Q$ is, up to isomorphism, the unique minimal ordered field. The embedding maps rational numbers $\frac{m}{n}$ to $(m1)(n1)^{-1}$ in $F$.
