(6.78) Theorem. Let $X$ be a locally compact Hausdorff space. Let $x \in X$ and let $U$ be a neighborhood of $x$. Then there exists a neighborhood $V$ is a theorem from Chapter 6 (Topology) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of topology.
