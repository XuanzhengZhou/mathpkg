Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a complete measure space and let $(f_n)$ be a sequence of $\mathcal{A}$-measurable functions each of which is defined $\mu$-a.e. on $X$. Suppose that $f$ is defined $\mu$-a.e. on $X$ and that $\lim_{n \to \infty} f_n(x) = f(x)$ $\mu$-a.e. on $X$. Then $f$ is $\mathcal{A}$-measurable.
