We have $2^p = \sum_{k=0}^{p} \binom{p}{k} > p$, and so $2^{-p} < \frac{1}{p}$. Since $\left(\frac{1}{p}\right)_{p=1}^{\infty}$ is null, $(2^{-p})$ is also null.
