This technical lemma relates the Lebesgue upper integral of a function $f$ on the whole space $X$ to its restriction to the set $E$ where $f$ is nonzero. It shows that $L_E(f)$ and $L(f)$ are equivalent, which is crucial for the proof of countable additivity of the integral.
