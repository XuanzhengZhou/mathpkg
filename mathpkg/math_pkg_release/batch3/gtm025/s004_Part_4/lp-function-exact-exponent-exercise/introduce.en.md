Consider the set $[0, \infty[$ and Lebesgue measure $\lambda$ on it. For every $p > 0$, find a function $f$ on $[0, \infty[$ such that $f \in \mathfrak{L}_p$ and $f \notin \mathfrak{L}_q$ if $p \neq q$. [Hint. Consider the function $g$ such that $g(x) = \frac{1}{x(1 + |\log(x)|)^2}$.]
