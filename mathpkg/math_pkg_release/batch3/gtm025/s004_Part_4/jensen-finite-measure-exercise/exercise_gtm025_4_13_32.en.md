Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a measure space such that $\mu(X) = 1$ and let $f$ be a function in $\mathfrak{L}_1^r(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$. Prove that

$$\exp\left(\int_X \log |f| d\mu\right) \le \int_X |f| d\mu,$$

with equality if and only if $|f|$ is constant almost everywhere.
