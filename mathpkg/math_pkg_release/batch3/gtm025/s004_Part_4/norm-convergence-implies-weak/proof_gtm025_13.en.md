For $p > 1$, $|\int_X f_n g d\mu - \int_X f g d\mu| \leq \int_X |f_n - f| |g| d\mu \leq \|f_n - f\|_p \|g\|_{p'} \to 0$. For $p = 1$, use the boundedness of $g$ instead.
