The $\mathfrak{L}_p$ spaces are among the most important function spaces in analysis. For $1 \le p < \infty$, $\mathfrak{L}_p$ consists of all measurable functions whose $p$-th power is integrable. The quantity $\|f\|_p$ defines a norm (after identifying functions equal almost everywhere) that makes $\mathfrak{L}_p$ a Banach space.
