Let $p$ be a positive real number, and let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be an arbitrary measure space. Let $f$ be a complex-valued $\mathcal{A}$-measurable function defined $\mu$-a.e. on $X$ such that $|f|^p \in \mathfrak{L}_1^r$. We then say that $f \in \mathfrak{L}_p(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$. For $p \geq 1$ and $f \in \mathfrak{L}_p$, we define $\|f\|_p = (\int_X |f|^p d\mu)^{1/p}$.
