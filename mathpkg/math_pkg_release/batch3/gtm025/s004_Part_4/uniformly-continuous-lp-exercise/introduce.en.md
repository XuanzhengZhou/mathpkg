Let $p$ be a real number such that $1 \le p < \infty$, and let $f$ be a function in $\mathfrak{L}_p(R)$ such that $f$ is uniformly continuous. Prove that $f \in \mathfrak{C}_0(R)$. Show by examples that each $\mathfrak{L}_p(R)$ contains an unbounded continuous function.
