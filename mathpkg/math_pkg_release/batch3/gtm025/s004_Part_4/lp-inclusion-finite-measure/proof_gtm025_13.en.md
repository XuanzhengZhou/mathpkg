Let $r = q/p > 1$. Apply Holder's inequality: $\int |f|^p d\mu \leq (\int |f|^{pr} d\mu)^{1/r} (\int 1^{r'} d\mu)^{1/r'} = (\int |f|^q d\mu)^{p/q} (\mu(X))^{(q-p)/q}$. Taking the $p$-th root gives the desired inequality.
