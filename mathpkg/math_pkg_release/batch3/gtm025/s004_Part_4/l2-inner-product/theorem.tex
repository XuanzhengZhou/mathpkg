For $f, g \in \mathfrak{L}_2(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$, define $\langle f, g \rangle = \int_X f \bar{g} d\mu$. This satisfies: $\langle f_1+f_2, g \rangle = \langle f_1, g \rangle + \langle f_2, g \rangle$; $\langle \alpha f, g \rangle = \alpha \langle f, g \rangle$; $\overline{\langle f, g \rangle} = \langle g, f \rangle$; and $\langle f, f \rangle > 0$ for $f \neq 0$.
