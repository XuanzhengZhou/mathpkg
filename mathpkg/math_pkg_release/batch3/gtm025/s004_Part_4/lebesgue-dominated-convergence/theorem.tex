Let $(f_n)$ be a sequence in $\mathfrak{L}_1$ and let $s \in \mathfrak{L}_1$ be such that $|f_n| \leq s$ $\mu$-a.e. for all $n \in N$. If $\lim_{n \to \infty} f_n(x)$ exists for $\mu$-almost all $x \in X$, then $\lim_{n \to \infty} f_n \in \mathfrak{L}_1$ and $$\lim_{n \to \infty} \int_X f_n d\mu = \int_X \lim_{n \to \infty} f_n d\mu.$$
