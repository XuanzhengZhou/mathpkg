Given a measure $\mu$ on $X$ and a continuous surjection $\varphi: X \to Y$ (with properness or finite mass conditions), one can push forward the measure to obtain a regular measure $\nu$ on $Y$ satisfying $\nu(B) = \mu(\varphi^{-1}(B))$.
