Let $E = \{x \in X : f(x) > 0\}$. If $L(f) = 0$, then it follows from (12.6) that $\mu(E) = 0$, i.e., $f = 0$ a.e. Conversely, suppose that $\mu(E) = 0$. Then\n\n$$0 \leq L(f) \leq L(\infty \cdot \xi_E) = \infty \cdot \mu(E) = 0.$$
