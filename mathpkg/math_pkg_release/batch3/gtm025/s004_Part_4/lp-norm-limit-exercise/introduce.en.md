Let $(X, \mathcal{A}, \mu)$ be a finite measure space and let $f$ be any bounded measurable function on $X$. Prove that

$$\lim_{p \to \infty} \|f\|_p = \inf \left\{ \alpha \in R : \alpha > 0, \mu(\{x \in X : |f(x)| > \alpha\}) = 0 \right\}.$$

That is, the limit of the $\mathfrak{L}_p$ norms equals the essential supremum of $|f|$.
