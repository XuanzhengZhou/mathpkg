Let $E = \{x \in X : f(x) \neq g(x)\}$. By hypothesis $\mu(E) = 0$.\n\nCase (I): $f \geq 0$, $g \geq 0$. Apply (12.12) and (12.13) to obtain the result.\n\nFor general $f, g$, decompose into positive and negative parts and apply the nonnegative case.
