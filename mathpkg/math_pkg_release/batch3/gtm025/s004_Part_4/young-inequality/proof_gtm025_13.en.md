The proof follows from the geometric interpretation of the integrals $\Phi(a)$ and $\Psi(b)$ as the areas under the graphs of $\varphi$ and $\psi$. The rectangle $[0,a] \times [0,b]$ is contained in the union of the regions under $\varphi$ (from 0 to $a$) and to the left of $\varphi$ (from 0 to $b$), giving $ab \leq \Phi(a) + \Psi(b)$.
