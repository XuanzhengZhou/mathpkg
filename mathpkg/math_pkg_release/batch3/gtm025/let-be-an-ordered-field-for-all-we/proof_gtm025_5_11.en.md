The proof is immediate from the definition of $P$ and the fact that

$$b - a = 0 \quad \text{if and only if} \quad b = a,$$

$$b - a \in P \quad \text{if and only if} \quad b > a,$$

$$a - b \in P \quad \text{if and only if} \quad a > b. \quad \square$$
