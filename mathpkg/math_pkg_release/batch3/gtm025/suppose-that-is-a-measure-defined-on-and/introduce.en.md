(11.20) Definition. Suppose that $\mu$ is a measure defined on $\mathcal{A}$ and that $B \in \mathcal{A}$ whenever $A \in \mathcal{A}$, $\mu(A) = 0$, is a definition from Chapter 11 (Measurable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
