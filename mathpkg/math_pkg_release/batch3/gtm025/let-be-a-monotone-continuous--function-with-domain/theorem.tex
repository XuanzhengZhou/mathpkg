Let $\varphi$ be a monotone continuous $N$-function with domain $[a, b]$ and range $[\alpha, \beta]$ ($\alpha < \beta$). Then $\varphi$ is absolutely continuous, and the function $w$ of (20.4) is equal to $|\varphi'| \lambda$-almost everywhere on $[a, b]$. Thus for $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1([\alpha, \beta])$, we have $(f \circ \varphi) |\varphi'| \in \mathfrak{L}_1([a, b])$, and

(i) $$\int_{\alpha}^{\beta} f(y) dy = \int_{a}^{b} f \circ \varphi(x) |\varphi'(x)| dx.$$
