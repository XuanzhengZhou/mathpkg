(12.8) Theorem. If $f \geq 0$ and $t \in R$, then we have $L(tf) = tL(f)$. The proof is easy and is omitted. is a theorem from Chapter 12 (The Lebesgue Integral) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
