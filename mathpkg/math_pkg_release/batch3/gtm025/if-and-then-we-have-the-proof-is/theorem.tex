If $f \geq 0$ and $t \in R$, then we have $L(tf) = tL(f)$. The proof is easy and is omitted.

Our immediate aim is to establish the extremely important identity

$$L\left(\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} f_n\right) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} L(f_n)$$

for all sequences $(f_n)$ of nonnegative $\mathcal{A}$-measurable functions. We begin with a lemma.
