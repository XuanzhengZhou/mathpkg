The function $f$ defined by $f(x) = \frac{1 - 2x}{x(1 - x)}$ is a one-to-one mapping of $]0, 1[$ onto $R$. Therefore $$]0, 1[ = \bar{R} = c.$$ The rest follows from the inequalities $c = ]0, 1[ \leq [0, 1] \leq [0, 1] \leq \bar{R} = c$ and the Schröder-Bernstein theorem. $\square$
