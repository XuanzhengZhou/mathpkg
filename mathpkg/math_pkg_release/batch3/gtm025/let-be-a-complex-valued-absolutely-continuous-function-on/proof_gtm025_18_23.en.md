The function $\varphi$ being continuous and the interval $[c_k, d_k]$ being compact, it is easy to see that $[c_k, d_k]$ contains points $u_k$ and $v_k$ such that $u_k < v_k$ and $|\varphi(u_k) - \varphi(v_k)| = \omega_{\varphi}(c_k, d_k)$. Since

$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}(v_k - u_k) \leq \sum_{k=1}^{n}(d_k - c_k) < \delta,$$

we obtain (i) at once from (18.10.i). $\square$
