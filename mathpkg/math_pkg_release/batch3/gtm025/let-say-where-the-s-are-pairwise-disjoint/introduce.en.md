(12.4) Theorem. Let $f \in \mathfrak{S}^+$, say $f = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \alpha_k \xi_{E_k}$, where the $E_k$'s are pairwise disjoint and in $\mathcal{A}$. is a theorem from Chapter 12 (The Lebesgue Integral) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
