(6.63) Theorem. Let $P$ be any Cantor-like set. Then $P$ is compact, nowhere dense in $R$, and perfect. is a theorem from Chapter 6 (Topology) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of topology.
