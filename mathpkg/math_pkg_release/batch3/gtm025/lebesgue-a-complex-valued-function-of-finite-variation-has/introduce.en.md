(17.17) Theorem [LEBESGUE]. A complex-valued function of finite variation has a finite derivative a.e. is a theorem from Chapter 17 (Haar Measure) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of harmonic-analysis.
