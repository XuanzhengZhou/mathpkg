(4.20) Lemma. If $A$ and $B$ are two nonvoid sets and if there is a mapping $f$ from $A$ onto $B$, then $\bar{A} \geq \bar{B}$. is a lemma from Chapter 4 (Unknown Chapter) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25).
