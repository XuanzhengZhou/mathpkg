(13.10) Theorem. For $1 \leq p < \infty$, $\mathfrak{L}_p$ is a normed linear space over $K$, where we agree that $f = g$ means $f(x) = g(x)$ is a theorem from Chapter 13 (Spaces of Integrable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
