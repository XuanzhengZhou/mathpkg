(21.51) Lemma.** Let $f \in \mathfrak{L}_1(R)$. Define $f$ on $R$ by $f(x) = f(-x)$. Then $f(y) = f(y)$ for all $y \in R$. is a lemma from Chapter 21 (Hilbert Spaces and Spectral Theory) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
