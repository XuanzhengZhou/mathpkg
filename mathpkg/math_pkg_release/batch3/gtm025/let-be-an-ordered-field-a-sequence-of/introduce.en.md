(5.19) Definition. Let $F$ be an ordered field. A sequence $(a_n)$ $[= (a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, \ldots)]$ of elements of $F$ is called bounded if there is a definition from Chapter 5 (Set Theory and Algebra) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of set-theory.
