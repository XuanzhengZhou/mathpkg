(12.9) Lemma. Let $f$ be any extended real-valued function on $X$ and suppose that $E = \{x \in X : f(x) \neq 0\}$ is an $\mathcal{A}$-measurable set. is a lemma from Chapter 12 (The Lebesgue Integral) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of integration.
