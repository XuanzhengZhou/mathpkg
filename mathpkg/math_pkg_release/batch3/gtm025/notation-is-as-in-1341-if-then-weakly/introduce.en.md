(13.42) Theorem. Notation is as in (13.41). If $\|f - f_n\|_p \to 0$, then $f_n \to f$ weakly. is a theorem from Chapter 13 (Spaces of Integrable Functions) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of functional-analysis.
