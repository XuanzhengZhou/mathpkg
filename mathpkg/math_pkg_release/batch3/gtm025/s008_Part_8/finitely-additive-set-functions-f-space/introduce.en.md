This definition introduces the space F(X, A, mu) of finitely additive complex-valued set functions that vanish on locally mu-null sets. The functions are required to be bounded in supremum, finitely additive on disjoint sets, and vanish on locally null sets. The variation norm is defined analogously to the case of countably additive measures. This space plays a central role in representing the dual of L_infinity for arbitrary measure spaces.
