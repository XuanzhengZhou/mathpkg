This theorem defines the convolution of two L^1(R) functions and establishes the submultiplicativity of the L^1 norm. The proof uses Fubini's theorem and translation invariance of Lebesgue measure. This makes L^1(R) a commutative Banach algebra under convolution, fundamental in harmonic analysis.
