This corollary gives a sufficient condition for the limit measure eta_omega to be absolutely continuous with respect to mu_omega. If each eta_n is absolutely continuous with respect to mu_n and the integrals of the derivatives f_n converge appropriately, then eta_omega is absolutely continuous with respect to mu_omega. This is a natural compatibility condition between convergence of measures and convergence of their densities.
