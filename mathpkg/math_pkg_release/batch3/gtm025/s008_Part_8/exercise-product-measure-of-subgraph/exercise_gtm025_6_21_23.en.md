Let $(X, \mathcal{M}, \mu)$ be a $\sigma$-finite measure space. Let $f$ be a nonnegative, extended real-valued, $\mathcal{M}$-measurable function on $X$. Prove that the set $E = \{(x, y) \in X \times R : 0 \leq y < f(x)\}$ belongs to $\mathcal{M} \times \mathcal{M}_\lambda$ and that $\mu \times \lambda(E) = \int_X f d\mu$.
