This lemma ensures that the integral of a simple function with respect to a finitely additive measure tau in F(X, A, mu) is well-defined. It states that the value of the sum of coefficients times tau of the associated sets does not depend on the particular representation of the simple function, which is a necessary prerequisite for extending the integral to bounded measurable functions via uniform approximation.
