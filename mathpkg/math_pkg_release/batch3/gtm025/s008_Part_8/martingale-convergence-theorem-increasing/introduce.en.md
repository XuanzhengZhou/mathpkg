This theorem is a fundamental martingale convergence result. For an increasing sequence of sigma-algebras, the sequence of derivatives f_n converges in an extended sense: both the limit inferior and limit superior are derivatives of eta restricted to the generated sigma-algebra. This is a version of the martingale convergence theorem of Sparre Andersen and Jessen, crucial for applications to infinite product measures.
