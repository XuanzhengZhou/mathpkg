This is the culminating Riesz representation theorem for C_0(X). It synthesizes (20.45) and (20.47) to establish that M(X), the space of complex regular Borel measures, is isometrically isomorphic to the dual space of C_0(X). As a corollary, M(X) is complete, hence a Banach space. This generalizes the classical Riesz representation theorem for C([0,1])^* to functions vanishing at infinity on locally compact Hausdorff spaces.
