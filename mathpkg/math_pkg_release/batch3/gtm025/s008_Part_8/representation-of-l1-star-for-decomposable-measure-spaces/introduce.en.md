This theorem establishes the dual space of L_1 for decomposable measure spaces. It states that every bounded linear functional on L_1 can be represented uniquely by a function g in L_infinity via integration. The mapping is norm-preserving, linear, and onto, establishing an isometric isomorphism between the Banach spaces L_infinity and the dual of L_1.
