Let $E$ be a normed linear space and let $(x_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ be a sequence of elements of $E$. We say that the series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} x_n$ converges, and we write $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} x_n = x$, if there exists an $x \in E$ such that $\lim_{p \to \infty} \left\| x - \sum_{n=1}^{p} x_n \right\| = 0$.
