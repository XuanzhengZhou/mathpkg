We have $g_1 + g_2 = \sup\{f_1 + f_2 : f_j \in \mathfrak{C}_{00}^+ \text{ and } f_j \leq g_j, j = 1, 2\}$, and therefore

$$\bar{I}(g_1 + g_2) = \sup\{I(f_1) + I(f_2) : f_j \in \mathfrak{C}_{00}^+, f_j \leq g_j\}$$

$$= \sup\{I(f_1) : f_1 \in \mathfrak{C}_{00}^+, f_1 \leq g_1\} + \sup\{I(f_2) : f_2 \in \mathfrak{C}_{00}^+, f_2 \leq g_2\}$$

$$= \bar{I}(g_1) + \bar{I}(g_2).$$
