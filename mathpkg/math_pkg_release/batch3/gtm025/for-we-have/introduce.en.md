(9.13) Corollary. For $g_1, g_2 \in \mathfrak{M}^+$, we have $\bar{I}(g_1 + g_2) = \bar{I}(g_1) + \bar{I}(g_2)$. is a corollary from Chapter 9 (Extending Functionals) of the book *Real and Abstract Analysis* by Edwin Hewitt and Karl Stromberg (GTM 25). It is part of the development of measure-theory.
