Let $X$ and $Y$ be locally compact Hausdorff spaces, and let $(X, M_\mu, \mu)$ and $(Y, M_\nu, \nu)$ be $\sigma$-finite measure spaces as in §§ 9 and 10. Then the completion $\overline{\mu \times \nu}$ of $\mu \times \nu$ is regular on $M_\mu \times M_\nu$ in the sense that if $E \in M_\mu \times M_\nu$, then

(i) $\overline{\mu \times \nu}(E) = \inf\{\mu \times \nu(U): E \subset U, U \in M_\mu \times M_\nu, U \text{ is open}\}$ and

(ii) $\overline{\mu \times \nu}(E) = \sup\{\mu \times \nu(F): E \supset F, F \in M_\mu \times M_\nu, F \text{ is compact}\}$.
