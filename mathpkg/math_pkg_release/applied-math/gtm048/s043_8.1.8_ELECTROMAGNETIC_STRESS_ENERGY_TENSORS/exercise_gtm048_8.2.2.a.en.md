Let $N$ be a manifold (Section 0.0.4). Thus $N$ is real, Hausdorff, finite-dimensional, paracompact and $C^\infty$.

Show there exists a Lorentzian metric on $N$ iff there exists a nowhere zero vector field on $N$ iff there exists a Lorentzian metric $g$ on $N$ such that $(N, g)$ is time orientable.
