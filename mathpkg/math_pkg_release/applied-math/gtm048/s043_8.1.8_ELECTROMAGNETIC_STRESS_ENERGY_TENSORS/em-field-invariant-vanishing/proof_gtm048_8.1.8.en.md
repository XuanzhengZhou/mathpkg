The proof is left as Exercise 8.1.8(b) to the reader. One approach is to compute the Lorentz invariants of the electromagnetic field. The scalar $F^{ij} F_{ij}$ is proportional to $g(B, B) - g(E, E)$, so its vanishing is equivalent to $g(E, E) = g(B, B)$. Compare Section 3.6.1 for the component notation and the algebraic decomposition of the electromagnetic field tensor.
