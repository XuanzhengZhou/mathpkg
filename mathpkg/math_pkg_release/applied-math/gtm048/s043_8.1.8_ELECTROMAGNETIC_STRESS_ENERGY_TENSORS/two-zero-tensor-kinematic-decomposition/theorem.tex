Let $S \in T_0^2(M_z)$ be a $(2,0)$ tensor at $z \in M$, let $\hat{h} = \hat{g} + Z \otimes Z$ be the projection tensor onto the rest space $R = Z^\perp$, where $Z$ is an instantaneous observer. Then there exist unique:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $a, \theta \in \mathbb{R}$,
    \item unique $X, Y \in R$,
    \item a unique symmetric tensor $\sigma \in R \otimes R$, trace-free with respect to $g|_R = h|_R$,
    \item a unique skew-symmetric tensor $\omega \in R \otimes R$
\end{itemize}
such that
$$S = aZ \otimes Z + Y \otimes Z + Z \otimes X + \sigma + \omega + \frac{1}{2}\theta \hat{h}.$$
