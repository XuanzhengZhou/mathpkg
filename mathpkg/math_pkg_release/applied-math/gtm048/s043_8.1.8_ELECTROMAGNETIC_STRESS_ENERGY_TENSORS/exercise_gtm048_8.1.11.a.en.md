Let $\mathcal{S}^2$ be the unit sphere in the rest space $R$ (cf. Section 8.1.0). Show

$$\operatorname{Ric}(Z, Z) = \frac{3}{4\pi} \int_{X \in \mathcal{S}^2} K_X \, \zeta,$$

where $\zeta$ is the standard volume element and $K_X$ is the sectional curvature (Bishop--Goldberg 5.14) of $\operatorname{span}\{X, Z\}$.
