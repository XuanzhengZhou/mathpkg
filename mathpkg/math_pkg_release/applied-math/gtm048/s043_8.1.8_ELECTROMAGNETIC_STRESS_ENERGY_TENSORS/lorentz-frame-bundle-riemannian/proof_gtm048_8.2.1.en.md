The proof is left as Exercise 8.2.1(c) to the reader. The hint refers to Sections 8.1.2a and 8.2.1b. One uses the fact that the structure group of $ON$ is a Lie group (the Lorentz group), which by Exercise 8.2.1(b) admits a Riemannian metric. Combined with a Riemannian metric on the base (constructed via a choice of timelike vector field), one obtains a Riemannian metric on the total space $ON$ using the standard construction of bundle metrics.
