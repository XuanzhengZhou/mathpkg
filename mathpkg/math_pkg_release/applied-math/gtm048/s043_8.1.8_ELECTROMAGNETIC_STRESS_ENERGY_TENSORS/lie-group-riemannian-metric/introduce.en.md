This result from Exercise 8.2.1b establishes that every Lie group admits a Riemannian metric. The proof uses the fact that a Lie group has trivial tangent bundle, hence a nowhere zero vector field, and thus by the equivalence results of Section 8.2.1a, is paracompact and admits a Riemannian metric.
