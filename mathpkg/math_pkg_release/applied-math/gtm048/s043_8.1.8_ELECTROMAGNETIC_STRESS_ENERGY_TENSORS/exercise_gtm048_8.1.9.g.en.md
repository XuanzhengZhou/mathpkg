Imagine an "infinitesimal" solid sphere in $Z^\perp$. Sketch the intrinsic shape of the slightly distorted sphere after an "infinitesimal" proper time, assuming it is dragged along by the flow of $Z$ (cf. Sections 2.0.3 and 2.3); identify $\theta$, $\omega$, and $\sigma$ on your figure. (You may assume $X = 0$ for simplicity.)
