We use the notation of Section 8.1.0. Suppose $S \in T_0^2(M_z)$, $\hat{h} = \hat{g} + Z \otimes Z$ is physically equivalent to the projection tensor (Section 2.1.5), and $R$ is the rest space $Z^\perp$.

Show there exist: unique $a, \theta \in \mathbb{R}$; unique $X, Y \in R$; a unique symmetric tensor $\sigma \in R \otimes R$, trace-free with respect to $g|_R = h|_R$; and a unique skew-symmetric tensor $\omega \in R \otimes R$ such that the following decomposition of $S$ holds:

$$S = aZ \otimes Z + Y \otimes Z + Z \otimes X + \sigma + \omega + \frac{1}{2}\theta \hat{h}.$$
