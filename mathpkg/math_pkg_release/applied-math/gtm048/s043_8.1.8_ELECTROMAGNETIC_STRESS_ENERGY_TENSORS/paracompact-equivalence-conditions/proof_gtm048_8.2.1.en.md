The proof is left as Exercise 8.2.1(a) to the reader. The argument proceeds by establishing a chain of implications. A Riemannian metric yields a distance function, giving metrizability. Metrizable plus connected and locally compact implies second countable. Second countable implies Lindel\"of. For manifolds, paracompactness is equivalent to metrizability (by the Smirnov metrization theorem). Each step uses standard results in manifold topology.
