For an electromagnetic field $F$ on a Lorentzian manifold, the exterior product $F \wedge F$ vanishes precisely when the electric and magnetic fields $E$ and $B$ measured by any instantaneous observer are orthogonal with respect to the spacetime metric $g$. This criterion relates the algebraic structure of the 2-form $F$ to the physical geometry of the observer's rest frame.
