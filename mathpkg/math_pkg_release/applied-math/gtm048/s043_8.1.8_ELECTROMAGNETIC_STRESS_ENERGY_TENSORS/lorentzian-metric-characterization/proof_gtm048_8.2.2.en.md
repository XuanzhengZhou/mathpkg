The proof is left as Exercise 8.2.2(a) to the reader. The key steps: (1 $\Rightarrow$ 2) Given a Lorentzian metric $g$, use the fact that at each point the lightcone has two components to construct a nowhere zero vector field via a time-orientation argument. (2 $\Rightarrow$ 3) Given a nowhere zero vector field $V$, choose any Riemannian metric $h$ and define $g(U, W) = h(U, W) - 2 h(U, V) h(W, V) / h(V, V)$, which is Lorentzian and time-orientable with $V$ as the time-orientation. (3 $\Rightarrow$ 1) Trivial.
