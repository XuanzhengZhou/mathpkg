Let $Z$ be an instantaneous observer at $z \in M$, let $R = Z^\perp$ be the rest space, and let $\mathcal{S}^2$ be the unit sphere in $R$. For each $X \in \mathcal{S}^2$, let $K_X$ be the sectional curvature of the plane spanned by $\{X, Z\}$. Then

$$\operatorname{Ric}(Z, Z) = \frac{3}{4\pi} \int_{X \in \mathcal{S}^2} K_X \, \zeta,$$

where $\zeta$ is the standard volume element on $\mathcal{S}^2$.
