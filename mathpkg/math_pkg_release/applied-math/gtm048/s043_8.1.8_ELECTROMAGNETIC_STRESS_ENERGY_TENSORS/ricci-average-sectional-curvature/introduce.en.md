This formula expresses the Ricci curvature $\operatorname{Ric}(Z, Z)$ in the direction of an instantaneous observer $Z$ as an average of sectional curvatures $K_X$ over all spatial directions $X$ in the observer's rest frame, weighted by the standard volume element on the unit 2-sphere. It provides a geometric interpretation of the Ricci tensor as a mean curvature and is essential for understanding the Einstein field equation in physical terms.
