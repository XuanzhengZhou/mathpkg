Let $F$ be an electromagnetic field on $M$, let $\hat{E}$ be its stress-energy tensor, and for each $z \in M$ and instantaneous observer $(z, Z)$ let $F = F_z \in T_2^0(M_z)$, $\hat{T} = \hat{E}_z \in T_0^2(M_z)$, and let $E$ (respectively, $B$) be the electric (respectively, magnetic) field measured by $(z, Z)$. Suppose $F \neq 0$. Show that

$$g(E, B) = 0 \iff F \wedge F = 0.$$
