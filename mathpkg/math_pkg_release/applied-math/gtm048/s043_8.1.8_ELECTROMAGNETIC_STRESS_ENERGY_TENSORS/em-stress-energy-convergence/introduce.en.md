The electromagnetic stress-energy tensor $\hat{T}$ satisfies the timelike convergence condition and the strong energy condition, which are crucial for singularity theorems in general relativity. However, $\hat{T}$ is not a normal operator, meaning it does not commute with its adjoint. This algebraic property distinguishes the electromagnetic stress-energy from simpler matter models.
