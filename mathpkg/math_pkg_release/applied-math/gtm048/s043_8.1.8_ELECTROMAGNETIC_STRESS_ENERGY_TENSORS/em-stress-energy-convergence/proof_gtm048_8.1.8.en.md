The proof is left as Exercise 8.1.8(d) to the reader. To show $\hat{T}$ is not normal, one computes $\hat{T} \hat{T}^* - \hat{T}^* \hat{T}$ and verifies it does not vanish in general. The timelike convergence condition $Ric(Z, Z) \geq 0$ for all timelike $Z$ follows from the algebraic form of the electromagnetic stress-energy tensor and Einstein's field equation. The strong energy condition is verified by computing the trace-reversed stress-energy and checking the sign condition.
