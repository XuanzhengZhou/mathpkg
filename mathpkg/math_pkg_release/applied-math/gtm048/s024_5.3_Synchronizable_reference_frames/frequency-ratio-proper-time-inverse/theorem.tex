Let $\sigma: [a, b] \times [-\varepsilon, \varepsilon] \to M$ be a rectangle with $\sigma_0$ a freely falling photon, and let $\gamma_A(v) = \sigma(A, v)$ for $A = a, b$ be the timelike observer curves with instantaneous observers $Z_A = \dot{\gamma}_A(0) / |\dot{\gamma}_A(0)|$. Then the frequency ratio $\tau$ for $([\sigma_0], Z_a, Z_b)$ satisfies

$$\tau = \left(\frac{|\dot{\gamma}_b(0)|}{|\dot{\gamma}_a(0)|}\right)^{-1},$$

that is, $\tau$ is the inverse of the proper time ratio for $\sigma$ at $\sigma_0$.
