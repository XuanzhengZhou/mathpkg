Let $(V, g)$ be an $N$-dimensional Lorentzian vector space and let $W \subset V$ be a subspace.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $W$ is timelike iff $W^\perp$ is spacelike. Moreover, $W$ is spacelike iff $W^\perp$ is timelike.
\item[(b)] $W$ is lightlike iff $W \cap W^\perp \neq \{0\}$ and $W$ contains no timelike vector. Moreover, $W$ is lightlike iff $W^\perp$ is lightlike.
\end{enumerate}
