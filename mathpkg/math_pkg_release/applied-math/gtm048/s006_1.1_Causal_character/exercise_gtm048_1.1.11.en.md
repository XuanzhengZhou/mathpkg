Let $(M, g)$ be a Lorentzian manifold and let $X \in M_x$ and $\omega \in M_x^*$ be physically equivalent. Show that the causal character assigned to $X$ by $g_x$ is the same as the causal character assigned to $\omega$ by $\hat{g}_x$, where $\hat{g}_x$ is the Lorentzian inner product on $M_x^*$ given in Exercise 0.0.15.
