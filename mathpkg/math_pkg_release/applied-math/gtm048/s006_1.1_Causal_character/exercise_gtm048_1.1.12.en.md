Let $(M, g)$ be a Lorentzian manifold, $N$ a manifold, and $\phi: N \to M$ an immersion. The pullback $\phi^* g$ is called the metric induced on $N$ by $\phi$ iff $\phi^* g$ is a metric on $N$. Show that $\phi^* g$ is a metric on $N$ iff $\phi(N)$ is timelike or spacelike.
