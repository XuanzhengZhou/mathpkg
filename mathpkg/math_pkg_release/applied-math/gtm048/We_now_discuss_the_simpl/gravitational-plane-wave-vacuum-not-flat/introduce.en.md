Proposition 7.6.1 establishes that gravitational plane wave spacetimes satisfy the vacuum Einstein field equations (Ricci tensor vanishes) while possessing non-trivial curvature. The proof proceeds by computing the connection forms and curvature components in the orthonormal frame dual to the natural coordinate basis. The only non-vanishing curvature components are $R_{1414} = -f''(\phi)/2$ and $R_{1424} = -g''(\phi)/2$ (up to constant factors depending on the observer's energy), confirming the wave carries genuine gravitational degrees of freedom encoded in the second derivatives of the amplitude functions.
