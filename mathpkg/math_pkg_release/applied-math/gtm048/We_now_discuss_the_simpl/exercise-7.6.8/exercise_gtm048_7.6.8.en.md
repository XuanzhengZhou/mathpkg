Let $\gamma: \mathcal{E} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^4$ be a geodesic observer, and let $W$ be a unit vector field over $\gamma$ such that $Wu$ is a principal direction for $\psi_u$ $\forall u \in \mathcal{E}$. Show $W$ is parallel $\forall$ such observer iff $(M, g)$ is linearly polarized.
