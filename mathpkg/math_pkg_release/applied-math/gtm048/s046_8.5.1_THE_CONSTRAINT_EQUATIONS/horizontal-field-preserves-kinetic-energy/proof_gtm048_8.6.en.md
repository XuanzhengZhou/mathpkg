We have $K = \frac{1}{2} g(I, I)$. Since $g$ is metric-compatible, $Dg = 0$, we compute

$$dK(H) = \frac{1}{2} H(g(I, I)) = g((\Pi^* D)_H I, I).$$

But $H$ is horizontal, meaning $(\Pi^* D)_H I = 0$ by definition. Hence $dK(H) = 0$.
