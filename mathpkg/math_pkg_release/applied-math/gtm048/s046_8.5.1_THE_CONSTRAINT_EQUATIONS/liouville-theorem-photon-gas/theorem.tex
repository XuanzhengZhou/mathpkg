Let $\omega$ be the 7-form on the future light cone $\mathcal{L}^+ \subset TM$ defined by $\omega = A \wedge (\Xi^* \Omega)$, where $A$ is the natural 3-form on each fiber $\mathcal{L}^+_x$ and $\Omega$ is the Lorentzian volume element of $(M, g)$. Let $L$ be the Liouville operator on $TM$, restricted to $\mathcal{L}^+$. Then

$$\mathcal{L}_L \omega = 0,$$

where $\mathcal{L}_L$ denotes the Lie derivative. Consequently, $\mathcal{L}_L [i(L) \omega] = 0$ as well.
