Let $B$ be a 3-manifold, let $\beta: B \rightarrow M$ be a spacelike imbedding, and let $k$ be the second fundamental form of $\beta$; write $b = \beta^* g$. Thus $(B, b)$ is a Riemannian 3-manifold. Denote its scalar curvature by $s: B \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ and let $\tilde{k}$ be the $(1, 1)$-tensor field on $B$ physically equivalent to $k$ via $b$. The triple $(B, b, \tilde{k})$ satisfies the \textbf{vacuum constraint equations} if:

$$
s = (\operatorname{tr} \tilde{k})^2 - \operatorname{tr}(\tilde{k} \circ \tilde{k}),
$$

$$
\operatorname{div} \tilde{k} = d(\operatorname{tr} \tilde{k}),
$$

where the Levi-Civita connection of $b$ is used to form the divergence $\operatorname{div}$, and $\operatorname{tr}(\tilde{k} \circ \tilde{k}) = \sum_{\rho=1}^{3} \tilde{k}_{\rho}^{\alpha} \tilde{k}_{\alpha}^{\rho}$ in local coordinates.
