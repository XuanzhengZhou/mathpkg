Let $B$ be a 3-manifold, $\beta: B \to M$ a spacelike imbedding, $k$ the second fundamental form, $b = \beta^* g$. Show that if $(M,g)$ has zero Ricci tensor, then $(B,b,k)$ obeys the constraint equations:
$$R_B = (\operatorname{trace} \tilde{k})^2 - \operatorname{trace}[\tilde{k} \otimes \tilde{k}],$$
$$\operatorname{div} \tilde{k} = d(\operatorname{trace} \tilde{k}).$$
Conversely, if these hold for every such imbedding, show $(M,g)$ is Ricci flat.
