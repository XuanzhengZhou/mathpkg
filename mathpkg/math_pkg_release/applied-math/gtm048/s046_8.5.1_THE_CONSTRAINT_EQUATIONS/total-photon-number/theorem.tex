Let $\mathcal{N} \subset \mathcal{L}^+$ be an imbedded 6-submanifold whose closure is an integration region intersected at most once by each integral curve of the Liouville operator $L$. Let $F$ be a photon gas distribution function on $M$ (pulled back to $\mathcal{L}^+$). The \textbf{total number of photons} in ("crossing") $\mathcal{N}$ is defined as

$$\left| \int_{\mathcal{N}} F \, i(L) \omega \right|,$$

where $\omega$ is the 7-form on $\mathcal{L}^+$ and $i(L)$ denotes interior multiplication by the Liouville operator.
