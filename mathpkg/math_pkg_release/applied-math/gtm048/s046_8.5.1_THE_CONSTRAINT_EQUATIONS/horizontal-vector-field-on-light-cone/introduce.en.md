A horizontal vector field on the future light cone $\mathcal{L}^+$ is the restriction of the horizontal condition from the full tangent bundle. The identity vector field $I_0$ is constrained to have vanishing covariant derivative along $H$ under the pullback connection $\Xi^* D$. This concept is essential for the geometric analysis of photon gases, where the light cone serves as the phase space for massless particles.
