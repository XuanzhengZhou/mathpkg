Let $\eta$ be a differential form on a semi-Riemannian manifold. Then the exterior derivative has the following expressions in components:

For a 1-form:
$$(d\eta)_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}(\eta_{j|i} - \eta_{i|j}).$$

For a 2-form:
$$(d\eta)_{ijk} = \frac{1}{3}(\eta_{ij|k} + \eta_{ki|j} + \eta_{jk|i}).$$

For a 3-form:
$$(d\eta)_{ijkl} = \frac{1}{4}(\eta_{ijk|l} - \eta_{ijl|k} + \eta_{ikl|j} - \eta_{jkl|i}).$$

Here the vertical bar denotes the covariant derivative (e.g., $\eta_{j|i} = D_{\partial_i} \eta(\partial_j)$).
