The charge-current density $J$ of a matter model $\mathcal{M}$ is a vector field on spacetime that encodes the flow of electric charge. For a finite collection of particle flows, each flow with charge $e_A$, world density $\eta_A$, and 4-velocity $P_A$ contributes a term $e_A \eta_A P_A$ to the total charge-current density. The vector field $J$ serves as the source term in Maxwell's equations, linking the matter content to the electromagnetic field.
