If a geodesic $\gamma: E \rightarrow M$ of $(M, g)$ is an (orientation-preserving) reparametrization of another geodesic $\xi: F \rightarrow M$, then $\gamma$ is necessarily a \textbf{positive affine reparametrization} of $\xi$; that is, the reparametrization function $\phi: E \rightarrow F$ satisfies $\phi(u) = au + b$ for some constants $a > 0$ and $b \in \mathbb{R}$.
