An orientation-preserving reparametrization re-traces the same image as the original curve but with a different parameterization obtained by composing with a strictly increasing smooth change of parameter $\phi$. The condition $\phi' > 0$ ensures that the direction of traversal along the curve is preserved. This notion is fundamental for studying geometric properties of curves that are invariant under reparametrization.
