This lemma is a spacetime analogue of the classical Gauss Lemma from Riemannian geometry. Under the assumptions that $X$ is a geodesic vector field with constant squared norm, and $W$ is Lie parallel along an integral curve $\gamma$ of $X$, the inner product $g(\gamma_*, W)$ remains constant along $\gamma$. Moreover, the first and second covariant derivatives of $W$ along $\gamma$ are orthogonal to $\gamma_*$. The proof uses the symmetry of the Levi-Civita connection $D$ and the fact that $L_X \tilde{W} = 0$ together with $g(X,X)$ constant to deduce $X g(X, \tilde{W}) = 0$.
