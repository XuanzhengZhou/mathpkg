Let $X$ be a vector field on a spacetime $(M, g)$. Suppose: $X$ is geodesic, $g(X, X)$ is constant on $M$, and $W$ is a vector field, Lie parallel with respect to $X$, over an integral curve $\gamma: \mathcal{E} \rightarrow M$ of $X$. Then
$$g(\gamma_*, W) = \text{constant}$$
and
$$g(D_{\gamma_*} W, \gamma_*) = 0 = g(D_{\gamma_*} D_{\gamma_*} W, \gamma_*).$$
