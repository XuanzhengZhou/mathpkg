Given an arbitrary tensor field $C$ on the target manifold $M$, precomposing with $\phi$ yields a tensor field over $\phi$, denoted $C \circ \phi$. This is called the restriction of $C$ to $\phi$. Together with the pushforward construction $\phi_* B$, these are the two most natural ways that tensor fields over a map arise.
