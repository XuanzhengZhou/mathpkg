Let $(M, g, D)$ be a spacetime and let $\gamma: \mathcal{E} \rightarrow M$ be a curve. For a vector field $X$ over $\gamma$, we write
$$g(\gamma_*, X) := (g \circ \gamma)(\gamma_*, X \circ \gamma).$$
For the norm of the tangent vector field, we write
$$|\gamma_*|^2 := |(g \circ \gamma)(\gamma_*, \gamma_*)|.$$
