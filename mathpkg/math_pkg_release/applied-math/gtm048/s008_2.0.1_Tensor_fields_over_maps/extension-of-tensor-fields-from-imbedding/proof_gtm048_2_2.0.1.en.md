The proof uses a partition of unity argument. Since $M$ is paracompact and $\phi(N)$ is closed, one can extend each locally defined tensor field on a coordinate neighborhood covering $\phi(N)$ using bump functions from a partition of unity subordinate to a suitable open cover. See Kobayashi--Nomizu [1], p. 58 for the complete argument.
