In the Einstein--de Sitter model, let $\Omega$ be the apparent size of a galaxy at cosmological redshift $\varsigma$, let $A$ be its intrinsic area, and let $d$ be the spatial distance. Then:
$$\frac{\Omega}{A} = \left(\frac{\varsigma}{d}\right)^2,$$
so that the area-distance is $d_A = d/\varsigma$.
