This proposition gives a clean formula for the frequency ratio of a photon in a stationary spacetime in terms of the norm of the Killing vector field at the emission and observation events. Together with the conservation law $g(\lambda_*, X \circ \lambda) = \text{constant}$ for geodesics in the presence of a Killing field, this provides the mathematical basis for computing gravitational redshifts.
