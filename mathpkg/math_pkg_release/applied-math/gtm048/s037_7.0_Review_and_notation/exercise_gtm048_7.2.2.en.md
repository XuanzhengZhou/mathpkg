Show:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Einstein-de Sitter spacetime is not stationary.
\item[(b)] $\left(1 - \frac{2\mu}{r}\right)^{-1/2} \frac{\partial}{\partial t}$ is a static reference frame on a normal Schwarzschild spacetime (see Section 1.4.2).
\end{enumerate}
