In general relativity, time independence of a gravitational field is characterized not by coordinates but by the existence of a distinguished family of observers who experience an unchanging local geometry. A reference frame $Z$ is stationary if some positive rescaling $fZ$ is a Killing vector field, meaning the metric is invariant under the flow of $fZ$. This generalizes the coordinate condition $\partial_4 g_{ij} = 0$ to a coordinate-free setting.
