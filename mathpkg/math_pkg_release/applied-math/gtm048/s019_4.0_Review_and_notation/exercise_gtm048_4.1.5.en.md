Show that in local coordinates the components of the Einstein tensor $G$ can be expressed as sums and products of the components of $g$ and $\hat{g}$, first derivatives of the components of $g$, and the second derivatives of the components of $g$. Show that the second derivatives of the components of $g$ appear linearly.
