Let $(M, \mathcal{M}, F)$ be a relativistic model. Let $G = \text{Ric} - \frac{1}{2} g S$ be the Einstein tensor of the spacetime $M$, let $T$ be the $(0,2)$ tensor field physically equivalent to the stress-energy tensor $\hat{T}$ of the matter model $\mathcal{M}$, and let $E$ be the $(0,2)$ tensor field physically equivalent to the stress-energy tensor $\hat{E}$ for the electromagnetic field $F$. Then $(M, \mathcal{M}, F)$ obeys the Einstein field equation iff

$$G = T + E.$$
