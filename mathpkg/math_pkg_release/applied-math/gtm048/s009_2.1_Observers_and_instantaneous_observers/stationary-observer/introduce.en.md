A stationary observer in the normal Schwarzschild spacetime is an integral curve of the future-pointing timelike unit vector field proportional to the coordinate vector field $\partial/\partial t$. The name "stationary" reflects that the spatial coordinates of such an observer do not change with respect to the Schwarzschild time coordinate. This is the simplest family of observers in the Schwarzschild geometry.
