A comoving observer in Einstein--de Sitter spacetime follows an integral curve of the vector field $Z = \partial_4$, which is the canonical future-pointing unit timelike vector field of this cosmological model. Such observers are "at rest" relative to the cosmic expansion, making them the natural family of fundamental observers for this spacetime.
