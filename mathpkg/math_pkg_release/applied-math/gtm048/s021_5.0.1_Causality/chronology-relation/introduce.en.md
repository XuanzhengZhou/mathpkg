The chronology relation $\ll$ is a binary relation on a space-time $M$ that encodes which events can be connected by a future-pointing timelike curve. It is a subset of $M \times M$ and forms the foundation of the causal hierarchy: $\ll$ is always a subset of the causality relation $\leq$. Locally, $\ll$ behaves as the notation suggests, but globally it can exhibit subtle and beautiful structure that is studied in depth in Section 8.3.
