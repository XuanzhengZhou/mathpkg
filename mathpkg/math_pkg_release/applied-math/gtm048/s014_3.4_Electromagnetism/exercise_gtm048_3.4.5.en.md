On Minkowski space, suppose

$$F = 2 \sum_{\alpha=1}^{3} E^\alpha \, du^\alpha \wedge du^4 + 2 \bigl[ B^1 \, du^2 \wedge du^3 + B^2 \, du^3 \wedge du^1 + B^3 \, du^1 \wedge du^2 \bigr],$$

where for each $\alpha \in \{1, 2, 3\}$, $E^\alpha$ and $B^\alpha$ are $C^\infty$ functions $\mathbb{R}^4 \to \mathbb{R}$. Show that for all $z \in \mathbb{R}^4$, the instantaneous observer $(z, \partial_4 z)$ measures electric and magnetic vectors

$$E = \sum_{\alpha=1}^{3} E^\alpha(z) \, \partial_\alpha z, \qquad B = \sum_{\alpha=1}^{3} B^\alpha(z) \, \partial_\alpha z,$$

respectively.
