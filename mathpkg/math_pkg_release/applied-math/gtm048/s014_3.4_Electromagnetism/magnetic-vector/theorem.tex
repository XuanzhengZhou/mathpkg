Let $F$ be an electromagnetic field on a spacetime $M$, and let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer. The \textbf{magnetic vector} that $(z, Z)$ measures for $F$ is the unique vector $B \in Z^\perp$ satisfying
$$4! \, \Omega(X, Y, B, Z) = F(X, Y) \quad \forall\, X, Y \in Z^\perp,$$
where $\Omega$ is the metric volume element on $M$.
