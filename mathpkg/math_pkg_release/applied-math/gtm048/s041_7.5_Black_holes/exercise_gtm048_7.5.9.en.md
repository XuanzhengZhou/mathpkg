Let $K$ be a Kruskal spacetime and $S = r^{-1}(a) \subset K$ be the level hypersurface of $r$ for $a \in (0, \infty)$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Show that $S$ can be regarded as an immersed $3$-submanifold which is imbedded iff $a \neq 2\mu$.
\item[(b)] Show that $S$ can contain a photon worldline iff $a = 3\mu$ or $a = 2\mu$. For $a = 2\mu$, we can regard the photon as one which runs outward as fast as it can in order to remain still.
\item[(c)] In (a) let $a = 2\mu$ and suppose $\psi: \mathbb{S}^2 \to S$ is any imbedding. Show $\psi$ is spacelike and that the intrinsic $2$-area of $\psi(\mathbb{S}^2) \subset K$ is $4\pi\mu^2$ no matter what shape $\psi(\mathbb{S}^2)$ has! [\textit{Hint}: compare Exercise 1.4.9 for part (c).]
\end{enumerate}
