Proposition 7.5.2 establishes that the open submanifold $M_I$ of the Kruskal spacetime (where $u > 0, v > 0$) is isometric to the normal Schwarzschild spacetime with active mass $8\pi\mu$. The proof constructs an explicit diffeomorphism $\psi: M_I \to (-\infty, \infty) \times (2\mu, \infty) \times \mathcal{S}^2$ via the coordinate transformation $t = 2\mu[\ln u - \ln(-v)]$, together with the Kruskal-Szekeres relations for $u$ and $v$ in terms of $r$ and $t$. The metric then transforms to the standard Schwarzschild form $-(1-2\mu/r)dt^2 + (1-2\mu/r)^{-1}dr^2 + r^2 h$.
