It is an empirical fact that all observed particles with zero rest-mass also have zero electric charge. While quantum theory can partially explain this relationship, the present treatment assumes it as an ad hoc axiom. This axiom is consistent with the known zero-mass particles: photons, neutrinos, and the hypothetical graviton, all of which carry zero electric charge.
