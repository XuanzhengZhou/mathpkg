The following spacetimes are globally hyperbolic:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] Minkowski space.
\item[(b)] Einstein--de Sitter spacetime.
\item[(c)] Any Kruskal spacetime.
\end{itemize}
There also exist complete spacetimes that are not globally hyperbolic, and in Minkowski space there exist 3-dimensional, spacelike imbedded submanifolds that are complete in the induced metric but are not Cauchy surfaces.
