Show that $(M, g)$ represents the trivial gravitational field if and only if $(M, g)$ is simply connected, complete, and flat (cf. the Ambrose–Hicks theorem — e.g., in Wolf [1]; or use the results of Bishop–Goldberg sections 5.6–5.12).
