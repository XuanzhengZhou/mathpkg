Let $X$ be a vector field on the normal Schwarzschild region $N \subset U$. Then $X$ is Killing and future-pointing iff there exists a constant $a > 0$ such that $X = a (\partial/\partial t)|_N$.

Moreover, for $a = 1$, $X = \partial/\partial t$ has the additional property that for every observer $\gamma: (b, \infty) \to N$ who escapes to infinity,
$$\lim_{u \to \infty} g(X \circ \gamma, X \circ \gamma) = -1$$
as $u \in (b, \infty)$ approaches infinity.
