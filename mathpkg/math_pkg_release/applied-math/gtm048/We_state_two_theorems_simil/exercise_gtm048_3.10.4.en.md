Suppose: $M$ is Einstein--de Sitter spacetime; $M = (m, 0, mZ, \eta)$ is a particle flow with $m \neq 0$ and $Z$ the comoving reference frame; and $F = 0$.

(a) Show $\hat{T} = \rho Z \otimes Z$, where $\rho = m^2 \eta$ is the energy density a comoving instantaneous observer measures for $\hat{T}$.

(b) $(M, M, F)$ obeys the differential energy-momentum 'conservation' law if and only if $\rho = (u^4)^{-2} h$ where $h: M \rightarrow [0, \infty)$ is a function such that $Zh = 0$ ("the matter energy density decreases as the universe ages").
