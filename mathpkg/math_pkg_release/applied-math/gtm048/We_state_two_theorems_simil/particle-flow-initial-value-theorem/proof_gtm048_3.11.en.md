The proof of Theorem 3.11.2 combines ideas from Proposition 3.2.3, Theorem 3.8.3, and Theorem 3.11.1; it is left as an exercise in the text. The fundamental idea is to use the local existence and uniqueness theory for ordinary differential equations (as in Proposition 3.2.3 and Theorem 3.8.3) together with the reduction to PDE techniques as used in the proof of Theorem 3.11.1.
