A quasi-gas on a spacetime $M$ is a finite superposition of particle flows on $M$ (in the sense of Section 3.5 and Example 3.13.3) satisfying the following two generic conditions:

(a) $M$ is a nonempty set.

(b) No two distinct particle flows in $M$ have the same 4-velocity at any $x \in M$; that is, their reference frames $Z_A = m_A^{-1} P_A$ are distinct at every spacetime point.
