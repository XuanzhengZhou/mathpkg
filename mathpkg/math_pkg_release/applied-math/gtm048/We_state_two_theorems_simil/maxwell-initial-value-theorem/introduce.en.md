Theorem 3.11.1 establishes existence and uniqueness of solutions to Maxwell's equations on a spacetime, given initial data on a spacelike submanifold. The initial data consist of a 2-form $F_0$ satisfying two compatibility conditions (the pullback of $dF_0$ vanishes and a certain divergence condition holds). The proof reduces Maxwell's equations to a symmetric hyperbolic system of PDEs in the sense of Friedrichs, to which the fundamental existence theorem for such systems applies.
