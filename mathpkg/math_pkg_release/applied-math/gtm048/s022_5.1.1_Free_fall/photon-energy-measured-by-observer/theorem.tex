Let $\lambda: \mathcal{E} \rightarrow M$ be a 1--1 photon with energy-momentum $Y = \lambda_* u$ at $x = \lambda u$. Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer at $z = x$. The \textbf{energy} that $(z, Z)$ measures for the photon is
$$
e = -g(Y, Z) > 0,
$$
where $g$ is the Lorentzian metric. The vector $Y$ decomposes as
$$
Y = e(Z + U),
$$
where $U \in Z^\perp$ is the spatial direction that $(z, Z)$ measures.
