Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer on a spacetime $(M, g)$. Let $p: M_z \to Z^\perp$ be the orthogonal projection onto the local rest space and let $h(X, Y) = g(pX, pY)$ be the induced Riemannian metric.

For a tangent vector $X \in M_z$, the \textbf{Newtonian length} of $X$ as observed by $(z, Z)$ is defined to be
$$[h(X, X)]^{1/2} = |pX|,$$
where $|pX| = \sqrt{g(pX, pX)}$ is the Euclidean norm of the projection of $X$ onto the local rest space $Z^\perp$.
