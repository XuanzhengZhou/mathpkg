Proposition 6.2.7 establishes the fundamental link between a dust matter model and the geometry of a simple cosmological spacetime. When the electromagnetic field is set to zero, the Einstein field equation forces the stress-energy tensor of the dust to match the Einstein tensor of the cosmological metric. This yields three consequences: the pressure vanishes ($p_0 = 0$), the comoving reference frame aligns with the cosmological time direction $Z = \partial_4$, and the energy density is given by $\rho = (4/3)(u^4)^{-2}$. This proposition underpins the Einstein--de Sitter model and shows that the simple cosmological spacetime plus dust is a consistent solution of the Einstein equations.
