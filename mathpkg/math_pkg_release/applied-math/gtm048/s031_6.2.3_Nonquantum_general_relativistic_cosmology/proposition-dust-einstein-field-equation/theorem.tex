Let $(M, g)$ be a simple cosmological spacetime with $\dot{R} > 0$. Suppose the Einstein field equation $G = T$ holds, where $\hat{T}$ is the stress-energy tensor of a matter model $\mathcal{M}$ on $M$ which is a dust. Let $Z$ be the reference frame of $\mathcal{M}$. Then $Z = \partial_4$ and $\mathcal{M} = (Z, \rho)$ with $\rho = (4/3)(u^4)^{-2}$; in particular, $M$ is the Einstein--de Sitter spacetime.
