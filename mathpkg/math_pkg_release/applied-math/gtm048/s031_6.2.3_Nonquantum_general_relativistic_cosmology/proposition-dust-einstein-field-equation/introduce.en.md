This proposition shows that combining the Einstein field equation with the assumption that matter is dust in a simple cosmological spacetime uniquely determines the spacetime to be the Einstein-de Sitter spacetime. The proposition is the mathematical justification for the Einstein-de Sitter model being the unique dust-filled simple cosmological model obeying the Einstein field equation.
