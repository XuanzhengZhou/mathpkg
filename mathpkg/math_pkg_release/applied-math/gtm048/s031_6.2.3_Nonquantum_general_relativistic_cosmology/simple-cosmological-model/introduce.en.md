A simple cosmological model generalizes the Einstein-de Sitter model by keeping the simple cosmological spacetime structure while allowing more flexible matter models. The condition $R \to 0$ as $u^4 \to 0$ encodes the big bang singularity, and the cosmological time at here-now is fixed at 10 billion years, consistent with observational estimates.
