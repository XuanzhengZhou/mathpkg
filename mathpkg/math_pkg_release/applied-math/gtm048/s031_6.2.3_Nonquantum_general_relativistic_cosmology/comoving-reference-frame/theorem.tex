Let $(M, \mathcal{M}, F)$ be a relativistic model satisfying assumptions (a)--(c) of Section 6.2.3. By assumption (b) (maximality of $M$) and Corollary 3.14.2, there is a unique eigenvector reference frame $Z$ for the stress-energy tensor $\hat{T}$ of the matter model $\mathcal{M}$. We call $Z$ the \textbf{comoving reference frame} for the model.
