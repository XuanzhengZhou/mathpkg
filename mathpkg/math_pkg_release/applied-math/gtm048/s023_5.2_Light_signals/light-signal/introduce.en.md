A light signal formalizes the trajectory of a photon in general relativity by identifying freely falling photons that represent the same physical signal. This equivalence relation captures the idea that two photon worldlines with the same image correspond to the same observable light ray. The concept provides a rigorous foundation for studying causality and optical measurements in curved spacetimes.
