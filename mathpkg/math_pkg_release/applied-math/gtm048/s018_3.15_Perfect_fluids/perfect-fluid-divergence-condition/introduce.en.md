This proposition analyzes the matter equation $\operatorname{div} \hat{T} = 0$ for a perfect fluid stress-energy tensor. The divergence-free condition splits into two coupled equations: a conservation law relating the divergence of the energy-momentum current $\rho Z$ to the pressure and expansion of the reference frame, and an acceleration equation expressing the covariant derivative $D_Z Z$ of the reference frame in terms of the pressure gradient. This decomposition is fundamental for deriving the equations of motion of a perfect fluid in general relativity.
