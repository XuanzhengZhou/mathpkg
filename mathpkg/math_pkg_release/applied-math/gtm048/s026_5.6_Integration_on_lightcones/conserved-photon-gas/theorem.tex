Let $\lambda: \mathcal{E} \to M$ be a lightlike geodesic and define the associated curve $\bar{\lambda}: \mathcal{E} \to \mathcal{L}^+$ by $\bar{\lambda}(u) = (\lambda u, \lambda_* u)$ for all $u \in \mathcal{E}$. Let $\mathcal{A} \subset M$ be a subset. A photon gas $F$ is \textbf{conserved on $\mathcal{A}$} iff $F \circ \bar{\lambda}$ is constant for every lightlike geodesic $\lambda$ whose image lies wholly in $\mathcal{A}$.

Equivalently, let $L$ be the vector field on $\mathcal{L}^+$ obtained by restricting the geodesic spray of the tangent bundle to $\mathcal{L}^+$. Then $F$ is conserved on $M$ iff $L F = 0$.
