Find a photon gas $F$ on Minkowski space $M$ such that $\operatorname{div} \hat{T} = 0 = \operatorname{div} N$, but $F$ is not conserved on $M$. This shows that the conservation of the stress-energy tensor and number density does not imply conservation of the photon gas itself.
