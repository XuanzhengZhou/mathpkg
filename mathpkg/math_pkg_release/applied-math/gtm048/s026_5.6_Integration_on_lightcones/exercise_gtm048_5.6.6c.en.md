Let $f: \mathcal{L}_x^+ 	o [0, \infty)$ be a function of rapid decay and suppose $\omega \in M_x^*$. Show that $\omega$ can be written as the sum of two causal 1-forms, and then show that
$$\int_{\mathcal{L}_x^+} 	ilde{\omega} f \Lambda_x$$
exists and is finite.
