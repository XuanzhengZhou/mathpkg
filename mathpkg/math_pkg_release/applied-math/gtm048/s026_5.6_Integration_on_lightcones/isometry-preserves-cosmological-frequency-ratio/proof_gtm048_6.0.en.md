*Proof.* Since $\psi$ is an isometry, it maps the comoving reference frame $\partial_4$ to itself and preserves the cosmological time $u^4$. Consequently, $\psi$ maps light signals to light signals while preserving the instantaneous observers $\partial_4 x$ and $\partial_4 z$ at the endpoints. The frequency ratio $\iota$, being defined in terms of these canonically determined quantities, is therefore invariant under $\psi$.
