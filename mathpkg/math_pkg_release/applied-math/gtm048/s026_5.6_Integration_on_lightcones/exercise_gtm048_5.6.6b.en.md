Let $F_X$ be a photon distribution function for an instantaneous observer $(x, X)$ and suppose $F_X$ is Planck. Using part (a), show that $F_X \circ \pi_X^{-1}$ is a function of rapid decay, where $\pi_X: P_X 	o \mathcal{L}_x^+$ is the natural diffeomorphism.
