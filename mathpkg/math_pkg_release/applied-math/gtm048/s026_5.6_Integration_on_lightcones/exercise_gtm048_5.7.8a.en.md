Find two photon gases $F$ and $F'$ on Minkowski space $M$ such that $F 
eq F'$, yet their stress-energy tensors coincide ($\hat{T} = \hat{T}'$) and their number density vectors coincide ($N = N'$). This demonstrates that a photon gas has many properties in addition to those described by its stress-energy tensor and number density.
