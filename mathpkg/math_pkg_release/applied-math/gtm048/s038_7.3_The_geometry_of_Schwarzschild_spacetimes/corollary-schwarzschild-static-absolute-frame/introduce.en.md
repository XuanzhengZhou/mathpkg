This corollary constructs a canonically determined static, absolute reference frame on the normal Schwarzschild spacetime. The observers at rest in this frame maintain constant $r$ coordinate, meaning they hover at a fixed distance from the central mass. The time function $t$ is uniquely determined up to an additive constant by the asymptotic condition that it approaches proper time at infinity.
