Proposition 7.3.7 gives the first integrals (conserved quantities) for freely falling particles in Schwarzschild spacetimes. The constants $E$ (energy), $J$ (total angular momentum), and $J_x$ (angular momentum about the $x$-axis) arise from the Killing symmetries of the spacetime: time-translation invariance yields $E$, and rotational invariance yields $J$ and $J_x$. These are the general-relativistic analogues of conserved energy and angular momentum in Newtonian central-force problems.
