Corollary 7.3.4 states that the scalar curvature invariant $f$, which measures the overall magnitude of the curvature tensor of a Schwarzschild spacetime, equals $144\mu^2/r^6$. This invariant is locally determinable by relative acceleration measurements (Proposition 2.3.3), and a particle escapes to infinity iff $\lim r \circ \gamma = \infty$.
