**Proof.** The proof proceeds by direct computation using the metric form of the Schwarzschild spacetime, the connection forms, and the curvature forms. The computation follows the same pattern as the curvature computation for Einstein-de Sitter spacetime in Proposition 1.4.4, using the orthonormal basis of 1-forms adapted to the Schwarzschild metric. The detailed calculation yields the stated expression for $\hat{R}$ in terms of the geometric quantities $dr$, $dt$, $T$, and $P^*\zeta$.
