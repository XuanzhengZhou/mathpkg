This proposition provides the explicit formula for the curvature tensor of Schwarzschild spacetimes. The curvature is proportional to $\mu/r^3$, showing that the gravitational field strength falls off as $1/r^3$ and that the spacetime is singular at $r = 0$. The formula also reveals that Schwarzschild spacetimes are Ricci flat (vacuum) but not flat.
