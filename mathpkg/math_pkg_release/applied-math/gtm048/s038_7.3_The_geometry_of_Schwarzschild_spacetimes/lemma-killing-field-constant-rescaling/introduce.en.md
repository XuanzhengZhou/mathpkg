This lemma states that a Killing vector field can only be rescaled by a constant to yield another Killing field. It is a fundamental result used in classifying symmetries of spacetimes. The proof uses the condition $g(D_X(\ell W), Y) + g(X, D_Y(\ell W)) = 0$ and shows that $X\ell = 0$ for all vectors $X$, forcing $\ell$ to be constant on a connected manifold.
