This proposition identifies the unique parallel vector field (up to scaling) in a gravitational plane wave spacetime as the lightlike propagation direction $Y$. This is analogous to the propagation direction of an electromagnetic plane wave. The fact that $Y$ is parallel means that the wave preserves its direction, and since $Y$ is also Killing, information propagates along integral curves of $Y$. The wavefronts are the 3-dimensional lightlike hypersurfaces orthogonal to $Y$.
