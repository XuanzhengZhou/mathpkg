Let $M_I \subset K$ be the open submanifold determined by $u > 0, v > 0$. Then $(M_I, g|_{M_I})$ is isometric to the normal Schwarzschild spacetime of active mass $8\pi\mu$. The isometry $\psi: M_I \to (-\infty, \infty) \times (2\mu, \infty) \times \mathcal{S}^2$ is given by:
$$\psi(n) = (t(n), r(n), P(n)),$$
where $t = 2\mu[\ln u - \ln(-v)]|_{M_I}$ and the inverse is determined by $u = (r - 2\mu)\exp[(r - 2\mu + t)/4\mu]$, $v = (r - 2\mu)\exp[(r - 2\mu - t)/4\mu]$.
