**Proof.** Let $\{\omega^i\}$ be the basis dual to the natural frame $\{X_i\}$ adapted to the plane wave. By algebra, $\omega^1 = du^1$, $\omega^2 = du^2$, $\omega^3 = \frac{1}{2}(du^4 + du^3) - F d\phi$, $\omega^4 = d\phi$. To compute the connection forms and curvature forms, one uses the orthonormal frame method. Direct computation shows that the Ricci tensor vanishes, while the curvature tensor is nonzero whenever $f$ or $g$ is non-constant, establishing that the spacetime is vacuum but not flat.
