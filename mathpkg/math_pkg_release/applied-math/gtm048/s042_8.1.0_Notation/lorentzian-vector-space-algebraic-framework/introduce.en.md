This section establishes the algebraic framework for studying Lorentzian vector spaces that arise as tangent spaces to spacetimes. Fixing an instantaneous observer $(z, Z)$ gives a preferred unit timelike vector, which allows the decomposition of tensors and the study of algebraic properties such as normal forms for stress-energy tensors. The concepts introduced here---wider metrics, Alexandrov topology, tensor decompositions---are fundamental tools used throughout general relativity.
