For all $X, Y \in M_z$ define $\langle X, Y \rangle = \{ V \in M_z \mid (Y - V) \in \mathcal{T}_z^+ \text{ and } (V - X) \in \mathcal{T}_z^+ \}$. Show that $\{ \langle X, Y \rangle \}$ is a basis for the standard topology of $M_z$; it is called the Alexandrov basis.
