This proposition gives an explicit coordinate characterization of the causal relation $x \leq z$ in Einstein-de Sitter spacetime. By introducing the auxiliary time function $u = 3(u^4)^{1/3}$ and the spatial distance function $\delta$, the causal relation reduces to a simple inequality: $\delta(x,z) \leq u z - u x$. The proof exploits the conformal relationship between Einstein-de Sitter spacetime and Minkowski space, where the causal structure is already understood.
