Let $\vec{E}$ be the stress-energy tensor of $F$.

Using the conventions in Section 9.0.3, show $\vec{E}$ becomes: the Newtonian electromagnetic energy density

$$U = \frac{1}{8\pi}\left(|\vec{E}|^2 + |\vec{B}|^2\right);$$

the Poynting vector

$$\vec{S} = \frac{1}{4\pi}(\vec{E} \times \vec{B});$$

and the Maxwell stress tensor $\vec{t}$, whose components are

$$t^{\alpha\beta} = -\frac{1}{4\pi} \left[ E^\alpha E^\beta + B^\alpha B^\beta - \frac{1}{2} \delta^{\alpha\beta} (|\vec{E}|^2 + |\vec{B}|^2) \right].$$
