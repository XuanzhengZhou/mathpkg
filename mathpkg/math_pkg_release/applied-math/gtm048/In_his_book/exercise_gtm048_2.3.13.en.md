Let $Q$ be a reference frame and $\omega$ be the physically equivalent 1-form.

(a) Show $d\omega = 0$ iff $Q$ is geodesic and irrotational (iff $Q$ is locally proper time synchronizable, by definition).

(b) On a normal Schwarzschild spacetime (Example 1.4.2) find a rigid, irrotational reference frame that is not geodesic.
