Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer on a spacetime $(M, g)$. Define
$$\mathcal{O}^3 = \{\psi: M_z \to M_z \mid \psi Z = Z,\; g(X, Y) = g(\psi X, \psi Y)\ \forall X, Y \in M_z\}.$$
Each $\psi \in \mathcal{O}^3$ is linear. Let $M_z = R \oplus T$ be the orthogonal decomposition where $T = \operatorname{span}\{Z\}$. Then $\psi|_T = \text{identity}$, so $\psi$ is determined by $\psi|_R$. Thus $\mathcal{O}^3$ is isomorphic to the group of automorphisms of $R$ preserving the positive definite inner product $g_z|_R$, i.e., $\mathcal{O}^3 \cong \mathrm{O}(3)$ (the rotation group of $\mathbb{R}^3$).
