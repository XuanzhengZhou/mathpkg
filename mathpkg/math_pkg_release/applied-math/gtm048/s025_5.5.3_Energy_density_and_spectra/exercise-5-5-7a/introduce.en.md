Exercise 5.5.7a asks the reader to make plausible an approximate equality connecting the integral of a photon distribution function $F_Z$ over a region $\mathcal{K}$ to a sum over photon beam parameters. The integral $\int F_Z \pi_Z$ (which counts photon worldlines) is approximated by $-\sum \eta_A z \, g(P_A, Z)$, where $\eta_A$ is the number density and $g(P_A, Z)$ involves the metric inner product of the photon 4-momentum with the observer's 4-velocity.
