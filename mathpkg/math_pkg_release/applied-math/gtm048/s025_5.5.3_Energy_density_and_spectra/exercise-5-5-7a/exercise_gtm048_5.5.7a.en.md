Suppose there is a very large, finite collection $\{(P_A, \eta_A)\}$ of photon beams that model the same physical situation that $F_Z$ describes at $z$. Then, roughly, $F_Z$ is world density per unit volume in 3-momentum space. More specifically, let $\mathcal{K} \subset \mathcal{S}_Z \times (0, \infty)$ be an integration region and define $K = \{A \mid P_A z \in \pi_Z \mathcal{K}\}$, where $\pi_Z$ is defined in Exercise 5.5.6a.

Make the following approximate equality plausible to yourself using Definition 3.2.1, Section 5.2.2, and some pictures:

$$\int_{\mathcal{K}} F_Z \pi_Z \cong -\sum_{A \in K} \eta_A z \, g(P_A, Z).$$
