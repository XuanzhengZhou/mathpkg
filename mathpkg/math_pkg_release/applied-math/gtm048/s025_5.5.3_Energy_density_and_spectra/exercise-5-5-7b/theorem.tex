Let $\{(P_A, \eta_A)\}$ be a large finite collection of photon beams modeling the same physical situation as $F_Z$ at $z$. Let $\mathcal{K} \subset \mathcal{S}_Z \times (0, \infty)$ be an integration region and $K = \{A \mid P_A z \in \pi_Z \mathcal{K}\}$. Then the following approximate equality is to be made plausible:

$$\int_{\mathcal{K}} eF_Z \pi_Z \cong \sum_{A \in K} [g(P_A, Z)]^2 \eta_A z = \sum_{A \in K} T_A(Z, Z),$$

where $\hat{T}_A$ is the stress-energy tensor of $(P_A, \eta_A)$.
