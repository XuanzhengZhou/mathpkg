Proposition 5.6.1 gives an intrinsic characterization of the 3-form $\Lambda_0$ on the future lightcone $\mathcal{L}_0^+$. The 3-form $\Lambda_0$ serves as a natural volume element on the lightcone, and the proposition shows that it is uniquely determined by the condition that its wedge product with the pullback of the canonical 1-form $\omega$ yields the pullback of the spacetime volume form $\Omega$. This characterization is independent of any choice of coordinates.
