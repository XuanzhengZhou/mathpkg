Let $(M, g)$ be a spacetime. Show there is a volume element $\chi$ on $M$ such that $\int_M \chi = 1$. (Remarks: $\chi$ is not unique; for suitable $A \in (0, \infty)$ and $M = \text{Minkowski space}$,
$$\chi = A \exp \left[ -\sum_{i=1}^{4} (u^i)^2 \right] du^1 \wedge \dots \wedge du^4$$
is an example.)
