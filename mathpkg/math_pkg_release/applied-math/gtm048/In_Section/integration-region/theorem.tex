Let $N$ be a $C^\infty$ manifold and let $\Lambda$ be a volume element on $N$, so that $N$ is orientable. An \textbf{integration region} in $N$ is a compact subset $\mathcal{K} \subset N$ such that the interior $\mathcal{K}^0$ is nonempty and $\mathcal{K}$ and $\partial \mathcal{K}$ are piecewise $C^\infty$. The manifold $N$ and its open submanifolds are taken as oriented via $\Lambda$.
