Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer on a spacetime $M$, let $\mathcal{L}_z^+$ be the future lightcone in $M_z$, let $Z^\perp$ be $(z, Z)$'s rest space, and let $\mathcal{S}_Z$ be $(z, Z)$'s celestial sphere. The \textbf{direction-energy space} of $(z, Z)$ is defined as
$$
P_z = \mathcal{S}_Z \times (0, \infty).
$$
The projection onto the second factor is denoted by $e: P_z \to (0, \infty)$, giving the measured energy of a photon with given direction.
