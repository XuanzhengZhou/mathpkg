Proposition 2.3.3 relates the 3-acceleration of a neighboring observer in a geodesic reference frame to the spacetime curvature via the operator $\psi_Z X = R_{ZX}Z$. This is the general relativistic analogue of the Newtonian tidal force equation: the relative acceleration between freely falling observers is governed by the Riemann curvature tensor.
