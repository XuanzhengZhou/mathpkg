Let $Q$ be a geodesic reference frame and let $W$ be a neighbor of an observer $\gamma: \mathcal{E} \rightarrow M$ in $Q$. Then the 3-acceleration of $W$ relative to $\gamma$ satisfies:
$$F_{\gamma_*}^2 W = \psi W,$$
where $(\psi W)u = \psi_{\gamma_* u}(W u)$ for all $u \in \mathcal{E}$, and $\psi_Z$ is the curvature operator defined by $\psi_Z X = R_{ZX}Z$ on the local rest space.
