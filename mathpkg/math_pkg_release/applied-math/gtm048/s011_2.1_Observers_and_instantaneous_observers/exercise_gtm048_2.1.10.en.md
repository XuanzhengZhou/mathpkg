**EXERCISE 2.1.10 (LENGTH CONTRACTION).** In 2-dimensional Minkowski space, let $\gamma_1$ and $\gamma_2$ be observers whose world lines intersect at $x$. Let $\mathcal{S}$ represent the complete history of a rigid rod. $\gamma_1$ is an observer at rest with respect to the rod and $\gamma_2$ is moving relative to the rod.

(a) Show $L_2 < L_1$, where $L_1$ and $L_2$ are the lengths of the rod measured by $\gamma_1$ and $\gamma_2$ respectively.

(b) If $|v|$ is the Newtonian speed of $\gamma_2$ observed by $(x, (\gamma_1)_* 0)$, compute $L_1/L_2$ in terms of $|v|$.
