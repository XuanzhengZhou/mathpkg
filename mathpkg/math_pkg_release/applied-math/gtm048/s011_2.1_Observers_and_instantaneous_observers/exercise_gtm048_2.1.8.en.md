**EXERCISE 2.1.8 (TIME DILATION).** In 2-dimensional Minkowski space (Section 0.2) let $\gamma_1$ and $\gamma_2$ be two freely falling observers such that $\gamma_1 0 = \gamma_2 0 = x$. For $s_1, s_2 > 0$, let $y = \gamma_1 s_1$, $z = \gamma_2 s_2$; assume that the geodesic joining $y$ to $z$ is orthogonal to the world line of $\gamma_1$ at $y$.

(a) Show $s_1 > s_2$.

(b) If $v$ is the Newtonian velocity of $\gamma_2$ observed by $(\gamma_1)_* 0$, show that $s_1/s_2 = 1/(1 - |v|^2)^{1/2}$.
