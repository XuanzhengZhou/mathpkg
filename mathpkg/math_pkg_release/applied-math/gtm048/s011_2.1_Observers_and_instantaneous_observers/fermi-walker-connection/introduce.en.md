The Fermi-Walker connection $F$ is the unique connection over an observer $\gamma$ obtained by projecting the pullback of the Levi-Civita connection $D$ onto the local rest space and local time axis separately. It provides the correct notion of "non-rotating" transport along an observer's world line, characterizing when a spatial direction remains unchanged over proper time. A gyroscope axis traces out an $F$-parallel vector field.
