A Lagrangian system with quadratic kinetic and potential energies

$$T = \frac{1}{2}(A \dot{q}, \dot{q}), \quad U = \frac{1}{2}(B q, q), \quad q \in \mathbb{R}^n$$

has $n$ characteristic oscillations, the directions of which are pairwise orthogonal with respect to the scalar product given by the kinetic energy matrix $A$:

$$(A \xi_i, \xi_j) = 0 \quad \text{for } i \neq j.$$
