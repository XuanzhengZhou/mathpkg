When the eigenvalue $\lambda = \omega^2$ is positive, the corresponding one-dimensional subsystem oscillates sinusoidally with frequency $\omega$. The full $n$-dimensional motion is obtained by exciting only this mode, setting all other coordinates $Q_j = 0$. The eigenvector $\xi$ satisfying $B\xi = \lambda A\xi$ gives the direction of this characteristic oscillation in the original configuration coordinates.
