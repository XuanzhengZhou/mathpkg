A Lagrangian system $(M, L)$ on a smooth manifold $M$ with Lagrangian $L: TM \to \mathbb{R}$ is said to admit a smooth map $h: M \to M$ if $L$ is invariant under the induced pushforward map $h_*: TM \to TM$. That is, for every tangent vector $\mathbf{v} \in TM$,

$$L(h_*\mathbf{v}) = L(\mathbf{v}).$$
