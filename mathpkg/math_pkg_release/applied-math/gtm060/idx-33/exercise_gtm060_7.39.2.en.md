Show that the operator $L_A$ is linear:
$$L_A(\lambda_1 \varphi_1 + \lambda_2 \varphi_2) = \lambda_1 L_A \varphi_1 + \lambda_2 L_A \varphi_2 \quad (\lambda_1, \lambda_2 \in \mathbb{R}).$$
Also, prove Leibniz's formula:
$$L_A(\varphi_1 \varphi_2) = \varphi_1 L_A \varphi_2 + \varphi_2 L_A \varphi_1.$$
