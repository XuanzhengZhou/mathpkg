Let $A^t$ and $B^s$ be the flows of vector fields $A$ and $B$ on a manifold $M$. For any smooth function $\varphi: M \to \mathbb{R}$ and any point $x \in M$, define
$$\Delta(t; s; x) = \varphi(A^t B^s x) - \varphi(B^s A^t x).$$
Then the mixed partial derivative at the origin is
$$\left. \frac{\partial^2}{\partial s \partial t} \right|_{s=t=0} \Delta(t; s; x) = \left( L_B L_A \varphi - L_A L_B \varphi \right)(x).$$
