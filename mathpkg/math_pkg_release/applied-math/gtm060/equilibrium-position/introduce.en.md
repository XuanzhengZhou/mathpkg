An equilibrium position in the phase plane is a phase point where the phase velocity vanishes: $(y, f(x)) = (0, 0)$. Equilibria correspond to critical points of the potential energy. Minima of $U$ are stable (Lyapunov stable), while maxima are unstable — a small perturbation causes the system to move away.
