Under motions in a central field, the angular momentum $\mathbf{M}$ relative to the center of the field $0$ does not change with time.

Proof. By definition $\mathbf{M} = [\mathbf{r}, \dot{\mathbf{r}}]$. By the lemma, $\dot{\mathbf{M}} = [\dot{\mathbf{r}}, \dot{\mathbf{r}}] + [\mathbf{r}, \ddot{\mathbf{r}}]$. Since the field is central it is apparent from the equations of motion that the vectors $\ddot{\mathbf{r}}$ and $\mathbf{r}$ are collinear. Therefore $\dot{\mathbf{M}} = 0$.
