The planets describe ellipses, with the Sun at one focus. In terms of the focal equation of a conic section, the orbit is given by

$$r = \frac{p}{1 + e \cos \varphi},$$

where $p = M^2/k$ is the parameter of the ellipse, $e = \sqrt{1 + 2EM^2/k^2}$ is the eccentricity, $M$ is the angular momentum, $E$ is the total energy, and the force field is $U(r) = -k/r$.
