By Theorem 1, the set of ellipsoids of revolution has codimension at least 2. A generic two-dimensional surface in the manifold of all ellipsoids intersects a codimension-2 submanifold transversally at isolated points. Thus in a typical two-parameter family, multiple spectra occur only at isolated parameter values. The proof in the presence of multiple spectra follows by passage to the limit.
