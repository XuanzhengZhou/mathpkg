For the binary quadratic form $Ax^2 + 2Bxy + Cy^2$, the discriminant of the characteristic equation factorises as

$$\Delta = (A + C)^2 - 4(AC - B^2) = (A - C)^2 + 4B^2.$$

Thus the single equation $\Delta = 0$ is equivalent to the system $A = C$, $B = 0$, determining a line in the three-dimensional space of quadratic forms, not a surface.
