If $|\operatorname{tr} A| < 2$, then a mapping $A'$ corresponding to a sufficiently close system will also have $|\operatorname{tr} A'| < 2$ by continuity of the trace functional, and hence will be stable. Therefore the trivial solution is strongly stable by definition.
