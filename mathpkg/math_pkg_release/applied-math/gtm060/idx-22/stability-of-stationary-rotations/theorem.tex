For a rigid body fixed at a point $O$ with distinct principal moments of inertia $I_1 > I_2 > I_3$, the stationary solutions of the Euler equations

$$M = M_1 e_1 \quad \text{and} \quad M = M_3 e_3$$

corresponding to the largest and smallest principal axes are stable in the sense of Liapunov, while the solution corresponding to the middle axis

$$M = M_2 e_2$$

is unstable.
