If the ellipsoid $E$ with semi-axes $a_1 \geq a_2 \geq \cdots \geq a_n$ contains the ellipsoid $E'$ with semi-axes $a'_1 \geq a'_2 \geq \cdots \geq a'_n$, both ellipsoids having the same center, then the semi-axes of the inside ellipsoid are smaller:
$$a_1 \geq a'_1,\quad a_2 \geq a'_2,\quad \ldots,\quad a_n \geq a'_n.$$
