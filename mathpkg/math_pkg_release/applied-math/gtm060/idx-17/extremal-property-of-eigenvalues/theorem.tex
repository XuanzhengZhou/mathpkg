The smallest semi-axis of any cross-section of the ellipsoid $E$ with semi-axes $a_1 \geq a_2 \geq \cdots \geq a_n$ by a subspace $\mathbb{R}^k$ is less than or equal to $a_k$:
$$a_k = \max_{\{\mathbb{R}^k\}} \min_{\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^k \cap E} \|\mathbf{x}\|$$
The upper bound is attained on the subspace spanned by the semi-axes $a_1 \geq a_2 \geq \cdots \geq a_k$.
