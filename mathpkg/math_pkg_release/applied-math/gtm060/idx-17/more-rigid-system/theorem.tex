A system with the same kinetic energy $T = \frac{1}{2}(A\dot{q}, \dot{q}) > 0$ and a new potential energy $U'$ is called \text{more rigid} if
$$U' = \frac{1}{2}(B'\mathbf{q}, \mathbf{q}) \geq \frac{1}{2}(B\mathbf{q}, \mathbf{q}) = U$$
for all $\mathbf{q}$, where $U = \frac{1}{2}(B\mathbf{q}, \mathbf{q})$ is the potential energy of the original system.
