Given a chart with local coordinates $(q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ on a manifold $M$, the components of a tangent vector represented by a curve $\varphi(t)$ are the time derivatives $\xi_i = d\varphi_i/dt|_{t=0}$ of the coordinate functions evaluated at the point of tangency. These $n$ numbers form the coordinate representation of the tangent vector in the given chart and transform according to the chain rule under changes of coordinates.
