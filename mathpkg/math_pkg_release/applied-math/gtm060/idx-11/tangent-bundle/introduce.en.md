The tangent bundle $TM$ of a manifold $M$ is the disjoint union of all tangent spaces $TM_x$ for $x \in M$, endowed with a natural differentiable manifold structure of dimension $2n$ (where $n = \dim M$). A point in $TM$ is a pair $(x, \xi)$ consisting of a base point $x \in M$ and a tangent vector $\xi \in TM_x$. In local coordinates $(q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ on $M$, the $2n$ numbers $(q_1, \ldots, q_n, \xi_1, \ldots, \xi_n)$ serve as local coordinates on $TM$, where the $\xi_i$ are the components of the tangent vector.
