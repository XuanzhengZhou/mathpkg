Two curves $x = \varphi(t)$ and $x = \psi(t)$ on a manifold $M$ are called equivalent if $\varphi(0) = \psi(0) = x$ and
$$\lim_{t \to 0} \frac{\varphi(t) - \psi(t)}{t} = 0$$
in some chart. Prove that this tangent relationship is true in any chart (i.e., the equivalence is independent of the choice of chart).
