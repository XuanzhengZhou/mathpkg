The differential of a functional is called its variation. The variation $h$ is called a variation of the curve.

For $\Phi(\gamma) = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} L(x, \dot{x}, t)dt$, the variation is
$$F(h) = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} \left[ \frac{\partial L}{\partial x} - \frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{x}} \right] h \, dt + \left( \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{x}} h \right)\Bigg|_{t_0}^{t_1}.$$
