Since $t = t_0$ is no worse than $t = 0$, Lemma 1 can be written in the form

$$\left. \frac{dv(t)}{dt} \right|_{t=t_0} = \int_{D(t_0)} \text{div } \mathbf{f} \, d\mathbf{x}.$$

If $\text{div } \mathbf{f} \equiv 0$, then

$$\frac{dv}{dt} \equiv 0,$$

and consequently $v(t) = v(0)$ for all $t$. Thus the flow preserves volume.
