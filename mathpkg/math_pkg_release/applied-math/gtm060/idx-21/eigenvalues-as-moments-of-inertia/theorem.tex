The eigenvalues $I_i$ of the inertia operator $A$ are the moments of inertia of the body with respect to the principal axes $\mathbf{e}_i$:

$$I_i = I_{\mathbf{e}_i} = \sum_j m_j r_{j,\mathbf{e}_i}^2,$$

where $r_{j,\mathbf{e}_i}$ is the distance of the $j$-th point to the principal axis $\mathbf{e}_i$.
