The quantity $M = r^2 \dot{\varphi}$ is preserved. Consequently, the angular momentum is

$$\mathbf{M} = [\mathbf{r}, \dot{\mathbf{r}}] = [\mathbf{r}, \dot{r} \mathbf{e}_r] + [\mathbf{r}, r \dot{\varphi} \mathbf{e}_\varphi] = r \dot{\varphi}[\mathbf{r}, \mathbf{e}_\varphi] = r^2 \dot{\varphi}[\mathbf{e}_r, \mathbf{e}_\varphi].$$

Thus, the sectorial velocity $C = \frac{1}{2} r^2 \dot{\varphi} = \frac{1}{2} M$ is half the angular momentum.
