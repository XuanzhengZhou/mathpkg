An equilibrium position is a state of the mechanical system where the phase velocity vanishes, so the system remains at rest.
These correspond exactly to critical points of the potential energy $U(x)$ with zero velocity.
Local minima of $U$ are stable equilibrium positions, while local maxima are unstable.
The nature of equilibrium positions can be inferred directly from the graph of the potential energy.
