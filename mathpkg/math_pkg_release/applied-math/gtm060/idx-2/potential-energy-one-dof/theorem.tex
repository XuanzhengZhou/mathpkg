The \textit{potential energy} of a system with one degree of freedom $\ddot{x} = f(x)$ is the function
$$U(x) = -\int_{x_0}^{x} f(\xi)\,d\xi.$$
The sign is chosen so that the potential energy is larger when the particle is higher off the ground.
The potential energy determines the force $f$ via $f(x) = -U'(x)$.
