$$\frac{dE}{dt} = (\dot{x}, \ddot{x}) + \left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial x}, \dot{x}\right) = \left(\ddot{x} + \frac{\partial U}{\partial x}, \dot{x}\right) = 0$$
by the equation of motion $\ddot{x} = -\partial U / \partial x$.
