The period of small oscillations near a stable equilibrium (minimum of potential energy) is $T_0 = 2\pi/\sqrt{U''(\xi)}$.
This recovers the familiar harmonic oscillator period, where the second derivative $U''(\xi)$ plays the role of the spring constant.
The result is obtained as the limiting period when the energy approaches the minimum value $E_0 = U(\xi)$.
