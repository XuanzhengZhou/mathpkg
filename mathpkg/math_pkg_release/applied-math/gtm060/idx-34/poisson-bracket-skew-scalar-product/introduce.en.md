This corollary expresses the Poisson bracket as the evaluation of the symplectic 2-form on the pair of hamiltonian vector fields generated by $H$ and $F$. The skew-symmetry of $\omega^2$ immediately implies the skew-symmetry of the Poisson bracket. This formulation makes manifest the deep relationship between the symplectic structure and the Lie algebra structure on functions.
