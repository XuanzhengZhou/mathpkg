This corollary provides an alternative expression for the Poisson bracket using differential forms. The value of the Poisson bracket $(F, H)$ equals the evaluation of the differential $dF$ on the hamiltonian vector field $I\,dH$ associated to $H$. This formulation highlights the geometric nature of the Poisson bracket as a pairing between exact 1-forms and hamiltonian vector fields via the isomorphism $I$ induced by the symplectic form.
