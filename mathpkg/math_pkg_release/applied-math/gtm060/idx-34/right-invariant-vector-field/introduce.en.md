A right-invariant vector field on a Lie group is obtained by right-translating a tangent vector at the identity throughout the group. The differential of the right translation map pushes vectors forward from the tangent space at the identity to any point of the group. These vector fields are uniquely determined by their value at the identity and provide a concrete realization of the Lie algebra of the group as vector fields.
