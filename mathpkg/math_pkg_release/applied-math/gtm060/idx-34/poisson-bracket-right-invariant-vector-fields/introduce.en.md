This proposition establishes the fundamental connection between two ways of defining the Lie bracket on the tangent space at the identity. The Poisson bracket of right-invariant vector fields remains right-invariant, and its value at the identity coincides with the Lie bracket computed directly from tangent vectors. This shows that the Lie algebra structure on $TG_e$ can be equivalently defined using either one-parameter subgroups or the Poisson bracket of vector fields.
