Show that the angle $\Phi$ between the pericenter and apocenter is equal to the semiperiod of an oscillation in the one-dimensional system with potential energy $W(x) = U(M/x) + (x^2/2)$.

Hint: The substitution $x = M/r$ gives
$$\Phi = \int_{x_{\min}}^{x_{\max}} \frac{dx}{\sqrt{2(E - W)}}.$$
