For which values of $U$ is the magnitude of $\Phi_{\text{cir}}$ independent of the radius $r$?

ANSWER: $U(r) = ar^\alpha$ ($\alpha \geq -2$, $\alpha \neq 0$) and $U(r) = b \log r$.

It follows that $\Phi_{\text{cir}} = \pi/\sqrt{\alpha + 2}$ (the logarithmic case corresponds to $\alpha = 0$). For example, for $\alpha = 2$ we have $\Phi_{\text{cir}} = \pi/2$, and for $\alpha = -1$ we have $\Phi_{\text{cir}} = \pi$.
