Given motion in a central field with angular momentum $M$ and energy $E$, the radial bounds $0 \leq r_{\min} \leq r \leq r_{\max} \leq \infty$ are defined by $V(r) \leq E$.

The points where $r = r_{\min}$ are called pericentral, and where $r = r_{\max}$, apocentral.

If the center is the earth: perigee and apogee; the sun: perihelion and aphelion; the moon: perilune and apolune.
