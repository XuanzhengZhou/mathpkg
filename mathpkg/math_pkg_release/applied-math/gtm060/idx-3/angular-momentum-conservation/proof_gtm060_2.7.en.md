By definition $M = [r, \dot{r}]$. By the lemma, $\dot{M} = [\dot{r}, \dot{r}] + [r, \ddot{r}]$. Since the field is central it is apparent from the equations of motion that the vectors $\ddot{r}$ and $r$ are collinear. Therefore $\dot{M} = 0$.
