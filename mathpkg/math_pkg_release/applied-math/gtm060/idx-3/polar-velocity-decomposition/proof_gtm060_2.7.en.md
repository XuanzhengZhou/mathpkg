Clearly, the vectors $e_r$ and $e_\varphi$ rotate with angular velocity $\dot{\varphi}$, i.e.,

$$\dot{e}_r = \dot{\varphi} e_\varphi, \quad \dot{e}_\varphi = -\dot{\varphi} e_r.$$

Differentiating the equality $r = r e_r$ gives us

$$\dot{r} = \dot{r} e_r + r \dot{e}_r = \dot{r} e_r + r \dot{\varphi} e_\varphi.$$
