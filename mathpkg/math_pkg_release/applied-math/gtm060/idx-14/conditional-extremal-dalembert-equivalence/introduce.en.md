This theorem establishes the equivalence between two fundamental formulations of constrained mechanics: the variational principle of least action restricted to the constraint manifold, and D'Alembert's equation expressing the orthogonality of the "effective force" to the constraint surface. The proof uses the normal field lemma to convert an integral orthogonality condition into a pointwise condition, demonstrating that the two perspectives yield identical equations of motion for holonomic systems.
