A curve $\mathbf{x} : \mathbb{R} \to M \subset \mathbb{R}^N$ on a constrained configuration manifold $M$ is a \textit{conditional extremal} of the action functional

$$\Phi[\mathbf{x}] = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} L(\mathbf{x}, \dot{\mathbf{x}}) \, dt, \quad L = \frac{\dot{\mathbf{x}}^2}{2} - U(\mathbf{x})$$

if for every variation $\delta \mathbf{x}$ tangent to $M$ vanishing at the endpoints,

$$\delta_M \Phi = 0.$$

In local coordinates $\mathbf{q}$ on $M$ with $\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{x}(\mathbf{q})$, this is equivalent to the Lagrange equations

$$\frac{d}{dt} \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{\mathbf{q}}} = \frac{\partial L}{\partial \mathbf{q}}.$$
