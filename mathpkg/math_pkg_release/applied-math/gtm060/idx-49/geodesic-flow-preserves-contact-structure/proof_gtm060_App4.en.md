The proof of this theorem is a reformulation in new terms of Huygens' principle (cf. Section 46). Each contact element is transported along the geodesic to which it is orthogonal; after time $$t$$ a new oriented element is obtained. The geodesic flow of oriented contact elements preserves the contact hyperplane distribution by construction.
