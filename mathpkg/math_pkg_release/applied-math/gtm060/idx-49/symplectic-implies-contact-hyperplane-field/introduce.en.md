If the derivative of the canonical 1-form on the symplectification of a contact manifold is a symplectic form, then the distribution of hyperplanes on the underlying odd-dimensional manifold satisfies the non-degeneracy condition of a contact structure. This proposition connects the symplectic geometry of the symplectification to the contact geometry of the base manifold.
