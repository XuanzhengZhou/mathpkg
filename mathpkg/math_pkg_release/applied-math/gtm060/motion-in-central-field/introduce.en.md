Motion in a central field is governed by $\ddot{\mathbf{r}} = \Phi(r)\mathbf{e}_r$, where the acceleration is purely radial. This includes Kepler's problem (gravitational attraction) and harmonic oscillators. The radial nature of the force leads to conservation of angular momentum, which is the key to solving such problems.
