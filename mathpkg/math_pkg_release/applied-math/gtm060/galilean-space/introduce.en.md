A Galilean space is a four-dimensional affine space $A^4$ equipped with a Galilean space-time structure: a time map and a Euclidean distance on simultaneous events. It provides the geometric arena for Newtonian mechanics, where the notions of simultaneity and spatial distance are absolute but position requires a coordinate system.
