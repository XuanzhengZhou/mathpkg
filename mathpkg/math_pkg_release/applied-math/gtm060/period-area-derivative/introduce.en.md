For a one-degree-of-freedom system with closed phase curves, the period $T$ of periodic motion relates to the area $S(E)$ enclosed by the phase curve via $T = dS/dE$. This elegant formula connects the geometry of phase curves (area) to the dynamics (period) and is a precursor to action-angle variables.
