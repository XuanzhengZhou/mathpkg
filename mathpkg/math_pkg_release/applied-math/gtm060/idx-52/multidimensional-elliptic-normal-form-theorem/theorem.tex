If the eigenvalues $\lambda_l$ of a multi-dimensional elliptic canonical transformation do not admit resonances
$$\lambda_1^{k_1} \cdots \lambda_n^{k_n} = 1$$
for any integers $k_1,\ldots,k_n$ with $|k_1| + \cdots + |k_n| \leq s$, then the transformation can be reduced by a canonical change of variables to a Birkhoff normal form of degree $s$ with error terms of degree $s+1$ and higher.
