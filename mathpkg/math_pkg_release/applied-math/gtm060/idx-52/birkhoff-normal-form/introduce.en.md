The Birkhoff normal form is a simplified polynomial Hamiltonian obtained by successive canonical coordinate changes near an equilibrium position. In the non-resonant case, the normal form depends only on the action variables $\tau_k = (P_k^2 + Q_k^2)/2$, making the truncated system integrable. The degree of the normal form determines how many terms of the Taylor expansion are preserved; terms of higher degree are gathered into a remainder $R$.
