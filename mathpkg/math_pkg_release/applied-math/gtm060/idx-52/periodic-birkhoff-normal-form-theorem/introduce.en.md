This theorem extends the Birkhoff normal form to Hamiltonian systems with time-periodic coefficients. Under the same non-resonance condition as in the autonomous case, there exists a $2\pi$-periodic time-dependent canonical transformation that brings the system to the autonomous Birkhoff normal form up to degree $s$. The essential difference is only that the higher-order remainder $R$ depends periodically on time. This result is crucial for studying the behavior near closed trajectories of autonomous systems via isoenergetic reduction.
