This fundamental relation connects the vorticity $r$ of a two-dimensional incompressible velocity field to the Laplacian of its stream function $\psi$. Since $v = I \operatorname{grad} \psi$ (where $I$ is $90^\circ$ rotation), the scalar curl $r = \operatorname{curl} v$ simplifies to $r = -\Delta \psi$. This equation provides a simple way to compute the vorticity directly from the stream function and highlights the elliptic nature of vorticity in two-dimensional ideal fluids.
