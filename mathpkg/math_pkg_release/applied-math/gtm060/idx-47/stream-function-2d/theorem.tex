Let $D$ be a two-dimensional oriented Riemannian manifold with metric and orientation giving a symplectic structure on $D$. A divergence-free vector field of velocities $v$ is Hamiltonian and is therefore given by a (possibly multi-valued if $D$ is not simply connected) Hamiltonian function $\psi$, called the **stream function**:
$$v = I \operatorname{grad} \psi,$$
where $I$ is the operator of clockwise rotation by $90^\circ$.
