Generalized coordinates $q = (q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ are any coordinates on the configuration space of a mechanical system. The derivatives $\dot{q}_i$ are called generalized velocities.

The generalized momenta are $p_i = \partial L/\partial \dot{q}_i$. Lagrange's equations can be written as $\dot{p}_i = \partial L/\partial q_i$.
