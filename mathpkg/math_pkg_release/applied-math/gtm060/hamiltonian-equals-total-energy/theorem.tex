\textbf{Theorem.} For a mechanical system with lagrangian $L = T - U$, where $T = \frac{1}{2}\sum a_{ij}\dot{q}_i\dot{q}_j$ is a quadratic form, the hamiltonian $H$ is the total energy $H = T + U$.

\textbf{Lemma.} The values of a quadratic form $f(x)$ and of its Legendre transform $g(p)$ coincide at corresponding points: $f(x) = g(p)$.
