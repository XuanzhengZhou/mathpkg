An equivalent characterization of conservative fields: the circulation around any closed loop must vanish. If $\oint (\mathbf{F}, d\mathbf{S}) = 0$ for every closed contour, then the field admits a single-valued potential. This formulation directly connects to the topological condition $\nabla \times \mathbf{F} = 0$ in simply connected domains.
