For a conservative system of $n$ particles interacting via pairwise central forces, the total potential energy is the sum over all pairs of two-body interaction potentials $U_{ij}$, plus any external potentials $U'_i$. The total mechanical energy $E = T + U$ is then a constant of motion. This general formulation encompasses gravitational systems, Coulomb interactions, and molecular dynamics.
