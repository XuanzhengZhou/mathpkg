The velocity vector $\dot{\mathbf{x}}(t_0)$ is the first derivative of the position $\mathbf{x}(t)$ with respect to time. It measures the instantaneous rate and direction of change of position. In the configuration space $\mathbb{R}^N$, the velocity vector belongs to the same vector space.
