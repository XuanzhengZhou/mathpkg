Let $\Phi_{q_0}(t)$ be the wave front of the point $q_0$ after time $t$. For every point $q$ of this front, consider the wave front after time $s$, $\Phi_q(s)$. Then the wave front of the point $q_0$ after time $s + t$, $\Phi_{q_0}(s + t)$, will be the envelope of the fronts $\Phi_q(s)$, $q \in \Phi_{q_0}(t)$.
