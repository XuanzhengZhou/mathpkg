Let $g: M \to M$ be a differentiable map. Show that a differential $k$-form $\omega^k$ is an integral invariant of $g$ (i.e., $\int_{gc} \omega^k = \int_c \omega^k$ for every $k$-chain $c$) if and only if $g^*\omega^k = \omega^k$.
