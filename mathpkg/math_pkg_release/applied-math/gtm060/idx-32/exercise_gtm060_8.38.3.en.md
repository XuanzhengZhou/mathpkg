Show that the correspondence $\xi \mapsto \omega^1_\xi$, defined by $\omega^1_\xi(\eta) = \omega^2(\eta, \xi)$ for all $\eta \in TM_x$, is an isomorphism between the $2n$-dimensional vector spaces of vectors and of $1$-forms on a symplectic manifold $(M^{2n}, \omega^2)$.
