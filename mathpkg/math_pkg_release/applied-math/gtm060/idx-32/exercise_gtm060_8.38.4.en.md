In $\mathbb{R}^{2n} = \{(p, q)\}$, identify vectors and $1$-forms by using the Euclidean structure $(x, x) = p^2 + q^2$. Then the correspondence $\xi \mapsto \omega^1_\xi$ determines a transformation $\mathbb{R}^{2n} \to \mathbb{R}^{2n}$. Calculate the matrix of this transformation in the basis $p, q$.
