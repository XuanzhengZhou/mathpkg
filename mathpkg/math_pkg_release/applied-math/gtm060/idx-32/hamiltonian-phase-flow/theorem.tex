Let $(M^{2n}, \omega^2)$ be a symplectic manifold and $H: M^{2n} \to \mathbb{R}$ a function. Assume that the Hamiltonian vector field $I\,dH$ corresponding to $H$ generates a $1$-parameter group of diffeomorphisms $g^t: M^{2n} \to M^{2n}$ satisfying
$$
\left. \frac{d}{dt} \right|_{t=0} g^t x = I\,dH(x).
$$
The group $g^t$ is called the \textbf{Hamiltonian phase flow} with Hamiltonian function $H$.
