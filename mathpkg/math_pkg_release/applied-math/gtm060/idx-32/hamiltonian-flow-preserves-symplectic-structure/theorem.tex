Let $(M^{2n}, \omega^2)$ be a symplectic manifold, $H: M^{2n} \to \mathbb{R}$ a function, and $g^t: M^{2n} \to M^{2n}$ the Hamiltonian phase flow with Hamiltonian function $H$. Then
$$
(g^t)^* \omega^2 = \omega^2.
$$
In the case $n = 1$, $M^{2n} = \mathbb{R}^2$, this theorem says that the phase flow $g^t$ preserves area (Liouville's theorem).
