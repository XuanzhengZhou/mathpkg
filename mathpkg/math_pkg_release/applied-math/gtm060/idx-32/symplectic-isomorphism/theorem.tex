To each vector $\xi$, tangent to a symplectic manifold $(M^{2n}, \omega^2)$ at the point $x$, associate a $1$-form $\omega^1_\xi$ on $TM_x$ by the formula
$$
\omega^1_\xi(\eta) = \omega^2(\eta, \xi) \quad \forall \eta \in TM_x.
$$
The correspondence $\xi \mapsto \omega^1_\xi$ is an isomorphism between the $2n$-dimensional vector spaces $TM_x$ and $T^*M_x$. This isomorphism is denoted by $I: T^*M_x \to TM_x$.
