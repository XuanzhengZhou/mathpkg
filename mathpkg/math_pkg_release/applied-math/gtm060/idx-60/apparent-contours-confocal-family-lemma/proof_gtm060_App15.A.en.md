On passage to the dual, sections become projections and vice versa. The apparent contours of the projections of confocal quadrics along a pencil of parallel lines are therefore dual to the sections of the dual quadrics by a hyperplane passing through the origin.

The sections of the quadrics in a euclidean pencil by a hyperplane through $0$ form a euclidean pencil of quadrics in the hyperplane. The lemma now follows by duality.
