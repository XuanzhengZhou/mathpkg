Lemma B concerns the Poisson bracket of functions induced on the space of lines. Given a smooth function on euclidean space, each nearby line has a point where it is tangent to a level surface, giving an induced function on the line space. When two point functions have orthogonal tangent planes at the tangency points of a given line, their induced line functions Poisson-commute.
