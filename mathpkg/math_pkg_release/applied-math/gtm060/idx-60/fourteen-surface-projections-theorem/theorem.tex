Every projection from a point outside a generic surface in $\mathbb{R}P^3$ is locally equivalent at each point of the surface to the projection along lines parallel to the $x$-axis of a surface $z = f(x, y)$, where $f$ is one of the following $14$ functions:
$$x,\quad x^2,\quad x^3 + xy,\quad x^3 \pm xy^2,\quad x^3 + xy^3,\quad x^4 + xy,$$
$$x^4 + x^2y + xy^2,\quad x^5 \pm x^3y + xy,\quad x^3 \pm xy^4,\quad x^4 + x^2y + xy^3,\quad x^5 + xy.$$
