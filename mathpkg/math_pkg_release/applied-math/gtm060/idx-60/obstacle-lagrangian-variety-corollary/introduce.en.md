This corollary applies the swallowtail normal form theorem to the obstacle problem. The Lagrangian variety formed by rays tangent to geodesics on the obstacle surface generically has a semicubic cuspidal edge along asymptotic rays and an open swallowtail singularity at biasymptotic rays. Unlike ordinary variational problems where the Lagrangian variety is smooth, the obstacle problem introduces intrinsic singularities.
