This theorem establishes that the geodesic flow on a central quadric in euclidean space is completely integrable in the Liouville sense. The proof constructs $n-1$ independent integrals in involution using the induced line functions from confocal quadrics. Lemma B guarantees the Poisson commutativity, while Lemma A identifies the phase curves as tangent lines to geodesics.
