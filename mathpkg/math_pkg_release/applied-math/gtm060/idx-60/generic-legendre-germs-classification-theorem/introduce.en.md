This 1973 theorem classifies generic Legendre mapping germs (wave front singularities) in dimensions up to 5 by the same A, D, E reflection groups. Legendre mappings describe projections of Legendre submanifolds in contact geometry, whose images are wave fronts. The fronts are locally diffeomorphic to the manifolds of non-regular orbits of the reflection groups.
