Givental's stabilization theorem (1981) describes the sequence of varieties of monic polynomials having a root of fixed comultiplicity $k$. As the polynomial degree $n$ increases, these varieties stabilize beginning at $n = 2k+1$, the point where self-intersections are eliminated. The first stable member (for $k=1$) is the open swallowtail over the ordinary swallowtail.
