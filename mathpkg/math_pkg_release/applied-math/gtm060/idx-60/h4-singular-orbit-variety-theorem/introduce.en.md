This theorem provides a geometric description of the $H_4$ singular orbit variety. Among the $\lambda$-parameters for which the curve $x^5 + y^3 + \lambda_1 x^3 y + \lambda_2 x^3 + \lambda_3 y + \lambda_4 = 0$ is singular, one irreducible component of the resulting three-dimensional hypersurface is diffeomorphic to the $H_4$ singular orbit variety. This relates the $H_4$ discriminant to a concrete algebraic curve singularity.
