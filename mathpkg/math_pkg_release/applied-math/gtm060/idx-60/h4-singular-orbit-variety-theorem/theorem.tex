Consider those values of $\lambda$ for which the curve $x^5 + y^3 + \lambda_1 x^3 y + \lambda_2 x^3 + \lambda_3 y + \lambda_4 = 0$ is singular. One of the irreducible components of this three-dimensional hypersurface in $\lambda$-space is diffeomorphic to the variety of singular orbits of the group $H_4$.
