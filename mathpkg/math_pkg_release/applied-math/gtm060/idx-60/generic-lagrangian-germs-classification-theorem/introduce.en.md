This 1972 theorem classifies all generic Lagrangian mapping germs in dimensions up to 5. The classification is finite and involves the reflection groups of types $A_k$, $D_k$, $E_6$, $E_7$, $E_8$. The caustics (sets of critical values) of Lagrangian mappings are locally diffeomorphic to the discriminants of the corresponding reflection groups. This unified the theory of singularities of ray systems with the theory of Weyl groups.
