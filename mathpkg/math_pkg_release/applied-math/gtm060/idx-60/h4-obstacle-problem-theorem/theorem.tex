The graph of the time function in the problem of bypassing an obstacle bounded by a smooth surface in three-dimensional euclidean space, for a pencil of geodesics beginning at a fixed point, has a singularity diffeomorphic to the variety of singular orbits of the reflection group $H_4$ (symmetries of the regular 120-vertex polyhedron in $\mathbb{R}^4$) at a point of tangency of an asymptotic ray with the bounding surface.
