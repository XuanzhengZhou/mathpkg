This 1981 theorem provides a normal form for symplectic structures near a point in the product of a swallowtail and a linear space. All generic symplectic structures in such a neighborhood are formally equivalent by diffeomorphisms preserving the product structure. This result is key to analyzing the Lagrangian variety of tangent rays near biasymptotic directions in the obstacle problem.
