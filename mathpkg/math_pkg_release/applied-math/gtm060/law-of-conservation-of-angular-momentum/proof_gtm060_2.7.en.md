By definition $M = [r, \dot{r}]$. Using the lemma for the derivative of a vector product:
$$\dot{M} = [\dot{r}, \dot{r}] + [r, \ddot{r}].$$
The first term $[\dot{r}, \dot{r}] = 0$ since the cross product of a vector with itself is zero. Since the field is central, the vectors $\ddot{r}$ and $r$ are collinear, so $[r, \ddot{r}] = 0$. Therefore $\dot{M} = 0$.
