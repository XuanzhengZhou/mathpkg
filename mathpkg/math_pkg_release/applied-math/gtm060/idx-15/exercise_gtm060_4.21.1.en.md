A rod of weight \(P\), tilted at an angle of \(60^\circ\) to the plane of a table, begins to fall with initial velocity zero (Figure 74). Find the constraint force of the table at the initial moment, considering the table as

(a) absolutely smooth, and
(b) absolutely rough.

In the first case, the holonomic constraint holds the end of the rod on the plane of the table, and in the second case, at a given point.
