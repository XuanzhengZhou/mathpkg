The derivative of the cross product of two time-dependent vectors satisfies the Leibniz (product) rule: $d[a,b]/dt = [\dot{a}, b] + [a, \dot{b}]$. This elementary vector calculus identity is the key technical step in proving the conservation of angular momentum for central force motion.
