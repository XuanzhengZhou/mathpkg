A conservative system is a mechanical system whose equations of motion derive from a potential energy function $U$. Newton's equations take the form $m_i \ddot{\mathbf{x}}_i = -\partial U/\partial \mathbf{x}_i$, where the force is the negative gradient of the potential. This structure underlies most of classical mechanics and leads to energy conservation.
