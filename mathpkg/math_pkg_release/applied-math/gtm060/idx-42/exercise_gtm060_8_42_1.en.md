**Problem.** Show that this system of $2^n$ types of generating functions is minimal: given any one of the $2^n$ determinants

$$\det \frac{\partial(P_{i_1}, \ldots, P_{i_k}, Q_{j_1}, \ldots, Q_{j_{n-k}})}{\partial(p_{i_1}, \ldots, p_{i_k}, q_{j_1}, \ldots, q_{j_{n-k}})},$$

there exists a canonical transformation for which only this determinant is nonzero.
