Let $\mathbf{X}$ be a vector field on a manifold $M$ and $\omega$ a differential $k$-form. Define the differential $(k-1)$-form $i_{\mathbf{X}}\omega$ (the *interior derivative* or *contraction* of $\omega$ by $\mathbf{X}$) by the relation:
$$(i_{\mathbf{X}}\omega)(\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_{k-1}) = \omega(\mathbf{X}, \xi_1, \ldots, \xi_{k-1}).$$
Investigate the properties of this operation, including its relation to the exterior derivative and Lie derivative.
