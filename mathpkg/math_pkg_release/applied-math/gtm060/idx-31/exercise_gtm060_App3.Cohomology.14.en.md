Show that for the circle $S^1$, the first cohomology group is $H^1(S^1, \mathbb{R}) = \mathbb{R}$.

*Hint.* A closed 1-form on $S^1$ is locally exact, but not necessarily globally. Consider the angle form $d\theta$, which is closed but not exact on $S^1$ since its integral around the circle equals $2\pi \neq 0$.
