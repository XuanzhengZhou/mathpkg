Show that the boundary of the boundary of any chain is zero: $\partial \partial c_k = 0$.

Hint. By the linearity of $\partial$ it is enough to show that $\partial \partial D = 0$ for a convex polyhedron $D$. It remains to verify that every $(k-2)$-dimensional face of $D$ appears in $\partial \partial D$ twice, with opposite signs.
