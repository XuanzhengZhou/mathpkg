Let $x_1, \ldots, x_n: M \to \mathbb{R}$ be functions on a manifold which form a local coordinate system on some region. Show that every differential form on this region can be written uniquely in the form
$$\omega^k = \sum_{i_1 < \cdots < i_k} a_{i_1, \ldots, i_k}(x) dx_{i_1} \wedge \cdots \wedge dx_{i_k}.$$
