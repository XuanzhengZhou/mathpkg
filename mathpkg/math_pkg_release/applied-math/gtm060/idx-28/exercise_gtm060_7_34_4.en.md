Calculate the value of the forms $\omega_1 = dx_1$, $\omega_2 = x_1 dx_2$, and $\omega_3 = dr^2$ ($r^2 = x_1^2 + x_2^2$) on the vectors $\xi_1, \xi_2$, and $\xi_3$ (Figure 142).

Answer.
$$\begin{array}{c|c|c}
\xi_1 & \xi_2 & \xi_3 \\
\omega_1 & 0 & -1 & 1 \\
\omega_2 & 0 & -2 & -2 \\
\omega_3 & 0 & -8 & 0
\end{array}$$
