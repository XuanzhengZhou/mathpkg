For a Lagrange top whose axis of symmetry is inclined at an angle $\theta_0$ to the vertical and is stably stationary, the frequency $\omega_{\text{nut}}$ of small nutational oscillations (small deviations of $\theta$ from $\theta_0$) is given by

$$\omega_{\text{nut}} = \frac{I_3 \omega_3}{I_1},$$

where $I_1 = I_2$ are the principal moments of inertia with respect to axes orthogonal to the symmetry axis, $I_3$ is the moment of inertia about the symmetry axis, and $\omega_3$ is the angular velocity of rotation about the symmetry axis.
