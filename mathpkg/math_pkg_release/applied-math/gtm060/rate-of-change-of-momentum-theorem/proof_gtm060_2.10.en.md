$$\frac{dP}{dt} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} m_i \ddot{r}_i = \sum_{i=1}^{n} F_i = \sum_{i,j} F_{ij} + \sum_i F'_i = \sum_i F'_i,$$
since $\sum_{i,j} F_{ij} = 0$ because for forces of interaction $F_{ij} = -F_{ji}$.
