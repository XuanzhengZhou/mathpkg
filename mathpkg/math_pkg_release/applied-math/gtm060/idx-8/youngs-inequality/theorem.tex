Let $f$ and $g$ be Legendre transforms of one another (dual in the sense of Young). Then for all $x$ and $p$,

$$px \leq f(x) + g(p).$$

In particular, if $f(x) = x^\alpha/\alpha$ and $g(p) = p^\beta/\beta$ with $(1/\alpha) + (1/\beta) = 1$, $\alpha > 1$, $\beta > 1$, then for all $x > 0, p > 0$,

$$px \leq \frac{x^\alpha}{\alpha} + \frac{p^\beta}{\beta}.$$
