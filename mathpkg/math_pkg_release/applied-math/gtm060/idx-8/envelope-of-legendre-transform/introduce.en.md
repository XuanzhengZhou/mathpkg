This corollary provides a geometric interpretation of the Legendre transformation in terms of envelopes of families of lines. Given a function $g(p)$, the family of lines $y = px - g(p)$ (parametrized by slope $p$) has as its envelope the graph of the Legendre transform $f$ of $g$. This reveals the deep connection between convex duality and the classical theory of envelopes of curves.
