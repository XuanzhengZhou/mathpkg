Mass is defined operationally through the ratio of accelerations: when two bodies are subjected to the same force, the inverse ratio of their accelerations defines the mass ratio $m_2/m_1 = \ddot{x}_1/\ddot{x}_2$. Masses are positive quantities, and a fixed reference mass (e.g., one liter of water) serves as the unit.
