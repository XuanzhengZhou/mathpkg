The configuration space of a system of $n$ point particles is $\mathbb{R}^{3n}$, the direct product of $n$ copies of physical space $\mathbb{R}^3$. A point in configuration space specifies the positions of all particles simultaneously. The motion of the entire system is a single curve in this $3n$-dimensional space.
