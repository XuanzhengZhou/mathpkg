The action is defined as the time integral of the lagrangian: \(\int_{t_0}^{t_1} L(\mathbf{q}, \dot{\mathbf{q}}, t)\, dt\). According to Hamilton's principle, physical trajectories are extremals (usually minima) of the action functional. The action has dimensions of energy $\times$ time.
