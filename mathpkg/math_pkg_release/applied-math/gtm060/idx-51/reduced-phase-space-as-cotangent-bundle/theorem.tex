Let $V$ be a smooth manifold on which a Lie group $G$ acts, and let $M = T^*V$ be its cotangent bundle with the usual symplectic structure. Let $W = V/G$ be the quotient of the configuration space by identifying the points of each orbit. Then the reduced phase space $F_p$ is symplectic and diffeomorphic to the cotangent bundle $T^*W$ of the quotient configuration manifold $W$.

For $p = 0$, the diffeomorphism $T^*W \to F_0$ is constructed as follows: if $\pi: V \to W$ is the factorization map and $\omega \in T^*W$ is a 1-form at $w = \pi(v)$, then the pullback $\pi^*\omega$ on $V$ at $v$ belongs to the zero momentum level set $M_0$ and projects to a point in $F_0$.

The case $p \neq 0$ is reduced to the case $p = 0$ using a $G$-invariant riemannian metric on $V$.
