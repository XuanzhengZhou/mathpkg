This corollary also follows from the fact that a continuous function on a sphere always has at least two critical points. After symplectic reduction, the reduced Hamiltonian for an axially symmetric rigid body fixed at a point on its symmetry axis is a function on the sphere $S^2$. The two stationary rotations correspond to the maximum and minimum of this function, and these exist for every value of the angular momentum.
