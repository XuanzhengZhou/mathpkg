**Proof.** Differentiating the momentum:
$$\frac{d\mathbf{P}}{dt} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} m_i \ddot{\mathbf{r}}_i = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \mathbf{F}_i = \sum_{i,j} \mathbf{F}_{ij} + \sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i = \sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i,$$
since the sum over all internal forces vanishes: $\sum_{i,j} \mathbf{F}_{ij} = 0$ because for forces of interaction $\mathbf{F}_{ij} = -\mathbf{F}_{ji}$.
