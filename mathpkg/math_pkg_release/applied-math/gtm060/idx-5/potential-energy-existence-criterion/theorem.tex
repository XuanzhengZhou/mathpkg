For a system to be conservative it is necessary and sufficient that there exist a potential energy, i.e., a function $U(\mathbf{r})$ such that
$$\mathbf{F} = -\frac{\partial U}{\partial \mathbf{r}},$$
where $\mathbf{r} = (\mathbf{r}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{r}_n)$ is the radius vector in configuration space and $\mathbf{F} = (\mathbf{F}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{F}_n)$ is the force vector.
