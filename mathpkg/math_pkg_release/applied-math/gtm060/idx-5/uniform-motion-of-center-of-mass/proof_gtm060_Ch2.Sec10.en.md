**Proof.** From the theorem on the motion of the center of mass, $(\sum m_i) \ddot{\mathbf{r}} = \sum_i \mathbf{F}'_i$. For a closed system, $\mathbf{F}'_i = 0$, hence $\ddot{\mathbf{r}} = 0$, which means $\dot{\mathbf{r}} = \text{const}$. Therefore the center of mass moves uniformly and linearly.
