A system is called \textit{conservative} if the forces depend only on the location of points in the system ($\mathbf{F} = \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{r})$) and if the work of $\mathbf{F}$ along any path depends only on the initial and final points of the path:
$$\int_{M_1}^{M_2} (\mathbf{F}, d\mathbf{r}) = \Phi(M_1, M_2).$$
