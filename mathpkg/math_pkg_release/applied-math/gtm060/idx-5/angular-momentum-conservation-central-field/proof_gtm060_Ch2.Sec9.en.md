**Proof.** In a central field, the force $\mathbf{F} = -\partial U / \partial \mathbf{r}$ is parallel to $\mathbf{r}$. Then
$$\frac{d\mathbf{M}}{dt} = \frac{d}{dt}[\mathbf{r}, \dot{\mathbf{r}}] = [\dot{\mathbf{r}}, \dot{\mathbf{r}}] + [\mathbf{r}, \ddot{\mathbf{r}}] = 0 + [\mathbf{r}, \mathbf{F}] = 0,$$
since $\mathbf{F} \parallel \mathbf{r}$ implies $[\mathbf{r}, \mathbf{F}] = 0$. Therefore $\mathbf{M}$ is constant.
