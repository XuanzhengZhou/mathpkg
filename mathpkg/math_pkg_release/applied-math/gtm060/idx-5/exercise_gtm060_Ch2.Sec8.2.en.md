**PROBLEM.** The first cosmic velocity is the velocity of motion on a circular orbit of radius close to the radius of the earth. Find the magnitude of the first cosmic velocity $v_1$ and show that $v_2 = \sqrt{2} v_1$ (cf. Section 3B).

**ANSWER.** 8.1 km/sec.
