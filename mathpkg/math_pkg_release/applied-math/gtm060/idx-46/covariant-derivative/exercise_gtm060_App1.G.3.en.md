Show that the curvature tensor satisfies the following identities:

$$\Omega(\xi, \eta)\zeta + \Omega(\eta, \zeta)\xi + \Omega(\zeta, \xi)\eta = 0$$

$$\langle \Omega(\xi, \eta)\zeta, \omega \rangle = \langle \Omega(\zeta, \omega)\xi, \eta \rangle.$$
