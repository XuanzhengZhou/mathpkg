Let $G$ be a Lie group with left-invariant metric given by scalar product $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle$ on $\mathfrak{g}$. The curvature of $G$ in the direction determined by an orthonormal pair $\xi, \eta \in \mathfrak{g}$ is

$$K_{\xi, \eta} = \langle \delta, \delta \rangle + 2\langle \alpha, \beta \rangle - 3\langle \alpha, \alpha \rangle - 4\langle B_\xi, B_\eta \rangle,$$

where

$$2\delta = B(\xi, \eta) + B(\eta, \xi), \quad 2\beta = B(\xi, \eta) - B(\eta, \xi), \quad 2\alpha = [\xi, \eta], \quad 2B_\xi = B(\xi, \xi), \quad 2B_\eta = B(\eta, \eta).$$
