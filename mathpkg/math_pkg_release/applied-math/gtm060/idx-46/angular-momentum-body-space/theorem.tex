Let $M \in T^*G_g$ be the momentum (cotangent vector) at $g \in G$. Translate it to the dual of the Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}^*$ by left or right translation:

$$M_c = L_g^* M \in \mathfrak{g}^*, \quad M_s = R_g^* M \in \mathfrak{g}^*.$$

$M_c$ is the \textbf{angular momentum relative to the body} and $M_s$ the \textbf{angular momentum relative to space}. The kinetic energy is

$$T = \frac{1}{2}(M_c, \omega_c) = \frac{1}{2}(M_s, \omega_s).$$
