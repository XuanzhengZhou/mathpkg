The angular momentum in the body $M_c$ and in space $M_s$ are the dual counterparts of the angular velocities. For the ordinary rigid body, $M_c$ is the angular momentum vector as seen in the body-fixed frame, and $M_s$ is the angular momentum in the space-fixed frame. The relationship $M = A\omega$ via the inertia operator connects momentum and velocity.
