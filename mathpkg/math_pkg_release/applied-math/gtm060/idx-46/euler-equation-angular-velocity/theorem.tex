The motion of the vector of angular velocity in the body $\omega_c$ is determined by its initial value and does not depend on the initial position of the body. It satisfies an equation with quadratic right-hand side:

$$\dot{\omega}_c = B(\omega_c, \omega_c),$$

where $B: \mathfrak{g} \times \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}$ is the bilinear operation associated to the left-invariant metric.
