The inertia operator $A: \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}^*$ encodes the mass distribution of a generalized rigid body with configuration space $G$. It maps angular velocities to angular momenta: $M = A\omega$. For the ordinary rigid body ($G = SO(3)$), $A$ is the usual moment of inertia tensor.
