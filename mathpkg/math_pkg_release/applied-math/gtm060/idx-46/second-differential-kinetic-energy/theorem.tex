The second differential of the kinetic energy $H$, restricted to the image of a coadjoint orbit in the algebra $\mathfrak{g}$, is given at a critical point $\omega \in \mathfrak{g}$ by

$$2\,d^2 H|_{\omega}(\xi) = \langle B(\omega, f), B(\omega, f) \rangle + \langle [f, \omega], B(\omega, f) \rangle,$$

where $\xi$ is a tangent vector to this image, expressed in terms of $f$ by $\xi = B(\omega, f)$ with $f \in \mathfrak{g}$.
