The proof is a straightforward computation using the definitions of the Kirillov symplectic form, the operation $B$, and the covariant derivative. Arnold notes that this is obtained by differentiating the Hamiltonian vector field condition and evaluating at the critical point. The expression involves the Lie bracket $[f, \omega]$ and the bilinear form $B$ encoding the metric.
