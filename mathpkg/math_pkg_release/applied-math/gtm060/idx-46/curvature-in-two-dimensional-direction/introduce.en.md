The curvature of a Riemannian manifold $M$ in the direction of a 2-plane $L \subset TM_x$ is defined as the Riemannian (Gaussian) curvature at $x$ of the smooth surface formed by geodesics emanating from $x$ in all directions lying in $L$. This gives a way to measure how the manifold bends in each two-dimensional slice. In general, the curvature depends on the direction $L$.
