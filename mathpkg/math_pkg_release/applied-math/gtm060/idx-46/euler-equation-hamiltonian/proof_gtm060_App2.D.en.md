Every vector $\xi$ tangent to $V$ at $M_c$ has the form $\xi = \{f, M_c\} = ad^*_f M_c$ for some $f \in \mathfrak{g}$. The Euler vector field is $X = \{dT, M_c\}$ (identifying $dT \in \mathfrak{g}^{**} \cong \mathfrak{g}$). Let $\Omega$ be the Kirillov symplectic form. Then for any tangent vector $\xi = \{f, M_c\}$:

$$\Omega(\xi, X) = (M_c, [f, dT]) = (dT, \{f, M_c\}) = (dH, \xi).$$

Thus $X$ is the Hamiltonian vector field of $H$ with respect to $\Omega$.
