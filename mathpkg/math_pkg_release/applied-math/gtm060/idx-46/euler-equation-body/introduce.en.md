This is the generalization of Euler's equations for a rigid body to an arbitrary Lie group. The right-hand side is quadratic in $M_c$ (since $\omega_c = A^{-1}M_c$). The equation shows that the motion of $M_c$ depends only on $M_c$ itself, not on the spatial orientation $g \in G$. This factorization means the phase flow on $T^*G$ (dimension $2n$) projects to a flow on $\mathfrak{g}^*$ (dimension $n$) given by Euler's equation.
