The apsidal angle $\Phi$ measures the angular advance between successive pericenters (or apocenters). The orbit closes if and only if $\Phi/2\pi$ is rational; otherwise the orbit densely fills an annulus. Computing $\Phi$ is key to understanding precession and the classification of central potentials.
