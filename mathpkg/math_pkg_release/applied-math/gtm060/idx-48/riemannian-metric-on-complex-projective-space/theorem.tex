Let $p: \mathbb{C}^{n+1} \setminus \{0\} \to \mathbb{C}P^n$ be the natural projection taking a nonzero vector $z$ to the complex line through $0$ and $z$. Every tangent vector $\zeta \in T_{pz}\mathbb{C}P^n$ can be represented as $\zeta = p_* \xi$ for some $\xi \in T\mathbb{C}_z^{n+1}$. The square of the length of $\zeta$ in the Riemannian metric is given by

$$ds^2(\zeta) = \frac{\langle \xi, \xi \rangle \langle z, z \rangle - \langle \xi, z \rangle \langle z, \xi \rangle}{\langle z, z \rangle^2}.$$
