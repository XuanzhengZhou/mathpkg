The symplectification of a contact manifold is the set of all contact forms on the manifold, endowed with a natural symplectic structure. This construction produces an even-dimensional symplectic manifold of dimension $N+1$ from an odd-dimensional contact manifold of dimension $N$, providing the bridge between contact and symplectic geometry.
