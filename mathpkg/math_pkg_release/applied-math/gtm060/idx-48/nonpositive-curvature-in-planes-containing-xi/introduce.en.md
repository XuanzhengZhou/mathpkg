Theorem 15 states that the sectional curvature of the group $S_0 \text{Diff} T^2$ in any two-dimensional plane containing a fixed direction $\xi$ is non-positive. The result provides an explicit formula for the curvature operator $\langle \Omega(\xi, \eta) \rangle$ as a manifestly non-positive sum of squares, implying that geodesic flows on the diffeomorphism group are predominantly unstable in the sense of exponential divergence, with zero curvature occurring only for parallel flows where $[\xi, \eta] = 0$.
