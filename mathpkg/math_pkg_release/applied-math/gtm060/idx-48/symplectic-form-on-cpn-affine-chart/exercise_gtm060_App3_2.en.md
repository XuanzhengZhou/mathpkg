Show that the symplectic structure $\Omega$ in the affine chart $w_k = z_k z_0^{-1}$ ($k = 1, \ldots, n$) of the projective space $\mathbb{C}P^n = \{(z_0 : z_1 : \ldots : z_n)\}$ is given by

$$\Omega = \frac{i}{2\pi} \frac{\sum_{0 \leq k < l \leq n} (w_k dw_l - w_l dw_k) (\bar{w}_k d\bar{w}_l - \bar{w}_l d\bar{w}_k)}{\left(\sum_{k=0}^{n} |w_k|^2\right)^2},$$

with the convention $w_0 = 1$.
