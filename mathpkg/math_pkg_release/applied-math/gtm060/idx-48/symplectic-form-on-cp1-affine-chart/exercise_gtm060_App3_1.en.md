Calculate the symplectic structure $\Omega$ in the affine chart $w = z_1 : z_0$ of the projective line $\mathbb{C}P^1$.

$$\Omega = \frac{1}{\pi} \frac{dx \wedge dy}{(1 + x^2 + y^2)^2}, \quad \text{where } w = x + iy.$$
