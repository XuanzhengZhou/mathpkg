A field of hyperplanes is said to be \textit{nondegenerate at a point} if the rank of the $2$-form $d\omega|_{\omega=0}$ in the plane of the field passing through this point is equal to the dimension of the plane. Equivalently, for any nonzero vector in the plane, there exists another vector in the plane such that the value of the $2$-form on this pair is nonzero.
