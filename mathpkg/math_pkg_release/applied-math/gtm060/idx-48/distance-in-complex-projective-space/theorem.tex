The distance between two points of complex projective space $\mathbb{C}P^n$ is the distance between the two corresponding circles on the unit sphere $S^{2n+1} \subset \mathbb{C}^{n+1}$, where a complex line (point in $\mathbb{C}P^n$) corresponds to the intersection of that line with the unit sphere, which is a great circle $S^1$.
