Theorem 14 provides explicit coordinate formulas for the geometric structure of the Lie group $S_0 \text{Diff} T^2$, the group of area-preserving diffeomorphisms of the two-dimensional torus that fix the center of mass. The formulas express the scalar product, commutator, operation $B$, connection, and Riemann curvature tensor of the right-invariant metric in terms of the Fourier basis $\{e_k\}$ of the Lie algebra of stream functions. These formulas allow the curvature to be computed in any two-dimensional direction, showing that curvature is mostly negative but positive in a few special directions.
