For a 2-DOF conservative system, the energy conservation $E = \text{const}$ defines a 3-dimensional hypersurface in the 4-dimensional phase space. Each energy level $\pi_{E_0} = \{(\mathbf{x}, \dot{\mathbf{x}}): E = E_0\}$ is invariant under the phase flow, and all phase curves with a given energy lie entirely on this hypersurface.
