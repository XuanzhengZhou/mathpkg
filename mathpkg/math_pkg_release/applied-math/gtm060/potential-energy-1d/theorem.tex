The potential energy is the function

$$U(x) = - \int_{x_0}^{x} f(\xi) d\xi.$$

The sign in this formula is taken so that the potential energy of a stone is larger if the stone is higher off the ground.

Notice that the potential energy determines $f$. Therefore, to specify a system of the form $\ddot{x} = f(x)$ it is enough to give the potential energy. Adding a constant to the potential energy does not change the equation of motion.
