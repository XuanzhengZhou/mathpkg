Two cocycles $C$ and $C'$ on a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ are said to be \textit{cohomologous} if there exists a linear function $\rho: \mathfrak{g} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that

$$C'(a,b) = C(a,b) + \rho([a,b])$$

for all $a, b \in \mathfrak{g}$. The term $\rho([a,b])$ is the coboundary of the $1$-cochain $\rho$.
