A class of cocycles of a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ which are cohomologous to one another is called a \textit{cohomology class} of the Lie algebra. The set of cohomology classes forms the second cohomology group $H^2(\mathfrak{g}; \mathbb{R})$ of the Lie algebra with coefficients in the trivial module $\mathbb{R}$.
