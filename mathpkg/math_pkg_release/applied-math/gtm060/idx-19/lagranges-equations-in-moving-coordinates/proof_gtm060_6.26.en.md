The trajectory $\gamma$ is an extremal:

$$\delta \int_{\gamma} L(q, \dot{q}, t)\,dt = 0.$$

Therefore,

$$\delta \int_{\gamma} L'(Q, \dot{Q}, t)\,dt = 0$$

and $\tilde{\Phi}(t)$ satisfies Lagrange's equations.
