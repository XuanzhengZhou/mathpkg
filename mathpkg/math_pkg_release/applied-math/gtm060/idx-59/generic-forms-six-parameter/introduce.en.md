This corollary catalogs all generic local normal forms for differential 3-forms with a simple pole $dx \wedge dy \wedge dz / f$ that appear in families depending on at most 6 parameters. The 24 types correspond to the beginning of the ADE hierarchy of singularities of the denominator $f$, arranged by increasing codimension. The first type ($f = 1$) is nonsingular; $f = x$ corresponds to type $A_0$; and the remaining types correspond to $A_1$, $A_2$, $A_3$, $D_4$, $A_4$, $D_5$, $A_5$, $D_6$, $A_6$, and $E_6$ singularities with appropriate choices of signs. This result is important for understanding which singularities of pole forms are unavoidable in low-parameter families.
