Every germ $f^\beta h \, dx$ (with $h(0) \neq 0$) is equivalent to a germ of the form

$$f^\beta \left(1 + \sum_{m,l} \lambda_{m,l} x^m f^l\right) dx,$$

where the $l$ are nonnegative integers and the $m$ are elements of the set $I$ of interior integral points of the Newton diagram of $f$, such that the weight of each form $f^\beta x^m f^l dx$ is equal to zero. The weight of each monomial is computed with respect to the quasi-homogeneous weights $w_i$ of the variables $x_i$.
