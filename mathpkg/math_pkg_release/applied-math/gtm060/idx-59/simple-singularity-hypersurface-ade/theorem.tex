The beginning of the hierarchy of singular points of hypersurfaces is given, in suitable local coordinates, by one of the following equations:

$$A_{\mu}: \pm x_1^{\mu+1} \pm x_2^2 \pm \cdots \pm x_n^2 = 0, \quad \mu \geq 0;$$

$$D_{\mu}: x_1^2 x_2 \pm x_2^{\mu-1} \pm x_3^2 \pm \cdots \pm x_n^2 = 0, \quad \mu \geq 4;$$

$$E_6: x_1^3 + x_2^4 \pm x_3^2 \pm \cdots \pm x_n^2 = 0;$$

$$E_7: x_1^3 + x_1 x_2^3 \pm x_3^2 \pm \cdots \pm x_n^2 = 0;$$

$$E_8: x_1^3 + x_2^5 \pm x_3^2 \pm \cdots \pm x_n^2 = 0.$$
