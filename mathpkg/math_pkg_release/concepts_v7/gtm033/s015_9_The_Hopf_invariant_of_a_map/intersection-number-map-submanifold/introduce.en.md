The intersection number $\#(f,N)$ counts, with orientation signs, the preimages of a submanifold $N$ under a map $f$ transverse to $N$. Each intersection point is assigned $+1$ or $-1$ depending on whether the composition $M_x \to W_y \to W_y/N_y$ preserves or reverses orientation. This is a fundamental topological invariant in differential topology, generalizing the degree of a map.
