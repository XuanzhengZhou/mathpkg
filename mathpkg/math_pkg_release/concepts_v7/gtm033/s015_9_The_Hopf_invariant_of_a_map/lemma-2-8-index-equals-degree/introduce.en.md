Lemma 2.8 relates the local index of a vector field at an isolated zero to the degree of the normalized map $g(y) = f(y)/|f(y)|$ from the boundary of a small disk around the zero to the unit sphere. This provides a geometric interpretation of the index and reduces its computation to the degree of a map between spheres.
