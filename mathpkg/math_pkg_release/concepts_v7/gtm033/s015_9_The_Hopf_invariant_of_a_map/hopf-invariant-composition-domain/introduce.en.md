This result describes how the Hopf invariant behaves under pre-composition with a self-map of the domain sphere $S^3$. The Hopf invariant scales linearly by the degree of the pre-composing map $g$, showing that $H$ is a homomorphism with respect to the action of $\pi_3(S^3) \cong \mathbb{Z}$ on $\pi_3(S^2)$ via pre-composition.
