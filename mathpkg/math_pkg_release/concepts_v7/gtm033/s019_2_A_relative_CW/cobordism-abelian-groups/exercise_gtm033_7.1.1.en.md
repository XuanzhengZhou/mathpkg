# Exercise 1

The operation of cartesian product of manifolds induces bilinear maps

$$\Omega^i \times \Omega^j \rightarrow \Omega^{i+j}.$$

These pairings are associative, and commutative in the sense of graded rings: if $\alpha \in \Omega^i$, $\beta \in \Omega^j$ then $\alpha\beta = (-1)^{i+j}\beta\alpha$. There are similar bilinear pairings

$$\mathfrak{N}^i \times \mathfrak{N}^j \rightarrow \mathfrak{N}^{i+j}$$

which are strictly commutative.
