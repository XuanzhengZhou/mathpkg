# Exercise 9.2.2

Let $P_{a,b}(x,y) = \int_0^x (t-a)(t-b)\,dt + y^2$ for real numbers $a \neq 0$, $b \neq 0$.

(a) $P_{a,b}$ has three nondegenerate critical points, two minima and one saddle.

(b) $P_{a,b}(a,0) \neq P_{a,b}(b,0)$ if and only if $a \neq -b$.

(c) Let $\alpha$ be in the image of $P_{a,b}$. Then $P_{a,b}^{-1}(-\infty, \alpha]$ is connected if and only if $\alpha \geq 0$.

(d) $P_{a,b}^{-1}(-\infty, \alpha] \approx D^k$ if $\alpha > 0$.
