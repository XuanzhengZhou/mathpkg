# Exercise 9.2.1

The proof of Theorem 2.1 can be varied slightly to prove the following. Let $f: M \to \mathbb{R}$ be an admissible Morse function on a compact connected $n$-manifold $M$. Suppose $f$ has critical points of types $0, \ldots, 0, 1$ (any number of minima, one saddle). Then $M$ is diffeomorphic to a disk with holes.
