Lemma 4.4 analyzes the situation when a closed connected codimension-1 submanifold separates its ambient connected manifold. Under these hypotheses, the normal bundle of the separating submanifold is forced to be trivial, the complement has exactly two connected components, and the topological boundary of each component equals the original submanifold. This is a fundamental structural result used to relate separation, orientability, and normal bundle triviality.
