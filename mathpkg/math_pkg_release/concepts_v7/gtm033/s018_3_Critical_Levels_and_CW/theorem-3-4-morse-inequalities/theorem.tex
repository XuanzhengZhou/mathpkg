Let $f: M \to [a, b]$ be an admissible Morse function on a compact manifold, of type $(v_0, \ldots, v_n)$. Let $F$ be a field and denote by $\beta_k$ the dimension of the relative homology group $H_k(M, f^{-1}(a); F)$. Then:
(a) $\sum_{k=0}^{m} (-1)^{k+m} v_k \geqslant \sum_{k=0}^{m} (-1)^{k+m} \beta_k$ for $0 \leqslant m \leqslant n$;
(b) $\sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k v_k = \sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k \beta_k = \chi(M, f^{-1}(a))$.
