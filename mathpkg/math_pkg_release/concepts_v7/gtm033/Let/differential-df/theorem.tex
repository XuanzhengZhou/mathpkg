Let $f: M \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be a $C^{r+1}$ map, $1 \leqslant r \leqslant \omega$. For each $x \in M$ the linear map $T_x f: M_x \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ belongs to the cotangent space $M_x^* = L(M_x, \mathbb{R})$. We write

$$T_x f = Df_x \in M_x^*.$$

Then the map

$$Df: M \rightarrow T^*M, \quad x \mapsto Df_x = Df(x)$$

is a $C^r$ section of the cotangent bundle $T^*M$. The local representation of $Df$, in terms of a chart on $M$ and the corresponding natural chart on $T^*M$, is a map from an open set in $\mathbb{R}^n$ to $(\mathbb{R}^n)^*$ of the form $x \mapsto Dg(x)$ where $g$ is the local representation of $f$.
