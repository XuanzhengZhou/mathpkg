Theorem 1.7 is the most general isotopy extension theorem in the chapter. It applies to closed subsets $A$ (not necessarily submanifolds) and requires only bounded velocity. The extension $G$ agrees with the given isotopy near $A \times I$ and has support contained in the image of the original isotopy. This theorem is the key to the ambient tubular neighborhood and smoothing theorems that follow.
