# Exercise 5

What are the bilinear functions $A_k$ in the proof of Theorem 2.4?

(Hint: In the proof of Theorem 2.4, the terms $A_k$ arise from the Leibniz rule when differentiating the product $\lambda_j \cdot (g_j - f)$ in the estimate

$$\left\| D^k(\lambda_j(g_j - f))(x) \right\| \leqslant \sum_{i=0}^k \binom{k}{i} \left\| D^{k-i}\lambda_j(x) \right\| \cdot \left\| D^i(g_j - f)(x) \right\|.$$

Identify the combinatorial constants and the role they play in proving that $m_i A \mu_i \beta_i < \varepsilon_i$.)
