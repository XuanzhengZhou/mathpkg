A $C^s$ differential structure $\beta$ is compatible with a $C^r$ differential structure $\alpha$ (with $s > r$) if $\beta \subset \alpha$. This inclusion means every chart of $M_\beta$ is already a chart of $M_\alpha$, which is equivalent to the identity map $M_\alpha \to M_\beta$ being a $C^r$ diffeomorphism. Compatibility is the key notion for upgrading the differentiability class of a manifold without changing its underlying smooth structure.
