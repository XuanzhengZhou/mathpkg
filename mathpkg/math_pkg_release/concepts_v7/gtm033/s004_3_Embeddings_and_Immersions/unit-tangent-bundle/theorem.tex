The unit tangent bundle of a Riemannian manifold $M$ is
$$T_1 M = \{z \in TM : |z| = 1\}.$$
It is a $C^{r-1}$ submanifold of $TM$, realized as $T_1 M = v^{-1}(1)$ where $v: TM \to \mathbb{R}$ is defined by $v(z) = |z|^2$. The map $v$ is $C^{r-1}$ and $1$ is a regular value, so $T_1 M$ is a $C^{r-1}$ submanifold. If $M$ is compact, then $T_1 M$ is compact.
