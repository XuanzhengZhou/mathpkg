# Exercise 12

A base for the strong topology on $C^r(M,N)$, $0 \leqslant r < \infty$, is obtained by taking only those sets $\mathcal{N}^r(f; \Phi, \Psi, K, \varepsilon)$ where it is further required that $K = \{K_i\}_{i \in \Lambda}$ be a covering of $M$ by compact sets.
