# Exercise 3

$C_S^{n+1}(\mathbb{R}, \mathbb{R})$ and $C_W^{n+1}(\mathbb{R}, \mathbb{R})$ are homeomorphic to $C_S^n(\mathbb{R}, \mathbb{R}) \times \mathbb{R}$ and $C_W^n(\mathbb{R}, \mathbb{R}) \times \mathbb{R}$ respectively, $n \geqslant 0$.
