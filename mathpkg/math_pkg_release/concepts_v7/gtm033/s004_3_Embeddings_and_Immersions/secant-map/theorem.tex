Define the secant map
$$\sigma: M \times M - \Delta \to S^{q-1}, \quad \sigma(x,y) = \frac{x - y}{|x - y|},$$
where $\Delta = \{(a,b) \in M \times M : a = b\}$ is the diagonal, and $M \subset \mathbb{R}^q$ is a submanifold. The map $\sigma$ is $C^r$ when $M \times M - \Delta$ is considered as an open submanifold of $M \times M$.
