Theorem 1.4 states that the set of $C^r$ embeddings from $M$ to $N$ is open in the strong topology. The proof constructs, using the Local Injectivity Lemma, a locally finite atlas and chart families for $M$ and $N$ along with compact subsets that control injectivity on overlapping charts. Together with Theorem 1.1 (openness of immersions), this establishes that embeddings form an open subset of the space of all $C^r$ maps.
