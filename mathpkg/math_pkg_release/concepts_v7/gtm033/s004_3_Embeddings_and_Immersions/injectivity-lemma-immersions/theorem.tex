**Lemma.** Let $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^n$ be a $C^1$ immersion, where $U \subset \mathbb{R}^m$ is an open set with $m \leq n$. Let $W$ be an open subset of $U$ with compact closure $\bar{W} \subset U$ such that $f|_{\bar{W}}$ is injective. Then there exists $\varepsilon > 0$ such that if $g: U \to \mathbb{R}^n$ is a $C^1$ map satisfying
$$\|Dg(x) - Df(x)\| < \varepsilon, \quad |g(x) - f(x)| < \varepsilon$$
for all $x \in W$, then $g|_W$ is injective.
