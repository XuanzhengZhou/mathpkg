# Exercise 9

A 1-dimensional connected paracompact Hausdorff $\partial$-manifold of class $C^r$, $0 \leqslant r \leqslant \infty$, is $C^r$ diffeomorphic to either a closed, or a half open, finite interval. (This is also true, but harder to prove, for topological manifolds.)
