# Exercise 4

Let $A \subset N$ be a neat $C^r$ submanifold, $r > 0$. Let $f: (M, \partial M) \to (N, \partial N)$ be a $C^r$ map. Suppose every point of $A$ [respectively $\partial A$] is a regular value of $f$ [resp. $f: \partial M \to \partial N$]. Then $f^{-1}(A)$ is a neat $C^r$ submanifold of $M$.
