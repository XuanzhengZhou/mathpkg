# Exercise 5

Let $f: M \to \mathbb{R}$ be $C^r$, $r > 0$. Suppose $f$ is constant on each component of $\partial M$. Let $a$ and $b$ be regular values. Then the sets $f^{-1}(a)$, $f^{-1}[a,b]$, $f^{-1}(a,b]$, and $f^{-1}[a,\infty)$ are $C^r$ submanifolds of $M$.
