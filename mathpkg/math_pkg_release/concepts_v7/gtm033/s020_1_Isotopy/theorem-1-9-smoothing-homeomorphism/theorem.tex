For $i = 0, 1$ let $W_i$ be an $n$-manifold without boundary which is the union of two closed $n$-dimensional submanifolds $M_i, N_i$ such that $M_i \cap N_i = \partial M_i = \partial N_i = V_i$. Let $h: W_0 \to W_1$ be a homeomorphism which maps $M_0$ and $N_0$ diffeomorphically onto $M_1$ and $N_1$ respectively. Then there is a diffeomorphism $f: W_0 \approx W_1$ such that $f(M_0) = M_1$, $f(N_0) = N_1$ and $f|V_0 = h|V_0$.
