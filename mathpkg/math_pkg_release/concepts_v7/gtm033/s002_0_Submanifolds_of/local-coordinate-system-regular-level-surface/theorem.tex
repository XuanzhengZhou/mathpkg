Let $M = \{x \in U : g(x) = 0\}$ be a regular level surface of a $C^r$ map $g: U \to \mathbb{R}^k$ of rank $k$, where $U \subset \mathbb{R}^{n+k}$. Define

$$W = M \cap (U \times V),$$
$$\varphi: W \to \mathbb{R}^n,$$
$$(x, g(x)) \mapsto x \quad (x \in U).$$

Then $(\varphi, W)$ is taken as a local coordinate system on $M$.
