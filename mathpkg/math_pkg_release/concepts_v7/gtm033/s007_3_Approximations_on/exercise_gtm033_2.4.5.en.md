# Exercise 2.4.5

Let $\{X_i\}_{i \in A}$ be a family of complete metric spaces. Let $X$ be the product of the sets $X_i$, with the following strong topology: a set $U \subset X$ is open if for each $x \in U$ there exist open sets $U_i \subset X_i$ such that $x \in \prod U_i \subset U$ and $U_i = X_i$ for all but finitely many $i$. Then $X$ has the Baire property.
