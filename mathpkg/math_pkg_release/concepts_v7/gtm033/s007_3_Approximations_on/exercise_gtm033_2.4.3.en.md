# Exercise 2.4.3

Let $X$ be paracompact and $Y$ metric. For each $\varepsilon \in C(X, \mathbb{R}_+)$ define a metric $d_\varepsilon$ on $C(X,Y)$:

$$d_\varepsilon(f,g) = \min\left\{1,\; \sup_x d(fx, gx)/\varepsilon(x)\right\}.$$

(a) If $Y$ is complete each metric $d_\varepsilon$ is complete.

(b) If $Q \subset C(X,Y)$ is uniformly closed and $\varepsilon$ is bounded then $Q$ is closed in the metric space $(C(X,Y), d_\varepsilon)$.

(c) The strong topology on $C(X,Y)$ is that induced by the family of metrics

$$\{d_\varepsilon : \varepsilon \in C(X, \mathbb{R}_+)\}.$$
