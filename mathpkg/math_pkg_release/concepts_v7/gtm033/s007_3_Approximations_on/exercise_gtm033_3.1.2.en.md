# Exercise 3.1.2

(a) For every $y_0 \in N$, the set

$$\{f \in C^r(M,N) : y_0 \text{ is a regular value}\}$$

is open and dense in $C^r_S(M,N)$, $1 \leqslant r \leqslant \infty$.

(b) Given $y_0 \in N$, $f_0: M \to N$, a neighborhood $\mathcal{N} \subset C^r_S(M,N)$ of $f_0$, and a neighborhood $W \subset M$ of $f_0^{-1}(y_0)$, there exists $g \in \mathcal{N}$ such that $g \pitchfork y_0$ and $g = f_0$ on $M - W$.
