# Exercise 2.4.15

An open set $P \subset J^r(m, n)$ is *natural* if it is closed under composition with jets of local diffeomorphisms of $\mathbb{R}^m$ and $\mathbb{R}^n$. Given such a $P$ and $C^r$ manifolds $M, N$ define $P(M,N)$ to be the set of $C^r$ maps from $M$ to $N$ all of whose local representations have their $r$-jets in $P$. Then $P(M,N)$ is open in $C_S^r(M,N)$.
