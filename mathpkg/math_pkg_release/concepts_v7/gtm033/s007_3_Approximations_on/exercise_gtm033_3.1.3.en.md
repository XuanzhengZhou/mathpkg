# Exercise 3.1.3

Let $M$ be a manifold without boundary and $K \subset M$ a closed set. Every neighborhood $U \subset M$ of $K$ contains a closed neighborhood of $K$ which is a smooth submanifold of $M$.

*Hint:* Consider $\lambda^{-1}[0, y]$ where $\lambda: M \to [0,1]$ is $1$ on $K$, has support in $U$, and $y$ is a regular value of $\lambda$.
