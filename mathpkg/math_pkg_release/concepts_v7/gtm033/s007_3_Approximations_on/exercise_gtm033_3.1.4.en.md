# Exercise 3.1.4

(a) Let $M$ be a connected manifold and $f: M \to N$ an analytic map. Let $\Sigma \subset M$ be the set of critical points. If $\Sigma \neq M$ then $f^{-1}(f(\Sigma))$ has measure zero.

(b) If $f$ is merely $C^\infty$, the conclusion of (a) can be false.
