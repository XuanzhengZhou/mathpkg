Let $M \subset V$ be a neat $C^r$ submanifold, $1 \leqslant r < \infty$. A $C^r$ \textit{tubular neighborhood} of $M$ in $V$ is a pair $(f, \xi)$ where $\xi = (p, E, M)$ is a $C^r$ vector bundle and $f: E \to V$ is a $C^r$ embedding such that $f|_M = \mathrm{id}_M$ and $f(E)$ is a neighborhood of $M$ in $V$, with the zero section identified with $M$.
