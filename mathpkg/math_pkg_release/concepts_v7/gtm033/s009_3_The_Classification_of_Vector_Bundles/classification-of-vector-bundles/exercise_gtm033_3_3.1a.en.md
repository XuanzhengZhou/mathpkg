# Exercise 1(a)

Let $i: G_{s,k} \to G_{s+1,k+1}$ be the natural inclusion. Prove that

$$i^*\gamma_{s+1,k+1} \cong \gamma_{s,k} \oplus \varepsilon^1,$$

where $\gamma_{s,k}$ denotes the universal $k$-plane bundle over the Grassmannian $G_{s,k}$, and $\varepsilon^1$ denotes the trivial line bundle.
