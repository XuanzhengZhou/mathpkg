The degree of a smooth map between compact oriented manifolds of the same dimension counts the signed number of preimages of a regular value. Each preimage contributes $+1$ if the derivative preserves orientation and $-1$ otherwise. The degree is a homotopy invariant and serves as a fundamental algebraic tool for classifying maps and proving extension and fixed-point theorems.
