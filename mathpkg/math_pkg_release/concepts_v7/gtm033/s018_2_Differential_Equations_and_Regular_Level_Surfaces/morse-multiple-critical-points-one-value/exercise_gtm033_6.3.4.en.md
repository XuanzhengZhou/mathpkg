# Exercise 4: Unique Critical Value and Stable/Unstable Manifolds

Let $f: M \to [a, b]$ be an admissible Morse function on a compact manifold, having a unique critical value $c$, $a < c < b$. Let $z_1, \ldots, z_m$ be the critical points of $f$ and put

$$W_s = \bigcup_{i=1}^{m} W_s(z_i), \qquad W_u = \bigcup_{i=1}^{m} W_u(z_i).$$

(See Exercise 1 for the definition of $W_s$ and $W_u$.) Prove that

$$M - (W_s \cup W_u) \approx \bigl[f^{-1}(a) - W_s\bigr] \times I \approx \bigl[f^{-1}(b) - W_u\bigr] \times I.$$
