The unstable manifold $W_u(z)$ of a critical point $z$ is the set of points whose backward gradient flow converges to $z$. For a Morse function with a suitable Riemannian metric, $W_u(z)$ is a connected $C^r$ submanifold of dimension $n - k$ where $k = 	ext{Ind}(z)$. If $\partial M = \emptyset$, then $W_u(z) pprox \mathbb{R}^{n - k}$.
