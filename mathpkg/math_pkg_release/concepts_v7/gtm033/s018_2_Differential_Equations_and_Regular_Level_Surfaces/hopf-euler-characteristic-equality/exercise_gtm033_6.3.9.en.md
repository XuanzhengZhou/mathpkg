# Exercise 9: Euler Characteristic of a Fibration

Let $\varphi: M \to N$ be a surjective submersion where $M$ and $N$ are compact manifolds without boundary. Let $V = \varphi^{-1}(y)$ for some $y \in N$. Prove that

$$\chi(M) = \chi(V) \chi(N).$$

*Hint:* Let $g: N \to \mathbb{R}$ be a Morse function and apply the result of Exercise 8 to $f = g \circ \varphi: M \to \mathbb{R}$.
