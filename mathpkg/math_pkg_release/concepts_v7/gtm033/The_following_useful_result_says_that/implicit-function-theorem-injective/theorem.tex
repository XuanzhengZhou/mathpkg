Let $U \subset \mathbb{R}^m$ be an open set and $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^n$ a $C^r$ map ($1 \leqslant r \leqslant \omega$). Let $q \in \mathbb{R}^n$ be such that $0 \in f^{-1}(q)$, and suppose $Df_0$ is injective. Then there is a local diffeomorphism $\psi$ of $\mathbb{R}^n$ at $q$ such that $\psi(q) = 0$ and

$$\psi \circ f(x) = (x_1, \ldots, x_m, 0, \ldots, 0).$$
