A short exact sequence of vector bundles splits when the middle term decomposes as a direct sum of the left and right terms, i.e., $\eta \cong \xi \oplus \zeta$. The two equivalent conditions — existence of a right inverse for $G$ (a section) or a left inverse for $F$ (a retraction) — mirror the algebraic splitting lemma. Over paracompact bases, every short exact sequence splits (Theorem 2.2).
