Lemma 2.3 establishes that the orthogonal structure on a vector bundle is essentially unique: any vector bundle isomorphism can be deformed through bundle maps to one that preserves inner products. This is because the inclusion $O(n) \hookrightarrow GL(n)$ is a homotopy equivalence (by Gram-Schmidt), so the space of orthogonal structures is contractible. Consequently, one may freely choose any convenient metric.
