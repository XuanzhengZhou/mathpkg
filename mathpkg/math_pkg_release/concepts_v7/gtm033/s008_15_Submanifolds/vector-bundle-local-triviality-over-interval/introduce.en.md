This lemma is the crucial first step in the proof of the covering homotopy theorem for vector bundles. It states that any vector bundle over a product of a base with the unit interval is locally trivial in the base direction. The proof uses compactness of $I$ and the local triviality of $\xi$ to cover $B \times I$ by trivializing neighborhoods, then proceeds by induction on a subdivision of the interval.
