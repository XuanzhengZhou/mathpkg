# Exercise 3.1

Let $R$ be a Noetherian domain, and let $M$ be a nonempty subset of $R \setminus \{0\}$ which is closed under multiplication. ($M$ is then called a multiplicative system in $R$.) Is the ring $R_M = \{r/m \mid r \in R,\ m \in M\}$ Noetherian?
