Let $C \subset \mathbb{P}^2(\mathbb{C})$ be an irreducible curve of degree $n$. Suppose that $C$ has only ordinary singularities, say $P_1, \ldots, P_m$, and suppose that the multiplicity of $C$ at each such $P_i$ is $r_i$. Then the genus $g$ of $C$ is equal to
$$g = \frac{(n-1)(n-2)}{2} - \sum_{i=1}^{m} \frac{r_i(r_i-1)}{2}.$$
