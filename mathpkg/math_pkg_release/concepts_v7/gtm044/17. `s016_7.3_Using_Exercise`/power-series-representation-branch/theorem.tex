Let $C \subset \mathbb{C}_{XY}$ be an irreducible curve with coordinate ring $\mathbb{C}[x, y]$ and function field $\mathbb{C}(x, y)$. Let $P$ be a given point of $C$, let $R$ be a valuation ring with center $P$ on $C$, and let $B_P^\sim$ be the associated branch at $P$ of $C$. Then a representative of $B_P^\sim$ (and therefore $B_P^\sim$ itself) can be represented by power series of the form
$$x = T^m \quad (m \text{ a positive integer})$$
$$y = f(T) \quad (f \text{ analytic at } (0) \in \mathbb{C}_T)$$
