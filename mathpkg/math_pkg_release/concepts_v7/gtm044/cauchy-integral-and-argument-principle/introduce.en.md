This theorem states the two fundamental integral formulas of complex analysis. The Cauchy integral formula recovers the value of an analytic function at any interior point from its boundary values. The argument principle relates the contour integral of $f'/f$ to the number of zeros of $f$ inside the contour, counting multiplicity. These are the main tools used to prove the local structure theorem for plane algebraic curves.
