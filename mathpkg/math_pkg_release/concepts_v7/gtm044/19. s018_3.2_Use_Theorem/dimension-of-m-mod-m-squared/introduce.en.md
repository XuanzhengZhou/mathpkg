This lemma bridges the algebraic and geometric viewpoints in the characterization of regular local rings. The quotient $\mathfrak{m}/\mathfrak{m}^2$ is the Zariski cotangent space of the local ring, a vector space over the residue field $R/\mathfrak{m}$. The condition $\mathfrak{m} = x_1 R + \cdots + x_k R + \mathfrak{m}^2$ means the images of $x_1, \ldots, x_k$ span $\mathfrak{m}/\mathfrak{m}^2$. Together with Lemma 4.11, this lemma transforms the problem of proving $\mathfrak{m}$ is generated by $r-s$ elements into the problem of showing $\dim_{R/\mathfrak{m}} \mathfrak{m}/\mathfrak{m}^2 \leq r-s$.
