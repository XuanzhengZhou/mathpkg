# Exercise 10.11 (R. Godement)

Let $G$ be a unimodular, locally compact topological group. The following conditions on $G$ are equivalent:
(a) $G$ is bi-uniform;
(b) $G$ is a group of finite class (i.e., for every central Radon measure $\mu$ on $G$ of positive type, the von Neumann algebras associated with the unitary double representation derived from $\mu$ are of finite class);
(c) the von Neumann algebras associated with the regular unitary double representation of $G$ are of finite class.
