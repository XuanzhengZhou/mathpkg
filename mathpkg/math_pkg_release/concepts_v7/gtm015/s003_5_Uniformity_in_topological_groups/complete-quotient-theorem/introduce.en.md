This fundamental theorem states that completeness passes to closed subgroups and, when the subgroup is normal, to quotient groups of metrizable complete topological groups. The result relies on the existence of a left-invariant compatible metric and the construction of a quotient metric. It has crucial applications in functional analysis: quotients of Banach spaces (and more generally Fréchet spaces) by closed subspaces remain complete.
