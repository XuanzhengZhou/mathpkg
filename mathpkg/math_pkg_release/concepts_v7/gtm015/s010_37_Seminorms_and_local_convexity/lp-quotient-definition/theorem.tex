Let $\mathcal{N}$ be the linear subspace of $\mathcal{L}^p$ consisting of functions that vanish almost everywhere. Then the quotient space
$$L^p = \mathcal{L}^p / \mathcal{N}$$
equipped with the quotient seminorm $\|[f]\|_p = \|f\|_p$, is a normed space (the seminorm becomes a norm on the quotient).
