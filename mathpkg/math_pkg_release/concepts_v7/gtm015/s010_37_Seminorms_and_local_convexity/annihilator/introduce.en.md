The annihilator (or orthogonal complement) generalizes the notion from inner product spaces to dual pairs. For a subset $S \subset E$, its annihilator $S^\perp \subset F$ consists of all elements of the dual space that "vanish on $S$". The double annihilator $^\perp(S^\perp)$ is the $\sigma(E,F)$-closed linear span of $S$. Annihilators provide a Galois connection that is fundamental to the duality theory of locally convex spaces.
