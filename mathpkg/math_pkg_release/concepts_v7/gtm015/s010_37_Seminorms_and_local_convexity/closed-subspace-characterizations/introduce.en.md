This corollary gives three equivalent characterizations of closedness for subspaces of a locally convex space: topological closedness, weak closedness, and the double annihilator condition. The equivalence of (a) and (b) is a special case of the convex closed = weakly closed theorem (since subspaces are convex). Condition (c) connects topological closure to the algebraic operation of taking double annihilators, which is often more tractable in proofs.
