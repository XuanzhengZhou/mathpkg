Berberian provides an elegant alternative proof of the analytical form of the Hahn-Banach theorem using the machinery of seminorms and locally convex spaces developed in Section 37. The strategy is to equip $E$ with the locally convex topology $\tau_p$, observe that the open unit ball $A = \{x : p(x) < 1\}$ is a convex open set disjoint from the affine subspace $N = \{y \in M : g(y) = 1\}$, then apply the geometric form of Hahn-Banach to separate $N$ from $A$ by a closed hyperplane, which yields the desired extension $f$.
