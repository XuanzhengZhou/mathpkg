# Exercise 4.7

In a separated topological group $G$, the centralizer $C(a) = \{x \in G : xa = ax\}$ of each $a \in G$ is a closed subgroup of $G$, and the center $Z(G) = \{x \in G : xa = ax \text{ for all } a \in G\}$ is a closed subgroup of $G$.
