# Exercise 45.6

If $M$ is a linear subspace of a reflexive Banach space $E$, the following conditions are equivalent:
(a) $M$ is closed;
(b) $M$ is weakly closed;
(c) $M = {}^\perp(M^\perp)$;
(d) if $M_0$ is the image of $M$ under the canonical mapping $x \mapsto x''$ (of $E$ onto $E''$), then $M_0 = (M^\perp)^\perp$.
