# Exercise 49.10

If $(A, \|\cdot\|)$ is a normed space with a bilinear mapping $\varphi: A \times A \to A$ that is continuous at $(0, 0)$, then there exists an equivalent norm $x \mapsto |x|$ on $A$ such that $|\varphi(x, y)| \leq |x| |y|$ for all $x, y \in A$.
