# Exercise 47.8

Let $E$ and $F$ be normed spaces over $\mathbb{K}$, let $T_n: E \to F$ be a sequence of continuous linear mappings. If $\lim_{n \to \infty} T_n x = T x$ exists for every $x \in E$, then $T$ is a continuous linear mapping.
