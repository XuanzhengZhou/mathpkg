The orthogonal complement $N^\perp$ of a closed subspace $N$ is the set of all vectors orthogonal to every element of $N$. The notation $H = N \oplus N^\perp$ captures the orthogonal decomposition: the whole Hilbert space is the direct sum of a closed subspace and its orthogonal complement, with trivial intersection. This decomposition is not only algebraic but also topological.
