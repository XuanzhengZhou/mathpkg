# Exercise 42.15

In an inner product space, $\|x + y\|^2 = \|x\|^2 + \|y\|^2$ if and only if $\operatorname{Re}(x|y) = 0$. In order that $x \perp y$, it is necessary and sufficient that $\|x + y\|^2 = \|x\|^2 + \|y\|^2$ and $\|x + iy\|^2 = \|x\|^2 + \|iy\|^2$.
