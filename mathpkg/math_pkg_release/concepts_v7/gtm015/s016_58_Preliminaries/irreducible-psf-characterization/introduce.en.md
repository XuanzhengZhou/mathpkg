The irreducibility of the GNS representation associated with a PSF $\varphi$ is equivalent to the extremality (purity) of the corresponding state $f(a) = \varphi(a, 1)$ in the convex set of all states. This bijection between pure states and irreducible representations is fundamental to the structure theory of $C^*$-algebras.
