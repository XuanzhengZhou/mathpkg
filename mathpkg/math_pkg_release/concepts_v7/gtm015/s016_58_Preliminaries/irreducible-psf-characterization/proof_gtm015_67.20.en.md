# Proof of Characterization of irreducible PSFs

Proof. This is immediate from (67.18) and (67.2): a $*$-representation derived from a PSF $\varphi$ is irreducible iff the only adjunctive PSFs $\psi$ on $A$ such that $\psi \leq \varphi$ are the forms $\psi = \alpha \varphi$, $0 \leq \alpha \leq 1$.
