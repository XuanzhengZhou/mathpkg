The Stone-Čech compactification $\beta T$ of a completely regular space $T$ is the maximal compactification: every continuous function from $T$ to a compact space extends uniquely to $\beta T$. Constructed as the closure of the image of $T$ under the evaluation map into a product of intervals, it is a fundamental tool connecting topology with functional analysis via $C^*$-algebras.
