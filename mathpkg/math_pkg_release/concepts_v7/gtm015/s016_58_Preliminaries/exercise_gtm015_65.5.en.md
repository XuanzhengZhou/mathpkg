# Exercise 5

(Fuglede-Putnam theorem) If $A$ is a $C^*$-algebra with unity, if $a, b \in A$ are normal, and if $x \in A$ satisfies $xa = bx$, then also $xa^* = b^*x$; in particular, $\{a\}'$ (the commutant of $a$ in $A$) is a $*$-subalgebra of $A$.
