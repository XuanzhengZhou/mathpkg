The character space (maximal ideal space) of $\ell^1(\mathbb{Z})$ is homeomorphic to the circle group $\mathbb{T} = \{\lambda \in \mathbb{C} : |\lambda| = 1\}$. Specifically, every nonzero multiplicative linear functional $\varphi: \ell^1(\mathbb{Z}) \to \mathbb{C}$ has the form
$$\varphi(x) = \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} x(n) \mu^n$$
for a unique $\mu \in \mathbb{T}$.
