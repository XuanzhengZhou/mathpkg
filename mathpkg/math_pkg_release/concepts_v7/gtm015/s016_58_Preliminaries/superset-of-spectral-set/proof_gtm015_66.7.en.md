# Proof of Supersets of spectral sets are spectral sets

Proof. If $f \in \mathbb{C}(t; \tau)$ then $f \in \mathbb{C}(t; \sigma)$ and $\|f(T)\| \leq \|f\|_{\sigma} \leq \|f\|_{\tau}$.
