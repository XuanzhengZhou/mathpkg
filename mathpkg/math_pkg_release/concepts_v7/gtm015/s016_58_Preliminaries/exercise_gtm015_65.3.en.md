# Exercise 3

Let $A$ be a $C^*$-algebra with unity, let $\tau$ be a nonempty compact subset of the complex plane, and suppose $\varphi : \mathcal{C}(\tau) \to A$ is an algebra homomorphism such that $\varphi(1) = 1$ and $\|\varphi(f)\| \leq \|f\|_\infty$ for all $f \in \mathcal{C}(\tau)$. Write $u$ for the identity function $u(\lambda) \equiv \lambda$ on $\tau$, and let $a = \varphi(u)$. Then $\tau \supset \sigma(a)$, $\varphi$ is a $*$-homomorphism, and $a$ is normal.
