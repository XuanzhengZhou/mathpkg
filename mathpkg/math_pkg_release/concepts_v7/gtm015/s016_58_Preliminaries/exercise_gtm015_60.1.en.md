# Exercise 1

Let $A$ and $B$ be $C^*$-algebras and let $\varphi: A \to B$ be a $*$-homomorphism.

(i) If $\varphi$ is injective, then $\|\varphi(a)\| = \|a\|$ for all $a \in A$.

(ii) For every $\varphi$, $\varphi(A)$ is a closed $*$-subalgebra of $B$.
