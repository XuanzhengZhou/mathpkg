For a normal operator on Hilbert space, the spectrum itself is a spectral set. This follows from the continuous functional calculus: for any rational function $f$ with poles off $\sigma(T)$, the $C^*$-norm condition gives $\|f(T)\| \leq \|f\|_{\sigma(T)}$. This makes the theory of spectral sets particularly natural for normal operators.
