For a self-adjoint element of a $C^*$-algebra, the numerical status (the set of values taken by states) equals the convex hull of its spectrum. This theorem provides a fundamental link between the state-space geometry and the spectral theory of $C^*$-algebras, and is a key ingredient in the proof of the spectral theorem.
