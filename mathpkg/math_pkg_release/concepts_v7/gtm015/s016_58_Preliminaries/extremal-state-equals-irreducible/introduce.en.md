The convex set of states on a $C^*$-algebra is a weak$^*$-compact convex set, and its extremal points (pure states) correspond precisely to irreducible GNS representations. This bijection between pure states and irreducible representations is a cornerstone of the representation theory of $C^*$-algebras.
