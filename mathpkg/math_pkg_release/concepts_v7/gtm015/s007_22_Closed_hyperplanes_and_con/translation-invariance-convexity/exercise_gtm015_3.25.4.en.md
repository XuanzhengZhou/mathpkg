# Exercise 25.30

If $E$ is a real vector space of finite dimension $n$, and if $S$ is a nonempty subset of $E$, then $\operatorname{conv} S$ is the set of all convex combinations

$$\sum_{k=0}^{n} \alpha_k x_k,$$

where $x_k \in S$, $\alpha_k \ge 0$, and $\sum_{k=0}^{n} \alpha_k = 1$ (the point is that sums of length $n + 1$ are sufficient).
