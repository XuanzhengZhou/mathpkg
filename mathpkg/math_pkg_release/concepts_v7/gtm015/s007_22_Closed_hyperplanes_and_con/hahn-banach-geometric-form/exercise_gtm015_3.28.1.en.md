# Exercise 28.10

Let $E$ be a TVS over $\mathbb{K}$ that has no nonzero continuous linear forms (cf. Section 15) and let $x$ be a nonzero vector in $E$. Then $\lambda x \mapsto \lambda$ is a continuous linear form $g$ on the one-dimensional subspace $M = \mathbb{K}x$, but $g$ cannot be extended to a continuous linear form $f$ on $E$.
