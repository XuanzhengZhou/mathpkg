# Proof of Characterization of closed maximal complex-linear subspace

In view of (22.2), the linear forms appearing in the proof of (21.14) are continuous.

Thus, a subset $M$ of $E$ is a closed, maximal $\mathbb{C}$-linear subspace if and only if $M = M_0 \cap (iM_0)$ with $M_0$ a closed, maximal $\mathbb{R}$-linear subspace, by applying (21.14) with the additional condition that the relevant linear forms are continuous.
