# Proof of Product of locally convex TVS is locally convex

Let $(E_i)_{i \in I}$ be a family of locally convex TVS over $\mathbb{K}$ and let $E = \prod_{i \in I} E_i$ be the product TVS (11.9). A basic neighborhood of $\theta$ in $E$ has the form $V = \prod_{i \in I} V_i$, where, for a suitable finite subset $J$ of $I$, $V_i = E_i$ for $\iota \notin J$, and $V_i$ is a convex neighborhood of $\theta$ in $E_i$ for $\iota \in J$. Such a set $V$ is convex by (25.8). Since the neighborhoods $V$ are basic in $E$, $E$ is locally convex.
