Let $E$ be a vector space over $\mathbb{K}$ and $A \subset E$ a convex set. Let $(F_i)_{i \in I}$ be faces of $A$, and $F, G$ faces of $A$.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item $A$ is a face of itself.
  \item If $\bigcap_{i \in I} F_i \neq \varnothing$, then $\bigcap_{i \in I} F_i$ is a face of $A$.
  \item If $F$ is a face of $A$ and $G$ is a face of $F$, then $G$ is a face of $A$.
\end{enumerate}
