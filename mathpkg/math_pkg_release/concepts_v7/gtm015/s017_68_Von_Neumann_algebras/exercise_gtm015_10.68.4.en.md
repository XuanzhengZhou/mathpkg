# Exercise 4

*(68.25) If $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ are von Neumann algebras and $\varphi: \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{B}$ is a *-isomorphism, then $\varphi$ is ultrastrongly bicontinuous and ultraweakly bicontinuous.
