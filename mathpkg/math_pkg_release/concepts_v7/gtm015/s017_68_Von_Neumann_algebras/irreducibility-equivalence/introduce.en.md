This lemma establishes that irreducibility is preserved under the correspondence between $*$-representations of $L^1(G)$ and unital $*$-representations of the group $C^*$-algebra. It is a crucial step in the proof of the Gel'fand-Raikov theorem, allowing one to transfer irreducible representations from the $C^*$-algebra setting back to the group.
