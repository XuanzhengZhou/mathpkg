The left regular representation embeds $L^1(G)$ as a $*$-subalgebra of the bounded operators on the Hilbert space $L^2(G)$. Faithfulness is proved using approximate identity techniques. This representation is the starting point for constructing the group $C^*$-algebra and for applying the Gel'fand-Na\u{i}mark theory to group representations.
