# Exercise 13.9

If $(E_n, d_n)$ is a sequence of spaces of type $(F)$, $E = \prod_{n=1}^{\infty} E_n$, and if, for $x = (x_n), y = (y_n)$ in $E$ one defines

$$d(x, y) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{2^{-n} d_n(x_n, y_n)}{1 + d_n(x_n, y_n)},$$

then $(E, d)$ is a space of type $(F)$, and the topology on $E$ derived from $d$ coincides with the product topology.
