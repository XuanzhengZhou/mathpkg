# Exercise 16.16

Let $T$ be a topological space, $\mathcal{B}_{\mathbb{K}}(T)$ the Banach space described in (16.15). Let $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{K}}^{\infty}(T)$ be the set of all $x \in \mathcal{B}_{\mathbb{K}}(T)$ that are continuous. Then $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{K}}^{\infty}(T)$ is a closed linear subspace of $\mathcal{B}_{\mathbb{K}}(T)$ and is therefore a Banach space for the norm $\|x\|_{\infty}$.
