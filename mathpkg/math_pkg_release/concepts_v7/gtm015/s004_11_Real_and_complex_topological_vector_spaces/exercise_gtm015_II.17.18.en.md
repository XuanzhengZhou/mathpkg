# Exercise 17.18

If $A$ is a balanced subset of a vector space $E$ over $\mathbb{K}$, the following conditions are equivalent: (a) $A$ is absorbent; (b) for each $x \in E$ there exists $\lambda \in \mathbb{K}$, $\lambda \neq 0$, such that $\lambda x \in A$.
