If $E$ is a set, $(E_\iota)_{\iota \in I}$ is a family of topological spaces, and $f_\iota: E \to E_\iota$ ($\iota \in I$) is a family of mappings, then the initial topology for the family $(f_\iota)_{\iota \in I}$ coincides with the initial topology for the mapping
$$f: E \to F = \prod_{\iota \in I} E_\iota$$
defined by $f(x) = (f_\iota(x))_{\iota \in I}$.
