This theorem verifies that the topology induced by a norm (via the metric $d(x,y) = \|x-y\|$) indeed satisfies the TVS axioms. The proof uses the estimate $\|\lambda_n x_n - \lambda x\| \leq |\lambda_n| \|x_n - x\| + |\lambda_n - \lambda| \|x\|$ to show joint continuity of scalar multiplication from the continuity of the norm, the boundedness of convergent scalar sequences, and the absolute homogeneity property. This confirms that every normed space is a legitimate topological vector space.
