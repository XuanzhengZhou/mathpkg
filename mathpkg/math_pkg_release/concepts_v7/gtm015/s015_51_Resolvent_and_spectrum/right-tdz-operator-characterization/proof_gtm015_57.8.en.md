# Proof of Right topological divisors of zero in L(E)

(57.8) Theorem. Let $E$ be a normed space and let $T \in \mathcal{L}(E)$. The following conditions on $T$ are equivalent:

(a) $T$ is a right TDZ in $\mathcal{L}(E)$;

(b) $T'$ is a left TDZ in $\mathcal{L}(E')$;

(c) there exists a sequence $f_n \in E'$ such that $\|f_n\| = 1$ and $f_n \rightarrow 0$ uniformly on $T(E_1)$, where $E_1 = \{x \in E : \|x\| \leq 1\}$.
