(iv) Every element of the radical of $A$ is QR. {The radical $R$ of $A$ is defined to be the intersection of all the modular maximal left ideals; $R$ coincides with the intersection of all the modular maximal right ideals (defined dually), hence is a two-sided ideal of $A$.}
