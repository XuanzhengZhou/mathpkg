# Proof of Left TDZ and approximate point spectrum

**Proof.** By definition, $\lambda \in \sigma_{\text{ap}}(T)$ iff there exists a sequence $(x_n)$ in $E$ with $\|x_n\| = 1$ and $(T - \lambda I)x_n \to \theta$. By (57.4), this is equivalent to $T - \lambda I$ being a left TDZ in $\mathcal{L}(E)$. Thus $\sigma_{\text{ap}}(T) = \{\lambda \in \mathbb{C} : T - \lambda I \text{ is a left TDZ}\}$.
