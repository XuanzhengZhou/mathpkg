The resolvent set is always open in the complex plane, which follows from the continuity of inversion in the group of units. Moreover, all $\lambda$ with $|\lambda| > \|x\|$ belong to the resolvent set, giving an explicit outer bound. The spectrum is therefore a compact subset of $\mathbb{C}$.
