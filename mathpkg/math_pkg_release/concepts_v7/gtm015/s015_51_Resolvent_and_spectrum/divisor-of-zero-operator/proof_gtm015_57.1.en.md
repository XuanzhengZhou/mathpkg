# Proof of Divisors of zero in L(E)

(57.1) Theorem. Let $E$ be a normed space and let $T \in \mathcal{L}(E)$.

(i) $T$ is a left divisor of zero in $\mathcal{L}(E)$ iff $T$ is not injective.

(ii) $T$ is a right divisor of zero in $\mathcal{L}(E)$ iff $T(E)$ is not dense in $E$.

$$Sx = f(x)y \quad (x \in E);$$
