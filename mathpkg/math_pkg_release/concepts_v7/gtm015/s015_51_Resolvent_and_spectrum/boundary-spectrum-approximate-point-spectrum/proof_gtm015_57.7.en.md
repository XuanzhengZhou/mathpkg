# Proof of Boundary of spectrum in approximate point spectrum

(57.7) Theorem. If $E$ is a Banach space and $T \in \mathcal{L}(E)$, then $\partial \sigma(T) \subset \sigma_{\text{ap}}(T)$.

Proof. If $\lambda \in \partial \sigma(T)$ then $T - \lambda I$ is a left (even two-sided) TDZ in $\mathcal{L}(E)$ (56.4), therefore $\lambda \in \sigma_{\text{ap}}(T)$ (57.6). {In particular, $\sigma_{\text{ap}}(T)$ contains every $\lambda \in \sigma(T)$ such that $|\lambda| = r(T)$ (51.11).}
