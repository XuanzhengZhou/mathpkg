A topological vector space (TVS) is the central object of study in functional analysis: a vector space over a topological division ring where both vector addition and scalar multiplication are continuous. The book deals exclusively with TVS over $\mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$. Every TVS is, in particular, a topological module over the scalar field, and the additive group is an abelian topological group.
