If $x, y \in \ell^1(\mathbb{Z})$, their convolution $xy: \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{C}$ is defined by
$$(xy)(m) = \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} x(m-n) y(n) \quad (m \in \mathbb{Z}).$$
Then $xy \in \ell^1(\mathbb{Z})$ and
$$\|xy\|_1 \leq \|x\|_1 \|y\|_1.$$
The convolution $xy$ is also called the product of $x$ and $y$.
