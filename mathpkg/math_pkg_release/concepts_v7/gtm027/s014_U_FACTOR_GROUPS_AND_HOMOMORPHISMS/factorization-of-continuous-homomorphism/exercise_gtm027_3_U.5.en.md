# Exercise U(e): Factorization of a continuous homomorphism

Let $f : G \to J$ be a continuous homomorphism of topological groups, and let $f[G]$ be endowed with the quotient topology induced by $f$. Prove that the map $q : G \to f[G]$ (defined by $q(x) = f(x)$) is a continuous open homomorphism, and that the identity map $i : f[G] \to J$ is a continuous one-to-one homomorphism. Conclude that every continuous homomorphism can be factored into a continuous open homomorphism followed by a continuous one-to-one homomorphism.
