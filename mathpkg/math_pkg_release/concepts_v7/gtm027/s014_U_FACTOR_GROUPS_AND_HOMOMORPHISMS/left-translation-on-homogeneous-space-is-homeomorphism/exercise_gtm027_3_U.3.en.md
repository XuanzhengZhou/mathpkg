# Exercise U(c): Left translation on homogeneous space is a homeomorphism

Let $G$ be a topological group and $H$ a subgroup. For a fixed element $a \in G$, define the map $L_a : G/H \to G/H$ by $L_a(A) = a \cdot A$ for each left coset $A \in G/H$. Prove that $L_a$ is a homeomorphism.
