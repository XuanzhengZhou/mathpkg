# Exercise U(d): Continuity criterion for homomorphisms

Let $f : G \to H$ be a homomorphism of topological groups. Prove that $f$ is continuous if and only if the inverse image under $f$ of every neighborhood of the identity element of $H$ is a neighborhood of the identity element of $G$.
