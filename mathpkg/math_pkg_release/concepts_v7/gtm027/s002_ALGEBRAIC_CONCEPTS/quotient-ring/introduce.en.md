The quotient ring $R/I$ is formed from a ring $R$ and a two-sided ideal $I$. Its elements are the additive cosets $x + I$, with addition and multiplication defined via representatives. This construction is the ring-theoretic analogue of the quotient group and is fundamental to commutative algebra and algebraic geometry.
