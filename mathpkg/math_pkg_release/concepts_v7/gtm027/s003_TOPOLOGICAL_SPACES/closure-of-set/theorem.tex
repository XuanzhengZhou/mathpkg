The \textbf{closure} ($\mathfrak{J}$-closure) of a subset $A$ of a topological space $(X, \mathfrak{J})$ is the intersection of all closed sets containing $A$. The closure of $A$ is denoted by $A^{-}$ or by $\overline{A}$. The set $A^{-}$ is the smallest closed set containing $A$, and $A$ is closed iff $A = A^{-}$.
