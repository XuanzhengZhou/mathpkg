The Gage Dictionary translates every fundamental concept of uniform spaces into the language of pseudo-metrics. The uniformity base, uniform topology, closures, Cauchy nets, uniform continuity, and product uniformities all have clean characterizations in terms of the gage P. This dictionary makes it possible to work entirely with pseudo-metrics when studying uniform spaces, often leading to simpler proofs by reducing general uniform arguments to the metric case.
