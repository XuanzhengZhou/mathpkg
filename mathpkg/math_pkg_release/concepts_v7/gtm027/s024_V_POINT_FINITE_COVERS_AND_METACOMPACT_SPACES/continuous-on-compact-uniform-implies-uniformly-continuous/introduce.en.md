On a compact uniform space, continuity implies uniform continuity. This generalizes the classical Heine-Cantor theorem from metric spaces to arbitrary uniform spaces. The proof uses Theorem 29: the composition of a continuous function with a continuous pseudo-metric from the codomain's gage yields a continuous pseudo-metric on the domain, which must belong to the domain's gage by the maximality property of compact uniformities.
