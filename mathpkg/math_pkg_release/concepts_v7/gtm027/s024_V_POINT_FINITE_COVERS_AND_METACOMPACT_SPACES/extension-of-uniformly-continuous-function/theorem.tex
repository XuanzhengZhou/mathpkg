Let $f : A \to Y$ be a uniformly continuous function whose domain $A$ is a subset of a uniform space $(X, \mathfrak{u})$ and whose values lie in a complete Hausdorff uniform space $(Y, \mathfrak{v})$. Then $f$ admits a unique uniformly continuous extension $f^{-} : A^{-} \to Y$ whose domain is the closure of $A$ in $X$.
