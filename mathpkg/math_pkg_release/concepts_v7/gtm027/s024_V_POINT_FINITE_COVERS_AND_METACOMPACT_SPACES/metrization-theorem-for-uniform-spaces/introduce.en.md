A uniform space is pseudo-metrizable precisely when its uniformity admits a countable base. Given a countable base, one inductively constructs a decreasing sequence satisfying the conditions of the Metrization Lemma, which then yields a compatible pseudo-metric. Adding the Hausdorff separation axiom upgrades pseudo-metrizability to metrizability. This theorem, first appearing in Alexandroff and Urysohn, provides the definitive link between uniform structures and metric structures.
