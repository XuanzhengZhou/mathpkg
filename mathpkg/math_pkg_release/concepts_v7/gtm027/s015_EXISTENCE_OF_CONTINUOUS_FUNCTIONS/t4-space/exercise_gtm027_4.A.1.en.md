# Exercise A.1 (Regular Spaces)

Let $X$ be a regular space and let $\mathfrak{D}$ be the family of all subsets of the form $\{x\}^{-}$ for $x$ in $X$. Then $\mathfrak{D}$ is a decomposition of $X$, the projection of $X$ onto the quotient space $\mathfrak{D}$ is both open and closed, and the quotient space is regular Hausdorff.
