This lemma, proved using A. H. Stone's trick, shows that in spaces where every open cover is even, every open cover admits an open $\sigma$-discrete refinement. The proof employs a well-ordering of the space and an iterative diagonal-neighborhood construction to partition the space into countably many discrete families. This is a critical step in establishing the equivalence between even covers and paracompactness.
