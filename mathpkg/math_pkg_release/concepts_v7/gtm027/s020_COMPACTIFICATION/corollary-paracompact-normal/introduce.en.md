This corollary establishes that paracompactness is a strong separation property: every paracompact space is normal. The proof follows from the observation that a family consisting of two disjoint closed sets is discrete, and Lemma 31 then provides disjoint open neighborhoods. This places paracompactness between regularity and full normality in the hierarchy of separation axioms.
