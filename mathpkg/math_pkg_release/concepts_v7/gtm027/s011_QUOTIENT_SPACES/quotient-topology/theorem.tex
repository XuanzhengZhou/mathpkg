Let $f$ be a function on a topological space $X$ with range $Y$. The family
$$\mathfrak{U} = \{\, U \subseteq Y : f^{-1}[U] \text{ is open in } X \,\}$$
is a topology for $Y$, called the \text{quotient topology} (relative to $f$ and the topology of $X$). It is the largest topology on $Y$ for which $f$ is continuous.
