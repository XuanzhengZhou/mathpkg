# Proof of Weak-Star Compactness of the Equicontinuous Closure

Let $F \subseteq X^*$ be equicontinuous. Then its $w^*$-closure $\overline{F}^{w^*}$ is $w^*$-compact.

**Proof.** By the first part of (d), $\overline{F}^{w^*}$ is equicontinuous, hence norm bounded. By the Alaoglu theorem (b), any norm-bounded $w^*$-closed subset is $w^*$-compact. Since $\overline{F}^{w^*}$ is $w^*$-closed by definition and norm bounded, it is $w^*$-compact.
