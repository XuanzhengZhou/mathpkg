Let $a_n$ be the $n$-th binomial coefficient in the expansion of $(1 - t)^{1/2}$ about $t = 0$. Then $a_0 = 1$, $a_n$ is negative for $n$ positive, $\sum \{a_n : n \in \omega\} = 0$, and $\sum \{a_n a_{p-n} : n \in \omega \text{ and } n \leq p\}$ is $1$, $-1$, and $0$ for $p = 0$, $p = 1$, and $p > 1$, respectively.
