The evaluation map $E: X \to G$ sending each point $x$ to the evaluation functional $f \mapsto f(x)$ is continuous when the function space $G$ carries the uniformity of uniform convergence on an equicontinuous family $\alpha$ of subsets. This theorem links the continuity of evaluation with equicontinuity, providing a symmetric perspective: just as equicontinuity ensures continuity of functions in the family, it also ensures continuity of the evaluation map itself.
