Let $X$ be a topological space, let $\mathfrak{D}$ be an upper semi-continuous decomposition of $X$ whose members are compact, and let $\mathfrak{D}$ have the quotient topology. Then $\mathfrak{D}$ has the following property whenever $X$ has it:

\begin{itemize}
\item Hausdorff,
\item regular,
\item locally compact,
\item satisfies the second axiom of countability.
\end{itemize}
