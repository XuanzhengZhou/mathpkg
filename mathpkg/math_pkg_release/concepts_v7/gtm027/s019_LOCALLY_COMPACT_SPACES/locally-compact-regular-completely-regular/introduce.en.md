Theorem 18 directly implies that every locally compact regular space is completely regular: the Urysohn function constructed in the theorem separates points from closed sets. Combined with the earlier result that locally compact Hausdorff spaces are regular, this yields the stronger statement that every locally compact Hausdorff space is a Tychonoff space (completely regular and $T_1$). This places locally compact Hausdorff spaces firmly within the classical separation axiom hierarchy.
