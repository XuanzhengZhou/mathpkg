# Exercise O.1 — Arbitrary Product of Connected Spaces is Connected

Prove: The product of an arbitrary family of connected topological spaces is connected.

(Hint: Fix a point $x$ in the product, and let $A$ be the set of all points $y$ such that there is a connected subset to which both $x$ and $y$ belong. Show that $A$ is dense.)
