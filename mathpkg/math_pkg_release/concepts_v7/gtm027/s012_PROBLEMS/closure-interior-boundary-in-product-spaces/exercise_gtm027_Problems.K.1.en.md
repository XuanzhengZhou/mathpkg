# Exercise K.1 — Closure, Interior, and Boundary of Product Sets

Let $X$ and $Y$ be topological spaces and let $X \times Y$ be the product space. For each set $C$ let $C^b$ be the boundary of $C$. Then, if $A$ and $B$ are subsets of $X$ and $Y$ respectively,

**(a)** $(A \times B)^- = A^- \times B^-$,

**(b)** $(A \times B)^0 = A^0 \times B^0$, and

**(c)** $(A \times B)^b = (A \times B)^- \sim (A \times B)^0 = ((A^b \cup A^0) \times (B^b \cup B^0)) \sim (A^0 \times B^0)$.
