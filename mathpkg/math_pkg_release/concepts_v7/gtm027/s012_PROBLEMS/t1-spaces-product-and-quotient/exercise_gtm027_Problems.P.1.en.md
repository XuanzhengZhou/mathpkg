# Exercise P.1 — Product and Quotient of $T_1$-Spaces

The product of $T_1$-spaces is a $T_1$-space. If $\mathfrak{D}$ is a decomposition of a topological space, then the quotient space is $T_1$ iff the members of $\mathfrak{D}$ are closed.
