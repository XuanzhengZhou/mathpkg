If a topology $\mathfrak{J}$ for a family $F$ is jointly continuous on compacta, then $\mathfrak{J} \supset \mathcal{C} \supset \wp$, where $\mathcal{C}$ is the compact open topology and $\wp$ is the pointwise topology. If $(F,\mathfrak{J})$ is compact and the range space $Y$ is Hausdorff, then $(F,\wp)$ is Hausdorff and consequently $\mathfrak{J} = \mathcal{C} = \wp$.
