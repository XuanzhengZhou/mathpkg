Let $F$ be a family of functions, each on a topological space $X$ to a topological space $Y$. The **compact-open topology** on $F$ is the topology having as a subbase all sets of the form
$$W(K, U) = \{f \in F : f[K] \subset U\}$$
where $K$ is a compact subset of $X$ and $U$ is an open subset of $Y$.
