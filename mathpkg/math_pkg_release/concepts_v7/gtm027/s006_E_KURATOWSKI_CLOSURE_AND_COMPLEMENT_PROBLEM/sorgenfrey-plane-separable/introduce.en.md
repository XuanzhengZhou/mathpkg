The product of two separable spaces is separable, so the Sorgenfrey plane inherits separability from the Sorgenfrey line. The set of points with both coordinates rational forms a countable dense subset. However, as the next results show, separability in the product does not guarantee the Lindelof property, and subspaces need not be separable.
