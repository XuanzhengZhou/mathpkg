# Exercise 5.M(a): The Helly space is compact Hausdorff

The *Helly space* $H$ is the family of all non-decreasing functions on the closed unit interval $[0,1]$ with values in $[0,1]$. It is a subset of the product space $[0,1]^{[0,1]}$, and its topology is the relative product topology. Prove that $H$ is compact Hausdorff.
