# Exercise 5.K: Product of normal and compact Hausdorff may not be normal

Show that the product of a locally compact, normal Hausdorff space and a compact Hausdorff space may fail to be normal.

*Hint:* Let $\Omega$ be the first uncountable ordinal. Let $\Omega' = \{\alpha : \alpha \leq \Omega\}$ and $\Omega_0 = \{\alpha : \alpha < \Omega\}$, each with the order topology. The difficult part has already been established in Problem 4.E; it is only necessary to show that $\Omega'$ and $\Omega_0$ are compact and locally compact Hausdorff respectively.
