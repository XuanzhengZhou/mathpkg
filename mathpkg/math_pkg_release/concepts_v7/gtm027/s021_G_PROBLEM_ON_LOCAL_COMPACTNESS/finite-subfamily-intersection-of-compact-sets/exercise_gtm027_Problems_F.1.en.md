# Exercise F(a): Finite subfamily intersection lemma for closed compact sets

Let $\alpha$ be a family of closed compact sets in a topological space $X$ such that $\bigcap \{A : A \in \alpha\}$ is a subset of an open set $U$. Prove that there exists a finite subfamily $F \subset \alpha$ such that $\bigcap \{A : A \in F\} \subset U$.
