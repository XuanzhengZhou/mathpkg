# Proof of Union of Ordinals is an Ordinal

Proof That $E$ connects $\bigcup x$ follows from theorems 110 and 111, and that $\bigcup x$ is full follows from the fact that members of $x$ are full.

It is not hard to see that if $x$ is a subset of $R$, then the ordinal $\bigcup x$ is the first ordinal which is greater than or equal to each member of $x$, and that $\bigcup x$ is a set iff $x$ is a set. These results will not be needed, however.
