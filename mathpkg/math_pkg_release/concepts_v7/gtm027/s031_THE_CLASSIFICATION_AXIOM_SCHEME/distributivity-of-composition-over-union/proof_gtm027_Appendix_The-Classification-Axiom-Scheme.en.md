# Proof of Distributivity of Composition over Union

59 THEOREM $r \circ (s \cup t) = (r \circ s) \cup (r \circ t)$ and $r \circ (s \cap t) \subset (r \circ s) \cap (r \circ t)$.60 DEFINITION $r^{-1} = \{(x,y): (y,x) \in r\}$.If $r$ is a relation $r^{-1}$ is the relation inverse to $r$.
