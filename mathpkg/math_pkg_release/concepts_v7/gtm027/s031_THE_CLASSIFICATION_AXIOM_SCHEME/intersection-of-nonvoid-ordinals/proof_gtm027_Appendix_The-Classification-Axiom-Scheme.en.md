# Proof of Intersection of Non-Void Collection of Ordinals

121 Theorem If $x \subset R$ and $x \neq 0$, then $\bigcap x \in x$.Indeed, in this case $\bigcap x$ is the $E$-first member of $x$.122 Definition $x + 1 = x \cup \{x\}$.
