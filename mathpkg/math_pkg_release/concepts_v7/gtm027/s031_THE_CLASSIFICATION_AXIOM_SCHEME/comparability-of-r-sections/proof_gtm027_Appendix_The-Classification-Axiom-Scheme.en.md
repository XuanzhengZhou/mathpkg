# Proof of Comparability of R-Sections

92 THEOREM If $x$ and $y$ are $r$-sections of $x$, then $x \subset y$ or $y \subset x$.93 DEFINITION $^*$ $f$ is $r-s$ order preserving if and only if $f$ is a function, $r$ well-orders domain $f$, $s$ well-orders range $f$, and $f(u) s f(v)$ whenever $u$ and $v$ are members of domain $f$ such that $u r v$.
