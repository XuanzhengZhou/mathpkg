# Proof of Full Proper Subclass of an Ordinal

PROOF If $uEv$ and $vEv$, then $uEv$ because $y$ is full. Hence $y$ is an $E$-section of $x$. Consequently there is a member $v$ of $x$ such that $y = \{u: u \in x \text{ and } uEv\}$ by theorem 91. Since every member of $v$ is a member of $x$, $y = \{u: u \in v\}$ and $y = v$.
