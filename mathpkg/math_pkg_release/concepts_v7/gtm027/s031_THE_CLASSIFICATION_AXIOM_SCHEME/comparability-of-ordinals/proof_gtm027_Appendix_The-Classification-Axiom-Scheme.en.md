# Proof of Comparability of Ordinals

PROOF The class $x \cap y$ is full and by the preceding theorem either $x \cap y = x$ or $x \cap y \in x$. In the first case $x \subset y$. If $x \cap y \in x$, then $x \cap y \notin y$ since in this case $x \cap y \in x \cap y$. Since $x \cap y \notin y$ the preceding theorem implies that $x \cap y = y$ and therefore $y \subset x$.
