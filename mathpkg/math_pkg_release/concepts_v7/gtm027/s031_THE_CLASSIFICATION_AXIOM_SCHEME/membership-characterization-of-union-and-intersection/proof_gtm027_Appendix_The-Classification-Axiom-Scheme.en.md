# Proof of Membership Characterization of Union and Intersection

Proof From the classification axiom $z \in x \cup y$ iff $z \in x$ or $z \in y$ and $z$ is a set. But in view of the definition 1 of set, $z \in x$ or $z \in y$ and $z$ is a set iff $z \in x$ or $z \in y$. A similar argument proves the corresponding result for intersection.
