# Proof of Characterization of Proper R-Sections

PROOF If $y$ is an $r$-section of $x$ and $y \neq x$, then there is an $r$-first member $v$ of $x \sim y$. If $u \in x$ and $u r v$, then, since $v$ is the $r$-first member of $x \sim y$, $u \notin x \sim y$ and hence $u \in y$. Therefore $\{u : u \in x \text{ and } u r v\} \subset y$. On the other hand, if $u \in y$, then since $v \notin y$ and $y$ is an $r$-section, it is false that $v r u$ and hence it is true that $u r v$. The required equality follows.
