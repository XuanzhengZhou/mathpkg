# Exercise E.5 — The Product of Two Normal Ordinal Spaces is Not Normal

Prove that the product $\Omega_0 \times \Omega'$ is not normal. (Here $\Omega_0 = [0, \Omega)$ and $\Omega' = [0, \Omega]$.)

(Hint: Let $A = \{(x, x) : x \in \Omega_0\}$ and $B = \Omega_0 \times \{\Omega\}$. If $U$ is a neighborhood of $A$, let $f(x)$ be the smallest ordinal larger than $x$ such that $(x, f(x)) \notin U$. Then the result of part (d) applies.)
