# Exercise E.3 — Normality of Ordinal Spaces

Prove that both $\Omega_0 = [0, \Omega)$ and $\Omega' = [0, \Omega]$ are normal spaces.

(Hint: If $A$ and $B$ are closed disjoint subsets and the first point of $A \cup B$ belongs to $A$, find a finite sequence $a_0, b_0, a_1, \ldots, a_n$ (or $b_n$) such that $a_i \in A$, $b_i \in B$, no point of $A$ is between $a_i$ and $b_i$, and no point of $B$ is between $b_i$ and $a_{i+1}$, for each $i$. The intervals $(a_i, b_i]$ are both open and closed.)
