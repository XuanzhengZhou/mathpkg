# Exercise E.4 — Accumulation Point of Graph for Expanding Functions

Let $f: \Omega_0 \to \Omega_0$ be a function such that $f(x) \geq x$ for every $x \in \Omega_0$. Prove that for some $x \in \Omega_0$, the point $(x, x)$ is an accumulation point of the graph of $f$ in $\Omega_0 \times \Omega_0$.

(Hint: Define a sequence inductively by $x_{n+1} = f(x_n)$. Observe that $x_n \leq f(x_n) \leq x_{n+1}$, and use the interlacing lemma from part (a).)
