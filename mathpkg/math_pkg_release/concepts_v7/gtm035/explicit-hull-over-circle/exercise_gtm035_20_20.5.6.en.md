# Exercise 20.6

For each $n$ and each $\lambda \in \Lambda = \{|\lambda| < 1\}$, $w \in \mathbb{C}$, the function $D(\lambda, w)$ arising from the Pick-Nevanlinna interpolation data can be expressed as

$$D(\lambda, w) = a |w|^2 + b w + \bar{b} \bar{w} + c,$$

where $a, b, c$ are rational functions of $\lambda$ and $\bar{\lambda}$.

Verify this formula.
