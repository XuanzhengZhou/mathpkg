% Banach Algebra Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

A commutative Banach algebra with unit is a commutative complex algebra $\mathfrak{A}$ equipped with a complete norm $\|\cdot\|$ satisfying $\|xy\| \leq \|x\|\|y\|$ for all $x, y \in \mathfrak{A}$ and $\|1\| = 1$.
