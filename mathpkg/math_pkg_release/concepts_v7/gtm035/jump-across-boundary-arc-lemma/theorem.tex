% Jump Across Boundary Arc Lemma
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $U_i, U_j$ be regions with a common smooth boundary arc $\alpha$, with $\alpha$ positively oriented for $U_j$. Functions constructed via Cauchy integrals on each side have a jump across $\alpha$ equal to the original data.
