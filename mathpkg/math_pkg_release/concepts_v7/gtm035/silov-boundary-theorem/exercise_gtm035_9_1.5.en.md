# Exercise 9.5

Let $B_n = \{z \in \mathbb{C}^n : \sum_{i=1}^n |z_i|^2 \leq 1\}$ and

$$A(B_n) = \{f \in C(B_n) : f \in H(\mathcal{O}), \; \mathcal{O} = \text{interior of } B_n\}.$$

Show that $\check{S}(A(B_n)) = \text{topological boundary of } B_n$.
