# Exercise 9.8

Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a Banach algebra and fix $p \in \check{S}(\mathfrak{A})$. Assume that $p$ is an isolated point of $\check{S}(\mathfrak{A})$, viewed in the topology induced on $\check{S}(\mathfrak{A})$ by $\mathcal{M}$. Show that $p$ is then an isolated point of $\mathcal{M}$.
