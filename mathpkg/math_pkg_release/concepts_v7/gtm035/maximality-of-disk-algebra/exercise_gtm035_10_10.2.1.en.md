# Exercise 10.1

Let $D$ be the closed unit disk in the $z$-plane and $\Gamma$ the unit circle. Let

$$\mathfrak{A}_0 = A(D)|_\Gamma$$

be the disk algebra restricted to $\Gamma$, consisting of functions in $C(\Gamma)$ that admit an analytic extension to $|z| < 1$.

Put

$$g = \sum_{\nu=-p}^{p} c_\nu e^{i\nu\theta},$$

where the $c_\nu$ are complex constants and $p$ is a positive integer. Compute the closed algebra generated by $\mathfrak{A}_0$ and $g$, i.e., the closure in $C(\Gamma)$ of all sums

$$\sum_{\nu=0}^{N} a_\nu g^\nu, \quad a_\nu \in \mathfrak{A}_0.$$

Determine for which choices of the coefficients $c_\nu$ the generated algebra equals $C(\Gamma)$.
