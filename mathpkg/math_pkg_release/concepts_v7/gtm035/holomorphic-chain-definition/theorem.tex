% Holomorphic Chain Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

A holomorphic chain of complex dimension $k$ in an open set $\Omega \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ is a formal sum $\sum n_j V_j$, where the branches $\{V_j\}$ constitute a locally finite family of irreducible analytic subvarieties of complex dimension $k$ in $\Omega$ and the $n_j$ are nonzero integers (possibly negative).
