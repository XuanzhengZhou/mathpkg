# Exercise 20.2

Let $X$ be a compact set in $\mathbb{C}^n$ such that $\pi(\hat{X}) \neq \pi(X)$. Let $W$ be a component of $\mathbb{C} \setminus \pi(X)$ with $\pi(\hat{X}) \supseteq W$. Fix a closed disk $\Delta \subseteq W$ and put $Y = \pi^{-1}(\partial \Delta)$.

Show that $\hat{Y} = \pi^{-1}(\Delta)$.
