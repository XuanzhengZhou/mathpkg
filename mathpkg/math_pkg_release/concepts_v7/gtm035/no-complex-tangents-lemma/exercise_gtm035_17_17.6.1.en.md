# Exercise 17.2

Prove the smoothness assertion; i.e., show that the squared distance function

$$d^2(z) = \operatorname{dist}(z, \Sigma)^2$$

is of class $C^2$ in some neighborhood of $\Sigma$, where $\Sigma$ is a sufficiently smooth submanifold of $\mathbb{C}^n$.
