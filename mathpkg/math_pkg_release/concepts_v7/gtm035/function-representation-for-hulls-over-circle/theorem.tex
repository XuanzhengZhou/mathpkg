% Function Representation For Hulls Over Circle
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

The algebra $\mathcal{F}$ consists of all functions $f$ on $\{|\lambda| < 1\}$ that can be written in the form $f(\lambda) = \int_{Y_\lambda} g(\lambda, \cdot) d\mu_\lambda$ for suitable choices of representing measures.
