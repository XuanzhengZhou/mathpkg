# Exercise 7.3

Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a uniform algebra on a compact space $X$ with generators $g_1, \ldots, g_n$ (i.e., $\mathfrak{A}$ is the closed subalgebra generated by $g_1, \ldots, g_n$ and the constants). Let $G = (g_1, \ldots, g_n): X \to \mathbb{C}^n$ be the Gelfand map.

(a) Show that the joint spectrum $\sigma(g_1, \ldots, g_n)$ equals $G(X)$.

(b) Prove that $X$ is polynomially convex if and only if $G(X)$ is polynomially convex in $\mathbb{C}^n$.

(c) Using the Oka-Weil theorem (Theorem 7.3), show that every function in $\mathfrak{A}$ can be uniformly approximated on $X$ by polynomials in $g_1, \ldots, g_n$.
