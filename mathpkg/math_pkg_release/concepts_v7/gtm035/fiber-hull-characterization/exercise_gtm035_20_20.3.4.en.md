# Exercise 20.4

Let $\alpha(\lambda) = 2\bar{\lambda}$ for $\lambda \in \Gamma$, and define

$$Y = \{(\lambda, w) : \lambda \in \Gamma,\; |w - 2\bar{\lambda}| \leq 1\}.$$

Thus $Y$ is a compact set in $\mathbb{C}^2$ lying over the unit circle $\Gamma$ whose fiber $Y_\lambda$ is the closed disk of radius $1$ centered at $2\bar{\lambda}$.

Determine the polynomial hull $\hat{Y}$.
