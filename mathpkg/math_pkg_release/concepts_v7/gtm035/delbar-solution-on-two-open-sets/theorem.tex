% Delbar Solution On Two Open Sets
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $K$ be a compact polynomially convex set. Given a $\bar{\partial}$-closed form on $U_1 \cup U_2$ that is locally solvable, there exists a global solution on a neighborhood of $K$.
