% Gelfand Transform Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

For $f \in \mathfrak{A}$ and $M \in \mathcal{M}$, the Gelfand transform $\hat{f}(M)$ is the value at $f$ of the homomorphism of $\mathfrak{A}$ into $\mathbb{C}$ corresponding to the maximal ideal $M$.
