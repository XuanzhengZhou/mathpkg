% Uniform Algebra Definition
% From GTM035, Section s001
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $X$ be a compact Hausdorff space. A uniform algebra on $X$ is an algebra $\mathfrak{A}$ of continuous complex-valued functions on $X$ satisfying
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathfrak{A}$ is closed under uniform convergence on $X$.
\item $\mathfrak{A}$ contains the constants.
\item $\mathfrak{A}$ separates the points of $X$.
\end{enumerate}
$\mathfrak{A}$ is normed by $\|f\| = \max_x |f|$ and so becomes a Banach algebra.
