% Measure Orthogonality And Delbar
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\mu$ be a measure on $X$ with $\|\mu\| < \infty$ and $\mu \perp \mathfrak{A}$. Fix $z$ such that $\int_X d|\mu|(\zeta)/|\zeta - z|^{2n-1} < \infty$. Then $\int_X K_j(\zeta, z) d\mu(\zeta) = 0$ for all $j$.
