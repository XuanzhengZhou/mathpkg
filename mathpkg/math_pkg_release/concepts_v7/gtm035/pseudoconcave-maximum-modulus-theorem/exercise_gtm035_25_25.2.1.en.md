# Exercise 25.1

Let $\Omega = \{|z| < 1\} \times \mathbb{C}$ be the cylindrical domain in $\mathbb{C}^2$. Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ be analytic functions on $\{|z| < 1\}$ and let $E$ be the subset of $\Omega$ given by

$$w^n + a_1(z)w^{n-1} + \cdots + a_n(z) = 0.$$

Show that $E$ is pseudoconcave in $\Omega$.
