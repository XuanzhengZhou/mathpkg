# Exercise 25.2

Verify the statement "this is true when $k = n = 1$" in the context of Browder's theorem (Section 25.5). Specifically, let $X$ be a compact set in $\mathbb{C}$ and suppose that $\widehat{X} \setminus X$ is nonempty. Show that the closure of $\widehat{X} \setminus X$ contains $X$.
