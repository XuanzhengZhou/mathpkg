% Pseudoconcave Maximum Modulus Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s026
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $X$ be a pseudoconcave set contained in the open bidisk $\{|z| < 1, |w| < 1\} \subseteq \mathbb{C}^2$. Denote by $A$ the algebra of polynomials in $z$ and $w$ restricted to $X$. Then $(A, X, D, \pi)$ with $\pi: (z,w) \mapsto z$ is a maximum modulus algebra.
