# Exercise 7.2

Let $u: \mathbb{C}^n \to \mathbb{C}^m$ be a holomorphic map, i.e., each component $u_j$ is holomorphic. For a differential form $\omega$ on $\mathbb{C}^m$, let $\omega(u)$ denote the pullback $u^*\omega$. Prove that:

$$d(\omega(u)) = (d\omega)(u) \quad \text{and} \quad \bar{\partial}(\omega(u)) = (\bar{\partial}\omega)(u).$$

That is, the exterior derivative $d$ and the $\bar{\partial}$-operator commute with pullback by holomorphic maps.

*Hint.* First verify the result for 0-forms (functions) and for the basic 1-forms $dz_j$ and $d\bar{z}_j$. Then extend by linearity and the wedge product rule.
