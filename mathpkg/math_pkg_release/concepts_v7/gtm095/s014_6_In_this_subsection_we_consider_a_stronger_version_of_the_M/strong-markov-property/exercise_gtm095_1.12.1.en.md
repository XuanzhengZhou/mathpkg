# Exercise 1

Let $\xi = (\xi_0, \xi_1, \ldots, \xi_n)$ be a Markov chain with values in a state space $X$, and let $f = f(x)$ where $x \in X$ be an arbitrary function. Determine whether the following sequences form a Markov chain:

(a) The sequence $(f(\xi_0), f(\xi_1), \ldots, f(\xi_n))$ obtained by applying $f$ to each element of the chain.

(b) The "reversed" sequence $(\xi_n, \xi_{n-1}, \ldots, \xi_0)$.

Provide proofs or counterexamples for each case.
