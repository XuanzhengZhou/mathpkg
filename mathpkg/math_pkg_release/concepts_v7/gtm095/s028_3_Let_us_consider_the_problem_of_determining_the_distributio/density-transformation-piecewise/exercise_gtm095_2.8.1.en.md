# Exercise 1

Verify the following formulas from Section 8:

**(a)** Formulas (9) and (10) — the joint distribution of the conditional expectation and conditional variance for a bivariate normal vector.

**(b)** Formula (24) — the density transformation formula for a piecewise monotone transformation:
$$
f_{\eta}(y) = \sum_{k=1}^{n} f_{\xi}(h_k(y)) \, |h'_k(y)| \cdot I_{D_k}(y).
$$

**(c)** Formula (27) — the distribution of the sum of normal random variables.

**(d)** Formula (28) — the distribution of $\chi_n = +\sqrt{\chi_n^2}$.

**(e)** Formulas (34)–(38) — the $\chi^2$-distribution, $\chi$-distribution, and densities of products and quotients of independent random variables.
