# Exercise 1

Prove the estimate (8) used in the proof of the Berry–Esseen inequality:

$$\left| (\log f)'''\!\left( \theta \frac{t}{\sqrt{n}} \right) \right| \leq 7\beta_3$$

for $|t| \leq T = \sqrt{n}/(5\beta_3)$, where $\beta_k = \mathbb{E}|\xi_1|^k$ and $f(t) = \mathbb{E}e^{it\xi_1}$.
