# Exercise 7

Prove formula (30). Let $\xi$ and $\eta$ be random elements on $(\Omega, \mathcal{F}, P)$ with values in a measurable space $(E, \mathcal{E})$. Show that

$$|\mathcal{P}\{\xi \in A\} - \mathcal{P}\{\eta \in A\}| \leq \mathcal{P}(\xi \neq \eta), \quad A \in \mathcal{E}.$$
