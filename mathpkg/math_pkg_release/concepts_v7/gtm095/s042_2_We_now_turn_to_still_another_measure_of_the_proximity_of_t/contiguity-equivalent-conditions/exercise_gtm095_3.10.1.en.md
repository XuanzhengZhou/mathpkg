# Exercise 1

Assume that $\mathcal{F} = \sigma(\bigcup_n \mathcal{F}_n)$. Let $P$ and $\tilde{P}$ be two probability measures on $(\Omega, \mathcal{F})$ and let $P_n = P \mid \mathcal{F}_n$, $\tilde{P}_n = \tilde{P} \mid \mathcal{F}_n$ be their restrictions to $\mathcal{F}_n$. Show that

$$(\tilde{P}_n) \triangleleft (P_n) \iff \tilde{P} \ll P,$$

$$(\tilde{P}_n) \triangleleft\!\triangleright (P_n) \iff \tilde{P} \sim P,$$

$$(\tilde{P}_n) \triangle (P_n) \iff \tilde{P} \perp P.$$
