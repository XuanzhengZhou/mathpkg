# Exercise 7

Find the number of allocations of $n$ different balls over $m$ indistinguishable boxes subject to the condition that neither box remains empty.

*Hint.* Compare this with the number of surjections from an $n$-element set onto an $m$-element set. How does the distinguishability of boxes affect the count?
