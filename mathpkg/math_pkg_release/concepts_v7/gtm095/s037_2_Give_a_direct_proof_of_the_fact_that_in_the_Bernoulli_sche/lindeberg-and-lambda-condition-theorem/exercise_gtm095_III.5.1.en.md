# Exercise 1: Asymptotic Negligibility for i.i.d. Sequences

Let $\xi_1, \xi_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of independent identically distributed random
variables with $\mathbb{E}\,\xi_1^2 < \infty$. Show that

$$\max\Bigl( \frac{|\xi_1|}{\sqrt{n}}, \ldots, \frac{|\xi_n|}{\sqrt{n}} \Bigr) \xrightarrow{\mathbb{P}} 0,
\qquad n \to \infty.$$
