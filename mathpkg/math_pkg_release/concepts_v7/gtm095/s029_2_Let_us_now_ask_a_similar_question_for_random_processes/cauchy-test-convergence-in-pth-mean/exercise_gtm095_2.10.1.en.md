# Exercise 1

Prove that $L^\infty$ is complete.

Recall that $\|\xi\|_\infty \equiv \operatorname{ess sup} |\xi| \equiv \inf\{0 \leq c \leq \infty : P(|\xi| > c) = 0\}$ and that $L^\infty$ consists of (equivalence classes of) random variables with finite essential supremum.
