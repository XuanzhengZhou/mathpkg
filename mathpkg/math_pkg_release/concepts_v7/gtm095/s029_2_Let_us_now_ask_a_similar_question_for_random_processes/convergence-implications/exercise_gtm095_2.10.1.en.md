# Exercise 1

Let the $d$-metric on the set of random variables be defined by

$$d(\xi, \eta) = E \frac{|\xi - \eta|}{1 + |\xi - \eta|}$$

and identify random variables that coincide almost surely. Show that $d = d(\xi, \eta)$ is a well defined metric and that convergence in probability is equivalent to convergence in the $d$-metric.
