# Exercise 1

Verify the consistency of the families of distributions in the corollaries to Theorems 1 and 2.

Specifically, check that the finite-dimensional distributions constructed in Corollary 2 (Markov process) and Corollary 3 (discrete-time Markov process) satisfy the Kolmogorov consistency condition.
