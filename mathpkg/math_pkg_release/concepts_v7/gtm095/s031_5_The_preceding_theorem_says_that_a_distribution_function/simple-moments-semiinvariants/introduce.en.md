Simple moments and semi-invariants are the special case where all exponents in the multi-index are either 0 or 1. Each subset $I$ corresponds to the mixed moment $E[\prod_{i\in I} \xi_i]$, which involves each component at most once. This notation simplifies decomposition formulas that express simple moments in terms of simple semi-invariants over all set partitions.
