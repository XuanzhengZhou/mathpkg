# Exercise 5

Let $\varphi_k(t)$, $k \geq 1$, be characteristic functions, and let the nonnegative numbers $\lambda_k$, $k \geq 1$, satisfy $\sum \lambda_k = 1$. Show that $\sum \lambda_k \varphi_k(t)$ is a characteristic function.

**Hint:** Consider a mixture of the corresponding probability distributions. If $F_k$ is the distribution function with characteristic function $\varphi_k$, then $F(x) = \sum \lambda_k F_k(x)$ is a distribution function. What is its characteristic function?
