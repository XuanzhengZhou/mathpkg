# Exercise 7

Prove that for any sequence of sets $\{A_n\}$ and $\{B_n\}$:

$$\limsup A_n = \overline{\liminf \overline{A}_n}, \quad \liminf A_n = \overline{\limsup \overline{A}_n},$$

$$\liminf A_n \subseteq \limsup A_n,$$

$$\limsup(A_n \cup B_n) = \limsup A_n \cup \limsup B_n,$$

$$\limsup A_n \cap \liminf B_n \subseteq \limsup(A_n \cap B_n) \subseteq \limsup A_n \cap \limsup B_n.$$

If $A_n \uparrow A$ or $A_n \downarrow A$, then

$$\liminf A_n = \limsup A_n.$$
