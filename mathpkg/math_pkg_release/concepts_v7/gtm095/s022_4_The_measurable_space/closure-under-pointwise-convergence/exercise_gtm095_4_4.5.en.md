# Exercise 5

Let $\xi$ and $\eta$ be random variables on $(\Omega, \mathcal{F})$, and $A \in \mathcal{F}$. Then the function

$$\zeta(\omega) = \xi(\omega) \cdot I_A(\omega) + \eta(\omega) \cdot I_{\overline{A}}(\omega)$$

is also a random variable.
