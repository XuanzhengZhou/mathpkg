# Exercise 1 — De Morgan's Laws

Establish the following properties of the operators $\cap$ and $\cup$:

$$A \cup B = B \cup A, \quad AB = BA \quad \text{(commutativity)},$$
$$A \cup (B \cup C) = (A \cup B) \cup C, \quad A(BC) = (AB)C \quad \text{(associativity)},$$
$$A (B \cup C) = AB \cup AC, \quad A \cup (BC) = (A \cup B)(A \cup C) \quad \text{(distributivity)},$$
$$A \cup A = A, \quad AA = A \quad \text{(idempotency)}.$$

Show also the following **De Morgan's laws**:

$$\overline{A \cup B} = \overline{A} \cap \overline{B}, \qquad \overline{A \cap B} = \overline{A} \cup \overline{B}.$$

Generalize these to $n$ events.
