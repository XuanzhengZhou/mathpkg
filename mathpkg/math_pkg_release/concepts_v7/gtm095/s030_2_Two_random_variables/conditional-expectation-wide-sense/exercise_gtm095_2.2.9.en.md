# Exercise 9

Suppose we are given three sequences $(\mathcal{G}_n^{(1)}), (\mathcal{G}_n^{(2)})$ and $(\mathcal{G}_n^{(3)})$ of $\sigma$-subalgebras of $\mathcal{F}$, and let $\xi$ be a bounded random variable. Suppose we know that

$$\mathcal{G}_n^{(1)} \subseteq \mathcal{G}_n^{(2)} \subseteq \mathcal{G}_n^{(3)} \quad \text{for each } n,$$

$$\mathsf{E}(\xi \mid \mathcal{G}_n^{(1)}) \xrightarrow{\mathsf{P}} \eta, \quad \mathsf{E}(\xi \mid \mathcal{G}_n^{(3)}) \xrightarrow{\mathsf{P}} \eta.$$

Prove that $\mathsf{E}(\xi \mid \mathcal{G}_n^{(2)}) \xrightarrow{\mathsf{P}} \eta$.
