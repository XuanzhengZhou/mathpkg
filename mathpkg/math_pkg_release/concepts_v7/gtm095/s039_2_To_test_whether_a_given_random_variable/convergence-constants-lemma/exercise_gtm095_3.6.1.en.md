# Exercise 1

Show that if $\xi_n \xrightarrow{d} \xi$ and $\xi_n \xrightarrow{d} \eta$, then $\xi \stackrel{d}{=} \eta$.

That is, the limit in distribution is unique (up to equality in distribution).
