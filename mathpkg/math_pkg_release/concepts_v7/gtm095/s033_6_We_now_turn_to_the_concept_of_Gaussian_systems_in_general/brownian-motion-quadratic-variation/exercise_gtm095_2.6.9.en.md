# Exercise 9

Let $f = \{f_n, n \geq 1\} \subset L^2(0,1)$ be a complete orthonormal system and let $(\xi_n)_{n \geq 1}$ be independent identically distributed $\mathcal{N}(0,1)$-random variables. Show that the process

$$B_t = \sum_{n \geq 1} \xi_n \int_0^t f_n(u) \, du, \qquad 0 \leq t \leq 1,$$

is a standard Brownian motion.

*Hint.* Verify that the series converges uniformly with probability one (use properties of Gaussian series). Show that the finite-dimensional distributions are Gaussian with the correct mean and covariance, and that the paths are continuous.
