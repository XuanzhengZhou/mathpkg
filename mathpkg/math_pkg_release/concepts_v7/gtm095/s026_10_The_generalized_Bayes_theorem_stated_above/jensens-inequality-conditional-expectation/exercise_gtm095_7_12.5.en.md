# Exercise 5

Let $g = g(x)$ be a convex Borel function with $\mathsf{E}|g(\xi)| < \infty$. Show that Jensen's inequality

$$g(\mathsf{E}(\xi \mid \mathcal{G})) \leq \mathsf{E}(g(\xi) \mid \mathcal{G})$$

holds (a.s.) for the conditional expectations.
