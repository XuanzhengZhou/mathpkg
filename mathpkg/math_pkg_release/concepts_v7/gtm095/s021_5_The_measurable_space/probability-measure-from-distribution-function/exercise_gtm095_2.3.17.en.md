# Exercise 17

Let $\mathcal{A}_1$ and $\mathcal{A}_2$ be two independent systems of sets each of which is a $\pi$-system. Show that $\sigma(\mathcal{A}_1)$ and $\sigma(\mathcal{A}_2)$ are also independent. Give an example of two independent systems $\mathcal{A}_1$ and $\mathcal{A}_2$, which are not $\pi$-systems, such that $\sigma(\mathcal{A}_1)$ and $\sigma(\mathcal{A}_2)$ are dependent.
