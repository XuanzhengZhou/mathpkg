# Exercise 2

Let $\xi_1, \xi_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of independent random variables with finite means $\mathbb{E}|\xi_n|$ and variances $\operatorname{Var}\xi_n$ such that $\operatorname{Var}\xi_n \leq K < \infty$, where $K$ is a constant. Use Chebyshev's inequality to prove the law of large numbers

$$P\!\left\{ \left| \frac{S_n - \mathbb{E}S_n}{n} \right| \geq \varepsilon \right\} \to 0, \qquad n \to \infty.$$
