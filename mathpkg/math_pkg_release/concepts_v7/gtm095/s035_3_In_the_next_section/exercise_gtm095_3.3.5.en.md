# Exercise 5

Let $X_1, X_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of independent identically distributed random vectors (with values in $\mathbb{R}^k$) with mean zero and (finite) covariance matrix $\Gamma$. Show that

$$\frac{X_1 + \cdots + X_n}{\sqrt{n}} \xrightarrow{d} \mathcal{N}(0, \Gamma).$$

(Compare with Theorem 3.)
