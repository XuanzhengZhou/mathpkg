Let $\beta \colon B_1 \to B_2$ and $\beta' \colon B_2 \to B_3$ be homomorphisms of $\Lambda$-modules. Then the induced maps on Hom satisfy:
$$
(\beta' \circ \beta)_{*} = \beta'_{*} \circ \beta_{*} \colon \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, B_1) \longrightarrow \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, B_3).
$$
Moreover, $(1_{B})_{*} = 1_{\operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, B)}$.
