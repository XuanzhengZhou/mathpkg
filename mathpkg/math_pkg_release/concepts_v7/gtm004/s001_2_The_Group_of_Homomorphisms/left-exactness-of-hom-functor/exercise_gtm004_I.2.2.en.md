# Exercise 2.2

Prove Theorem 2.2. Show that $\mu^* = \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(\mu, B)$ is not, in general, surjective even if $\mu$ is injective. (Take $\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}$, $B = \mathbb{Z}_n$, the integers mod $n$, and the short exact sequence $\mathbb{Z} \xrightarrow{\mu} \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}_n$, where $\mu$ is multiplication by $n$.)
