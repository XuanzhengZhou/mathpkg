Let $\mathfrak{B}_d$ be the discrete subcategory of a small category $\mathfrak{B}$ (same objects, only identity morphisms) and let $I: \mathfrak{B}_d \hookrightarrow \mathfrak{B}$ be the inclusion. For a functor $\{A_V\}: \mathfrak{B}_d \rightarrow \mathfrak{A}$, the left Kan extension $\tilde{I}\{A_V\}: \mathfrak{B} \rightarrow \mathfrak{A}$ is given by
$$
(\tilde{I}\{A_V\})_{V'} = \coprod_{V \in \mathfrak{B}} \coprod_{v \in \mathfrak{B}(V, V')} (A_V)_v,
$$
where $(A_V)_v = A_V$, with the obvious values on morphisms.
