# Exercise 2.2

Using a free presentation of the abelian group $A$ and Theorem 2.1, deduce the sequence

$$0 \to \operatorname{Tor}(A, G') \to \operatorname{Tor}(A, G) \to \operatorname{Tor}(A, G'') \to A \otimes G' \to A \otimes G \to A \otimes G'' \to 0$$

associated with the short exact sequence $G' \rightarrowtail G \twoheadrightarrow G''$ (see Theorem III.8.2).
