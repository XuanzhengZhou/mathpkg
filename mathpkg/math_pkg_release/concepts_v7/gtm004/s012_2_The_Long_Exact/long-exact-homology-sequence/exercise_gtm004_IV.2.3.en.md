# Exercise 2.3

Let

$$
\begin{array}{ccccccc}
0 & \longrightarrow & C' & \longrightarrow & C & \longrightarrow & C'' & \longrightarrow & 0 \\
& & \downarrow{\varphi'} & & \downarrow{\varphi} & & \downarrow{\varphi''} & & \\
0 & \longrightarrow & D' & \longrightarrow & D & \longrightarrow & D'' & \longrightarrow & 0
\end{array}
$$

be a map of short exact sequences of chain complexes. Show that if any two of $\varphi', \varphi, \varphi''$ induce isomorphisms in homology, so does the third.
