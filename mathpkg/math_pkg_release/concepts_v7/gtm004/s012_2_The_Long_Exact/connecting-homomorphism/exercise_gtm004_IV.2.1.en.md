# Exercise 2.1

Use Theorem 2.1 to associate with a chain map $\varphi: C \to D$ an exact sequence

$$\cdots \to H_n(C) \to H_n(D) \to H_n(E(\varphi)) \to H_{n-1}(C) \to \cdots.$$

*Hint:* Use the exact sequence $D_n \rightarrowtail E_n \twoheadrightarrow C_{n-1}$.

Deduce that $H(E(\varphi)) = 0$ if and only if $\varphi_*: H(C) \xrightarrow{\sim} H(D)$.

Show that the association proved above is functorial.
