Axiom A3 postulates the existence of an identity morphism $1_A$ for every object $A$ in a category. The identity morphism acts as a unit for composition: pre-composing or post-composing any morphism with the appropriate identity leaves the morphism unchanged. This generalizes the role of the identity function $x \mapsto x$ in the category of sets.
