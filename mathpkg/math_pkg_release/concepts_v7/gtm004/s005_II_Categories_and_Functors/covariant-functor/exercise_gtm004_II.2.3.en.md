# Exercise 2.3

Show that the center is not a functor $\mathfrak{G} \rightarrow \mathfrak{G}$ in any obvious way. Let $\mathfrak{G}_{\text{epi}}$ be the subcategory of $\mathfrak{G}$ in which the morphisms are the surjections. Show that the center is a functor $\mathfrak{G}_{\text{epi}} \rightarrow \mathfrak{G}$. Is it a functor $\mathfrak{G}_{\text{epi}} \rightarrow \mathfrak{G}_{\text{epi}}$?
