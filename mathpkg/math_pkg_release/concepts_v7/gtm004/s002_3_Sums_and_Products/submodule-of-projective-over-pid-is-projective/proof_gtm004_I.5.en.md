# Proof: Over a PID Every Submodule of a Projective Module is Projective

Let $P$ be a projective $\Lambda$-module over a principal ideal domain $\Lambda$, and let $R$ be a submodule of $P$. By Corollary 5.2, $P$ is free (since projective modules over a PID are free). Thus $R$ is a submodule of a free module.

By Theorem 5.1, every submodule of a free module over a PID is free. Hence $R$ is free and therefore projective.
