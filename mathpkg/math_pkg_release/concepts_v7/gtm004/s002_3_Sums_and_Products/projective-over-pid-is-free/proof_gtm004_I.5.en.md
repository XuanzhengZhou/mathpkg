# Proof: Over a PID Every Projective Module is Free

Let $P$ be a projective $\Lambda$-module over a principal ideal domain $\Lambda$. By Theorem 4.7(5), every projective module is a direct summand in a free module; thus there exists a free module $F$ such that $F \cong P \oplus Q$ for some $\Lambda$-module $Q$.

Since $P$ is a submodule of the free module $F$, Theorem 5.1 implies that $P$ is free.
