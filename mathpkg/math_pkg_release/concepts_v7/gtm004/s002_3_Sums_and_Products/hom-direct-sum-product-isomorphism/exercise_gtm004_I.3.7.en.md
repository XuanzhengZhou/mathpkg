# Exercise 3.7

Show that the following statements about the exact sequence

$$0 \to A' \xrightarrow{\alpha'} A \xrightarrow{\alpha''} A'' \to 0$$

of $\Lambda$-modules are equivalent:

(i) there exists $\mu : A'' \to A$ with $\alpha'' \mu = 1$ on $A''$;

(ii) there exists $\varepsilon : A \to A'$ with $\varepsilon \alpha' = 1$ on $A'$;

(iii) $0 \to \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(B, A') \xrightarrow{\alpha'_*} \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(B, A) \xrightarrow{\alpha''_*} \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(B, A'') \to 0$ is exact for all $B$;

(iv) $0 \to \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A'', C) \xrightarrow{(\alpha'')^*} \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, C) \xrightarrow{(\alpha')^*} \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A', C) \to 0$ is exact for all $C$;

(v) there exists $\mu : A'' \to A$ such that $\langle \alpha', \mu \rangle : A' \oplus A'' \xrightarrow{\sim} A$.
