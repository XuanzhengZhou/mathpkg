The homotopy category of chain complexes is the quotient category obtained from the ordinary category of chain complexes by identifying chain maps that are homotopic. Since the homotopy relation $\simeq$ is an equivalence relation compatible with composition (Lemmas 3.2 and 3.3), this identification is well-defined. Additive functors descend to functors between homotopy categories (Lemma 3.4), and the homology functor $H$ factors through the homotopy category (Proposition 3.1), meaning homotopic maps cannot be distinguished by homology alone.
