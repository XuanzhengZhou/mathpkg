# Exercise 1.8

Prove Theorem 1.4 using explicit constructions of pullback and pushout. That is, given a representative $B \xrightarrow{\kappa} E \xrightarrow{\nu} A$ of an extension class, give explicit module-theoretic descriptions of the pullback $E^\alpha$ and the pushout $E_\beta$, and verify directly that the resulting constructions $(E^\alpha)_\beta$ and $(E_\beta)^\alpha$ yield equivalent extensions.
