# Exercise 1.1

Show that the following two extensions are nonequivalent:

\[
\mathbb{Z} \xrightarrow{\mu} \mathbb{Z} \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} \mathbb{Z}_3, \qquad
\mathbb{Z} \xrightarrow{\mu'} \mathbb{Z} \xrightarrow{\varepsilon'} \mathbb{Z}_3
\]

where $\mu = \mu'$ is multiplication by $3$, $\varepsilon(1) = 1 \pmod{3}$ and $\varepsilon'(1) = 2 \pmod{3}$.
