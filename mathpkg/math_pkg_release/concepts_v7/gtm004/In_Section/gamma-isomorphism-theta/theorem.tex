\textbf{Proposition 6.7.} In a special Rees system (6.9) the automorphism $\theta$ induces an isomorphism
$$\theta : \Gamma^- \xrightarrow{\cong} \Gamma^+,$$
where
\begin{align*}
\Gamma^- &= \ker \bar{\varphi} = \operatorname{im} \xi, \\
\Gamma^+ &= \operatorname{coker} \varphi = D / \operatorname{im} \varphi.
\end{align*}

\textit{Remark.} There is an isomorphism $\Gamma^- \cong \Gamma^+$ in any Rees system, even if it is not special (see Theorem 7.25 of [10]).
