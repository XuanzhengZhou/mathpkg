Theorem 5.2 states that taking limits over a tower of pullback squares preserves the pullback property. Starting from pullback squares relating $A_i$ and $B_i$, the limit yields a pullback square relating $A_{\infty}$ and $B_{\infty}$. This result is used in the proof of Theorem 5.3 to relate the limit of the spectral sequence to the original exact couple. The dual statement holds for colimits and pushouts.
