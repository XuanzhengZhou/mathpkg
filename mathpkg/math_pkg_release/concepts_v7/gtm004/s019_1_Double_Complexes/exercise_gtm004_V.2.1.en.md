# Exercise 2.1

Let $C$ be a resolution of $\mathbb{Z}_k$; thus $C_0 = F$, $C_1 = R$, $\partial_1$ is the inclusion, where $0 \to R \to F \to \mathbb{Z}_k \to 0$ is a presentation of $\mathbb{Z}_k$. Similarly let $D$ be a resolution of $\mathbb{Z}_l$. Compute $H(C \otimes D)$, $H(C \otimes D \otimes \mathbb{Z}_m)$.
