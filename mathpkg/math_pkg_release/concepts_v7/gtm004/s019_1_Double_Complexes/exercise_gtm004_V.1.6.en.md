# Exercise 1.6

Show that if $\{A_i\}$, $-\infty < i < +\infty$, is a graded algebra over the commutative ring $\Lambda$ then $A = \bigoplus_i A_i$ is an algebra over $\Lambda$. (We then call $A$ internally graded.)
