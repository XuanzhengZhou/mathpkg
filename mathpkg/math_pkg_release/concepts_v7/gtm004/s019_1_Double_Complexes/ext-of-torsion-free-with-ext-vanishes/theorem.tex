If $A'$ is a torsion-free abelian group, then for all abelian groups $A, A''$,
$$\operatorname{Ext}_{\mathbb{Z}}(A', \operatorname{Ext}_{\mathbb{Z}}(A, A'')) = 0.$$

More generally, for a torsion-free group $A'$, the composite functor $\operatorname{Ext}(A', \operatorname{Ext}(A, -))$ vanishes identically.
