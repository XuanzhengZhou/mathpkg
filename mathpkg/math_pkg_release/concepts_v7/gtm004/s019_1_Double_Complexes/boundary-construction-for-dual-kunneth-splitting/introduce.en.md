Lemma 3.2 is a technical result used in proving that the dual Künneth sequence splits when both complexes are free. It shows that a certain obstruction term $eta$, constructed from a family of homomorphisms between homology groups, is always a boundary in the Hom complex.

This lemma is the dual analogue of the construction used for the ordinary Künneth theorem, and it relies on the explicit splitting $C_p = Z_p \oplus Y_p$ available for free chain complexes over a principal ideal domain.
