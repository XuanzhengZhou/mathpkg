Theorem 1.1 establishes that the tensor product and Hom functors for chain complexes form an adjoint pair, extending the classical tensor-hom adjunction for modules to the category of chain complexes. This is the reason the second total complex $\operatorname{Tot}'$ (direct product) is used in defining the Hom complex: with $\operatorname{Tot}$, the adjunction would not hold.

This theorem is essential for deriving the associativity relations between the bifunctors $\otimes$, $\operatorname{Tor}$, $\operatorname{Hom}$, and $\operatorname{Ext}$ in Section 4.
