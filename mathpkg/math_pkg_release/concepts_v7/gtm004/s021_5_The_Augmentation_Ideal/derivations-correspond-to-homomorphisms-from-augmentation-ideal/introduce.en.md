Theorem 5.1 establishes a natural isomorphism between the abelian group of derivations $\operatorname{Der}(G,A)$ and the group of $G$-module homomorphisms from the augmentation ideal $IG$ to $A$. The isomorphism sends a homomorphism $\varphi$ to the derivation $y \mapsto \varphi(y-1)$, and its inverse sends a derivation $d$ to the map $y-1 \mapsto d(y)$. This correspondence is the key bridge that allows the first cohomology group $H^1(G,A)$ to be expressed in terms of derivations.
