# Exercise 5.2

Let $A \xrightarrow{i} E \xrightarrow{p} G$ be an exact sequence of groups with $A$ abelian. Let $s: G \to E$ be a one-sided inverse of $p$, $ps = 1_G$. Show that $E \cong A \rtimes G$.
