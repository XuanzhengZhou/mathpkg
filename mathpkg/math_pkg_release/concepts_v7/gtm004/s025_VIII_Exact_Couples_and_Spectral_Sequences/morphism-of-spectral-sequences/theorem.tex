Let $E = \{(E_n, d_n)\}$ and $E' = \{(E'_n, d'_n)\}$ be spectral sequences in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$. A \textbf{morphism} $\varphi: E \to E'$ of spectral sequences is a sequence of morphisms $\varphi_n: E_n \to E'_n$ of $\mathfrak{A}$ such that
$$H(\varphi_n) = \varphi_{n+1}, \quad n = 0, 1, \ldots$$
(where the differentials $d_n, d'_n$ are understood).
