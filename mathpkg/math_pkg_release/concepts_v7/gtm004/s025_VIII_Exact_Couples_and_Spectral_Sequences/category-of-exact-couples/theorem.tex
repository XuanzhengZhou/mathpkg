Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an abelian category. The \textbf{category of exact couples} in $\mathfrak{A}$, denoted $\mathfrak{EC}(\mathfrak{A})$ or briefly $\mathfrak{EC}$, has as objects exact couples $EC = \{D, E, \alpha, \beta, \gamma\}$ and as morphisms the morphisms of exact couples $\Phi = (\kappa, \lambda): EC \to EC'$.
