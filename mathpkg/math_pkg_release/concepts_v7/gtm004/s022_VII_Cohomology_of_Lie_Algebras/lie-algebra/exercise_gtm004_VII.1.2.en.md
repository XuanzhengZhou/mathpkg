# Exercise 1.2

Show that the following are examples of Lie algebras over $\mathbb{C}$, under a suitable bracket operation.

(a) The skew-hermitian $n \times n$ matrices over $\mathbb{C}$.

(b) The skew-hermitian $n \times n$ matrices over $\mathbb{C}$ with trace $0$.
