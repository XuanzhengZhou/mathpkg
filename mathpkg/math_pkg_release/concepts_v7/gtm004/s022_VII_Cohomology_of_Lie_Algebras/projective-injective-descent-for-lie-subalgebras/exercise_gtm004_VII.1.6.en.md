# Exercise 1.6

Prove Corollaries 1.5 and 1.6.

**Corollary 1.5.** Every $\mathfrak{g}$-projective (injective) module is $\mathfrak{h}$-projective (injective).

**Corollary 1.6.** If $\mathfrak{n} \rightarrowtail \mathfrak{g} \twoheadrightarrow \mathfrak{h}$ is an exact sequence of Lie algebras, then $K \otimes_{U\mathfrak{n}} U\mathfrak{g} \cong U\mathfrak{h}$ as right $\mathfrak{g}$-modules.
