This theorem provides the second fundamental long exact sequence for left derived functors, dual to Theorem 6.1. Whereas the first theorem varies the module argument while keeping the functor fixed, this result varies the functor through a short exact sequence of functors that is exact on projectives. Given a projective resolution of $A$, applying the three functors yields a short exact sequence of chain complexes, and the ordinary long exact homology sequence then produces the connecting homomorphisms $\omega_n$ and the exactness of the derived functor sequence.
