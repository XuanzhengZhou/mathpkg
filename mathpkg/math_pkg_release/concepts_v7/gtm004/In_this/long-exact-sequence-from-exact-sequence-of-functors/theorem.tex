Let $T' \xrightarrow{\tau'} T \xrightarrow{\tau''} T''$ be a sequence of additive covariant functors $\mathfrak{M}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$ which is exact on projectives. Then for any object $A$ in $\mathfrak{M}_A$, there exist connecting homomorphisms
$$\omega_n: L_n T'' A \rightarrow L_{n-1} T' A, \quad n = 1, 2, \ldots$$
such that the following sequence is exact:
$$\cdots \rightarrow L_n T' A \xrightarrow{L_n \tau'} L_n T A \xrightarrow{L_n \tau''} L_n T'' A \xrightarrow{\omega_n} L_{n-1} T' A \rightarrow \cdots$$
$$\cdots \rightarrow L_1 T'' A \xrightarrow{\omega_1} L_0 T' A \xrightarrow{L_0 \tau'} L_0 T A \xrightarrow{L_0 \tau''} L_0 T'' A \rightarrow 0.$$
