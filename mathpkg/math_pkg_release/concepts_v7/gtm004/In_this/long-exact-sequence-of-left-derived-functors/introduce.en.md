This theorem establishes the first fundamental long exact sequence for left derived functors. Given an additive covariant functor $T$ and a short exact sequence of objects, there exist connecting homomorphisms $\omega_n$ that link the derived functors into an infinite exact sequence. The construction uses the Horseshoe Lemma to lift the short exact sequence to a short exact sequence of projective resolutions, then applies the ordinary long exact homology sequence. This result shows that derived functors measure the failure of $T$ to be exact.
