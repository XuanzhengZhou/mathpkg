A morphism $\varphi : E \to E'$ of spectral sequences is a sequence of morphisms $\varphi_n : E_n \to E_n'$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ such that $H(\varphi_n) = \varphi_{n+1}$ for $n = 0, 1, \ldots$. The category of spectral sequences in $\mathfrak{A}$ is denoted $\mathfrak{E}(\mathfrak{A})$, or simply $\mathfrak{E}$.
