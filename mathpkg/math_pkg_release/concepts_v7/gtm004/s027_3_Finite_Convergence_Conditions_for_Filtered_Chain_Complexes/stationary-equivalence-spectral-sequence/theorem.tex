Let
\[
D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D \xrightarrow{\beta} E \xrightarrow{\gamma} D
\]
be an exact couple of bigraded objects with $\deg \alpha = (1,0)$, and let
\[
\bar{D} \xrightarrow{\bar{\alpha}} \bar{D} \xrightarrow{\bar{\beta}} E \xrightarrow{\bar{\gamma}} \bar{D}
\]
be the exact couple arising from the filtration, where $\bar{D}^{p,q} = H_q(C/C^{(p-1)})$, with $\deg \bar{\alpha} = (1,0)$, $\deg \bar{\beta} = (-1,-1)$, $\deg \bar{\gamma} = (0,0)$.

The following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $\alpha$ is positively stationary;
\item[(ii)] $E$ is negatively graded;
\item[(iii)] $\bar{\alpha}$ is positively stationary.
\end{itemize}

By symmetry, the dual statement interchanging \text{positive} and \text{negative} throughout also holds.
