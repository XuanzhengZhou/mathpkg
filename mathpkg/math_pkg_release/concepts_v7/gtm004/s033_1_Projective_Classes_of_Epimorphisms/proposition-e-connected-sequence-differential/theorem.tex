Let $T = \{T_j\}$ be an $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequence of functors. Then for each short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence
$$0 \rightarrow A' \rightarrow A \rightarrow A'' \rightarrow 0$$
in $\mathfrak{U}$, the sequence
$$\cdots \rightarrow T_j(A') \rightarrow T_j(A) \rightarrow T_j(A'') \xrightarrow{\omega_j} T_{j-1}(A') \rightarrow T_{j-1}(A) \rightarrow \cdots$$
is differential (i.e., the composite of any two consecutive morphisms is zero).
