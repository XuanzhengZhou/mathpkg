# Exercise 1.1

Prove that the class $\mathcal{E}_2$ in $\mathfrak{M}_\Lambda$ is closed.

(Recall that $\mathcal{E}_2$ is the class of epimorphisms $\varepsilon : B \to C$ in the category of $\Lambda$-modules such that the induced homomorphism $\varepsilon_* : \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(\Lambda, B) \to \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(\Lambda, C)$ is surjective.)
