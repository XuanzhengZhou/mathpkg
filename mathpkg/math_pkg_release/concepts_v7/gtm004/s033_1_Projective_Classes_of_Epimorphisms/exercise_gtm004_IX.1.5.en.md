# Exercise 1.5

Let $\gamma : \Lambda \to \Lambda'$ be a ring homomorphism. Let $U^\gamma : \mathfrak{M}_{\Lambda'} \to \mathfrak{M}_\Lambda$ be the change-of-rings functor (see Section IV.12). Define a class $\mathcal{E}'$ of epimorphisms in $\mathfrak{M}_{\Lambda'}$ as follows: The epimorphism $\varepsilon' : B' \to C'$ is in $\mathcal{E}'$ if, and only if, the homomorphism $U^\gamma \varepsilon' : U^\gamma B' \to U^\gamma C'$ in $\mathfrak{M}_\Lambda$ splits. Is the class $\mathcal{E}'$ projective?
