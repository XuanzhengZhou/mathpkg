# Exercise 2.2

Evaluate $L_m^{\mathcal{E}_3}(A \otimes -)$ for $\mathcal{E}_3$ the class of pure epimorphisms in the category of abelian groups. Show that the left $\mathcal{E}_3$-derived functors $L_n(A \otimes -)$ are trivial for $n \geq 1$.

(Compare with the text remark that for the projective class $\mathcal{E}_3$ of pure epimorphisms, $L_n^{\mathcal{E}_3}(A \otimes -)$ is trivial for $n \geq 1$.)
