Proposition 5.8 is the contravariant counterpart of Proposition 5.5, adapted to right derived functors. Given an exact sequence $K_q \rightarrow P_{q-1} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow P_0 \rightarrow A$ with projective $P_i$, and a left exact contravariant functor $S$, the sequence $SP_{q-1} \rightarrow SK_q \rightarrow R^q SA \rightarrow 0$ is exact. This result provides a method for computing the $q$-th right derived functor of a contravariant functor from a partial projective resolution. It plays the same role for right derived contravariant functors as Proposition 5.5 does for left derived covariant functors.
