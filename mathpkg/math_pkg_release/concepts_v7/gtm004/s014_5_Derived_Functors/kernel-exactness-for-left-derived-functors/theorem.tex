Let
$$K_q \xrightarrow{\mu} P_{q-1} \rightarrow P_{q-2} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow P_0 \rightarrow A$$
be an exact sequence of $\Lambda$-modules with $P_0, P_1, \ldots, P_{q-1}$ projective. If $T : \mathfrak{M}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$ is a right exact covariant functor and $q \geq 1$, then the sequence
$$0 \rightarrow L_q TA \rightarrow TK_q \xrightarrow{T(\mu)} TP_{q-1}$$
is exact.
