# Exercise 6.2

Let $\beta : \mathfrak{g} \times \mathfrak{g} \to K$ be the Killing form of the semi-simple Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ over the field $K$ of characteristic 0. Show that

$$f(x, y, z) = \beta([x, y], z), \quad x, y, z \in \mathfrak{g}$$

defines a 3-cocycle in $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathfrak{g}}(C, K)$, where $C$ denotes the resolution of Theorem 4.2. In fact (see [8, p. 113]), $f$ is not a coboundary. Deduce that $H^3(\mathfrak{g}, K) \neq 0$.
