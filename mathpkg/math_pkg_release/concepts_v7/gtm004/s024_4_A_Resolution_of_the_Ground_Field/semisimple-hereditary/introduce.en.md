Corollary 5.5 establishes that the class of semi-simple Lie algebras is closed under taking ideals and quotients. This follows from Corollary 5.4: an ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ has a complement $\mathfrak{b}$, so $\mathfrak{a} \cong \mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{b}$ and $\mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{a} \cong \mathfrak{b}$. Since $\mathfrak{b}$ and $\mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{b}$ are semi-simple (any abelian ideal would lift or project to an abelian ideal in $\mathfrak{g}$), both $\mathfrak{a}$ and $\mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{a}$ are semi-simple.
