Let $C$ be a filtered differential object with filtration $\{C^{(p)}\}$. From the short exact sequence

$$0 \rightarrow C^{(p-1)} \rightarrow C^{(p)} \rightarrow C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)} \rightarrow 0,$$

one obtains the homology exact triangle

$$H(C^{(p-1)}) \xrightarrow{\alpha} H(C^{(p)})$$
$$H(C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)}).$$

Define the graded objects $D$ and $E$ by

$$D^p = H(C^{(p)}), \qquad E^p = H(C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)}).$$

These graded objects form the terms of an exact couple associated to the filtered differential object.
