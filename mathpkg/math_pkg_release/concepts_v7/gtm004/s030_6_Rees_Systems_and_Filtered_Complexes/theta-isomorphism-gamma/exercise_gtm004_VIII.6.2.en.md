# Exercise 6.2

Interpret the relations $\bar{\varphi}\theta^{-1}\varphi = \bar{\gamma}\beta$ and $\bar{\varphi}_1\theta^{-1}\varphi_1 = \bar{\gamma}_1\beta_1$ for the Rees system of the triple $F(C)$.

*Hint.* In the filtered chain complex case, $\theta$ is the identity with degree $(1,0)$, and the morphisms $\beta$ and $\bar{\gamma}$ arise from the exact couples $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$. Express both sides as maps $D \to \bar{D}$ (respectively $D_1 \to \bar{D}_1$) and interpret them in terms of the filtration data: $D_{p,q} = H_q(C^{(p)})$, $\bar{D}_{p,q} = H_q(C/C^{(p-1)})$, $\Gamma_{p,q} = H_q(C)$.
