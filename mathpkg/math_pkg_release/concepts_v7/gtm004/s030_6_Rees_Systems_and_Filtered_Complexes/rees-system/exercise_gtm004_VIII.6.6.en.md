# Exercise 6.6

Formulate the ladder of a Rees system!

*Instructions.* Generalize the ladder construction of Section 4 (for exact couples) to the setting of a Rees system (6.9). Given a Rees system with couples $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$, morphisms $\varphi : D \to \Gamma$, $\bar{\varphi} : \Gamma \to \bar{D}$, and $\xi : \bar{D} \to D$, define the $n$-th derived Rees system and establish the ladder diagram connecting the successive derived systems. In particular, determine the bidegrees (6.25) of the morphisms in the $n$-th derived Rees system when it arises from a filtered chain complex $C$. What is the relation between the ladder of $\textcircled{1}$, the ladder of $\textcircled{2}$, and the ladder of the Rees system?
