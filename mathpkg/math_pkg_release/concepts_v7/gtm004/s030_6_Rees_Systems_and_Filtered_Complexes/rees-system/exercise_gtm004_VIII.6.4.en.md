# Exercise 6.4

Do the couples $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$ of (6.9) together contain all information necessary to determine if $E_{\infty} \cong \operatorname{Gr} \circ H(C)$?

*Discussion.* Consider what data appears in $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$ versus what appears in the full Rees system (6.9). In $\textcircled{1}$, only the filtered subcomplexes $C^{(p)}$ appear; the total complex $C$ itself is not present. In the Rees system, the morphisms $\varphi$ and $\bar{\varphi}$ connecting $D$ and $\bar{D}$ through $\Gamma$ encode the relationship between the filtration subobjects and the total object. Explain why this additional data is essential for comparing $E_{\infty}$ (a limit of the spectral sequence of $\textcircled{1}$) with $\operatorname{Gr} \circ H(C)$.
