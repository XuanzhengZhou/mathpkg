# Exercise 6

Let $G$ be a finite $p$-group. Show that if $|G| = p^n$, then

$$|H_2(G, \mathbb{Z})| \leq p^{\frac{n(n-1)}{2}}$$

and that this inequality is best possible. (Here $|G|$ denotes the order of the group $G$.)
