Let $F: \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ be a left exact additive functor and let $I$ be an injective resolution of $A$ in $\mathfrak{A}$,

$$I: I_0 \to I_1 \to I_2 \to \cdots.$$

Write $F_r = FI_r$ and display the cocycles and coboundaries of the cochain complex

$$F_0 \to F_1 \to F_2 \to \cdots$$

as

$$Z_0 \to F_0 \to B_1 \to Z_1 \to F_1 \to B_2 \to Z_2 \to F_2 \to \cdots.$$

Then this may be resolved as a commutative diagram

$$
\begin{array}{cccccccc}
Z_0 \to & F_0 \to & B_1 \to & Z_1 \to & F_1 \to & B_2 \to & \cdots \\
\downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \\
K_{0,0} \to & J_{0,0} \to & L_{1,0} \to & K_{1,0} \to & J_{1,0} \to & L_{2,0} \to & \cdots \\
\downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \\
K_{0,1} \to & J_{0,1} \to & L_{1,1} \to & K_{1,1} \to & J_{1,1} \to & L_{2,1} \to & \cdots \\
\downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow & \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots &
\end{array}
$$

where each column is an (augmented) injective resolution of the object appearing at its head, and

$$K_{r,s} \to J_{r,s} \to L_{r+1,s}$$

is exact for all $r, s$.
