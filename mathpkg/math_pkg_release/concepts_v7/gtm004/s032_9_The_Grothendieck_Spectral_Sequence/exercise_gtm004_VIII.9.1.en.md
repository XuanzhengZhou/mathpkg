# Exercise 1

Prove the functoriality of the Grothendieck spectral sequence with respect to the object $A$. That is, given a morphism $f : A \rightarrow A'$ in $\mathfrak{A}$, show that there is an induced morphism of spectral sequences $f_* : E_n(A) \rightarrow E_n(A')$ for all $n \geq 1$, compatible with the convergence to the derived functors $R^q(GF)(A)$ and $R^q(GF)(A')$.
