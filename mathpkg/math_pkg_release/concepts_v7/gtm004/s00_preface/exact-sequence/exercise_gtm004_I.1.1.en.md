# Exercise 1.1

Complete the proof of Lemma 1.1. Show moreover that, in the commutative diagram with exact rows

$$
\begin{array}{ccccccc}
0 & \longrightarrow & A' & \xrightarrow{\mu} & A & \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} & A'' & \longrightarrow & 0 \\
& & \downarrow \alpha' & & \downarrow \alpha & & \downarrow \alpha'' & & \\
0 & \longrightarrow & B' & \xrightarrow{\mu'} & B & \xrightarrow{\varepsilon'} & B'' & \longrightarrow & 0
\end{array}
$$

the following hold:

(a) If $\alpha'$ and $\alpha''$ are isomorphisms, then $\alpha$ is an isomorphism.

(b) If $\alpha'$ and $\alpha''$ are surjective, then $\alpha$ is surjective.

(c) If $\alpha'$ and $\alpha''$ are injective, then $\alpha$ is injective.
