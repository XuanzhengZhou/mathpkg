Corollary 7.4 gives a precise algebraic characterization of when the natural homomorphic relation $\Theta$ between the $E_\infty$ term and the limit object $\Gamma^+$ is an isomorphism. The vanishing of $\operatorname{coker}\bar{\alpha}'$ and $\ker\alpha''$ provides the exact obstruction. For filtered graded differential groups, this translates into concrete conditions on the induced maps on homology subgroups $I_p$ and $\bar{U}_p$, which generalize the homological finiteness condition (3.9) used earlier for convergence.
