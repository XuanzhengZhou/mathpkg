# Exercise 7.3

Apply Theorem 7.5 (the main theorem on conditions for the isomorphism $\Gamma^+ \cong E_\infty$) to filtered cochain complexes. Specifically, translate the conditions (7.8) into the language of cochain complexes (as opposed to chain complexes) and verify that the dual formulations yield the expected convergence criterion for cohomological spectral sequences.
