# Exercise 7.4

Let $m\mathfrak{Ab}$ be the full subcategory of $\mathfrak{Ab}$ consisting of those abelian groups $A$ such that $mA = 0$. Show that $m\mathfrak{Ab}$ admits kernels, cokernels, arbitrary products and arbitrary coproducts. Let $E: m\mathfrak{Ab} \to \mathfrak{Ab}$ be the embedding and let $F: \mathfrak{Ab} \to m\mathfrak{Ab}$ be given by $F(A) = A/mA$. Show that $F \dashv E$.
