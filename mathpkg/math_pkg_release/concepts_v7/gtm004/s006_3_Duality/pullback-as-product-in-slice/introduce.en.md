A pull-back in a category $\mathfrak{C}$ can be viewed as a product in the slice category $\mathfrak{C}/X$. This perspective unifies the notions of product and pull-back and allows properties of products to be transferred to pull-backs. The diagonal morphism $\Delta = \varphi\alpha = \psi\beta$ plays the role of the product object, with $\alpha$ and $\beta$ as the projections.
