Adjunctions lift pointwise to functor categories: if $F$ is left adjoint to $G$, then for any small category $\mathfrak{P}$, the induced functors $F^{\mathfrak{P}}$ and $G^{\mathfrak{P}}$ on the functor categories are also adjoint. This is a useful technical lemma that allows one to apply adjunction arguments componentwise.
