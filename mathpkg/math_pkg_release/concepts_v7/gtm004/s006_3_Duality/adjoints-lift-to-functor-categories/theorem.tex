If $F \dashv G$ where $F : \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{D}$ and $G : \mathfrak{D} \to \mathfrak{C}$, then for any small category $\mathfrak{P}$, the induced functors on functor categories also form an adjunction:
$$
F^{\mathfrak{P}} \dashv G^{\mathfrak{P}}, \qquad F^{\mathfrak{P}} : \mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{P}} \to \mathfrak{D}^{\mathfrak{P}}, \quad G^{\mathfrak{P}} : \mathfrak{D}^{\mathfrak{P}} \to \mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{P}}.
$$
