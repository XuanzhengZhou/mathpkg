# Exercise 9.5

Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an abelian category with arbitrary products and coproducts. Define the canonical sum-to-product morphism $\omega: \bigoplus_i A_i \to \prod_i A_i$, and prove that it is not true in general that $\omega$ is a monomorphism.
