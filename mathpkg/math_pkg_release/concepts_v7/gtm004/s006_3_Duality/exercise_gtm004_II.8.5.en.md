# Exercise 8.5

Show that if $S$ is a (commutative) semiring (i.e., $S$ satisfies all the ring axioms except for the existence of additive inverses), then $\operatorname{Gr}(S)$ acquires, in a natural way, the structure of a (commutative) ring.
