Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an abelian category, and let $A \xrightarrow{\beta} B \xrightarrow{\gamma} C$ be a diagram in $\mathfrak{A}$. Let $\varrho: C_1 \rightarrowtail C$ be a monomorphism and $\sigma: A \twoheadrightarrow A_1$ an epimorphism such that $\gamma\beta$ factors through both $\varrho$ and $\sigma$. Then

$$Q^{\varrho}Q_{\sigma}(\beta, \gamma) = Q_{\sigma}Q^{\varrho}(\beta, \gamma).$$

Moreover, the resulting object is

$$B_1 = \gamma^{-1}(C_1) / \beta\sigma^{-1}(0),$$

and both orders of applying the processes yield the same morphisms $\beta_1: A_1 \to B_1$ and $\gamma_1: B_1 \to C_1$.
