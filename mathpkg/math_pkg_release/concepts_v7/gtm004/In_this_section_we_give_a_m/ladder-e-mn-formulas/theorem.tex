In the ladder of an exact couple $\langle D, E; \alpha, \beta, \gamma \rangle$, define

$$\alpha^{\infty}D = \bigcap_{n} \alpha^{n}D, \quad \alpha^{-\infty}(0) = \bigcup_{m} \alpha^{-m}(0).$$

Then the terms of the ladder are given by the following formulas (in a concrete category):

$$E_{m,n} = \gamma^{-1}(\alpha^{n}D) / \beta\alpha^{-m}(0),$$

$$E_{m,\infty} = \lim_{\substack{\longrightarrow \\ n}} (E_{m,n}; \varrho_{m,n}) = \bigcap_{n} E_{m,n} = \gamma^{-1}(\alpha^{\infty}D) / \beta\alpha^{-m}(0),$$

$$E_{\infty,n} = \lim_{\substack{\longrightarrow \\ m}} (E_{m,n}; \sigma_{m,n}) = E_{m,n} / \bigcup_{k} \sigma^{-k}(0) = \gamma^{-1}(\alpha^{n}D) / \beta\alpha^{-\infty}(0),$$

$$E_{\infty} = \lim_{\substack{\longrightarrow \\ n}} \lim_{\substack{\longrightarrow \\ m}} (E_{m,n}; \varrho_{m,n}, \sigma_{m,n}) = \gamma^{-1}(\alpha^{\infty}D) / \beta\alpha^{-\infty}(0).$$
