The universal enveloping algebra transforms a Lie algebra into an associative algebra while preserving the Lie bracket as the commutator. It is the universal solution to the problem of representing a Lie algebra inside an associative algebra. The category of left $\mathfrak{g}$-modules is equivalent to the category of left $U\mathfrak{g}$-modules, allowing the full machinery of associative ring theory to be applied to Lie algebra representations.
