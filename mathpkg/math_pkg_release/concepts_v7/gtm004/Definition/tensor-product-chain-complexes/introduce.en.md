The tensor product $C \otimes_\Lambda D$ of two chain complexes is a double complex whose $(p,q)$-component is $C_p \otimes_\Lambda D_q$. The horizontal differential acts only on $C$ and the vertical differential acts only on $D$, with a sign $(-1)^p$ inserted in the vertical differential to ensure anticommutativity. The total complex of this double complex gives a chain complex whose homology is studied by the Künneth theorem.
