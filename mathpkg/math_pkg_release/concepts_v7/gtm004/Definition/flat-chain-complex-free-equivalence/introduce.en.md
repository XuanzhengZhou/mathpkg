Proposition 2.4 asserts that over a principal ideal domain, any flat chain complex is chain equivalent to a free chain complex. The proof constructs a free chain complex $F_p = \bigoplus H_p$ (free presentation of homology) and a chain map inducing an isomorphism on homology. This result is crucial for extending the Künneth theorem from the free case to the flat case, and for establishing that the splitting (though unnatural) holds in full generality.
