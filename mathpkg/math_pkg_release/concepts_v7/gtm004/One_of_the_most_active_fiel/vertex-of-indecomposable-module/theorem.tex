Let $G$ be a finite group, $k$ a field of characteristic $p$, and $M$ an indecomposable $kG$-module. Consider the class of subgroups $U \leq G$ with the property that $M$ is projective relative to $U$. The minimal elements of this class (with respect to inclusion) form a conjugacy class of $p$-subgroups of $G$. Any representative $V$ of this conjugacy class is called a \textbf{vertex} of $M$.
