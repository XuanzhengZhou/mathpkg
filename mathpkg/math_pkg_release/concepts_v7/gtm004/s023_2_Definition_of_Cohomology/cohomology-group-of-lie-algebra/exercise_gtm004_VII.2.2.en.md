# Exercise 2.2

List the properties of $H^n(\mathfrak{g}, A)$ and $H_n(\mathfrak{g}, B)$ analogous to the properties stated in Section VI.2 for the (co)homology of groups. In particular, state the functorial behavior, the long exact cohomology/homology sequences associated with short exact sequences of coefficients, the dimension-shifting properties, and the characterization of $H^0$ and $H_0$.
