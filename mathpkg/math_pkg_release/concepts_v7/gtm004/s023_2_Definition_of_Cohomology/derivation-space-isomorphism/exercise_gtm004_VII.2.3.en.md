# Exercise 2.3

Regard $\mathfrak{g}$ as a $\mathfrak{g}$-module via the adjoint action. Show that $\operatorname{Der}(\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g})$ has the structure of a Lie algebra, where the Lie bracket of two derivations $d_1, d_2 \in \operatorname{Der}(\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g})$ is defined by the commutator
$$[d_1, d_2] = d_1 \circ d_2 - d_2 \circ d_1.$$
Verify that the Jacobi identity for this bracket follows from the Jacobi identity in $\mathfrak{g}$.
