# Exercise 9.4

Define addition in $\operatorname{Yext}_A^n(A, B)$ directly in terms of $n$-extensions, independently of the equivalence $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n \cong \operatorname{Yext}_A^n$ established in Theorem 9.1.

(a) Given two $n$-extensions $E_1, E_2$ of $A$ by $B$, construct their sum $E_1 + E_2$ (hint: use the diagonal map $A \rightarrow A \oplus A$ and the codiagonal map $B \oplus B \rightarrow B$, together with the direct sum of extensions).

(b) Describe a representative of the zero element $0 \in \operatorname{Yext}_A^m(A, B)$ for $m \geq 2$. Show that $\xi + 0 = \xi$ for all $\xi \in \operatorname{Yext}_A^m(A, B)$.

(c) Show that the addition defined in this way coincides, via the natural equivalence $\theta$ of Theorem 9.1, with the addition in $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B)$.
