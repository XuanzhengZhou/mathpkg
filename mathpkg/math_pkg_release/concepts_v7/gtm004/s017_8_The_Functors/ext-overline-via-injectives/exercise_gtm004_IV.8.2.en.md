# Exercise 8.2

Show that the category of torsion abelian groups has enough injectives but no non-zero projectives.

*Hint.* Every torsion abelian group is a direct limit of finite abelian groups. For injectives: show that a torsion divisible group is injective in this category, and that every torsion group embeds into a torsion divisible group. For projectives: show that any non-zero torsion group admits an epimorphism from a torsion group that does not split.
