# Exercise 1.1

Let $A$ be a ring with unit and let $U(A)$ be the set of units of $A$. Show that $U$ is a functor $\mathfrak{R}_1 \to \mathfrak{G}$ from rings with unity to groups, and that $U$ is right adjoint to the group ring functor $\mathbb{Z}(\cdot)$. Deduce that if $G$ is the free product of the groups $G_1$ and $G_2$, then $\mathbb{Z}G$ is the coproduct of $\mathbb{Z}G_1$ and $\mathbb{Z}G_2$ in the category of rings with unity.
