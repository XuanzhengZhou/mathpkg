Lemma 9.4 provides the technical construction needed for the proof of the Grothendieck spectral sequence. It resolves the complex $FI$ by injective objects in the category of cochain complexes, producing a double complex whose two associated spectral sequences can be separately analyzed. One spectral sequence collapses to $R^q(GF)(A)$ and the other yields $R^p G \circ R^{q-p} F(A)$ on the $E_1$ page.
