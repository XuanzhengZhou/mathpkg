Let $C : C_0 \to C_1 \to \cdots$ be an acyclic cochain complex and $D : D_0 \to D_1 \to \cdots$ an injective cochain complex. Then, for every homomorphism $\varphi : H^0(C) \to H^0(D)$, there exists a cochain map $\Phi : C \to D$ inducing $\varphi$. Moreover, any two cochain maps inducing $\varphi$ are homotopic.
