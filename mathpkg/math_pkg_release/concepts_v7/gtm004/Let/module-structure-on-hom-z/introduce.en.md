Using the module structure on $A$, one can endow the abelian group of $\mathbb{Z}$-linear maps $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(A, G)$ with a $\Lambda$-module structure. This construction is fundamental for defining cofree modules and establishing the existence of enough injectives.
