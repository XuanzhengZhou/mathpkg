Homotopy provides a sufficient condition for two chain maps to induce the same map in homology. The relation $\varphi \simeq \psi$ implies $H(\varphi) = H(\psi)$. The concept is fundamental: the existence of a contracting homotopy proves a complex is exact, and homotopy invariance underlies the well-definedness of derived functors.
