Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a closed class of epimorphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$. A morphism $\varphi$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ is \textit{$\mathcal{E}$-admissible} if, in the canonical factorization $\varphi = \mu \varepsilon$ with $\mu$ monic and $\varepsilon$ epic, we have $\varepsilon \in \mathcal{E}$. An exact sequence in $\mathfrak{A}$ is \textit{$\mathcal{E}$-exact} if all its morphisms are $\mathcal{E}$-admissible.
