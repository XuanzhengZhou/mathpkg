An additive functor $T : \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ between abelian categories is called \emph{right $\mathcal{E}$-exact} if, for every $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence
$$A' \longrightarrow A \longrightarrow A'' \longrightarrow 0,$$
the induced sequence
$$T(A') \longrightarrow T(A) \longrightarrow T(A'') \longrightarrow 0$$
is exact in $\mathfrak{B}$.
