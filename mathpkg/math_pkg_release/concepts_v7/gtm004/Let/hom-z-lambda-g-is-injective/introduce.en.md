For any divisible abelian group $G$, the coinduced module $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\Lambda, G)$ is injective. This theorem provides an abundant supply of injective $\Lambda$-modules and, together with Theorem 8.4, guarantees that every module can be embedded in an injective module.
