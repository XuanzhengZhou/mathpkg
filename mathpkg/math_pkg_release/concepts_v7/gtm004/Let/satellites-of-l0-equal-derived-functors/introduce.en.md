This corollary shows that all higher derived functors can be recovered as satellites of the zeroth derived functor. Since $L_0^{\mathcal{E}} H(P) = H(P)$ for $\mathcal{E}$-projectives $P$, the satellites of $L_0 H$ coincide with the derived functors of $H$. This provides an alternative construction of derived functors via the satellite formalism.
