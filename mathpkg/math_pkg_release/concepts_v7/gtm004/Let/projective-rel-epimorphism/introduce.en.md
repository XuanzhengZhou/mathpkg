This definition relativizes the notion of projectivity: instead of requiring the lifting property for all epimorphisms, one restricts to a specified class $\mathcal{E}$. This allows homological algebra to be developed in contexts where there are not enough projectives in the absolute sense, by choosing an appropriate projective class.
