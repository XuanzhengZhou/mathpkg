# Exercise 7.2

Suppose given the exact sequence

$$0 \to K_q \to P_{q-1} \to \cdots \to P_0 \to A \to 0$$

with $P_0, \ldots, P_{q-1}$ projective. Prove that the sequence

$$\operatorname{Hom}_A(P_{q-1}, B) \to \operatorname{Hom}_A(K_q, B) \to \operatorname{Ext}_A^q(A, B) \to 0$$

is exact.
