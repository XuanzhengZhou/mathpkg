# Exercise 7.4

Show that $\omega: \operatorname{Hom}_A(A, B'') \to \operatorname{Ext}_A^1(A, B')$ factors through $\Pi P(A, B'')$ (Exercise 5.7) and deduce that

$$\Pi P(A, B') \to \Pi P(A, B) \to \Pi P(A, B'') \to \operatorname{Ext}_A^1(A, B') \to \operatorname{Ext}_A^1(A, B) \to \cdots$$

is exact. What does this tell us about left derived functors of $\Pi P(A, -)$?
