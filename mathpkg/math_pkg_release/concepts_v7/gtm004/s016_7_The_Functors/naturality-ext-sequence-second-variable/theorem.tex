Let $\alpha : A \to A'$ be a homomorphism of $A$-modules and let

$$\begin{CD}
B' @>>> B @>>> B'' \\
@VVV @VV{\psi'}V @VV{\psi''}V \\
\bar{B}' @>>> \bar{B} @>>> \bar{B}''
\end{CD}$$

be a commutative diagram with short exact rows. Then the following diagram commutes:

$$\begin{CD}
\cdots @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A', B') @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A', B) @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A', B'') @>{\omega_n}>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^{n+1}(A', B') @>>> \cdots \\
@. @VV{\alpha^*}V @VV{\alpha^*}V @VV{\alpha^*}V @VV{\alpha^*}V @. \\
\cdots @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B') @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B) @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B'') @>{\omega_n}>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^{n+1}(A, B') @>>> \cdots \\
@. @VV{\psi'_*}V @VV{\psi_*}V @VV{\psi''_*}V @VV{\psi'_*}V @. \\
\cdots @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, \bar{B}') @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, \bar{B}) @>>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, \bar{B}'') @>{\omega_n}>> \operatorname{Ext}_A^{n+1}(A, \bar{B}') @>>> \cdots
\end{CD}$$

That is, the long exact Ext-sequence in the second variable is natural with respect to morphisms in both variables.
