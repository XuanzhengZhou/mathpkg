Let $\mathfrak{B}$ be an abelian category. A \textbf{filtered object} in $\mathfrak{B}$ is an object $B$ of $\mathfrak{B}$ together with a family of subobjects
$$
\cdots \subseteq B^{(p-1)} \subseteq B^{(p)} \subseteq \cdots \subseteq B, \quad -\infty < p < \infty.
$$
A morphism $\varphi : B \to B'$ of filtered objects is a morphism in $\mathfrak{B}$ such that $\varphi(B^{(p)}) \subseteq B'^{(p)}$ for all $p$. The category of filtered objects in $\mathfrak{B}$ is denoted by $(\mathfrak{B}, f)$.
