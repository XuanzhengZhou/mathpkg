For a filtered differential object, each inclusion of successive filtration layers $C^{(p-1)} \subseteq C^{(p)}$ yields a short exact sequence of differential objects. Applying the homology functor produces an exact triangle connecting $H(C^{(p-1)})$, $H(C^{(p)})$, and $H(C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)})$. This homology exact triangle is the fundamental building block that, when assembled across all filtration degrees $p$, gives rise to an exact couple and ultimately a spectral sequence.
