This proposition asserts that the two natural transformations on Ext groups induced by the change-of-rings functor $U^\gamma$ — one constructed via the left adjoint $F$ and the other via the right adjoint $\bar{F}$ — are in fact the same map. The proof relies on the Yoneda description of Ext and shows that both constructions yield the same element at the level of $n$-fold extensions.
