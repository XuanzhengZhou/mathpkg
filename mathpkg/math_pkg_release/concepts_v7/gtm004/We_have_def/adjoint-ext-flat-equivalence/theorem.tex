Let $\gamma : \Lambda \rightarrow \Lambda'$ be a ring homomorphism and let $U^\gamma : \mathfrak{M}_{\Lambda'} \rightarrow \mathfrak{M}_\Lambda$ be the change-of-rings functor. Let $F : \mathfrak{M}_\Lambda \rightarrow \mathfrak{M}_{\Lambda'}$ be the left adjoint given by $F(A) = A' \otimes_\Lambda A$, where $A'$ is regarded as a right $\Lambda$-module via $\gamma$. If $A'$ is flat as a right $\Lambda$-module via $\gamma$, then

$$\Phi : \operatorname{Ext}_{\Lambda'}^n(A' \otimes_\Lambda A, B') \xrightarrow{\cong} \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^n(A, U^\gamma B')$$

is a natural equivalence for all $n \geq 0$.
