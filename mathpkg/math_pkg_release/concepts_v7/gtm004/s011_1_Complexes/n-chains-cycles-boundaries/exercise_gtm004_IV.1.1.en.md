# Exercise 1.1

Show that if $C$ is a chain complex of abelian groups in which each $C_n$ is free, then $Z_n = \ker \partial_n$ and $B_n = \operatorname{im} \partial_{n+1}$ are also free, and that $Z_n$ is a direct summand in $C_n$.
