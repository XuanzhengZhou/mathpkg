# Exercise 8.3

For any $\Lambda$-module $A$, let $A^*$ be the right $\Lambda$-module $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(A, \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z})$. Show that $A$ is naturally embedded in $A^{**} = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(A^*, \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z})$.

*Hint:* Apply Proposition 8.1 twice. The canonical evaluation map $A \rightarrow A^{**}$ is given by $a \mapsto (\varphi \mapsto \varphi(a))$. Show that this is a monomorphism of $\Lambda$-modules by reducing to the abelian group case.
