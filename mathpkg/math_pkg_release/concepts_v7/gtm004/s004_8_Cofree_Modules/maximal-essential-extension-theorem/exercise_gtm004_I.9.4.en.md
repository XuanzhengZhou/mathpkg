# Exercise 9.4

Give a procedure for calculating the maximal essential extension of $A$ in $B$, where $B$ is a finitely generated abelian group.

*Hint:* Use the structure theorem for finitely generated abelian groups. The maximal essential extension can be constructed by saturating $A$ within $B$; an element of $B$ belongs to the maximal essential extension of $A$ if and only if some nonzero multiple of it lies in $A$.
