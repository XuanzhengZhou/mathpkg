Theorem 8.3 establishes the long exact sequences for the Tor functor, analogous to the Hom-Ext sequences of Section 5. Given a short exact sequence in either the first or second argument, Tor fits into a six-term exact sequence that connects Tor groups with tensor products. The proof applies Lemma 5.1 (the kernel-cokernel exact sequence) to the diagram obtained by tensoring a projective presentation with the given short exact sequence. Unlike the Hom-Ext case, there is only one type of sequence since $A$ and $B$ play symmetric roles in $\operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B)$.
