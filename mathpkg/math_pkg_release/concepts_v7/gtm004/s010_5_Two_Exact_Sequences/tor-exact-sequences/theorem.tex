Let $A$ be a right $\Lambda$-module and let $B' \xrightarrow{\kappa} B \xrightarrow{\lambda} B''$ be a short exact sequence of left $\Lambda$-modules. Then there exists a connecting homomorphism $\omega : \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B'') \to A \otimes_\Lambda B'$ such that the following sequence is exact:
$$
\operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B') \xrightarrow{\kappa_*} \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B) \xrightarrow{\lambda_*} \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B'') \xrightarrow{\omega} A \otimes_\Lambda B' \xrightarrow{\kappa_*} A \otimes_\Lambda B \xrightarrow{\lambda_*} A \otimes_\Lambda B'' \to 0.
$$
Similarly, if $A' \xrightarrow{\kappa} A \xrightarrow{\lambda} A''$ is a short exact sequence of right $\Lambda$-modules, there is a connecting homomorphism $\omega : \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A'', B) \to A' \otimes_\Lambda B$ with an analogous exact sequence.
