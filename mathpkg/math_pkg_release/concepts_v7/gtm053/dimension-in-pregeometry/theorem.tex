In a pregeometry $(M, \operatorname{cl})$, a set $U \subseteq M$ is \text{independent} if $u \notin \operatorname{cl}(U \setminus \{u\})$ for every $u \in U$. A \text{basis} is a maximal independent subset of $M$. All bases have the same cardinality, called the \text{dimension} of $M$. The dimension of $X \subseteq M$ is the cardinality of a basis of $\operatorname{cl}(X)$.
