Let $G$ be a group and $\alpha, \beta : A \to G$ be embeddings of a subgroup $A$. The \text{HNN extension} of $G$ relative to $\alpha, \beta$ is
$$G *_A = \langle G \cup \{t\} : t^{-1}\alpha(a)t = \beta(a) \text{ for all } a \in A \rangle.$$
More generally, for a family $\alpha_i, \beta_i : A_i \to G$ ($i \in I$) and a subgroup $H \subset G$, with $B_i = \alpha_i^{-1}(\alpha_i(A_i) \cap H) = \beta_i^{-1}(\beta_i(A_i) \cap H)$:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] The stable letters $\{t_i\}$ freely generate a free subgroup.
\item[(b)] The natural maps $G \to K_G$ and $K_H \to K_G$ are embeddings, and $K_H \cap G = H$ in $K_G$.
\end{itemize}
