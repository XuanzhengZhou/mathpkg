# Exercise 1.4 (iii)

An isomorphism $h : A \rightarrow B$ between $L$-structures preserves any $L$-formula $P(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$, i.e., for any $\bar{a} \in A^n$,

$$A \vDash P(\bar{a}) \quad \text{iff} \quad B \vDash P(h(\bar{a})).$$
