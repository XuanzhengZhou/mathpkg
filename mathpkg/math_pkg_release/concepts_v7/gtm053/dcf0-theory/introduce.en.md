DCF$_0$ is a central example in stability theory where the geometry is nontrivial. The solution space of a differential equation of order $n$ has Morley rank $n$. The trichotomy principle applies: strongly minimal sets in DCF$_0$ are either locally modular (related to the constants) or interpret a field. This theory provides a model-theoretic framework for differential algebra.
