# Exercise 1.4 (ii)

Given an $\forall$-formula $P(\bar{a})$, that is, one of the form

$$\forall x_1 \cdots \forall x_m \; Q(x_1, \ldots, x_m, \bar{a})$$

with $Q$ quantifier-free, and an embedding $h : A \rightarrow B$, $\bar{a}$ in $A$,

$$B \vDash P(\bar{a}) \quad \text{implies} \quad A \vDash P(h(\bar{a})).$$
