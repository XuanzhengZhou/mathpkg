The theory $\mathrm{ACF}_p$ of algebraically closed fields of characteristic $p > 0$ is given in the language $L_1\mathrm{Ar} = \{+, \cdot, 0, 1\}$ by:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(I)] Axioms of fields.
\item[(II)] Algebraic closedness: for each $n \in \mathbf{N}$, $n > 0$,
$$\forall y_1, \ldots, y_n \exists x \; x^n + y_1 x^{n-1} + \cdots + y_n = 0.$$
\item[(III)] Characteristic $p$: $\underbrace{1 + \cdots + 1}_{p} = 0.$
\end{enumerate}
The theory $\mathrm{ACF}_0$ of algebraically closed fields of characteristic zero consists of axioms (I), (II), and the negations of (III) for all primes $p$:
$$\neg \underbrace{1 + \cdots + 1}_{p} = 0.$$
