This is Theorem 143 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

At least one relation must hold for all ideals $a$ of the field which are coprime to $d$, namely,

$$\prod_{i=1}^{t} \gamma_i^{c_i}(a) = 1,$$

where the rational integers $c_i$ are independent of $a$ and are not all divisible by 2.

Thus for $t = 1$ the equation

$$\gamma_1(a) = \chi_1(|N(a)|) = 1$$
