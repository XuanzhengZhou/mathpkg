A residue character modulo $n$ is a Dirichlet character: a completely multiplicative function on integers, periodic modulo $n$, nonzero exactly on numbers coprime to $n$. These correspond to characters of $\mathfrak{R}(n)$ and are fundamental to Dirichlet L-functions.
