Every element of $K(	heta)$ is uniquely expressible as a polynomial in $	heta$ of degree at most $n-1$ with coefficients in $k$. Thus $K(	heta)$ is an $n$-dimensional vector space over $k$, and $\{1, 	heta, \ldots, 	heta^{n-1}\}$ is a basis.
