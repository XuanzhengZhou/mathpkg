This is Theorem 65 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

Every ideal $a$ can be written in the form $(\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_r)$ with the $\alpha$ suitably chosen integers in $K$. Moreover, we may even take $r \leq n$.
