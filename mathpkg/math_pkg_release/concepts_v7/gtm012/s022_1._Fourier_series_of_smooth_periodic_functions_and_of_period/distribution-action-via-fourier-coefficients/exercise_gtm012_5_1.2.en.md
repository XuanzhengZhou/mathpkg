# Exercise 2

If $F \in \mathcal{P}'$ has Fourier coefficients $(a_n)_{-\infty}^{\infty}$, compute the Fourier coefficients of

$$T_t F, \quad F^*, \quad F^{-},$$

where $T_t F$ denotes translation by $t$, $F^*$ denotes the conjugate distribution, and $F^{-}$ denotes the reflection (reversal) of $F$.
