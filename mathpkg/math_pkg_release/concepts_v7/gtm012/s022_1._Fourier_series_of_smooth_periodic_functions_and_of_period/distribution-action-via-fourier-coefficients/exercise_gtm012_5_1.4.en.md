# Exercise 4

Suppose $(\varphi_m)_{1}^{\infty} \subset \mathcal{C}$ is an approximate identity. Let

$$(a_{m,n})_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}$$

be the sequence of Fourier coefficients of $\varphi_m$. Show that

$$|a_{m,n}| \leq 1, \quad m \geq 1, \quad n = 0, \pm 1, \pm 2, \ldots;$$

$$\lim_{m \to \infty} a_{m,n} = 1, \quad \text{all } n.$$
