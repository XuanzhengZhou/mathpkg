\textbf{Definition.} For a bounded real sequence $(x_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$, define:
\begin{align}
x_n &= \sup\{x_k \mid k \geq n\}, \\
x_n &= \inf\{x_k \mid k \geq n\}.
\end{align}
Then:
\[
\limsup_{n \to \infty} x_n = \lim_{n \to \infty} x_n, \qquad
\liminf_{n \to \infty} x_n = \lim_{n \to \infty} x_n.
\]
