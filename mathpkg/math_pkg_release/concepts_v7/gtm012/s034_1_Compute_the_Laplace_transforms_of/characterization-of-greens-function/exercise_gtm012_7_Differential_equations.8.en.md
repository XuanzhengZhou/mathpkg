# Exercise 8

Show that any solution of

$$p(D)u(t) = 0, \qquad t > 0$$

is a linear combination of functions $t^k \exp(z_j t)$, where $z_1, \ldots, z_r$ are the roots of $p$ with multiplicities $m_1, \ldots, m_r$, and $0 \leq k < m_j$.
