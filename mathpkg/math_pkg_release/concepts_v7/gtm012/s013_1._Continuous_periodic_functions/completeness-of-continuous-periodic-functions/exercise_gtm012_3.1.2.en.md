# Exercise 2

Suppose $u: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{C}$ is a continuous function and suppose there is a constant $M$ such that $u(x) = 0$ if $|x| \geq M$. Show that for any $x$ the series

$$v(x) = \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} u(x + 2n\pi)$$

converges. Show that the function $v$ is in $\mathcal{C}$.
