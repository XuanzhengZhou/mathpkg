# Exercise 1

Prove the identities (2), (3), (4) from Proposition 7.1:

(2) $(aF) * u = a(F * u) = F * (au)$, for $F \in \mathcal{P}'$, $u \in \mathcal{P}$, $a \in \mathbb{C}$.

(3) $(F + G) * u = F * u + G * u$, for $F, G \in \mathcal{P}'$, $u \in \mathcal{P}$.

(4) $F * (u + v) = F * u + F * v$, for $F \in \mathcal{P}'$, $u, v \in \mathcal{P}$.

These identities follow directly from the definition $(F * u)(x) = F(T_x \tilde{u})$ and the linearity of $F$.
