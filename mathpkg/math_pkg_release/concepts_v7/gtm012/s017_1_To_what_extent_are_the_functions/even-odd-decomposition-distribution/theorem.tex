Any periodic distribution $F \in \mathcal{P}'$ can be decomposed into its \textbf{even} and \textbf{odd} parts:
$$F = G + H,$$
where
$$G = \tfrac{1}{2}(F + F^\sim) \quad \text{is even}, \qquad H = \tfrac{1}{2}(F - F^\sim) \quad \text{is odd}.$$
Here $F^\sim$ is the reversal of $F$, defined by $F^\sim(u) = F(\tilde{u})$ with $\tilde{u}(x) = u(-x)$.
