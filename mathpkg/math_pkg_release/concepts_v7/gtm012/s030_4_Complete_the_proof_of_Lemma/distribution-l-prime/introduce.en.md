A distribution is an element of the continuous dual space $\mathcal{L}'$ of the Schwartz space $\mathcal{L}$. Distributions generalize the notion of a function: every sufficiently nice function defines a distribution via integration, but there also exist distributions (such as the Dirac delta) that do not correspond to ordinary functions. Distributions can be differentiated, translated, and conjugated.
