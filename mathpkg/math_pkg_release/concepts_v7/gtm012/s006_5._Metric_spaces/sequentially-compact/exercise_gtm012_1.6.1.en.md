# Exercise 6.1

Suppose $(x_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ is a sequence in a metric space $(S, d)$ which converges to $x \in S$. Let $A = \{x\} \cup \{x_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}$. Then $A$ is compact and sequentially compact.
