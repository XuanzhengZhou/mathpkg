# Exercise 7.3

If $X$ is a finite-dimensional vector space and $Y \subset X$ is a subspace, then $Y$ is finite-dimensional. Moreover, $\dim Y \leq \dim X$, and $\dim Y < \dim X$ unless $Y = X$.
