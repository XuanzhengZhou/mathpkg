# Exercise 7.11

If $X$ and $Y$ are vector spaces and $T: X \to Y$ is a linear transformation, then the sets

$$N(T) = \{x \mid x \in X, T(x) = 0\}$$

$$R(T) = \{T(x) \mid x \in X\}$$

are subspaces of $X$ and $Y$, respectively.
