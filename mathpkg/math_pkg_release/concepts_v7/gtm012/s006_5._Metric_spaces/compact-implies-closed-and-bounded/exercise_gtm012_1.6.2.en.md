# Exercise 6.2

Let $\mathbb{Q}$, the rationals, have the usual metric. Let $A = \{x \mid x \in \mathbb{Q}, x^2 < 2\}$. Then $A$ is bounded, and is closed as a subset of $\mathbb{Q}$, but is not compact.
