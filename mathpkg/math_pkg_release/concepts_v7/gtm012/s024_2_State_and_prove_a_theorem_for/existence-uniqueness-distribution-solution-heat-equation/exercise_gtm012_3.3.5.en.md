# Exercise 5

Let

$$g_t(x) = \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} \exp \left(-n^2 t + inx\right), \quad t > 0, \quad x \in \mathbb{R}.$$

Show that $g_t \in \mathcal{P}$. In Theorem 3.1, show that

$$u_t = G * g_t.$$
