# Exercise 4

Suppose $(\varphi_m)_{m=1}^{\infty} \subset \mathcal{C}$ is an approximate identity in the algebra of continuous periodic functions. Let

$$(a_{m,n})_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}$$

be the sequence of Fourier coefficients of $\varphi_m$. Prove the following properties:

(a) $|a_{m,n}| \leq 1$ for all $m \geq 1$ and all $n = 0, \pm 1, \pm 2, \ldots$;

(b) $\displaystyle\lim_{m \to \infty} a_{m,n} = 1$ for every $n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
