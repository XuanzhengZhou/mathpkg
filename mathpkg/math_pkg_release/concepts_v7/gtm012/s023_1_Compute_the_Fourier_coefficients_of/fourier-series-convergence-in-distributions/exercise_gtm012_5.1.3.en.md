# Exercise 3

Let $F \in \mathcal{P}'$ have Fourier coefficients $(a_n)_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}$. Give necessary and sufficient conditions on the sequence $(a_n)$ for $F$ to be:

(a) Real (i.e., $F(\varphi) \in \mathbb{R}$ whenever $\varphi$ is real-valued);

(b) Even (i.e., $F = F^-$, where $F^-$ is the reflection defined in Exercise 2);

(c) Odd (i.e., $F = -F^-$).
