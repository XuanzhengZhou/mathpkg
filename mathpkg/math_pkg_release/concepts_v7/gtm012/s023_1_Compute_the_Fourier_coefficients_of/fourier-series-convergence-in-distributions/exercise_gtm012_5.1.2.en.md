# Exercise 2

Let $F \in \mathcal{P}'$ have Fourier coefficients $(a_n)_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}$. Compute the Fourier coefficients of the following distributions in terms of $(a_n)$:

(a) The translate $T_t F$, defined by $(T_t F)(u) = F(T_{-t} u)$;

(b) The conjugate (or adjoint) $F^*$, defined by $F^*(u) = \overline{F(\bar{u})}$;

(c) The reflection $F^-$, defined by $F^-(u) = F(u^-)$ where $u^-(x) = u(-x)$.
