# Exercise 1

Suppose $\mathbf{H}$ and $\mathbf{H}_1$ are two infinite-dimensional separable complex Hilbert spaces. Use Theorems 4.2, 4.3, and 5.1 to show that there is a linear transformation $U$ from $\mathbf{H}$ onto $\mathbf{H}_1$ such that

$$(U\mathbf{u}, U\mathbf{v}) = (\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}), \quad \text{all } \mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v} \in \mathbf{H}.$$
