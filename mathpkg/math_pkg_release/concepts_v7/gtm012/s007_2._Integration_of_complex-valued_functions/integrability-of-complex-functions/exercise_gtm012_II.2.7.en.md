# Exercise 7

Do Exercise 6 without the assumption that \(f\) is real-valued.

*Hint:* Write \(f = u + iv\) with \(u, v\) real. If \(z = \int_a^b f = re^{i\theta}\), consider \(e^{-i\theta}f\) and use the fact that \(|z| = \operatorname{Re}(e^{-i\theta}z)\).
