# Exercise 8

Let \(f: [0, 1] \to \mathbb{C}\) be defined by: \(f(x) = 0\) if \(x = 0\) or \(x\) is irrational, \(f(x) = 1/q\) if \(x = p/q\) where \(p, q\) are integers with no common factor and \(q > 0\). Show that \(f\) is Riemann integrable and \(\int_0^1 f = 0\).
