Although the field axioms only assert the existence of identity elements and inverses, their uniqueness follows from the axioms together with the group properties of addition and multiplication. The text notes this fact and leaves the proofs as an exercise. This lemma is foundational: it justifies speaking of "the" additive identity, "the" multiplicative identity, and "the" inverse of a given element.
