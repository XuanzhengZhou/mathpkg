# Exercise 9

A set $\Omega \subset \mathbb{C}$ is connected if for any points $z_0, z_1 \in \Omega$ there is a (continuous) curve $\gamma: [a, b] \to \Omega$ with $\gamma(a) = z_0$, $\gamma(b) = z_1$. Suppose $\Omega$ is open and connected, and suppose $f$ is holomorphic in $\Omega$. Show that if $f$ is identically zero in any nonempty open subset $\Omega_1 \subset \Omega$, then $f \equiv 0$ in $\Omega$.
