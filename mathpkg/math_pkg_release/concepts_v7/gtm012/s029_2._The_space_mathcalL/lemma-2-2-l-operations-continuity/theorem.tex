The operations of differentiation, translation, complex conjugation, and integration are continuous from $\mathcal{L}$ to $\mathcal{L}$ with respect to convergence in the sense of $\mathcal{L}$. Moreover, if $u \in \mathcal{L}$, then the difference quotient converges to the derivative:

$$s^{-1}(T_{-s}u - u) \to Du \quad (\mathcal{L})$$

as $s \to 0$, where $(T_s u)(t) = u(t - s)$.
