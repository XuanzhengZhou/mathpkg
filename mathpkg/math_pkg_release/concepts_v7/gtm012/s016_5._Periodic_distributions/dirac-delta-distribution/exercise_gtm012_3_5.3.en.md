# Exercise 3

Express the distributions in parts (a) and (f) of Exercise 1 in terms of the $\delta$-distribution and its translates and derivatives.

Recall:
- (a) $F(u) = Du(1) - 3u(2\pi)$
- (f) $F(u) = \sum_{j=0}^n a_j D^j u(t_j)$

The $\delta$-distribution is defined by $\delta(u) = u(0)$. Its translate is $T_t\delta(u) = \delta(T_{-t}u) = u(t)$, and its derivative is $(D\delta)(u) = -\delta(Du) = -Du(0)$.
