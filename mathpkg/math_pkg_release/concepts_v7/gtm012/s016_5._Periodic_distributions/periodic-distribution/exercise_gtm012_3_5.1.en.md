# Exercise 1

Which of the following define periodic distributions?

(a) $F(u) = Du(1) - 3u(2\pi)$.

(b) $F(u) = \int_0^{2\pi} (u(x))^2\,dx$.

(c) $F(u) = \int_0^{2\pi} u(x)\,dx$.

(d) $F(u) = \int_0^\pi u(x)(1+x)^7\,dx$.

(e) $F(u) = -\int_0^{2\pi} D^3 u(x)\,|\cos 2x|\,dx$.

(f) $F(u) = \sum_{j=0}^n a_j D^j u(t_j)$, where $a_j \in \mathbb{C}$, $t_j \in \mathbb{R}$.

(g) $F(u) = \sum_{j=0}^\infty (j!)^{-1} D^j u(0)$.
