# Exercise 5

Show that $\operatorname{Re} F$ and $\operatorname{Im} F$ are real distributions. Show that

$$F = \operatorname{Re} F + i\operatorname{Im} F.$$

Recall that for $F \in \mathcal{P}'$,
$$\operatorname{Re} F = \frac{1}{2}(F + F^*), \quad \operatorname{Im} F = \frac{1}{2i}(F - F^*),$$
where $F^*(u) = \overline{F(\overline{u})}$. A distribution $G$ is called real if $G = G^*$.
