# Exercise 2

Suppose $\sum a_n$ and $\sum b_n$ each have all non-negative terms. If there are constants $M > 0$ and $N$ such that $b_n \geq M a_n$ whenever $n \geq N$, and if $\sum a_n = \infty$, then $\sum b_n = \infty$.

*Hint.* Consider the partial sums and show that the partial sums of $\sum b_n$ are unbounded.
