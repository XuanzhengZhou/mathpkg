# Exercise 4

Prove that $\lim_{n \to \infty} n^{1/n} = 1$.

(Hint: let $n^{1/n} = 1 + y_n$. For $n \geq 2$, $n = (1 + y_n)^n \geq 1 + ny_n + \frac{1}{2}n(n-1)y_n^2 > \frac{1}{2}n(n-1)y_n^2$, so $y_n^2 \leq 2(n-1)^{-1} \to 0$. Thus $y_n \to 0$.)
