A sequence $(a_n)_{-\infty}^{\infty} \subset \mathbb{C}$ is the sequence of Fourier coefficients of a function $u \in \mathcal{P}$ if and only if it is of rapid decrease.

If $u \in \mathcal{P}$ with coefficients $(a_n)$, then the coefficients of $D^k u$ are $((in)^k a_n)$, and $|n|^k |a_n| \leq \|D^k u\|$, giving the estimate $|a_n| \leq c|n|^{-k}$.
