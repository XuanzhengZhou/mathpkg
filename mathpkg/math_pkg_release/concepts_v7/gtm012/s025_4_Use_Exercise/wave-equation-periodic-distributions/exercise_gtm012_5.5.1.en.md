# Exercise 1

In Theorem 5.1, suppose $DG$ and $H$ are in $\mathbf{L}^2$. Show that

$$DF_t \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{d}{dt} F_t$$

are in $\mathbf{L}^2$ for each $t$, and

$$\| DF_t \|^2 + \left\| \frac{d}{dt} F_t \right\|^2 = \| DG \|^2 + \| H \|^2.$$
