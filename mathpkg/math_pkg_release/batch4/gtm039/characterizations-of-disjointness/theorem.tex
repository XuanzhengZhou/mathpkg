Let $\pi_1$ and $\pi_2$ be two representations of $A$. The following are equivalent: (i) $\pi_1 \perp \pi_2$ (they are disjoint); (ii) the only bounded operator $T$ satisfying $T\pi_1(x) = \pi_2(x)T$ for all $x \in A$ is $T = 0$; (iii) the projection $1 \oplus 0$ belongs to the weak closure of $(\pi_1 \oplus \pi_2)(A)$.
