(i)$\Rightarrow$(ii): If $T\pi_1 = \pi_2 T$, then $|T| \in \pi_1(A)'$ and polar decomposition $T = U|T|$ gives $U$ intertwining with orthogonal initial/final projections, so $U = 0$, $T = 0$. (ii)$\Rightarrow$(iii): If $1 \oplus 0 \notin (\pi_1 \oplus \pi_2)(A)''$, the commutant contains a nonzero operator intertwining, violating (ii). $\square$
