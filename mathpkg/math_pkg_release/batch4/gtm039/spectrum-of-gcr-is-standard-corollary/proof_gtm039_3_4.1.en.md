The quotient map $\text{irr}(A,\mathcal{H}_n) \to \hat{A}_n$ has a Borel cross section by 4.1.3. Apply 3.4.2: the range of a Borel map with a Borel cross section from a standard domain is standard. Thus each $\hat{A}_n$ is standard, and $\hat{A} = \sum_n \hat{A}_n$ is standard. $\square$
