Any irreducible representation $\pi$ of $\mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H})$ is a $*$-homomorphism onto $\pi(\mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H}))$. By 1.4.4, $\pi$ is equivalent to a subrepresentation of the identity representation, hence equivalent to the identity representation itself since $\mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H})$ is irreducible. $\square$
