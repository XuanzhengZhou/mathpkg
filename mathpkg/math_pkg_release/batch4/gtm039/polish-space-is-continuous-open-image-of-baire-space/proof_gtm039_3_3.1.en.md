Using the open sieve $\{A_{n_1\ldots n_k}\}$, define $f: N^\infty \to P$ by $\{f(n_1,n_2,\ldots)\} = \bigcap_k A_{n_1\ldots n_k}$. Each such intersection is a singleton by completeness and diameter bounds. $f$ is continuous and surjective. $f$ is open because $f(U_{n_1\ldots n_k}) = A_{n_1\ldots n_k}$ is open. $\square$
