An operator $A \in \mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$ is said to be \emph{bounded below} if there is an $\varepsilon > 0$ such that $\|Ax\| \geqslant \varepsilon \|x\|$ for every $x \in \mathcal{H}$. Prove that for such an operator $A$, $C^*(A)$ contains both factors of the polar decomposition of $A$.
