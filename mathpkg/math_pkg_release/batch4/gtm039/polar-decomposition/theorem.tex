For every bounded operator $T$ on a Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}$, there exists a unique partial isometry $U$ and a unique positive operator $|T| = (T^*T)^{1/2}$ such that $T = U|T|$, where $U^*U$ is the projection onto $(\ker T)^\perp = [|T|\mathcal{H}]$. Moreover, $U$ belongs to the von Neumann algebra generated by $T$.
