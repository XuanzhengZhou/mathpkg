Take the direct sum of all GNS representations $(\pi_f, \mathcal{H}_f, \xi_f)$ corresponding to pure states $f$ of $A$. Let $\pi = \bigoplus_f \pi_f$ on $\mathcal{H} = \bigoplus_f \mathcal{H}_f$. For any nonzero $z \in A$, by 1.7.2 there exists a pure state $f$ with $f(z^*z) = \|z\|^2$. Then $\|\pi(z)\| \geqslant \|\pi_f(z)\xi_f\| = f(z^*z)^{1/2} = \|z\|$. Since $\|\pi(z)\| \leqslant \|z\|$ by continuity, $\|\pi(z)\| = \|z\|$, so $\pi$ is an isometric representation. $\square$
