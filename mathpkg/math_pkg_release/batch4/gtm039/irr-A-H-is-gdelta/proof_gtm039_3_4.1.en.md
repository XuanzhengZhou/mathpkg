$\text{irr}(A,\mathcal{H}) = \bigcap_{\xi,\eta} \{\pi: \pi \text{ nondegenerate}\} \cap \bigcap_{T \in \mathcal{K}} (\mathcal{S}_T)^c$ for a countable family $\mathcal{K}$ of operators testing irreducibility. Each set in the intersection is a $G_\delta$ by 4.1.1. A countable intersection of $G_\delta$'s is a $G_\delta$, hence Polish by 3.1.2. $\square$
