The unit ball of $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$ is a compact metrizable space in the weak operator topology when $\mathcal{H}$ is separable. The unit ball of $\mathcal{R}$ is a weakly closed subset, hence separable. Choose a countable weakly dense subset and let $A$ be the $C^*$-algebra they generate; $A$ is separable, contained in $\mathcal{R}$, and its weak closure is $\mathcal{R}$. $\square$
