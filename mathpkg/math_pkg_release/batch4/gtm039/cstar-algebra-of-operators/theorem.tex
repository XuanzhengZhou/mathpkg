A \emph{$C^*$-algebra of operators} is a subset $A$ of the algebra $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$ of all bounded operators on a Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}$, which is closed under all algebraic operations on $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$ (addition, multiplication, multiplication by complex scalars), is closed in the norm topology of $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$, and is closed under the adjoint operation $T \mapsto T^*$ in $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$.
