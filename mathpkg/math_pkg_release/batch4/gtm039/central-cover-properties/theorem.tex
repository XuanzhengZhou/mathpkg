Let $\sigma_1$ and $\sigma_2$ be subrepresentations of $\pi$, with central covers $\bar{\sigma}_1$ and $\bar{\sigma}_2$: (i) $\sigma_1 \preceq \sigma_2 \implies \bar{\sigma}_1 \leqslant \bar{\sigma}_2$; (ii) if $\sigma_1$ is multiplicity-free and $\bar{\sigma}_1 \leqslant \bar{\sigma}_2$, then $\sigma_1 \preceq \sigma_2$; (iii) if both are multiplicity-free and $\bar{\sigma}_1 = \bar{\sigma}_2$, then $\sigma_1 \sim \sigma_2$; (iv) $\sigma_1 \perp \sigma_2 \iff \bar{\sigma}_1 \perp \bar{\sigma}_2$.
