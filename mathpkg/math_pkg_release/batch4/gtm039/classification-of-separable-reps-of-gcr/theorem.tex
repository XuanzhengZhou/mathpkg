Every separable representation $\pi$ of a separable GCR algebra $A$ is unitarily equivalent to a representation of the form $\pi = \int^{\oplus}_{\hat{A}} m(\zeta) \cdot \zeta \, d\mu(\zeta)$, where $\mu$ is a finite Borel measure on $\hat{A}$ and $m: \hat{A} \to \{\infty, 1, 2, \ldots\}$ is a Borel multiplicity function. Two such representations are equivalent iff their measure classes coincide and their multiplicity functions agree almost everywhere.
