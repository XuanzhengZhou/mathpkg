A $C^*$-algebra $A$ is GCR if and only if it admits a composition series $\{J_\alpha : 0 \leqslant \alpha \leqslant \alpha_0\}$ such that every successive quotient $J_{\alpha+1}/J_\alpha$ is a CCR algebra. Moreover, every GCR algebra has exactly one composition series with the property that $J_{\alpha+1}/J_\alpha$ is the largest CCR ideal in $A/J_\alpha$ for each $\alpha$.
