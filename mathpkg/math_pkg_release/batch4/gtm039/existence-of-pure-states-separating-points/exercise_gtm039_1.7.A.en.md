Let $A$ be a Banach algebra with unit $e$, and let $x, y$ belong to $A$. Show that $e - xy$ is invertible if and only if $e - yx$ is invertible. Deduce that $\operatorname{sp}(xy)$ and $\operatorname{sp}(yx)$ agree except possibly for the point $0$.
