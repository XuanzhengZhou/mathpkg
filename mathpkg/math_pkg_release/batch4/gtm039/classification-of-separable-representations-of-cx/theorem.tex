Every separable representation of $C(X)$ is unitarily equivalent to $\pi = \infty \cdot \pi_{\mu_\infty} \oplus 1 \cdot \pi_{\mu_1} \oplus 2 \cdot \pi_{\mu_2} \oplus \cdots$, where $\mu_\infty, \mu_1, \mu_2, \ldots$ are mutually singular finite Borel measures on $X$. Two such representations are equivalent iff the corresponding measure classes coincide up to unitary equivalence.
