For irreducible representations, $\pi \not\sim \pi_0$ iff $\pi \circ \pi_0$. The complement of the equivalence class is $\text{irr}(A,\mathcal{H}) \cap \{\pi: \pi \circ \pi_0\}$, which is a $G_\delta$ in $\text{irr}(A,\mathcal{H})$. Thus the equivalence class is an $F_\sigma$. $\square$
