Let $\{E_n\}$ be a countable generating and separating family. Define $f: X \to \{0,1\}^\infty$ by $f(x) = (\chi_{E_1}(x),\ldots)$. $f$ is 1-1 and Borel. By 3.3.4, $f(X)$ is analytic. Apply 3.3.2 to $f^{-1}: f(X) \to X$. $\square$
