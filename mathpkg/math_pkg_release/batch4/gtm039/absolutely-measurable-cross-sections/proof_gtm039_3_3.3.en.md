Given $A = f(N^\infty)$, construct minimal Borel covers $E_{n_1\ldots n_k}$ of $f(U_{n_1\ldots n_k})$. Define $B = \bigcap_k \bigcup_{n_1,\ldots,n_k} E_{n_1\ldots n_k}$. Then $A \subseteq B$ and $B$ is Borel. The measure estimate $\mu(B \setminus A) = 0$ follows from the regularity of the measure and the compactness of nested intersections in $N^\infty$. $\square$
