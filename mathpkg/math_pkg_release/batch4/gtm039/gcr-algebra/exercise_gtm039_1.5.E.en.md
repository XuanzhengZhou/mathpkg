A unilateral weighted shift is an operator defined on an orthonormal base $e_1, e_2, \ldots$ by the condition $A: e_n \mapsto w_n e_{n+1}$, where $\{w_n\}$ is a bounded sequence of nonnegative real numbers.

a. Show that if $w_n > 0$ for every $n$, then $C^*(A)$ is irreducible.
b. Show that if $w_n \geqslant \delta > 0$ for every $n$, then $C^*(A)$ cannot be an NGCR algebra.
