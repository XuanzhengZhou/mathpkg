Write $z^*z = u^2 - v^2$ as in 1.7.1. The condition $\text{sp}(z^*z) \leqslant 0$ means $u = 0$ in the decomposition, so $z^*z = -v^2$. Then $zz^*z = -zv^2$, and the spectral radius estimate forces $z^*z = 0$, hence $z = 0$ by the $C^*$-identity. $\square$
