If $\mu \sim \nu$ with $h = d\mu/d\nu$, $U\xi = h^{1/2}\xi$ is unitary and $U\pi_\mu = \pi_\nu U$. If $\mu \perp \nu$, let $E$ be a Borel set with $\mu(E^c) = \nu(E) = 0$. The characteristic function $\chi_E$ gives $L_{\chi_E} \in \pi_{\mu+\nu}(C(X))''$, which under the identification becomes $1 \oplus 0$, proving $\pi_\mu \circ \pi_\nu$ by 2.1.4. $\square$
