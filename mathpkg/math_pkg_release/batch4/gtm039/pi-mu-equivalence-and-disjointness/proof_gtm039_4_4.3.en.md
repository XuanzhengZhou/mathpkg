The proof of 2.2.2 translates verbatim, using 4.3.1 in place of 2.2.1. If $\mu \sim \nu$, the Radon-Nikodym derivative gives a unitary intertwiner via $U\xi = h^{1/2}\xi$. If $\mu \perp \nu$, choose a Borel set separating supports; its characteristic function gives $1 \oplus 0$ in the weak closure, proving disjointness by 2.1.4. $\square$
