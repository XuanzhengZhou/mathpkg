(This proof is given by the structure of compact operators: every compact operator is a norm-limit of finite-rank operators. Since $\mathcal{A} \subseteq \mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H})$ is a $C^*$-algebra, it contains the spectral projections of its self-adjoint elements. By 1.4.1, it contains a minimal projection $E$, which has finite rank by compactness. Finite induction then shows $\mathcal{A}$ contains all operators of finite rank supported on the range of $E$, and irreducibility forces the span to be the whole space.) $\square$
