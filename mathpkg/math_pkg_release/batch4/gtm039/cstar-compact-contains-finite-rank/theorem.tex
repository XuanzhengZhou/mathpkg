Let $\mathcal{A}$ be a nonzero $C^*$-subalgebra of $\mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H})$. Then there exists a nonzero subspace $\mathcal{H}_0 \subseteq \mathcal{H}$ such that $\mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H}_0) \subseteq \mathcal{A}|_{\mathcal{H}_0}$. Moreover, $\mathcal{A}$ contains every finite rank operator whose range is contained in $\mathcal{H}_0$ and whose kernel contains $\mathcal{H}_0^\perp$.
