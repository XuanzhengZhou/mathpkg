Let $\mathcal{A}$ be a nonzero $C^*$-subalgebra of $\mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H})$, and let $E$ be a nonzero spectral projection of a self-adjoint operator in $\mathcal{A}$. Then $E$ contains a nonzero minimal projection $F$ of $\mathcal{A}$ such that, for every $T \in \mathcal{A}$, $FTF = \lambda F$ for some $\lambda \in \mathbb{C}$.
