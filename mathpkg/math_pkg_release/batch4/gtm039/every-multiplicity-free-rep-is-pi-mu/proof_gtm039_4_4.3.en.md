Let $\pi$ be multiplicity-free. Decompose the center $\mathcal{C}$ of $\pi(A)''$ into parts $C_n$ of constant multiplicity $n$. The restriction $\pi_n = \pi|_{C_n\mathcal{H}}$ has a separable weakly dense $C^*$-subalgebra in $\mathcal{C}$. Its spectrum $Z$ is a compact metric space. The Gelfand map $\phi: C(Z) \to \mathcal{C}_0$ has multiplicity $n$. By 2.2.4, there is a measure on $Z$, giving an isomorphism of $Z$ into $\hat{A}_n$, converting $\pi_n$ into $\pi_{\mu_n}$. $\square$
