Write $S = \bigcap G_i$ with $G_i$ open. Each $G_i$ is Polish (3.1.1) via homeomorphisms $f_i: P_i \to G_i$. $Q = \{(p_i) \in \prod P_i: f_1(p_1)=f_2(p_2)=\cdots\}$ is closed in the product, hence Polish. The map $f(p_1,p_2,\ldots) = f_1(p_1)$ is a homeomorphism onto $S$. $\square$
