Let $X$ be a compact metric space, $\mu$ a finite Borel measure on $X$, and $\pi_\mu: C(X) \to \mathcal{L}(L^2(X,\mu))$ the representation by multiplication. Let $\mathcal{Z} = \{L_f : f \in \mathcal{B}(X)\}$ be the algebra of multiplication by bounded Borel functions. Then $\mathcal{Z} = \mathcal{Z} = \text{strong closure of } \pi_\mu(C(X))$.
