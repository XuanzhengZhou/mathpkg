A \emph{Borel space} is a pair $(X, \mathcal{B})$ where $\mathcal{B}$ is a $\sigma$-field of subsets of $X$. A Borel space is \emph{standard} if it is isomorphic to the Borel space of a Polish space. It is \emph{analytic} if there is an analytic set $A$ in a Polish space such that $X$ is isomorphic to $A$ with its relative Borel structure.
