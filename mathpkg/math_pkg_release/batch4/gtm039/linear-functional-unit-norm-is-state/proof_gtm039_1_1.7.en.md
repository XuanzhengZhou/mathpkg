By 1.7.1, $\text{sp}(z^*z) \geqslant 0$ for all $z$. It suffices to show $f(y) \geqslant 0$ for $y = y^*$ with $\text{sp}(y) \geqslant 0$. For such $y$, the closed convex hull of $\text{sp}(y)$ is contained in $[0,\infty)$. If $f(y)$ were outside this hull, separate by a line; the functional calculus would then contradict $\|f\| = f(e) = 1$. Thus $f(y) \geqslant 0$. $\square$
