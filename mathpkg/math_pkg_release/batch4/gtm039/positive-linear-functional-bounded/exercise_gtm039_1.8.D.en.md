Let $\pi$ be a nondegenerate representation of a $C^*$-algebra $A$ on a Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}$, and let $\{e_\lambda\}$ be an approximate identity for $A$. Show that the net of operators $\pi(e_\lambda)$ converges in the strong operator topology to the identity operator on $\mathcal{H}$.
