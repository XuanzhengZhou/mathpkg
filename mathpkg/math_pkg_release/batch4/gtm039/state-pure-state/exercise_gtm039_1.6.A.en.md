For every unit vector $\xi$ in a Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}$, let $\omega_{\xi}$ be the linear functional on $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$ defined by
$$\omega_{\xi}(T) = (T\xi, \xi).$$

a. Show that each $\omega_{\xi}$ is a pure state.
b. Show that the representations of $\mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$ associated to the $\omega_{\xi}$'s via the GNS construction are all equivalent to the identity representation.
