Let $\{\pi_i\}$ be a maximal family of mutually orthogonal irreducible subrepresentations of $\pi$, each equivalent to a subrepresentation of the identity representation. If the direct sum is not all of $\pi$, the orthogonal complement would be a nonzero subrepresentation of $\pi$. By 1.4.3, this would contain an irreducible subrepresentation equivalent to some $\pi_i$, contradicting maximality. Hence $\pi = \sum_i \pi_i$. $\square$
