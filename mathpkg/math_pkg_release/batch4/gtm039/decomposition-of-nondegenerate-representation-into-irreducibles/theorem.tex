Let $\pi$ be any nondegenerate representation of a $C^*$-algebra of compact operators $\mathcal{A}$. Then there is an orthogonal family $\{\pi_i\}$ of irreducible subrepresentations of $\pi$ such that $\pi = \sum_i \pi_i$, and each $\pi_i$ is equivalent to a subrepresentation of the identity representation of $\mathcal{A}$.
