By 2.1.4, $\sigma \circ \pi_0$ iff $1 \oplus 0 \in (\sigma \oplus \pi_0)(A)''$. The set of $\lambda$ with this property is a $G_\delta$ by 4.1.1. The map $\sigma \mapsto \sigma \oplus \pi_0$ is continuous. The inverse image of a $G_\delta$ under a continuous map is a $G_\delta$. $\square$
