Let $x$ be a normal element in a $C^*$-algebra with identity $e$, and let $B$ be the $C^*$-algebra generated by $x$ and $e$. The map $\omega \mapsto \omega(x)$ is a homeomorphism of $\hat{B}$ onto $\operatorname{sp}(x)$, inducing an isometric $\ast$-isomorphism of $C(\operatorname{sp}(x))$ onto $B$, written $f \mapsto f(x)$.
