For $T \in \mathcal{Z}'$ and $u \in \mathcal{K}$, define $F_u(x) = T(u \otimes 1)(x)$. Show $\|F_u(x)\| \leqslant \|T\|\|u\|$ a.e. using integral estimates. This defines a bounded operator-valued function $F(x) \in \mathcal{L}(\mathcal{K})$ a.e. with $(T\xi)(x) = F(x)\xi(x)$. Conversely, any such multiplication clearly commutes with diagonal operators. $\square$
