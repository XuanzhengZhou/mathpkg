Write $z^*z = u^2 - v^2$ with $u = f(z^*z)$, $v = g(z^*z)$ where $f(t) = \sqrt{t}$ for $t \geqslant 0$ (0 otherwise), $g(t) = 0$ for $t \geqslant 0$ ($\sqrt{-t}$ otherwise). Then $fg = 0$, $f^2 - g^2 = \text{id}$, and $uv = 0$. Compute $vz^*zv = -v^4$, so $\text{sp}((zv)^*(zv)) \leqslant 0$. By Lemma 2, $(zv)^*(zv) = 0$, hence $v^4 = 0$ and $v = 0$ (as $v$ is self-adjoint). Thus $z^*z = u^2 \geqslant 0$. $\square$
