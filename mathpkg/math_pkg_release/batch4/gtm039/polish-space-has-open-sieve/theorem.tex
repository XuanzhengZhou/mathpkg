Every Polish space $P$ admits an \emph{open sieve}: a family $\{A_{n_1\ldots n_k}\}$ of nonvoid open subsets indexed by finite sequences of positive integers such that: (i) $A_{n_1} = \text{int}(\overline{A_{n_1}})$, (ii) $A_{n_1\ldots n_k} = \bigcup_{\ell=1}^\infty A_{n_1\ldots n_k\ell}$, (iii) $\text{diam}(A_{n_1\ldots n_k}) \leqslant 1/k$, (iv) $\overline{A_{n_1\ldots n_{k+1}}} \subseteq A_{n_1\ldots n_k}$, (v) $\bigcup_{n_1} A_{n_1} = P$.
