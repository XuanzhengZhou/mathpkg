The \emph{spectrum} $\hat{A}$ of a separable $C^*$-algebra $A$ is the set of all unitary equivalence classes of irreducible representations of $A$, equipped with the Borel structure generated by the quotient map from $\text{irr}(A, \mathcal{H}_n)$. It decomposes as $\hat{A} = \bigcup_{n} \hat{A}_n$, where $\hat{A}_n$ consists of classes of irreducible representations acting on an $n$-dimensional Hilbert space.
