Problems 21 to 27 refer to the coin tossing process. The state space $S$ consists of all finite sequences of $H$ (heads) and $T$ (tails), together with a distinguished state $0$. From state $a = (a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n)$ the process is equally likely to go to $(a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, H)$ or to $(a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, T)$.

Find the potential kernel $N$.
