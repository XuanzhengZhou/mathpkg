Let $P$ be a recurrent chain, $0$ a distinguished state, and $Q$ the associated transient chain. For the transient chain $Q$, we have $N_{ij} = {}_0 N_{ij} + \delta_{j0}$. Choose $f$ to be the column vector placing unit weight at $0$, so that $Nf = \mathbf{1}$. Form the Martin entrance boundary of $Q$ with respect to the reference function $f$. The entrance kernel is

$$J(i,j) = \frac{N_{ij}}{(Nf)_i} = {}_0 N_{ij} + \delta_{j0}.$$

The compact metric space $*S$ is the completion of $S$ in the metric of Chapter 10. The spaces $*S$ and $*S \setminus S$ are called the **completed space** and the **recurrent entrance boundary**, respectively, of the chain $P$. The set $B^e$ of extreme points of $*S \setminus S$ is the set of extreme points of the recurrent boundary.
