For the coin tossing process, let $h$ be defined by

$$h_a = \begin{cases}
1 & \text{if } a = 0 \\
2 & \text{if } a = (H) \\
4 & \text{if } a \text{ begins with } a_1 = a_2 = H \\
0 & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}$$

Prove that $h$ is a normalized regular function. Find the $h$-process $P^h$ and the induced harmonic measure $\mu^h$. Show that $h$ is continuous and that $h(x) = d\mu^h/d\mu$ for almost every $x$ (with respect to $\mu$).
