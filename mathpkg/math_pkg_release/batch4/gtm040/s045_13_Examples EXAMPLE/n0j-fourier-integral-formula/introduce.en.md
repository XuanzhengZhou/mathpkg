This lemma expresses the potential kernel $N_{0j}$ of the three-dimensional symmetric random walk as the $j$-th Fourier coefficient of a certain periodic function. The integral representation is the starting point for obtaining the asymptotic estimate $N_{0j} \sim 3/(2\pi|j|)$. The proof relies on the fact that $(I-P)^{-1}$ acts as the inverse of the discrete Laplacian and that both $N_{0\cdot}$ and the integral formula satisfy the same difference equation with the same boundary behavior at infinity.
