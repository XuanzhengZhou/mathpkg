For the coin tossing process, let $f_a = 1/m$ if $a$ consists of exactly $m$ $H$'s and $m$ $T$'s, and let $f_a$ equal $0$ otherwise. Show that $f$ is a charge, and verify that

$$\Pr[\lim g(x_n) = 0] = 1,$$

where $g = Nf$ and $x_n$ is the process.
