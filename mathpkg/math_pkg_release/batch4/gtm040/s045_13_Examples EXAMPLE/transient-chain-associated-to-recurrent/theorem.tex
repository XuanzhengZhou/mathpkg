Let $P$ be a recurrent Markov chain on a countable state space $S$, let $0 \in S$ be a distinguished state, and let $\alpha$ be a finite-valued positive regular measure for $P$ (which is unique up to a constant factor). Define a transient chain $Q$ associated to $P$ and $0$ by

$$Q_{ij} = \begin{cases} P_{ij} & \text{if } i \neq 0 \\ 0 & \text{if } i = 0. \end{cases}$$

That is, $Q$ agrees with $P$ except that the process is forced to disappear upon reaching state $0$.
