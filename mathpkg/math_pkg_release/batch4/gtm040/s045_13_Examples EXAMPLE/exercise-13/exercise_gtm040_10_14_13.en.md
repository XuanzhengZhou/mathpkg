Problems 13 to 20 refer to a "double basic example." Let

$$S = \{0, 1, 2, \ldots, 0', 1', 2', \ldots\}.$$

The non-zero entries of $P$ are

$$P_{i-1,i} = p_i; \quad P_{i-1,0'} = 1 - p_i; \quad P_{(i-1)',i'} = p_{i'}; \quad P_{(i-1)',0} = 1 - p_{i'}.$$

Let

$$\beta_0 = \beta_{0'} = 1, \quad \beta_i = \prod_{k=1}^{i} p_k, \quad \beta_{i'} = \prod_{k=1}^{i} p_{k'},$$

$$\beta_\infty = \lim \beta_i, \quad \beta_{\infty'} = \lim \beta_{i'}.$$

Assume that $\beta_\infty > 0$ and $\beta_{\infty'} > 0$.

Find the hitting probability matrix $H$. [Hint: Use Propositions 4-14 and 4-16.]
