For a subset $E$ of the state space $S$ containing $0$ and an admissible row vector $\nu$, let $\theta^E$ be the row vector whose $j$th entry is the probability that the $Q^\nu$-process, starting at $0$, is last in $E$ at state $j$. The measure $\theta^E$ depends on $P$, $0$, $\nu$, and $E$, and satisfies $\theta^E_j = 0$ for $j \notin E$. When $E$ is finite and $0 \in E$, $\theta^E$ is a probability measure (total measure $1$).
