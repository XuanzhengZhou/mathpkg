If $\{E_k\}$ is an increasing sequence of finite sets with union $S$ and $P$ is a normal Markov chain, then the normalized entrance measures $\lambda^{E_k}$ converge weak-star to the harmonic measure $\beta$ on ${}^*S$. The measure $\beta$ is independent of the choice of the distinguished state $0$. Moreover,
$$G_{ij} = \int_{S \cup B^e} {}_iN(x, j) \, d\beta(x)$$
for all states $i, j$, where ${}_iN(x, j)$ is the Martin kernel based at $i$.
