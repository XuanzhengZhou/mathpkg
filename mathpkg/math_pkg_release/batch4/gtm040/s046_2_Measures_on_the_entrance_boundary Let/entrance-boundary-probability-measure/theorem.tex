If $\nu$ satisfies conditions (1), (2), and (3) for an admissible row vector, then there exists a unique probability measure $\beta^\nu$ on the Borel sets of $S \cup B^e$ such that
$$\nu = \int_{S \cup B^e} {}_0N(x, \cdot) \, d\beta^\nu(x),$$
where ${}_0N(x, \cdot)$ is the Martin kernel based at state $0$.
