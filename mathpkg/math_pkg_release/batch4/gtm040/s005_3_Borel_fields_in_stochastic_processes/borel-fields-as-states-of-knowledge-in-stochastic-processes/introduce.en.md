In a stochastic process, an increasing sequence of Borel fields $\{\mathcal{B}_n\}$ represents the accumulation of knowledge over time. Each $\mathcal{B}_n$ encodes the information available up to time $n$, with the nesting $\mathcal{B}_n \subset \mathcal{B}_{n+1}$ reflecting that knowledge increases monotonically.
