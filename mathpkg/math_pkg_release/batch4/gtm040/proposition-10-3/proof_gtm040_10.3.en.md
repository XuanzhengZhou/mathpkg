**Proof:** We prove the result for $u_E$. We have

$$\{\omega \mid u_E(\omega) \leq k\} = \bigcup_{i \in E} \bigcup_{n = -\infty}^{k} \{\omega \mid x_n(\omega) = i\},$$

and the union of these sets on $k$ is the domain of $u_E$.
