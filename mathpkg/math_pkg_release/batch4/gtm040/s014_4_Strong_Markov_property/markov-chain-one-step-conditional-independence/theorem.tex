Let $\{x_n\}$ be a Markov chain with starting distribution $\pi$, let $q$ be a statement relative to $\mathcal{F}_{n-1}$, let $r$ be a statement relative to $\mathcal{T}_{n+1} \cap \mathcal{F}$, and suppose $\Pr_{\pi}[q \land x_n = i] > 0$. Then

$$\Pr_{\pi}[r \mid q \land x_n = i] = \Pr_{\pi}[r \mid x_n = i] = \Pr_i[r']$$

where $\omega \in R$ if and only if $\omega_n \in R'$.
