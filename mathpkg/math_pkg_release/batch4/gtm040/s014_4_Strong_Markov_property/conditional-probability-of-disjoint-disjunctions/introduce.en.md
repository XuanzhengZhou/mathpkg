Lemma 4-4 from Denumerable Markov Chains provides a basic property of conditional probability. It states that if a statement $p$ has the same conditional probability $c$ given each of a collection of mutually exclusive statements $p_k$, then the conditional probability of $p$ given the disjunction of those statements is also $c$. This lemma is used throughout Chapter 4 to reduce proofs about general statements to proofs about elementary cylinder statements.
