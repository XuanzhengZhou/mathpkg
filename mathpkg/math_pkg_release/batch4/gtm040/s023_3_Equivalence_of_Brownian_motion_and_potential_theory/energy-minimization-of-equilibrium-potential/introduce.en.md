This theorem characterizes the equilibrium potential by a variational principle. Among all potentials whose charges are supported on an equilibrium set $E$ and whose total charge equals the capacity of $E$, the equilibrium potential uniquely minimizes the energy. This provides an energy-minimization interpretation of the equilibrium potential, analogous to Dirichlet's principle in classical potential theory. The result connects the concepts of capacity, energy, and equilibrium sets within a unified variational framework.
