\textbf{Theorem 12-40.} If $S$ is finite, then $|\mathcal{G}_{Q'}| = 1$. The unique element of $\mathcal{G}_{Q'}$ is the Markov chain with transition matrix $Q$ and stationary measure $\alpha$, where $Q$ is defined as in Lemma 12-39 and $\alpha$ is the unique strictly positive probability measure satisfying $\alpha Q = \alpha$.
