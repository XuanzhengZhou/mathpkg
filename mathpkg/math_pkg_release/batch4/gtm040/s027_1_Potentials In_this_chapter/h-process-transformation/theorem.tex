The $h$-process transformation is defined on square matrices $Y$, row vectors $\pi$, and column vectors $f$ by
$$Y^* = UYU^{-1}, \quad \pi^* = \pi U^{-1}, \quad f^* = Uf,$$
where $U$ is a diagonal matrix with positive diagonal entries. Sums, products, equalities, inequalities, and limits are preserved entry-by-entry. If $P$ is a transient chain, then $P^*$ is also transient, $N^*$ is the fundamental matrix for $P^*$, and $(Nf)^* = N^* f^*$.
