By Lemma 8-2, $Nf$ is well defined and finite-valued. Hence so are both $Nf^+$ and $Nf^-$. By monotone convergence,
$$\lim_n [(I + P + \cdots + P^{n-1})f^+] = Nf^+$$
and
$$\lim_n [(I + P + \cdots + P^{n-1})f^-] = Nf^-.$$
Thus
$$\lim_n [(I + P + \cdots + P^{n-1})f] = Nf^+ - Nf^- = Nf.$$
Therefore $f$ satisfies the definition of a charge with potential $g = Nf$.
