Lemma 8-17 gives a game-theoretic decomposition: the expected total gain $g_i$ splits into the expected gain after first entering $E$ plus the expected gain before entering $E$. Formally, $N = B^E N + (I - B^E)N$, and for a potential $g = Nf$, $g = B^E g + (I - B^E)Nf$. The term $B^E N f$ uses the hitting probabilities $B^E$, representing the expected visits from the first hitting time of $E$ onward. If the charge $f$ is supported in $E$, then the pre-$E$ term vanishes, yielding $g = B^E g$.
