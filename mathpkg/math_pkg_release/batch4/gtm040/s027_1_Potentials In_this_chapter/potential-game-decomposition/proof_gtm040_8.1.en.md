Note that $f_j$ is the difference of the total number of times the process is in $j$ and the number of times it is in $j$ before reaching $E$. Hence
$$M_i[f_j] = N_{ij} - {}_E N_{ij},$$
and the first equation follows. To get the second equation, multiply through by $f$; associativity in $B^E N f$ holds because $B^E N |f|$ is finite-valued.
