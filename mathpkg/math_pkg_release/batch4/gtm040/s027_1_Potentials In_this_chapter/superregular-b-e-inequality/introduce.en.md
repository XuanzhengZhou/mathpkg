Proposition 8-15 is a fundamental inequality in potential theory: for any non-negative superregular function $h$, the balayage $B^E h$ (the expected value of $h$ upon first hitting $E$) never exceeds $h$ itself. This reflects the supermartingale property of superregular functions. The proof uses the $h$-process transformation to reduce to the case $h = 1$, where $(B^E)^* 1 \leq 1$ is obvious.
