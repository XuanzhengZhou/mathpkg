Corollary 8-21 follows directly from Proposition 8-20 and Corollary 8-9: since each column of $B^E$ is a potential, applying iterated $P$ to $B^E$ yields zero in the limit. This means that the hitting distribution of $E$ from a remote starting point eventually decays.
