Lemma 8-24 establishes a monotonicity result for total charge: if the potential of a non-negative function $\bar{f}$ is dominated by the potential of $f$ (and $f$ has finite total charge), then the total charge of $\bar{f}$ is also bounded by that of $f$. The proof uses an approximation of the distinguished measure $\alpha$ by finite measures of the form $\pi^{(n)} N$ (dual of Proposition 8-23) and monotone convergence. This lemma is a step toward proving that a non-negative superregular function dominated by a potential is itself a potential.
