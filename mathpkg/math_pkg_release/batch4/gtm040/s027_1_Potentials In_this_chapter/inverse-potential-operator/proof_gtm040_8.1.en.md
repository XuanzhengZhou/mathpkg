Let $f$ be a charge with potential $g$. By Theorem 8-3, $g = Nf$. Hence, by Lemma 5-9, $(I - P)g = (I - P)Nf = f$. Thus $(I-P)g$ recovers the charge $f$. The one-to-one correspondence follows because each charge determines a unique potential (via $g = Nf$) and each potential determines a unique charge (via $f = (I-P)g$).
