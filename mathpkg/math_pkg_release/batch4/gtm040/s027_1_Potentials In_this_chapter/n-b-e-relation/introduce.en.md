Proposition 8-20 expresses the balayage matrix $B^E$ as a product of the fundamental matrix $N$ and the matrix $\begin{pmatrix} I - P^E & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$. This factorization reveals that each column of $B^E$ is itself a potential whose charge is supported in $E$, providing a direct link between the hitting probabilities and the potential operator $N$.
