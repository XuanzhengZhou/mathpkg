Potentials transform into potentials; hence $g^* = 1$ is a potential. Since $1$ is then of the form $N^* f^*$, $\bar{P}^*$ is absorbing. The absorbing state $a$ can be reached only from a state $i$ such that $(P^* 1)_i < 1$; for such a state $i$,
$$0 < [(I - P^*)1]_i = f_i^*.$$
Hence $f_i > 0$ and $i$ must be in $E$. Thus the $P^*$-process with probability one reaches $E$ from all states, and $(B^E)^* 1 = 1$.
