Proposition 9-73 establishes two key facts about the potential operator $G$ in strong ergodic chains. First, if $f$ is any bounded function, then $Gf$ is finite-valued everywhere. Second, if $f$ additionally satisfies $\alpha f = 0$ (i.e., has mean zero with respect to the stationary distribution), then $f$ is a charge whose potential is given by $-Gf$, connecting to Theorem 9-50.
