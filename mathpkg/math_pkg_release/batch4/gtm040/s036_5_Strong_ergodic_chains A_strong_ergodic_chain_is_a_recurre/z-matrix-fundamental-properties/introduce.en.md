Proposition 9-76 collects five fundamental identities for the matrix $Z$ in a strong ergodic chain. These properties establish $Z$ as a generalized inverse of $I - P + A$, with $Z$ acting as the identity on the stationary distribution ($Z1 = 1$, $\alpha Z = \alpha$). The duality relation $\text{dual } Z = \hat{Z}$ shows that $Z$ behaves naturally under time reversal, making it a convenient unified potential operator for both right and left potentials.
