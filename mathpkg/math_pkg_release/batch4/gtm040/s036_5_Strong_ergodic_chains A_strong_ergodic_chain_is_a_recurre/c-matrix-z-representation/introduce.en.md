Proposition 9-77 expresses the fundamental matrix $C$ (and consequently the mean first passage matrix $M$) in terms of the matrix $Z$. Specifically, $C = E Z_{\mathrm{dg}} - Z$, where $Z_{\mathrm{dg}}$ is the diagonal matrix formed from the diagonal entries of $Z$, and $E$ is the matrix of all ones. The second formula $M = (E Z_{\mathrm{dg}} - Z) D$ follows directly using $C = M D^{-1}$, providing a compact representation of mean first passage times.
