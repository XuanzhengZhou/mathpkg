A strong ergodic chain is a recurrent Markov chain whose ergodic degree is at least 2. Every strong ergodic chain is necessarily ergodic, and all finite recurrent chains are strong ergodic by Proposition 9-72. The condition that the ergodic degree is 2 or greater is equivalent to the property that $\alpha M$ is finite-valued, where $M$ is the mean first passage matrix and $\alpha$ is the stationary distribution.
