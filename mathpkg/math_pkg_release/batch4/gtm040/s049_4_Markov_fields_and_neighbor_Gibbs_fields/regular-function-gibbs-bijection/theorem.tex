There is a bijection $\mu \leftrightarrow h$ between the set $\mathcal{G}_V$ of all Gibbs fields with potential $V$ and the set $\mathcal{H}$ of all non-negative normalized $P$-regular functions, given by

$$
h_{(n, \kappa^n)} = \frac{\mu([\kappa^n])}{\nu([\kappa^n])},
\qquad
\mu([\kappa^n]) = \nu([\kappa^n]) \cdot h_{(n, \kappa^n)},
$$

where $\nu \in \mathcal{G}_V$ is a fixed reference measure and $P$ is the transition matrix of the Markov chain defined on the pairs $(n, \kappa^n)$.
