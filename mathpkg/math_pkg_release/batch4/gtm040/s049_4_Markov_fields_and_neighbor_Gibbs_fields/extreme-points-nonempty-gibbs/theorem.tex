Let $\nu \in \mathcal{G}_V$ be a fixed reference measure and let $\kappa^n(\omega)$ denote the configuration $\omega$ restricted to $K(n)$. Then

$$
\nu\left(\left\{ \omega \mid t\text{-}\lim_{n \to \infty} \nu^{\kappa^n(\omega)} \in \mathcal{E}_V \right\}\right) = 1.
$$

In particular, $\mathcal{E}_V \neq \varnothing$ whenever $\mathcal{G}_V \neq \varnothing$.
