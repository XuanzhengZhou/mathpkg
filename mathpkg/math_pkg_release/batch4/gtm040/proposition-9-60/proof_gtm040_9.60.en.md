**Proof:**

$$\lim_j i\lambda_j = \lim_j \sum_k \alpha_k iH_{kj} \quad \text{by Proposition 9-55}$$

$$= \sum_k \alpha_k \lim_j iH_{kj} \quad \text{by dominated convergence}$$

$$= 0 \quad \text{by Lemma 9-59.}$$
