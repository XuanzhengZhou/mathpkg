This lemma states that the pointwise supremum of two submartingales adapted to the same filtration is again a submartingale. The proof verifies integrability and the submartingale inequality for the supremum by comparing conditional expectations. It is used as a technical step in the Upcrossing Lemma, specifically to show that the positive part of a submartingale shifted by a constant remains a submartingale.
