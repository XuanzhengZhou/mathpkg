Since $P$ is normal, $P^n \,^0N \to 1 \,^0\nu$. The bound $\sum_k (P^n)_{ik} \,^0N_{kj} \leq \,^0N_{jj}$ together with finiteness of $\,^0N f^+$ and $\,^0N f^-$ gives dominated convergence in Proposition 9-13. Hence $f$ is a charge and $g = [\,^0N - 1 \,^0\nu] f$.
