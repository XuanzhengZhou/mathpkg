This corollary provides a computationally practical formula for the taboo probability matrix $P^E$ in terms of the inverse restricted $K$-matrix $K_E^{-1}$ and the stationary distribution $\alpha_E$. Unlike the $T + UNR$ formula which involves matrices indexed by the infinite complement $\tilde{E}$, this expression uses only $E$-indexed quantities. The formula is derived by solving for $l_E^E$ and $\lambda_E^E$ from $K_E^{-1}$ and substituting into the Propositon 9-91 expression.
