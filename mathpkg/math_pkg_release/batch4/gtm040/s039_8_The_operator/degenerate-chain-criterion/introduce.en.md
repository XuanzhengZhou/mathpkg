This lemma simplifies the verification of degeneracy: it suffices to check that capacity vanishes for all sets containing a single reference point and for all one-point sets. The proof uses the change-of-reference formula $k'(E) = k(E) + k(\{0'\})$ and the monotonicity of capacity to extend the vanishing property to all finite sets under any reference point.
