This lemma provides two alternative expressions for the $K$-matrix in terms of the $C$ and $G$ matrices. The first expresses $K_{ij}$ using $C_{ij}$ with a correction based on the reference row $0$. The second expresses $K_{ij}$ using $G_{ij}$ with a correction based on the reference column $0$ and the $\alpha$ coefficients. These formulas are fundamental for establishing the existence and properties of $K$.
