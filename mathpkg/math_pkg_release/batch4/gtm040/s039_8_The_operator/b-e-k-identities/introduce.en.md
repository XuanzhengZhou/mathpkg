This lemma establishes two fundamental identities linking the $K$-matrix with the taboo probability matrix $B^E$. The first identity expresses $B^E K$ in terms of $K$ plus correction terms involving the mean occupation time $^E N$ and the equilibrium quantity $^E d\alpha$. The second identity is the dual version. These identities are essential for proving the extended identities in Lemma 9-95.
