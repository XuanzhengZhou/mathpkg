This corollary establishes a duality identity for the equilibrium-weighted mean occupation matrix $M$: for any state $j$ in $E$, the $j$-th entry of $\lambda^E M$ equals the $j$-th entry of $\hat{\lambda}^E \hat{M}$. The proof is elegantly simple: choosing $j$ as the reference point, both expressions equal the capacity $k(E)$, which is self-dual.
