Let
$$\nu = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left[ \mu(I + P + \cdots + P^n) \right]$$
and
$$g = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left[ (I + P + \cdots + P^n) f \right]$$
be potentials of weak charges in a normal chain $P$. Then $\nu = -\mu K$ and $g = -Kf$. If $Y$ is any matrix such that for all potentials $\nu$ and $g$ of finite support $\nu = \mu Y$ and $g = Yf$, then $Y = -(K + k(1\alpha))$, where $k$ is a scalar.
