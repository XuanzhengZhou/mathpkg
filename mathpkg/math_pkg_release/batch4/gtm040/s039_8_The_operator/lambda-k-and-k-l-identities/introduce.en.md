This lemma extends the capacity identities from Proposition 9-87 to the full (non-restricted) matrices. The identities $\lambda^E K = k(E)\alpha + {}^E\nu$ and $K l^E = k(E)1 + {}^E d$ show how the equilibrium vector $\lambda^E$ and the escape vector $l^E$ interact with $K$ beyond the support of $E$. The correction terms $^E\nu$ and $^E d$ account for contributions outside $E$.
