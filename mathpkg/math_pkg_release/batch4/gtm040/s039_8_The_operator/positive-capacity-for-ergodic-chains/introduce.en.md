This proposition guarantees positive capacity for two important classes of chains. For noncyclic ergodic chains, the equilibrium distribution gives $\lambda^E = \alpha B^E$ (by Proposition 9-55), ensuring the condition of Proposition 9-89 is met. For symmetric sums of independent random variables (including classical random walks), positivity follows from the symmetric structure. This result is reassuring: for most chains of interest, capacity is well-behaved.
