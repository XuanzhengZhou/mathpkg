By Proposition 6-17,
$$B^E N^{(n)} - N^{(n)} = {}^E N P^{n+1} - {}^E N.$$
Now
$$\operatorname{dual}({}^E N P^{n+1}) = \hat{P}^{n+1} {}^E \hat{N} \to 1^E \hat{\nu} \quad \text{by Proposition 9-47},$$
so that
$${}^E N P^{n+1} \to {}^E d\alpha.$$
Therefore,
$$\lim_{n \to \infty} (B^E N^{(n)} - N^{(n)}) = {}^E d\alpha - {}^E N.$$
