This technical lemma evaluates the limit of $(B^E N^{(n)} - N^{(n)})$ as $n \to \infty$, where $N^{(n)} = I + P + \cdots + P^n$ is the truncated potential matrix. The result is expressed in terms of $^E N$ (the mean occupation time matrix before hitting $E$) and $^E d$ (related to the dual equilibrium distribution). It serves as a key intermediate step for proving Lemma 9-94.
