This corollary restricts the identities of Proposition 9-85 to square matrices indexed by the states of the finite set $E$. The resulting equations provide a direct relationship between the restricted $K$-matrix $K_E$ and the restricted transition matrix $P^E$. These identities are particularly useful for computing $P^E$ from $K_E$, offering a practical alternative to the formula $P^E = T + UNR$ for infinite chains.
