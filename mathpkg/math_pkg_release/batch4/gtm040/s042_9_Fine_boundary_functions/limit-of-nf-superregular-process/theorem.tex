Let $g$ be a $\pi$-integrable function of the form $g = Nf$ with $f \geq 0$. Then
\[
\Pr\left[\lim_{n \to \infty} g(x_n) = 0\right] = 1.
\]
For almost every $x \in B_e$ (with respect to $\mu$) for which $K(\cdot, x)$ is normalized,
\[
\Pr^{K(\cdot, x)}\left[\lim_{n \to \infty} g(x_n) = 0\right] = 1.
\]
