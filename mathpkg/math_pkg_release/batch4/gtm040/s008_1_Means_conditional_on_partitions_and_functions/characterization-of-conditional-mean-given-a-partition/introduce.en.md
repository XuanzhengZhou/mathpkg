Proposition 3-2 gives an equivalent characterization of the conditional mean: it is the unique (a.e.) function constant on cells of the partition whose integral over each cell equals that of the original function. This is analogous to the modern definition of conditional expectation as an $\mathcal{R}$-measurable function satisfying the averaging property.
