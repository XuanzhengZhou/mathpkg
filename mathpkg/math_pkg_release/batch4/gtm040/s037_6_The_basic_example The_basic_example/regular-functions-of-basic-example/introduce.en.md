The regular functions of the basic example are determined by solving the functional equation $r = Pr$. The computation shows that only constant functions satisfy this equation for the forward chain, while the reverse chain admits no regular functions at all. This clean dichotomy illustrates the general duality between regular functions and regular signed measures, and determines the structure of nonnegative superregular functions as potentials plus constants.
