Operations on stochastic processes are defined by

(1) $(f_n, \mathcal{R}_n) \leq (g_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ if $f_n \leq g_n$ a.e. for all $n$.

(2) $a(f_n, \mathcal{R}_n) + b(g_n, \mathcal{R}_n) = (af_n + bg_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$.

(3) $\lim_k (f_n^{(k)}, \mathcal{R}_n) = (f_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ if $\lim f_n^{(k)} = f_n$ a.e. for all $n$.
