**Proof:** We are again to prove that $\pi(Af) = (\pi A)f$, and we do so by setting $\pi = \pi^+ - \pi^-$, $A = A^+ - A^-$, and $f = f^+ - f^-$, applying distributivity, and using Proposition 1-4 on the resulting non-negative matrices.
