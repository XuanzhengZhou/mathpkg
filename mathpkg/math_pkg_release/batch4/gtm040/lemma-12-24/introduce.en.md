If $\mu \in \mathcal{G}_V$, $\iota = (\kappa^0, \kappa^1, \ldots) \in \Omega$, then

$$\frac{\mu([\kappa^0, \kappa^1, \ldots, \kappa^n])}{\nu([\kappa^0, \kappa^1, \ldots, \kappa^n])} = \frac{\mu([\kappa^m, \ldots, \kappa^n])}{\nu([\kappa^m, \ldots, \kappa^n])} \quad 1 \leq m \leq n < \infty.$$
