Let $h \geq 0$ be a normalized regular function, and let $\mu^h = f \mu + \mu_s$ be the Lebesgue decomposition of $\mu^h$ with respect to harmonic measure $\mu$, where $\mu_s$ is singular with respect to $\mu$ and $f$ vanishes on $S$. Then
$$\Pr\left[\lim_{n \to \infty} h(x_n) = f(x_v)\right] = 1.$$
