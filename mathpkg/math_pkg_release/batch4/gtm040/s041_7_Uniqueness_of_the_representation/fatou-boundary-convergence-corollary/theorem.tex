Let $h \geq 0$ be a normalized regular function, and let $\mu^h = f \mu + \mu_s$ (with $f$ equal to zero on $S$) be the Lebesgue decomposition of $\mu^h$ with respect to $\mu$. Then for almost every $x$ $[\mu]$ for which $K(\cdot, x)$ is normalized,
$$\Pr^{K(\cdot, x)}\left[\lim_{n \to \infty} h(x_n) = f(x)\right] = 1.$$
