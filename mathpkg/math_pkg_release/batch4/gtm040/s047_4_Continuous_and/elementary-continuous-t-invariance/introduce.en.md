Lemma 11-10 establishes that every elementary continuous function is fixed by the $T$-transform, i.e., is $T$-continuous. An elementary continuous function is a finite linear combination of columns of $B^E$ for finite sets $E$. The proof reduces to checking the statement for a single column $B^E_j$, which follows from the definition of $T$ and Proposition 11-9.
