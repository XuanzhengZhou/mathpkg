Lemma 11-8 provides a key algebraic identity relating the potential kernel ${}_0N$, the taboo probability $B^E$, and the restricted transition matrix $P^E$ for any finite set $E$ containing the reference state $0$ and a target state $j$. The identity is used to establish continuity properties of the right-hand side $B_{ij}^E - \delta_{0j}$ in the Martin compactification. The lemma generalizes to infinite sets $E$ via duality and Propositions 6-16 and 6-17.
