Theorem 6-43 provides a complete characterization of the mean first passage time matrix $M$ for ergodic Markov chains. The three properties --- diagonal zero with non-negativity, finite-valuedness, and the linear equation $(I-P)M = E-D$ --- uniquely determine $M$. The significance is practical: once a candidate matrix satisfying these three properties is found (even by guessing), it must be the true $M$; no separate probabilistic verification is needed.
