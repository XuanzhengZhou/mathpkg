Let $P$ be a noncyclic recurrent chain. Then $\lim_{n \to \infty} P^n$ exists.
If $P$ is ergodic, then $\lim_{n \to \infty} P^n = 1\alpha$, where $\alpha$ is the unique regular measure with $\alpha 1 = 1$ and $1\alpha = A$ is the Cesaro limit of the powers of $P$.
If $P$ is null, then $\lim_{n \to \infty} P^n = 0$.
