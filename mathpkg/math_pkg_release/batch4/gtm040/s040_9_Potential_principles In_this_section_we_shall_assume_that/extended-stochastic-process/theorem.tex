An \text{extended stochastic process} $\{x_n\}$ is the set of outcome functions for a measure space $(\Omega, \mathcal{G}, \Pr)$ such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\Omega$ is a double sequence space with state space $S \cup \{a, b\}$.
\item $\mathcal{G}$ is the smallest Borel field containing the field of cylinder sets of $\Omega$.
\item $\Pr$ is a $\sigma$-finite measure on $\mathcal{G}$.
\end{enumerate}
