By Proposition 9-109, $(I - P^E)g_E = f_E - (\alpha f)l_E^E$. Hence
$$\nu_E(I - P^E)g_E = \nu_E f_E - (\alpha f)(\nu_E l_E^E) = I(g) - (\alpha f)(\hat{\lambda}^E g).$$
Rearranging gives the first formula. If $P = \hat{P}$, then $\hat{\lambda}^E g = \lambda^E g = -(\alpha f)k(E)$ by Proposition 9-109, and the second formula follows.
