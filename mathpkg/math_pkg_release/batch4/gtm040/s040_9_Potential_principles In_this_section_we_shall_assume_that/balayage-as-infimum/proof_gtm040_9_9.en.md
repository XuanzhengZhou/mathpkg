If $\bar{g}$ is a pure potential and $\bar{g}_E \geq g_E$, then $\bar{g}_E \geq g'_E$ where $g'$ is the balayage potential of $g$. Hence $\bar{g} \geq g'$ by the Domination Principle (Theorem 9-116). Thus $g'$ is the infimum of all such potentials.
