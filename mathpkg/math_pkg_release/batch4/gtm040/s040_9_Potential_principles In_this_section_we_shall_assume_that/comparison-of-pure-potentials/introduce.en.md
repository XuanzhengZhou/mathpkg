This lemma compares two potentials pointwise on their common support $E$, and deduces an ordering of their total charges. If a pure potential $g$ is dominated by a generalized potential $g'$ on the set $E$, then the total charge $\alpha f'$ of $g'$ is at least the total charge $\alpha f$ of $g$. It is a key ingredient in the proof of the Principle of Balayage.
