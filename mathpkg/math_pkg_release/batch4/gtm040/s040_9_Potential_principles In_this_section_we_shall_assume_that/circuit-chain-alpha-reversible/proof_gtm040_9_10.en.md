We have $\alpha_i P_{ij} = \alpha_i \frac{c_{ij}}{\sum_k c_{ik}} = c_{ij} = c_{ji} = \alpha_j P_{ji}$. Hence
$$\sum_i \alpha_i P_{ij} = \sum_i \alpha_j P_{ji} = \alpha_j.$$
Therefore $\alpha$ is regular. Since $\hat{P}_{ij} = (\alpha_j P_{ji}) / \alpha_i = P_{ij}$, the $\alpha$-dual of $P$ is $P$.
