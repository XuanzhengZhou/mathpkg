Consider the null chain on integer lattice points $z = (x, y)$ with $x, y \geq 1$. Let $n = x + y$ and state $0 = (1, 1)$. Define
$$P_{(x,y), (x+1,y)} = \frac{x}{x+y+1}, \quad P_{(x,y), (x,y+1)} = \frac{y}{x+y+1}, \quad P_{(x,y), 0} = \frac{1}{x+y+1}.$$
Verify that $P$ is null recurrent and that $\alpha_z = 2/[(n-1)n]$.
