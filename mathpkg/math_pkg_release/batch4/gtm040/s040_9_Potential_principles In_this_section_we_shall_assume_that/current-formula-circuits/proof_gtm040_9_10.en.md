We have
$$_0 \bar{H}_{0\bar{F}} = \sum_k P_{0k} (1 - {}_{\bar{F}}H_{k0}) = \sum_k P_{0k} (v_0 - v_k) = \frac{1}{\alpha_0} \sum_k c_{0k}(v_0 - v_k) = \frac{\mu_0}{\alpha_0},$$
where the first equality uses the Markov property and the definition of hitting probabilities, and the last equality uses the definition of current from Kirchhoff's law.
