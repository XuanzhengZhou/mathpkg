Every non-negative superregular function $h$ admits a Poisson-Martin representation as an integral of the kernel $K(i, x)$ against a Borel measure $\mu^h$ on the Martin completion $S^*$. This generalizes the classical Poisson integral formula for harmonic functions on the unit disk.
