An extended stochastic process $\{x_n\}$ is an \text{extended chain} with transition matrix $P$ if the following conditions hold for each finite subset $E$ of $S$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item The domain of $u_E$ has positive measure.
\item $\Pr[u_E = -\infty] = 0$.
\item The process watched starting in $E$ is a Markov chain with transition matrix $P$ and finite starting measure.
\item For all $j \in S$, $\nu_j < \infty$, where $\nu_j = \sum_n \Pr[x_n = j]$.
\end{enumerate}
