Let $\{x_n\}$ be an extended chain defined on $\Omega$ with measure $\Pr$. Set $\Pr'[\omega \in A] = \Pr[\omega' \in A]$ for all sets $A$ for which the right side is defined, where $\omega'_n = \omega_{-n}$. The extended stochastic process on $\Omega$ defined by the measure $\Pr'$ is called the \text{reverse} of the extended chain.
