We prove the result for $u_E$. We have
$$\{\omega \mid u_E(\omega) \leq k\} = \bigcup_{i \in E} \bigcup_{n=-\infty}^{k} \{\omega \mid x_n(\omega) = i\},$$
and each set on the right is a countable union of cylinder sets, hence in $\mathcal{G}$. The union of these sets over $k$ is the domain of $u_E$, which is thus measurable.
