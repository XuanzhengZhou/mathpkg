The Recurrent Principle of Balayage constructs, for any pure potential $g$, a unique pure potential $g'$ supported on a finite set $E$ that agrees with $g$ on $E$, is dominated by $g$ everywhere, and has larger total charge. The explicit formula uses the balayage operator $B^E$ and the equilibrium measure.
