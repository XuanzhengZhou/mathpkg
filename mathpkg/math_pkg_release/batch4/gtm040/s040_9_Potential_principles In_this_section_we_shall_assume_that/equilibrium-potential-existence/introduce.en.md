For any finite set $E$ containing both distinguished states $0$ and $1$, there exists a unique equilibrium potential. Its charge is the normalized left eigenvector $l^E$, its value on $E$ is $-k(E)$, and it extends to states outside $E$ by $-k(E) - {}^E d_j$.
