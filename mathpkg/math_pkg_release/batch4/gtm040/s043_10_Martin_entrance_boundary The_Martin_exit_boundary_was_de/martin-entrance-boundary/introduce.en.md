The Martin entrance boundary $*S$ is the completion of the state space $S$ under the Martin entrance metric $d'$, analogous to the Martin exit boundary but designed for representing $P$-superregular measures rather than superregular functions. Each boundary point $x \in *S$ carries a limiting superregular measure $J(x,\cdot)$. The extreme points $B^e$, where these measures are minimal and normalized, form a Borel subset disjoint from $S$ and serve as the Poisson-Martin representing measures for superregular measures.
