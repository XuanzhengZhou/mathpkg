Proposition 10-51 establishes the almost-sure existence of the limit $z_v$ of an extended Markov chain along the time direction, taking values in the Martin exit boundary $S^*$. On paths where $v$ is finite, the limit lies in the original state space $S$; when $v$ is infinite, the limit falls in the exit boundary's extreme points $B_e$. The probability distribution of $z_v$ is expressed as an integral involving the measure $\mu$ on the boundary and the function $q^\nu$.
