This corollary characterizes the balayage potential $\bar{g} = B^E g$ as the minimal pure potential that dominates $g$ on $E$. Among all pure potentials that agree with or exceed $g$ on $E$, the balayage potential is the smallest in the pointwise ordering. This extremal characterization is fundamental to understanding the balayage operation.
