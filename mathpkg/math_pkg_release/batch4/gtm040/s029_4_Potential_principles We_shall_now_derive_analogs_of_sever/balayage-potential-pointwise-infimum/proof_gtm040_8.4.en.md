Apply conclusion (2) of Proposition 8-16, which states that $B^E g$ is the pointwise infimum of all non-negative superregular functions that dominate $g$ on $E$. Since $B^E g$ is itself a pure potential (by Proposition 8-25), it is the pointwise infimum of all pure potentials which dominate $g$ on $E$.
