This lemma establishes that the class of superregular functions is closed under arbitrary pointwise infima. If $\{h_\beta\}$ is any family of functions satisfying $P h_\beta \leq h_\beta$, then their pointwise infimum $\inf_\beta h_\beta$ also satisfies this superregularity inequality. This closure property is essential for constructing minimal superregular majorants and for the potential-theoretic arguments that follow.
