This theorem establishes that the class of pure potentials is closed under arbitrary pointwise infima. Combined with Lemma 8-49 (which shows superregular functions are closed under infima), this result confirms that the infimum of any family of pure potentials remains a pure potential. This closure property is essential for constructing canonical potentials via extremal operations in Markov potential theory.
