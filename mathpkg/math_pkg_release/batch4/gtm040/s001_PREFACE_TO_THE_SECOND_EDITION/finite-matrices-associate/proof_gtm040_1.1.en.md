The result follows from Corollary 1-5. Since $A$, $B$, and $C$ have finite index sets and finite entries, their absolute values $|A|$, $|B|$, $|C|$ also have finite index sets and finite entries. The product $|A|\,|B|\,|C|$ is a finite sum of finite terms and therefore has all finite entries. By Corollary 1-5, the matrices associate.
