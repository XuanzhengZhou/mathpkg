Let $A$ be a matrix with index sets $K$ and $M$, and let $B$ be a matrix with index sets $M$ and $N$. The product $C = AB$ is defined to be the matrix with index sets $K$ and $N$ whose entries are given by
$$C_{kn} = \sum_{m \in M} A_{km} B_{mn},$$
provided that the sum is well defined for every $k \in K$ and $n \in N$.
