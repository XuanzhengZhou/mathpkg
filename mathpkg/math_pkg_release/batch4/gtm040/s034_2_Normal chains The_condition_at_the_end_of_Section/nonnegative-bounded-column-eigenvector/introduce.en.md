Lemma 9-32 is a monotonicity result for Markov chains: if $h$ is a non-negative bounded column vector satisfying $Ph \leq h$ (i.e., $h$ is superharmonic), then the limit $\lim_n P^n h$ exists. This lemma is the foundation for defining the hitting probability matrix $\lambda^E$ and studying ergodic sets, as it guarantees the convergence of the iterates needed in Definition 9-35.
