Corollary 9-28 provides the most elegant formula for potentials in a normal chain: $g = -Gf$ whenever $f$ is finitely supported with zero total charge. This result is obtained by substituting the identity of Corollary 9-27 into Proposition 9-21. Its dual statement asserts that for a signed measure $\mu$ with $\mu 1 = 0$ and finite $\sum_k \mu_k {}^0N_{kk}$, the potential $\nu$ satisfies $\nu = -\mu C$.
