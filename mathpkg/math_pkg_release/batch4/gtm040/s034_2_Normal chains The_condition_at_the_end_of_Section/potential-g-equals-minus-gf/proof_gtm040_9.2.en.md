By Proposition 9-21,
$$g = [{}^0N - 1\,{}^0\nu]f.$$
Using Corollary 9-27, $${}^0N - 1\,{}^0\nu = -G + \frac{1}{\alpha_0}\{G_{i0}\alpha_j\}_{i,j},$$
so
$$g = \left[-G + \frac{1}{\alpha_0}\{G_{i0}\alpha_j\}_{i,j}\right]f = -Gf + \frac{1}{\alpha_0} G_{i0} (\alpha f).$$
Since $\alpha f = 0$, the second term vanishes, giving $g = -Gf$.
