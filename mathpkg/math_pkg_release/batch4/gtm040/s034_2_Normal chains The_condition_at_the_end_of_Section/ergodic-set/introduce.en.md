Definition 9-40 introduces the notion of an ergodic set: a set $E$ of states for which the restricted chain $P^E$ (obtained by deleting rows and columns not in $E$) is an ergodic chain. Ergodic sets provide the natural context for studying potentials with finite support, as the restriction to an ergodic set preserves the ergodic property.
