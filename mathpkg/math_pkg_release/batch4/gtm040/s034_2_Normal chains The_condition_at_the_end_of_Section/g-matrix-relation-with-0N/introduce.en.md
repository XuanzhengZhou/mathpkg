Corollary 9-27 provides a fundamental algebraic identity linking the fundamental matrix ${}^0N$ with the matrix $G$ in a normal chain. The difference ${}^0N_{ij} - {}^0\nu_j$ is expressed as $-G_{ij}$ plus a correction term $G_{i0} \alpha_j / \alpha_0$. This identity is a key computational tool, reducing many potential-theoretic calculations to manipulations of the $G$ matrix.
