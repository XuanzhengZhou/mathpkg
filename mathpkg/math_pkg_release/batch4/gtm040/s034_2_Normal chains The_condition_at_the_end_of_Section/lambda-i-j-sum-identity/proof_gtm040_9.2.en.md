By definition, ${}^i\lambda_j = \lambda^{\{i,j\}}_j$ and ${}^j\lambda_i = \lambda^{\{i,j\}}_i$ are the two non-zero entries in the row of $\lambda^{\{i,j\}}$ (the entry for $i$ and the entry for $j$). Since $\{i,j\}$ is a finite set, Proposition 9-38 applies and $\{i,j\}$ is a small set. Therefore each row of $\lambda^{\{i,j\}}$ sums to $1$, giving ${}^i\lambda_j + {}^j\lambda_i = \lambda^{\{i,j\}}_i + \lambda^{\{i,j\}}_j = 1$.
