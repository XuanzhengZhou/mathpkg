Proposition 9-38 establishes that in a normal chain, every finite set of states is a small set. The proof uses Lemma 9-37: for a finite set $E$, the finiteness condition $\sum_{k \in E} {}^0N_{kk} P^E_{kj} < \infty$ is automatically satisfied, so $\lambda^E$ exists, and the row sum $\lambda^E 1$ equals $1$ by an interchange of limit and finite sum. This result ensures a plentiful supply of small sets in normal chains.
