Let $E$ and $F$ be disjoint small ergodic sets. There exists a unique function $x$ such that (1) $x$ is 1 on $E$ and 0 on $F$, (2) for $i \in E$, $x_i$ is the probability of return to $F$ before $E$ and for $i \in F$, $-x_i$ is the probability of return to $E$ before $F$, and (3) $x$ differs from a potential by a constant.
