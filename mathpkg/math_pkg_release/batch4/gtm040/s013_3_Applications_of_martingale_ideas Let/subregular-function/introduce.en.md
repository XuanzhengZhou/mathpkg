A subregular function is a column vector $f$ on the state space of a Markov chain with transition matrix $P$ satisfying $f \leq Pf$. This means the function value at each state is at most the $P$-weighted average of its values at neighboring states. Subregular functions are the discrete analogue of subharmonic functions and correspond naturally to submartingales when evaluated along sample paths of the Markov chain.
