$$(\text{dual } B^E)_{ij} = \begin{cases} {}^E \hat{N}_{ij} & \text{if } i \in E \\ 0 & \text{if } i \notin E, \end{cases}$$
where ${}^E \hat{N}_{ij}$ is the mean number of times that the $\alpha$-dual process started at $i$ is in $j$ before returning to $E$.
Equivalently,
$$\text{dual } B^E = \begin{pmatrix} {}^E \hat{N} \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}.$$
