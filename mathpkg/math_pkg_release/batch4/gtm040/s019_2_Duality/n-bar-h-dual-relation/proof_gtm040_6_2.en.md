Apply Proposition 6-19 with $E = \{i\}$. Then the sum on the left reduces to the single term $\alpha_i {}^i \bar{N}_{ij}$, and the dual hitting probability $\hat{h}_j^E$ becomes $\hat{H}_{ji}$ (the probability starting from $j$ to hit $i$ in the dual chain). Thus

$$\alpha_i {}^i \bar{N}_{ij} = \alpha_j \hat{H}_{ji},$$

which yields the stated formula.
