This proposition shows that the taboo probability matrix $P^E$ (representing the process watched until it enters the set $E$) commutes with duality in a natural way: the dual of the taboo probability matrix for $P$ equals the taboo probability matrix for the dual chain $\hat{P}$. This result is essential for transferring probabilistic identities between the original chain and its dual, and will be used extensively in Chapters 8 and 9.
