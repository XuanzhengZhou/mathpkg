This proposition is obtained by applying duality to the first identity of Proposition 6-17, using Propositions 6-15 and 6-16. The result gives an identity relating the expected visit matrix ${}^E \bar{N}$, the transition matrix $P$, and the taboo probability matrix $P^E$. Because the identity is first derived for all reverse processes $\hat{P}$ via duality, and every process can be viewed as the dual of some reverse chain, the identity holds universally for all Markov chains. This result will be used in Chapters 8 and 9.
