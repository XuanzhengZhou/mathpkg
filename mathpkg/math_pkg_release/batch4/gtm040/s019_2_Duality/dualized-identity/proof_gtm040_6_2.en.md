Apply duality to the first identity of Proposition 6-17, using Propositions 6-15 and 6-16. Then

$$\begin{pmatrix} {}^E \hat{N} \end{pmatrix} (I - \hat{P}) = \begin{pmatrix} I - \hat{P}^E \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}.$$

Since this identity holds for all reverse processes $\hat{P}$, and since every process is the dual of some reverse chain, it holds for all processes.
