This proposition establishes that the alpha-dual operation preserves the fundamental structure of a Markov chain. First, the dual matrix $\hat{P}$ is itself a valid transition matrix, with non-negative entries and row sums at most 1. Second, if every pair of states communicates in the original chain $P$ (i.e., each state is reachable from every other), then the same holds in the dual chain $\hat{P}$, so the communication structure is preserved under duality.
