Apply Proposition 6-19. Since $P$ is recurrent, $\hat{P}$ is also recurrent (by Proposition 6-12), and therefore $\hat{h}_j^E = 1$ for all $j$ (hitting probability in a recurrent chain is always 1). Thus

$$\sum_{i \in E} \alpha_i {}^E \bar{N}_{ij} = \alpha_j \cdot 1 = \alpha_j.$$
