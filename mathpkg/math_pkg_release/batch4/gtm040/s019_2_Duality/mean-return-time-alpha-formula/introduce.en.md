This proposition provides a weighted sum formula for the mean return times to a set $E$, using the regular measure $\alpha$ of a recurrent chain. The sum $\sum_{i \in E} \alpha_i \bar{M}_{iE}$ equals the total measure $\alpha 1 = \sum_j \alpha_j$. This identity, derived from Corollary 6-22, shows how the regular measure $\alpha$ relates to mean return times and will be used to compute explicit expressions for mean first passage times in terms of the measure $\alpha$.
