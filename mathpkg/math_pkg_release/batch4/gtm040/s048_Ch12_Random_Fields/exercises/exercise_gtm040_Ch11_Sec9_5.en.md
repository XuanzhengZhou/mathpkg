Let $P$ be normal and let $h$ be continuous. The balayage potential of $h$ on a small ergodic set $E$ is $B^E h - (\lambda^E h)1$ (see Proposition 9-43). Let $\{E_k\}$ be an increasing sequence of finite sets with union $S$. Prove that the balayage potentials of $h$ on $E_k$ converge to $h - (\int h \, d\beta)1$ on $S$.
