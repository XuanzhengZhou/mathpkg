Verify the interpretations of $\beta$ and $\hat{\beta}$ as limits of rows of $P^m$ and $\hat{P}^m$ by showing this: For either chain $n$ is most probably large in the long run. For $\hat{P}$, the ratio $x/n$ cannot change much in a few steps (if $n$ is large), but for $P$ a transition to $0$ is always possible.
