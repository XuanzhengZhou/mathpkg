**Proof:** Let $g_i = {}^F H_{iE}$, the probability starting at $i$ that $E$ is reached before $F$. Clearly, $g$ is 1 on $E$ and 0 on $F$. Furthermore, $0 \leq g \leq h^E$. Since $P^n h^E \to 0$ for the equilibrium set $E$, we have $P^n g \to 0$.
