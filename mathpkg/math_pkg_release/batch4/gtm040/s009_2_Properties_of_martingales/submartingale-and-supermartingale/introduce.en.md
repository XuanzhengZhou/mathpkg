A supermartingale satisfies $f_n \geq M[f_{n+1} \mid \mathcal{R}_n]$ (decreasing on average), while a submartingale satisfies $f_n \leq M[f_{n+1} \mid \mathcal{R}_n]$ (increasing on average). These generalize martingales to processes with drift in a preferred direction.
