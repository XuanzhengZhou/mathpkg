The Strong Law of Large Numbers (Theorem 3-19) asserts that the sample average $s_n^* = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n y_k$ of i.i.d. random variables with finite mean $a$ converges almost surely to $a$. The proof given uses the Martingale Convergence Theorem via generating functions.
