Proposition 4-13 relates the shifted expected-visit matrix $\bar{N}$ to the original $N$ via the transition matrix $P$. The identity $\bar{N} = PN$ follows from applying the systems theorem (Theorem 4-11) with a constant shift of one step. Adding the identity $I$ to both sides yields $N = I + PN$, which decomposes the total expected visit count into one initial visit plus the expected visits after the first transition.
