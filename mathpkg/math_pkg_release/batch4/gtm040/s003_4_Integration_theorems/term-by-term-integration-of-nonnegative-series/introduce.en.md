This corollary justifies integrating a series of non-negative functions term-by-term. It follows directly from the linearity of the integral (Proposition 1-45) applied to partial sums and the Monotone Convergence Theorem (Theorem 1-44). This is a powerful tool that has no general counterpart in Riemann integration.
