This proposition describes the complete solution structure for linear difference equations with constant coefficients, paralleling the theory of linear ODEs. The homogeneous solution space has dimension $ and is spanned by functions ^j a^k$ for each characteristic root $ of multiplicity $. The general solution of a nonhomogeneous equation is the sum of a particular solution and the homogeneous solution.
