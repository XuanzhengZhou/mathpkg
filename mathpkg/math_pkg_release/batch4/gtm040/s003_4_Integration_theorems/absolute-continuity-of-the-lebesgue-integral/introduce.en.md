This proposition establishes that the indefinite Lebesgue integral of an integrable function is absolutely continuous with respect to the measure $\mu$. In other words, the integral over very small sets becomes very small. The proof truncates $f$ at level $n$ and uses the Monotone Convergence Theorem to control the tail.
