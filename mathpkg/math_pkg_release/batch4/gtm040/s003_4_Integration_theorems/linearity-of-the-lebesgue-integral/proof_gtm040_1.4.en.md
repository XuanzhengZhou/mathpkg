For non-negative $f, g$: trivial for simple functions. For general non-negative functions, apply Proposition 1-38 to find monotone sequences of simple functions $\{t_n\} \to f$ and $\{u_n\} \to g$. Then $s_n = t_n + u_n \to h$, and $\int_E s_n d\mu = \int_E t_n d\mu + \int_E u_n d\mu$. The result follows from Theorem 1-44. For mixed signs, decompose using positive and negative parts.
