By the discussion in Section 1-6e and Lemma 1-66: a non-empty set of positive integers closed under addition contains all sufficiently large multiples of its greatest common divisor. Lemma 6-26 established that $T$ is non-empty and closed under addition. Therefore $T$ contains all sufficiently large multiples of $d = \gcd(T)$.
