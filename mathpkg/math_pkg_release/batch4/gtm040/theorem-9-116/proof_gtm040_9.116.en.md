**Proof:**

$$g \geq B^E g - (\alpha f')^E d \quad \text{by Proposition 9-110}$$
$$\geq B^E g' - (\alpha f')^E d \quad \text{by hypothesis and by Lemma 9-112}$$
$$= g' \quad \text{by Proposition 9-109}.$$
