Definition 8-76 extends the notion of charges and potentials from Markov chains to general stochastic processes. A stochastic process $(f_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ is called a charge if its cumulative expected absolute value is finite and its corresponding function on the space-time chain is a charge in the potential-theoretic sense. The potential $(g_n, \mathcal{R}_n)$ is then the corresponding potential function.
