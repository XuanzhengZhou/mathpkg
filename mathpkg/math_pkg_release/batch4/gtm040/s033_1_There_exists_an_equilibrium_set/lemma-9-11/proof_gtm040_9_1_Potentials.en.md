If $\mu \mathbf{1}$ is finite, then
$$\sum_i |\mu_i| \, {}_0 N_{ij} = \sum_i |\mu_i| \, {}_0 H_{ij} \, {}_0 N_{jj} \leq {}_0 N_{jj} \sum_i |\mu_i| < \infty.$$
The dual statement follows by duality.
