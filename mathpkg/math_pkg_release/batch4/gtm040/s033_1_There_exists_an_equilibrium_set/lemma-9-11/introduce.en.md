Lemma 9-11 establishes that finiteness of the total charge ($\mu \mathbf{1}$ or $\alpha f$) implies finiteness of the corresponding product with the excluded-state fundamental matrix ${}_0 N$. This ensures that the expressions appearing in the main theorems are well-defined.
