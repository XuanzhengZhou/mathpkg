Lemma 8-74 is a crucial bridge between probability theory and potential theory. It states that the conditional expectation operator on stochastic processes corresponds exactly to the transition operator $P$ acting on functions on the space-time chain. This correspondence allows results from potential theory (about superregular functions, potentials, and charges) to be translated into results about martingales, supermartingales, and stochastic processes.
