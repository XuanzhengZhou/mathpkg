If $\mu \mathbf{1} = 0$, then
$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_k \mu_k \left[ N_{ki}^{(n)} - \frac{\alpha_i}{\alpha_0} N_{k0}^{(n)} \right] = \sum_k \mu_k \, {}_0 N_{ki}.$$
Dually, if $\alpha f = 0$, then
$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_k \left[ N_{ik}^{(n)} - N_{0k}^{(n)} \right] f_k = \sum_k {}_0 N_{ik} f_k.$$
