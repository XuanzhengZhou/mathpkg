Corollary 9-16 provides a clean criterion for recognizing potentials in recurrent chains: a function $g$ satisfying $(I-P)g = f$ is a potential precisely when its charge $f$ has finite total charge and $g$ vanishes under iterated application of $P$. This is the recurrent analogue of the transient criterion and is widely used in applications.
