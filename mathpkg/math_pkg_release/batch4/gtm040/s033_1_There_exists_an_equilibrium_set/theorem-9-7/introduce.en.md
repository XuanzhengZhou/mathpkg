Theorem 9-7 is a technical but essential limit formula in recurrent potential theory. It expresses the fundamental matrix ${}_k N_{ij}$ (with state $k$ excluded) as the limit of a particular linear combination of occupation time counts. The formula involves four occupation time sequences and the regular measure $\alpha$, and its proof uses Lemma 9-6 (a convolution convergence lemma) to handle the intricate cancellation of divergent terms.
