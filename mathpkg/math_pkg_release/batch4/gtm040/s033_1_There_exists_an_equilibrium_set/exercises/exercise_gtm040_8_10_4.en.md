Let $P$ have only transient states, let $\pi$ be a specified probability vector, and let $\alpha = \pi N$. Let $f \geq 0$ be a charge, and define the random variable
$$s = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} f(x_n).$$
Show that $s$ is finite a.e. and that $M_{\pi}[s] = \alpha f$. What is the value of $M_i[s]$? Give a game-interpretation.
