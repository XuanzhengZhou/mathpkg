Lemma 9-9 provides a linear relation connecting three different excluded-state $N$-matrices. These identities are fundamental building blocks in recurrent potential theory, used to relate quantities defined with respect to different excluded states. The proof combines hitting probability identities with the relationship between ${}_j N_{ii}$ and ${}_i N_{jj}$ from Lemma 9-8.
