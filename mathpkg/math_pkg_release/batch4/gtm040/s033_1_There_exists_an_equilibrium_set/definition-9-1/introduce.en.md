Definition 9-1 extends the notion of charges and potentials from transient chains to recurrent chains (null or noncyclic ergodic). The key difference from the transient case is that the limit $\lim_n (I + P + \cdots + P^{n-1})$ replaces the fundamental matrix $N = \sum_{k=0}^\infty P^k$, since in recurrent chains $N$ has all entries infinite. Duality is defined with respect to the regular measure $\alpha$, and the dual of a left charge for $P$ is a right charge for the dual chain $\hat{P}$.
