Lemma 8-66 shows that the truncated charges ${}_m f$ form a monotone increasing sequence converging to the full charge $f$. This monotone convergence property is crucial for extending Theorem 8-67 from bounded to unbounded random variables: since each ${}_m z$ is bounded, Lemma 8-64 applies to each truncation, and the limits are taken via the monotone convergence theorem.
