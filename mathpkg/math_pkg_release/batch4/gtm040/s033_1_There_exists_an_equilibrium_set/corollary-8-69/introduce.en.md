Corollary 8-69 provides a comparison between successive moments of the absorption time. It establishes that $a^{(r)}$ and $N a^{(r-1)}$ are comparable in the sense that each is bounded by a constant multiple of the other. As a consequence, the finiteness of the $r$-th moment is equivalent to the finiteness of $N a^{(r-1)}$.
