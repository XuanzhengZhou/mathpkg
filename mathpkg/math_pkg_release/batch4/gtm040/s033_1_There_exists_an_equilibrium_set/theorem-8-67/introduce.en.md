Theorem 8-67 generalizes the potential representation $h = Nf$ to unbounded random variables. Using truncation (${}_m z = \min(m, z)$) and Lemmas 8-64 through 8-66, the theorem extends the bounded case to the unbounded setting. The proof applies Lemma 8-64 to each bounded truncation ${}_m z$ and then takes limits via monotone convergence.
