Definition 8-73 formalizes the algebraic and order structure on stochastic processes adapted to a filtration $\{\mathcal{R}_n\}$. These operations are defined componentwise almost everywhere and preserve the correspondence between stochastic processes and functions on the space-time chain. Under this definition, the correspondence preserves inequalities, linear combinations, and limits.
