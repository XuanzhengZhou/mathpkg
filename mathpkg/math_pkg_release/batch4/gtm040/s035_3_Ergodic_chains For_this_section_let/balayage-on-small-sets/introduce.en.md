This corollary provides a computational formula for the balayage operator $B^E$ acting on functions in a noncyclic ergodic chain. It expresses $B^E h$ in terms of $h$ minus the taboo potential $^{E}\!N$ applied to the charge $f = (I-P)h$. When the charge $f$ is supported entirely within $E$, the balayage leaves $h$ unchanged, and for bounded $h$ the equilibrium measure satisfies $\lambda^E h = \alpha h$.
