This theorem gives a clean characterization of integrable potentials in noncyclic ergodic chains: a function $g$ with finite stationary mean $\alpha g$ is a potential precisely when its stationary mean is zero. Moreover, the potential can be expressed using the taboo potential operator $^{0}\!N$ and the deviation from the stationary limit $I - A$. This is the ergodic-chain analogue of Corollary 9-17 for null chains.
