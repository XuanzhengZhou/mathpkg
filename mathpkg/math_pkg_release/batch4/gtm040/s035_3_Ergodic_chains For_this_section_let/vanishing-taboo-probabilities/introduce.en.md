This lemma establishes that in any infinite ergodic chain, the taboo expected occupation times $^{i}\!N_{kj}$ and the taboo hitting probabilities $^{i}\!H_{kj}$ both tend to zero as the target state $j$ tends to infinity. The result follows from the finiteness of the mean first passage time $M_{ki}$, since the sum over all $j$ of $^{i}\!N_{kj}$ equals $M_{ki}$. This lemma is essential for proving the vanishing of small set measures at infinity.
