This theorem establishes sufficient conditions for the existence of potentials of charges in noncyclic ergodic chains. For functions $f$ with zero stationary mean ($\alpha f = 0$), the finiteness of $Gf$ guarantees that $f$ is a charge with potential $g = -Gf$. The dual statement for signed measures is also given. This generalizes earlier existence results to a broader class of charges, providing a practical criterion for potential existence.
