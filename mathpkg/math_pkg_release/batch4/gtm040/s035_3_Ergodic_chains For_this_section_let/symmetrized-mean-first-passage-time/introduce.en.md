This lemma establishes several important identities involving the symmetrized mean first passage time $S_{ij} = M_{ij} + M_{ji}$. The quantity $S_{ij}$ is symmetric ($S_{ij} = S_{ji}$) and invariant under duality ($S_{ij} = \hat{S}_{ij}$). It can be expressed as $^{i}\!N_{jj}/\alpha_j$, linking it to taboo occupation times. The lemma also provides formulas expressing $M_{ij}$ in terms of dual equilibrium measures and gives the ratio $M_{ji}/M_{ij}$ in terms of dual small set measures, which is used to study asymptotic behavior.
