This lemma establishes that the $r$-th moment vector $c_0^{(r)}$ (encoding the stationary-weighted $r$-th moment of the hitting time to state $0$) is identical to its dual counterpart $\hat{c}_0^{(r)}$. The proof exploits the duality relation $\alpha_i \Pr_i[t_0 = m] = \alpha_0 \Pr_0[\hat{t}_i = m]$ and Lemma 9-69 to show equality of the entire distribution, from which equality of all moments follows by multiplying by $m^r$ and summing.
