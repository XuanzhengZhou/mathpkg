This corollary provides the fundamental operator equations linking the transition matrix $P$, the potential matrix $C$, the Green matrix $G$, and the stationary limit matrix $A$ in a noncyclic ergodic chain. These identities are the ergodic-chain analogues of the resolvent equations and are essential for the algebraic manipulation of potentials.
