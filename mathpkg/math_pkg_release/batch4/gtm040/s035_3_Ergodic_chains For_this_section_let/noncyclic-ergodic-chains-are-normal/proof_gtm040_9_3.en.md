By Lemma 9-48, $C$ exists and satisfies $C_{ij} = M_{ij}\alpha_j$. Since $\hat{P}$ is noncyclic ergodic, $\hat{C}$ exists and hence $G$ exists. Thus $P$ is normal by Theorem 9-26.

Dually, $G_{ij} = \alpha_j\hat{M}_{ji}$ or $G = \hat{M}^T D^{-1}$.
