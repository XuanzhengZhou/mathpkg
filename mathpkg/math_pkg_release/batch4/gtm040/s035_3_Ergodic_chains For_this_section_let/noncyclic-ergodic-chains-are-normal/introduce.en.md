This theorem establishes that every noncyclic ergodic Markov chain possesses the normality property required for potential theory. The potential matrix $C$ is expressed in terms of the mean first passage time matrix $M$ and the stationary distribution $\alpha$, while the dual potential matrix $G$ admits an analogous expression using the dual chain $\hat{P}$. This gives concrete computational formulas for the fundamental potential-theoretic quantities.
