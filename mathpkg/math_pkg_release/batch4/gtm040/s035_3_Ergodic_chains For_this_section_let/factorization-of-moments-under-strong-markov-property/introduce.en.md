This lemma establishes that under the strong Markov property, the joint moment of the hitting time of $i$ raised to power $r$ and the subsequent passage time from $i$ to $j$ raised to power $s$ factorizes as the product of $M_\pi[t_i^r]$ and $M_i[t_j^s]$. This factorization is crucial for relating the moment vectors $b^{(r)}$, $c^{(r)}$, and the moment matrix $M^{(r)}$ in the study of ergodic degree.
