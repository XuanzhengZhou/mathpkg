**Proof:** $T$ is clearly non-empty since $P$ is recurrent. Suppose $m$ and $n$ are integers in $T$. Then $(P^m)_{ii} > 0$ and $(P^n)_{ii} > 0$, so that

$$(P^{m+n})_{ii} = \sum_k (P^m)_{ik}(P^n)_{ki}$$

$$\geq (P^m)_{ii}(P^n)_{ii}$$

$$> 0.$$

Hence $m + n$ is in $T$.
