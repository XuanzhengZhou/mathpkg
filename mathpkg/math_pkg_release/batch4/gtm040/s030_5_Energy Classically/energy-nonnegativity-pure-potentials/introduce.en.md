Classically energy is non-negative. This result is fundamental to potential theory. The proof uses the restricted transition operator $P^E$ and the fact that $P^E$ is a transition matrix with $\alpha_E$ being $P^E$-superregular, which makes the coefficients $m_i$ and $\pi_i$ non-negative.
