This lemma computes the inner product of a potential $g$ with the equilibrium potential $h^E$ when the chain is symmetric ($P = \hat{P}$). The result $(g, h^E) = \alpha f$ is crucial for proving the fundamental equilibrium theorem (Theorem 8-61), which justifies the name "equilibrium potential."
