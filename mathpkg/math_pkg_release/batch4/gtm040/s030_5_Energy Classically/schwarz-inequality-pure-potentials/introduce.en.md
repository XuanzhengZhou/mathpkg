This lemma extends Schwarz's inequality from potentials of finite support to all pure potentials of finite energy. The proof uses approximation by finite restrictions (Lemma 8-56): one forms approximations $g^{(n)}$ and $\bar{g}^{(n)}$ with finite support, applies Lemma 8-55 to the approximations, and then passes to the limit.
