This proposition shows that the energy of a potential depends only on the values of the potential on its support $E$ and can be expressed using the restricted transition operator $P^E$. The formula $\mathbf{I}(g) = \nu_E[(I - P^E)g_E]$ makes explicit the dependence of energy on $P^E$, which was disguised in the original definition $\mathbf{I}(g) = \mu g$.
