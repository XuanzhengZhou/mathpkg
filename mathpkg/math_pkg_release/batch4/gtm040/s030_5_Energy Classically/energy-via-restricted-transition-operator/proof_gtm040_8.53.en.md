Since $f$ has support in $E$,
$$(I - P^E)g_E = f_E$$
by Proposition 8-19, and
$$\nu_E[(I - P^E)g_E] = \nu_E f_E = \nu f = \mathbf{I}(g).$$

The other half of the proposition is the dual of the first half.
