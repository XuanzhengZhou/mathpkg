This approximation lemma allows the inner product of two arbitrary potentials to be expressed as the limit of inner products of their restrictions to finite sets. By expanding a finite set $E_n$ to cover the whole state space $S$, the corresponding finite-restriction potentials $g^{(n)}$ converge monotonically to $g$.
