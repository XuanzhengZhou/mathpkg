Let $\{E_n\}$ be an increasing sequence of finite sets with union the set of all states $S$, and let
$$f^{(n)} = \binom{f_{E_n}}{0}, \quad \bar{f}^{(n)} = \binom{\bar{f}_{E_n}}{0},$$
$$g^{(n)} = Nf^{(n)}, \quad \bar{g}^{(n)} = N\bar{f}^{(n)}.$$
Then
$$(g, \bar{g}) = \lim_{n \to \infty} (g^{(n)}, \bar{g}^{(n)}).$$
