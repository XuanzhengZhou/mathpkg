The class of measurable functions is closed under pointwise addition and multiplication. The proof uses a clever rational-number trick: for the sum, one writes $\{x \mid (f+g)(x) > c\}$ as a countable union over rational $r_n$ of $\{x \mid f(x) > c + r_n\} \cap \{x \mid g(x) > -r_n\}$, reducing to the measurability of $f$ and $g$ individually.
