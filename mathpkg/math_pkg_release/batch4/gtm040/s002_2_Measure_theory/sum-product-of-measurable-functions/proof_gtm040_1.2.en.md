We prove only the assertion about $f + g$. Order the rational numbers and call the $n$th one $r_n$. Then
$$\{x \mid (f + g)(x) > c\} = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} \left(\{x \mid f(x) > c + r_n\} \cap \{x \mid g(x) > -r_n\}\right),$$
so that $f + g$ is measurable.
