From
$$\{x \mid f(x) \leq c\} = \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} \left\{x \mid f(x) < c + \frac{1}{n}\right\},$$
$$\{x \mid f(x) > c\} = X - \{x \mid f(x) \leq c\},$$
$$\{x \mid f(x) \geq c\} = \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty} \left\{x \mid f(x) > c - \frac{1}{n}\right\},$$
and
$$\{x \mid f(x) < c\} = X - \{x \mid f(x) \geq c\},$$
we see that (1) implies (2), that (2) implies (3), that (3) implies (4), and that (4) implies (1).
