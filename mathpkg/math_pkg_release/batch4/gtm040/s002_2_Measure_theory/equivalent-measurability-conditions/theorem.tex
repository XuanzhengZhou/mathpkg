The following four conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $\{x \mid f(x) < c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
\item[(2)] $\{x \mid f(x) \leq c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
\item[(3)] $\{x \mid f(x) > c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
\item[(4)] $\{x \mid f(x) \geq c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
\end{enumerate}
