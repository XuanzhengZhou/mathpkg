Let $\epsilon > 0$ be given. Let $\{B_k^{(n)}\}$ with $k = 1, 2, 3, \ldots$ be a denumerable covering of $A_n$ such that $B_k^{(n)}$ is in $\mathcal{F}$ and $\sum_k \nu(B_k^{(n)}) \leq \mu(A_n) + \epsilon/2^n$. This choice is possible by the definition of $\mu$. Then since all the $B$'s form a covering of $A$, we have
$$\mu(A) \leq \sum_n \sum_k \nu(B_k^{(n)}) \leq \sum_n \mu(A_n) + \epsilon$$
and the assertion follows.
