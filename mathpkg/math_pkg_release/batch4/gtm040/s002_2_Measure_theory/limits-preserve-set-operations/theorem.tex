If $A_n \to A$ and $B_n \to B$ with respect to the outer measure pseudometric, then $A_n \cup B_n \to A \cup B$, $A_n \cap B_n \to A \cap B$, and $\tilde{A}_n \to \tilde{A}$. Moreover, the limit of $\mu(C_n)$ is established by the inequalities
$$\mu(C_n) \leq d(C_n, C) + \mu(C) \quad \text{and} \quad \mu(C) \leq d(C_n, C) + \mu(C_n).$$
