This lemma establishes that the pseudometric convergence of sets is compatible with the basic set-theoretic operations: union, intersection, and complementation are continuous with respect to this notion of convergence. Furthermore, it shows that the outer measure $\mu$ is itself continuous with respect to this convergence, which is a crucial step in proving that $\mu$ is additive and then countably additive on the completed classes.
