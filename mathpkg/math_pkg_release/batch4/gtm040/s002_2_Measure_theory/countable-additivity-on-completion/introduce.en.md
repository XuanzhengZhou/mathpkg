This lemma builds on the finite additivity established in Lemma 1-26 to prove countable additivity for disjoint unions in $\mathcal{F}^*$ whose union happens to fall in the larger class $\mathcal{G}^*$. The proof uses finite additivity for partial unions and takes limits, leveraging the monotonicity of $\mu$.
