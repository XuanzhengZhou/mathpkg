This lemma provides key inequalities for the pseudometric $d(A,B) = \mu(A \oplus B)$ defined on subsets of $X$. It shows that the set operations of union, intersection, and complementation are continuous with respect to this pseudometric. These continuity properties are essential for proving that the limit of sets preserves these operations (Lemma 1-25).
