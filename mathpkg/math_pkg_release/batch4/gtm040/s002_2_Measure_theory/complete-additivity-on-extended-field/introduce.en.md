This lemma is the culmination of the extension process: the outer measure $\mu$ restricted to $\mathcal{G}^*$ is a genuine measure (completely additive), and $\mathcal{G}^*$ is a Borel field (sigma-algebra). Since $\mathcal{G}^*$ contains $\mathcal{F}$, it contains the smallest Borel field $\mathcal{G}$ generated by $\mathcal{F}$, completing the proof of the Carathéodory Extension Theorem.
