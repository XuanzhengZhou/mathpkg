This lemma establishes that the symmetric difference operation satisfies the axioms needed for $d(A,B) = \mu(A \oplus B)$ to behave like a metric. These properties are used in the Carathéodory extension theorem to show that limits of sets with respect to this pseudometric preserve the set-theoretic operations and that the outer measure $\mu$ is additive on the completion $\mathcal{F}^*$.
