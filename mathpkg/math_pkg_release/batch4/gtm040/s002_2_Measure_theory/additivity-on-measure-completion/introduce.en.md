This lemma is a key step in the Carathéodory extension proof. It shows that the outer measure $\mu$, which was only known to be countably subadditive on all sets, becomes finitely additive when restricted to the class $\mathcal{F}^*$ of sets that can be approximated by the original field. The proof uses the continuity of set operations and the fact that $\mu$ agrees with $\nu$ on $\mathcal{F}$.
