Theorem 9-49 establishes that all noncyclic ergodic chains are normal, and provides an explicit formula for the potential kernel $C$: $C_{ij} = M_{ij}\alpha_j$, where $M$ is the mean first passage time matrix and $\alpha$ is the stationary distribution.
