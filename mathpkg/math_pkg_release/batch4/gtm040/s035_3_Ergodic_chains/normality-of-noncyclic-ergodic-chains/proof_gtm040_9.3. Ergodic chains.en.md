By Lemma 9-48, $C$ exists and satisfies $C_{ij} = M_{ij}\alpha_j$. Since $\hat{P}$ is also noncyclic ergodic (by duality), $\hat{C}$ exists and hence $G$ exists. Thus $P$ is normal by Theorem 9-26 (characterization of normality via existence of $C$ and $G$).
