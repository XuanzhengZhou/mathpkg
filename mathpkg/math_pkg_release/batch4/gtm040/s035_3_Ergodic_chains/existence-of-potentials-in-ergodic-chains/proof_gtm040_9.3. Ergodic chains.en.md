We prove the dual statement for signed measures $\mu$ with $\mu 1 = 0$. Using $[\mu N^{(n)}]_i = \sum_k \mu_k N^{(n)}_{ki} = -\sum_k \mu_k [N^{(n)}_{ii} - N^{(n)}_{ki}]$ (since $\sum_k \mu_k N^{(n)}_{ii} = N^{(n)}_{ii}(\mu 1) = 0$), and the bound $N^{(n)}_{ii} - N^{(n)}_{ki} \leq M_{ki}$ from Lemma 9-48, the finiteness of $\mu C$ gives dominated convergence, showing $\mu$ is a charge with potential $-\mu C$.
