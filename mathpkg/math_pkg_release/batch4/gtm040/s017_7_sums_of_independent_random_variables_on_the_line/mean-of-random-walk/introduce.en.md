The mean $m = \sum_k k p_k$ is the expected one-step displacement of the random walk. It characterizes the drift: when $m > 0$ the walk has a positive trend, when $m < 0$ it has a negative trend, and when $m = 0$ the walk is centered. The mean is said to exist only when both the positive-step and negative-step contributions are not simultaneously infinite, ensuring a well-defined finite or infinite value.
