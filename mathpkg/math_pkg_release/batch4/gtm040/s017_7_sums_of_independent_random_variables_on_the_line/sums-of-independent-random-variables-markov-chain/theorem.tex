Let $P$ be a sums of independent random variables Markov chain indexed by the integers and defined by the probability distribution $\{p_k\}$. The set of integers $k$ for which $p_k > 0$ is called the set of $k$-\textit{values} associated with the chain. It is assumed that the greatest common divisor of the $k$-values is one. Consequently, if both positive and negative $k$-values exist, all pairs of states communicate (by Lemma 1-66).
