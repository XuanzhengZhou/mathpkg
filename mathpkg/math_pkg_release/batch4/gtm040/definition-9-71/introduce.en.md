A recurrent chain $P$ has ergodic degree $r$ if $b_0^{(r)} < \infty$ but $b_0^{(r+1)} = \infty$. If $b_0^{(r)} < \infty$ for every $r$, then $P$ is said to have infinite ergodic degree. [It should be noted that $b_0^{(0)} = 1$; hence the degree is always defined.]
