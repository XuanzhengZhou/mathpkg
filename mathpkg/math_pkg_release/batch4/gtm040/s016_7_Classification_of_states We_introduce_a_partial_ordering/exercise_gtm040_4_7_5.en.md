Show that an independent trials process is a Markov chain in which $P_{ij}$ is independent of $i$. Let 0 be any fixed state and let $t$ be any stopping time. Show that $\Pr_n[x_{t+1} = j] = P_{0j}$, and give an example to show that $\Pr_n[x_t = j]$ does not have to equal $P_{0j}$.
