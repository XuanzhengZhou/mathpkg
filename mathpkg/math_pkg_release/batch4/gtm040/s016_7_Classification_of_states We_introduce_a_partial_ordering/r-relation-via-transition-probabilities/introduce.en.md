Proposition 4-17 provides a convenient characterization of the $R$-relation in terms of the $n$-step transition probabilities $P^n$. Two states are $R$-related precisely when there is some number of steps $n$ after which the $n$-step transition probability $(P^n)_{ij}$ is positive. The proof uses the connection between first-entrance probabilities $F^{(n)}_{ij}$ and the $n$-step transition probabilities, together with the definition of the hitting probability $H_{ij}$ as the sum of first-entrance probabilities.
