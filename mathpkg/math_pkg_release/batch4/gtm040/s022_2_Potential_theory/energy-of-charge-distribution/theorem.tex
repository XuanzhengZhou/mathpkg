The energy of a charge distribution $\mu$ is defined as the integral of its potential function with respect to the charge, provided the integral exists:

$$\text{Energy}(\mu) = \int_{\mathbb{R}^3} g(x) \, d\mu(x) = \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{\mathbb{R}^3} \int_{\mathbb{R}^3} \frac{d\mu(x) \, d\mu(y)}{|x - y|}.$$
