Let $P$ be any Markov chain and let $N = \sum P^n$. Any non-negative $P$-superregular finite-valued function $h$ has a unique representation $h = Nf + r$, where $r$ is regular. In the representation $f$ and $r$ are both non-negative, and $f = (I - P)h$.
