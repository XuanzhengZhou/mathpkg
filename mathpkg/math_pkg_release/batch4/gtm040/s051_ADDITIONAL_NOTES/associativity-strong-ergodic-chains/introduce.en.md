For a general ergodic chain, no useful condition guarantees the associativity identity $A(0Nf) = (A^0N)f$. However, for a strong ergodic chain the identity holds whenever the charge $f$ is bounded. This follows from the finiteness of the expected occupation measure, and yields the representation $g = -Gf$ for bounded charges, recovering a conclusion previously obtained more directly in Proposition 9-73.
