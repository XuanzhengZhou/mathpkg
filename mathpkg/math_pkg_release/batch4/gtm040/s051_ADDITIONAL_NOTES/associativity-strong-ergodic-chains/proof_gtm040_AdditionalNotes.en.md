The text notes that for a strong ergodic chain associativity holds if $f$ is bounded. The argument rests on observing that $\alpha^0 N 1 \leq M_{\alpha 0} < \infty$, where $\alpha^0$ is the initial distribution and $N$ is the fundamental matrix. Since $f = (I-P)g$ for the potential $g$, boundedness of $g$ implies boundedness of $f$, and the associativity follows. The converse direction (that $g = -Gf$ when $f$ is bounded) is then a direct consequence.
