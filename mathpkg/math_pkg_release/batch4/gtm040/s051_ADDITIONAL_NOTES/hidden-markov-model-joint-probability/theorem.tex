Let $P$ be a Markov chain on a state space $S$ with initial distribution $\pi$, and let $F: S \times Y \to [0,1]$ be a matrix where each row $F_i$ is a probability distribution on an outcome space $Y$. The joint probability of observing outcomes $y_1 = k_1, \dots, y_N = k_N$ is

$$\Pr[y_1 = k_1 \wedge \dots \wedge y_N = k_N] = \sum_{i_0, \dots, i_N \in S} \pi_{i_0} P_{i_0 i_1} F_{i_1 k_1} P_{i_1 i_2} F_{i_2 k_2} \dots P_{i_{N-1} i_N} F_{i_N k_N}.$$
