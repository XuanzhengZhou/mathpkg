The section only cites the result; the full proof appears in Day [1964]. The essential difficulty lies in handling the lack of symmetry, which prevents a direct application of spectral-theoretic arguments for self-adjoint operators on $\ell^2$. Day's proof uses functional-analytic methods to establish the equivalence without the symmetry hypothesis.
