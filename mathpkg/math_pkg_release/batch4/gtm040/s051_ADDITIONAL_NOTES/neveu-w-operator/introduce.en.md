Neveu constructed the operator $W$ as a potential kernel for recurrent chains, starting from the operator $U_h$ and subtracting the rank-one term $1\alpha$ to obtain $V = U_h - 1\alpha$. The operator $W$ is then defined via a Neumann series in $V D_h$. The finiteness and positivity of $W$ are established by the identities $W h = c 1$ and $\alpha D_h W = c \alpha$, showing that $W$ acts as a Green kernel in Neveu's recurrent potential theory.
