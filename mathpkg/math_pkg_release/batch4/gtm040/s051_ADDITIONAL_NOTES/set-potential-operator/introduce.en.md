The set potential operator $U_E$ is a specialization of Neveu's $U_h$ operator when $h$ is taken as the characteristic function of a subset $E$. The operator sums the function $f$ over the chain trajectory up to (and including) the hitting time $t_E$ of the set $E$. When $f$ is specialized to the indicator of $E$, $(U_E f)_i$ yields the probability of ever returning to $E$ starting from state $i$, linking the potential-theoretic formalism with recurrence properties of the chain.
