Let $P$ be recurrent with positive regular measure $\alpha$. Then there exists a function $h$ on $S$ with $0 < h \leq 1$ such that $U_h h = 1$ and $U_h \geq 1\alpha$. Fix such an $h$, put $V = U_h - 1\alpha$, and define

$$W = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (V D_h)^n V.$$

Then $W \geq 0$, and the finiteness of $W$ is settled by the identities $W h = c 1$ and $\alpha D_h W = c \alpha$, where $c = (1 - \alpha h)/(\alpha h)$.
