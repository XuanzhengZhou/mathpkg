The following four conditions are equivalent:

(1) $\{x \mid f(x) < c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
(2) $\{x \mid f(x) \leq c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
(3) $\{x \mid f(x) > c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
(4) $\{x \mid f(x) \geq c\}$ is measurable for every $c$.
