Proposition 2-10 connects the mean number of visits to state $j$ (denoted $n_j$) with the sum over all times $n$ of the probability that the process is in state $j$ at time $n$. The proof uses the representation of $n_j$ as a sum of indicator functions $u_j^{(n)}$ and applies the monotone convergence theorem for Lebesgue integrals to interchange the sum and the mean.
