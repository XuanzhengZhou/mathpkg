Start with 4 jacks and 4 queens from a bridge deck. Find the probability of drawing two cards, both of which are jacks. Then compute the conditional probability of the same on each of the following conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] One card drawn is a jack.
\item[(b)] One card drawn is a red jack.
\end{enumerate}
