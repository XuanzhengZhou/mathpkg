The product topology for the cartesian product $\times\{X_t : t \in A\}$ is the topology having as a base the family of all sets of the following form: the set of all $y$ such that $y(t) \in U_t$ for each $t$ in a finite set of indices, where $U_t$ is an open neighborhood of $x(t)$ in $X_t$. A net $\{x_\alpha, \alpha \in B, \geq\}$ in the product converges to a point $x'$ of the product if and only if for each $t$ in $A$ the net $\{x_\alpha(t), \alpha \in B, \geq\}$ converges to $x'(t)$.
