Each factor space $X_t$ embeds homeomorphically into the product $\prod_{s \in A} X_s$ via the canonical injection $I_t$, which places a point $z \in X_t$ in the $t$-th coordinate and fills all other coordinates with a fixed base point $\theta(s)$. This shows that a product of nonempty spaces contains topological copies of each factor. A function into the product is continuous iff its composition with each projection is continuous.
