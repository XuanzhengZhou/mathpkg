If $\{f_{\gamma}, \gamma \in \Gamma\}$ is a net of functions on $S$ to $E$ such that $\{f_{\gamma}(t), \gamma \in \Gamma\}$ converges to $f(t)$ for each $t$ in a subset $A$ of $S$, and if $\{f_{\gamma}, \gamma \in \Gamma\}$ is a Cauchy net relative to the topology of uniform convergence on $A$, then $\{f_{\gamma}, \gamma \in \Gamma\}$ converges to $f$ uniformly on $A$.
