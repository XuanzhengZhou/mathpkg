Part (iii) of Corollary 18.6: for a barrelled space, four natural properties of a subset of the adjoint are equivalent -- equicontinuity, weak* compactness of the weak* closure, strong boundedness, and weak* boundedness. This follows from Theorem 18.5 and the characterization of barrelled spaces.
