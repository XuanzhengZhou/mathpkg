Suppose $E$ is a locally convex space, $F$ is a dense subspace which with the relativized topology is a Mackey space, and $C$ is a $w(E^*,E)$-compact convex circled subset of $E^*$. Then $C_0 \cap F$ is a neighborhood of $0$ in $F$, where $C_0$ is the polar of $C$ in $E$. Consequently, if $F$ is a dense Mackey subspace of $E$, then $E$ is a Mackey space.
