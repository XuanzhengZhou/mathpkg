If a locally convex space contains a dense subspace that is Mackey in the relativized topology, then the whole space is Mackey. The proof uses the restriction map from $E^*$ to $F^*$, which is weak* continuous, to relate weak* compact convex circled sets in $E^*$ to those in $F^*$.
