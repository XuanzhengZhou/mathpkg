If $E$ and $F$ are paired linear spaces, then $m(E,F)$, the \textit{Mackey topology} for $E$ (also called the \textit{maximal} or \textit{relatively strong} topology), has for a local base the family of all polars of $w(F,E)$-compact circled convex subsets of $F$. It is the topology $\mathcal{T}_c$ of uniform convergence on $w(F,E)$-compact circled convex sets.
