The Mackey topology $m(E,F)$ on a paired linear space $E$ is the topology of uniform convergence on all $w(F,E)$-compact circled convex subsets of $F$. It is also called the maximal or relatively strong topology and plays a fundamental role in the duality theory of locally convex spaces.
