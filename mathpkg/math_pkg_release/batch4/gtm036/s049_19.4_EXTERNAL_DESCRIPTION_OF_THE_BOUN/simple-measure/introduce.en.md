A simple measure on a set $A$ is a two-valued countably additive set function defined on all subsets. Such measures are of interest in the study of measurable cardinals and arise in the context of determining when arbitrary products of bound spaces remain bound spaces. The existence of simple measures with additional properties (Ulam measures) is tied to large cardinal axioms.
