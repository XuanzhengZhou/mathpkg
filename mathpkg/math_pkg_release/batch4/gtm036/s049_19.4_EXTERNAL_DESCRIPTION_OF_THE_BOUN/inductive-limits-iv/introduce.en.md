This problem (Part A of the Chapter 19 exercises, continuing the "Inductive Limits" series) investigates the preservation of topological vector space properties under the formation of inductive limits. The inductive limit of barrelled spaces is barrelled, of bound spaces is bound, and (under a Hausdorff condition) of fully complete spaces is fully complete. Part (b) extends these ideas to continuous linear maps into fully complete spaces via the closed graph / weak homomorphism criterion.
