This technical lemma bridges functional analysis and measure theory. Given a bounded linear functional $g$ on a product of bound spaces whose restriction to any subset of coordinates is either the full functional or zero, the assignment $C \mapsto 1$ when $g_*C = g$ and $C \mapsto 0$ otherwise yields a countably additive two-valued measure. This construction is central to the proof characterizing when products of bound spaces remain bound.
