Let $E$ be a locally convex space with a bound topology $\mathcal{T}$. Let $\mathcal{A}$ be a family of convex, circled bounded subsets of $E$ that forms a dual base for the family of all bounded sets. For each $A \in \mathcal{A}$, let $E_A$ be the subspace generated by $A$, equipped with the pseudo-norm $p_A$ given by the Minkowski functional of $A$. Let $T$ be the natural linear map from the direct sum $\bigoplus \{E_A : A \in \mathcal{A}\}$ onto $E$. Then $\mathcal{T}$ coincides with the quotient topology induced by $T$.
