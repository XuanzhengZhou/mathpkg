Let $S$ be an infinite set and let $a_1, a_2, \ldots \in S$ be a sequence of distinct elements. For each scalar $\alpha$ with $\alpha \neq 0$ and $|\alpha| \leq 1$, define $u_\alpha \in B(S)$ by
$$
u_\alpha(x) = \begin{cases}
\alpha^n & \text{if } x = a_n \text{ for some } n \in \mathbb{N}, \\
0 & \text{otherwise}.
\end{cases}
$$
Then the family $\{u_\alpha : \alpha \neq 0,\ |\alpha| \leq 1\}$ is linearly independent in $B(S)$.
