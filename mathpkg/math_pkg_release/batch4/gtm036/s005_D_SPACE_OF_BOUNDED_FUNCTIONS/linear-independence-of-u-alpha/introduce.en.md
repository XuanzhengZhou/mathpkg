This lemma constructs an uncountable linearly independent family $\{u_\alpha\}$ in the space $B(S)$ of bounded functions. The construction uses a fixed sequence of distinct points $a_n \in S$ and defines each $u_\alpha$ to take geometrically decaying values $\alpha^n$ along this sequence. The linear independence follows from the fact that distinct $\alpha$ produce distinct exponential sequences, and a vanishing finite linear combination would force all coefficients to be zero by properties of the geometric progression.
