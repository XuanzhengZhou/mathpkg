Let $S$ be an infinite set and let $B(S)$ be the linear space of all bounded scalar valued functions on $S$. Let $s = |S|$ be the cardinality of $S$ and let $c$ be the cardinality of the scalar field $K$ (either $\mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$). Then the algebraic dimension of $B(S)$ is
$$
\dim B(S) = c^s.
$$
