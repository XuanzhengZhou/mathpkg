Let $T : E \to F$ be a linear mapping of one linear space $E$ into another linear space $F$, and let $N = \ker T$ be the null space of $T$. Then the quotient space $E/N$ is isomorphic to the subspace $T(E)$ (the range of $T$) via the canonical map
$$
\tilde{T} : E/N \to T(E), \quad \tilde{T}(x + N) = T(x).
$$
