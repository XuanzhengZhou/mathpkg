The topology of pointwise convergence on $C([0,1])$ is strictly weaker than the supremum norm topology. The identity map from $(C([0,1]), \|\cdot\|_\infty)$ to $(C([0,1]), \tau_{\text{ptwise}})$ is continuous, providing an explicit illustration of a continuous image of a metrizable linear topological space that is itself not metrizable. This relates to the discussion in Section 9G of the book.
