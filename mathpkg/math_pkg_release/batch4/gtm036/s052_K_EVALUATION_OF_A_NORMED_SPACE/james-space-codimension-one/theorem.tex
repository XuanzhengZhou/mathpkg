\text{For each } z \in E^* \text{ there exist scalars } \zeta_n' \text{ such that }
z = \zeta_0' e_0 + \sum_{n=1}^\infty \zeta_n' e_n,
\text{ where } e_0 \text{ is the sequence with every term } 1 \text{ and the series converges in norm. The canonical image of } E \text{ in } E^* \text{ has codimension one.}
