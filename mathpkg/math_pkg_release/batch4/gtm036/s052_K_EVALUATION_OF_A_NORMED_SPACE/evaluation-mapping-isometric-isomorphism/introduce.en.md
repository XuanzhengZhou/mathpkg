The evaluation mapping of a normed space assigns to each vector the linear functional on the dual space given by evaluation at that vector. This map embeds the normed space canonically into its second dual (bidual). The fundamental fact stated here is that this embedding preserves the norm exactly — it is an isometry — and is a linear isomorphism onto its image. For a general normed space the image may be a proper subspace of the bidual; when the map is surjective the space is called reflexive.
