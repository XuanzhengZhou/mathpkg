This exercise shows that in the dual of a barrelled space, the operation of taking the weak* closed convex hull preserves weak* compactness. This is in contrast to the counter-example in Problem F, where the same operation fails in a non-barrelled space. The result highlights the good behavior of barrelled spaces with respect to compactness in the weak* dual.
