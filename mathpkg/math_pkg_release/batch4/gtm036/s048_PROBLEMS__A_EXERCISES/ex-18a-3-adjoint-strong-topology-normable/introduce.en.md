This exercise establishes a duality between the normability of the strong dual and the pseudo-normability of the bound extension of the original topology. If the adjoint $E^*$ equipped with the strong topology $\beta(E^*, E)$ is normable, then the bound extension $\mathcal{T}^b$ makes $E$ pseudo-normable, and conversely. This connects the bornological and dual perspectives on topological vector spaces.
