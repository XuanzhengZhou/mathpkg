Let $\{T_{\alpha} : \alpha \in A\}$ be a family of continuous linear mappings of a pseudo-normable space $E$ of the second category into a pseudo-normed space $(F,p)$. If $\sup \{p(T_{\alpha}(x)) : \alpha \in A\} < \infty$ for each $x$ in $E$, then $\sup \{\|T_{\alpha}\| : \alpha \in A\} < \infty$.
