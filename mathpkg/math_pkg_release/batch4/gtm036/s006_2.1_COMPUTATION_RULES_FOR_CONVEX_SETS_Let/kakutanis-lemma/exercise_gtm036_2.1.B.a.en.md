(Kakutani's lemma) If $A$ and $B$ are disjoint convex sets and $x$ is a point not in their union, then either $\langle A \cup \{x\} \rangle$ and $B$ are disjoint or else $\langle B \cup \{x\} \rangle$ and $A$ are disjoint.
