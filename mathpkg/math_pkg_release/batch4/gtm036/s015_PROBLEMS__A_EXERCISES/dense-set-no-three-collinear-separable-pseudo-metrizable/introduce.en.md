This exercise constructs a remarkable dense subset of a separable pseudo-metrizable linear topological space of dimension at least $2$: a countable dense set with no three collinear points. Moreover, the complement has the curious property of being radial at each point (every line through a point meets the complement in a set radial at that point) yet having empty interior. The construction proceeds by a careful diagonal selection.
