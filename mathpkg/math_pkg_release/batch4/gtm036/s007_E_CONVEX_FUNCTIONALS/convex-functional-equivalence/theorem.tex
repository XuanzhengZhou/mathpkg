For a real-valued function $p$ on a linear space $E$ with $p(0) = 0$, any two of the following statements imply the third:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $p$ is non-negatively homogeneous;
\item[(ii)] $p$ is subadditive;
\item[(iii)] $p$ is convex, that is,
$$
p\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i x_i\right) \leq \sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i p(x_i)
$$
for any finite combination with each $a_i \geq 0$ and $\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i = 1$.
\end{enumerate}
