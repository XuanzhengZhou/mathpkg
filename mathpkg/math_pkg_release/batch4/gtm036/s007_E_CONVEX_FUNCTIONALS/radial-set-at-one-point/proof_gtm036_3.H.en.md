The text asserts the existence without explicit construction. A standard example: take the set $A = \{(x,y) \in \mathbb{R}^2 : y \geq x^2 \text{ or } y \leq -x^2\}$ together with the point $(0,0)$. This set is radial at $(0,0)$ because any line through the origin eventually enters the region between the two parabolas, but at any other point of $A$, one can find a direction where the line segment immediately leaves $A$.
