The collection of all cones in a real linear space is closed under several natural operations. Arbitrary intersections of cones are again cones, and unions of families directed by inclusion are also cones. Finite linear combinations of cones remain cones. The conical extension operator $C(A)$ satisfies a sublinearity property with respect to linear combinations and commutes with arbitrary unions.
