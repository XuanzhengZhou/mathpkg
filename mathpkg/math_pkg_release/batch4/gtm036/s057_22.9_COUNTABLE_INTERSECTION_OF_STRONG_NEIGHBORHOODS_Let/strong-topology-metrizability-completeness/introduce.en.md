This result states that for a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space $E$, the adjoint space $E^*$ endowed with the strong topology is locally convex, metrizable, and complete. Local convexity and metrizability are immediate from the definition of the strong topology, while completeness follows from part (iii) of Theorem 18.4 (the completeness of the adjoint under the topology of uniform convergence on bounded sets when the original space is bornological).
