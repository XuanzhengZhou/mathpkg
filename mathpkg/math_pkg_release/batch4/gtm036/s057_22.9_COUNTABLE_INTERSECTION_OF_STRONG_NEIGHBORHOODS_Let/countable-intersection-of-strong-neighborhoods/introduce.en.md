This theorem characterizes when a countable intersection of strong neighborhoods of zero in the adjoint $E^*$ of a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space $E$ remains a strong neighborhood. The necessary and sufficient condition is that the intersection must absorb every strongly bounded subset of $E^*$. The proof uses a co-base of weak* compact convex circled strongly bounded sets and constructs a weak* closed convex circled radial set to establish the result.
