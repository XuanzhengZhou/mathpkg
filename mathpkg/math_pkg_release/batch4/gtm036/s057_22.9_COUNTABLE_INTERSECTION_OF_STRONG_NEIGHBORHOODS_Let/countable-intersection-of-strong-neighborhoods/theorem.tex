Let $E$ be a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space, and let $\{U_n\}$ be a sequence of convex circled strong neighborhoods of $0$ in $E^*$. Then the intersection
\[
\bigcap \{U_n : n = 1, 2, \cdots\}
\]
is a strong neighborhood of $0$ if and only if this intersection absorbs each strongly bounded set.
