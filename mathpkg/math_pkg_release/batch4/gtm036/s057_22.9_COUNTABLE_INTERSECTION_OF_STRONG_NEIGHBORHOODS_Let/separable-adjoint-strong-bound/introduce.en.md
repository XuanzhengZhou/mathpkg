This theorem gives the second special case where the adjoint of a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space is barrelled (equivalently, the strong topology is bound). If $E^*$ is separable under the strong topology, then by Theorem 22.15 it suffices to show that $E^*$ is evaluable, meaning each convex circled strongly closed subset that absorbs strongly bounded sets is a strong neighborhood. The proof uses the separability to construct a countable basis argument.
