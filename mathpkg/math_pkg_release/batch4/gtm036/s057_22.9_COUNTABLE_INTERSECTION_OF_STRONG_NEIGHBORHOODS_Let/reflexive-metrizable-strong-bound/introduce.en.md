This theorem provides the first of two special cases where the adjoint $E^*$ of a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space is barrelled (equivalently, the strong topology is bound). If $E$ is reflexive and metrizable, then $E^*$ is also reflexive and therefore evaluable (see 20.5), which implies that the strong topology on $E^*$ is bound.
