This theorem establishes the equivalence of three important properties for the adjoint $E^*$ of a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space $E$ under the strong topology: evaluability, boundness, and barrelledness. The proof relies on Theorem 22.13 (for evaluable implies bound), the completeness of $E^*$ under the strong topology (for bound implies barrelled via Theorem 19.5), and Theorem 20.4 (for barrelled implies evaluable).
