Let $C$ be a convex cone such that $C \cap (-C) = \{0\}$. The only extreme point of $C$ is the vertex, zero. A half-line $L$ from zero is an extreme half-line of $C$ if and only if $L$ is a support of $C$. Then $\langle \limsup A_\alpha \rangle$, that is, $\limsup A_\alpha$ contains all the extreme points of $\limsup C_\alpha$.

(Let $B_\alpha = \bigcup \{A_\beta : \beta \geq \alpha\}^-$ and use (b.).)
