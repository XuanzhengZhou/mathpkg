Part (i) is a direct consequence of the Hahn-Banach theorem 3.4, part (ii) is the geometric formulation 3.6 of the same result, and (iii) follows from (ii) in view of the fact that there is a convex circled neighborhood $K$ of $0$ in $E$ such that $|f|$ is bounded on $K \cap F$, because $f$ is continuous on $F$.
