This lemma establishes that the spectrum $X$ of the adjoint of an $L$-space is an extremally disconnected compact Hausdorff space: the closure of every open set is clopen. Moreover, every representing measure $m_x$ vanishes on all Borel sets of the first category. The proof constructs a continuous function $f$ as the supremum of a directed family of continuous functions supported in $X \setminus U$, showing that $f$ coincides with the characteristic function of $U^-$ except on the nowhere dense boundary.
