For a linear operator $T$ on a locally convex Hausdorff space, consider $S = \lambda I - T$. The operator $T$ and its adjoint $T^{*}$ share the same eigenvalues. Moreover, for each $r$, the null spaces $S^{-r}[0]$ and $S^{*-r}[0]$ have equal dimension. This follows from the previous problem applied to $T^{*}$ together with the duality relations $(S^{r}[E])^{0} = S^{*-r}[0]$ and $(S^{*-r}[E^{*}])^{0} = S^{-r}[0]$.
