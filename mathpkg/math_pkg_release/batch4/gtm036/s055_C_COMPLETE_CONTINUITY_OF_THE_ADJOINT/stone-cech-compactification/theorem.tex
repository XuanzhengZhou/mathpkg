The mapping $e$ of $X$ into the adjoint $M(X)$ of $B(X)$ with the weak* topology, defined by $(e(x))(f) = f(x)$ for all $f \in B(X)$, is a homeomorphism of $X$ onto a subset of the surface of the unit sphere of $M(X)$. Let $\beta(X) = \overline{e[X]}$; then $\beta(X)$ is a compact Hausdorff space, called the Stone-\v{C}ech compactification of $X$.
