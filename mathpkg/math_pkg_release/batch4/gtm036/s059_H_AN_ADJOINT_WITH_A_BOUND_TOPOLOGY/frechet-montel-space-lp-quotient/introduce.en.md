This theorem constructs, for each $p \in [1, \infty)$, a Fréchet Montel space $E_p$ that contains a closed subspace $F_p$ whose quotient is topologically isomorphic to the sequence space $l_p$. The construction uses the $E^p(\Omega)$ spaces defined in section 22F with a particular choice of weight sequence $\Omega$, demonstrating that Montel spaces can have quotients isomorphic to classical non-Montel Banach spaces.
