This theorem asserts that the space $E_p = E^p(\Omega)$ constructed with the specific weight sequence $\Omega$ is a Fréchet Montel space for every $p \in [1, \infty)$. The key to the proof is showing that every closed bounded subset of $E_p$ is compact, by approximating points of a bounded set with elements from a bounded finite-dimensional set.
