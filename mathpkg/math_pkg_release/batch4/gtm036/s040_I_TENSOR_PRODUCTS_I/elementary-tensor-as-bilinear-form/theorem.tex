Let $E$ and $F$ be linear spaces, with algebraic duals $E'$ and $F'$. For each $x \in E$ and $y \in F$, the elementary tensor $x \otimes y$ is the bilinear form on $E' \times F'$ defined by
$$(x \otimes y)(x', y') = \langle x, x' \rangle \langle y, y' \rangle,$$
where $\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle$ denotes the canonical pairing between a space and its algebraic dual.
