This theorem asserts that the completed projective tensor product operation preserves metrizability and complete metrizability: if both factors $E$ and $F$ are metrizable locally convex spaces, then $E \hat{\otimes} F$ is a Fréchet space (a complete metrizable locally convex space). Furthermore, barrelledness is also preserved. The proof is referred to 12G(b) of the text, which concerns the projective topology and its interaction with countability and barrelledness axioms.
