Let $E$ and $F$ be locally convex spaces, and let $\mathcal{T}$ be the projective tensor product topology on the algebraic tensor product $E \otimes F$. The completion of $E \otimes F$ with respect to $\mathcal{T}$ is denoted by $E \hat{\otimes} F$ and is called the completed projective tensor product of $E$ and $F$.
