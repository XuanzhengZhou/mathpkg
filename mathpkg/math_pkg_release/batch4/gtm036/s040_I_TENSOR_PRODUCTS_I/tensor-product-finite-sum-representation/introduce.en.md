Since $E \otimes F$ is defined as the linear span of the set of all elementary tensors $\{x \otimes y : x \in E, y \in F\}$, every element of the tensor product is, by definition of the linear span, a finite linear combination of elementary tensors. The explicit formula for the evaluation of such a sum as a bilinear form follows directly from the bilinearity of the pairing and the definition of the elementary tensor. This representation is the starting point for all subsequent topological considerations on tensor products.
