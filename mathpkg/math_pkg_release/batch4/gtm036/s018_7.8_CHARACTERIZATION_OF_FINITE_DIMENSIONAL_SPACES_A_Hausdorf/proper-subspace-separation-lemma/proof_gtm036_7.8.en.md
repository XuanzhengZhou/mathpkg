For, otherwise, $V + V \subset U \subset Y + V$. By induction it follows that, for each positive integer $m$, $mV \subset Y + V$ or $V \subset Y + (1/m)V$. Since $V$ is bounded and $Y$ is closed, $V \subset \bigcap \{Y + (1/m)V : m = 1, 2, \dots\} = Y$, which implies that $Y$ is not a proper subspace.
