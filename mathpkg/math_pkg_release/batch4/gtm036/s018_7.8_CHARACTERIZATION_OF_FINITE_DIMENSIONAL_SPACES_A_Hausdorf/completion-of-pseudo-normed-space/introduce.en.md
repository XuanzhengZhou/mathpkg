For a pseudo-normed linear space $E$ with pseudo-norm $p$, the completion $E^\wedge$ inherits a natural pseudo-norm $p^\wedge$. This pseudo-norm is defined on equivalence classes of Cauchy sequences by taking the limit of the original pseudo-norm along the sequence: $p^\wedge(\{x_n\}) = \lim p(x_n)$. The embedding map $T$ preserves the pseudo-norm, i.e., $p^\wedge(T(x)) = p(x)$. This construction generalizes the classical completion of a normed space to a Banach space.
