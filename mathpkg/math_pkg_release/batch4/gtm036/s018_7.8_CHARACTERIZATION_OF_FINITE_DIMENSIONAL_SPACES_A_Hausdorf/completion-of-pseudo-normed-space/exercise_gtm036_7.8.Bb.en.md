Let $E$ be a pseudo-normed linear space with a pseudo-norm $p$. Then the completion $E^\wedge$ of $E$ is a pseudo-normed space whose pseudo-norm $p^\wedge$ is defined by $p^\wedge(\{x_n\}) = \lim p(x_n)$ for each Cauchy sequence $\{x_n\}$ in $E$, and $p^\wedge$ extends $p$ in the sense that $p^\wedge(T(x)) = p(x)$ for each $x \in E$.
