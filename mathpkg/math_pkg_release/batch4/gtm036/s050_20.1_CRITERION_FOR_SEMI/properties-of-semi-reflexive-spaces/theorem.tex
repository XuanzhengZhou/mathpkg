Let $E$ be a semi-reflexive locally convex space.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] Each closed bounded subset of $E$ is complete.
\item[(ii)] If $F$ is a closed subspace of $E$, then $F$ is semi-reflexive and the strong topology of $F^*$ is the quotient topology induced by the canonical map of $E^*$ onto $F^* \cong E^* / F^0$.
\item[(iii)] Products and direct sums of semi-reflexive spaces are semi-reflexive.
\end{enumerate}
