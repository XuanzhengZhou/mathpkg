Theorem 20.2 establishes three fundamental structural properties of semi-reflexive locally convex spaces. First, every closed bounded subset of a semi-reflexive space is complete, which is notable since semi-reflexive spaces themselves need not be complete. Second, semi-reflexivity is inherited by closed subspaces, and the strong dual of a closed subspace is topologically isomorphic to the quotient of the strong dual by the annihilator. Third, the class of semi-reflexive spaces is closed under the formation of arbitrary products and direct sums.
