\textbf{20.7 PROPERTIES OF REFLEXIVE SPACES.}

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] If $E$ is reflexive so is $E^*$.
\item[(ii)] Each reflexive space is a barrelled space, and each closed bounded subset of a reflexive space is complete.
\item[(iii)] Products and direct sums of reflexive spaces are reflexive.
\end{enumerate}
