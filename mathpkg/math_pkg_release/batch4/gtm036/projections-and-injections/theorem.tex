The projection $P_s$ is a linear map of the product $\times \{E_t : t \in A\}$ onto the coordinate space $E_s$. The injection $I_s$ is a linear isomorphism of the coordinate space $E_s$ onto the subspace of the direct sum $\sum \{E_t : t \in A\}$ which consists of all vectors $x$ such that $x(t) = 0$ for $t \neq s$.
