Let $E = \sum \{E_t : t \in A\}$ be a locally convex direct sum, so that $E^* = \bigwedge \{E_t^* : t \in A\}$. Let $I_t$ be the injection of $E_t^*$ into the product $\bigwedge \{E_t^* : t \in A\}$, and let $\phi$ be a linear functional on $E^*$ which is weak* continuous on every equicontinuous set. Then there exists a finite subset $B \subseteq A$ such that $\phi \circ I_t = 0$ for each $t \in A \setminus B$.
