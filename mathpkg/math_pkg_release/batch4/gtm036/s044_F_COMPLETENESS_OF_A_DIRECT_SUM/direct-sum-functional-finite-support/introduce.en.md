A linear functional $\phi$ on the dual $E^*$ of a locally convex direct sum, which is weak* continuous on every equicontinuous set, must vanish on all but finitely many of the component duals $E_t^*$. This follows from a contradiction argument using the boundedness of a constructed set in the product topology and the weak* continuity of $\phi$ on equicontinuous sets.
