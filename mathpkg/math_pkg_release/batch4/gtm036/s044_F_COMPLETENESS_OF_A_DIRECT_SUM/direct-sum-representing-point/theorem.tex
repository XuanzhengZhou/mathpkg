Under the hypotheses of Lemma (b), let $B \subseteq A$ be the finite subset with $\phi \circ I_t = 0$ for $t \in A \setminus B$. Then there exists a point $x \in \sum \{E_t : t \in B\} \subseteq E$ such that $\phi(f) = f(x)$ for all $f \in E^*$. Consequently, $\phi$ is the evaluation functional at $x$.
