Let $E = \sum \{E_t : t \in A\}$ be a locally convex direct sum, $E^* = \bigwedge \{E_t^* : t \in A\}$, and $I_t$ the injection of $E_t^*$ into the product. Let $\phi$ be a linear functional on $E^*$, weak* continuous on every equicontinuous set, and let $B \subseteq A$ be the finite subset from Lemma (a) such that $\phi \circ I_t = 0$ for all $t \in A \setminus B$. Then for each $f \in E^*$,
$$\phi(f) = \sum_{t \in B} (\phi \circ I_t)(f_t).$$
