Given a linear functional $\phi$ on $E^*$ whose compositions with the injections $I_t$ vanish outside a finite set $B \subseteq A$, the value $\phi(f)$ decomposes as the finite sum of $(\phi \circ I_t)(f_t)$ over $t \in B$. The proof uses the weak* continuity of $\phi$ on the equicontinuous set formed by finite partial sums.
