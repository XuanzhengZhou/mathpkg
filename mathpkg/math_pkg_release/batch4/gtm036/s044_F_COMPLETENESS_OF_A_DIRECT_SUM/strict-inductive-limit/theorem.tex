Assume that for each $n$, $E_n$ is a closed subspace of $E_{n+1}$ and that the topology $\mathcal{T}_n$ on $E_n$ is the relativization of $\mathcal{T}_{n+1}$ to $E_n$. Let $E = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} E_n$ and let $\mathcal{T}$ be the finest locally convex topology on $E$ such that each injection of $E_n$ into $E$ is continuous. The space $(E, \mathcal{T})$ is then called the **strict inductive limit** of the sequence $\{(E_n, \mathcal{T}_n)\}$.
