Let $E$ be a separable and pseudo-metrizable locally convex space. Then its continuous dual space $E^*$ equipped with the weak* topology $\sigma(E^*, E)$ is separable. This result complements the fact that the dual of a separable normed space is weak* separable, extending it to the broader class of pseudo-metrizable spaces.
