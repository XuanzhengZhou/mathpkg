Let $X$ be a locally compact Hausdorff space whose topology has a countable base and let $\{\mu_n\}$ be a sequence of Borel measures on $X$ for which $\{\mu_n(X): n = 1, 2, \cdots\}$ is bounded. Then there is a Borel measure $\mu$ on $X$ and a subsequence $\{n_k\}$ such that
$$
\int_X f \, d\mu_{n_k} \rightarrow \int_X f \, d\mu
$$
for each continuous real valued function $f$ on $X$ vanishing at infinity.
