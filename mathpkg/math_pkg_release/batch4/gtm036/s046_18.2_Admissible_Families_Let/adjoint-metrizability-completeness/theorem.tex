Let $E$ be a locally convex linear topological space, and let the adjoint $E^*$ have the strong topology. Then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] The space $E^*$ is metrizable if and only if the family of all bounded subsets of $E$ has a countable co-base.

\item[(ii)] $E^*$ is dense, relative to the strong topology, in the space of all linear functionals on $E$ which are continuous on each bounded subset of $E$.

\item[(iii)] $E^*$ is complete relative to the strong topology if and only if each linear functional which is continuous on bounded subsets of $E$ is continuous on $E$.
\end{enumerate}
