Let $E$ be a locally convex space and let $B$ be a subset of the adjoint $E^*$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] If $B$ is equicontinuous, then the weak* closed convex circled extension of $B$ is both equicontinuous and weak* compact.

\item[(ii)] If $B$ is weak* compact and convex, then $B$ is strongly bounded.

\item[(iii)] If $B$ is strongly bounded, then $B$ is weak* bounded. Moreover, if $B$ is weak* bounded and $E$ is sequentially complete, then $B$ is strongly bounded.
\end{enumerate}
