This theorem describes the relationship between the continuous dual spaces of a subspace $F$, the quotient space $E/F$, and the original space $E$. The dual of the quotient $(E/F)^*$ is canonically isomorphic to the annihilator $F^\perp$ in $E^*$. When $E$ is locally convex, the quotient $E^*/F^\perp$ is canonically isomorphic to the dual $F^*$ of the subspace, which relies on the Hahn-Banach extension theorem to guarantee that every continuous functional on $F$ extends to $E$.
