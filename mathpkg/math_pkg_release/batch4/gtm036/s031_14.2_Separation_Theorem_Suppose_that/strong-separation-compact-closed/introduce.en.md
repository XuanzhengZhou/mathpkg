This corollary of the Strong Separation Criterion guarantees that any non-void disjoint convex sets in a locally convex space, one compact and the other closed, can be strongly separated by a continuous linear functional. Moreover, if the closed set is circled, a stronger separation in terms of absolute values can be achieved. A special case is that any point outside a closed convex set can be strongly separated from it.
