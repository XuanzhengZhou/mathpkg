A linear functional $f$ on a linear space $E$ is said to separate two sets $A$ and $B$ if $f$ is not identically zero and the real part $r$ of $f$ satisfies $r(x) \leq r(y)$ for all $x \in A$ and $y \in B$. Continuity of $f$ is equivalent to continuity of its real part $r$.
