This theorem characterizes when the canonical extension $T^\wedge$ of a continuous linear map to the completion is injective. The condition states that if a Cauchy net is mapped by $T$ to a limit that equals $T(x_0)$, then the net itself must converge to $x_0$. In other words, $T^\wedge$ is one-to-one precisely when $T$ reflects limits of Cauchy nets.
