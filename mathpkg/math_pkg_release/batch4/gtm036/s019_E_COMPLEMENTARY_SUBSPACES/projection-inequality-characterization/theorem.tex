Let $F$ be a closed convex set in a Hilbert space $H$ and let $P$ be the projection onto $F$. Then:
$$
\operatorname{Re} (x - P(x), y - P(x)) \leq 0
$$
for each $x \in H$ and $y \in F$.

In particular:
\begin{itemize}
\item If $F$ is a cone, then $\operatorname{Re} (x - P(x), y) \leq 0$ and $\operatorname{Re} (x - P(x), P(x)) = 0$ for each $x \in H$ and $y \in F$.
\item If $F$ is a linear subspace, then $(x - P(x), y) = 0$ for each $x \in H$ and $y \in F$.
\end{itemize}
