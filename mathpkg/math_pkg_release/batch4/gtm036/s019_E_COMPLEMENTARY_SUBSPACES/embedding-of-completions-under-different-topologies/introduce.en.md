This theorem gives a necessary and sufficient condition for the completion under a stronger vector topology $\mathcal{U}$ to embed into the completion under a coarser topology $\mathcal{T}$. The condition is that any $\mathcal{U}$-Cauchy net that already $\mathcal{T}$-converges must also $\mathcal{U}$-converge to the same limit. A sufficient condition is the existence of a local base of $\mathcal{T}$-closed $\mathcal{U}$-neighborhoods of $0$.
