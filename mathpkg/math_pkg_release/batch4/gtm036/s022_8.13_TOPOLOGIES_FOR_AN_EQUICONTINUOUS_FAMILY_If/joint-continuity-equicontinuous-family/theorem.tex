Let $M$ be an equicontinuous family of functions on $S$ to $E$, and let $M$ have the topology of pointwise convergence on $S$. Then $f(s)$ is jointly continuous in $f$ and in $s$, in the sense that the map of $M \times S$ into $E$ defined by $(f,s) \mapsto f(s)$ is continuous relative to the product topology.
