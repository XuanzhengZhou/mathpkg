Let $G$ be the family of all continuous functions on a compact space $S$ to a compact metric space $Z$, let $G$ have the topology of pointwise convergence, and let $F$ be a subfamily of $G$ such that each sequence in $F$ has a cluster point in $G$. Then each function $f$ which belongs to the closure of $F$ is a cluster point of a sequence in $F$, and each cluster point of a sequence in $F$ is the limit of a subsequence.
