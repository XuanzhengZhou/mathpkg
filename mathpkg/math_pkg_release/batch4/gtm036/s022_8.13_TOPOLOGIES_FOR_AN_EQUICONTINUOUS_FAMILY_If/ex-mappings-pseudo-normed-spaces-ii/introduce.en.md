This problem (a continuation of Problem 6B) characterizes the space of continuous linear mappings between pseudo-normed linear spaces. Taking the family $\mathcal{A}$ to consist of the unit sphere $S$ of $E$, the intersection $L(E,F) \cap B_{\mathcal{A}}(E,F)$ is precisely the space of all continuous linear mappings from $E$ into $F$.
