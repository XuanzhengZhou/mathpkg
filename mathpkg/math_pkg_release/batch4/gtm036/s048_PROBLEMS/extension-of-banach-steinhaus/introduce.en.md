This problem extends the classical Banach-Steinhaus theorem to the setting of general linear topological spaces. It introduces property (P), which requires that the only vector topologies with local bases of $\mathcal{T}$-closed sets are those weaker than $\mathcal{T}$. This property, together with Problem 7C, yields an alternative proof of Theorem 18.3 on the Banach-Steinhaus principle for barrelled spaces.
