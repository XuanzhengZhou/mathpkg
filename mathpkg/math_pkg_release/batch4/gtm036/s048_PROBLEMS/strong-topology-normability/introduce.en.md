This result characterizes when the strong dual of a locally convex space is normable. The criterion involves the bound extension $\mathcal{T}^b$ of the original topology: the strong dual is normable precisely when the bound topology makes the original space pseudo-normable. This connects the normability of the dual to the bounded structure of the original space.
