This result generalizes the classical Banach-Alaoglu theorem. While the standard version asserts weak* compactness of the polar of a neighborhood, this extension identifies the strongest admissible topology on the dual for which the polar remains compact: the topology of uniform convergence on totally bounded subsets. The proof uses Theorem 8.17 and Problem 16A.
