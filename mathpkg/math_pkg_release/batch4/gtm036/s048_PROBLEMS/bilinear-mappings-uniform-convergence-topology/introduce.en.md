This problem develops the theory of topologies on spaces of bilinear mappings. Given families of bounded sets covering the domain spaces, one can define a locally convex topology of uniform convergence on products of those sets. The result characterizes completeness of the space of separately continuous bilinear functionals in terms of completeness of the dual spaces, and extends this to vector-valued bilinear mappings when the domain spaces are Mackey spaces.
