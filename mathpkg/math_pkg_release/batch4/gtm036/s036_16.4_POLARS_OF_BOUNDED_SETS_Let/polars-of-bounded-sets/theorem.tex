Let $E$ and $F$ be paired linear spaces. Then

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] a subset $A$ of $E$ is $w(E,F)$-bounded if and only if its polar $A^{o}$ is radial at $0$; and
\item[(ii)] if $A$ is a subset of $E$, then $A^{o}$ is $w(F,E)$-bounded if and only if the $w(E,F)$-closed convex circled extension $A^{o}_{0}$ of $A$ is radial at $0$.
\end{enumerate}
