A cone is generating (or reproducing) if its difference set $C - C$ equals the entire space $E$. Geometrically, this means every element of $E$ can be expressed as the difference of two elements from the cone. In the context of ordered linear spaces, a generating positive cone means every element is the difference of two positive elements, which is the most interesting case for analysis.
