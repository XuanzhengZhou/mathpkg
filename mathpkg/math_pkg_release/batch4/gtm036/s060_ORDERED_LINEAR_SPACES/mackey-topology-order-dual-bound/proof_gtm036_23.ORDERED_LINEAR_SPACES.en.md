This consequence follows from the preceding theorem characterizing functionals as differences of positive functionals. As stated in the text: "A linear functional on a vector lattice is the difference of positive functionals if and only if it is bounded on order bounded sets. Hence the Mackey topology $m(E, E^\star)$, where $E^\star$ is the order dual of $E$, is bound." The full proof appears later in the chapter.
