The Mackey topology $m(E, E^\star)$ determined by the pairing of a vector lattice $E$ with its order dual $E^\star$ is a bound topology. This follows from the characterization that linear functionals on $E$ are differences of positive functionals exactly when they are bounded on order-bounded sets. The "bound" property of the Mackey topology means that every $m(E, E^\star)$-bounded set is order-bounded, connecting the topological and order structures.
