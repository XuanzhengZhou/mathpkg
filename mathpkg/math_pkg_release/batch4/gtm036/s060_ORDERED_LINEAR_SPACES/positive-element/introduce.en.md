A positive element in an ordered linear space is simply an element that belongs to the positive cone. The positive cone consists precisely of all positive elements. The notion of positivity is fundamental: positive linear functionals, positive operators, and the comparison $x \geq y \iff x - y \geq 0$ all rely on distinguishing the positive elements.
