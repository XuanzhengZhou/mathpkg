The generating cone condition is equivalent to the ordering being a directed set. For any two elements $x, y \in E$, there is a positive element $z$ that dominates both. This is a standard reformulation obtained by a simple translation argument from the characterization that every element has a positive upper bound. Directedness is a fundamental property in the theory of ordered vector spaces.
