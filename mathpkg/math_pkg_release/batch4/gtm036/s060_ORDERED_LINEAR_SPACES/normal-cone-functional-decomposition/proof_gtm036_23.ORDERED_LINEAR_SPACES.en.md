This corollary follows from the preceding theorem. If the positive cone of $E$ is normal in the sense of Krein, then there exists an equivalent monotonic norm on $E$. By the theorem, the existence of such a norm implies that each continuous linear functional decomposes as the difference of two positive functionals. The details of the construction of the equivalent monotonic norm from a normal cone are given later in the chapter.
