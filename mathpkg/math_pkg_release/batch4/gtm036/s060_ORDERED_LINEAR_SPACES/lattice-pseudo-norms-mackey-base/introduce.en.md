On a vector lattice $E$ paired with its order dual $E^\star$, the Mackey topology $m(E, E^\star)$ is generated by lattice pseudo-norms. A lattice pseudo-norm is one that respects the lattice structure (e.g., $|x| \leq |y|$ implies $\|x\| \leq \|y\|$). This result shows that the topological and lattice structures are deeply intertwined: the natural topology determined by the order dual is precisely the one generated by all lattice-respecting pseudo-norms.
