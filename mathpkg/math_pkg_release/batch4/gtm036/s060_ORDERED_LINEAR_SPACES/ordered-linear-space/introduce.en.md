An ordered linear space (or partially ordered linear space) is a real linear space equipped with a vector ordering. The ordering is completely determined by the positive cone $\{x : x \geq 0\}$, making the cone the fundamental geometric object. The study of ordered linear spaces investigates the interplay between the algebraic (linear) structure and the order structure.
