The adjoint $T^*$ of a linear transformation $T$ between locally convex spaces is the specialization of the dual transformation to the natural pairings $\langle E, E^*\rangle$ and $\langle F, F^*\rangle$. It is defined by the relation $T^*(h) = h \circ T$ for all $h \in F^*$, which uniquely determines $T^*$ whenever it exists. The adjoint maps continuous linear functionals on $F$ to continuous linear functionals on $E$ by precomposition with $T$.
