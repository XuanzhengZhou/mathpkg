This is the Krein-Smulian theorem for the dual of a complete pseudo-metrizable linear topological space. It states that a convex subset of the dual is weak* closed if and only if its intersection with every equicontinuous set is weak* closed in that set. The result follows from the identification of the topology $\mathcal{T}_S$ in Theorem 22.5 together with the Mackey-Arens theorem and the bipolar theorem.
