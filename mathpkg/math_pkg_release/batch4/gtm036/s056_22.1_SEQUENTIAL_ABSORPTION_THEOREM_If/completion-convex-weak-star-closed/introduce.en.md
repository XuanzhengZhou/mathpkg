This corollary strengthens Theorem 22.6 by observing that for a locally convex pseudo-metrizable space $E$, the dual $(E^\wedge)^*$ is isomorphic to $E^*$. Each equicontinuous subset of $E^*$ is contained in a $w(E^*, E^\wedge)$-compact set, and since $w(E^*, E)$ is a weaker Hausdorff topology, the relativizations coincide. Combined with Theorem 22.6, this yields the stated equivalence for convex subsets.
