Let $E$ be a pseudo-metrizable linear topological space, and let $W$ be a subset of $E^*$ such that $0 \in W$ and $U^0 \cap W$ is weak* open in $U^0$ for each neighborhood $U$ of $0$ in $E$. Then there is a countable set $S$ in $E$ such that $S^0 \subset W$ and the points of $S$ can be arranged to form a sequence converging to $0$.
