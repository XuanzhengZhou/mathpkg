Let $E$ and $F$ be two linear spaces over the same scalar field. A bilinear functional on the product $E 	imes F$ is a functional $B$ such that $B(ax + by, f) = aB(x,f) + bB(y,f)$ and $B(x, af + bg) = aB(x,f) + bB(x,g)$. A pairing is an ordered pair $\langle E,F angle$ of linear spaces together with a fixed bilinear functional on their product.
