Let $E$ be a complete metrizable space, let $F$ be a metrizable space, and let $T$ be a continuous linear transformation of $E$ into $F$. Then the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(i)] $T$ is a relatively open map of $E$ into $F$;
  \item[(ii)] $T$ is weakly relatively open;
  \item[(iii)] the range $T[E]$ is closed;
  \item[(iv)] the adjoint map $T^*$ is weak$^*$ relatively open;
  \item[(v)] the range of $T^*$ is weak$^*$ closed.
\end{enumerate}
