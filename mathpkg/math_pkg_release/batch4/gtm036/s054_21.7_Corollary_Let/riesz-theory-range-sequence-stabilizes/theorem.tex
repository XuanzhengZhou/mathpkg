Let $E$ be a linear topological space and $T$ a completely continuous linear mapping of $E$ into itself. Let $\lambda$ be a non-zero scalar and write $\lambda I - T = S$. Then the sequence $\{S^r[E]\}$ decreases; there is an integer $m \geq 0$ such that $S^r[E] \supsetneq S^{r+1}[E]$ properly for $0 \leq r < m$ and $S^r[E] = S^{r+1}[E]$ for $r \geq m$.
