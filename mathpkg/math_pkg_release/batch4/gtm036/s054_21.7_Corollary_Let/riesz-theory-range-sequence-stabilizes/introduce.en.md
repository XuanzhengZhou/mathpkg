For a completely continuous operator $S = \lambda I - T$, the descending sequence of ranges $S^r[E]$ eventually stabilizes at some finite index $m$. The proof is similar to that of part (b), using the Riesz closed subspace lemma, and relies on the fact that each $S^r[E]$ is closed in the topology of $E$.
