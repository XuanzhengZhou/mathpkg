The stabilization indices for the ascending sequence of nullspaces and the descending sequence of ranges of $S = \lambda I - T$ are equal. Moreover, the space $E$ decomposes as the direct sum of the terminal nullspace $S^{-n}[0]$ and the terminal range $S^n[E]$. This is the culmination of the Riesz theory of completely continuous operators, providing a Fredholm-type decomposition for general linear topological spaces.
