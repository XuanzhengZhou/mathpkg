Let $E$ be a linear topological space and $T$ a completely continuous linear mapping of $E$ into itself. Let $\lambda$ be a non-zero scalar and write $\lambda I - T = S$. Let $n$ be the stabilization index of the nullspace sequence $\{S^{-r}[0]\}$ from part (b) and $m$ the stabilization index of the range sequence $\{S^r[E]\}$ from part (c). Then $n = m$, and $E$ is the direct sum
$$E = S^{-n}[0] \oplus S^n[E].$$
