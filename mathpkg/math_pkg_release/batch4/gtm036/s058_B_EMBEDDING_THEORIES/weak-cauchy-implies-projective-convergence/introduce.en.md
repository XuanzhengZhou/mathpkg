This proposition establishes a striking relationship between weak and strong notions of convergence in the projective limit space $E^p(\Omega)$. Any sequence that is Cauchy with respect to the weak topology automatically converges in the finer projective limit topology. This property reflects the special structure of $E^p(\Omega)$ as a projective limit of $l^p$ spaces and implies a form of weak sequential completeness that forces actual topological convergence.
