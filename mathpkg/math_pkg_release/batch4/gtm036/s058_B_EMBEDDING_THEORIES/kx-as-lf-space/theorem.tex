Let $X$ be a topological space in which there is a countable co-base for the compact sets (for example, a space which is locally compact and is the union of a sequence of compact sets), and let $K(X)$ be the space of continuous functions on $X$ of compact support with the inductive limit topology. Then $K(X)$ is an $LF$ space; if $X$ is not compact, $K(X)$ cannot be metrizable.
