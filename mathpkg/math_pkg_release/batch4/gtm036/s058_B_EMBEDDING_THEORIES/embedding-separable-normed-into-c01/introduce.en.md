This theorem states that every separable normed space can be isometrically embedded into the Banach space $C([0,1])$ of continuous functions on the unit interval with the supremum norm. This provides a universal isometric representation for all separable normed spaces, showing that $C([0,1])$ is a universal separable Banach space in the sense of isometric embeddings. The result is a classical consequence of the embedding theory for locally convex spaces.
