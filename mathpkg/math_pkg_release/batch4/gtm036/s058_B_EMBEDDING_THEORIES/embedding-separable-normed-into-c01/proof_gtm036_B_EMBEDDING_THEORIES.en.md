This result follows from the embedding theorem for locally convex metrizable separable spaces into $C(R)$. A separable normed space is in particular a locally convex metrizable separable space, hence it is isomorphic to a subspace of $C(R)$. By considering the restriction to $[0,1]$ and using the fact that normed spaces carry a uniform structure compatible with isometries, one obtains the stronger isometric embedding into $C([0,1])$ with the uniform convergence topology.
