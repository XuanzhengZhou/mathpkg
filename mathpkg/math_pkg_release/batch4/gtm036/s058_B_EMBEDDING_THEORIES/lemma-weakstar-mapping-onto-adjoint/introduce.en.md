This lemma constructs a continuous surjection $f$ from the real line $R$ onto the weak* dual $E^*$ of a locally convex metrizable separable space $E$. The key property is that the images $f[K]$ of compact subsets $K \subset R$ form a co-base for the equicontinuous subsets of $E^*$. The construction uses a nested sequence of weak* compact, convex, circled, metrizable sets $B_n$ and continuous surjections from the Cantor set onto each $B_n$.
