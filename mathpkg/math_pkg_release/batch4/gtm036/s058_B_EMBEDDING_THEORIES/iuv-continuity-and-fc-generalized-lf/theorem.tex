Each of the mappings $I_{UV}$ is continuous. Since $\mathcal{U}$ contains a countable cofinal subset $\{U_n: n = 1, 2, \cdots\}$,

$$F(C) = \lim_{\substack{\longrightarrow \\ n}} \{A(U_n): n = 1, 2, \cdots\}$$

and the inductive limit topology defined by the Fréchet spaces $A(U_n)$ is the topology of $F(C)$. Thus $F(C)$ is a generalized $LF$ space.
