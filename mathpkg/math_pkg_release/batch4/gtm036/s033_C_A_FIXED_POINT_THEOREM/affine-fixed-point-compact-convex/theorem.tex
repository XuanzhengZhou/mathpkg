Let $A$ be a non-void compact convex subset of a linear topological space $E$ and suppose that for each non-zero member $x$ of $A - A$ there is a continuous linear functional $f$ on $E$ with $f(x) \neq 0$. Let $T$ be a continuous mapping of $A$ into itself such that
$$T\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i x_i\right) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i T(x_i)$$
whenever $x_i \in A$ and $a_i \geq 0$ for $1 \leq i \leq n$ and $\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i = 1$. Then there is a point $x_0$ in $A$ such that $T(x_0) = x_0$.
