This theorem establishes the compatibility of weak topologies with the operations of taking subspaces and quotients in paired linear spaces. Given a subspace $H$ of $E$, the relative weak topology $w(E,F)|_H$ coincides with the weak topology induced by the quotient pairing $H \times (F/H^\circ)$. Dually, the quotient topology on $E/H$ derived from $w(E,F)$ coincides with the weak topology $w(E/H, H^\circ)$ arising from the natural pairing between $E/H$ and the annihilator $H^\circ$.
