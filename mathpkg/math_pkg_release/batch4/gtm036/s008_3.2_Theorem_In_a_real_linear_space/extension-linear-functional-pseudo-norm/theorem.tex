Let $E$ be a real or complex linear space, $F$ a linear subspace, $p$ a pseudo-norm on $E$, and $f$ a linear functional on $F$ such that $|f(x)| \leq p(x)$ for $x$ in $F$. Then there is a linear functional $\overline{f}$ on $E$, an extension of $f$, such that $|\overline{f}(x)| \leq p(x)$ for all $x$ in $E$.
