This theorem characterizes continuity and openness of additive maps between linear topological spaces using the Baire property. For continuity: if preimages of neighborhoods satisfy the condition of Baire and the domain is second category, then the map is continuous. Dually, for openness: if images of neighborhoods are radial with the Baire property and the codomain is second category, then the map is open. These results are fundamental in the automatic continuity theory for linear operators.
