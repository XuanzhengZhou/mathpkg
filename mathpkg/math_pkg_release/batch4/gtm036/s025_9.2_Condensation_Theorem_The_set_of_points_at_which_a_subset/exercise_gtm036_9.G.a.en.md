Let $T$ be an additive mapping of a linear topological space $E$ into a linear topological space $F$ such that the image of each neighborhood of 0 in $E$ is a somewhere dense set in $F$. Then the closure of the image of each neighborhood of 0 in $E$ is a neighborhood of 0 in $F$. If, in addition, $T$ is continuous and $E$ is pseudo-metrizable, then $F$ is pseudo-metrizable.
