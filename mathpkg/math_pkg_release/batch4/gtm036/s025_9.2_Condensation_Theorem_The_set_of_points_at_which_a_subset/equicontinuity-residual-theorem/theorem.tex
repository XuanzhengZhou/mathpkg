Let $X$ be a topological space and let $\{f_n\}$ be a sequence of continuous functions on $X$ to a pseudo-metric space, convergent at every point of $X$ to a function $f$. Then the set of points of equicontinuity of $\{f_n\}$ is a residual subset of $X$ (its complement is of the first category in $X$). In particular, $f$ is continuous at the points of a residual subset of $X$.
