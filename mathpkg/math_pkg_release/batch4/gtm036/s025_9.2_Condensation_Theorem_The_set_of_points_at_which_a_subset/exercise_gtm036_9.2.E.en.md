**Embedding of a Finite Dimensional Compact Metric Space into a Euclidean Space.** Let $X$ be a compact metric space of dimension less than or equal to $n$. Show that $X$ can be homeomorphically mapped into $E_{2n+1}$ (the Euclidean space of dimension $2n+1$).

*Hint:* Let $E$ be the space of all continuous maps on $X$ into $E_{2n+1}$, with the topology of uniform convergence. Let $B_e$ be the set of all continuous $e$-maps on $X$ into $E_{2n+1}$. Show that $B_e$ is an open dense subset of $E$, and use the Baire category theorem.
