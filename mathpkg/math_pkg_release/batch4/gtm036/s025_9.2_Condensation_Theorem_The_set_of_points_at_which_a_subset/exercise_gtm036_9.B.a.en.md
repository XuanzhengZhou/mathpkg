Let $X$ and $Y$ be topological spaces, and let $f$ be a continuous mapping of $X$ into $Y$ such that the image of each open subset of $X$ is a somewhere dense subset of $Y$. Let $A$ be a subset of $X$ and $B$ a subset of $Y$.

If $B$ is of the first category in $Y$, then $f^{-1}[B]$ is of the first category in $X$.
