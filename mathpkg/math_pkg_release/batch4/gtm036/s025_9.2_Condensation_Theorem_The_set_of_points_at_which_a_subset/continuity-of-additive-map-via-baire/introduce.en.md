This corollary characterizes continuity and openness of additive maps between linear topological spaces using the Baire condition. When the domain is second category, continuity is equivalent to inverse images of neighborhoods having the Baire property. Dually, when the codomain is second category, openness is equivalent to images of neighborhoods being radial with the Baire property.
