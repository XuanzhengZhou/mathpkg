This corollary restates the continuity and openness criteria for additive maps in a neater form: when the domain is second category, continuity is equivalent to the preimages of neighborhoods having the Baire property. Dually, when the codomain is second category, openness is equivalent to the images of neighborhoods being radial with the Baire property. These formulate the automatic continuity/openness problem in terms of the Baire property.
