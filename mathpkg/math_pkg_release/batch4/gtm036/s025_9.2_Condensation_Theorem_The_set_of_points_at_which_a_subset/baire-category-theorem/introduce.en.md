The Baire Category Theorem is one of the most important results in functional analysis. It states that in a pseudo-metric space, a complete subset with non-empty interior cannot be written as a countable union of nowhere dense sets. The proof proceeds by constructing a nested sequence of closed spheres whose intersection lies in the given set but avoids each nowhere dense set.
