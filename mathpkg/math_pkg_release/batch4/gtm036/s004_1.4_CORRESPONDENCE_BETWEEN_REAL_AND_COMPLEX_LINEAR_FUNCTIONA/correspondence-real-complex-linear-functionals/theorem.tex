The correspondence defined by assigning to each linear functional on $E$ its real part is a one-to-one correspondence between all the linear functionals on $E$ and all the linear functionals on the real restriction of $E$. If $f$ and $r$ are paired under this correspondence, where $f$ is a linear functional on $E$ and $r$ is a linear functional on the real restriction of $E$, then
$$
f(x) = r(x) - i\, r(ix)
$$
for every $x$ in $E$.
