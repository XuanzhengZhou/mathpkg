The projective limit (inverse limit) of the projective system $\{E_t, P_{st}\}$ is the subspace of the product $\prod \{E_t : t \in A\}$ consisting of all $x$ such that for every pair of indices $t$ and $s$ with $t \geq s$,
$$
P_{st}(x(t)) = x(s).
$$
The projective limit is denoted by $\varprojlim \{E_t : t \in A\}$.
