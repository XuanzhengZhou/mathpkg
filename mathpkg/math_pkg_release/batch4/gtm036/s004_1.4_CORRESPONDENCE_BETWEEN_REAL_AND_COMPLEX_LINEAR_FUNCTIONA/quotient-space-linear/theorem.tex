If $F$ is a linear subspace of a linear space $E$, the quotient space (factor space, coset space, difference space) $E/F$ is defined as follows. The elements of $E/F$ are sets of the form $x + F$, where $x$ is an element of $E$; two sets of this form are either disjoint or identical. Addition $\oplus$ and scalar multiplication $\cdot$ in $E/F$ are defined by:
$$
(x + F) \oplus (y + F) = (x + y) + F, \qquad a \cdot (x + F) = a x + F.
$$
With these operations $E/F$ is a linear space.
