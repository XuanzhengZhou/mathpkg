This lemma provides a pointwise criterion for a continuous function to belong to the uniform closure of a lattice-closed subset $A \subseteq C(X)$. It states that $h$ is in the uniform closure of $A$ precisely when $h$ can be approximated from below at each point by functions in $A$ that match $h$ at that point. This technical result is a key tool in proving the density of the range of the evaluation map in the characterization of $M$ spaces (Theorem 24.6), and is related in spirit to the Stone-Weierstrass theorem.
