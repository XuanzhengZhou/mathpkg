Let $E$ be a normed lattice and identify $E$ with its image under the evaluation map in $E^{**}$. Let $E^-$ be the closure of $E$ in $E^{**}$ with respect to the norm topology. Then whenever $x, y \in E^-$, both $x \wedge y$ and $x \vee y$ belong to $E^-$. Consequently, $E^-$ is a Banach lattice in which the given normed lattice $E$ is densely embedded.
