Uniform convexity is not automatically preserved under the formation of product spaces with the standard product norm, but it can be recovered by choosing an equivalent norm. This proposition establishes that the class of uniformly convex spaces is closed under finite products up to equivalent renorming. A typical construction uses an $\ell^p$-sum of the norms for $1 < p < \infty$ or related uniformly convex renormings.
