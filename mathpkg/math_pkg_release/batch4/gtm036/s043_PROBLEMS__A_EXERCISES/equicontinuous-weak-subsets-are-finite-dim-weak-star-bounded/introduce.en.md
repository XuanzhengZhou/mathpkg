This exercise characterizes the subsets of the dual space $E^*$ that are equicontinuous with respect to the weak topology on $E$. Remarkably, they are exactly the weak* bounded subsets that are contained in finite-dimensional subspaces. This provides a structural link between equicontinuity (an analytical condition) and finite dimensionality (an algebraic condition) in the context of weak topologies.
