In a Hausdorff linear topological space, a subset of the dual space is total precisely when it separates points of the original space. This is the dual counterpart to the notion of a total subset in the space itself. The condition asserts that the common zero set of the functionals in the total set is trivial, which in a Hausdorff setting reflects the fact that the weak topology induced by the total set on $E$ is Hausdorff.
