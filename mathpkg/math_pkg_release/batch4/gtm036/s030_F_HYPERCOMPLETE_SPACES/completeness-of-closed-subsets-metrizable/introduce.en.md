This theorem establishes that the Hausdorff uniformity on the collection of non-void closed subsets preserves completeness when the underlying space is complete and metrizable. The proof follows from the classical theory of the Hausdorff metric (see Section 4D of GTM036): when $E$ is metrizable, the Hausdorff uniformity is induced by the Hausdorff metric on closed subsets, and it is a standard result that the space of closed subsets of a complete metric space is complete under the Hausdorff metric.
