A net of closed sets is Cauchy with respect to the Hausdorff uniformity if eventually the terms of the net are mutually close: for any neighborhood $U$ of $0$, after a sufficiently large index $\gamma(U)$, any two later sets $A_\alpha$ and $A_\beta$ satisfy $A_\alpha \subset A_\beta + U$. This is the direct translation of the uniform Cauchy condition into the language of the Hausdorff uniformity on the space $\mathcal{E}$.
