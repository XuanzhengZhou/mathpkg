Let $\mathcal{E}$ be the space of non-void closed subsets of a linear topological space $E$ with the Hausdorff uniformity. Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a local base of $E$. A net $\{A_\gamma: \gamma \in \Gamma\}$ in $\mathcal{E}$ \text{converges} to $A \in \mathcal{E}$ if and only if for each $U \in \mathcal{U}$ there exists $\gamma(U) \in \Gamma$ such that for all $\gamma \geq \gamma(U)$,
$$A_\gamma \subset A + U \quad \text{and} \quad A \subset A_\gamma + U.$$
