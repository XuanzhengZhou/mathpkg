Proposition 17.13 summarizes the behavior of weak topologies under three fundamental constructions in functional analysis: subspaces, quotient spaces, and products. The weak topology on a subspace is the relative topology; on a quotient it is the quotient topology; and on a product it is the product of weak topologies. These facts are essential for working with weak topologies in locally convex spaces.
