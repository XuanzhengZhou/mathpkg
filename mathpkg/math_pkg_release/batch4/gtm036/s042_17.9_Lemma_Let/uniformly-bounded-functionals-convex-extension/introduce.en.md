Theorem 17.11 states that if a sequence of continuous linear functionals is uniformly bounded on a compact set $C$ and converges pointwise to zero on $C$, then the convergence extends to the entire closed convex extension of $C$. This result is a key step in establishing the equivalence of weak compactness conditions and avoids the use of measure theory.
