Lemma 17.9 provides a crucial technical result for the proof of the weak compactness theorem. Given a non-negative additive set function $m$ on an algebra and a sequence of sets each of measure exceeding a fixed positive bound $e$, one can extract a subsequence whose every finite intersection still has positive measure. The proof uses a boundedness argument on unions and a diagonal process.
