Theorem 17.12 (Theorem on Weak Compactness) is the principal result of Chapter 17. In a Hausdorff locally convex space, for a bounded set $A$ whose convex extension has complete closure, weak compactness is equivalent to: (i) every sequence having a weak cluster point, (ii) an iterated limit condition for equicontinuous functionals, (iii) weak compactness of the weak closure of $A$, and (iv) weak compactness of the weak closure of the convex extension.
