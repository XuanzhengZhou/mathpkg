Let $E$ be a Hausdorff locally convex linear topological space, and let $A$ be a bounded subset of $E$ such that the closure $C^-$ of the convex extension $C$ of $A$ is complete. Then the following are equivalent:

(i) Each sequence in $A$ has a weak cluster point in $E$.

(ii) For each sequence $\{x_n\}$ in $A$ and each equicontinuous sequence $\{f_n\}$ of linear functionals it is true that $\lim_{m \to \infty} f_n(x_m) = \lim_{n \to \infty} f_n(x_m)$ whenever each of the limits exist.

(iii) The weak closure of $A$ is weakly compact.

(iv) The weak closure of $C$ is weakly compact.
