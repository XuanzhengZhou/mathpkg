Let $T$ be a continuous linear operator from a linear topological space $E$ to a linear topological space $H$, and let $\bar{T}$ be its closure (the operator whose graph is the closure $\bar{G}_T$ of the graph $G_T$ of $T$). Let $D_T$ be the domain of $T$ and $D_{\bar{T}}$ the domain of $\bar{T}$. Then

$$
D_{\bar{T}} \subset \bar{D}_T.
$$

If $H$ is complete, then $D_{\bar{T}} = \bar{D}_T$.
