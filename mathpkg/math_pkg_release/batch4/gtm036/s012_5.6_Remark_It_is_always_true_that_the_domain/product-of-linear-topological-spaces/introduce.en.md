The product $\bigwedge \{E_t : t \in A\}$ of an indexed family of linear topological spaces $E_t$, equipped with the product topology, is itself a linear topological space. The projections $P_t$ and injections $I_t$ are the standard coordinate maps. The proof is a direct application of the definitions of a linear topological space and the preceding results on product topologies and continuity of linear operations.
