If, for each member $t$ of an index set $A$, $E_t$ is a linear topological space, then the product $\bigwedge \{E_t : t \in A\}$ is a linear space with the product topology. For each $t \in A$, let $P_t$ be the projection of the product onto the coordinate space $E_t$, and let $I_t$ be the injection of $E_t$ into the product. Under the product topology, the product space is a linear topological space.
