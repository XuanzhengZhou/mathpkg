Let $\Phi(x)$ be the rational function field. The valuations trivial on $\Phi$ are: (i) the degree valuation $v_\infty(f/g) = \deg g - \deg f$, and (ii) for each irreducible polynomial $p(x) \in \Phi[x]$, the $p$-adic valuation $v_p(p^k f/g) = k$ where $f, g$ are coprime to $p$. These correspond to points on the projective line over $\Phi$.
