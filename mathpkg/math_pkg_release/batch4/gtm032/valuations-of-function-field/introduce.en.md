The valuations of the rational function field $\Phi(x)$ that are trivial on constants correspond to points of the projective line $\mathbb{P}^1_\Phi$: finite points give $p$-adic valuations, the point at infinity gives the degree valuation. This connects valuation theory with algebraic geometry, with valuations generalizing the notion of codimension-1 points.
