Let $\mathfrak{A}/\Phi$ be a finite dimensional algebra. The regular representation of $\mathfrak{A}$ is the homomorphism $R: a \mapsto a_R$ where $a_R$ is the right multiplication $x \mapsto xa$. Relative to a basis, this gives a matrix representation. For $\mathfrak{A} = \Phi[a]$ with minimum polynomial $f(x) = x^n - \alpha_1 x^{n-1} + \cdots + (-1)^n \alpha_n$, the matrix of $a_R$ is the companion matrix of $f$.
