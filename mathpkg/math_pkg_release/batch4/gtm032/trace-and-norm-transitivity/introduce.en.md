The trace and norm are transitive in towers of finite extensions. The trace (resp. norm) from $P$ to $\Phi$ equals the trace (resp. norm) from $E$ to $\Phi$ applied to the trace (resp. norm) from $P$ to $E$. This is a fundamental computational tool and follows from the corresponding property for the regular representation.
