Let $R$ be a real closed field and $f(x) \in R[x]$ a polynomial. The Sturm sequence of $f$ is defined by $f_0 = f$, $f_1 = f'$, and $f_{i-1} = q_i f_i - f_{i+1}$ (Euclidean algorithm with sign change). Then the number of distinct real roots of $f$ in any interval $[a, b]$ (with $f(a)f(b) \neq 0$) equals $V(a) - V(b)$, where $V(c)$ is the number of sign changes in the Sturm sequence evaluated at $c$.
