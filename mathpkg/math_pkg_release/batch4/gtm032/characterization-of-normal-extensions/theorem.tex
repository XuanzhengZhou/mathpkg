For a finite dimensional extension $P/\Phi$, the following conditions are equivalent:

(1) $P/\Phi$ is a splitting field of a polynomial $f(x) \in \Phi[x]$.

(2) Every isomorphism $s$ of $P/\Phi$ into an extension field $\Delta/\Phi$ is an automorphism of $P/\Phi$.

(3) Every irreducible polynomial $g(x) \in \Phi[x]$ which has a root in $P$ is a product of linear factors in $P[x]$.
