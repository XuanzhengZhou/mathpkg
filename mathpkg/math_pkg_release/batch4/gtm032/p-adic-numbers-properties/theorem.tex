The field $\mathbb{Q}_p$ of $p$-adic numbers is the completion of $\mathbb{Q}$ with respect to the $p$-adic valuation. Its valuation ring is $\mathbb{Z}_p$, the ring of $p$-adic integers. $\mathbb{Q}_p$ is a locally compact, complete, non-archimedean valued field of characteristic $0$ with residue field $\mathbb{F}_p$. Every element can be uniquely expressed as $\sum_{i=N}^\infty a_i p^i$ with $0 \leq a_i < p$.
