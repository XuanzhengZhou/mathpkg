The $p$-adic numbers form a complete non-archimedean field with rich arithmetic structure. Elements have unique $p$-adic expansions, and the topology is totally disconnected. $\mathbb{Q}_p$ and its finite extensions are the "local fields" of algebraic number theory, and their study reveals deep information about global fields.
