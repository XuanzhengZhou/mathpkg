The existence and uniqueness of the algebraic closure: every field admits an algebraically closed algebraic extension, and any two such extensions are isomorphic. The proof uses Zorn's Lemma (or well-ordering) to construct a maximal algebraic extension, then shows it must be algebraically closed. This is foundational for all of field theory.
