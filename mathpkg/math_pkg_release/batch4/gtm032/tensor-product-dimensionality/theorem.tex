If $\mathfrak{M}$ and $\mathfrak{N}$ are vector spaces over $\Phi$ with bases $\{e_\alpha\}$ and $\{f_\beta\}$, then $\{e_\alpha \otimes f_\beta\}$ is a basis for $\mathfrak{M} \otimes_\Phi \mathfrak{N}$. In particular, if both $\mathfrak{M}$ and $\mathfrak{N}$ are finite dimensional, then
$$[\mathfrak{M} \otimes_\Phi \mathfrak{N} : \Phi] = [\mathfrak{M} : \Phi][\mathfrak{N} : \Phi].$$
