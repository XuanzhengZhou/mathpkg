Let $\{e_\alpha\}$ and $\{f_\beta\}$ be generators for vector spaces $\mathfrak{M}$ over $\Phi$ and $\mathfrak{N}$ over $\Phi$ respectively. Then the set $\{e_\alpha \otimes f_\beta\}$ generates $\mathfrak{M} \otimes_\Phi \mathfrak{N}$. Moreover, if $\{e_\alpha\}$ and $\{f_\beta\}$ are bases, then $\{e_\alpha \otimes f_\beta\}$ is a basis for $\mathfrak{M} \otimes_\Phi \mathfrak{N}$.
