A finite dimensional extension $P/\Phi$ is Galois (i.e., $\Phi$ is the subfield of invariants of a finite group of automorphisms) if and only if $P/\Phi$ is a splitting field of a separable polynomial over $\Phi$. In this case, every element of $P$ is separable over $\Phi$, and $P/\Phi$ is normal and separable.
