This theorem characterizes finite-dimensional Galois extensions precisely as splitting fields of separable polynomials. The equivalence shows that the abstract definition of a Galois extension (being the fixed field of a finite automorphism group) coincides with the concrete construction via separable polynomials. The result also implies that Galois extensions are exactly those that are both normal and separable.
