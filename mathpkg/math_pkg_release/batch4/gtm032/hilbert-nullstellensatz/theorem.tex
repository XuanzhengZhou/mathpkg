Let $\Phi$ be an algebraically closed field and let $\mathfrak{A} = \Phi[t_1, \ldots, t_r]$ be a finitely generated commutative algebra over $\Phi$. If $M$ is a maximal ideal of $\mathfrak{A}$, then $\mathfrak{A}/M \cong \Phi$ and the images of the $t_i$ satisfy the same polynomial relations as their residue classes. Equivalently: if polynomials $f_1, \ldots, f_m \in \Phi[x_1, \ldots, x_n]$ have no common zero in $\Phi^n$, then there exist $g_i$ such that $\sum g_i f_i = 1$.
