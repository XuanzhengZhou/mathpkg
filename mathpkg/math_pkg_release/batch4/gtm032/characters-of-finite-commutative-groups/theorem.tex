Let $A$ be a finite commutative group. A character of $A$ in a field $\Phi$ is a homomorphism $\chi: A \to \Phi^*$. The set of characters $\hat{A}$ forms a group under pointwise multiplication, isomorphic to $A$. The characters are linearly independent over $\Phi$, and for any subgroup $B \leq A$, the characters trivial on $B$ correspond to $\widehat{A/B}$.
