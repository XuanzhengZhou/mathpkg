This lemma gives two useful properties of splitting fields. The first states that a splitting field remains a splitting field when the base field is enlarged to an intermediate extension. The second gives a transitivity property: if $P$ is a splitting field over an intermediate field $\Sigma$ that is generated by roots of $f(x)$, then $P$ is already a splitting field over the original base field.
