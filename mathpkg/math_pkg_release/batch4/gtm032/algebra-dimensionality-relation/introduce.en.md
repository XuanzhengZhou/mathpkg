This theorem gives the transitivity of finite dimensionality for algebras over nested fields. If $\mathfrak{A}$ is finite-dimensional over $\Phi$ and $\Phi$ is finite-dimensional over $\mathrm{E}$, then $\mathfrak{A}$ is finite-dimensional over $\mathrm{E}$ and the dimension multiplies. The proof constructs a basis for $\mathfrak{A}/\mathrm{E}$ from bases of $\mathfrak{A}/\Phi$ and $\Phi/\mathrm{E}$.
