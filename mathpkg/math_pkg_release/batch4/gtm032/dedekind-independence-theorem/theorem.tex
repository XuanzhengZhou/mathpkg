Let $E$ and $P$ be fields and let $s_1, s_2, \ldots, s_n$ be distinct isomorphisms of $E$ into $P$. Then the $s_i$ are right $P$-independent in the right $P$-vector space $\mathfrak{L}(E, P)$ of additive homomorphisms from $E$ to $P$. That is, if
$$s_1 \rho_1 + s_2 \rho_2 + \cdots + s_n \rho_n = 0, \quad \rho_i \in P,$$
then every $\rho_i = 0$.
