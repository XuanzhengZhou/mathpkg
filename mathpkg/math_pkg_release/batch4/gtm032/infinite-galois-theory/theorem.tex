Let $P/\Phi$ be a (possibly infinite) Galois extension. The Krull topology on $G = \text{Gal}(P/\Phi)$ has as neighborhood basis of $1$ the subgroups $\text{Gal}(P/E)$ for finite Galois $E/\Phi$. Then $G$ is a profinite group, and there is a bijection between closed subgroups of $G$ and intermediate fields, with open subgroups corresponding to finite extensions.
