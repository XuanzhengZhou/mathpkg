Generalized Sturm Theorem: Let $R$ be a real closed field, $f, g \in R[x]$. The number of roots $\alpha$ of $f$ such that $g(\alpha) > 0$ minus the number of roots $\alpha$ of $f$ such that $g(\alpha) < 0$ can be computed from the signs of the Sturm sequence of $f$ weighted by signs of $g$. This gives a root-counting method for inequalities.
