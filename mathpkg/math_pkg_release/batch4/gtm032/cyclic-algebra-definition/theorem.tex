Let $P/\Phi$ be a cyclic Galois extension of degree $n$ with generator $g$. For $\alpha \in \Phi^*$, the cyclic algebra $(P, g, \alpha)$ is the crossed product $(G, P, \mu)$ where $G = \langle g \rangle$ and
$$\mu_{g^i, g^j} = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if } i+j < n \\ \alpha & \text{if } i+j \geq n. \end{cases}$$
The algebra has $P$-basis $\{1, u, u^2, \ldots, u^{n-1}\}$ with $u^n = \alpha$ and $u\rho = \rho^g u$ for $\rho \in P$.
