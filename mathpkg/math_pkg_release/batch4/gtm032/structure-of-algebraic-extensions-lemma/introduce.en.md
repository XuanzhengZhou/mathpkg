This lemma establishes that separability is transitive when the intermediate extension is Galois. Using the Galois group of $E/\Phi$, one constructs from the minimum polynomial of $\theta$ over $E$ a polynomial over $\Phi$ with distinct roots having $\theta$ as a root. This is a key step in proving that separable elements form a subfield.
