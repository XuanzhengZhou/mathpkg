This construction describes extending the base field of a vector space via tensor product. Given a field extension $P/\Phi$ and a $\Phi$-vector space $M$, the tensor product $P \otimes_\Phi M$ becomes a $P$-vector space $M_P$ of the same dimension. Linear transformations on $M$ extend naturally to $M_P$ with unchanged matrices. This is a fundamental technique for studying vector spaces and linear transformations under field extensions.
