Ostrowski's theorem classifies all non-trivial valuations of the rational numbers. There are exactly two types: the archimedean (standard) valuation and the $p$-adic valuations for each prime $p$. This fundamental result shows that $\mathbb{R}$ and $\mathbb{Q}_p$ are the only completions of $\mathbb{Q}$.
