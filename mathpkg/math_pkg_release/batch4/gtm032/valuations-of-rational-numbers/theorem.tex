Ostrowski's Theorem: Every non-trivial real valuation of $\mathbb{Q}$ is equivalent either to the standard absolute value $|\cdot|_\infty$ or to a $p$-adic absolute value $|\cdot|_p$ defined by $|p^k a/b|_p = p^{-k}$ for $a, b$ coprime to $p$. These are the only completions of $\mathbb{Q}$ (up to isomorphism).
