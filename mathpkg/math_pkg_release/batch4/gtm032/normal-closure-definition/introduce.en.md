The normal closure of a finite extension $P/\Phi$ is the smallest normal extension containing $P$. It can be constructed by taking a splitting field of the product of the minimum polynomials of a generating set of $P/\Phi$. The normal closure is unique up to isomorphism and plays a key role in studying the Galois-theoretic properties of arbitrary finite extensions.
