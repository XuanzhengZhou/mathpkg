Let $p$ be a prime. The ring of Witt vectors $W(\Phi)$ over a field $\Phi$ of characteristic $p$ is a complete discrete valuation ring of characteristic $0$ with residue field $\Phi$. Its elements are infinite sequences $(a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots)$ with $a_i \in \Phi$, with addition and multiplication defined by certain universal polynomials (the Witt polynomials).
