Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be the collection of finite groups of automorphisms in a field $\mathfrak{P}$, and let $\mathcal{I}$ be the collection of subfields of $\mathfrak{P}$ which are Galois and of finite co-dimension in $\mathfrak{P}$. For $\Phi \in \mathcal{I}$, let $A(\Phi)$ be the Galois group of $\mathfrak{P}/\Phi$, and for $G \in \mathfrak{A}$, let $I(G)$ be the subfield of $G$-invariants. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $|A(\Phi)| = [\mathfrak{P}:\Phi]$;
\item[(ii)] $I(A(\Phi)) = \Phi$;
\item[(iii)] If $H \leq G$, then $I(H) \supseteq I(G)$ and $A(I(H)) = H$;
\item[(iv)] If $H \trianglelefteq G$ and $E = I(H)$, then $E$ is Galois over $\Phi$ and $A_E(\Phi) \cong G/H$.
\end{enumerate}
Thus $G \mapsto I(G)$ and $\Phi \mapsto A(\Phi)$ give a bijection between $\mathfrak{A}$ and $\mathcal{I}$.
