The automorphisms of $\mathfrak{L}_\Phi(P)$ that fix right multiplications correspond to solutions of Noether's equations. Combined with the Skolem-Noether theorem, this gives a cohomological interpretation: the set of automorphism classes modulo inner automorphisms is isomorphic to the first cohomology set $H^1(G, P^*)$.
