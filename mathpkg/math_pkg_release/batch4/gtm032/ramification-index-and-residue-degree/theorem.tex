Let $K$ be a field with a real valuation $v$, let $L/K$ be a finite extension, and let $w_1, \ldots, w_g$ be the distinct extensions of $v$ to $L$. For each $w_i$, define the ramification index $e_i = (w_i(L^*) : v(K^*))$ and the residue degree $f_i = [\overline{L}_i : \overline{K}]$, where $\overline{L}_i, \overline{K}$ are residue fields. Then $\sum_{i=1}^g e_i f_i \leq [L:K]$, with equality when $K$ is complete and the valuation is discrete.
