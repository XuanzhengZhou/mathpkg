Let $K$ be a valued field with valuation ring $\mathfrak{o}$ and $L/K$ a finite extension. The integral closure $\mathfrak{O}$ of $\mathfrak{o}$ in $L$ is the set of elements of $L$ satisfying monic polynomials over $\mathfrak{o}$. $\mathfrak{O}$ is the intersection of all valuation rings of $L$ extending $\mathfrak{o}$. The structure of $\mathfrak{O}$ is described by ramification theory.
