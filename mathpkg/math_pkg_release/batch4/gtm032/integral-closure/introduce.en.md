The integral closure of a valuation ring in a finite extension is the intersection of the extensions of the valuation. This construction links valuation theory with integral extensions and Dedekind domains. In algebraic number theory, the integral closure of $\mathbb{Z}$ in a number field is the ring of integers, whose prime ideal structure encodes ramification.
