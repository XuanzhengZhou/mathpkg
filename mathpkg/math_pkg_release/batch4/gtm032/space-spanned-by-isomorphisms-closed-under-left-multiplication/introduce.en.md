The right $P$-vector space spanned by finitely many distinct field isomorphisms is closed under left multiplication by elements of the domain field. This follows from the operator identity $\eta_R s = s(\eta^s)_R$, which relates left multiplication in $E$ to right multiplication in $P$ via the isomorphism $s$. This closure property is crucial for the Jacobson-Bourbaki correspondence and for the study of finite groups of automorphisms in Galois theory.
