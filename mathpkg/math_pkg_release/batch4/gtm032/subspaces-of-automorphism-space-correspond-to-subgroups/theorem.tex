Let $G$ be a finite group of automorphisms of $P$ and let $\mathfrak{A} = \{\sum_{i} s_i \rho_i \mid s_i \in G, \rho_i \in P\}$. If $\mathfrak{B}$ is a subring of $\mathfrak{A}$ which is also a $P$-subspace, then $\mathfrak{B}$ is invariant under left multiplication by all $\rho_R$, $\rho \in P$, and

$$\mathfrak{B} = t_1 P + t_2 P + \cdots + t_r P$$

where $H = \{t_j\} = G \cap \mathfrak{B}$ is a subgroup of $G$. Thus every such subring-subspace $\mathfrak{B}$ is spanned by a uniquely determined subgroup $H$ of $G$.
