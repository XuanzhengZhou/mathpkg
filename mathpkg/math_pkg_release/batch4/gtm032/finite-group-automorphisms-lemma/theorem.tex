Let $G$ be a finite group of automorphisms in a field $P$ and let
$$\mathfrak{A} = \left\{ \sum_{i=1}^{n} s_i \rho_i \mid s_i \in G, \, \rho_i \in P \right\}.$$
Then $\mathfrak{A}$ is a subring of $\mathfrak{L}(P,P)$ which is a right $P$-subspace, $[\mathfrak{A}:P]_R = (G:1)$, and the subfield $\Phi$ given by $\Phi = \{\alpha \in P \mid \alpha_R A = A \alpha_R, \, A \in \mathfrak{A}\}$ is the subfield of $G$-invariants.
