This lemma connects finite groups of automorphisms with the Jacobson-Bourbaki correspondence. The ring $\mathfrak{A}$ spanned by a finite group $G$ of automorphisms over $P$ satisfies the three conditions of Theorem 2, and the associated subfield $\Phi$ is exactly the fixed field of $G$. The dimension $[\mathfrak{A}:P]_R$ equals the order of $G$, establishing the quantitative link between the group order and field degree in Galois theory.
