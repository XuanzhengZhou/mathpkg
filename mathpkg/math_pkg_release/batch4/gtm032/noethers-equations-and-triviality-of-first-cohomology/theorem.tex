Let $P/\Phi$ be a finite dimensional Galois extension with Galois group $G$. A mapping $s \mapsto \mu_s \in P^*$ ($s \in G$) satisfies Emmy Noether's equations if

$$\mu_{st} = \mu_s^t \mu_t$$

for all $s, t \in G$. 

Theorem 18: Every solution of Noether's equations has the form $\mu_s = \gamma(\gamma^s)^{-1}$ for some $\gamma \in P^*$. In cohomological language, $H^1(G, P^*) = 1$.
