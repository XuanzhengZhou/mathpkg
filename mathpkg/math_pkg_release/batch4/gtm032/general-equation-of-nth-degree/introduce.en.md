The general equation of degree $n$ has symmetric group $S_n$ as Galois group. The proof uses the fact that the elementary symmetric functions generate the field of symmetric rational functions, so the splitting field over $K$ has automorphism group $S_n$ permuting the roots. For $n \geq 5$, $S_n$ is not solvable, proving the classical theorem of Abel-Ruffini.
