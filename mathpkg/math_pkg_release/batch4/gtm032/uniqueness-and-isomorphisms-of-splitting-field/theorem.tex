Let $\alpha \mapsto \bar{\alpha}$ be an isomorphism of a field $\Phi$ onto $\bar{\Phi}$, let $f(x) \in \Phi[x]$ be a polynomial of positive degree with leading coefficient $1$, and let $\bar{f}(x)$ be the corresponding polynomial in $\bar{\Phi}[x]$. Let $P$ and $\bar{P}$ be splitting fields over $\Phi$ and $\bar{\Phi}$ of $f(x)$ and $\bar{f}(x)$ respectively. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item There exists an isomorphism of $P$ onto $\bar{P}$ extending the given isomorphism on $\Phi$.
\item If $\bar{f}(x)$ is a product of distinct linear factors in $\bar{P}[x]$, then the number of such extensions is $[P:\Phi]$.
\end{enumerate}
