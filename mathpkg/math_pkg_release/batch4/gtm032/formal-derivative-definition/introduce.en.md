The formal derivative is defined purely algebraically on the polynomial ring $\Phi[x]$ without any limiting process. By specifying its action on the basis $\{1, x, x^2, \ldots\}$ and extending linearly, one obtains a derivation satisfying the Leibniz rule $(fg)' = f'g + fg'$. The formal derivative provides a purely algebraic criterion for detecting multiple roots of polynomials.
