Let $E_1/\Phi$ and $E_2/\Phi$ be field extensions. Their tensor product $E_1 \otimes_\Phi E_2$ is a commutative algebra over $\Phi$. It is a field if and only if $E_1$ and $E_2$ are linearly disjoint over $\Phi$. In general, $E_1 \otimes_\Phi E_2$ decomposes as a direct sum of local Artin rings whose residue fields are the composites of $E_1$ and $E_2$ under various embeddings.
