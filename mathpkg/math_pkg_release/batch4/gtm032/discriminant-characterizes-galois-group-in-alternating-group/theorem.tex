Let $\Phi$ be a field of characteristic $\neq 2$ and $f(x) \in \Phi[x]$ a polynomial without multiple roots. Let $P/\Phi$ be a splitting field with roots $\rho_1, \dots, \rho_m$, and $G_f \subseteq S_m$ the Galois group. Set

$$\Delta = \prod_{i < j} (\rho_i - \rho_j).$$

Then the subfield of invariants of $G_f \cap A_m$ is $\Phi(\Delta)$. Consequently, $G_f \subseteq A_m$ if and only if $\Delta \in \Phi$, i.e., the discriminant $\delta = \Delta^2$ is a square in $\Phi$.
