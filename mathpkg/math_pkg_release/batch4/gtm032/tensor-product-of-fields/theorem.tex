Let $E/\Phi$ and $F/\Phi$ be extension fields of a field $\Phi$. The tensor product $E \otimes_\Phi F$ is an algebra over $\Phi$. It is a field if $E$ and $F$ are linearly disjoint over $\Phi$.

More generally, if $P$ is an extension field of $\Phi$ and $M$ is a vector space over $\Phi$, then $P \otimes_\Phi M$ can be considered as a vector space over $P$ via $\rho(\sum \rho_i \otimes x_i) = \sum \rho\rho_i \otimes x_i$. This space, denoted $M_P$, is obtained from $M$ by extending the base field to $P$. If $(e_\alpha)$ is a basis for $M/\Phi$, then $(1 \otimes e_\alpha)$ is a basis for $M_P/P$.
