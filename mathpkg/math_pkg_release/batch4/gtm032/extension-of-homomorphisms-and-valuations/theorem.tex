Extension Theorem for Valuations: Let $K$ be a field with valuation $v$, $L/K$ an algebraic extension. Then $v$ extends to a valuation $w$ on $L$. If $L/K$ is finite, there are only finitely many extensions $w_1, \ldots, w_g$ (with $g \leq [L:K]$), and the completion $\hat{K}$ embeds into each $\hat{L}_i$. In the complete case, the extension is unique.
