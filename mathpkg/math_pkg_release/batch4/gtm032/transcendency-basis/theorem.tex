Let $P/\Phi$ be a field extension. A transcendence basis of $P/\Phi$ is a subset $B \subseteq P$ algebraically independent over $\Phi$ such that $P$ is algebraic over $\Phi(B)$. Any two transcendence bases have the same cardinality, called the transcendence degree $\text{tr.deg}(P/\Phi)$. For a finitely generated extension, $\text{tr.deg}(P/\Phi) < \infty$.
