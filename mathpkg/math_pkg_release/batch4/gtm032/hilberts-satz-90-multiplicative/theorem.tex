Let $P/\Phi$ be a finite dimensional cyclic extension field and let $g$ be a generator of the Galois group of $P$ over $\Phi$. Then any element $\mu \in P$ such that $N_{P|\Phi}(\mu) = 1$ has the form $\mu = \gamma(\gamma^g)^{-1}$ for some $\gamma \in P^*$, where $N_{P|\Phi}$ denotes the norm from $P$ to $\Phi$.
