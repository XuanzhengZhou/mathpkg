Let $E_1/\Phi$ and $E_2/\Phi$ be subfields of a common extension $\Omega/\Phi$. The composite $E_1 E_2$ is the subfield of $\Omega$ generated by $E_1$ and $E_2$. If $E_1$ and $E_2$ are finite dimensional over $\Phi$, then $[E_1 E_2 : \Phi] \leq [E_1 : \Phi][E_2 : \Phi]$, with equality if and only if $E_1$ and $E_2$ are linearly disjoint over $\Phi$.
