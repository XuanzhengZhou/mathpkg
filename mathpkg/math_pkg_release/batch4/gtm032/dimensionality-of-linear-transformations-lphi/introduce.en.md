For a finite extension $E/\Phi$, the space $\mathfrak{L}_\Phi(E, P)$ of $\Phi$-linear transformations from $E$ into an extension field $P$, viewed as a right $P$-vector space, has dimension equal to $[E:\Phi]$. The proof constructs a basis from the dual basis of $E/\Phi$, showing that the projections $E_i$ form a $P$-basis. This result is fundamental for the Galois theory developed in subsequent sections.
