Hilbert's Theorem 90 in multiplicative form: Solutions to Noether's equations $\mu_{st} = \mu_s^t \mu_t$ are precisely the "coboundaries" $\mu_s = \gamma(\gamma^s)^{-1}$. The proof uses Dedekind independence to find $\beta$ with $\sum \beta^s \mu_s \neq 0$, then sets $\gamma$ to this sum. The classical formulation for cyclic extensions states that norm-1 elements are quotients of conjugates.
