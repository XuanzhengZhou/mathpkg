This corollary follows by duality from Corollary 10.73 (simultaneous universal instantiation) using the relationship between universal and existential quantifiers (10.68: $\vdash \varphi \rightarrow \exists\alpha\varphi$). By representing simultaneous substitution as iterated ordinary substitution as described after 10.74, one applies existential generalization repeatedly to obtain the desired result. $\square$
