A formula $\psi$ is in prenex normal form provided there is an $m \in \omega$, a $Q \in {}^m\{\forall, \exists\}$, an $\alpha \in {}^m\text{Rng } v$, and a quantifier-free formula $\varphi$ such that

$$\psi = Q_0\alpha_0 \cdots Q_{m-1}\alpha_{m-1}\varphi.$$

The formula $\varphi$ is called the matrix of $\psi$; $Q_0\alpha_0 \cdots Q_{m-1}\alpha_{m-1}$ is the prefix of $\psi$.
