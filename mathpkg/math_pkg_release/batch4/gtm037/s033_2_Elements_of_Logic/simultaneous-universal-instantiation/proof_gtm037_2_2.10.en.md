This corollary is an immediate consequence of earlier results (specifically, universal specification, Theorem 10.61), upon noticing that simultaneous substitution can be obtained by iterated ordinary substitution. In the notation of 10.72,

$$\varphi(\sigma_0, \dots, \sigma_{m-1}) = \text{Subf}_{\sigma_0}^{v(k+n+1)} \text{Subf}_{\sigma_0}^{v(k+n+2)} \cdots \text{Subf}_{\sigma_0}^{v(k+n+m)} \psi,$$

where $\psi$ is a suitable variant of $\varphi$ formed to avoid variable capture. Applying universal specification repeatedly yields the desired result. $\square$
