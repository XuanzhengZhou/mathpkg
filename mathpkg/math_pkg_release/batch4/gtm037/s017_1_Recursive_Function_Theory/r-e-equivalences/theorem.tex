For $A \subseteq \omega$ the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $A = \emptyset$ or $A$ is the range of an elementary function;
\item[(ii)] $A = \emptyset$ or $A$ is the range of a primitive recursive function;
\item[(iii)] $A$ is recursively enumerable;
\item[(iv)] $A$ is the range of a partial recursive function;
\item[(v)] $A$ is the domain of a partial recursive function;
\item[(vi)] $A \in \Sigma_1$.
\end{enumerate}
