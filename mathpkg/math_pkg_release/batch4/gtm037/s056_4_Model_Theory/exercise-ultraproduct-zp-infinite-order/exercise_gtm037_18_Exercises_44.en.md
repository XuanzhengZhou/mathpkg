Let $P$ be the set of all positive prime numbers, and let $F$ be a nonprincipal ultrafilter over $P$. For each $p \in P$, let $\mathfrak{A}_p$ be the group $\mathbb{Z}_p$ of residues of integers mod $p$. Show that $\prod_{p \in P} \mathfrak{A}_p / \bar{F}$ is a group all elements of which have infinite order.
