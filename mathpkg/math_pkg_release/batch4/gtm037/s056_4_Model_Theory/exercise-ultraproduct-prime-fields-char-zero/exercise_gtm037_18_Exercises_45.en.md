Let $P$ and $F$ be as in Exercise 18.44. For each $p \in P$, let $\mathfrak{B}_p$ be the prime field of characteristic $p$. Show that $\prod_{p \in P} \mathfrak{B}_p / \bar{F}$ is a field of characteristic $0$.
