This proposition is the key technical lemma underlying an alternative proof of the downward Löwenheim–Skolem theorem (Theorem 19.17) using Skolem functions. It states that for a Skolem expansion $L'$ of $L$, if $B$ models the Skolem set of $L'$ over $L$, then the purely set-theoretic substructure relation $A \subseteq B$ is equivalent to the logical relation of elementary substructure $A \preceq B$ for any $L'$-structure $A$. The proof uses the Tarski–Vaught criterion (Theorem 19.16) together with the Skolem term witnessing property.
