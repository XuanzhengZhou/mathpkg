Let $R$ be a Hanf system. Then there is a cardinal $m$ such that for all $\Gamma \in \mathrm{pr}_0^* R$, condition (i) implies condition (ii):

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] there exist $\mathfrak{A}$, $n$ with $(\Gamma, \mathfrak{A}, n) \in R$ and $n \geq m$;
\item[(ii)] for every cardinal $p$ there is an $n \geq p$ and an $\mathfrak{A}$ such that $(\Gamma, \mathfrak{A}, n) \in R$.
\end{enumerate}
