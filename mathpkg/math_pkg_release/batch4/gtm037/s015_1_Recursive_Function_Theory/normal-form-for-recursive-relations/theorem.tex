Let $m > 1$. If $R$ is an $m$-ary recursive relation, then there exist $e, e' \in \omega$ such that for all $x_0, \dots, x_{m-2} \in \omega$,

\begin{align*}
\text{(i)}\quad &\exists y \, R(x_0, \dots, x_{m-2}, y) \iff \exists y \, (e, x_0, \dots, x_{m-2}, y) \in T_{m-1},\\
\text{(ii)}\quad &\forall y \, R(x_0, \dots, x_{m-2}, y) \iff \forall y \, (e', x_0, \dots, x_{m-2}, y) \in T_{m-1}.
\end{align*}
