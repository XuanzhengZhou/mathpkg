Assume the notation of generalized products (Definition 23.1). Then for each formula $\chi$ of $\mathcal{L}_{\text{prod}}$ there is a partitioning $\mathcal{L}_{\text{fac}}$, $\mathcal{L}_{\text{ind}}$-sequence $\xi = (\varphi, \psi_0, \ldots, \psi_n)$ with the following properties (for any system $\langle \mathfrak{A}_i : i \in I \rangle$ of $\mathcal{L}_{\text{fac}}$-structures and any $\mathcal{L}_{\text{ind}}$-structure $\mathfrak{B}$):

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $v_i$ is free in $\chi$ iff it is free in some $\psi_j$;
\item[(ii)] $\xi$ is effectively obtained from $\chi$;
\item[(iii)] $\prod_{i \in I} \mathfrak{A}_i \models \chi[f]$ iff $\mathfrak{B} \models \varphi[K_{\psi_0}^2 f, \ldots, K_{\psi_m}^2 f]$.
\end{enumerate}
