$\varphi_{\text{At}}$ is the formula $\neg(v_0 = 0) \land \forall v_1(v_1 \cdot v_0 = v_1 \rightarrow v_1 = 0 \lor v_1 = v_0)$ in the language $\mathcal{L}_{\text{BA}}$ of Boolean algebras. Its only free variable is $v_0$, and for any Boolean algebra $\mathfrak{A}$, the set $\varphi_{\text{At}}^{\mathfrak{A}}$ is precisely the set of all atoms of $\mathfrak{A}$. An atom is a minimal nonzero element: the only elements below it are $0$ and itself.
