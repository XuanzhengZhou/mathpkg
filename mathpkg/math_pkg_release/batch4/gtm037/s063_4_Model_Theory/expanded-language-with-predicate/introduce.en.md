$\mathcal{L}_{\text{At}}$ is the first-order language obtained by expanding the language $\mathcal{L}_{\text{BA}}$ of Boolean algebras with a new unary relation symbol $P$. This expansion allows one to express properties about atoms of a Boolean algebra within a richer language, and is instrumental in model-theoretic constructions involving atomic Boolean algebras and their substructures.
