**Lemma 30.7.** Let $G$ be a frame for $\mathfrak{A}$, let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an $\mathcal{L}$-structure, and let $x$ be an assignment over $\mathfrak{A}$ (i.e., $xP_i^\sigma \in G^\sigma$ for each type $\sigma \neq 0$, and $xv_i^0 \in A$ for each $i \in \omega$). Then

$$(\mathfrak{A}, G) \models \varphi[x] \quad \text{iff} \quad \mathfrak{A}_G^* \models \varphi^*[x].$$
