This theorem establishes that weak second-order logic can be embedded into $\omega$-logic. A class is $\omega$-projective if it can be expressed as the class of reducts of $\omega$-models of a first-order theory in an $\omega$-expansion. The proof translates weak second-order quantifiers over finite sets into first-order quantifiers over codes for finite sets.
