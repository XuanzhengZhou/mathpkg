Fuhrken's theorem extends Łoś's ultraproduct theorem to languages with the cardinality quantifier $\mathbf{Q}$ (interpreted as "there exist at least $m$ elements such that"). The proof goes by induction on formulas, with the crucial case for $\mathbf{Q}v_j\varphi$ requiring the condition $|I| < \pi m$ to ensure that a union of fewer than $m$ sets each of size less than $m$ still has size less than $m$.
