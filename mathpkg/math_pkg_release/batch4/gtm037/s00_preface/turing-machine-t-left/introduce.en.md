$T_{\text{left}}$ is a simple two-state Turing machine that serves as the dual of $T_{\text{right}}$. Its sole function is to move the tape one square to the left and then halt, regardless of what symbol is read from the current square. Like $T_{\text{right}}$, this machine illustrates the basic mechanics of state transitions and tape movement. Together, $T_{\text{right}}$ and $T_{\text{left}}$ provide the elementary building blocks for controlling tape head movement in more complex Turing machine constructions.
