This proposition verifies that the relation $\equiv_\Gamma$ is indeed a congruence on the free algebra $\mathfrak{Fr}_\mathcal{L}$. This is essential because only congruence relations allow the formation of well-defined quotient algebras in universal algebra. The proof follows from the fact that logical consequence respects substitution of terms.
