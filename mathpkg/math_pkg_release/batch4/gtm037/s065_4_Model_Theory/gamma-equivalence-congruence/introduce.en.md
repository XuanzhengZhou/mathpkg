Definition 24.14 introduces the equivalence relation $\equiv_\Gamma$ on the term algebra, where two terms are equivalent if the equation $\sigma = \tau$ is a semantic consequence of $\Gamma$. This relation is fundamental in universal algebra and equational logic, as it provides the bridge between proof-theoretic and model-theoretic approaches to equations.
