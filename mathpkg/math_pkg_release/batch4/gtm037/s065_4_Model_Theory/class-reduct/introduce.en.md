This definition extends the notation of reduct from individual structures to classes of structures. If $\mathbf{K}$ is a class of structures in an expanded language $\mathcal{L}'$, then $\mathbf{K} \upharpoonright \mathcal{L}$ is the class obtained by forgetting the extra non-logical symbols present in $\mathcal{L}'$ but not in $\mathcal{L}$.
