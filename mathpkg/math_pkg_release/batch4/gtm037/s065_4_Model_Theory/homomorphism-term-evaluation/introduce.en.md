This proposition establishes the natural relationship between homomorphisms from the free algebra and term evaluation in the target structure. It states that applying the homomorphism $g$ to a term $\sigma$ yields the same result as evaluating $\sigma$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ under the assignment $x$ determined by the images of the generators.
