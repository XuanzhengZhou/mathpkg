Proposition 25.3 states a basic inclusion between class operators: the class of ultraproducts of substructures is contained in the class of substructures of ultraproducts. This algebraic fact about the interaction of $\mathbf{Up}$ and $\mathbf{S}$ is useful in preservation theory.
