Definition 24.17 introduces a purely equational deduction system $\Gamma\text{-eqthm}$: equations derivable from $\Gamma$ using only reflexivity, substitution, and transitivity (plus replacement under operation symbols). This system avoids all logical axioms of first-order logic—no quantifiers, no sentential connectives—making it a calculus of pure equational reasoning central to universal algebra.
