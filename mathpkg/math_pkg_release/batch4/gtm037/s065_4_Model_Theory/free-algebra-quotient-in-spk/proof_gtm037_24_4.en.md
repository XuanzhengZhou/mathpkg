Let $I = {}^2\text{Trm} \sim \{(\sigma, \tau) : \sigma = \tau \in \Gamma\}$. For each $(\sigma, \tau) \in I$ there is a structure $\mathfrak{A}_{\sigma\tau} \in K$ such that $\sigma = \tau$ fails to hold in $\mathfrak{A}_{\sigma\tau}$; hence there is an assignment witnessing the failure. By the axiom of choice, select appropriate witnesses. For each $a \in A$ define a function $g_a$ that projects onto the appropriate coordinate. The map sending $a$ to the equivalence class $[g_a]$ in the reduced product gives the desired embedding into $\mathbf{SPK}$.
