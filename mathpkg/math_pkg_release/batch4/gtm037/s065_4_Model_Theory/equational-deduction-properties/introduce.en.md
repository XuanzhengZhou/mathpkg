Proposition 24.18 establishes that the equational deduction relation $\Gamma \vdash_{\text{eq}}$ satisfies the fundamental properties expected of an equality relation: reflexivity, symmetry, transitivity, and congruence (compatibility with operation symbols). These follow from the closure conditions in the definition of $\Gamma\text{-eqthm}$.
