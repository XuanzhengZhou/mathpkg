Definition 25.4 extends the notion of reduct from individual structures to classes: if $\mathbf{K}$ is a class of structures in an expanded language, $\mathbf{K} \upharpoonright \mathcal{L}$ is the class of reducts to the smaller language. This operation is essential for relating classes across language expansions, particularly in the study of projective classes.
