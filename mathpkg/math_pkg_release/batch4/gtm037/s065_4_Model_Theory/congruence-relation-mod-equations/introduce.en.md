The congruence relation $\equiv_\Gamma$ is defined on the term algebra of an algebraic language $\mathcal{L}$ relative to a set of equations $\Gamma$. Two terms are equivalent under $\equiv_\Gamma$ precisely when the equation $\sigma = \tau$ is a semantic consequence of $\Gamma$. This relation is fundamental to the study of equational logic and varieties, as it enables the construction of quotient algebras that serve as free models.
