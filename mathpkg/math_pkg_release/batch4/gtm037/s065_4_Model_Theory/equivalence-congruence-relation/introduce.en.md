Proposition 24.15 verifies that $\equiv_\Gamma$ is a congruence relation on the term algebra $\mathfrak{Fr}_\mathcal{L}$, meaning it respects the algebraic operations. This is essential for forming the quotient algebra $\mathfrak{Fr}_\mathcal{L} / \equiv_\Gamma$, which plays a key role in Birkhoff's variety theorem.
