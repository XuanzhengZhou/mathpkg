This lemma provides an equivalent characterization of the notion of a tautology for first-order formulas. Instead of quantifying over all possible truth assignments to the atomic subformulas (the standard definition), condition (ii) states that any valuation $f$ on the set of subformulas $S$ of $\varphi$ that respects the standard truth-table semantics of the connectives $\neg$, $\vee$, and $\wedge$ must assign value $1$ to $\varphi$. This simplified criterion is useful for proving results about the recursiveness of the set of tautologies via Godel numbering.
