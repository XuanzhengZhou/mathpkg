Given a subset $X$ of a cylindric algebra, the ideal generated by $X$ is defined as the intersection of all ideals containing $X$. This is the smallest ideal that includes $X$ and provides the natural way to "close" a set of elements under the ideal operations, analogous to the ideal generated by a subset of a Boolean algebra.
