The proof of Proposition 12.21 is indicated by the original text with a "Proof" marker, but the proof body was not captured in the OCR extraction of this section. The statement generalizes Theorem 9.23 from Boolean algebras to cylindric algebras. The proof would proceed by showing: (1) every element of the described form belongs to every ideal containing $X$, hence to the generated ideal; and (2) the set of all such elements forms an ideal containing $X$, hence contains the generated ideal. The cylindrification condition uses the property that $c_\kappa$ is additive and monotone.
