If $X \subseteq A$, where $\mathfrak{A}$ is a $\mathrm{CA}_{\alpha}$, then the ideal generated by $X$ is the collection of all $y \in A$ such that there exist $m, n \in \omega$ and $x \in {}^{m}X$, $\kappa \in {}^{n}\alpha$ with
$$y \leq c_{\kappa_0} \cdots c_{\kappa_{n-1}}(x_0 + \cdots + x_{m-1}).$$
