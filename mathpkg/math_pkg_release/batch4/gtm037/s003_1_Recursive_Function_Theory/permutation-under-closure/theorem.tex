Let $\mathcal{A}$ be closed under elementary recursive operations. If $f$ is $m$-ary and $f\in\mathcal{A}$, and $\pi$ is a permutation of $\{0,\ldots,m-1\}$, then the $m$-ary function $g$ defined by
$$g(x_0,\ldots,x_{m-1}) = f(x_{\pi 0},\ldots,x_{\pi(m-1)})$$
for all $x_0,\ldots,x_{m-1}\in\omega$ is also in $\mathcal{A}$.
