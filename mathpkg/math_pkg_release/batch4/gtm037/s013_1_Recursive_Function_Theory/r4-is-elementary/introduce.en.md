The relation $R_4$ consists of pairs $(m,n)$ where $m$ is the Gödel number of a Markov algorithm and $n$ is the Gödel number of a computation under that algorithm. Lemma 4.16 asserts that $R_4$ is an elementary relation, meaning its membership can be decided by a formula whose quantifiers are bounded.
