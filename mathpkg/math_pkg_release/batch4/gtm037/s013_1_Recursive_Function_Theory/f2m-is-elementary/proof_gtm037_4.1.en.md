The function $f_2^1$ is defined as $\text{Cat}(2, f_1 x)$, and $f_2^{m+1}$ is defined recursively as $\text{Cat}(f_2^m(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-1}), f_2^1 x_m)$. Since $\text{Cat}$ and $f_1$ are elementary, and the class of elementary functions is closed under composition, $f_2^1$ is elementary. By induction on $m$, each $f_2^m$ is elementary.
