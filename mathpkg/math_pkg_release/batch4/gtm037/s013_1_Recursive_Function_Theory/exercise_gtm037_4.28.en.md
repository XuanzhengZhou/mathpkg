Let $A$ be the algorithm

$$\begin{align*}
2 &\rightarrow 0 &\rightarrow 2 \\
2 &\rightarrow 1 &\rightarrow 2 \\
2 &\rightarrow \cdot &1^{(3)} \\
&\rightarrow 2
\end{align*}$$

Show that $A$ converts any word $a$ on $0, 1$ (i.e., involving only $0$ and $1$) into $a 1^{(3)}$.
