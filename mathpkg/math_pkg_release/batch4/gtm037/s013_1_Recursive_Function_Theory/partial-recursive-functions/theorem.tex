\textbf{(i) Composition.} We extend the operator $\text{K}_n^m$ of 2.1 to act upon partial functions. Let $f$ be an $m$-ary partial function, and $g_0, \ldots, g_{m-1}$ $n$-ary partial functions. Then $\text{K}_n^m(f, g_0, \ldots, g_{m-1})$ is the $n$-ary partial function $h$ such that for any $x_0, \ldots, x_{n-1} \in \omega,$

$$h(x_0, \ldots, x_{n-1}) \simeq f(g_0(x_0, \ldots, x_{n-1}), \ldots, g_{m-1}(x_0, \ldots, x_{n-1})).$$
