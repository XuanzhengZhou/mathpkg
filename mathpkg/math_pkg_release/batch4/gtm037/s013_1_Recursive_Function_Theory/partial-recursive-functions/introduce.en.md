Definition 5.3 extends the notion of recursive functions to partial functions. The first clause generalizes the composition operator $\text{K}_n^m$: the composed function $h$ is defined at an $n$-tuple precisely when all the component functions $g_0, \ldots, g_{m-1}$ are defined at that tuple and $f$ is defined at the resulting $m$-tuple of values. The symbol $\simeq$ (Kleene equality) is used to express that both sides are defined exactly under the same conditions and are equal when defined.
