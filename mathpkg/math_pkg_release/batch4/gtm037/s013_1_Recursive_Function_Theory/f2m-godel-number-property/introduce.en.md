Lemma 4.21 provides a concrete interpretation of the function $f_2^m$: its value on the arguments $x_0, \ldots, x_{m-1}$ is the Gödel number of the word consisting of alternating blocks of a single $0$ followed by $x_i+1$ copies of $1$, for each $i$ from $0$ to $m-1$. This encoding is fundamental to the arithmetization of Markov algorithms.
