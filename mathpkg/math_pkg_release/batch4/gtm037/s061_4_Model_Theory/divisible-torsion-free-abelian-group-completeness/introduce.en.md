The theory of nontrivial divisible torsion-free Abelian groups is complete and decidable. It is well known that for any uncountable cardinal $m > \aleph_0$, any two divisible torsion-free Abelian groups of power $m$ are isomorphic, establishing $m$-categoricity for all uncountable powers. Applying the Łoś–Vaught test yields completeness, and since the axioms are recursively enumerable, the theory is also decidable.
