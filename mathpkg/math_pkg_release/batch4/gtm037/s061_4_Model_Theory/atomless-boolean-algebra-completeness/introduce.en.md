The theory of nontrivial atomless Boolean algebras is complete and decidable. By Theorem 9.48, any two denumerable atomless Boolean algebras are isomorphic, which establishes $\aleph_0$-categoricity. The Łoś–Vaught test then yields completeness, and since the theory is recursively axiomatizable, decidability follows from completeness.
