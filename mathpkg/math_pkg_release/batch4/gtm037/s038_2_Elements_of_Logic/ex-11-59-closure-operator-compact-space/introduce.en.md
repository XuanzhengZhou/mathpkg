Exercise 11.59 defines a closure operator $C$ on subsets of $K$ (a class of $L$-structures) where $CL$ consists of those structures in $K$ that satisfy every sentence true in all members of $L$. The exercise asks to show that with respect to this closure operator, $K$ forms a compact topological space. This is closely related to the compactness theorem of first-order logic and the Stone representation theorem for Boolean algebras.
