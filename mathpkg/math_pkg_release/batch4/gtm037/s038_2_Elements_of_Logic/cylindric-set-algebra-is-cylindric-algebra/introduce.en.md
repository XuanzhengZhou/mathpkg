This corollary confirms that the concrete cylindric set algebras, defined from subsets of Cartesian powers ${}^\alpha U$, satisfy all seven axioms (C1)-(C7) of the abstract notion of a cylindric algebra. In other words, the class of cylindric set algebras is contained in the class of all cylindric algebras of dimension $\alpha$. The verification is a routine check that the natural set-theoretic operations of union, intersection, complement, cylindrification $C_\kappa$, and the diagonal sets $D_{\kappa\lambda}$ obey the algebraic laws required by Definition 12.2.
