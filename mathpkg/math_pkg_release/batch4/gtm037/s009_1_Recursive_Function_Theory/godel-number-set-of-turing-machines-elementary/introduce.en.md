This lemma shows that the set of G\"{o}del numbers of Turing machines, $g^*T$, is an elementary set. The proof provides an explicit elementary characterization: a number $x$ belongs to $g^*T$ exactly when its prime factorization satisfies several arithmetic conditions involving length, state consistency, and the relationship between even- and odd-indexed components of the encoding.
