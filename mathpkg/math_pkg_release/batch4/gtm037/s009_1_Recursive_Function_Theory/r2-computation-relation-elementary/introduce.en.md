This lemma establishes that the computation relation $R_2$, which encodes complete Turing machine computations as sequences of configurations, is an elementary set. The proof provides an explicit characterization in terms of the constituent configurations belonging to $g^*\mathbb{C}$, consistency of initial and final states, and the single-step transition relation $R_1$ between consecutive configurations.
