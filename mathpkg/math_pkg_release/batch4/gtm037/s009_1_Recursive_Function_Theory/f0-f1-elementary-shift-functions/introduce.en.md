This lemma introduces two elementary functions $f_0$ and $f_1$ that shift natural numbers in a way that corresponds to incrementing and decrementing integers under the standard bijection $g: \mathbb{Z} \to \omega$. Concretely, $f_0$ and $f_1$ act on the G\"{o}del numbers of integers so that $f_0 g e = g(e+1)$ and $f_1 g e = g(e-1)$. These shift functions are fundamental building blocks for arithmetizing Turing machine operations.
