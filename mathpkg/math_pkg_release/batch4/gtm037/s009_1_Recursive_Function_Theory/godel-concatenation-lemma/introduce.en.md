This lemma establishes that the G\"{o}del numbering of binary sequences respects concatenation: the code of the concatenation $hk$ of two binary sequences equals $\operatorname{Cat}(gh, gk)$, the arithmetic concatenation of their individual codes. This is a fundamental property ensuring that the G\"{o}del numbering provides a faithful embedding of the monoid of binary sequences under concatenation into the natural numbers under the Cat operation.
