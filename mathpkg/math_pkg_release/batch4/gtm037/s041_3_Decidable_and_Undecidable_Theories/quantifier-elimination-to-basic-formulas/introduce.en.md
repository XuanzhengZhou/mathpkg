Lemma 13.8 provides the essential quantifier elimination step for the theory $\Gamma_2$ of Presburger arithmetic. It states that every formula $\varphi$ can be effectively transformed into a formula $\psi$ that is a Boolean combination of basic formulas (equalities, strict inequalities, and congruences) and is equivalent to $\varphi$ under $\Gamma_2$, with the free variables of $\psi$ contained in those of $\varphi$. This lemma is the technical core of the decidability proof for $\Gamma_2$ and exemplifies the quantifier elimination method applied to theories of moderate complexity.
