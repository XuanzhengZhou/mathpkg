This lemma states that any formula implies its own double negation. Together with the converse direction $\neg \neg \varphi \to \varphi$ (the law of double negation elimination, which follows from axiom schema A3), this establishes the equivalence of $\varphi$ and $\neg \neg \varphi$. The proof uses the deduction theorem and the principle of explosion (Lemma 8.14).
