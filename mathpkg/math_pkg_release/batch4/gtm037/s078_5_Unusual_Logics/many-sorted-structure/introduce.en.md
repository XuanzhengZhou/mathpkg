This definition specifies the semantics of many-sorted logic. Each sort $s$ is interpreted as a nonempty set $A_s$, operations are functions between the appropriate product of sorts, and relations are subsets of the appropriate product. The satisfaction relation is then defined in the natural way, with quantifiers ranging over the domain of the appropriate sort.
