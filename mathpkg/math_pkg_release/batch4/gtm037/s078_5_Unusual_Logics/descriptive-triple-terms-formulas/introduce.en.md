Definition 29.18 introduces the syntax of the description operator $\tau$ in first-order logic. A descriptive triple $(L, \tau, O)$ enriches a first-order language with a new term-forming operator $\tau$ and a distinguished constant $O$. The term $\tau v_i \varphi$ is read as "the unique $v_i$ such that $\varphi$, or $O$ if no such $v_i$ exists or several exist."
