Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a relational first-order language with equality, $\varphi \in \text{Sent}_{\mathcal{L}}$, and $\Gamma$ a set of $\mathcal{L}$-sentences such that equality does not appear in any sentence of $\Gamma$. Then
$$\Gamma \models_{\mathcal{L}} \varphi \quad \text{iff} \quad \Gamma \models_{\mathcal{L}} \bigwedge E\{\varphi\} \rightarrow \varphi,$$
where $E\{\varphi\}$ is the set of equality axioms relativized to the symbols of $\varphi$.
