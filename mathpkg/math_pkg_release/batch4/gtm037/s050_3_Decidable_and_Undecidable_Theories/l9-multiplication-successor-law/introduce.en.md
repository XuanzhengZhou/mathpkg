Lemma 16.47 establishes that the language $\mathcal{L}_9$ satisfies the recursive equation $v_0 \cdot \mathbf{s}v_1 = v_0 \cdot v_1 + v_0$, which is the characteristic axiom of multiplication with respect to the successor function in Robinson arithmetic Q. The proof proceeds by case analysis on the defining axiom of the multiplication operation $\cdot$ from Definition 16.45, considering whether $\Omega x$ holds and the possible values of $x \cdot y$. This lemma, together with Lemma 16.46, confirms that $\mathcal{L}_9$ validates two of the essential axioms of Q.
