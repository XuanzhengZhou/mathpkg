From Theorem 28.7 and Theorem 27.12 we obtain this corollary. Since $\Gamma$ is $\aleph_0$-categorical with only infinite models and the language is countable, the hypotheses of Theorem 28.7 are satisfied (one can verify that there are only countably many $1$-types over $\Gamma$, as $\aleph_0$-categoricity implies that all countable models are saturated). A more direct argument can be given which also generalizes to Corollary 28.9. In fact, all countable models of $\Gamma$ are $\aleph_0$-saturated in this case.
