Let $\varphi$ be a formula. Then there is a unique $m \in \omega$ and $\alpha \in {}^m\text{Rng}\;v$ such that $\forall\alpha_0\cdots\forall\alpha_{m-1}\varphi$ is a sentence (the \textit{universal closure} of $\varphi$), and such that every variable occurring free in $\varphi$ appears among $\alpha_0,\ldots,\alpha_{m-1}$.
