Definition 10.94 establishes the standard method for forming a sentence from an arbitrary formula by taking its universal closure. For any formula $\varphi$, there is a unique (up to ordering) tuple of variables such that prefixing universal quantifiers over those variables yields a sentence. Since one is mainly interested in sentences as opposed to formulas in general, this construction provides a canonical way to associate a sentence with each formula.
