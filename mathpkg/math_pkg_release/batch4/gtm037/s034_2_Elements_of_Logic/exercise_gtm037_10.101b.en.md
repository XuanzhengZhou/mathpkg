Let $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$ be distinct variables. Prove:

$$\vdash \exists \alpha [\varphi \land \psi \land \exists \beta (\varphi \land \neg \psi)] \rightarrow \exists \beta (\exists \alpha \varphi \land \neg \alpha = \beta).$$
