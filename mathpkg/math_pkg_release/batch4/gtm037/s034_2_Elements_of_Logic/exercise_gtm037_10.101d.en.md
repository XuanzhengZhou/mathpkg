Let $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$ be distinct variables. Prove:

$$\vdash \exists \alpha \varphi \land \exists \beta \psi \land \exists \gamma \chi \rightarrow \exists \alpha \exists \beta \exists \gamma [\exists \alpha (\exists \beta \chi \land \exists \gamma \psi) \land \exists \beta (\exists \gamma \varphi \land \exists \alpha \chi) \land \exists \gamma (\exists \alpha \psi \land \exists \beta \varphi)].$$
