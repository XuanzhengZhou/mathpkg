Let $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$ be distinct variables. Prove:

$$\vdash \exists \gamma \{ \exists \alpha [\exists \gamma (\exists \alpha \varphi \land \exists \beta \psi) \land \alpha = \gamma] \land \exists \beta \chi \} \leftrightarrow \exists \gamma [\exists \beta (\exists \gamma (\exists \beta \psi \land \beta = \gamma) \land \alpha = \gamma) \land \exists \beta \chi \land \beta = \gamma) \land \exists \alpha \varphi].$$
