By Theorem 6.5, $K$ is r.e. Now $U_0^1$ is a productive function for $\omega \sim K$. For if $e \in \omega$ and $\mathrm{Dmn}\,\varphi_e^1 \subseteq \omega \sim K$, then $e \in (\omega \sim K) \sim \mathrm{Dmn}\,\varphi_e^1$; for

$$e \in K \Rightarrow e \in \mathrm{Dmn}\,\varphi_e^1 \quad \text{by definition of } K, \text{ 6.4}$$
$$\Rightarrow e \notin \omega \sim K \quad \text{by assumption } \mathrm{Dmn}\,\varphi_e^1 \subseteq \omega \sim K$$

Thus $e \notin K$, so $e \in \omega \sim K$, and hence by definition of $K$, $e \notin \mathrm{Dmn}\,\varphi_e^1$. Therefore $U_0^1$ witnesses that $\omega \sim K$ is productive, establishing that $K$ is creative.
