This definition introduces four central notions in the classification of classes of structures. The theory $\Theta_{\rho}K$ collects all sentences true in every member of $K$. A class is elementarily closed if it is closed under elementary equivalence. Projective (or pseudo-elementary) classes are reducts of elementary classes. Compact classes satisfy the compactness property relativized to the class. These notions form a hierarchy studied in abstract model theory.
