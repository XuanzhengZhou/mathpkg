Give an example of a system $\langle \mathfrak{A}_i : i \in \omega \rangle$ of structures such that $\mathfrak{A}_i \subseteq \mathfrak{A}_{i+1}$ for all $i \in \omega$, $\mathfrak{A}_i \equiv \mathfrak{A}_{i+1}$ for all $i$, but not
$$(\mathfrak{A}_0 \equiv \bigcup_{i \in \omega} \mathfrak{A}_i).$$
