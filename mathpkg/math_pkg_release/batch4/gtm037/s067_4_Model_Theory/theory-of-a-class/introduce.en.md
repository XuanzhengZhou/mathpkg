The theory $\Theta_{\rho}K$ of a class $K$ of $\mathcal{L}$-structures is the set of all $\mathcal{L}$-sentences that hold in every member of $K$. This is the natural generalization of the theory of a single structure. When $K$ is an elementary class, $\Theta_{\rho}K$ provides an axiomatization of $K$.
