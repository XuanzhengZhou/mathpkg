For any formula $\varphi$ of second-order logic there is a formula $\psi$ in the following very particular prenex normal form such that $\models \varphi \leftrightarrow \psi$:
$$\psi = Q_0 \alpha_0 \cdots Q_{m-1} \alpha_{m-1} \forall \beta_0 \cdots \forall \beta_{n-1} \exists \gamma_0 \cdots \exists \gamma_{p-1} \chi,$$
where the $\alpha$'s are all relation variables, the $Q$'s are quantifiers $\forall$ or $\exists$, the $\beta$'s and $\gamma$'s are all individual variables, and $\chi$ is quantifier-free.
