Show that the following functions and relations are elementary:

(1) $(a, b) = \gcd(\text{greatest common divisor})$ of $a$ and $b$, $= 0$ if $a = 0$ or $b = 0$.

(2) $sa = \sum$ of positive divisors of $a$.

(3) the set of perfect numbers, i.e., numbers $a$ with $sa = 2a$.

(4) the Euler $\varphi$ function: $\varphi a =$ the number of elements of $\{x : 1 \leq x \leq a\}$ with $(x, a) = 1$.
