Let $fn = [e \cdot n]$ = greatest integer $\leq e \cdot n$, for every $n \in \omega$, where $e$ is the base of the natural system of logarithms. Show that $f$ is elementary. Hint: write

$$e = 1 + \frac{1}{1!} + \frac{1}{2!} + \cdots + \frac{1}{n!} + \frac{1}{(n+1)!} + \cdots,$$

$$e \cdot n = \frac{n}{n!} \left( n! + \frac{n!}{1!} + \cdots + \frac{n!}{n!} \right) + \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{n}{(n+k)!}.$$
