Let $\{\mathcal{A}_i\}_{i \in I}$ be a family of $L$-structures indexed by a nonempty set $I$, and let $F$ be an ultrafilter over $I$. Let $\mathcal{C} = \prod_{i \in I} \mathcal{A}_i / F$ be the ultraproduct. Then for every $L$-formula $\varphi(v_0, \ldots, v_{n-1})$ and every assignment $x: \{v_k : k \in \omega\} \to \prod_{i \in I} A_i$,

$$\mathcal{C} \models \varphi[[\ ] \circ x] \quad \text{iff} \quad \{i \in I : \mathcal{A}_i \models \varphi[\operatorname{pr}_i \circ x]\} \in F,$$

where $[\ ] \circ x$ denotes the equivalence class of the assignment $x$ in the reduced product, and $\operatorname{pr}_i$ denotes the projection onto the $i$-th component.
