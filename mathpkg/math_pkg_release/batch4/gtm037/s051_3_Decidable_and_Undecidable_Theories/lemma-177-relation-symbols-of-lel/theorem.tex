Let $\mathbf{O}$ be an $m$-ary operation symbol of $\mathcal{L}_{\text{el}}$. Then $\#R_{\mathbf{O}}$ is an $m$-ary relation on $\omega$ and for any $x_0, \ldots, x_{m-1} \in \omega$:

$$(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-1}) \in \#R_{\mathbf{O}} \text{ implies } \mathcal{P} \models R_{\mathbf{O}}(\Delta x_0, \ldots, \Delta x_{m-1}),$$

$$(x_0, \ldots, x_{m-1}) \notin \#R_{\mathbf{O}} \text{ implies } \mathcal{P} \models \neg R_{\mathbf{O}}(\Delta x_0, \ldots, \Delta x_{m-1}).$$
