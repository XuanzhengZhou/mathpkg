This exercise develops a relativization technique for formulas with respect to a parameter $y \in {}^{\omega}\{0,1\}$ that specifies which variables are interpreted in a substructure identified by an individual constant. The construction shows how to translate truth in a structure $(\omega, \cdot)$ to truth in $(\omega \sim 1, \cdot)$ via the relativized formula $\varphi_y$, establishing a semantic connection between the full natural numbers and the positive natural numbers.
