Given a congruence relation $R$ on an $\mathcal{L}$-structure $\mathfrak{A}$, we can define a uniquely determined $\mathcal{L}$-structure $\mathfrak{A}/R$ whose universe is the set $A/R$ of $R$-equivalence classes and whose operations are given by

$$\mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{A}/R}([a_0], \ldots, [a_{m-1}]) = [\mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{A}}(a_0, \ldots, a_{m-1})];$$

this structure is denoted by $\mathfrak{A}/R$.
