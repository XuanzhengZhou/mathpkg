Let $\mathcal{L}$ be an algebraic language. The \text{absolutely free $\mathcal{L}$-algebra} $\mathfrak{Fr}_\mathcal{L}$ is constructed as follows. Its universe is $\mathrm{Trm}_\mathcal{L}$, the set of all terms of $\mathcal{L}$. For each operation symbol $\mathbf{O}$ of rank $m$, the operation $\mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{Fr}_\mathcal{L}}$ is defined by

$$\mathbf{O}^{\mathfrak{Fr}_\mathcal{L}}(\tau_0, \ldots, \tau_{m-1}) = \mathbf{O}\tau_0 \cdots \tau_{m-1}.$$
