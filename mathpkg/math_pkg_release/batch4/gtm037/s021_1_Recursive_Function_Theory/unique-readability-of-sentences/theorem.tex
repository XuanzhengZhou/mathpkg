Every sentence satisfies exactly one of the following:
(i) it is an atomic sentence $\langle s \rangle$ for a unique $s \in P$;
(ii) it is $\neg \varphi$ for a unique sentence $\varphi$;
(iii) it is $\varphi \rightarrow \psi$ for unique sentences $\varphi$ and $\psi$.
Moreover, no proper initial segment of a sentence is a sentence, and if $\varphi \rightarrow \psi = \theta \rightarrow \sigma$ for sentences $\varphi, \psi, \theta, \sigma$, then $\varphi = \theta$ and $\psi = \sigma$.
