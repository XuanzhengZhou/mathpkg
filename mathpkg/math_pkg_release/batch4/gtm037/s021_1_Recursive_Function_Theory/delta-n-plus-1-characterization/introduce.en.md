This theorem characterizes the $\Delta_{n+1}$ level of the arithmetical hierarchy in terms of relative computability from characteristic functions of $\Pi_n$ or $\Sigma_n$ sets. A relation belongs to $\Delta_{n+1}$ precisely when it is recursive relative to the collection of all $\Pi_n$ (equivalently, $\Sigma_n$) characteristic functions. This clarifies the role of functionals in the arithmetical hierarchy.
