This is the separation theorem for the analytical hierarchy: any two disjoint $\Sigma_1^1$ relations can be separated by a $\Delta_1^1$ relation. This result parallels classical separation theorems in descriptive set theory and demonstrates that $\Delta_1^1$ (the hyperarithmetical sets) has the separation property, while $\Sigma_1^1$ does not.
