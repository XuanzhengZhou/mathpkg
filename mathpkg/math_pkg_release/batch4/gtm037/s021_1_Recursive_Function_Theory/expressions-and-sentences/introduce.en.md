This definition formally constructs the language of sentential logic. Expressions are finite sequences of symbols. The operations $\neg$ (negation) and $\rightarrow$ (implication) are defined by prefixing the negation symbol $n$ or implication symbol $c$ respectively. The set of well-formed sentences is the smallest set containing all atomic sentences (as length-1 sequences) and closed under these operations. This is Polish notation, which avoids the need for parentheses through prefix positioning of connectives.
