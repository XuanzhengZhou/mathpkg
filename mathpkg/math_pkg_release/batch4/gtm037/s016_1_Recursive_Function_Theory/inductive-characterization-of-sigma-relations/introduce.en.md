Proposition 5.25 provides an inductive characterization of the arithmetic hierarchy classes. It establishes that $\Sigma_{m+1}$ relations are precisely those obtained by prefixing an existential quantifier to a $\Pi_m$ relation, and dually $\Pi_{m+1}$ relations are obtained by prefixing a universal quantifier to a $\Sigma_m$ relation. This inductive formulation serves as the foundation for proving closure properties and structural results about the arithmetic hierarchy by induction on $m$.
