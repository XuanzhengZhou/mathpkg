Proposition 5.33 shows that every $\Sigma_m$ or $\Pi_m$ relation is also a $\Delta_{m+1}$ relation, meaning it belongs to both $\Sigma_{m+1}$ and $\Pi_{m+1}$. This establishes that the arithmetic hierarchy is strictly cumulative: each level is contained in all higher levels. As corollaries, one obtains $\Sigma_m \subseteq \Pi_{m+1}$, $\Pi_m \subseteq \Sigma_{m+1}$, and that the sequences $\Sigma_m$ and $\Pi_m$ are each monotonically increasing with $m$.
