Let $\Gamma \subseteq \operatorname{Fmla}_{\mathcal{L}}$. Then for any $\varphi \in \operatorname{Qf}\Gamma$ such that $\neg\varphi$ is not a tautology there exist $p, m \in \omega$ and a function $\psi$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] the domain of $\psi$ is $(p + 1) \times (m + 1)$;
\item[(ii)] for each $i \leq p$ and $j \leq m$, $\psi_{ij} \in \Gamma$ or $\psi_{ij} = \neg\chi$ with $\chi \in \Gamma$;
\item[(iii)] $\models \varphi \leftrightarrow \bigvee_{i \leq p} \bigwedge_{j \leq m} \psi_{ij}$.
\end{enumerate}
