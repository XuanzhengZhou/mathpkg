Proposition 12.28 constructs a homomorphism from the term algebra $\mathfrak{M}_0^{\mathcal{L}}$ of a first-order language with no operation symbols into any $\omega$-dimensional cylindric algebra $\mathfrak{A}$, given a suitable assignment of the relation symbols. The proof proceeds by first defining an auxiliary function $h$ on formulas via induction, with the atomic case involving careful manipulation of cylindric substitutions $s_{\kappa}^{\lambda}$. The construction shows that every logical axiom evaluates to the unit element under $h$, from which the desired homomorphism is obtained by factoring through the Lindenbaum–Tarski equivalence.
