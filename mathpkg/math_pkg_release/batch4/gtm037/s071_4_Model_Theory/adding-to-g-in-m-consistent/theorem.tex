Let $n \geq \aleph_0$, and assume that $n^{\partial} \leq m \geq \aleph_0$. Suppose that $(F, 0, D)$ is $m$-consistent over $n$ and $|F| > m$. Suppose that $G$ is a finite subset of $\bigcup_{\rho < n^{\partial}} {}^n \rho$.

Then there is an $F' \subseteq F$ with $|F'| \leq m$ such that $(F \setminus F', G, D)$ is $m$-consistent over $n$.
