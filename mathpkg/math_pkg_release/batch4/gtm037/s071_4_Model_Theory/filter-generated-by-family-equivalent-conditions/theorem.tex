If $K$ is a nonempty collection of subsets of $I$, then for any $X \subseteq I$ the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $X \in \mathcal{F}_g K$;
\item[(ii)] $\exists m \in \omega \setminus 1 \; \exists Y \in {}^m K \; (Y_0 \cap \cdots \cap Y_{m-1} \subseteq X)$.
\end{itemize}
