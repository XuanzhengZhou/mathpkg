This technical lemma is a key step in the Keisler-Shelah construction. It shows that by removing at most $m$ elements from the large set $F$, one can incorporate a finite set $G$ of new functions while preserving $m$-consistency over $n$. This enables the gradual extension of the filter in the construction.
