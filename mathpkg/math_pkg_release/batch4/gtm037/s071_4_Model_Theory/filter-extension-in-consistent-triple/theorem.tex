Suppose that $(F, G, D)$ is $m$-consistent over $n$ and that $\Gamma_\alpha \subseteq n$ for every $\alpha < m$. Assume that $n^\partial \leq m$. Then there exist $F' \subseteq F$ with $|F \sim F'| \leq m$, and $D' \supseteq D$ such that $(F', G, D')$ is $m$-consistent over $n$ and for every $\alpha < m$, either $\Gamma_\alpha \in D'$ or $n \sim \Gamma_\alpha \in D'$.
