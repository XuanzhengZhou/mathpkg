The occurrence relation $R_0$ is a binary relation on natural numbers that encodes the subword relationship between words via their Gödel numbers. A pair $(m, n)$ belongs to $R_0$ when $m$ and $n$ are Gödel numbers of words $a$ and $b$, and $a$ occurs as a contiguous substring within $b$. This relation provides the foundation for arithmetizing syntactic operations on words, which is essential for formalizing Markov algorithms within first-order arithmetic.
