**Exercise 8.50** (or 8.50-8.53) investigates a variant of sentential logic where Peirce's law $[(\varphi \rightarrow \psi) \rightarrow \varphi] \rightarrow \varphi$ is added as an axiom schema alongside the standard positive implicational axioms. The system defines I-sentences (ISent), IAxm as the set of axioms, and IThm as the deductive closure under detachment. The exercise asks to establish: (a) $\vdash_{\text{I}} \varphi \rightarrow \varphi$, (b) the deduction theorem for $\vdash_{\text{I}}$, (c) transitivity of implication, (d) a completeness property, (e) a derived schema, and (f) a model existence lemma for I-consistent sets. This system is related to intuitionistic sentential logic.
