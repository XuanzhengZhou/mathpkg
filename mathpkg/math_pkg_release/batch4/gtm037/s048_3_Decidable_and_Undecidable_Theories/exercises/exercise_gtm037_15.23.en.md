Let $L$ be a language with unary relation symbols $R_m$, $m \in \omega$, but no other nonlogical constants. Show that $\Gamma = \{\varphi \in \mathrm{Sent}_L : {} \models \varphi\}$ is decidable but has $\exp \aleph_0$ complete undecidable extensions.

Hint: use elimination of quantifiers, as with the pure theory of equality.
