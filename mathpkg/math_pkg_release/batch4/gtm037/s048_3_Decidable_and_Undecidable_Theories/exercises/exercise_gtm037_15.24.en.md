(See the comments following 5.7.) Let $L$ be a language with a single binary relation symbol $R$, and let $\Gamma$ be the theory in $L$ with the axiom $\{\forall v_0 \forall v_1 (v_0 = v_1)\}$. Show that $\Gamma$ is decidable.
