There are theories $\Gamma$, $\Delta$ with $\Gamma \subseteq \Delta$, $\Delta$ inseparable, $\Gamma$ decidable.

Hint: take $L$ and $\Gamma$ as in 15.23. Let $\Delta$ have the following axioms:

$$\{\exists v_0 R_m v_0 : m \in A\} \cup \{\neg \exists v_0 R_m v_0 : m \in B\},$$

where $A$ and $B$ are effectively inseparable.
