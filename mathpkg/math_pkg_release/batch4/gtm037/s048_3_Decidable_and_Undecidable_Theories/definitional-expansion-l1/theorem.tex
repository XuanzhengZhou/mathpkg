$\mathcal{L}_1$ is a definitional expansion of $\mathcal{L}_{\text{set}}$ with the following new symbols and axioms:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $0$, an individual constant. New axiom:
$$\forall v_0 \neg (v_0 \in 0).$$

\item[(ii)] $\mathrm{Op}$, a binary operation symbol. New axiom:
$$\forall v_0 \forall v_1 \forall v_2 [v_2 \in \mathrm{Op}(v_0, v_1) \leftrightarrow v_2 \in v_0 \lor v_2 = v_1].$$

\item[(iii)] $\{\,\}$, a unary operation symbol. New axiom:
$$\forall v_0 [\{v_0\} = \mathrm{Op}(0, v_0)].$$

\item[(iv)] $\{\,\}$, a binary operation symbol.
\end{enumerate}
