Let $\Gamma$ be a theory in $\mathcal{L}_{\text{nos}}$ in which every unary recursive function is syntactically definable, and let $\varphi$ be a formula of $\mathcal{L}_{\text{nos}}$ with $\operatorname{Fv}(\varphi) \subseteq \{v_0\}$. Then there is a sentence $\psi$ of $\mathcal{L}_{\text{nos}}$ such that

$$\Gamma \models \varphi(\Delta g^+ \psi) \leftrightarrow \psi.$$
