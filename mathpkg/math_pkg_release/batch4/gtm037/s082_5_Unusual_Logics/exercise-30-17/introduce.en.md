Exercise 30.17 asks to complete the roles of $\mathfrak{A}$ and $\mathfrak{B}$ interchanged in the proof of Theorem 30.7, and to show that if $\mathfrak{A} \equiv_m^\omega \mathfrak{B}$ for every $m \in \omega$, then $\mathfrak{A}$ is weak second-order elementarily equivalent to $\mathfrak{B}$.
