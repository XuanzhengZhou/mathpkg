Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a first-order language and $K$ a class of $\mathcal{L}$-structures. $K$ is $\omega$-projective if there is an expansion $\mathcal{L}'$ of $\mathcal{L}$, an $\omega$-expansion $\mathcal{L}''$ of $\mathcal{L}'$, and a theory $\Theta$ in $\mathcal{L}''$ such that $K$ is the class of all $\mathcal{L}$-reducts of $\omega$-models of $\Theta$.
