Theorem 31.8 combines Lemma 31.6 with earlier results to show that strongly compact cardinals are strongly inaccessible. Since the existence of uncountable strongly inaccessible cardinals is not provable in ZFC, this means the (m,infty)-compactness of L_{mm} for m > aleph_0 is also not provable.
