Given an infinite cardinal $m$, the $\mathfrak{m}$-interpretation of $\mathbf{Q}v_i$ is ``there are at least $m$ $v_i$ such that.'' Thus to the other satisfaction rules we add:

$$\mathfrak{A} \models_{m} \mathbf{Q}v_i\varphi[x] \quad \text{iff} \quad |\{a \in A : \mathfrak{A} \models \varphi[x_a^i]\}| \geq m.$$
