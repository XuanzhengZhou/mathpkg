Let $\langle \mathfrak{A}_i : i \in I \rangle$ be a system of $\mathcal{L}$-structures, with $|I| < \pi m$, where $m \geq \aleph_0$. Let $\varphi$ be a $Q$-formula and $x \in {}^\omega \bigcap_{i \in I} A_i$. Then

$$\prod_{i \in I} \mathfrak{A}_i / F \models_m \varphi[[\ ] \circ x] \quad \text{iff} \quad \{i \in I : \mathfrak{A}_i \models_m \varphi[\text{pr}_i \circ x]\} \in F.$$
