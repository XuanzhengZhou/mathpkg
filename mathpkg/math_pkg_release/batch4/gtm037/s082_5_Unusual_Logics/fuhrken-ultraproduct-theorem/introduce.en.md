Fuhrken's Theorem (30.13) extends Lós's fundamental theorem on ultraproducts to $Q$-languages with the $m$-interpretation, under the condition that the index set has cardinality less than $\pi m$ (where $\pi m$ is a certain cardinal invariant). The proof proceeds by induction on formulas, with the $Q$-quantifier step requiring careful cardinality estimates.
