Theorem 30.11 establishes that weak second-order logic can be reduced to $\omega$-logic: any weak second-order theory with only infinite models is $\omega$-projective. The proof uses an expansion with binary and ternary relation symbols to encode finite sets as pairs of elements, and translates second-order quantification into first-order quantification over the encoding.
