Take a $\mathcal{Q}$-language $\mathcal{L}$ with the $m$-interpretation. Then every set $\Gamma$ of sentences of $\mathcal{L}$ which has a model of power $\geq m + |\operatorname{Fmla}_{\mathcal{L}}|$ has a model of power $m + |\operatorname{Fmla}_{\mathcal{L}}|$.
