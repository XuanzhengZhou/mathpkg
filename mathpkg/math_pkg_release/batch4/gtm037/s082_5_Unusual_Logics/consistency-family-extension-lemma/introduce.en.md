Lemma 31.2 states that the consistency family conditions for $L_{\omega_1\omega}$ are preserved under taking downward closures. If $S$ satisfies (C1)-(C9), (C3'), and (C4'), then the family $S'$ of all subsets of members of $S$ satisfies the full set of conditions (C0)-(C9), (C3'), and (C4'). The proof is analogous to Lemma 18.7 for first-order logic.
