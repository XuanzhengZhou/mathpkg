Exercise 30.21 asks to prove that any class $K$ of models of a $\mathcal{Q}$-language theory $\Gamma$ under the $\aleph_0$-interpretation (where $\mathbf{Q}v_i$ means "there are infinitely many") is $\omega$-projective. This connects cardinality quantifiers at $\aleph_0$ (which resemble weak second-order logic) to the $\omega$-projective framework.
