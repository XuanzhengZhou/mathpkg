Let $F$ be a filter over $I$ with $\emptyset \notin F$. Then the following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $F$ is an ultrafilter.
\item[(ii)] For all $a, b \subseteq I$, if $a \cup b \in F$, then $a \in F$ or $b \in F$.
\item[(iii)] For any filter $G$ on $I$, if $F \subset G$, then $\emptyset \in G$.
\end{enumerate}
