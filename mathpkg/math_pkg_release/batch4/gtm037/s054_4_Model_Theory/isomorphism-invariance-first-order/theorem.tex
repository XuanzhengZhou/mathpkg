If $f$ is an isomorphism from $\mathfrak{A}$ onto $\mathfrak{B}$, $x \in {}^\omega A$, $\sigma$ is a term, and $\varphi$ is a formula, then:

$$f(\sigma^{\mathfrak{A}}[x]) = \sigma^{\mathfrak{B}}(f \circ x);$$
$$\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi[x] \quad \text{iff} \quad \mathfrak{B} \models \varphi[f \circ x].$$

Thus for any sentence $\varphi$, $\varphi$ holds in $\mathfrak{A}$ iff $\varphi$ holds in $\mathfrak{B}$.
