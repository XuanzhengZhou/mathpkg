$T_{r\text{seek}1}$ is the following machine:

$$\begin{array}{cccc}
0 & 0 & 3 & 1 \\
0 & 1 & 3 & 1 \\
1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
1 & 1 & 4 & 1
\end{array}$$

$T_{r\text{seek}1}$ finds the first $1$ to the right of the square it first looks at and stops at that $1$. But again, it may be that no such $1$ exists.
