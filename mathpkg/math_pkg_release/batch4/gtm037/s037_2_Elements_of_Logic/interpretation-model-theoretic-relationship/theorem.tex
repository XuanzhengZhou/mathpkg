Let $\mathfrak{A} = (\chi, f, R, \Gamma')$ be a syntactical $\mathcal{L}$-structure in $\mathcal{L}'$, and let $\mathfrak{B}$ be a model of $\Gamma'$. Then for any formula $\varphi$ of $\mathcal{L}$ and any $x \in {}^\omega B^{\Gamma' \mathfrak{A}}$ we have
$$\mathfrak{B}^{\Gamma' \mathfrak{A}} \models \varphi[x] \quad \text{iff} \quad \mathfrak{B} \models \varphi^{2\mathfrak{A}}[x].$$
