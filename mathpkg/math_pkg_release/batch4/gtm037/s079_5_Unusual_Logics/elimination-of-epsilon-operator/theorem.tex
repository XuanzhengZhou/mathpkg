For any choice triple $(\mathcal{L}, \varepsilon, \mathbf{O})$ and any formula $\varphi$ of $(\mathcal{L}, \varepsilon, \mathbf{O})$, there is an $\varepsilon$-free formula $\varphi^*$ (in an expanded language $\mathcal{L}'$) such that for any $\mathcal{L}$-structure $\mathfrak{A}$ and choice function $f$, there is an $\mathcal{L}'$-expansion $\mathfrak{B}_{\mathfrak{A}f}$ with $(\mathfrak{A}, f) \models \varphi[x]$ iff $\mathfrak{B}_{\mathfrak{A}f} \models \varphi^*[x]$.
