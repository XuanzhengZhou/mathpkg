Theorem 29.20 establishes the eliminability of the description operator $\tau$: every formula and term involving $\tau$ is equivalent (relative to a given theory $\Gamma$) to a formula not involving $\tau$. This is analogous to the elimination of definable function symbols and justifies the use of definite descriptions as a convenient abbreviation.
