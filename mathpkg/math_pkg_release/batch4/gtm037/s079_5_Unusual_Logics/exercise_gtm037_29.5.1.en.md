**29.31.** Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a relational language without equality. Let $\langle \mathfrak{A}_i : i \in I \rangle$ be a system of $\mathcal{L}$-structures. Define and study the notion of an ultraproduct of these structures, and formulate an analogue of Los's theorem for languages without equality.
