Let $L$ be a many-sorted language, $L^*$ the associated first-order language (with unary predicates $P_s$ for each sort $s$), and $\mathfrak{A}$ an $L$-structure. Then $\mathfrak{A}^*$ is a model of the sort axioms $\Gamma$. Furthermore, for any assignment $x \in \bigcup_{s \in \mathcal{S}} {}^\omega A_s$, we have $\sigma^{\mathfrak{A}}x = \sigma^{\mathfrak{A}^*}x$ and $\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi[x]$ iff $\mathfrak{A}^* \models \varphi^*[x]$, where $\varphi^*$ is the relativization of $\varphi$ to the sort predicates.
