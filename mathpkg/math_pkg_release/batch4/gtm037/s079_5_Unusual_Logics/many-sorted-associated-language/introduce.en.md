Proposition 29.28 shows that many-sorted logic can be reduced to ordinary first-order logic: with each many-sorted language associate a first-order language with unary predicates for each sort, and relativize formulas accordingly. Then satisfaction in the many-sorted sense corresponds exactly to satisfaction in the associated first-order sense. This reduction allows transferring metatheorems between the two frameworks.
