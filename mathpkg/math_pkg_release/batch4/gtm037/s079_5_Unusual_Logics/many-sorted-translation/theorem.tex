Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a many sorted language with associated first-order language $\mathcal{L}^*$, and let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an $\mathcal{L}$-structure. Then the associated structure $\mathfrak{A}^*$ is a model of the sort axioms $\Gamma$ (which include $\exists v_0 \mathbf{P}_s(v_0)$ for each $s \in \mathcal{S}$ and closure conditions on the relation and operation symbols respecting sorts).

Furthermore, for any $x \in \bigsqcup_{s \in \mathcal{S}} {^\omega A_s}$, we have $\sigma^{\mathfrak{A}}[x] = \sigma^{\mathfrak{A}^*}[x]$ and

$$\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi[x] \quad \text{iff} \quad \mathfrak{A}^* \models \varphi^*[x],$$

where $\varphi^*$ is the translation of the many-sorted formula $\varphi$ into the ordinary first-order language $\mathcal{L}^*$.
