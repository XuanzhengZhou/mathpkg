\textbf{Definition 8.30.} For any sentences $\varphi$ and $\psi$ let
$$\varphi \lor \psi = \neg \varphi \rightarrow \psi \quad \text{(disjunction of $\varphi$ and $\psi$);}$$
$$\varphi \land \psi = \neg (\varphi \rightarrow \neg \psi) \quad \text{(conjunction of $\varphi$ and $\psi$);}$$
$$\varphi \leftrightarrow \psi = (\varphi \rightarrow \psi) \land (\psi \rightarrow \varphi) \quad \text{(biimplication between $\varphi$ and $\psi$).}$$
