Definition 8.30 introduces the standard logical connectives of disjunction ($\lor$), conjunction ($\land$), and biimplication ($\leftrightarrow$) as abbreviations in terms of the primitive connectives negation ($\neg$) and implication ($\rightarrow$). This shows that the sentential logic formulated with only $\neg$ and $\rightarrow$ is expressively complete, and allows the formulation of results about duality and normal forms.
