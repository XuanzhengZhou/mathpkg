Proposition 8.31 gives the standard truth-table semantics for the defined logical connectives disjunction ($\lor$), conjunction ($\land$), and biimplication ($\leftrightarrow$) in terms of a model valuation $f^+$. This proposition makes it straightforward to determine whether sentences built using these defined symbols are tautologies: one simply evaluates them according to the usual Boolean rules.
