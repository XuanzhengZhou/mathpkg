In any Boolean algebra, a natural partial order arises from the join operation: $x \leq y$ if and only if $x + y = y$. Dually, this is equivalent to $x \cdot y = x$. This partial order makes the Boolean algebra a complemented distributive lattice, with $0$ as the least element and $1$ as the greatest element.
