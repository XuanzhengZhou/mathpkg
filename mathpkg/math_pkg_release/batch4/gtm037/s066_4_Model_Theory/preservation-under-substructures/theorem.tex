Let $\Gamma \cup \{\varphi\}$ be a collection of sentences. Then the following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\varphi$ is preserved under substructures relative to $\Gamma$, i.e., for all $\mathfrak{A}, \mathfrak{B} \in \mathrm{Mod}\,\Gamma$, if $\mathfrak{A} \subseteq \mathfrak{B}$ and $\mathfrak{B} \models \varphi$, then $\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi$;

\item[(ii)] there is a universal sentence $\psi$ such that $\Gamma \models \varphi \leftrightarrow \psi$.
\end{enumerate}
