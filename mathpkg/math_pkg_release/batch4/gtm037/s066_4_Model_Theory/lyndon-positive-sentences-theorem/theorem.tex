For any set $\Gamma$ of sentences the following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\mathrm{Mod}\,\Gamma$ can be characterized by a set of positive sentences;
\item[(ii)] $\mathrm{Mod}\,\Gamma$ is closed under homomorphisms, i.e., if $\mathfrak{A} \in \mathrm{Mod}\,\Gamma$ and $\mathfrak{A} \twoheadrightarrow \mathfrak{B}$, then $\mathfrak{B} \in \mathrm{Mod}\,\Gamma$.
\end{enumerate}
