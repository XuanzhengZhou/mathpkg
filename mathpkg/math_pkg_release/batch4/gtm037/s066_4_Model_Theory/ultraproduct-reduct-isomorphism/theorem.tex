With notation as in 25.6, suppose that for each $i \in I$, an $\mathcal{L}$-structure $\mathfrak{C}_i$ is a model of the $\mathcal{L}$-closure conditions for $\mathbf{U}$. Then

$$\mathfrak{P}_{i \in I} (\mathfrak{C}_i \upharpoonright \mathbf{U}) / F \cong (\mathfrak{P}_{i \in I} \mathfrak{C}_i / F) \upharpoonright \mathbf{U}.$$
