Let $f: {}^m\omega \rightarrow \omega$ and let $\Gamma$ be a consistent recursively axiomatizable theory in $\mathcal{L}_{\text{nos}}$ such that $\neg(\mathbf{n} = \mathbf{p}) \in \Gamma$ whenever $n \neq p$. If $f$ is syntactically definable in $\Gamma$ then $f$ is recursive. A similar statement holds for relations $R \subseteq {}^m\omega$.
