This theorem provides the companion result to Theorem 14.5 for weak syntactic definability. If a relation $R$ is weakly syntactically definable within a consistent, recursively axiomatizable theory $\Gamma$ containing distinctness of numerals, then $R$ is recursively enumerable. The intuitive proof enumerates all theorems of $\Gamma$ and outputs $x$ whenever $\varphi(\mathbf{x})$ appears.
