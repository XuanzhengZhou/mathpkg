Exercise 9.68 from Chapter 9 of Monk's Mathematical Logic. This exercise establishes that within ZF set theory (without the Axiom of Choice), the Boolean prime ideal theorem is equivalent to the statement that every non-trivial Boolean algebra has a maximal ideal. This is a foundational result in the metamathematics of Boolean algebras, situating the prime ideal theorem within the hierarchy of choice principles.
