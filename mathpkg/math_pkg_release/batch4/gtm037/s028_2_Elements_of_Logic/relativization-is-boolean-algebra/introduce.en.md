This proposition confirms that the relativization construction in Definition 9.42 is well-defined as a Boolean algebraic operation. One must verify that the modified complement $-'(x) = a \cdot -x$ and the inherited operations satisfy all Boolean algebra axioms within the restricted universe $A \upharpoonright a$. The verification is routine and establishes that $\mathfrak{A} \upharpoonright a$ is indeed a Boolean algebra with $a$ as its unit element.
