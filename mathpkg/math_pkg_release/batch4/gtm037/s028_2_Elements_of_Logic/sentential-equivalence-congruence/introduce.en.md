This proposition verifies that the relation $\equiv_{\Gamma}$ defined in 9.54 is a congruence relation on the algebra of sentences. It is an equivalence relation, and it respects the logical connectives: if $\varphi$ is equivalent to $\varphi'$ and $\psi$ is equivalent to $\psi'$, then their negations and implications are respectively equivalent. This property ensures the quotient $\text{Sent}_{\mathcal{P}}/{\equiv_{\Gamma}}$ is a well-defined Boolean algebra, the Lindenbaum algebra.
