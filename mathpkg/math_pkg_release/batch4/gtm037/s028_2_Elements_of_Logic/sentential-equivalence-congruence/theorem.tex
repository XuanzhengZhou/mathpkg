Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a sentential language and $\Gamma \subseteq \text{Sent}_{\mathcal{P}}$. Then for any $\varphi, \psi, \varphi', \psi' \in \text{Sent}_{\mathcal{P}}$,

(i) $\equiv_{\Gamma}$ is an equivalence relation on $\text{Sent}_{\mathcal{P}}$;

(ii) if $\varphi \equiv_{\Gamma} \varphi'$ and $\psi \equiv_{\Gamma} \psi'$, then $\neg \varphi \equiv_{\Gamma} \neg \varphi'$ and $\varphi \rightarrow \psi \equiv_{\Gamma} \varphi' \rightarrow \psi'$.
