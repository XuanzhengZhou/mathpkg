For any BA $\mathfrak{A} = \langle A, +, \cdot, -, 0, 1 \rangle$ and any $a \in A$ we let $\mathfrak{A} \upharpoonright a = \langle A \upharpoonright a, +', \cdot', -', 0, a \rangle$, where

$$A \upharpoonright a = \{x \in A : x \leq a\},$$

$$x +' y = x + y,$$

$$x \cdot' y = x \cdot y,$$

$$-' x = a \cdot -x,$$

for any $x, y \in A \upharpoonright a$. $\mathfrak{A} \upharpoonright a$ is the relativization of $\mathfrak{A}$ to $a$.
