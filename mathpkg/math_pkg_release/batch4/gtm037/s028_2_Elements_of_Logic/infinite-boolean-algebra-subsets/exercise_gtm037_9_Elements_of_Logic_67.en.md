Every infinite Boolean algebra has an infinite subset $X$ such that if $x, y \in X$ and $x \neq y$, then $x \cdot y = 0$, and an infinite subset $Y$ such that if $x, y \in Y$, then $x \leq y$ or $y \leq x$.
