There is a one-one correspondence between ideals and filters on a given Boolean algebra $\mathfrak{A}$. In more detail, if $I$ is an ideal of $\mathfrak{A}$ and $F$ is a filter of $\mathfrak{A}$, then:

(i) $I^f = \{x : -x \in I\}$ is a filter of $\mathfrak{A}$;

(ii) $F^i = \{x : -x \in F\}$ is an ideal of $\mathfrak{A}$;

(iii) $I^{fi} = I$;

(iv) $F^{if} = F$.
