Every finite Boolean algebra is isomorphic to the power set of its set of atoms. If the algebra has $m$ atoms, its cardinality is $2^m$. Conversely, for any natural number $m$, the power set algebra of an $m$-element set is a Boolean algebra of cardinality $2^m$. Thus the possible cardinalities of finite Boolean algebras are exactly the powers of two.
