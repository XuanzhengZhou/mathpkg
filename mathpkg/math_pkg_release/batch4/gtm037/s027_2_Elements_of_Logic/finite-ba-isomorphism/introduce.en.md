Two finite Boolean algebras are isomorphic precisely when they have the same number of elements. This follows from the fact that every finite Boolean algebra is isomorphic to the power set algebra of its set of atoms, and the number of atoms is $\log_2(|A|)$. Thus the cardinality alone is a complete isomorphism invariant for finite Boolean algebras.
