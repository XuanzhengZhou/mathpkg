If $\mathfrak{A} \cong \mathfrak{B}$, then clearly $|A| = |B|$. Conversely, if $|A| = |B| = 2^m$, then both $\mathfrak{A}$ and $\mathfrak{B}$ are isomorphic to the power set algebra on an $m$-element set, hence $\mathfrak{A} \cong \mathfrak{B}$.
