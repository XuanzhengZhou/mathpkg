If $\Omega x$, then $\mathbf{s} x = \mathbf{U} x \neq 0$ by 16.24, since $x \in \mathbf{U} x$. If $\neg \Omega x$, then $\mathbf{s} x = x$, and $x \neq 0$ since $\Omega 0$ by 16.17.
