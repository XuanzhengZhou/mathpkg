Lemma 16.39 verifies that in the language $\mathcal{L}_7$, the addition operation $+$ satisfies the recursion equation $v_0 + \mathbf{s} v_1 = \mathbf{s}(v_0 + v_1)$. This is the second axiom of Robinson's Q arithmetic (the successor recursion for addition) being validated in the formal set theory. The proof is case-based on the $\Omega$ predicate. The proof in the source text is truncated; the complete proof would consider whether $\Omega x$ and $\Omega y$ hold, using the properties of $\sigma$ and the definition of $\mathbf{s}$.
