If $\alpha$ is a variable which does not occur bound in $\varphi$ and does not occur in $\sigma$, and if no free occurrence of $\alpha$ in $\varphi$ is within the scope of a quantifier on a variable occurring in $\sigma$, then
$$\vdash \forall \alpha\varphi \rightarrow \text{Subf}_{\sigma}^{\alpha}\varphi.$$
