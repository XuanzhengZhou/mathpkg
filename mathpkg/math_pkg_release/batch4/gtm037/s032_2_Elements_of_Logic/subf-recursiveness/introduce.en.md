Proposition 10.52 establishes that the function assigning to the G\"odel numbers of a variable, a term, and a formula the G\"odel number of the result of free substitution is recursive. This formalizes the intuition that the syntactic operation of substituting a term for free occurrences of a variable in a formula is an effective (computable) procedure.
