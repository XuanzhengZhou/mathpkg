The relation $K$ is the candidate isomorphism between the reduced products $^n A / E$ and $^n B / E$ arising from two elementarily equivalent structures $\mathfrak{A}$ and $\mathfrak{B}$. It is defined at the final stage $\mathfrak{p} = 2^{|n|}$ of the transfinite inductive construction of partial isomorphisms $H_\gamma$ and filters $D_\gamma$. The key property of $K$ is that it is well-defined with respect to the equivalence classes modulo the ultrafilter $E = D_\mathfrak{p}$: equality of equivalence classes in the domain corresponds exactly to equality in the range, so $K$ is a partial isomorphism preserving the equality relation.
