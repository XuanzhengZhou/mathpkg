Let $\mathfrak{p} = 2^{|n|}$ and let $E = D_\mathfrak{p}$ be the ultrafilter over $n$ constructed by the transfinite induction. Define the relation $K$ between the reduced products $^n A / E$ and $^n B / E$ by:
$$K = \{([a]_E, [b]_E) : a H_\mathfrak{p} b\},$$
where $H_\mathfrak{p}$ is the relation at the final stage of the inductive construction. By conditions (5) and (6), $K$ has domain $^n A / E$ and range $^n B / E$. Moreover, $K$ is well-defined: for all $a, a', b, b'$ with $a H_\mathfrak{p} b$ and $a' H_\mathfrak{p} b'$,
$$[a]_E = [a']_E \iff [b]_E = [b']_E.$$
