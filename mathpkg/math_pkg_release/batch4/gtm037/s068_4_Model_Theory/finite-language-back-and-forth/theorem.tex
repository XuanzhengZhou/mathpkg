Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a language with no operation symbols and only finitely many relation symbols. Assume that $\mathfrak{A}$ and $\mathfrak{B}$ are $\mathcal{L}$-structures, $n \in \omega \setminus \{1\}$, and that for any $\varphi \in \Delta_n^n$, $\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi$ iff $\mathfrak{B} \models \varphi$. Then there is an $(n + 1)$-sequence for $\mathfrak{A}$, $\mathfrak{B}$.
