This theorem combines Lemmas 28.17 and 28.20 to give a complete characterization: for structures in a language with fewer than $m$ formulas, $m$-saturation is equivalent to the conjunction of $m$-universality and $m$-homogeneity. This result is fundamental in the theory of saturated models.
