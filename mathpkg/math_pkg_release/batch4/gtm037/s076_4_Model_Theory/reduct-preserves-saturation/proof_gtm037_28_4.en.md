Immediate from the definition of $m$-saturation: if $\mathcal{L}'$ is a reduct of $\mathcal{L}$, then every $\mathcal{L}'$-type is also an $\mathcal{L}$-type. Since $\mathfrak{A}$ realizes all $\mathcal{L}$-types over parameters of size $< m$, it realizes all $\mathcal{L}'$-types over the same parameters in the reduct.
