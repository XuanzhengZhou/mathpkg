An $m$-saturated structure is one that realizes all types over parameter sets of size less than $m$. An $m$-homogeneous structure is one where any partial elementary map of size less than $m$ can be extended by one element. Lemma 28.17 establishes that $m$-saturation implies $m$-homogeneity: any structure that realizes all types must also admit extensions of partial elementary maps.
