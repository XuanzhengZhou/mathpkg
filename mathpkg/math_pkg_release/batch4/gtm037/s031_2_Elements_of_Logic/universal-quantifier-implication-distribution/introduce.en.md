This proposition (10.30) establishes that the universal quantifier distributes over implication. For any finite sequence of variables of length $m$, if the implication $\varphi \rightarrow \psi$ holds universally, then the universal quantification of $\varphi$ implies the universal quantification of $\psi$. The proof proceeds by induction on $m$, with the base case $m=0$ being a tautology, and the inductive step using axiom schema 10.23(2) together with detachment.
