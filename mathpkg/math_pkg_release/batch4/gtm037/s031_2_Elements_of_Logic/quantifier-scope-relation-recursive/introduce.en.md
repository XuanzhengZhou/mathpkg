Proposition 10.42 provides a rigorous formulation of the effectivity of recognizing quantifier scopes in formulas via Gödel numbering. The relation $R$ encodes the statement that a universal quantifier on variable $\alpha$ appears at position $i$ in formula $\varphi$ with subformula $\psi$ as its scope. The proof shows $R$ is recursive by characterizing membership through finitely many decidable conditions on the Gödel numbers of the constituent objects.
