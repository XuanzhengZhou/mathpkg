$(m, i, x, y) \in R$ iff $m \in \text{Rng}(g \circ v)$ and $x, y \in g^{+*}\text{Fmla}$ and $i \leq \text{lx}$ and $(x)_i = gL_3 + 1$ and $(x)_{i+1} = m + 1$ and $i + \text{ly} \leq \text{lx}$ and $\forall j \leq \text{ly}[(y)_j = (x)_{i+j}]$.

All conditions on the right-hand side are recursive, hence $R$ is recursive.
