By Lemma 10.37 we have, for each $i < m$,

$$\vdash \sigma_i = \tau_i \rightarrow O \tau_0 \cdots \tau_{i-1} \sigma_i \sigma_{i+1} \cdots \sigma_{m-1} = O \tau_0 \cdots \tau_i \sigma_{i+1} \cdots \sigma_{m-1}.$$

Hence by repeated application of 10.36 (transitivity of equality) and an easy but long propositional tautology, the desired result follows. $\square$
