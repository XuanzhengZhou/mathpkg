The following formula is an instance of the equality axiom schema 10.23:

$$\sigma_i = \tau \rightarrow (O \sigma_0 \cdots \sigma_{m-1} = O \sigma_0 \cdots \sigma_{i-1} \tau \sigma_{i+1} \cdots \sigma_{m-1}).$$

Hence by 10.34 (modus ponens / detachment) and a suitable tautology, the desired result follows. $\square$
