The converse of 26.5 fails in general. Hint: consider $\mathfrak{A} = \langle A, R_i \rangle_{i \in \omega}$ and $\mathfrak{B} = \langle B, S_i \rangle_{i \in \omega}$, with $A = B = \omega$, $R_0 = S_0 = \omega$, $R_{i+1} = R_i \setminus \{i + 1\}$, $S_{i+1} = S_i \setminus \{i\}$.
