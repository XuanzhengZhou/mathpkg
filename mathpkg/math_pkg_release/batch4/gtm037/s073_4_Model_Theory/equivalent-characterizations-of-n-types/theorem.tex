Let $(\Gamma, \mathcal{L})$ be a theory and let $\Delta \subseteq \mathrm{Fmla}^n_{\mathcal{L}}$. The following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\Delta$ is an $n$-type over $\Gamma$;
\item[(ii)] $\Delta$ is maximal consistent over $\Gamma$;
\item[(iii)] there is a model $\mathfrak{A}$ of $\Gamma$ with $|A| \leq |\mathrm{Fmla}^n_{\mathcal{L}}|$ and an $a \in {}^n A$ such that $\Delta$ is the $n$-type of $a$ in $\mathfrak{A}$.
\end{enumerate}
