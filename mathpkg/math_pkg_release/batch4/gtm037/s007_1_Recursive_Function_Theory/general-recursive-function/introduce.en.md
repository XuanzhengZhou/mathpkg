The class of **general recursive functions** is defined as the intersection of all function classes that contain the successor function $s$ and all projection functions $U_i^n$, and are closed under composition, primitive recursion, and minimalization (applied to special functions). This is an inductive generation characterization: the general recursive functions are exactly those obtained from the basic functions by finitely many applications of the three closure operations. A relation is called general recursive precisely when its characteristic function is general recursive. The term "recursive" is often used as an abbreviation for "general recursive."
