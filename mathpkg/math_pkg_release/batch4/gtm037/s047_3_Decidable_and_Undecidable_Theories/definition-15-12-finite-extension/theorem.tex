Let $\Gamma$ and $\Delta$ be theories in languages $\mathcal{L}$ and $\mathcal{L}'$, with $\Delta$ an extension of $\Gamma$ (hence $\mathcal{L}'$ an expansion of $\mathcal{L}$). We say that $\Delta$ is a finite extension of $\Gamma$ provided that there is a finite set $\Theta$ of sentences of $\mathcal{L}'$ such that $\Gamma \cup \Theta$ axiomatizes $\Delta$.
