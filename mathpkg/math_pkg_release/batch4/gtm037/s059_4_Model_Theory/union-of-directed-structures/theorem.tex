Let $K$ be a set of $L$-structures directed by $\subseteq$. The \textbf{union} $\bigcup K$ is the $L$-structure defined as follows. For each $m$-ary operation symbol $O$,

$$
O^{\cup K} a_0 \cdots a_{m-1} = O^{\mathcal{A}} a_0 \cdots a_{m-1},
$$

where $\mathcal{A}$ is any member of $K$ such that $a_0, \ldots, a_{m-1} \in A$. For each $m$-ary relation symbol $R$,

$$
R^{\cup K} = \bigcup_{\mathcal{A} \in K} R^{\mathcal{A}}.
$$
