Theorem 6.13 provides ten equivalent characterizations of recursively enumerable $m$-ary relations. These range from enumerability by elementary functions to definability as the domain of a partial recursive function, and crucially include the $\Sigma_1$ representation: $R$ is r.e. iff it is the projection of a recursive relation. This result unifies the syntactic (enumeration-based) and semantic (definability-based) perspectives on r.e. relations.
