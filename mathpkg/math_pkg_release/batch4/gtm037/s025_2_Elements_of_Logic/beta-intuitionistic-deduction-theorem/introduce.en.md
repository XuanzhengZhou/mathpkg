This exercise asks the reader to verify that the deduction theorem holds for intuitionistic sentential logic axiomatized by (I1)-(I7) with detachment as the only rule of inference. The deduction theorem states that if a formula $\psi$ is derivable from assumptions $\Gamma \cup \{\varphi\}$, then $\varphi \to \psi$ is derivable from $\Gamma$ alone. Since the intuitionistic system shares the first two axiom schemes (I1 and I2) with classical logic, the standard proof of the deduction theorem carries over.
