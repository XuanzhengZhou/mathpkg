This exercise asks to show that there are exactly $2^{\aleph_0}$ (continuum many) distinct sentential logic systems over a language with a single sentence symbol. A "system" is defined as the deductive closure under detachment (modus ponens) of an arbitrary set of sentences. The hint constructs a family of continuum many pairwise distinct systems by encoding subsets $M \subseteq \omega$ into carefully chosen sets of formulas $\Gamma_M$.
