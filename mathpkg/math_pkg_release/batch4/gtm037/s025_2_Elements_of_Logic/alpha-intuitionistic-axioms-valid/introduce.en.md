This exercise asks the reader to verify that each of the seven axiom schemes (I1)-(I7) for intuitionistic sentential logic is valid according to the informal (intuitive) notion of intuitionistic truth. This serves as a sanity check that the formal axiom system correctly captures the intended semantics before a formal completeness theorem is established.
