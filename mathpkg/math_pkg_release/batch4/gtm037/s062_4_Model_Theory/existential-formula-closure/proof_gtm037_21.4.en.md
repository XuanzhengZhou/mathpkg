The proof is analogous to that of Proposition 21.21: let $\Theta$ be the set of $\varphi$ for which there exists an existential formula $\psi$ with $\operatorname{Fv}(\varphi) = \operatorname{Fv}(\psi)$ and $\models \varphi \leftrightarrow \psi$. One verifies that $\Theta$ contains all atomic formulas and their negations and is closed under $\vee$, $\wedge$, and $\exists v_i$ for each $i < \omega$. Hence $\Delta \subseteq \Theta$, which is exactly the statement of the proposition.
