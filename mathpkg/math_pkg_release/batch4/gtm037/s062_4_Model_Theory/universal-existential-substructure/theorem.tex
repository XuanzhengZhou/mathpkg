Assume that $\mathfrak{A} \subseteq \mathfrak{B}$, $\varphi$ is a formula, and $x \in {}^\omega A$.

(i) If $\varphi$ is universal and $\mathfrak{B} \models \varphi[x]$, then $\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi[x]$.

(ii) If $\varphi$ is existential and $\mathfrak{A} \models \varphi[x]$, then $\mathfrak{B} \models \varphi[x]$.
