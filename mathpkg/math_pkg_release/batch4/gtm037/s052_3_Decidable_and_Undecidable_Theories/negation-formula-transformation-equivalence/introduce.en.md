Proposition 18.2 states that for any first-order formula $\varphi$, the transformed formula $\varphi \rightarrow$ is logically equivalent to $\neg \varphi$. This follows directly from the inductive definition of $\varphi \rightarrow$, which effectively pushes the negation sign one step inward (when possible) and eliminates any initial double negation. The observation is immediate but essential for verifying condition (C2) in the definition of a consistency family.
