The claim follows directly from the definition of a special structure. If $|A|$ is a successor cardinal and $\mathfrak{A}$ is saturated, then its reduct remains saturated (by 28.25) and hence special. If $|A|$ is a limit cardinal, the elementary chain witnessing specialness also works for the reduct, since elementary substructure relations and saturation are preserved under taking reducts.
