This exercise shows that under the hypotheses of Theorem 27.12 (the Ryll-Nardzewski theorem characterizing $\aleph_0$-categorical theories), the existence of a model that is simultaneously $\aleph_0$-saturated and elementarily prime is equivalent to the other characterizations of $\aleph_0$-categoricity.
