This lemma states that every uncountable special structure is $|A|^+$-universal, meaning it realizes all types over subsets of size at most $|A|$. The proof is described as a "one-sided" version of the proof of Theorem 28.30, analogous to the relationship between the proofs of 28.18 and 28.22. This result allows special structures to replace saturated structures in applications requiring universality.
