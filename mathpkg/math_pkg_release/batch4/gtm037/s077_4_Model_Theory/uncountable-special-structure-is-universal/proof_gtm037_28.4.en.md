The proof of this lemma is a "one-sided" version of the proof of Theorem 28.30 (uniqueness of special structures). There is an analogous relationship between the proofs of Theorems 28.18 and 28.22. The key idea is to use the elementary chain decomposition of the special structure to realize types step by step.
