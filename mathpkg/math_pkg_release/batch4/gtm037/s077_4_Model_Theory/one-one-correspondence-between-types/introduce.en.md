This exercise asks the reader to establish a one-to-one correspondence between 1-types over a theory $\Gamma$ in an expanded language (with $m$ new individual constants) and $(m+1)$-types over $\Gamma$ in the original language. The proof uses the technique from the proof of 28.7.
