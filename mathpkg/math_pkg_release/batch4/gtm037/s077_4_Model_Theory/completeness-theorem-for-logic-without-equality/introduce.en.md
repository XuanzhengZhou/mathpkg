This is the completeness theorem for first-order logic without equality, restricted to relational languages. It states that any set of sentences consistent under the proof system $\vdash_{\text{ne}}$ has a model whose cardinality is bounded by the number of formulas in the language. The theorem follows immediately from Theorem 29.2 (the model existence theorem for consistency families).
