The proof proceeds by showing that a maximal element of $S$ exists (using Zorns lemma and condition (C7)), and then constructing a term model from it. Conditions (C1)-(C6) ensure that the maximal element is a Henkin theory, while (C8) ensures the term model has the right cardinality. The proof of the key claim (7), by induction on $\varphi$, is straightforward using the auxiliary claims (1)-(6); the text references the proof of 18.9 for the induction structure.
