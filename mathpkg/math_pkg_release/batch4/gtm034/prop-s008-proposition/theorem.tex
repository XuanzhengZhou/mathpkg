(a) $1 - \mathbf{E}[t^{\mathrm{T}} z^{\mathrm{S}} \mathbf{r}] = f_i(t; z)$,
(b) $1 - \mathbf{E}[t^{\mathrm{T}'} z^{\mathrm{S}} \mathbf{r}] = c(t) f_i(t; z)$,
(c) $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} t^k \mathbf{E}[z^{\mathrm{S}}_k; \mathbf{T} > k] = [c(t) f_e(t; z)]^{-1}$,
(d) $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} t^k \mathbf{E}[z^{\mathrm{S}}_k; \mathbf{T}' > k] = [f_e(t; z)]^{-1}$.

Here $0 \leq t < 1$, $|z| \leq 1$ in (a) and (b), while $|z| \geq 1$ in (c) and (d).
