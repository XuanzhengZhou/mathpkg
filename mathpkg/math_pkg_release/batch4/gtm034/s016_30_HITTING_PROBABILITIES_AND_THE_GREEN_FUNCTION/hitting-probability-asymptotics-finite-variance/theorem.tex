For aperiodic recurrent one-dimensional random walk with \(\sigma^2 < \infty\), or with \(\sigma^2 = \infty\) but neither left nor right continuous, and for any finite set \(B\) with \(2 \leq |B| < \infty\):
\[
\lim_{|x| \to \infty} \frac{H_B(x,y)}{a(x)} = \frac{1}{\sum_{z \in B} e_B(z)},
\]
where \(e_B\) is the equilibrium measure of \(B\).
