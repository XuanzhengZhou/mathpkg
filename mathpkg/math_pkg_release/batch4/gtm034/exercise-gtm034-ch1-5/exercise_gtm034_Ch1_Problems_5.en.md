5. Show that one-dimensional random walk has $m < \infty$ and $\mu = 0$ if and only if

$$\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{k} P\left[\left| \frac{S_k}{k} \right| > \epsilon\right] < \infty$$

for every $\epsilon > 0.$
