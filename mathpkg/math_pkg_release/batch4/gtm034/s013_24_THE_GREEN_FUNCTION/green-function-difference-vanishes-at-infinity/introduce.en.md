The difference between the Green function at two points \(y\) and \(0\), viewed as a function of the starting point \(x\), tends to zero as \(|x| 	o \infty\). This is a consequence of the Choquet-Deny theorem and shows that the asymptotic behavior of \(G(x,y)\) is independent of \(y\).
