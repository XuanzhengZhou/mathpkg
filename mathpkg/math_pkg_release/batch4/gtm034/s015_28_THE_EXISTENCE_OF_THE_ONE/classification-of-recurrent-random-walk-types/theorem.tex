For aperiodic recurrent random walk there are two possibilities:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[Type I:] The potential kernel \(a(x)\) is unbounded and grows like a slowly varying function.
\item[Type II:] The variance \(\sigma^2 < \infty\), and \(a(x) \sim \frac{2}{\sigma^2} |x|\) as \(|x| \to \infty\).
\end{enumerate}
