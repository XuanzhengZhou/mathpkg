This lemma provides the generating-function formulation of T1 for recurrent random walk with finite variance. By establishing that $\lim_{t \to 1} (1 - E_0[t^{T_x}])/(1 - E_0[t^{T_0}]) = a(-x)$, it translates the ratio limit into an Abel-sum form. The proof uses stopping time decomposition, the Green function identity $g_{(z)}(0,0) = a(x) + a(-x)$ from P29.4, and the fact that $E_z[\sum_{n=0}^{T_x-1} \phi_0(x_n)] = 1$ from P10.3.
