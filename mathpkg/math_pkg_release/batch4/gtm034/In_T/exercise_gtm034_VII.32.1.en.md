For one-dimensional aperiodic recurrent random walk with absolute moment of order $3 + \delta$ for some $\delta > 0$, show that

$$\lim_{x \to \infty} \left[ a(x) - \frac{|x|}{\sigma^2} \right]$$

exists and is finite. Is this true if only $\sigma^2$ is finite? (To obtain a sharp result, use problem 2 in Chapter II.)
