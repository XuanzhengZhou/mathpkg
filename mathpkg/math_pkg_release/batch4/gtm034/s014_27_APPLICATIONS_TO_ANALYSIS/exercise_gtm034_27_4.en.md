Given an aperiodic transient random walk with state space $R$, let $B \subseteq R$ be any additive subgroup of $R$. Prove that the set $B$ is recurrent (i.e., visited infinitely often with probability one) if and only if
$$\sum_{x \in B} G(0, x) = \infty.$$
Hint: The above sum is the total expected time the random walk spends in the set $B$.
