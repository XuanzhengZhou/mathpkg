$$1 - F(0, a) \geq P(0, x_1) \ldots P(x_{n-1}, x_n)[1 - F(x_n, a)] > 0.$$

This inequality shows that left continuity is a necessary property of an aperiodic random walk with $\mu < 0$ and $F(0, a) = 1$ for some $a < 0$. The proof of P9 is therefore complete.
