The eigenvalues of $Q_N$ are

(a) $\lambda_k = \cos \frac{k+1}{N+2} \pi, \quad k = 0, 1, \ldots, N,$

and the associated eigenvectors are

(b) $v_k(x) = \sqrt{\frac{2}{N+2}} \sin \frac{k+1}{N+2} \pi(x+1), \quad k = 0, 1, \ldots, N;$
$0 \leq x \leq N,$

where for $k,m = 0, 1, \ldots, N,$

(c) $Q_N v_k = \lambda_k v_k, \quad (v_k, v_m) = \sum_{x=0}^{N} v_k(x) v_m(x) = \delta(k,m).$
