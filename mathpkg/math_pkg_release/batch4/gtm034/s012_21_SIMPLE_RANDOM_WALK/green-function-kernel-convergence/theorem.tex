For a random walk with mean $\mu = 0$ and variance $0 < \sigma^2 < \infty$, and for $f(x)$ bounded and Riemann integrable on $0 \leq x \leq 1$,
$$\lim_{N \to \infty} \frac{\sigma^2}{2N^2} \sum_{k=0}^{N} g_N([Nt], k) f\left(\frac{k}{N}\right) = \int_0^1 f(s) R(s,t) \, ds,$$
uniformly for $0 \leq t \leq 1$, where $R(s,t) = \min(s,t) - st$.
