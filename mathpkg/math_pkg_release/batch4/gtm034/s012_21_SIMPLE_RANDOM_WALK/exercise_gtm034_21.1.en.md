Derive the analogue of P21.1 for simple random walk in the plane, i.e., find the eigenfunctions and eigenvalues of the transition function restricted to the rectangle
$$R_{M,N} = \{ z \mid 0 \leq \operatorname{Re}(z) \leq M; \; 0 \leq \operatorname{Im}(z) \leq N \}.$$
