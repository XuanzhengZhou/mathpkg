Let $D_N$ be as defined in problem 5. Prove that for aperiodic random walk with $\mu = 0$ and $0 < \sigma^2 < \infty$,
$$\lim_{N \to \infty} P_0\left[ \frac{D_N}{2N} \leq x \right]$$
exists for $0 \leq x \leq 1$ and compute the limit distribution. (This is an arc-sine type result for the range of random walk on an interval.)
