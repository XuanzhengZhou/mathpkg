Since \(g_N = (I_N - Q_N)^{-1}\) and the eigenvectors \(\{v_k\}\) form an orthonormal basis, the spectral theorem gives \((I_N - Q_N)^{-1} = \sum_{k=0}^{N} \frac{1}{1-\lambda_k} v_k v_k^T\). Writing this componentwise yields the formula for \(g_N(x,y)\). The formulas for \(R_N\) and \(L_N\) follow from their definitions in D21.1 by substituting the spectral representation of \(g_N\).
