The number of visits to a fixed point \(y\) before leaving the interval \([-N,N]\), properly normalized, has an exponential limit distribution. This is a consequence of the Green function asymptotics and illustrates how the occupation time of interior points behaves like a geometric random variable with success probability proportional to \(1/N\).
