This definition introduces the fundamental operators for studying random walk restricted to a finite interval \([0,N]\). \(Q_N\) is the transition matrix restricted to the interval, \(g_N\) is the corresponding Green function (expected number of visits before leaving), and \(R_N, L_N\) are the hitting probabilities of exiting the interval to the right or left, respectively.
