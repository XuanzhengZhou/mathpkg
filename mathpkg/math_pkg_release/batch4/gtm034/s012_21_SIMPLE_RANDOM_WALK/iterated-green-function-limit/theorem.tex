Define the iterates of the kernel $R(s,t) = \min(s,t) - st$ by
$$R^{p+1}(s,t) = \int_0^1 R(s,x) R^p(x,t) \, dx, \quad p \geq 1.$$

Then for each integer $p \geq 1$,
$$\left( \frac{\sigma^2}{2} \right)^p N^{1-2p} g_N^p(x,y) \to R^p\left(\frac{x}{N}, \frac{y}{N}\right)$$
as $N \to \infty$, uniformly in $0 \leq x, y \leq N$, where $g_N^p$ denotes the $p$-th matrix power of $g_N$.
