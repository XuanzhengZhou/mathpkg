For random walk with \(\mu = 0\) and \(0 < \sigma^2 < \infty\), and for bounded Riemann integrable \(f\),
\[
\lim_{N \to \infty} \frac{\sigma^2}{2N^2} \sum_{k=0}^{N} g_N([Nt],k) f\!\left(\frac{k}{N}\right) = \int_0^1 R(t,s) f(s) \, ds,
\]
uniformly for \(0 \leq t \leq 1\), where \(R(s,t) = \min(s,t) - st\).
