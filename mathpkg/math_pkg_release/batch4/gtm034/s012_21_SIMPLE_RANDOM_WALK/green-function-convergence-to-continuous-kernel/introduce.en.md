This deep result shows that the properly scaled Green function \(g_N\) of the interval converges to the continuous kernel \(R(s,t) = \min(s,t) - st\), which is the Green function of \(-d^2/dx^2\) with Dirichlet boundary conditions. Its proof requires probabilistic methods from Chapter IV (fluctuation theory) and is a cornerstone of the general absorption theory for random walk with finite variance.
