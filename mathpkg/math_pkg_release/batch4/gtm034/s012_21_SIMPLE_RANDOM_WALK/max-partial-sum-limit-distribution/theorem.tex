For a one-dimensional random walk with $\mu = 0$ and $0 < \sigma^2 < \infty$,
$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \mathbf{P}\left[ \max_{1 \leq k \leq n} |\mathbf{S}_k| \leq \sigma x \sqrt{n} \right] = 1 - F\left(\frac{1}{x^2}\right), \quad x > 0,$$
where $F(x)$ is the distribution defined in T2, with Laplace transform $(\cosh \sqrt{2s})^{-1}$.
