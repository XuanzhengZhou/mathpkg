The events \(\{\max_{1 \leq k \leq n} |S_k| \leq \sigma x \sqrt{n}\}\) and \(\{T_{[\sigma x \sqrt{n}]} > n\}\) are identical. Setting \(m = [\sigma x \sqrt{n}]\), we have \(m^2/(\sigma^2 x^2) \leq n < (m+1)^2/(\sigma^2 x^2)\). By monotonicity of \(T_m\), sandwiching and applying T23.2 yields \(\lim G_n(x) = 1 - F(1/x^2)\).
