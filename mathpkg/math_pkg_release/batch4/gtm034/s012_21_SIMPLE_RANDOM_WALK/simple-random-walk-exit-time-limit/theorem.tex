For simple symmetric random walk starting at $x_0 = N$ (the midpoint of $[0,2N]$), the scaled exit time satisfies
$$\lim_{N \to \infty} P_N\left[ T_{2N} > N^2 x \right] = 1 - F(x),$$
where
$$F(x) = 1 - \frac{4}{\pi} \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^j}{2j+1} e^{-\frac{\pi^2}{8}(2j+1)^2 x}, \quad x > 0.$$

The expected exit time is $E_N[T_{2N}] = (N+1)^2$.
