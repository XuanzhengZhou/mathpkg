This theorem generalizes the simple random walk result \(R_N(x) = (x+1)/(N+2)\) to all random walks with zero mean and finite variance. It shows that the probability of exiting to the right is asymptotically \(x/N\) — the uniform distribution — regardless of the details of the step distribution, as long as the first two moments exist.
