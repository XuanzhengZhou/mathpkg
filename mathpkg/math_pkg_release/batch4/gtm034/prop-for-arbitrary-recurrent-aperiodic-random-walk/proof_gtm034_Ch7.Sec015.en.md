Proof: The formula for $g_{(0)}(x,y)$, defined in D10.1, was derived for two-dimensional random walk in P11.6. The same proof applies in general, as it required only information which is now in our possession even for one-dimensional random walk (all we need is contained in T28.1 and P1). The inequality in P4 follows from

$$0 \leq g_{(0)}(x,-y) = a(x) + a(y) - a(x + y), \quad y \in R.$$
