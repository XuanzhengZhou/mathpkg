Proposition P1 establishes T1 for transient random walk using only the elementary renewal methods of Chapter I. Since $P_z[T = \infty] > 0$ in the transient case, the limit of the ratio $P_x[T > n]/P_0[T > n]$ can be evaluated directly as $(1 - \sum F_n(x,0))/(1 - F(0,0))$, which simplifies to $G(0,0) - G(x,0) = a(x)$ via the renewal identity $F(x,0) = G(x,0)/G(0,0)$.
