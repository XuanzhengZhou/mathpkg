Proposition P2 provides the Abel-summation version of T1 for one-dimensional recurrent random walk. The proof uses generating function identities, the strong Markov property at the stopping time $T_x$, Abel's theorem for the difference $R_t(x,0) - R_t(0,0) \to -a(x)$, and the Green function relation $g_{(x)}(0,0) = a(x) + a(-x)$ from P29.4. Combined with P3 and Karamata's theorem, this yields the stronger asymptotic P4 for the finite variance case.
