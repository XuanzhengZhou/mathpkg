For aperiodic recurrent random walk in two dimensions, with $B \subset R$, $2 \leq |B| < \infty$,

$$H_B(x,y) = \mu_B(y) + \sum_{t \in B} A(x,t)[\Pi_B(t,y) - \delta(t,y)], \quad x \in R, \quad y \in B,$$

where

$$K_B(\cdot) > 0, \quad \mu_B(y) = \frac{K_B(\cdot y)}{K_B(\cdot)} \text{ for } y \in B,$$

$$\Pi_B(x,y) - \delta(x,y) = K_B(x,y) - \frac{K_B(x \cdot) K_B(\cdot y)}{K_B(\cdot)} \text{ for } x, y \in B.$$
