The random variables $T_n$ denote the first time at which the random walk attains its maximum $M_n$ during the first $n$ steps. It will play an auxiliary role in deriving the characteristic function of $M_n$. For a result of independent interest concerning $T_n$, see problem 7 at the end of the chapter.
