For aperiodic recurrent two-dimensional random walk, and $2 \leq |B| < \infty$

$$g_B(x,y) = -A(x,y) - \frac{1}{K_B(\cdot)} + \sum_{s \in B} \mu(s)A(s,y) + \sum_{t \in B} A(x,t)\mu^*(t)$$
$$+ \sum_{s \in B} \sum_{t \in B} A(x,t)[\Pi_B(t,s) - \delta(t,s)]A(s,y), \quad x,y \in R.$$

The matrix $\Pi_B$ is given by T11.1 and so are

$$\mu(s) = \mu_B(s) = \frac{K_B(\cdot s)}{K_B(\cdot)}.$$
$$\mu^*(t) = \mu_B^*(t) = \frac{K_B(t \cdot)}{K_B(\cdot)}.$$
