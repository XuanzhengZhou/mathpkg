For a Stein manifold $X$, the cohomology groups of the sheaf of non-vanishing holomorphic functions $\mathcal{O}^*$ are isomorphic to the singular cohomology groups of $X$ with integer coefficients, shifted by one degree. This theorem connects the multiplicative sheaf-theoretic cohomology to classical topological invariants. The proof follows from Cartan's Theorem B together with the exponential exact sequence.
