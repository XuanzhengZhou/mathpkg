Let the complex function $f$ defined on the region $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be complex differentiable at $z_0 \in B$. If $f(z) = f(z_0) + \sum_{v=1}^{n} (z_v - z_v^{(0)}) \Delta_v(z)$, then we call $\Delta_v(z_0)$ the partial derivative of $f$ with respect to $z_v$ at $z_0$, and write $\Delta_v(z_0) = \frac{\partial f}{\partial z_v}(z_0) = f_{z_v}(z_0) = f_v(z_0)$.
