Let $\varphi: S_1 \to S_2$ be an analytic sheaf morphism. Then $\operatorname{Ker} \varphi$ is the subsheaf of $S_1$ with stalks $(\operatorname{Ker} \varphi)_z = \operatorname{Ker}(\varphi_z)$, $\operatorname{Im} \varphi$ is the subsheaf of $S_2$ with stalks $(\operatorname{Im} \varphi)_z = \operatorname{Im}(\varphi_z)$, and $\operatorname{Coker} \varphi = S_2 / \operatorname{Im} \varphi$.
