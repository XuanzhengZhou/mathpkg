Let $G \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be a domain with $0 \in G$, $\pi: \mathbb{C}^n - \{0\} \to \mathbb{P}^{n-1}$ the natural projection. Then $X := \{(z, x) \in (G - \{0\}) \times \mathbb{P}^{n-1}: x = \pi(z)\} \cup (\{0\} \times \mathbb{P}^{n-1})$ is a complex manifold, and the map $X \to G$ given by $(z, x) \mapsto z$ is a modification with exceptional fiber $\mathbb{P}^{n-1}$ over $0$.
