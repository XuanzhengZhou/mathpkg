The sheaf $\Omega^p$ is the sheaf of germs of holomorphic $(p,0)$-forms on a complex manifold $X$. Each local section is given by a holomorphic $p$-form expressed in local coordinates as a linear combination of wedge products $dz_{i_1} \wedge \cdots \wedge dz_{i_p}$ with holomorphic coefficient functions. The sheaf $\Omega^p$ is locally free of rank $\binom{n}{p}$ (where $n = \dim X$), hence in particular coherent. The canonical injection $\varepsilon: \Omega^p \hookrightarrow \mathcal{A}^{p,0}$ embeds holomorphic $p$-forms into the sheaf of smooth $(p,0)$-forms.
