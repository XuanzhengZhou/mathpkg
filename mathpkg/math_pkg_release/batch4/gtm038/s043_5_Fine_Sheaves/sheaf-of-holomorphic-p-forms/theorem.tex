The sheaf of germs of holomorphic $(p,0)$-forms on $X$ is denoted by $\Omega^p$. A holomorphic $(p,0)$-form $\varphi = \varphi^{(p,0)}$ has a local representation
$$
\varphi = \sum_{1 \leqslant i_1 < \cdots < i_p \leqslant n} a_{i_1 \cdots i_p} \, dz_{i_1} \wedge \cdots \wedge dz_{i_p},
$$
with holomorphic coefficients $a_{i_1 \cdots i_p}$. The sheaf $\Omega^p$ is locally isomorphic to the free sheaf $\binom{n}{p} \cdot \mathcal{O}$; in particular $\Omega^p$ is a locally free sheaf and is coherent. There is a canonical injection $\varepsilon: \Omega^p \hookrightarrow \mathcal{A}^{p,0}$.
