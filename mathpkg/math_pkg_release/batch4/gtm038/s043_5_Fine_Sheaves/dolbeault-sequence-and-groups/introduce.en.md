The Dolbeault sequence is the cochain complex obtained by applying the global section functor $\Gamma(X, -)$ to the exact sheaf sequence $0 \to \Omega^p \to \mathcal{A}^{p,0} \to \mathcal{A}^{p,1} \to \cdots$. Its cohomology groups $H^{p,q}(X)$ are the Dolbeault groups, defined as the quotient of $d''$-closed $(p,q)$-forms modulo $d''$-exact $(p,q)$-forms. When the sheaves $\mathcal{A}^{p,q}$ are fine (hence acyclic for $\Gamma$), the Dolbeault groups compute the sheaf cohomology $H^q(X, \Omega^p)$ via the Dolbeault isomorphism theorem.
