Let $G \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be a domain. Then:

1. $\varnothing$ and $G$ are analytic subsets of $G$.
2. If $M_1, \ldots, M_\ell$ are analytic in $G$, then $\bigcup_{i=1}^{\ell} M_i$ is analytic in $G$.
3. If $M_1, \ldots, M_\ell$ are analytic in $G$, then $\bigcap_{i=1}^{\ell} M_i$ is analytic in $G$.
4. If $(M_i)_{i \in I}$ is a system of analytic sets, then $\bigcap_{i \in I} M_i$ is analytic in $G$.
