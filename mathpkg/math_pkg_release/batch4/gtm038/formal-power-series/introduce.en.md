A formal power series about a point  \in \mathbb{C}^n$ is an infinite sum over multi-indices  = (v_1,\ldots,v_n)$ with complex coefficients $. For a specific $, the series does not necessarily represent a complex number; a suitable notion of convergence must be introduced.
