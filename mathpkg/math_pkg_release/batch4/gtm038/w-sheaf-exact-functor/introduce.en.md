Theorem 1.6 establishes that the flabby sheaf construction $\mathfrak{W} = (W_\ell)_{\ell \geq 0}$ is an exact functor on the category of sheaves of $\mathcal{O}$-modules. This means that $\mathfrak{W}$ sends short exact sequences of sheaves to short exact sequences of flabby sheaves. The proof proceeds by induction on $\ell$, using the base case $\ell = 0$ established in Theorem 1.4, and a diagram-chasing argument involving the auxiliary quotient sheaves $\mathcal{Q} = W_0(\mathcal{S})/\mathcal{S}$. The exactness of $\mathfrak{W}$ is a cornerstone of sheaf cohomology theory.
