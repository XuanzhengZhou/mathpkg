Let $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be a region, $g: B \to \mathbb{C}^n$ a holomorphic mapping. Let $\mathfrak{z}_0 \in B$ and $\mathfrak{w}_0 = g(\mathfrak{z}_0)$. There are open neighborhoods $U = U(\mathfrak{z}_0) \subset B$ and $V = V(\mathfrak{w}_0) \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ such that $g: U \to V$ is biholomorphic if and only if the complex Jacobian determinant $M_g(\mathfrak{z}_0) \neq 0$.
