A complex ringed space is a topological space equipped with a sheaf of local $\mathbb{C}$-algebras. This notion generalizes complex manifolds by axiomatizing the structure sheaf of holomorphic functions. For any open subset $W$, each section $f \in \Gamma(W, \mathcal{H})$ gives rise to a complex-valued function $[f]$ on $W$ via evaluation at the residue fields of the stalks.
