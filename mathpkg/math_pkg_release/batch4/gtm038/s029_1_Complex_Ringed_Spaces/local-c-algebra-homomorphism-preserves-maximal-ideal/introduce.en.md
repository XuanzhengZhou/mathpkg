This lemma states that any $\mathbb{C}$-algebra homomorphism between local $\mathbb{C}$-algebras maps the maximal ideal of the domain into the maximal ideal of the codomain. It is a fundamental property of local homomorphisms: elements of the maximal ideal (the non-units) cannot be sent to units. When the homomorphism is an isomorphism, the image of the maximal ideal equals the entire maximal ideal of the target.
