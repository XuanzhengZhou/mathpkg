Let $\varphi = (\tilde{\varphi}, \varphi_*): (X_1, \mathcal{H}_1) \rightarrow (X_2, \mathcal{H}_2)$ be an isomorphism of complex ringed spaces. Then for every open set $V \subset X_2$ there is a $\mathbb{C}$-algebra isomorphism
$$\tilde{\varphi}_V: \Gamma(V, \mathcal{H}_2) \xrightarrow{\sim} \Gamma(\tilde{\varphi}^{-1}(V), \mathcal{H}_1),$$
given by $\tilde{\varphi}_V(s) := \varphi_*^{-1}(s \circ \tilde{\varphi})$ for $s \in \Gamma(V, \mathcal{H}_2)$.
