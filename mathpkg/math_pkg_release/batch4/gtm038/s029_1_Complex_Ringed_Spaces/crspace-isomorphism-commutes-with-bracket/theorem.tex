Let $\varphi = (\tilde{\varphi}, \varphi_*): (X_1, \mathcal{H}_1) \rightarrow (X_2, \mathcal{H}_2)$ be an isomorphism of complex ringed spaces. For an open set $V \subset X_2$ let $\varphi^*: \mathcal{F}(V, \mathbb{C}) \rightarrow \mathcal{F}(\tilde{\varphi}^{-1}(V), \mathbb{C})$ be defined by $\varphi^*(f) := f \circ \tilde{\varphi}$. Then for every $s \in \Gamma(V, \mathcal{H}_2)$,
$$[\varphi_*(s)] = \varphi^*([s]).$$
Consequently, for $t \in \Gamma(\tilde{\varphi}^{-1}(V), \mathcal{H}_1)$,
$$[(\varphi_*)^{-1}(t)] = (\varphi^*)^{-1}([t]).$$
