Let $\{M_W, r_V^W\}$ be a pre-sheaf of $\mathbb{C}$-algebras, $\{\tilde{M}_W, \tilde{r}_V^W\}$ a pre-sheaf of abelian groups, and $\mathcal{A}$ resp. $\mathcal{S}$ the corresponding sheaves. If for every open set $W \subset B$, $\tilde{M}_W$ is a (unitary) $M_W$-module and for every $s \in M_W$ and every $\tilde{s} \in \tilde{M}_W$, $\tilde{r}_V^W(s \cdot \tilde{s}) = r_V^W(s) \cdot \tilde{r}_V^W(\tilde{s})$, then $(\{M_W, r_V^W\}, \{\tilde{M}_W, \tilde{r}_V^W\})$ is called a pre-sheaf of modules.
