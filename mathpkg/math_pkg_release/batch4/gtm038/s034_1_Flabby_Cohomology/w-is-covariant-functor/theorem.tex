The assignment $W$ defined by $W(\mathcal{S})$ from the pre-sheaf of generalized sections extends to a covariant functor from the category of $R$-module sheaves over $X$ to itself. Specifically:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $W(\varphi \circ \psi) = W\varphi \circ W\psi$ for composable sheaf homomorphisms.
\item $W(\text{id}_{\mathcal{S}}) = \text{id}_{W(\mathcal{S})}$.
\end{enumerate}
