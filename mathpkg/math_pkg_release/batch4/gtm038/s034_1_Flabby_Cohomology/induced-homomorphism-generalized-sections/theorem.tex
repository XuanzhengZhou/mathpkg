If $\varphi : \mathcal{S}_1 \to \mathcal{S}_2$ is a homomorphism of $R$-module sheaves over $X$, then the induced map on generalized sections is
$$
\varphi_* : \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}_1) \to \hat{\Gamma}(U, \mathcal{S}_2), \qquad \varphi_*(s) := \varphi \circ s,
$$
which is an $R$-module homomorphism.
