**Proof.** If $f$ is holomorphic in $B$, then $f|P = \operatorname{ch}(f|T)$, where the distinguished boundary of $P$ is denoted by $T$. From Theorem 3.2, $f|P$ can be expanded as a power series in all of $P$. $\square$
