Let $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be a region, $f$ holomorphic in $B$ and $\zeta_0$ a point in $B$. If $P \subset B$ is a polycylinder about $\zeta_0$ with $\bar{P} \subset B$, then there is a power series $\mathfrak{P}(\zeta) = \sum_{v=0}^{\infty} a_v(\zeta - \zeta_0)^v$ which converges to $f$ on all of $P$.
