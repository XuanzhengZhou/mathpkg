Let $\mathfrak{U} = (U_i)_{i \in I}$ be an open covering of $X$. An $\ell$-dimensional alternating cochain over $\mathfrak{U}$ with values in $\mathcal{S}$ is a mapping assigning to each ordered $(\ell+1)$-tuple $i_0, \ldots, i_\ell$ with $U_{i_0} \cap \cdots \cap U_{i_\ell} \neq \emptyset$ a section $c_{i_0 \ldots i_\ell} \in \Gamma(U_{i_0} \cap \cdots \cap U_{i_\ell}, \mathcal{S})$, alternating under permutation of indices.
