**Proof.** For each $\mu$ with $1 \leqslant \mu \leqslant n$, define $G_\mu := G - E_\mu$ where $E_\mu := \{\mathfrak{z} : z_\mu = 0\}$. By Theorem 5.1, each $G_\mu$ is connected. Since $G_0 = \bigcap_{\mu=1}^{n} G_\mu$ and the intersection of finitely many connected open sets that pairwise intersect is connected, $G_0$ is a domain. $\square$
