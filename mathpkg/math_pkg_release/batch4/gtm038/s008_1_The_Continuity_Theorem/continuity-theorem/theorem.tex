Let $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ and $\tilde{P} \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be domains. Let $\tilde{H} \subset B \cap \tilde{P}$ be a nonempty subset such that every holomorphic function on $\tilde{H}$ extends uniquely to a holomorphic function on $\tilde{P}$. If $B \cap \tilde{P}$ is connected, then every holomorphic function $f$ on $B$ has a unique holomorphic extension $F$ to $B \cup \tilde{P}$.
