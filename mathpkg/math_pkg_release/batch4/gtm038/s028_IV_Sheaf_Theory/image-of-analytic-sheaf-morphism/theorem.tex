Let $\varphi: \mathcal{S}_1 \rightarrow \mathcal{S}_2$ be an analytic sheaf morphism. Then the image of $\varphi$ is defined as
$$
\operatorname{Im} \varphi := \bigcup_{\mathfrak{z} \in B} \operatorname{Im}(\varphi_{\mathfrak{z}}),
$$
where $\varphi_{\mathfrak{z}}: (\mathcal{S}_1)_{\mathfrak{z}} \rightarrow (\mathcal{S}_2)_{\mathfrak{z}}$ is the induced stalk homomorphism.
