**Theorem 4.6** establishes two fundamental closure properties of coherent sheaves:

(1) The quotient of a coherent sheaf by a coherent subsheaf is coherent. This follows from the canonical exact sequence $0 \to \mathcal{S}^* \to \mathcal{S} \to \mathcal{S}/\mathcal{S}^* \to 0$ and Theorem 4.4.

(2) A finite direct sum of coherent sheaves is coherent. For two sheaves, this uses the exact sequence $0 \to \mathcal{S}_1 \xrightarrow{j_1} \mathcal{S}_1 \oplus \mathcal{S}_2 \xrightarrow{p_2} \mathcal{S}_2 \to 0$ and Theorem 4.3. The general case follows by induction.

Together with Theorems 4.3, 4.4, and 4.7, these results establish that coherent sheaves form an abelian category.
