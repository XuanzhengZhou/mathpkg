1. If $\mathcal{S}$ is a coherent sheaf over $B$ and $\mathcal{S}^* \subset \mathcal{S}$ is a coherent analytic subsheaf, then $\mathcal{S}/\mathcal{S}^*$ is also coherent.

2. If $\mathcal{S}_1, \ldots, \mathcal{S}_\ell$ are coherent analytic sheaves over $B$, then $\mathcal{S}_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathcal{S}_\ell$ is also coherent.
