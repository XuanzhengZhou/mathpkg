This is an immediate consequence of Theorem 3.2 (First Isomorphism Theorem for Sheaves). The mapping $\bar{\varphi}: \mathcal{S}_1/\operatorname{Ker}\varphi \to \operatorname{Im}\varphi$ defined by $\bar{\varphi}(\bar{\sigma}) := \varphi(\sigma)$ is a sheaf isomorphism by Theorem 3.2. Composing with the canonical quotient $\mathfrak{q}: \mathcal{S}_1 \twoheadrightarrow \mathcal{S}_1/\operatorname{Ker}\varphi$ and the inclusion $\iota: \operatorname{Im}\varphi \hookrightarrow \mathcal{S}_2$ yields the stated decomposition.
