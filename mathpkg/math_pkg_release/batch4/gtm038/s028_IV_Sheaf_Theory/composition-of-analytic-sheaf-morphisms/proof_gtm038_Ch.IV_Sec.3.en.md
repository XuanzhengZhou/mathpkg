At each stalk $\mathfrak{z} \in B$, define $(\psi \circ \varphi)_{\mathfrak{z}} := \psi_{\mathfrak{z}} \circ \varphi_{\mathfrak{z}}$, which is an $\mathcal{O}_{\mathfrak{z}}$-module homomorphism as the composition of two such homomorphisms. For continuity: if $s \in \Gamma(U, \mathcal{S}_1)$ is a section, then $\varphi \circ s \in \Gamma(U, \mathcal{S}_2)$ is a section of $\mathcal{S}_2$ (by continuity of $\varphi$), and $\psi \circ (\varphi \circ s) = (\psi \circ \varphi) \circ s \in \Gamma(U, \mathcal{S}_3)$ is a section of $\mathcal{S}_3$ (by continuity of $\psi$). Hence $\psi \circ \varphi$ is continuous and therefore an analytic sheaf morphism.
