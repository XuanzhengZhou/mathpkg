Let $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ be a region, $K \subset B$ a subset. Then

$$\hat{K}_B := \{ z \in B : |f(z)| \leq \sup|f(K)| \text{ for every holomorphic function } f \text{ in } B \}$$

is called the **holomorphically convex hull** of $K$ in $B$. When no misunderstanding can arise, we write $\hat{K}$ instead of $\hat{K}_B$.
