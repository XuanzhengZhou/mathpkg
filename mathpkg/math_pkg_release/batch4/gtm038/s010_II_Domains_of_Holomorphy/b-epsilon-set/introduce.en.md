Definition 4.1 introduces the sets $B_\varepsilon$, which form an inner exhaustion of a region $B \subset \mathbb{C}^n$. As $\varepsilon \to 0$, the $B_\varepsilon$ exhaust $B$: $B = \bigcup_{\varepsilon > 0} B_\varepsilon$. These sets are essential for uniform power series estimates (Theorem 4.3) and the Thullen theorem.
