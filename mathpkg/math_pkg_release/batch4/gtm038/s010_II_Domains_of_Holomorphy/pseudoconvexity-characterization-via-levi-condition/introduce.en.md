Theorem 2.5 is a fundamental result in several complex variables, establishing the equivalence between pseudoconvexity and the Levi condition. For a domain with $C^2$ boundary, pseudoconvexity is characterized by positive semidefiniteness of the Levi form at every boundary point. The proof is deep and is not presented in the text.
