Definition 4.2 is a fundamental concept in the theory of several complex variables. It appears in Chapter III of Grauert and Fritzsche's textbook on Several Complex Variables (GTM038).

This concept is part of the systematic development of holomorphic function theory in higher dimensions, where the behavior differs significantly from the one-variable case. The definition provides essential structure for understanding the weierstrass preparation theorem.

For further reading, see Grauert and Fritzsche, Several Complex Variables, GTM 38, Springer-Verlag, 1976.
