In the sequence
$$S_1 \xrightarrow{\varphi_1} S_2 \xrightarrow{\varphi_2} S_3 \xrightarrow{\varphi_3} S_4 \xrightarrow{\varphi_4} S_5$$
let $\varphi_1$ be surjective, $\varphi_4$ injective and $\operatorname{Ker} \varphi_3 = \operatorname{Im} \varphi_2$.

Then
$$S_1 \xrightarrow{\varphi_2 \circ \varphi_1} S_3 \xrightarrow{\varphi_4 \circ \varphi_3} S_5$$
is exact.
