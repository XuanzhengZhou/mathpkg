Let $(S_1, \pi_1), \ldots, (S_\ell, \pi_\ell)$ be sheaves over $B$. Their Whitney sum $S = S_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus S_\ell$ is the sheaf associated to the presheaf $W \mapsto \Gamma(W, S_1) \times \cdots \times \Gamma(W, S_\ell)$. For every $z \in B$, $S_z \cong (S_1)_z \times \cdots \times (S_\ell)_z$.
