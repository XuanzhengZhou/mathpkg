Let $\mathcal{S}$, $\mathcal{S}'$ be fine sheaves over a paracompact complex manifold $X$, and let $\varphi: \mathcal{S} \rightarrow \mathcal{S}'$ be an epimorphism of sheaves of $\mathcal{T}$-modules. Then the induced map on global sections

$$\varphi_*: \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}) \rightarrow \Gamma(X, \mathcal{S}')$$

is surjective.
