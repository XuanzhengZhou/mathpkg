The proof of this theorem relies on solving the $\bar{\partial}$-equation on strongly pseudo-convex domains with uniform estimates. The argument uses integral kernel methods and the positive definiteness of the Levi form to control the solution operator. A modern treatment can be found in the work of Henkin, Ramirez, and Kerzman on integral representations.

According to Siu and Range there exists a generalization of this theorem for domains with piece-wise smooth boundaries (see R. M. Range, and Y.-T. Siu: *Uniform estimates for the $\bar{\partial}$-equation on intersections of strictly pseudo-convex domains*. Bull. Amer. Math. Soc., 78(5):721-722, 1972).
