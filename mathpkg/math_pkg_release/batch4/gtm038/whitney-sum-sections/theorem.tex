Let $(S_1, \pi_1), \ldots, (S_\ell, \pi_\ell)$ be sheaves over $B$, and let $S = S_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus S_\ell$ be their Whitney sum. Then for every $z \in B$ there is a bijection $\varphi: S_3 \rightarrow (S_1)_3 \times \cdots \times (S_\ell)_3$ defined by $(W, (s_1, \ldots, s_\ell))_3 \mapsto (s_1(3), \ldots, s_\ell(3))$.
