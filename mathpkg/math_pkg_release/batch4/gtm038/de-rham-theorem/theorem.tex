The de Rham cohomology groups $H_{dR}^\ell(X, \mathbb{C})$ are canonically isomorphic to the sheaf cohomology groups $H^\ell(X, \underline{\mathbb{C}})$, where $\underline{\mathbb{C}}$ is the constant sheaf. On a complex manifold, the de Rham complex is a fine resolution of $\underline{\mathbb{C}}$.
