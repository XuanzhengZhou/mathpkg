For a domain $G \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ with $C^2$ boundary, the Levi condition is satisfied at a point $\mathfrak{z}_0 \in \partial G$ if there is an open neighborhood $U = U(\mathfrak{z}_0)$ and a function $\varphi$ on $U$ defining the boundary such that the Levi form $\sum_{j,k=1}^{n} \frac{\partial^2 \varphi}{\partial z_j \partial \overline{z}_k}(\mathfrak{z}_0) w_j \overline{w}_k \geqslant 0$ for all $w$ with $\sum_{j=1}^{n} \frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial z_j}(\mathfrak{z}_0) w_j = 0$.
