Let $(M, \varepsilon_1, A^{\bullet})$, $(M, \varepsilon_2, B^{\bullet})$ be two augmented cochain complexes. Let there be given a double complex $(C_{\nu\mu}, d', d'')$ and homomorphisms $d'_j: A^j \rightarrow C_{0j}$ and $d'_i: B^i \rightarrow C_{i0}$ such that

(1) $d'_0 \circ \varepsilon_2 = d'_0 \circ \varepsilon_1$, $d'' \circ d'_j = d'_{j+1} \circ d$ and $d' \circ d'_i = d'_{i+1} \circ d$, where $d$ denotes the operation in $A^{\bullet}$ and $B^{\bullet}$;

(2) $(A^j, d'_j, C_{\bullet j})$ and $(B^i, d'_i, C_{i\bullet})$ are augmented cochain complexes.

Then $H^j(A^{\bullet}) \simeq H_{0j}$ and $H^i(B^{\bullet}) \simeq H_{i0}$, where $H_{ij}$ denotes the cohomology of the double complex at position $(i,j)$.
