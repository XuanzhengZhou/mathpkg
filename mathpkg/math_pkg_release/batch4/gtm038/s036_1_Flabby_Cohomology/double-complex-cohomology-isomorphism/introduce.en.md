This theorem establishes an isomorphism between the cohomology of augmented cochain complexes and the "edge" cohomology of an associated double complex. Given two augmented complexes equipped with compatible maps into a double complex, the cohomology of each original complex is isomorphic to the cohomology along the corresponding edge ($j=0$ or $i=0$) of the double complex. This is the key algebraic mechanism underlying the comparison between Čech cohomology and derived functor (sheaf) cohomology via the canonical double complex.
