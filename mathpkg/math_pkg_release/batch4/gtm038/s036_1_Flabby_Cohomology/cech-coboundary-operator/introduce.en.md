The Čech coboundary operator $\delta^{\ell}$ extends local sections via alternating sums with omitted indices, mirroring the simplicial coboundary formula. Together with the augmentation map $\varepsilon$, which embeds global sections into $0$-cochains, this defines the Čech complex $C^{\bullet}(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S})$. The cohomology of this complex is the Čech cohomology $H^{\bullet}(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S})$ of the covering $\mathfrak{U}$ with values in the sheaf $\mathcal{S}$.
