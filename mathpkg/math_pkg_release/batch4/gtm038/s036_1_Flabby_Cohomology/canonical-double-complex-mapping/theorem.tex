For the canonical double complex $(C_{ij}, d', d'')$ of $(X, \mathcal{S}, \mathfrak{U})$, where $C_{ij} = C^j(\mathfrak{U}, \mathcal{S}_i)$ with $d'$ induced by the flabby resolution differential and $d''$ the Čech coboundary, there exist well-defined $R$-module homomorphisms
$$\varphi_{ij} : H_{ij} \rightarrow H_{i-1,j+1} \quad (i \geqslant 1)$$
$$\psi_{ij} : H_{i,j+1} \rightarrow H_{i+1,j} \quad (j \geqslant 1)$$
where $H_{ij}$ denotes the cohomology of the double complex at position $(i,j)$. These maps satisfy $\psi_{i-1,j+1} \circ \varphi_{ij} = \varphi_{i,j+1} \circ \psi_{ij}$.
