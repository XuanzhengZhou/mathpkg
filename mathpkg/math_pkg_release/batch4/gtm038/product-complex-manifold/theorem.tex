Let $X_1, \ldots, X_\ell$ be complex manifolds. Then the Cartesian product
$$X := X_1 \times \cdots \times X_\ell$$
equipped with the product topology and the complex atlas formed by products of local coordinate maps is an $n$-dimensional complex manifold, where $n = \sum_{i=1}^\ell \dim X_i$. Moreover, the natural projections $p_i: X \to X_i$ are holomorphic mappings.
