The Cartesian product of complex manifolds is a fundamental construction that yields another complex manifold. The manifold structure is defined using the product topology with basis consisting of products of open sets from each factor, and complex charts given by the product of the individual coordinate isomorphisms. The holomorphy of the transition maps follows from the fact that the product of holomorphic maps is holomorphic. The canonical projections onto each factor are holomorphic, and any holomorphic function on a factor pulls back to a holomorphic function on the product.
