Let $(S_1, \pi_1), \ldots, (S_\ell, \pi_\ell)$ be sheaves over $B$. For open sets $W \subset B$ let $M_W := \Gamma(W, S_1) \times \cdots \times \Gamma(W, S_\ell)$, for $s = (s_1, \ldots, s_\ell) \in M_W$ and open subsets $V \subset W$ let $r_V^W s := (s_1|V, \ldots, s_\ell|V) \in M_V$. Then $\{M_W, r_V^W\}$ is a pre-sheaf and the corresponding sheaf $S = S_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus S_\ell$ is called the Whitney sum of the sheaves $S_1, \ldots, S_\ell$.
