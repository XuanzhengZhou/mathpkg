Let $P \subset \mathbb{C}$ be a region with piecewise smooth boundary $T = \partial P$, and let $g$ be continuously differentiable on $P$ with $f := g_{\bar{z}}$ bounded. Then for $w \in P$,
$$g(w) = \operatorname{ch}(g|T)(w) + \operatorname{Ch}_f^{(P)}(w),$$
where
$$\operatorname{ch}(g|T)(w) := \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_T \frac{g(z)}{z-w}\,dz,$$
$$\operatorname{Ch}_f^{(P)}(w) := \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_P \frac{f(z)}{z-w}\,dz \wedge d\bar{z}.$$
