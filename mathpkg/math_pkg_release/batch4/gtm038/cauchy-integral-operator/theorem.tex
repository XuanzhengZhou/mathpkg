Let $P$ be a polycylinder and $T$ the corresponding $n$-dimensional torus. Let $f$ be a continuous function on $T$. Then the continuous function $\operatorname{ch}(f): P \to \mathbb{C}$ is defined by
$$\operatorname{ch}(f)(z) := \left(\frac{1}{2\pi i}\right)^n \int_{T} \frac{f(\zeta)}{(\zeta_1 - z_1) \cdots (\zeta_n - z_n)} d\zeta_1 \cdots d\zeta_n$$
for $z \in P$.
