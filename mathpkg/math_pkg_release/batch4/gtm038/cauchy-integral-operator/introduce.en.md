The Cauchy integral operator $\operatorname{ch}(f)$ generalizes the one-variable Cauchy integral formula to polycylinders in $\mathbb{C}^n$. Given a continuous function on the distinguished boundary (an 569JNRXghkldimensional torus), it produces a continuous function on the interior of the polycylinder.
