Let $\Omega$ be any set of linear transformations on a finite-dimensional vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ over a field. Then there exists a composition series for $\Omega$ on $\mathfrak{R}$, i.e., a chain of invariant subspaces
$$0 = \mathfrak{S}_0 \subset \mathfrak{S}_1 \subset \cdots \subset \mathfrak{S}_t = \mathfrak{R}$$
such that each $\mathfrak{S}_i$ is irreducible over $\mathfrak{S}_{i-1}$ relative to $\Omega$.
