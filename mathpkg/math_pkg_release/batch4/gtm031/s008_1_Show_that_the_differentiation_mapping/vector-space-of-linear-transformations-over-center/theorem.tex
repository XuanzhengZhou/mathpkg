Let $\Phi$ be the center of the division ring $\Delta$. Define scalar multiplication of $A \in \mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_2)$ by $\gamma \in \Phi$ as $\gamma A = \gamma_l \circ A$, where $\gamma_l$ is left multiplication by $\gamma$. Then $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_2)$ is a vector space over $\Phi$ with respect to this scalar multiplication and pointwise addition.
