The additive inverse $-A$ of a linear transformation $A$ maps each vector $x$ to the negative of its image under $A$. It is itself a linear transformation, and satisfies $A + (-A) = 0$, where $0$ is the zero transformation. This completes the verification that $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_2)$ is a group under addition.
