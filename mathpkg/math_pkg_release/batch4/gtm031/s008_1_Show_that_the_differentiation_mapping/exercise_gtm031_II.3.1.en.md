Let $\mathfrak{R}$ be the vector space of polynomials of degree $< n$ with real coefficients and let $D$ denote the differentiation operator. Show that $D$ is nilpotent in the sense that $D^n = 0$ (the $n$-th power of $D$ is the zero transformation), while $D^{n-1} \neq 0$.
