Let $\mathfrak{R}_1$ and $\mathfrak{R}_2$ be finite-dimensional vector spaces over $\Delta$ with $\dim \mathfrak{R}_1 = n_1$ and $\dim \mathfrak{R}_2 = n_2$. Then the correspondence $A \mapsto (\alpha)$ that sends each linear transformation to its matrix relative to fixed bases $(e_1, \dots, e_{n_1})$ and $(f_1, \dots, f_{n_2})$ is a bijection between $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_2)$ and the set of all $n_1 \times n_2$ matrices with entries in $\Delta$.
