Let $\mathfrak{R}$ and $\mathfrak{S}$ be vector spaces over a division ring $\Delta$ with bases $(e_i)_{i \in I}$ and $(f_j)_{j \in J}$ respectively. The matrix $(\alpha_{ij})$ of a linear transformation $A: \mathfrak{R} \to \mathfrak{S}$ defined by
$$e_i A = \sum_j \alpha_{ij} f_j$$
is \textit{row-finite}: for each fixed $i$, only finitely many $\alpha_{ij}$ are non-zero. The set of all $I \times J$ row-finite matrices is denoted by $M_{I \times J}^{\text{rf}}(\Delta)$.
