The submodule generated by a subset $S$ of a module $\mathfrak{R}$, denoted $[S]$, consists of all finite linear combinations of elements of $S$ with coefficients in the ring $\mathfrak{o}$. It is characterized as the intersection of all submodules containing $S$, and is the smallest submodule with this property.
