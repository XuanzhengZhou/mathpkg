A \textbf{right vector space} over a division ring $\Delta$ is a system consisting of a commutative group $\mathfrak{R}'$, a division ring $\Delta$, and a function of pairs $(\rho, x')$, $\rho \in \Delta$, $x' \in \mathfrak{R}'$, having values $x'\rho \in \mathfrak{R}'$ and satisfying:

$$
\text{S}'1 \quad (x' + y')\alpha = x'\alpha + y'\alpha,
$$
$$
\text{S}'2 \quad x'(\alpha + \beta) = x'\alpha + x'\beta,
$$
$$
\text{S}'3 \quad x'(\alpha\beta) = (x'\alpha)\beta,
$$
$$
\text{S}'4 \quad x'1 = x' \quad \text{for all } x' \in \mathfrak{R}'.
$$
