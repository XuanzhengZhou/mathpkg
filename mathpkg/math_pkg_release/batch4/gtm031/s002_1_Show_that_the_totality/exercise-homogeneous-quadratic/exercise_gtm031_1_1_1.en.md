Show that the totality $\mathfrak{S}$ of homogeneous quadratic polynomials

$$
\sum_{i \leq j} \alpha_{ij} \lambda_i \lambda_j, \quad \alpha_{ij} \in \Delta,
$$

is a finite dimensional subspace of $\Delta[\lambda_1, \lambda_2]$.
