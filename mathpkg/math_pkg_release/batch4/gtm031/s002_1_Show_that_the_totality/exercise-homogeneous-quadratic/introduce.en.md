This exercise asks the reader to verify that the set of all homogeneous quadratic polynomials in two variables forms a finite-dimensional subspace of the polynomial ring. A basis can be given by $\{\lambda_1^2, \lambda_1\lambda_2, \lambda_2^2\}$, showing the dimension is $3$.
