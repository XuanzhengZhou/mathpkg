Every real orthogonal matrix is conjugate in the group of orthogonal matrices to a block diagonal matrix
$$\begin{bmatrix}
(\beta_1) & & \\
& (\beta_2) & \\
& & \ddots
\end{bmatrix}$$
in which each $(\beta_i)$ is either a one-rowed matrix $(\pm 1)$ or a two-rowed rotation matrix of the form
$$\begin{bmatrix} \cos \theta_i & \sin \theta_i \\ -\sin \theta_i & \cos \theta_i \end{bmatrix}.$$
