Show that the subset of elements of the form $1' \times B$ is a subring of $\mathfrak{M}$ isomorphic to $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{S}, \mathfrak{S})$ and that the subset of elements of the form $A' \times 1$ is a subring of $\mathfrak{M}$ isomorphic to $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}', \mathfrak{R}')$.
