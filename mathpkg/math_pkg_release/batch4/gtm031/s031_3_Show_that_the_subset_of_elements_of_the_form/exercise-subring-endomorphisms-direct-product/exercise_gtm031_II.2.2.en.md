Let $\mathfrak{P} = \mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{S}$ and let $\mathfrak{M}$ be the totality of endomorphisms $\sum A_i' \times B_i$ where $A_i'$ is a linear transformation in $\mathfrak{R}'$ and $B_i$ is one in $\mathfrak{S}$. Show that $\mathfrak{M}$ is a subring of the ring of endomorphisms of $\mathfrak{P} = \mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{S}$.
