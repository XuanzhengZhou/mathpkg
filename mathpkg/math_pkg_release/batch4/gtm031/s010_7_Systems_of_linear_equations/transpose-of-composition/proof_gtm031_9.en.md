For $h \in \mathfrak{R}_3^*$, we have $h(AC)^* = (AC)h$ by definition of the transpose. Then
$$
h(AC)^* = (AC)h = A(Ch) = A(hC^*) = (hC^*)A^* = h(C^* A^*).
$$
Since this holds for all $h \in \mathfrak{R}_3^*$, we conclude $(AC)^* = C^* A^*$.
