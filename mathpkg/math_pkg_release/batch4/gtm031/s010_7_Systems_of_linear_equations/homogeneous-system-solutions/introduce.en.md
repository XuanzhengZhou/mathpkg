Theorem 8 describes the structure of the solution space for a homogeneous system of linear equations. The dimension (nullity) of the solution space equals $n - \rho$, where $n$ is the number of unknowns and $\rho$ is the row rank of the coefficient matrix. Every solution is a left linear combination of $n - \rho$ linearly independent fundamental solutions.
