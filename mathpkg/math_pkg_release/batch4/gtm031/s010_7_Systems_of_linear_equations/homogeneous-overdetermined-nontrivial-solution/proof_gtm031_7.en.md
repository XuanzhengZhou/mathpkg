This is an immediate consequence of Theorem 8. If the system has $m$ equations in $n > m$ unknowns, then the row rank $\rho$ of the coefficient matrix satisfies $\rho \leq m < n$. By Theorem 8, the solution space has dimension $n - \rho \geq n - m > 0$, so there exists a non-trivial solution.
