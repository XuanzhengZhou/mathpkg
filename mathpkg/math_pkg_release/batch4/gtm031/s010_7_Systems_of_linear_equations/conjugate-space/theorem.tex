The set $\mathfrak{R}^*$ of all linear functions on a left vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ over a division ring $\Delta$ is a right vector space over $\Delta$ with operations
$$
(f + g)(x) = f(x) + g(x), \quad (f\mu)(x) = f(x)\mu,
$$
for $f, g \in \mathfrak{R}^*$, $\mu \in \Delta$. This space is called the conjugate space of $\mathfrak{R}$.
