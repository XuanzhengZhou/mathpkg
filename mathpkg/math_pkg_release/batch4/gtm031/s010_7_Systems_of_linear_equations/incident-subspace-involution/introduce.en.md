This proposition establishes that the incident subspace map $j$ is an involution (its own inverse) and defines a perfect duality between the subspace lattices of $\mathfrak{R}$ and $\mathfrak{R}^*$. As an order-reversing bijection, it interchanges sums and intersections: $\overline{\mathfrak{S}_1 + \mathfrak{S}_2} = \overline{\mathfrak{S}_1} \cap \overline{\mathfrak{S}_2}$ and $\overline{\mathfrak{S}_1 \cap \mathfrak{S}_2} = \overline{\mathfrak{S}_1} + \overline{\mathfrak{S}_2}$.
