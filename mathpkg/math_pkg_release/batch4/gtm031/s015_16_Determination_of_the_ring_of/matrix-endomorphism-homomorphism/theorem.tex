Let $\mathfrak{R} = \{f_1\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_t\}$ with order ideals $(\delta_i)$, and let $\mathfrak{M}$ be the subring of $\mathfrak{o}_t$ of matrices satisfying $\delta_i \beta_{ij} \equiv 0 \pmod{\delta_j}$. For $(\beta) \in \mathfrak{M}$, define $B \in \mathfrak{B}$ by
$$f_i B = \sum_{j=1}^{t} \beta_{ij} f_j, \quad i = 1, \ldots, t.$$
Then the correspondence $(\beta) \mapsto B$ is a ring homomorphism from $\mathfrak{M}$ onto $\mathfrak{B}$.
