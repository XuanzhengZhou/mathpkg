When an $\mathfrak{o}$-endomorphism $B$ acts on the generators $f_i$ of a finitely generated module over a principal ideal domain, the image $g_i = f_i B$ has an order ideal that necessarily divides the order ideal of $f_i$. This follows from the fact that $\delta_i f_i = 0$ and applying $B$ yields $\delta_i g_i = 0$, so the annihilator of $g_i$ contains $\delta_i$.
