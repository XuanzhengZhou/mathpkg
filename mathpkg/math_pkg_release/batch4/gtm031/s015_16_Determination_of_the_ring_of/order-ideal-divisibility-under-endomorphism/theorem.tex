Let $\mathfrak{R} = \{f_1\} \oplus \{f_2\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_t\}$ be a finitely generated $\mathfrak{o}$-module with order ideals $\mathfrak{J}_i = (\delta_i)$, where $\delta_i \mid \delta_j$ for $i \leq j$. Let $B \in \mathfrak{B}$ be an $\mathfrak{o}$-endomorphism of $\mathfrak{R}$, and let $f_i B = g_i$. If $(\epsilon_i)$ is the order ideal of $g_i$, then
$$\epsilon_i \mid \delta_i.$$
Equivalently, $\delta_i g_i = 0$.
