The set $\mathfrak{M}$ of all matrices $(\beta)$ satisfying the endomorphism congruence condition $\delta_i \beta_{ij} \equiv 0 \pmod{\delta_j}$ forms a subring of the full $t \times t$ matrix ring $\mathfrak{o}_t$. This subring parametrizes the $\mathfrak{o}$-endomorphisms of $\mathfrak{R}$ via the matrix representation with respect to the cyclic generator decomposition.
