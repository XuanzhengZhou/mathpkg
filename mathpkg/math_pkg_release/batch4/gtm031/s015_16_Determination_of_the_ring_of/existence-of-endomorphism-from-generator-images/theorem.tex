Let $\mathfrak{R} = \{f_1\} \oplus \{f_2\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_t\}$ be a finitely generated $\mathfrak{o}$-module, $\mathfrak{o}$ a principal ideal domain, with order ideals $(\delta_i)$. Suppose that for each $i$, $g_i$ is an element of $\mathfrak{R}$ whose order ideal $(\epsilon_i)$ satisfies $\epsilon_i \mid \delta_i$. Define $B$ to be the mapping
$$\sum \xi_i f_i \mapsto \sum \xi_i g_i, \quad \xi_i \in \mathfrak{o}.$$
Then $B$ is an $\mathfrak{o}$-endomorphism of $\mathfrak{R}$, i.e., $B \in \mathfrak{B}$.
