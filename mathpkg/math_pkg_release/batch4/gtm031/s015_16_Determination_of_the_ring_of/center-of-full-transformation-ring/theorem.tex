The only linear transformations of a vector space over a field $\Phi$ that commute with every linear transformation are the scalar multiplications. Equivalently, if $\mathfrak{L}$ is the ring of all linear transformations of a vector space over $\Phi$, then the center of $\mathfrak{L}$ is $\Phi_1 = \{\alpha 1 : \alpha \in \Phi\}$.
