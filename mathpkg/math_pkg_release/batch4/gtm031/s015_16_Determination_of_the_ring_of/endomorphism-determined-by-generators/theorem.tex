Let $\mathfrak{R}$ be a finitely generated $\mathfrak{o}$-module, $\mathfrak{o}$ a principal ideal domain, with $\mathfrak{R} = \{f_1\} \oplus \{f_2\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_t\}$. Let $B \in \mathfrak{B}$, the ring of $\mathfrak{o}$-endomorphisms of $\mathfrak{R}$, and suppose $f_i B = g_i$. Then for any $x = \sum \xi_i f_i \in \mathfrak{R}$ with $\xi_i \in \mathfrak{o}$,
$$
x B = \left(\sum \xi_i f_i\right) B = \sum \xi_i (f_i B) = \sum \xi_i g_i.
$$
Thus $B$ is completely determined by its effect on the generators $f_i$ of $\mathfrak{R}$.
