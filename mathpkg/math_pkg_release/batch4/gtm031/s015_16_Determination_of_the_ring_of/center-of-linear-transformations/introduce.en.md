Corollary 2 of Theorem 20 restates the result in the classical language of linear algebra: the center of the ring of all linear transformations on a vector space over a field $\Phi$ is precisely the set $\Phi_1$ of scalar multiples of the identity transformation. This is an important structural fact about the full transformation ring, emphasizing that the only operators that universally commute are the scalars.
