Let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a subspace of a finite-dimensional vector space $\mathfrak{R}$. Let $(f_1, f_2, \cdots, f_r)$ be a basis for $\mathfrak{S}$. Extend this to a basis $(f_1, f_2, \cdots, f_r, f_{r+1}, \cdots, f_n)$ for $\mathfrak{R}$. Then the cosets
$$
\bar{f}_{r+1}, \bar{f}_{r+2}, \cdots, \bar{f}_n
$$
form a basis for the factor space $\bar{\mathfrak{R}} = \mathfrak{R} / \mathfrak{S}$. Consequently,
$$
\dim(\mathfrak{R} / \mathfrak{S}) = \dim \mathfrak{R} - \dim \mathfrak{S}.
$$
