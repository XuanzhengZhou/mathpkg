In the lattice of subspaces of a vector space, the intersection of any two subspaces $\mathfrak{S}_1 \cap \mathfrak{S}_2$ is again a subspace and serves as the greatest lower bound (meet) under inclusion. Any subspace contained in both $\mathfrak{S}_1$ and $\mathfrak{S}_2$ is necessarily contained in their intersection, making intersection the largest lower bound.
