A subspace $\mathfrak{S}^*$ of a vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ is called a \textbf{complement} of the subspace $\mathfrak{S}$ in $\mathfrak{R}$ if
$$
\mathfrak{S} \cap \mathfrak{S}^* = 0 \quad \text{and} \quad \mathfrak{S} + \mathfrak{S}^* = \mathfrak{R}.
$$
Equivalently, $\mathfrak{R} = \mathfrak{S} \oplus \mathfrak{S}^*$ is a direct sum.
