In the lattice $L$ of all subspaces of a vector space $\mathfrak{R}$, the whole space $\mathfrak{R}$ acts as the all element (top element). Specifically, for every subspace $\mathfrak{S}$:
$$
\mathfrak{R} + \mathfrak{S} = \mathfrak{R} = \mathfrak{S} + \mathfrak{R}
$$
and
$$
\mathfrak{R} \cap \mathfrak{S} = \mathfrak{S} = \mathfrak{S} \cap \mathfrak{R}.
$$
