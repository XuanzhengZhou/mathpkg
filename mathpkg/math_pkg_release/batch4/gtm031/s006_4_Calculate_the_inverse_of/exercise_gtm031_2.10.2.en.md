Prove that if $\mathfrak{R} = \mathfrak{R}_1 \oplus \mathfrak{R}_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_r$ and each $\mathfrak{R}_i = \mathfrak{R}_{i1} \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_{in_i}$, then

$$
\mathfrak{R} = \mathfrak{R}_{11} \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_{1n_1} \oplus \mathfrak{R}_{21} \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_{2n_2} \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_{r1} \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_{rn_r}.
$$
