Let $A \in \mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}, \mathfrak{R})$ be a linear transformation on a finite-dimensional vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ over a field $\Phi$. Let $m$ be the first integer such that $A^m$ is linearly dependent on $1, A, \dots, A^{m-1}$, and let
$$A^m = \mu_0 1 + \mu_1 A + \cdots + \mu_{m-1} A^{m-1}$$
with $\mu_i \in \Phi$. Then the polynomial
$$\mu(\lambda) = \lambda^m - \mu_{m-1} \lambda^{m-1} - \mu_{m-2} \lambda^{m-2} - \cdots - \mu_0$$
is the minimum polynomial of $A$. In particular, $\mu(A) = 0$ and $\mu(\lambda)$ is the monic polynomial of least degree with this property.
