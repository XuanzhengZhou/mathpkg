A unitary linear transformation is the complex analogue of an orthogonal transformation. It preserves the hermitian scalar product and satisfies $A'A = AA' = 1$. The characteristic roots of a unitary transformation all have absolute value 1. Unitary transformations form the unitary group $U(\Phi, n)$.
