This lemma is a fundamental property of unitary transformations: invariance of a subspace implies invariance of its orthogonal complement. This allows the direct spectral decomposition of unitary operators using induction on dimension, similar to the hermitian case. The proof relies on the fact that a unitary transformation is invertible and preserves the hermitian scalar product.
