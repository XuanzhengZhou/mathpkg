This proposition establishes a key spectral property of unitary transformations: all eigenvalues have absolute value 1. The proof follows directly from the defining property $A'A = 1$ of unitary transformations, which preserves the hermitian scalar product. This is analogous to the fact that eigenvalues of real orthogonal matrices lie on the unit circle.
