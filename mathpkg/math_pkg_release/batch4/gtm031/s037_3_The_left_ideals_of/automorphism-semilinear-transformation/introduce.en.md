This corollary of Theorem 8 characterizes all automorphisms of the ring $\mathfrak{L}$ of linear transformations. Every such automorphism is induced by conjugation by a semilinear transformation of the underlying vector space onto itself. This result is fundamental for understanding the structure and symmetries of the full ring of linear transformations.
