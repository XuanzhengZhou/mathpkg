There exists a lattice isomorphism from the lattice of subspaces of the vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ onto the lattice of left ideals of the ring of linear transformations $\mathfrak{L}$, given by the correspondence $\mathfrak{S} \mapsto \mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{S}$, where $\mathfrak{R}'$ is the dual space of $\mathfrak{R}$. This correspondence preserves order: if $\mathfrak{S}_1 \supseteq \mathfrak{S}_2$, then $\mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{S}_1 \supseteq \mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{S}_2$.
