Let $\mathfrak{R}$ be a finite-dimensional vector space over a division ring $\Delta$ with an involutorial anti-automorphism, and let $g$ be a non-degenerate hermitian scalar product on $\mathfrak{R}$ with $\dim \mathfrak{R} = n$. Then for any subspace $\mathfrak{S} \subseteq \mathfrak{R}$:

1. $\dim \mathfrak{S}^{\perp} = n - \dim \mathfrak{S}$.
2. $\mathfrak{S}^{\perp\perp} = \mathfrak{S}$.
3. The mapping $\mathfrak{S} \mapsto \mathfrak{S}^{\perp}$ is an involutorial anti-automorphism of the lattice of subspaces of $\mathfrak{R}$ onto itself.
