The **trace** of an $n \times n$ matrix $(\alpha) = (\alpha_{ij})$ over a commutative ring is defined as
$$\operatorname{tr}(\alpha) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \alpha_{ii}.$$
For matrices over a field, the trace equals the sum of the characteristic roots (counted with multiplicity) and is invariant under similarity: $\operatorname{tr}(\mu \alpha \mu^{-1}) = \operatorname{tr}(\alpha)$.
