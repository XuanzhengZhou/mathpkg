Let $\mathfrak{R} = \{x\}$ be a cyclic $\mathfrak{o}$-module with order ideal $\mathfrak{J}_x = (\mu)$. Then there is an isomorphism of rings:
$$\operatorname{End}_{\mathfrak{o}}(\mathfrak{R}) \cong \mathfrak{o} / (\mu).$$
An endomorphism $\phi$ is determined by its value $\phi(x) = \alpha x$ for a unique coset $\alpha + (\mu) \in \mathfrak{o} / (\mu)$.
