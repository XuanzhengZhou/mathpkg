Let $\mathfrak{o}$ be a principal ideal domain. Two $m \times n$ matrices with elements in $\mathfrak{o}$ are said to be \text{equivalent} if there exist a unit $(\mu)$ in $\mathfrak{o}_n$ and a unit $(\nu)$ in $\mathfrak{o}_m$ such that

$$(\tau) = (\nu)(\sigma)(\mu).$$

This relation is an equivalence relation.
