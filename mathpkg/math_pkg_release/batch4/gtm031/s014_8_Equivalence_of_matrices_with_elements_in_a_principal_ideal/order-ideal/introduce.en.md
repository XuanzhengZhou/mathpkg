The order ideal of an element $x$ in a module over a commutative ring $\mathfrak{o}$ is the annihilator ideal $\{\beta \in \mathfrak{o} : \beta x = 0\}$. Over a principal ideal domain, this ideal is principal, generated by an element $\mu_x$ called the order of $x$. In the special case of a $\Phi[\lambda]$-module determined by a linear transformation, the order corresponds to the minimal polynomial of the cyclic submodule generated by $x$.
