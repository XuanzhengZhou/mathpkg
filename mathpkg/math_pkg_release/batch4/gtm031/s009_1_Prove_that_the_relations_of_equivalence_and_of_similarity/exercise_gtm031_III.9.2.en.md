Prove that, if $\Delta$ has characteristic $0$, then the following two matrices are similar in $\Delta_n$:

$$
(\alpha) = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 \\
1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 \\
\cdot & \cdot & \cdots & \cdot \\
1 & 1 & \cdots & 1
\end{bmatrix}, \quad
(\beta) = \begin{bmatrix}
n & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
\cdot & \cdot & \cdots & \cdot \\
0 & 0 & \cdots & 0
\end{bmatrix}.
$$
