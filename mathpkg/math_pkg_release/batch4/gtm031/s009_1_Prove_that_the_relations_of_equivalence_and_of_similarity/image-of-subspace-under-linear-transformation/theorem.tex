If $A$ is a linear transformation of $\mathfrak{R}_1$ into $\mathfrak{R}_2$, and $\mathfrak{S}_1$ is a subspace of $\mathfrak{R}_1$, then the image $\mathfrak{S}_1 A$ consisting of all vectors of the form $x_1 A$, $x_1 \in \mathfrak{S}_1$, is a subspace of $\mathfrak{R}_2$. In the special case $\mathfrak{S}_1 = \mathfrak{R}_1$, the image $\mathfrak{R}_1 A$ is called the \text{rank space} of $A$.
