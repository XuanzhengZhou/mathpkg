This exercise asks to find the canonical form under cogredience for a $3 \times 3$ quaternionic hermitian matrix. Over the quaternions, Sylvester's theorem still holds: every hermitian matrix is cogredient to a diagonal matrix of the form $\operatorname{diag}(1, \dots, 1, -1, \dots, -1, 0, \dots, 0)$. The task is to determine the signature of the given matrix by finding its inertia.
