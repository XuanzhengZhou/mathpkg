This exercise asks to show that two specific $4 \times 4$ skew-symmetric matrices over the rational numbers are cogredient, and to find an explicit transformation matrix. Both matrices represent alternate scalar products, and the cogredience follows from the fact that they have the same rank (both are non-degenerate, rank 4). An explicit rational transformation matrix $(\mu)$ can be found by solving the system of equations $(\mu)(\alpha)(\mu)' = (\beta)$.
