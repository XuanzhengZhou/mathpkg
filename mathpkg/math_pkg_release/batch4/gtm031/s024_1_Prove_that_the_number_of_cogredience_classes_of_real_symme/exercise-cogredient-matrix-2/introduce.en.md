This exercise asks to find the canonical form under cogredience for a specific $3 \times 3$ real symmetric matrix. The form (34) refers to the diagonal canonical form $\operatorname{diag}(1, \dots, 1, -1, \dots, -1, 0, \dots, 0)$ given by Sylvester's theorem. The task is to compute the signature by finding the eigenvalues or performing simultaneous row and column operations.
