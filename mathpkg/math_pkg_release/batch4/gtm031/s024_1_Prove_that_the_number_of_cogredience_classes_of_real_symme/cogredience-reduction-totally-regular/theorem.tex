Any hermitian matrix is cogredient to a matrix of the form

$$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{bmatrix} \oplus B$$

where $B$ is a matrix of the scalar product restricted to a totally regular subspace. Two matrices of this form with totally regular $B$ are cogredient if and only if the corresponding $B$ matrices are cogredient.
