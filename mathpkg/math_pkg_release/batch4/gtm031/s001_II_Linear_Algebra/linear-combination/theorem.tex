Let $\mathfrak{R}$ be a vector space over a division ring $\Delta$. For vectors $v_1, v_2, \cdots, v_k \in \mathfrak{R}$ and scalars $\xi_1, \xi_2, \cdots, \xi_k \in \Delta$, the expression

$$\sum_{i=1}^{k} \xi_i v_i = \xi_1 v_1 + \xi_2 v_2 + \cdots + \xi_k v_k$$

is called a \emph{linear combination} of the vectors $v_1, \ldots, v_k$ with coefficients $\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_k$ (or \emph{scalar coefficients}).
