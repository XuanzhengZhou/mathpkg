If
$$0 \subset \mathfrak{R}_1 \subset \mathfrak{R}_2 \subset \cdots \subset \mathfrak{R}_t = \mathfrak{R}$$
and
$$0 \subset \mathfrak{S}_1 \subset \mathfrak{S}_2 \subset \cdots \subset \mathfrak{S}_u = \mathfrak{R}$$
are two composition series for a set $\Omega$ of linear transformations (i.e., maximal chains of $\Omega$-invariant subspaces with no proper $\Omega$-invariant intermediate subspaces), then $t = u$ and the factors of composition $\mathfrak{R}_i/\mathfrak{R}_{i-1}$ and $\mathfrak{S}_j/\mathfrak{S}_{j-1}$ can be put into 1-1 correspondence in such a way that corresponding pairs are $\Omega$-isomorphic.
