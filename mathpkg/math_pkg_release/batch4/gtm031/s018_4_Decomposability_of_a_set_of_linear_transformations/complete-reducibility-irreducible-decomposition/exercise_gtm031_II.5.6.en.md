*Relation to the theory of operator groups and the theory of modules.* The theory of sets of linear transformations that we are considering can be viewed through the lens of operator groups and module theory. Investigate the connection between invariants subspaces under $\Omega$ and submodules over the enveloping ring $\mathfrak{o}(\Omega)$.
