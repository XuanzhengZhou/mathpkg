This classification theorem states that every proper two-sided ideal in the ring of all linear transformations of an infinite dimensional vector space R is of the form L_e, consisting of all transformations whose rank is less than some infinite cardinal e <= dim R. These ideals are totally ordered by inclusion according to the cardinal bound.
