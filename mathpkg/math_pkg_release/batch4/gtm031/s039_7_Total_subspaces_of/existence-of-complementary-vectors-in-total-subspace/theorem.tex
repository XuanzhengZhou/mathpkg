\textbf{Lemma.} If $\mathfrak{R}'$ is a total subspace of $\mathfrak{R}^*$ and $x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_m$ are any linearly independent vectors in $\mathfrak{R}$, then $\mathfrak{R}'$ contains a set of vectors $x_1^*, \cdots, x_m^*$ complementary to the $x_i$; that is, $x_i^*(x_j) = \delta_{ij}$ for $i, j = 1, \ldots, m$.
