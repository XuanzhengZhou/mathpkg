**Proof.** Choose a basis for $\ker A$ and extend it to a basis of $\mathfrak{R}$. The images of the extending basis vectors form a basis of $\operatorname{Im} A$. Counting dimensions yields $\dim \ker A + \dim \operatorname{Im} A = \dim \mathfrak{R}$.
