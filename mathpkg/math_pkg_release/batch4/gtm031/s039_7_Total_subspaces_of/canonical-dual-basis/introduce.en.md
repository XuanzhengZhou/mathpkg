The canonical dual basis (e_i^*) of the column-finite subspace R_0^* is defined by the Kronecker delta condition e_i^*(e_k) = delta_{ik}. Each e_i^* has a matrix with 1 in the i-th position and zeros elsewhere, forming a natural basis dual to the original basis (e_i) of R.
