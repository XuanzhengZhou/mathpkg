The Kronecker product (tensor product) of arbitrary vector spaces over a field is defined using duality: given dual spaces R and R' and a left vector space S, the Kronecker product R' times S is the space of linear transformations from R to S of the form x -> sum g(x, x_k') y_k. This extends the finite-dimensional construction to arbitrary vector spaces.
