**Proof.** Write $u = \beta_1 e_{i_1} + \beta_2 e_{i_2} + \cdots + \beta_m e_{i_m}$ where $\beta_1 \neq 0$. Then $e_{i_1}^*(u) = \beta_1 \neq 0$ as required. Since $e_{i_1}^* \in \mathfrak{R}_0^*$, this verifies that $\mathfrak{R}_0^*$ separates every non-zero vector.
