Let $\mathfrak{R}$ have a denumerable basis and let $\mathfrak{R}'$ be a total subspace of $\mathfrak{R}^*$. Show that there exists a basis $(u_i)$ for $\mathfrak{R}$ which has a complementary set of vectors $(u_i^*)$ in $\mathfrak{R}'$ in the sense that $u_i^*(u_j) = \delta_{ij}$ holds for $i, j = 1, 2, \cdots$.
