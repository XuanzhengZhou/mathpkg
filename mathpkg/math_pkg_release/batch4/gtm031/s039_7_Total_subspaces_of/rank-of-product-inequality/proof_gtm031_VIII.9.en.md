**Proof.** $\operatorname{Im}(AB) = A(\operatorname{Im} B) \subseteq \operatorname{Im} A$, so $\rho(AB) \leq \rho(A)$. Also $\rho(AB) = \dim A(\operatorname{Im} B) \leq \dim \operatorname{Im} B = \rho(B)$, since a linear map cannot increase dimension. Hence $\rho(AB) \leq \min(\rho(A), \rho(B))$.
