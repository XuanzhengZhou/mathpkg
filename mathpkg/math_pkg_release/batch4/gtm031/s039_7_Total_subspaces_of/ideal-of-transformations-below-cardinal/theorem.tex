\textbf{Definition.} Let $\mathfrak{L}$ be the ring of linear transformations of an infinite dimensional vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ with $b = \dim \mathfrak{R}$. Let $e$ be any infinite cardinal such that $e \leq b$, and let $\mathfrak{L}_e$ be the set of linear transformations $A$ such that $\rho(A) < e$. Then $\mathfrak{L}_e$ is a two-sided ideal in $\mathfrak{L}$.
