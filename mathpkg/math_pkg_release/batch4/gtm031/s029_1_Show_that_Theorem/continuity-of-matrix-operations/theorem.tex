If $(\alpha^{(k)}) \rightarrow (\alpha)$ and $(\beta^{(k)}) \rightarrow (\beta)$, then

$$(\alpha^{(k)}) + (\beta^{(k)}) \rightarrow (\alpha) + (\beta)$$

and

$$(\alpha^{(k)})(\beta^{(k)}) \rightarrow (\alpha)(\beta).$$

Moreover, if $(\alpha^{(k)}) \rightarrow (\alpha)$ and $(\mu)$ is non-singular, then

$$(\mu)(\alpha^{(k)})(\mu)^{-1} \rightarrow (\mu)(\alpha)(\mu)^{-1}.$$
