Let $\gamma_0 + \gamma_1 \lambda + \gamma_2 \lambda^2 + \cdots$ be an ordinary power series with radius of convergence $r$. Then the power series

$$\gamma_0 1 + \gamma_1 A + \gamma_2 A^2 + \cdots$$

converges for every linear transformation $A$ whose characteristic roots $\rho_i$ satisfy $|\rho_i| < r$.
