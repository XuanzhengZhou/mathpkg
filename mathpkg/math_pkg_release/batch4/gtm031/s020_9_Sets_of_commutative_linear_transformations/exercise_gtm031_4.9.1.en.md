Let $\Phi$ be the field of complex numbers and let

$$(\alpha) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 \end{bmatrix}, \qquad
(\beta) = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 1 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 & 3 \end{bmatrix}.$$

Verify that $(\alpha)(\beta) = (\beta)(\alpha)$ and illustrate Theorem 7 with this pair of matrices.
