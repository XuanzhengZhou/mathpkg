If $\Phi$ is the field of rational numbers, find a diagonal matrix $(\beta)$ cogredient to

$$(\alpha) = \begin{bmatrix}
-2 & 3 & 5 \\
3 & 1 & -1 \\
5 & -1 & 4
\end{bmatrix}.$$

Also find a non-singular matrix $(\mu)$ such that $(\mu)(\alpha)(\mu)' = (\beta)$.
