This proposition follows immediately from the definition of multiplication in the Kronecker product algebra: $(x_1 \times x_2)(y_1 \times y_2) = x_1y_1 \times x_2y_2$. The associativity of the product in $\mathfrak{A}_1 \times \mathfrak{A}_2$ is a direct consequence of associativity in each factor, since the multiplication is performed componentwise.
