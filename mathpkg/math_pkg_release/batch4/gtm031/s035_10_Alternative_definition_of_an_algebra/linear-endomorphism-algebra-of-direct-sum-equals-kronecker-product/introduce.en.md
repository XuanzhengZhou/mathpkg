Theorem 6 establishes that the algebra of linear transformations on the Kronecker product of two vector spaces is isomorphic, as an algebra, to the Kronecker product of the endomorphism algebras of the individual spaces. This is a structural result showing that the Kronecker product of algebras correctly captures the algebra structure of linear transformations on tensor product spaces. For finite-dimensional spaces, this isomorphism respects both the additive and multiplicative (composition) structures.
