Let $A: \mathfrak{R}_1 \to \mathfrak{R}_2$ be a linear transformation, and let $g_i$ be non-degenerate bilinear forms pairing $\mathfrak{R}_i$ with $\mathfrak{R}_i'$ for $i = 1, 2$. The transpose $A': \mathfrak{R}_2' \to \mathfrak{R}_1'$ is uniquely characterized by the relation

$$g_1(x_1, y_2' A') = g_2(x_1 A, y_2')$$

for all $x_1 \in \mathfrak{R}_1$ and $y_2' \in \mathfrak{R}_2'$.
