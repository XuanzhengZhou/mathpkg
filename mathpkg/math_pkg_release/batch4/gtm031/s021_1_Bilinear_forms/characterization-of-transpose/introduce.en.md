The transpose $A'$ is characterized by a fundamental adjoint relation: applying the bilinear form $g_1$ to $x_1$ and the image of $y_2'$ under $A'$ yields the same result as applying $g_2$ to the image of $x_1$ under $A$ and $y_2'$. This relation is the natural generalization of the defining property of the transpose in the dual space setting, where $(f A^*)(x) = f(A x)$. It expresses the compatibility of $A$ and $A'$ with the given bilinear pairings.
