The transpose operation $A \mapsto A'$ is an additive homomorphism between spaces of linear transformations, and it is contravariant with respect to composition: the transpose of a product is the product of the transposes in reverse order. These properties follow directly from the corresponding properties of the usual transpose $A^*$ between dual spaces, transferred through the isomorphisms $R_i$ that identify each space with the dual of its paired space.
