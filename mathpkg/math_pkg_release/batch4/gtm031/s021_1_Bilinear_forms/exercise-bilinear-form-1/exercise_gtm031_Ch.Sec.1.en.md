Show that if $g(x, y')$ is a bilinear form on $\mathfrak{R} \times \mathfrak{R}'$ and $A$ is a linear transformation in $\mathfrak{R}$, then $h(x, y') = g(xA, y')$ is a bilinear form on $\mathfrak{R} \times \mathfrak{R}'$.
