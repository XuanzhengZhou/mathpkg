Let $\Re$ be a vector space over a division ring $\Delta$ and let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a subspace of $\Re$. The **natural mapping** (or canonical projection) $P$ of $\Re$ onto the quotient space $\bar{\Re} = \Re / \mathfrak{S}$ is defined by

$$P: x \mapsto \bar{x} = x + \mathfrak{S}, \quad x \in \Re.$$

The mapping $P$ is a linear transformation of $\Re$ onto $\bar{\Re}$ over $\Delta$.
