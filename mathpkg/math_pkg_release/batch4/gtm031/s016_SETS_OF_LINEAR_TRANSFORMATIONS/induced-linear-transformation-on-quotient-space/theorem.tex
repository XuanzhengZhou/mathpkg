Let $\Re$ be a vector space over a division ring $\Delta$ and let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a subspace invariant under a linear transformation $A \in \Omega$.

Define the quotient space $\bar{\Re} = \Re / \mathfrak{S}$ whose elements are the cosets $\bar{x} = x + \mathfrak{S}$.  The **induced linear transformation** $\bar{A}$ on $\bar{\Re}$ is defined by

$$\bar{x} \bar{A} = \overline{xA},$$

i.e., $(\bar{x})\bar{A} = (x + \mathfrak{S})\bar{A} = xA + \mathfrak{S}$.  This mapping is well-defined (single-valued) and is a linear transformation of $\bar{\Re}$ over $\Delta$.
