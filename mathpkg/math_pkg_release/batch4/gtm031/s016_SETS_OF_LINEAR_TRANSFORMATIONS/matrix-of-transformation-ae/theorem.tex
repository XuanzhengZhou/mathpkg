Let $\Re$ be a vector space with basis $(f_1, \ldots, f_n)$ chosen as before so that $(f_1, \ldots, f_r)$ is a basis for an invariant subspace $\mathfrak{S}$ under $A \in \Omega$. Let the matrix of $A$ relative to this basis have the reduced form

$$\begin{bmatrix}
A_{11} & 0 \\
A_{21} & A_{22}
\end{bmatrix}$$

where $A_{11}$ is $r \times r$, $A_{22}$ is $(n-r) \times (n-r)$, and $A_{21}$ is $(n-r) \times r$.

Then the $(n-r) \times r$ lower-left block $A_{21}$ is the matrix of the transformation $A_E$ relative to the bases $(\bar{f}_{r+1}, \ldots, \bar{f}_n)$ for $\bar{\Re}$ and $(f_1, \ldots, f_r)$ for $\mathfrak{S}$.
