Prove that any right ideal of $\mathfrak{J}(\mathfrak{R}' \mid \mathfrak{R}) = \mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{R}$ has the form $\mathfrak{S}' \times \mathfrak{R}$ where $\mathfrak{S}'$ is a subspace of $\mathfrak{R}'$, and that any left ideal has the form $\mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{S}$ where $\mathfrak{S}$ is a subspace of $\mathfrak{R}$. (Cf. \S 3-4 of Chapter VIII.)
