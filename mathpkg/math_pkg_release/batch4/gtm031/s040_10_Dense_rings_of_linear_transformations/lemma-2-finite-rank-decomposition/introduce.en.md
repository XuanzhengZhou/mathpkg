Lemma 2 provides the technical mechanism for showing that any finite rank transformation in $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}' \mid \mathfrak{R})$ actually belongs to $\mathfrak{J} \cap \mathfrak{A}$ when $\mathfrak{A}$ is a dense subring containing certain finite rank transformations. The lemma uses the density property to interpolate coefficients via elements $A_{ji} \in \mathfrak{A}$ and demonstrates that the sum $\sum F_j A_{ji}$ recovers the original transformation $F$. This is the key step in proving the inclusion $\mathfrak{J}(\mathfrak{R}' \mid \mathfrak{R}) \subseteq \mathfrak{A}$ in Theorem 6.
