Verify that, if the vectors $(e_i)$ and $(\phi_i)$ are complementary in the sense that $\phi_i(e_k) = \delta_{ik}$, then the linear transformations $e_{ik} = \phi_i \times e_k$ satisfy the multiplication table for matrix units:
$$e_{ij} e_{kl} = \delta_{jk} e_{il}.$$
