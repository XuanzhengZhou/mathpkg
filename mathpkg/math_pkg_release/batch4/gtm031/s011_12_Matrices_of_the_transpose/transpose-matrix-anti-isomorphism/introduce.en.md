The transpose operation on matrices corresponds to the anti-isomorphism $A \to A^*$ between rings of linear transformations. Concretely, the mapping sending a matrix to its transpose reverses the order of multiplication: the transpose of a product is the product of the transposes in reverse order. This property can also be verified by direct matrix computation.
