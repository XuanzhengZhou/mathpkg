$$\operatorname{Rank} A^* = \dim (\mathfrak{R}_2^* A^*) = \dim j(\mathfrak{R})$$

where $\mathfrak{R}$ is the null space of $A$. By the dimension formula and Theorem 11, this equals $\dim(\mathfrak{R}_1 / \mathfrak{R}) = \operatorname{Rank} A$.
