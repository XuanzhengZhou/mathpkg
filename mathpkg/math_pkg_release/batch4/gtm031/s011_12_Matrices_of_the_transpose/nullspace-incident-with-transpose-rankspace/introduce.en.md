This theorem characterizes the relationship between the null space and rank space of a linear transformation $A$ and its transpose $A^*$. The null space of $A$ is exactly the subspace incident with (i.e., annihilated by) the rank space of $A^*$. Conversely, the rank space of $A$ is incident with the null space of $A^*$. This is a fundamental duality result in linear algebra over division rings.
