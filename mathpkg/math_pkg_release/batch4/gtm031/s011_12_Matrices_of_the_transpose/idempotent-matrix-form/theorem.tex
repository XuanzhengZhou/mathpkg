Let $E_1$ be an idempotent linear transformation on $\mathfrak{R}$. Then $E_1$ and $E_2 = 1 - E_1$ are orthogonal and supplementary projections. Relative to a suitable basis of $\mathfrak{R}$, the matrix of $E_1$ has the form

$$\operatorname{diag}\{1, 1, \ldots, 1, 0, \ldots, 0\}$$

where the number of $1$'s equals $\operatorname{Rank} E_1$.
