Show that, if $A$ is a linear transformation that has the diagonal matrix $\operatorname{diag}\{\alpha_1, \alpha_2, \dots, \alpha_n\}$ where the $\alpha_i$ are distinct, then the minimum polynomial of $A$ is $\prod_{i=1}^n (\lambda - \alpha_i)$. Hence prove $A$ is cyclic.
