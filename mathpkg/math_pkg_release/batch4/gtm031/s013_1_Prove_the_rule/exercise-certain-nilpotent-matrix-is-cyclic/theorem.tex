\text{Show that, if } A \text{ is a linear transformation with matrix}

$$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 & \alpha_{13} & \cdots & \alpha_{1n} \\
0 & 0 & 1 & \alpha_{24} & \cdots & \alpha_{2n} \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\
0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 1 \\
0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 0
\end{bmatrix},$$

\text{then } A \text{ is cyclic. Use this to prove that the given matrix is similar to the matrix with } 1 \text{'s on the superdiagonal and zeros elsewhere.}
