This lemma describes the behavior of orders under direct sums of cyclic subspaces. When vectors $f_i$ generate independent cyclic subspaces and their orders are pairwise coprime, the order of their sum is the product of the individual orders. This result is essential for constructing a vector whose order equals the minimum polynomial (Theorem 1) by combining vectors whose orders correspond to the distinct prime power factors of the minimum polynomial.
