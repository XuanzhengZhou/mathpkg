Let $f_i \in \Re$ and suppose the cyclic subspaces $\{f_i\}$ are independent, so that $\{f_1\} + \{f_2\} + \cdots + \{f_s\} = \{f_1\} \oplus \{f_2\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_s\}$. Let $\mu_i(\lambda) = \mu_{f_i}(\lambda)$ be the order of $f_i$, and suppose the $\mu_i(\lambda)$ are pairwise relatively prime. Let $f = f_1 + f_2 + \cdots + f_s$. Then the order of $f$ is the product:

$$\mu_f(\lambda) = \mu_1(\lambda)\mu_2(\lambda) \cdots \mu_s(\lambda).$$
