Let $\mu(\lambda) = \lambda^n - \mu_{n-1}\lambda^{n-1} - \cdots - \mu_0$ be any monic polynomial. The $n \times n$ matrix

$$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\
0 & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & 1 \\
\mu_0 & \mu_1 & \mu_2 & \cdots & \mu_{n-1}
\end{bmatrix}$$

is called the \text{companion matrix} of $\mu(\lambda)$. The characteristic polynomial and the minimum polynomial of a companion matrix both equal $\mu(\lambda)$.
