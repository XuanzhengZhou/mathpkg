Exercise 2 from Section 7. Show that the $r$th compound of a scalar matrix $\alpha I$ is the scalar matrix $\alpha^r I$. This follows directly from the explicit formula for $C_r(\alpha)$ since each $r \times r$ minor of a scalar matrix is either $\alpha^r$ (on the diagonal) or $0$ (off the diagonal).
