The $r$th compound $C_r(\alpha)$ of an $n \times n$ matrix $\alpha$ is the $\binom{n}{r} \times \binom{n}{r}$ matrix representing the induced action of $A$ on the space of skew-symmetric tensors of rank $r$. Its entries are the $r \times r$ minors (determinants of $r \times r$ submatrices) of $\alpha$, indexed by $r$-element subsets of $\{1, \ldots, n\}$ in lexicographic order. The compound matrix satisfies $C_r(\alpha\beta) = C_r(\alpha) C_r(\beta)$.
