The induced transformation on contravariant tensors respects composition: the $r$-fold Kronecker product of the composition equals the composition of the $r$-fold Kronecker products. This multiplicativity property follows directly from the definition of $A_0^r$ and makes the assignment $A \mapsto A_0^r$ a (multiplicative) homomorphism.
