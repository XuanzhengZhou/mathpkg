A skew-symmetric (or alternating) tensor is a contravariant tensor of rank $r$ that transforms by the sign of the permutation under any reordering of its factors. Every transposition (swap of two adjacent factors) multiplies the tensor by $-1$. Skew-symmetric tensors generalize the notion of determinants and are central to exterior algebra.
