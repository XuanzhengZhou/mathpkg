Show that
$$[x_1 x_2 \cdots x_n] \equiv \sum_{\sigma} \epsilon_\sigma x_{1'} x_{2'} \cdots x_{n'} = |\xi_{11} \xi_{22} \cdots \xi_{nn}| \, [e_1 e_2 \cdots e_n]$$
where $|\xi_{11} \cdots \xi_{nn}|$ is the determinant of the coordinates of the $x$'s relative to the $e$'s.
