Exercise 1 from Section 7 of Jacobson's Lectures in Abstract Algebra II. Show that the fully antisymmetrized product of $n$ vectors in an $n$-dimensional space equals the determinant of their coordinate matrix times the basis antisymmetrized product. This exercise verifies the connection between the exterior product and the determinant.
