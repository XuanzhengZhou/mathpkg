A tensor $\Sigma x_1 \times x_2 \times \cdots \times x_r \in \Re_0^r$ is \textit{symmetric} if for any permutation $i \mapsto i'$ of $1, 2, \cdots, r$,
$$\Sigma x_{1'} \times x_{2'} \times \cdots \times x_{r'} = \Sigma x_1 \times x_2 \times \cdots \times x_r.$$
Equivalently, $P(\sigma)x = x$ for all $\sigma \in \mathfrak{S}_r$.
