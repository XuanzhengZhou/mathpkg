An interchange of two indices in the component $\xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r}$ of a skew-symmetric tensor changes its sign by equation (26). If the two indices are equal, the component must equal its own negative, forcing it to be zero. This is a fundamental property of alternating multilinear maps and underlies the definition of the exterior power.
