A symmetry operator $P(\sigma)$ on the space $\Re_0^r$ of contravariant tensors of rank $r$ is the linear transformation that permutes the factors of each decomposable tensor according to the permutation $\sigma \in \mathfrak{S}_r$. There is one such operator for each element of the symmetric group $\mathfrak{S}_r$, and these operators form the foundation for studying symmetry classes of tensors.
