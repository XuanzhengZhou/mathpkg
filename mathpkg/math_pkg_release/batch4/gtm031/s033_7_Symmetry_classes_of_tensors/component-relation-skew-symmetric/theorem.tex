Let $T = \Sigma \xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r} e_{i_1} \times e_{i_2} \times \cdots \times e_{i_r}$ be a skew-symmetric tensor. Then for any permutation $\sigma: i \mapsto i'$,
$$\Sigma \xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r} e_{i_1'} \times e_{i_2'} \times \cdots \times e_{i_r'} = \epsilon_\sigma \Sigma \xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r} e_{i_1} \times e_{i_2} \times \cdots \times e_{i_r}. \tag{25}$$
Since $\epsilon_\sigma^{-1} = \epsilon_\sigma$, this implies
$$\xi^{i_{1'} i_{2'} \cdots i_{r'}} = \epsilon_\sigma \xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r}. \tag{26}$$
