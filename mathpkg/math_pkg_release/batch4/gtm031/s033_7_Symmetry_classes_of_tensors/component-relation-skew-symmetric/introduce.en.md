When a skew-symmetric tensor is expressed in terms of a basis, its components $\xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r}$ transform by the sign of any permutation applied to the indices. This component relation is the computational manifestation of skew-symmetry and leads to important consequences: sign change under transposition, vanishing when indices repeat, and the dimension restriction $r \leq n$.
