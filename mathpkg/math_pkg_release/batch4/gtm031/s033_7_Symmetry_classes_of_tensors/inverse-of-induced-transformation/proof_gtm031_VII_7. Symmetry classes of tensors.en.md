Since $1_0^r = 1$, if $A$ has an inverse $A^{-1}$, then
$$A_0^r (A^{-1})_0^r = (A A^{-1})_0^r = 1_0^r = 1.$$
Hence $A_0^r$ is non-singular and $(A_0^r)^{-1} = (A^{-1})_0^r$.
