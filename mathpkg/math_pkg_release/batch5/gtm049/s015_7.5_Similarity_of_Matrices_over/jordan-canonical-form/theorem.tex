If the minimum polynomial of $f$ factors into linear factors over $F$ as
$$m(X) = (X - x_1)^{s_1} \cdots (X - x_t)^{s_t},$$
then $V$ has an ordered basis with respect to which $f$ has matrix
$$\begin{pmatrix}
A_1 & \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & \cdots & A_t
\end{pmatrix}$$
where, for each $i$, $A_i$ is a block diagonal matrix
$$\begin{pmatrix}
C_{i1} & \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & \cdots & C_{ik_i}
\end{pmatrix}$$
and $C_{ij}$ is the $n_{ij} \times n_{ij}$ Jordan block
$$\begin{pmatrix}
x_i & 1 & & & 0 \\
& x_i & 1 & & \\
& & \ddots & \ddots & \\
& & & x_i & 1 \\
0 & & & & x_i
\end{pmatrix}.$$
The $n_{ij}$ are the exponents appearing in the elementary divisors $(X - x_i)^{n_{ij}}$ of $f$.
