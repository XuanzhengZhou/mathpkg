If $V_f$ is a cyclic $F[X]$-module with generator $a$, then $V$ has an ordered basis with respect to which $f$ has matrix
$$B = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & & & \\
& 0 & 1 & & \\
& & \ddots & \ddots & \\
& & & 0 & 1 \\
d_s & d_{s-1} & \cdots & d_2 & d_1
\end{pmatrix},$$
where $m_a(X) = X^s - d_1 X^{s-1} - \cdots - d_s$ is the minimal polynomial of $a$.
