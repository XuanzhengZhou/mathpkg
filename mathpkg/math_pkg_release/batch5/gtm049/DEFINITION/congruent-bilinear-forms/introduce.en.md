Two bilinear forms on the same vector space are congruent if one can be obtained from the other by applying the same automorphism to both arguments. Congruence is an equivalence relation on the space of all bilinear forms $\mathcal{B}(V)$. The matrices of congruent bilinear forms with respect to the same basis are congruent in the matrix sense.
