Verify that if $f$ is a linear isomorphism of a euclidean space $(V, \sigma)$ onto $(V', \sigma')$, then $\sigma(a, b) = \sigma'(af, bf)$ for all $a, b \in V$ is equivalent to $d(a, b) = d'(af, bf)$ for all $a, b \in V$.
