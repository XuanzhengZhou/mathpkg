For a non-degenerate bilinear form, the orthogonal complement operations $\perp$ and $\top$ are bijections on the lattice of subspaces that reverse inclusions and exchange sums with intersections. This theorem is the bilinear form analogue of the corresponding theorem for annihilators in the dual space (Theorem 6 of Chapter IV). The non-degeneracy condition $V^{\top} = V^{\perp} = 0$ is essential for these properties.
