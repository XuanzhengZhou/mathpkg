A distance on a real vector space is a metric satisfying additional axioms of translation invariance, homogeneity, and the parallelogram law. These six properties characterize those metrics that arise from a positive definite bilinear form. The parallelogram law D.6 is the key condition distinguishing inner product spaces from general normed spaces.
