This simple but important lemma states that applying the right orthogonal complement followed by the left orthogonal complement (or vice versa) yields a superspace of the original subspace. In the non-degenerate case, this inclusion becomes an equality, establishing a Galois connection between the lattices of subspaces under $\perp$ and $\top$.
