This lemma establishes the link between orthogonal complements and the annihilator mapping of the dual space. The right orthogonal complement of $M$ is precisely the annihilator of the image of $M$ under the linear map $\varrho: V \to V^*$. This connection allows dimension formulas for orthogonal complements to be derived from the corresponding formulas for annihilators.
