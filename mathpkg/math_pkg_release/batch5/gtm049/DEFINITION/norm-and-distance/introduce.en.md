The norm and distance derived from a positive definite bilinear form satisfy the usual axioms of a metric (D.1-D.3) together with additional properties reflecting their linear origin: translation invariance (D.4), homogeneity (D.5), and the parallelogram law (D.6). The parallelogram law characterizes those metrics that come from an inner product.
