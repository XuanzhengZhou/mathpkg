If a subspace $M$ is non-degenerate (i.e., the restriction of the bilinear form to $M$ is non-degenerate), then $M$ and its orthogonal complement form a direct sum decomposition of the whole space. This is fundamental for the structure theorems of section 5.4, where the space is decomposed into an orthogonal direct sum of irreducible components.
