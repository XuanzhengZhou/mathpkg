The rank of a bilinear form is defined as the rank of its matrix with respect to any ordered basis. This definition is unambiguous because all matrices of the same bilinear form lie in a single congruence class, and congruent matrices have the same rank. A bilinear form is non-degenerate when its rank equals the dimension of the underlying vector space.
