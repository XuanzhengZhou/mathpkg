For any subspace $M$ of a bilinear space $(V, \sigma)$, two orthogonal complements are defined: $M^{\perp}$ consists of all vectors orthogonal to every vector in $M$ when placed second, and $M^{\top}$ consists of all vectors orthogonal to every vector in $M$ when placed first. These are always subspaces of $V$, and the mappings $\perp$ and $\top$ are inclusion-reversing operations on the lattice of subspaces.
