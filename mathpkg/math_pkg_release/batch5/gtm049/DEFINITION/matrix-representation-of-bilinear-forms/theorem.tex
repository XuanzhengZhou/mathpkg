Let $(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$ be an ordered basis of $V$ over $F$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item The mapping $\sigma \mapsto (\sigma; (a_i))$ is a linear isomorphism of $\mathcal{B}(V)$ onto $F^{n \times n}$.
\item The mapping $\sigma \mapsto \sum \sigma(a_i, a_j) X_i Y_j$ is a linear isomorphism of $\mathcal{B}(V)$ onto the vector space of all bilinear polynomials in $X_1, \ldots, X_n; Y_1, \ldots, Y_n$.
\end{enumerate}
