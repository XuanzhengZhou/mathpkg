If $\sigma \in \mathcal{B}(V)$, show that $(\operatorname{Ker} \varrho)^{\perp} = V$ and that $(M^{\top})_{\varrho} = M^{\circ} \cap V_{\varrho}$, where $\varrho: V \to V^*$ is the linear map defined by $a\varrho(b) = \sigma(a, b)$.
