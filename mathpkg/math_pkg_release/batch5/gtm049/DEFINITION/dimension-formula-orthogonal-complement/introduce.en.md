For any subspace $M$ of a finite-dimensional bilinear space, the dimension of its orthogonal complement $M^{\perp}$ is related to the dimension of $M$ by a formula involving the intersection of $M$ with the radical $V^{\top}$. When the bilinear form is non-degenerate, this simplifies to $\dim M + \dim M^{\perp} = \dim V$, analogous to the familiar relation for orthogonal complements in inner product spaces.
