Proposition 5 is the crucial decomposition theorem that underpins the structure theory of bilinear forms developed in §5.4. When a subspace $M$ is non-degenerate (its own restriction of $\sigma$ is non-degenerate), then $V$ splits as the direct sum of $M$ and its orthogonal complement. The proof combines Lemma 3 (which gives $M \cap M^{\perp} = 0$) with the dimension formula of Proposition 4 to establish $\dim M + \dim M^{\perp} = \dim V$.
