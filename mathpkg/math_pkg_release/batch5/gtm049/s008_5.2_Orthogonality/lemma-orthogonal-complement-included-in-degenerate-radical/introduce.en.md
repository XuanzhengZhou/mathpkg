Lemma 3 states that for a non-degenerate subspace $M$, the intersection of $M$ with its orthogonal complement is trivial. This is derived from the fact that $M^{\perp} \subset V^{\perp}$: any $b \in M^{\perp}$ satisfying $\sigma(M + V^{\perp}, b) = 0$ must lie in $V^{\perp}$, and non-degeneracy of $M$ forces $M \cap V^{\perp} = 0$, hence $M \cap M^{\perp} = 0$. This lemma provides the "intersection zero" half of the direct sum decomposition in Proposition 5.
