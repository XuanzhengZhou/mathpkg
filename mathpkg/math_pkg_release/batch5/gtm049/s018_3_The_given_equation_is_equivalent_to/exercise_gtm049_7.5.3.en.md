Apply elementary moves (over the smaller field $$F$$) to the rows and columns of the matrix $$X1 - A$$ to obtain an equivalent matrix of the form $$\operatorname{diag}(d_1, \ldots, d_t)$$ (as in Proposition 8). The invariant factors are then $$[d_{s+1}] \supset \ldots \supset [d_t]$$ relative to $$F$$ or to any field containing $$F$$. The minimum polynomial is $$d_t$$ (to within a non-zero constant multiple).

The elementary divisors of $$\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 2 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$$ are (i) $$X^2 - 2$$ relative to $$Q$$ and (ii) $$X - \sqrt{2}, X + \sqrt{2}$$ relative to $$R$$.
