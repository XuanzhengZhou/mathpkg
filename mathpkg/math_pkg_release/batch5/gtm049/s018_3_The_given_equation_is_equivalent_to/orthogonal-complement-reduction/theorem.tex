Let $\sigma$ be a bilinear form on $V$, and let $f, g$ be linear mappings of $V$. Let $c$ be a common eigenvector of $f$ and $g$ with $\sigma(c, c) = 1$. Set $M = [c]^{\perp(\sigma)}$, the orthogonal complement of $[c]$ with respect to $\sigma$. Then the reduction of $f$ and $g$ to $M$ as in §6.3 Exercise 3 satisfies $(fg)' = f'g'$, and the result follows by induction on $\dim V$.
