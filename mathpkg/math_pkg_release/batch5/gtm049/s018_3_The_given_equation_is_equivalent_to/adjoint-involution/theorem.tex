Let $\sigma$ be a non-degenerate sesquilinear form on $V$, and let $f \mapsto f^*$ be the adjoint operation defined relative to a semi-linear automorphism $g$ as in Exercise 5 of §6.5. Then $f^{**} = f$ for every linear mapping $f$ of $V$. More explicitly,
$$\sigma(af^{**}, b) = \sigma(af, b) \quad \text{for all } a, b \in V.$$
