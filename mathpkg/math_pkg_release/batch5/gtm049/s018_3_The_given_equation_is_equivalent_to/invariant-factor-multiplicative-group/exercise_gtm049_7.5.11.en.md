If $m$ is the positive generator of the smallest invariant factor of the multiplicative group $F^*$ viewed as a $\mathbf{Z}$-module, then $x^m = 1$ for all $x$ in $F^*$ and there exists an element $x_0$ whose annihilator is $\mathbf{Z}m$.
