Let $f$ be an automorphism of the euclidean space $(V, \sigma)$. Extend $f$ to $V_{(C)}$ by the rule $(a + ib)f = af + i(bf)$. Then the extended $f$ is an automorphism of the Hilbert space $(V_{(C)}, \sigma')$.

Every eigenvalue of $f$ (considered as acting on $V_{(C)}$) is of the form $e^{i\theta}$ for some real $\theta$ satisfying $-\pi < \theta \leq \pi$.
