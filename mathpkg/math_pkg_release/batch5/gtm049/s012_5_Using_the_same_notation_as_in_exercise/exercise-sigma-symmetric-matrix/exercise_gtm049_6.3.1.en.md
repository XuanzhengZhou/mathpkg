Suppose $\sigma$ is a symmetric bilinear form on a vector space $V$ and $f$ a linear mapping of $V$ into $V$. If $(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$ is an ordered basis of $V$ and $S, A$ are the matrices of $\sigma, f$ with respect to this basis, prove that $f$ is $\sigma$-symmetric if, and only if, $AS = SA^t$.
