For an orthogonal automorphism (one preserving the bilinear form), all eigenvalues lie on the unit circle. The proof uses the preservation of the extended bilinear form on the complexification: if $$cf = xc$$, then $$\sigma(\bar{c}f, cf) = \bar{x}x\sigma(\bar{c}, c)$$ must equal $$\sigma(\bar{c}, c)$$, forcing $$|x| = 1$$.
