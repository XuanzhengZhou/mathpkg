Let $$f$$ be an automorphism of the Euclidean space $$(V, \sigma)$$. Then there exists a Cartesian basis of $$(V, \sigma)$$ with respect to which $$f$$ has matrix
$$\begin{pmatrix} A_1 & & & & & \\ & \ddots & & & & \\ & & A_r & & & \\ & & & I_s & & \\ & & & & -I_t \end{pmatrix}$$
where $$A_k = \begin{pmatrix} \cos \theta_k & -\sin \theta_k \\ \sin \theta_k & \cos \theta_k \end{pmatrix}$$ with $$0 < |\theta_k| < \pi$$. The integers $$r, s, t$$ and the numbers $$|\theta_1|, \ldots, |\theta_r|$$ are invariants of $$f$$.
