For an orthogonal automorphism, the orthogonal complement of an invariant subspace is also invariant. The proof uses the fact that $$f^{-1}$$ is also orthogonal: for $$a \in M$$ and $$b \in M^\perp$$, $$\sigma(a, bf) = \sigma(af^{-1}, b) = 0$$. This lemma enables the inductive decomposition in the canonical form theorem.
