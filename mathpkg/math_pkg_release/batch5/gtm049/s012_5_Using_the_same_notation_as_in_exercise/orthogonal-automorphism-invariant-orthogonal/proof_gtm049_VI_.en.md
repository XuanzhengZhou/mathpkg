For any $$a \in M$$ and $$b \in M^\perp$$, we have $$af^{-1} \in M$$ (since $$Mf = M$$ and $$f$$ is an automorphism). Then
$$\sigma(a, bf) = \sigma(af^{-1}, b) = 0.$$
Thus $$(M^\perp)f \subset M^\perp$$. Equality follows because $$f$$ is one-one and $$\dim(M^\perp)f = \dim M^\perp$$.
