Orientation is an equivalence relation on Cartesian bases of a Euclidean space with exactly two classes. Two bases have the same orientation if the change-of-basis automorphism has determinant $$+1$$ (is a rotation). Choosing an orientation is equivalent to selecting one of the two connected components of the orthogonal group.
