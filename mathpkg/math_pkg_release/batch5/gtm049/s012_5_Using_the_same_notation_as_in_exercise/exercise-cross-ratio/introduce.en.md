The cross-ratio is a fundamental projective invariant of four collinear points. This exercise defines the cross-ratio algebraically via homogeneous coordinates and establishes its invariance under collineations. The formula for the cross-ratio in terms of coordinate coefficients is derived, yielding the classical expression $\operatorname{cr} = (y_0z_2 - y_2z_0)(y_1z_3 - y_3z_1) / (y_0z_3 - y_3z_0)(y_1z_2 - y_2z_1)$.
