The ideals $\mathfrak{a}(a_i)$, $i = 1, \ldots, k$, in the decomposition
$$M = [a_1] \oplus \cdots \oplus [a_k], \quad \mathfrak{a}(a_1) \subset \cdots \subset \mathfrak{a}(a_k)$$
are called the \emph{invariant factors} of $M$. The monic generators of these ideals are also frequently called the invariant factors of $M$.
