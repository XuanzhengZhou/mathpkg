Given an ascending chain of ideals of a principal ideal domain $R$
$$\mathfrak{a}_1 \subset \mathfrak{a}_2 \subset \cdots$$
show that their union $\bigcup \mathfrak{a}_i$ is an ideal of $R$. Deduce that the chain terminates, i.e., there is an integer $n$ such that $\mathfrak{a}_n = \mathfrak{a}_{n+r}$ for all $r \geq 0$.
