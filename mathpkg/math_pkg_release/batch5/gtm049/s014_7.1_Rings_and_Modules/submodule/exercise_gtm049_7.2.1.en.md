Let $R$ be a commutative ring with identity and suppose that $xy = 0$ always implies that at least one of $x$ and $y$ is $0$. (We call such a ring an integral domain: cf. also the end of \S 7.3.) Prove that, if $M$ is a finitely generated torsion module over $R$, then $\mathfrak{a}(M) \neq 0$.
