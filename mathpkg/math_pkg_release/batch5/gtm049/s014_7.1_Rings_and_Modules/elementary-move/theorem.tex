An \emph{elementary move} on an ordered basis $(v_1, \ldots, v_n)$ of a torsion-free $F[X]$-module is one of the following:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] an interchange of two generators $v_i \leftrightarrow v_j$;
\item[(ii)] the addition of a multiple of one generator to another: $v_i \mapsto v_i + q v_j$ for some $q \in F[X]$, $i \neq j$.
\end{itemize}
An elementary move on the basis of the domain changes the matrix of a homomorphism by the corresponding row operation; an elementary move on the basis of the codomain changes the matrix by the corresponding column operation.
