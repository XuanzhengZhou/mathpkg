Let $V$ be the vector space $F^2$ and $f$ the linear mapping of $V$ into $V$ whose matrix with respect to the standard basis is
$$\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 2 & 4 \end{pmatrix}.$$
If $a_1 = (1, 0), a_2 = (0, 1), a_3 = (2, -1), a_4 = (1, 2)$, find $m_{a_i}(X)$ for $i = 1, 2, 3, 4$ and also $m(X)$.
