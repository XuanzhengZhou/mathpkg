This exercise shows that for a one-dimensional vector space $V$, the general linear group $\operatorname{Aut} V$ is isomorphic to the multiplicative group $F^*$ of the ground field. Every automorphism of $V$ is simply multiplication by a non-zero scalar, giving the natural isomorphism $f \mapsto x$ where $af = xa$ for a fixed non-zero basis vector $a$.
