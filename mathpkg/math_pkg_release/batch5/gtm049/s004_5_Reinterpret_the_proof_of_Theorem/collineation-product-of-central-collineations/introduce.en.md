Theorem 8 is a striking structural result: every collineation (projective automorphism) of an $n$-dimensional projective space can be factored as a product of at most $n$ central collineations. The proof uses an inductive argument on the dimension of the subspace of invariant points, together with Lemma 3 to construct central collineations that gradually eliminate the non-invariant behavior. This gives a concrete way to understand the general projective group in terms of simpler building blocks.
