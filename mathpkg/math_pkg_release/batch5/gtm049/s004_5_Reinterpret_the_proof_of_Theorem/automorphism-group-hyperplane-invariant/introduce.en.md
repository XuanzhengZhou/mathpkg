Theorem 7 formalizes the group-theoretic relationship between projective and affine automorphism groups. The subgroup of projective automorphisms that leave a given hyperplane $H$ invariant is isomorphic to the full affine automorphism group of the affine geometry determined by $H$. When restricted to projectivities (collineations), this gives an isomorphism with the affine group. This reframes Proposition 3 in the language of group theory.
