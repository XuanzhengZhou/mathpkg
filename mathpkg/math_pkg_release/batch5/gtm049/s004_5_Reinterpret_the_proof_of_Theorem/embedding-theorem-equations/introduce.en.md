This exercise provides the explicit coordinate formulas for the embedding of an affine geometry into a projective geometry. The affine hyperplane $c+H$ has equation $X_0 = 1$, and the mapping sends the affine point $(1, x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ to the projective point $[1, x_1, \ldots, x_n]$. Affine linear equations $\sum a_{ij}X_j = b_i$ become homogeneous projective equations $\sum a_{ij}X_j = b_i X_0$, showing how the embedding homogenizes the equations.
