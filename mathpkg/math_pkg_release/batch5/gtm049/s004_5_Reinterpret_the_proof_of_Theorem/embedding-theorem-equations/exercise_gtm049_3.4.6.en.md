In the notation of the Embedding Theorem of Chapter II (p. 32), choose a basis $\{a_0, \ldots, a_n\}$ of $V$ such that $a_0 = c$ and $[a_1, \ldots, a_n] = H$. What are the equations of $H$ and $c+H$? Show that the affine point with coordinates $(1, x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ is mapped by $\varphi$ to the projective point with homogeneous coordinates $(1, x_1, \ldots, x_n)$, and that the coset in $c+H$ defined by the equations
$$\sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} X_j = b_i, \quad i = 1, \ldots, m,$$
is mapped by $\varphi$ onto the subspace with equations
$$\sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} X_j = b_i X_0, \quad i = 1, \ldots, m.$$
