Lemma 2 states that the question of whether a projective isomorphism is a projectivity (i.e., arises from a linear map) can be decided by examining its restriction to a single line. This is a surprising and powerful result that forms the basis for the study of central collineations, where understanding the action on one line suffices to determine the global nature of the isomorphism.
