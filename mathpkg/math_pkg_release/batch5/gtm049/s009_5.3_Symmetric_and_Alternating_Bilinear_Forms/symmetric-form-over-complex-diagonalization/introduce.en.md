Proposition 7 states that every symmetric bilinear form over the complex numbers can be diagonalized to the form $\operatorname{diag}(I_r, 0)$ by a suitable choice of basis. The proof uses the fact that every non-zero complex number has a square root, allowing the diagonal entries to be normalized to 1.
