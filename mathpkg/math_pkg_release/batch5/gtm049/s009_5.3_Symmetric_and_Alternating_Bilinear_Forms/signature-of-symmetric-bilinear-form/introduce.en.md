The signature $p - q$ is a numerical invariant of a real symmetric bilinear form, where $p$ and $q$ are the dimensions of the positive-definite and negative-definite summands respectively. Sylvester's Law implies there are exactly $n+1$ distinct non-degenerate real symmetric spaces of dimension $n$, classified by their signature. The "space-time" of relativity, for instance, has rank 4 and signature 2.
