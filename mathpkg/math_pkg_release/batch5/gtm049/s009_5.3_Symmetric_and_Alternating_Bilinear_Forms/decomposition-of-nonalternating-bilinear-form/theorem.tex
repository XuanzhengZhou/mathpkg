Let $\sigma$ be a non-alternating bilinear form on a vector space $V$ over a field $F$ containing more than two elements. Then

$$V = A_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus A_r \oplus V^\perp$$

where $A_1, \ldots, A_r$ are non-degenerate one-dimensional subspaces and

$$\sigma(A_i, A_j) = 0 \quad \text{for all} \quad 1 \leq i < j \leq r.$$

Moreover $r = \operatorname{rank} \sigma$.
