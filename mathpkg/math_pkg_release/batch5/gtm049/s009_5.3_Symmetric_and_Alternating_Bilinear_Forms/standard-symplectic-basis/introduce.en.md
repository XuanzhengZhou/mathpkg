Corollary 2 of Theorem 2 asserts that every alternating bilinear form admits an ordered basis relative to which its matrix is block-diagonal with $2 \times 2$ symplectic blocks $\begin{pmatrix}0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0\end{pmatrix}$. This provides the canonical form for alternating matrices, solving the congruence problem for alternating matrices over any ground field.
