There exists an ordered basis with respect to which the alternating bilinear form $\sigma$ has matrix

$$A_{n,s} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
-1 & 0
\end{pmatrix}$$

where $n$ is the dimension and the matrix consists of $s$ blocks of $\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$ on the diagonal, with all other entries zero.
