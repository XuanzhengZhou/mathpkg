This exercise shows how the dual frame construction restricts to a subgeometry. For $n \geq 2$, the intersection of the line $A_nU$ with the hyperplane $A_n'$ yields a point $U_n$ that serves as unit point for a frame in $\mathcal{P}(A_n')$. The dual frame of this subgeometry is obtained by intersecting the original dual frame elements with $A_n'$, establishing an inductive relationship.
