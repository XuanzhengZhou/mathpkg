Let $(A_0, \ldots, A_n, U)$ be a frame of reference for the projective geometry $\mathcal{P}(V)$ and $(A_0', \ldots, A_n', U')$ the dual frame of reference. We have seen that $A_i'$ is the hyperplane face of the simplex $(A_0, \ldots, A_n)$ opposite $A_i$.

If $n = 1$, show that $U'$ (which is a point) is the harmonic conjugate of $U$ with respect to $A_0, A_1$.
