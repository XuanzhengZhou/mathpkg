This exercise shows that on a projective line ($n=1$), the dual unit point $U'$ is precisely the harmonic conjugate of $U$ with respect to the basis points $A_0$ and $A_1$. This gives a geometric interpretation of the dual frame construction: the dual coordinate system is obtained from the original by the harmonic construction.
