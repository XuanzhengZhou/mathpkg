This exercise asks for a purely geometric, coordinate-free inductive construction of the dual unit point $U'$. Using the results of parts (1) and (2), one can build $U'$ step by step: for $n=1$, it is the harmonic conjugate; for $n>1$, it is obtained by intersecting the dual unit points of the subgeometries, providing an elegant geometric recursive definition.
