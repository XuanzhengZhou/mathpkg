Let $f \in \mathcal{L}(V, V^*)$ and let $A, B$ be the matrices of $f$ with respect to two ordered bases of $V$. Let $S$ be the change-of-basis matrix between the two bases. For all $x, p \in V$, the matrix of $f$ with respect to the second basis satisfies $R S^t = I$, whence $R^{-1} = S^t$. Substituting the change-of-basis relations into the expression for the bilinear form $x^t A y$ yields $B = S^t A S$.
