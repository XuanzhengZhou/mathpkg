Given two distinct points $$A, B$$ in a projective plane $$M$$ and a point $$G$$ on $$AB$$. We choose any point $$C$$ in $$M$$ but not on $$AB$$ and also any point $$D$$ on $$GC$$ distinct from $$G$$ and $$C$$. Then
$$E = AD \cap BC,\quad F = BD \cap CA,\quad H = EF \cap AB$$
are points and $$H$$ is independent of the choice of $$C$$ and $$D$$.
