Let $F$ be a finite field with $p^{n+1}$ elements and let $a$ be a generator of the cyclic group $F^*$. Then $f: x \rightarrow xa$ is an automorphism of $F$ viewed as a vector space over $\mathbf{F}_p$ with order $p^{n+1}-1$, and $\mathcal{P}(f)$ is a collineation of $\mathcal{P}(F)$ of order $1+p+\cdots+p^n$.
