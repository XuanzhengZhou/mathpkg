If $Q$ is a non-degenerate conic in a projective plane $P$ and if $A$ is a point on $Q$, show that every line of $P$ through $A$, other than the polar line $A^\perp$, intersects $Q$ in two points.

Deduce that there is a one-to-one mapping of $Q$ onto the points of any line in $P$.
