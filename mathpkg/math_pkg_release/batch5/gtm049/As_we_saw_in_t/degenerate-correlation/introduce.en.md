A degenerate correlation is the composition of a degenerate projectivity with the orthocomplementation map $\circ$. In the language of bilinear forms, it corresponds to a correlation $ot(\sigma)$ where $\sigma$ is a degenerate bilinear form (i.e., $V^\perp 
eq \{0\}$). When $\sigma$ is symmetric, it is called a degenerate polarity. Degenerate polarities play a role in the study of affine polarities and degenerate quadrics.
