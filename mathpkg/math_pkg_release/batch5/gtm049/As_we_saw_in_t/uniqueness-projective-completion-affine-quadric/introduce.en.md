Proposition 13 addresses the question of whether an affine quadric determines a unique projective quadric that completes it. For affine quadrics not contained in a hyperplane, the projective completion is unique. The criterion is geometric: a point at infinity belongs to the completion if and only if every line through it meets the affine quadric in at most one point. For quadrics contained in a hyperplane, the completion is not unique.
