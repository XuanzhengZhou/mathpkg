Show that a non-degenerate quadric $Q(\sigma)$ in $\mathcal{P}(\mathbf{R}^4)$ has no points if the signature of $\sigma$ is $\pm 4$; has points but no generating lines if the signature of $\sigma$ is $\pm 2$; and has generating lines if the signature of $\sigma$ is 0.
