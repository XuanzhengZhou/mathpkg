By Theorem 2, a non-degenerate alternating space $(V, \sigma)$ decomposes as an orthogonal direct sum

$$V = L_1 \oplus L_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus L_s$$

where each $L_i$ is a 2-dimensional non-degenerate alternating subspace (a hyperbolic plane). Thus $\dim V = 2s$, which is even.
