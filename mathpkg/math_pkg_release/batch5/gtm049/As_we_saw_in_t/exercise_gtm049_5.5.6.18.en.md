Let $\mathcal{P}(V)$ be a projective geometry of dimension $n$ and let $Q(\sigma)$ be a quadric in $\mathcal{P}(V)$ containing at least one point outside $V^\perp$. If $P$ is a point not on $Q$, give a construction for the polar hyperplane $P^{\perp(\sigma)}$ using lines through $P$.
