A \textbf{polarity} is a correlation $\bot(\sigma)$ of the projective geometry $\mathcal{P}(V)$ for which the bilinear form $\sigma$ is \textbf{symmetric}. It is usual to refer to $M^\perp$ as the \textbf{polar} of $M$ with respect to $\bot$.

A \textbf{null-polarity} is a correlation $\bot(\sigma)$ for which $\sigma$ is \textbf{alternating}.
