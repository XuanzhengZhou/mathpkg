Proposition 8 is Sylvester's Law of Inertia: for a real symmetric bilinear form, the number of positive squares $p = \dim P$ and the number of negative squares $q = \dim Q$ in any diagonalization are invariants, independent of the choice of basis. These numbers determine the signature $p - q$, which completely classifies real symmetric bilinear forms up to congruence.
