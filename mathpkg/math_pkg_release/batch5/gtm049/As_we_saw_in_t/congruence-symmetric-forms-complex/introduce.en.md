This corollary states that over the complex numbers, the rank is a complete invariant for congruence of symmetric bilinear forms. Two complex symmetric matrices are congruent if and only if they have the same rank. Together with Proposition 7, this provides the complete classification of symmetric bilinear forms over $\mathbb{C}$.
