Lemma 4 states that if two bilinear forms $\sigma$ and $\tau$ on the same vector space $V$ have identical left orthogonal complement operators, then the forms are proportional: $\sigma = z\tau$ for some non-zero scalar $z$. This is a fundamental uniqueness result showing that a bilinear form is determined up to a scalar factor by its orthogonality relation.
