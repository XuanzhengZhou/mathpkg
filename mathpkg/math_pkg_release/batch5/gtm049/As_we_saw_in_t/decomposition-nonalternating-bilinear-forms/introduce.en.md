Theorem 4 generalizes the symmetric decomposition theorem (Theorem 3) to arbitrary non-alternating bilinear forms over fields with more than two elements. It shows that any such form can be represented by a triangular matrix after a suitable change of basis. The theorem is false over the field $\mathbb{F}_2$, as shown in the exercises.
