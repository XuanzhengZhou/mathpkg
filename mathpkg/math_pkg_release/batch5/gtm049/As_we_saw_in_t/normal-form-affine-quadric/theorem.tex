Given an affine quadric in an $n$-dimensional affine geometry $A$, a coordinate system for $A$ can be chosen with respect to which the quadric has an equation of one of the following two types:

$$d_1 X_1^2 + \cdots + d_n X_n^2 + d_0 = 0,$$

$$X_1 + d_2 X_2^2 + \cdots + d_n X_n^2 = 0$$

(where some, or all, of the $d_i$'s may be zero).
