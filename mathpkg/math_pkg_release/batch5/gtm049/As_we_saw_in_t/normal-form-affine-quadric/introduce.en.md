Proposition 12 provides a complete classification of affine quadrics up to affine coordinate change. Any affine quadric can be reduced to one of two standard forms: a purely quadratic form plus constant (type 1), or a linear term plus a sum of squares (type 2). These normal forms are the affine analogues of the diagonal form for projective quadrics and form the basis for the classical classification of conics and quadrics.
