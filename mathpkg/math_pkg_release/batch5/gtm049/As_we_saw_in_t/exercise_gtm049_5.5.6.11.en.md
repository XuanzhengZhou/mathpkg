Show that all the points of $\mathcal{P}(\mathbf{Q}^3)$ on the conic with equation

$$X_0^2 + X_1^2 - 2X_2^2 = 0$$

are of the form

$$[(m^2 + 2mn - n^2, -m^2 + 2mn + n^2, -m^2 - n^2)]$$

where $m, n$ are integers. (Use exercise 10 and note that the point $(1, 1, 1)$ lies on the conic.)
