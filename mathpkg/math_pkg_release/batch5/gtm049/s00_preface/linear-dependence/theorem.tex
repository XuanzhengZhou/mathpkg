**Proposition 2.** (1) If $a_1, \ldots, a_r$ where $r \geq 2$ are linearly dependent, then one of the $a_i$ is linearly dependent on the rest.

(2) If $b_1, \ldots, b_s$ are linearly independent, but $v, b_1, \ldots, b_s$ are linearly dependent, then $v$ is linearly dependent on $b_1, \ldots, b_s$.
