**Proposition 3.** Let $A, A'$ be affine geometries and $\alpha$ an isomorphism of $A$ onto $A'$.

If $(S_i)_{i \in I}$ is a family of elements of $A$, then
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $(\cap S_i) \alpha = \cap (S_i \alpha)$ if $\cap S_i$ is not empty, and
\item[(2)] $\mathcal{J}(S_i) \alpha = \mathcal{J}(S_i \alpha)$.
\end{enumerate}
Further,
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(3)] $\dim S \alpha = \dim S$, for each $S$ in $A$, and
\item[(4)] the elements $S, T$ of $A$ are parallel if, and only if, $S \alpha, T \alpha$ are parallel.
\end{enumerate}
