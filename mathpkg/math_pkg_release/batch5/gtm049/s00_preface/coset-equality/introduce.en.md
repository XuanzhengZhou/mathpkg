Lemma 1 gives three equivalent conditions for two cosets $a+M$ and $b+M$ to be equal: (1) they are equal as sets, (2) $b$ belongs to $a+M$, or (3) $-a+b \in M$. This lemma is fundamental for manipulating cosets in affine geometry.
