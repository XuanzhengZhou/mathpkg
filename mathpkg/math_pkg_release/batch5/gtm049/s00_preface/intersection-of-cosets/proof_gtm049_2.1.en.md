Let $(S_i)_{i \in I}$ be a given family of cosets and suppose that $c \in \cap S_i$. By Lemma 1, each $S_i$ is of the form $c + M_i$ where $M_i$ is the subspace belonging to $S_i$. Thus $\cap S_i = \cap (c + M_i) = c + \cap M_i$, which is a coset by Proposition 1 of Chapter I (p. 7).
