The subspace $[S]$ spanned by a set $S$ of vectors is defined as the intersection of all subspaces containing $S$. It is the smallest subspace containing $S$. Equivalently, when $S$ is non-empty, $[S]$ consists of all finite linear combinations of elements from $S$.
