Define a mapping $d$ of the ordered pairs of vectors of the real vector space $V$ by setting $d(a, a) = 0$ for all $a$ and $d(a, b) = 1$ whenever $a \neq b$. Show that this $d$ satisfies D.1, D.2, D.3, and D.4, but fails to satisfy D.5.
