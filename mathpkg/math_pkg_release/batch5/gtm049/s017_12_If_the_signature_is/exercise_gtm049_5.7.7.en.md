Let $$A$$ be determined by $$H$$ in $$\mathcal{P}(V)$$. Let $$Q'$$ be an affine quadric contained in a hyperplane $$K$$ of $$A$$ and let $$Q$$ be a projective quadric completing $$Q'$$. Now $$Q \subset H \cup K$$. If $$Q \subset H$$ then $$Q'$$ is empty and if $$Q \subset K$$ then $$Q'$$ consists of all the affine points in some subspace $$N$$ of $$K$$ (using exercise 3). The remaining cases follow from exercise 6.
