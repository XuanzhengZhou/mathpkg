If the signature is $$\pm 2$$, then the equation of the quadric in a suitable coordinate system is

$$X_0^2 + X_1^2 + X_2^2 - X_3^2 = 0.$$

If there were a generator, the plane $$X_3 = 0$$ would have to meet it in a point (or points) satisfying $$X_0^2 + X_1^2 + X_2^2 = 0$$ (impossible over $$\mathbf{R}$$).
