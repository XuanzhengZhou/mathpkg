This exercise completes the verification that a distance on an affine geometry satisfying the euclidean distance axioms D.1--D.6 yields a bilinear form $\sigma$ on the underlying real vector space. Using the rational-linearity result from exercise 4 together with the continuity of the mapping $x \mapsto \sigma(xa, b)$, one deduces that $\sigma$ is $\mathbb{R}$-bilinear.
