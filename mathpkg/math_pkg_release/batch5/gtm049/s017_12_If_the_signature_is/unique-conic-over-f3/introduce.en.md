This exercise from Section 5.7 characterizes the unique non-degenerate conic over the field $\mathbf{F}_3$ through four points in general position. The conic is given by the equation $X_0X_1 + X_1X_2 + X_2X_0 = 0$. The problem also analyzes the completions of an affine 2-point set to a projective conic.
