This exercise establishes that a distance-preserving bijection between euclidean geometries automatically preserves collinearity and induces a full euclidean isomorphism. The first part proves that equality in the triangle inequality implies collinearity via the Schwarz inequality. The second part shows that a distance-preserving bijection maps lines to lines, hence is an affine isomorphism that preserves distance.
