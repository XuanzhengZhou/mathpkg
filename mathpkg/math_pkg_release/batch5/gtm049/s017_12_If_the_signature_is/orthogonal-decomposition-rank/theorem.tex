Let $V$ be a vector space with a symmetric bilinear form $\sigma$. There exists an orthogonal decomposition $V = M \oplus V^\perp$ where $\dim M = \operatorname{rank} \sigma = r \geq 1$ and $Q(\sigma_M)$ is empty. In suitable coordinates, $Q$ has equation
$$a_0 X_0^2 + a_1 X_1^2 + \cdots + a_{r-1} X_{r-1}^2 = 0$$
with $a_0 a_1 \cdots a_{r-1} \neq 0$.
