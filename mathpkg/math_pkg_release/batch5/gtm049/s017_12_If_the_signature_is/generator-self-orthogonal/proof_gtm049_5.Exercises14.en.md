Since $L$ is a generator of the quadric $Q$, it is a maximal totally isotropic subspace with respect to the associated symmetric bilinear form $\sigma$. By definition of the orthogonal complement, $L \subseteq L^\perp$. Since $L$ is maximal isotropic, we must have $L^\perp = L$; otherwise $L^\perp$ would be a larger isotropic subspace containing $L$, contradicting maximality.
