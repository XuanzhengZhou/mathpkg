The mapping $M \mapsto M^\circ$ from subspaces of $V$ to subspaces of $V^*$ satisfies:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\dim M^\circ = \dim V - \dim M$;
\item $M^{\circ\circ} = M$ (under the identification $V^{**} = V$);
\item $M \subset N$ if, and only if, $M^\circ \supset N^\circ$;
\item $(M + N)^\circ = M^\circ \cap N^\circ$;
\item $(M \cap N)^\circ = M^\circ + N^\circ$.
\end{enumerate}
