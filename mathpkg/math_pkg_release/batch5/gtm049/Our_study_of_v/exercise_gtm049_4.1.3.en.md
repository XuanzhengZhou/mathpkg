Let $V$ be the vector space of all polynomials in $X$ over a field $F$ of degree less than $n$ (including the zero polynomial). If $D$ denotes the differentiation operator applied to $V$, show that $\operatorname{Ker} D + VD$ is not the whole space $V$ provided $n \geq 2$.
