If $A = (a_{ij}) \in F^{n \times n}$, let $At$ denote the sum of the diagonal elements of $A$: i.e.,
$$
At = a_{11} + \cdots + a_{nn}.
$$
We call $At$ the *trace* of $A$ and $t$ the trace mapping. Prove that $t$ is a linear mapping of $F^{n \times n}$ onto $F$ and verify that
$$
(AB)t = (BA)t
$$
for all $A, B$ in $F^{n \times n}$.
