If $$f, g$$ are linear mappings of $$W$$ into $$W$$, the adjoint $$f^*$$ is defined by $$\sigma(af^*, b) = \overline{\sigma(a, bf)}$$. Prove:

(i) $$\|wf\| = \|wf^*\|$$ for all $$w$$ in $$W$$;
(ii) if $$w$$ is an eigenvector of $$f$$ with eigenvalue $$x$$, then $$w$$ is an eigenvector of $$f^*$$ with eigenvalue $$\bar{x}$$;
(iii) if $$[w]f \subset [w]$$ then $$[w]^\perp f \subset [w]^\perp$$.

Deduce the Spectral Theorem for normal mappings.
