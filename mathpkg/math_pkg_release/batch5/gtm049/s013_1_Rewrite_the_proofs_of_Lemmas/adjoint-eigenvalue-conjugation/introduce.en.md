If $w$ is an eigenvector of a linear mapping $f$ on a Hilbert space with eigenvalue $x$ (so $wf = xw$), then the same vector $w$ is an eigenvector of the adjoint $f^*$ with eigenvalue $\bar{x}$ (the complex conjugate). This lemma provides a direct link between the spectral properties of $f$ and $f^*$ and is essential for understanding normal operators, whose eigenvectors form an orthogonal basis.
