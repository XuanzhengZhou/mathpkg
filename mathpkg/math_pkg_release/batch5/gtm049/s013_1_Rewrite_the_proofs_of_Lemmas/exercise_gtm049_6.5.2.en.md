Prove that

$$4\sigma(a, b) = \|a + b\|^2 - \|a - b\|^2 + i\|a - ib\|^2 - i\|a + ib\|^2$$

for all $$a, b$$ in $$W$$.

(If $$d$$ is a mapping which satisfies the rules D.1–D.6 of §6.1, p. 125 as modified on p. 146, and if we write $$\|a - b\| = d(a, b)$$, then the above equation may be used to define a positive definite Hermitian form $$\sigma$$. Cf. exercises 4, 5 of §6.1.)
