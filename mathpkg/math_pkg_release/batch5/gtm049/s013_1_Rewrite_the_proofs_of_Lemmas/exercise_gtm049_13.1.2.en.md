Prove that

$$4\sigma(a, b) = \|a + b\|^2 - \|a - b\|^2 + i\|a - ib\|^2 - i\|a + ib\|^2$$

for all $a, b$ in $W$.

(If $d$ is a mapping which satisfies the rules D.1--D.6 of \S 6.1, p. 125 as modified on p. 146 of this section and if we write $\|a - b\| = d(a, b)$ then the above equation may be used to define a positive definite hermitian form $\sigma$ (cf. exercises 4, 5 of \S 6.1.).)
