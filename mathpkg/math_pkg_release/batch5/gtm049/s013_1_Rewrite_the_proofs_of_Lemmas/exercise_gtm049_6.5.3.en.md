Let $$\tau$$ be a sesquilinear form on $$W$$ with respect to complex conjugation. Show that the equation

$$\sigma(af, b) = \tau(a, b)$$

for all $$a, b$$ in $$W$$ defines a linear mapping $$f$$ of $$W$$ into $$W$$. Deduce that there exists a Cartesian basis of $$(W, \sigma)$$ with respect to which $$\tau$$ has a triangular matrix.
