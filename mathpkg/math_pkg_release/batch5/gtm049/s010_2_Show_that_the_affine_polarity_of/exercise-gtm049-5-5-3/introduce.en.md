This exercise characterizes projective quadrics that are contained in a hyperplane. Such quadrics are precisely the set of points in the radical $V^{\perp(\sigma)}$ of the associated bilinear form. This gives all projective quadrics that determine the empty affine quadric, as the hyperplane can be taken as the hyperplane at infinity.
