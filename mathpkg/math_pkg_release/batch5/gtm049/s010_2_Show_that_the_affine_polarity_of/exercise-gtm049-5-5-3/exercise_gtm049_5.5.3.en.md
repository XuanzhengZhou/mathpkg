If $Q(\sigma)$ is a projective quadric in $\mathcal{P}(V)$ contained in a hyperplane of $\mathcal{P}(V)$, show that $Q(\sigma)$ is precisely the set of all points in $V^{\perp}(\sigma)$. (Hint: consider the lines which meet $Q(\sigma)$ but do not lie in $H$.) (Note that this gives all the projective quadrics of $\mathcal{P}(V)$ that determine the empty quadric in $A$.)
