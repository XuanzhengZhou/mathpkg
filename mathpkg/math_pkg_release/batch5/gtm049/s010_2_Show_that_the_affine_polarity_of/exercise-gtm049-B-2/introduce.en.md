This exercise derives the transformation law for the matrix of a sesquilinear form under a change of basis. The formula $S' = (P\zeta)^t S P$ generalizes the congruence relation $S' = P^t S P$ for bilinear forms, where the automorphism $\zeta$ is applied to the entries of the transition matrix $P$.
