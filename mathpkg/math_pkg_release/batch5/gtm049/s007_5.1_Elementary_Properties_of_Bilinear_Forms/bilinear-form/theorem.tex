Let $V$ be a vector space over a field $F$. A bilinear form $\sigma$ on $V$ is a mapping of the ordered pairs of vectors of $V$ into $F$ such that

$$\sigma(xa + yb, c) = x\sigma(a, c) + y\sigma(b, c),$$
$$\sigma(a, xb + yc) = x\sigma(a, b) + y\sigma(a, c),$$

for all vectors $a, b, c$ in $V$ and all scalars $x, y$ in $F$.
