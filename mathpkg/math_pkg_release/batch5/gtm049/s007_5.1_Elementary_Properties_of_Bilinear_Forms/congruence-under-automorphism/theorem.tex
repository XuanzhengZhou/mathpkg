If $f$ is an automorphism of $V$ and $(a_1, \ldots, a_n)$ is an ordered basis of $V$, then the matrices $(\sigma^f; (a_i))$ and $(\sigma; (a_i))$ are congruent. Moreover, the matrices of a bilinear form $\sigma$ with respect to all ordered bases of $V$ lie in a single congruence class, and every matrix in that congruence class is the matrix of $\sigma$ with respect to some ordered basis.
