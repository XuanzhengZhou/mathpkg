Lemma 1 establishes the relationship between frames of reference in a projective geometry $\mathcal{P}(V)$ and ordered bases of the underlying vector space $V$. Every frame of reference $(A_0,\ldots,A_n,U)$ is determined by at least one ordered basis $(a_0,\ldots,a_n)$ of $V$ such that $[a_i] = A_i$ and $[a_0 + \cdots + a_n] = U$. Two ordered bases determine the same frame of reference precisely when they are scalar multiples of each other (with the same non-zero scalar for all basis vectors).
