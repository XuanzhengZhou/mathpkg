A projective (or homogeneous) coordinate system for an $n$-dimensional projective geometry $P$ over a field $F$ is a projectivity $\pi$ of $P$ onto the standard projective geometry $\mathcal{P}(F^{n+1})$. The (ordered) $(n+1)$-tuple $(A_0, \ldots, A_n)$ where $A_i\pi = [e_i]$ (with $e_i$ the standard basis vectors) is called the simplex of reference. Combined with the unit point $U = [e_0 + \cdots + e_n]$, this gives the standard frame of reference for $\mathcal{P}(F^{n+1})$.
