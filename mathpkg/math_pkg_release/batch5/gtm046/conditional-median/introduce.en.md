The conditional median generalizes the ordinary median to the conditional setting by replacing unconditional probabilities with conditional probabilities given a $\sigma$-field $\mathcal{B}$. It provides a centering quantity more robust than the conditional expectation, particularly useful for extending symmetrization arguments and maximal inequalities from the independent to the dependent case.
