The Submartingale Closure Theorem characterizes when a (sub)martingale can be closed by an integrable random variable, establishing the equivalence between $L_r$-convergence and uniform integrability. Moreover, it shows that the almost sure limit provides the minimal closing random variable. This theorem is fundamental for martingale convergence theory and its applications to likelihood ratios and density processes.
