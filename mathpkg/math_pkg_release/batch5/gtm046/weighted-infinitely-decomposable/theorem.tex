A weighted infinitely divisible law has characteristic function
$$f_W(u) = \int \exp\left[ iu\alpha + \int g(x, u) d\Psi(x) \right] dW(\alpha, \Psi),$$
where $g(x, u) = (e^{iux} - 1 - \frac{iux}{1+x^2}) \frac{1+x^2}{x^2}$ is the Lévy-Khintchine kernel and $\Psi$ is nondecreasing, left-continuous, of bounded variation.
