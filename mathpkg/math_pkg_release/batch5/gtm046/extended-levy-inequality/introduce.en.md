P. L\'{e}vy's maximal inequality extends from independent to dependent summands when conditional medians replace ordinary medians. The proof is modified by replacing $PA_k B_k$ with $E\{I_{A_k}P(B_k \mid S_1, \cdots, S_k)\}$, preserving the essential structure of the original argument.
