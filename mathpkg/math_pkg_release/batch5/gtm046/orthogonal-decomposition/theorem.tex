A second-order random function $X(t)$ continuous in quadratic mean on a closed interval $I$ admits an orthogonal decomposition $$X(t) = \sum_n \xi_n \psi_n(t)$$ where $\psi_n$ are orthonormalized eigenfunctions of the covariance $\Gamma(t, t')$ and $\xi_n = \int X(t) \bar{\psi}_n(t) dt$ are orthogonal random variables with $E\xi_m \bar{\xi}_n = \delta_{mn}$. The convergence is uniform in q.m. on $I$.
