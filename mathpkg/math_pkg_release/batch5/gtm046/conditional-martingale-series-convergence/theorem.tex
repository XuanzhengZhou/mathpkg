Let $X_n = \sum_{k=1}^{n} Y_k$ be a martingale. For $r = 2$, the summands are orthogonal, $EX_n^2 = \sum_{k=1}^{n} EY_k^2$, and the inequality $P[\sup_{k \leq n} |X_k| > c] \leq E|X_n|^r/c^r$ reduces to the extended Kolmogorov inequality. Moreover, $X_n \xrightarrow{a.s.} X$ finite on $[\sum E^{\mathcal{B}_{n-1}}Y_n^2 < \infty]$.
