A weighted symmetric stable law with exponent $\gamma \in (0, 2]$ has characteristic function
$$f_W(u) = \int_0^\infty \exp\left[ -c|u|^\gamma / 2 \right] dW(c).$$
There is a one-to-one correspondence between $f_W$ and the weight distribution $W$ up to additive constants. Weighted symmetric stable laws are closed under limits with the same exponent $\gamma$.
