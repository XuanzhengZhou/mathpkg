The basic ergodic theorem gives sufficient conditions for the almost sure convergence of ratios of partial sums. It encompasses Birkhoff's individual ergodic theorem (when $Y_n = 1$) and Hopf's ratio ergodic theorem. The proof uses the basic ergodic inequality to show that the event of divergence has probability zero.
