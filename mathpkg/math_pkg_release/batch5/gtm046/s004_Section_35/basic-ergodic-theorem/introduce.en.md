The Basic Ergodic Theorem establishes a.s. convergence of $X^n/Y^n$ under tail integrability conditions. The proof writes the divergence set as a countable union $D=\bigcup_{a,b}[\liminf<a<b<\limsup]$, then applies the basic ergodic inequality to show each component has probability zero. Extension B' allows replacing $Y^n$ by an equivalent normalizing sequence $Z^n$.
