Let $T$ be a translation on $\mathfrak{B}$. The following are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $\limsup\overline{P^n}A\to 0$ as $A\downarrow\emptyset$, where $\overline{P^n}A=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n PT^{k-1}A$.
\item[(ii)] $\lim\overline{P^n}=\overline{P}$ exists and is a probability on $\mathfrak{B}$.
\item[(iii)] There exists on $\mathfrak{B}$ a probability $\overline{P}$ invariant under $T$ and coinciding with $P$ on the $\sigma$-field $\mathfrak{C}$ of invariant events; then $\lim\overline{P^n}=\overline{P}$.
\end{itemize}
