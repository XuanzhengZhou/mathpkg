A translation on a $\sigma$-field generalizes the inverse point transformation of classical ergodic theory. Rather than mapping points, it maps events to events while preserving countable Boolean operations. This abstraction defines translates of measurable functions: $\xi_{k+1}=T^k\xi$ is determined by $[\xi_{k+1}\in S]=T^k[\xi\in S]$. Invariant events are fixed points of $T$.
