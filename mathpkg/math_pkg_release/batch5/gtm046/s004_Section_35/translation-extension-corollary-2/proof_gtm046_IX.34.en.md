The extension to simple functions is uniquely determined by linearity from the action on indicators (Corollary 1). For arbitrary $\xi$, define $T\xi$ as the limit of $T\xi^{(n)}$ for any simple $\xi^{(n)}\to\xi$. Since $T$ commutes with $\sup_n$: $[T\sup\xi^{(n)}>x]=T\bigcup[\xi^{(n)}>x]=\bigcup[T\xi^{(n)}>x]=[\sup T\xi^{(n)}>x]$, the limit is independent of the approximating sequence, so the extension is unique. $T1=1$ because $T\Omega=\Omega$. $TI_{AB}=T(I_AI_B)=TI_A\cdot TI_B$ holds for indicators and extends by continuity.
