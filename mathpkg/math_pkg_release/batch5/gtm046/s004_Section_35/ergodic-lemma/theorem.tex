Let $T$ be a fixed translation. Set $\overline{T^n}=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n T^{k-1}$, $\overline{T^n}\xi=(\xi_1+\cdots+\xi_n)/n$. Assume $\limsup\overline{P^n}A\to 0$ as $A\downarrow\emptyset$ where $\overline{P^n}A=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n PT^{k-1}A$. Then for every r.v. $X$ with
$$\frac{1}{n}\int|T^{n-1}X|\to 0,\qquad \frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n\int_{T^{k-1}A}T^{k-1}X\to 0$$
as $n\to\infty$ then $A\downarrow\emptyset$, we have $\overline{T^n}X\xrightarrow{\text{a.s.}}\overline{T}X$ invariant. In particular, this holds for every bounded $X$.
