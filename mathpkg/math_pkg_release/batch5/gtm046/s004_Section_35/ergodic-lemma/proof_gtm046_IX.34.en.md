Set $Y_n=1$ in the Basic Ergodic Theorem. Then $Y^n=n\uparrow\infty$ and the conditions reduce to the given integrability conditions on $T^{n-1}X$, yielding $\overline{T^n}X\xrightarrow{\text{a.s.}}\overline{T}X$. For bounded $X$ with $|X|\leq c$: $\frac{1}{n}\int|T^{n-1}X|\leq c/n\to 0$ and $\frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n\int_{T^{k-1}A}T^{k-1}X\leq c\cdot\overline{P^n}A\to 0$ by the assumption on $\overline{P^n}$.
