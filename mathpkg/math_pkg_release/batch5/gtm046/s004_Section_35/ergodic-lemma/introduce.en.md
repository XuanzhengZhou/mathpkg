The Ergodic Lemma establishes a.s. convergence of Cesaro means $\overline{T^n}X$ under a condition on $\overline{P^n}A$. It follows from the Basic Ergodic Theorem by setting $Y_n=1$. The bounded case is particularly important: every bounded random variable satisfies the integrability conditions, guaranteeing a.s. convergence to an invariant limit. This is the classical pointwise ergodic theorem.
