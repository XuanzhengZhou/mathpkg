This follows directly from $\bar{P}_t(S)=\int\bar{P}_t(dx)P(x,S)$ proved in the Ergodic Decomposition Theorem, which means $\bar{P}_t$ is invariant under $P(x,S)$. The $S_t$ are atoms of the invariant $\sigma$-field, so no further decomposition into non-trivial invariant sub-chains is possible.
