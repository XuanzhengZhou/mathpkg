Each ergodic component of a Markov chain under an invariant probability forms a stationary, indecomposable chain. "Indecomposable" means the chain cannot be further split into smaller invariant sub-chains, analogous to the concept of ergodicity in dynamical systems. This is the Markov chain analog of the ergodic decomposition theorem.
