The conditional distribution defining properties:
$$(\text{CD}_1) \quad P(x_1, S_2) \text{ is an } \alpha_1\text{-measurable probability on } \alpha_2,$$
$$(\text{CD}_2) \quad P(S_1 \times S_2) = \int_{S_1} P(dx_1) P(x_1, S_2).$$
(CD1) characterizes c.d. of $X_2$ given $X_1$; (CD2) relates the distributions of $X_1$ and $(X_1, X_2)$.
