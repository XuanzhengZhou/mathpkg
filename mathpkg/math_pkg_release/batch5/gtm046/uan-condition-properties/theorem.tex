Under the uan' (uniformly asymptotically negligible, conditional) condition, for every $\epsilon > 0$: (i) $\max_k \int_{|x| < \epsilon} |x|^s dF'_{nk} \xrightarrow{P} 0$ for $s > 0$; (ii) $\max_k \int_{|x| < \epsilon} |x - \alpha'_{nk}(\epsilon)|^s dF'_{nk} \xrightarrow{P} 0$ for $s \geq 1$; (iii) $\max_k \gamma'_{nk}(u) = \max_k \int(e^{iu(x - \alpha'_{nk}(\epsilon))} - 1) dF'_{nk} \xrightarrow{P} 0$.
