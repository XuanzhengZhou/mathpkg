A random variable $\xi$ is integral invariant (the sequence $\xi_1, \xi_2, \ldots$ is integral stationary) if the integrals exist and for every event $A$ and every $k$,
$$\int_{A_k} \xi_k = \int_{A_1} \xi_1,$$
where $A_k$ is the translate by $k-1$ of $A$ and $\xi_k$ is the translate by $k-1$ of $\xi$.
