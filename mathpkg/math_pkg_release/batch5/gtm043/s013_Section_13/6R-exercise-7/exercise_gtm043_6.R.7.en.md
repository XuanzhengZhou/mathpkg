If a sequence of sets $A_n'$ has the finite intersection property, then $\bigcap_n A_n'$ contains a nonempty, open subset of $\beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$. [Choose distinct $x_n \in A_1 \cap \cdots \cap A_n$, and consider the set $A = \{x_n\}_{n \in \mathbf{N}}$.]
