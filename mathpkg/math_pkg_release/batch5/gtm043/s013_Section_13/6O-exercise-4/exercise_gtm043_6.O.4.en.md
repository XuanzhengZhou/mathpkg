$\beta \mathbf{Q} - \mathbf{Q}$ is not $C^*$-embedded in $\beta \mathbf{Q}$. [The function sgn on $\mathbf{Q} - \{0\}$ has a continuous extension to $\beta \mathbf{Q} - \{0\}$, but not to $\beta \mathbf{Q}$.] Hence $\beta(\beta \mathbf{Q} - \mathbf{Q}) \neq \beta \mathbf{Q}$.
