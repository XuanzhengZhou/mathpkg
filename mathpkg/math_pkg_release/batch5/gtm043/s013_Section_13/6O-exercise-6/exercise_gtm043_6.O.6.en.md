Every countable set $E$ in $\beta \mathbf{N}$ is $C^*$-embedded in $\beta \mathbf{N}$. [By 3B.5, $\mathbf{N} \cup E$ is normal; apply 3D.1.] Hence every infinite closed set in $\beta \mathbf{N}$ contains a copy of $\beta \mathbf{N}$. [0.13.]
