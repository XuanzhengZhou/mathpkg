Let $\tau$ be a continuous mapping from $X$ into $Y$, where now $Y$ is not necessarily compact. Then $\bigcap \tau \# \mathcal{F} = \bigcap_{Z \in \mathcal{F}} \operatorname{cl}_Y \tau[Z]$; thus, $\tau$ carries cluster points of $\mathcal{F}$ to cluster points of $\tau \# \mathcal{F}$.
