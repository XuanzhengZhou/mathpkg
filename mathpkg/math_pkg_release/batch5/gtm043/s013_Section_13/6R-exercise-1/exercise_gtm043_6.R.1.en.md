The closed subspace $\beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$ of the extremally disconnected space $\beta \mathbf{N}$ is not extremally disconnected. [In 6Q, let $A$ and $B$ be disjoint subsets of $D$ that cannot be separated by open subsets of $\Pi$. Consider the unions of the corresponding sets $E'$, and apply 1H.1 and the fact that $\beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$ is normal.] (For another proof, see 6W.)
