Let $X_0$ denote the set of all points $p$ in $T$ with the following property: for all the $z$-ultrafilters $\mathcal{A}$ on $X$ that converge to $p$, the $z$-filters $\tau \# \mathcal{A}$ all converge in $Y$, and to a common limit $\tau_0 p$. Then $X_0 \supset X$.
