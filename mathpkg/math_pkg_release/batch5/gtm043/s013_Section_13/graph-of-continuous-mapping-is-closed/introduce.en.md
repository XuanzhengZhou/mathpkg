For any continuous mapping $\varphi: A \to B$ between topological spaces, its graph $\{(a, \varphi a) : a \in A\}$ is a closed subset of the product space $A \times B$. This is an important and frequently used fact in general topology. The proof constructs a continuous map from $A \times B$ onto the graph whose restriction to the graph is the identity.
