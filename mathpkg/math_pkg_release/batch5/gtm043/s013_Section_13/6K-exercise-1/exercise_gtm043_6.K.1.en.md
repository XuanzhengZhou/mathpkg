Let $N_1$ and $N_2$ denote the subsets of odd and even integers in $\mathbf{N}$, respectively, and let $Y$ be the subspace $N_1 \cup \operatorname{cl}_{\beta \mathbf{N}} N_2$ of $\beta \mathbf{N}$. Then $Y$ contains a copy of $\beta Y$.
