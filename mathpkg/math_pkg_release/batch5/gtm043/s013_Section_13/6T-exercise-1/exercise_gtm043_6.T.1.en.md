Let $f \in C^*(\mathbf{N})$, and $p \in \beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$, with $f^\beta(p) = 0$. If $f^\beta[U] = \{0\}$ for some neighborhood $U$ of $p$ in $\beta \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$, then there exists a sequence $(s_n)$ in $\mathbf{N}$ such that $p$ belongs to the closure of $\{s_n\}_{n \in \mathbf{N}}$ and $f(s_n) \to 0$.
