Theorem 15.13 reveals a fundamental connection between uniform space completions and the two central compactifications from the theory of rings of continuous functions. The completion of a completely regular space in the structure $\mathcal{C}(X)$ is the Hewitt realcompactification $vX$, while the completion in $\mathcal{C}^*(X)$ is the Stone-Cech compactification $\beta X$. This elegant result unifies the theories of uniform spaces and rings of continuous functions.
