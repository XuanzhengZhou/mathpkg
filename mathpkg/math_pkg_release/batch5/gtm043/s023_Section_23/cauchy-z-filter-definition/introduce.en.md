A Cauchy $z$-filter in a uniform space is a $z$-filter that contains sets of arbitrarily small diameter with respect to every pseudometric in the uniform structure. This definition extends the classical Cauchy criterion from metric spaces to general uniform spaces using the language of $z$-filters, which is natural in the context of rings of continuous functions.
