This succinct characterization, derived from Corollary 14.22 and Theorem 14.25, states that $X$ is an $F$-space exactly when the ideal generated by $f$ and its absolute value $|f|$ is principal for every continuous function $f$. This provides one of the most direct algebraic tests for the $F$-space property.
