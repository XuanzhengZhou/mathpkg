For a completely regular space $X$, the following conditions are equivalent:

(1) $X$ is an $F$-space.

(2) For each $p \in \beta X$, the ideal $O^p$ is prime.

(3) For each $p \in \beta X$, the prime ideals contained in $M^p$ form a chain.

(4) For each $h \in C(X)$, $\operatorname{pos} h$ and $\operatorname{neg} h$ are completely separated.

(5) For each $f \in C(X)$, $(f, |f|)$ is a principal ideal in $C(X)$.

(6) For each $h \in C(X)$, $X - Z(h)$ is $C^*$-embedded in $X$.

(7) For every $f, g \in C(X)$ with $0 \leq f \leq g$, $f$ is a multiple of $g$.

(8) Every ideal in $C(X)$ is absolutely convex.

(9) Every finitely generated ideal in $C(X)$ is principal.

(10) Every finitely generated ideal in $C^*(X)$ is principal.
