Every uniform structure induces a natural topology called the uniform topology. Neighborhoods of a point $x$ are generated by $\epsilon$-balls with respect to the pseudometrics in the structure. A key feature is the formula for closure: a point belongs to the closure of a set $A$ precisely when its distance to $A$ is zero with respect to every pseudometric in the structure.
