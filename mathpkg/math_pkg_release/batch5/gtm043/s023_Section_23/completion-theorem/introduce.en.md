The Completion Theorem (15.9) states that every Hausdorff uniform space has a completion. The proof given in the text uses the Stone-Cech compactification $\beta X$ to construct $\gamma X$: each pseudometric $d$ is extended first from $X$ to $\beta X$ via continuity, then the equivalence relation of having zero $d$-distance for all $d$ is used to quotient to a Hausdorff space. This approach elegantly reveals the relationship between $\beta X$ and $\gamma X$.
