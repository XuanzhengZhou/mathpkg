Theorem 15.16 provides four equivalent characterizations of precompact uniform spaces — those whose completion is compact. The equivalences relate finiteness of $d$-discrete sets, total boundedness (covering by finitely many small zero-sets), and the coincidence of the Stone-Cech compactification with the uniform completion. This theorem is the uniform-space analogue of the classical characterization of precompact metric spaces.
