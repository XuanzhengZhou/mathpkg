Every cozero-set in an $F$-space inherits the $F$-space property. This follows from the fact that cozero-sets are $C^*$-embedded in $F$-spaces (condition (6) of Theorem 14.25) and that $C^*$-embedded subspaces of $F$-spaces are $F$-spaces.
