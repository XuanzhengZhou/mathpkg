Let $f(x) = \sin \pi x$ ($x \in \mathbf{Q}$), and let $p \in \operatorname{cl}_{\beta \mathbf{Q}} \mathbf{N} - \mathbf{N}$. If $V$ is an open set in $\beta \mathbf{Q}$ containing $p$, then $f^\beta$ assumes both positive and negative values on $V$. [$V$ is a neighborhood of some point of $\mathbf{N}$.]
