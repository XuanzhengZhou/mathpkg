The mapping $\mathcal{V} \rightarrow P = \{f \in C(\mathbf{T}): Z(f) \cap \mathbf{N} \in \mathcal{V}\}$ is one-one from the family of all free ultrafilters on $\mathbf{N}$ onto the family of all nonmaximal, prime $z$-ideals in $C(\mathbf{T})$ contained in $M^t$. Compare 14G.3.
