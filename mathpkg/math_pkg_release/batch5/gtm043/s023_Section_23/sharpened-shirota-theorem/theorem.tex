The following are equivalent for any Hausdorff uniform space $[X; \mathcal{D}]$ in which every $d$-discrete subspace (for all $d \in \mathcal{D}$) has nonmeasurable cardinal:

(1) $X$ is complete.

(2) $X$ is realcompact as a topological space.

(3) Every real $z$-ultrafilter on $X$ is a Cauchy $z$-filter and is fixed.
