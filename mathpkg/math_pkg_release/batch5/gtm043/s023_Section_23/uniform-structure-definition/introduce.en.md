A uniform structure on a set $X$ is defined as a family of pseudometrics closed under finite suprema and under a certain completeness condition (any pseudometric that can be uniformly approximated by members of the family belongs to the family). This approach, using pseudometrics rather than entourages, provides efficient access to uniform space theory and directly connects to continuous functions on the space.
