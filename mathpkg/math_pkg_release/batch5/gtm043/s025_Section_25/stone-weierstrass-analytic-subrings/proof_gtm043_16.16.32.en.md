Let $\bar{B}$ denote the family in question. It is an intersection of analytic subrings of the type in Theorem 16.30, hence analytic. Obviously $\bar{B} \supset B$. For any analytic subring $A$ containing $B$, by Lemma 16.31 every stationary set of $A$ is contained in a connected one, which is a stationary set of $B$, hence of $\bar{B}$. By the Stone-Weierstrass theorem, $\operatorname{cl} A \supset \bar{B}$, i.e., $A \supset \bar{B}$.
