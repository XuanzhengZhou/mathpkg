A subring $A$ of $C^*(X)$ is called an \textit{algebraic subring} provided that
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] all constant functions belong to $A$; and
\item[(ii)] $f^2 \in A$ implies $f \in A$.
\end{enumerate}
The set $A_0$ of all functions in $C^*(X)$ with finite range is the smallest algebraic subring of $C^*(X)$.
