Let $B$ be any subfamily of $C^*(X)$. The intersection $A$ of all analytic subrings containing $B$ is the smallest analytic subring containing $B$. $B$ is called an \emph{analytic base} for $A$. The smallest analytic subring of $C^*(X)$ has base $\emptyset$; it is the closure of $A_0$, the algebraic subring of functions with finite range.
