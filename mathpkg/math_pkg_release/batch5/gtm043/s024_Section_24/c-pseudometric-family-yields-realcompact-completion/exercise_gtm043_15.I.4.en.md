Let $X$ be a completely regular space. Prove: If an admissible structure $\mathcal{D}$ on $X$ is generated by a family of pseudometrics $\psi_f$, with $f \in C(X)$, then $\gamma[X; \mathcal{D}]$ is realcompact. [15.23(a) and Theorem 15.21.] The converse is not true, as is shown by 15.23(b). See also 15L.4,5.
