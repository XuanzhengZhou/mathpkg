Prove: The uniform structure on $Y$ is contained in $\mathcal{C}(Y)$. [Consider the pseudometric $\psi_f \vee \psi_{g_\epsilon}$, where $f(x) = \sum_n x_n$, and $g_\epsilon(x) = 2^n(|x_n| - \epsilon)$ for $|x_n| > \epsilon$.]
