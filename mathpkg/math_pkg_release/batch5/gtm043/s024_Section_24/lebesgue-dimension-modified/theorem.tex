The dimension of $X$, denoted by $\dim X$, is the least cardinal $m$ such that every basic cover of $X$ has a basic refinement of order at most $m$. Clearly, $0 \leq \dim X \leq \aleph_0$; and $\dim X = \aleph_0$ provided that for every $n \in \mathbb{N}$, there exists a basic cover having no basic refinement of order $\leq n$.
