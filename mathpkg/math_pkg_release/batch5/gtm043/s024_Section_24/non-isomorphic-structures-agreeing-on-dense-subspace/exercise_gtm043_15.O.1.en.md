Let $\mathcal{U}$ and $\mathcal{U}$ be Hausdorff uniform structures on $T$, and let $X$ be a subset of $T$, dense in both uniform topologies, and having the same relative structure in $\mathcal{U}$ as in $\mathcal{U}$. Show that the spaces $[T; \mathcal{U}]$ and $[T; \mathcal{U}]$ need not be uniformly isomorphic. [Take for $X$ an open interval of $\mathbf{R}$ with one point deleted.]
