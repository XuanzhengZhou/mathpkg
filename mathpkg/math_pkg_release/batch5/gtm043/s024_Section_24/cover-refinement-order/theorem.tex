A cover of $X$ is a finite collection of open sets whose union is $X$. A family $\mathcal{V}$ refines $\mathcal{U}$ if for every $V \in \mathcal{V}$ there exists $U \in \mathcal{U}$ such that $V \subset U$. The order of a cover $\mathcal{U}$ is the largest integer $n$ for which there exist $n+1$ members of $\mathcal{U}$ with nonempty intersection.
