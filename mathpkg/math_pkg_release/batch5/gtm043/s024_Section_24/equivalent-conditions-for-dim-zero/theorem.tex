For a completely regular space $X$, consider the conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\dim X = 0$.
\item[(b)] Any two completely separated sets in $X$ are separated by a partition.
\item[(c)] $X$ has a base of open-and-closed sets.
\item[(d)] $\beta X$ is totally disconnected.
\end{enumerate}
