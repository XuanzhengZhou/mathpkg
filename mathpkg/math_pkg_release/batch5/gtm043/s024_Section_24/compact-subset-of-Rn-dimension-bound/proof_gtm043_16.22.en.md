Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a cover of $X$. By Lemma 16.21, there exists $\epsilon > 0$ such that the cells (in $X$) of radius $\epsilon$ refine $\mathcal{U}$. Therefore, the system of cells of Lemma 16.20, scaled down by a factor of $\epsilon$, and restricted to $X$, is a refinement of $\mathcal{U}$ of order $\leq n$. Thus, $\dim X \leq n$.
