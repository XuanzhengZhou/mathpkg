Let $E$ be the unit ball in real, separable Hilbert space (see 15D.3), and let $Y$ be the subspace of $E$ consisting of all points $x$ having at most one coordinate $x_n$ different from $0$. Prove that $Y$ is complete. [If some member of a Cauchy $z$-filter $\mathcal{F}$ does not contain $(0)$, then it is bounded away from $(0)$. It follows that $\mathcal{F}$ contains a compact set. Alternatively, use 15F.2.]
