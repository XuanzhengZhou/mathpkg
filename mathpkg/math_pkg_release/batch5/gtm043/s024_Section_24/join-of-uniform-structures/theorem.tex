Let $D$ and $E$ be uniform structures on $X$. The smallest structure containing both is denoted by $D \vee E$. Then $D \vee E$ is generated by all pseudometrics of the form $d \vee e$, for $d \in D$ and $e \in E$. Furthermore,
$$c[X; D \vee E] = c[X; D] \cap c[X; E].$$
If $D$ and $E$ are admissible, then $D \vee E$ is admissible.
