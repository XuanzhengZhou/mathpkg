Given nonempty open sets $T_1, \cdots, T_s$ in $\mathbf{R}^n$, there exist vectors $y^k \in T_k$ such that for any $n+1$ of these vectors $y^{k_i}$, and any vector $y \in \mathbf{R}^n$, the equations
$$\sum_{i=1}^{n+1} a_i y^{k_i} = y, \quad \sum_{i=1}^{n+1} a_i = 0$$
have only the trivial solution $a_1 = \cdots = a_{n+1} = 0$.
