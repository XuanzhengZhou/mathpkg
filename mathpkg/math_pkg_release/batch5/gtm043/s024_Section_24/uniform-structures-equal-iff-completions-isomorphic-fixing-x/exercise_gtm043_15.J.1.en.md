Let $\gamma X$ and $\gamma X$ be the completions of $X$ in the admissible structures $\mathcal{D}$ and $\mathcal{D}$, respectively. Prove: $\mathcal{D} = \mathcal{D}$ if and only if there exists a uniform isomorphism of $\gamma X$ onto $\gamma X$ that leaves $X$ pointwise fixed.
