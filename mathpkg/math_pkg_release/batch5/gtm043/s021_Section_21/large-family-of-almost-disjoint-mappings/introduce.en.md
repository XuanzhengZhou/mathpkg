This lemma constructs a large family of pairwise "almost disjoint" functions: any two agree on fewer than $\mathfrak{m}$ points. The proof uses Zorn's lemma to maximize the family, then shows by contradiction that it must exceed $\mathfrak{m}$. This is a standard set-theoretic tool used to sharpen bounds on residue class fields.
