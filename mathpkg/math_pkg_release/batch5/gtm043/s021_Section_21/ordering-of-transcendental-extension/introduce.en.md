For a real-closed field $E$, the ordering of the simple transcendental extension $E(x)$ is completely determined by the cut of $x$ in $E$. The proof factors polynomials into linear and quadratic pieces over $E$, showing the sign of each factor depends only on comparing $x$ with roots in $E$. This is crucial for the back-and-forth isomorphism construction between $\eta_1$-fields.
