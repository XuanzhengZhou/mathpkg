An $\eta_1$-field is an ordered field whose underlying order is an $\eta_1$-set. Such fields are automatically nonarchimedean, as archimedean fields have countable cofinality, contradicting the $\eta_1$ property. This concept is central to the isomorphism theory of hyper-real fields.
