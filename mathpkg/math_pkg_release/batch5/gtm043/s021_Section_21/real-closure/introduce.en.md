The real-closure is the smallest real-closed field containing $E$ as an ordered subfield. For example, $\mathcal{R}\mathbb{Q}$ is the field of all algebraic real numbers. The real-closure operation is central to the transfinite induction in the isomorphism theorem for $\eta_1$-fields.
