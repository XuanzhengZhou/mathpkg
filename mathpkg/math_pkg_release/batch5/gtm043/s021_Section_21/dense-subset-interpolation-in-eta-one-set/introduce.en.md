Any dense subset of an $\eta_1$-set inherits the interpolation property for countable gaps. The proof is a sandwich argument: find $v_1$ and $v_2$ in $F$ with $A < v_1 < v_2 < B$, then use density to pick $v \in S$ between them. This is used in constructing dense transcendence bases.
