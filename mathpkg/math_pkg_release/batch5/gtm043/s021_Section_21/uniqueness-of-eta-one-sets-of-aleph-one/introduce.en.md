Under the continuum hypothesis, all $\eta_1$-sets of cardinality $\aleph_1$ are order-isomorphic. The proof uses back-and-forth transfinite induction: well-order both sets in type $\omega_1$ and inductively match elements using the $\eta_1$ interpolation property. An $\eta_1$-set of any cardinal contains a copy of every ordered set of cardinality $\leq \aleph_1$.
