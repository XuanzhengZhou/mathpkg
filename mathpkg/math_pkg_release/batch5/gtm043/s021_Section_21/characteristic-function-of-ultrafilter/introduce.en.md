The characteristic function of an ultrafilter $\mathcal{U}$ assigns $1$ to members of $\mathcal{U}$ and $0$ to non-members. This establishes a correspondence between ultrafilters and finitely additive $\{0,1\}$-valued functions, which is fundamental to the characterization of realcompactness for discrete spaces.
