A $\{0,1\}$-valued measure on a set $X$ is a countably additive function from the power set of $X$ to $\{0, 1\}$. Such measures serve as a set-theoretic tool for characterizing realcompactness of discrete spaces. They generalize the notion of ultrafilters by adding countable additivity.
