title: "Closed discrete subspaces of W(alpha) x N"
---
Let $X = W(\alpha) \times N$, where $|\alpha|$ is a (hypothetical) measurable cardinal.

1. Every closed discrete subspace of $X$ is realcompact.

2. There exists a space $Y$, in which $X$ is dense, such that not every closed discrete subspace of $Y$ is realcompact.
