A transcendence base for $F/E$ is a maximal algebraically independent subset whose existence follows from Zorn's lemma. Its cardinality is independent of the choice of base and measures the "algebraic dimension" of the extension. For uncountable ordered fields, the cardinal equals the transcendence degree over $\mathbb{Q}$.
