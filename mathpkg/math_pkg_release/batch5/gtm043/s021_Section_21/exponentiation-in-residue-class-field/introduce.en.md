Real exponentiation $u^r$ can be defined in $C/M$ by lifting a nonnegative representative $f$ and computing $f^r$ pointwise. Well-definedness follows from $f^r - g^r$ vanishing on $Z(f - g)$. This operation preserves order and algebraic laws, and is essential for lower-bounding the transcendence degree of hyper-real fields.
