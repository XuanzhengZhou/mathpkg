title: "Beta X for discrete X"
---
Let $X$ be any infinite discrete space, and let $S$ denote the set of all points $p \in \beta X$ such that every neighborhood of $p$ meets $X$ in a set of cardinal $|X|$. Then $S$ is compact, and $S$ contains a copy of $\beta X$. [Cf. 6.10(a).] Hence $|S| = 2^{|X|}$.
