Let $U_p$ and $V_p$ be disjoint neighborhoods of $E$ and $p$, respectively. A finite collection
$$\{V_{p_1}, \cdots, V_{p_n}\}$$
covers $F$, by compactness of $F$. Then $\bigcap_{k=1}^{n} U_{p_k}$ and $\bigcup_{k=1}^{n} V_{p_k}$ are disjoint neighborhoods of $E$ and $F$, respectively.
