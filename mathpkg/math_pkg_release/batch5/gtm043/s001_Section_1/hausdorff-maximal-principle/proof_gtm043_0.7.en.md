The Hausdorff Maximal Principle is stated as an axiom-equivalent in this text. It is equivalent to the Axiom of Choice and to the Well-Ordering Theorem. All three forms are used without proof in this book.

References. [B$_2$, Chapters 1--3], [B$_3$, Chapters 2--3], [H$_1$, pp. 45--83, 97--141], [K$_9$, pp. 31--36], and [S$_5$].
