Let $X$ be dense in $T$. Prove directly from the fact that $T$ is a Hausdorff space that
$$|T| \leq 2^{2^{|X|}}.$$
[Associate with each point $p$ of $T$ the family of all sets of the form $V \cap X$, where $V$ is a neighborhood of $p$.] (Hence $|\beta Q| \leq 2^c$ and $|\beta R| \leq 2^c$.)
