Every $G_\delta$-point is a zero-set (3.11(b)). If a point $p \in \beta X - X$ were a $G_\delta$ in $\beta X$, then $\{p\}$ would be a nonempty zero-set disjoint from $X$, and by Theorem 9.5 would contain a copy of $\beta N$, contradicting that $\{p\}$ is a singleton.
