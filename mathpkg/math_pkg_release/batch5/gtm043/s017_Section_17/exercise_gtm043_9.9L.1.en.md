Let $X$ be the subspace of $W(\omega_2)$ obtained by deleting all nonisolated points having a countable base. As an ordered subset of $W(\omega_2)$, $X$ is of order type $\omega_2$, and hence no subset of $X$ of power $< \aleph_2$ is cofinal. [9K.1.] However, the topology on $X$ is not the interval topology (3O).
