The product theorem for realcompact spaces states that the arbitrary product of realcompact spaces is again realcompact. This parallels the Tychonoff theorem for compact spaces, establishing that realcompactness is also preserved under arbitrary products. The result relies on the fact that each projection map extends continuously to the Hewitt realcompactification and that a product of completely regular spaces is completely regular.
