If a subset of $$W \times N^*$$ contains points $$(\alpha_n, n)$$ for arbitrarily large $$n \in N$$, then its closure meets $$W$$. [5M.6.] Hence every real-valued continuous function on the Tychonoff plank $$T$$ becomes constant on a suitable set of this form.
