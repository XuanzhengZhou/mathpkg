Let $$X$$ be realcompact and let $$f \in C(X)$$. Then

$$X - Z(f) = f^{\leftarrow}[\mathbb{R} - \{0\}].$$

Since $$\mathbb{R} - \{0\}$$ is realcompact, Theorem 8.13 implies that $$X - Z(f)$$ is realcompact.
