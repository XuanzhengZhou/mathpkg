Let $$X$$ be dense and $$C^*$$-embedded in $$T$$. Establish directly (i.e., without invoking Theorem 8.6): If $$X$$ is $$C$$-embedded, then $$\operatorname{cl}_T Z(f) = Z(f^{\circ})$$, for $$f \in C(X)$$, and $$f^{\circ}$$ its continuous extension to $$T$$.
