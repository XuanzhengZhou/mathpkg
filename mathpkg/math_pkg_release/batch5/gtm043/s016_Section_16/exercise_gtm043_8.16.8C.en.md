**Stone Extension of an Injection.** Let $$S$$ be a cozero-set in $$X$$, and let $$\sigma$$ denote the identity map on $$S$$ and $$\bar{\sigma}$$ its Stone extension into $$\beta X$$. For each $$x \in \operatorname{cl}_X S - S$$, $$\bar{\sigma}^{\leftarrow}(x)$$ is contained in $$\beta S - vS$$.
