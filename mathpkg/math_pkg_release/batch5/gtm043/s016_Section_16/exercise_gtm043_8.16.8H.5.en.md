A realcompact space can have a free $$z$$-filter with the countable intersection property. In fact, a space has the Lindelöf property if and only if every $$z$$-filter with the countable intersection property is fixed. [Sufficiency. Pass from closed sets to zero-sets as in the proof of Lemma 4.10.]
