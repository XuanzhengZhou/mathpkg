Since each one-element set $$\{y\}$$ is a $$G_{\delta}$$, it is a zero-set in the completely regular space $$Y$$. Hence $$Y - \{y\}$$ is a cozero-set, and therefore realcompact by Corollary 8.14. By Theorem 8.9, this implies that every proper subspace of $$Y$$ is realcompact. Thus every subspace of $$Y$$ is realcompact.

(Alternatively, this follows from the equivalence (4) $$\Leftrightarrow$$ (3) in Theorem 8.17.)
