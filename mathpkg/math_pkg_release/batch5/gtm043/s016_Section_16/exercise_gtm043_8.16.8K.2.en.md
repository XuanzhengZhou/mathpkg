Construct functions $$f, g \in C(X)$$ such that $$g \in M^t$$, and $$Z(f)$$ is a neighborhood of $$Z(g)$$, but $$f \notin O^t$$, i.e., $$\operatorname{cl} Z(f)$$ is not a neighborhood of $$t$$. Compare Theorem 7.14.
