Let $X$ be dense in $T$. The following statements are equivalent.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] Every continuous mapping $\tau$ from $X$ into any realcompact space $Y$ has an extension to a continuous mapping from $T$ into $Y$.
\item[(2)] $X$ is $C$-embedded in $T$.
\item[(3)] If a countable family of zero-sets in $X$ has empty intersection, then their closures in $T$ have empty intersection.
\item[(4)] For any countable family of zero-sets $Z_n$ in $X$,
  $$\mathrm{cl}_T \bigcap_n Z_n = \bigcap_n \mathrm{cl}_T Z_n.$$
\item[(5)] Every point of $T$ is the limit of a unique, real, $z$-ultrafilter on $X$.
\item[(6)] $X \subset T \subset vX$.
\item[(7)] $vT = vX$.
\end{enumerate}
