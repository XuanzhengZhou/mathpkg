This theorem provides seven equivalent characterizations of those spaces $T$ lying between $X$ and $\beta X$ in which $X$ is both dense and $C$-embedded. Notably, condition (6) shows that such spaces are precisely those with $X \subset T \subset vX$, making $vX$ the maximal one. Among them, $vX$ is the only realcompact space. The theorem also establishes that $C$-embedding is equivalent to the extension property for mappings into arbitrary realcompact spaces (condition (1)), which generalizes the analogous property for compact spaces.
