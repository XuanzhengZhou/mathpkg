If $X$ is $C$-embedded in a subspace $T$ of $\beta X$, then for each $p \in T$, every $f \in C(X)$ extends continuously to $p$, so $f^*(p) \neq \infty$. By condition (2) of Theorem 8.4, $p \in vX$. Hence $T \subset vX$.
