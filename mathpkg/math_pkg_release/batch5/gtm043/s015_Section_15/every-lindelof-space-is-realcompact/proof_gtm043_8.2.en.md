In a Lindel\"of space, every $z$-filter with the countable intersection property is fixed. Therefore every real $z$-ultrafilter on $X$ is fixed, which means that every real maximal ideal in $C(X)$ is fixed. By definition (5.9), this is equivalent to $X$ being realcompact.
