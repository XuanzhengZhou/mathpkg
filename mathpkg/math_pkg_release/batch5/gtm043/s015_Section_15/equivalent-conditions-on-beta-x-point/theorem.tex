For a point $p \in \beta X$, the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $M^p$ is real --- alternatively, $A^p$ has the countable intersection property.
\item[(2)] $f^*(p) \neq \infty$, for all $f \in C(X)$.
\item[(3)] $f^*(p) = M^p(f)$ for all $f \in C(X)$.
\item[(4)] $f^*(p) = 0$ implies $M^p(f) = 0$, i.e., $f \in M^p$, for all $f \in C(X)$.
\end{enumerate}
