Under the isomorphism $C(W^*) \cong C(W)$ given by restriction, the fixed maximal ideal $M_{\omega_1}$ in $C(W^*)$ corresponds to the ideal

$$M^{\omega_1} = \{f \in C(W) : f \text{ vanishes on a tail } W - W(\alpha) \text{ for some } \alpha < \omega_1\}.$$

For $f \in C(W)$ with final constant value $r$ on a tail, we have $f \equiv r \pmod{M^{\omega_1}}$ and $M^{\omega_1}(f) = r$.
