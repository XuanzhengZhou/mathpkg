For every element $a$ of a lattice-ordered ring, $|a| \geq 0$.

[Put $x = |a| \wedge 0$, $y = a \wedge 0$, and $z = -a \wedge 0$. Then $y \leq x$ and $z \leq x$. Next, $z = y - a$. Hence

$$0 \leq (x - y) \wedge (x - z) = (x - y) + (0 \wedge a) \leq |a|.$$]
