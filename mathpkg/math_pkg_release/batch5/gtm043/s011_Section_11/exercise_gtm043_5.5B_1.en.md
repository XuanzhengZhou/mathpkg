An arbitrary intersection of [absolutely] convex ideals is [absolutely] convex. An arbitrary union of [absolutely] convex ideals is totally ordered, hence a lattice, but the canonical mapping of $A$ onto $A/I$, which is order-preserving, is not a lattice homomorphism.
