If there exists $\epsilon > 0$ such that $f - \epsilon$ is positive on some member of $E(I)$, then $f \equiv |f| \not\equiv 0 \pmod{I}$. And if $I$ is maximal, the converse holds as well. [2L.14.] (For maximal ideals, these results also follow from 7.2 and 7R.2.)
