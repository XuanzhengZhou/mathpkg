$X$ is pseudocompact if and only if every countable, $C$-embedded subset is compact. [Necessity. Every $C$-embedded subset of $X$ is pseudocompact; apply 2. Sufficiency. Corollary 1.21.] (The space $\Lambda$ of 6P is pseudocompact and has a countable, $C^*$-embedded subset that is not compact.)
