Verification of the Hausdorff axiom is trivial. Now let $H$ and $K$ be disjoint closed sets. For each $\tau \in H$, let $U_{\tau}$ be an open interval of the form $[\sigma, \tau]$ that does not meet $K$; correspondingly, define $V_{\tau}$ for $\tau \in K$. Then $\bigcup_{\tau \in H} U_{\tau}$ and $\bigcup_{\tau \in K} V_{\tau}$ are disjoint open sets separating $H$ and $K$. Hence $W(\alpha)$ is normal.
