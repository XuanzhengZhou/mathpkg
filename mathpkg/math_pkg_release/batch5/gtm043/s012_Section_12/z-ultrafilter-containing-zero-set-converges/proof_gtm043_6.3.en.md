Let $\mathcal{E}$ be the $z$-filter on $T$ of all zero-set-neighborhoods (in $T$) of $p$, and let $\mathcal{B}$ be the trace of $\mathcal{E}$ on $X$. Since $p \in \operatorname{cl}_T Z$, $\mathcal{B} \cup \{Z\}$ has the finite intersection property, and so is contained in a $z$-ultrafilter $\mathcal{A}$. Clearly, $\mathcal{A}$ converges to $p$.

This result holds even if $X$ is not dense in $T$.
