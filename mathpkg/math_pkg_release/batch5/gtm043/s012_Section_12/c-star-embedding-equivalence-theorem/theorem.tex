Let $X$ be dense in $T$. The following are equivalent.

(1) $X$ is $C^*$-embedded in $T$.

(2) Every continuous mapping from $X$ into a compact space can be extended continuously to $T$.

(3) Any two disjoint zero-sets in $X$ have disjoint closures in $T$.

(4) For any two zero-sets $Z_1$ and $Z_2$ in $X$,

$$\operatorname{cl}_T(Z_1 \cap Z_2) = \operatorname{cl}_T Z_1 \cap \operatorname{cl}_T Z_2.$$

(5) Distinct $z$-ultrafilters on $X$ have distinct limits in $T$.
