When $X$ is dense in $T$, every point of the larger space $T$ arises as the limit of some $z$-ultrafilter on $X$. This follows from the preceding lemma (6.3(a)) by taking $Z = X$. The result ensures that in the Stone-Čech compactification $\beta X$, the new points correspond bijectively to $z$-ultrafilters, establishing a natural correspondence crucial for the maximal ideal characterization.
