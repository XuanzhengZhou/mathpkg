This theorem completely characterizes when residue class fields are real. For $C^*(X)$, the ring of bounded continuous functions, every maximal ideal is real -- the quotient field is always $\mathbf{R}$. For $C(X)$, all maximal ideals are real precisely when $X$ is pseudocompact (every continuous real-valued function on $X$ is bounded). Thus hyper-real residue class fields exist exactly for non-pseudocompact spaces.
