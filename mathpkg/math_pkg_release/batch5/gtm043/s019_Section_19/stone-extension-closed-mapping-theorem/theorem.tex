Let $\tau$ be a continuous mapping from $X$ onto a dense subspace of $Y$, let $\bar{\tau}$ be its Stone extension into $\beta Y$, and let $X_0 = \bar{\tau}^{\leftarrow}[Y]$. Then $\tau_0 = \bar{\tau}|X_0$ is a closed mapping from $X_0$ onto $Y$, and the preimage of every compact set is compact.

The space $X_0$ is the largest subspace of $\beta X$ to which $\tau$ has a continuous extension into $Y$. It is the only one for which the extension is a closed mapping such that the preimage of each point is compact.
