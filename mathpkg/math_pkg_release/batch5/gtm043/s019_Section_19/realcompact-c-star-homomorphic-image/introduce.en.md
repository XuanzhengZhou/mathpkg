This proposition characterizes when the ring of bounded continuous functions $C^*(Y)$ is a homomorphic image of the full ring $C(Y)$, for a realcompact space $Y$. The condition is that $Y$ must contain a copy of its own Stone-Cech compactification $\beta Y$, which is a very restrictive requirement — typically only satisfied when $Y$ is already compact (so $Y = \beta Y$) or in certain pathological examples like $Y = N_1 \cup \beta N_2$.
