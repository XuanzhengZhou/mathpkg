The mapping $\delta^\#$ sends each filter $F$ on $X$ to the $z$-filter consisting of all zero-sets belonging to $F$. This is a natural many-one surjection from the set of all filters to the set of all $z$-filters. It provides the bridge between convergence theory (filters) and the theory of ideals in $C(X)$ (where $z$-filters arise from zero-sets of functions). The mapping also preserves convergence of filters.
