Theorem 2.12 extends the ideal/$z$-filter correspondence to the prime level: a $z$-filter is prime exactly when the associated $z$-ideal $Z^{\leftarrow}[\mathcal{F}]$ is prime. This yields a one-to-one correspondence between prime $z$-filters on $X$ and prime $z$-ideals in $C(X)$.
