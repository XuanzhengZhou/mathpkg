A $z$-ultrafilter is a maximal $z$-filter on a topological space $X$. It is the topological counterpart of an ultrafilter, restricted to the lattice of zero-sets. $z$-ultrafilters correspond one-to-one with maximal ideals in $C(X)$ via the mapping $Z$, forming a central bridge between the algebraic structure of the function ring and the topology of $X$.
