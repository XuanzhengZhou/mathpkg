For an ideal $I$ in $C(X)$, define
$$\theta(I) = \{p \in \beta X : M^p \supset I\}.$$
Then $\theta(I) = \bigcap_{f \in I} \mathrm{cl}_{\beta X} Z(f)$, that is, $\theta(I)$ is the set of all cluster points of the $z$-filter $Z[I]$.
