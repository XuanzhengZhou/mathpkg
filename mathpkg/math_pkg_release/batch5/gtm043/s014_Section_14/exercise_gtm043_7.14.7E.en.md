The family $C_K(X)$ of all functions in $C(X)$ with compact support (4D) is the same as the family of all $f$ in $C^*(X)$ such that $Z_{\beta X}(f^\beta)$ is a neighborhood of $\beta X \setminus X$. Thus,
$$C_K(X) = \bigcap_{p \in \beta X \setminus X} O^p.$$
Hence $C_K$ is the intersection of all the free ideals in $C$, and of all the free ideals in $C^*$. [4D.5.]
