The equality $M_p \cap C^* = M^*_p$ for fixed ideals was established in 4.7. For the general case (c): The necessity of $M^p$ being real follows from result (b) [concerning real maximal ideals]. The sufficiency is obvious from result (a) [the general relationship between $M^p$ and $M^{*p}$].
