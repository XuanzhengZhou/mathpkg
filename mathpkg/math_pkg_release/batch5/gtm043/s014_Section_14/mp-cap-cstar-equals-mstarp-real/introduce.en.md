This theorem characterizes when the trace of a maximal ideal $M^p$ of $C(X)$ on the subring $C^*(X)$ of bounded functions coincides with the corresponding maximal ideal $M^{*p}$. The condition holds exactly when $M^p$ is a real maximal ideal. The necessity follows from earlier results about real ideals, while the sufficiency is immediate from the general relationship between $M^p$ and $M^{*p}$.
