If $M^p = (\cdots, f_\alpha, \cdots)$, then
$$\bigcap_\alpha \mathrm{cl}_{\beta X} Z(f_\alpha) = \{p\}.$$
In case the $f_\alpha$ are bounded (see 2A), $\bigcap_\alpha Z(f_\alpha^\beta)$ can contain points other than $p$.
