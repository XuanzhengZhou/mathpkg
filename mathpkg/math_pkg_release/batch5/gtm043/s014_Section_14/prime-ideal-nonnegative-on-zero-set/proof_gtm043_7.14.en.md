If $f$ is nonnegative on a zero-set $Z \in Z[O^p]$, then $f - |f|$ vanishes on $Z$. Since $Z \in Z[O^p]$, we have $Z \supset Z(h)$ for some $h \in O^p$. Then $f - |f|$ is a multiple of some function in $O^p$, hence $f - |f| \in O^p$. Since $O^p \subset P$, we have $f - |f| \in P$. In the ordered residue field $C/P$, this means $P(f) = P(|f|) \geq 0$.
