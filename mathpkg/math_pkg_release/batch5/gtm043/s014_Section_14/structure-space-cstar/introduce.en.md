The structure space of $C^*(X)$ is the set of all maximal ideals in the ring of bounded continuous functions on a topological space $X$, equipped with the Stone topology. This topology is defined by taking the sets $\mathfrak{E}^*(f) = \{M \in \mathfrak{M}^* : f \in M\}$ for $f \in C^*(X)$ as a base for the closed sets. Under the natural bijection $p \mapsto M^{*p}$ with the Stone-Čech compactification $\beta X$, the basic closed sets correspond to the zero-sets $Z_{\beta X}(f^\beta)$, making the structure space homeomorphic to $\beta X$.
