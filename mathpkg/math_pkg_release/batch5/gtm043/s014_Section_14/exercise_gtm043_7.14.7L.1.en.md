The following are equivalent.
(1) $X$ is a $P$-space (4J).
(2) $M^p = O^p$ for all $p \in \beta X$. Note that for the special case of a discrete space, (1) implies (2) by 6E.6.
(3) Every prime ideal in $C(X)$ is an intersection of maximal ideals.
(4) Every $z$-ideal in $C(X)$ is an intersection of maximal ideals.
