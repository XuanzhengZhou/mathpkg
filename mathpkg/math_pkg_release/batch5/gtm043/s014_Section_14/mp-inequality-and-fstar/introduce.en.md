This proposition establishes one-directional implications between inequalities in the ordered residue class field $C/M^p$ and the extended real numbers. If the residue class of $f$ is bounded above by a real number $r$, then the extended value $f^*(p)$ is also bounded by $r$. The dual statement also holds. However, the converses fail when the residue class differs from $f^*(p)$ by an infinitesimal amount.
