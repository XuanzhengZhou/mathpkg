The proof is similar to that of 11.4(a). Since $\sigma_*$ maps $X$ homeomorphically onto $\sigma_*[X]$ and $\sigma_*[X]$ is $C^*$-embedded in $P_*$, the restriction map from $C^*(\operatorname{cl} \sigma_*[X])$ to $C^*(\sigma_*[X])$ is an isomorphism, yielding the identification $f \mapsto \pi_{*f}|_{\operatorname{cl} \sigma_*[X]}$.
