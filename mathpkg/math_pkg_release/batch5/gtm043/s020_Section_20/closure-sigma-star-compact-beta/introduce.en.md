Theorem 11.5 provides a new construction of the Stone-Čech compactification. By Tychonoff's theorem, the subspace $\prod_{f \in C^*(X)} \operatorname{cl}_{\mathbf{R}} f[X]$ of $P_*$ is compact, and it contains $\sigma_*[X]$. Therefore $\operatorname{cl} \sigma_*[X]$ is a compactification in which $\sigma_*[X]$ is $C^*$-embedded, making it a model for $\beta X$.
