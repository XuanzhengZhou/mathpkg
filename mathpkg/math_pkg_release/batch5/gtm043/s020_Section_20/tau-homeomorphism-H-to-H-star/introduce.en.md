Theorem 11.11 shows how the model $H$ for $vX$ embeds naturally into the model $H_*$ for $\beta X$. The map $\tau$ is simply the restriction of the natural projection $P \to P_*$ (forgetting the unbounded coordinates). The identity $\tau \circ \sigma = \sigma_*$ confirms that this embedding respects the original embeddings of $X$. This gives a concrete realization of the fact that $vX$ can be embedded in $\beta X$.
