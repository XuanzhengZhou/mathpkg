Let $Y_1$ and $Y_2$ be realcompactifications of $X$. The functions in $C(X)$ that are extendable to $C(Y_1)$ are the same as those extendable to $C(Y_2)$ when and only when there exists a homeomorphism of $Y_1$ onto $Y_2$ that leaves $X$ pointwise fixed. [Theorem 10.6.] A similar result holds for $C^*$.
