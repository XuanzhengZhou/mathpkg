Let $Y_1$ and $Y_2$ be compactifications of $X$, and suppose that whenever two $z$-ultrafilters on $X$ converge to the same point in $Y_1$, they also converge to the same point in $Y_2$. Then there exists a continuous mapping of $Y_1$ onto $Y_2$ that leaves $X$ pointwise fixed, and sends $Y_1 - X$ onto $Y_2 - X$. [6G.4 and 6F.3.]
