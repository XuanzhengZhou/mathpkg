Let $f \in C(\beta \mathbf{R}^+)$, with $Z(f) - \mathbf{R}^+ \neq \emptyset$. There exist sequences $(r_n)$ and $(t_n)$ in $\mathbf{R}^+$, with $r_n < t_n < r_{n+1}$, and $\lim_n r_n = \infty$, such that $|f(x)| \leq 1/n$ whenever $r_n \leq x \leq t_n$.
