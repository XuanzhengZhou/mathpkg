If $X$ is realcompact, then $\sigma[X] = H$, and $\sigma$ embeds $X$ as a closed subspace of the product $P = \mathbf{R}^{C(X)}$. Conversely, any closed subspace of a product of real lines is realcompact, by Theorems 8.11 and 8.10 or by 8.12 and 11E.1.
