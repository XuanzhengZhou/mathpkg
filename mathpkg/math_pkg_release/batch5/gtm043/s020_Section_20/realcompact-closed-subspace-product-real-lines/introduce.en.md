Theorem 11.12 is the realcompact analogue of the classical characterization of compact spaces as closed subspaces of Tychonoff cubes. If $X$ is realcompact, then $\sigma[X] = H$ and $\sigma$ embeds $X$ as a closed subspace of the product $P$. Conversely, any closed subspace of a product of real lines is realcompact (by Theorems 8.11 and 8.10, or 8.12 and 11E.1).
