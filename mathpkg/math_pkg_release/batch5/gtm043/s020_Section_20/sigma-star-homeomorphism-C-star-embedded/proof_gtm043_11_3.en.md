By definition of $\sigma_*$, $\pi_{*f} \circ \sigma_* = f$, for each $f \in C^*(X)$. Therefore $\sigma_*$ is continuous, by 3.10(c). Next, the induced homomorphism $\sigma_*'$ maps $C(P_*)$ onto $C^*(X)$; for,

$$\sigma_*' \pi_{*f} = \pi_{*f} \circ \sigma_* = f.$$

By Theorem 10.3(b), $\sigma_*$ is a homeomorphism, and $\sigma_*[X]$ is $C^*$-embedded.
