Given \(g \in C(X)\), and \(\varepsilon > 0\), there exists \(f\) such that \(|g - f| \leq \varepsilon\) and \(Z(f)\) is a neighborhood of \(Z(g)\).

[Define \(f\) so that \(f(x) = 0\) wherever \(|g(x)| \leq \varepsilon\).]
