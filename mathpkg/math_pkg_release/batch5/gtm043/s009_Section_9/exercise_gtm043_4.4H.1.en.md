Let \(X\) and \(Y\) be spaces with the same underlying point set, such that the identity mapping \(\tau\) from \(X\) onto \(Y\) is continuous.

If \(\mathcal{F}\) is a \(z\)-filter on \(X\), then \(\tau^\#\mathcal{F} = \mathcal{F} \cap Z(Y)\).
