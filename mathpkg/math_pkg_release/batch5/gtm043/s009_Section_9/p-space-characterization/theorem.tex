For a completely regular space \(X\), the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] \(X\) is a \(P\)-space.
\item[(2)] For all \(p \in X\), \(M_p = O_p\), i.e., every function in \(C(X)\) is constant on a neighborhood of \(p\).
\item[(3)] Every zero-set in \(X\) is open.
\item[(4)] Every \(G_{\delta}\) in \(X\) is open.
\item[(5)] Every ideal in \(C(X)\) is a \(z\)-ideal.
\item[(6)] Every ideal in \(C(X)\) is an intersection of prime ideals.
\item[(7)] For every \(f, g \in C(X)\), the ideal \((f,g)\) is the principal ideal \((f^2 + g^2)\).
\item[(8)] For every \(f \in C(X)\), there exists \(f_0 \in C(X)\) such that \(f^2 f_0 = f\) (i.e., \(C(X)\) is a regular ring).
\item[(9)] Every ideal in \(C(X)\) is an intersection of maximal ideals.
\item[(10)] Every cozero-set in \(X\) is \(C\)-embedded.
\item[(11)] Every principal ideal in \(C(X)\) is generated by an idempotent.
\end{enumerate}
