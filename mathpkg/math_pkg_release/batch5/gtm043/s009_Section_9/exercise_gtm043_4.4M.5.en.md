The dense subspace \(\mathbf{N}\) is \(C^*\)-embedded in \(\Sigma\), but not \(C\)-embedded.

[If \(g \in C^*(\mathbf{N})\), then \(g^\#\mathcal{U}\) is a prime \(z\)-filter on \(\mathbf{R}\). Alternatively, use 1H.6.] Moreover, every subspace of \(\Sigma\) is \(C^*\)-embedded.
