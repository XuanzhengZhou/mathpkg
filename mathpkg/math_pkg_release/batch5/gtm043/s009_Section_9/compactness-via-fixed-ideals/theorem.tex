For a completely regular space \(X\), the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] \(X\) is compact.
\item[(2)] Every ideal in \(C(X)\) is fixed, i.e., every \(z\)-filter is fixed.
\item[(2^*)] Every ideal in \(C^*(X)\) is fixed.
\item[(3)] Every maximal ideal in \(C(X)\) is fixed, i.e., every \(z\)-ultrafilter is fixed.
\item[(3^*)] Every maximal ideal in \(C^*(X)\) is fixed.
\end{enumerate}
