If \(P\) is a prime ideal in \(C\), and \(P \subset M_p\), then \(P \supset O_p\).

[Let \(f\) vanish on a neighborhood \(U\) of \(p\); by complete regularity of \(X\), there exists \(g \in C\) such that \(g(p) = 1\) and \(g[X - U] = \{0\}\); then \(fg \in P\).] Compare Theorem 3.17.
