The set \(\mathfrak{M} = \mathfrak{M}(X)\) of all maximal ideals in \(C(X)\), endowed with the Stone topology (basic closed sets: \(\{M \in \mathfrak{M} : f \in M\}\) for \(f \in C(X)\)), is called the structure space of the ring \(C(X)\). For the definition and properties of the structure space of an abstract ring, see 7M.
