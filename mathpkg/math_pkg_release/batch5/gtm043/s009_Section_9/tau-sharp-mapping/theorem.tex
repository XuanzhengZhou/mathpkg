Let \(\tau : X \to Y\) be a continuous mapping and let \(\mathcal{F}\) be a \(z\)-filter on \(X\). The mapping \(\tau^\#\) is defined by

\[
\tau^\#\mathcal{F} = \{ Z \in Z(Y) : \tau^{\leftarrow}[Z] \in \mathcal{F} \}.
\]

The total preimage of a zero-set under a continuous map is a zero-set:
\[
\tau^{\leftarrow}[Z_Y(g)] = Z_X(g \circ \tau).
\]
