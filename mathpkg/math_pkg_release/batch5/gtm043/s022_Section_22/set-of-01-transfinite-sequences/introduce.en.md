In Section 13.16, the authors introduce the set $s$ of all $\{0,1\}$-valued transfinite sequences of length $\omega_1$, ordered lexicographically. This construction serves as the fundamental building block for studying $\eta_1$-sets and their Dedekind completions. The concepts of lower elements and upper elements are defined via the immediate successor relation, providing a combinatorial description of the order structure of $s$.
