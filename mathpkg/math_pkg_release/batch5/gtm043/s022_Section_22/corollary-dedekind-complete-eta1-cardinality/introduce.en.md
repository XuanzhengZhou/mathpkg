Corollary 13.24 provides a striking cardinality lower bound: any Dedekind-complete ordered set that contains an $\eta_1$-set must have cardinality at least $2^{\aleph_1}$. The proof is immediate from Theorem 13.22 (every $\eta_1$-set contains a copy of $Q$) and Theorem 13.23 (the Dedekind completion of $Q$ is $R$, with $|R| = 2^{\aleph_1}$). This result is applied in Chapter 14 to chains of prime ideals in $C(X)$.
