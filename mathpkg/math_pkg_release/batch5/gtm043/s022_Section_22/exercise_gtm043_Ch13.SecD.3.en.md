Let $F$ be an ordered field. If $F(\tau)$ is a transcendental extension of $F$, then the condition $\tau > F$ determines an ordering of $F(\tau)$; in this ordering, if $c_n \neq 0$, then
$$c_n \tau^n + \cdots + c_0 > 0 \iff c_n > 0.$$
