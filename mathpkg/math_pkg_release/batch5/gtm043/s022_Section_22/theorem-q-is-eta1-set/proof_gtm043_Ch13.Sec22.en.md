The proof proceeds by induction on $\alpha$. One shows that each $Q_\alpha$ is an $\eta_1$-set. The crucial inductive step uses Lemma 13.21 (countable cofinal/coinitial subsets) and the interpolation properties of Lemma 13.19. Since $Q_{\omega_1} = Q$, the induction completes the proof of the theorem.
