The formula
$$Z(f) = \bigcap_{n \in \mathbb{N}} \{x \in X: |f(x)| < 1/n\}$$
expresses $Z(f)$ as a countable intersection of open sets $\{x: |f(x)| < 1/n\}$ (each open by continuity of $f$). Hence $Z(f)$ is a $G_\delta$.
