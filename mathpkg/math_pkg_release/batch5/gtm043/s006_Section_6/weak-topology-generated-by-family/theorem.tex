Let $C'$ be a family of functions on a set $X$ to the real line $\mathbf{R}$, and let $\mathcal{S}$ denote the family of all sets $f^{\leftarrow}[V]$, where $f \in C'$ and $V$ is open in $\mathbf{R}$. The \emph{weak topology} generated by $C'$ is the smallest topology containing the family $\mathcal{S}$. Therefore, it is the topology for which $\mathcal{S}$ is a subbase.
