Let $\Phi$ be a family of mappings $\varphi_{\alpha}: X \to Y_{\alpha}$. The weak topology induced by $\Phi$ on $X$ is the smallest topology making all $\varphi_{\alpha}$ continuous. When this weak topology coincides with a given topology on $X$, we say that the latter is \emph{determined} by $\Phi$. The spaces $Y_{\alpha}$ need not be all the same.
