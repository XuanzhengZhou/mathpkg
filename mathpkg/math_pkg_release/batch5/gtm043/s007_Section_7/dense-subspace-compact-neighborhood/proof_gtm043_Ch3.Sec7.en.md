Let $U$ be the interior of a compact neighborhood of $p$ in $X$. Then $\operatorname{cl}_X U$ is compact, hence closed in $T$, so that $\operatorname{cl}_T U = \operatorname{cl}_X U$. Let $V$ be an open set in $T$ such that $V \cap X = U$. Since $X$ is dense, we have $\operatorname{cl}_T V = \operatorname{cl}_T U \subset X$, so that $V = U$. Thus $U$ is open in $T$, and the compact neighborhood of $p$ in $X$ contains $U$, which is an open neighborhood of $p$ in $T$.
