Let $X$ be a completely regular space. Let $\{x_n\}_{n \in \mathbf{N}}$ be a discrete set (not necessarily closed) in $X$, and let $(r_n)_{n \in \mathbf{N}}$ be any convergent sequence of real numbers. Then there exists $f \in C^*(X)$ such that $f(x_n) = r_n$ for all $n \in \mathbf{N}$. [There exists a family $(U_n)$ of disjoint open sets with $x_n \in U_n$.]
