This corollary follows immediately from the fact that $z$-ultrafilters are prime $z$-filters, and prime $z$-filters have at most one cluster point. If two distinct $z$-ultrafilters shared a common cluster point $p$, then each would be contained in $A_p$ (by 3.18(a)), contradicting their maximality (since $A_p$ is itself a $z$-ultrafilter).
