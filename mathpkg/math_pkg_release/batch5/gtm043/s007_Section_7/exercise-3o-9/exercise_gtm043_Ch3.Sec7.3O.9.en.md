$X$ is normal. [Given disjoint closed sets $H$ and $K$, define $f \in C(X)$ as follows. Let $f(x) = 0$ for all $x \in H$, and $f(y) = 1$ for all $y \in K$. Express the complement of $H \cup K$ as a union of maximal open intervals, and, using complete regularity, extend $f$ to all of $X$ by considering separately its definition on each maximal open interval.]
