Let $\mathcal{F}$ be a $z$-filter converging to $p$, and let $\mathcal{U}$ be a $z$-ultrafilter containing $\mathcal{F}$. By 3.16(a), $\mathcal{F}$ contains the $z$-filter of all zero-set-neighborhoods of $p$. Since $\mathcal{F} \subset \mathcal{U}$, it follows that $\mathcal{U}$ also contains all zero-set-neighborhoods of $p$. By 3.16(a) again, $\mathcal{U}$ converges to $p$.
