This proposition states a monotonicity property for $z$-ultrafilters with respect to convergence: if a $z$-filter converges to $p$, then any $z$-ultrafilter extending it also converges to $p$. This follows from the convergence criterion (3.16(a)): the original $z$-filter contains all zero-set-neighborhoods of $p$, and therefore any larger filter, in particular any $z$-ultrafilter containing it, also contains them.
