By 3.18(a), $p$ is a cluster point of a $z$-filter $\mathcal{F}$ if and only if $\mathcal{F} \subset A_p$. If $\mathcal{F}$ is a $z$-ultrafilter converging to $p$, then $p$ is a cluster point, so $\mathcal{F} \subset A_p$. Since $A_p$ is itself a $z$-ultrafilter, maximality forces $\mathcal{F} = A_p$. Thus $A_p$ is the unique $z$-ultrafilter converging to $p$.
