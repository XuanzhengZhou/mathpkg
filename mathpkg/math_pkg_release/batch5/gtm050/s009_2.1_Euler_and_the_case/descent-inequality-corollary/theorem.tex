Under the hypotheses of the infinite descent construction,
$$\alpha^3 \beta^3 \gamma^3 = 2a(a - 3b)(a + 3b) = 2p,$$
which is a proper divisor of either $z^3$ or $x^3$. Hence $|\alpha\beta\gamma|^3 < z^3$, and the new solution $(\alpha, \beta, \gamma)$ is strictly smaller than the original. By infinite descent, the original equation $x^3 + y^3 = z^3$ has no solution in positive integers.
