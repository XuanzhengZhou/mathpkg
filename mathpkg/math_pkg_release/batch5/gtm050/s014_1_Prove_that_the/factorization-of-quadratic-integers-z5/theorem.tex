Let $a$ and $b$ be relatively prime integers of opposite parity, and let
$$a^2 - 5b^2 = P_1^{n_1} P_2^{n_2} \cdots P_k^{n_k}$$
be the prime factorization of $a^2 - 5b^2$. Then there exist integers $p_i, q_i$ for $i = 1, 2, \ldots, k$ and integers $t, u$ such that
$$P_i = p_i^2 - 5q_i^2, \quad 1 = t^2 - 5u^2,$$
and
$$a + b\sqrt{5} = (p_1 + q_1\sqrt{5})^{n_1} (p_2 + q_2\sqrt{5})^{n_2} \cdots (p_k + q_k\sqrt{5})^{n_k} (t + u\sqrt{5}).$$
