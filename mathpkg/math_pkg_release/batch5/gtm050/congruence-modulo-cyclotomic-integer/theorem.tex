Two cyclotomic integers $f(\alpha)$ and $g(\alpha)$ are said to be \text{congruent modulo} a cyclotomic integer $h(\alpha)$, written
$$f(\alpha) \equiv g(\alpha) \pmod{h(\alpha)},$$
if $h(\alpha)$ divides their difference $f(\alpha) - g(\alpha)$. This relation is reflexive, symmetric, transitive, and consistent with addition and multiplication.
