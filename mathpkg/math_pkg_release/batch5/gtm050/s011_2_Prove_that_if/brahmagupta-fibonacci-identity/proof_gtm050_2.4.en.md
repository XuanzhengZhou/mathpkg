The identity follows by direct algebraic expansion:

$$(ap - bq)^2 + (aq + bp)^2 = (a^2 p^2 - 2abpq + b^2 q^2) + (a^2 q^2 + 2abpq + b^2 p^2)$$
$$= a^2(p^2 + q^2) + b^2(p^2 + q^2) = (a^2 + b^2)(p^2 + q^2).$$

The symmetric form is obtained by swapping $p$ and $q$:
$$(a^2 + b^2)(q^2 + p^2) = (aq - bp)^2 + (ap + bq)^2.$$
