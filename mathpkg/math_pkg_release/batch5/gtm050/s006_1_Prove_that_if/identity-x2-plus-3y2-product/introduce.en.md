This identity is the analog of the Brahmagupta-Fibonacci identity for the quadratic form $x^2 + 3y^2$. It shows that the product of two numbers representable in this form is again representable in this form. Fermat discovered that representations by $x^2 + 3y^2$ follow similar multiplicative rules.
