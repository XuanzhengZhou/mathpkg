Fermat numbers are numbers of the form $2^{2^n} + 1$. Fermat conjectured that all such numbers are prime, believing he could prove it. Although the first five are indeed prime, Euler later proved $F_5 = 2^{32} + 1$ is composite. Fermat numbers later gained profound importance when Gauss showed their connection to ruler-and-compass constructions of regular polygons.
