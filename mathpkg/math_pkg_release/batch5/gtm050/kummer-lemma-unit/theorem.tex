If $\lambda$ is a prime such that the class number of $\mathbb{Q}(\alpha)$ is not divisible by $\lambda$ (where $\alpha^\lambda = 1$, $\alpha \neq 1$), then every unit $e(\alpha)$ of the cyclotomic field that is congruent to an ordinary integer modulo $\lambda$ is a $\lambda$-th power.

That is, Condition (A) implies Condition (B) in Kummer's theorem on regular primes.
