Let $f(\alpha) = a_0 + a_1\alpha + a_2\alpha^2 + \cdots + a_{\lambda-1}\alpha^{\lambda-1}$ be a cyclotomic integer. Its conjugates are the $\lambda-1$ cyclotomic integers
$$f(\alpha),\ f(\alpha^2),\ f(\alpha^3),\ \ldots,\ f(\alpha^{\lambda-1})$$
obtained by replacing $\alpha$ with its powers $\alpha^2, \alpha^3, \ldots, \alpha^{\lambda-1}$ in the polynomial expression $f$. The operation is well-defined because if $f(\alpha) = g(\alpha)$, then for each $j = 2, 3, \ldots, \lambda-1$, $f(\alpha^j) = g(\alpha^j)$.
