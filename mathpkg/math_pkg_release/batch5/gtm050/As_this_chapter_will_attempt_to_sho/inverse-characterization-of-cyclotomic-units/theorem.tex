If $f(\alpha)$ and $g(\alpha)$ are cyclotomic integers such that $f(\alpha)g(\alpha) = 1$, then $f(\alpha)$ is a unit and
$$g(\alpha) = f(\alpha^2) f(\alpha^3) \cdots f(\alpha^{\lambda-1}).$$
In particular, the inverse of a unit $f(\alpha)$ is given by the product of all its conjugates except $f(\alpha)$ itself:
$$f(\alpha)^{-1} = f(\alpha^2) f(\alpha^3) \cdots f(\alpha^{\lambda-1}).$$
