The ring of cyclotomic integers for a prime $\lambda$ is isomorphic to the quotient
$$\mathbb{Z}[\alpha] \;\big/\; \langle 1 + \alpha + \alpha^2 + \cdots + \alpha^{\lambda-1} \rangle,$$
where $\mathbb{Z}[\alpha]$ denotes the ring of polynomials in one variable $\alpha$ with integer coefficients, and addition, subtraction, and multiplication are defined in the obvious way subject to the single relation
$$1 + \alpha + \alpha^2 + \cdots + \alpha^{\lambda-1} = 0.$$
