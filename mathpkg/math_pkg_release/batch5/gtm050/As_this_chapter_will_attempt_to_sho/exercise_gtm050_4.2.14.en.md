Show that if $f(\alpha)$ and $g(\alpha)$ are cyclotomic integers such that $f(\alpha)g(\alpha) = 1$, then $f(\alpha)$ is a unit and
$$g(\alpha) = f(\alpha^2) f(\alpha^3) \cdots f(\alpha^{\lambda-1}).$$
[Hint: It is simplest to prove $Nf(\alpha) = \pm 1$ and use the fact that norms are nonnegative.]
