Representations of cyclotomic integers in the standard form $a_0 + a_1\alpha + \cdots + a_{\lambda-1}\alpha^{\lambda-1}$ are not unique. This lemma characterizes exactly when two such polynomial expressions represent the same cyclotomic integer: they must differ by an integer multiple of the cyclotomic polynomial $1 + \alpha + \cdots + \alpha^{\lambda-1}$. This is the only relation beyond the ring axioms that cyclotomic integers satisfy.
