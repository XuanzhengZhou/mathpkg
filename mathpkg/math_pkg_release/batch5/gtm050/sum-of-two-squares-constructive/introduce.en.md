While Euler's proof that every prime $p \equiv 1 \pmod{4}$ is a sum of two squares is non-constructive (by contradiction), the proof can be modified to give an explicit algorithm. Fermat himself claimed to have such a constructive method. The algorithm reduces the problem to expressing successively smaller integers as sums of two squares, using the division algorithm at each step.
