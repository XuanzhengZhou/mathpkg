Show that if $r$ and $R$ are the values of $r$ which precede and follow the line $P^2 - AQ^2 = K$ then $r + R$ is divisible by $K$. $[P - rQ$ is divisible by $K$.$]$ Note that this vastly simplifies the determination of $R$ at each step.
