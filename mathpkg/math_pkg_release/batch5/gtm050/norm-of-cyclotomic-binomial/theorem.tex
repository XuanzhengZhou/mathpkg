For relatively prime integers $x, y$ and a primitive $\lambda$-th root of unity $\alpha$, the norm of the cyclotomic binomial $x + \alpha^j y$ is:
$$N(x + \alpha^j y) = \frac{x^\lambda + y^\lambda}{x + y}.$$
More generally, $N(x + \alpha^j y) = \frac{x^\lambda \pm y^\lambda}{x \pm y}$ where the sign depends on $j$.
