Show that when $\lambda = 3$ the norm of any cyclotomic integer is of the form $\frac{1}{4}(A^2 + 3B^2)$ where $A$ and $B$ are integers which are either both odd or both even. Conclude that in this case there are just $6$ units and find them all.
