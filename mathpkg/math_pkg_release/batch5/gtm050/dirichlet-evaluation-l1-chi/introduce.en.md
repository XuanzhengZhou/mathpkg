Dirichlet's evaluation of $L(1,\chi)$ expresses the value of the Dirichlet $L$-function at $s=1$ as a finite linear combination of logarithms of cyclotomic units. This formula is the analytic core of the class number formula for cyclotomic fields. It connects the behavior of the $L$-function to the arithmetic of cyclotomic integers, and through the class number formula, it determines when a prime $\lambda$ is regular.
