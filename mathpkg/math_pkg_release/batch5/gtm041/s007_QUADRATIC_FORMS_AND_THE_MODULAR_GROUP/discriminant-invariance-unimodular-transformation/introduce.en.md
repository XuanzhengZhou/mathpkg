Under a unimodular change of variables from the modular group $\Gamma$, the discriminant $d = 4ac - b^2$ of a binary quadratic form remains invariant. This is a key structural property that makes the discriminant a well-defined invariant on equivalence classes of quadratic forms, and it connects the theory of quadratic forms to the action of the modular group.
