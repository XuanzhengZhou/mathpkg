Lemma 1 describes how the multiplier $\varepsilon(A)$ in Dedekind's functional equation $\eta(A\tau) = \varepsilon(A)\{-i(c\tau+d)\}^{1/2}\eta(\tau)$ transforms under right multiplication by the translation generator $T$. Since $A T^m$ changes $a$ and $b$ but leaves $c$ unchanged, and the Dedekind sum $s(cm+d, c) = s(d,c)$ by periodicity, the transformation law follows directly from the definition $\varepsilon(A) = \exp\{\pi i((a+d)/(12c) - s(d,c))\}$.
