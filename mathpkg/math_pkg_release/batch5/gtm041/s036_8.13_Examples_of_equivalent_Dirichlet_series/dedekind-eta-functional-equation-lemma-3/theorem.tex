Assume Dedekind's functional equation
$$
\eta(A\tau) = \varepsilon(A) \{-i(c\tau + d)\}^{1/2} \eta(\tau)
$$
holds for a transformation $A = \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \in \Gamma$ with $c > 0$. Then it also holds for $A T^m$ and $A S$ for every integer $m$, with the corresponding multiplier $\varepsilon$ as given in Lemmas 1 and 2.
