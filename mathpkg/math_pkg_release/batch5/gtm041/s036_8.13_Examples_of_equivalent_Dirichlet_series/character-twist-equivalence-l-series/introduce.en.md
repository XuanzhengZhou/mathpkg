This corollary of Theorem 8.17 shows that Dirichlet $L$-series for different characters (modulo the same modulus $k$) are pairwise Bohr-equivalent, provided their coefficients are supported on integers coprime to $k$. Since $L$-series naturally satisfy this coprime-support condition, any twist by another Dirichlet character modulo $k$ preserves equivalence. This result has implications for the study of value distributions of $L$-series and was used by Apostol in his work on the sets of values taken by Dirichlet $L$-series.
