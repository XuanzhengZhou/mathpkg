The Eisenstein series $G_{2k}(\tau)$ has the Fourier expansion
$$G_{2k}(\tau) = 2\zeta(2k) + \frac{2(2\pi i)^{2k}}{(2k-1)!} \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \sigma_{2k-1}(n) e^{2\pi i n \tau},$$
where $\zeta(s)$ is the Riemann zeta function and $\sigma_{\alpha}(n) = \sum_{d \mid n} d^{\alpha}$ is the sum of the $\alpha$-th powers of the divisors of $n$.
