Theorem 6.10 states that the Hecke operator $T_n$ commutes with the weight-$k$ slash operator of the full modular group $\Gamma$. In other words, if $f$ is a modular form of weight $k$ for $\Gamma$, then $T_n f$ transforms with the same automorphy factor $(\gamma \tau + \delta)^k$ under any $V \in \Gamma$. This property is essential for proving that $T_n$ maps the space $M_k$ into itself.
