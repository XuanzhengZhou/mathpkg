This proposition records the dimension $\kappa = \dim M_{2k,0}$ of the space of cusp forms of weight $2k$ (with $2k \geq 12$) for the full modular group $\Gamma = \mathrm{SL}(2, \mathbb{Z})$. The formula depends on the residue of $2k$ modulo $12$: when $2k \equiv 2 \pmod{12}$ the dimension is $\lfloor 2k/12 \rfloor - 1$, and otherwise it is $\lfloor 2k/12 \rfloor$. This dimension count is fundamental for understanding how many linearly independent simultaneous eigenforms can exist in $M_{2k,0}$.
