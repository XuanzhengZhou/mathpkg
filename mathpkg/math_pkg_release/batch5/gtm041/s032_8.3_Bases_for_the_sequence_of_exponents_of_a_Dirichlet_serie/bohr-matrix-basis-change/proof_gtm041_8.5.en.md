We have $\Lambda = R_B B$ and $\Lambda = R_\Gamma \Gamma = R_\Gamma AB$. Hence $R_B B = R_\Gamma AB$, so $(R_B - R_\Gamma A)B = 0$. Since $R_B - R_\Gamma A$ is a Bohr matrix and $B$ is a basis, the linear independence of the basis elements over the rationals forces $R_B - R_\Gamma A = 0$. Therefore $R_B = R_\Gamma A$.
