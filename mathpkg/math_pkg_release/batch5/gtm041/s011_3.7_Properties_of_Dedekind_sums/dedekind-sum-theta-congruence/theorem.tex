\textbf{Theorem 3.8.}
Let $\theta = (3, k)$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $12ks(h, k) \equiv 0 \pmod{3}$ if, and only if, $3 \nmid k$.
\item[(b)] $12ks(h, k) \equiv h^2 + 1 \pmod{\theta k}$.
\end{enumerate}
Consequently, together with Theorem 3.6(c),

$$
s(h, k) = 0 \quad \text{if, and only if, } h^2 + 1 \equiv 0 \pmod{k}.
$$
