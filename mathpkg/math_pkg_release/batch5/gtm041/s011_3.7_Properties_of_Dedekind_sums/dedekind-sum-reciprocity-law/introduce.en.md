The reciprocity law for Dedekind sums is the central result of Section 3.7. It relates $s(h,k)$ and $s(k,h)$ through a symmetric rational expression in $h$ and $k$. This law generalizes the classical quadratic reciprocity and is fundamental for establishing congruence properties and the transformation formula for the Dedekind eta function.
