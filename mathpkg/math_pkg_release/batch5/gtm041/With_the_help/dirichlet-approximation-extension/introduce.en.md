This is Exercise 1 of Chapter 7, extending Dirichlet's classical approximation theorem to simultaneous approximation of $n$ real numbers. The bound $1 \leq k \leq N^n$ generalizes the one-dimensional case where fractions $h/k$ with denominator bounded by $N$ approximate a single real number to within $1/(kN)$.
