A unimodular transformation is a Möbius transformation $\tau \mapsto (a\tau + b)/(c\tau + d)$ with integer coefficients and determinant $ad - bc = 1$. These transformations preserve the upper half-plane $H$ and correspond to changes of basis for the period lattice of an elliptic function. The set of all unimodular transformations forms the modular group under composition, which is isomorphic to $\text{PSL}(2, \mathbb{Z})$.
