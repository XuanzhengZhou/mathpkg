If $\tau \in H$ we have the Fourier expansions

$$g_2(\tau) = \frac{4\pi^4}{3} \left\{ 1 + 240 \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \sigma_3(k)e^{2\pi ik\tau} \right\}$$

and

$$g_3(\tau) = \frac{8\pi^6}{27} \left\{ 1 - 504 \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \sigma_5(k)e^{2\pi ik\tau} \right\},$$

where $\sigma_\alpha(k) = \sum_{d|k} d^\alpha$.
