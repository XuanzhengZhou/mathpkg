A quadratic polynomial $1 - Ax + Bx^2$ with real coefficients $A$ and $B$ can be factored as follows:
$$1 - Ax + Bx^2 = (1 - r_1 x)(1 - r_2 x),$$
where $r_1$ and $r_2$ are the roots of the quadratic equation $r^2 - Ar + B = 0$. Apply this factorization to the Euler product factors appearing in Theorem 6.19.
