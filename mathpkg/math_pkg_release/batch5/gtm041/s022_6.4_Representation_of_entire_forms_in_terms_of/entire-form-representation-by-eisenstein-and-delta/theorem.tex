Let $f$ be an entire modular form of even weight $k \geq 0$ and define $G_0(\tau) = 1$ for all $\tau$. Then $f$ can be expressed in one and only one way as a sum of the type
$$
f = \sum_{r=0}^{[k/12]} a_r G_{k-12r} \Delta^r,
$$
where the $a_r$ are complex numbers. The cusp forms of even weight $k$ are those sums with $a_0 = 0$.
