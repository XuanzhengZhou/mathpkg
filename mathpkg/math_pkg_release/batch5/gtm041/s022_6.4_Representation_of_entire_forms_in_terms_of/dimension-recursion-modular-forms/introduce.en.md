The dimension recursion $\dim M_k = 1 + \dim M_{k-12}$ for $k \geq 12$ follows from combining the two fundamental relations: $\dim M_{k,0} = \dim M_k - 1$ and $\dim M_{k,0} = \dim M_{k-12}$. With the small-weight base cases ($\dim M_0 = 1$, $\dim M_2 = 0$, $\dim M_4 = \dim M_6 = \dim M_8 = \dim M_{10} = 1$), this recurrence gives a complete computation of dimensions for all spaces of entire modular forms, confirming the explicit formula in terms of $[k/12]$.
