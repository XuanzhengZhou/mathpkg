The symbol $M_k$ denotes the complex vector space consisting of all entire modular forms of weight $k$ with respect to the full modular group $\Gamma = \mathrm{SL}(2, \mathbb{Z})$. An entire modular form is holomorphic on the upper half-plane $H$ and at the cusp $i\infty$. This notation provides a convenient shorthand throughout the study of modular forms, allowing one to discuss linear operators, dimensions, and subspaces such as the cusp forms $M_{k,0}$.
