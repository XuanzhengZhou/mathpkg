Rademacher's formula provides an exact convergent series for the partition function $p(n)$, improving upon the asymptotic formula of Hardy and Ramanujan. The series involves a sum over $k$ of terms containing Dedekind sums $s(h,k)$ (encoded in the coefficients $A_k(n)$) and a derivative of a hyperbolic sine expression. The proof uses Rademacher's path of integration $P(N)$, built from Ford circles, together with the functional equation for the Dedekind eta function. As $N \to \infty$, the error terms vanish, yielding the exact convergent series.
