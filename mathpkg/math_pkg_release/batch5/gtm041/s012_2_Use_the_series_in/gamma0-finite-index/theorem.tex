Let $S\tau = -1/\tau$ and $T\tau = \tau + 1$ be the generators of the modular group $\Gamma$. For any prime $p$, each element of $\Gamma$ can be represented in terms of elements of $\Gamma_0(p)$ and powers of the translation $T$. In the language of group theory, $\Gamma_0(p)$ is of finite index in $\Gamma$.
