Theorem 5.5 describes the inductive structure of Farey sequences: $F_{n+1}$ is obtained from $F_n$ by inserting the mediant of each pair of consecutive fractions whose denominator sum equals $n+1$. More importantly, it establishes that every pair of consecutive fractions in $F_n$ satisfies the unimodular relation $bc - ad = 1$, a property essential for the geometry of Ford circles and the circle method.
