Equation (16) of Lemma 2 is the central technical identity in Apostol's proof of Dedekind's functional equation for $\eta(\tau)$. It relates two infinite series of the $\lambda$-function $\lambda(x) = -\log(1 - e^{-2\pi i x})$, one in the variable $z$ and one in $1/z$, each shifted by rational imaginary quantities determined by $h$ and $k$. The error term involves the Dedekind sum $s(h, k)$ and a simple polynomial in $z - 1/z$, vanishing entirely when $k = 1$. Section 3.6 deduces this formula from Iseki's formula (Theorem 3.5) by a careful choice of parameters and limit arguments.
