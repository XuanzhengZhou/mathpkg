Let $\omega_1$ and $\omega_2$ be complex numbers with nonreal ratio. Let $f(z)$ be an entire function and assume there are constants $a$ and $b$ such that

$$f(z + \omega_1) = af(z), \quad f(z + \omega_2) = bf(z),$$

for all $z$. Prove that $f(z) = Ae^{Bz}$, where $A$ and $B$ are constants.
