Assume that $f \in M_{2k}$, where $k \geq 2$, and that $f$ is not a cusp form. Then $f$ is a normalized simultaneous eigenform if, and only if,
$$f(\tau) = \frac{(2k-1)!}{2(2\pi i)^{2k}} \zeta(2k) + \sum_{m=1}^{\infty} \sigma_{2k-1}(m) x^m,$$
where $x = e^{2\pi i\tau}$ and $\sigma_{2k-1}(m) = \sum_{d \mid m} d^{2k-1}$.
