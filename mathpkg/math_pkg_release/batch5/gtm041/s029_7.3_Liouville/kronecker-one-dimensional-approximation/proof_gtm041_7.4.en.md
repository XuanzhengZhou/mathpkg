Write $\alpha = [\alpha] + \{\alpha\}$. By Theorem 7.7 there exists $k > 0$ such that $|\{k\theta\} - \{\alpha\}| < \varepsilon$. Hence

$$|k\theta - [k\theta] - (\alpha - [\alpha])| < \varepsilon$$

or

$$|k\theta - ([k\theta] - [\alpha]) - \alpha| < \varepsilon.$$

Taking $h = [k\theta] - [\alpha]$ completes the proof.
