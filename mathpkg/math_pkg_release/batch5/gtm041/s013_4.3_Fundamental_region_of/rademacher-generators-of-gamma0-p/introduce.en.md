Theorem 4.3, due to Rademacher, describes a generating set for the congruence subgroup $\Gamma_0(p)$ for any prime $p$. The theorem states that $\Gamma_0(p)$ has exactly $2[p/12] + 3$ generators, which can be chosen from among the translation $T$ and the elements $V_k = ST^k S T^{-k'} S$ for $k = 1, \ldots, p-1$, where $k'$ is the multiplicative inverse of $-k$ modulo $p$. Special cases are given for $p = 2$ and $p = 3$. The proof is omitted in the text, and this result is noted as not essential for the later development.
