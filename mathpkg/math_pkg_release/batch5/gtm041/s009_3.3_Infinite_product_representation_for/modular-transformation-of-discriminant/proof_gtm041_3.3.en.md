This section recalls Theorem 3.2 as a known property of $\Delta(\tau)$ and does not reproduce its proof. The proof that $\Delta(\tau)$ is a modular form of weight $12$ is established earlier in the text using the Eisenstein series representation $\Delta = g_2^3 - 27g_3^2$, where $g_2$ and $g_3$ are modular forms of weights $4$ and $6$ respectively.
