Lemma 2 (Iseki's transformation formula) is the central technical result that underpins the proof of Dedekind's functional equation for $\eta(\tau)$. It relates two infinite series involving the $\lambda$-function under a modular change of variables parameterized by integers $h,k,H$ satisfying $(h,k)=1$ and $hH\equiv -1\pmod{k}$. The formula introduces a logarithmic term $\frac{1}{2}\log z$, a rational correction $-\frac{\pi}{12k}(z-1/z)$, and the Dedekind sum $\pi i s(h,k)$, revealing the deep arithmetic structure behind the eta multiplier.
