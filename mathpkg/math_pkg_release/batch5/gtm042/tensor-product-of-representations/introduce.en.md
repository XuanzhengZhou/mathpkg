The tensor product of two representations is a natural way to build new representations from existing ones. On the tensor product space $V_1 \otimes V_2$, the group acts diagonally: $\rho_s(v_1 \otimes v_2) = \rho_s^1(v_1) \otimes \rho_s^2(v_2)$. This is one of the fundamental operations in representation theory, with the character of the tensor product being the product of the characters.
