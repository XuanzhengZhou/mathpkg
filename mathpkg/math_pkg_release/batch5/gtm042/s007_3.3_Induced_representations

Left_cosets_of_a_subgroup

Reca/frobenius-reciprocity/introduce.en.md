Frobenius reciprocity expresses a fundamental adjunction between the operations of restriction and induction of class functions. The scalar product of a restricted class function $f_H$ with $\chi_\theta$ on the subgroup $H$ equals the scalar product of $f$ with the induced character $\chi_\rho$ on the full group $G$. This formula underlies many applications of induced representations, including Mackey's irreducibility criterion and the theorems of Artin and Brauer.
