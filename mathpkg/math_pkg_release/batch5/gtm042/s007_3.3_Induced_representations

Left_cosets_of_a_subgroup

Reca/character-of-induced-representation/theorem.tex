Let $(V, \rho)$ be a representation of $G$ induced by $(W, \theta)$, a representation of $H \leq G$. Let $\chi_\rho$ and $\chi_\theta$ be the corresponding characters. Then for $u \in G$,
$$\chi_\rho(u) = \sum_{r \in R_u} \chi_\theta(r^{-1}ur),$$
where $R$ is a system of representatives of $G/H$ and $R_u = \{ r \in R \mid r^{-1}ur \in H \}$. Equivalently,
$$\chi_\rho(u) = \frac{1}{|H|} \sum_{\substack{s \in G \\ s^{-1}us \in H}} \chi_\theta(s^{-1}us).$$
