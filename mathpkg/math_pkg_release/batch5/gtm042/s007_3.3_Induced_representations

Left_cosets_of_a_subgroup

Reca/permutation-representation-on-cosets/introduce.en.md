The permutation representation of $G$ on the coset space $G/H$ is constructed by linearly extending the natural left action of $G$ on cosets. The basis vector $e_H$ corresponding to the trivial coset is $H$-invariant, so the restriction to $H$ contains the unit (trivial) representation. Inducing this unit representation back up to $G$ recovers the full permutation representation.
