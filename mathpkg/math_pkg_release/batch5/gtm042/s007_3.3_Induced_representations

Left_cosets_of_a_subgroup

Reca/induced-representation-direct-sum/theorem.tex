Let $(V, \rho)$ be a representation of $G$ induced by $(W, \theta)$, a representation of $H \leq G$. If $R$ is a system of representatives of $G/H$, then the vector space $V$ is the direct sum
$$V = \bigoplus_{r \in R} \rho_r W.$$
In particular,
$$\dim(V) = \sum_{r \in R} \dim(\rho_r W) = (G:H) \cdot \dim(W).$$
