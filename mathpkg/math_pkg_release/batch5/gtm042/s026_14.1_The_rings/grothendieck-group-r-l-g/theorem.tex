Let $L$ be a field and $G$ a finite group. The \emph{Grothendieck group} $R_L(G)$ of the category of finitely generated $L[G]$-modules is a commutative ring with unit with respect to the external tensor product (relative to $L$). If $E$ is an $L[G]$-module, let $[E]$ denote its image in $R_L(G)$; the set of all $[E]$ is denoted by $R_L^+(G)$.

Let $S_L$ denote the set of isomorphism classes of simple $L[G]$-modules (i.e., irreducible representations of $G$ over $L$).
