Schur's orthogonality relations are a matrix-coefficient-level version of Schur's Lemma. They state that the matrix coefficients of irreducible representations, averaged over the group, are orthogonal. When the matrices are chosen to be unitary, these relations simplify to standard inner product orthogonality. They are the foundation for the character orthogonality relations developed in Section 2.3.
