Let $H$ be a subgroup of a finite group $G$, and let $R$ be a system of representatives of left cosets of $H$ in $G$.
Let $\sigma: H \to \mathrm{GL}(W)$ be a linear representation of $H$.
The induced representation $\mathrm{Ind}_H^G(\sigma)$ of $G$ is defined on the vector space
$$V = \bigoplus_{r \in R} W_r, \quad W_r = r \otimes W$$
by the formula $\rho_s(\sum r \otimes w_r) = \sum sr \otimes \sigma_{h(s,r)}(w_r)$, where $sr = (sr)^* \cdot h(s,r)$ with $h(s,r) \in H$ is the decomposition according to $G = \bigcup_{r \in R} rH$.
