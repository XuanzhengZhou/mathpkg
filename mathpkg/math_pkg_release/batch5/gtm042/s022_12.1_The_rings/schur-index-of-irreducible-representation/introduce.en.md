The Schur index $m_i$ of a $K$-irreducible representation $V_i$ measures the non-commutativity of its commuting algebra $D_i = \operatorname{Hom}^G(V_i, V_i)$. When $m_i = 1$, the representation is absolutely irreducible (remains irreducible after scalar extension to $\mathbb{C}$). The Schur indices are the fundamental invariants relating the ring $R_K(G)$ to $\overline{R}_K(G)$, with precise index $[\overline{R}_K(G) : R_K(G)] = \prod_i m_i$.
