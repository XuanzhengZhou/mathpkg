Let $L/K$ be a finite extension of fields of characteristic zero, and let $d = [L : K]$. Let $G$ be a finite group. Then

$$d \cdot \overline{R}_K(G) \subset R_K(G).$$

More precisely, for any $\chi \in R_L(G)$, the trace $\operatorname{Tr}_{L/K}(\chi)$ belongs to $R_K(G)$, and if $\chi$ takes values in $K$, then $\operatorname{Tr}_{L/K}(\chi) = d \cdot \chi$.
