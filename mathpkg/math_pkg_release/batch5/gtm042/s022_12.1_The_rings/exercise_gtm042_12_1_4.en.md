Show that the Schur indices $m_i$ divide the index $a$ of the center of $G$. [Observe that the degree of the irreducible representation with character $\psi_{i,\sigma}$ is $n_i m_i$ and apply Proposition 17.] Deduce that $a \cdot \overline{R}_K(G)$ is contained in $R_K(G)$.
