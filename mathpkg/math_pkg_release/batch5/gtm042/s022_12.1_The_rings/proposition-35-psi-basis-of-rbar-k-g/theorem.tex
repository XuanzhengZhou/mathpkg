Let $K$ be a field of characteristic zero, $\mathbb{C}$ an algebraic closure of $K$, and $G$ a finite group. Let $\chi_i$ be the character of the $i$-th irreducible representation of $G$ over $K$, and let $m_i$ be its Schur index. Then the characters

$$\psi_i = \frac{\chi_i}{m_i}$$

form a basis of $\overline{R}_K(G)$ over $\mathbb{Z}$.
