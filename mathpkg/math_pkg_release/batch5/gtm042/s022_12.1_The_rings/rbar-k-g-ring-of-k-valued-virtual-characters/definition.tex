Let $K$ be a field of characteristic zero, $G$ a finite group, and $R(G) = R_{\mathbb{C}}(G)$ the ring of virtual characters of $G$ over $\mathbb{C}$. Then

$$\overline{R}_K(G) = \{\chi \in R(G) \mid \chi(s) \in K \text{ for all } s \in G\}$$

is the subring of $R(G)$ consisting of virtual characters whose values lie in $K$. Equivalently, $\overline{R}_K(G) = R(G) \cap \operatorname{Class}_K(G)$, the intersection of the complex representation ring with the ring of $K$-valued class functions on $G$.
