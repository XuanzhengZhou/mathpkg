$\overline{R}_K(G)$ is the subring of the complex representation ring $R(G)$ consisting of all virtual characters that take values in the subfield $K$. It serves as an intermediate object between $R_K(G)$ (characters actually coming from $K$-representations) and the full ring $R(G)$. Proposition 34 shows that $R_K(G)$ has finite index in $\overline{R}_K(G)$, and the Schur indices measure this index precisely.
