Let $G$ be a finite group, $g = |G|$, and $K$ a field of characteristic zero. Let $(V_i, \rho_i)$ be the distinct (up to isomorphism) irreducible linear representations of $G$ over $K$, and let $\chi_i$ be their characters. Then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] The $\chi_i$ form a $\mathbb{Z}$-basis of $R_K(G)$.
\item[(b)] The $\chi_i$ are mutually orthogonal with respect to
$$\langle \varphi, \psi \rangle = \frac{1}{g} \sum_{s \in G} \varphi(s^{-1})\psi(s).$$
\end{enumerate}
