Proposition 1 lists the three fundamental properties of characters. First, the value at the identity gives the degree. Second, the character at an inverse equals the complex conjugate. Third, characters are class functions -- constant on conjugacy classes. These properties follow directly from the trace definition and the fact that $\rho_s$ can be diagonalized with eigenvalues of absolute value 1.
