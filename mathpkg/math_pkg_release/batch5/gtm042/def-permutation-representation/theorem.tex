Suppose $G$ acts on a finite set $X$. Let $V$ be a vector space having a basis $(e_x)_{x \in X}$ indexed by the elements of $X$.
For $s \in G$ let $\rho_s$ be the linear map of $V$ into $V$ which sends $e_x$ to $e_{sx}$.
The linear representation of $G$ thus obtained is called the permutation representation associated with $X$.
