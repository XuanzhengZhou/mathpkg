The Swan character $sw_G$, obtained by subtracting the augmentation character $u_G = r_G - 1$ from the Artin character $a_G$, is itself a genuine character of the Galois group $G$. This follows from Artin's theorem on the Artin character, since $\langle sw_G, \chi \rangle = \langle a_G, \chi \rangle - \langle u_G, \chi \rangle$, and one checks that $\langle sw_G, \chi \rangle \geqslant 0$ for all characters $\chi$.
