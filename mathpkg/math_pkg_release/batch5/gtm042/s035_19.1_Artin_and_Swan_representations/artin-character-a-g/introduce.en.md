The Artin character $a_G$ is a class function on the Galois group $G = \operatorname{Gal}(F/E)$ of a finite Galois extension of a complete discretely valued field. It is built from the ramification function $i_G$: for non-identity elements it equals $-i_G(s)$, while its value at the identity is the sum of $i_G(s)$ over all $s \neq 1$. Artin proved that $a_G$ is in fact the character of a representation of $G$.
