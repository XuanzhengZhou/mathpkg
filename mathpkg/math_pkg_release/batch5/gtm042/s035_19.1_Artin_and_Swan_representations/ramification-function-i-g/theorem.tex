Let $E$ be a field complete with respect to a discrete valuation, let $F/E$ be a finite Galois extension of $E$, with Galois group $G$, and assume that $E$ and $F$ have the same residue field. If $s \neq 1$ is an element of $G$ and if $\pi$ is a prime element of $F$, define

$$i_G(s) = v_F(s(\pi) - \pi),$$

where $v_F$ denotes the valuation of $F$, normalized so that $v_F(\pi) = 1$.
