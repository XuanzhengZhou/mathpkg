For a projective $k[G]$-module $F$, one can lift it to a projective $A[G]$-module $\tilde{F}$ (by the cdvrs property), and then extend scalars to $K$ to obtain an ordinary $K[G]$-module. The ordinary character $\Phi_F$ of this lift extends the Brauer character $\phi_F$ — it is defined on all of $G$, not just $G_{\text{reg}}$, and equals zero on elements that are not $p$-regular.
