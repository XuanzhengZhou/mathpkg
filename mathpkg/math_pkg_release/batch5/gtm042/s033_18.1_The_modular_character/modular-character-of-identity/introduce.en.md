The value of the modular character at the identity element equals the $k$-dimension of the module $E$. This is immediate from the definition, since the identity acts trivially with eigenvalue $1$ (multiplicity $n$), and the lift of $1 \in \mu_k$ is $1 \in \mu_K$, so $\phi_E(1) = \sum_{i=1}^n 1 = n$.
