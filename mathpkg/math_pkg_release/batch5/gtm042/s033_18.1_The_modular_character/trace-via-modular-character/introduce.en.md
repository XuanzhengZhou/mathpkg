For any group element $t$, the trace of $t_E$ in characteristic $p$ equals the reduction modulo $\mathfrak{m}$ of the modular character evaluated at the $p'$-component $s$ of $t$. This holds because the eigenvalues of $(t^{-1}s)_E$ are $p$-power roots of unity, hence equal to $1$ in characteristic $p$, so the eigenvalues of $t_E$ and $s_E$ coincide. This property allows modular characters to recover all trace information.
