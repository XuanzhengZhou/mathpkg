Let $F$ be a $K[G]$-module with ordinary character $\chi$, let $E_1$ be a $G$-stable $A$-lattice in $F$, and let $E = E_1/\mathfrak{m}E_1$ be its reduction modulo $\mathfrak{m}$. Then the modular character $\phi_E$ of $E$ is the restriction of $\chi$ to $G_{\text{reg}}$:
$$\phi_E = \chi|_{G_{\text{reg}}}.$$

Moreover, by Theorem 32, $\phi_E$ does not depend on the choice of stable lattice $E_1$.
