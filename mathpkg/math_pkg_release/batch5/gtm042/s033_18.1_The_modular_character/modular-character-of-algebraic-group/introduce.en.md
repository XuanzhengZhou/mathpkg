The definition of modular characters extends from finite groups to arbitrary linear algebraic groups over an algebraically closed field $k$ of characteristic $p$. In this setting, the role of $p$-regular elements is played by semisimple elements. For a semisimple element $s$, the representing endomorphism is again diagonalizable, and the modular character $\phi_E(s)$ is defined by lifting the eigenvalues and summing. The resulting $\phi_E$ is a class function on the set of semisimple elements.
