For a projective $k[G]$-module $F$, the dimension of its invariant subspace $F^G$ equals the inner product of the trivial character with $\Phi_F$, the ordinary character of the lift of $F$ to characteristic zero. This is the modular analogue of the classical formula $\dim V^G = \langle 1, \chi_V \rangle$ from ordinary representation theory, and the sum is taken only over $p$-regular elements $G_{\text{reg}}$.
