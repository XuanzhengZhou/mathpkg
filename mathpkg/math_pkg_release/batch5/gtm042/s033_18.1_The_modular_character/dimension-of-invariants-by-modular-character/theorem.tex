For a projective $k[G]$-module $F$, let $F^G$ be the subspace of elements invariant under $G$. Then
$$\dim_k F^G = \langle 1, \Phi_F \rangle = \frac{1}{|G|} \sum_{s \in G_{\text{reg}}} \Phi_F(s),$$
where $\Phi_F$ is the ordinary character of the lift of $F$ to characteristic zero, and the inner product $\langle 1, \Phi_F \rangle$ is taken in the sense of ordinary characters.
