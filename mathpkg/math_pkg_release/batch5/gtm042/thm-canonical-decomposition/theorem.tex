Let $\rho: G \to \mathrm{GL}(V)$ be a linear representation of $G$.
Let $\chi_1, \ldots, \chi_h$ be the distinct irreducible characters of $G$ and let $V = \bigoplus_{i=1}^h V_i$ be the decomposition of $V$ into isotypic components.
Then each $V_i$ is the direct sum of irreducible representations all isomorphic to the same irreducible representation with character $\chi_i$.
The projection $p_i$ of $V$ onto $V_i$ is given by the formula
$$p_i = \frac{n_i}{g} \sum_{t \in G} \chi_i(t)^* \rho_t$$
where $n_i = \chi_i(1)$ and $g = |G|$.
