Given a group action of $G$ on a finite set $X$, the permutation representation realizes each group element as a permutation matrix acting on the vector space freely generated by $X$. The regular representation is the special case where $X = G$ with left translation action. Permutation representations provide a rich source of examples and are central to combinatorial applications of representation theory.
