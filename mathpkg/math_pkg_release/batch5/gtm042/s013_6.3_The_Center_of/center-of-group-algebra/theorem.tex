The center of $C[G]$ is the set of elements of $C[G]$ which commute with all elements of $C[G]$ (equivalently, with all elements of $G$).

For each conjugacy class $c$ of $G$, set
$$e_c = \sum_{s \in c} s.$$
The $\{e_c\}$ form a basis for the center of $C[G]$, which therefore has dimension $h$, where $h$ is the number of conjugacy classes of $G$.
