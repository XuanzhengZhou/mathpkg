The proof is a consequence of the Peter--Weyl theorem for compact groups. For the complete proof, see the references cited in the bibliography: [1], [4], [6].

The significance of this result is that it allows one to restrict attention to finite-dimensional unitary representations when studying compact groups, since any representation in a Hilbert space can be decomposed into such representations.
