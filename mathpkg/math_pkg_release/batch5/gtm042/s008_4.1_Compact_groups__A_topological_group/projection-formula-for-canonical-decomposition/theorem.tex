The projections $p_i$ of the canonical decomposition are given by the formula
\[
p_i x = n_i \int_G \chi_i(t)^* \rho_t x \, dt,
\]
where $n_i$ is the degree of the irreducible representation with character $\chi_i$, $\chi_i(t)^*$ denotes the complex conjugate of $\chi_i(t)$, and $\rho_t x$ is the action of the representation.
