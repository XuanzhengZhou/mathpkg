This proposition provides an explicit integral formula for the projection operators $p_i$ onto the isotypic components in the canonical decomposition of a representation of a compact group. Each projection is expressed in terms of the character $\chi_i$ of the irreducible representation, its degree $n_i$, and the Haar integral over the group. This formula generalizes the corresponding finite-group result, where the integral is replaced by a finite sum over group elements.
