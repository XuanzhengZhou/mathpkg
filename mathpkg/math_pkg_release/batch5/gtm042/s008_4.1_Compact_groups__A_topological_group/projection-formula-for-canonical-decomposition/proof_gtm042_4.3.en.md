The proof of this formula carries over without change from the finite group case (cf. Theorem 8 and Proposition 8 of Serre's text), with the finite sum over group elements replaced by the Haar integral $\int_G \cdots dt$. The formula

\[
p_i x = n_i \int_G \chi_i(t)^* \rho_t x \, dt
\]

is the compact-group analogue of the finite-group projection formula

\[
p_i x = \frac{n_i}{g} \sum_{t \in G} \chi_i(t)^* \rho_t x,
\]

obtained by replacing the normalized sum $(1/g)\sum_{t \in G}$ with the Haar integral $\int_G \cdots dt$.
