Let $V_1$ and $V_2$ be two $\mathbb{C}[G]$-modules, and let $\varphi_1$ and $\varphi_2$ be their characters. Define

$$\langle \varphi_1, \varphi_2 \rangle_G = \frac{1}{g} \sum_{s \in G} \varphi_1(s^{-1})\varphi_2(s), \quad \text{where } g = \operatorname{Card}(G),$$

and

$$\langle V_1, V_2 \rangle_G = \dim \operatorname{Hom}^G(V_1, V_2).$$

\textbf{Lemma 2.} We have

$$\langle \varphi_1, \varphi_2 \rangle_G = \langle V_1, V_2 \rangle_G.$$
