Proposition 19 provides a geometric interpretation of induced representations. When a representation $V$ decomposes as a direct sum of subspaces that are permuted transitively by the group action, the entire representation is induced from the stabilizer subgroup acting on any one of the subspaces. This result is particularly useful for recognizing when a given representation arises via induction, and it sets the stage for Mackey's analysis of induced representations in terms of double cosets.
