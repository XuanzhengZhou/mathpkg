Let $V$ be a $\mathbb{C}[G]$-module which is a direct sum $V = \bigoplus_{i \in I} W_i$ of vector subspaces permuted transitively by $G$. Let $i_0 \in I$, $W = W_{i_0}$ and let $H$ be the stabilizer of $W$ in $G$ (i.e., the set of all $s \in G$ such that $sW = W$). Then $W$ is stable under the subgroup $H$ and the $\mathbb{C}[G]$-module $V$ is induced by the $\mathbb{C}[H]$-module $W$.
