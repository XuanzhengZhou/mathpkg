Let $E$ and $F$ be two $k[G]$-modules, with $E$ projective. Show that

$$\dim \operatorname{Hom}^G(E, F) = \dim \operatorname{Hom}^G(F, E).$$

[Apply part (b) of exercise 14.4 to the projective $k[G]$-module $\operatorname{Hom}(E, F)$, and observe that its dual is isomorphic to $\operatorname{Hom}(F, E)$.]
