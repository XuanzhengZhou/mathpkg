Every projective $A[G]$-module is a direct sum of projective indecomposable $A[G]$-modules; this decomposition is unique up to isomorphism. A projective indecomposable $A[G]$-module is characterized up to isomorphism by its reduction mod. $\mathfrak{m}$ which is a projective indecomposable $k[G]$-module (i.e., the projective envelope of a simple $k[G]$-module).
