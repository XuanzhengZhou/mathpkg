Proposition 6 shows that averaging a class function over the group action on an irreducible representation yields a scalar operator. The proof uses Schur's Lemma: $\rho_f$ commutes with all $\rho_s$, hence must be a homothety. This result is crucial for proving that irreducible characters form an orthonormal basis of the space of class functions.
