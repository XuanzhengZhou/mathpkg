Let $G$ be a finite group, $K$ a field of characteristic zero, and $p$ a prime number. Write $g = p^n l$ for the order of $G$, where $(p, l) = 1$. Let $X_K(p)$ be the family of $\Gamma_K$-$p$-elementary subgroups of $G$, and let $V_{K,p}$ be the image of the map

$$\operatorname{Ind} : \bigoplus_{H \in X_K(p)} R_K(H) \rightarrow R_K(G).$$

Then the constant function $l$ belongs to $V_{K,p}$. In particular, the index of $V_{K,p}$ in $R_K(G)$ is finite and prime to $p$.
