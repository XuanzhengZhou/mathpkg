Let $G$ be a finite group and $K$ a field of characteristic zero. The rank of $R_K(G)$ (i.e., the number of isomorphism classes of irreducible representations of $G$ over $K$) equals the number of $\Gamma_K$-classes of $G$. Equivalently, the characters of the irreducible representations of $G$ over $K$ form a $K$-basis of the space of class functions $f: G \to K$ that are constant on the $\Gamma_K$-classes of $G$.
