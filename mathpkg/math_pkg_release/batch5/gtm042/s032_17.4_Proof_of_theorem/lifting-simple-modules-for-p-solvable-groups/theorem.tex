Let $G$ be a finite $p$-solvable group, let $K$ be a complete discrete valuation ring with residue field $k$ of characteristic $p$, and assume $K$ is sufficiently large (i.e., $K$ contains sufficiently many roots of unity for $G$). Then every simple $k[G]$-module lifts to an $A$-free $A[G]$-module, where $A$ is the valuation ring of $K$.
