Let $G$ be a finite group, let $p$ be a prime, and let $p^n$ be the largest power of $p$ dividing the order of $G$. Let $k$ be a field of characteristic $p$ containing sufficiently many roots of unity for $G$. Then the cokernel of the Cartan homomorphism
$$
c: P_k(G) \to R_k(G)
$$
is killed by $p^n$.
