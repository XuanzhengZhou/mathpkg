Since $\overline{Sw_G}$ is a projective $k[G]$-module (cf. 14.5), the functor $\operatorname{Hom}_{k[G]}(\overline{Sw_G}, -)$ is exact. Applying it to the short exact sequence $0 \to M' \to M \to M'' \to 0$ yields a short exact sequence

$$0 \to \operatorname{Hom}_{k[G]}(\overline{Sw_G}, M') \to \operatorname{Hom}_{k[G]}(\overline{Sw_G}, M) \to \operatorname{Hom}_{k[G]}(\overline{Sw_G}, M'') \to 0.$$

Taking dimensions gives $b(M) = b(M') + b(M'')$.
