The invariant $b(M)$ is additive on short exact sequences of $k[G]$-modules. This property follows from the fact that $\overline{Sw_G}$ is a projective $k[G]$-module, so the functor $\operatorname{Hom}_{k[G]}(\overline{Sw_G}, -)$ is exact.
