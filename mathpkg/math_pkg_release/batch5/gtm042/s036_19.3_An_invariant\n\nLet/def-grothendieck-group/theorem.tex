Let $A$ be a ring and let $\mathcal{F}$ be a category of left $A$-modules. The \emph{Grothendieck group} of $\mathcal{F}$, denoted $K(\mathcal{F})$, is the abelian group defined by generators and relations as follows:

\noindent \textbf{Generators.} A generator $[E]$ is associated with each $E \in \mathcal{F}$.

\noindent \textbf{Relations.} The relation $[E] = [E'] + [E'']$ is associated with each exact sequence

$$0 \to E' \to E \to E'' \to 0 \quad \text{where } E, E', E'' \in \mathcal{F}.$$
