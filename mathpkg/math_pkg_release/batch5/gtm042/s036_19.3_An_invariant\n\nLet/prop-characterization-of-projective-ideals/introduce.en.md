A left ideal of a ring $A$ is a projective module if and only if it is generated by an idempotent element $e$, i.e., $\mathfrak{a} = Ae$ with $e^2 = e$. In this case the ideal is a direct summand of the regular module $A$, with complement $A(1-e)$.
