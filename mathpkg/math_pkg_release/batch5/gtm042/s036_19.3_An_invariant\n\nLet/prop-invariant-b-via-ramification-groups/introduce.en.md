The invariant $b(M)$ can be expressed as a weighted sum involving the codimensions of the subspaces fixed by the higher ramification groups $G_i$. This formula reveals the connection between the Swan representation and the ramification filtration of the Galois group, making $b(M)$ a measure of wild ramification.
