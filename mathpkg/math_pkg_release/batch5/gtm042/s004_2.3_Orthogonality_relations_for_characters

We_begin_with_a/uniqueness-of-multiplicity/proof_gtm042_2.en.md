By Theorem 4, the number of $W_i$ isomorphic to $W$ equals $(\phi|\chi)$. Since the scalar product $(\phi|\chi)$ is defined directly from the character $\phi$ of $V$ and the character $\chi$ of $W$, it does not depend on any particular decomposition of $V$ into irreducible summands. $\square$
