Let $\chi_i$ be the character of $W_i$. By Proposition 2 (character of a direct sum), we have
$$\phi = \chi_1 + \cdots + \chi_k.$$
Thus
$$(\phi|\chi) = (\chi_1|\chi) + \cdots + (\chi_k|\chi).$$
By Theorem 3, $(\chi_i|\chi)$ is equal to $1$ if $W_i$ is isomorphic to $W$, and $0$ otherwise. Summing these contributions gives precisely the number of $W_i$ isomorphic to $W$. $\square$
