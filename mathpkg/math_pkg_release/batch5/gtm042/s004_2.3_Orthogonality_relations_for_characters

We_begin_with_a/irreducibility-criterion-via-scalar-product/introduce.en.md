Theorem 5 gives a remarkably simple numerical test for irreducibility: compute the scalar product of a character with itself. If the result is $1$, the representation is irreducible; any larger integer signals reducibility. This criterion is extremely convenient in practice, turning the abstract problem of decomposing representations into a concrete computation with character values.
