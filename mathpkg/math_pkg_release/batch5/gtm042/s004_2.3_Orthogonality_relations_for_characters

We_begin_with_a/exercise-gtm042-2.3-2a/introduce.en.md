Exercise 2.6(a). This exercise connects the geometric concept of orbits under a group action to the representation-theoretic multiplicity of the unit character. Burnside's lemma or the orbit-counting formula is the key insight: the number of orbits equals the average number of fixed points, which is exactly the scalar product $(\chi|1)$.
