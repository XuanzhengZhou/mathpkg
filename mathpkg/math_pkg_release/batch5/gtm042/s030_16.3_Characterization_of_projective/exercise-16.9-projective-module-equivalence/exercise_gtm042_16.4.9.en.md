Let $S \in S_k$. Prove the equivalence of the following properties:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $S$ is a projective $k[G]$-module.
\item[(ii)] $S$ is isomorphic to the reduction modulo $\mathfrak{m}$ of a module $P$ satisfying the conditions of Proposition 46.
\item[(iii)] The diagonal coefficient $C_{SS}$ of the Cartan matrix of $G$ is equal to $1$. (For the equivalence of (i) and (iii), see ex. 15.7.)
\end{enumerate}
