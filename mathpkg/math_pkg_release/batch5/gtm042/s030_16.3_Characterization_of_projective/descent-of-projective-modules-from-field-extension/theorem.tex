Let $K'$ be a finite extension of $K$, and let $A'$ be the ring of integers of $K'$. Assume the following two conditions on an element $x$ of $R_{K'}(G)$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] The character of $x$ has values in $K$.
\item[(b)] There exists an integer $n \geqslant 1$ such that $nx$ arises, by scalar extension, from a projective $A'[G]$-module.
\end{enumerate}
Then $x$ arises from a projective $A[G]$-module, uniquely determined up to isomorphism.
