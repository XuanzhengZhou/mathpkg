Proposition 44 provides a descent criterion for projective $A[G]$-modules. If an element $x$ of the representation ring $R_{K'}(G)$ over a finite extension $K'$ of $K$ has character values in $K$, and some positive integer multiple $nx$ comes from a projective $A'[G]$-module by scalar extension, then $x$ itself arises from a projective $A[G]$-module, uniquely up to isomorphism. This result is essential for reducing questions about projective modules over larger fields to the base field $K$.
