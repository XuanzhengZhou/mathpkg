Show that, for $K$ sufficiently large, condition (R) is equivalent to the condition
$$e(P_A^+(G)) = e(P_A(G)) \cap R_K^+(G).$$
(Observe that an element $x$ of $P_k(G)$ belongs to $P_k^+(G)$ if and only if $\langle x, y \rangle_k \geq 0$ for all $y \in R_k^+(G)$.)
