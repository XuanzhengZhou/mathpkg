Proposition 45 gives a characterization of those virtual characters that come from projective $A[G]$-modules, under a technical condition (R) on the decomposition map. Condition (R) requires the existence of a finite extension $K'$ of $K$ such that the decomposition map $d$ surjects the semigroup $R_{K'}^+(\mathbf{G})$ of genuine characters onto the semigroup $R_{k'}^+(\mathbf{G})$ of projective $k'[G]$-module classes. When (R) holds, a virtual character $x$ lies in the image of $P_A^+(\mathbf{G})$ if and only if it lies in the image of $P_A(\mathbf{G})$ and is a genuine character (i.e., belongs to $R_{K'}^+(\mathbf{G})$).
