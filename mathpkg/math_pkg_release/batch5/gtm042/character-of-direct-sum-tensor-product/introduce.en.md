Proposition 2 shows how characters behave under the basic constructions of representation theory. The character of a direct sum is the sum of the characters (since trace is additive on block diagonal matrices). The character of a tensor product is the product of the characters (since trace is multiplicative across tensor factors). These rules are essential for computing characters of representations built from simpler ones.
