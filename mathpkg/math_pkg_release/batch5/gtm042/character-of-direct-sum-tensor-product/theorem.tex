Let $\rho^1: G \to \text{GL}(V_1)$ and $\rho^2: G \to \text{GL}(V_2)$ be two linear representations of $G$, with characters $\chi_1$ and $\chi_2$. Then:

(i) The character $\chi$ of the direct sum representation $V_1 \oplus V_2$ is $\chi = \chi_1 + \chi_2$.

(ii) The character $\psi$ of the tensor product representation $V_1 \otimes V_2$ is $\psi = \chi_1 \cdot \chi_2$.
