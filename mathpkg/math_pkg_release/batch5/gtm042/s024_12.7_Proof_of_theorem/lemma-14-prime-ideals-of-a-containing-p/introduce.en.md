Lemma 14 describes the structure of the prime ideals of $A$ (the ring generated by the $m$th roots of unity) that contain the prime $p$. There are only finitely many such prime ideals $\mathfrak{p}_1, \ldots, \mathfrak{p}_h$, each quotient $A/\mathfrak{p}_i$ is a finite field of characteristic $p$, and some power of their intersection is contained in $pA$. This lemma is a standard fact about the ring of integers of a cyclotomic field, used in the proof of Brauer's theorem (Theorem 28).
