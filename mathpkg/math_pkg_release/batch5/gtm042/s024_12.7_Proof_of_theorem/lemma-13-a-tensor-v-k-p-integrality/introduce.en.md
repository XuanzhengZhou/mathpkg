Lemma 13 states that if an element $l$ belongs to the tensor product $A \otimes V_{K,p}$, then $l$ is already an element of $V_{K,p}$. Here $A$ is the subring of the field $L$ generated by the $m$th roots of unity, and $V_{K,p}$ is the space of $p$-adic valued class functions on $G$ over $K$. This lemma is an integrality result used in the proof of Theorem 28 (Brauer's theorem), and its proof follows the same argument as Lemma 5 in Section 10.2.
