Let $(x_\lambda)_{\lambda \in \Lambda}$ be a system of representatives for the $p'$-regular $\Gamma_K$-classes, i.e., those consisting of $p'$-elements. For each $\lambda \in \Lambda$, the preceding lemma (Lemma 17) allows us to construct $\varphi_\lambda \in A \otimes V_{K,p}$ such that

$$\varphi_\lambda(x_\lambda) \not\equiv 0 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}_i} \quad \text{for each } i,$$

and

$$\varphi_\lambda(x_\mu) \equiv 0 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}_i} \quad \text{if } \lambda \neq \mu.$$

Put $\varphi = \sum_{\lambda} \varphi_\lambda$. Then $\varphi$ belongs to $A \otimes V_{K,p}$ and we have $\varphi(x) \not\equiv 0 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}_i}$ for each $p'$-element $x$ in $G$. Lemma 16 shows that the same holds for each $x$ in $G$.
