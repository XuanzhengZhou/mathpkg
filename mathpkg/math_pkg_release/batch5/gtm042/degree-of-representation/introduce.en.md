The degree of a representation is simply the dimension of the underlying vector space $V$ on which the group acts. When $V$ is finite-dimensional with basis $(e_i)$ of $n$ elements, each $\rho(s)$ corresponds to an $n \times n$ invertible matrix, and the degree $n$ measures the "size" of the representation.
