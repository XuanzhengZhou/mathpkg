Exercise 8.11 constructs the binary tetrahedral group $G$ of order 24, which is a solvable group arising as a semidirect product of a cyclic group of order 3 with the quaternion group $E$ of order 8. The group $G$ is the set of 24 Hurwitz units in the quaternion algebra. Using the isomorphism between the complexified quaternions and $2 \times 2$ complex matrices, one obtains a natural irreducible 2-dimensional representation of $G$, providing a concrete example of Theorem 16.
