Exercise 8.10 asks to generalize Theorem 16 (which concerns irreducible representations of supersolvable groups) to groups that are semidirect products of a supersolvable group by an abelian normal subgroup. The proof strategy is to use Proposition 25 (the "little groups" method of Wigner and Mackey) to reduce to the supersolvable case.
