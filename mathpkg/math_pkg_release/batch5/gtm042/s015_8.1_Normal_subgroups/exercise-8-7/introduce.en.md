Exercise 8.7 concerns lifting Sylow $p$-subgroups from a quotient group $G/H$ back to $G$. Part (a) asserts that every Sylow $p$-subgroup of the quotient lifts to a Sylow $p$-subgroup of $G$. Part (b) gives conditions under which this lift is unique: when $H$ is itself a $p$-group or when $H$ is central in $G$.
