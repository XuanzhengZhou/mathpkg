The invariant measure on the infinite dihedral group $D_{\infty}$ is the normalized Haar measure of this compact Lie group. Since $D_{\infty}$ is the disjoint union of two circles (the rotation subgroup $C_{\infty}$ and its coset $s C_{\infty}$), the total measure is distributed uniformly with weight $1/4\pi$ on each circle. This explicit integration formula makes it possible to compute character inner products and verify the orthogonality relations for the irreducible representations of $D_{\infty}$.
