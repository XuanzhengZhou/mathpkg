For each integer $h = 1, 2, \ldots$, there is an irreducible representation $\rho^{h}$ of $D_{\infty}$ of degree $2$ defined by:
$$
\rho^{h}(r_{\alpha}) = \begin{pmatrix} e^{i h \alpha} & 0 \\ 0 & e^{-i h \alpha} \end{pmatrix}, \qquad
\rho^{h}(s r_{\alpha}) = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & e^{-i h \alpha} \\ e^{i h \alpha} & 0 \end{pmatrix}.
$$
