The degree-1 irreducible representations of $D_{\infty}$ are obtained by lifting the two characters of the quotient $D_{\infty} / C_{\infty} \cong \{\pm 1\}$. The trivial character $\psi_{1}$ assigns the value $1$ to all group elements, while the sign character $\psi_{2}$ distinguishes rotations ($+1$) from reflections ($-1$). These are the direct analogues of the corresponding degree-1 characters of the finite dihedral groups $D_n$.
