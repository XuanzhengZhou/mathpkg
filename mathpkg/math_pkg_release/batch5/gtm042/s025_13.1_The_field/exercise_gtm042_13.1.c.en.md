Put $\psi_d = 1_G^G$. Show that $\psi_d = \sum_{d' \mid d} \chi_{d'}$ and that $\chi_d = \sum_{d' \mid d} \mu(d/d') \psi_{d'}$, where $\mu$ denotes the Möbius function. Deduce that the $\psi_d$ form a basis for $R_Q(G)$.
