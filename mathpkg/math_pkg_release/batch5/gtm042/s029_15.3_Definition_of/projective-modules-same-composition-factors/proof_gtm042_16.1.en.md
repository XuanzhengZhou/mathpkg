This is a restatement of the injectivity of $c$. The Cartan map $c: P_k(G) \to R_k(G)$ sends the class $[P] \in P_k(G)$ of a projective module $P$ to its class in $R_k(G)$, which records precisely its composition factors with multiplicities. The injectivity of $c$ (Corollary 1 to Theorem 35) means that $c([P]) = c([P'])$ implies $[P] = [P']$, hence $P \cong P'$.
