Suppose that $g$ is prime to $p$. Let $E$ be a free $A$-module.

(a) Let $n \geqslant 1$ be an integer, and let

$$\rho_n: G \to \mathbf{GL}(E/\mathfrak{m}^n E)$$

be a homomorphism of $G$ into the group of automorphisms of $E/\mathfrak{m}^n E$. Show that $\rho_n$ can be lifted to

$$\rho_{n+1}: G \to \mathbf{GL}(E/\mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E)$$

and that this lifting is unique, up to conjugation by an automorphism of $E/\mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E$ congruent to $1$ mod $\mathfrak{m}^n$. [Use the fact that the cohomology groups of dimension 1 and 2 of $G$ with values in $\operatorname{End}(E/\mathfrak{m}E)$ are zero.]

(b) Obtain from (a) the fact that every linear representation

$$\rho_1: G \to \mathbf{GL}(E/\mathfrak{m}E)$$

of $G$ over $k$ can be lifted, in an essentially unique way, to a representation of $G$ over $A$.
