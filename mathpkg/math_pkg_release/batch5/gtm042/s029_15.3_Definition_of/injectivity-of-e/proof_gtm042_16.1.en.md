We know from the theory of projective $A[G]$-modules that the equality $[P] = [P']$ in $R_A(G) \simeq R_k(G)$ is equivalent to $P \simeq P'$ (cf. 14.4). Suppose $K \otimes_A P \cong K \otimes_A P'$ as $K[G]$-modules. Then $e([\overline{P}]) = [K \otimes_A P] = [K \otimes_A P'] = e([\overline{P'}])$ in $R_K(G)$. Since $e$ is injective (Theorem 34), we have $[\overline{P}] = [\overline{P'}]$ in $P_k(G)$, which implies $[P] = [P']$ in $R_A(G) \simeq R_k(G)$, and thus $P \cong P'$ as $A[G]$-modules.
