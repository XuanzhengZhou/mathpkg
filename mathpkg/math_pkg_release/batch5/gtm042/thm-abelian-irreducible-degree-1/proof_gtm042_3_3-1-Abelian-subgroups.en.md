By Schur's lemma, if $G$ is abelian, each $\rho(s)$ commutes with all $\rho(t)$, hence is a scalar multiple of the identity.
Thus every subspace is stable, and irreducibility forces dimension 1.
The number of degree-1 representations equals $|G|$ since the group algebra $\mathbb{C}[G]$ decomposes as $\mathbb{C}^{|G|}$.
