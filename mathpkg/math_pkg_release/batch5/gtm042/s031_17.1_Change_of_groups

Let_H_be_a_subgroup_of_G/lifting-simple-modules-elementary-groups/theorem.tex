Let $l$ be a prime number. Assume that $G$ is the semidirect product of an $l$-group $P$ by a cyclic normal subgroup $C$ of order prime to $l$. Then every simple $k[G]$-module $E$ can be lifted, i.e., is the reduction mod $\mathfrak{m}$ of an $A$-free $A[G]$-module.

In other words, $d$ maps $R_K^+(G)$ onto $R_k^+(G)$.
