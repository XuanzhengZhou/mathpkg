Proposition 7 provides the dual orthogonality relations (column orthogonality) for the character table. While Theorem 3 gives orthogonality of rows (characters), Proposition 7 gives orthogonality of columns (conjugacy classes). These relations are equivalent to the fact that the character table matrix, suitably scaled, is unitary.
