This proposition characterizes the only way one geodesic can be a reparametrization of another: the reparametrization must be a positive affine transformation of the form $\phi(u) = au + b$ with $a > 0$. This means geodesics are defined only up to an affine change of parameter, a fundamental fact that underlies the definition of affine parameters in general relativity and semi-Riemannian geometry. Any nonlinear reparametrization of a geodesic will no longer satisfy the geodesic equation.
