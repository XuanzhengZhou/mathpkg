Since $\gamma$ is a geodesic, we have $D_{\gamma_*} \gamma_* \equiv 0$ by definition. Using the metric compatibility of the Levi-Civita connection, the derivative of $g(\gamma_*, \gamma_*)$ along $\gamma$ is

$$\frac{d}{du} g(\gamma_* u, \gamma_* u) = 2 g(D_{\gamma_* u} \gamma_*, \gamma_* u) = 2 g(0, \gamma_* u) = 0.$$

Hence $g(\gamma_* u, \gamma_* u)$ is constant, i.e., there exists $a \in \mathbb{R}$ such that $g(\gamma_* u, \gamma_* u) = a$ for all $u \in E$.
