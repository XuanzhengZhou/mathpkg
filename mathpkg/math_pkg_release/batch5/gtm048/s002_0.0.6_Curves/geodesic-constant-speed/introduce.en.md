A fundamental property of geodesics in a semi-Riemannian manifold is that the squared length of their tangent vector, $g(\gamma_* u, \gamma_* u)$, is constant along the curve. The sign of this constant $a$ classifies geodesics as timelike ($a < 0$), null ($a = 0$), or spacelike ($a > 0$) in the Lorentzian case. This constancy follows directly from the geodesic equation and metric compatibility of the Levi-Civita connection.
