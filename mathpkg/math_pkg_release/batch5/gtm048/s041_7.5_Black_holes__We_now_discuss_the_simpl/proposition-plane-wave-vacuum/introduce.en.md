This proposition shows that gravitational plane wave spacetimes are vacuum solutions of the Einstein field equation (Ricci flat), but they are not flat---they carry genuine spacetime curvature representing gravitational radiation. This is the gravitational analogue of electromagnetic plane waves, which are source-free solutions of Maxwell's equations but carry nonzero electromagnetic fields.
