Let $\gamma: \mathcal{E} \to K$ be a particle or observer in a Kruskal spacetime. Suppose for some $s_0 \in \mathcal{E}$, $\gamma(s_0) \in M_{II}$ (the Schwarzschild black hole region). Define $\hat{\mathcal{E}} = \{s \in \mathcal{E} \mid s \geq s_0\}$. Then $\gamma(\hat{\mathcal{E}}) \subset M_{II}$. That is, once a particle enters the black hole region, it can never escape.
