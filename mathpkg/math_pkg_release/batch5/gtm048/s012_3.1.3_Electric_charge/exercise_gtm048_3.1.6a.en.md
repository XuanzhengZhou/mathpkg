Suppose $x \in M$, $X, Y \in M_x$. Show that any one of the following three conditions implies $X = Y$:
1. $\omega(X) = \omega(Y)$ for all $\omega \in M_x^*$;
2. $\omega(X) = \omega(Y)$ for all timelike $\omega \in M_x^*$;
3. $g(X, Z) = g(Y, Z)$ for all instantaneous observers $(x, Z)$.
