A particle in general relativity is characterized not only by its energy-momentum but also by its rest-mass $m \in [0, \infty)$ and electric charge $e \in \mathbb{R}$. The notation $(\gamma, m, e)$ compactly encodes all three pieces of information. The ordered pair $(m, e) \in [0, \infty) \times \mathbb{R}$ is referred to as the type of the particle $\gamma$, distinguishing particles by their intrinsic physical parameters.
