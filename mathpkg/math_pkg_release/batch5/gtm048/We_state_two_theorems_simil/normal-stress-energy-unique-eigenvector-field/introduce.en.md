Corollary 3.14.2 globalizes Proposition 3.14.1 from a pointwise statement to a smooth global statement: a normal stress-energy tensor on a spacetime possesses a unique $C^\infty$ future-pointing unit timelike eigenvector field with a smooth eigenvalue function. The smoothness is proved by noting that at each point the eigenvalue is a simple root of the characteristic polynomial (so it varies smoothly by the implicit function theorem), and the corresponding eigenvector is given by solving a homogeneous linear system of rank 3. For a dust, this eigenvector field is precisely the comoving reference frame $Z$.
