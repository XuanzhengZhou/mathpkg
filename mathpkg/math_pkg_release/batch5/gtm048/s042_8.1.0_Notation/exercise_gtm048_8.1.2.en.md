Let $h = g + a\zeta \otimes \zeta$ with $a \in \mathbb{R}$ and $\zeta$ as in Section 8.1.0. Show that on $M_z$:

(a) $h$ is a positive definite inner product iff $a > 1$;

(b) $h$ is a Lorentzian inner product narrower than $g$ iff $0 < a < 1$;

(c) $h$ is a Lorentzian inner product wider than $g$ iff $0 > a$.
