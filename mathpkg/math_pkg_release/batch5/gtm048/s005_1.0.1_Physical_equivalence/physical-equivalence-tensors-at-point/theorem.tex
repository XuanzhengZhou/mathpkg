Let $(M, g)$ be an $N$-dimensional semi-Riemannian manifold and let $x \in M$. The inner product $g_x$ on $M_x$ induces the metric isomorphism $\phi_x: M_x \to M_x^*$ determined by
$$(\phi_x v)w = g_x(v, w) \quad \forall\, v, w \in M_x.$$
The metric isomorphism $\phi_x$ induces isomorphisms $\phi_{xq}^p: T_q^p(M_x) \to T_{r+s}^0(M_x)$ for each tensor type.

Suppose $r + s = p + q$, $A \in T_q^p(M_x)$, and $B \in T_s^r(M_x)$. The equivalence relation $\sim$ on tensors of equal total order is defined by
$$A \sim B \quad \iff \quad \phi_{xq}^p(A) = \phi_{xs}^r(B),$$
where $\phi_{xq}^p$ and $\phi_{xs}^r$ are the isomorphisms induced by $\phi_x$ of $T_q^p(M_x)$ and $T_s^r(M_x)$ onto $T_{r+s}^0(M_x)$, respectively.
