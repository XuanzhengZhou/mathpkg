Show that the proper time function $t$ obeys the global version of the wrong-way triangle inequality (Exercise 1.2.4b) — that is,
$$t(x, z) \geq t(x, y) + t(y, z)$$
for all $x, y, z \in M$ such that $x \ll y \ll z$.
