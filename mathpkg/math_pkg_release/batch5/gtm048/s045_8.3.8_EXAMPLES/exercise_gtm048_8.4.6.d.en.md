Suppose $(M, g)$ is Einstein–de Sitter spacetime and $H$ is one of the subgroups with the properties in the definition of spherical symmetry. Show that there are $0$-dimensional orbits, and that all these orbits lie on one integral curve of $\partial_4$. (Roughly: each such orbit is a "center of rotation.")
