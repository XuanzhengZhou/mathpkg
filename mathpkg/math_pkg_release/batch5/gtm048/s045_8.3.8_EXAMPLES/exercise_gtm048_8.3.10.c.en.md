Let $(M, g)$ be a globally hyperbolic spacetime, and let $t$ be the proper time function. Show that for all $(p, q) \in \ll$ there is a future-pointing timelike geodesic $\gamma$ from $p$ to $q$ such that $L(\gamma) = t(p, q)$.
