\textbf{Proposition 3.5.2.} Let $(M, \mathcal{M}, F)$ be a relativistic model with $\mathcal{M}$ a finite collection of particle flows, and let $\hat{T}$ be the stress-energy tensor of $\mathcal{M}$. Then for any instantaneous observer $(z, Z)$,

$$T(Z, Z) = 0 \;\Longleftrightarrow\; \hat{T}z = 0 \;\Longleftrightarrow\; \eta_A z = 0$$

holds for the world density $\eta_A$ of each particle flow in $\mathcal{M}$.
