Let $(M, \mathcal{M}, F)$ be a relativistic model, and suppose $\mathcal{M}$ is a finite collection $\{(m_A, e_A, P_A, \eta_A) \mid A = 1, \ldots, N\}$ of particle flows on $M$. Then

$$\hat{T} = \sum_{A=1}^{N} \eta_A P_A \otimes P_A$$

is a stress-energy tensor on $M$; by definition $\hat{T}$ is the \textbf{stress-energy tensor of $\mathcal{M}$}.
