Let $F$ be an electromagnetic field on a spacetime $M$. The \textbf{stress-energy tensor of $F$} is the $(0,2)$-tensor field $E$ on $M$ whose components are given by

$$E_{ij} = \frac{1}{4\pi} \left[ F_{im} F_j^{\,m} - \frac{1}{4} g_{ij} F_{mn} F^{mn} \right],$$

where indices are raised and lowered with the spacetime metric $g$, and $F_{mn}F^{mn}$ is the full contraction of $F$ with its physically equivalent $(2,0)$-tensor.
