Proposition 4.2.1 provides a clean physical interpretation of Ricci flatness for relativistic models satisfying Einstein's field equation $G = T + E$. A spacetime is Ricci flat precisely when both the matter stress-energy tensor $T$ and the electromagnetic field $F$ vanish identically — that is, in the vacuum case with no electromagnetic field. The forward direction ($T=0=F \implies \text{Ric}=0$) follows directly from Einstein's equation and the definition of $G$, while the converse uses the dominant energy condition for matter and the positivity of electromagnetic stress-energy to force both $T$ and $E$ to zero from $G=0$.
