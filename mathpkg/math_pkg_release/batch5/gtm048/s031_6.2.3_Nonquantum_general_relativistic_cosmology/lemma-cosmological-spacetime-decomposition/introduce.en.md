This lemma provides an explicit decomposition of the Einstein tensor for simple cosmological spacetimes in terms of the scale factor $R$ and its derivatives. The functions $\rho_0$ and $p_0$ are expressed in terms of $\dot{R}$ and $\ddot{R}$, which allows the Einstein field equation to be translated into differential equations governing the cosmic expansion.
