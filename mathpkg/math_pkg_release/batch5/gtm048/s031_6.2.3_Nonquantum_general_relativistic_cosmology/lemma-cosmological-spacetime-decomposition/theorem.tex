Let $(M, g)$ be a simple cosmological spacetime with Einstein tensor $G$ and $\rho_0, p_0$ the functions on $M$ defined by:
$$G = \rho_0 \, du^4 \otimes du^4 + p_0\left(g + du^4 \otimes du^4\right).$$
Then:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $\rho_0 = 3(\dot{R}/R)^2 \circ u^4$.
\item[(b)] $p_0 = -[2(\ddot{R}/R) + (\dot{R}/R)^2] \circ u^4$.
\item[(c)] $\operatorname{div} \partial_4 = 3(\dot{R}/R) \circ u^4$.
\end{itemize}
