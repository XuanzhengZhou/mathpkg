The Einstein-de Sitter model is the simplest and most naive cosmological model in general relativity. It uses Einstein-de Sitter spacetime with a dust matter model and sets the cosmological time at here-now to 10 billion years. Despite its intentional oversimplification, this model provides a common meeting ground for an enormous variety of observational data and serves as a zeroth-order approximation for the universe near here-now.
