Let $(M, \mathcal{M}, z)$ be a cosmological model. By assumption, the stress-energy tensor $\hat{T}$ of $\mathcal{M}$ is normal. Then by Corollary 3.14.2 there exists a unique eigenvector reference frame $Z$ for $\hat{T}$. This $Z$ is called the \textbf{comoving reference frame} for the model. The distinguished instantaneous observer $(z, Z)$ satisfies $Z = Zz$ and $\operatorname{div} Z(z) > 0$.
