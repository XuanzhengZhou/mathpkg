The comoving reference frame $Z$ is the unique eigenvector reference frame of the normal stress-energy tensor $\hat{T}$ in a maximal spacetime. It is defined by applying Corollary 3.14.2 under the assumptions of nonquantum relativistic cosmology. The name is motivated by the interpretation that galaxies are (approximately) at rest in this frame, as discussed in Section 6.2.5. The comoving frame plays a central role in cosmological models: the actual observer at here-now is required to have $Z$ as her reference frame.
