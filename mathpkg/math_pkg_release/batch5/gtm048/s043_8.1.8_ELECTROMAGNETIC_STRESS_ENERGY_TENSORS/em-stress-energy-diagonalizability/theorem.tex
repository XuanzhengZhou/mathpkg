Let $F$ be an electromagnetic field on a Lorentzian manifold $M$, and let $\hat{T}$ be its stress-energy tensor measured by an instantaneous observer $(z, Z)$. Let $E$ and $B$ be the electric and magnetic fields measured by this observer. Assume $F \neq 0$. Then the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item $\hat{T}$ is diagonalizable;
    \item $\hat{T}$ obeys the dominant energy condition;
    \item either $g(E, B) \neq 0$ or $g(E, E) \neq g(B, B)$.
\end{enumerate}
