For $S \in T_0^2(M_z)$ and $Z$ a unit timelike vector with rest space $R = Z^\perp$, there exist unique $a, \theta \in \mathbb{R}$, unique $X, Y \in R$, a unique symmetric trace-free tensor $\sigma \in R \otimes R$, and a unique skew-symmetric tensor $\omega \in R \otimes R$ such that
$$S = a Z \otimes Z + Y \otimes Z + Z \otimes X + \sigma + \omega + \frac{1}{2}\theta \hat{h},$$
where $\hat{h} = g + Z \otimes Z$ is the projection tensor onto $R$.
