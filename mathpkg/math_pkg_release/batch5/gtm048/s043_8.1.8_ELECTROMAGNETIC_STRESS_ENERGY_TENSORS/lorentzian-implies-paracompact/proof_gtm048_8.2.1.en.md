The proof is left as Exercise 8.2.1(d) to the reader. By Exercise 8.2.1(c), the Lorentzian orthonormal frame bundle $ON$ admits a Riemannian metric. A Riemannian metric on $ON$ implies $ON$ is paracompact by Exercise 8.2.1(a). The bundle projection $\pi: ON \to N$ is a surjective continuous open map, and paracompactness descends through such maps, so $N$ is also paracompact.
