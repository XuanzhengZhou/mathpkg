This kinematic identity expresses the contraction $Z^{ij} Z_{ji}$ of the covariant derivative of the 4-velocity $Z$ in terms of the shear squared $\sigma^{ij}\sigma_{ij}$, the vorticity squared $\omega^{ij}\omega_{ij}$ (with a relative minus sign), and the expansion squared $\frac{1}{3}\theta^2$. It plays a central role in the Raychaudhuri equation and singularity theorems.
