The proof is left as Exercise 8.1.9(h) to the reader. One computes $Z^{ij} Z_{ji}$ by substituting the kinematic decomposition of $Z^i_{\; j}$ (the case $S = \nabla Z$) and using the orthogonality relations among the components $a Z \otimes Z$, $Y \otimes Z$, $Z \otimes X$, $\sigma$, $\omega$, and $\frac{1}{2}\theta \hat{h}$. The cross terms vanish, yielding the stated identity. Compare Section 3.6.1 for component notation conventions.
