The scalar invariant $F^{ij} F_{ij}$ of an electromagnetic field $F$ characterizes the balance between electric and magnetic energy densities. When this invariant vanishes, the squared norms of the electric field $E$ and magnetic field $B$ measured by any instantaneous observer are equal, a condition related to null (radiation-like) electromagnetic fields.
