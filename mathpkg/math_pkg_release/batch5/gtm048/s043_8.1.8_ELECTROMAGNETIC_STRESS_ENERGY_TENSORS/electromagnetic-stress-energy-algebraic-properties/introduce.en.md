This result catalogs the fundamental algebraic properties of electromagnetic stress-energy tensors at a point. The relationships between the electric and magnetic fields $(E, B)$ and the algebraic invariants $F \wedge F$ and $F^{ij}F_{ij}$ characterize when the stress-energy tensor is diagonalizable and which energy conditions it satisfies. The electromagnetic stress-energy tensor always obeys the timelike convergence condition and the strong energy condition, making it well-behaved for singularity theorems.
