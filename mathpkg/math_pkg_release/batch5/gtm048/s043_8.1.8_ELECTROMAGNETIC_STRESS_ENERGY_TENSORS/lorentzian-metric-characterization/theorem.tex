For a manifold $N$ (real, Hausdorff, finite-dimensional, paracompact, $C^\infty$), the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item There exists a Lorentzian metric on $N$.
  \item There exists a nowhere zero vector field on $N$.
  \item There exists a Lorentzian metric $g$ on $N$ such that $(N,g)$ is time orientable.
\end{enumerate}
