The proof is left as Exercise 8.1.11(a) to the reader. One chooses an orthonormal basis for the rest space $R$, expresses $\operatorname{Ric}(Z, Z)$ as a sum of sectional curvatures $\sum_{i=1}^3 K_{e_i}$ where $\{e_1, e_2, e_3\}$ is an orthonormal basis of $R$, and then uses the fact that the average of $K_X$ over the unit sphere $\mathcal{S}^2$ equals $(1/3)\sum_i K_{e_i}$ for any orthonormal basis. Compare Bishop-Goldberg 5.14 for the definition of sectional curvature.
