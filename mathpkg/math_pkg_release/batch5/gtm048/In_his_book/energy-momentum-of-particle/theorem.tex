Let $\gamma$ be a particle on a spacetime $(M, g, D)$. The tangent vector $\gamma_*$ (i.e., the 4-velocity up to parametrization) is defined as the **energy-momentum** of $\gamma$. Historically, energy-momentum unifies and replaces the two separate Newtonian concepts of energy and momentum into a single 4-vector. For a particle of rest-mass $m$, the energy-momentum satisfies $g(\gamma_*, \gamma_*) = -m^2$.
