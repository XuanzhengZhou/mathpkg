Let $Q$ be a geodesic reference frame on $M$ and let $\{\partial_i\}$ be coordinate vector fields defined in some neighborhood $U$ in $M$. Let $Q = \sum_i Q^i \partial_i$ in $U$, and for fixed $i, j$, define a function $f_{ij}$ on $U$ by $f_{ij} = g(D_{\partial_i} Q, \partial_j)$. Show that $f_{ij}$ determines the spatial deformation tensor $A_Q$ and relate the symmetry properties of $f_{ij}$ to the irrotational/rigid classification of $Q$.
