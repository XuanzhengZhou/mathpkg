Let $\gamma$ be an observer on a spacetime $(M, g, D)$. Let $p_u: M_{\gamma u} \to R_u$ and $q_u: M_{\gamma u} \to T_u$ be the orthogonal projections onto the local rest space and local time axis of $(\gamma u, \gamma_* u)$, and let $pX$, $qX$ be the corresponding vector fields over $\gamma$. The **Fermi-Walker connection** $F$ over $\gamma$ is the unique connection over $\gamma$ satisfying, for every vector field $Y$ on $\mathcal{E}$ and every vector field $X$ over $\gamma$,
$$F_Y X = \bigl[p(\gamma^* D)_Y p + q(\gamma^* D)_Y q\bigr] X.$$
We write $F_{\gamma_*} = F_{d/du}$. The Fermi-Walker connection extends uniquely to a derivation on all tensor fields over $\gamma$ that commutes with contractions and satisfies $F_Y f = Yf$ for $C^\infty$ functions $f$ over $\gamma$.
