Let $N$ be a causal box in a spacetime $M$, with boundary manifolds $B_1, B_2, B_3, C_1, C_2$ as defined for the standard cylinder $N_0$. Let $D$ denote the oriented manifold-with-boundary
$$D = B_2^- = B_2 \cup C_2,$$
where $B_2^-$ is $B_2$ with the opposite of its induced orientation (and $C_2$ inherits the boundary orientation). Such a $D$ is called a **space-section**. Note that $B_1^-$ is not a space-section due to the difference in orientation.

A 3-form $\omega$ on $M$ is closed iff $\int_{\partial N} \omega = 0$ for all causal boxes $N$. A 2-form $\psi$ is closed iff $\int_{\partial D} \psi = 0$ for all space-sections $D$.
