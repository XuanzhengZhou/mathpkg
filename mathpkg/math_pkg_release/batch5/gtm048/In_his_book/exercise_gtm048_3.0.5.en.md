Let $(\mathbb{R}^2, du^1 \otimes du^1 - du^2 \otimes du^2)$ be the 2-dimensional version (Section 0.2) of Minkowski space. Thus $\omega = du^1 \wedge du^2$ is the metric area element. For $a \in [-1, 1]$, $P = \partial_2 + a\partial_1$ is a future-pointing vector field. Now suppose a finite number of inextendible integral curves of $P$ intersect a given spacelike curve. Describe the configuration and its physical interpretation in terms of particle worldlines.
