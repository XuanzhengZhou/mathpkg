**Proof.** 
$$\tau = \frac{g(\lambda_*a, Z\lambda a)}{g(\lambda_*b, Z\lambda b)} = \frac{|X\lambda b|}{|X\lambda a|} \cdot \frac{g(\lambda_*a, X\lambda a)}{g(\lambda_*b, X\lambda b)}.$$

Since $X$ is Killing and $\lambda$ is a geodesic, the quantity $(g \circ \lambda)(\lambda_*, X \circ \lambda)$ is constant along $\lambda$ (see Section 3.6.3). Therefore $g(\lambda_*a, X\lambda a) = g(\lambda_*b, X\lambda b)$, and the second factor equals 1, yielding the stated result.
