Proposition 7.2.1 gives a clean formula for the frequency ratio $\tau$ of a freely falling photon observed at two events in a stationary spacetime. The formula $\tau = |X\lambda b| / |X\lambda a|$ uses the Killing vector field $X$ associated to the stationary reference frame and relies on the fact that the inner product $g(\lambda_*, X \circ \lambda)$ is constant along the null geodesic $\lambda$ when $X$ is Killing. This constancy is a conserved quantity along geodesics in spacetimes with a Killing symmetry.
