Let $Z$ be a stationary reference frame on a spacetime $M$ and let $X = fZ$ be the associated Killing vector field. Suppose $\lambda: [a, b] \to M$ is a freely falling photon (null geodesic) with $\lambda a = x$ and $\lambda b = y$. Then the frequency ratio is
$$
\tau = \frac{g(\lambda_* a, Z \circ \lambda a)}{g(\lambda_* b, Z \circ \lambda b)} = \frac{|X \circ \lambda b|}{|X \circ \lambda a|}.
$$
