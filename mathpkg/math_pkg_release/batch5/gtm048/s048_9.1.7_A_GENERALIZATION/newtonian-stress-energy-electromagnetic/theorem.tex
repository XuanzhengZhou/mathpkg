In Newtonian form, the electromagnetic stress-energy tensor $\tilde{E}$ becomes:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Energy density: $U = \frac{1}{8\pi}(|\vec{E}|^2 + |\vec{B}|^2)$
  \item Poynting vector: $\vec{S} = \frac{1}{4\pi}(\vec{E} \times \vec{B})$
  \item Maxwell stress tensor: $t^{\alpha\beta} = -\frac{1}{4\pi}\left[E^\alpha E^\beta + B^\alpha B^\beta - \frac{1}{2}\delta^{\alpha\beta}(|\vec{E}|^2 + |\vec{B}|^2)\right]$
\end{itemize}
