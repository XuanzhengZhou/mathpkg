Section 9.3 introduces the weak-field metric on naive spacetime that encodes Newtonian gravity: the Newtonian potential $\phi$ appears as a perturbation of the Minkowski metric, with $(1+2\phi)$ multiplying the time part and $(1-2\phi)$ the spatial part.
