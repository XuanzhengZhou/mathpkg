For $u \in \mathcal{E}$, let $\gamma_* u = e \partial_4 \gamma u + p$ be the usual decomposition into energy and 3-momentum (cf. Section 3.1.2). With RC as in 9.2.2, show:

$$e = \frac{m}{(1 - |\dot{\vec{r}}|^2)^{1/2}} = m + \frac{1}{2} m |\dot{\vec{r}}|^2 + \text{RC};$$

$$p \leftrightarrow \frac{m \dot{\vec{r}}}{(1 - |\dot{\vec{r}}|^2)^{1/2}} = m\dot{\vec{r}} + \text{RC}.$$
