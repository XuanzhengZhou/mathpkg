(c) Generalize from (b) and Section 9.2.5b to show that under appropriate assumptions and approximations

$$\frac{1}{2}m|\dot{\vec{r}}|^2 + e\varphi \circ \vec{r} + m\phi \circ \vec{r} = \text{constant}.$$

The constant is interpreted as the total Newtonian energy of the particle.
