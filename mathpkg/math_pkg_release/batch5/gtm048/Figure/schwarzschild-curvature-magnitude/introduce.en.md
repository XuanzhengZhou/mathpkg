Corollary 7.3.4 gives the explicit formula $f = 144\mu^2/r^6$ for the curvature magnitude in Schwarzschild spacetimes. This result follows from computing the four appropriate traces of $\hat{R} \otimes \hat{R}$, where $\hat{R}$ is the curvature tensor given by Proposition 7.3.2. The curvature magnitude $f$ measures the overall strength of the gravitational field and can be determined locally by observers through relative acceleration measurements. The formula shows that curvature decays as $r^{-6}$ and scales quadratically with the mass $\mu$, and it establishes the equivalence between escaping to infinity and $r \to \infty$.
