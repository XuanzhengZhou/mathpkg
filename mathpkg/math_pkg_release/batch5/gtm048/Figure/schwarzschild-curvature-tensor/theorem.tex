Let $U = N \cup B$ be a Schwarzschild spacetime with mass parameter $\mu$. Let $r: U \to (0,\infty)$ be the radial coordinate, let $P: U \to \mathcal{S}^2$ be the projection, let $h$ be the standard round metric on $\mathcal{S}^2$, and let $\zeta$ be the area form on $\mathcal{S}^2$. Let $\hat{R}: U \to T_4^0 U$ be the $(4,0)$-tensor physically equivalent to the curvature tensor of $U$. Then

$$\hat{R} = \frac{4\mu}{r^3}\Big[2(dr \wedge dt) \otimes (dr \wedge dt) - r^2 T + 2r^4(P^* \zeta) \otimes (P^* \zeta)\Big],$$

where $T$ is the auxiliary $(4,0)$-tensor field defined by
$$T(X_1, X_2, X_3, X_4) = \frac{1}{4}\Big\{[j(X_1, X_3)(P^* h)(X_2, X_4) - (1 \leftrightarrow 2)] - [3 \leftrightarrow 4]\Big\}$$
with $j = g - r^2 P^* h$.
