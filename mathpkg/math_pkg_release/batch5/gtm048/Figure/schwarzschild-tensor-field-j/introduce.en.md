The tensor field $j = g - r^2 P^* h$ on a Schwarzschild spacetime $U = N \cup B$ measures the deviation of the spacetime metric $g$ from the round-sphere pullback $r^2 P^* h$. It serves as an auxiliary construction used to express the curvature tensor $\hat{R}$ of the Schwarzschild spacetime in a compact algebraic form. The field $j$ appears in the definition of the $(4,0)$-tensor $T$, which together with $j$ encodes the curvature via Proposition 7.3.2.
