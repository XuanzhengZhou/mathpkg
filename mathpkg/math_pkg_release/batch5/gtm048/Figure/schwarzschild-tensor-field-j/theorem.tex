Let $U = N \cup B$ be a Schwarzschild spacetime. Define a tensor field $j: U \to T_2^0 U$ by
$$j = g - r^2 P^* h,$$
where $g$ is the Lorentzian metric on $U$, $r: U \to (0, \infty)$ is the radial coordinate, $P: U \to \mathcal{S}^2$ is the projection onto the unit 2-sphere, and $h$ is the standard round metric on $\mathcal{S}^2$.
