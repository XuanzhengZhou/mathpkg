The tensor field $T: U \to T_4^0 U$ on a Schwarzschild spacetime $U = N \cup B$ is an auxiliary $(4,0)$-tensor constructed from the auxiliary tensor $j = g - r^2 P^* h$ and the round-sphere metric pullback $P^* h$. Its definition uses antisymmetrization over index pairs to ensure curvature-like symmetries. The field $T$, together with the wedge products $dr \wedge dt$ and the pullback $P^* \zeta$ of the area form on $\mathcal{S}^2$, appears in the compact algebraic expression for the curvature tensor $\hat{R}$ of the Schwarzschild spacetime given in Proposition 7.3.2.
