(a) A submanifold $N$ of $M$ is called totally geodesic iff any geodesic of $M$ tangent to $N$ at one point must lie completely in $N$. Show that the zero set of a Killing vector field is a totally geodesic submanifold (possibly disconnected).

(b) Use (a) and Exercise 3.6.6 to show that the lemma of Corollary 7.3.5 remains valid if $W$ is only required to be nonzero at one point.
