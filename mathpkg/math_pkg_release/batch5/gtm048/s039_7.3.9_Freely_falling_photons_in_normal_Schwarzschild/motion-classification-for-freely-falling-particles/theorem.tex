Let $\alpha: \hat{\mathcal{E}} \rightarrow N$ be a freely falling particle in normal Schwarzschild spacetime.

(a) $\alpha$ is said to:
\begin{itemize}
  \item go in iff $dr(\alpha_*) < 0$,
  \item maintain height iff $dr(\alpha_*) = 0$,
  \item go out iff $dr(\alpha_*) > 0$.
\end{itemize}

(b) $\alpha$ is said to:
\begin{itemize}
  \item go directly in iff $\alpha$ maintains direction ($J = 0$) and $dr(\alpha_*) < 0$,
  \item hover iff $\alpha$ maintains direction ($J = 0$) and $dr(\alpha_*) = 0$,
  \item go directly out iff $\alpha$ maintains direction ($J = 0$) and $dr(\alpha_*) > 0$.
\end{itemize}

(c) $\alpha$ is said to circle iff $\alpha$ maintains height and $(P \circ \alpha)(\hat{\mathcal{E}}) \subset \mathcal{S}^2$ is an arc of a great circle.
