Suppose $M$ is a simple cosmological spacetime with $\dot{R} > 0$. Let $\mathbf{M} = (\rho, p, \partial_4)$ be a perfect fluid on $M$ such that $\partial_\mu \rho = 0 = \partial_\mu p$ for $\mu = 1, 2, 3$, and that the stress-energy tensor $\hat{T}$ of $\mathbf{M}$ obeys $\operatorname{div} \hat{T} = 0$. Then:

\text{(a)} $\forall y \in M$, the inequalities
$$
\left( \frac{R(u^4 y)}{R \circ u^4} \right)^3 \leq \frac{\rho}{\rho y} \leq \left( \frac{R(u^4 y)}{R \circ u^4} \right)^4
$$
hold in the region determined by $u^4 \leq u^4 y$.

\text{(b)} The first (respectively, second) inequality is an equality valid on all of $M$ iff we have the dust case $p = 0$ (respectively, rest-mass zero case $p = \rho/3$).
