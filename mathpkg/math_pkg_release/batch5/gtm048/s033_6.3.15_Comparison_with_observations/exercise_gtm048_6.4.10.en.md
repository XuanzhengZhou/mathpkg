Let $\mathbf{M}_1$ and $\mathbf{M}_2$ be perfect fluids on $M$. Let $\mathbf{M}$ be the superposition of $\mathbf{M}_1$ and $\mathbf{M}_2$ and choose $z \in M$ so that $u^4 z = 10^{10}$ years.

**(a)** Show $(M, \mathbf{M}, z)$ is a simple cosmological model. (Hint: To check the Einstein field equation use Lemma 6.2.6; to check maximality use Exercise 6.4.9.)

**(b)** Show that assumptions 6.4.5a--c hold. (Hint: For $\operatorname{div} \hat{T}_2 = 0$, use Proposition 3.15.3.)

**(c)** Finally show by algebra that if $b$ is chosen as in Proposition 6.4.5d, then $\rho_2 z = a \rho_1 z$.
