\textbf{Proposition 6.0.8.} Suppose $x, z \in M$ (Einstein--de Sitter spacetime) and there exists a light signal from $x$ to $z$. Then the cosmological frequency ratio for $(x, z)$ is
$$\iota(x, z) = \frac{R(u^4 z)}{R(u^4 x)} = \left(\frac{u^4 z}{u^4 x}\right)^{2/3} > 1.$$
The cosmological redshift is $z = \iota - 1 > 0$.
