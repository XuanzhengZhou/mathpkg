\textbf{Corollary 5.7.4.} For a Planck-distributed photon gas with temperature function $T: M \to (0, \infty)$, the stress-energy tensor is given by
$$\hat{T} = (8\pi) j^{-4}(\hat{g} + 4 X \otimes X),$$
where $j = (kT)^{-1}$, $\hat{g}$ is the inverse metric, and $X$ is the comoving reference frame.
