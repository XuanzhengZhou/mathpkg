Suppose there exists a reference frame $X$ such that, for all $x \in M$, $F$ is spatially isotropic for $(x, X_x)$. Define $ho = T(X, X)$, where $T$ is the $(0,2)$-tensor physically equivalent to $\hat{T}$. Show that $(ho, ho/3, X)$ is a rest-mass zero perfect fluid with the same stress-energy tensor as $F$.
