Lemma 6.0.10 computes the cosmological frequency ratio for the standard photon in Einstein--de Sitter spacetime. The standard photon is a distinguished lightlike geodesic that serves as a reference for all other light signals via the action of isometries. The lemma states that the frequency ratio between two points on the standard photon is given by the ratio of the scale factor $R \circ u^4$ evaluated at the cosmological times of the two points. Together with Lemma 6.0.9, this yields the general formula of Proposition 6.0.8.
