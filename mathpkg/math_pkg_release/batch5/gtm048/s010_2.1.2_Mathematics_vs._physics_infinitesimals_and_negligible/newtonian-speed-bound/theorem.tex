Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer on a spacetime $(M, g)$. Let $X \in M_z$ be a future-pointing causal vector with decomposition $X = eZ + p$, $e = -g(X, Z) > 0$, $p \in Z^\perp$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item The Newtonian speed satisfies $|p|/e \leq 1$.
\item $X$ (equivalently, $\gamma_* u$ for an observer $\gamma$) is timelike if and only if the Newtonian speed is strictly less than $1$.
\end{enumerate}
