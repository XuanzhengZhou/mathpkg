Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer on a spacetime $(M, g)$. Let $X \in M_z$ be a future-pointing causal vector with orthogonal decomposition
$$X = eZ + p, \qquad e = -g(X, Z) > 0, \quad p \in Z^\perp,$$
where $|p| = \sqrt{g(p, p)}$ is the norm induced by the positive-definite restriction $g|_{Z^\perp}$.

The \textbf{Newtonian speed} of $X$ (or of an observer $\gamma$ with $\gamma_* u = X$) observed by $(z, Z)$ is defined to be
$$\frac{|p|}{e}.$$
