Let $F$ be an electromagnetic field on spacetime $M$, and let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer. The \text{electric vector} $(z, Z)$ measures for $F$ is
$$E = \tilde{F}Z \in Z^\perp,$$
where $\tilde{F}$ denotes the $(1,1)$ tensor field equivalent to $F$ via the metric (i.e., $g(\tilde{F}X, Y) = F(X, Y)$ for all vector fields $X, Y$).
