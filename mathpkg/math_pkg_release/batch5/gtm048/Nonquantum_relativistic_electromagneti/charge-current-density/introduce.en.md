A charge-current density on spacetime $M$ is formally a vector field $J$ on $M$. Historically, $J$ replaces and unifies two prerelativistic concepts: electric charge per unit $\mathbb{R}^3$ volume, and electric current density (charge flux). This definition provides the relativistic formulation of electric charge and current as a single geometric object on spacetime.
