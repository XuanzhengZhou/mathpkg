Corollary 1.1.4 specializes Lemma 1.1.3(a) to the case where the subspace is the 1-dimensional span of a single vector. It states that a vector $w$ is timelike precisely when its orthogonal complement $w^\perp$ (a hyperplane of codimension 1) is spacelike. This is a useful criterion: instead of checking the sign of $g(w,w)$, one can verify that $g$ is positive definite on the $(N-1)$-dimensional subspace orthogonal to $w$.
