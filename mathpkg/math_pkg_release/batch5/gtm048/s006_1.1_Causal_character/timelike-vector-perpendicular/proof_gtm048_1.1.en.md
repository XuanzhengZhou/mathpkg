The one-dimensional subspace $W = \operatorname{span}\{w\}$ is timelike precisely when $w$ is timelike. Applying Proposition 1.1.3(a), $W$ is timelike if and only if $W^\perp$ is spacelike. But $W^\perp = (\operatorname{span}\{w\})^\perp = w^\perp$. Hence $w$ is timelike iff $w^\perp$ is spacelike.
