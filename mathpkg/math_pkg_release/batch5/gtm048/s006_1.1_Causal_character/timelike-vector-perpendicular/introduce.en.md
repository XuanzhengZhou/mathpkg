This corollary follows directly from the causal character properties of subspaces. Applying Proposition 1.1.3(a) to the one-dimensional subspace $W = \operatorname{span}\{w\}$, we obtain that $W$ is timelike iff $W^\perp$ is spacelike. Since a nonzero $w$ is timelike precisely when $\operatorname{span}\{w\}$ is a timelike subspace, the result follows. This equivalence is fundamental in Lorentzian geometry for relating the causal nature of a vector to the geometry of its orthogonal hyperplane.
