Lemma 1.1.3 establishes a clean duality between the causal character of a subspace and that of its orthogonal complement in a Lorentzian vector space. The timelike and spacelike characters are swapped under orthogonal complement, while the lightlike character is self-dual. The lightlike case is characterized by the existence of a non-trivial intersection between the subspace and its orthogonal complement -- a phenomenon that has no analogue in Euclidean geometry.
