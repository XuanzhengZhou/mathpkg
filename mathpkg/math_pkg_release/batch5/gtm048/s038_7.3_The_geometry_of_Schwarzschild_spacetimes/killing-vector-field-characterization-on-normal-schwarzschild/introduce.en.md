Corollary 7.3.5 characterizes all Killing vector fields on the normal Schwarzschild region $N$. It states that up to a constant scalar multiple, $\partial/\partial t$ is the unique future-pointing Killing vector field on $N$. This establishes $N$ as a static spacetime and gives a geometric interpretation of the coordinate $t$.
