Let $f$ be the scalar curvature invariant defined by tracing $\hat{R} \otimes \hat{R}$ appropriately. Then on $U$:
$$f = \frac{144\mu^2}{r^6}.$$
A particle or observer $\gamma: (a, b) \to M$ escapes to infinity iff $\lim_{u \to b} f(\gamma u) = 0$, which is equivalent to $\lim_{u \to b} r(\gamma u) = \infty$.
