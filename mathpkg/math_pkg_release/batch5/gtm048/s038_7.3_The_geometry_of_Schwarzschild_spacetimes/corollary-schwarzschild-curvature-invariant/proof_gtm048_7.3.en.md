**Proof.** Let $\tilde{T}$ be the $(4,0)$-tensor field physically equivalent on $U$ to $T$ in Proposition 7.3.2; let $A$ be the $(2,0)$-tensor field physically equivalent to $P^*\zeta$. Using the Lorentzian metric $g$ we find that the $(4,0)$ tensor field $\tilde{R}$ physically equivalent to $\hat{R}$ on $U$ is:

$$\tilde{R} = \frac{4\mu}{r^3}\left[2(\partial/\partial r \wedge \partial/\partial t) \otimes (\partial/\partial r \wedge \partial/\partial t) - r^{-2}\tilde{T} + 2r^{-4}A \otimes A\right].$$

Taking the appropriate four traces of $\tilde{R} \otimes \tilde{R}: M \to T_4^4 M$ yields $f = 144\mu^2/r^6$.
