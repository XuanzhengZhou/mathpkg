This corollary gives a simple, coordinate-independent characterization of the distance from the gravitational source in Schwarzschild spacetimes. The curvature invariant $f = 144\mu^2/r^6$ measures the overall magnitude of curvature, and the condition $f \to 0$ defines what it means for a particle to escape to infinity. Even observers in an opaque box can determine $f$ locally by relative acceleration measurements.
