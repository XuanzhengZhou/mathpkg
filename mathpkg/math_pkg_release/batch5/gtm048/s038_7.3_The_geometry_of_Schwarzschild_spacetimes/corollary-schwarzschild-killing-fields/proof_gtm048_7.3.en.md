**Proof.** The proof uses the explicit form of the curvature from Proposition 7.3.2 and the properties of Killing vector fields. If $X$ is Killing, then the Lie derivative of the metric vanishes: $L_X g = 0$. Computing this condition in the Schwarzschild coordinates yields $dr(X) = 0$, and the remaining components force $X$ to be proportional to $\partial/\partial t$ on $N$. The uniqueness up to a positive constant factor follows from the lemma: if $W$ is a nowhere zero Killing vector field and $\ell W$ is also Killing, then $\ell$ is constant.
