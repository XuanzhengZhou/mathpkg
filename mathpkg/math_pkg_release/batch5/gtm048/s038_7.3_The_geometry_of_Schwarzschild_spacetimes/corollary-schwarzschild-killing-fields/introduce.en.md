This corollary shows that, up to a positive constant factor, $\partial/\partial t$ is the unique future-pointing Killing vector field on the normal Schwarzschild spacetime. This characterizes the time-translation symmetry of Schwarzschild spacetimes and establishes $N$ as stationary. The geometric interpretation is that $t$ is a time coordinate adapted to this symmetry.
