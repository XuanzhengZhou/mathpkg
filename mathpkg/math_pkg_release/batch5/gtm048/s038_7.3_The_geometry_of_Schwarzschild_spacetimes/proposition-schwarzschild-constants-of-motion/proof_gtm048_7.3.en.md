**Proof.** Since $\partial/\partial t$ is Killing, (a) follows from Section 3.6.3e: for a geodesic $\gamma$ and Killing field $X$, $g(\gamma_*, X \circ \gamma)$ is constant. Similarly, the vector field $V: U \to TU$ determined by $P_*V = K_x$ and $Q_*V = 0$ is Killing, and $\rho^2 h(\delta_*, K_x) = g(V, \gamma_*)$, so (c) follows for some $J_x \in \mathbb{R}$. Part (b) and the inequality $J_x \in [-J, J]$ follow from Exercise 7.0.1, which expresses the metric $h$ on $\mathcal{S}^2$ in terms of the 1-forms physically equivalent to the rotation generators.
