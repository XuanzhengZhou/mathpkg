This proposition provides the first integrals (constants of motion) for freely falling particles in Schwarzschild spacetimes. The energy $E$ is conserved due to the time-translation Killing field $\partial/\partial t$, while the angular momenta $J$ and $J_x$ are conserved due to the rotational symmetries (the $SO(3)$ isometry group acting on the $\mathcal{S}^2$ factor). These constants are fundamental for analyzing orbits in Schwarzschild geometry.
