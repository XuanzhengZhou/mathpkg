On $U = N \cup B$ (the union of normal Schwarzschild and Schwarzschild black hole), let $\hat{R}: U \to T_4^0 U$ be the $(0,4)$-tensor physically equivalent to the curvature tensor. Then:
$$\hat{R} = \frac{4\mu}{r^3}\left[2(dr \wedge dt) \otimes (dr \wedge dt) - r^2 T + 2r^4(P^*\zeta) \otimes (P^*\zeta)\right],$$
where $T$ has all the symmetry properties of a curvature tensor and $P^*\zeta$ is the pullback of the standard volume element on $\mathcal{S}^2$.
