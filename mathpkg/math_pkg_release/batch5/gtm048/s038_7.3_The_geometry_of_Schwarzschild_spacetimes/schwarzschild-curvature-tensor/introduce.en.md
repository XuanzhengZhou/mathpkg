Proposition 7.3.2 gives the explicit formula for the curvature tensor $\hat{R}$ of a Schwarzschild spacetime $U = N \cup B$ in terms of the radial coordinate $r$, the mass parameter $\mu$, the canonical area form $\zeta$ on the unit 2-sphere, and the auxiliary tensor fields $T$ and $P^*\zeta$. The tensor $T$ is constructed from $j = g - r^2 P^* h$ and has all the algebraic symmetry properties of a Riemann curvature tensor.
