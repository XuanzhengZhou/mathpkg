This Lemma (proved within the discussion of Corollary 7.3.5) states that if multiplying a nowhere-zero Killing vector field $W$ by a smooth function $\ell$ yields another Killing field, then $\ell$ must be constant. This is used to establish the uniqueness (up to scaling) of the static Killing field on the normal Schwarzschild region.
