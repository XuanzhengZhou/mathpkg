Let $M$ be Minkowski spacetime with metric $g = \sum_{\mu=1}^{3} du^\mu \otimes du^\mu - du^4 \otimes du^4$ and flat connection $D$. Let $F = 2B_0\, du^1 \wedge du^2$ be a uniform magnetic field aligned with the $u^3$-direction, with $B_0 \in \mathbb{R}$. If a particle $(\gamma, m, e)$ on $M$ obeys the Lorentz world-force law with respect to $F$, then there exist $y \in \mathbb{R}^4$ and constants $a, b, c, \phi \in \mathbb{R}$ such that for all $u \in \mathcal{E}$,
$$\gamma u = y + \bigl(a\sin(\omega m u + \phi),\; a\cos(\omega m u + \phi),\; b m u,\; c m u\bigr),$$
where $\omega = eB_0/m$, and the parameters satisfy $c^2 = 1 + a^2\omega^2 + b^2$.
