This proposition explicitly solves the Lorentz world-force law in the simplest nontrivial case: a charged particle in flat Minkowski spacetime subjected to a uniform magnetic field along the $u^3$-axis. The solution shows that the world-line is a helix — the particle executes uniform circular motion in the plane orthogonal to the magnetic field while drifting uniformly along the field direction and in time. The circular frequency $\omega = eB_0/m$ is the synchrotron frequency, corresponding to the cyclotron motion observed in laboratory plasmas, particle accelerators, and astrophysical settings such as charged particles spiraling along Earth's magnetic field lines.
