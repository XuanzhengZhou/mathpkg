A particle flow of rest-mass $m$ is extended by assigning a constant electric charge $e \in \mathbb{R}$ to every particle in the flow. The resulting quadruple $(m, e, P, \eta)$ encodes both the mass and charge properties of the particle species, where $(m, e)$ is called the *type* of the particle flow. This generalizes the earlier notion of a particle flow $(P, \eta)$ to include electromagnetic coupling via the charge parameter $e$.
