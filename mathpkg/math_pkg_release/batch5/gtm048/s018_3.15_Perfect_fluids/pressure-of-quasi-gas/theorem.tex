For a quasi-gas $M$ with stress-energy tensor $\hat{T} = \rho Z \otimes Z + p(\hat{g} + Z \otimes Z)$ as in Proposition 3.15.1, the scalar function $p$ is called the \textbf{pressure} of the quasi-gas $M$. The pressure satisfies $p \geq 0$ and $\rho \geq 3p$, with $\rho = 3p$ exactly when all components have zero rest-mass.
