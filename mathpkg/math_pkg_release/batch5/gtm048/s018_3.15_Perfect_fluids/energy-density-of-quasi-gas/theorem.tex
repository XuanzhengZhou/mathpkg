For a quasi-gas $M$ with stress-energy tensor $\hat{T} = \rho Z \otimes Z + p(\hat{g} + Z \otimes Z)$ as in Proposition 3.15.1, let $T$ be the $(0,2)$-tensor field physically equivalent to $\hat{T}$. Then for all $z \in M$,
$$\rho(z) = T(Z_z, Z_z),$$
the energy density of $\hat{T}$ observed by the instantaneous observer $(z, Z_z)$. The scalar function $\rho$ is called the \textbf{energy density} of the quasi-gas $M$.
