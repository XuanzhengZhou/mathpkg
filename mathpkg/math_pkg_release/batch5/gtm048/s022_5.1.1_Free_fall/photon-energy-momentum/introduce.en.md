The energy-momentum vector of a photon at a spacetime point is the pushforward of the photon's parametrization. For a 1--1 photon $\lambda: \mathcal{E} ightarrow M$, the energy-momentum at $x = \lambda u$ is given by $Y = \lambda_* u \in M_x$. This vector encodes the photon's physical momentum and energy content at that spacetime event.
