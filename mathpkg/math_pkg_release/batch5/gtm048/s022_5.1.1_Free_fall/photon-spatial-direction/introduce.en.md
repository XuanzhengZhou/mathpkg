The spatial direction of a photon measured by an instantaneous observer $(z, Z)$ is the component $U \in Z^\perp$ in the decomposition $Y = e(Z + U)$ of the photon's energy-momentum vector. The spatial direction is orthogonal to the observer's time axis $Z$ and encodes the direction from which the photon appears to arrive.
