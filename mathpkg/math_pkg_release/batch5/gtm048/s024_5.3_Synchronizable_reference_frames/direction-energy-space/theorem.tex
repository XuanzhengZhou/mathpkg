Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer on spacetime $M$, let $\mathcal{L}_z^+$ be the future lightcone in $M_z$, and let $\mathcal{S}_Z$ be the celestial sphere of $(z, Z)$. For each photon energy-momentum $Y \in \mathcal{L}_z^+$, the observer assigns a measured energy $e = -g(Y, Z) \in (0, \infty)$ and a measured spatial direction $U \in \mathcal{S}_Z$. The **direction-energy space** of $(z, Z)$ is defined as

$$P_z = \mathcal{S}_Z \times (0, \infty),$$

with the projection onto the second factor denoted by $e: P_z \to (0, \infty)$.
