If $\bar{D}$ is a connection on $M$ and $\phi: N \rightarrow M$ is a $C^\infty$ map, then $\bar{D}$ gives rise to a connection over $\phi$ as follows: the induced connection $\phi^* \bar{D}$ is the unique connection over $\phi$ such that
$$(\phi^* \bar{D})_X (A \circ \phi) = \bar{D}_{\phi_* X} A$$
for all $X \in T_x N$, for all $x \in N$, and for every tensor field $A$ on $M$ (Bishop--Goldberg 5.7.1).
