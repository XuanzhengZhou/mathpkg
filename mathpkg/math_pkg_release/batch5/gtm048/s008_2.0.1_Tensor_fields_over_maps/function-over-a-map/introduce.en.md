A function over a smooth map $\phi: N \to M$ is simply an ordinary real-valued function on the domain $N$. This is the $(0,0)$-tensor field case of the more general concept of a tensor field over a map, and it serves as a building block for more elaborate geometric constructions along maps between manifolds.
