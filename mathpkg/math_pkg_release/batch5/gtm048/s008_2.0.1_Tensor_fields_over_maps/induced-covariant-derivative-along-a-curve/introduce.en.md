In a spacetime $(M,g,D)$, the induced connection $\gamma^* D$ along a curve $\gamma$ appears so frequently that abbreviated notation is introduced. The symbol $D_{\gamma_*}$ denotes the covariant derivative along $\gamma$ with respect to the parameter derivative $d/du$, applied either to tensor fields $A$ on $M$ (via restriction $A \circ \gamma$) or to vector fields $X$ defined over $\gamma$. This notation is used throughout the remainder of the book.
