**EXERCISE 2.3.14.** Let $Q$ be a geodesic reference frame on $M$ and let $\{\partial_i\}$ be coordinate vector fields defined in some neighborhood $U$ in $M$. Let $Q = \sum_i Q^i \partial_i$ in $U$, and for fixed $i, j$, define a function $f_{ij}$ on $U$ by $f_{ij} = g(D_{\partial_i} Q, \partial_j)$. Show that the spatial expansion tensor $A_Q$ can be expressed in terms of the functions $f_{ij}$.
