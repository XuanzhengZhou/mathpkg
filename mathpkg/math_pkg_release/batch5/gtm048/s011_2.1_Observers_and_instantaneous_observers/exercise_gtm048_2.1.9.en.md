**EXERCISE 2.1.9.** Let $\gamma_1, \gamma_2, \gamma_3$ be observers in 2-dimensional Minkowski space whose world lines intersect at $x = \gamma_1 0 = \gamma_2 0 = \gamma_3 0$. Let $s_{ij}$ be the Newtonian speed of $\gamma_i$ observed by $(x, (\gamma_j)_* 0)$.

(a) Show $s_{ij} = s_{ji}$.

(b) (Einstein addition law) Verify $s_{31} = (s_{21} + s_{32})(1 + s_{21}s_{32})^{-1}$.
