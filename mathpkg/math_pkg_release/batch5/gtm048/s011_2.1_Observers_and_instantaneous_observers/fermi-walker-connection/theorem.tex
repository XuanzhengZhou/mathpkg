Let $\gamma$ be an observer, with $p_u: M_{\gamma u} \rightarrow R_u$ and $q_u: M_{\gamma u} \rightarrow T_u$ the orthogonal projections. There is precisely one connection $F$ over $\gamma$ such that
$$F_Y X = [p(\gamma^* D)_Y p + q(\gamma^* D)_Y q]X$$
for every vector field $Y$ on $\mathcal{E}$ and every vector field $X$ over $\gamma$.
