The metric volume element $\Omega$ is the canonical nowhere-vanishing 4-form on an oriented spacetime $(M, g, D)$. On an orthonormal frame it evaluates to $1/4!$; in coordinates it is $\sqrt{|g|}\,dx^1\wedge\cdots\wedge dx^4$. $\Omega$ is essential for integration on $M$ and for formulating integral conservation laws via Stokes' theorem. It satisfies $D\Omega = 0$ (covariantly constant).
