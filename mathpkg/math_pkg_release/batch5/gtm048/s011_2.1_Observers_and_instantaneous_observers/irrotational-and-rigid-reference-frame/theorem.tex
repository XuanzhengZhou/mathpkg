Let $Q$ be a reference frame and let $A_Q: R_u \rightarrow R_u$ be the spatial expansion tensor. $Q$ is called:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{irrotational} at $x = \gamma u$ iff $A_Q$ is self-adjoint with respect to $gx|_{R_u}$,
  \item \textbf{rigid} at $x = \gamma u$ iff $A_Q$ is skew-adjoint with respect to $gx|_{R_u}$,
\end{itemize}
and \textbf{irrotational} or \textbf{rigid} (globally) iff it is irrotational or rigid at every $x \in M$.
