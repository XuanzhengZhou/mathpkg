Let $X, Y$ be vector fields over $\gamma$. The Fermi-Walker connection $F$ over $\gamma$ satisfies:
\begin{itemize}
  \item[(a)] $F_{\gamma_*} X = p D_{\gamma_*} pX + q D_{\gamma_*} qX$.
  \item[(b)] $F_{\gamma_*} g = 0$ (metric compatibility).
  \item[(c)] $p F_{\gamma_*} X = F_{\gamma_*} pX$ and $q F_{\gamma_*} X = F_{\gamma_*} qX$.
\end{itemize}
Two unit vectors in the local rest space at different proper times have the same spatial direction iff one is the $F$-parallel transport of the other.
