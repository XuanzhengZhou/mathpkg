Let $(z, Z)$ be an instantaneous observer. Define:
$$
\mathcal{O}^3 = \{\psi: M_z \rightarrow M_z \mid \psi Z = Z,\ \text{and}\ g(X, Y) = g(\psi X, \psi X)\ \forall X, Y \in M_z\}.
$$
Thus each $\psi \in \mathcal{O}^3$ is linear. Let $\psi \in \mathcal{O}^3$, and let $M_z = R \oplus T$ be the associated orthogonal decomposition of $M_z$. Since $\psi|_T = \text{identity}$, $\psi$ is completely determined by $\psi|_R$. Thus $\mathcal{O}^3$ is isomorphic to the group of automorphisms of $R$ which preserve the positive definite inner product $gz|_R$ of $R$. In other words, $\mathcal{O}^3$ is isomorphic to the rotation group of $\mathbb{R}^3$.
