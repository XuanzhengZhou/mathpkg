A causal box is a spacetime region obtained by smoothly embedding a standard cylinder from Minkowski space into a general spacetime, preserving causal character of the boundaries. It serves as the fundamental domain of integration for formulating integral conservation laws: a 3-form $\omega$ is closed iff $\int_{\partial N} \omega = 0$ for all causal boxes $N$.
