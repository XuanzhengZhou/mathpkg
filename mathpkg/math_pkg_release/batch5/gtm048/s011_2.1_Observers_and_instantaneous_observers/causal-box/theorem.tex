Let $N_0$ be the standard cylinder in Minkowski space $(\mathbb{R}^4, g, D)$:
$$N_0 = \{(u^1, \ldots, u^4) \mid \sum_{\nu=1}^{3} u^\nu u^\nu \leq 1,\ |u^4| \leq 1\}.$$
The boundary manifolds of $N_0$ are the five imbedded submanifolds $B_1, B_2, B_3, C_1, C_2$ (where $B_3$ is timelike and the rest are spacelike). A \textbf{causal box} in $M$ is a $C^\infty$ imbedding $\phi: N_0 \rightarrow M$ which preserves orientation, time orientation, and the causal character of each boundary manifold. By abuse of language, $N \equiv \phi N_0$ is also called a causal box.
