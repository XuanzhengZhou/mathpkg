An instantaneous stationary observer is the restriction of the stationary observer concept to a single point in Schwarzschild spacetime. It pairs a spacetime point $x$ with the stationary vector field value $Wx$ at that point. This is the instantaneous version of the stationary observer, useful when only the local tangent information is needed.
