An instantaneous comoving observer is the restriction of the comoving observer to a single spacetime point $(x, Zx)$ in Einstein--de Sitter cosmology. It pairs a point $x \in M$ with the canonical future-pointing unit timelike vector $Zx = \partial_4|_x$ at that point, giving a snapshot of the cosmological expansion at a single event.
