This proposition characterizes when a relativistic model that obeys Einstein's field equation yields a Ricci flat spacetime. Under Einstein's equation, the spacetime manifold $M$ is Ricci flat precisely when both the matter stress-energy tensor $T$ and the electromagnetic field $F$ vanish identically. This establishes that vacuum models (no matter, no electromagnetism) correspond exactly to Ricci flat geometries.
