Proposition 7.5.2 shows that the open submanifold $M_I$ of a Kruskal spacetime, defined by $u>0, v>0$ in Kruskal null coordinates, is isometric to the normal Schwarzschild spacetime of active mass $8\pi\mu$. The isometry is constructed explicitly by introducing the Schwarzschild time coordinate $t = 2\mu[\ln u - \ln(-v)]$ on $M_I$. This result connects the maximal Kruskal extension back to the familiar exterior Schwarzschild solution and is the starting point for analyzing global causal structure.
