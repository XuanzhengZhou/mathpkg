Proposition 7.6.3 gives a complete characterization of parallel vector fields in a gravitational plane wave spacetime: they are exactly the constant scalar multiples of the propagation direction $Y$. The forward direction uses the explicit connection forms computed in Proposition 7.6.1 to verify $D(aY) = 0$. The converse follows from the curvature tensor: if $DX = 0$, then $\hat{R}(\cdot, X, \cdot, \cdot) = 0$ by definition of the curvature tensor, and analyzing the non-zero curvature components forces $X$ to be proportional to $Y$.
