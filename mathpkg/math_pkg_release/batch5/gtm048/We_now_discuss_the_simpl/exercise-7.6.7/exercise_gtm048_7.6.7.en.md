In Exercise 7.6.5 show $X \equiv \partial_1$ has the following properties.

(a) $|X| = 1$.

(b) $\forall z \in M$, $\mathcal{P}_z \equiv$ span $\{Xz, Yz\}$ is lightlike.

(c) $D(X \wedge Y) = 0$.

(d) $\forall$ geodesic observer $\gamma: \mathcal{E} \rightarrow M$, and $\forall u \in \mathcal{E}$, a principal direction of the relative acceleration transformation $\psi_u$ is the line $(\mathcal{P}_{\gamma u}) \cap R_u \subset R_u \subset M_{\gamma u}$.

Remark: Here $(M, g)$ is linearly polarized; (d) identifies $\mathcal{P}_{\gamma u} \subset M_{\gamma u}$ as the spacetime polarization plane; and (c) shows this plane is "constant" (parallel).
