This proposition gives the fundamental ordinary differential equation satisfied by the dimensionless inverse radius $f = \mu/\tilde{\gamma}$ along a freely falling particle worldline in the Schwarzschild exterior. The derivation uses the Killing vector fields to express conserved energy $E$ and angular momentum $J$, then substitutes into the normalization condition $g(\gamma_*, \gamma_*) = -m^2$. The $(2f^3)$ term is the relativistic correction absent in Newtonian mechanics; dropping it yields the Newtonian orbit equation as a first-order approximation.
