(a) Show that a perfect fluid with zero pressure is a dust.

(b) Let $M$ be a quasi-gas on $M$ and $W$ be a reference frame on $M$. Suppose that the energy-momentum $P_A$ of each component of $M$ satisfies $P_A = f_A W$, where $f_A > 0$. Show that $M' = (W, \sum f_A^2 \eta_A)$ is a dust on $M$.
