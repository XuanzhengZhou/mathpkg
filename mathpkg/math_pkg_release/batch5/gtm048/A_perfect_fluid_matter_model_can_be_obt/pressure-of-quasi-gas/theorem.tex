For a quasi-gas $M$ with stress-energy tensor $\hat{T} = \rho Z \otimes Z + p(\hat{g} + Z \otimes Z)$ as in Proposition 3.15.1, the function $p$ on $M$ is called the \text{pressure} of the quasi-gas $M$. The pressure satisfies $\rho \geq 3p \geq 0$ and admits a physical interpretation in terms of random velocities (see Exercise 3.15.6).
