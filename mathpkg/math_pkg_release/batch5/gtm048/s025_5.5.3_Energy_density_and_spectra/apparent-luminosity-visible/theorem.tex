Let $K \subset \mathcal{S}_Z$ be as in the definition of apparent size, and suppose the photon distribution $F_Z$ vanishes outside $K \times (0, \infty)$. Then the apparent luminosity in the visible $\ell_V$ is defined by

$$\ell_V = \int_{K \times [a, b]} eF_z \pi_Z,$$

where $[a, b]$ corresponds to the visible range, with $(a/h) \cong 3 \times 10^{14}$ (seconds)$^{-1}$ and $(b/h) \cong 10^{15}$ (seconds)$^{-1}$.
