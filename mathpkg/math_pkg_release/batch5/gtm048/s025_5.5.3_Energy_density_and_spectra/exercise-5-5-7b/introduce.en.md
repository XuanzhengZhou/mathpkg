Exercise 5.5.7b asks the reader to make plausible an approximate equality connecting the energy integral of a photon distribution function to a sum of stress-energy tensor components. The integral $\int eF_Z \pi_Z$ (which measures energy density) is approximated by $\sum [g(P_A, Z)]^2 \eta_A z$, which equals the sum of $T_A(Z,Z)$ components of the stress-energy tensors of the individual photon beams.
