Suppose there is a very large, finite collection $\{(P_A, \eta_A)\}$ of photon beams that model the same physical situation that $F_Z$ describes at $z$. Let $\mathcal{K} \subset \mathcal{S}_Z \times (0, \infty)$ be an integration region and define $K = \{A \mid P_A z \in \pi_Z \mathcal{K}\}$, where $\pi_Z$ is defined in Exercise 5.5.6a.

Make the following approximate equality plausible to yourself using Definition 3.2.1, Section 5.2.2, and some pictures:

$$\int_{\mathcal{K}} eF_Z \pi_Z \cong \sum_{A \in K} [g(P_A, Z)]^2 \eta_A z = \sum_{A \in K} T_A(Z, Z),$$

where $\hat{T}_A$ is the stress-energy tensor of $(P_A, \eta_A)$.
