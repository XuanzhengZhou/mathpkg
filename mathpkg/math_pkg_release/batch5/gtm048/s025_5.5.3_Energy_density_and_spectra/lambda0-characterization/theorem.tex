The 3-form $\Lambda_0 = \iota^*[(1/u^4) du^1 \wedge du^2 \wedge du^3]$ on $\mathcal{L}_0^+$ can be intrinsically characterized as follows. For every 3-form $\chi$ over $\iota$ such that $(\omega \circ \iota) \wedge \chi = \Omega \circ \iota$, we have $\Lambda_0 w = \iota^*(\chi w)$ for all $w \in \mathcal{L}_0^+$.
