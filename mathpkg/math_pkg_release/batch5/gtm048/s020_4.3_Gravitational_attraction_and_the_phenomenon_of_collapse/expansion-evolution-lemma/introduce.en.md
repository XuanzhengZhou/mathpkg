This lemma gives the two fundamental differential equations governing a pressureless perfect fluid (dust) with an irrotational comoving frame in general relativity. Part (a) is a Raychaudhuri-type equation showing that the derivative of the expansion scalar $\operatorname{div} Z$ along the flow is strictly negative whenever the Ricci curvature is positive — gravity drives the dust to contract. Part (b) expresses conservation of energy: the change in energy density $\rho$ along the flow is proportional to the negative of the expansion, so contraction increases density and expansion decreases it.
