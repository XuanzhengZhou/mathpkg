Let $(M, \mathbf{M}, F)$ be a dust spacetime ($p = 0$) with irrotational comoving reference frame $Z$, and zero electromagnetic field. Then $D_Z Z = 0$, $\operatorname{div}(\rho Z) = 0$, and $Z$ is geodesic and irrotational. Let $g(A_Z X, W) = g(X, A_Z W)$ for all $X, W \perp Z$, and set $\operatorname{div} Z = -\operatorname{trace} A_Z$.

\textbf{(a)} The derivative of $\operatorname{div} Z$ along $Z$ satisfies
$$
Z(\operatorname{div} Z) = -\operatorname{Ric}(Z, Z) - \operatorname{trace}(A_Z^2),
$$
where $\operatorname{trace}(A_Z^2) = f_\alpha^\beta f_\beta^\alpha = \sum_{\alpha,\beta} (f_\alpha^\beta)^2 \geq 0$. In particular, $Z(\operatorname{div} Z) < 0$ wherever $\operatorname{Ric}(Z, Z) > 0$.

\textbf{(b)} The energy density evolves according to
$$
Z\rho = -\rho \operatorname{div} Z.
$$
