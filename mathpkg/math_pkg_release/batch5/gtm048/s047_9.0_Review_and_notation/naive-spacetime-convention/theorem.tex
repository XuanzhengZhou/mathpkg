On the naive spacetime $(\mathbb{R}^4, g)$ (Section 9.0.2), a scalar field $\phi(\vec{x}, t)$ is regarded as $\phi: \mathbb{R}^4 \to \mathbb{R}$ via $\phi(w) = \phi(u^1 w, u^2 w, u^3 w, u^4 w)$. A time-dependent vector field $\vec{j} = j^\alpha \partial_\alpha$ with $du^4(j) = 0$ is regarded as a vector field on $\mathbb{R}^4$. A stress-energy tensor $T^{ij} \partial_i \otimes \partial_j$ decomposes into a scalar $T^{44}$, vector $T^{4\alpha}$, and tensor $T^{\alpha\beta}$ fields on $\mathbb{R}^3$.
