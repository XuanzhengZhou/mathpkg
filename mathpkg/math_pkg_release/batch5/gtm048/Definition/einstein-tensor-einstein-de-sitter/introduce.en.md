This proposition provides the explicit computation of the curvature tensor, Ricci tensor, and Einstein tensor for the Einstein–de Sitter spacetime. The computation proceeds by constructing an orthonormal frame of 1-forms, computing the connection forms via Cartan's first structure equation, then the curvature forms via the second structure equation. The result shows that the Ricci tensor is proportional to the metric (as expected for a Robertson–Walker spacetime) and that the scalar curvature diverges as $u^4 \to 0$, indicating the Big Bang singularity.
