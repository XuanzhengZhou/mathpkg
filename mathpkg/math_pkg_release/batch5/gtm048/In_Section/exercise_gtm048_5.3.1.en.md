**EXERCISE 5.3.1**

Generalize the radar synchronization discussion to the case that the reference frame $Z$ is synchronizable but not proper time synchronizable, by assuming one autocrat observer $\gamma$ and other observers who regard the consistency condition
$$
t_1 = \frac{1}{2}(t_0 + t_2)
$$
for every nearby radar measurement as more important than insisting on their own proper time.
