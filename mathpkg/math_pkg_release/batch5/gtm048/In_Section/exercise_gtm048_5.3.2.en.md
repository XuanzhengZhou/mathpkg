**EXERCISE 5.3.2**

Take a rotating reference frame on Minkowski spacetime and show explicitly how the radar synchronization procedure (with an autocrat) determines a proper time function $t$ that is consistent with the condition $t_1 = \frac{1}{2}(t_0 + t_2)$ for nearby radar measurements, despite the frame not being proper time synchronizable.
