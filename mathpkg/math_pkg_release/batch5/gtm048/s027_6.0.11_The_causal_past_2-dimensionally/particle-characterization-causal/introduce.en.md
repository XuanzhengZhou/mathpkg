This proposition gives two characterizations of the causal relation $x \leq z$ in Einstein-de Sitter spacetime in terms of particles. Part (c) states that $x$ causally precedes $z$ exactly when they are the same point or there exists a particle worldline connecting them; this holds in any spacetime (Penrose). Part (d) strengthens this by requiring the connecting particle to be freely falling, which is a special property of Einstein-de Sitter spacetime -- removing even a single point from Minkowski space destroys this property.
