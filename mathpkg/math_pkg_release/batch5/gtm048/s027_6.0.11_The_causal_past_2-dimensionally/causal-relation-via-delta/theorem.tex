Let $M$ be Einstein-de Sitter spacetime. For $x, z \in M$, define $u = 3(u^4)^{1/3}$ and
$$
\delta(x, z) = \left\{ \sum_{\mu=1}^{3} (u^\mu z - u^\mu x)^2 \right\}^{1/2}.
$$
Then
$$
x \leq z \iff 0 \leq \delta(x, z) \leq u z - u x.
$$
Equivalently,
$$
x \leq z \iff 3\big\{ (u^4 z)^{1/3} - (u^4 x)^{1/3} \big\} \geq \delta(x, z).
$$
