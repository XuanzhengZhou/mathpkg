Suppose $x, z \in M$ and $\delta(x, z) \neq 0$ (Section 6.0.12). With $O_z^3$ the group (Section 6.0.5g) of spatial rotations about $z$, denote by $S$ the set $\{y \mid y = \psi x \text{ for some } \psi \in O_z^3\}$.

(a) Show $S$ is a spacelike $2$-submanifold, diffeomorphic to $S^2$ and given explicitly by: $y \in S$ iff $u^4 y = u^4 x$ and $\delta(x, z) = \delta(y, z)$.

(b) Show the induced metric on $S$ is that for a sphere of radius $(u^4 x)^{2/3} \delta(x, z)$ in $\mathbb{R}^3$. (Hint: regard $S$ as a submanifold of the level surface $u^4 = u^4 x$).
