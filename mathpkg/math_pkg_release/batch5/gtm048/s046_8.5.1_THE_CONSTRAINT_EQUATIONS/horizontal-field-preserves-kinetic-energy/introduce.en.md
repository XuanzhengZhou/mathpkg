This proposition states that horizontal vector fields on the tangent bundle preserve the kinetic energy function. The proof uses the fact that $(\Pi^* D)_H I = 0$ together with the metric compatibility of the Levi-Civita connection. This property is crucial for photon gas dynamics, where the kinetic energy function $K$ is constant along the flow of horizontal vector fields.
