Let $B$ be a 3-manifold, $\beta: B \to M$ a spacelike imbedding into a spacetime $(M, g)$, and $k$ the second fundamental form of $\beta$. Write $b = \beta^* g$, so $(B, b)$ is a Riemannian 3-manifold. Let $\tilde{k}$ be the $(1,1)$-tensor field on $B$ physically equivalent to $k$ via $b$. If $(M, g)$ has zero Ricci tensor, then $(B, b, k)$ obeys the \textbf{constraint equations}:
$$b = (\operatorname{trace} \tilde{k})^2 - \operatorname{trace}[\operatorname{contraction} \tilde{k} \otimes \tilde{k}],$$
$$\operatorname{div} \tilde{k} = d(\operatorname{trace} \tilde{k}).$$
Conversely, if these equations hold for every such spacelike imbedding, then $(M, g)$ is Ricci flat.
