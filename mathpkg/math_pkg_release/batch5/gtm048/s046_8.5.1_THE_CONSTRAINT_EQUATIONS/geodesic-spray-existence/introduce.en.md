The geodesic spray is the unique horizontal vector field $G$ on $TM$ that projects to the identity vector field $I$ under $\Pi_*$. Its integral curves project to geodesics of the spacetime $(M, g)$. The existence and uniqueness of $G$ follows from the defining properties of the Levi-Civita connection and the horizontal condition $(\Pi^* D)_G I = 0$.
