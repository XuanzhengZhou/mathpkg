Let $(M, g)$ be a Ricci-flat spacetime and $h$ a symmetric $(0, 2)$-tensor field on $M$. Then $h$ \textbf{obeys the vacuum Einstein equation linearized about $(M, g)$} iff in every local coordinate system

$$g^{ij} \left( h_{ki|j|l} - h_{ki|l|j} - h_{kl|j|i} + h_{ij|k|l} \right) = 0,$$

where the vertical bar denotes covariant differentiation with respect to the Levi-Civita connection of $g$.
