A symmetric $(0, 2)$-tensor field $h$ on a Ricci-flat spacetime $(M, g)$ is said to obey the linearized vacuum Einstein equation when it satisfies a specific second-order linear PDE involving covariant derivatives and traces of $h$. This equation describes first-order perturbations of the metric that preserve the Ricci-flat condition to linear order. Such tensor fields are fundamental in the study of gravitational waves and the initial value formulation of general relativity.
