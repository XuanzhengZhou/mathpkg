This result shows that horizontal vector fields respect the light cone structure: at any point $v$ on the future light cone $\mathcal{L}^+$, the horizontal vector $H(v)$ lies in the tangent space to $\mathcal{L}^+$. This follows from the fact that $\mathcal{L}^+$ is a level set of the kinetic energy function $K$, and $dK(H) = 0$. The proposition is essential for studying photon gases, where the dynamics are confined to the light cone.
