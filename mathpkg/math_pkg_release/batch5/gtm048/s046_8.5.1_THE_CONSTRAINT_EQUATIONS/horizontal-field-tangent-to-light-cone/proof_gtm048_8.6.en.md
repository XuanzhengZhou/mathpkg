The future light cone $\mathcal{L}^+$ is the level set $K^{-1}(0) \cap \{v: v \text{ is future-pointing}\}$, where $K(v) = \frac{1}{2} g(v, v)$. By the preceding exercise, $dK(H) = 0$ for any horizontal vector field $H$. Therefore $H$ is everywhere tangent to the level sets of $K$, and in particular to $\mathcal{L}^+$. Thus $H(v) \in T_v \mathcal{L}^+$ for all $v \in \mathcal{L}^+$.
