Let $(M, g)$ be a spacetime. The following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $(M, g)$ is Ricci flat, i.e., $\operatorname{Ric}(g) = 0$.
\item[(b)] For every spacelike imbedding $\beta: B \rightarrow M$ of a 3-manifold $B$, the induced triple $(B, b, \tilde{k})$ --- where $b = \beta^* g$ is the induced Riemannian metric and $\tilde{k}$ is the $(1,1)$-tensor field on $B$ physically equivalent to the second fundamental form $k$ of $\beta$ via $b$ --- satisfies the vacuum constraint equations:

$$
s = (\operatorname{tr} \tilde{k})^2 - \operatorname{tr}(\tilde{k} \circ \tilde{k}),
\qquad
\operatorname{div} \tilde{k} = d(\operatorname{tr} \tilde{k}),
$$

where $s$ is the scalar curvature of $(B, b)$.
\end{enumerate}
