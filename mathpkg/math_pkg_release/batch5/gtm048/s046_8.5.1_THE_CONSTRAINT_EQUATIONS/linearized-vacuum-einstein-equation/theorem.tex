Let $g_u$ be a smooth family of Lorentzian metrics on $\mathbb{R}^4$ parametrized by $u \in (a, c)$ with $0 < a < c$. Fix $b \in (a, c)$ and set $g = g_b$. Define the symmetric $(0,2)$-tensor field

$$
h = \sum_{i,j=1}^{4} \left.\frac{\partial g_{ij}}{\partial u}\right|_{u=b} du^i \otimes du^j,
$$

where $g_{ij}(x,u) = g_u(\partial_i x, \partial_j x)$. If each spacetime $(\mathbb{R}^4, g_u)$ is Ricci flat, then $h$ satisfies the \textbf{linearized vacuum Einstein equation}:

$$
g^{ij}\left(h_{ki|j|\ell} - h_{k\ell|i|j} - h_{ki|\ell|j} + h_{ij|k|\ell}\right) = 0,
$$

using the covariant derivative notation of Section 3.6.1, where the stroke $|$ denotes covariant differentiation with respect to the Levi-Civita connection of $g$.
