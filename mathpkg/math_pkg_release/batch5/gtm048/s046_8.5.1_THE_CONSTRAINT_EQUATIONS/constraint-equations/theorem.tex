Let $B$ be a 3-manifold, let $\beta: B \to M$ be a spacelike imbedding, and let $k$ be the second fundamental form of $\beta$. Set $b = \beta^* g$, so $(B,b)$ is a Riemannian 3-manifold. Denote its scalar curvature by $R_B: B \to \mathbb{R}$ and let $\tilde{k}$ be the $(1,1)$-tensor field on $B$ physically equivalent to $k$ via $b$.

(a) If $(M,g)$ has zero Ricci tensor, then $(B,b,k)$ satisfies:
$$R_B = (\operatorname{trace} \tilde{k})^2 - \operatorname{trace}[\tilde{k} \otimes \tilde{k}],$$
$$\operatorname{div} \tilde{k} = d(\operatorname{trace} \tilde{k}).$$

(b) Conversely, if these constraint equations hold for every such spacelike imbedding $\beta$, then $(M,g)$ is Ricci flat.
