Let $\mathcal{L}^+ \subset TM$ be the future light cone, let $\Xi: \mathcal{L}^+ \to M$ be the restriction of the projection $\Pi$, and let $I_0$ be the restriction of the identity map $I$ to $\mathcal{L}^+$, regarded as a vector field over $\Xi$. A vector field $H: \mathcal{L}^+ \to T\mathcal{L}^+$ is \textbf{horizontal} iff

$$(\Xi^* D)_H I_0 = 0.$$
