**Geodesics and Reflections.** Let $(M, g)$ be Einstein--de Sitter spacetime and suppose $z = (0, 0, 0, a) \in M$, $a \in (0, \infty)$.

(a) Show that the "spatial reflection" $(u^1, u^2, u^3, u^4) \rightarrow (-u^1, u^2, u^3, u^4)$ determines an isometry $\phi: M \rightarrow M$ with $(\phi z, \phi_* \partial_4 z) = (z, \partial_4 z)$.

(b) Show from (a) and Section 5.0.2 that if $\gamma: (0, \infty) \rightarrow M$ is a geodesic and $(\gamma b, \gamma_* b) = (z, \partial_4 z)$ for some $b \in (0, \infty)$ then $u^1 \circ \gamma = 0$.

(c) Generalizing, show in (b) that $u^2 \circ \gamma = 0 = u^3 \circ \gamma$ as well.

(d) Now show, without computing connection coefficients but using Section 5.0.2a instead, that each integral curve of $\partial_4$ in Einstein--de Sitter spacetime is a geodesic.
