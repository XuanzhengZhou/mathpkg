Suppose $(\mathbb{R}^4, g)$ is Minkowski space and $x, z \in \mathbb{R}^4$. Show $x \ll z$ iff

$$\left[ \sum_{\rho=1}^{3} (u^\rho z - u^\rho x)^2 \right]^{1/2} < u^4 z - u^4 x,$$

and $x \leq z$ iff the same condition with "$<$" replaced by "$\leq$" holds.

In view of Exercise 3.7.8 and the remarks on conformal invariance in Section 5.0.1, this result is applicable in a less trivial case (Section 6.0.13).
