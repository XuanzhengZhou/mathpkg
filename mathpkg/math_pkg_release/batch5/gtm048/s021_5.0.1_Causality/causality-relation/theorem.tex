The \emph{causality relation} $\leq$ on $M$ is the binary relation defined by $x \leq z$ iff $x = z$ or there exists a future-pointing (and thus causal) curve from $x$ to $z$. Intuitively, $x \leq z$ iff $z$ can get some information about $x$. Formally, $\leq$ is a subset of $M \times M$, and $\ll \subset \leq$.
