This framework provides the systematic dictionary for translating between Newtonian physics concepts and their relativistic counterparts. By embedding Newtonian space and time into a "naive spacetime" $\mathbb{R}^4$ with a metric that approximates the Minkowski metric plus small corrections from the Newtonian gravitational potential, one can see how relativistic concepts reduce to Newtonian ones in the appropriate limit. The correspondence is essential for understanding the physical interpretation of relativistic models.
