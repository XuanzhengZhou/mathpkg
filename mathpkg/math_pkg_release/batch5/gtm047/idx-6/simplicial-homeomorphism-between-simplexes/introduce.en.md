Any two $n$-simplexes in $\mathbb{R}^m$ are simplicially homeomorphic via a map that pairs corresponding vertices. The homeomorphism is defined by sending a point expressed in barycentric coordinates relative to the first simplex to the point with the same barycentric coordinates relative to the second simplex. This is a fundamental fact about the topological equivalence of simplexes.
