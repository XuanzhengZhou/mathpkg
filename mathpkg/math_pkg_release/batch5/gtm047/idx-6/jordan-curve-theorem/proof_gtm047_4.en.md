The proof of the Jordan curve theorem is developed throughout Chapter 4 through a series of preliminary results (Theorems 1 and 2 of this chapter, among others) and geometric arguments about the separation properties of arcs and polygons in the plane. The full proof is completed in the subsequent sections of the chapter.
