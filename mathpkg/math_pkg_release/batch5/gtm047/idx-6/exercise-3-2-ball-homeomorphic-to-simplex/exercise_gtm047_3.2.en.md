Let $\sigma^2$ be a 2-simplex in $\mathbb{R}^2$, and let $J = \operatorname{Fr} \sigma^2$. Prove that there is a homeomorphism $g: \mathbf{B}^2 \leftrightarrow \sigma^2$. (For the definition of $\mathbf{B}^2$, see the beginning of Section 9.)
