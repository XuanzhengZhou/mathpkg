Prove or disprove: any homeomorphism of the boundary of a 2-simplex onto itself can be extended to a homeomorphism of the whole simplex. This exercise tests whether the boundary data of a self-homeomorphism on the frontier of a triangle determines an extension to the filled triangle. The result relates to the question of whether the extension property holds for simplexes as it does for disks in the plane.
