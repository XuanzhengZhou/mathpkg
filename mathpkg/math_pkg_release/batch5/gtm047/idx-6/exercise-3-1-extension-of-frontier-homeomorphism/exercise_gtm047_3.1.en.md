Prove or disprove: Let $\sigma^2$ be a 2-simplex in $\mathbb{R}^2$, and let $J = \operatorname{Fr} \sigma^2$. Let $f$ be a homeomorphism $J \leftrightarrow J$. Then $f$ can be extended to give a homeomorphism $f'': \sigma^2 \leftrightarrow \sigma^2$.
