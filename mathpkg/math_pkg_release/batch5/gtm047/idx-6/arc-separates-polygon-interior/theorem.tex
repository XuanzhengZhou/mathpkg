Let $I$ be the interior of a polygon in $\mathbb{R}^2$, and let $P, Q, R$, and $S$ be points of $\operatorname{Fr} I$, appearing in the stated cyclic order on $\operatorname{Fr} I$. Let $A$ be an arc from $P$ to $R$, lying in $\bar{I}$, such that $A \cap \operatorname{Fr} I = \{P, R\}$. Then $I - A$ is the union of two disjoint open sets $U_Q, U_S$, containing $Q$ and $S$ in their frontiers.
