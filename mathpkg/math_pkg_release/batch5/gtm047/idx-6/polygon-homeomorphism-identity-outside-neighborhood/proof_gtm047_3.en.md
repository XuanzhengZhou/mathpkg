In the proof of Theorem 4, we choose our homeomorphisms so that each of them satisfies condition (2): each homeomorphism that removes a free 2-simplex is constructed to be the identity outside a small neighborhood of that simplex, and this neighborhood can be taken to lie within $U$. By composing such localized homeomorphisms, the resulting homeomorphism $h$ is also the identity on $\mathbb{R}^2 - U$.
