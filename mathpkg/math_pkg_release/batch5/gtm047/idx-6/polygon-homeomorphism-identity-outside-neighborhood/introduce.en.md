Theorem 4 can be strengthened: the homeomorphism sending a polygon to the frontier of a 2-simplex can be chosen to be the identity outside any prescribed open set containing the closure of the polygon's interior. This localized version is important for constructions that modify the polygon without disturbing the rest of the plane, and follows by carefully choosing the homeomorphisms in the proof of Theorem 4 to each satisfy this locality condition.
