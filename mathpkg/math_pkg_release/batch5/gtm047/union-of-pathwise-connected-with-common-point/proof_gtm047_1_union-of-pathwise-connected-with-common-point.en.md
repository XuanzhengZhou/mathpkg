Given $Q \in g_Q \in G$, $R \in g_R \in G$, let $p$ be a path in $g_Q$, from $Q$ to $P$, and let $q$ be a path in $g_R$, from $P$ to $R$. Then $p$ and $q$ fit together to give a path $r$, in $g_Q \cup g_R \subset G^*$, from $Q$ to $R$.
