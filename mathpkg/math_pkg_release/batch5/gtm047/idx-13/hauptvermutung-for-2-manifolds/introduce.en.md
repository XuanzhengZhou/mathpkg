The Hauptvermutung (principal conjecture) for 2-manifolds states that any two triangulations of the same topological 2-manifold are combinatorially equivalent. Equivalently, if there exists a homeomorphism between the polyhedra of two triangulated 2-manifolds, then there exists a piecewise-linear homeomorphism (PLH) between them. This fundamental result shows that the combinatorial structure of a triangulated 2-manifold is uniquely determined by its topology.
