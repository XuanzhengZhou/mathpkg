Let $K_1$ and $K_2$ be triangulated 2-manifolds, let $U$ be an open set in $|K_1|$, let $h$ be a homeomorphism $U \rightarrow |K_2|$, and let $\phi$ be a strongly positive function on $U$. Then there is a PLH $f: U \rightarrow |K_2|$ such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item $f$ is a $\phi$-approximation of $h$, and
\item $f(U) = h(U)$.
\end{enumerate}
