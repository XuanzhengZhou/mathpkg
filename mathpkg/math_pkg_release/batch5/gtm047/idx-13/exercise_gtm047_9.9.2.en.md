Let $M$ be a closed set in $\mathbf{R}^2$, let $P \in M$, let $h$ be a homeomorphism $M \leftrightarrow M' \subset \mathbf{R}^2$, and let $P' = h(P)$. Prove or disprove: If $P$ is arcwise accessible from $\mathbf{R}^2 - M$, then $P'$ is arcwise accessible from $\mathbf{R}^2 - M'$.
