Let $L_1, L_2, \ldots, L_n$ be the lines that contain edges of $J$. Each line $L_i$ decomposes $\mathbb{R}^2$ into two closed half-planes; and $\bigcup_{i=1}^{n} L_i$ decomposes $\mathbb{R}^2$ into a finite collection of closed convex regions. From these we can construct a triangulation of $\overline{I}$.
