Let $K$ and $L$ be complexes, and let $f$ be a homeomorphism $|K| \leftrightarrow |L|$. If there is a subdivision $K_1$ of $K$ such that each mapping $f \mid \sigma$ ($\sigma \in K_1$) maps $\sigma$ linearly into a simplex of $L$, then $f$ is piecewise linear. PL stands for piecewise linear, and PLH stands for PL homeomorphism.
