Theorem 7 strengthens Theorem 6: not only can paths be replaced by PL representatives, but any based homotopy between two PL paths can itself be replaced by a PL homotopy. This ensures that the fundamental group of an open set in $\mathbb{R}^3$ can be completely understood using PL methods, a crucial technical tool for the geometric arguments in the remainder of the book.
