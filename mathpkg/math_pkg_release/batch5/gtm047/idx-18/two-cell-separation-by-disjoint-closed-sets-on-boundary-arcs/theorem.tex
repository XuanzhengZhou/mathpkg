Let $C^2$ be a $2$-cell, with $\operatorname{Bd} C^2 = J = B_1 \cup B_2$, where $B_1$ and $B_2$ are arcs with common end-points $Q$, $S$. Let $M_1$ and $M_2$ be disjoint closed sets in $C^2$, such that $M_i \cap J \subset \operatorname{Int} B_i$ ($i = 1, 2$). Then $Q$ and $S$ are in the same component of $C^2 - (M_1 \cup M_2)$.
