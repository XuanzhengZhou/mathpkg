Theorem 3 verifies the group axioms for $\pi(X, P_0)$: associativity holds up to homotopy (via reparametrization), the constant loop $\bar{e}$ serves as the identity, and the inverse of $\bar{p}$ is $\overline{p^{-1}}$ where $p^{-1}(t) = p(1-t)$ is the reverse path. This makes the fundamental group a well-defined algebraic invariant of the space.
