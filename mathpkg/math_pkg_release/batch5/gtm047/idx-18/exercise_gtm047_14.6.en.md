For each vertex $v_i$ of $K$, let $b_i$ be a simplicial path from $P_0$ to $v_i$. (We allow the constant path $[0, 1] \to P_0$, in the case $v_i = P_0$.) These paths $b_i$ are chosen at random, subject to the stated conditions, but are fixed hereafter. For each $2$-simplex $\sigma^2 = v_i v_j v_k$ of $K$, let $q(\sigma^2)$ be a path which is the product of (a) $b_i$, (b) a path which traverses $\operatorname{Bd} \sigma^2$, and (c) $b_i^{-1}$. Show that $\overline{q(\sigma^2)}$ is the identity in $\pi(|K|, P_0)$.
