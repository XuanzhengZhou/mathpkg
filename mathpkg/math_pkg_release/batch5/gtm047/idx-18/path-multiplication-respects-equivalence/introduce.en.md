Theorem 2 of Chapter 14 shows that the path multiplication operation is compatible with the equivalence relation $\cong$. This guarantees that concatenation induces a well-defined binary operation on the set of equivalence classes $\pi(X, P_0) = \operatorname{CP}(X, P_0)/{\cong}$, which will be the group operation.
