The fundamental group $\pi(X, P_0)$ of a pathwise connected space $X$ with base point $P_0$ is the set of based homotopy equivalence classes of loops at $P_0$, endowed with the group operation induced by path concatenation. It is the first and most important homotopy group, capturing information about $1$-dimensional holes in the space.
