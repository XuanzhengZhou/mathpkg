Let $\bar{p} = \{ q \in \operatorname{CP}(X, P_0) \mid q \cong p \}$ denote the equivalence class of $p$ under $\cong$. Define

$$\pi(X, P_0) = \{ \bar{p} \mid p \in \operatorname{CP}(X, P_0) \},$$

with multiplication $\bar{p} \cdot \bar{q} = \overline{pq}$ induced by path concatenation in $\operatorname{CP}(X, P_0)$.
