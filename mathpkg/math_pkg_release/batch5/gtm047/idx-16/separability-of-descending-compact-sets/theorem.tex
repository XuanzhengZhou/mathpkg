Let $M_1, M_2, \ldots$ be a descending sequence of compact sets in a metrizable space $[X, \emptyset]$, and let $A$ and $B$ be disjoint closed sets in $X$ such that $A$ and $B$ are inseparable in each $M_i$. Let $M_{\infty} = \bigcap_{i=1}^{\infty} M_i$. Then $A$ and $B$ are inseparable in $M_{\infty}$.
