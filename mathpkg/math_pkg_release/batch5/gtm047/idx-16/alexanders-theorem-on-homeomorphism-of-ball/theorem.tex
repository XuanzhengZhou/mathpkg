In $\mathbf{R}^n$, let $\mathbf{B}^n = \{P \mid \|P\| \leq 1\}$, $\mathbf{S}^{n-1} = \operatorname{Fr} \mathbf{B}^n = \{P \mid \|P\| = 1\}$. Let $f_1$ be a homeomorphism $\mathbf{B}^n \leftrightarrow \mathbf{B}^n$, such that $f_1|_{\mathbf{S}^{n-1}}$ is the identity. Then $f_1$ is isotopic to the identity mapping $f_0: \mathbf{B}^n \leftrightarrow \mathbf{B}^n$, $P \mapsto P$.
