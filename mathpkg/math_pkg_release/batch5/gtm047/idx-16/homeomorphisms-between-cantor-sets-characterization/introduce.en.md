Theorem 7 provides a constructive characterization of homeomorphisms between Cantor sets. Given two compact metrizable spaces equipped with compatible sequences of finite clopen coverings whose mesh tends to zero, any consistent family of bijections between the coverings induces a unique homeomorphism. This theorem is central to the proof that any homeomorphism between Cantor sets in $\mathbf{R}^2$ extends to a homeomorphism of the whole plane.
