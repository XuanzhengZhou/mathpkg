Theorem 5 states that any clopen (both open and closed) subset of a Cantor set is itself a Cantor set. This property is essential for the recursive decomposition of Cantor sets. Since the sets $g_i$ in Theorem 4 are both open and closed in $C$, this theorem applies directly to them, showing that each piece in the finite decomposition is itself a Cantor set.
