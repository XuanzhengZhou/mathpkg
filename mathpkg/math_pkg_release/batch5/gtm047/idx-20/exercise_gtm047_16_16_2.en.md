Let $K_1$ and $K_2$ be knots in $\mathbf{R}^3$, and let $f: \mathbf{R}^3 \leftrightarrow \mathbf{R}^3$, $K_1 \leftrightarrow K_2$ be a PLH (piecewise-linear homeomorphism). Prove or disprove: if $K_1$ is unknotted, then so also is $K_2$.
