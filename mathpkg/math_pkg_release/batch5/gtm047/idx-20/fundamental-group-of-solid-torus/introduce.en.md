The fundamental group of a solid torus $T \cong D \times S^1$ is also infinite cyclic. The proof parallels that for the annulus: the solid torus is split by a 2-cell rather than a linear interval. As with the annulus, a single generator suffices and there are no relations. Topologically, this reflects that the solid torus deformation retracts onto its core circle $S^1$.
