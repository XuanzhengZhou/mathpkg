Let $L$ be the union of two simply linked plane polygons in $\mathbf{R}^3$. Prove or disprove:

$$\pi(\mathbf{R}^3 - L) \approx \mathbf{Z} + \mathbf{Z},$$

where the hypothesis that the polygons are simply linked means that the diagram looks like Figure 16.6, and $\mathbf{Z} + \mathbf{Z}$ denotes the direct sum.
