This technical theorem states that for three simply linked plane polygons arranged in series, if a closed path in the punctured disk $U = D - (J_1 \cup J_2 \cup J_3)$ is null-homotopic in the larger space $\mathbb{R}^3 - (J_1 \cup J_3)$, then it is already null-homotopic in $U$. This is used to justify that removing the middle polygon $J_2$ does not create new relations in the fundamental group.
