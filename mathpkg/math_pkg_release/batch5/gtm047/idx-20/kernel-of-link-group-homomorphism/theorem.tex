Let $L$ be a link in $\mathbf{R}^3$ with diagram giving generators $G = \{\bar{g}_1, \bar{g}_2, \ldots\}$ and relations $R = \{r_i\}$. Let $[R]$ be the set of equivalence classes of the relation words in $F(G)$. Let

$$\phi^*: F(G) \rightarrow \pi(\mathbf{R}^3 - L, P)$$

be the homomorphism induced by $\phi: W(G) \rightarrow \pi$, where $\phi$ maps each generator word to the corresponding product in $\pi$. Let $N([R])$ be the normal closure of $[R]$ in $F(G)$, i.e., the intersection of all normal subgroups of $F(G)$ containing $[R]$. Then

$$\ker \phi^* = N([R]).$$

Hence $\phi^*$ induces an isomorphism

$$\phi^{**}: F(G)/N([R]) \leftrightarrow \pi(\mathbf{R}^3 - L, P_0).$$
