Let $M = |K|$ be a connected finite linear graph, let $P$ and $Q$ be vertices of $K$, and suppose that no point of $M$ separates $P$ from $Q$ in $M$. Then $M$ contains a polygon which contains $P$ and $Q$. (The converse is trivial.)
