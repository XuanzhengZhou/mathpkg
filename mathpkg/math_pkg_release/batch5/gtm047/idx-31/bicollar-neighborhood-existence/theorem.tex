Let $M^3 = |K|$ be a triangulated 3-manifold with boundary, let $B = \text{Bd } M^3$, and suppose that $B$ is compact. Then there is a PLH

$$\rho: B \times [0, 1] \leftrightarrow W \subset M^3$$

such that (1) $W$ is a neighborhood of $B$ in $M^3$ and (2) for each point $P \in B$, $\rho(P, 0) = P$.
