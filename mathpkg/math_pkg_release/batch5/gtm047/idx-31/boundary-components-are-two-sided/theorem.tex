Let $M^3 = |K|$ be a triangulated 3-manifold with boundary, and let $M^2$ be a polyhedral 2-manifold lying in $\text{Int } M^3$. Suppose that $M^2$ is the union of a collection of components of the boundary $\text{Bd } N$ of a 3-manifold $N$ with boundary, lying in $M^3$. Then $M^2$ is two sided in $M^3$.
