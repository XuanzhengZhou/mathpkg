Let $L$ be a link in $\mathbf{R}^3$ and let $\{g_1, g_2, \ldots, g_n\}$ be the set of generators of $\pi(\mathbf{R}^3 - L, P_0)$ as constructed from the link diagram. For each crossing point in the diagram, define a crossing word $r_i$ in one of the two forms

$$r_i = g_i g_k g_j^{-1} g_k^{-1}, \quad \text{or} \quad r_i = g_i g_k^{-1} g_j^{-1} g_k,$$

according to the orientation of $a_k$ in the diagram. Let $R = \{r_i\}$ be the set of all crossing words, and note that $\bar{r}_i = \bar{e} \in \pi(\mathbf{R}^3 - L, P_0)$ for each $i$.

Given a generator word

$$p = \prod_{i=1}^{m} g_{j_i}^{\alpha_i} \quad (\alpha_i = \pm 1),$$

if $p \cong e$ (i.e., $p$ is null-homotopic in $\mathbf{R}^3 - L$), then the generator word on the right can be reduced to the identity $e$ by a finite sequence of operations, each of which inserts or deletes an expression of one of the forms

$$g_i r_j^{\pm 1} g_i^{-1}, \quad g_i g_i^{-1}, \quad g_i^{-1} g_i.$$
