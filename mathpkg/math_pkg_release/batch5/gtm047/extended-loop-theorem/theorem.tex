**Theorem 4 (Extended Loop theorem).** Let $M$ be a PL 3-manifold, let $M^2$ be a 2-manifold in $\operatorname{Bd} M$, and let $i: M^2 \to M$ be the inclusion. If $\ker i^*: \pi(M^2) \to \pi(M)$ is nontrivial, then there is a polyhedral 2-cell $D$ in $M$ such that $\operatorname{Bd} D = D \cap M^2$ and $\operatorname{Bd} D$ is not contractible in $M^2$.
