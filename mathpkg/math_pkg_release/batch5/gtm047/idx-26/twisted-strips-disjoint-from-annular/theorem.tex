In the $2$-cell with strips decomposition of Theorem 3, the $2$-cells can be chosen so that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] the intersections $S_i \cap D$ for twisted strips ($i > k$) lie in disjoint arcs in $\operatorname{Bd} D$, and
\item[(b)] $\bigcup_{i > k} (S_i \cap D)$ lies in an arc in $\operatorname{Bd} D$ which intersects no annular strip $S_j$ ($j \leqslant k$).
\end{enumerate}
