Operation $\alpha$ is a piecewise-linear homeomorphism of the $2$-manifold that slides the attachment points of a twisted strip along the boundary of $D \cup S_i$, moving an arc $QT$ onto $RT$ while fixing everything outside a neighborhood of $PT$. This operation allows one to reposition twisted strips so that their intersections with $D$ become disjoint and lie within a single arc on $\operatorname{Bd} D$, which is a key simplification step in the classification proof. The operation preserves the topological type of $D \cup S_i$.
