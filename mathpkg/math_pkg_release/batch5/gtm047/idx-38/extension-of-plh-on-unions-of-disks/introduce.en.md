This lemma provides the inductive step in constructing a piecewise linear homeomorphism (PLH) on the regular neighborhood of a 1-skeleton. Given two families of polyhedral disks with matching intersection patterns (governed by a 1-complex), a PLH can be built inductively by extending across each disk, using repeated applications of Theorem 5.4 (which extends PLHs between boundaries to PLHs between disks). The inductive construction ensures that intersections are preserved under the homeomorphism.
