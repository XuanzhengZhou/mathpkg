The following is a strengthened form of Theorem 9. Let $C$ and $C'$ be Cantor sets, let $D$ and $D'$ be countable dense sets in $C$ and $C'$ respectively, and let $h$ be a homeomorphism $D \leftrightarrow D'$. Then $h$ can be extended so as to give a homeomorphism $C \leftrightarrow C'$.
