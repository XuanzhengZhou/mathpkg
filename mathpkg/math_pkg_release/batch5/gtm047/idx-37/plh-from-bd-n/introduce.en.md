This lemma constructs a PL homeomorphism $f$ from the boundary of the regular neighborhood $\operatorname{Bd} N$ to the boundary $\operatorname{Bd} X$, matching corresponding pieces $A_v$ and $A_v'$. Since each $A_v'$ is a disk or disk with holes (Lemma 12), and the combinatorial structure of $\operatorname{Bd} N$ matches that of $\operatorname{Bd} X$, the PL homeomorphism exists and can be extended to the interior of $N$, completing the proof of Theorem 1.
