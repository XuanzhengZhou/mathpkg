This lemma shows that the boundaries $\operatorname{Bd} X$ and $\operatorname{Bd} N$ are homeomorphic. The proof uses the fundamental group isomorphism from Lemma 10 to deduce equality of first Betti numbers, applies orientability (Theorem 26.8), and then invokes the classification theorem for compact 2-manifolds (Theorem 22.9).
