(The statement of Lemma 6 is partially truncated in the source text. The proof follows from the general position arguments and the decomposition of $N'$ by the pseudocells $E$. Each $C_v''$ is a polyhedral 3-cell intersected with $\operatorname{Bd} X$, and under the general position conditions established in the preceding lemmas, the intersection is a connected 2-manifold whose boundary lies in the splitting disks $E \cap \operatorname{Bd} X$.)
