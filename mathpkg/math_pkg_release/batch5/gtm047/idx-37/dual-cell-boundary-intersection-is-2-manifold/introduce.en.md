This lemma describes the structure of the intersection of each dual cell region $C_v''$ with the boundary of $X$. Each such intersection is a connected polyhedral 2-manifold $A_v'$ with boundary, where the boundary of $A_v'$ is contained in the pseudocell intersections $E \cap \operatorname{Bd} X$. This provides the piecewise structure needed for constructing the PL homeomorphism.
