This lemma classifies each piece $A_v' = C_v'' \cap \operatorname{Bd} X$ of the boundary as either a disk or a disk with holes (a sphere with holes and no handles). The proof is a Betti number argument: if any $A_v'$ had a handle, the first Betti number of $\operatorname{Bd} X$ would strictly exceed that of $\operatorname{Bd} N$, contradicting the homeomorphism established in Lemma 11.
