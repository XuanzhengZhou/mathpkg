This lemma establishes the existence of a polyhedral 3-manifold $X$ with boundary that serves as the image of the desired PL homeomorphism. The manifold $X$ is constructed as a neighborhood of $K' = h(K)$ whose boundary lies in the interior of $N' = h(N)$ and separates $K'$ from the boundary of $N'$.
