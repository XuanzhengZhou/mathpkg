Let $C_{v_1}''$ be given, and let $E_1$ be a pseudo-cell lying in $C_{v_1}''$. Let $\Delta$ be a polyhedral disk lying in $C_{v_1}'' \cap \operatorname{Int} N'$, such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $\operatorname{Bd} \Delta = \Delta \cap E_1$,
\item[(2)] $\operatorname{Bd} \Delta$ bounds a disk in $E_1$, containing the center $P_1'$ of $E_1$ in its interior, and
\item[(3)] $\Delta$ intersects no other set $C_{v_2}''$.
\end{enumerate}
Then $\Delta$ intersects $K'$.
