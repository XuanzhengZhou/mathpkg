**Theorem 1.** Let $M$ be an $n$-manifold. Then there is a sequence $(N_1, N_1'), (N_2, N_2'), \ldots$ of ordered pairs of open sets in $M$, such that (1) for each $i$ there is a homeomorphism $h_i: \overline{N}_i \leftrightarrow \overline{D}, \overline{N}_i' \leftrightarrow \overline{D}'$ and (2) $\{N_i'\}$ covers $M$.
