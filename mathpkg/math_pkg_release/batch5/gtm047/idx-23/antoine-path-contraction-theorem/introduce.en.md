This theorem is a key step in the study of Antoine's necklace. It shows that if a path is contractible in the complement of the full Antoine set $\mathcal{Q}$, then it is also contractible in the complement of the first stage $T_1$ of the Antoine construction. The proof uses induction on the stages $T_n$ of the Antoine set, relying on the fact that each component of $T_{n-1}$ together with its intersection with $T_n$ forms a configuration similar to the pair $(T_1, T_2)$.
