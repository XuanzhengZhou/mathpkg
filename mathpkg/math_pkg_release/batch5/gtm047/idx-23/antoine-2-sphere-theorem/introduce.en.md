This theorem constructs a wild 2-sphere in $\mathbf{R}^3$ whose complement has infinitely many components, known as Antoine's 2-sphere. It is obtained by taking the closure of an ascending union $A = \bigcup A_i$ of multiple annuli that accumulate on the Antoine set $\mathcal{Q}$, yielding $S = A \cup \mathcal{Q}$. The 2-sphere is verified by copying the structure of the $A_i$ into a standard $S^2$ in such a way that diameters of complementary components approach zero, then extending a homeomorphism step by step.
