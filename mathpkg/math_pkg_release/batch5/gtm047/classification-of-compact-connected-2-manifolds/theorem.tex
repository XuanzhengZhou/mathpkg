**Theorem (Classification of compact connected 2-manifolds).** Every compact connected 2-manifold is homeomorphic to either a 2-sphere with $n$ handles (orientable case, $n \geqslant 0$) or a 2-sphere with $n$ cross-caps (nonorientable case, $n \geqslant 1$). The Euler characteristic, orientability, and the number of boundary components form a complete set of invariants.
