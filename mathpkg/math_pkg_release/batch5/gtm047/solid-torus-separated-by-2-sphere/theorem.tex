**Theorem 6.** Let $T$ be a solid torus in $\mathbb{R}^3$, with $\operatorname{Bd} T = T_0$ and let $T_1$ be a polygon which is a spine of $T$. Then $\mathbb{R}^3 - T$ contains no polyhedral 2-sphere which separates $T_1$ from $T_0$. However, there is a polyhedral 2-sphere $S$ in $\operatorname{Int} T$ separating $T_1$ from $T_0$.
