This theorem establishes that orientability is hereditary for triangulated 3-manifolds with boundary. If $K$ is an orientable triangulated 3-manifold with boundary and $K'$ is a subcomplex that is itself a triangulated 3-manifold with boundary, then $K'$ inherits orientability from $K$.
