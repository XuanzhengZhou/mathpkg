For a compact orientable 2-manifold $M$, the total number of handles $h(M)$ is defined as the sum of the numbers of handles of its connected components. By the classification theorem, each connected component $M_i$ is homeomorphic to a 2-sphere with $h_i$ handles, and $h(M)$ is the sum of these $h_i$. This invariant relates the topology of the boundary of a regular neighborhood to the first Betti number of the subcomplex (Theorem 18).
