This theorem establishes a useful isotopy result within triangulated 3-manifolds. Given a combinatorial 3-cell $C^3$ whose boundary is decomposed as the union of two 2-cells $D_1, D_2$ meeting along a simple closed curve $J$, there exists a piecewise-linear homeomorphism of the entire manifold that swaps $D_1$ with $D_2$ and is the identity outside a prescribed neighborhood of $C^3 - J$. The proof reduces to working within the star of a vertex and applying a PL homeomorphism derived from the structure of the standard 3-simplex.
