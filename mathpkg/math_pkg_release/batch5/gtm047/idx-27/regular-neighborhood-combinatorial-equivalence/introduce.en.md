This theorem states that if $L$ is a subcomplex of a triangulated 3-manifold $K$ and $L$ is itself a triangulated 3-manifold with boundary, then the geometric realization $|L|$ and its regular neighborhood $N(L)$ are combinatorially equivalent. The proof proceeds by a sequence of PL homeomorphisms that successively collapse the regular neighborhood onto $|L|$, using Theorem 10 to handle the 2-simplexes in the boundary of $L$.
