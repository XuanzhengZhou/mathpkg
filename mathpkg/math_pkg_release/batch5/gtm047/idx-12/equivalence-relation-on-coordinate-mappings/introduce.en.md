The relation $\sim$ on the set $C(X)$ of all coordinate mappings into a space $X$ is defined in terms of the simplicial structure of the domain simplexes. This theorem establishes that for each PL space $[X, \emptyset]$, this relation is an equivalence relation, which is fundamental because it allows the definition of PL simplexes as equivalence classes $[h]$ of coordinate mappings. The resulting equivalence classes form the building blocks of PL complexes.
