Let $\mathcal{K}$ be a PL complex and $M \subset |\mathcal{K}|$. Suppose there is a PL complex $\mathcal{K}'$ such that:

(1) $M = |\mathcal{K}'|$, and

(2) every element of $\mathcal{K}'$ is a rectilinear subsimplex of some simplex of $\mathcal{K}$.

If $M$ is compact (which implies $\mathcal{K}'$ is finite), this means that $M$ forms a subcomplex of a subdivision of $\mathcal{K}$.
