Let $M$ be an $n$-manifold. Then there is a sequence

$$(N_1, N_1'), (N_2, N_2'), \dots$$

of ordered pairs of open sets in $M$, such that:

(1) for each $i$ there is a homeomorphism

$$h_i: \overline{N}_i \leftrightarrow \overline{D}, \quad \overline{N}_i' \leftrightarrow \overline{D}',$$

where $D = \{ P \mid P \in \mathbf{R}^n \text{ and } \|P\| < 1 \}$ and $D' = \{ P \mid P \in \mathbf{R}^n \text{ and } \|P\| < \tfrac{1}{2} \}$, and

(2) $\{N_i'\}$ covers $M$.
