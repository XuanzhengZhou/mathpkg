In Antoine's construction of a wild Cantor set, $T_1$ is a circular solid torus and $T_2$ is a union of linked solid tori inside $T_1$. This theorem states that if a closed path in the complement of $T_1$ can be contracted in the complement of the smaller set $T_2$, then it can already be contracted in the complement of $T_1$. This gives a crucial relation between the fundamental groups of the complements at successive stages of the Antoine construction.
