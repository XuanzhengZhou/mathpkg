Let $g: \tilde{M} \rightarrow M$ be a covering, and let $f: \Delta \rightarrow M$ be a mapping of a 2-cell into $M$. Let $Q_0 \in \Delta$, let $P_0 = f(Q_0)$, and let $\tilde{P}_0 \in g^{-1}(P_0)$. Then there is one and only one lifting $\tilde{f}$ of $f$ such that $\tilde{f}(Q_0) = \tilde{P}_0$. A similar result holds for paths $p: [0, 1] \rightarrow M$, $Q_0 \mapsto P_0$.
