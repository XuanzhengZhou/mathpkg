**Proof.** (1) Given that $S = \bigcup_{i=1}^{n} C_i^3$, as in the definition of a CST, decompose $[0, 1]$ into $n$ linear intervals $I_i$, end to end, with end-points $x_i = i/n$ for $0 \leqslant i \leqslant n$. Then define $\phi | \sigma^2 \times \{i/n\}$ as a PLH $\sigma^2 \times \{i/n\} \leftrightarrow C_{i-1}^3 \cap C_i^3$. Then extend so that $\phi$ maps $\sigma^2 \times [i/n, (i+1)/n]$ by a PLH onto $C_i^3$.

(2) The proof of the converse is left to the reader.
