If $I_i$ intersects $I_j$ ($i \neq j$), then there is a broken line from an interior point of $a_i$ to an interior point of $a_j$, lying in $N_v$, and not intersecting the arcs $e'_k$ which lie in $\operatorname{Fr} I_i$. This contradicts Theorem 4.2.
