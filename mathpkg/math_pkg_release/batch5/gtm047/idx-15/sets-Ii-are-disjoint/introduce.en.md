In the taming construction for a linear graph without end-points, around each vertex $v$ we take a convex polyhedral 2-cell neighborhood $N_v$. The interior arcs of $M$ inside $N_v$, together with arcs on the boundary $J_v$, form 1-spheres that bound disjoint open 2-cells $I_i$. This lemma asserts that these regions $I_i$ are pairwise disjoint, which is needed to decompose $N_v$ cleanly.
