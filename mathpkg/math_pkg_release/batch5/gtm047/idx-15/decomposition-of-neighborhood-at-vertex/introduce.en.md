This lemma establishes that the convex polyhedral 2-cell neighborhood $N_v$ of a vertex $v$ is exactly the union of the closures of the disjoint open 2-cells $I_i$ formed by the 1-spheres $J_i$. This decomposition is essential for constructing the homeomorphisms that straighten the image of a linear graph into a polyhedron in the proof of Theorem 7.
