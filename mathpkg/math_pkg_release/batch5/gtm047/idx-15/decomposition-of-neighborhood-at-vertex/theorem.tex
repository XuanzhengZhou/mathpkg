Let $N_v$ be a convex polyhedral 2-cell neighborhood of a vertex $v$ of $M$, with boundary $J_v = \operatorname{Bd} N_v$. Let $e'_j$ be the arcs into which the intersection points of the edges of $M$ with $J_v$ decompose $J_v$. Let $a_i$ be the arcs of $\operatorname{Int} N_v \cap M$, and for each $a_i$ with endpoints $w_j, w_k$ on $J_v$, define
$$J_i = a_i \cup e'_j \cup e'_k,$$
a 1-sphere with interior $I_i$ lying in $N_v$. Then
$$N_v = \bigcup \overline{I}_i = \bigcup J_i \cup \bigcup I_i.$$
