Let $C^2$ be a 2-cell in $\mathbf{R}^2$ with boundary $J = \operatorname{Bd} C^2$, and let $P, Q, R, S$ be four points on $J$ appearing in cyclic order. Let $M_1$ and $M_2$ be disjoint closed subsets of $C^2$ such that $P, R \in M_1$ and $Q, S \in M_2$, and such that $M_1 \cap J$ and $M_2 \cap J$ are closed in $J$. Then $Q$ and $S$ are in the same component of $C^2 - (M_1 \cup M_2)$.
