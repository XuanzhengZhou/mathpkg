Let $M$ be a compact connected 2-manifold with boundary in $\mathbf{R}^2$, such that $\operatorname{Bd} M$ is the union of two disjoint 1-spheres. Then $M$ is an annulus; that is, $M$ is homeomorphic to a closed plane region bounded by two concentric circles.
