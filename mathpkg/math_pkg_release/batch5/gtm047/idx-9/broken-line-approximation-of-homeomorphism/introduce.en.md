This theorem shows that any homeomorphism from a 1-simplex into the plane can be uniformly approximated by a broken line lying within an epsilon-neighborhood of the image. This is the first step in the PL approximation theory developed in Chapter 6, establishing that 1-dimensional homeomorphisms can be approximated by piecewise linear maps.
