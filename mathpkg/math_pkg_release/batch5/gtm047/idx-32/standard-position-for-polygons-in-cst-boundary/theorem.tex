Let $M^3$ be a PL 3-manifold and let $T$ be the boundary of a combinatorial solid torus in $M^3$. Let $J_1, J_2, \dots, J_n$ be disjoint polygons in $T$, and let $J_x$ be a fixed polygon in $T$. Then there exists a PL homeomorphism $h$ of $M^3$ onto itself such that each $h(J_i)$ is in standard position relative to $J_x$. Moreover, given any neighborhood $W$ of $T$, $h$ can be chosen so that $h|_{M^3 - W}$ is the identity.
