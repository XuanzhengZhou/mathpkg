The $i$-skeleton $\Phi^i$ of an abstract complex $\Phi$ collects all simplexes of dimension at most $i$. The $0$-skeleton $\Phi^0$ consists precisely of the vertices. Skeletons form subcomplexes and provide a filtration of $\Phi$, mirroring the familiar skeletal decomposition of a CW complex or simplicial complex.
