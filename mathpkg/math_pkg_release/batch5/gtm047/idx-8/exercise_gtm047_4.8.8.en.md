Let $D, P, Q, B_1$, and $B_2$ be as in Problem 7. Let $M_1$ and $M_2$ be disjoint compact subsets of $D$, such that $M_i \cap \operatorname{Bd} D \subset \operatorname{Int} B_i$ ($i = 1, 2$). Then $P$ and $Q$ lie in the same component of $D - (M_1 \cup M_2)$.
