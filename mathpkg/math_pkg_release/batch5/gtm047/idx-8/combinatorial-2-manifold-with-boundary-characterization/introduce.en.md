This fundamental theorem states that every triangulated 2-manifold with boundary is automatically a combinatorial 2-manifold. Moreover, it identifies the topological boundary $\operatorname{Bd} M$ with the set of edges that lie in exactly one 2-simplex of the triangulation $K$. The proof builds on the previous lemmas: every edge lies in one or two 2-simplexes, and the link of every vertex is connected, implying the star is a combinatorial 2-cell.
