Operation $\beta$ inserts both a new vertex and a new edge into a face $C^2$ of an open cell-complex. The new edge connects the newly inserted vertex to an existing vertex on the frontier of the face. This operation, together with $\alpha$, $\gamma$, and $\delta$, forms a complete set of elementary moves for subdividing open cell-complexes.
