Operation $\alpha$ is one of four elementary subdivision operations on open cell-complexes. It inserts a new vertex into an existing edge, splitting that edge into two new edges that share the inserted vertex as their common frontier point. Together with operations $\beta$, $\gamma$, and $\delta$, it generates all subdivisions of open cell-complexes.
