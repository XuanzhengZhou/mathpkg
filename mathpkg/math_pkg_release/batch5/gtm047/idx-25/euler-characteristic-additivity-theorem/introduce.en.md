Theorem 8 establishes additivity of the Euler characteristic for two finite complexes whose intersection is a polygon forming a subcomplex of each. Since a polygon has Euler characteristic zero, the overlapping contribution cancels out, yielding the simple sum formula $\chi(K_1 \cup K_2) = \chi(K_1) + \chi(K_2)$.
