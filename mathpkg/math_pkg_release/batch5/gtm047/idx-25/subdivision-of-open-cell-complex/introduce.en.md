An open cell-complex $\mathcal{C}_2$ is a subdivision of $\mathcal{C}_1$, denoted $\mathcal{C}_2 < \mathcal{C}_1$, when every cell of $\mathcal{C}_2$ is contained in some cell of $\mathcal{C}_1$. This refinement relation generalizes barycentric subdivision from simplicial complexes to the more flexible setting of open cell decompositions.
