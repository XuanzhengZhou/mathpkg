Operation $\gamma$ inserts a new edge into a face $C^2$, connecting two existing vertices that lie on the frontier of the face. This operation splits the face without introducing new vertices. Together with $\alpha$, $\beta$, and $\delta$, these four elementary operations generate all subdivisions of open cell-complexes.
