In a metric space, let $P$ and $Q$ be points, and let $M_1, M_2, \ldots$ be a nested sequence of compact sets, such that (1) $P, Q \in M_i$ for each $i$, and (2) no set $M_i$ is the union of two disjoint closed sets $H$ and $K$, containing $P$ and $Q$ respectively. Then $\cap M_i$ has Properties (1) and (2).
