Given $P, Q \in N$, since $f$ is surjective there exist $P' \in f^{-1}(P)$ and $Q' \in f^{-1}(Q)$ in $M$. Since $M$ is pathwise connected, there is a path $p$ in $M$ from $P'$ to $Q'$. Then $f \circ p$ is a path in $N$ from $P$ to $Q$.
