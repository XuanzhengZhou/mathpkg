This exercise asks the reader to understand the effect of the homeomorphism $\phi$ (from the proof of Theorem 3) on the second arc $A_2$. The mapping $\phi$ is a composition of homeomorphisms that untie knots in $A_1$, but each step introduces knots into $A_2$. The goal is to develop intuition for how $A_2$ acquires an infinite sequence of knots under this process.
