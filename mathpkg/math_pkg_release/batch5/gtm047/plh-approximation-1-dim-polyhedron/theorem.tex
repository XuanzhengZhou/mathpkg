**Theorem 1.** Let $K$ be a 1-dimensional polyhedron in a PL 3-manifold $M_1$. Let $U$ be an open set containing $K$, and let $h$ be a homeomorphism of $U$ into a PL 3-manifold $M_2$. Let $\phi$ be a strongly positive function on $U$. Then there is a regular neighborhood $N$ of $K$ in $U$, and a PLH $f: N \leftrightarrow X \subset M_2$, such that (1) $X$ is a neighborhood of $K' = h(K)$ and (2) $f$ is a $\phi$-approximation of $h \mid N$.
