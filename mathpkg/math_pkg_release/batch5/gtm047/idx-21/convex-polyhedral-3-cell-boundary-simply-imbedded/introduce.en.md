The boundary of any convex polyhedral 3-cell in $\mathbb{R}^3$ is simply imbedded. The proof constructs a triangulation of $C^3$ as the join of an interior point with its boundary, then incrementally removes free 3-simplexes via piecewise-linear homeomorphisms, ultimately reducing $C^3$ to a single 3-simplex, which is trivially simply imbedded.
