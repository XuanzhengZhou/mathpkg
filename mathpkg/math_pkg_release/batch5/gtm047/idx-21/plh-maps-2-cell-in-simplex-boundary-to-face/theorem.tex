Given $\sigma^3 \subset \mathbb{R}^3$. Let $D$ be a polyhedral 2-cell in $\operatorname{Bd} \sigma^3$, and let $W$ be an open set containing $\sigma^3$. Then there is a PLH

$$f: \mathbb{R}^3 \leftrightarrow \mathbb{R}^3, \quad \sigma^3 \leftrightarrow \sigma^3, \quad D \leftrightarrow \sigma^2_0,$$

where $\sigma^2_0$ is a 2-face of $\sigma^3$, such that $f|(\mathbb{R}^3 - W)$ is the identity.
