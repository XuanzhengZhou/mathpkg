**Theorem 7.** Let $J$ be a polygon in $\mathbb{R}^2$, with interior $I$, and let $U$ be an open set containing $\overline{I}$. Then there is a homeomorphism $h: \mathbb{R}^2 \leftrightarrow \mathbb{R}^2$, such that (1) $h(J)$ is the frontier of a 2-simplex and (2) $h \mid (\mathbb{R}^2 - U)$ is the identity.
