This lemma establishes that the complement of a polygon $J$ in the plane can have at most two connected components. The proof uses a strip neighborhood $N$ of $J$: by traveling along paths within $N - J$, every point in the complement can be connected to one of two designated points, showing there are at most two distinct path-components.
