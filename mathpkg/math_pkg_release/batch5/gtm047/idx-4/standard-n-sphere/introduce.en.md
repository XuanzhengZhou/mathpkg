The standard $n$-sphere $S^n$ is the unit sphere in $n$-dimensional Euclidean space $\mathbf{R}^n$, centered at the origin $P_0$. It consists of all points whose Euclidean distance from the origin equals exactly 1. The standard $n$-sphere is the boundary of the standard $(n+1)$-ball and serves as the model for all $n$-dimensional spherical manifolds.
