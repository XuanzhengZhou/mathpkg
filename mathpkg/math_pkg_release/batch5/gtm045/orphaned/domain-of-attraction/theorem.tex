A distribution $F$ belongs to the \textit{domain of attraction} of a stable law $G$ if for i.i.d. random variables $X_n$ with d.f. $F$, there exist constants $a_n, b_n > 0$ such that

$$P\left[\frac{S_n - a_n}{b_n} < x\right] \to G(x).$$

$F$ belongs to the domain of attraction of a normal law if and only if

$$\frac{x^2 P[|X| \geq x]}{\int_{|y| < x} y^2 \, dF(y)} \to 0 \quad \text{as } x \to \infty.$$
