A nonempty class $\mathfrak{C}$ of sets in $\Omega$ is a \textit{field} (or \textit{algebra}) if it is closed under complementation and finite unions (equivalently, finite intersections). That is:

$$A \in \mathfrak{C} \implies A^c \in \mathfrak{C}, \quad A, B \in \mathfrak{C} \implies A \cup B \in \mathfrak{C}.$$
