The uan (or infinitesimality) condition requires that no single term in a triangular array dominates the sum. It is the natural generalization of the i.i.d. setting where each $X_k/\sqrt{n}$ becomes negligible. The uan condition is necessary for the limit distribution of sums to be infinitely divisible and is central to the general central limit criterion.
