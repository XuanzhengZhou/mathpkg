The \textit{outer extension} $\mu^0$ of a measure $\mu$ defined on a field $\mathfrak{C}$ is defined for all $A \subset \Omega$ by

$$\mu^0(A) = \inf \sum \mu(A_j),$$

where the infimum is taken over all countable coverings $\{A_j\} \subset \mathfrak{C}$ of $A$ (i.e., $A \subset \bigcup A_j$). The outer extension of $\mu$ is an extension of $\mu$ to an outer measure.
