If $F_n \Rightarrow F$ (nondegenerate) and there exist sequences $a_n, b_n > 0$ and $\alpha_n, \beta_n > 0$ such that

$$F_n(a_n x + b_n) \Rightarrow U(x), \quad F_n(\alpha_n x + \beta_n) \Rightarrow V(x),$$

with $U, V$ nondegenerate, then $\alpha_n/a_n \to A > 0$ and $(\beta_n - b_n)/a_n \to B$, and $V(x) = U(Ax + B)$.
