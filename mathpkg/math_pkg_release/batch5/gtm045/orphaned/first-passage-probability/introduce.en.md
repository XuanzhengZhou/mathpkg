The first passage (taboo) probability $Q_{jk}^n$ is the probability of reaching state $k$ for the first time in exactly $n$ steps, starting from $j$ and avoiding $k$ in intermediate steps. The central relation decomposes the $n$-step transition probability according to the time of first visit to $k$, yielding a renewal-type equation fundamental to the analysis of state classification and asymptotic behavior.
