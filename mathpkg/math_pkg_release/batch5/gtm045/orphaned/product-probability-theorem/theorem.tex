The product probability $P_T = \prod P_t$ defined on the class $\mathfrak{C}_T$ of interval cylinders by

$$P_T\left(\prod_{t \in T_N} A_t \times \prod_{t \in T \setminus T_N} \Omega_t\right) = \prod_{t \in T_N} P_t A_t$$

is $\sigma$-additive and determines its extension to a probability $P_T$ on the product $\sigma$-field $\mathfrak{A}_T$. Thus $(\Omega_T, \mathfrak{A}_T, P_T)$ is a probability space.
