The characteristic function $\varphi$ of an infinitely divisible distribution admits the \textit{Lévy-Khintchine representation}:

$$\log \varphi(u) = i\gamma u + \int_{\mathbb{R}} \left(e^{iux} - 1 - \frac{iux}{1+x^2}\right) \frac{1+x^2}{x^2} \, dG(x),$$

where $\gamma \in \mathbb{R}$ and $G$ is a bounded nondecreasing function with $G(-\infty) = 0$.
