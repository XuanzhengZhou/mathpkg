The modern definition of a probability space upgrades the finite-case field to a $\sigma$-field and finite additivity to $\sigma$-additivity (Kolmogorov axioms). The probability measure $P$ is continuous with respect to monotone sequences, and the liminf/limsup inequalities (Fatou-type for probabilities) follow from $\sigma$-additivity.
