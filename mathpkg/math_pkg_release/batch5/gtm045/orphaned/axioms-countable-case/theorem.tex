\textbf{Axiom I'.} Events form a $\sigma$-field $\alpha$: complements $A^c$, countable intersections $\bigcap_j A_j$, and countable unions $\bigcup_j A_j$ of events are events.

\textbf{Axiom II'.} $P$ on $\alpha$ is normed, nonnegative, and $\sigma$-additive:

$$P\Omega = 1, \quad PA \geq 0, \quad P \sum_{j} A_j = \sum_{j} PA_j.$$
