The set-theoretic liminf and limsup are fundamental tools for describing convergence of events. A point belongs to $\liminf A_n$ if it eventually stays in the sequence, and to $\limsup A_n$ if it occurs infinitely often. These definitions underpin the Borel-Cantelli lemmas and almost-sure convergence theory.
