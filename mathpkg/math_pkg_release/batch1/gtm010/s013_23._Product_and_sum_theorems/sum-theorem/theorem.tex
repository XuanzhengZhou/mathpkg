If $K = K_1 \cup K_2$ and $L = L_1 \cup L_2$ are CW complexes with $K_0 = K_1 \cap K_2$ and $L_0 = L_1 \cap L_2$, and if $f: (K; K_1, K_2) \to (L; L_1, L_2)$ is a map of triads that restricts to homotopy equivalences on each piece, then $\tau(f)$ satisfies an additivity formula in terms of the torsions of the restrictions.
