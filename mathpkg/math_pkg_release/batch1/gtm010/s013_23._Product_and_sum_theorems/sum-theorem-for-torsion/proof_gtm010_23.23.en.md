Let M_alpha denote the mapping cylinder of f_alpha. Then M_alpha -> K_alpha since f_alpha is a homotopy equivalence. It follows that (M_0 \cup K_1) -> K_1. So, by exactness of the triple (M_1, M_0 \cup K_1, K_1), pi_i(M_1, M_0 \cup K_1) = 0 for all i. Hence M_1 \searrow M_0 \cup K_1 (rel K_1). Using the additivity of torsion under short exact sequences and (5.9), the formulas follow.
