Let $C$ be an acyclic $(R, G)$-complex with preferred basis $c$ and chain contraction $\delta$. Define
$$\tau(C) = \tau(\langle (d + \delta)_{\text{odd}} \rangle_c) \in K_G(R)$$
where $(d + \delta)_{\text{odd}}: C_{\text{odd}} \to C_{\text{even}}$ is the restriction of $d + \delta$ to odd-dimensional components, and $\tau$ is the canonical map $GL(R) \to K_G(R)$. This is well-defined: independent of the choice of preferred basis and chain contraction.
