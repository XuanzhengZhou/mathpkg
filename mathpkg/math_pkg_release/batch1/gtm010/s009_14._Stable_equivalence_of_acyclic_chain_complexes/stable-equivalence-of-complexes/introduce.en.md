Stable equivalence is the algebraic relation that identifies acyclic chain complexes differing only by trivial summands. This equivalence relation is the correct algebraic analog of the geometric relation of simple-homotopy equivalence: just as adding a trivially attached cell pair does not change simple-homotopy type, adding a trivial complex does not change the stable equivalence class. The set of stable equivalence classes of acyclic complexes forms a group isomorphic to the Whitehead group $K_G(R)$.
