This was proven in the course of proving (8.5). The argument shows that the integral group ring $\mathbb{Z}\{1\} \cong \mathbb{Z}$ has only trivial units $\{\pm 1\}$, and consequently $K_T(\mathbb{Z}) = 0$, yielding $Wh(\{1\}) = 0$.
