A chain map between $(R, G)$-complexes is a simple isomorphism if it is a chain isomorphism and each component map is a simple isomorphism of modules. Two complexes that are simply isomorphic are considered indistinguishable for the purposes of torsion theory. This notion is the algebraic counterpart of the fact that CW-isomorphic complexes have the same simple-homotopy type.
