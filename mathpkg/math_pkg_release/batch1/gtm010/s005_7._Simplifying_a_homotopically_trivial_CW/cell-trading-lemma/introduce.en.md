The cell trading lemma is the fundamental constructive tool for simplifying homotopically trivial CW pairs. When a relative homotopy group vanishes ($\pi_r(K,L)=0$), one can formally deform $K$ into a complex where the $r$-cells are replaced by $(r+1)$-cells. Iterating this process reduces the pair to a simplified form where all non-essential cells have been eliminated.
