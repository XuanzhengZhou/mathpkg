Any homotopically trivial CW pair can be simplified, via formal deformations, to a pair with cells in only two adjacent dimensions. The boundary map between these two dimensions is an isomorphism of $\mathbb{Z}\pi_1$-modules. The matrix of this boundary map, with respect to chosen characteristic maps, is the algebraic invariant that leads to the Whitehead group.
