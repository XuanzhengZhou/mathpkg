If $K_0 = L \cup e_0$ and $K_1 = L \cup e_1$ are CW complexes, where the $e_i$ ($i = 0, 1$) are $n$-cells with characteristic maps $\varphi_i: I^n \rightarrow K_i$ such that $\varphi_0 | \partial I^n$ and $\varphi_1 | \partial I^n$ are homotopic maps of $\partial I^n$ into $L$, then $K_0 \wedge K_1$, rel $L$.
