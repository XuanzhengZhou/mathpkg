Every finite CW complex is simple-homotopy equivalent to a finite simplicial complex of the same dimension. This result by J.H.C. Whitehead shows that, up to simple-homotopy type, there is no loss of generality in restricting attention to simplicial complexes. The proof uses induction on the number of cells and the triangulability of mapping cylinders of simplicial maps.
