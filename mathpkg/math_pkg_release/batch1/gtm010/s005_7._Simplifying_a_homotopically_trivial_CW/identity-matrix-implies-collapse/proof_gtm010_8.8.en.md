Consider the characteristic maps $\varphi_1: (I^{r+1}, I^r, J^r) \rightarrow (K, K_r, e^0)$ and $\psi_1: (I^r, \partial I^r) \rightarrow (K_r, e^0)$. By hypothesis, $[\psi_1] = \partial[\varphi_1] \equiv [\varphi_1|I^r] \in \pi_r(K_r, L; e^0)$. Thus there is a homotopy between $\varphi_1|I^r$ and $\psi_1$, which can be used to construct an elementary collapse. Iterating over all pairs $(\varphi_i, \psi_i)$ yields $K \wedge L$.
