Suppose that $(K, L)$ is a pair of connected CW complexes such that $K \supset L$. Let $n = \dim(K-L)$ and let $r \geq n-1$ be an integer. Let $e^0$ be a 0-cell of $L$. Then $K \wedge M$ rel $L$, where $M$ is a CW complex of the form
$$M = L \cup \bigcup_{q=1}^{a} e_q^r \cup \bigcup_{q=1}^{b} e_q^{r+1} \cup \text{(cells of dimension } > r+1\text{)}$$
and where each $e_q^r$ is trivially attached at $e^0$.
