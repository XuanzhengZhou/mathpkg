The torsion of a CW pair satisfies an additivity property with respect to intermediate subcomplexes. This sum formula is the algebraic counterpart of the geometric fact that composing two formal deformations adds their torsions. The proof uses the short exact sequence of $Wh(G)$-complexes induced by the triple $(K, L, M)$ and the additivity property (17.2) of the algebraic torsion for short exact sequences of acyclic complexes.
