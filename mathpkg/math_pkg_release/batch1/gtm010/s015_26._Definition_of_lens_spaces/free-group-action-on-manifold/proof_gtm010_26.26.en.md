We must define a p.l. atlas on M/G. For each x in M, choose a coordinate chart (U, h) about x such that U is small enough that g(U) intersect U = empty for all 1 != g in G. (This is possible because G is finite and acts freely.) Then pi|U is a homeomorphism onto pi(U). Define a chart (pi(U), h pi^{-1}). The transition functions are p.l. because g is p.l. and pi_j^{-1} pi_i agrees with some element of G on components of pi_i^{-1} [pi(U_i) cap pi(U_j)].
