For p > 2, let S^{2n-1} be the unit sphere in R^{2n} = C^n. Define g_bar(z_1, ..., z_n) = (e^{2pi i q_1/p} z_1, ..., e^{2pi i q_n/p} z_n). Then G_bar is a group of orthogonal transformations acting freely. The radial projection T induces a commutative diagram connecting Sigma^{2n-1}/G to S^{2n-1}/G_bar, and T is a p.l. homeomorphism at the quotient level.
