A piecewise linear map between p.l. manifolds is one that is locally p.l. when expressed in coordinate charts. Specifically, for every point $x \in M_1$, the map $f$ can be represented as $g f h^{-1}$ using appropriate charts, and this local representative is required to be piecewise linear. This definition makes the p.l. category a well-defined setting in which to study manifolds and their maps combinatorially.
