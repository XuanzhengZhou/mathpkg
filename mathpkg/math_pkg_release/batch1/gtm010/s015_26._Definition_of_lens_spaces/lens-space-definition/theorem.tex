Suppose that $p \geq 2$ is an integer and that $q_1, q_2, \ldots, q_n$ are integers relatively prime to $p$. The lens space $L(p; q_1, q_2, \ldots, q_n)$ is a $(2n-1)$-dimensional p.l. manifold defined as $\Sigma^{2n-1}/G$ where:
$$\Sigma^{2n-1} = \Sigma_1 * \Sigma_2 * \cdots * \Sigma_n$$
is the iterated join of regular $p$-gons $\Sigma_j$ (simplicial 1-spheres), and $G = \{1, g, g^2, \ldots, g^{p-1}\}$ is the cyclic group generated by the simplicial isomorphism $g$ which rotates $\Sigma_j$ through $2\pi q_j/p$ radians and extends linearly over the join.
