For a finite CW complex $L$, let $S_K = \{\tau(f) \in Wh(L) \mid f: K \to L \text{ is a homotopy equivalence}\}$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] The sets $S_K$ partition $Wh(L)$ as $K$ ranges over representatives of simple-homotopy types of complexes homotopy equivalent to $L$.
\item[(b)] $S_K \cap S_{K'} \neq \emptyset$ iff $K$ and $K'$ have the same simple-homotopy type.
\item[(c)] $S_K = S_{K'}$ iff $K$ and $K'$ have the same simple-homotopy type.
\end{enumerate}
