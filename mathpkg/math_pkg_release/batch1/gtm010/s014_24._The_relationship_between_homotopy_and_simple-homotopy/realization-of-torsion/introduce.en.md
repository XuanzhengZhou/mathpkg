Every element of the Whitehead group can be realized as the torsion of some homotopy equivalence. This shows that Conjecture I of Section 4 (every homotopy equivalence is simple) is definitely false: whenever Wh(L) is non-zero, there exist homotopy equivalences with non-zero torsion.
