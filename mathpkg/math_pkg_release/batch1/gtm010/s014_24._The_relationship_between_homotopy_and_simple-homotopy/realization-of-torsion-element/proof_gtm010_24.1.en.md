Let $K$ be a CW complex such that $K \supset L$ and $\tau(K, L) = -\tau_0$. Such a complex exists by the definition of $Wh(L)$. Let $f: K \to L$ be a homotopy inverse to the inclusion map $i: L \to K$. Then by (22.3)-(22.5):
$$\tau(f) = -f_* \tau(i) = -f_* i_* \tau(K, L) = -\tau(K, L) = \tau_0.$$
