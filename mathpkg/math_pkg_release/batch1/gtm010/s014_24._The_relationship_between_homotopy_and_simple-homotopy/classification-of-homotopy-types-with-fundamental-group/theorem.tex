For a fixed finite CW complex $L$, let $\mathcal{F}$ be the set of simple-homotopy types of finite CW complexes homotopy equivalent to $L$. Let $\mathcal{E}(L)$ be the group of homotopy classes of self-homotopy equivalences of $L$. Then there is a bijection between $\mathcal{F}$ and the set of orbits of $Wh(L)$ under the action of $\mathcal{E}(L)$ given by $f \cdot \alpha = \tau(f) + f_*(\alpha)$.
