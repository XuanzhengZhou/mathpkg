Define a map from ST(L) = {tau(f): f is a homotopy equivalence K -> L} to the orbit set. By (24.2), the sets S_K = {tau(f): f: K -> L a homotopy equivalence} are equivalence classes. (24.1) shows surjectivity. The orbit structure follows from the composition formula.
