Let $\nu_L$ be the number of distinct simple-homotopy types of finite CW complexes homotopy equivalent to $L$, let $\mathcal{E}(L)$ be the group of self-homotopy equivalences of $L$ modulo homotopy, and let $Wh_0(L) = \{\tau(f) \in Wh(L) \mid f_* = \text{id on } Wh(L)\}$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] There exist complexes whose simple-homotopy type is determined by their torsion set.
\item[(2)] $\nu_L = [Wh(L) : Wh_0(L)]$, where the right side is the index of the subgroup.
\item[(3)] There exist lens spaces $L$ with $\nu_L > 1$ and with infinitely many simple-homotopy types within their homotopy type.
\end{enumerate}
