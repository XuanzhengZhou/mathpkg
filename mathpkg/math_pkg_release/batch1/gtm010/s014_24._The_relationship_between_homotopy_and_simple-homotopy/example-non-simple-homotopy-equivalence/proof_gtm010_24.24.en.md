By (11.4), Wh(Z_p) is infinite for p != 1,2,3,4,6. Lens spaces L(p; q_1,...,q_n) have pi_1 = Z_p. The self-homotopy equivalence group E(L) is finite (in 1-1 correspondence with {a: 0 < a < p, a^n = +/-1 mod p}). Thus the orbit set Wh(L)/E(L) is infinite, giving infinitely many simple-homotopy types homotopy equivalent to L.
