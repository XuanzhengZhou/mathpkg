Suppose that $R$ is a commutative ring and $G$ is a subgroup of the group $U$ of all units of $R$. Let $SK_1(R) = \tau_G(SL(R))$ where $\tau_G: GL(R) \rightarrow K_G(R)$ and $SL(R)$ is the subgroup of $GL(R)$ of matrices of determinant 1. Then there is a split short exact sequence
$$0 \rightarrow SK_1(R) \rightarrow K_G(R) \rightarrow U/G \rightarrow 0.$$
