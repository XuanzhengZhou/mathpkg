Let $f: M_1 \to M_2$ be a module homomorphism between free $R$-modules with ordered bases $x = \{x_1, \dots, x_p\}$ and $y = \{y_1, \dots, y_q\}$. The matrix of $f$ is denoted $\langle f \rangle_{x,y}$ or simply $\langle f \rangle$, with entries given by $f(x_i) = \sum_j a_{ij} y_j$.

For composition: $\langle f_2 \circ f_1 \rangle = \langle f_1 \rangle \langle f_2 \rangle$.

For change of basis: if $x, y, z$ are bases of $M$, then $\langle x/z \rangle = \langle x/y \rangle \langle y/z \rangle$.

For basis change of homomorphisms: $\langle f \rangle_{x',y'} = \langle x'/x \rangle \langle f \rangle_{x,y} \langle y/y' \rangle$.
