The elementary subgroup $E(R)$ is normal in $GL(R)$, and in fact equals the commutator subgroup. This makes $K_1(R) = GL(R)/E(R)$ an abelian group. The fact that elementary operations generate exactly the commutator subgroup is the algebraic foundation of Whitehead torsion theory.
