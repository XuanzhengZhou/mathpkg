(A) g = 1 + delta d is clearly an isomorphism since delta d maps delta_bar B_{i-1} isomorphically onto delta B_{i-1}. Using stable freeness to add free modules, g becomes a matrix that is clearly a simple isomorphism.
(B) Follows from (A) because tau(g) = 0 implies equality of the torsions.
