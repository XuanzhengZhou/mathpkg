Let $S = \{j \in \mathbb{Z} \mid 0 < j < p, (j, p) = 1\}$. Suppose that $\{a_j\}_{j \in S}$ is a sequence of integers satisfying:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $\sum_{j \in S} a_j = 0$
\item[(2)] $a_j = a_{p-j}$ for all $j \in S$
\item[(3)] $\prod_{j \in S} (\xi^j - 1)^{a_j} = 1$ for every $p$-th root of unity $\xi \neq 1$.
\end{enumerate}
Then $a_j = 0$ for all $j \in S$.
