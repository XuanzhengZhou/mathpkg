The Franz theorem is a linear independence result for the numbers $\xi^j - 1$ (where $\xi$ is a primitive $p$-th root of unity) in the multiplicative group of the cyclotomic field. It states that the only integer relation among these numbers (with symmetric coefficients and zero sum) is the trivial one. This theorem is the crucial number-theoretic input that allows one to extract individual lens space parameters from the Whitehead torsion equality. Without it, one could only conclude that products of parameters match, giving only the homotopy classification rather than the finer simple-homotopy classification.
