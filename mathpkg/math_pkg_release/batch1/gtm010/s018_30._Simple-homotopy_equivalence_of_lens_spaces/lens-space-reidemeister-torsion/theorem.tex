For the lens space $L = L(p; q_1, \ldots, q_n)$, the Reidemeister torsion (equivalently, the Whitehead torsion $\tau(L, *)$ where $*$ is a point) is
$$\tau(L) = \prod_{j=1}^n (t^{q_j} - 1) \in Wh(\mathbb{Z}_p)$$
where $t$ is a generator of $\mathbb{Z}_p$, viewed as an element of the Whitehead group via the identification $Wh(\mathbb{Z}_p) \cong \mathbb{Z}(\mathbb{Z}_p)^\times / \{\pm t^k\}$.
