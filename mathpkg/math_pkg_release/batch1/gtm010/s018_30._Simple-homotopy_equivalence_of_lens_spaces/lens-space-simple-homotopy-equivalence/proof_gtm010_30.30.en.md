The torsion of a lens space is computed from the chain complex of its universal cover. The boundary maps are multiplication by (t^{q_j} - 1). The torsion simplifies to the product formula given. Two lens spaces are simple-homotopy equivalent exactly when their Reidemeister torsions agree up to a trivial unit and a square.
