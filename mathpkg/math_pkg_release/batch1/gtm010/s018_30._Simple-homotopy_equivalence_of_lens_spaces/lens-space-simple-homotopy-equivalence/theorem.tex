For lens spaces $L = L(p; q_1, \ldots, q_n)$ and $L' = L(p; q'_1, \ldots, q'_n)$, a homotopy equivalence $f: L \rightarrow L'$ is a simple-homotopy equivalence if and only if
$$\prod_{j=1}^n (\xi^{q_j} - 1) \equiv \pm \xi^m \prod_{j=1}^n (\xi^{q'_j} - 1) \cdot (\text{square}) \pmod{\text{units}}$$
in $\mathbb{Q}(\xi)/\mathbb{Q}$ where $\xi = e^{2\pi i/p}$. Equivalently, the Reidemeister torsion of $L$ and $L'$ must be equal.
