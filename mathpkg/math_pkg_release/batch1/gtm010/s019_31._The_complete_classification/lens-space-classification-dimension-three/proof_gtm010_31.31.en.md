The homotopy classification follows from (29) with n=2. The homeomorphism classification was proved by Reidemeister [1935] and uses the fact that the fundamental group determines the Reidemeister torsion, which must be preserved by homeomorphism. For 3-manifolds, the classification is complete and was one of the first triumphs of algebraic topology.
