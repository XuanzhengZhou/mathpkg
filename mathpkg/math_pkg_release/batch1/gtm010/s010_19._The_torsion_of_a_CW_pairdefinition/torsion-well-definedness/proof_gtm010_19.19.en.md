The proof shows that different choices of characteristic maps and lifts change the preferred basis of C(K_tilde, L_tilde) by a matrix with zero torsion in Wh(G). The change-of-basepoint is handled using the functoriality of Wh and the natural isomorphisms induced by path isomorphisms between fundamental groups.
