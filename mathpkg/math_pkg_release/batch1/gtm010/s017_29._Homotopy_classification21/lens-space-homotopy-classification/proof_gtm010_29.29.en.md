The proof uses obstruction theory. A map f: L -> L' between lens spaces lifts to an equivariant map between universal covers S^{2n-1} -> S^{2n-1}. The degree of this lift determines the induced map on top homology. Equivariance forces the relation between the q_j and q'_j given in the theorem. The sign +/- corresponds to the possibility of orientation reversal.
