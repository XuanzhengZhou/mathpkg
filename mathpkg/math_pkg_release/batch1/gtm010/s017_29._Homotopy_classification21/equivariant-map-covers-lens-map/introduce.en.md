There is a bijective correspondence between maps $f: L \to L'$ of lens spaces (with specified behavior on fundamental groups) and equivariant maps between their universal covers. Moreover, homotopies of lens space maps correspond precisely to equivariant homotopies. This correspondence reduces the homotopy classification of lens spaces to the study of equivariant maps between spheres, which can be analyzed using degree theory and the algebraic constraints imposed by equivariance.
