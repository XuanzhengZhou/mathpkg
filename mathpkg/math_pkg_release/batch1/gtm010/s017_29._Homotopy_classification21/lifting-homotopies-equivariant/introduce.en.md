Homotopies between maps of lens spaces can be lifted to equivariant homotopies between their covering maps. The proof uses a cellular induction: one constructs the lifted homotopy skeleton by skeleton, extending equivariantly at each step using the fact that the sphere $\Sigma^{2n-1}$ is $(2n-1)$-connected. This result is essential for establishing that the set of equivariant homotopy classes of equivariant maps is in bijection with the set of homotopy classes of maps between lens spaces with prescribed behavior on $\pi_1$.
