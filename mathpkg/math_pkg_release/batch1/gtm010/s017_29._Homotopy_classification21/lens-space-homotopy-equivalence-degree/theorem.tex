If $f: L(p; q_1, \ldots, q_n) \rightarrow L(p; q'_1, \ldots, q'_n)$ is a homotopy equivalence, then the degree of any lift $\tilde{f}: S^{2n-1} \rightarrow S^{2n-1}$ to the universal covers satisfies $\deg \tilde{f} \equiv \pm k \pmod{p}$ where $k$ is the integer from the homotopy classification theorem.
