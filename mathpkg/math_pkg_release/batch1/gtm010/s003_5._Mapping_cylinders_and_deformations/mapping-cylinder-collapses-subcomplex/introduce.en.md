When one shrinks the domain of a cellular map, the mapping cylinder collapses accordingly. Specifically, if $K_0$ is a subcomplex of $K$, then $M_f$ collapses to $M_{f|K_0}$. This is a key technical lemma linking mapping cylinders to formal deformations.
