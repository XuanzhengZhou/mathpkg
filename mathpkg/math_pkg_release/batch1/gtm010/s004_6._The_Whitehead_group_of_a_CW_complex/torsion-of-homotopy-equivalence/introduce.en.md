The torsion of a cellular homotopy equivalence $f$ is an element of the Whitehead group of the target space. It is defined via the class of the double mapping cylinder and measures the obstruction to $f$ being a simple-homotopy equivalence. A homotopy equivalence is simple if and only if its torsion vanishes.
