Let $[K, L] \in \mathrm{Wh}(L)$ and let $D: K \to L$ be a cellular strong deformation retraction. Let $2M_D$ consist of two copies of the mapping cylinder $M_D$, identified by the identity on $K$. Precisely,
$$2M_D = K \times [-1, 1]$$
with the identifications $(x, -1) = (D(x), -1)$ and $(x, 1) = D(x)$ for all $x \in K$. Then
$$[2M_D, L] = -[K, L].$$
