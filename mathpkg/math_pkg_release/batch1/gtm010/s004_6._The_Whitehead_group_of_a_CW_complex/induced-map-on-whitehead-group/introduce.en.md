A cellular map $f: L_1 	o L_2$ induces a homomorphism $f_*: Wh(L_1) 	o Wh(L_2)$ between Whitehead groups. The two equivalent definitions --- via attaching along $f$ or via the mapping cylinder --- correspond to geometric and algebraic perspectives on the same construction.
