The Whitehead group $Wh(L)$ is an abelian group whose elements are equivalence classes of homotopically trivial CW pairs over $L$. Addition is defined by taking the union along $L$, and the inverse is constructed using a double mapping cylinder. The construction transforms geometric operations on CW pairs into an algebraic group structure.
