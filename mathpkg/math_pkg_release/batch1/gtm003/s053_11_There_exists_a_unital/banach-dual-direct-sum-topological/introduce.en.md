When the dual $W$ of a Banach space $V$ decomposes as a direct algebraic sum of finitely many weak*-closed subspaces $M_j$, the decomposition is automatically topological: each coordinate projection $\pi_i$ is weak*-continuous. This result links algebraic and topological direct sum decompositions in the dual of a Banach space, with applications to the structure theory of dual spaces.
