Let $V$ denote a Banach space with dual $W$. If $W$ is the direct algebraic sum of $n$ ($n \in \mathbb{N}$) $\sigma(W, V)$-closed subspaces $M_j$ ($j = 1, \ldots, n$), then the sum is topological; i.e., each of the projections $\pi_i : W \to M_i$ with kernel $N_i = \sum_{j \neq i} M_j$ is $\sigma(W, V)$-continuous.
