\textit{Proof sketch (Exercise).} The result follows from (6.9), Corollary~2. One identifies $L^\infty(X, \mu; \mathcal{L}(H))$ as the dual of the Banach space $L^1(X, \mu; \mathcal{L}(H)_*)$, where $\mathcal{L}(H)_*$ is the predual of $\mathcal{L}(H)$ (the trace-class operators). The general case for $W^*$-algebras with separable predual is proved in Takesaki~\cite{takesaki1979}, Chapter~IV, Theorem~7.17 and Ionescu-Tulcea~\cite{ionescu-tulcea1969}, Chapter~VI.
