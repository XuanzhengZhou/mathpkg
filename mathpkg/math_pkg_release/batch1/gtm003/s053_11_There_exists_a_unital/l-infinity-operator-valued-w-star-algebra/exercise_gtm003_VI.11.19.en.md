Let $H$ be a separable Hilbert space and $(X, \Sigma, \mu)$ a $\sigma$-finite measure space. Then $L^\infty(X, \Sigma, \mu; \mathcal{L}(H))$ is a $W^*$-algebra (cf.\ (6.9), Corollary~2). The result continues to hold if $\mathcal{L}(H)$ is replaced by any $W^*$-algebra with separable predual (cf.\ Takesaki~\cite{takesaki1979}, Chapter~IV, Theorem~7.17; Ionescu-Tulcea~\cite{ionescu-tulcea1969}, Chapter~VI).
