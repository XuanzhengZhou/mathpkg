For a unital $C^*$-algebra $A$, the GNS representation $(\pi_\phi, H_\phi)$ associated to a state $\phi$ is irreducible precisely when $\phi$ is an extreme point of the convex state space $\mathcal{S}(A)$. This deep result connects the algebraic notion of irreducibility (no invariant subspaces) with the geometric notion of extremality in the state space, and is a cornerstone of the representation theory of $C^*$-algebras.
