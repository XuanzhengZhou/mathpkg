\textit{Proof sketch (Exercise).} Part (a): If $E$ has a weak order unit $u$, the principal lattice ideal $E_u$ is an $AM$-space with unit, hence isometrically order-isomorphic to $C(K)$ by (V.8.5). Since $E_u$ is dense in $E$, the norm on $E$ defines a positive Radon measure $\mu$ on $K$, and one shows $E \cong L^1(K, \mu)$. Cf.\ Chapter~V, Exercise~22.

Part (b): Using a maximal orthogonal family $(u_\lambda)$, decompose $E$ into the $\ell^1$-direct sum of the subspaces $\overline{E_{u_\lambda}}$, each of which has a weak order unit and thus, by (a), is isomorphic to some $L^1(K_\lambda, \mu_\lambda)$.
