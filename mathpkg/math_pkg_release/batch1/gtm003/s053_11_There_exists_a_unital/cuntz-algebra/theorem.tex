There exists a unital $C^*$-algebra $O(n)$ generated by $n$ ($n \in \mathbb{N}$) elements $v_j$ that satisfy these relations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $v_j^* v_j = e$ ($j = 1, \ldots, n$);
\item[(ii)] $\sum_{j=1}^{n} v_j v_j^* = e$.
\end{enumerate}
The algebra $O(n)$ is unique (to within isomorphism) and called the \text{Cuntz algebra}.
