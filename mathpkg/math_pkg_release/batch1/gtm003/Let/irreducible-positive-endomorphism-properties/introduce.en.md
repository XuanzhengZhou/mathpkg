For an irreducible positive endomorphism whose spectral radius $r$ is a pole of the resolvent: $r > 0$, the pole order is 1, there exist positive eigenvectors for both $u$ and $u'$, positive eigenvectors for $r$ are quasi-interior to $C$, and under mild conditions (finite multiplicity, interior of $C$, or Banach lattice) the multiplicity is 1. This is a deep generalization of Perron-Frobenius.
