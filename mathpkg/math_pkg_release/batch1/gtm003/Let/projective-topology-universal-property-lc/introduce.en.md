With the projective topology on $E \otimes F$, the universal property extends to the continuous setting: there is a natural isomorphism between $\mathcal{L}(E \otimes F, G)$ and $\mathcal{B}(E, F; G)$. This means continuous bilinear maps on $E \times F$ correspond bijectively to continuous linear maps on the projective tensor product.
