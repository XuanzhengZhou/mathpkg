The family $\mathfrak{C}$ of all convex, circled, $\sigma(G, F)$-compact sets in $G$ is contained in the family $\mathfrak{S}$ of (III, 3.4) with $F = K_0$, since every $C \in \mathfrak{C}$ is $\sigma(G, F)$-complete. Thus the first assertion follows from (III, 3.4); the second is immediate.
