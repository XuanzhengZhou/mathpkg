The quotient topology on $E/M$ is an example of an inductive topology, namely the inductive topology with respect to the canonical quotient map. It is Hausdorff precisely when $M$ is closed. The quotient topology can be generated by a family of semi-norms derived from generating families of semi-norms on $E$.
