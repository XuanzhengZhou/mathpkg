The algebraic adjoint $u^*$ of a linear map $u: F \to F_1$ is the transpose map from $F_1^*$ to $F^*$ defined by $\langle ux, y^* \rangle = \langle x, u^*y^* \rangle$. When the spaces carry weak topologies, $u$ is weakly continuous if and only if $u^*(G_1) \subset G$ for dualities $\langle F, G \rangle$ and $\langle F_1, G_1 \rangle$.
