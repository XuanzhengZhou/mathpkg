Let $E, F$ be ordered locally convex spaces with respective positive cones $C, D$. If $C$ is weakly normal in $E$ and $F = D - D$, then $\mathcal{H} - \mathcal{H}$ is dense in $\mathcal{L}_s(E, F)$, where $\mathcal{H} = \{u \in \mathcal{L}(E, F): u(C) \subset D\}$ is the cone of positive linear maps and $\mathcal{L}_s(E, F)$ denotes $\mathcal{L}(E, F)$ under the topology of simple convergence.
