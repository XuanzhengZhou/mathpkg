This proposition identifies the product topology as a projective topology with respect to the family of canonical projections. This is a fundamental observation: the product of locally convex spaces is again locally convex, and the product topology coincides with the coarsest topology making all projections continuous. This perspective allows the product construction to be subsumed under the general theory of projective limits.
