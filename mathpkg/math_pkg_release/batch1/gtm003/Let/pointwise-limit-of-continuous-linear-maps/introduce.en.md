If $E$ is barreled (or a Baire space) and a sequence of continuous linear maps converges pointwise to some map $u$, then $u$ is automatically continuous and the convergence is uniform on precompact sets. This is a direct consequence of the Banach-Steinhaus theorem combined with (4.5).
