Let $\{E_\alpha: \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ be a family of locally convex spaces. Let $\mathcal{H}$ be the family of all non-empty finite subsets of $\mathbf{A}$, ordered by inclusion. For $\mathbf{H} \in \mathcal{H}$, set $E_{\mathbf{H}} = \prod_{\alpha \in \mathbf{H}} E_\alpha$, and for $\mathbf{H} \subset \Lambda$, let $g_{\mathbf{H}, \Lambda}$ denote the canonical projection of $E_\Lambda$ onto $E_{\mathbf{H}}$. Then
$$\prod_{\alpha \in \mathbf{A}} E_\alpha = \varprojlim g_{\mathbf{H}, \Lambda} E_\Lambda.$$
