In the locally convex direct sum of a family of ordered spaces, the direct sum cone is normal exactly when each component cone is normal. This follows from the characterization of normal cones via $C$-saturated 0-neighborhood bases and the fact that the saturated hull of a product neighborhood is the convex hull of the union of the component saturated hulls.
