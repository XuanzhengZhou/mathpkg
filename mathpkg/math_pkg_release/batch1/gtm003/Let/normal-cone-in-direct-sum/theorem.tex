If $\{L_\alpha : \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ is a family of l.c.s., $C_\alpha$ a cone in $L_\alpha$ $(\alpha \in \mathbf{A})$, and $L = \bigoplus_\alpha L_\alpha$ the locally convex direct sum, then $C = \bigoplus_\alpha C_\alpha$ is a normal cone in $L$ if and only if $C_\alpha$ is normal in $L_\alpha$ $(\alpha \in \mathbf{A})$.
