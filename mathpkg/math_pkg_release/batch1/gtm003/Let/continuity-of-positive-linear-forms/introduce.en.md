Several conditions guarantee automatic continuity of all positive linear forms: (i) the positive cone has interior points; (ii) the space is a complete metrizable t.v.s. where $E = C - C$; (iii) the space is bornological with $C$ a semi-complete strict $\mathfrak{B}$-cone. These results are fundamental for the theory of positive operators.
