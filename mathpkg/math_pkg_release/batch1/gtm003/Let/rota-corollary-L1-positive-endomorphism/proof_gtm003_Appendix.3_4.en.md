The linear form $h: f \mapsto \int f d\mu$ is strictly positive on $L^1(\mu)$. When $\|u\| = r(u)$ and $f \geq 0$, $\langle u(f), h \rangle = \int u(f) d\mu \leq \|u\| \int f d\mu = r(u) \langle f, h \rangle$, so $u'(h) \leq r(u)h$. The result follows from (3.4).
