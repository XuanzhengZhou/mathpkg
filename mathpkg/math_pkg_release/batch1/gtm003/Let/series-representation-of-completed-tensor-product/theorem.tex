Let $E, F$ be metrizable l.c.s. Every element $u$ of the completion $E \tilde{\otimes} F$ (under the projective topology) can be represented as $$u = \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \lambda_i x_i \otimes y_i$$ where $\{x_i\}$ and $\{y_i\}$ are null sequences in $E$ and $F$ respectively, and $\sum |\lambda_i| < \infty$.
