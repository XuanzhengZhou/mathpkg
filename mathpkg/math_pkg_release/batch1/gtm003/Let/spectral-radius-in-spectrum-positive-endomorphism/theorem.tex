Let $E$ be an ordered complex Banach space, not reduced to $\{0\}$, with positive cone $C$ such that $C$ is normal and $E = C - C$. For any positive (necessarily continuous) endomorphism $u$ of $E$, the spectral radius $r(u)$ is an element of $\sigma(u)$. If $r(u)$ is a pole of the resolvent, it is of maximal order on the spectral circle of $u$.
