For a positive endomorphism $u$ on an ordered Banach space with normal generating cone, the spectral radius $r(u)$ always belongs to the spectrum $\sigma(u)$. This is a consequence of the Pringsheim theorem. If $r(u)$ is a pole of the resolvent, it has maximal order on the spectral circle.
