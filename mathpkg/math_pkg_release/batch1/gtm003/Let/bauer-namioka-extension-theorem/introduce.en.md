The Bauer-Namioka theorem gives a necessary and sufficient condition for extending a positive linear form from a subspace to the whole space while preserving continuity and positivity. The condition involves boundedness of $\text{Re} f_0$ on the intersection of $M$ with $U - C$ for some convex 0-neighborhood $U$.
