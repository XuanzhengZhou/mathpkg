Let $E$ be an ordered t.v.s. with positive cone $C$ and let $M$ be a subspace of $E$. For a linear form $f_0$ on $M$ to have an extension $f$ to $E$ which is a continuous positive linear form, it is necessary and sufficient that $\text{Re} f_0$ be bounded above on $M \cap (U - C)$, where $U$ is a suitable convex 0-neighborhood in $E$.
