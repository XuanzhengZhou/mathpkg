A cone $C$ in a topological vector space is normal if the $C$-saturated hulls of 0-neighborhoods form a 0-neighborhood base. This means the family $\{[U]: U \in \mathfrak{U}\}$ is a fundamental system of neighborhoods. Normality of the positive cone is an essential condition for ordered t.v.s. theory.
