A regularly ordered vector space is one where the positive cone $C$ is closed in some Hausdorff locally convex topology, and the order is Archimedean. Equivalently, $C$ is normal for some Hausdorff l.c. topology. Regular order is preserved under subspaces, products, and direct sums. Every ordered locally convex space is regularly ordered.
