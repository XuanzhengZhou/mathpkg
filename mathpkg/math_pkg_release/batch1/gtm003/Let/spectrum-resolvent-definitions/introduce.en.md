For a continuous endomorphism $u$ on a complex Banach space, the spectrum $\sigma(u)$ is a non-empty compact set. The resolvent $R(\lambda) = (\lambda e - u)^{-1}$ is holomorphic on the resolvent set $\rho(u) = C \setminus \sigma(u)$. The spectral radius satisfies $r(u) = \lim \|u^n\|^{1/n}$. The spectrum can be characterized as those $\lambda$ for which $\lambda e - u$ fails to be an automorphism.
