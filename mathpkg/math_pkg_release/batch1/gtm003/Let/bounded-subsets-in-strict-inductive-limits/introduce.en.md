In a strict inductive limit of a sequence of locally convex spaces (with each $E_n$ closed in $E_{n+1}$), a subset is bounded if and only if it is contained in some $E_n$ and bounded there. This provides a complete characterization of bounded sets in (LB) and (LF) spaces.
