Let $\tilde{\mathfrak{S}}$ and $\tilde{\mathfrak{S}}_1$ be the families of all weakly closed, convex, circled sets contained in $\mathfrak{S}$ and $\mathfrak{S}_1$. These are fundamental subfamilies. The condition $u(\mathfrak{S}) \subset \mathfrak{S}_1$ implies $u(\tilde{\mathfrak{S}}) \subset \tilde{\mathfrak{S}}_1$, and the polar relationship gives $(u')^{-1}(\tilde{S}^{\circ}) = (u(\tilde{S}))^{\circ}$, establishing the claimed continuity.
