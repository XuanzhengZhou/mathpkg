The subset of $\sigma(u)$ in which $(\lambda e - u)$ fails to be one-to-one is called the point spectrum $\pi(u)$ of $u$. An element $\lambda_0 \in \pi(u)$ is an eigenvalue of $u$, the null space of $(\lambda_0 e - u)$ the corresponding eigenspace $N(\lambda_0)$. The dimension of $N(\lambda_0)$ is the (geometric) multiplicity of $\lambda_0$, and the non-zero elements of $N(\lambda_0)$ are eigenvectors of $u$ for $\lambda_0$.
