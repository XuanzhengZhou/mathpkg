The Banach-Steinhaus theorem (uniform boundedness principle) states that for a barreled domain $E$ (or a Baire t.v.s. domain), every pointwise bounded set of continuous linear maps is automatically equicontinuous and hence bounded for all $\mathfrak{S}$-topologies. This is one of the cornerstones of functional analysis.
