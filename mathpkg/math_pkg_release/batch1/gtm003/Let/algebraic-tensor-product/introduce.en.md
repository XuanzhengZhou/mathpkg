The algebraic tensor product $E \otimes F$ of two vector spaces is defined as the linear hull of the canonical bilinear map $\chi(x,y) = x \otimes y$ into the dual of the space of bilinear forms. Every element is a finite sum $\sum \lambda_i(x_i \otimes y_i)$. The tensor product satisfies $\lambda(x \otimes y) = (\lambda x) \otimes y = x \otimes (\lambda y)$, distributivity, and the universal property that bilinear maps factor uniquely through $\chi$.
