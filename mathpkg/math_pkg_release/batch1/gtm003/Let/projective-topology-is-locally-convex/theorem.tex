Let $E$ and $E_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$) be vector spaces over $K$, let $f_\alpha$ be a linear map on $E$ into $E_\alpha$, and let $\mathfrak{T}_\alpha$ be a locally convex topology on $E_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$). Then the projective topology $\mathfrak{T}$ on $E$ with respect to the family $\{(E_\alpha, \mathfrak{T}_\alpha, f_\alpha): \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ is a locally convex topology on $E$.
