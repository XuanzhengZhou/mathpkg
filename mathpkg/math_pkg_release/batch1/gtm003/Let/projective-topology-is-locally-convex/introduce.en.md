This proposition states that the class of locally convex topologies is closed under the formation of projective topologies. Since the $f_\alpha$ are linear and each $\mathfrak{T}_\alpha$ is locally convex, the resulting projective topology $\mathfrak{T}$ inherits translation invariance and admits a base of convex $0$-neighborhoods satisfying the defining conditions of a locally convex topology. This is essential because it shows the category of locally convex spaces admits all projective limits.
