The projective tensor product topology $\mathfrak{T}_p$ on $E \otimes F$ is the finest locally convex topology making the canonical bilinear map $\chi: E \times F \to E \otimes F$ continuous. A 0-neighborhood base is given by the convex circled hulls $\Gamma(U \otimes V)$ where $U, V$ run through 0-neighborhood bases of $E, F$. The projective topology is always Hausdorff.
