For equicontinuous subsets of $\mathcal{L}(E, F)$, the topology of pointwise convergence coincides with the topology of uniform convergence on precompact (or compact, when $E$ is complete) subsets of $E$. This is a key fact used in the Banach-Steinhaus theorem and Ascoli-type theorems.
