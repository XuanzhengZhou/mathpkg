The vector-valued Pringsheim theorem: a power series with coefficients in a weakly normal positive cone and radius of convergence 1 must be singular at $z = 1$. Moreover, if the singularity is a pole, it has maximal order on the unit circle. This is fundamental for spectral theory of positive operators.
