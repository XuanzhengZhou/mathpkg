In the context of ordered vector spaces, a cone is a convex cone with vertex 0. The notation $[x,y] = (x+C) \cap (y-C)$ generalizes order intervals. The $C$-saturated hull $[A]$ of a set $A$ is the intersection of all $C$-saturated sets containing $A$. The operation $A \mapsto [A]$ is monotone, preserves convexity, and preserves circledness.
