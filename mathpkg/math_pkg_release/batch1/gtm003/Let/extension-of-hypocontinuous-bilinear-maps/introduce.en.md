An $(\mathfrak{S}, \mathfrak{T})$-hypocontinuous bilinear map from a dense product $E \times F$ into a quasi-complete space $G$ extends uniquely to the closures of the bounded sets, yielding a bilinear $(\mathfrak{S}_1, \mathfrak{T}_1)$-hypocontinuous map on $E_1 \times F_1$. This extension theorem is fundamental for the theory of tensor products and spaces of bilinear maps.
