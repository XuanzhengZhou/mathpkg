If $\{E_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ is a family of vector spaces over $K$, the algebraic direct sum $\oplus_\alpha E_\alpha$ is defined to be the subspace of $\prod_\alpha E_\alpha$ for whose elements $x$ all but a finite number of the projections $x_\alpha = p_\alpha(x)$ are 0. Denote by $g_\alpha$ $(\alpha \in A)$ the injection map $E_\alpha \rightarrow \oplus_\beta E_\beta$. The locally convex direct sum of the family $\{E_\alpha(\mathfrak{T}_\alpha) : \alpha \in A\}$ of l.c.s. is defined to be $\oplus_\alpha E_\alpha$ under the inductive topology with respect to the family $\{(E_\alpha, \mathfrak{T}_\alpha, g_\alpha) : \alpha \in A\}$.
