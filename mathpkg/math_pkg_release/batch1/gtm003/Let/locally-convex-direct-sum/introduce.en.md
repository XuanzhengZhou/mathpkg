The locally convex direct sum of a family of locally convex spaces is the algebraic direct sum equipped with the inductive topology with respect to the canonical injections. It is a Hausdorff topology, and a 0-neighborhood base is provided by all sets of the form $U = \prod_\alpha g_\alpha(U_\alpha)$. For a finite family, the locally convex sum coincides with the product.
