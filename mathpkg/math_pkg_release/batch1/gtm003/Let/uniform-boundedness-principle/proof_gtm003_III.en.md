The linear hull $E_M$ of $M$, which is clearly dense in $E$ and a Baire space since $M$ is not meager in $E$, has the property that $H_0$ (restrictions of $u \in H$ to $E_M$) is simply bounded in $\mathcal{L}(E_M, F)$. Hence by (4.2), $H_0$ is equicontinuous and thus norm bounded in $\mathcal{L}(E_M, F)$. Since the unit ball of $E_M$ is dense in the unit ball of $E$, the restriction map $u \mapsto u|_{E_M}$ is a norm isomorphism of $\mathcal{L}(E, F)$ into $\mathcal{L}(E_M, F)$; hence $H$ is norm bounded.
