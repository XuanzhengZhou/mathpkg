Under a dominance condition ($u'(x'_0) \leq r x'_0$ for a strictly positive linear form), the peripheral point spectrum of a positive endomorphism on a Banach lattice with extendable absolute value is cyclic: if $r\alpha$ is a peripheral eigenvalue, then the entire cyclic group generated by $\alpha$ belongs to the peripheral point spectrum. This applies to $L^p$ spaces.
