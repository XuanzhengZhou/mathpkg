The gauge of the convex circled hull $\Gamma(U \otimes V)$ can be expressed using the semi-norms $p$ and $q$. The value $r(u)$ is the infimum over all representations $u = \sum x_i \otimes y_i$ of $\sum p(x_i)q(y_i)$. In particular $r(x \otimes y) = p(x)q(y)$, showing that $r$ is a cross-semi-norm. This is fundamental for computing the projective topology.
