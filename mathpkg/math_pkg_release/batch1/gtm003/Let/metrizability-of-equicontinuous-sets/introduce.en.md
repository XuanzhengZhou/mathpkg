For an equicontinuous set $H$ of linear maps, if the domain $E$ is separable and the codomain $F$ is metrizable, the topology of simple convergence on $H$ is metrizable. With the additional hypothesis that $F$ is separable, $H$ becomes separable. This provides useful metrizability criteria in duality theory.
