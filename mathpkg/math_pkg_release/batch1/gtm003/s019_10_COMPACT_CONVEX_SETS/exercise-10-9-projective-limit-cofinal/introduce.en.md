This exercise shows that the projective (resp. inductive) limit of a family of locally convex spaces depends only on the cofinal part of the index set. If $B$ is a cofinal subset of the directed index set $A$, then the limit over $B$ is isomorphic to the limit over $A$, provided the transition maps satisfy the natural compatibility conditions.
