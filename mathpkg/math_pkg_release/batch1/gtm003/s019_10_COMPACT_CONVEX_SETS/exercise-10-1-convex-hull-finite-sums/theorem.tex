Let $E$ be a vector space and let $A \neq \emptyset$ be a subset of $E$. The convex hull (resp. the convex, circled hull) of $A$ consists of all finite sums $\sum \lambda_i x_i$ with $x_i \in A$, $\lambda_i \geq 0$, $\sum \lambda_i = 1$ (resp. with the additional condition $\sum |\lambda_i| \leq 1$).
