Let $L$ be a vector space over a complete, non-discrete valuated field $K$ (not necessarily $\mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$), and call $(L, p)$ a normed space over $K$ if $p$ satisfies conditions (i) through (iii) of Section 2. Find out to what extent the results of Section 2 can be carried over to this more general case. Show that the norm topology on $L$ is always a locally convex topology.
