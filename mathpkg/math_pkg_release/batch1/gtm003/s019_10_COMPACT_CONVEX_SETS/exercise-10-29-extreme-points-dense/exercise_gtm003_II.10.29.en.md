Let $E$ be the Hilbert space $\ell^2$ over $R$. Denote by $C$ the subset of $E$ determined by $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (2^n x_n)^2 \leq 1$. Then $C$ is a convex and compact set, and the closure of its subset $\mathcal{E}$ of extreme points equals $C$. (Let $E_n$ be the subspace of $E$ determined by $x_k = 0$ for $k > n$; the boundary points of the ellipsoid $C \cap E_n$ are extreme points of $C$.)
