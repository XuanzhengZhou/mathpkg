This lemma states that every unitary element in a von Neumann algebra can be written as the exponential of $i$ times a self-adjoint element from the same algebra. The proof reduces to the commutative $W^*$-algebra generated by $u$, which by the Gelfand representation is isomorphic to an $L^\infty$ space, where the result follows from representing the unimodular function as a pointwise exponential.
