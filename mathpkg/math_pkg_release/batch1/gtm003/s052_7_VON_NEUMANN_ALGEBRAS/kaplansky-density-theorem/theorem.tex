Let $A$ be any non-degenerate $C^*$-subalgebra of $\mathcal{L}(H)$ with double commutant $M := A^{cc}$. With respect to the strong operator topology the following assertions are valid:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $A_{sa}$ is dense in $M_{sa}$.
\item[(b)] $U_A \cap A_{sa}$ is dense in $U_M \cap M_{sa}$.
\item[(c)] $U_A$ is dense in $U_M$.
\item[(d)] If $A$ is unital, the group $G_A$ of all unitary elements of $A$ is dense in the group $G_M$ of all unitary elements of $M$.
\end{itemize}
Here $U$ denotes the closed unit ball of $\mathcal{L}(H)$ and $U_C$ the unit ball of any $C^*$-subalgebra $C$.
