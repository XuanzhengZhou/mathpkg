Let $M$ be a $C^*$-subalgebra of $\mathcal{L}(H)$ containing the unit $e = \mathrm{id}_H$ of $\mathcal{L}(H)$. The following assertions are mutually equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $M = M^{cc}$,
\item[(b)] $M$ is closed in $\mathcal{L}_w(H)$ (weak operator topology),
\item[(c)] $M$ is closed in $\mathcal{L}_s(H)$ (strong operator topology),
\item[(d)] $M$ is closed in $\mathcal{L}_\sigma(H)$ ($\sigma$-weak topology).
\end{itemize}
