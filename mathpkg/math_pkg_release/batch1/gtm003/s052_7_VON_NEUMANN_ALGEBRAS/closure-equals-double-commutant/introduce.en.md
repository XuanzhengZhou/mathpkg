This corollary of the bicommutant theorem states that for any non-degenerate $C^*$-subalgebra of bounded operators on a Hilbert space, the strong operator closure coincides with the algebraic double commutant. The result connects the topological and algebraic perspectives on von Neumann algebras: the strong closure of a non-degenerate $C^*$-algebra is always a $W^*$-algebra (von Neumann algebra).
