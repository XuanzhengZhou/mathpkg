The Murray--von Neumann equivalence relation $\sim$ on projections in a $W^*$-algebra is defined via partial isometries: $p \sim q$ if $p = u^*u$ and $q = uu^*$ for some partial isometry $u$ in the algebra. This leads to a pre-order $\preceq$ on projections. In the case $W = \mathcal{L}(H)$, two projections are equivalent exactly when their ranges have the same Hilbert dimension, so the equivalence classes correspond bijectively to cardinal numbers.
