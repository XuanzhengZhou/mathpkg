Let $A$ be a (complex) B-algebra with involution $x \mapsto x^*$.

(a) If $\|x\|^2 \leq \|x^*x\|$ holds for all $x \in A$, then $A$ is a $C^*$-algebra.

(b) Suppose $A$ is a $C^*$-algebra without unit, and let $\tilde{A}$ be its unitization. Let $\mathcal{L}(A)$ denote the algebra of bounded endomorphisms of the underlying Banach space $A$, and let $L_a \in \mathcal{L}(A)$ be left multiplication $x \mapsto ax$ for $x \in A$. Show that $\tilde{A}$ is isometrically isomorphic to the closed subalgebra of $\mathcal{L}(A)$ generated by $\{L_a : a \in A\} \cup \{\mathrm{id}_A\}$.
