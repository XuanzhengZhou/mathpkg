On $\mathcal{L}(H)$, where $H$ is a Hilbert space $\neq \{0\}$, we consider the following operator topologies in addition to the standard norm topology and the ultra-weak (or $\sigma$-weak) topology $\sigma(\mathcal{L}(H), H \hat{\otimes} H)$:
\begin{itemize}
\item The \text{weak operator topology} $\sigma(\mathcal{L}(H), H \otimes H)$, generated by the semi-norms $u \mapsto |[u\xi, \eta]|$ for $\xi, \eta \in H$.
\item The \text{strong operator topology}, generated by the semi-norms $u \mapsto \|u\xi\|$ for $\xi \in H$.
\end{itemize}
Endowed with one of these topologies, $\mathcal{L}(H)$ is denoted by $\mathcal{L}_w(H)$, $\mathcal{L}_s(H)$, or $\mathcal{L}_\sigma(H)$ respectively.
