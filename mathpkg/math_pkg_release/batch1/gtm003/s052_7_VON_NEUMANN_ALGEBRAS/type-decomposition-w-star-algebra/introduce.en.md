Theorem 8.7 is the fundamental structure theorem for $W^*$-algebras: every von Neumann algebra decomposes uniquely into a direct sum of algebras of types I, II$_1$, II$_\infty$, and III. The proof constructs each summand using maximal families of centrally orthogonal projections with appropriate properties. The decomposition is topological for both the norm and $\sigma$-weak topologies.
