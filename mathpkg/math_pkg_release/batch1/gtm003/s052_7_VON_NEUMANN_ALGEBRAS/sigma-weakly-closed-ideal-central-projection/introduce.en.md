This structure theorem (8.5) characterizes $\sigma$-weakly closed ideals in a $W^*$-algebra: every such ideal is a principal ideal generated by a central projection. That is, $J = Wp$ where $p$ is a projection in the center $Z(W)$. The proof uses the fact that $J$ is itself a $W^*$-subalgebra and hence unital, with its unit element being the required central projection.
