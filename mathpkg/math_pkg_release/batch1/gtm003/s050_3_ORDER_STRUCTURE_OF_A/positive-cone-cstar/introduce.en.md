Proposition 3.1 establishes that the set $A_+$ of elements with non-negative spectrum forms a proper, closed, convex, and normal cone in a C*-algebra. This makes the self-adjoint part $A_{\mathrm{sa}}$ into a real ordered Banach space, providing the foundation for the order-theoretic study of C*-algebras.
