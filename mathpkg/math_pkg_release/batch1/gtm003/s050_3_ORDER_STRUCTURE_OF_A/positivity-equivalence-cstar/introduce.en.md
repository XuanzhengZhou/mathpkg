This lemma provides an alternative characterization of positive elements in a unital C*-algebra: $x \in A_{\mathrm{sa}}$ is positive if and only if $\|te - x\| \leq t$ whenever $t \geq \|x\|$, where $e$ is the unit. This gives a norm-based criterion for positivity that is useful in proofs involving the order structure.
