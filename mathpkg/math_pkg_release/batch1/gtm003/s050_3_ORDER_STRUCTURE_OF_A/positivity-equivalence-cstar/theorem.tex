Let $A$ be unital and $x \in A_{\mathrm{sa}}$. The following are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] There exists $t \in \mathbb{R}_+$ satisfying $\|x\| \leq t$ and $\|te - x\| \leq t$.
\item[(ii)] For all $t \in \mathbb{R}_+$, $\|x\| \leq t$ implies $\|te - x\| \leq t$.
\end{itemize}
These conditions characterize $x \in A_+$.
