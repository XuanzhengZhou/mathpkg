Proposition 3.2 collects fundamental order inequalities in C*-algebras. Key highlights include the monotonicity of the map $x \mapsto c^*xc$, the norm-monotonicity of the modulus, the monotonicity of the square root, and the reversal of order for inverses of positive invertible elements.
