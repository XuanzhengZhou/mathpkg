This exercise explores the duality theory of spaces of continuous linear maps between Banach spaces, with particular attention to nuclear spaces. It covers: (a) the dual of $\mathcal{L}_c(E,F)$ under compact convergence; (b) the characterization of complete nuclear spaces as (M)-spaces; (c) identification of strong duals of tensor products for nuclear spaces; and (d) a result on the equality of certain boundedness notions for nuclear spaces under an (F)-space hypothesis. These results connect nuclearity, Montel properties, and tensor product theory.
