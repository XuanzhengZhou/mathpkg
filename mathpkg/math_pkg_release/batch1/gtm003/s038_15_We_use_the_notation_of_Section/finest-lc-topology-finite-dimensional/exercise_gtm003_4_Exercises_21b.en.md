It is sufficient that $\tau(E, E')$ be the finest l.c. topology on $E$ (cf. Exercise 6). Infer from this that if $E$ is metrizable and $E'_\sigma$ is complete, then $E$ is finite dimensional. (Use Chapter II, Exercise 7.) If $E$ is normable and $E'_\sigma$ complete, then $E$ is finite dimensional. (Apply the preceding result to $E'_\beta$.)
