(Theorem of Banach-Mackey). If $E$ is a l.c.s. and $B$ is a bounded, convex, circled subset of $E$ such that the normed space $E_B$ is complete, then $B$ is bounded for $\beta(E, E')$. (Observe that for each barrel $D \subset E$, $D \cap E_B$ is a barrel in $E_B$, hence a 0-neighborhood since $E_B$ is complete. Therefore $B$ absorbs every barrel, so $B$ is strongly bounded.)
