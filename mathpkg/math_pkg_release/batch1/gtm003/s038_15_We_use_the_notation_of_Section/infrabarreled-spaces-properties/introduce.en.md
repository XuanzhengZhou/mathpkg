Infrabarreled spaces are a class of locally convex spaces that generalize barreled spaces. This proposition establishes the stability of infrabarreled spaces under products and locally convex direct sums, as well as their preservation under separated quotients. However, closed subspaces of infrabarreled spaces need not be infrabarreled, providing a counterexample to the hereditary property.
