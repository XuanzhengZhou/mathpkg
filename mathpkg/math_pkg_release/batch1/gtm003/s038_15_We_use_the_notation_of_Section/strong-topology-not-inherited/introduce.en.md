In the context of a duality $\langle F, G \rangle$, the strong topology $\beta(F, G)$ generally fails to be inherited by closed subspaces or separated quotients. However, for Banach spaces and their strong duals, the strong topology does behave well with respect to subspaces and quotients. This statement highlights a fundamental distinction between general locally convex spaces and Banach spaces.
