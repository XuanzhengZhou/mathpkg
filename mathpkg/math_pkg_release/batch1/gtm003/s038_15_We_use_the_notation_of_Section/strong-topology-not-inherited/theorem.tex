If $\langle F, G \rangle$ is a duality, the strong topology $\beta(F, G)$ is, in general, not inherited by closed subspaces or separated quotients. If $F$ is a Banach space, then the strong topology is inherited by closed subspaces and separated quotients; the same is true (with respect to $\langle F, F' \rangle$) for the strong dual of $F$.
