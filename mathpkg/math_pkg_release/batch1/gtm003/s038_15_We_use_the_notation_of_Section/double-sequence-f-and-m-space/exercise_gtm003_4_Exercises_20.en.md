Let $E$ be the vector space (over $\mathbb{R}$) of all double sequences $x = (x_{ij})$ such that for each $n \in \mathbb{N}$, $p_n(x) = \sum_{i,j} a_{ij}^{(n)} |x_{ij}| < +\infty$, where $a_{ij}^{(n)} = j^n$ for $i < n$ and all $j$, and $a_{ij}^{(n)} = i^n$ for $i \geq n$ and all $j$. Under the topology generated by the semi-norms $p_n$ ($n \in \mathbb{N}$), $E$ is an (F)-space and an (M)-space (cf. Exercise 13). The dual $E'$ has additional properties to be investigated.
