This exercise constructs a specific locally convex space $E$ of double sequences, equipped with a sequence of weighted $\ell^1$-type seminorms $p_n$. The space $E$ is shown to be both an (F)-space (a complete metrizable locally convex space) and an (M)-space (a Montel space). This example illustrates the existence of non-trivial spaces possessing both the Fréchet and Montel properties simultaneously.
