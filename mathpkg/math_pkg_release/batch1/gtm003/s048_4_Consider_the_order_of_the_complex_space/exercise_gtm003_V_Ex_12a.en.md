Let $L_1, L_2$ be vector lattices.

If $N$ is a solid subspace of $L_1$, the canonical image of the positive cone of $L_1$ in $L_1/N$ defines the canonical order of $L_1/N$ under which $L_1/N$ is a vector lattice, and the canonical map $L_1 \to L_1/N$ is a lattice homomorphism. If $L$ is order complete and $N$ is a band in $L_1$, then $L_1/N$ is isomorphic with $N^\perp$.
