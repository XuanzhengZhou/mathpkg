Let $L$ be an ordered vector space over $\mathbb{R}$ with positive cone $C$. If $M$ is a subspace of $L$ such that $M + C = M - C$, then every linear form $f_0$ on $M$, positive for the canonical order of $M$, can be extended to a positive linear form on $L$. (Day [2], §6 Theorem 1.)
