In a Hausdorff topological vector space, the notions of total boundedness and precompactness coincide. A separated uniform space is precompact if its completion is compact, and this characterization carries over to the natural uniform structure of a Hausdorff t.v.s. This equivalence allows one to work interchangeably with the combinatorial finite-cover definition of total boundedness and the topological compactness-of-the-completion definition of precompactness.
