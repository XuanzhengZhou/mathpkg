Let $u$ be a continuous endomorphism of a Banach space $E$ with spectrum $\sigma(u)$ and spectral radius $r(u)$. Let $\pi(u)$ denote the point spectrum of $u$. The subset of $\sigma(u)$ located on the spectral circle $\{\lambda: |\lambda| = r(u)\}$ is called the **peripheral spectrum** of $u$, and its intersection with $\pi(u)$ is called the **peripheral point spectrum** of $u$.
