A prenuclear seminorm is a continuous seminorm dominated by the $L^1$-norm with respect to a positive Radon measure on an equicontinuous subset of the dual. The concept serves as a bridge between the abstract notion of nuclearity and the concrete measure-theoretic characterization: a space is nuclear if and only if every continuous seminorm is prenuclear. Every prenuclear seminorm is automatically continuous, and every prenuclear subset of the dual is necessarily equicontinuous.
