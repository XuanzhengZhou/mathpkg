As a consequence of Theorem 9.9, the strong dual of the space $\mathcal{L}_b(E'_\beta, F)$ of continuous linear maps from the strong dual $E'_\beta$ into $F$, equipped with the topology of bounded convergence, is canonically isomorphic to the completed projective tensor product $E'_\beta \tilde{\otimes} F'_\beta$. In particular, when $F$ is reflexive, $\mathcal{L}_b(E'_\beta, F)$ is itself a reflexive space.
