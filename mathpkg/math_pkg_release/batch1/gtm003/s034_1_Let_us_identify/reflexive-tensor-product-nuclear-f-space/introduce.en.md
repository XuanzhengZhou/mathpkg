Under the same hypotheses as Theorem 9.9, if $F$ is additionally reflexive, then the completed projective tensor product $E \tilde{\otimes} F$ is a reflexive locally convex space. Consequently, $E'_\beta \tilde{\otimes} F'_\beta$ is also reflexive. This shows that reflexivity is preserved under completed projective tensor products with a nuclear (F)-space.
