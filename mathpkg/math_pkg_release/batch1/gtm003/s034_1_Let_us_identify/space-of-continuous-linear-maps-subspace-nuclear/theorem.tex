\text{If } E \text{ and } F \text{ are locally convex spaces and } E \text{ is nuclear, then } \mathcal{L}_e(E, F) \text{ is isomorphic with a subspace of the nuclear space } F^E. \text{ Consequently, } \mathcal{L}_e(E, F) \text{ is a nuclear space. Under additional assumptions on } E, \text{ a corresponding result holds for } \mathcal{L}_b(E, F) \text{ (topology of bounded convergence).}
