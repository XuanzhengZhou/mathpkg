When $E$ is a nuclear locally convex space, the space $\mathcal{L}_e(E, F)$ of continuous linear maps, equipped with the topology of equicontinuous convergence, is isomorphic to a subspace of the product space $F^E$. Since $F^E$ is nuclear when $F$ is nuclear, this shows that $\mathcal{L}_e(E, F)$ inherits nuclearity. An analogous result holds for $\mathcal{L}_b(E, F)$ with the topology of bounded convergence under additional assumptions on $E$.
