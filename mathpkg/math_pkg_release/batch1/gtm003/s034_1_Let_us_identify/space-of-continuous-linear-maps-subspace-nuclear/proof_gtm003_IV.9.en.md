Note that by (III, 6.5), the equicontinuous sequences $\{x'_i\}, \{y'_i\}$ in the kernel theorem can even be supposed to be null sequences in $E'_\sigma$ and $F'_\sigma$, respectively. From this it follows that $\mathcal{L}_e(E, F)$ is isomorphic with a subspace of the nuclear space $F^E$. Under additional assumptions on $E$, a corresponding result holds for $\mathcal{L}_b(E, F)$ (topology of bounded convergence).
