This is immediate in view of (9.1); let us remark also that if $E$ is nuclear and precompact subsets of $E'_\tau$ are equicontinuous, then $E'_\tau$ possesses the approximation property by (III, 9.1). More importantly, the identity map of $E \otimes F$ (which is continuous for the projective topology on the domain, and the topology of bi-equicontinuous convergence on the range) is an isomorphism if $E$ is nuclear.
