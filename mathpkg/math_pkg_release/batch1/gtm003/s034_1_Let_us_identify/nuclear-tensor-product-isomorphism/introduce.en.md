When $E$ is a nuclear locally convex space, the canonical mapping from the completed projective tensor product $E \tilde{\otimes} F$ into the completed tensor product equipped with the topology of bi-equicontinuous convergence is a topological isomorphism. This means that for nuclear spaces, the projective tensor topology and the bi-equicontinuous convergence topology on $E \otimes F$ yield the same completion, unifying two a priori distinct topological tensor products.
