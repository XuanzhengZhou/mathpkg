By (7.2) and Corollary 2 of (7.3), the condition that the range of $u'$ is closed in $E'_\sigma$ is equivalent to $u$ being a weak homomorphism. Since $u$ has closed graph, the result follows from the dual characterization of weak homomorphisms: a densely defined closed linear map is weakly open iff its adjoint has weakly closed range.
