If $E, F$ are Fréchet spaces and $u$ is a continuous linear map of $E$ into $F$, the following are equivalent: (a) $u$ is a topological homomorphism. (b) $u$ is a weak homomorphism. (c) $u$ has a closed range. (d) $u$ is a homomorphism for $\sigma(F, F)$ and $\sigma(E, E)$. (e) $u$ has a range closed for $\sigma(E, E)$.
