Let $E$ be a Ptak space and let $u$ be a nearly open linear map with domain dense in $E$ and range in any locally convex space $F$, and such that the graph of $u$ is closed in $E \times F$. Then $u$ is open. If, in addition, $u$ is one-to-one it suffices for the conclusion that $E$ be $B_r$-complete.
