Let $\langle F, G \rangle$ be a duality and let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a saturated family, covering $F$, of weakly bounded subsets of $F$. Denote by $G_1$ the vector space of all $f \in F^*$ whose restrictions to each $S \in \mathfrak{S}$ are weakly continuous, and endow $G_1$ with the $\mathfrak{S}$-topology. Then $G_1$ is a complete locally convex space in which $G$ is dense.
