Every Hilbert space is reflexive. This follows from the fact that any Hilbert space $H$ is isometrically isomorphic to the sequence space $l_d^2$, where $d$ is the Hilbert dimension of $H$. Since $l_d^2$ spaces are reflexive as special cases of $L^p$ spaces with $p=2$, the result follows immediately from the isomorphism.
