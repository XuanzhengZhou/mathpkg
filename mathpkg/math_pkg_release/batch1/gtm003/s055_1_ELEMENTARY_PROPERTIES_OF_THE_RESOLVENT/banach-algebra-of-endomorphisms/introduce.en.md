For a complex Banach space $E$, the set $\mathcal{L}(E)$ of all continuous linear endomorphisms of $E$ forms a Banach algebra when equipped with the operator norm. This algebra serves as the fundamental setting for the spectral theory developed in the appendix of Schaefer's Topological Vector Spaces. The identity map $e$ of $E$ is the unit element of this algebra.
