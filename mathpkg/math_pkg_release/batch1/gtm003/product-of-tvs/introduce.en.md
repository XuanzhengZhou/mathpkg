The product of an arbitrary family of topological vector spaces over the same scalar field is again a topological vector space under the product topology. Addition and scalar multiplication are defined componentwise. The product inherits the separation property (Hausdorff) and completeness from its factors: the product is Hausdorff or complete if and only if each factor is.
