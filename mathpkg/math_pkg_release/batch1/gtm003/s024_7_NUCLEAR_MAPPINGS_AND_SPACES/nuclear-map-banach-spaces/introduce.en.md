A nuclear map between Banach spaces is one that belongs to the range of the canonical continuous extension $\tau: E' \hat{\otimes} F \to \mathcal{L}(E, F)$ of the natural embedding of the algebraic tensor product into the space of continuous linear maps. Nuclear maps thus arise from elements of the completed projective tensor product of the dual of the domain with the codomain, making them a fundamental bridge between tensor product theory and operator theory.
