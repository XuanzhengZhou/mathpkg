Proposition 7.1 gives the canonical characterization of nuclear maps between locally convex spaces in terms of an absolutely summable series of rank-one operators. A linear map is nuclear precisely when it factors through a nuclear map between suitable Banach spaces associated with a zero-neighborhood and a bounded set. The series representation with equicontinuous coefficient functionals and bounded image vectors is the working definition used throughout the theory.
