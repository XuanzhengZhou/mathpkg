Corollary 1 of Proposition 7.1 establishes that every nuclear map is automatically compact. This inclusion is fundamental: nuclear maps form a proper subclass of compact maps, possessing additional structure through their absolutely summable series representation. The compactness follows from the fact that the image of a suitable zero-neighborhood under a nuclear map is contained in the closed convex circled hull of a null sequence in a complete bounded disk.
