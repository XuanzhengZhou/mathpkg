Corollary 2 of Theorem 7.3 specializes the embedding result to nuclear Fréchet spaces: each such space admits a neighborhood basis of zero consisting of Hilbert discs (i.e., the associated Banach spaces are Hilbert spaces). This follows from the $\ell^p$ embedding theorem by taking $p = 2$, since $\ell^2$ is a Hilbert space. The result is crucial for the application of Hilbert space methods to nuclear Fréchet spaces.
