Let $E$ be a nuclear space, let $U$ be an arbitrary convex, circled $0$-neighborhood in $E$, and let $1 \leq p \leq \infty$. Then the canonical map $\phi_U: E \to \tilde{E}_U$ factors as $E \xrightarrow{v} \ell^p \xrightarrow{w} \tilde{E}_U$ where $v$ is a continuous linear map and $w$ is the restriction of a continuous linear map on $\ell^p$ to the closed subspace $\overline{v(E)}$.

In particular, for each such $U$ there exists a $0$-neighborhood $V \subset U$ such that $\tilde{E}_V$ is isometrically isomorphic to a closed subspace of $\ell^p$.
