The algebraic dual $L^*$ of a vector space $L$ over a field $K$ is the set of all linear forms (linear functionals) $f: L \to K$, endowed with the natural vector space structure. This is a purely algebraic notion, distinct from the topological (continuous) dual which will be introduced later for topological vector spaces.
