This theorem shows that every metrizable locally convex space is bornological. Since metrizable spaces possess countable decreasing 0-neighborhood bases, any convex circled set absorbing all bounded sets must contain a scalar multiple of some basis element, and thus be a 0-neighborhood. This result implies that Fréchet spaces are bornological.
