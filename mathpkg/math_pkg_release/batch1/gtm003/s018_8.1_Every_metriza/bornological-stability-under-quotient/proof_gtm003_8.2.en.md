Every separated quotient of a bornological space is the inductive limit of the bornological space (as the quotient topology is the inductive topology with respect to the quotient map). The locally convex direct sum is the inductive limit of the summands. The inductive limit of any family of bornological spaces is bornological by Theorem 8.2.
