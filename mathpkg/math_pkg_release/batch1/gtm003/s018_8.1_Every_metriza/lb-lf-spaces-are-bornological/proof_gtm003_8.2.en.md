Every Fréchet space is metrizable, hence bornological by Theorem 8.1. Every Banach space is a Fréchet space, hence bornological. An (LB) space is an inductive limit of Banach spaces, and an (LF) space is an inductive limit of Fréchet spaces. By Corollary 1 of Theorem 8.2, the inductive limit of any family of bornological spaces is bornological. Therefore every space of type (LB) or (LF) is bornological.
