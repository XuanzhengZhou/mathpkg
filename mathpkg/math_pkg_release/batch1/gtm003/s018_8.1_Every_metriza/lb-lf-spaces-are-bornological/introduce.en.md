An (LB) space is a strict inductive limit of a sequence of Banach spaces, and an (LF) space is a strict inductive limit of a sequence of Fréchet spaces. This corollary follows from Theorem 8.1 (which shows Fréchet spaces are bornological) and Corollary 1 (which shows inductive limits of bornological spaces are bornological).
