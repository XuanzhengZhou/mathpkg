Let $f: L_1 \to L_2$ be linear, $L_1$ and $L_2$ being vector spaces over a non-discrete valuated field $K$. If $A \subset L_1$ and $B \subset L_2$ are circled, then $f(A)$ and $f^{-1}(B)$ are circled. If $B$ is radial then $f^{-1}(B)$ is radial; if $A$ is radial and $f$ is surjective, then $f(A)$ is radial.
