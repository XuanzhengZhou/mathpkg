Given a vector subspace M of L, the relation x - y belonging to M defines an equivalence relation on L. The equivalence classes are the cosets x + M, and the set of all such cosets forms the quotient vector space L/M under the natural operations of addition and scalar multiplication. This construction is fundamental for reducing problems about vector spaces by factoring out a subspace.
