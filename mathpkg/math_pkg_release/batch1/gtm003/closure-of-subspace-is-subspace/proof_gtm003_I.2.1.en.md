**Proof.** It follows from $(LT)_1$ that the mapping $(x, y) \mapsto x + y$ is continuous from $L \times L$ to $L$. Hence $\overline{M} + \overline{M} \subset \overline{M + M} \subset \overline{M}$. From $(LT)_2$ we obtain that $\lambda \overline{M} \subset \overline{M}$ for every $\lambda \in K$. Thus $\overline{M}$ is closed under addition and scalar multiplication, i.e., a subspace.
