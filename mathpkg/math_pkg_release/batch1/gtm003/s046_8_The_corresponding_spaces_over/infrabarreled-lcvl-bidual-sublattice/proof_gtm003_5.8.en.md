The assumption that $E$ is infrabarreled (Chapter IV, Section 5) means precisely that the evaluation map $x \rightarrow \tilde{x}$ is a homeomorphism of $E$ into $E''$; the remainder follows from (1.6), since $E'$ is a solid subspace of $E^+$.
