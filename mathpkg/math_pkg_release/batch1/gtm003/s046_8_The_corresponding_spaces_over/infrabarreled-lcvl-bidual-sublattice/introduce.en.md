Corollary 3 to (7.4) provides the topological counterpart of (1.6): for an infrabarreled locally convex vector lattice $E$, the evaluation map embeds $E$ as a topological vector sublattice of its strong bidual $E''$. The bidual carries its natural topology and canonical order, under which it is an order complete l.c.v.l. The infrabarreled condition ensures the evaluation map is a homeomorphism onto its image.
