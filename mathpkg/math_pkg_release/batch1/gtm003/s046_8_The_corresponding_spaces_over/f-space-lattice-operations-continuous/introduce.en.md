This corollary to (7.3) asserts that for any vector lattice $E$ which is also an ordered (F)-space with a normal positive cone, the lattice operations (supremum and infimum) are automatically continuous. The result follows from the characterization of the order topology as the finest locally convex vector lattice topology, combined with the fact that in an (F)-space the topology is complete metrizable.
