Theorem (7.4) establishes that the strong dual $E'_\beta$ of any locally convex vector lattice $E$ is itself an order complete locally convex vector lattice under the canonical dual order, and forms a solid subspace of the order dual $E^+$. When $E$ is additionally barreled, the dual $E'$ is even a band in $E^+$ and $E'_\beta$ is a complete locally convex space. This result shows that duality preserves and strengthens the order structure.
