This proposition characterizes the order topology $\mathfrak{T}_0$ on a regularly ordered vector lattice $E$ as the finest locally convex topology under which $E$ becomes a locally convex vector lattice. The result establishes a maximality property of $\mathfrak{T}_0$ among all locally convex topologies compatible with the lattice structure, complementing the earlier description of $\mathfrak{T}_0$ as the topology of uniform convergence on order intervals.
