This corollary of Theorem (7.4) specializes the general result to normed lattices: the strong dual of a normed lattice is always a Banach lattice under the dual norm and canonical ordering. Moreover, when $E$ is a Banach space (i.e., a complete normed lattice), the topological dual coincides with the order dual, so $E' = E^+$. This follows from the solidness of the unit ball and the general theory of (5.5) and (7.2).
