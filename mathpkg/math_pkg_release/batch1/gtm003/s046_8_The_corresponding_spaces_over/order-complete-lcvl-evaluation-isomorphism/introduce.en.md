Theorem (7.5) gives three equivalent characterizations for when an order complete locally convex vector lattice $(E, \mathfrak{T})$ can be identified, via the evaluation map, with an order complete sublattice of its strong bidual $E''$. Condition (b) relates to the convergence of section filters of majorized directed sets to their suprema, while condition (c) requires every order convergent filter to converge with respect to the given topology $\mathfrak{T}$. The equivalence shows a deep connection between order completeness, bidual embedding, and topological order convergence.
