Theorem 5.4 is a fundamental structure theorem: every complete locally convex space can be represented as a projective limit of Banach spaces. The proof constructs the projective system from the continuous semi-norms defining the topology, completing the associated normed spaces to obtain Banach spaces. This result shows that Banach spaces are the "building blocks" of all complete locally convex spaces via the projective limit construction.
