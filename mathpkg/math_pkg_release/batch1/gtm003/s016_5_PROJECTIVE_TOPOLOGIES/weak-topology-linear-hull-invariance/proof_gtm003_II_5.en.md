The weak topology $\sigma(E, F)$ is the coarsest topology making each $f \in F$ continuous. Since continuity of each $f \in F$ implies continuity of every finite linear combination $\sum \lambda_i f_i$ (by the continuity of addition and scalar multiplication in $K$), the coarsest topology making all $f \in F$ continuous is the same as the coarsest topology making all $g \in \operatorname{span}(F)$ continuous. Hence the two projective topologies coincide.
