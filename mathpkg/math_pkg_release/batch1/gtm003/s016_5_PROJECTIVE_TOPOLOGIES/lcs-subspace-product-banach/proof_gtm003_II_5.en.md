Let $E$ be a locally convex space and $\hat{E}$ its completion. By Theorem 5.4, $\hat{E}$ is isomorphic to a projective limit $\varprojlim \tilde{E}_\alpha$ of Banach spaces. Since a projective limit is (by construction) a subspace of the product $\prod_\alpha \tilde{E}_\alpha$, it follows that $\hat{E}$ is isomorphic to a subspace of a product of Banach spaces. Since $E$ is a subspace of $\hat{E}$, composing the embeddings yields an isomorphism of $E$ onto a subspace of $\prod_\alpha \tilde{E}_\alpha$.
