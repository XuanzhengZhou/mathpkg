The least upper bound (supremum) of any family of locally convex topologies on a fixed vector space $E$ is again a locally convex topology. Moreover, it can be realized as a projective topology by taking the identity map of $E$ with each topology in the family. This shows that the lattice of locally convex topologies on $E$ is closed under the operation of taking suprema.
