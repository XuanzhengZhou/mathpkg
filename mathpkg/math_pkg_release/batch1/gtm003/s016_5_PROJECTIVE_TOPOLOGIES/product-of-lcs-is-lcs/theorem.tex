Let $\{(E_\alpha, \mathfrak{T}_\alpha): \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ be a family of topological vector spaces, each $\mathfrak{T}_\alpha$ being a locally convex topology. The product topology $\mathfrak{T}$ on $E = \prod_{\alpha \in \mathbf{A}} E_\alpha$ is locally convex; $\mathfrak{T}$ is the projective topology on $E$ with respect to the projections $p_\alpha \colon E \rightarrow E_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$). In particular, the product of any family of locally convex spaces is a locally convex space.
