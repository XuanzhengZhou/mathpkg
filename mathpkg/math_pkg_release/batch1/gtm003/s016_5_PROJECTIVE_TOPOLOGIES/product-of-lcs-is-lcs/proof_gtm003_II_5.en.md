The product topology on $E = \prod_\alpha E_\alpha$ is by definition the coarsest topology making all projections $p_\alpha: E \to E_\alpha$ continuous. This is exactly the projective topology with respect to the family $\{(E_\alpha, \mathfrak{T}_\alpha, p_\alpha): \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$. Since each $\mathfrak{T}_\alpha$ is locally convex and each $p_\alpha$ is linear, the projective topology is locally convex by the general result on projective topologies. Hence $E$ with the product topology is a locally convex space.
