Let $\mathfrak{T}$ be the projective topology on $E$ with respect to the family $\{(E_\alpha, \mathfrak{T}_\alpha, f_\alpha): \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$, where each $f_\alpha$ is a linear map and each $\mathfrak{T}_\alpha$ is a locally convex topology on $E_\alpha$. Then $\mathfrak{T}$ is a translation-invariant topology on $E$ with a base of convex $0$-neighborhoods satisfying conditions (a) and (b) of (I, 1.2); hence $\mathfrak{T}$ is a locally convex topology on $E$.
