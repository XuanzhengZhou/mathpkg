Let $E$ be a vector space over $K$ and let $F$ be a non-empty subset of the algebraic dual $E^*$. Set $E_f = K_0$ (the one-dimensional vector space $K$ with its natural topology) for every $f \in F$. The projective topology on $E$ with respect to the family $\{(E_f, f): f \in F\}$ is called the weak topology generated by $F$, and is denoted by $\sigma(E, F)$.
