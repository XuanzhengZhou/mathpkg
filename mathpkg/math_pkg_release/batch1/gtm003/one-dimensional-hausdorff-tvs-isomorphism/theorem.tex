Every one-dimensional Hausdorff t.v.s. $L$ over $K$ is isomorphic with $K_0$ (the one-dimensional t.v.s. obtained by considering $K$ as a vector space over itself). More precisely, $\lambda \mapsto \lambda x_0$ is an isomorphism of $K_0$ onto $L$ for each $x_0 \in L$, $x_0 \neq 0$, and every isomorphism of $K_0$ onto $L$ is of this form.
