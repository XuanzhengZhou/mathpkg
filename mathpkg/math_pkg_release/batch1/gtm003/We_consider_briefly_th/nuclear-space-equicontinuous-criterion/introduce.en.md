This theorem gives a characterization of nuclear locally convex spaces purely through the behavior of equicontinuous subsets of the weak dual. A space is nuclear precisely when every equicontinuous, closed, convex, circled subset of the dual can be enlarged to another such set so that the canonical inclusion map between the associated Banach spaces is nuclear. The condition dualizes the standard definition using neighborhoods of the origin, providing a flexible tool for verifying nuclearity in practice.
