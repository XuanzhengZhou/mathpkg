An affine subspace (linear manifold) is a translated vector subspace. Hyperplanes are maximal proper affine subspaces, corresponding to subspaces of codimension 1. These geometric notions are central to the Hahn-Banach separation theorems, where hyperplanes are used to separate convex sets.
