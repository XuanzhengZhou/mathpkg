This fundamental characterization shows that nuclear maps are precisely those that can be expressed as an absolutely summable series of rank-one operators $f_n(\cdot) y_n$ with controlled growth conditions on the sequences $\{f_n\}$ and $\{y_n\}$. This bridges the abstract tensor product definition with a concrete series representation.
