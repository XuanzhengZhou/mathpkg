Banach's Homomorphism Theorem is the open mapping theorem for topological vector spaces, generalizing Banach's classical result for Banach spaces. For a continuous linear map with dense range between complete metrizable t.v.s., either the range is meager or it is the whole space and the map is open. The theorem is a consequence of the Baire category theorem.
