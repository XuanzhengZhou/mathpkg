From the series representation (7.1), choose a $0$-neighborhood $U$ such that $|f_n(x)| \leq 1$ for all $x \in U$ and all $n$. Then $u(U)$ is contained in the closed, convex, circled hull $C$ of the null sequence $\{y_n\}$ in $F_B$. Since $\{y_n\}$ is a null sequence in the Banach space $F_B$ and $F_B$ is complete, $C$ is compact in $F_B$ (by I, 5.2 and II, 4.3), and hence compact in $F$ by continuity of the imbedding $\psi_B: F_B \to F$.
