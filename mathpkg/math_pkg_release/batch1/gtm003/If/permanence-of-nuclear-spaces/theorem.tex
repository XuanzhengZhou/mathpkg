Let $\{E_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ be an arbitrary family of nuclear spaces. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Every subspace and every separated quotient space of a nuclear space is nuclear.
\item The product $\prod_{\alpha \in A} E_\alpha$ is nuclear.
\item The locally convex direct sum $\bigoplus_{\alpha \in A} E_\alpha$ is nuclear.
\item The projective tensor product of two nuclear spaces is nuclear.
\end{enumerate}
