The order topology is the natural locally convex topology on an ordered vector space, defined as the finest locally convex topology for which all order intervals are bounded. It is the upper bound of all locally convex topologies with this property and generalizes the norm topology in spaces like $C(X)$ and $L^\infty(\mu)$ which possess order units.
