The Dieudonné-Schwartz theorem extends Banach's Homomorphism Theorem beyond the metrizable case to (LF)-spaces. It states that any surjective continuous linear map from an (LF)-space to a Fréchet space or (LF)-space is automatically open. The proof proceeds by reducing to the complete metrizable case via the defining inductive limit structure.
