When the building blocks $L_a$ of the inductive limit representation are complete (and hence normable and barreled by Proposition 6.2), the inductive limit $(L, \mathfrak{T}_O)$ inherits the barreled property (by II, 7.2). This is important for applications of the Banach-Steinhaus theorem.
