This lemma provides the key technical estimate for proving Banach's Homomorphism Theorem (the Open Mapping Theorem for Fréchet spaces). It shows that if the closure of the image of a ball in the domain has nonempty interior in the codomain, then for suitable radii the containment is complete. The proof uses completeness of the domain to construct a convergent series.
