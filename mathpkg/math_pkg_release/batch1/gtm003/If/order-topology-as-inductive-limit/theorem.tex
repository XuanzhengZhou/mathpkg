Let $L$ be an Archimedean ordered vector space over $\mathbb{R}$ and let $H$ be a cofinal subset of the positive cone $C$ (in the sense that for each $x \geq 0$ there exists $a \in H$ with $a \geq x$). For each $a \geq 0$, denote by $L_a = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} n[-a, a]$ the ordered subspace generated by the order interval $[-a, a]$.

Then the order topology $\mathfrak{T}_O$ on $L$ is the locally convex inductive limit of the topologies $\mathfrak{T}_a$ on $L_a$, where $\mathfrak{T}_a$ is the topology generated by the gauge of $[-a, a]$.
