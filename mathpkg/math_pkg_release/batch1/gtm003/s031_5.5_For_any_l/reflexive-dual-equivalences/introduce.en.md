This theorem establishes a cycle of equivalent conditions linking reflexivity of a locally convex space $E_\tau$ with reflexivity of its Mackey dual $E'_\tau$, semi-reflexivity of the weak topologies $E_\sigma$ and $E'_\sigma$, and barreledness of the Mackey topologies on both $E$ and $E'$. The proof uses the preceding results (5.5) and (5.6) together with the relationship between consistent topologies and the bipolar theorem (1.6).
