By (1.6) and (5.5)(c), every bounded subset of $E$ is equicontinuous if $E$ is semi-reflexive. Equicontinuous subsets of $E$ (when $E$ is identified with a subspace of the algebraic dual of $E'_\tau$) are relatively compact for the weak topology $\sigma(E, E')$ by the Alaoglu-Bourbaki theorem. Since each relatively weakly compact set is weakly complete, it follows that every closed bounded subset of $E$ is complete; hence $E$ is quasi-complete.
