This corollary shows that for normed spaces, the distinction between semi-reflexivity and reflexivity disappears. The argument combines two facts: every semi-reflexive space is quasi-complete (Corollary 1 of 5.5), so a semi-reflexive normed space is automatically complete, hence a Banach space; and every Banach space is barreled, so the semi-reflexive Banach space satisfies the characterization of reflexivity given in Theorem 5.6. More generally, semi-reflexivity and reflexivity agree for infrabarreled (in particular, bornological) spaces.
