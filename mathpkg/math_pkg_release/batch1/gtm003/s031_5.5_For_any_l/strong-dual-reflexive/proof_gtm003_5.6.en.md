If $E$ is reflexive, then $\beta(E', E) = \tau(E', E)$ and $E'_\beta = E'_\tau$ is barreled by (5.5), since $E$ is semi-reflexive. Since, also, $E$ is barreled, each bounded subset of $E'_\beta$ is relatively compact for $\sigma(E', E)$ by (5.2); hence $E'_\beta$ is reflexive by (5.6).
