The family $\mathfrak{C}$ of all convex, circled, $\sigma(G, F)$-compact sets in $G$ is contained in (in fact, identical with) the family $\mathfrak{S}$ of (III, 3.4) if, in (III, 3.4), we take $F = K_0$, for every $C \in \mathfrak{C}$ is $\sigma(G, F)$-complete (cf. (5.5), Corollary 2). Thus the first assertion follows from (III, 3.4); the second is immediate.
