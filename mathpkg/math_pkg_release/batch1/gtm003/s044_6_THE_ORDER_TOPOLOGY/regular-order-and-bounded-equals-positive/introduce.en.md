Corollary 1 of Theorem 6.2 states that for Archimedean ordered vector spaces with an order unit, the order is automatically regular, and the space $L^b$ of order-bounded linear forms coincides with the space $L^+$ of positive linear forms (more precisely, differences of positive linear forms). This follows immediately from Theorem 6.1 and Proposition 3.3.
