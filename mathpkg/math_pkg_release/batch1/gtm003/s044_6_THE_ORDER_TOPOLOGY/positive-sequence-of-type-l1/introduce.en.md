A positive sequence of type $l^1$ is bounded in an order-theoretic sense by a scalar $l^1$ sequence multiplied by a fixed non-negative element. This definition provides a quantitative measure of order-boundedness for sequences. It is used in Theorem 6.2 to characterize when $(L, \mathfrak{T}_0)$ is complete: completeness is equivalent to order summability of all positive sequences of type $l^1$.
