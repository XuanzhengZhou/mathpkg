Theorem 6.1 identifies the topological dual of an ordered vector space equipped with the order topology $\mathfrak{T}_0$ as the space $L^b$ of all order-bounded linear forms. This is a fundamental result connecting the order structure and the topological structure: continuous linear forms for $\mathfrak{T}_0$ are exactly those that carry order intervals into bounded subsets of $\mathbb{R}$.
