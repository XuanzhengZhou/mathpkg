This corollary provides sufficient conditions for the positive cone $C$ to be normal under the order topology. When $L$ is regularly ordered and $C$ satisfies the decomposition property (D) (every element of $C - C$ can be expressed as a difference of elements of $C$ satisfying a certain compatibility), then $C$ is normal for $\mathfrak{T}_0$, and the topological dual equals $L^+$. The proof uses the inductive limit description of $\mathfrak{T}_0$ and the decomposition property to verify the normality criterion.
