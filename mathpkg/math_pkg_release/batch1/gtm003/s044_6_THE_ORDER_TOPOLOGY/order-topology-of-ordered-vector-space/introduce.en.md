The order topology $\mathfrak{T}_0$ is the natural locally convex topology on an ordered vector space over $\mathbb{R}$. It is defined as the finest locally convex topology for which every order interval is bounded. This topology serves as the topology of many ordered vector spaces occurring in analysis, including spaces of continuous functions and $L^\infty$ spaces. The order topology is studied further in relation to Archimedean order, order units, and the normality of the positive cone.
