Let $P$ be a family of semi-norms on $E$.
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] For $\{U_{p,n}: p \in P, n \in N\}$ where $U_{p,n} = \{x: p(x) < n^{-1}\}$ to be a 0-neighborhood base of a locally convex topology on $E$, it is necessary and sufficient that the family $\{cp: c > 0, p \in P\}$ of semi-norms be directed.
\item[(b)] The family of suprema of non-empty finite subsets of a generating family is directed.
\item[(c)] For a subspace $M \subset E$, the quotient semi-norm $\hat{p}(\hat{x}) = \inf \{p(x): x \in \hat{x}\}$ on $E/M$ generates the quotient topology.
\end{itemize}
