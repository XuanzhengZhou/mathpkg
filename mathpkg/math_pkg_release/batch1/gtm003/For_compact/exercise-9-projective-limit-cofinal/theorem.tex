Let $E(\mathfrak{T}) = \lim_{g_{\alpha \beta}} E_{\beta}(\mathfrak{T}_\beta)$ be the projective limit of a family of l.c.s. and suppose that $g_{\alpha \gamma} = g_{\alpha \beta} \circ g_{\beta \gamma}$ whenever $\alpha \leq \beta \leq \gamma$. Then $E(\mathfrak{T})$ is isomorphic with $\lim_{g_{\delta, \varepsilon}} E_{\varepsilon}(\mathfrak{T}_\varepsilon)$ ($\delta, \varepsilon \in B$) if $B$ is a cofinal subset of $A$. A corresponding result holds for inductive limits.
