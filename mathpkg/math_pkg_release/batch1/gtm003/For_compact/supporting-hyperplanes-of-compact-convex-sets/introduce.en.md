For any compact convex subset $C$ of a locally convex space, and any closed real hyperplane $H$, there are at most two supporting hyperplanes of $C$ parallel to $H$. These correspond to the minimum and maximum of a continuous linear functional restricted to $C$. Furthermore, the set $C$ can be recovered as the intersection of all closed half-spaces that contain $C$ and are bounded by its supporting hyperplanes.
