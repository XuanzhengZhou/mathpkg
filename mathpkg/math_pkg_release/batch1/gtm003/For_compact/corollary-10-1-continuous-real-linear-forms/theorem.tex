If $E$ is a locally convex space, $E'_0$ is the space of all continuous real linear forms on $E$. For each non-empty compact convex subset $C \subset E$, every closed real hyperplane supporting $C$ is of the form $\{x \in E : f(x) = \alpha\}$ where $f \in E'_0$ and $\alpha = \inf f(C)$ or $\alpha = \sup f(C)$.
