This corollary of Theorem 10.1 identifies the supporting hyperplanes of a compact convex set $C$ in a locally convex space $E$ as precisely the level sets of continuous real linear forms $f \in E'_0$ at the extreme values of $f$ on $C$. It establishes the role of the real dual space $E'_0$ in describing the geometry of compact convex subsets.
