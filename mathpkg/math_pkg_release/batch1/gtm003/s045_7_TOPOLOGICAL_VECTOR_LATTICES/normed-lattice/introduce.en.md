A normed lattice is a normed vector space over the real numbers that is also a vector lattice, with the additional property that the closed unit ball is a solid set. This condition ensures compatibility between the norm and the lattice operations: if $|y| \leq |x|$, then $\|y\| \leq \|x\|$. The completion of a normed lattice is a Banach lattice.
