Let $E$ be a vector lattice and let $G$ be a solid sublattice of the order dual $E^+$. Then for each $x \in E$, the canonical image $\tilde{x}^+ = \sup(0, \tilde{x})$ taken in $G^+$ under the evaluation map equals the canonical image of $x^+ \in E$. In particular, the canonical image of $E$ in $G^+$ is a sublattice of $G^+$.
