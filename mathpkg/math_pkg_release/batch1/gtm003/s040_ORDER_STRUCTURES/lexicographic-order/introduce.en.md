The lexicographical order on $R_0^\beta$ is the natural dictionary ordering on functions with finite support, comparing them by their values at the smallest ordinal where they differ. It provides an important example of a totally ordered vector lattice that is non-Archimedean (hence not regular) when the index ordinal exceeds 1, and whose order dual is only one-dimensional.
