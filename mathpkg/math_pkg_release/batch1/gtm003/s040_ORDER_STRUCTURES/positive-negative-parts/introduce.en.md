Every element of a vector lattice decomposes uniquely as the difference of its positive and negative parts, which are disjoint non-negative elements. This decomposition $x = x^+ - x^-$ generalizes the decomposition of a real number into its positive and negative parts, and is fundamental to all computations in vector lattices.
