Let $\langle F, G \rangle$ be a dual system and let $M$ be a subspace of $F$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item The weak topology $\sigma(M, G/M^\circ)$ is the topology induced on $M$ by $\sigma(F, G)$.
\item The weak topology $\sigma(G/M^\circ, M)$ is the quotient topology of $\sigma(G, F)$ if and only if $M$ is closed in $F$.
\end{enumerate}
