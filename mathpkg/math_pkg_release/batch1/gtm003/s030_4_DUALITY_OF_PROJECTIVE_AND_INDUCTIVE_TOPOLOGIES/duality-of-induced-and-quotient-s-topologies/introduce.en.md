Theorem 4.1 establishes the duality relationship between $\mathfrak{S}$-topologies under the operations of taking a subspace and forming a quotient. Given compatible saturated families of subsets on $G$ and $G/M^\circ$, the induced topology on $M$ and the quotient topology on $G/M^\circ$ correspond via the mappings $\psi$ (imbedding) and $\phi$ (surjection). The theorem requires consistency conditions on the topologies for the reverse implications, and $M$ must be closed for the quotient implication.
