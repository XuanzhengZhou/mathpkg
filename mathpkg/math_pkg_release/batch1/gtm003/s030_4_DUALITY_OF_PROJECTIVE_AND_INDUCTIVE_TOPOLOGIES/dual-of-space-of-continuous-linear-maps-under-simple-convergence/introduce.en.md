This corollary identifies the topological dual of the space of continuous linear maps equipped with the topology of simple convergence. The dual is algebraically isomorphic to the algebraic tensor product $E \otimes F'$, with the pairing given by evaluation on elementary tensors. This result connects the duality theory of product spaces with tensor products and provides a concrete algebraic description of $\mathcal{L}_s(E, F)'$.
