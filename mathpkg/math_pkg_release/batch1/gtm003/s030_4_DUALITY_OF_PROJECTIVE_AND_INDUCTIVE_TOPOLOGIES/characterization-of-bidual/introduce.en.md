Theorem 5.4 characterizes the bidual $E''$ within the algebraic dual $E'^*$ as those linear forms that are weakly continuous on each equicontinuous subset of $E'$. This condition reflects the fact that $E''$ is the dual of the strong dual $E'_\beta$, and the strong topology coincides with the weak topology on equicontinuous sets by the Alaoglu-Bourbaki theorem. The result provides a practical criterion for membership in $E''$.
