This corollary provides the dual statement of Theorem 4.3: the topological dual of a locally convex direct sum is algebraically the product of the individual duals. The Mackey topology on the direct sum is the direct sum of the Mackey topologies, and the Mackey topology on its dual is the product of the Mackey dual topologies. This exhibits a perfect symmetry between products and direct sums under the Mackey duality.
