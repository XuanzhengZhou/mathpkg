Theorem 4.2 establishes the duality between products and direct sums for $\mathfrak{S}$-topologies. On the product side $F = \prod F_\alpha$, the $\mathfrak{S}$-topology is the product of the individual $\mathfrak{S}_\alpha$-topologies. On the direct sum side $G = \bigoplus G_\alpha$, the corresponding $\mathfrak{S}$-topology is the locally convex direct sum. This duality structure underpins the analysis of duals of product spaces and direct sums of locally convex spaces.
