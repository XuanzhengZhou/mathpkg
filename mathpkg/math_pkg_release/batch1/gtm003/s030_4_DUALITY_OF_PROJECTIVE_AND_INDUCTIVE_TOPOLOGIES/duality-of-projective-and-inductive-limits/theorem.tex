Let $\{E_\alpha\}_{\alpha \in A}$ be a projective system of locally convex spaces with linking maps $g_{\alpha\beta}: E_\beta \to E_\alpha$ ($\alpha \leq \beta$), and let $E = \varprojlim g_{\alpha\beta} E_\beta$ be the projective limit, considered as the subspace
$$E = \bigcap_{\alpha \leq \beta} u_{\alpha\beta}^{-1}(0)$$
of $\prod_\beta E_\beta$. Then the Mackey dual $(E', \tau(E', E))$ is the inductive limit
$$E' = \varinjlim h_{\beta\alpha} (E'_\alpha, \tau(E'_\alpha, E_\alpha))$$
of the Mackey duals.
