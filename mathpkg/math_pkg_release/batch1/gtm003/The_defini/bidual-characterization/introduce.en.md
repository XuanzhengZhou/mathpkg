Theorem 5.4 characterizes the bidual $E''$ as a subspace of the algebraic dual $E'^*$. An element $f \in E'^*$ belongs to $E''$ precisely when it is continuous on every equicontinuous subset of $E'$ under the weak topology $\sigma(E', E)$. Via the canonical evaluation map, this gives the inclusion chain $E \subset E'' \subset E'^*$, where both inclusions are generally proper.
