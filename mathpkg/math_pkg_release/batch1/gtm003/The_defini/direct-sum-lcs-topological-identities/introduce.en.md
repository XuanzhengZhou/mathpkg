This corollary of Theorem 4.3 gives the dual topological identities for locally convex direct sums: the dual of a direct sum is algebraically the product of the duals, and the Mackey topologies behave dually to the product case. The strong topology on the dual of a direct sum is the product of the strong dual topologies.
