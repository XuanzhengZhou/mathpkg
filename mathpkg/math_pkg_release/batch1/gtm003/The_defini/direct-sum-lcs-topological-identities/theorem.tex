Let $\{E_{\alpha}: \alpha \in A\}$ be a family of l.c.s. and let $E$ be their locally convex direct sum. $E'$ is algebraically isomorphic with $\prod_{\alpha} E'_{\alpha}$, and the following topological identities are valid:

1. $\tau(E, E') = \bigoplus_{\alpha} \tau(E_{\alpha}, E'_{\alpha})$.
2. $\tau(E', E) = \prod_{\alpha} \tau(E'_{\alpha}, E_{\alpha})$.
