Corollary 4 of Theorem 4.1 addresses Mackey topologies: the Mackey topology on any quotient space is the quotient of the Mackey topology on the original space, and every metrizable subspace inherits the Mackey topology from the ambient space. As a consequence, the class of Mackey spaces is stable under taking separated quotients and metrizable subspaces.
