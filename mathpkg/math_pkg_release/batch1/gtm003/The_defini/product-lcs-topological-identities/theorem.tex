Let $\{E_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ be a family of l.c.s. and let $E = \prod_\alpha E_\alpha$. The dual $E'$ of $E$ is algebraically isomorphic with $\oplus_\alpha E'_\alpha$, and the following topological identities are valid:

1. $\sigma(E, E') = \prod_\alpha \sigma(E_\alpha, E'_\alpha)$.
2. $\tau(E, E') = \prod_\alpha \tau(E_\alpha, E'_\alpha)$.
3. $\tau(E', E) = \oplus_\alpha \tau(E'_\alpha, E_\alpha)$.

Remark: $\sigma(E', E) = \oplus_\alpha \sigma(E'_\alpha, E_\alpha)$ if and only if the family $\{E_\alpha\}$ is finite (Exercise 8).
