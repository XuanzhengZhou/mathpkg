An infrabarreled space is a locally convex space in which every barrel that absorbs all bounded sets is a neighborhood of zero. Equivalently, the families of equicontinuous and strongly bounded subsets of the dual coincide. Every barreled space and every bornological space is infrabarreled. This concept is crucial for studying when the strong bidual coincides with the original space.
