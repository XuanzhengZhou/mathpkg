Denote by $E_\sigma$, $F_\sigma$ two l.c.s. under their respective weak topologies. The space of continuous bilinear forms on $E_\sigma \times F_\sigma$ (equivalently, the dual of the projective tensor product $E_\sigma \otimes F_\sigma$) is the tensor product $E' \otimes F'$.
