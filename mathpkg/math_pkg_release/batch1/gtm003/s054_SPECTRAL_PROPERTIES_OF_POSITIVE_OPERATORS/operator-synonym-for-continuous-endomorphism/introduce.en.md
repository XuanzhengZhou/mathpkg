In the Appendix on Spectral Properties of Positive Operators, Schaefer adopts the terminological convention that "operator" means "continuous endomorphism" of a topological vector space. This simplifies the exposition by avoiding the longer phrase throughout the spectral-theoretic discussion that follows. The convention is local to the Appendix and does not override the book's general usage elsewhere.
