This theorem provides three equivalent characterizations of compactness in Hausdorff spaces: the finite intersection property for closed sets, the existence of cluster points for every filter, and the convergence of every ultrafilter. These reformulations are essential tools in functional analysis, especially the ultrafilter characterization which is often the most useful in topological vector spaces.
