A closed map is a mapping between topological spaces whose graph is a closed subset of the topological product $X \times Y$. This definition (via the graph) is equivalent, in the context of Hausdorff spaces, to the more common definition that a closed map sends closed sets to closed sets. The Closed Graph Theorem in functional analysis relies on this concept.
