For metrizable uniform spaces, the notion of completeness (every Cauchy filter converges) coincides with semi-completeness (every Cauchy filter has a cluster point). This is because in a first-countable uniform space, a Cauchy filter with a cluster point necessarily converges. This fact simplifies completeness verifications in metric and metrizable contexts.
