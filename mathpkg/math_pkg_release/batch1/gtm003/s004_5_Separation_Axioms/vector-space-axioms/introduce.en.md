The vector space axioms define the algebraic structure of a left vector space over a field $K$. Axioms (1)-(4) assert that $L$ is an abelian group under addition; axioms (5)-(8) govern how scalars from the field $K$ act on vectors in $L$. The field $K$ is allowed to be non-commutative, so the axiomatization takes care to distinguish left scalar multiplication.
