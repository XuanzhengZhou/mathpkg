This proposition characterizes Hausdorff (separated) spaces in terms of filter convergence: a topological space is Hausdorff precisely when every convergent filter has a unique limit. This equivalence is fundamental in general topology and provides a useful criterion for checking the Hausdorff property, particularly in the context of topological vector spaces.
