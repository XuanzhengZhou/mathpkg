Let $\{X_\alpha : \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ be a family of topological spaces, $X$ their Cartesian product, and $f_\alpha$ the projection of $X$ onto $X_\alpha$. The projective topology on $X$ with respect to the family $\{(X_\alpha, f_\alpha) : \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ is called the \textbf{product topology} on $X$.
