A Banach lattice $E$ is called an \emph{(AM)-space} (abstract $M$-space) if the norm of $E$ satisfies
$$
\|\sup(x, y)\| = \sup(\|x\|, \|y\|)
$$
for all $x, y$ in the positive cone $C$ of $E$. Every (AM)-space is isomorphic with a closed vector sublattice of a suitable $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X)$ (Kakutani).
