The positive face of the dual unit ball is a central object in Kakutani's representation theorem for (AM)-spaces with unit. For an (AM)-space $E$ with unit $u$, the positive face $H_0$ is the set of positive linear functionals taking value $1$ on $u$. It is convex, weakly compact, and forms a base of the dual cone. The extreme points of $H_0$ provide the underlying compact space $X$ of the representation $E \cong \mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X)$.
