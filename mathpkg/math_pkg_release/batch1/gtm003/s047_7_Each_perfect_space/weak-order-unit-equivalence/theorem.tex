Let $E$ be an order complete vector lattice of minimal type. For each $x > 0$, the following assertions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $x$ is a weak order unit.
\item[(b)] For each positive linear form $f \neq 0$ on $E$, $f(x) > 0$.
\item[(c)] For each topology $\mathfrak{T}$ on $E$ such that $(E, \mathfrak{T})$ is a locally convex vector lattice, $x$ is a quasi-interior point of the positive cone.
\end{enumerate}
