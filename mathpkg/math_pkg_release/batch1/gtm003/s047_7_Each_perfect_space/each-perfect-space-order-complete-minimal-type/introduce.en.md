This proposition establishes a fundamental structural property of perfect spaces (dual pairs $(\lambda, \lambda^+)$ where $\lambda$ is a perfect space of measures): every perfect space is order complete. Moreover, when every order interval is $\sigma(\lambda, \lambda^+)$-compact, the perfect space is of minimal type, meaning its section filter for any directed upward and majorized set is a Cauchy filter for the norm topology.
