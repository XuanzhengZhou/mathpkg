Let $E \neq \{0\}$ be an (AM)-space with unit and let $X$ be the set of extreme points of the positive face of the dual unit ball. Then $X$ is non-empty and $\sigma(E', E)$-compact, and the evaluation map $x \mapsto f$ defined by
$$
f(t) = \langle x, t \rangle \quad (t \in X)
$$
is an isomorphism of the (AM)-space $E$ onto $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X)$.
