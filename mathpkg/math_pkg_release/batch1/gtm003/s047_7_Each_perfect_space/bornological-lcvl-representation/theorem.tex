Let $(E, \mathfrak{T})$ be a locally convex vector lattice which is bornological and sequentially complete. There exists a family of compact spaces $X_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$) and a family of vector lattice isomorphisms $f_\alpha$ of $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{R}}(X_\alpha)$ into $E$ ($\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$) such that $\mathfrak{T}$ is the finest locally convex topology on $E$ for which each $f_\alpha$ is continuous.
