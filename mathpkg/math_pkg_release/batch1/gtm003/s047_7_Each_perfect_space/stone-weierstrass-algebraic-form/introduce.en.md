Theorem 8.2 is the classical algebraic form of the Stone-Weierstrass theorem, replacing the lattice hypothesis of 8.1 with the assumption that $F$ is a subalgebra. The proof in this text, following de Branges, uses the Krein-Milman theorem on the set $F^\circ \cap [-e, e]^\circ$ of Radon measures and is a beautiful application of functional-analytic rather than purely topological methods. The result also extends to complex-valued functions $\mathcal{C}_{\mathbb{C}}(X)$.
