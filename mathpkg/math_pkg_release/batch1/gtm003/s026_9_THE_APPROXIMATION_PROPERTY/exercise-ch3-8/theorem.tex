Let $E$ be a Banach space with the approximation property, and let $u: E \rightarrow E$ be a nuclear operator. Show that the trace of $u$, defined as $\sum_{n} \langle x_n', u(x_n) \rangle$ for a nuclear representation $u(x) = \sum_n \lambda_n \langle x_n', x \rangle y_n$, is independent of the representation.
