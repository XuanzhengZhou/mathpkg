An exercise demonstrating that nuclear maps between Banach spaces admit a factorization through a Hilbert space. This is related to Pietsch's factorization theorem for absolutely $p$-summing operators. The factorization property is characteristic of nuclear maps and explains why Hilbert space methods are so effective in the study of nuclear operators on Banach spaces.
