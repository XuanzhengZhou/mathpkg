Let $E$ be a Banach space and let $E'$ be its strong dual. Consider the following assertions:

\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $E$ has the approximation property.
\item[(b)] For every Banach space $F$, the closure of $F' \otimes E$ in $\mathcal{L}_b(F, E)$ is identical with the space of compact maps in $\mathcal{L}(F, E)$.
\item[(c)] $E'$ has the approximation property.
\item[(d)] For every Banach space $F$, the closure of $E' \otimes F$ in $\mathcal{L}_b(E, F)$ is identical with the space of compact maps in $\mathcal{L}(E, F)$.
\end{itemize}

Then $(a) \Leftrightarrow (b)$ and $(c) \Leftrightarrow (d)$.
