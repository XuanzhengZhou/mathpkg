Corollary 2 generalizes the result that products of Hilbert spaces have the approximation property to arbitrary projective limits of Hilbert spaces. Since projective limits of Hilbert spaces encompass a large class of locally convex spaces, this result is particularly significant. An important special case is that every nuclear space possesses the approximation property, as nuclear spaces can be realized as subspaces of projective limits of Hilbert spaces. This corollary follows immediately from the product result together with the characterization of projective limits in terms of subspaces of products (Corollary 2 of Section 7.3).
