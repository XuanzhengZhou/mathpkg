If $A \neq \emptyset$, the **convex hull** of $A$ is the set $\{\sum \lambda_v a_v\}$, where $\lambda_v > 0$, $\sum \lambda_v = 1$ and $\{a_v\}$ ranges over all non-empty finite subsets of $A$. It is the unique smallest convex set containing $A$.
