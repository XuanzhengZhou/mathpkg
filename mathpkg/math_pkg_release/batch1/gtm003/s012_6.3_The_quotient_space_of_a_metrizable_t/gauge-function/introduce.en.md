The gauge function (or Minkowski functional) $p_M$ associates to each $x$ in a vector space the infimum of positive scalars $\lambda$ for which $x$ belongs to $\lambda M$, where $M$ is a radial set. When $M$ is additionally convex and circled, the gauge becomes a semi-norm. The gauge provides a fundamental link between geometry (convex sets) and analysis (semi-norms) in the theory of locally convex spaces.
