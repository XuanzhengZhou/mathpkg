This theorem characterizes when scalar multiplication on a topological vector space $L$ over a real subfield $H \subset \mathbb{R}$ can be continuously extended to the complex extension $H(i) = H + iH$. The condition is the existence of a topological automorphism $u$ of $L$ satisfying $u^2 = -e$, which corresponds to multiplication by the imaginary unit $i$. The extension formula is $(\lambda + i\mu)x = \lambda x + \mu u(x)$.
