This is a fundamental lemma about convex sets in topological vector spaces. It states that if $x$ is an interior point of a convex set $A$ and $y$ belongs to the closure $\overline{A}$, then every point of the form $\lambda x + (1-\lambda)y$ with $0 < \lambda < 1$ is interior to $A$. The result is crucial for proving that the interior of a convex set is convex, and for establishing separation theorems.
