Let $d$ be a metric on a vector space $L$ such that under the topology $\mathfrak{T}$ generated by $d$, $L$ is a t.v.s., and such that the metric space $(L, d)$ is complete. Then the t.v.s. $(L, \mathfrak{T})$ is complete. (V. L. Klee [1].)
