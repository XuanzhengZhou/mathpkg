The topology of the subspace $N$ is the trivial topology whose only members are $N$ and $\emptyset$. If $M$ is any algebraic complementary subspace of $N$ in $L$, then $L = M \oplus N$ and $M$ is isomorphic with the Hausdorff t.v.s. associated with $L$ (use (2.4).)
