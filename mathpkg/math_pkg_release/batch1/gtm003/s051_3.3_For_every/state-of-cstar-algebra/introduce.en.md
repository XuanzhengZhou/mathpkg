A state on a $C^*$-algebra $A$ is a positive linear form of norm one. States are the fundamental objects in the representation theory of $C^*$-algebras via the GNS construction. In the unital case, the state space $S(A)$ is the intersection of the $\sigma(A', A)$-closed hyperplane $\{\phi : \phi(e) = 1\}$ with the positive cone $A'_+$, and it is $\sigma(A', A)$-compact, forming a base for the study of representations.
