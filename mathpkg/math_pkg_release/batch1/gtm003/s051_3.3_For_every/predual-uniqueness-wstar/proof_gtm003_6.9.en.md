Denoting by $(W'_n)_+$ the cone of all normal linear forms on $W$, the bijection $T_0 : V_+ \rightarrow (W'_n)_+$ extends to a linear bijection $T : V \rightarrow W'_n$. But the respective unit balls in $V$ and $W'_n$ are the polars of the unit ball $U$ of $W$ with respect to the dualities $\langle W, V \rangle$ and $\langle W, W'_n \rangle$; hence $T$ is an isometry. In particular, $W'_n$ is a Banach subspace of $W'$.
