Every $W^*$-algebra is order complete: every upward directed, order-bounded set of positive elements has a supremum, and the section filter converges to this supremum in the weak-* topology. Moreover, the supremum is compatible with the action $x \mapsto a^*xa$ for invertible $a$, and by approximation for arbitrary $a$. This property is the $W^*$-algebraic analogue of the monotone convergence theorem in measure theory.
