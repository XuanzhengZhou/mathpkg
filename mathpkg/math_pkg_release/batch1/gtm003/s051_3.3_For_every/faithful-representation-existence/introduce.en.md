Every $C^*$-algebra admits a faithful representation as a concrete algebra of bounded operators on a Hilbert space. The construction uses the Hilbert direct sum of the GNS representations associated to all states in the state space $S(A)$. This is known as the universal representation and is a cornerstone result in the theory of $C^*$-algebras, showing that the abstract definition is equivalent to the concrete operator-theoretic one.
