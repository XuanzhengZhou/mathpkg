For every $C^*$-algebra $A$, there exists a faithful representation $\pi : A \to \mathcal{L}(H)$ on a suitable Hilbert space $H$. One may take $H = \bigoplus_{\phi \in S(A)} H_\phi$ as the Hilbert direct sum of the GNS Hilbert spaces and define $\pi(x) = \bigoplus_{\phi \in S(A)} \pi_\phi(x)$ acting coordinatewise.
