A representation of a $C^*$-algebra $A$ is a *-homomorphism from $A$ into the algebra $\mathcal{L}(H)$ of bounded linear operators on a Hilbert space $H$. A representation is faithful if it is injective. A representation is cyclic if there exists a vector $\xi \in H$ whose orbit under the representation is dense in $H$. Representations are the primary tool for studying abstract $C^*$-algebras via concrete operator algebras.
