For any projection $p$ in a $W^*$-algebra $W$, the corner subalgebra $pWp$ is closed in the canonical weak-* topology and is itself a $W^*$-algebra. Moreover, the compression map $x \mapsto pxp$ is weak-* continuous. This result is important for the structure theory of $W^*$-algebras, as it shows that corners inherit the $W^*$-algebra structure.
