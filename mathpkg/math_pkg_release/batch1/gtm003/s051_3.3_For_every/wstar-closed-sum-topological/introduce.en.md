When a dual Banach space $F$ decomposes as the algebraic direct sum of two $\sigma(F, F_0)$-closed subspaces, the decomposition is automatically topological with respect to the weak-* topology: the associated linear projections onto each summand are $\sigma(F, F_0)$-continuous. This result is used repeatedly in the structure theory of $W^*$-algebras to establish separate weak-* continuity of multiplication.
