Let $0 < x \in W$; by Zorn's lemma, there exists a maximal commutative $C^*$-subalgebra $A$ containing $x$. Let $S \subset A$ be directed $(\leq)$ and let $b = \sup S \in W$. For every unitary element $u \in A$ we have, by (6.3), $u^*bu = \sup(u^*Su) = \sup S = b$; thus $b$ commutes with $A$ by (2.2), Corollary 2, hence $b \in A$. From the discussion preceding the corollary, it follows that the spectrum space $K$ of $A$ is Stonian; this proves the first assertion. The proof of the second assertion is left to the reader.
