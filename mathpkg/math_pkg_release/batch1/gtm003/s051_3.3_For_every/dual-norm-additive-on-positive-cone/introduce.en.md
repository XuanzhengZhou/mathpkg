In the dual space $A'$ of a $C^*$-algebra $A$, the operator norm is additive when restricted to the cone $A'_+$ of positive linear forms. This means that for any two positive linear forms $\phi, \psi \in A'_+$, we have $\|\phi + \psi\| = \|\phi\| + \|\psi\|$. The result is a direct consequence of the characterization of positive linear forms via approximate units (Proposition 4.1).
