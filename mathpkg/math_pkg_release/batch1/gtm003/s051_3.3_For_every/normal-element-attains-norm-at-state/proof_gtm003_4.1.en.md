We may suppose $A$ unital. The $C^*$-subalgebra $B$ of $A$ generated by $x$ and $e$ is isomorphic to $C(K)$ by (2.2), Corollary 1. If $f \in C(K)$ corresponds to $x \in B$ under this isomorphism, there exists a point functional (Dirac measure) $\delta_s$ ($s \in K$) such that $|\delta_s(f)| = |f(s)| = \|f\|$. By the Hahn-Banach theorem ((II, 3.2) Corollary) for normed spaces, there exists a norm-preserving linear extension $\phi \in A'$; we have $1 = \|\delta_s\| = \|\phi\| = \delta_s(1_K) = \phi(e)$. Now (4.1) shows $\phi$ to be positive, hence we obtain $\phi \in S(A)$.
