If $A$ is unital and $\mathcal{B}$ is a commuting family of projections containing $0$ and $e$, closed under the Boolean operations $p \vee q = p + q - pq$ and $p \wedge q = pq$, then $\mathcal{B}$ is called a \textit{Boolean algebra of projections} or a \textit{resolution of the identity} $e$. Resolutions of the identity are the principal tool in the investigation of self-adjoint and normal elements of $A$.
