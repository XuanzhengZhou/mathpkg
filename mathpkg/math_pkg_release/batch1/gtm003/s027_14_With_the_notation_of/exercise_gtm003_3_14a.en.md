With the notation of Exercise 13, let $P$ be a countable subset of $I$ and let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a countable family of orthonormal bases in $L^2(\mu)$, each of which satisfies $\limsup L_n(t) = \infty$ for all $t \in P$. Show that there exists an $f \in E$ whose Fourier expansion with respect to any member of $\mathfrak{S}$ is unbounded at each $t \in P$. (Use Exercises 12, 13.)
