This is the fundamental dimension formula for the algebraic tensor product: the dimension of $E \otimes F$ equals the product of the dimensions of $E$ and $F$ (as cardinals). This holds for both finite and infinite dimensions, using cardinal arithmetic. In particular, if $\{e_\alpha\}$ and $\{f_\beta\}$ are bases of $E$ and $F$ respectively, then $\{e_\alpha \otimes f_\beta\}$ is a basis of $E \otimes F$.
