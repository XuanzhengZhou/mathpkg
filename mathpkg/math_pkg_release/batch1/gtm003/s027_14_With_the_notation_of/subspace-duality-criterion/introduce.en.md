The dual of a subspace of a locally convex space is canonically isomorphic to the quotient of the dual by the polar of the subspace. This criterion provides a precise relationship between subspaces and quotient spaces under duality, enabling one to transfer problems about subspaces to problems about quotients of dual spaces, often simplifying computations in functional analysis.
