This proposition characterizes the rank of a tensor $u \in E \otimes F$ as the minimal number of pure tensor summands needed to represent $u$, and shows this equals the dimension of the range of an associated linear map $v: E^* \to F$. Moreover, any minimal representation necessarily involves linearly independent sets of vectors on both sides. This is a fundamental structural result about the algebraic tensor product of two vector spaces.
