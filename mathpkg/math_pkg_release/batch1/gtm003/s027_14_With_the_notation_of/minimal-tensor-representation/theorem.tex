For each $u \in E \otimes F$, the minimal number $k$ ($\geq 0$) of summands $x_i \otimes y_i$ such that $u = \sum_{i=1}^{k} x_i \otimes y_i$ is identical with the dimension $r$ of the range of the linear map $v: x^* \mapsto v(x^*) = \sum_{i=1}^{k} x^*(x_i) y_i$ from $E^*$ to $F$. In each representation of $u$ by $r$ summands, both sets $\{x_i\}$ and $\{y_i\}$ are linearly independent.
