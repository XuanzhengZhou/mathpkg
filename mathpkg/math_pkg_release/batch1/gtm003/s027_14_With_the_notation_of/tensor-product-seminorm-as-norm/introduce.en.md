This proposition states that the projective tensor product seminorm $p \otimes q$ on $E \otimes F$ is a genuine norm precisely when both $p$ and $q$ are norms (i.e., have trivial kernels). If either $p$ or $q$ is only a seminorm with nontrivial kernel, then $p \otimes q$ will also only be a seminorm, not a norm. This is fundamental for constructing normed tensor products from normed spaces.
