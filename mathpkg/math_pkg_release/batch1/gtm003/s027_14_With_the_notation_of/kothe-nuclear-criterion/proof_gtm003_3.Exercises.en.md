[This is an exercise. The proof is left to the reader, with hints: For the proof of sufficiency, use (7.2)(c). Concerning the necessity of the condition, note that the dual $E'$ of $E$ can be identified with the space of sequences, each of which is absolutely majorized by some $n\sigma_n$. If $B_n$ denotes the set of sequences $\{y_m\}$ such that $|y_m| \leq s_m^{(n)}$ for all $m$, the family $\{nB_n\}$ is a fundamental family of equicontinuous subsets of $E'$.]
