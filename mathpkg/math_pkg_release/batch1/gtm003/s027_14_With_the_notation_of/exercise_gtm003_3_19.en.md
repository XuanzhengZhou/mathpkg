Let $E_i$ ($i = 1, \ldots, n$), $G$ be vector spaces over $K$. A mapping $(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \mapsto f(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ of $\prod_i E_i$ into $G$ is \textbf{multilinear} (\textbf{$n$-linear}) if each of the partial maps $E_i \to G$, obtained by fixing the coordinates $x_j$ ($j \neq i$) of $x$, is linear. Define the tensor product $F = \bigotimes_i E_i$, and if the $E_i$ are locally convex spaces, define the projective tensor product topology on $F$. Formulate and prove for this case results analogous to those in Section 6.
