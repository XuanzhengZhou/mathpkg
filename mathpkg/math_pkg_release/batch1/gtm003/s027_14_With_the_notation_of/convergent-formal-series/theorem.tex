Let $E$ be a Hausdorff topological vector space. A formal series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} x_n$, where $x_n \in E$ ($n \in \mathbb{N}$), is \textbf{convergent} to $x \in E$ if the sequence of partial sums $s_n = \sum_{k=1}^{n} x_k$ ($n \in \mathbb{N}$) converges to $x$ in $E$. This is expressed by writing $x = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} x_n$.
