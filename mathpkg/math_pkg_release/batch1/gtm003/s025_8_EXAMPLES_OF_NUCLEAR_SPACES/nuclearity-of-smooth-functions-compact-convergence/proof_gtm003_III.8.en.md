**Proof.** The nuclearity of $\mathcal{E}$ is not verified directly here. It follows as a special case of Theorem (IV, 9.7), which characterizes nuclearity of function spaces in terms of nuclearity of their duals. Since $\mathcal{E}$ is the strong dual of a suitable nuclear LF-space, its nuclearity is a consequence of the general duality theory of nuclear spaces. $\square$
