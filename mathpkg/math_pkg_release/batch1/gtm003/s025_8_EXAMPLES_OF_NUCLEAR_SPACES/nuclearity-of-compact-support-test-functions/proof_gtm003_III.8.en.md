**Proof.** Any compact subset $C \subset \mathbb{R}^k$ is contained in some $k$-dimensional interval $I$. The space $\mathcal{D}_C$ is a closed subspace of $\mathcal{D}_I$, and $\mathcal{D}_I$ is nuclear by the preceding result. By property (7.4), closed subspaces of nuclear spaces are nuclear; hence $\mathcal{D}_C$ is nuclear. $\square$
