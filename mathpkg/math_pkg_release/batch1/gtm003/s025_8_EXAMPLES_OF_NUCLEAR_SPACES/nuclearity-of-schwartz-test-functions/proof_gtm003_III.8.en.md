**Proof.** The space $\mathcal{D}$ of L. Schwartz is, by definition, the inductive limit of a sequence of spaces $\mathcal{D}_C$ where $C$ ranges over an increasing sequence of compact subsets exhausting $\mathbb{R}^k$. Each $\mathcal{D}_C$ is nuclear by Example 1. By the corollary of (7.4), the strict inductive limit of nuclear spaces is nuclear. Hence $\mathcal{D}$ is nuclear. $\square$
