The space $\mathcal{D}_I$ consists of all smooth functions on $\mathbb{R}^k$ whose support lies inside a fixed $k$-dimensional interval $I$. Its nuclearity follows directly from the nuclearity of the test function space $\mathcal{D}_T$ on the torus via the hereditary property (7.4) of nuclear spaces. This provides a bridge between the periodic setting and the local setting on $\mathbb{R}^k$.
