This corollary of Banach's homomorphism theorem states that if a complete metrizable t.v.s. is the direct algebraic sum of two closed subspaces, the sum is automatically topological. In other words, the canonical map $(x_1, x_2) \to x_1 + x_2$ from the product space to the sum is an isomorphism.
