The Dieudonné-Schwartz homomorphism theorem extends Banach's homomorphism theorem from Fréchet spaces to (LF)-spaces. It states that every continuous linear surjection from an (LF)-space onto an (F)-space or (LF)-space is automatically open, hence a topological homomorphism.
