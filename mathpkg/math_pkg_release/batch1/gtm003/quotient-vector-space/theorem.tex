Let $L$ be a vector space over $K$ and $M$ a subspace. The set $L/M$ of equivalence classes $\hat{x} = x + M$ ($x \in L$) is a vector space over $K$ under the operations $\hat{x} + \hat{y} = \widehat{x + y}$ and $\lambda \hat{x} = \widehat{\lambda x}$. The natural map $\phi: L \to L/M$, $x \mapsto \hat{x}$, is a linear surjection with kernel $M$.
