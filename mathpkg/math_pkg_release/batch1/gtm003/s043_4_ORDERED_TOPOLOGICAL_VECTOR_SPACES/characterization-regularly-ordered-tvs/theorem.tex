For an ordered t.v.s. $L$ with positive cone $C$, the following assertions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(a)] $L$ is regularly ordered.
  \item[(b)] The order of $L$ is Archimedean, and $C$ is sequentially closed for some Hausdorff l.c. topology on $L$.
  \item[(c)] The order of $L$ is Archimedean, and $C$ is normal for some Hausdorff l.c. topology on $L$.
\end{enumerate}
