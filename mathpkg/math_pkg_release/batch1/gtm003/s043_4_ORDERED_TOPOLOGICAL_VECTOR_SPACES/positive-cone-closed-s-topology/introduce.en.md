This proposition shows that the positive cone in the space of continuous linear maps between ordered topological vector spaces is closed under the S-topology (the topology of uniform convergence on members of a saturated family S of bounded sets). The result is fundamental for studying spaces of positive operators and their topological properties. The totalness of the domain cone is characterized as the necessary and sufficient condition for the cone of positive maps to be proper.
