Let $E$ be a Banach space and let $C$ be a cone in $E$ with closure $\bar{C}$. The following assertions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(a)] $C$ is a $\mathfrak{B}$-cone in $E$.
  \item[(b)] $E = \bar{C} - \bar{C}$.
  \item[(c)] $\bar{C}$ is a strict $\mathfrak{B}$-cone in $E$.
\end{enumerate}
