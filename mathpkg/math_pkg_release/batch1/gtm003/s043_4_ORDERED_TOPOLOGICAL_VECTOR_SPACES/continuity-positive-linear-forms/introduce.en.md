This theorem gives three sufficient conditions under which every positive linear form on an ordered topological vector space is automatically continuous. The conditions cover important cases: (i) when the positive cone has nonempty interior, (ii) for complete metrizable spaces generated by their cone, and (iii) for bornological spaces where the cone is a semi-complete strict B-cone. The automatic continuity of positive linear forms is a crucial property in the theory of ordered vector spaces.
