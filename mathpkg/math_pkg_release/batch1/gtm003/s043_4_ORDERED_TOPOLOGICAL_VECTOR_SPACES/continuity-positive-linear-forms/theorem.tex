Let $E$ be an ordered t.v.s. with positive cone $C$. Each of the following conditions is sufficient to ensure the continuity of every positive linear form on $E$:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(i)] $C$ has non-empty interior.
  \item[(ii)] $E$ is metrizable and complete, and $E = C - C$.
  \item[(iii)] $E$ is bornological, and $C$ is a semi-complete strict $\mathfrak{B}$-cone.
\end{enumerate}
