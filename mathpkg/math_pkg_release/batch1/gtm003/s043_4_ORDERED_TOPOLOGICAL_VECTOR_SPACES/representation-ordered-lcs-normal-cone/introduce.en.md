This representation theorem shows that every ordered locally convex space with a normal positive cone can be embedded as an ordered topological vector subspace of the space of real-valued continuous functions on a locally compact space, equipped with the compact-open topology. The construction uses the dual cone and equicontinuous subsets to build the representing space X as a topological sum. This result generalizes the classical representation of Banach lattices as spaces of continuous functions.
