Let $S$ be a directed ($\leq$) subset of $E$ such that $x_0 = \sup S$, where $E$ is an ordered l.c.s. with normal positive cone. If for every real linear form $f$ which is positive and continuous on $E$ one has $f(x_0) = \sup \{f(x) : x \in S\}$, then $\lim g(x) = g(x_0)$ for all $g \in E'$, uniformly on each equicontinuous subset of $E'$.
