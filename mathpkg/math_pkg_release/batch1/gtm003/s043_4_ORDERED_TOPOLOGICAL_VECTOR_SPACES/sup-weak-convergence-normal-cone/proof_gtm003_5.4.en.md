In fact, in view of (3.3), Corollary 3, the weak convergence of $\mathfrak{J}(S)$ to $x_0$ is equivalent to the relation $f(x_0) = \sup \{f(x) : x \in S\}$ for every real linear form $f$ on $E$ which is positive and continuous (cf. (I, 7.2)).
