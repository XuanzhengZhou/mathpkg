Hypocontinuous bilinear maps admit unique extensions to completions while preserving hypocontinuity. This extension property is crucial for the theory of topological tensor products, as it allows one to work with complete spaces without loss of generality. The result follows from the uniform continuity of the bilinear map on bounded sets and the universal property of completions.
