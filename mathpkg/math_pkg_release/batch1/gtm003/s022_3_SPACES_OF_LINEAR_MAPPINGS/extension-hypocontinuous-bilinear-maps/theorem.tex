Let $E, F, G$ be l.c.s. and let $b: E \times F \rightarrow G$ be a separately continuous bilinear map that is hypocontinuous with respect to bounded subsets of $F$. Then $b$ extends uniquely to a separately continuous bilinear map on $\tilde{E} \times F$ that remains hypocontinuous with respect to bounded subsets of $F$, where $\tilde{E}$ denotes the completion of $E$.
