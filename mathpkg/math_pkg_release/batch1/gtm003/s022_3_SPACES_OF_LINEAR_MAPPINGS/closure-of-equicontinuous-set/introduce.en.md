This result establishes two important topological facts: first, the space of all (not necessarily continuous) linear maps is closed under pointwise limits in the product space; second, equicontinuity is preserved under taking pointwise closures. The second part is crucial for compactness arguments involving the Alaoglu-Bourbaki theorem.
