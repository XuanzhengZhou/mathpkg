Since \(E\) is a Banach space, it is complete and hence a Baire space. The hypotheses of Theorem 4.6 are satisfied with \(E\) barreled (every Banach space is barreled). The conclusion follows directly from Theorem 4.6, noting that in a complete space, precompact subsets are precisely those with compact closure, so uniform convergence on precompact sets implies uniform convergence on compact sets.
