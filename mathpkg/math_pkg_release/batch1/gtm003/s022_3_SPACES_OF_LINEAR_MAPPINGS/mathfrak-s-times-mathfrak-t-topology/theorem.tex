Let $E, F, G$ be t.v.s. and let $\mathfrak{S}$, $\mathfrak{T}$ be families of bounded subsets of $E$ and $F$ respectively. The $(\mathfrak{S} \times \mathfrak{T})$-topology on the space $\mathcal{B}(E, F; G)$ of continuous bilinear maps is the topology of uniform convergence on sets $S \times T$ where $S \in \mathfrak{S}$ and $T \in \mathfrak{T}$.
