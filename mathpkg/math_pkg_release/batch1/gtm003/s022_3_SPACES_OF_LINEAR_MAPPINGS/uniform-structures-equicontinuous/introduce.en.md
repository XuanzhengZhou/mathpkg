On an equicontinuous set \(H\), the topology of pointwise convergence on a total subset already captures the full structure of uniform convergence on precompact sets. This is a key technical result used to prove metrizability and separability of equicontinuous sets, as well as in the proof of the generalized Banach-Steinhaus theorem.
