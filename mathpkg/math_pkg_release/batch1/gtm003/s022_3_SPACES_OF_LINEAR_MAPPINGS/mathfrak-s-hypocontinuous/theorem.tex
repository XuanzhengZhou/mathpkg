A bilinear map $b: E \times F \rightarrow G$ is $\mathfrak{S}$-\text{hypocontinuous} (with respect to a family $\mathfrak{S}$ of bounded subsets of $E$) if $b$ is separately continuous and for every $0$-neighborhood $W$ in $G$ and every $S \in \mathfrak{S}$, there exists a $0$-neighborhood $V$ in $F$ such that $b(S \times V) \subset W$.
