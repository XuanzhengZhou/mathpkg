Proposition 4.1 provides two useful reformulations of equicontinuity for a set \(H\) of continuous linear maps. Condition (b) states that the intersection of the inverse images of any \(0\)-neighborhood is again a \(0\)-neighborhood, while condition (c) asserts the existence of a \(0\)-neighborhood whose image under all maps in \(H\) is contained in a given \(0\)-neighborhood of the target.
