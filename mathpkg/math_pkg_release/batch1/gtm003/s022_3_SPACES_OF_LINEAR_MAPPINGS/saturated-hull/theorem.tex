Since the family of all bounded subsets of \(E\) is saturated and since the intersection of any non-empty collection of saturated families is saturated, a given family \(\mathfrak{S}\) of bounded sets in \(E\) determines a smallest saturated family \(\overline{\mathfrak{S}}\) containing it; \(\overline{\mathfrak{S}}\) is called the \textit{saturated hull} of \(\mathfrak{S}\).
