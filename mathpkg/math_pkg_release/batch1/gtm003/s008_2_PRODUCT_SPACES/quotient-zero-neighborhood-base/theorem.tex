Let $(L, \mathfrak{T})$ be a topological vector space, let $M$ be a subspace of $L$, and let $\phi : L \to L/M$ be the canonical quotient map. If $\mathfrak{B}$ is a $0$-neighborhood base in $L$, then the family $\phi(\mathfrak{B}) = \{\phi(B) : B \in \mathfrak{B}\}$ is a $0$-neighborhood base in the quotient space $L/M$.
