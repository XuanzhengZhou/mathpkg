The resolvent set of an element $x$ in a unital Banach algebra consists of those complex numbers $\lambda$ for which $\lambda e - x$ is invertible. The complement, called the spectrum, is always a non-empty compact subset of $\mathbf{C}$. This generalizes the notion of eigenvalues from linear operators to abstract Banach algebra elements.
