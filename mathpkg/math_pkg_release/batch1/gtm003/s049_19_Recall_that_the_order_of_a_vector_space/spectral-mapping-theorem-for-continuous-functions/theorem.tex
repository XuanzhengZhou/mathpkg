Let $A$ be a unital $C^*$-algebra, $a \in A$ a normal element, and let $B$ be the $C^*$-subalgebra generated by $\{e, a, a^*\}$. Then there exists an isometric $*$-isomorphism $\Psi : \mathcal{C}(X) \to B$ where $X = \sigma(a)$, such that $\Psi(1_X) = e$ and $\Psi(\mathrm{id}_X) = a$. Moreover, for any $f \in \mathcal{C}(X)$,

$$\sigma(f(a)) = f(\sigma(a)).$$
