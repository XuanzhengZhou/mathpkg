The bounded linear operators $\mathcal{L}(H)$ on a Hilbert space $H$ form a non-commutative W*-algebra with the operator norm and the adjoint as involution. The compact operators form a distinguished closed two-sided ideal, which is proper precisely when $H$ is infinite-dimensional.
