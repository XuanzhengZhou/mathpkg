Every closed left ideal in a C*-algebra admits an approximate unit: an increasing sequence of positive elements $e_n$ with spectrum in $[0,1]$ such that $x e_n 	o x$ for every $x$ in the ideal. This is a fundamental structural result with far-reaching consequences for the theory of ideals and quotients.
