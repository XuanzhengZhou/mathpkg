Since $\|x\|^2 = \|x^*x\| = \|e\| = 1$, we have $\|x\| = 1$, so $r(x) \leq 1$. Also $x^{-1} = x^*$ is unitary with $\|x^{-1}\| = 1$, so $\lambda \in \sigma(x)$ implies $\lambda^{-1} \in \sigma(x^{-1})$ and $|\lambda^{-1}| \leq 1$. Thus $|\lambda| \geq 1$. Combined with $r(x) \leq 1$, we have $|\lambda| = 1$ for all $\lambda \in \sigma(x)$.
