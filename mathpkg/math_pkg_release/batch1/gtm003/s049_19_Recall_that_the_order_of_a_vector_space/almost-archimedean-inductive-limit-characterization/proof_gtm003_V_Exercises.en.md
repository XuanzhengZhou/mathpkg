The order-bound dual $L^b$ distinguishes points, so the natural embedding $L \to (L^b)^*$ holds. Represent $L$ as the inductive limit of the family of order ideals generated by order intervals $[-y, y]$ for $y \in L^+$, each equipped with the norm $p_y(x) = \inf\{\lambda > 0 : -\lambda y \leq x \leq \lambda y\}$. This is analogous to Theorem (6.3) in the text. When $L$ is a vector lattice, the cone is normal under this inductive limit topology, yielding a l.c.v.l.
