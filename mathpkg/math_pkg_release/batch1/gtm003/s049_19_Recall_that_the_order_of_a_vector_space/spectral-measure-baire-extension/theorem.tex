There exists a unique extension $\bar{\mu}$ of a spectral measure $\mu$ to the Banach algebra $\mathcal{B}(X)$ of bounded Baire functions on $X$, such that $\bar{\mu}$ is an algebraic homomorphism of $\mathcal{B}(X)$ into $A$. Moreover, $\bar{\mu}$ induces a homomorphism of the Boolean algebra of Baire subsets of $X$ onto a $\sigma$-complete Boolean algebra of idempotents of $A$.
