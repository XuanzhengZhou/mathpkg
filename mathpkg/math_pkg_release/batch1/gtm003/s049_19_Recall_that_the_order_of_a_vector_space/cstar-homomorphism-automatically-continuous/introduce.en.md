Homomorphisms between C*-algebras enjoy remarkable automatic continuity properties: they are always contractive, open onto their image, and the image is a C*-subalgebra. An injective *-homomorphism is automatically an isometry. This is a sharp contrast with general Banach algebras, where homomorphisms need not be continuous.
