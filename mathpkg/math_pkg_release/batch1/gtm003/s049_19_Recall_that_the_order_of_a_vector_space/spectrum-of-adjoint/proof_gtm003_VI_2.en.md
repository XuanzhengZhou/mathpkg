Since $(\lambda e - x)R(\lambda, x) = e$ implies $(\overline{\lambda}e - x^*)R(\lambda, x)^* = e^* = e$. Thus $\lambda \in \rho(x)$ if and only if $\overline{\lambda} \in \rho(x^*)$, giving the desired equality for spectra.
