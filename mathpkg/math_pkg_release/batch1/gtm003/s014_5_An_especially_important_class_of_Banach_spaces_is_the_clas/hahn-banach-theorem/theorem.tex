Let $L$ be a vector space, let $p$ be a semi-norm on $L$, and let $M$ be a subspace of $L$. If $f$ is a linear form on $M$ such that
$$|f(x)| \leq p(x) \quad \text{for all } x \in M,$$
there exists a linear form $f_1$ extending $f$ to $L$ and such that
$$|f_1(x)| \leq p(x) \quad \text{for all } x \in L.$$
