Analytically, a locally convex topology on $E$ is determined by an arbitrary family $\{p_\alpha : \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ of semi-norms. For each $\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$ and each $\varepsilon > 0$, the sets
$$\{x \in E : p_\alpha(x) < \varepsilon\}$$
form a subbase of $0$-neighborhoods, and the topology generated by this subbase is the unique locally convex topology for which each $p_\alpha$ is continuous.
