Let $L$ be a topological vector space and let $L$ be the algebraic direct sum of the subspaces $M, N$. Then $L$ is the topological direct sum of $M$ and $N$: $L = M \oplus N$, if and only if the mapping $v$ which orders to each equivalence class $\text{mod } M$ its unique representative in $N$ is an isomorphism of the t.v.s. $L/M$ onto the t.v.s. $N$.
