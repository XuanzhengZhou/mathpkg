Let $M$ have dimension $n$ and let $\{f_\mu\}$ be $n$ continuous linear forms on $E$ whose restrictions to $M$ are linearly independent (cf. Corollary 1). Then

$$
N = \bigcap_{\mu=1}^{n} f_\mu^{-1}(0)
$$

is a closed subspace of $E$ (as an intersection of closed hyperplanes) and an algebraic complementary subspace of $M$. The assertion follows from (I, 3.5).
