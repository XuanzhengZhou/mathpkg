If $p$ is any member of a family $\mathcal{P}$ of generating semi-norms on $E$, $p$ has a unique continuous extension $\bar{p}$ to $\tilde{E}$ by (1.6). If $U = \{x \in E: p(x) \leq 1\}$, then $\bar{U} = \{x \in \tilde{E}: \bar{p}(x) \leq 1\}$ is the closure of $U$ in $\tilde{E}$. It follows from (I, 1.5) that $\tilde{E}$ is a locally convex space (since $\bar{U}$ is convex) and that $\{\bar{p}: p \in \mathcal{P}\}$ generates the topology of $\tilde{E}$.
