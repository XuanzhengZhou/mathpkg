Let $E$ be any vector space with algebraic dual $E^*$, and suppose that $M$ is a non-empty subset of $E^*$. The semi-norms
$$
p_f(x) = |f(x)| \quad (f \in M)
$$
generate a locally convex topology on $E$. Under this topology, $E$ is a locally convex space if and only if $M$ separates the points of $E$ (i.e., for every $x \neq 0$ in $E$ there exists $f \in M$ such that $f(x) \neq 0$).
