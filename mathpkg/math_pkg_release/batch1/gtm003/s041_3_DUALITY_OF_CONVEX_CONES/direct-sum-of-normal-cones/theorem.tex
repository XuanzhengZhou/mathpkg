Let $\{L_\alpha : \alpha \in \mathbf{A}\}$ be a family of locally convex spaces, $C_\alpha$ a cone in $L_\alpha$ for each $\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$, and let $L = \bigoplus_\alpha L_\alpha$ be the locally convex direct sum of this family. Then $C = \bigoplus_\alpha C_\alpha$ is a normal cone in $L$ if and only if $C_\alpha$ is normal in $L_\alpha$ for all $\alpha \in \mathbf{A}$.
