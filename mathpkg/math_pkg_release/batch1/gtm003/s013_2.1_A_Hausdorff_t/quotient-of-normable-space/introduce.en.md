This theorem shows that normability is preserved under forming quotients by closed subspaces. Given a normed space $(L, \|\cdot\|)$, the quotient $L/M$ (with $M$ closed) is normed via the infimum norm on cosets, and this quotient norm generates the quotient topology. Moreover, completeness is also preserved: if $L$ is a Banach space, then $L/M$ is a Banach space as well.
