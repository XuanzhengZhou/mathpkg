This proposition characterizes when an algebraic direct sum decomposition of a topological vector space is also a topological direct sum. The condition is expressed in terms of the mapping that selects the unique representative in $N$ for each coset in $L/M$. This provides a bridge between the algebraic and topological structures of direct sum decompositions.
