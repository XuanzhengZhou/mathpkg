This theorem shows that every one-dimensional Hausdorff topological vector space over $K$ is isomorphic to the canonical one-dimensional space $K_0$. The isomorphism is given by scalar multiplication with any non-zero element. This result is fundamental for the structure theory of finite-dimensional t.v.s., as it provides the base case for induction arguments.
