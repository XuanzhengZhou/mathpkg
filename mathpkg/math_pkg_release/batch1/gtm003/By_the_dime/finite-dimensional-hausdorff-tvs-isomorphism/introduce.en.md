This fundamental theorem classifies all finite-dimensional Hausdorff topological vector spaces over a complete field $K$: every such space of dimension $n$ is isomorphic to $K_0^n$ with the product topology. It follows that the product topology is the unique Hausdorff vector topology on a finite-dimensional space over a complete field. This is a cornerstone result in the theory of topological vector spaces.
