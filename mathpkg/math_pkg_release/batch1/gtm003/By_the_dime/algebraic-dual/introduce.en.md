The algebraic dual $L^*$ of a vector space $L$ over $K$ is the space of all linear forms (linear functionals) on $L$. It is a right vector space over $K$ under pointwise operations. The algebraic dual is a fundamental construction that underlies the geometric theory of hyperplanes and the Hahn-Banach theorem in topological vector spaces.
