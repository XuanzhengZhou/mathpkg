Let $\mu$ be a positive Radon measure on a locally compact space $T$, and let $E$ be a locally convex space. Denote by $L^1(\mu)$ the space of $\mu$-integrable scalar functions and by $L^1_E(\mu)$ the space of $\mu$-integrable $E$-valued functions (constructed via completion of the associated Hausdorff t.v.s. of step functions).

The natural linear imbedding $L^1(\mu) \otimes E \hookrightarrow L^1_E(\mu)$ defined by
$$f \otimes x \mapsto [t \mapsto f(t) \cdot x]$$
induces an isomorphism of locally convex spaces
$$L^1(\mu) \,\tilde{\otimes}_\pi\, E \;\cong\; L^1_E(\mu),$$
where $L^1(\mu) \,\tilde{\otimes}_\pi\, E$ denotes the completion of $L^1(\mu) \otimes E$ under the projective tensor product topology.

If $E$ is a Banach space, this isomorphism is norm-preserving with respect to the projective norm on the tensor product and the usual $L^1$-norm on $L^1_E(\mu)$ given by $\|f\| = \int \|f(t)\|_E \, d\mu(t)$.
