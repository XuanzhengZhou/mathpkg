Theorem 6.5 identifies the completed projective tensor product $L^1(\mu) \tilde{\otimes}_\pi E$ with the space $L^1_E(\mu)$ of Bochner-integrable $E$-valued functions. The natural imbedding sends an elementary tensor $f \otimes x$ to the vector-valued function $t \mapsto f(t)x$, and this extends to an isomorphism of locally convex spaces. When $E$ is a Banach space, the isomorphism is norm-preserving. This result is fundamental in vector-valued integration theory and is proved by showing that on the dense subspace of simple (step) functions, the projective tensor norm coincides with the $L^1$-norm of the vector-valued function.
