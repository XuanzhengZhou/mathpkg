This equivalence is established in the coordinatization sections of Chapter IV. If the plane is $(k,k)$-transitive for $k$, then choosing $k = [\infty]$ in the coordinatization yields a PTR with associative addition satisfying the group axioms; the ternary operation reduces to a binary operation giving a quasifield structure. The translation group with axis $k$ provides the additive group. That $(V,k)$-transitivity for two distinct points $V$ on $k$ implies $(k,k)$-transitivity is a consequence of the group generated by the corresponding central collineations. Conversely, if the PTR is a quasifield, the plane is a translation plane with respect to the line at infinity, giving the required transitivity.
