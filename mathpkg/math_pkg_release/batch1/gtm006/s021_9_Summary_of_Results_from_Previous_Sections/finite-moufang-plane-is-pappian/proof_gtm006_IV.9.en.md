This corollary follows from the theorem on maximal Moufang subplanes proved in the preceding sections, together with the classification of alternative division rings. In a finite Moufang plane, every coordinating planar ternary ring is a finite alternative division ring. By the Artin-Zorn theorem, every finite alternative division ring is a field. Hence the plane is pappian. Since every pappian plane is desarguesian, the plane is also desarguesian.
