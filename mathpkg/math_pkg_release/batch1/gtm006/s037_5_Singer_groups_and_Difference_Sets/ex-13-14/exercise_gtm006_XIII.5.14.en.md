Show that if $\mathcal{P}$ is any non-empty set of points and lines with an incidence relation such that any pair of distinct points (lines) are on exactly one line (point), then if every line has more than two points and every point is on more than two lines, it follows that $\mathcal{P}$ is a projective plane.
