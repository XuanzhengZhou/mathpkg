Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a finite projective plane of order $n$ and suppose that $\Gamma$ is an abelian Singer group for $\mathcal{P}$. Let $\Phi$ be the group of all collineations of $\mathcal{P}$ generated by the mappings $\bar{\phi}$ of Theorem 13.14 as $p$ varies over all prime divisors of $n$. Then there exists a line of $\mathcal{P}$ which is fixed by all elements of $\Phi$.
