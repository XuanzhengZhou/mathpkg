This result collects four fundamental properties of finite Galois fields $GF(q)$, where $q = p^n$ is a prime power. Part (i) gives the characteristic and prime subfield. Part (ii) asserts the multiplicative group is cyclic -- a crucial fact for many constructions. Parts (iii) and (iv) describe the quadratic structure: every element is a square in characteristic 2, exactly half the nonzero elements are squares otherwise, and every element is a sum of two squares in all cases.
