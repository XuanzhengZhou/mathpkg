For the quadratic extension $GF(q^2)$ of the finite field $GF(q)$, the Frobenius automorphism $x \mapsto x^q$ has order 2. The norm map $N(x) = x \cdot x^q = x^{q+1}$ sends every element of $GF(q^2)$ to an element of the subfield $GF(q)$, and every element of $GF(q)$ arises as a norm. This result is fundamental for the theory of unitary polarities and unitals in projective planes.
