Result 14.4 combines Results 14.1–14.3 to derive a powerful conclusion: if a doubly transitive permutation group on $n+1$ points (with $n$ odd) has stabilizers containing even-order Frobenius subgroups whose kernels are normal in the stabilizer, then those kernels are elementary abelian and $n$ is a prime power. This result is the central group-theoretic fact used in Theorem 14.9 to handle the odd-order case of Wagner's Theorem.
