The Corollary to Theorem 14.6 provides explicit formulas for the orders of various stabilizers in a line-transitive collineation group of a finite affine plane of order $n$. These formulas relate the total group order, stabilizers of points at infinity, stabilizers of affine lines, and stabilizers of affine flags through a single parameter $k = |\Pi_{C,l}|$. These counting relations are used extensively in the proof of Wagner's Theorem to derive divisibility constraints that force $n$ to be a prime power.
