Result 14.4 is the key group-theoretic ingredient in Wagner's Theorem. It forces the translation group of the affine plane to be elementary abelian, which implies the plane can be coordinatized by a vector space over a field. The proof combines the Feit-Thompson theorem (solvability of groups of odd order), Frobenius' theorem, and properties of primitive permutation groups.
