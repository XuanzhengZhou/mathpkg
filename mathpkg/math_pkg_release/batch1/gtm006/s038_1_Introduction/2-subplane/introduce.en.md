A 2-subplane is a subplane of a projective plane whose points and lines are exactly the fixed elements of some 2-subgroup of a given collineation group. Since the trivial group is a 2-group, the whole projective plane is always a 2-subplane of itself. A minimal 2-subplane is one containing no proper subplane with the same property; such minimal 2-subplanes always exist in finite projective planes.
