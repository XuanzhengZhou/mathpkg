Result 14.5 is a basic but frequently used fact in permutation group theory: the set of points fixed by a normal subgroup is invariant under the action of the whole group. This elementary observation plays an important technical role in Wagner's proof, where it is used to show that certain collineations must fix all points of a line at infinity.
