Lemma 14.12 establishes the existence of a rich supply of involutory homologies in the stabilizer of a minimal 2-subplane. Part (i) guarantees that for every affine flag there is an involutory homology fixing that flag. Part (ii) is a propagation property: if an involutory homology fixes a special point $A$, a point $B$ at infinity, and one line through $B$, then there exist involutory homologies fixing $A$, $B$, and any other line through $B$. This lemma provides the structural input needed for Lemma 14.13's classification.
