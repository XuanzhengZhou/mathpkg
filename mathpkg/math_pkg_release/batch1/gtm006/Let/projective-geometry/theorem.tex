Let $K$ be a skewfield and $V$ a finite dimensional (left or right) vector space over $K$. The collection of subspaces of $V$ together with the natural containment relation is called the projective geometry $\mathcal{P}(V)$. We say that the subspace $W$ is incident with the subspace $U$ if either $W$ contains $U$, or $U$ contains $W$.
