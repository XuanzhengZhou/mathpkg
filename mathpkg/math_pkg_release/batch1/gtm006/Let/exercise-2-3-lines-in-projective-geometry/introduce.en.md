Exercise 2.3 examines the possible incidence relations between two distinct lines in a projective geometry $\mathcal{P}(V)$. The exercise shows that either the lines intersect in a unique point (and then span a unique plane), or they are skew and are contained in a unique subspace of $g$-dimension three. This result is foundational for understanding the lattice structure of projective spaces.
