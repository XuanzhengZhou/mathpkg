Exercise 14.1 asks to show that the full collineation group of any Hall plane of order greater than $9$ is not transitive on its affine lines. This demonstrates that Hall planes (which are translation planes) typically do not satisfy the hypothesis of Wagner's Theorem, so Wagner's Theorem gives a proper subclass of translation planes characterized by line-transitivity.
