Lemma 14.8 is a key reduction in the proof of Wagner's Theorem. It states that if a finite affine plane of order $n$ has a collineation group transitive on affine lines, and if $n$ is either even or a prime power, then the plane is already a translation plane (and the group contains all translations). This handles the "easy" cases of Wagner's Theorem. The remaining and hardest part of the chapter is devoted to showing that if $n$ is odd, then $n$ must be a prime power, after which this lemma applies to complete the proof. The proof uses Sylow theory and the classification of involutions in projective planes from Theorem 4.2.
