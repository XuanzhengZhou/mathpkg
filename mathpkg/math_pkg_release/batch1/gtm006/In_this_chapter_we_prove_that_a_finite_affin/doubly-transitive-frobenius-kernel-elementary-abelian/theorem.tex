Let $\Pi$ be a doubly transitive permutation group on a set $\mathcal{S}$ of $n+1$ elements, where $n$ is odd. Further let the subgroup $\Pi_a$ contain a subgroup $\Gamma_a$ of even order which is a Frobenius group on $\mathcal{S} \setminus \{a\}$. If $\Sigma_a$, the kernel of $\Gamma_a$, is normal in $\Pi_a$ then $\Sigma_a$ is elementary abelian and $n$ is a prime power.
