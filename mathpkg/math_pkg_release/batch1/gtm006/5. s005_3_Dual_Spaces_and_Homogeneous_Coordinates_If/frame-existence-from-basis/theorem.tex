Let $V$ be an $(n+1)$-dimensional left vector space over a skewfield $K$ and let $e_1, e_2, \dots, e_{n+1}$ be a basis for $V$. Then
$$
E_1 = \langle e_1 \rangle,\; E_2 = \langle e_2 \rangle,\; \dots,\; E_{n+1} = \langle e_{n+1} \rangle,\; E_{n+2} = \langle e_1 + e_2 + \cdots + e_{n+1} \rangle
$$
is a frame for $\mathcal{P}(V)$.
