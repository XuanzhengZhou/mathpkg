This four-variable identity relates associators with different middle arguments $y$ and $w$ to right-multiplications by $z$. It is derived from the fact that $[x, y+w, z] \in A(y+w, z)$ and the defining property of the auxiliary set $A$. This identity is used in later stages of the proof to manipulate expressions involving multiple associators.
