Theorem 6.17 establishes that every alternative division ring possesses the inverse property (IP). Together with the Skornyakov-San Soucie Theorem (RIP implies alternative) and Lemma 6.13 (RIP implies RA and the Moufang identity), this gives a chain of implications connecting the right inverse property, alternativity, and the full inverse property in division rings. The proof uses the $k$-function and its alternating property (Lemma 19) to show that the defining identities of IP hold in any alternative division ring.
