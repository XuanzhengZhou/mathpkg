By expanding $[a, bc] = a(bc) - (bc)a$ and adding and subtracting appropriate terms, then using additivity of the commutator, one obtains:
$$[a, bc] = a(bc) - (bc)a = (ab)c - (ab)c + a(bc) - b(ca) + b(ca) - (bc)a = [a, b]c + b[a, c].$$
The identity is verified by expanding and canceling intermediate terms. $\square$
