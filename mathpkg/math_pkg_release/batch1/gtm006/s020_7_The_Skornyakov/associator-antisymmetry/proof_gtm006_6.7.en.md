By (RA), $[x, y, y] = 0$ for all $x, y \in D$. Then
$$0 = [a, b + c, b + c] = [a, b, b] + [a, b, c] + [a, c, b] + [a, c, c] = [a, b, c] + [a, c, b].$$
Hence $[a, b, c] = -[a, c, b]$. $\square$
