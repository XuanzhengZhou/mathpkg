Lemma 11.10 generalizes Lemma 11.9 from non-degenerate finite open configurations to arbitrary openly finite (o.f.) configurations. It states that every o.f. configuration is free equivalent to a canonical form in which all elements outside the core lie on a single line. The number of such extra elements equals the open rank. Together with Lemma 11.9, this provides the essential reduction needed to prove Theorem 11.11, which characterizes when two free closures are isomorphic.
