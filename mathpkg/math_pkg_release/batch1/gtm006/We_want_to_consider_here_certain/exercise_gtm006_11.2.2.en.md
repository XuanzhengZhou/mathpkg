Consider the following confined configuration with eight points and eight lines: the points are the symbols $0, 1, \dots, 7$, and the lines are the point sets
$$ \{0, 1, 2\},\; \{5, 6, 7\},\; \{0, 6, 3\},\; \{5, 1, 4\},\; \{1, 7, 3\},\; \{6, 2, 4\},\; \{0, 7, 4\},\; \{5, 2, 3\}. $$
Show that this example is a sub-configuration of $A_2(3)$.
