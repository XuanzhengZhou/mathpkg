Lemma 11.1 establishes two fundamental lattice-theoretic properties of the classes of open and confined configurations. Open configurations are closed under arbitrary intersections: any intersection of open sub-configurations remains open. Confined configurations are closed under arbitrary unions: any union of confined sub-configurations remains confined. These closure properties are central to the structure theory of free closures and underpin the characterization results in the remainder of the chapter.
