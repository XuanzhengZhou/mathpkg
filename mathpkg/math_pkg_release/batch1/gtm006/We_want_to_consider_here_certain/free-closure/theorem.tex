Let $\mathcal{C}$ be any configuration of points and lines with an incidence relation. Generate successively larger configurations by introducing, at each step, a new element defined to be the meet of exactly two existing elements that do not yet have an element in common (or dually, the join of two elements). The union of this inductive process is called the \textbf{free closure} of the given configuration $\mathcal{C}$ and is (in general) a projective plane.
