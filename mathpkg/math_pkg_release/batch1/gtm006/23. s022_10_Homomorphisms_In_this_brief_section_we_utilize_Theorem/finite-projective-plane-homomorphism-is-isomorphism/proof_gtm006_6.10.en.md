This is an immediate corollary of Theorem 6.21. By Theorem 6.21, if $\phi$ is not an isomorphism, then the pre-image of any element of $\mathcal{P}_2$ consists of infinitely many elements. But $\mathcal{P}_1$ is finite, so no element of $\mathcal{P}_2$ can have an infinite pre-image. Therefore $\phi$ must be an isomorphism.
