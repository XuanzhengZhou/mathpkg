This corollary to Theorem 10.18 establishes that for finite Hall planes of order greater than 9, the full collineation group is exactly the inherited group from the desarguesian plane under derivation. In other words, every collineation of such a Hall plane comes from a collineation of the original desarguesian plane that preserves the derivation set. This result means that to understand the full symmetry group of a Hall plane, it suffices to study the subgroup of the desarguesian collineation group that stabilizes the derivation set.
