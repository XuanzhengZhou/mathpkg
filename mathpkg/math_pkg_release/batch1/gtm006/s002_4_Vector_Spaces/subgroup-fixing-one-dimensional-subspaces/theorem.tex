Let $V$ be a finite-dimensional left (or right) vector space over the skewfield $K$. Let $N = \{\gamma \in \Gamma L(V) \mid \langle v^\gamma \rangle = \langle v \rangle \text{ for all } v \in V\}$ be the subgroup fixing every one-dimensional subspace. Then $N \cong K^*$, and if $\dim V > 1$, then $N \cap GL(V) \cong Z(K^*)$, the centre of the multiplicative group of $K$.
