Let $V$ and $W$ be left vector spaces over the skewfield $K$, and let $\alpha$ be an automorphism of $K$. A mapping $\phi: V \to W$ is a semi-linear transformation with associated automorphism $\alpha$ if:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $(v_1 + v_2)^\phi = v_1^\phi + v_2^\phi$ for all $v_1, v_2 \in V$,
\item[(ii)] $(kv)^\phi = k^\alpha v^\phi$ for all $v \in V$, $k \in K$.
\end{itemize}
If $\alpha$ is the identity, $\phi$ is a linear transformation.
