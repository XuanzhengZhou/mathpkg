If $V$ is a finite-dimensional left or right vector space over the skewfield $K$, then $\Gamma L(V) = A \cdot GL(V)$, where $GL(V)$ is normal in $\Gamma L(V)$, $A \cap GL(V) = \{1\}$, and $A \cong \operatorname{Aut}(K)$. Thus $\Gamma L(V)$ is the semidirect product of $GL(V)$ by $\operatorname{Aut}(K)$.
