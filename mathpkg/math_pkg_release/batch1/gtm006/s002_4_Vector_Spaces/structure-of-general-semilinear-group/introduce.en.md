Result 1.21 decomposes the general semi-linear group as a semidirect product: every semi-linear transformation is the composition of a linear transformation and an automorphism-induced map. This structural result is foundational for analyzing collineation groups of Desarguesian projective planes and understanding which collineations are induced by linear versus truly semi-linear transformations.
