The dual space $V'$ inverts the left/right orientation: if $V$ is a left vector space, $V'$ is a right vector space, and vice versa. This alternation is fundamental to projective duality, where the dual of a projective space $\mathcal{P}(V)$ is $\mathcal{P}(V')$. The bidual $V''$ is naturally isomorphic to $V$ when $V$ is finite-dimensional, even over a skewfield.
