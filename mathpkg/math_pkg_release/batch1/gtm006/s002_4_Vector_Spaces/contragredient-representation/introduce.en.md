The contragredient representation (Result 1.26) establishes a natural isomorphism between the semi-linear groups of $V$ and its dual $V'$. This isomorphism is fundamental for studying correlations and polarities of projective spaces: a correlation is essentially an isomorphism between the projective geometry of $V$ and that of its dual, induced by a semi-linear transformation combined with the contragredient map.
