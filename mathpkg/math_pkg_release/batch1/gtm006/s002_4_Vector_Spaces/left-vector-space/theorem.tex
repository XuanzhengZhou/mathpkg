A left vector space $V$ over the skewfield $K$ is an additive abelian group $V$ with a scalar multiplication $K \times V \to V$, written $(k, v) \mapsto kv$, satisfying for all $v, w \in V$ and $a, b \in K$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $a(v+w) = av + aw$
\item $(a+b)v = av + bv$
\item $a(bv) = (ab)v$
\item $1v = v$
\end{enumerate}
