The projective groups $P\Gamma L(V)$, $PGL(V)$, and $PSL(V)$ are obtained by factoring out the subgroup $N$ of semilinear transformations that preserve every 1-dimensional subspace (i.e., scalar multiples). This theorem identifies the induced collineation groups on $\mathcal{P}(V)$ with these quotient groups.
