Lemma 2.4 constructs a frame from any basis of the underlying vector space by taking the coordinate axes plus the diagonal point. The proof verifies that no $n+1$ of these points lie in a hyperplane. This construction shows frames always exist and provides the canonical example used in coordinatization and the proof of the Fundamental Theorem.
