Let $V$ be an $(n+1)$-dimensional left vector space over a skewfield $K$ and let $e_1, e_2, \dots, e_{n+1}$ be a basis for $V$. Then the points $E_i = \langle e_i \rangle$ for $i = 1, \dots, n+1$, together with $E_{n+2} = \langle e_1 + e_2 + \cdots + e_{n+1} \rangle$, form a frame for $\mathcal{P}(V)$.
