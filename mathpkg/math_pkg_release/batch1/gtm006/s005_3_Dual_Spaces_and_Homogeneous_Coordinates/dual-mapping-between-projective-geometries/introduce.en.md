The dual mapping $a_V$ is the geometric realization of the annihilator construction from linear algebra. It provides the fundamental connection between a projective geometry $\mathcal{P}(V)$ and its dual $\mathcal{P}(V')$. In the projective plane ($n=2$), this maps points to lines and lines to points, establishing the principle of duality: any theorem about points and lines remains true when the roles of points and lines are exchanged.
