Choose arbitrary non-zero vectors $g_i \in E_i$ for $i = 1, \dots, n+1$, and suppose $E_{n+2} = \langle x_1 g_1 + \cdots + x_{n+1} g_{n+1} \rangle$. Then define $f_i = x_i g_i$ for each $i$. The $f_i$ form the required basis. If each $x_i$ were zero, then $E_{n+2}$ would be contained in the subspace generated by the $f_j$ for $j \neq i$, implying $n+1$ points in a hyperplane.
