Theorem 2.10 identifies the projective groups $P\Gamma L(V)$, $PGL(V)$, and $PSL(V)$ as quotient groups of their linear counterparts by the subgroup of transformations that act as scalar multiplication on every vector (i.e., preserve all 1-dimensional subspaces). This is essential for understanding the structure of collineation groups of projective spaces and for computing their orders in the finite case.
