This corollary is the Fundamental Theorem of Projective Geometry specialized to automorphisms: every collineation (automorphism) of the projective plane $\mathcal{P}(V)$ is induced by a semi-linear transformation of the underlying vector space $V$. Consequently, the full automorphism group of the projective plane is precisely the projective semi-linear group $P\Gamma L(V)$. This result is the starting point for the systematic study of collineation groups of projective planes, as it reduces a geometric problem to an algebraic one involving linear algebra and field automorphisms.
