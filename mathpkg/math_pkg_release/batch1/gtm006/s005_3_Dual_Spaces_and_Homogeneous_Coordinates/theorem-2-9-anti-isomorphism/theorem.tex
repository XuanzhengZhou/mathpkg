Let $V$ be a left vector space over the skewfield $K$ and $W$ a right vector space over the skewfield $F$, each of dimension three. If there is an isomorphism from $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto $\mathcal{P}(W)$, then $K$ and $F$ are anti-isomorphic and the isomorphism from $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto $\mathcal{P}(W)$ is induced by a semi-linear transformation from $V$ onto $W$.
