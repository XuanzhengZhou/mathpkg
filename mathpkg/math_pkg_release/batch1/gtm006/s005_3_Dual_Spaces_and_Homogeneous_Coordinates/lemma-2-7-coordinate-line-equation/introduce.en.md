This lemma gives a geometric interpretation of the skewfield operations: the equation $y = xm + b$ describes the incidence of a point with coordinates $(x, y)$ on a line with parameters $m$ (slope) and $b$ (intercept). This coordinatization shows how the algebraic structure of the skewfield is reflected in geometric incidence.
