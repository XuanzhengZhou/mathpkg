Lemma 11.15 shows that the automorphism group of a configuration lifts to its full free extension. Every collineation of $\mathcal{A}$ extends uniquely to a collineation of $\mathcal{B}$ by mapping each new element (the intersection of two elements of $\mathcal{A}$) to the intersection of their images. This requires both freeness (each new element is determined by exactly two old elements) and fullness (the intersection always exists).
