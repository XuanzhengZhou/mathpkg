If $\mathcal{C}$ is a configuration with a polarity $\theta$ and if $\bar{\theta}$ is the extension of $\theta$ to $\bar{\mathcal{C}}$, the full free one-step extension of $\mathcal{C}$, show that all the absolute elements of $\bar{\theta}$ are in $\mathcal{C}$ and are absolute elements of $\theta$. Hence the extension of $\theta$ to the free closure of $\mathcal{C}$ has all its absolute elements in $\mathcal{C}$.
