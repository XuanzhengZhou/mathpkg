Corollary 1 to Lemma 2.34 shows that every nonempty conic can be parametrized by a projective line. This is a crucial structural result: despite being defined by a quadratic form on a 3-dimensional vector space, a nonempty conic has the same cardinality as a 1-dimensional projective space. The bijection is established via the polarity associated with the conic.
