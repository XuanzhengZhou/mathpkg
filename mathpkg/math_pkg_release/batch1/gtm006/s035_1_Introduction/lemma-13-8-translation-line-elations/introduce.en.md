If a collineation group contains a non-trivial elation with axis $l$ and, for each point $A$ on $l$, some line $a$ through $A$ is the axis of an elation with centre $A$, then $l$ must be a translation line. This lemma provides a powerful sufficient condition for recognizing translation planes.
