Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a finite projective plane and let $\Gamma$ be a collineation group of $\mathcal{P}$. If $l$ is a line of $\mathcal{P}$ such that (i) $\Gamma_{(l,l)} \neq 1$ and (ii) for any $A$ on $l$ there is a line $a$ through $A$ such that $\Gamma_{(a,A)} \neq 1$, then $l$ is a translation line.
