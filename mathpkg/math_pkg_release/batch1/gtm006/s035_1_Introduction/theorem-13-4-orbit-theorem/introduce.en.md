The Orbit Theorem, due to Brauer, is one of the most important results about collineation groups of finite projective planes. Any collineation group has the same number of orbits on points as on lines. The proof combines Theorem 13.3 (equal fixed points and lines for each element) with Burnside's Lemma to equate the orbit counts.
