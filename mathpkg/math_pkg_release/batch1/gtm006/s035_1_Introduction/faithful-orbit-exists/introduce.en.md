This corollary to Theorem 13.1 guarantees that every collineation group of a finite projective plane has at least one faithful orbit — an orbit on which the group acts faithfully (i.e., only the identity element fixes all elements of the orbit). If a permutation group acts faithfully on a given orbit, that orbit is called a faithful orbit.
