A proper closed Baer subset $\mathcal{B}$ of a projective plane $\mathcal{P}$ is a maximal closed configuration of $\mathcal{P}$. That is, if $\mathcal{B} \subseteq \mathcal{C}$ for any closed configuration $\mathcal{C}$ of $\mathcal{P}$, then either $\mathcal{B} = \mathcal{C}$ or $\mathcal{C} = \mathcal{P}$.
