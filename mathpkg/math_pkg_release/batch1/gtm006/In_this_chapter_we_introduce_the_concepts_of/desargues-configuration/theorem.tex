Let $\Delta_i$ ($i=1,2$) be two triangles with vertices $A_i, B_i, C_i$ and opposite edges $a_i, b_i, c_i$ such that they are in perspective from a point $V$ and a line $\ell$. Then the configuration formed by the ten points
$$V, A_1, A_2, B_1, B_2, C_1, C_2, \ell \cap a_1a_2, \ell \cap b_1b_2, \ell \cap c_1c_2$$
and the ten lines
$$\ell, VA_1A_2, VB_1B_2, VC_1C_2, A_1B_1 \cap A_2B_2, \dots$$
is called the \emph{Desargues configuration}.
