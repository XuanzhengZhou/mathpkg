Let $\Delta_i$ ($i=1,2$) be triangles with vertices $A_i, B_i, C_i$ and opposite edges $a_i, b_i, c_i$. If there is a point $V$ such that $VA_1 = VA_2$, $VB_1 = VB_2$, and $VC_1 = VC_2$, then $\Delta_1$ and $\Delta_2$ are said to be \emph{in perspective from $V$}. Being \emph{in perspective from a line $\ell$} is defined dually.
