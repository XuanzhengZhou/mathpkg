If $\alpha\beta \neq \beta\alpha$, then $\gamma = \alpha^{-1}\beta^{-1}\alpha\beta \neq 1$. The proof proceeds by analyzing the fixed structures and showing that $\gamma$ must itself be a perspectivity. Lemma 4.11 is used to track centres and axes under conjugation.
