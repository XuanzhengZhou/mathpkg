Since each point of $\ell$ other than $Q$ is the centre of $h-1$ non-trivial elations, there are at least $n(h-1)$ non-trivial elations in $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$. Thus $|\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}| \geq n(h-1) + 1 > n$. By Theorem 4.17, $|\Gamma_{(Q,\ell)}| = k > 1$. Since $Q$ is the centre of $k-1$ non-trivial elations, counting yields $k = n$.
