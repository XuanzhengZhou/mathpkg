Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a finite projective plane of order $n$ and let $\Gamma$ be a collineation group of $\mathcal{P}$. Suppose there is a line $\ell$ and a point $Q$ on $\ell$ such that $|\Gamma_{(A,\ell)}| = h > 1$ for all $A \in \ell$, $A \neq Q$. Then $|\Gamma_{(Q,\ell)}| = n$, i.e. $\mathcal{P}$ is $(Q,\ell)$-transitive.
