If $\Gamma$ is any collineation group of a projective plane $\mathcal{P}$ such that $\mathcal{F}(\Gamma)$ is a Baer subset, show that $\Gamma$ acts semi-regularly on the elements of $\mathcal{P}$ not in $\mathcal{F}(\Gamma)$.
