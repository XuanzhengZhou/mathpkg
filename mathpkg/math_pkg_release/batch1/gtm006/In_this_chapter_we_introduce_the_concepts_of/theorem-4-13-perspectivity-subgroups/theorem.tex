Let $\Gamma$ be a collineation group of a projective plane $\mathcal{P}$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] For any line $\ell$ and $k \neq \ell$, $$\Gamma_{(k,\ell)} = \bigcup_{A \in k} \Gamma_{(A,\ell)}$$ is a subgroup of $\Gamma$.
\item[(ii)] For any line $\ell$, $$\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)} = \bigcup_{X \in \ell} \Gamma_{(X,\ell)}$$ is a subgroup of $\Gamma$.
\end{enumerate}
