Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a projective plane.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] If $\ell$ and $m$ are translation lines of $\mathcal{P}$, then every line through $\ell m$ is also a translation line.
\item[(b)] If $\mathcal{P}$ has three non-concurrent translation lines, then every line of $\mathcal{P}$ is a translation line.
\end{enumerate}
