This follows directly from the definition: if $\alpha \in \Gamma_{(V,\ell)}$, then $\gamma^{-1}\alpha\gamma$ fixes $\ell^\gamma$ pointwise and $V^\gamma$ linewise, so it belongs to $\Gamma_{(V^\gamma,\ell^\gamma)}$. The reverse inclusion is symmetric.
