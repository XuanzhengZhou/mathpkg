Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a finite projective plane of order $n$ and let $\Pi = \operatorname{Aut}\mathcal{P}$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\mathcal{P}$ is $(V,\ell)$-transitive for $V \in \ell$ if and only if $|\Pi_{(V,\ell)}| = n$.
\item[(ii)] $\mathcal{P}$ is $(V,\ell)$-transitive for $V \notin \ell$ if and only if $|\Pi_{(V,\ell)}| = n-1$.
\end{enumerate}
