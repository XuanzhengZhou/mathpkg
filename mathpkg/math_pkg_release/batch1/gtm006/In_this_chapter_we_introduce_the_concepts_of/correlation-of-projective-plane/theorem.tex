A \emph{correlation} $\theta$ of a projective plane $\mathcal{P}$ is a one-to-one mapping of the points of $\mathcal{P}$ onto the lines of $\mathcal{P}$ and the lines of $\mathcal{P}$ onto the points of $\mathcal{P}$ such that incidence is preserved: if a point $P$ is on a line $\ell$, then the line $P^\theta$ contains the point $\ell^\theta$.
