By hypothesis, $\mathcal{H} = \mathcal{P}^\ell$. By Theorem 4.25, $\mathcal{H}$ is an orbit under $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$. Since $\mathcal{H}$ consists of all points not on $\ell$, the orbit-stabilizer counting forces $\Gamma_{(\ell,\ell)}$ to be the full elation group, making $\ell$ a translation line.
