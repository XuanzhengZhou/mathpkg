Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a finite projective plane. If $\alpha \neq 1$ is an $(A,a)$-elation and $\beta \neq 1$ is a $(B,b)$-homology with $B \in a$ but $A \notin b$, show that $\langle\alpha,\beta\rangle$ contains a non-identity $(B,a)$-elation.
