Let $\Delta$ have vertices $A, B, C$ and opposite sides $a, b, c$. Since $\alpha$ fixes all lines through $V$, the lines $AA^\alpha, BB^\alpha, CC^\alpha$ all pass through $V$, so the triangles are in perspective from $V$. Dually, since $\alpha$ fixes all points on $\ell$, the intersections $a \cap a^\alpha, b \cap b^\alpha, c \cap c^\alpha$ all lie on $\ell$, so they are in perspective from $\ell$.
