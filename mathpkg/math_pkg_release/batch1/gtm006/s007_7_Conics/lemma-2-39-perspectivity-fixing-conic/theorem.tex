Suppose $P_1$ and $P_2$ are two distinct points on a conic $\mathcal{C}$. Then there is a unique involutory perspectivity $\alpha$ in the orthogonal group which fixes $P_1$ and $P_2$. The centre of $\alpha$ is the intersection of the tangent lines at $P_1$ and $P_2$, and the axis is the line $P_1 + P_2$.
