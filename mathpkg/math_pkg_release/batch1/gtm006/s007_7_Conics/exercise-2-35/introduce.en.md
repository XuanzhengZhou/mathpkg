Exercise 2.35 asks the reader to verify the action of the collineation $\beta$ from Theorem 2.37 on the point at infinity $\langle(1,0,0)\rangle$. This point corresponds to the parametric coordinate $\infty$ on the conic, and the exercise confirms that under the fractional linear transformation $y \mapsto (ay+b)/(cy+d)$, the point at infinity maps to $a/c$ (or $\infty$ if $c=0$), consistent with the standard action of $PGL(2,K)$ on the projective line.
