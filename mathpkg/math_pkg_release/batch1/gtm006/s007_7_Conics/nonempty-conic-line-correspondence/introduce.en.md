Corollary 1 of Lemma 2.34 states that every nonempty conic in a projective plane has the same cardinality as a projective line. This follows from the fact that through each point of the conic, every other line meets the conic in exactly one additional point (since absolute lines meet the conic only at their unique absolute point). The bijection is established by fixing one point on the conic and projecting the remaining points onto any line not through that fixed point.
