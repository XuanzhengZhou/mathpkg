If $\mathcal{P}_1$ and $\mathcal{P}_2$ are finite, then the pre-image of any element of $\mathcal{P}_2$ under $\phi$ must be finite. By Theorem 6.21, if the pre-image of any element is finite, then $\phi$ must be an isomorphism (the alternative being that all fibers are infinite, which is impossible in a finite plane). Therefore $\phi$ is an isomorphism.
