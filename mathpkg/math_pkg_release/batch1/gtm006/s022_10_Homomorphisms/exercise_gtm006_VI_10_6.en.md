Let $(R_1, T_1)$ be the planar ternary ring of rational numbers, and let $(R_2, T_2)$ be the planar ternary ring $\mathrm{GF}(p)$, where $p$ is a prime. Let $\mathcal{P}_1$ and $\mathcal{P}_2$ be the corresponding projective planes. Show that the natural map $\mathbb{Q} \to \mathrm{GF}(p)$ induces a homomorphism from $\mathcal{P}_1$ onto $\mathcal{P}_2$.
