Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a finite projective plane. If $\Pi$ is a collineation group of $\mathcal{P}$ such that every point of $\mathcal{P}$ is the centre of a non-trivial perspectivity in $\Pi$ and every line is the axis of a non-trivial perspectivity in $\Pi$, then $\mathcal{P}$ is desarguesian and $\Pi$ contains the little projective group of $\mathcal{P}$.
