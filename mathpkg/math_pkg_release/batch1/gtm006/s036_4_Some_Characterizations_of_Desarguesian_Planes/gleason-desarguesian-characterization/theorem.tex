Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a finite projective plane and let $\Pi$ be a collineation group of $\mathcal{P}$. If every point of $\mathcal{P}$ is the centre of a non-trivial elation in $\Pi$ and if every line is the axis of a non-trivial elation in $\Pi$, then $\mathcal{P}$ is desarguesian and $\Pi$ contains the little projective group of $\mathcal{P}$.
