Every autotopism of a division ring $D$ — a triple of additive bijections $(P,Q,R)$ satisfying compatibility — corresponds to an autotopism of its coordinatized projective plane $\mathcal{P}(D)$. This natural isomorphism allows the study of division ring structure through geometric autotopisms.
