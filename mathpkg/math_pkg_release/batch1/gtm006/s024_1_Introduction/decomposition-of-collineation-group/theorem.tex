Let $\mathcal{P}(D)$ be a proper division ring plane and let $\Pi = \operatorname{Aut}(\mathcal{P}(D))$. If

$$\Sigma = \Pi_{([\infty], [\infty])} \cdot \Pi_{([\infty], [0])}$$

and if $\Lambda$ is the autotopism group of $\mathcal{P}(D)$, then
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\Pi = \Sigma \Lambda$,
\item[(ii)] $\Sigma \cap \Lambda = 1$.
\end{enumerate}
