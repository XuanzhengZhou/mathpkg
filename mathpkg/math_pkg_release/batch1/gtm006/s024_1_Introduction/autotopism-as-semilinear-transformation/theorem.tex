Let $\alpha(P, Q, R)$ be an autotopism of a division ring $D$. Then $Q$ (respectively $R$) is a semi-linear transformation of the left vector space $D$ over $N_l$, and $P$ (respectively $Q$) is a semi-linear transformation of the right vector space $D$ over $N_m$. In particular, an automorphism of $D$ is a semi-linear transformation of $D$ as a vector space over any one of its semi-nuclei.
