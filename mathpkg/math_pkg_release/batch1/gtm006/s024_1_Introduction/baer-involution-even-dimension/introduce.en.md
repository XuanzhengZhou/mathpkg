This lemma provides a necessary condition for the existence of Baer involutions in the autotopism group: the dimension of $D$ as a vector space over any semi-nucleus must be even. It follows from the fact that the fixed-point set of a Baer involution on a coordinatizing line corresponds to an eigenspace whose dimension is exactly half the total dimension.
