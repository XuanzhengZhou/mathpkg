Let $D$ be finite. If $\Delta_1$ is the normal subgroup of the autotopism group $\Delta$ consisting of all those autotopisms for which the three companion automorphisms $\alpha_r, \alpha_l, \alpha_m$ (of the semi-nuclei $N_r, N_l, N_m$ respectively) are all the identity, then $\Delta / \Delta_1$ is soluble.

**Corollary.** $\Delta$ is soluble if and only if $\Delta_1$ is soluble.
