Corollary 2 to Theorem 10.14 states that any affine plane coordinatized by a division ring $D$ of dimension two over its left or middle nucleus is derivable. This provides another large class of derivable planes beyond the right quasifield case covered by Corollary 1. The derived plane of such a division ring plane will be a translation plane, unlike the general quasifield case.
