Theorem 10.16 provides the explicit multiplication formula for the planar ternary ring $(R,T_1)$ of the derived plane when the original $(R,T)$ is a quasifield. The formula splits into two cases: when the first factor has nonzero "$t$-component" ($\beta \neq 0$), the multiplication is defined via solving two auxiliary equations in the original ring; when $\beta = 0$, the multiplication simplifies to componentwise scalar multiplication. This theorem completes the algebraic description of derivation for translation planes.
