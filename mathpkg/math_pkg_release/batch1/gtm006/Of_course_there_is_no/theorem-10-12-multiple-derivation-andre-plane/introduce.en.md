Theorem 10.12 identifies the result of applying multiple derivation to a desarguesian affine plane $\mathcal{A}_2(q^2)$. When the derivation sets $\mathcal{S}_{t_1}, \dots, \mathcal{S}_{t_r}$ are chosen corresponding to distinct elements $t_i$ of $F^*$ (where $F = GF(q)$ is the subfield of $K = GF(q^2)$), the multiply derived plane is an Andr\'{e} plane. The proof involves auxiliary results on the number of solutions to $x^{q-1}=a$ in $K$ and on linear equations over $GF(q^2)$.
