Corollary 1 to Theorem 10.14 states that any affine plane coordinatized by a right quasifield $Q$ of dimension two over its kernel is derivable. This follows directly from the general derivation theorem, as such a quasifield satisfies the algebraic conditions required in Theorem 10.14. The derived planes obtained from this construction are generally not translation planes, providing examples of planes with no translation lines or points (see Chapter IX).
