The derived plane $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}}(\mathcal{A})$ of the desarguesian affine plane $\mathcal{A} = \mathcal{A}_2(q^2)$, where $\mathcal{S}$ is the derivation set from Theorem 10.8, is a Hall plane. This follows from the analysis showing that the group induced on $\mathcal{A}_H$ by the perspectivities fixing $\mathcal{S}$ acts regularly on the points of the special line, combined with the identification of the derived plane as an Andr\'{e} plane via Theorem 10.12. The corollary is referenced to the Corollary to Theorem 9.2.
