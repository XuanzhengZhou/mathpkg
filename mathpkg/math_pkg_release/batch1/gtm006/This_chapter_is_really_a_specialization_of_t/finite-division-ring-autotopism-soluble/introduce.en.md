This theorem establishes the solubility of the autotopism group for large classes of finite division ring planes. It is well known that many finite non-desarguesian translation planes have non-soluble collineation groups, while all known finite non-desarguesian division ring planes have soluble groups. This theorem proves that this must be so when the order of the plane is not a square.
