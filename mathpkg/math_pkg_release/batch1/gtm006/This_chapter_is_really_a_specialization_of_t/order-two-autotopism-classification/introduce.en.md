This lemma gives precise information about the involutory homologies in the autotopism group of a finite division ring plane. An involution fixing the autotopism triangle must either be a homology (occurring only when the plane has odd order) or fix a Baer subplane pointwise. In the homology case, the involution is induced by multiplication by $-1$ in the semi-nuclei.
