For a proper division ring plane, the full collineation group $\Pi$ admits a semidirect product decomposition $\Pi = \Sigma times \Lambda$, where $\Sigma$ is the group generated by translations and shears, and $\Lambda$ is the autotopism group (the stabilizer of the autotopism triangle). This structural decomposition is fundamental for analyzing the collineation groups of division ring planes.
