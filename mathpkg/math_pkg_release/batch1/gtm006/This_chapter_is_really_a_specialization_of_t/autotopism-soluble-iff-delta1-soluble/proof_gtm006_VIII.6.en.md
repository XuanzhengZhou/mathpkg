By Lemma 8.14, $\Delta/\Delta_1$ is soluble. If $\Delta_1$ is soluble, then since an extension of a soluble group by a soluble group is soluble, $\Delta$ is soluble. Conversely, if $\Delta$ is soluble, then $\Delta_1$ as a subgroup of a soluble group is also soluble. $\square$
