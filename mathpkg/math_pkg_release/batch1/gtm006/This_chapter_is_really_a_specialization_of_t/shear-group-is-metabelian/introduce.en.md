In a division ring plane, a shear is an elation with centre $[\infty]$ and affine axis. The group $\Sigma$ generated by the shears with axis $[0]$ and the elations with centre $[\infty]$ and axis $[\infty]$ (the translation line) is shown to be metabelian, meaning its commutator subgroup is abelian. This structural result is important for analyzing the full collineation group.
