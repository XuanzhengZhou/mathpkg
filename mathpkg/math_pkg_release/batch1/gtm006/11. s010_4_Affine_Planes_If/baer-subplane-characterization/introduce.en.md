This theorem gives a precise numerical characterization of Baer subplanes in finite projective planes. If a finite projective plane of order $n$ contains a proper subplane of order $m$, then that subplane is a Baer subplane exactly when $n$ equals $m^2$. This result is a direct consequence of the proof of Theorem 3.5 concerning the relationship between the orders of a projective plane and its subplane.
