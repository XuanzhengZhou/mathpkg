Let $F_{\phi}$ be an André quasifield. The following conditions are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $F_{\phi}$ is a division ring,
\item[(ii)] $F_{\phi}$ is a field,
\item[(iii)] $F_{\phi}$ is isomorphic to $F$,
\item[(iv)] $k^{\phi} = \varepsilon$ for all $k \in N$, where $\varepsilon$ is the identity of $\Gamma$ and $N = (F^*)^{\nu}$.
\end{enumerate}
