Let $F$ be a field with an automorphism group $\Gamma$, and let $K$ be the fixed field of $\Gamma$. Let $\nu: F^* \to \Gamma$ be a homomorphism and let $N = (F^*)^{\nu}$ be its image.

Let $\phi: N \to \Gamma$ be any mapping sending the identity to the identity. Define the mapping $\alpha = \nu\phi: F^* \to \Gamma$, and write $x^{\alpha}$ as $x^{\bar{\alpha}}$.

The **André quasifield** $F_{\phi}$ has the same elements and addition as $F$, with multiplication $\odot$ defined by:

$$x \odot y = xy^{\bar{\alpha}}, \quad 0 \odot y = 0 \quad \text{for all } x, y \in F, x \neq 0.$$
