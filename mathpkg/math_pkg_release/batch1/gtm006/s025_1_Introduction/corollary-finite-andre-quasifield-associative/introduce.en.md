This corollary specializes the associativity condition for André quasifields to the finite case. Since the automorphism group $\Gamma$ of a finite field is cyclic, any anti-homomorphism is automatically a homomorphism. Moreover, by considering orders (as shown in Exercise 9.4), one can prove that $N = K^*$ in the finite case. Thus a finite André quasifield is associative precisely when the defining map $\phi$ is a group homomorphism.
