Lemma 11.1 establishes two fundamental closure properties of families of sub-configurations. The intersection of any collection of open sub-configurations remains open, and the union of any collection of confined sub-configurations remains confined. These properties are immediate consequences of the definitions and play a crucial role in the theory of free closures, where open configurations are the building blocks from which projective planes are generated.
