Result 1.23 characterizes the subgroup $N$ of $\Gamma L(V)$ consisting of all semi-linear transformations that fix every one-dimensional subspace set-wise. Over a skewfield, such transformations form a group isomorphic to the multiplicative group $K^*$ of the skewfield. When the dimension is greater than one, the intersection of $N$ with $GL(V)$ is isomorphic to the center $Z(K^*)$. This result is important for understanding the collineation group of the associated projective space.
