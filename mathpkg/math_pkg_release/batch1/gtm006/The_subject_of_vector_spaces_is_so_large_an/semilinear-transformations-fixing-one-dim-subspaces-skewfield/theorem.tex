Let $V$ be a finite-dimensional left (or right) vector space over a skewfield $K$. Define
$$N = \{\gamma \in \Gamma L(V) \mid \langle v^\gamma \rangle = \langle v \rangle \text{ for all } v \in V\},$$
the set of all semi-linear transformations of $V$ that fix every one-dimensional subspace of $V$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $N \cong K^*$ (the multiplicative group of $K$).
\item If $\dim V > 1$, then $N \cap GL(V) \cong Z(K^*)$ (the center of $K^*$).
\end{enumerate}
