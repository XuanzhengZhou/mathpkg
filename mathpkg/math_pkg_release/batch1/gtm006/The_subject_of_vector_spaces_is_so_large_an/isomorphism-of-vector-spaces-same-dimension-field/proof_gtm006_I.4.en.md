The theorem is cited as familiar to the student and no explicit proof is given in this section. The standard proof proceeds by fixing a basis $\{e_1,\dots,e_n\}$ in each vector space and mapping $\sum x_i e_i \mapsto \sum x_i f_i$, which is a well-defined linear isomorphism.
