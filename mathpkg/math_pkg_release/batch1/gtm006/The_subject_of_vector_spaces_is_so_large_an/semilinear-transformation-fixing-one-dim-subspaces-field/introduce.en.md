Result 1.24 specializes Result 1.23 to the case where the ground ring is a field rather than a skewfield. Over a field, any semi-linear transformation fixing every one-dimensional subspace must in fact be a linear transformation (the associated automorphism is trivial, since fields have only the identity automorphism when the only automorphisms are those fixing the prime field) and must be represented by a scalar multiple of the identity matrix -- hence it lies in the center of $GL(V)$.
