No explicit proof is given in this section. The result follows from Result 1.23 specialized to a field: since $K$ is commutative, $Z(K^*) = K^*$, so $N \cap GL(V) \cong K^*$. The condition that $\alpha$ fixes every one-dimensional subspace forces the associated automorphism to be the identity (any non-trivial automorphism of $K$ would move some scalar, breaking the invariance of all one-dimensional subspaces). Hence $\alpha$ is linear and acts as scalar multiplication, placing it in the center of $GL(V)$.
