The special linear group $SL(V)$ is the subgroup of the general linear group consisting of all linear transformations (equivalently, matrices) with determinant equal to one. It plays a fundamental role in projective geometry, as $PSL(V) = SL(V)/Z(SL(V))$ is often a simple group and appears as the automorphism group of the associated projective space.
