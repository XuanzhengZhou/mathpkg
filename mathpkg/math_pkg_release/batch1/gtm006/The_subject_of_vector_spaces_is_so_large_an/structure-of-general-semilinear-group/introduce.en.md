Result 1.21 of GTM006 describes the structure of the general semi-linear group $\Gamma L(V)$. It is the product of the general linear group $GL(V)$ (which is normal in $\Gamma L(V)$) and a subgroup $A$ isomorphic to the automorphism group of the skewfield $K$, with $A \cap GL(V) = 1$. This shows that every semi-linear transformation factors as a linear transformation followed by an automorphism-induced scaling.
