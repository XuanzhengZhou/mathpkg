Let $V$ be a left vector space over a skewfield $K$, and let $V'$ be the set of all linear functionals on $V$. Define addition and scalar multiplication on $V'$ by:
\begin{itemize}
\item For $f', g' \in V'$ and $v \in V$: $v(f' + g') = vf' + vg'$.
\item For $f' \in V'$, $b \in K$, and $v \in V$: $v(f'b) = (vf')b$.
\end{itemize}
Under these operations, $V'$ is a right vector space over $K$.
