Let $V$ and $W$ be left vector spaces over a skewfield $K$, and let $\alpha$ be an automorphism of $K$. A mapping $\phi \colon V \to W$ is a **semi-linear transformation** from $V$ to $W$ with associated automorphism $\alpha$ if:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] For all $v_1, v_2 \in V$: $(v_1 + v_2)^\phi = v_1^\phi + v_2^\phi$.
\item[(ii)] For all $v \in V$ and $k \in K$: $(kv)^\phi = k^\alpha (v^\phi)$.
\end{enumerate}
