Theorem 10.18 describes the orbit structure of the full collineation group acting on the special points of a Hall plane of order $q^2 > 9$. The group partitions the special points into exactly two orbits: one orbit of size $q + 1$ and another orbit of size $q^2 - q$. This is a key structural result that, together with its corollary, shows that the full collineation group of such a Hall plane coincides with the group inherited from the desarguesian plane under derivation.
