Let $k$ be a line of $\mathcal{A}$ that meets $\ell_{\infty}$ in $\mathcal{S}'$. Then the "lines" of $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}'}(k)$ are the intersections of $k$ with the Baer subplanes of $\mathcal{A}$ belonging to $\mathcal{S}$. The structure $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}'}(k)$ is an affine plane of order $n$.
