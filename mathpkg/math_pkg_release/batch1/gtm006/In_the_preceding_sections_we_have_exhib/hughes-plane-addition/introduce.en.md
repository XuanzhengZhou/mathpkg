Lemma 9.17 shows that the ternary ring addition $a \oplus b = T(1,a,b)$ in the Hughes plane $\mathcal{H}$ coincides with the ordinary addition $a+b$ in the underlying nearfield $N$. The proof sets $m=1$ in the equations describing the line through $(1,0,-m)$ and a given affine point $(u,v)$, then uses the fact that $t=1$ is in the kernel $F$ to simplify the incidence equations and extract the addition formula.
