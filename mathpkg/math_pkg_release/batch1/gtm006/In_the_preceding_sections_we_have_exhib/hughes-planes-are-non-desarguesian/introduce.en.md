Corollary 1 of Lemma 9.17 establishes that the Hughes planes are non-desarguesian. Since the ternary ring addition $a \oplus b = a + b$ coincides with nearfield addition (Lemma 9.17), the planarity of the ternary ring forces the multiplication to be non-associative when the nearfield $N$ is not a field. A projective plane coordinatized by a non-associative planar ternary ring cannot be desarguesian, hence the Hughes planes are non-desarguesian.
