Corollary 2 establishes that a Hughes plane is not $(V,\ell)$-transitive for any choice of point $V$ and line $\ell$, and consequently is neither a translation plane nor a dual translation plane. This follows from Exercise 4.28 and the structure of the Hughes plane's collineation group, which does not fix any point or line. The result is significant because the Hughes planes are the only known finite non-desarguesian projective planes whose full collineation group has no fixed point or line.
