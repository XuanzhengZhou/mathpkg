This corollary establishes that the translation group $\Phi$ is normal in $\Sigma$, the subgroup of the collineation group fixing the line at infinity. The proof uses the geometric characterization of $\Phi$ as the set of perspectivities with axis $ and center on $, which is invariant under conjugation by elements of $\Sigma$.
