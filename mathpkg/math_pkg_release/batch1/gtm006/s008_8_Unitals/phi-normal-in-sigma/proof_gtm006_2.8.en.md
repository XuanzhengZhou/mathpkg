*Proof.* By Lemma 2.54, $\Phi$ is exactly the set of all perspectivities with axis $W$ whose center lies on $W$. Conjugation by any element of $\Sigma$ (which fixes $W$) sends a perspectivity with axis $W$ to another perspectivity with axis $W$, and preserves the property that the center lies on $W$. Hence $\Phi$ is invariant under conjugation by $\Sigma$, so $\Phi$ is normal in $\Sigma$. $\square$
