Let $K$ be a field with an involutory automorphism $\phi$, and let $F$ be the fixed field of $\phi$. Let $N = \{n \in K^* \mid n^{1+\phi} = 1\}$ be the set of elements of norm $1$. Then:

(a) $N \neq \{1\}$, i.e., there exist elements $k \in K$ with $k^{\phi} \neq k$ such that $k^{\phi-1} \in N$.

(b) If $K = \text{GF}(q^2)$, then $N$ is a cyclic subgroup of $K^*$ of order $q+1$.
