Lemma 2.43 characterizes the solvability of the norm equation $x^{1+\phi} = a$ in a field equipped with an involutory automorphism. Solutions exist only when $a$ lies in the fixed field $F$, and when they do, they form a coset of the norm-one subgroup $N$. This lemma provides the algebraic foundation for analyzing the intersection of a line with a unital.
