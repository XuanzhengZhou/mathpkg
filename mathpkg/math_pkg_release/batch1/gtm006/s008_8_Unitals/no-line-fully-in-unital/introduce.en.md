Lemma 2.46 establishes that no projective line can be fully contained in a unital (unless the underlying field is $\text{GF}(4)$). This follows from analyzing the equation (JU) that governs the intersection of a line with the unital: if every point on the line were in the unital, the coefficients of the associated quadratic equation would all vanish, contradicting the non-singularity of the polarity.
