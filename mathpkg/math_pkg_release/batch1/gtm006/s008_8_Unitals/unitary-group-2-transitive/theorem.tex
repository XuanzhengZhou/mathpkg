Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a non-empty unital in the projective plane $\mathcal{P} = \mathcal{P}_2(K)$ and let $\Gamma \leq \text{PGL}(V)$ be the unitary group fixing $\mathcal{U}$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] The subgroup of $\Gamma$ fixing a point of $\mathcal{U}$ is transitive on the remaining points of $\mathcal{U}$.
\item[(ii)] Consequently, $\Gamma$ is 2-transitive on the points of $\mathcal{U}$.
\end{enumerate}
