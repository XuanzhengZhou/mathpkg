This theorem establishes that the unitary group preserving a non-empty unital acts 2-transitively on its points. The proof constructs explicit matrices $ (with parameter  \in K$) that fix a chosen point $ and map any other point of the unital to any other point of the unital. By the corollary above, any two non-empty unitals are equivalent, so the unitary group is independent of the particular unital.
