The group $\text{Aut}\,\mathcal{A}$ of the affine line $\mathcal{A}$ over the field $K$ is (in parametric form) the group of all mappings
$$x \mapsto k x^{\alpha} + b$$
of the elements of $K$, where $k$ and $b$ are arbitrary elements of $K$, $k \neq 0$, and $\alpha$ is an arbitrary automorphism of $K$. This group has a normal subgroup $\Lambda_1$ consisting of all mappings for which $\alpha = 1$, and $\Lambda_1$ has a normal subgroup $\Lambda_2$ consisting of all elements for which $\alpha = 1$ and $k = 1$. The factor groups are:
$$\text{Aut}\,\mathcal{A} / \Lambda_1 \cong \text{Aut}\,K,$$
$$\Lambda_1 / \Lambda_2 \cong (K^*, \cdot),$$
$$\Lambda_2 \cong (K, +).$$
