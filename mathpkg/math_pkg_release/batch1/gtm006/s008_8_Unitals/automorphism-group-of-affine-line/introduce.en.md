Theorem 2.51 completely describes the automorphism group of the affine line over an arbitrary field $K$. The group has the structure of a semidirect product built from field automorphisms, scalar multiplications, and translations. The normal series reveals three layers: the additive group of $K$ (translations), the multiplicative group $K^*$ (dilations), and the automorphism group of $K$ (field automorphisms). This is the one-dimensional analog of the affine group.
