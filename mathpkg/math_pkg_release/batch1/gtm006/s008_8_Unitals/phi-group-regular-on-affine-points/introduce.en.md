Lemma 2.53 establishes that the affine translation-like mappings $\phi_{a,b}$ form a group acting regularly (transitively and freely) on the points of the affine plane $\mathcal{A}$. This group is the analogue of the translation group in classical affine geometry and plays a fundamental role in analyzing the automorphism group of the affine plane.
