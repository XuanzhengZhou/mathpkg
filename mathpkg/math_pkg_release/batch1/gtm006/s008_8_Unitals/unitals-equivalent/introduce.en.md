This corollary to Theorem 2.49 states that all non-empty unitals in a given projective plane are projectively equivalent. Just as all non-empty conics are equivalent, the unitary group is independent of the particular choice of unital. This fact is fundamental for the study of the unitary group acting on the unital, as it allows one to work with any convenient canonical form.
