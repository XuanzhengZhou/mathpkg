Lemma 2.58 shows that up to affine collineation, there is only one type of affine hyperbola and only one type of affine parabola. This follows from the fact that $\text{Aut}\,\mathcal{A}$ is 3-transitive on the line at infinity $, combined with the projective classification of conics. The ellipse case is more subtle and requires separate treatment.
