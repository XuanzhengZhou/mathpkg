Lemma 2.65 is a structural result about abelian collineation groups acting semi-regularly on points. It constrains the fixed line configuration: such a group can fix at most one line, and acts semi-regularly on all other lines. This is part of a sequence of lemmas characterizing regular abelian collineation groups (Singer groups).
