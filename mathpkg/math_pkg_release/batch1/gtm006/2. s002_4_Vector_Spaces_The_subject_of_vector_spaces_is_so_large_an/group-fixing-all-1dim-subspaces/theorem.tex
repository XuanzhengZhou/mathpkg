Let $V$ be a finite dimensional left (or right) vector space over the skewfield $K$. Let $N$ be the group of all semi-linear transformations of $V$ which fix every one-dimensional subspace of $V$, i.e.

$$N = \{\gamma \in \Gamma L(V) \mid \langle v^\gamma \rangle = \langle v \rangle \text{ for all } v \in V\}.$$

Then $N \cong K^*$ (the multiplicative group of $K$). Moreover, if $\dim V > 1$, then

$$N \cap GL(V) \cong Z(K^*),$$

the centre of the multiplicative group of $K$.
