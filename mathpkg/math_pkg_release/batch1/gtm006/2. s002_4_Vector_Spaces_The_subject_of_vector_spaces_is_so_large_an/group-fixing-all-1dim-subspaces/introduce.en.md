Result 1.23 identifies the subgroup $N$ of $\Gamma L(V)$ consisting of all semi-linear transformations that preserve every 1-dimensional subspace (as a set, not necessarily pointwise). The theorem states that $N$ is isomorphic to the multiplicative group $K^*$ of the skewfield, and its intersection with the linear subgroup $GL(V)$ is isomorphic to the centre $Z(K^*)$. Geometrically, elements of $N$ correspond to scalar multiplications (possibly composed with field automorphisms), which act trivially on the associated projective space $\mathrm{PG}(V)$.
