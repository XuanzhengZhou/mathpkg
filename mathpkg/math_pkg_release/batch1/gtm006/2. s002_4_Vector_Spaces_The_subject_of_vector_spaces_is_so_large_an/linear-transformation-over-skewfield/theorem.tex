Let $V$ and $W$ be left vector spaces over the skewfield $K$. A semi-linear transformation $\phi: V \to W$ with associated automorphism $\alpha$ is called a \textit{linear transformation} if $\alpha$ is the identity automorphism of $K$. Equivalently, $\phi$ satisfies:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] For all $v_1, v_2 \in V$, $(v_1 + v_2)^\phi = v_1^\phi + v_2^\phi$.
\item[(ii)] For all $v \in V$ and $k \in K$, $(kv)^\phi = k (v^\phi)$.
\end{enumerate}
