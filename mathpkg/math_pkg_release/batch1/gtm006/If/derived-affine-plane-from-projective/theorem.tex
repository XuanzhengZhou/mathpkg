Let $\mathcal{P}$ be a projective plane and let $l$ be any line of $\mathcal{P}$. Define $\mathcal{P}^l$ to be the incidence structure obtained by deleting the line $l$ and all points incident with $l$ from $\mathcal{P}$. The incidence relation of $\mathcal{P}^l$ is the restriction of the incidence of $\mathcal{P}$ to the remaining points and lines.
