Let $V$ be a left vector space over the skewfield $K$ and $W$ a left vector space over the skewfield $F$, and suppose each has dimension three. If there is an isomorphism from $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto $\mathcal{P}(W)$, then $F$ and $K$ are isomorphic and the isomorphism from $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto $\mathcal{P}(W)$ is induced by a semi-linear transformation from $V$ onto $W$.
