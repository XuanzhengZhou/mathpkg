Let $V$ be a left vector space over the skewfield $K$. Let $\Gamma L(V)$ be the group of all non-singular semi-linear transformations of $V$ onto $V$, and let $GL(V)$ be the subgroup consisting of all non-singular linear transformations. If $K$ is a field, define $SL(V)$ to be the subgroup of $GL(V)$ consisting of elements of determinant one. Each of these groups induces a group of automorphisms of $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto itself, denoted by writing "$P$" in front: $P\Gamma L(V)$, $PGL(V)$, and $PSL(V)$ (when defined).
