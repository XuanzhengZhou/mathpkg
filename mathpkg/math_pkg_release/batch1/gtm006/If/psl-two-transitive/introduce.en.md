Lemma 2.15 establishes that the projective special linear group $PSL(V)$ acts two-transitively on the points of the projective line $\mathcal{P}_1(K)$ over a field $K$. Two-transitivity means that any ordered pair of distinct points can be mapped to any other ordered pair of distinct points by some element of $PSL(V)$. The proof uses the fractional linear representation and the fact that any field element can be expressed as a quotient of appropriate determinants.
