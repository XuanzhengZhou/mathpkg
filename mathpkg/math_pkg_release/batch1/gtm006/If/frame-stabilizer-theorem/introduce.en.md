Theorem 2.12 determines the subgroup of $P\Gamma L(V)$ that fixes a given frame pointwise. This stabilizer is isomorphic to $\operatorname{Aut} K$, the automorphism group of the skewfield, and its intersection with $PGL(V)$ is isomorphic to $\operatorname{In} K$, the inner automorphism group. This result connects the geometric action on frames to the algebraic structure of the underlying skewfield, showing that pointwise frame stabilizers recover the field automorphisms.
