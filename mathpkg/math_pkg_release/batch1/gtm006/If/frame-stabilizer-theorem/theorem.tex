Let $\mathcal{P} = \mathcal{P}(V)$ where $V$ is a left vector space of dimension $n+1$ over a skewfield $K$, and let $E_1, E_2, \dots, E_{n+2}$ be a frame for $\mathcal{P}$. Let $G$ denote the subgroup of $P\Gamma L(V)$ fixing the given frame pointwise. Then $G \cong \operatorname{Aut} K$. Moreover, $G \cap PGL(V) \cong \operatorname{In} K$, the group of inner automorphisms of $K$.
