Lemma 2.13 gives an explicit parametric representation of the automorphism group $\operatorname{Aut} \mathcal{P}_1(K)$ of the projective line over a field $K$. Each automorphism can be written as a semi-linear fractional transformation $x \mapsto (x^\alpha a + b)(x^\alpha c + d)^{-1}$ where $\alpha \in \operatorname{Aut} K$ and $ad - bc \neq 0$. With the convention that $\infty$ maps appropriately, this completely describes $\operatorname{Aut} \mathcal{P}_1(K) = P\Gamma L_2(K)$ in the familiar form of Möbius transformations composed with field automorphisms.
