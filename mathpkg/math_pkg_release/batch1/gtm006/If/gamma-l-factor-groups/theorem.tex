Let $V$ be a left vector space over a skewfield $K$. Then:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $\Gamma L(V)/GL(V) \cong \operatorname{Aut} K$.
\item[(b)] $P\Gamma L(V)/PGL(V) \cong \operatorname{Aut} K / \operatorname{In} K$, where $\operatorname{In} K$ is the group of inner automorphisms of $K$.
\item[(c)] If $K$ is a field, then $PGL(V)/PSL(V) \cong K^*/(K^*)^2$, where $K^*$ is the multiplicative group of $K$ and $(K^*)^2$ is the subgroup of squares.
\end{itemize}
