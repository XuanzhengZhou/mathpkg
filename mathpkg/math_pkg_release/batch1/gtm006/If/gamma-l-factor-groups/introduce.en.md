Theorem 2.11 (a corollary to Theorem 2.10) computes the factor groups of the projective group chain. It establishes that $\Gamma L(V)/GL(V) \cong \operatorname{Aut} K$, $P\Gamma L(V)/PGL(V) \cong \operatorname{Aut} K / \operatorname{In} K$, and when $K$ is a field, $PGL(V)/PSL(V) \cong K^*/(K^*)^2$. These identities relate the projective automorphism groups to the automorphism group, inner automorphism group, and square class group of the underlying skewfield.
