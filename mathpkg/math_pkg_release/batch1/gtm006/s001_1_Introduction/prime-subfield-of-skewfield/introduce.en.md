The prime subfield is the intersection of all subfields of a given field (or the subfield generated by 1). It is uniquely determined by the characteristic: $GF(p)$ for characteristic $p$, or $\mathbb{Q}$ for characteristic 0. This result is fundamental for understanding the structure of all field extensions.
