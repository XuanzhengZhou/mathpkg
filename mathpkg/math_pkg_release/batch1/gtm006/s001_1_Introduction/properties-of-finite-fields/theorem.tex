Let $GF(q)$ be a finite field with $q = p^n$ elements, $p$ prime.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $GF(q)$ has characteristic $p$ and its prime subfield is $GF(p)$.
\item[(ii)] The multiplicative group $GF(q)^*$ is cyclic of order $q-1$.
\item[(iii)] If $p=2$, every element is a square. If $p \neq 2$, exactly half the non-zero elements are squares.
\item[(iv)] Every element of $GF(q)$ is a sum of two squares.
\end{enumerate}
