t-transitivity is a strong homogeneity condition on group actions. A $t$-transitive group can map any ordered $t$-tuple of distinct points to any other such $t$-tuple. In projective geometry, doubly transitive collineation groups are particularly important and are closely related to Desarguesian planes and fields with rich automorphism groups.
