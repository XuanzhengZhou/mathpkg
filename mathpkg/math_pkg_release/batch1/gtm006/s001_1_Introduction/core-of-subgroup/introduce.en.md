The core of a subgroup $\Delta \leq \Gamma$ is $\bigcap_{g \in \Gamma} g^{-1}\Delta g$, the largest normal subgroup of $\Gamma$ contained in $\Delta$. It is also the kernel of the permutation representation of $\Gamma$ acting on the right cosets of $\Delta$, making it a fundamental concept in permutation group theory.
