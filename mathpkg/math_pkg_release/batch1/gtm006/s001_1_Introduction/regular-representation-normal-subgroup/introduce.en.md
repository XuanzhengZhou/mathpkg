Result 1.16 characterizes when the standard permutation representation on cosets yields a regular action. The action on cosets of $\Delta$ is regular precisely when $\Delta$ is normal, in which case the representation is essentially the regular representation of the quotient group $\Gamma/\Delta$ acting on itself by translation. This is a key structural result connecting normal subgroups with regular permutation groups.
