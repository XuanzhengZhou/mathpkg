Burnside's Lemma (also known as the Cauchy-Frobenius orbit-counting theorem) expresses the number of orbits of a permutation group as the average number of fixed points. It is a key tool in the proof of the Orbit Theorem (Theorem 13.4) for collineation groups of finite projective planes.
