Result 1.5 gives a complete and elegant description of the subfield lattice of finite fields: subfields exist exactly when the exponent divides, and they are unique. This means the subfield structure of $GF(p^n)$ is isomorphic to the divisor lattice of $n$. This result is critical for understanding the possible subplanes and coordinatizing subfields in finite projective planes.
