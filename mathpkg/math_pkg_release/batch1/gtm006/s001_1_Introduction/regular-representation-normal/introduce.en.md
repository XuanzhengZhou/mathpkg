The permutation representation of a group $\Gamma$ on the right cosets of a subgroup $\Delta$ is a fundamental construction. This representation is regular (i.e., sharply transitive) exactly when $\Delta \trianglelefteq \Gamma$ is normal. This result connects normal subgroups with regular permutation actions.
