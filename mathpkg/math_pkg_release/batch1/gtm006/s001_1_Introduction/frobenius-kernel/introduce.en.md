The Frobenius kernel is the distinguished normal subgroup of a Frobenius group. It is regular on the underlying set and, by a theorem of Thompson, is always nilpotent. In the context of projective geometry, the Frobenius kernel often corresponds to the additive group of the coordinatizing algebraic structure (e.g., the additive group of a nearfield), while the Frobenius complement corresponds to the multiplicative group.
