Verify that the following three $4 \times 4$ matrices form a complete set of mutually orthogonal Latin squares of order $4$:
$$
\{1\} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & a & b \\
1 & 0 & b & a \\
a & b & 0 & 1 \\
b & a & 1 & 0
\end{pmatrix}, \quad
\{a\} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & a & b \\
a & b & 0 & 1 \\
b & a & 1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & b & a
\end{pmatrix}, \quad
\{b\} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 & a & b \\
b & a & 1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & b & a \\
a & b & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}.
$$
