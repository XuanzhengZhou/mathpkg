This theorem establishes one direction of the fundamental equivalence between finite projective planes and complete sets of mutually orthogonal Latin squares. Given any finite projective plane, one can coordinatize it with a planar ternary ring (PTR) and then apply Lemma 5.7 to produce $n-1$ pairwise orthogonal Latin squares of order $n$, which is precisely a complete set of MOLS. This connection makes Latin squares an indispensable tool for studying the existence and classification of finite projective planes.
