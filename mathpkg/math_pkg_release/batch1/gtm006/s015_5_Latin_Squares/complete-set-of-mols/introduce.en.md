A collection of $n-1$ pairwise orthogonal Latin squares of order $n$ is called a complete set of mutually orthogonal Latin squares (MOLS). The number $n-1$ is the theoretical maximum by Property 2. Complete sets of MOLS are deeply tied to finite projective planes: a projective plane of order $n$ exists if and only if a complete set of MOLS of order $n$ exists.
