Exercise 5.6 asks the reader to classify all loops of order $4$. The result is that the only loops of order $4$ are the two groups: the cyclic group $\mathbb{Z}_4$ and the Klein four-group $\mathbb{Z}_2 \times \mathbb{Z}_2$. This classification is used in conjunction with Exercise 5.7 to determine the possible additive structures of planar ternary rings of order $4$.
