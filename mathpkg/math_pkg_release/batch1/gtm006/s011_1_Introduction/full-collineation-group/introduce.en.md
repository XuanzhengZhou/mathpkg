The full collineation group $\operatorname{Aut}\mathcal{B}$ of a projective or affine plane $\mathcal{B}$ is the group of all collineations (automorphisms) of the plane under the usual composition of mappings. Any subgroup of $\operatorname{Aut}\mathcal{B}$ is referred to as a collineation group of $\mathcal{B}$. This group plays a central role in the study of the symmetries of projective and affine planes.
