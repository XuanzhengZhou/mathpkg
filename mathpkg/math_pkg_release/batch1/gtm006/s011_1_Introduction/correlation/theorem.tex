A \textit{correlation} $\theta$ of a projective plane $\mathcal{P}$ is a one-to-one mapping of the points of $\mathcal{P}$ onto the lines of $\mathcal{P}$ and the lines of $\mathcal{P}$ onto the points of $\mathcal{P}$ such that incidence is preserved. Clearly the product of any two correlations of a projective plane $\mathcal{P}$ is a collineation of $\mathcal{P}$.
