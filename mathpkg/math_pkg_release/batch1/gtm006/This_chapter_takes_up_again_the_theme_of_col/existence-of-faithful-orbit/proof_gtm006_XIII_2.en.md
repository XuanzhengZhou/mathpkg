This follows immediately from Theorem 13.1. Since $\Pi$ is finite, there exists at least one orbit $\mathcal{O}$ of maximal length. By Theorem 13.1, $\Pi$ induces a faithful permutation group on $\mathcal{O}$. Hence there is at least one faithful orbit.
