Corollary 1 of Theorem 13.1 asserts that every finite collineation group of a projective plane has at least one faithful orbit -- specifically, any orbit of maximal length. Since the group is finite, a maximal-length orbit always exists, and Theorem 13.1 guarantees faithfulness on it. This provides a minimal structural guarantee about the permutation representation of the group.
