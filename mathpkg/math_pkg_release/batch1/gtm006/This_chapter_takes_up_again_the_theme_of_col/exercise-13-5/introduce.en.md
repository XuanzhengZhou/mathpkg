Exercise 13.5 builds on Exercise 13.4 to show that double transitivity on points is equivalent to double transitivity on lines for a collineation group of a finite projective plane. Since Exercise 13.4 characterized double point-transitivity via flag and anti-flag transitivity, and the notions of flag and anti-flag are symmetric under duality, the result follows by applying the same characterization to lines.
