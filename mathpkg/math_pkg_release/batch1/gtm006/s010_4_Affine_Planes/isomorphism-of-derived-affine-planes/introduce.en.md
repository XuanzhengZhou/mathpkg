Lemma 3.11 provides a precise criterion for when two affine planes obtained from the same projective plane by deleting different lines are isomorphic. They are isomorphic exactly when there is a collineation (automorphism) of the projective plane mapping one deleted line to the other. This lemma is a key step in proving the uniqueness part of Theorem 3.10.
