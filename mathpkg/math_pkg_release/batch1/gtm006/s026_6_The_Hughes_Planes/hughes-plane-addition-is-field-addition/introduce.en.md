Lemma 9.17 shows that the addition operation $\oplus$ in the planar ternary ring obtained from the coordinatization of the Hughes plane $\mathcal{H}$ coincides with the ordinary addition $+$ in the nearfield $N$. This is proved by setting $m = 1$ in the incidence equation (5) for the ternary operation $T(m,u,v)$, which yields $a - c + (a' - c')t = 0$. For $t = 1$, this gives $a + a' = c + c'$, and substituting into equation (6) produces $(u + v)(a + a')$, establishing that the ternary value is $u + v$.
