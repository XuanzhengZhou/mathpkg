If $\mathcal{H}$ is coordinatized by the method introduced in Chapter V with $(\infty) = (0,0,1)$, $(0) = (1,0,0)$, $(0,0) = (0,1,0)$, and $(1) = (1,0,-1)$, then for all $u, v \in N$:
$$(u, v) \text{ is the point } \langle (u, 1, v) \rangle,$$
$$(m) \text{ is the point } \langle (1, 0, -m) \rangle,$$
$$(\infty) \text{ is the point } \langle (0, 0, 1) \rangle.$$
