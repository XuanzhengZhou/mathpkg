For any configuration $\mathcal{C}$, there exists a closed configuration $\mathcal{F}(\mathcal{C})$, unique up to isomorphism, such that $\mathcal{F}(\mathcal{C})$ is a free extension of $\mathcal{C}$ and $\langle \mathcal{C} \rangle_{\mathcal{F}(\mathcal{C})} = \mathcal{F}(\mathcal{C})$. Moreover, any free extension of $\mathcal{C}$ is contained in an isomorphic copy of $\mathcal{F}(\mathcal{C})$.
