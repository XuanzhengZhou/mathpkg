Suppose $\mathcal{A}$, $\mathcal{B}$, $\mathcal{C}$ are configurations with $\mathcal{A} < \mathcal{B} < \mathcal{C}$. If any two of the following three statements are true, then so is the third:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(a)] $\mathcal{C}$ is a free extension of $\mathcal{A}$.
  \item[(b)] $\mathcal{C}$ is a free extension of $\mathcal{B}$.
  \item[(c)] $\mathcal{B}$ is a free extension of $\mathcal{A}$.
\end{enumerate}
