A one-step extension of a configuration $\mathcal{C}$ is a configuration $\mathcal{C}_1$ that properly contains $\mathcal{C}$ as a sub-configuration. This is the basic building block for constructing larger configurations from smaller ones by adding new elements (points or lines) together with their incidence relations. The notion can be relativized to an ambient configuration $\mathcal{P}$, in which case both $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{C}_1$ must be sub-configurations of $\mathcal{P}$.
