Suppose $\mathcal{C}$ is contained in the closed configuration $\mathcal{P}$ and let $\mathcal{B} = \langle \mathcal{C} \rangle_{\mathcal{P}}$. Let $X$ be an element of $\mathcal{B}$. Since $\mathcal{B}$ is the union of full extensions of $\mathcal{C}$, there is an integer $n$ such that $X$ is in the full $n$-step extension $\mathcal{C}_n$ of $\mathcal{C}$, but not in $\mathcal{C}_{n-1}$ (if $X$ is in $\mathcal{C}$, then $n = 0$). Then $n$ is the $\mathcal{C}$-stage of $X$, written $s_{\mathcal{C}}(X)$.
