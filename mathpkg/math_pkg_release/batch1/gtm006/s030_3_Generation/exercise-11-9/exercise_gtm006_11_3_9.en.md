Show that if the word "free" is deleted in (a), (b), (c) of Lemma 11.6, then (a) and (c) imply (b), and (b) and (c) imply (a). That is, for configurations $\mathcal{A} < \mathcal{B} < \mathcal{C}$, if $\mathcal{C}$ is an extension of $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ is an extension of $\mathcal{A}$, then $\mathcal{C}$ is an extension of $\mathcal{B}$; and if $\mathcal{C}$ is an extension of $\mathcal{B}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ is an extension of $\mathcal{A}$, then $\mathcal{C}$ is an extension of $\mathcal{A}$.
