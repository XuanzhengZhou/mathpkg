In the division ring $D$ constructed from $F, \theta, a, b$, the associator of three elements $r = r_1 + \lambda r_2$, $s = s_1 + \lambda s_2$, $t = t_1 + \lambda t_2$ is given by:
\[
[r, s, t] = (r_2 s_1^\theta - r_1 s_2) t_2^\theta (a^\theta - a) + \lambda \big[ r_2 s_2^\theta t_2^\theta (b - b^\theta) + \cdots \big],
\]
where $[r,s,t] = (rs)t - r(st)$. In particular, when $D$ is associative, all associators vanish.
