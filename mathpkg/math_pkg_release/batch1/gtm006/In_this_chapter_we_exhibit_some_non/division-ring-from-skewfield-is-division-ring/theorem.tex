Under the hypotheses of the construction, $D$ is a division ring. That is, $D$ has no non-zero divisors and for any $x + \lambda y \neq 0$ and any $p + \lambda q \in D$, the equation $(x + \lambda y)(z + \lambda t) = p + \lambda q$ has a unique solution for $z + \lambda t$ (and similarly for the right factor).
