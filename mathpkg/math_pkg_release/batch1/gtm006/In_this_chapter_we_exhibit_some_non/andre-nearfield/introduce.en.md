An André nearfield is the associative special case of an André quasifield. Since any associative quasifield is a nearfield, these structures are nearfields in the usual sense. André nearfields form an important subclass of finite nearfields and were studied extensively by Johannes André. The Andre nearfields coordinatize a class of translation planes intermediate between the Desarguesian planes and the general André planes.
