Let $D$ be the Dickson commutative division ring constructed from $F = \mathrm{GF}(p^n)$ ($p$ odd, $n > 1$), non-square $a \in F$, and automorphism $\theta$ given by $x^\theta = x^{p^r}$ ($1 \leq r < n$). Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$ is a commutative division ring;
\item $D$ is never associative.
\end{enumerate}
