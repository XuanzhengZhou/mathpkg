Exercise 10.4 generalizes Theorem 10.12 by showing that partitioning the nonzero elements of $F^*$ into two complementary families of derivation parameters yields isomorphic multiply derived planes. This result indicates that the multiply derived desarguesian plane depends only on the choice of a subset of derivation sets up to complementation, reducing the number of potentially distinct planes obtainable by multiple derivation.
