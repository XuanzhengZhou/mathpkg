Exercise 10.2 generalizes aspects of Theorem 10.9 to dual translation planes. Given a right quasifield $K$ of order $n^2$ coordinatizing a dual translation plane $\mathcal{A}$ with a derivation set $\mathcal{S}$, the exercise asks to show that the derived plane contains a Baer subplane that is fixed pointwise by a collineation group of order $n$. The hint suggests imitating the proof of Theorem 10.9 by first finding an additive subgroup of $(K, +)$ of order $n$.
