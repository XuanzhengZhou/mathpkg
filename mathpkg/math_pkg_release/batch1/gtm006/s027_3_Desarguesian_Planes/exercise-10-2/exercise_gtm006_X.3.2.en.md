Let $K$ be a right quasifield of order $n^2$, and let $\mathcal{A}$ be a dual translation plane coordinatized by $K$. If $\mathcal{A}$ has a derivation set $\mathcal{S}$ containing $(\infty)$, then show that $\mathcal{D}_{\mathcal{S}}(\mathcal{A})$ has a Baer subplane fixed pointwise by a group of collineations of order $n$. (Hint: try to imitate the proof of Theorem 10.9, first finding a subgroup of $(K, +)$ of order $n$.)
