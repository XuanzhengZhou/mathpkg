Corollary 1 to Theorem 10.14 states that any affine plane coordinatized by a right quasifield of dimension $2$ over its kernel is derivable. This provides a broad sufficient condition for derivability: whenever the coordinatizing algebraic structure has the appropriate dimension property, the associated plane automatically admits a derivation set. The resulting derived planes are not necessarily translation planes, giving further examples of planes possessing neither translation lines nor translation points.
