Exercise 10.7 is an algebraic lemma about finite fields: every irreducible monic quadratic $g(x)$ over $GF(q)$ can be written in the specific form $g(x) = x^2 + (v/u)x - 1/u$, where the companion quadratic $x^2 - vx - u$ is also irreducible. This parametrization is used in the proof of Theorem 10.16 to show that the multiplicative structure of the derived quasifield can realize any Hall quasifield, by choosing the defining quadratic appropriately.
