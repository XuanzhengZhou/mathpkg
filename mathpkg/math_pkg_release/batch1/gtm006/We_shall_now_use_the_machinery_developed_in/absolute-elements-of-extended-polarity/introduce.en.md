This exercise studies the behavior of absolute elements when a polarity is extended from a configuration to its full free one-step extension. The key insight is that no new absolute elements are created in the extension process: all absolute elements of the extended polarity $\bar{\theta}$ lie within the original configuration $\mathcal{C}$ and are already absolute for the original polarity $\theta$. Consequently, the extension of $\theta$ to the full free closure has all its absolute elements contained in $\mathcal{C}$.
