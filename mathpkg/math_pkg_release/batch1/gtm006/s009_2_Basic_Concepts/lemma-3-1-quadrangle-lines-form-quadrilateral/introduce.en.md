Lemma 3.1 establishes a basic relationship between quadrangles and quadrilaterals in a projective plane: the six lines determined by the four points of a quadrangle contain a quadrilateral formed by the four "boundary" lines. This result demonstrates that the existence of a quadrangle (guaranteed by axiom (iii)) implies the dual existence of a quadrilateral, and is a key step in establishing the Principle of Duality.
