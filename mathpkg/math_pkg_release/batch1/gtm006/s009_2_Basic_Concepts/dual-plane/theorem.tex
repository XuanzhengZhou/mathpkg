If $\mathcal{P}$ is any projective plane, the \textbf{dual plane} $\mathcal{P}^*$ is the set of points and lines together with an incidence relation such that:
\begin{itemize}
  \item the points (lines) of $\mathcal{P}^*$ are the lines (points) of $\mathcal{P}$,
  \item two elements are incident in $\mathcal{P}^*$ if and only if they are incident in $\mathcal{P}$.
\end{itemize}
