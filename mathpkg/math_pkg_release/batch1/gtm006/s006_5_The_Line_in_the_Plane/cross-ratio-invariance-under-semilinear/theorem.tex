Let $V$ and $W$ be vector spaces of dimension two over the field $K$, let $\beta$ be the mapping from $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto $\mathcal{P}(W)$ induced by a non-singular semi-linear transformation $\beta$ from $V$ to $W$, and let $\phi$ be the automorphism of $K$ associated with $\beta$. Then for any choice of four points $P_1, P_2, P_3, P_4$ in $\mathcal{P}(V)$, no three of them equal, we have:
$$(P_1^\beta, P_2^\beta; P_3^\beta, P_4^\beta) = (P_1, P_2; P_3, P_4)^\phi.$$
