Let $V$ and $W$ be two-dimensional vector spaces over a field $K$, and let $\beta$ be a one-to-one mapping from $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto $\mathcal{P}(W)$. If there exists an automorphism $\phi$ of $K$ such that for all appropriate choices of four points $P_i$,
$$(P_1^\beta, P_2^\beta; P_3^\beta, P_4^\beta) = (P_1, P_2; P_3, P_4)^\phi,$$
then $\beta$ is induced by a unique non-singular semi-linear transformation from $V$ to $W$ with companion automorphism $\phi$.
