This theorem establishes the converse to Theorem 2.22: any bijection between projective lines over a field that preserves the cross-ratio (up to a field automorphism) must be induced by a semi-linear transformation. This gives a geometric characterization of $\operatorname{P\Gamma L}(V)$ purely in terms of the cross-ratio invariant.
