Let $V$ be a vector space of dimension three over a field $K$. Using homogeneous coordinates and any fixed basis for $V$, a correlation $\beta$ can be represented as:
$$\beta: \langle v \rangle \to \langle A(v^\phi)' \rangle, \qquad \langle (w') \rangle \to \langle (w^\phi A^{-1})' \rangle,$$
where $\phi$ is some automorphism of $K$ and $A$ is a non-singular $3 \times 3$ matrix.
