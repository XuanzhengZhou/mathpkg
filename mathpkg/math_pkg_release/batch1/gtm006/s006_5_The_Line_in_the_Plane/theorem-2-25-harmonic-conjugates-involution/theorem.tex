Let $A$ and $B$ be distinct points in the projective line $\mathcal{P}(V)$ over a field $K$ of characteristic not two. Then the points $X$ and $Y$ are harmonic conjugates with respect to $A$ and $B$ if and only if there is an element of order two in $PGL(V)$ which fixes $A$ and $B$ and interchanges $X$ and $Y$.
