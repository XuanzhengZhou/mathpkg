On a projective line over a field of characteristic $\neq 2$, two points $X$ and $Y$ are harmonic conjugates with respect to $A$ and $B$ exactly when there is an involutory collineation fixing $A$ and $B$ and swapping $X$ with $Y$. The unique involution fixing $A$ and $B$ is $x \mapsto -x$ in the frame $A = \infty$, $B = 0$.
