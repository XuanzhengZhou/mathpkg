Lemma 2.19 establishes that perspectivities are abundant: through any point not on two given lines, there is a perspectivity mapping one line onto the other. The proof constructs an explicit linear transformation of determinant 1 using a suitably chosen frame. This lemma is crucial for proving that $PSL(V)$ is 2-transitive on points (Theorem 2.20).
