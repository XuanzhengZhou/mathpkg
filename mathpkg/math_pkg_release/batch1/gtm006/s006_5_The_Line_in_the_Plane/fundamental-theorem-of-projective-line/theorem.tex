Let $\beta$ be a one-to-one mapping of $\mathcal{P}(V)$ onto $\mathcal{P}(W)$ (dimension two vector spaces over a field $K$) such that there is an automorphism $\phi$ of $K$ with $(P_1^\beta, P_2^\beta; P_3^\beta, P_4^\beta) = (P_1, P_2; P_3, P_4)^\phi$ for all choices. Then $\beta$ is induced by a semi-linear transformation from $V$ to $W$ with associated automorphism $\phi$.
