A semi-linear transformation defined on a two-dimensional subspace of a three-dimensional vector space can always be extended to a non-singular semi-linear transformation of the full space. The extension can be chosen to map any vector outside the original subspace to any vector outside the target subspace. This lemma guarantees that when a projective line is embedded in a projective plane, every collineation of the line lifts to a collineation of the plane belonging to $P\Gamma L(V)$.
