Theorem 2.20 establishes that the special projective group acts 2-transitively on the points of a Desarguesian projective plane over a field. The proof uses perspectivities (Lemma 2.19) to first show transitivity on lines, then uses the three-transitivity of $PSL(2,K)$ on the projective line to move points within a fixed line. This is a fundamental result about the symmetry of classical projective planes.
