Theorem 2.21 provides the explicit formula for computing the cross-ratio from parametric coordinates. The formula shows that the cross-ratio is a rational function of the coordinates, and in the case $P_1 = \infty, P_2 = 0, P_3 = 1$, it simplifies to $t_4$, consistent with the definition. This formula is the standard way to compute cross-ratios in practice.
