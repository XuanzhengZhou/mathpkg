Suppose $P_1, P_2, P_3$ are three distinct points in $\mathcal{P}(V)$ (where $V$ is a two-dimensional vector space over a field $K$), with parametric coordinates $t_1, t_2, t_3$ in some fixed frame. Then if $P_4 \neq P_1$ has parametric coordinate $t_4$,
$$(P_1, P_2; P_3, P_4) = \frac{(t_3 - t_1)(t_4 - t_2)}{(t_4 - t_1)(t_3 - t_2)}.$$
