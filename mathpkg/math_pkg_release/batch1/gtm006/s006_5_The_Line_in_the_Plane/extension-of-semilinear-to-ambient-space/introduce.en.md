Lemma 2.17 shows that any projective isomorphism between two lines of a projective plane can be realized as the restriction of a collineation of the whole plane. The extension is highly non-unique: given any vector $e$ outside $W_1$ and any vector $f$ outside $W_2$, the semi-linear transformation can be extended to map $e$ to $f$. This flexibility is essential for studying the action of collineation groups on lines.
