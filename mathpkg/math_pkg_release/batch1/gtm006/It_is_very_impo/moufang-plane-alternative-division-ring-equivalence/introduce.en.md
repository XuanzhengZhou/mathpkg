A projective plane is Moufang precisely when its planar ternary ring is an alternative division ring. This lies between the desarguesian and general division ring cases in the classification hierarchy. The Moufang condition admits multiple equivalent geometric characterizations: transitivity at all incident point-line pairs, transitivity of the form $(k,k)$ for all (or even just two distinct) lines, or transitivity of the form $(V,V)$ for all (or two distinct) points.
