Lemma 2.43 characterizes the solution set of the norm equation $x^{1+\phi} = a$ in a field $K$ with an involutory automorphism $\phi$. A necessary condition for solvability is that $a$ belongs to the fixed field $F$ of $\phi$. When solutions exist, they form exactly one coset of the norm-one subgroup $N$ in the multiplicative group $K^*$. This result is fundamental for analyzing the intersection of a line with a unital, where equations of this type arise from the sesquilinear form defining the unitary polarity.
