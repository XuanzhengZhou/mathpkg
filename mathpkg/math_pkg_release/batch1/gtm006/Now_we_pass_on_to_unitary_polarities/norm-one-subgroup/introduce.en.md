In a field $K$ equipped with an involutory automorphism $\phi$, the norm of an element $x$ is $x^{1+\phi} = x \cdot x^{\phi}$. The set $N$ of elements having norm equal to $1$ forms a subgroup of the multiplicative group $K^*$. This subgroup plays a crucial role in the study of unitals: the intersection of a non-absolute line with a unital, when non-empty, is in one-to-one correspondence with the elements of $N$. When $K$ is a finite field $GF(q^2)$, the subgroup $N$ has order $q+1$.
