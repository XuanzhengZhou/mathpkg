Let $K$ be a skewfield and $V$ a finite dimensional (left or right) vector space over $K$. The collection of subspaces of $V$ together with the natural containment relation is called the \textbf{projective geometry} $\mathcal{P}(V)$. A subspace $W$ is \textbf{incident} with a subspace $U$ if either $W$ contains $U$, or $U$ contains $W$.
