The Riemann zeta function is the fundamental object of analytic number theory. Its Euler product expresses the relationship between additive and multiplicative number theory. $\zeta(s)$ extends meromorphically to $\mathbb{C}$ with a simple pole at $s=1$, and encodes the distribution of primes through its zeros.
