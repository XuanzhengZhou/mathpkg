The standard fundamental domain $D$ for $G$ acting on $H$ is the region bounded by the unit circle and vertical lines $x = \pm 1/2$. The quotient $H/G$ can be identified with $\mathcal{R}/\mathbb{C}^*$ (isomorphism classes of elliptic curves) and compactified by adding the cusp at $\infty$.
