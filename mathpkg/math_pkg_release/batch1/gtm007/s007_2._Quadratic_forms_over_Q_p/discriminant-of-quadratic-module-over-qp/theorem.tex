Let $(V, Q)$ be a quadratic module of rank $n$ over $k = \mathbb{Q}_p$. If $e = (e_1, \ldots, e_n)$ is an orthogonal basis of $V$, and if we put $a_i = e_i \cdot e_i$, the discriminant $d(Q)$ is defined by
$$d(Q) = a_1 a_2 \cdots a_n \quad (\text{in } k^*/k^{*2}).$$
The discriminant is an element of the quotient group $k^*/k^{*2}$.
