This identity partitions the elements of a cyclic group of order $n$ by their orders: for each divisor $d$ of $n$, exactly $\phi(d)$ elements have order $d$. The proof counts the number of generators of each unique subgroup of $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}$.
