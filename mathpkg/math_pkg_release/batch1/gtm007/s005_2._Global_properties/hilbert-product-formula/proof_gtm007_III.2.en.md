By the product formula for the Legendre symbol (cf. chap. I, n\degree 3.3, th. 6) one has

$$\left( \frac{\ell'}{\ell} \right) \left( \frac{\ell}{\ell'} \right) = (-1)^{\varepsilon(\ell) \varepsilon(\ell')};$$

hence the product is equal to $1$. This completes the proof.
