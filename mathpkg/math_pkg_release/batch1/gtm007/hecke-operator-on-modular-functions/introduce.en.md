The explicit formula for $T(n)$ acting on a modular function comes from summing over matrices $\begin{pmatrix}a&b\\0&d\end{pmatrix}$ with $ad=n$, $0 \leq b < d$, which parametrize sublattices of index $n$. The factor $n^{2k-1}$ ensures compatibility with the weight.
