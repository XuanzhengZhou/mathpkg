Indefinite even unimodular lattices decompose into $p$ copies of the hyperbolic plane $U$ and $q$ copies of $\Gamma_8$. The existence of enough $U$ factors follows from Theorem 3 (existence of isotropic vectors) and Lemma 4 (splitting off $I_+ \oplus I_-$).
