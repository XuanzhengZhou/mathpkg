For $n = 4k$ with $k \geq 1$, let $E_1 = \mathbb{Z}^n$ with the standard quadratic form $x \cdot x = \sum x_i^2$, let $E_0 = \{x \in E_1 : \sum x_i \equiv 0 \pmod{2}\}$, and let $e = (\frac{1}{2}, \ldots, \frac{1}{2})$. The module $\Gamma_n$ is the sublattice of $\mathbb{Q}^n$ generated by $E_1$ and $e$. It has discriminant $+1$. When $n \equiv 0 \pmod{8}$, $\Gamma_n$ is of type II.
