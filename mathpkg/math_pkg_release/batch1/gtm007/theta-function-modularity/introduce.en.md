The functional equation for theta functions follows from the Poisson summation formula applied to the Gaussian $f(x) = e^{-\pi t x \cdot x}$. For a self-dual lattice, this implies $\theta_E(-1/z) = (z/i)^{n/2}\theta_E(z)$, which together with the obvious periodicity $\theta_E(z+1) = \theta_E(z)$ shows $\theta_E$ is a modular form of weight $n/2$.
