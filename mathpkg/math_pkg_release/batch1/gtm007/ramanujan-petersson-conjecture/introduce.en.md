The Ramanujan-Petersson conjecture bounds the Fourier coefficients of cusp forms. It was proved by Deligne as a consequence of the Weil conjectures on étale cohomology of algebraic varieties over finite fields. The bound $|c(n)| \leq n^{k-1/2}\sigma_0(n)$ is the optimal generalization.
