This compactness lemma is the set-theoretic underpinning of the $p$-adic "Hensel's lemma" style arguments. If the $D_n$ are finite and nonempty, one can find a compatible sequence by noting that the images $D_{n,p} = \operatorname{im}(D_{n+p} \to D_n)$ stabilize as $p \to \infty$ for each fixed $n$.
