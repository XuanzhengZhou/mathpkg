The Petersson inner product makes $M_k^0$ a finite-dimensional Hilbert space. The measure $y^{2k} dx\,dy/y^2$ is $G$-invariant, so the integral over the fundamental domain $D$ is well-defined. Hecke operators are Hermitian with respect to this inner product.
