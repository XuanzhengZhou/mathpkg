These auxiliary functions encode parity information about an odd integer modulo 4 and modulo 8. They appear in the supplementary laws of quadratic reciprocity: $\varepsilon(n)$ determines whether $-1$ is a square mod $n$, and $\omega(n)$ determines whether $2$ is a square mod $n$.
