The space $M_k$ of modular forms of weight $2k$ is completely described: $\dim M_k = [k/6]$ if $k \equiv 1 \pmod{6}$ and $\dim M_k = [k/6] + 1$ otherwise. The monomials $G_2^\alpha G_3^\beta$ with $2\alpha + 3\beta = k$ form a basis.
