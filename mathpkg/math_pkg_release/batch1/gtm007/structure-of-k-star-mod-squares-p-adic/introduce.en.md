This combinatorial lemma describes the structure of the square-class group of a $p$-adic field. For $p \neq 2$, $\mathbb{Q}_p^*/\mathbb{Q}_p^{*2} \cong (\mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z})^2$; for $p = 2$, it is $(\mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z})^3$. The sets $H_a^\varepsilon$ count how many square classes have a given Hilbert symbol with $a$, crucial for the classification of quadratic forms.
