This corollary justifies the local-global principle for quadratic forms over $\mathbb{Q}_p$. Since the discriminant is a unit, a nonsingular zero modulo $p$ lifts to a $p$-adic zero. This fact is essential for computing Hilbert symbols and proving the Hasse-Minkowski theorem.
