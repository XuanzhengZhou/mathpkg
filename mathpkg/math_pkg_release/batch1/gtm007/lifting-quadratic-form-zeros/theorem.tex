\textbf{Corollary 2.} Suppose $p \neq 2$. Let $f(X) = \sum a_{ij} X_i X_j$ with $a_{ij} = a_{ji}$ be a quadratic form with coefficients in $\mathbb{Z}_p$ whose discriminant is a $p$-adic unit. Every nontrivial zero of the reduction of $f$ modulo $p$ lifts to a nontrivial zero of $f$ in $\mathbb{Q}_p$.
