The Weierstrass $\wp$-function parametrizes elliptic curves $\mathbb{C}/\Gamma$ via $(\wp, \wp')$. The Eisenstein series appear in its Laurent expansion and the discriminant $\Delta = g_2^3 - 27g_3^2$ is a cusp form of weight 12.
