Let $K$ be a field of prime characteristic $p$. The Frobenius map $x \mapsto x^p$ is a field homomorphism because $(x+y)^p = x^p + y^p$ in characteristic $p$ (the binomial coefficients $\binom{p}{k}$ vanish mod $p$ for $0 < k < p$). It is injective, hence an isomorphism onto its image $K^p$.
