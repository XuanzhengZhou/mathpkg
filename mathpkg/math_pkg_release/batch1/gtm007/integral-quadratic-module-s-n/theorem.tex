Let $n \geq 0$. An object $E$ of the category $S_n$ is a free abelian group of rank $n$ (i.e., isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}^n$) together with a symmetric bilinear form $E \times E \to \mathbb{Z}$, denoted $(x,y) \mapsto x \cdot y$, such that the homomorphism $E \to \operatorname{Hom}(E,\mathbb{Z})$ defined by this form is an isomorphism. Equivalently, if $(e_i)$ is a basis of $E$ and $a_{ij} = e_i \cdot e_j$, then $\det(a_{ij}) = \pm 1$.
