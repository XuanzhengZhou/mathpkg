Same as Theorem 5, proved in §3.5. The integers $p$ and $q$ satisfy $2p + 8q = r(E)$ and $8q = \tau(E)$, hence $q = \tau(E)/8$ (an integer since $\tau(E) \equiv 0 \pmod{8}$ by Theorem 2) and $p = (r(E)-\tau(E))/2$.
