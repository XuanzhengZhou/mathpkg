The semigroup $K_+(S)$ captures the structure of unimodular lattices up to stable isomorphism. It satisfies the cancellation property, which allows the construction of the Grothendieck group $K(S)$. The rank $r$ and index $	au$ descend to homomorphisms $K_+(S) 	o \mathbf{Z}$, and together they give an isomorphism $K(S) \cong \{(a,b) \in \mathbf{Z}^2 : a \equiv b \pmod{2}\}$.
