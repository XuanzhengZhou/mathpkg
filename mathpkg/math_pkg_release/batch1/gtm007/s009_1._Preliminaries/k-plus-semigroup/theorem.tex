Let $S$ be the category of unimodular integral quadratic modules. Define $K_+(S)$ as the quotient of $S$ by the stable isomorphism relation: $E \sim E'$ if $\exists F \in S$ such that $E \oplus F \simeq E' \oplus F$. The operation $\oplus$ induces a commutative, associative composition law $+$ on $K_+(S)$ with neutral element $0$ (the class of the zero module). The class of $E$ in $K_+(S)$ is denoted by $(E)$.
