Theorem 4 is the structure theorem for indefinite type I (odd) quadratic modules. It states that every such module decomposes as an orthogonal direct sum $s I_+ \oplus t I_-$, where $I_+$ and $I_-$ are the one-dimensional modules $\mathbf{Z}$ with quadratic forms $x^2$ and $-x^2$ respectively. The proof proceeds by induction on the rank, using Lemma 4 to peel off an $I_+ \oplus I_-$ summand at each step.
