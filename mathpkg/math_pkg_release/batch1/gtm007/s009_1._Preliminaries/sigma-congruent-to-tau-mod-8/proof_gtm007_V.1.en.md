Indeed, $\tau$ reduced modulo $8$ and $\sigma$ are homomorphisms of $K(S)$ into $\mathbf{Z}/8\mathbf{Z}$ which are equal on the generators $I_+$ and $I_-$ of $K(S)$; hence they coincide.
