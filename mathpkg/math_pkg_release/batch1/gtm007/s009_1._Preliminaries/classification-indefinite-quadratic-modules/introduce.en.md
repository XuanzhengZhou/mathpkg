Theorem 6 is the definitive classification result: any two indefinite quadratic modules in $S$ having the same rank, index, and type (I or II) are isomorphic. This follows by combining Theorem 4 (type I) and Theorem 5 (type II), since in both cases the isomorphism class is determined by the rank and index.
