Let $E \in S_n$ be an indefinite unimodular lattice of type I. Then $E$ is isomorphic to a direct sum of copies of $I_+$ and $I_-$:
$$
E \simeq s I_+ \oplus t I_-,
$$
where $s = \frac{1}{2}(r(E) + \tau(E))$ and $t = \frac{1}{2}(r(E) - \tau(E))$ are nonnegative integers. In particular, $E$ is determined up to isomorphism by its rank and index.
