The discriminant of a unimodular lattice over $\mathbf{Z}$ is $\pm 1$, and its sign is determined by the signature over $\mathbf{R}$. Since $d(E) = \pm 1$, the sign equals $(-1)^s$ where $s$ is the number of negative squares in the signature.
