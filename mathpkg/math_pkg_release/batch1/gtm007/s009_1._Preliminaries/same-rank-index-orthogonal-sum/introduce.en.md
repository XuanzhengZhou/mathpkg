This corollary states that if two quadratic modules have the same rank and index, then taking the orthogonal sum with either $I_+$ or $I_-$ makes them isomorphic. The proof uses Theorem 4: after ensuring both modules are indefinite by adding $I_+$ or $I_-$, they both decompose as $s I_+ \oplus t I_-$ with the same $s$ and $t$ determined by rank and index.
