This is the culmination of the structure theory: indefinite unimodular lattices are classified completely by their rank, index (signature), and type (I/II). The result combines Theorem 4 (type I) and Theorem 5 (type II). In contrast, definite lattices in the same genus can have many non-isomorphic classes, counted by the Minkowski-Siegel mass formula.
