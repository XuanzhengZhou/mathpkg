This theorem (the collective result of cases i-iv in Chapter V, §3.2) establishes that every indefinite unimodular lattice of rank at least 2 represents zero nontrivially over $\mathbf{Q}$. This is a crucial input for the structure theory of indefinite unimodular lattices.
