Indeed, $\sigma(E) = 0$ for type II, and by Theorem 2, $\sigma(E) \equiv \tau(E) \pmod{8}$, hence $\tau(E) \equiv 0 \pmod{8}$.

(Note that this implies that $r(E) \equiv 0 \pmod{2}$ and $d(E) = (-1)^{r(E)/2}$.)
