Corollary 1 to Theorem 2 states that for a type II (even) quadratic module, the index $\tau(E)$ is divisible by 8. This follows immediately from Theorem 2 ($\sigma \equiv \tau \pmod{8}$) and the fact that $\sigma(E) = 0$ for type II. As a consequence, $r(E)$ is even and $d(E) = (-1)^{r(E)/2}$.
