Theorem 5 (Chapter V, §2.2) is the structure theorem for indefinite type II (even) lattices. These are determined by the number of hyperbolic planes $pU$ and copies of $\Gamma_8$. Since $	au(E) \equiv 0 \pmod{8}$ for type II, $q = 	au(E)/8$ is an integer. The proof is deferred to §3.5.
