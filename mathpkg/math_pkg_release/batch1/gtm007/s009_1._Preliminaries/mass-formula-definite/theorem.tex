Let $C_n$ be the set of isomorphism classes of even, positive definite unimodular lattices of rank $n$. For $n = 8k$ (necessary for such lattices to exist), the Minkowski-Siegel mass formula gives:
$$
\sum_{E \in C_n} \frac{1}{|\operatorname{Aut}(E)|} = M_n,
$$
where $M_n$ is an explicit rational number expressed in terms of Bernoulli numbers. For $n=8$, $M_8 = 2^{-14}3^{-5}5^{-2}7^{-1}$. For $n=16$, $M_{16}$ involves $B_2, B_4, B_6, B_8$.
