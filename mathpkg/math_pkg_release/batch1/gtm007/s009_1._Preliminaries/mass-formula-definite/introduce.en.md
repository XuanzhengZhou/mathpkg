The Minkowski-Siegel mass formula computes the sum of reciprocals of automorphism group orders over all isomorphism classes in a given genus of quadratic forms. For even, positive definite unimodular lattices of rank $n = 8k$, the genus consists of a single class for small ranks but grows rapidly. The masses are rational numbers with large denominators.
