Lemma 4 (Chapter V, §3.3) is the key step in the proof of Theorem 4 (structure theorem for indefinite type I lattices). It shows that every such lattice contains an orthogonal direct summand isomorphic to $I_+ \oplus I_-$, which is a hyperbolic plane of signature $(1,1)$. The proof uses the existence of an isotropic primitive vector and constructs a complementary vector with odd norm.
