Lemma 4 is the key technical lemma for the structure theory of indefinite type I quadratic modules. It states that any indefinite type I module $E \in S_n$ contains $I_+ \oplus I_-$ as an orthogonal direct summand, i.e., $E \simeq I_+ \oplus I_- \oplus F$ for some $F \in S_{n-2}$. The proof constructs explicit basis vectors $e_1, e_2$ from an isotropic vector and adjusts them to achieve the desired quadratic form.
