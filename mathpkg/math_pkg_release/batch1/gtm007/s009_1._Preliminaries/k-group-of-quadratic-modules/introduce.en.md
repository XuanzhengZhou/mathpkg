The group $K(S)$ (or the semigroup $K_+(S)$ of stable isomorphism classes) is the natural invariant for studying unimodular lattices up to stable equivalence. It is a free abelian group of rank 2, with generators $(I_+)$ and $(I_-)$. Every element can be written uniquely as $s(I_+) + t(I_-)$ with $s, t \in \mathbf{Z}$, where $s$ and $t$ are determined by the rank and index.
