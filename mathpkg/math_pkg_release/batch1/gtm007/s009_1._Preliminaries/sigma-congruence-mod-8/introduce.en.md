Theorem 2 (Chapter V, §1.4) establishes a fundamental congruence between the $\sigma$-invariant (computed from an element with odd norm modulo 8) and the index $	au = r - s$. This relation is proved by observing that both $\sigma$ and $	au mod 8$ are homomorphisms from $K(S)$ to $\mathbf{Z}/8\mathbf{Z}$ which agree on the generators $I_+$ and $I_-$.
