The hyperbolic plane is the fundamental building block of quadratic spaces that represent zero. Its matrix $\begin{pmatrix}0&1\\1&0\end{pmatrix}$ has discriminant $-1$ and it represents all scalars. Witt's theorem shows that hyperbolic planes can be split off whenever the form represents zero.
