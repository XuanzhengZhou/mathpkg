\textbf{Theorem 2.} The multiplicative group $\mathbb{Q}_p^*$ is isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z} \times V \times U_1$, where $\mathbb{Z}$ corresponds to the valuation, $V$ is the group of $(p-1)$-th roots of unity (for $p \neq 2$), and $U_1 = 1 + p\mathbb{Z}_p$. For $p = 2$, $\mathbb{Q}_2^* \cong \mathbb{Z} \times \{\pm 1\} \times U_2$.
