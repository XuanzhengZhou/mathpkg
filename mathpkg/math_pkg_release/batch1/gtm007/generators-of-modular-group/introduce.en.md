$G$ is generated by $S$ (inversion) and $T$ (translation). This means any fractional linear transformation in $\mathbf{PSL}_2(\mathbb{Z})$ can be expressed as a word in $S$ and $T$. This is used to check modularity: it suffices to verify $f(z+1) = f(z)$ and $f(-1/z) = z^{2k}f(z)$.
