The global classification follows from the local-global principle: a rational quadratic form is determined up to equivalence by its local invariants $\{d_v(f), \varepsilon_v(f)\}_{v \in V}$ and its signature $(r,s)$. The proof uses induction on rank and Witt's cancellation theorem.
