The Hilbert symbol satisfies:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $(a, b) = (b, a)$ (symmetry).
\item[(ii)] $(a, -a) = 1$ and $(a, 1-a) = 1$.
\item[(iii)] $(aa', b) = (a, b)(a', b)$ (bilinearity).
\item[(iv)] The induced bilinear form on $k^*/k^{*2} \times k^*/k^{*2} \to \{\pm 1\}$ is nondegenerate.
\end{itemize}
