A lattice $\Gamma$ in a finite-dimensional real vector space $V$ is a subgroup satisfying: (i) $\Gamma$ is discrete and $V/\Gamma$ is compact; or equivalently (ii) $\Gamma$ is discrete and generates $V$; or equivalently (iii) there exists an $\mathbb{R}$-basis of $V$ which is a $\mathbb{Z}$-basis of $\Gamma$.
