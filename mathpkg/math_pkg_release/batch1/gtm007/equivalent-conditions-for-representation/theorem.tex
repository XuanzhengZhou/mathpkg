\textbf{Corollary 1.} Let $g = g(X_1, \ldots, X_{n-1})$ be a nondegenerate quadratic form and let $a \in k^*$. The following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $g$ represents $a$.
\item[(ii)] $g \sim h + aZ^2$ where $h$ is a form in $n-2$ variables.
\item[(iii)] The form $f = g \div aZ^2$ represents $0$.
\end{enumerate}
