The coefficients of Eisenstein series grow like $n^{2k-1}$ because $a_n = c \cdot \sigma_{2k-1}(n)$ and $n^{2k-1} \leq \sigma_{2k-1}(n) \leq \zeta(2k-1) n^{2k-1}$. This is the baseline against which cusp form coefficients (which grow like $n^k$) are compared.
