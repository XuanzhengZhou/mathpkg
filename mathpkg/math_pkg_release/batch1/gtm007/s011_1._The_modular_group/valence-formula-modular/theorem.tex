For a nonzero modular form $f$ of weight $k$ (allowing meromorphy at cusps), the valence formula states:
$$
\sum_{p \in \Gamma \backslash \mathcal{H}} \frac{v_p(f)}{|\operatorname{Stab}_\Gamma(p)|} = \frac{k}{12},
$$
where the sum runs over $\Gamma$-orbits in $\mathcal{H}$, $v_p(f)$ is the order of zero of $f$ at $p$, and $|\operatorname{Stab}_\Gamma(p)|$ is the order of the stabilizer (1 for generic points, 2 for $i$, 3 for $\rho$). If $f$ is also meromorphic at cusps, one adds the order at $i\infty$.
