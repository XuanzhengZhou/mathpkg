Same proof as the valence formula. The stabilizer contributions reflect the elliptic fixed points: $\operatorname{Stab}(i) = \{1, S\}$ (order 2) and $\operatorname{Stab}(\rho) = \{1, ST, (ST)^2\}$ (order 3). The term $k/12$ arises from integrating $f'/f$ along the boundary of the fundamental domain, where the transformation law $f(\gamma z) = (cz+d)^k f(z)$ contributes $k$ times the logarithmic derivative of $(cz+d)$.
