Let $\mathcal{F} \subset \mathcal{H}$ (the upper half-plane) be the set
$$
\mathcal{F} = \{z \in \mathcal{H} : |z| \geq 1, \; |\operatorname{Re}(z)| \leq 1/2\}.
$$
Then $\mathcal{F}$ is a fundamental domain for the action of the modular group $\Gamma = \operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})/\{\pm 1\}$ on $\mathcal{H}$. That is:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] For every $z \in \mathcal{H}$, there exists $\gamma \in \Gamma$ such that $\gamma z \in \mathcal{F}$.
\item[(ii)] If $z, z' \in \mathcal{F}$ with $z \neq z'$ and $z' = \gamma z$ for some $\gamma \in \Gamma \setminus \{1\}$, then $z$ and $z'$ lie on the boundary of $\mathcal{F}$.
\end{enumerate}
