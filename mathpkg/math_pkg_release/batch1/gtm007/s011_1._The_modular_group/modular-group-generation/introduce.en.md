The modular group acts on the upper half-plane by Möbius transformations. It is generated by the inversion $S$ and the unit translation $T$. These two transformations generate all integer Möbius transformations with determinant 1. The presentation $\langle S, T \mid S^2 = (ST)^3 = 1 angle$ is fundamental for the theory of modular forms.
