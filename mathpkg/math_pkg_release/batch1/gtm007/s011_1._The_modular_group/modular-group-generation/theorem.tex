The modular group $\Gamma = \operatorname{PSL}_2(\mathbf{Z}) = \operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})/\{\pm 1\}$ is generated by the two elements
$$
S = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} \quad \text{(inversion } z \mapsto -1/z\text{)},
$$
$$
T = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \quad \text{(translation } z \mapsto z+1\text{)},
$$
with relations $S^2 = 1$ and $(ST)^3 = 1$. Thus $\Gamma \cong \mathbf{Z}/2\mathbf{Z} * \mathbf{Z}/3\mathbf{Z}$ is the free product of a cyclic group of order 2 and a cyclic group of order 3.
