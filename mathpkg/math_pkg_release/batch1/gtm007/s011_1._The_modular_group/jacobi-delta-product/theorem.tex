(Jacobi) The discriminant modular form $\Delta = g_2^3 - 27 g_3^2$ has the infinite product expansion
$$\Delta = (2\pi)^{12} q \prod_{n=1}^{\infty} (1 - q^n)^{24}, \qquad q = e^{2\pi i z}.$$

Equivalently, defining the normalized cusp form $F(z) = (2\pi)^{-12} \Delta(z)$,
$$F(z) = q \prod_{n=1}^{\infty} (1 - q^n)^{24} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \tau(n) q^n,$$
where $\tau(n)$ is the Ramanujan tau function.
