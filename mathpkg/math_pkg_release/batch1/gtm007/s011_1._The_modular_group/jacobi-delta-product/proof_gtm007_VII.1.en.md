The proof uses the theory of elliptic functions or the Jacobi triple product identity. In terms of the Dedekind eta function $\eta(z) = q^{1/24} \prod_{n=1}^\infty (1-q^n)$, one shows that $\eta^{24}$ is a cusp form of weight 12. Since $S_{12}$ is one-dimensional (spanned by $\Delta$), and both have leading coefficient 1, they coincide: $\Delta = (2\pi)^{12} \eta^{24}$. The product expansion of $\eta$ then gives Jacobi's formula.
