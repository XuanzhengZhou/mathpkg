Jacobi's identity $\Delta = (2\pi)^{12} \eta^{24}$ is a fundamental result connecting modular forms, infinite products, and the Dedekind eta function. It provides a concrete combinatorial interpretation of the Ramanujan $	au$-function through the product expansion, and is the starting point for the theory of Hecke operators.
