Since the modular group is generated by $S$ and $T$, checking modularity reduces to verifying two functional equations. This criterion is routinely used to prove that a given function (e.g., an Eisenstein series, the $j$-invariant) is modular. For weight 0, weakly modular functions are $\Gamma$-invariant and correspond to meromorphic functions on the modular curve.
