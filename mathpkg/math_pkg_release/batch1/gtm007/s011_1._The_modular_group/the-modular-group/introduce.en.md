The modular group, denoted $G = \mathbf{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z}) / \{\pm 1\}$, is the projective special linear group of $2 \times 2$ integer matrices with determinant 1. It acts on the upper half-plane $H$ by fractional linear transformations $z \mapsto (az+b)/(cz+d)$. The group $\mathbf{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})$ is a discrete subgroup of $\mathbf{SL}_2(\mathbf{R})$, and the quotient by $\{\pm 1\}$ ensures faithful action on $H$.
