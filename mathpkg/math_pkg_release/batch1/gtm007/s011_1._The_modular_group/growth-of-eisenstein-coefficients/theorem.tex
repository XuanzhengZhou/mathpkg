Let $f = G_k$ be the Eisenstein series of weight $2k$ ($k \geq 2$), with $q$-expansion
$$f(z) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_n q^n, \qquad q = e^{2\pi i z}.$$

Then the order of magnitude of $a_n$ is $n^{2k-1}$. More precisely, there exist constants $A, B > 0$ such that
$$A n^{2k-1} \leq |a_n| \leq B n^{2k-1}.$$
