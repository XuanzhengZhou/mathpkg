Let $\mathcal{R}$ be the set of lattices of $\mathbf{C}$ considered as an $\mathbf{R}$-vector space. Let $M$ be the set of pairs $(\omega_1, \omega_2) \in \mathbf{C}^* \times \mathbf{C}^*$ such that $\operatorname{Im}(\omega_1/\omega_2) > 0$, with the map
$$\Gamma(\omega_1, \omega_2) = \mathbf{Z}\omega_1 \oplus \mathbf{Z}\omega_2$$
giving a surjection $M \to \mathcal{R}$.

The map $(\omega_1, \omega_2) \mapsto \omega_1/\omega_2$ gives, by passing to the quotient, a bijection of $\mathcal{R}/\mathbf{C}^*$ onto $H/G$.

Thus an element of $H/G$ can be identified with a lattice of $\mathbf{C}$ defined up to a homothety. Equivalently, $H/G$ is the set of isomorphism classes of elliptic curves $E_\Gamma = \mathbf{C}/\Gamma$.
