The Fourier coefficients of Eisenstein series are given by divisor functions $\sigma_{k-1}(n)$, which have polynomial growth $O(n^{k-1})$. This contrasts with cusp forms, whose coefficients satisfy the Ramanujan-Petersson bound $a_n = O(n^{(k-1)/2+arepsilon})$. The explicit formulas involve Bernoulli numbers.
