For a cusp form $f \in S_k$, its Fourier coefficients $a_n$ satisfy the estimate
$$
|a_n| \leq C_f \cdot n^{k/2},
$$
where $C_f$ is a constant depending only on $f$. Moreover, if $f$ is a normalized Hecke eigenform, the bounds can be refined: for prime $p$, $|a_p| \leq 2p^{(k-1)/2}$ (Ramanujan-Petersson bound).
