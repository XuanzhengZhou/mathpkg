The proof proceeds as for the Hecke bound. For a normalized Hecke eigenform, the Ramanujan-Petersson bound $|a_p| \leq 2p^{(k-1)/2}$ for prime $p$ was conjectured by Ramanujan for the $\Delta$ function ($k=12$) and proved in general by Deligne (1974) as a consequence of the Weil conjectures.
