A \textbf{cusp form} of weight $k$ (for the full modular group $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})$) is a modular form $f \in M_k$ whose Fourier expansion at infinity has zero constant term:
$$
f(z) = \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n e^{2\pi i n z}, \quad a_0 = 0.
$$
The space of cusp forms of weight $k$ is denoted by $S_k$.
