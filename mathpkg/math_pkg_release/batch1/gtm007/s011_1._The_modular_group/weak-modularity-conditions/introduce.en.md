A meromorphic function $f$ on $H$ is weakly modular of weight $2k$ if the differential form $f(z) dz^k$ is $G$-invariant. Since $G$ is generated by $S(z) = -1/z$ and $T(z) = z+1$, this invariance reduces to two functional equations: periodicity $f(z+1) = f(z)$ and the symmetry $f(-1/z) = z^{2k} f(z)$.
