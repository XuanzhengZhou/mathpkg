The explicit basis for modular form spaces of all even weights demonstrates the fundamental role of $G_4$ and $G_6$ as generators. Every modular form can be uniquely expressed as a polynomial in these two Eisenstein series. The dimension formula $\dim M_k = \#\{(a,b) \in \mathbf{Z}_{\geq 0}^2 : 4a+6b = k\}$ follows.
