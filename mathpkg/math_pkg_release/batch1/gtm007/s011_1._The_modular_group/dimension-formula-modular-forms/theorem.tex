For every integer $k \geq 0$:
$$\dim M_k = \begin{cases}
[k/6] & \text{if } k \equiv 1 \pmod{6}, \\
[k/6] + 1 & \text{otherwise},
\end{cases}$$
where $[x]$ denotes the integral part of $x$.

Equivalently, $\dim M_k^0 = \dim M_k - 1$ for $k \geq 6$ with $k \not\equiv 1 \pmod{6}$, and $\dim M_k^0 = \dim M_k$ otherwise. More precisely,
$$\dim M_k^0 = \begin{cases}
0 & \text{if } k < 6, \\
k/6 - 1 & \text{if } k \equiv 0 \pmod{6},\\
[(k-1)/6] & \text{if } k \not\equiv 0 \pmod{6}.
\end{cases}$$
