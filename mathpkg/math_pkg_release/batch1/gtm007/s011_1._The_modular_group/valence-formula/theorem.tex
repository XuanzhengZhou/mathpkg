Let $f$ be a nonzero modular form of weight $k$ for the full modular group $\Gamma = \operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})/\{\pm 1\}$, meromorphic at $i\infty$. Let $v_p(f)$ denote the order of zero (or pole) of $f$ at $p \in \mathcal{H}$. Then
$$
v_\infty(f) + \frac{1}{2} v_i(f) + \frac{1}{3} v_\rho(f) + \sum_{p \in \mathcal{F} \setminus \{i,\rho\}} v_p(f) = \frac{k}{12},
$$
where $i = e^{\pi i/2}$, $\rho = e^{2\pi i/3}$, and $\mathcal{F}$ is the fundamental domain. Here $v_\infty(f)$ is the order of the zero of $f$ at $i\infty$ (the exponent of the leading $q$-term in the Fourier expansion).
