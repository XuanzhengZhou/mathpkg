The valence formula (Theorem 4, Chapter VII) is the fundamental tool for proving dimension formulas for spaces of modular forms. It counts the number of zeros of a modular form in the fundamental domain, weighted by the stabilizer orders ($1/2$ at $i$, $1/3$ at $ho$). The total weighted number of zeros equals $k/12$, which forces $k \geq 0$ for holomorphic modular forms.
