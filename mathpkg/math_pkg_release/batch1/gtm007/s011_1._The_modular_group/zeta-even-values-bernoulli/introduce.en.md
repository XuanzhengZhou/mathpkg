Proposition 7 expresses the values of the Riemann zeta function at positive even integers in terms of Bernoulli numbers and powers of $\pi$. The formula $\zeta(2k) = 2^{2k-1} B_k \pi^{2k} / (2k)!$ follows from the product expansion of $\sin z$ and the defining generating function of the Bernoulli numbers. This connection is essential for computing the constant terms of Eisenstein series.
