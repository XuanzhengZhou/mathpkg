By the valence formula (Theorem 4), every modular form $f$ of weight $k$ satisfies
$$
v_\infty(f) + \frac{1}{2}v_i(f) + \frac{1}{3}v_\rho(f) + \sum_{p \in \mathcal{F} \setminus \{i,\rho\}} v_p(f) = \frac{k}{12},
$$
where $i = e^{\pi i/2}$ and $\rho = e^{2\pi i/3}$. Counting monomials $G_4^a G_6^b$ with $4a+6b = k$ gives the dimension formula. Since $\dim M_k$ equals this count, these monomials form a basis.
