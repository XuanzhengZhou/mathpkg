The space $M_k$ of modular forms of weight $k$ for $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})$ is a finite-dimensional complex vector space. Together they form a graded algebra under pointwise multiplication. Theorem 4 (the valence formula) and the dimension formula give a complete description: $M_* = \mathbf{C}[G_4, G_6]$, freely generated by the Eisenstein series $G_4$ and $G_6$.
