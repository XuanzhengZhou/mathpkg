The set of all modular forms of all weights forms a graded $\mathbf{C}$-algebra
$$
M_* = \bigoplus_{k \geq 0} M_k,
$$
where $M_k$ denotes the space of modular forms of weight $k$ (for the full modular group $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})$). The product of a form of weight $k$ and a form of weight $\ell$ is a form of weight $k+\ell$.
