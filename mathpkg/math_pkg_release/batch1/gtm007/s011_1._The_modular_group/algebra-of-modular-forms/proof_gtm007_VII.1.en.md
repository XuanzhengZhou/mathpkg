The product of two modular forms $f \in M_k$, $g \in M_\ell$ transforms as
$$
(fg)\!\left(\frac{az+b}{cz+d}\right) = (cz+d)^k f\!\left(\frac{az+b}{cz+d}\right) \cdot (cz+d)^\ell g\!\left(\frac{az+b}{cz+d}\right) = (cz+d)^{k+\ell} f(z)g(z),
$$
so $fg \in M_{k+\ell}$. The graded algebra structure follows. The finite-dimensionality of each $M_k$ is proved using the valence formula (Theorem 4). The structure theorem $M_* = \mathbf{C}[G_4, G_6]$ follows from dimension counting.
