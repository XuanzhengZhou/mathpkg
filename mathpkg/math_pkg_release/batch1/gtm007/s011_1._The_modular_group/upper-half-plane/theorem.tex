The \textbf{upper half-plane} is the open subset of the complex plane defined by
$$
\mathcal{H} = \{z \in \mathbf{C} : \operatorname{Im}(z) > 0\}.
$$
The group $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{R})$ acts on $\mathcal{H}$ by Möbius transformations:
$$
\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \cdot z = \frac{az + b}{cz + d}.
$$
For the modular group $\Gamma = \operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})/\{\pm 1\}$, this action restricts to a properly discontinuous action on $\mathcal{H}$.
