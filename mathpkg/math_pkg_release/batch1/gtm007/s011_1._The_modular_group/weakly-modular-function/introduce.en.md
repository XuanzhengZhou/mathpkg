A weakly modular function satisfies the basic transformation property under Möbius transformations from the modular group. For weight $k=0$, these are simply $\Gamma$-invariant functions. Adding the condition of holomorphy at cusps (including $i\infty$) upgrades a weakly modular function to a modular form.
