The Hecke bound (or "trivial bound") for Fourier coefficients of cusp forms follows from the boundedness of $y^{k/2}|f(z)|$ on the upper half-plane. The Ramanujan-Petersson conjecture (proved by Deligne for weight $k \geq 2$) improves this to $|a_n| \leq d(n) n^{(k-1)/2}$, where $d(n)$ is the divisor function.
