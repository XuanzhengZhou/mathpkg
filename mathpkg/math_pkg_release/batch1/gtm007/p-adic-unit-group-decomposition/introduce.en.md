This decomposes the $p$-adic unit group $U = \mathbb{Z}_p^*$ into the finite cyclic group $V$ of $(p-1)$-st roots of unity (Teichmüller representatives) and the pro-$p$ group $U_1 = 1 + p\mathbb{Z}_p$. The map $U \to \mathbb{F}_p^*$ splits canonically when $p \neq 2$.
