The Hecke operators $T(n)$ are Hermitian (self-adjoint) with respect to the Petersson scalar product: $\langle T(n)f, g\rangle = \langle f, T(n)g\rangle$. Combined with their commutativity, this implies that $M_k^0$ admits an orthogonal basis of simultaneous eigenfunctions of all $T(n)$. Moreover, the eigenvalues are totally real algebraic integers, a consequence of the integrality of the characteristic polynomial of $T(n)$.
