With respect to the Petersson scalar product on $M_k^0$, the Hecke operators $T(n)$ are Hermitian:
$$\langle T(n)f, g \rangle = \langle f, T(n)g \rangle \quad \text{for all } f, g \in M_k^0.$$
Since the $T(n)$ commute with each other, there exists an orthogonal basis of $M_k^0$ consisting of simultaneous eigenfunctions of all $T(n)$. The eigenvalues of $T(n)$ are totally real algebraic numbers.
