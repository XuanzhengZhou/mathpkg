The Petersson scalar product is a natural Hermitian inner product on the space $M_k^0$ of cusp forms of weight $2k$, defined by $\langle f,g\rangle = \int_D f(z)\overline{g(z)} y^{2k-2}dxdy$. The measure $f(z)\overline{g(z)} y^{2k} dxdy/y^2$ is invariant under the modular group, making the integral well-defined over the fundamental domain $D$. This inner product makes $M_k^0$ into a Hilbert space and is fundamental for the spectral theory of automorphic forms.
