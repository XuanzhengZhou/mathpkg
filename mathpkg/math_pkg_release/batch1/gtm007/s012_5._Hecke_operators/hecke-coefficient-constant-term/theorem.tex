For the Fourier coefficients $\gamma(m)$ of $T(n)f$ as defined in Proposition 12, the constant term and the first coefficient are given by:
$$\gamma(0) = \sigma_{2k-1}(n)c(0) \quad \text{and} \quad \gamma(1) = c(n),$$
where $\sigma_{2k-1}(n) = \sum_{d \mid n} d^{2k-1}$ is the sum-of-divisors function.
