Corollary 1 of Theorem 8 decomposes the theta function of an even unimodular lattice as $\theta_\Gamma = E_{n/4} + f_\Gamma$, where $E_{n/4}$ is an Eisenstein series and $f_\Gamma$ is a cusp form. When the space of cusp forms of the corresponding weight vanishes (as for $n=8$ giving weight $4$, or $n=16$ giving weight $8$), the theta function is exactly an Eisenstein series, giving explicit formulas for representation numbers $r_\Gamma(m)$ in terms of divisor sums.
