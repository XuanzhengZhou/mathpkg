Let $f$ be a modular function with Laurent expansion
$$f(z) = \sum_{m \in \mathbf{Z}} c(m)q^m, \quad q = e^{2\pi iz}.$$
Then $T(n)f$ is a modular function and its Fourier expansion is
$$T(n)f(z) = \sum_{m \in \mathbf{Z}} \gamma(m)q^m$$
with coefficients given by
$$\gamma(m) = \sum_{\substack{a \mid (n, m) \\ a \geq 1}} a^{2k-1}c\left(\frac{mn}{a^2}\right).$$
