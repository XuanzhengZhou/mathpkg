Corollary 3 of Proposition 12 states that Hecke operators $T(n)$ preserve the spaces $M_k$ of modular forms and $M_k^0$ of cusp forms of weight $2k$. This is immediate from the coefficient formula: if $c(0)=0$ (cusp form) then $\gamma(0)=0$, and the growth conditions are preserved. This makes $M_k$ and $M_k^0$ into modules over the Hecke algebra.
