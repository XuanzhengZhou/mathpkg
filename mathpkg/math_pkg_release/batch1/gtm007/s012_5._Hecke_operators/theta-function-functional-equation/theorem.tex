Let $V$ be a real vector space of dimension $n$ endowed with a positive, nondegenerate symmetric bilinear form $x \cdot y$. Let $\Gamma$ be a lattice in $V$ and $v = \mu(V/\Gamma)$. For $t > 0$, define
$$\Theta_\Gamma(t) = \sum_{x \in \Gamma} e^{-\pi t (x \cdot x)}.$$
Then the functional equation holds:
$$\Theta_\Gamma(t) = t^{-n/2} v^{-1} \Theta_{\Gamma'}(t^{-1}),$$
where $\Gamma'$ is the dual lattice of $\Gamma$ with respect to the bilinear form.
