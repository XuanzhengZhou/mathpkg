The Hecke operator $T(n)$ is a correspondence on the set $\mathcal{R}$ of lattices in $\mathbf{C}$ that sends a lattice $\Gamma$ to the formal sum of all its sublattices of index $n$. Since sublattices of index $n$ all contain $n\Gamma$, the sum is finite. This operator is the geometric origin of Hecke theory on modular forms.
