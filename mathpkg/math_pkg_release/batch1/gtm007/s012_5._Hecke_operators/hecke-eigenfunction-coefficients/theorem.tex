Let $f(z) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} c(n)q^n$ be a modular form of weight $2k$ with $k > 0$, not identically zero. Assume that $f$ is an eigenfunction of all Hecke operators $T(n)$:
$$T(n)f = \lambda(n)f \quad \text{for all } n \geq 1.$$
Then:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] The coefficient $c(1)$ of $q$ in $f$ is nonzero.
\item[(b)] If $f$ is normalized by the condition $c(1) = 1$, then
$$c(n) = \lambda(n) \quad \text{for all } n \geq 1.$$
\end{itemize}
