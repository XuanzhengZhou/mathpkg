Let $f = \sum c(n)q^n$ be a normalized eigenfunction of all Hecke operators with $c(1) = 1$. Let
$$\Phi_f(s) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{c(n)}{n^s}$$
be the associated Dirichlet series. Then $\Phi_f(s)$ admits the Euler product expansion:
$$\Phi_f(s) = \prod_{p \in \mathcal{P}} \frac{1}{1 - c(p)p^{-s} + p^{2k-1-2s}},$$
where the product runs over all primes $p$.
