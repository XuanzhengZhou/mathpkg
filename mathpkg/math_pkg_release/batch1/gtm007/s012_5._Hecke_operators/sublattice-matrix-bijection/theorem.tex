Let $\Gamma$ be a lattice in $\mathbf{C}$ with basis $\{\omega_1, \omega_2\}$. Let $S_n$ be the set of integer matrices $\sigma = \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ 0 & d \end{pmatrix}$ with $ad = n$, $a \geq 1$, and $0 \leq b < d$. For $\sigma \in S_n$, define the sublattice $\Gamma_\sigma$ of $\Gamma$ with basis
$$\omega'_1 = a\omega_1 + b\omega_2, \quad \omega'_2 = d\omega_2.$$
Then the map $\sigma \mapsto \Gamma_\sigma$ is a bijection of $S_n$ onto the set $\Gamma(n)$ of sublattices of index $n$ in $\Gamma$.
