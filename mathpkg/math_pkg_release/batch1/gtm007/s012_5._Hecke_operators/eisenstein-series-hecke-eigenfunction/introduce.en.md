Proposition 13 exhibits the Eisenstein series $G_k$ as the prototypical Hecke eigenform. Using the lattice definition $G_k(\Gamma) = \sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma} 1/\gamma^{2k}$, one computes directly that $T(p)G_k = (1 + p^{2k-1})G_k = \sigma_{2k-1}(p)G_k$. The associated Dirichlet series is the product $\zeta(s)\zeta(s-2k+1)$, reflecting the divisor-sum coefficients of Eisenstein series.
