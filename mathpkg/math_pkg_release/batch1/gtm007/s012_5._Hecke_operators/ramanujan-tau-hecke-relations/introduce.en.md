As a corollary of the general Hecke theory applied to the unique normalized cusp form $\Delta$ of weight $12$, the Ramanujan tau function satisfies $\tau(mn) = \tau(m)\tau(n)$ for coprime $m,n$ and the recurrence $\tau(p)\tau(p^n) = \tau(p^{n+1}) + p^{11}\tau(p^{n-1})$. These relations, originally conjectured by Ramanujan, follow from the fact that the space $M_6^0$ of cusp forms of weight $12$ is one-dimensional.
