The Poisson summation formula is the analytic foundation for theta functions. It relates a sum over a lattice to a sum over the dual lattice of the Fourier transform. When applied to the Gaussian $f(x) = e^{-\pi t x \cdot x}$, it yields the modularity of theta functions.
