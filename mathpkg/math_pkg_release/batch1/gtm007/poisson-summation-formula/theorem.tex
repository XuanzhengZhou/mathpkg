\textbf{Proposition 15 (Poisson formula).} Let $V$ be a real vector space of finite dimension, $\Gamma$ a lattice in $V$, $\mu$ a Haar measure on $V$, and $f$ a rapidly decreasing function on $V$. Then
$$\sum_{x \in \Gamma} f(x) = \frac{1}{v} \sum_{y \in \Gamma'} \hat{f}(y),$$
where $v = \mu(V/\Gamma)$, $\Gamma'$ is the dual lattice, and $\hat{f}$ is the Fourier transform.
