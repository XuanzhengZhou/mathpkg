This fundamental theorem classifies all finite fields. Up to isomorphism, there is exactly one finite field for each prime power $q = p^f$. The field $\mathbb{F}_q$ is characterized as the splitting field of $X^q - X$ over $\mathbb{F}_p$, and its elements are precisely the roots of this polynomial in any algebraically closed extension.
