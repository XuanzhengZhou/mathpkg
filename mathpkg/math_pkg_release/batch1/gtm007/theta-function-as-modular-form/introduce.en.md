Theta functions of even unimodular lattices are modular forms. Since $M_k$ is known explicitly, this gives strong constraints on representation numbers. For example, when $n=8$, $M_4$ is 1-dimensional, so $\theta_{\Gamma_8} = E_2$, giving $r_{\Gamma_8}(m) = 240\sigma_3(m)$.
