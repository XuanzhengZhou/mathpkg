Translation of Theorem 1 into the language of forms: every quadratic form is equivalent to a diagonal form $a_1X_1^2 + \cdots + a_nX_n^2$. The coefficients $a_i$ are determined up to squares. The rank is the number of nonzero diagonal entries.
