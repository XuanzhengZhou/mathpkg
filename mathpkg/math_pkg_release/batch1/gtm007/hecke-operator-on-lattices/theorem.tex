Let $\mathcal{R}$ be the set of lattices of $\mathbb{C}$ and let $n \geq 1$. The Hecke operator $T(n)$ is the correspondence on $\mathcal{R}$ that transforms a lattice $\Gamma$ to the sum (in the free abelian group $X_{\mathcal{R}}$) of its sublattices of index $n$:
$$T(n)\Gamma = \sum_{(\Gamma:\Gamma') = n} \Gamma'.$$
