Let $0 \rightarrow A \rightarrow E \rightarrow B \rightarrow 0$ be an exact sequence of commutative groups (written additively) with $A$ and $B$ finite, of orders $a$ and $b$ respectively, where $a$ and $b$ are coprime. Let $B' = \{x \in E \mid bx = 0\}$ be the $b$-torsion subgroup. Then:

\textbf{(i)} $E$ is the direct sum $E = A \oplus B'$.

\textbf{(ii)} $B'$ is the unique subgroup of $E$ isomorphic to $B$.
