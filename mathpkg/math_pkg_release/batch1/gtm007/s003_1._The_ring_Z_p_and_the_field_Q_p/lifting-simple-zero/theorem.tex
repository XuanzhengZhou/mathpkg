Let $f \in \mathbb{Z}_p[X_1, \ldots, X_m]$ be a polynomial with $p$-adic integer coefficients. Every simple zero of the reduction of $f$ modulo $p$ lifts to a zero of $f$ with coefficients in $\mathbb{Z}_p$.

(If $g$ is a polynomial over a field $k$, a zero $x$ of $g$ is called \textit{simple} if at least one of the partial derivatives $\partial g / \partial X_j$ is nonzero at $x$.)
