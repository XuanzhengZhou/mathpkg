Let $U = \mathbb{Z}_p^\times$ be the group of $p$-adic units, and let
$$U_n = \{x \in U \mid x \equiv 1 \pmod{p^n}\}, \quad n \geq 1,$$
be the subgroups of $U$ forming its canonical filtration. For $n \geq 1$, the map $x \mapsto (x-1)/p^n$ induces an isomorphism $U_n/U_{n+1} \xrightarrow{\sim} \mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z}$. In particular, $U_1/U_n$ has order $p^{n-1}$.

One has the decomposition
$$U = V \times U_1,$$
where $V = \{x \in U \mid x^{p-1} = 1\} \cong \mathbb{F}_p^*$ is the group of Teichm\"uller representatives, i.e., the $(p-1)$-th roots of unity in $\mathbb{Z}_p$.
