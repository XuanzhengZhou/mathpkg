Let $U_1, \ldots, U_m$ be vector subspaces of $V$. One says that $V$ is the \textbf{orthogonal direct sum} of the $U_i$ if they are pairwise orthogonal and if $V$ is the direct sum of them. One writes then:

$$V = U_1 \;\hat{\oplus}\; \cdots \;\hat{\oplus}\; U_m.$$

If $x \in V$ has components $x_i$ in $U_i$, then $Q(x) = \sum Q(x_i)$.
