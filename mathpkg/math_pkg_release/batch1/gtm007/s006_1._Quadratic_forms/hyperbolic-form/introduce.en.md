A hyperbolic form in two variables is the translation of the hyperbolic plane concept into the language of quadratic forms (polynomials in variables $X_i$). Up to equivalence, a hyperbolic form is either $X_1 X_2$ or $X_1^2 - X_2^2$, representing the simplest example of a nondegenerate isotropic quadratic form.
