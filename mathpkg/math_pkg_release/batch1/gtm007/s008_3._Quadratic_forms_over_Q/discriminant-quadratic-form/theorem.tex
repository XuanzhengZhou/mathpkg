Let $f \sim a_1 X_1^2 + \cdots + a_n X_n^2$ be a nondegenerate quadratic form of rank $n$ with coefficients in $\mathbf{Q}$. The discriminant $d(f) \in \mathbf{Q}^*/\mathbf{Q}^{*2}$ is defined by

$$d(f) = a_1 \cdots a_n \pmod{\mathbf{Q}^{*2}}.$$

The discriminant is an invariant of the equivalence class of $f$ over $\mathbf{Q}$.
