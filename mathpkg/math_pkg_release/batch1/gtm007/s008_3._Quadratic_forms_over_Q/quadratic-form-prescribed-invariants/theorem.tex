Let $n \geq 1$. For a nondegenerate quadratic form $f$ of rank $n$ over $\mathbf{Q}$ to exist with prescribed local invariants $(r, s)$ (signature over $\mathbf{R}$), $d \in \mathbf{Q}^*/\mathbf{Q}^{*2}$ (discriminant), and $\varepsilon_v \in \{\pm 1\}$ for each $v \in V$, it is necessary and sufficient that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $r + s = n$, and the signature determines $\varepsilon_\infty = (-1)^{s(s-1)/2}$,
\item[(2)] Almost all $\varepsilon_v = 1$ and $\prod_{v \in V} \varepsilon_v = 1$,
\item[(3)] $\varepsilon_v = 1$ for all $v$ whenever $n = 1$,
\item[(4)] For $n = 2$, the image $d_v$ of $d$ in $\mathbf{Q}_v^*/\mathbf{Q}_v^{*2}$ must satisfy $\varepsilon_v = (d_v, -1)_v$ for each $v$,
\item[(5)] For $n = 3$, the local conditions relating $d_v$ and $\varepsilon_v$ from Chapter IV, §2 must hold.
\end{enumerate}
