Theorem 9 extends the Hasse–Minkowski local-global principle to the equivalence of quadratic forms. Two rational quadratic forms are equivalent over $\mathbf{Q}$ precisely when they are equivalent over every completion $\mathbf{Q}_v$. The proof uses induction on the rank and Corollary 1 to Theorem 8 for common representation, combined with Witt's cancellation theorem.
