For a lattice $\Gamma$ in a vector space $V$ with a positive definite quadratic form, the theta function is
$$\Theta_\Gamma(t) = \sum_{x \in \Gamma} e^{-\pi t x \cdot x}, \quad (t > 0).$$
For a unimodular lattice $E \in S_n$, the theta function as a function on $H$ is
$$\theta_E(z) = \sum_{x \in E} e^{\pi i z (x \cdot x)} = \sum_{m=0}^{\infty} r_E(m) q^m, \quad q = e^{2\pi i z},$$
where $r_E(m) = \#\{x \in E : x \cdot x = 2m\}$.
