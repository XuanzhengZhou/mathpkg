The orthogonality relations: the sum of a nontrivial character over the whole group is zero. This is the finite analogue of $\int_0^1 e^{2\pi i n x} dx = 0$ for $n \neq 0$. The dual relation $\sum_{\chi} \chi(x) = 0$ for $x \neq 1$ follows by applying the same to $\hat{G}$.
