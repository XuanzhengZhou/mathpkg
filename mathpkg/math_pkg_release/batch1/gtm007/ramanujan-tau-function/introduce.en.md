Ramanujan's $\tau$-function is the prototypical cusp form coefficient. It is multiplicative: $\tau(mn) = \tau(m)\tau(n)$ for coprime $m,n$, and satisfies the recurrence $\tau(p^{n+1}) = \tau(p)\tau(p^n) - p^{11}\tau(p^{n-1})$. Deligne's proof of $|\tau(p)| \leq 2p^{11/2}$ was a major achievement.
