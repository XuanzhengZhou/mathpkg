The quotient $\mathbb{Q}_p^*/\mathbb{Q}_p^{*2}$ is an $\mathbb{F}_2$-vector space of dimension $2$, generated by $p$ (valuation) and a quadratic non-residue $u$ modulo $p$. This fact is crucial for computing the Hilbert symbol, which is a nondegenerate bilinear form on this space.
