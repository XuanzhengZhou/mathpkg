The $p$-adic valuation $v_p$ measures the power of $p$ dividing a $p$-adic number. It induces the $p$-adic absolute value $|x|_p = p^{-v_p(x)}$, with respect to which $\mathbb{Q}_p$ is complete and $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is the closed unit ball.
