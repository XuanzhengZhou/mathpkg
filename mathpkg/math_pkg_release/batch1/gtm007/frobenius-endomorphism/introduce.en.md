In characteristic $p$, the map $x \mapsto x^p$ is a field homomorphism. Additivity follows from the binomial theorem and the fact that $\binom{p}{k} \equiv 0 \pmod{p}$ for $0 < k < p$. The map is injective, hence an isomorphism onto its image $K^p$. For finite fields it is an automorphism.
