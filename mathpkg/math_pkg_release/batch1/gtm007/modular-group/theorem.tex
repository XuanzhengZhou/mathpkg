Let $\mathbf{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z})$ be the group of $2 \times 2$ integer matrices with determinant $1$. The modular group is
$$G = \mathbf{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z})/\{\pm 1\} = \mathbf{PSL}_2(\mathbb{Z}).$$
It acts on the upper half-plane $H = \{z \in \mathbb{C} : \operatorname{Im}(z) > 0\}$ by $gz = \frac{az+b}{cz+d}$ for $g = \begin{pmatrix}a&b\\c&d\end{pmatrix}$.
