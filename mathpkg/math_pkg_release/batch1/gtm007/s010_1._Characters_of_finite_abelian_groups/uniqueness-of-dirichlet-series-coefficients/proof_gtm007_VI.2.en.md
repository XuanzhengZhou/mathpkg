We show $a_0 = 0$ first. Multiply $f$ by $e^{\lambda_0 z}$ and let $z \to +\infty$ (with $z$ real). The uniform convergence shows that $e^{\lambda_0 z} f(z)$ tends to $a_0$, hence $a_0 = 0$. Then proceed similarly for $a_1$: multiply by $e^{\lambda_1 z}$, and so on. By induction, all coefficients vanish.
