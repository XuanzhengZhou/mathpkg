A character of a finite abelian group $G$ is a group homomorphism from $G$ into the multiplicative group $\mathbb{C}^*$ of nonzero complex numbers. Characters are the fundamental building blocks of harmonic analysis on finite abelian groups. They provide a way to study the structure of $G$ through its one-dimensional complex representations, and the set of all characters forms the dual group $\widehat{G}$.
