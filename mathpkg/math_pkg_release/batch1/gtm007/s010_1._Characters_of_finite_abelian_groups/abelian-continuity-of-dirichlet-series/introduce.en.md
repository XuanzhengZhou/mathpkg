Corollary 3 states that a convergent Dirichlet series is continuous at $z_0$ when approaching $z_0$ from within a Stolz angle (angular region). This follows from the uniform convergence established in Proposition 6 together with the continuity of each term $e^{-\lambda_n z}$.
