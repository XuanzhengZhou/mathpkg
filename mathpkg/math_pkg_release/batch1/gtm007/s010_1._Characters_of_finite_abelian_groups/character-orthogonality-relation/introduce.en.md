Proposition 4 and its corollary (Chapter VI, §1.2) give the orthogonality relations: the characters of a finite abelian group satisfy the same fundamental identities as the irreducible characters of any finite group. These formulas are essential for Fourier analysis on finite abelian groups and for detecting membership in subgroups.
