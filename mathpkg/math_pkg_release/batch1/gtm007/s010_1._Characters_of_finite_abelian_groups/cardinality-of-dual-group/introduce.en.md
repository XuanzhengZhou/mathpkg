Proposition 2 establishes that a finite abelian group $G$ and its dual $\widehat{G}$ have the same number of elements. The proof uses induction on $|G|$ together with the exact sequence from Proposition 1: for a nontrivial cyclic subgroup $H$, we have $|\widehat{G}| = |\widehat{G/H}| \cdot |\widehat{H}| = |G/H| \cdot |H| = |G|$ by the induction hypothesis applied to $G/H$ and the cyclic case for $H$.
