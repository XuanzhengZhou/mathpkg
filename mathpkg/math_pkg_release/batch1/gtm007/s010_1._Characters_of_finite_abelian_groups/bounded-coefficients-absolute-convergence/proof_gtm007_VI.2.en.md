If $|a_n| \leq M$ for all $n$, then $|a_n / n^s| \leq M / n^{\Re(s)}$. The series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} 1/n^{\alpha}$ is well known to converge for $\alpha > 1$, so by comparison $\sum |a_n / n^s|$ converges for $\Re(s) > 1$.
