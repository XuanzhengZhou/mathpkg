For an integer $m \geq 1$, let $G(m) = (\mathbf{Z}/m\mathbf{Z})^*$ be the multiplicative group of invertible residue classes modulo $m$. A \textbf{character modulo $m$} is a character of the finite abelian group $G(m)$, extended to all of $\mathbf{Z}$ by setting $\chi(a) = 0$ if $\gcd(a,m) \neq 1$. The order of $G(m)$ is $\phi(m)$.
