$G(m) = (\mathbb{Z}/m\mathbb{Z})^*$ is the multiplicative group of residue classes modulo $m$ that are coprime to $m$. Its order is $\phi(m)$, Euler's totient function. This group is the natural domain for Dirichlet characters modulo $m$, which are simply characters of this finite abelian group. The structure of $G(m)$ (e.g., its decomposition into cyclic factors) determines the complete set of characters modulo $m$.
