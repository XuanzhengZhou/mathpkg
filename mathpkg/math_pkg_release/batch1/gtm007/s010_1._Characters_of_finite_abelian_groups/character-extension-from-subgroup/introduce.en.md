Proposition 1 states that for any subgroup $H$ of a finite abelian group $G$, every character of $H$ can be extended to a character of $G$. This means the restriction map $\rho \colon \widehat{G} \to \widehat{H}$ is surjective. The kernel of $\rho$ consists of characters trivial on $H$, which is naturally isomorphic to $\widehat{G/H}$, giving the short exact sequence $\{1\} \to \widehat{G/H} \to \widehat{G} \to \widehat{H} \to \{1\}$.
