Apply the second orthogonality formula (Corollary to Proposition 4) to the dual group $\hat{G}$ with $x = a^{-1}p$. The sum $\sum_\chi \chi(a^{-1}p)$ equals $\phi(m)$ if $a^{-1}p \equiv 1 \pmod{m}$ (i.e., $p \equiv a \pmod{m}$) and $0$ otherwise. This is exactly the formula used in Lemma 9.
