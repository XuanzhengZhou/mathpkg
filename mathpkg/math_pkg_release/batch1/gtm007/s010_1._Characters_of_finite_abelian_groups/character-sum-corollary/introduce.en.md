This corollary applies the orthogonality relations to the group $G(m) = (\mathbf{Z}/m\mathbf{Z})^*$ of order $\phi(m)$. It is a key computational tool used in Lemma 9 and Theorem 2 of Chapter VI to detect when a prime belongs to a given residue class modulo $m$.
