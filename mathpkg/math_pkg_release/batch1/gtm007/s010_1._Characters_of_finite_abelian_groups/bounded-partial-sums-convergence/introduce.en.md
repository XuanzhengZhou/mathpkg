Proposition 9 (Chapter VI, §2.4): Bounded partial sums guarantee conditional convergence for $R(s) > 0$. This is proved using Abel's lemma (summation by parts). The result is crucial for establishing the convergence of $L$-functions $L(s,\chi)$ for nontrivial characters $\chi 
eq 1$.
