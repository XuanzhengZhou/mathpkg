Lemma 8 (Chapter VI, §4.2): The key analytic input is that $L(1,\chi) 
eq 0$ for $\chi 
eq 1$. This ensures that $\log L(s,\chi)$ remains bounded as $s 	o 1^+$, and the difference between $\log L(s,\chi)$ and $f_\chi(s)$ (coming from higher prime powers) is also bounded.
