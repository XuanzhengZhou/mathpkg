Proposition 2 states that for any finite abelian group $G$, the dual group $\widehat{G}$ has the same cardinality as $G$: $|\widehat{G}| = |G|$. The proof uses induction on the order of $G$, the extension property (Proposition 1), and the fact that cyclic groups are self-dual. This result is a crucial step toward establishing the bidual isomorphism.
