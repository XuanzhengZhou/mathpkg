Proposition 6 provides a key convergence result for general Dirichlet series: convergence at a point $z_0$ implies uniform convergence in a angular region extending to the right of $z_0$. This is an Abelian-type theorem. The proof uses Abel's lemma (summation by parts) and an estimate for differences of exponentials (Lemma 3). The result is essential for establishing analyticity of $L$-functions in half-planes.
