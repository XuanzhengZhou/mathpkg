For the principal character $\chi = 1$ modulo $m$,
$$
L(s,1) = F(s)\zeta(s), \quad \text{where } F(s) = \prod_{p \mid m} \left(1 - \frac{1}{p^s}\right).
$$
In particular, $L(s,1)$ extends analytically to $R(s) > 0$ (except for a simple pole at $s=1$) and has a simple pole at $s=1$ with residue $\phi(m)/m$.
