This proposition shows that $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is a discrete valuation ring with uniformizer $p$. Every nonzero $p$-adic integer factors uniquely as $p^n$ times a unit. The $p$-adic valuation $v_p(x) = n$ measures divisibility by $p$.
