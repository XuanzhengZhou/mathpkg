The theorem **Inf($b$) $\wedge 2 \leq \bar{a} \leq \overline{\mathcal{P}(b)} \rightarrow \bar{a}^b = \overline{\mathcal{P}(b)}$** (Theorem 11.21.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
