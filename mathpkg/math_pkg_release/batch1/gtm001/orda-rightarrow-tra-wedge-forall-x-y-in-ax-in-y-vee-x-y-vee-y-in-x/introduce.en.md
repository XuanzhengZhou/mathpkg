The definition **Ord($A$) $\rightarrow$ Tr($A$) $\wedge (\forall x, y \in A)[x \in y \vee x = y \vee y \in x]$** (Definition 7.3.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
