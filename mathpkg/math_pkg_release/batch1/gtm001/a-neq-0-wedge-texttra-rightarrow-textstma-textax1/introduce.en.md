The proposition **$A \neq 0 \wedge \text{Tr}(A) \rightarrow \text{STM}(A, \text{Ax.1})$** (Proposition 13.24.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
