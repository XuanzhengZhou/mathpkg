The proposition **$M[G]$ is transitive and the ordinals in $M[G]$ are the ordinals in $M$** (Proposition 18.13.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
