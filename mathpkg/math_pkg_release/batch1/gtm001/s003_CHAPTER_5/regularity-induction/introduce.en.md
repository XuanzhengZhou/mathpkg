Proposition 5.22 derives an induction principle from the strong form of the Axiom of Regularity: if a class $A$ has the property that whenever all elements of a set $x$ belong to $A$, then $x$ itself belongs to $A$, then $A$ must be the universal class $V$. This is the set-theoretic analogue of $\in$-induction.
