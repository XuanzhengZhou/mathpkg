The ordered $n$-tuple $\langle a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n \rangle$ is defined recursively for $n \geq 3$ as the ordered pair $\langle \langle a_1, \ldots, a_{n-1} \rangle, a_n \rangle$. Since ordered pairs are sets, it follows by induction that ordered $n$-tuples are also sets.
