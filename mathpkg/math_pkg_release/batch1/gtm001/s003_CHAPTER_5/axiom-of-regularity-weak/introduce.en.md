The weak form of the Axiom of Regularity (Axiom 6) asserts that every nonempty set $a$ contains an element $x$ that is disjoint from $a$, i.e., $x \cap a = 0$. This axiom prevents infinite descending $\in$-chains and rules out sets that contain themselves as elements.
