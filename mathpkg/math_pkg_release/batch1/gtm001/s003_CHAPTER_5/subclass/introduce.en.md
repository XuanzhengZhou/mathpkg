$A$ is a subclass of $B$, written $A \subseteq B$, if every element of $A$ is also an element of $B$. $A$ is a proper subclass of $B$, written $A \subset B$, if $A \subseteq B$ and $A \neq B$. The symbol $A \subseteq B$ is read as "$A$ is a subclass of $B$."
