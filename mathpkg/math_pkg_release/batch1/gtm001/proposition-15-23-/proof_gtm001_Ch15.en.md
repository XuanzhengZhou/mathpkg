(1) $x \in y \land y \in L \rightarrow x \in F' \text{ Od}'y$

$$\rightarrow x \in F'' \text{ Od}'y$$

$$\rightarrow (\exists \beta < \text{Od}'y)[x = F'\beta]$$

$$\rightarrow \text{Od}'x < \text{Od}'y.$$

(2) The proof is left to the reader. $\square$
