The proposition **Let $\varphi$ be an atomic sentence for which $E(\varphi)$ is defined. Then** (Proposition 18.16.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
