The lemma **$b = \mathcal{F}_n(c, d)$ Abs $M$, $n = 1, \ldots, 8$** (Lemma 5.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
