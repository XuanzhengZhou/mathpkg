The proposition **Ord($A$) $\wedge$ Ord($B$) $\wedge F \text{ Isom}_{E, E}(A, B) \rightarrow A = B$** (Proposition 7.39.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
