The definition **$\rho(\hat{x}_i^\alpha \varphi(x_i)) \triangleq \alpha$, $\rho(\mathbf{k}) \triangleq \text{rank}(k)$ and $\rho(\mathbf{G}) \triangleq \text{rank}(P) \triangleq \rho(\mathbf{P})$** (Definition 18.7.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
