$\vdash \varphi$ indicates that $\varphi$ is deducible from the logical axioms and nonlogical axioms by the rules of inference.

$\vdash_{\text{LA}} \varphi$ indicates that $\varphi$ is deducible using only the logical axioms.

Two wffs $\varphi$ and $\psi$ are logically equivalent iff $\vdash_{\text{LA}} \varphi \leftrightarrow \psi$.
