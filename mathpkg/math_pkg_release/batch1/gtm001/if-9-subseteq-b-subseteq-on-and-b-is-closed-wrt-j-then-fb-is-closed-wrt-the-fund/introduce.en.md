The lemma **If $9 \subseteq b \subseteq$ On and $b$ is closed w.r.t. $J$, then $F''b$ is closed w.r.t. the fundamental operations** (Lemma 1.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
