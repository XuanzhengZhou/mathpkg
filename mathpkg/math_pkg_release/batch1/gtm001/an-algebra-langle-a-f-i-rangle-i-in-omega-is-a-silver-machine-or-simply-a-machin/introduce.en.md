The definition **An algebra $\langle A, F_i \rangle_{i \in \omega}$ is a Silver machine, or simply a machine, iff** (Definition 16.11.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
