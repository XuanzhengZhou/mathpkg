The corollary **Corollary 6.8.** (Corollary 6.8.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 6 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
