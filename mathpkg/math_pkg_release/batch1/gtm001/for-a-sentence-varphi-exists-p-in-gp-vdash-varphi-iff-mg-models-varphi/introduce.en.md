The theorem **For a sentence $\varphi$, $(\exists p \in G)(p \Vdash \varphi)$ iff $M[G] \models \varphi$** (Theorem 19.7.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 19 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
