(1) $[A, R] \models a \in b \leftrightarrow a \in A \wedge b \in A \wedge a R b$.

(2) $[A, R] \models \neg \psi \leftrightarrow \neg [[A, R] \models \psi]$.

(3) $[A, R] \models \psi \wedge \eta \overset{\Delta}{\rightarrow} [[A, R] \models \psi] \wedge [[A, R] \models \eta]$.

(4) $[A, R] \models (\forall x) \psi(x) \overset{\Delta}{\rightarrow} (\forall x \in A) [[A, R] \models \psi(x)]$.
