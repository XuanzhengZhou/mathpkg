The proposition **$A \subseteq \text{On} \land (\forall \beta \in A)[\beta \leq \alpha] \rightarrow \cup(A) \leq \alpha$** (Proposition 7.21.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
