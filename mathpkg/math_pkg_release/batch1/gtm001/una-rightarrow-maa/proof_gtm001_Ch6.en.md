From Definition 6.4(2)

$$Un(A) \leftrightarrow (\forall x)(\forall y)(\forall z)[\langle x, y \rangle \in A \land \langle x, z \rangle \in A \rightarrow y = z]$$

Then from the Axiom Schema of Replacement it follows that

$$\{y | (\exists x \in a)[\langle x, y \rangle \in A]\}$$

is a set, that is, $A“a$ is a set.

Remark. Proposition 6.7 assures us that single valued relations, i.e., functions, map sets onto sets.
