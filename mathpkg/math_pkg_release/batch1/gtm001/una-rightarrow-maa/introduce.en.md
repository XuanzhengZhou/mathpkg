The proposition **$Un(A) \rightarrow M(A“a)$** (Proposition 6.7.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 6 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
