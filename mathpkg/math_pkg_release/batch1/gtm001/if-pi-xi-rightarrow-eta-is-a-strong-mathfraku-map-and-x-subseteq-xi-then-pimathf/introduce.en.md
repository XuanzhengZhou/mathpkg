The theorem **If $\pi: \xi \rightarrow \eta$ is a strong $\mathfrak{U}$-map and $X \subseteq \xi$, then $\pi“\mathfrak{U}^\xi(X) = \mathfrak{U}^\eta(\pi“X)$** (Theorem 16.19.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
