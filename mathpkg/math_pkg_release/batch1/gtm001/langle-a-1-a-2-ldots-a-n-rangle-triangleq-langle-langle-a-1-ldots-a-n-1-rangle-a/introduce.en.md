The definition **$\langle a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n \rangle \triangleq \langle \langle a_1, \ldots, a_{n-1} \rangle, a_n \rangle, n \geq 3$** (Definition 5.4.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 5 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
