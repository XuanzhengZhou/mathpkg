Defines the second and third converse operations on classes of ordered triples. $\mathrm{Cnv}_2(A)$ cyclically permutes the triple $\langle z, x, y \rangle$ to $\langle x, y, z \rangle$, while $\mathrm{Cnv}_3(A)$ swaps the second and third coordinates. These operations are part of Gödel's eight fundamental operations used in the definition of the constructible universe $.
