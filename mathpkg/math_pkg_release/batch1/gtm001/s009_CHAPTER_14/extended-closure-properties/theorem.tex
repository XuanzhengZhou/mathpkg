If $M$ is transitive, almost universal, and closed under the eight fundamental operations and $a \in M$, then
\begin{itemize}
\item[(1)] $a^n \in M$, $n \geq 1$
\item[(2)] $a^{-1} \in M$
\item[(3)] $\mathcal{D}(a) \in M$
\item[(4)] $\mathcal{W}(a) \in M$
\item[(5)] $\mathrm{Cnv}_2(a) \in M$
\item[(6)] $\mathrm{Cnv}_3(a) \in M$
\end{itemize}
