The main theorem of Chapter 14. A nonempty transitive class $ that is almost universal and closed under Gödel's eight fundamental operations is a standard transitive model of ZF. This provides the sufficient conditions that will be verified for the constructible universe $ in Chapter 15. The proof verifies each ZF axiom using the closure properties established in Propositions 14.5, 14.7, and 14.10.
