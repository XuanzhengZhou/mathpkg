If $M$ is transitive, almost universal, and closed under the eight fundamental operations and $a, b \in M$, then
\begin{itemize}
\item[(1)] $\{a, b\} \in M$
\item[(2)] $\langle a, b \rangle \in M$
\item[(3)] $a \times b \in M$
\item[(4)] $a - b \in M$
\item[(5)] $a \cap b \in M$
\item[(6)] $a \cup b \in M$
\end{itemize}
