If $M$ is transitive, almost universal, and closed under the eight fundamental operations, and if $\varphi(a_1, \ldots, a_m)$ is a wff, if $c_1, \ldots, c_m \in M$, and if $b_1, \ldots, b_n \in M$, then
$$\{\langle x_1, \ldots, x_m \rangle \mid x_1 \in c_1 \land \cdots \land x_m \in c_m \land \varphi^M(x_1, \ldots, x_m)\} \in M.$$
