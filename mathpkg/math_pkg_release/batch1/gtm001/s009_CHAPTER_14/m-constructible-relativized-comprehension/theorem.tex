If $M$ is transitive, almost universal, and closed under the eight fundamental operations, and if $\varphi(a_1, \ldots, a_m)$ is a wff all of whose free variables are among $b_1, \ldots, b_n, a_1, \ldots, a_m$, then $b_1 \in M, \ldots, b_n \in M$ implies
$$A \triangleq \{\langle x_1, \ldots, x_m \rangle \in M^m \mid \varphi^M(x_1, \ldots, x_m)\}$$
is $M$-constructible.
