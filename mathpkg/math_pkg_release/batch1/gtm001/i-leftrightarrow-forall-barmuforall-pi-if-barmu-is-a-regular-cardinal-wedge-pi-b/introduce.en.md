The definition **I $\Leftrightarrow (\forall \bar{\mu})(\forall \pi)$ [If $\bar{\mu}$ is a regular cardinal $\wedge \pi: \bar{\mu} \rightarrow \text{On}$ is a medium $M$-map, then $\pi$ is the identity map on $\bar{\mu}]$** (Definition 17.20.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
