The definition **A subset $G$ of $P$ (outside $M$) is said to be $\mathcal{P}$-generic if** (Definition 18.4.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
