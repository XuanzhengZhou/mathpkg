The definition **If $A$ and $B$ are terms then** (Definition 4.5.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 4 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
