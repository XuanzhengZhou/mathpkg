The definition **$T_\alpha \triangleq \{t | t \text{ is a constant term, and } \rho(t) < \alpha\}$ and $T \triangleq \bigcup_{\alpha \in M} T_\alpha$** (Definition 18.8.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
