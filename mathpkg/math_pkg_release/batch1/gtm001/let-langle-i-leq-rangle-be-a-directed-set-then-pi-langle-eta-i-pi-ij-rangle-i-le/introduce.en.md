The definition **Let $\langle I, \leq \rangle$ be a directed set. Then $\Pi = \langle \eta_i, \pi_{ij} \rangle_{i \leq j}$ is a direct system of order preserving maps iff** (Definition 16.24.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 15 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
