If $\alpha = 0$ or $\beta = 0$, then from Proposition 8.18, $0 \cdot \beta = 0 \wedge \alpha \cdot 0 = 0$. If $\alpha \neq 0$ and $\beta \neq 0$, then $\alpha \geq 1$ and $\beta \geq 1$ and hence $1 \leq \alpha \leq \alpha \beta$, i.e., $\alpha \beta \neq 0$.
