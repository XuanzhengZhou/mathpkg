The proposition **$(\forall x)(\exists y)[x \subseteq y \land \operatorname{Tr}(y) \land (\forall z)[x \subseteq z \land \operatorname{Tr}(z) \rightarrow y \subseteq z]]$** (Proposition 9.1.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
