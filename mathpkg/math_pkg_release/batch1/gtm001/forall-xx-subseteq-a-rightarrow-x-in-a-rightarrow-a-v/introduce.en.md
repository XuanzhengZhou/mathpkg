The proposition **$(\forall x)[x \subseteq A \rightarrow x \in A] \rightarrow A = V.$** (Proposition 5.22.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 5 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
