The proposition **If $R$ Wfr $A$ and $a \subseteq A$ then there exists a set $b$ such that $[a \subseteq b \subseteq A]$ and** (Proposition 9.3.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
