The proposition **$A \in \{x \mid \varphi(x)\} \leftrightarrow \mathcal{M}(A) \wedge \varphi(A)$** (Proposition 4.12.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 4 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
