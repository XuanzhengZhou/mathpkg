The proposition **$\alpha \in K_{\mathrm{II}} \rightarrow \text{cof}(\aleph_\alpha, \alpha)$** (Proposition 11.6.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
