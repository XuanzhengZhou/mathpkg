The lemma **Let $\varphi(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ be a first-order formula without any occurrence of $\exists^\alpha$, or $\hat{x}^\alpha$ and let $t_1, \ldots, t_n \in T$. Then** (Lemma 18.19.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 17 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
