If $\alpha \in N'$, then $\alpha \in K_{\text{II}}$. Since $\alpha$ is cofinal with $cf(\alpha)$ it follows, from Proposition 11.2 that $cf(\alpha) \in K_{\text{II}}$. But then $cf(\alpha) \geq \omega$ and so from Proposition 11.11, $cf(\alpha) \in N'$.
