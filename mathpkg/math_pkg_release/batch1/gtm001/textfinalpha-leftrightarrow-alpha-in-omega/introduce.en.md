The proposition **$\text{Fin}(\alpha) \leftrightarrow \alpha \in \omega$** (Proposition 10.32.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
