A formula $arphi$ is absolute with respect to a class $A$ if its truth is unchanged by relativization to $A$. For formulas with free variables, $arphi$ Abs $A$ means all free variables in $A$ implies $arphi^A \leftrightarrow arphi$. Absoluteness is fundamental for studying inner models.
