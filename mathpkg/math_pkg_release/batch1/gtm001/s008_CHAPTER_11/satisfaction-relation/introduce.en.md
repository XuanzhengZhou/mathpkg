The satisfaction relation $[A, R] \models \varphi$ is defined by induction on the number of logical symbols in $\varphi$, using $\neg$, $\wedge$, and $\forall$ as primitive. A structure consists of a nonempty class $A$ (the universe) and a relation $R \subseteq A \times A$ interpreted as membership.
