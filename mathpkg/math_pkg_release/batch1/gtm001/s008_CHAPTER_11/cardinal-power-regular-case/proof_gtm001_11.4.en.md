Since $\aleph_\beta < cf(\aleph_\alpha)$, every $f: \aleph_\beta \to \aleph_\alpha$ is bounded, so $\aleph_\alpha^{\aleph_\beta} = \cup\{ \aleph_\gamma^{\aleph_\beta} : \gamma < \alpha\}$. For $\gamma < \beta$, $\aleph_\gamma^{\aleph_\beta} \leq \aleph_\beta^{\aleph_\beta} = 2^{\aleph_\beta} < \aleph_\alpha$. For $\beta \leq \gamma < \alpha$, Corollary 11.22 gives $\aleph_\gamma^{\aleph_\beta} \leq 2^{\aleph_\gamma} < \aleph_\alpha$. Since the union has at most $\aleph_\alpha$ terms, $\overline{\cup} \leq \aleph_\alpha \times \aleph_\alpha = \aleph_\alpha$.
