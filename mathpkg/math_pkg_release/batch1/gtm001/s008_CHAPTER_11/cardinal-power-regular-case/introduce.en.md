If the exponent $\aleph_\beta$ is smaller than the cofinality of the base $\aleph_\alpha$, then $\aleph_\alpha^{\aleph_\beta} = \aleph_\alpha$. Every function from $\aleph_\beta$ to $\aleph_\alpha$ is bounded, so the set of all such functions is the union of at most $\aleph_\alpha$ sets of size at most $\aleph_\alpha$.
