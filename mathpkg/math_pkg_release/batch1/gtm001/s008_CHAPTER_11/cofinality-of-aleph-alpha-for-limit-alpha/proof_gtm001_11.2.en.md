If $\alpha \in K_{\text{II}}$, then $\aleph_\alpha$ is cofinal with $\alpha$ (Prop 11.6). By transitivity, $\aleph_\alpha$ is cofinal with $cf(\alpha)$, so $cf(\aleph_\alpha) \leq cf(\alpha)$. Conversely, $\aleph_\alpha$ is cofinal with $cf(\aleph_\alpha)$, so Prop 11.9 gives $cf(\alpha) \leq cf(\aleph_\alpha)$. Equality follows.
