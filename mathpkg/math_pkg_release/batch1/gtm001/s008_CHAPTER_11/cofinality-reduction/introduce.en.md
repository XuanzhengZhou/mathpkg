If there exists a function from $\beta$ into $\alpha$ that is cofinal in $\alpha$, then there exists an ordinal $\eta \leq \beta$ such that $\alpha$ is cofinal with $\eta$ via a strictly monotone function. This refines any cofinal map into a strictly monotone one.
