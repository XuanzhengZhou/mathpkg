For a nonempty transitive class $A$ to model the Power Set Axiom, it is necessary and sufficient that $\mathcal{P}(x) \cap A \in A$ for all $x \in A$. The power set relative to $A$ consists of those subsets of $x$ that happen to be in $A$.
