If $\varphi$ is a theorem of ZF and $A$ is a nonempty class satisfying all nonlogical axioms used in some proof of $\varphi$, then the relativization $\varphi^A$ is also provable. Thus any theorem of ZF remains true when relativized to a model of ZF.
