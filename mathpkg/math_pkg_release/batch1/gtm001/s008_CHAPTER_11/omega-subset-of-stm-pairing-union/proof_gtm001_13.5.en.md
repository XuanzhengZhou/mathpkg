By induction. $0 \in A$ by Prop 13.36. If $k \in A$, then $\{k\}, \{k,\{k\}\} \in A$ by Pairing, and $\cup\{k,\{k\}\} = k+1 \in A$ by Union. Thus $\omega \subseteq A$.
