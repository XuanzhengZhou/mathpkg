By Proposition 10.44, $\overline{\mathcal{P}(b)} = 2^b$. If $\bar{a} \leq 2^b$, then $\bar{a}^b \leq (2^b)^b = 2^{b \times b} = 2^b = \overline{\mathcal{P}(b)}$. On the other hand, $2 \leq \bar{a}$ implies $2^b \leq \bar{a}^b$. Thus equality holds.
