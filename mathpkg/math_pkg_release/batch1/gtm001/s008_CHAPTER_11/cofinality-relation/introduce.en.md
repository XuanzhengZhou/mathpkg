The cofinality relation $\text{cof}(\alpha, \beta)$ expresses that the ordinal $\beta$ is cofinal with the ordinal $\alpha$. This means $\beta \leq \alpha$ and there exists a strictly monotone ordinal function $f$ mapping $\beta$ into $\alpha$ such that every element of $\alpha$ is bounded above by some value in the range of $f$.
