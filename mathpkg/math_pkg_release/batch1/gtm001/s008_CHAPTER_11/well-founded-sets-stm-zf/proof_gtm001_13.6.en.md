$0 \in M$ so $M \neq 0$. Transitivity: if $x \in M$, all elements of $x$ are well-founded. Pairing: well-founded sets form well-founded pairs. Union: union of well-founded sets is well-founded. Powerset: subsets of a well-founded set are well-founded. Replacement is verified using the cumulative hierarchy $R_1\alpha$.
