If $\beta \leq \alpha$ and $\beta$ is equinumerous with $\alpha$, then there exists an ordinal $\eta \leq \beta$ such that $\alpha$ is cofinal with $\eta$. Follows from Proposition 11.7 since a bijection provides a cofinal map.
