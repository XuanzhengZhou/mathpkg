The corollary **$A \subseteq \text{On} \wedge \mathcal{M}(A) \rightarrow (\exists ! \alpha)(\exists ! f)[f \text{Isom}_{E, E}(\alpha, A)]$** (Corollary 7.54.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
