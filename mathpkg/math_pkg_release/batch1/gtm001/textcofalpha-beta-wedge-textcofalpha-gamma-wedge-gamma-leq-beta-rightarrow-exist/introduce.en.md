The proposition **$\text{cof}(\alpha, \beta) \wedge \text{cof}(\alpha, \gamma) \wedge \gamma \leq \beta \rightarrow (\exists \eta \leq \gamma)[\text{cof}(\beta, \eta)]$** (Proposition 11.9.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
