This lemma shows that every strong $M$-map on the ordinals can be lifted to a $\Sigma_1$ elementary embedding of the constructible universe $L$. The embedding $h$ is defined on terms by $h(D(t)) = D(\pi(t))$, and the proof verifies that this is well-defined and preserves $\Sigma_1$ truth. This is a crucial connection between the algebraic structure of the Silver machine and the model-theoretic structure of $L$.
