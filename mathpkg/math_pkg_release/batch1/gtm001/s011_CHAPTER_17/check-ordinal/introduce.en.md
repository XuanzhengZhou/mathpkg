The check notation $\check{\alpha}$ defines a canonical term in the language of the $L$-machine $M$ that represents the ordinal $\alpha$. It is defined via the abstraction term $\hat{x}_0^\alpha \mathrm{Ord}(x_0)$, which selects the unique $x_0$ of level $\alpha$ satisfying the predicate $\mathrm{Ord}(x_0)$. One can verify by induction that $D(\check{\alpha}) = \alpha$, so the check operator provides a canonical embedding of the ordinals into the term model.
