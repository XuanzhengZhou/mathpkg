$T_\alpha$ collects all constant terms of the forcing language whose rank is strictly less than $\alpha$. The full set of constant terms $T$ is the union over all $\alpha$ in the ground model $M$. This hierarchy stratifies the forcing names by their complexity, which is essential for the inductive definitions of the forcing relation and the generic extension $M[G]$.
