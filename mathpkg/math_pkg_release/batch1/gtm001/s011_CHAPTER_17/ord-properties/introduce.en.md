This proposition establishes the key monotonicity properties of the ordinal function Ord on limited formulas. It verifies that when a formula is built from simpler constituents -- by bounded quantification, negation, conjunction, or abstraction -- the resulting formula has a strictly larger ordinal than each constituent. This is essential for the inductive definitions of the forcing relation and the generic extension $M[G]$.
