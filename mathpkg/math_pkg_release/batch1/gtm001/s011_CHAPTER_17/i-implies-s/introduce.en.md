This theorem establishes that property $I$ (medium $M$-maps on regular cardinals are identity) implies property $S$ (a statement about cardinal arithmetic in $L$). The proof proceeds by contrapositive: assuming $\neg S$, one constructs an elementary embedding $h: L_{\bar{\kappa}} \rightarrow L_\kappa$ whose restriction to $\bar{\kappa}$ is not the identity, thereby producing a counterexample to $I$.
