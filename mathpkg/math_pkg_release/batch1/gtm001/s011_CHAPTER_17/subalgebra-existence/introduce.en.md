This technical lemma shows that any set $X$ of cardinality less than a regular uncountable cardinal $\kappa$ can be extended to a small subalgebra $Y$ of the $L$-machine $M^\lambda$. The subalgebra has cardinality below $\kappa$ and its intersection with $\kappa$ is itself an ordinal. The proof constructs an increasing chain $Y_n$ inductively and uses the regularity of $\kappa$ to bound the union.
