The expansion operator $E$ transforms atomic limited sentences into logically equivalent forms with strictly smaller ordinal. When a membership statement involves an abstraction term on the right, $E$ substitutes the argument. When both sides are constant terms with the right side having lower or equal grade, $E$ rewrites the formula using bounded quantification. This reduction is the key mechanism that makes the forcing relation well-defined by induction on $\text{Ord}(\varphi)$.
