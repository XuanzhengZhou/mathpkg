Let $M$ be a transitive set which is elementarily equivalent to $L_\kappa$ for some uncountable cardinal in $L$. If $\pi: \langle \delta, \alpha, P \rangle \rightarrow \langle \delta', \alpha', P' \rangle$ is an acceptable map such that $\langle \delta, \alpha, P \rangle, \langle \delta', \alpha', P' \rangle \in M$, then $\pi \in M$.
