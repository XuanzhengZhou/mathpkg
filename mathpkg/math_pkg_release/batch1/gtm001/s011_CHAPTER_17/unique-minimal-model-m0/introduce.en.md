This theorem establishes the existence and uniqueness of a minimal model $M_0$ for ZF set theory, assuming any standard model exists at all. $M_0$ is the unique countable standard transitive model of ZF plus $V = L$ that embeds as a submodel into every standard transitive model of ZF. It is defined as $L^{N_0}$ where $N_0$ is a standard transitive model whose set of ordinals is minimal. The proof uses Mostowski's collapsing theorem and the Lowenheim-Skolem theorem.
