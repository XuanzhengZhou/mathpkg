This proposition is the crucial technical result that justifies the inductive definition of the forcing relation. It states that whenever the expansion $E(\varphi)$ of an atomic limited sentence is defined, the ordinal of the expanded formula is strictly less than the ordinal of the original. This ensures that the recursive definition of $D^G$ for atomic sentences terminates, since repeated application of $E$ must eventually reach a base case.
