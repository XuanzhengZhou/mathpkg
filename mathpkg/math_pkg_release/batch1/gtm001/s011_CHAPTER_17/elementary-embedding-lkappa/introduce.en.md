This lemma constructs an elementary embedding $h$ from $L_{\bar{\kappa}}$ to $L_\kappa$ whose well-founded part $\mathcal{W}(h)$ contains a prescribed set $X$ and has the same cardinality as $X$. Moreover, $h$ preserves well-foundedness of direct limit systems. The proof is split into two cases: when the cofinality of $\kappa$ is $\omega$, and when it is greater than $\omega$. The construction uses closing-off arguments to build an elementary submodel $Z$ of $L_\kappa$.
