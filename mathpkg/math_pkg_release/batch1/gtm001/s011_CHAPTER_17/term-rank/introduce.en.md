The rank function $\rho$ assigns an ordinal rank to each constant term in the forcing language $\mathcal{L}(M, \mathbf{G})$. For abstraction terms, the rank is the bounding ordinal $\alpha$. For constants $\mathbf{k}$ representing elements of the ground model $M$, the rank is the set-theoretic rank of $k$. The special symbol $\mathbf{G}$ and the poset symbol $\mathbf{P}$ are assigned the rank of the forcing poset $P$.
