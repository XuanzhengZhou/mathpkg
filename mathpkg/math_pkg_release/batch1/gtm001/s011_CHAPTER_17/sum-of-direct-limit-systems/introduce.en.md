This lemma describes how to combine two $\kappa$-direct limit systems into a single sum system. The key difficulty is defining the ordering and connecting maps between elements from different index sets. The condition for $i < j$ (with $i \in I_1$, $j \in I_2$) involves the well-founded part of the second system's limit map. The connecting map is defined via composition with the inverse of the second limit map, which is justified by Proposition 16.18.
