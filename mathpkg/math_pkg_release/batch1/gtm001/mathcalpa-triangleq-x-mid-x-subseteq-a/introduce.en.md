The definition **$\mathcal{P}(a) \triangleq \{x \mid x \subseteq a\}$** (Definition 5.10.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 5 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
