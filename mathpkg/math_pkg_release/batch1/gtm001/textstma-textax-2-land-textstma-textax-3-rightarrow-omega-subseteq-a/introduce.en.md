The proposition **$\text{STM}(A, \text{Ax. 2}) \land \text{STM}(A, \text{Ax. 3}) \rightarrow \omega \subseteq A$** (Proposition 13.37.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
