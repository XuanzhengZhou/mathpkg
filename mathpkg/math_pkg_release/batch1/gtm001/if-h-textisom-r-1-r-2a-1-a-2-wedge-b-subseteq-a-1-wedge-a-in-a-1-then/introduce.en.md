The proposition **If $H \text{Isom}_{R_1, R_2}(A_1, A_2) \wedge B \subseteq A_1 \wedge a \in A_1$, then** (Proposition 6.31.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 6 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
