The proposition **If $\varphi$ Abs $A$, if $\psi$ Abs $A$, if $a_1, \ldots, a_m, b_1, \ldots, b_n$ is a list of distinct variables containing all of the free variables of $\varphi$ and of $\psi$, and if** (Proposition 13.3.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
