** Note that $\alpha^{\beta n} m_n + \cdots + \alpha^{\beta 0} m_0 \leq \alpha^{\beta n} (m_n + 1)$. Therefore by Proposition 8.47 $(\alpha^{\beta n} m_n + \cdots + \alpha^{\beta 0} m_0)^\gamma \leq [\alpha^{\beta n} (m_n + 1)]^\gamma \leq \alpha^{\beta n \gamma} (m_n + 1).$
