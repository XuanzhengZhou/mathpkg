By *Proposition 10.34
$$\mathcal{Un}(F) \rightarrow \overline{F''b} < \bar{b}.$$

Furthermore if $x \in F''b$ then $(\exists y \in b)[x = F'y]$. Consequently if $\overline{F'y} \leq \bar{a}$ for each $y$ in $b$, then by *Proposition 10.47
$$\overline{\cup F''b} \leq \overline{a \times F''b} \leq \overline{a \times b}.$$
