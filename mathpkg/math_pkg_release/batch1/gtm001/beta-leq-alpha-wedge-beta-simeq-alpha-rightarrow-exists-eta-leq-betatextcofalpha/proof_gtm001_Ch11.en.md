** If $\beta \simeq \alpha$, then $(\exists f)[f: \beta \xrightarrow{1-1} \alpha]$ and so $(\forall \gamma < \alpha)(\exists \delta < \beta)[f' \delta = \gamma]$. Therefore, by Proposition 11.7, $(\exists \eta \leq \beta)[\text{cof}(\alpha, \eta)]$.
