**Proof.** Let $\Pi$ be a well-founded direct system of strong $\mathcal{P}_<$ maps. By induction on $J(\hat{\pi}_{i\infty}(\boldsymbol{\alpha}))$, one proves that if $J^{\eta_i}(\boldsymbol{\alpha}) \simeq \beta$, then $J(\hat{\pi}_{i\infty}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})) = \pi_{i\infty}(\beta)$. This uses the collapsing property and properties of ordinal codes.
