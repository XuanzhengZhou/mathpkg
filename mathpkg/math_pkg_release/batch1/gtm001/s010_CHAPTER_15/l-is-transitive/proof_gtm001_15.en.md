**Proof.** (1) If $x \in L$, then $(\exists \alpha)[x = F'\alpha]$. So $x = F'\alpha \in F''(\alpha + 1)$. Since $F''(\alpha + 1)$ is transitive (Proposition 15.21), $x \subseteq F''(\alpha + 1) \subseteq L$.
