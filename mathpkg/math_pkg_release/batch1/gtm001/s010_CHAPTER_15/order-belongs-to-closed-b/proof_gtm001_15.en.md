**Proof.** From Lemma 1: $x \in F''b \rightarrow \{x\} \in F''b \rightarrow (\exists \alpha \in b)[\{x\} = F' \alpha]$. But $\mathrm{Od}'x = \mathrm{Od}' \mathrm{As}' \{x\}$. Since $b$ is closed w.r.t. $C$ (and $C' \alpha = \mathrm{Od}' \mathrm{As}' F' \alpha$), the result follows.
