**Proof.** By transfinite induction using Lemmas 1-9. One proves by cases on $K_3'\eta$ (0 through 8, corresponding to the eight fundamental operations and the power set operation) that $(\forall \gamma \in b \cap \eta)[F' \gamma \in F' \eta \leftrightarrow F' f' \gamma \in F' f' \eta]$. The full argument uses the closure and structure-preservation lemmas at each case, with symmetry providing the reverse implications.
