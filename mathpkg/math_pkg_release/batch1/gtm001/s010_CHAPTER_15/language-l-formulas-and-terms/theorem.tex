Formulas and constant terms of $\mathcal{L}$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $(t_1 \in t_2)$, $(t_1 = t_2)$ for terms/variables $t_1, t_2$,
\item $(\neg \varphi)$, $(\varphi \lor \psi)$ for formulas $\varphi, \psi$,
\item $(\exists^\alpha x_i \varphi)$ for formula $\varphi$,
\item $(\hat{x}_i^\alpha \varphi(x_i))$ is a constant term if $g(\varphi) < g(\wedge^\alpha)$ and $x_i$ is the only free variable.
\end{enumerate}
