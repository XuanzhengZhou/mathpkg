A mapping $\pi$ is a monomorphism from $\langle A, F_x \rangle$ to $\langle B, G_x \rangle$ iff
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\pi: A \xrightarrow{1-1} B,$
\item $(\forall x \in I)[\pi \circ F_x = G_x \circ \hat{\pi}],$ i.e., $\pi'F_x'\mathbf{a} \simeq G_x'\hat{\pi}'\mathbf{a}$ for all $\mathbf{a} \in A^\omega$.
\end{enumerate}
