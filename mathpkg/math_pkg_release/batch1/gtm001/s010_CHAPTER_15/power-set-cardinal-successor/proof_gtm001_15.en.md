**Proof.** (1) If $\mathcal{P}(F''\aleph_\alpha) \subseteq F''\aleph_{\alpha+1}$, then by Proposition 15.37: $\overline{2^{\aleph_\alpha}} = \overline{\mathcal{P}(\aleph_\alpha)} = \overline{\mathcal{P}(F''\aleph_\alpha)} \subseteq \overline{F''\aleph_{\alpha+1}} = \aleph_{\alpha+1}$. By Cantor, $\overline{2^{\aleph_\alpha}} > \aleph_\alpha$, so equality follows.
