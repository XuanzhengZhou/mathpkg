**Proof.** If such sequences exist, $\langle \pi_{i_n \infty}(\sigma_n) \mid n < \omega \rangle$ descends in $M/\equiv$. Conversely, if $\langle [j_n, \tau_n] \rangle$ descends, pick $i_n \geq j_n$ with $i_0 \leq i_1 \leq \cdots$, set $\sigma_n = \pi_{j_n i_n}(\tau_n)$; then $\pi_{i_n i_{n+1}}(\sigma_n) > \sigma_{n+1}$.
