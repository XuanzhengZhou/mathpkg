**Proof.** (1) If $F' \alpha = 0$ then $C' \alpha = 0 \leq \alpha$. If $F' \alpha \neq 0$ then $\mathrm{As}' F' \alpha \in F' \alpha$, so $C' \alpha = \mathrm{Od}' \mathrm{As}' F' \alpha < \mathrm{Od}' F' \alpha \leq \alpha$.
