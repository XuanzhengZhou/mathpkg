**Proof.** Clearly $\mathfrak{U}^\eta(\pi''X) \subseteq \pi''\mathfrak{U}^\xi(X)$ since the image is a subalgebra. Let $Y = (\pi^{-1})''\mathfrak{U}^\eta(\pi''X)$. Then $X \subseteq Y$. For $\alpha \in Y^\omega$ with $F_i^\xi(\alpha)$ defined: $\pi(\alpha) \in \mathfrak{U}^\eta(\pi''X)$ and $F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha))$ is defined, so $F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha)) \in \mathfrak{U}^\eta(\pi''X)$. Then $F_i^\xi(\alpha) = (\pi^{-1})'F_i^\eta(\pi(\alpha)) \in Y$, so $Y$ is a subalgebra. Hence $\pi''\mathfrak{U}^\xi(X) \subseteq \mathfrak{U}^\eta(\pi''X)$.
