**Proof.** (By finite induction on $n$). For $n = 0$, $m \cdot 0 = 0 \in \omega$. If $m \cdot n \in \omega$, then $m \cdot (n + 1) = m \cdot n + m \in \omega$ since the sum of two natural numbers is a natural number.
