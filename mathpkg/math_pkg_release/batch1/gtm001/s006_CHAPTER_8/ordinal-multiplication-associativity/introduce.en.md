Ordinal multiplication is associative: $(\alpha \cdot \beta) \cdot \gamma = \alpha \cdot (\beta \cdot \gamma)$. The proof uses transfinite induction on $\gamma$, with the limit case requiring careful handling when $\alpha \cdot \beta = 0$ versus $\alpha \cdot \beta \neq 0$.
