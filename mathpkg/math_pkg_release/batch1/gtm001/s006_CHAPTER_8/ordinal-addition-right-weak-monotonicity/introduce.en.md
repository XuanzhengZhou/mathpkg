If $\alpha \le \beta$, then adding the same ordinal $\gamma$ on the right preserves the inequality in the weak sense: $\alpha + \gamma \le \beta + \gamma$. The proof uses transfinite induction on $\gamma$. Note that the strict inequality is not necessarily preserved (e.g., $1 < 2$ but $1 + \omega = 2 + \omega$).
