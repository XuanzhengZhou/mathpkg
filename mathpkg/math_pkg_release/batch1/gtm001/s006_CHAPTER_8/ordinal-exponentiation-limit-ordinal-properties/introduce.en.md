If $\alpha > 1$ and $\beta$ is a limit ordinal, then $\alpha^\beta$ is a limit ordinal. More generally, if $\alpha$ is a limit ordinal and $\beta > 0$, then $\alpha^\beta$ is also a limit ordinal. The proofs use contradiction arguments based on the definition of exponentiation for limit ordinals.
