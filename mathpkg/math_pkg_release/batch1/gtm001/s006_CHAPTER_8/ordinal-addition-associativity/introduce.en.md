Ordinal addition is associative. The proof proceeds by transfinite induction on $\gamma$. The cases $\gamma = 0$ and $\gamma$ a successor are handled directly. For $\gamma \in K_{\mathrm{II}}$, the proof uses Proposition 8.11 to handle the fact that $\beta + \gamma$ is also a limit ordinal.
