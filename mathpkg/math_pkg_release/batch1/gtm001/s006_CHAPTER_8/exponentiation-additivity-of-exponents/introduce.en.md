Ordinal exponentiation satisfies the law of additivity of exponents: $\alpha^{\beta+\gamma} = \alpha^\beta \cdot \alpha^\gamma$. This is the ordinal analog of $a^{b+c} = a^b \cdot a^c$ for natural numbers. The proof uses transfinite induction on $\gamma$ and properties of ordinal multiplication.
