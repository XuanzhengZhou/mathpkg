For a Cantor Normal Form sum raised to a power $\gamma$, we have the upper bound $(\alpha^{\beta_n} \cdot m_n + \cdots + \alpha^{\beta_0} \cdot m_0)^\gamma \le \alpha^{\beta_n \cdot \gamma} \cdot (m_n + 1)$. This inequality is established by noting that the sum is bounded by $\alpha^{\beta_n} \cdot (m_n + 1)$ and applying Proposition 8.47.
