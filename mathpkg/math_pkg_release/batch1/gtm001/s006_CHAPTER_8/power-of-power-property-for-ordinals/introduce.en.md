Ordinal exponentiation satisfies the power-of-power law: $(\alpha^\beta)^\gamma = \alpha^{\beta \cdot \gamma}$. This is the ordinal analog of $(a^b)^c = a^{bc}$. The proof uses transfinite induction on $\gamma$ with careful handling of the cases $\beta = 0$, $\alpha = 0$, and limit ordinals.
