If the base $\gamma > 1$ and $\gamma^\alpha < \gamma^\beta$, then necessarily $\alpha < \beta$. This is the contrapositive of Proposition 8.33. Together they imply that for $\gamma > 1$, the map $\alpha \mapsto \gamma^\alpha$ is strictly order-preserving.
