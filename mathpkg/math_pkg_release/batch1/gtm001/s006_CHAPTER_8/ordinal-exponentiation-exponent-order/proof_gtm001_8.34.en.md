**Proof.** By Proposition 8.33, if $1 < \gamma$ and $\beta \le \alpha$, then $\gamma^\beta \le \gamma^\alpha$. Taking the contrapositive, $\gamma^\alpha < \gamma^\beta$ implies $\beta \not\le \alpha$, i.e., $\alpha < \beta$.
