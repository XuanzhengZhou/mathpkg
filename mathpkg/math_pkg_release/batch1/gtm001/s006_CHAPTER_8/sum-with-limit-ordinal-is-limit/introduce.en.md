If $\beta$ is a limit ordinal (i.e., $\beta \in K_{\mathrm{II}}$), then the sum $\alpha + \beta$ is also a limit ordinal, regardless of what $\alpha$ is. This means that adding a limit ordinal on the right always produces a limit ordinal.
