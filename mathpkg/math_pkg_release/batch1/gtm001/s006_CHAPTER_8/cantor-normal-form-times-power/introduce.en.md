When a Cantor Normal Form sum is multiplied on the right by $\alpha^\delta$ (with $\delta$ infinite), the lower-degree terms are absorbed and the result simplifies to a single power $\alpha^{\beta_n + \delta}$. This illustrates the non-commutative and absorbing nature of ordinal arithmetic for infinite ordinals.
