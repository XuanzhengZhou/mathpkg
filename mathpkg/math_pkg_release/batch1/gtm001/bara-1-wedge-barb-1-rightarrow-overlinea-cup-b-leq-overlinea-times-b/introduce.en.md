The proposition **$\bar{a} > 1 \wedge \bar{b} > 1 \rightarrow \overline{a \cup b} \leq \overline{a \times b}.$** (Proposition 10.36.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
