$$a \in \text{On} \rightarrow a \subseteq \text{On}$$

i.e., On is transitive. From Propositions 7.6 and 7.10

$(\forall x, y \in \text{On}) [x \in y \lor x = y \lor y \in x]$.

Therefore, by Definition 7.3, On is an ordinal. $\
