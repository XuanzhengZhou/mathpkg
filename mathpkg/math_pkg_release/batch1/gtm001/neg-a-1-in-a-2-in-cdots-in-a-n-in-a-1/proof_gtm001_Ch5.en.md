Let $a = \{a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n\}$. Suppose that $a_1 \in a_2 \in \cdots \in a_n \in a_1$. Then $(\forall x)[x \in a \rightarrow x \cap a \neq 0]$. This contradicts Regularity.
