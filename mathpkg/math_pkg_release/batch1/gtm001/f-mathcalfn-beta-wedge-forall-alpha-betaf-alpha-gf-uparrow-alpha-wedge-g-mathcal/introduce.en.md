The lemma **$f \mathcal{F}n \beta \wedge (\forall \alpha < \beta)[f' \alpha = G'(f \uparrow \alpha)] \wedge g \mathcal{F}n \gamma$** (Lemma 1.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
