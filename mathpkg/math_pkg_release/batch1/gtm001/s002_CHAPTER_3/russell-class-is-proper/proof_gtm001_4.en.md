\text{From Proposition 4.12: }
\mathcal{M}(\text{Ru}) \rightarrow [\text{Ru} \in \text{Ru} \leftrightarrow \text{Ru} \notin \text{Ru}].
\text{This is a contradiction. Therefore $\text{Ru}$ is a proper class.}
