The proposition **$[f: a \rightarrow N] \rightarrow \cup(f' a) \in N.$** (Proposition 11.16.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 11 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
