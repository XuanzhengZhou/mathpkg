The proposition **$\beta \in K_{\text{II}} \wedge \gamma < \alpha \beta \rightarrow (\exists \delta)[\delta < \beta \wedge \gamma < \alpha \delta]$** (Proposition 8.23.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 8 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
