The proposition **$F \mathcal{F}_n \omega \rightarrow (\exists n)[F'(n + 1) \notin F'n]$** (Proposition 7.34.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 7 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
