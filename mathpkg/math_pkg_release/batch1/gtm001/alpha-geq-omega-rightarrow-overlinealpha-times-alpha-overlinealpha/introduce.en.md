The proposition **$\alpha \geq \omega \rightarrow \overline{\alpha \times \alpha} = \overline{\alpha}$** (Proposition 10.39.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
