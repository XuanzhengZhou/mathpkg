The proposition **$(\exists \alpha)(\forall x \in A)[\text{rank}(x) \leq \alpha] \rightarrow \mathcal{M}(A)$** (Proposition 9.19.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
