(1) Ord$^1(\varphi)$ is the number of occurrences of the form $t_1 \in t_2$ in $\varphi$, where $t_1$ and $t_2$ are constant terms and $g(\varphi) = g(t_1)$.

(2) Ord$^2(\varphi)$ is the number of logical symbols in $\varphi$ which are not contained in any constant term in $\varphi$.

(3) Ord$(\varphi) \triangleq \omega^2 g(\varphi) + \omega \cdot \text{Ord}^1(\varphi) + \text{Ord}^2(\varphi) + 1$.
