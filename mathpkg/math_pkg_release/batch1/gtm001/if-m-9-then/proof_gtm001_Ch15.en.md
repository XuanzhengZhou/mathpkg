By Proposition 15.1 we have

$$J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle < \aleph_\gamma.$$

If $J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle < \aleph_0$ then

$$J' \langle \alpha, \beta, m \rangle = 9 \cdot J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle + m < \aleph_0 \leq \aleph_\gamma.$$

If $J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle \geq \aleph_0$ then since

$$9 \cdot J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle \simeq 9 \times J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle$$

we have that

$$\overline{9 \cdot J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle} = \overline{J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle} < \aleph_\gamma$$

and hence

$$J' \langle \alpha, \beta, m \rangle = 9 \cdot J'_0 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle + m < \aleph_\gamma.$$
