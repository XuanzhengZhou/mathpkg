(1) If $F' \alpha = 0$ then $\text{As}' F' \alpha = 0$ and $\text{Od}' \text{As}' F' \alpha = 0$, i.e., $C' \alpha = 0 \leq \alpha$. If $F' \alpha \neq 0$ then $\text{As}' F' \alpha \in F' \alpha$. Therefore
$$C' \alpha = \text{Od}' \text{As}' F' \alpha < \text{Od}' F' \alpha \leq \alpha.$$
(2) The proof is left to the reader. $\square$
