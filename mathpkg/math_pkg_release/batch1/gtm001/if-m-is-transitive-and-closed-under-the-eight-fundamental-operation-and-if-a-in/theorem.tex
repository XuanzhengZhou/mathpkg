Lemma. If $M$ is transitive, almost universal, and closed under the eight fundamental operations, if $A$ is $M$-constructible, if $a \in M$ and if $G_n$ is a function on a defined by

$$G_n^*b = \{y \in A \mid \langle y, b \rangle \in Q_n \wedge (\forall x \in A)[\langle x, b \rangle \in Q_n \rightarrow \text{rank}(y) \leq \text{rank}(x)]\}$$

then

(1) $(\exists y \in M)[\cup(G_n^*a) \subseteq y]$,

(2) $(\exists z \in M)[a \cap Q_n^*z = a \cap Q_n^*A]$.
