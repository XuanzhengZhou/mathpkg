The definition **$\text{rank}(a) \triangleq \mu_{\alpha}\left(a \in R_{1}^{\prime}(\alpha + 1)\right)$** (Definition 9.14.) is a fundamental notion in axiomatic set theory, introduced in Chapter 9 of Takeuti and Zaring's *Introduction to Axiomatic Set Theory* (2nd ed., 1982). It forms part of the formal development of Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with classes (GB or ZF).
