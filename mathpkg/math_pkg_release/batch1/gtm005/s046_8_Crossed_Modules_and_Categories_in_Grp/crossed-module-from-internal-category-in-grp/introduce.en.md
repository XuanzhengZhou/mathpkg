Every internal category in the category of groups gives rise to a crossed module. The construction takes the kernel of the domain map $d_0$ as the group $H$, the object-of-objects group $C_0$ as $P$, and the restriction of the codomain map $d_1$ to $\ker d_0$ as the structure homomorphism $\alpha: H \to P$. The action of $P$ on $H$ is the standard conjugation action arising from the short exact sequence $1 \to \ker d_0 \to C_1 \xrightarrow{d_0} C_0 \to 1$. This is the forward direction of the equivalence between crossed modules and internal categories in $\mathbf{Grp}$.
