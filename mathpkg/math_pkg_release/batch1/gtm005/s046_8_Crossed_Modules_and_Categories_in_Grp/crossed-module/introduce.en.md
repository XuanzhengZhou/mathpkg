A crossed module is an algebraic model for connected homotopy 2-types. They arise naturally from the boundary map of a second relative homotopy group $\partial: \pi_2(X, A) \to \pi_1(A)$, where the action is by conjugation of loops. Crossed modules are equivalent to internal categories in the category of groups.
