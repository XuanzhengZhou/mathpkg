Mac Lane proves the equivalence of several formulations of adjunctions: hom-set bijections, universal arrows from each object to the right adjoint, universal arrows from the left adjoint to each object, and the unit-counit with triangle identities. This theorem is fundamental for working with adjunctions in practice, as each formulation is convenient in different contexts.
