An \textbf{adjunction} between categories $C$ and $D$ consists of two functors $F: C \rightarrow D$ and $G: D \rightarrow C$, together with a natural isomorphism
\[
\varphi = \varphi_{c,d}: \hom_D(Fc, d) \cong \hom_C(c, Gd)
\]
which is natural in $c \in C$ and $d \in D$. We write $F \dashv G$, say $F$ is \textbf{left adjoint} to $G$, and $G$ is \textbf{right adjoint} to $F$.
