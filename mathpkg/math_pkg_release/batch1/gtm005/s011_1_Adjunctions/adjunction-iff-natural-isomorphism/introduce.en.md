An adjunction can be expressed purely in terms of hom-sets: it is a natural isomorphism between the bifunctors $\hom_D(F(-), -)$ and $\hom_C(-, G(-))$. This formulation makes clear that adjunctions are about a correspondence between morphisms in two categories.
