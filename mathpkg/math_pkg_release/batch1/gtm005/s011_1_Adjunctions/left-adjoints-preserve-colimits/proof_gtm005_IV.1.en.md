For a colimit cocone $\{c_i \rightarrow c\}$ in $C$, apply $F$ and use the adjunction isomorphism: $\hom_D(Fc, d) \cong \hom_C(c, Gd) \cong \lim \hom_C(c_i, Gd) \cong \lim \hom_D(Fc_i, d)$, showing $Fc$ is a colimit of $\{Fc_i\}$.
