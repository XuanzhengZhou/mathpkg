The unit of an adjunction maps each object into the composite $GF$. It is the 'insertion of generators' in free constructions (e.g., the inclusion of a set into the free group on that set). The unit components are universal arrows, meaning every other arrow $c \to Gd$ factors uniquely through $\eta_c$.
