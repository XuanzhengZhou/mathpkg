A fundamental result in category theory: every presheaf is a colimit of representables. This "density theorem" means that the representable functors generate the entire presheaf category under colimits. The proof constructs the category of elements of $H$ and shows $H$ is the colimit of representables indexed by this category. This result is used extensively in the theory of Kan extensions and weighted limits.
