A \emph{binary coproduct} of objects $a, b$ in a category $C$ is an object $a + b$ together with two arrows (injections) $i_1: a \to a + b$ and $i_2: b \to a + b$ such that for any object $c$ and arrows $f: a \to c$, $g: b \to c$, there is a unique arrow $h: a + b \to c$ with $h \circ i_1 = f$ and $h \circ i_2 = g$.
