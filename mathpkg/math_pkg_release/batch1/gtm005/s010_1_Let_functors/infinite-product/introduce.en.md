An infinite (or small) product generalizes the binary product to an arbitrary indexed family of objects. It is the limit of a functor from a discrete category with object set $I$. The universal property is $\hom(c, \prod_i a_i) \cong \prod_i \hom(c, a_i)$. Categories with all small products are called complete.
