The covariant Yoneda Lemma handles presheaves (contravariant set-valued functors). It states that natural transformations from the contravariant hom-functor $\hom(-, r)$ to a presheaf $K$ correspond to elements of $K(r)$. This is the dual form of the Yoneda Lemma.
