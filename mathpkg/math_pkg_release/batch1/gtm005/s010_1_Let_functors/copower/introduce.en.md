The copower $J \cdot a$ is the dual notion to the power. It is the $J$-fold coproduct of $a$, also called the tensor of $J$ with $a$ in the enriched category context. In $\mathbf{Set}$, $J \cdot a \cong J \times a$; in $\mathbf{Ab}$, $J \cdot G$ is the direct sum of $|J|$ copies of $G$.
