The cokernel is the dual notion to the kernel in a category with zero morphisms. It is the coequalizer of $f$ and the zero arrow, representing the quotient of the codomain by the image of $f$. In abelian categories, the cokernel is a fundamental part of the kernel-cokernel exact sequence.
