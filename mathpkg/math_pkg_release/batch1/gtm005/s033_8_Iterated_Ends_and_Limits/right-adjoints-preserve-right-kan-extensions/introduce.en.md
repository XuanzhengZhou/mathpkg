This corollary shows that right adjoints preserve right Kan extensions. Given an adjunction $F \dashv G$, applying $G$ to a right Kan extension yields a right Kan extension of the composite $GT$. The proof is a straightforward application of the adjunction bijection, demonstrating how naturally Kan extensions interact with adjunctions.
