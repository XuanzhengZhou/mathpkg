Let $F \dashv G$ be an adjunction with $F: X \to A$ left adjoint to $G: A \to X$. If $\langle R, \varepsilon: RK \to T \rangle$ is a right Kan extension of $T: M \to A$ along $K: M \to C$, then $G \circ R$ together with $G\varepsilon: (GR)K \to GT$ is a right Kan extension of $GT$ along $K$. That is,
$$G \circ \mathrm{Ran}_K T \cong \mathrm{Ran}_K(GT).$$
