This corollary specializes the limit formula for right Kan extensions to the case where $M$ is a full subcategory of $C$. In this setting, the right Kan extension actually extends the given functor $T$ (i.e., restricts to $T$ on $M$), and the counit is simply the identity natural transformation. This makes the right Kan extension a genuine extension of the functor from the subcategory to the larger category.
