When the indicated powers $a^X$ (satisfying $A(b, a^X) \cong \mathrm{Ens}(X, A(b, a))$) and the corresponding end exist, the right Kan extension of $T$ along $K$ is given by
$$(\mathrm{Ran}_K T)c = \int_{m \in M} Tm^{\,C(c, Km)},$$
valid when $A$ has the requisite powers and the end exists. For $\mathrm{Ab}$-categories, powers are replaced by cotensors $a^C$ defined by the adjunction $A(b, c^C) \cong \mathrm{Ab}(C, A(b, c))$.
