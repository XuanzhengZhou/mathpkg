This lemma establishes a key bijection between cones over the diagram $(c \downarrow K) \to M \to A$ and natural transformations between certain hom-functors. A cone assigns to each $f: c \to Km$ an arrow $a \to Tm$ satisfying compatibility conditions, while a natural transformation assigns to each $m$ and $f: c \to Km$ an arrow $a \to Tm$ satisfying naturality. The bijection is essentially a reorganization of these data and is used in characterizing pointwise Kan extensions.
