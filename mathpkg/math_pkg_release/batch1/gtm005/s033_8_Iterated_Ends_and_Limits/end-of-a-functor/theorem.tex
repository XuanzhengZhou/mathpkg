For a functor $T: C^{\text{op}} \times C \rightarrow D$, an \textbf{end} $\int_c T(c, c)$ is a universal dinatural transformation (wedge) $\omega: \int_c T(c, c) \xrightarrow{\cdot\cdot} T$. It consists of an object $\int_c T(c, c) \in D$ and arrows $\omega_c: \int_c T(c, c) \rightarrow T(c, c)$, dinatural in $c$, universal among all such wedges.
