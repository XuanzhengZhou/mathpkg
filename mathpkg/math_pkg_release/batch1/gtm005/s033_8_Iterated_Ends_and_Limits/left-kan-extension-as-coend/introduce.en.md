This theorem provides a coend formula for the left Kan extension, expressing it as a weighted colimit. The formula $(\mathrm{Lan}_K T)c = \int^m C(Km, c) \cdot Tm$ generalizes the familiar fact that left Kan extensions of representable functors are represented by the image under $K$. The proof elegantly uses the Yoneda lemma, the end formula for natural transformations, and the Fubini theorem for ends to establish the required natural bijection.
