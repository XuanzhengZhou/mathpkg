For subobjects $u \leq v$ of an object $a$ in an abelian category, define a "quotient" object $v/u$ (to agree with the usual notion in $\mathbf{Ab}$). If $g f = 0$, prove that $\ker g / \operatorname{im} f$ is isomorphic to the dual object $\operatorname{coim} g / \operatorname{coker} f$.
