In an abelian category $A$, a chain complex is a sequence

$$\cdots \rightarrow c_{n+1} \xrightarrow{\partial_{n+1}} c_n \xrightarrow{\partial_n} c_{n-1} \rightarrow \cdots$$

of composable arrows, with $\partial_n \partial_{n+1} = 0$ for all $n$. The sequence need not be exact at $c_n$; the deviation from exactness is measured by the $n$-th homology object.
