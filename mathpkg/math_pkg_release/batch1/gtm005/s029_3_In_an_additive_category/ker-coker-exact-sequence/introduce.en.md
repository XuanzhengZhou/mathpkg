This theorem constructs the kernel-cokernel exact sequence from any morphism of short exact sequences in an abelian category. The key innovation is the connecting homomorphism $\delta$, which links the kernel of the right-hand map to the cokernel of the left-hand map. This is the abelian-category version of the Snake Lemma, proved here using the calculus of members. The naturality of $\delta$ makes this construction functorial, establishing it as a natural transformation between appropriate functors on the category of short exact sequences.
