In a category with zero morphisms, the \textbf{kernel} of an arrow $f: a \rightarrow b$ is an arrow $k: \ker f \rightarrow a$ such that $f \circ k = 0$, and any arrow $g: c \rightarrow a$ with $f \circ g = 0$ factors uniquely through $k$. The kernel is the equalizer of $f$ and the zero arrow $0: a \rightarrow b$.
