(The free $\mathbf{Ab}$-category on a given $C$.) Given a category $C$, construct an $\mathbf{Ab}$-category $A$ and a functor $C \rightarrow A$ which is universal from $C$ to an $\mathbf{Ab}$-category. (Hint: The objects of $A$ are those of $C$, while $A(b, c) = \mathbf{Z}(C(b, c))$ is the free abelian group on the set $C(b, c)$.)
