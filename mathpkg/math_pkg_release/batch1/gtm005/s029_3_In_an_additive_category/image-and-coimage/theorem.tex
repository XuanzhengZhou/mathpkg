In an abelian category, every arrow $f: a \rightarrow b$ factors uniquely (up to isomorphism) as
\[
a \twoheadrightarrow \operatorname{coim} f \xrightarrow{\bar{f}} \operatorname{im} f \rightarrowtail b,
\]
where $\operatorname{coim} f = \operatorname{coker}(\ker f)$, $\operatorname{im} f = \ker(\operatorname{coker} f)$, and $\bar{f}$ is an isomorphism. This yields the factorization $f = (\operatorname{im} f) \circ \bar{f} \circ (\operatorname{coim} f)$.
