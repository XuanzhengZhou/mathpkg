For a category $C$, the **subdivision category** $C^{\S}$ has as objects all symbols $c^{\S}$ and $f^{\S}$ for $c \in C$ and $f$ an arrow in $C$ (note that $c^{\S}$ and $(1_c)^{\S}$ are different objects). The arrows of $C^{\S}$ are the identity arrows for these objects, plus for each arrow $f: b \rightarrow c$ in $C$ two arrows

$$b^{\S} \rightarrow f^{\S} \leftarrow c^{\S}$$

in $C^{\S}$. The only meaningful compositions for these arrows are compositions with one factor an identity arrow.
