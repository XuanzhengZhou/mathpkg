A final subcategory is a subcategory whose inclusion functor is final. This means that for every object $k$ of the ambient category, there exists an object in the subcategory with an arrow from $k$, and any two such arrows are connected by a zigzag in the comma category. Final subcategories determine the same colimits as the full category.
