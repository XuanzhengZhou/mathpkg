For $L: J' \rightarrow J$ final and $F: J \rightarrow X$ with $X$ cocomplete, the canonical map

$$h: \operatorname{Colim} F L \rightarrow \operatorname{Colim} F$$

is an isomorphism, where $h$ is the unique arrow defined by the universal property of the colimit $\operatorname{Colim} F L$ applied to the cone $\mu \circ L : F L \rightarrow \operatorname{Colim} F$.
