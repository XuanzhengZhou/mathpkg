This corollary follows immediately from the pointwise limits theorem: given a small diagram $F : J \rightarrow X^P$ where $J$ is small and $X$ is small-complete, the composite $E_p F : J \rightarrow X$ has a limit in $X$ for each $p \in P$. These pointwise limits assemble into a limit of $F$ in $X^P$, establishing that $X^P$ inherits small-completeness from $X$. This is one of the most frequently used facts about functor categories: completeness lifts from the codomain to the functor category.
