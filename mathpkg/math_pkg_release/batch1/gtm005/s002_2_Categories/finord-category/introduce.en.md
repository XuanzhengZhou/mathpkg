The category $\mathbf{Finord} = \mathbf{Set}_\omega$ is a convenient skeletal model for the category of all finite sets. Its objects are the finite ordinal numbers and its arrows are all functions between them. By taking one representative object for each finite cardinality, this construction avoids set-theoretic issues while capturing the essential structure of finite sets and functions.
