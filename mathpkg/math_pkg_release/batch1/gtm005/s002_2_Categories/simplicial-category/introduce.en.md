The simplicial category $\Delta$ has as objects all finite ordinal numbers and as arrows all order-preserving (monotone) functions between them. This category is fundamental in algebraic topology and homological algebra because presheaves on $\Delta$ are simplicial sets, which model topological spaces combinatorially. The structure of $\Delta$ encodes the face and degeneracy maps that organize simplicial objects.
