If $C_G$ is the free category generated by a graph $G$, the quotient $C_G/R$ is called the \emph{category with generators $G$ and relations $R$}. For example, the category $\mathbf{3}$ may be described as the category generated by three objects $0, 1, 2$, three arrows $f: 0 \to 1$, $g: 1 \to 2$, and $h: 0 \to 2$, and one relation $h = g \circ f$.
