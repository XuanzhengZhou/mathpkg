The free category on a graph is the category whose arrows are formal paths in the graph, with composition by concatenation. This construction is the left adjoint to the forgetful functor from categories to graphs, giving the bijection $\operatorname{Cat}(C_G, B) \cong \operatorname{Grph}(G, UB)$, natural in $G$ and $B$.
