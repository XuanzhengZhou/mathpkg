Let $G$ be a small graph. There exists a small category $C_G$ and a morphism $P: G \rightarrow UC_G$ of graphs such that for any category $B$ and any morphism $D: G \rightarrow UB$ of graphs, there is a unique functor $D': C_G \rightarrow B$ with $D = UD' \circ P$. The category $C_G$ is called the \textbf{free category generated by the graph} $G$.
