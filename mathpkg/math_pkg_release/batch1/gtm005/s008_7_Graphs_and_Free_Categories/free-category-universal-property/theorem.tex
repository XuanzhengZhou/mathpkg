For any graph $G$ and any category $B$, each morphism of graphs $D: G \to UB$ (where $U: \mathbf{Cat} \to \mathbf{Grph}$ is the forgetful functor) extends uniquely to a functor $D': C_G \to B$ such that $D = UD' \circ P$. Thus there is a natural bijection
$$\mathbf{Cat}(C_G, B) \cong \mathbf{Grph}(G, UB),$$
which asserts that the free category functor $C: \mathbf{Grph} \to \mathbf{Cat}$ is left adjoint to the forgetful functor $U$.
