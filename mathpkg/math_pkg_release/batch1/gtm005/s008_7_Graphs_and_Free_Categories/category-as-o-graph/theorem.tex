A category with objects $O$ may be described as an $O$-graph $A$ equipped with two morphisms $c: A \times_O A \to A$ (composition) and $i: O \to A$ (identity) of $O$-graphs such that the diagrams

\[
\begin{CD}
(A \times_O A) \times_O A @>{\cong}>> A \times_O (A \times_O A) @>{1 \times c}>> A \times_O A \\
@VV{c \times 1}V @VVV{c}V \\
A \times_O A @>{c}>> A
\end{CD}
\]

and

\[
\begin{CD}
A @>{\cong}>> A \times_O O @>{1 \times i}>> A \times_O A @<{i \times 1}<< O \times_O A @<{\cong}<< A \\
@| @VVV{c}V @| \\
A @= A @= A
\end{CD}
\]

are commutative.
