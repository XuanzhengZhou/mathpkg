A faithful functor is one that is injective on each hom-set: distinct parallel arrows in the domain category are mapped to distinct arrows in the codomain. This property ensures that the functor does not collapse different morphisms between the same pair of objects. Faithfulness is a weakening of the notion of an embedding of categories.
