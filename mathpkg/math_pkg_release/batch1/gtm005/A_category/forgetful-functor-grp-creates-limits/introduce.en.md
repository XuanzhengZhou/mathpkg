**Theorem 3.** The forgetful functor $U: \mathbf{Grp} \to \mathbf{Set}$ creates limits. This is simply a restatement of Theorem 2 using the precise terminology of "creates limits." The result means that to compute any small limit in $\mathbf{Grp}$, one computes the limit of the underlying sets and then equips it with the unique group structure making all projections into homomorphisms.
