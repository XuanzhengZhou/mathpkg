$\mathbf{Finord}$, also denoted $\mathbf{Set}_\omega$, is the category whose objects are finite ordinals and whose arrows are all functions between them. This category is essentially the skeleton of the category of finite sets: by using one representative $n = \{0, 1, \ldots, n-1\}$ for each finite cardinal, it avoids set-theoretic issues while capturing the same structure.
