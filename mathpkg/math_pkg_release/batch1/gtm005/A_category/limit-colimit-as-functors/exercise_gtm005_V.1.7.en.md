($\varprojlim$ and $\varinjlim$ as functors.) If $F, F' : J \rightarrow C$ have limiting cones $\mu, \mu'$ (or colimiting cones $\nu, \nu'$), show that each natural transformation $\beta : F \rightarrow F'$ determines a unique arrow $\varprojlim \beta$ (or $\varinjlim \beta$) that is compatible with the limiting cones.
