The ring $\mathbb{Z}_p$ of $p$-adic integers can be elegantly constructed as an inverse limit in the category of rings. For each $n$, take the ring $\mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$ of integers modulo $p^n$, connected by the canonical projections. The limit consists of compatible strings $(\lambda_n)$ with $\lambda_n \in \mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$, where addition and multiplication are defined termwise. This categorical description is more concise than the classical construction via $p$-adic valuations and Cauchy sequences.
