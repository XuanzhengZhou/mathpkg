For any set of sets $V$, the category $\operatorname{Ens}_V$ has all sets in $V$ as objects and all functions between them as arrows. This is the prototypical concrete category, where objects are structured sets and arrows are structure-preserving functions. The notation $\operatorname{Ens}$ refers to any such category of sets, illustrating how category theory abstracts the notion of sets and functions.
