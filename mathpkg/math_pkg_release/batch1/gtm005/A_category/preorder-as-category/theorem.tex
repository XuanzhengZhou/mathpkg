A \emph{preorder} is a category $P$ in which, given objects $p$ and $p'$, there is at most one arrow $p \to p'$. In any preorder $P$, define a binary relation $\leq$ on the objects of $P$ by $p \leq p'$ if and only if there is an arrow $p \to p'$ in $P$. This relation is reflexive (because of the identity arrow) and transitive (because of composition).
