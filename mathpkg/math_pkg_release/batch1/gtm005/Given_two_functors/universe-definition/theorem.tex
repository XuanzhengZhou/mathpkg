A \emph{universe} is a set $U$ with properties: (i) $x \in u \in U \implies x \in U$; (ii) $u, v \in U \implies \{u, v\}, \langle u, v \rangle, u \times v \in U$; (iii) $x \in U \implies \mathcal{P}x, \bigcup x \in U$; (iv) $\omega \in U$; (v) if $f: a \to b$ is surjective with $a \in U, b \subset U$, then $b \in U$.
