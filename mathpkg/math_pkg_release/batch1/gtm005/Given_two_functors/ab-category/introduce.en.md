An Ab-category (or preadditive category) is a category enriched over the monoidal category of abelian groups. Each hom-set carries an abelian group structure and composition is bilinear. Examples include $\mathbf{Ab}$, $R	ext{-}\mathbf{Mod}$, and all additive categories.
