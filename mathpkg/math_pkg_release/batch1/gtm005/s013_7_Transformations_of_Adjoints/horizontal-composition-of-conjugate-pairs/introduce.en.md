The horizontal composition of conjugate pairs endows the category Adj with 2-categorical structure. Given a conjugate pair between adjunctions X to A and another between adjunctions A to D, their horizontal composite yields a conjugate pair between the composite adjunctions X to D. The proof is visualized by the compatibility of hom-set diagrams. This establishes the interchange law for the 2-category of adjunctions: vertical composition (within each hom-category) and horizontal composition (along composition of adjunctions) are compatible.
