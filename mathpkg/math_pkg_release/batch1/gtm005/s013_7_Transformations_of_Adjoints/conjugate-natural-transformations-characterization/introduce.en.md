This theorem provides multiple equivalent characterizations of conjugate natural transformations. Instead of checking the hom-set diagram, one may verify any of four simpler diagrams involving only units and counits. Moreover, the theorem establishes a bijective correspondence: each natural transformation between left adjoints uniquely determines a conjugate transformation between right adjoints, and vice versa. The proof uses the Yoneda Lemma and the universal property of the counit to construct the required transformation.
