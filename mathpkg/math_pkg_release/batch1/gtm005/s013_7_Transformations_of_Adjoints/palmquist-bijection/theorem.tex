Given functors $H, K$ and two adjunctions $\langle F, G, \varphi \rangle : X \rightharpoonup A$ and $\langle F', G', \varphi' \rangle : X' \rightharpoonup A'$ as in the adjoint square, there is a bijection between natural transformations
$$\alpha : F'HG \xrightarrow{\cdot} K$$
and natural transformations
$$\beta : H \xrightarrow{\cdot} G'KF.$$
