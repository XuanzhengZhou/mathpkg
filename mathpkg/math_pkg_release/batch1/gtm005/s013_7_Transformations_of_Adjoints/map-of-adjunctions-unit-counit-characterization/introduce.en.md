A map of adjunctions requires a hom-set compatibility condition. This proposition shows that the hom-set condition is equivalent to the simpler unit condition $L\eta = \eta'L$ (the left-hand square with units commutes) and also equivalent to the counit condition $arepsilon'K = Karepsilon$ (the right-hand square with counits commutes). The proof uses the standard technique of chasing identity arrows through the adjunction bijections.
