$\mathbf{Moncat}$ is the category whose objects are all small monoidal categories and whose morphisms are strict morphisms of monoidal categories. This category possesses finite products, with the terminal object being the one-object category $1$ equipped with the evident strict monoidal structure. It also contains a full subcategory consisting of all strict monoidal categories, where the conditions on the coherence isomorphisms $\alpha$, $\lambda$, and $\varrho$ can be omitted from the definition of morphisms.
