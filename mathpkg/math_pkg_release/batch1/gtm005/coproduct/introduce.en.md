The coproduct (or sum) of two objects is the universal way to map both objects into a common target. The injections $i, j$ embed $a$ and $b$ into the coproduct, and any pair of arrows out of $a$ and $b$ factors uniquely through the coproduct. In Set, the coproduct is the disjoint union; in Grp, it is the free product; in Ab, it is the direct sum.
