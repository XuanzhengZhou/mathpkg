In the category $\mathbf{Grp}$ of groups, a fork arises naturally from any normal subgroup $N \triangleleft G$. The semidirect product $G \ltimes N$ serves as the domain of the parallel pair, with $\partial_0$ projecting onto $G$ and $\partial_1$ multiplying the components. The projection $p : G \to G/N$ completes the fork. This construction illustrates how forks capture quotient-like structures in algebraic categories.
