For given objects and arrows in the configuration
$$a \xrightarrow{f} b \xrightarrow{g} c \xrightarrow{k} d$$
one always has the equality
$$k \circ (g \circ f) = (k \circ g) \circ f.$$

This axiom asserts that the associative law holds for the operation of composition whenever it makes sense (i.e., whenever the composites on either side are defined).
