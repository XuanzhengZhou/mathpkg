The associativity axiom for metacategories states that composition of arrows is associative whenever defined. Specifically, for any composable triple $f: a \to b$, $g: b \to c$, $k: c \to d$, we have $k \circ (g \circ f) = (k \circ g) \circ f$. This is one of the two foundational axioms that define a metacategory, ensuring that composition behaves in a well-defined manner.
