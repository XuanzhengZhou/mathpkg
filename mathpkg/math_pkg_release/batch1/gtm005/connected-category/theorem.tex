A category $J$ is called \textbf{connected} when for any two objects $j, k \in J$ there is a finite sequence of arrows (in either direction)
$$j = j_0 \to j_1 \leftarrow j_2 \to \cdots \to j_{2n-1} \leftarrow j_{2n} = k$$
joining $j$ to $k$. Every category is a disjoint union (coproduct) of its connected components.
