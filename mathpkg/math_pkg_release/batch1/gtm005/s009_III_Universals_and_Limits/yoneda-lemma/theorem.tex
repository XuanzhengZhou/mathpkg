\textbf{Lemma (Yoneda).} If $K: D \to \mathbf{Set}$ is a functor from $D$ and $r$ an object in $D$ (for $D$ a category with small hom-sets), there is a bijection

\[
y: \operatorname{Nat}(D(r, -), K) \cong Kr
\]

which sends each natural transformation $\alpha: D(r, -) \to K$ to $\alpha_r 1_r$, the image of the identity $r \to r$.
