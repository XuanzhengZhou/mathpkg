A universal arrow from $c$ to $S: D \to C$ with universal pair $\langle r, u: c \to Sr \rangle$ is equivalent to a representation of the functor $\hom_C(c, S-): D \to \mathbf{Set}$ with representing object $r$. Conversely, a representation $\hom_D(r, -) \cong H$ gives a universal element $1_r \in \hom_D(r, r) \cong Hr$.
