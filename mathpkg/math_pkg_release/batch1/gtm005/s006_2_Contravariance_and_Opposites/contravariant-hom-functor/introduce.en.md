The contravariant hom-functor $C(-, b): C^{\text{op}} \to \mathbf{Set}$ is the dual of the covariant hom-functor. For a fixed object $b$, it assigns to each object $a$ the set of all arrows from $a$ to $b$, and to each arrow $g: a \to a'$ the function "compose with $g$ on the right." To maintain the convention that functors between categories are always covariant, it is written as a functor on the opposite category $C^{\text{op}}$.
