A \emph{symmetric monoidal category} is a braided monoidal category in which the braiding $\gamma$ satisfies the additional condition
$$\gamma_{b,a} \circ \gamma_{a,b} = 1_{a \square b},$$
or equivalently $\gamma^2 = 1$, i.e., the diagram
\[
\begin{CD}
a \square b @>{\gamma_{a,b}}>> b \square a \\
@| @VV{\gamma_{b,a}}V \\
a \square b @= a \square b
\end{CD}
\]
commutes for all objects $a, b$. In this case, either one of the hexagon axioms (7) implies the other.
