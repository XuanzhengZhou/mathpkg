Let $M$ be a monoidal category, and let $w$ be a $\square$-word in $n$ letters, determining a functor $w_M : M^n \to M$. For each permutation $\tau \in S_n$, the \emph{permuted word functor} $(w\tau)_M : M^n \to M$ is defined by permuting the arguments of $w$ by $\tau$:
$$(w\tau)_M(a_1, \ldots, a_n) = w_M(a_{\tau 1}, \ldots, a_{\tau n}), \quad a_i \in M.$$
