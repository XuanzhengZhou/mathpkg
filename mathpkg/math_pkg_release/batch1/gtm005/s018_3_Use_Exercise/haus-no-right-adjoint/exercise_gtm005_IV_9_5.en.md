Show that the inclusion $\mathbf{Haus} \to \mathbf{Top}$ has no right adjoint, by showing that a coequalizer in $\mathbf{Top}$ of Hausdorff spaces need not be Hausdorff. Conclude that the forgetful functor $\mathbf{Haus} \to \mathbf{Set}$ has no right adjoint.
