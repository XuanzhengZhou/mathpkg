Construct left adjoints for each of the inclusion functors $\mathbf{Top}_{n+1} \to \mathbf{Top}_n$, $n = 0, 1, 2, 3$, where $\mathbf{Top}_n$ denotes the full subcategory of all $T_n$-spaces in $\mathbf{Top}$, with $T_4 = \text{Normal}$, $T_3 = \text{Regular}$, $T_2 = \text{Hausdorff}$, etc.
