Exercise 9.4 asks to construct left adjoints for the inclusion functors between categories of spaces satisfying progressively stronger separation axioms: $\mathbf{Top}_{n+1} \to \mathbf{Top}_n$ for $n = 0, 1, 2, 3$. Each left adjoint should send a $T_n$-space to a universal $T_{n+1}$-space (a "best approximation" satisfying the stronger separation axiom). For example, the $T_0$-ification sends a space to its Kolmogorov quotient, and the $T_2$-ification is the Hausdorff quotient $H$ from Proposition 2.
