This exercise asks to construct a left adjoint for the induced functor $G_*: \mathbf{Top}^J \to \mathbf{Set}^J$ on $J$-indexed diagram categories, where $J$ is discrete. The left adjoint equips a set $S$ with the weakest (initial) topology that makes a given family of functions $f_j: S \to GX_j$ continuous. This generalizes the construction of initial topologies via adjunctions.
