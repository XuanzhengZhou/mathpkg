Exercise 9.3(c) asks to conclude from the left adjoint constructed in part (b) that $\mathbf{Top}$ has all products. The product topology on $\prod_j X_j$ is precisely the weakest topology making all projection maps $\pi_j: \prod_j X_j \to X_j$ continuous. This follows from the adjunction that constructs the weakest topology for a family of functions.
