For a pair $\langle X, A \rangle$ (a space $X$ with subspace $A$) and a pointed topological space $Y$,

$$\mathbf{Top}_* (X/A, Y) = \mathbf{Top}^{(2)} (\langle X, A \rangle, \langle Y, * \rangle).$$

Thus the functor $\langle X, A \rangle \mapsto X/A$ is left adjoint to the functor $Y \mapsto \langle Y, * \rangle$ which sends each pointed space to the pair $\langle Y, * \rangle$.
