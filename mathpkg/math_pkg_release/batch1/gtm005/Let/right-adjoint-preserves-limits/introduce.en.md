This is one of the most useful properties of adjoint functors: right adjoints are automatically continuous. The proof exploits the fact that Lim is itself a right adjoint (to the diagonal functor $\Delta$), and adjunctions compose: passing to functor categories preserves adjunctions, and the resulting diagram of adjoints forces the natural isomorphism $\operatorname{Lim} \circ G^J \cong G \circ \operatorname{Lim}$. This explains why forgetful functors in algebra preserve products and equalizers.
