For objects $c_1, \ldots, c_m, a_1, \ldots, a_n$ in an $\textit{Ab}$-category $A$ with finite biproducts, there is an isomorphism of abelian groups
$$
A\!\left( \bigoplus_{k=1}^m c_k,\; \bigoplus_{j=1}^n a_j \right) \;\cong\; \bigoplus_{j=1}^n \bigoplus_{k=1}^m A(c_k, a_j),
$$
where the right-hand side denotes the iterated biproduct of abelian groups. Consequently, each arrow $f : \bigoplus_k c_k \to \bigoplus_j a_j$ is uniquely determined by the matrix of its components $f_{jk} = p_j f i_k : c_k \to a_j$, and composition of such arrows corresponds to matrix multiplication.
