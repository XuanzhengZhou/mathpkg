For given objects $a_1, \ldots, a_n \in A$ in an $\textit{Ab}$-category, the iterated biproduct $\bigoplus_j a_j$ is an object characterized (up to isomorphism in $A$) by the diagram
$$
a_j \xrightarrow{i_j} \bigoplus_j a_j \xrightarrow{p_k} a_k, \quad j, k = 1, \ldots, n
$$
and the equations
$$
i_1 p_1 + \cdots + i_n p_n = 1, \qquad p_k i_j = \delta_{kj},
$$
where $\delta_{kj} = 0$ for $k \neq j$ and $\delta_{kj} = 1$ for $k = j$.
