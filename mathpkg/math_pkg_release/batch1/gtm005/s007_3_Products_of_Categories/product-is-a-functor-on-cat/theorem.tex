The operation $\times$, which assigns to each pair $\langle B, C \rangle$ of small categories the product category $B \times C$ and to each pair of functors $\langle U, V \rangle$ the product functor $U \times V$, is itself a functor
$$\times: \text{Cat} \times \text{Cat} \to \text{Cat}.$$

Similarly, there are product functors $\text{Grp} \times \text{Grp} \to \text{Grp}$, $\text{Top} \times \text{Top} \to \text{Top}$, etc.
