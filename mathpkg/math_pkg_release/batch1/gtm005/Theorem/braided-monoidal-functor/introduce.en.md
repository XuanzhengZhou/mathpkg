A braided monoidal functor is a monoidal functor between braided monoidal categories that respects the braiding structure. It requires that applying the braiding after the monoidal structure coincides with applying the monoidal structure after the braiding. The category of braided monoidal categories has as morphisms precisely these braided monoidal functors.
