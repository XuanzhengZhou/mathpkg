This theorem is the converse direction of Mac Lane's Strictification Theorem: if a monoidal category is equivalent via a strong monoidal functor to a strict monoidal category, then the coherence condition for the associativity of the tensor product holds. Together with Theorem 1, this establishes the equivalence between coherence and strictifiability for monoidal categories.
