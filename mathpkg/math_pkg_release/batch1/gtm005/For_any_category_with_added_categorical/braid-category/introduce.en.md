The braid category $B$ assembles all braid groups $B_n$ into a single categorical structure. Its objects are natural numbers and morphisms from $n$ to $n$ are braids on $n$ strings. The tensor product is given by addition of numbers (placing braids side by side), making $B$ into a strict monoidal category that serves as the universal example of a braided monoidal category.
