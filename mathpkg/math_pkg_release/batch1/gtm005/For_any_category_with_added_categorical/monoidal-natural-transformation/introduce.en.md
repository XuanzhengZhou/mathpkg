A monoidal natural transformation is a natural transformation $\theta: F \to G$ between the underlying ordinary functors of two monoidal functors that additionally respects the monoidal structure. This means $\theta$ is compatible with the comparison maps $F_2$, $G_2$ for tensor products and with the unit comparisons $F_0$, $G_0$. Monoidal natural transformations serve as the 2-cells in the 2-category of monoidal categories, monoidal functors, and monoidal natural transformations.
