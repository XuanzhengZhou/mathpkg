The Artin braid group $B_n$ admits a finite presentation with $n-1$ generators $\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_{n-1}$ satisfying the braid relations $\sigma_i \sigma_{i+1} \sigma_i = \sigma_{i+1} \sigma_i \sigma_{i+1}$ (for adjacent indices) and the far-commutativity relations $\sigma_i \sigma_j = \sigma_j \sigma_i$ when $|i-j| \neq 1$. The generator $\sigma_i$ corresponds to crossing the $i$-th string under the $(i+1)$-st string.
