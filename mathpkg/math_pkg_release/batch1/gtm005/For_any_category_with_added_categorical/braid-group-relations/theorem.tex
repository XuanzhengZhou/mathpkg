The \textbf{braid group} $B_n$ can be generated by the $n-1$ braids $\sigma_i$, where $\sigma_i$ twists the $i$-th string once under the $(i+1)$-st string. The defining relations for these generators are:

$$\sigma_i \sigma_{i+1} \sigma_i = \sigma_{i+1} \sigma_i \sigma_{i+1}, \quad \text{all } i = 1, \ldots, n-1,$$

$$\sigma_i \sigma_j = \sigma_j \sigma_i, \quad |i-j| \neq 1.$$

The braid group $B_2$ is simply the infinite cyclic group on the (single) generator $\sigma_1$.
