Just as every adjunction induces a monad on the domain category, it dually induces a comonad on the codomain category. The endofunctor is $L = FG$, the counit of the comonad is the counit $\varepsilon$ of the adjunction, and the comultiplication $\delta = F\eta G$ is obtained by whiskering the unit $\eta$ with $F$ and $G$. This dual construction is essential in descent theory and in the coalgebraic approach to logic and computation.
