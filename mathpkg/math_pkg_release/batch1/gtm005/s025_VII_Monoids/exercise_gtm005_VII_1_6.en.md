Show by examples that the axioms (5) (the pentagon) and (7) (the triangle) are independent. That is, construct a category with a bifunctor $\square$, a unit object $e$, and isomorphisms $\alpha$, $\lambda$, $\varrho$ such that (5) holds but (7) fails, and another example where (7) holds but (5) fails.
