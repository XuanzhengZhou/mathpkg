For $B$ a monoidal category and $C$ any category, show that the functor category $B^C$ is monoidal, with multiplication $S \square T$ defined by $(S \square T)c = Sc \square Tc$ and $e : C \to B$ the constant functor at $e$. Show that the adjunction $B^{C \times D} \cong (B^C)^D$ is an isomorphism of monoidal categories.
