For $B$ a monoidal category, show that the opposite category $B^{\mathrm{op}}$ has the evident monoidal structure. Specifically, define the monoidal product on $B^{\mathrm{op}}$ using the monoidal product of $B$, and verify that the associativity and unit isomorphisms (obtained by reversing arrows) satisfy the pentagon and triangle axioms.
