Let $W$ be the free monoidal category on one generator $(-)$. For any monoidal category $B$ and any object $b \in B$, there is a unique morphism $W \to B$ of monoidal categories with $(-) \mapsto b$.

Equivalently, every formal diagram built from the associativity isomorphism $\alpha$, the left unit isomorphism $\lambda$, and the right unit isomorphism $\varrho$, using the bifunctor $\square$, commutes in any monoidal category.
