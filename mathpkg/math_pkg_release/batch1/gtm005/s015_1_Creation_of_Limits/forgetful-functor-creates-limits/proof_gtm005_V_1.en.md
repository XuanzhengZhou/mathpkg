This is a restatement of Theorem 2 in the terminology of creation of limits. By Theorem 1 (completeness of $\mathbf{Set}$), the limit $L = \operatorname{Cone}(*, UH)$ exists in $\mathbf{Set}$ with limiting cone $v : L \to UH$. Theorem 2 shows there is exactly one group structure on $L$ making each $v_j$ a homomorphism, and this $L$ with cone $v$ is a limit of $H$ in $\mathbf{Grp}$. Thus $U$ satisfies both conditions of the definition of creating limits.
