(Manes.) A parallel pair of arrows $f, g : a \to b$ in $C$ has a common left inverse $h$ when there is an arrow $h : b \to a$ with $hf = 1_a = hg$.

Prove that a category $C$ with all small products and with equalizers for all those parallel pairs with a common left inverse is small-complete. (Hint: The parallel pair used in the proof of Theorem 1 does in fact have a common left inverse.)
