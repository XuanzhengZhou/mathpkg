The equalizer of two parallel arrows is the universal subobject on which the two arrows agree. In Set, the equalizer of $f, g: X \to Y$ is the subset $\{x \in X \mid f(x) = g(x)\}$ with the inclusion map. Equalizers are the categorical formulation of equation-solving and solution sets.
