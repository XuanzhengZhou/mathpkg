Given an internal category in Grp with groups $C_0$ and $C_1$ and structure maps $d_0, d_1, i, \gamma$ satisfying the internal category axioms, define

$$H = \ker d_0, \qquad P = C_0.$$

Let $\partial : H \to P$ be the restriction of $d_1$ to $\ker d_0$. Then the short exact sequence of groups

$$1 \to \ker d_0 \to C_1 \xrightarrow{d_0} C_0 \to 1$$

determines an action of $C_0$ on $H = \ker d_0$ in the standard way (by conjugation in $C_1$ via a lift under $d_0$). With this action and the homomorphism $\partial$, the pair $(H, P)$ forms a crossed module.
