The \textbf{axiom of choice} (AC) in a category requires that every surjection $f : a \to b$ has a right inverse $\tau : b \to a$, i.e., a morphism satisfying

$$f \circ \tau = 1_b.$$

The right inverse $\tau$ picks out, for each point $p : 1 \to b$ of $b$, a point of $a$ (namely the composite $\tau \circ p$) which is mapped by $f$ onto $p$.
