An \textbf{internal category in Grp} consists of two groups $C_0$ and $C_1$ together with four group homomorphisms

$$d_0, d_1 : C_1 \rightarrow C_0, \quad i : C_0 \rightarrow C_1, \quad \gamma : C_1 \times_{C_0} C_1 \rightarrow C_1$$

satisfying the following conditions:

$$d_0 i = 1 = d_1 i,$$

and the vanishing of the commutator subgroup of $C_1$ formed from the kernels of $d_0$ and $d_1$:

$$[\ker d_0, \ker d_1] = 1.$$
