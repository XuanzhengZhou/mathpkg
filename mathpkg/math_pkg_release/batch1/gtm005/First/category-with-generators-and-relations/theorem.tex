Let $G$ be a graph and let $R$ be a family of relations on the arrows of the free category $C_G$ generated by $G$. The category presented by generators $G$ and relations $R$ is the quotient category
$$C_G / R,$$
where $R$ is extended to the least congruence on $C_G$ and the quotient is formed as in Proposition 1.

As a special case (when $G$ has a single object), this reduces to a monoid presented by generators and relations.
