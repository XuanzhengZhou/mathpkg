Let $G$ be a graph. A diagram of shape $G$ in a category $B$ is a morphism of graphs
$$D : G \to UB,$$
where $U : \text{Cat} \to \text{Grph}$ is the forgetful functor. Equivalently, via the free category adjunction, $D$ corresponds uniquely to a functor $D' : C_G \to B$ by the bijection
$$\text{Cat}(C_G, B) \cong \text{Grph}(G, UB), \qquad D' \mapsto D = UD' \circ P,$$
where $C_G$ is the free category generated by $G$ and $P : G \to UC_G$ is the canonical inclusion.
