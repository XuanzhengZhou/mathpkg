Show that every finite ordinal number is a free category. That is, for each $n \geq 0$, the category $\mathbf{n}$ (with objects $0, 1, \ldots, n-1$ and a unique arrow $i \to j$ whenever $i \leq j$) is isomorphic to the free category $C_G$ generated by some graph $G$. Describe the generating graph $G$ explicitly.
