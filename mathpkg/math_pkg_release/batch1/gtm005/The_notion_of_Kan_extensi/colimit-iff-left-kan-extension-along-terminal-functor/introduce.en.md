Colimits and limits can be understood as special cases of Kan extensions: the colimit of $F: J 	o C$ is the left Kan extension of $F$ along the terminal functor $J 	o \mathbf{1}$, and the limit is the right Kan extension. This unification reveals Kan extensions as the fundamental concept of category theory.
