A left Kan extension of $T: M \to A$ along $K: M \to C$ is a pair $\langle L, \eta: T \to LK \rangle$ where $L = \mathrm{Lan}_K T: C \to A$ is a functor and $\eta$ is a natural transformation, universal from $T$ to $A^K$. This gives a bijection

$$\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{Lan}_K T, S) \cong \mathrm{Nat}(T, SK),$$

natural in $S \in A^C$.
