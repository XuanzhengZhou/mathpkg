A right Kan extension of a functor $T: M \to A$ along $K: M \to C$ is a functor $R = \mathrm{Ran}_K T: C \to A$ together with a universal natural transformation $\varepsilon: RK \to T$. It provides the "best approximation" to an extension of $T$ along $K$, characterized by a natural bijection $\mathrm{Nat}(S, \mathrm{Ran}_K T) \cong \mathrm{Nat}(SK, T)$.
