Corollary 4 characterizes pointwise right Kan extensions via a bijection of hom-sets: $A(a, Rc) \cong \mathrm{Nat}(C(c, K-), A(a, T-))$. This reformulates the universal property without explicit reference to the limit formula, and is useful when working in enriched or internal category theory.
