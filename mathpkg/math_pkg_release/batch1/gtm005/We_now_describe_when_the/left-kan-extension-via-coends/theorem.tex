If $A$ has small copowers and all small coends, then for functors $K: M \to C$ and $T: M \to A$, the left Kan extension of $T$ along $K$ exists and is given pointwise by the coend

$$(\mathrm{Lan}_K T)c = \int^{m} C(Km, c) \cdot Tm,$$

where $X \cdot a$ denotes the copower (copower) of $a \in A$ by the set $X$. The universal arrow $\eta: T \to (\mathrm{Lan}_K T)K$ has components given by the copower injections at identity morphisms.
