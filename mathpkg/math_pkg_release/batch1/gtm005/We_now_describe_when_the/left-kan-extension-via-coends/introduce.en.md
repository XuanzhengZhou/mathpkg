Theorem 1 provides the coend formula for computing left Kan extensions: $(\mathrm{Lan}_K T)c = \int^m C(Km, c) \cdot Tm$. The proof uses the Yoneda lemma, the end formula for natural transformations, and the Fubini theorem for ends to establish the required natural bijection $\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{Lan}_K T, S) \cong \mathrm{Nat}(T, SK)$.
