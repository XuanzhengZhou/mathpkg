Corollary 2 states that right adjoints preserve right Kan extensions: if $F \dashv G$ and $\mathrm{Ran}_K T$ exists, then $G \circ \mathrm{Ran}_K T \cong \mathrm{Ran}_K(GT)$. The proof follows directly by composing the adjunction bijection with the Kan extension universal property.
