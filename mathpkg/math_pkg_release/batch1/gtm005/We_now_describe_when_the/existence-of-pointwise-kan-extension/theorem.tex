If in the limit formula of the pointwise right Kan extension theorem the limit exists and if $K: M \to C$ is the insertion of a full subcategory $M \subset C$, then the constructed functor $R: C \to A$ satisfies $RK = T$ (i.e., $R$ extends $T$), and the identity natural transformation $1: RK \to T$ makes $R$ the right Kan extension of $T$ along the insertion $K: M \to C$.
