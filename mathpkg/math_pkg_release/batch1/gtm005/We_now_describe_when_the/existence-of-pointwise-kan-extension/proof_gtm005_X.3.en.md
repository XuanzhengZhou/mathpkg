Apply the pointwise right Kan extension theorem to the insertion $K: M \to C$. When $K$ is the inclusion of a full subcategory, for each $n \in M$, the comma category $(Kn \downarrow K)$ has the terminal object $\langle 1_{Kn}, n \rangle$, so the limit over this comma category reduces to evaluating $T$ at $n$, giving $R(Kn) = Tn$. The limiting cone at the identity gives the identity natural transformation.
