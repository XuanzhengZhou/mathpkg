An immediate corollary of the Yoneda Lemma: every natural transformation between covariant hom-functors $D(r, -) \to D(s, -)$ is induced by precomposition with a unique arrow $h \colon s \to r$. This implies that the Yoneda embedding $y \colon D^{\mathrm{op}} \to \mathbf{Set}^D$ sending $r$ to $D(r, -)$ is fully faithful — i.e., category theory can be done entirely within functor categories.
