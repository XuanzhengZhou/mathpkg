Use only universality (of projections) to prove the following isomorphisms of group theory:
  (a) For normal subgroups $M, N$ of $G$ with $M \subset N$, $(G/M)/(N/M) \cong G/N$.
  (b) For subgroups $S$ and $N$ of $G$, $N$ normal, with join $SN$, $SN/N \cong S/(S \cap N)$.
