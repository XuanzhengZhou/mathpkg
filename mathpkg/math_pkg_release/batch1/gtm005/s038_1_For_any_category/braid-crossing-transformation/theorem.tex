In the braid category $\mathcal{B}$, for any two objects $m, n \in \mathbb{N}$, the \emph{braid crossing transformation}
$$
\gamma_{m,n} : m + n \longrightarrow n + m
$$
is the braid formed by crossing $m$ strings over $n$ strings. This family of morphisms is natural in $m$ and $n$: for any braids $\xi : m \to m'$ and $\eta : n \to n'$, the following diagram commutes:
\[
\begin{CD}
m + n @>{\gamma_{m,n}}>> n + m \\
@V{\xi + \eta}VV @VV{\eta + \xi}V \\
m' + n' @>{\gamma_{m',n'}}>> n' + m'.
\end{CD}
\]
