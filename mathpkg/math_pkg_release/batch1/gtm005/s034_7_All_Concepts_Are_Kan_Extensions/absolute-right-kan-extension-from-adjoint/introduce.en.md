This proposition establishes that the right Kan extension arising from an adjoint functor is absolute: it is preserved not just by the original functor $G$, but by every functor whatsoever. For any $H: A \to C$, the composite $HF$ is the right Kan extension of $H$ along $G$ with unit $H\varepsilon$. The absolute nature of these Kan extensions provides an essential tool for manipulating and composing Kan extensions in further categorical constructions.
