The \textbf{fundamental groupoid} $\pi(X)$ of a topological space $X$ has as objects the points $x$ of $X$, and as arrows $x \rightarrow x'$ the homotopy classes of paths $f: I \rightarrow X$ from $x$ to $x'$, where $I = [0,1]$, $f(0) = x$, $f(1) = x'$. Composition is given by concatenation of paths; identities are constant paths.
