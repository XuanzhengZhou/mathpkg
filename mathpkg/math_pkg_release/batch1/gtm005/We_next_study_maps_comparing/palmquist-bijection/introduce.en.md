This proposition (due to Palmquist) establishes a natural bijection between two families of natural transformations in the context of an adjoint square. Given functors $H$ and $K$ and two adjunctions, there is a one-to-one correspondence between natural transformations $\alpha : F'HG \to K$ and $\beta : H \to G'KF$. This is a further generalization of the conjugate pair correspondence established in Theorem 2.
