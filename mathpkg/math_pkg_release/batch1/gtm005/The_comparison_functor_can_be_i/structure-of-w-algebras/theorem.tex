For the word-monad $W$ on $\mathbf{Set}$ arising from the free semigroup adjunction, a $W$-algebra has the form
$$\langle S, v_1, v_2, \dots \rangle$$
where $S$ is a set equipped with one $n$-ary operation
$$v_n: S^n \to S$$
for each positive integer $n$, such that the following coherence conditions hold for all positive integers $k$ and all $k$-tuples $n_1, \dots, n_k$ of positive integers:
$$v_k \circ (v_{n_1} \times \cdots \times v_{n_k}) = v_{n_1 + \cdots + n_k}.$$
In particular, $v_1 = 1_S$ is the identity on $S$, and $v_2: S \times S \to S$ is an associative binary operation.
