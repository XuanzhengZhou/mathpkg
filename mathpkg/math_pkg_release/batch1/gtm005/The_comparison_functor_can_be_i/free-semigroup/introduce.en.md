The free semigroup $WX$ on a set $X$ is the collection of all finite non-empty words over the alphabet $X$, with multiplication given by concatenation. This construction arises from the adjunction between the forgetful functor $G: \mathbf{Smgrp} \to \mathbf{Set}$ and the free semigroup functor $W$, yielding a monad $\langle W, \eta, \mu \rangle$ on $\mathbf{Set}$. The unit $\eta_X$ embeds each element as a word of length one, while the multiplication $\mu_X$ flattens a word of words by removing the outer brackets.
