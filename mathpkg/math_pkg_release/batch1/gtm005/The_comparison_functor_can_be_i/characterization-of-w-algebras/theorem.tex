A system $\langle S, v_1, v_2, \ldots \rangle$ with $n$-ary operations $v_n: S^n \to S$ for each positive integer $n$ is a $W$-algebra for the word monad $W$ if and only if
$$v_1 = 1_S,$$
$v_2: S \times S \to S$ is an associative binary operation on $S$, and for all $n \geq 2$,
$$v_{n+1} = v_n \circ (v_2 \times 1_S) : S^{n+1} \to S.$$

Consequently, the comparison functor
$$K: \mathbf{Smgrp} \to \mathbf{Set}^W$$
sending a semigroup $\langle S, v \rangle$ to the $W$-algebra $\langle S, 1_S, v_2, \ldots, v_n, \ldots \rangle$ where $v_2 = v$ and each $v_n$ is the $n$-fold iterate of $v$, is an isomorphism of categories.
