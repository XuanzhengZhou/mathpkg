For $S : C^{\mathrm{op}} \times C \to B$ and $b, b' \in B$, show that dinatural transformations $b \xrightarrow{\bullet} S$ and $S \xrightarrow{\bullet} b'$ do not in general have a well-defined composite $b \to b'$.
