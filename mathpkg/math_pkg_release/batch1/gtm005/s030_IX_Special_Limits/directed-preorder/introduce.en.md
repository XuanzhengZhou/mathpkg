A directed preorder (also called a directed set or filtered set) is a preorder in which every pair of elements has an upper bound. This property extends by induction to any finite set of elements. Directed preorders serve as the classical index categories for filtered colimits, generalizing the intuitive notion of "going to infinity" along a partial order. They are the simplest examples of filtered categories and provide the foundation for constructing colimits from coproducts and coequalizers.
