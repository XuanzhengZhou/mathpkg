A filtered colimit is the colimit of a diagram whose indexing category is filtered. Filtered colimits generalize colimits over directed preorders to arbitrary categories. They enjoy particularly favorable properties: finite limits commute with filtered colimits in Set, and all small colimits can be constructed from finite coproducts, coequalizers, and filtered colimits. This makes filtered colimits a fundamental tool in categorical algebra and homological algebra.
