\text{Proposition 2.} The forgetful functor $\mathbf{Grp} \to \mathbf{Set}$ creates filtered colimits. Equivalently, if $J$ is a filtered category and $G : J \to \mathbf{Grp}$ is a functor, then the colimit of $G$ exists and its underlying set is the colimit of the composite $J \xrightarrow{G} \mathbf{Grp} \to \mathbf{Set}$.
