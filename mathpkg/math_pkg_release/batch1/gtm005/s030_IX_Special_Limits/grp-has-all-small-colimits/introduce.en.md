This corollary establishes that the category of groups is cocomplete. The proof combines three ingredients: finite coproducts exist (free products of two groups), filtered colimits exist by Proposition 2, hence all small coproducts exist by Theorem 1. Adding coequalizers (quotients by normal subgroups) then yields all small colimits by the dual of the standard colimit construction theorem. The explicit description of the infinite coproduct as a directed union of finite free products is a concrete illustration of Theorem 1 in action.
