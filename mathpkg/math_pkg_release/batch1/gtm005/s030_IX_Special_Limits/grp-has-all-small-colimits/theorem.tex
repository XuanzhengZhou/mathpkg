\text{Corollary.} The category $\mathbf{Grp}$ of groups has all small colimits. In particular, the coproduct $\coprod_i G_i$ of groups is the free product, obtained as the union of all finite free products $G_{i_1} * G_{i_2} * \cdots * G_{i_k}$ identified along the inclusion maps. The coequalizer of two homomorphisms $u, v : G \to H$ is the projection $H \to H/N$ onto the quotient by the least normal subgroup $N$ containing all elements $(u g)(v g)^{-1}$ for $g \in G$.
