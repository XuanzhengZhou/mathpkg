If the base category $B$ has finite products, so does the category $c\mathbf{Lact}$ of left $c$-actions, in such a way that the projections $a \times a' \rightarrow a$, $a'$ of the product (in $B$) become morphisms of actions (in $c\mathbf{Lact}$).
