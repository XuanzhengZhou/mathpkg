(Generalization of the tensor product of a right module by a left module.) If $B$ has coequalizers, $c$ is a monoid, $\sigma : b \square c \rightarrow b$ a right action, and $\nu : c \square a \rightarrow a$ a left action, construct a "tensor product" $b \square_c a \in B$ as the coequalizer of two maps $b \square (c \square a) \rightarrow b \square a$ given by the actions, and prove that this yields a functor $\square_c : \mathbf{Ract}_c \times c\mathbf{Lact} \rightarrow B$.
