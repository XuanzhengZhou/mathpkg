Infinite products generalize binary products to arbitrary families of objects. In Set, the infinite product is the cartesian product of the family; in Grp, it is the direct product. The right-hand side product $\prod_j C(c, a_j)$ is taken in Set (assuming small hom-sets), showing that the hom-functor preserves products.
