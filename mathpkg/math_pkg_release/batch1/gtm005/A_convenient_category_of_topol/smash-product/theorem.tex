For pointed spaces $Z, Y \in \mathbf{CGHaus}_*$, the \textbf{wedge} is $Z \vee Y = (Z \mathbin{\square} *) \cup (* \mathbin{\square} Y) \subset Z \mathbin{\square} Y$. The \textbf{smash product} is the identification space
$$Z \wedge Y = (Z \mathbin{\square} Y) /\!/ (Z \vee Y),$$
obtained by collapsing the wedge $Z \vee Y$ to the base point. Equivalently, $Z \wedge Y$ is the quotient of $Z \mathbin{\square} Y$ by the closed subspace $(Z \mathbin{\square} *) \cup (* \mathbin{\square} Y)$.
