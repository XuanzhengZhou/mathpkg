The smash product $Z \wedge Y$ is the fundamental monoidal product in the category $\mathbf{CGHaus}_*$ of pointed compactly generated Hausdorff spaces. It is obtained by taking the $\square$-product $Z \mathbin{\square} Y$ and collapsing the wedge $Z \vee Y = (Z \mathbin{\square} *) \cup (* \mathbin{\square} Y)$ to the new base point. The smash product is commutative and associative up to natural isomorphism, making $\mathbf{CGHaus}_*$ a symmetric monoidal category with the two-point space $S^0$ as unit. Together with the internal hom $X^{(*)Y}$ of base-point-preserving maps, it yields the adjunction $\mathbf{CGHaus}_*(Z \wedge Y, X) \cong \mathbf{CGHaus}_*(Z, X^{(*)Y})$.
