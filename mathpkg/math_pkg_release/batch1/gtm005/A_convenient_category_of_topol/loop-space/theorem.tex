The \textbf{loop space} functor $\Omega: \mathbf{CGHaus}_* \to \mathbf{CGHaus}_*$ is defined by
$$\Omega X = X^{(*)S^1},$$
the subspace of $X^{S^1}$ consisting of all base-point-preserving maps $S^1 \to X$, i.e., continuous maps $f: I \to X$ (where $I = [0,1]$) with $f(0) = f(1) = *_X$, equipped with the subspace topology from the Kelleyfication of the compact-open topology. The constant loop at $*_X$ serves as the base point of $\Omega X$.
