For topological spaces $Y$ and $Z$, the \textbf{compact-open topology} on the set $\operatorname{Top}(Y, Z)$ of all continuous maps $Y \to Z$ has as a subbasis for its open sets the collection
$$N(C, U) = \{\, h \in \operatorname{Top}(Y, Z) \mid h(C) \subset U \,\}$$
where $C$ ranges over all compact subsets of $Y$ and $U$ ranges over all open subsets of $Z$. The resulting topological space is denoted $\operatorname{Cop}(Y, Z)$.
