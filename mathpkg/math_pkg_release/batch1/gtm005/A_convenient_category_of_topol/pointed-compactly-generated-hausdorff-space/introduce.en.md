The category $\mathbf{CGHaus}_*$ is the pointed variant of the category of compactly generated Hausdorff spaces. Its objects are compactly generated Hausdorff spaces with a chosen base point, and its morphisms are base-point-preserving continuous maps. This category serves as the foundation for pointed homotopy theory, where the reduced suspension $\Sigma$ and loop space $\Omega$ functors form an adjunction $\Sigma \dashv \Omega$. The smash product $X \wedge Y$ provides a symmetric monoidal structure on $\mathbf{CGHaus}_*$, analogous to the tensor product in algebra.
