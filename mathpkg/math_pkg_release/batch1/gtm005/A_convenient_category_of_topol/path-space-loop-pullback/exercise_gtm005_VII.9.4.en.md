The Path space functor $P: \mathbf{CGHaus} \to \mathbf{CGHaus}$ has $PX = X^{(*)I}$, where $0$ is taken as the base point of the interval $I$. For each path $f \in PX$, $f \mapsto f(1)$ defines a natural transformation $\pi: P \to \mathrm{Id}$. Show that $\Omega$ can be obtained as the pullback of a diagram $P \to \mathrm{Id} \leftarrow *$. (Classically, $\Omega X$ is the "fibre" of $\pi_X: PX \to X$.)
