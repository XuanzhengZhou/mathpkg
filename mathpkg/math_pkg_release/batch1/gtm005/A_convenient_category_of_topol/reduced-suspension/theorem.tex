The \textbf{reduced suspension} functor $\Sigma: \mathbf{CGHaus}_* \to \mathbf{CGHaus}_*$ is defined by
$$\Sigma X = X \wedge S^1,$$
where $S^1 = I /\!/ \{0, 1\}$ is the circle obtained from the unit interval $I = \{t \mid 0 \leq t \leq 1\}$ by identifying the endpoints, with base point $0 = 1$. Equivalently, $\Sigma X$ is the double cone over $X$ (the cylinder $X \times I$ with top $X \times \{1\}$ and bottom $X \times \{0\}$ collapsed) with the generator $* \times I$ collapsed to the base point.
