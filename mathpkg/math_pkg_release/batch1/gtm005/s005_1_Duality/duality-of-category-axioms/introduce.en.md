Under the duality operation, each axiom of a category transforms into another axiom. This is because the category axioms are symmetric with respect to arrow reversal: for instance, the axiom that every object has an identity arrow dualizes to the same statement. This property is the essential justification for the duality principle --- since the axioms themselves are closed under dualization, any logical consequence of the axioms must also be.
