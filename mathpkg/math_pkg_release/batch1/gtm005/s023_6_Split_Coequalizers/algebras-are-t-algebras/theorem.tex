Let $\Omega$ be a set of operators, $E$ a set of identities (on the operators derived from $\Omega$), $G$ the forgetful functor from the category $\langle \Omega, E \rangle\text{-}\mathbf{Alg}$ of all small $\langle \Omega, E \rangle$-algebras to $\mathbf{Set}$, and $T$ the resulting monad in $\mathbf{Set}$. Then the comparison functor
$$K: \langle \Omega, E \rangle\text{-}\mathbf{Alg} \to \mathbf{Set}^T$$
is an isomorphism.
