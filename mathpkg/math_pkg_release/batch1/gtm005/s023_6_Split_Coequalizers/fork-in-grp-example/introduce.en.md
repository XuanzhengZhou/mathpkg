This example shows a fork in the category of groups $\mathbf{Grp}$. Given a normal subgroup $N$ of $G$, the semidirect product $G \ltimes_0 N$ provides the domain of a parallel pair, with the two projections $\partial_0$ (forgetting $N$) and $\partial_1$ (multiplying). The quotient map $p: G \to G/N$ coequalizes them, forming a fork that encodes the quotient group construction.
