Let $N \triangleleft G$ be a normal subgroup of $G$ and form the semidirect product $G \ltimes_0 N$, whose elements are pairs $\langle x, n \rangle$ for $x \in G$, $n \in N$ with multiplication
$$\langle x, n \rangle \langle y, m \rangle = \langle xy, (y^{-1}ny)m \rangle.$$
Then
$$G \ltimes_0 N \xrightarrow[\partial_1]{\partial_0} G \xrightarrow{p} G/N$$
is a fork, where $p$ is the usual projection to the quotient group $G/N$, $\partial_0\langle x, n\rangle = x$, and $\partial_1\langle x, n\rangle = xn$.
