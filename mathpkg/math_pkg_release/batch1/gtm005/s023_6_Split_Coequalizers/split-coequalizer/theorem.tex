By a \textbf{split coequalizer} of $\partial_0$ and $\partial_1$ we mean the arrow $e$ of a split fork on $\partial_0$ and $\partial_1$. That is, $e: b \to c$ is a split coequalizer if there exist arrows $s: c \to b$ and $t: b \to a$ such that
$$e \partial_0 = e \partial_1, \quad es = 1_c, \quad \partial_0 t = 1_b, \quad \partial_1 t = se.$$
