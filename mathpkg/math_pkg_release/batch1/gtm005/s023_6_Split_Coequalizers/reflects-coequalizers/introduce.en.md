A functor $G$ reflects coequalizers if whenever a fork in the domain category becomes a coequalizer after applying $G$, it was already a coequalizer. This is the special case of reflecting colimits for the diagram shape of a parallel pair. Reflection of coequalizers appears in alternative formulations and equivalence versions of Beck's monadicity theorem.
