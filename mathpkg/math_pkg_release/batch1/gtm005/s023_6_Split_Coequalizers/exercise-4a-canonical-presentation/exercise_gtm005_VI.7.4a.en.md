Show that the canonical presentation of a $T$-algebra $\langle x, h \rangle$ is the fork
$$\langle T^2 x, \mu_{Tx} \rangle \xrightarrow[Th]{h} \langle Tx, \mu_x \rangle \xrightarrow{h} \langle x, h \rangle$$
in the category $X^T$ of $T$-algebras.
