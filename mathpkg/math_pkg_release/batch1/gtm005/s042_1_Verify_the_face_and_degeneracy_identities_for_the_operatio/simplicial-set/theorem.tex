A \textbf{simplicial set} $X$ is a contravariant functor $X: \Delta^{\text{op}} \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}$. Equivalently, it consists of sets $X_n$ ($n \geq 0$) together with face operators $d_i: X_n \rightarrow X_{n-1}$ ($0 \leq i \leq n$) and degeneracy operators $s_i: X_n \rightarrow X_{n+1}$ ($0 \leq i \leq n$) satisfying the dual simplicial identities.
