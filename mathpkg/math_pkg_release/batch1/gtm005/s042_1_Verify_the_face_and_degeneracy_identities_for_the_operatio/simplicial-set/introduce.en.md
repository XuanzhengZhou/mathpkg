Simplicial sets are the combinatorial models of topological spaces, forming the foundation of modern homotopy theory. The category of simplicial sets, $\mathbf{sSet}$, is a presheaf topos and supports a Quillen model structure equivalent to the homotopy theory of topological spaces.
