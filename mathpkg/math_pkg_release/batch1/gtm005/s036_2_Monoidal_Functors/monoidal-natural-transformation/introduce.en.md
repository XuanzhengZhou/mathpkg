A monoidal natural transformation is a natural transformation $\theta : F \to G$ between two monoidal functors that is compatible with the monoidal structure. This means the naturality squares involving the coherence maps $F_2$, $G_2$ and $F_0$, $G_0$ must commute. Monoidal natural transformations are the 2-cells in the 2-category of monoidal categories, monoidal functors, and monoidal natural transformations.
