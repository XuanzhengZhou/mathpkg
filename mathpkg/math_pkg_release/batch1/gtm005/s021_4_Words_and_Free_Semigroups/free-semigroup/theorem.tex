The \text{free semigroup} $WX$ on a set $X$ consists of all words
$$\langle x_1 \rangle \ldots \langle x_n \rangle$$
of positive length $n$ spelled in letters $x_i \in X$, where we write $\langle x \rangle$ to distinguish the word $\langle x \rangle$ in $WX$ from the element $x \in X$. Words are multiplied by juxtaposition,
$$(\langle x_1 \rangle \ldots \langle x_n \rangle)(\langle y_1 \rangle \ldots \langle y_m \rangle) = \langle x_1 \rangle \ldots \langle x_n \rangle \langle y_1 \rangle \ldots \langle y_m \rangle.$$
