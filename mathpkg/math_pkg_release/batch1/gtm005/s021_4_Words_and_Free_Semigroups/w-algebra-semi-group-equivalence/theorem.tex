The system $\langle S, v_1, v_2, \ldots \rangle$ is a $W$-algebra, as above, if and only if $v_1 = 1$, $v_2 : S \times S \to S$ is an associative binary operation on $S$, and for all $n \geq 2$,
$$v_{n+1} = v_n \circ (v_2 \times 1) : S^{n+1} \to S.$$

Thus the comparison functor $K : \mathbf{Smgrp} \to \mathbf{Set}^W$ is the evident map
$$\langle S, v \rangle \mapsto \langle S, 1, v_2, \ldots, v_n, \ldots \rangle$$
where $v_n$ is the $n$-fold iterate of the binary operation $v = v_2$. In particular, $K$ is an isomorphism of categories.
