This proposition states that the identity arrow $1_b$ for each object $b$ in a metacategory is uniquely determined by the unit law axioms. This fact justifies the common practice of identifying an identity arrow with its corresponding object, writing $b: b 	o b$ to denote $1_b$. In an arrows-only formulation, this uniqueness becomes the defining property of identity arrows.
