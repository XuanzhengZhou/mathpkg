An Ab-category (also called a preadditive category) is a category enriched over the monoidal category of abelian groups. This means that each hom-set carries the structure of an additive abelian group, and composition is bilinear with respect to this addition. Ab-categories form the foundational setting for additive and abelian categories, where one can take sums, differences, and zero morphisms of parallel arrows.
