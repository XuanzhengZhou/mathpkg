The Yoneda Corollary states that the Yoneda embedding $Y: D^{\mathrm{op}} \to \mathbf{Set}^D$ is fully faithful. Every natural transformation between representable functors comes from a unique morphism between the representing objects (in the opposite direction). Two objects are isomorphic iff their representable functors are naturally isomorphic. This embeds any locally small category into a functor category.
