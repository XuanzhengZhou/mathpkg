Enriched categories generalize ordinary categories (enriched over Set) by replacing hom-sets with hom-objects in a closed monoidal category $V$. This allows categories with topological hom-spaces, metric hom-spaces, and abelian group hom-sets, among others. Enriched category theory provides a unified framework for these structures.
