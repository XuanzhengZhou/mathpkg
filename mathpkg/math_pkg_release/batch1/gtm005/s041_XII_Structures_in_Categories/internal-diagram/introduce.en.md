"Internal diagram" is an alternative terminology (used by Borceux) for a left $C$-object. This is the internal analogue of a covariant functor from $C$ to the ambient category $\mathbf{Set}$. See the canonical entry \texttt{left-c-object} for the full definition and discussion.
