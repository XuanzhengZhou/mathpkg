(Lawvere.) Given functors $G: A \to X$ and $F: X \to A$, show that each adjunction $\langle F, G, \varphi \rangle$ can be described as an isomorphism $\theta$ of comma categories such that $\theta: (F \downarrow I_A) \cong (I_X \downarrow G)$ with the natural compatibility condition.
