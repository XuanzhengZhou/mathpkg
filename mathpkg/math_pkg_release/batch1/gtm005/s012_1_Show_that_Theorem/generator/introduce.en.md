A generator (or separator) is an object whose arrows can distinguish between distinct parallel arrows. In Set, the one-point set is a generator. In Grp, $\mathbb{Z}$ is a generator. The existence of a generator is a hypothesis of the Special Adjoint Functor Theorem.
