For the adjunction $\langle \Delta, \times, \varphi \rangle$ -- product right adjoint to diagonal -- show that the unit $\delta_c: c \to c \times c$ for each object $c \in C$ is the unique arrow such that the diagram commutes. (This arrow $\delta_c$ is often called the diagonal arrow of $c$.) If $C = \mathbf{Set}$, describe $\delta_c$.
