A full subcategory $A$ of $B$ is \emph{reflective} in $B$ when the inclusion functor $K: A \to B$ has a left adjoint $F: B \to A$. The functor $F$ is called the \emph{reflector}, and the adjunction $\langle F, K, \varphi \rangle: B \to A$ with unit $\eta_b: b \to KFb$ gives a universal arrow from $b$ to the inclusion.
