The category $\mathbf{CGHaus}$ of compactly generated weak Hausdorff spaces is:
\begin{itemize}
\item Complete and cocomplete;
\item Cartesian closed: there is a natural isomorphism $\hom(X \times Y, Z) \cong \hom(X, \operatorname{Map}(Y, Z))$, where the mapping space has the compact-open topology modified by $k$-ification;
\item The geometric realization functor from simplicial sets preserves finite products.
\end{itemize}
