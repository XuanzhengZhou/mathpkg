The category $\mathbf{CGHaus}$ of compactly generated Hausdorff spaces is cartesian closed. For spaces $X, Y \in \mathbf{CGHaus}$, the exponential $Y^X$ is given by the \emph{Kelleyfication} $k(C(X, Y))$ of the compact-open topology on the set of continuous maps, and the natural bijection $\hom_{\mathbf{CGHaus}}(X \times Y, Z) \cong \hom_{\mathbf{CGHaus}}(X, Z^Y)$ holds.
