CGHaus is the category of compactly generated Hausdorff spaces. A Hausdorff space is compactly generated if its topology is determined by its compact subsets — a set is closed precisely when its intersection with every compact set is closed. This category serves as a convenient category for topological studies because, unlike the ordinary category of topological spaces, it is cartesian closed and has well-behaved function spaces.
