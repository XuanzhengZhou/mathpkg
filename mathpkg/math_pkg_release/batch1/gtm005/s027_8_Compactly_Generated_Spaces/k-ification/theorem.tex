The \textbf{$k$-ification} functor $k: \mathbf{Top} \rightarrow \mathbf{Top}$ sends each space $X$ to the same underlying set equipped with the $k$-ification topology: a subset $U \subset X$ is open if and only if $U \cap K$ is open in $K$ for every compact Hausdorff $K$ mapping continuously into $X$. This is the right adjoint to the inclusion of $k$-spaces into Top.
