The category of compactly generated spaces provides a 'convenient category' for homotopy theory where the exponential law $\operatorname{Map}(X \times Y, Z) \cong \operatorname{Map}(X, \operatorname{Map}(Y, Z))$ holds naturally. Compactly generated weak Hausdorff spaces form a Cartesian closed category suitable for algebraic topology.
