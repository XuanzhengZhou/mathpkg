The unit law is the second of the two axioms defining a metacategory, alongside associativity. It requires that each object $b$ has an identity arrow $1_b$ that serves as a left identity for arrows into $b$ and a right identity for arrows out of $b$: $1_b \circ f = f$ and $g \circ 1_b = g$ whenever the compositions are defined.
