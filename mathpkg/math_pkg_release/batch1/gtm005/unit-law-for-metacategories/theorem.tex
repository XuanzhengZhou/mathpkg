For all arrows $f: a \rightarrow b$ and $g: b \rightarrow c$, composition with the identity arrow $1_b$ gives
$$1_b \circ f = f \quad \text{and} \quad g \circ 1_b = g.$$

This axiom asserts that the identity arrow $1_b$ of each object $b$ acts as an identity for the operation of composition, whenever this makes sense.
