The opposite of a product is the product of opposites: $(C 	imes D)^{\mathrm{op}} \cong C^{\mathrm{op}} 	imes D^{\mathrm{op}}$. This basic isomorphism is used repeatedly when dealing with bifunctors of mixed variance and the calculus of ends and coends.
