The projection functors $P: B \times C \to B$ and $Q: B \times C \to C$ extract the first and second components of objects and arrows in the product category. They are the canonical forgetful maps from the product to each factor and satisfy a universal property: any pair of functors into $B$ and $C$ from a common domain factors uniquely through them.
