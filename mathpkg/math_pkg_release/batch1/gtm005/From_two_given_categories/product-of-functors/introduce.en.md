The product of two functors $U: B \to B'$ and $V: C \to C'$ is a functor $U \times V: B \times C \to B' \times C'$ that acts componentwise on objects and arrows. It is the unique functor making the square of projections commute, and satisfies $(U' \times V') \circ (U \times V) = U'U \times V'V$ when compositions are defined.
