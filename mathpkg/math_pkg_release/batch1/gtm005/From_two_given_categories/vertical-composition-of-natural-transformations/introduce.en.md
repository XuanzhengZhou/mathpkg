Vertical composition of natural transformations is defined componentwise: if $\sigma: S \Rightarrow T$ and $	au: T \Rightarrow U$, then $(	au \circ \sigma)_c = 	au_c \circ \sigma_c$. This is the composition in the functor category $[C, B]$, giving it the structure of a category with functors as objects and natural transformations as arrows.
