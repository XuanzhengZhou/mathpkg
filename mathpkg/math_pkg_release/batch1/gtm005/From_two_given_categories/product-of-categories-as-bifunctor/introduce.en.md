The product of categories extends to a bifunctor on $\mathbf{Cat}$: the assignment $(C, D) \mapsto C 	imes D$ and $(F, G) \mapsto F 	imes G$ is a functor from $\mathbf{Cat} 	imes \mathbf{Cat}$ to $\mathbf{Cat}$, making $\mathbf{Cat}$ a monoidal category under the Cartesian product.
