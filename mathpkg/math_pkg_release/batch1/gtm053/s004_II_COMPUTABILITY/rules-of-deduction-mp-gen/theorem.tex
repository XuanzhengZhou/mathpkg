\textbf{Modus Ponens (MP):} The formula $Q$ is called a direct consequence of the formulas $P$ and $P \Rightarrow Q$ using the rule of deduction MP. That is, if $P$ and $P \Rightarrow Q$ are true, then $Q$ is true.

\textbf{Generalization (Gen):} The formula $\forall x P$ is called a direct consequence of the formula $P$ using the rule of deduction Gen. That is, if $P$ is true (for all values of $x$), then $\forall x P$ is true.
