This lemma establishes that the pairing between left and right parentheses in an expression of a language in $\mathfrak{L}_1$ is unique whenever it exists. It is a combinatorial result used in the proof of the Unique Reading Lemma. The proof uses a function $\varepsilon$ taking value $1$ on left parentheses, $-1$ on right parentheses, and shows that $c(i)$ must be the least $l > i$ for which the cumulative sum from $i$ to $l$ equals zero.
