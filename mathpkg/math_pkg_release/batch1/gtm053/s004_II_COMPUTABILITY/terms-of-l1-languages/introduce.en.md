In any first-order language of class $\mathfrak{L}_1$, the distinguished expressions are divided into terms and formulas. Terms are built recursively from variables and constants by applying operation symbols. This definition is well-founded because the intersection of any two sets satisfying conditions (a) and (b) also satisfies them, so the least such subset exists. Every term is obtained from atomic terms in a finite number of steps.
