This corollary follows immediately from Proposition 2.10. Since a closed formula has no free variables, the condition that two assignments agree on all free variables is vacuously satisfied for any $\xi, \xi' \in \overline{M}$. Therefore, the truth value $|P|_\phi(\xi)$ is the same for all $\xi$, making it a property of the formula and the interpretation alone.
