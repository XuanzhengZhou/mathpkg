The interpretation of terms extends the primary mappings to all terms of the language. Each term becomes a function on the interpretation class $\overline{M}$, which assigns values to all variables. Constants are interpreted as constant functions, variables as projection functions, and compound terms $f(t_1,\ldots,t_r)$ are interpreted by applying the interpreted operation $\phi(f)$ to the interpreted subterms.
