The quantifier axioms consist of the following schemas:

\textbf{(a)} $\forall x (P \Rightarrow Q) \Rightarrow (P \Rightarrow \forall x Q)$, provided $x$ does not occur freely in $P$.

\textbf{(b)} $\forall x P \Rightarrow P(t)$, where $t$ is a term free for $x$ in $P$ (axiom of specialization).

\textbf{(c)} $P(t) \Rightarrow \exists x P(x)$, where $t$ is a term free for $x$ in $P$.
