Formulas are defined recursively as follows:

(a) If $p$ is a relation of degree $r$ and $t_1, \ldots, t_r$ are terms, then $p(t_1, \ldots, t_r)$ is an (atomic) formula.

(b) If $P$ and $Q$ are formulas, and $x$ is a variable, then the expressions
$$
(P) \Leftrightarrow (Q),\quad (P) \Rightarrow (Q),\quad (P) \vee (Q),\quad (P) \wedge (Q),
$$
$$
\neg(P),\quad \forall x(P),\quad \exists x(P)
$$
are formulas.
