\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] The negation of the continuum hypothesis cannot be deduced from the other axioms of set theory if those axioms are consistent (Godel, 1938).
\item[(b)] The continuum hypothesis cannot be deduced from the other axioms of set theory if those axioms are consistent (Cohen, 1963).
\end{enumerate}
The same holds true for the generalized continuum hypothesis.
