The Schanuel predimension is the specific counting function $\delta(X) = \text{trd}(X \cup \text{ex}X) - \text{ldim}(X)$ that governs Hrushovski's construction of algebraically closed fields with pseudoexponentiation (ACFExp). It balances the transcendence degree of $X$ together with its exponential image against the $\mathbb{Q}$-linear dimension of $X$. The Hrushovski inequality $\delta(X) \geq 0$ then becomes equivalent to Schanuel's conjecture.
