Let the original class $H$ be the class of algebraically closed fields $F$ of characteristic $0$, and suppose we add a new function $\text{ex}$ to the field, satisfying the homomorphism condition
$$\text{ex}(x_1 + x_2) = \text{ex}(x_1) \cdot \text{ex}(x_2).$$
The free fusion principle uniquely determines the predimension $\delta$ as
$$\delta(X) = \text{trd}(X \cup \text{ex}X) - \text{ldim}(X), \quad \text{for any finite } X \subseteq F,$$
where $\text{ldim}(X)$ is the dimension of the $\mathbb{Q}$-vector space generated by $X$.
