This theorem, obtained via Hrushovski's amalgamation construction, axiomatizes algebraically closed fields with pseudoexponentiation (ACFExp). The axioms are equivalent to Schanuel's conjecture together with a regularity condition on zeros of exponential-polynomial systems. The theorem establishes that ACFExp is categorical in all uncountable powers and carries a homogeneous pregeometry, implying that Schanuel's conjecture is not only consistent but, together with the other axioms, forces the algebra of the structure to be uniquely well-behaved.
