$$\forall x \Bigl(\neg x = \varnothing \Rightarrow \exists y \bigl(\text{"$y$ is a function with domain of definition } x\text{"}$$
$$\wedge \forall u (u \in x \wedge \neg u = \varnothing \Rightarrow \exists w (w \in u \wedge \langle u, w \rangle \in y))\bigr)\Bigr).$$

That is, $y$ chooses one element from each nonempty element $u \in x$.
