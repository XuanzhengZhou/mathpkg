For an $L_1\text{Ar}$-sentence $P$ the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\mathbf{C} \vDash P$;
\item[(ii)] $F \vDash P$, for any algebraically closed field $F$ of characteristic $0$;
\item[(iii)] $\tilde{F}_p \vDash P$, for all but finitely many primes $p$.
\end{enumerate}

Here $\tilde{F}_p$ is the algebraic closure of the $p$-element field $F_p$.
