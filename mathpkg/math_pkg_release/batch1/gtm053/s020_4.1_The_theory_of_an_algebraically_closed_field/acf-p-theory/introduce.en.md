$\text{ACF}_p$ is the first-order theory of algebraically closed fields of a fixed positive characteristic $p$. It is formulated in the language $L_1\text{Ar}$ of arithmetic, which has binary operation symbols $+$ and $\cdot$ along with constant symbols $0$ and $1$. The theory is given by three groups of axioms: the standard field axioms (I), an axiom schema expressing that every non-constant polynomial has a root (II), and a single axiom fixing the characteristic to be $p$ (III). The theory $\text{ACF}_p$ is a central example in model theory because it is complete and admits quantifier elimination, as proved by Tarski.
