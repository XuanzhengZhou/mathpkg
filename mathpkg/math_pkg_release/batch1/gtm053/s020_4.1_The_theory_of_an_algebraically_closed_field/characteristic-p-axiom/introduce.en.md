The characteristic $p$ axiom is a single first-order sentence in the language $L_1\text{Ar}$ stating that the sum of the multiplicative identity $1$ with itself $p$ times equals the additive identity $0$. For $\text{ACF}_0$, this axiom is negated for every prime $p$, yielding the infinite axiom set $\neg(1 + \cdots + 1 = 0)$ for all primes $p$, which together assert that the field has characteristic zero.
