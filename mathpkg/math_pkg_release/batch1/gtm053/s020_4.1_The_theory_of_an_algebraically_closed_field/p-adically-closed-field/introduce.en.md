A $p$-adically closed field is a valued field that satisfies exactly the same first-order sentences as the field $\mathbf{Q}_p$ of $p$-adic numbers. The theory $TQ_p$ of $p$-adically closed fields is the model-theoretic analogue of $\text{RCF}$ and $\text{ACF}_p$ in the $p$-adic setting. For quantifier elimination purposes, one needs to extend the language with Macintyre's predicates $P_n$ that single out the $n$-th powers in the field. The theory in this extended language is complete, decidable, and admits quantifier elimination (Ax–Kochen, Ershov, Macintyre).
