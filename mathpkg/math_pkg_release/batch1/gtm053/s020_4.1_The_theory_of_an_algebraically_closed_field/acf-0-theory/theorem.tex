The theory $\text{ACF}_0$ of algebraically closed fields of characteristic zero is given by axioms I, II and negations of axioms III for all prime $p$:

$$\neg\left(\underbrace{1 + \cdots + 1}_{p \text{ times}} = 0\right).$$

Equivalently, $\text{ACF}_0$ consists of:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[I.] Axioms of fields.
\item[II.] The axioms of algebraic closedness: for each positive $n \in \mathbf{N}$,
$$\forall y_1, \ldots, y_n \; \exists x \; (x^n + y_1 x^{n-1} + \cdots + y_n = 0).$$
\end{enumerate}
together with, for every prime $p$, the axiom $\neg(\underbrace{1 + \cdots + 1}_{p} = 0)$.
