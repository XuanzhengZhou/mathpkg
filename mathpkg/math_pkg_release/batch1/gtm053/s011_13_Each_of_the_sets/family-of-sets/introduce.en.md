An indexed family of sets $\{X_Y \mid Y \in Z\}$ is formalized within the von Neumann universe as a function whose domain is the index set $Z$. This identification reduces the informal notion of "a collection of sets labeled by elements of $Z$" to a single set-theoretic object --- a function --- ensuring the family remains an element of $V$.
