Every ordinal number is an element of the von Neumann universe $V$. This ensures that the ordinals, which index the stages of the cumulative hierarchy, are themselves sets within that hierarchy. The statement is typically proved by transfinite induction, showing that each ordinal $\alpha$ belongs to $V_{\alpha+1}$.
