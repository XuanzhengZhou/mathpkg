The rank of a set $X$ in the von Neumann universe is the least ordinal $\alpha$ such that $X$ appears by stage $V_{\alpha+1}$. The rank function is strictly increasing along the membership relation: every element of $X$ has rank strictly less than the rank of $X$. This provides a well-founded measure of the complexity of sets within the cumulative hierarchy.
