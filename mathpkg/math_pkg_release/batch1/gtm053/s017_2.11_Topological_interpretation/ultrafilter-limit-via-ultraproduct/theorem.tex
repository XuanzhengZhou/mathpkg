Let $I \subseteq \mathcal{S}$ be a set of points in the Stone space and $U$ an ultrafilter on $I$. In the compact Hausdorff space $\mathcal{S}$, there exists a unique limit point along the ultrafilter, denoted $\lim_U I$. This limit point is given by the equivalence class represented by the ultraproduct
$$\prod_{i \in I} \mathbf{A}_i / U,$$
where $\mathbf{A}_i \in \mathbf{S}$ are representatives of the corresponding points $i \in I$.
