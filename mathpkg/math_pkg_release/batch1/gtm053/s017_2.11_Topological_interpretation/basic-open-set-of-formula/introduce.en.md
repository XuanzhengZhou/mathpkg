For an $L$-sentence $P$, the basic open set $[P]$ is the collection of all elementary equivalence classes of $L$-structures that satisfy $P$. These sets form an open basis for the Stone topology on the space $\mathcal{S}$ of $L$-structures. The correspondence $P \mapsto [P]$ translates logical properties into topological ones: finite satisfiability corresponds to the finite intersection property.
