An ultrapower $\mathbf{A}^I / U$ is a special case of the ultraproduct construction in which all factors are identical copies of the same structure $\mathbf{A}$, and the quotient is taken with respect to a nonprincipal ultrafilter $U$ on the index set $I$. Ultrapowers provide elementary extensions $\mathbf{A} \prec \mathbf{A}^I / U$ and are a fundamental tool in model theory for constructing models with saturation-like properties.
