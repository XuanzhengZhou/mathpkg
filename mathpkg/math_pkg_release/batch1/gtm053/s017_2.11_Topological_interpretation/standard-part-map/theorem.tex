The \textbf{standard part map} is the well-defined surjective ring homomorphism
$$\operatorname{st} : B \to \mathbb{R}, \quad \operatorname{st}(r + \alpha) = r,$$
where $B \subseteq {^*\mathbf{R}}$ is the ring of bounded elements, $\mu$ is the maximal ideal of infinitesimals, $r \in \mathbb{R}$, and $\alpha \in \mu$. When identified with the partial map ${^*\mathbf{R}} \dashrightarrow \mathbb{R}$, it is defined exactly on $B = \mathbb{R} \oplus \mu$.
