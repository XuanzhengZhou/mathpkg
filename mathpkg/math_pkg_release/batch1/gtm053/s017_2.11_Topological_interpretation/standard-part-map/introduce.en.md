The standard part map $\operatorname{st} : B \to \mathbb{R}$ is the well-defined surjective ring homomorphism sending each bounded element $r + \alpha$ (with $r \in \mathbb{R}$ standard and $\alpha$ infinitesimal) to its standard part $r$. It can be viewed as the quotient map $B \to B / \mu \cong \mathbb{R}$. This map is fundamental in nonstandard analysis, allowing one to extract standard limits from nonstandard arguments.
