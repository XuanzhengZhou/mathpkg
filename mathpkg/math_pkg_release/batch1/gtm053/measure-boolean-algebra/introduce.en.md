This measure algebra is the Boolean algebra of truth values used in the Scott-Solovay Boolean-valued version of Cohen forcing. The countable chain condition (ccc) is crucial: it ensures that cardinals and cofinalities are preserved when passing to the Boolean-valued model. The interpretation of formulas in this algebra defines "truth with probability one," yielding a model where the negation of CH holds.
