The Zermelo-Fraenkel axioms (without Replacement and Choice) are the foundational axioms for set theory expressed in the first-order language L1Set. When interpreted in the von Neumann universe V, each axiom is true. Together they provide the basic operations for constructing sets: the empty set, forming pairs, unions, power sets, and the well-foundedness of the membership relation.
