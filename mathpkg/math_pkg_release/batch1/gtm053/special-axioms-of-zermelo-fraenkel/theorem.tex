The following formulas are true in the standard interpretation of $\mathrm{L}_1\mathrm{Set}$ in the von Neumann universe $V$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Axiom of the empty set: $\forall x \neg (x \in \varnothing)$.
\item[(b)] Axiom of extensionality: $\forall z (z \in x \iff z \in y) \iff x = y$.
\item[(c)] Axiom of pairing: $\forall u \forall w \exists x \forall z (z \in x \iff z = u \vee z = w)$.
\item[(d)] Axiom of the union: $\forall x \exists y \forall u (\exists z (u \in z \land z \in x) \iff u \in y)$.
\item[(e)] Axiom of the power set: $\forall x \exists y \forall z (z \subset x \iff z \in y)$.
\item[(f)] Axiom of regularity: $\forall x (\neg x = \varnothing \Rightarrow \exists y (y \in x \land y \cap x = \varnothing))$.
\end{enumerate}
