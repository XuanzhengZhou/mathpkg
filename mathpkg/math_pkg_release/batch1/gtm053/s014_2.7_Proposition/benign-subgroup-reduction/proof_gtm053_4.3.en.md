The proof proceeds by constructing embeddings that exhibit the required intersection properties. One constructs groups $M_1, M_2$ and uses the amalgamated free product $K = M_1 *_G M_2$. Let $L = \phi'' \circ \phi_2(G)$, and embed $G$ in $K$ via $\phi_2'' \circ \phi_2'$. One verifies that $G \cap L = E \cap F$ as a subgroup of $G$ in $K$, using properties of amalgamated free products. The subgroups $\phi_1(|E \cup F|)$ and $\phi_2(G)$ have the same intersection with the amalgamation in $G *_E G$. Applying Proposition 2.3(d) yields the required intersection formulas, establishing that the subgroups are benign.
