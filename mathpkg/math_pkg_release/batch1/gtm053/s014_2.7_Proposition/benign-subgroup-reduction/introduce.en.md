This proposition reduces the proof of Higman's embedding theorem to verifying that all enumerable subgroups of the free group $\mathbb{Z} * \mathbb{Z}$ are benign. Combined with Lemma 4.2, it provides the final step connecting benign subgroups to embeddability in finitely presented groups.
