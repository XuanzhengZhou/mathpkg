This definition formalizes the notion of a category whose morphisms are themselves algorithms (programs) for computing morphisms in an underlying constructive universe. The category $\mathcal{A}$ is enriched over $\mathcal{C}$, meaning its hom-sets live in the constructive universe themselves. The functor $J$ interprets each algorithm as the function it computes.
