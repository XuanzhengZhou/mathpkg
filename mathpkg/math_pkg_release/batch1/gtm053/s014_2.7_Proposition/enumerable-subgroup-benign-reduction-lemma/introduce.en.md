This lemma provides the final reduction step for Higman's embedding theorem. It shows that if a specific subgroup $H'$ of an auxiliary group $G'$ is benign, then any enumerable subgroup of the free 2-generator group is benign. The proof constructs a chain of embeddings and intersections to transfer benignness.
