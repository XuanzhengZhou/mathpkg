A collection $\mathcal{P}$ in a category $\mathcal{B}$ is "the same as" a $\mathcal{B}$-valued functor $\mathcal{P}$ on the groupoid of oriented corollas. Specifically, $\mathcal{P}(n,m)$ can be identified with the value of $\mathcal{P}$ on a corolla with inputs $\{1, \ldots, m\}$ and outputs $\{1, \ldots, n\}$. The action of $\mathbf{S}_m \times \mathbf{S}_n^{\text{op}}$ is determined by values of $\mathcal{P}$ on the automorphisms of this corolla.
