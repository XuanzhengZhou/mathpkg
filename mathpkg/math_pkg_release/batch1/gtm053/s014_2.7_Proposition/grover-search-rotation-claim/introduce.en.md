This claim describes the geometric core of Grover's quantum search algorithm. The operator $T = VJVI_F$ acts as a rotation in a 2-dimensional plane spanned by the uniform superposition and the target state. Applying $T$ approximately $\pi\sqrt{N}/4$ times rotates the state from the uniform superposition to near the target $|x_0\rangle$, enabling quantum search in $O(\sqrt{N})$ steps.
