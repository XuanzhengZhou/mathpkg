Let $F : \mathbf{F}_2^n \rightarrow \{0,1\}$ take the value $1$ at exactly one point $x_0$, and define
$$I_F : |x\rangle \mapsto e^{\pi i F(x)} |x\rangle,$$
the reflection inverting the sign of $|x_0\rangle$. Let $J = -I_\delta$ where $\delta$ takes value $1$ only at $0$, and $V$ be the uniform superposition operator. Then:

(i) The real plane in $H_n$ spanned by the uniform superposition $\xi$ of all classical states and by $|x_0\rangle$ is invariant with respect to $T := VJVI_F$.

(ii) $T$ restricted to this plane is the rotation from $\xi$ to $|x_0\rangle$ by angle $\varphi_N$, where
$$\cos \varphi_N = 1 - \frac{2}{N}, \quad \sin \varphi_N = 2\frac{\sqrt{N-1}}{N}.$$
