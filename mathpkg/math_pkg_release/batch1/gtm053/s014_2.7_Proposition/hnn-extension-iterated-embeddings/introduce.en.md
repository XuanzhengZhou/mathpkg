This proposition describes the behavior of iterated HNN-extensions with respect to a subgroup $H \subset G$. Given a family of embeddings $\alpha_i, \beta_i : A_i \rightarrow G$, one constructs two multiple HNN-extensions: $K_G$ over $G$ and $K_H$ over $H$. The result shows that the stable letters $t_i$ freely generate a free subgroup, and that both $G$ and $K_H$ embed naturally into $K_G$, with the intersection $K_H \cap G$ recovering exactly $H$.
