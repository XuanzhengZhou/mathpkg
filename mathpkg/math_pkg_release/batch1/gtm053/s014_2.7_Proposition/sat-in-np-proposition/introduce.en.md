This proposition establishes that the Boolean satisfiability problem (SAT) belongs to the complexity class NP. The proof constructs a set $E'$ of pairs $(u,v)$ where $v$ satisfies the Boolean formula $b_u$, shows that $E' \in P$, and notes that SAT is the polynomially truncated projection of $E'$.
