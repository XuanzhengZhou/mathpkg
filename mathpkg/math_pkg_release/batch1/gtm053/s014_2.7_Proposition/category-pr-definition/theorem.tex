Consider the category $Pr$ whose set of objects is the set $\text{Prim}$ of decorated trees (flowcharts for primitive recursive functions), and morphisms are compositions of:
(i) Isomorphisms.
(ii) Contractions of subtrees all of whose vertices are decorated by $c$ (composition). After contraction, the resulting vertex is marked by $c$.
(iii) Contractions of subtrees all of whose vertices are decorated by $b$ (bracketing). After contraction, the resulting vertex is marked by $b$.

The localization of $Pr$ with respect to all morphisms, denoted $P$, has the natural structure of a category of programming methods for which composition and bracket operations become associative.
