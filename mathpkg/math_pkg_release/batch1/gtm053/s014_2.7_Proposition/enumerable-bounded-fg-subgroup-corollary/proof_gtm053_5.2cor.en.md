This follows directly from Proposition 5.2 combined with Lemma 4.5, which allows the transfer of benignness from a free group to a finitely generated subgroup of a finitely presented group. By Lemma 4.5, the bounded enumerable subset $R'$ can be lifted to a bounded enumerable subset in the free group covering $G'$, where Proposition 5.2 applies. The intersection properties of benign subgroups then descend to $G'$.
