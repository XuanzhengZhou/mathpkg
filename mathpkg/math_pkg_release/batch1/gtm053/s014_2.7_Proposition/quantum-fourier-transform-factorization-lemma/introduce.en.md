This lemma gives an explicit factorization of the quantum Fourier transform (QFT) on $n$ qubits in terms of elementary quantum gates. The QFT is a key subroutine in Shor's factoring algorithm and other quantum algorithms. The factorization shows that QFT can be implemented with $O(n^2)$ gates.
