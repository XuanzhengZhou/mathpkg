This proposition establishes that any countable (or finite) group can be embedded into a group generated by just two elements. Part (b) refines this result for recursive groups: if $G$ is a recursive group, the embedding can also be made recursive. The proof uses HNN-extensions and free products with amalgamation to algebraically encode the group structure into a 2-generator group.
