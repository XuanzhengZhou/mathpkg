This defines the reduced constructive universe $C_0$, which is a full monoidal subcategory of the full constructive universe $C$. By excluding finite objects of cardinality at least 2, $C_0$ simplifies to a category equivalent to Cartesian powers of $\mathbf{Z}^+$ with partial recursive functions as morphisms.
