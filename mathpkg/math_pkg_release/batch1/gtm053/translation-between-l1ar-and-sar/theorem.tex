Two translation mappings
$$\{ \text{formulas of } \mathrm{L}_1\mathrm{Ar} \} \rightleftharpoons \{ \text{formulas of SAr} \}$$
can be explicitly defined such that: (a) at every point $\xi$ the truth values of any formula and its translation coincide; (b) the sets of free variables of any formula and its translation coincide.
