Skolem functions provide a constructive method for building elementary submodels. By adding a choice function for each existential formula (a Skolem function), the closure of any subset under all Skolem functions is an elementary submodel. This is the standard proof of the downward Lowenheim-Skolem theorem and is a precursor to the notion of Skolemization in proof theory.
