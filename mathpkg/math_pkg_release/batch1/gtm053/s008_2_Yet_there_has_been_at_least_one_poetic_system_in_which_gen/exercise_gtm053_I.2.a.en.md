Find the simple kennings from which the last two kennings in Martynov's poem are deduced.

The last two kennings in Martynov's poem are:

1. "throwers of the fire of the storm of the witch of the moon of the steed of the ship stables"
2. "squanderers of the amber of the cold earth of the great boar"

Decompose each into its constituent simple kennings using the known equivalences (e.g., "storm of spears" = "battle", "tree of battle" = "warrior", "sea of the wagon" = "earth", "fire of war" = "gold", "sky of sand" = "sea", "field of seals" = "sea").
