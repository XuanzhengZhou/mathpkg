Two translation mappings

$$\{ \text{formulas of } \mathrm{L}_1\mathrm{Ar} \} \rightleftharpoons \{ \text{formulas of SAr} \}$$

can be explicitly defined with the following properties:

\textbf{(a)} At every point $\xi$ the truth values of any formula and its translation coincide.

\textbf{(b)} The sets of free variables of any formula and its translation coincide.

The mappings are not inverse to each other.
