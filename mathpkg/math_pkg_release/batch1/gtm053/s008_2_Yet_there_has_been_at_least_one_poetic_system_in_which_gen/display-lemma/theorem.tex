Let $P(x)$ be a formula in SAr with one free variable $x$. Let $P_E(x)$ denote the formula $P((x) \cdot ((\bar{1}0) \uparrow (x)))$ (i.e., the term "$x \cdot 10^x$" is substituted in place of all free occurrences of $x$). Then the set of expressions satisfying $P_E$ coincides with the set of expressions displayed in $P$.
