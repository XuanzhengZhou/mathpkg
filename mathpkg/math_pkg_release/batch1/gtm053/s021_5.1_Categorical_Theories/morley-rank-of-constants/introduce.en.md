This proposition states that the field of constants in a model of $\operatorname{DCF}_0$ has Morley rank exactly 1, identifying it as a strongly minimal set. More generally, the solution space of a differential equation of order $n$ has Morley rank $n$, establishing a direct correspondence between the order of a differential equation and the model-theoretic dimension of its solution set. This is a key result in the model theory of differential fields.
