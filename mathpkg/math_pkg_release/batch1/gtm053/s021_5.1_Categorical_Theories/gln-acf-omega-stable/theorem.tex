The group $\operatorname{GL}_n(F)$ (in the language of groups) is definable in the field $F \models \operatorname{ACF}_p$. Consequently, the theory of $\operatorname{GL}_n(F)$ is $\aleph_0$-stable and Morley rank is well defined in this theory. Every definable set $S$ in this theory is constructible and $\operatorname{rk} S = \dim S$.
