A difference field is a field equipped with a distinguished field automorphism $\sigma$. This structure is the basic object of study in difference algebra and the model theory of difference fields. The theory ACFA (algebraically closed fields with an automorphism) is the model companion of the theory of difference fields, analogous to how ACF is the model companion of the theory of fields.
