The real closed field $\mathbf{R}$ has a definable total order: $x < y$ iff $\exists z (z \neq 0 \land y = x + z^2)$. This order is definable on an infinite set (the whole field). By the general theorem that any theory with a definable order on an infinite subset is unstable (see order-implies-unstable), $\operatorname{RCF}$ cannot be $\kappa$-stable for any infinite $\kappa$. Therefore $\operatorname{RCF}$ is not stable.
