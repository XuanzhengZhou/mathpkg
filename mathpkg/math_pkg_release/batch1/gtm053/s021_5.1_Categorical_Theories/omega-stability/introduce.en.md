$\aleph_0$-stability is the strongest stability condition in Shelah's classification hierarchy. A theory is $\aleph_0$-stable if over any countable parameter set, there are at most countably many complete 1-types. This condition implies $\kappa$-stability for all infinite $\kappa$ and guarantees that Morley rank is well-defined, making it a cornerstone for the structural analysis of models in classification theory.
