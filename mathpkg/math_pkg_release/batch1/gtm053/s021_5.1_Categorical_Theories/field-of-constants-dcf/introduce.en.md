In a differential field, the field of constants is the subfield consisting of all elements annihilated by the derivation $D$. In the theory $\operatorname{DCF}_0$ of differentially closed fields of characteristic zero, the field of constants is a strongly minimal set of Morley rank 1, analogous to an algebraically closed field within the differential structure. It plays a fundamental role in the analysis of definable sets via orthogonality to the constants.
