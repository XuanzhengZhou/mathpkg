The alphabet of $L$ is said to be \text{sufficient} for a set of formulas $\mathcal{E}$ if, for each closed formula $\neg \forall x P(x)$ in $\mathcal{E}$, there exists a constant $c_P$ (depending on $P$) such that the formula
$$R_P: \neg \forall x P(x) \Rightarrow \neg P(c_P)$$
belongs to $\mathcal{E}$.
