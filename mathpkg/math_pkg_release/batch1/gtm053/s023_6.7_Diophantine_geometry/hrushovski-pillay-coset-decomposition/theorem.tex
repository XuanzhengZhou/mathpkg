Let $\Delta$ be a strongly minimal group definable in some expansion of a field $K$ (e.g., a differentially closed field or a difference field). Suppose that $\Delta$ satisfies condition (ii) of Section 6.4, i.e., $\Delta$ is locally projective. Then any definable subset of $\Delta^n$ is a finite union of cosets of definable subgroups of $\Delta^n$.
