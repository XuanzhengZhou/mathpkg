This theorem establishes that the Lang property for a subgroup $\Gamma$ of a commutative algebraic group is equivalent to one-basedness, a fundamental model-theoretic notion generalizing local projectivity. One-basedness means that the model-theoretic independence relation is governed by a modular pregeometry, and this theorem shows that the Diophantine-geometric Lang property is precisely the model-theoretic manifestation of this structural condition.
