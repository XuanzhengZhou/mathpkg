Let $K$ be a field of characteristic zero and let $A$ be a semiabelian variety over $K$. Then any finitely generated subgroup $\Gamma \subseteq A(K)$ has the Lang property: for every algebraic variety $V \subseteq A$, the intersection $\Gamma \cap V(K)$ is a union of finitely many cosets of subgroups of the form $\Gamma \cap B(K)$, where $B$ is an algebraic subgroup of $A$.
