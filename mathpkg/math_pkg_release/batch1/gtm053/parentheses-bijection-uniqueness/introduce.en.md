This lemma establishes that the pairing of left and right parentheses in any well-formed expression of a first-order language is uniquely determined. The constructive description using the running sum of $\varepsilon$ provides a simple algorithm for matching parentheses. This uniqueness is a prerequisite for the Unique Reading Lemma and for all inductive arguments about the syntactic structure of terms and formulas.
