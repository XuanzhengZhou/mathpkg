The construction proceeds via the well-ordering of triples (Lemma 1.7), defining an isomorphism $K$ from ordinals to "important triples," and then defining $N$ recursively: $N(0) = \varnothing$, and for $\gamma > 0$ with $K(\gamma) = \langle i, \alpha, \beta \rangle$, $N(\gamma) = F_i(N(\alpha), N(\beta))$ for $i = 1,2,3$, and $N(\gamma) = F_i(N(\alpha))$ for $i = 4,\ldots,8$. The absoluteness is verified in Sections 5.18-5.20 through detailed analysis of the defining formulas.
