Proposition 7.6 establishes that the set of numbers of true formulas is productive. The proof constructs an enumerable family $\{P_k(x_1)\}$ of formulas defining the versal family $\{E_k\}$, then applies the Self-Reference Lemma 7.3 to $\neg P_k$ to obtain a true but unprovable formula (if $E_k$ is a subset of the set of provable formulas).
