This theorem, proved by G\"odel (1938) and Cohen (1963), establishes that the Continuum Hypothesis is independent of the Zermelo-Fraenkel axioms with Choice (ZFC). G\"odel showed CH cannot be disproved by exhibiting the constructible universe L where CH holds. Cohen showed CH cannot be proved by the method of forcing.
