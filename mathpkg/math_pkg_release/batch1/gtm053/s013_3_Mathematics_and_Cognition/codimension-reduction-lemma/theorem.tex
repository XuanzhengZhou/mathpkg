For each $m \geqslant 1$ there exists a one-to-one mapping $t^{(m)}: \mathbb{Z}^+ \to (\mathbb{Z}^+)^m$ such that:
(a) The component functions $t_i^{(m)} = \operatorname{pr}_i^m \circ t^{(m)}$ are primitive recursive for all $1 \leqslant i \leqslant m$.
(b) The inverse function $\tau^{(m)}: (\mathbb{Z}^+)^m \to \mathbb{Z}^+$ is primitive recursive.
