Godel's theorem establishes that the Generalized Continuum Hypothesis is true in the constructible universe L. Since all ZF axioms also hold in L, this shows that GCH is consistent with ZF (and with ZFC, since AC also holds in L). Combined with Cohen's forcing result, this proves the independence of CH from ZFC.
