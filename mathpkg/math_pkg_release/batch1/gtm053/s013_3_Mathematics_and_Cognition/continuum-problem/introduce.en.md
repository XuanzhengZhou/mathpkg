The Continuum Problem asks: where does the cardinality of the continuum $2^{\omega_0}$ sit on the scale of alephs? By Cantor's theorem, $2^{\omega_0} > \omega_0$, so the continuum is at least $\omega_1$. The question of whether it equals $\omega_1$ or is larger is the subject of the Continuum Hypothesis.
