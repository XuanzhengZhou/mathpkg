For an $L$-structure $A$, let $L_A = L \cup \{c_a : a \in A\}$ be the expansion of the language by constant symbols naming each element of $A$, and let $A_A$ be the natural expansion of $A$ to $L_A$ assigning to $c_a$ the element $a$. The diagram of $A$ is

$$\text{Diag}(A) = \{S : \text{atomic or negation of atomic } L_A\text{-sentence such that } A_A \models S\}.$$
