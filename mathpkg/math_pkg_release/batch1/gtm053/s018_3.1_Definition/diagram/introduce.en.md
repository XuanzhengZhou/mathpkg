The diagram of an $L$-structure $A$, denoted $\text{Diag}(A)$, is the set of all atomic and negated atomic sentences true in the expansion $A_A$ of $A$ to the language $L_A$, which adds a constant symbol $c_a$ for each element $a \in A$. The diagram encodes the "multiplication table" of the structure — it records all basic relations holding among the named elements, and an expansion of $B$ satisfies $\text{Diag}(A)$ precisely when the map $a \mapsto c_a$ is an embedding of $A$ into $B$.
