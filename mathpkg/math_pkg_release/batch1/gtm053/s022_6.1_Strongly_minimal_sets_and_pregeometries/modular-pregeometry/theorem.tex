A pregeometry $(M, \operatorname{cl})$ satisfies the property called \emph{modularity} if for all $U \subseteq M$ and $v, w \in M$,
$$w \in \operatorname{cl}(U, v) \iff \exists u \in \operatorname{cl}(U) : w \in \operatorname{cl}(u, v).$$
A pregeometry satisfying this condition is called \emph{modular}.
