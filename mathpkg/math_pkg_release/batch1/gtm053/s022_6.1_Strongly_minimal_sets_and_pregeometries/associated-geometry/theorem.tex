Given a pregeometry $(M, \operatorname{cl})$, one associates the geometry $(\bar{M}, \bar{\operatorname{cl}})$ with it by setting
$$\bar{M} := \bigl( M \setminus \operatorname{cl}(\varnothing) \bigr) / \sim,$$
where $u \sim v \iff \operatorname{cl}(\{u\}) = \operatorname{cl}(\{v\})$, and $\bar{\operatorname{cl}}$ is the closure operator induced by $\operatorname{cl}$ on the quotient.
