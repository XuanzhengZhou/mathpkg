\textbf{Zilber Trichotomy Conjecture.} The pregeometries arising from strongly minimal structures fall into exactly three types:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] Trivial (disintegrated) geometry;
\item[(2)] Projective geometry over a division ring (locally modular, non-trivial);
\item[(3)] Geometry of an algebraically closed field (non-locally-modular).
\end{enumerate}

In other words, any strongly minimal structure is either trivial, bi-interpretable with a vector space over a division ring, or bi-interpretable with an algebraically closed field.
