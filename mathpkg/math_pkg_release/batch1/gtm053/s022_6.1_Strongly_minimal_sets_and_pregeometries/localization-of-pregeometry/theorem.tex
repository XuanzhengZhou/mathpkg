Given a pregeometry $(M, \operatorname{cl})$ and a fixed element $a \in M$, the \emph{localization} of $(M, \operatorname{cl})$ at $a$ is the pregeometry $(M, \operatorname{cl}_a)$ defined by
$$\operatorname{cl}_a(X) := \operatorname{cl}(X, a) \qquad \text{for any } X \subseteq M.$$
The model-theoretic meaning of localization is precisely the extension of the language by a constant symbol for $a$.
