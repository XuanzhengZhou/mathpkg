Let $\langle M, P \rangle$ be a setting for forcing. Suppose $M$ is a model of $\mathsf{AC}$ that satisfies the countable chain condition on $P$, i.e.,

$$(\forall S \subseteq P)\left[(\forall p_1, p_2 \in S)[p_1 \neq p_2 \rightarrow \neg \operatorname{Comp}(p_1, p_2)] \rightarrow \overline{S} \leq \aleph_0\right].$$

If $G$ is $P$-generic over $M$, then the cardinals in $M$ and $M[G]$ respectively are the same: every cardinal in $M$ is a cardinal in $M[G]$ and vice versa.
