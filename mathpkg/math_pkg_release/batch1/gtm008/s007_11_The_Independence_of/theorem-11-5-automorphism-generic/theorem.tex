Let $\langle M, P \rangle$ be a setting for forcing and let $\pi \in M$ be an automorphism of $P$ ($\pi \in \operatorname{Aut}(P)$). Let $G$ be $P$-generic over $M$, and let $F$ and $h$ be the $M$-complete ultrafilter for the Boolean algebra $B$ of regular open sets of $P$ in $M$ and the $M$-complete homomorphism of $B$ into $2$, respectively, obtained from $G$.

Then $\pi''G$ is $P$-generic over $M$, $\tilde{\pi}''F$ is the $M$-complete ultrafilter for $B$, and $h \circ \tilde{\pi}^{-1}$ is the $M$-complete homomorphism of $B$ into $2$ corresponding to $\pi''G$. Furthermore,

$$M[G] = M[F] = M[h] = M[\pi''G] = M[\tilde{\pi}''F] = M[h \circ \tilde{\pi}^{-1}].$$
