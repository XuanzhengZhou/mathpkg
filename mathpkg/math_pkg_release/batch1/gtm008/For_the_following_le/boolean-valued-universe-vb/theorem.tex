Let $C_{\alpha} = B \cap R(\alpha)$ for $\alpha \in On$. Then:
1. $V_{0}^{(B)} \triangleq 0$.
2. $V_{\alpha}^{(B)} \triangleq \{u \in {}^{C_{\alpha}}\mathcal{D}(u) \mid (\exists \xi < \alpha)[\mathcal{D}(u) \subseteq V_{\xi}^{(B)}]\}$.
3. $V^{(B)} \triangleq \bigcup_{\alpha \in On} V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$.
