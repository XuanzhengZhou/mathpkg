Definition 23.1 extends the Boolean-valued universe construction to the case where $B$ is a proper class rather than a set. The key modification is that at each level $\alpha$, we restrict to those functions whose values lie in $C_{\alpha} = B \cap R(\alpha)$, ensuring each $V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$ remains a set.
