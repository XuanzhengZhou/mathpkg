Definition 23.2 constructs $V^{(\tilde{B})}$ where $\tilde{B}$ is a complete Boolean algebra (possibly a proper class). Unlike $V^{(B)}$, the functions in $V^{(\tilde{B})}$ may take arbitrary values in $\tilde{B}$, making this universe potentially larger. This requires $\tilde{B}$ to be complete to ensure Boolean sums over classes exist.
