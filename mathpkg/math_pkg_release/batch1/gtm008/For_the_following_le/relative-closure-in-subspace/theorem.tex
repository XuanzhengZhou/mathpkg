Let $X$ be a topological space and let $S \subseteq X$ be a subspace with the relative topology. Denote by $^{-}$, $^{0}$ the closure and interior in $X$, and by $^{-S}$, $^{0S}$ those in $S$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item $A \subseteq S \rightarrow A^{-S} = A^{-} \cap S$.
\item $A \subseteq S \rightarrow A^{0S} = S - (S - A)^{-}$.
\end{enumerate}
