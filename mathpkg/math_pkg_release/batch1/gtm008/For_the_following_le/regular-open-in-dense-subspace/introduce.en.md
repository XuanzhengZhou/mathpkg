If $S$ is a dense subspace of $X$ and $A$ is regular open in $S$, then $A$ can be reconstructed as the intersection of $S$ with the regular open hull $A^{-0}$ of $A$ computed in the ambient space $X$. This result is a key step in establishing that the Boolean algebras of regular open sets in $X$ and in a dense subspace $S$ are isomorphic.
