This theorem complements Theorem 22.2 by showing that the operation of taking regular open sets in $X$ and intersecting with a dense subspace $S$ always yields regular open sets in $S$. Together, Theorems 22.2 and 22.4 establish a bijective correspondence between the complete Boolean algebras of regular open sets in $X$ and in a dense subspace $S$, which is the foundation for the isomorphism result in Theorem 22.5.
