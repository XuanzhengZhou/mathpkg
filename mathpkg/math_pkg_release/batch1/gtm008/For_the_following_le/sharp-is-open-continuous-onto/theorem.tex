Let $B_0, B_1$ be complete Boolean algebras and $i: B_0 \rightarrow B_1$ a complete monomorphism. Equip $P_j = B_j - \{0\}$ with the topology having basic open sets $[b_j] = \{p \in P_j \mid p \leq b_j\}$. Then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $(\#^{-1})``[b_0] = [i(b_0)]$ for all $b_0 \in B_0$.
\item $\#$ is an open continuous map from $P_1$ onto $P_0$.
\item The complete monomorphism from $B_0$ into $B_1$ associated with $\#$ is $i$.
\end{enumerate}
