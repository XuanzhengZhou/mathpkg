Let $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ and $b \in B$. Define $b \cdot u$ by

$$\mathcal{D}(b \cdot u) = \mathcal{D}(u) \land (\forall x \in \mathcal{D}(u))[(b \cdot u)(x) = b \cdot u(x)].$$

Then for all $v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$:

$$\llbracket v \in b \cdot u \rrbracket = b \cdot \llbracket v \in u \rrbracket$$

and

$$\llbracket b \cdot u = b \cdot v \rrbracket = -b + \llbracket u = v \rrbracket.$$
