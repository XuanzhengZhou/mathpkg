Let $u, v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ be definite and uniform. If $f \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ and $\llbracket f: u \rightarrow v \rrbracket = 1$, then there exists a (real) function $\varphi: \mathcal{D}(u) \rightarrow \mathcal{D}(v)$ such that $\varphi$ is extensional and

$$(\forall x \in \mathcal{D}(u))[\llbracket f(x) = \varphi(x) \rrbracket = 1].$$
