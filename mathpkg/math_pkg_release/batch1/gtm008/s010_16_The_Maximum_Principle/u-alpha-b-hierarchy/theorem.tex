$$U_0^{(\mathbf{B})} \triangleq 0$$
$$U_{\alpha+1}^{(\mathbf{B})} \triangleq B^{U_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})}}$$
$$U_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})} \triangleq \bigcup_{\beta < \alpha} U_{\beta}^{(\mathbf{B})}, \quad \alpha \in K_{\mathrm{II}}$$
$$U^{(\mathbf{B})} \triangleq \bigcup_{\alpha \in \mathrm{On}} U_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})}.$$

Here $B$ is the complete Boolean algebra and $B^{X}$ denotes the set of all functions from $X$ into $B$. $K_{\mathrm{II}}$ denotes the class of limit ordinals and $\mathrm{On}$ the class of all ordinals.
