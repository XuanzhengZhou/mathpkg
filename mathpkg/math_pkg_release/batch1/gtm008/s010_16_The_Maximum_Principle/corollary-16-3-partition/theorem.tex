Suppose $\{u_\xi \mid \xi < \alpha\} \subseteq V^{(\mathbf{B})}$, $\{b_\xi \mid \xi < \alpha\} \subseteq B$ is a partition of unity, and $d \subseteq V^{(\mathbf{B})}$. Then there exists $v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ with $\mathcal{D}(v) = d$ such that $b_\xi \cdot v(x) = b_\xi \cdot u_\xi(x)$ for all $\xi < \alpha$ and $x \in d$.
