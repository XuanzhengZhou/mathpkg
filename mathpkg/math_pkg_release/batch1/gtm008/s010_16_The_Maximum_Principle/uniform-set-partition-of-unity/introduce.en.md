Theorem 16.14 establishes a linearity property for uniform (extensional with complete domain) Boolean-valued sets. Given a partition of unity $\{b_i\}$ in $B$ and elements $\{x_i\}$ from the domain of a uniform set $u$, the membership value of the mixed element $\sum b_i x_i$ equals the corresponding Boolean-linear combination $\sum b_i u(x_i)$ of the individual membership values. This property is essential for the subsequent decomposition theorems (Theorems 16.19-16.21) that analyze the structure of uniform sets in terms of definite sets and Boolean scalars.
