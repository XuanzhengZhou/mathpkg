Let $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ be extensional and $b \in B$. Prove:

1. $\llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = \llbracket \sup(u) \Rightarrow b \rrbracket$.

As consequences, prove the following:

2. $\llbracket b \geq \sup(u) \rrbracket \rightarrow \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = 1$,
3. $\llbracket b < \sup(u) \rrbracket \rightarrow \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket < 1$,
4. $\llbracket u = 0 \rrbracket = \neg \sup(u)$,
5. $\llbracket b \cdot u \subseteq u \rrbracket = 1$.
