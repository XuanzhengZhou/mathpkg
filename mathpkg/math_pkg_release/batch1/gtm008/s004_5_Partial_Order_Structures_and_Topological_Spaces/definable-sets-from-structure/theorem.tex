If $\mathbf{A}$ is a set (i.e., its domain $A$ is a set), then
$$Df(\mathbf{A}) \triangleq \{a \mid \exists \ulcorner \varphi(x) \urcorner \in Fml^1(\mathbf{A}) \; [a = \{x \in A \mid \mathbf{A} \models \varphi(x)\}]\}.$$
Here $Fml^1(\mathbf{A})$ denotes the Godel numbers of formulas of $\mathcal{L}(C(A))$ having at most one free variable.
