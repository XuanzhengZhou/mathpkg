Let $P = \prod_{i \in I} P_i$ be the product partial order structure, and let $\mathcal{F}$ and $\mathcal{F}_i$ be the $T_2$-spaces corresponding to $P$ and $P_i$ respectively in accordance with Definition 5.6. Then $\mathcal{F}$ is homeomorphic to the product space $\prod_{i \in I} \mathcal{F}_i$.
