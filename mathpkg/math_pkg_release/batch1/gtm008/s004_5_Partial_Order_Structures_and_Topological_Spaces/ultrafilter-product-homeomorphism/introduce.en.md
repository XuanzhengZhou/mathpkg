Theorem 5.20 establishes that the construction of the ultrafilter space respects products: the space $\mathcal{F}$ of ultrafilters on the product partial order $P = \prod P_i$ is homeomorphic to the topological product $\prod \mathcal{F}_i$ of the individual ultrafilter spaces. The proof constructs the homeomorphism via projection maps: for $F \in \mathcal{F}$, the $i$-th coordinate ultrafilter is $F_i = \{a_i \in P_i : \hat{a}_i \in F\}$ where $\hat{a}_i$ has $a_i$ in the $i$-th coordinate and $1_j$ elsewhere.
