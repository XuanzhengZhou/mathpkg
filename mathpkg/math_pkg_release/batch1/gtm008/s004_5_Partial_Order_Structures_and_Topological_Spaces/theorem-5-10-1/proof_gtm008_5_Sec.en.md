1. $a \in G_1 \rightarrow [a] \subseteq G_1$
   $\rightarrow N(a) \subseteq G_1^*$
   $\rightarrow [a] \subseteq G_1^* \Delta$ (since $G_1^*$ is an open subset of $F$)
   $\rightarrow a \in G_1^* \Delta$.

2. $F \in G_2 \rightarrow (\exists a \in F)[N(a) \subseteq G_2]$
   $\rightarrow (\exists a \in F)[[a] \subseteq G_2^*]$
   $\rightarrow (\exists a \in F)[N(a) \subseteq G_2^*]$ (since $G_2^*$ is an open subset of $P$)
   $\rightarrow F \in G_2^*$.
