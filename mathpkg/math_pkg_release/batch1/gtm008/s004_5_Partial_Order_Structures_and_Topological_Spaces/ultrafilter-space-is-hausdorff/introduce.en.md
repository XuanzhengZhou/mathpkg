Theorem 5.8 strengthens Theorem 5.7: when the partial order structure $P$ is the one associated with a Boolean algebra $B$, the ultrafilter space $\langle \mathcal{F}, \mathcal{T} \rangle$ is not merely $T_1$ but actually Hausdorff ($T_2$). This means distinct ultrafilters on $P$ have disjoint neighborhoods, a property that depends on the additional algebraic structure provided by the Boolean algebra.
