Theorem 5.5 establishes the equivalence of the filter notion for a Boolean algebra $B$ and its associated partial order structure $P = B \setminus \{0\}$. A proper filter in $B$ (containing $1$, closed under meets, upward closed, excluding $0$) corresponds exactly to a filter in $P$. This correspondence is fundamental for translating algebraic filter properties to order-theoretic filter properties.
