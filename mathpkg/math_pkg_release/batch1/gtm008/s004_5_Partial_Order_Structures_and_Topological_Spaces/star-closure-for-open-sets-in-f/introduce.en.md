Theorem 5.14 states that for any open subset $G$ of the ultrafilter space $\mathcal{F}$, the double application of the inverse star operations $G^{\Delta *}$ recovers exactly $G$. This result, together with Theorem 5.13, shows that the star operations establish a bijection between certain open sets of $P$ and $\mathcal{F}$.
