\begin{align*}
F \in G^{\Delta *} &\rightarrow (\exists a \in F)[[a] \subseteq G^{\Delta}] \\
&\rightarrow (\exists a \in F)[N(a) \subseteq G] \\
&\rightarrow F \in G.
\end{align*}
Therefore $G^{\Delta *} \subseteq G$. By Theorem 5.10, $G \subseteq G^{\Delta *}$. Hence $G^{\Delta *} = G$.
