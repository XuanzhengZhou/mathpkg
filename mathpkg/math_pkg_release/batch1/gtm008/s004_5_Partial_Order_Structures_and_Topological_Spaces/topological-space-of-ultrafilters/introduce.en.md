For a partial order structure $P$, the topological space of ultrafilters $\langle \mathcal{F}, \mathcal{T} \rangle$ is defined by taking $\mathcal{F}$ as the set of all ultrafilters on $P$, with basic open sets $N(p) = \{F \in \mathcal{F} : p \in F\}$ for $p \in P$. This construction is analogous to the Stone space of a Boolean algebra.
