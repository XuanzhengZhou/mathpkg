The star operations $*$ and $\Delta$ define a Galois connection between the open subsets of a partial order structure $P$ and the open subsets of its associated ultrafilter space $\mathcal{F}$. For an open $G_1 \subseteq P$, $G_1^*$ is the union of all basic open sets $N(p)$ whose corresponding lower set $[p]$ is contained in $G_1$. Conversely, $G_2^\Delta$ maps open subsets of $\mathcal{F}$ back to open subsets of $P$. The composed operations are inflationary and monotone.
