Example. If $B$ is the complete Boolean algebra of all regular open sets of the product space $2^\omega$ then $B$ does not satisfy the $(\omega, 2)$-DL: If

$$b_{i0} \triangleq \{f \in 2^\omega \mid f(i) = 0\}, \quad b_{i1} \triangleq \{f \in 2^\omega \mid f(i) = 1\}$$

then $b_{i0}$, and $b_{i1}$ are each clopen and hence regular open. Then

$$\prod_{i \in \omega} (b_{i0} + b_{i1}) = 1.$$
