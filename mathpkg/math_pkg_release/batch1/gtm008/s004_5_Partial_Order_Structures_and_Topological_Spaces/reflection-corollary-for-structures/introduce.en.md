Corollary 7.20 states that for any formula $\varphi$, there are arbitrarily large ordinals $\beta$ such that $\varphi$ is absolute between $\mathbf{M}_\beta$ and the full structure $\mathbf{M}$ for all parameters from $M_\beta$. This is obtained by combining the semi-normal functions for the subformulas of $\varphi$ with the fixed-point theorem (7.15), yielding a common closure ordinal where all subformulas reflect simultaneously. From this, one proves that $\mathbf{M}$ satisfies the axioms of ZF.
