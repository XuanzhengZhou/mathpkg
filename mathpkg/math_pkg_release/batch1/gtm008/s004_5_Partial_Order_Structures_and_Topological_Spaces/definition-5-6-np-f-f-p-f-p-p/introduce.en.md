Definition 5.6: N(p) \triangleq \{F \in F \mid p \in F\}, p \in P from Partial Order Structures and Topological Spaces of Takeuti and Zaring's "Axiomatic Set Theory" (GTM 8). This definition is part of the foundational development of Boolean-valued models and forcing techniques in set theory.
