Let $M$ be a standard transitive model of $ZF$ and let $\varphi(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ be a formula of $\mathcal{L}_0$ containing no free variables other than $x_1, \ldots, x_n$. Then $\varphi$ is \textit{absolute} with respect to $M$ iff
$$(\forall x_1, \ldots, x_n \in M)[\varphi(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \leftrightarrow \varphi^M(x_1, \ldots, x_n)],$$
where $\varphi^M$ is the relativization of $\varphi$ to $M$.
