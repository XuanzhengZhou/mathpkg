Theorem 5.10 establishes that the two star operations $*$ and $\Delta$ are inflationary when composed: every open set $G_1 \subseteq P$ is contained in its double image $G_1^{*\Delta}$, and every open set $G_2 \subseteq \mathcal{F}$ is contained in $G_2^{\Delta *}$. This is a key property showing that $*$ and $\Delta$ form an isotone Galois connection.
