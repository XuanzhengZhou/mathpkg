Theorem 5.11 states that both star operations $*$ and $\Delta$ are monotone with respect to set inclusion: if one open set is contained in another, their images under the star operation preserve this inclusion. This monotonicity is an essential property of the Galois connection between the open set lattices of $P$ and $\mathcal{F}$.
