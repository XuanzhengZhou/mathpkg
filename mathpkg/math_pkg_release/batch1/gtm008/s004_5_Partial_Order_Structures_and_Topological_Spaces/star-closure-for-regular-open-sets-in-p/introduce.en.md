Theorem 5.13 shows that for regular open subsets $G$ of the partial order space $P$, the double application of the star operations $G^{*\Delta}$ yields exactly $G$ again. A regular open set is one that equals the interior of its closure. This result implies that the regular open algebras of $P$ and $\mathcal{F}$ are isomorphic, establishing a deep connection between the order structure and its dual space.
