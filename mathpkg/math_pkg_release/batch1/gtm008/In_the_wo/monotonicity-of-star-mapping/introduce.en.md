The star mapping is monotone with respect to set inclusion in both directions (from $P$ to $\mathcal{F}$ and from $\mathcal{F}$ to $P$). This monotonicity is a standard property of Galois connections and ensures that the star operation respects the order structure of the lattices of open sets, which is essential for establishing the isomorphism between the Boolean algebras of regular open sets.
