This is the general theorem establishing that the cumulative hierarchy of definable powersets, starting from any transitive base, yields a model of ZF. The verification checks each axiom individually: Extensionality, Foundation, and Infinity are relatively straightforward; Pairing, Union, Power Set, and Separation rely on the definability construction; Replacement uses the Reflection Principle.
