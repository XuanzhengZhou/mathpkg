Let $\langle \mathbf{M}_\alpha \mid \alpha \in \mathrm{On} \rangle$ be a sequence of transitive structures satisfying the cumulative conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\alpha < \beta \to \mathbf{M}_\alpha$ is a substructure of $\mathbf{M}_\beta$.
\item $M_\alpha = \bigcup_{\beta < \alpha} M_\beta$ for limit $\alpha$.
\item $M_{\alpha+1}$ is obtained from $M_\alpha$ by a suitable operation (such as $\operatorname{Df}$).
\end{enumerate}
Then $\mathbf{M} = \bigcup_{\alpha \in \mathrm{On}} \mathbf{M}_\alpha$ is a standard transitive model of $ZF$ containing all ordinals.
