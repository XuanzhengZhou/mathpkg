The union of a cumulative hierarchy of transitive structures, built by iterating the definable power set operation (or a suitable variant), is a standard transitive model of ZF containing all ordinals. The proof verifies each axiom of ZF using the reflection principle and the subset absorption theorem. This general method yields not only Gödel's $L$ but also the relative constructible models $L_K$, $L_a$, and $L[a]$, providing a unified framework for relative constructibility.
