For open subsets of the ultrafilter space $\mathcal{F}$, the composition $\Delta*$ is the identity map. This means every open set in the ultrafilter space is "$\Delta*$-closed." Together with the dual result for open subsets of $P$, this establishes a bijection between the fixed points of the two closure operations, which correspond exactly to the regular open sets studied in Boolean-valued model theory.
