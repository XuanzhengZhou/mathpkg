**Proof.** 
$$F \in G^{\Delta*} \rightarrow (\exists a \in F)[[a] \subseteq G^\Delta]$$
$$\rightarrow (\exists a \in F)[N(a) \subseteq G]$$
$$\rightarrow F \in G.$$

Therefore $G^{\Delta*} \subseteq G$. By Theorem 5.10(2), $G \subseteq G^{\Delta*}$. Hence $G^{\Delta*} = G$. $\square$
