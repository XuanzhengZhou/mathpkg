A $B$-valued structure $A = \langle A, \equiv, \bar{R}_0, \ldots, \bar{c}_0, \ldots \rangle$ is \text{complete} iff whenever $\langle b_i \mid i \in I \rangle$ is a partition of unity in $B$ and $\langle a_i \mid i \in I \rangle$ is any family of elements of $A$, then
$$(\exists a \in A)(\forall i \in I)[b_i \leq [\![a = a_i]\!]].$$
