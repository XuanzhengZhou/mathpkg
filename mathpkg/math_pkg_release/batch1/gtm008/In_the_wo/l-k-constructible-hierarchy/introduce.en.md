$L_K$ is the constructible universe relativized to a class predicate $K$. The construction proceeds by transfinite recursion: starting from the empty set, at successor stages one takes all subsets definable in the current structure (with $K$ as an additional predicate), and at limit stages one takes the union. When $K$ is the empty class, this yields Gödel's original $L$; when $K = \{a\}$ is a singleton, it yields $L_a$; and other choices produce other relative constructibility models.
