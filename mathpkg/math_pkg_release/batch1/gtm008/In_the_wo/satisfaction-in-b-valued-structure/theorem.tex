For a $B$-valued structure $\mathbf{A} = \langle A, \|{\cdot}\| \rangle$ and a formula $\varphi(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$,

$$\mathbf{A} \models \varphi[a_1, \ldots, a_n] \xrightarrow{\Delta} \|\varphi(a_1, \ldots, a_n)\| = 1.$$

The satisfaction relation satisfies the usual properties: $\mathbf{A} \models \neg \varphi$ iff it is not the case that $\mathbf{A} \models \varphi$, and $\mathbf{A} \models \varphi \rightarrow \psi$ iff $\mathbf{A} \models \varphi$ implies $\mathbf{A} \models \psi$.
