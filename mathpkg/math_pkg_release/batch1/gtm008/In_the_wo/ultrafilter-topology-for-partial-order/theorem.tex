Let $P = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ be a partial order structure and let $\mathcal{F}$ be the set of all ultrafilters for $P$. Then

$$N(p) \triangleq \{F \in \mathcal{F} \mid p \in F\}, \quad p \in P$$

$$T \triangleq \{G \subseteq \mathcal{F} \mid (\forall F \in G)(\exists p \in P)[F \in N(p) \subseteq G]\}.$$

The pair $\langle \mathcal{F}, T \rangle$ is the topological space of ultrafilters for $P$.
