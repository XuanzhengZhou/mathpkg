$L_K$ is a standard transitive model of ZF that contains all ordinals. The proof follows the general method established earlier: the sequence $\langle \mathbf{A}_\alpha \rangle$ satisfies the cumulative conditions, so the union $L_K = \bigcup_\alpha A_\alpha$ is a model of ZF. This provides a unified framework for relative constructibility, encompassing $L_a$ (where $K$ codes the set $a$) and $L[a]$ as special cases.
