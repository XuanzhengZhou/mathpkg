This theorem establishes that the ultrafilter space construction respects products: the space of ultrafilters of a product partial order is homeomorphic to the topological product of the individual ultrafilter spaces. The proof proceeds by mapping each ultrafilter $F$ on the product to the family of its projections $F_i = \{a_i \in P_i \mid \hat{a}_i \in F\}$, where $\hat{a}_i$ equals $a_i$ in the $i$-th coordinate and $1_j$ elsewhere. Each $F_i$ is an ultrafilter on $\mathcal{P}_i$, and this correspondence yields a homeomorphism.
