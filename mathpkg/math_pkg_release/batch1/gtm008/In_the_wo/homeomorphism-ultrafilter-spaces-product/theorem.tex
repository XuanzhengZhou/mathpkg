Let $\mathcal{P} = \prod_{i \in I} \mathcal{P}_i$ be the product partial order structure and let $\mathcal{F}$ and $\mathcal{F}_i$ be the $T_2$-spaces corresponding to $\mathcal{P}$ and $\mathcal{P}_i$ respectively (as in Definition 5.6). Then $\mathcal{F}$ is homeomorphic to the product space $\prod_{i \in I} \mathcal{F}_i$.
