The definable power set operation $\operatorname{Def}(x)$ is absolute between the universe and any standard transitive model of ZF. This means the constructible hierarchy $L_\alpha = \operatorname{Def}(L_{<\alpha})$ is correctly computed within any transitive model containing the relevant ordinals. This absoluteness is the fundamental reason why $L$ is the minimal inner model of ZF and why constructibility is an absolute notion.
