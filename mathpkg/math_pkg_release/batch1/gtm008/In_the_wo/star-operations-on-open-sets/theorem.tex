Let $P = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ be a partial order structure and $\mathcal{F}$ its space of ultrafilters. For subsets $A \subseteq P$ and $B \subseteq \mathcal{F}$, define
$$[p] = \{q \in P \mid q \leq p\}, \quad N(p) = \{F \in \mathcal{F} \mid p \in F\}.$$

Let $G_1$ be an open subset of $P$ and $G_2$ be an open subset of $\mathcal{F}$. Then:
$$G_1^* \triangleq \bigcup \{N(p) \mid [p] \subseteq G_1\}$$
$$G_2^\Delta \triangleq \bigcup \{[p] \mid N(p) \subseteq G_2\}.$$

Clearly $G_1^*$ and $G_2^\Delta$ are open subsets of $\mathcal{F}$ and $P$ respectively.
