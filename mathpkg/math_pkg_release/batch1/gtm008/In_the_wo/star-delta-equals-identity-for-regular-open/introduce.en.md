For regular open sets, the compositions $*\Delta$ and $\Delta*$ act as the identity. This means the star operations, when restricted to regular open sets, provide an order-isomorphism between the regular open algebras of $P$ and $\mathcal{F}$. This is the core result establishing that the Boolean algebra associated with a partial order structure is isomorphic to the Boolean algebra of regular open sets of its ultrafilter space.
