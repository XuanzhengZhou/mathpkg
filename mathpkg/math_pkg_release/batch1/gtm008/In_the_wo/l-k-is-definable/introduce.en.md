The class $L_K$ is definable in the language of set theory augmented with a predicate for $K$. This means there is a single formula $\varphi(x, K)$ such that $L_K = \{x \mid \varphi(x, K)\}$. Definability of the constructible hierarchy is a fundamental property that allows one to work with $L_K$ internally in ZF (augmented with $K$ as a predicate) and to prove that various statements hold in $L_K$ by showing they are satisfied at each stage of the construction.
