This result follows from the Axiom Schema of Replacement: for each $x \in a$, let $\mu_\beta$ be the least ordinal such that $x \in M_\beta$. The set of such ordinals for all $x \in a$ is bounded (by Replacement), so $a$ is contained in some $M_\alpha$. This is a key technical lemma establishing that the hierarchy $M = \bigcup_\alpha M_\alpha$ satisfies the separation and power set axioms of ZF.
