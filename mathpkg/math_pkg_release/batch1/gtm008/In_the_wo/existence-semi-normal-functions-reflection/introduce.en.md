Given any finite list of formulas, one can construct a single semi-normal function whose fixed points simultaneously reflect all the formulas. The fixed points $F(\beta) = \beta$ are exactly the ordinals where the truth of each $\varphi_i$ in $M$ coincides with its truth in $M_\beta$. This uniformity is essential for the systematic treatment of definability and truth in the constructible hierarchy.
