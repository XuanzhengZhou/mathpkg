Let $K$ be a class. Define the hierarchy $L_\alpha(K)$ by recursion on ordinals:

$$L_0(K) \triangleq \text{trcl}(K) \cap V_\omega$$
$$L_{\alpha+1}(K) \triangleq \operatorname{Def}(L_\alpha(K))$$
$$L_\alpha(K) \triangleq \bigcup_{\beta < \alpha} L_\beta(K), \quad \alpha \in K_{\mathrm{II}}$$

Then $L(K) \triangleq \bigcup_{\alpha \in \mathrm{On}} L_\alpha(K)$.
