This is the dual statement for the ultrafilter space side of the Galois connection. For regular open subsets of $\mathcal{F}$, the composition $\Delta*$ (first take the regular open closure in $\mathcal{F}$, then apply the star map to $P$) returns the original set when composed with $*$. Together with the $P$-side version, this completes the proof of the isomorphism between the two regular open algebras.
