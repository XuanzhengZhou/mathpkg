A filter for a partial order structure $P = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ generalizes the notion of a filter from Boolean algebras to arbitrary partial orders. It requires that the set be nonempty, downward-directed (strongly compatible), and closed upward (upward hereditary). This definition enables the construction of the topological space of ultrafilters on a partial order.
