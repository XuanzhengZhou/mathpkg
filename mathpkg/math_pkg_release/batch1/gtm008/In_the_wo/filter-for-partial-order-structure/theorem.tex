Let $P = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ be a partial order structure and let $F$ be a nonempty subset of $P$. Then $F$ is a filter for $P$ iff

1. $F$ is strongly compatible, i.e., $(\forall x, y \in F)(\exists z \in F)[z \leq x \wedge z \leq y]$.
2. $F$ is upward hereditary, i.e., $(\forall x \in F)(\forall y \in P)[x \leq y \rightarrow y \in F]$.
