If $\mathbf{A}$ is a set (structure), then
$$\operatorname{Df}(\mathbf{A}) \triangleq \{a \mid \exists \ulcorner \varphi(x) \urcorner \in \operatorname{Fml}^1(\mathbf{A}) \; [a = \{x \in A \mid \mathbf{A} \models \varphi(x)\}]\}.$$
Here $\operatorname{Fml}^1(\mathbf{A})$ is the set of (Gödel numbers of) formulas of $\mathcal{L}(C(A))$ having at most one free variable.
