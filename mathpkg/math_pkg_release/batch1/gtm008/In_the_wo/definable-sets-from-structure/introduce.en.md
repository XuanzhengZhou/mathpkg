$\operatorname{Df}(\mathbf{A})$ is the collection of all subsets of $A$ that are definable in the structure $\mathbf{A}$ using parameters from $A$. This is the key operation in the transfinite construction of the constructible hierarchy: $L_{\alpha+1} = \operatorname{Df}(L_\alpha)$. For the general theory of relative constructibility, the same operation is applied to structures enriched with additional predicates, allowing the construction of $L_K$, $L_a$, and $L[a]$.
