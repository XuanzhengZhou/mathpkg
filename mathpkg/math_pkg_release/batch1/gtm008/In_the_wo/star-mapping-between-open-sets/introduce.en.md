The star operations $\ast$ establish a Galois connection between the open subsets of a partial order structure $P$ and its associated ultrafilter space $\mathcal{F}$. The operation maps open sets in one space to open sets in the other. Together, the compositions $\ast\Delta$ (star then interior-of-closure) and $\Delta\ast$ (interior-of-closure then star) yield the regular open algebras of $P$ and $\mathcal{F}$ respectively, and the star operation provides the isomorphism between these Boolean algebras of regular open sets.
