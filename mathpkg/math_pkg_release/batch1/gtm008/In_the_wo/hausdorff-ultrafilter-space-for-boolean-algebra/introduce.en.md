When the partial order structure arises from a Boolean algebra, the topological space of ultrafilters is not merely $T_1$ but in fact Hausdorff ($T_2$). The proof proceeds by contradiction: if two distinct ultrafilters could not be separated, one could construct a filter containing both, contradicting their maximality. This result is fundamental to Stone duality, where the space of ultrafilters forms a totally disconnected compact Hausdorff space.
