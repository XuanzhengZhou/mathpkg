The star operations compose with the interior-of-closure operations ($\Delta$) to give inclusion relations. For any open set $G_1$ in $P$, we have $G_1 \subseteq G_1^{*\Delta}$, and similarly for open sets in $\mathcal{F}$. These inclusions are the first step toward establishing the Galois connection that yields the isomorphism between the Boolean algebras of regular open sets of $P$ and $\mathcal{F}$.
