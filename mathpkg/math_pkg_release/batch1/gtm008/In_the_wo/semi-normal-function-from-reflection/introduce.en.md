By coding all existential formulas into a single semi-normal function $F$ on the ordinals, the Reflection Principle can be expressed as: for any formula $\varphi$ (existential), the truth in $M$ reflects to $M_{F(\alpha)}$ for parameters in $M_\alpha$. A semi-normal function satisfies $F(\alpha) \geq \alpha$, and its fixed points (where $F(\alpha) = \alpha$) are exactly the ordinals where full reflection occurs.
