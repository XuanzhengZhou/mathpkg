A filter for a partial order structure $P$ is a nonempty subset that is both strongly compatible (any two elements have a common lower bound in $F$) and upward hereditary (closed under taking larger elements). This generalizes the notion of a filter from Boolean algebras to arbitrary partial orders. For Boolean algebras, filters defined in the algebraic sense coincide with filters of the associated partial order structure when the zero element is excluded.
