Let $P = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ be a partial order structure and let $F$ be a nonempty subset of $P$. Then $F$ is a \text{filter} for $P$ iff:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $F$ is \text{strongly compatible}: $(\forall x, y \in F)(\exists z \in F)[z \leq x \wedge z \leq y]$.
\item $F$ is \text{upward hereditary}: $(\forall x \in F)(\forall y \in P)[x \leq y \rightarrow y \in F]$.
\end{enumerate}
