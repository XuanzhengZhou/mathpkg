In the Boolean-valued model $V^{(B)}$, the Boolean value of the statement that the standard powerset $\mathcal{P}(\omega)$ in the ground model (denoted $\mathcal{P}(\omega)^{\vee}$) is a subset of the powerset of $\check{\omega}$ computed in $V^{(B)}$ (denoted $\mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})$) is $1$, i.e., this inclusion holds with full Boolean truth value. This follows from the fact that for every subset $s \subseteq \omega$ in the ground model, its canonical name $\check{\check{s}}$ satisfies $[\check{\check{s}} \subseteq \check{\omega}] = 1$ and hence $[\check{\check{s}} \in \mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})] = 1$.
