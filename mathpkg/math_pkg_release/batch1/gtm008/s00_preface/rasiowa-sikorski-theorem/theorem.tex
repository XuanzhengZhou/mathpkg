If $B$ is a Boolean algebra and:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $a_0 \in |B| - \{0\}$, and
\item $A_n \subseteq |B| \land b_n \in |B| \land b_n = \sum_{a \in A_n} a$, for $n \in \omega$,
\end{enumerate}
then there exists a Boolean homomorphism $h: |B| \rightarrow |2|$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $h(a_0) = 1$, and
\item $h(b_n) = \sum_{a \in A_n} h(a)$, for $n \in \omega$.
\end{enumerate}
