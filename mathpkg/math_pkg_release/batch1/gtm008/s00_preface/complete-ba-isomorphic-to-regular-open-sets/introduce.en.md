This is the central duality theorem of Chapter 1: every complete Boolean algebra is isomorphic to the Boolean algebra of regular open subsets of its nonzero elements under the order topology. The isomorphism is given by the map $F(b) = [b]$ sending each element to its downward closure. This establishes a perfect correspondence between complete Boolean algebras and the regular open set algebras of order-topological spaces, bridging the algebraic and topological perspectives.
