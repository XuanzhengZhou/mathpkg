This theorem relates the concepts of regular open, open, closed, and dense sets. A regular open set (one satisfying $A = A^{-0}$) is always open. The complement of an open set is closed, and vice versa. Density is upward-closed: if $A$ is dense and $A \subseteq B$, then $B$ is also dense.
