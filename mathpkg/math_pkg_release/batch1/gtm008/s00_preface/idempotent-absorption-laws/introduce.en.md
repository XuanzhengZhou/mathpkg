The idempotent laws state that adding or multiplying an element by itself yields the same element. The absorption laws describe how the two operations interact: adding $a$ to the product $ab$ returns $a$, and multiplying $a$ by the sum $a+b$ also returns $a$. These are among the first elementary consequences derived from the Boolean algebra axioms.
