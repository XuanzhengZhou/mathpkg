This theorem (labelled Theorem 1.19 in the text) establishes that any collection $T'$ of subsets covering $X$ and closed under "intersection refinement" generates a topology by taking all unions of subcollections. The collection $T'$ is then called a base for the resulting topology. This construction is used later to define the order topology on a partial order structure.
