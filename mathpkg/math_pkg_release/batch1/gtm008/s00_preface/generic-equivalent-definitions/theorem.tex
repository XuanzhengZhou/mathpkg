If $P = \langle P, \leq \rangle$ is a partial order structure and $G \subseteq P$, then the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $G$ is $P$-generic over $A$ in the strong sense.
\item $(\forall S \in A)[S \text{ is a dense subset of } P \rightarrow G \cap S \neq 0]$.
\item $(\forall S \in A)[S \subseteq P \rightarrow (\exists p \in G)[p \in S \lor [p] \cap S = 0]]$.
\end{enumerate}
