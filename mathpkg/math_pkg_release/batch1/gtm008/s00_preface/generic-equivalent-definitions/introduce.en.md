This theorem provides equivalent formulations of genericity in the strong sense. Condition (2) is the standard genericity condition (meeting all dense sets in $A$). Condition (3) states that for every subset $S$ in $A$, there is a condition in $G$ that either belongs to $S$ or is incompatible with everything in $S$. This predensity formulation is useful for working with Boolean completions and the maximum principle.
