Theorem 3.6: A_0 = T \cup \{X - a \mid a \in T\} from Boolean Algebra of Takeuti and Zaring's "Axiomatic Set Theory" (GTM 8). This theorem is part of the foundational development of Boolean-valued models and forcing techniques in set theory.
