This theorem establishes the fundamental duality between ideals and filters via complementation: $F$ is a filter precisely when its complement-image $I = \{-a \mid a \in F\}$ is an ideal, and vice versa. Moreover, maximal ideals correspond exactly to ultrafilters. This duality allows results about ideals to be translated into results about filters, and is used throughout the theory of Boolean-valued models.
