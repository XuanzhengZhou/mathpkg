$$\tilde{D}((\forall x)\varphi(x)) \xrightarrow{\Delta} (\forall t \in T) \tilde{D}(\varphi(t))$$

Remark. Since $T$ is a proper class, $\tilde{D}$ is no longer definable in the language $\mathcal{L}_0(K(), F())$, simultaneously for all unlimited formulas. As in the case of truth definitions $\tilde{D}(\varphi)$ is definable in $\mathcal{L}_0(K(), F())$ for any particular formula $\varphi$ or indeed for any set of formulas $\varphi$ with less than $n$ quantifiers, $n$ a fixed natural number.
