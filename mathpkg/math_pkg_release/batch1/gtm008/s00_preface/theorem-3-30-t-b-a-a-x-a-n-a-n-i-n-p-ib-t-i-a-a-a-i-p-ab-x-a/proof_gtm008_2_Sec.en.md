If $A \neq 0$ then $\exists b \in A$. If $f \in \prod_{a \in A} X_a$ then $f(b) \in X_b$ and hence $\exists N(f(b)) \in T_b$. Then

$$f \in \left\{ g \in \prod_{a \in A} X_a \mid g(b) \in N(f(b)) \right\} \in T'.$$

Clearly, if $B_1, B_2 \in T'$ then $B_1 \cap B_2 \in T'$. Therefore by Theorem 1.18 $T'$ is a base for a topology on $\prod_{a \in A} X_a$.
