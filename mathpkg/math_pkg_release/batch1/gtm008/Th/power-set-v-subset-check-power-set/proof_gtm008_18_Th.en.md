For all $s \subseteq \omega$, we have:

$$
[\check{s} \subseteq \check{\omega}] = 1,
$$

and therefore:

$$
[\check{s} \in \mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})] = 1.
$$

Since this holds for every $s \subseteq \omega$, the Boolean truth value of $\mathcal{P}(\omega)^{\vee} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})$ is 1, i.e.:

$$
[\mathcal{P}(\omega)^{\vee} \subseteq \mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})] = 1.
$$
