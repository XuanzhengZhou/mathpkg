Theorem 18.2 provides a model-theoretic characterization of the $(\omega, 2)$-distributive law for a complete Boolean algebra $B$. It states that $B$ satisfies the $(\omega, 2)$-distributive law if and only if the power set of $\omega$ as interpreted in the Boolean-valued model $V^{(B)}$ coincides with the check of the external power set, i.e. $[\mathcal{P}(\omega)^{\vee} = \mathcal{P}(\check{\omega})] = 1$. This connects a purely algebraic property of $B$ with the structure of the corresponding forcing extension.
