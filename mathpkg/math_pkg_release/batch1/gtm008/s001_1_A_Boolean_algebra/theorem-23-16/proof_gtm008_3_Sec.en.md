(By induction on the least $\alpha$ such that $v \in V

Proof. For given $v$ and $p > 0$ let

$$b'_\alpha = \prod_{\beta \geq \alpha} \prod_{x \in \mathcal{D}(v)} (b_{\beta x} \Leftrightarrow v(x)).$$

Since $\sum_\alpha b'_\alpha = 1$, there exists an $\alpha$ such that $b'_\alpha \cdot p > 0$. Then $u_\alpha$ has the desired property.
