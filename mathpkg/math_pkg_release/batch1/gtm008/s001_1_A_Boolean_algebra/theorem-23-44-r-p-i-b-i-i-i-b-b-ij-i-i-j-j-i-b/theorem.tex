$$(\forall i \in I) \left[ b_i = \sum_{j \in J_i} b_{ij} \right].$$

1. $\bar{r} \leq r$
2. $\Gamma \leq \aleph_\alpha$ and

$$(\exists p \in \Lambda)(\exists j \in J_i) [p \cdot r' > 0 \land [p \cdot \bar{r} \leq b_{ij} \lor p \cdot \bar{r} \leq -b_i]].$$
