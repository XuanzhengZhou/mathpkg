Definition 13.3 assigns Boolean truth values to the atomic formulas $u \in v$ and $u = v$ in the Boolean-valued universe $V^{(B)}$. The definitions proceed by simultaneous recursion on the well-founded relation of rank. The Boolean value $[\![u \in v]\!]$ is the supremum over elements $y$ in the domain of $v$ of the "degree" to which $y$ belongs to $v$ and $u$ equals $y$. Equality encodes the axiom of extensionality as a Boolean equation.
