Example 5 extends the pointwise induction of Example 4 to the full induction principle for $\omega$ in $V^{(B)}$. It shows that if a $B$-valued set $a$ contains $0$ and is closed under successor, then $\check{\omega}$ is a subset of $a$ (with Boolean value $1$). This is a key step in proving that the natural numbers are absolute.
