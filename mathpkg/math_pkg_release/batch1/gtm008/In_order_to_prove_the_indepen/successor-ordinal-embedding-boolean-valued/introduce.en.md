This example shows that the successor operation on ordinals commutes with the canonical embedding: the successor of $\check{\alpha}$ in $V^{(B)}$ is (with Boolean value $1$) equal to the canonical name of the successor ordinal $\alpha + 1$. This verifies that the ordinal arithmetic structure of $V$ is faithfully represented in $V^{(B)}$.
