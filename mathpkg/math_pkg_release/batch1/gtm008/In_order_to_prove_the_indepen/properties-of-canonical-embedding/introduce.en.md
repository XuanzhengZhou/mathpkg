Theorem 13.17 establishes the fundamental properties of the canonical embedding $y \mapsto \check{y}$ of $V$ into $V^{(B)}$. Parts 1 and 2 show that the embedding is a $\Delta_0$-elementary embedding: membership and equality are absolute between $V$ and $V^{(B)}$ for standard sets. Part 3 states that for the 2-element Boolean algebra, the image of the embedding exhausts $V^{(2)}$ up to Boolean-valued equality, effectively identifying $V$ with $V^{(2)}$.
