Definition 13.8 forms the restricted $B$-valued structure $V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$ by restricting the Boolean-valued membership and equality relations to the set $V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$. This sequence of structures $\langle V_{\alpha}^{(B)} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$ satisfies the conditions of the ramified language framework from Section 9, allowing the transfer of logical properties from the finite stages to the full universe $V^{(B)}$.
