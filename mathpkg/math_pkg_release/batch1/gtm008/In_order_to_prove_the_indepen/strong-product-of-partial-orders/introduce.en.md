The strong product of a family of partial order structures $\{P_i\}_{i \in I}$ is the Cartesian product equipped with the pointwise (coordinatewise) partial order. This is the standard product in the category of posets. In the context of forcing, the strong product corresponds to adding $I$-many generic sets simultaneously without any restriction on the support.
