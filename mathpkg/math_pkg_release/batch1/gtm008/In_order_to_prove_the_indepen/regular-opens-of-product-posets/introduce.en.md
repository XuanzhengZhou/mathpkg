This is an open problem posed after Definition 12.1. It asks whether the complete Boolean algebra of regular open sets of the strong or weak product of a family of partial order structures can be expressed purely in terms of the complete Boolean algebras of regular open sets of the individual factor posets $P_i$. This relates to the question of how the Boolean-valued model of a product forcing relates to the Boolean-valued models of the individual forcings.
