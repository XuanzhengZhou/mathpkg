**Problem.** Is the complete Boolean algebra of regular open sets of $\prod_{i \in I}^{s} P_i$ or $\prod_{i \in I}^{w} P_i$ determined by the complete Boolean algebras of regular open sets of $P_i$ ($i \in I$)?

Here $\prod^{s}$ denotes the strong (full) product of partial order structures and $\prod^{w}$ denotes the weak (finite support) product, as defined in Definition 12.1.
