Theorem 12.3 states that every formula $arphi$, every term $t$, and every condition $p$ in the weak product forcing depends on only finitely many coordinates (indices). More precisely, there exists a natural number $m$ such that any permutation of $\omega$ that fixes the first $m$ elements leaves $arphi$, $t$, and $p$ invariant. This finite support property is essential for carrying out symmetry arguments in the independence proof of the Axiom of Choice.
