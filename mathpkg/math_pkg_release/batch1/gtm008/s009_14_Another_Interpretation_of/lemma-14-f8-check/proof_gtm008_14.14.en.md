By the same method used for the preceding lemmas (Lemmas 2-7). The proof proceeds by computing the Boolean value of membership in $\mathcal{F}_8(\check{k}_1, \check{k}_2)$ and showing it equals membership in the check of $\mathcal{F}_8(k_1, k_2)$, using the reduction formulas for existential quantifiers over pairsets and ordered pairs established in Theorems 14.15-14.17.
