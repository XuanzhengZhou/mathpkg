By Theorem 13.16, $[x = \check{b}] = 0$ unless $x = b$ in the ground model. The Boolean value $[\{\check{k}_2, \check{k}_3\} = \check{k}_1]$ is 1 if and only if the sets are equal in $V$, since the check operation respects set-theoretic constructions and the equality axioms of Boolean-valued models.
