This theorem provides a simplified verification that every axiom $\phi$ of ZF relativized to $M$ holds with Boolean value 1 in $\mathbf{V}^{(B)}$. The key insight is that $[\phi^M]$ reduces to a product (for universal formulas) or sum (for existential formulas) over the ground model $V$ of $[\psi(\check{k})]$, where $\psi(k)$ is true in $V$ because $V$ itself is a model of ZF. Since $\mathbf{V}^{(B)} \models ZF$, we get $[\psi(\check{k})] = 1$, so the entire expression equals 1.
