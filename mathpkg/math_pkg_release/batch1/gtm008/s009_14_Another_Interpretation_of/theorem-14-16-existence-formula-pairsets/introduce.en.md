This theorem provides a reduction formula for existential quantifiers over pairsets in $V^{(B)}$. When evaluating $[(\exists x)(\exists y)[\{x, y\} = \check{k}_1 \land \varphi(x, y)]]$, the Boolean value can be computed as the sum of $[\varphi(\check{k}_2, \check{k}_3)]$ over all unordered pairs $\{k_2, k_3\}$ that equal $k_1$ in the ground model. This lemma is used heavily in the analysis of Boolean-valued formulas involving unordered pair constructions.
