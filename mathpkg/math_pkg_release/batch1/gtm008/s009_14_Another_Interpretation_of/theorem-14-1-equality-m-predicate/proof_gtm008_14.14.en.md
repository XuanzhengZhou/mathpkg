For $u, v \in V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$,

$$[u = v] \cdot [M(u)]_{\alpha} = \sum_{k \in R(\alpha)} [u = v] \cdot [u = \check{k}]$$
$$\leq \sum_{k \in R(\alpha)} [v = \check{k}] = [M(v)]_{\alpha}.$$

The inequality follows from the equality axioms, which give $[u = v] \cdot [u = \check{k}] \leq [v = \check{k}]$.
