When $B$ has an atom $b_0$, the generic filter $F$ equals the canonical name $\check{S}$ of the principal ultrafilter generated by $b_0$, with Boolean value exactly $b_0$. This shows atoms correspond to the possibility that the generic filter is in the ground model. Together with Theorem 14.9, this gives a complete dichotomy.
