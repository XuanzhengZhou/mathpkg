This lemma (Lemma 3 of Section 14) computes the Boolean value of membership in the intersection of two check sets. For any $u \in V^{(B)}$, the value $[u \in (\check{k}_1 \cap \check{k}_2)]$ reduces to the sum of $[u = \check{k}]$ over all $k$ in the ground-model intersection $k_1 \cap k_2$. This equality is used to show that the check operation preserves Boolean set-theoretic operations.
