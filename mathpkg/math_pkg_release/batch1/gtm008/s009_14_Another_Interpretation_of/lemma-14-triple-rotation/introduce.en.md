This lemma (comprising Lemmas 5-7 of Section 14) demonstrates that the check embedding commutes with the operation of rotating the last two coordinates of an ordered triple. The Boolean-valued set of ordered triples $\langle x, y, z \rangle$ formed from $\langle x, z, y \rangle \in \check{k}_1$ equals the check of the corresponding ground-model construction, with Boolean value 1. This is part of a series of lemmas establishing that arbitrary set-theoretic operations are preserved by the check embedding.
