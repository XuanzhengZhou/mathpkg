This lemma (Lemma 2 of Section 14) establishes that the canonical check embedding of the set difference $k_1 \setminus k_2$ equals the Boolean-valued set difference of the check embeddings of $k_1$ and $k_2$, with Boolean truth value 1. This is one of a series of lemmas demonstrating that the check operation is a Boolean-valued elementary embedding, preserving all fundamental set-theoretic operations.
