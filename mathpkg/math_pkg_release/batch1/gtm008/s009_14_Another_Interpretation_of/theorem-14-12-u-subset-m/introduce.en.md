This theorem provides a crucial link between subsets of $M$ in the Boolean-valued universe and elements of $M$. It states that if $u$ is a subset of the ground model $M$ in the Boolean sense, then with Boolean value at least $[u \subseteq M]$, there exists some $y \in M$ such that $u \subseteq y$. This is a Boolean-valued version of the replacement/collection principle applied to the ground model.
