Let $h_0: \mathbf{B} \to 2$ be an $N$-complete homomorphism, and define $h$ on $(V^{(B)})^N$ by recursion. For $u, v \in (V^{(B)})^N$:

1. $h_0([u = v]) = 1 \leftrightarrow h(u) = h(v)$.
2. $h_0([u \in v]) = 1 \leftrightarrow h(u) \in h(v)$.
3. $(\forall k \in N)[h(\check{k}) = k]$.
4. $[M(u)] = 1$ iff $h(u) \in N$.
5. $[F = \check{G}]$ where $G$ is the generic filter associated to $h_0$.
6. For any formula $\varphi$, $h_0$ preserves sums $\sum_{x} [\varphi(x)]$.
7. $h_0([\varphi(u_1,\ldots,u_n)]) = 1 \leftrightarrow N_1 \models \varphi(h(u_1),\ldots,h(u_n))$.
8. $\forall u \in (V^{(B)})^N, h(u) \in N[G]$.
