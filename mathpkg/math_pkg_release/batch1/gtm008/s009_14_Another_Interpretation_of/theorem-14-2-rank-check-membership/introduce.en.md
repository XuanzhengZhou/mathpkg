This theorem establishes the relationship between rank and Boolean membership for canonical names. Part 1 states that if $\alpha$ is at most the rank of $k$, then $[\check{k} \in u] = 0$ for any $u \in V_{\alpha+1}^{(B)}$. Part 2 asserts that when the rank of $k$ is at most $\alpha$, the ground model predicate holds for $\check{k}$ at level $\alpha$ with value 1.
