This is an immediate consequence of Theorem 14.22, property 7. Let $N_1 = \{h(x) \mid x \in (V^{(B)})^N\}$. Then $N_1 = N[G]$ by property 8 (surjectivity). Property 7 gives
$$h_0([\varphi(u_1, \ldots, u_n)]) = 1 \leftrightarrow N_1 \models \varphi(h(u_1), \ldots, h(u_n)),$$
and substituting $N_1 = N[G]$ yields the stated equivalence.
