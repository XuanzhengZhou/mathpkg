This corollary follows directly from property 7 of Theorem 14.22 and states that the homomorphism $h$ provides a truth-preserving translation between the Boolean-valued model $(V^{(B)})^N$ and the two-valued generic extension $N[G]$. For any formula $\varphi$, the two-valued truth in $N[G]$ corresponds exactly to $h_0$ mapping the Boolean truth value to 1. This is the key result that equates the forcing relation (truth in $N[G]$) with Boolean-valued truth.
