Extending the pairset reduction formula (Theorem 14.16) to ordered pairs, this theorem provides a reduction for $[(\exists x)(\exists y)[\langle x, y \rangle = \check{k}_1 \land \varphi(x, y)]]$ in $V^{(B)}$. The Boolean value reduces to summing $[\varphi(\check{k}_2, \check{k}_3)]$ over all ordered pairs $\langle k_2, k_3 \rangle$ that equal $k_1$ in the ground model. This is essential for handling ordered-pair constructions within Boolean-valued forcing arguments.
