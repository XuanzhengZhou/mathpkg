Corollary 9.15 strengthens Theorem 9.14 by showing that the bound $\beta$ for the existential quantifier can be chosen uniformly for all parameter tuples $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ from a given level $M_{\alpha}$. This uniformity is essential for the proof of the Replacement Axiom (Theorem 9.25), where one needs a single $\beta$ that works for all $x \in M_{\alpha}$ simultaneously.
