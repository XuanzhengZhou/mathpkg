Definition 10.10 introduces the localized notion of a set $S \subseteq P$ being "dense beneath $p$," which means that every condition extending $p$ is compatible with some element of $S$. This is a refinement of ordinary density that plays a key role in the analysis of the existential quantifier in forcing (Theorem 10.12) and in relating generic filters to the forcing relation.
