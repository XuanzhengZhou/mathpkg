A sequence $\langle \mathbf{T}_{\alpha} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$ of $B$-valued structures for the ramified language $\mathcal{L}$ is defined, where

$$
\mathbf{T}_{\alpha} = \langle T_{\alpha}, \bar{\equiv}, \bar{\in}, \bar{V}_{\alpha}, \bar{F}_{\alpha} \rangle,
$$

with $\bar{\equiv}$ and $\bar{\in}$ interpreted as $B$-valued equality and membership respectively. The definition of $\llbracket \varphi \rrbracket_{\mathbf{T}_{\alpha}}$ is extended to unlimited formulas by adding clause 9:

$$
\llbracket (\forall x)\varphi(x) \rrbracket_{\mathbf{T}_{\alpha}} \triangleq \prod_{t \in T_{\alpha}} \llbracket \varphi(t) \rrbracket_{\mathbf{T}_{\alpha}}.
$$
