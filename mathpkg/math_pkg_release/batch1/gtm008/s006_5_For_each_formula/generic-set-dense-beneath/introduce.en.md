Theorem 10.11 establishes that $P$-generic filters intersect every set $S \in M$ that is dense beneath any of its elements. This is a refinement of the standard genericity property (which requires intersection with all dense sets) and is tailored for analyzing the existential quantifier in forcing. The proof constructs an auxiliary dense set $S'$ from $S$ and applies the defining property of genericity.
