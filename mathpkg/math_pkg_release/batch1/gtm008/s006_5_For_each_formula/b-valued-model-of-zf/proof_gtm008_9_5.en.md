The conditions 1--5 define the cumulative $B$-valued hierarchy. The proof that $M$ is a $B$-valued model of $\mathsf{ZF}$ proceeds by verifying each axiom: Extensionality (Theorem 9.8), Unions (Theorem 9.9), Powers (Theorem 9.24), and Replacement (Theorem 9.25). Since all $\mathsf{ZF}$ axioms hold with Boolean value $1$ in $M$, $M$ is a $B$-valued model of $\mathsf{ZF}$.
