Theorem 9.26 is the culminating result of Section 5, asserting that the cumulative $B$-valued hierarchy $\langle M_{\alpha} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$ produces a full $B$-valued model of Zermelo--Fraenkel set theory. The proof synthesizes the verification that $M$ satisfies Extensionality (9.8), Unions (9.9), Powers (9.24), and Replacement (9.25).
