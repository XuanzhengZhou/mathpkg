Theorem 9.9 verifies that $M$ satisfies the Axiom of Unions with Boolean truth value $1$. The proof uses the fact that the union of a set $a \in M$ can be constructed using condition 5 of the hierarchy: the defining formula $\varphi(x, a) = (\exists y \in a)(x \in y)$ can be used with the bounded quantifier results of Theorem 9.7 to produce the required $b \in M$.
