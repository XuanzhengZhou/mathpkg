Theorem 9.8 verifies that the Boolean-valued structure $M$ built from the cumulative hierarchy $\langle M_{\alpha} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$ satisfies the Axiom of Extensionality with Boolean truth value $1$. The proof reduces the universal quantifier to a product over an appropriate $M_{\alpha}$ and applies the extensionality property that holds within each $M_{\alpha}$.
