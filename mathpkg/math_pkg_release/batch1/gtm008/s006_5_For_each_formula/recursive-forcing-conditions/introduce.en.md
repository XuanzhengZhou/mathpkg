Theorem 10.4 lists the recursive conditions that the forcing relation $p \Vdash \varphi$ satisfies, mirroring the Tarski-style satisfaction relation for $M[G]$. These conditions provide a purely combinatorial characterization of forcing: negation corresponds to "no stronger condition forces," conjunction is pointwise, universal quantification over all terms or bounded by rank, and the predicates $V(t)$ and $F(t)$ unwind through approximations. These recursive clauses allow one to compute forcing for complex formulas from forcing for atomic formulas.
