Theorem 9.24 establishes that $M$ satisfies the Axiom of Powers with Boolean truth value $1$. The proof is a central technical challenge in the construction of $B$-valued models, requiring one to find a bound for all $b$ with $\llbracket b \subseteq a \rrbracket = 1$. It uses Theorem 9.20 (definable subsets are defined over the preceding level) and Theorem 9.22 (bounding ordinal).
