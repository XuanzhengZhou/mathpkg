$M$ satisfies the Axiom Schema of Replacement. In the form
$$(\exists b)(\forall x \in a)(\exists y \in b)[(\exists y')\varphi(x, y') \to \varphi(x, y)],$$
abbreviating the consequent by $\varphi'(x, y)$, we have
$$\llbracket (\forall a \in M)(\exists b)(\forall x \in a)(\exists y \in b)\varphi'(x, y) \rrbracket = 1.$$
