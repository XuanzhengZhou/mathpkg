Let $\gamma$ be a cardinal with $\gamma \geq \aleph_0$. Let $\{X_i \mid i \in I\}$ be a family of pairwise disjoint sets. For each $i \in I$, let $b_i$ be a family of subsets of $X_i$ with $\bar{b}_i \leq \gamma$.

Suppose $\{O^{(t)} \mid t \in T\}$ is a family such that for each $t \in T$,
$$O^{(t)} = \prod_{i \in I} O_i^{(t)}$$
where $O_i^{(t)} \in b_i \cup \{X_i\}$ and the set $\{i \in I \mid O_i^{(t)} \neq X_i\}$ is finite. If $(\forall t, t' \in T)[t \neq t' \rightarrow O^{(t)} \cap O^{(t')} = 0]$, then
$$\bar{T} \leq \gamma.$$
