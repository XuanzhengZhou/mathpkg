Let $\gamma$ be a cardinal with $\gamma \geq \aleph_0$. If
\begin{enumerate}
\item $(\forall t \in T)[\bar{A}_t < \omega \;\wedge\; \bar{B}_t \leq \gamma]$,
\item $(\forall t, t' \in T)[t \neq t' \rightarrow A_t \cap B_{t'} \neq 0 \;\wedge\; A_t \cap B_t = 0]$,
\end{enumerate}
then $\bar{T} \leq \gamma$.
