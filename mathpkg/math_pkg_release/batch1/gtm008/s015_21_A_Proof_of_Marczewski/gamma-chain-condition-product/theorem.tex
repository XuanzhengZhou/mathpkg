If $\{X_i \mid i \in I\}$ is a family of topological spaces ($I$ a set) and each $X_i$ has a base of cardinality $\leq \gamma$, where $\gamma$ is an infinite cardinal, then the Boolean algebra $B$ of all regular open sets of the product space $\prod_{i \in I} X_i$ satisfies the $\gamma$-chain condition.
In particular, if each $X_i$ is 2nd countable (i.e., $X_i$ has a countable base) then $B$ satisfies the countable chain condition (ccc).
