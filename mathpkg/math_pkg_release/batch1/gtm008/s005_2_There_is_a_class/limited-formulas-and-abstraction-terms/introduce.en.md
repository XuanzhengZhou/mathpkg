In the ramified language $R(K,F)$, limited formulas and abstraction terms form the predicative core. Abstraction terms $\hat{x}^\alpha \varphi(x^\alpha)$ build sets at level $\alpha$ using only lower-rank constants and previously defined sets, ensuring a predicative construction of the set-theoretic universe.
