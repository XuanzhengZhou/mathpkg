When $t_1$ is a constant term of rank less than $\alpha$, the extensional equivalence $t \simeq t_1$ has strictly lower ordinal measure than the membership formula $t \in \hat{x}^\alpha \varphi(x^\alpha)$. This inequality supports inductive definitions of the denotation operator.
