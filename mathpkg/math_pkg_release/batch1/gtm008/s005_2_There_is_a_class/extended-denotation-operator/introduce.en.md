The denotation operator extends to $\tilde{D}$ for unlimited formulas by interpreting universal quantification as ranging over all constant terms $T$. However, since $T$ is a proper class, $\tilde{D}$ is not globally definable in $\mathcal{L}_0(K(), F())$ for all unlimited formulas simultaneously, only for bounded collections of formulas.
