The Generalized Continuum Hypothesis (GCH) holds in the constructible universe. This fundamental result of G\"odel shows that the cardinality of the power set of $\aleph_\alpha$ is exactly $\aleph_{\alpha+1}$, establishing the consistency of GCH relative to ZF.
