This theorem establishes three fundamental properties of relative constructibility: (1) $L[K;F]$ and $L[F]$ are definable in the appropriate languages, (2) every set belongs to its own relative constructible universe, and (3) $L[K]$ can be decomposed as a union over increasing rank initial segments.
