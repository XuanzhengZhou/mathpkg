The notation $L[F]$ denotes the constructible universe relative to a class $F$, defined as $L[K_0; F]$ where $K_0$ is the transitive closure of $F$. More generally, for a standard transitive model $M$ containing all ordinals, $M[F]$ is defined as $L[M; F]$, the smallest extension of $M$ by $F$ in the constructible hierarchy.
