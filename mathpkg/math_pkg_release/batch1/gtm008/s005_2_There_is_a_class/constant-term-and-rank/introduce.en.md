Constant terms encompass individual constants (from $K$) and abstraction terms. The rank $\rho$ assigns an ordinal to each constant term: for $k \in K$ it is the set-theoretic rank, and for abstraction terms it is the superscript ordinal. $T_\alpha$ collects all constant terms of rank less than $\alpha$, and $T$ is the total collection.
