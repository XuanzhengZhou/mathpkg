Take $m$ to be the maximum of all $i \in \omega$ for which $F_i$ occurs in $\varphi$ or $t$, or for which $p_i$ is not $1_i$ (the greatest element of $P_i$). Then any permutation $\pi \in \mathbf{G}_m$ (fixing all $i \leq m$) will fix all predicate symbols $F_i$ appearing in $\varphi$ (since they occur only for $i \leq m$), fix the term $t$, and fix $p$ (since $p(i) = 1_i$ for all $i > m$). Thus $m$ has the required properties.
