The Boolean-valued universe $V^{(B)}$ is constructed by transfinite recursion over the ordinals, analogous to the cumulative hierarchy but with functions into a complete Boolean algebra $B$ instead of subsets. Each stage $V_\alpha^{(B)}$ collects functions whose domain is included in earlier stages. The full universe is the union over all ordinals. This construction, due to Scott and Solovay, provides a Boolean-valued semantics for set theory equivalent in expressive power to forcing while avoiding the complexity of the ramified language.
