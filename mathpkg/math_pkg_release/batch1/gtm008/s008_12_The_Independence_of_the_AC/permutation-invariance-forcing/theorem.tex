Let $\pi$ be a permutation of $\omega$. Extend $\pi$ to the ramified language by induction on the order of terms and formulas. For $p^1, \ldots, p^n \in P$ and $p = \{ \ldots p_{i_1}^{1}, \ldots, p_{i_n}^{n} \} \in \prod_{i \in \omega}^{w} P_i$ where $p_{i_k}^{k} = j_{i_k}(p^k)$, define
$$\pi(p) = \{ \ldots p_{\pi(i_1)}^{1}, \ldots, p_{\pi(i_n)}^{n} \}.$$

Then for every formula $\varphi$ and every $p \in \prod_{i \in \omega}^{w} P_i$,
$$p \Vdash \varphi \leftrightarrow \pi(p) \Vdash \pi(\varphi).$$
