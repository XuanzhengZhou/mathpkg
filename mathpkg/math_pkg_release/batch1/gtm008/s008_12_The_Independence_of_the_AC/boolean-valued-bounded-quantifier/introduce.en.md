This theorem provides explicit formulas for computing the Boolean truth values of bounded quantifier formulas in $V^{(B)}$. For $\exists x \in u$, one takes the Boolean sum over $x \in \mathcal{D}(u)$ of $u(x) \cdot [\![\varphi(x)]\!]$. For $\forall x \in u$, one takes the Boolean product of the implications $u(x) \Rightarrow [\![\varphi(x)]\!]$. These formulas are essential for practical computations in Boolean-valued models.
