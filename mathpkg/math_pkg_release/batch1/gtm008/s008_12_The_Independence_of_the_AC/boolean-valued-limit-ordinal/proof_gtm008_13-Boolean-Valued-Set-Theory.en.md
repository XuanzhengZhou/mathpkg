For $\alpha \in K_{\Pi}$: if $u \in V_\alpha^{(B)}$, then by definition there exists $\xi < \alpha$ with $\mathcal{D}(u) \subseteq V_\xi^{(B)}$, so $u \in V_{\xi+1}^{(B)} \subseteq \bigcup_{\xi < \alpha} V_\xi^{(B)}$. Conversely, if $u \in \bigcup_{\xi < \alpha} V_\xi^{(B)}$, then $u \in V_\xi^{(B)}$ for some $\xi < \alpha$, hence $\mathcal{D}(u) \subseteq V_{\xi}^{(B)} \subseteq \bigcup_{\zeta < \alpha} V_\zeta^{(B)}$ with $\xi < \alpha$, so $u \in V_\alpha^{(B)}$.
