The Boolean-valued hierarchy $V_\alpha^{(B)}$ is continuous at limit ordinals, just as the standard cumulative hierarchy satisfies $V_\lambda = \bigcup_{\xi < \lambda} V_\xi$ for limit $\lambda$. This ensures the hierarchy behaves coherently and is a well-defined transfinite construction.
