Each rank initial segment $V_\alpha^{(B)}$ of the Boolean-valued universe is made into a bounded $B$-valued structure by restricting the global Boolean values of equality and membership. The resulting sequence $\langle V_\alpha^{(B)} \mid \alpha \in On \rangle$ is designed to satisfy the five conditions of the general framework from Section 9, which then guarantees via Theorem 9.26 that the full universe $V^{(B)}$ is a Boolean-valued model of ZF.
