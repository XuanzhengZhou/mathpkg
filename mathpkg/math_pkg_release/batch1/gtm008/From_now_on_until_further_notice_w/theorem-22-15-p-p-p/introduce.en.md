Theorem 22.15: p_{\alpha\beta} \circ p_{\beta} = p_{\alpha} from The Completion of a Boolean Algebra of Takeuti and Zaring's "Axiomatic Set Theory" (GTM 8). This theorem is part of the foundational development of Boolean-valued models and forcing techniques in set theory.
