This is the converse direction of the correspondence between extensional functions and Boolean-valued functions. Given any Boolean-valued function $f$ between definite sets $u$ and $v$, one can extract an extensional function $\varphi$ on the underlying domains by reading off the truth values $\llbracket \langle x, y \rangle \in f \rrbracket$. Together with Theorem 16.8, this establishes a complete bijective correspondence between extensional functions on definite domains and Boolean-valued functions.
