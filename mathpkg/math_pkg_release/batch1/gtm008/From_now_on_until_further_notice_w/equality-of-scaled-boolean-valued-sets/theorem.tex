Let $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ be extensional and $b \in B$. Show that:
$$\llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = \llbracket \sup(u) \Rightarrow b \rrbracket.$$

Consequently:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\llbracket b \geq \sup(u) \rrbracket = 1$ implies $\llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = 1$,
\item $\llbracket b < \sup(u) \rrbracket = 1$ implies $\llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket < 1$,
\item $\llbracket u = 0 \rrbracket = \neg \sup(u)$.
\end{enumerate}
