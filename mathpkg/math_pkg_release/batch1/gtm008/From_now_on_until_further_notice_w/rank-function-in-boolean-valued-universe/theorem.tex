For $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$:

\text{1.}\quad $\llbracket u \in R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = \sum_{v \in V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})}} \llbracket u = v \rrbracket = \llbracket u \in V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})} \times \{1\} \rrbracket$, and therefore $\llbracket R(\check{\alpha}) = V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})} \times \{1\} \rrbracket = 1$.

\text{2.}\quad Assume $\mathcal{D}(u) \subseteq V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})}$. Then $\llbracket u \subseteq R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = 1$.

\text{3.}\quad $\llbracket u \subseteq R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = \sum_{t \in A_{\alpha}} \llbracket u = t \rrbracket$, where $A_{\alpha} = \{t \in V^{(\mathbf{B})} \mid \mathcal{D}(t) = V_{\alpha}^{(\mathbf{B})} \land \llbracket t \subseteq R(\check{\alpha}) \rrbracket = 1\}$.
