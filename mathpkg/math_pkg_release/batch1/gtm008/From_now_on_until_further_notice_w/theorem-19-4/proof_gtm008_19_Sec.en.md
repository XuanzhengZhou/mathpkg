Let $b \in B$ and $0 < b < 1$. Then there exist $p$ and $q$ such that $[p] \subseteq b$ and $[q] \subseteq -b$. By Theorem 11.6, $\exists \pi \in \text{Aut}(P)$ such that $\pi(p)$ and $q$ are compatible. Using $\pi$ to also denote the automorphism on $B$ induced by $\pi$ we have $\pi(b) \cdot (-b) \geq \pi([p]) \cdot [q] > 0$. Therefore $\pi(b) \neq b$.
