Continuing from the previous exercise, deduce the following Boolean inequalities for an extensional set $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\llbracket b \geq \sup(u) \rrbracket \to \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket = 1$,
\item $\llbracket b < \sup(u) \rrbracket \to \llbracket u = b \cdot u \rrbracket < 1$,
\item $\llbracket u = 0 \rrbracket = \neg \sup(u)$,
\item $\llbracket b \cdot u = 0 \rrbracket = \neg b + \llbracket u = 0 \rrbracket$.
\end{enumerate}
