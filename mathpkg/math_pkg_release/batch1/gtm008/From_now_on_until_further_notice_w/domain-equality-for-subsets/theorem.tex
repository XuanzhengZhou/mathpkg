Let $u, v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ be extensional and suppose $\llbracket v \subseteq u \rrbracket = 1$. Define $v'$ by:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathcal{D}(v') = \mathcal{D}(u)$,
\item $(\forall x \in \mathcal{D}(u))\; v'(x) = \llbracket x \in v \rrbracket$.
\end{enumerate}
Then $\llbracket v = v' \rrbracket = 1$.
