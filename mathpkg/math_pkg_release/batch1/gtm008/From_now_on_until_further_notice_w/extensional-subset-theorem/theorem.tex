Let $v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ be extensional, $u \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$, and $b = \llbracket v \subseteq u \rrbracket$. Define $v' = b \cdot v$. Then:

\text{1.}\quad $v'$ is extensional,

\text{2.}\quad $\llbracket v' \subseteq u \rrbracket = 1$,

\text{3.}\quad $\llbracket v \subseteq u \rrbracket = \llbracket v' = v \rrbracket$.
