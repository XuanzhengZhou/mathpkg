Suppose $\{u_i \mid i \in I\} \subseteq V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ and $(\forall i \in I)[\mathcal{D}(u_i) \subseteq d]$ for some $d \subseteq V^{(\mathbf{B})}$. Then there is a family $\{u'_i \mid i \in I\}$ such that

\text{1.}\quad (\forall i \in I)[\llbracket u_i = u'_i \rrbracket = 1],

\text{2.}\quad (\forall i \in I)[\mathcal{D}(u'_i) = d].
