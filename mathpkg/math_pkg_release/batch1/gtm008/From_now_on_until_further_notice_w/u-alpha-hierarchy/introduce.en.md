The $U_\alpha^{(B)}$ hierarchy is an alternative cumulative hierarchy for the Boolean-valued universe where each $u \in U^{(B)}$ is a function from the full $U_\alpha^{(B)}$ into $B$, rather than merely from a subset as in $V^{(B)}$. Each $U_{\alpha+1}^{(B)}$ is the full function space $B^{U_\alpha^{(B)}}$, making the domain structure simpler at the cost of larger domains. The $U$ and $V$ hierarchies are essentially equivalent.
