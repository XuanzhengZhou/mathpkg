For each formula $\varphi$ with one free variable,
$$(\exists v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})})\; \llbracket (\exists u) \varphi(u) \rrbracket = \llbracket \varphi(v) \rrbracket.$$
That is, for each formula $\varphi$ there exists some $v \in V^{(\mathbf{B})}$ such that the truth value of "$(\exists u)\varphi(u)$" equals the truth value of $\varphi(v)$.
