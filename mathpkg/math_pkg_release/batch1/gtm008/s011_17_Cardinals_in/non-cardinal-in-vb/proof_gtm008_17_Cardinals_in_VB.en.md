We have

$$\neg \text{Card}(\alpha) \rightarrow (\exists f)(\exists \beta < \alpha)[f: \beta \rightarrow \alpha \land \mathcal{W}(f)^* = \alpha].$$

Therefore $\neg \text{Card}(\alpha) \rightarrow (\exists f)\phi(f, \alpha)$ where $\phi(f, \alpha)$ is a bounded formula. Thus, by Corollary 13.18, if $\alpha$ is not a cardinal,

$$[\phi(f, \check{\alpha})] = 1$$

for some $f \in V$, hence

$$[\neg \text{Card}(\check{\alpha})] = 1.$$
