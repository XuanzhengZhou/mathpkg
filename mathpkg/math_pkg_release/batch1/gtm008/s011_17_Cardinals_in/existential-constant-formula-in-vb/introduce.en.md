This corollary of Theorem 17.8 provides a reduction for computing the Boolean truth value of existential statements over constant functions in $V^{(B)}$. Instead of quantifying over all elements of $V^{(B)}$ that satisfy $\text{Const}(u)$, one can sum the Boolean values of $\varphi(\check{x})$ as $x$ ranges over the constructible universe $L$. This is the Boolean-valued analogue of the fact that the only constants in the model are the check-names of constructible sets.
