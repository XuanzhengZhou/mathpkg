This corollary states that under the countable chain condition (c.c.c.) on the complete Boolean algebra $B$, all cardinals are absolute between $V$ and $V^{(B)}$. In other words, the Boolean-valued model $V^{(B)}$ does not collapse any cardinals when $B$ satisfies the c.c.c. This is a fundamental result connecting chain conditions to cardinal preservation in Boolean-valued forcing.
