Theorem 3.22 is a critical property of locally compact Hausdorff spaces: given any point in an open set, one can find a smaller open neighborhood whose closure is still contained within the original open set. This "nesting" of closures inside open sets is the main technical tool used in the proof of the Baire Category Theorem.
