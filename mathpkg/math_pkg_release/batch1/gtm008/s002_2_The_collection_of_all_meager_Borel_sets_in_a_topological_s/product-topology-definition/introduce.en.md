Definition 3.31 names the topology generated by the base defined in Theorem 3.30 as the product topology (also known as the Tychonoff topology) on the Cartesian product of an indexed family of topological spaces. This is the coarsest topology making all projection maps continuous, and it is the standard topology used for product spaces in general topology.
