Let $S$ be a family of pairwise disjoint nonzero elements of $B$, where $B$ satisfies c.c.c. Consider the collection of all finite intersections derived from the Boolean structure of $S$. Let $U_n$ range over a maximal antichain property. Then:
$$(\forall A, B \in S) \left[ [U_n \subseteq A] \wedge [U_n \subseteq B] \rightarrow A = B \right].$$
Therefore $S$ is countable.
