A topological space $\langle X, T \rangle$ is

1. a Hausdorff space iff
   $$(\forall a, b \in X) [a \neq b \rightarrow (\exists N(a)) (\exists N'(b)) [N(a) \cap N'(b) = 0]],$$

2. a compact space iff $X$ is a compact set,

3. a locally compact space iff
   $$\forall a \in X, \exists N(a) \text{ such that } N(a)^- \text{ is compact}.$$
