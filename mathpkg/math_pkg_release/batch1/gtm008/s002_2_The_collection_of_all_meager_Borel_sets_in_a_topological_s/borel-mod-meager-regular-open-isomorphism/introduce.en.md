Theorem 3.24 is the culmination of the Baire category development: in a locally compact Hausdorff space, the quotient of the Borel $\sigma$-algebra by the $\sigma$-ideal of meager Borel sets is isomorphic to the complete Boolean algebra of regular open sets. The isomorphism sends each regular open set $G$ to its equivalence class $G/I$. Well-definedness and injectivity follow from the Baire Category Theorem; surjectivity follows from Corollary 3.11.
