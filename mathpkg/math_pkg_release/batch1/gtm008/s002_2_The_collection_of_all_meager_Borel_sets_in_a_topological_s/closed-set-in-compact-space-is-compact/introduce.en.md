If $A$ is a closed subset of a compact space, then $A$ itself is compact. The proof appends to any open cover of $A$ the open set $X - A$ to obtain a cover of $X$, extracts a finite subcover by compactness of $X$, and discards $X - A$ to obtain a finite subcover of $A$.
