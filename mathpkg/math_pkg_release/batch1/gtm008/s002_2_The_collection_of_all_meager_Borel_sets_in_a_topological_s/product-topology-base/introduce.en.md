Theorem 3.30 constructs the product topology on an indexed family of topological spaces. The base consists of "basic open boxes": subsets $B$ of the product where finitely many coordinates are restricted to open sets in their respective factor spaces, while all other coordinates range over the whole factor. The proof verifies the two defining properties of a topological base.
