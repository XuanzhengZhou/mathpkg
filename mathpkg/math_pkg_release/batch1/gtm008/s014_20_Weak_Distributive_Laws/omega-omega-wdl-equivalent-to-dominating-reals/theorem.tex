$B$ satisfies the $(\omega, \omega)$-WDL if and only if
$$
\llbracket (\forall g \in (\omega^{\omega})^{\vee}) (\exists f \in (\omega^{\omega})^{\vee}) (\forall n < \check{\omega}) [g(n) \leq f(n)] \rrbracket = 1,
$$
where the quantifier $(\exists f \in (\omega^{\omega})^{\vee})$ ranges over the canonical check of the ground model functions.
