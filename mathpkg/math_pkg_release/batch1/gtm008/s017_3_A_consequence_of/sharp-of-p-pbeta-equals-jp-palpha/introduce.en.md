Theorem 24.5 computes the $\#$-projection of a basic open set $[p]_{P_\beta}$ in $P_\beta$ onto the complete subalgebra $B_\alpha$. The result is simply $[j(p)]_{P_\alpha}$, the basic open set determined by the projected condition in $P_\alpha$. This theorem follows from the definition of $\#$ as the infimum of all elements in $B_\alpha$ that dominate the given element, combined with the properties of $j$ established in Theorem 24.4. This computation is fundamental for the Easton pair construction.
