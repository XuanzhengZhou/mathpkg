By the definition of $\#$ (see Remark following Theorem 22.9):
$$\#([p]_{P_\beta}) = \inf \{b \in B_\alpha \mid [p]_{P_\beta} \leq i(b)\}$$
$$= \inf \{b \in B_\alpha \mid [p]_{P_\beta} \subseteq i(b)\}.$$

Since $j$ is open, continuous, and onto, and by Theorem 24.4 we have $j''[p]_{P_\beta} = [j(p)]_{P_\alpha}$, it follows that the infimum is exactly $[j(p)]_{P_\alpha}$. The complete monomorphism $i$ is induced by $j$, so $i([j(p)]_{P_\alpha})$ is the smallest element of $i(B_\alpha)$ containing $[p]_{P_\beta}$.
