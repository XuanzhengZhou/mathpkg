This lemma states that the Boolean-valued universe over a Boolean algebra $B$ is contained in the Boolean-valued universe over its completion $\tilde{B}$. This is a fundamental embedding result that allows one to pass from a Boolean-valued model over $B$ to one over its completion while preserving all elements. The proof proceeds by induction on the least ordinal $\alpha$ such that $u \in V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$, showing that membership in $V_{\alpha}^{(B)}$ implies membership in $V^{(\tilde{B})}$.
