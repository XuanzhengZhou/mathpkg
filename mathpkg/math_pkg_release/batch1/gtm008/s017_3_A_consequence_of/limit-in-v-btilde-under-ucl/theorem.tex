Let $\tilde{B}$ be the completion of $B$. If $\tilde{B}$ satisfies the UCL, then
$$(\forall v \in V^{(\tilde{B})})(\exists \{u_\alpha \mid \alpha \in On\} \subseteq V^{(B)})[\lim_{\alpha \in On} u_\alpha = v],$$
i.e., every $v \in V^{(\tilde{B})}$ can be obtained as the limit of elements of $V^{(B)}$.
