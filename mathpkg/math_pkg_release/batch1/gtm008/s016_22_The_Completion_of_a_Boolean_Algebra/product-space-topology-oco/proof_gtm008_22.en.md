The verification that $\langle X, T \rangle$ is a topological space follows from the definition of the open base. That $p_{\alpha}$ is an open continuous onto map is immediate from the construction of $T$. The identity $p_{\alpha\beta} \circ p_{\beta} = p_{\alpha}$ follows directly from the definition of the projections.
