Under the same conditions as in the preceding theorem, let $B_{\alpha}$ be the complete Boolean algebra of regular open sets in $P_{\alpha}$, let $B = \bigcup_{\alpha < \kappa} B_{\alpha}$ and let $\tilde{B}$ be the completion of $B$. Moreover, if $B_{\alpha}$ is a complete subalgebra of $B_{\beta}$ for $\alpha \leq \beta < \kappa$, then $\bigcup_{\alpha < \kappa} B_{\alpha}$ forms a Boolean algebra under operations inherited from sufficiently large $B_{\alpha}$.$$
