Under the assumptions that $B_{\alpha}$ is a complete subalgebra of $B_{\beta}$ for $\alpha \leq \beta < \kappa$, the union $\bigcup_{\alpha < \kappa} B_{\alpha}$ becomes a Boolean algebra by defining operations in $B_{\alpha}$ where $\alpha$ is the least ordinal such that all operands belong to $B_{\alpha}$.
