1 and 2 are obvious from the definition.

3. Suppose $\sum_{\alpha} b_{\alpha} < b$. Since $\tilde{B}$ is the completion of $\mathbf{B}$, there exists $p \in B$ such that

$$0 < p \leq b - \sum_{\alpha \in \mathrm{On}} b_{\alpha}$$

(by the density property). Therefore $(\exists \alpha)[p \in B \cap R(\alpha)]$ and hence $p \leq b_{\alpha}$. This is a contradiction.
