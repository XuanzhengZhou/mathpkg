There are two complete monomorphisms $j: B_{P_0} \rightarrow B$ and $j_1: B_{P_1} \rightarrow B$ induced by the regular open algebra embeddings. Therefore, for all $b_0 \in B_0$ and $b_1 \in B_1$,

$$\#(b_1) \leq b_0 \leftrightarrow b_1 \leq i(b_0) \leftrightarrow f(b_1) \leq b_0,$$

which gives $\#(b_1) = f(b_1)$.
