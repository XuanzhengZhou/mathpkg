Let $P_1 = \langle P_1, \leq_1 \rangle$ and $P_0 = \langle P_0, \leq_0 \rangle$ be partial order structures and $p: P_1 \rightarrow P_0$ be o.c.o. Then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item For every $x, y \in P_1$,
$$x \leq_1 y \rightarrow p(x) \leq_0 p(y).$$
\item $p``[x]_{P_1} = [p(x)]_{P_0}$.
\end{enumerate}
