1. Suppose $x \leq_1 y$. Then $x \in [y]_{P_1} \subseteq (p^{-1})``[p(y)]_{P_0}$ (since $p$ is continuous and $[y]_{P_1}$ is the smallest neighborhood of $y$). Therefore

$$p(x) \in [p(y)]_{P_0}.$$

2. $p``[x]_{P_1} \supseteq [p(x)]_{P_0}$ since $p$ is an open map. The reverse inclusion follows from 1. Therefore $p``[x]_{P_1} = [p(x)]_{P_0}$.
