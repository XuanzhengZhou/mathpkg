Let $\kappa$ be a cardinal or $\mathrm{On}$. Let $P_0 \subseteq P_1 \subseteq \cdots \subseteq P_{\alpha} \subseteq \cdots$ ($\alpha < \kappa$) be a normal limiting system. If $x \in P_{\alpha}$, $y \in P_{\beta}$, $\alpha < \beta < \kappa$, then

$$\mathrm{Comp}(x, p_{\alpha\beta}(y)) \leftrightarrow \mathrm{Comp}(x, y).$$
