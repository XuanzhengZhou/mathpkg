Let $\kappa > \omega$ be a regular cardinal and let $B_0 \subseteq B_1 \subseteq \cdots \subseteq B_{\alpha} \subseteq \cdots$ ($\alpha < \kappa$) be a direct system of complete Boolean algebras such that $B_{\alpha}$ is a completion of $\bigcup_{\beta < \alpha} B_{\beta}$ for every limit ordinal $\alpha < \kappa$. Define $B = \bigcup_{\alpha < \kappa} B_{\alpha}$ and $\bar{B}$ to be the completion of $B$. If $(\forall \alpha < \kappa)[|B_{\alpha}| < \kappa]$, then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $B$ satisfies the $\kappa$-chain condition.
\item $\bar{B} = B$.
\end{enumerate}
