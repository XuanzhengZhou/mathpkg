Define $C_{\alpha}$ ($\alpha \leq \kappa$) and $C$ as follows:

\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $C_0 = \{b \in B_0 \mid b > 0\}$.
\item[(ii)] $C_{\alpha+1} = \{b \in B_{\alpha+1} \mid \#_{\alpha}(b) \in C_{\alpha}\}$.
\item[(iii)] $C_{\alpha} = \bigcup_{\beta < \alpha} C_{\beta}$ for every limit ordinal $\alpha$.
\item[(iv)] $C = C_{\kappa}$.
\end{itemize}

Define $P_{\alpha} = \langle C_{\alpha}, \leq \rangle$ and apply the preceding theorem (Theorem 22.30).
