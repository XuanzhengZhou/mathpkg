For all $\alpha, \beta \in H^*$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $[L_{\alpha} L_{\beta}] \subset L_{\alpha+\beta}$.
\item If $x \in L_{\alpha}$ and $\alpha \neq 0$, then $\mathrm{ad} x$ is nilpotent.
\item If $\alpha+\beta \neq 0$, then $L_{\alpha}$ is orthogonal to $L_{\beta}$ relative to the Killing form $\kappa$ of $L$.
\end{enumerate}
