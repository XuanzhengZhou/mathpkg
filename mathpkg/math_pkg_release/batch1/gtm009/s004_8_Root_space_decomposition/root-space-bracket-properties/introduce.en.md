This proposition establishes the fundamental structural properties of the root space decomposition of a semisimple Lie algebra relative to a maximal toral subalgebra. The bracket of two root spaces lies in the root space corresponding to the sum of the roots. Moreover, nonzero root elements are ad-nilpotent, and distinct root spaces not opposite to each other are orthogonal under the Killing form.
