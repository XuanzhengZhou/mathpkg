Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra over an algebraically closed field $F$ of characteristic $0$, $H$ a maximal toral subalgebra of $L$, and $\Phi \subset H^*$ the set of roots of $L$ relative to $H$. Let $E_{\mathbb{Q}}$ be the $\mathbb{Q}$-span of $\Phi$ in $H^*$, and $E = \mathbb{R} \otimes_{\mathbb{Q}} E_{\mathbb{Q}}$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $\dim_{\mathbb{R}} E = \dim_F H^* = \ell$.
\item[(b)] The symmetric bilinear form $(\lambda, \mu) = \kappa(t_\lambda, t_\mu)$ extends to a positive definite form on $E$, making $E$ a euclidean space.
\item[(c)] $\Phi$ is a root system in $E$ (in the sense of Section 9.2), with Weyl group $\mathcal{W}$.
\end{enumerate}
