This theorem is the culmination of Section 8, establishing that the set $\Phi$ of roots of a semisimple Lie algebra relative to a maximal toral subalgebra satisfies the axioms of a root system. The rational span $E_{\mathbb{Q}}$ of the roots is an $\ell$-dimensional rational vector space, and extending scalars to $\mathbb{R}$ yields a euclidean space $E$ with a positive definite inner product derived from the Killing form. The axioms (R1)-(R4) are verified using Propositions 8.3 and 8.4. This connects the structure theory of Lie algebras to the axiomatic theory of root systems developed in Chapter III.
