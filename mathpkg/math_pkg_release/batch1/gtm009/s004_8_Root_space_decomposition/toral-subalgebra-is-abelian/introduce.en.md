A toral subalgebra of a semisimple Lie algebra consists entirely of semisimple elements. Since each element of a toral subalgebra is ad-diagonalizable over an algebraically closed field, the subalgebra must be abelian. This lemma is roughly analogous to Engel's Theorem for nilpotent subalgebras, establishing that the maximal toral subalgebras provide the correct Cartan subalgebras for studying the structure of semisimple Lie algebras.
