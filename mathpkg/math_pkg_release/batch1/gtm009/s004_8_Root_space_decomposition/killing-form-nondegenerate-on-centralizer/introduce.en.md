This corollary follows from the fact that the Killing form is nondegenerate on all of $L$ (Cartan's criterion) and that $L_0 = C_L(H)$ is orthogonal to all nonzero root spaces under the Killing form. The nondegeneracy of the Killing form restricted to the centralizer is a crucial step in proving that $H$ itself equals its centralizer, establishing $H$ as a Cartan subalgebra.
