Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra over a field of characteristic 0. Then every finite-dimensional representation $\phi: L \rightarrow \mathfrak{gl}(V)$ is completely reducible, i.e., $V$ is a direct sum of irreducible $L$-submodules. Equivalently, every $L$-submodule of $V$ admits a complementary $L$-submodule.
