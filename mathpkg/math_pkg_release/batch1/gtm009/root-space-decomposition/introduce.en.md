The root space decomposition expresses a semisimple Lie algebra as the direct sum of a Cartan subalgebra $H$ and one-dimensional root spaces $L_\alpha$ indexed by roots $\alpha$ in the dual of $H$. This decomposition generalizes the eigenspace decomposition of $\mathfrak{sl}(2)$ and is the foundation for the entire classification theory. The set $\Phi$ of roots forms an abstract root system, which is completely classified by Dynkin diagrams.
