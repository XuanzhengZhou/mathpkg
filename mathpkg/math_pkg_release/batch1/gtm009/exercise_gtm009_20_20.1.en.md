If $V$ is an arbitrary $L$-module generated by a maximal vector $v^+$ of weight $\lambda$, prove that $V$ is standard cyclic of weight $\lambda$. Conclude that every homomorphic image of a standard cyclic module is standard cyclic of the same weight, and that the irreducible quotient of a standard cyclic module is uniquely determined by $\lambda$.
