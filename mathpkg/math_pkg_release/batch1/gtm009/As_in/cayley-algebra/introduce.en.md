The Cayley algebra $\mathfrak{C}$ is the 8-dimensional alternative (but non-associative) algebra over $F$, also known as the octonions. It is constructed from the field $F$ and the 3-dimensional space $F^3$ equipped with dot and cross products. The commutator subspace $\mathfrak{C}_o$ has codimension 1 inside $\mathfrak{C}$ and is complementary to the identity element. The derivation algebra of $\mathfrak{C}$ yields the exceptional simple Lie algebra of type $G_2$.
