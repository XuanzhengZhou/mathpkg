Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra, $H$ a fixed Cartan subalgebra, and $V$ an $L$-module. For $\lambda \in H^*$, the \text{weight space} $V_\lambda$ is defined by
$$V_\lambda = \{v \in V \mid h \cdot v = \lambda(h)v \text{ for all } h \in H\}.$$
When $V_\lambda \neq 0$, it is called a weight space, and $\lambda$ is called a \text{weight} of $V$ (a weight of $H$ on $V$). If $\dim V < \infty$, then $H$ acts diagonally on $V$ and $V = \bigoplus V_\lambda$.
