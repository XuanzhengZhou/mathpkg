Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra with Cartan subalgebra $H$ and base $\Delta$. An $L$-module $V$ is called \text{standard cyclic} (of weight $\lambda \in H^*$) if $V = \mathfrak{U}(L) \cdot v^+$ for some maximal vector $v^+$ of weight $\lambda$. In this case $\lambda$ is called the \text{highest weight} of $V$.
