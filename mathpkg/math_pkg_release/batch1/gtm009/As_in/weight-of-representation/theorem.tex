Let $V$ be an $L$-module for a semisimple Lie algebra $L$ with Cartan subalgebra $H$. A linear functional $\lambda \in H^*$ is called a \text{weight} of $V$ if the weight space $V_\lambda = \{v \in V \mid h \cdot v = \lambda(h)v \text{ for all } h \in H\}$ is nonzero. The set of all weights of $V$ is denoted by $\Pi(V)$.
