Let $V$ be a standard cyclic $L$-module generated by a maximal vector $v^+$ of weight $\lambda$. Then $\lambda$ is called the \text{highest weight} of $V$. With respect to the partial order $\lambda \succ \mu$ iff $\lambda - \mu$ is a sum of positive roots, every weight $\mu$ of $V$ satisfies $\mu \preceq \lambda$.
