The classical Lie algebras $B_\ell$ ($\ell \geq 2$), $C_\ell$ ($\ell \geq 3$), and $D_\ell$ ($\ell \geq 4$) are semisimple. In each case the $\ell$-dimensional subalgebra $H$ spanned by diagonal matrices is a maximal toral subalgebra (hence a Cartan subalgebra), equal to its own centralizer in $L$.
