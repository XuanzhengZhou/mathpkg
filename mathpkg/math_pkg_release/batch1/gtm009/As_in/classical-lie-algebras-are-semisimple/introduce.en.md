This result establishes the semisimplicity of the classical families $B_\ell, C_\ell, D_\ell$ using the criterion of Proposition 19.1. The proof reduces semisimplicity to two verifications: that each algebra consists of trace-zero endomorphisms, and that each acts irreducibly in its natural representation. Irreducibility is shown by generating all matrix units from the identity and the algebra using ordinary matrix multiplication.
