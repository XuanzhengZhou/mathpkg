A parabolic subalgebra is a subalgebra of a Lie algebra that contains a Borel subalgebra. In the semisimple case, parabolic subalgebras correspond to subsets of a base of the root system and generalize Borel subalgebras. The standard parabolic $P(\Delta')$ is generated by the Cartan subalgebra together with root spaces for simple roots and negatives of selected simple roots. Parabolic subalgebras are self-normalizing and provide a natural filtration between Borel subalgebras and the full Lie algebra. They play a crucial role in representation theory and the geometry of flag varieties.
