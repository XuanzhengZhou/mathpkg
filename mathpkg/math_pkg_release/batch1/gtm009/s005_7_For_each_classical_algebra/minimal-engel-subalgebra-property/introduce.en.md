This lemma is a crucial technical tool in the structure theory of Cartan subalgebras. It states that if an Engel subalgebra $L_0(\operatorname{ad} z)$ is chosen to be minimal among all Engel subalgebras coming from elements of a subalgebra $K$, and if $K$ is contained in this minimal Engel subalgebra, then this Engel subalgebra is contained in all other Engel subalgebras $L_0(\operatorname{ad} x)$ for $x \in K$. Together with Lemma B of (15.2), this property is used to prove that Cartan subalgebras are precisely the minimal Engel subalgebras.
