This theorem provides the fundamental characterization of Cartan subalgebras: they are exactly the Engel subalgebras that are minimal with respect to inclusion. The proof uses Lemma A and Lemma B of (15.2) together with Engel's Theorem to establish both directions. A key consequence is that Cartan subalgebras always exist in Lie algebras over algebraically closed fields, since minimal Engel subalgebras always exist by finite dimensionality.
