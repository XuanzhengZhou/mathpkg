This lemma provides a lifting property for Cartan subalgebras through epimorphisms. Given a CSA $H'$ in the quotient $L'$, any CSA $H$ of the preimage $K = \phi^{-1}(H')$ is automatically a CSA of the whole algebra $L$. This result is crucial for inductive arguments in the conjugacy proof of Chapter 16, where one needs to relate CSAs of a Lie algebra to those of its quotients and subalgebras.
