Let $L = \mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$, with standard basis $(x, h, y)$. For $c \in \mathbb{F}$, write $x(c) = \exp \operatorname{ad}(cx)$, $y(c) = \exp \operatorname{ad}(cy)$. Define inner automorphisms:

$$w(c) = x(c) \, y(-c^{-1}) \, x(c), \quad h(c) = w(c) \, w(1)^{-1} \quad (= w(c) \, w(-1)),$$

for $c \neq 0$. Compute the matrices of $w(c)$, $h(c)$ relative to the given basis of $L$, and deduce that all diagonal automorphisms (16.5) of $L$ are inner. Conclude in this case that $\operatorname{Aut} L = \operatorname{Int} L = \mathcal{E}(L)$.
