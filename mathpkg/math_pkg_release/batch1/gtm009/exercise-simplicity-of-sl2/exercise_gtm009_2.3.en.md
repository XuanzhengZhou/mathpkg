Suppose $\dim L = 3$, $L = [LL]$. Prove that $L$ must be simple. [Observe first that any homomorphic image of $L$ also equals its derived algebra.] Recover the simplicity of $\mathfrak{sl}(2, F)$, $\text{char } F \neq 2$.
