Let $V$ be any finite dimensional $L$-module, $L = \mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$. Then the eigenvalues of $h$ on $V$ are all integers, and each occurs along with its negative (an equal number of times). Moreover, in any decomposition of $V$ into a direct sum of irreducible submodules, the number of summands is precisely $\dim V_0 + \dim V_1$.
