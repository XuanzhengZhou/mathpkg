Apply Theorem 6.4 to the semisimple Lie algebra $\phi(L) \subset \mathfrak{gl}(V)$. (The image of a semisimple Lie algebra under a representation is again semisimple, being a quotient of $L$ by an ideal.) The abstract Jordan decomposition in $\phi(L)$ corresponds, via the isomorphism $L/\ker \phi \cong \phi(L)$, to the image under $\phi$ of the abstract Jordan decomposition in $L$. By Theorem 6.4, $\phi(L)$ contains the semisimple and nilpotent parts in $\mathfrak{gl}(V)$ of each of its elements, which yields the result.
