Since $L$ is semisimple, $L = [L, L]$ (Lemma 5.2). For any $x, y \in L$, $\operatorname{Tr}(\phi([x,y])) = \operatorname{Tr}([\phi(x), \phi(y)]) = 0$. Therefore $\phi(L) = \phi([L,L]) = [\phi(L), \phi(L)]$ consists of traceless endomorphisms, i.e., $\phi(L) \subset \mathfrak{sl}(V)$. For a one-dimensional module, $\mathfrak{sl}(V) = 0$, so $L$ acts trivially.
