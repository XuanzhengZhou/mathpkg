Let $V$ be a finite dimensional vector space over $\mathbb{F}$, and let $L \subset \mathfrak{gl}(V)$ be a semisimple Lie algebra. Then $L$ contains the semisimple and nilpotent parts of all its elements. In other words, for each $x \in L$, the abstract Jordan decomposition $x = x_s + x_n$ in $\mathfrak{gl}(V)$ has both components $x_s, x_n$ also lying in $L$.
