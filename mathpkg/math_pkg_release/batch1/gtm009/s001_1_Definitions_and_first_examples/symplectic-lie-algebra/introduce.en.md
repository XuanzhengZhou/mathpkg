The symplectic Lie algebra $\mathfrak{sp}(2\ell, F)$ is the Lie algebra of type $C_\ell$ in the Cartan-Killing classification. It consists of linear transformations that are skew-adjoint with respect to a non-degenerate alternating bilinear form. As a linear Lie algebra, it is a subalgebra of $\mathfrak{gl}(2\ell, F)$ characterized by the matrix condition $sx = -x^t s$. The symplectic algebras, together with the special linear and orthogonal algebras, form the four infinite families of classical simple Lie algebras.
