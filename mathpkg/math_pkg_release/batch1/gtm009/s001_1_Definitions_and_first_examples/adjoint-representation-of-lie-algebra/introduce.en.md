The adjoint representation $\operatorname{ad}: L \to \mathfrak{gl}(L)$ maps each element $x$ to the linear transformation $\operatorname{ad} x$ given by bracketing with $x$. It is a Lie algebra homomorphism whose kernel is the center of $L$. The adjoint representation is the canonical representation that every Lie algebra carries on itself.
