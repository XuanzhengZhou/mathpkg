The commutation relations $[e_{ij}, e_{kl}] = \delta_{jk}e_{il} - \delta_{li}e_{kj}$ describe the Lie bracket on the standard basis $\{e_{ij}\}$ of $\mathfrak{gl}(n, F)$. These formulas show that the structure constants of the general linear Lie algebra are all 0 or $\pm 1$, reflecting the combinatorial simplicity of matrix multiplication. These relations are fundamental for explicit computations in $\mathfrak{gl}(n, F)$ and its subalgebras.
