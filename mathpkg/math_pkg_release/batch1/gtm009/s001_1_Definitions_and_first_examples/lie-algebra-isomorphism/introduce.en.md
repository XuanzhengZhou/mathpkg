An isomorphism of Lie algebras is a vector space isomorphism that additionally preserves the bracket structure. This is the natural notion of "sameness" for Lie algebras: two isomorphic Lie algebras have identical algebraic properties. The condition $\phi([xy]) = [\phi(x)\phi(y)]$ expresses that the bracket commutes with the isomorphism.
