This exercise asks to verify that the given commutation relations on a 3-dimensional vector space define a Lie algebra. The relations $[xy]=z$, $[xz]=y$, $[yz]=x$ (together with those forced by bilinearity and anticommutativity) must be checked against the Jacobi identity. The resulting Lie algebra is $\mathfrak{sl}(2)$ (or $\mathfrak{so}(3)$ depending on the field).
