The symplectic algebra $\mathfrak{sp}(2\ell, F)$ is the Lie algebra of endomorphisms of a symplectic vector space that are skew-adjoint with respect to the symplectic form. It is one of the four classical families of Lie algebras (type $C_\ell$). Its dimension is $2\ell^2 + \ell$, and every element has trace zero.
