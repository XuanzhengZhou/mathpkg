The structure constants of a Lie algebra with respect to a basis encode the bracket operation. The axioms (L2) (alternativity) and (L3) (Jacobi identity) translate into algebraic identities on the structure constants: antisymmetry and a cyclic sum condition. These identities are necessary and sufficient for a set of scalars to define a Lie algebra structure.
