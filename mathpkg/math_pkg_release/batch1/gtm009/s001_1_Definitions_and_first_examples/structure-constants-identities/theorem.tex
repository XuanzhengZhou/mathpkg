Let $L$ be a Lie algebra with basis $\{x_1, \ldots, x_n\}$. Define the \text{structure constants} $a_{ij}^k$ by
$$[x_i, x_j] = \sum_k a_{ij}^k x_k.$$

The axioms (L2) and (L3) impose the following identities on the scalars $\{a_{ij}^k\}$:
$$a_{ii}^k = 0 = a_{ij}^k + a_{ji}^k,$$
$$\sum_k (a_{ij}^k a_{k\ell}^m + a_{j\ell}^k a_{ki}^m + a_{\ell i}^k a_{kj}^m) = 0.$$
