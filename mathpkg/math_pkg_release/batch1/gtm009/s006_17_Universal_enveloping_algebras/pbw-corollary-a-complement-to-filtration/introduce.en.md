Corollary A of the PBW theorem provides a concrete way to find complements for the natural filtration of the universal enveloping algebra. If a subspace $W$ of the $m$-th tensor power maps isomorphically onto the $m$-th symmetric power, then its image under the canonical projection $\pi: \mathfrak{T}(L) \to \mathfrak{U}(L)$ gives a complement to $U_{m-1}$ in $U_m$. This is the key to obtaining the PBW basis.
