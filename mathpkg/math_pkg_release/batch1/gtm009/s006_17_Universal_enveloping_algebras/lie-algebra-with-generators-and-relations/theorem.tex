Let $L$ be the free Lie algebra on a set $X$, and let $R$ be the ideal of $L$ generated by elements $f_j$ ($j$ running over some index set). The quotient algebra $L/R$ is called the \text{Lie algebra with generators} $x_i$ \text{and relations} $f_j = 0$, where $x_i$ are the images in $L/R$ of the elements of $X$.
