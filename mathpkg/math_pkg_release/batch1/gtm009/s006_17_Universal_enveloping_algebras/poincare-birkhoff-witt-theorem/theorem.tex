Let $L$ be a Lie algebra over $F$, $\mathfrak{U}(L)$ its universal enveloping algebra with the canonical filtration $U_0 \subset U_1 \subset \dots$, and let $\mathfrak{G} = \bigoplus_{m=0}^{\infty} G^m$ be the associated graded algebra, where $G^m = U_m / U_{m-1}$ (with $U_{-1} = 0$). Let $\mathfrak{S} = \mathfrak{S}(L)$ be the symmetric algebra on the vector space $L$. Then there is a canonical homomorphism $\omega: \mathfrak{S} \to \mathfrak{G}$ of graded algebras, and $\omega$ is an isomorphism.
