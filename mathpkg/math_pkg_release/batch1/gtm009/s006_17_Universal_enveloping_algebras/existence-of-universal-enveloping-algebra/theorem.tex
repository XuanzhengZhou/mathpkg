Let $L$ be a Lie algebra over $F$, let $\mathfrak{T}(L)$ be its tensor algebra, and let $J$ be the two-sided ideal of $\mathfrak{T}(L)$ generated by all elements
$$
x \otimes y - y \otimes x - [xy] \quad (x, y \in L).
$$
Define $\mathfrak{U}(L) = \mathfrak{T}(L)/J$, and let $\pi: \mathfrak{T}(L) \to \mathfrak{U}(L)$ be the canonical homomorphism. Let $i: L \to \mathfrak{U}(L)$ be the restriction of $\pi$ to $L = T^1L$. Then $(\mathfrak{U}(L), i)$ is a universal enveloping algebra of $L$.
