Let $V$ be a vector space over $F$, and let $I$ be the two-sided ideal of $\mathfrak{T}(V)$ generated by all elements
$$
x \otimes y - y \otimes x \quad (x, y \in V).
$$
The \text{symmetric algebra} on $V$ is the quotient algebra
$$
\mathfrak{S}(V) = \mathfrak{T}(V)/I.
$$
It inherits a grading $\mathfrak{S}(V) = \prod_{i=0}^{\infty} S^iV$, where $S^iV$ is the image of $T^iV$ under the canonical projection.
