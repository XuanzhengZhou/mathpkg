A Chevalley basis is a basis of a semisimple Lie algebra with respect to which all structure constants are integers. This remarkable basis, discovered by Claude Chevalley, allows the construction of Lie algebras over arbitrary fields (including finite fields) from the root system data. The Chevalley basis is the foundation for the theory of Chevalley groups and finite simple groups of Lie type.
