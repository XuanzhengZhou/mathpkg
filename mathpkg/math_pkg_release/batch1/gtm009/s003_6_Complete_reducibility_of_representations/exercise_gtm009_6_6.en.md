Let $L$ be a simple Lie algebra. Let $\beta(x,y)$ and $\gamma(x,y)$ be two symmetric associative bilinear forms on $L$. If $\beta, \gamma$ are nondegenerate, prove that $\beta$ and $\gamma$ are proportional. [Use Schur's Lemma.]
