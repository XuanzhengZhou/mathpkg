$M = \mathfrak{sl}(3, \mathbb{F})$ contains a copy of $L = \mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$ in its upper left-hand $2 \times 2$ position. Write $M$ as direct sum of irreducible $L$-submodules ($M$ viewed as $L$-module via the adjoint representation): $V(0) \oplus V(1) \oplus V(1) \oplus V(2)$.
