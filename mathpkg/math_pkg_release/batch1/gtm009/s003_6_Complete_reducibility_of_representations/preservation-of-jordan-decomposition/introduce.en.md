Theorem 6.4 establishes that the abstract Jordan decomposition in a semisimple Lie algebra is preserved under any finite-dimensional representation. The proof uses Weyl's Theorem (complete reducibility) and Schur's Lemma to show that the semisimple and nilpotent parts of elements in the image remain within $\phi(L)$.
