This lemma gives explicit formulas for the action of the standard basis $(x, h, y)$ of $\mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$ on the basis vectors $v_i$ generated from a maximal vector. These formulas completely determine the structure of any irreducible finite-dimensional $\mathfrak{sl}(2)$-module and reveal that the module is spanned by vectors of weights $\lambda, \lambda-2, \lambda-4, \ldots$.
