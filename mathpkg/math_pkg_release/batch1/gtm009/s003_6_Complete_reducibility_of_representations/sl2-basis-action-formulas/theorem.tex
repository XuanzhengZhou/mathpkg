Let $V$ be an irreducible $L$-module for $L = \mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$. Choose a maximal vector $v_0 \in V_\lambda$ and set $v_{-1} = 0$, $v_i = (1/i!) y^i.v_0$ for $i \geq 0$.

**Lemma (7.2).** For $i \geq 0$:
\begin{align*}
\text{(a)} &\quad h.v_i = (\lambda - 2i)v_i, \\
\text{(b)} &\quad y.v_i = (i+1)v_{i+1}, \\
\text{(c)} &\quad x.v_i = (\lambda - i + 1)v_{i-1}.
\end{align*}
