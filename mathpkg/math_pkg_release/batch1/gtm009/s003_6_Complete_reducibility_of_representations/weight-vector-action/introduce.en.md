This lemma describes how the standard basis elements $x$ (raising operator) and $y$ (lowering operator) of $\mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$ shift weight vectors by $\pm 2$. This $\mathfrak{sl}(2)$-structure provides the fundamental building block for understanding representations of all semisimple Lie algebras.
