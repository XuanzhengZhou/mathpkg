This corollary describes the structure of arbitrary finite-dimensional $\mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$-modules: eigenvalues of $h$ are integers occurring symmetrically about zero, and the number of irreducible components equals the sum of dimensions of the 0-weight and 1-weight spaces.
