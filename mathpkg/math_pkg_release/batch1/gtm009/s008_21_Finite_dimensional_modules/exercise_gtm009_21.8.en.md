Let $\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_\ell$ be the fundamental dominant weights for the root system $\Phi$ of $L$ (13.1). Show how to construct an arbitrary $V(\lambda)$, $\lambda \in \Lambda^+$, as a direct summand in a suitable tensor product of modules $V(\lambda_1), \ldots, V(\lambda_\ell)$ (repetitions allowed).
