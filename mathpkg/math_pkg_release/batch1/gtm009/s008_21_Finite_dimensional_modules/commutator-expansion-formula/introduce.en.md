This combinatorial lemma expresses the commutator of a power $y^k$ with an arbitrary element $z$ in an associative algebra as a sum of iterated commutators multiplied by binomial coefficients. When specialized to the universal enveloping algebra $\mathfrak{U}(L)$ with $y = y_i$ (a Chevalley generator), it provides the key estimate: if a monomial's coset in $V'(\lambda)$ is annihilated by $y_i^k$, then a slightly longer monomial is annihilated by $y_i^{k+3}$, enabling an induction proving local nilpotence of each $y_i$.
