Combining the necessary condition (Theorem 21.1) and the sufficient condition (Theorem 21.2), one obtains a complete classification: the map sending a dominant integral weight $\lambda \in \Lambda^+$ to the isomorphism class of the finite dimensional irreducible module $V(\lambda)$ is a bijection. This is the fundamental classification theorem for finite dimensional representations of a semisimple Lie algebra.
