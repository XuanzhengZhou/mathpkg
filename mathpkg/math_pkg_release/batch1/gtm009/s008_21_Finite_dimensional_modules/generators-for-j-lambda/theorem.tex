Let $\lambda \in \Lambda^+$, $m_i = \langle \lambda, \alpha_i \rangle$ for $1 \leq i \leq \ell$. Let $I(\lambda)$ be the left ideal of $\mathfrak{U}(L)$ generated by all $x_\alpha$ ($\alpha > 0$) and all $h_\alpha - \lambda(h_\alpha) \cdot 1$ ($\alpha \in \Phi$), and let $J(\lambda)$ be the left ideal annihilating a maximal vector of $V(\lambda)$. Then $J(\lambda)$ is generated by $I(\lambda)$ along with all $y_i^{m_i+1}$ ($1 \leq i \leq \ell$).
