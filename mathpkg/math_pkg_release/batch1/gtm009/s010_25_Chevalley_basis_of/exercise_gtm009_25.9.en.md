Prove that for $L$ of type $A_\ell$, the resulting Chevalley group $G(K)$ of adjoint type is isomorphic to $\operatorname{PSL}(\ell+1, K) = \operatorname{SL}(\ell+1, K)$ modulo scalars (the scalars being the $(\ell+1)$st roots of unity in $K$).
