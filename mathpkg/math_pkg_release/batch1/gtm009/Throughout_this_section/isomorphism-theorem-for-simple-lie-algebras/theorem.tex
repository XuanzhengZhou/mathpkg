Let $L, L'$ be simple Lie algebras over an algebraically closed field $\mathbb{F}$ of characteristic $0$, with respective maximal toral subalgebras $H, H'$ and corresponding root systems $\Phi, \Phi'$. Suppose there is an isomorphism of $\Phi$ onto $\Phi'$ (as root systems). Then this isomorphism can be extended to an isomorphism of Lie algebras $\pi: L \to L'$ sending prescribed root vectors $x_\alpha \in L_\alpha$ to prescribed root vectors $x'_{\alpha'} \in L'_{\alpha'}$ ($\alpha \in \Delta$, $\alpha'$ the image of $\alpha$), provided the choices satisfy $[x_\alpha x_\beta] = N_{\alpha,\beta} x_{\alpha+\beta}$ whenever $\alpha+\beta$ is a root, with the same structure constants $N_{\alpha,\beta}$ as in $L'$.
