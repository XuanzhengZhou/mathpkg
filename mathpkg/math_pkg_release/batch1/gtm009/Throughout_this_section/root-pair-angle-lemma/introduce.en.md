This fundamental lemma provides a simple criterion for determining when sums or differences of nonproportional roots are again roots. It follows from the integrality condition (R4) of root system axioms and the classification of possible angles between roots. The result is heavily used throughout the theory of root systems, particularly in studying root strings and the action of the Weyl group.
