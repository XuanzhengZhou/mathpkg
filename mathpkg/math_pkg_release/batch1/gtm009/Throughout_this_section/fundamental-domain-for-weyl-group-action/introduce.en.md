Lemma 10.3B shows that the closure of the fundamental Weyl chamber is a fundamental domain for the action of the Weyl group on the euclidean space $E$: each vector in $E$ is $W$-conjugate to precisely one point in $\overline{\mathfrak{C}(\Delta)}$. This is essential for the theory of weights and the Weyl character formula.
