The **fundamental dominant weights** $\lambda_i$ form the dual basis to the simple coroots. Every weight is an integer linear combination of fundamental weights, and every dominant weight is a nonnegative integer combination. The fundamental weights are the building blocks for the representation theory of semisimple Lie algebras: the irreducible finite-dimensional representation of highest weight $\lambda = \sum m_i \lambda_i$ is parametrized by the nonnegative integers $m_i$.
