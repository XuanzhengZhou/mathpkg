The fundamental dominant weights $\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_\ell$ (relative to a base $\Delta = \{\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_\ell\}$) are defined by the condition $\langle \lambda_i, \alpha_j \rangle = \delta_{ij}$. They form a $\mathbb{Z}$-basis of the weight lattice $\Lambda$, and every dominant weight can be written uniquely as $\sum_{i=1}^\ell m_i \lambda_i$ with $m_i \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}$.
