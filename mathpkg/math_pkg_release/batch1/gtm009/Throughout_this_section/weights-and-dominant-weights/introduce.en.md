**Weights** are vectors in $E$ whose pairing with all roots (via $\langle\cdot, \cdot\rangle$) yields integers. The weight lattice $\Lambda$ contains the root lattice $\Lambda_r$, and the quotient $\Lambda/\Lambda_r$ is the fundamental group. **Dominant weights** are those with nonnegative Cartan integers relative to all simple roots. Dominant weights parametrize finite-dimensional irreducible representations of semisimple Lie algebras.
