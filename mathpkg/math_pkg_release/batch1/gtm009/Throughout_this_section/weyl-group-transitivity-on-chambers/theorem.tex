Let $\Delta$ be a base of $\Phi$ with Weyl group $\mathcal{W}$. Then:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $\mathcal{W}$ acts transitively on Weyl chambers.
\item[(b)] $\mathcal{W}$ acts transitively on bases.
\item[(c)] Every root is $\mathcal{W}$-conjugate to a simple root.
\item[(d)] $\mathcal{W}$ is generated by the simple reflections $\sigma_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \Delta$).
\item[(e)] $\mathcal{W}$ acts simply transitively on bases.
\end{itemize}
