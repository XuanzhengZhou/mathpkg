For all $\alpha, \beta \in H^*$:
(1) $[L_\alpha, L_\beta] \subset L_{\alpha+\beta}$.
(2) If $x \in L_\alpha$, $\alpha \neq 0$, then $\operatorname{ad} x$ is nilpotent.
(3) If $\alpha, \beta \in H^*$ and $\alpha+\beta \neq 0$, then $L_\alpha$ is orthogonal to $L_\beta$ relative to the Killing form $\kappa$ of $L$.
