For any nonzero vector $\alpha$ in a euclidean space $E$, the reflection $\sigma_\alpha$ with reflecting hyperplane $P_\alpha = \{\beta \in E \mid (\beta, \alpha) = 0\}$ is defined by:
$$\sigma_\alpha(\beta) = \beta - \frac{2(\beta, \alpha)}{(\alpha, \alpha)}\alpha = \beta - \langle\beta, \alpha\rangle \alpha,$$
where $\langle\beta, \alpha\rangle = 2(\beta, \alpha)/(\alpha, \alpha)$.
