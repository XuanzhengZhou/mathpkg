Fix an ordering $(\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_\ell)$ of the simple roots. The Cartan matrix is the $\ell \times \ell$ integer matrix $(\langle \alpha_i, \alpha_j \rangle)_{i,j=1}^\ell$, where $\langle \alpha_i, \alpha_j \rangle = 2(\alpha_i, \alpha_j)/(\alpha_j, \alpha_j)$. It satisfies: (i) diagonal entries are $2$; (ii) off-diagonal entries are in $\{0, -1, -2, -3\}$; (iii) $\langle \alpha_i, \alpha_j \rangle = 0$ iff $\langle \alpha_j, \alpha_i \rangle = 0$.
