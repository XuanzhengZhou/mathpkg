For a **maximal toral subalgebra** $H$ of a semisimple Lie algebra $L$, the centralizer $C_L(H)$ equals $H$ itself. This crucial result justifies the use of $H$ for the root space decomposition. The proof proceeds in seven steps, using the abstract Jordan decomposition, Engel's Theorem, and properties of the Killing form.
