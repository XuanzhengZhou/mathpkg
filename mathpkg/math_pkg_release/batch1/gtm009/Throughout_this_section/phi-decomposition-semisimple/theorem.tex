Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra with decomposition into simple ideals $L = L_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus L_t$, maximal toral subalgebra $H$, and root system $\Phi$. Let $H_i = H \cap L_i$ and $\Phi_i$ be the root system of $L_i$ relative to $H_i$. Then $\Phi = \Phi_1 \cup \cdots \cup \Phi_t$ is the decomposition of $\Phi$ into its irreducible components.
