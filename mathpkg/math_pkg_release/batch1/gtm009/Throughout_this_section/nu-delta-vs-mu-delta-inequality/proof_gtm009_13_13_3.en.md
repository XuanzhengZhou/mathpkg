Compute:
$$(\nu + \delta, \nu + \delta) = (\sigma(\nu + \delta), \sigma(\nu + \delta)) = (\mu + \sigma\delta, \mu + \sigma\delta).$$
Since $\sigma\delta \prec \delta$ (as $\sigma$ sends some positive roots to negative ones while $\delta$ is the half-sum of all positive roots), we have $\delta - \sigma\delta$ is a sum of positive roots. Then:
$$(\mu + \delta, \mu + \delta) - (\mu + \sigma\delta, \mu + \sigma\delta) = 2(\mu, \delta - \sigma\delta) + (\delta, \delta) - (\sigma\delta, \sigma\delta) \geq 0,$$
since $(\mu, \delta - \sigma\delta) \geq 0$ (dominant weight paired with positive combination), and $(\delta, \delta) = (\sigma\delta, \sigma\delta)$. Equality forces $\sigma\delta = \delta$, hence $\nu = \mu$.
