Let $K$ be the subalgebra of $L$ generated by all $L_\alpha$, $L_{-\alpha}$ ($\alpha \in \Delta$). Every positive root is a sum of simple roots with all partial sums being roots (Corollary 10.2A), so by induction on height, each $L_\beta$ ($\beta > 0$) is contained in $K$. Similarly for negative roots. Finally, $H = \sum_{\alpha \in \Delta} [L_\alpha L_{-\alpha}]$ by Proposition 8.3, so $H \subset K$. Hence $K = L$.
