The **Isomorphism Theorem** is one of the central results of the structure theory: two simple Lie algebras with isomorphic root systems are themselves isomorphic. Moreover, the isomorphism can be chosen to send arbitrarily prescribed root vectors for simple roots to their counterparts. Combined with the classification of root systems, this yields the complete classification of simple Lie algebras over algebraically closed fields of characteristic zero.
