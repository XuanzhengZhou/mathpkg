Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra over $\mathbb{F}$, $H$ a maximal toral subalgebra, $\Phi \subset H^*$ the set of roots. Let $E$ be the real vector space $\mathbb{R} \otimes_{\mathbb{Q}} E_{\mathbb{Q}}$, where $E_{\mathbb{Q}}$ is the $\mathbb{Q}$-span of $\Phi$ in $H^*$. Extend the bilinear form dual to the Killing form to $E$. Then $\Phi$ is a root system in $E$.
