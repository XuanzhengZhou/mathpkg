Verify that $\delta(x)$, for $x \in \mathfrak{sl}(3, F)$, as defined in (19.3), is a derivation of the Cayley algebra $\mathfrak{C}$. (Here $\delta(x)$ is the linear map on $\mathfrak{C}$ induced by the natural action of $\mathfrak{sl}(3, F)$ on the $F^3$ components of the Cayley–Dickson matrices.)
