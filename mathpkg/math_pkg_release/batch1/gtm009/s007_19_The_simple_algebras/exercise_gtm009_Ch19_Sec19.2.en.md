Supply details for the argument outlined in (19.2). Specifically, verify that for each of the classical Lie algebras $B_\ell$, $C_\ell$, $D_\ell$, the diagonal matrices in $H$ together with $1$ generate (via addition, scalar multiplication, and ordinary multiplication) all endomorphisms of $V$, thereby proving that each algebra acts irreducibly in its natural representation. Conclude using Proposition 19.1 that these algebras are semisimple.
