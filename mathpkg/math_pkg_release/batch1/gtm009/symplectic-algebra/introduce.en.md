The symplectic Lie algebra $\mathfrak{sp}(V)$ is one of the four classical families of Lie algebras. It consists of linear transformations that are skew-adjoint with respect to a nondegenerate alternating bilinear form. The symplectic algebra is of type $C_\ell$ in the Cartan-Killing classification and plays a fundamental role in symplectic geometry and Hamiltonian mechanics.
