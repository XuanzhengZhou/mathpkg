The conjugacy theorem states that all Cartan subalgebras of a Lie algebra over an algebraically closed field of characteristic zero are conjugate under a natural group of automorphisms. This implies that the root system extracted from a semisimple Lie algebra is independent of the choice of Cartan subalgebra, making it an invariant of the algebra. The theorem is crucial for the classification proof.
