A Cartan subalgebra is a nilpotent subalgebra equal to its normalizer. These subalgebras generalize maximal toral subalgebras to arbitrary Lie algebras and provide the setting for root space decompositions. In a semisimple Lie algebra over an algebraically closed field of characteristic zero, Cartan subalgebras coincide with maximal toral subalgebras and are abelian.
