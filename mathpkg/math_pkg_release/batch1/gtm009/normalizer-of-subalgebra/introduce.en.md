The normalizer of a subspace $K$ in a Lie algebra $L$ is the set of elements whose adjoint action preserves $K$. It is always a subalgebra. For subalgebras, the normalizer is the largest subalgebra containing the given one as an ideal, analogous to the normalizer of a subgroup.
