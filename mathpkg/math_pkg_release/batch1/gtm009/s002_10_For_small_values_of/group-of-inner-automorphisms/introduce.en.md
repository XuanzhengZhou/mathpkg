The group $\operatorname{Int} L$ of inner automorphisms is the subgroup of the full automorphism group $\operatorname{Aut} L$ generated by all automorphisms of the form $\exp(\operatorname{ad} x)$ with $\operatorname{ad} x$ nilpotent. This group is analogous to the group of inner automorphisms of a group. As will be shown, $\operatorname{Int} L$ is a normal subgroup of $\operatorname{Aut} L$.
