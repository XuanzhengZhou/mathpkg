The \text{normalizer} of a subalgebra (or just subspace) $K$ of $L$ is defined by
$$N_L(K) = \{x \in L \mid [xK] \subset K\}.$$
By the Jacobi identity, $N_L(K)$ is a subalgebra of $L$; it may be described verbally as the largest subalgebra of $L$ which includes $K$ as an ideal (in case $K$ is a subalgebra to begin with).
