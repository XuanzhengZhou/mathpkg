This exercise proves a structural property of the adjoint representation: for any element $x$ of a Lie algebra $L$, the subspace spanned by all eigenvectors of the adjoint action $\operatorname{ad} x$ forms a subalgebra of $L$. This result underpins the root space decomposition theory developed later in the study of semisimple Lie algebras.
