This exercise verifies the explicit action of the inner automorphism $\sigma = \exp(\operatorname{ad} x) \exp(\operatorname{ad} (-y)) \exp(\operatorname{ad} x)$ on the standard basis $(x, y, h)$ of $\mathfrak{sl}(2, F)$. The automorphism interchanges the nilpotent elements $x$ and $y$ (up to sign) and negates the semisimple element $h$, realizing the nontrivial outer automorphism of the Dynkin diagram $A_1$.
