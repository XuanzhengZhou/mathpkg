Any nonzero nilpotent Lie algebra contains an ideal of codimension 1. This follows from the existence of a maximal proper subalgebra (which must be an ideal, by the normalizer growth property from Exercise 7) and the fact that maximal subalgebras in nilpotent algebras have codimension 1. This structural result is used in Exercise 9 to prove the existence of outer derivations.
