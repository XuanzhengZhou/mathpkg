For small values of $\ell$, isomorphisms occur among certain of the classical algebras. Show that $A_1$, $B_1$, $C_1$ are all isomorphic, while $D_1$ is the one dimensional Lie algebra. Show that $B_2$ is isomorphic to $C_2$, and $D_3$ to $A_3$. What can you say about $D_2$?
