This exercise examines the normalizers of the standard subalgebras of $\mathfrak{gl}(n, F)$. The upper triangular matrices $\mathfrak{t}(n, F)$ and the diagonal matrices $\mathfrak{d}(n, F)$ are each equal to their own normalizer in $\mathfrak{gl}(n, F)$. In contrast, the strictly upper triangular matrices $\mathfrak{n}(n, F)$ are not self-normalizing; their normalizer is the full algebra of upper triangular matrices $\mathfrak{t}(n, F)$.
