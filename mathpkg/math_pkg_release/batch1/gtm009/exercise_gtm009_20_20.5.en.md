If $\mu \in H^*$, define $\mathcal{P}(\mu)$ to be the number of sets of nonnegative integers $\{k_\alpha \mid \alpha \succ 0\}$ for which $\mu = \lambda - \sum_{\alpha \succ 0} k_\alpha \alpha$. Prove that $\dim Z(\lambda)_\mu = \mathcal{P}(\mu)$, the Kostant partition function evaluated at $\lambda - \mu$.
