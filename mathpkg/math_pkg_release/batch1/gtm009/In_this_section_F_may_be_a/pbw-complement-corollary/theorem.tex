Let $W$ be a subspace of $T^m L$. Suppose the canonical map $T^m L \to S^m L$ sends $W$ isomorphically onto $S^m L$. Then $\pi(W)$ is a complement to $U_{m-1}$ in $U_m$, where $\pi: \mathfrak{T}(L) \to \mathfrak{U}(L)$ is the canonical map and $U_m$ is the $m$-th term in the natural filtration of $\mathfrak{U}(L)$.
