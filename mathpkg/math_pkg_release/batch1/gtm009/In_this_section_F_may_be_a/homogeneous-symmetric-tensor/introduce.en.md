A homogeneous symmetric tensor of order $m$ is an element of the $m$-th tensor power $T^m V$ that is invariant under all permutations of its tensor factors. When the characteristic of the underlying field is zero, one can construct an explicit basis for the space of symmetric tensors by averaging over the symmetric group $\mathcal{S}_m$. The images of these symmetrized basis tensors in the symmetric algebra $\mathfrak{S}(V)$ form a basis, establishing the close relationship between symmetric tensors and the symmetric algebra.
