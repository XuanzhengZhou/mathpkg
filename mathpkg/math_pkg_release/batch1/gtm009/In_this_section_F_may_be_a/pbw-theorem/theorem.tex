Let $L$ be a Lie algebra over a field $F$ with universal enveloping algebra $\mathfrak{U}(L)$. Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be the associated graded algebra of $\mathfrak{U}(L)$ with respect to its natural filtration, and let $\mathfrak{S}(L)$ be the symmetric algebra on $L$. Then the canonical homomorphism $\omega: \mathfrak{S}(L) \to \mathfrak{G}$ is an isomorphism of algebras.
