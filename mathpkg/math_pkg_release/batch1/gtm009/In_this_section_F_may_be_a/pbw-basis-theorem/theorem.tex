Let $(x_1, x_2, x_3, \ldots)$ be any ordered basis of the Lie algebra $L$. Then the elements
$$x_{i(1)} \cdots x_{i(m)} = \pi(x_{i(1)} \otimes \cdots \otimes x_{i(m)}), \quad m \in \mathbb{Z}^+, \; i(1) \leq i(2) \leq \cdots \leq i(m),$$
together with $1$, form a basis of the universal enveloping algebra $\mathfrak{U}(L)$.
