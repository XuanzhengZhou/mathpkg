A Borel subalgebra is a maximal solvable subalgebra of a Lie algebra. In a semisimple Lie algebra, Borel subalgebras correspond to choices of positive roots and are fundamental in both the algebraic and geometric theories. The flag variety $L/B$ is a projective variety, and the study of Borel subalgebras provides the bridge to algebraic geometry and the theory of algebraic groups.
