Let $\phi: L \rightarrow \mathfrak{gl}(V)$ be a faithful representation of a semisimple Lie algebra $L$ for which the trace form $\beta(x,y) = \text{Tr}(\phi(x)\phi(y))$ is nondegenerate. Let $\{x_1, \ldots, x_n\}$ be a basis of $L$ and $\{y_1, \ldots, y_n\}$ the dual basis with respect to $\beta$ (so $\beta(x_i, y_j) = \delta_{ij}$). The \text{Casimir operator} is
$$c_\phi = \sum_{i=1}^n \phi(x_i)\phi(y_i) \in \text{End } V.$$
The Casimir operator commutes with $\phi(L)$ (i.e., $[c_\phi, \phi(x)] = 0$ for all $x \in L$) and $\text{Tr}(c_\phi) = \dim L$.
