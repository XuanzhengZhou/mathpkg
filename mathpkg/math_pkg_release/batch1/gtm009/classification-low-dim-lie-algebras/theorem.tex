Up to isomorphism, the Lie algebras of dimension $\leq 2$ over a field $F$ are:

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Dimension 1:} The unique (abelian) Lie algebra with basis $\{x\}$ and bracket $[x x] = 0$.

\item \textbf{Dimension 2:} Exactly two isomorphism classes:
  \begin{enumerate}
  \item The abelian Lie algebra: $[x y] = 0$ for all basis elements.
  \item The nonabelian Lie algebra: With basis $\{x, y\}$ such that $[x y] = x$.
  \end{enumerate}
\end{itemize}
