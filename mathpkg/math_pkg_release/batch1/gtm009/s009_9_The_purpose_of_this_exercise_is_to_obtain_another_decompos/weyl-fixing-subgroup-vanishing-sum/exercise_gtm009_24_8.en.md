Let $\lambda \in \Lambda$. If there exists $\sigma \neq 1$ in the Weyl group $\mathcal{W}$ such that $\sigma(\lambda) = \lambda$, prove that
$$
\sum_{\sigma \in \mathcal{W}} \operatorname{sn}(\sigma) \, \varepsilon_{\sigma(\lambda)} = 0.
$$

[*Hint:* Use the fact that $\lambda$ lies in the closure but not the interior of some Weyl chamber to find a reflection fixing $\lambda$, and deduce that the group fixing $\lambda$ has even order.]
