Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra with Casimir element $c = c_L \in \mathfrak{U}(L)$, constructed as
$$
c = \sum_{i=1}^{\ell} h_i k_i + \sum_{\alpha \in \Phi} x_\alpha z_\alpha,
$$
where dual bases of $L$ relative to the Killing form are chosen so that $\{h_i\}$, $\{k_i\}$ are dual bases of a Cartan subalgebra $H$ and $\{x_\alpha\}$, $\{z_\alpha\}$ are dual with respect to root vectors. Then $c$ lies in the center of $\mathfrak{U}(L)$ and acts on a standard cyclic module of highest weight $\lambda \in H^*$ (generated by a maximal vector $v^+$) as the scalar
$$
(\lambda + \delta, \lambda + \delta) - (\delta, \delta),
$$
where $\delta$ is half the sum of positive roots and $(\cdot, \cdot)$ denotes the Killing form on $H^*$.
