Let $L$ be a Lie algebra with ordered basis $(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n)$. Then the monomials
$$x_1^{a_1} x_2^{a_2} \cdots x_n^{a_n} \quad (a_i \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}),$$
together with the unit element 1, form a basis of the universal enveloping algebra $\mathfrak{U}(L)$. In particular, the canonical map $i: L \rightarrow \mathfrak{U}(L)$ is injective.
