A category measure is a finite measure on the Baire-property $\sigma$-algebra for which the measure-zero sets are precisely the sets of first category. This identification makes the extended principle of duality trivial. Category measures are studied on regular Baire spaces where they satisfy strong regularity properties: open sets can be approximated from inside by closed sets and vice versa.
