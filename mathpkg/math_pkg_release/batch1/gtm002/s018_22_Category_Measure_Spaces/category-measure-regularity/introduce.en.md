Theorem 22.1 shows that a category measure on a regular Baire space is regular in the measure-theoretic sense: open sets can be approximated from inside by closed subsets (inner regularity) and closed sets can be approximated from outside by open supersets (outer regularity). This follows from writing an open set as a union of basic open sets whose closures are contained in it.
