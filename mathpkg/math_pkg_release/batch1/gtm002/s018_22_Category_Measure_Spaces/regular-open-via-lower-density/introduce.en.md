Theorem 22.8 provides a clean algebraic characterization of regular open sets in the density topology: they are precisely the values of the lower density $\phi$ on measurable sets. In particular, every $\phi(A)$ is regular open (equal to the interior of its closure), and conversely every regular open set arises this way. This shows that the Boolean algebra of regular open sets in the density topology is isomorphic to the measure algebra $S / \mathcal{N}$.
