Let $(X, \varrho)$ be a metric space, and suppose that there exists a sequence $\{f_i\}$ of real-valued continuous functions on $X$ with the property that a Cauchy sequence $\{x_n\}$ is convergent whenever each of the sequences $\{f_1(x_n)\}, \{f_2(x_n)\}, \ldots$ is bounded. Then $X$ can be remetrized so as to be complete.
