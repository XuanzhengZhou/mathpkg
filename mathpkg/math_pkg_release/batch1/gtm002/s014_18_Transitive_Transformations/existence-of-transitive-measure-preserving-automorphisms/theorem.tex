Let $M$ be the space of all measure-preserving automorphisms of the closed unit square. Then there exists $T \in M$ such that $T$ is transitive — i.e., there exists a point $x$ whose positive semiorbit $\{x, Tx, T^2x, \ldots\}$ is dense in the square. Moreover, the set of all such transitive $T$ is a dense $G_{\delta}$ in $M$, hence residual.
