Let $H$ be the space of all automorphisms of the closed unit square $X = [0,1] \times [0,1]$, equipped with the uniform metric
$$\varrho(S, T) = \sup_{x \in X} |Sx - Tx|.$$
If $T \in H$ is transitive, then in every neighborhood of $T$ there exists an automorphism $S \circ T \in H$ that is not transitive. Consequently, the set of transitive automorphisms is not open in $H$.
