Theorem 9.1 extends the Baire Category Theorem from complete metric spaces to topologically complete metric spaces. If a space is topologically complete, then the complement of any meager (first category) set is dense. Equivalently, a topologically complete metric space is not the countable union of nowhere dense sets; it is a Baire space.
