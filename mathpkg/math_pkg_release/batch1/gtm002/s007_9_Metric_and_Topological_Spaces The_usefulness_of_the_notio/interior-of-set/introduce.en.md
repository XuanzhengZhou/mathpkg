The interior of a set $A$ is the union of all open sets contained in $A$; equivalently, it is the largest open subset of $A$. In the notation $A'^{-'}$, the prime denotes complement, so the interior equals the complement of the closure of the complement: $A^\circ = (A^c)^{-c}$.
