This lemma establishes that each $E_n$ is a closed subset of $C[0,1]$ under uniform convergence. The proof uses a diagonal argument: given a sequence $\{f_k\}$ in $E_n$ converging uniformly to $f$, one extracts witnesses $x_k$ with $|f_k(x_k+h) - f_k(x_k)| \leq nh$, passes to a convergent subsequence $x_k \to x$, and applies the triangle inequality to show the limiting function $f$ also satisfies the defining inequality at $x$. This is the first step in proving $E_n$ is nowhere dense.
