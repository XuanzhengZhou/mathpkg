Let $S$ be a $\sigma$-algebra of measurable sets. Define

$$\varrho(E, F) = m(E \triangle F),$$

where $E \triangle F = (E - F) \cup (F - E)$ is the symmetric difference. Then $\varrho$ is a pseudometric on $S$, and becomes a metric on the quotient $\tilde{S}$ obtained by identifying sets that differ by a nullset.
