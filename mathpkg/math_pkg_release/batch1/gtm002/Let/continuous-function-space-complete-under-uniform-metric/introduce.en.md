A fundamental result in analysis is that the space $C[a,b]$ with the uniform metric is complete. The proof shows that any uniformly Cauchy sequence of continuous functions converges uniformly to a continuous limit function. The argument uses the completeness of the real numbers to obtain pointwise limits, then shows the convergence is uniform, and finally invokes the well-known theorem that the uniform limit of continuous functions is continuous.
