The metric space of (equivalence classes of) measurable sets under the symmetric difference metric is complete. The proof takes a Cauchy sequence of measurable sets, extracts a rapidly Cauchy subsequence, and constructs the limit as the set of points belonging to all but finitely many of the subsequents. The key estimate uses subadditivity of measure and geometric series convergence to bound the symmetric difference.
