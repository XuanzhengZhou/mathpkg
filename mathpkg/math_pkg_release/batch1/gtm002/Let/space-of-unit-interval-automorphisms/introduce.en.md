The space $H$ consists of all homeomorphisms of the unit interval $[0,1]$ onto itself that fix the endpoints $0$ and $1$. Equipped with the uniform metric inherited from $C[0,1]$, $H$ is a subspace of the space of continuous functions. Although $H$ is not complete under this metric, it is a $G_\delta$ subset of $C[0,1]$, making it topologically complete (by Theorem 12.1 of Alexandroff).
