Let $\mathcal{T}$ be the density topology on $X$ defined by a lower density $\phi$ on a complete finite measure space $(X, S, \mu)$. A subset $A \subset X$ belongs to $S$ if and only if $A$ has the property of Baire relative to $\mathcal{T}$. Consequently, $\mu$ is a category measure in $(X, \mathcal{T})$.
