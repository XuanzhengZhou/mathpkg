In the density topology, the regular open sets are in one-to-one correspondence with the images of measurable sets under the lower density $\phi$. Since $\phi$ selects a canonical representative from each equivalence class modulo nullsets, this theorem shows that the regular open sets of the density topology correspond to the Boolean algebra of measurable sets modulo nullsets.
