In a category measure space over a regular Baire space, the measure has the same regularity properties as an ordinary Borel measure. Open sets can be approximated from within by closed sets, and closed sets from without by open sets, up to any prescribed $\varepsilon$ in measure. This shows that the topology and the category measure are compatible in a strong sense.
