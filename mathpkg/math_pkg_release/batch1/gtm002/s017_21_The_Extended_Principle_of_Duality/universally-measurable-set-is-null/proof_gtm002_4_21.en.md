This is a restatement of Theorem 5.5 (see Chapter 1, Section 5). The proof is given in that earlier section, where it is shown that if every subset of $E$ is measurable then $m(E) = 0$, and the category analogue follows from the properties of sets with the Baire property. In the present section the theorem is recalled to motivate the extended principle of duality.
