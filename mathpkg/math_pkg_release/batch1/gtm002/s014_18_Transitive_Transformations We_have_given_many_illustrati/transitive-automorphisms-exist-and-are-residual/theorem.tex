Let $M$ be the space of all measure-preserving automorphisms of the unit square $X$ with the uniform metric. For each pair of basic open sets $U_i, U_j$ define
$$E_{ij} = \left\{ T \in M : U_i \cap \bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty} T^{-k} U_j \neq \emptyset \right\}.$$
Then $\bigcap_{i,j} E_{ij}$ is a dense $G_{\delta}$ set in $M$, and therefore non-empty. Moreover, every $T \in \bigcap_{i,j} E_{ij}$ is a transitive automorphism of the unit square.
