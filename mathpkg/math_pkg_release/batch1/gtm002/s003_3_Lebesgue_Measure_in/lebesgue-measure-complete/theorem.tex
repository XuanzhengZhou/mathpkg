\begin{theorem}[Theorem 3.16]
The class $S$ of measurable sets is the $\sigma$-algebra of subsets of $r$-space $X$ generated by the open sets together with the nullsets. Lebesgue measure $m$ is a measure on $S$ such that $m(I) = |I|$ for every interval $I$. Moreover, $(X, S, m)$ is a complete measure space.
\end{theorem}
