The \textit{outer measure} of a subset $A$ of $r$-space is defined as

$$
m^*(A) = \inf \left\{ \sum_i |I_i| : A \subset \bigcup_i I_i \right\},
$$

where $\{I_i\}$ ranges over all sequences of intervals that cover $A$. If the series $\sum |I_i|$ diverges for every covering sequence, then $m^*(A) = \infty$; otherwise $m^*(A)$ is a nonnegative real number.
