Let $M$ be the space of all measure-preserving automorphisms of the closed unit square. For each pair of positive integers $i$ and $j$, let
$$E_{ij} = \{ T \in M : U_i \cap \bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty} T^{-k} U_j \neq \emptyset \}.$$
Then $\bigcap_{i,j=1}^{\infty} E_{ij}$ is a dense $G_{\delta}$ in $M$, and is therefore nonempty.
