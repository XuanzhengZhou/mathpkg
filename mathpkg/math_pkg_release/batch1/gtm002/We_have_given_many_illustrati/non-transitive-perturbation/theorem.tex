Let $T$ be a transitive automorphism of the closed unit square $X$, and let $x$ be a point whose positive semiorbit is dense. Then there exists an automorphism $S$ of $X$, equal to the identity outside an arbitrarily small disk $U$ containing $x$, such that $S \circ T$ is not transitive. Moreover, any automorphism sufficiently close to $S \circ T$ in the uniform metric $\varrho$ is also non-transitive.
