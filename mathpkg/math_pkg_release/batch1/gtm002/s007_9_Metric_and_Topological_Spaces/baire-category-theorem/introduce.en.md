The Baire Category Theorem (Theorem 9.1) states that in a topologically complete metric space, the complement of any first-category set is dense. Equivalently, no topologically complete metric space is of first category in itself. This theorem is one of the cornerstones of analysis and topology, providing a powerful tool for existence proofs via category arguments.
