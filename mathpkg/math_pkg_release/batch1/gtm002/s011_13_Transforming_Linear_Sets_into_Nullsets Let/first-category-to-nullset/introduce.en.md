Every first category subset of the unit interval can be transformed into a Lebesgue nullset by a suitable homeomorphism of the interval onto itself. Moreover, the set of all such homeomorphisms that accomplish this for a given first category set is not merely nonempty—it is residual (comeager) in the space $H$ of all automorphisms of $I$ fixing the endpoints. This is Theorem 13.1 of Oxtoby's \emph{Measure and Category}, proved via a Baire category argument on the space $H$ itself.
