Theorem 4.3 shows that the property of Baire is preserved under countable unions. Together with Theorem 4.2 (closure under complementation), this establishes that the class of sets with the property of Baire forms a $\sigma$-algebra, specifically the $\sigma$-algebra generated by the open sets and the sets of first category.
