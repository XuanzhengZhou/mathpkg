A \textbf{metric space} is a set $X$ together with a distance function or metric $\varrho(x, y)$ defined for all pairs of points of $X$ and satisfying the following conditions:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $\varrho(x, y) \geq 0$, $\varrho(x, x) = 0$,
\item[(2)] $\varrho(x, y) = \varrho(y, x)$,
\item[(3)] $\varrho(x, z) \leq \varrho(x, y) + \varrho(y, z)$ (triangle inequality),
\item[(4)] $\varrho(x, y) = 0$ implies $x = y$.
\end{enumerate}
