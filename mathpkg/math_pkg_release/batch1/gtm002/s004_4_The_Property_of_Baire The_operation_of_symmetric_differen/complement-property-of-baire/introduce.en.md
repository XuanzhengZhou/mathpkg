Theorem 4.2 states that the property of Baire is preserved under complementation. Together with Theorem 4.3 (closure under countable unions), this shows that the class of sets having the property of Baire forms a $\sigma$-algebra. The proof follows from the closed-representation characterization (Theorem 4.1) and the fact that the complement of a closed set is open.
