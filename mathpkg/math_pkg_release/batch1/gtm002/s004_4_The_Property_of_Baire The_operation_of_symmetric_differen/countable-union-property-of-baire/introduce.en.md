Theorem 4.3 establishes that the property of Baire is closed under countable unions, making the class a $\sigma$-algebra. Combined with complementation (Theorem 4.2), this gives full $\sigma$-algebra structure. The class is the smallest $\sigma$-algebra containing all open sets and all first-category sets, analogous to how the measurable sets form the smallest $\sigma$-algebra containing open sets and nullsets.
