Proposition 20.3* is the measure-theoretic dual of Proposition 20.3. It asserts that any set of positive outer measure can be decomposed into continuum many disjoint subsets, each of positive outer measure. Since any set with positive outer measure contains a non-measurable subset (Theorem 5.5), this implies the existence of $\mathfrak{c}$ disjoint non-measurable subsets of $E$. By Zorn's lemma, these can be extended to a maximal family, whose complement must have measure zero, giving a decomposition of $E$ into $\mathfrak{c}$ disjoint non-measurable sets.
