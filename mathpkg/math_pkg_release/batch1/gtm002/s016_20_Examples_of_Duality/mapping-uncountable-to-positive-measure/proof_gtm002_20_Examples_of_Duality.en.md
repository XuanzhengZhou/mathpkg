This proposition is the dual of Proposition 20.2. By Proposition 20.1*, there exists a set $N$ of power $\mathfrak{c}$ such that every uncountable subset of $N$ has positive outer measure. Let $f$ be any one-to-one mapping of the line onto $N$. Then for any uncountable set $E$, the image $f(E)$ is an uncountable subset of $N$, hence has positive outer measure.
