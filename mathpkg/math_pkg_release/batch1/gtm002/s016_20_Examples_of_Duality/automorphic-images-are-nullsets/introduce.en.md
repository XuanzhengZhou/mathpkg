Corollary 20.5 follows from the dual of Proposition 20.4 and the fact that the class of all automorphisms (homeomorphisms) of the line onto itself has cardinality $\mathfrak{c}$. It asserts the existence of an uncountable set $E$ whose image under every automorphism of the line is a nullset. This shows that in Theorem 13.2, the hypothesis that $A$ be a closed set cannot be omitted, though it can be weakened to $A$ being a Borel set. Sierpiński later proved an even stronger form without assuming the continuum hypothesis.
