Theorem 16.5 is the culmination of Chapter 16, described as "perhaps the ultimate generalization of Theorem 1.6." Under mild conditions on the metric space (the weight has measure zero) and the measure (nonatomic, with certain regularity properties), the entire space decomposes into a $G_\delta$ null set and a set of first category. This theorem weaves together the measure-theoretic and category-theoretic strands of the book into a single structural decomposition.
