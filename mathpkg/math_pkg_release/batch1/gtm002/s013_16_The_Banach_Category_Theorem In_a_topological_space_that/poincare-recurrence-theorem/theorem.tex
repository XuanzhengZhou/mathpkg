Let $X$ be a bounded open region of $r$-space and let $T$ be a homeomorphism of $X$ onto itself that preserves volume. Under iteration of $T$, each point $x$ generates a sequence $x, Tx, T^2x, \ldots$ called the positive semiorbit of $x$. A point $x$ of an open set $G$ is said to be recurrent with respect to $G$ if $T^i x$ belongs to $G$ for infinitely many positive integers $i$. Then for any open set $G \subset X$, all points of $G$ are recurrent with respect to $G$ except a set of first category and measure zero.
