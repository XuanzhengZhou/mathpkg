A Borel measure is any measure defined on the Borel $\sigma$-algebra of a topological space. A normalized Borel measure assigns total measure 1 to the whole space, while a nonatomic Borel measure has no point masses. These concepts are essential for the measure-theoretic generalizations of the Banach Category Theorem discussed in this chapter.
