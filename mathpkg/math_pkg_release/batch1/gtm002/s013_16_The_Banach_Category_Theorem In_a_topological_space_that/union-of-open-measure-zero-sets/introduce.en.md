Theorem 16.3 provides the measure-theoretic counterpart to the Banach Category Theorem. For a finite Borel measure in a metric space, the union of any family of open sets of measure zero has measure zero, provided the cardinality of the family has measure zero in the sense of Marczewski and Sikorski. This generalizes the trivial fact that in second-countable spaces the union of any family of open null sets is null.
