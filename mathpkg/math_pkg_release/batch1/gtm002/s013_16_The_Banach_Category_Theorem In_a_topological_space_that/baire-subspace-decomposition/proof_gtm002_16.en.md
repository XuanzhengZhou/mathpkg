By the Banach Category Theorem, the union of all open sets of first category in $X$ is an open set $H$ of first category. The subspace $Y = X - \overline{H}$ is a closed Baire subspace: any non-empty relatively open subset of $Y$ is of second category in $X$ (otherwise it would be contained in $H$). Alternatively, $X - H$ is an open Baire subspace, and $H$ is of first category. Thus $X = (X - H) \cup H$ gives the required decomposition. $\square$
