In a metric space $X$, let $\{F_\alpha : \alpha \in A\}$ be a family of closed sets such that for each $\alpha \in A$ there exists an open set $G_\alpha \supset F_\alpha$, and for $\alpha \neq \beta$ the distance between $F_\alpha$ and $F_\beta$ is positive. Then the union $\bigcup_{\alpha \in A} F_\alpha$ is an $F_\sigma$ set.
