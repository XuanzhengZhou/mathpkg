Theorem 16.4 refines the measure-theoretic Banach Category Theorem by showing that when a family of open sets has measure-zero union, the union can effectively be reduced to a subfamily drawn from a fixed base. This is the measure-theoretic analogue of the observation that in a space with countable base, the union of any family of open first-category sets is of first category. However, unlike the category case, Theorem 16.4 can fail in nonmetrizable spaces.
