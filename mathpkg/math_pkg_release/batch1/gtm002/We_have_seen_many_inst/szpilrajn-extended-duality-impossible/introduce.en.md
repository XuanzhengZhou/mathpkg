Szpilrajn (Marczewski) considered whether the duality between measure and category could be sharpened into a single bijection of the real line that converts measurable sets to sets with the Baire property and nullsets to first category sets. He proved that no such mapping exists. The proof proceeds by contradiction: assuming such an $f$, one takes the preimage of the unit interval, constructs an open superset $G$ whose boundary is nowhere dense, and derives a measure contradiction showing the unit interval would be covered by a nullset.
