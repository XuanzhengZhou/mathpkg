Let $E_{i,j}$ be a double sequence of measurable sets such that $E_{i,j} \supset E_{i,j+1}$ for all positive integers $i$ and $j$, and such that $\bigcap_j E_{i,j}$ is a nullset for each $i$. Then there exists a sequence of mappings $n_k(i)$ of the set of positive integers into itself such that
$$\bigcap_k \bigcup_i E_{i,n_k(i)}$$
is a nullset.
