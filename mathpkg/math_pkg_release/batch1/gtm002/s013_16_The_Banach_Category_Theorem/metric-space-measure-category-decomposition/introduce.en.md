Theorem 16.5, described as the ultimate generalization of Theorem 1.6, shows that under mild conditions on the measure and the metric space, the space itself decomposes into a measure-zero $G_\delta$ set and a first-category set. This theorem elegantly unifies the measure-theoretic and categorical perspectives on small sets.
