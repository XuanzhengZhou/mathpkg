Theorem 16.3 provides a measure-theoretic analogue of the Banach Category Theorem: the union of a family of open measure-zero sets has measure zero, provided the cardinality of the family has measure zero. Unlike the Banach Category Theorem, this result requires both a metric structure and a cardinality restriction.
