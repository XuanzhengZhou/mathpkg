Corollary 20.5 follows from Proposition 20.4* by noting that the class of all automorphisms (homeomorphisms of the line onto itself) has cardinality $\mathfrak{c}$. Under the continuum hypothesis, there exists an uncountable set $E$ such that every automorphic image of $E$ is a nullset. This demonstrates that the hypothesis in Theorem 13.2 requiring the set to be closed (or Borel) cannot be omitted entirely, though a weaker hypothesis may suffice. Sierpinski later showed a stronger form without the continuum hypothesis.
