A category measure space is a topological space $X$ equipped with a finite measure $\mu$ defined on the $\sigma$-algebra of sets with the property of Baire, where the nullsets coincide exactly with the sets of first category. In such a space, the extended principle of duality becomes a tautology: measure and category are identified. These spaces exhibit unusual topological properties and are the primary objects of study in Chapter 22 of Oxtoby's Measure and Category.
