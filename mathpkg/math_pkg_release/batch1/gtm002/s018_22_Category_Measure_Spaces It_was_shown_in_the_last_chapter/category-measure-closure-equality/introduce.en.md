Theorem 22.3 shows that for a category measure on a regular Baire space, the measure of any set with the property of Baire equals the measure of its closure, which in turn equals the measure of the interior of its closure (its regular open hull). This follows because the boundary $\bar{E} - E'^{-'}$ is nowhere dense, and nowhere dense sets have measure zero in a category measure space.
