Theorem 22.8 gives a complete description of the regular open sets in the density topology: they are exactly the images $\phi(A)$ of measurable sets under the lower density. This shows that the lower density $\phi$ serves as a canonical selector for the regular open sets, analogous to how Theorem 4.7 relates regular open sets to canonical representatives in the Baire property context.
