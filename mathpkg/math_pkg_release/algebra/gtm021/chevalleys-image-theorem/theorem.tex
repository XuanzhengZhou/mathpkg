Let $\varphi: X \rightarrow Y$ be a dominant morphism of irreducible varieties, $r = \dim X - \dim Y$.

(a) $\varphi(X)$ contains a nonempty open subset $U$ of $Y$.

(b) If all irreducible components of all fibres $\varphi^{-1}(y)$ ($y \in \varphi(X)$) have the same dimension $r$, then $\varphi$ is an open mapping.
