Prove any semisimple s is conjugate into T, and C_G(s) is connected. By conjugacy in 19.3, assume s in T. Then C_G(s) = T semidirect C_{G_u}(s). By Theorem 18.3, C_{G_u}(s) is connected, so C_G(s) is connected. For arbitrary semisimple s, conjugate into T to get isomorphic centralizer.
