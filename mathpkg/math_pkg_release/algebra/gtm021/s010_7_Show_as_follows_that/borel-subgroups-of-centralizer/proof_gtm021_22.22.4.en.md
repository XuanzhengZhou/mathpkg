C stabilizes Y. Assume B is isotropy group of y in Y. Show C.y = Y. Let Z be inverse image of Y under G -> G/B; need CB = Z. Z is connected (Y connected, canonical map has connected fibers). For z in Z, z^{-1}Sz subset B. The morphism Z x S -> B: (z,s) -> z^{-1}sz lands in B. Using rigidity of tori and connectedness of Z, S is centralized by elements of Z modulo B, yielding Z = CB.
