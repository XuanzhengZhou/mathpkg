Let U be a connected unipotent subgroup of G, s in G a semisimple element normalizing U. Define gamma_s(x) = x s x^{-1} s^{-1}, M = gamma_s(U), C = C_U(s). Then: (a) C is a closed subgroup and M a closed connected subvariety of U. (b) The product morphism tau: C x M to U is bijective; consequently C is connected. (c) gamma = gamma_s restricted to M is a bijection of M onto itself.
