Induction on n. If dim G = n-1: G is kernel of some p-polynomial f(T): V -> G_a (20.3). Proposition 20.4 gives automorphism phi with phi(G) containing V_1. If H is projection of phi(G) on product of V_i for i>=2, then phi(G) = V_1 x H. H has codim 1, induction applies. If dim G < n-1: project G to G' in product of V_i for i>=2. By induction, automorphism of V' sends G' to product of some V_i. Extend to V fixing V_1. Then phi(G) lies in product of fewer than n V_i, apply induction again.
