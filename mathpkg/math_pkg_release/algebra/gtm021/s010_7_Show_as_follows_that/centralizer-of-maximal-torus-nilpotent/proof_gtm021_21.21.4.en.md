T is the unique maximal torus of C (21.3A), so C is nilpotent by 21.4B(b). T is normal in N_G(C)^circ, hence central by rigidity of tori (16.3). Thus N_G(C)^circ subset C_G(T)^circ = C, so C = N_G(C)^circ.
