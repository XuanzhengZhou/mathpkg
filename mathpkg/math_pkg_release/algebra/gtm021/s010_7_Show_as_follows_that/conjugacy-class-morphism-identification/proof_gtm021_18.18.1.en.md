Since H is normalized by s, C_H(s) = H intersect C_G(s). The conjugacy class Cl_H(s) is the orbit of s under H-conjugation, isomorphic as a variety to H/C_H(s). The identification follows from the bijective H-equivariant morphism H/C_H(s) -> Cl_H(s).
