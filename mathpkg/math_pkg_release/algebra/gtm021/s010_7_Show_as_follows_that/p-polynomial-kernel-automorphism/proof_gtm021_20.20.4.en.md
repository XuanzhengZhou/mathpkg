Write the principal part as sum_i c_i T_i^{p^{r(i)}}. Assume c_i nonzero and r(1) >= ... >= r(n). Induction on sum r(i). If 0, f is linear and result follows. For induction step, make change U_1 = T_1 + g(T_2,...,T_n) with g a suitable p-polynomial to eliminate lower-degree terms.
