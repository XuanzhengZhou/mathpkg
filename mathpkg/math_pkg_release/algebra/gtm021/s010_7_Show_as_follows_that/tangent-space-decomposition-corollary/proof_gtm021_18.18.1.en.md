Since Ad s is semisimple, h = c_h(s) + n' where n' is the sum of nontrivial eigenspaces. Cl_G(s) is locally closed (8.3) of dimension complementary to dim C_G(s) (4.3). M = Cl_G(s) s^{-1} passes through e, so its tangent space T(M)_e = m. The dimension count and injectivity of d tau_{(e,e)} (from the product decomposition in 18.3) force it to be an isomorphism. The second statement follows from differentiating gamma_s(x) = x s x^{-1} s^{-1}.
