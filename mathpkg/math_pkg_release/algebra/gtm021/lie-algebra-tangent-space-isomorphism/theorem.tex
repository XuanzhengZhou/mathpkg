For an algebraic group $G$, evaluation at $e$ gives a $K$-linear isomorphism
$$\theta: \mathcal{L}(G) \xrightarrow{\sim} \mathcal{T}(G)_e = \mathfrak{g}.$$

Moreover, if $\varphi: G \rightarrow G'$ is a morphism of algebraic groups, then the differential $d\varphi: \mathfrak{g} \rightarrow \mathfrak{g}'$ preserves the Lie bracket operation.
