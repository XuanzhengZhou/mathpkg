Let $G, H$ be algebraic groups with $H$ acting on $G$, $s \in G$. Let $\tau: C_H(s) \times M' \rightarrow H$ be the product morphism. Then $d\tau_{(e,e)}$ is an isomorphism
$$\mathfrak{c}_\mathfrak{h}(s) \oplus \mathfrak{m}' \simeq \mathfrak{h},$$
and $d(\gamma_s|_{M'}) = (1 - \operatorname{Ad} s)|_{\mathfrak{m}'}: \mathfrak{m}' \rightarrow \mathfrak{m}'$ is an isomorphism.
