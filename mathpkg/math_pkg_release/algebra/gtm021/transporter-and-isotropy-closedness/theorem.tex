Let the algebraic group $G$ act morphically on the variety $X$. Let $Y, Z$ be subsets of $X$, with $Z$ closed.

(a) $\operatorname{Tran}_G(Y, Z) = \{x \in G \mid x \cdot Y \subset Z\}$ is a closed subset of $G$.
(b) For each $y \in X$, the isotropy group $G_y$ is a closed subgroup of $G$; in particular, $C_G(Y)$ is closed.
(c) The fixed point set of $x \in G$ is closed in $X$; in particular, $X^G$ is closed.
(d) If $G$ is connected, $G$ stabilizes each connected component of $X$.
