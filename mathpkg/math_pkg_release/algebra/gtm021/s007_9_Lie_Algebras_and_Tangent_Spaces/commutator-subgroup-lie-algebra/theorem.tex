Let $A, B$ be closed subgroups of $G$, and let $C$ be the closure of the commutator group $(A, B)$ generated by all commutators $aba^{-1}b^{-1}$ ($a \in A, b \in B$). Then the Lie algebra $\mathfrak{c}$ contains all elements of the form
$$Y - \operatorname{Ad} x(Y), \quad X - \operatorname{Ad} y(X), \quad [X, Y]$$
for $x \in A, X \in \mathfrak{a}, y \in B, Y \in \mathfrak{b}$.
