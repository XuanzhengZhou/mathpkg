This corollary (derived from Corollary A of Theorem 10.4) states that normality at the group level implies the ideal property at the Lie algebra level. When $H \trianglelefteq G$, the adjoint action of $G$ preserves $\mathfrak{h}$, and differentiating yields $[\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{h}] \subset \mathfrak{h}$. Conversely, in characteristic 0, the ideal property implies normality (Theorem 13.3).
