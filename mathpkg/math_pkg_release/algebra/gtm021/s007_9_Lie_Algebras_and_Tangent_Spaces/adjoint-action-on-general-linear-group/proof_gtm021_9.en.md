Since right and left translations commute, the right translation $\rho_x$ preserves left invariance. For $\delta \in \mathcal{L}(G)$, we compute $\operatorname{Ad} x(\delta)$ via its effect on $f \in K[G]$: $(\operatorname{Ad} x(\delta))(f) = \delta(\lambda_{x^{-1}} \rho_x f)$. The claimed formula follows from the identification of $\mathcal{L}(G)$ with left invariant derivations and the fact that $\rho_x \delta \rho_x^{-1}$ is again left invariant.
