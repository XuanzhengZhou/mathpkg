Lemma B identifies the adjoint action on $\mathfrak{gl}(n,K)$ with ordinary matrix conjugation. This makes the abstractly defined adjoint representation completely explicit for $\mathrm{GL}(n,K)$. The proof relies on Lemma A and the description $\operatorname{Ad} x(X) = \rho_x(*X) \rho_x^{-1}$ acting on coordinate functions $T_{ij}$.
