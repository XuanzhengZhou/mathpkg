A Lie algebra over a field $K$ is defined as a subspace of an associative $K$-algebra that is closed under the commutator bracket $[x,y] = xy - yx$. This definition is chosen for the purpose of attaching a Lie algebra to each algebraic group in a functorial way. It provides the algebraic framework needed to study the infinitesimal structure of algebraic groups.
