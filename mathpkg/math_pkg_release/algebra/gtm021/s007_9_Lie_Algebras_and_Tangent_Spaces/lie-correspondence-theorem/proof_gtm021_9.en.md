This is a consequence of the classical Lie correspondence for Lie groups, adapted to the algebraic setting via the fact that every algebraic group over a field of characteristic $0$ is smooth (Cartier's theorem). The correspondence associates to each subgroup $H$ its Lie algebra $\mathfrak{h} = \mathcal{L}(H)$, and to each subalgebra $\mathfrak{h}$ the smallest closed subgroup whose Lie algebra contains $\mathfrak{h}$.
