Combining Theorem 10.4 ($d(\operatorname{Ad}) = \operatorname{ad}$) with the fact that $\operatorname{Ad} x$ preserves $\mathfrak{h}$ when $x$ normalizes $H$ yields this corollary. The Lie bracket of an element from the normalizer with an element from the subgroup stays in the subalgebra. This is a key structural result connecting group-theoretic normality to Lie-algebraic ideals.
