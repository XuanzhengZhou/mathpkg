Let $H$ be a closed normal subgroup of an algebraic group $G$. Then $\mathfrak{h}$ is an ideal in $\mathfrak{g}$, i.e.,
$$[\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{h}] \subset \mathfrak{h}.$$
More generally, if $H$ is normalized by a closed subgroup $K$ of $G$, then $[\mathfrak{k}, \mathfrak{h}] \subset \mathfrak{h}$.
