For $G = \operatorname{GL}(n, K)$ with coordinate functions $T_{ij}$, the left and right convolution actions of the Lie algebra $\mathfrak{gl}(n, K)$ satisfy $(T_{ij} * x)(y) = (\mathbf{T}x)_{ij}(y)$ for $x \in \mathfrak{gl}(n, K)$, where $\mathbf{T}$ denotes the matrix of coordinate functions. Similarly, $(\rho_x T_{ij})(y) = T_{ij}(yx) = (\mathbf{T}x)_{ij}(y)$.
