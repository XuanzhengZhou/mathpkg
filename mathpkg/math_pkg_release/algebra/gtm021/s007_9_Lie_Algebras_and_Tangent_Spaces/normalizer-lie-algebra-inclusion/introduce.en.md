The Lie algebra $\mathfrak{n}$ of the normalizer $N_G(H)$ is contained in the Lie algebra normalizer $\mathfrak{n}_{\mathfrak{g}}(\mathfrak{h})$. This follows by applying Corollary A to $H$ as a normal subgroup of $N$. In characteristic 0, the inclusion is an equality (Exercise 13.1), reflecting the close correspondence between groups and their Lie algebras. In prime characteristic, however, the inclusion can be strict (Exercise 10.4).
