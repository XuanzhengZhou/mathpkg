If $f \in K[G]$ and $\mu^*f = \sum f_i \otimes g_i$, then for $(x, y) \in \mathfrak{g} \oplus \mathfrak{g}$, $d\mu_{(e,e)}(x, y)(f) = \sum x(f_i)g_i(e) + \sum f_i(e)y(g_i)$. Using the identity $f = \sum g_i(e)f_i = \sum f_i(e)g_i$, this equals $(x + y)(f)$.
