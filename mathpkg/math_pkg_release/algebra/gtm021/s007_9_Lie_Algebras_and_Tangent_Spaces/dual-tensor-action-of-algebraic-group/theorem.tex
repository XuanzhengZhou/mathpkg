If $G$ acts linearly on vector spaces $V$ and $W$, then $G$ acts naturally on the dual space $V^*$ via $(g \cdot f)(v) = f(g^{-1} \cdot v)$, and on the tensor product $V \otimes W$ via $g \cdot (v \otimes w) = (g \cdot v) \otimes (g \cdot w)$. The corresponding Lie algebra actions satisfy $x \cdot f = -f \circ x$ on $V^*$ and $x \cdot (v \otimes w) = (x \cdot v) \otimes w + v \otimes (x \cdot w)$ on $V \otimes W$.
