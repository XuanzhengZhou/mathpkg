Let $G$ be an algebraic group, $H$ a closed subgroup. Then there exists a rational representation $\varphi: G \to \operatorname{GL}(V)$ and a $1$-dimensional subspace $L \subset V$ such that:
$$H = \{x \in G \mid \varphi(x)L = L\}, \qquad \mathfrak{h} = \{X \in \mathfrak{g} \mid d\varphi(X)L \subset L\}.$$
