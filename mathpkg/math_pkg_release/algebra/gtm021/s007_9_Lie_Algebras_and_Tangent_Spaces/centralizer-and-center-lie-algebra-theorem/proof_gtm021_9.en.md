The center $Z(G)$ is the intersection of centralizers $C_G(x)$ for all $x \in G$. Taking Lie algebras, $\mathcal{L}(Z(G)) \subset \bigcap_{x \in G} \mathfrak{c}_{\mathfrak{g}}(x) \subset \mathfrak{z}(\mathfrak{g})$. For $\operatorname{GL}(n, K)$, the center consists of scalar matrices $\lambda I$, whose Lie algebra is $\{t I \mid t \in K\}$, which equals $\mathfrak{z}(\mathfrak{gl}(n, K))$.
