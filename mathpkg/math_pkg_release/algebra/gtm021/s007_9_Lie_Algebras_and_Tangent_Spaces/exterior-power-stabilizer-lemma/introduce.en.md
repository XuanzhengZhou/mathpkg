This lemma connects the stabilizer of a subspace $M$ under the group action with the stabilizer of the corresponding line $\wedge^d M$ in the exterior power. Part (a) is used to construct homogeneous spaces $G/H$ as orbits in projective space: a closed subgroup $H$ is recovered as the stabilizer of a suitable line. Part (b) provides the corresponding Lie algebra statement, crucial for Theorem 11.2 (Chevalley's theorem).
