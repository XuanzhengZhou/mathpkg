Let $W = K^n$, and let $M$ be a $d$-dimensional subspace of $W$. Let $L = \wedge^d M \subset V = \wedge^d W$, a $1$-dimensional subspace. Then for $x \in \operatorname{GL}(W)$ and $X \in \mathfrak{gl}(W)$:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] $(\wedge^d x)L = L$ if and only if $x(M) = M$.
\item[(b)] $(d\wedge^d)(X)L \subset L$ if and only if $X(M) \subset M$.
\end{itemize}
