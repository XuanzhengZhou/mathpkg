The centralizer $C_G(x) = \gamma_x^{-1}(e)$ is the fibre of the commutator map. From $(d\gamma_x)_e = 1 - \operatorname{Ad} x$, we deduce $\mathcal{L}(C_G(x)) \subset \ker(1 - \operatorname{Ad} x)$. For $G = \mathrm{GL}(n,K)$, Lemma B identifies this fixed-point space with matrices commuting with $x$, and $C_G(x)$ is a principal open subset thereof, so equality of Lie algebras follows. In prime characteristic, the inclusion may be strict.
