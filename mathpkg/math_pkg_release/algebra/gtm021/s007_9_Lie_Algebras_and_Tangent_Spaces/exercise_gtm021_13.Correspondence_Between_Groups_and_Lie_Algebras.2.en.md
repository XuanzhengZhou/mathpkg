Let $G$ be a connected algebraic group (char $0$), and let $\mathfrak{n}$ be a subalgebra of $\mathfrak{g}$. Prove that $\mathcal{L}(C_G(\mathfrak{n})) = \mathfrak{c}_{\mathfrak{g}}(\mathfrak{n}) = \{X \in \mathfrak{g} \mid [X, Y] = 0 \text{ for all } Y \in \mathfrak{n}\}$.
