Let $H$ be a closed subgroup of the algebraic group $G$, with defining ideal $I \subset A = K[G]$. Then
$$\mathcal{L}(H) = \{\delta \in \mathcal{L}(G) \mid \delta I \subset I\}.$$
Equivalently,
$$\mathfrak{h} = \{x \in \mathfrak{g} \mid I * x \subset I\},$$
where $(f * x)(y) = x(\lambda_{y^{-1}} f)$.
