Let $\rho: G \rightarrow \operatorname{GL}(V)$ be a rational representation of a reductive group $G$, with $\operatorname{Ker} \rho \subset Z(G)$. Let $V_\chi$ be a weight space relative to $\rho(T)$. If $\alpha \in \Phi$, then $\rho(U_\alpha)$ sends $V_\chi$ into $\sum_{k \in \mathbb{Z}^+} V_{\chi+k\alpha}$.
