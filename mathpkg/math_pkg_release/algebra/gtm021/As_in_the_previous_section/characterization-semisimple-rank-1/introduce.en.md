This theorem provides several equivalent characterizations of a connected algebraic group having semisimple rank 1. The equivalences connect the algebraic structure (rank 1), the Weyl group (order 2), the number of Borel subgroups containing a maximal torus (2), the dimension of the flag variety (1), and the isomorphism type of the semisimple part ($\operatorname{PGL}(2)$).
