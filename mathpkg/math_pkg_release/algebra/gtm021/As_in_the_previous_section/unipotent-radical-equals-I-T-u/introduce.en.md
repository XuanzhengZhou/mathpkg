This theorem identifies the unipotent radical $R_u(G)$ with the unipotent part of the intersection of all Borel subgroups containing a maximal torus. This provides a concrete description of the unipotent radical in terms of Borel subgroups and is fundamental for the structure theory of reductive groups.
