Corollary A of the Normalizer Theorem asserts that a Borel subgroup is maximal among all solvable subgroups of $G$, even those that are not connected or closed. However, there may exist maximal solvable subgroups that are not Borel subgroups; such subgroups are necessarily disconnected and of lower dimension than $B$.
