The Normalizer Theorem states that a Borel subgroup of a connected algebraic group is self-normalizing: $N_G(B) = B$. Combined with Lemma 23.1, this shows the connectedness of the normalizer. This theorem is fundamental for identifying the variety of all Borel subgroups with the projective variety $G/B$.
