Let $T \subset \operatorname{GL}(V)$ be a torus, acting naturally on $\mathbf{P}(V)$. Let $X$ be an irreducible closed set in $\mathbf{P}(V)$ stabilized by $T$. Assume that $X$ lies in no hyperplane $\mathbf{P}(W)$ of $\mathbf{P}(V)$.
(a) If $\dim X \geqslant 1$, then $T$ fixes at least two points of $X$.
(b) If $\dim X \geqslant 2$, then $T$ fixes at least three points of $X$.
