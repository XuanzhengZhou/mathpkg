Evidently $B$ is a Borel subgroup of $N^\circ$. Thanks to the conjugacy theorem (21.3) and the fact that $B$ is normal in $N^\circ$, $B$ is the sole Borel subgroup of $N^\circ$. The density theorem (22.2) then forces $B = N^\circ$.
