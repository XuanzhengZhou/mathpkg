The rank of a symmetric bilinear function $\Phi$ is defined as the difference between the dimension of the ambient space $E$ and the dimension of its nullspace. Equivalently, it is the rank of the associated linear map $\varphi: E \to E^*$. A symmetric bilinear function is non-degenerate precisely when its rank equals the full dimension of $E$.
