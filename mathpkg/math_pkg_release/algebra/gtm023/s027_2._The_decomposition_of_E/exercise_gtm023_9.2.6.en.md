Let $\Phi$ be a symmetric bilinear function in $E$. Assume that $E_1$ is a subspace of $E$ such that $\Phi$ is non-degenerate in $E_1$. Define the subspace $E_2$ as follows: a vector $x_2 \in E$ is contained in $E_2$ if

$$\Phi(x_1, x_2) = 0 \quad \text{for all vectors } x_1 \in E_1.$$

Prove that

$$E = E_1 \oplus E_2.$$
