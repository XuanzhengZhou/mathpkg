This corollary gives a practical matrix criterion for non-degeneracy: a symmetric bilinear function is non-degenerate exactly when its matrix has nonzero determinant. This follows directly from the fact that the rank of $\Phi$ equals the rank of its matrix, and non-degeneracy means the rank is maximal.
