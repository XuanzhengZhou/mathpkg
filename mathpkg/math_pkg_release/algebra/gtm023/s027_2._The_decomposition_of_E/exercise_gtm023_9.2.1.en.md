Let $\Phi \neq 0$ be a given quadratic function. Prove that $\Phi$ can be written in the form

$$\Phi(x) = \varepsilon f(x)^2, \quad \varepsilon = \pm 1$$

where $f$ is a linear function, if and only if the corresponding symmetric bilinear function has rank $1$.
