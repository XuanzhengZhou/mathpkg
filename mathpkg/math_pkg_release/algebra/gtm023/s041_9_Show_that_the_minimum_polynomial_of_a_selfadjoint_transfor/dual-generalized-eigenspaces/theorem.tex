Let $\mu = f_1^{k_1} \cdots f_r^{k_r}$ be the decomposition of the common minimum polynomial of $\varphi$ and $\varphi^*$. Consider the direct decompositions
$$E = E_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus E_r, \quad E^* = F_1^* \oplus \cdots \oplus F_r^*$$
where $E_i = K(f_i^{k_i})$ are the generalized eigenspaces of $\varphi$, and $F_i^* = (\sum_{j \neq i} E_j)^\perp$ are the dual stable subspaces. Then
$$F_i^* \subset E_i^*$$
where $E_i^* = K(f_i^{k_i}(\varphi^*))$ are the generalized eigenspaces of $\varphi^*$.
