Given a primary decomposition of a vector space into generalized eigenspaces, the dual decomposition provides subspaces $F_i^*$ that are contained within the corresponding generalized eigenspaces $E_i^*$ of the dual transformation. This inclusion shows that the dual decomposition is a refinement of the generalized eigenspace decomposition of $\varphi^*$, and a dimension comparison establishes equality.
