Let $E = F_0 \oplus F_1$ be the Fitting decomposition of $E$ with respect to $\varphi$, and let $\varphi_0: F_0 \to F_0$ and $\varphi_1: F_1 \to F_1$ denote the restrictions of $\varphi$ to $F_0$ and $F_1$ respectively. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $F_0$ and $F_1$ are stable subspaces.
\item $\varphi_0$ is nilpotent; i.e., $\varphi_0^l = 0$ for some $l > 0$.
\item $\varphi_1$ is a linear isomorphism.
\item The corresponding projection operators are polynomials in $\varphi$, being sums of the projection operators $\pi_i$ defined in sec. 13.5.
\end{enumerate}
