Let $E$ be an $n$-dimensional real vector space and $\varphi: E \rightarrow E$ be a regular linear transformation. Show that $\varphi$ can be written $\varphi = \varphi_1 \varphi_2$ where every eigenvalue of $\varphi_1$ is positive and every eigenvalue of $\varphi_2$ is negative.
