Any subspace that is invariant under a linear transformation admits a natural decomposition in terms of the generalized eigenspaces. Specifically, the stable subspace is the direct sum of its intersections with each generalized eigenspace. This result is a direct consequence of the fact that the projection operators onto the generalized eigenspaces are polynomials in the transformation, and therefore preserve any stable subspace.
