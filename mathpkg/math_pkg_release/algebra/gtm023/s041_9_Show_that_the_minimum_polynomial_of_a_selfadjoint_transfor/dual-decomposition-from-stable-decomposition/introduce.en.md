A direct sum decomposition of the original space into $\varphi$-stable subspaces naturally induces a dual decomposition of the dual space into $\varphi^*$-stable subspaces. The dual pieces are defined as the orthogonal complements of the sums of all other pieces. This construction preserves the duality pairing: each pair of subspaces $F_i$ and $F_i^*$ is dual, and the restricted transformations are dual to each other.
