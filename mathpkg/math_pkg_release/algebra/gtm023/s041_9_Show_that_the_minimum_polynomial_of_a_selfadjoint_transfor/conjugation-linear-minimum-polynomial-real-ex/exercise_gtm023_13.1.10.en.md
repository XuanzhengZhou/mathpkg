Assume that a conjugation $z \rightarrow \bar{z}$ is defined in the complex vector space $E$ (cf. sec. 11.7). Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow E$ be a linear transformation such that $\overline{\varphi z} = \varphi \bar{z}$. Prove that the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$ has real coefficients.
