The rank of a symmetric bilinear function $\Phi$ is the dimension minus the dimension of its nullspace. Via the map $\varphi: E \to E^*$, the rank equals the rank of the corresponding linear mapping and the rank of the matrix of $\Phi$. Non-degeneracy is equivalent to full rank.
