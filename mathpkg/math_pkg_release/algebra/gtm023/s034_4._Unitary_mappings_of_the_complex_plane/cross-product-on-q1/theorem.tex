Let $Q_1 = \{\varphi \in Q \mid \operatorname{tr} \varphi = 0\}$ be the traceless subalgebra of $Q$. For $\varphi, \psi \in Q_1$, the commutator $\varphi \circ \psi - \psi \circ \varphi$ again belongs to $Q_1$. A bilinear map $\times \colon Q_1 \times Q_1 \to Q_1$ is defined by
$$
\varphi \times \psi = \frac{1}{2}(\varphi \circ \psi - \psi \circ \varphi).
$$
This cross product is orthogonal to both factors: $(\varphi \times \psi, \varphi) = 0$ and $(\varphi \times \psi, \psi) = 0$.
