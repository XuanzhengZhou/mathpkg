For every $\varphi \in Q$, the algebra of complex linear transformations satisfying $\varphi + \tilde{\varphi} = \iota \cdot \operatorname{tr} \varphi$, we have the fundamental identity
$$
\varphi \tilde{\varphi} = \varphi \operatorname{ad} \varphi = \iota \cdot \det \varphi.
$$
Here $\tilde{\varphi}$ is the adjoint, $\operatorname{ad} \varphi$ is the adjugate, and $\iota$ is the identity transformation.
