The collection of all unitary mappings of the complex plane whose determinant equals $+1$ is closed under composition and inversion, hence constitutes a subgroup of the full unitary group. This is the special unitary group $\operatorname{SU}(1)$ (or $\operatorname{SU}(2)$ in real terms), playing a fundamental role in the geometry of the complex plane and in the construction of the quaternions.
