A minor of order $k$ of an $n \times m$-matrix $A$ is the determinant of a $k \times k$ submatrix formed by selecting $k$ rows and $k$ columns. Minors are fundamental invariants that capture the linear dependence structure of the matrix; in particular, the rank of $A$ is the largest $r$ for which there exists a non-zero minor of order $r$.
