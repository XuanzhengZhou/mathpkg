The determinant of the dual transformation $\varphi^*$ equals the determinant of $\varphi$, i.e., $\det \varphi^* = \det \varphi$. This is proved using dual determinant functions and the pairing identity $\langle \varphi^* x^{*i}, x_j \rangle = \langle x^{*i}, \varphi x_j \rangle$. An important corollary is that a matrix and its transpose have the same determinant: $\det A^* = \det A$.
