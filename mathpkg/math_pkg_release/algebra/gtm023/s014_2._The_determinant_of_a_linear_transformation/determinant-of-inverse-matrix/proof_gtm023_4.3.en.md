From the multiplicativity formula (4.18), $\det(AB) = \det A \det B$, we have for $B = A^{-1}$:

$$\det A \cdot \det(A^{-1}) = \det(AA^{-1}) = \det I = 1.$$

Since $\det A \neq 0$ for an invertible matrix, we can divide to obtain $\det(A^{-1}) = (\det A)^{-1}$.
