Suppose $\Delta \subset \Gamma$ is a subfield such that $\Gamma$ has finite dimension over $\Delta$. Suppose further that $A \subset \Gamma$ is a subalgebra such that $\Delta \subset A$. Prove that $A$ is a subfield of $\Gamma$.
