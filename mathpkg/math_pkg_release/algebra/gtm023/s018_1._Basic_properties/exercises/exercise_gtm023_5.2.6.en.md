Let $I$ be any principal ideal in $S$ (cf. Problem 3). Show that there exists a complementary principal ideal $I_1$. Conversely, if $S = I \oplus I_1$ is a decomposition of $S$ into ideals, prove that $I$ and $I_1$ are principal.
