Let $A$ be any algebra with identity and consider the multiplication operator $\mu: A \to A(A; A)$. Show that $\mu$ is a monomorphism. If $A = L(E; E)$, show that by a suitable restriction of $\mu$ a monomorphism $GL(E) \to GL(L(E; E))$ can be obtained.
