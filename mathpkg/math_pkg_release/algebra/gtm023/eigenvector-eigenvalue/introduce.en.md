Eigenvectors are non-zero vectors that are mapped to scalar multiples of themselves by a linear transformation. The scalar factor is the eigenvalue. Eigenvalues are exactly the roots of the characteristic equation $\det(\varphi - \lambda\iota) = 0$. Not every linear transformation has eigenvectors over a given field (e.g., rotations over $\mathbb{R}$).
