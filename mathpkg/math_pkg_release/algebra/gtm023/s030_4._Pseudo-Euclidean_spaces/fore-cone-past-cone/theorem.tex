Let $E$ be a pseudo-Euclidean space of index $n-1$. Define an equivalence relation on the set $T$ of all time-like vectors by
$$z_1 \sim z_2 \quad \text{if} \quad (z_1, z_2) < 0.$$
This relation is symmetric, reflexive, and transitive, and induces a decomposition of $T$ into two classes $T^+$ and $T^-$, called the \text{fore-cone} and \text{past-cone}, which are obtained from each other by the reflection $x \mapsto -x$.
