For an orthochroneous Lorentz transformation, any eigenvalue associated with an eigenvector on the light-cone must be positive. This follows from the fact that orthochroneous transformations preserve the fore-cone and past-cone, and can be proved by considering a time-like perturbation of the light-vector and using the invariance of the causal structure.
