Find the eigenvectors of the Lorentz-transformation defined by the matrix
$$\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{1}{2} & 0 & -1 & -\frac{1}{2} \\
0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\
\frac{1}{2} & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2}
\end{bmatrix}.$$
Verify that there exists an eigenvector on the light-cone.
