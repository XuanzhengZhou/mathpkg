This lemma establishes the inductive step needed for the simultaneous diagonalization of a pair of symmetric bilinear functions. Given a non-degenerate symmetric bilinear function $\Psi$ and a vector $e_1$ satisfying the generalized eigenvalue equation from the previous step, the orthogonal complement $E_1 = \{z : \Psi(e_1, z) = 0\}$ has dimension $n-1$ and inherits the non-degeneracy of $\Psi$. This allows the construction to be repeated on $E_1$, yielding a sequence of vectors $e_1, e_2, \ldots, e_n$ that form a basis in which both bilinear functions are diagonal.
