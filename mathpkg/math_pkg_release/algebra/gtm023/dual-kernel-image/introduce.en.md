The relationships between kernels and images of a map and its dual are expressed via the annihilator operation. Surjectivity of $\varphi$ implies injectivity of $\varphi^*$. For dual spaces of linear functions $L(E)$ and $L(F)$, injectivity of $\varphi$ implies surjectivity of $\varphi^*$, and $\operatorname{Im}\varphi^* = (\ker\varphi)^\perp$. These formulas are fundamental in duality theory.
