Show that a real 3-dimensional vector space cannot be irreducible with respect to a semisimple linear transformation. Conclude that the pseudo-Euclidean rotation of problem 5 is not semisimple. Use this to show that a linear transformation in a self dual space which satisfies $\varphi \varphi^* = \varphi^* \varphi$ is not necessarily semisimple (cf. problem 1).
