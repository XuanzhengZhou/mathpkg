Theorem III (13.26) describes the structure of $C(\varphi)$ when $\varphi$ is semisimple. Decomposing $E$ into generalized eigenspaces $E_i$, the transformations in $C(\varphi)$ preserving each $E_i$ form an ideal $I_i \cong C(\varphi_i)$. Since the minimum polynomial of $\varphi_i$ is irreducible, $\Gamma(\varphi_i)$ is a field, and $C(\varphi_i) = A_{\Gamma(\varphi_i)}(E_i; E_i)$ is the full transformation algebra over this extension field.
