A nilpotent transformation has all eigenvalues equal to zero. The minimum polynomial is a pure power $t^m$, and the Fitting null component is the entire space. The sum and composition of commuting nilpotent transformations remain nilpotent, making the set of nilpotent transformations commuting with a given $\varphi$ an algebraic structure closed under these operations.
