Prove that a linear transformation $\varphi$ with minimum polynomial $\mu$ is diagonalizable if and only if

(i) $\mu$ is completely reducible

(ii) $\varphi$ is semisimple

Show that (i) and (ii) are equivalent to
$$\mu = (t - \lambda_1) \ldots (t - \lambda_r)$$
where the $\lambda_i$ are distinct scalars.
