Let
$$E = F_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus F_s$$
be any decomposition of $E$ into a direct sum of stable subspaces. Then the following three conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(i)] Each $F_j$ is a direct sum of some of the generalized eigenspaces $E_i$ of $\varphi$.
  \item[(ii)] The projection operators $\varrho_j$ associated with the decomposition are polynomials in $\varphi$.
  \item[(iii)] Every stable subspace $U \subset E$ satisfies $U = \sum_j U \cap F_j$.
\end{enumerate}
