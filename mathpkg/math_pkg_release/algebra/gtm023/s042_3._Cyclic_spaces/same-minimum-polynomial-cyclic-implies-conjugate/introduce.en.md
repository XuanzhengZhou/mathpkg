Lemma I shows that for cyclic spaces, the minimum polynomial alone determines the transformation up to conjugacy. Given generators $a$ and $b$ of $E$ and $F$, their iterates form bases, and the isomorphism is defined by mapping $\varphi^j(a) \mapsto \psi^j(b)$. The equal minimum polynomial ensures the same linear relations among iterates.
