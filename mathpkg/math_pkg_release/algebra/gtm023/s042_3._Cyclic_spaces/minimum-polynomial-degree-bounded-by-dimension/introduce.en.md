Theorem I establishes the fundamental inequality $\deg \mu \leq \dim E$ for any linear transformation $\varphi$, with equality precisely when $E$ is cyclic. Any cyclic subspace $F \subset E$ satisfies $\dim F \leq \deg \mu$, since its minimum polynomial $\nu$ divides $\mu$ and $\dim F = \deg \nu$. The proof uses Proposition V to produce a cyclic subspace of dimension $\deg \mu$, giving the inequality.
