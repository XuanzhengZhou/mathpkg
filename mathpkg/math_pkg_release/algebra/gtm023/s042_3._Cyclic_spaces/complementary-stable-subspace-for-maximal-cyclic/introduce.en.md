Lemma I is the crucial step for decomposing $E$ into cyclic subspaces. Given a maximal cyclic subspace $E_a$ (one whose dimension equals $\deg \mu$), there exists a complementary subspace $F$ that is also stable under $\varphi$. The proof uses the dual transformation and the fact that the dual quotient $E^*/E_a^\perp$ is cyclic.
