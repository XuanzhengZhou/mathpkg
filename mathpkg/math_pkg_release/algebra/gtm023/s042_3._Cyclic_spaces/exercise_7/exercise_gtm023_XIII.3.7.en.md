Suppose $F \subset E$ is stable under $\varphi$ and let $\varphi_F: F \rightarrow F$ (minimum polynomial $\mu_F$) and $\bar{\varphi}: E/F \rightarrow E/F$ (minimum polynomial $\bar{\mu}$) be the induced transformations. Show that $E$ is irreducible if and only if

(i) $E/F$ is irreducible.

(ii) $F$ is irreducible.

(iii) $\mu_F = f^k$, $\bar{\mu} = f^l$, $\mu = f^{k+l}$ where $f$ is an irreducible polynomial.
