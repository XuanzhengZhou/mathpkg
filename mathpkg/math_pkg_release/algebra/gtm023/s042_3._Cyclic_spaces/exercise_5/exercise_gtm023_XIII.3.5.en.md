Let $E_1 \subset E$ be a stable subspace under $\varphi$ and consider the induced mappings $\varphi_1: E_1 \rightarrow E_1$ and $\bar{\varphi}: E/E_1 \rightarrow E/E_1$. Let $\mu, \mu_1, \bar{\mu}$ be the corresponding minimum polynomials.

a) Prove that $E$ is cyclic if and only if
   (i) $E_1$ is cyclic
   (ii) $E/E_1$ is cyclic
   (iii) $(\mu_1, \bar{\mu}) = 1$ (the polynomials are relatively prime).
