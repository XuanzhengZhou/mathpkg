Let $E$ be irreducible (and hence cyclic) with respect to $\varphi$. Show that the set $S$ of generators of the cyclic space $E$ is not a subspace. Construct a subspace $F$ such that $S$ is in $1-1$ correspondence with the non-zero elements of $E/F$.
