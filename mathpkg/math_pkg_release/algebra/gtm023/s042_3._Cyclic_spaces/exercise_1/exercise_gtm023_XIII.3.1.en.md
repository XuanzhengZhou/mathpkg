Let $\Phi: A(E; E) \rightarrow A(E; E)$ be a non-zero algebra endomorphism.

(i) Show that for any projection operator $\pi$,
$$r(\Phi(\pi)) = r(\pi), \quad \varphi \in A(E; E).$$

(ii) Use (i) to prove that $r(\Phi(\varphi)) = r(\varphi)$.
