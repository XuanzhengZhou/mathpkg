Proposition I asserts that $E$ can be written as a direct sum of subspaces that are irreducible with respect to $\varphi$. The proof uses a maximality argument: take a decomposition into the maximal possible number of stable subspaces (bounded by $\dim E$), and each component must then be irreducible, otherwise the decomposition could be further refined.
