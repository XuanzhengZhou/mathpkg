For a cyclic space $E$, the degree of the minimum polynomial equals the dimension of the space. This follows because if $a$ is a generator of $E$, then $K_a = I_\mu$ and $\sigma_a$ induces an isomorphism $\Gamma[t]/I_\mu \cong E$. The dimension of the quotient ring $\Gamma[t]/I_\mu$ is exactly $\deg \mu$, giving the equality. This is a key structural result that characterizes cyclic spaces.
