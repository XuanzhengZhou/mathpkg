Let $a$ be a generator of $E$. Then, since $K_a = I_{\mu}$, $\sigma_a$ induces an isomorphism
$$\Gamma[t] / I_{\mu} \xrightarrow{\cong} E.$$

It follows that (cf. Proposition I, sec. 12.11)
$$\dim E = \dim \Gamma[t] / I_{\mu} = \deg \mu.$$
