Theorem II states that any vector space $E$ with a linear transformation $\varphi$ can be decomposed into a direct sum of cyclic subspaces. The proof proceeds by induction on $\dim E$, using Lemma I which provides a complementary stable subspace for a maximal cyclic subspace $E_a$ with $\dim E_a = \deg \mu$.
