The number of irreducible subspaces of each dimension in a decomposition of $E$ is uniquely determined by $\varphi$. When $\mu = f^k$, the ranks of the iterates $\psi^j = f(\varphi)^j$ determine the distribution of irreducible subspaces via the second difference formula. This shows that while the decomposition itself is not unique, the multiplicity of irreducible subspaces of each dimension is an invariant of $\varphi$.
