Let $\partial$ be a differential operator in $E$. Consider the set $I$ of transformations $\psi \in C(\partial)$ such that $\psi Z(E) \subset B(E)$ (cf. sec. 6.7).

Show that $I$ is an ideal in $C(\partial)$ and establish an algebra isomorphism
$$C(\partial) / I \rightarrow A(H(E); H(E)).$$
