In view of Proposition I there is a vector $a \in E$ such that $\ker \sigma_a = I_\mu$. Then $\sigma_a$ induces an isomorphism
$$\Gamma[t] / I_\mu \xrightarrow{\cong} E_a.$$

It follows that
$$\dim E_a = \dim \Gamma[t] / I_\mu = \deg \mu.$$
