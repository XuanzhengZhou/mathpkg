Proposition II characterizes conjugate transformations by two computable invariants: the minimum polynomial and the ranks of all polynomial evaluations. Condition (ii) is equivalent to the equality of the numbers $N_i(\varphi) = N_i(\psi)$ of irreducible subspaces of each dimension, which is the complete set of invariants for conjugacy.
