Let $\varphi$ be nilpotent of degree $k$ in a 6-dimensional vector space $E$. For each $k$ ($1 \leq k \leq 6$) determine the possible ranks of $\varphi$ and show that $k$ and $r(\varphi)$ determine the numbers $N_\lambda$ explicitly. Conclude that two nilpotent transformations $\varphi$ and $\psi$ are conjugate if and only if
$$r(\varphi) = r(\psi) \quad \text{and} \quad \deg \varphi = \deg \psi.$$
