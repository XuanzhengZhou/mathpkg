It is easily checked that $\mu(\varphi) = 0$ and so $\mu$ is a multiple of the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$. On the other hand, by Proposition II, the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$ has degree $n$ and thus it must coincide with $\mu$.
