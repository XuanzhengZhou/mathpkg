We verify that $y^{-1}x^{-1}$ is indeed the inverse of $xy$:
$$(xy)(y^{-1}x^{-1}) = x(yy^{-1})x^{-1} = xex^{-1} = xx^{-1} = e,$$
$$(y^{-1}x^{-1})(xy) = y^{-1}(x^{-1}x)y = y^{-1}ey = y^{-1}y = e.$$
By uniqueness of the inverse, $(xy)^{-1} = y^{-1}x^{-1}$.
