Given an inner product space \(E\) and a subspace \(E_1\) of finite dimension, consider a decomposition

$$x = x_1 + x_2, \quad x_1 \in E_1$$

and the projection

$$x = p + h, \quad p \in E_1, \; h \in E_1^{\perp}.$$

Prove that

$$|x_2| \geq |h|$$

and that equality is assumed only if \(x_1 = p\) and \(x_2 = h\).
