Let \(F^3\) be the space of all polynomials of degree \(\leq 2\). Define the inner product of two polynomials as follows:

$$(P, Q) = \int_{-1}^{1} P(t) Q(t) \, dt.$$

The vectors \(1, t, t^2\) form a basis in \(F^3\). Orthogonalize and orthonormalize this basis. Generalize the result for the case of the space \(F^n\) of polynomials of degree \(\leq n-1\).
