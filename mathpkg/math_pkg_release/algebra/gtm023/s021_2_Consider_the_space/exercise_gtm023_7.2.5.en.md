Consider a subspace \(E_1\) of \(E\). Assume an orthogonal decomposition

$$E_1 = F_1 \oplus G_1, \quad F_1 \perp G_1.$$

Establish the relations

$$F_1^{\perp} = E_1^{\perp} \oplus G_1, \quad E_1^{\perp} \perp G_1 \quad \text{and} \quad G_1^{\perp} = E_1^{\perp} \oplus F_1, \quad E_1^{\perp} \perp F_1.$$
