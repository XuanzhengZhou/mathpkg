Formula (7.20) from the orthogonal decomposition theorem gives

$$|x|^2 = |p|^2 + |h|^2.$$

Since \(|h|^2 \geq 0\), it follows immediately that

$$|x|^2 \geq |p|^2,$$

and taking square roots yields \(|x| \geq |p|\). Equality \(|x| = |p|\) holds if and only if \(|h|^2 = 0\), i.e., \(h = 0\). By the decomposition \(x = p + h\), this is equivalent to \(x = p \in E_1\).
