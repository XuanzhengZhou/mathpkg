Prove that the area of the parallelogram generated by the vectors \(x_1\) and \(x_2\) is given by

$$A = 2 \sqrt{s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c)},$$

where

$$a = |x_1|, \quad b = |x_2|, \quad c = |x_2 - x_1|, \quad s = \tfrac{1}{2}(a + b + c).$$
