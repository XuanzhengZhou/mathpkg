Consider two inner product spaces \(E_1\) and \(E_2\). Prove that an inner product is defined in the direct sum \(E_1 \oplus E_2\) by

$$((x_1, x_2), (y_1, y_2)) = (x_1, y_1) + (x_2, y_2), \quad x_1, y_1 \in E_1, \; x_2, y_2 \in E_2.$$
