Let $E_1, E_2, E_3$ be $G$-graded vector spaces. Suppose that $\varphi: E_1 \rightarrow E_2$ and $\psi: E_1 \rightarrow E_3$ are linear mappings, homogeneous of degree $k$ and $\ell$ respectively. Assume that $\psi$ can be factored over $\varphi$.
