Suppose $E, E^*, F$ are as in problem 8. Let $F_1$ be a complementary $G$-graded subspace for $F$ in $E$ (cf. problem 2). Prove that
$$E^* = F^\perp \oplus F_1^\perp$$
and that $F, F_1^\perp$ and $F_1, F^\perp$ are two pairs of dual $G$-graded spaces.
