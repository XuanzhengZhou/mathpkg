Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ be an injective linear mapping. Assume that $F$ is a $G$-graded vector space and that $\operatorname{Im} \varphi$ is a $G$-graded subspace of $F$. Prove that there is a unique $G$-gradation in $E$ so that $\varphi$ becomes homogeneous of degree zero.
