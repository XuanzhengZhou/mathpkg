Let $E = \sum_{k \in G} E_k$ and $F = \sum_{k \in G} F_k$ be two $G$-graded vector spaces, and let $\varphi: E \times F \rightarrow \Gamma$ be a scalar product which respects the $G$-gradations. Then $E_k$ and $F_k$ are dual, and
$$\dim E_k = \dim F_k \quad k \in G.$$
In particular, if $G = \mathbb{Z}$ and the gradations of $E$ and $F$ are positive, we have
$$P_E = P_F.$$
