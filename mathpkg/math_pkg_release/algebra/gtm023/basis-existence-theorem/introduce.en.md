Theorem I establishes the existence of bases for all vector spaces using Zorn's Lemma. It shows that given any linearly independent subset $R$ contained in a system of generators $S$, there exists a basis $T$ with $R \subset T \subset S$. This implies that every vector space has a basis and every linearly independent set extends to a basis.
