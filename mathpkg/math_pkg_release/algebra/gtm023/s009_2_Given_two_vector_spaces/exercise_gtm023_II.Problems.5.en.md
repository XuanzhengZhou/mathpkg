Let $\pi_i$ ($i = 1, \dots, r$) be projection operators in $E$ such that

$$\text{Im} \, \pi_i = F \quad (i = 1, \dots, r)$$

where $F$ is a fixed subspace of $E$. Let $\lambda^i$ ($i = 1, \dots, r$) be scalars. Show that:

a) If $\sum_i \lambda^i = 1$, then $\pi = \sum_i \lambda^i \pi_i$ is again a projection operator with $\text{Im} \, \pi = F$.
