Consider the mapping $\varphi: E_1 \times E_2 \rightarrow E$ defined by $\varphi(x, -x)$ where $x \in E_1 \cap E_2$. Show that $\varphi$ is a linear isomorphism if and only if the decomposition $E = E_1 + E_2$ is direct.
