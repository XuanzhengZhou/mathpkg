From the first distributive law with $\mu = -\lambda$:
$$\lambda x + (-\lambda)x = (\lambda - \lambda)x = 0 \cdot x = 0,$$
hence $(-\lambda)x = -\lambda x$.

Similarly, the second distributive law gives
$$\lambda(-x) = -\lambda x.$$
