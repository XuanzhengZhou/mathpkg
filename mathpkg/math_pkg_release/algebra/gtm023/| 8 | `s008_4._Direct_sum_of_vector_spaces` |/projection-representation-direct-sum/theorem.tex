A projection operator $\varphi$ on a vector space $E$ can be expressed as the direct sum of the identity map on its image and the zero map on its kernel:

$$
\varphi = \iota_{\operatorname{Im} \varphi} \oplus 0_{\ker \varphi},
$$

where $\iota_{\operatorname{Im} \varphi}$ denotes the identity transformation on $\operatorname{Im} \varphi$ and $0_{\ker \varphi}$ denotes the zero transformation on $\ker \varphi$.
