Suppose that

$$
E = \bigoplus_v E_v
$$

is a decomposition of $E$ as a direct sum of subspaces $E_v$. Let $\pi_v: E \to E_v$ and $i_v: E_v \to E$ denote the canonical projections and injections, and consider the linear mappings $\varrho_v: E \to E$ defined by

$$
\varrho_v = i_v \circ \pi_v.
$$

Then the $\varrho_v$ are projection operators satisfying

$$
\varrho_v \circ \varrho_\mu = 0 \quad (v \neq \mu), \qquad \sum_v \varrho_v = \iota_E,
$$

and moreover $\operatorname{Im} \varrho_v = E_v$.
