Let $E$ be a Minkowski space and $\varphi$ a proper orthochronous Lorentz transformation satisfying Case II (minimum polynomial completely reducible, not all roots equal to $1$). Let $e$ and $e'$ be eigenvectors as above, and let $F$ be the plane generated by $e$ and $e'$. For any vector $z = \xi e + \eta e'$ in $F$,

$$(z, z) = 2(e, e') \xi \eta.$$

This equation shows that the induced inner product on $F$ has index $1$.
