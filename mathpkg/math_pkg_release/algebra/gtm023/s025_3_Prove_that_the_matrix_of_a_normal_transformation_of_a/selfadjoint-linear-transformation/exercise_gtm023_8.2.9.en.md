Let $\varphi$ be a linear transformation of $E$ and $\tilde{\varphi}$ the adjoint map. Denote by $|\varphi|$ the norm of $\varphi$ which is induced by the Euclidean norm of $E$. Prove that

$$|\varphi|^2 = \lambda$$

where $\lambda$ is the largest eigenvalue of the selfadjoint mapping $\tilde{\varphi} \circ \varphi$.
