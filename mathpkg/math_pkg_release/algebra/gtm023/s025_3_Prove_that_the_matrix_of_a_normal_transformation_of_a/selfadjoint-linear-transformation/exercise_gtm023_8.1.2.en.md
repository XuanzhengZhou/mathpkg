Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow E$ be a linear transformation and $\tilde{\varphi}$ be the adjoint transformation. Prove that if $F \subset E$ is stable under $\varphi$, then $F^{\perp}$ is stable under $\tilde{\varphi}$.
