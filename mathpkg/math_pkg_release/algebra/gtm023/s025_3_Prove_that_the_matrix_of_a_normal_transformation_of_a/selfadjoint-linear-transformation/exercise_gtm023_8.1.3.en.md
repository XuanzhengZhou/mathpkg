Prove that the matrix of a normal transformation of a 2-dimensional space with respect to an orthonormal basis has the form

$$\begin{pmatrix} \alpha & \beta \\ -\beta & \alpha \end{pmatrix} \quad \text{or} \quad \begin{pmatrix} \alpha & \beta \\ \beta & \delta \end{pmatrix}.$$
