If the sequence
$$F \xrightarrow{\varphi} E \xrightarrow{\psi} G$$
is exact at $E$, then the dual sequence
$$L(F) \xleftarrow{\varphi^*} L(E) \xleftarrow{\psi^*} L(G)$$
is exact at $L(E)$.

If
$$0 \to F \xrightarrow{\varphi} E \xrightarrow{\psi} G \to 0$$
is a short exact sequence, then the dual sequence
$$0 \leftarrow L(F) \xleftarrow{\varphi^*} L(E) \xleftarrow{\psi^*} L(G) \leftarrow 0$$
is again a short exact sequence.
