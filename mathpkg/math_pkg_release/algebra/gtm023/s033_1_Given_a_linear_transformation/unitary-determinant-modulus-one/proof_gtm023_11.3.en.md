Let $\varphi$ be a unitary mapping. Passing to determinants, the defining relation $\varphi^* \varphi = \text{id}$ yields

$$\det(\varphi^*) \cdot \det(\varphi) = \det(\text{id}) = 1.$$

Since $\det(\varphi^*) = \overline{\det \varphi}$ for a unitary mapping with respect to an orthonormal basis, we obtain

$$\det \varphi \cdot \overline{\det \varphi} = 1,$$

whence

$$|\det \varphi| = 1.$$
