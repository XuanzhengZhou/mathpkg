Every linear map $\varphi: E \to F$ factors as $E \xrightarrow{\pi} E/\ker\varphi \xrightarrow{\bar{\varphi}} \operatorname{Im}\varphi \xrightarrow{i} F$, where $\pi$ is surjective, $\bar{\varphi}$ is an isomorphism, and $i$ is injective. This is the fundamental factorization (or canonical decomposition) of a linear map. The factoring condition $\ker\varphi \subset \ker\psi$ characterizes when $\psi$ factors through $\varphi$.
