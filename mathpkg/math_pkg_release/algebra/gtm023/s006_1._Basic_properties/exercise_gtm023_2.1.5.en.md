Prove that if $\varphi: E \to F$ and $\psi: E \to G$ are linear mappings, then the relation
$$\ker \varphi \subset \ker \psi$$
is necessary for the existence of a linear mapping $\chi: F \to G$ such that $\psi = \chi \circ \varphi$.
