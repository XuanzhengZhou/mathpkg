A sequence of vector spaces and linear mappings
$$
\cdots \longrightarrow E_{i-1} \xrightarrow{\varphi_{i-1}} E_i \xrightarrow{\varphi_i} E_{i+1} \longrightarrow \cdots
$$
is called an \text{exact sequence} if at each intermediate space the image of the incoming map equals the kernel of the outgoing map:
$$
\operatorname{Im} \varphi_{i-1} = \ker \varphi_i.
$$
