Unlike the situation for general modules over a ring, every short exact sequence of vector spaces splits. This is because every subspace of a vector space admits a complementary subspace. The splitting map $\chi$ is constructed by restricting $\psi$ to a complementary subspace of $\ker \psi$, where it becomes an isomorphism, and then inverting.
