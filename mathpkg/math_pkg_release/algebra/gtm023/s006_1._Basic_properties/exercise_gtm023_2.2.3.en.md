Let $\varphi: E \to F$ be a linear mapping.

a) Consider the space $M^l(\varphi)$ of linear mappings $\psi: F \to E$ such that $\psi \circ \varphi = 0$. Prove that if $\varphi$ is surjective then $M^l(\varphi) = 0$.

b) Consider the space $M^r(\varphi)$ of linear mappings $\psi: F \to E$ such that $\varphi \circ \psi = 0$. Prove that if $\varphi$ is injective then $M^r(\varphi) = 0$.
