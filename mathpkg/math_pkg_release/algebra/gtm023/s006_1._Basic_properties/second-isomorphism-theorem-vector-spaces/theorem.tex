For any two subspaces $E_1 \subset E$ and $E_2 \subset E$ of a vector space $E$, there is a natural linear isomorphism
$$
E_1 / (E_1 \cap E_2) \xrightarrow{\cong} (E_1 + E_2) / E_2.
$$
In particular, if $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$, then $E_1 \cap E_2 = 0$ and the isomorphism reduces to
$$
E_1 \xrightarrow{\cong} E / E_2.
$$
