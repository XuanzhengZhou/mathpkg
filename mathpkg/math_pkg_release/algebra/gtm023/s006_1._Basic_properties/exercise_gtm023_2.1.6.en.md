Consider the pairs $(\psi, \varphi)$ in parts a, b, c of problems 2 and 3. Decide in each case if $\psi$ can be factored over $\varphi$, or if $\varphi$ can be factored over $\psi$, or if both factorings are possible. Whenever $\psi$ can be factored over $\varphi$ (or conversely) construct an explicit factoring map.
