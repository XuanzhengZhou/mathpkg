Let $E$ be a finite-dimensional normed linear space. Then every linear transformation $\varphi$ of $E$ is bounded, i.e.,

$$B(E; E) = L(E; E).$$

Moreover, since the closed unit sphere is compact in a finite-dimensional space, the operator norm is given by

$$\|\varphi\| = \max_{\|x\| = 1} \|\varphi x\|.$$
