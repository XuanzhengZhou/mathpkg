The kernel of a linear mapping $\varphi: E \to F$ is the subspace $\ker \varphi = \{x \in E \mid \varphi x = 0\}$. The image is $\operatorname{Im} \varphi = \{\varphi x \mid x \in E\}$. The kernel measures failure of injectivity: $\varphi$ is injective iff $\ker \varphi = \{0\}$.
