The internal direct sum of two subspaces $E_1$ and $E_2$ is their sum with the additional condition that $E_1 \cap E_2 = 0$. In a direct sum, every vector has a unique decomposition as $x = x_1 + x_2$ with $x_i \in E_i$. If $T_1$ and $T_2$ are bases of $E_1$ and $E_2$, then $T_1 \cup T_2$ is a basis of $E_1 \oplus E_2$.
