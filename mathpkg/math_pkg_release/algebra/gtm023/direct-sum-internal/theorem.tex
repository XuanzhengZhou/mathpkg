Let $E_1$ and $E_2$ be subspaces of $E$. Their sum is
$$E_1 + E_2 = \{x_1 + x_2 \mid x_1 \in E_1, x_2 \in E_2\}.$$

The decomposition $x = x_1 + x_2$ of a vector $x \in E_1 + E_2$ is uniquely determined if and only if $E_1 \cap E_2 = 0$. In this case $E_1 + E_2$ is called the (internal) direct sum of $E_1$ and $E_2$ and is denoted by $E_1 \oplus E_2$.
