Let $\varphi$ be a linear transformation of the vector space $E$. A subspace $E_1 \subset E$ is called stable under $\varphi$ if
$$\varphi x \in E_1 \quad \text{for} \quad x \in E_1.$$

The subspaces $\ker \varphi$ and $\operatorname{Im} \varphi$ are stable. If $E_1$ is a stable subspace, the restriction $\varphi_1$ of $\varphi$ to $E_1$ is a linear transformation of $E_1$, and the induced map $\bar{\varphi}: E/E_1 \to E/E_1$ is a linear transformation of $E/E_1$.
