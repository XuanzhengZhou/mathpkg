Suppose $E = \bigoplus_\alpha E_\alpha$ is a direct sum of subspaces. The \textbf{canonical injections} $i_\alpha: E_\alpha \to E$ and \textbf{canonical projections} $\pi_\alpha: E \to E_\alpha$ satisfy:
$$\pi_\alpha \circ i_\beta = \begin{cases} 1 & \beta = \alpha \\ 0 & \beta \neq \alpha \end{cases}$$
$$\sum_\alpha i_\alpha \pi_\alpha x = x \quad x \in E.$$
The $\pi_\alpha$ are surjective linear mappings.
