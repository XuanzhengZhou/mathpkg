Since $\pi_E$ is surjective, $\bar{\varphi}$ is uniquely determined by the relation $\bar{\varphi} \circ \pi_E = \pi_F \circ \varphi$ if it exists. To define $\bar{\varphi}$, note that $\pi_F \varphi x_1 = \pi_F \varphi x_2$ whenever $\pi_E x_1 = \pi_E x_2$ (since $x_1 - x_2 \in E_1$ implies $\varphi(x_1 - x_2) \in F_1$, so $\pi_F \varphi x_1 = \pi_F \varphi x_2$). Thus $\bar{\varphi}(\pi_E x) := \pi_F \varphi x$ is well-defined and linear.
