Let $\varphi: E \to F$ be a linear mapping and $\varphi_1: E_1 \to F_1$ be its restriction to subspaces $E_1 \subset E$ and $F_1 \subset F$. Then there exists precisely one linear mapping
$$\bar{\varphi}: E/E_1 \to F/F_1$$
such that
$$\bar{\varphi} \circ \pi_E = \pi_F \circ \varphi$$
where $\pi_E: E \to E/E_1$ and $\pi_F: F \to F/F_1$ are the canonical projections.
