Given a basis of $E$ that extends a basis of the subspace $E_1$, the images under the canonical projection of the complementary basis vectors form a basis of the factor space $E/E_1$. This theorem provides a concrete description of the factor space basis and implies the dimension formula $\dim E = \dim E_1 + \dim E/E_1$.
