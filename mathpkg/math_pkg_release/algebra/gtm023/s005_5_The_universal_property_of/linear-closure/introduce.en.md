The linear closure (or span) of a subset $S$ of a vector space $E$ is the smallest subspace containing $S$. It consists of all finite linear combinations of vectors from $S$. The set $S$ serves as a system of generators for its linear closure, and if $S$ is linearly independent, it forms a basis for this subspace.
