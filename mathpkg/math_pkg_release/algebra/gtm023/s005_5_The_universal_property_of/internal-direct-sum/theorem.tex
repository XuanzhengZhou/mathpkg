Let $E_1$ and $E_2$ be subspaces of a vector space $E$. A vector $x \in E_1 + E_2$ can generally be written in several ways as $x = x_1 + x_2$ with $x_1 \in E_1$, $x_2 \in E_2$. The decomposition is uniquely determined if and only if $E_1 \cap E_2 = \{0\}$. In this case $E_1 + E_2$ is called the \textbf{internal direct sum} of $E_1$ and $E_2$ and is denoted by $E_1 \oplus E_2$.
