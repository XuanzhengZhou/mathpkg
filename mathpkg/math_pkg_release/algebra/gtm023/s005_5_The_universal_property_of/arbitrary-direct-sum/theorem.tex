Let $E_\alpha \subset E$, $\alpha \in A$, be an arbitrary family of subspaces. The sum $\sum_\alpha E_\alpha$ is called a \textbf{direct sum} if for every $\alpha \in A$,
$$E_\alpha \cap \sum_{\beta \neq \alpha} E_\beta = \{0\}.$$
In this case each vector $x \in \sum_\alpha E_\alpha$ has a unique representation as a finite sum $x = \sum_\alpha x_\alpha$ with $x_\alpha \in E_\alpha$.
