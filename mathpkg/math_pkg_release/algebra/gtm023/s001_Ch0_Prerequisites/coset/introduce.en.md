Cosets are the equivalence classes of a commutative group $G$ under the relation $x \sim x' \iff x - x' \in H$, where $H$ is a subgroup. They partition $G$ into disjoint translates of $H$. In linear algebra, cosets correspond to the affine subspaces parallel to a given subspace.
