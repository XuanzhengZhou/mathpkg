Every short exact sequence of vector spaces splits: this follows from the existence of complementary subspaces. Let $E_1$ be a complement of $\ker\psi$, then $\psi|_{E_1}$ is an isomorphism whose inverse provides the splitting. This is a special property of vector spaces (not true for modules over general rings), reflecting the fact that every subspace has a complement.
