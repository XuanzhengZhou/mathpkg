Let $E, E^*$ be a pair of dual spaces and assume that $E$ has finite dimension. Then the injection $\Phi: E^* \rightarrow L(E)$ defined by

$$\langle \Phi x^*, x \rangle = \langle x^*, x \rangle, \quad x^* \in E^*, \quad x \in E$$

is surjective and hence a linear isomorphism. In particular, $E^*$ has finite dimension and

$$\dim E^* = \dim E.$$
