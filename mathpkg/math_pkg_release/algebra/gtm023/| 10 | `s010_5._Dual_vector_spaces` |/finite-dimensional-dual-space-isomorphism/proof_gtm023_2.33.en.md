Since $\Phi$ is injective and $\dim L(E) = \dim E$, it follows that

$$\dim E^* \leq \dim E.$$

Hence $E^*$ has finite dimension. In view of the symmetry between $E$ and $E^*$, we also have that

$$\dim E \leq \dim E^*,$$

whence $\dim E^* = \dim E$. On the other hand, $\dim L(E) = \dim E = \dim E^*$, and since $\Phi$ is injective, it must also be surjective. Thus $\Phi$ is a linear isomorphism.
