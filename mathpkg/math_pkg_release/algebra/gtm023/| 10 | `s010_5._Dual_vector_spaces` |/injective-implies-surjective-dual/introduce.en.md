When a linear mapping $\varphi$ is injective, its dual $\varphi^*: L(E) \leftarrow L(F)$ is surjective. This follows directly from the relation $\operatorname{Im} \varphi^* = (\ker \varphi)^\perp$: injectivity means $\ker \varphi = 0$, so $(\ker \varphi)^\perp = L(E)$, and thus every linear function on $E$ is a pullback of some linear function on $F$.
