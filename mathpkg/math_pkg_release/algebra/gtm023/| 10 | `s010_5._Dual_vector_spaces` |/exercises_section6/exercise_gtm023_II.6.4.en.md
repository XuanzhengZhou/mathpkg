Given a pair of dual bases $x^{*v}, x_v$ ($v = 1, \ldots, n$) of $E^*, E$, show that the bases

$$(x^{*1} + \sum_{v=2}^n \lambda_v x^{*v}, x^{*2}, \ldots, x^{*n}) \quad \text{and} \quad (x_1, x_2 - \lambda_2 x_1, \ldots, x_n - \lambda_n x_1)$$

are again dual.
