The orthogonal complement of the kernel of the dual mapping equals the image of the original mapping. This is the dual counterpart to the relation $\operatorname{Im} \varphi^* = (\ker \varphi)^\perp$, completing the symmetric picture: the image of a map and the kernel of its dual are mutual orthogonal complements.
