For any subspace $E_1$ of a vector space $E$, taking the orthogonal complement twice recovers the original subspace: $E_1^{\perp\perp} = E_1$. Here the orthogonal complement is taken with respect to the natural scalar product between $E$ and its space of linear functions $L(E)$. This result is fundamental for understanding the duality between subspaces of $E$ and subspaces of $L(E)$.
