The natural scalar product between $E$ and $L(E)$ induces a well-defined and non-degenerate scalar product between the orthogonal complement $E_1^\perp$ and the quotient space $E/E_1$. This shows that $E_1^\perp$ and $E/E_1$ form a dual pair, providing a duality-theoretic interpretation of the quotient construction.
