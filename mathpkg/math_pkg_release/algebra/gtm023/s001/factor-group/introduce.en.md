The factor group (or quotient group) $G/H$ of a commutative group $G$ by a subgroup $H$ is formed by partitioning $G$ into cosets of $H$. The group operation on cosets is well-defined and satisfies the group axioms. The canonical projection $\pi: G \to G/H$ is a surjective homomorphism. This construction is fundamental throughout algebra, particularly in the isomorphism theorems.
