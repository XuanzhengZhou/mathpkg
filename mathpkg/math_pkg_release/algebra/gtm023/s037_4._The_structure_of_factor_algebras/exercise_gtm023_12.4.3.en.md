Suppose $f \in \Gamma[t]$ is an irreducible polynomial, and consider the polynomial algebra $\Gamma[t]/I_f[t]$ denoted by $\Gamma_f[t]$.

a) Show that $\Gamma[t]$ may be identified in a natural way with a subalgebra of $\Gamma_f[t]$.

b) Prove that if two polynomials in $\Gamma[t]$ are relatively prime, then they are relatively prime when considered as polynomials in $\Gamma_f[t]$.

c) Construct an example to prove that an irreducible polynomial in $\Gamma[t]$ is not necessarily irreducible in $\Gamma_f[t]$.

d) Suppose that $f$ has degree 2, and that $g$ is an irreducible polynomial of degree $m$ in $\Gamma[t]$. Determine the possible degrees of the irreducible factors of $g$ in $\Gamma_f[t]$.
