Consider the irreducible polynomial $f = t^2 + 5t + 1$ as an element of $\mathbb{Q}[t]$ (where $\mathbb{Q}$ is the field of rational numbers). Let

$$\pi: \mathbb{Q}[t] \to \mathbb{Q}[t]/I_f$$

be the canonical projection.

a) Decide whether the polynomials of problem 6, §1, problem 8, §2 (except for part d) and problems 11a) and b), §2 are in the kernel of $\pi$.

b) For each polynomial $p$ of part a) such that $\pi p \neq 0$, construct a polynomial $g \in \mathbb{Q}[t]$ such that

$$\pi g = (\pi p)^{-1}.$$
