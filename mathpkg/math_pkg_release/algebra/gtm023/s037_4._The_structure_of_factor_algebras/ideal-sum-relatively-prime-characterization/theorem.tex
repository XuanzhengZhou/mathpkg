Let $f = f_1 \cdots f_m$ and let $I_j$ denote the ideal in $\Gamma(\bar{t})$ generated by $\bar{f}_j$. Consider the ideal

$$I = \sum_j I_j.$$

Then $I = \Gamma(\bar{t})$ if and only if the polynomials $f_j$ are relatively prime. The sum is direct if and only if, for each $j$, the polynomials $f_j$ and $\bigvee_{i \neq j} f_i$ have $f$ as least common multiple.
