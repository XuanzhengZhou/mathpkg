With the notation of Theorem I let

$$\varphi_i: \Gamma[t] \to I_i$$

be the homomorphism defined by

$$\varphi_i(1) = \bar{e}_i, \quad \varphi_i(t) = \bar{t}_i.$$

Then $\varphi_i$ is an epimorphism and $\ker \varphi_i = I_{f_i^{k_i}}$.

Thus $\varphi_i$ induces an isomorphism

$$\Gamma[t] / I_{f_i^{k_i}} \xrightarrow{\cong} I_i.$$
