The trace provides a natural pairing between the spaces $L(E;F)$ and $L(F;E)$ via $\langle \varphi, \psi \rangle = \operatorname{tr}(\psi \circ \varphi)$. This bilinear form is non-degenerate, establishing a duality between the two spaces. This construction generalizes the duality between a vector space and its dual, where the trace pairing reduces to the canonical evaluation $\langle x, x^* \rangle$ when mappings are interpreted as rank-one operators.
