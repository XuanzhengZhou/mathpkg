By the result of sec. 4.7 on determinants of direct sums of stable subspaces, if $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$ with both summands stable under $\varphi$, then for the induced transformations $\varphi_1 = \varphi|_{E_1}$ and $\varphi_2 = \varphi|_{E_2}$, we have
$$\det \varphi = \det \varphi_1 \cdot \det \varphi_2.$$
Applying this to $\varphi - \lambda \iota$ (noting that $\iota|_{E_i} = \iota_i$), we obtain
$$\det(\varphi - \lambda \iota) = \det(\varphi_1 - \lambda \iota_1) \cdot \det(\varphi_2 - \lambda \iota_2).$$
