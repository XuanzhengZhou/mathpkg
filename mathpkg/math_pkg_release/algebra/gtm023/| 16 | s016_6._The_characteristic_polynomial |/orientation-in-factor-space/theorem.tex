Let $E$ be an oriented vector space and $F$ be an oriented subspace. Let $\Delta$ be a representing determinant function in $E$ and $a_1, \ldots, a_p$ be a positive basis of $F$. Then the function

$$\bar{\Delta}(\bar{x}_{p+1}, \ldots, \bar{x}_n) = \Delta(a_1, \ldots, a_p, x_{p+1}, \ldots, x_n), \quad x_i \in E$$

depends only on the classes $\bar{x}_i \in E/F$ and defines a determinant function in $E/F$. The orientation in $E/F$ represented by $\bar{\Delta}$ is independent of the choices of $\Delta$ and of the positive basis of $F$.
