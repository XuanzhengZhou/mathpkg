The trace satisfies the cyclic property: $\operatorname{tr}(\psi \circ \varphi) = \operatorname{tr}(\varphi \circ \psi)$. This fundamental identity is a consequence of the alternating property of determinant functions and underlies many important results, including the invariance of trace under similarity transformations and the formula for the coefficient of $\lambda^{n-1}$ in the characteristic polynomial.
