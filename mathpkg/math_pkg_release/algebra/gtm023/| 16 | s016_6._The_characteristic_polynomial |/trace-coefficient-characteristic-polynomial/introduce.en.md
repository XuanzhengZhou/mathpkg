The trace appears as (up to a sign) the coefficient of the second-highest-degree term in the characteristic polynomial. Together with the determinant (which gives the constant term $f(0) = \det \varphi$), the trace provides the second elementary symmetric function of the eigenvalues. This connection bridges the additive invariant (trace) with the multiplicative invariant (determinant) through the polynomial structure.
