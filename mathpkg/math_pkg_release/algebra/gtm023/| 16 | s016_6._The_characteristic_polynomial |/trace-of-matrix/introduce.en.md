The trace of a matrix is simply the sum of its diagonal entries. When a linear transformation is represented by a matrix with respect to a basis, the trace of the transformation equals the trace of its matrix. This provides a convenient computational formula and shows that the trace is independent of the choice of basis, since $\operatorname{tr}(P^{-1}AP) = \operatorname{tr}(APP^{-1}) = \operatorname{tr} A$.
