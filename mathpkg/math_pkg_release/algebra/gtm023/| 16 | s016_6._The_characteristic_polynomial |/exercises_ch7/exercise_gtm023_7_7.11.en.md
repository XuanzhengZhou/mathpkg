Let $E$ be a 2-dimensional vector space and $\varphi$ be a linear transformation of $E$. Prove that $\varphi$ satisfies the equation $\varphi^2 = -\lambda \iota$, $\lambda > 0$, if and only if

$$\det \varphi > 0 \quad \text{and} \quad \operatorname{tr} \varphi = 0.$$
