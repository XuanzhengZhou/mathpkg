Given three linear transformations $\varphi$, $\psi$, $\chi$ of $E$, show that

$$\operatorname{tr}(\chi \circ \psi \circ \varphi) \neq \operatorname{tr}(\chi \circ \varphi \circ \psi)$$

in general.
