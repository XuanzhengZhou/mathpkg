Suppose $\varphi_1: E_1 \to F_1$ and $\varphi_2: E_2 \to F_2$ are linear mappings. The direct sum of $\varphi_1$ and $\varphi_2$ is the linear mapping
$$\varphi_1 \oplus \varphi_2: E_1 \oplus E_2 \to F_1 \oplus F_2$$
defined by
$$(\varphi_1 \oplus \varphi_2)(x_1, x_2) = (\varphi_1 x_1, \varphi_2 x_2).$$
