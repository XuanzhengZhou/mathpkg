The direct sum of linear mappings behaves well with respect to images and kernels: the image of the direct sum is the direct sum of the images, and the kernel of the direct sum is the direct sum of the kernels. These properties follow immediately from the componentwise definition of $\varphi_1 \oplus \varphi_2$.
