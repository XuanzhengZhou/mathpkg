Proposition III establishes a complete correspondence between direct sum decompositions of a vector space and families of pairwise orthogonal projection operators summing to the identity. Given such a family, the space decomposes as the direct sum of the images of the projections. Conversely, any direct sum decomposition yields projections via composition of canonical injections and projections. This generalizes the two-subspace case to an arbitrary finite number of subspaces.
