Proposition I establishes that the external direct sum $E \oplus F$ is characterized up to isomorphism by the algebraic relations satisfied by its canonical injections and projections. Given any vector space $G$ equipped with four linear maps satisfying the same relations as the canonical injections and projections, there exists a unique isomorphism identifying $G$ with $E \oplus F$ in a way that respects these structure maps.
