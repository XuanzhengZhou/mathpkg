Suppose $\tilde{f} = 0$, i.e., $\tilde{f}(\xi) = 0$ for all $\xi \in \Gamma$. If $f \neq 0$, then $f$ has at most $\deg f$ roots. But since $\Gamma$ has characteristic zero, it is infinite, so $f$ would have infinitely many roots, a contradiction. Hence $f = 0$, proving injectivity.
