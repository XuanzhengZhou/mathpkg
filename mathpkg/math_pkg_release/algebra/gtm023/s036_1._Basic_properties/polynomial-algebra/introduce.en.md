The polynomial algebra $\Gamma[t]$ is the set of all polynomials over a field $\Gamma$ equipped with termwise addition and convolution (Cauchy product) multiplication. It forms a commutative associative algebra with unit element $(1, 0, 0, \ldots)$. This algebraic structure is fundamental in algebra and serves as the prototypical example of a free commutative algebra on one generator.
