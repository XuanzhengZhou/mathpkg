Show that if $\partial : \Gamma[t] \rightarrow \Gamma[t]$ is a non-trivial homogeneous antiderivation in the graded algebra $\Gamma[t]$ with respect to the canonical involution, and $\partial^2 = 0$, then
$$H_p(\Gamma[t]) = 0, \quad p \geq 1.$$
