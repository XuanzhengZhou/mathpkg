The chain rule for formal polynomials states that the derivative of the composition $f(g)$ equals $f'(g) \cdot g'$. This is the algebraic analogue of the chain rule from calculus. The proof uses the power rule for derivatives, the Leibniz rule, and linearity of the differentiation operator.
