The differentiation map $d: \Gamma[t] \to \Gamma[t]$ is the unique linear map satisfying $d(t^p) = p t^{p-1}$ for $p \geq 1$ and $d(1) = 0$. This formal algebraic definition of the derivative works over any field of characteristic zero and does not rely on limits. It is the unique derivation sending $t$ to $1$.
