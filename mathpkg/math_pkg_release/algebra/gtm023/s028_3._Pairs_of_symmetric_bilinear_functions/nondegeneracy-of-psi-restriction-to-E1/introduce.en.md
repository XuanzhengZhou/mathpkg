This lemma is the key to the iterative step in the simultaneous diagonalization proof (Section 9.15). After finding the first vector $e_1$ satisfying $\Phi(e_1, y) = \lambda_1 \Psi(e_1, y)$ for all $y$, one considers the $\Psi$-orthogonal complement $E_1$. The lemma guarantees that $\Psi|_{E_1}$ remains non-degenerate, so the same construction can be applied to $E_1$ to find $e_2$, and the process can be repeated until a full basis is obtained.
