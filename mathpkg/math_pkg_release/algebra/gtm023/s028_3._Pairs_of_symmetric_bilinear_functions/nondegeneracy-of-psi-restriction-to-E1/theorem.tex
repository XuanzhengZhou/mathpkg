Let $\Psi$ be a non-degenerate symmetric bilinear function on a vector space $E$, and let $e_1 \in E$ be a vector such that $\Psi(e_1, e_1) \neq 0$. Define the subspace
$$E_1 = \{z \in E : \Psi(e_1, z) = 0\}.$$
Then $\dim E_1 = \dim E - 1$, and the restriction of $\Psi$ to $E_1$ is again non-degenerate.
