The adjoint mapping $\tilde{\varphi}$ is the inner-product-space analogue of the transpose. The key relation is $(\varphi x, y) = (x, \tilde{\varphi}y)$. The orthogonal decomposition $F = \operatorname{Im}\varphi \oplus \ker\tilde{\varphi}$ is fundamental for solving linear equations in inner product spaces.
