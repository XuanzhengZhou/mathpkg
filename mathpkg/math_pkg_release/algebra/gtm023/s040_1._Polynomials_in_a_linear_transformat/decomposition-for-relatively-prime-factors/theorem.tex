Let $\varphi: E \to E$ be a linear transformation on a finite-dimensional vector space over a field of characteristic $0$. Suppose

$$f = f_1 \cdots f_r$$

is a decomposition of the polynomial $f \in \Gamma[t]$ into pairwise relatively prime factors. Then

$$K(f) = K(f_1) \oplus \cdots \oplus K(f_r).$$
