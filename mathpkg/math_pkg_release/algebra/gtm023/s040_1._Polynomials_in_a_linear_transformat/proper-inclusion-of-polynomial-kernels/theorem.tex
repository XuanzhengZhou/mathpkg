Let $\varphi: E \to E$ be a linear transformation on a finite-dimensional vector space over a field of characteristic $0$, and let $\mu$ be the minimum polynomial of $\varphi$. Let $f, g \in \Gamma[t]$ be non-zero polynomials such that

(i) $f \mid \mu$,

(ii) $g \mid f$ properly (i.e., $\deg g < \deg f$).

Then

$$K(g) \subsetneq K(f).$$
