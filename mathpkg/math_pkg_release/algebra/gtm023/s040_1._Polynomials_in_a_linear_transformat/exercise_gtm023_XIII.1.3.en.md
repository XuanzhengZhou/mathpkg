Suppose $E = E_1 \oplus E_2$ and $\varphi = \varphi_1 \oplus \varphi_2$ where $\varphi_i: E_i \to E_i$ ($i = 1, 2$) are linear transformations. Let $\mu$, $\mu_1$, $\mu_2$ be the minimum polynomials of $\varphi$, $\varphi_1$, and $\varphi_2$. Prove that $\mu$ is the least common multiple of $\mu_1$ and $\mu_2$.
