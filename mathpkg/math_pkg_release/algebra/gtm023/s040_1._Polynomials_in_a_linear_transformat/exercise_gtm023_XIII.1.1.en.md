Calculate the minimum polynomials for the following linear transformations:

(a) $\varphi = \lambda \iota$ (scalar multiple of the identity)

(b) $\varphi$ is a projection operator

(c) $\varphi$ is an involution (i.e., $\varphi^2 = \iota$)

(d) $\varphi$ is a differential operator on the space of polynomials of degree $\leq n$

(e) $\varphi$ is a (proper or improper) rotation of a Euclidean plane or of Euclidean $3$-space.
