Suppose $E_1 \subset E$ is a subspace stable under $\varphi$. Let $\mu$, $\mu_1$, and $\bar{\mu}$ be the minimum polynomials of $\varphi: E \to E$, $\varphi_1: E_1 \to E_1$, and $\bar{\varphi}: E/E_1 \to E/E_1$ (the induced map on the quotient space). Let $\nu$ be the least common multiple of $\mu_1$ and $\bar{\mu}$. Determine the relationship between $\mu$ and $\nu$.
