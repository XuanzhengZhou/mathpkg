Proposition IV is the central structure theorem of the chapter: factoring the minimum polynomial into coprime factors decomposes the entire vector space into a direct sum of invariant subspaces, each associated with one factor. On each summand, the restricted operator has precisely that factor as its minimum polynomial. This is the rational canonical form / primary decomposition theorem in kernel language.
