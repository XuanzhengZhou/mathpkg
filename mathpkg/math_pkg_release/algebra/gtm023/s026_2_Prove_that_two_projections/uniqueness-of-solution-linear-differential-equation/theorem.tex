The solution to the differential equation $\dot{\varphi}(t) = \psi(t) \circ \varphi(t)$ with the initial condition $\varphi(t_0) = \iota$ is unique. If $\varphi_1(t)$ and $\varphi_2(t)$ are two solutions, then their difference $\varphi(t) = \varphi_2(t) - \varphi_1(t)$ satisfies $|\varphi(t)| \leq M \int_{t_0}^{t} |\varphi(t)| \, dt$, which forces $\varphi(t) = 0$ for all $t$.
