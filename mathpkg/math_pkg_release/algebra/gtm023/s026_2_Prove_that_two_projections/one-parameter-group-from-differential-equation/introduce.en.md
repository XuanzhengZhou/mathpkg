When the generator $\psi$ in the differential equation $\dot{\varphi} = \psi \circ \varphi$ is constant, the solution with $\varphi(0) = \iota$ automatically satisfies the group property $\varphi(t+\tau) = \varphi(t) \circ \varphi(\tau)$. This is the operator analogue of the scalar exponential solving $\dot{y} = a y$, and the solution is denoted $\varphi(t) = \exp(t\psi)$.
