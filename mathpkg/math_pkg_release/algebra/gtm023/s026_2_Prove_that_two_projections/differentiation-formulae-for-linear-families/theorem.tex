The following differentiation formulae hold for differentiable families of linear transformations:
1. $(\lambda \varphi + \mu \psi)^{\cdot} = \lambda \dot{\varphi} + \mu \dot{\psi}$ ($\lambda, \mu$ constants)
2. $(\psi \circ \varphi)^{\cdot} = \dot{\psi} \circ \varphi + \psi \circ \dot{\varphi}$
3. $(\tilde{\varphi})^{\cdot} = \widetilde{\dot{\varphi}}$
