Let $E$ have dimension $n$ with basis $x_v$ ($v = 1 \dots n$). A symmetric bilinear function $\Phi$ is expressed as a bilinear form:
$$\Phi(x, y) = \sum_{v, \mu} \alpha_{v\mu} \xi^v \eta^\mu$$
where $x = \sum_v \xi^v x_v$, $y = \sum_v \eta^v x_v$, and $\alpha_{v\mu} = \Phi(x_v, x_\mu)$ is a symmetric matrix: $\alpha_{v\mu} = \alpha_{\mu v}$.

The corresponding quadratic form is:
$$\Phi(x) = \sum_{v, \mu} \alpha_{v\mu} \xi^v \xi^\mu.$$
