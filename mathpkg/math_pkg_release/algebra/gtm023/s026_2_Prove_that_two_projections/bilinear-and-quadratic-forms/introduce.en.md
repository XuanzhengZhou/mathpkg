In coordinates relative to a fixed basis, a symmetric bilinear function is expressed as $\Phi(x, y) = \sum \alpha_{v\mu} \xi^v \eta^\mu$ where $(\alpha_{v\mu})$ is a symmetric matrix. The associated quadratic form is $\Phi(x) = \sum \alpha_{v\mu} \xi^v \xi^\mu$. Conversely, every symmetric matrix defines a symmetric bilinear function, making the identification between symmetric bilinear forms and quadratic forms completely explicit.
