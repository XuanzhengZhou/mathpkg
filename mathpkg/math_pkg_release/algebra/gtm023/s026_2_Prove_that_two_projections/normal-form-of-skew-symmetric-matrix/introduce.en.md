Every skew-symmetric linear transformation on a finite-dimensional inner product space admits a canonical block-diagonal normal form relative to a suitable orthonormal basis. The matrix consists of $2 \times 2$ blocks $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & \kappa_i \\ -\kappa_i & 0 \end{bmatrix}$ along the diagonal, with remaining entries zero. The parameters $\kappa_i > 0$ are related to the eigenvalues of $\psi^2$, and the rank $2p$ of $\psi$ is always even.
