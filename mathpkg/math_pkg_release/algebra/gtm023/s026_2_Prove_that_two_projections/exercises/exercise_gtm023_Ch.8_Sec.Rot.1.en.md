Given a skew transformation $\psi$ of $E$, prove that $\varphi = (\psi + \iota) \circ (\psi - \iota)^{-1}$ is a rotation and that $-1$ is not an eigenvalue of $\varphi$. Conversely, if $\varphi$ is a rotation not having the eigenvalue $-1$, prove that $\psi = (\varphi - \iota) \circ (\varphi + \iota)^{-1}$ is a skew mapping.
