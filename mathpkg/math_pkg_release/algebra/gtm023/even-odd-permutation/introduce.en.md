The sign of a permutation is the homomorphism $\varepsilon: S_p \to \{\pm 1\}$ determined by the action on the Vandermonde product. Even permutations have sign $+1$, odd permutations have sign $-1$. Transpositions are odd. The sign is fundamental for defining determinant functions as skew-symmetric multilinear forms.
