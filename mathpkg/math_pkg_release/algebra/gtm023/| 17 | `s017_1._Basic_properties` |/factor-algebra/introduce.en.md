The factor algebra $A/B$ is the quotient of an algebra $A$ by an ideal $B$, equipped with a well-defined multiplication induced from $A$. The canonical projection $\pi: A \to A/B$ is an algebra homomorphism. This construction preserves associativity, commutativity, and the existence of a unit element.
