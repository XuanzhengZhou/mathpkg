The multiplication operator $\mu(a)$ is the linear map on an algebra $A$ given by left multiplication by a fixed element $a$. Since the algebra multiplication is linear in the second argument by axiom $(M_1)$, each $\mu(a)$ is indeed a linear transformation of the underlying vector space. This construction embeds $A$ into the algebra of linear transformations $A(A;A)$ when $A$ is associative.
