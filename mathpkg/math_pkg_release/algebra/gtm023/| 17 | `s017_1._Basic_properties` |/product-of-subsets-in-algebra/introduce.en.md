The product of two subsets $U$ and $V$ of an algebra $A$ is defined as the set of all finite sums of products $u_i v_i$ with $u_i \in U$ and $v_i \in V$. This notation extends the algebra multiplication from individual elements to subsets and is used throughout the theory of algebras, particularly in the study of ideals and subalgebras.
