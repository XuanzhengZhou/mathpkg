The external direct sum constructs a new vector space from two given ones. It is characterized by canonical injections $i_1: E \to E \oplus F$, $i_1 x = (x, 0)$ and $i_2: F \to E \oplus F$, $i_2 y = (0, y)$, and canonical projections $\pi_1(x, y) = x$, $\pi_2(x, y) = y$, satisfying $\pi_\lambda \circ i_\mu = \delta_{\lambda\mu}$ and $i_1 \circ \pi_1 + i_2 \circ \pi_2 = \iota$.
