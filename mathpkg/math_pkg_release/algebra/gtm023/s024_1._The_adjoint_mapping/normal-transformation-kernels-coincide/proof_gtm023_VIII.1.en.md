From the equivalent condition for a normal transformation, we have $|\varphi x|^2 = |\tilde{\varphi} x|^2$ for all $x \in E$. Hence $\varphi x = 0$ if and only if $\tilde{\varphi} x = 0$. Therefore $\ker \varphi = \ker \tilde{\varphi}$.
