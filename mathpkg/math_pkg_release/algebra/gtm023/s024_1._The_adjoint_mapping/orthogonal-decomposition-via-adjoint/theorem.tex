Let $\varphi: E \rightarrow F$ be a linear mapping between inner product spaces and let $\tilde{\varphi}: F \rightarrow E$ be its adjoint. Then the image of $\varphi$ and the kernel of $\tilde{\varphi}$ are orthogonal complements in $F$:

$$F = \operatorname{Im} \varphi \oplus \ker \tilde{\varphi}.$$
