By definition of the adjoint and symmetry of the inner product:

$$\tilde{\Phi}(x, y) = (\tilde{\varphi} x, y) = (x, \varphi y) = (\varphi y, x) = \Phi(y, x).$$

The first equality is the definition of $\tilde{\Phi}$, the second uses the defining relation of the adjoint, and the third uses symmetry of the inner product.
