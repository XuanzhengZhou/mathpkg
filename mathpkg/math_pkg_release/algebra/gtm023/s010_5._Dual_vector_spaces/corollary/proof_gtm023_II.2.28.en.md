If $\ker \varphi = 0$, then $(\ker \varphi)^\perp = (0)^\perp = L(E)$. By Proposition III, $\operatorname{Im} \varphi^* = (\ker \varphi)^\perp = L(E)$, so $\varphi^*$ is surjective.
