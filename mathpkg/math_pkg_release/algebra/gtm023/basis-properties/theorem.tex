A family of vectors $(x_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ in $E$ is called a \textbf{basis} of $E$ if it is simultaneously a system of generators and linearly independent. Equivalently, $(x_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ is a basis if and only if every vector $x \in E$ can be written in precisely one way as
$$x = \sum_\alpha \xi^\alpha x_\alpha, \quad \xi^\alpha \in \Gamma.$$
