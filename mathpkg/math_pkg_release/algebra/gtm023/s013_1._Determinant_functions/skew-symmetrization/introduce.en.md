The skew-symmetrization (or alternation) construction produces a skew symmetric $p$-linear map from any $p$-linear map by summing over all permutations weighted by their signs. This averaging process over the symmetric group is the standard technique for constructing alternating multilinear maps and is the algebraic precursor to the definition of the determinant as an alternating sum over permutations.
