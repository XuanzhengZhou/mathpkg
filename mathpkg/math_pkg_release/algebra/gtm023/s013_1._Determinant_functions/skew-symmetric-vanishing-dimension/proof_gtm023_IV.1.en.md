If $p > n = \dim E$, then any $p$ vectors $x_1, \ldots, x_p \in E$ are linearly dependent. By condition (iii) of Proposition I, $\Phi(x_1, \ldots, x_p) = 0$ for every skew symmetric $p$-linear map $\Phi$.
