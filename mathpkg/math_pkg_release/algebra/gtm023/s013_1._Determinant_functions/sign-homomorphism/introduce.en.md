The sign map $\varepsilon: S_p \to \{-1,+1\}$ is a group homomorphism derived from the composition law $\tau(\sigma\Phi) = (\tau\sigma)\Phi$ applied to the alternating product $\prod_{i<j}(x_i-x_j)$. This homomorphism partitions the symmetric group into even and odd permutations, a structure essential for the definition of determinants.
