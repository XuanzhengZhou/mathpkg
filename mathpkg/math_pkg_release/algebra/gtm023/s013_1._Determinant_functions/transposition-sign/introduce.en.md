A transposition swaps two elements while leaving all others fixed. Since swapping two variables in the alternating product $\prod_{i<j}(x_i-x_j)$ introduces exactly one sign change, the sign of any transposition is $-1$. This fact makes transpositions the building blocks for distinguishing even from odd permutations.
