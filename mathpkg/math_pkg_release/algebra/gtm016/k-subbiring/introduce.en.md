A $K$-subbiring of $\End_k K$ is a $K$-subring that is also coclosed. These objects form the class $\mathbf{S}$ and are the algebraic side of the Biring Correspondence Theorem, pairing with finite-degree subfields $k \subseteq K$ via $H(K/k) = \End_k K$.
