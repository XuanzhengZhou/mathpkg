This result shows that the bialgebra structure on $H(K/k)$ restricts to any coclosed subset $H$: the restricted $\Delta_H$ and $\varepsilon_H$ satisfy the coalgebra axioms (1)-(3) and compatibility (5). If $H$ is a $K$-subbiring (closed under multiplication), then $\varepsilon_H$ is also multiplicative (property 4), making $(H, \Delta_H, \varepsilon_H)$ a full $K_H/k$-bialgebra.
