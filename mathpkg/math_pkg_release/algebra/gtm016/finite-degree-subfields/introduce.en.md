$\mathbf{F}$ is the set of all subfields $k \subseteq K$ such that $[K:k] < \infty$. This forms the "field side" of the Biring Correspondence Theorem, which establishes a Galois-like correspondence between finite-degree subfields $k \in \mathbf{F}$ and finite-dimensional $K$-subbirings $H \in \mathbf{S}$ of $\End K$.
