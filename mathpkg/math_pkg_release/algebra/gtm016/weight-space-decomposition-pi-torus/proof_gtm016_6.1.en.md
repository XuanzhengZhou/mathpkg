Since each $t \in T_\pi$ satisfies $t^p - t = 0$, the minimum polynomials of the elements of $T_\pi$ are divisors of the separable polynomial $X^p - X = \prod_{i \in \pi} (X - i)$. Thus, each $t \in T_\pi$ is diagonalizable on $V$ with all eigenvalues in $\pi$. It follows that $V = \sum_{\alpha \in T_\pi^*} V_\alpha(T_\pi)$ (direct).
