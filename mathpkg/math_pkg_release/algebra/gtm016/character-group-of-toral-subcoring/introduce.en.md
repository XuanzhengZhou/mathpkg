For a diagonalizable toral $k$-subcoring $T$ of $H(K/k)$, the set $G(T)$ consists of the nonzero weight spaces $K_\alpha(T) = \{x \in K \mid t(x) = \alpha(t)x \text{ for all } t \in T\}$ indexed by characters $\alpha \in T^*$. The weight space decomposition $K = \bigoplus_{\alpha} K_\alpha(T)$ exhibits $K$ as a direct sum, and the convolution product $(\alpha * \beta)(t) = \sum_i \alpha({}_i t) \beta(t_i)$ where $\Delta t = \sum_i {}_i t \otimes_K t_i$ defines a group structure on the set of characters with $K_\alpha(T) K_\beta(T) \subset K_{\alpha * \beta}(T)$.
