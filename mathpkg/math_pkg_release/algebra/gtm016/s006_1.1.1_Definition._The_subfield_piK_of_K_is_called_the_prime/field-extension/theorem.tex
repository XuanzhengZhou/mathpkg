An extension field (or extension) of $k$ is a field $K$ containing $k$ as a subfield together with the natural vector space structure over $k$. Denoted $K/k$. The degree of $K/k$ is the dimension $[K:k] = K:k$ of $K$ over $k$. A subextension is an extension $k'$ of $k$ such that $k'$ is a subfield of $K$.
