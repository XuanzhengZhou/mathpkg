The exponent characteristic of $K$ is $1$ if $\pi(K)$ is isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}_0$ and is $p$ if $\pi(K)$ is isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}_p$, $p$ a prime number. The exponent characteristic of $K$ is denoted $\operatorname{Exp Char} K$.
