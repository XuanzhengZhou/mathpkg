This proposition constructs a Galois splitting group \(G\) for a field extension \(K/k\) when \(K\) can be expressed as an internal tensor product \(K = k'(x_1)\cdots k'(x_n)\) of \(k'\)-algebras with pure indeterminates \(x_i\) such that \(x_i^{e_i} \in k\). The splitting group \(G\) consists of all monomials \(k x_1^{f_1} \cdots x_n^{f_n}\) with exponents bounded by \(e_i - 1\), and the elements \(g_i = k' x_i\) form a basis for the splitting. The fixed field is \(K^G = k'\).
