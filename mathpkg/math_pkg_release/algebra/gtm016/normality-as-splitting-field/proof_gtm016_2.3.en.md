If $K$ is the splitting field of $\mathbf{P}$, then $K = k(\operatorname{Roots}_{k_{\text{Alg}}} \mathbf{P})$ which is clearly stable under $\operatorname{Aut}_k k_{\text{Alg}}$. Conversely, if $K/k$ is normal, take $\mathbf{P}$ to be the minimum polynomials of elements of $K$.
