Let $m = \operatorname{Exp} A$. Then $A \subset \operatorname{Roots}_L(X^m - 1)$, so $|A| \leq m$. But by the structure of finite Abelian groups (0.2.4), $A$ has a cyclic subgroup of order $m$. Hence $|A| = m$ and $A$ is cyclic.
