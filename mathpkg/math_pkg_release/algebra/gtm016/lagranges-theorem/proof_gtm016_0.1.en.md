The left cosets of $H$ in $G$ partition $G$. Each left coset $xH$ has cardinality $|H|$ since the map $h \mapsto xh$ is a bijection from $H$ to $xH$. There are $(G:H)$ distinct cosets. Therefore $|G| = (G:H)|H|$. The statement about element orders follows by applying the theorem to the cyclic subgroup generated by the element.
