Let $K/k$ be an algebraic Galois extension and $K = k_1 \cdots k_n$ (disjoint product over $k$). Then $k_1 \otimes_k \cdots \otimes_k k_n$ and $K$ are $k$-isomorphic via the mapping $x_1 \otimes \cdots \otimes x_n \mapsto x_1 \cdots x_n$.
