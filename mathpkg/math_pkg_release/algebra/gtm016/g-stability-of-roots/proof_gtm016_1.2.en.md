If $f \in K^G[X]$ and $x$ is a root, $f(\sigma(x)) = \sigma(f(x)) = \sigma(0) = 0$. Thus roots are $G$-stable. If $x$ is algebraic over $K^G$, its orbit is contained in the finite set of roots of its minimum polynomial. Conversely, if $Gx = \{x_1,\ldots,x_n\}$ is finite, let $f(X) = \prod(X-x_i)$. For $\sigma \in G$, $\sigma(f(X)) = f(X)$, so $f \in K^G[X]$, and $x$ is algebraic over $K^G$.
