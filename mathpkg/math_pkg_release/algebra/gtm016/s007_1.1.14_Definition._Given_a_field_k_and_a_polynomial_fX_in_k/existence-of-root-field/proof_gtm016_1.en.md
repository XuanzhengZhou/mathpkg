Let $g(X)$ be an irreducible factor of $f(X)$ in $k[X]$. Then $K = k[X]/(g(X))$ is a field containing $k$, and the coset $\bar{X}$ is a root of $g(X)$ in $K$. Hence $K$ is a root field for $g(X)$, and therefore also for $f(X)$.
