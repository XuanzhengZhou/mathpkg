Let $a \neq 0$ in $A$. Left translation $a_L(b) = ab$ is a $k$-linear map $A \to A$, injective since $A$ is an integral domain. Since $\dim_k A < \infty$, $a_L$ is surjective, so $a_L(x) = 1$ has a solution $x \in A$, giving $ax = 1$. Thus $a$ is a unit.
