Let $y \in k' \setminus k$ and write $y = u(x)/v(x)$ with $u, v$ relatively prime in $k[x]$. Let $n = \max(\deg u, \deg v)$. Then $[k(x):k(y)] = n$ and $k' = k(y)$ follows from degree considerations.
