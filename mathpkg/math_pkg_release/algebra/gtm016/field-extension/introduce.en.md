An extension field (or extension) of $k$ is a field $K$ containing $k$ as subfield together with the vector space structure over $k$. Such an extension field is denoted $K/k$. The degree of an extension $K/k$ is the dimension of $K$ over $k$. A subextension is an extension $k'$ of $k$ such that $k'$ is a subfield of $K$.
