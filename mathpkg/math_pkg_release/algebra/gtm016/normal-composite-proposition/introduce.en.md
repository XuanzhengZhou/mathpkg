Let $A, B$ be subfields of $K$ containing $k$ and suppose that $A/k$ and $B/k$ are algebraic and normal. Then the homomorphism $f: \operatorname{Aut}_k AB \to \operatorname{Aut}_k A \times \operatorname{Aut}_k B$ defined by $f(\sigma) = \sigma|_A \times \sigma|_B$ is injective.
