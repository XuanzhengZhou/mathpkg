Let $k'$ be a subfield of $K$ containing $k$ and suppose that $K/k'$ is a finite dimensional separable extension. Then each derivation $D'$ from $k'$ to $L$ over $k$ has a unique extension to a derivation $D$ from $K$ to $L$ over $k$.
