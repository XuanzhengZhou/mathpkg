Since $K/k$ is Abelian, $K/k$ is separable and $\operatorname{Aut}_k K^{\text{norm}}$ is Abelian. Now $K^{\text{norm}}/k$ is Galois and every subgroup of an Abelian group is normal. By the Galois Correspondence Theorem, $K/k$ is Galois.
