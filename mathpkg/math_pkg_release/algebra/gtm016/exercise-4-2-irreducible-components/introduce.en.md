Exercise E.4.2 introduces the $k$-Zariski topology on $\bar{k}^n$ and the notion of irreducible closed subsets. A subset $C \subset \bar{k}^n$ is closed if $C = C(I)$ for some ideal $I$ of $k[X]$. The exercise asks to prove that the open sets form a topology, and that irreducibility of closed sets corresponds to primality of the vanishing ideal.
