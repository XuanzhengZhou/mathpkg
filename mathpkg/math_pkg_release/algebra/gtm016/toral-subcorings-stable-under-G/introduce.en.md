This theorem, together with 6.3.4, establishes the full ascent-descent dictionary between the toral structure of $H(K/k)$ and the $G$-equivariant toral structure of $H(\bar{K}/k)$. It is a key technical tool for analyzing radical extensions by transferring problems to the algebraic closure where the toral subcorings become diagonalizable.
