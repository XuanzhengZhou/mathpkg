Exercise E.4.7 establishes that $p$-dependence, defined by $x \prec_p S$ if $x \in k(K^p)(S)$, satisfies the axioms of a dependence relation in the sense of E.1.20. A subset $S$ of $K$ is $p$-independent over $k$ if it is independent with respect to this relation. The exercise connects this to $p$-bases: $S$ is a basis for $K/k$ in the dependence sense if and only if $S$ is a $p$-basis for $K/k$, which implies every extension has a $p$-basis with uniquely determined cardinality.
