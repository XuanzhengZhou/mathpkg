Let $T$ be a $k$-torus on $K$ containing $I$ such that $\varepsilon(T) \subset k$ and for each $t \in T$, there exist ${}_i t, t_i \in T$ such that $\Delta t = \sum_i {}_i t \otimes_K t_i$. Thus, $\bar{T}$ is a torus in $H(\bar{K}/k)$. The $G$-stability gives the bijection.
