Let $n$ and $p$ be relatively prime and let $K$ be the cyclotomic extension of $X^n - 1$ over $k$. Then $K$ contains $\varphi(n)$ primitive $n$th roots of unity, and if $x$ is any primitive $n$th root of unity in $K$, then $K = k(x)$. The extension $K/k$ is Galois, $\operatorname{Aut}_k K$ is Abelian (cyclic if $n$ is prime), and $\operatorname{Aut}_k K$ is isomorphic to a subgroup of $\mathbb{Z}_n^*$.
