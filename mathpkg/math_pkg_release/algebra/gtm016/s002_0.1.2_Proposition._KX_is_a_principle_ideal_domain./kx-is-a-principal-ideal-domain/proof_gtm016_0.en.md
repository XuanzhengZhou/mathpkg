Let $I$ be a nonzero ideal of $K[X]$. Take $f(X) \in I$ of least degree. For any $g(X) \in I$, by the division algorithm $g(X) = q(X)f(X) + r(X)$ with $\deg r < \deg f$. Since $r(X) = g(X) - q(X)f(X) \in I$, minimality of $\deg f$ forces $r(X) = 0$. Thus $g(X) = q(X)f(X)$, so $I = (f(X))$.
