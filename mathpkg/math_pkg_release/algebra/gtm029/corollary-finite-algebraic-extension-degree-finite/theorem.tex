If $K^\star$ is a finite algebraic extension of $K$, of degree $n$, then also $\Delta^\star$ is a finite algebraic extension of $\Delta$, and we have $[\Delta^\star:\Delta] \leq [K^\star:K]$.

The integer $[\Delta^\star:\Delta]$ is called the relative degree of $\mathcal{P}^\star$ with respect to $\mathcal{P}$ (or with respect to $K$).
