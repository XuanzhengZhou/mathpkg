The order function $v(x)$ on an $A$-module $E$ with respect to an ideal $\mathfrak{m}$ measures how deeply an element $x$ lies in the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic filtration: it is the largest integer $n$ such that $x \in \mathfrak{m}^n E$, or $+\infty$ if $x$ lies in all $\mathfrak{m}^n E$. This function serves as an $\mathfrak{m}$-adic analogue of a valuation and is fundamental for defining the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology.
