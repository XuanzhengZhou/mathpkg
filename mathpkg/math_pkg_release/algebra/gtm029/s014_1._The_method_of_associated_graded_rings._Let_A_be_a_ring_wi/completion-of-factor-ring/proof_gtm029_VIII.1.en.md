The $(\mathfrak{m}+\mathfrak{a})/\mathfrak{a}$-topology of $A/\mathfrak{a}$ coincides with the $\mathfrak{m}$-topology of $A/\mathfrak{a}$ considered as an $A$-module. Applying Theorem 6 to the exact sequence $0 \to \mathfrak{a} \to A \to A/\mathfrak{a} \to 0$ and noting that $\mathfrak{a}$ is closed (hence its completion is $\hat{A}\mathfrak{a}$ as a submodule of $\hat{A}$) yields the isomorphism $\widehat{(A/\mathfrak{a})} \cong \hat{A}/\hat{A}\mathfrak{a}$.
