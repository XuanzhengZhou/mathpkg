When an ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ is closed in the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic topology, completion commutes with taking the quotient: the completion of $A/\mathfrak{a}$ is naturally isomorphic to $\hat{A}/\hat{A}\mathfrak{a}$. This follows from the exactness of completion (Theorem 6) applied to the exact sequence $0 \to \mathfrak{a} \to A \to A/\mathfrak{a} \to 0$.
