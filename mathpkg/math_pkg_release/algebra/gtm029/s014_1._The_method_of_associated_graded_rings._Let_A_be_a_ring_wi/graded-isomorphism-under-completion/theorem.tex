Let $A$ be a noetherian ring, $\mathfrak{m}$ an ideal in $A$ and $E$ a finite $A$-module. If $A$ and $E$ are Hausdorff spaces with respect to their $\mathfrak{m}$-topologies, then

$$G_{\mathfrak{m}}(A) \cong G_{\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}}(\hat{A}), \quad G_{\mathfrak{m}}(E) \cong G_{\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}}(\hat{E})$$

canonically.
