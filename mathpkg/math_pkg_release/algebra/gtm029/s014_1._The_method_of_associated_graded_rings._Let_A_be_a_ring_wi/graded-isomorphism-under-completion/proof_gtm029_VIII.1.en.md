Since $\mathfrak{m}^n E$ is open, it is closed, and $\mathfrak{m}^{n+1}E$ is an open and closed submodule of $\mathfrak{m}^n E$. Thus $\mathfrak{m}^n E/\mathfrak{m}^{n+1}E$ is discrete for its $\mathfrak{m}$-topology. Therefore it is identical to its completion, which by Theorem 6 is isomorphic to $\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}^n E/\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}^{n+1}E$. Summing over all $n$ gives $G_{\mathfrak{m}}(E) \cong G_{\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}}(\hat{E})$.
