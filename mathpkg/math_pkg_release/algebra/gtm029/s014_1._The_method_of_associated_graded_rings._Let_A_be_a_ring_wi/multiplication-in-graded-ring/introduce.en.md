The multiplication in the associated graded ring $G(A)$ and the associated graded module $G(E)$ is defined by lifting homogeneous representatives: for $\bar{a} \in \mathfrak{m}^n/\mathfrak{m}^{n+1}$ and $\bar{x} \in \mathfrak{m}^q E/\mathfrak{m}^{q+1}E$, the product $\bar{a}\bar{x}$ is the class of $ax$ in $\mathfrak{m}^{n+q}E/\mathfrak{m}^{n+q+1}E$. This multiplication is well-defined, associative, and commutative, making $G(A)$ into a graded ring and $G(E)$ into a graded $G(A)$-module.
