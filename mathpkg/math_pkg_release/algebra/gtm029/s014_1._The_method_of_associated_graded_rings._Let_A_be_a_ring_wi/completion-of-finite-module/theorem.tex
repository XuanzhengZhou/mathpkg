Let $A$ be a noetherian ring, $\mathfrak{m}$ an ideal in $A$, $E$ a finite $A$-module, and $\hat{A}$, $\hat{E}$ the completions of $A$ and $E$ for their $\mathfrak{m}$-topologies. Then the natural mapping $E \otimes_A \hat{A} \to \hat{E}$ is an isomorphism, and $\hat{E}$ is a finite $\hat{A}$-module. Moreover, if $\{x_1, \ldots, x_q\}$ generate $E$ over $A$, then they also generate $\hat{E}$ over $\hat{A}$.
