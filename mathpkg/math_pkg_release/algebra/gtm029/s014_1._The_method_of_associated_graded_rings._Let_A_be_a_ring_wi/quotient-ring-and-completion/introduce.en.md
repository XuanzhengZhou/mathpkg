For a Zariski ring, the quotient ring with respect to the multiplicatively closed set $S = 1 + \mathfrak{m}$ embeds naturally into the completion $\hat{A}$. The kernel of this embedding is precisely the intersection of all powers of $\mathfrak{m}$, which is the closure of zero. In the Hausdorff case, both $A$ and $A_S$ embed into $\hat{A}$.
