Let $R = K[[X_1, \cdots, X_n]]$ be a formal power series ring over a field $K$, and let $F \in R$ be a power series regular in $X_n$ of order $s$. Then every element $G \in R$ may be written uniquely in the form

$$G = UF + \sum_{j=0}^{s-1} S_j X_n^j,$$

where $U \in R$ and $S_j \in R' = K[[X_1, \cdots, X_{n-1}]]$.

This is proved by applying Theorem 7, Corollary 2, with $A = K[[X_1, \cdots, X_{n-1}, F]]$, $\mathfrak{m} = (X_1, \cdots, X_{n-1}, F)$, and $E = R$.
