The $A/\mathfrak{m}$-module $\mathfrak{m}^n E / \mathfrak{m}^{n+1} E$ is generated by the images of monomials of degree $n$ in the generators of $\mathfrak{m}$ applied to the basis elements $e_j$. Summing over all $n$, $G(E)$ is generated as a $G(A)$-module by the images $\bar{e}_j$ of $e_j$ in $E/\mathfrak{m}E$. Since there are only finitely many $e_j$, $G(E)$ is a finite $G(A)$-module.
