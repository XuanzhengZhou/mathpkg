Examples of Zariski rings:

(1) A noetherian local ring, with respect to its maximal ideal.

(2) A noetherian semi-local ring $A$ with maximal ideals $\mathfrak{m}_i$, with respect to their intersection $\mathfrak{m} = \bigcap_i \mathfrak{m}_i$.

(3) A noetherian ring $A$ which is a complete Hausdorff space in its $\mathfrak{m}$-topology.

(4) A factor ring $A/\mathfrak{a}$ of a Zariski ring, with respect to the ideal $(\mathfrak{m} + \mathfrak{a})/\mathfrak{a}$.
