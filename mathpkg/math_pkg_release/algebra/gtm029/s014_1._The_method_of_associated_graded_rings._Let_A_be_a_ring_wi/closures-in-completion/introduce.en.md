Under the completion, the closures of the $\mathfrak{m}$-adic filtration submodules are given by the corresponding filtration with respect to the extended ideal $\hat{A}\mathfrak{m}$. Moreover, the original submodules can be recovered by intersecting with the original ring/module, and the topology on the completion coincides with the $(\hat{A}\mathfrak{m})$-adic topology.
