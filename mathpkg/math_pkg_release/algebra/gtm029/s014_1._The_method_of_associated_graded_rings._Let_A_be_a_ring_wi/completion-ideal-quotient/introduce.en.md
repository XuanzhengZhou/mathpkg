The completion of the submodule quotient $(F :_E \mathfrak{a})$ equals the quotient of the completions. This follows by writing $F :_E \mathfrak{a} = \bigcap_j (F :_E a_j)$ for a finite generating set of $\mathfrak{a}$, and applying Corollaries 2 and 4 to Theorem 11.
