Let $\{a_1, \ldots, a_q\}$ be a finite basis of $\mathfrak{a}$. Then $F :_E \mathfrak{a} = \bigcap_{j=1}^{q} (F :_E a_j)$. By Corollary 2 to Theorem 11, $\hat{A}(F :_E \mathfrak{a}) = \bigcap_{j=1}^{q} \hat{A}(F :_E a_j)$. By Corollary 4, each $\hat{A}(F :_E a_j) = \hat{A}F :_{\hat{E}} a_j$. Thus $\hat{A}(F :_E \mathfrak{a}) = \bigcap_{j=1}^{q} (\hat{A}F :_{\hat{E}} a_j) = \hat{A}F :_{\hat{E}} \hat{A}\mathfrak{a}$.
