Let $A$ be a Zariski ring, $E$ a finite $A$-module, $E'$ a submodule of $E$ and $z$ an element of $E$. Then

$$\hat{A}(E' :_A z) = \hat{A}E' :_{\hat{A}} z.$$

In particular, if $\mathfrak{b}$ is an ideal in $A$ and $a$ an element of $A$, then

$$\hat{A}(\mathfrak{b} :_A a) = \hat{A}\mathfrak{b} :_{\hat{A}} a.$$
