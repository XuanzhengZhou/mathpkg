Apply Corollary 3 to Theorem 11 with $E = A$, $F = E$, $F' = E'$, and $f$ being the map $a \mapsto az$ from $A$ to $E$. The exactness of completion gives $\hat{A}(E' :_A z) = \hat{A}E' :_{\hat{A}} z$. The special case for ideals follows by taking $E = A$ and $E' = \mathfrak{b}$.
