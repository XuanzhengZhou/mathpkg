Theorem 12 relates the primary decomposition of an ideal in a ring to that of its extension in the completion. A primary decomposition of $\hat{A}\mathfrak{a}$ induces a primary decomposition of $\mathfrak{a}$ by intersecting the primary components with $A$. The associated primes of $\mathfrak{a}$ contain the contractions of the associated primes of $\hat{A}\mathfrak{a}$.
