Let $A$ be a ring, $\mathfrak{m}$ an ideal in $A$, $E$ an $A$-module and $F$ a submodule of $E$. Suppose that $A$ is a complete Hausdorff space for its $\mathfrak{m}$-topology, and that $E$ is a Hausdorff space for its $\mathfrak{m}$-topology. Let $\{x_1, \cdots, x_q\}$ be a finite system of elements of $F$ such that their initial forms $G(x_i)$ generate (over $G_{\mathfrak{m}}(A)$) the leading submodule of $F$ in $G_{\mathfrak{m}}(E)$. Then the elements $\{x_1, \cdots, x_q\}$ generate $F$.
