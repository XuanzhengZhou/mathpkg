When the ideal $\mathfrak{m}$ is generated by $q$ elements, the associated graded ring $G(A)$ is a quotient of the polynomial ring $(A/\mathfrak{m})[x_1, \ldots, x_q]$ by a homogeneous ideal. This structure theorem shows that $G(A)$ is noetherian whenever $A/\mathfrak{m}$ is noetherian and $\mathfrak{m}$ is finitely generated, making it a powerful tool for studying the original ring $A$.
