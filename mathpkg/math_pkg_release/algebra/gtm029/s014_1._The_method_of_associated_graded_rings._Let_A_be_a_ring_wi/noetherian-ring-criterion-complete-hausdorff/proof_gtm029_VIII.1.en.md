Under the hypotheses, $G(A) \cong (A/\mathfrak{m})[x_1,\ldots,x_q]/\mathfrak{I}$ is noetherian (by the Hilbert basis theorem, since $A/\mathfrak{m}$ is noetherian and $\mathfrak{m}$ is finitely generated). Applying Corollary 3 to Theorem 7 with $E=A$, we conclude that $A$ is a noetherian $A$-module, i.e., $A$ is a noetherian ring.
