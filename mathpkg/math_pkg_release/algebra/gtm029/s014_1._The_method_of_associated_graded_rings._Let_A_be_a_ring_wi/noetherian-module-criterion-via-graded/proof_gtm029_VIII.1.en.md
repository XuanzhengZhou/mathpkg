For every submodule $F$ of $E$, its leading submodule $F'$ in $G(E)$ is a $G(A)$-submodule. Since $G(E)$ is noetherian, $F'$ is finitely generated. By Theorem 7, the generators of $F'$ correspond to elements of $F$ whose initial forms generate $F'$, and these elements generate $F$. Thus every submodule of $E$ is finitely generated, so $E$ is noetherian.
