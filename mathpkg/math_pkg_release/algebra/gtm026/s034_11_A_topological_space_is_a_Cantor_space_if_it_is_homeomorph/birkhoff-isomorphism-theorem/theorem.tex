Let $W: \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathcal{K}^T$ be an abstract Birkhoff subcategory of $\mathcal{K}^T$. Let $\mathbf{T}'$, $\lambda: \mathbf{T} \longrightarrow \mathbf{T}'$, and $V: \mathcal{K}^{T'} \longrightarrow \mathcal{K}^T$ be induced by $\mathcal{A}$ as in Lemma 3.2. Then $V$ and $W$ are isomorphic; that is, there exists an isomorphism of categories

$$\mathcal{K}^{T'} \cong \mathcal{A}.$$

In particular, $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ is algebraic in $\text{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$, where $U = WU^T$.
