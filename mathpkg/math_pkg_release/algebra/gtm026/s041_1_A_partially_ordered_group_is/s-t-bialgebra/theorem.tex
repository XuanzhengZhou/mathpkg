Let $S$ and $T$ be algebraic theories in $\mathbf{Set}$. An \textit{$S$-$T$ bialgebra} is a triple $(X, \xi, \theta)$ such that $(X, \xi)$ is an $S$-algebra, $(X, \theta)$ is a $T$-algebra, and for all semantic $T$-operations $\alpha: (U^T)^n \rightarrow U^T$, the map $(X, \theta)\alpha: (X, \xi)^n \rightarrow (X, \xi)$ is an $S$-homomorphism.
