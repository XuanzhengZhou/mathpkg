Let $\mathcal{L}$ be the full subcategory of all $(X, d, A)$ with $d < \infty$ and $A \neq \emptyset$, and let PLS ("pseudonormed linear spaces") be the pullback along $\mathcal{L}$. Use the previous exercise to prove that $U_2$ is algebraic. [Hint: given $f: (X, d, A) \longrightarrow (Y, e, \{0\}, \xi)$ in $\mathcal{C}$ with $d < \infty$ and $A \neq \emptyset$, a suitable $\mathcal{L}$-structure $(\bar{d}, \bar{A})$ on the vector space $XT$ is $\bar{d}((\lambda_x), (\mu_x)) = \sum |\lambda_x - \mu_x| d(a, x)$ where $a$ is any element of $A$, and $\bar{A} = \{0\}$.]
