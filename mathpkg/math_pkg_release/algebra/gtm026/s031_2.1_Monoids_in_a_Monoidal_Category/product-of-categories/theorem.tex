Define the product $\mathcal{K}_1 \times \cdots \times \mathcal{K}_n$ of the categories $\mathcal{K}_i$ (for $n > 0$) to have as objects all ordered $n$-tuples $(A_1, \ldots, A_n)$ with $A_i$ an object of $\mathcal{K}_i$, and to have as morphisms $(A_1, \ldots, A_n) \to (B_1, \ldots, B_n)$ all $n$-tuples $(f_1, \ldots, f_n)$ with $f_i: A_i \to B_i$ in $\mathcal{K}_i$. Composition is given by
$$(f_1, \ldots, f_n)(g_1, \ldots, g_n) = (f_1 g_1, \ldots, f_n g_n)$$
and
$$\mathrm{id}_{(A_1, \ldots, A_n)} = (\mathrm{id}_{A_1}, \ldots, \mathrm{id}_{A_n}).$$
