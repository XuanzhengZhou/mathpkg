This corollary establishes the uniqueness of Birkhoff subcategories: if two abstract Birkhoff subcategories share a common reflection data for every free $\mathbf{T}$-algebra, then they coincide. The proof follows immediately from Theorems 3.2, 3.3, and the Birkhoff Variety Theorem (3.4). In categorical terms, this says that an abstract Birkhoff subcategory is completely determined by the way free algebras reflect into it.
