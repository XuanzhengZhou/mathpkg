Let $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ be two abstract Birkhoff subcategories of $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$. Assume that for all $X$ in $\mathcal{K}$ there exists $(XT', \xi_X)$ in $\mathcal{A} \cap \mathcal{B}$ and a $\mathbf{T}$-homomorphism $X\lambda : (XT, X\mu) \longrightarrow (XT', \xi_X)$ which is simultaneously a reflection of $(XT, X\mu)$ in $\mathcal{A}$ and in $\mathcal{B}$. Then $\mathcal{A} = \mathcal{B}$.
