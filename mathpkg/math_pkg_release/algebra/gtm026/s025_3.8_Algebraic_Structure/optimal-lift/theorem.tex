A family of $\mathcal{K}$-morphisms $f_i: K \to K_i$ from an object $K$ to objects $(K_i, s_i)$ in a structured category $\mathcal{C}$ has an optimal lift if, whenever $(K', s')$ is a $\mathcal{C}$-structure and $g: K \to K'$ is a $\mathcal{K}$-morphism such that $f_i \circ g$ is admissible for all $i$, then $g$ is also admissible.
