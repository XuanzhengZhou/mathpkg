A category of $K$-objects with structure $C \in \text{Struct}(K)$ is algebraic over the base category $K$ precisely when it is isomorphic, in the category of structured categories over $K$, to the category of algebras $K^T$ for some algebraic theory $T$ in $K$. This definition classifies those structured categories that arise from purely equational presentations. The notion is central to the book's program of characterizing structure via algebraic theories.
