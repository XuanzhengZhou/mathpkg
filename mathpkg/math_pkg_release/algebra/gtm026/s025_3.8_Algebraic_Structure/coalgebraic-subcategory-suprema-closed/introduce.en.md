Dual to the characterization of algebraic subcategories, a full subcategory of a complete poset is coalgebraic over the base precisely when it is closed under arbitrary suprema. This follows by applying the infimum-closed criterion to the dual poset and dual category, reflecting the general principle that coalgebraicity is algebraicity of the dual.
