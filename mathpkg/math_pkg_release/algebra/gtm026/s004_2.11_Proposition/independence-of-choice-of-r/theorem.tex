Let $X$ be a nonempty set, $\delta$ an $(\Omega, E)$-structure on $X$, and $\omega' \in \Omega'_n$. For $x_1, \ldots, x_n \in X$, define
$$
(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \delta'_{\omega'} = \langle [v_1 \cdots v_n \omega'], \Gamma^{-1}.r^{\#} \rangle
$$
where $r: V \longrightarrow X$ is any function such that $v_i r = x_i$ (for $n=0$, let $r$ be arbitrary). Then $(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \delta'_{\omega'}$ is independent of the choice of $r$.
