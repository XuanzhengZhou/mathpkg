The dual of a categorical concept is obtained by the following algorithm:
\[
\begin{cases}
\text{if the term begins with ``co-''} & \text{remove the prefix ``co-''} \\
\text{otherwise} & \text{add the prefix ``co-''}
\end{cases}
\]
(One usually omits the hyphen; but note exceptions such as co-complete and co-optimal.)
