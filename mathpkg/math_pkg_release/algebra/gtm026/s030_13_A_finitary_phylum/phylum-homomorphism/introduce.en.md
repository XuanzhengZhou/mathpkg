A $(P, \Omega)$-homomorphism is a sort-indexed family of functions that respects the algebraic structure. For each operation symbol $\omega$, applying the operation in the source algebra and then mapping via $f$ yields the same result as mapping each argument and then applying the operation in the target algebra. This is the natural many-sorted generalization of a homomorphism between ordinary algebras.
