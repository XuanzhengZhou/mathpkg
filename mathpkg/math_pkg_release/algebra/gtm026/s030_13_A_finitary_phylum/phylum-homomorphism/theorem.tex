A **$(P, \Omega)$-homomorphism** $f: (X, \delta) \rightarrow (X', \delta')$ is a family of functions of the form $f(p): X(p) \rightarrow X'(p)$ satisfying, for each $\omega \in \Omega_{p_1 \cdots p_{n+1}}$,

\[
f(p_{n+1}) \circ \delta_\omega = \delta'_\omega \circ (f(p_1) \times \cdots \times f(p_n)).
\]

That is, the following diagram commutes:
\[
\begin{CD}
X(p_1) \times \cdots \times X(p_n) @>{\delta_\omega}>> X(p_{n+1})\\
@V{f(p_1) \times \cdots \times f(p_n)}VV @VV{f(p_{n+1})}V\\
X'(p_1) \times \cdots \times X'(p_n) @>>{\delta'_\omega}> X'(p_{n+1})
\end{CD}
\]
