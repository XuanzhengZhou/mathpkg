The **$n$th cohomology group** of a cochain complex $(A_\bullet, d^\bullet)$ is the quotient group

$$H^n(A_\bullet) = \frac{\operatorname{Ker}(d^{n+1})}{\operatorname{Im}(d^n)}.$$

This construction is functorial: morphisms of cochain complexes (diagram morphisms) induce homomorphisms between the corresponding cohomology groups.
