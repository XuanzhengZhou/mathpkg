The cohomology group $H^n$ measures the failure of exactness at position $n$ in a cochain complex. When $H^n = 0$, every $n$-cocycle is an $n$-coboundary, meaning the complex is exact at $A_n$. Functoriality means that maps between cochain complexes induce maps between their cohomology groups, making this a powerful computational tool in algebraic topology and homological algebra.
