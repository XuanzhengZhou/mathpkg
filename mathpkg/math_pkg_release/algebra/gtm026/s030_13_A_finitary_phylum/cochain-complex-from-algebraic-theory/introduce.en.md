This construction produces a cochain complex from any $\mathbf{T}$-algebra by applying a functor $F$ to the canonical resolution of the algebra. The resolution is built using the theory structure (the multiplication $\mu$ and the algebra structure map $\xi$). The alternating sum formula for the differential is the standard simplicial differential, generalizing the bar resolution. The resulting cochain complex is functorial in the algebra $(X, \xi)$, yielding a contravariant functor from $\mathbf{T}$-algebras to cochain complexes.
