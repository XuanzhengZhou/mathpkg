Let $(A, \xi_0)$ be a subalgebra of $(X, \xi)$ with inclusion $j$. Define

$$[XT^{n+1}, Y]_A = \operatorname{Ker}\big(jT^{n+1}(-): [XT^{n+1}, Y] \rightarrow [AT^{n+1}, Y]\big).$$

Then

$$0 \xrightarrow{d^0} [XT, Y]_A \xrightarrow{d^1} \cdots \xrightarrow{d^n} [XT^{n+1}, Y]_A \xrightarrow{d^{n+1}} [XT^{n+2}, Y]_A \rightarrow \cdots$$

is a subcomplex of the triple cochain complex. The **relative cohomology groups** $H^n(X, \xi, A)$ are the cohomology groups of this subcomplex.

This construction is functorial in $(X, \xi, A)$, and each such triple induces a long exact sequence

$$0 \rightarrow H^0(X, \xi, A) \rightarrow H^0(X, \xi) \rightarrow H^0(A, \xi_0) \rightarrow H^1(X, \xi, A) \rightarrow \cdots$$
