This construction shows that Čech cohomology of a topological space with respect to an open cover can be recovered as a special case of triple (Beck) cohomology. The functor "pullback along the codiagonal map $\gamma$" from the slice category over $X$ to the slice category over $Z$ is algebraic (monadic). The resulting algebraic theory on $(\mathcal{K}, Z)$ yields a triple cohomology that agrees with the classical Čech construction. This unifies the seemingly disparate theories of sheaf cohomology and algebraic cohomology.
