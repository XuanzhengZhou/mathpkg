Let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathcal{K}$ and let $(Y, \theta; +, i, 0)$ be an abelian group in $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$. For any $\mathbf{T}$-algebra $(X, \xi)$, isomorphism classes of principal objects over $(X, \xi)$ correspond bijectively to elements of the first triple cohomology group $H^1(X, \xi)$.
