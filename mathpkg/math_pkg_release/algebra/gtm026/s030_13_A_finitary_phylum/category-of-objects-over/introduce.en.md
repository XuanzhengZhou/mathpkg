The category of objects over $K$ (or slice category $\mathcal{K}/K$) is a fundamental construction in category theory where objects are morphisms into $K$ and morphisms are commutative triangles. This construction is used in Exercise 24 to define an algebraic functor via pullback along a map $\gamma$, providing the setting for the triple cohomology interpretation of Čech cohomology.
