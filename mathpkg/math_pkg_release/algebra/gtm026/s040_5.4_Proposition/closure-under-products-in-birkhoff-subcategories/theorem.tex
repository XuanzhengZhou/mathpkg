Let $\mathcal{C}$ be a full replete subcategory of $\mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$ as in Theorem 5.6. Given $(X_i, s_i, \xi_i)$ in $\mathcal{C}$ with product $p_i: (X, s, \xi) \longrightarrow (X_i, s_i, \xi_i)$ in $\mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$, then for each semantic operation $\alpha: (U^{\bar{T}})^n \longrightarrow U^{\bar{T}}$, the map $(X, \xi)\alpha$ is admissible. Consequently, the product object $(X, s, \xi)$ belongs to $\mathcal{C}$, i.e., $\mathcal{C}$ is closed under products.
