Lemma 5.7 is an auxiliary result used in the proof of Theorem 5.6. It establishes that the full replete subcategory $\mathcal{C}$ of $\mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$ is closed under products. The key observation is that product projections in $\mathcal{A}^{\bar{T}}$ are admissible, and since admissible maps compose, the semantic operation $(X, \xi)\alpha$ on the product is admissible, which forces the product to lie in $\mathcal{C}$.
