Corollary 5.5 states that the lifting construction $\mathcal{B} \mapsto \bar{\mathcal{B}}$ preserves the property of being an abstract Birkhoff subcategory. If $\mathcal{B}$ is a full replete abstract Birkhoff subcategory of the category of $T$-algebras $\mathcal{K}^T$, then its lift $\bar{\mathcal{B}}$ (consisting of all $(K, s, \xi)$ with $(K, \xi) \in \mathcal{B}$) is an abstract Birkhoff subcategory of $\mathcal{A}^T$. The proof uses the co-optimal lift of the theory map induced by the inclusion.
