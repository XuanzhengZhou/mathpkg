A representative subcategory of $\mathcal{B}$ is a full subcategory such that every object of $\mathcal{B}$ is isomorphic to some object in the subcategory. Together with fullness and repleteness, this notion provides a useful decomposition of what it means for a subcategory to be "nearly isomorphic" to the ambient category.
