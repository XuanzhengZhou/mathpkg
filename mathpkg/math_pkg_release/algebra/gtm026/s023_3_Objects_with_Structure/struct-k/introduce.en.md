$\operatorname{Struct}(\mathcal{K})$ is the full replete subcategory of $\operatorname{Con}(\mathcal{K})$ consisting of all categories of sets with structure over $\mathcal{K}$. The fact that it is defined as a full replete subcategory reflects the guiding principle that the invariants of a category of structured objects are implicit in its forgetful functor — a principle that the text traces back to section 1.2.1.
