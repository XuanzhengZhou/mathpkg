\mathbf{Set} \text{ is the category whose objects are arbitrary sets and whose morphisms are functions between sets. For any sets $A, B$,}
$$
\mathbf{Set}(A, B) = \{ f \mid f: A \to B \}.
$$
\text{Composition is ordinary function composition, and $\operatorname{id}_A$ is the identity function on $A$.}
