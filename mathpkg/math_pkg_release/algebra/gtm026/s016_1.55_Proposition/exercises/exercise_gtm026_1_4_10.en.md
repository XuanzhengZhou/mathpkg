Let $\mathcal{K}$ be the category whose objects are sets and whose morphisms $f:X \rightarrow Y$ are relations, that is, the Kleisli category of the theory of 1.3.5. Show that the identity function on objects and the passage from a relation to its inverse defines a functor from $\mathcal{K}$ to $\mathcal{K}^{\text{op}}$ which establishes an isomorphism of categories $\mathcal{K} \cong \mathcal{K}^{\text{op}}$.
