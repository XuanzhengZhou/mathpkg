An object $C$ in a category $\mathcal{K}$ is a cogenerator if whenever $f, g: A \longrightarrow B$ are distinct morphisms ($f \neq g$) there exists $h: B \longrightarrow C$ such that $f \cdot h \neq g \cdot h$. Equivalently, the hom functor $\mathcal{K}(-, C): \mathcal{K}^{\text{op}} \longrightarrow \text{Set}$ is faithful.
