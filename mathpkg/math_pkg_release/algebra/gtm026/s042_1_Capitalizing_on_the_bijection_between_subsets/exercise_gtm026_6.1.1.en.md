Capitalizing on the bijection between subsets $A$ of $X$ and characteristic functions $\chi_A: X \to 2$, we regard the elements of $2^X$ as subsets of $X$. Show that a typical basic open set in the Tychonoff topology for $X$ copies of discrete $2$ is
$$
B_{(G, H)} = \{A \subset X : A \cap G = H\}
$$
where $G$ is a finite subset of $X$ and $H \subset G$.
