Assume $X$ is an output process and $Y$ is the output object of an $X$-automaton. The \textbf{observability map} is the unique dynamorphic coextension of the output morphism $\beta: Q \to Y$, given as the $X$-dynamorphism
$$\sigma: (Q, \delta) \longrightarrow (YX_{\sharp}, YL)$$
obtained via the universal property of the cofree dynamics $(YX_{\sharp}, YL; Y\Lambda)$.
