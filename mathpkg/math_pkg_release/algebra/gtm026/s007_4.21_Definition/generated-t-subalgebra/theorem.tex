Let $(X, \xi)$ be a $T$-algebra and let $A \subset X$ be a subset. The $T$-subalgebra $\langle A \rangle$ generated by $A$ is defined by the image factorization:

$$\xymatrix{
AT \ar[r]^{iT} \ar[d] & XT \ar[d]^{\xi} \\
\langle A \rangle \ar[r] & X
}$$

where $i: A \hookrightarrow X$ is the inclusion. Equivalently, $\langle A \rangle = \mathrm{Im}(iT \cdot \xi)$, the image of the composite map $AT \xrightarrow{iT} XT \xrightarrow{\xi} X$.
