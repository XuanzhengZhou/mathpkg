The only details left to check are the preservation of identities and composition, and these are immediate:

$$X\lambda.\xi = \xi \text{ if } X\lambda = \text{id}_{XT}$$
$$(X\lambda.X\lambda').\xi'' = X\lambda.(X\lambda'.\xi'')$$
