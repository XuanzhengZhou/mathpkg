Let $Y = \{0, 1\}$. Each response $f: X^* \rightarrow Y$ is then the characteristic function $\chi_A$ of a subset (``language'') $A$ of $X^*$. A system realizing such a response is called an acceptor (i.e., $M$ ``accepts'' $w$ in $X^*$ if $w$ is in $A$). Let $X = \{x, y\}$ and let $A = y^*\{x, y\}$.

Show that the deterministic minimal realization of $\chi_A$ has four states. [Hint: for $z$ in $X$, $B \subset X^*$, $L_zB = \{w:zw \in B\}$; the four states are $A, y^*x \cup y^*, \{A\}, \emptyset$.]
