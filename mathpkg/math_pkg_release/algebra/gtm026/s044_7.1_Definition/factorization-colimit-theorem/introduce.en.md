This theorem constructs small colimits in a category $\mathcal{C}$ structured over a base category $\mathcal{K}$ using the interplay of $E$-$M$ factorizations, quasi-colimits, and the $E$-co-well-poweredness condition. The proof proceeds by taking an arbitrary lower bound of a diagram $D$ in $\mathcal{C}^{\Delta}$, $E$-$M$ factoring its components to obtain a diagram $E$ in $\mathcal{K}$, applying a quasi-colimit of $E$, lifting via the left adjoint to $U$, and using optimal lifts to produce a small solution set. The theorem is noted as new in Manes, generalizing several prior results in the literature.
