*Proof.* Apply Corollary 7.9 with $\mathbf{T}$ taken to be the identity algebraic theory on $\mathcal{K}$ (i.e., $T = \mathrm{id}_{\mathcal{K}}$, $\eta = \mathrm{id}$, $\mu = \mathrm{id}$). Then $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}} \cong \mathcal{K}$, and the hypotheses of Corollary 7.9 reduce to the given conditions on $\mathcal{K}$. $\square$
