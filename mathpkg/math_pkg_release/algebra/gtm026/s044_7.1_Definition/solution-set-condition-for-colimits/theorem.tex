Let $\mathcal{K}$ be a locally small category which has small limits and let $\Delta$ be a diagram scheme. Then necessary and sufficient for every diagram $(\Delta, D)$ in $\mathcal{K}$ to have a colimit is that the embedding functor $(\tilde{\cdot}): \mathcal{K} \longrightarrow \mathcal{K}^{\Delta}$ of 7.1 satisfies the solution set condition.
