Let $(\tilde{\cdot}): \mathcal{K} \longrightarrow \mathcal{K}^{\Delta}$ be the embedding functor of 7.1, and let $(L, \psi)$ be an upper bound of a diagram $D$ in $\mathcal{K}^{\Delta}$. Then $(L, \psi)$ is a colimit of $D$ if and only if $(L, \psi)$ is free over $D$ with respect to $(\tilde{\cdot})$.
