Let $(\mathcal{A}, U) \in \operatorname{Struct}(\mathbf{Set})$ be a category of sets with structure. If $A \in \mathcal{A}$ and if $R$ is an equivalence relation on the set $AU$ with coordinate projections $p, q: R \to AU$, say that $R$ is a \textbf{congruence} on $A$ if $R$ lifts to $\bar{R}$ in $\mathcal{A}$ such that $\bar{R}U = R$ and $p, q: \bar{R} \to A$ are admissible in $\mathcal{A}$.
