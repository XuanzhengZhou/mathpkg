Let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathbf{Set}$ and fix a set $I$ and an element $p$ of $IT$. For $f, g: I \to X$ say that $f \sim_p g$ if $f$ and $g$ agree on a support of $p$. Prove that $\sim_p$ is an equivalence relation on $X^I$. [Hint: use exercise 1.5.2.]
