Let Cat/$\mathcal{K}$ be the category whose objects are pairs $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ with $U: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{K}$ an arbitrary functor and whose morphisms are functors over $\mathcal{K}$ just as in 3.3. Show that every such $(\mathcal{A}, U)$ has a reflection in Con($\mathcal{K}$), and hence in Struct($\mathcal{K}$). [Hint: keep the same objects and divide out by the obvious equivalence relation on morphisms.]
