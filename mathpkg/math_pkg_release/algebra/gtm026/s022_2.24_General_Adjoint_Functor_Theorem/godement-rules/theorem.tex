Given $\mathcal{K} \xrightarrow{F_1 \xrightarrow{\tau} F_2} \mathcal{L} \xrightarrow{G} \mathcal{M} \xrightarrow{H} \mathcal{N}$, then $(\tau G) H = \tau(G H): F_1 G H \rightarrow F_2 G H$. We write $\tau G H$.

Given $\mathcal{K} \xrightarrow{F} \mathcal{L} \xrightarrow{G} \mathcal{M} \xrightarrow{H_1 \xrightarrow{\tau} H_2} \mathcal{N}$, then $F(G \tau) = (FG) \tau: F G H_1 \rightarrow F G H_2$. We write $F G \tau$.

Given $\mathcal{K} \xrightarrow{F} \mathcal{L} \xrightarrow{G_1 \xrightarrow{\tau} G_2} \mathcal{M} \xrightarrow{H} \mathcal{N}$, then $(F \tau) H = F(\tau H): F G_1 H \rightarrow F G_2 H$. We write $F \tau H$.

Given $\mathcal{K} \xrightarrow{F} \mathcal{L} \xrightarrow{G_1 \xrightarrow{\tau} G_2 \xrightarrow{\sigma} G_3} \mathcal{M} \xrightarrow{H} \mathcal{N}$, then $F(\tau \cdot \sigma) H = F \tau H \cdot F \sigma H: F G_1 H \rightarrow F G_3 H$.
