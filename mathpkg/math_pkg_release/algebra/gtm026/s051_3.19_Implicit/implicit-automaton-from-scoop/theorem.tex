Let $\bar{M} = (\bar{Q}, \bar{\delta}, \xi, I, \bar{\tau}, Y, \theta, \bar{\beta})$ be an implicit $\lambda$-automaton and let $(Q, i, c)$ be any scoop of $\bar{Q}$. Then the $\lambda$-automaton $M = (Q, \delta, I, \tau, Y, \theta, \beta)$ where
\begin{align*}
\delta &= QX \xrightarrow{iX} \bar{Q}X \xrightarrow{\bar{\delta}} \bar{Q} \xrightarrow{c} QT,\\[4pt]
\tau &= I \xrightarrow{\bar{\tau}} \bar{Q} \xrightarrow{c} QT,\\[4pt]
\beta &= Q \xrightarrow{i} \bar{Q} \xrightarrow{\bar{\beta}} Y
\end{align*}
has the same response as $\bar{M}$.
