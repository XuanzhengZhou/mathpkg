The **pushout** is the dual concept of the pullback. Given morphisms $f: C \longrightarrow A$ and $g: C \longrightarrow B$ in a category, a pushout of $(f, g, C)$ is an object $P$ equipped with morphisms $a: A \longrightarrow P$ and $b: B \longrightarrow P$ such that $f \cdot a = g \cdot b$, and universal with this property.
