Corollary 2.11 sharpens Theorem 2.10: two algebraic categories (categories of algebras with their forgetful functors) are isomorphic exactly when their corresponding algebraic theories are isomorphic. This confirms that the theory is a complete invariant of the algebraic structure — the theory determines the category of algebras up to isomorphism, and conversely the isomorphism type of the category of algebras determines the theory.
