Exercise 2 of Section 2 establishes the equivalence between the Godement calculus of natural transformations and the monoidal structure on the endofunctor category. The five Godement rules governing the interaction of horizontal and vertical composition of natural transformations are exactly the axioms of a monoidal category when interpreted in $\mathcal{K}^\mathcal{K}$ with composition as the tensor product.
