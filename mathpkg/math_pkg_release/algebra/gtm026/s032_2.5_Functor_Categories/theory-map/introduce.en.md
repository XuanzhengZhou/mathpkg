A theory map is a morphism between algebraic theories, defined as a natural transformation between the underlying endofunctors that respects the monoid structure (unit and multiplication). Theory maps form the morphisms of the category of algebraic theories. This definition provides the correct notion of homomorphism between theories, analogous to how monoid homomorphisms relate monoids.
