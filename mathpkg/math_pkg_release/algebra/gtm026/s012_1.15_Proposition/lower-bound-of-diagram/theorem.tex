A \emph{lower bound} of a diagram $(\Delta, D)$ in a category $\mathcal{K}$ is a pair $(L, \psi)$ where $L$ is an object of $\mathcal{K}$ and $\psi$ assigns to each node $i \in N(\Delta)$ a $\mathcal{K}$-morphism $\psi_i: L \longrightarrow D_i$ such that the diagram

$$\psi_i \circ D_\alpha = \psi_j$$

commutes for every edge $\alpha \in \Delta(i, j)$.
