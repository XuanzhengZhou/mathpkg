If $\mathcal{K}$ is a preordered category, a lower bound of a diagram $(\Delta, D)$ is an object $L$ with $L \leqslant D_i$ for all $i \in N(\Delta)$. Moreover, $L$ is a limit of $(\Delta, D)$ if and only if $L = \operatorname{Inf}(D_i)$, the greatest lower bound of the family $\{D_i\}_{i \in N(\Delta)}$.
