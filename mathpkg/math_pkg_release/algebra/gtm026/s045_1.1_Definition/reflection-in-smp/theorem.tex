Let $\lambda: (1T, 1\mu) \longrightarrow E(X, \xi)$ be the unique $\mathbf{T}$-homomorphism such that $\langle 1\eta, \lambda \rangle = \mathrm{id}_X$. Then $\lambda$ is the reflection of $(1T, 1\mu)$ in the Birkhoff subcategory $\mathrm{SMP}(X, \xi)$ generated by $(X, \xi)$ (see 3.4.25; $S$ means "onto" and $M$ means "injective").
