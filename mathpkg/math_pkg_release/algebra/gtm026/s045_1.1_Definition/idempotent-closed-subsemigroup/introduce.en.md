Lemma 1.17 states that any nonempty closed subsemigroup of a compactible monoid contains an idempotent element (an element $u$ satisfying $uu = u$). The proof uses Zorn's lemma to find a minimal closed subsemigroup and then shows that such a minimal subsemigroup must consist of a single idempotent. This lemma is a fundamental result in topological semigroup theory and a crucial tool for establishing that compactible monoids are dynamic.
