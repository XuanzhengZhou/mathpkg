Corollary 1.23 establishes that the class of distal $T$-algebras forms a Birkhoff subcategory of the category of all $T$-algebras. This means the class is closed under products, subalgebras, and quotient algebras — the defining properties of a Birkhoff subcategory. The proof uses the equivalent characterizations from Theorem 1.21: closure under subalgebras and quotients follows from conditions (4) and (5), while closure under products uses the fact that injectivity is preserved by products.
