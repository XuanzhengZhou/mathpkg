A universal minimal $T$-algebra is a minimal $T$-algebra that maps homomorphically onto every other minimal $T$-algebra, and all of its endomorphisms are isomorphisms. The first two properties characterize the universal minimal algebra up to isomorphism: it is minimal and every other minimal algebra is a homomorphic image of it. The third property is a consequence that further illuminates the rigidity of such algebras. This notion is the algebraic analogue of the universal minimal transformation group in topological dynamics.
