An $\mathsf{E}$-Birkhoff subcategory is an abstract Birkhoff subcategory of the Eilenberg–Moore category $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$ whose reflection maps on free algebras lie in the distinguished class $\mathsf{E}$. This theorem provides two equivalent characterizations: the first in terms of closure under limits, $\mathsf{M}$-morphisms, and $\mathsf{U}^{\mathbf{T}}$-split epimorphisms; the second, under the additional hypothesis that every equalizer belongs to $\mathsf{M}$, replaces closure under limits with closure under products.
