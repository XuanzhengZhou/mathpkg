Let $(\mathcal{E}, \mathcal{M})$ be an image factorization system in $\mathcal{K}$. If $F, G : \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathcal{K}$ are functors and $\alpha : F \longrightarrow G$ is a natural transformation, let

$$AF \xrightarrow{Ae} AI \xrightarrow{Am} AG$$

be an $\mathcal{E}$-$\mathcal{M}$ factorization of $A\alpha$. Show that this yields a factorization of the natural transformation $\alpha$ into an $\mathcal{E}$-natural transformation followed by an $\mathcal{M}$-natural transformation. [Hint: use 4.13.]
