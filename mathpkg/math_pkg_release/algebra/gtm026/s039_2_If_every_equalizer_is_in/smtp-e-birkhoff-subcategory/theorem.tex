Let $(\mathcal{K}, \mathsf{E}, \mathsf{M})$ be a regular category in which every equalizer is in $\mathsf{M}$ and let $\mathbf{T}$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathcal{K}$ such that $e\mathbf{T}$ is in $\mathsf{E}$ whenever $e$ is in $\mathsf{E}$. Let $(\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}, \mathsf{E}^{\mathbf{T}}, \mathsf{M}^{\mathbf{T}})$ be the regular category of 4.17, and let $\mathcal{B}$ be a full replete subcategory of $\mathcal{K}^{\mathbf{T}}$. Then $\mathsf{S}^{\mathbf{T}}\mathsf{M}^{\mathbf{T}}\mathsf{P}(\mathcal{A})$ is the $\mathsf{E}$-Birkhoff subcategory generated by $\mathcal{A}$, where $\mathsf{S}^{\mathbf{T}}(\mathcal{A})$ is the class of all $\mathbf{T}$-algebras admitting a $\mathsf{U}^{\mathbf{T}}$-split epimorphism from an algebra in $\mathcal{A}$.
