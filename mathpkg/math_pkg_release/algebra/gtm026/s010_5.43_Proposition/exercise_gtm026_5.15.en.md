Let $\omega \in XT$ and let $S$ be a subset of $X$ with inclusion map $i : S \to X$. Show that $S$ is a support of $\tilde{\omega}$ if and only if $\omega$ lies in the image of $ST \xrightarrow{iT} XT$.
