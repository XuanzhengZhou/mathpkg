Tractability at a cardinal $n$ is a size condition on a functor $U: A \to \mathbf{Set}$ requiring that the collection of natural transformations from the $n$-fold power functor $U^n$ to $U$ forms a small set (i.e., a set rather than a proper class). This condition is crucial for ensuring that equational presentations correspond to genuine algebraic theories without running into set-theoretic size issues.
