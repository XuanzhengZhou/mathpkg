$X: \text{Set} \longrightarrow \text{Set}$ is finitary if, for all $A$, every element of $AX$ has finite support, i.e., is in the image of $fX$ for some $f:n \longrightarrow A$ with $n$ finite.

Show that $X$ is finitary if and only if $X$ is a quotient functor of $\coprod_{n \in \mathbb{N}} (-)^n \times X_n$, where $X_n$ is the set of elements of $nX$ with full support.
