Prove that if $X_{\Omega}$ is an output process in $\text{Set}$ then $\Omega_n$ is empty whenever $n \neq 1$.

[Hint: by the Beck theorem, $\text{Dyn}(X_{\Omega}) \longrightarrow \text{Set}$ is co-algebraic, so creates colimits.]
