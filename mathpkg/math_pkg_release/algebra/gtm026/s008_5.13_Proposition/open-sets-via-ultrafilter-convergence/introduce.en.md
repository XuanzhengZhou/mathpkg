Statement 5.28 provides a purely convergence-theoretic characterization of open sets: a subset $A$ is open precisely when it belongs to every ultrafilter that converges to some point inside $A$. This means the convergence relation between ultrafilters and points completely determines the topology. This result underpins the ultrafilter-based approach to topology, where the fundamental notion is convergence rather than open sets.
