If $X$ is a complete atomic Boolean algebra, the \text{structure map} of $X$ is defined by

$$XT \xrightarrow{\xi} X$$
$$A \mapsto \sup(A' : A \in A)$$

where $A' = \operatorname{Inf}(\operatorname{prin}(x) : x \in A) \wedge \bigwedge((\operatorname{prin}(x))' : x \notin A)$ and $\operatorname{prin}(x) = \{B \subset X : x \in B\}$ is the principal ultrafilter at $x$.
