Definition 5.18 defines the structure map that makes a complete atomic Boolean algebra into an algebra over the double power-set theory of 3.19. Each element $A$ of $XT$ (a subset of $X$) is sent to the supremum of all "atoms" $A'$ associated with elements $A \in A$. Here $\operatorname{prin}(x)$ denotes the principal ultrafilter at $x$. This structure map satisfies the T-algebra laws, establishing that complete atomic Boolean algebras are precisely the algebras for the double power-set theory.
