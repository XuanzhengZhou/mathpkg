Let $X$ be a set. A subset $\mathcal{F} \subset 2^X$ is a \text{filter} on $X$ if:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathcal{F} \neq \varnothing$
\item $\varnothing \notin \mathcal{F}$
\item If $F_1, F_2 \in \mathcal{F}$ then $F_1 \cap F_2 \in \mathcal{F}$
\item If $F \in \mathcal{F}$ and $F \subset G \subset X$ then $G \in \mathcal{F}$
\end{enumerate}
