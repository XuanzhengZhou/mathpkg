This proposition establishes a technical lifting property for surjective $\Omega$-homomorphisms that is needed to verify the associativity of clone composition. Given a surjective $\Omega$-homomorphism between term algebras, the induced map on tuples of terms is compatible with the substitution operations that define clone composition. The statement in the source text is partially corrupted by OCR artifacts, but its role is to justify that clone composition as defined in Definition 2.7 is well-behaved.
