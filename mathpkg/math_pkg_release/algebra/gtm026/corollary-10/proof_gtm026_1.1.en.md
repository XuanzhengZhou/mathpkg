That “distal” is closed under subalgebras and quotient algebras is immediate from 1.21 (4) and (5). Now let $(X, \xi)$ be the product of the family $(X_i, \xi_i)$ of distal algebras. The diagram shows that $\xi^g = \prod \xi_i^g$ so that $\xi^g$ is injective. $\square$
