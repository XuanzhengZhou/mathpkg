Prove that the category of reflexive and transitively ordered sets and order-preserving maps is isomorphic in $\text{Struct}(\text{Set})$ to the category of topological spaces in which every intersection of open sets is open and continuous maps.

[*Hint:* $x \leqslant y$ if and only if $x \in \{y\}^-$].

Use this as an aid to constructing the lattice of all topologies on a three-element set; there are 29 such topologies and 9 homeomorphism classes.
