Prove that if $U^T: \mathbf{Set}^T \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}$ preserves coproducts, then $\mathbf{T}$ is the algebraic theory corresponding to the monoid $1T$. [Hint: since $T$ preserves coproducts and every set is the coproduct of its elements, $T$ is naturally equivalent to $1T \times -$.]
