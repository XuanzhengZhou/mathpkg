(M. Barr.) A Beck functor $U: \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \mathcal{B}$ is **crude** if whenever $h = \text{coeq}(f, g)$ then $hU = \text{coeq}(fU, gU)$. Prove that every algebraic functor is a crude Beck functor. Show that the converse is false.
