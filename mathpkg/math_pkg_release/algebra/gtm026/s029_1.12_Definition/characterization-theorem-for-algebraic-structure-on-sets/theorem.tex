Let $(\mathcal{A}, U) \in \text{Struct}(\text{Set})$ be a category of sets with structure. Then $U: \mathcal{A} \longrightarrow \text{Set}$ is algebraic if and only if the following three conditions are satisfied:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $U$ has a left adjoint;
\item $\mathcal{A}$ has separators (1.11); and
\item $U$ creates quotients of congruences (1.12).
\end{enumerate}
