If $\psi: L \to D$ is a limit cone for a diagram $(\Delta, D)$ in a category $\mathcal{K}$, then the family of projection morphisms $(\psi_i: L \to D_i)$ is collectively monic. This means that two morphisms into $L$ are equal whenever their compositions with every $\psi_i$ coincide. The proof is immediate from the universal property of limits and generalizes the special case 2.1.15.
