**Proof.** $W$ has a left adjoint by 2.2.30. Suppose that

$$P_1W \xrightarrow{fW} P_2W \xrightarrow{h} K$$

is an absolute coequalizer in $\mathcal{K}$. We will show that $K \in \mathcal{L}$. As $P_1Wsi = P_1Visi = P_1Vi = P_1W$ and, similarly, $P_2Wsi = P_2W$, $(fW, gW, hsi)$ is a coequalizer in $\mathcal{K}$ and there exists an isomorphism $\psi: Ksi \longrightarrow K$ such that $hsi \cdot \psi = h$. Since $\mathcal{L}$ is replete, $K$ is in $\mathcal{L}$.

The remaining details follow from the properties of the pullback diagram and the hypothesis that $U$ creates coequalizers of $U$-absolute pairs. $\square$
