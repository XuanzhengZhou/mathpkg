This proposition combines Beck's theorem with the General Adjoint Functor Theorem (2.24) and the Special Adjoint Functor Theorem (2.29). A Beck functor is algebraic precisely when it has a left adjoint -- equivalently, when it satisfies the solution set condition. In the presence of a cogenerator and well-poweredness, every Beck functor automatically has a left adjoint, hence is algebraic.
