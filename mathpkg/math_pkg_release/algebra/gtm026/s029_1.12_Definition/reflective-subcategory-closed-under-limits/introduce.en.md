This is an immediate corollary of Propositions 1.33 and 1.19: since the inclusion functor of a full replete reflective subcategory is algebraic, and every algebraic functor is Beck (hence creates limits), the subcategory is closed under limits. This generalizes the familiar fact that reflective subcategories are closed under limits that exist in the ambient category.
