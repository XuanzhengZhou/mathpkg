Let $X$ be a set and let $\mathbf{T} = (T, e, m)$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathbf{Set}$. For each set $Q$, define the map
$$
\lambda_Q \colon QT \times X \longrightarrow (Q \times X)T
$$
by
$$
(p, x) \longmapsto \langle p, \mathrm{in}_x T \rangle
$$
where $\mathrm{in}_x \colon Q \longrightarrow Q \times X$ sends $q$ to $(q, x)$. Then $\lambda$ is a distributive law of the functor $- \times X$ over the algebraic theory $\mathbf{T}$.
