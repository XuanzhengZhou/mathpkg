Let $X$ be an input process in $\mathcal{K}$ with induced algebraic theory $\mathbf{X}^{\mathbf{@}} = (X^{\mathbf{@}}, \eta, \mu)$, let $\mathbf{T} = (T, e, m)$ be an algebraic theory in $\mathcal{K}$, and let $\lambda \colon TX \longrightarrow XT$ be a distributive law of $X$ over $\mathbf{T}$. Then the category $\mathcal{K}^{\lambda}$ of $\lambda$-algebras is algebraic over $\mathcal{K}$.
