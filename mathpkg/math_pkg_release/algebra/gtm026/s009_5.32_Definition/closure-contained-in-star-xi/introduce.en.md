This lemma establishes the relationship between the topological closure in the $\mathcal{T}$-topology induced by an abstract $\beta$-algebra and the star operation. For any subset $B \subset X$, the $\mathcal{T}$-closure $B^-$ is contained in $B^*\xi$. The proof relies on the algebra axioms of the $\beta$-algebra, using the fact that $B^*\xi = \langle B \rangle$ (the generated subalgebra) and verifying that $B^*\xi$ is $\mathcal{T}$-closed.
