Theorem 5 establishes the fundamental Galois correspondence between finite groups of automorphisms and Galois subfields of finite co-dimension. The order of the Galois group equals the field degree, the fixed field of the Galois group recovers the base field, and there is an inclusion-reversing bijection between subgroups and intermediate fields. Normal subgroups correspond to Galois intermediate extensions with quotient isomorphic to the Galois group.
