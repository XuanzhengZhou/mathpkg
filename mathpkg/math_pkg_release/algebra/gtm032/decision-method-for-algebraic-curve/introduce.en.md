The Tarski-Seidenberg decision method: The elementary theory of real closed fields is decidable via quantifier elimination. Any first-order formula involving polynomial equations and inequalities can be algorithmically reduced to an equivalent quantifier-free formula. This implies decidability of elementary geometry (Tarski's result) and is based on Sturm's theorem and the algebraic properties of real closed fields.
