This theorem characterizes when a homomorphism from a subring $\mathfrak{A}$ can be extended to the subring generated by elements $t_i$ that may satisfy algebraic relations. The necessary and sufficient condition is that every polynomial relation satisfied by the $t_i$ over $\mathfrak{A}$ is also satisfied by the chosen images $u_i$ after applying $s$ to the coefficients. This provides the universal property of rings presented by generators and relations.
