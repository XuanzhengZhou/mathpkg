Let $P/\Phi$ be a finitely generated extension of characteristic $p$. A $p$-basis of $P/\Phi$ is a minimal set $B$ such that $P = \Phi(B, P^p)$. The $p$-degree (or Ershov invariant) is $|B|$. Derivations of $P/\Phi$ form a $P$-vector space of dimension equal to the $p$-degree. Separability is characterized by the existence of enough derivations.
