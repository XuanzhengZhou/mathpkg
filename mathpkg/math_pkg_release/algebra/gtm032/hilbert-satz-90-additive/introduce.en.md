Hilbert's Theorem 90 in additive form: Solutions to the additive cocycle condition $\delta_{st} = \delta_s^t + \delta_t$ are precisely "coboundaries" $\delta_s = \gamma - \gamma^s$. The proof constructs $\gamma$ using an element of non-zero trace. The classical formulation for cyclic extensions states that trace-zero elements are differences of conjugates.
