Let $P/\Phi$ be a finite dimensional Galois extension with Galois group $G$. If $\delta_s \in P$ satisfies $\delta_{st} = \delta_s^t + \delta_t$ for all $s, t \in G$, then there exists $\gamma \in P$ such that $\delta_s = \gamma - \gamma^s$. In particular, if $G$ is cyclic generated by $g$ and $T_{P|\Phi}(\mu) = 0$, then $\mu = \gamma - \gamma^g$ for some $\gamma \in P$.
