Lueroth's Theorem: Every intermediate field between $\Phi$ and the rational function field $\Phi(x)$ is a simple transcendental extension. That is, any subfield of $\Phi(x)$ containing $\Phi$ is isomorphic to $\Phi(y)$ for some transcendental $y$. This is a fundamental result in the theory of transcendence degree one extensions and is a special case of the more general Castelnuovo criterion.
