Let $\Phi$ be a subfield of a field $P$. The Galois group of $P$ over $\Phi$, denoted $A(\Phi)$ (or $A_P(\Phi)$), is the group of automorphisms $s$ of $P$ such that $\alpha^s = \alpha$ for all $\alpha \in \Phi$. Thus

$$A(\Phi) = \{s \in \operatorname{Aut}(P) \mid \alpha^s = \alpha \text{ for all } \alpha \in \Phi\}.$$
