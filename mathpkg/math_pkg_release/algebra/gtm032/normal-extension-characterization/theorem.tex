The following three conditions on a finite dimensional extension $P/\Phi$ are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] $P/\Phi$ is a splitting field of a polynomial $f(x) \in \Phi[x]$.
\item[(2)] Every isomorphism $s$ of $P/\Phi$ into an extension field $\Delta/\Phi$ is an automorphism of $P/\Phi$.
\item[(3)] Every irreducible polynomial $g(x) \in \Phi[x]$ which has a root in $P$ is a product of linear factors in $P[x]$.
\end{enumerate}
An extension satisfying these equivalent conditions is called \text{normal}.
