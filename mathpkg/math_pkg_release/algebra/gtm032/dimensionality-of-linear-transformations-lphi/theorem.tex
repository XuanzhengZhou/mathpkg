Let $E$ and $P$ be fields over a field $\Phi$ and let $\mathfrak{L}_\Phi(E, P)$ be the set of linear transformations of $E/\Phi$ into $P/\Phi$, considered as a right vector space over $P$. If $[E:\Phi] = n < \infty$, then
$$[\mathfrak{L}_\Phi(E, P) : P]_R = n.$$
Moreover, if $(\eta_1, \ldots, \eta_n)$ is a basis for $E/\Phi$ and $E_1, \ldots, E_n$ are the associated projections (defined by $\eta_j E_i = \delta_{ij}$), then $\{E_1, \ldots, E_n\}$ is a basis for $\mathfrak{L}_\Phi(E, P)$ over $P$.
