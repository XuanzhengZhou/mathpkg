This corollary makes explicit that the Jacobson-Bourbaki theorem establishes a perfect Galois correspondence between subfields of finite co-dimension and subrings of endomorphisms satisfying the three conditions. The mappings $R$ (taking a subfield to its ring of $\Phi$-linear transformations) and $F$ (taking a subring to its "commuting field") are mutual inverses, giving a complete classification.
