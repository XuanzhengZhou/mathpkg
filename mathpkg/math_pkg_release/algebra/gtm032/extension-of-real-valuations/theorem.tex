Let $K$ be a field with a real valuation $v$ and $L = K(\theta)$ a finite simple extension where $\theta$ has minimum polynomial $f(x) \in K[x]$. The extensions $w$ of $v$ to $L$ correspond to the irreducible factors of $f(x)$ over the completion $\hat{K}$. If $f(x) = \prod f_i(x)$ over $\hat{K}$, then each $f_i$ determines an extension $w_i$, and $\hat{L}_{w_i} \cong \hat{K}[x]/(f_i(x))$.
