Let $\Phi$ be a field and $f(x)$ a polynomial of positive degree with coefficients in $\Phi$ and leading coefficient $1$. An extension field $P/\Phi$ is called a \text{splitting field} of $f(x)$ over $\Phi$ if in $P[x]$
$$f(x) = (x - \rho_1)(x - \rho_2) \cdots (x - \rho_n), \quad \rho_i \in P,$$
and $P = \Phi(\rho_1, \rho_2, \ldots, \rho_n)$.
