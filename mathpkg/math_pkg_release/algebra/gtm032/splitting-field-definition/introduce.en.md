A splitting field of a polynomial $f(x)$ over $\Phi$ is a minimal extension of $\Phi$ in which $f(x)$ factors completely into linear factors. It is generated over $\Phi$ by the roots of $f(x)$. The splitting field is a central construction in Galois theory: it is the smallest field over which the polynomial "splits," and its automorphism group (fixing $\Phi$) is the Galois group of the polynomial.
