Let $\Phi$ be a field of characteristic $p \neq 0$. Artin-Schreier theory classifies cyclic extensions of degree $p$: they have the form $\Phi(\theta)$ where $\theta^p - \theta = a \in \Phi$, with $a$ not of the form $b^p - b$ for $b \in \Phi$. More generally, Witt vectors classify all abelian $p$-extensions of a field of characteristic $p$.
