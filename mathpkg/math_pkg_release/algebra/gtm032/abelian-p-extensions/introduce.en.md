Abelian $p$-extensions in characteristic $p$ are classified by Artin-Schreier-Witt theory. Cyclic extensions of degree $p$ are generated by roots of Artin-Schreier equations $x^p - x = a$. Higher degree extensions use Witt vectors to generalize this construction. This is the characteristic $p$ analogue of Kummer theory.
