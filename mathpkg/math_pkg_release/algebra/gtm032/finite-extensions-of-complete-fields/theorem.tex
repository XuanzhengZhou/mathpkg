Let $K$ be complete with respect to a discrete valuation, and $L/K$ a finite extension. Then $L$ is also complete, the valuation extends uniquely to $L$, and $[L:K] = ef$ where $e$ is the ramification index and $f$ is the residue degree. The extension of residue fields is $\bar{L}/\bar{K}$, and any finite extension of the residue field lifts to an unramified extension.
