Within the space $\mathfrak{A}$ spanned by a finite automorphism group $G$, the $P$-subspaces that are also subrings correspond bijectively to subgroups $H$ of $G$. Such a subspace $\mathfrak{B}$ is precisely the $P$-linear span of $H = G \cap \mathfrak{B}$. This theorem refines the Jacobson-Bourbaki correspondence for the special case of automorphism groups, showing that the lattice of $P$-subspace-subrings of $\mathfrak{A}$ mirrors the lattice of subgroups of $G$.
