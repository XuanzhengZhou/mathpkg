Indeterminates are elements that satisfy no non-trivial polynomial relations with coefficients in $\mathfrak{A}$. In other words, they are algebraically independent over $\mathfrak{A}$. A polynomial ring $\mathfrak{A}[x_1, \ldots, x_r]$ with indeterminates $x_i$ has the property that distinct polynomials represent distinct elements of the ring.
