This theorem describes the behavior of the tensor product on bases: if $\{e_\alpha\}$ and $\{f_\beta\}$ are bases of $\mathfrak{M}$ and $\mathfrak{N}$ respectively, then $\{e_\alpha \otimes f_\beta\}$ is a basis of the tensor product $\mathfrak{M} \otimes_\Phi \mathfrak{N}$. Consequently, tensor products of vector spaces are characterized up to isomorphism by this property among bilinear maps.
