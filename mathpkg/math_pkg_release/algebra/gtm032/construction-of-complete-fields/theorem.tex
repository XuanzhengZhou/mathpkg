Given a field $\Phi$ and an ordered group $\Gamma$, one can construct a complete field $K$ whose valuation has value group $\Gamma$ and residue field $\Phi$. For $\Gamma = \mathbb{Z}$, the construction yields the field of formal Laurent series $\Phi((t))$ with the $t$-adic valuation. More general constructions yield fields with prescribed residue field and value group.
