Every automorphism of the algebra $\mathfrak{L}_\Phi(P)$ of $\Phi$-linear transformations of $P/\Phi$ which fixes the subring $P_R$ of right multiplications is inner. Equivalently, the Skolem-Noether theorem for the central simple algebra $\mathfrak{L}_\Phi(P)$: every $\Phi$-automorphism fixing $P_R$ is given by conjugation by an invertible element.
