If $\Delta/P$ and $P/\Phi$ are finite dimensional extensions, then

$$[\Delta:\Phi]_s = [\Delta:P]_s [P:\Phi]_s, \quad [\Delta:\Phi]_i = [\Delta:P]_i [P:\Phi]_i,$$

where $[K:k]_s$ denotes the separability degree and $[K:k]_i$ the inseparability degree. Moreover,

$$[K:k] = [K:k]_s [K:k]_i$$

for any finite dimensional extension $K/k$.
