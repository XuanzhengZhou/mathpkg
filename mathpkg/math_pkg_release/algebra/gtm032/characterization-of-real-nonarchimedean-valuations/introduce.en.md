This theorem distinguishes archimedean from non-archimedean valuations: a rank-1 valuation is non-archimedean exactly when the integers are "bounded" in the valuation. The only archimedean completions are $\mathbb{R}$ and $\mathbb{C}$; all other completions of global fields are non-archimedean local fields.
