Let $K$ be a field with a real valuation $v$. The completion $\hat{K}$ of $K$ with respect to $v$ is the set of equivalence classes of Cauchy sequences, with field operations extended by continuity. $\hat{K}$ is a complete field with a unique extension of $v$, and $K$ is dense in $\hat{K}$. The elements of $\hat{K}$ can be represented by "series" in a uniformizing element.
