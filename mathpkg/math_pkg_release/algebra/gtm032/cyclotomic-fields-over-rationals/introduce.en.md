Cyclotomic fields are obtained by adjoining roots of unity to $\mathbb{Q}$. They are abelian extensions with Galois group $(\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z})^*$. The Kronecker-Weber theorem states that every abelian extension of $\mathbb{Q}$ is contained in some cyclotomic field. Cyclotomic fields are fundamental in algebraic number theory and class field theory.
