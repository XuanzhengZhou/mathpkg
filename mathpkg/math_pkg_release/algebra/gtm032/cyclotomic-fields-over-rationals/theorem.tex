Let $\zeta_n$ be a primitive $n$-th root of unity over $\mathbb{Q}$. The cyclotomic field $\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_n)$ is a Galois extension of $\mathbb{Q}$ with Galois group isomorphic to $(\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z})^*$, the multiplicative group of units modulo $n$. The degree is $\varphi(n)$, Euler's totient function. The ring of integers is $\mathbb{Z}[\zeta_n]$.
