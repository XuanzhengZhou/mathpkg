This lemma establishes that the distinct isomorphisms of a finite separable extension into an extension of an infinite field are algebraically independent. The proof uses Dedekind's independence theorem to show that the determinant $\det(\epsilon_i^{s_j})$ is non-zero for a basis, then uses the infiniteness of $\Phi$ to deduce algebraic independence.
