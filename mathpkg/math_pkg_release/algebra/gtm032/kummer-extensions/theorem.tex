Let $\Phi$ be a field containing a primitive $n$-th root of unity, and let $P/\Phi$ be a finite dimensional Galois extension with abelian Galois group of exponent dividing $n$. Then $P$ is generated over $\Phi$ by elements $\rho_i$ satisfying $\rho_i^n = a_i \in \Phi$. Conversely, any extension generated by such radicals is abelian of exponent dividing $n$. The correspondence is given by the Kummer pairing.
