Kummer theory classifies abelian extensions of exponent $n$ over fields containing $n$-th roots of unity. Such extensions are generated by radicals $\sqrt[n]{a_i}$, and there is a perfect duality (the Kummer pairing) between the Galois group and the subgroup of $\Phi^*/(\Phi^*)^n$ generated by the $a_i$. This is the fundamental structure theory for abelian extensions.
