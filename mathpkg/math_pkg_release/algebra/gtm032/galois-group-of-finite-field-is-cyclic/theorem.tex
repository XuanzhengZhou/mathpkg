Let $P$ be a finite field of characteristic $p$ with $|P| = p^N$. Then the Frobenius automorphism $\pi: \xi \mapsto \xi^p$ generates the Galois group of $P$ over any subfield. If $\Phi \subseteq P$ with $[\Phi] = p^m$, then the Galois group of $P/\Phi$ is cyclic of order $N/m$, generated by $\pi^m$.
