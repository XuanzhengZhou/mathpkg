Let $\mathfrak{A}/\Phi$ be a finite dimensional algebra and $a \in \mathfrak{A}$. The trace $T_{\mathfrak{A}|\Phi}(a)$ is the trace of the matrix of the right multiplication $a_R$ relative to any basis. The norm $N_{\mathfrak{A}|\Phi}(a)$ is the determinant of this matrix. For a finite extension $P/\Phi$, $T_{P|\Phi}(\rho) = \sum_{s \in \text{Iso}} \rho^s$ and $N_{P|\Phi}(\rho) = \prod_{s \in \text{Iso}} \rho^s$, where the sum and product are over all isomorphisms of $P/\Phi$ into an algebraic closure.
