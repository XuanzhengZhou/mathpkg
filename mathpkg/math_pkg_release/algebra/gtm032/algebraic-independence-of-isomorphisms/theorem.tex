Let $E$ be a field over $\Phi$ and $\Omega$ an extension of $E$. Isomorphisms $s_1, \ldots, s_m$ of $E/\Phi$ into $\Omega/\Phi$ are called algebraically independent over $\Omega$ if the only polynomial $f(x_1, \ldots, x_m) \in \Omega[x_1, \ldots, x_m]$ such that $f(ta^{s_1}, \ldots, ta^{s_m}) = 0$ for all $ta \in E$ is the zero polynomial.
