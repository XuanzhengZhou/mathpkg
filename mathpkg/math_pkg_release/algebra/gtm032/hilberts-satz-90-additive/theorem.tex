Let $P/\Phi$ be a finite dimensional cyclic extension, $g$ a generating automorphism of the Galois group of $P/\Phi$. Then any element $\mu \in P$ such that $T_{P|\Phi}(\mu) = 0$ has the form $\mu = \gamma - \gamma^g$ for some $\gamma \in P$, where $T_{P|\Phi}$ denotes the trace from $P$ to $\Phi$.
