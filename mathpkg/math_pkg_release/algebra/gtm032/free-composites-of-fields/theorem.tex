Let $E_i/\Phi$ ($i \in I$) be a family of field extensions. A free composite is an extension $P/\Phi$ with embeddings $\sigma_i: E_i \to P$ such that $P$ is generated by the images and the images are "freely" joined. Equivalently, the canonical map $\bigotimes_\Phi E_i \to P$ has kernel generated by zero divisors. Free composites exist and are unique up to isomorphism.
