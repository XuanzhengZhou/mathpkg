Prove that the following are necessary and sufficient conditions that the subspaces $\mathfrak{S}_i$ be independent:

$$
\mathfrak{S}_1 \cap \mathfrak{S}_2 = 0, \quad (\mathfrak{S}_1 + \mathfrak{S}_2) \cap \mathfrak{S}_3 = 0,
$$
$$
(\mathfrak{S}_1 + \mathfrak{S}_2 + \mathfrak{S}_3) \cap \mathfrak{S}_4 = 0, \quad \cdots
$$
