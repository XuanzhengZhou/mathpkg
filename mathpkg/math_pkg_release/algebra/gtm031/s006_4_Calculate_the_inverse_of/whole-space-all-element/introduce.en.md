The whole vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ serves as the all element (top element) in the lattice of subspaces. For any subspace $\mathfrak{S}$, the join with $\mathfrak{R}$ yields $\mathfrak{R}$ and the meet (intersection) with $\mathfrak{R}$ returns $\mathfrak{S}$. This dual nature complements the zero subspace which acts as the null element.
