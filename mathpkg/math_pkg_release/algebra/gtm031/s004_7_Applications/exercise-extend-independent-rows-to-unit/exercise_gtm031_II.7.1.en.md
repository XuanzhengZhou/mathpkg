Prove that, if $(\alpha_{11}, \alpha_{12}, \cdots, \alpha_{1n}), (\alpha_{21}, \alpha_{22}, \cdots, \alpha_{2n}), \cdots, (\alpha_{r1}, \alpha_{r2}, \cdots, \alpha_{rn})$ are (left) linearly independent, then there exist $\alpha_{ij}$, $i = r + 1, \cdots, n$, $j = 1, 2, \cdots, n$, such that $(\alpha) = (\alpha_{ij})$ is a unit.
