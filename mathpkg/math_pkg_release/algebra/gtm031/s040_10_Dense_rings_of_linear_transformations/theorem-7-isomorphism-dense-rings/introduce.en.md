Theorem 7 generalizes the classical isomorphism theorem for full rings of linear transformations to arbitrary dense rings containing non-zero finite rank transformations. It states that every isomorphism between such rings is induced by a semi-linear transformation $U$ between the underlying vector spaces, with the additional condition that the transpose $U^*$ maps the dual spaces onto each other. This theorem implies that the base division rings are isomorphic and the vector spaces have the same dimension, extending the finite-dimensional result to the infinite-dimensional setting.
