Apply Theorem 7 with $\mathfrak{A}_1 = \mathfrak{A}_2 = \mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}' \mid \mathfrak{R})$, $\mathfrak{R}_1 = \mathfrak{R}_2 = \mathfrak{R}$, $\Delta_1 = \Delta_2 = \Phi$. Since the automorphism fixes the center $\Phi$ elementwise, the associated isomorphism of $\Phi$ is the identity, so the semi-linear transformation $U$ is actually linear. The conclusion $A\phi = U^{-1}AU$ for all $A$ shows that the automorphism is inner.
