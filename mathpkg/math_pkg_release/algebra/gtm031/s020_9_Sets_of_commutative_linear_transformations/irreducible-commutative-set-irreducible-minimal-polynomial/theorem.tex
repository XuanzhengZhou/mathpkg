Let $\Omega$ be a commutative set of linear transformations in a vector space $\Re$ over a field $\Phi$, and assume that $\Re$ is irreducible relative to $\Omega$ (i.e., $\Re$ has no proper non-zero $\Omega$-invariant subspaces). Then the minimum polynomial $\mu(\lambda)$ of every $A \in \Omega$ is irreducible over $\Phi$.
