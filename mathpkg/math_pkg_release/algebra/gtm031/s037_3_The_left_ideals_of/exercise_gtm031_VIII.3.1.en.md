A left (right, two-sided) ideal $\mathfrak{J}'$ is called **minimal** if $\mathfrak{J}' \neq 0$ and there exists no left (right, two-sided) ideal $\mathfrak{J}''$ such that $\mathfrak{J}' \supset \mathfrak{J}'' \supset 0$. Show that $\mathfrak{L}$ possesses minimal left ideals and find their form.
