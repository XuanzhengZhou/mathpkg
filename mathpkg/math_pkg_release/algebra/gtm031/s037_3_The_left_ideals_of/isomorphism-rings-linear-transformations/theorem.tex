Let $\mathfrak{R}_i$, $i = 1, 2$, be vector spaces over division rings $\Delta_i$, and let $\mathfrak{L}_i$ be the ring of linear transformations in $\mathfrak{R}_i$ over $\Delta_i$. If $\mathfrak{L}_1$ is isomorphic to $\mathfrak{L}_2$, then $\Delta_1$ is isomorphic to $\Delta_2$ and the two vector spaces $\mathfrak{R}_1$ and $\mathfrak{R}_2$ have the same dimensionality.
