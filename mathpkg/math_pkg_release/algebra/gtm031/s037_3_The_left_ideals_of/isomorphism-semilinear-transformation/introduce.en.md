Theorem 8 provides a complete description of all isomorphisms between rings of linear transformations of vector spaces over division rings. It shows that every such ring isomorphism is inner in a generalized sense: it is given by conjugation by a semilinear bijection between the underlying vector spaces. This is one of the foundational structure theorems in the theory of rings of linear transformations, and it also implies that the division ring and the dimension are invariants of the ring of linear transformations.
