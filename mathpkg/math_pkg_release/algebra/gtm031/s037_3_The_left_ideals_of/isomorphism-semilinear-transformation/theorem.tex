Let $\mathfrak{R}_i$, $i = 1, 2$, be vector spaces over division rings $\Delta_i$, and let $\mathfrak{L}_i$ be the ring of linear transformations in $\mathfrak{R}_i$ over $\Delta_i$. Then any isomorphism $A_1 \mapsto A_1\phi$ of $\mathfrak{L}_1$ onto $\mathfrak{L}_2$ is given by a 1-1 semilinear transformation $U$ of $\mathfrak{R}_1$ onto $\mathfrak{R}_2$ in the sense that
$$A_1\phi = U^{-1} A_1 U$$
for all $A_1 \in \mathfrak{L}_1$.
