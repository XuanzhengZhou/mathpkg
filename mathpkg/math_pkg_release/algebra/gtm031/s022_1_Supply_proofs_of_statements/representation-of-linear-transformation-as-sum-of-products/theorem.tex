Let $\mathfrak{R}_i$ and $\mathfrak{R}_i'$, $i = 1, 2$, be dual vector spaces relative to non-degenerate bilinear forms $g_i : \mathfrak{R}_i \times \mathfrak{R}_i' \to \Delta$. Let $A : \mathfrak{R}_1 \to \mathfrak{R}_2$ be a linear transformation.

Then there exist vectors $u_1', u_2', \dots, u_r' \in \mathfrak{R}_1'$ and $v_1, v_2, \dots, v_r \in \mathfrak{R}_2$ such that $A$ can be expressed uniquely as

$$A = u_1' \times v_1 + u_2' \times v_2 + \cdots + u_r' \times v_r,$$

where $u_i' \times v_i$ denotes the linear transformation $x \mapsto g_1(x, u_i')\, v_i$. The integer $r$ is the rank of the transformation $A$.
