If
$$\mathfrak{R} = \mathfrak{R}_1 \oplus \mathfrak{R}_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_s = \mathfrak{S}_1 \oplus \mathfrak{S}_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{S}_t$$
are two direct sum decompositions of $\mathfrak{R}$ into indecomposable $\Omega$-invariant subspaces $\mathfrak{R}_i$ and $\mathfrak{S}_j$, then $s = t$ and the summands can be put into 1-1 correspondence in such a way that corresponding summands are $\Omega$-isomorphic.
