This dimension formula relates the sizes of the left and right radicals to the dimensions of the vector spaces and the rank of the bilinear form. It follows from the rank-nullity theorem applied to the linear transformations $L$ and $R$, together with the fact that both images have dimension equal to the rank $r$. The formula shows that the radicals vanish precisely when the dimensions of both spaces equal the rank.
