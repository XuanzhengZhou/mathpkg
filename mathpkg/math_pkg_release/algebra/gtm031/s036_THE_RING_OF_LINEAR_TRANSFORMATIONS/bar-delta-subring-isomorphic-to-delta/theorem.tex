The set $\bar{\Delta} = \{\bar{\alpha} \mid \alpha \in \Delta\}$ of scalar transformations (relative to a fixed basis) is a subring of the endomorphism ring $\mathfrak{L}$, and the map

$$\varphi: \Delta \to \bar{\Delta}, \quad \alpha \mapsto \bar{\alpha}$$

is a ring isomorphism. Thus $\bar{\Delta} \cong \Delta$.
