Let $\mathfrak{B} \neq 0$ be a two-sided ideal in the endomorphism ring $\mathfrak{L}$ of a finite dimensional vector space $\mathfrak{R}$ over a division ring $\Delta$. Then for any one-dimensional subspace $[x]$ of $\mathfrak{R}$, the ideal $\mathfrak{B}$ contains a projection $E$ of $\mathfrak{R}$ onto $[x]$.
