Show that, if $A$ is a linear transformation with matrix

$$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 & \alpha_{13} & \cdots & \alpha_{1n} \\
0 & 0 & 1 & \alpha_{24} & \cdots & \alpha_{2n} \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\
0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 1 \\
0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 0
\end{bmatrix},$$

then $A$ is cyclic. Use this to prove that the given matrix is similar to the matrix with $1$'s on the superdiagonal and zeros elsewhere.
