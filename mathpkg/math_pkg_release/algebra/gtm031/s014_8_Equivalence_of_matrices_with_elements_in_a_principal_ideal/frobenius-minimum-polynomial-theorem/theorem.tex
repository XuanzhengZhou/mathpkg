Let $\lambda I - (\alpha)$ have invariant factors $\delta_1(\lambda), \delta_2(\lambda), \ldots, \delta_n(\lambda)$ normalized to be monic, with $\delta_1 \mid \delta_2 \mid \cdots \mid \delta_n$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\delta_n(\lambda) = \mu_{(\alpha)}(\lambda)$, the minimum polynomial of $(\alpha)$,
\item $\delta_1(\lambda) \delta_2(\lambda) \cdots \delta_n(\lambda) = \chi_{(\alpha)}(\lambda)$, the characteristic polynomial.
\end{enumerate}
