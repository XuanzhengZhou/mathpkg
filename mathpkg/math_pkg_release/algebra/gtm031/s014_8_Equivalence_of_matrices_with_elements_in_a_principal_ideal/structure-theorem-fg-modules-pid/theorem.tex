Let $\mathfrak{o}$ be a principal ideal domain. Every finitely generated $\mathfrak{o}$-module $\mathfrak{R}$ is a direct sum of cyclic submodules:
$$\mathfrak{R} = \{f_1\} \oplus \{f_2\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_t\}$$
where the order ideals satisfy $\mathfrak{J}_{f_j} = (\delta_j)$ for $j = 1, \ldots, u$ (the torsion part), $\mathfrak{J}_{f_k} = (0)$ for $k = u+1, \ldots, t$ (the free part), and the divisibility condition $\delta_1 \mid \delta_2 \mid \cdots \mid \delta_u$ holds.
