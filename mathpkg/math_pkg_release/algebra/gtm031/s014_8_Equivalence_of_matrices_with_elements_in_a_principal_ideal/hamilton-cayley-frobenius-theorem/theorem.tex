Let $A$ be a linear transformation of a finite-dimensional vector space over a field $\Phi$, with minimum polynomial $\mu(\lambda)$ and characteristic polynomial $\chi(\lambda)$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mu(\lambda) \mid \chi(\lambda)$ (the minimum polynomial divides the characteristic polynomial),
\item $\mu(\lambda)$ and $\chi(\lambda)$ have the same irreducible factors (over $\Phi$),
\item $\chi(\lambda)$ is the product of the invariant factors of $\lambda I - A$.
\end{enumerate}
