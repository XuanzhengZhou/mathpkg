Let $\mathfrak{R} = \{f_1\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_t\} = \{f_1'\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_{t'}'\}$ be two decompositions of a finitely generated $\mathfrak{o}$-module ($\mathfrak{o}$ a PID) into non-zero cyclic submodules, with order ideals satisfying the divisibility conditions:
$$\mathfrak{J}_{f_1} \supseteq \mathfrak{J}_{f_2} \supseteq \cdots \supseteq \mathfrak{J}_{f_t}, \qquad \mathfrak{J}_{f_1'} \supseteq \mathfrak{J}_{f_2'} \supseteq \cdots \supseteq \mathfrak{J}_{f_{t'}'}.$$
Then $t = t'$ and $\mathfrak{J}_{f_i} = \mathfrak{J}_{f_i'}$ for $i = 1, 2, \ldots, t$.
