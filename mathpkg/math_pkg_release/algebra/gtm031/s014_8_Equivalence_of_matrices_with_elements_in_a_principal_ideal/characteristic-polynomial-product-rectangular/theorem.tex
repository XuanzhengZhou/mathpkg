For a linear transformation $A$ of an $n$-dimensional vector space over a field $\Phi$, let $\delta_1(\lambda), \delta_2(\lambda), \ldots, \delta_n(\lambda)$ be the invariant factors (normalized to be monic) of the characteristic matrix $\lambda I - (\alpha)$, where $(\alpha)$ is any matrix of $A$. Then the characteristic polynomial of $A$ is
$$\chi_A(\lambda) = \det(\lambda I - A) = \delta_1(\lambda) \delta_2(\lambda) \cdots \delta_n(\lambda).$$
