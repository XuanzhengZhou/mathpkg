Exercise 3 from Section 2 of Chapter VIII. This exercise asks the student to identify two natural subrings of $\mathfrak{M}$ (the ring of finite sums of Kronecker products): the elements of the form $1' \times B$ form a subring isomorphic to the endomorphism ring of $\mathfrak{S}$, while elements $A' \times 1$ form a subring isomorphic to the endomorphism ring of $\mathfrak{R}'$. This illustrates how the Kronecker product construction embeds the individual endomorphism rings as subrings.
