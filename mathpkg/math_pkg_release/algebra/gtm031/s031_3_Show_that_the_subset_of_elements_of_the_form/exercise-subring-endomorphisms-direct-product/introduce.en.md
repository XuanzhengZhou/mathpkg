Exercise 2 from Section 2 of Chapter VIII. The student is asked to prove that the set of all finite sums of Kronecker products $A_i' \times B_i$ of linear transformations forms a subring of the full endomorphism ring of the direct product space $\mathfrak{P} = \mathfrak{R}' \times \mathfrak{S}$. This exercise reinforces the relationship between the Kronecker product construction and the ring structure of endomorphisms.
