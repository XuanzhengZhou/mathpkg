The incident subspace $j(\mathfrak{S})$ is the annihilator of $\mathfrak{S}$ in the conjugate space. This construction sets up a Galois connection between the lattice of subspaces of $\mathfrak{R}$ and the lattice of subspaces of $\mathfrak{R}^*$. The correspondence $\mathfrak{S} \mapsto j(\mathfrak{S})$ is order-reversing: if $\mathfrak{S}_1 \supseteq \mathfrak{S}_2$, then $j(\mathfrak{S}_1) \subseteq j(\mathfrak{S}_2)$.
