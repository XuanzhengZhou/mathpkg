Find all the solutions of the following system over $\Delta$, the field of rational numbers:
$$
\begin{align*}
2\xi_1 - \xi_2 + \xi_3 - 3\xi_4 &= 0 \\
\xi_1 + \xi_2 - \xi_3 + 2\xi_4 &= 0 \\
4\xi_1 + \xi_2 - \xi_3 + \xi_4 &= 0.
\end{align*}
$$
