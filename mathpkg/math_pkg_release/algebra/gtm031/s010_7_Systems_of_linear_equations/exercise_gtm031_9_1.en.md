Prove that, if $\mathfrak{S} \to \bar{\mathfrak{S}}$ is a $1$-$1$ order-reversing correspondence of one lattice onto a second one, then $\bar{\mathfrak{S}}_1 + \bar{\mathfrak{S}}_2 = \overline{\mathfrak{S}_1 \cap \mathfrak{S}_2}$ and $\bar{\mathfrak{S}}_1 \cap \bar{\mathfrak{S}}_2 = \overline{\mathfrak{S}_1 + \mathfrak{S}_2}$.
