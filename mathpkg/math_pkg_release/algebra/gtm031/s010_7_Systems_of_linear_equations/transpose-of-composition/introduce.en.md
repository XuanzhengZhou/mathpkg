This proposition gives the fundamental contravariance property of the transpose operation: the transpose of a composition is the composition of the transposes in reverse order. This is analogous to the matrix identity $(AC)^T = C^T A^T$ and establishes that the assignment $A \mapsto A^*$ is a contravariant functor from the category of vector spaces to itself.
