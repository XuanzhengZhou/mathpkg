The mapping $\mathfrak{S} \mapsto j(\mathfrak{S})$ is a $1$-$1$ order-reversing correspondence between the lattice $L(\mathfrak{R})$ of subspaces of $\mathfrak{R}$ and the lattice $L(\mathfrak{R}^*)$ of subspaces of $\mathfrak{R}^*$. Moreover, $j(j(\mathfrak{S})) = \mathfrak{S}$ for every subspace $\mathfrak{S}$, and if $\mathfrak{S}^*$ is any subspace of $\mathfrak{R}^*$, then $\mathfrak{S}^* = j(\mathfrak{S})$ where $\mathfrak{S} = j(\mathfrak{S}^*)$.
