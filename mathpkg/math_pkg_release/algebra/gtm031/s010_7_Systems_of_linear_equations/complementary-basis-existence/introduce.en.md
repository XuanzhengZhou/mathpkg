This theorem establishes the symmetry of the duality between $\mathfrak{R}$ and $\mathfrak{R}^*$: any basis of the conjugate space arises as the dual basis of some basis of the original space. This is a key step in proving that the natural map $x \mapsto x(f)$ from $\mathfrak{R}$ into $\mathfrak{R}^{**}$ is an isomorphism.
