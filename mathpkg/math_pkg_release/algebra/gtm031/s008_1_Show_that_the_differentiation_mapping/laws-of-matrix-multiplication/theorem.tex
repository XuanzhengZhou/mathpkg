For matrices of compatible sizes over a division ring $\Delta$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item (Associativity) $(\alpha)[(\beta)(\gamma)] = [(\alpha)(\beta)](\gamma)$.
\item (Distributivity) $[(\alpha) + (\beta)](\gamma) = (\alpha)(\gamma) + (\beta)(\gamma)$ and $(\alpha)[(\beta) + (\gamma)] = (\alpha)(\beta) + (\alpha)(\gamma)$.
\end{enumerate}
These follow from the corresponding properties of linear transformations via the matrix-linear transformation correspondence.
