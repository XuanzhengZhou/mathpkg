The scalar multiplication by elements of the center field $\Phi$ commutes with composition of linear transformations in all possible ways: $\gamma(AB) = (\gamma A)B = A(\gamma B)$. This relation is the essential compatibility condition that makes $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}, \mathfrak{R})$ into an algebra over $\Phi$, connecting the scalar multiplication with the ring multiplication.
