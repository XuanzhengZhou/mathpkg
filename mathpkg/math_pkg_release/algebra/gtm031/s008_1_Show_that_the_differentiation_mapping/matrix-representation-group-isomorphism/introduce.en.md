The bijection between linear transformations and matrices is not merely set-theoretic; it is a group isomorphism. Addition of linear transformations corresponds exactly to entrywise addition of their representing matrices. This means the group structure of $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_2)$ under addition is faithfully captured by matrix addition.
