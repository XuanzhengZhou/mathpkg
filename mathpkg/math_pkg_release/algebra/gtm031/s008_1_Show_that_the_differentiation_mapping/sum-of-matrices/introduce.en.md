Matrix addition is defined entrywise: the sum of two matrices of the same dimensions is obtained by adding corresponding entries. This definition is designed to be compatible with addition of linear transformations: if matrices $(\alpha)$ and $(\beta)$ represent linear transformations $A$ and $B$, then $(\alpha) + (\beta)$ represents $A + B$.
