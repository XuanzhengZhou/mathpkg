Verify that, if $C \in \mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_2, \mathfrak{R}_2)$ and $X \in \mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_2)$, then $(AX)C = A(XC)$ for $A \in \mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_1)$, showing that the left and right module structures on $\mathfrak{L}(\mathfrak{R}_1, \mathfrak{R}_2)$ are compatible.
