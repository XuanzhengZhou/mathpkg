The image of a subspace under a linear transformation $A: \mathfrak{R}_1 \to \mathfrak{R}_2$ is the set of all vectors $x_1 A$ where $x_1$ ranges over the subspace. This image is itself a subspace of the codomain $\mathfrak{R}_2$. When the subspace equals the entire domain $\mathfrak{R}_1$, the resulting image $\mathfrak{R}_1 A$ is called the rank space of $A$, and its dimension is the rank of the linear transformation.
