Let $\mu(\lambda)$ be the minimum polynomial of the linear transformation $A$ in the vector space $\Re$ over the field $\Phi$, and let
$$
\mu(\lambda) = \pi_1(\lambda)^{k_1} \pi_2(\lambda)^{k_2} \cdots \pi_s(\lambda)^{k_s}
$$
be the factorization of $\mu(\lambda)$ into powers of distinct irreducible polynomials $\pi_i(\lambda)$ with leading coefficient $1$. Define the subspaces
$$
\Re_i = \{\, x_i \in \Re \mid x_i \pi_i(A)^{k_i} = 0 \,\}, \qquad i = 1, 2, \dots, s.
$$
Then
$$
\Re = \Re_1 \oplus \Re_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus \Re_s,
$$
each $\Re_i$ is invariant under $A$, and the minimum polynomial of the induced transformation $A|_{\Re_i}$ is $\pi_i(\lambda)^{k_i}$.
