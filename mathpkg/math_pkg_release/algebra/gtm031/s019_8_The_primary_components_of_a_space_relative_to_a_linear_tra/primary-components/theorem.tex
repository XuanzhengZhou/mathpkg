Let $A$ be a linear transformation in a vector space $\Re$ over a field $\Phi$, and let $\mu(\lambda)$ be its minimum polynomial with factorization
$$
\mu(\lambda) = \pi_1(\lambda)^{k_1} \pi_2(\lambda)^{k_2} \cdots \pi_s(\lambda)^{k_s}
$$
into powers of distinct irreducible polynomials $\pi_i(\lambda)$ having leading coefficients equal to $1$. The \textbf{primary components} of $\Re$ relative to $A$ are the subspaces $\Re_i$ defined by
$$
\Re_i = \{\, x_i \in \Re \mid x_i \pi_i(A)^{k_i} = 0 \,\}, \qquad i = 1, 2, \dots, s.
$$
