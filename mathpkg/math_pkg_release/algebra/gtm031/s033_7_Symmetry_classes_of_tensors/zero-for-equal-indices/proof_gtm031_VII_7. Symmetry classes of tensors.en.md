By equation (26), interchanging any two indices $i_p$ and $i_q$ changes the sign of $\xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r}$:
$$\xi^{\cdots i_q \cdots i_p \cdots} = -\xi^{\cdots i_p \cdots i_q \cdots}.$$
If $i_p = i_q$, then the component remains the same under the transposition, so $\xi^{\cdots i_p \cdots i_p \cdots} = -\xi^{\cdots i_p \cdots i_p \cdots}$, hence $\xi^{i_1 i_2 \cdots i_r} = 0$.
