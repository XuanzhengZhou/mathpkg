By definition, $C_r(\alpha)$ is the matrix of the linear transformation $A_0^r$ restricted to the space of skew-symmetric tensors, relative to the lexicographically ordered basis $[e_{i_1} \cdots e_{i_r}]$, $i_1 < \cdots < i_r$. Since $(AB)_0^r = A_0^r B_0^r$ by equation (24), the matrix of the composition is the product of the matrices, giving $C_r((\alpha)(\beta)) = C_r(\alpha) C_r(\beta)$.
