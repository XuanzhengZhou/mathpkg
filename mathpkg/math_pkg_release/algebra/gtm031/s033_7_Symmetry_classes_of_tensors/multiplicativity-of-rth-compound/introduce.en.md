The $r$th compound operation $C_r$ is multiplicative: the $r$th compound of a product equals the product of the $r$th compounds. This follows from the fact that $C_r(\alpha)$ represents the induced transformation and the induced transformation itself is multiplicative: $(AB)_0^r = A_0^r B_0^r$. This property is the matrix-level expression of the Cauchy--Binet formula.
