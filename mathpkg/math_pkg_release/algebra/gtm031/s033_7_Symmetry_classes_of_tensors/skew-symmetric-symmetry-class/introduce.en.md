The skew-symmetric (or alternating) symmetry class is determined by the set of operators $\{P(\sigma) - \epsilon_\sigma \mid \sigma \in \mathfrak{S}_r\}$, where $\epsilon_\sigma$ is the sign of the permutation $\sigma$. A tensor in this class transforms by the sign of the permutation under any reordering of its factors. This class is fundamental to the theory of determinants and exterior algebra.
