This theorem gives the explicit formula for the entries of the $r$th compound matrix $C_r(\alpha)$: each entry is an $r \times r$ minor (determinant) of the original matrix $\alpha$, with rows indexed by the ordered $r$-tuple $I = (i_1 < \cdots < i_r)$ and columns indexed by $J = (j_1 < \cdots < j_r)$. This result connects the induced transformation on skew-symmetric tensors with determinants and is related to the Cauchy--Binet formula.
