Given a linear transformation $A$ on the base vector space $\Re$, the induced transformation $A_0^r$ on the space of contravariant tensors of rank $r$ acts componentwise as the $r$-fold Kronecker product $A \times A \times \cdots \times A$. This induced map is fundamental for studying how linear transformations on the base space lift to tensor spaces, and it respects composition and inverses.
