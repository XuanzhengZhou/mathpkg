If $A$ is a linear transformation in the base space $\Re$, the \textit{induced} linear transformation $A_0^r \equiv A \times A \times \cdots \times A$ (with $r$ factors) on $\Re_0^r$ is defined by
$$(\Sigma x_1 \times x_2 \times \cdots \times x_r) A_0^r = \Sigma x_1 A \times x_2 A \times \cdots \times x_r A.$$
