From the definition of the induced transformation, for any decomposable tensor $x_1 \times \cdots \times x_r$,
$$(x_1 \times \cdots \times x_r)(AB)_0^r = x_1(AB) \times \cdots \times x_r(AB).$$
On the other hand,
$$(x_1 \times \cdots \times x_r) A_0^r B_0^r = (x_1 A \times \cdots \times x_r A) B_0^r = x_1 A B \times \cdots \times x_r A B.$$
Since the action on decomposable tensors determines the action on all tensors (by linear extension), we obtain $(AB)_0^r = A_0^r B_0^r$.
