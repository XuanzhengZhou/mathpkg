Since $1_0^r = 1$ (the identity on tensors), the multiplicativity property $(AB)_0^r = A_0^r B_0^r$ implies that the induced transformation of the inverse is the inverse of the induced transformation. In particular, non-singularity of $A$ lifts to non-singularity of $A_0^r$.
