Given a composition series of invariant subspaces for a set $\Omega$ of linear transformations, choosing a basis adapted to the filtration yields a canonical block upper-triangular matrix form for every $A \in \Omega$. Each diagonal block $(\beta_i)$ is the matrix of the linear transformation induced by $A$ on the factor space $\mathfrak{S}_i/\mathfrak{S}_{i-1}$. The irreducibility condition means that no simultaneous similarity transformation can further reduce all the diagonal blocks $(\beta_i)$ to a common nontrivial block structure.
