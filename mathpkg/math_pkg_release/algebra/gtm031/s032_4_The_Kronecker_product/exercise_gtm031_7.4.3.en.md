Let $\mathfrak{F}$ be as in Exercise 2. Assume the characteristic of $\Phi \neq 2$ and let $\mathfrak{C}$ be the ideal in $\mathfrak{F}$ generated by the vectors $x \times y + y \times x$, $x, y$ in $\Re$. Then $\mathfrak{F}/\mathfrak{C}$ is called the Grassmann (or exterior) algebra based on $\Re$. Show that $\dim \mathfrak{F}/\mathfrak{C} = 2^n$.
