Let $\Re$ be a vector space over a division ring $\Delta$, let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a subspace invariant under a linear transformation $A$, and let $\bar{\Re} = \Re / \mathfrak{S}$. Define the natural mapping $P: \Re \to \bar{\Re}$ by $P(x) = x + \mathfrak{S}$ and the induced transformation $\bar{A}$ on $\bar{\Re}$ by $\bar{x}\bar{A} = \overline{xA}$.

Then the following relation holds:

$$P \bar{A} = A P.$$

Explicitly, for every $x \in \Re$,

$$(P\bar{A})(x) = \bar{x}\bar{A} = \overline{xA} = (AP)(x).$$
