Let $\Re$ be a finite dimensional vector space over a division ring $\Delta$ and let $\Omega$ be a set of linear transformations in $\Re$ over $\Delta$. A subspace $\mathfrak{S}$ of $\Re$ is called **invariant** under $\Omega$ if

$$\mathfrak{S} A \subseteq \mathfrak{S} \quad \text{for every } A \in \Omega.$$
