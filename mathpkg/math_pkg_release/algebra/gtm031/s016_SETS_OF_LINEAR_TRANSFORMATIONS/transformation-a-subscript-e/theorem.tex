Let $\Re$ be a vector space over a division ring $\Delta$, let $\mathfrak{S}$ be a subspace invariant under a linear transformation $A$, and let $\bar{\Re} = \Re / \mathfrak{S}$. Let $E$ be a projection of $\Re$ onto $\mathfrak{S}$ (determined by a decomposition $\Re = \mathfrak{S} \oplus U$).

Define the linear transformation $A_E : \bar{\Re} \to \mathfrak{S}$ by

$$\bar{x} A_E = x(AE - EA), \quad x \in \Re.$$

This mapping is single-valued (well-defined) and is a linear transformation from the quotient space $\bar{\Re}$ into the invariant subspace $\mathfrak{S}$. Moreover, the relation

$$AE - EA = P A_E$$

holds, where $P$ is the natural mapping of $\Re$ onto $\bar{\Re}$.
