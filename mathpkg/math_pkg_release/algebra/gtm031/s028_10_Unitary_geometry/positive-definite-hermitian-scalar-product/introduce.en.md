A positive definite hermitian scalar product is the complex analogue of a real inner product. It is a hermitian scalar product that satisfies the positivity condition $(x, x) > 0$ for all nonzero vectors $x$, with $(x, x)$ always real. This structure is the foundation of unitary geometry, just as the Euclidean inner product is the foundation of Euclidean geometry.
