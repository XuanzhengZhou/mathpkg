The unitary group $U(\Phi, n)$ is the subgroup of the general linear group $L(\Phi, n)$ consisting of all $n \times n$ unitary matrices over the complex field $\Phi$. It is the complex analogue of the orthogonal group. The Schmidt orthogonalization process holds in unitary spaces, which allows many results from Euclidean geometry to be carried over.
