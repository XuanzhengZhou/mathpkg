**Proof sketch.** The proofs of commutativity and associativity for Kronecker products in the finite-dimensional case rely only on the bilinearity of the defining maps and the universal property, neither of which depends on finite dimension. The construction via duality given above provides the necessary framework for arbitrary spaces. Commutativity follows from the symmetry of the bilinear form $g$ in the definition of elementary tensors. Associativity follows from the associativity of composition of the corresponding linear transformations.
