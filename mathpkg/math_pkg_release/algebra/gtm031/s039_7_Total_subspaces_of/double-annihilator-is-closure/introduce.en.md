Theorem 4 establishes that the double annihilator operation j(j(-)) coincides with topological closure in the R-topology. A subspace S' of R' is closed in the R-topology precisely when it equals its double annihilator. This is the infinite-dimensional analog of the finite-dimensional theorem that the double orthogonal complement recovers the original subspace.
