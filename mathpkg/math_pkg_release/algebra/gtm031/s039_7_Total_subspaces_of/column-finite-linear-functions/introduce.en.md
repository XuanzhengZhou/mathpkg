The column-finite linear functions form the subspace R_0^* of the conjugate space R^* consisting of those linear functions whose matrices relative to a given basis are column-finite. This subspace is spanned by the canonical dual basis elements e_i^* defined by e_i^*(e_k) = delta_{ik}, and has the same dimension as the original space R.
