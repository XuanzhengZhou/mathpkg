This technical lemma states that if the rank of A does not exceed the rank of B, then A can be factored as PBQ for suitable transformations P and Q. This is the key tool for classifying two-sided ideals: if an ideal contains a transformation of a given rank, it must contain all transformations of equal or smaller rank.
