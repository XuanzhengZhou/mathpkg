**Proof.** Choose bases for $\operatorname{Im} A$ and $\operatorname{Im} B$. Since $\rho(A) \leq \rho(B)$, there exists a surjective linear map from $\operatorname{Im} B$ to $\operatorname{Im} A$. Compose with the natural projection onto $\operatorname{Im} B$ and inclusion from $\operatorname{Im} A$ to obtain the factorization $A = PBQ$. The explicit construction uses the basis representations of A and B.
