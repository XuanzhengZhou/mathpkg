\textbf{Definition.} Let $\mathfrak{R}$ and $\mathfrak{R}'$ be dual relative to $g(x, y')$. If $\mathfrak{S}$ is a subspace of $\mathfrak{R}$, we denote by $j(\mathfrak{S})$ the subspace of $\mathfrak{R}'$ of vectors $y'$ which are incident to every $x \in \mathfrak{S}$ in the sense that $g(x, y') = 0$. Similarly, with every subspace $\mathfrak{S}'$ of $\mathfrak{R}'$ we associate a subspace $j(\mathfrak{S}')$ of $\mathfrak{R}$.
