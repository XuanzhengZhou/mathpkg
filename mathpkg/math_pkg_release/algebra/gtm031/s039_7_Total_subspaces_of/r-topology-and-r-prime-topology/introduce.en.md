Given dual vector spaces R and R' with bilinear form g, the R-topology on R' is defined by transferring the finite topology from the conjugate space via the natural identification. Basic open sets are determined by prescribing values g(x_i, y') = beta_i for finitely many vectors x_i in R. The R'-topology on R is defined symmetrically.
