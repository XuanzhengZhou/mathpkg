**Proof.** For any $x \in \mathfrak{R}$, $(A+B)x = Ax + Bx$, so $\operatorname{Im}(A+B) \subseteq \operatorname{Im} A + \operatorname{Im} B$. Therefore $\dim \operatorname{Im}(A+B) \leq \dim(\operatorname{Im} A + \operatorname{Im} B) \leq \dim \operatorname{Im} A + \dim \operatorname{Im} B$.
