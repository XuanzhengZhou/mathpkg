The matrix of the transpose of a linear transformation is the transpose of the matrix of the original transformation. More precisely, if $A$ has matrix $(\alpha)$ relative to chosen bases, then the transpose $A^*$ has matrix $(\alpha)'$ (the transposed matrix) relative to the complementary dual bases. This is the fundamental reason why the transpose operation on matrices corresponds to the dual transformation on vector spaces.
