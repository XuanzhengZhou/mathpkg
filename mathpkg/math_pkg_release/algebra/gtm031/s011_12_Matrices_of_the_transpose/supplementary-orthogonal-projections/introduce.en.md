A supplementary set of orthogonal projections is a collection of idempotent linear transformations $E_i$ that are pairwise orthogonal ($E_i E_j = 0$ for $i \neq j$) and sum to the identity. This theorem establishes a one-to-one correspondence between direct sum decompositions of a vector space and finite supplementary sets of orthogonal projections. Every such set of projections comes from a unique direct sum decomposition, and vice versa.
