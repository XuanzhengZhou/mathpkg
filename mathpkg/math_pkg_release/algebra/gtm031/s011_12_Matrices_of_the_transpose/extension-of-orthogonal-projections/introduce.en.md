Any finite set of pairwise orthogonal projections can be completed to a supplementary set by adding the complement projection $E_{r+1} = 1 - \sum E_i$. This shows that orthogonal projections that do not necessarily sum to the identity still fit into the framework of direct sum decompositions, with the complementary projection corresponding to the remaining subspace.
