It is sufficient to prove the first of these statements. Hence let $z$ be a vector such that $zA = 0$. Then $f(zA) = 0$ and $(fA^*)(z) = 0$ for all $f$. Hence $z \in j(\mathfrak{R}_2^* A^*)$. The converse follows by retracing the steps.
