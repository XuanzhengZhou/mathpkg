For a direct sum decomposition $\mathfrak{R} = \mathfrak{R}_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathfrak{R}_r$, each vector $x$ can be written as $x = xE_1 + xE_2 + \cdots + xE_r$, where $E_i$ is the projection onto $\mathfrak{R}_i$. This is equivalent to the identity $E_1 + E_2 + \cdots + E_r = 1$, showing that the projections form a partition of the identity transformation.
