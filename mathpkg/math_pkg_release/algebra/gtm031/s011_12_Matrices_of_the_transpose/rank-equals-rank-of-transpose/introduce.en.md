A fundamental result in linear algebra: a linear transformation $A$ and its transpose $A^*$ always have the same rank. This follows from Theorem 11 which relates the null space of one to the rank space of the other, combined with the dimension formula. The equality holds for linear transformations between finite-dimensional vector spaces over any division ring.
