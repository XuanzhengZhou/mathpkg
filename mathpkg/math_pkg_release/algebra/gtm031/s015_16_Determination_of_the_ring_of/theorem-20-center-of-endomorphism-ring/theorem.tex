Let $\mathfrak{R}$ be a finitely generated $\mathfrak{o}$-module, $\mathfrak{o}$ a principal ideal domain. Let $\mathfrak{B}$ be the ring of $\mathfrak{o}$-endomorphisms of $\mathfrak{R}$ and let $\mathfrak{C}$ be the center of $\mathfrak{B}$. Let $\mathfrak{o}_l$ be the ring of scalar multiplications $x \mapsto \alpha x$, $\alpha \in \mathfrak{o}$. Then
$$\mathfrak{C} = \mathfrak{o}_l.$$
That is, the center of the ring of $\mathfrak{o}$-endomorphisms of $\mathfrak{R}$ consists precisely of the scalar multiplications.
