The assignment sending each matrix $(\beta)$ in the subring $\mathfrak{M}$ to the endomorphism $B$ defined by $f_i B = \sum_j \beta_{ij} f_j$ is a surjective ring homomorphism onto the endomorphism ring $\mathfrak{B}$. This maps matrix addition to endomorphism addition and matrix multiplication to composition, reflecting the fact that the action on generators determines the entire endomorphism.
