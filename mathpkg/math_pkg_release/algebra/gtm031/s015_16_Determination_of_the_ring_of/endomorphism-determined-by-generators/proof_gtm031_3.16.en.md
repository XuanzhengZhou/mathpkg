Let $\mathfrak{R} = \{f_1\} \oplus \{f_2\} \oplus \cdots \oplus \{f_t\}$ be the decomposition into cyclic submodules. Any $x \in \mathfrak{R}$ can be written uniquely (in terms of the direct sum decomposition) as $x = \sum_{i=1}^{t} \xi_i f_i$ with $\xi_i \in \mathfrak{o}$. Then using the $\mathfrak{o}$-linearity of $B$:

$$x B = \left(\sum_{i=1}^{t} \xi_i f_i\right) B = \sum_{i=1}^{t} (\xi_i f_i) B = \sum_{i=1}^{t} \xi_i (f_i B) = \sum_{i=1}^{t} \xi_i g_i.$$

Since the representation $x = \sum \xi_i f_i$ is determined by $x$ and the decomposition of $\mathfrak{R}$, the image $x B$ is completely determined once the images $g_i = f_i B$ are specified.
