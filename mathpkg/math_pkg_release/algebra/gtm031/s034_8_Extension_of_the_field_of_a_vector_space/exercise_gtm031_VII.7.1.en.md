Show that

$$[x_1 x_2 \cdots x_n] \equiv \sum_{\sigma} \epsilon_\sigma x_1' x_2' \cdots x_n' = |\xi_{11}\xi_{22} \cdots \xi_{nn}| \, [e_1 e_2 \cdots e_n]$$

where $|\xi_{11} \cdots \xi_{nn}|$ is the determinant of the coordinates of the $x$'s relative to the $e$'s, and $\epsilon_\sigma$ denotes the sign of the permutation $\sigma$.
