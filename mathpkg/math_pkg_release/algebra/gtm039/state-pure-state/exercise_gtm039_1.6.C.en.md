Let $\mathcal{A}$ be the $C^*$-algebra $C^*(S)$ generated by the unilateral shift.

a. Find all irreducible representations of $\mathcal{A}$ (up to unitary equivalence).
b. Let $\rho$ be a pure state of $\mathcal{A}$ which does not vanish on $\mathcal{C}(\mathcal{H})$. Show that there is a unit vector $\xi$ in $\mathcal{H}$ such that $\rho(T) = (T\xi, \xi), T \in \mathcal{A}$.
c. Find all the remaining pure states of $\mathcal{A}$.
