Let $A$ be the algebra of all polynomials in a single real variable having complex coefficients. Give $A$ the norm
$$\|f\| = \sup_{0 \leqslant t \leqslant 1} |f(t)|$$
and the involution $f^*(t) = \overline{f(t)}$.

a. Show that $A$ has all the properties of a commutative $C^*$-algebra with identity except completeness.
b. Show that $\rho(f) = f(2)$ defines a linear functional on $A$ which satisfies $\rho(f^*f) \geqslant 0$ but is not continuous.
