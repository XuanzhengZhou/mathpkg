Let $\pi, \sigma$ be two representations of a $C^*$-algebra $A$ having respective cyclic vectors $\xi, \eta$. Assuming that $(\pi(x)\xi, \xi) = (\sigma(x)\eta, \eta)$ for each $x \in A$, show that $\pi$ and $\sigma$ are equivalent.
