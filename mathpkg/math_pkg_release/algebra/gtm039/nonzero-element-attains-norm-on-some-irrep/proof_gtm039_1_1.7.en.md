Take $x = z^*z$ in 1.7.2 to obtain a pure state $f$ with $f(z^*z) = \|z^*z\| = \|z\|^2$. Let $(\pi, \mathcal{H}, \xi)$ be the GNS representation of $f$. Then $\|\pi(z)\xi\|^2 = f(z^*z) = \|z\|^2 > 0$. By 1.6.6, $\pi$ is irreducible. $\square$
