(Exercise on the weak and strong operator topologies.) Let $H$ be a Hilbert space and let $f$ be a linear functional on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ which is continuous in the strong operator topology.

a. Show that there exist vectors $\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_n \in H$ such that
$$|f(T)| \leqslant \left( \|T\xi_1\|^2 + \cdots + \|T\xi_n\|^2 \right)^{1/2}, \quad T \in \mathcal{L}(H).$$

b. With $\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_n$ as in part a, show that there exist vectors $\eta_1, \ldots, \eta_n$ in $H$ such that $f$ has a representation
$$f(T) = (T\xi_1, \eta_1) + \cdots + (T\xi_n, \eta_n),$$
$T \in \mathcal{L}(H)$.

c. Deduce that the weak and strong operator topologies have the same continuous linear functionals and the same closed convex sets.
