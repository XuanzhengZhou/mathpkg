(Exercise on the polar decomposition.) Let $T \in \mathcal{L}(\mathcal{H})$ have the polar decomposition $T = U|T|$.

a. Show that if $R$ is a positive operator on $\mathcal{H}$ and $V$ is a partial isometry whose initial space is $[R\mathcal{H}]$, and which satisfy $T = VR$, then $V = U$ and $R = |T|$.

b. Show that $U$ maps the subspace $(\ker T)^\perp$ onto $[T\mathcal{H}]$.
