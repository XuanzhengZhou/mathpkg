An \emph{approximate unit} for a $C^*$-algebra $A$ is a net $\{e_\lambda\}$ of self-adjoint elements of $A$ satisfying: (i) $0 \leqslant e_\lambda \leqslant 1$; (ii) $\lambda \leqslant \mu \implies e_\lambda \leqslant e_\mu$; (iii) $\lim_\lambda \|x e_\lambda - x\| = \lim_\lambda \|e_\lambda x - x\| = 0$ for all $x \in A$.
