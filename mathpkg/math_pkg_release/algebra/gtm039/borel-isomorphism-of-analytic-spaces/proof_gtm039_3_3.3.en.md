Embed $X,Y$ as analytic subspaces of Polish $P,Q$. A 1-1 Borel map $f: X \to Y$ has analytic image (3.3.4). $f(X)$ and $Y \setminus f(X)$ are analytic; the separation theorem gives a Borel set $C$ with $f(X) = Y \cap C$, so $f(X)$ is Borel in $Y$. Apply 3.3.2 to get that $f$ is an isomorphism. $\square$
