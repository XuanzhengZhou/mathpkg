(Exercise on adjoining a unit.) Let $A$ be a $C^*$-algebra without unit, and for each $x$ in $A$ let $L_x$ be the linear operator on $A$ defined by $y \mapsto xy$. Let $B$ be the set of all operators on $A$ of the form $\lambda 1 + L_x$, $\lambda \in \mathbb{C}$, $x \in A$. Show that $B$ is a $C^*$-algebra with unit relative to the operator norm and the involution $(\lambda 1 + L_x)^* = \bar{\lambda} 1 + L_{x^*}$, and that $x \mapsto L_x$ is an isometric $\ast$-isomorphism of $A$ onto a closed ideal in $B$ of codimension 1.
