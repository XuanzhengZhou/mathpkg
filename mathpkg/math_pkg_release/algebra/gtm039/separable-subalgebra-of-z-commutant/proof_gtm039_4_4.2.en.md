Let $A_0$ be the countable $*$-algebra over $\mathbb{Q}+i\mathbb{Q}$ generated by a countable dense subset of $A$. For each $T \in A_0$, choose a Borel function $F_T$ with $L_{F_T} = T$. On a conull set, the algebraic relations hold pointwise. Define $\pi(T)(x) = F_T(x)$; this is a $*$-homomorphism for each $x$. Extend by continuity to all of $A$ to obtain the required isometric $*$-homomorphism $\pi: A \to B(X,\mathcal{K})$. $\square$
