Use a sequence of countable partitions $\mathcal{P}_n$ of $P$ by $G_\delta$ sets. $f$ maps atoms to disjoint sequences of analytic sets. By Corollary 3, there are disjoint Borel sets $B_{nk}$ with $f(A_{nk}) \subseteq B_{nk} \subseteq f(A_{nk})^-$. After refining, $f(P) = \bigcap_n \bigcup_k B_{nk}$, a Borel set. $\square$
