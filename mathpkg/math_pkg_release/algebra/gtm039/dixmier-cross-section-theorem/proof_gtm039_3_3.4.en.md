Reduce to $P = N^\infty$ via 3.1.4. For each $y \in Y$, $f^{-1}(\{y\})$ is closed, so it has a smallest element $h(y)$ under the dictionary well-ordering. To show $h$ is Borel: $h^{-1}(-\infty,\nu) = f(-\infty,\nu)$, which is open in $Y$ (since $f$ is open), hence Borel. Thus $h$ is a Borel cross section. $\square$
