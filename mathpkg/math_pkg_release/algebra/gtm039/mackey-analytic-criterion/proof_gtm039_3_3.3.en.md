If $X/\sim$ is countably separated, let $\{E_n\}$ separate points. The map $q(x) \mapsto (\chi_{E_1}(q(x)),\ldots)$ is a Borel embedding of $X/\sim$ into $\{0,1\}^\infty$. By 3.3.4, the image of $X$ is analytic, so $X/\sim$ is analytic. $\square$
