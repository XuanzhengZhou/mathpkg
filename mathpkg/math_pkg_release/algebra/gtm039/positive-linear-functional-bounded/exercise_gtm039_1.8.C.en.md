Let $J$ be an ideal in a $C^*$-algebra $A$, let $\{e_\lambda\}$ be an approximate unit for $J$, and let $x \mapsto \dot{x}$ be the projection of $A$ on $A/J$. Prove that, for each element $x$ in $A$,
$$\lim_{\lambda} \|x - xe_\lambda\| = \|\dot{x}\|.$$
