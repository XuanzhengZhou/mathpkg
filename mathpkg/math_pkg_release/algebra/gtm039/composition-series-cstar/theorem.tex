A \emph{composition series} in a $C^*$-algebra $A$ is a family of ideals $\{J_\alpha : 0 \leqslant \alpha \leqslant \alpha_0\}$ indexed by ordinals, satisfying: (i) $J_\alpha \subsetneq J_{\alpha+1}$ for $\alpha < \alpha_0$; (ii) $J_0 = 0$, $J_{\alpha_0} = A$; (iii) if $\beta$ is a limit ordinal, $J_\beta$ is the norm closure of $\bigcup_{\alpha < \beta} J_\alpha$.
