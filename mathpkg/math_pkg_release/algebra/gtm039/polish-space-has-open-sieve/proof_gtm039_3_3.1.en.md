By separability, choose a countable dense set $\{x_i\}$. Let $A_i$ be open balls of radius $1/2$ around $x_i$. Their closures are contained in $P$ and their union is $P$. Inductively, for each $A_{n_1\ldots n_k}$, choose open balls of radius $1/2^{k+1}$ whose closures lie in $A_{n_1\ldots n_k}$ and whose union is $A_{n_1\ldots n_k}$. This produces the required open sieve. $\square$
