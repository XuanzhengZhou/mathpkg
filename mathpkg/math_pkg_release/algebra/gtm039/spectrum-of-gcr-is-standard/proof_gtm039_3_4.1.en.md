By 4.1.3, the quotient map $\text{irr}(A,\mathcal{H}_n) \to \hat{A}_n$ has a Borel cross section. By 3.4.2, $\hat{A}_n$ is standard. Since $\hat{A} = \bigcup_n \hat{A}_n$ is a countable direct sum of standard Borel spaces, $\hat{A}$ is standard. $\square$
