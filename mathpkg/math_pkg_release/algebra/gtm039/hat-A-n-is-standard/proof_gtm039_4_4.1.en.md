Since $\text{irr}(A,\mathcal{H}_n)$ is a Polish space (the intersection of countably many $G_\delta$ conditions), and the equivalence relation is Borel, the quotient map $q: \text{irr}(A,\mathcal{H}_n) \to \hat{A}_n$ has a Borel cross section by 4.1.3. Hence by 3.4.2, $\hat{A}_n$ is a standard Borel space. $\square$
