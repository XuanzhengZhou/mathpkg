# Exercise 2.1

For a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ over $K$ and a right $\mathfrak{g}$-module $B$, define homology groups of $\mathfrak{g}$ by

$$H_n(\mathfrak{g}, B) = \operatorname{Tor}_n^{\mathfrak{g}}(B, K), \quad n \geq 0.$$

(a) Show that $H_0(\mathfrak{g}, B) = B / B\mathfrak{g}$, where $B\mathfrak{g}$ stands for the submodule of $B$ generated by $\{b \circ x : b \in B, x \in \mathfrak{g}\}$.

(b) Show that $H_1(\mathfrak{g}, B) = \ker(B \otimes_{\mathfrak{g}} I\mathfrak{g} \to B \otimes_{\mathfrak{g}} U\mathfrak{g})$.

(c) Show that for $B$ a trivial $\mathfrak{g}$-module, $H_1(\mathfrak{g}, B) \cong B \otimes_K \mathfrak{g}_{\text{ab}}$, where $\mathfrak{g}_{\text{ab}} = \mathfrak{g} / [\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g}]$.
