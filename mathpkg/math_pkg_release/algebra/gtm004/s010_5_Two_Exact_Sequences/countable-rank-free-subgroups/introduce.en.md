Lemma 6.2 provides the inductive step needed for the Stein-Serre theorem. If an abelian group $A$ has countable rank and all its finite-rank subgroups are free, then $A$ itself is free. The proof picks a maximal countable linearly independent set and considers the filtration by subgroups $A_n$ of elements linearly dependent on the first $n$ generators. Each $A_n$ has finite rank $n$, hence is free by hypothesis, and the union property of the filtration yields the freeness of $A$.
