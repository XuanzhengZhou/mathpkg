# Exercise 5.1

Consider the following commutative diagram with exact rows:

$$
\begin{array}{cccccc}
A' & \xrightarrow{\mu'} & B' & \xrightarrow{\varepsilon'} & C' & \rightarrow & 0 \\
\downarrow \alpha & & \downarrow \beta & & \downarrow \gamma & & \\
A & \xrightarrow{\mu} & B & \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} & C & \rightarrow & 0
\end{array}
$$

Show that with the terminology of Lemma 3.1 we have

$$
\operatorname{Im} \Sigma_1 \cong \operatorname{Ker} \Sigma_2 \cong \operatorname{Im} \Sigma_3 \cong \operatorname{Ker} \Sigma_4 \cong \operatorname{Im} \Sigma_5.
$$

Deduce Lemma 5.1 from Lemma 3.1.
