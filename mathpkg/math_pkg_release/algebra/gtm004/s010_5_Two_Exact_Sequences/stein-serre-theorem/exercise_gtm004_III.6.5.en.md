# Exercise 6.5

Show that the simultaneous equations $\operatorname{Ext}(A, \mathbb{Z}) = \mathbb{Q}$, $\operatorname{Hom}(A, \mathbb{Z}) = 0$ have no solution. Generalize this by replacing $\mathbb{Q}$ by an arbitrary non-zero divisible group.
