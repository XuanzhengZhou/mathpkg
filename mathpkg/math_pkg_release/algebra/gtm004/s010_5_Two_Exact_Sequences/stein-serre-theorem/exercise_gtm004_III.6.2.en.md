# Exercise 6.2

Show that $\operatorname{Ext}(\mathbb{Q}, \mathbb{Z})$ is divisible and torsionfree, and hence a $\mathbb{Q}$-vector space. (Compare Exercise 2.4.) Deduce that $\operatorname{Ext}(\mathbb{Q}, \mathbb{Z}) \cong \mathbb{R}$, $\operatorname{Hom}(\mathbb{Q}, \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}) \cong \mathbb{R}$. Compute $\operatorname{Ext}(\mathbb{R}, \mathbb{Z})$.
