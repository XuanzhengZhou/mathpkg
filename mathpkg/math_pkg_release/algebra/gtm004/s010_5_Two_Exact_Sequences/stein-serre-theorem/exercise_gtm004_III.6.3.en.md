# Exercise 6.3

Show that $\operatorname{Ext}(\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z})$ fits into exact sequences

$$
0 \rightarrow \mathbb{Z} \rightarrow \operatorname{Ext}(\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}) \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \rightarrow 0,
$$

$$
0 \rightarrow \operatorname{Ext}(\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}) \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z} \rightarrow 0.
$$
