For any left $\Lambda$-module $B$, the assignment $A \mapsto A \otimes_\Lambda B$ defines a covariant functor $- \otimes_\Lambda B : \mathfrak{M}_\Lambda^r \to \mathfrak{Ab}$. For any right $\Lambda$-module $A$, the assignment $B \mapsto A \otimes_\Lambda B$ defines a covariant functor $A \otimes_\Lambda - : {}_\Lambda\mathfrak{M} \to \mathfrak{Ab}$. Moreover, $- \otimes_\Lambda -$ is a bifunctor.
