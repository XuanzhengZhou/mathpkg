For any right $\Lambda$-module $A$, the functor $A \otimes_\Lambda - : {}_\Lambda\mathfrak{M} \to \mathfrak{Ab}$ is left adjoint to the functor $\operatorname{Hom}_\mathbb{Z}(A, -) : \mathfrak{Ab} \to {}_\Lambda\mathfrak{M}$. That is, there is a natural isomorphism
$$
\operatorname{Hom}_\mathbb{Z}(A \otimes_\Lambda B, G) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(B, \operatorname{Hom}_\mathbb{Z}(A, G))
$$
for all abelian groups $G$ and left $\Lambda$-modules $B$.
