# Exercise 7.1 (Baer's Criterion)

Prove the following proposition: The $\Lambda$-module $I$ is injective if and only if for every left ideal $J \subset \Lambda$ and for every $\Lambda$-homomorphism $f: J \to I$, there exists a $\Lambda$-homomorphism $g: \Lambda \to I$ such that $g|_J = f$.
