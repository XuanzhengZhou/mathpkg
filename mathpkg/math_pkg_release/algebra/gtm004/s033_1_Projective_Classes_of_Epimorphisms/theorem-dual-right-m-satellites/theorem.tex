Let $H : \mathfrak{A} \rightarrow \mathfrak{B}$ be a left $\mathcal{M}$-exact functor, where $\mathcal{M}$ is an injective class of monomorphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$. Then the $\mathcal{M}$-connected sequence of right $\mathcal{M}$-derived functors $\{R^{\mathcal{M}}_j H\}$ is the right $\mathcal{M}$-satellite of $H$:
$$R_j^{\mathcal{M}} H = S^{\mathcal{M}}_j H \quad (j \geq 0),$$
with $R_j^{\mathcal{M}} H = 0$ for $j < 0$ and connecting morphisms $\omega^j : S^{\mathcal{M}}_j H(A'') \to S^{\mathcal{M}}_{j+1} H(A')$.
