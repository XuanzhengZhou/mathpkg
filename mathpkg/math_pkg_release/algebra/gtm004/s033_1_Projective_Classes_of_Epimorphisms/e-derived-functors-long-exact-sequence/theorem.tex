Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a projective class of epimorphisms in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$, and let $\mathfrak{B}$ be an abelian category. Let
\[
0 \to T' \to T \to T'' \to 0
\]
be a sequence of additive functors $\mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ which is exact on $\mathcal{E}$-projective objects. Then for any object $A$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ there exist connecting homomorphisms
\[
\omega_n : L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T''(A) \to L_{n-1}^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A)
\]
such that the sequence
\[
\cdots \to L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A) \to L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T(A) \to L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T''(A) \xrightarrow{\omega_n} L_{n-1}^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A) \to \cdots
\]
\[
\cdots \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A) \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} T(A) \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} T''(A) \to 0
\]
is exact.
