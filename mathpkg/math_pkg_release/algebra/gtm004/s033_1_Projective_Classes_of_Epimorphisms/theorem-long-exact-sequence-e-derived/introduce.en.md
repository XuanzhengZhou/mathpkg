Theorem 2.3 establishes the fundamental long exact sequence for $\mathcal{E}$-derived
functors. Given a short exact sequence of additive functors that is $\mathcal{E}$-exact
on $\mathcal{E}$-projectives, one obtains natural connecting homomorphisms linking
the $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors in all degrees. This is the relative version of
the classical long exact sequence for derived functors (Theorem IV.6.1) and is the
principal tool for computing $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors in practice.
