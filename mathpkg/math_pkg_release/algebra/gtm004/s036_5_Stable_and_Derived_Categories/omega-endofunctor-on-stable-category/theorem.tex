In the stable category $\mathfrak{StM}_R$, the passage from a module $A$ to the kernel $\Omega(A)$ of a projective presentation of $A$ defines an additive endofunctor
$$
\Omega : \mathfrak{StM}_R \longrightarrow \mathfrak{StM}_R.
$$
Up to isomorphism in $\mathfrak{StM}_R$, the object $\Omega(A)$ does not depend on the chosen projective presentation of $A$, and given a morphism $\varphi: A \to A'$, the lifting of $\varphi$ produces a well-defined element $\Omega(\varphi)$ in $\underline{\mathrm{Hom}}_R(\Omega(A), \Omega(A'))$.
