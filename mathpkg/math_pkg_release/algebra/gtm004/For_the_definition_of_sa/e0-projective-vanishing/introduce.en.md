This proposition establishes that $\mathcal{E}_0$-projective functors are acyclic with respect to the left derived functor of the Kan extension, provided $\mathfrak{A}$ has enough projectives and exact coproducts. The proof uses the fact that every functor $\mathfrak{U}_d \rightarrow \mathfrak{A}$ is $\mathcal{E}_0$-projective, and reduces to checking the case $R = \tilde{K}_U A_U$ by additivity and Theorem 4.1. A projective resolution of $A_U$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ lifts via $\tilde{K}_U$ to an $\mathcal{E}_1$-projective resolution of $R$.
