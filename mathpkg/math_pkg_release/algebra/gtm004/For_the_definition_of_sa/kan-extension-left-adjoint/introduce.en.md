This theorem constructs the left Kan extension of a functor $T: \mathfrak{U} \rightarrow \mathfrak{A}$ along $J: \mathfrak{U} \rightarrow \mathfrak{B}$ as a pointwise colimit. The functor $\tilde{J}$ is the left adjoint to the restriction functor $J^*$ given by precomposition with $J$. The construction requires $\mathfrak{A}$ to admit colimits, and the value $\tilde{J}T(V)$ at an object $V \in \mathfrak{B}$ is computed as the colimit of $T$ over the comma category $\mathfrak{J}_V$ of $J$-objects over $V$.
