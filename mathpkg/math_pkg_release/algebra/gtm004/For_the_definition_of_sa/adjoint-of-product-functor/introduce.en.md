This lemma describes how adjunctions behave with respect to products of categories. Given a family of adjoint pairs $(F_i, G_i)$ between categories $\mathfrak{C}_i$ and $\mathfrak{D}$, the coproduct of the left adjoints $F\{C_i\} = \coprod_i F_i C_i$ is left adjoint to the product of the right adjoints $GD = \{G_i D\}$. The natural isomorphism follows from the universal property of coproducts and the individual adjunctions.
