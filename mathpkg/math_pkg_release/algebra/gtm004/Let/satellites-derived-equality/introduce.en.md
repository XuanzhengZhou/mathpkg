Corollary 3.4 relates satellites of the zeroth derived functor to higher derived functors. Since $L_0 H$ is right $\mathcal{E}$-exact and agrees with $H$ on $\mathcal{E}$-projectives, Theorem 3.2 applies. This provides an alternative way to define $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors as satellites of the $\mathcal{E}$-exact approximation.
