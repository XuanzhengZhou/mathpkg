Let $(B, \partial', \partial'')$ be a double complex with horizontal differential $d' = \partial'$ and vertical differential $d'' = (-1)^r \partial''$ on $B_{r,s}$. For the first spectral sequence associated with the filtration by columns:
$${}_1 E_0^{p,q} = H_{q-p}(B_{p,*}, d''), \quad {}_1 E_1^{p,q} = H_p(H_{q-p}(B, d''), d').$$
For the second spectral sequence associated with the filtration by rows:
$${}_2 E_0^{p,q} = H_{q-p}(B_{*,p}, d'), \quad {}_2 E_1^{p,q} = H_p(H_{q-p}(B, d'), d'').$$
