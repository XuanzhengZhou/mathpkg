Theorem 3.2 establishes that the $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors constructed via $\mathcal{E}$-projective resolutions coincide with the $\mathcal{E}$-satellites defined by a universal property. This unifies the two approaches to relative derived functors: the constructive approach using resolutions and the axiomatic approach using connected sequences of functors.
