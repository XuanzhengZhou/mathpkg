This is the dual of Theorem 4.1, stated for cochain complexes. It provides the foundation for right derived functors: given an acyclic complex $C$ (e.g., a module concentrated in degree $0$) and an injective resolution $D$, any map on $H^0$ lifts to a cochain map, unique up to homotopy. This ensures right derived functors are well-defined.
