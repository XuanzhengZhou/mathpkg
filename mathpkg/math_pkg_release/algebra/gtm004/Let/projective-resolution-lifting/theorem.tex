Let $C : \cdots \to C_n \to C_{n-1} \to \cdots \to C_0$ be projective and let $D : \cdots \to D_n \to D_{n-1} \to \cdots \to D_0$ be acyclic. Then there exists, to every homomorphism $\varphi : H_0(C) \to H_0(D)$, a chain map $\underline{\varphi} : C \to D$ inducing $\varphi$. Moreover, two chain maps inducing $\varphi$ are homotopic.
