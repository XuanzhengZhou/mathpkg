The short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence $0 \to A' \xrightarrow{\alpha'} A \xrightarrow{\alpha''} A'' \to 0$ may be embedded in a commutative diagram with all rows and columns $\mathcal{E}$-exact and with $\mathcal{E}$-projective objects in the middle row. The construction uses $\mathcal{E}$-projective presentations of $A'$ and $A''$, setting $P = P' \oplus P''$.
