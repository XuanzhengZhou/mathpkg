A \textit{chain complex} $C = \{C_n, \partial_n\}$ over $\Lambda$ is an object in the category of $\mathbb{Z}$-graded modules $\mathfrak{M}_\Lambda^{\mathbb{Z}}$ together with an endomorphism $\partial : C \to C$ of degree $-1$ such that $\partial \partial = 0$. Equivalently, a family $\{C_n\}_{n \in \mathbb{Z}}$ of $\Lambda$-modules with homomorphisms $\partial_n : C_n \to C_{n-1}$ satisfying $\partial_{n-1} \partial_n = 0$ for all $n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
