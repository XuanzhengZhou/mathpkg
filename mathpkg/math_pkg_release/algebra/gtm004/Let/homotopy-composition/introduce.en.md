Lemma 3.3 shows that the homotopy relation is compatible with composition of chain maps. This ensures that the homotopy category — with chain complexes as objects and homotopy classes of chain maps as morphisms — is a well-defined category. The proof simply composes the individual homotopies and uses the fact that chain maps commute with differentials.
