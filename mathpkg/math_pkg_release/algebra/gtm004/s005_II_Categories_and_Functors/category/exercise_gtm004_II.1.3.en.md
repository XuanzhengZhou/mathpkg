# Exercise 1.3

Show that the category of groups has a generator. (A generator $U$ of a category $\mathfrak{C}$ is an object such that if $f, g : X \rightarrow Y$ in $\mathfrak{C}$, $f \neq g$, then there exists $u : U \rightarrow X$ with $fu \neq gu$).
