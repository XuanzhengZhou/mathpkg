# Exercise 7.2

Prove the identity (7.4):

$$\bar{\varphi}_I \theta^{-1} \varphi_U = \bar{\gamma}_\infty \beta_\infty.$$

This identity expresses a fundamental relation among the morphisms of the limit diagram. *(Hint: Use the commutativities (7.3) and the third of the identities (6.11), which "goes to the limit." Work in the diagram of derived Rees systems and pass to limits and colimits.)*
