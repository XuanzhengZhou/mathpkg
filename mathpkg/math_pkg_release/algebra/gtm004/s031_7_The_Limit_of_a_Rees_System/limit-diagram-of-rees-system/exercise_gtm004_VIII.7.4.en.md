# Exercise 7.4

Show that, in a category of modules, the morphism $\alpha': U \to U$ occurring in the exact sequence (7.2) is a monomorphism. More generally, characterize the kernel of $\alpha'$ in terms of the filtration data of the underlying filtered differential group. *(Hint: Use the description of $U$ as a colimit over the derived Rees systems and examine the action of $\alpha'$ on elements of finite filtration degree.)*
