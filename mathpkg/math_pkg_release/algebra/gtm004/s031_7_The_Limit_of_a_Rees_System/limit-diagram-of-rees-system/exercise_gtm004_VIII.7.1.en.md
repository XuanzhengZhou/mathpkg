# Exercise 7.1

Specify the morphisms $\varphi_U, \varphi_I$ of diagram (7.1). Recall that the morphism $\varphi_U: U \to \Gamma$ is obtained by means of the morphisms $\varphi_n: D_n \to \Gamma$ of the successive derived Rees systems using the universal property of the colimit, and the morphism $\bar{\varphi}_I: \Gamma \to \bar{I}$ is obtained dually using the universal property of the limit. Give explicit descriptions of these morphisms in terms of the construction of the derived Rees system.
