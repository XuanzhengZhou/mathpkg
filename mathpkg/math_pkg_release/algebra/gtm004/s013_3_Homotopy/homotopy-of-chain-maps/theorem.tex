A \text{homotopy}~\Sigma : \varphi \Rightarrow \psi~\text{between two chain maps}~\varphi, \psi : C \rightarrow D~\text{is a morphism of degree}~+1~\text{of graded modules}~\Sigma : C \rightarrow D~\text{such that}

$$\psi - \varphi = \partial \Sigma + \Sigma \partial,$$

\text{i.e., such that, for}~n \in \mathbb{Z},

$$\psi_n - \varphi_n = \partial_{n+1} \Sigma_n + \Sigma_{n-1} \partial_n.$$

\text{We say that}~\varphi, \psi~\text{are \text{homotopic}, and write}~\varphi \simeq \psi.
