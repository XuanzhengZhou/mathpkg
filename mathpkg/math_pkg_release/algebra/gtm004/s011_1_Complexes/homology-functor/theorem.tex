Let $\Lambda$ be a ring. The assignment $C \mapsto H(C)$ together with the assignment $\varphi \mapsto H(\varphi) = \varphi_*$ defines a functor
$$
H(-): \mathbf{Ch}(\Lambda) \longrightarrow \mathfrak{M}_\Lambda^{\mathbb{Z}},
$$
called the \textit{homology functor}, from the category of chain complexes over $\Lambda$ to the category of graded $\Lambda$-modules. Moreover, each $H_n(-)$ is a functor from $\mathbf{Ch}(\Lambda)$ to $\mathfrak{M}_\Lambda$.
