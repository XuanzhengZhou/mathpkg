Let $A, B$ be $\Lambda$-modules and let $R \xrightarrow{\mu} P \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} A$ be a projective presentation of $A$. Define a cochain complex $C$ of abelian groups by
$$
0 \longrightarrow \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(P, B) \xrightarrow{\mu^*} \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(R, B) \longrightarrow 0,
$$
with $C^0 = \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(P, B)$, $C^1 = \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(R, B)$, and $C^n = 0$ for $n \neq 0, 1$. Then
$$
H^0(C) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, B), \qquad
H^1(C) \cong \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^1(A, B), \qquad
H^n(C) = 0 \ \text{for}\ n \neq 0, 1.
$$
