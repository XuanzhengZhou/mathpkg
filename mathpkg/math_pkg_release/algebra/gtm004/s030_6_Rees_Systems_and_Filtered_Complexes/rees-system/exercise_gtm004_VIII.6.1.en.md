# Exercise 6.1

Identify the morphisms of (6.9), including $\theta$, for the Rees system of the triple $F(C)$, and establish the commutativity relations (6.10), (6.11).

*Guide.* For a filtered chain complex $C$, the functor $F$ (6.2) produces $G(C) = \bigoplus_{p} C$, $A(C) = \bigoplus_{p} C^{(p)}$, and $\theta : G(C) \to G(C)$ is the identity morphism of degree $+1$. Set $B = \theta A$ and identify the exact couples $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$ with (2.9) and (3.4) respectively. Then write down $\varphi$, $\bar{\varphi}$, $\xi$, and all the endomorphisms $\alpha$, $\bar{\alpha}$, $\beta$, $\bar{\beta}$, $\gamma$, $\bar{\gamma}$ explicitly in terms of the filtration on $C$, and verify the commutativities.
