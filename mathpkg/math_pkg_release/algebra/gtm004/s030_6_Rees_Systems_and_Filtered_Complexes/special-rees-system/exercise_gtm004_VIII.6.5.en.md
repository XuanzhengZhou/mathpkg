# Exercise 6.5

Obtain a special Rees system for a filtered *cochain* complex, paying special attention to the degrees of the morphisms involved.

*Instructions.* Let $(C, d)$ be a cochain complex with an increasing filtration $\cdots \subseteq C_{(p-1)} \subseteq C_{(p)} \subseteq \cdots \subseteq C$. Define the analogues of $G(C)$, $A(C)$, $B(C)$, and $\theta$. Construct the four exact sequences $S_1, S_2, S_3, S_4$ analogous to (6.3), and determine the diagram (6.5). Then identify the exact couples $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$, the connecting morphisms $\varphi$, $\bar{\varphi}$, $\xi$, and the automorphism $\theta$. Verify the commutativity relations (6.10) and the specialness relations (6.11). Finally, list the bidegrees of all morphisms, paying careful attention to the signs of the $p$-degrees compared to the chain complex case (6.24).
