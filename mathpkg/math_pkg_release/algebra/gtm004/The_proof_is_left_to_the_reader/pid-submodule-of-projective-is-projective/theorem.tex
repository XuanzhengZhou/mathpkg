Over a principal ideal domain $\Lambda$, every submodule of a projective $\Lambda$-module is projective.

By Corollary 5.2, every projective module over a PID is free. Hence any projective module $P$ is free, and any submodule of $P$ is a submodule of a free module. By Theorem 5.1, every submodule of a free module over a PID is free, hence projective. Therefore submodules of projective modules are projective.
