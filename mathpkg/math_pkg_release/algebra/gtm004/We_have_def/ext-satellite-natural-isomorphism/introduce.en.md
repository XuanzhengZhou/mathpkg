This theorem establishes a fundamental bridge between the Ext functor and left satellite functors. For any additive covariant functor $T$ from $\Lambda$-modules to abelian groups, natural transformations from $\operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^q(A, -)$ to $T$ correspond bijectively to elements of the $q$-th left satellite $\tilde{L}_q TA$. The isomorphism is natural in both $A$ and $T$, and generalizes the Yoneda description of Ext groups.
