This theorem applies Corollary 10.2 to the right exact functor $A \otimes_\Lambda -$ to obtain a characterization of Tor groups. Natural transformations from $\operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^n(B, -)$ to $A \otimes_\Lambda -$ are in natural bijection with $\operatorname{Tor}_n^\Lambda(A, B)$. The proof is immediate once one recognizes $\operatorname{Tor}_n^\Lambda(A, B)$ as $L_n(A \otimes_\Lambda -)(B)$.
