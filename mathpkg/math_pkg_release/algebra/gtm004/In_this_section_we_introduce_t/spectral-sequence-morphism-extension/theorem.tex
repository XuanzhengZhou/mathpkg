If $\varphi_* : E_k \to E_k'$ is a morphism of spectral sequences, then $\varphi_*$ extends uniquely to a morphism
$$\varphi_* : E_n \to E_n'$$
for all $n$ with $k \leq n \leq \infty$.

In particular, the term $E_{m,n}$ for $m, n \geq k$ depends naturally on the part of the spectral sequence $E$ beginning with $E_k$.
