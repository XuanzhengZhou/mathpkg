# Exercise 1.6

Show that the functor $- \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}G} \mathbb{Z}$ is left adjoint to the functor which assigns to an abelian group the structure of a trivial $G$-module. Deduce that if $P$ is a projective $G$-module, then $P_G = P \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}G} \mathbb{Z}$ is a projective $\mathbb{Z}$-module (i.e., a free abelian group).
