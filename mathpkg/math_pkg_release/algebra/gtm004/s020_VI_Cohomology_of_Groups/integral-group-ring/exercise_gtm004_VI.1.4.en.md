# Exercise 1.4

Propose a definition of $\Lambda G$ where $\Lambda$ is a ring with unity and $G$ is a group. This is the group ring of $G$ over $\Lambda$. Develop the concepts related to $G$-modules as in this section, replacing "abelian groups" by "$\Lambda$-modules". What is a $\Lambda G$-module when $\Lambda$ is the field $K$?
