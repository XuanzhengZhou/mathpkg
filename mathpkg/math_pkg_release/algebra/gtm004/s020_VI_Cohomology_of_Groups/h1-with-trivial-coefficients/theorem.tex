Let $G$ be a group and $A$ a trivial $G$-module. Then
$$H^1(G, A) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}G}(IG, A) \cong \operatorname{Hom}(IG/(IG)^2, A) \cong \operatorname{Hom}(G_{\text{ab}}, A).$$

More generally, for an arbitrary $G$-module $A$, the class of a derivation $\varphi: IG \to A$ in $H^1(G, A)$ is zero if and only if $\varphi$ is principal, i.e.\ there exists $a \in A$ such that $\varphi(x-1) = xa - a$ for all $x \in G$.
