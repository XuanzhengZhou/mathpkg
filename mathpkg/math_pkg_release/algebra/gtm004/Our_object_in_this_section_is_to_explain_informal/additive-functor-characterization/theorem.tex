Let $F: \mathfrak{U} \to \mathfrak{B}$ be a functor between additive categories. The following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $F$ preserves sums (of two objects);
\item[(ii)] $F$ preserves products (of two objects);
\item[(iii)] For each $A, A'$ in $\mathfrak{U}$, the map $F: \mathfrak{U}(A, A') \to \mathfrak{B}(FA, FA')$ is a group homomorphism.
\end{enumerate}
