Just as the product is a right adjoint to the constant functor $\mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{C}^I$, the pull-back is a right adjoint to a constant functor $\mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{L}}$ where $\mathfrak{L}$ is the "span" category. In this case the constant functor is a full embedding, so the counit of the adjunction is exactly the pull-back square with its universal property. This perspective generalizes: any universal construction is a left or right adjoint to a suitable constant functor.
