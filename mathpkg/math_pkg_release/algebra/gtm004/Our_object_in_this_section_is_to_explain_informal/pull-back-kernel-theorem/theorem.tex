Let $\varphi \alpha = \psi \beta$ be a pull-back diagram in a category $\mathfrak{C}$ with zero object. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] If $(J, \mu)$ is the kernel of $\beta$, then $(J, \alpha\mu)$ is the kernel of $\varphi$;
\item[(ii)] If $(J, \nu)$ is the kernel of $\varphi$, then $\nu$ may be factored as $\nu = \alpha\mu$ where $(J, \mu)$ is the kernel of $\beta$.
\end{enumerate}
