An adjunction between categories lifts pointwise to an adjunction between the corresponding functor categories: post-composition with the left adjoint $F$ is left adjoint to post-composition with the right adjoint $G$. This is a key technical lemma used in proving Theorem 8.6, which states that right adjoints commute with limits. The proof is a straightforward verification of the adjunction axioms at the level of natural transformations.
