Let $F \dashv G$ with $F: \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{D}$ and $G: \mathfrak{D} \to \mathfrak{C}$, and let $\mathfrak{P}$ be a small category. Then there is an induced adjunction $F^{\mathfrak{P}} \dashv G^{\mathfrak{P}}$ between the functor categories $\mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{P}}$ and $\mathfrak{D}^{\mathfrak{P}}$, where $F^{\mathfrak{P}}(H) = F \circ H$ and $G^{\mathfrak{P}}(K) = G \circ K$.
