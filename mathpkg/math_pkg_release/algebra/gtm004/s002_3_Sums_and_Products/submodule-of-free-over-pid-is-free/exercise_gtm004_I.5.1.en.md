# Exercise 5.1

Prove the following proposition, due to Kaplansky: Let $\Lambda$ be a ring in which every left ideal is projective. Then every submodule of a free $\Lambda$-module is isomorphic to a direct sum of modules each of which is isomorphic to a left ideal in $\Lambda$. Hence every submodule of a projective module is projective. (Hint: Proceed as in the proof of Theorem 5.1.)
