# Exercise 4.2

Given two projective $\Lambda$-modules $P, Q$, show that there exists a free $\Lambda$-module $R$ such that $P \oplus R \cong Q \oplus R$ is free. (Hint: Let $P \oplus P'$ and $Q \oplus Q'$ be free. Define $R = P' \oplus (Q \oplus Q') \oplus (P \oplus P') \oplus \cdots \cong Q' \oplus (P \oplus P') \oplus (Q \oplus Q') \oplus \cdots$.)
