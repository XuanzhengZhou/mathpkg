# Exercise 3.3

Show that if, in the short exact sequence

$$0 \to A' \xrightarrow{\alpha'} A \xrightarrow{\alpha''} A'' \to 0,$$

$\alpha'$ is an isomorphism, then the sequence

$$0 \to A \xrightarrow{\langle\alpha''^{-1}, \beta\rangle} A'' \oplus B'' \xrightarrow{(\beta'', -\iota)} B'' \to 0$$

is exact. State and prove the converse.
