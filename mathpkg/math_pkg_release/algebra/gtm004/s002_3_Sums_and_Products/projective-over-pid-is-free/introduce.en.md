Over a principal ideal domain, the notions of "projective" and "free" coincide. This is an immediate consequence of Theorem 5.1 together with the characterization of projective modules as direct summands of free modules (Theorem 4.7(5)). Since a direct summand of a free module is a submodule of a free module, and submodules of free modules over a PID are free, projective modules must be free.
