The Yoneda Embedding is a direct corollary of the Yoneda Lemma. It embeds the opposite of a category $\mathfrak{C}$ fully and faithfully into the functor category of set-valued functors on $\mathfrak{C}$. This means that every small category can be viewed as a full subcategory of a functor category, which is a powerful tool in category theory and homological algebra.
