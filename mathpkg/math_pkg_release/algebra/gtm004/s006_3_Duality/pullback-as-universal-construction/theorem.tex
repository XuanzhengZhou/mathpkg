Let $\mathfrak{L}$ be the category with three objects and two non-identity morphisms (a span), and let $F : \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{L}}$ be the constant functor. If $F \dashv G$, then for any pair $(\varphi, \psi)$ in $\mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{L}}$, the counit $\pi : FG(\varphi, \psi) \to (\varphi, \psi)$ is the pull-back of $(\varphi, \psi)$. The functor $F$ is a full embedding, so the unit may be taken as the identity.
