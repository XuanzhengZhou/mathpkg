# Exercise 7.1

Prove Proposition 7.1: Let $F: \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{D}$, $F': \mathfrak{D} \to \mathfrak{E}$, $G: \mathfrak{D} \to \mathfrak{C}$, $G': \mathfrak{E} \to \mathfrak{D}$ be functors and let $\eta: F \dashv G$, $\eta': F' \dashv G'$ be adjugants. Then $\eta'': F'F \dashv GG'$, where $\eta'' = \eta \circ \eta'$.
