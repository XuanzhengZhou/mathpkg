Coproducts (direct sums, in the additive case) of free objects are free, and coproducts of projective objects are projective. The free case follows because the free functor, being a left adjoint, preserves coproducts. The projective case follows from the lifting property componentwise. The converse also holds under mild conditions: if a coproduct is projective, each summand is projective (as a retract).
