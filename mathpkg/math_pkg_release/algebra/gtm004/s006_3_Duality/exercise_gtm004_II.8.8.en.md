# Exercise 8.8

Consider in the category $\mathfrak{C}$ (for example, $\mathfrak{S}$, $\mathfrak{Ab}$, $\mathfrak{M}_A$, $\mathfrak{G}$) the situation

$$\cdots \to C_{-2} \to C_{-1} \to C_0 \to C_1 \to C_2 \to \cdots, \quad C_i \in \mathfrak{C}.$$

Set $\varprojlim C_i = \bigcap_i C_i$ and $\varinjlim C_i = \bigcup_i C_i$, regarding the maps as embeddings. What are the universal properties satisfied by $\varprojlim C_i$ and $\varinjlim C_i$? Describe $\varprojlim$ and $\varinjlim$ in terms of adjoint functors.
