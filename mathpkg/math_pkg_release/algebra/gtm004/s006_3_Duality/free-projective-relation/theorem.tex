Let $\operatorname{Fr} \dashv U$, where $U : \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{S}$ is the underlying functor. Then every object $A$ of $\mathfrak{C}$ is the image of an epimorphism $\delta_A : \operatorname{Fr}U(A) \to A$, natural in $A$. If, moreover, $U$ maps epimorphisms to surjections, then an object $P$ is projective if and only if it is a retract of a free object.
