# Exercise 9.1

Consider the commutative diagram

$$\begin{array}{ccc}
A & \xrightarrow{\varphi} & B & \xrightarrow{\psi} & C \\
{\scriptstyle \alpha}\downarrow & & {\scriptstyle \beta}\downarrow & & {\scriptstyle \gamma}\downarrow \\
A' & \xrightarrow{\varphi'} & B' & \xrightarrow{\psi'} & C'
\end{array}$$

in the abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$. Show that if $A \xrightarrow{\beta\varphi} B' \xrightarrow{\psi'} C'$ is exact and $\beta$ is a monomorphism, then $A \xrightarrow{\varphi} B \xrightarrow{\psi \beta} C'$ is exact. What is the dual of this?
