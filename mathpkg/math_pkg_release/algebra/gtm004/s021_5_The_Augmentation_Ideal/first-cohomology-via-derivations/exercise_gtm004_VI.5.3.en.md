# Exercise 5.3

Let the (multiplicative) cyclic group of order 2, $C_2$, operate on $\mathbb{Z}$ by

$$x \circ n = -n,$$

where $x$ generates $C_2$. Use Corollary 5.2 to compute $H^1(C_2, \mathbb{Z})$, for this action of $C_2$ on $\mathbb{Z}$.
