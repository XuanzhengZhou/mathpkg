\begin{lemma}[4.1]
There is a natural isomorphism of abelian groups
$$IG / (IG)^2 \cong G / G' = G_{\mathrm{ab}},$$
where $G' = [G, G]$ is the commutator subgroup of $G$.
\end{lemma}

The isomorphism is given explicitly by
$$\psi: IG \to G/G', \quad \psi(x - 1) = xG', \qquad x \in G,$$
which vanishes on $(IG)^2$, and its inverse is induced by
$$\varphi: G \to IG/(IG)^2, \quad \varphi(x) = (x - 1) + (IG)^2.$$
