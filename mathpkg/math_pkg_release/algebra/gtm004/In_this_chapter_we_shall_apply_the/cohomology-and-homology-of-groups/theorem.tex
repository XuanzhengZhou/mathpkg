Let $G$ be a group. For a left $G$-module $A$, define the $n$-th \emph{cohomology group} of $G$ with coefficients in $A$ by
$$H^n(G, A) = \operatorname{Ext}_{\mathbb{Z}G}^n(\mathbb{Z}, A), \qquad n \geq 0,$$
where $\mathbb{Z}$ is regarded as a trivial $G$-module. For a right $G$-module $B$, define the $n$-th \emph{homology group} of $G$ with coefficients in $B$ by
$$H_n(G, B) = \operatorname{Tor}_n^{\mathbb{Z}G}(B, \mathbb{Z}), \qquad n \geq 0.$$
