# Exercise 7.1

Let

$$P: \qquad \cdots \to P_n \to P_{n-1} \to \cdots \to P_0 \to A \to 0,$$
$$R_n$$
$$R_1$$

be a projective resolution of $A$ and let

$$J: \qquad 0 \to B \to J^0 \to J^1 \to \cdots \to J^{q-1} \to J^q \to \cdots$$
$$\phantom{J: \qquad 0 \to B \to J^0 \to J^1 \to \cdots \to J^{q-1} \to} K_q \nearrow$$

be an injective resolution of $B$. Establish isomorphisms

$$\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B) \cong \operatorname{Ext}_A^{n-1}(R_1, B) \cong \cdots \cong \operatorname{Ext}_A^1(R_{n-1}, B),$$

$$\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B) \cong \operatorname{Ext}_A^{n-1}(A, K_1) \cong \cdots \cong \operatorname{Ext}_A^1(A, K_{n-1}),$$

for $n \geq 1$.
