# Exercise 8.3

Suppose given the exact sequence

$$0 \longrightarrow B \longrightarrow I_0 \longrightarrow \cdots \longrightarrow I_{q-1} \longrightarrow S_q \longrightarrow 0$$

with $I_0, \ldots, I_{q-1}$ injective. Show that the sequence

$$\operatorname{Hom}_A(A, I_{q-1}) \xrightarrow{\epsilon} \operatorname{Hom}_A(A, S_q) \longrightarrow \overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^q(A, B) \longrightarrow 0$$

is exact.

*Hint.* Use the definition of $\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^q$ as the $q$-th right derived functor of $\operatorname{Hom}_A(A, -)$ and apply the dual of Proposition 5.5 iteratively.
