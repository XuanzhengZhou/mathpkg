# Exercise 9.2

Given the diagram of $n$-extensions

\[
\begin{CD}
E: 0 @>>> B @>>> E_n @>>> \cdots @>>> E_1 @>>> A @>>> 0 \\
@. @V{\beta}VV @VVV @. @VVV @. \\
E': 0 @>>> B' @>>> E_n' @>>> \cdots @>>> E_1' @>>> A @>>> 0
\end{CD}
\]

show that $E'$ and $E_\beta$ (the push-out of $E$ along $\beta$) are in the same equivalence class in $\operatorname{Yext}_A^n(A, B')$.

Dualize: given a homomorphism $\alpha : A' \rightarrow A$, show that $E'$ (ending in $A'$) and $E^\alpha$ (the pull-back of $E$ along $\alpha$) are in the same equivalence class in $\operatorname{Yext}_A^n(A', B)$.
