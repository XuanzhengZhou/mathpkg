# Exercise 6.3

In the setting of Exercise 6.2 define an additive $J$-homology by

$$H_n^+(J, -) = (S_n^+ \widetilde{J}^+): \mathfrak{B} \rightarrow \mathfrak{A}, \quad n \geq 0.$$

Show the existence of this homology if $\mathfrak{A}$ has enough projectives.
