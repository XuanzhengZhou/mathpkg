Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a Lie algebra and $\mathfrak{h}$ a Lie subalgebra. Let $J$ be a totally ordered basis of $\mathfrak{g}$ containing a subset $J'$ which is a basis of $\mathfrak{h}$. Extend the total order of $J$ to a total order on a basis of $U\mathfrak{g}$. Then the elements

$$i(x_{j_1})^{e_1} \, i(x_{j_2})^{e_2} \, \cdots \, i(x_{j_k})^{e_k},$$

where $j_1 < j_2 < \dots < j_k$ are taken from $J \setminus J'$ and $e_i \geq 0$, together with $1$, form a basis of $U\mathfrak{g}$ as a (left or right) $\mathfrak{h}$-module.
