A Lie subalgebra is a subspace of a Lie algebra that is itself a Lie algebra under the restriction of the bracket. Lie subalgebras correspond to subobjects in the category of Lie algebras. Important examples include the special linear Lie algebra as a subalgebra of the general linear Lie algebra, and Cartan subalgebras in the structure theory of semisimple Lie algebras.
