# Exercise 4.3

Prove (by a suitable counterexample) that the isomorphism

$$\operatorname{Tor}(A', A) \otimes A'' \oplus \operatorname{Tor}(A' \otimes A, A'') \cong A' \otimes \operatorname{Tor}(A, A'') \oplus \operatorname{Tor}(A', A \otimes A'')$$

is not natural.
