The Hom complex $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(D, E)$ of two chain complexes is a double complex whose $(p,q)$-component consists of $\Lambda$-module homomorphisms from $D_{-p}$ to $E_q$. The differentials incorporate the differentials of $D$ and $E$ with appropriate sign conventions to ensure anticommutativity. Its second total complex $\operatorname{Tot}'$ is taken as the chain complex of homomorphisms, and its homology is analyzed by the dual Künneth theorem.
