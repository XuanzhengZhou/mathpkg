Lemma 2.3 states that any homomorphism between homology groups can be lifted to a chain map, provided the source chain complex is free over a principal ideal domain. The proof uses the freeness of cycles and boundaries to construct the lifting step by step, first on cycles and then extended to the full chain complex via a splitting $C_p = Z_p \oplus Y_p$. This lemma is a key technical tool in the proof of the Künneth theorem and the splitting of the Künneth sequence.
