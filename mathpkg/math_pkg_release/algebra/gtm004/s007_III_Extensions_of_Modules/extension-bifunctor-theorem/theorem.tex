$E(-,-)$ is a bifunctor from the category of $\Lambda$-modules to the category of sets. It is contravariant in the first variable and covariant in the second variable. That is:
\begin{enumerate}
\item For each $B$, $E(-,B)$ is a contravariant functor with $E(\alpha,B) = \alpha^*$.
\item For each $A$, $E(A,-)$ is a covariant functor with $E(A,\beta) = \beta_*$.
\item The two structures are compatible: $\beta_* \circ \alpha^* = \alpha^* \circ \beta_*$ for all $\alpha: A' \to A$ and $\beta: B \to B'$.
\end{enumerate}
