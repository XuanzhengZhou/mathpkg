# Exercise 1.7

Show that $E(A, B_1 \oplus B_2) \cong E(A, B_1) \times E(A, B_2)$. Is there a corresponding formula with respect to the first variable? That is, does $E(A_1 \oplus A_2, B) \cong E(A_1, B) \times E(A_2, B)$ hold?
