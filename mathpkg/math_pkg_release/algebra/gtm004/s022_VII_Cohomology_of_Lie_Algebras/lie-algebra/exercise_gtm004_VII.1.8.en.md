# Exercise 1.8

Let $\mathfrak{g}_1, \mathfrak{g}_2$ be two Lie algebras over $K$. Show that $\mathfrak{g} = \mathfrak{g}_1 \oplus \mathfrak{g}_2$ has a natural Lie algebra structure, which makes $\mathfrak{g}$ the product of $\mathfrak{g}_1$ and $\mathfrak{g}_2$ in the category of Lie algebras over $K$.
