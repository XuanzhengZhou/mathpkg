# Exercise 1.3

Show that the set of all elements $x \in \mathfrak{g}$ with $[x, y] = 0$ for all $y \in \mathfrak{g}$ is an ideal. (This ideal is called the center of $\mathfrak{g}$. Clearly the center is an abelian ideal.)
