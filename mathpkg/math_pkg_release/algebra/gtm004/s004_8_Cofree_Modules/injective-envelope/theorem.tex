Let $A$ be a $\Lambda$-module. The \emph{injective envelope} (or \emph{injective hull}) of $A$ is the unique (up to isomorphism) maximal essential extension of $A$ that is contained in (and is a direct summand of) an injective module. It is denoted by $E(A)$ and is characterized by the properties:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $A \subseteq E(A)$ is an essential extension,
\item $E(A)$ is an injective $\Lambda$-module.
\end{enumerate}
