# Exercise 9.1

Compute the injective envelope of the following abelian groups:

- $\mathbb{Z}$
- $\mathbb{Z}_p$, $p$ prime
- $\mathbb{Z}_n$, $n \geq 2$

Recall that for abelian groups ($\Lambda = \mathbb{Z}$), a module is injective if and only if it is divisible. The injective envelope of an abelian group $A$ is the minimal divisible group containing $A$ as an essential extension.
