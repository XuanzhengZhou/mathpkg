# Exercise 5.5

Show that $0 \rightarrow L_q TA \rightarrow L_0 T K_q \xrightarrow{\mu_*} T P_{q-1}$ is exact, $q \geq 1$, giving the appropriate interpretation of $\mu_*$. Show also that

$$L_q T A \cong L_{q-1} T K_1 \cong L_{q-2} T K_2 \cong \cdots \cong L_1 T K_{q-1}, \quad q \geq 1.$$
