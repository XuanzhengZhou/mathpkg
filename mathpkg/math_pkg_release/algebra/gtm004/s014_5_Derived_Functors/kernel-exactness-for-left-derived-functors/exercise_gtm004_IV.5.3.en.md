# Exercise 5.3

Prove Proposition 5.6.

**Proposition 5.6.** Let $P_q \rightarrow P_{q-1} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow P_0 \rightarrow A$ be an exact sequence with $P_0, \ldots, P_{q-1}$ projective. Let $K_q = \operatorname{im} \delta_q$. Then if $T$ is left exact, and $q \geq 1$, the sequence

$$TP_{q-1} \xrightarrow{\delta_*} TK_q \rightarrow L_q TA \rightarrow 0$$

is exact.
