# Exercise 5.7

Let $\mathrm{Phom}_A(A, B)$ consist of those homomorphisms $A \rightarrow B$ which factor through projectives. Show that $\mathrm{Phom}_A(A, B)$ is a subgroup of $\mathrm{Hom}_A(A, B)$. Let $\pi P(A, B)$ be the quotient group. Show that if $0 \rightarrow B' \rightarrow B \rightarrow B'' \rightarrow 0$ is exact, then

$$\pi P(A, B') \rightarrow \pi P(A, B) \rightarrow \pi P(A, B'')$$

is exact. Show that $\pi P(A, -)$ is additive, and that it is left exact if $A$ is a principal ideal domain. Dualize.
