When \(T\) is right exact, its left satellites coincide with its left derived functors: \(\tilde{L}_q T = L_q T\). Substituting this into Theorem 10.1 yields a clean characterization of left derived functors entirely in terms of the Ext functor. For any right exact functor \(T\) and module \(A\), the group of natural transformations \([\operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^q(A, -), T]\) is naturally isomorphic to \(L_q TA\). This formulation highlights the universal property satisfied by left derived functors and shows that they are completely determined by natural transformations out of the Ext bifunctor.
