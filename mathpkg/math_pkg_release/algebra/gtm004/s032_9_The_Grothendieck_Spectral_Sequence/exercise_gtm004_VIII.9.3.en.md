# Exercise 3

Apply the Grothendieck spectral sequence to the following situation. Let $\mathfrak{n} \rightarrow \mathfrak{g} \rightarrow \mathfrak{h}$ be a short exact sequence of Lie algebras over $K$, and let $A$ be a $\mathfrak{g}$-module. Consider the functors $F : \mathfrak{M}_{\mathfrak{g}} \rightarrow \mathfrak{M}_{\mathfrak{h}}$, $F(A) = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathfrak{n}}(K, A)$, and $G : \mathfrak{M}_{\mathfrak{h}} \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ab}$, $G(B) = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathfrak{h}}(K, B)$. Deduce a spectral sequence (Hochschild--Serre) with

$$E_2^{p,q} = H^p(\mathfrak{h}, H^{q-p}(\mathfrak{n}, A)),$$

converging to the graded vector space $\{H^q(\mathfrak{g}, A)\}$, suitably filtered. Identify the sequence of Exercise 9.2 in this case.
