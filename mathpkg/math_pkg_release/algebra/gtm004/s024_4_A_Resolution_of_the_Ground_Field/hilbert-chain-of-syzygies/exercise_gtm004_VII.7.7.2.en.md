# Exercise 7.2

Let $J$ be a graded ideal in the polynomial algebra $P = K[x_1, \ldots, x_n]$, i.e. an internally graded submodule of the $P$-module $P$. Prove that

$$\operatorname{proj.dim} J \leq n-1.$$

**Hint:** Consider a projective resolution of $J$ and extend it by $J \to P \to P/J$. Then use Exercise IV.7.7.
