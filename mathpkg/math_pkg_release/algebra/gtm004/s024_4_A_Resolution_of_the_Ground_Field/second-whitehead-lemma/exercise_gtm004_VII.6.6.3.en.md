# Exercise 6.3

Using Exercise 6.2, show that for $\mathfrak{g}$ semi-simple,

$$H^2(\mathfrak{g}, I\mathfrak{g}) \neq 0,$$

where $I\mathfrak{g}$ is the augmentation ideal of $U\mathfrak{g}$. Note that $I\mathfrak{g}$ is not finite dimensional!
