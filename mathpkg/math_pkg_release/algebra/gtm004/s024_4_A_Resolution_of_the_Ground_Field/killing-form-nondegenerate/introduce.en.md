Corollary 5.3 follows from Theorem 5.2 by applying it to the adjoint representation $\operatorname{ad} : \mathfrak{g} \rightarrow \operatorname{End}_K \mathfrak{g}$. Since the kernel of $\operatorname{ad}$ is the center of $\mathfrak{g}$, which is an abelian ideal and hence trivial in a semi-simple Lie algebra, the adjoint representation is faithful. The trace form of $\operatorname{ad}$ is precisely the Killing form, which must therefore be non-degenerate.
