# Exercise 6.1

Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a Lie algebra, finite-dimensional over a field of characteristic 0. Use the exact sequence of Exercise 3.1 and the Whitehead Lemmas to prove that

$$\mathfrak{r} \cap [\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g}] = [\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{r}],$$

where $\mathfrak{r}$ is the radical of $\mathfrak{g}$. The ideal $[\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{r}]$ is called the *nilpotent radical*.
