For a field $k$, the following properties are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $k$ is algebraically closed.
\item[(b)] Every irreducible polynomial in $k[X]$ is of degree $1$.
\item[(c)] Every polynomial in $k[X]$, of positive degree, factors completely in $k[X]$ into polynomials of degree $1$.
\item[(d)] Every polynomial in $k[X]$, of positive degree, has at least one root in $k$.
\end{enumerate}
