Theorem 11 provides a criterion for when the colon ideal $\mathfrak{a} : \mathfrak{b}$ equals $\mathfrak{a}$: this happens precisely when $\mathfrak{b}$ is not contained in any associated prime of $\mathfrak{a}$. This theorem links the colon ideal operation (a form of ideal division) with the associated primes from the primary decomposition.
