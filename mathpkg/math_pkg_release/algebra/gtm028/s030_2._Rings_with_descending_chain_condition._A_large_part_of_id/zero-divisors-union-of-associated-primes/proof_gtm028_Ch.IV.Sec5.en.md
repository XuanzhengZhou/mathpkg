Apply Corollary 2 of Theorem 11 to the ideal $\mathfrak{a} = (0)$. An element $x$ is a zero-divisor if and only if $(0) : (x) \neq (0)$, which by Theorem 11 means $x$ is contained in some associated prime ideal of $(0)$.
