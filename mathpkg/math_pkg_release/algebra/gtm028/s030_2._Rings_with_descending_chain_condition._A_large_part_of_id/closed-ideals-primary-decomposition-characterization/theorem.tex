Let $R$ be a noetherian ring, $\mathfrak{m}$ an ideal of $R$, and $\mathfrak{a}$ an ideal of $R$ with irredundant primary decomposition $\mathfrak{a} = \bigcap_i \mathfrak{a}_i$, where $\mathfrak{p}_i = \sqrt{\mathfrak{a}_i}$. Then the intersection $\bigcap_n (\mathfrak{a} + \mathfrak{m}^n)$ equals the intersection of those primary components $\mathfrak{a}_i$ whose radical $\mathfrak{p}_i$ satisfies $\mathfrak{m} + \mathfrak{p}_i \neq R$.
