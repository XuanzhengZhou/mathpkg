If $R$ satisfies the d.c.c., then the integral domain $R/\mathfrak{P}$ also satisfies the d.c.c. By the fact that the only integral domains satisfying the d.c.c. are fields, $R/\mathfrak{P}$ is a field, whence $\mathfrak{P}$ is maximal in $R$.
