Let $f : R \to S$ be a homomorphism of commutative rings, and let $\mathfrak{a}$ be an ideal of $R$. The \text{extension} $\mathfrak{a}^e$ of $\mathfrak{a}$ is the ideal $S f(\mathfrak{a})$ generated by $f(\mathfrak{a})$ in $S$. It consists of all finite sums $\sum_i s_i f(a_i)$ with $s_i \in S$ and $a_i \in \mathfrak{a}$.
