By Lemma 1, in a ring with a.c.c. every ideal $\mathfrak{a}$ is a finite intersection $\mathfrak{a} = \bigcap_i \mathfrak{q}_i$ of irreducible ideals. By Lemma 2, each irreducible ideal $\mathfrak{q}_i$ is primary in a ring with a.c.c. Therefore $\mathfrak{a}$ is a finite intersection of primary ideals.
