Let $R$ be an integral domain and $R[x]$ a polynomial ring in one variable over $R$. If $T$ is an $R$-automorphism of $R[x]$, then $xT = a_0 + a_1 x$, where $a_1$ is a unit in $R$. Conversely, if $x' = a_0 + a_1 x$ and $a_1$ is a unit in $R$, then there exists a unique $R$-automorphism $T$ of $R[x]$ such that $xT = x'$.
