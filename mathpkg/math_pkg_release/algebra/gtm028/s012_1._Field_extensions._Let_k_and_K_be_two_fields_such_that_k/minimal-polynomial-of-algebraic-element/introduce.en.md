For an algebraic element $x$ over a field $k$, the minimal polynomial is the unique monic irreducible polynomial $f(X) \in k[X]$ having $x$ as a root. It is the polynomial of least positive degree satisfying $f(x) = 0$. The degree of the minimal polynomial equals the degree of the field extension $[k(x) : k]$, and the minimal polynomial divides every polynomial in $k[X]$ that vanishes at $x$.
