Let $x$ be an algebraic element over a field $k$, and let $f(X)$ be a polynomial of least positive degree in $k[X]$ such that $f(x) = 0$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $f(X)$ is irreducible in $k[X]$.
\item For any polynomial $g(X) \in k[X]$ such that $g(x) = 0$, $f(X)$ divides $g(X)$.
\end{enumerate}
