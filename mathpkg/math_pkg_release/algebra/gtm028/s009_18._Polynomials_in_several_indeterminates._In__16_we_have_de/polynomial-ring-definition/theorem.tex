**DEFINITION 2.** Let $R$ be a ring with identity and $S^\prime$ a ring unitary over $R$. Then $S^\prime$ is called a **polynomial ring** over $R$ (in $n$ indeterminates) if there exist elements $x_1, \cdots, x_n \in S^\prime$ which are algebraically independent over $R$ and such that $S^\prime = R[x_1, \cdots, x_n]$.
