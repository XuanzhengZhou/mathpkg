For a ring $\Delta$ unitary over $R$ and chosen elements $x_1, \dots, x_n \in \Delta$, the substitution map sends each polynomial $f \in R[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ to the element $f(x_1, \dots, x_n) \in \Delta$ obtained by replacing each indeterminate $X_i$ with $x_i$. This yields an $R$-algebra homomorphism whose image $R[x_1, \dots, x_n]$ is the smallest subring of $\Delta$ containing $R$ and all $x_i$.
