Let $L, K$ and $k'$ be fields and let $K$ be a finite extension of $L$. Then:
$$[(K, k') : (L, k')] \leq [K : L],$$
$$[(K, k') : (L, k')]_s \leq [K : L]_s,$$
$$[(K, k') : (L, k')]_i \leq [K : L]_i.$$
If the fields $K$ and $k'$ are linearly disjoint over some common subfield $k$ of $L$ and $k'$, then the equality sign holds in all three inequalities.
