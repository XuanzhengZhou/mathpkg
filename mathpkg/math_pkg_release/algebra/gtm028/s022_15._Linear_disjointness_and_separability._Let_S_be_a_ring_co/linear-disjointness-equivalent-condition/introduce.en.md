The condition (LD) provides an equivalent but asymmetric characterization of linear disjointness: two subspaces $L$ and $L'$ are linearly disjoint over $k$ precisely when any set of elements of $L$ that is linearly independent over $k$ remains linearly independent over the larger coefficient ring $L'$. This formulation is particularly useful in field theory because it connects linear disjointness with the notion of linear independence under extension of scalars. The equivalence follows directly from expanding a linear relation over $L'$ in terms of a $k$-basis.
