**Proof.** Since $\bar{k}$ is a perfect field, the order of inseparability $[\bar{k}(x):\bar{k}]_i = 1$. Applying Theorem 38 with $K = \bar{k}$ and noting that $\bar{k}$ and $k(x)$ are free over $k$ (since $\bar{k}/k$ is algebraic), we obtain the stated formula.
