This corollary factorizes the inseparable degree $[k(x):k(z)]_i$ as the product of the order of inseparability $[k(x):k]_i$ and the inseparable degree after extending scalars to the algebraic closure $\bar{k}$. Since $\bar{k}$ is perfect, the field $\bar{k}(x)$ is separably generated over $\bar{k}$, which simplifies many computations.
