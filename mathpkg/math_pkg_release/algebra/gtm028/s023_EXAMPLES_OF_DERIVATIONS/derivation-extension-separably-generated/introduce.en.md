A separably generated extension can be factored as a purely transcendental extension followed by a separable algebraic extension. Corollary 3 follows immediately by applying Corollary 1' to the purely transcendental part and Corollary 2' (or Corollary 2) to the separable algebraic part, composing the two extensions to obtain a derivation on the full field.
