Let $F$ be a field of characteristic $p \neq 0$ and let $S$ be a subset of $F$. The following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $S$ is a $p$-basis of $F$.
  \item For each element $x \in S$, there exists a derivation $D_x$ of $F$ over $F^p$ such that $D_x(x) = 1$ and $D_x(y) = 0$ for all $y \in S$ with $y \neq x$.
\end{enumerate}
Moreover, if $F$ is a finitely generated extension of $F^p$ and $S = \{x_1, \ldots, x_s\}$ is a $p$-basis of $F$, then the derivations $D_{x_i}$ form a basis of the $F$-vector space $\mathcal{D}_{F/F^p}$.
