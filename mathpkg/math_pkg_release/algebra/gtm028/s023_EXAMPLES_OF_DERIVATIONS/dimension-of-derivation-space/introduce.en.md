Theorem 41 connects the dimension of the derivation space $\mathcal{D}_{F/K}$ to the separable transcendence degree of the extension $F/K$. The dimension equals the minimal number of elements needed to make the extension separable algebraic. For perfect base fields, this reduces to the ordinary transcendence degree. This result provides a cohomological interpretation of the notion of separating transcendence basis.
