The degree of a field extension is multiplicative in towers: $[L:k] = [L:K] \cdot [K:k]$. This is an immediate corollary of the Tower Theorem, which constructs a basis of $L/k$ from bases of $K/k$ and $L/K$. The formula is one of the most frequently used identities in field theory, allowing degrees to be computed by factoring an extension into simpler intermediate steps.
