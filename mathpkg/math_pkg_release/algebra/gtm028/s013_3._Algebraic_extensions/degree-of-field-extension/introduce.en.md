The degree $[K:k]$ of a field extension $K/k$ is the dimension of $K$ as a vector space over $k$. This fundamental numerical invariant measures the "size" of the extension in linear-algebraic terms. By Theorem 4, a finite degree implies the extension is algebraic, and the degree bounds the minimal polynomial degree of every element in $K$.
