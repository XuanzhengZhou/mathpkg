The quotient ring $R_M$ is the localization of $R$ at the multiplicative system $M$. Its elements are formal fractions $a/m$ with numerator from $R$ and denominator from $M$, constructed inside the total quotient ring $F$. The ring $R_M$ is an intermediate ring satisfying $R \subseteq R_M \subseteq F$. This construction generalizes the formation of the field of fractions of an integral domain to arbitrary rings with regular elements, by restricting which denominators are allowed.
