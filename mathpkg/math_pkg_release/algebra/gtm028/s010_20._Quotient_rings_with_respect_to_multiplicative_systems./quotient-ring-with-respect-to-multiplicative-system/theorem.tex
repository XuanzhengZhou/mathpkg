Let $R$ be a ring containing regular elements and let $F$ be its total quotient ring. Let $M$ be a multiplicative system in $R$. Since $M$ is closed under multiplication, the set of all quotients $a/m$, where $a \in R$, $m \in M$, is a subring of $F$ containing $R$. It is denoted by $R_M$ and is called the \textbf{quotient ring of $R$ with respect to the system $M$}.
