A necessary and sufficient condition that a product $(C, \varphi, \psi)$ of $A$ and $B$ (over $k$) be a tensor product of $A$ and $B$ is that given any two $k$-homomorphisms $g$ and $h$ of $A$ and $B$ respectively into a ring $R$, there should exist a homomorphism $f$ of $C$ into $R$ such that $f = \varphi^{-1}g$ on $A\varphi$ and $f = \psi^{-1}h$ on $B\psi$.
