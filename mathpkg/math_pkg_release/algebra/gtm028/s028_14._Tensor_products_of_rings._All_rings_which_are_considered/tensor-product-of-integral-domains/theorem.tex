Let $A$ and $B$ be integral domains containing $k$, and let $K$ and $K'$ be their respective quotient fields. If $K$ and $K'$ are linearly disjoint over $k$, then every regular element of $A \otimes_k B$ is regular in $K \otimes_k K'$, and the total quotient ring of $A \otimes_k B$ is canonically isomorphic to $K \otimes_k K'$.
