Theorem 19 establishes that the span operation $s$ on a vector space $V$ satisfies five fundamental properties: monotonicity $(S_1)$, finitary character $(S_2)$, extensivity $(S_3)$, idempotence $(S_4)$, and the exchange property $(S_5)$. These axioms abstract the essential behavior of linear span and form an axiomatic foundation from which all elementary properties of vector spaces can be derived. The same axioms also govern algebraic dependence in field theory, making this treatment broadly applicable.
