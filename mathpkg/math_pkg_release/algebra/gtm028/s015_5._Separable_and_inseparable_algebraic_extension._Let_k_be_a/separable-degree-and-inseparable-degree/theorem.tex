Let $K$ be a finite algebraic extension of a field $k$ of characteristic $p \neq 0$. Let $k_0$ be the maximal separable extension of $k$ in $K$. Set
$$n_0 = [k_0 : k],$$
called the **separable degree** of $K$ over $k$. The degree $[K : k_0]$ is a power of $p$ (since $K$ is purely inseparable over $k_0$); set
$$[K : k_0] = p^e,$$
where the non-negative integer $e$ is called the **exponent of inseparability** (or **inseparable degree**) of $K$ over $k$. Then
$$[K : k] = n = n_0 p^e.$$
