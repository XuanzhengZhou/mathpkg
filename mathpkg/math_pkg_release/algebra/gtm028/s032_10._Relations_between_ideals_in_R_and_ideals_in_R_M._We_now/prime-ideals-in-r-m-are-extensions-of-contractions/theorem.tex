The mapping $\mathfrak{p}' \to \mathfrak{p}'^{c}$ is a $1$-$1$ mapping of the set of all prime ideals of $R_M$ onto the set of all prime ideals of $R$ which are contracted and disjoint from $M$. Every prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}'$ in $R_M$ is the extension of its contraction: $\mathfrak{p}' = \mathfrak{p}'^{ce}$.
