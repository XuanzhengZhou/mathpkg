Corollary 1 to Theorem 16 establishes a bijection between prime ideals of $R_M$ and contracted prime ideals of $R$ that are disjoint from $M$. Every prime ideal in the localized ring is the extension of its contraction, and the contraction of a prime ideal is always a prime ideal. This is one of the most frequently used facts about localization: the prime spectrum of $R_M$ embeds into the prime spectrum of $R$.
