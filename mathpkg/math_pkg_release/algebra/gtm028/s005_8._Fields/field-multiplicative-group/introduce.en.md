The definition of a field via three separate axioms ($F_1$, $F_2$, $F_3$) is equivalent to the single group-theoretic condition that the set of nonzero elements forms a group under multiplication. This multiplicative group perspective unifies the treatment of division in fields with general group theory and is fundamental to the study of field extensions and Galois theory.
