A composition series between $M$ and $N$ is the relative version of a composition series, running from $M$ down to a submodule $N$ rather than to $(0)$. This concept is particularly useful in ring theory and corresponds bijectively to composition series of the quotient module $M/N$. All the properties of ordinary composition series — invariance of length, refinability, and equivalence — carry over to this relative setting.
