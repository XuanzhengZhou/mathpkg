Theorem 16 is a fundamental structural result: chain conditions are preserved under taking submodules and quotients, and conversely, if both a submodule $N$ and the quotient $M/N$ satisfy a chain condition, then so does $M$. This is essential for inductive arguments and for understanding how Noetherian and Artinian properties propagate through module constructions.
