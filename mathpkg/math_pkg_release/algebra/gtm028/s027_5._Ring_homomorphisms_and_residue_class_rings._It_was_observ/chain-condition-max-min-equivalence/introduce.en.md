Theorem 15 establishes the equivalence between chain conditions (ascending/descending) and extremal conditions (maximum/minimum) for modules. The a.c.c. is equivalent to every non-empty family of submodules possessing a maximal element, while the d.c.c. corresponds to the existence of minimal elements. This equivalence allows one to use whichever formulation is more convenient in a given context, and is fundamental for the theory of Noetherian and Artinian modules.
