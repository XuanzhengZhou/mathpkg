Theorem 26 states that transcendence degree is additive in a tower of field extensions $k \subset K \subset \Delta$: the transcendence degree of the full extension $\Delta/k$ equals the sum of $\operatorname{tr.d.} \Delta/K$ and $\operatorname{tr.d.} K/k$. The proof shows that the union of a transcendence basis of $K/k$ and a transcendence basis of $\Delta/K$ forms a transcendence basis of $\Delta/k$, and that these two sets are disjoint and algebraically independent together.
