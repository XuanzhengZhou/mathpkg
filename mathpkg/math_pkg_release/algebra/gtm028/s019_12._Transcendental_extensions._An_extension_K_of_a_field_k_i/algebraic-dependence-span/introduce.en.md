The algebraic closure span operator $s(X)$ on subsets of a field extension $K/k$ satisfies the five dependence axioms $(S_1)$-$(S_5)$ from Chapter I, §21. $(S_1)$ is monotonicity, $(S_2)$ is finite character, $(S_3)$ is extensivity, $(S_4)$ is idempotence (transitivity of algebraic dependence), and $(S_5)$ is the Steinitz exchange property. These conditions allow the general theory of dependence relations -- including Zorn's Lemma arguments -- to be applied to algebraic dependence, yielding the existence and cardinality uniqueness of transcendence bases.
