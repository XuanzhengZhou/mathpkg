Theorem 23 generalizes the basis extension property from vector spaces to arbitrary dependence relations satisfying $(S_1)$-$(S_5)$. Given a free (algebraically independent) subset $L$ and a system of generators $S$, one can find a subset $S'$ of $S$, disjoint from $L$, such that $L \cup S'$ forms a basis. The proof uses Zorn's Lemma on the set of subsets $S_\alpha$ of $S$ disjoint from $L$ such that $L \cup S_\alpha$ remains free, partially ordered by inclusion.
