This lemma is a technical tool used in the first proof of the Hilbert Basis Theorem (via the maximum condition). For an ideal $\mathfrak{A}$ of the polynomial ring $S = R[x]$, it defines the ideals $L_i(\mathfrak{A})$ of leading coefficients of polynomials of degree $i$ in $\mathfrak{A}$. The key insight is that the sequence $L_i(\mathfrak{A})$ is increasing, and that two ideals of $S$ with the same leading-coefficient-ideals at every degree must be equal. This allows one to use the maximum condition in $R$ to control ideals in $S$.
