This lemma, also used in the proof of Theorem 34, gives a precise length formula for the colon ideal. The length of $\mathfrak{q} : \mathfrak{a}$ equals the difference of the lengths of $\mathfrak{q}$ and $\mathfrak{a}$. This is proved by considering a composition series joining $R$ to $\mathfrak{q}$ that passes through $\mathfrak{a}$, and applying Lemma 1 to show the colon operation gives a refinement-free normal series.
