Theorem 28 gives a complete characterization of simple modules over commutative rings: every simple $R$-module is isomorphic to the quotient $R/\mathfrak{p}$ for a unique maximal ideal $\mathfrak{p}$. The proof uses the cyclicity of simple modules and the correspondence between submodules of $R/\mathfrak{a}$ and ideals containing $\mathfrak{a}$.
