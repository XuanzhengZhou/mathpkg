This lemma, used in the proof of Theorem 34, describes the behavior of the colon ideal operation on minimal overideals. When $\mathfrak{b} \subsetneq \mathfrak{a}$ are adjacent in the lattice of ideals containing $\mathfrak{q}$, the colon ideals $\mathfrak{q} : \mathfrak{a}$ and $\mathfrak{q} : \mathfrak{b}$ are also adjacent (or equal), with containment reversed.
