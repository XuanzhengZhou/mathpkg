The restriction of a ring homomorphism $T: R \to \bar{R}$ to a subring $R_0 \subseteq R$ yields a homomorphism $T_0: R_0 \to \bar{R}$. If $T$ is an isomorphism, its restriction to any subring is also an isomorphism (injective), though the converse is false. This allows one to study how homomorphic properties behave relative to subring structures.
