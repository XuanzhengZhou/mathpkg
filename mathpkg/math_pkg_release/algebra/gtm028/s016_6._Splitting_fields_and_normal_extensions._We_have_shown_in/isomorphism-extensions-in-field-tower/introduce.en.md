This lemma describes how isomorphisms behave in a tower of fields when the top field is normal. If the middle extension admits $n$ isomorphisms fixing the lower field, then every isomorphism of the lower field extends in exactly $n$ ways to the middle field. The proof uses the group of automorphisms of the normal closure and coset decompositions.
