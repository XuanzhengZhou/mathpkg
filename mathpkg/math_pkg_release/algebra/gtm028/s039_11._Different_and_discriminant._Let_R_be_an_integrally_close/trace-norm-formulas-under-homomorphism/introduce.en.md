This lemma establishes the fundamental relationship between the trace (and norm) of an element and the traces (and norms) of its residue classes modulo prime ideals. It is essential for computing differential exponents and discriminants using residue field data. The formula shows that the global trace decomposes as a weighted sum of local traces, with weights given by the ramification indices.
