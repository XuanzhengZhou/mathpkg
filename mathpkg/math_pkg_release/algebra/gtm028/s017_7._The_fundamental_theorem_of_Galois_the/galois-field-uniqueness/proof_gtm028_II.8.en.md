By the preceding theorem, a Galois field with $p^n$ elements is a splitting field over $\mathcal{F}_p$ of the polynomial $X^{p^n} - X$. By Theorem 12 of §6, any two splitting fields of a given polynomial are isomorphic. Hence any two Galois fields with the same number of elements (and consequently of the same characteristic $p$) are isomorphic.
