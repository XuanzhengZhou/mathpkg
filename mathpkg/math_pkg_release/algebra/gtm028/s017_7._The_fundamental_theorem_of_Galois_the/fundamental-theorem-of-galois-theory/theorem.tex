Let $K$ be a finite normal extension of $k$. If $H$ is any subgroup of $G(K/k)$, let $L$ be the set of elements of $K$ that are fixed under all automorphisms belonging to $H$. Then $L$ is a subfield of $K$ containing $k$, and
\[
H = G(K/L), \qquad [K:L] = |H|.
\]
Thus the mapping $H \mapsto L$ establishes a one-to-one correspondence between the subgroups of $G(K/k)$ and the intermediate fields $L$ between $k$ and $K$.
