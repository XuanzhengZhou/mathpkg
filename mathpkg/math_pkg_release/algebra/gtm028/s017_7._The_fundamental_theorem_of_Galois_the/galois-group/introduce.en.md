The Galois group $G(K/k)$ is the group of all $k$-automorphisms of a finite normal extension $K$ of a field $k$. By Theorem 16 of Section 6, this group is finite. The Galois group is the central object of Galois theory, encoding the symmetries of the field extension and establishing a correspondence between subgroups of $G(K/k)$ and intermediate fields between $k$ and $K$.
