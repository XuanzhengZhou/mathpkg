This lemma describes how the commutator of elements from different levels of the higher ramification filtration descends further down the filtration. It implies that the successive quotients $G_{V_n}/G_{V_{n+1}}$ are not just abelian but that the group structure of the entire ramification filtration is tightly controlled by the commutator relations, reinforcing the solvability of ramification groups.
