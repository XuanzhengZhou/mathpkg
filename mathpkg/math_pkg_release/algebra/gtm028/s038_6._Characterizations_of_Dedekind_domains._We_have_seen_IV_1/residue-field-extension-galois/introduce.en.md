Theorem 24 reveals the Galois-theoretic structure of the residue field extension. The quotient $G_Z/G_T$ is the Galois group of the residue field extension $R'/\mathfrak{P}$ over $R/\mathfrak{p}$ (or more precisely, of the maximal separable subextension). This shows that the decomposition group splits as an extension of the Galois group of the residue field by the inertia group: ramification is captured by $G_T$, while residue field growth is captured by $G_Z/G_T$.
