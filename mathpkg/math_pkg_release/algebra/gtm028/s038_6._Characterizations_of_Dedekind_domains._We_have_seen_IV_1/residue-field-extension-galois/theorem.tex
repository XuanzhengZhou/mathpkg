Let $R$ be a Dedekind domain, $L$ a finite normal extension of the quotient field $K$ of $R$, and $R'$ the integral closure of $R$ in $L$. Let $\mathfrak{P}$ be a prime ideal of $R'$ lying over $\mathfrak{p}$ in $R$, with decomposition group $G_Z$ and inertia group $G_T$. Then:

The residue field $R'/\mathfrak{P}$ is a normal extension of $R/\mathfrak{p}$, and $G_Z/G_T$ is (isomorphic to) the Galois group of the maximal separable extension of $R/\mathfrak{p}$ in $R'/\mathfrak{P}$. The fixed field of $G_T$ is the inertia field $K_T$.

If $R'/\mathfrak{P}$ is separable over $R/\mathfrak{p}$, then $R'/\mathfrak{P} = R_T/\mathfrak{P}_T$, $[L:K_T] = e$, and the relative degree of $\mathfrak{P}$ over $\mathfrak{P}_T$ is $1$.
