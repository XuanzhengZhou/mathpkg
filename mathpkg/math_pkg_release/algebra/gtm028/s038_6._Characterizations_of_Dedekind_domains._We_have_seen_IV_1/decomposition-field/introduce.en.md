The decomposition field $K_Z$ is the subfield of $L$ fixed by all automorphisms that stabilize a given prime ideal $\mathfrak{P}$. The extension degree $[K_Z:K]$ equals the number $g$ of distinct primes of $R'$ lying over $\mathfrak{p}$, while $[L:K_Z] = ef$ captures the combined effect of ramification and residue field extension above $K_Z$.
