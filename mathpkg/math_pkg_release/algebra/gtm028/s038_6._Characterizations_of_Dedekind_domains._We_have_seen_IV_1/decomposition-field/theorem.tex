The fixed field $K_Z$ of the decomposition group $G_Z$ of a prime ideal $\mathfrak{P}$ is called the \textit{decomposition field} of $\mathfrak{P}$. We have
$$[K_Z : K] = g, \quad [L : K_Z] = ef,$$
where $g$ is the number of distinct prime ideals of $R'$ lying over $\mathfrak{p}$, $e$ is the ramification index, and $f$ is the relative degree.
