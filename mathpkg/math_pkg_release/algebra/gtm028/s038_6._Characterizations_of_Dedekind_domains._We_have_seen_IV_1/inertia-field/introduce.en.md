The inertia field $K_T$ is the largest subfield of $L$ over which the prime ideal is unramified (in the separable case, the ramification index equals 1 over $K_T$). The extension $L/K_T$ is totally ramified, while $K_T/K_Z$ is unramified with the residue field growing from $R_Z/\mathfrak{P}_Z$ to $R'/\mathfrak{P}$.
