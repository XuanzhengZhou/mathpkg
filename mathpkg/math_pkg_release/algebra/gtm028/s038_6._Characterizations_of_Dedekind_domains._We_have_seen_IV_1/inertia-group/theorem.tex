Given a prime ideal $\mathfrak{P}$ of $R'$ lying over $\mathfrak{p}$ in $R$, the \textit{inertia group} $G_T$ of $\mathfrak{P}$ is the subgroup of the decomposition group $G_Z$ consisting of all automorphisms $s \in G$ such that
$$s(x) \equiv x \pmod{\mathfrak{P}} \quad \text{for every } x \in R'.$$

$G_T$ is an invariant subgroup of $G_Z$.
