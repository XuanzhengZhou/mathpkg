This is a temporary definition of discrete valuation rings as local Dedekind domains. By Theorem 15, such rings are principal ideal domains where every ideal is a power of the unique maximal ideal. The localization of any Dedekind domain at a nonzero prime ideal yields a discrete valuation ring, which is fundamental for the local study of Dedekind domains.
