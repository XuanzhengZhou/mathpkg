This lemma connects the notion of invertibility to finite generation. The proof uses the relation $\mathfrak{a} \cdot \mathfrak{a}^{-1} = R$ to produce finite families $\{x_i\} \subset \mathfrak{a}$ and $\{x_i'\} \subset \mathfrak{a}^{-1}$ such that $\sum_i x_i x_i' = 1$, from which it follows that the $x_i$ generate $\mathfrak{a}$ as an $R$-module.
