Theorem 18 establishes the equivalence between the Chinese Remainder Theorem property for a ring and the mutual distributivity of ideal addition and intersection. This is proved by showing that $(D_1)$ implies (CRT) for all $n$ by induction, and conversely that (CRT) for $n = 3$ implies both distributive laws. In Dedekind domains, the distributivity is easily verified using the prime exponent functions $n_{\mathfrak{p}}$, since $\min$ and $\max$ distribute over each other in the integers.
