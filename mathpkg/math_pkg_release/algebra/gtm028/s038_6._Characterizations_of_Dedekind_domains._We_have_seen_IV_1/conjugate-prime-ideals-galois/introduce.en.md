Theorem 22 states that in a Galois extension of Dedekind domains, the Galois group acts transitively on the set of prime ideals lying over a given prime $\mathfrak{p}$ of the base ring. Consequently, all such primes have identical ramification indices and relative degrees. The fundamental inequality $efg \leq n$ holds in general, with equality $efg = n$ in the separable case.
