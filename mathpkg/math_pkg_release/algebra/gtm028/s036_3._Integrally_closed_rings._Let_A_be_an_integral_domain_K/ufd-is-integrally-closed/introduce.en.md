A unique factorization domain (UFD) is an integral domain in which every non-zero non-unit element factors uniquely into irreducibles. The key observation is that if $x/y$ (in lowest terms) satisfies an integral dependence equation over $A$, then unique factorization forces the denominator to be a unit, so $x/y \in A$. This provides a large and important class of integrally closed rings, including all polynomial rings over fields and the ring of integers.
