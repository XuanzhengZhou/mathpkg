Let $K/k$ be a finite algebraic extension of degree $n$ and let $\{\omega_1, \omega_2, \dots, \omega_n\}$ be a basis of $K/k$. The \textbf{discriminant} of this basis is defined as
$$d(\omega_1, \omega_2, \dots, \omega_n) = \det\big( T_{K/k}(\omega_i \omega_j) \big)_{i,j=1}^{n},$$
where $T_{K/k}$ denotes the trace from $K$ to $k$.
