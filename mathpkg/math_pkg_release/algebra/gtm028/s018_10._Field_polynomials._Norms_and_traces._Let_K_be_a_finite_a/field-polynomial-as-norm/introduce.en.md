The field polynomial admits an elegant interpretation as a norm in the rational function field extension: $f(X)$ equals the norm of $X - x$ from $K(X)$ to $k(X)$. This connects the field polynomial to the norm formalism and follows directly from the fact that $(X - x)\Omega = (XE - A)\Omega$ where $A$ is the representing matrix of $x$.
