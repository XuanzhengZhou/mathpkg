Let $K/k$ be a finite algebraic extension of degree $n$ with basis $\omega_1, \dots, \omega_n$. Consider the rational function field extension $K(X)/k(X)$. Then $[K(X):k(X)] = n$ and $\{\omega_1, \dots, \omega_n\}$ is a basis of $K(X)$ over $k(X)$. For $x \in K$, the field polynomial $f(X)$ of $x$ over $k$ satisfies
$$f(X) = N_{K(X)/k(X)}(X - x).$$
