In a Galois (or more generally normal) extension, the norm and trace of an element admit concrete expressions as the product and sum of all its $k$-conjugates. When the extension is inseparable, each distinct conjugate is repeated $p^e$ times, where $p^e$ is the inseparable degree. These formulas generalize the familiar product-of-roots and sum-of-roots relations for polynomials.
