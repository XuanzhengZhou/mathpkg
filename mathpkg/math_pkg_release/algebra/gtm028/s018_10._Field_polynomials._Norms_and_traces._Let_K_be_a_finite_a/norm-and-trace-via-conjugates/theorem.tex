Let $K = k(x)$ be a simple finite algebraic extension of $k$ of degree $n$. Let $f(X) = X^n + a_1 X^{n-1} + \cdots + a_n$ be the minimal (and field) polynomial of $x$ over $k$. In a normal extension $K'$ of $k$ containing $k(x)$, factor
$$f(X) = \prod_{i=1}^{n} (X - x_i), \quad x_i \in K', \; x_1 = x.$$
Then the norm and trace of $x$ are given by:
$$N_{k(x)/k}(x) = \prod_{i=1}^{n} x_i = (-1)^n a_n,$$
$$T_{k(x)/k}(x) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i = -a_1.$$
If $p = \operatorname{char}(k) > 0$ and $x$ has only $n_0$ distinct conjugates with $p^e = [k(x):k]_i$, then
$$N(x) = \left(\prod_{i=1}^{n_0} x_i\right)^{p^e}, \qquad T(x) = p^e \cdot \sum_{i=1}^{n_0} x_i.$$
