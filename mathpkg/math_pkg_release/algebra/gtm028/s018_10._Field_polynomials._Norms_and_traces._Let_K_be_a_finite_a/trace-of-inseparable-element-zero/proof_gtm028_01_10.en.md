If $x$ is inseparable over $k$, then the inseparable degree $p^e = [k(x):k]_i$ satisfies $p^e > 1$. By formula (16), $T_{k(x)/k}(x) = p^e \cdot \sum_{i=1}^{n_0} x_i$. Since $\operatorname{char}(k) = p$, the factor $p^e$ annihilates the sum, giving $T_{k(x)/k}(x) = 0$.

For the general trace $T_{K/k}(x)$, apply formula (9): $T_{K/k}(x) = m \cdot T_{k(x)/k}(x)$ where $m = [K:k(x)]$. Since $T_{k(x)/k}(x) = 0$, we obtain $T_{K/k}(x) = 0$.
