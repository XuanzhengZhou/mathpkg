The trace $T_{K/k}(x)$ is a $k$-linear map from $K$ to $k$ obtained as the trace of the regular representation of $x$. It generalizes the sum of all conjugates in a Galois extension and satisfies the transitivity property $T_{\Delta/k} = T_{K/k} \circ T_{\Delta/K}$ for a tower of extensions. The trace plays a central role in the theory of discriminants and separability.
