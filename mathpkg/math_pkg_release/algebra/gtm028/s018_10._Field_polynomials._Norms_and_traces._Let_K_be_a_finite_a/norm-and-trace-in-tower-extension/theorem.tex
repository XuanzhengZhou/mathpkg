Let $k \subset K \subset \Delta$ be successive finite algebraic extensions, with $[K:k] = n$ and $[\Delta:K] = m$. For any $x \in K$, the norm and trace relative to the larger extension $\Delta$ are related to those relative to $K$ by:
$$N_{\Delta/k}(x) = [N_{K/k}(x)]^m,$$
$$T_{\Delta/k}(x) = m \cdot T_{K/k}(x).$$
