These formulas express the norm and trace for an arbitrary finite extension (not necessarily simple or separable) using all $k$-isomorphisms into a normal closure. Each isomorphism contributes one "conjugate" of $x$, and in the inseparable case each such value is raised to the power $p^f$ (for the norm) or multiplied by $p^f$ (for the trace), where $p^f$ is the inseparable degree. These are the most general formulas for norm and trace.
