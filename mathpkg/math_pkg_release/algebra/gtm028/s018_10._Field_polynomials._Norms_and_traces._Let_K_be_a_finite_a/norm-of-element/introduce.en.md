The norm $N_{K/k}(x)$ is a multiplicative map from $K$ to $k$ obtained as the determinant of the regular representation of $x$. It generalizes the product of all conjugates in a Galois extension and satisfies the transitivity property $N_{\Delta/k} = N_{K/k} \circ N_{\Delta/K}$ for a tower of extensions.
