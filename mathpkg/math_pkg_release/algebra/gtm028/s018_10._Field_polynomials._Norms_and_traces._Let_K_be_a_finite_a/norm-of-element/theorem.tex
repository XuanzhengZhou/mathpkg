Let $K/k$ be a finite algebraic extension of degree $n$, and let $\omega_1, \dots, \omega_n$ be a basis of $K/k$. For $x \in K$, write the multiplication map as
$$x\omega_i = \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij}\omega_j, \quad a_{ij} \in k.$$
The \textbf{norm} of $x$ from $K$ to $k$ is defined as the determinant of the matrix $A = \|a_{ij}\|$:
$$N_{K/k}(x) = \det(A).$$
