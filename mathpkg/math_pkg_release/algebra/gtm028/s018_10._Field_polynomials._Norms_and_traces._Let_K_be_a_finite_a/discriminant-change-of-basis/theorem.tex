Let $\{\omega_1, \dots, \omega_n\}$ and $\{\omega'_1, \dots, \omega'_n\}$ be two bases of $K/k$ related by the transition matrix $A = (a_{ij})$:
$$\omega'_i = \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} \omega_j, \quad a_{ij} \in k.$$
Then the discriminants are related by
$$d(\omega'_1, \dots, \omega'_n) = d(\omega_1, \dots, \omega_n) \cdot (\det A)^2.$$
