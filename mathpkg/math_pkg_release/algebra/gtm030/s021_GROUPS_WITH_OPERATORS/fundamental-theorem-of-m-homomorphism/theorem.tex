Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be an $M$-group. Any $M$-factor group $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$ (where $\mathfrak{H}$ is an invariant $M$-subgroup) is an $M$-homomorphic image of $\mathfrak{G}$. Conversely, if $\mathfrak{G}'$ is an $M$-homomorphic image of $\mathfrak{G}$, then $\mathfrak{G}'$ is $M$-isomorphic to an $M$-factor group $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{R}$ where $\mathfrak{R}$ is the kernel of the homomorphism.
