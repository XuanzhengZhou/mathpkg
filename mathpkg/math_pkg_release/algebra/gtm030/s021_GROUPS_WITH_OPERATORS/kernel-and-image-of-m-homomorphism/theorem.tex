If $\eta$ is an $M$-homomorphism of the $M$-group $\mathfrak{G}$ into the $M$-group $\mathfrak{G}'$, then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item The image $\mathfrak{G}\eta$ is an $M$-subgroup of $\mathfrak{G}'$.
\item The kernel $\mathfrak{R}$ of $\eta$ is an invariant $M$-subgroup of $\mathfrak{G}$.
\end{enumerate}
