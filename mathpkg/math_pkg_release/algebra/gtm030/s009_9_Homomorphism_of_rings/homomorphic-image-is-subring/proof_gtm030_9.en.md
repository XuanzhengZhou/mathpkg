Since $$\eta$$ is a homomorphism of the additive group of $$\mathfrak{A}$$, the set $$\mathfrak{A}\eta$$ is a subgroup of the additive group of $$\mathfrak{A}'$$. Because

$$(a\eta)(b\eta) = (ab)\eta,$$

the image $$\mathfrak{A}\eta$$ is closed under multiplication. Hence $$\mathfrak{A}\eta$$ is a subring of $$\mathfrak{A}'$$.
