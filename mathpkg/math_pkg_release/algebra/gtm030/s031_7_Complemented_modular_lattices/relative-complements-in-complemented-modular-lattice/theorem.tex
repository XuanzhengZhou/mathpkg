Let $L$ be a complemented modular lattice and let $a$ and $b$ be any two elements of $L$ such that $b \leq a$. Then there exists an element $b_1 \leq a$ such that
$$b \cup b_1 = a, \quad b \cap b_1 = 0.$$
In other words, in a complemented modular lattice, relative complements exist for any $b \leq a$ in $L$.
