The mapping $a \to a_l$ is 1-1 of $\mathfrak{G}$ onto $\mathfrak{G}_l$ but in general this is not an isomorphism. In order to obtain an isomorphism we replace this mapping by the mapping $a \to a_l^{-1} = (a^{-1})_l$; for then we have
$$(ab)_l^{-1} = (a_l b_l)^{-1} = b_l^{-1} a_l^{-1} = a_l^{-1} b_l^{-1}.$$
We call the isomorphism $a \to a_l^{-1}$ the \text{left regular realization} of $\mathfrak{G}$.
