Show that the subgroup $\mathfrak{H}$ of order $t$ (where $r = st$) of a cyclic group of order $r$ can be characterized in either of the following ways: (a) $\mathfrak{H}$ consists of the elements $a$ such that $a^t = 1$. (b) $\mathfrak{H}$ consists of the elements $a^{ks}$, $k = 0, 1, \ldots, t-1$.
