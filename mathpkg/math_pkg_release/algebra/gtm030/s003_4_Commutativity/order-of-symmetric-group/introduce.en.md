The symmetric group $S_n$ on $n$ letters has exactly $n!$ elements. This follows from a simple counting argument: when writing a permutation in two-row notation, there are $n$ choices for the image of the first element, $n-1$ for the second, and so on, yielding $n!$ possible permutations in total.
