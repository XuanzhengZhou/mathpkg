The following conditions are necessary and sufficient in order that a subset $\mathfrak{H}$ of a group $\mathfrak{G}$ determines a subgroup of $\mathfrak{G}$:

1. $a, b \in \mathfrak{H}$ implies that $ab \in \mathfrak{H}$ (closure).
2. $1 \in \mathfrak{H}$.
3. $a \in \mathfrak{H}$ implies that $a^{-1} \in \mathfrak{H}$.
