Let $L$ be a modular lattice with $0$, $1$ and of finite length. For $a \in L$, let $l(a)$ denote the length (rank) of the sublattice $L_a = \{x \mid x \leq a\}$. Then for any $a, b \in L$,

$$l(a \cup b) = l(a) + l(b) - l(a \cap b).$$

This is called the \textit{fundamental dimensionality relation} for modular lattices.
