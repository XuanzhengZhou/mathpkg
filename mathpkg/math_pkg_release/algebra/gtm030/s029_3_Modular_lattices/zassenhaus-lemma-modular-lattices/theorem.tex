Let $a_1, a_1', a_2, a_2'$ be elements of a modular lattice such that $a_1 \geq a_1'$ and $a_2 \geq a_2'$. Then the following three intervals are projective:

$$I[(a_1 \cap a_2) \cup a_1', (a_1 \cap a_2') \cup a_1'],$$
$$I[a_1 \cap a_2, (a_1' \cap a_2) \cup (a_1 \cap a_2')],$$
$$I[(a_1 \cap a_2) \cup a_2', (a_1' \cap a_2) \cup a_2'].$$
