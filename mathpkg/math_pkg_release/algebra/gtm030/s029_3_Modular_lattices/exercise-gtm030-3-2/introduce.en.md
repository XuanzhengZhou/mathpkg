This exercise illustrates that while the lattice of invariant (normal) subgroups of any group is always modular (Theorem 2), the lattice of all subgroups may fail to be modular. The alternating group $A_4$ provides a concrete counterexample: its subgroup lattice contains a copy of the pentagon $N_5$, which by Exercise 1 characterizes non-modular lattices.
