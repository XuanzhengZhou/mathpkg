If $\mathfrak{G} = \mathfrak{G}_1 \times \mathfrak{G}_2 \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{G}_n$, then $\mathfrak{G} = \mathfrak{H}_1 \times \mathfrak{H}_2 \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{H}_r$ where

$$\mathfrak{H}_1 = \mathfrak{G}_1 \times \mathfrak{G}_2 \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{G}_{n_1}, \quad \mathfrak{H}_2 = \mathfrak{G}_{n_1+1} \times \mathfrak{G}_{n_1+2} \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{G}_{n_2}, \quad \cdots,$$

$$\mathfrak{H}_r = \mathfrak{G}_{n_{r-1}+1} \times \mathfrak{G}_{n_{r-1}+2} \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{G}_{n_r}$$

for any $n_1 < n_2 < \cdots < n_r = n$.
