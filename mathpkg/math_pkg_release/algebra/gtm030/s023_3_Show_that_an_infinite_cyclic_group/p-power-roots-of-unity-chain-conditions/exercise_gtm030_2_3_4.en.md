Let $U_{(p)}$ be the multiplicative group of $p^k$ complex roots of unity for $p$ a fixed prime and $k = 0, 1, 2, 3, \cdots$. Show that every proper subgroup of $U_{(p)}$ is finite cyclic. Hence show that $U_{(p)}$ satisfies the descending chain condition but not the ascending chain condition.
