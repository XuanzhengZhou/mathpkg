This theorem gives a structure theorem for complemented modular lattices satisfying the ascending and descending chain conditions. The forward direction shows that such lattices are generated by their "points" (atoms) in an independent way. The converse establishes that any modular lattice whose top element is a finite join of points is automatically complemented and satisfies both chain conditions. This result is fundamental in the lattice-theoretic approach to the structure of modules and rings.
