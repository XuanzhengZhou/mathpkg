Show that the correspondence
$$\alpha_0 + \alpha_1 i + \alpha_2 j + \alpha_3 k \mapsto \begin{bmatrix}
\alpha_0 & \alpha_1 & \alpha_2 & \alpha_3 \\
-\alpha_1 & \alpha_0 & -\alpha_3 & \alpha_2 \\
-\alpha_2 & \alpha_3 & \alpha_0 & -\alpha_1 \\
-\alpha_3 & -\alpha_2 & \alpha_1 & \alpha_0
\end{bmatrix}$$
is an isomorphism of the quaternion algebra $Q$ into the ring $R_4$ of $4 \times 4$ real matrices.
