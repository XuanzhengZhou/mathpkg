Since $\eta$ is a homomorphism of the additive group of $\mathfrak{A}$, $\mathfrak{A}\eta$ is a subgroup of the additive group of $\mathfrak{A}'$. Because $(a\eta)(b\eta) = (ab)\eta$, the set $\mathfrak{A}\eta$ is closed under multiplication. Therefore $\mathfrak{A}\eta$ is a subring of $\mathfrak{A}'$.
