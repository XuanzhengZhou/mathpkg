Prove that, if $a \mapsto \bar{a}$ is a homomorphism of a ring $\mathfrak{R}$ into a ring $\bar{\mathfrak{R}}$, then the mapping $(a_{ij}) \mapsto (\bar{a}_{ij})$ is a homomorphism of the matrix ring $\mathfrak{R}_n$ into $\bar{\mathfrak{R}}_n$.
