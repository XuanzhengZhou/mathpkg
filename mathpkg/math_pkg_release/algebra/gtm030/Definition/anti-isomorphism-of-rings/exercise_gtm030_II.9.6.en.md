(Hua's Theorem) Let $S$ be a mapping of a ring $\mathfrak{A}$ into a ring $\mathfrak{B}$ such that $(a+b)^S = a^S + b^S$ and for each pair $a,b$ either $(ab)^S = a^S b^S$ or $(ab)^S = b^S a^S$. Prove that $S$ is either a homomorphism or an anti-homomorphism.
