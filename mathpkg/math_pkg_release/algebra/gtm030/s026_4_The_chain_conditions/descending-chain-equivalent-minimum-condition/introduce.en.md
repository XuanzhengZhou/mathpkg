This proposition establishes the equivalence between the descending chain condition and the minimum condition for submodules of a module. The proof uses the axiom of choice. The forward direction constructs a strictly descending chain from the failure of the minimum condition, forcing termination by DCC. The converse extracts a minimal element from the collection formed by the terms of any descending chain.
