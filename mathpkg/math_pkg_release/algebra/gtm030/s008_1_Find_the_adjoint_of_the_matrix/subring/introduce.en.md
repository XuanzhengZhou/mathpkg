A subring is a subset of a ring that is itself a ring under the induced operations. Equivalently, it is a subset closed under addition and multiplication that forms an additive group and contains the zero element. This concept formalizes the idea of a ring sitting inside a larger ring, such as the quaternions forming a subring of the complex $2 \times 2$ matrices.
