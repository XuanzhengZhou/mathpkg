The set $Q$ of matrices in $C_2$ ($C$ the field of complex numbers) of the form
$$\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
-\bar{b} & \bar{a}
\end{bmatrix} \equiv \begin{bmatrix}
\alpha_0 + \alpha_1\sqrt{-1} & \alpha_2 + \alpha_3\sqrt{-1} \\
-\alpha_2 + \alpha_3\sqrt{-1} & \alpha_0 - \alpha_1\sqrt{-1}
\end{bmatrix}, \quad \alpha_i \text{ real},$$
determines a subring of $C_2$ and is a division ring. Every nonzero element has an inverse given by
$$\begin{bmatrix}
(\alpha_0 - \alpha_1\sqrt{-1})\Delta^{-1} & -(\alpha_2 + \alpha_3\sqrt{-1})\Delta^{-1} \\
(\alpha_2 - \alpha_3\sqrt{-1})\Delta^{-1} & (\alpha_0 + \alpha_1\sqrt{-1})\Delta^{-1}
\end{bmatrix}$$
where $\Delta = \alpha_0^2 + \alpha_1^2 + \alpha_2^2 + \alpha_3^2$.
