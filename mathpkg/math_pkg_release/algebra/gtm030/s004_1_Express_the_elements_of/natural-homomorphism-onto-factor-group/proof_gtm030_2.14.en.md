Define $\pi: \mathfrak{G} \to \mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$ by $\pi(x) = x\mathfrak{H}$. Then
\[
\pi(xy) = xy\mathfrak{H} = (x\mathfrak{H})(y\mathfrak{H}) = \pi(x)\pi(y),
\]
so $\pi$ is a homomorphism. Every coset $x\mathfrak{H} \in \mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$ is $\pi(x)$, so $\pi$ is surjective (onto). Thus $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$ is a homomorphic image of $\mathfrak{G}$.
