This exercise asks for both the right and left coset decompositions of the subgroup $\mathfrak{H} = \{1, (1\;2)\}$ in the symmetric group $S_3$. Since $\mathfrak{H}$ is not normal in $S_3$, the right and left coset decompositions will differ, illustrating the distinction between the two types of cosets.
