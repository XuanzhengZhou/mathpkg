The factor group $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$ is the group whose elements are the cosets of an invariant subgroup $\mathfrak{H}$ and whose multiplication is induced by the multiplication in $\mathfrak{G}$. The natural projection map $\pi: \mathfrak{G} \to \mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{H}$ sending $x \mapsto x\mathfrak{H}$ is a surjective homomorphism. Factor groups are fundamental to the structure theory of groups, as they allow one to "factor out" a normal subgroup and study the resulting quotient.
