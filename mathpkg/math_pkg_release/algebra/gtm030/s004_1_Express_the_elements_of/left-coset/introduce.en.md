The left coset $\mathfrak{H}x$ is the set obtained by multiplying all elements of the subgroup $\mathfrak{H}$ on the right by a fixed element $x$. Left cosets arise from the left action of $\mathfrak{H}$ on $\mathfrak{G}$. In general, the decomposition of a group into right cosets need not coincide with the decomposition into left cosets. However, for invariant (normal) subgroups, the two decompositions are identical.
