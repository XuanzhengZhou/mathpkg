Let $\mathfrak{H}$ be a subgroup of $\mathfrak{G}$. The left congruence relation is defined by $x \equiv_{\ell} y \pmod{\mathfrak{H}}$ if there exists $h \in \mathfrak{H}$ such that $y = hx$, or equivalently $yx^{-1} \in \mathfrak{H}$. The equivalence class determined by $x$ is the set $\mathfrak{H}x$, called the left coset of $x$ relative to $\mathfrak{H}$.
