Let $m$ be the order of the cyclic subgroup $[x]$ generated by $x$. Then $x^m = 1$. By Lagrange's theorem, the order of any subgroup divides the order of the group, so $m \mid n$. Write $n = mr$ for some integer $r$. Then
\[
x^n = x^{mr} = (x^m)^r = 1^r = 1.
\]
