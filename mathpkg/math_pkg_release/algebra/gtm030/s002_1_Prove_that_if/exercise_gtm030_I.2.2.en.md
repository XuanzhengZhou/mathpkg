For a given binary composition define a simple product of $n$ $a$'s inductively as either $a_1u$ where $u$ is a simple product of $a_2, \cdots, a_n$ or $va_n$ where $v$ is a simple product of $a_1, \cdots, a_{n-1}$. Show that any product of $\geq 2^r$ elements can be regarded as a simple product of $r$ elements (that are themselves products).
