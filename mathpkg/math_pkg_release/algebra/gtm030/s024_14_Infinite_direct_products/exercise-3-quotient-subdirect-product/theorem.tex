Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be an $M$-group and let $\{\mathfrak{R}_{\alpha} \mid \alpha \in J\}$ be a collection of invariant $M$-subgroups of $\mathfrak{G}$ such that

$$\bigcap_{\alpha \in J} \mathfrak{R}_{\alpha} = 1.$$

Then $\mathfrak{G}$ is isomorphic to a subdirect product of the quotient groups $\mathfrak{G}_{\alpha} = \mathfrak{G} / \mathfrak{R}_{\alpha}$.
