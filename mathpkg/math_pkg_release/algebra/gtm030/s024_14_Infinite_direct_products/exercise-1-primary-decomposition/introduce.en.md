This exercise establishes the primary decomposition theorem for commutative torsion groups: every such group is the restricted direct product of its $p$-primary components. The key steps are showing each $\mathfrak{G}_p$ is a subgroup (closure under the group operation and inverses) and that every element can be uniquely expressed as a product of elements of prime-power order from distinct primes.
