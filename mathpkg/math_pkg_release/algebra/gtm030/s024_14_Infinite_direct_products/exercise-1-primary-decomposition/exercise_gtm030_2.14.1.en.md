Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be a commutative group without elements of infinite order. For each prime $p$ let $\mathfrak{G}_p$ be the subset of elements of order a power of $p$. Show that $\mathfrak{G}_p$ is a subgroup of $\mathfrak{G}$ and that $\mathfrak{G} = \prod_p \mathfrak{G}_p$.
