Theorem 1 gives a convenient criterion for determining whether a bijection between two lattices is an isomorphism: instead of checking both join and meet preservation separately, it suffices to verify that the map and its inverse both preserve the partial order. This connects the order-theoretic and algebraic perspectives on lattices, showing that the order structure alone determines the lattice operations.
