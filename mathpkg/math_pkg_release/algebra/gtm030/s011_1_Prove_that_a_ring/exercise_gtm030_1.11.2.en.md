If $\mathfrak{A}$ is any ring, the powers $\mathfrak{A}^2, \mathfrak{A}^3, \ldots$ are ideals of $\mathfrak{A}$ (where $\mathfrak{A}^k$ denotes the set of all finite sums of products of $k$ elements from $\mathfrak{A}$). Determine these ideals explicitly for the subring of the $3 \times 3$ matrix ring $I_3$ consisting of all matrices of the form

$$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & a & b \\
0 & 0 & c \\
0 & 0 & 0
\end{bmatrix}$$

where $a, b, c$ are integers.
