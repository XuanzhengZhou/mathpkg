Let $\mathfrak{B}$ and $\mathfrak{C}$ be additive subgroups of a ring $\mathfrak{A}$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item If $\mathfrak{B}$ is a left ideal and $\mathfrak{C}$ is any subgroup, then the product $\mathfrak{B}\mathfrak{C}$ is a left ideal.
\item If $\mathfrak{B}$ is a left ideal and $\mathfrak{C}$ is a right ideal, then $\mathfrak{B}\mathfrak{C}$ is a two-sided ideal.
\end{enumerate}
