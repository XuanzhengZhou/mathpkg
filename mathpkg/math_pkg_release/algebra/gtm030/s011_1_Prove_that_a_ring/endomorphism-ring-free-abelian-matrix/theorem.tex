Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be a free abelian group of rank $n$ with basis $e_1, \ldots, e_n$, and let $\mathfrak{E}$ be its ring of endomorphisms. The correspondence $\eta \mapsto (a_{ij})$ that sends each endomorphism to its matrix with respect to the basis $\{e_i\}$ is an injective ring homomorphism from $\mathfrak{E}$ into the ring $I_n$ of $n \times n$ matrices with integer entries.

Moreover, this correspondence is an isomorphism of $\mathfrak{E}$ onto the full matrix ring $I_n$; that is, $\mathfrak{E} \cong I_n$ as rings.
