Let $A$ be the set of all real valued functions on $(-\infty, \infty)$. Show that $A$ is a group with the ordinary addition and that $A$ is a semi-group relative to $f \cdot g(x) = f(g(x))$. Is $A$ a ring relative to these two compositions?
