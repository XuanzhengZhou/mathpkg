The circle composition a∘b = a + b − ab defines an associative operation on any ring, with 0 serving as the identity. This operation provides the algebraic framework for quasi-regularity and the Jacobson radical. When the ring has an identity, the circle composition is connected to ordinary multiplication via the map z ↦ 1 − z.
