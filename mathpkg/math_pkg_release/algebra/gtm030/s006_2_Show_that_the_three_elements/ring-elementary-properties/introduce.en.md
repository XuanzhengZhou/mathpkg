In any ring, the additive group structure and multiplicative semi-group structure together imply several elementary properties. The negation of a sum equals the sum of negations, multiples distribute as expected, and both the generalized associative and commutative laws hold for the additive group. These properties follow directly from the ring axioms and are used routinely in algebraic manipulations.
