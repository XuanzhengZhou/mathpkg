For an $n \times n$ matrix $(a)$ with cofactors $A_{ij}$, the sum of the products of the elements of any row (or column) by the cofactors of a different row (or column) is zero:
$$a_{i1}A_{j1} + a_{i2}A_{j2} + \cdots + a_{in}A_{jn} = 0, \quad i \neq j,$$
$$a_{1i}A_{1j} + a_{2i}A_{2j} + \cdots + a_{ni}A_{nj} = 0, \quad i \neq j.$$
