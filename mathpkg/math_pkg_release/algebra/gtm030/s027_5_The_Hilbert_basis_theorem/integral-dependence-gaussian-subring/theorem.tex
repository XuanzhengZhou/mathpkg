Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a Gaussian subring of a field $\mathfrak{J}$ and let $\mathfrak{J}_0$ be the subfield of $\mathfrak{J}$ generated by $\mathfrak{g}$. An element $a \in \mathfrak{J}$ is integrally dependent on $\mathfrak{g}$ if and only if $a$ is algebraic over $\mathfrak{J}_0$ and its minimum polynomial over $\mathfrak{J}_0$ has coefficients in $\mathfrak{g}$.
