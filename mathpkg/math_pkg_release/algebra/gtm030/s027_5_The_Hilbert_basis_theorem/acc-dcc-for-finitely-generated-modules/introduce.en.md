Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a ring satisfying the ascending (or descending) chain condition for left ideals. This theorem asserts that the same chain condition is inherited by any finitely generated unitary module over $\mathfrak{A}$. The proof uses the $J_j$ ideal construction: for a fixed set of generators $x_1, \dots, x_r$ and an ascending chain of submodules, one considers the corresponding chains of left ideals $\mathfrak{J}_j$, which must stabilize by hypothesis, and the stabilization of the submodule chain follows from Lemma 1.
