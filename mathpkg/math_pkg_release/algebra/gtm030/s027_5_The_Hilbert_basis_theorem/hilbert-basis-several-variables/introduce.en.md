This corollary extends the Hilbert Basis Theorem from a single indeterminate to finitely many indeterminates. If $\mathfrak{A}$ is a ring with identity in which every left ideal is finitely generated (i.e., $\mathfrak{A}$ is left Noetherian), then the polynomial ring $\mathfrak{A}[x_1, \dots, x_r]$ in $r$ variables is also left Noetherian. The proof follows by induction on $r$, applying the Hilbert Basis Theorem at each step via the isomorphism $\mathfrak{A}[x_1, \dots, x_r] \cong (\mathfrak{A}[x_1, \dots, x_{r-1}])[x_r]$.
