Prove that $\mathfrak{R}(\mathfrak{B}_1 \cap \mathfrak{B}_2) = \mathfrak{R}(\mathfrak{B}_1) \cap \mathfrak{R}(\mathfrak{B}_2)$ for any ideals $\mathfrak{B}_1$, $\mathfrak{B}_2$ in a commutative ring, where $\mathfrak{R}(\mathfrak{B})$ denotes the radical of $\mathfrak{B}$.
