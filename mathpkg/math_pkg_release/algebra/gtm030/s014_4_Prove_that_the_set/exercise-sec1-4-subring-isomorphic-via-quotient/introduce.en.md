This exercise completes the Section 1 study of ring extensions by showing that after factoring out the annihilator ideal $\mathfrak{Z}$, the original integral domain $\mathfrak{A}$ embeds as a subring of the quotient $\mathfrak{B}/\mathfrak{Z}$. Together with Exercise 3, this establishes that any integral domain can be imbedded in an integral domain with identity, providing a refinement of Theorem 1.
