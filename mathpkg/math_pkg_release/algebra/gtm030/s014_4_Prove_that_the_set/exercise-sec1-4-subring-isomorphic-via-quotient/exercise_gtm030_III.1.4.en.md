Prove that the set $\overline{\mathfrak{A}}$ of cosets of the form $a + \mathfrak{Z}$, where $a \in \mathfrak{A}$, is a subring of $\mathfrak{B}/\mathfrak{Z}$ isomorphic to $\mathfrak{A}$. Hence $\mathfrak{A}$ is imbedded in $\mathfrak{B}/\mathfrak{Z}$.
