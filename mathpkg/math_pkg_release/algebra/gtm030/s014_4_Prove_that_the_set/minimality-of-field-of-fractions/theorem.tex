The field of fractions $\mathfrak{F}$ constructed from a commutative integral domain $\mathfrak{A}$ is a minimal field containing the image $\overline{\mathfrak{A}}$ of $\mathfrak{A}$. Every element $a/b \in \mathfrak{F}$ can be written as $\overline{a} \cdot \overline{b}^{-1}$, i.e., as a quotient of elements in the isomorphic copy of $\mathfrak{A}$ in $\mathfrak{F}$.
