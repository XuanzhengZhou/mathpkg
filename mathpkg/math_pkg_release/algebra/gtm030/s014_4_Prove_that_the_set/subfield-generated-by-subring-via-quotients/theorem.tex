Let $\mathfrak{F}$ be a field and let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a subring $\neq 0$ of $\mathfrak{F}$. Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be the subfield of $\mathfrak{F}$ generated by $\mathfrak{A}$ (i.e., the intersection of all subfields of $\mathfrak{F}$ containing $\mathfrak{A}$). Then

$$\mathfrak{G} = \{ab^{-1} \mid a, b \in \mathfrak{A}, b \neq 0\}.$$

Consequently, every element of the subfield generated by $\mathfrak{A}$ can be expressed as a quotient $ab^{-1}$ with $a, b \in \mathfrak{A}$ and $b \neq 0$.
