Show that, if $\mathfrak{A}$ is a field, then $\mathfrak{F} = \overline{\mathfrak{A}}$, where $\mathfrak{F}$ is the field of fractions of $\mathfrak{A}$ constructed in the text and $\overline{\mathfrak{A}}$ is the isomorphic image of $\mathfrak{A}$ in $\mathfrak{F}$ under the mapping $a \mapsto \overline{a} = ab/b$.
