Prove that the set $\mathfrak{A}$ of cosets of the form $a + \mathfrak{Z}$, $a$ in $\mathfrak{A}$, is a subring of $\mathfrak{B}/\mathfrak{Z}$ isomorphic to $\mathfrak{A}$. Hence $\mathfrak{A}$ is imbedded in $\mathfrak{B}/\mathfrak{Z}$.
