Any element $a/b \in \mathfrak{F}$ can be written as $(ab/b)(b/b^2)^{-1} = \bar{a}\bar{b}^{-1}$, where $\bar{a}, \bar{b} \in \bar{\mathfrak{A}}$. Hence $\mathfrak{F}$ is contained in the subfield generated by $\bar{\mathfrak{A}}$, so it is equal to that subfield. In particular, $\mathfrak{F}$ is a minimal field extension of $\bar{\mathfrak{A}}$.
