Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be any ring and let $\mathfrak{E}$ be the ring of endomorphisms of the additive group $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$. The \textbf{ring of right multiplications} of $\mathfrak{A}$ is the set

$$\mathfrak{A}_r = \{a_r \mid a \in \mathfrak{A}\}$$

where $a_r$ is the right multiplication $x \mapsto xa$. The set $\mathfrak{A}_r$ is a subring of $\mathfrak{E}$, being the image of the ring homomorphism $a \mapsto a_r$.
