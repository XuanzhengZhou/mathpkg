Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be any ring. The kernel of the anti-homomorphism $a \mapsto a_l$ from $\mathfrak{A}$ into the endomorphism ring $\mathfrak{E}$ of $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$ is the set

$$\mathfrak{Z}_l = \{ a \in \mathfrak{A} \mid ax = 0 \text{ for all } x \in \mathfrak{A} \},$$

the ideal of left annihilators of $\mathfrak{A}$. If $\mathfrak{A}$ has an identity, then $\mathfrak{Z}_l = 0$ and $a \mapsto a_l$ is an anti-isomorphism.
