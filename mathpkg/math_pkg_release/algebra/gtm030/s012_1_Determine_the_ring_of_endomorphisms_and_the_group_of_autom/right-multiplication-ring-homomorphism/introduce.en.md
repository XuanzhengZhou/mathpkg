The map $a \mapsto a_r$ sending each ring element to its right multiplication is a ring homomorphism into the endomorphism ring $\mathfrak{E}$ of the additive group. This follows from the distributive and associative laws: right multiplication respects both addition and multiplication of ring elements. The image of this homomorphism is the ring of right multiplications $\mathfrak{A}_r$, a subring of $\mathfrak{E}$.
