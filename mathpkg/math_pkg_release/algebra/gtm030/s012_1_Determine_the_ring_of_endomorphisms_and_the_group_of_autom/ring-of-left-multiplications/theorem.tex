Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be any ring and let $\mathfrak{E}$ be the ring of endomorphisms of the additive group $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$. The \textbf{ring of left multiplications} of $\mathfrak{A}$ is the set

$$\mathfrak{A}_l = \{a_l \mid a \in \mathfrak{A}\}$$

where $a_l$ is the left multiplication $x \mapsto ax$. The set $\mathfrak{A}_l$ is a subring of $\mathfrak{E}$, being the image of the anti-homomorphism $a \mapsto a_l$. If $\mathfrak{A}$ has an identity, then $\mathfrak{Z}_l = 0$ and $a \mapsto a_l$ is an anti-isomorphism.
