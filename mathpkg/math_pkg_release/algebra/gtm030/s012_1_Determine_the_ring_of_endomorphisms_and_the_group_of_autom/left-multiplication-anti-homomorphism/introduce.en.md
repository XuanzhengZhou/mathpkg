The left multiplication map $a \mapsto a_l$ is not a homomorphism but an anti-homomorphism: it reverses the order of multiplication, sending $ab$ to $b_l a_l$ rather than $a_l b_l$. This reflects the fundamental asymmetry between left and right multiplication in a non-commutative ring. The image is still a subring $\mathfrak{A}_l$ of the endomorphism ring $\mathfrak{E}$.
