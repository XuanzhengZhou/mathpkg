Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be any ring and let $\mathfrak{E}$ be the ring of endomorphisms of the additive group $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$. The left multiplications $a_l$, defined by $x a_l = ax$, satisfy

$$(a + b)_l = a_l + b_l, \qquad (ab)_l = b_l a_l.$$

Thus the correspondence $a \mapsto a_l$ is an anti-homomorphism of the ring $\mathfrak{A}$ into $\mathfrak{E}$.
