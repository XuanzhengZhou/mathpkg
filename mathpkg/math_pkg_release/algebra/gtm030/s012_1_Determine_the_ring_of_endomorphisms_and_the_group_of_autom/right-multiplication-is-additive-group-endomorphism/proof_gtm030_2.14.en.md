By the right distributive law in the ring $\mathfrak{A}$, for any $x, y \in \mathfrak{A}$ we have

$$(x + y) a_r = (x + y)a = xa + ya = x a_r + y a_r.$$

This establishes that $a_r$ respects the group operation of $(\mathfrak{A}, +)$, so $a_r$ is an endomorphism of the additive group.
