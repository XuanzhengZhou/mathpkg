If $\mathfrak{F}$ is an infinite field and $f(x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_r)$ is a non-zero polynomial in the polynomial domain $\mathfrak{F}[x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_r]$ with $x_i$ algebraically independent, then there exist elements $c_1, c_2, \cdots, c_r \in \mathfrak{F}$ such that $f(c_1, c_2, \cdots, c_r) \neq 0$.
