Let $[x]$ be a cyclic group of order $s$, and $[y]$ a cyclic group of order $t$. Show that there is a homomorphism $\eta$ of $[x]$ into $[y]$, such that $x\eta = y^k$, if and only if $sk$ is a multiple of $t$. If $sk = mt$, show that $\eta$ is an isomorphism if and only if $(s,m) = 1$.
