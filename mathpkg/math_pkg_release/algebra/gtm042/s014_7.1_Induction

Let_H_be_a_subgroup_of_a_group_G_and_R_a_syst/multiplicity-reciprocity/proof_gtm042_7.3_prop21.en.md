This follows from Theorem 13 (Frobenius Reciprocity), applied to the character $\psi$ of $W$ and to the character $\varphi$ of $E$. By the orthogonality relations for irreducible characters, the multiplicity of $W$ in $\operatorname{Res} E$ is precisely $\langle \psi, \operatorname{Res} \varphi \rangle_H$, and the multiplicity of $E$ in $\operatorname{Ind} W$ is $\langle \operatorname{Ind} \psi, \varphi \rangle_G$. Frobenius Reciprocity asserts these two inner products are equal. One may also apply the Hom-space formulation: $\dim \operatorname{Hom}^H(W, \operatorname{Res} E) = \dim \operatorname{Hom}^G(\operatorname{Ind} W, E)$, and for irreducible representations these dimensions are exactly the multiplicities.
