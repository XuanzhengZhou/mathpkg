Proposition 18 provides an alternative characterization of induced representations using the formalism of tensor products. The natural map $i$ from the scalar extension $\mathbb{C}[G] \otimes_{\mathbb{C}[H]} W$ to $V$ is a $\mathbb{C}[G]$-module homomorphism, and $V$ is induced by $W$ precisely when $i$ is an isomorphism. This reformulation makes the existence and uniqueness of the induced representation obvious, and it connects the theory of induced representations to the fundamental algebraic operation of extension of scalars.
