Let $X$ be the set of irreducible characters of $G$ over $\mathbb{Q}(m)$, and let $Y$ be the set of conjugacy classes of $G$. Let the group $\Gamma_Q = (\mathbb{Z}/m\mathbb{Z})^*$ act on $X$ by $\chi \mapsto \sigma_t(\chi)$ and on $Y$ by $x \mapsto x^t$.

Show that the $\Gamma_Q$-sets $X$ and $Y$ are weakly isomorphic (cf. Exercise 13.5).
