Let $G$ be a finite group of order $g$, and let $m$ be a multiple of the orders of all the elements of $G$. Let $Q(m)$ be the field obtained by adjoining the $m$th roots of unity to $Q$. Then the Galois group $\Gamma_Q$ of $Q(m)$ over $Q$ is the full group of units modulo $m$:

$$\Gamma_Q = (\mathbb{Z}/m\mathbb{Z})^*.$$

Equivalently, the $m$th cyclotomic polynomial $\Phi_m$ is irreducible over $Q$.
