Let $H$ be a subgroup of $G$. If $E$ is a $k[H]$-module, then

$$\text{Ind}\,E = k[G] \otimes_{k[H]} E$$

is a $k[G]$-module (said to be induced by $E$), which is projective if $E$ is projective. Hence we have homomorphisms

$$\text{Ind}_H^G: R_k(H) \rightarrow R_k(G) \quad \text{and} \quad \text{Ind}_H^G: P_k(H) \rightarrow P_k(G).$$
