The ramification function $i_G$ is defined on a finite Galois extension $F/E$ of a complete discretely valued field with equal residue fields. For a non-identity element $s \in G = \operatorname{Gal}(F/E)$, the value $i_G(s)$ measures the valuation of the difference between a prime element $\pi$ of $F$ and its Galois translate $s(\pi)$. This function is the foundation for constructing the Artin and Swan characters of the Galois group.
