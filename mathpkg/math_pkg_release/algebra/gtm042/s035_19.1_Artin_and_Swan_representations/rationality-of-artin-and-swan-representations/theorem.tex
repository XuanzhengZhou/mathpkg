Let $l$ be a prime number unequal to the residue characteristic of $E$.

(i) The representations of Artin and Swan are realizable over the field $\mathbb{Q}_l$ of $l$-adic numbers.

(ii) There exists a projective $\mathbb{Z}_l[G]$-module $\mathrm{Sw}_G$, unique up to isomorphism, such that $\mathbb{Q}_l \otimes \mathrm{Sw}_G$ has character $sw_G$.
