Let $E$ be a field complete with respect to a discrete valuation, let $F/E$ be a finite Galois extension with Galois group $G$, and assume $E$ and $F$ have the same residue field. Let $a_G$ be the Artin character of $G$.

**Theorem.** The function $a_G$ is the character of a representation of $G$ (over a sufficiently large field).

Equivalently, if $\chi$ is any character of $G$, then the number

$$f(\chi) = \langle a_G, \chi \rangle$$

is a non-negative integer.
