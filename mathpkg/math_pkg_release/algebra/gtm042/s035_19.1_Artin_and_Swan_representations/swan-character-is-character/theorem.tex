Let $G$ be the Galois group of a finite Galois extension $F/E$ of a complete discretely valued field with equal residue fields. Let $r_G$ be the character of the regular representation of $G$, $u_G = r_G - 1$, and let $sw_G = a_G - u_G$ be the Swan character.

**Proposition.** The function $sw_G$ is the character of a representation of $G$. Equivalently, for every character $\chi$ of $G$, the scalar product $\langle sw_G, \chi \rangle$ is a non-negative integer.
