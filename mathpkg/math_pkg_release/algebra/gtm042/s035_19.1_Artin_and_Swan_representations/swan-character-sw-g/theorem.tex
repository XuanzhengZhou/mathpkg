Let $G$ be the Galois group of a finite Galois extension $F/E$ of a complete discretely valued field with equal residue fields. Let $r_G$ be the character of the regular representation of $G$, and put $u_G = r_G - 1$. Define the Swan character $sw_G$ by

$$sw_G = a_G - u_G.$$

Then $sw_G$ has the following explicit description:

$$sw_G(s) = 1 - i_G(s) \quad \text{if } s \neq 1,$$

$$sw_G(1) = \sum_{s \neq 1} (i_G(s) - 1).$$
