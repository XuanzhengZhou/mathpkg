Lemma 19 is the final auxiliary result needed to complete the proof of Brauer's theorem (Theorem 28) in Serre's "Linear Representations of Finite Groups". It asserts the existence of a class function $\varphi$ in $A \otimes V_{K,p}$ (the $p$-adic completion of the space of $A$-valued class functions) that is nonzero modulo every prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}_i$ of $A$ containing $p$, when evaluated at any element $x \in G$. This function is constructed using the properties established in Lemma 17 for a system of representatives of the $p'$-regular $\Gamma_K$-classes, combined with Lemma 16 which extends the non-vanishing property from $p'$-elements to all elements of $G$.
