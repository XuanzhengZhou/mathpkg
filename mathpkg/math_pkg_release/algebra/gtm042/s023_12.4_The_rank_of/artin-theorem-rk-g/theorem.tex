Let $G$ be a finite group, $K$ a field of characteristic zero, and $T$ the set of cyclic subgroups of $G$. Then the map

$$\mathbb{Q} \otimes \operatorname{Ind} : \bigoplus_{H \in T} \mathbb{Q} \otimes R_K(H) \rightarrow \mathbb{Q} \otimes R_K(G)$$

defined by the maps $\mathbb{Q} \otimes \operatorname{Ind}_H^G$, $H \in T$, is surjective.
