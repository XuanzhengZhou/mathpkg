Let $\chi_1, \ldots, \chi_h$ be the distinct irreducible characters of $G$ with corresponding representations $W_1, \ldots, W_h$. Let $V = U_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus U_m$ be any decomposition into irreducible representations. For $i = 1, \ldots, h$, let $V_i$ be the direct sum of those $U_j$ isomorphic to $W_i$. Then

$$V = V_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus V_h$$

is called the \textbf{canonical decomposition} of $V$. Each $V_i$ is the \textbf{isotypic component} of type $W_i$.
