This corollary reveals a deep connection between an irreducible representation and the regular representation: each irreducible $W_i$ appears exactly $n_i = \dim W_i$ times in the regular representation. This follows from computing $(r_G|\chi_i) = \frac{1}{g} \cdot g \cdot \chi_i(1) = n_i$. It implies the fundamental relation $\sum n_i^2 = g$.
