Corollary 1 to Theorem 34 extends the split injectivity result to finite extensions of the characteristic-zero field. It states that the composition of $e: P_k(G) \to R_K(G)$ with the natural map $R_K(G) \to R_{K'}(G)$ induced by extension of scalars is still a split injection, for any finite extension $K'$ of $K$. This is an immediate consequence of the proof of Theorem 34.
