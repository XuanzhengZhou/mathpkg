Proposition 5 gives the character of the regular representation: it is $g$ at the identity and 0 elsewhere. This is because for $s \neq 1$, the matrix of $\rho_s$ in the natural basis has all zeros on the diagonal (since $st \neq t$ for all $t$). This simple formula makes the regular representation a powerful tool for decomposing characters.
