**Proof of Corollary 3.**

By Theorem 42, the irreducible modular characters $\phi_E$ ($E \in S_k$) form a basis of the $K$-vector space $\mathcal{C}(G_{\text{reg}}, K)$ of class functions on $G_{\text{reg}}$ with values in $K$. Therefore,
$$\dim_K \mathcal{C}(G_{\text{reg}}, K) = |S_k|.$$

On the other hand, a class function on $G_{\text{reg}}$ is by definition a function constant on each $p$-regular conjugacy class. Hence the dimension of $\mathcal{C}(G_{\text{reg}}, K)$ equals the number of $p$-regular conjugacy classes of $G$.

Equating the two expressions for the dimension yields $|S_k| = \#\{\text{$p$-regular conjugacy classes of } G\}$, as claimed. $\square$
