The Brauer character is additive with respect to short exact sequences of $k[G]$-modules. This property follows from the fact that in an exact sequence, the eigenvalues of the middle term are the union of the eigenvalues of the outer terms (with multiplicities), and the lift of eigenvalues commutes with taking sums.
