Let $G$ be a finite group and let $p$ be a prime number (the characteristic of the residue field $k$). The set of $p$-regular elements of $G$, denoted $G_{\text{reg}}$, is the set of all elements of $G$ whose order is prime to $p$.

Equivalently, $s \in G_{\text{reg}}$ if and only if $p \nmid |s|$ (the order of $s$ is not divisible by $p$).
