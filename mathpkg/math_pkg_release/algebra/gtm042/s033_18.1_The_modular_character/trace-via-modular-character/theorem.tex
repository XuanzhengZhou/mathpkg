Let $t \in G$ have $p'$-component $s \in G_{\text{reg}}$ (i.e., $t = su$ where $u$ is a $p$-element and $su = us$). Then the trace of the endomorphism $t_E$ of a $k[G]$-module $E$ is the reduction modulo $\mathfrak{m}$ of $\phi_E(s)$:
$$\operatorname{Tr}(t_E) = \overline{\phi_E(s)},$$
where the bar denotes reduction modulo $\mathfrak{m}$.
