The Brauer character of a tensor product of two $k[G]$-modules is the pointwise product of their Brauer characters. This follows because the eigenvalues of the tensor product are the pairwise products of eigenvalues of the factors, and the lifting map $\lambda \mapsto \tilde{\lambda}$ is a group homomorphism from $\mu_k$ to $\mu_K$.
