Prove that the irreducible representations of degree 2 of $\mathfrak{A}_5$ in characteristic 2 are realizable over the field $\mathbb{F}_4$ of 4 elements; obtain from this an isomorphism of $\mathfrak{A}_5$ with $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{F}_4)$.
