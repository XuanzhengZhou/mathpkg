Let $X$ be a finite set on which $G$ acts, and let $\rho$ be the corresponding permutation representation with character $\chi$. Let $c$ be the number of distinct orbits of $G$ on $X$. Then $c$ is equal to the number of times that $\rho$ contains the unit representation $1$, i.e.,

$$(\chi|1) = \frac{1}{g} \sum_{s \in G} \chi(s) = c.$$

In particular, if $G$ is transitive ($c = 1$), then $\rho = 1 \oplus \theta$ where $\theta$ does not contain the unit representation, and $(\psi|1) = 0$ where $\psi$ is the character of $\theta$.
