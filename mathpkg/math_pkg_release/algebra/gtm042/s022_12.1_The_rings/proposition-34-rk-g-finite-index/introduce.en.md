Proposition 34 establishes that the inclusion $R_K(G) \subset \overline{R}_K(G)$ is of finite index. Every irreducible complex representation can be realized over some finite extension $L/K$, and by scalar restriction one obtains the bound via Lemma 12. The precise index is given by the product of Schur indices (Proposition 35), refining this qualitative statement.
