The cyclic group $C_n$ of order $n$ has $n$ irreducible representations, all of degree 1.
If $r$ is a generator with $r^n = 1$, the characters $\chi_h$ ($h = 0, 1, \ldots, n-1$) are given by
$$\chi_h(r^k) = e^{2\pi i hk/n}.$$
We have $\chi_h \cdot \chi_{h'} = \chi_{h+h'}$ with indices taken modulo $n$.
