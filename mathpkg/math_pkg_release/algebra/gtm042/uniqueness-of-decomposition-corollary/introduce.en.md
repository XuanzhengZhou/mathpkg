Corollary 1 establishes the essential uniqueness of the decomposition of a representation into irreducibles. While the actual irreducible summands are not unique (only unique up to isomorphism), the multiplicity with which each isomorphism type appears is an invariant of the representation, computable via the inner product $(\phi|\chi)$.
