The group $\mathfrak{S}_4$ has a normal subgroup $N$ of order 4 such that $\mathfrak{S}_4/N$ is isomorphic to $\mathfrak{S}_3$. Show that $N$ acts trivially in each irreducible representation of $\mathfrak{S}_4$ in characteristic 2. Use this to classify such representations.
