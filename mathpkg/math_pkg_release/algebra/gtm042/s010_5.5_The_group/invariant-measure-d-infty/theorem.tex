The invariant measure on the infinite dihedral group $D_{\infty}$ is given by $\frac{d\alpha}{4\pi}$ on each connected component. More precisely, for any integrable function $f$ on $D_{\infty}$, the Haar integral is
$$
\int_{G} f(t)\,dt = \frac{1}{4\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} f(r_{\alpha})\,d\alpha + \frac{1}{4\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} f(s r_{\alpha})\,d\alpha.
$$
