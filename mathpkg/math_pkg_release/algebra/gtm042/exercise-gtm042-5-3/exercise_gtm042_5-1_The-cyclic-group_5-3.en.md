Show that the usual realization of $D_n$ as a group of rigid motions in $\mathbb{R}^3$ (Eyring [5]) is reducible and has character $\chi_1 + \psi_2$, and that the realization of $D_n$ as $C_{nv}$ (loc. cit.) has $\chi_1 + \psi_1$ for its character.
