This corollary is a profound consequence of the orthogonality relations: the character is a complete invariant of a representation up to isomorphism. Since the multiplicities of all irreducible representations are determined by scalar products with the character, two representations with identical characters must contain each irreducible representation the same number of times, hence be isomorphic.
