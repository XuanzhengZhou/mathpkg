Let $V$ be a linear representation of a finite group $G$, with character $\phi$, and suppose $V$ decomposes into a direct sum of irreducible representations:
$$V = W_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus W_k.$$
Then, if $W$ is an irreducible representation with character $\chi$, the number of $W_i$ isomorphic to $W$ is equal to the scalar product $(\phi|\chi)$.
