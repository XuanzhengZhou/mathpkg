This corollary extends the Krull–Schmidt–Azumaya type decomposition theorem from the residue field case ($k[G]$-modules) to the $p$-adic setting ($A[G]$-modules, where $A$ is a complete discrete valuation ring). Every projective $A[G]$-module splits uniquely as a direct sum of projective indecomposable $A[G]$-modules, and these indecomposables are classified by their reduction modulo the maximal ideal, which yields the projective envelopes of simple $k[G]$-modules. This establishes a bijection between projective indecomposable $A[G]$-modules and simple $k[G]$-modules.
