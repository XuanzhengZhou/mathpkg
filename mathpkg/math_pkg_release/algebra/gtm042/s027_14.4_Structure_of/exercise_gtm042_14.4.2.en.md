Let $\Lambda$ be a commutative ring, and let $P$ be a $\Lambda[G]$-module which is projective over $\Lambda$. Prove the equivalence of the following properties:

(i) $P$ is a projective $\Lambda[G]$-module.

(ii) For each maximal ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ of $\Lambda$, the $(\Lambda/\mathfrak{p})[G]$-module $P/\mathfrak{p}P$ is projective.
