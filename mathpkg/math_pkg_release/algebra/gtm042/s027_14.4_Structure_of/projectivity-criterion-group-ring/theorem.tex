Let $\Lambda$ be a commutative ring, and $P$ a $\Lambda[G]$-module. In order that $P$ be projective over $\Lambda[G]$, it is necessary and sufficient that it be projective over $\Lambda$, and that there exists a $\Lambda$-endomorphism $u$ of $P$ such that

$$\sum_{s \in G} s \cdot u(s^{-1}x) = x \text{ for all } x \in P.$$
