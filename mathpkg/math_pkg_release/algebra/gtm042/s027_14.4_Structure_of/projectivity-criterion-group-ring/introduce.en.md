This lemma (Lemma 20 of Chapter 14) characterizes projective modules over a group ring $\Lambda[G]$ when the base ring $\Lambda$ is commutative. A $\Lambda[G]$-module $P$ is projective precisely when it is already projective as a $\Lambda$-module and admits a $\Lambda$-linear endomorphism $u$ satisfying a certain averaging identity $\sum s \cdot u(s^{-1}x) = x$. This condition generalizes the classical criterion for projectivity of $k[G]$-modules and provides a bridge between $\Lambda$-linear and $\Lambda[G]$-linear structure.
