Let $N = [K' : K] = [A' : A]$. Let $E'$ be a projective $A'[G]$-module with image $nx$ in $R_{K'}(G)$, and let $E$ be the $A[G]$-module obtained from $E'$ by restriction to $A[G]$. One checks easily that the character of $E \otimes_A K$ is $N$ times the character of $x$. Using condition (a) and the theory of Schur indices, one deduces that $x$ is the image of a projective $A[G]$-module, which is uniquely determined up to isomorphism.
