(i) The decomposition $V = V_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus V_h$ into isotypic components does not depend on the initially chosen decomposition of $V$ into irreducible representations.

(ii) The projection $p_i$ of $V$ onto $V_i$ is given by the formula:

$$p_i = \frac{n_i}{g} \sum_{t \in G} \chi_i(t)^* \rho_t$$

where $n_i = \dim W_i$ and $g = |G|$.
