The vector space of an induced representation decomposes as a direct sum of subspaces $\rho_r W$, each isomorphic to the original $H$-module $W$, indexed by a system of coset representatives $R$. This yields the dimension formula $\dim(V) = (G:H) \cdot \dim(W)$, showing that induction multiplies dimension by the index of the subgroup.
