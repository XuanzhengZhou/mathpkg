Let $G$ be a finite group and $H$ a subgroup. Let $V$ be the regular representation of $G$, with basis $(e_t)_{t \in G}$ and action $\rho_s e_t = e_{st}$ for $s, t \in G$. Let $W$ be the subspace of $V$ with basis $(e_t)_{t \in H}$. Then the representation $\theta$ of $H$ in $W$ is the regular representation of $H$, and $\rho$ is induced by $\theta$.
