Congruence modulo a subgroup $H$ is the equivalence relation on a group $G$ defined by belonging to the same left coset. The condition $s^{-1}s' \in H$ provides an explicit algebraic criterion for checking when two group elements are congruent. This equivalence partitions $G$ into the left cosets $G/H$, which serve as the indexing set in the construction of induced representations.
