A system of representatives $R$ for the coset space $G/H$ is a transversal --- a set containing exactly one element from each left coset. In the theory of induced representations, such a system provides a decomposition of the representation space $V$ as a direct sum $\bigoplus_{r \in R} \rho_r W$, with each summand isomorphic to the original $H$-module $W$.
