\textbf{Frobenius Reciprocity.} Let $H \leq G$ be finite groups. Let $f$ be a class function on $G$ and let $f_H$ be its restriction to $H$. Let $\theta$ be a representation of $H$ with character $\chi_\theta$, and let $\rho$ be the induced representation of $G$ with character $\chi_\rho$. Then
$$\left( f_H \mid \chi_\theta \right)_H = \left( f \mid \chi_\rho \right)_G,$$
where the scalar products are calculated on $H$ and $G$ respectively.
