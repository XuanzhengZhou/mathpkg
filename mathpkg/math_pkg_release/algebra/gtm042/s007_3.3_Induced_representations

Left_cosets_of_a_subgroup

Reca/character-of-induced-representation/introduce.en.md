The character of an induced representation can be computed from the character of the original subgroup representation via a summation over coset representatives. The formula involves only those representatives $r$ for which $r^{-1}ur$ belongs to $H$, reflecting the block-diagonal structure of $\rho(u)$ in the direct sum decomposition $V = \bigoplus_{r \in R} \rho_r W$. The equivalent averaging formula extends the sum to all elements of $G$.
