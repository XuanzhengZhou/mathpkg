A subrepresentation is a vector subspace $W \subseteq V$ that is invariant under the group action. This means that applying any $\rho_s$ to any vector in $W$ stays within $W$. Subrepresentations are the natural substructures in representation theory, analogous to subgroups in group theory or subspaces in linear algebra.
