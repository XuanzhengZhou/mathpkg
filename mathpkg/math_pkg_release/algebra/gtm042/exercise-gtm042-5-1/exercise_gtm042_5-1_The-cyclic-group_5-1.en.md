Show that in $D_n$, $n$ even (resp. odd), the reflections form two conjugacy classes (resp. one), and that the elements of $C_n$ form $(n/2) + 1$ classes (resp. $(n+1)/2$ classes). Obtain from this the number of classes of $D_n$ and check that it coincides with the number of irreducible characters.
