The invariant $b(M)$ can be expressed as the scalar product of the modular character $\phi_M$ with the Swan character $sw_G$. This formula, analogous to the orthogonality relations for ordinary characters, involves a sum over the regular elements $G_{\text{reg}}$ of the group, as described in Section 18.1, formula (viii).
