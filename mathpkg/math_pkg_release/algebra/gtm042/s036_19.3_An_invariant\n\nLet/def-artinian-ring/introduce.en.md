An artinian ring is a ring satisfying the descending chain condition on left ideals. This finiteness condition is equivalent to the left regular module having finite length, or to every finitely generated left module having finite length. Artinian rings generalize finite-dimensional algebras and play a central role in the structure theory of noncommutative rings.
