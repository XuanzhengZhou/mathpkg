For an artinian ring $A$, the Jacobson radical $\mathfrak{r}$ is nilpotent and the quotient $A/\mathfrak{r}$ is a semisimple ring. By the Wedderburn-Artin theorem, this semisimple quotient decomposes as a product of simple matrix algebras over division rings. Consequently, the simple modules over $A$ are precisely the simple modules over these matrix components, classified up to isomorphism by the $E_i$.
