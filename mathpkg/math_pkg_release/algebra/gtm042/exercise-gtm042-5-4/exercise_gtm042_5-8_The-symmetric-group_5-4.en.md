Set $\theta(1) = \theta(x) = 1$ and $\theta(y) = \theta(z) = -1$; this is a representation of degree 1 of H. The representation of $\mathfrak{A}_4$ induced by $\theta$ (cf. 3.3) is of degree 3; show that it is irreducible and has character $\psi$.
