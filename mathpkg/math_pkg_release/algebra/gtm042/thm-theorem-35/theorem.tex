Let $p^n$ be the largest power of $p$ dividing the order of $G$. If $\phi$ is a modular character of $G$, and if $\Phi$ is defined by the formula

$$\Phi(s) = p^n \phi(s) \quad \text{if } s \in G_{\text{reg}}$$

$$\Phi(s) = 0 \quad \text{if } s \notin G_{\text{reg}}$$

then $\Phi$ is a virtual character of $G$.

We leave to the reader the task of making further reformulations of this type.
