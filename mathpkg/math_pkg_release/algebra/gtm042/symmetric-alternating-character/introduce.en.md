Proposition 3 gives explicit formulas for the characters of the symmetric and alternating squares in terms of the original character $\chi$. These formulas are fundamental tools for decomposing tensor squares and analyzing representations. The sum $\chi_\sigma^2 + \chi_\alpha^2 = \chi^2$ corresponds to the decomposition of $V \otimes V$.
