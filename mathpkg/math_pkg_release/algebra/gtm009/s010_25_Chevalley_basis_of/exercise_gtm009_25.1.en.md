Prove Proposition 25.1(c): If $\alpha + \beta \in \Phi$, then
$$r + 1 = \frac{q(\alpha + \beta, \alpha + \beta)}{(\beta, \beta)},$$
where $\beta - r\alpha, \ldots, \beta, \ldots, \beta + q\alpha$ is the $\alpha$-string through $\beta$.
