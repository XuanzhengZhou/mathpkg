Keep the notation of Exercise 8. Let $M$ be any admissible lattice, $M_{\max} \supset M \supset M_{\min}$. Then $M$ has a $Z$-basis $(z_0, \ldots, z_\lambda)$ with $z_i = a_i w_i$ ($a_i \in \mathbf{Z}$), $a_0 = a_\lambda = 1$. Define integers $b_i$.
