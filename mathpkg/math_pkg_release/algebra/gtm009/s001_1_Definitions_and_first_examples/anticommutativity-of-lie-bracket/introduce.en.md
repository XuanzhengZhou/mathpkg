The anticommutativity property $[xy] = -[yx]$ is a direct consequence of bilinearity and the condition $[xx] = 0$. It is derived by expanding $[x+y, x+y] = 0$. In characteristic not equal to 2, anticommutativity is equivalent to $[xx] = 0$.
