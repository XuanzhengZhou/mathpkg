This exercise asks to verify that $\mathbf{R}^3$ with the cross product as bracket satisfies the Lie algebra axioms (L1)-(L3). Since the cross product is bilinear, anticommutative, and satisfies the Jacobi identity $x \times (y \times z) + y \times (z \times x) + z \times (x \times y) = 0$, the structure forms a Lie algebra isomorphic to $\mathfrak{so}(3)$.
