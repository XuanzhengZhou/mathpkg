Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an $F$-algebra. A \text{derivation} of $\mathfrak{A}$ is a linear map $\delta: \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{A}$ satisfying the product rule
$$\delta(ab) = a\delta(b) + \delta(a)b$$
for all $a, b \in \mathfrak{A}$.

The collection $\operatorname{Der} \mathfrak{A}$ of all derivations of $\mathfrak{A}$ is a vector subspace of $\operatorname{End} \mathfrak{A}$.
