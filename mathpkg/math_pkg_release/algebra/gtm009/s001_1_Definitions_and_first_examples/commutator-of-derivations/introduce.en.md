The commutator of two derivations is again a derivation, which makes the vector space of all derivations $\operatorname{Der} \mathfrak{A}$ a Lie subalgebra of $\mathfrak{gl}(\mathfrak{A})$. This is an important source of Lie algebras: many classical Lie algebras arise as derivation algebras of suitable non-associative algebras.
