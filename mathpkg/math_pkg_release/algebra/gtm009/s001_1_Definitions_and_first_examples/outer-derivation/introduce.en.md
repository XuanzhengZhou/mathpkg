An outer derivation is a derivation that cannot be expressed as the adjoint action of any element of the Lie algebra. The quotient of all derivations by inner derivations is the first Lie algebra cohomology group $H^1(L, L)$. Semisimple Lie algebras in characteristic zero have no outer derivations.
