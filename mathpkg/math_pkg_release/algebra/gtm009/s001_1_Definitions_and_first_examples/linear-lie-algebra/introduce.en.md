A linear Lie algebra is any Lie subalgebra of the general linear algebra $\mathfrak{gl}(V)$. By Ado's theorem, every finite-dimensional Lie algebra over a field of characteristic zero is isomorphic to a linear Lie algebra. This makes the class of linear Lie algebras universal for finite-dimensional Lie algebras in characteristic zero.
