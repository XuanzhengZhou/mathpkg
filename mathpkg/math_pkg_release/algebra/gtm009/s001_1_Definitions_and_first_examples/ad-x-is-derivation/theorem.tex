For a Lie algebra $L$ and $x \in L$, the map $\operatorname{ad} x: L \to L$ defined by $y \mapsto [xy]$ is a derivation of $L$, i.e., $\operatorname{ad} x \in \operatorname{Der} L$.

Equivalently, using (L2') (anticommutativity), the Jacobi identity can be rewritten as
$$[x[yz]] = [[xy]z] + [y[xz]].$$
