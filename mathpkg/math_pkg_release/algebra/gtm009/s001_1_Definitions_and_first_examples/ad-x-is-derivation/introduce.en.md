The map $\operatorname{ad} x$ is always a derivation of the Lie algebra $L$. This fact is equivalent to the Jacobi identity (after applying anticommutativity), and it provides the link between the Lie bracket and the derivation algebra: the adjoint representation maps $L$ into $\operatorname{Der} L$.
