A \text{Borel subalgebra} $B$ of a Lie algebra $L$ is a maximal solvable subalgebra. For a semisimple Lie algebra $L$ over an algebraically closed field of characteristic 0 with Cartan subalgebra $H$, root system $\Phi$, and base $\Delta$, the \text{standard Borel subalgebra} relative to $\Delta$ is
$$B(\Delta) = H \oplus \bigoplus_{\alpha > 0} L_\alpha,$$
where $\alpha > 0$ means $\alpha$ is a positive root relative to $\Delta$. All Borel subalgebras are conjugate under $\mathcal{E}(L)$.
