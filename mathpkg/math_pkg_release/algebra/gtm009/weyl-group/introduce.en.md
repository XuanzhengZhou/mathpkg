The Weyl group is the finite reflection group generated by reflections through hyperplanes orthogonal to roots. It is the symmetry group of the root system and acts simply transitively on the set of bases. The Weyl group controls the conjugacy of Borel subalgebras, the structure of Bruhat decomposition, and the character formula for representations. It is a fundamental Coxeter group.
