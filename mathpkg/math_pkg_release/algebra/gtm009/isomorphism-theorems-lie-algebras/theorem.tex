Let $L$ be a Lie algebra and $I, J$ ideals of $L$.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] If $\phi: L \rightarrow L'$ is a homomorphism of Lie algebras, then $L/\text{Ker } \phi \cong \text{Im } \phi$.
\item[(b)] If $I \subset J$, then $J/I$ is an ideal of $L/I$ and $(L/I)/(J/I) \cong L/J$.
\item[(c)] There is a natural isomorphism $(I+J)/J \cong I/(I \cap J)$.
\end{enumerate}
