Lemma 7.2 provides explicit formulas for the action of the standard basis $(x, h, y)$ of $\mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$ on a canonical basis $\{v_i\}$ constructed from a maximal vector $v_0$. Part (a) shows that $v_i$ is a weight vector of weight $\lambda - 2i$, part (b) gives the lowering action of $y$, and part (c) gives the raising action of $x$. Formula (c) is proved by induction and the commutation relation $[x, y] = h$. These formulas completely determine the structure of any irreducible $\mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$-module.
