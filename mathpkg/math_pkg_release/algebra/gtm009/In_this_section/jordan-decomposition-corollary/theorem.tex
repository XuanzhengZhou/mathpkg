Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra, $\phi: L \rightarrow \mathfrak{gl}(V)$ a finite dimensional representation of $L$. If $x = s + n$ is the abstract Jordan decomposition of $x \in L$ (where $s$ is ad-semisimple, $n$ is ad-nilpotent, and $[s, n] = 0$), then $\phi(x) = \phi(s) + \phi(n)$ is the usual Jordan decomposition of $\phi(x)$ in $\mathfrak{gl}(V)$.
