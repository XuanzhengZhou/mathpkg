This corollary follows after proving that $C_L(H) = H$, which is established through a series of structural arguments in Section 8.2. The nondegeneracy of the Killing form on $H$ allows us to identify $H$ with its dual $H^*$ via the correspondence $\phi \mapsto t_\phi$, where $\phi(h) = \kappa(t_\phi, h)$ for all $h \in H$. This identification is fundamental for the entire theory of root systems.
