For $\mathfrak{sl}(n, \mathbb{F})$ (and other classical algebras), calculate explicitly the root strings and Cartan integers. In particular, prove that all Cartan integers $2(\alpha, \beta)/(\beta, \beta)$, $\alpha \neq \pm \beta$, for $\mathfrak{sl}(n, \mathbb{F})$ are $0, \pm 1$.
