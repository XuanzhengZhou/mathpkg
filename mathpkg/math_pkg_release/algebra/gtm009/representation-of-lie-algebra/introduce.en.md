A representation of a Lie algebra is a Lie algebra homomorphism from $L$ into the general linear algebra of some vector space $V$. This allows abstract Lie algebras to be realized concretely as linear transformations, making the abstract theory amenable to linear algebra techniques. Representations are the primary tool for studying the structure of Lie algebras.
