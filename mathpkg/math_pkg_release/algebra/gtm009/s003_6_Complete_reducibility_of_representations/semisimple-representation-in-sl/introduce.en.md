This lemma states that any representation of a semisimple Lie algebra takes values in the special linear Lie algebra, since semisimple algebras satisfy $L = [L, L]$ and $\mathfrak{sl}(V)$ is the derived algebra of $\mathfrak{gl}(V)$. Consequently, semisimple Lie algebras act trivially on any one-dimensional module.
