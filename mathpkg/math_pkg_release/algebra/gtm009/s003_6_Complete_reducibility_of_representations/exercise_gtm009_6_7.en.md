It will be seen later on that $\mathfrak{sl}(n, \mathbb{F})$ is actually simple. Assuming this and using Exercise 6, prove that the Killing form $\kappa$ on $\mathfrak{sl}(n, \mathbb{F})$ is related to the ordinary trace form by $\kappa(x, y) = 2n \operatorname{Tr}(xy)$.
