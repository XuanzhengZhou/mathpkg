This lemma establishes that the property of being a Cartan subalgebra is preserved under surjective Lie algebra homomorphisms. The image of a CSA under an epimorphism remains nilpotent and self-normalizing in the target algebra. This functorial property is essential for inductive arguments in the proof that all Cartan subalgebras of a solvable Lie algebra are conjugate, as it allows one to pass to quotient algebras.
