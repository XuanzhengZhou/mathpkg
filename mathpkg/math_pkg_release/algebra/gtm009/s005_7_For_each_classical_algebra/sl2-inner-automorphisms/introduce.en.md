For $\mathfrak{sl}(2, F)$, the group $\operatorname{Int} L$ of inner automorphisms is generated by exponentials $\exp(\operatorname{ad}(c x))$ and $\exp(\operatorname{ad}(c y))$ for $c \in F$. The automorphisms $w(c) = x(c)y(-c^{-1})x(c)$ act on the standard basis $(x, h, y)$ and generate, together with $h(c)$, all diagonal automorphisms of $L$. This exercise shows that for the rank-1 simple Lie algebra, the entire automorphism group coincides with the inner automorphism group, unlike in higher rank where outer automorphisms corresponding to graph automorphisms may exist.
