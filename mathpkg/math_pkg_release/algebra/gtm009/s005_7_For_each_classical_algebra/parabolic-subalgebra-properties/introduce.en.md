A parabolic subalgebra of a semisimple Lie algebra $L$ is a subalgebra containing some Borel subalgebra. The standard parabolic subalgebras $P(\Delta')$ are parametrized by subsets $\Delta'$ of a base $\Delta$ of the root system; they are generated by $H$, all positive root spaces $L_\alpha$ ($\alpha > 0$), and the negative root spaces $L_{-\alpha}$ for $\alpha \in \Delta'$. Parts (b) and (c) of this exercise establish that all parabolic subalgebras containing a fixed Borel subalgebra are standard, and that all parabolic subalgebras are conjugate under the group $\mathcal{E}(L)$, generalizing the conjugacy of Borel subalgebras.
