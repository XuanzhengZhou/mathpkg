For a semisimple Lie algebra $L$ over an algebraically closed field of characteristic $0$, all Cartan subalgebras (and all maximal toral subalgebras) are conjugate under the group $\operatorname{Int} L$ of inner automorphisms. This theorem is proved via the broader conjugacy of Cartan subalgebras in arbitrary Lie algebras and Borel subalgebras, then specializes to the semisimple case. The result implies that the root system of a semisimple Lie algebra is uniquely determined up to isomorphism by $L$ alone, independent of the choice of Cartan subalgebra.
