Lemma C of Section 16.3 establishes that all standard Borel subalgebras relative to a fixed Cartan subalgebra $H$ are conjugate under the group $\mathcal{E}(L)$. The proof uses the fact that simple reflections $\sigma_{\alpha}$ of the Weyl group can be lifted to inner automorphisms $\tau_{\alpha}$ in $\mathcal{E}(L)$, and any two bases of the root system are related by a sequence of such reflections. Since $\tau_{\alpha}$ sends $B(\Delta)$ to $B(\sigma_{\alpha}\Delta)$, the transitivity of the Weyl group action on bases yields the result.
