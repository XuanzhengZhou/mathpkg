This exercise asks the reader to verify the low-dimensional isomorphisms among the classical families of Lie algebras $A_\ell$, $B_\ell$, $C_\ell$, and $D_\ell$. For $\ell = 1$, the algebras $A_1$, $B_1$, and $C_1$ coincide, while $D_1$ reduces to the abelian one-dimensional algebra. For $\ell = 2$, the coincidence $B_2 \cong C_2$ reflects the well-known isomorphism between the orthogonal and symplectic Lie algebras in dimension 5 and 4 respectively. The isomorphism $D_3 \cong A_3$ arises from the exceptional identification $\mathfrak{so}(6, F) \cong \mathfrak{sl}(4, F)$.
