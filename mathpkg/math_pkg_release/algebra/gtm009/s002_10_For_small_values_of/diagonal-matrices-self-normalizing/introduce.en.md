This exercise asserts that in each classical Lie algebra ($A_\ell$, $B_\ell$, $C_\ell$, $D_\ell$) over a field of characteristic zero, the subalgebra of diagonal matrices is self-normalizing. These diagonal subalgebras are examples of Cartan subalgebras, and the self-normalizing property is one of the key features that characterizes them in the structure theory of semisimple Lie algebras.
