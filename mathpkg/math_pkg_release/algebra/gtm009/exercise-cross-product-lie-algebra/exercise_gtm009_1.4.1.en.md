Let $L$ be the real vector space $\mathbf{R}^3$. Define $[xy] = x \times y$ (cross product of vectors) for $x, y \in L$. Verify that $L$ is a Lie algebra. Write down the structure constants relative to the usual basis of $\mathbf{R}^3$.
