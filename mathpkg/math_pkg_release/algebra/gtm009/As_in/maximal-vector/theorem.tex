Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra, $H$ a Cartan subalgebra, and $\Delta$ a base of the root system $\Phi$. A \text{maximal vector} (of weight $\lambda$) in an $L$-module $V$ is a nonzero vector $v^+ \in V_\lambda$ such that $x_\alpha \cdot v^+ = 0$ for all $\alpha \succ 0$ (equivalently, for all $\alpha \in \Delta$), where $x_\alpha \in L_\alpha$.
