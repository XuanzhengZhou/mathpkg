The tensor algebra $\mathfrak{T}(V)$ of a vector space $V$ is the free associative algebra on $V$. It is constructed as the direct sum of all tensor powers $T^mV = V^{\otimes m}$, with multiplication given by concatenation of tensors. This graded algebra serves as the universal source for linear maps from $V$ into associative algebras, making it a fundamental construction preparatory to the universal enveloping algebra of a Lie algebra.
