In characteristic zero, the symmetric group $\mathcal{S}_m$ acts on the $m$-th tensor power $T^mV$ by permuting tensor factors. Elements invariant under this action are called homogeneous symmetric tensors. Averaging over the symmetric group yields canonical symmetric tensors associated to each non-decreasing sequence of basis indices. These tensors project onto the basis of $S^mV$ and provide a complement used in later structural results.
