The weight space decomposition generalizes the root space decomposition from the adjoint representation to arbitrary finite-dimensional representations of semisimple Lie algebras. A module decomposes into simultaneous eigenspaces for a Cartan subalgebra. The theory of highest weights establishes a bijection between dominant integral weights and finite-dimensional irreducible representations, completing the representation theory of semisimple Lie algebras.
