Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an associative algebra over $F$, and let $y, z \in \mathfrak{A}$. Then for all $k \geq 0$:

$$[y^k, z] = \binom{k}{1}[y, z]y^{k-1} + \binom{k}{2}[y, [y, z]]y^{k-2} + \cdots + [y, [y, \ldots, [y, z] \ldots]]$$

where the last term is the $k$-fold iterated commutator $[y, [y, \ldots, [y, z] \ldots]]$.
