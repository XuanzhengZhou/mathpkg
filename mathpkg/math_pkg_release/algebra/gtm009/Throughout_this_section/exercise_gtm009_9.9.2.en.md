Prove that $\Phi^\vee$ is a root system in $E$, whose Weyl group is naturally isomorphic to $W$; show also that $\langle \alpha^\vee, \beta^\vee \rangle = \langle \beta, \alpha \rangle$, and draw a picture of $\Phi^\vee$ in the cases $A_1, A_2, B_2, G_2$.
