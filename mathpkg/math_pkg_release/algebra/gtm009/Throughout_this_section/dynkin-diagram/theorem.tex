The Dynkin diagram of $\Phi$ is a graph with vertices $1, \ldots, \ell$ corresponding to simple roots. Vertices $i \neq j$ are joined by $n_{ij} = \langle \alpha_i, \alpha_j \rangle \langle \alpha_j, \alpha_i \rangle$ edges (0, 1, 2, or 3). When a double or triple edge occurs ($n_{ij} > 1$), an arrow points from the longer to the shorter root (i.e., from vertex $i$ to $j$ if $\|\alpha_i\| > \|\alpha_j\|$).
