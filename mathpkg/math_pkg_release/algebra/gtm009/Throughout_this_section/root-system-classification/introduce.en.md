The **classification theorem** states that every irreducible root system is isomorphic to exactly one of the classical types $A_\ell, B_\ell, C_\ell, D_\ell$ or the exceptional types $E_6, E_7, E_8, F_4, G_2$. Combined with the isomorphism theorem (Chapter 14), this yields the complete classification of simple Lie algebras over algebraically closed fields of characteristic zero.
