This is the fundamental Isomorphism Theorem: two simple Lie algebras with isomorphic root systems are themselves isomorphic. Moreover, the isomorphism can be prescribed on a generating set (the simple root vectors), as long as the structure constants match. This theorem, together with the classification of root systems, gives the classification of simple Lie algebras.
