Let $\delta = \frac{1}{2} \sum_{\alpha > 0} \alpha$ where the sum is over all positive roots of a root system $\Phi$ with base $\Delta$. Then $\delta = \sum_{j=1}^{\ell} \lambda_j$ where $\lambda_j$ are the fundamental dominant weights. Consequently $\delta$ is a (strongly) dominant weight and $\langle \delta, \alpha_i \rangle = 1$ for all simple roots $\alpha_i \in \Delta$.
