Let $L$ be a semisimple Lie algebra, $H$ a maximal toral subalgebra, $\Phi$ the root system, and $\Delta$ a base of $\Phi$. Then $L$ is generated (as a Lie algebra) by the root spaces $L_\alpha$, $L_{-\alpha}$ for $\alpha \in \Delta$. Equivalently, $L$ is generated by arbitrary nonzero root vectors $x_\alpha \in L_\alpha$, $y_\alpha \in L_{-\alpha}$ ($\alpha \in \Delta$).
