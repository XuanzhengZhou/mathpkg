A **reflection** $\sigma_\alpha$ in a euclidean space $E$ is an orthogonal transformation that fixes pointwise the hyperplane $P_\alpha$ orthogonal to $\alpha$ and sends $\alpha$ to $-\alpha$. The number $\langle\beta, \alpha\rangle = 2(\beta, \alpha)/(\alpha, \alpha)$ appears frequently in root system theory and is linear only in the first variable. Reflections generate the Weyl group.
