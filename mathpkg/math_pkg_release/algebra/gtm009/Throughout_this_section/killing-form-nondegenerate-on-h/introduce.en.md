The restriction of the **Killing form** to the maximal toral subalgebra $H$ is **nondegenerate**. This allows identification of $H$ with its dual $H^*$: to each $\phi \in H^*$ corresponds a unique element $t_\phi \in H$ satisfying $\phi(h) = \kappa(t_\phi, h)$ for all $h \in H$. In particular, the root system $\Phi$ corresponds to the subset $\{t_\alpha : \alpha \in \Phi\}$ of $H$.
