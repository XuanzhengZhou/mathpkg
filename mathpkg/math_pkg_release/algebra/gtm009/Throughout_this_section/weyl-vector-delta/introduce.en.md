The **Weyl vector** $\delta$ is half the sum of all positive roots. It equals the sum of all fundamental dominant weights and satisfies $\langle \delta, \alpha_i \rangle = 1$ for all simple roots. The Weyl vector appears in the Weyl dimension formula, the Weyl character formula, and Freudenthal'\''s multiplicity formula, playing a ubiquitous role in representation theory.
