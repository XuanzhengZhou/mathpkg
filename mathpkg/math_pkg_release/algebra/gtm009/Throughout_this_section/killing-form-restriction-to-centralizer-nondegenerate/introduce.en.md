This corollary follows from the orthogonality properties of root spaces and the nondegeneracy of the Killing form on the whole Lie algebra. Since $L_0$ is orthogonal to all $L_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \Phi$) by the previous proposition, any element of $L_0$ orthogonal to all of $L_0$ would be orthogonal to all of $L$, forcing it to be zero by nondegeneracy of $\kappa$.
