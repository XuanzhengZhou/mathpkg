A **base** (or system of simple roots) $\Delta$ of a root system $\Phi$ provides a partition of $\Phi$ into positive and negative roots. Every root is a $\mathbb{Z}$-linear combination of simple roots with coefficients either all nonnegative or all nonpositive. Bases are in natural bijection with Weyl chambers — the connected components of $E \setminus \bigcup_{\alpha \in \Phi} P_\alpha$.
