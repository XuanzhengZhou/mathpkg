The restriction of the **Killing form** to $L_0 = C_L(H)$ (the centralizer of the maximal toral subalgebra $H$) is **nondegenerate**. This follows because $\kappa$ is nondegenerate on all of $L$, while $L_0$ is orthogonal to all root spaces $L_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \Phi$) by the previous proposition. If $z \in L_0$ were orthogonal to $L_0$, then $\kappa(z, L) = 0$, forcing $z = 0$.
