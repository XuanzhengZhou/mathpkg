$\kappa$ is nondegenerate on $L$ by Theorem 5.1. By Proposition 8.1, $L_0$ is orthogonal to all $L_\alpha$ ($\alpha \in \Phi$). If $z \in L_0$ is orthogonal to $L_0$ as well, then $\kappa(z, L) = 0$, forcing $z = 0$. Hence the restriction of $\kappa$ to $L_0$ is nondegenerate. $\square$
