The **Chevalley involution** $\sigma$ is an automorphism of order 2 of a semisimple Lie algebra that negates the maximal toral subalgebra $H$ and interchanges positive and negative root spaces. For $\mathfrak{sl}(2, \mathbb{F})$ it is the familiar automorphism $x \mapsto -y$, $y \mapsto -x$, $h \mapsto -h$. The Chevalley involution is essential for constructing Chevalley bases and real forms (split real Lie algebras).
