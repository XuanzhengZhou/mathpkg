Let $L$ be semisimple, $H$ a maximal toral subalgebra. If $h \in H$, prove that $C_L(h)$ is reductive (in the sense of Exercise 6.5). Prove that $H$ contains elements $h$ for which $C_L(h) = H$; for which $h$ in $\mathfrak{sl}(n, \mathbb{F})$ is this true?
