This proposition establishes the fundamental **properties of the root space decomposition** of a semisimple Lie algebra $L$ relative to a maximal toral subalgebra $H$. The bracket of root spaces is contained in the sum root space, nonzero root vectors act nilpotently, and distinct root spaces whose root sum is nonzero are orthogonal under the Killing form. These facts form the backbone of the structure theory of semisimple Lie algebras.
