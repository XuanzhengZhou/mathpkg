In the algebra $L_o$ (the quotient of the free Lie algebra by the ideal generated by the relations (S1)-(S3)), let $x_{ij} = (\operatorname{ad} x_i)^{-c_{ji}+1}(x_j)$ and $y_{ij} = (\operatorname{ad} y_i)^{-c_{ji}+1}(y_j)$ for $i \neq j$. Then for each $i \neq j$ and all $1 \leq k \leq \ell$,
$$\operatorname{ad} x_k(y_{ij}) = 0.$$
