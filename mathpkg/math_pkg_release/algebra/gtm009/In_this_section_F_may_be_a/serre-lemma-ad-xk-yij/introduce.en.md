This technical lemma is used in the proof of Serre's Theorem. It shows that in the algebra $L_o$ obtained from the free Lie algebra by imposing only the relations (S1)-(S3), the elements $y_{ij} = (\operatorname{ad} y_i)^{-c_{ji}+1}(y_j)$ (which correspond to the Serre relations) are annihilated by the adjoint action of each $x_k$. The proof splits into two cases: when $k \neq i$, using commutativity of $\operatorname{ad} x_k$ and $\operatorname{ad} y_i$; and when $k = i$, using the $\mathfrak{sl}(2, F)$ structure to analyze the adjoint representation.
