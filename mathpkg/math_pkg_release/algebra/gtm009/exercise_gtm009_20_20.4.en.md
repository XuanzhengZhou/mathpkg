Let $L = \mathfrak{sl}(2, F)$, and let $\lambda \in H^*$. Prove that the module $Z(\lambda)$ for $\lambda = \lambda(h)$ constructed in Exercise 7.7 is isomorphic to the module $Z(\lambda)$ constructed in (20.3). Deduce that $\dim V(\lambda) < \infty$ if and only if $\lambda(h)$ is a nonnegative integer.
