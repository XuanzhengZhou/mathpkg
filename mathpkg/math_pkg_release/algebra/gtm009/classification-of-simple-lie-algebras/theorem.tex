Every finite-dimensional simple Lie algebra over an algebraically closed field of characteristic 0 is isomorphic to exactly one of the following:

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{lll}
\textbf{Type} & \textbf{Lie Algebra} & \textbf{Dynkin Diagram} \\
$A_\ell$ ($\ell \geq 1$) & $\mathfrak{sl}(\ell+1, F)$ & $\overset{1}{\circ}\!-\!\overset{2}{\circ}\!-\!\cdots\!-\!\overset{\ell}{\circ}$ \\
$B_\ell$ ($\ell \geq 2$) & $\mathfrak{so}(2\ell+1, F)$ & $\overset{1}{\circ}\!-\!\overset{2}{\circ}\!-\!\cdots\!-\!\overset{\ell-1}{\circ}\!\Rrightarrow\!\overset{\ell}{\circ}$ \\
$C_\ell$ ($\ell \geq 3$) & $\mathfrak{sp}(2\ell, F)$ & $\overset{1}{\circ}\!-\!\overset{2}{\circ}\!-\!\cdots\!-\!\overset{\ell-1}{\circ}\!\Lleftarrow\!\overset{\ell}{\circ}$ \\
$D_\ell$ ($\ell \geq 4$) & $\mathfrak{so}(2\ell, F)$ & $\overset{\ell-1}{\circ}\!\diagup_{\overset{1}{\circ}-\overset{2}{\circ}-\cdots-\overset{\ell-2}{\circ}}\!\diagdown_{\overset{\ell}{\circ}}$ \\
$E_6, E_7, E_8$ & Exceptional & (Exceptional Dynkin diagrams) \\
$F_4$ & Exceptional & $\overset{1}{\circ}\!-\!\overset{2}{\circ}\!\Rrightarrow\!\overset{3}{\circ}\!-\!\overset{4}{\circ}$ \\
$G_2$ & Exceptional & $\overset{1}{\circ}\!\Rrightarrow\!\overset{2}{\circ}$
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

Low-dimensional isomorphisms occur: $A_1 \cong B_1 \cong C_1$, $B_2 \cong C_2$, $D_2 \cong A_1 \times A_1$, $D_3 \cong A_3$.
