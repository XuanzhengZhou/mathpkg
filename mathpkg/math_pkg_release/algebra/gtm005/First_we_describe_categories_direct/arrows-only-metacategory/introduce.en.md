An arrows-only metacategory is an alternative, more parsimonious definition of a metacategory that dispenses entirely with objects, dealing only with arrows and composable pairs. Since objects correspond exactly to identity arrows, and identity arrows are uniquely determined, one can recover the object-based formulation from the arrows-only data. This arrows-only perspective highlights the primacy of morphisms over objects in category theory.
