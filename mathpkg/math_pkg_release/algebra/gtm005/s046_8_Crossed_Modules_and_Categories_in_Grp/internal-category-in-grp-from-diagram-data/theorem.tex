Given a diagram in $\mathbf{Grp}$

$$C_1 \xrightarrow{d_0} C_0 \xrightarrow{i} C_1 \xrightarrow{d_1} C_0$$

where $d_0, d_1 : C_1 \rightarrow C_0$ and $i : C_0 \rightarrow C_1$ are group homomorphisms satisfying

$$d_0 i = 1 = d_1 i$$

and the commutator condition

$$[\ker d_0, \ker d_1] = 1,$$

define a categorical composition $\gamma : C_1 \times_{C_0} C_1 \rightarrow C_1$ on composable pairs $(g, f)$ (where $d_1(f) = d_0(g) = b$) by setting

$$\gamma(g, f) = f \, 1_b^{-1} \, g = g \, 1_b^{-1} \, f,$$

where $1_b = i(b)$. Then this data defines an internal category in $\mathbf{Grp}$.
