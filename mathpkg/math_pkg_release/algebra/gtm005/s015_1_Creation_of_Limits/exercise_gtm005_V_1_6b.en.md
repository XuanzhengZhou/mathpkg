In $\mathbf{Set}$, show that a parallel pair of arrows $f, g : X \to Y$ has a common right inverse if and only if the corresponding function $(f, g) : X \to Y \times Y$ has image containing the diagonal $\{\langle y, y \rangle \mid y \in Y\}$.
