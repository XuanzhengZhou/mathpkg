An injective object is the categorical dual of a projective object: every morphism into it extends along any monomorphism. Equivalently, $\hom(-, q)$ sends monics to epis. The concept is fundamental in homological algebra, where injective resolutions are constructed using injective objects. In $R$-Mod, the injectives are characterized by Baer's criterion.
