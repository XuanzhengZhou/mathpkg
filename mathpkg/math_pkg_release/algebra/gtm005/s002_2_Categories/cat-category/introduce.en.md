$\mathbf{Cat}$ is the category of small categories, where objects are categories whose object and arrow sets are small (members of the chosen universe) and arrows are functors between them. This category is fundamental because it shows that category theory can be applied to itself: one can study categories and functors categorically, leading to notions such as adjunctions between $\mathbf{Cat}$ and other categories.
