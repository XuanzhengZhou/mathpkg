$\mathbf{Ens}$ is the generic notation for a category of sets, where the specific universe $V$ of allowed sets is left variable. It generalizes $\text{Ens}_V$ by not fixing a particular $V$, allowing the same categorical statements to apply regardless of which universe of sets one works within.
