The category $\text{Ens}_V$ is the concrete category whose objects are all sets belonging to a given set $V$ of sets, and whose arrows are all functions between those sets with the usual composition. The notation $\text{Ens}$ is used generically for any such category, making it the prototypical example of a category. This construction makes precise the intuition that categories abstract the properties of sets and functions.
