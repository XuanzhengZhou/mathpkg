A skeleton of a category is a minimal full subcategory containing exactly one representative from each isomorphism class of objects. Every category is equivalent to its skeleton, and two categories are equivalent iff their skeletons are isomorphic. Skeletons provide a canonical way to replace a category with an equivalent one that has no redundant isomorphic copies of objects.
