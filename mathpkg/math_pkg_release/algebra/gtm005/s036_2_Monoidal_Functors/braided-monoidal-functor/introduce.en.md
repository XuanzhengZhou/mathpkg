A braided monoidal functor is a monoidal functor between braided monoidal categories that additionally preserves the braiding structure. This means the coherence map $F_2$ intertwines the braidings $\gamma$ of the source category and $\gamma'$ of the target category. The category of braided monoidal categories has as morphisms the braided monoidal functors, forming a subcategory of the category of monoidal categories and monoidal functors.
