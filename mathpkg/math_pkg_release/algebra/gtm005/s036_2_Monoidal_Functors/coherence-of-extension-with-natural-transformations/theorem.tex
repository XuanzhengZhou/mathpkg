For any monoidal natural transformation $\theta : F \to G$ between two monoidal functors and for any word $v$, the following diagram commutes:

\begin{center}
\begin{tikzcd}
v(F a_1, \ldots, F a_n) \arrow[r, "F_v"] \arrow[d, "v(\theta_{a_1}, \ldots, \theta_{a_n})"'] & F v(a_1, \ldots, a_n) \arrow[d, "\theta_{v(a_1, \ldots, a_n)}"] \\
v(G a_1, \ldots, G a_n) \arrow[r, "G_v"] & G v(a_1, \ldots, a_n)
\end{tikzcd}
\end{center}

This condition generalizes the conditions (5) and (6) in the definition of a monoidal natural transformation.
