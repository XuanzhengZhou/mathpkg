The free category $C_G$ generated by a small graph $G$ generalizes the free monoid construction from sets to directed graphs. Its objects are the vertices of $G$ and its arrows are finite paths (strings of composable edges). The universal property asserts that every graph morphism from $G$ into the underlying graph of a category $B$ extends uniquely to a functor from $C_G$ to $B$. This makes the free category construction left adjoint to the forgetful functor from categories to graphs.
