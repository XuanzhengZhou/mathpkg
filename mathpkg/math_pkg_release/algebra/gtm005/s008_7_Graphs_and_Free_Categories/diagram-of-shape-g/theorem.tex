If $G$ is any graph, a \textbf{diagram of shape $G$} in a category $B$ is a morphism $D: G \rightarrow UB$ of graphs. By the free category theorem, such morphisms correspond exactly to functors $D': C_G \rightarrow B$ via the natural bijection $\operatorname{Cat}(C_G, B) \cong \operatorname{Grph}(G, UB)$.
