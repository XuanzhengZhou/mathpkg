A (directed) \textbf{graph} $G$ consists of a set $O$ of \textbf{objects} (vertices), a set $A$ of \textbf{arrows} $f$ (edges), and a pair of functions
$$\partial_0, \partial_1: A \rightrightarrows O,$$
where $\partial_0 f$ is the \textbf{domain} of $f$ and $\partial_1 f$ is the \textbf{codomain} of $f$.
