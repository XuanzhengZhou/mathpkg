If $A$ and $B$ are (the sets of arrows of) two $O$-graphs, the \emph{product over $O$} is

\[
A \times_O B = \{ \langle g, f \rangle \mid \partial_0 g = \partial_1 f,\; g \in A,\; f \in B \};
\]

it is the set of ``composable pairs'' of arrows $\cdot \xrightarrow{f} \cdot \xrightarrow{g}$. The definitions

\[
\partial_0 \langle g, f \rangle = \partial_0 f, \qquad \partial_1 \langle g, f \rangle = \partial_1 g
\]

make this set an $O$-graph.
