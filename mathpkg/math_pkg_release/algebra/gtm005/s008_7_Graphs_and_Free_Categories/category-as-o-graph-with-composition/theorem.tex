A category with objects $O$ may be described as an $O$-graph $A$ equipped with two morphisms of $O$-graphs:
\begin{itemize}
\item $c: A \times_O A \to A$ (composition), and
\item $i: O \to A$ (identity),
\end{itemize}
such that the associativity diagram commutes:
\[
\begin{CD}
(A \times_O A) \times_O A @>{\cong}>> A \times_O (A \times_O A) @>{1 \times c}>> A \times_O A \\
@V{c \times 1}VV @VV{c}V \\
A \times_O A @>>{c}> A
\end{CD}
\]
and the unit laws hold:
\[
\begin{CD}
O \times_O A @>{i \times 1}>> A \times_O A @<{1 \times i}<< A \times_O O \\
@| @VV{c}V @| \\
A @= A @= A
\end{CD}
\]
