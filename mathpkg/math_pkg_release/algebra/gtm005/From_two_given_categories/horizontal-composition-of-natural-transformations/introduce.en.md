Horizontal composition (the Godement product) combines natural transformations along functors: given $\sigma: S \Rightarrow S'$ and $	au: T \Rightarrow T'$, the horizontal composite $	au * \sigma$ goes from $T \circ S$ to $T' \circ S'$. The two possible definitions coincide by naturality, and this operation is associative and has identities.
