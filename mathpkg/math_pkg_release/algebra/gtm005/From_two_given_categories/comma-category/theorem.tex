For functors $S: A \to C$ and $T: B \to C$, the \emph{comma category} $(S \downarrow T)$ has as objects triples $\langle a, b, f: Sa \to Tb \rangle$, and as arrows $\langle a, b, f \rangle \to \langle a', b', f' \rangle$ pairs $\langle h: a \to a', k: b \to b' \rangle$ such that $Tk \circ f = f' \circ Sh$.
