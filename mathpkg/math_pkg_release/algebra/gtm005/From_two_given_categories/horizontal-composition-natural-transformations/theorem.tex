The \emph{Godement product} (horizontal composition) $\tau \circ \sigma$ of natural transformations $\sigma: S \Rightarrow S': A \to B$ and $\tau: T \Rightarrow T': B \to C$ yields a natural transformation $T \circ S \Rightarrow T' \circ S'$ with $(\tau \circ \sigma)_a$ given by either path in the naturality square of $\tau$.
