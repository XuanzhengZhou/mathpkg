The functor category $[C, B]$ has functors $C 	o B$ as objects and natural transformations between them as morphisms. It is the exponential object in the category of small categories, making $\mathbf{Cat}$ cartesian closed. Functor categories are fundamental: presheaf categories are functor categories $[C^{\mathrm{op}}, \mathbf{Set}]$, and many categorical constructions are expressed as functors between functor categories.
