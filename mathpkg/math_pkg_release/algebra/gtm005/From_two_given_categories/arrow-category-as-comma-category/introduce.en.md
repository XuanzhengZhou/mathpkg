The arrow category $C^{\mathbf{2}}$ (functors from $\mathbf{2} = \{0 	o 1\}$ to $C$) is a special case of the comma category construction: it is precisely $(I_C \downarrow I_C)$. This identification shows how comma categories generalize the idea of 'arrows as objects.'
