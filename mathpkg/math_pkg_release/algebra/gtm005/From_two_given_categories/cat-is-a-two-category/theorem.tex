The collection of all small categories, functors, and natural transformations forms a 2-category $\mathbf{Cat}$ with horizontal composition given by the Godement product $(\sigma \circ \tau)_c = \sigma_{Tc} \circ S'\tau_c$ for natural transformations $\sigma: S \Rightarrow S'$ and $\tau: T \Rightarrow T'$ between composable functors.
