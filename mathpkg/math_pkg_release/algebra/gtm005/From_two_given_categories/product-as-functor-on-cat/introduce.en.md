The product construction on categories is itself functorial: it defines a bifunctor $\times: \mathbf{Cat} \times \mathbf{Cat} \to \mathbf{Cat}$ on the category of small categories. This parallels the fact that the Cartesian product defines functors $\mathbf{Grp} \times \mathbf{Grp} \to \mathbf{Grp}$ and $\mathbf{Top} \times \mathbf{Top} \to \mathbf{Top}$.
