The product category $B \times C$ satisfies the universal property of a categorical product in $\mathbf{Cat}$: any pair of functors $R: D \to B$ and $T: D \to C$ factors uniquely through the projections $P$ and $Q$. The mediating functor $F$ acts componentwise: $Fh = \langle Rh, Th \rangle$ for any arrow $h$ in $D$.
