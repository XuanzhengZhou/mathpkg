The functor category construction is functorial in both arguments: given functors $F: C' 	o C$ and $G: B 	o B'$, there is an induced functor $G^F: B^C 	o B'^{C'}$. This makes the assignment $(C, B) \mapsto [C, B]$ a bifunctor from $\mathbf{Cat}^{\mathrm{op}} 	imes \mathbf{Cat}$ to $\mathbf{Cat}$.
