By iterating the binary biproduct construction, one obtains $n$-ary biproducts (finite direct sums) in any $\textit{Ab}$-category that already has binary biproducts. The iterated biproduct $\bigoplus_j a_j$ is equipped with injections $i_j : a_j \to \bigoplus_j a_j$ and projections $p_k : \bigoplus_j a_j \to a_k$ satisfying $p_k i_j = \delta_{kj}$ and $\sum_j i_j p_j = 1$. This yields a simultaneous finite product and finite coproduct structure, making the $\textit{Ab}$-category additive in the sense of having all finite biproducts.
