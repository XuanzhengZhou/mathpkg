The kernel pair of an arrow $f$ is the pullback of $f$ along itself. It captures the equivalence relation induced by $f$: two elements are related if they map to the same value. Kernel pairs generalize the notion of kernel in abelian categories and are essential in the theory of regular and exact categories.
