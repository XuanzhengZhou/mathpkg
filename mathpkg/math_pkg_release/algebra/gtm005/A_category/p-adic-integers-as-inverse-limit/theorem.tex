Let $F$ be the functor $\omega^{\text{op}} \rightarrow \mathbf{Rng}$ which sends each $n$ to the ring $\mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$ of integers modulo $p^n$ and each arrow $n+1 \rightarrow n$ to the canonical projection $\mathbb{Z}/p^{n+1}\mathbb{Z} \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$. The limit $L = \varprojlim F$ consists of strings $\lambda = \{\lambda_n\}$ with $\lambda_n \in \mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$ and $f_n \lambda_{n+1} = \lambda_n$ for all $n$. Two $p$-adic integers $\lambda$ and $\mu$ can be added and multiplied termwise by

$$(\lambda + \mu)_n = \lambda_n + \mu_n, \quad (\lambda \mu)_n = \lambda_n \mu_n.$$

These operations make $\mathbb{Z}_p = \varprojlim F$ a ring, the ring of $p$-adic integers.
