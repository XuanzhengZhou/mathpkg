A Hausdorff space $X$ is \textbf{compactly generated} if a subset $A \subset X$ is closed whenever $A \cap C$ is closed in $X$ for every compact subset $C \subset X$. Equivalently, $X$ has the final topology with respect to the inclusions of its compact subsets. The category $\mathbf{CGHaus}$ is the full subcategory of $\mathbf{Haus}$ (Hausdorff spaces) whose objects are all compactly generated Hausdorff spaces.
