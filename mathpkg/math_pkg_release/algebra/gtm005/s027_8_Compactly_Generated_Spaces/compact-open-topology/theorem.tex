Let $Y$ and $Z$ be topological spaces. The \textbf{compact-open topology} on the set $\text{Top}(Y, Z)$ of all continuous maps $Y \to Z$ has as a subbase for its open sets the collection
$$\{N(C, U) \mid C \subset Y \text{ compact}, \; U \subset Z \text{ open}\},$$
where
$$N(C, U) = \{h \in \text{Top}(Y, Z) \mid h(C) \subset U\}.$$
The resulting topological space is denoted $\text{Cop}(Y, Z)$.
