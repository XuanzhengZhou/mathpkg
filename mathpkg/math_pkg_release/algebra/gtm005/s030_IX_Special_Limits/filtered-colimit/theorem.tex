A \text{filtered colimit} is a colimit of a functor $F : J \to C$ whose domain $J$ is a filtered category. Classically, colimits were defined only over directed preorders; the notion of filtered category removes this needless restriction while retaining the essential properties, such as the interchange formulas for filtered colimits and the reduction of arbitrary colimits to finite coproducts, coequalizers, and colimits over directed preorders.
