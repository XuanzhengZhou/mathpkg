\text{Theorem.} In the category $\mathbf{Set}$ of sets, finite limits commute with filtered colimits. That is, for a finite category $P$ and a filtered category $J$, the canonical map
$$
\kappa : \varinjlim_j \varprojlim_p F(p,j) \to \varprojlim_p \varinjlim_j F(p,j)
$$
is an isomorphism for any functor $F : P \times J \to \mathbf{Set}$.
