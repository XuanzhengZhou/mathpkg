A filtered category generalizes the notion of a directed preorder from partially ordered sets to arbitrary categories. Condition (a) ensures that any two objects can be mapped into a common target, while condition (b) coequalizes parallel arrows. Together, these conditions guarantee that any finite diagram admits a cone with vertex in the category itself. Filtered categories are the natural domains for filtered colimits, which enjoy particularly well-behaved commutation properties with finite limits.
