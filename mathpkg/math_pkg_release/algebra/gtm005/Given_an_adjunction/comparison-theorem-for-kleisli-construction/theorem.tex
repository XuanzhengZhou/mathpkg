Let $\langle F, G, \eta, \varepsilon \rangle : X \to A$ be an adjunction and let $T = \langle GF, \eta, G\varepsilon F \rangle$ be the monad it defines in $X$. Then there exists a unique functor $L : X_T \to A$ such that

$$G L = G_T \quad \text{and} \quad L F_T = F,$$

where $F_T$ and $G_T$ are the functors of the Kleisli construction for the monad $T$.
