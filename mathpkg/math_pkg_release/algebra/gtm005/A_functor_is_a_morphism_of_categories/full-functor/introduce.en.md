A full functor is one that is surjective on each hom-set: every morphism between the images of two objects in the codomain category comes from a morphism between the original objects. Together with faithfulness, fullness gives a fully faithful functor, which is a bijection on hom-sets but not necessarily essentially surjective on objects.
