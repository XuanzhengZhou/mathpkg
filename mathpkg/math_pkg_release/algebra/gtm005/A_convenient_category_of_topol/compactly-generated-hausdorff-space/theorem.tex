A Hausdorff space $X$ is called \textbf{compactly generated} if it satisfies the following condition: a subset $A \subset X$ is closed if and only if $A \cap C$ is closed in $X$ for every compact subset $C \subset X$. The full subcategory of $\mathbf{Haus}$ consisting of all compactly generated Hausdorff spaces is denoted $\mathbf{CGHaus}$.
