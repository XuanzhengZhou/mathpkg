The category $\mathbf{CGHaus}$ is cartesian closed. For $X, Y \in \mathbf{CGHaus}$, define the exponential object
$$X^Y = K(\operatorname{Cop}(Y, X)),$$
the Kelleyfication of the compact-open topology. The evaluation map $e: X^Y \mathbin{\square} Y \to X$ given by $\langle f, y \rangle \mapsto f(y)$ is continuous, and the natural bijection
$$\mathbf{CGHaus}(Z \mathbin{\square} Y, X) \cong \mathbf{CGHaus}(Z, X^Y)$$
holds for all $Z \in \mathbf{CGHaus}$. Consequently,
$$X^{Z \mathbin{\square} Y} \cong (X^Y)^Z.$$
