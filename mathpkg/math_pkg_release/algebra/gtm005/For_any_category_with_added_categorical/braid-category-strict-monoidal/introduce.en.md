Under the tensor product given by addition of natural numbers and the side-by-side placement of braids, the braid category $B$ is a strict monoidal category. Moreover, it is almost symmetric monoidal: the braiding $\gamma_{m,n}: m+n \to n+m$ is defined geometrically by crossing $m$ strings over $n$ strings, and this transformation is natural in both arguments.
