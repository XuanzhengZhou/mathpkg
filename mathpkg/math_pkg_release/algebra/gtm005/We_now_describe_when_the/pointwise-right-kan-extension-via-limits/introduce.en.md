The pointwise right Kan extension theorem gives an explicit formula for computing $\mathrm{Ran}_K T$ when $A$ is complete: for each $c \in C$, the value $Rc$ is the limit of $T$ over the comma category $(c \downarrow K)$. The universal natural transformation $\varepsilon$ is given by the components of the limiting cones at the identity morphisms.
