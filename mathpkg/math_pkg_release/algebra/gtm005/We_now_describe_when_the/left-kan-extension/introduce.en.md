A left Kan extension of $T: M \to A$ along $K: M \to C$ is a functor $L = \mathrm{Lan}_K T: C \to A$ together with a universal natural transformation $\eta: T \to LK$. It is the left-sided analogue of the right Kan extension, characterized by a natural bijection $\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{Lan}_K T, S) \cong \mathrm{Nat}(T, SK)$.
