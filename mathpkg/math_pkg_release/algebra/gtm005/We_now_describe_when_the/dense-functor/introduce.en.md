A dense functor $K: A 	o C$ is one whose image generates $C$ under colimits in a precise sense: every object of $C$ is a canonical colimit of objects in the image of $K$. The Yoneda embedding is the prototypical dense functor. Density generalizes the fact that every presheaf is a colimit of representables.
