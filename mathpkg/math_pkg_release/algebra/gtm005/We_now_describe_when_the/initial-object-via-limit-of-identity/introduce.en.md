Lemma 1 states that if an object $d$ carries a cone over the identity functor of $C$, and this cone restricts to a limiting cone for some functor $F: J \to C$, then $d$ is an initial object in $C$. This lemma connects the concepts of limits and initial objects, and is a key technical tool in the formal criterion for the existence of adjoints.
