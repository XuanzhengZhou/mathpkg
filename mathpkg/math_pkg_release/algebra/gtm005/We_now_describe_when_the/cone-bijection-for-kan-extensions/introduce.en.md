This lemma provides the key bijection between cones over the diagram $(c \downarrow K) \to M \to A$ and natural transformations $C(c, K-) \to A(a, T-)$. It is the technical heart of the proof that the pointwise Kan extension formula via limits is equivalent to the characterization via representability of cones.
