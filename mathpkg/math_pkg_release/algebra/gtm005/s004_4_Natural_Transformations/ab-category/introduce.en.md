An Ab-category is a category enriched over the monoidal category of abelian groups. The hom-sets carry an abelian group structure, and composition distributes over addition. Categories of modules (R-Mod), abelian groups (Ab), and chain complexes are Ab-categories. This structure is the first step toward abelian categories.
