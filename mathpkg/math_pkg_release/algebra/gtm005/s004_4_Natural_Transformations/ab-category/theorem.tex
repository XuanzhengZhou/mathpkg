An \textbf{Ab-category} (also called a \textbf{preadditive category}) is a category $A$ in which each hom-set $A(a, b)$ is an additive abelian group and for which composition is bilinear: for arrows $f, f': a \rightarrow b$ and $g, g': b \rightarrow c$,
\[
(g + g') \circ (f + f') = g \circ f + g \circ f' + g' \circ f + g' \circ f'.
\]
