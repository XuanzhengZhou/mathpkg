The hom-set collects all arrows between two objects. In locally small categories, each hom-set is a genuine set. The structure of hom-sets encodes the essential information of the category: functors are assignments on hom-sets, natural transformations correspond to families of elements of hom-sets, and enriched categories generalize by replacing hom-sets with objects of a monoidal category.
