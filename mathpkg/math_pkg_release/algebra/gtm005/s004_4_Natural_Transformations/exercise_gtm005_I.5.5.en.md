In $\mathbf{Grp}$ prove that every epi is surjective. (Hint. If $\varphi: G \to H$ has image $M$ not $H$, use the factor group $H/M$ if $M$ has index 2. Otherwise, let $\operatorname{Perm} H$ be the group of all permutations of the set $H$, choose three different cosets $M, Mu$ and $Mv$ of $M$, define $\sigma \in \operatorname{Perm} H$ by $\sigma(xu) = xv$, $\sigma(xv) = xu$ for $x \in M$, and $\sigma$ otherwise the identity. Let $\psi: H \to \operatorname{Perm} H$ send each $h$ to left multiplication $\psi_h$ by $h$, while $\psi'_h = \sigma^{-1}\psi_h\sigma$. Then $\psi\varphi = \psi'\varphi$, but $\psi \neq \psi'$.)
