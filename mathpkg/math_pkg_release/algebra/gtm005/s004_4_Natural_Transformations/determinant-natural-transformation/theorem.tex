Let $\det_K M$ be the determinant of the $n \times n$ matrix $M$ with entries in the commutative ring $K$, and let $K^*$ denote the group of units of $K$. The determinant is a natural transformation between the general linear group functor $\operatorname{GL}_n$ and the group-of-units functor $(-)^*$ on the category of commutative rings.
