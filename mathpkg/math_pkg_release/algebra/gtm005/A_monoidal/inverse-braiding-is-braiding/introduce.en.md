For any braided monoidal category, the inverse of the braiding is again a braiding. This follows from the fact that the first hexagon axiom for $\gamma$ implies the second for $\gamma^{-1}$, and vice versa. Thus a braided monoidal category comes with two naturally associated braidings: $\gamma$ and $\gamma^{-1}$.
