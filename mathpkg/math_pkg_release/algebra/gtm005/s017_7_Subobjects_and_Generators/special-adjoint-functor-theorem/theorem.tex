\text{\textbf{Theorem 2 (The Special Adjoint Functor Theorem).} Let the category $A$ be small-complete, with small hom-sets, and a small cogenerating set $Q$, while every set of subobjects of an object $a \in A$ has a pullback (and hence has an intersection).}

\text{Let the category $X$ have small hom-sets. Then every continuous functor $G: A \to X$ has a left adjoint.}
