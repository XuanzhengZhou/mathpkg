In the strict monoidal category $([C, C], \circ, I_C)$ of endofunctors with composition, a \textbf{monoid object} is precisely a \textbf{monad} on $C$: an endofunctor $T$ with multiplication $\mu: T \circ T \xrightarrow{\cdot} T$ and unit $\eta: I \xrightarrow{\cdot} T$ satisfying the usual monad axioms.
