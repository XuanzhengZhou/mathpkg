When the ambient finitely complete category $E$ is taken to be the category of sets $\mathbf{Set}$, the internal definition of a category object specializes exactly to the ordinary notion of a small category. The object $C_0$ becomes the set of objects, $C_1$ the set of arrows, and the four structure maps $i, d_0, d_1, \gamma$ become the usual identity assignment, domain, codomain, and composition functions. The commutativity conditions (3)-(6) translate directly into the standard category axioms: identity laws and associativity of composition.
