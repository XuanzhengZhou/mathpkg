A copower $X \cdot b$ is the coproduct of $X$ copies of the same object $b$. In Set, $X \cdot Y = X \times Y$ (the cartesian product of the set $X$ and the set $Y$). Copowers formalize the idea of multiplying an object by a set, analogous to the tensor product in linear algebra.
