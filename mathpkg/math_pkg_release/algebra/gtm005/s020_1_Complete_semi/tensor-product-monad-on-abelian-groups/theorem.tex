If $R$ is a (small) ring, then for each (small) abelian group $A$, the definitions

$$TA = R \otimes A,$$
$$\eta_A : A \to R \otimes A, \quad a \mapsto 1 \otimes a,$$
$$\mu_A : R \otimes (R \otimes A) \to R \otimes A, \quad r_1 \otimes (r_2 \otimes a) \mapsto r_1 r_2 \otimes a,$$

for $a \in A$, $r_1, r_2 \in R$, define a monad $T$ on the category $\mathbf{Ab}$ of abelian groups. The $T$-algebras are exactly the left $R$-modules.
