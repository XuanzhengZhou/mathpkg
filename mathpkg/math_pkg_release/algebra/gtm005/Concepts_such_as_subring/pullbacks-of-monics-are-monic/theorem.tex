In any pullback square
\[
\begin{array}{ccc}
p & \xrightarrow{f'} & t \\
{\scriptstyle g'}\Big\downarrow & & \Big\downarrow{\scriptstyle g} \\
s & \xrightarrow{f} & a
\end{array}
\]
if $f$ is monic then $f'$ is monic. Dually, if $g$ is monic then $g'$ is monic.

Briefly, pullbacks of monics are monic.
