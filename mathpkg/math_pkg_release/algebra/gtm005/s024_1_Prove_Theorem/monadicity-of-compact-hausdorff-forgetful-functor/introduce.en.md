This theorem states that the forgetful functor from the category of compact Hausdorff spaces to sets is monadic, meaning the category of compact Hausdorff spaces is equivalent to the Eilenberg-Moore category of algebras for the induced monad on Set. The proof, due to R. Paré, uses the Stone-Čech compactification as the left adjoint and closure operations to construct the algebra structure, demonstrating that compact Hausdorff spaces are precisely the algebras for the ultrafilter monad.
