A Galois connection is an adjunction between preorders (or posets). It captures the classical Galois correspondence: for a group acting on a set, the maps sending a subset to its pointwise stabilizer and a set of group elements to their common fixed points form a Galois connection. Galois connections also arise in logic (quantifiers as adjoints) and geometry (inverse image having both existential and universal adjoints).
