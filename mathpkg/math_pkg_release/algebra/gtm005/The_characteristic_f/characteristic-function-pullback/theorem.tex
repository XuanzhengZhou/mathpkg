Let $S \subset X$ be a subset with inclusion $i: S \hookrightarrow X$, and let $\psi_S : X \to \{0, 1\}$ be its characteristic function. Let $t : \{0\} \hookrightarrow \{0, 1\}$ be the inclusion of the singleton. Then the following square is a pullback:
$$
\begin{array}{ccc}
S & \longrightarrow & \{0\} \\[4pt]
\downarrow\scriptstyle{i} & & \downarrow\scriptstyle{t} \\[4pt]
X & \xrightarrow{\;\psi_S\;} & \{0, 1\}.
\end{array}
$$
Equivalently, $S$ is the inverse image of $\{0\}$ under $\psi_S$, i.e., $S = \psi_S^{-1}(\{0\})$.
