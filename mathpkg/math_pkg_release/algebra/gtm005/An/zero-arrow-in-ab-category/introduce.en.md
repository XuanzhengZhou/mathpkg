In an Ab-category, the additive group structure on each hom-set provides a canonical zero morphism between any two objects. This zero arrow is the identity for the addition operation and is preserved under composition from either side. The zero arrow generalizes the zero homomorphism between abelian groups and is fundamental to the additive structure, serving as the trivial map in any hom-set.
