This proposition provides an alternative, structural characterization of additive functors: rather than checking the additive condition $T(f+g) = Tf + Tg$ on all parallel pairs, it suffices to check that $T$ preserves binary biproduct diagrams. Since the additive structure of an Ab-category is encoded in its biproducts, preserving biproducts is equivalent to preserving the addition of arrows. This equivalence is fundamental in the theory of additive and abelian categories.
