A \textbf{crossed module} is a pair of groups $H$ and $P$ together with an action of $P$ on $H$ and a group homomorphism $\partial : H \rightarrow P$ such that, denoting the action of $p \in P$ on $h \in H$ by $h^p$, the following conditions hold for all $h, k \in H$ and $p, q \in P$:

$$h^1 = h,$$

$$(h^p)^q = h^{pq},$$

$$(hk)^p = h^p k^p,$$

$$\partial(h^p) = p (\partial h) p^{-1}.$$
