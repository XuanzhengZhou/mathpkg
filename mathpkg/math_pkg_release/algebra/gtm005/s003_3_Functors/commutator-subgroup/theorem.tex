For any group $G$, the \emph{commutator subgroup} $[G, G]$ of $G$ is the subgroup generated by all commutators $xyx^{-1}y^{-1}$ for $x, y \in G$. It is a normal subgroup of $G$, and any homomorphism $G \rightarrow H$ of groups carries commutators to commutators, so the assignment $G \mapsto [G, G]$ defines a functor $\mathbf{Grp} \rightarrow \mathbf{Grp}$.
