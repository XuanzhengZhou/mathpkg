A coequalizer identifies (coequalizes) two parallel arrows by forcing their images to be equal. In Set, the coequalizer of $f, g: X \to Y$ is the quotient $Y/{\sim}$ where $\sim$ is the smallest equivalence relation identifying $f(x) \sim g(x)$ for all $x$. Coequalizers are the categorical formulation of quotients by equivalence relations.
