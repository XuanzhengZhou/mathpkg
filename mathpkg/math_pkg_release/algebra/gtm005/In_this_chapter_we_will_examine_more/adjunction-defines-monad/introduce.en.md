Every adjunction between categories naturally produces a monad on the domain category. Given an adjunction $\langle F, G, \eta, \varepsilon \rangle: X \rightleftharpoons A$, the composite endofunctor $T = GF$ together with the unit $\eta$ of the adjunction and the whiskered counit $\mu = G\varepsilon F$ satisfies the monad axioms. For instance, the free group monad on $\mathbf{Set}$ arises from the free-forgetful adjunction $\mathbf{Set} \rightleftharpoons \mathbf{Grp}$.
