A \emph{Galois connection} from a poset $P$ to a poset $Q$ is a pair of order-preserving functions $L: P \to Q$ and $R: Q \to P$ such that

\[
Lp \leq q \quad \text{in } Q \quad \text{if and only if} \quad p \leq Rq \quad \text{in } P.
\]

Equivalently, $p \leq RLp$ for all $p \in P$ and $LRq \leq q$ for all $q \in Q$. This is precisely an adjunction $\langle L, R \rangle: P \to Q$ when $P$ and $Q$ are regarded as categories.
