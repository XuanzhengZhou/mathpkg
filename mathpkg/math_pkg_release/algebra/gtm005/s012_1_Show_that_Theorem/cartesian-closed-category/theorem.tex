A category $C$ is \emph{cartesian closed} if it has a terminal object $t$, binary products $a \times b$, and for each functor $- \times b: C \to C$ a right adjoint $(-)^b$, with the bijection

\[
\hom(a \times b, c) \cong \hom(a, c^b)
\]

natural in $a$ and $c$. The object $c^b$ is called the \emph{exponential}, and the counit $e: c^b \times b \to c$ is the evaluation map.
