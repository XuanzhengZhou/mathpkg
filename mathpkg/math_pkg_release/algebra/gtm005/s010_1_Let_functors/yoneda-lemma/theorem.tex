For a functor $K: D \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}$ and an object $r \in D$, there is a bijection
\[
y: \operatorname{Nat}(\hom_D(r, -), K) \cong Kr
\]
which sends each natural transformation $\alpha: \hom_D(r, -) \xrightarrow{\cdot} K$ to the element $\alpha_r(1_r) \in Kr$. This bijection is natural in $r$ and $K$.
