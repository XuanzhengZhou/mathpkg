A colimit is the dual notion to a limit, and generalizes coproducts, coequalizers, pushouts, and direct limits. Given a diagram $F: J \to C$, the colimit $\varinjlim F$ is the "universal cocone" under the diagram. Colimits are constructed by "gluing together" objects according to the shape of $J$. A category with all small colimits is called cocomplete.
