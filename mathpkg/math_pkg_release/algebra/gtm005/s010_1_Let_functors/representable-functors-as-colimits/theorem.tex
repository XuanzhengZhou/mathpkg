Every set-valued functor $H: C^{\mathrm{op}} \to \mathbf{Set}$ is a colimit of representable functors. More precisely, there is a canonical presentation of $H$ as the colimit of the composite
$$\int H \to C \xrightarrow{y} [C^{\mathrm{op}}, \mathbf{Set}]$$
where $\int H$ is the category of elements of $H$ and $y$ is the Yoneda embedding.
