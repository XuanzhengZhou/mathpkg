Prove that the unit $\eta_x : x \rightarrow G(p, F(x, p))$ of an adjunction with parameter is dinatural in $p$, and that this property is equivalent to the naturality of the adjunction itself in $p$ (cf. IV.7., Exercise 2). Dualize.
