This theorem establishes that in the category of sets, taking finite limits and filtered colimits can be interchanged. Using the construction of colimits via coproducts and coequalizers, the proof exploits the filteredness condition to show that every cone over a finite diagram of colimits can be represented by elements all coming from a single index in the filtered category. This commutation property is fundamental in categorical algebra and topos theory.
