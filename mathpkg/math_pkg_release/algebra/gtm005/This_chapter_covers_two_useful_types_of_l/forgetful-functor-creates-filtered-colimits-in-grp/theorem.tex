The forgetful functor $U : \mathbf{Grp} \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}$ creates filtered colimits. That is, for any filtered category $J$ and functor $G : J \rightarrow \mathbf{Grp}$, if the composite $U \circ G : J \rightarrow \mathbf{Set}$ has a colimit with limiting cone $\mu : UG \rightarrow S$, then there is a unique group structure on the set $S$ such that all components $\mu_j : G_j \rightarrow S$ are group homomorphisms, and this makes $S$ the colimit of $G$ in $\mathbf{Grp}$.
