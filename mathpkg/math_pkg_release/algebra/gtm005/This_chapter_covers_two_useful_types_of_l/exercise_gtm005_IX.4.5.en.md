For $S : C^{\text{op}} \times C \rightarrow B$ and $b, b' \in B$, show that dinatural transformations $b \mathbin{\dot{\rightarrow}} S$ and $S \mathbin{\dot{\rightarrow}} b'$ do not in general have a well defined composite $b \rightarrow b'$.
