A filtered colimit is a colimit whose indexing category is filtered. Classically, colimits were defined only over directed preorders (sometimes just over directed orders), but this restriction is needlessly conceptual. The key advantages of filtered colimits are their interchange formulas with finite limits and the fact that all colimits can be constructed from finite coproducts, coequalizers, and colimits over directed preorders.
