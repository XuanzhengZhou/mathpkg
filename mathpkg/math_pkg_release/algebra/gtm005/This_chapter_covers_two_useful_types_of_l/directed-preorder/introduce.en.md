A directed preorder, also called a directed set or filtered set, is a preorder in which every pair of elements has a common upper bound. This condition implies that every finite subset also has an upper bound, making directed preorders the classical domain for taking limits and colimits over "directed index sets." The notion generalizes to filtered categories, which replace the preorder condition with a categorical structure having similar cone-completion properties for arbitrary finite diagrams.
