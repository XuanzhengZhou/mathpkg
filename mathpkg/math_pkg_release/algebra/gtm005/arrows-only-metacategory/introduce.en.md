Since objects in a metacategory correspond exactly to identity arrows, it is technically possible to dispense with objects entirely and formulate the theory using only arrows. This arrows-only formulation uses composable pairs of arrows and a composition operation as primitive notions, with identity arrows serving the role of objects. The axioms ensure composability conditions match the usual domain/codomain structure.
