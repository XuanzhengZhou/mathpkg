For objects $A, B$ in a bicategory $B$, the hom-category $B(A, B)$ has as objects the 1-cells $f, g: A \rightarrow B$ and as arrows the 2-cells $\alpha: f \Rightarrow g$, with vertical composition given by composition within the hom-category. Horizontal composition is given by the composition functors $c_{A,B,C}$.
