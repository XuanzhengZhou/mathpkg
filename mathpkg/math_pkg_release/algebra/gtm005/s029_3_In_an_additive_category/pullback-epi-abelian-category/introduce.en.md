In an abelian category, the pullback of an epimorphism is again an epimorphism, and pullbacks interact well with kernels. This proposition is fundamental for performing diagram chases in abelian categories without relying on element-based arguments. It also shows that pulling back a short exact sequence along a morphism into the right-hand object yields another short exact sequence with the same kernel object, a crucial property for homological algebra constructions.
