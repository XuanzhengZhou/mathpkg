In a category with zero morphisms, the \textbf{cokernel} of $f: a \rightarrow b$ is an arrow $q: b \rightarrow \operatorname{coker} f$ such that $q \circ f = 0$, and any arrow $h: b \rightarrow c$ with $h \circ f = 0$ factors uniquely through $q$. The cokernel is the coequalizer of $f$ and the zero arrow.
