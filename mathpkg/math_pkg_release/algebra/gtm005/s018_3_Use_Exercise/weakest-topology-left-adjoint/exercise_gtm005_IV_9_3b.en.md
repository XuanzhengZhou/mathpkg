For $G$ the forgetful functor $\mathbf{Top} \to \mathbf{Set}$ and $J$ discrete, construct a left adjoint for $G_*$, showing that it constructs on a set $S$ the weakest topology making a given $J$-indexed family of functions $f_j: S \to GX_j$ continuous.
