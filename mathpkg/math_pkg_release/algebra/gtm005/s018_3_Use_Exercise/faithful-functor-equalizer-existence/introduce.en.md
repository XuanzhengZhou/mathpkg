Proposition 1 provides a general categorical method for constructing equalizers in a concrete category $C$. Given a faithful functor $G: C \to D$ where $D$ has equalizers and slice categories admit right-adjoint-right-inverses $L$, one obtains equalizers in $C$ by: take the equalizer in $D$, then lift it back using $L$. This is an element-free version of the usual construction of equalizers in $\mathbf{Top}$ by taking the subspace of points where two maps agree.
