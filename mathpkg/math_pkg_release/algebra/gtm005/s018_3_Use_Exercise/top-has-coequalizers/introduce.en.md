Since Proposition 1 was proved purely from categorical axioms, its dual statement holds automatically. Applying the dual of Proposition 1 together with the quotient topology adjunction $M \dashv (X \downarrow G)$ shows that $\mathbf{Top}$ has coequalizers. A coequalizer in $\mathbf{Top}$ is constructed by taking the coequalizer in $\mathbf{Set}$ and equipping it with the quotient topology.
