The quotient construction $X/A$ in pointed topological spaces is a left adjoint. The adjunction $\mathbf{Top}_* (X/A, Y) \cong \mathbf{Top}^{(2)} (\langle X, A \rangle, \langle Y, * \rangle)$ states that pointed continuous maps from the quotient $X/A$ to $Y$ correspond bijectively to maps of pairs from $\langle X, A \rangle$ to $\langle Y, * \rangle$, i.e., continuous maps $X \to Y$ sending $A$ to the basepoint $*$.
