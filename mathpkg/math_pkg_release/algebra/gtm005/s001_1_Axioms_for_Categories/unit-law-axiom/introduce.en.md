The unit law axiom is the second fundamental axiom of a metacategory. It requires that for each object $b$, the identity arrow $1_b: b 	o b$ serves as a two-sided unit for composition: pre-composing any arrow $f: a 	o b$ with $1_b$ leaves $f$ unchanged, and post-composing any arrow $g: b 	o c$ with $1_b$ leaves $g$ unchanged. This is the categorical analogue of the identity function.
