If a category $C$ has a null object $z$, then for any two objects $a$ and $b$ of $C$ there is a unique arrow $a \rightarrow z \rightarrow b$ (the composite through $z$), called the \text{zero arrow} from $a$ to $b$, denoted $0: a \rightarrow b$. Any composite with a zero arrow is itself a zero arrow.
