The product of functors respects composition: applying $U \times V$ followed by $U' \times V'$ is the same as applying the composite functors $U'U \times V'V$. This identity, together with the fact that the product of identity functors is the identity functor on the product category, shows that the operation $\times$ is itself functorial.
