The projection functors $P: B \times C \to B$ and $Q: B \times C \to C$ are the natural forgetful maps that extract the first and second components, respectively, from objects and arrows of the product category. They are the canonical structure maps for the categorical product and play a role analogous to the coordinate projections in the Cartesian product of sets.
