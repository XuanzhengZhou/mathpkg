If $B$ is the braid category and $M$ a braided monoidal category, with $M_0$ the underlying (ordinary) category, there is an equivalence of categories
$$\hom_{\mathsf{BMC}}(B, M) \approx M_0,$$
where $\hom_{\mathsf{BMC}}$ stands for the category of strong braided monoidal functors $F : B \to M$. The equivalence is given by evaluating each such functor $F : B \to M$ at the object $1$ of the braid category $B$.
