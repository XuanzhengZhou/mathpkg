A key consequence of the Yoneda lemma is that natural transformations between covariant representable functors $\hom(r, -)$ and $\hom(s, -)$ correspond uniquely to arrows $h: s \to r$ in the opposite direction. This establishes that the Yoneda embedding $y: C^{\mathrm{op}} \to [C, \mathbf{Set}]$ is fully faithful, allowing any category to be faithfully represented as a full subcategory of a functor category.
