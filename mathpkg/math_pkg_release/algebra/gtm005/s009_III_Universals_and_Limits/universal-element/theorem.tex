If $D$ is a category and $H: D \to \mathbf{Set}$ a functor, a \emph{universal element} of the functor $H$ is a pair $\langle r, e \rangle$ consisting of an object $r \in D$ and an element $e \in Hr$ such that for every pair $\langle d, x \rangle$ with $x \in Hd$ there is a unique arrow $f: r \to d$ of $D$ with $(Hf)e = x$.
