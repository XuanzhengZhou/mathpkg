The free category construction $C : \text{Grph} \to \text{Cat}$ sending each graph $G$ to its free category $C_G$ and the forgetful functor $U : \text{Cat} \to \text{Grph}$ form an adjunction
$$C \dashv U : \text{Cat} \to \text{Grph}.$$

Equivalently, there is a bijection
$$\text{Cat}(C_G, B) \cong \text{Grph}(G, UB),$$
natural in the graph $G$ and the category $B$.
