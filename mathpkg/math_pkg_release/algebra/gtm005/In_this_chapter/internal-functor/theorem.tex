Let $\mathbf{C} = (C_0, C_1, i_C, d_0^C, d_1^C, \gamma_C)$ and $\mathbf{D} = (D_0, D_1, i_D, d_0^D, d_1^D, \gamma_D)$ be internal categories in the same ambient finitely complete category $\mathcal{E}$.

An \textbf{internal functor} $f : \mathbf{C} \to \mathbf{D}$ is a pair of morphisms
\[
f_0 : C_0 \to D_0, \qquad f_1 : C_1 \to D_1
\]
in $\mathcal{E}$ (the ``object function'' and ``arrow function'') making the following diagrams commute:
\[
\begin{CD}
C_1 @>{d_0^C}>> C_0 @>{i_C}>> C_1 \\
@V{f_1}VV @VV{f_0}V @VV{f_1}V \\
D_1 @>>{d_0^D}> D_0 @>>{i_D}> D_1
\end{CD}
\qquad
\begin{CD}
C_1 @>{d_1^C}>> C_0 \\
@V{f_1}VV @VV{f_0}V \\
D_1 @>>{d_1^D}> D_0
\end{CD}
\]
\[
\begin{CD}
C_1 \times_{C_0} C_1 @>{f_1 \times f_1}>> D_1 \times_{D_0} D_1 \\
@V{\gamma_C}VV @VV{\gamma_D}V \\
C_1 @>>{f_1}> D_1
\end{CD}
\]
