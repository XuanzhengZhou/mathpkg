This proposition establishes that the hom-set compatibility condition defining a map of adjunctions can be equivalently expressed in two simpler forms. The condition $L\eta = \eta'L$ states that the map $L$ preserves the unit of the adjunction, while $\varepsilon'K = K\varepsilon$ states that $K$ preserves the counit. Either of these equalities suffices to characterize a map of adjunctions, providing a convenient way to verify the hom-set condition.
