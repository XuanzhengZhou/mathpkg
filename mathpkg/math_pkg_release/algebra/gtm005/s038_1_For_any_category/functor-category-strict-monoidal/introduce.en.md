For any category $C$, the functor category $C^C$ of endofunctors carries a natural strict monoidal structure. The tensor product is given by composition of endofunctors, and the unit object is the identity functor $1_C$. This exercise asks to verify that composition of functors is strictly associative and that $1_C$ acts as a strict two-sided unit, making $(C^C, \circ, 1_C)$ a strict monoidal category.
