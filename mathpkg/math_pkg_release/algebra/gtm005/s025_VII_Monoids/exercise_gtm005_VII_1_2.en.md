Construct the product in $\mathbf{Moncat}$ of two monoidal categories. That is, given two monoidal categories $B$ and $B'$, define a monoidal structure on the product category $B \times B'$ and verify the monoidal category axioms. Show that this construction gives the categorical product in the category of small monoidal categories with strict morphisms.
