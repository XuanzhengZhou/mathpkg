Many important adjunctions, such as the Hom-tensor adjunction, depend on a parameter. This theorem generalizes that situation: given a bifunctor $F(x, p)$ that has a right adjoint for each fixed parameter $p$, there is a unique way to promote the pointwise right adjoints into a full bifunctor $G(p, a)$ contravariant in the parameter, making the adjunction natural in all variables. The construction uses the theory of conjugate natural transformations.
