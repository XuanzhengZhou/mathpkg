Building on the adjoint square framework, Palmquist's result establishes a natural bijection between natural transformations of the form $\alpha : F'HG \to K$ and those of the form $\beta : H \to G'KF$. This bijection arises from the adjunction data and provides another perspective on how left and right adjoint data are intertwined across different categories.
