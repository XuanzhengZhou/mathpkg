Let $K: M \to C$ and $T: M \to A$ be functors with $M$ small and $A$ cocomplete. If $A$ has copowers $X \cdot a$ for all sets $X$ and all $a \in A$, then the left Kan extension of $T$ along $K$ exists and is given at each $c \in C$ by the coend formula
$$(\mathrm{Lan}_K T)c = \int^{m \in M} C(Km, c) \cdot Tm.$$
This formula provides a natural bijection $\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{Lan}_K T, S) \cong \mathrm{Nat}(T, SK)$ for all $S: C \to A$.
