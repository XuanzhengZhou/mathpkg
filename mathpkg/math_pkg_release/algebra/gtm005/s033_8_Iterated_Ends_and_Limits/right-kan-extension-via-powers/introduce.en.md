This proposition gives the dual coend/end formula for right Kan extensions. While left Kan extensions are expressed as coends using copowers (copower by a hom-set), right Kan extensions are expressed as ends using powers (power by a hom-set). For additive categories (Ab-categories), the power is replaced by the cotensor, leading to the theory of derived functors as a special case of additive Kan extensions.
