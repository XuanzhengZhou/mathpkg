Let $E$ be an injective left $R$-module. The following statements are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $E^{(A)}$ is injective for all sets $A$;
\item[(b)] The $E$-annihilator left ideals in $R$ satisfy the ascending chain condition;
\item[(c)] $E^{(\mathbb{N})}$ is injective.
\end{enumerate}
