For a ring $R$ the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $R$ is left noetherian;
\item[(b)] Every injective left $R$-module is a direct sum of indecomposable modules;
\item[(c)] Every injective left $R$-module has a decomposition that complements direct summands;
\item[(d)] Every injective left $R$-module has a decomposition that complements maximal direct summands.
\end{enumerate}
