The Goldie dimension $\operatorname{G.dim}(M)$ of a module $M$ is the infimum of the set of cardinal numbers $c$ such that $M$ contains an essential submodule which is a direct sum of $c$ uniform submodules. If $M = 0$, then $\operatorname{G.dim}(M) = 0$.
