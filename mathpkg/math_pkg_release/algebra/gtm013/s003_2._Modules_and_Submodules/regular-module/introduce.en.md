The regular module structure on a ring $R$ is the natural left and right $R$-module structures given by ring multiplication on the additive group of $R$. A ring is always a bimodule over itself, denoted ${}_R R$, $R_R$, and ${}_R R_R$ for the left, right, and two-sided regular modules respectively. The submodule lattices of regular modules correspond to the lattices of left, right, and two-sided ideals.
