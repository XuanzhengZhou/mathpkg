In module theory, maximal submodule conventionally means a maximal proper submodule (not the whole module), and minimal submodule means a minimal non-zero submodule. These extremal submodules play crucial roles in the structure theory of modules. For instance, a module is simple exactly when it is both minimal and maximal in this sense.
