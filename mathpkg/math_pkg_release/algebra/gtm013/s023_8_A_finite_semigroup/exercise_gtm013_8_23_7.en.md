Let $R$ be a finite-dimensional algebra over a field $K$. Prove:

(1) If $R$ is basic, then $R$ is split iff $KR = Ke_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus Ke_n \oplus J(R)$.

(2) If $K$ is algebraically closed, then $R$ is split.

[Hint: First show that $K$ is the only finite-dimensional division algebra over $K$.]
