This exercise uses the following functors:

$$F_P = (S P \otimes_R) : R M \rightarrow S M$$
$$G_P = (R Q \otimes_S) : S M \rightarrow R M$$
$$H_P = \mathrm{Hom}_S(S P_R, S) : S M \rightarrow R M.$$

Prove that if $P_R$ is finitely generated projective, then $(G_P, F_P)$ is an adjoint pair. [Hint: Exercise (20.7(2)) and Proposition 20.11.]
