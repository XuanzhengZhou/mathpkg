Let $C_1$ and $C_2$ be categories. Their product is the category $C_1 \times C_2$ with objects $\mathcal{C}_1 \times \mathcal{C}_2$, morphisms $\mathrm{mor}_{C_1} \times \mathrm{mor}_{C_2}$ applied coordinate-wise, and composition the product composition. Prove that if $R_1$ and $R_2$ are rings then

$$R_1 M \times R_2 M \approx R_1 \times R_2 M.$$
