This exercise uses the following functors:

$$F_P = (S P \otimes_R) : R M \rightarrow S M$$
$$G_P = (R Q \otimes_S) : S M \rightarrow R M$$
$$H_P = \mathrm{Hom}_S(S P_R, S) : S M \rightarrow R M.$$

Let both $P_R$ and $S P$ be finitely generated projective. Then $S P_R$ is Frobenius in case as $(R, S)$-bimodules

$$\mathrm{Hom}_R(S P_R, {}_R R_R) \cong \mathrm{Hom}_S(S P_R, {}_S S_S).$$

Prove that $S P_R$ is Frobenius iff $G_P \cong H_P$. [Hint: (20.10).]
