The following pairs of concepts are dual to one another in an arbitrary category $\mathcal{C}$, meaning that under a duality between $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{D}$, the first corresponds to the second:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] Projective object; injective object.
\item[(2)] Superfluous epimorphism; essential monomorphism.
\item[(3)] Projective cover; injective envelope.
\item[(4)] Generator; cogenerator.
\end{enumerate}
