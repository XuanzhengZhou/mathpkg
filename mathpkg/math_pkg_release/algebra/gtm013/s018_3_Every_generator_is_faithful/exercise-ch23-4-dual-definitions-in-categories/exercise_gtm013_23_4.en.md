Each of the following terms has been defined in module categories ${}_R \mathcal{M}$. Extend these definitions to arbitrary categories $C$ in such a way that in each pair the terms are dual to one another (i.e., so that, under a duality between $C$ and $D$, the first corresponds to the second).
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(1)] Projective object; injective object.
    \item[(2)] Superfluous epimorphism; essential monomorphism.
\end{enumerate}
