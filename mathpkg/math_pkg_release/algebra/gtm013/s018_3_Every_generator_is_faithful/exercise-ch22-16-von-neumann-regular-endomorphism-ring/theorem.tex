Let $R$ be von Neumann regular and left self-injective. Prove that if $P_R$ is finitely generated projective, then $\operatorname{End}(P_R)$ is von Neumann regular and left self-injective. In particular, if $e \in R$ is a non-zero idempotent, then $eRe$ is von Neumann regular and left self-injective.
