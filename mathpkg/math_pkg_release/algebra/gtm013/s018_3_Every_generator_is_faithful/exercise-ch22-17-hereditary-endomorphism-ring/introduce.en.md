This exercise establishes that the hereditary property is Morita invariant for finitely generated projective modules. Over a hereditary ring $R$, the endomorphism ring of any finitely generated projective module is also hereditary. This extends to corner rings $eRe$ for non-zero idempotents $e$, since $eRe \cong \operatorname{End}(eR_R)$ and $eR$ is finitely generated projective.
