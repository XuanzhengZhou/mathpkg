This exercise provides concrete examples of projectors and injectors in the ring of $3 \times 3$ upper triangular matrices over a field. Different idempotents yield modules with different homological behaviors: some are both projectors and injectors, some are only one or the other, and the two-sided ideal $ReR$ fails to have either property. This illustrates the subtlety of relative homological algebra in non-semisimple rings.
