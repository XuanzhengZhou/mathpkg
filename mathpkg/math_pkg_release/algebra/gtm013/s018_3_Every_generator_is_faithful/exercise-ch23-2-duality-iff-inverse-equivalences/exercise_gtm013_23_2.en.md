Let $C$ and $D$ be categories and $H': C \to D$ and $H'': D \to C$ be contravariant functors. Prove that $(H', H'')$ is a duality between $C$ and $D$ iff
$$\operatorname{op} \circ H': C \to D^{\operatorname{op}} \quad \text{and} \quad H'' \circ \operatorname{op}: D^{\operatorname{op}} \to C$$
are inverse equivalences. (See Exercise (23.1).)
