Let $R$ be a ring. If $P_R$ is a progenerator, then $R$ and $S = \operatorname{End}(P_R)$ are equivalent. In fact, if

$$P^{\circledast} = \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, R),$$

then ${}_S P_R$ and ${}_R P^{\circledast}_S$ are bimodules and

$$(P \otimes_R -) : {}_R\mathbf{M} \to {}_S\mathbf{M},$$

$$(P^{\circledast} \otimes_S -) : {}_S\mathbf{M} \to {}_R\mathbf{M}$$

are inverse equivalences. Moreover,

$$\operatorname{Hom}_R(P^{\circledast}, -) \cong (P \otimes_R -) \quad \text{and} \quad \operatorname{Hom}_S(P, -) \cong (P^{\circledast} \otimes_S -).$$
