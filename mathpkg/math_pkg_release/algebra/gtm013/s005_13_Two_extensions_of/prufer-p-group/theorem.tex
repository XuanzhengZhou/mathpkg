For a prime $p$, the Prüfer $p$-group $\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}$ is the abelian group
$$\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty} = \left\{ \exp\left(2\pi i \frac{k}{p^n}\right) \mid k \in \mathbb{Z}, n \in \mathbb{N} \right\}$$
or equivalently, the direct limit $\varinjlim \mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z}$ under the natural inclusions $\mathbb{Z}/p^n\mathbb{Z} \hookrightarrow \mathbb{Z}/p^{n+1}\mathbb{Z}$.
