For any two modules $K$ and $N$, one always has at least one extension: the split extension given by the direct product $K \times N$ with the natural inclusion and projection maps. This extension is called the trivial or split extension and represents the zero element in the Ext group $\operatorname{Ext}^1_R(N, K)$.
