*Proof.* This is Exercise 14(1) in the source text. Let $\bar{x} = x + T(M) \in M/T(M)$ be a torsion element annihilated by $0 \neq r \in R$. Then $rx \in T(M)$, so there exists $0 \neq s \in R$ with $s(rx) = (sr)x = 0$. Since $R$ is an integral domain, $sr \neq 0$, hence $x \in T(M)$ and $\bar{x} = 0$.
