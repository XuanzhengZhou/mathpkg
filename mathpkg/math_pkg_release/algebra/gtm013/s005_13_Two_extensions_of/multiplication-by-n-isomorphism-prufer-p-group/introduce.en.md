For the Prüfer $p$-group, multiplication by any integer $n$ coprime to $p$ is an automorphism. This is because $n$ is invertible modulo $p^k$ for every $k$, so the restriction to each finite subgroup $\mathbb{Z}/p^k\mathbb{Z}$ is an automorphism, and the property lifts to the direct limit.
