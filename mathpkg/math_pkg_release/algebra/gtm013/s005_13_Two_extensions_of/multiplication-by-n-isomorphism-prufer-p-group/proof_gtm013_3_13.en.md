*Proof.* This is Exercise 16(1) in the source text. Since $p \nmid n$, $n$ is invertible modulo $p^k$ for all $k$. Hence multiplication by $n$ restricts to an automorphism of each $\mathbb{Z}/p^k\mathbb{Z}$. Since $\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}$ is the direct limit of these groups, the induced map is an automorphism.
