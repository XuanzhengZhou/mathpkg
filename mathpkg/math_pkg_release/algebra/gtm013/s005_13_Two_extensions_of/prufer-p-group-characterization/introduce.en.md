This theorem provides a generator-and-relations characterization of the Prüfer $p$-group: it is the abelian group generated by countably many elements $g_1, g_2, \ldots$ with relations $p g_1 = 0$ and $p g_{n+1} = g_n$. This presentation reveals $\mathbb{Z}_{p^\infty}$ as the colimit of the system $\mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z} \hookrightarrow \mathbb{Z}/p^2\mathbb{Z} \hookrightarrow \cdots$.
