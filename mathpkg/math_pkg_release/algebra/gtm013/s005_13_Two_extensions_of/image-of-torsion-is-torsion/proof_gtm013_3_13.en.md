*Proof.* This is Exercise 14(3) in the source text. Any $y = f(x) \in \operatorname{Im} f$ with $x \in M$. Since $M$ is torsion, there exists $0 \neq r \in R$ with $rx = 0$. Then $r y = r f(x) = f(rx) = f(0) = 0$, so $y \in T(N)$.
