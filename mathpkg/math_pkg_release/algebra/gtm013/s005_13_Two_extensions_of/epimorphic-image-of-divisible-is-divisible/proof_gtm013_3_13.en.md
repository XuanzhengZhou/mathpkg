*Proof.* This is Exercise 15(3) in the source text. Let $n \in N$ and $0 \neq a \in R$. Since $f$ is surjective, choose $m \in M$ with $f(m) = n$. Since $M$ is divisible, $m = a x$ for some $x \in M$. Then $n = f(m) = f(ax) = a f(x)$, witnessing divisibility of $N$.
