Let $_R M_S$ be a faithfully balanced bimodule. Show that there is a ring isomorphism $\phi: \operatorname{Cen} R \rightarrow \operatorname{Cen} S$ such that $km = m\phi(k)$ for all $m \in M$ and $k \in \operatorname{Cen} R$.
