If $R$ is a ring and if $\lambda$ and $\rho$ denote left and right multiplication, then

$$\lambda: R \rightarrow \operatorname{End}(R_R) \quad \text{and} \quad \rho: R \rightarrow \operatorname{End}(_R R)$$

are ring isomorphisms; in particular, the regular bimodule $_R R_R$ is a faithfully balanced bimodule.
