Suppose we have two $S$-modules $_S M$ and $_S N$ and a ring homomorphism $\phi: R \rightarrow S$. Then $\phi$ induces an $R$-module structure on both $M$ and $N$ and every $S$-homomorphism $f: M \rightarrow N$ is also an $R$-homomorphism. Thus as abelian groups

$$\operatorname{Hom}_S(M, N) \leq \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N) = \operatorname{Hom}_{\phi(R)}(M, N) \leq \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(M, N).$$
