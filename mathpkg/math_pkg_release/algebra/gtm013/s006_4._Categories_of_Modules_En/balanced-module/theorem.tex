A non-zero left $R$-module $_R M$ is \textit{balanced} in case the derived bimodule

$$_R M_{\operatorname{End}(_R M)}$$

is a balanced bimodule. Thus $_R M$ is balanced iff the natural homomorphism

$$\lambda: R \rightarrow \operatorname{BiEnd}(_R M)$$

is surjective, and $_R M$ is \textit{faithfully balanced} iff $\lambda$ is an isomorphism.
