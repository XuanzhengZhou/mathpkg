Theorem 15.19 states that for a left artinian ring $R$, the Jacobson radical $J(R)$ is the unique largest nilpotent ideal (whether left, right, or two-sided). This is a fundamental structure theorem: it implies that in a left artinian ring, every nil one-sided ideal is nilpotent, and the radical is simultaneously the smallest ideal modulo which $R$ is semisimple and the largest nilpotent ideal.
