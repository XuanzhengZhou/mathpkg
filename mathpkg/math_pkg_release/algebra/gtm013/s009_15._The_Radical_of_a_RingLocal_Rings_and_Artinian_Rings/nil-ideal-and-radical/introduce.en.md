Corollary 15.12 provides two criteria for an ideal to equal the Jacobson radical. First, a nil ideal whose factor ring is semiprimitive must be the radical. Second, an ideal contained in the radical whose factor ring has the property that every non-zero left ideal contains a non-zero idempotent must also equal the radical.
