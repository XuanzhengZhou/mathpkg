A (left) primitive ideal $P$ of a ring $R$ is a two-sided ideal such that the factor ring $R/P$ is a (left) primitive ring. Since every simple ring is primitive, every maximal ideal is both left and right primitive. However, a left primitive ideal need not be right primitive (Bergman [64]).
