For a ring $R$ with radical $J = J(R)$, the following statements are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $R/J$ is semisimple;
\item[(b)] $R/J$ is left artinian;
\item[(c)] Every product of simple left $R$-modules is semisimple;
\item[(d)] Every product of semisimple left $R$-modules is semisimple;
\item[(e)] For every left $R$-module $M$, $\operatorname{Soc} M = r_M(J)$.
\end{enumerate}
