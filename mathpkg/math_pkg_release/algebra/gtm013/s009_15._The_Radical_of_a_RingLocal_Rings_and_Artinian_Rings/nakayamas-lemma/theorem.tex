For a left ideal $I$ of a ring $R$, the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $I \leq J(R)$;
\item[(b)] For every finitely generated left $R$-module $M$, if $IM = M$, then $M = 0$;
\item[(c)] For every finitely generated left $R$-module $M$, $IM$ is superfluous in $M$.
\end{enumerate}
