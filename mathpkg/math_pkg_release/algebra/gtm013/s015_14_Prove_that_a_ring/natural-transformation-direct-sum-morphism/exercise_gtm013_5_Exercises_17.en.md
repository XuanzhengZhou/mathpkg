Let $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{D}$ be full subcategories of ${}_R \mathbf{M}$ and ${}_S \mathbf{M}$, respectively. Let $F$ and $G$ be additive functors from $\mathcal{C}$ to $\mathcal{D}$ that preserve direct sums, and let $\eta: F \to G$ be a natural transformation. Let $(M_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ be an indexed class in $\mathcal{C}$ with a direct sum $(M, (\iota_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A})$ in $\mathcal{C}$. Prove that $\eta_M$ is an isomorphism if and only if $\eta_{M_\alpha}$ is an isomorphism for each $\alpha \in A$.
