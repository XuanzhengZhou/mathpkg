This is a structural property of the class of $U$-reflexive modules. For a fixed bimodule ${}_R U_S$, a left $R$-module $M$ is $U$-reflexive if the canonical evaluation map $M \to \operatorname{Hom}_S(\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, U), U)$ is an isomorphism. The fact that this class is closed under finite direct sums and direct summands is fundamental to the study of Morita duality and is referenced as Exercise 20.13.
