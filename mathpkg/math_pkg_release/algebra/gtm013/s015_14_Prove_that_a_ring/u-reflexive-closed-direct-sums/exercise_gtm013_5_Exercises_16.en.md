Let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule. Let ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ denote the class of $U$-reflexive left $R$-modules. Then ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ is closed under the formation of finite direct sums and direct summands (20.13). Let

$$0 \to {}_R K \to {}_R M \to {}_R N \to 0$$

be exact. Prove that

(1) If ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are injective and $N$ is $U$-torsionless, then $M$ is reflexive if and only if $K$ and $N$ are reflexive.

(2) If ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are injective and ${}_R U$ is a cogenerator, then ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ is closed under submodules, epimorphic images, and extensions (of modules in ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ by modules in ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$).
