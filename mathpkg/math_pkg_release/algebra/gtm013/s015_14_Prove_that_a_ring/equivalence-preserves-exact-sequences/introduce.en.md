Category equivalences between module categories preserve short exact sequences, including split exact sequences. This means the entire homological algebra of ${}_R \mathbf{M}$ is faithfully mirrored in ${}_S \mathbf{M}$. The proof uses the Fundamental Lemma (21.3) to show that the image of a kernel is the kernel of the image. This result is essential for establishing that Morita equivalent rings share all homological properties.
