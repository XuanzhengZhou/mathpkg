This result strengthens the structural properties of the class of $U$-reflexive modules when the bimodule $U$ satisfies stronger conditions: injectivity on both sides and the left module being a cogenerator. Under these hypotheses, the $U$-reflexive class becomes a Serre subcategory-like class, closed under submodules, quotients, and extensions. This is a key step toward establishing Morita duality, where the class of reflexive modules forms a well-behaved subcategory closed under these basic operations.
