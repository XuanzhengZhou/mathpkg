Let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule. Prove that if ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are injective and ${}_R U$ is a cogenerator, then ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ is closed under submodules, epimorphic images, and extensions (of modules in ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ by modules in ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$).
