Let ${}_R U_S$ be a bimodule. If ${}_R U$ and $U_S$ are injective and ${}_R U$ is a cogenerator, then the class ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ of $U$-reflexive left $R$-modules is closed under submodules, epimorphic images, and extensions (of modules in ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$ by modules in ${}_R \mathcal{R}(U)$).
