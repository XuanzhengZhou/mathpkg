This proposition establishes that a category equivalence between module categories induces isomorphisms on all Hom sets and preserves the epimorphism/monomorphism distinction. In particular, an equivalence restricts to a ring isomorphism between the endomorphism rings of nonzero modules. This is the first structural consequence of equivalence: it preserves the entire morphism structure between modules, not just the objects.
