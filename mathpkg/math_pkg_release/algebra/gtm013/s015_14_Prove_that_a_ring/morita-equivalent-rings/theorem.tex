Two rings $R$ and $S$ are **(Morita) equivalent**, written

$$R \approx S,$$

if the categories of left modules are equivalent:

$${}_R \mathbf{M} \approx {}_S \mathbf{M},$$

i.e., there exist additive equivalences between these categories of modules. Equivalently, ${}_R \mathbf{M}$ and ${}_S \mathbf{M}$ are equivalent if and only if $\mathbf{M}_R$ and $\mathbf{M}_S$ are equivalent.
