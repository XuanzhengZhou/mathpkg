(a) ⇒ (b): If $R$ has a completely faithful semisimple module $S = \bigoplus_A T_\alpha$ with each $T_\alpha$ simple, then for any $R$-module $M \neq 0$, $S \otimes_R M \neq 0$ by Exercise (19.21). This implies $S$ is a faithfully flat module. By Exercise (18.23), a commutative ring with a completely faithful semisimple module is von Neumann regular.

(b) ⇒ (c): Every von Neumann regular commutative ring is co-semisimple by Exercise (18.23).

(c) ⇒ (a): If $R$ is co-semisimple, let $S = \bigoplus_A T_\alpha$ be the direct sum of all simple $R$-modules (one from each isomorphism class). Then $S \otimes T_\alpha \neq 0$ for each $\alpha$. By Exercise (19.16), $S$ is completely faithful and semisimple.
