This exercise is a Yoneda-type result: a natural isomorphism between the covariant Hom functors $\operatorname{Hom}_R(U, -) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_R(V, -)$ implies that $U \cong V$ as bimodules. Explicitly, the bimodule isomorphism is given by evaluating the natural isomorphism at $U$ on the identity map. The same holds for the contravariant Hom functors.
