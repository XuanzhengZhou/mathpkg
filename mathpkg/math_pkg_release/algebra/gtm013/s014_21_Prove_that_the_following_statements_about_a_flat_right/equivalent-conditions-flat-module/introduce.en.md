This exercise characterizes completely faithful flat right modules through four equivalent conditions. A flat right $R$-module $V$ is completely faithful iff it tensor-annihilates no nonzero left module, iff it tensor-annihilates no simple left module, iff $VI \neq V$ for every maximal left ideal $I$. These equivalences connect faithfulness, simplicity, and maximal ideals in the context of flat modules.
