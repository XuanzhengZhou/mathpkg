This exercise characterizes when a pair of tuples forms a dual basis. For elements $x_i \in P_R$ and functionals $f_i \in P^* = \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, R)$, the pair $((x_i), (f_i))$ is a dual basis for $P$ if and only if the transposed pair $((f_i), (\sigma_P(x_i)))$ is a dual basis for $P^*$, where $\sigma_P: P \to P^{**}$ is the evaluation map into the double dual.
