This proposition establishes two fundamental natural isomorphisms in module theory. The first states that the covariant Hom functor $\operatorname{Hom}_R(R, -)$ is naturally isomorphic to the identity functor on $R\mathcal{M}$, via the evaluation map $\rho_M(m)(r) = rm$. The second states that the tensor product functor $(R \otimes_R -)$ is naturally isomorphic to the identity functor via the multiplication map $\tau_M(r \otimes m) = rm$.
