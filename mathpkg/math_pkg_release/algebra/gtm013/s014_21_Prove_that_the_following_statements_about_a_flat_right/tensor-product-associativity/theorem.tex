For every triple of modules $(M_R, {}_R W_S, {}_S N)$ there is an isomorphism

$$v: M \otimes_R (W \otimes_S N) \to (M \otimes_R W) \otimes_S N,$$

defined via

$$v: m \otimes_R (w \otimes_S n) \mapsto (m \otimes_R w) \otimes_S n,$$

that is natural in each of the three variables $M$, $W$, and $N$.
