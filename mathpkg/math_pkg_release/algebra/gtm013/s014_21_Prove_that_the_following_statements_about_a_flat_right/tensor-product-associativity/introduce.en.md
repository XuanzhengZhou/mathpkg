The associativity of the tensor product states that for modules $(M_R, {}_R W_S, {}_S N)$, there is a natural isomorphism $M \otimes_R (W \otimes_S N) \cong (M \otimes_R W) \otimes_S N$ given by $(m \otimes w) \otimes n \leftrightarrow m \otimes (w \otimes n)$. This justifies "dropping parentheses" in iterated tensor products and is natural in all three variables, making it a foundational coherence property of the tensor product.
