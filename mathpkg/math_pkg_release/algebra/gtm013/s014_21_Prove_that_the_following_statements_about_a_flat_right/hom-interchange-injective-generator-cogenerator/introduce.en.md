This exercise uses the Hom interchange isomorphism from Proposition 20.7 to propagate module-theoretic properties between ${}_R U$ and $\operatorname{Hom}_S(N, {}_R U_S)$. For example, if $N_S$ is projective and ${}_R U$ is injective, then $\operatorname{Hom}_S(N, {}_R U_S)$ is injective. Similar implications hold for generators and cogenerators.
