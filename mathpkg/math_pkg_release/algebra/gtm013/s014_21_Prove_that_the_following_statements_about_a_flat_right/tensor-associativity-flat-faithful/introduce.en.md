Using the associativity of tensor product (Proposition 20.8), this exercise derives how flatness and complete faithfulness propagate through tensor products. For instance, the tensor product of two flat modules is flat, and the tensor product of two completely faithful modules is completely faithful. Conversely, complete faithfulness of a tensor product implies complete faithfulness of the first factor.
