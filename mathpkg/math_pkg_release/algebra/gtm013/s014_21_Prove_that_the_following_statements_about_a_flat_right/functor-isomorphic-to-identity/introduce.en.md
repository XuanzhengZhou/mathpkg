If a functor $F$ is naturally isomorphic to the identity functor via $\eta: 1_{\mathcal{C}} \to F$, then $F$ induces an isomorphism on Hom sets: $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_R(F(M), F(N))$. The inverse is given by conjugating with the natural isomorphism: $g \mapsto \eta_N^{-1} \circ g \circ \eta_M$. This shows that any functor isomorphic to the identity is fully faithful.
