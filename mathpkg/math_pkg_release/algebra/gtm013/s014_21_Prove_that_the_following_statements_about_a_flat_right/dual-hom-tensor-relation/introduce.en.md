This is the dual version of Proposition 20.10, providing a natural map $N \otimes_T \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, U) \to \operatorname{Hom}_R(P, N \otimes_T U)$ defined by $(n \otimes \gamma)(p) = n \otimes \gamma(p)$. When $P_R$ is finitely generated and projective, this map is an isomorphism. Together with Proposition 20.10, these results establish the fundamental commutation relations between Hom and tensor for projective modules.
