Given a bimodule ${}_R U_S$, the $U$-dual functor $(-)^* = \operatorname{Hom}(-, {}_R U_S)$ is a contravariant functor from ${}_R\mathcal{M}$ to $\mathcal{M}_S$ (or from $\mathcal{M}_S$ to ${}_R\mathcal{M}$). For a module $M$, its $U$-dual is $M^*$, and iterating gives the double dual $M^{**}$. The dual functor generalizes the familiar vector space dual from linear algebra to the module-theoretic setting with a parameterizing bimodule $U$.
