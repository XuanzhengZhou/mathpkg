Lemma 19.11 is the fundamental Tensor-Hom Adjunction: $\operatorname{Hom}_S(U \otimes_R M, N) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, \operatorname{Hom}_S(U, N))$. This natural isomorphism is the heart of many duality arguments in homological algebra. It underpins the exactness test (19.12), the relationship between flatness and injectivity (19.14), and ultimately the derived functors $\operatorname{Tor}$ and $\operatorname{Ext}$. The adjunction expresses the fact that giving an $S$-homomorphism from $U \otimes_R M$ to $N$ is equivalent to giving an $R$-homomorphism from $M$ to $\operatorname{Hom}_S(U, N)$.
