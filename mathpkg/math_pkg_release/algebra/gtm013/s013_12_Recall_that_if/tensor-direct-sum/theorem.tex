If $(M_R, (i_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A})$ is a direct sum of $(M_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}$ and $({}_R N, (j_\beta)_{\beta \in B})$ is a direct sum of $(N_\beta)_{\beta \in B}$, then
$$(M \otimes_R N, (i_\alpha \otimes j_\beta)_{\alpha, \beta \in A \times B})$$
is a direct sum of $(M_\alpha \otimes_R N_\beta)_{\alpha, \beta \in A \times B}$.
