The tensor product $M \otimes_R N$ of a right $R$-module $M$ and a left $R$-module $N$ is the universal recipient of $R$-balanced maps from $M \times N$. It transforms bilinear phenomena into linear maps: each $R$-balanced map $\beta: M \times N \to A$ corresponds uniquely to a $\mathbb{Z}$-homomorphism $M \otimes_R N \to A$. The elements of $M \otimes_R N$ are finite sums $\sum_i (m_i \otimes n_i)$ subject to bilinearity relations, making the tensor product one of the most fundamental constructions in homological algebra.
