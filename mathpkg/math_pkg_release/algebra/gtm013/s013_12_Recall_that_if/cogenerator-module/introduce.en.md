A cogenerator in the category of left $R$-modules is the dual notion of a generator. While a generator ${}_R G$ has the property that every module is an epimorphic image of a direct sum of copies of $G$, a cogenerator ${}_R C$ has the property that every module can be embedded in a direct product of copies of $C$. An important example is $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$ as a cogenerator in the category of abelian groups.
