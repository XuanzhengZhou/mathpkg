Corollary 18.17 gives a useful external characterization of finitely cogenerated modules: a module is finitely cogenerated exactly when any embedding into an arbitrary direct product can be factored through a finite subproduct. This is dual to the characterization of finitely generated modules via epimorphisms from direct sums. The proof relies on the fact that finitely cogenerated modules are cogenerated by finitely many injective envelopes of simple modules.
