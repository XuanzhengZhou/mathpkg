By (16.4), $(\bigoplus_A V_\alpha)^* \cong \prod_A (V_\alpha)^*$. By (16.11), the product $\prod_A (V_\alpha)^*$ is injective iff each $(V_\alpha)^*$ is injective. Applying Lemma (19.14), $\bigoplus_A V_\alpha$ is flat iff $(\bigoplus_A V_\alpha)^*$ is injective iff each $(V_\alpha)^*$ is injective iff each $V_\alpha$ is flat.
