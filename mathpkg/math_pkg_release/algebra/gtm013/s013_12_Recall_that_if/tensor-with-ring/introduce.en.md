Proposition 19.6 shows that the regular module $R$ acts as the identity for the tensor product: $M \otimes_R R \cong M$ and $R \otimes_R N \cong N$. These isomorphisms are natural and fundamental for computations. For example, they imply that $R \otimes_R f$ is monic whenever $f$ is monic, which is the key step in proving that projective modules (hence free modules) are flat. The proof constructs the isomorphism using the $R$-balanced map $(m, r) \mapsto mr$.
