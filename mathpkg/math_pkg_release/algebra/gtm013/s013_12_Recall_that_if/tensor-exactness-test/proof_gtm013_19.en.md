By (19.11), there is a commutative diagram where the vertical maps $\phi$, $\phi$, $\phi$ are isomorphisms relating $\operatorname{Hom}_R(-, \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(U, C))$ and $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(U \otimes_R -, C)$. The top row is exact iff the bottom row is exact. But since $C$ is an injective cogenerator in $\mathbb{Z}\mathbf{M}$, the bottom row (involving $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(-, C)$) is exact iff $U \otimes_R M \to U \otimes_R M \to U \otimes_R M$ is exact (by (18.1) and (18.14)).
