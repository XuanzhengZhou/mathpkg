Lemma 19.12 provides a powerful tool for checking exactness of tensor product sequences by translating them into exactness of Hom sequences into the dual module $U^* = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(U, C)$, where $C$ is an injective cogenerator in the category of abelian groups (typically $\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}$). This translation uses the Tensor-Hom Adjunction (19.11) and the fact that $C$ both preserves and reflects exactness. This lemma is the key step in proving that $V$ is flat iff $V^*$ is injective (19.14).
