This proposition establishes that the class of injective modules is closed under direct products and direct summands. It follows directly from the properties of the Hom functor: $\operatorname{Hom}_R(-, \prod E_\alpha) \cong \prod \operatorname{Hom}_R(-, E_\alpha)$ and the fact that a direct summand of an injective module inherits injectivity because exactness of the Hom sequence is preserved when restricting to a summand.
