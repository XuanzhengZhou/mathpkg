(1) Prove that if $R$ is a ring with $J(R) = 0$, then no non-projective $R$-module has a projective cover. In particular, no finite $\mathbb{Z}$-module has a projective cover.
(2) Prove that if $R$ is the endomorphism ring of an infinite dimensional vector space $M_D$, then $R \times R^{op}$ is von Neumann regular but neither left nor right co-semisimple.
