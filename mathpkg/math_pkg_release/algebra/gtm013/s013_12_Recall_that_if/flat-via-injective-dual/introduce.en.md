Lemma 19.14 establishes a fundamental duality between flatness and injectivity: a right $R$-module $V$ is flat exactly when its character module $V^* = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(V, \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z})$ is an injective left $R$-module. This translation allows one to use the well-developed theory of injective modules (Baer Criterion, injective envelopes, etc.) to study flat modules. The lemma is an immediate consequence of the exactness test (19.12) applied to monomorphisms. This duality is a recurring theme: projective $\leftrightarrow$ injective, flat $\leftrightarrow$ absolutely pure.
