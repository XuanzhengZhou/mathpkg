Apply (19.12) to the monomorphism $0 \to K \to M$. The sequence $V \otimes_R K \to V \otimes_R M$ is exact (i.e., $V$ is $M$-flat) iff $\operatorname{Hom}_R(M, V^*) \to \operatorname{Hom}_R(K, V^*)$ is exact (i.e., $V^*$ is $M$-injective). Taking this over all monomorphisms $K \to M$ gives the equivalence between $V$ being flat and $V^*$ being injective.
