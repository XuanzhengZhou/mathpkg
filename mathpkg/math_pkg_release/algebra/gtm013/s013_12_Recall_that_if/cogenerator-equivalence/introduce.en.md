Proposition 18.14 gives several useful characterizations of cogenerators in terms of the Hom functor. Condition (b) says that $\operatorname{Hom}_R(-, C)$ detects zero morphisms, and condition (c) says this functor is faithful. Condition (d) is particularly important: it says that $C$ is a cogenerator precisely when $\operatorname{Hom}_R(-, C)$ reflects exactness. This is dual to the characterization of generators in (8.3).
