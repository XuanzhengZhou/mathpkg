Proposition 19.16 establishes that the class of flat modules contains all projective modules. The proof reduces to showing that the regular module $R_R$ is flat (since projective modules are direct summands of free modules, and direct summands of flat modules are flat). The flatness of $R_R$ follows from the isomorphism $R \otimes_R M \cong M$ (19.6): if $f: M \to M$ is monic, then $R \otimes_R f$ is isomorphic to $f$ itself, hence monic.
