The identity clearly holds on the generators $m \otimes n$ of $M \otimes_R N$:
$$(f \otimes g)((f \otimes g)(m \otimes n)) = (f \otimes g)(f(m) \otimes g(n)) = f(f(m)) \otimes g(g(n)) = (f f)(m) \otimes (g g)(n) = ((f f) \otimes (g g))(m \otimes n).$$
Since the identity holds on generators and all maps are $\mathbb{Z}$-homomorphisms, it holds everywhere.
