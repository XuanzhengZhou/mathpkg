**Maschke's Theorem.** Let $G$ be a finite group of order $n$ and let $K$ be a field whose characteristic does not divide $n$. Thus $n = n \cdot 1$ is invertible in $K$. Using the results of Exercise (7.17) prove that the group ring $KG$ is semisimple.
