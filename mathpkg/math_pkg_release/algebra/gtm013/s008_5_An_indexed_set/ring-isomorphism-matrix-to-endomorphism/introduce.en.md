For a left $R$-module $M$, the $n \times n$ matrix ring over the endomorphism ring of $M$ is naturally isomorphic to the endomorphism ring of the $n$-fold direct sum $M^{(n)}$. This isomorphism sends a matrix $[\alpha_{ij}]$ of endomorphisms to the endomorphism of $M^{(n)}$ defined by matrix multiplication on column vectors. The result is fundamental for relating matrix representations to module decompositions.
