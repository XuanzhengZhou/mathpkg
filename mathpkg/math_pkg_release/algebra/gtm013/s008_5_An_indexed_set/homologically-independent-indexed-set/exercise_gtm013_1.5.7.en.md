Let $M$ be a left $R$-module and set $B = \operatorname{BiEnd}({}_R M)$. Let $K, L, M_\alpha$ $(\alpha \in A)$ and $N_\gamma$ $(\gamma \in C)$ be submodules of ${}_R M$. Prove that

(1) $K$ is an (indecomposable) direct summand of ${}_R M$ iff $K$ is an (indecomposable) direct summand of ${}_B M$.
