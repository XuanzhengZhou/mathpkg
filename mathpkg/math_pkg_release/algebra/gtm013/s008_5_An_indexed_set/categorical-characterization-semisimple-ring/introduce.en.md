This corollary characterizes semisimple rings purely in categorical terms: $R$ is semisimple if and only if in the category ${}_R\mathbf{M}$ of left $R$-modules, every monomorphism splits (i.e., is a section), equivalently every epimorphism splits (i.e., is a retraction). This follows immediately from the fact that in a semisimple category all short exact sequences split.
