Let $R$ have a simple generator ${}_R T$ and let $D = \operatorname{End}({}_R T)$.
(1) Prove that if ${}_R M$ is simple, then $M \cong T$.
(2) For some $n$, ${}_R R \cong T^{(n)}$ and $R \cong \mathbb{M}_n(D)$. (See (13.4).) Prove that $\dim(T_D) = n$ and that $\lambda: R \to \operatorname{BiEnd}({}_R T)$ is an isomorphism. [Hint: With the notation of (13.1), $\mathbb{C}_n(D)$ is a simple left $R$-module.]
(3) Prove that $\operatorname{Cen} R \cong \operatorname{Cen} D$ and hence that the center of $R$ is a field.
