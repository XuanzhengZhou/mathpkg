For a direct sum $M = M' \oplus M''$, the restriction of biendomorphisms from $M$ to the summand $M'$ yields a ring homomorphism between biendomorphism rings. This restriction is injective when $M'$ either generates or cogenerates $M''$, and is an isomorphism when $M'$ both generates and cogenerates $M''$. This lemma is a key technical tool used in the proof of the Density Theorem.
