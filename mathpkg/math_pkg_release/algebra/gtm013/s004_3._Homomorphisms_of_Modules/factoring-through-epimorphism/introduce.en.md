This factorization result is a module-theoretic analogue of the universal property of quotient maps. If a homomorphism $f$ vanishes on the kernel of an epimorphism $g$, then $f$ descends uniquely to a map $h$ on the image of $g$. This is one half of the fundamental factorization lemma for module homomorphisms.
