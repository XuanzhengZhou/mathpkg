Let $M$ and $N$ be left $R$-modules and let $f: M \to N$ be an $R$-homomorphism. The following statements are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item[(a)] $f$ is an epimorphism onto $N$;
  \item[(b)] $\text{Im}\, f = N$;
  \item[(c)] For every left $R$-module $K$ and every pair $g, h: N \to K$ of $R$-homomorphisms, $gf = hf$ implies $g = h$;
  \item[(d)] For every left $R$-module $K$ and every $R$-homomorphism $g: N \to K$, $gf = 0$ implies $g = 0$.
\end{enumerate}
