Any ring homomorphism $\phi: R \to S$ endows the target ring $S$ with the structure of an $(R,R)$-bimodule. When $\phi$ is surjective, this yields a ring-theoretic version of the lattice isomorphism theorem (Corollary 3.8), establishing correspondences between the ideal lattices of $S$ and the ideals of $R$ containing the kernel.
