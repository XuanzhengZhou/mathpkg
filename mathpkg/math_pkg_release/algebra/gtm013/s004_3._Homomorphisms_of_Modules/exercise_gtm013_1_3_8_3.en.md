Prove that there exists an exact sequence (over $\mathbb{Z}$)
$$\cdots \to \mathbb{Z}_4 \to \mathbb{Z}_4 \to \mathbb{Z}_4 \to \mathbb{Z}_4 \to \cdots$$
(i.e., an infinite exact sequence in which every module is $\mathbb{Z}_4$).
