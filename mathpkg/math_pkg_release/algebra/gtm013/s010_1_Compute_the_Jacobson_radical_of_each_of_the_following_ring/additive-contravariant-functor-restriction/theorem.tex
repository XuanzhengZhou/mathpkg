Let $\mathbf{C}$ be a full subcategory of $R$-modules and let $\mathbf{D}$ be a full subcategory of $S$-modules. If $T: \mathbf{C} \to \mathbf{D}$ is an additive contravariant functor, then for each pair $M, N$ of modules in $\mathbf{C}$, the restriction

$$T|_{M,N}: \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, N) \longrightarrow \operatorname{Hom}_S(T(N), T(M))$$

is an abelian group homomorphism.
