This exercise connects Morita duality theory with semiperfect rings. Part (1) shows that rings admitting a Morita duality are necessarily semiperfect. Part (2) proves that the endomorphism ring of any injective module has the idempotent lifting property modulo its Jacobson radical. The hint for part (2) suggests using the injective hull decomposition from (18.20).
