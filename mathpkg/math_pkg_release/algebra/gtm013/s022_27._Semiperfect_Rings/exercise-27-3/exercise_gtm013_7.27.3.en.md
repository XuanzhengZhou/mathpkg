Prove:

(1) If ${}_R U_S$ defines a Morita duality, then $R$ and $S$ are semiperfect.

(2) If $R$ is any ring and ${}_R E$ is injective with $S = \operatorname{End}({}_R E)$, then idempotents lift modulo $J(S)$.

[Hint: If $K = \operatorname{Ker}(g - g^2) \trianglelefteq E$ (18.20), then $E = E(K) \oplus E(K(1-g))$. Let $e$ and $1-e$ be the corresponding idempotents.]
