Exercise 27.6 characterizes semiperfect rings that are Morita equivalent to a local ring. Such rings are precisely the matrix rings over local rings, or equivalently semiperfect rings having (up to isomorphism) exactly one simple module. This is the semiperfect analogue of the Wedderburn-Artin characterization of simple Artinian rings as matrix rings over division rings.
