Since property (c) of (27.6) — every simple module has a projective cover — is clearly categorical, (21.6), (21.8), we have the final assertion: any ring Morita equivalent to $R$ is semiperfect. On the other hand, $\operatorname{End}({}_R P)$ is of the form $e S e$ for some $S$ Morita equivalent to $R$ and some idempotent $e \in S$. Thus (27.7) applies.
