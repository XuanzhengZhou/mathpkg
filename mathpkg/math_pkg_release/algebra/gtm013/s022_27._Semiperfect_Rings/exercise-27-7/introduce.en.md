This exercise asks to compute the basic ring of a block upper triangular matrix ring over a division ring $D$. Such rings are semiperfect and their basic rings turn out to be the subring of matrices where each diagonal block is $D$ itself (rather than a full matrix ring). The indecomposability comes from the fact that the off-diagonal blocks ensure all primitive idempotents are linked.
