Let $R = \{m/n \in \mathbb{Q} \mid 2 \nmid n \text{ and } 3 \nmid n \text{ (} m/n \text{ in lowest terms)}\}$.

(1) Prove that $R$ is a ring and that $2R$ and $3R$ are the only maximal ideals in $R$.

(2) Show that $R/J(R)$ is semisimple but idempotents do not lift modulo $J(R)$.
