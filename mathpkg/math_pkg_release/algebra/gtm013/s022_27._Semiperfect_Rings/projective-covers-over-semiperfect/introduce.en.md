This characterization provides an explicit construction of projective covers for finitely generated modules over semiperfect rings. Given a finitely generated module $M$, its projective cover $P$ is completely determined by the semisimple module $M/JM$: one simply takes the direct sum of the indecomposable projectives $R e_i$ with multiplicities given by the composition factors of $M/JM$. This shows that in a semiperfect ring, the indecomposable projective modules serve as "building blocks" and the projective cover is accessible via the Jacobson radical.
