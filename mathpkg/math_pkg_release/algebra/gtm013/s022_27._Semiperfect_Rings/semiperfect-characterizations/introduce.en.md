Theorem 27.6 provides four equivalent characterizations of semiperfect rings, linking the internal idempotent structure (condition b), the module-theoretic property that simple modules admit projective covers (condition c), and the global property that all finitely generated modules have projective covers (condition d). The equivalence $(a) \Leftrightarrow (b)$ shows that a semiperfect ring decomposes into a finite direct sum of left ideals each with a local endomorphism ring, generalizing the structure of semisimple rings.
