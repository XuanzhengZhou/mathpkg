It is easy to show that condition (b) of (27.6) is preserved under surjective ring homomorphisms. If $R$ has a complete orthogonal set of idempotents $e_1, \ldots, e_n$ with each $e_i R e_i$ local, and $\varphi: R \to R/I$ is the natural surjection, then $\varphi(e_1), \ldots, \varphi(e_n)$ form a complete orthogonal set for $R/I$, and each $\varphi(e_i)(R/I)\varphi(e_i) \cong e_i R e_i / (e_i R e_i \cap I)$ is a factor ring of a local ring, hence local.
