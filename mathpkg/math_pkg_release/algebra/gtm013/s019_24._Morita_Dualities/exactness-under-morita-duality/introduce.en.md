Corollary 24.2 states that under a Morita duality, the Hom functor $(\cdot)^* = \operatorname{Hom}(-, U)$ preserves and reflects exactness of sequences. This is a direct consequence of $U$ being an injective cogenerator: the functor is exact, and because $U$ cogenerates, the evaluation map $\sigma_M$ is monic, giving the reflection property. The statement implies that $f$ is epic exactly when $f^*$ is monic, and $f$ is monic exactly when $f^*$ is epic.
