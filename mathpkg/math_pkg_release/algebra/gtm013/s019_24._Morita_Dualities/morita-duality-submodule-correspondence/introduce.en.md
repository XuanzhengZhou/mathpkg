Proposition 24.5 establishes that under a Morita duality, the annihilator maps induce a perfect lattice anti-isomorphism between the submodule lattices of a $U$-reflexive module $M$ and its dual $M^*$. This is the key structural result that allows properties of submodules to be dualized. Crucially, sums correspond to intersections and vice versa, and the essential/superfluous dichotomy is also dualized.
