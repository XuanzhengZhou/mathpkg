Let $R$ and $S$ be rings. Then the following are equivalent:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] There exists a duality between the category ${}_R \mathbf{FM}$ of finitely generated left $R$-modules and the category $\mathbf{FM}_S$ of finitely generated right $S$-modules;
\item[(b)] $R$ is left artinian and there exists a Morita duality between $R$ and $S$;
\item[(c)] $S$ is right artinian and there exists a Morita duality between $R$ and $S$.
\end{enumerate}

In particular, in order to have a duality between ${}_R \mathbf{FM}$ and $\mathbf{FM}_S$ it is necessary that $R$ be left artinian and $S$ be right artinian.
