Proposition 24.4 connects the general theory of annihilators to the canonical map $\sigma_M: M 	o M^{**}$ and the reject of $U$ in $M$. Part (1) identifies the left annihilator of the dual $M^*$ with both the reject $\operatorname{Rej}_M(U)$ and the kernel of the evaluation map. Part (2) gives a precise condition under which the annihilator Galois connection closes: $l_M(r_{M^*}(K)) = K$ exactly when $U$ cogenerates the quotient $M/K$.
