Osofsky's Lemma is a crucial technical result that places a strong finiteness restriction on Morita dualities. It shows that under a Morita duality, infinite direct sums of non-zero modules can never be $U$-reflexive. This result is the key step in proving that rings admitting a Morita duality must be artinian.
