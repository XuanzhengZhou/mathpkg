A finitely generated projective module is precisely a direct summand of a finite-rank free module. This corollary refines Theorem 17.2 by adding the finitely generated condition, yielding a clean structural description: $P$ is finitely generated projective iff it appears as a direct summand of $R^{(n)}$ for some finite $n$.
