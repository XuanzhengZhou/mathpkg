A generator in a module category is a module whose Hom functor reflects zero morphisms, epimorphisms, and exactness. This proposition gives the dual analogues of the projective module characterizations in Proposition 17.1, showing how the Hom functor ${}\\operatorname{Hom}_R(G, -)$ detects the structure of morphisms and sequences in the category when $G$ is a generator.
