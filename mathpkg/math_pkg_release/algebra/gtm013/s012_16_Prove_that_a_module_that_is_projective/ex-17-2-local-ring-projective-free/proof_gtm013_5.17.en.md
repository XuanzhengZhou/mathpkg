**Proof.** Exercise 17.2. [Hint: See (12.7).] The proof uses the fact that over a local ring $R$ with unique maximal ideal $J$, the quotient $R/J$ is a division ring. If $P$ is finitely generated projective, then $P/JP$ is a finite-dimensional vector space over $R/J$. Lifting a basis gives a minimal generating set for $P$, which turns out to be a free basis. Equivalently, using (17.3), $P \oplus P' \cong R^{(n)}$, and the local property forces $P' = 0$.
