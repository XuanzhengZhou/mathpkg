Two idempotents generate isomorphic cyclic projective left ideals precisely when their quotients modulo the radical are isomorphic, or equivalently when there exist elements intertwining them in a manner analogous to Morita equivalence. This proposition connects the module-theoretic and ring-theoretic perspectives on projective modules generated by idempotents.
