This corollary characterizes faithful modules in terms of the reject: a module  M$ is faithful if and only if $\mathrm{Rej}_R M = 0$, which happens exactly when $ cogenerates the regular module $. The reject $\mathrm{Rej}_R M$ equals the left annihilator (M)$, the intersection of annihilators of all elements of $ in $.
