Let $\mathcal{U}$ be the set of all simple abelian groups. Prove that for an abelian group $M$,

(1) $\mathrm{Tr}_M(\mathcal{U}) = \{x \in M \mid x \text{ has square free order}\}$.

(2) $\mathrm{Rej}_M(\mathcal{U}) = \bigcap \{N \leq M \mid N \text{ is a maximal subgroup of } M\}$.
