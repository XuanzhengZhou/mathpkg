Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a class of modules, let $M$ and $N$ be modules, and let $f: M \to N$ be a homomorphism. Then

$$f(\mathrm{Tr}_M(\mathcal{U})) \leq \mathrm{Tr}_N(\mathcal{U}) \quad \text{and} \quad f(\mathrm{Rej}_M(\mathcal{U})) \leq \mathrm{Rej}_N(\mathcal{U}).$$

In particular, $\mathrm{Tr}_M(\mathcal{U})$ and $\mathrm{Rej}_M(\mathcal{U})$ are left $R$-right $\mathrm{End}(_R M)$-bisubmodules of $M$.
