Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a class of modules. The **reject** of $\mathcal{U}$ in $M$ is defined by

$$\mathrm{Rej}_M(\mathcal{U}) = \cap \{ \mathrm{Ker}\, h \mid h: M \rightarrow U \text{ for some } U \in \mathcal{U} \}.$$

In the particular case where $\mathcal{U} = \{U\}$ is a singleton,

$$\mathrm{Rej}_M(U) = \cap \{ \mathrm{Ker}\, h \mid h \in \mathrm{Hom}_R(M, U) \}.$$
