Proposition 8.5 is the dual of Proposition 8.4 and establishes the closure properties of $\mathrm{Cog}(\mathcal{U})$ and $\mathrm{FCog}(\mathcal{U})$. Part (1) states that if a module is cogenerated by $\mathcal{U}$, then any submodule (via a monomorphism) is also cogenerated by $\mathcal{U}$. Part (2) states that arbitrary (resp. finite) direct products of modules cogenerated by $\mathcal{U}$ remain cogenerated by $\mathcal{U}$. Together with Proposition 8.4, these results show that generation and cogeneration behave well with respect to the basic categorical constructions.
