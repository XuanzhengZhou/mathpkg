Prove that there exist modules $U$ and $M$ such that:

(1) $M$ is generated by $U$ but not every submodule of $M$ is generated by $U$.

[Hint: Let $R$ be the ring of $2 \times 2$ upper triangular matrices over a field and let $U = M$ be a left ideal of $R$.]

(2) $M$ is cogenerated by $U$ but not every factor module of $M$ is cogenerated by $U$.
