Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a class of modules. The **trace** of $\mathcal{U}$ in $M$ is defined by

$$\mathrm{Tr}_M(\mathcal{U}) = \Sigma \{ \mathrm{Im}\, h \mid h: U \rightarrow M \text{ for some } U \in \mathcal{U} \}.$$

In the particular case where $\mathcal{U} = \{U\}$ is a singleton,

$$\mathrm{Tr}_M(U) = \Sigma \{ \mathrm{Im}\, h \mid h \in \mathrm{Hom}_R(U, M) \}.$$
