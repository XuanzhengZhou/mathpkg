Let $\mathcal{U}$ be a class of modules, and let $M$ be a module. Then

(1) $\mathrm{Tr}_M(\mathcal{U})$ is the unique largest submodule $L$ of $M$ generated by $\mathcal{U}$;

(2) $\mathrm{Rej}_M(\mathcal{U})$ is the unique smallest submodule $K$ of $M$ such that $M/K$ is cogenerated by $\mathcal{U}$.
