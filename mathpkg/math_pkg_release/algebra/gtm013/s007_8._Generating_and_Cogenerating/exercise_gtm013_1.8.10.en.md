Let $R$ be the ring of $n \times n$ upper triangular matrices over a field $Q$, and let $\mathcal{U}$ be the class of simple left $R$-modules. Prove that:

(1) $\mathrm{Tr}_R(\mathcal{U}) = \{[a_{ij}] \in R \mid a_{ij} = 0 \ (i \geq 2)\}$.

(2) $\mathrm{Rej}_R(\mathcal{U}) = \{[a_{ij}] \in R \mid a_{kk} = 0 \ (k = 1, \ldots, n)\}$.
