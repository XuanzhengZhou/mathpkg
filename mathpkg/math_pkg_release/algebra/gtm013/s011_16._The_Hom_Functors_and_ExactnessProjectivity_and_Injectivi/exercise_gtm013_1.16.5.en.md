(1) Let $E$ be $U$-injective and let $U$ finitely cogenerate $V$. Prove that $Tr_E(V) \leq Tr_E(U)$. In particular, if $V$ generates $E$, then so does $U$.

(2) Let $P$ be $U$-projective and let $U$ generate $V$. Prove that $Rej_P(V) \geq Rej_P(U)$. In particular, if $V$ cogenerates $P$, then so does $U$.
