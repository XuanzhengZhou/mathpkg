Let ${}_R M$ be a module. Denote by $\mathcal{P}_R(M)$ the class of all $M$-projective modules and by $\mathcal{I}_R(M)$ the class of all $M$-injective modules. Both $\mathcal{P}_R(M)$ and $\mathcal{I}_R(M)$ contain all projective and injective modules, respectively, and in particular both contain the zero module.
