The projectivity class $\mathcal{P}_R(M)$ (respectively, injectivity class $\mathcal{I}_R(M)$) is the collection of all modules that are $M$-projective (respectively, $M$-injective). These classes generalize the notions of projective and injective modules: every projective module belongs to every projectivity class, and every injective module belongs to every injectivity class.
