Corollary 16.5 asserts that the isomorphisms $Hom_R(\oplus_A U_\alpha, M) \cong \prod_A Hom_R(U_\alpha, M)$ and $Hom_R(M, \prod_A U_\alpha) \cong \prod_A Hom_R(M, U_\alpha)$ from Proposition 16.4 are natural in the module argument. The naturality means these isomorphisms commute with morphisms $f: M \to N$, making them natural $\mathbb{Z}$-isomorphisms of functors.
