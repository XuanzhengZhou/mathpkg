A module $U$ is $M$-projective (projective relative to $M$) if every homomorphism from $U$ to a quotient of $M$ lifts to a homomorphism from $U$ to $M$. This is a relative version of the usual notion of projectivity: $U$ is projective if and only if it is $M$-projective for all modules $M$. The $M$-projectivity concept is tied to the exactness of the covariant Hom functor $Hom_R(U, -)$ on sequences with middle term $M$.
