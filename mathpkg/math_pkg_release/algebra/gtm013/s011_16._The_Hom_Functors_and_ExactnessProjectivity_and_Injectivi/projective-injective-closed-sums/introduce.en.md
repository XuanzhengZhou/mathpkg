Corollary 16.11 specializes Proposition 16.10 from the relative notions of $M$-projectivity and $M$-injectivity to the absolute notions. Since a module is projective precisely when it is $M$-projective for all $M$, and injective precisely when it is $M$-injective for all $M$, the closure properties carry over: direct sums of projectives are projective, and direct products of injectives are injective.
