Theorem 16.1 establishes that the two Hom functors $Hom_R(U, -)$ and $Hom_R(-, U)$ induced by a bimodule ${}_R U_S$ are additive functors. The covariant functor $Hom_R(U, -)$ preserves addition and composition of morphisms in the forward direction, while the contravariant functor $Hom_R(-, U)$ reverses composition and preserves addition.
