Let $U$ and $M$ be left $R$-modules. Then the following are equivalent:

(a) $U$ is $M$-injective;

(b) For every short exact sequence with middle term $M$

$$0 \to K \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} N \to 0$$

the sequence

$$0 \to Hom_R(N, U) \xrightarrow{g^*} Hom_R(M, U) \xrightarrow{f^*} Hom_R(K, U) \to 0$$

is exact.
