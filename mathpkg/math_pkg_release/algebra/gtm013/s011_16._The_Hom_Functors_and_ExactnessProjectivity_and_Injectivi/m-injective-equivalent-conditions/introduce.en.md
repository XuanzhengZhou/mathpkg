Proposition 16.8 gives equivalent characterizations of $M$-injectivity analogous to those for $M$-projectivity. Condition (a) is the diagrammatic extension definition. Condition (b) characterizes $M$-injectivity in terms of exactness of the contravariant Hom functor $Hom_R(-, U)$ on short exact sequences with middle term $M$. The proof uses methods similar to those of Proposition 16.7.
