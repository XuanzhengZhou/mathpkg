Proposition 16.10 establishes that $M$-projectivity is closed under arbitrary direct sums and $M$-injectivity is closed under arbitrary direct products. Specifically, a direct sum is $M$-projective precisely when each summand is, and a direct product is $M$-injective precisely when each factor is. This follows from the natural isomorphisms of Corollary 16.5.
