Proposition 16.6 establishes that both Hom functors $Hom_R(U, -)$ and $Hom_R(-, U)$ are left exact. Given a short exact sequence $0 \to K \to M \to N \to 0$, the covariant Hom functor preserves exactness at the left and middle terms, while the contravariant Hom functor reverses arrows and preserves exactness at the corresponding positions.
