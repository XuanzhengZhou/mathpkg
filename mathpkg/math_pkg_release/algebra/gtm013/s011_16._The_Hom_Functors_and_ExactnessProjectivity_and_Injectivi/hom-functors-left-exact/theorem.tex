The Hom functors are left exact. Thus, in particular, if ${}_R U$ is a module, then for every short exact sequence

$$0 \to K \xrightarrow{f} M \xrightarrow{g} N \to 0$$

in ${}_R\mathcal{M}$, the induced sequences

$$0 \to Hom_R(U, K) \xrightarrow{f_*} Hom_R(U, M) \xrightarrow{g_*} Hom_R(U, N)$$

and

$$0 \to Hom_R(N, U) \xrightarrow{g^*} Hom_R(M, U) \xrightarrow{f^*} Hom_R(K, U)$$

are exact.
