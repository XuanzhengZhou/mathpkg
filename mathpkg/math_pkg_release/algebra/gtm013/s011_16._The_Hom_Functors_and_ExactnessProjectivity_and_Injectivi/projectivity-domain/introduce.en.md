The projectivity domain $\mathcal{P}_R^{-1}(U)$ of a module $U$ is the class of all modules $M$ for which $U$ is $M$-projective. While the projectivity class $\mathcal{P}_R(M)$ collects modules projective relative to a fixed $M$, the projectivity domain fixes $U$ and collects the modules relative to which $U$ is projective. Propositions 16.12 and 16.13 describe the structure of projectivity and injectivity domains.
