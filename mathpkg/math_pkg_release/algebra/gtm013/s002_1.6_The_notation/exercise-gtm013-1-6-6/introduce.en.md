This exercise realizes the quaternion division ring $\mathbb{H}$ as a subring of $2 \times 2$ complex matrices. The standard basis $\{1, i, j, k\}$ satisfies the quaternion relations $i^2 = j^2 = k^2 = -1$ and $ijk = -1$. This matrix representation provides a concrete model of a noncommutative division ring.
