The set $\mathbb{CFM}_{\Gamma}(R)$ of all column-finite square matrices over $R$ forms a ring under matrix addition and multiplication. Similarly, $\mathbb{RFM}_{\Gamma}(R)$, the row-finite square matrices, form a ring. Scalar matrices (diagonal matrices with a constant element $r$ on the diagonal) embed $R$ into these matrix rings.
