Let $G$ and $H$ be groups and $\theta: H \to \operatorname{Aut} G$ a homomorphism. The \emph{semidirect product} $G \times_{\theta} H$ is the set $G \times H$ with the binary operation:
$$(g,h)(g',h') = (g[\theta(h)(g')], hh').$$
The identity is $(e,e)$ and $(g,h)^{-1} = (\theta(h^{-1})(g^{-1}), h^{-1})$.
