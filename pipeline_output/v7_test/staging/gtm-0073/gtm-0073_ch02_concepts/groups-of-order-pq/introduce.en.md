Classifies all groups of order $pq$ (product of two primes, $p > q$) up to isomorphism. The classification splits into two cases: the cyclic abelian case $\mathbb{Z}_{pq}$ and the non-abelian case described by the semidirect product $C_p \rtimes_\theta C_q$, which exists only when $q \mid p-1$.
