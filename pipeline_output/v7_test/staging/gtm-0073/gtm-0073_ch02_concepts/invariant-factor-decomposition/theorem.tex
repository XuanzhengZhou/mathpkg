Every finitely generated abelian group $G$ is (isomorphic to) a finite direct sum of cyclic groups in which the finite cyclic summands (if any) are of orders $m_1, \ldots, m_t$, where $m_1 > 1$ and
$$m_1 \mid m_2 \mid \cdots \mid m_t.$$
That is,
$$G \cong \mathbb{Z} \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathbb{Z} \oplus \mathbb{Z}_{m_1} \oplus \cdots \oplus \mathbb{Z}_{m_t}.$$
