The following conditions on a nonzero ring $R$ with identity are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $R$ is semisimple left Artinian;
\item[(ii)] every unitary left $R$-module is projective;
\item[(iii)] every unitary left $R$-module is injective;
\item[(iv)] every short exact sequence of unitary left $R$-modules is split exact;
\item[(v)] every nonzero unitary left $R$-module is semisimple;
\item[(vi)] $R$ is itself a unitary semisimple left $R$-module;
\item[(vii)] every left ideal of $R$ is of the form $Re$ with $e$ idempotent;
\item[(viii)] $R$ is the (internal) direct sum of minimal left ideals $K_1, \ldots, K_m$ such that $K_i = Re_i$ and $\{e_1, \ldots, e_m\}$ is a set of orthogonal idempotents with $e_1 + \cdots + e_m = 1_R$.
\end{itemize}
