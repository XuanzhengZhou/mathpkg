Let $A$ be a semisimple module over a ring $R$. If there are direct sum decompositions
$$A = B_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus B_m \quad \text{and} \quad A = C_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus C_n,$$
where each $B_i, C_i$ is a simple submodule of $A$, then $m = n$ and (after reindexing) $B_i \cong C_i$ for $i = 1, 2, \ldots, m$.
