Let $V$ be a vector space over a division ring $D$. A subring $R$ of $\operatorname{Hom}_D(V,V)$ is said to be \textbf{dense} (or a \textit{dense ring of endomorphisms of $V$}) if for every finite linearly independent subset $\{u_1, \ldots, u_n\}$ of $V$ and every subset $\{v_1, \ldots, v_n\}$ of $V$, there exists $\theta \in R$ such that $\theta(u_i) = v_i$ for $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$.
