Let $R$ be a semisimple left Artinian ring with simple components $I_1, \ldots, I_t$, so $R = I_1 \times \cdots \times I_t$. Then:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] Every simple left $R$-module is isomorphic to a minimal left ideal $K_i$ of $R$ (which is contained in some $I_j$).
\item[(ii)] The isomorphism classes of simple left $R$-modules are in one-to-one correspondence with the simple components $I_j$.
\end{itemize}
