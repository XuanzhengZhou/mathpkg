The following conditions on a ring $R$ are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $R$ is simple and left Artinian;
\item[(ii)] $R$ is primitive and left Artinian;
\item[(iii)] $R$ is isomorphic to the endomorphism ring of a nonzero finite dimensional vector space $V$ over a division ring $D$;
\item[(iv)] for some positive integer $n$, $R$ is isomorphic to the ring of all $n \times n$ matrices over a division ring.
\end{itemize}
