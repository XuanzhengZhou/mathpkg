The following conditions on a nonzero ring $R$ are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $R$ is semisimple and left Artinian;
\item[(ii)] $R$ is a direct product of a finite number of simple ideals each of which is isomorphic to the endomorphism ring of a finite dimensional vector space over a division ring;
\item[(iii)] $R$ is isomorphic to a direct product of a finite number of matrix rings over division rings.
\end{itemize}
