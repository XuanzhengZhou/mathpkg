Let $K(G)$ be the group algebra of a finite group $G$ over an algebraically closed field $K$. If char $K = 0$ or char $K = p$ and $p \nmid |G|$, then there exist positive integers $n_1, \ldots, n_t$ and an isomorphism of $K$-algebras
$$K(G) \cong \operatorname{Mat}_{n_1}K \times \cdots \times \operatorname{Mat}_{n_t}K.$$
