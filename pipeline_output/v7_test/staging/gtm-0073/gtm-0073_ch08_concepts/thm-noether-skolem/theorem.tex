Let $R$ be a simple left Artinian ring and let $K$ be the center of $R$ (so that $R$ is a $K$-algebra). Let $A$ and $B$ be finite dimensional simple $K$-subalgebras of $R$ that contain $K$. If $\alpha : A \to B$ is a $K$-algebra isomorphism that leaves $K$ fixed elementwise, then $\alpha$ extends to an inner automorphism of $R$.
