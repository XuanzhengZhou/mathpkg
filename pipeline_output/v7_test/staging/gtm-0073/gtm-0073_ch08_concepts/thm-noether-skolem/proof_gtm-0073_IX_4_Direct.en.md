By Wedderburn-Artin, assume R = Hom_D(V,V) where V is n-dimensional over division ring D, with center K inside center of D. View V as a module over D otimes_K R. Let barA = D otimes_K A and barB = D otimes_K B. Show V has two barA-module structures; by the uniqueness of simple modules over simple Artinian rings, they are isomorphic via some beta in Hom_D(V,V) = R. Then beta r beta^{-1} = alpha(r) for all r in A.
