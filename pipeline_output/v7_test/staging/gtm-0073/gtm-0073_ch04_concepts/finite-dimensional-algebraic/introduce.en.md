Finite dimensional extensions are always algebraic and finitely generated. The proof uses that any $n+1$ powers $\{1, u, u^2, \ldots, u^n\}$ of an element $u$ in an $n$-dimensional space must be linearly dependent, giving a polynomial relation for $u$ over $K$.
