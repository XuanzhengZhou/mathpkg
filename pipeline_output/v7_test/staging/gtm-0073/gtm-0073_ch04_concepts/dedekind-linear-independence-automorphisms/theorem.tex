Let $F$ be a field and $H$ a set of distinct automorphisms of $F$. Then $H$ is linearly independent as a subset of the vector space of all functions $F \to F$ over $F$. Equivalently, if $\{\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_n\}$ are distinct automorphisms of $F$, then the only elements $a_1, \ldots, a_n \in F$ such that $\sum_{i=1}^n a_i \sigma_i(u) = 0$ for all $u \in F$ are $a_1 = \cdots = a_n = 0$.
