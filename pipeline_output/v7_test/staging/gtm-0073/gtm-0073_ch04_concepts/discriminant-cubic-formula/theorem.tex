Let $K$ be a field with $\mathrm{char}\,K \neq 2,3$. If $f(x) = x^3 + bx^2 + cx + d \in K[x]$ has roots $u_1, u_2, u_3$ in some splitting field, then the discriminant $\Delta^2$ of $f$ is $b^2c^2 - 4c^3 - 4b^3d - 27d^2 + 18bcd$.

If $f$ is replaced by the reduced cubic $g(x) = f(x - b/3) = x^3 + px + q$, the discriminant of $g$ (and hence of $f$) is $-4p^3 - 27q^2$.
