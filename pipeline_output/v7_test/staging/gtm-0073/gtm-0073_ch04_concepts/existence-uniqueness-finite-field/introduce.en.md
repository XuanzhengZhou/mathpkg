For every prime power $p^n$, there exists exactly one finite field of that order up to isomorphism. This field is often denoted $\mathbb{F}_{p^n}$ or $\mathrm{GF}(p^n)$. Existence follows from Proposition 5.6, and uniqueness from the uniqueness of splitting fields.
