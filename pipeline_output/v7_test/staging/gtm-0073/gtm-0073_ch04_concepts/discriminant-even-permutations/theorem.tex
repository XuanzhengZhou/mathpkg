Let $K$, $f$, $F$, $\Delta$ be as in Definition 4.4 (so that $F$ is Galois over $K$) and consider $G = \mathrm{Aut}_K F$ as a subgroup of $S_n$. In the Galois correspondence, the subfield $K(\Delta)$ corresponds to the subgroup $G \cap A_n$. In particular, $G$ consists of even permutations if and only if $\Delta \in K$.
