Hilbert's Theorem 90 (and its additive counterpart) characterize when an element of a cyclic extension has norm 1 (or trace 0). Element with norm 1 are exactly the "coboundaries" $v\sigma(v)^{-1}$. This result is the key to the structure theory of cyclic extensions.
