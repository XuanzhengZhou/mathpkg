The total degree $[F:K]$ factors as $[F:K]_s[F:K]_i$ where $[F:K]_s$ measures the separable part and $[F:K]_i$ the purely inseparable part. In characteristic zero, $[F:K]_i = 1$ always. In characteristic $p$, $[F:K]_i$ is a power of $p$.
