The Fundamental Theorem of Galois Theory establishes a lattice isomorphism between intermediate fields $K \subset E \subset F$ and subgroups of $\mathrm{Aut}_K F$, with dimensions matching indices. Normality of subgroups corresponds to Galoisness of intermediate fields, and quotient groups correspond to Galois groups of intermediate extensions.
