For a stable intermediate field $E$, the quotient $\mathrm{Aut}_K F / \mathrm{Aut}_E F$ captures precisely those $K$-automorphisms of $E$ that lift to $F$. This lemma provides the group-theoretic mechanism behind part (ii) of the Fundamental Theorem.
