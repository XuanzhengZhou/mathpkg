Artin's Theorem provides a powerful way to construct Galois extensions: take any field $F$ and a group $G$ of automorphisms, and let $K$ be the fixed field. Then $F$ is automatically Galois over $K$, and if $G$ is finite, $\mathrm{Aut}_K F = G$. This is the key tool for realizing arbitrary finite groups as Galois groups.
