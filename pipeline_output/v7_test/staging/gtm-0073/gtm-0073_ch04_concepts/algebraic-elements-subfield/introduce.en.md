The set of all elements algebraic over $K$ forms a subfield of $F$, called the algebraic closure of $K$ in $F$. This follows because $u-v$ and $uv^{-1}$ belong to the algebraic extension $K(u,v)$ whenever $u$ and $v$ are algebraic.
