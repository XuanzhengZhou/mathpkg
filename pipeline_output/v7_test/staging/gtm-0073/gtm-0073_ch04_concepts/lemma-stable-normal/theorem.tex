Let $F$ be an extension field of $K$.

(i) If $E$ is a stable intermediate field of the extension, then $E' = \mathrm{Aut}_E F$ is a normal subgroup of the Galois group $\mathrm{Aut}_K F$;

(ii) if $H$ is a normal subgroup of $\mathrm{Aut}_K F$, then the fixed field $H'$ of $H$ is a stable intermediate field of the extension.
