When the degree equals the characteristic, cyclic extensions are described by the Artin-Schreier equation $x^p - x - a = 0$. This is the characteristic $p$ analogue of the Kummer equation $x^n - a = 0$ for degree prime to characteristic, using the additive version of Hilbert 90.
