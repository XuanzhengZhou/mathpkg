In characteristic $p$, the Frobenius map $u \mapsto u^{p^r}$ is a field homomorphism (the freshman's dream identity $(u+v)^{p^r} = u^{p^r} + v^{p^r}$ holds). For finite fields, it is an automorphism and generates the Galois group.
