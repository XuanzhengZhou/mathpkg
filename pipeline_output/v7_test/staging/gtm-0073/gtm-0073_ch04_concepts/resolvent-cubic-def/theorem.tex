Let $K$ be a field and $f \in K[x]$ a separable polynomial of degree $4$ with roots $u_1, u_2, u_3, u_4$ in some splitting field. Let
$$\alpha = u_1u_2 + u_3u_4, \quad \beta = u_1u_3 + u_2u_4, \quad \gamma = u_1u_4 + u_2u_3.$$
The polynomial $(x - \alpha)(x - \beta)(x - \gamma) \in K[x]$ is called the resolvent cubic of $f$.
