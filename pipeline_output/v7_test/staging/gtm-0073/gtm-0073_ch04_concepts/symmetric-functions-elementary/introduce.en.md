The field of symmetric rational functions is precisely the field generated by the elementary symmetric functions. This fundamental theorem of symmetric functions shows that the Galois group of $K(x_1,\ldots,x_n)$ over $K(f_1,\ldots,f_n)$ is $S_n$, establishing the general equation's connection to $S_n$.
