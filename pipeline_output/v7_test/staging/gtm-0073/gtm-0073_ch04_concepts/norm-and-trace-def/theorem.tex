Let $F$ be a finite dimensional extension of $K$ and $\bar{K}$ an algebraic closure of $K$ containing $F$. Let $\sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_r$ be all the distinct $K$-monomorphisms $F \to \bar{K}$. For each $u \in F$, the norm $N_K^F(u)$ and trace $T_K^F(u)$ are defined by
$$N_K^F(u) = \left( \prod_{i=1}^{r} \sigma_i(u) \right)^{[F : K]_i}, \quad T_K^F(u) = [F : K]_i \left( \sum_{i=1}^{r} \sigma_i(u) \right).$$
