The splitting field of $x^n-1$ (the cyclotomic extension) is obtained by adjoining a single primitive $n$th root of unity. Its Galois group embeds into $(\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z})^\times$, making it abelian. Cyclotomic extensions are fundamental building blocks for abelian extensions and provide the roots of unity needed for Kummer theory.
