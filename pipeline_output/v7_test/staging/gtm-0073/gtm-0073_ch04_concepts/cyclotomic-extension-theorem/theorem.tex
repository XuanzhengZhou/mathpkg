Let $K$ be a field and $n$ a positive integer such that $\mathrm{char}\,K \nmid n$. Let $F$ be a splitting field of $x^n - 1_K$ over $K$. Then:

(i) $F = K(\zeta)$ where $\zeta$ is a primitive $n$th root of unity;

(ii) $F$ is an abelian extension of $K$ (that is, $F$ is Galois over $K$ and $\mathrm{Aut}_K F$ is abelian);

(iii) there is a monomorphism $\mathrm{Aut}_K F \to \mathbb{Z}_n^\times$ (the multiplicative group of units of $\mathbb{Z}_n$), so that $\mathrm{Aut}_K F$ is isomorphic to a subgroup of the group of units of $\mathbb{Z}_n$. In particular, $[\mathrm{Aut}_K F : 1] = [F:K]$ divides $\varphi(n)$.
