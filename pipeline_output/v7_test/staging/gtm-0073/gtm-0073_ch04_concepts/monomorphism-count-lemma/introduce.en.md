The number of $K$-monomorphisms from $F$ into a normal extension $N$ is multiplicative for a tower $K \subset E \subset F$. Composing $E$-monomorphisms of $F$ with $K$-monomorphisms of $E$ gives all $K$-monomorphisms of $F$, and this lemma counts them precisely.
