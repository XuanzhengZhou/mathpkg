Let $F$ be an extension field of $E$, $E$ an extension field of $K$ and $N$ a normal extension field of $K$ containing $F$. If $r$ is the cardinal number of distinct $E$-monomorphisms $F \to N$ and $t$ is the cardinal number of distinct $K$-monomorphisms $E \to N$, then $rt$ is the cardinal number of distinct $K$-monomorphisms $F \to N$.
