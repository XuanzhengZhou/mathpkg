The resolvent cubic partitions the possible Galois groups of a separable quartic: $V$, $Z_4$, $D_4$, $A_4$, or $S_4$. The factorization of the resolvent cubic over $K$ determines which case occurs, providing a complete computational algorithm for quartic Galois groups.
