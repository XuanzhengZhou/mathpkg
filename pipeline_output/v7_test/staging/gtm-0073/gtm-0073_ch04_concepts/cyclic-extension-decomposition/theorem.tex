Let $F$ be a cyclic extension field of $K$ of degree $n$ and suppose $n = mp^t$ where $0 \neq p = \mathrm{char}\,K$ and $(m,p) = 1$. Then there is a chain of intermediate fields $F \supset E_0 \supset E_1 \supset \cdots \supset E_{t-1} \supset E_t = K$ such that $F$ is a cyclic extension of $E_0$ of degree $m$ and for each $0 \leq i \leq t$, $E_{i-1}$ is a cyclic extension of $E_i$ of degree $p$.
