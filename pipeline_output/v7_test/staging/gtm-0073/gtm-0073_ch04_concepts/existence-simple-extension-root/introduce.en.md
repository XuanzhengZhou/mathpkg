Given any irreducible polynomial $f$ over a field $K$, one can always construct a simple extension $K(u) \cong K[x]/(f)$ in which $f$ has a root $u$. This construction is fundamental: it shows that every irreducible polynomial admits a root in some extension field, paving the way for splitting fields.
