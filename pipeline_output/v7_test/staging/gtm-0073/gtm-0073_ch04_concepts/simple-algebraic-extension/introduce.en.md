For an algebraic element $u$ over $K$, the simple extension $K(u)$ equals the polynomial ring $K[u]$, which is isomorphic to the quotient $K[x]/(f)$ where $f$ is the unique monic irreducible polynomial of $u$. The degree of the extension equals $\deg f$, and $\{1, u, \ldots, u^{n-1}\}$ forms a $K$-basis.
