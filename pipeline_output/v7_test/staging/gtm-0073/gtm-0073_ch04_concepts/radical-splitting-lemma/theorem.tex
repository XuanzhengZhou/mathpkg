Let $K$ be a field, $a \in K$, and $n \geq 1$ an integer such that $\mathrm{char}\,K \nmid n$. If $u$ is a root of $x^n - a$ in some extension field, then $K(u)$ is a splitting field of $x^n - a$ over $K$ and the roots of $x^n - a$ are $u, \zeta u, \zeta^2 u, \ldots, \zeta^{n-1} u$ where $\zeta$ is a primitive $n$th root of unity in some extension of $K$.
