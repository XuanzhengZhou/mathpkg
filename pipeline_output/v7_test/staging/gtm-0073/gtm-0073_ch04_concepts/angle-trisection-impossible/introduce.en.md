Trisecting a $60^\circ$ angle would require constructing $\cos 20^\circ$, which has degree $3$ over $\mathbb{Q}$. Since $3$ is not a power of $2$, the trisection is impossible by ruler and compass. This settles a classical problem of antiquity using field theory.
