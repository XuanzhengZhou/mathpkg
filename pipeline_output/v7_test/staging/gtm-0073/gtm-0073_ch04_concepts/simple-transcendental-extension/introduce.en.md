A simple transcendental extension $K(u)$ is isomorphic to the field of rational functions $K(x)$. Thus transcendental extensions behave like adjoining a free variable, and there is an isomorphism $f(x)/g(x) \mapsto f(u)/g(u)$ that is the identity on $K$.
