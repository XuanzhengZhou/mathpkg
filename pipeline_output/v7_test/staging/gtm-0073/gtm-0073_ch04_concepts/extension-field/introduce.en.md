An extension field $F$ of $K$ is simply a larger field containing $K$ as a subfield. This makes $F$ into a vector space over $K$, and the degree $[F:K]$ measures the size of $F$ relative to $K$. The entire chapter studies the structure of field extensions, distinguishing between finite and infinite dimensional cases.
