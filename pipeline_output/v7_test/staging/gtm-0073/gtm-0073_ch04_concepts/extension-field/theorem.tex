A field $F$ is said to be an extension field of $K$ (or simply an extension of $K$) provided that $K$ is a subfield of $F$.

If $F$ is an extension field of $K$, then $1_K = 1_F$ and $F$ is a vector space over $K$. The dimension $[F : K]$ of the $K$-vector space $F$ is called the degree of the extension. $F$ is said to be a finite dimensional extension or infinite dimensional extension of $K$ according as $[F : K]$ is finite or infinite.
