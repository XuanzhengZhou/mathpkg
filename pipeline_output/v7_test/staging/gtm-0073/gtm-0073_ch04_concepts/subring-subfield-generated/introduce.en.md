This theorem describes concretely what elements look like in the subring $K[X]$ (polynomial expressions) and subfield $K(X)$ (rational expressions) generated by a set $X$ over $K$. Every element of $K(X)$ is a quotient of two polynomial expressions, and only finitely many generators are needed for each element.
