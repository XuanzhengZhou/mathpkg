An irreducible polynomial $f$ factors as $g(x)^{[K(u_1):K]_i}$ where $g$ is separable and has $n = [K(u_1):K]_s$ distinct roots. Thus the inseparable degree measures root multiplicity, while the separable degree counts distinct roots. This decomposes irreducibility into separable and purely inseparable parts.
