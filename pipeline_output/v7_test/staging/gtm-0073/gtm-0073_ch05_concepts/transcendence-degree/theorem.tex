Let $F$ be an extension field of $K$. The \textbf{transcendence degree} of $F$ over $K$ (denoted $\operatorname{tr.d.} F/K$) is the cardinal number $|S|$, where $S$ is any transcendence base of $F$ over $K$.
In particular:
\[
\operatorname{tr.d.} F/K = 0 \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad F \text{ is algebraic over } K.
\]
