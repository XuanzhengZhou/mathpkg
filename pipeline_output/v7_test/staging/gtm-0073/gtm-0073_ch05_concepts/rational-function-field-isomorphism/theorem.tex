Let $F$ be an extension field of $K$ and $\{s_1, \ldots, s_n\}$ a subset of $F$ which is algebraically independent over $K$. Then there exists a $K$-isomorphism
\[
K(s_1, \ldots, s_n) \cong K(x_1, \ldots, x_n),
\]
where $K(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ is the field of rational functions in $n$ indeterminates over $K$.
