Transcendence degree is additive in towers of field extensions, exactly as vector space dimension is multiplicative. The proof uses the fact that if $S$ is a transcendence base of $E$ over $K$ and $T$ is a transcendence base of $F$ over $E$, then $S \cap T = \varnothing$ and $S \cup T$ is a transcendence base of $F$ over $K$.
