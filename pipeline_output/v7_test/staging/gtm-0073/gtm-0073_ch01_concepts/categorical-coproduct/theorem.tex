\begin{definition}
A \textbf{coproduct} (or \textbf{sum}) for the family $\{A_i \mid i \in I\}$ of objects in a category $\mathcal{C}$ is an object $S$ of $\mathcal{C}$, together with a family of morphisms $\{\iota_i : A_i \rightarrow S \mid i \in I\}$ such that for every object $B$ and family of morphisms $\{\psi_i : A_i \rightarrow B \mid i \in I\}$, there is a unique morphism $\psi : S \rightarrow B$ such that $\psi \circ \iota_i = \psi_i$ for all $i \in I$.
\end{definition}
