\begin{definition}
Let $\{N_i \mid i \in I\}$ be a family of normal subgroups of a group $G$ such that $G = \langle \bigcup_{i \in I} N_i \rangle$ and for each $k \in I$, $N_k \cap \langle \bigcup_{i \neq k} N_i \rangle = \langle e \rangle$. Then $G$ is said to be the \textbf{internal weak direct product} of the family $\{N_i \mid i \in I\}$ (or the \textbf{internal direct sum} if $G$ is (additive) abelian).
\end{definition}
