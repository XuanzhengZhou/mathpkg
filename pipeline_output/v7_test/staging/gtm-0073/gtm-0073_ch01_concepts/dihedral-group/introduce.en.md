The dihedral group $D_n$ of order $2n$ is the symmetry group of a regular $n$-gon. It is generated by a rotation $a$ of order $n$ and a reflection $b$ of order 2, subject to the relation $bab^{-1} = a^{-1}$. These generators and relations completely characterize $D_n$ up to isomorphism.
