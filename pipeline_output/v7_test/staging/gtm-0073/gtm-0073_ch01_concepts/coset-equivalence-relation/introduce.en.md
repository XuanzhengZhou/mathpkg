Congruence modulo a subgroup $H$ (defined by $a \equiv b \pmod{H}$ iff $ab^{-1} \in H$ for right congruence) is an equivalence relation on $G$. The equivalence classes are the right cosets $Ha$, which partition $G$ into disjoint subsets of equal cardinality $|H|$. Left cosets $aH$ are defined analogously using $a^{-1}b \in H$.
