Every subgroup of a quotient group $G/N$ corresponds uniquely to a subgroup $K$ of $G$ with $N \subseteq K \subseteq G$, namely $K/N$. Normality is preserved in both directions: $K \triangleleft G$ iff $K/N \triangleleft G/N$. This completes the picture of how the subgroup lattice of $G/N$ is determined by that of $G$.
