\begin{theorem}
If $G$ is a group and $X$ is a nonempty subset of $G$, then the subgroup $\langle X \rangle$ generated by $X$ consists of all finite products $a_1^{n_1} a_2^{n_2} \cdots a_t^{n_t}$ ($a_i \in X; n_i \in \mathbb{Z}$). In particular for every $a \in G$, $\langle a \rangle = \{a^n \mid n \in \mathbb{Z}\}$.
\end{theorem}
