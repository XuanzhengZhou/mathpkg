\begin{theorem}
Let $\{N_i \mid i \in I\}$ be a family of normal subgroups of a group $G$. $G$ is the internal weak direct product of the family $\{N_i \mid i \in I\}$ if and only if every nonidentity element of $G$ is a unique product $a_{i_1}a_{i_2} \cdots a_{i_n}$ with $i_1, \ldots, i_n$ distinct elements of $I$ and $e \neq a_{i_k} \in N_{i_k}$ for each $k = 1, 2, \ldots, n$.
\end{theorem}
