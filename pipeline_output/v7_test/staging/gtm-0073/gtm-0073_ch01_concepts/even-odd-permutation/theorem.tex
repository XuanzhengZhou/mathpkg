\begin{definition}
A permutation $\tau \in S_n$ is said to be \textbf{even} [resp.\ \textbf{odd}] if $\tau$ can be written as a product of an even [resp.\ odd] number of transpositions. The \textbf{sign} of a permutation $\tau$, denoted $\operatorname{sgn} \tau$, is $1$ or $-1$ according as $\tau$ is even or odd.
\end{definition}
