The weak direct product is a normal subgroup of the full direct product. Each factor group $G_k$ embeds as a normal subgroup via the natural injection $\iota_k$, which places an element in the $k$-th coordinate and identities elsewhere. These injections are the building blocks for the coproduct structure in abelian groups.
