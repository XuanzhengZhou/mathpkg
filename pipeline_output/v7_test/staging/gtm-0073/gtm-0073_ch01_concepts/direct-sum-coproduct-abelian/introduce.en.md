In the category of abelian groups, the direct sum (weak direct product) with the natural injections satisfies the universal property of a coproduct. Given homomorphisms from each $A_i$ to an abelian group $B$, there is a unique homomorphism from the direct sum extending them, defined by summing the finitely many non-identity coordinates.
