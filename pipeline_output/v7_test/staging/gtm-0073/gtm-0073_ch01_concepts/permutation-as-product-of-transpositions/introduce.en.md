Transpositions (2-cycles) generate the symmetric group $S_n$. The proof uses the cycle decomposition theorem and the identity $(x_1 x_2 \cdots x_r) = (x_1 x_r)(x_1 x_{r-1}) \cdots (x_1 x_2)$. This fact is the basis for defining even and odd permutations.
