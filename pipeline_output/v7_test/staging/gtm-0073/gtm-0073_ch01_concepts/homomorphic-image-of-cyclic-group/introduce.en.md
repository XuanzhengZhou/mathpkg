The property of being cyclic is preserved under homomorphisms and is inherited by subgroups. If $G = \langle a \rangle$, any nontrivial subgroup is generated by $a^m$ where $m$ is the smallest positive integer with $a^m$ in the subgroup. This gives a complete description of the subgroup lattice of a cyclic group.
