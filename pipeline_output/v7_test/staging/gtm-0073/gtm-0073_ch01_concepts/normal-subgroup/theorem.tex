\begin{definition}
A subgroup $N$ of a group $G$ is \textbf{normal} (denoted $N \triangleleft G$) if it satisfies the equivalent conditions of Theorem 5.1. The set of all left cosets of $N$ in $G$ (which equals the set of all right cosets) is denoted $G/N$ and is a group (called the \textbf{quotient group} or \textbf{factor group} of $G$ by $N$) under the binary operation $(aN)(bN) = abN$.
\end{definition}
