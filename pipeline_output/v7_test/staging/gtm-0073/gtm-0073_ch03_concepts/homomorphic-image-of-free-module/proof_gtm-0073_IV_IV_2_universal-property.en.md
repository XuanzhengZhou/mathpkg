Let $X$ be a set of generators of $A$ and $F$ the free $R$-module on $X$. The inclusion map $X \to A$ induces an $R$-module homomorphism $\bar{f}: F \to A$ such that $X \subset \operatorname{Im} \bar{f}$ (Theorem 2.1(iv)). Since $X$ generates $A$, $\operatorname{Im} \bar{f} = A$.
