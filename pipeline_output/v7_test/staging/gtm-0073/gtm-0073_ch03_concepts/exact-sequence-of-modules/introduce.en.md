An exact sequence is a sequence of module homomorphisms where the image of each map equals the kernel of the next. A short exact sequence $0 \to A \to B \to C \to 0$ encodes the data of a submodule $A$ of $B$ together with its quotient module $C \cong B/A$. Exact sequences are the fundamental tool for relating modules and their homomorphisms.
