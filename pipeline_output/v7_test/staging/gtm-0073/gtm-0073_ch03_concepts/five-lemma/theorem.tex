Let
\[
\begin{CD}
A_1 @>>> A_2 @>>> A_3 @>>> A_4 @>>> A_5 \\
@V{\alpha_1}VV @V{\alpha_2}VV @V{\alpha_3}VV @V{\alpha_4}VV @V{\alpha_5}VV \\
B_1 @>>> B_2 @>>> B_3 @>>> B_4 @>>> B_5
\end{CD}
\]
be a commutative diagram of $R$-modules and $R$-module homomorphisms, with exact rows.

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] If $\alpha_1$ is an epimorphism and $\alpha_2, \alpha_4$ are monomorphisms, then $\alpha_3$ is a monomorphism.
\item[(b)] If $\alpha_5$ is a monomorphism and $\alpha_2, \alpha_4$ are epimorphisms, then $\alpha_3$ is an epimorphism.
\end{enumerate}

Consequently, if $\alpha_1$ is epic, $\alpha_5$ is monic, and $\alpha_2, \alpha_4$ are isomorphisms, then $\alpha_3$ is an isomorphism.
