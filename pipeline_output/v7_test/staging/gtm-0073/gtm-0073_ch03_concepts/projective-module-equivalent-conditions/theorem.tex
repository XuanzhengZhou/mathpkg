Let $R$ be a ring. The following conditions on an $R$-module $P$ are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $P$ is projective;
\item[(ii)] every short exact sequence $0 \to A \xrightarrow{f} B \xrightarrow{g} P \to 0$ is split exact (hence $B \cong A \oplus P$);
\item[(iii)] there is a free module $F$ and an $R$-module $K$ such that $F \cong K \oplus P$.
\end{enumerate}
