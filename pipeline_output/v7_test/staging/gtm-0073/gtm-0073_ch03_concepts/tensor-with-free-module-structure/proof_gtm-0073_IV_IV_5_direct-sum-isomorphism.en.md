For each $y \in Y$, let $A_y$ be a copy of $A$ and consider $\sum_{y \in Y} A_y$. Since $\{y\}$ is linearly independent, $\varphi: R \to Ry$ is an isomorphism, inducing $A \cong A \otimes_R Ry$. Summing over $Y$ and using Theorem 5.9 yields $A \otimes_R F \cong \sum A_y$. Under this isomorphism, $a \otimes y$ corresponds to $a$ in the $y$-th coordinate.
