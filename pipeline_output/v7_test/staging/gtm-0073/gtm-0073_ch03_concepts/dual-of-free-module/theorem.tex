Let $F$ be a free left $R$-module with finite basis $X = \{x_1,\ldots,x_n\}$ over a ring $R$ with identity. For each $x \in X$, define $f_x : F \to R$ by $f_x(\sum r_i x_i) = r_x$. Then $\{f_x \mid x \in X\}$ is a basis of the right $R$-module $F^*$, and $F^*$ is a free right $R$-module of the same rank as $F$.
