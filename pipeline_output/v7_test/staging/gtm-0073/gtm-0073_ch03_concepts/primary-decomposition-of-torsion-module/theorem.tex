Let $A$ be a torsion module over a PID $R$ and $A(p) = \{a \in A \mid a \text{ has order a power of } p\}$.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $A(p)$ is a submodule of $A$ for each prime $p \in R$;
\item[(ii)] $A = \sum A(p)$, where the sum is over all primes $p \in R$. If $A$ is finitely generated, only finitely many $A(p)$ are nonzero.
\end{enumerate}
