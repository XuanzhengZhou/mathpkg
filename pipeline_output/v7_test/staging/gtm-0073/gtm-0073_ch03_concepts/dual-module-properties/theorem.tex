Let $A, B$ be left modules over a ring $R$ and $A^* = \operatorname{Hom}_R(A,R)$.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] If $\varphi : A \to C$ is a left $R$-module homomorphism, $\bar{\varphi} : C^* \to A^*$ is a homomorphism of right $R$-modules.
\item[(ii)] $(A \oplus C)^* \cong A^* \oplus C^*$.
\item[(iii)] If $R$ is a division ring and $0 \to A \to B \to C \to 0$ is exact, then $0 \to C^* \to B^* \to A^* \to 0$ is exact.
\end{enumerate}
