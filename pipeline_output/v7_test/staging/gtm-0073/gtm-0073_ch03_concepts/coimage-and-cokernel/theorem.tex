Let $f: A \to B$ be an $R$-module homomorphism. The \emph{coimage} of $f$, denoted $\operatorname{Coim} f$, is the quotient module
\[
\operatorname{Coim} f = A / \operatorname{Ker} f.
\]
The \emph{cokernel} of $f$, denoted $\operatorname{Coker} f$, is the quotient module
\[
\operatorname{Coker} f = B / \operatorname{Im} f.
\]

Each of the following sequences is exact:
\begin{align*}
0 &\to \operatorname{Ker} f \to A \to \operatorname{Coim} f \to 0,\\
0 &\to \operatorname{Im} f \to B \to \operatorname{Coker} f \to 0,\\
0 &\to \operatorname{Ker} f \to A \xrightarrow{f} B \to \operatorname{Coker} f \to 0,
\end{align*}
where the unlabeled maps are the obvious inclusions and projections.
