The coimage and cokernel of a module homomorphism $f: A \to B$ are the natural quotient modules $A/\operatorname{Ker} f$ and $B/\operatorname{Im} f$. They complete the kernel and image to short exact sequences. In module categories the coimage is always isomorphic to the image via the canonical factorization $A \to A/\operatorname{Ker} f \cong \operatorname{Im} f \to B$.
