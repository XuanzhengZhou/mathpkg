Let $R$ be a ring and $0 \to A \xrightarrow{f} B \xrightarrow{g} C \to 0$ a short exact sequence of $R$-modules. Then the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] There is an $R$-module homomorphism $h : C \to B$ such that $gh = 1_C$;
\item[(ii)] There is an $R$-module homomorphism $k : B \to A$ such that $kf = 1_A$;
\item[(iii)] The given sequence is isomorphic to the direct sum short exact sequence $0 \to A \to A \oplus C \to C \to 0$, and in particular $B \cong A \oplus C$.
\end{enumerate}
