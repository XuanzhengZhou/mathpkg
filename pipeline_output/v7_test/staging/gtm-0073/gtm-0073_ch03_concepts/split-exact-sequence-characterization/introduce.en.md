A short exact sequence splits if it satisfies any of the three equivalent conditions: a right inverse (section) for the epimorphism, a left inverse (retraction) for the monomorphism, or isomorphism to the trivial direct sum sequence. Theorem 1.18 shows these are equivalent and that $B \cong A \oplus C$.
