If $R$ is a ring, $\{A_i \mid i \in I\}$ a family of $R$-modules, $C$ an $R$-module, and $\{\varphi_i : C \to A_i \mid i \in I\}$ a family of $R$-module homomorphisms, then there is a unique $R$-module homomorphism $\varphi : C \to \prod_{i \in I} A_i$ such that $\pi_i \varphi = \varphi_i$ for all $i \in I$. The direct product is uniquely determined up to isomorphism by this property.
