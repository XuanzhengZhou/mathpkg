Theorem 7.2 gives a categorical/tensor characterization: a $K$-module $A$ is a $K$-algebra precisely when equipped with an associative product map $\pi: A \otimes_K A \to A$, and is unital when there is a unit map $I: K \to A$. This perspective is fundamental to the theory of coalgebras and Hopf algebras.
