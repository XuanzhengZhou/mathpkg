There is a natural evaluation map $\theta: A \to A^{**}$ defined by $\theta(a)(f) = f(a)$. For free modules this is injective; for finitely generated free modules it is an isomorphism (reflexive). This generalizes the fact that a finite-dimensional vector space is naturally isomorphic to its double dual.
