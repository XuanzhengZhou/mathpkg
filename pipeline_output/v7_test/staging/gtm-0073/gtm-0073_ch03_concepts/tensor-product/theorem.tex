Let $A$ be a right module and $B$ a left module over a ring $R$. Let $F$ be the free abelian group on $A \times B$. Let $K$ be the subgroup of $F$ generated by all elements:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $(a + a',b) - (a,b) - (a',b)$;
\item[(ii)] $(a,b + b') - (a,b) - (a,b')$;
\item[(iii)] $(ar,b) - (a,rb)$.
\end{enumerate}
The quotient $F/K$ is the \emph{tensor product} $A \otimes_R B$. The coset $(a,b) + K$ is denoted $a \otimes b$.
