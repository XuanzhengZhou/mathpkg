Let $I$ be a maximal ideal of $R$. Then $R/I$ is a field. If $X$ and $Y$ are bases of $F$, then $\pi(X)$ and $\pi(Y)$ are bases of the vector space $F/IF$ over $R/I$ by Theorem 2.10. By Theorem 2.7, $|\pi(X)| = |\pi(Y)|$. Since $\pi$ is injective on bases, $|X| = |Y|$.
