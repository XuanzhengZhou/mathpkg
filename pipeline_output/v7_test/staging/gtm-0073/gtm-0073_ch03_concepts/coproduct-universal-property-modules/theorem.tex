If $R$ is a ring, $\{A_i \mid i \in I\}$ a family of $R$-modules, $D$ an $R$-module, and $\{\psi_i : A_i \to D \mid i \in I\}$ a family of $R$-module homomorphisms, then there is a unique $R$-module homomorphism $\psi : \sum A_i \to D$ such that $\psi \iota_i = \psi_i$ for all $i \in I$. The direct sum is uniquely determined up to isomorphism by this property.
