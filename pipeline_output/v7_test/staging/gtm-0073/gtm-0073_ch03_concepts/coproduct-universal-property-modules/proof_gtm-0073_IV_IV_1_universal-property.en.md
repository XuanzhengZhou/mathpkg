By Theorem I.8.5 there is a unique abelian group homomorphism $\psi : \sum A_i \to D$ with the desired property, given by $\psi(\{a_i\}) = \sum_i \psi_i(a_i)$. It is easy to see that $\psi$ is an $R$-module map. Hence $\sum A_i$ is a coproduct in the category of $R$-modules (Definition I.7.4), and therefore determined up to isomorphism by Theorem I.7.5.
