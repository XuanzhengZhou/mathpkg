Let $R$ be a ring and $\{A_i \mid i \in I\}$ a family of submodules of an $R$-module $A$ such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $A$ is the sum of the family $\{A_i \mid i \in I\}$;
\item[(ii)] for each $k \in I$, $A_k \cap A_k^* = 0$, where $A_k^*$ is the sum of $\{A_i \mid i \neq k\}$.
\end{enumerate}
Then there is an isomorphism $A \cong \sum_{i \in I} A_i$.
