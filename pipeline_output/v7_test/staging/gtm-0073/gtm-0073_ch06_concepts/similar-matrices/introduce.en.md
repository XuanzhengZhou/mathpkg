When the domain and codomain are the same module, changing the basis transforms the matrix of an endomorphism by similarity: $B = P^{-1}AP$ where $P$ is the change-of-basis matrix. This is the special case of equivalence (Theorem 1.6) where the same change-of-basis is applied to both domain and codomain.
