Let $R$ be a ring with identity and $E$ a free left $R$-module with an ordered basis $U$ of finite cardinality $n$. Let $A$ be the $n \times n$ matrix of $f \in \operatorname{Hom}_R(E,E)$ relative to $U$. Then $f$ has $n \times n$ matrix $B$ relative to an ordered basis $U'$ of $E$ if and only if $B = P^{-1}AP$ for some invertible matrix $P \in \operatorname{Mat}_n R$.
