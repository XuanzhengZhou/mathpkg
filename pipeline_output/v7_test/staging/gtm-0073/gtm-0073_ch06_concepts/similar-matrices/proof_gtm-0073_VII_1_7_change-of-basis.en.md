This follows directly from Theorem 1.6. If $U'$ is another ordered basis of $E$, let $P$ be the matrix of $1_E$ relative to $U'$ and $U$. Then by Theorem 1.6, the matrix of $f$ relative to $U'$ is $P^{-1}AP$ (since the matrix of $1_E$ relative to $U$ and $U'$ is $P^{-1}$).
