Let $A$ be an $n \times n$ matrix over a field $K$. Then the matrix of polynomials $xI_n - A \in \operatorname{Mat}_n K[x]$ is equivalent (over $K[x]$) to a diagonal matrix $D$ with nonzero diagonal entries $f_1, \ldots, f_n \in K[x]$ such that each $f_i$ is monic and $f_1 \mid f_2 \mid \cdots \mid f_n$. Those polynomials $f_i$ which are not constants are the invariant factors of $A$.
