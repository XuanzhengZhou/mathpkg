Let $R$ be a ring with identity and $E$ a free left $R$-module with a finite basis of $n$ elements. Then there is a ring anti-isomorphism
\[
\operatorname{Hom}_R(E,E) \cong \operatorname{Mat}_n R
\]
given by assigning to each endomorphism its matrix relative to the given basis. Consequently there is a ring isomorphism $\operatorname{Hom}_R(E,E) \cong \operatorname{Mat}_n R^{\mathrm{op}}$.
