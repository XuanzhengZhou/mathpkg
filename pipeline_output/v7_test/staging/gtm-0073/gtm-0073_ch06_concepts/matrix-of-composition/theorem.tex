Let $R$ be a ring with identity and let $E,F,G$ be free left $R$-modules with finite ordered bases $U = \{u_1, \ldots, u_n\}$, $V = \{v_1, \ldots, v_m\}$, $W = \{w_1, \ldots, w_p\}$ respectively. If $f \in \operatorname{Hom}_R(E,F)$ has $n \times m$ matrix $A$ (relative to bases $U$ and $V$) and $g \in \operatorname{Hom}_R(F,G)$ has $m \times p$ matrix $B$ (relative to bases $V$ and $W$), then $gf \in \operatorname{Hom}_R(E,G)$ has $n \times p$ matrix $AB$ (relative to bases $U$ and $W$).
