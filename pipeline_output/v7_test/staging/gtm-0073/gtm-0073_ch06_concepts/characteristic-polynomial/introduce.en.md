The characteristic polynomial of a square matrix $A$ is the determinant $|xI_n - A|$, a monic polynomial of degree $n$ in the indeterminate $x$ with coefficients in the base ring. It is invariant under similarity: similar matrices share the same characteristic polynomial. For a linear transformation (or endomorphism of a free module), the characteristic polynomial is defined via any matrix representation and is independent of the chosen basis.
