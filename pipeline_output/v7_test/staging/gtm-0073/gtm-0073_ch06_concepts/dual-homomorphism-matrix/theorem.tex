Let $R$ be a ring with identity and $f: E \to F$ a homomorphism of finitely generated free left $R$-modules. If $A$ is the matrix of $f$ relative to ordered bases $U$ and $V$, then $A$ is also the matrix of the dual homomorphism $\bar{f}: F^* \to E^*$ of free right $R$-modules relative to the dual bases $V^*$ and $U^*$.
