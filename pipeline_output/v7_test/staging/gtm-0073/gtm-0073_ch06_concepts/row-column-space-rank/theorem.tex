The \textbf{row space} [resp. \textbf{column space}] of an $n \times m$ matrix $A$ over a ring $R$ with identity is the submodule of the free left [resp. right] module $R^m$ [resp. $R^n$] generated by the rows [resp. columns] of $A$ considered as elements of $R^m$ [resp. $R^n$]. If $R$ is a division ring, then the \textbf{row rank} [resp. \textbf{column rank}] of $A$ is the dimension of the row [resp. column] space of $A$.
