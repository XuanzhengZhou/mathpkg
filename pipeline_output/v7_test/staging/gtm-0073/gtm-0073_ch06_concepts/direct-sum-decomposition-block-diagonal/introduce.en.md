When a vector space decomposes as a direct sum of $\phi$-invariant subspaces, the matrix of $\phi$ is block diagonal, with each diagonal block being the matrix of the restriction of $\phi$ to the corresponding invariant subspace. Conversely, any block diagonal matrix corresponds to such a direct sum decomposition. This lemma is the crucial link between the algebraic decomposition from Theorem 4.2 and the matrix representation of $\phi$.
