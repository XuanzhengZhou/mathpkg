Let $A \in \operatorname{Mat}_n K$ where $K$ is a field.
\begin{enumerate}
\item $A$ is similar to a matrix that is the direct sum of the companion matrices of its invariant factors $q_1, \ldots, q_t$. Two matrices are similar if and only if they have the same invariant factors.
\item $A$ is similar to a matrix that is the direct sum of the companion matrices of its elementary divisors $p_i^{m_{ij}}$. Two matrices are similar if and only if they have the same elementary divisors.
\item If the irreducible factors of the invariant factors are all linear, $A$ is similar to a matrix in Jordan canonical form. Two matrices are similar if and only if they have the same Jordan canonical form (up to order of blocks).
\end{enumerate}
