Verify that $I_n$ may be obtained from $E$ by performing a single elementary row operation $T$. If $F$ is the elementary matrix obtained by performing $T$ on $I_n$, then $FE = I_n$ by Theorem 2.8. Verify directly that $EF = I_n$.
