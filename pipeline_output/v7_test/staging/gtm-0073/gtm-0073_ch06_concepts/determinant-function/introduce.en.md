The determinant is the unique alternating $n$-linear form on $n \times n$ matrices (viewed as $n$-tuples of row vectors) that sends the identity matrix to $1_R$. It assigns to each square matrix an element of the base ring, capturing the signed volume of the parallelepiped spanned by its rows (or columns).
