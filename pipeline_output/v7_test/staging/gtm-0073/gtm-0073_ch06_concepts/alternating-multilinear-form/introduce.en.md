Multilinear functions are classified by their behavior under permutation of arguments. Symmetric forms are invariant under all permutations, skew-symmetric forms change sign according to the permutation's parity, and alternating forms vanish whenever two arguments are equal. For rings where $2$ is not a zero divisor, alternating and skew-symmetric are equivalent notions.
