Let $R$ be a ring with identity and $E,F$ free left $R$-modules. Let $U,U'$ be ordered bases of $E$ and $V,V'$ ordered bases of $F$. Let $f \in \operatorname{Hom}_R(E,F)$. If $A$ is the matrix of $f$ relative to $U,V$ and $B$ is the matrix of $f$ relative to $U',V'$, then $B = PAQ$ where $P$ is the invertible matrix of $1_E$ relative to $U'$ and $U$, and $Q$ is the invertible matrix of $1_F$ relative to $V$ and $V'$.
