Let $f : D^n \to D^m$ be a linear transformation of left vector spaces with matrix $A$ relative to the standard bases. Then the dual map $\bar{f}$ of right vector spaces also has matrix $A$ (Proposition 1.10). By Theorem 2.3 and Proposition 2.4:
$\operatorname{row rank} A = \operatorname{rank} f = \operatorname{rank} \bar{f} = \operatorname{column rank} A$.
