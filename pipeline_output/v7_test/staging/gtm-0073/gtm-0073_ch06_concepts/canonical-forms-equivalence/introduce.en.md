Over a division ring, two matrices are equivalent if and only if they have the same rank. Moreover, every matrix of rank $r$ is equivalent to the canonical form $E_r^{n,m}$ (an $n \times m$ matrix with $r$ ones on the main diagonal and zeros elsewhere). Thus rank is a complete invariant for the equivalence relation on matrices over a division ring.
