Let $\phi : E \to E$ be a linear transformation of a vector space $E$ over a field $K$ and let $k \in K$ be an eigenvalue of $\phi$. The \textbf{eigenspace} (or \textbf{characteristic space}) of $k$ is
\[
C(\phi, k) = \{ v \in E \mid \phi(v) = kv \}.
\]
$C(\phi, k)$ is a nonzero subspace of $E$ consisting of all eigenvectors with eigenvalue $k$ together with the zero vector.
