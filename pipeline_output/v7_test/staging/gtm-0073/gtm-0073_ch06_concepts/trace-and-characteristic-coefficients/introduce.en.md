The trace of a square matrix is the sum of its diagonal entries. It is a linear functional satisfying the cyclic property $\operatorname{Tr}(AB) = \operatorname{Tr}(BA)$, which implies similarity invariance. For an endomorphism, the trace is defined via any matrix representation and is independent of the chosen basis because it is (up to sign) a coefficient of the characteristic polynomial.
