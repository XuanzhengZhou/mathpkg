An $R$-multilinear function is a function of $n$ module arguments that is $R$-linear in each argument separately when the others are held fixed. When the codomain is the base ring $R$, it is called a multilinear form. The 2-linear case recovers bilinear maps, which play a central role in tensor products. Multilinear forms are the foundation for the theory of determinants.
