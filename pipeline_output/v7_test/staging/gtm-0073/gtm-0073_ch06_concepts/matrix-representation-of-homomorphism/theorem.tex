Let $R$ be a ring with identity. Let $E$ be a free left $R$-module with a finite basis of $n$ elements and $F$ a free left $R$-module with a finite basis of $m$ elements. Let $M$ be the left $R$-module of all $n \times m$ matrices over $R$. Then there is an isomorphism of abelian groups:
\[
\operatorname{Hom}_R(E,F) \cong M.
\]
If $R$ is commutative this is an isomorphism of left $R$-modules.
