Every $R$-module homomorphism between finitely generated free left $R$-modules can be uniquely represented by a matrix once ordered bases are chosen for the domain and codomain. The matrix entries record the coefficients expressing the image of each basis element of the domain in terms of the basis of the codomain. This establishes a bijective correspondence that is an isomorphism of abelian groups, and of $R$-modules when $R$ is commutative.
