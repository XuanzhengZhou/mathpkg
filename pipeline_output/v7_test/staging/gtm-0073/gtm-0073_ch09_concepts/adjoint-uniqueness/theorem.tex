Any two left adjoints of a covariant functor $T : \mathcal{D} \to \mathcal{C}$ are naturally isomorphic.

Specifically, if $S_1 : \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ and $S_2 : \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ are left adjoints of $T$, with natural isomorphisms
\[
\alpha : \hom_{\mathcal{D}}(S_1(-), -) \xrightarrow{\cong} \hom_{\mathcal{C}}(-, T(-)), \qquad
\beta : \hom_{\mathcal{D}}(S_2(-), -) \xrightarrow{\cong} \hom_{\mathcal{C}}(-, T(-)),
\]
then there is a natural isomorphism $f : S_1 \to S_2$.
