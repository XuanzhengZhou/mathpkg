A difference kernel (equalizer) of a parallel pair $f, g : C \to D$ is a morphism $i$ that equalizes $f$ and $g$ ($fi = gi$) and is universal with this property. Dually, a difference cokernel (coequalizer) is a morphism $j$ that coequalizes $f$ and $g$ ($jf = jg$) universally. These are the categorical generalizations of equation solutions and quotient constructions.
