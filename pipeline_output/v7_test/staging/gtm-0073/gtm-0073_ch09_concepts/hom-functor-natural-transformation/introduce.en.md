This lemma provides the fundamental connection between natural transformations out of a hom functor $h_A$ and elements of $T(A)$. It states that a natural transformation $\alpha : h_A \to T$ is completely determined by the image of the identity $1_A$, namely $u = \alpha_A(1_A)$, via the formula $\alpha_C(g) = T(g)(u)$. Conversely, any element $u \in T(A)$ induces a natural transformation by the same formula.
