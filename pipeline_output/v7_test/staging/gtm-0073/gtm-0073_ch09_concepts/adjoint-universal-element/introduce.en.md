This corollary combines Theorem 2.2 and Theorem 1.6 to give a concrete criterion for the existence of a left adjoint: for each $C$, there must be a universal arrow $u_C : C \to T(S(C))$ from $C$ to $T$. This universal property captures many familiar constructions, such as free objects, tensor products, and completions.
