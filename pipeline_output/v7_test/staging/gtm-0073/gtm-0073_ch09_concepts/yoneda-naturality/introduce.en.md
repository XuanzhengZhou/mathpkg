This corollary states that the Yoneda bijection $\psi_A : \operatorname{Nat}(h_A, T) \cong T(A)$ is natural in both variables: varying the object $A$ (via precomposition with $f$) corresponds to applying $T(f)$, and varying the functor $T$ (via postcomposition with a natural transformation $\sigma$) corresponds to applying $\sigma_A$. This naturality is essential for the full Yoneda embedding.
