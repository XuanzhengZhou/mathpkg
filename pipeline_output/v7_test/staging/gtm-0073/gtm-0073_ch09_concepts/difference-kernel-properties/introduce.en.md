Difference kernels (equalizers) are always monomorphisms, and difference cokernels (coequalizers) are always epimorphisms. Moreover, difference kernels and cokernels are unique up to equivalence: any two difference kernels of the same pair of morphisms have isomorphic domains, with a unique isomorphism commuting with the kernel morphisms.
