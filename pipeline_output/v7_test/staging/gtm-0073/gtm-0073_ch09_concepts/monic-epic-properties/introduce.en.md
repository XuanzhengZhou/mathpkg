This proposition collects basic facts about monic and epic morphisms under composition. Monos and epis are each closed under composition, and the composite $gf$ being monic forces $f$ to be monic, while $gf$ being epic forces $g$ to be epic. Every equivalence (isomorphism) is both monic and epic, though the converse is false in general categories.
