(Noether Normalization Lemma) Let $R$ be an integral domain which is a finitely generated extension ring of a field $K$ and let $r$ be the transcendence degree over $K$ of the quotient field $F$ of $R$. Then there exists an algebraically independent subset $\{t_1, t_2, \ldots, t_r\}$ of $R$ such that $R$ is integral over $K[t_1, \ldots, t_r]$.
