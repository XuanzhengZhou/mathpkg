The proof proceeds by contradiction assuming \(P_1\) is distinct from all \(P_j'\). One constructs the set \(C^* = \{x \in B \mid r^n x \in C\}\) for a suitable \(r\) not in \(P_1\) and shows that \(C = C^*\), leading to a contradiction with either \(C \neq B\) or the irredundancy of the decompositions. The full argument uses induction and the properties of primary submodules.
