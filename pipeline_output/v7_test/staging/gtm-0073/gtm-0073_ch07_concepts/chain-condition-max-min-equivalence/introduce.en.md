The ascending chain condition (ACC) is equivalent to the maximum condition on submodules, and the descending chain condition (DCC) is equivalent to the minimum condition. The proof for DCC ⇒ minimum condition uses the Axiom of Choice and the Recursion Theorem to construct an infinite strictly descending chain from the failure of the minimum condition.
