A finite direct sum of modules satisfies the ascending or descending chain condition if and only if each summand satisfies it. The proof uses induction on the number of summands, with the base case \(n=2\) handled by the short exact sequence \(0 \to A_1 \to A_1 \oplus A_2 \to A_2 \to 0\) and Theorem 1.5.
