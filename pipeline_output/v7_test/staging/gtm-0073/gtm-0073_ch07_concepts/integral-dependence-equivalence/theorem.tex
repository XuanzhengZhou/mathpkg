Let $S$ be an extension ring of $R$ and $s \in S$. The following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $s$ is integral over $R$;
  \item $R[s]$ is a finitely generated $R$-module;
  \item there exists a subring $T$ of $S$ such that $R[s] \subset T$, $T$ is a finitely generated $R$-module, and the annihilator of $T$ in $R[s]$ is zero.
\end{enumerate}
