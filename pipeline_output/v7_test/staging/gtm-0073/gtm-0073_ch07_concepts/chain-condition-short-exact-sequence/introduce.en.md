Chain conditions behave well with respect to short exact sequences. If the middle term satisfies ACC (or DCC), so do the outer terms. Conversely, if both outer terms satisfy ACC (or DCC), the middle term does as well. The proof uses the Short Five Lemma to show that certain inclusion maps between submodule chains are actually identities.
