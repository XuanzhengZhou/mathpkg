Chain conditions on a module \(B\) are inherited by its submodules and quotients, and conversely, if both a submodule \(A\) and the quotient \(B/A\) satisfy the ACC (or DCC), then \(B\) itself satisfies the same condition. This follows directly from the Short Five Lemma applied to the exact sequence \(0 \to A \to B \to B/A \to 0\).
