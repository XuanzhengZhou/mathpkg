(\(\Leftarrow\)) Let \(S\) be the set of all ideals of \(R\) which are not finitely generated. If \(S\) is nonempty, use Zorn's Lemma to find a maximal element \(P\) of \(S\). By Proposition 2.4, \(P\) is prime and hence finitely generated by hypothesis. This contradicts \(P \in S\) unless \(S = \emptyset\). Therefore \(R\) is Noetherian by Theorem 1.9. The forward direction (\(\Rightarrow\)) is immediate from Theorem 1.9.
