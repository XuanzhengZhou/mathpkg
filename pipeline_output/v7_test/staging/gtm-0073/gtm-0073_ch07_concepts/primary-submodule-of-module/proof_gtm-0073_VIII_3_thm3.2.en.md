This is a definition paired with a structural result: one verifies that \(Q_A\) is an ideal of \(R\) and that the primary property for the submodule \(A\) translates directly to the primary property for the ideal \(Q_A\). The radical \(\operatorname{Rad} Q_A\) is then prime by Theorem 2.9.
