This definition extends the notion of genus from compact connected orientable 2-manifolds to certain singular spaces obtained from such manifolds by point identifications. The genus is defined via the underlying nonsingular manifold $M$, and it is well-defined (independent of the choice of $M$). This definition is used to assign a genus to algebraic curves with ordinary singularities.
