This lemma is a crucial step in the proof of the Hilbert Nullstellensatz. It states that if adjoining finitely many elements to a field yields another field (rather than just a ring), then each adjoined element must be algebraic over the base field. The proof proceeds by induction on $n$ and uses the transitivity of algebraicity along with integral dependence arguments.
