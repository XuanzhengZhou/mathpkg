A projective set in $\mathbb{P}^{n-1}(k)$ is represented by a homogeneous set in $k^n$, the so-called affine cone over the projective set. This correspondence allows one to translate between projective geometry and affine geometry one dimension higher, a fundamental technique in algebraic geometry.
