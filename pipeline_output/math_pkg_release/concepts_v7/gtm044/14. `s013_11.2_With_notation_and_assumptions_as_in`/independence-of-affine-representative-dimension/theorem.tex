Let $V \subset \mathbb{P}^n(\mathbb{C})$ be a variety and $P \in V$. Let $H(V) \subset \mathbb{C}^{n+1}$ be the homogeneous variety corresponding to $V$, and let $L_P$ be the 1-subspace of $\mathbb{C}^{n+1}$ corresponding to $P$. The dimension of the affine representative $H(V) \cap H$ at $L_P \cap H$ is independent of the choice of any hyperplane $H$ such that $0 \notin H$ and $\varnothing \neq L_P \cap H$.
