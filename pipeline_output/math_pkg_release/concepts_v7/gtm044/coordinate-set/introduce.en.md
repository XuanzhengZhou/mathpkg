A coordinate set of a point $P$ in projective space $\mathbb{P}^n(k)$ is any nonzero $(n+1)$-tuple representing a vector in the $1$-subspace $L_P \subset k^{n+1}$ corresponding to $P$. Coordinate sets are not unique: any two coordinate sets of the same point differ by a nonzero scalar multiple. These are often called homogeneous coordinates in the literature.
