Let $R$ be the coordinate ring of an affine variety and let $\mathfrak{p} \subset R$ be a prime ideal. For any ideals $\mathfrak{a}, \mathfrak{b}$ in $\mathcal{I}(R)$,
$$(\mathfrak{a} \cap \mathfrak{b})^e = \mathfrak{a}^e \cap \mathfrak{b}^e,$$
where $(\cdot)^e$ denotes extension of ideals from $R$ to the localization $R_{\mathfrak{p}}$.
