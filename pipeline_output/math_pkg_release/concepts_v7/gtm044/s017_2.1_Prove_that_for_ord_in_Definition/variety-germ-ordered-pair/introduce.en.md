The notation $V_W$ formalizes the germ of a variety $V$ along an irreducible subvariety $W$, encoding local behavior near $W$. It is defined as an ordered pair $(V_{(W)}, W)$ where $V_{(W)}$ collects those irreducible components of $V$ that contain $W$. The operations of equality, inclusion, intersection, and union are defined to respect the common "center" $W$, forming a lattice structure used to study local properties such as nonsingularity.
