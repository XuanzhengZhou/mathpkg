Let $\{(X_n, d_n), n \in \omega\}$ be a sequence of pseudo-metric spaces, each of diameter at most one. Define
$$d(x,y) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty 2^{-n} d_n(x_n, y_n).$$
Then $d$ is a pseudo-metric for the Cartesian product $\prod_{n \in \omega} X_n$, and the pseudo-metric topology is the product topology.
