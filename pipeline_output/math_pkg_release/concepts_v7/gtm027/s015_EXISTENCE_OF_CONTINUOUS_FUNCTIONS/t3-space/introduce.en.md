A $T_3$-space is a regular space that also satisfies the $T_1$ axiom. In the hierarchy of separation axioms for $T_1$-spaces, $T_3$ lies between Hausdorff ($T_2$) and $T_4$ (normal). Every metric space is $T_3$, and every $T_3$ Lindelof space is $T_4$ by Tychonoff's lemma.
