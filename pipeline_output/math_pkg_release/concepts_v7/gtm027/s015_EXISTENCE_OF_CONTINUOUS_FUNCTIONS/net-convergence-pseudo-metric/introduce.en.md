In pseudo-metric spaces, net convergence admits a simple metric characterization: $S_n \to s$ if and only if $d(S_n, s) \to 0$ as real numbers. This follows because the open $r$-spheres form a neighborhood base at each point. The result unifies the metric notion of convergence with the general topological notion via nets.
