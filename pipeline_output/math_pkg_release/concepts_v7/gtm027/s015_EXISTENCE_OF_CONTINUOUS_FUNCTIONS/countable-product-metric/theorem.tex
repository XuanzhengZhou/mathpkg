Let $\{(X_n, d_n) : n \in \omega\}$ be a sequence of pseudo-metric spaces, each of diameter at most one. Define $d$ on the Cartesian product $X = \prod_{n \in \omega} X_n$ by:
$$d(x, y) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} 2^{-n} d_n(x_n, y_n).$$
Then $d$ is a pseudo-metric for $X$, and the pseudo-metric topology coincides with the product topology.
