This lemma concerns the behavior of intersections of closed compact sets with respect to open covers. If the total intersection of a family of closed compact sets is contained in an open set $U$, then already the intersection of some finite subfamily is contained in $U$. This is a compactness result in the spirit of the finite intersection property, showing that for closed compact sets, containment in an open set is essentially a finite condition.
