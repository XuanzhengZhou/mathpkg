This is the dual open mapping theorem. A homomorphism : H \to G$ from a metrizable right-complete group with a closed graph is open, provided the closure of the image of each identity neighborhood in $ is an identity neighborhood in $. The proof is dual to the closed graph theorem, applying Lemma 6.36 to the inverse relation ^{-1}$.
