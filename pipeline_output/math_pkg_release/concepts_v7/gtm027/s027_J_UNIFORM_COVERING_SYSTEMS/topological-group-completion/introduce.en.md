Given a Hausdorff topological group $ with its right uniformity, the uniform space completion $ carries a unique extension of the group operation making it a topological group. Moreover, the right uniformity on the completion becomes the two-sided uniformity. This theorem provides the foundation for studying complete topological groups and shows that the completion of a topological group is again a topological group in a natural way.
