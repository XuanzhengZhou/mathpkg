Let $F$ be a family of functions on a set $X$ to a uniform space $(Y, \mathcal{V})$ and let $\alpha$ be a family of subsets of $X$. The **uniformity of uniform convergence on members of $\alpha$**, denoted $\mathfrak{U} \mid \alpha$, is the uniformity for $F$ having as a subbase all sets of the form
$$W(A, V) = \{(f, g) : (f(x), g(x)) \in V \text{ for each } x \in A\}$$
for $A$ in $\alpha$ and $V$ in $\mathcal{V}$.
