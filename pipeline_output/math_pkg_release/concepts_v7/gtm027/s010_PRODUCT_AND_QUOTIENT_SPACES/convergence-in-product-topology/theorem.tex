A net $\{S_n, n \in D\}$ in a product space $\bigwedge \{X_a : a \in A\}$ converges to a point $s$ in the product topology if and only if for each $a$ in $A$, the net $\{P_a(S_n), n \in D\}$ converges to $P_a(s) = s_a$ in $X_a$. That is, convergence in the product topology is \textbf{coordinatewise convergence}.
