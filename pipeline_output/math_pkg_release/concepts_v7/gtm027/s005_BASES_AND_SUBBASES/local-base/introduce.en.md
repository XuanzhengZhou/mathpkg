A local base at a point $x$ is a collection of neighborhoods of $x$ such that every neighborhood of $x$ contains a member of this collection. This localizes the notion of a base to a single point. The family of all open neighborhoods of a point always forms a local base. Local bases are key to local topological properties and to the first axiom of countability.
