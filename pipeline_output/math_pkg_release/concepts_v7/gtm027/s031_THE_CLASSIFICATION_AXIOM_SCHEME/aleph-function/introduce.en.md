There exists a unique order-isomorphism $\aleph$ between the ordinals and the infinite cardinals. This is the $\aleph$-function: $\aleph_0 = \omega$, $\aleph_1$ is the first uncountable cardinal, and so on. The Continuum Hypothesis asserts $\aleph_1 = P(\omega) = 2^{\aleph_0}$.
