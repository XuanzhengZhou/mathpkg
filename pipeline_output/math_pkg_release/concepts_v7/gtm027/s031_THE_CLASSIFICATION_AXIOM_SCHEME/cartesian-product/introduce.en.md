The Cartesian product $x \times y$ is the class of all ordered pairs with first coordinate in $x$ and second coordinate in $y$. The proof that $x \times y$ is a set when $x$ and $y$ are sets uses the Axiom of Substitution and the Axiom of Amalgamation. The Cartesian product is fundamental for defining relations, functions, product spaces in topology, and many other mathematical constructions.
