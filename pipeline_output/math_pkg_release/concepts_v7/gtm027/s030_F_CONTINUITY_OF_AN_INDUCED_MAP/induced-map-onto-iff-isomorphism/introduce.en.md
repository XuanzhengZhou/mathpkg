A continuous map $F: X \to Y$ between compact Hausdorff spaces is surjective precisely when the induced algebra homomorphism $F^*$ is injective (an isomorphism onto its image) and its image contains the constant function $1$. This establishes the correspondence between surjective maps and unital subalgebra embeddings in Gelfand duality.
