With respect to the relativized usual topology for the real numbers:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] Any two open intervals of real numbers are homeomorphic.
\item[(b)] Any two closed intervals are homeomorphic, and any two half-open intervals are homeomorphic.
\item[(c)] No open interval is homeomorphic to a closed or half-open interval, and no closed interval is homeomorphic to a half-open interval.
\end{itemize}
