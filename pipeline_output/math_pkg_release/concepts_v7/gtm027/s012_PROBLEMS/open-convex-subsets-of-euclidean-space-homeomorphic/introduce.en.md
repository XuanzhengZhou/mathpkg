This theorem states that all non-empty open convex subsets of Euclidean n-space belong to a single homeomorphism class. In particular, the open unit ball, the open cube, and the entire space are all homeomorphic. This result is a topological manifestation of the fact that convex open sets are all "radially" equivalent and can be mapped onto each other by suitable stretching.
