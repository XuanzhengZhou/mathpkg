Theorem 12 establishes the equivalence between upper semi-continuous decompositions and closed quotient maps. This result bridges the definition of upper semi-continuity (in terms of saturated neighborhoods around each decomposition member) with the topological property of the projection being a closed mapping. The proof uses Theorem 10's characterization of closed projections to translate between the two formulations.
