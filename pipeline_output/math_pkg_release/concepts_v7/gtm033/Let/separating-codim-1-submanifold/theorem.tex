Let $N$ be a connected manifold and let $M \subset N$ be a connected closed submanifold of codimension $1$, $\partial M = \partial N = \varnothing$. If $M$ separates $N$ then the normal bundle $\nu$ of $M$ in $N$ is trivial, and $N - M$ has exactly two components; and the (topological) boundary of each component is $M$.
