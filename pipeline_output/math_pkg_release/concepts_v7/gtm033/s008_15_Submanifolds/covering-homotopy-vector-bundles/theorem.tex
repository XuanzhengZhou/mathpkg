Let $B_0$ be paracompact. Let $F: \xi_0 \to \xi_1$ be a morphism of vector bundles covering $f: B_0 \to B_1$. Let $h: B_0 \times I \to B_1$ be a homotopy of $f$. Then there is a morphism $H: \xi_0 \times I \to \xi_1$ of $F$ covering $h$. If $F$ is a mono-, epi- or bimorphism, $H$ has the same property.
