This theorem, the main result of Section 5.1, establishes that the degree is a complete homotopy invariant for maps from a compact $n$-manifold $M$ into the $n$-sphere $S^n$. Two such maps are homotopic precisely when they have the same degree. This result (a form of the Hopf Degree Theorem) reduces the classification of homotopy classes $[M, S^n]$ to the computation of an integer (or mod 2) invariant, translating topological questions entirely into algebra.
