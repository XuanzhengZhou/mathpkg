This proposition extends the tubular neighborhood theorem from the smooth ($C^\infty$) category to the $C^r$ category for any finite $r \geqslant 1$. It asserts both existence and uniqueness: every neat $C^r$ submanifold possesses a $C^r$ tubular neighborhood, and all such neighborhoods are equivalent via a $C^r$ isotopy of the ambient manifold.
