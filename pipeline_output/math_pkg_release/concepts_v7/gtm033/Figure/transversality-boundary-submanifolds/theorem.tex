Let $A \subset N$ be a $C^r$ submanifold. Suppose that either $A$ is neat, or $A \subset N - \partial N$, or $A \subset \partial N$. If $f: M \to N$ is a $C^r$ map with both $f$ and $f|_{\partial M}$ transverse to $A$, then $f^{-1}(A)$ is a $C^r$ submanifold and
$$\partial f^{-1}(A) = f^{-1}(\partial A).$$
