Theorem 4.2 generalizes the transversality theorem to manifolds with boundary. When $f$ and its boundary restriction are both transverse to a submanifold $A$, the preimage $f^{-1}(A)$ is a $C^r$ submanifold whose boundary is exactly the preimage of $\partial A$. The statement covers three cases: when $A$ is neat, when $A$ lies in the interior, or when $A$ lies on the boundary.
