The result of attaching a handle to a surface is independent, up to diffeomorphism, of the choice of gluing map within an isotopy class. The proof uses the fact that the diffeomorphism relating the two gluing maps extends from the boundary to the whole cylinder $D^1 \times S^1$, yielding a diffeomorphism of the adjunction spaces.
