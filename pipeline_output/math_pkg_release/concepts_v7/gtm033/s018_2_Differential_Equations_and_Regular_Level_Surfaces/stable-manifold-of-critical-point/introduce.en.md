The stable manifold $W_s(z)$ of a critical point $z$ is the set of points whose forward gradient flow converges to $z$. For a Morse function with a suitable Riemannian metric, $W_s(z)$ is a connected $C^r$ submanifold of dimension $k = 	ext{Ind}(z)$. Stable and unstable manifolds intersect transversely at $z$ and partition the manifold.
