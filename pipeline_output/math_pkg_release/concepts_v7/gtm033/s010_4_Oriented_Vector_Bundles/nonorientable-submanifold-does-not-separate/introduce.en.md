Theorem 4.6 is the contrapositive of Theorem 4.5 adapted to the fact that simply connected manifolds are orientable: any compact connected nonorientable codimension-1 submanifold of a simply connected manifold necessarily fails to separate the ambient space. This is a basic topological obstruction that underlies several classical nonembedding results, including the fact that real projective spaces of even dimension cannot be embedded in codimension one.
