Instead of specifying a maximal atlas — which is enormous — it is enough to give a single $C^r$ atlas: it uniquely extends to a maximal one. In particular, $\mathbb{R}^n$ and its open subsets carry a canonical $C^r$ structure given by the identity (or inclusion) map.
