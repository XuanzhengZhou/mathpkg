For any $k$-linear map
$$S: \mathbb{R}^m \times \cdots \times \mathbb{R}^m \to \mathbb{R}^n,$$
the norm $\|S\|$ is defined to be the maximum of $|S(x_1, \ldots, x_k)|$ where the vectors $x_1, \ldots, x_k$ vary over the unit sphere in $\mathbb{R}^m$. For all $y_1, \ldots, y_k \in \mathbb{R}^m$,
$$|S(y_1, \ldots, y_k)| \leqslant \|S\| \cdot |y_1| \cdots |y_k|.$$
