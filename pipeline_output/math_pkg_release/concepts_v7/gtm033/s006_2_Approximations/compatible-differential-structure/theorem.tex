Let $\alpha$ be a $C^r$ differential structure on a manifold $M$. A $C^s$ differential structure $\beta$ on $M$, $s > r$, is \text{compatible} with $\alpha$ if $\beta \subset \alpha$. This means that every chart of $M_\beta$ is a chart of $M_\alpha$. Equivalently, the identity map of $M$ is a $C^r$ diffeomorphism $M_\alpha \to M_\beta$.
