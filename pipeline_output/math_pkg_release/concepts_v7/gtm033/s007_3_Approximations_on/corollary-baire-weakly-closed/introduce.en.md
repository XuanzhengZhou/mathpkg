This corollary asserts that any weakly closed subset $Q$ of the space of continuous maps between $C^0$ manifolds is a Baire space when equipped with the strong topology. The proof uses a recursive construction of nested strong neighborhoods to show that a countable intersection of dense open subsets of $Q$ remains dense. This Baire property is essential for the density arguments in transversality and approximation theorems.
