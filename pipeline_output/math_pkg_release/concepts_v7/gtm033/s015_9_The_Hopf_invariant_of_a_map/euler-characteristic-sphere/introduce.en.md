Theorem 2.3 computes the Euler characteristic of the $n$-sphere. It equals $2$ for even-dimensional spheres and $0$ for odd-dimensional spheres. The proof uses stereographic projections to construct a vector field on $S^n$ with two zeros (at the poles) whose indices are $1$ and $(-1)^n$, summing to the stated result.
