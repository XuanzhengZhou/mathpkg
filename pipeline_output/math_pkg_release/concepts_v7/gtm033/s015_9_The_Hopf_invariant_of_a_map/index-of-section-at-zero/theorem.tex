Let $f \colon M \to TM$ be a section of the tangent bundle transverse to the zero section $M \subset TM$. For a point $x \in f^{-1}(M)$, the tangent map $Tf$ induces an automorphism $\Phi_{x}$ via the composition
$$
\Phi_{x} \colon M_{x} \xrightarrow{Tf} (TM)_{x} \to TM_{x} / M_{x} \approx M_{x}.
$$
Define
$$
\operatorname{Ind}_{x} f = \frac{\operatorname{Det} \Phi_{x}}{|\operatorname{Det} \Phi_{x}|},
$$
and set
$$
\chi_{f}(M) = \sum_{x \in f^{-1}(M)} \operatorname{Ind}_{x} f.
$$
