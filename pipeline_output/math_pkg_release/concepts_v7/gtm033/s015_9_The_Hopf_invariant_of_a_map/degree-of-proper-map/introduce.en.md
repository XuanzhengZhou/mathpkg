This definition extends the notion of the degree of a map to the setting of proper maps between noncompact oriented manifolds. Properness ensures that $h^{-1}(y)$ is a finite set for any regular value $y$, so the sum of local degrees is well defined. The definition generalizes the compact case, where properness is automatic.
