Let $f \colon S^{3} \to S^{2}$ be a continuous map. The \textbf{Hopf invariant} of $f$ is defined to be the linking number
$$
H(f) = \operatorname{Lk}\bigl(g^{-1}(a), g^{-1}(b)\bigr)
$$
where $g$ is a $C^{\infty}$ map homotopic to $f$ and $a, b$ are distinct regular values of $g$. The linking number is computed in
$$
\mathbb{R}^{3} = S^{3} \setminus \{c\}, \qquad c \neq a, b.
$$
