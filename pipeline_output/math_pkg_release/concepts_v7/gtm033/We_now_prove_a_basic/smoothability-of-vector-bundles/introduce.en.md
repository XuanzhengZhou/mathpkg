This theorem states that any $C^r$ vector bundle ($r \geqslant 0$) over a $C^\infty$ manifold admits a $C^\infty$ structure, and this smooth structure is essentially unique. The proof uses the classification theorem: take a $C^r$ classifying map $g: M \to G_{s,k}$, approximate it by a $C^\infty$ map $h$ (which is homotopic to $g$), and pull back the universal bundle; the resulting bundle $h^*\gamma_{s,k}$ provides the desired $C^\infty$ structure.
