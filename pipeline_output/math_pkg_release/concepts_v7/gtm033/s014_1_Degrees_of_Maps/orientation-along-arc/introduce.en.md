Lemma 1.1 relates the quotient orientations at the two endpoints of an arc embedded in an oriented manifold with boundary. When the arc is transverse to the boundary, the orientation relation at one endpoint determines the opposite relation at the other endpoint. This lemma underpins the proof that the algebraic count of preimages along a 1-dimensional fiber is zero.
