For a compact connected $n$-manifold $M$ without boundary, homotopy classes of maps $M 	o S^n$ are completely classified by the degree (if $M$ is oriented) or the mod 2 degree (if $M$ is nonorientable). When $\partial M 
eq arnothing$, every map to $S^n$ is nullhomotopic.
