Theorem 5. Let $X = X(\omega)$ be a random element with values in the Borel space $(E, \mathcal{E})$. Then there is a regular conditional distribution of $X$ with respect to $\mathcal{G} \subseteq \mathcal{F}$. This theorem establishes an important result in probability theory.
