Let $\xi_1, \ldots, \xi_n$ be independent standard normal random variables ($\mathcal{N}(0,1)$). The random variable

$$\chi_n^2 = \xi_1^2 + \cdots + \xi_n^2$$

has the chi-square distribution with $n$ degrees of freedom, with density

$$f_{\chi_n^2}(x) = \begin{cases} \dfrac{1}{2^{n/2} \Gamma(n/2)} \, x^{(n/2)-1} e^{-x/2}, & x > 0, \\[8pt] 0, & x \leq 0. \end{cases}$$
