Minkowski's Inequality establishes the triangle inequality for $L^p$ norms: the $p$-norm of the sum is bounded above by the sum of the $p$-norms. The proof first verifies the elementary inequality $(a+b)^p \leq 2^{p-1}(a^p + b^p)$ to ensure integrability, then for $p > 1$ applies Hölder's inequality with conjugate exponent $q$ to factor $|\xi+\eta|^p$ and obtain the desired bound.
