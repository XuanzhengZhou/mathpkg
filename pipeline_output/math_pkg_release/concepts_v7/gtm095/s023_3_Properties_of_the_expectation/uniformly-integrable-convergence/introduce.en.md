Theorem 4 extends Fatou's Lemma and the dominated convergence theorem to uniformly integrable families. Part (a) provides the same liminf/limsup inequalities as Fatou's Lemma without requiring a dominating variable. Part (b) shows that almost sure convergence together with uniform integrability implies both convergence of expectations and $L^1$ convergence. The proof splits each expectation into tail and central parts to leverage uniform integrability.
