Lemma 3.2 states that for a smooth $2\pi$-periodic function $u \in \mathcal{P}$, the difference quotient $t^{-1}(T_{-t}u - u)$ converges uniformly to the derivative $Du$ as $t \to 0$. The proof uses the Mean Value Theorem and the uniform continuity of $Du$ on the compact interval $[0, 2\pi]$. This lemma is the crucial technical link between translation and differentiation, and it is used to prove that convolving a smooth function with a continuous function yields a smooth function.
