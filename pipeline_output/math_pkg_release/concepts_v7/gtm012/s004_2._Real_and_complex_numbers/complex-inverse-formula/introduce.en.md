Every nonzero complex number has a multiplicative inverse, which can be expressed concisely in terms of the complex conjugate and the modulus. From $z^* z = |z|^2$, dividing by $|z|^2$ yields $z^{-1} = z^* / |z|^2$. This formula is the standard practical method for computing the reciprocal of a complex number.
