Using the field axioms: $(-x) \cdot y = ((-1) \cdot x) \cdot y = (-1) \cdot (xy) = -(xy)$. This establishes the familiar sign rule that the product of a negative number and a positive number is negative. It follows directly from the earlier lemmas $0 \cdot x = 0$ and $(-1) \cdot x = -x$.
