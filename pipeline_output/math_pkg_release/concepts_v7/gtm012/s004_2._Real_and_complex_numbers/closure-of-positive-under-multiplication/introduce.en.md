The product of two positive real numbers is again positive. This is Axiom O3 in the order axiom system for $\mathbb{R}$. Together with O2, it guarantees the positive cone $P$ is closed under both addition and multiplication. The statement was truncated in the OCR and has been reconstructed from context.
