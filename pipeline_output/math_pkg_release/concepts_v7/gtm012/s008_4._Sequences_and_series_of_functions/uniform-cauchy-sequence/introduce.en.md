A uniform Cauchy sequence is the analogue of a Cauchy sequence in the metric defined by the sup norm on the space of bounded functions. Two functions $f_n$ and $f_m$ are close in this sense when their values are uniformly close over the entire domain. Uniform Cauchy sequences are precisely those sequences that converge uniformly to some bounded function, as established by Theorem 4.1.
