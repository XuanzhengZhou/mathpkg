Suppose $a_n \neq 0$ for $n \geq N$, and suppose $\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} \left| \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} \right|$ exists. Then the radius of convergence $R$ of the power series $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_n (z - z_0)^n$ is given by

$$R = \left( \lim_{n \to \infty} \left| \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} \right| \right)^{-1} \quad \text{if } \lim_{n \to \infty} \left| \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} \right| > 0,$$

$$R = \infty \quad \text{if } \lim_{n \to \infty} \left| \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} \right| = 0.$$
