The Dirichlet kernel $D_N$ of order $N$ is the function defined by
$$D_N(x) = \frac{\sin\left((N + \tfrac{1}{2})x\right)}{\sin\left(\tfrac{1}{2}x\right)}, \quad x \in \mathbb{R}.$$
It satisfies $D_N * u = u_N$, where $u_N$ is the $N$-th symmetric partial sum of the Fourier series of a continuous periodic function $u \in \mathcal{C}$:
$$u_N(x) = \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_0^{2\pi} D_N(x - y) \, u(y) \, dy.$$
