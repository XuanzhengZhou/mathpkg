Theorem 7.2 is a version of Fubini's theorem for continuous functions on a rectangle. It states that the order of integration can be interchanged when integrating a continuous function over a rectangular domain. The proof constructs auxiliary functions $F_1$ and $F_2$ using iterated integrals and shows their partial derivatives coincide, forcing them to be identical.
