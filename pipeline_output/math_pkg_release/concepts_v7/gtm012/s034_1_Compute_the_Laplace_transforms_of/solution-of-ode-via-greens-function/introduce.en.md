Theorem 7.4 provides the explicit solution to an $n$th-order linear ODE with constant coefficients and homogeneous initial conditions. The solution $u(t)$ is the convolution of the Green's function $G$ with the forcing term $f$, integrated over $[0, t]$. This is the continuous analog of the Duhamel principle: the response to a continuous forcing is the superposition of responses to impulses, with $G(t-s)$ representing the response at time $t$ to a unit impulse at time $s$.
