The Open Mapping Theorem states that nonconstant holomorphic functions send open sets to open sets. This is a fundamental result with many consequences: the Maximum Modulus Principle, the fact that holomorphic functions are either constant or open, and the local behavior $f(z) \approx a_0 + a_m(z - z_0)^m$ showing $m$-to-1 mapping near critical points.
