The space $\mathcal{L}'$ is the continuous dual of the Schwartz space $\mathcal{L}$. It contains all continuous linear functionals on $\mathcal{L}$, including those defined by ordinary functions (via integration) and singular objects like the Dirac delta. The space $\mathcal{L}'$ supports operations such as translation, complex conjugation, and differentiation, making it a flexible framework for generalized functions.
