A von Neumann algebra is a unital, strongly closed $*$-subalgebra of the bounded operators on a Hilbert space. Equivalently, it is a $*$-subalgebra that coincides with its own bicommutant. Von Neumann algebras form the foundation of noncommutative measure theory and provide the mathematical framework for quantum mechanics with infinitely many degrees of freedom.
