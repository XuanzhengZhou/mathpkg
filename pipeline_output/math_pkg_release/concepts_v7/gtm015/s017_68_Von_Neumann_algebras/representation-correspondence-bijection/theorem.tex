\begin{theorem}
The correspondences
$$\varphi \mapsto U_{(\cdot)}, \qquad U_{(\cdot)} \mapsto \varphi$$
of Theorems (69.20) and (69.21) are mutually inverse bijections between the set of all nondegenerate $*$-representations $\varphi$ of $L^1(G)$ and the set of all continuous unitary representations $t \mapsto U_t$ of $G$.
\end{theorem}
