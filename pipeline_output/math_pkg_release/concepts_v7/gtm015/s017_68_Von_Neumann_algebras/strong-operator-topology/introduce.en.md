The strong operator topology is the topology of pointwise convergence: a net $T_j$ converges to $T$ strongly if and only if $T_j x \to Tx$ in norm for every $x \in H$. Multiplication is separately strongly continuous. The commutant of any subset of $\mathcal{L}(H)$ is strongly closed, and every von Neumann algebra is strongly closed.
