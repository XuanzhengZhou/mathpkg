The ultrastrong topology on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ is defined via the auxiliary Hilbert space $K = H \oplus H \oplus \cdots$ (orthogonal sum of countably many copies). A basic zero neighborhood is characterized by a square-summable sequence of vectors. This topology is stronger than the strong operator topology but weaker than the norm topology.
