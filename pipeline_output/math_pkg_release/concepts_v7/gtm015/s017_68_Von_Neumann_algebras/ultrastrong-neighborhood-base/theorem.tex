\begin{proposition}
A basic neighborhood of $0 \in \mathcal{L}(H)$ for the ultrastrong topology is given by
$$\left\{T \in \mathcal{L}(H): \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \| Tx_i \|^2 \leq 1\right\},$$
where $(x_i)$ is a sequence of vectors in $H$ such that $\sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \| x_i \|^2 < \infty$.
\end{proposition}
