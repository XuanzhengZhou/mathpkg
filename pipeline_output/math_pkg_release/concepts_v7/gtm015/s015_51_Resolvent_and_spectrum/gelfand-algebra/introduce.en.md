A Gel'fand algebra is a commutative Banach algebra with unity of norm one. This is the setting for Gel'fand's representation theorem, which represents such algebras as algebras of continuous functions on a compact space (the maximal ideal space). The condition $\|1\| = 1$ is a convenient normalization.
