In a Hilbert space, an operator is right-invertible if and only if it is surjective. The proof uses the orthogonal decomposition $H = N \oplus N^{\perp}$ where $N$ is the null space, and the open mapping theorem to invert the restriction of $T$ to $N^{\perp}$.
