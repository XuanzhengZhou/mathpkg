For operators on a normed space, being a left topological divisor of zero is equivalent to not being bounded below: there is a sequence of unit vectors whose images tend to zero. The proof constructs rank-one operators $S_n x = f(x) x_n$ to link the operator TDZ condition with vector convergence. This connects algebraic and geometric properties of operators.
