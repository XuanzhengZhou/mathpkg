The evaluation homomorphism $\varphi_\lambda$ is the special case of the rational functional calculus when $A = \mathbb{C}$ and $x = \lambda$. It sends a rational function $f$, regular at $\lambda$, to its value $f(\lambda)$. This formalizes ordinary evaluation within the algebraic framework of the functional calculus.
