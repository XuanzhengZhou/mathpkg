If $B$ is a full subring of a Banach algebra $A$, then $B$ is closed under taking inverses of its invertible elements. This result states that the norm-closure $\bar{B}$ inherits the same property: it too is a full subring. This is a basic continuity property of the inversion operation in Banach algebras.
