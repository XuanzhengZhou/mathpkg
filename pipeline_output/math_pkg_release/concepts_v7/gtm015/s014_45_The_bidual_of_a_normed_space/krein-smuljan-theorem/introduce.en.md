The Krein-Smuljan theorem provides a practical criterion for checking weak* closedness of convex sets in the dual of a Banach space. It states that a convex subset of $E'$ is weak* closed if and only if its intersection with every closed ball is weak* closed. This is particularly useful because weak* compactness of closed balls (Banach-Alaoglu) can be leveraged to verify the condition.
