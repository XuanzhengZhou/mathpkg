If $M$ is a linear subspace of a reflexive Banach space $E$, the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $M$ is closed;
\item[(b)] $M$ is weakly closed;
\item[(c)] $M = {}^\perp(M^\perp)$;
\item[(d)] if $M_0$ is the image of $M$ under the canonical mapping $x \mapsto x''$ (of $E$ onto $E''$), then $M_0 = (M^\perp)^\perp$.
\end{enumerate}
