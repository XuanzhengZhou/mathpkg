The $L^p$ spaces are reflexive for all $1 < p < \infty$, meaning the canonical embedding into the bidual is surjective. The spaces $L^1$ and $\ell^1$ are not reflexive. This fact is crucial for weak compactness arguments and the application of the Banach-Alaoglu theorem in $L^p$ spaces.
