The defining universal property of the Stone-Čech compactification: every continuous map from $T$ to a compact Hausdorff space extends uniquely to a continuous map from $\beta T$. This extension property is the key to applications in functional analysis, where it connects to the maximal ideal space of $C_b(T)$.
