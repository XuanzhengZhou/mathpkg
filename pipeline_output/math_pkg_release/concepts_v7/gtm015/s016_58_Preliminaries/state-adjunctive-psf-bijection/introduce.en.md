On a unital $*$-algebra, there is a natural bijection between states (positive linear functionals with $f(1)=1$) and adjunctive positive sesquilinear forms. The correspondence sends a state $f$ to the PSF $\varphi(a,b) = f(b^*a)$ and recovers $f$ as $f(a) = \varphi(a,1)$. This bridges the algebraic and geometric formulations of positivity.
