The Gel'fand-Naimark theorem is the fundamental representation theorem for $C^*$-algebras: every abstract $C^*$-algebra (defined axiomatically) can be concretely realized as a norm-closed $*$-subalgebra of bounded operators on some Hilbert space. The proof constructs a Hilbert space from the set of all states on the algebra via the GNS construction.
