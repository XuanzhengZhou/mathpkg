Let $A$ be a nonempty closed convex subset of a Hilbert space $H$. For each $x \in H$, there exists a unique vector $y_0 \in A$ such that
$$\|x - y_0\| \leq \|x - y\| \quad \text{for all } y \in A.$$
Equivalently, $y_0$ is the unique element of $A$ that minimizes the distance to $x$:
$$\|x - y_0\| = \inf_{y \in A} \|x - y\|.$$
