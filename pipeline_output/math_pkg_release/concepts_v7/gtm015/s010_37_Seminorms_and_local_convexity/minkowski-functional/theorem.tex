Let $E$ be a TVS over $\mathbb{K}$ and let $B$ be a balanced, convex body in $E$ (i.e., a balanced, closed, convex set with nonempty interior). Then there exists a unique seminorm $p$ on $E$ such that
$$B = \{x \in E : p(x) \leq 1\},$$
namely,
$$p(x) = \inf\{\alpha > 0 : x \in \alpha B\} \quad (x \in E).$$
Moreover, $\operatorname{int} B = \{x : p(x) < 1\}$.
