Let $E$ be a TVS over $\mathbb{C}$ and let $H \subset E$. The following conditions on $H$ are equivalent:
(a) $H$ is a closed $\mathbb{C}$-hyperplane;
(b) there exist a closed, maximal $\mathbb{R}$-linear subspace $M_0$ and vectors $x_0, y_0 \in E$, such that $H = (x_0 + M_0) \cap (y_0 + iM_0)$;
(c) there exist a closed, maximal $\mathbb{R}$-linear subspace $M_0$ and a vector $z_0 \in E$, such that $H = z_0 + M_0 \cap (iM_0)$.
