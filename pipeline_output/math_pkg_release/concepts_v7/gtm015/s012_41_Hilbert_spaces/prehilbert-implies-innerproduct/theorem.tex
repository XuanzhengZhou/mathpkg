Every pre-Hilbert space (normed space whose norm satisfies the parallelogram law) may be regarded as an inner product space. Specifically, if $E$ is a (real or complex) pre-Hilbert space, then the polarization formulas of (41.4) equip $E$ with an inner product $(\cdot,\cdot)$ or $(\cdot|\cdot)$, as appropriate, satisfying all the axioms of an inner product (by Theorems 41.5 and 41.6).
