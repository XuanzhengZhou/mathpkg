Given a subgroup H of a group G, the quotient set G/H is defined as the collection of all left cosets xH, which are the equivalence classes under the relation xRy meaning x^{-1}y is in H. The canonical projection π maps each element x to its coset xH. When H is a normal subgroup, left and right cosets coincide, so G/H carries a natural group structure, making it the quotient group.
