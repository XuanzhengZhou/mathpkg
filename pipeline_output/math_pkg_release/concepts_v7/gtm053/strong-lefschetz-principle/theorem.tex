For an $L_1\mathrm{Ar}$-sentence $P$, the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathbf{C} \vDash P$;
\item $F \vDash P$ for every algebraically closed field $F$ of characteristic $0$;
\item $\tilde{\mathbf{F}}_p \vDash P$ for all but finitely many primes $p$,
\end{enumerate}
where $\tilde{\mathbf{F}}_p$ is the algebraic closure of the $p$-element field.
