Strong embedding is the correct notion of substructure in Hrushovski's fusion method, analogous to algebraic closedness within the class. The amalgamation property with respect to strong embeddings is essential for the existence of a universal homogeneous model (the existentially closed structure). This technique produced continuum many new strongly minimal structures (Hrushovski 1988).
