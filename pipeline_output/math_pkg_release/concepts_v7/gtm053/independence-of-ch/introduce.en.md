The independence of the continuum hypothesis from ZFC is one of the most profound results in mathematical logic. Godel showed CH holds in the constructible universe L (so CH is consistent with ZFC). Cohen invented the method of forcing to construct a model where CH fails (so its negation is also consistent). This established that the standard axioms of set theory do not determine the cardinality of the continuum.
