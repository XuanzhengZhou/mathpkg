The Hrushovski-Zilber classification theorem (1993) proves Zilber's trichotomy conjecture for Zariski geometries. It implies the trichotomy holds for strongly minimal sets in DCF_0, Hasse-differentially closed fields, ACFA, and compact complex manifolds. Applications include new proofs of function-field Mordell-Lang (Hrushovski) and Manin-Mumford (torsion points on abelian varieties).
