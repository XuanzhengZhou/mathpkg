The Hrushovski inequality defines a class of structures with controlled dependencies. Taking an existentially closed model in this class yields a new stable structure. Hrushovski used this to construct strongly minimal structures disproving the naive trichotomy conjecture, and Zilber applied it to construct pseudoexponentiation (ACFExp), connecting the method to Schanuel's conjecture.
