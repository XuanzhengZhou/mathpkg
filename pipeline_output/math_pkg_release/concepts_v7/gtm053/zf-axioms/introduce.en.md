The Zermelo-Fraenkel axioms (ZFC with Choice) are the standard foundation for modern mathematics. They describe the cumulative hierarchy of sets built from the empty set by iterating the power set operation. All of classical mathematics can be formalized in ZFC. Godel proved the consistency of CH with ZFC, and Cohen proved the independence of CH from ZFC via forcing.
