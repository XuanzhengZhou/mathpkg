This criterion states that a theory admits quantifier elimination precisely when the quantifier-free type of a tuple determines its orbit under automorphisms in a saturated model. This connects the syntactic property of quantifier elimination to the semantic property of homogeneity, and is the key tool for proving quantifier elimination in algebraic theories like ACF and RCF.
