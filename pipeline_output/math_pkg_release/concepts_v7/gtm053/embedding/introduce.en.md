An embedding is a structure-preserving map between two $L$-structures (structures sharing the same first-order language $L$). It generalizes the algebraic notion of homomorphism to the model-theoretic setting, requiring preservation of relations, operations, and constants in both directions. Every embedding is injective, and a surjective embedding is called an isomorphism.
