For a nonempty subset $B_0 \subseteq A$ of cardinality $\kappa$ and a fixed $a_0 \in B_0$, define for each $L$-formula $P(v_1, \ldots, v_n)$ a \text{Skolem function} $g_P : A^{n-1} \rightarrow A$ by
$$g_P(a_1, \ldots, a_{n-1}) = \begin{cases}
\text{an element } a \in A \text{ such that } A \vDash P(a_1, \ldots, a_{n-1}, a) & \text{if such exists,} \\
a_0 & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}$$
Let $B$ be the closure of $B_0$ under all $g_P$. Then the induced substructure $B$ satisfies $B \preceq A$ and has cardinality $\kappa$.
