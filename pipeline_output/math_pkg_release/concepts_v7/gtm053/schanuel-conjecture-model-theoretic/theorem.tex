For any $x_1, \ldots, x_n \in \mathbf{C}$,
$$\operatorname{trd}_{\mathbf{Q}}(x_1, \ldots, x_n, e^{x_1}, \ldots, e^{x_n}) \geq \operatorname{ldim}_{\mathbf{Q}}(x_1, \ldots, x_n).$$
In the context of ACFExp, this is equivalent to the Hrushovski inequality $\delta(X) \geq 0$ for the predimension $\delta(X) = \operatorname{trd}(X \cup \operatorname{ex} X) - \operatorname{ldim} X$.
