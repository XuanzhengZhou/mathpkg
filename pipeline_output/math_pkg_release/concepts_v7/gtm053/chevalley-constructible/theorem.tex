In an algebraically closed field $F$, the family of $L_1\mathrm{Ar}$-definable (with parameters) subsets of $F^n$ coincides with the family of \text{constructible} subsets — Boolean combinations of zero-sets of polynomials. Moreover, the definable sets are precisely those obtained from zero-sets by Boolean operations and projections $F^{n+1} \to F^n$.
