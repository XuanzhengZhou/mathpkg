Powers of a group element are defined by repeated composition: $A^m$ is the product of $m$ copies of $A$ for positive $m$, with $A^0 = E$ and $A^{-m} = (A^{-1})^m$. The usual exponent laws $A^{m+n} = A^m A^n$ and $(A^m)^n = A^{mn}$ hold, making the powers of a single element into a cyclic subgroup.
