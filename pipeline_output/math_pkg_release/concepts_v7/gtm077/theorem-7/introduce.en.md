This is Theorem 7 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

If $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ forms a complete system of residues mod $n(n > 0)$, then $ax_1 + b, \ldots, ax_n + b$ is also such a system, as long as $a$ and $b$ are integers and $(a, n) = 1$.
