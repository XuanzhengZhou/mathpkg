Gauss sums are exponential sums weighted by quadratic residue characters. They satisfy $G(1,d)^2 = d$ (up to sign), establishing a fundamental link between quadratic characters and roots of unity. Gauss sums are the key to proving quadratic reciprocity and determining the sign in the class number formula.
