Theorem 7 states that an affine transformation $x \mapsto ax + b$ preserves complete residue systems modulo $n$, provided $a$ is coprime to $n$. The coprimality condition ensures injectivity modulo $n$ (by the cancellation rule of Theorem 6). This fact is used in constructing residue systems for composite moduli and in the proof of the Chinese Remainder Theorem.
