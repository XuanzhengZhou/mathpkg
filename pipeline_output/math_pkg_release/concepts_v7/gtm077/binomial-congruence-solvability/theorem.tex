Let $q$ and $p$ be primes, $p \neq 2$, and $(a, p) = 1$. The congruence
$$x^q \equiv a \pmod{p^\alpha}$$
has exactly one solution if $q \nmid \varphi(p^\alpha)$. If $q \mid \varphi(p^\alpha)$, it has exactly $q$ solutions provided
$$a^{\varphi(p^\alpha)/q} \equiv 1 \pmod{p^\alpha},$$
and no solutions otherwise.
