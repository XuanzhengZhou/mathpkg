Let $\mathfrak{G}_1$ and $\mathfrak{G}_2$ be Abelian groups of orders $h_1$ and $h_2$. The set of pairs $(A_1, A_2)$ with $A_i \in \mathfrak{G}_i$, under componentwise composition
$$(A_1, A_2) \cdot (B_1, B_2) = (A_1 B_1, A_2 B_2),$$
forms an Abelian group $\mathfrak{G}$ of order $h_1 h_2$, called the direct product of $\mathfrak{G}_1$ and $\mathfrak{G}_2$. The subgroups $\{(A_1, E_2)\}$ and $\{(E_1, A_2)\}$ are isomorphic to $\mathfrak{G}_1$ and $\mathfrak{G}_2$ respectively.
