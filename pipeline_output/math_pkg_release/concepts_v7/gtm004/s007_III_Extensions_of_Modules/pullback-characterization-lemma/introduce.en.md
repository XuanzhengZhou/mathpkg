Lemma 1.1 provides a fundamental characterization of pullback squares in abelian categories (specifically, for modules). A commutative square is a pullback precisely when the associated sequence $0 \to Y \to A \oplus B \to X$ is exact. This lemma connects the categorical notion of pullback to the homological notion of exactness and is the key technical tool used to define the induced map $\alpha^*$ on extension classes via the pullback construction.
