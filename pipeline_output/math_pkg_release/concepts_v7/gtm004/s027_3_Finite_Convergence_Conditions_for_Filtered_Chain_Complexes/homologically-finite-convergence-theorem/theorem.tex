Let $\{C^{(p)}\}_{p \in \mathbb{Z}}$ be a filtration of the chain complex $C$, and let $\{E_r^{p,q}, d_r\}$ be the associated spectral sequence. Let the induced filtration on $H(C)$ be defined by
\[
H_q(C)^{(p)} = \operatorname{im}\bigl(H_q(C^{(p)}) \to H_q(C)\bigr).
\]

If the filtration is homologically finite, then:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] the associated spectral sequence converges finitely;
\item[(ii)] the induced filtration of $H(C)$ is finite;
\item[(iii)] $E_\infty \cong \operatorname{Gr} \circ H(C)$; precisely,
\[
E_\infty^{p,q} \cong (\operatorname{Gr} \circ H_q(C))_p = \operatorname{im} H_q(C^{(p)}) / \operatorname{im} H_q(C^{(p-1)}).
\]
\end{itemize}
