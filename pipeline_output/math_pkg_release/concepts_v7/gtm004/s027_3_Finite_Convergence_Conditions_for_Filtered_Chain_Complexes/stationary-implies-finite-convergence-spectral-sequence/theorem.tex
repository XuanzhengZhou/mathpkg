Let
\[
D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D \xrightarrow{\beta} E \xrightarrow{\gamma} D
\]
be an exact couple of bigraded objects with $\deg \alpha = (1,0)$, $\deg \beta = (0,0)$, $\deg \gamma = (-1,-1)$, and let $\{E_r, d_r\}_{r \geq 1}$ be the associated spectral sequence.

If $\alpha$ is stationary (both positively and negatively stationary), then the spectral sequence converges finitely; that is, given $(p,q)$, there exists $r$ such that
\[
E_r^{p,q} = E_{r+1}^{p,q} = \cdots = E_\infty^{p,q}.
\]
