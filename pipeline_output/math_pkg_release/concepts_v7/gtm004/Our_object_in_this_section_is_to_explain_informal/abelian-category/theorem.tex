An \text{abelian category} is an additive category $\mathfrak{A}$ in which:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] Every morphism has a kernel and a cokernel;
\item[(ii)] Every monomorphism is the kernel of its cokernel; every epimorphism is the cokernel of its kernel;
\item[(iii)] Every morphism $\varphi$ is expressible as the composite $\varphi = \nu \eta$ of an epimorphism $\eta$ followed by a monomorphism $\nu$.
\end{enumerate}
