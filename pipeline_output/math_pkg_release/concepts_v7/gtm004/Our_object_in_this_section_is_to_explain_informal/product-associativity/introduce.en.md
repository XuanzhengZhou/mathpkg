The categorical product is associative up to canonical isomorphism: $(X \times Y) \times Z \cong X \times (Y \times Z) \cong X \times Y \times Z$. This means the iterated product with appropriate composite projections satisfies the universal property of the ternary product. The associativity is essential for working with products of more than two objects without worrying about parenthesization.
