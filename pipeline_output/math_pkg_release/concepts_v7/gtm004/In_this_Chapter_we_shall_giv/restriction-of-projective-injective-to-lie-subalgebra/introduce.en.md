Corollary 1.5 states that projectivity and injectivity are preserved under restriction from a Lie algebra to a Lie subalgebra. This follows from the fact that $U\mathfrak{g}$ is a free $U\mathfrak{h}$-module (Corollary 1.4) together with a general change-of-rings theorem. The result is crucial for comparing cohomological invariants across different Lie algebras and for induction/restriction arguments.
