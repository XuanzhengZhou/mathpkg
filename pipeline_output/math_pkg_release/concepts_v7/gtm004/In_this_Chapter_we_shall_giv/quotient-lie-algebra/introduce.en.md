The quotient Lie algebra generalizes the construction of quotient vector spaces to the Lie algebra setting. When a Lie ideal is quotiented out, the bracket descends to the quotient, yielding a well-defined Lie algebra structure. This construction is fundamental for exact sequences of Lie algebras and plays a role in the homological algebra of Lie algebras analogous to quotient groups in group cohomology.
