Let $F : \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{D}$, $F' : \mathfrak{D} \to \mathfrak{E}$, $G : \mathfrak{D} \to \mathfrak{C}$, $G' : \mathfrak{E} \to \mathfrak{D}$ be functors and let $\eta : F \dashv G$, $\eta' : F' \dashv G'$ be adjugants. Then $F'F \dashv GG'$, where the composite adjugant is $\eta'' = \eta \circ \eta'$.
