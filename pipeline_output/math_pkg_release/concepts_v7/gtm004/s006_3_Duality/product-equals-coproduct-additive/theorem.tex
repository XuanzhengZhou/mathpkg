In an additive category $\mathfrak{A}$, for any two objects $A_1, A_2$, the product $A_1 \oplus A_2$ (with projections $p_1, p_2$) is also a coproduct with injections
$$
i_1 = \{1, 0\} : A_1 \to A_1 \oplus A_2, \qquad i_2 = \{0, 1\} : A_2 \to A_1 \oplus A_2.
$$
Moreover, given $\varphi_i : A_i \to B$, the coproduct morphism is given by
$$
\langle \varphi_1, \varphi_2 \rangle = \varphi_1 p_1 + \varphi_2 p_2 : A_1 \oplus A_2 \to B.
$$
The injections and projections satisfy $i_1 p_1 + i_2 p_2 = 1$ and $p_k i_\ell = \delta_{k\ell}$.
