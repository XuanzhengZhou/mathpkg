For any functor $F : \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{D}$ and small category $\mathfrak{P}$, the diagram
$$
\begin{array}{ccc}
\mathfrak{C} & \xrightarrow{F} & \mathfrak{D} \\
{\scriptstyle P}\downarrow & & \downarrow{\scriptstyle P} \\
\mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{P}} & \xrightarrow{F^{\mathfrak{P}}} & \mathfrak{D}^{\mathfrak{P}}
\end{array}
$$
commutes, where $P$ denotes the constant functor.
