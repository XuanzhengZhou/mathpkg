A \textit{long exact sequence} in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$ is a sequence
$$
\cdots \to A_{n+1} \xrightarrow{\varphi_n} A_n \xrightarrow{\varphi_{n-1}} A_{n-1} \to \cdots
$$
where for each $n$, writing $\varphi_n = \mu_n \varepsilon_n$ with $\mu_n$ monomorphic and $\varepsilon_n$ epimorphic, $\mu_n$ is the kernel of $\varepsilon_{n+1}$ (equivalently, $\varepsilon_{n+1}$ is the cokernel of $\mu_n$).
