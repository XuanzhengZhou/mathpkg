Let $\varphi: C \rightarrow D$ be a chain map of degree zero between chain complexes over $\Lambda$. Then $\varphi$ induces a well-defined morphism of graded modules of degree zero,
$$
H(\varphi) = \varphi_*: H(C) \longrightarrow H(D),
$$
given on each degree $n$ by $\varphi_*[z] = [\varphi_n(z)]$ for $z \in \ker \partial_n^C$, where $[z]$ denotes the homology class of $z$.
