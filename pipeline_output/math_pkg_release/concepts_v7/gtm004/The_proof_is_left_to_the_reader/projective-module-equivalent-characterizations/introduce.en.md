This theorem provides five equivalent characterizations of projective modules, unifying their definition across different perspectives in homological algebra. The equivalence of (1) and (2) shows projectivity means the Hom functor preserves exactness of short exact sequences. Condition (3) says every surjection onto a projective splits. Conditions (4) and (5) give structural descriptions: projectives are exactly the direct summands of free modules. This is one of the most important theorems in the chapter, forming the foundation for projective resolutions and derived functors.
