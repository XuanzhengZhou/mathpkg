Over a principal ideal domain $\Lambda$, every projective $\Lambda$-module is free.

This follows immediately from Theorem 5.1 together with the fact that every projective module is a direct summand of a free module (Theorem 4.7, condition (5)). Since a direct summand of a free module is a submodule of a free module, and by Theorem 5.1 every submodule of a free module over a PID is free, every projective module must be free.
