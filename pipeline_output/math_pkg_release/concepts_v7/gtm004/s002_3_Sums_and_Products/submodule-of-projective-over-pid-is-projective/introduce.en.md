Over a principal ideal domain $\Lambda$, projectivity is inherited by submodules. Since every projective $\Lambda$-module is free (Corollary 5.2), and every submodule of a free $\Lambda$-module is free (Theorem 5.1), any submodule of a projective module is free, hence projective. This completes a clean picture: over a PID, free = projective, and both properties are inherited by submodules.
