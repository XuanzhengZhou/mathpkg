Theorem 8.4 provides five equivalent characterizations of an injective $\Lambda$-module, dualizing Theorem 4.7 for projective modules. The conditions range from the defining lifting property (1) to the exactness of the contravariant Hom functor (2), the splitting of monomorphisms from $I$ (3), the property of being a direct summand in every containing module (4), and finally the structural characterization that every injective module is a direct summand of a cofree module (5). This last condition is the injective analogue of the fact that every module is a quotient of a free (hence projective) module.
