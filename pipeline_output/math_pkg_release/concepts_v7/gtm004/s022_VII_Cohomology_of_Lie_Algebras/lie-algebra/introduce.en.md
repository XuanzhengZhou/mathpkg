A Lie algebra is an algebraic structure whose product (the Lie bracket) is bilinear, alternating, and satisfies the Jacobi identity. It captures the infinitesimal structure of Lie groups and is fundamental to both algebra and differential geometry. The Jacobi identity replaces associativity and encodes a compatibility condition among nested brackets.
