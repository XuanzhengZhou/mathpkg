Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a Lie algebra over a field $K$. The \textbf{universal enveloping algebra} $U\mathfrak{g}$ of $\mathfrak{g}$ is the quotient of the tensor algebra $T\mathfrak{g}$ by the two-sided ideal $I$ generated by all elements of the form
$$
x \otimes y - y \otimes x - [x, y], \qquad x, y \in \mathfrak{g}.
$$
Thus
$$
U\mathfrak{g} = T\mathfrak{g} \,/\, (x \otimes y - y \otimes x - [x, y]).
$$
There is a canonical $K$-linear map $i : \mathfrak{g} \to U\mathfrak{g}$ induced by the inclusion $\mathfrak{g} = T_1 \mathfrak{g} \hookrightarrow T\mathfrak{g}$ followed by the quotient projection.
