Let $\mathfrak{n} \to \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{h}$ be an exact sequence of Lie algebras over a field $K$ (i.e., $\mathfrak{n}$ is a Lie ideal of $\mathfrak{g}$ with quotient $\mathfrak{h}$). Then there is an isomorphism of right $\mathfrak{g}$-modules
$$
K \otimes_{U\mathfrak{n}} U\mathfrak{g} \cong U\mathfrak{h},
$$
where $K$ is the trivial $U\mathfrak{n}$-module (on which $\mathfrak{n}$ acts as zero).
