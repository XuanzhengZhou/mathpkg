Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a Lie algebra over a field $K$, and let $U\mathfrak{g}$ be its universal enveloping algebra with canonical map $i : \mathfrak{g} \to U\mathfrak{g}$. Let $LU\mathfrak{g}$ denote the Lie algebra associated to the associative algebra $U\mathfrak{g}$ (with bracket $[a, b] = ab - ba$). Then the canonical map
$$
i : \mathfrak{g} \longrightarrow LU\mathfrak{g}
$$
is an injective Lie algebra homomorphism, i.e., an embedding.
