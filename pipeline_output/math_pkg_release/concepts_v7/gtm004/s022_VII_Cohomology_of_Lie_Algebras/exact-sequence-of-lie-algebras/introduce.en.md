An exact sequence of Lie algebras formalizes the relationship between a Lie algebra, a Lie ideal, and the corresponding quotient. Just as in abelian categories, exactness means the image of one map equals the kernel of the next. Short exact sequences $\mathfrak{n} 	o \mathfrak{g} 	o \mathfrak{h}$ encode how $\mathfrak{g}$ is an extension of $\mathfrak{h}$ by $\mathfrak{n}$.
