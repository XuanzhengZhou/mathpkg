\text{An extension of $A$ by $B$ is called a \textbf{split extension} if it is equivalent to the direct sum extension}
$$
B \xrightarrow{\iota_B} A \oplus B \xrightarrow{\pi_A} A
$$
\text{where $\iota_B$ is the canonical inclusion and $\pi_A$ the canonical projection. Equivalently, there exist maps $\iota_A: A \to A \oplus B$ and $\pi_B: A \oplus B \to B$ satisfying $\pi_A \iota_A = 1_A$ and $\pi_B \iota_B = 1_B$.}
