Let $f: G \to G'$ be a group homomorphism, and let $Uf: {}_{\mathbb{Z}G'}\mathbf{M} \to {}_{\mathbb{Z}G}\mathbf{M}$ be the restriction functor. For a $G'$-module $A'$ there is a natural homomorphism
$$\theta: H^n(G', A') \to H^n(G, UfA').$$

For a right $G'$-module $B'$ there is a natural homomorphism
$$\theta: H_n(G, UfB') \to H_n(G', B').$$

Thus $H^n(-, -)$ and $H_n(-, -)$ are functors on the category $\mathfrak{G}^*$ whose objects are pairs $(G, A)$ with $G$ a group and $A$ a $G$-module.
