Property (2.3) records the long exact sequences in group cohomology and homology induced by short exact sequences of coefficient modules. These are immediate consequences of the definition of $H^n$ and $H_n$ as derived functors ($\operatorname{Ext}$ and $\operatorname{Tor}$). They are the fundamental computational tool, linking cohomology groups in different dimensions via connecting homomorphisms.
