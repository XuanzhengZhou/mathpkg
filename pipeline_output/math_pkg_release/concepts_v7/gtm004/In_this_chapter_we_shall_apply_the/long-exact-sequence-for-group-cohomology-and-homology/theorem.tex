To a short exact sequence $A' \xrightarrow{} A \xrightarrow{} A''$ of $G$-modules there corresponds a long exact cohomology sequence
$$0 \to H^0(G, A') \to H^0(G, A) \to H^0(G, A'') \to H^1(G, A') \to \cdots$$
$$\cdots \to H^n(G, A') \to H^n(G, A) \to H^n(G, A'') \to H^{n+1}(G, A') \to \cdots.$$

To a short exact sequence $B' \xrightarrow{} B \xrightarrow{} B''$ of right $G$-modules there corresponds a long exact homology sequence
$$\cdots \to H_n(G, B') \to H_n(G, B) \to H_n(G, B'') \to H_{n-1}(G, B') \to \cdots$$
$$\cdots \to H_1(G, B'') \to H_0(G, B') \to H_0(G, B) \to H_0(G, B'') \to 0.$$
