Lemma 4.1 establishes a fundamental isomorphism $IG/(IG)^2 \cong G_{\mathrm{ab}}$ between the quotient of the augmentation ideal by its square and the abelianization of the group $G$. The map $\psi(x-1) = xG'$ sends basis elements to cosets of the commutator subgroup, while its inverse sends $x$ to $(x-1) + (IG)^2$. This lemma is the key to computing $H_1$ and $H^1$ with trivial coefficients, yielding $H_1(G, \mathbb{Z}) \cong G_{\mathrm{ab}}$ and $H^1(G, A) \cong \operatorname{Hom}(G_{\mathrm{ab}}, A)$ for trivial $A$.
