Corollary 5.7 specializes the Hom-Ext sequences to the case of a principal ideal domain. Over a PID, submodules of projective modules are projective, which forces certain maps in the Ext portion of the exact sequences to be epimorphic. Specifically, $\psi_*$ in the second-variable sequence and $\varphi^*$ in the first-variable sequence are onto. This result does not hold for general rings, as noted in Exercise 5.3, and highlights the special homological behavior of PIDs.
