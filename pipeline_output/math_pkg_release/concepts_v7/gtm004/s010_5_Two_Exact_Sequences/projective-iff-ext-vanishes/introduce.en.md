Corollary 5.5 gives a homological characterization of projective modules: $A$ is projective precisely when $\operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B) = 0$ for every module $B$. The forward direction follows directly from the definition of Ext via a projective presentation, and the converse uses the Hom-Ext sequence of Theorem 5.2 to show that every short exact sequence ending in $A$ splits. This is the Ext analog of the fact that $A$ is projective iff $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, -)$ is exact.
