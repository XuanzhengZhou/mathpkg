A left $\Lambda$-module $B$ is called \textbf{flat} if for every monomorphism $\mu : A' \to A$ of right $\Lambda$-modules, the induced homomorphism
$$
\mu_* = \mu \otimes 1_B : A' \otimes_\Lambda B \to A \otimes_\Lambda B
$$
is a monomorphism. Equivalently, for every short exact sequence $A' \xrightarrow{\mu} A \xrightarrow{\nu} A'' \to 0$, the induced sequence
$$
0 \to A' \otimes_\Lambda B \xrightarrow{\mu_*} A \otimes_\Lambda B \xrightarrow{\nu_*} A'' \otimes_\Lambda B \to 0
$$
is exact.
