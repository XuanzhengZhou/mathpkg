Theorem 4.4 of Hilton--Stammbach is the dual of Theorem 4.1. Given a positive acyclic cochain complex $C$ and a positive injective cochain complex $D$, any homomorphism $\varphi : H^0(C) \to H^0(D)$ lifts to a cochain map $\varphi : C \to D$, uniquely up to homotopy. The proof is dual to that of Theorem 4.1, using the injectivity of each $D^n$ instead of projectivity. This theorem is the foundation for right derived functors constructed via injective resolutions.
