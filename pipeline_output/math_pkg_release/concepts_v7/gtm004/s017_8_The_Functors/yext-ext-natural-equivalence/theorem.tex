There is a natural equivalence of set-valued bifunctors
$$
\theta_n \;:\; \operatorname{Yext}_A^n(-,-) \;\xrightarrow{\;\cong\;}\; \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(-,-), \qquad n = 1, 2, 3, \dots
$$

Since $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A,B)$ carries a natural abelian group structure, the equivalence $\theta_n$ endows $\operatorname{Yext}_A^n(A,B)$ with a natural abelian group structure as well.
