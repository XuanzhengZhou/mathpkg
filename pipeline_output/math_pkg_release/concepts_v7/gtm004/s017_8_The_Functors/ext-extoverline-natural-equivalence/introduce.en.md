Proposition 8.1 establishes that the two definitions of Ext — via projective resolutions ($\operatorname{Ext}_A^n$) and via injective resolutions ($\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^n$) — are naturally equivalent. The natural isomorphisms $\Phi^n$ are constructed inductively using the connecting homomorphisms and the fact that both functors give the same values on injective modules. Together with Proposition 8.2, this shows that $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n$ is a balanced bifunctor, and from this point onward only the unified notation $\operatorname{Ext}$ is used.
