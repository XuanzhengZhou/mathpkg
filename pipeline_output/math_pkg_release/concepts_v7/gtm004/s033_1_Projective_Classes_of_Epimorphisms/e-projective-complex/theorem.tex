Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a closed class of epimorphisms in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$. A chain complex
\[
K : \cdots \to K_n \to K_{n-1} \to \cdots \to K_0
\]
in $\mathfrak{A}$ is called {\it $\mathcal{E}$-projective} if each $K_n$ is an $\mathcal{E}$-projective object.

The complex $K$ is called {\it $\mathcal{E}$-acyclic} if the augmented complex
\[
\cdots \to K_n \to K_{n-1} \to \cdots \to K_0 \to H_0(K) \to 0
\]
is $\mathcal{E}$-exact.
