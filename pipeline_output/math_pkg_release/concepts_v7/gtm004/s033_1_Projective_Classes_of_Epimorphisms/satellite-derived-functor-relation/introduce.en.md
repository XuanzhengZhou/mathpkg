This corollary shows that applying the satellite construction to the zeroth $\mathcal{E}$-derived functor recovers all higher $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors. The proof relies on the facts that $L_0^{\mathcal{E}} H$ is right $\mathcal{E}$-exact and that $L_0^{\mathcal{E}} H(P) = H(P)$ for any $\mathcal{E}$-projective object $P$, so that the derived functors of $H$ and $L_0^{\mathcal{E}} H$ coincide. This result reinforces the universal nature of the $\mathcal{E}$-derived functor construction.
