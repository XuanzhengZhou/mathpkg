A distinguished triangle is the triangulated category analogue of a short exact sequence in an abelian category. In the homotopy category of cochain complexes, distinguished triangles arise from the mapping cone construction: every cochain map fits into a distinguished triangle. These triangles satisfy axioms that generalize the behavior of exact sequences and provide the foundation for the derived category formalism.
