The homotopy category of cochain complexes is the intermediate step between the category of cochain complexes and the derived category. Its morphisms are homotopy classes of cochain maps. While the homotopy category is additive, it is generally not abelian; however, it does carry the structure of a triangulated category, a discovery of Verdier that makes the construction of the derived category possible.
