For a cochain complex $\mathbf{C} = \{C^n\}_{n \in \mathbb{Z}}$, the \textbf{translation functor} (or shift functor) $T$ is defined by
$$
(T(\mathbf{C}))^n = C^{n+1},
$$
with the differential $d_{T(\mathbf{C})}^n = -d_{\mathbf{C}}^{n+1}$. The functor $T$ is an additive invertible endofunctor on the category of cochain complexes and on the homotopy category, serving as the translation functor in the triangulated structure.
