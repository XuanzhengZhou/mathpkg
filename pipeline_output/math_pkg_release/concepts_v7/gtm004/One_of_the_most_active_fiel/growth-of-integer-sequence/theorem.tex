Let $\{s_n\}_{n \geq 0}$ be a sequence of nonnegative integers. The \textbf{growth} of the sequence $\{s_n\}$ is defined to be the least nonnegative integer $c$ such that there exists a constant $C$ with
$$s_n \leq C n^{c-1}$$
for all $n \geq 0$. If no such integer exists, the growth is said to be infinite.
