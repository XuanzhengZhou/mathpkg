Proposition 4.3 establishes that Q-processes compose functorially: applying $Q^{\varrho}$ followed by $Q^{\varrho_1}$ is equivalent to applying $Q^{\varrho\varrho_1}$ in one step, and similarly for epimorphisms. This composition property, together with commutativity (Theorem 4.1), allows us to write $Q_{\sigma}^{\varrho} = Q^{\varrho}Q_{\sigma} = Q_{\sigma}Q^{\varrho}$ unambiguously and to iterate the processes cleanly in the ladder construction.
