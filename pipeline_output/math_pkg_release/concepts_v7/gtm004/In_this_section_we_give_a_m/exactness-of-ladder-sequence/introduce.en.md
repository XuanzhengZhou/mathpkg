Theorem 4.5 is the central result of the ladder construction, proving that the sequence linking $E_{m,n}$, $D_n$, and $E_{m+1,n}$ is exact for all indices $m, n$. The exactness at $E_{m,n}$ follows from Theorem 4.4, while exactness at $D_n$ is established by the relation $\gamma_{0,n}E_{0,n} = \alpha_n^{-1}(0)$. This theorem shows that the ladder forms a two-dimensional grid of exact sequences, with the diagonal $m=n$ recovering the original derived couples.
