This theorem gives five equivalent ways to characterize injective modules. It is the dual of the characterization of projective modules (Theorem 4.7). The most useful criteria are (1) the diagram-lifting property, (3) that monomorphisms out of $I$ split, and (5) that every injective is a direct summand of a cofree module, guaranteeing enough injectives.
