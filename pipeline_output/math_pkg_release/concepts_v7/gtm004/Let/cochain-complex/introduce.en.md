A cochain complex is the formal dual of a chain complex, with differentials increasing degree rather than decreasing it. The two notions are interchangeable via reindexing ($C^n = C_{-n}$). Cohomology (as opposed to homology) arises naturally from contravariant constructions such as Hom and in applications to differential forms.
