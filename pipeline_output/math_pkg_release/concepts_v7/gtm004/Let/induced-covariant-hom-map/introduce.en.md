Given a $\Lambda$-module homomorphism $\beta : B_1 \to B_2$, post-composition with $\beta$ yields an induced map on Hom-sets. This construction makes $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, -)$ into a covariant functor from $\Lambda$-modules to abelian groups.
