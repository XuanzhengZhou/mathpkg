This technical lemma constructs a Cartan-Eilenberg resolution of the complex obtained by applying $F$ to an injective resolution of $A$. The double complex structure is essential for producing the $E_1$ page of the Grothendieck spectral sequence. The construction resolves cycles, boundaries, and homology objects simultaneously.
