Theorem 4.4 is the injective dual of Theorem 4.1. For positive cochain complexes, any map in $H^0$ from an acyclic complex to an injective complex lifts to a cochain map, unique up to homotopy. This dual result justifies the use of injective resolutions in defining right derived functors.
