Let $0 \to T' \to T \to T'' \to 0$ be a sequence of additive functors $\mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ which is $\mathcal{E}$-exact on $\mathcal{E}$-projectives. Then for any object $A$ in $\mathfrak{A}$, there exist connecting homomorphisms $\omega_n : L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T''(A) \to L_{n-1}^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A)$ such that the sequence
\[
\cdots \to L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A) \to L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T(A) \to L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T''(A) \xrightarrow{\omega_n} L_{n-1}^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A) \to \cdots
\]
\[
\cdots \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A) \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} T(A) \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} T''(A) \to 0
\]
is exact.
