An $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequence of functors generalizes the notion of a $\delta$-functor to the relative setting. The connecting morphisms satisfy naturality with respect to morphisms of short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequences, and the resulting long sequence forms a complex (is differential), which is the minimal requirement for a cohomological $\delta$-functor.
