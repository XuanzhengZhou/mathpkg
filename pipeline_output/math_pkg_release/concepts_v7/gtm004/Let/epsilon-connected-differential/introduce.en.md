Proposition 3.1 verifies that an $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequence of functors automatically yields a differential (semi-exact) sequence for each short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence. The proof uses naturality of the connecting homomorphisms and the zero short exact sequences $0 \to 0 \to A \to A \to 0$ and $0 \to A \to A \to 0 \to 0$.
