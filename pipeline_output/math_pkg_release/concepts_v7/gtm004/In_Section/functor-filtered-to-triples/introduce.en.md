This functor $F$ is the first step in the chain connecting filtered chain complexes to spectral sequences via Rees systems. By packaging a filtered complex $C$ into a triple $(G(C), A(C), \theta)$ — where $G(C)$ is the direct sum of copies of $C$, $A(C)$ is the sum of the filtration subcomplexes, and $\theta$ is the shift automorphism — the data needed to form the exact couples of the associated Rees system is organized. Composing $F$ with the functor $R: \mathfrak{T} \to \mathfrak{S}$ yields the Rees system whose exact couple $\textcircled{1}$ coincides with the classical exact couple of the filtered complex.
