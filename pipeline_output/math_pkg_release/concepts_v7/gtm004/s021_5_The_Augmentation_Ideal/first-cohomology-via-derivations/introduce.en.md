Corollary 5.2 states that the first cohomology group $H^1(G,A)$ is isomorphic to the quotient of all derivations $\operatorname{Der}(G,A)$ by the subgroup of inner derivations $\operatorname{Ider}(G,A)$. This generalizes the interpretation of $H^1$ from the case of trivial modules (where it reduces to $\operatorname{Hom}(G_{ab}, A)$) to arbitrary $G$-modules. The result follows directly from Theorem 5.1 together with the description of $H^1$ as a quotient of $\operatorname{Hom}_G(IG, A)$.
