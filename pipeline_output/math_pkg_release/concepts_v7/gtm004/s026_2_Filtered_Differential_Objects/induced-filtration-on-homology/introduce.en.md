Passing from a filtered differential object $C$ to its homology $H(C)$ induces a natural filtration on $H(C)$. The $p$-th filtration level $M^{(p)}$ is the image of $H(C^{(p)})$ under the map induced by the inclusion $C^{(p)} \hookrightarrow C$. This induced filtration is the target that a convergent spectral sequence aims to approximate: the $E_\infty$ term describes the associated graded object $Gr(H(C))$.
