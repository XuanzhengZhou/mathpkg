Proposition 7.1 establishes that the first right derived functor $\operatorname{Ext}_A^1(A, B)$ of the contravariant Hom functor is naturally isomorphic to the classical Ext group $\operatorname{Ext}_A(A, B)$. This isomorphism justifies the notation and shows that the derived functor approach faithfully recovers the classical extension group in degree one. The proof proceeds by considering a projective presentation of $A$ and comparing the resulting cohomology computation with the classical definition of Ext.
