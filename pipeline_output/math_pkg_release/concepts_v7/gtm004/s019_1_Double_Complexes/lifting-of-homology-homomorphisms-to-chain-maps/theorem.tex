Let $C, D$ be chain complexes over the principal ideal domain $\Lambda$ and let $C$ be free. Let $\psi : H(C) \rightarrow H(D)$ be a homomorphism of graded modules (of any degree). Then there exists a chain map $\varphi : C \rightarrow D$ such that $\varphi_* = \psi$.

More explicitly, using the splitting $C_p = Z_p \oplus Y_p$ where $\partial_p|_{Y_p} : Y_p \xrightarrow{\cong} B_{p-1}$, one constructs $\varphi$ by lifting $\psi_p : H_p(C) \rightarrow H_{p+n}(D)$ along the projections $Z_p \twoheadrightarrow H_p$ and inclusions $\bar{Z}_{p+n} \hookrightarrow D_{p+n}$.
