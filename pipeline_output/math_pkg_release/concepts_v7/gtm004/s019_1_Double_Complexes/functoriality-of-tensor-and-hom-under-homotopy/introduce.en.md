Proposition 1.2 establishes that both the tensor product and Hom constructions for chain complexes are functorial with respect to chain homotopy. This means that if two chain maps are homotopic, the induced maps on the tensor product and Hom complexes are also homotopic.

This is a crucial technical result: it ensures that the tensor product and Hom descend to well-defined functors on the homotopy category of chain complexes, which is essential for derived functor theory.
