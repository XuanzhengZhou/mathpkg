Corollary 1.3 follows immediately from Proposition 1.2: since chain equivalence means there exist chain maps that are homotopy inverses to each other, the induced maps on tensor products and Hom complexes are also homotopy inverses. Thus the tensor product and Hom complex constructions are well-defined up to chain homotopy equivalence.
