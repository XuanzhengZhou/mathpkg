Given two chain complexes $C$ of right $\Lambda$-modules and $D$ of left $\Lambda$-modules, their \text{tensor product} $C \otimes_\Lambda D$ is the double complex of abelian groups defined by
$$(C \otimes_\Lambda D)_{p,q} = C_p \otimes_\Lambda D_q,$$
with differentials
$$\partial'(c \otimes d) = \partial c \otimes d, \quad \partial''(c \otimes d) = (-1)^p c \otimes \partial d, \qquad c \in C_p, \; d \in D_q.$$
The total complex $\operatorname{Tot}(C \otimes_\Lambda D)$ is denoted by $C \otimes_\Lambda D$, with differential
$$\partial^{\otimes}(c \otimes d) = \partial c \otimes d + (-1)^p c \otimes \partial d.$$
