% Peak Function Extension
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a Banach algebra, $T$ a closed subset of $\mathcal{M}$ and $U$ an open neighborhood of $T$. Suppose that there exists $\phi \in \mathfrak{A}$ with $\hat{\phi} = 1$ on $T$, $|\hat{\phi}| < 1$ on $U \setminus T$. Then there exists $\Phi \in \mathfrak{A}$ with $\hat{\Phi} = 1$ on $T$, $|\hat{\Phi}| < 1$ on $\mathcal{M} \setminus T$.
