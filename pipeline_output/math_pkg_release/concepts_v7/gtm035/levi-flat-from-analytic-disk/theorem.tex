% Levi Flat From Analytic Disk
% From GTM035, Section s023
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\Omega$ be a region in $\mathbb{C}^2$ and fix $z^0 \in \partial \Omega$ with $\partial \Omega$ smooth near $z^0$. Assume there exists an analytic disk contained in $\partial \Omega$ passing through $z^0$ with nonzero derivative. Then $\partial \Omega$ is Levi-flat at $z^0$.
