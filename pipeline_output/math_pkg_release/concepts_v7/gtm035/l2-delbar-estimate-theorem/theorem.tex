% L2 Delbar Estimate Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s016
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

For a smoothly bounded domain $\Omega \subset \mathbb{C}^n$, there exists a constant $C$ such that for every $\bar{\partial}$-closed $(0,1)$-form $g$, there exists $u$ with $\bar{\partial}u = g$ and $\|u\|_{L^2} \leq C\|g\|_{L^2}$.
