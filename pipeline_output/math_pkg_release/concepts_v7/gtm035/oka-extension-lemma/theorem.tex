% Oka Extension Lemma
% From GTM035, Section s008
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Fix $k$ and polynomials $q_1, \ldots, q_r$ in $z = (z_1, \ldots, z_k)$. Let $f$ be holomorphic in a neighborhood $W$ of $\Pi = P^k(q_1, \ldots, q_r)$. Then there exists $F$ holomorphic in a neighborhood of $\Pi' = P^{k+1}(q_2, \ldots, q_r)$ such that
$$F(z, q_1(z)) = f(z), \quad z \in \Pi.$$
