% Holomorphic Pullback Of Forms
% From GTM035, Section s008
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

If $u = (u_1, \ldots, u_k)$ is a holomorphic map and $\omega(u)$ is a differential form, then $d(\omega(u)) = (d\omega)(u)$ and $\bar{\partial}(\omega(u)) = (\bar{\partial}\omega)(u)$.
