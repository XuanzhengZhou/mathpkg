% Polyhedron Approximation Polynomially Convex
% From GTM035, Section s008
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $X$ be a compact polynomially convex subset of $\Delta^n$. Let $\mathcal{O}$ be an open set containing $X$. Then there exists a $p$-polyhedron $\Pi$ with $X \subset \Pi \subset \mathcal{O}$.
