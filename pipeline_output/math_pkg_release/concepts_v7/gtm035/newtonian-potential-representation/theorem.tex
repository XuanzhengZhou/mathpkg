% Newtonian Potential Representation
% From GTM035, Section s002
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $G \in C_0^2(\mathbb{C})$. Then
$$G(\zeta) = -\frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{\mathbb{C}} \Delta G(z) \log \frac{1}{|z-\zeta|} \,dx\,dy, \quad \text{all } \zeta \in \mathbb{C}.$$
