% Area Lower Bound For Polynomial Hull
% From GTM035, Section s022
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $X$ be a compact subset of $\mathbb{C}^n$. Suppose that $p \in \hat{X}$ and that $B(p, r) \subseteq \mathbb{C}^n \setminus X$. Then $\mathcal{H}^2(\hat{X} \cap B(p, r)) \geq \pi r^2$.
