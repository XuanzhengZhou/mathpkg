% Exponential Representation In Banach Algebra
% From GTM035, Section s005
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Assume that $\sigma(x)$ is contained in a simply connected region $\Omega$, where $0 \notin \Omega$. Then there exists $y \in \mathfrak{A}$ with $x = e^y$.
