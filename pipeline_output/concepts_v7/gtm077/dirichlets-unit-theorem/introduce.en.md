Dirichlet's Unit Theorem determines the structure of the unit group: it is a finitely generated Abelian group of rank $r = r_1 + r_2 - 1$, where $r_1$ and $r_2$ count real and complex embeddings. The regulator of the field measures the covolume of the logarithmic embedding of the units.
