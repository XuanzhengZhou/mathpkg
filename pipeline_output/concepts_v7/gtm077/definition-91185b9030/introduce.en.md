This is Definition  from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. Let $p$ be an odd prime ideal in $k$, $\alpha$ an arbitrary integer in $k$ which is not divisible by $p$. We call $\alpha$ a quadratic residue mod $p$ and set  $$\left( \frac{\alpha}{p} \right) = +1,$... This concept appears in the section §54 of the book and is part of the foundational development of algebraic number theory.
