This is Theorem 97 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. The $h$th power of each ideal in $K$ is a principal ideal.  From this we can finally prove the statement formulated in §24.... This result appears in the section §30 of the book and is part of the foundational development of algebraic number theory.
