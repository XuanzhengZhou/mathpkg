Let $K \supset k \supset \mathbb{Q}$ be a tower of number fields. The relative different $\mathfrak{D}_k$ of $K$ with respect to $k$ is defined analogously using the relative trace. The relative discriminant $\mathfrak{d}_k$ is the relative norm $N_{K/k}(\mathfrak{D}_k)$. The tower formula holds:
$$\mathfrak{D} = \mathfrak{D}_k \cdot \mathfrak{d},$$
where $\mathfrak{D}$ and $\mathfrak{d}$ are the absolute differents of $K$ and $k$ respectively, and the product is taken in $K$. The prime factors of the relative different are those prime ideals of $k$ that ramify in $K$.
