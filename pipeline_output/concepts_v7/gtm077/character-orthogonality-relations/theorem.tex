For characters $\chi$ of a finite Abelian group $\mathfrak{G}$ of order $h$:
$$\sum_{A \in \mathfrak{G}} \chi(A) = \begin{cases}
h & \text{if } \chi \text{ is the principal character}, \\
0 & \text{otherwise}.
\end{cases}$$
Likewise,
$$\sum_{\chi} \chi(A) = \begin{cases}
h & \text{if } A = E, \\
0 & \text{otherwise}.
\end{cases}$$
