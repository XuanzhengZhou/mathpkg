Simultaneous congruences with respect to coprime ideals have a unique solution modulo the product. The Euler phi-function for ideals is multiplicative: $arphi(\mathfrak{a}\mathfrak{b}) = arphi(\mathfrak{a})arphi(\mathfrak{b})$ when $\mathfrak{a}, \mathfrak{b}$ are coprime.
