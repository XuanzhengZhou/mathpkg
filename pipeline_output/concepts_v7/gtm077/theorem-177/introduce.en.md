This is Theorem 177 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

If the ideal $\mathfrak{D}_k$ in $k$ is the relative discriminant of a field with respect to $k$, then $\mathfrak{D}_k$ is equivalent to a square in $k$.
