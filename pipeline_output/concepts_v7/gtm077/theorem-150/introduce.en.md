This is Theorem 150 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

For each rational integer $n$

$$G(n, d) = \chi(n)G(1, d), \quad c_n = \chi(n) \frac{G(1, d)}{d}.$$
