Theorem 23 shows that a finite Abelian group decomposes into a direct product of subgroups corresponding to coprime factors of the group order. This is the "primary decomposition": if $h = \prod a_i$ with pairwise coprime $a_i$, each element $C$ is uniquely a product of elements $A_i$ with $A_i^{a_i} = E$. In particular, the group is the direct product of its Sylow $p$-subgroups. This reduces the classification to groups of prime-power order.
