Let $\mathfrak{G}$ be a finite Abelian group, $p$ a prime dividing $h$. Let $\mathfrak{U}_p$ be the subgroup of elements representable as $p$th powers. Then the order of $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{U}_p$ is $p^e$, where $e$ is the basis number belonging to $p$. The groups $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{U}_p$ and the subgroup of elements $C$ with $C^p = 1$ are isomorphic.
