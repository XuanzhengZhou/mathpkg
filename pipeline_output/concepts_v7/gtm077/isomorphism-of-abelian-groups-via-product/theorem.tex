If a finite Abelian group $\mathfrak{G}$ contains subgroups $\mathfrak{H}_1, \mathfrak{H}_2, \ldots, \mathfrak{H}_r$ of orders $h_1, \ldots, h_r$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item The $h_i$ are pairwise relatively prime,
\item $h = h_1 h_2 \cdots h_r$,
\item Each element of $\mathfrak{G}$ is representable as a product of elements from the $\mathfrak{H}_i$,
\end{enumerate}
then $\mathfrak{G}$ is isomorphic to the direct product $\mathfrak{H}_1 \times \mathfrak{H}_2 \times \cdots \times \mathfrak{H}_r$.
