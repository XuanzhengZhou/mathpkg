This is Theorem 62 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. If $\omega$ is a root of an equation  $$x^m + \alpha x^{m-1} + \beta x^{m-2} + \cdots + \lambda = 0$$  where $\alpha, \beta, \ldots, \lambda$ are integers, then $\omega$ is also an integer.
