This is Theorem 97 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

The $h$th power of each ideal in $K$ is a principal ideal.

From this we can finally prove the statement formulated in §24.

This result is part of the foundational material in algebraic number theory, appearing in the section on Second Type of Decomposition Theorem for Rational Primes.
