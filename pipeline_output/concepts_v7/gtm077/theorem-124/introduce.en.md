This is Theorem 124 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

For $s > 1$, the equation

$$\zeta_k(s) = \prod_p \frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{N(p)^s}}.$$

holds where $p$ runs through all distinct prime ideals $p$ of $k$.
