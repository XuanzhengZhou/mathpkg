The quadratic reciprocity law relates the Legendre/Jacobi symbols $\left(rac{a}{n}ight)$ and $\left(rac{n}{a}ight)$ with a sign factor depending on $a$ and $n$ modulo 4. Along with the supplementary theorems for $-1$ and $2$, it allows efficient computation of quadratic residue symbols. First proved by Gauss in 1796.
