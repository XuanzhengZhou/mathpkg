This is Theorem 116 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. If $\mu$ is a number in $k$ which is not the $l$th power of a number in $k$, then the field $K(\sqrt{l}\mu; k)$ has the relative degree $l$ with respect to $k$.
