The direct product of two Abelian groups $\mathfrak{G}_1$ and $\mathfrak{G}_2$ is the Cartesian product with componentwise composition. The original groups embed as commuting subgroups that intersect trivially and together generate the whole product. This construction formalizes the idea of building a group from independent components and is fundamental to the structure theorem for finite Abelian groups.
