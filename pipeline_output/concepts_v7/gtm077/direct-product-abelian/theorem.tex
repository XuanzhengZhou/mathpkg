Let $\mathfrak{G}_1$ and $\mathfrak{G}_2$ be Abelian groups of orders $h_1$ and $h_2$, with elements denoted by $A_1$ and $A_2$ respectively. The direct product $\mathfrak{G} = \mathfrak{G}_1 \times \mathfrak{G}_2$ consists of all ordered pairs $(A_1, A_2)$ with composition
$$(A_1, A_2) \cdot (B_1, B_2) = (A_1 B_1, A_2 B_2).$$
The identity is $(E_1, E_2)$. The subgroups $\{(A_1, E_2)\}$ and $\{(E_1, A_2)\}$ are isomorphic to $\mathfrak{G}_1$ and $\mathfrak{G}_2$ respectively, intersect only in $(E_1, E_2)$, and their elements commute.
