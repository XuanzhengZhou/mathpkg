This is Theorem 117 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

Only the following possibilities exist for the behavior of a prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ of $k$ under passage to $K$:

- $\mathfrak{p}$ remains a prime ideal in $K$,
- $\mathfrak{p}$ becomes the $l$th power of a prime ideal in $k$,
