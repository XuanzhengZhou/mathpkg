This is Theorem 141 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. The group $\mathfrak{R}(d)$ of norm residues mod $d$ for a quadratic field with discriminant $d$ has index $2^t$ in $\mathfrak{R}(d)$, where $t$ is the number of distinct prime factors of $d$.
