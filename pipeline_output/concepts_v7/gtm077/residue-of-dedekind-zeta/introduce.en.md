The residue of the Dedekind zeta-function at $s=1$ gives the analytic class number formula: $h = (w\sqrt{|d|}/2^{r_1}(2\pi)^{r_2}R) \cdot \operatorname{Res}_{s=1}\zeta_K(s)$. This connects the algebraic invariant $h$ with the analytic object $\zeta_K(s)$.
