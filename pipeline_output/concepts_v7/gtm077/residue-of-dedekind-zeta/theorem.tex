As $s \to 1^+$,
$$\zeta_K(s) = \frac{2^{r_1}(2\pi)^{r_2} h R}{w\sqrt{|d|}} \cdot \frac{1}{s-1} + \text{bounded},$$
where $h$ is the class number, $R$ the regulator, $w$ the number of roots of unity, $d$ the discriminant, and $(r_1, r_2)$ the signature of $K$. This is the analytic class number formula.
