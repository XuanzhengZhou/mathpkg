The $n$ field isomorphisms of $K(	heta)$ into $\mathbb{C}$ are given by mapping $	heta$ to each of its conjugates. The images of any field element under these isomorphisms are its conjugates in the field, each occurring with multiplicity equal to $n$ divided by the degree of the element.
