This is Lemma None from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

For each number $B$ in $R_k(\theta)$

$$S_k\left(\frac{B}{\Phi'(\theta)}\right) \text{ is integral.}$$

This concept is part of the foundational material in algebraic number theory, appearing in the section on Relative Norms of Numbe.
