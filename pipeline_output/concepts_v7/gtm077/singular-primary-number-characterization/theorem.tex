There are exactly $e_0$ independent singular primary numbers, say $\omega_1, \ldots, \omega_{e_0}$. Here $e_0$ is the basis number belonging to 2 of the group of strict ideal classes of the field. The $e_0$ characters $Q(\omega_i, \mathfrak{a})$ form the complete system of characters of the group of the strict class complexes for odd $\mathfrak{a}$.
