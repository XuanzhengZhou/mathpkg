This is Theorem 87 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

The content of a product of two polynomials is equal to the product of the contents of the two factors.

With this we have achieved a considerable strengthening of Kronecker’s Theorem 67 and also the generalization of Theorem 13 of Gauss to several variables and arbitrary algebraic number fields.
