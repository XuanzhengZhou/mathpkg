This is Theorem 50 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. If $\alpha, \beta$ are algebraic numbers over $k$, then the same is true for $\alpha + \beta, \alpha - \beta, \alpha\beta$, and, if $\beta \neq 0$, for $\alpha/\beta$.
