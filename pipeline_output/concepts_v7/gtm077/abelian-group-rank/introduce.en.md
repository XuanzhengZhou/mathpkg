Theorem 37 establishes that the number of basis elements (the rank) is an invariant of a finitely generated torsion-free Abelian group. Any $n+1$ elements must be dependent — a consequence of solving a homogeneous linear system over the integers. This is exactly analogous to the dimension of a vector space, confirming that such groups are free $\mathbb{Z}$-modules of well-defined rank.
