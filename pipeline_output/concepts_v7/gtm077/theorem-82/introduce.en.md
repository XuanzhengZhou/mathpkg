$$x^m + \alpha_1 x^{m-1} + \cdots + \alpha_{m-1} x^{m-1} + \alpha_m \equiv 0 \pmod{\mathfrak{p}}$$

with integer coefficients $\alpha$, has at most $m$ solutions $x$ which are incongruent modulo $\mathfrak{p}$.
