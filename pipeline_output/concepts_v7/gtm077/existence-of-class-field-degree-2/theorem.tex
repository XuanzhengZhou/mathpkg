For each subgroup $\mathfrak{H}$ of index $2$ in the group of strict ideal classes of $k$, there exists a quadratic extension $K/k$ (the class field) such that a prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$ of $k$ splits completely in $K$ if and only if $\mathfrak{p}$ belongs to $\mathfrak{H}$. This establishes the one-to-one correspondence between quadratic extensions and subgroups of index $2$ in the strict class group, the first case of the general reciprocity law of class field theory.
