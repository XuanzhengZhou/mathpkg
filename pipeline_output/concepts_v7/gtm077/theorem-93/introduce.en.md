$$\xi_1 \rho_1 + \cdots + \xi_r \rho_r,$$

where $\rho_1, \ldots, \rho_r$ are certain integers or fractions in $g$, while the $\xi_i$ run through all integers in $K$. We write $g = (\rho_1, \ldots, \rho_r)$.
