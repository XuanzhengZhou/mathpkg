Let $K$ be a number field of degree $n$ with $r_1$ real and $2r_2$ complex conjugates. Let $Z(s)$ be the number of ideals in a fixed ideal class whose norm does not exceed $s$. Then as $s \to \infty$,
$$Z(s) \sim \kappa \cdot s,$$
where
$$\kappa = \frac{2^{r_1}(2\pi)^{r_2} R}{w\sqrt{|d|}},$$
with $R$ the regulator, $w$ the number of roots of unity, and $d$ the discriminant. The constant $\kappa$ is independent of the ideal class.
