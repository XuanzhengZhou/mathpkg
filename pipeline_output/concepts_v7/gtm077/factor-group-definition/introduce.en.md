The factor group $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{U}$ is the group of cosets of a subgroup $\mathfrak{U}$ with composition induced from $\mathfrak{G}$. Its order is the index $[\mathfrak{G}:\mathfrak{U}]$. Two elements are identified if they differ by an element of $\mathfrak{U}$.
