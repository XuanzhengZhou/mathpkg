Gauss's genus theory for quadratic fields: the strict ideal class group modulo squares has rank $t-1$ over $\mathbb{F}_2$, where $t$ is the number of prime divisors of the discriminant. The genus characters are quadratic residue symbols, providing the first deep insight into the 2-part of the class group.
