Let $k$ be a totally real algebraic number field. For odd ideals $\mathfrak{a}$ and $\mathfrak{b}$ in $k$,
$$C_{\mathfrak{a}}(1) C_{\mathfrak{b}}(1) = \left(\frac{\mathfrak{a}}{\mathfrak{b}}\right) \left(\frac{\mathfrak{b}}{\mathfrak{a}}\right) C_{\mathfrak{a}\mathfrak{b}}(1) \cdot \text{(root of unity factor)},$$
where $C_{\mathfrak{a}}(\omega)$ is the Gauss sum with denominator $\mathfrak{a}$. The proof uses the transformation formula of the theta function under $t \mapsto 1/t$ and the resulting reciprocity relation.
