This is Theorem 143 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. At least one relation must hold for all ideals $a$ of the field which are coprime to $d$, namely,  $$\prod_{i=1}^{t} \gamma_i^{c_i}(a) = 1,$$  where the rational integers $c_i$ are independent of $a$... This result appears in the section §46 of the book and is part of the foundational development of algebraic number theory.
