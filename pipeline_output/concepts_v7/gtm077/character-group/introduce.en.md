The characters of a finite Abelian group themselves form a group — the dual group $\widehat{\mathfrak{G}}$ — under pointwise multiplication. Theorem 31 establishes that $\widehat{\mathfrak{G}} \cong \mathfrak{G}$ (a special case of Pontryagin duality). The principal character (identically 1) is the identity, and basis characters provide an explicit isomorphism.
