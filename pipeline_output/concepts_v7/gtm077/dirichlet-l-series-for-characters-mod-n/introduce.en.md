Dirichlet $L$-functions serve as building blocks for the zeta-functions of abelian extensions. The nonvanishing of $L(1,\chi)$ for nonprincipal characters is the key to Dirichlet's theorem on primes in arithmetic progressions and the decomposition law in cyclotomic fields.
