Let $\mathfrak{U}$ be a subgroup of an Abelian group $\mathfrak{G}$. The cosets $R = A\mathfrak{U}$ form a group under the operation
$$R_1 \cdot R_2 = R_3 \quad \text{where } R_3 \text{ is the coset containing } A_1 A_2.$$
This group, denoted $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{U}$, is called the factor (quotient) group of $\mathfrak{G}$ by $\mathfrak{U}$. Its order is the index $[\mathfrak{G} : \mathfrak{U}]$.

**Theorem 29.** If $\mathfrak{U} = \mathfrak{U}_p$ is the subgroup of $p$th powers in $\mathfrak{G}$, then the order of $\mathfrak{G}/\mathfrak{U}_p$ is $p^e$, where $e$ is the basis number belonging to $p$.
