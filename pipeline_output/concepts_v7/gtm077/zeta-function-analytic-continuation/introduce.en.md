This is Theorem 124 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. Finally, if $\chi$ is not the principal character $\chi_1$ mod $m$, then, by the basic property of characters, $\sum_{n} \chi(n) = 0$, where $n$ runs through any complete system of residues mod $m$.
