This is Theorem 113 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke. The GCD of all ideals in $K$ which contain only numbers in $R_k(\theta)$ is $\mathfrak{F}$, where $\mathfrak{F}\mathfrak{D}_k = \Phi'(\theta)$.... This result appears in the section §38 of the book and is part of the foundational development of algebraic number theory.
