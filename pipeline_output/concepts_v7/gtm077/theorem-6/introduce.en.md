This is Theorem 6 from Lectures on the Theory of Algebraic Numbers by Erich Hecke.

If $ca \equiv cb \pmod{n}$, then

$$a \equiv b \pmod{\frac{n}{d}}, \quad \text{where} \quad (c, n) = d,$$

and conversely.
