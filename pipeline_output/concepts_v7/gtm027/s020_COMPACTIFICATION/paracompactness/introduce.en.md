A paracompact space is a regular space in which every open cover admits an open locally finite refinement. Paracompactness is a natural generalization of compactness: while compactness requires finite subcovers, paracompactness only requires locally finite refinements. Every compact space is paracompact, and every metric space is paracompact (Stone's theorem).
