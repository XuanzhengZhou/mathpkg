Let $X$ be a Tychonoff space and let $(e, \beta(X))$ be its Stone-Čech compactification. Let $Y$ be a compact Hausdorff space and let $f: X \to Y$ be a continuous function. Then there exists a continuous extension $\tilde{f}: \beta(X) \to Y$ of $f \circ e^{-1}$; i.e., the following diagram commutes:
\[
\begin{CD}
X @>{e}>> \beta(X) \\
@V{f}VV @VV{\tilde{f}}V \\
Y @= Y
\end{CD}
\]
