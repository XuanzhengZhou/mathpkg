The Stone-Čech extension theorem is the defining universal property of $\beta(X)$: every continuous map from a Tychonoff space $X$ to a compact Hausdorff space $Y$ extends uniquely to a continuous map on $\beta(X)$. This makes $\beta(X)$ the maximal Hausdorff compactification and the free compact Hausdorff space on $X$.
