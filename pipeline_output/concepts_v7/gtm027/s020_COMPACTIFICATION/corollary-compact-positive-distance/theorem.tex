Let $(X, d)$ be a pseudo-metric space, let $A$ be a compact subset of $X$, and let $U$ be a neighborhood of $A$. Then there exists a positive number $r > 0$ such that $U$ contains the open $r$-sphere about every point of $A$. Equivalently, the distance from $A$ to $X \setminus U$ is positive:
\[
d(A, X \setminus U) > 0.
\]
