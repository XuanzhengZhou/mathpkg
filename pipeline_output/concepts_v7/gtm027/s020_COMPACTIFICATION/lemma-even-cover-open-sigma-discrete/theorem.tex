Let $X$ be a topological space such that every open cover of $X$ is even. Then every open cover of $X$ has an open $\sigma$-discrete refinement. That is, for each open cover $\mathfrak{u}$ of $X$, there exists a refinement $\mathfrak{v} = \bigcup_{n \in \omega} \mathfrak{v}_n$ where each $\mathfrak{v}_n$ is a discrete family of open sets.
