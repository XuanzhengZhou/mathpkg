Let $X$ be a topological space such that every open cover of $X$ is even. Let $\alpha$ be a family of subsets of $X$ which is either locally finite or discrete. Then there exists a neighborhood $V$ of the diagonal $\Delta$ in $X \times X$ such that the enlarged family
\[
\{V[A] : A \in \alpha\},
\]
where $V[A] = \{y \in X : (x, y) \in V \text{ for some } x \in A\}$, is locally finite (respectively discrete).
