This lemma establishes that precomposition with a set function induces a continuous map between product cubes. Given $f: A \to B$, the map $f^*$ sends each $y \in Q^B$ to its pullback $y \circ f \in Q^A$. The continuity of $f^*$ follows from the characterization of maps into product spaces: a map is continuous iff its composition with every projection is continuous.
