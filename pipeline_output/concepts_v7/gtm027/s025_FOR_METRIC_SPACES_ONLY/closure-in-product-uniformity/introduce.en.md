This proposition characterizes the closure of a relation (a subset of a product space) in the product uniformity. The closure equals the intersection over all entourage pairs of the composition V∘R∘U⁻¹. This formula is a direct consequence of the fact that the W(U,V) entourages form a fundamental system for the product uniformity. It provides a purely algebraic description of the topological closure in terms of entourage composition, which is particularly useful in the theory of uniform spaces and relations.
