\begin{proposition}[Closure in Product Uniformity]
Let $(X, \mathfrak{u})$ and $(Y, \mathfrak{v})$ be uniform spaces, and let $R$ be a subset of $X \times Y$. The closure of $R$ in the product uniformity is given by
\[
R^{-} = \bigcap \{V \circ R \circ U^{-1} : U \in \mathfrak{u} \text{ and } V \in \mathfrak{v}\}.
\]
\end{proposition}
