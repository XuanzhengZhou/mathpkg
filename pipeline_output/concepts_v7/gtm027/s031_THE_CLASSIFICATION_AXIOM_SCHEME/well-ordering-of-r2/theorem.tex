Define $\max[x,y] = x \cup y$. Define a relation $\ll$ on $R \times R$ by:
$(u,v) \ll (x,y)$ iff $\max[u,v] < \max[x,y]$, or $\max[u,v] = \max[x,y]$ and $u < x$, or $\max[u,v] = \max[x,y]$ and $u = x$ and $v < y$.

Then $\ll$ well-orders $R \times R$. Moreover, for any infinite cardinal $x$, $P(x \times x) = P(x)$.
