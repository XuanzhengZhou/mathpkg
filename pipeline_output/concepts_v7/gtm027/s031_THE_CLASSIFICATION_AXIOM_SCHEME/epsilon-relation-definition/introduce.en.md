The $\varepsilon$-relation $E$ is the class of all ordered pairs $(x,y)$ where $x \in y$. This encodes the membership relation as a class of ordered pairs, enabling the use of relation-theoretic concepts (well-ordering, sections, etc.) in the study of the set-theoretic universe.
