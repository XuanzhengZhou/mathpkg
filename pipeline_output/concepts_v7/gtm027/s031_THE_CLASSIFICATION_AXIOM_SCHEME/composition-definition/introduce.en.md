The composition $r \circ s$ of two relations is the relation consisting of all pairs $(x,z)$ for which there exists $y$ with $(x,y) \in s$ and $(y,z) \in r$. Note the order: $r \circ s$ means first apply $s$, then $r$. The notation $\{(x,z): \cdots\}$ abbreviates $\{u: \text{for some } x, \text{some } z, u = (x,z) \text{ and } \cdots\}$.
