\textbf{Definition.} A relation $r$ \textbf{well-orders} a class $x$ iff $r$ connects $x$ and every non-empty subclass $y \subset x$ has an $r$-first member. (A \textbf{well-ordering} is a relation that well-orders its domain.)

If $r$ well-orders $x$, then $r$ is transitive in $x$ and asymmetric in $x$.
