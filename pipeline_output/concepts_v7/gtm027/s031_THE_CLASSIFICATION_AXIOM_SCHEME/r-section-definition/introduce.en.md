An $r$-section (initial segment) of a class $x$ is a subclass $y$ of $x$ that is downward closed: if $u$ is in $y$ and $v$ in $x$ $r$-precedes $u$, then $v$ is also in $y$. $R$-sections are the fundamental building blocks for analyzing well-ordered structures.
