The union of two classes $x$ and $y$ is defined via the Classification Axiom Scheme as the class of all sets belonging to either $x$ or $y$. Together with intersection, complement, and difference, union forms the Boolean algebra of classes. The basic properties (commutativity, associativity, idempotence, and the distributive laws) are proved as theorems in the elementary algebra of classes.
