A net in a uniform space is Cauchy precisely when the associated net of pairs is eventually in every member of some subbase for the uniformity. Equivalently, it is Cauchy if p(S_m, S_n) converges to zero for each pseudo-metric p in any generating family for the gage. This lemma lets one verify the Cauchy property using a smaller, more convenient family of entourages or pseudo-metrics rather than the entire uniformity.
