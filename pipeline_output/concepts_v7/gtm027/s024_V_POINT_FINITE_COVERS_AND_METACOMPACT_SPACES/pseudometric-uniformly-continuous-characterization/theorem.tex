Let $(X, \mathfrak{u})$ be a uniform space and let $d$ be a pseudo-metric for $X$. Then $d$ is uniformly continuous on $X \times X$ (relative to the product uniformity derived from $\mathfrak{u}$) if and only if
$$V_{d,r} = \{(x,y) : d(x,y) < r\} \in \mathfrak{u}$$
for every positive real number $r$.
