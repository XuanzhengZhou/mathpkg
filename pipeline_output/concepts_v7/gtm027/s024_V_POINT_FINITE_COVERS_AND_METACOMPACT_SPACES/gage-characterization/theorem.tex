Let $P$ be a family of pseudo-metrics for a set $X$ and let $Q$ be the gage generated by $P$ (the family of all pseudo-metrics uniformly continuous relative to the uniformity generated by $P$). Then a pseudo-metric $q$ belongs to $Q$ if and only if for each positive number $s$ there exist a positive number $r$ and a finite subfamily $\{p_1, \dots, p_n\} \subset P$ such that
$$\bigcap_{i=1}^{n} V_{p_i, r} \subset V_{q, s},$$
where $V_{p,r} = \{(x,y) : p(x,y) < r\}$.
