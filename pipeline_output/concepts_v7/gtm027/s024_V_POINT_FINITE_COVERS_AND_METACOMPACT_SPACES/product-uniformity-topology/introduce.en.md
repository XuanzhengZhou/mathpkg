The product uniformity on a product of uniform spaces induces exactly the product topology. Moreover, uniform continuity into a product can be checked coordinatewise: a map into a product is uniformly continuous precisely when its composition with each projection is uniformly continuous. This result ensures that the category of uniform spaces is well-behaved with respect to products, paralleling the situation in topological spaces.
