Uniform continuity with respect to the uniformities on domain and codomain implies continuity with respect to the associated uniform topologies. As a corollary, every uniform isomorphism (a bijection that is uniformly continuous in both directions) is automatically a homeomorphism. This establishes that the uniform structure is a refinement of the topological structure: uniform invariants are topological invariants, but not conversely.
