A topological space $(Y, \mathfrak{u})$ is a \textbf{subspace} of a space $(X, \mathfrak{J})$ iff $Y \subset X$ and $\mathfrak{u}$ is the relativization of $\mathfrak{J}$ to $Y$. If $(Y, \mathfrak{u})$ is a subspace of $(X, \mathfrak{J})$ and $(Z, \mathfrak{w})$ is a subspace of $(Y, \mathfrak{u})$, then $(Z, \mathfrak{w})$ is a subspace of $(X, \mathfrak{J})$.
