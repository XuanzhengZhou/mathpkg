# Product Criterion for Total Boundedness

This corollary follows from the Subbase Theorem for Total Boundedness and characterizes when a product of uniform spaces is totally bounded. A product of uniform spaces is totally bounded precisely when every coordinate (factor) space is totally bounded. This parallels the Tychonoff product theorem for compactness in the uniform setting, establishing that total boundedness is a productive property.
