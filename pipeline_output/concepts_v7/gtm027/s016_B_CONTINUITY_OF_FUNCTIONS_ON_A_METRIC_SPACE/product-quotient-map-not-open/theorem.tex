There is a natural map of $X \times X$ onto $(X/S) \times (X/S)$ which carries $(x, y)$ into $(S[x], S[y])$. It is natural to ask whether this map is open, provided $X/S$ is given the quotient topology and $(X/S) \times (X/S)$ and $X \times X$ are given the product topologies. (This is equivalent to asking whether the product of quotients is topologically equivalent to the quotient of the product.)

If $S$ is the relation defined above, then the map is not open. (Consider the neighborhood $X \times X \setminus (A \times A \cup B \times B \cup \Delta)$ of $A \times B$.)
