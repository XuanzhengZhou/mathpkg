The product $\Omega_0 \times \Omega'$ is not normal.

(Let $A$ be the set of all points $(x, x)$, and let $B = \Omega_0 \times \{\Omega\}$. If $U$ is a neighborhood of $A$, let $f(x)$ be the smallest ordinal larger than $x$ such that $(x, f(x)) \notin U$. Then the theorem on accumulation points of graphs of expanding functions applies.)
