The "ce" operator (closure under finite intersections) is a fundamental tool in the Wallman compactification construction. Given a family $\mathcal{F}$ of closed sets, $\mathcal{F}^{\text{ce}}$ augments $\mathcal{F}$ by adding all closed supersets of finite intersections. This operator generates filters from arbitrary families of closed sets and is used to construct the closed ultrafilters that form the points of the Wallman compactification. The notation "ce" reflects the operation of taking closures under the "e" (intersection) operation within the lattice of closed sets.
