For a topological space $X$, let $\mathcal{F}$ be a family of closed subsets of $X$. The \textbf{ce operator} maps $\mathcal{F}$ to $\mathcal{F}^{\text{ce}}$, the family of all closed subsets $A$ of $X$ such that $A$ contains the intersection of some finite subfamily of $\mathcal{F}$. Equivalently, $\mathcal{F}^{\text{ce}}$ is the smallest family containing $\mathcal{F}$ that is closed under finite intersections and supersets (within the closed sets).
