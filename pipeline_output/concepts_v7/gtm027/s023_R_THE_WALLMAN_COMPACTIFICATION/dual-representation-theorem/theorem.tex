\textbf{Stone Duality Theorem.} The category of Boolean algebras and Boolean homomorphisms is dually equivalent to the category of Boolean spaces (compact Hausdorff zero-dimensional spaces) and continuous maps. Concretely:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Every Boolean algebra $B$ is isomorphic to the Boolean algebra of all clopen subsets of its Stone space $S(B)$ (the space of ultrafilters on $B$).
\item[(b)] Every Boolean space $X$ is homeomorphic to the Stone space of its characteristic Boolean ring $S(\mathcal{R}(X))$.
\end{enumerate}
