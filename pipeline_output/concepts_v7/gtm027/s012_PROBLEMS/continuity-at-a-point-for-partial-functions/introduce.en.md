This result characterizes continuity at a point for a function defined on a subset of a topological space with values in a Hausdorff space. The first characterization uses neighborhoods and the inverse image condition, while the second provides an equivalent net-based formulation: continuity at a point requires that all nets converging to the point are mapped to nets converging to the same value. The Hausdorff condition on the codomain is essential for uniqueness of the limit.
