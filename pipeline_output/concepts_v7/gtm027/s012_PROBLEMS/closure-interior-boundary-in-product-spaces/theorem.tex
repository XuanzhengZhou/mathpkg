Let $X$ and $Y$ be topological spaces and let $X \times Y$ carry the product topology. For subsets $A \subseteq X$ and $B \subseteq Y$, denote by $A^{-}$, $A^{0}$, and $A^{b}$ the closure, interior, and boundary of $A$, respectively. Then:
\begin{align*}
\text{(a)} &\quad (A \times B)^{-} = A^{-} \times B^{-}, \\[4pt]
\text{(b)} &\quad (A \times B)^{0} = A^{0} \times B^{0}, \\[4pt]
\text{(c)} &\quad (A \times B)^{b} = (A \times B)^{-} \setminus (A \times B)^{0}
= \bigl( (A^{b} \cup A^{0}) \times (B^{b} \cup B^{0}) \bigr) \setminus (A^{0} \times B^{0}).
\end{align*}
