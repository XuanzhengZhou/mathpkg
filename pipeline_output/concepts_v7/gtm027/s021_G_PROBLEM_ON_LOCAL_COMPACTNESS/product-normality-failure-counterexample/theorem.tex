The product of a locally compact, normal Hausdorff space and a compact Hausdorff space may fail to be normal. A counterexample is given by the product $\Omega' \times \Omega_0$, where $\Omega'$ is the space of all ordinals less than or equal to the first uncountable ordinal $\Omega$ with the order topology (compact Hausdorff), and $\Omega_0$ is the space of ordinals less than $\Omega$ (locally compact, normal Hausdorff).
