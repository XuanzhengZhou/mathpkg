Let $c$ be a closure operator on a set $X$ (satisfying the four Kuratowski axioms). Let $\mathfrak{F}$ be the family of all subsets $A \subset X$ such that $A^{c} = A$, and let $\mathfrak{J}$ be the family of complements of members of $\mathfrak{F}$. Then $\mathfrak{J}$ is a topology for $X$, and for each subset $A \subset X$, $A^{c}$ is precisely the $\mathfrak{J}$-closure of $A$.
