Kuratowski's four axioms provide an elegant algebraic characterization of the closure operation in a topological space. Any operator satisfying these axioms on the power set of $X$ defines a unique topology whose closure operation is exactly the given operator. This is a foundational result: topology can be axiomatized without ever mentioning open sets, purely through the behavior of the closure operator.
