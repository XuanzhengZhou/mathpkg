The closure operator distills the entire topology into a single unary operation on the power set. Its algebraic properties—the Kuratowski axioms—completely characterize those operators that arise from a topology. This perspective allows one to define a topological space purely in terms of a closure operator satisfying four simple axioms, unifying the abstract and the concrete.
