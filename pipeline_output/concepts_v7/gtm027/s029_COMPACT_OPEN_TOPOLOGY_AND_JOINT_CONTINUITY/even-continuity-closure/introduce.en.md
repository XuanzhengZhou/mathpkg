Theorem 7.19 is the even-continuity analog of Theorem 7.14. It states that the pointwise closure of an evenly continuous family (of functions to a regular space) remains evenly continuous. Moreover, on the closure $F^{-}$, the pointwise topology is jointly continuous. The joint continuity follows directly from the reformulation of even continuity: $\{f : f(x) \in W\} \times V$ maps into $U$, and the set $\{f : f(x) \in W\}$ is $\wp$-open when $W$ is open.
