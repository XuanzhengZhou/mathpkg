Theorem 7.12 states that for a $k$-space $X$, the family of all continuous functions from $X$ to a complete uniform space is itself complete under the uniformity of uniform convergence on compacta. This follows from the definition of $k$-spaces (where continuity is determined by compact subsets) together with Theorem 7.10(d). The result is particularly useful because locally compact Hausdorff spaces and first countable Hausdorff spaces are $k$-spaces (Theorem 7.13).
