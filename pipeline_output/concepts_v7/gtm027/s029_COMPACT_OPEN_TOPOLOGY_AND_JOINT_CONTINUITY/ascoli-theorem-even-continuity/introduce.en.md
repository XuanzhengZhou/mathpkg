Theorem 7.21 is a purely topological Ascoli theorem that uses even continuity in place of equicontinuity, eliminating any reference to uniform spaces. The conditions are: closedness in $C$, pointwise compact closure, and even continuity. The proof uses Theorem 7.19 (pointwise closure preserves even continuity and makes the pointwise topology jointly continuous) together with Tychonoff's theorem. This version requires only topological (not uniform) hypotheses on $Y$: it must be regular Hausdorff.
