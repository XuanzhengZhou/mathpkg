The product of Hausdorff spaces is a Hausdorff space. That is, if each coordinate space $X_a$ ($a \in A$) is Hausdorff, then the product space $\bigwedge \{X_a : a \in A\}$ with the product topology is also Hausdorff.

Conversely, if a product space is Hausdorff, then each coordinate space is also Hausdorff.
