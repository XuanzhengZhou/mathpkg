Theorem 8 of Kelley's General Topology shows that if a continuous surjection is either open or closed, then the topology on the codomain is forced to be the quotient topology. This means the codomain topology is completely determined by the map and the domain topology. The proof relies on the fact that the quotient topology is the largest topology making $f$ continuous, while openness (or closedness) forces it to also be the smallest topology with this property.
