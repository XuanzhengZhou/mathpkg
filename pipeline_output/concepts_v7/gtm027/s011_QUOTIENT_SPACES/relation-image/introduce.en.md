For an equivalence relation $R$ on $X$, the image $R[A]$ of a subset $A$ is the union of all equivalence classes that intersect $A$. This is also called the $R$-saturation of $A$. The set $R[x]$, the image of the singleton $\{x\}$, is precisely the equivalence class of $x$. These notations are fundamental for expressing when the projection $X \to X/R$ is an open or closed map.
