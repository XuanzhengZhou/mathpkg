Let $X$ be a topological space, $R$ an equivalence relation on $X$, and $X/R$ the quotient space.
\begin{enumerate}
  \item If $X/R$ is Hausdorff, then $R$ is a closed subset of the product space $X \times X$.
  \item If the natural projection $P: X \to X/R$ is open and $R$ is closed in $X \times X$, then $X/R$ is Hausdorff.
\end{enumerate}
