Let $P$ be the projection of a topological space $X$ onto the quotient space $X/R$. The following statements are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
  \item[(a)] $P$ is an open mapping.
  \item[(b)] If $A$ is an open subset of $X$, then $R[A]$ is open in $X$.
  \item[(c)] If $A$ is a closed subset of $X$, then the union of all members of $X/R$ which are subsets of $A$ is closed in $X$.
\end{itemize}
If ``open'' and ``closed'' are interchanged in (a), (b), and (c), the resulting statements are equivalent.
