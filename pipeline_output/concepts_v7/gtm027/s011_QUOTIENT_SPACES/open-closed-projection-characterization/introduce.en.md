Theorem 10 provides characterizations of when the natural projection $P: X \to X/R$ is an open or closed map. For openness, the key condition is that the $R$-saturation $R[A]$ of every open set $A$ is open. For closedness, the condition is that the union of all equivalence classes contained in a closed set is itself closed. These formulations are essential for verifying openness or closedness of quotient maps in practice.
