Let $X$ be a topological space and $R$ an equivalence relation on $X$. Let $X/R$ be the family of all equivalence classes and $P: X \to X/R$ the natural projection $P(x) = R[x]$. The \text{quotient space} is the set $X/R$ endowed with the quotient topology relative to $P$. Thus $\alpha \subseteq X/R$ is open (respectively closed) if and only if
$$\bigcup \{\, A : A \in \alpha \,\}$$
is open (respectively closed) in $X$.
