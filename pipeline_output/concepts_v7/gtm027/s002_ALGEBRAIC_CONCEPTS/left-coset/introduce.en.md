A left coset of a subgroup $H$ in a group $G$ is the set $xH$ obtained by multiplying a fixed element $x$ on the left by every element of $H$. The collection of all left cosets forms a partition of $G$ and is denoted $G/H$. When $H$ is a normal subgroup, $G/H$ becomes a group called the quotient group.
