A \textbf{ring} is a triple $(R, +, \cdot)$ such that $(R, +)$ is an abelian group and $\cdot$ is a function on $R \times R$ to $R$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
\item the operation $\cdot$ is associative;
\item the distributive laws hold:
$$u \cdot (x + y) = u \cdot x + u \cdot y \quad \text{and} \quad (u + v) \cdot x = u \cdot x + v \cdot x$$
for all members $x, y, u, v$ of $R$.
\end{enumerate}
