The projection (or quotient map) is the canonical surjective homomorphism $\pi \colon G \to G/H$ sending each element $x$ to its coset $xH$. It is the universal example of a homomorphism whose kernel contains $H$: any homomorphism whose kernel contains $H$ factors uniquely through the projection.
