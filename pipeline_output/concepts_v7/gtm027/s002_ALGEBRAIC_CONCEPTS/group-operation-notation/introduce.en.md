The group operation $\cdot$ is written in infix notation $x \cdot y$ rather than the usual function notation $\cdot(x,y)$. When no ambiguity arises, the dot may be omitted and the operation indicated by simple juxtaposition $xy$. This notational convention aligns with the standard practice in abstract algebra.
