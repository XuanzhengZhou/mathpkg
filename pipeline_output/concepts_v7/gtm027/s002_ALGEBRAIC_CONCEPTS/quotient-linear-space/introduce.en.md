The quotient space $X/Y$ of a linear space $X$ by a subspace $Y$ has as elements the additive cosets $x + Y$. Addition and scalar multiplication are defined via representatives, making the natural projection $\pi \colon X \to X/Y$ a linear map. Quotient spaces are essential in functional analysis for constructing normed spaces and studying the first isomorphism theorem.
