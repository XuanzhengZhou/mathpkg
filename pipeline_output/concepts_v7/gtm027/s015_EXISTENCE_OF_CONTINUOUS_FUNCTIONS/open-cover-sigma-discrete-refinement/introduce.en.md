This theorem completes the proof of the Bing-Nagata-Smirnov metrization theorem by establishing that metrizable spaces have $\sigma$-discrete bases (take $\sigma$-discrete refinements of covers by $1/n$-spheres). The proof uses a well-ordering and "concentric disk" decomposition. This result is also the starting point of Stone's proof that every metrizable space is paracompact.
