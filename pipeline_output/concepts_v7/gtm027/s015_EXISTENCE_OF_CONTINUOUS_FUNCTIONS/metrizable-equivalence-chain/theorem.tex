For a topological space $X$, the following are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $X$ is a regular $T_1$-space with a countable base.
\item[(b)] $X$ is homeomorphic to a subspace of $[0,1]^\omega$ (hence metrizable and second countable).
\item[(c)] $X$ is a separable metrizable space.
\end{enumerate}
