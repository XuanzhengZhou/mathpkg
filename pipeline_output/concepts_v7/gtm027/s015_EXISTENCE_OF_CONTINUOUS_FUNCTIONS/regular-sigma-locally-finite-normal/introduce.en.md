This lemma generalizes Tychonoff's lemma (regular Lindelof implies normal) by replacing the Lindelof condition with the existence of a $\sigma$-locally finite base. The proof decomposes covers of disjoint closed sets into locally finite families and constructs separating open sets by induction. This is a crucial step in the general metrization theorem.
