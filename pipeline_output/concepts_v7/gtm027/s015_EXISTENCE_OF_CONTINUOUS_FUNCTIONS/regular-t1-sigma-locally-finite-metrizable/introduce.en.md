This lemma proves the sufficiency direction of the Bing-Nagata-Smirnov theorem. The proof constructs a countable family of continuous pseudo-metrics from the $\sigma$-locally finite base that distinguish points and closed sets, then applies the Embedding Lemma and the countable product metric construction to obtain a metric for the space.
