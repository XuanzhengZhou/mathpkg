Tychonoff's lemma states that every regular Lindelöf space is normal. This result connects two separation properties: the point-set separation of regularity with the set-set separation of normality, under the countability condition of the Lindelöf property. It is a key ingredient in the proof of Urysohn's metrization theorem.
