Given a topological group $G$ and any subgroup $H$, the set $G/H$ of left cosets $x \cdot H$ inherits the quotient topology from the natural projection $\pi: G \to G/H$. The resulting space is always a homogeneous space, meaning that for any two cosets $A, B \in G/H$, there exists a homeomorphism of $G/H$ sending $A$ to $B$. This follows from the left translation action of $G$ on the coset space.
