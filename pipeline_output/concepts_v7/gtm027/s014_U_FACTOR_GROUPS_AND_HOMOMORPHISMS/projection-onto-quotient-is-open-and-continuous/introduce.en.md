The natural projection $\pi: G \to G/H$ from a topological group onto the coset space is always continuous by definition of the quotient topology. The nontrivial part is that $\pi$ is also an open map: the image of any open set $U \subseteq G$ under $\pi$ is open in $G/H$. This follows from the observation that the union of all left cosets intersecting an open set $U$ equals $U \cdot H$, which is open in $G$ since right translation by elements of $H$ is a homeomorphism.
