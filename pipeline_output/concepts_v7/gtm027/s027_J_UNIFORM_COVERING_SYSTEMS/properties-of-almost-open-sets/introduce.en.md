The collection of almost open sets is closed under countable unions and complements, hence forms a $\sigmaehBalgebra. Moreover, this $\sigmaehBalgebra contains all Borel sets. This means that modulo meager sets, every Borel set is indistinguishable from an open set—a powerful fact used in the proof of the Banach-Kuratowski-Pettis theorem and other category-based arguments in topological groups.
