This is the closed graph theorem in the setting of topological groups. If the target group $ is metrizable and right-complete, any homomorphism : G \to H$ with a closed graph satisfying a mild condition on preimages of identity neighborhoods must be continuous. The proof applies Lemma 6.36 and uses a right-invariant metric on $. This generalizes the classical Banach closed graph theorem for linear operators.
