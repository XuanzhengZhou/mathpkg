When the target group $ is Lindelöf and the domain $ is non-meager, the technical condition (ii) on preimages of identity neighborhoods holds automatically. For linear maps between linear topological spaces, surjectivity of $ and non-meagerness of $ suffice for both conditions (ii) and (ii)*. This reveals the classical Banach closed graph and open mapping theorems as special cases of these more general group-theoretic results.
