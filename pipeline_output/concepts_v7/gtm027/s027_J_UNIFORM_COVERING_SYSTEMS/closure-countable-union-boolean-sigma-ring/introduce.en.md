In a Boolean $\sigmaehBring represented as compact open subsets of a locally compact Boolean (Stone) space, the closure of any countable union of members remains compact and open. This property is characteristic of $\sigmaehBrings and reflects the fact that countable operations in the algebraic sense correspond to topological operations on the Stone space that preserve compact openness.
