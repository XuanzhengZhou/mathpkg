When $ is a closed entourage and $ is compact, the uniform neighborhood [A]$ is compact provided that the double-relational image  \circ U[x]$ is compact for each point of $. The proof uses the result from 6.A that [A]$ is closed. This theorem provides conditions under which the uniform thickening of a compact set remains compact.
