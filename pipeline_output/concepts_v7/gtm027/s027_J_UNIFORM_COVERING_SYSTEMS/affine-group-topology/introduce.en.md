The affine group  = \{x \mapsto ax + b : a \neq 0\}$ of the real line, under composition, becomes a topological group when topologized by proximity of the parameters $ to $. This concrete example illustrates the natural topological group structure on a familiar transformation group and serves as a test case for the properties of left, right, and two-sided uniformities.
