The product directed set construction endows the Cartesian product of directed sets with a natural directed order: the product order, where one tuple follows another if each coordinate follows the corresponding coordinate. This construction works for both finite products and arbitrary families of directed sets. Product directed sets are essential for studying iterated limits, where one needs a single directed index that simultaneously refines the indices of multiple limiting processes.
