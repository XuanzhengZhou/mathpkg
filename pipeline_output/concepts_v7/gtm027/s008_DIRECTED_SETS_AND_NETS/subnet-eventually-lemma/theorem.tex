\textbf{Lemma 5.} Let $S = \{S_n, n \in D, \geq\}$ be a net and let $\alpha$ be a family of sets such that $S$ is frequently in each member of $\alpha$, and such that the intersection of any two members of $\alpha$ contains a member of $\alpha$. Then there exists a subnet of $S$ which is eventually in each member of $\alpha$.
