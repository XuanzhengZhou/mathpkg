Symmetry means the relation goes both ways: if $x$ relates to $y$, then $y$ relates to $x$. The algebraic characterization $R = R^{-1}$ is both elegant and practical. Symmetry is the second property of equivalence relations. In topology, the relation "is homeomorphic to" is symmetric, while "is a subspace of" is not.
