The \text{domain} of a relation $R$ is the set of all first coordinates of members of $R$, and its \text{range} is the set of all second coordinates:
\begin{align*}
\operatorname{domain} R &= \{x: \exists y,\; (x,y) \in R\}, \\
\operatorname{range} R &= \{y: \exists x,\; (x,y) \in R\}.
\end{align*}
