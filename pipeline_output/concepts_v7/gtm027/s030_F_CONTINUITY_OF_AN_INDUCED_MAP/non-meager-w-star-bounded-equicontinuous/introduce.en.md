This is a formulation of the Banach-Steinhaus theorem (uniform boundedness principle) in the language of Kelley's text. For a non-meager normed space $X$ (which includes all complete metric spaces by the Baire category theorem), every weak-star bounded family of functionals is automatically equicontinuous, hence norm bounded. The hypothesis that $X$ is non-meager is essential.
