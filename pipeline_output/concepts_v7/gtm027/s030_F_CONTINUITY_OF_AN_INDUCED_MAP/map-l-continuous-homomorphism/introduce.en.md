The map $L: G \to \alpha[G]$ sending each group element to its left translation operator on the space of almost periodic functions is a continuous group homomorphism. Moreover, the initial topology on $G$ induced by $L$ coincides with the topology generated by the almost periodic functions themselves. The space $\alpha[G]$ can be described as the completion of $G$ modulo the subgroup of elements indistinguishable by almost periodic functions.
