A subset $F$ of the dual space $X^*$ is weak-star bounded if, for every fixed point $x \in X$, the set of values $\{f(x) : f \in F\}$ is a bounded set of scalars. This is the natural notion of pointwise boundedness for families of linear functionals, corresponding to boundedness in the weak-star topology.
