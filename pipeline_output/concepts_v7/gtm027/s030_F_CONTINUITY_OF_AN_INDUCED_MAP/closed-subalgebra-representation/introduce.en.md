Every closed unital subalgebra $R$ of $C(X)$ can be represented as the image of $C(Y)$ under an induced isomorphism $F^*$, where $Y$ is a compact Hausdorff space obtained as the range of the evaluation map. This structural theorem shows that closed unital subalgebras of $C(X)$ are precisely the algebras of the form $F^*(C(Y))$ for some continuous surjection $F$ from $X$ onto another compact Hausdorff space $Y$.
