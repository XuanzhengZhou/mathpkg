On the compactification $\alpha[G]$ constructed from left almost periodic functions, the topology inherited from pointwise convergence on $A$ and the product topology from $A^A$ coincide. Consequently, convergence in $\alpha[G]$ is characterized simply by pointwise convergence of the operators applied to all almost periodic functions at all points of the group.
