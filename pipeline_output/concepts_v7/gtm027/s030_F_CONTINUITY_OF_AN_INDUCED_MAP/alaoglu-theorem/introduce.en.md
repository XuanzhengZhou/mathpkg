Alaoglu's theorem asserts that the closed unit ball of the dual space $X^*$ is compact in the weak-star topology. The proof uses Tychonoff's product theorem by embedding the unit sphere as a closed subset of the product $\prod_{x \in X} [-\|x\|, \|x\|]$. An immediate consequence is that every norm-bounded and weak-star closed subset of $X^*$ is weak-star compact.
