Let $c$ be a closure operator on $X$ satisfying the Kuratowski axioms, let $\mathfrak{F}$ be the family of all subsets $A$ of $X$ for which $A^{c} = A$, and let $\mathfrak{J}$ be the family of complements of members of $\mathfrak{F}$. Then $\mathfrak{J}$ is a topology for $X$, and $A^{c}$ is the $\mathfrak{J}$-closure of $A$ for each subset $A$ of $X$.
