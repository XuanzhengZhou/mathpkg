The \textbf{usual topology} for the real numbers is the family of all those sets which contain an open interval about each of their points. That is, a subset $A$ of the set of real numbers is open iff for each member $x$ of $A$ there are numbers $a$ and $b$ such that $a < x < b$ and the open interval 
$$\{y : a < y < b\}$$
is a subset of $A$.
