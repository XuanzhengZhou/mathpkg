Let $E$ be an inner product space. For each vector $y \in E$, define a linear form $y'$ on $E$ by
$$y'(x) = (x \mid y) \qquad (x \in E),$$
where $(\cdot \mid \cdot)$ denotes the inner product of $E$. By the Cauchy--Schwarz inequality,
$$|y'(x)| = |(x \mid y)| \leq \|x\| \, \|y\|,$$
hence $y' \in E'$ and $\|y'\| \leq \|y\|$.
