This theorem generalizes the best approximation result for Hilbert spaces: the ambient space need not be complete, only the convex subset must be complete. The proof is identical in structure to the Hilbert space case, using the parallelogram law to show that a minimizing sequence is Cauchy, whose limit in the complete convex set gives the unique minimizer.
