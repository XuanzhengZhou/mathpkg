A $\sigma$-analytic function, being a uniform limit of rational functions with poles off $\sigma$, is necessarily analytic (holomorphic) at every interior point of $\sigma$. This connects the abstract functional-analytic notion of $\sigma$-analyticity with classical complex analysis on the interior of the spectral set.
