The notation $f(a)$ denotes the image of a continuous function $f$ under the continuous functional calculus for a normal element $a$. For polynomials in $z$ and $\bar{z}$, the calculus reduces to the natural polynomial expression in $a$ and $a^*$. For general continuous functions, $f(a)$ is defined by uniform approximation via the Weierstrass-Stone theorem.
