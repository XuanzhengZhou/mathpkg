Let $A$ and $B$ be $C^*$-algebras with unity, let $\varphi: A \to B$ be a unital $*$-homomorphism, and let $a \in A$ be normal. Then for every continuous function $f$ on $\sigma(a)$,
$$\sigma(\varphi(f(a))) = f(\sigma_A(a)).$$
In particular, taking $\varphi$ the identity, $\sigma(f(a)) = f(\sigma(a))$.
