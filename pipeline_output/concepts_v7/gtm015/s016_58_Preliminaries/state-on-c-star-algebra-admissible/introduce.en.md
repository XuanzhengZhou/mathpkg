For a unital $C^*$-algebra, the positivity and the $C^*$-norm condition automatically imply that every state satisfies the admissibility condition required for the GNS construction. This fact ensures that the GNS correspondence between states and cyclic representations is universally valid in the $C^*$-algebraic setting.
