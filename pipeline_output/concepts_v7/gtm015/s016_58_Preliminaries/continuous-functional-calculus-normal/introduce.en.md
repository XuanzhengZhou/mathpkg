The continuous functional calculus for a normal element $a$ of a $C^*$-algebra provides an isometric $*$-isomorphism from the algebra of continuous functions on the spectrum $\sigma(a)$ onto the $C^*$-subalgebra generated by $a$ and the identity. This is the $C^*$-algebraic formulation of the spectral theorem for normal operators.
