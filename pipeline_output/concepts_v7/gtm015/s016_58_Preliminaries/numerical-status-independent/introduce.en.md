The numerical status $\Sigma(a)$ of a self-adjoint element in a $C^*$-algebra (the set of values taken by states) is independent of the containing $C^*$-algebra. This "permanence of numerical status" implies that properties like positivity and spectral radius are intrinsic to the element and do not depend on the ambient algebra.
