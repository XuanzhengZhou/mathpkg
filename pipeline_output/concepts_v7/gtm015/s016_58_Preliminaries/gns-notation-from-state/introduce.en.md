The GNS (Gel'fand-Naimark-Segal) construction associates to each state $f$ on a $C^*$-algebra a Hilbert space $H_f$, a distinguished vector $x_f$, and a $*$-representation $\varphi_f$. The notation emphasizes the dependence on the state: $N_f$ is the left ideal of vectors annihilated by $f$, and $A_f = A/N_f$ carries an inner product induced by $f$.
