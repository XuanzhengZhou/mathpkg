If $f$ is a state on a $*$-algebra $A$ and $\varphi = \varphi_f$ is the adjunctive PSF on $A$ derived from $f$, the notations $N_\varphi, x_\varphi, A_\varphi, H_\varphi$ are replaced by $N_f, x_f, A_f, H_f$. Thus
$$N_f = \{x \in A : f(x^*x) = 0\},$$
$A_f = A/N_f$ with inner product induced by $f$, and $H_f$ is the Hilbert space completion of $A_f$.
