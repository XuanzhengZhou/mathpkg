This elementary lemma establishes the scaling relation $B_{\varepsilon} = \varepsilon B_1$ for seminorm balls. The equivalence chain $x \in B_{\varepsilon} \iff p(x) \leq \varepsilon \iff p(\varepsilon^{-1}x) \leq 1 \iff \varepsilon^{-1}x \in B_1 \iff x \in \varepsilon B_1$ makes the proof immediate. This scaling property is essential for verifying that the family $(B_{\varepsilon})$ forms a fundamental system of neighborhoods of zero for the locally convex topology generated by a seminorm.
