If $E$ is a vector space over $\mathbb{K}$ and $p, q$ are seminorms on $E$, then the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $p$ is continuous for the topology $\tau_q$;
\item[(b)] $\tau_p \subset \tau_q$;
\item[(c)] there exists a constant $M > 0$ such that $p(x) \leq M q(x)$ for all $x \in E$.
\end{enumerate}
