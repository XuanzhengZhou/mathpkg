This exercise extends the equivalence of closedness characterizations to closures: for any linear subspace $M$ (not necessarily closed), its closure in the original topology $\tau$ equals its closure in the weak topology $\sigma(E,E')$, and both equal the double annihilator $^\perp(M^\perp)$. This is a straightforward consequence of Theorem 38.14(vi) applied to the subspace $M$.
