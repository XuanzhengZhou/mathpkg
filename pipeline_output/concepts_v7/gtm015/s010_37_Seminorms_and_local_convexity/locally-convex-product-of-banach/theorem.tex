A separated TVS $E$ is locally convex if and only if it is linearly homeomorphic with a subspace of the product of a family of Banach spaces; i.e., there exists a family of Banach spaces $(F_i)_{i \in I}$ and an injective linear mapping $T: E \to \prod_{i \in I} F_i$ such that $T$ maps $E$ homeomorphically onto $T(E)$ (equipped with the relative topology).
