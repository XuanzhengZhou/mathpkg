This structure theorem reveals that separated locally convex spaces are precisely the subspaces of products of Banach spaces. The construction proceeds by taking for each continuous seminorm $p$ the quotient $E/N_p$ (where $N_p = \{x : p(x) = 0\}$), completing it to a Banach space, and embedding $E$ into the product of these Banach spaces via the natural quotient mappings. This is one of the deepest structural results about locally convex spaces, showing that Banach spaces are the "building blocks" of all separated locally convex spaces.
