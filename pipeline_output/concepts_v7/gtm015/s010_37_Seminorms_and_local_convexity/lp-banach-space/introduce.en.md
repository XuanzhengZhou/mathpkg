The $L^p$ spaces are complete normed spaces (Banach spaces) for all $p \geq 1$. The completeness proof generalizes the classical Riesz-Fischer theorem: every Cauchy sequence in $L^p$ converges in norm to an $L^p$ function. This result is fundamental to modern analysis and the theory of partial differential equations.
