This theorem collects the fundamental algebraic and topological properties of the annihilator operation in a dual pair. Properties (i)-(iii) show that $S \mapsto S^\perp$ and $T \mapsto {}^\perp T$ form a Galois connection. Property (vi) characterizes the double annihilator as the weakly closed linear span, which is the key to relating closure operations in the original topology to those in the weak topology.
