Let $(E, \tau)$ be a locally convex, separated TVS over $\mathbb{K}$, and consider the canonical duality between $E$ and $E'$. If $M$ is a linear subspace of $E$, the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $M$ is $\tau$-closed;
\item[(b)] $M$ is $\sigma(E, E')$-closed (weakly closed);
\item[(c)] $M = {}^\perp(M^\perp)$.
\end{enumerate}
