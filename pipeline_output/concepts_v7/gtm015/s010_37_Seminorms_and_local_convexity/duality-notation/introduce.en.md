This notation is a convenient shorthand for partial evaluation of the bilinear form defining a duality. For fixed $x \in E$, the map $B_x: F \to \mathbb{K}$ is the linear functional obtained by "plugging $x$ into the first slot"; similarly, $B^y: E \to \mathbb{K}$ is the linear functional from fixing $y$ in the second slot. These maps provide the natural embeddings of $E$ into the algebraic dual of $F$ and vice versa.
