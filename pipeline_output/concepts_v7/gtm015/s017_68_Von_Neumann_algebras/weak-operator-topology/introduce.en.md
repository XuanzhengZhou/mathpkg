The weak operator topology is the coarsest topology on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ making all matrix coefficient functionals $T \mapsto (Tx \mid y)$ continuous. It is weaker than the strong operator topology. A basic neighborhood of $0$ is $\{T : |(Tx_i \mid y_i)| \leq 1,\; i=1,\ldots,n\}$ for vectors $x_i, y_i \in H$.
