\begin{definition}
Let $G$ be a locally compact group with Haar measure. If $f$ and $g$ are Baire functions on $G$, the set of all $s \in G$ such that the function
$$t \mapsto f(st)g(t^{-1}) \quad (t \in G)$$
is integrable is the complement of a Baire set. For all such $s$, the \text{convolution} of $f$ and $g$ is defined by
$$(f * g)(s) = \int f(st)g(t^{-1})\, dt = \int f(t)g(t^{-1}s)\, dt.$$
\end{definition}
