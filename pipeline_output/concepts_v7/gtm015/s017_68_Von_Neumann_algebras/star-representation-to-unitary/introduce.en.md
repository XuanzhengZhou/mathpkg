This theorem constructs a continuous unitary representation of $G$ from a nondegenerate $*$-representation of the group algebra $L^1(G)$. The unitary operators $U_t$ arise as strong limits of the translated approximate identity. This is one half of the fundamental correspondence between unitary representations of $G$ and $*$-representations of $L^1(G)$.
