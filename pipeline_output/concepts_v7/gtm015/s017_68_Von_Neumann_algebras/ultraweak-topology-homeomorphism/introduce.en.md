This proposition shows that the ultraweak topology on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ can be realized concretely as the weak operator topology on the amplified operators acting on the countably infinite direct sum $K$. This perspective unifies the treatment and connects ultraweak continuity on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ to ordinary weak continuity on $\mathcal{L}(K)$.
