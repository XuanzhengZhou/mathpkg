The adjoint (or involution) on $L^1(G)$ is defined using the modular function $\Delta$ and the pointwise complex conjugate. It makes $L^1(G)$ into a Banach $*$-algebra, which is the foundation for connecting $*$-representations of $L^1(G)$ with unitary representations of the group $G$. The adjoint satisfies $[f * g]^{\sim} = \tilde{g} * \tilde{f}$.
