This lemma establishes a matricial criterion for an operator $P$ on the countably infinite direct sum $K$ to commute with the amplification $\tilde{T}$ of $T$. It is a key technical tool in the proof of von Neumann's bicommutant theorem, which states that the bicommutant coincides with the ultrastrong closure.
