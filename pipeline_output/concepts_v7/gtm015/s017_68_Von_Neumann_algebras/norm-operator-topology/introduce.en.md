The norm topology on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ is the metric topology induced by the operator norm. It is the strongest among the standard locally convex topologies considered on $\mathcal{L}(H)$ in the theory of von Neumann algebras. Every von Neumann algebra is norm-closed, hence a $C^*$-algebra.
