This lemma establishes the automatic continuity of algebra homomorphisms from a Banach algebra to the complex field. The proof passes through the unitification of $A$ and uses the fact that the kernel is a maximal ideal, hence closed. This result is fundamental to Gel'fand theory, where characters (nonzero algebra homomorphisms to $\mathbb{C}$) are automatically continuous.
