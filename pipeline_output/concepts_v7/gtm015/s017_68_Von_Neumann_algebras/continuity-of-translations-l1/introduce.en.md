This lemma states that the left and right regular actions of a locally compact group $G$ on $L^1(G)$ are strongly continuous. It is a cornerstone for the theory of $L^1(G)$ as a Banach $*$-algebra, used in constructing approximate identities and in proving the correspondence between $*$-representations of $L^1(G)$ and unitary representations of $G$.
