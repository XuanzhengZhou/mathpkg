This proposition reveals the deep connection between Gel'fand theory and Fourier analysis: the Gel'fand transform of $L^1(G)$ is precisely the Fourier transform on the dual group. This unifies commutative Banach algebra theory with classical harmonic analysis and lays the foundation for the Pontryagin duality theorem for locally compact abelian groups.
