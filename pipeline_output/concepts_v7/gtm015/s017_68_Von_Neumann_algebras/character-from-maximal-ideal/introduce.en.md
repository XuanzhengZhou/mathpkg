For a locally compact abelian group, the Gel'fand theory of the commutative Banach algebra $L^1(G)$ identifies the maximal ideal space with the dual group (character group) of $G$. Each maximal ideal corresponds to a continuous group character, establishing the foundation for Pontryagin duality via the Gel'fand transform.
