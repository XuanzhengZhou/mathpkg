\begin{definition}
Let $G$ be an LCA group. For each maximal ideal $M \in \mathcal{M}$ of $L^1(G)$, let $\varphi_M: L^1(G) \to \mathbb{C}$ be the algebra epimorphism with kernel $M$. The \text{continuous character} $\alpha_M$ of $G$ corresponding to $M$ is the unique character satisfying the identity obtained from the correspondence between characters and multiplicative linear functionals on $L^1(G)$.
\end{definition}
