\begin{lemma}
Let $\mathcal{A}$ be the group $C^*$-algebra of $G$, let $\Phi: \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{L}(H)$ be a unital $*$-representation on a nonzero Hilbert space $H$, and let $\varphi(u) = \Phi(T_u)$ be the associated $*$-representation of $L^1(G)$. Then
$$\{\varphi(u) : u \in L^1(G)\}' = \{\Phi(A) : A \in \mathcal{A}\}'.$$
In particular, $\varphi$ is irreducible if and only if $\Phi$ is irreducible.
\end{lemma}
