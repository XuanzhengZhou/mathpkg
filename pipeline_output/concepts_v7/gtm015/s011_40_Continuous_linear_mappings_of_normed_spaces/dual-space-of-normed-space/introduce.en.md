The continuous dual (or topological dual) of a normed space E is the space of all continuous linear functionals from E to the scalar field, equipped with the operator norm. Unlike the algebraic dual, the continuous dual respects the topological structure and is itself a normed space. It plays a central role in duality theory and the Hahn-Banach theorem.
