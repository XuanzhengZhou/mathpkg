The transpose operation T ↦ T' is a linear isometry from L(E,F) into L(F',E'). It preserves the operator norm and extends the original operator to the bidual in the sense that T'' coincides with T on the canonically embedded image of E. Moreover, if T is an isometric surjection, the transpose inherits both properties.
