This lemma characterizes maximal $\mathbb{C}$-linear subspaces of a complex vector space as intersections of the form $M_0 \cap iM_0$, where $M_0$ is a maximal $\mathbb{R}$-linear subspace. Since $iM_0$ is also a maximal $\mathbb{R}$-linear subspace (by complex structure), a $\mathbb{C}$-maximal subspace is the intersection of two $\mathbb{R}$-maximal subspaces related by multiplication by $i$. This structural result is the key step in establishing the relationship between $\mathbb{C}$-hyperplanes and $\mathbb{R}$-hyperplanes.
