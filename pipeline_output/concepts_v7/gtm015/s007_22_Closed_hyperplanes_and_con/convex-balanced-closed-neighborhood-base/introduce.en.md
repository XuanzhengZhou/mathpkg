Every locally convex topological vector space admits a fundamental system of neighborhoods of the origin consisting of convex, balanced, and closed sets. The proof constructs such a neighborhood from an arbitrary one by successive refinements: taking a closed neighborhood, then a convex one inside it, then a balanced one inside that, and finally taking the closed convex balanced hull.
