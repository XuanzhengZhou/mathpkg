Let $E$ be a TVS over $\mathbb{C}$. The formulas
$$g(x) = \operatorname{Re} f(x) \quad (x \in E)$$
and
$$f(x) = g(x) - ig(ix) \quad (x \in E)$$
define mutually inverse bijective mappings $f \mapsto g$ and $g \mapsto f$ between the set of all continuous $\mathbb{C}$-forms $f$ and the set of all continuous $\mathbb{R}$-forms $g$.
