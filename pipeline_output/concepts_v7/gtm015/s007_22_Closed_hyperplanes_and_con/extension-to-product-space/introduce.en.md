A continuous linear map from a subspace of a locally convex space into a product of copies of the scalar field extends continuously to the whole space. The proof decomposes the map into its coordinate components (each a continuous linear form), extends each component via the Hahn-Banach theorem for locally convex spaces, and reassembles the extensions into a map into the product space.
