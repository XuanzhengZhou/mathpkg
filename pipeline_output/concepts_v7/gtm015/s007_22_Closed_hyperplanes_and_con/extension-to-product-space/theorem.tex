Suppose $E$ is a locally convex TVS over $\mathbb{K}$, $I$ is a nonempty set of indices, and $\mathbb{K}^I$ is the product TVS. If $M$ is a linear subspace of $E$ and if $u: M \rightarrow \mathbb{K}^I$ is a continuous linear mapping, then $u$ may be extended to a continuous linear mapping $\bar{u}: E \rightarrow \mathbb{K}^I$.
