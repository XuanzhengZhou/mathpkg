Let $E$ be a real vector space and let $H = \{x : g(x) = \alpha\}$ be a hyperplane determined by a nonzero linear form $g$ and a scalar $\alpha$. Define $S_1 = \{x : g(x) \leq \alpha\}$, $S_2 = \{x : g(x) \geq \alpha\}$, $S_3 = \{x : g(x) < \alpha\}$, $S_4 = \{x : g(x) > \alpha\}$. Then $S_1, S_2$ are the closed half-spaces and $S_3, S_4$ are the open half-spaces determined by $H$. Every half-space is convex. If $E$ is a real TVS and $g$ is continuous, then $S_1, S_2$ are closed and $S_3, S_4$ are open.
