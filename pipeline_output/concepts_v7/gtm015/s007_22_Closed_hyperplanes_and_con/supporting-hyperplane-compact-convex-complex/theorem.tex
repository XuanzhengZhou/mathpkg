Let $E$ be a locally convex, separated TVS over $\mathbb{C}$, and let $A$ be a nonempty compact convex subset of $E$. If $b \notin A$, then there exist a continuous linear form $f$ on $E$ and a real number $\alpha$, such that $\operatorname{Re} f \leq \alpha$ on $A$, $\operatorname{Re} f(a) = \alpha$ for at least one $a \in A$, and $\operatorname{Re} f(b) > \alpha$.
