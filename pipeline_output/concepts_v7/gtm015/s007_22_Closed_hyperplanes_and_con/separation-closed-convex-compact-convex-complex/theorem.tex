Let $E$ be a locally convex TVS over $\mathbb{C}$, let $A$ and $B$ be nonempty convex sets with $A \cap B = \varnothing$, and suppose that $A$ is closed and $B$ is compact. Then there exist a continuous linear form $f$ on $E$ and a real number $\alpha$, such that $\operatorname{Re} f < \alpha$ on $A$ and $\operatorname{Re} f > \alpha$ on $B$.
