A fundamental result in the theory of amenable semigroups: every abelian semigroup possesses a right-invariant mean. The proof combines the functional constructed in the preceding lemma with the classical Hahn-Banach theorem to produce a linear form satisfying both the translation-invariance and the infimum-supremum bounding conditions. This result has important applications to the existence of Banach generalized limits on bounded sequences.
