Let $E$ be a real vector space with hyperplane $H$ determined by $g$ and $\alpha$, and let $A$ be a subset of $E$. $A$ lies to one side of $H$ if $A \subset S_1$ or $A \subset S_2$; $A$ lies strictly to one side if $A \subset S_3$ or $A \subset S_4$. $H$ separates $A$ and $B$ if $A \subset S_1$ and $B \subset S_2$, or vice versa; $H$ separates $A$ and $B$ strictly if $A \subset S_3$ and $B \subset S_4$, or vice versa.
