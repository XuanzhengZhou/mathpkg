This theorem complements (20.3) and (20.6) by characterizing the topological direct sum purely in terms of quotient spaces: the canonical algebraic isomorphism from the supplement to the quotient is a homeomorphism exactly when the decomposition is topological. This perspective is especially useful when working with quotient constructions in functional analysis.
