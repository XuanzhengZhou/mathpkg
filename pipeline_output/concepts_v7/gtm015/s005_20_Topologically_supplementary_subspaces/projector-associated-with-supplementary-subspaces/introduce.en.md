Given a decomposition of a vector space into two supplementary subspaces, the projector onto one along the other is the linear idempotent map extracting the component in that subspace. The projector's range and kernel are exactly the two supplementary subspaces, establishing a bijective correspondence between direct sum decompositions and idempotent linear operators.
