In a topological group $G$, the following terminology is used for the homeomorphisms in Theorem (2.8):
\begin{itemize}
\item $x \mapsto x^{-1}$ is the inversion mapping;
\item $x \mapsto ax$ is left translation by $a$;
\item $x \mapsto xb$ is right translation by $b$;
\item $x \mapsto axa^{-1}$ is the inner automorphism of $G$ induced by $a$.
\end{itemize}
