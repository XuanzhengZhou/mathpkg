If $A$ is a subset of a topological group $G$ and if $U$ is an open subset of $G$, then the following subsets are also open:
$$U^{-1}, \quad AU, \quad UA.$$
Here $ST = \{st : s \in S, t \in T\}$ for subsets $S, T$ of $G$, with the convention that $ST = \varnothing$ if either $S = \varnothing$ or $T = \varnothing$, and $S^{-1} = \{s^{-1} : s \in S\}$.
