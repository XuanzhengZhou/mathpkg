This corollary follows directly from the fact that inversion and translations are homeomorphisms. It states that the inverse of an open set is open, and that the product $AU$ (and $UA$) of an arbitrary set $A$ with an open set $U$ is open, because it can be expressed as a union of translates of $U$. This result is fundamental for working with open neighborhoods in topological groups.
