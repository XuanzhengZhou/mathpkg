Milman's theorem is a celebrated result that bridges the geometric notion of uniform convexity with the topological notion of reflexivity. It implies that important classical spaces such as the $L^p$ spaces for $1 < p < \infty$ are reflexive, since they are uniformly convex. The theorem is attributed to D. P. Milman and marked with an asterisk in Berberian's text, indicating its depth.
