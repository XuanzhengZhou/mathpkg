Let $E$ be a normed space over $\mathbb{K}$, let $E''$ be its bidual, and let $x \mapsto x''$ be the canonical embedding of $E$ into $E''$. Denote $E_0 = \{x'' : x \in E\}$ the image of $E$ in $E''$. Then the closed unit ball of $E_0$ is weak* dense in the closed unit ball of $E''$; that is,
$$
\overline{\{x'' : x \in E,\ \|x\| \leq 1\}}^{\sigma(E'', E')} = \{\varphi \in E'' : \|\varphi\| \leq 1\}.
$$
