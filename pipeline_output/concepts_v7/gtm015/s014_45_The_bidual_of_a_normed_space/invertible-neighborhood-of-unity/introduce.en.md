In a Banach algebra with unity, any element within distance 1 of the unity is invertible. This follows immediately from the Neumann series: writing $x = 1 - z$ with $\|z\| < 1$, the inverse is $\sum z^n$. Thus the open unit ball centered at 1 consists entirely of invertible elements.
