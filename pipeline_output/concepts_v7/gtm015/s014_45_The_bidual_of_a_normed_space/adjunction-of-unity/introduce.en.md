This theorem shows that the algebraic unitification can be given a natural norm $\|x + \lambda u\| = \|x\| + |\lambda|$ that makes it into a normed algebra with unity of norm one. The original normed algebra embeds isometrically. This result allows one to reduce problems about normed algebras without unity to the case with unity, which is often technically simpler.
