This theorem shows that every normed algebra can be completed to a Banach algebra, just as every normed space can be completed to a Banach space. The proof first constructs the Banach space completion, then extends the multiplication by continuity using the fact that Cauchy sequences multiply to Cauchy sequences. This is a standard result that allows one to work with normed algebras by passing to their Banach algebra completions.
