Let $E$ be a Banach space and $(E_i)_{i \in I}$ a family of normed spaces over $\mathbb{K}$, and let $T_i : E \to E_i$ ($i \in I$) be a family of continuous linear mappings. If, for each $x \in E$, the family $(\|T_i x\|)_{i \in I}$ is bounded, then $\|T_i\|$ is bounded (i.e., $\sup_{i \in I} \|T_i\| < \infty$).
