For a topological space $X$, the following conditions are equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] The complement of every meager subset of $X$ is dense in $X$;
\item[(b)] every nonempty open subset of $X$ is nonmeager;
\item[(c)] if $(A_n)$ is a sequence of closed subsets of $X$ such that $\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} A_n$ has nonempty interior, then at least one $A_n$ has nonempty interior.
\end{enumerate}
