Let $E$ be an additively written group and let $f \colon E \times E \to \mathbb{K}$ (where $\mathbb{K} = \mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$) be a function satisfying the following conditions:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $f(x, x) \geq 0$ for all $x \in E$,
\item[(ii)] $f(x + y, z) = f(x, z) + f(y, z)$ for all $x, y, z \in E$,
\item[(iii)] $f(x, y + z) = f(x, y) + f(x, z)$ for all $x, y, z \in E$.
\end{enumerate}

Then the function $N(x) = f(x, x)^{1/2}$ satisfies the parallelogram law

$$N(x + y)^2 + N(x - y)^2 = 2N(x)^2 + 2N(y)^2$$

for all $x, y \in E$.
