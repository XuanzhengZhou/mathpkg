This proposition distills the content of Theorems 41.5 and 41.6 into a single assertion: the norm-based definition of pre-Hilbert space (via the parallelogram law) automatically yields an inner product structure via the polarization formulas. Thus the "pre-Hilbert space" perspective (norm first, then inner product) is fully compatible with the "inner product space" perspective (inner product first, then norm). This is one half of the equivalence between the two notions.
