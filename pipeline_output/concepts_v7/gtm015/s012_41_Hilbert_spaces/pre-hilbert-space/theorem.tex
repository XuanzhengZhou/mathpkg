Suppose $E$ is a normed space and $\hat{E}$ is its completion. The norm of $E$ satisfies the parallelogram law

$$\|x + y\|^2 + \|x - y\|^2 = 2\|x\|^2 + 2\|y\|^2$$

if and only if the norm of $\hat{E}$ does. Thus, the norm of $E$ satisfies the parallelogram law if and only if its completion is a Hilbert space. A normed space whose norm satisfies the parallelogram law is called a \textbf{pre-Hilbert space}.
