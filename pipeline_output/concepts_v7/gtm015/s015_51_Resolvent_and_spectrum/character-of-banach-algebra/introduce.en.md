A character of a Gel'fand algebra is a nonzero algebra homomorphism into $\mathbb{C}$. Every character is automatically continuous with norm one, and its kernel is a maximal ideal. The set $\mathcal{X}$ of all characters, equipped with the weak* topology, is a compact space -- the character space -- which is homeomorphic to the maximal ideal space.
