Gel'fand's spectral radius formula expresses the spectral radius as the limit of $\|x^n\|^{1/n}$. The proof uses the analyticity of the resolvent function and power series: the series $\sum \mu^{n+1} x^n$ converges for $|\mu| < 1/r(x)$, and by uniqueness of analytic continuation, its radius of convergence is exactly $1/r(x)$. The formula also yields $\lim \|x^n\|^{1/n} = \inf \|x^n\|^{1/n}$.
