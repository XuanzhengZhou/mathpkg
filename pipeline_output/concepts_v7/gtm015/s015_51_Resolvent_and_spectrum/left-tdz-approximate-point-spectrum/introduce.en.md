An operator $T$ is a left topological divisor of zero precisely when $0$ belongs to its approximate point spectrum. More generally, $\lambda \in \sigma_{\text{ap}}(T)$ iff $T - \lambda I$ is a left TDZ. This bridges the algebraic notion of TDZ with the analytic notion of approximate eigenvalues.
