The approximate point spectrum of $T \in \mathcal{L}(E)$ is defined as
$$\sigma_{\text{ap}}(T) = \{\lambda \in \mathbb{C} : T - \lambda I \text{ is not bounded below}\}.$$
Equivalently, $\lambda \in \sigma_{\text{ap}}(T)$ iff there exists a sequence $x_n \in E$ with $\|x_n\| = 1$ such that $(T - \lambda I)x_n \to 0$.
