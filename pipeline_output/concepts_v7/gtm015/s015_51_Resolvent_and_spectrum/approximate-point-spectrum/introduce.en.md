The approximate point spectrum consists of $\lambda$ for which $T - \lambda I$ fails to be bounded below. Equivalently, there exists a sequence of unit vectors $(x_n)$ with $(T - \lambda I)x_n \to 0$, meaning $\lambda$ is an approximate eigenvalue. This spectrum is closely tied to topological divisors of zero in $\mathcal{L}(E)$.
