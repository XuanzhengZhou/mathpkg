The point spectrum of $T$ is $\sigma_p(T) = \{\lambda \in \mathbb{C} : T - \lambda I \text{ is not injective}\}$.

The compression spectrum of $T$ is $\sigma_{\text{com}}(T) = \{\lambda \in \mathbb{C} : (T - \lambda I)(E) \text{ is not dense in } E\}$.

Obviously $\sigma_p(T) \subset \sigma(T)$ and $\sigma_{\text{com}}(T) \subset \sigma(T)$.
