When $I$ is a closed proper ideal, the quotient $A/I$ inherits a Banach algebra structure with the quotient norm. The unity element is $1+I$ and it has norm one. The quotient map is a continuous algebra homomorphism of norm one. This lemma is essential for constructing the Gelfand representation.
