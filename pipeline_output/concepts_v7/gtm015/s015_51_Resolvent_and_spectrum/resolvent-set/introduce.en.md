The resolvent set $\rho(x)$ of an element $x$ in a Banach algebra $A$ collects all complex numbers $\lambda$ for which $\lambda 1 - x$ is invertible. It is the complement of the spectrum $\sigma(x)$. The study of $\rho(x)$ and the resolvent function $R(\lambda) = (\lambda 1 - x)^{-1}$ is central to spectral theory.
