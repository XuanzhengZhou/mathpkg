The character space $\mathcal{X}$ of a Gel'fand algebra is compact in the weak* topology (as a closed subset of the unit ball of the dual space, by Banach-Alaoglu). The bijection between maximal ideals and characters establishes a natural correspondence between the algebraic and topological viewpoints, which is the foundation of the Gelfand representation.
