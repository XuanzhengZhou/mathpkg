A fundamental property of self-adjoint operators on a Hilbert space is that their spectrum is real. For any non-real complex number $\alpha + i\beta$ with $\beta \neq 0$, the operator $T - (\alpha + i\beta)I$ is invertible. This follows from the invertibility of $T^2 + I$ when $T$ is self-adjoint.
