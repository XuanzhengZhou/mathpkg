The composition homomorphism $\Phi_g$ is the special case of the rational functional calculus (54.1) applied to the algebra $A = \mathbb{C}(t)$ with $x = g$. It sends a rational function $f$ to the composed rational function $f \circ g$. This algebraic formulation of composition is the key to proving the substitution formula $f(g(x)) = (f \circ g)(x)$.
