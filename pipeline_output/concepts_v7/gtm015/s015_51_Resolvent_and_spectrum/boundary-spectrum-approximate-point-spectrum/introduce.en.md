Every boundary point of the spectrum of an operator on a Banach space belongs to the approximate point spectrum. The proof uses that boundary points correspond to two-sided TDZs (56.4), and hence to left TDZs, which are exactly the approximate point spectrum. In particular, spectral points of maximum modulus are approximate eigenvalues.
