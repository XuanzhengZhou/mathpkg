For operators, right TDZs are characterized via duality: $T$ is a right TDZ iff its adjoint $T'$ is a left TDZ. The third condition characterizes it in terms of dual functionals converging uniformly to zero on the image of the unit ball. This duality relationship simplifies the analysis on reflexive spaces where left and right conditions become symmetric.
