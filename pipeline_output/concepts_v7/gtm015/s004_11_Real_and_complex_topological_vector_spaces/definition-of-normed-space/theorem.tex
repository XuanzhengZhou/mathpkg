A \textbf{normed space} (over $\mathbb{K}$) is a pair $(E, \|\cdot\|)$, where $E$ is a vector space over $\mathbb{K}$ and $x \mapsto \|x\|$ is a norm on $E$. The terms \textbf{real normed space} and \textbf{complex normed space} are used according as $\mathbb{K} = \mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{K} = \mathbb{C}$.
