If $S$ is a subset of a vector space $E$ over $\mathbb{K}$, then the balanced hull of $S$ is the set
$$\{\lambda x : x \in S, \lambda \in \mathbb{K}, |\lambda| \leq 1\}.$$
Writing $D = \{\lambda \in \mathbb{K} : |\lambda| \leq 1\}$ (a closed interval or a closed disc, according as $\mathbb{K} = \mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{K} = \mathbb{C}$), the balanced hull is $DS$.
