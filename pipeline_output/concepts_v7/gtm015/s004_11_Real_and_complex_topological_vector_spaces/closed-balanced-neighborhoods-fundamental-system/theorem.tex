Let $E$ be any TVS over $\mathbb{K}$ and let $\mathcal{B}$ be the class of all neighborhoods of $\theta$ that are closed and balanced. Then
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\mathcal{B}$ is a fundamental system of neighborhoods of $\theta$,
\item[(ii)] $\lambda V \in \mathcal{B}$ for all $V \in \mathcal{B}$, $\lambda \in \mathbb{K}$, $\lambda \neq 0$.
\end{enumerate}
