Let $E$ be a vector space over $\mathbb{K}$, $A \subset E$ a nonempty convex set, and $H$ an $\mathbb{R}$-hyperplane of $E$ tangent to $A$ (i.e., $H \cap A \neq \varnothing$ and $\exists$ an $\mathbb{R}$-linear form $g$ on $E$, $\alpha \in \mathbb{R}$ such that $H = \{g = \alpha\}$ and $g \leq \alpha$ on $A$). Then $H \cap A$ is a face of $A$.
