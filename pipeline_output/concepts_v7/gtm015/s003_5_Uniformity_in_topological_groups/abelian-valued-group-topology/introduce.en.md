For an abelian group, the distinction between left and right value topologies disappears because $|xy^{-1}| = |x^{-1}y|$. The resulting topology is compatible with the group operations and makes the group a metrizable topological group. This result is particularly important because most applications of valued groups (especially in functional analysis) involve abelian groups, where the left-right ambiguity is irrelevant.
