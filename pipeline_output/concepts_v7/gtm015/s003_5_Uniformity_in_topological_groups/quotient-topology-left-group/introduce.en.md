This lemma extends standard facts about quotient maps from topological groups to the more general setting of left topological groups (where only left translations are homeomorphisms). The key properties are that the quotient map $\pi: G \to H \setminus G$ is both continuous and open, and that neighborhoods in the quotient space have a simple description in terms of identity neighborhoods in the original group.
