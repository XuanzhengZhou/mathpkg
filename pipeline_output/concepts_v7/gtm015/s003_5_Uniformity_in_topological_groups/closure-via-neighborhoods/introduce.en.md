In a topological group, the closure of any subset $A$ can be expressed purely algebraically as the intersection of all translates $AV$ (or $VA$) where $V$ ranges over a fundamental system of identity neighborhoods. This result follows from the uniform space theory applied to the left (or right) uniformity of the group, and it highlights how the algebraic and topological structures intertwine.
