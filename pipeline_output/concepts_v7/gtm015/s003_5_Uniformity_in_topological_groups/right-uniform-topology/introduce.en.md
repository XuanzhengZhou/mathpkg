Just as the left uniform structure induces the original topology on a topological group, the right uniform structure does the same. The topology derived from the right uniformity $(G, \mathcal{U}_r)$ coincides with the given topology on $G$. This means both left and right uniformities are compatible with the group topology, even though they may be distinct as uniform structures.
