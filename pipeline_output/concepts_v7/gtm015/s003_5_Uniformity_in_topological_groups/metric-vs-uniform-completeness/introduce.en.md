For a metric space, the usual notion of completeness (every Cauchy sequence converges) is equivalent to the uniform notion of completeness (every Cauchy filter converges). This lemma bridges the sequential and filter-based definitions of completeness and is essential for transferring results between metric space theory and the theory of uniform spaces applied to topological groups.
