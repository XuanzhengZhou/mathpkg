For $x \in \ell^1(\mathbb{Z})$, the Fourier transform of $x$ is the function $\hat{x}: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{C}$ defined by
$$\hat{x}(t) = \sum_{n=-\infty}^{\infty} x(n) e^{int} \quad (t \in \mathbb{R}).$$
By Remark 1, $\hat{x}$ is a continuous function on $\mathbb{R}$, periodic of period $2\pi$, and the series converges uniformly and absolutely on $\mathbb{R}$.
