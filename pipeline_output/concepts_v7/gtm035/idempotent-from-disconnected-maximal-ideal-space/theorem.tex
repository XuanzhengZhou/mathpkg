% Idempotent From Disconnected Maximal Ideal Space
% From GTM035, Section s009
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

If the maximal ideal space $\mathcal{M}$ of a Banach algebra $\mathfrak{A}$ is disconnected, then $\mathfrak{A}$ contains a nontrivial idempotent.
