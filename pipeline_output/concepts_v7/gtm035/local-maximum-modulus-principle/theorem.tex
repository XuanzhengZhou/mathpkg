% Local Maximum Modulus Principle
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be a Banach algebra and fix $x \in \mathcal{M} \setminus \check{S}(\mathfrak{A})$. Let $U$ be a neighborhood of $x$ with $U \subset \mathcal{M} \setminus \check{S}(\mathfrak{A})$. Then for all $f \in \mathfrak{A}$,
$$|\hat{f}(x)| \leq \max_{\partial U} |\hat{f}|.$$
