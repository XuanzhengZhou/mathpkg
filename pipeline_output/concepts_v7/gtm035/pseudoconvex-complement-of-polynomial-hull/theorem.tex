% Pseudoconvex Complement Of Polynomial Hull
% From GTM035, Section s023
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Fix a compact set $Y$ in $\mathbb{C}^2$ and fix a point $p^0 \in \hat{Y} \setminus Y$. Let $B$ be an open ball in $\mathbb{C}^2$, centered at $p^0$, such that $\bar{B}$ does not meet $Y$. Then each connected component of $B \setminus \hat{Y}$ is pseudoconvex.
