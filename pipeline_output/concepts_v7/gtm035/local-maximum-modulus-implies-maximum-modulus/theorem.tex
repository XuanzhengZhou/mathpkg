% Local Maximum Modulus Implies Maximum Modulus
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

If $\mathfrak{A}$ is a uniform algebra whose maximal ideal space satisfies the local maximum modulus principle, then $\mathfrak{A}$ gives rise to a maximum modulus algebra structure over suitable domains.
