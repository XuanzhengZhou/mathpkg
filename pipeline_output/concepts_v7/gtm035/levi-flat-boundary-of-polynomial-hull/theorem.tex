% Levi Flat Boundary Of Polynomial Hull
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $Y$ be a compact set in $\mathbb{C}^2$ lying over the circle $\{|z_1| = 1\}$. Let $\Omega$ be the interior of $\hat{Y}$. Then $\partial \Omega$ is Levi-flat at each smooth point $z^0 = (z_1^0, z_2^0)$ with $|z_1^0| < 1$.
