% First Cohomology Limit Group
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

$H^1(X, \mathbb{Z})$ is the limit group of the direct system of groups $\{H^1(\mathcal{U}, \mathbb{Z}) \mid \mathcal{U}\}$ over all open covers $\mathcal{U}$ of $X$.
