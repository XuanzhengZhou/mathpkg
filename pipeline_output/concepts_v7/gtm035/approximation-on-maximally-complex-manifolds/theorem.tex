% Approximation On Maximally Complex Manifolds
% From GTM035, Section s017
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\Sigma$ be a $k$-dimensional sufficiently smooth submanifold of an open set in $\mathbb{C}^n$ with no complex tangents. Then functions on $\Sigma$ can be approximated by polynomials in the coordinates.
