% Type Three Zero Restriction
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\alpha$ be a form of type $(3,0)$ defined on a neighborhood of a maximally complex real $3$-manifold $M$ in $\mathbb{C}^3$. Then the restriction of $\alpha$ to $M$ is identically zero.
