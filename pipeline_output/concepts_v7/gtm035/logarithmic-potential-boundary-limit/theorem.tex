% Logarithmic Potential Boundary Limit
% From GTM035, Section s003
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\alpha$ be a real measure on $E$ satisfying
$$\int_E \left| \log \left| \frac{1}{z_0 - \zeta} \right| \right| d|\alpha|(\zeta) < \infty.$$
Then
$$\lim_{t \to 0} \int \alpha^* d\sigma_t(z) = \alpha^*(z_0).$$
