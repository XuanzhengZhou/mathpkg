% Finitely Generated Uniform Algebra Is Full
% From GTM035, Section s015
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\mathcal{L}$ be a finitely generated uniform algebra on a space $X$ with $X = \mathcal{M}(\mathcal{L})$. Then $\mathcal{L}$ is full as a subalgebra of $C(X)$.
