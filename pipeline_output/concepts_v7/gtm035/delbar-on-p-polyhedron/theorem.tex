% Delbar On P Polyhedron
% From GTM035, Section s008
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\Pi$ be a $p$-polyhedron in $\mathbb{C}^n$ and $\Omega$ a neighborhood of $\Pi$. Given $\phi \in \wedge^{p,q}(\Omega)$, $q > 0$, with $\bar{\partial}\phi = 0$, there exists a neighborhood $\Omega_1$ of $\Pi$ and $\psi \in \wedge^{p,q-1}(\Omega_1)$ with $\bar{\partial}\psi = \phi$.
