% Delbar Product Rule
% From GTM035, Section s006
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

If $\omega^k \in \wedge^k(\Omega)$ and $\omega^l \in \wedge^l(\Omega)$, then
$$\bar{\partial}(\omega^k \wedge \omega^l) = \bar{\partial}\omega^k \wedge \omega^l + (-1)^k \omega^k \wedge \bar{\partial}\omega^l.$$
