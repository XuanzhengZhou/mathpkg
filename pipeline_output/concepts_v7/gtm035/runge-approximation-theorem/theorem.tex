% Runge Approximation Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s002
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $K$ be a compact subset of the plane and let $E$ be a set containing at least one point from each bounded component of $\mathbb{C}\setminus K$. Then every function holomorphic in a neighborhood of $K$ can be uniformly approximated on $K$ by rational functions whose poles lie in $E$.
