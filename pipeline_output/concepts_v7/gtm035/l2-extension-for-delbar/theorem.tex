% L2 Extension For Delbar
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\Omega$ be a bounded domain in $\mathbb{C}^n$ and $u \in L^2(\Omega)$. If all distributional $\bar{\partial}$-derivatives of $u$ lie in $L^2$, then $u$ can be extended as an $L^2$ solution of the $\bar{\partial}$-equation.
