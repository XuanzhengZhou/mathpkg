% Edge Fiber Cardinality Bound
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

For almost all points $\lambda$ on an edge arc $\alpha$, the fiber $f^{-1}(\lambda) \cap \hat{\gamma}$ contains at most $k+s$ points, where $k$ and $s$ are bounds from adjacent regions.
