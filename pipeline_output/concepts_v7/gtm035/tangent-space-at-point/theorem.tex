% Tangent Space At Point
% From GTM035, Section s005
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Fix $x \in \Omega$. $T_x$ is the collection of all maps $v : C^\infty \to \mathbb{C}$ for which
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $v$ is linear.
\item[(b)] $v(f \cdot g) = f(x) \cdot v(g) + g(x) \cdot v(f)$, $f, g \in C^\infty$.
\end{enumerate}
$T_x$ is called the tangent space at $x$ and its elements tangent vectors at $x$.
