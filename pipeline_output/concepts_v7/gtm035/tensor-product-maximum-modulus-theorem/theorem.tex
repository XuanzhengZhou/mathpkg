% Tensor Product Maximum Modulus Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $(A, X, \Omega, p)$ be a maximum modulus algebra over $\Omega$ with $\Delta \subset \Omega$. Fix $F \in \otimes^n A$ and fix $x^0 \in \Pi^{-1}(0, \ldots, 0)$. Then
$$|F(x^0)| \leq \max_{\Pi^{-1}(\gamma)} |F|.$$
