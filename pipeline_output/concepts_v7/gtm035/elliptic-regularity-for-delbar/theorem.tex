% Elliptic Regularity For Delbar
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $B = \{z \in \mathbb{C}^n \mid |z| < 1\}$. Let $u \in L^2(B)$. Assume that for each $j$, $\partial u / \partial \bar{z}_j$, defined as distribution on $B$, is continuous. Then $u$ is continuous.
