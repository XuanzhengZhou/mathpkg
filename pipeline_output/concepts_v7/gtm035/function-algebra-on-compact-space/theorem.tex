% Function Algebra On Compact Space
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $X$ be a compact Hausdorff space. A function algebra on $X$ is a subalgebra $\mathfrak{A}$ of $C(X)$ that is closed under uniform convergence on $X$ and contains the constant functions.
