% Rationally Convex Hull Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $X$ be a compact subset of $\mathbb{C}^n$. The rationally convex hull of $X$, denoted $h_r(X)$, is the set $\{z \in \mathbb{C}^n : |f(z)| \leq \max_X |f| \text{ for all rational functions } f \text{ holomorphic on } X\}$.
