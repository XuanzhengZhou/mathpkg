% Maximal Ideal Space Definition
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

$\mathcal{M}(\mathfrak{A})$ is the space of maximal ideals of $\mathfrak{A}$, identified with the set of nonzero complex homomorphisms $\mathfrak{A} \to \mathbb{C}$, equipped with the Gelfand topology (the weak-$^*$ topology).
