% Analytic Function Of Projection For One Sheeted
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $(A, X, \Omega, f)$ be a maximum modulus algebra and fix a closed disk $\Delta \subseteq \Omega$. Assume that $X$ lies one-sheeted over $\Delta$. Then every $g \in A$ is an analytic function of $f$ on $f^{-1}(\Delta)$.
