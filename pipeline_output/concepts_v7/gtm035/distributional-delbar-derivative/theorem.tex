% Distributional Delbar Derivative
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $u \in L^2(\Omega)$. Fix $k \in L^2(\Omega)$ and fix $j$. We say $\partial u / \partial \bar{z}_j = k$ in the distributional sense if $\int_\Omega u \cdot \partial \phi / \partial z_j = -\int_\Omega k \phi$ for all test functions $\phi \in C_0^\infty(\Omega)$.
