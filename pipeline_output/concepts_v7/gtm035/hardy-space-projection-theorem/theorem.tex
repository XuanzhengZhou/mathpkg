% Hardy Space Projection Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Fix $F \in A$. Let $G$ denote the orthogonal projection in $L^2(\mu)$ of $F$ to $C$. Then $G \in H^2$, $G(0) = F(x^0)$, and $G(\lambda) \in \overline{co}(F(p^{-1}(\lambda)))$ for a.a. $\lambda \in \Gamma$.
