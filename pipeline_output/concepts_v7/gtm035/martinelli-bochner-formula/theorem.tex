% Martinelli Bochner Formula
% From GTM035, Section s013
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

For $f \in A(D) \cap C^1(\bar{D})$,
$$\frac{(2\pi i)^n}{(n-1)!} f(z) = \int_{\partial D} f(\zeta) K_{MB}(\zeta, z), \quad z \in D,$$
where $K_{MB}$ is the Bochner-Martinelli kernel.
