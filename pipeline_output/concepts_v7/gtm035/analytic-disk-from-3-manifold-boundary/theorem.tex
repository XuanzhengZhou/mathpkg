% Analytic Disk From 3 Manifold Boundary
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\psi(\zeta, \eta)$ be a function analytic on a neighborhood of $M$ in $\mathbb{C}^3$, where $\zeta = (\zeta_1, \zeta_2)$. The associated kernel construction produces an analytic disk from the boundary data on $M$.
