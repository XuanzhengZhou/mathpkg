% Kernel Identity Lemma
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

$-\bar{\partial}_{\zeta} K_1(\zeta, z) = \bar{\partial}_z K(\zeta, z)$, where $K$ is the Martinelli-Bochner kernel and $K_1$ is a related kernel used in the Harvey-Lawson theory.
