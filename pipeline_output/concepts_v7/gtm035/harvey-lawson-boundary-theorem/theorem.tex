% Harvey Lawson Boundary Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s019
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\gamma$ be a smooth simple closed curve in $\mathbb{C}^n$ satisfying the moment condition. Then there exists a holomorphic chain $V$ of complex dimension 1 such that the boundary of $V$ (in the sense of currents) equals $\gamma$.
