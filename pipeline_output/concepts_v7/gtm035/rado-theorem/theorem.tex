% Rado Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $f$ be a continuous function on a domain $\Omega$ that is analytic on $\Omega \setminus Z(f)$, where $Z(f) = \{z \in \Omega : f(z) = 0\}$. Then $f$ is analytic on $\Omega$.
