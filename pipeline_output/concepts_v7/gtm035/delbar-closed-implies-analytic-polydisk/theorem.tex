% Delbar Closed Implies Analytic Polydisk
% From GTM035, Section s006
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Assume that $\partial f / \partial \bar{z}_j = 0$, $j = 1, \ldots, n$, in the polydisk $\Omega = \{z \in \mathbb{C}^n \mid |z_j| < R_j\}$. Then there exist constants $A_\nu$ in $\mathbb{C}$ for each tuple $\nu = (\nu_1, \ldots, \nu_n)$ of nonnegative integers such that
$$f(z) = \sum_{\nu} A_\nu z^\nu,$$
the series converging absolutely in $\Omega$ and uniformly on every compact subset of $\Omega$.
