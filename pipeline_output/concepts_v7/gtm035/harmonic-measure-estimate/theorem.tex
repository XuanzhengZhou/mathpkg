% Harmonic Measure Estimate
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\Omega$ be a bounded domain in $\mathbb{C}$ such that $0 \in \partial \Omega$ and $\Omega$ is disjoint from the negative real axis. Then the harmonic measure of $\alpha_r$ with respect to $\Omega_r$ is $O(\sqrt{r})$ as $r \to 0$.
