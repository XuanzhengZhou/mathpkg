% Hardy Extension Lemma
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Under the hypothesis of the tensor product theorem, there exists $G \in H^\infty(T^n)$ such that $G(0, \ldots, 0) = F(x^0)$ and $G(\zeta) \in \overline{co}(F(\Pi^{-1}(\zeta)))$ for a.a. $\zeta \in T^n$.
