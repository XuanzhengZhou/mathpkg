% Leray Cauchy Fantappie Formula
% From GTM035, Section s013
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Given $w = (w_1, \ldots, w_n)$ defined on $\bar{D} \times \bar{D} \setminus \{\zeta = z\}$ with $\sum w_j(\zeta, z)(\zeta_j - z_j) = 1$, the Cauchy-Fantappiè kernel $K_w$ satisfies the integral representation formula for $f \in A(D) \cap C^1(\bar{D})$.
