% Kernel Residue At Diagonal
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Fix $z \in D$, and choose $\epsilon > 0$ such that the closed ball $\{|\zeta - z| \leq \epsilon\}$ is contained in $D$. Then
$$1 = a_0 \int_{\{|\zeta - z| = \epsilon\}} K_w(\zeta, z),$$
where $a_0 = (-1)^{n(n-1)/2}(n-1)!/(2\pi i)^n$.
