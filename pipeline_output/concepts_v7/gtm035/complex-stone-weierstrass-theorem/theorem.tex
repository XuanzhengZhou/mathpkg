% Complex Stone Weierstrass Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s002
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

$\mathfrak{A}$ is a subalgebra of $C(X)$ containing the constants and separating points. If
$$f \in \mathfrak{A} \Rightarrow \bar{f} \in \mathfrak{A},$$
then $\mathfrak{A}$ is dense in $C(X)$.
