% Rudin Analyticity From Maximum Principle
% From GTM035, Section s010
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $\mathcal{L}$ be an algebra of continuous functions on $D$ such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] The function $z$ is in $\mathcal{L}$.
\item[(b)] $\mathcal{L}$ satisfies a maximum principle relative to $\Gamma$: $|G(x)| \leq \max_{\Gamma} |G|$ for all $x \in D, G \in \mathcal{L}$.
\end{enumerate}
Then $\mathcal{L} \subseteq A(D)$.
