% Polynomial Sequence Construction Lemma
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

There exist two sequences of positive constants $c_j$ and $\epsilon_j$ with $c_1 = 1/10$, $c_{j+1} \leq (1/10)c_j$, and a sequence of polynomials $P_n$ in $z$ and $w$ with controlled growth on specified sets.
