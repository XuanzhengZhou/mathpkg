% Generalized Perturbation Stone Weierstrass
% From GTM035, Section s014
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Assume that there exists $k$, $0 \leq k < 1$, with $|R(z) - R(z')| \leq k|z - z'|$ for $z, z' \in N$. Then $\mathfrak{A} = [z_1, \ldots, z_n, \bar{z}_1 + R_1, \ldots, \bar{z}_n + R_n]$ is dense in $C(X)$.
