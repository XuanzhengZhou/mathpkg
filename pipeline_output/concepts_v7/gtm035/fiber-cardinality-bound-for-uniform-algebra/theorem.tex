% Fiber Cardinality Bound For Uniform Algebra
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Let $(A, X, \mathcal{M})$ be a uniform algebra. Fix $p \in \mathcal{M} \setminus X$, $f \in A$, and put $\lambda_0 = f(p)$. Then the fiber $f^{-1}(\lambda_0) \cap X$ has cardinality bounded in terms of the behavior of $f$ on $X$.
