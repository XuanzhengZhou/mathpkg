% Hartogs Rosenthal Theorem
% From GTM035, Section s002
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Assume that $X \subset \mathbb{C}$ has Lebesgue two-dimensional measure $0$. Then rational functions whose poles lie off $X$ are uniformly dense in $C(X)$.
