% Polynomial Hull Of Curves Is Analytic Variety
% From GTM035, Section s012
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

If $\gamma$ is a finite union of smooth compact curves in $\mathbb{C}^N$ and $\gamma$ is not polynomially convex, then $\hat{\gamma} \setminus \gamma$ is a one-dimensional analytic subvariety of $\mathbb{C}^N \setminus \gamma$.
