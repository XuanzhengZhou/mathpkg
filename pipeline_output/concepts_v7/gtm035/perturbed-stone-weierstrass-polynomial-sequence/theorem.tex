% Perturbed Stone Weierstrass Polynomial Sequence
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

Given an integer $n \geq 1$, there exists a polynomial $P_n$ approximating $1/w$ on a closed semidisk $S \subset \{\text{Re } w \geq 0\}$.
