% Cauchy Transform Inversion Formula
% From GTM035, Section s002
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

For $F \in C_0^1(\mathbb{C})$,
$$\frac{\partial F}{\partial \bar{z}} \text{ satisfies } \int \frac{\partial F}{\partial \bar{z}} \frac{dx\,dy}{\zeta - z} = -\pi F(\zeta).$$
