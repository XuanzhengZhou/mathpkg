% Polynomial Approximation With Estimates
% From GTM035
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

There exists a sequence of polynomials $\{P_n\}$ such that $P_n(w) \to 1/w$ on $S \setminus \{0\}$ and $|P_n(w)| \leq C/|w|$ for $w \in S \setminus \{0\}$.
