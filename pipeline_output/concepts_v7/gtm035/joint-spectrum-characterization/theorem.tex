% Joint Spectrum Characterization
% From GTM035, Section s008
% Source: Alexander & Wermer, Several Complex Variables and Banach Algebras (GTM 35), 3rd Edition

$(\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_n)$ in $\mathbb{C}^n$ lies in $\sigma(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ if and only if the equation
$$\sum_{j=1}^{n} y_j(x_j - \lambda_j) = 1$$
has no solution $y_1, \ldots, y_n \in \mathfrak{A}$.
