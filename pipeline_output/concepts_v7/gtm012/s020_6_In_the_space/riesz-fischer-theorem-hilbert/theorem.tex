Let $H$ be a Hilbert space with orthonormal basis $(e_n)$. If $(a_n)$ is any sequence of scalars such that $\sum |a_n|^2 < \infty$, then there exists a unique $u \in H$ such that its Fourier coefficients are $(a_n)$, i.e.,
$$u = \sum_{n} a_n e_n,$$
with convergence in the norm of $H$. Equivalently, the map $u \mapsto ((u, e_n))_n$ is a unitary isomorphism from $H$ onto $\ell^2$.
