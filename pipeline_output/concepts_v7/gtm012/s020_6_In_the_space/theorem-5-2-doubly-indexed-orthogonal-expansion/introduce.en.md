Theorem 5.2 extends the orthogonal expansion result to the case where the orthonormal basis is indexed by all integers rather than just the positive integers. This is essential for the $L^2$ theory of Fourier series in Chapter 3, where the natural orthonormal basis is $(e^{inx})_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}$. The proof is essentially identical to Theorem 5.1 but uses symmetric partial sums. Bessel's equality, Bessel's inequality, and Parseval's identity all carry over to this setting.
