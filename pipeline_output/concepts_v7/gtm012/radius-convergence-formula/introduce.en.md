The Hadamard formula gives the radius of convergence of a power series in terms of the limsup of the $n$-th roots of the coefficients. This is the sharpest general formula. When the ratio limit exists, the radius can also be computed as $R = (\lim |a_{n+1}/a_n|)^{-1}$.
