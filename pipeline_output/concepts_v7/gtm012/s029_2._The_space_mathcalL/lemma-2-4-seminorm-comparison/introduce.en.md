Lemma 2.4 provides comparison inequalities among the seminorms defining the topology of $\mathcal{L}$. Increasing the derivative order $k$, the exponential weight $a$, or decreasing the cutoff $M$ yields a larger seminorm that dominates the original. This ordering is essential for the characterization of continuous linear functionals on $\mathcal{L}$ (Theorem 3.3), where a single seminorm suffices to bound the functional.
