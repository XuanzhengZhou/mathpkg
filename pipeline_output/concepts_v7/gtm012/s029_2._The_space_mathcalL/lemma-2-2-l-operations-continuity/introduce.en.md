Lemma 2.2 establishes that the fundamental operations on $\mathcal{L}$—differentiation $D$, translation $T_s$, complex conjugation $u \mapsto u^*$, and right-integration $S_+$—are all continuous with respect to the $\mathcal{L}$-topology. Crucially, it also shows that the difference quotient in $\mathcal{L}$ converges to the derivative, confirming that differentiation is the limit of difference quotients in the topology of $\mathcal{L}$.
