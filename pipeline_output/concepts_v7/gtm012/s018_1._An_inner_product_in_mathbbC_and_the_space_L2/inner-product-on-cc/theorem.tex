Let $\mathbb{C}$ denote the space of continuous complex-valued periodic functions on $[0, 2\pi]$. For $u, v \in \mathbb{C}$, the inner product $(u, v)$ is defined by
$$
(u, v) = \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} u(x) v(x)^* \, dx.
$$

This inner product satisfies the following properties:
\begin{align}
(au, v) &= a(u, v) = (u, a^*v), \\
(u_1 + u_2, v) &= (u_1, v) + (u_2, v), \\
(u, v_1 + v_2) &= (u, v_1) + (u, v_2), \\
(v, u) &= (u, v)^*, \\
(u, u) &\geq 0, \quad (u, u) = 0 \text{ only if } u = 0.
\end{align}
