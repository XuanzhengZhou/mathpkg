Formula (7.3) generalizes differentiation under the integral sign to the case where the upper limit of integration coincides with the variable of differentiation. The derivative receives an extra boundary term $f(y,y)$ in addition to the integral of the partial derivative. This is a special case of the full Leibniz integral rule for variable limits.
