If $f$ is a complex-valued function of class $C^1$ defined on a rectangle $|x - a| < r_1$, $|y - b| < r_2$, then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item The derivative with respect to $y$ of
$$
\int_a^x f(s, y) \, ds
$$
is
$$
\int_a^x D_2 f(s, y) \, ds.
$$
\item The derivative with respect to $x$ of
$$
\int_b^y f(x, t) \, dt
$$
is
$$
\int_b^y D_1 f(x, t) \, dt.
$$
\end{enumerate}
