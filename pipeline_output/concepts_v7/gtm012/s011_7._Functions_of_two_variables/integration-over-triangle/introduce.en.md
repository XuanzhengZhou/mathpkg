Theorem 7.3 extends the change of order of integration (Theorem 7.2) from rectangular domains to triangular domains. When the domain is $\{(x,y) : 0 \leq x \leq a, 0 \leq y \leq x\}$, interchanging the order of integration converts the inner limit $0$ to $x$ into an outer limit $y$ to $a$. This technique is fundamental for evaluating double integrals over non-rectangular regions.
