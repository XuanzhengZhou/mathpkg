Let $A$ be an open subset of $\mathbb{R}^2$ and let $f: A \to \mathbb{C}$. Suppose $(x_0, y_0) \in A$. The **partial derivative of $f$ with respect to the first variable** at $(x_0, y_0)$ is defined by
$$
D_1 f(x_0, y_0) = \lim_{x \to x_0} \frac{f(x, y_0) - f(x_0, y_0)}{x - x_0},
$$
provided the limit exists. The partial derivative $D_2 f(x_0, y_0)$ with respect to the second variable is defined analogously.
