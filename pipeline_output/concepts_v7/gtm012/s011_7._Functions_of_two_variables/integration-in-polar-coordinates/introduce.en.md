Theorem 7.4 gives the change of variables formula for double integrals over a disc using polar coordinates. The Cartesian double integral over the disc is transformed into an iterated integral in polar coordinates with the Jacobian factor $r$ (the area element $dx\,dy$ becomes $r\,dr\,d\theta$). This is the prototype for the general change of variables formula in multiple integrals.
