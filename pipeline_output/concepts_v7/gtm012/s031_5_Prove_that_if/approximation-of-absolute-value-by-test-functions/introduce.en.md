Lemma 4.2 provides a technical approximation result: the piecewise linear function $h(t) = |t|$ for $t \leq 0$ and $h(t) = 0$ for $t > 0$, though not itself in $\mathcal{L}$, can be approximated in the seminorm $|\cdot|_{0,a,M}$ by a sequence of test functions in $\mathcal{L}$. This lemma is essential in the proof of Theorem 4.3, where the action of a distribution on translates of $h$ is used to construct a function representing the distribution after integration.
