The real numbers are an ordered field via a designated set $P$ of positive elements. These axioms capture trichotomy (every nonzero element is either positive or its negative is), and closure under addition and multiplication. The ordering $x < y$ is defined by $y - x \in P$, and the Archimedean property and completeness are needed to fully characterize $\mathbb{R}$.
