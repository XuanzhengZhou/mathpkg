The Laurent series generalizes the Taylor series to allow negative powers. It represents a holomorphic function in an annulus, and the classification of isolated singularities (removable, pole, essential) depends on the negative part of the Laurent expansion. The coefficient $a_{-1}$ is the residue, which plays a central role in evaluating contour integrals via the residue theorem.
