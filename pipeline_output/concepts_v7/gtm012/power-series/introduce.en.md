A power series is an infinite series in powers of $(z - z_0)$. Every power series has a radius of convergence $R$ such that the series converges absolutely inside the disk $|z - z_0| < R$ and diverges outside. Power series define analytic functions within their disk of convergence and are central to complex analysis.
