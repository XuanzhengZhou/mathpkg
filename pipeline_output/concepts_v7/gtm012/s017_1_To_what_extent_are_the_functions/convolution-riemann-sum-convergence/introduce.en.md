Corollary 7.3 provides an approximation result: the convolution $u * v$ of a smooth periodic function with a continuous periodic function can be obtained as the limit of discrete Riemann sum approximations $w_n$ in the topology of $\mathcal{P}$. Moreover, all derivatives $D^k w_n$ converge uniformly to $D^k(u * v)$. This result bridges the continuous convolution operation with its discrete counterpart and is used in proving the representation formula for the convolution of a distribution with a smooth function.
