For all $F, G, H \in \mathcal{P}'$ and $a \in \mathbb{C}$:

\textit{Commutativity:}
$$F * G = G * F.$$

\textit{Scalar multiplication:}
$$(aF) * G = a(F * G) = F * (aG).$$

\textit{Distributivity:}
$$(F + G) * H = F * H + G * H.$$

\textit{Associativity:}
$$(F * G) * H = F * (G * H).$$

\textit{Translation:}
$$T_t(F * G) = (T_t F) * G = F * (T_t G).$$

\textit{Differentiation:}
$$D^k(F * G) = (D^k F) * G = F * (D^k G).$$
