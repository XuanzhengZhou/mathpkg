A set $A$ is a subset of $B$, written $A \subset B$, if every element of $A$ also belongs to $B$. The subset relation is reflexive ($A \subset A$), antisymmetric (if $A \subset B$ and $B \subset A$ then $A = B$), and transitive. The empty set is a subset of every set, making it the least element under inclusion.
