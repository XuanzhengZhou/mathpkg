Let $p$ be a polynomial of degree $n \geq 1$. Let $C_R$ be the directed line segment from $b - iR$ to $b + iR$, where $b \in \mathbb{R}$ is chosen so that $p(z) \neq 0$ for $\operatorname{Re} z \geq b$. The \textbf{Green's function} $G$ for the operator $p(D)$ is defined by
$$G(t) = \lim_{R \to \infty} \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{C_R} e^{tz} p(z)^{-1} \, dz,$$
whenever the limit exists.
