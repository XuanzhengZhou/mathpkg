Let $p$ be the polynomial
$$p(z) = \sum_{k=0}^{n} a_k z^k, \quad a_n \neq 0,$$
where $a_k \in \mathbb{C}$ are constants. Let $D$ denote differentiation, with $D^0 = \text{identity}$. The \textbf{polynomial differential operator} $p(D)$ is defined by
$$p(D) = \sum_{k=0}^{n} a_k D^k.$$
For a function or distribution $u$, the action is
$$p(D)u = \sum_{k=0}^{n} a_k D^k u.$$
