\textbf{Definition.} For $z = x + iy \in \mathbb{C}$, the \textit{complex conjugate} of $z$ is:
\[
z^* = (x + iy)^* = x - iy.
\]
\textbf{Properties:} For all $z, w \in \mathbb{C}$:
\begin{align}
(z + w)^* &= z^* + w^*, \\
(zw)^* &= z^* w^*, \\
(z^*)^* &= z, \\
z^* z &= x^2 + y^2 \geq 0.
\end{align}
