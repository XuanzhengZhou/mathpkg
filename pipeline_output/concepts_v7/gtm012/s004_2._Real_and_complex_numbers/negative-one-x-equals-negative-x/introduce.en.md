From the field axioms one deduces that multiplying by $-1$ yields the additive inverse: $x + (-1) \cdot x = 1 \cdot x + (-1) \cdot x = (1 + (-1)) \cdot x = 0 \cdot x = 0$, so $(-1) \cdot x = -x$ by uniqueness of the additive inverse. This connects the multiplicative structure with the additive structure.
