The real numbers contain a distinguished nonzero element $1$, called the multiplicative identity, which leaves any real number unchanged under multiplication. This is Axiom M3 in the field axiom system for $\mathbb{R}$. The text references this axiom when noting that $1$ is unique, though the statement was truncated by OCR.
