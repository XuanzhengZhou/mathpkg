The Dirichlet problem asks for a harmonic function on a domain with prescribed boundary values. On the disc, Fourier series provide an explicit solution: the $n$-th Fourier mode decays as $r^{|n|}$ toward the origin. The Poisson kernel representation shows the solution is an integral average of the boundary data, and the solution is $C^\infty$ in the interior regardless of boundary regularity (elliptic regularity).
