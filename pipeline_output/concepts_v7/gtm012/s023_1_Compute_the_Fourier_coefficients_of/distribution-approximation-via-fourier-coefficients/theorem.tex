Let $(F_m)_{m=1}^{\infty} \subset \mathcal{P}'$ with Fourier coefficients
$$(a_{m,n})_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}.$$
Suppose there exist $r > 0$ and a constant $c$ such that
$$|a_{m,n}| \leq c|n|^r, \quad \text{for all } m \geq 1, \text{ all } n \neq 0.$$
Suppose further that for each $n \in \mathbb{Z}$,
$$a_{m,n} \to a_n \quad \text{as } m \to \infty.$$
Then $(a_n)_{n=-\infty}^{\infty}$ is the sequence of Fourier coefficients of some $F \in \mathcal{P}'$,
and
$$F_m \to F \quad \text{in } \mathcal{P}'.$$
