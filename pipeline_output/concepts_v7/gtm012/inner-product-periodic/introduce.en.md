The inner product on the space of continuous periodic functions generalizes the dot product from finite-dimensional vector spaces to function spaces. It satisfies conjugate symmetry, linearity in the first argument, and positive definiteness. The associated norm $\|u\| = (u, u)^{1/2}$ is the $L^2$ norm.
