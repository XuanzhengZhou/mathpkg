A linear functional on the Schwartz space $\mathcal{L}$ is a map from $\mathcal{L}$ to the complex numbers that respects scalar multiplication and vector addition. It is the fundamental algebraic object from which distributions (continuous linear functionals) are built. The linearity condition ensures compatibility with the vector space structure of $\mathcal{L}$.
