The exponential distribution is a distribution defined by integration of a test function against the exponential kernel $e^{zt}$ over the positive half-line. For each fixed complex number $z$, this defines a continuous linear functional on $\mathcal{L}$. It illustrates how ordinary functions (with appropriate growth conditions) embed into the space of distributions.
