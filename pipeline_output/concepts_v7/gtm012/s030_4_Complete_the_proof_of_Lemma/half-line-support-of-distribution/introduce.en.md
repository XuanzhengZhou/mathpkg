Corollary 3.4 is a consequence of Theorem 3.3: since every continuous linear functional on $\mathcal{L}$ satisfies a seminorm bound that involves a parameter $M$, the support of the distribution must be contained in the half-line $[M, \infty)$. This reflects the fact that the Schwartz space $\mathcal{L}$ consists of functions vanishing on a left half-line, so its dual $\mathcal{L}'$ consists of distributions supported on a right half-line.
