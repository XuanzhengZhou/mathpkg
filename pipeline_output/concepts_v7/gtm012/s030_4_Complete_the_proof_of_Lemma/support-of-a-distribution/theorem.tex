For $F \in \mathcal{L}'$, a point $t \in \mathbb{R}$ is not in the support of $F$ if and only if there exists $\varepsilon > 0$ such that
$$F(u) = 0$$
whenever $u \in \mathcal{L}$ and $\operatorname{supp}(u) \subset (t - \varepsilon, t + \varepsilon)$. The support of $F$ is denoted $\operatorname{supp}(F)$.
