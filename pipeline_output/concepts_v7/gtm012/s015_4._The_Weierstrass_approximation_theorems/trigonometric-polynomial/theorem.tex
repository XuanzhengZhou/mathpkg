A trigonometric polynomial is a function of the form

$$\varphi(x) = \sum_{k=-n}^{n} a_k \exp(ikx),$$

where the coefficients $a_k$ are in $\mathbb{C}$. For $k > 0$,

$$\exp(\pm ikx) = [\exp(\pm ix)]^k = (\cos x \pm i \sin x)^k.$$

Therefore any function of this form can be written as a polynomial in the trigonometric functions $\cos x$ and $\sin x$.
