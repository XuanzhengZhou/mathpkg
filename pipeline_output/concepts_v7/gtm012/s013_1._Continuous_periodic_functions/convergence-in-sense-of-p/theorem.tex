A sequence $(u_n)_{n=1}^{\infty} \subset \mathcal{P}$ is said to \emph{converge to $u \in \mathcal{P}$ in the sense of $\mathcal{P}$} if for each $k = 0, 1, 2, \ldots$,
$$|D^k u_n - D^k u| \to 0 \quad \text{as} \quad n \to \infty,$$
where $D^0 u = u$. We denote this by $u_n \to u \; (\mathcal{P})$.
