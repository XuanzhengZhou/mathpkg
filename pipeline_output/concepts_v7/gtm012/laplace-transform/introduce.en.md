The Laplace transform converts functions on the half-line into holomorphic functions on a half-plane. It diagonalizes differentiation: $L(Du) = z Lu$, turning differential equations into algebraic equations. This makes it a powerful tool for solving linear ODEs with constant coefficients. The inversion formula recovers $u$ via a contour integral.
