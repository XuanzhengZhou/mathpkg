Suppose $(z_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ is a sequence in $\mathbb{C}$ and $(a_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ a sequence of non-negative real numbers. If there exists a constant $M > 0$ and an integer $N$ such that $|z_n| \leq M a_n$ for all $n \geq N$, and if $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n$ converges, then $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} z_n$ converges.
