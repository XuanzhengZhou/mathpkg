Suppose $(z_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}$ is a sequence in $\mathbb{C}$ and suppose $z_n \neq 0$ for all $n$.

(a) If $\displaystyle \limsup_{n \to \infty} \left| \frac{z_{n+1}}{z_n} \right| < 1$, then $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} z_n$ converges.

(b) If $\displaystyle \liminf_{n \to \infty} \left| \frac{z_{n+1}}{z_n} \right| > 1$, then $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} z_n$ diverges.
