$\ell^2$ is the space of square-summable sequences, a concrete infinite-dimensional Hilbert space. It is the sequence analog of $L^2$, and every separable Hilbert space is isometrically isomorphic to $\ell^2$ (or $\mathbb{C}^n$ if finite-dimensional). The orthonormal basis of standard unit vectors makes $\ell^2$ particularly convenient for calculations.
