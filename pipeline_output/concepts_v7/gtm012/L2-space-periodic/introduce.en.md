$L^2$ is the completion of the continuous periodic functions with respect to the $L^2$ norm. Its elements are identified with certain periodic distributions. This construction parallels the usual measure-theoretic $L^2$ space but uses distribution theory instead. $L^2$ is a Hilbert space, complete with respect to the $L^2$ norm, and is the natural setting for Fourier series.
