A closed tubular neighborhood of radius $\varepsilon > 0$ of a submanifold $M \subset V$ is an embedding

$$D_\varepsilon(\nu): D_\varepsilon(M) \to V$$

where $D_\varepsilon(M)$ is the closed $\varepsilon$-disk bundle of the normal bundle $\nu$ of $M$ in $V$, extending the zero section $M \hookrightarrow V$.
