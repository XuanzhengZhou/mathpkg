Let $M$ be a connected $n$-manifold without boundary. Suppose that $f_i, g_i: D^n \hookrightarrow M$ ($i = 1, 2$) are embeddings such that $f_1(D^n) \cap f_2(D^n) = \varnothing = g_1(D^n) \cap g_2(D^n)$. If $M$ is orientable suppose further that $f_i$ and $g_i$ both preserve, or both reverse, orientation. Then there is a diffeotopy of $M$ carrying $f_i$ to $g_i$ for $i = 1, 2$.
