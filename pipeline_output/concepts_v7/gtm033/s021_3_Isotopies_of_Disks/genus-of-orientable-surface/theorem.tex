Let $p \geqslant 0$ be an integer. An orientable surface $M$ is of \textbf{genus} $p$ provided $M$ can be obtained from $S^2$ by successively attaching handles $p$ times. That is, there must exist a sequence of orientable surfaces $M_0, \ldots, M_p$ and orientable embeddings $f_i: S^0 \times D^2 \hookrightarrow M_{i-1}$, $i = 1, \ldots, p$ (if $p > 0$) such that
$$
M_0 \approx S^2, \qquad M_i \approx M_{i-1}[f_i], \qquad M_p = M.
$$
Thus each $M_i$ has genus $i$.
