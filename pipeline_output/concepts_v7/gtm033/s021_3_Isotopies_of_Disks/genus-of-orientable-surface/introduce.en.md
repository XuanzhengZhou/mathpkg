An orientable surface has genus $p$ if it can be built from the 2-sphere by attaching $p$ handles via orientable embeddings. Surfaces of different genus are not diffeomorphic (they have different Euler characteristics: $2 - 2p$), while any two orientable surfaces of the same genus are diffeomorphic. Every compact connected orientable surface has a well-defined genus.
