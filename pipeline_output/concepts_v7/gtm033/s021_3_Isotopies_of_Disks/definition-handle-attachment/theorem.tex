Let $M$ be a surface and $f: S^0 \times D^2 \hookrightarrow M - \partial M$ an embedding whose image is a pair of disjoint disks. The surface obtained by attaching a handle is
$$M[f] = [M - \operatorname{Int} f(S^0 \times D^2)] \bigcup_f D^1 \times S^1$$
where the gluing is along $f|S^0 \times S^1$. This adds a cylinder (handle) connecting the two disks.
