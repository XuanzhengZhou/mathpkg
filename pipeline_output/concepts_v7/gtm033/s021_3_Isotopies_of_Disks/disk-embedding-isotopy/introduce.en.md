In a connected manifold, any two embeddings of a disk are isotopic provided they agree on orientation when the disk dimension equals the manifold dimension. If both embeddings lie in the interior, the isotopy can be realized as a diffeotopy of the ambient manifold with compact support. This result shows that, up to isotopy, there is essentially only one way to embed a disk in a connected manifold.
