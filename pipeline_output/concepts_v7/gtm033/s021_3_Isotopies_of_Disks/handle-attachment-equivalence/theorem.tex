Let $M$ be a connected surface and $f, g: S^0 \times D^2 \hookrightarrow M - \partial M$ embeddings. Then $M[f] \approx M[g]$ in the following cases:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] $M$ is nonorientable;
\item[(b)] $M$ is oriented and $f$ and $g$ both preserve or both reverse orientation;
\item[(c)] $M$ is reversible and both $f$ and $g$ are orientable.
\end{enumerate}
