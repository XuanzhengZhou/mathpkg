An orientable surface $M$ has genus $p \geqslant 0$ if $M$ can be obtained from $S^2$ by successively attaching handles $p$ times. A nonorientable surface has genus $p \geqslant 1$ if it can be obtained from $S^2$ by attaching $p$ crosscaps (i.e., taking connected sum with $p$ copies of the real projective plane).
