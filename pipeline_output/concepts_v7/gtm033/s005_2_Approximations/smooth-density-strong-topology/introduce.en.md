Theorem 2.4 establishes that every $C^r$ map between open subsets of Euclidean spaces can be approximated arbitrarily closely in the strong $C^r$ Whitney topology by a $C^\infty$ map. The proof uses convolution on coordinate neighborhoods together with a partition of unity to patch local smooth approximations into a global one, while controlling all derivatives up to order $r$.
