Let $U \subset \mathbb{R}^m$ be open, $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^n$ a map, and $\theta: \mathbb{R}^m \to \mathbb{R}$ a convolution kernel. The \textbf{convolution} $\theta * f$ is defined by
$$(\theta * f)(x) = \int_{\mathbb{R}^m} \theta(y) f(x - y) \, dy,$$
where $f$ is extended to $\mathbb{R}^m$ by setting $f = 0$ outside $U$.
