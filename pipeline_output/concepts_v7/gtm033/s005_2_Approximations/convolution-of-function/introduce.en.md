The convolution $\theta * f$ of a map $f$ with a convolution kernel $\theta$ is a weighted average of the values of $f$ near each point. If $\theta$ is smooth, then $\theta * f$ is smooth regardless of the regularity of $f$, making convolution the fundamental tool for constructing smooth approximations in differential topology.
