Theorem 1.10 provides a complete classification of homotopy classes of maps from a compact connected $n$-manifold into the $n$-sphere. For closed oriented manifolds, the integer degree is a complete invariant and every integer is realized. For closed nonorientable manifolds, the mod 2 degree is a complete invariant. For manifolds with nonempty boundary, every map is null-homotopic. This is a precursor to the Hopf degree theorem.
