Lemma 1.2 states that if a smooth map is defined on the boundary of a compact oriented manifold and extends to the whole manifold, its degree at any regular value must be zero. The proof uses the fact that the preimage of a regular value is a compact 1-manifold whose boundary components come in pairs of opposite signs.
