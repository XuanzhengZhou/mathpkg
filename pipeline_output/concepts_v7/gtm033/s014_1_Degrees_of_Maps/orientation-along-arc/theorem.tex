Let $(W, \omega)$ be an oriented $\partial$-manifold. Suppose $K \subset W$ is an embedded arc which is transverse to $\partial W$ at its endpoints $u, v \in \partial W$. Let $\kappa$ be an orientation of $K$, and consider the quotient orientation $\omega/\kappa$ of the algebraic normal bundle of $K$. Then
$$
\omega_u/\kappa_u = (\partial \omega)_u \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad \omega_v/\kappa_v = -(\partial \omega)_v.
$$
