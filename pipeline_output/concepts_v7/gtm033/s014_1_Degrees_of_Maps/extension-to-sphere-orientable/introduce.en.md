Theorem 1.8 completely characterizes when a map from the boundary of a compact oriented manifold into the sphere can be extended to the interior: the degree must vanish. Necessity follows from Theorem 1.6(b). Sufficiency is the deeper direction: if the degree is zero, one can pair up preimages of a regular value along embedded arcs, use Lemma 1.7 to remove them, and extend the map using Tietze's theorem.
