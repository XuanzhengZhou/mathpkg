Let $W$ be a compact oriented $\partial$-manifold of dimension $n + 1$. Let $f: \partial W \to S^n$ be a $C^\infty$ map and $K \subset W$ a neat arc whose endpoints are in $\partial W$ and which is otherwise disjoint from $\partial W$. Suppose $y \in S^n$ is a regular value for $f$ and $f^{-1}(y) = \partial K$. Suppose further that the two endpoints of $K$ are of opposite type for $f$. Then $f$ extends to a map $W \to S^n$ having $y$ as a regular value, with the preimage of $y$ being exactly $K$.
