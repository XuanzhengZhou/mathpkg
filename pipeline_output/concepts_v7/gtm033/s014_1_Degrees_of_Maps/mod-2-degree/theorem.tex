Let $M$ and $N$ be compact $n$-manifolds without boundary, with $N$ connected (orientations not required). Let $f: M \to N$ be a $C^\infty$ map and $y \in N$ a regular value of $f$. The \textbf{mod 2 degree} of $f$ at $y$ is
$$
\deg_2(f, y) = |f^{-1}(y)| \pmod{2},
$$
i.e., the parity of the number of preimages of $y$.
