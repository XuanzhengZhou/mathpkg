Let $(M, \omega)$ be an oriented $\partial$-manifold and $\partial f: \partial M \times [0, \infty) \to M$ a collar. Then $T_{\partial M}f$ induces an isomorphism of the trivial bundle $\partial M \times \mathbb{R}$ onto the normal bundle $\nu$ of $\partial M$ in $M$. The standard orientation of $\mathbb{R}$ orients each fibre of $\partial M \times \mathbb{R}$; via $T_{\partial M}f$ this induces an orientation $\iota$ of $\nu$ which does not depend on the collar. In other words, $\nu$ is oriented by inward pointing vectors tangent to $M$ at $\partial M$.
