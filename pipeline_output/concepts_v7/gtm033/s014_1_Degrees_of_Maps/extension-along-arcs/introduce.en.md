Lemma 1.7 provides a technical tool for extending a map defined near the endpoints of an embedded arc to a neighborhood of the entire arc, while preserving a regular value. When the two endpoints have the same sign with respect to the map, one can join the local inverses near the endpoints via a tubular neighborhood of the arc. This lemma is the key geometric step in proving the extension theorems (1.8 and 1.9).
