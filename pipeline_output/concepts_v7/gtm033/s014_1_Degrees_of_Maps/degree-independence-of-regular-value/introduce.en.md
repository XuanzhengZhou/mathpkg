Lemma 1.4 shows that the degree of a smooth map between compact oriented manifolds does not depend on which regular value is chosen. The proof constructs a diffeomorphism homotopic to the identity that sends one regular value to the other. Together with Lemma 1.5 on smooth approximation, this makes the degree a well-defined homotopy invariant for continuous maps.
