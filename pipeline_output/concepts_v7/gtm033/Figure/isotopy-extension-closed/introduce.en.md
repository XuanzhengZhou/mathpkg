Theorem 1.6 removes the compactness assumption from Theorem 1.3 by replacing it with a bounded velocity condition. An isotopy of a closed (possibly noncompact) submanifold with bounded velocity extends to a diffeotopy of the ambient manifold. The bounded velocity condition replaces compactness in guaranteeing completeness of the flow.
