Theorem 1.5 states that proper maps form an open subset in the strong $C^r$ topology. The proof uses a compact cover of $M$ and a locally finite open cover of $N$ to construct a neighborhood of a proper map that consists entirely of proper maps. Since properness of an embedding is equivalent to having closed image, this theorem is useful for studying closed embeddings.
