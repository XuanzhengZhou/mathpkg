A variant of the isotopy extension theorem for closed (rather than compact) submanifolds. Instead of requiring compact support, the isotopy need only have bounded velocity — the tangent vectors to the isotopy curves have bounded lengths in some Riemannian metric. The conclusion yields an extension diffeotopy that also has bounded velocity, which is suitable for noncompact ambient manifolds.
