Theorem 1.1 establishes that the set of $C^r$ immersions from $M$ to $N$ is open in the strong $C^r$ topology. This means any immersion has a whole neighborhood of immersions, making the immersion property stable under small perturbations. The proof uses local atlases to construct a neighborhood where the derivative remains injective.
