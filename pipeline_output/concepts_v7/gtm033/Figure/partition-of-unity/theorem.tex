Let $M$ be a $C^r$ manifold, $0 \leqslant r \leqslant \infty$, and $\mathcal{U} = \{U_i\}_{i \in \Lambda}$ an open cover. A $C^r$ partition of unity subordinate to $\mathcal{U}$ is a family of $C^r$ maps $\lambda_i: M \to [0,1]$, $i \in \Lambda$, such that:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $\operatorname{Supp} \lambda_i \subset U_i$ for all $i \in \Lambda$,
\item $\{\operatorname{Supp} \lambda_i\}_{i \in \Lambda}$ is locally finite,
\item $\sum_{i \in \Lambda} \lambda_i(x) = 1$ for all $x \in M$.
\end{enumerate}
