The isotopy extension theorem: an isotopy of a compact submanifold can be extended to an ambient diffeotopy of the entire manifold. The isotopy must stay either entirely in the boundary or entirely in the interior. The proof constructs a time-dependent vector field along the image of the isotopy and extends it via a tubular neighborhood and partition of unity to a compactly supported field on $M$, which then generates the desired ambient diffeotopy.
