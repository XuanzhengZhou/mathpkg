Theorem 1.4 asserts that the set of $C^r$ embeddings is open in the strong topology on the function space. Combined with Theorem 1.1 (immersions are open), this shows that being an embedding is a stable property: a sufficiently small perturbation of an embedding remains an embedding. The proof constructs a neighborhood using local atlases and the preceding lemma on immersion persistence.
