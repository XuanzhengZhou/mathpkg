Theorem 1.8, the ambient tubular neighborhood theorem, states that an isotopy of tubular neighborhoods of a closed neat submanifold $A$ can be extended to a diffeotopy of the whole manifold with support in any prescribed neighborhood of $A$. This follows from Theorem 1.7 because such an isotopy leaves $A$ pointwise fixed and hence has bounded velocity near $A$.
