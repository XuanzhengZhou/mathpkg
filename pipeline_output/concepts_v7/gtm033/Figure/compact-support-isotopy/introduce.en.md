Theorem 1.2 is a direct corollary of Theorem 1.1: compactly supported time-dependent vector fields automatically have bounded velocity, so they generate isotopies. On compact manifolds, every time-dependent vector field generates an isotopy since the whole manifold provides compact support. This makes the isotopy machinery particularly convenient on compact manifolds.
