Theorem 1.9 is a smoothing result: given two manifolds each split into two closed submanifolds meeting along their common boundary, a homeomorphism that is piecewise a diffeomorphism on each half can be smoothed to a global diffeomorphism. The proof uses collars on the splitting submanifold and the ambient tubular neighborhood theorem to adjust the collars so they match, yielding a diffeomorphism that agrees with the original homeomorphism on the splitting locus and outside any neighborhood of it.
