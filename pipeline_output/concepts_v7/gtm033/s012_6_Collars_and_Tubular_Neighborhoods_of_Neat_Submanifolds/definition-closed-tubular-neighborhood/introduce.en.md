A closed tubular neighborhood of radius $arepsilon$ is a tubular neighborhood whose fibres are closed disks of radius $arepsilon$ in the normal bundle. For $\partial V = arnothing$, a closed tubular neighborhood of radius 1 at a point $x_0 \in V$ is an embedding $(D^n, 0) 	o (V, x_0)$ where $n = \dim V$.
