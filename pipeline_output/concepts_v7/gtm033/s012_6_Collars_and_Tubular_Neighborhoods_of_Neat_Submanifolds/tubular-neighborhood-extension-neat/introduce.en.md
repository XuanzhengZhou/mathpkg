This extension theorem for tubular neighborhoods states that any tubular neighborhood of the boundary of a neat submanifold inside the boundary of the ambient manifold can be extended to a tubular neighborhood of the entire submanifold. The proof first reduces to the product case using collars, then applies isotopy of tubular neighborhoods. This result is essential for constructing tubular neighborhoods inductively and for extending bundle maps in cobordism theory.
