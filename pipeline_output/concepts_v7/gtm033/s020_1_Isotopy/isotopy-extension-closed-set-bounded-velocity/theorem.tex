Let $A \subset M$ be a closed set and $U \subset M$ an open neighborhood of $A$. Let $F: U \times I \to M$ be an isotopy of $U$ having bounded velocity, such that $\widehat{F}(U \times I)$ is open in $M \times I$. Then there is a diffeotopy $G$ of $M$ having bounded velocity, which agrees with $F$ on a neighborhood of $A \times I$; and $\operatorname{Supp} G \subset F(U \times I)$.
