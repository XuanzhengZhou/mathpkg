Let $V$ and $M$ be $C^r$ manifolds. An isotopy of $V$ in $M$ is a $C^r$ map $F: V \times I \to M$ such that for each $t \in I$, the map $F_t: V \to M$, $F_t(x) = F(x, t)$, is an embedding. Two embeddings $f, g: V \hookrightarrow M$ are isotopic if there is an isotopy $F$ with $F_0 = f$ and $F_1 = g$.
