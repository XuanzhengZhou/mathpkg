The collaring theorem states that every boundary of a differentiable manifold admits a collar neighborhood diffeomorphic to $\partial M \times [0, \infty)$. This version proves the theorem by constructing an inward-pointing vector field near the boundary and using its flow to push the boundary into the interior, producing the collar embedding.
