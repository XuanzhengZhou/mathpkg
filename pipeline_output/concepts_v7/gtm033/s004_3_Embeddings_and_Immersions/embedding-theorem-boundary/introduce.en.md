Theorem 4.3 extends the Whitney embedding theorem to manifolds with boundary. Every $C^r$ $n$-dimensional $\partial$-manifold admits a neat $C^r$ embedding into a halfspace of $\mathbb{R}^{2n+1}$. The neatness condition ensures the boundary $\partial M$ is mapped into the boundary of the halfspace, preserving the $\partial$-manifold structure.
