Theorem 4.1 generalizes the regular value theorem to manifolds with boundary. If $y$ is a regular value for both the map $f: M \to N$ and its restriction to the boundary $f|_{\partial M}$, then the preimage $f^{-1}(y)$ is a neat submanifold of $M$. The neatness condition ensures the preimage meets $\partial M$ cleanly, with its own boundary equal to $f|_{\partial M}^{-1}(y)$.
