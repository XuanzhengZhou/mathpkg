The unit tangent bundle $T_1 M$ is the subbundle of the tangent bundle consisting of all tangent vectors of length one. It is a $C^{r-1}$ submanifold of $TM$ obtained as the inverse image of $1$ under the norm-squared map. In the context of the Whitney embedding theorem, $T_1 M$ serves as the domain for a map whose image dictates which projection directions respect the immersion condition.
