Theorem 1.1 asserts that the set of $C^r$ immersions from $M$ to $N$ is open in the strong topology. This means that any map sufficiently close (in the strong sense) to an immersion is also an immersion. The openness of immersions is a foundational fact that enables perturbation and approximation arguments throughout differential topology.
