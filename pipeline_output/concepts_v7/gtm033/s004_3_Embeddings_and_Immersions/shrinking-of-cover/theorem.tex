A cover $\mathcal{V} = \{V_i\}_{i \in \Lambda}$ is a **shrinking** of a cover $\mathcal{U} = \{U_i\}_{i \in \Lambda}$ if $\bar{V}_i \subset U_i$ for all $i \in \Lambda$, where $\bar{V}_i$ denotes the closure of $V_i$. A partition of unity subordinate to $\mathcal{U}$ yields a shrinking via $\{\operatorname{Int} \operatorname{Supp} \lambda_i\}_{i \in \Lambda}$.
