The map $\tau: T_1 M \to S^{q-1}$ sends each unit tangent vector at a point of $M$ to a parallel unit vector at the origin. For a linear projection along direction $v$ to be an immersion on $M$, the vector $v$ must avoid the image of $\tau$. Combined with the secant map $\sigma$, the images of $\tau$ and $\sigma$ together determine the "bad" projection directions.
