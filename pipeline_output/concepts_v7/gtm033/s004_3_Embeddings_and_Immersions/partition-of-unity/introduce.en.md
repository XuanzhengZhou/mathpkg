A $C^r$ partition of unity subordinate to an open cover $\mathcal{U}$ is a locally finite family of $C^r$ functions $\lambda_i: M \to [0,1]$ whose supports are contained in the corresponding $U_i$ and which sum to $1$ everywhere. Partitions of unity are the fundamental technical tool for gluing together locally defined constructions—such as Riemannian metrics, differential forms, or maps into $\mathbb{R}^n$—into global objects on a manifold.
