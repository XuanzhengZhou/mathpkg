A neat embedding is an embedding of a $\partial$-manifold $A$ into a $\partial$-manifold $M$ whose image is a neat submanifold. This means the image meets $\partial M$ exactly along the image of $\partial A$, and the embedding is transverse to the boundary. Neat embeddings are the morphisms that respect the boundary structure of $\partial$-manifolds.
