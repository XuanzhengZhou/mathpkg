Let $f: U \to \mathbb{R}^n$ be a $C^r$ map, where $U \subset \mathbb{R}^m$ is open. For any subset $K \subset U$, define the $C^r$ \textbf{norm} of $f$ on $K$ as
$$
\|f\|_{r, K} = \sup \left\{ \|D^k f(x)\| : x \in K,\; 0 \leqslant k \leqslant r \right\},
$$
where $\|D^k f(x)\|$ is the norm of the $k$-th derivative of $f$ at $x$, with $\|D^0 f(x)\| = |f(x)|$. The value $\infty$ is allowed. If $K$ is the entire domain of $f$, we write simply $\|f\|_r$.
