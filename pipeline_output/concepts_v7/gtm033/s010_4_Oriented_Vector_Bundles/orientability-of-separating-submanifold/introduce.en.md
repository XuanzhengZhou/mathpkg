Theorem 4.5 asserts that in a simply connected ambient manifold, a closed connected separating hypersurface inherits orientability from the ambient manifold. The proof combines Lemma 4.4 (which guarantees the normal bundle is trivial, hence orientable) with Lemma 4.1 on orientability in short exact sequences, applied to the exact sequence relating tangent bundles of the submanifold, the ambient manifold, and the normal bundle.
