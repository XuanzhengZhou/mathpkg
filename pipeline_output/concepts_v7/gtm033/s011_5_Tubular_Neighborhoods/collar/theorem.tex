Let $M$ be a $C^\infty$ manifold with boundary $\partial M$. A \text{collar} of $\partial M$ in $M$ is a diffeomorphism from $\partial M \times [0,\infty)$ onto a neighborhood of $\partial M$ in $M$ which is the identity on $\partial M \times 0$. Equivalently, it is a map of the form $h^{-1} \circ \varphi$ where $h = (r,g): W \to \partial M \times [0,\infty)$ maps a neighborhood $W \subset M$ of $\partial M$ diffeomorphically onto a neighborhood of $\partial M \times 0$, with $h(x) = (x,0)$ for $x \in \partial M$, $r: W \to \partial M$ a $C^\infty$ retraction, $g: W \to [0,\infty)$ having $0$ as a regular value and $g^{-1}(0) = \partial M$, and $\varphi: \partial M \times [0,\infty) \to h(W)$ an embedding fixing $\partial M \times 0$.
