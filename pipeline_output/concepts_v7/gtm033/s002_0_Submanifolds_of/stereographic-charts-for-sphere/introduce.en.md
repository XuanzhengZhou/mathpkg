Each open hemisphere $U_i$ of $S^n$ is paired with a coordinate map $\varphi_i$ that projects onto $\mathbb{R}^n$ by omitting one coordinate. These maps are homeomorphisms onto the open unit $n$-disk, and their inverses are analytic, providing the standard charts for the smooth structure of $S^n$.
