Real projective $n$-space $P^n$ is the $C^\omega$ manifold whose underlying topological space is the identification space of $S^n$ under the antipodal map, identifying $x \in S^n$ with $-x$. If $p: S^n \to P^n$ is the quotient map, the differential structure is defined by requiring $p$ to be a local $C^\omega$ diffeomorphism.
