A $C^r$ atlas is a collection of charts on a topological space whose transition maps are all $C^r$ diffeomorphisms between open subsets of Euclidean space. The class $r$ may be any natural number, $\infty$ for smooth, or $\omega$ for real analytic, encoding the degree of differentiability of the manifold structure.
