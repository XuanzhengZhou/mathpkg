The product of two $C^r$ manifolds is naturally a $C^r$ manifold whose charts are products of charts from each factor. The dimension adds: an $m$-manifold times an $n$-manifold yields an $(m+n)$-manifold. This construction is fundamental for building new manifolds from known ones.
