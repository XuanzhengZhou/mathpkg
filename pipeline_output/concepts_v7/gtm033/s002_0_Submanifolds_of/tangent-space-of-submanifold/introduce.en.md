The tangent space $M_x$ at a point $x$ of a submanifold $M$ is an $n$-dimensional vector space capturing all directions tangent to $M$ at $x$. Via a chart, it is linearly isomorphic to $\mathbb{R}^n$, and geometrically it is realized as an affine plane in the ambient Euclidean space, consisting of all velocity vectors of curves in $M$ through $x$.
