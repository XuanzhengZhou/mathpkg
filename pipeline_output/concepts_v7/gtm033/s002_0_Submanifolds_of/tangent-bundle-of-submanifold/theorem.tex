The \textit{tangent bundle} of a submanifold $M \subset \mathbb{R}^{n+k}$ is the union of all tangent spaces:

$$TM = \bigcup_{x \in M} M_x \subset M \times \mathbb{R}^{n+k} \subset \mathbb{R}^{n+k} \times \mathbb{R}^{n+k}.$$

The linear maps $Tf_x$ assemble to a map $Tf: TM \to TN$, which plays the role of the derivative of $f: M \to N$.
