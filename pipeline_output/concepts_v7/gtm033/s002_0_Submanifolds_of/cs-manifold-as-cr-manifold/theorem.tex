Let $\alpha$ be a $C^s$ differential structure on $M$ and let $r$ be an integer with $1 \leqslant r < s$. Then $\alpha$, regarded as a $C^r$ atlas, belongs to a unique $C^r$ differential structure on $M$ obtained by adding all charts having $C^r$ overlap with every chart in $\alpha$. In this way every $C^s$ manifold may be considered a $C^r$ manifold.
