A chart on $S^n$ consists of a coordinate neighborhood $U_i$ together with a homeomorphism $\varphi_i$ onto an open subset of $\mathbb{R}^n$. The collection of all $2n+2$ such charts forms an atlas, which provides the foundational language for defining differentiability on the sphere.
