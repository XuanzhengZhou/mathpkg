A differential structure $\Phi$ on $M$ is obtained by \textit{collation} of differential structures $\Phi_i$ on open sets $U_i$ covering $M$ if

$$\Phi_i|U_i \cap U_j = \Phi_j|U_i \cap U_j \quad \text{for all } i, j,$$

and $\Phi$ is the unique differential structure on $M$ containing each $\Phi_i$ as a subset.
