Lemma 4.4 characterizes the situation when a closed connected codimension-1 submanifold separates its ambient manifold. In this case the normal bundle must be trivial, the complement has exactly two components, and each component has the submanifold as its topological boundary. This lemma is a crucial step toward the Jordan-Brouwer type separation theorems in differential topology.
