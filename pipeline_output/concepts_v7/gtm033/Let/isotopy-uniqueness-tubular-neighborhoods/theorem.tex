Let $M \subset V$ be a submanifold, $\partial M = \partial V = \varnothing$. Then any two tubular neighborhoods of $M$ in $V$ are isotopic. That is, if $(f_0, \xi_0)$ and $(f_1, \xi_1)$ are tubular neighborhoods of $M$ in $V$, there exists an isotopy $F: E_0 \times I \rightarrow V$ such that $F_0 = f_0$, $F_1(E_0) = f_1(E_1)$, and $f_1^{-1} F_1: E_0 \rightarrow E_1$ is a vector bundle isomorphism $\xi_0 \rightarrow \xi_1$.
