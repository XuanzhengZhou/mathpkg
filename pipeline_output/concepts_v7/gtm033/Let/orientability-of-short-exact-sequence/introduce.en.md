Given a short exact sequence of vector bundles, orientations on any two terms canonically determine an orientation on the third via the direct sum of orientations on fibres. This lemma states that orientability of two terms is equivalent to orientability of the third, making it a powerful tool for deducing orientability in algebraic constructions involving vector bundles.
