Let $f: M \rightarrow [a,b]$ be an admissible Morse function on a compact manifold, of type $(v_0, \ldots, v_n)$. Assume that $f^{-1}(a) = \varnothing$. If $\beta_k$ denotes the dimension of $H_k(M; F)$ where $F$ is a field, then the inequalities (a) and equality (b) of Theorem 3.4 are valid. In particular the alternating sum of the type numbers equals the Euler characteristic of $M$:

$$\sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k v_k = \chi'(M).$$
