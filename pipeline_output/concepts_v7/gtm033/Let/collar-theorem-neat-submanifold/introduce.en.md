For a closed neat submanifold $M$ of $V$, a collar of the boundary $\partial V$ can be chosen to simultaneously restrict to a collar of $\partial M$ in $M$. This compatibility between the collar structures of the ambient manifold and the submanifold is a key technical ingredient in proving that neat submanifolds admit tubular neighborhoods.
