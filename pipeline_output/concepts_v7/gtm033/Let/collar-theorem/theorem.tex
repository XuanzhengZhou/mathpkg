\textbf{Theorem 6.1 (Collar Neighborhood Theorem).} Let $M$ be a $C^r$ manifold with boundary $\partial M$, $1 \leqslant r \leqslant \omega$. Then there exists a $C^r$ embedding

$$f: \partial M \times [0, 1) \rightarrow M$$

such that $f(x, 0) = x$ for all $x \in \partial M$, and $f(\partial M \times [0, 1))$ is an open neighborhood of $\partial M$ in $M$. Such an embedding (or its image) is called a \text{collar} of $\partial M$ in $M$.
