Theorem 3.3 generalizes Theorem 3.1 to the case where multiple critical points lie at the same critical value. When all critical points share a single critical value, the manifold can be built from the lower level set by attaching disjoint cells, one for each critical point, with the dimension of each cell matching the index of its corresponding critical point. This is the basis for constructing a CW-complex structure from a Morse function.
