The cotangent bundle $T^*M$ of a manifold $M$ is the dual bundle to the tangent bundle, with fibres $M_x^* = L(M_x, \mathbb{R})$ consisting of linear functionals on the tangent space. A smooth function $f: M \to \mathbb{R}$ induces a section $Df$ of the cotangent bundle via its differential at each point. Natural charts on $T^*M$ are induced from charts on $M$ using the dual of the derivative of the transition map.
