The homological Euler characteristic equals the Euler characteristic for a compact manifold without boundary. That is, if $M$ is a compact manifold without boundary, then

$$\chi'(M) = \chi(M)$$

where $\chi'(M)$ is the alternating sum of the Betti numbers and $\chi(M)$ is the sum of indices of zeros of any vector field on $M$ with finitely many zeros.
