Let $M$ and $N$ be $C^r$ manifolds, $1 \leqslant r \leqslant \infty$, with $\dim N \geqslant 2 \dim M + 1$.
\begin{itemize}
\item If $M$ is compact, then embeddings are dense in $C^r_S(M,N)$.
\item If $M$ is not compact, then embeddings are dense in $\operatorname{Prop}^r_S(M,N)$ (the space of proper $C^r$ maps with the strong topology).
\end{itemize}
