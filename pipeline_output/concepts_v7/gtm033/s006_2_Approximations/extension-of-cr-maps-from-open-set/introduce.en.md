Lemma 2.8 provides a tool for extending $C^r$ maps from an open subset to the whole manifold. Given a $C^r$ map $f$ on $U$ and a $C^r$-close approximation $g_0$ on an open subset $W$, one can patch $g_0$ with $f$ on the complement to obtain a global $C^r$ map $g$. The patching is continuous in the strong topology. This lemma is essential for the proof of Theorem 2.9, where it enables the inductive construction of compatible $C^s$ differential structures.
