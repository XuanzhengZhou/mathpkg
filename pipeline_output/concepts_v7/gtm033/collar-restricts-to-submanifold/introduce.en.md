When $M$ is a neat submanifold of $V$, a collar of $\partial V$ can be chosen so that it restricts to a collar of $\partial M$ in $M$. This compatibility of collars is essential for the construction of tubular neighborhoods for neat submanifolds, ensuring that the boundary geometry is properly aligned.
