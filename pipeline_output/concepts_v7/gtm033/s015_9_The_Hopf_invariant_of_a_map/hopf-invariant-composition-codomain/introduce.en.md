This result describes how the Hopf invariant behaves under post-composition with a self-map of the codomain sphere $S^2$. The Hopf invariant scales quadratically by the degree $q$ of the post-composing map $h$. This quadratic dependence reflects the fact that the linking number of two curves in $S^3$ changes by a factor of $q^2$ when the target $S^2$ is mapped by a degree-$q$ map.
