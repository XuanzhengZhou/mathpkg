Theorem 2.10 is a fundamental result in obstruction theory: the Euler number $\chi(\xi)$ is the primary obstruction to the existence of a global nonvanishing section of an oriented $n$-plane bundle over a compact $n$-manifold. The proof uses Lemma 2.9 to remove zeros when the sum of indices vanishes, yielding a nowhere-zero section.
