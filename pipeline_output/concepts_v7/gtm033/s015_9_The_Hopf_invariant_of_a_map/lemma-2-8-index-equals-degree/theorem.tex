Let $D \subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$ be an $n$-disk with center $x$. Let $U \subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$ be an open set containing $D$ and $f \colon U \to \mathbb{R}^{n}$ a $C^{\infty}$ map, considered as a vector field on $U$. Suppose $0$ is a regular value and $x \in D \cap f^{-1}(0)$. Define a map
$$
g \colon \partial D \to S^{\,n-1}, \qquad g(y) = \frac{f(y)}{|f(y)|}.
$$
Then
$$
\deg g = \operatorname{Ind}_{x} f.
$$
