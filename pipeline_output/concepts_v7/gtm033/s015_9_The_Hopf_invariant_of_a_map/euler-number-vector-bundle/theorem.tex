Let $\xi = (p, E, M)$ be an $n$-dimensional oriented vector bundle over a connected, compact, $n$-dimensional manifold $M$ without boundary, where $M$ is identified with the zero section. The \textbf{Euler number} of $\xi$ is defined to be
$$
\chi(\xi) = \#(M, M) = \#(M, M; E),
$$
the self-intersection number of the zero section inside the total space $E$.
