Let $\xi_{i} = (p_{i}, E_{i}, M_{i})$ for $i = 0, 1$ be oriented $n$-plane bundles over compact oriented $n$-manifolds without boundary. Define the product bundle
$$
\xi_{0} \times \xi_{1} = (p_{0} \times p_{1},\; E_{0} \times E_{1},\; M_{0} \times M_{1}).
$$
Then
$$
\chi(\xi_{0} \times \xi_{1}) = \chi(\xi_{0}) \cdot \chi(\xi_{1}).
$$
