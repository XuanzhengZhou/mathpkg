Theorem 2.5 states that the Euler characteristic is multiplicative with respect to Cartesian products: $\chi(M \times N) = \chi(M) \chi(N)$. This formula is a fundamental property of the Euler characteristic and reflects the fact that the tangent bundle of a product splits as the direct sum of the pullbacks of the individual tangent bundles.
