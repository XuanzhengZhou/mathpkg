Lemma 2.9 provides a necessary and sufficient condition for extending a given boundary map to a nowhere-vanishing map on the interior: the sum of the indices at all interior zeros must vanish. This is a key technical lemma used in the proof that a vector bundle has a nonvanishing section if and only if its Euler number is zero.
