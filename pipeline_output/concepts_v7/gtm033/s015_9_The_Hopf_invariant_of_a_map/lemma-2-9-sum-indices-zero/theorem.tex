Let $W \subset \mathbb{R}^{n}$ be a connected compact $n$-dimensional submanifold and $f \colon W \to \mathbb{R}^{n}$ a $C^{\infty}$ map. Assume $f^{-1}(0)$ is a finite subset of $W \setminus \partial W$. Then
$$
\sum_{x \in f^{-1}(0)} \operatorname{Ind}_{x} f = 0
$$
if and only if there exists a map $g \colon W \to \mathbb{R}^{n} \setminus \{0\}$ which equals $f$ on $\partial W$.
