The index of a section $f \colon M \to TM$ at a zero $x$ is the sign ($\pm 1$) of the determinant of the induced automorphism $\Phi_x$ on the tangent space. This algebraic count of zeros recovers the Euler characteristic when summed over all zeros of a section transverse to the zero section. When $M$ is oriented, $\chi_f(M)$ equals $\chi(M)$ independently of the choice of $f$.
