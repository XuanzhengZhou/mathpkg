Let $f \colon M \to W$ be a $C^{\infty}$ map transverse to a closed submanifold $N \subset W$ with complementary dimensions. A point $x \in f^{-1}(N)$ has \textbf{positive type} or \textbf{negative type} according as the composite linear isomorphism
$$
M_{x} \xrightarrow{Tf} W_{y} \to W_{y} / N_{y}, \qquad y = f(x)
$$
preserves or reverses orientation. Write $\#_{x}(f, N) = 1$ or $-1$ respectively. The \textbf{intersection number} of $(f, N)$ is the integer
$$
\#(f, N) = \sum_{x \in f^{-1}(N)} \#_{x}(f, N).
$$
