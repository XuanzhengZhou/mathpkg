Corollary 2.4 follows immediately from Theorem 2.3: since $\chi(S^{2n}) = 2 \neq 0$, every continuous vector field on an even-dimensional sphere must have at least one zero. This is the classical "hairy ball theorem" for $S^2$ generalized to all even-dimensional spheres.
