Let $M, N, P$ be $C^{r+1}$ manifolds and $f: M \to N$, $g: N \to P$ be $C^{r+1}$ maps. Then:
$$T(g \circ f) = (Tg) \circ (Tf),$$
$$T(1_M) = 1_{TM}.$$
The diagram
$$f \circ p_M = p_N \circ Tf$$
commutes. These properties may be summarized by saying that the assignments $M \mapsto TM$, $f \mapsto Tf$ define a covariant functor $T$ from the category of $C^{r+1}$ manifolds to the category of $C^r$ manifolds.
