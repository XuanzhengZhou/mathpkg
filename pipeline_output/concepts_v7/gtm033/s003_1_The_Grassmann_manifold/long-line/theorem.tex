Let $M$ be the set consisting of the first uncountable ordinal $\omega_1$ together with a copy of the open interval $(0,1)$ between each ordinal $\alpha$ and its successor $\alpha+1$. Endow $M$ with the order topology. Then $M$ is a $1$-manifold which is Hausdorff but not paracompact, called the long line. $M$ has a $C^\omega$ differential structure but no Riemannian metric.
