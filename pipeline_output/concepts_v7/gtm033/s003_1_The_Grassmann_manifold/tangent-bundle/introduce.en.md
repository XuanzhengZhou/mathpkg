The tangent bundle $TM$ of a $C^{r+1}$ manifold $M$ is the set of all tangent vectors endowed with a topology and $C^r$ manifold structure via the charts $T\varphi_i$. The natural projection $p: TM \to M$ maps each tangent vector to its base point, with fibers $M_x = p^{-1}(x)$ being $n$-dimensional vector spaces. For an open subset of Euclidean space, the tangent bundle is simply the product $W \times \mathbb{R}^n$, and the general construction makes $TM$ locally trivial.
