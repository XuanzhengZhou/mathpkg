The tangent space $M_x$ at a point $x$ on a manifold $M$ is the fiber of the tangent bundle $TM$ over $x$, consisting of all tangent vectors based at $x$. The restriction $T_x f: M_x \to N_{f(x)}$ of the tangent map $Tf$ gives a linear map between tangent spaces, which in natural charts is exactly the Jacobian derivative of the local coordinate representation of $f$. This linear map captures the first-order behavior of $f$ near $x$.
