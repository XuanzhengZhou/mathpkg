A submersion is a $C^1$ map between manifolds whose derivative is surjective at every point. This "infinitesimal" condition means that locally the map looks like a linear projection $\mathbb{R}^m \to \mathbb{R}^n$ with $m \geq n$. By the regular value theorem, the preimage of any point under a submersion is a submanifold. Submersions are the dual concept to immersions and are essential for defining fibrations and fiber bundles.
