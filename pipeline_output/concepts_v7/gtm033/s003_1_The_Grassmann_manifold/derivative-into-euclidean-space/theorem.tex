If $U \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ is open and $f: M \to U$ is $C^1$, for each $x \in M$ we define the linear map
$$Df_x = Df(x): M_x \to \mathbb{R}^n$$
to be the composition
$$M_x \xrightarrow{Tf} TU = U \times \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}^n$$
where the second map is the projection onto the second factor.
