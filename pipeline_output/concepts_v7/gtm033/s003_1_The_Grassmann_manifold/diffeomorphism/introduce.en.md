A $C^r$ diffeomorphism is an isomorphism in the category of $C^r$ manifolds: a $C^r$ map that is bijective with a $C^r$ inverse. Diffeomorphic manifolds are considered equivalent in differential topology. A central theorem (proved in Chapter 3) states that for $1 \leq r < s \leq \infty$, every $C^r$ manifold is $C^r$ diffeomorphic to a $C^s$ manifold, so the differential category is essentially independent of the differentiability class $r \geq 1$.
