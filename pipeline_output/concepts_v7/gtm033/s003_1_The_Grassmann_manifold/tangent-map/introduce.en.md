The tangent map $Tf: TM \to TN$ (also called the derivative or pushforward) is the global version of the derivative of a $C^{r+1}$ map $f: M \to N$ between manifolds. It is defined via local representations using natural charts on the tangent bundles and is independent of the choice of charts. The tangent map restricts fiberwise to linear maps $T_x f: M_x \to N_{f(x)}$ between tangent spaces, generalizing the Jacobian of a map between Euclidean spaces.
