Let $\xi$ be a $k$-dimensional $C^r$ vector bundle over a manifold $M$, $0 \leqslant r \leqslant \infty$. Let $U \subset M$ be a neighborhood of a closed set $A \subset M$. Suppose that
$$F: \xi|_U \to U \times \mathbb{R}^s$$
is a $C^r$ monomorphism (of vector bundles) over $\mathbf{1}_U$. If $s \geqslant k + \dim M$, then there is a $C^r$ monomorphism $\xi \to M \times \mathbb{R}^s$ over $\mathbf{1}_M$ which agrees with $F$ over some neighborhood of $A$ in $U$.
