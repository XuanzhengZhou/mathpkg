This theorem generalizes the classification of vector bundles from manifolds to any space $X$ having the homotopy type of a finite-dimensional CW or simplicial complex. The natural map $\Theta_X$ sends a $k$-plane bundle to the homotopy class of its classifying map into the Grassmannian $G_{s,k}$. When $\dim X < s - k$, this correspondence is a bijection, meaning isomorphism classes of $k$-plane bundles over $X$ are in one-to-one correspondence with homotopy classes $[X, G_{s,k}]$.
