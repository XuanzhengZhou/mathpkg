A $C^r$ monomorphism $F: \xi \to M \times \mathbb{R}^s$ over $\mathbf{1}_M$ determines a classifying map $f_\xi: M \to G_{s,k}$ such that $\xi \cong f_\xi^* \gamma_{s,k}$, where $\gamma_{s,k}$ is the universal $k$-plane bundle over the Grassmann manifold $G_{s,k}$. If $s > k + \dim M$, then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Every $C^r$ $k$-plane bundle $\xi$ over $M$ admits such a classifying map $f_\xi: M \to G_{s,k}$.
\item Two bundles $\xi, \eta$ over $M$ are $C^r$ isomorphic if and only if their classifying maps are homotopic: $\xi \cong_r \eta \iff f_\xi \simeq f_\eta$.
\end{enumerate}
Thus the correspondence $\xi \mapsto [f_\xi]$ gives a bijection between isomorphism classes of $k$-plane bundles over $M$ and homotopy classes of maps $M \to G_{s,k}$.
