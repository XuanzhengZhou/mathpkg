Let $B_1, \ldots, B_k \subset D^2 - \partial D^2$ be disjoint embedded disks. Define

$$H_k = D^2 - \bigcup_{i=1}^{k} \text{Int}\, B_i,$$

and $H_0 = D^2$. A \text{disk with $k$ holes} is a surface diffeomorphic to $H_k$, i.e., an orientable model surface of genus $0$ with $k + 1$ boundary circles. Its Euler characteristic is $\chi(H_k) = 1 - k$.
