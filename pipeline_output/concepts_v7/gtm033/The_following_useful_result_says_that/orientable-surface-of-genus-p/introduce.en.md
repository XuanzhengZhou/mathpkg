An orientable surface has genus $p$ if it can be built from the 2-sphere by attaching $p$ handles via orientable embeddings. Induction shows that such a surface is compact, connected, and reversible, with Euler characteristic $2 - 2p$. Surfaces of different genera are not diffeomorphic.
