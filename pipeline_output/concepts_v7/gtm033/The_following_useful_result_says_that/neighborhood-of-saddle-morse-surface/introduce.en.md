This lemma analyzes the topology of a neighborhood of a saddle critical point in a surface. Depending on how the four branches of the critical level set connect, the neighborhood is either an orientable pair-of-pants (three boundary components) or a nonorientable surface with two boundary components. The analysis relies on how the ascending and descending manifolds intersect the boundary of the neighborhood.
