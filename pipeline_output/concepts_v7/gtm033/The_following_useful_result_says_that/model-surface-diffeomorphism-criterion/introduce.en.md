The diffeomorphism type of a model surface is completely determined by three invariants: genus, number of boundary components, and orientability. The Euler characteristic combines genus and boundary count: $\chi = 2-2g-b$ for orientable surfaces and $\chi = 2-g-b$ for nonorientable surfaces.
