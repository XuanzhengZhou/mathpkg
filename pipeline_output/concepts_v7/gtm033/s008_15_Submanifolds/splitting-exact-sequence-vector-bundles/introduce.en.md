Theorem 2.2 states that over a paracompact base, every short exact sequence of vector bundles splits. The proof uses local left inverses (obtained from the fact that a monomorphism's image is a subbundle) glued together via a partition of unity. This theorem is one of the most useful facts about vector bundles: it guarantees that subbundles are always direct summands, and that every epimorphism admits a section.
