Every short exact sequence of $C^r$ vector bundles splits, $0 \leqslant r \leqslant \infty$, provided the base space is paracompact.

Equivalently: if $F: \xi \to \eta$ is a monomorphism over $1_B$, then $F$ has a left inverse; and if $G: \eta \to \zeta$ is an epimorphism over $1_B$, then $G$ has a right inverse.
