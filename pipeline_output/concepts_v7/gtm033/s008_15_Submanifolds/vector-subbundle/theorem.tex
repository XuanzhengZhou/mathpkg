A \textbf{subbundle} of a bundle $\xi = (p, E, B)$ is a bundle $\xi_0 = (p_0, E_0, B)$ such that $E_0 \subset E$, $p_0 = p|E_0$, and for each $x \in B$ the fibre $\xi_{0x}$ is a linear subspace of $\xi_x$.

If $F: \xi \to \xi'$ is a monomorphism covering $1_B$, then the image of $F$ is a subbundle of $\xi'$. Dually, if $F: \xi \to \xi'$ is an epimorphism covering $1_B$, then for each $x \in B$ the kernel of $F_x: \xi_x \to \xi_x'$ defines a unique subbundle of $\xi$.
