A subbundle is a vector bundle whose total space and fibres are subsets of another vector bundle, with the induced projection. Subbundles arise naturally as images of monomorphisms and kernels of epimorphisms over the identity base map. Together with quotient bundles, they form the basic building blocks for exact sequences of vector bundles.
