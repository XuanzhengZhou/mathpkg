Theorem 2.4, a direct corollary of the covering homotopy theorem, states that homotopic maps induce isomorphic pullback bundles over a paracompact base. This is the fundamental reason why isomorphism classes of vector bundles over a space $M$ correspond to homotopy classes of maps from $M$ into a classifying space, forming the basis of characteristic class theory.
