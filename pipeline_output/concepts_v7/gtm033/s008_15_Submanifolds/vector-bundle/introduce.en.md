A vector bundle is a family of disjoint vector spaces continuously parameterized by a base space, such that locally the total space is a product with $\mathbb{R}^n$. Vector bundles generalize the tangent bundle of a manifold and form a mixed topological-algebraic structure. Many deep invariants of a manifold are intimately linked to the tangent bundle, and their development requires a general theory of vector bundles.
