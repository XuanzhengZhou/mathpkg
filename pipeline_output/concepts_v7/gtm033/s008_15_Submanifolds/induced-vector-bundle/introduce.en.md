The induced bundle or pullback $f^*\xi$ is the vector bundle obtained by pulling $\xi$ back along a continuous map $f$, with fibres $f^*\xi_x = \xi_{f(x)}$. The pullback satisfies a universal property: any vector bundle map over $f$ factors uniquely through the natural map $f^*\xi \to \xi$. This construction is central to the classification theorem for vector bundles, which identifies isomorphism classes of bundles with homotopy classes of maps into a classifying space.
