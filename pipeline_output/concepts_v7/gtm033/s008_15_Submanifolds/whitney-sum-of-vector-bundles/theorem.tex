The \textbf{Whitney sum} (or \textbf{direct sum}) of bundles $\xi$, $\zeta$ over $B$ is the bundle $\xi \oplus \zeta$ whose fibre over $x$ is $\xi_x \oplus \zeta_x$. If $\varphi$, $\psi$ are charts for $\xi$, $\zeta$ respectively over $U$, a chart $\theta$ for $\xi \oplus \zeta$ over $U$ is obtained by setting
$$\theta_x = \varphi_x \oplus \psi_x: \xi_x \oplus \zeta_x \to \mathbb{R}^m \oplus \mathbb{R}^n.$$

The natural exact sequences of vector spaces
$$0 \to \xi_x \xrightarrow{F_x} \xi_x \oplus \zeta_x \xrightarrow{G_x} \zeta_x \to 0$$
fit together to give a split exact sequence
$$0 \to \xi \xrightarrow{F} \xi \oplus \zeta \xrightarrow{G} \zeta \to 0.$$
