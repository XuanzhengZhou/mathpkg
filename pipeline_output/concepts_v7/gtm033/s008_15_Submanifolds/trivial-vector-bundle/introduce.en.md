The trivial vector bundle $\varepsilon_B^n = B \times \mathbb{R}^n$ is the simplest possible vector bundle, where the total space is simply a product of the base and a fixed vector space. All vector bundles are locally trivial by definition, meaning they locally resemble this product structure. A trivialization is an explicit isomorphism identifying a bundle with the trivial bundle.
