The Morse inequalities relate the number $v_k$ of critical points of index $k$ to the Betti numbers $eta_k$. The alternating sum of the $v_k$ equals the Euler characteristic. Moreover, each partial alternating sum of the $v_k$ is at least the corresponding sum of the $eta_k$, meaning there must be at least as many critical points of each index as there are independent homology classes.
