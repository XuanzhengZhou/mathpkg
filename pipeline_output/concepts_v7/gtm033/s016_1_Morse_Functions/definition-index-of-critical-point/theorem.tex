Let $x$ be a nondegenerate critical point of $f: M \to \mathbb{R}$. The index of $f$ at $x$, denoted $\operatorname{Ind}_x f$, is the maximal dimension of a subspace of $M_x$ on which the Hessian $H_x f$ is negative definite. Equivalently, it is the number of negative eigenvalues of the Hessian matrix in any coordinate system.
