The cotangent bundle is the dual vector bundle to the tangent bundle. Its fibre at $x \in M$ is the dual space $M_x^*$ of linear maps $M_x 	o \mathbb{R}$. The differential $Df$ of a real-valued function $f: M 	o \mathbb{R}$ is a section of the cotangent bundle.
