Lemma 3.2 asserts that smooth ($C^\infty$) maps between open subsets of halfspaces are dense in the strong $C^r$ topology. The lemma also covers the relative case for maps preserving boundaries, i.e., maps sending $\partial U$ into $\partial V$. This result extends the standard convolution approximation to the setting of $\partial$-manifolds and manifold pairs, forming a key technical step toward the smoothing theorems for manifold pairs.
