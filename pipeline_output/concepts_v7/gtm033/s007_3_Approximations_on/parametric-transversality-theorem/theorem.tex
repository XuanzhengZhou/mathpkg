Let $F: V \times M \to N$ be a $C^r$ map, $1 \leqslant r \leqslant \infty$, and $A \subset N$ a $C^r$ submanifold. Define
$$\phi(F; A) = \{v \in V : F_v \pitchfork A\}$$
where $F_v(x) = F(v,x)$. If $F \pitchfork A$, then $\phi(F; A)$ is residual and therefore dense. If $A$ is closed in $N$ and $F$ is continuous for the strong topology on $C^r(M,N)$, then $\phi(F; A)$ is also open.
