Let $(M,M_0)$ be a $C^r$ manifold pair. If $0 < r < s \leqslant \infty$ then $(M,M_0)$ has a compatible $C^s$ structure (that is, $(M,M_0)$ is $C^r$ diffeomorphic to a $C^s$ manifold pair). If also $M_0$ is closed in $M$, and $M_0 \subset M - \partial M$ or $M_0 \subset \partial M$ or $M_0$ is neat, then the compatible $C^s$ structure is unique up to $C^r$ diffeomorphism of manifold pairs.
