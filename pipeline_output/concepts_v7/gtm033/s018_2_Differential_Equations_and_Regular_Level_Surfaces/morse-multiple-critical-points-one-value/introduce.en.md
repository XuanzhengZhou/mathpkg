Theorem 3.3 generalizes Theorem 3.1 to the case where multiple critical points share the same critical value. It states that $M$ deformation retracts onto $f^{-1}(a)$ with $v_k$ disjoint $k$-cells attached (one per critical point of index $k$). This provides the complete cell decomposition associated to a single critical level of an admissible Morse function.
