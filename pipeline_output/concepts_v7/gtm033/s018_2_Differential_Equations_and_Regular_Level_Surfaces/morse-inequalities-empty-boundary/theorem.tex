Let $f: M \rightarrow [a,b]$ be an admissible Morse function on a compact manifold, of type $(v_0, \ldots, v_n)$. Assume that $f^{-1}(a) = \emptyset$. If $\beta_k$ denotes the dimension of $H_k(M;F)$ where $F$ is a field then parts (a) and (b) of Theorem 3.4 are valid:
\begin{align*}
\text{(a)}\quad &\sum_{k=0}^{m} (-1)^{k+m} v_k \geqslant \sum_{k=0}^{m} (-1)^{k+m} \beta_k \quad \text{for } 0 \leqslant m \leqslant n; \\
\text{(b)}\quad &\sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k v_k = \sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k \beta_k = \chi'(M).
\end{align*}
