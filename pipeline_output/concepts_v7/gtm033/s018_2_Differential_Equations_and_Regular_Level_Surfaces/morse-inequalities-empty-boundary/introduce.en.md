Theorem 3.5 is the specialization of the Morse inequalities to the case $f^{-1}(a) = \emptyset$, i.e., when the manifold has no lower boundary component. In this case the relative homology groups become absolute homology groups $H_k(M;F)$, and the alternating sum of type numbers equals the homological Euler characteristic $\chi'(M)$ of the manifold itself.
