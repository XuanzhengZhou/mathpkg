When two neat submanifolds $M_0$ and $M_1$ of $V$ intersect transversely (i.e., are in general position), their intersection $M_0 \cap M_1$ inherits tubular neighborhood data from each $M_i$. This proposition states that the Whitney sum of the normal bundles of $M_0 \cap M_1$ inside each $M_i$ yields a tubular neighborhood of $M_0 \cap M_1$ directly inside the ambient manifold $V$, extending the individual tubular neighborhoods.
