A bounded system of generators is a subset of a free group where all elements have uniformly bounded word length. The proof that every subgroup generated by a bounded enumerable subset is benign uses the fact that all enumerable sets are Diophantine (Matiyasevich's theorem) to encode the generator relations into integer equations, which are then simulated by group-theoretic constructions.
