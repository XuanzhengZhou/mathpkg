Schanuel's conjecture is a major open problem in transcendental number theory, generalizing the Lindemann-Weierstrass theorem. Model-theoretically, it is precisely the condition that the complex exponential field satisfies the Hrushovski inequality. Zilber showed this inequality, together with existential closedness, yields a categorical theory ACFExp, whose unique model of cardinality continuum is conjecturally the complex exponential field.
