Let $\mathbf{A}_i = (A_i, L)$, $i \in I$, be a family of $L$-structures, $U$ an ultrafilter on $I$, and $\prod_{i \in I} \mathbf{A}_i / U$ the ultraproduct along $U$. For every $L$-formula $P(x_1, \ldots, x_n)$ with free variables $x_1, \ldots, x_n$ and $\varphi_1, \ldots, \varphi_n \in \prod_{i \in I} A_i$,
$$\prod_{i \in I} \mathbf{A}_i / U \vDash P(\tilde{\varphi}_1, \ldots, \tilde{\varphi}_n) \text{ iff } \{i \in I : \mathbf{A}_i \vDash P(\varphi_1(i), \ldots, \varphi_n(i))\} \in U,$$
where $\tilde{\varphi}$ denotes the equivalence class of $\varphi$ in the ultraproduct.
