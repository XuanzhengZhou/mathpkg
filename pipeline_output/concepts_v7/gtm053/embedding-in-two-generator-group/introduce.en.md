This theorem shows that the number of generators is not a meaningful invariant for countable groups. The proof embeds the countable free group into the free product $\mathbf{Z} * \mathbf{Z}$ (which has two generators) and then uses HNN extensions to handle relations. A recursive version ensures effectiveness for groups with computable presentations.
