The constructible universe L is Godel's inner model of set theory, built by a predicative hierarchy. It is the minimal inner model containing all ordinals and satisfying ZFC. The fact that GCH holds in L shows that GCH cannot be disproved from ZFC. The study of L led to the development of fine structure theory (Jensen) and inner model theory for large cardinals.
