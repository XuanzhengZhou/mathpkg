The theory RCF is the first-order theory of the real numbers as an ordered field. It is complete and admits quantifier elimination (Tarski-Seidenberg theorem). The definable sets in RCF are precisely the semialgebraic sets. RCF is o-minimal, a property that enables a rich geometric structure theory.
