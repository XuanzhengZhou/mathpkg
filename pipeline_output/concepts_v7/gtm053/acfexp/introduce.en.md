ACFExp is a model-theoretic construction by Zilber that produces an algebraically closed field with a well-behaved exponential function. The axioms encode Schanuel's conjecture and existential closedness. Zilber proved ACFExp is categorical in uncountable powers and carries a homogeneous pregeometry. The conjecture that the unique model of cardinality continuum is isomorphic to the complex numbers with the usual exponential function would imply Schanuel's conjecture.
