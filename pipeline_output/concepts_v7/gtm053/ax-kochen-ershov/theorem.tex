The theory $T\mathbf{Q}_p$ of $p$-adically closed fields (the complete theory of $\mathbf{Q}_p$ in the language of valued fields) is complete, decidable, and admits quantifier elimination in the Macintyre language $L_{\mathrm{valf}}^{\mathrm{Mac}}$, which extends the valued field language by countably many unary predicates $P_n$ ($n \geq 2$) defined by
$$\forall x [P_n(x) \leftrightarrow \exists y (y^n = x)],$$
singling out the $n$th powers in the field.
