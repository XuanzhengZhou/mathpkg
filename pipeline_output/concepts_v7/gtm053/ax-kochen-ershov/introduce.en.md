The Ax-Kochen-Ershov theorem is a landmark result establishing the model-theoretic tameness of $p$-adic fields, analogous to Tarski's theorem for the reals. The Macintyre predicates $P_n$ (singling out $n$th powers) are essential for quantifier elimination, much as the ordering is essential for RCF. The theorem has applications to $p$-adic integration (Denef, Igusa) and rationality of Poincaré series.
