The Stone space of theories topologizes the class of models modulo elementary equivalence. Each sentence defines a basic clopen set, and the compactness theorem translates to topological compactness. In a compact Hausdorff space, every ultrafilter has a unique limit, given by the ultraproduct construction via Loś's theorem.
