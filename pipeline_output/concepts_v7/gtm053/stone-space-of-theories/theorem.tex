Let $\mathcal{S}$ be the set of all $L$-structures of bounded cardinality (e.g., $\leq |L| + \aleph_0$) modulo elementary equivalence. For each $L$-sentence $P$, define
$$[P] = \{ \mathbf{A} \in \mathcal{S} : \mathbf{A} \vDash P \}.$$
The topology on $\mathcal{S}$ with open basis $\{[P]\}$ makes $\mathcal{S}$ a compact topological space. The compactness theorem is equivalent to the statement: The topological space $\mathcal{S}$ of $L$-structures is compact.
