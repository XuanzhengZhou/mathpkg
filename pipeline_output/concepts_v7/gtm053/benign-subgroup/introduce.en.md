Benign subgroups provide the key reduction for Higman's embedding theorem. The proof that all recursively enumerable subgroups of a free group are benign (via bounded systems of generators and Diophantine representations) is a tour de force combining combinatorial group theory, number theory (Matiyasevich's theorem), and recursion theory.
