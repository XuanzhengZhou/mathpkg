The special axioms of $L_1\mathrm{Ar}$ (first-order Peano arithmetic) are:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(a)] Axioms of equality.
\item[(b)] Axioms of addition: $x + 0 = x$; $x + y = y + x$; $(x + y) + z = x + (y + z)$; $x + z = y + z \Rightarrow x = y$.
\item[(c)] Axioms of multiplication: $x \cdot 0 = 0$; $x \cdot 1 = x$; $x \cdot y = y \cdot x$; $(x \cdot y) \cdot z = x \cdot (y \cdot z)$.
\item[(d)] Distributivity: $x \cdot (y + z) = x \cdot y + x \cdot z$.
\item[(e)] Induction schema: for each formula $P$ with one free variable,
$$P(0) \wedge \forall x(P(x) \Rightarrow P(x + 1)) \Rightarrow \forall x P(x).$$
\end{enumerate}
