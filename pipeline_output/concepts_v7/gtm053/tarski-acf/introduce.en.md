Tarski's theorem is a cornerstone of model-theoretic algebra. Completeness means every sentence true in one algebraically closed field of characteristic $p$ is true in all such fields. Quantifier elimination means every definable set is a Boolean combination of zero-sets of polynomials (constructible sets, Chevalley's theorem).
