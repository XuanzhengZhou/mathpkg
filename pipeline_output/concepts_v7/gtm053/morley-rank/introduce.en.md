Morley rank is a model-theoretic dimension notion for definable sets in $\aleph_0$-stable theories. It generalizes the concept of dimension in algebraic geometry: for ACF, Morley rank equals the Zariski dimension of constructible sets. The additivity property of Morley rank is a key tool in geometrical stability theory.
