In the class $\mathcal{H}_\delta$, a \text{strong embedding} $A \leq_\delta B$ means $A \subseteq B$ and for every finite $X \subseteq A$,
$$\min\{\delta(Y) : X \subseteq Y \subseteq B \text{ finite}\} = \delta(X).$$
Equivalently, the predimension of any finite subset of $A$ does not decrease when computed in the larger structure $B$.
