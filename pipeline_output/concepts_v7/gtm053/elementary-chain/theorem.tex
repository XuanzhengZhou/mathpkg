Let, for an ordinal $\kappa$,
$$A_0 \preceq A_1 \preceq \cdots \preceq A_\alpha \preceq \cdots \quad (\alpha < \kappa)$$
be a $\kappa$-sequence of $L$-structures forming an elementary chain. For limit ordinals $\delta \leq \kappa$, define $A_\delta$ with domain $\bigcup_{\alpha < \delta} A_\alpha$ and the natural induced structure. Then $A_\alpha \preceq A_\delta$ for all $\alpha < \delta \leq \kappa$.
