The continuum hypothesis, posed by Cantor in 1878, asks whether there is a cardinal strictly between the countable and the continuum. Godel (1938) proved CH cannot be disproved from ZFC (by constructing the constructible universe L). Cohen (1963) proved CH cannot be proved from ZFC (by the method of forcing). Thus CH is independent of the standard axioms of set theory.
