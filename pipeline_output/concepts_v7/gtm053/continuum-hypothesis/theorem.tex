The \text{Continuum Hypothesis} (CH) asserts:
$$2^{\aleph_0} = \aleph_1.$$
Equivalently, every uncountable subset of the real numbers has the cardinality of the continuum. The \text{Generalized Continuum Hypothesis} (GCH) asserts that for every infinite cardinal $\kappa$:
$$2^{\kappa} = \kappa^+,$$
where $\kappa^+$ is the successor cardinal of $\kappa$.
