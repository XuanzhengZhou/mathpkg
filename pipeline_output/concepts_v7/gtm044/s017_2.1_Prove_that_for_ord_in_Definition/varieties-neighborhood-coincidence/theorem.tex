Let $V_1$ and $V_2$ be varieties in $\mathbb{P}^n(\mathbb{C})$ or in $\mathbb{C}^n$, each of whose irreducible components contains a given point $P$. If there exists an open neighborhood $U$ of $P$ in $\mathbb{P}^n(\mathbb{C})$ or $\mathbb{C}^n$ such that
$$V_1 \cap U = V_2 \cap U,$$
then $V_1 = V_2$.
