This proposition states that the extension map from the ideal lattice $\mathcal{I}(R)$ to $\mathcal{I}(R_{\mathfrak{p}})$ preserves finite intersections. The proof reduces to the case of irreducible ideals using primary decomposition, employs Theorem 3.10 for irreducibles contained in $\mathfrak{p}$, and uses the fact that irreducibles not contained in $\mathfrak{p}$ extend to the whole ring $R_{\mathfrak{p}}$.
