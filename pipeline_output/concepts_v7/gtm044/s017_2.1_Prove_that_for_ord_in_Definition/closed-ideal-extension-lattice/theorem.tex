The extension map $(\cdot)^e : \mathcal{J}(R) \to \mathcal{J}(R_{\mathfrak{p}})$ on closed ideals preserves both sums and intersections. That is, for any closed ideals $\mathfrak{a}, \mathfrak{b} \in \mathcal{J}(R)$:
\begin{align*}
(\mathfrak{a} \cap \mathfrak{b})^e &= \mathfrak{a}^e \cap \mathfrak{b}^e, \\
\sqrt{\mathfrak{a} + \mathfrak{b}}^{\,e} &= \sqrt{\mathfrak{a}^e + \mathfrak{b}^e}.
\end{align*}
