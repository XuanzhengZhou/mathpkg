Let $R$ be the coordinate ring of an affine variety and let $\mathfrak{p} \subset R$ be a prime ideal. Then for any irreducible ideal $\mathfrak{q} \subset \mathfrak{p}$,
$$\mathfrak{q} = \mathfrak{q}^{ec},$$
where $(\cdot)^e$ and $(\cdot)^c$ denote extension to $R_{\mathfrak{p}}$ and contraction back to $R$, respectively.
