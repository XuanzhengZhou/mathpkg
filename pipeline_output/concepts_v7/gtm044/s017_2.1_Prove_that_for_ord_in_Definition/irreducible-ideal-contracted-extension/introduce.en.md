Theorem 3.10 states that for an irreducible ideal $\mathfrak{q}$ contained in the prime ideal $\mathfrak{p}$, the extension-contraction operation $\mathfrak{q} \mapsto \mathfrak{q}^{ec}$ is the identity. This is a crucial technical result used to prove that extension preserves intersections of ideals (equation 13) and to establish the ideal correspondence between $R$ and its localization $R_{\mathfrak{p}}$.
