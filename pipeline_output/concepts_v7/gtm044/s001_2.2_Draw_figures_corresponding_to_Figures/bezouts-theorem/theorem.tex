Let $p_1, p_2 \in \mathbb{C}[X,Y,Z]$ be homogeneous polynomials with no repeated factors and no common factor, defining curves $V(p_1)$ and $V(p_2)$ in $\mathbb{P}^2(\mathbb{C})$. Let $P_1, \ldots, P_k$ be all the points of intersection of $V(p_1)$ and $V(p_2)$, and for each $i$ let $n_{P_i}$ be the intersection multiplicity at $P_i$. Then
$$\sum_{i=1}^{k} n_{P_i} = (\deg p_1) \cdot (\deg p_2).$$
Equivalently, the degree of the intersection is
$$\deg(V(p_1) \cap V(p_2)) = (\deg p_1) \cdot (\deg p_2).$$
