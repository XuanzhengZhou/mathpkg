Let $\mathfrak{a}$ be any ideal of $\mathbb{C}[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$. Then
$$\bigcap_{\mathfrak{p} \supset \mathfrak{a}} \mathfrak{p} = \bigcap_{\mathfrak{m} \supset \mathfrak{a}} \mathfrak{m},$$
where $\mathfrak{p}$ ranges over all prime ideals containing $\mathfrak{a}$ and $\mathfrak{m}$ ranges over all maximal ideals containing $\mathfrak{a}$.
