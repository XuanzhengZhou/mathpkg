A closed ideal in the coordinate ring $R_V = \mathbb{C}[X_1, \ldots, X_n]/\mathfrak{p}$ is the natural analogue of a closed ideal in the polynomial ring. Just as closed ideals of $\mathbb{C}[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$ correspond to varieties in $\mathbb{C}^n$, closed ideals of $R_V$ correspond to subvarieties of $V$. The definition via intersection of prime ideals ensures independence from the particular representation of the coordinate ring as a quotient.
