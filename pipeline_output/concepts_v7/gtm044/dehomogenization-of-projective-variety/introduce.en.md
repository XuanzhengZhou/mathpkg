This lemma states that the dehomogenization of a projective variety coincides with the affine variety defined by the dehomogenized polynomials. Geometrically, $D_i(V(q_1, \ldots, q_r))$ is the intersection of the projective variety with the affine chart $X_i = 1$. This result is fundamental for studying the local affine structure of projective varieties.
