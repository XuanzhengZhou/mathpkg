Let $f = (f_1, f_2): U \to U'$ be a smooth function whose graph locally represents $V(p)$ near $P = (0) \in \mathbb{R}^4$. Define $F_i = Y_i - f_i(X_1, X_2)$ for $i = 1, 2$. Let $T$ be the real tangent plane to $V(p)$ at $(0)$. Then for each real line in $T$ through $(0)$, the corresponding directional derivative at $(0)$ of each $F_i$ is zero.
