Remark 2.21 translates the geometric notion of an irreducible curve into purely ring-theoretic language: the coordinate ring of an irreducible curve has Krull dimension 1, meaning the only prime ideals are (0) and maximal ideals. This is a concrete illustration of the ring-theoretic approach to dimension developed in Section IV.2.
