Let $V \subset \mathbb{P}^n(\mathbb{C})$ be a variety, $P \in V$ a point, and let $H(V) \subset \mathbb{C}^{n+1}$ be the homogeneous variety corresponding to $V$. Let $H$ be a hyperplane in $\mathbb{C}^{n+1}$ with $0 \notin H$, let $L_P$ be the 1-subspace of $\mathbb{C}^{n+1}$ corresponding to $P$, and assume $L_P \cap H \neq \varnothing$. Regard $H(V) \cap H$ as a subvariety of $H$. Then

$$
\operatorname{rank}\bigl(J(H(V))_{L_P \cap H}\bigr) = \operatorname{rank}\bigl(J(H(V) \cap H)_{L_P \cap H}\bigr).
$$
