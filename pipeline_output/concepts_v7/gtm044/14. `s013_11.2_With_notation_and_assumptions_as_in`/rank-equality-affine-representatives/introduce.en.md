This lemma ensures that the Jacobian rank of the homogeneous variety $H(V)$ at an affine representative point matches the Jacobian rank of the affine representative $H(V) \cap H$ at that same point. This is a crucial technical fact that allows one to define the dimension of a projective variety by reducing to the affine case. The proof uses a coordinate change to place the hyperplane $H$ at $X_{n+1}=1$, making the relationship between the two Jacobian arrays transparent.
