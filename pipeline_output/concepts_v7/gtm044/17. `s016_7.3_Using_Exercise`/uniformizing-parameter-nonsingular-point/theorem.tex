Let $P = (0, 0)$ be an arbitrary nonsingular point of an irreducible plane curve $C = V(p) \subset \mathbb{C}_{XY}$, and suppose $V(X) = \mathbb{C}_Y \subset \mathbb{C}_{XY}$ is not tangent to $C$ at $P$. Then $X$ (or more precisely, the image of $X$ in the coordinate ring $\mathbb{C}[X, Y]/(p(X, Y))$ of $C$) is a uniformizing parameter for $C$ at $P$.
