This theorem generalizes the classical genus formula for nonsingular plane curves to curves with ordinary singularities. For a smooth curve of degree $n$, the genus is $(n-1)(n-2)/2$. Each ordinary singularity of multiplicity $r_i$ reduces the genus by $r_i(r_i-1)/2$. This formula is a fundamental bridge between the algebraic degree and the topological genus of a projective plane curve.
