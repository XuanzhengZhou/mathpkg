Let $K$ be any field and let $\mathrm{ord}$ be any discrete rank one valuation of $K$ (a surjective homomorphism $\mathrm{ord}: K \setminus \{0\} \to \mathbb{Z}$ satisfying $\mathrm{ord}(ab) = \mathrm{ord}(a) + \mathrm{ord}(b)$ and $\mathrm{ord}(a+b) \geq \min(\mathrm{ord}(a), \mathrm{ord}(b))$). The ring
$$R = \{0\} \cup \{a \in K \mid \mathrm{ord}(a) \geq 0\}$$
is the valuation ring of $\mathrm{ord}$; it is called a discrete rank one valuation ring.
