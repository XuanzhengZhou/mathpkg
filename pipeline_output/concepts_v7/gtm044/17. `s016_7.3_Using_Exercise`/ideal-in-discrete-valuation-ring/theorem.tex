Let $R$ be a discrete rank one valuation ring of a field, with associated order function $\mathrm{ord}$. For any ideal $\mathfrak{a} \subset R$,
$$\mathfrak{a} = \{a \in R \mid \mathrm{ord}(a) \geq \mathrm{ord}(\mathfrak{a})\},$$
where $\mathrm{ord}(\mathfrak{a}) = \min\{\mathrm{ord}(a) \mid a \in \mathfrak{a}\}$.
