This notation defines the coordinate projection maps from a Cartesian product onto one of its factors. The subscript indicates the factor along which we project: $\pi_T$ projects $S \times T$ onto $S$ by forgetting the $T$-coordinate. The same notation carries over to restrictions to subsets and to the affine plane $\mathbb{C}_{XY}$, where $\pi_Y : \mathbb{C}_{XY} \to \mathbb{C}_X$ projects onto the $X$-axis by forgetting $Y$.
