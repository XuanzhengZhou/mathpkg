This lemma states that if you start with a compact Hausdorff space $T$, remove a single point $P$, and then take the one-point compactification of the resulting punctured space, you recover the original space $T$. In other words, the one-point compactification is the inverse operation to puncturing a compact Hausdorff space. The proof follows from the definitions and is left as an exercise.
