Let $V_1$ and $V_2$ in $\mathbb{P}^n(\mathbb{C})$ or $\mathbb{C}^n$ be of pure dimensions $r$ and $s$, respectively. If they intersect properly at a point $P$, then for almost every $P$-containing transform $L'$ of a linear variety $L^{(n-r)+(n-s)}$, the intersection multiplicity $i(V_1, V_2, L'; P)$ is defined and has a common, fixed value.
