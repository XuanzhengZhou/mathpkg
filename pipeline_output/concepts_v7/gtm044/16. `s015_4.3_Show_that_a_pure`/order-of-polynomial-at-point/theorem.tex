Let $p \in \mathbb{C}[X_1, \ldots, X_m]$ and let $P = (a_1, \ldots, a_m) \in \mathbb{C}^m$. Let $A$ be a linear subspace of $\mathbb{C}^m$ through $P$. The \textit{order of $p$ at $P$ with respect to $A$} is the order of vanishing of the restricted polynomial $p|_A$ at $P$, i.e., the smallest integer $k \geq 0$ such that the $k$-th homogeneous term in the Taylor expansion of $p$ about $P$ does not vanish identically on $A$.
