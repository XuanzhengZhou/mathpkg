A homogeneous subset of $k^n$ is a set closed under scalar multiplication by all elements of the field $k$. Such a set is either empty, consists of the origin alone, or is a union of $1$-subspaces through the origin. Homogeneous sets in $k^n$ correspond naturally to subsets of the projective space $\mathbb{P}^{n-1}(k)$, providing the bridge between affine cones and projective varieties.
