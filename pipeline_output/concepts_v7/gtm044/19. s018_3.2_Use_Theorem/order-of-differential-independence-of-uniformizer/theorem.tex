Let $C$ be an irreducible nonsingular curve, $P$ a point of $C$, and $\omega \in \Omega(K_C, \mathbb{C})$ a differential. The order $\operatorname{ord}_P(\omega)$ is independent of the choice of uniformizing variable. That is, if $z$ and $w$ are two uniformizing variables in $\mathfrak{o}(P; C)$ and $\omega = f\,dz = g\,dw$, then $\operatorname{ord}_P(f) = \operatorname{ord}_P(g)$.
