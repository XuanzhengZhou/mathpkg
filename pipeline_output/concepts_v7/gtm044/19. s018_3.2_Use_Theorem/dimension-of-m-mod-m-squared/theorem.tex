Let $R$ be a local ring with maximal ideal $\mathfrak{m}$ and residue field $R/\mathfrak{m}$. For any elements $x_1, \ldots, x_k \in \mathfrak{m}$,

$$\mathfrak{m} = x_1 R + \cdots + x_k R + \mathfrak{m}^2$$

if and only if $\mathfrak{m}/\mathfrak{m}^2$ has dimension $k$ over $R/\mathfrak{m}$, with basis elements $x_1 + \mathfrak{m}^2, \ldots, x_k + \mathfrak{m}^2$.
