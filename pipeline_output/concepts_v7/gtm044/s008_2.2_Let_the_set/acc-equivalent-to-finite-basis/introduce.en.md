Lemma 3.3 establishes the equivalence between the ascending chain condition and the finite basis property for ideals. It asserts that a ring is Noetherian precisely when every ideal is finitely generated. This elementary but crucial characterization is the linchpin that connects the a.c.c. to the Hilbert basis theorem, since the latter is proved by showing that if every ideal of $R$ has a finite basis, then the same holds for $R[X]$.
