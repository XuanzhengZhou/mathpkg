The multidimensional hypergeometric distribution extends the ordinary hypergeometric from two types to $r$ types of items when sampling without replacement. Its probability mass function involves a product of binomial coefficients divided by a central binomial coefficient. This distribution arises in survey sampling with multiple categories, quality control, and the statistical analysis of contingency tables with fixed margins.
