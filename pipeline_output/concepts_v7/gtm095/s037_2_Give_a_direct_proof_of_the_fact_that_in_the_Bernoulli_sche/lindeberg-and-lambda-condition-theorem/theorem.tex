Let $\xi_{n1}, \ldots, \xi_{nn}$ be a triangular array of independent random variables with distribution functions $F_{nk}(x)$. Then:

\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] The Lindeberg condition (L) implies that condition $(\Lambda)$ is satisfied:
$$(L) \;\Longrightarrow\; (\Lambda).$$

\item[(2)] If $\displaystyle\max_{1 \leq k \leq n} \mathbb{E}\,\xi_{nk}^{2} \rightarrow 0$ as $n \rightarrow \infty$, then condition $(\Lambda)$ implies the Lindeberg condition (L):
$$(\Lambda) \;\Longrightarrow\; (L).$$
\end{enumerate}
