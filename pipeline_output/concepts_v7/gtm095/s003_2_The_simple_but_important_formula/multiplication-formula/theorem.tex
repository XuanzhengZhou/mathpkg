The \text{multiplication formula for probabilities}: for events $A$ and $B$ with $\text{P}(A) > 0$,
$$\text{P}(AB) = \text{P}(B \mid A) \text{P}(A).$$
More generally, if $A_1, \ldots, A_n$ are events with $\text{P}(A_1 \cdots A_{n-1}) > 0$, then
$$\text{P}(A_1 A_2 \cdots A_n) = \text{P}(A_1) \text{P}(A_2 \mid A_1) \cdots \text{P}(A_n \mid A_1 \cdots A_{n-1}).$$
