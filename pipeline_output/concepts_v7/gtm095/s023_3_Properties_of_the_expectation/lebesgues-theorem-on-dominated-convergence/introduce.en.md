Theorem 3 (Lebesgue’s Theorem on Dominated Convergence). Let $\eta, \xi, \xi_1, \xi_2, \ldots$ be random variables such that $|\xi_n| \leq \eta$, $E\eta < \infty$ and $\xi_n \to \xi$ (a.s.). Then $E|\xi| < \infty$,. This theorem establishes an important result in probability theory.
