Whenever the expectation of a random variable $\xi$ is defined (possibly infinite), its expectation restricted to any measurable set $A$ is also defined. The restricted expectation $E(\xi I_A)$ is often written as $\int_A \xi \, dP$ and called the Lebesgue integral of $\xi$ over $A$.
