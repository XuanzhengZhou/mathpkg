Lyapunov's Inequality establishes the monotonicity of $L^p$ norms for a fixed random variable: the $L^t$ norm dominates the $L^s$ norm whenever $t > s$. The proof sets $r = t/s$, $\eta = |\xi|^s$, and applies Jensen's inequality to the convex function $g(x) = |x|^r$. This implies the chain of inequalities $(E|\xi|) \leq (E|\xi|^2)^{1/2} \leq \cdots \leq (E|\xi|^n)^{1/n}$.
