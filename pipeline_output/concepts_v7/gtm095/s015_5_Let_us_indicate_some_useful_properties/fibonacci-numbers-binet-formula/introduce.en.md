The generating function method provides an elegant derivation of the Binet formula for Fibonacci numbers. Starting from the recurrence $F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2}$, one derives the generating function $G(x) = 1/(1-x-x^2)$. Partial fraction decomposition using the roots of the denominator yields the closed-form expression $F_n$ in terms of powers of the golden ratio and its conjugate. This illustrates how generating functions transform linear recurrences into algebraic equations.
