The Fibonacci numbers $F_n$, defined by $F_1 = 1$, $F_2 = 2$, and $F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2}$ for $n \geq 3$, have the generating function

$$G(x) = \sum_{n \geq 0} F_n x^n = \frac{1}{1 - x - x^2},$$

with $F_0 = 0$. The closed-form Binet formula is

$$F_n = \frac{1}{a - b} \left( \frac{1}{a^{n+1}} - \frac{1}{b^{n+1}} \right),$$

where $a = \frac{1}{2}(-1 - \sqrt{5})$ and $b = \frac{1}{2}(-1 + \sqrt{5})$ are the roots of $1 - x - x^2 = 0$.
