The generating function method converts the Fibonacci recurrence into a rational function, which is then decomposed by partial fractions to yield Binet's closed-form formula. This technique — translating a recurrence into an algebraic equation for the generating function, solving it, and expanding — is a universal recipe for solving linear recurrences with constant coefficients, making it one of the most elegant applications of generating functions.
