A Lebesgue-Stieltjes measure is a $\sigma$-finite measure on the Borel $\sigma$-algebra of the real line that assigns finite measure to every bounded interval. These measures generalize Lebesgue measure (which corresponds to $G(x) = x$) and are in one-to-one correspondence with generalized distribution functions. Lebesgue-Stieltjes measures provide the natural framework for representing probability measures and more general $\sigma$-finite measures on the real line through their associated distribution functions.
