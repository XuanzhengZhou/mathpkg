Let $T = [0,1]$ and let $C$ be the space of continuous functions $x = (x_t)$, $0 \leq t \leq 1$, with the metric

$$\rho(x,y) = \sup_{t \in T} |x_t - y_t|.$$

Let $\mathcal{B}(C)$ be the $\sigma$-algebra generated by the cylinder sets, and $\mathcal{B}_0(C)$ the $\sigma$-algebra generated by the open sets with respect to $\rho$. Then

$$\mathcal{B}(C) = \mathcal{B}_0(C).$$
