\textbf{Theorem (Generalized Theorem 1).} The formula

$$\mu(a, b] = G(b) - G(a)$$

establishes a one-to-one correspondence between Lebesgue--Stieltjes measures $\mu$ on $(R, \mathcal{B}(R))$ and generalized distribution functions $G$ on $R$. The case $G(x) = x$ yields Lebesgue measure $\lambda$ on $(R, \mathcal{B}(R))$.
