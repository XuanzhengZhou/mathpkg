Lemma 1. Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a collection of subsets of $\Omega$. Then there are the smallest algebra $\alpha(\mathcal{E})$ and the smallest $\sigma$-algebra $\sigma(\mathcal{E})$ containing all the sets that are in $\mathcal{E}$. This lemma provides a key technical result used in proving theorems.
