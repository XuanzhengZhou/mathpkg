A collection
$$\mathcal{D} = \{D_1, \ldots, D_n\}$$
of sets is called a \text{decomposition} of $\Omega$ if the $D_i$ are nonempty, pairwise disjoint, and their union equals $\Omega$:
$$D_1 + \cdots + D_n = \Omega, \quad D_i \cap D_j = \varnothing \text{ for } i \neq j, \quad D_i \neq \varnothing.$$
