Let $\mathcal{D}_1$ and $\mathcal{D}_2$ be two decompositions of $\Omega$, and let $\alpha(\mathcal{D}_1)$ and $\alpha(\mathcal{D}_2)$ be their induced algebras. We say that $\mathcal{D}_2$ is \text{finer} than $\mathcal{D}_1$, written
$$\mathcal{D}_1 \preceq \mathcal{D}_2,$$
if $\alpha(\mathcal{D}_1) \subseteq \alpha(\mathcal{D}_2)$.
