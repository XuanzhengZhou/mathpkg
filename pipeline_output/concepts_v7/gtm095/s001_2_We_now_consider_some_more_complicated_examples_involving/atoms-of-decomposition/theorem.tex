Let $\mathcal{D} = \{D_1, \ldots, D_n\}$ be a decomposition of a sample space $\Omega$. The sets $D_1, \ldots, D_n$ are called the \text{atoms} of the decomposition $\mathcal{D}$. They satisfy:
$$D_i \neq \varnothing, \quad D_i \cap D_j = \varnothing \text{ for } i \neq j, \quad D_1 + \cdots + D_n = \Omega.$$
