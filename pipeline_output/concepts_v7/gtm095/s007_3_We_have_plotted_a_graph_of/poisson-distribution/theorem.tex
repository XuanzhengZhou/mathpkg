The Poisson probability distribution with parameter $\lambda > 0$ is the discrete probability distribution $\{\pi_k, k = 0, 1, 2, \ldots\}$ defined by

$$\pi_k = \frac{\lambda^k e^{-\lambda}}{k!}, \quad k = 0, 1, 2, \ldots,$$

satisfying $\pi_k \geq 0$ for all $k$ and $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \pi_k = 1$.
