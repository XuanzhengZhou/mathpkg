An estimator is a function of the observed data that produces a guess for an unknown parameter of the underlying probabilistic model. In the Bernoulli scheme with $n$ independent trials, where the success probability $\theta$ is unknown, an estimator $T_n$ is any function mapping the $n$ observed outcomes to a value in the parameter space $[0, 1]$. The quality of an estimator is assessed through its statistical properties such as consistency, unbiasedness, and efficiency.
