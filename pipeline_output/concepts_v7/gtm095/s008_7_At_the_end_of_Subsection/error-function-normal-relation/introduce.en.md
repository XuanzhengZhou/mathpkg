These identities establish the precise relationship between the error function $\operatorname{erf}$ and the standard normal distribution function $\Phi$. The first identity expresses $\Phi(x)$ in terms of $\operatorname{erf}(x/\sqrt{2})$, while the second gives the inverse relation. These formulas allow one to convert between the two representations commonly used in probability and statistics.
