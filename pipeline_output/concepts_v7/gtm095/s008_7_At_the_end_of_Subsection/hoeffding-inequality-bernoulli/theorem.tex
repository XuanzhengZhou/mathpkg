Let $S_n = \xi_1 + \cdots + \xi_n$ where $\xi_i$ are independent Bernoulli random variables with $P(\xi_i = 1) = p$, $P(\xi_i = 0) = 1 - p$. Then for every $\varepsilon > 0$ and $0 \leq p \leq 1$,
$$
P\left\{\frac{S_n}{n} - p \leq -\varepsilon\right\} \leq e^{-2n\varepsilon^2}, \tag{41}
$$
$$
P\left\{\left|\frac{S_n}{n} - p\right| \geq \varepsilon\right\} \leq 2e^{-2n\varepsilon^2}. \tag{42}
$$
