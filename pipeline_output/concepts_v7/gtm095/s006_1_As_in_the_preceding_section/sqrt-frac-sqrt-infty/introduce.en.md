Corollary. The conclusion of the local limit theorem can be put in the following equivalent form: For all $x \in R^1$ such that $x = o(npq)^{1/6}$, and for $np + x\sqrt{npq}$ an integer from the set $\{0, 1, \ldots, n\},$. This corollary follows directly from a preceding result.
