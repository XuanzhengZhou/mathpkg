Let $A$ be a set of $n$ elements and $B$ a set of $m$ elements. The numbers of functions, injections, surjections, and bijections from $A$ to $B$ are:

$$N(\mathbb{F}) = m^n,$$

$$N(\mathbb{I}) = (m)_n = m(m-1) \cdots (m-n+1) \quad (n \leq m),$$

$$N(\mathbb{S}) = \sum_{i=0}^{m} (-1)^i C_m^i (m-i)^n \quad (n \geq m),$$

$$N(\mathbb{B}) = n! \quad (n = m).$$
