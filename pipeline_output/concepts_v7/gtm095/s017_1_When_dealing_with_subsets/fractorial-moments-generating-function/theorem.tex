Let $\xi$ be a nonnegative integer-valued random variable with generating function $G_\xi(x) = E x^\xi$. The \text{factorial moment} of order $r$ is
$$m_{(r)} = E[\xi(\xi-1) \cdots (\xi-r+1)].$$
Then
$$m_{(r)} = G_\xi^{(r)}(1),$$
where $G_\xi^{(r)}$ is the $r$-th derivative of $G_\xi(x)$ evaluated at $x = 1$.
