Let $\mathcal{P} = \{P_{\theta}, \theta \in \Theta\}$ be a family of probability measures on a measurable space $(\Omega, \mathcal{F})$, these measures depending on a parameter $\theta$ running over a parameter set $\Theta$. The triple
$$\mathcal{E} = (\Omega, \mathcal{F}, \mathcal{P})$$
is called a \text{probabilistic-statistical model} (or a \text{probabilistic-statistical experiment}).
