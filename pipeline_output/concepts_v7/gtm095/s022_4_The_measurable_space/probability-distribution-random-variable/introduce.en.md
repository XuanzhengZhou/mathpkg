The probability distribution $P_\xi$ is the push-forward (image measure) of $P$ under the random variable $\xi$, transferring probabilistic structure from the abstract sample space $(\Omega, \mathcal{F})$ to the concrete real line $(R, \mathcal{B}(R))$. This definition unifies discrete, continuous, and absolutely continuous distributions under a single measure-theoretic framework, where the distribution function $F_\xi(x) = P_\xi(-\infty, x]$ serves as a complete descriptor.
