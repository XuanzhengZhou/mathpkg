This technical lemma is the key step in proving that the definition of the Lebesgue integral (expectation) for nonnegative random variables is well-defined — that the limit $\lim_n E\xi_n$ does not depend on the choice of the approximating sequence of simple functions. It establishes that any simple function dominated by the limit is eventually dominated by the approximating sequence in expectation, ensuring consistency of the integral definition.
