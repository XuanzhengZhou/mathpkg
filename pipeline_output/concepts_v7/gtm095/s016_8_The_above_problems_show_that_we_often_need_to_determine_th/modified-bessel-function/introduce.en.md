The modified Bessel function of the first kind $I_k$ is a special function that appears in solutions of differential equations with cylindrical symmetry and in probability theory, particularly in the distribution of the difference of independent Poisson random variables (Skellam distribution). The key identity $e^{\lambda(t+1/t)} = \sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} t^k I_k(2\lambda)$ serves as a generating function, allowing the extraction of probability distributions involving these functions.
