Let $\xi_1$ and $\xi_2$ be independent Poisson random variables with parameters $\lambda_1 > 0$ and $\lambda_2 > 0$. Then for $k = 0, \pm 1, \pm 2, \ldots$,

$$P(\xi_1 - \xi_2 = k) = e^{-(\lambda_1 + \lambda_2)} \left(\frac{\lambda_1}{\lambda_2}\right)^{k/2} I_k\!\left(2\sqrt{\lambda_1 \lambda_2}\right),$$

where $I_k(\cdot)$ is the modified Bessel function of the first kind of order $k$.
