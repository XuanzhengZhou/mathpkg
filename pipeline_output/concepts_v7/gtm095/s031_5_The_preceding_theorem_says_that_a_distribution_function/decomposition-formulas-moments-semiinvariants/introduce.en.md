These formulas express simple moments in terms of simple semi-invariants (and vice versa) via set partitions. The moment $m_\xi(I)$ is the sum over all partitions of $I$ of the product of semi-invariants of the blocks. The inverse formula involves alternating signs and factorial factors, analogous to Möbius inversion on the partition lattice. These are combinatorial specializations of Corollary 1 where each component appears at most once.
