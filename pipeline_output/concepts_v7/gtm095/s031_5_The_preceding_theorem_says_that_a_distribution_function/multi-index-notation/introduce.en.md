This notation simplifies the multivariate Taylor expansions of characteristic functions. For a multi-index $\nu = (\nu_1,\ldots,\nu_k)$ with nonnegative integer components, the factorial $\nu!$ is the product of component factorials, $|\nu|$ is the total order, and $t^\nu$ is the monomial $t_1^{\nu_1}\cdots t_k^{\nu_k}$. Mixed moments $m_\xi^{(\nu)}$ and semi-invariants $s_\xi^{(\nu)}$ are indexed by these multi-indices.
