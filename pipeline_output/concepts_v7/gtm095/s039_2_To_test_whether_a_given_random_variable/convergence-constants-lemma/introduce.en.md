This is the classical convergence-of-types lemma. When a sequence of random variables $\xi_n$ converges in distribution to $\xi$, and an affine rescaling $a_n\xi_n + b_n$ converges to another nondegenerate limit $\tilde{\xi}$, the scaling sequences $a_n$ and $b_n$ must themselves converge to finite constants $a > 0$ and $b$. The conclusion is that $\tilde{\xi}$ has the same type as $\xi$, related by the limiting affine transformation.
