Let $\xi_n \xrightarrow{d} \xi$ and let there exist constants $a_n > 0$ and $b_n$ such that

$$a_n \xi_n + b_n \xrightarrow{d} \tilde{\xi},$$

where the random variables $\xi$ and $\tilde{\xi}$ are not degenerate. Then there exist constants $a > 0$ and $b$ such that $a_n \to a$, $b_n \to b$ as $n \to \infty$, and

$$\tilde{\xi} \stackrel{d}{=} a \xi + b.$$
