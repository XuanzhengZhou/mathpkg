(a) A necessary and sufficient condition that $\xi_n \to \xi$ (P-a.s.) is that

$$P \left\{ \sup_{k \geq n} |\xi_k - \xi| \geq \varepsilon \right\} \to 0, \quad n \to \infty,$$
