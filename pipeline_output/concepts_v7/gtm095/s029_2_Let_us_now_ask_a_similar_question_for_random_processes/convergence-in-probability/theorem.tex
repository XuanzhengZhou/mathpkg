A sequence $\xi_1, \xi_2, \ldots$ of random variables \textbf{converges in probability} to a random variable $\xi$ (notation: $\xi_n \xrightarrow{P} \xi$) if for every $\varepsilon > 0$,

$$P\{|\xi_n - \xi| \geq \varepsilon\} \to 0, \quad n \to \infty.$$

In analysis, this is known as convergence in measure.
