The following implications hold between the modes of convergence:

$$\xi_n \xrightarrow{\text{a.s.}} \xi \;\Longrightarrow\; \xi_n \xrightarrow{P} \xi,$$

$$\xi_n \xrightarrow{L^p} \xi \;\Longrightarrow\; \xi_n \xrightarrow{P} \xi, \quad p > 0,$$

$$\xi_n \xrightarrow{P} \xi \;\Longrightarrow\; \xi_n \xrightarrow{d} \xi.$$
