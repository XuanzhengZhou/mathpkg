A necessary and sufficient condition that a sequence $(\xi_n)_{n \geq 1}$ of random variables in $L^p$ ($p \geq 1$) converges in the mean of order $p$ to a random variable in $L^p$ is that the sequence is fundamental in the mean of order $p$:

$$\|\xi_n - \xi_m\|_p \to 0, \quad n, m \to \infty.$$

Consequently, $L^p$ is a complete normed linear space (a Banach space).
