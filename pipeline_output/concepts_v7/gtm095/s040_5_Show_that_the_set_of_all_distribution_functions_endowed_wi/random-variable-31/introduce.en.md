Definition 1. A sequence of random elements $X_n$, $n \geq 1$, is said to converge in distribution, or in law (notation: $X_n \xrightarrow{\mathcal{D}} X$, or $X_n \xrightarrow{\mathcal{L}} X$), if $P_n \xrightarrow{w} P$. This definition introduces a fundamental concept in probability theory.
