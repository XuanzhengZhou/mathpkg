Formula (4.5) describes $H^1(G, A)$ as the cokernel of $\iota^*: A \to \operatorname{Hom}_G(IG, A)$, where $\iota^*(a)$ is the principal derivation $d_a(x) = xa - a$. Since $\operatorname{Hom}_G(IG, A)$ is the group of all derivations (crossed homomorphisms) from $G$ to $A$, this formula shows that $H^1(G, A)$ classifies derivations modulo principal derivations — a concrete interpretation fundamental to Galois cohomology and group extensions.
