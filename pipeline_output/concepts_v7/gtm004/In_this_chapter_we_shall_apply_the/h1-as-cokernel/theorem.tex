For an arbitrary $G$-module $A$,
$$H^1(G, A) = \operatorname{coker}\bigl(\iota^* : A \to \operatorname{Hom}_G(IG, A)\bigr),$$
where $\iota^*(a)(x - 1) = xa - a$ for $a \in A$, $x \in G$.

The map $\iota^*$ sends an element $a \in A$ to the principal derivation $d_a(x) = xa - a$. Thus $H^1(G, A)$ classifies derivations modulo principal derivations.
