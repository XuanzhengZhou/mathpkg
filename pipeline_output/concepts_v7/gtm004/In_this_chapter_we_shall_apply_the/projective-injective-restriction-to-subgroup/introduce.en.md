Corollary 1.4 states that the restriction functor from $G$-modules to $U$-modules (for a subgroup $U \leq G$) preserves projectivity and injectivity. This follows from Lemma 1.3 together with the adjoint functor theorem (Theorem IV.12.5). The result is useful because it allows homological properties established for a group $G$ to be inherited by its subgroups.
