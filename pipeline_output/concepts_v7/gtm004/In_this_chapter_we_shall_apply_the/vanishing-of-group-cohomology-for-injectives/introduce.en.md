Property (2.4) states that cohomology vanishes in positive dimensions when the coefficient module is injective, and homology vanishes when the coefficient module is flat (or projective). This follows directly from the derived functor definition: $\operatorname{Ext}^n(\mathbb{Z}, A)$ vanishes for $n \geq 1$ when $A$ is injective, and $\operatorname{Tor}_n(B, \mathbb{Z})$ vanishes for $n \geq 1$ when $B$ is flat.
