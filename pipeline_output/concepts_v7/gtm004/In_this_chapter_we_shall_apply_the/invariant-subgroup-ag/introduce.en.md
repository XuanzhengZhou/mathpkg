The invariant subgroup $A^G$ consists of all elements of the $G$-module $A$ that are fixed by every group element. It is the largest trivial submodule of $A$. In the derived functor framework, $A^G$ is precisely the $0$-th cohomology group $H^0(G, A)$, making it the starting point for the cohomological study of group actions.
