Lemma 1.2 describes the structure of the augmentation ideal $IG$ both as an abelian group and as a $G$-module. Part (i) shows that $IG$ is a free abelian group with basis $\{x-1 : x \neq 1\}$, while part (ii) identifies $G$-module generators: if $S$ generates $G$, then $\{s-1 : s \in S\}$ generates $IG$ as a $G$-module. This structural result is essential for computations of $H_1$ and $H^1$.
