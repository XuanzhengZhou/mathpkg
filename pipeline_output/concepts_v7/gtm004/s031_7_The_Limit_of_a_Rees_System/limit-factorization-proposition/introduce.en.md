Proposition 8.2 is a technical result about the behavior of limits under factorization of the filtration inclusions through a common intermediate object $Y$. It states that the property of being the limit of the cofiltration system is preserved when passing from $X$ to $Y$, provided the diagram is bicartesian. This result is used in the construction of completions of filtered objects.
