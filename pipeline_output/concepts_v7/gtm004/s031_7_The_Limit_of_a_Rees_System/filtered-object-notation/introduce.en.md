Notation (8.4) encodes the full structure of a filtered object in an abelian category. The diagram simultaneously displays the filtration inclusions $\xi_p$, the canonical embeddings $\nu_p$, the quotient projections $\eta_p$, and the induced transition maps $\xi_p$ of the associated cofiltration. This unified notation facilitates the study of completeness properties and the passage between a filtration and its associated graded object.
