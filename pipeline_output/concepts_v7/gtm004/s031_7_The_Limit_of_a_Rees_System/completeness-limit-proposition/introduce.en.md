Proposition 8.3 provides an equivalent characterization of completeness for filtered objects in terms of limits and colimits of the associated systems of subquotients $X_q^p = X^p / X^q$. Right completeness guarantees that each filtration level $X^p$ is the projective limit of its subquotients as the denominator varies, while left completeness guarantees that each quotient $X_q$ is the inductive limit of its subquotients as the numerator varies.
