Proposition 8.1 establishes the fundamental functoriality property of spectral sequences: a morphism defined at any page $E_k$ naturally extends to all later pages, including the limit page $E_\infty$. This follows from the definition of spectral sequence pages as successive derived couples (see (5.11)), ensuring that the morphism is compatible with the differentials and the passage to homology at each stage.
