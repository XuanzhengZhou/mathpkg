\textbf{Theorem 8.4.} Let $\psi : X \to X'$ be a morphism of filtered objects in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$, i.e.,
$$
\psi^p : X^p \to X'^p, \quad \psi : X \to X', \quad \psi_p : X_p \to X'_p
$$
compatible with the filtration structures. Suppose that $\psi$ induces an isomorphism on the associated graded objects,
$$
\psi_* : \operatorname{Gr}(X) \xrightarrow{\sim} \operatorname{Gr}(X').
$$
\begin{enumerate}
\item If the filtrations of $X$ and $X'$ are left complete, then $\psi_p$ is an isomorphism for all $p$.
\item If the filtrations of $X$ and $X'$ are right complete, then $\psi^p$ is an isomorphism for all $p$.
\item If the filtrations of $X$ and $X'$ are complete, then $\psi$ is an isomorphism.
\end{enumerate}
