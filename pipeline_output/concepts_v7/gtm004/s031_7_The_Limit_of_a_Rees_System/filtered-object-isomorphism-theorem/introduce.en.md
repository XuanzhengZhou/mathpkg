Theorem 8.4 is the central result that justifies the use of spectral sequences for comparing filtered objects. It states that an isomorphism of associated graded objects lifts to an isomorphism of the filtered objects themselves, provided the filtrations are complete. This generalizes the finite filtration case and is the key step in deducing $H(C) \cong H(C')$ from $E_\infty \cong E'_\infty$ when the filtrations on homology are complete.
