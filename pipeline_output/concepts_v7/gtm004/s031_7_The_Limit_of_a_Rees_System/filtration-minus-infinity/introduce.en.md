This definition provides an intuitive interpretation of the algebraic conditions arising in the convergence of spectral sequences for filtered differential groups. An element has filtration $-\infty$ if it persists in the image of all lower filtration levels, while stability means the element persists non-trivially through all higher filtration levels. Under this interpretation, condition (7.8) states that elements of filtration $-\infty$ are stable, and stable elements have filtration $-\infty$.
