Theorem 7.3 provides the fundamental structural relationship between a Rees system and its associated spectral sequence. The theorem constructs a limit diagram with exact rows and columns involving cokernels of the induced maps, the $E_\infty$ term, kernels, and the limit object $\Gamma^+$. This diagram serves as the key tool for comparing $E_\infty$ with $\Gamma^+$ and for establishing convergence conditions in the spectral sequence arising from a filtered differential object.
