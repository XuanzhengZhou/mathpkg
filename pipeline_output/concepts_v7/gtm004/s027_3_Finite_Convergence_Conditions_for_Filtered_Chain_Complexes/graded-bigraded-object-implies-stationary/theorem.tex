Let
\[
D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D \xrightarrow{\beta} E \xrightarrow{\gamma} D
\]
be an exact couple of bigraded objects, and let
\[
\bar{D} \xrightarrow{\bar{\alpha}} \bar{D} \xrightarrow{\bar{\beta}} E \xrightarrow{\bar{\gamma}} \bar{D}
\]
be the exact couple arising from the filtration, where $\bar{D}^{p,q} = H_q(C/C^{(p-1)})$.

If $D$ (respectively $\bar{D}$) is positively graded, then $\alpha$ (respectively $\bar{\alpha}$) is negatively stationary. If $D$ (respectively $\bar{D}$) is negatively graded, then $\alpha$ (respectively $\bar{\alpha}$) is positively stationary.
