A filtration $\{C^{(p)}\}_{p \in \mathbb{Z}}$ of the chain complex $C$ is called \text{homologically finite} if, for each $q$, there exist integers $p_0, p_1$ such that
\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] $H_q(C^{(p)}) = 0$ for $p \leq p_0$,
\item[(ii)] $H_q(C^{(p)}) = H_q(C)$ for $p \geq p_1$.
\end{itemize}
