The connecting homomorphism (or boundary map) $\omega$ is the morphism that links the homology groups of $C$ to those of $A$ in the long exact sequence induced by a short exact sequence of chain complexes. It is constructed by a diagram chase: a cycle in $C_n$ is lifted to $B_n$, differentiated, and the resulting element in the kernel of the differential is pulled back along $\varphi$ to $A_{n-1}$. The connecting homomorphism is natural with respect to morphisms of short exact sequences of complexes.
