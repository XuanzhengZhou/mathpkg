Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be semi-simple over a field of characteristic $0$, and let $A$ be a $\mathfrak{g}$-module. If the structure map $\varrho : \mathfrak{g} \rightarrow \operatorname{End}_K A$ is injective (i.e., $\varrho$ is a faithful representation), then the bilinear form $\beta$ corresponding to $A$, defined by $\beta(x, y) = \operatorname{Tr}(\varrho x \circ \varrho y)$, is non-degenerate.
