The $\mathfrak{g}$-module homomorphisms $d_n : C_n \rightarrow C_{n-1}$, $n = 0, 1, 2, \ldots$, are defined inductively by
$$d_n \langle x_1, \ldots, x_n \rangle = (-\theta(x_1) - \sigma(x_1) d_{n-1}) \langle x_2, \ldots, x_n \rangle,$$
with $d_0 = 0$. Explicitly,
$$d_n \langle x_1, \ldots, x_n \rangle = \sum_{i=1}^{n} (-1)^{i+1} x_i \langle x_1, \ldots, \hat{x}_i, \ldots, x_n \rangle + \sum_{1 \leq i < j \leq n} (-1)^{i+j} \langle [x_i, x_j], x_1, \ldots, \hat{x}_i, \ldots, \hat{x}_j, \ldots, x_n \rangle.$$
