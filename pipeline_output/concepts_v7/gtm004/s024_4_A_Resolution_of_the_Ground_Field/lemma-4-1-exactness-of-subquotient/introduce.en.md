Lemma 4.1 establishes the exactness of the subquotient complexes $W^p = F_p C / F_{p-1} C$ by constructing an explicit $K$-linear contracting homotopy $\Sigma$. This filtration argument is the crucial step in proving that the Chevalley--Eilenberg complex $(C, d)$ is a resolution of the trivial $\mathfrak{g}$-module $K$. The contracting homotopy is built using a PBW basis of $C_n$ obtained from an ordered basis of $\mathfrak{g}$.
