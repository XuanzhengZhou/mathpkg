The complex
$$C: \dots \rightarrow C_n \xrightarrow{d_n} C_{n-1} \rightarrow \dots \rightarrow C_0 \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} K \rightarrow 0,$$
where $C_n = U\mathfrak{g} \otimes_K E_n \mathfrak{g}$, $d_n$ is the Chevalley--Eilenberg differential, and $\varepsilon : C_0 = U\mathfrak{g} \rightarrow K$ is the augmentation, is a $\mathfrak{g}$-projective resolution of the trivial $\mathfrak{g}$-module $K$.
