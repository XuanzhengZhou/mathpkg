For any $K$-vector space $V$ and $n \geq 1$, we define $E_n V$ to be the quotient of the $n$-fold tensor product $T_n V$ by the subspace generated by
$$x_1 \otimes x_2 \otimes \cdots \otimes x_n - (\operatorname{sig} \sigma) x_{\sigma 1} \otimes x_{\sigma 2} \otimes \cdots \otimes x_{\sigma n},$$
for $x_1, \ldots, x_n \in V$ and all permutations $\sigma$ of $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$. The symbol $\operatorname{sig} \sigma$ denotes the parity of the permutation $\sigma$. We use $\langle x_1, \ldots, x_n \rangle$ to denote the element of $E_n V$ corresponding to $x_1 \otimes \cdots \otimes x_n$.
