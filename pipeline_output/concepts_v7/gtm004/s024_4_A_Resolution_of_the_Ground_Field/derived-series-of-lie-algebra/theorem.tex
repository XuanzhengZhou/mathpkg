For any Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$, we define $\mathfrak{g}_0 = \mathfrak{g}$, and recursively
$$\mathfrak{g}_n = [\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g}_{n-1}], \quad n = 1, 2, \ldots,$$
where $[S, T]$ for subsets $S, T$ of $\mathfrak{g}$ denotes the Lie subalgebra generated by all $[s, t]$ for $s \in S$, $t \in T$. Each $\mathfrak{g}_n$ is automatically an ideal in $\mathfrak{g}$.
