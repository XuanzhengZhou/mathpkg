Every finite-dimensional Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ (over a field of characteristic $0$) is the split extension of a semi-simple Lie algebra $\mathfrak{s}$ by its radical $\mathfrak{r}$:
$$0 \rightarrow \mathfrak{r} \rightarrow \mathfrak{g} \rightarrow \mathfrak{s} \rightarrow 0,$$
with $\mathfrak{s} \cong \mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{r}$.
