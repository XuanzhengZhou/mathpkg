Lemma 6.4 states that solvability is an extension-closed property: a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ is solvable exactly when both a subalgebra $\mathfrak{h}$ and the quotient $\mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{h}$ are solvable. This is the Lie algebra analogue of the group-theoretic fact that extensions of solvable groups are solvable.
