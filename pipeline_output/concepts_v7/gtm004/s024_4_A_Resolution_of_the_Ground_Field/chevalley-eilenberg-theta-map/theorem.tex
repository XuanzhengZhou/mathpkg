For every $y \in \mathfrak{g}$, we define a $\mathfrak{g}$-module homomorphism $\theta(y) : C_n \rightarrow C_n$, $n = 0, 1, \ldots$, by
$$\theta(y) \langle x_1, \ldots, x_n \rangle = -y \langle x_1, \ldots, x_n \rangle + \sum_{i=1}^{n} (-1)^{i+1} \langle [y, x_i], x_1, \ldots, \hat{x}_i, \ldots, x_n \rangle,$$
where $\hat{x}_i$ indicates that $x_i$ is to be omitted. Note that $(-1)^{i+1} \langle [y, x_i], x_1, \ldots, \hat{x}_i, \ldots, x_n \rangle = \langle x_1, \ldots, [y, x_i], \ldots, x_n \rangle$.
