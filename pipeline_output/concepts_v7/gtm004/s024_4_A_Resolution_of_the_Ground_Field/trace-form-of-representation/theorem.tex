Let $\varrho : \mathfrak{g} \rightarrow \operatorname{End}_K A$ be a finite-dimensional representation of a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$. The associated bilinear form $\beta : \mathfrak{g} \times \mathfrak{g} \rightarrow K$ is defined by
$$\beta(x, y) = \operatorname{Tr}(\varrho x \circ \varrho y), \quad x, y \in \mathfrak{g}.$$
This form is symmetric and associative: $\beta([x, y], z) = \beta(x, [y, z])$.
