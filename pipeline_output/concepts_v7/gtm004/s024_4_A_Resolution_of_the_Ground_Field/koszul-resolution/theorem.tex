Let $D_n = P \otimes_K E_n \alpha$, where $P = K[x_1, \ldots, x_m]$ and $\alpha$ is the abelian Lie algebra with $K$-basis $x_1, \ldots, x_m$. Define $d_n : D_n \rightarrow D_{n-1}$ by
$$d_n(p \otimes \langle x_{j_1}, \ldots, x_{j_n} \rangle) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} (-1)^{i+1} p x_{j_i} \otimes \langle x_{j_1}, \ldots, \hat{x}_{j_i}, \ldots, x_{j_n} \rangle,$$
where $j_1 < j_2 < \cdots < j_n$. Then
$$D : 0 \rightarrow D_m \rightarrow D_{m-1} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow D_0$$
is a $P$-free resolution of $K$ (regarded as a graded $P$-module via the augmentation $\varepsilon : P \rightarrow K$).
