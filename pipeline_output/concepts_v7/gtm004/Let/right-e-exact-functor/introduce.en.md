A right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functor preserves the exactness of $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequences at the right end. This relativizes the notion of right exactness and is the correct condition for defining left $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors. Every right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functor is automatically additive.
