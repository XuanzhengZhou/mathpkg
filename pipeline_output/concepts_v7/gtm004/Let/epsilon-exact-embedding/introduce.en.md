Lemma 2.2 is the relative version of the horseshoe lemma. It shows that any short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence can be embedded in a diagram with $\mathcal{E}$-projective presentations and $\mathcal{E}$-exact rows and columns. This is the key technical tool for constructing the long exact sequence of $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors, parallel to the absolute case.
