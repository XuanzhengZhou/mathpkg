Given a short exact sequence of groups $N \hookrightarrow K \twoheadrightarrow Q$ and a $K$-module $A$, there is a natural action of $Q$ on the cohomology groups $H^m(N, A)$. Moreover, there is a spectral sequence $\{E_n(A)\}$ with
$$E_1^{p,q} = H^p(Q, H^{q-p}(N, A)) \Rightarrow H^q(K, A),$$
which converges finitely to the graded group associated with $\{H^q(K, A)\}$, suitably filtered.
