Let $N \rightarrowtail K \twoheadrightarrow Q$ be a short exact sequence of groups (so $N \trianglelefteq K$ with quotient $Q$), and let $A$ be a $K$-module. There is a natural action of $Q$ on the cohomology groups $H^m(N, A)$, and there exists a convergent spectral sequence
$$E_1^{p,q} = H^p(Q, H^{q-p}(N, A)) \Longrightarrow H^q(K, A),$$
which converges finitely to the graded group associated with $\{H^q(K, A)\}$, suitably filtered.
