This spectral sequence relates the cohomology of a group $K$ to the cohomology of a normal subgroup $N$ and the quotient $Q = K/N$. It is obtained by applying the Grothendieck spectral sequence to the functors $F(A) = A^N$ (invariants under $N$) and $G(B) = B^Q$. The resulting five-term exact sequence in low degrees is particularly useful for computations.
