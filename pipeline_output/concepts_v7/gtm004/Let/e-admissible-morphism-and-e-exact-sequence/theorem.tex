Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a closed class of epimorphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$.
\begin{itemize}
\item A morphism $\varphi$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ is \emph{$\mathcal{E}$-admissible} if, in its canonical factorization $\varphi = \mu \varepsilon$ with $\mu$ monic and $\varepsilon$ epic, we have $\varepsilon \in \mathcal{E}$.
\item An exact sequence in $\mathfrak{A}$ is \emph{$\mathcal{E}$-exact} if all its morphisms are $\mathcal{E}$-admissible.
\item A complex $K : \cdots \to K_n \to K_{n-1} \to \cdots \to K_0$ is \emph{$\mathcal{E}$-projective} if each $K_n$ is $\mathcal{E}$-projective, and \emph{$\mathcal{E}$-acyclic} if the augmented complex $\cdots \to K_0 \to H_0(K) \to 0$ is $\mathcal{E}$-exact.
\end{itemize}
