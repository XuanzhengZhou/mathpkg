If $H : \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ is a right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functor, where $\mathcal{E}$ is a projective class of epimorphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$, then the $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequence of functors $\{L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H\}$ is the left $\mathcal{E}$-satellite of $H$, i.e., $L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H = S_j^{\mathcal{E}} H$ (with $L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H = 0$ for $j < 0$).
