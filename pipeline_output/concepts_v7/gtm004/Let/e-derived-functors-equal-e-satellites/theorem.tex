If $H : \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ is a right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functor, where $\mathcal{E}$ is a projective class of epimorphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$, then the $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequence of functors $\{L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H\}$ is the left $\mathcal{E}$-satellite of $H$. That is,
$$S_j^{\mathcal{E}} H = L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H, \quad j \ge 0,$$
and $L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H = 0$ for $j < 0$. The sequence $\{L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H\}$ satisfies the universal property: any natural transformation $\varphi : T_0 \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} H$ extends uniquely to a morphism of $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequences $\{\varphi_j : T_j \to L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H\}$.
