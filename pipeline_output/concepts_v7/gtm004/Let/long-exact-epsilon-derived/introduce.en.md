Theorem 2.3 provides the long exact sequence for relative derived functors. Given a short exact sequence of functors that is exact on $\mathcal{E}$-projective objects, the corresponding $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors fit into a long exact sequence. This mirrors the absolute case (Theorem IV.6.1) and is essential for computing relative derived functors.
