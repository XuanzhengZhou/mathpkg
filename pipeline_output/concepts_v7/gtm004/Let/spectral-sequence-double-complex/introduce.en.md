Proposition 9.1 computes the initial pages of the two spectral sequences arising from a double complex with its two standard filtrations. Taking homology first vertically then horizontally yields one spectral sequence; taking homology first horizontally then vertically yields the other. Both converge to the homology of the total complex under suitable finiteness conditions, providing a powerful computational tool.
