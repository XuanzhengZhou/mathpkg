Theorem 3.5 is the dual of Theorem 3.2 for right derived functors relative to an injective class $\mathcal{M}$ of monomorphisms. The right $\mathcal{M}$-derived functors constructed via $\mathcal{M}$-injective resolutions coincide with the right $\mathcal{M}$-satellites. The connecting morphisms go in the opposite direction: $\omega_j : T_j(A'') \to T_{j+1}(A')$.
