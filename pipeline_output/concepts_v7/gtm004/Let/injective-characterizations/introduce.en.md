Theorem 8.4 dualizes the characterizations of projective modules (Theorem 4.7). It gives five equivalent conditions for a module to be injective: (1) the usual definition via lifting along monomorphisms; (2) exactness of the contravariant Hom functor; (3) every embedding of $I$ splits; (4) $I$ is a direct summand of any containing module; (5) $I$ is a direct summand of a cofree module. This is the foundational structure theorem for injective modules.
