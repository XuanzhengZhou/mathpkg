Let $C : C_0 \to C_1 \to \cdots \to C_n \to C_{n+1} \to \cdots$ be acyclic and $D : D_0 \to D_1 \to \cdots \to D_n \to D_{n+1} \to \cdots$ be injective. Then there exists, to every homomorphism $\varphi : H^0(C) \to H^0(D)$, a cochain map $\underline{\varphi} : C \to D$ inducing $\varphi$, and two such cochain maps are homotopic.
