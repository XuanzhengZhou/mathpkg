Proposition 2.4 verifies that the relative exactness condition implies additivity of the functor. This uses the fact that a zero object is characterized by the exactness of $A \xrightarrow{1} A \xrightarrow{1} A \to 0$, which is $\mathcal{E}$-exact, so right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functors preserve zero objects. The remainder of the additivity proof follows as in the absolute case.
