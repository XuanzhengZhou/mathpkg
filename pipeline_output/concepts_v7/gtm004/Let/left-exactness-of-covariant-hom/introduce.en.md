The covariant Hom functor $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, -)$ is left exact: it preserves kernels and monomorphisms, but does not generally preserve epimorphisms. Given an exact sequence $B' \to B \to B''$, the induced sequence of Hom-groups is exact at $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, B')$ and $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, B)$, with $\mu_*$ injective and $\operatorname{im} \mu_* = \ker \varepsilon_*$.
