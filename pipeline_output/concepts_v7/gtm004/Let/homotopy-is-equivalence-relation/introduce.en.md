Homotopy defines an equivalence relation on chain maps, allowing one to form the homotopy category. This lemma verifies the three axioms: reflexivity, symmetry, and transitivity, each following directly from the homotopy equation $\psi - \varphi = \partial \Sigma + \Sigma \partial$.
