Lemma 3.4 establishes that additive functors send homotopic chain maps to homotopic chain maps. This is crucial for the theory of derived functors: applying an additive functor $F$ to a projective resolution and then taking homology yields a result that depends only on the homotopy type of the resolution, not on the specific resolution chosen.
