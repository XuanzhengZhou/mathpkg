Lemma 3.2 verifies that the homotopy relation between chain maps satisfies the axioms of an equivalence relation. Reflexivity and symmetry are straightforward; transitivity follows from adding the homotopies: if $\psi - \varphi = \partial \Sigma + \Sigma \partial$ and $\chi - \psi = \partial T + T \partial$, then $\chi - \varphi = \partial(\Sigma + T) + (\Sigma + T)\partial$.
