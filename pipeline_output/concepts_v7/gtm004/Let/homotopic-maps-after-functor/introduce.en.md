Corollary 3.5 combines Proposition 3.1 and Lemma 3.4: applying an additive functor to homotopic chain maps yields maps that induce the same map in homology. This is the theoretical justification for derived functors: the homology of $F$ applied to a resolution depends only on the homotopy type of the resolution.
