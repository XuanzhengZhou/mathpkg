Let $\{A_j\}_{j \in J}$ be a family of $\Lambda$-modules and let $\bigoplus_{j \in J} A_j$ be their direct sum with injections $\iota_k : A_k \to \bigoplus_{j \in J} A_j$. For any $\Lambda$-module $M$ and any family of $\Lambda$-module homomorphisms $\{\psi_j : A_j \to M\}_{j \in J}$, there exists a unique homomorphism
$$\psi = \langle \psi_j \rangle : \bigoplus_{j \in J} A_j \longrightarrow M$$
such that $\psi \circ \iota_k = \psi_k$ for every $k \in J$.
