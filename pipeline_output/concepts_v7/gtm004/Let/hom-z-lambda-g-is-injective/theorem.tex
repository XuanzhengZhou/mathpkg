Let $G$ be a divisible abelian group. Then $\overline{\Lambda} = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\Lambda, G)$, regarded as a left $\Lambda$-module via the right $\Lambda$-module structure of $\Lambda$, is an injective $\Lambda$-module. In particular, $\Lambda^* = \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\Lambda, \mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z})$ is injective.
