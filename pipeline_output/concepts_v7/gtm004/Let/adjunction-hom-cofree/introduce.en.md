Proposition 8.1 establishes a natural isomorphism between $\Lambda$-module homomorphisms from $A$ into the coinduced module $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\Lambda, G)$ and abelian group homomorphisms from $A$ into $G$. This is an adjunction isomorphism: the functor $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\Lambda, -)$ is right adjoint to the forgetful functor from $\Lambda$-modules to abelian groups. The naturality condition means the isomorphism commutes with precomposition by module homomorphisms.
