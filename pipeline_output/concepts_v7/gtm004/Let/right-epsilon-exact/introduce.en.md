A right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functor is the relative analog of a right exact functor: it preserves the exactness of $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequences on the right. This notion is fundamental for defining left $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors, just as ordinary right exactness is needed for left derived functors. Every additive functor has a right $\mathcal{E}$-exact approximation $L_0^{\mathcal{E}} T$.
