This theorem provides the long exact sequence for $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors, generalizing the classical long exact sequence of derived functors. The connecting homomorphisms $\omega_n$ are natural, and the entire sequence is functorial in the short exact sequence of functors. This is the relative homological algebra analog of the fundamental exact sequence for Tor and Ext.
