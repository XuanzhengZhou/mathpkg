Let $(B, d', d'')$ be a double complex with commuting horizontal and vertical differentials. For the \emph{first} spectral sequence (filtration by rows):
$${}_1E_0^{p,q} = H_{q-p}(B_{p,*}, d''), \quad {}_1E_1^{p,q} = H_p(H_{q-p}(B, d''), d').$$
For the \emph{second} spectral sequence (filtration by columns):
$${}_2E_0^{p,q} = H_{q-p}(B_{*,p}, d'), \quad {}_2E_1^{p,q} = H_p(H_{q-p}(B, d'), d'').$$
