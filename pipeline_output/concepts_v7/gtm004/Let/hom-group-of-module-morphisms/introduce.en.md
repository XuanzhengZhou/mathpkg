The set of all $\Lambda$-module homomorphisms between two modules $A$ and $B$ forms an abelian group under pointwise addition. This group is denoted $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, B)$ and serves as the fundamental building block for the Hom functor. It is not, in general, a $\Lambda$-module unless $\Lambda$ is commutative.
