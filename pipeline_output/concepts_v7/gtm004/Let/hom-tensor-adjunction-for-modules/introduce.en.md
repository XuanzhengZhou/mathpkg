This adjunction isomorphism is the key technical tool for constructing injective modules. It relates $\Lambda$-linear maps into the coinduced module $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\Lambda, G)$ with $\mathbb{Z}$-linear maps into $G$. In categorical terms, the forgetful functor from $\Lambda$-modules to abelian groups has the right adjoint $\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\Lambda, -)$.
