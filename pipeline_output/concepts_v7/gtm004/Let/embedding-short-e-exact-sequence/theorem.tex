Every short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence $0 \to A' \xrightarrow{\alpha'} A \xrightarrow{\alpha''} A'' \to 0$ may be embedded in a commutative diagram
\[
\begin{array}{ccccc}
K' \to & K \to & K'' \\
\downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow \\
P' \to & P \to & P'' \\
\downarrow & \downarrow & \downarrow \\
A' \to & A \to & A''
\end{array}
\]
with all rows and columns $\mathcal{E}$-exact and $P', P, P''$ $\mathcal{E}$-projective. Moreover, $P = P' \oplus P''$.
