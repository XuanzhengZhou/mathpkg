This is the relative version of the Horseshoe Lemma. It shows that any short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence can be resolved by $\mathcal{E}$-projective presentations in a compatible way. This lemma is the crucial technical step for proving the existence of long exact sequences of $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors.
