This proposition confirms that in the category of modules, the categorical definition of epimorphism (right-cancellativity) coincides with the set-theoretic notion of surjectivity. The forward direction is straightforward: surjective maps are right-cancellative. The converse uses a clever argument with the quotient map onto the cokernel and the zero map. This equivalence validates the dualization procedure, as the definition of projective modules using "surjective" can be reformulated purely categorically as "epimorphic."
