This proposition is the exact dual of Proposition 4.5 for projective modules. Where projectivity is preserved under direct sums, injectivity is preserved under direct products. This reflects the categorical duality: the direct sum is a coproduct while the direct product is a product, and the Hom functor's variance exchanges them. This result is useful for constructing injective modules from known injective ones, and it highlights the systematic nature of dualization in homological algebra.
