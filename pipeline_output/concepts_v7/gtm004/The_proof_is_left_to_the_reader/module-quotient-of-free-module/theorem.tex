Every $\Lambda$-module $A$ is a quotient of a free $\Lambda$-module. More precisely, let $S$ be a set of generators of $A$. Let $P = \bigoplus_{s \in S} \Lambda_s$, where $\Lambda_s = \Lambda$ for all $s \in S$, be the free module on $S$. Define $\varphi : P \rightarrow A$ to be the unique homomorphism extending the map $f : S \rightarrow A$ given by $f(1_{\Lambda_s}) = s$. Then $\varphi$ is surjective, so $A \cong P / \ker \varphi$.
