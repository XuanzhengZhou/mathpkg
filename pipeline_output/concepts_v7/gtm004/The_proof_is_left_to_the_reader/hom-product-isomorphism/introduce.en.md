This proposition shows that the covariant Hom functor distributes over direct products in the second argument. A homomorphism from a fixed module into a product is equivalent to a family of homomorphisms into each factor. The correspondence is given by composing with the canonical projections and is an isomorphism of abelian groups. Together with Proposition 3.4, these two results express the additivity of the Hom functor in both arguments with respect to direct sums and products.
