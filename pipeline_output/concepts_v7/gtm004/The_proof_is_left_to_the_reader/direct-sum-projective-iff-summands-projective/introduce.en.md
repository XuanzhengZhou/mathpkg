This proposition establishes that projectivity is an additive property with respect to direct sums. A direct sum of modules is projective precisely when each direct summand is projective. The proof uses the universal property of the direct sum to combine or restrict lifts. This result implies in particular that any direct summand of a free module is projective, providing a large class of projective modules beyond free modules themselves.
