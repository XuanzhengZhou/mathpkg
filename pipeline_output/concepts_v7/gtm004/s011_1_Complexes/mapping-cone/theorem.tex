Let $\varphi: C \rightarrow D$ be a chain map between chain complexes. The \textit{mapping cone} of $\varphi$ is the chain complex $E = E(\varphi) = \{E_n, \partial_n\}$ defined by
$$
E_n = C_{n-1} \oplus D_n,
$$
with differential
$$
\partial_n(a, b) = (-\partial_{n-1}^C a, \;\varphi_{n-1} a + \partial_n^D b), \qquad a \in C_{n-1},\; b \in D_n.
$$
Then $E(\varphi)$ is indeed a chain complex and the inclusion $D \hookrightarrow E(\varphi)$ is a chain map.
