Let $C = \{C_n, \partial_n\}$ be a chain complex over $\Lambda$.

\begin{itemize}
\item Elements of $C_n$ are called \textit{$n$-chains}.
\item Elements of $\ker \partial_n$ are called \textit{$n$-cycles}; the submodule $\ker \partial_n$ is denoted $Z_n = Z_n(C)$.
\item Elements of $\operatorname{im} \partial_{n+1}$ are called \textit{$n$-boundaries}; the submodule $\operatorname{im} \partial_{n+1}$ is denoted $B_n = B_n(C)$.
\end{itemize}

Thus $H_n(C) = Z_n(C) / B_n(C)$.
