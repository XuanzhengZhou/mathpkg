A chain complex $C = \{C_n, \partial_n\}$ over a ring $\Lambda$ is an object in the category $\mathfrak{M}_\Lambda^{\mathbb{Z}}$ of graded (left) $\Lambda$-modules together with an endomorphism $\partial: C \rightarrow C$ of degree $-1$ satisfying $\partial \partial = 0$.

Explicitly, one is given a family $\{C_n\}_{n \in \mathbb{Z}}$ of $\Lambda$-modules together with module homomorphisms $\partial_n: C_n \rightarrow C_{n-1}$ such that $\partial_n \circ \partial_{n+1} = 0$ for all $n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
