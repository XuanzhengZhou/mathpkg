Given a chain complex $C = \{C_n, \partial_n\}$ over $\Lambda$, one obtains a cochain complex $D = \{D^n, \delta^n\}$ by setting
$$
D^n = C_{-n}, \qquad \delta^n = \partial_{-n}, \quad n \in \mathbb{Z}.
$$
Conversely, given a cochain complex $D$ one obtains a chain complex $C$ by the same procedure. Thus the theories of chain complexes and cochain complexes are formally equivalent.
