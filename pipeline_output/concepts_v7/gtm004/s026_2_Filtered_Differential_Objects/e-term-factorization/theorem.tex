Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an abelian category. The $E$-term functor

$$E : (\mathfrak{A}, d, f) \rightarrow \mathfrak{A}^Z$$

from the category of filtered differential objects to graded objects of $\mathfrak{A}$ factorizes as

$$E = H \circ Gr,$$

where $Gr : (\mathfrak{A}, d, f) \rightarrow (\mathfrak{A}^Z, d)$ is the associated graded functor (applied to differential graded objects) and $H$ is the homology functor.
