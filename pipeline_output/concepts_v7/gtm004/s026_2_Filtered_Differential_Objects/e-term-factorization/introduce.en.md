This proposition establishes that the $E$-term of the exact couple associated to a filtered differential object can be obtained by first taking the associated graded object and then applying homology. In symbols, $E = H \circ Gr$. This factorization is important because it conceptually separates the algebraic process of forming subquotients from the topological process of taking homology, and it explains how the $E^1$-term of many concrete spectral sequences is computed.
