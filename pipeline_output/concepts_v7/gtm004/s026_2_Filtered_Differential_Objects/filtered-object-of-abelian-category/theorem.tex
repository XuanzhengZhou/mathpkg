Let $\mathfrak{B}$ be an abelian category. A filtered object $B$ of $\mathfrak{B}$ is an object $B$ equipped with a family of subobjects

$$\cdots \subseteq B^{(p-1)} \subseteq B^{(p)} \subseteq \cdots \subseteq B, \quad -\infty < p < \infty.$$

The category of filtered objects of $\mathfrak{B}$ is denoted $(\mathfrak{B}, f)$. A morphism $\varphi : B \rightarrow B'$ of filtered objects sends $B^{(p)}$ to $B'^{(p)}$ for all $p$.
