From any filtered differential object, the short exact sequences of successive filtration terms yield homology exact triangles. Packing the homology groups of the filtration terms and of their subquotients into graded objects $D$ and $E$ produces the data of an exact couple. This construction is the fundamental bridge from filtered differential objects to spectral sequences: $D$ and $E$ become the $D$-term and $E$-term of the exact couple.
