Let $C$ be a filtered differential object with filtration $\{C^{(p)}\}$. Passing to homology, one obtains an induced filtration on $M = H(C)$ defined by

$$M^{(p)} = \operatorname{Im}\left( H(C^{(p)}) \rightarrow H(C) \right).$$

Thus

$$\cdots \subseteq M^{(p-1)} \subseteq M^{(p)} \subseteq \cdots \subseteq M.$$
