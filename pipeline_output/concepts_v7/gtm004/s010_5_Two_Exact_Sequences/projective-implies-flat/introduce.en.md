Proposition 7.4 establishes that projective modules are always flat. The proof reduces to showing that free modules are flat (since projectives are direct summands of free modules and flatness is preserved under direct summands). For a free module $\Lambda$, tensoring with a monomorphism is essentially the identity, hence a monomorphism. This result places flatness between freeness and projectivity: free $\implies$ projective $\implies$ flat, with both implications strict in general.
