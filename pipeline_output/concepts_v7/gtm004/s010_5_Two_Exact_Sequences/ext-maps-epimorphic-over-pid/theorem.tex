Let $\Lambda$ be a principal ideal domain. Then in the Hom-Ext sequence in the second variable
$$
\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda(A, B'') \xrightarrow{\omega} \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B') \xrightarrow{\varphi_*} \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B) \xrightarrow{\psi_*} \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B''),
$$
the homomorphism $\psi_* : \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B) \to \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B'')$ is epimorphic. Similarly, in the Hom-Ext sequence in the first variable, the homomorphism $\varphi^* : \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B) \to \operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda(A', B)$ is epimorphic.
