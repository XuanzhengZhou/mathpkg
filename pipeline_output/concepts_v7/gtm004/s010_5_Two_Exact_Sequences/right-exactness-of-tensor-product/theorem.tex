The functor $A \otimes_\Lambda - : {}_\Lambda\mathfrak{M} \to \mathfrak{Ab}$ is right exact. That is, for any short exact sequence $B' \xrightarrow{\mu} B \xrightarrow{\nu} B'' \to 0$ in ${}_\Lambda\mathfrak{M}$, the induced sequence
$$
A \otimes_\Lambda B' \xrightarrow{1 \otimes \mu} A \otimes_\Lambda B \xrightarrow{1 \otimes \nu} A \otimes_\Lambda B'' \to 0
$$
is exact. In general, even if $\mu$ is monomorphic, $1 \otimes \mu$ need not be monomorphic. Similarly, $- \otimes_\Lambda B$ is right exact for fixed $B$.
