Lemma 5.1 constructs a connecting homomorphism between kernel and cokernel from a commutative diagram with exact rows. This is a variant of the Snake Lemma, producing a six-term exact sequence linking the kernels and cokernels of the vertical maps. The connecting homomorphism $\omega$ is defined by lifting to $B$, pushing forward via $\beta$, and factoring through the kernel condition. This lemma serves as the key technical tool for deriving the Hom-Ext and Tor exact sequences in the subsequent theorems.
