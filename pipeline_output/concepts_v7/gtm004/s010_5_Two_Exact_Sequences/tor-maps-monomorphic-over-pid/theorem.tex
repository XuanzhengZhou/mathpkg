Let $\Lambda$ be a principal ideal domain. Then in the Tor exact sequence of Theorem 8.3, the homomorphisms
$$
\kappa_* : \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B') \to \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B) \quad\text{and}\quad \kappa_* : \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A', B) \to \operatorname{Tor}^\Lambda(A, B)
$$
are monomorphic.
