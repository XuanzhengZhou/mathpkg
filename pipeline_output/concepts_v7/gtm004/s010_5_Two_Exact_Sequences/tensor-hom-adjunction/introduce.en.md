Theorem 7.2 is the fundamental tensor-hom adjunction: for a fixed right $\Lambda$-module $A$, the tensor product $A \otimes_\Lambda -$ is left adjoint to the Hom functor $\operatorname{Hom}_\mathbb{Z}(A, -)$. The left $\Lambda$-module structure on $\operatorname{Hom}_\mathbb{Z}(A, G)$ is induced by the right module structure of $A$. This adjunction has far-reaching consequences — it implies that $A \otimes_\Lambda -$ preserves colimits (coproducts and cokernels) and is right exact, which provides the foundation for the definition of the Tor functor as its left derived functor.
