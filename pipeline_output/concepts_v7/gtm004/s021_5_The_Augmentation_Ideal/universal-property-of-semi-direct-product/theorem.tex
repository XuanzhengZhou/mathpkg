Suppose given a group $G$ and a $G$-module $A$. To every group homomorphism $f : X \to G$ and to every $f$-derivation $d : X \to A$ (where $A$ is regarded as an $X$-module via $f$), there exists a unique group homomorphism $h : X \to A \rtimes G$ such that the diagram
$$\xymatrix{
& X \ar[dl]_h \ar[dr]^f \ar@/^/[dd]^d & \\
A \rtimes G \ar[r]_p \ar[d]_q & G \\
A &
}$$
is commutative, where $p(a,x) = x$ and $q(a,x) = a$.
