Corollary 1.6 establishes the compatibility of the universal enveloping algebra functor with exact sequences of Lie algebras. It shows that the enveloping algebra of a quotient Lie algebra can be obtained by tensoring with the trivial module over the kernel. This result is the Lie algebra analogue of the isomorphism $\mathbb{Z} \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}N} \mathbb{Z}G \cong \mathbb{Z}(G/N)$ for groups, and is fundamental for the Hochschild–Serre spectral sequence relating the cohomology of $\mathfrak{g}$, $\mathfrak{n}$, and $\mathfrak{h}$.
