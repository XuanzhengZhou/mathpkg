If $\mathfrak{n} \to \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{h}$ is an exact sequence of Lie algebras (so that $\mathfrak{n}$ is a Lie ideal of $\mathfrak{g}$ and $\mathfrak{h} \cong \mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{n}$), then there is an isomorphism of right $\mathfrak{g}$-modules

$$K \otimes_{U\mathfrak{n}} U\mathfrak{g} \cong U\mathfrak{h},$$

where $K$ is regarded as the trivial $U\mathfrak{n}$-module.
