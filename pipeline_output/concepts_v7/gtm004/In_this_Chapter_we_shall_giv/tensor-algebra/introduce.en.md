The tensor algebra of a vector space is the free associative algebra generated by that vector space. Its universal property makes it the left adjoint to the forgetful functor from algebras to vector spaces. The tensor algebra is the starting point for constructing the universal enveloping algebra of a Lie algebra, as well as other important algebraic objects such as the symmetric algebra and the exterior algebra.
