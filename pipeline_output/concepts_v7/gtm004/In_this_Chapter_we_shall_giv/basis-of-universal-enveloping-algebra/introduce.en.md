Corollary 1.4 of the PBW theorem describes $U\mathfrak{g}$ as a free module over a Lie subalgebra $\mathfrak{h}$. This structural result is the Lie algebra analogue of the fact that for a subgroup $H$ of $G$, the group ring $\mathbb{Z}G$ is a free $\mathbb{Z}H$-module. It underlies the change-of-rings spectral sequence and is used to relate cohomology of $\mathfrak{g}$ to cohomology of $\mathfrak{h}$.
