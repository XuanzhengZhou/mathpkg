The canonical map $i: \mathfrak{g} \to U\mathfrak{g}$ (where $U\mathfrak{g}$ is regarded as a Lie algebra via the commutator bracket) is an embedding of Lie algebras. In particular, every Lie algebra over $K$ is isomorphic to a Lie subalgebra of a Lie algebra of the form $LA$ for some associative $K$-algebra $A$.
