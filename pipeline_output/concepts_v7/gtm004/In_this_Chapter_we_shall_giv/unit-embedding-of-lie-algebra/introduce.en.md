Corollary 1.3 of the PBW theorem states that the natural map from a Lie algebra to its universal enveloping algebra is injective. This fact justifies viewing every Lie algebra as a subalgebra of an associative algebra under commutator, and underlies the equivalence between Lie algebra representations and $U\mathfrak{g}$-modules. It guarantees that no information is lost when passing from a Lie algebra to its enveloping algebra.
