A Lie algebra homomorphism is a linear map between Lie algebras that preserves the Lie bracket. It is the natural notion of structure-preserving map in the category of Lie algebras. Together with Lie algebras as objects, Lie algebra homomorphisms form a category that serves as the domain for derived functor cohomology theories.
