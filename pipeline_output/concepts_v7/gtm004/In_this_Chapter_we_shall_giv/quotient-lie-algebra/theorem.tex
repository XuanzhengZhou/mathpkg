If $\mathfrak{h}$ is a Lie ideal of $\mathfrak{g}$, then the quotient vector space $\mathfrak{g}/\mathfrak{h}$ carries a natural Lie algebra structure induced by the Lie bracket in $\mathfrak{g}$, given by

$$[x + \mathfrak{h}, \; y + \mathfrak{h}] = [x, y] + \mathfrak{h}, \quad x, y \in \mathfrak{g}.$$
