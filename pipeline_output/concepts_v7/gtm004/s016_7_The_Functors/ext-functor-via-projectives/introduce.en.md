The contravariant Ext functors $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(-, B)$ are defined as the right derived functors of the contravariant Hom functor $\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)$. For a given $A$-module $A$, one computes $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B)$ by taking a projective resolution $P$ of $A$, applying $\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)$ to obtain a cochain complex, and then taking its $n$-th cohomology. In degree zero, left exactness of $\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)$ yields the identification $\operatorname{Ext}_A^0(A, B) = \operatorname{Hom}_A(A, B)$. This definition mirrors the classical Ext groups but expressed in the general framework of derived functors.
