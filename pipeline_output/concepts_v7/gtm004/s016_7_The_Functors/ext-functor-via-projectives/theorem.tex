The (contravariant) functor $\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)$ is additive. We define the right derived functors of $\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)$:

$$\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(-, B) = R^n(\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)), \quad n = 0, 1, 2, \dots$$

Concretely, the abelian group $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(A, B)$ is computed by choosing a projective resolution $P$ of $A$ and taking cohomology in the cochain complex $\operatorname{Hom}_A(P, B)$. Since $\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)$ is left exact, it follows that

$$\operatorname{Ext}_A^0(A, B) = \operatorname{Hom}_A(A, B).$$
