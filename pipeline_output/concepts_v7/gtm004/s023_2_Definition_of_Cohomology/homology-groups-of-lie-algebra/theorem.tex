For a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ over $K$ and a right $\mathfrak{g}$-module $B$, the homology groups of $\mathfrak{g}$ with coefficients in $B$ are defined by
$$H_n(\mathfrak{g}, B) = \text{Tor}_n^{\mathfrak{g}}(B, K), \quad n \geq 0.$$
In low degrees one has:
$$H_0(\mathfrak{g}, B) = B / B\mathfrak{g},$$
where $B\mathfrak{g}$ is the submodule generated by $\{b \circ x \mid b \in B, x \in \mathfrak{g}\}$, and
$$H_1(\mathfrak{g}, B) = \ker\bigl(B \otimes_{\mathfrak{g}} I\mathfrak{g} \rightarrow B \otimes_{\mathfrak{g}} U\mathfrak{g}\bigr).$$
For $B$ a trivial $\mathfrak{g}$-module, $H_1(\mathfrak{g}, B) \cong B \otimes_K \mathfrak{g}_{\text{ab}}$, where $\mathfrak{g}_{\text{ab}} = \mathfrak{g}/[\mathfrak{g},\mathfrak{g}]$.
