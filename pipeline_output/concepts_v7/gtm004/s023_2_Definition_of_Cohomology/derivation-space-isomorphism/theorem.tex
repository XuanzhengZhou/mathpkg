The vector space $\text{Der}(\mathfrak{g}, A)$ of derivations from $\mathfrak{g}$ into $A$ is naturally isomorphic to the vector space of Lie algebra homomorphisms
$$\{ f : \mathfrak{g} \to A \rtimes \mathfrak{g} \mid p_{\mathfrak{g}} \circ f = 1_{\mathfrak{g}} \},$$
where $p_{\mathfrak{g}} : A \rtimes \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}$ is the canonical projection. The mutually inverse maps are given by $d_f = p_A \circ f$ and $f_d(x) = (d(x), x)$ for $x \in \mathfrak{g}$.
