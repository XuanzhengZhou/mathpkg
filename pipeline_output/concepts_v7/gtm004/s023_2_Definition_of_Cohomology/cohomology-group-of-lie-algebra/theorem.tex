Given a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ over a field $K$ and a $\mathfrak{g}$-module $A$, the $n^{\text{th}}$ cohomology group of $\mathfrak{g}$ with coefficients in $A$ is defined by
$$H^n(\mathfrak{g}, A) = \text{Ext}_{\mathfrak{g}}^n(K, A), \quad n = 0, 1, \ldots$$
where $K$ is regarded as a trivial $\mathfrak{g}$-module, and $\text{Ext}_{\mathfrak{g}}^n$ denotes $\text{Ext}_{U\mathfrak{g}}^n$.
