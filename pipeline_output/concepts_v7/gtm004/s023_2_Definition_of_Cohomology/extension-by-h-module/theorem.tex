An extension of a Lie algebra $\mathfrak{h}$ by an $\mathfrak{h}$-module $A$ is an extension of Lie algebras
$$A \xrightarrow{i} \mathfrak{g} \xrightarrow{p} \mathfrak{h}$$
with abelian kernel, such that the given $\mathfrak{h}$-module structure on $A$ coincides with the $\mathfrak{h}$-module structure induced by the extension via a (hence any) section.
