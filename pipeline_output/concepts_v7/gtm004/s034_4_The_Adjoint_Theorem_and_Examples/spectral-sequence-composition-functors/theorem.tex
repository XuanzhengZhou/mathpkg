Let $\mathfrak{U} \xrightarrow{J} \mathfrak{V} \xrightarrow{I} \mathfrak{W}$ be functors between small categories and let $\mathfrak{A}$ have enough projectives. Then there is a spectral sequence
$$
E_1^{p,q} = H_p(I, H_{q-p}(J, -)) \Rightarrow H_q(IJ, -).
$$
Here $H_*(J, -)$ and $H_*(I, -)$ are the $J$-homology and $I$-homology functors, and $H_*(IJ, -)$ is the homology for the composite functor $IJ$.
