Let $\mathfrak{A}$ have enough projectives and exact coproducts. If $R \in [\mathfrak{U}, \mathfrak{A}]$ is an $\mathcal{E}_0$-projective functor, then
$$
(L_n^{\mathcal{E}_1} \tilde{J}) R = 0 \quad \text{for all } n \geq 1.
$$
Consequently, the connected sequences of functors $\{L_n^{\mathcal{E}_1} \tilde{J}\}$ and $\{L_n^{\mathcal{E}_0} \tilde{J}\}$ are equivalent.
