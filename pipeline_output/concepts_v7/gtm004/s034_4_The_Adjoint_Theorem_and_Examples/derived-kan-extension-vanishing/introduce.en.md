This proposition shows that under the hypotheses of enough projectives and exact coproducts, $\mathcal{E}_0$-projective functors are acyclic for the derived Kan extension with respect to the larger projective class $\mathcal{E}_1$. As a result, the absolute $J$-homology (derived with respect to $\mathcal{E}_1$) coincides with the relative $J$-homology (derived with respect to $\mathcal{E}_0$). The proof reduces to checking the case of a single-object functor $\tilde{K}_U A$ by additivity and the explicit description of projectives from Corollary 5.4.
