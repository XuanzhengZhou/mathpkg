This corollary provides an explicit formula for the left Kan extension along the inclusion of the discrete subcategory $\mathfrak{B}_d$ into $\mathfrak{B}$. The value at an object $V'$ is a coproduct over all objects $V$ and all morphisms from $V$ to $V'$, where each copy of $A_V$ is indexed by a morphism. This construction is the categorical formalization of the "free functor" on a family of objects indexed by the objects of $\mathfrak{B}$, and it is central to understanding projective objects in functor categories.
