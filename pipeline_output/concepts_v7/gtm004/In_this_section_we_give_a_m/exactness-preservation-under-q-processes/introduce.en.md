Theorem 4.4 guarantees that exactness is inherited when passing from the bottom row of the ladder diagram to all derived rows via Q-processes. Since $\gamma^{-1}(0) \subseteq \gamma^{-1}(C_1)$, the subobject construction preserves the kernel condition at the middle term. This theorem is crucial because it ensures that each rung of the ladder remains an exact couple, so the entire spectral sequence machinery applies at every level.
