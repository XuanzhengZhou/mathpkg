If the bottom row of the Q-process ladder diagram (4.4) is exact, then all rows of diagrams (4.4) and (4.7) are exact. That is, exactness is preserved under the application of $Q^{\varrho}$ and $Q_{\sigma}$ processes.

More precisely, if the sequence

$$A \xrightarrow{\beta} B \xrightarrow{\gamma} C$$

is exact at $B$, then $\gamma^{-1}(0) \subseteq \gamma^{-1}(C_1)$ ensures that the middle row of (4.4) is exact, and the remaining exactness assertions follow by duality.
