In the ladder of an exact couple, for each $m, n$ the square

$$\begin{array}{ccc}
E_{m,n} & \xrightarrow{\sigma_{m,n}} & E_{m+1,n} \\
\varrho_{m,n} \downarrow & & \downarrow \varrho_{m+1,n} \\
E_{m,n+1} & \xrightarrow{\sigma_{m,n+1}} & E_{m+1,n+1}
\end{array}$$

is bicartesian (i.e., both a pull-back and a push-out). Here $\varrho_{m,n}$ and $\sigma_{m,n}$ are the morphisms induced by the Q-processes applied to the ladder sequence (4.17).
