Let $A \xrightarrow{\beta} B \xrightarrow{\gamma} C$ be a diagram in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$, and let $\varrho: C_1 \rightarrowtail C$ and $\varrho_1: C_2 \rightarrowtail C_1$ be monomorphisms such that $\gamma\beta$ factors through $\varrho\varrho_1$. Then, for $(\beta', \gamma') = Q^{\varrho}(\beta, \gamma)$, the composite $\gamma'\beta'$ factors through $\varrho_1$, and

$$Q^{\varrho\varrho_1}(\beta, \gamma) = Q^{\varrho_1}Q^{\varrho}(\beta, \gamma).$$

Dually, for epimorphisms $\sigma: A \twoheadrightarrow A_1$ and $\sigma_1: A_1 \twoheadrightarrow A_2$,

$$Q_{\sigma_1\sigma}(\beta, \gamma) = Q_{\sigma_1}Q_{\sigma}(\beta, \gamma).$$
