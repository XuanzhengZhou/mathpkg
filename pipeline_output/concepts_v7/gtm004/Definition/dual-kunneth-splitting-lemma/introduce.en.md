Lemma 3.2 provides the technical foundation for splitting the dual Künneth exact sequence. When both chain complexes are free, a homomorphism on homology can be canonically lifted to a cycle in the Hom complex, and any two such lifts differ by a boundary. The construction uses a fixed splitting of each $D_{p+n}$ into cycles and a complement, together with the inverse of the differential restricted to that complement. This lemma is the dual counterpart to the splitting construction for the ordinary Künneth theorem.
