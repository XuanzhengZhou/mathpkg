Let $C$, $D$ be chain complexes over a principal ideal domain $\Lambda$, and suppose that either $C$ or $D$ is flat. Then there is a natural short exact sequence
$$0 \rightarrow \bigoplus_{p+q=n} H_p(C) \otimes_\Lambda H_q(D) \xrightarrow{\zeta} H_n(C \otimes_\Lambda D) \xrightarrow{\omega} \bigoplus_{p+q=n-1} \operatorname{Tor}_1^\Lambda(H_p(C), H_q(D)) \rightarrow 0,$$
where $\zeta$ is induced by the inclusion of representative cycles. Moreover, the sequence splits, but not naturally.
