Proposition 4.1 establishes that the tensor product of chain complexes is associative up to natural chain equivalence. This is the chain-level analog of the familiar associativity of tensor products of modules. The result is used in Section 4 as the starting point for deriving associativity relations between the derived functors Tor and Ext by applying the Künneth formula to both sides of the equivalence and comparing homology groups.
