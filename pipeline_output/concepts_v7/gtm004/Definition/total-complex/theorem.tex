Let $B = \{B_{p,q}\}$ be a double complex of chains with differentials $\partial'$ and $\partial''$. The \text{total complex} $\operatorname{Tot} B$ is the graded module defined by
$$(\operatorname{Tot} B)_n = \bigoplus_{p+q=n} B_{p,q},$$
with differential
$$\partial = \partial' + \partial'' : (\operatorname{Tot} B)_n \rightarrow (\operatorname{Tot} B)_{n-1}.$$
The identity $\partial^2 = 0$ follows from $\partial'\partial' = 0$, $\partial''\partial'' = 0$, and $\partial''\partial' + \partial'\partial'' = 0$.
