Theorem 4.3 establishes associativity relations connecting the four fundamental bifunctors $\operatorname{Hom}$, $\operatorname{Ext}$, $\operatorname{Tor}$, and $\otimes$ for abelian groups. The natural isomorphism $\operatorname{Ext}(\operatorname{Tor}(A', A), A'') \cong \operatorname{Ext}(A', \operatorname{Ext}(A, A''))$ shows that $\operatorname{Ext}$ is "associative" in a precise sense. The theorem is derived from the dual Künneth formula applied to the Hom-Tensor adjunction for chain complexes. Together with Theorem 4.2, these results reveal the underlying algebraic structure that is obscured by the traditional left-side notation for Hom, Ext, and Tor.
