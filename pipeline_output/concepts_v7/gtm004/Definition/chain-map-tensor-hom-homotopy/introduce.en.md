Proposition 1.2 establishes the functoriality of the tensor product and Hom constructions for chain complexes: chain maps between complexes induce chain maps between their tensor products and Hom complexes. Moreover, this construction respects the chain homotopy relation, so that homotopic maps induce homotopic maps. This is the foundation for the homotopy-invariance properties used throughout homological algebra.
