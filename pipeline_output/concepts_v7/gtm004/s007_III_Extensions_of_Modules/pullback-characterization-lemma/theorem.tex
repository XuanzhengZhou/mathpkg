Consider the commutative square
$$
\begin{CD}
Y @>{\alpha}>> A \\
@V{\beta}VV @VV{\varphi}V \\
B @>>{\psi}> X
\end{CD}
$$
This square is a pullback if and only if the sequence
$$
0 \longrightarrow Y \xrightarrow{\{\alpha,\beta\}} A \oplus B \xrightarrow{\langle \varphi, -\psi \rangle} X
$$
is exact, where $\{\alpha,\beta\}(y) = (\alpha y, \beta y)$ and $\langle \varphi, -\psi \rangle(a,b) = \varphi a - \psi b$.
