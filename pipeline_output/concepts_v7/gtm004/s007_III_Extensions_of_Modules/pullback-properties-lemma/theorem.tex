Let
$$
\begin{CD}
Y @>{\alpha}>> A \\
@V{\beta}VV @VV{\varphi}V \\
B @>>{\psi}> X
\end{CD}
$$
be a pullback diagram. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $\beta$ induces a homomorphism $\ker \alpha \to \ker \psi$;
\item[(ii)] if $\psi$ is an epimorphism, then $\alpha$ is also an epimorphism.
\end{enumerate}
