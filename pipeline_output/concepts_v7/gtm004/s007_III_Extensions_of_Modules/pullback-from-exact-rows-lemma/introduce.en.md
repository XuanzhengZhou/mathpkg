Lemma 1.3 provides a partial converse to Lemma 1.2(i). Given a morphism of short exact sequences (a commutative diagram with exact rows where the map on $B$ is the identity), the right-hand square is automatically a pullback. This result is fundamental for the functoriality of $E(-,-)$: it ensures that the pullback construction used to define $\alpha^*$ is compatible with equivalence of extensions.
