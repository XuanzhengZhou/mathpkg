Let
$$
\begin{CD}
B @>{\kappa'}>> E' @>{\nu'}>> A' \\
@| @V{\xi}VV @VV{\alpha}V \\
B @>>{\kappa}> E @>>{\nu}> A
\end{CD}
$$
be a commutative diagram with exact rows (each row is a short exact sequence). Then the right-hand square
$$
\begin{CD}
E' @>{\nu'}>> A' \\
@V{\xi}VV @VV{\alpha}V \\
E @>>{\nu}> A
\end{CD}
$$
is a pullback diagram.
