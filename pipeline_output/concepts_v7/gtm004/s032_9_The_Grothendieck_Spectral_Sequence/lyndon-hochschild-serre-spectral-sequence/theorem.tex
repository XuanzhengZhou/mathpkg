Let

$$N \xrightarrow{\iota} K \xrightarrow{\pi} Q$$

be a short exact sequence of groups (so $N$ is a normal subgroup of $K$ with quotient $Q$) and let $A$ be a $K$-module. Then there is a natural action of $Q$ on the cohomology groups $H^m(N, A)$. Moreover, there exists a spectral sequence $\{E_n(A)\}$ such that

$$
E_1^{p,q} = H^p(Q, H^{q-p}(N, A)) \Rightarrow H^q(K, A),
$$

which converges finitely to the graded group associated with $\{H^q(K, A)\}$, suitably filtered.
