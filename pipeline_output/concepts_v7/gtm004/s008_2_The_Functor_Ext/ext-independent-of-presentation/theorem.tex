For any two projective presentations $R \to P \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} A$ and $R' \to P' \xrightarrow{\varepsilon'} A$ of the same $\Lambda$-module $A$, there is a natural equivalence
$$\text{Ext}_\Lambda^\varepsilon(A, B) \cong \text{Ext}_\Lambda^{\varepsilon'}(A, B)$$
for every $\Lambda$-module $B$. Consequently, the superscript may be dropped and one writes simply $\text{Ext}_\Lambda(A, B)$.
