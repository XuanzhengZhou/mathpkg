Given a module homomorphism $\beta \colon B_1 \to B_2$, one obtains an induced map $\beta_{*}$ on Hom-sets by post-composition: each $\varphi \colon A \to B_1$ is sent to $\beta \circ \varphi \colon A \to B_2$. The resulting map $\beta_{*} = \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, \beta)$ is itself a homomorphism of abelian groups. This construction makes $\operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, -)$ a covariant functor from the category of $\Lambda$-modules to the category of abelian groups.
