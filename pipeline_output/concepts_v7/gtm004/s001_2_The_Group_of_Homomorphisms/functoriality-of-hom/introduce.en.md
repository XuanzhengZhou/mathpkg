The assignment $B \mapsto \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, B)$ together with the induced maps $\beta_{*} = \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, \beta)$ satisfies the two axioms of a covariant functor: it respects composition, i.e., $(\beta' \circ \beta)_{*} = \beta'_{*} \circ \beta_{*}$, and it preserves identity morphisms. These rules are elementary to verify and establish $\operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, -)$ as a functor from $\Lambda$-Mod to $\mathbf{Ab}$.
