Theorem 5.3 is the main result of Section VIII.5, establishing the rigorous categorical foundation for the $E_{\infty}$ terms of a spectral sequence. Using limits and colimits, it shows that $E_{\infty,\infty} = \varprojlim_m \varprojlim_n E_{m,n}$ and provides exact sequences characterizing all limit terms. Remarkably, the limit and colimit processes commute in this setting, and the result holds in any abelian category with the required limits, without element-based diagram-chasing arguments.
