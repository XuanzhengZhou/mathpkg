This theorem establishes a spectral sequence that computes the homology of a composition of functors $IJ$ in terms of the homologies of the individual functors $I$ and $J$. It is the categorical analog of the Grothendieck spectral sequence for composed derived functors. The key observation is that projectives in $[\mathfrak{U}, \mathfrak{A}]$ are transformed by the Kan extension $\tilde{J}$ into $\tilde{I}$-acyclic objects in $[\mathfrak{B}, \mathfrak{A}]$.
