This corollary gives an explicit formula for the Kan extension when the domain category is the discrete category $\mathfrak{B}_d$ on the objects of $\mathfrak{B}$. The Kan extension $\tilde{I}$ of a collection of objects $\{A_V\}_{V \in \mathfrak{B}}$ indexed by objects of $\mathfrak{B}$ is expressed as a double coproduct: for each $V'$, one takes the coproduct over all $V$ and all morphisms $v: V' \rightarrow V$ of the object $A_V$. This formula is a consequence of Lemma 5.5 and Proposition 5.3.
