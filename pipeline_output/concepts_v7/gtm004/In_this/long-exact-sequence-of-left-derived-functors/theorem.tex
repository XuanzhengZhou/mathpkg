Let $T: \mathfrak{M}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$ be an additive covariant functor and let
$$A' \xrightarrow{\alpha'} A \xrightarrow{\alpha''} A''$$
be a short exact sequence in $\mathfrak{M}_A$. Then there exist connecting homomorphisms
$$\omega_n: L_n T A'' \rightarrow L_{n-1} T A', \quad n = 1, 2, \ldots$$
such that the following sequence is exact:
$$\cdots \rightarrow L_n T A' \xrightarrow{L_n T \alpha'} L_n T A \xrightarrow{L_n T \alpha''} L_n T A'' \xrightarrow{\omega_n} L_{n-1} T A' \rightarrow \cdots$$
$$\cdots \rightarrow L_1 T A'' \xrightarrow{\omega_1} L_0 T A' \xrightarrow{L_0 T \alpha'} L_0 T A \xrightarrow{L_0 T \alpha''} L_0 T A'' \rightarrow 0.$$
