Projectivity is preserved under arbitrary direct sums and passes to direct summands. That is, a direct sum $\bigoplus_i P_i$ is projective if and only if each individual summand $P_i$ is projective. The proof uses the universal property of the direct sum to assemble lifts for each summand into a lift for the whole sum, and conversely, to restrict a lift for the sum to each individual summand.
