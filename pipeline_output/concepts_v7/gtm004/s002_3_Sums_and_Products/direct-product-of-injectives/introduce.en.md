This is the dual statement to Proposition 4.5. Just as a direct sum is projective if and only if each summand is projective, a direct product is injective if and only if each factor is injective. The proof follows by dualizing the argument for projectives: the universal property of the direct product (with projections) replaces that of the direct sum (with injections), and the extension property of injectives replaces the lifting property of projectives.
