Every $\Lambda$-module $A$ can be expressed as an epimorphic image (quotient) of a free $\Lambda$-module. To construct this, take a set $S$ of generators of $A$ and form the free module $P = \bigoplus_{s \in S} \Lambda_s$ with $\Lambda_s = \Lambda$. The surjective homomorphism $\varphi: P \to A$ is the unique extension of the assignment sending the identity element of $\Lambda_s$ to the generator $s$. This shows that every module has a free presentation.
