An abelian Lie algebra is one in which the Lie bracket is identically zero. In such an algebra, the Jacobi identity holds trivially, and the structure reduces to that of a plain vector space. Abelian Lie algebras correspond to commutative Lie groups and are the simplest class of Lie algebras.
