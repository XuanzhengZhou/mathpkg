This corollary of the PBW theorem provides an explicit $\mathfrak{h}$-module basis of the universal enveloping algebra, extending a basis of the subalgebra $\mathfrak{h}$ by ordered monomials in a complement. It is a key structural result used to relate the homological algebra of $\mathfrak{g}$ to that of its subalgebra $\mathfrak{h}$.
