For any Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$ over a field $K$, the \textbf{abelianization} $\mathfrak{g}_{\mathrm{ab}}$ is the largest abelian quotient of $\mathfrak{g}$. Its kernel is the derived subalgebra $[\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g}]$, which is the Lie subalgebra generated by all brackets $[x, y]$ with $x, y \in \mathfrak{g}$. Thus
$$
\mathfrak{g}_{\mathrm{ab}} = \mathfrak{g} / [\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g}].
$$
