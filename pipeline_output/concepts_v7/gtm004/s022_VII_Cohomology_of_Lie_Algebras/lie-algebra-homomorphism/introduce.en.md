A Lie algebra homomorphism is a linear map between Lie algebras that respects the Lie bracket. This is the natural notion of a structure-preserving map in the category of Lie algebras. Together with Lie algebras as objects and Lie algebra homomorphisms as morphisms, one obtains the category of Lie algebras over a field.
