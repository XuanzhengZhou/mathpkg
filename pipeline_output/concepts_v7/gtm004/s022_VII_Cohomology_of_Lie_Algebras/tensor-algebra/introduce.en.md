The tensor algebra $TM$ of a vector space $M$ is the free associative $K$-algebra generated by $M$. It is a graded algebra whose degree-$n$ component is the $n$-fold tensor power of $M$. Its universal property makes it the left adjoint to the forgetful functor from algebras to vector spaces, meaning any linear map from $M$ into an algebra extends uniquely to an algebra homomorphism from $TM$.
