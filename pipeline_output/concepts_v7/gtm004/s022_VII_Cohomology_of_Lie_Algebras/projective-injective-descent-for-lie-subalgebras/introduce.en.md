This corollary states that the restriction-of-scalars functor from $\mathfrak{g}$-modules to $\mathfrak{h}$-modules preserves projectives and injectives. It follows from Corollary 1.4 (the PBW basis result) together with the general homological criterion that a ring extension making the larger ring free over the smaller one preserves projectivity and injectivity upon restriction.
