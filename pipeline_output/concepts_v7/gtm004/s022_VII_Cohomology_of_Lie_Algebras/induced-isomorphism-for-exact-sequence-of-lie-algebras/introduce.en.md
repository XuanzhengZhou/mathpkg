This corollary relates the universal enveloping algebras in a short exact sequence of Lie algebras. The isomorphism $K \otimes_{U\mathfrak{n}} U\mathfrak{g} \cong U\mathfrak{h}$ is the Lie-algebraic analogue of the fact that for groups, $\mathbb{Z} \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}N} \mathbb{Z}G \cong \mathbb{Z}[G/N]$ when $N 	rianglelefteq G$. It plays a crucial role in the Lyndon–Hochschild–Serre spectral sequence for Lie algebra cohomology.
