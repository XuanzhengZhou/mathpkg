The universal enveloping algebra $U\mathfrak{g}$ is the universal associative algebra that encodes the Lie algebra structure of $\mathfrak{g}$. The defining relations translate the Lie bracket into the commutator in $U\mathfrak{g}$: $[x, y]$ becomes $xy - yx$. This construction is fundamental because it allows one to study representations of $\mathfrak{g}$ as modules over the associative algebra $U\mathfrak{g}$.
