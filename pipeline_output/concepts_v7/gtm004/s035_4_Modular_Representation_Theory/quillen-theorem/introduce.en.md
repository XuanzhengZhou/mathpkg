Quillen's theorem (formerly the Atiyah-Swan conjecture) is a landmark result in group cohomology. It identifies the Krull dimension of the cohomology ring $H^*(G,k)$ with the maximal rank of an elementary abelian $p$-subgroup of $G$. The proof uses deep methods from algebraic topology and has had profound consequences for both cohomology theory and modular representation theory.
