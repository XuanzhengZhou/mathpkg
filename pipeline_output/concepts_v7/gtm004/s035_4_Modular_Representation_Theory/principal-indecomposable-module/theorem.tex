\begin{definition}[Principal Indecomposable Module]
\label{def:principal-indecomposable-module}
Let $kG$ be the group algebra of a finite group $G$ over a field $k$. The \textbf{principal indecomposable modules} of $kG$ are the indecomposable direct summands of the left regular module $kG$. They correspond bijectively to the isomorphism classes of simple $kG$-modules.
\end{definition}
