Let $G$ be a finite group and let $k$ be a field of characteristic $p$ dividing $|G|$. Let $S_0 = k, S_1, \ldots, S_r$ be a complete set of representatives of isomorphism classes of simple $kG$-modules. The {\it extension graph} $\Gamma(G)$ has vertices $\{S_0, S_1, \ldots, S_r\}$ and an edge connecting $S_i$ and $S_j$ if and only if
\[
\operatorname{Ext}_{kG}^1(S_i, S_j) \neq 0 \quad \text{or} \quad \operatorname{Ext}_{kG}^1(S_j, S_i) \neq 0.
\]
