Let $G$ be a finite group and $k$ a field of characteristic $p$. The Krull dimension $c(G)$ of the cohomology algebra $H^*(G, k)$ satisfies
\[
c(G) = \max_{E} c(E),
\]
where $E$ runs through the elementary abelian $p$-subgroups of $G$. Since $c(E) = n$ for an elementary abelian $p$-group of order $p^n$, it follows that $c(G)$ equals the $p$-rank of $G$, i.e., the maximal rank of an elementary abelian $p$-subgroup of $G$.
