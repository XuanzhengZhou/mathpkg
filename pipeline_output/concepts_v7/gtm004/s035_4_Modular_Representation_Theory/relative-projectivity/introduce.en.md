Relative projectivity generalizes the notion of a projective module by replacing the requirement that all surjections onto $M$ split with the condition that only those surjections which split upon restriction to a subgroup $U$ must split over $G$. This concept is central to J.A. Green's theory of vertices and sources, which classifies indecomposable modules by the minimal subgroups relative to which they are projective.
