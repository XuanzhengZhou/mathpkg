\begin{definition}[Extension Graph $\Gamma(G)$]
\label{def:extension-graph}
Let $G$ be a finite group and let $k$ be a field. The \textbf{extension graph} $\Gamma(G)$ is defined as follows:
\begin{itemize}
\item The vertices of $\Gamma(G)$ correspond bijectively to the isomorphism classes of simple $kG$-modules $S_0 = k, S_1, \ldots, S_i$.
\item The graph $\Gamma(G)$ has an edge connecting $S_i$ and $S_j$ if and only if $\operatorname{Ext}_k^1(S_i, S_j) \neq 0$ or $\operatorname{Ext}_k^1(S_j, S_i) \neq 0$.
\end{itemize}
\end{definition}
