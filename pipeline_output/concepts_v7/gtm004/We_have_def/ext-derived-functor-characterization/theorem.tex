Let $T : \mathfrak{M}_\Lambda \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$ be a right exact functor and let $A$ be a $\Lambda$-module. Then there are natural isomorphisms

$$\Gamma : [\operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^q(A, -), T] \xrightarrow{\cong} L_q TA, \quad q = 0, 1, \dots$$

where $L_q T$ denotes the $q$-th left derived functor of $T$.
