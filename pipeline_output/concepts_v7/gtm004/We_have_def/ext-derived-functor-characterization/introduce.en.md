When the additive covariant functor $T$ is right exact, its left satellite functors coincide with its left derived functors. Thus Theorem 10.1 specializes to a clean characterization: natural transformations from $\operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^q(A, -)$ to $T$ are in natural bijection with $L_q TA$. This provides an alternative, conceptually elegant definition of left derived functors entirely in terms of Ext and natural transformations.
