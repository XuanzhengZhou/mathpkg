The Tor functor $\operatorname{Tor}_n^\Lambda(A, B)$ is defined as the $n$-th left derived functor of the tensor product $A \otimes_\Lambda -$ applied to $B$. Equivalently, it is the $n$-th homology of $A \otimes_\Lambda P$ for any projective resolution $P$ of $B$. The functor $\operatorname{Tor}_n^\Lambda(-, -)$ is a bifunctor, vanishes on projective arguments in positive degrees, and converts short exact sequences in the second variable into long exact sequences.
