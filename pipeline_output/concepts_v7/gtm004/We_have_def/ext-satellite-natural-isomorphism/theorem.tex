Let $T : \mathfrak{M}_\Lambda \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$ be an additive covariant functor and let $A$ be a $\Lambda$-module. Then there are natural isomorphisms

$$\Gamma : [\operatorname{Ext}_\Lambda^q(A, -), T] \xrightarrow{\cong} \tilde{L}_q TA, \quad q = 0, 1, \dots$$

where $\tilde{L}_q T$ denotes the $q$-th left satellite of $T$, and $[S, S']$ denotes the set of natural transformations from $S$ to $S'$.
