This proposition states that the natural transformation $\Phi$ relating Ext groups across a change-of-rings adjunction is an isomorphism when the coefficient module $A'$ is flat. The flatness condition ensures that the left adjoint $F = A' \otimes_\Lambda -$ is exact, so a projective resolution of $A$ maps to a projective resolution of $F(A)$, yielding the equivalence directly from the definition of Ext.
