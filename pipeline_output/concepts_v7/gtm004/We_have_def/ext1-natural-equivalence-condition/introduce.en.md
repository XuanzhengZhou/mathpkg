This theorem classifies when a homomorphism $\alpha : A' \rightarrow A$ induces a natural equivalence between the corresponding $\operatorname{Ext}^1$ functors. The condition is that $\alpha$ is, up to isomorphism, a split projection from a direct sum with a projective module. The proof uses the long exact Ext sequence and reduces the general case to the $q=1$ case via projective presentations and induction.
