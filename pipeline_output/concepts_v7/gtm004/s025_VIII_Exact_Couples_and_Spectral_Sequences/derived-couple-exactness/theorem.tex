Let $EC = \{D, E, \alpha, \beta, \gamma\}$ be an exact couple in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$. Define
\begin{align*}
D_1 &= \alpha D, \\
E_1 &= H(E, d_0) \quad \text{where } d_0 = \beta\gamma, \\
\alpha_1 &= \alpha|_{D_1}, \\
\beta_1(\alpha x) &= [\beta x], \\
\gamma_1[z] &= \gamma(z).
\end{align*}
Then the \textbf{derived couple} $EC_1 = \{D_1, E_1, \alpha_1, \beta_1, \gamma_1\}$ is an exact couple.
