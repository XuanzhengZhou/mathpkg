Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an abelian category. An \textbf{exact couple} $EC = \{D, E, \alpha, \beta, \gamma\}$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ is an exact triangle of morphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$,

$$\begin{tikzcd}
D \arrow[rr, "\alpha"] & & D \arrow[dl, "\gamma"] \\
& E \arrow[ul, "\beta"] &
\end{tikzcd}$$

That is, the sequence
$$D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D \xrightarrow{\beta} E \xrightarrow{\gamma} D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D$$
is exact at each vertex.
