A morphism of spectral sequences is a collection of morphisms $\varphi_n: E_n \to E'_n$ between corresponding terms that is compatible with taking homology: the morphism induced by $\varphi_n$ on homology coincides with $\varphi_{n+1}$. This compatibility condition ensures that the morphism respects the iterative structure of the spectral sequence. Together with spectral sequences, these morphisms form the category $\mathfrak{E}(\mathfrak{A})$ of spectral sequences in $\mathfrak{A}$.
