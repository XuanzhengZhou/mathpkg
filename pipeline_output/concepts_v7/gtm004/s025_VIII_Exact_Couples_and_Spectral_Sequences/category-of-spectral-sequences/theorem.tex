Let $\mathfrak{A}$ be an abelian category. The \textbf{category of spectral sequences} in $\mathfrak{A}$, denoted $\mathfrak{E}$ or $\mathfrak{E}(\mathfrak{A})$, has as objects spectral sequences $E = \{(E_n, d_n)\}$ in $\mathfrak{A}$ and as morphisms the morphisms of spectral sequences $\varphi = \{\varphi_n\}: E \to E'$ satisfying $H(\varphi_n) = \varphi_{n+1}$ for all $n$.
