To compute $\text{Ext}(\mathbb{Z}_r, \mathbb{Z})$, one uses the standard projective presentation of the cyclic group $\mathbb{Z}_r$: the map $\mu: \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}$ given by multiplication by $r$, followed by the quotient map $\varepsilon: \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}_r$. Applying $\text{Hom}(-, \mathbb{Z})$ yields an exact sequence from which $\text{Ext}(\mathbb{Z}_r, \mathbb{Z})$ is the cokernel of $\mu^*$. Since $\mu^*$ on $\text{Hom}(\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}) \cong \mathbb{Z}$ is also multiplication by $r$, the cokernel is $\mathbb{Z}_r$. Thus $\text{Ext}(\mathbb{Z}_r, \mathbb{Z}) \cong \mathbb{Z}_r$.
