In any Rees system (6.9), the exact couples $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$ yield the same spectral sequence:
$$SS(\textcircled{1}) = SS(\textcircled{2}).$$

This follows because the two exact couples share the same $E$-term and the morphisms connecting them ensure that the successive derived couples of $\textcircled{1}$ and $\textcircled{2}$ have the same $E_r$-terms for all $r \geq 1$.
