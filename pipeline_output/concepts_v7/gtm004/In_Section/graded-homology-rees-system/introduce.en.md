Proposition 6.6 locates the graded homology $Gr \circ H(C)$ of a filtered chain complex within the Rees system associated to $F(C)$. The result expresses $Gr \circ H(C)$ as the quotient $i_A H(A)/i_B H(B)$, where $i_A$ and $i_B$ are the homology morphisms induced by the inclusion maps from the filtered pieces. This identification is fundamental for formulating the convergence criterion $E_\infty \cong Gr \circ H(C)$ purely in terms of the Rees system, without explicit reference to the original complex $C$.
