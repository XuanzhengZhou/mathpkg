\textbf{Proposition 6.6.} For the triple $F(C)$ obtained from a filtered chain complex $C$, let $G = G(C)$ and $A = A(C)$. Then
$$Gr \circ H(C) = i_A H(A) \,/\, i_B H(B) = \ker j_A \,/\, \ker j_B,$$
where $i_A : H(A) \to H(G)$ and $i_B : H(B) \to H(G)$ are the morphisms induced by the inclusions, and $j_A, j_B$ are the corresponding projection morphisms in the exact couples. Explicitly,
\begin{align*}
i_A H(A) &= \bigoplus_p \operatorname{im} H(C^{(p)}), \\
i_B H(B) &= \bigoplus_p \operatorname{im} H(C^{(p-1)}),
\end{align*}
so that
$$i_A H(A) \,/\, i_B H(B) = \bigoplus_p \operatorname{im} H(C^{(p)}) \,/\, \operatorname{im} H(C^{(p-1)}).$$
