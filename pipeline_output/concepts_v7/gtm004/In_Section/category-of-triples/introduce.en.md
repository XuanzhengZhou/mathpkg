The category of triples $\mathfrak{T}(\mathfrak{A}, d)$ is an auxiliary construction used to bridge filtered differential objects and Rees systems. Its objects package a differential object with a distinguished differential subobject and a compatible automorphism. The functor $F$ maps filtered chain complexes into $\mathfrak{T}(\mathfrak{A}^{\mathbb{Z}}, d)$, and subsequently a functor $R$ maps $\mathfrak{T}$ into the category of special Rees systems $\mathfrak{S}$, yielding the commutative diagram that connects filtered complexes to spectral sequences.
