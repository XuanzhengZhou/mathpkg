\text{Two extensions $B \xrightarrow{\kappa_1} E_1 \xrightarrow{\nu_1} A$ and $B \xrightarrow{\kappa_2} E_2 \xrightarrow{\nu_2} A$ are \textbf{equivalent} if there exists a homomorphism $\xi: E_1 \to E_2$ making the diagram}
$$
\begin{CD}
B @>{\kappa_1}>> E_1 @>{\nu_1}>> A \\
@| @V{\xi}VV @| \\
B @>{\kappa_2}>> E_2 @>{\nu_2}>> A
\end{CD}
$$
\text{commutative. By Lemma I.1.1, $\xi$ is necessarily an isomorphism, making this an equivalence relation.}
