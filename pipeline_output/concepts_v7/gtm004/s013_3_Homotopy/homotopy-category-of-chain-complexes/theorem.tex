\text{The \text{homotopy category} is the category obtained from the category of chain complexes by identifying two chain maps if and only if they are homotopic.}

\text{By Lemma 3.4 an additive functor}~F~\text{induces a functor between the homotopy categories, and by Proposition 3.1 the homology functor factors through the homotopy category.}
