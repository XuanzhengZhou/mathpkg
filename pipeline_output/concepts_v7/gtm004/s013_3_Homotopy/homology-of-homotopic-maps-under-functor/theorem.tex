\text{Let}~\varphi \simeq \psi : C \rightarrow D~\text{be homotopic chain maps and let}~F~\text{be an additive functor. Then}

$$H(F(\varphi)) = H(F(\psi)) : H(F(C)) \rightarrow H(F(D)).$$

\text{Equivalently, if}~H(F(\varphi)) \neq H(F(\psi))~\text{for some additive functor}~F,~\text{then}~\varphi~\text{and}~\psi~\text{are not homotopic.}
