Lemma 3.4 of Hilton--Stammbach states that any additive functor $F$ preserves the homotopy relation: if $\varphi \simeq \psi$ as chain maps, then $F(\varphi) \simeq F(\psi)$ as well. This is a crucial observation because it means additive functors descend to functors between the corresponding homotopy categories. Together with Proposition 3.1 (homotopic maps induce equal homology maps), this implies that the homology functor factors through the homotopy category.
