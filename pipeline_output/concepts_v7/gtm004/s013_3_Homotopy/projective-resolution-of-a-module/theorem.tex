\text{A projective and acyclic positive chain complex}

$$P : \cdots \rightarrow P_n \rightarrow P_{n-1} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow P_0$$

\text{together with an isomorphism}~H_0(P) \cong A~\text{is called a \text{projective resolution} of}~A.~\text{In the sequel we identify}~H_0(P)~\text{with}~A~\text{via the given isomorphism.}
