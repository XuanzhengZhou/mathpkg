Proposition 8.2 is a technical result about the behavior of limits under a factorization of the inclusion maps in a filtered object. When every inclusion $X^p \hookrightarrow X$ factors through a fixed object $Y$, the induced quotient objects $Y_p = Y/X^p$ form a system whose limit is $Y$, provided that $X$ is already the limit of the $X_p$. This "bicartesian" property is used to establish Propositions 8.3 and ultimately Theorem 8.5 on the completion of filtered objects.
