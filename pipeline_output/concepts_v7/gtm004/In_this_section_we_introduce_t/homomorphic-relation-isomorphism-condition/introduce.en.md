Corollary 7.4 follows directly from Theorem 7.3 by analyzing the exactness of the limit diagram. The homomorphic relation $\Theta$ is defined as $\operatorname{im}\{\beta_*, \varphi^+\}$, which gives an exact sequence relating $\operatorname{coker} \alpha'$, $E_\infty \oplus \Gamma^+$, and $\ker \bar{\alpha}''$. The vanishing of $\operatorname{coker} \bar{\alpha}'$ and $\ker \alpha''$ ensures that the rows and columns of the limit diagram collapse, making $\Theta$ an isomorphism. This is the precise condition for the spectral sequence to converge to the target $\Gamma^+$.
