Let $\psi : X \to X'$ be a morphism of filtered objects in the abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$, with filtrations
$$X^p \hookrightarrow X \twoheadrightarrow X_p, \qquad X'^p \hookrightarrow X' \twoheadrightarrow X'_p.$$

Suppose that $\psi$ induces an isomorphism on the associated graded objects:
$$\psi_* : \operatorname{Gr}(X) \xrightarrow{\sim} \operatorname{Gr}(X'),$$
where $\operatorname{Gr}(X)_p = X^p / X^{p-1}$.

Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item If the filtrations of $X$ and $X'$ are left complete, then $\psi_p : X_p \to X'_p$ is an isomorphism for all $p$.
\item If the filtrations of $X$ and $X'$ are right complete, then $\psi^p : X^p \to X'^p$ is an isomorphism for all $p$.
\item If the filtrations of $X$ and $X'$ are complete, then $\psi : X \to X'$ is an isomorphism.
\end{enumerate}
