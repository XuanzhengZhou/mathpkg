Theorem 8.5 guarantees that the completion construction yields a filtered object that is both left and right complete, and that the associated graded object is unchanged. The proof shows that right completeness follows by construction (the limit defines $Y$), while left completeness follows from the dual of Proposition 8.2. The theorem also establishes that limits and colimits commute in this construction: $(X^\infty)_{-\infty} = (X_{-\infty})^\infty$, so the order of taking colimit and limit does not matter. This means that any filtered object can be replaced by its completion without affecting the associated graded, which is exactly what is needed to apply Theorem 8.4.
