Let $X$ be a filtered object in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$ with filtration
$$X^{p-1} \xrightarrow{\xi^p} X^p \xrightarrow{\nu^p} X \xrightarrow{\eta_p} X_p \xrightarrow{\xi_p} X_{p+1}.$$

Construct its completion $Y = (X^\infty)_{-\infty}$ as the bottom row of the diagram:
$$X^{p-1} \xrightarrow{\xi^p} X^p \xrightarrow{\nu^{\infty, p}} X^\infty \xrightarrow{\eta^\infty_p} X^\infty_p \xrightarrow{\xi^\infty_p} X^\infty_{p+1}$$
$$\downarrow \lambda \qquad \downarrow \lambda_p \qquad \downarrow \lambda_{p+1}$$
$$X^{p-1} \xrightarrow{\xi^p} X^p \xrightarrow{\nu^{\infty, p}} X^\infty \xrightarrow{\eta^\infty_p} X^\infty_p \xrightarrow{\xi^\infty_p} X^\infty_{p+1}$$

Then the completion $Y$ is a complete filtered object and
$$\operatorname{Gr} Y = \operatorname{Gr} X.$$

Moreover,
$$\left(X^\infty\right)_{-\infty} = \left(X_{-\infty}\right)^\infty = \varprojlim_q \varinjlim_p \left(X_q^p; \varrho_q^p, \sigma_q^p\right) = \varinjlim_p \varprojlim_q \left(X_q^p; \varrho_q^p, \sigma_q^p\right).$$
