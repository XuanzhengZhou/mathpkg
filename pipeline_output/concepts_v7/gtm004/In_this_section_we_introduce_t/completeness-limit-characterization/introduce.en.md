Proposition 8.3 characterizes left and right completeness of a filtration in terms of limits of the intermediate subquotients $X_q^p = X^p / X^q$. Right completeness guarantees that each $X^p$ can be recovered as the projective limit of the quotients $X_q^p$ as $q \to -\infty$. Dually, left completeness guarantees that each quotient $X_q$ can be recovered as the inductive limit of the subquotients $X_q^p$ as $p \to \infty$. This result follows from Proposition 8.2 and its dual, and is essential for proving Theorem 8.4 on the isomorphism of complete filtered objects.
