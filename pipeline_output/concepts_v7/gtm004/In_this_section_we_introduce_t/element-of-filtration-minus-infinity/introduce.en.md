In the theory of spectral sequences, the condition $I_p \cap \alpha^{-1}(0) = 0$ from Corollary 7.4 has a concrete interpretation for filtered differential groups. An element has filtration $-\infty$ if it persists in the homology of all finer subfiltrations $C^{(r)}$ for $r \leq p$. This terminology captures the idea that such an element comes from "infinitely far back" in the filtration, analogous to an element that belongs to $\bigcap_r C^{(r)}$ on the chain level.
