The Rees system

$$\Gamma \xrightarrow{\theta} \Gamma$$
$$\bar{D} \xrightarrow{\bar{\alpha}} \bar{D} \xrightarrow{\bar{\beta}} \bar{E} \xrightarrow{\bar{\gamma}} \bar{I} \xrightarrow{\bar{\alpha}''} \bar{I}$$
$$\xi \downarrow \qquad \xi \downarrow \qquad \xi \downarrow \qquad \xi \downarrow$$
$$D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D \xrightarrow{\beta} E \xrightarrow{\gamma} I \xrightarrow{\alpha''} I$$

gives rise to the limit diagram

$$\begin{array}{ccccc}
\operatorname{coker} \bar{\alpha}' & \xrightarrow{\xi'} & \operatorname{coker} \alpha' & \xrightarrow{\varphi^+} & \Gamma^+ \\
& & \beta_* \downarrow & & \\
\operatorname{coker} \bar{\alpha}' & \xrightarrow{\bar{\beta}_*} & E_\infty & \xrightarrow{\bar{\gamma}_*} & \ker \bar{\alpha}'' \\
& & \gamma_* \downarrow & & \\
& & \ker \alpha'' & = & \ker \alpha''
\end{array}$$

with exact rows and columns.
