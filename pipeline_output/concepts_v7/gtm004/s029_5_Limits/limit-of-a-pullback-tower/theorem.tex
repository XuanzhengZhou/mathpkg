Let a tower of objects and morphisms be given for $i \geq 1$:
$$
\begin{CD}
A_i @>{\varphi_i}>> B_i \\
@V{\alpha_i}VV @VV{\beta_i}V \\
A_{i-1} @>>{\varphi_{i-1}}> B_{i-1}
\end{CD}
$$
where each square is a pullback. Let $A_{\infty} = \lim_i A_i$ and $B_{\infty} = \lim_i B_i$ with structure maps $\delta_i^A : A_{\infty} \rightarrow A_i$, $\delta_i^B : B_{\infty} \rightarrow B_i$ given by the counit. Let $\varphi_{\infty} : A_{\infty} \rightarrow B_{\infty}$ and $\bar{\alpha} : A_{\infty} \rightarrow A_0$, $\bar{\beta} : B_{\infty} \rightarrow B_0$ be the induced limit morphisms. Then the square
$$
\begin{CD}
A_{\infty} @>{\varphi_{\infty}}>> B_{\infty} \\
@V{\bar{\alpha}}VV @VV{\bar{\beta}}V \\
A_0 @>>{\varphi_0}> B_0
\end{CD}
$$
is a pullback. This result holds in any category provided the required limits exist.
