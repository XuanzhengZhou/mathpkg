A complex $K$ in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$,
$$K: \dots \rightarrow K_n \rightarrow K_{n-1} \rightarrow \dots \rightarrow K_0,$$
is called \textbf{$\mathcal{E}$-acyclic} if the augmented complex
$$\dots \rightarrow K_n \rightarrow K_{n-1} \rightarrow \dots \rightarrow K_0 \rightarrow H_0(K) \rightarrow 0$$
is exact.
