Let $\mathfrak{A}, \mathfrak{B}$ be abelian categories and let $\mathfrak{E}$ be a projective class of epimorphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$. If $H : \mathfrak{A} \rightarrow \mathfrak{B}$ is a right $\mathfrak{E}$-exact functor, then the $\mathfrak{E}$-connected sequence of functors $\{L_j^{\mathfrak{E}} H\}$ is the left $\mathfrak{E}$-satellite of $H$:
$$L_j^{\mathfrak{E}} H = S_j^{\mathfrak{E}} H \quad (j \geq 0),$$
with $L_j^{\mathfrak{E}} H = 0$ for $j < 0$.
