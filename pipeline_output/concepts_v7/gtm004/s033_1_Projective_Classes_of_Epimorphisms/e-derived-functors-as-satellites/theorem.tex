Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a projective class of epimorphisms in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$, and let $H : \mathfrak{A} \to \mathfrak{B}$ be a right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functor to an abelian category $\mathfrak{B}$. Then the $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequence of functors $\{L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H\}_{j \geq 0}$ is the left $\mathcal{E}$-satellite of $H$, i.e.
\[
L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H = S_j^{\mathcal{E}} H, \qquad L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H = 0 \;\; (j < 0).
\]

The $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors satisfy the universal property: for any $\mathcal{E}$-connected sequence $T$ and natural transformation $\varphi : T_0 \to L_0^{\mathcal{E}} H$, there exist unique natural transformations $\varphi_j : T_j \to L_j^{\mathcal{E}} H$ extending $\varphi$ and commuting with the connecting homomorphisms.
