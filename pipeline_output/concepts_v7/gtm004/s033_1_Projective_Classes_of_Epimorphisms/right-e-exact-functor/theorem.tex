Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a closed class of epimorphisms. A functor $T : \mathfrak{A} \rightarrow \mathfrak{B}$ between abelian categories is called \textbf{right $\mathcal{E}$-exact} if, for every $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence
$$A' \rightarrow A \rightarrow A'' \rightarrow 0,$$
the sequence $TA' \rightarrow TA \rightarrow TA'' \rightarrow 0$ is exact.
