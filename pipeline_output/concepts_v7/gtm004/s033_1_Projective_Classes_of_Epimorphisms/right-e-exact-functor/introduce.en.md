A right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functor is the relative analogue of a right exact
functor: it preserves exactness of $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequences of the form
$A' 	o A 	o A'' 	o 0$. This notion replaces the usual right exactness when
working in the relative setting. Just as ordinary right exact functors are the
natural input for left derived functors, right $\mathcal{E}$-exact functors
serve as the base for left $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors.
