Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a closed class of epimorphisms. Every short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence
$$0 \rightarrow A' \xrightarrow{\alpha'} A \xrightarrow{\alpha''} A'' \rightarrow 0$$
may be embedded in a commutative diagram
$$
\begin{array}{ccccccccc}
0 & \rightarrow & K' & \xrightarrow{\kappa'} & K & \xrightarrow{\kappa''} & K'' & \rightarrow & 0 \\
& & \downarrow & & \downarrow & & \downarrow & & \\
0 & \rightarrow & P' & \xrightarrow{\lambda'} & P & \xrightarrow{\lambda''} & P'' & \rightarrow & 0 \\
& & \downarrow & & \downarrow & & \downarrow & & \\
0 & \rightarrow & A' & \xrightarrow{\alpha'} & A & \xrightarrow{\alpha''} & A'' & \rightarrow & 0 \\
& & \downarrow & & \downarrow & & \downarrow & & \\
& & 0 & & 0 & & 0 & &
\end{array}
$$
with all rows and columns $\mathcal{E}$-exact and $P', P, P''$ $\mathcal{E}$-projective.
