Let $0 \rightarrow T' \rightarrow T \rightarrow T'' \rightarrow 0$ be a sequence of additive functors $\mathfrak{A} \rightarrow \mathfrak{B}$ which is $\mathcal{E}$-exact on $\mathcal{E}$-projectives. Then, for any object $A$ in $\mathfrak{A}$, there exist connecting homomorphisms
$$\omega_n : L_n^{\mathcal{E}} T''(A) \rightarrow L_{n-1}^{\mathcal{E}} T'(A)$$
such that the sequence
$$\cdots \rightarrow L_n T'(A) \rightarrow L_n T(A) \rightarrow L_n T''(A) \xrightarrow{\omega_n} L_{n-1} T'(A) \rightarrow \cdots$$
$$\cdots \rightarrow L_0 T'(A) \rightarrow L_0 T(A) \rightarrow L_0 T''(A) \rightarrow 0$$
is exact.
