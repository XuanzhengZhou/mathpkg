Let $\mathcal{E}_3$ be the projective class of all pure epimorphisms in the category of abelian groups. Define
$$\text{Pext}(-, B) = R_1^{\mathcal{E}_3} \text{Hom}(-, B).$$
Then for any pure exact sequence $0 \rightarrow A' \rightarrow A \rightarrow A'' \rightarrow 0$, there are exact sequences
$$0 \rightarrow \text{Hom}(A'', B) \rightarrow \text{Hom}(A, B) \rightarrow \text{Hom}(A', B) \rightarrow \text{Pext}(A'', B) \rightarrow \text{Pext}(A, B) \rightarrow \text{Pext}(A', B) \rightarrow 0$$
and
$$0 \rightarrow \text{Hom}(B, A') \rightarrow \text{Hom}(B, A) \rightarrow \text{Hom}(B, A'') \rightarrow \text{Pext}(B, A') \rightarrow \text{Pext}(B, A) \rightarrow \text{Pext}(B, A'') \rightarrow 0.$$
