The Pext functor is the first right $\mathcal{E}_3$-derived functor of Hom, where
$\mathcal{E}_3$ is the projective class of pure epimorphisms of abelian groups.
It measures the failure of the Hom functor to be exact on pure exact sequences,
in the same way that the ordinary Ext functor measures the failure of Hom to be
exact on all short exact sequences. The higher derived functors $R_n^{\mathcal{E}_3}$
vanish for $n \geq 2$, so Pext captures all the higher-order information.
The functor is contravariant in the first argument and covariant in the second,
yielding two long exact sequences from a pure exact sequence.
