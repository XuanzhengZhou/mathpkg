Let $\mathcal{E}$ be a closed projective class of epimorphisms in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$. Any short $\mathcal{E}$-exact sequence
\[
0 \to A' \xrightarrow{\alpha'} A \xrightarrow{\alpha''} A'' \to 0
\]
can be embedded in a commutative diagram
\[
\begin{array}{ccccccccc}
0 & \to & K' & \to & K & \to & K'' & \to & 0 \\
& & \downarrow\kappa' & & \downarrow\kappa & & \downarrow\kappa'' & & \\
0 & \to & P' & \to & P & \to & P'' & \to & 0 \\
& & \downarrow\varepsilon' & & \downarrow\varepsilon & & \downarrow\varepsilon'' & & \\
0 & \to & A' & \xrightarrow{\alpha'} & A & \xrightarrow{\alpha''} & A'' & \to & 0
\end{array}
\]
with all rows and columns $\mathcal{E}$-exact and $P', P, P''$ $\mathcal{E}$-projective. Moreover, $\kappa'', \varepsilon'', \kappa', \varepsilon' \in \mathcal{E}$.
