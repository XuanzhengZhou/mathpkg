Corollary 3.4 states that the $\mathcal{E}$-satellite of the zeroth $\mathcal{E}$-derived
functor recovers the full sequence of $\mathcal{E}$-derived functors. The proof is
immediate from Theorem 3.2 together with the observations that $L_0 H$ is right
$\mathcal{E}$-exact and that $L_j(L_0 H) = L_j H$ (since $L_0 HP = HP$ for any
$\mathcal{E}$-projective $P$). This corollary shows that any additive functor can
serve as the base for an $\mathcal{E}$-derived functor theory by first passing to
its zeroth left $\mathcal{E}$-derived functor.
