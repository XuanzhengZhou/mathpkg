Let $A$ be a submodule of a $\Lambda$-module $M$. Consider the set $\Phi$ of essential extensions of $A$ contained in $M$. Under inclusion, $\Phi$ is inductive, and by Zorn's Lemma there exists a maximal essential extension $E$ of $A$ contained in $M$.

Moreover, if $A$ is embedded in an injective module $I$, then a maximal essential extension $E$ of $A$ in $I$ admits no proper essential monomorphism, and $E$ is a direct summand of $I$; consequently $E$ is itself injective.
