A left module over a ring $\Lambda$ generalizes the notion of a vector space over a field, where the scalars come from an arbitrary ring rather than a field. The four axioms M1--M4 ensure that the ring action respects both the additive structure of the module and the ring operations. Equivalently, a module structure can be encoded as a ring homomorphism from $\Lambda$ into the endomorphism ring of the underlying abelian group, making the connection to representation theory explicit.
