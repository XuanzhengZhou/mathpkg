A short exact sequence encodes the relationship between a module, a submodule, and the corresponding quotient module in a single diagram. It is the most fundamental type of exact sequence in homological algebra, serving as the basic building block for derived functors, extension theory, and the study of module categories. The data of a short exact sequence is equivalent to specifying that $A'$ embeds into $A$ with quotient $A''$.
