Let $\Delta = \varphi \alpha = \psi \beta$ be the diagonal of a pull-back square in $\mathfrak{C}$. Then $(\Delta; \alpha, \beta)$ is the product of $\varphi$ and $\psi$ in the slice category $\mathfrak{C}/X$. Here $\alpha : \Delta \to \varphi$ and $\beta : \Delta \to \psi$ are regarded as morphisms in $\mathfrak{C}/X$.
