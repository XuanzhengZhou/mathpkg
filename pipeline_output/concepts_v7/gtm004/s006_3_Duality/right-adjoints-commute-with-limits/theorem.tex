Let $F \dashv G$ where $F : \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{D}$, and let $P \dashv R$ where $P$ is the constant functor (for both $\mathfrak{C}$ and $\mathfrak{D}$) into a functor category indexed by a small category $\mathfrak{P}$. Then there is a natural equivalence
$$
GR \simeq R G^{\mathfrak{P}},
$$
uniquely determined by the given adjugants. In words: right adjoints commute with limit functors $R$.
