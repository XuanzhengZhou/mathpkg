The constant functor $P$ is natural: it commutes with any functor $F$ in the sense that applying $F$ and then taking the constant diagram is the same as taking the constant diagram and then applying $F^{\mathfrak{P}}$. This simple lemma is a building block for the theorem that right adjoints commute with limits.
