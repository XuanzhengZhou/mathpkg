In a concrete category with a free functor left adjoint to the underlying functor, every object is a quotient of a free object (via the counit of the adjunction). When the underlying functor preserves epimorphisms, an object is projective precisely when it is a retract of a free object. This provides a structural characterization of projectives in categories like $\mathfrak{M}_A$ and $\mathfrak{G}$.
