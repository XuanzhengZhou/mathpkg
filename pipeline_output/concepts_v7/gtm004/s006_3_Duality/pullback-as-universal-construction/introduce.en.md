Just as products arise as right adjoints to the constant functor into a product category, pull-backs arise as right adjoints to the constant functor into the category of spans. This reveals pull-backs as another instance of the general paradigm of universal constructions as adjoints. The constant functor for pull-backs is a full embedding, reflecting the fact that pull-backs of constant diagrams are trivial.
