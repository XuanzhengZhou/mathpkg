An \textit{abelian category} is an additive category $\mathfrak{A}$ in which:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] Every morphism has a kernel and a cokernel.
\item[(ii)] Every monomorphism is the kernel of its cokernel; every epimorphism is the cokernel of its kernel.
\item[(iii)] Every morphism $\varphi$ is expressible as $\varphi = \nu \eta$ where $\eta$ is an epimorphism and $\nu$ is a monomorphism.
\end{enumerate}
