This corollary explains the fundamental connection between freeness and projectivity. Since every set is projective in $\mathfrak{S}$, and the free functor is left adjoint to the underlying functor, the image of a set under the free functor is projective, provided the underlying functor preserves epimorphisms (which it does for modules, abelian groups, and groups, but not for integral domains). This unifies the proofs that free modules, free groups, etc. are projective.
