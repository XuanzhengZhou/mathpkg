Let $P : \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{C}^I$ be the constant functor assigning to each object $X$ the constant family $\{X\}_{i \in I}$. Then the product functor $\prod_I : \mathfrak{C}^I \to \mathfrak{C}$ is right adjoint to $P$, and the projections $p_i$ are given by the counit of the adjunction. Dually, the coproduct is left adjoint to $P$, with injections given by the unit.
