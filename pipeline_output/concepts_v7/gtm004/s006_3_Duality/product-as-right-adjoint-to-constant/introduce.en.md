The formation of products in a category is a universal construction: the product functor is right adjoint to the constant (diagonal) functor. This perspective unifies the notion of limits: a universal construction corresponding to a functor is either a left adjoint or a right adjoint to that functor, with the (co)unit providing the structural morphisms.
