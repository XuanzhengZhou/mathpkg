In an abelian category, every morphism admits an essentially unique epi-mono factorization, analogous to factoring a homomorphism as a surjection onto its image followed by the inclusion of the image. This factorization is functorial and is fundamental for defining exact sequences and homology in abelian categories. The image is constructed as the cokernel of the kernel.
