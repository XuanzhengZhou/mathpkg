If a category $\mathfrak{C}$ admits products for all families indexed by a set $I$, then the assignment $\{X_i\}_{i \in I} \mapsto \prod_i X_i$ together with $\{f_i\}_{i \in I} \mapsto \prod_i f_i$ defines a functor
$$
\prod_i : \mathfrak{C}^I \to \mathfrak{C}
$$
from the product category $\mathfrak{C}^I$ to $\mathfrak{C}$.
