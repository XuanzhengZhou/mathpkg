This proposition characterizes when the functors in an adjunction are full and faithful (fully faithful). A right adjoint is fully faithful precisely when the counit is an isomorphism; a left adjoint is fully faithful precisely when the unit is an isomorphism. When the unit is an isomorphism, the right adjoint can be modified so that $GF = 1$ and the unit becomes the identity.
