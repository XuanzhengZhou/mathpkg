Let $F \dashv G$ with unit $\varepsilon$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] $G$ is full and faithful if and only if the counit $\delta$ is a natural equivalence.
\item[(ii)] $F$ is full and faithful if and only if the unit $\varepsilon$ is a natural equivalence.
\end{enumerate}
If $\varepsilon$ is a natural equivalence, we may replace $G$ by a functor $G'$ such that $F \dashv G'$ with $G'F = 1_{\mathfrak{C}}$ and unit $1$.
