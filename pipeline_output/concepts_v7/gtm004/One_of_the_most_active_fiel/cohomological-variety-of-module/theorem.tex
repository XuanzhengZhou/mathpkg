Let $G$ be a finite group and $k$ a field of characteristic $p$ dividing $|G|$. Let
$$X_G = \operatorname{MaxSpec} H^{\text{ev}}(G, k)$$
be the maximal ideal spectrum of the even-dimensional cohomology algebra $H^{\text{ev}}(G, k) = \bigoplus_{n \geq 0} H^{2n}(G, k)$ (if $p \neq 2$; for $p = 2$, one uses $H^*(G, k)$ itself). For a $kG$-module $M$, a subvariety $X_G(M) \subseteq X_G = X_G(k)$ is defined such that
$$\dim X_G(M) = c_G(M),$$
where $c_G(M)$ is the complexity of $M$.
