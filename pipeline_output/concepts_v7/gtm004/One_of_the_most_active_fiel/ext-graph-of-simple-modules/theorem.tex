Let $G$ be a finite group and let $k$ be a field of characteristic $p$ dividing $|G|$. Let $S_0 = k, S_1, \ldots, S_i$ be a complete set of representatives of the isomorphism classes of simple $kG$-modules. The \textbf{extension graph} $\Gamma(G)$ is defined as follows:
\begin{itemize}
\item The vertices of $\Gamma(G)$ correspond bijectively to the simple modules $S_0, S_1, \ldots, S_i$.
\item $\Gamma(G)$ has an edge connecting $S_i$ and $S_j$ if and only if
$$\operatorname{Ext}_k^1(S_i, S_j) \neq 0.$$
\end{itemize}
