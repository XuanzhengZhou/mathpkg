Let $\Gamma(G)$ be the extension graph of simple $kG$-modules. Two simple modules $S_i$ and $S_j$ are said to be in the same \textbf{block} $\mathcal{B}$ if the vertices $S_i$ and $S_j$ lie in the same connected component of $\Gamma(G)$. The block containing the trivial module $S_0 = k$ is called the \textbf{principal block}.
