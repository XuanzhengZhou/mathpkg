Let $G$ be a finite group and let $k$ be a field of characteristic $p$. The indecomposable modules that are projective over $kG$ are precisely the indecomposable direct summands of the module $kG$. They are called the \textbf{principal indecomposable modules} of $kG$. Over $kG$, the notions of projective and injective module coincide.
