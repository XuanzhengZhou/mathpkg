Let $M$ be an indecomposable $kG$-module with vertex $V$. There exists an indecomposable $kV$-module $N$, essentially uniquely determined up to isomorphism and conjugacy, such that $M$ is a direct summand of the induced module $kG \otimes_{kV} N$. The $kV$-module $N$ is called the \textbf{source} of $M$.
