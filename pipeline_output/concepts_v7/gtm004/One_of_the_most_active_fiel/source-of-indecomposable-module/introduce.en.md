The source of an indecomposable $kG$-module $M$ is the indecomposable $kV$-module (where $V$ is a vertex of $M$) from which $M$ is induced. Together, the vertex and source form the Green pair that uniquely characterises $M$. The source is essentially unique and provides a way to relate the module theory of $G$ to that of its $p$-subgroups. The theory of vertices and sources has proved extremely important in modular representation theory.
