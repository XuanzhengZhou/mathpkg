The Green correspondence is a fundamental structural result in modular representation theory, establishing a bijection between indecomposable modules of a group $G$ and those of the normalizer of a $p$-subgroup, when both have the same vertex $Q$. The correspondence is realised concretely via restriction and induction. It generalises the Brauer correspondence from character theory and is a powerful tool for reducing questions about $kG$-modules to questions about modules over smaller subgroups.
