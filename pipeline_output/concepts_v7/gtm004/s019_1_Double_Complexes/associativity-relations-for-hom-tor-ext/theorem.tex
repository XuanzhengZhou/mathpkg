Let $A', A, A''$ be abelian groups. There is an \emph{unnatural} isomorphism
\begin{align*}
\operatorname{Hom}(\operatorname{Tor}(A', A), A'') &\oplus \operatorname{Ext}(A' \otimes A, A'') \\
&\cong \operatorname{Hom}(A', \operatorname{Ext}(A, A'')) \oplus \operatorname{Ext}(A', \operatorname{Hom}(A, A'')),
\end{align*}
and a \emph{natural} isomorphism
$$\operatorname{Ext}(\operatorname{Tor}(A', A), A'') \cong \operatorname{Ext}(A', \operatorname{Ext}(A, A'')),$$
where $\operatorname{Tor} = \operatorname{Tor}_1^{\mathbb{Z}}$ and $\operatorname{Ext} = \operatorname{Ext}_{\mathbb{Z}}^1$.
