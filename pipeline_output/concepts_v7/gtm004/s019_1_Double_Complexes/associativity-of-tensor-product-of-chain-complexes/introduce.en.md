Proposition 4.1 states that the tensor product of chain complexes is associative up to natural isomorphism, just as the tensor product of modules is. This associativity is the starting point for deriving the deeper associativity relations between the derived functors $\operatorname{Tor}$ and $\operatorname{Ext}$ in Theorems 4.2 and 4.3.

By taking free resolutions and applying the Künneth formula to both sides of the associativity isomorphism, one obtains nontrivial relations among $\operatorname{Tor}$ and $\operatorname{Ext}$ that are not obvious from their definitions.
