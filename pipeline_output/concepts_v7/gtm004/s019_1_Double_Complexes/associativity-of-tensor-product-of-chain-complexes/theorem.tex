Let $C', C, C''$ be chain complexes over a commutative ring $\Lambda$. There is a natural isomorphism of chain complexes
$$(C' \otimes_{\Lambda} C) \otimes_{\Lambda} C'' \cong C' \otimes_{\Lambda} (C \otimes_{\Lambda} C'').$$

In particular, if $C'$, $C$, $C''$ are free resolutions of $\Lambda$-modules $A'$, $A$, $A''$ respectively, then the homology of either side may be computed via the Künneth formula.
