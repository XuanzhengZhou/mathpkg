Let $C, D, E$ be chain complexes of $\Lambda$-modules. There is a natural isomorphism of chain complexes
$$\operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(C \otimes_{\Lambda} D, E) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(C, \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(D, E)).$$

At the level of components, this isomorphism is induced by the standard tensor-hom adjunction
$$\operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(C_p \otimes_{\Lambda} D_q, E_r) \cong \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(C_p, \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(D_q, E_r)),$$
and is compatible with the differentials $\partial^H$ on both sides.
