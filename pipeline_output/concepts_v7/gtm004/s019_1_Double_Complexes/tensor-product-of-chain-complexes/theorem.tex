Let $C$ be a chain complex of right $\Lambda$-modules and $D$ a chain complex of left $\Lambda$-modules. The \emph{tensor product} $C \otimes_{\Lambda} D$ is the chain complex $\operatorname{Tot} B$, where $B$ is the double complex of abelian groups defined by
$$B_{p,q} = C_p \otimes_{\Lambda} D_q,$$
$$\partial'(c \otimes d) = \partial c \otimes d, \quad \partial''(c \otimes d) = (-1)^p c \otimes \partial d, \qquad c \in C_p,\; d \in D_q.$$

The differential in $C \otimes_{\Lambda} D$ is given explicitly by
$$\partial^{\otimes}(c \otimes d) = \partial c \otimes d + (-1)^p c \otimes \partial d, \quad c \in C_p,\; d \in D_q.$$
