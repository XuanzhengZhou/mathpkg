Corollary 1.3 is the immediate consequence of Proposition 1.2: chain homotopy equivalences between complexes induce chain homotopy equivalences between their tensor products and Hom complexes. This means that $\otimes_\Lambda$ and $\operatorname{Hom}_\Lambda$ are well-defined bifunctors on the homotopy category of chain complexes.

This result is fundamental for derived functors: $\operatorname{Tor}$ and $\operatorname{Ext}$ are defined using projective/injective resolutions, and homotopy invariance guarantees that different resolutions yield isomorphic derived functors.
