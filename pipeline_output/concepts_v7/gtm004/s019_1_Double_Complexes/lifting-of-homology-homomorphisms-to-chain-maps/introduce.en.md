Lemma 2.3 states that when the source chain complex $C$ is free, every graded homomorphism between homology modules can be lifted to a chain map between the complexes. The proof uses the freeness (projectivity) of cycles in a free chain complex over a p.i.d. to lift along the exact sequences $0 	o B_p 	o Z_p 	o H_p 	o 0$.

This lemma is a crucial technical tool used in proving both the Künneth theorem and its dual, as it allows one to construct chain maps inducing prescribed maps on homology.
