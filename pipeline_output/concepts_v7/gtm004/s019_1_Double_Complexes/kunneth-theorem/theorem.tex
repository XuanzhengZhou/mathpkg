Let $\Lambda$ be a principal ideal domain and let $C, D$ be chain complexes over $\Lambda$ with $C$ (or $D$) flat. Then there is a natural short exact sequence
$$0 \rightarrow \bigoplus_{p+q=n} H_p(C) \otimes_{\Lambda} H_q(D) \xrightarrow{\zeta} H_n(C \otimes_{\Lambda} D) \rightarrow \bigoplus_{p+q=n-1} \operatorname{Tor}_1^{\Lambda}(H_p(C), H_q(D)) \rightarrow 0,$$
where $\zeta$ is induced by the inclusion of representative cycles
$$Z_p(C) \otimes_{\Lambda} Z_q(D) \rightarrow Z_{p+q}(C \otimes_{\Lambda} D).$$

Moreover, the sequence splits, but \emph{not naturally}.
