Theorem 4.2 derives associativity relations for the $\operatorname{Tor}$ functor by applying the Künneth formula to both sides of the associativity isomorphism for the tensor product of chain complexes. The first relation is an unnatural isomorphism (involving direct sums of mixed terms), while the second is a natural isomorphism showing that $\operatorname{Tor}$ is associative.

These relations reveal the "true nature" of $\operatorname{Tor}$ as an associative bifunctor, though this structure is partially obscured by the traditional notation that writes $\operatorname{Tor}$ to the left of its arguments rather than between them.
