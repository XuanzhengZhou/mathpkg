Let $B$ be a double complex of chains. The \emph{second total complex} $\operatorname{Tot}' B$ is the chain complex defined by
$$(\operatorname{Tot}' B)_n = \prod_{p+q=n} B_{p,q},$$
with differential
$$\partial = \partial' + \partial'' : (\operatorname{Tot}' B)_n \rightarrow (\operatorname{Tot}' B)_{n-1}.$$

The relation $\partial \circ \partial = 0$ follows from $\partial' \partial' = 0$, $\partial'' \partial'' = 0$, and $\partial'' \partial' + \partial' \partial'' = 0$.
