Proposition 2.3 states that short exact sequences of coefficient modules induce long exact sequences in group (co)homology. This follows from the general properties of the derived functors $\operatorname{Ext}$ and $\operatorname{Tor}$. These long exact sequences are the primary computational tool in group cohomology, allowing one to relate the (co)homology of different coefficient modules.
