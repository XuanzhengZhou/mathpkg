There is a natural isomorphism of abelian groups
$$IG / (IG)^2 \cong G_{\text{ab}} = G / [G, G],$$
where $G_{\text{ab}}$ is the abelianization of $G$, i.e.\ the quotient of $G$ by its commutator subgroup $G' = [G, G]$.

The isomorphism is given by the map $\psi: G_{\text{ab}} \to IG/(IG)^2$ induced by $x \mapsto (x-1) + (IG)^2$, with inverse $\varphi: IG/(IG)^2 \to G_{\text{ab}}$ induced by $(x-1) + (IG)^2 \mapsto x G'$.
