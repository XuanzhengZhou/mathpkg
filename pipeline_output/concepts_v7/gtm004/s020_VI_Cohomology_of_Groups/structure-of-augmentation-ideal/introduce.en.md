Lemma 1.2 describes the structure of the augmentation ideal $IG$ both as an abelian group and as a $G$-module. As an abelian group, $IG$ is free on the non-identity elements of $G$ shifted by $-1$. As a $G$-module, it is generated by $s-1$ for any set $S$ of group generators, which is crucial for computations involving group presentations.
