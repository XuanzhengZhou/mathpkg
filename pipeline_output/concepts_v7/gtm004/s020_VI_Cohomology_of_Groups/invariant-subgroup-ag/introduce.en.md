The invariant subgroup $A^G$ consists of all elements of a $G$-module $A$ that are fixed by every group element. It is the largest trivial submodule of $A$. This subgroup coincides with the zeroth cohomology group $H^0(G, A)$, establishing a fundamental link between fixed points and derived functors.
