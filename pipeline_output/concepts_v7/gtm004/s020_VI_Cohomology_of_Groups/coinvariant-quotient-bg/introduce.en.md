The coinvariant quotient $B_G$ is the largest quotient of $B$ on which $G$ acts trivially, obtained by factoring out the submodule generated by elements of the form $b(x-1)$. It is the homological counterpart of the fixed-point subgroup $A^G$, and it coincides with the zeroth homology group $H_0(G, B)$.
