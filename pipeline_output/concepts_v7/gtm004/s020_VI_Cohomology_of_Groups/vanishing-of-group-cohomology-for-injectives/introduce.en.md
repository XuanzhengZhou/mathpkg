Proposition 2.4 states that group cohomology vanishes in positive dimensions for injective coefficient modules, and group homology vanishes in positive dimensions for flat (or projective) coefficient modules. This is a direct consequence of the definition via derived functors: an injective module is acyclic for $\operatorname{Hom}$, and a flat module is acyclic for $\otimes$.
