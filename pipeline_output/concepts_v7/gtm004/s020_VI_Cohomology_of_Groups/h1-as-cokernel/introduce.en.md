Proposition 4.5 gives an explicit description of the first cohomology group $H^1(G, A)$ as the cokernel of the map $A \to \operatorname{Hom}_G(IG, A)$ sending $a$ to the map $(x-1) \mapsto xa - a$. This follows from applying $\operatorname{Ext}_{\mathbb{Z}G}^1(\mathbb{Z}, -)$ to the standard presentation. The elements of $\operatorname{Hom}_G(IG, A)$ are called \emph{derivations} or \emph{crossed homomorphisms}.
