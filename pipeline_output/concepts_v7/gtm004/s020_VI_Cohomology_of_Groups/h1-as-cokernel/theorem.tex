Let $G$ be a group and $A$ a left $G$-module. Using the $\mathbb{Z}G$-free presentation $IG \hookrightarrow \mathbb{Z}G \twoheadrightarrow \mathbb{Z}$ of the trivial module, one obtains
$$H^1(G, A) = \operatorname{Ext}_{\mathbb{Z}G}^1(\mathbb{Z}, A) = \operatorname{coker}\!\big( \iota^* : A \to \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}G}(IG, A) \big),$$
where $\iota^*(a)(x-1) = xa - a$ for $a \in A$, $x \in G$.
