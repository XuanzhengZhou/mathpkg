Let $C$ be a filtered differential object in $(\mathfrak{A}, d, f)$ with filtration
$$
\cdots \subseteq C^{(p-1)} \subseteq C^{(p)} \subseteq \cdots \subseteq C.
$$
For each $p$, the short exact sequence of differential objects
$$
0 \to C^{(p-1)} \to C^{(p)} \to C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)} \to 0
$$
induces a homology exact triangle
$$
\begin{CD}
H(C^{(p-1)}) @>{\alpha}>> H(C^{(p)}) \\
@AAA @VVV \\
& H(C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)}) &
\end{CD}
$$
where the connecting morphism raises degree by $1$.
