The $E$-term of the exact couple arising from a filtered differential object admits a clean factorization: first apply the associated graded functor to extract the successive quotients of the filtration, then apply the homology functor degreewise. This factorization $E = H \circ Gr$ separates the algebraic structure of the spectral sequence into two independent steps -- the filtration data (via $Gr$) and the differential data (via $H$) -- and clarifies why the $E_0$-page (in the convention starting with $E_0$) is precisely the homology of the associated graded complex.
