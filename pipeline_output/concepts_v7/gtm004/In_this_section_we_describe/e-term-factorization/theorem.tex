The $E$-term functor $E : (\mathfrak{A}, d, f) \to \mathfrak{A}^{\mathbb{Z}}$ associated to a filtered differential object factors as
$$
E = H \circ Gr,
$$
where:
\begin{itemize}
\item $Gr : (\mathfrak{A}, d, f) \to (\mathfrak{A}, d)^{\mathbb{Z}} = (\mathfrak{A}^{\mathbb{Z}}, d)$ is the associated graded functor applied to the underlying filtered differential object, and
\item $H : (\mathfrak{A}^{\mathbb{Z}}, d) \to \mathfrak{A}^{\mathbb{Z}}$ is the homology functor applied degreewise.
\end{itemize}
Explicitly, for $X \in (\mathfrak{A}, d, f)$,
$$
E(X)^p = H\bigl(Gr(X)^p\bigr) = H\bigl(X^{(p)} / X^{(p-1)}\bigr).
$$
