Let $C$ be a filtered differential object in $(\mathfrak{A}, d, f)$. Define graded objects $D$ and $E$ in $\mathfrak{A}^{\mathbb{Z}}$ by
$$
D^p = H(C^{(p)}), \qquad E^p = H(C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)}).
$$
Then the homology exact triangles for all $p$ assemble into an exact couple
$$
\begin{CD}
D @>{i}>> D \\
@A{k}AA @VV{j}V \\
& E &
\end{CD}
$$
where $i$ is induced by the inclusions $C^{(p-1)} \hookrightarrow C^{(p)}$, $j$ is induced by the projections $C^{(p)} \twoheadrightarrow C^{(p)}/C^{(p-1)}$, and $k$ is the connecting morphism of degree $+1$. This construction defines a functor from the category $(\mathfrak{A}, d, f)$ of filtered differential objects to the category of exact couples of $\mathfrak{A}^{\mathbb{Z}}$.
