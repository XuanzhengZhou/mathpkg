Let
$$K_q \xrightarrow{\mu} P_{q-1} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow P_0 \rightarrow A$$
be an exact sequence of $\Lambda$-modules with $P_0, P_1, \ldots, P_{q-1}$ projective. If $S : \mathfrak{M}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$ is a left exact contravariant functor and $q \geq 1$, then the sequence
$$SP_{q-1} \xrightarrow{S(\mu)} SK_q \rightarrow R^q SA \rightarrow 0$$
is exact, where $R^q S$ denotes the $q$-th right derived functor of $S$.
