Proposition 5.5 provides a partial exactness result connecting the $q$-th left derived functor $L_q T$ to a truncated projective resolution. Given an exact sequence $K_q \rightarrow P_{q-1} \rightarrow \cdots \rightarrow P_0 \rightarrow A$ where the $P_i$ are projective, and assuming $T$ is right exact, the sequence $0 \rightarrow L_q TA \rightarrow TK_q \rightarrow TP_{q-1}$ is exact. This result is useful for computing $L_q T$ from a partial resolution and is a key tool in the dimension-shifting technique.
