A contravariant functor $S : \mathfrak{M}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$ is called \textbf{left exact} if $S$, taken as a covariant functor $\mathfrak{M}_A^{\text{opp}} \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ab}$, is left exact. Equivalently, for every exact sequence
$$A' \rightarrow A \rightarrow A'' \rightarrow 0$$
of $\Lambda$-modules, the induced sequence
$$0 \rightarrow SA'' \rightarrow SA \rightarrow SA'$$
is exact.

An instance of a left exact contravariant functor is $\operatorname{Hom}_A(-, B)$ for a fixed $\Lambda$-module $B$.
