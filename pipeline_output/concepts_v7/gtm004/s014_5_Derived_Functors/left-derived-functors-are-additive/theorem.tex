The functors $L_n T : \mathfrak{M}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{Ub}$, $n = 0, 1, \ldots$, are additive. That is, for $\Lambda$-modules $A, B$,
$$L_n T(A \oplus B) \cong L_n TA \oplus L_n TB,$$
and $L_n T(\iota_A)$, $L_n T(\iota_B)$ are the canonical injections, where $\iota_A : A \rightarrow A \oplus B$, $\iota_B : B \rightarrow A \oplus B$ denote the canonical inclusions.
