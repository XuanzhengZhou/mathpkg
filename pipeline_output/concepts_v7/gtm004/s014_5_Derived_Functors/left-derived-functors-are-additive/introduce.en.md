Proposition 5.4 states that each left derived functor $L_n T$ is additive: for any two $\Lambda$-modules $A$ and $B$, there is a natural isomorphism $L_n T(A \oplus B) \cong L_n TA \oplus L_n TB$. The proof uses the fact that the direct sum of projective resolutions $P \oplus Q$ is a projective resolution of $A \oplus B$, together with the additivity of the original functor $T$. This additivity property ensures that the derived functors respect the direct sum decomposition of modules.
