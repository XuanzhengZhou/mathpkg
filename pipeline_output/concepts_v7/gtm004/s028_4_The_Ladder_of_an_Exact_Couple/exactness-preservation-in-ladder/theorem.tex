\begin{theorem}[Theorem 4.4]
Consider the ladder diagram constructed by applying the $Q$-processes to a sequence
\[
A \xrightarrow{\beta} B \xrightarrow{\gamma} C.
\]
If this bottom row is exact (i.e., $\operatorname{im}\beta = \ker\gamma$), then all rows of the constructed diagrams (4.4) and (4.7) are exact. In particular, the middle row of (4.4) is exact because
\[
\gamma^{-1}(0) \subseteq \gamma^{-1}(C_1).
\]
The remaining exactness assertions follow by duality.
\end{theorem}
