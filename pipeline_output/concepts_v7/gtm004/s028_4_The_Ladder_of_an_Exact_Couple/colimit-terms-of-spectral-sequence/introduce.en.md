The term $E_{\infty,n}$ is the colimit of the $E_{m,n}$ terms as $m$ varies, taken with respect to the horizontal structure maps $\sigma_{m,n}$. It provides an intermediate description of the $E_{\infty}$ page: one first passes to the colimit over the rung index $m$ to obtain $E_{\infty,n}$, and then takes the limit over the column index $n$ to obtain the full $E_{\infty}$ term. In concrete module categories, $E_{\infty,n}$ admits the explicit description $\gamma^{-1}(\alpha^n D)/\beta\alpha^{-\infty}(0)$, which shows that only the $\alpha^{-\infty}(0)$ part involves the $m$-direction, while the numerator involves the finite iterate $\alpha^n$.
