The right derived functor in the derived category framework generalizes the classical right derived functor of homological algebra. Instead of producing a sequence of cohomology objects, it produces a single triangulated functor between derived categories. The classical derived functors are recovered by taking cohomology: the n-th cohomology of RF(A) equals the usual n-th right derived functor applied to A, when F is left exact.
