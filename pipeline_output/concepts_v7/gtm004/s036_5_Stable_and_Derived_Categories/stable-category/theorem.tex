Let $R$ be a ring and $\mathfrak{M}_R$ the category of left $R$-modules. The {\it stable category} $\operatorname{StM}_R$ is defined as follows:
\begin{itemize}
\item The objects of $\operatorname{StM}_R$ are the same as those of $\mathfrak{M}_R$.
\item The morphisms are the equivalence classes of module homomorphisms modulo projective homomorphisms:
\[
\underline{\operatorname{Hom}}_R(A, B) = \operatorname{Hom}_R(A, B) / \operatorname{Phom}_R(A, B).
\]
\end{itemize}
In $\operatorname{StM}_R$, every projective module is isomorphic to the zero object. Two modules $A$ and $B$ are isomorphic in $\operatorname{StM}_R$ if and only if there exist projective modules $P, Q$ with $A \oplus P \cong B \oplus Q$.
