The \textbf{stable category} $\mathfrak{StM}_R$ of the module category $\mathfrak{M}_R$ is defined as follows:
\begin{itemize}
\item The objects of $\mathfrak{StM}_R$ are the same as those of $\mathfrak{M}_R$, i.e., all left $R$-modules.
\item The morphisms are the equivalence classes of morphisms in $\mathfrak{M}_R$ modulo projective homomorphisms; i.e., for objects $A, B$,
$$
\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathfrak{StM}_R}(A, B) = \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}_R(A, B) = \mathrm{Hom}_R(A, B) / \mathrm{Phom}_R(A, B).
$$
\end{itemize}
