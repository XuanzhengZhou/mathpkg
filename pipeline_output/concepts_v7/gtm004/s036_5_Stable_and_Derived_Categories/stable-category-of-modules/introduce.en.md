The stable category of modules is constructed by keeping the same objects as the ordinary module category but replacing morphisms with their equivalence classes modulo projective homomorphisms. In this category, projective modules become isomorphic to the zero object. The stable category is a fundamental tool in modular representation theory, although it is not in general an abelian category.
