A stable equivalence is an equivalence between the stable categories of two module categories. This is a weaker notion than ordinary equivalence or Morita equivalence: two module categories can be stably equivalent even when they are not Morita equivalent. Stable equivalences occur frequently in modular representation theory and are often induced from functors between the original module categories.
