\textbf{Proposition 6.6.} For the triple $F(C)$ associated to a filtered chain complex $C$, we have

$$\operatorname{Gr} \circ H(C) = i_A H(A) / i_B H(B) = \ker j_A / \ker j_B.$$

Equivalently, if $G = G(C)$ and $A = A(C)$, then

$$i_A H(A) = \bigoplus_p \operatorname{im} H(C^{(p)}), \quad i_B H(B) = \bigoplus_p \operatorname{im} H(C^{(p-1)}),$$

so that

$$\operatorname{Gr} \circ H(C) = \bigoplus_p \frac{\operatorname{im} H(C^{(p)})}{\operatorname{im} H(C^{(p-1)})}.$$
