This proposition reveals the deep connection between pull-backs and products: a pull-back over $X$ is precisely a product in the comma category $\mathfrak{C}/X$ (the category of objects over $X$). This perspective allows many properties of products to be transferred immediately to pull-backs and their generalizations to families of morphisms sharing a common codomain.
