Let $\mathfrak{L}$ be the category with three objects and two non-identity morphisms (a span), and let $F: \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{C}^{\mathfrak{L}}$ be the constant functor. Suppose $F \dashv G$ with counit $\pi: FG \to 1$. Then $\pi: FG(\varphi, \psi) \to (\varphi, \psi)$ is the pull-back of the pair $(\varphi, \psi)$. The unit $\varepsilon$ for this adjunction is the identity, reflecting that $F$ is a full embedding and the pull-back of $F(Z)$ is $(1_Z, 1_Z)$.
