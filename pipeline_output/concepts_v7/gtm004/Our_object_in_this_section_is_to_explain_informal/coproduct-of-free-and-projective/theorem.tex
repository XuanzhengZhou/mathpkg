Let $\mathfrak{C}$ be a category.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(i)] A coproduct of free objects is free. (Since the free functor $Fr$ has a right adjoint, it maps coproducts — disjoint unions in $\mathfrak{S}$ — to coproducts.)
\item[(ii)] A coproduct of projective objects is projective. Specifically, if $P = \coprod_i P_i$ where each $P_i$ is projective, then $P$ is projective.
\end{enumerate}
