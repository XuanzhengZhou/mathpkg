These identities express the functoriality of the product construction: pre-composing the family $\{f_i\}$ with $g$ simply pre-composes each component, and post-composing with $\prod_i g_i$ post-composes each component. Both can be proved by checking that each side projects properly onto the $i$-th component under the projection $p_i$, using the universal property of the product.
