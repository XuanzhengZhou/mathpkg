The constant functor is "natural" with respect to arbitrary functors: applying a functor $F$ and then taking the constant diagram is the same as taking the constant diagram and then applying $F$ pointwise. This trivial but crucial lemma is part of the proof of Theorem 8.6 establishing that right adjoints commute with limits.
