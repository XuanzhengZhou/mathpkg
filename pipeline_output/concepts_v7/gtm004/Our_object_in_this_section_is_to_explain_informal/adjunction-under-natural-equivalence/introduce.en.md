If $F$ is left adjoint to $G$ and $G'$ is naturally equivalent to $G$, then $F$ is also left adjoint to $G'$ via a transferred adjunction. The unit and counit transform accordingly by pre- and post-composition with the natural equivalence and its inverse. This proposition is useful for replacing a right adjoint with a more convenient naturally equivalent version while preserving the adjunction structure.
