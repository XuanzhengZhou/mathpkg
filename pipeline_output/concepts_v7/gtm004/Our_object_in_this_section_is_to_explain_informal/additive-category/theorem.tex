An \text{additive category} $\mathfrak{A}$ is a category with zero object in which any two objects have a product and in which the morphism sets $\mathfrak{A}(A, B)$ are abelian groups such that the composition map
$$\mathfrak{A}(A, B) \times \mathfrak{A}(B, C) \to \mathfrak{A}(A, C)$$
is bilinear.
