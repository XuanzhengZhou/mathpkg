Let $F, G$ be two functors from the category $\mathfrak{C}$ to the category $\mathfrak{D}$. A \text{natural transformation} $t$ from $F$ to $G$ is a rule assigning to each object $X$ in $\mathfrak{C}$ a morphism $t_X: FX \to GX$ in $\mathfrak{D}$ such that, for any morphism $f: X \to Y$ in $\mathfrak{C}$, the diagram
$$\begin{array}{ccc}
FX & \xrightarrow{t_X} & GX \\
{\scriptstyle Ff}\downarrow & & \downarrow{\scriptstyle Gf} \\
FY & \xrightarrow{t_Y} & GY
\end{array}$$
commutes.
