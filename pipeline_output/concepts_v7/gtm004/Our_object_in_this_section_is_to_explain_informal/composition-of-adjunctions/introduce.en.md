Adjunctions compose: if $F$ is left adjoint to $G$ and $F'$ is left adjoint to $G'$, then $F'F$ is left adjoint to $GG'$. Note that the order of the right adjoints is reversed — the right adjoint of the composite $F'F$ is the composite $GG'$ (not $G'G$). This behavior is analogous to the transpose of a product of matrices.
