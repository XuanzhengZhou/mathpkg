Let $P: \mathfrak{C} \to \mathfrak{C}^I$ be the constant functor sending each object $X$ to the constant family $\{X\}_{i \in I}$. If $P$ has a right adjoint $R: \mathfrak{C}^I \to \mathfrak{C}$, then the product of the objects $\{X_i\}_{i \in I}$ is $(X; p_i)$ where $X = R(\{X_i\})$ and the projections $p_i: X \to X_i$ are given by the counit $\delta: PR \to 1$. Dually, the coproduct is given by a left adjoint to $P$, with the injections arising from the unit.
