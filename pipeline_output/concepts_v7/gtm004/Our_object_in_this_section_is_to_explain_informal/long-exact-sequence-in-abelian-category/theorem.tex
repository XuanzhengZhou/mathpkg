A \text{long exact sequence} in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$ is a sequence
$$\cdots \to A_{n-1} \xrightarrow{\varphi_{n-1}} A_n \xrightarrow{\varphi_n} A_{n+1} \to \cdots$$
where each $\varphi_n$ factors as $\varphi_n = \mu_n \varepsilon_n$ with $\mu_n$ monomorphic and $\varepsilon_n$ epimorphic, and for each $n$, $\mu_n$ is the kernel of $\varepsilon_{n+1}$ (equivalently, $\varepsilon_{n+1}$ is the cokernel of $\mu_n$).
