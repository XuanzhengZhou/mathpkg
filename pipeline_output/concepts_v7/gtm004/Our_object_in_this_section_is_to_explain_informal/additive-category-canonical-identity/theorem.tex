In an additive category $\mathfrak{A}$, let $p_1: A_1 \oplus A_2 \to A_1$, $p_2: A_1 \oplus A_2 \to A_2$ be the product projections, and let $i_1: A_1 \to A_1 \oplus A_2$, $i_2: A_2 \to A_1 \oplus A_2$ be the coproduct injections. Then
$$i_1 p_1 + i_2 p_2 = 1: A_1 \oplus A_2 \to A_1 \oplus A_2.$$
More generally, $p_j i_k = \delta_{jk}$ (Kronecker delta).
