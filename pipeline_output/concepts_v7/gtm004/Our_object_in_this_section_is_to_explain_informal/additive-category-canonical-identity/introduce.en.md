The identity $i_1 p_1 + i_2 p_2 = 1$ is the fundamental relation of the biproduct (direct sum) in an additive category. It generalizes the block-matrix identity from linear algebra and is the key to proving that the product is also the coproduct. The relations $p_j i_k = \delta_{jk}$ and $i_1 p_1 + i_2 p_2 = 1$ characterize the biproduct structure completely.
