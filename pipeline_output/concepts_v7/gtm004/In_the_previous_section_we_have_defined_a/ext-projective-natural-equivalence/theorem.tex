For any two projective presentations of a $\Lambda$-module $A$, the resulting groups $\operatorname{Ext}_{\Lambda}^{\varepsilon}(A, B)$ and $\operatorname{Ext}_{\Lambda}^{\varepsilon'}(A, B)$ are naturally equivalent. Consequently, one may drop the superscript and write simply $\operatorname{Ext}_{\Lambda}(A, B)$.
