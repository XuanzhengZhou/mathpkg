Let
$$\begin{array}{ccc}
A' \xrightarrow{\alpha_1} A \xrightarrow{\alpha_2} A'' \\
\downarrow\scriptstyle{\varphi} & \downarrow\scriptstyle{\theta} & \downarrow \\
B' \xrightarrow{\beta_1} B \xrightarrow{\beta_2} B''
\end{array}$$
be a commutative diagram with exact rows. Then $\varphi$ induces an isomorphism
$$\Phi : \ker(\theta \alpha_2) \big/ \bigl(\ker \alpha_2 + \ker \varphi\bigr) \xrightarrow{\sim} \bigl(\operatorname{im} \varphi \cap \operatorname{im} \beta_1\bigr) \big/ \operatorname{im}(\varphi \alpha_1).$$
