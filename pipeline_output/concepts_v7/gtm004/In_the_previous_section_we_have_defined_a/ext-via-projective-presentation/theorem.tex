Let $R \xrightarrow{\mu} P \xrightarrow{\varepsilon} A$ be a projective presentation of the $\Lambda$-module $A$. For any $\Lambda$-module $B$, this induces an exact sequence
$$\operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(A, B) \xrightarrow{\varepsilon^*} \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(P, B) \xrightarrow{\mu^*} \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(R, B).$$
Define the abelian group
$$\operatorname{Ext}_{\Lambda}^{\varepsilon}(A, B) = \operatorname{coker}\bigl(\mu^* : \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(P, B) \to \operatorname{Hom}_{\Lambda}(R, B)\bigr).$$
The superscript $\varepsilon$ records dependence on the chosen projective presentation.
