There is a natural equivalence of set-valued bifunctors
$$\eta : E(-, -) \xrightarrow{\sim} \operatorname{Ext}_{\Lambda}(-, -).$$
Here $E(A, B)$ is the set of equivalence classes of short exact sequences $B \rightarrowtail E \twoheadrightarrow A$, and $\operatorname{Ext}_{\Lambda}(A, B)$ is defined via any projective presentation of $A$.
