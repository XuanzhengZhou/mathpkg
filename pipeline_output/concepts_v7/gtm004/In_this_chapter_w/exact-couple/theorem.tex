An exact couple $\mathit{EC} = \{D, E, \alpha, \beta, \gamma\}$ in an abelian category $\mathfrak{A}$ is an exact triangle of morphisms in $\mathfrak{A}$,
$$\begin{CD}
D @>\alpha>> D \\
@V\gamma VV @VV\beta V \\
E
\end{CD}$$
that is, the sequence
$$D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D \xrightarrow{\beta} E \xrightarrow{\gamma} D \xrightarrow{\alpha} D$$
is exact at each vertex.
