The derived couple is constructed from an exact couple by replacing $D$ with its image under $\alpha$ and $E$ with the homology of $E$ with respect to the differential $d_0 = \beta\gamma$. This process can be iterated to produce a sequence of exact couples whose $E$-terms form a spectral sequence. The derived couple construction is the algebraic engine that drives the spectral sequence machinery.
