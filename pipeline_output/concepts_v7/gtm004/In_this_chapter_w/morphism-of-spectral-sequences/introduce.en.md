A morphism of spectral sequences is a sequence of morphisms $\varphi_n: E_n \to E_n'$ compatible with the differentials in the sense that taking homology commutes with the morphisms: $H(\varphi_n) = \varphi_{n+1}$. This defines the category $\mathfrak{E}(\mathfrak{A})$ of spectral sequences in $\mathfrak{A}$. The compatibility condition ensures that morphisms preserve the iterative structure of the spectral sequence.
