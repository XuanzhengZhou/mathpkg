The spectral sequence functor $SS: \mathfrak{EC} \to \mathfrak{E}$ sends an exact couple to the spectral sequence obtained by iterating the derived couple construction. Each iteration replaces $E_n$ by its homology $H(E_n, d_n) = E_{n+1}$ and $D_n$ by $\alpha_n D_n = D_{n+1}$, producing a new exact couple. This functor formalizes the relationship between exact couples and spectral sequences, showing that every exact couple gives rise to a spectral sequence.
