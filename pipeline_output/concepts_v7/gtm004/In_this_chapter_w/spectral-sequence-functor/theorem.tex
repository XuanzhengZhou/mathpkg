The spectral sequence functor $\mathit{SS} : \mathfrak{EC}(\mathfrak{A}) \to \mathfrak{E}(\mathfrak{A})$ is defined by iterating the derived couple construction. Given an exact couple $\mathit{EC} = \{D, E, \alpha, \beta, \gamma\}$, set $E_0 = E$, $d_0 = \beta\gamma$, and for $n \geq 0$,
$$(E_{n+1}, d_{n+1}) = (H(E_n, d_n), \beta_{n+1}\gamma_{n+1})$$
where $\{D_n, E_n, \alpha_n, \beta_n, \gamma_n\}$ is the $n$-th derived couple of $\mathit{EC}$.
