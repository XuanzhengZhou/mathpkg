Theorem 1.1 states that the derived couple of an exact couple is again an exact couple. This is the crucial result that enables the iteration of the derived couple construction: starting from one exact couple, one obtains an infinite sequence of exact couples whose $E$-terms form a spectral sequence. The proof verifies exactness at each of the four positions using the definitions of $\alpha_1, \beta_1, \gamma_1$ and the exactness of the original couple.
