The bifunctors $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n(-,-)$ and $\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^n(-,-)$, $n = 0, 1, 2, \dots$, are naturally equivalent.

That is, there exist natural isomorphisms
$$
\Phi^n : \operatorname{Ext}_A^n(-,-) \;\xrightarrow{\;\cong\;}\; \overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^n(-,-)
$$
for all $n \geq 0$.
