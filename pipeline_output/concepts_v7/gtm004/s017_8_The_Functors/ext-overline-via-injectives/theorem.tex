The covariant functor $\operatorname{Hom}_A(A, -)$ is additive, so one may form its right derived functors. These are the $\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}$ functors.

For $A, B \in \operatorname{Mod}_A$ and $n \geq 0$,
$$
\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^n(A, -) \;=\; R^n(\operatorname{Hom}_A(A, -)).
$$

Concretely, $\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^n(A, B)$ is computed by choosing an injective resolution $I$ of $B$ and taking the $n$-th cohomology of the cochain complex $\operatorname{Hom}_A(A, I)$.
