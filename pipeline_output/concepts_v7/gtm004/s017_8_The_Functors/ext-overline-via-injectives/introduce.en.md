The functor $\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^n$ is defined as the $n$-th right derived functor of the covariant Hom functor $\operatorname{Hom}_A(A, -)$. Concretely, to compute $\overline{\operatorname{Ext}}_A^n(A,B)$, one takes an injective resolution of $B$, applies $\operatorname{Hom}_A(A,-)$, and takes the $n$-th cohomology. This yields an alternative definition of Ext using injective resolutions, which is naturally equivalent to the definition via projective resolutions (Proposition 8.1).
