Proposition 8.2 strengthens Proposition 8.1 by showing that the natural equivalence $\Phi$ between the projective and injective definitions of Ext is compatible with the connecting homomorphisms. This ensures that the long exact sequences obtained from a short exact sequence in the second variable are the same whether one uses projective or injective resolutions. The proof uses a cube diagram argument and the surjectivity of the connecting homomorphism $\omega$. Together, Propositions 8.1 and 8.2 justify treating $\operatorname{Ext}_A^n$ as a single balanced bifunctor and dropping the overline notation.
